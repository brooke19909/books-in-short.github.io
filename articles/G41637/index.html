What are the conditions and insurance rates

Consider the conditions of the SC in more detail. CTP payments are made at the MAKS insurance company based on individual calculations. In the course of working with a client, company employees take into account:

  • base tariff rate (it is calculated using a single coefficient for all insurers);
  • place of registration of the insured client;
  • characteristics of the machine;
  • the number of owners and drivers of the vehicle (their driving experience and age are also taken into account);
  • how much the car has already served;
  • Did any of the vehicle owners get into an emergency, how often did this happen.




Let's look at an example. To date (2017), vehicles of category "A" and "M" receive a tariff rate in the amount of from 870 to 1580 rubles. If the car belongs to the group "B" or "BE", then it increases to 2580-6170 rubles. Provided that the client uses vehicles of category "C" and "CE", he has the right to receive a rate equal to the amount from 3500 to 6300 rubles.

For public transport, lower tariffs range from 1750 to 3300 rubles. Construction machinery has been moved to a separate category. For her, tariff rates will range from 1,100 to 1,500 rubles.

Moreover, insurance is provided if:

  • 2 drivers of the vehicle were in an accident, and both of them have a valid OSAGO insurance policy;
  • vehicle owners do not make financial claims to each other;
  • During the accident, third parties were not injured.

What risks are taken into account when signing documents

CTP from the insurance company "MAX" insures against insured cars causing property damage or harm to another person. These are the basic conditions.

At the same time, the policy will not be valid if the car was not moving at the time of the damage. In addition, situations when the car owner was driving another car will not apply to insured events.





Also, compensation can not be considered if the car owner is accused of causing moral harm or environmental pollution. In addition, damage to the car during unloading or loading operations is not taken into account. It should be understood that professional insurance and compulsory motor liability insurance are different types of obligations.

The car owner will not wait for compensation for damage in the event of damage to architectural monuments or any other buildings bearing the status of a landmark. If the car owner himself damaged his car, then there will be no payments from the insurance either.

If, at the time of the accident or any other situation during which the car or person was injured, the car owner was intoxicated, the IC shall not be held liable. The same applies to car owners who are deprived of their rights or hiding from the scene of an accident.
