In today's market there are a huge number of budget laptops. However, far from all of them correspond to the notorious price-quality ratio. Choosing a more or less adequate model is sometimes quite difficult. A recognized leader in the production of budget laptops is Lenovo. And indeed it is. Only this manufacturer offers relatively powerful and moderately high-quality models at an adequate and non-burdensome price. Lenovo IdeaPad 310 15ISK also refers to such devices. In the meantime, remember the main milestones of the development and formation of Lenovo.

Let's talk about the manufacturer

Lenovo was founded in China in 1984. And at that time, she was engaged in adapting personal computers from IBM for use in China. The company also imported computers with might and main. In 1990, the first Lenovo personal computer was assembled. Since that time, the company continues to successfully build PCs. In 2010, Lenovo achieved such impressive success that it was able to buy the legendary IBM brand along with all its debts and merits. As a result, legendary series such as the ThinkPad and IdeaPad, a legacy of the acquired IBM, appeared in the Lenovo mobile line of computers. This is what allowed the Lenovo IdeaPad 310 15ISK to be born. Reviews about him, by the way, are pretty good.





But the manufacturer did not stop there. More recently, the company bought the Motorola brand. The legendary manufacturer was mired in debt and did not find anything better than to sell its business to the Chinese giant. Now Lenovo can release smartphones under the Moto brand. But they will not become Motorola. But with personal computers and laptops, the manufacturer is fine. They are reliable, productive, cost adequate money and can easily compete with recognized masters (like HP). This is exactly the laptop Lenovo IdeaPad 310 15ISK. We will analyze the reviews about this creation a little later, but for now let's turn to the technical characteristics of the device.

Battery life

What kind of battery is installed in this laptop? According to the technical specifications, it has a battery with a capacity of 3687 mAh and a capacity of 30 watts per hour. What does this mean in ordinary language? This means that when watching a video at a comfortable brightness and volume, it will last for 4 hours maximum. However, by turning on special energy-saving modes, lowering the brightness of the device to a minimum and artificially limiting the processor frequency, an increase of 1-2 hours can be achieved. Of course, this laptop does not shine with battery life. But do not forget about its power. It is not surprising that with such parameters he does not "live" for a long time. This is still a normal result.





Positive feedback from owners

So what are the customer reviews for the Lenovo IdeaPad 310 15? I must say that for the most part, users are happy with the purchase. The vast majority of owners of this laptop praise the performance and excellent appearance. Quite a few honors go to a rather voluminous hard drive.
