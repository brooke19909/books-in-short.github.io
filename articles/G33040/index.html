The best hotels of Severomorsk

Severomorsk is a small city in the Murmansk region. But despite the remoteness from the center of the country, severe climatic conditions. every day a fairly large number of guests arrive here: businessmen, military, business travelers. That is why the issue of accommodation in local hotels is very relevant. As part of this material, we will consider the best hotels in Severomorsk, present only the most comfortable options, where you can get the necessary conditions for an affordable fee. Guest reviews will ensure the objectivity of the review.





Features

There are not as many hotels and hotels in Severomorsk as we would like. All those who happened to come here, from their own experience, felt that one couldn’t find luxury apartments with perfect service: you have to be content with little. Yes, and you should book a room in advance, so that simply upon arrival in this city you will not be left without housing. You should not look for hotels on modern Internet sites: most hotels and apartments are known only to local residents.

Severomorsk

I want to start a review of Severomorsk hotels with the option of the same name. Perhaps it is the most popular in the city, and all because it offers a relatively good combination of the cost of accommodation and the level of comfort.





The choice of visitors offers 26 non-smoking rooms, a separate place is organized for smoking. The rooms are small, but bright and comfortable, equipped with modern furniture. Of the appliances in them, a TV, a refrigerator, wireless high-speed Internet.

All visitors can count on parking at the hotel, a round-the-clock information desk - specialists will answer all your questions and will definitely populate you even if you arrive at night. By the way, among the additional services there is a transfer from the airport to the hotel - a great opportunity to save on taxi services. A daily stay at the hotel will cost guests an average of 2500 rubles.

Address: Severomorsk, Murmansk highway, 17/5

"Vaenga"

Hotel "Vaenga" in Severomorsk is the oldest in the city. Book a room in it should be exclusively by phone, the Internet has not reached here yet. But perhaps the main advantage lies in the ability to isolate oneself from the modern realities of life. For 1,500 rubles, guests will receive a small room without a trace of repair - the furniture is old, on the walls are wallpapers that have been left over from Soviet times. Here no one has heard about the Internet, you can’t even count on it.





When checking into the room, the staff will definitely warn the guests about the lack of superior rooms, power and satellite TV - the antenna catches only one channel. The bathroom is old: in some places peeling paint, plumbing is old, all in rust. This hotel in Severomorsk is far from ideal, but perhaps this is the only place where you can always stay. If you did not manage to book a room in a hotel of a higher level, go here.

Address: Severomorsk, Lomonosov street, 2

"Flagship"

Mini-hotel "Flagman" occupies the third and fourth floors of the business center "Primorsky". Few people know about the existence of this Severomorsk hotel, you can only book a room here by phone. That is why, as part of our review, we decided to talk about what guests can count on.

Hotel "Flagman" in Severomorsk is a great option for businessmen, business travelers, as it occupies part of the business center, where conferences, business trainings, and meetings are often held. Five comfortable rooms of various categories are what all guests can count on. It offers views of Primorsky Square and the Kola Bay.





In addition to a set of modern furniture, each room has a TV, a fridge, an electric kettle, and a private bathroom with a set of towels and cosmetic products. High-speed wireless internet is available on site. The price includes breakfast. The administration of this Severomorsk hotel pays great attention to the comfort and safety of all guests, equipping the public premises with a 24-hour video surveillance system, fire alarms.

In the hotel restaurant, visitors can have breakfast, lunch, dinner, or order dishes from the menu directly to their room for an additional fee. Reviews about staying at this hotel are positive, guests focus on the comfort and friendliness of the staff. The cost of daily accommodation in a hotel depends on the category of the selected room and averages 3,000 rubles.

Address: Severomorsk, Primorskaya Square, 1

Apartments

Do not have time to book a hotel room in advance? Pay attention to apartments and apartments for daily rent. Their average cost is 1500-2000 rubles. For the money you get a full apartment with a kitchen, a bathroom and a room. Of course, there are some difficulties with obtaining keys, checking in and subsequent eviction, but if you discuss all the conditions in advance, you will be able to settle in an apartment in the most convenient area of ​​the city.

To summarize

Severomorsk is a very small northern town in the Murmansk region. Here, as you can see, there are no luxury hotels, and their number is not impressive. The most famous hotel in Severomorsk (Murmansk highway, 17/5) with the same name. But in order to occupy a room in it, you must book it in advance by calling the contact phone number. Hotel "Vaenga" - the oldest in the city, cannot offer either comfort or the necessary living conditions; it is most often visited if there are no rooms in other hotels. "Flagman" is a mini-hotel, which only a small number of city guests know about, but it offers excellent living conditions, first-class service, and is located not far from the city center.





We hope that our material will be useful to all those who come to Severomorsk on business, work or to stay. Yes, there are very few hotels here, but with the right approach and advance planning of the trip, you will be able to book a comfortable room for yourself.
