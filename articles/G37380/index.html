The oldest Pagan Gods of the Slavs



In the pre-Christian era, the Slavs had their own faith, the formation of which took place in the culture for centuries. Now the pagan gods are again returning from oblivion, awakening a new, very strong interest among the descendants of those peoples. Despite the planting of Christianity, which was alien to the Russians, which for the most part took place by force, fire and sword, the people preserved in folklore, fairy tales and festivals mention of their true gods. And sometimes, without suspecting it, we mention their names, which have become common nouns.

Rod and Svarog

The pagan gods of ancient Russia are, first of all, a family. It is in that understanding how we are accustomed to imagine it. At the head is the ancestor of all the other gods - Rod. He is the creator of all things: light and darkness (White-God and Chernobog) and the universe. Through his companion, a woman in labor, Lada, he gave birth to Svarog, who became the main god of earthlings. It is believed that every person is a descendant of Svarog himself, that is, he can rightfully consider him to be his great-great-great ... grandfather.

How did Rod appear? It was created by the Supreme or the Absolute, that is, the very energy that has always been and will always be, the Force that figures in all world religions without exception .

Svarog completed the work of the ancestor - the creation of our world. Now it consists of three parts located at different levels. The upper, heavenly world of Reign, inhabited by the gods. Middle - Appear - is inhabited by people and is visible to us. On the lower level - Navi - the souls of the dead dwell. From there are obsessions and bad dreams come.

Lada mother

Pagan gods always had companions who bore and bore them children. God Rod was the very first to create Lada - the goddess of love and beauty. She is also called the Beregina, the Holy Virgin, the Virgin Swan. She became the wife of Belobog.

The mother of the Gods of the Great Race is the patroness of the family, children and mothers, orphans and widows. In honor of her, people called the ideal way of life - a way where everything is peaceful and good, and people get along with each other. She also cared for abundance, ripening crops,

The goddess of childbirth is also the daughter of Lada - Lelya. In addition, her children are considered the twelve months of which the year consists.

Belobog (Light-sighted) and Chernobog (Dark-eyed)

Belobog and Chernobog are also the oldest pagan gods. Svetovid - the husband of Lada - rules the world of Reveal. He is the personification of peace, harmony, friendship, sincerity, good luck and good. Patronizes trade and art.

Dark-sighted (aka Morok, Pestilence, Darkness) - chained in armor, with a fierce face, the lord of the world Navi, the eternal enemy of Belobog. Its symbol is the skull. The wise Slavs respected Chernobog along with its opposite, because without darkness there is no light, everything in the world should be in balance.
