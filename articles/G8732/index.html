Lawsonia Hotel Apartments 3 *, Cyprus, Protaras: reviews

If you plan to spend your long-awaited vacation in Protaras, Cyprus, and are looking for a budget accommodation option, be sure to pay attention to the three-star Lawsonia Hotel Apartments.





Description, photo, location

The hotel boasts a very good location. It is just a 10-minute walk from the center of Protaras with lots of bars, restaurants, clubs and other entertainment and shopping facilities. Larnaca Airport is 60 kilometers away. The sea can be reached in 15 minutes in measured steps.

Lawsonia Hotel Apartments was built in 1990. Two years ago, repairs were made here. This is a small hotel consisting of 27 rooms (studios, one bedroom and a bridal suite). All apartments have a TV, telephone, air conditioning, refrigerator, kitchenette, bathroom, hairdryer, balcony. All rooms have sea views.

The hotel has a small pool with a sun terrace. Parking is also free. The hotel reception is open 24 hours a day. Tourists are offered services for organizing excursions, airport transfers, and car rental.





Lawsonia Hotel Apartments (Cyprus): Travel Reviews

As you know, when planning any trip abroad, it is very important to responsibly approach the issue of choosing a hotel where you plan to stay for several days or even weeks. After all, this can greatly affect your impressions of a long-awaited vacation. In order not to make a mistake in choosing, many experienced travelers are advised to read the comments of people who have already lived in the place you are interested in. After all, former guests try to share their impressions and information about the pros and cons of a particular hotel.

Today we offer to find out together what opinion has developed among our compatriots who lived in Lawsonia Hotel Apartment (Protaras) during their vacation in Cyprus. Immediately, it can be noted that almost all guests were not disappointed in their choice. According to them, the hotel is well worth the money spent. Confirmation of this is its rating, compiled on the basis of estimates of tourists. It makes 4.8 points out of 5 maximum possible.





Hotel itself

As already mentioned, almost all the guests were very pleased with this hotel. According to them, it is small but very comfortable. The owners of the hotel managed to create an almost homely atmosphere here, which makes the rest even more enjoyable. The hotel area is quite compact, but there is also a swimming pool, and a sun terrace with sunbeds, where you can spend time escaping from the heat of the day, and parking.

Pleased guests and the location of the hotel. It is located on the main street of Protaras, in a place with well-developed infrastructure. Within walking distance for guests there are several supermarkets and shops, cafes and restaurants, car rental and travel agencies, bus stops and much more.



Housing stock

Despite the fact that Lawsonia Hotel Apartments (Cyprus) is located next to the busy roadway, the hotel rooms are always quite quiet. The fact is that they do not overlook the road, but the sea. Therefore, you will be ensured not only silence, but also a magnificent view. In general, the rooms, according to our compatriots, are quite spacious, comfortable, pleasantly decorated. Here you will not find much luxury, but all the furniture and appliances are of high quality and in good condition. Guests in their reviews note that the rooms have everything you need. So, in addition to basic furniture (beds, cabinets, tables and chairs) and appliances (TV, phone, refrigerator, air conditioning), here you will find a full kitchenette with a full set of dishes (pot, pan, kettle, cups, plates, forks , spoons, etc.). Therefore, you can always save on food by buying products in the market and in the supermarket and preparing food directly in your apartment. Also, tourists liked the spacious balconies with plastic tables and chairs. Here you can spend your time enjoying the view of the sea and the surrounding landscapes. As for the TV, as noted by travelers, you will not find Russian channels here. But you can always take a flash drive with recorded films or programs from home. Some complaints from guests related to the Internet. You can connect to it at the hotel for an additional fee (about 30 euros per week). However, the connection speed, according to our compatriots, leaves much to be desired. Therefore, it is best to get a SIM card of a local mobile operator with a mobile Internet package upon arrival. It will be cheaper and will allow you to always be in touch.

Check in, departure

Many tourists who visited the Lawsonia Hotel Apartments 3 * (Cyprus, Protaras) note that even in case of an early arrival at the hotel, they were given the room keys almost immediately. Of course, this is a big plus, because for sure you will want to immediately unpack your things and go to the sea, and not wait for the checkout time, sitting in the lobby.

As for the day of departure, according to the rules, the occupied apartments should be vacated before 12 noon. However, as former guests note, this hotel very often meets tourists and allows you to stay in your room for a few more hours if you need to leave the airport later.



Staff, service

Most of the guests were satisfied with the work of the staff of the hotel in question. According to them, all the workers here are very welcoming and hospitable. They are always happy to help vacationers. A big plus is the fact that almost everyone here understands the Russian language.

As for the service, the rooms are cleaned every day. Moreover, maids visit apartments during the absence of tenants, so as not to disturb them. Towels are changed every day, and bed linen is updated a couple of times a week.

Beach holiday

This item, according to tourists, will not disappoint guests of Lawsonia Hotel Apartments. So, the hotel is located just 800 meters from the magnificent large sandy beach. Here you can rent sunbeds and umbrellas. There are also cafes and bars on the beach, and water activities are offered. Sunset at sea is gentle and comfortable. So here it will be comfortable for tourists to relax, even with young children.
