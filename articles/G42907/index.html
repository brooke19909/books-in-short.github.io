Water Park Samara: an unforgettable experience for children and adults



Many people would like to extend the summer for a whole calendar year. In Samara, there is such a place - this is the largest indoor water park in Europe, whose name is Victoria. Water Park Samara was built one of the very first like it in Russia. The territory of the complex is almost 7,000 m 2 . Now tourists from all over Russia come here, all these people want to get an unforgettable pleasure that will remain in their memory for a long time. The water park of Samara has gained very great popularity thanks to year-round work, regardless of what time of year is outside the walls of the water entertainment complex: summer is all year round.

This center is part of the worldwide association, which once again proves its very high class. Water park of Samara is a genuine exotic island of pleasure, where the whole year is summer (air temperature is always more than 30 degrees, and water temperature rises to 28 degrees). The territory of the complex resembles a smaller version of resort island states: here you will find waterfalls and mysterious caves, tropical trees and rocks of unprecedented beauty, water and dry

bars, as well as a relaxation area with sun loungers - all this will allow you to travel far from your everyday place of stay. And that's not all: there are also nine pools and eleven slides for every taste on the territory. A great mood will give 30 fascinating attractions. A well-designed system allows you to equally relax and enjoy all visitors - from very young to very adults, so your desire to relax with your whole family will be a great idea. For example, high and winding slides are good for those who want to get an adrenaline rush, small slides are designed for children.

The high level of service will amaze even the most demanding tourists who, after receiving enough strong emotions, will want to relax a bit in a pleasant environment. This will be facilitated by bars where you can try great cocktails. And all this - without leaving the pool! You will not stay hungry either: you will be offered excellent hot dishes and soft drinks. The cost of complex meals is as follows: for breakfast - 50 rubles, for lunch - 80 rubles, for dinner - 70 rubles. A place where time flies by is a water park in Samara. Photos of the most beautiful places are presented in this article. Additional services include first-class parking, many shops.

And this is not all that will surprise you at the water park. Samara offers fairly affordable prices: for children under four years admission is free, for older children the ticket price will be 850 rubles for a whole day, adults will have to pay 1,500 rubles for a day at the water park. During the months of summer vacations in the water park there are special preferential offers for all children of school groups, summer health camps and sanatoriums. What more can be said? Only one thing - come to Samara Victoria water park and get a lot of vivid and unforgettable impressions.
