Greece, about. Crete, Nana Beach 5 *: overview, description and reviews of tourists

If you want your vacation to include swimming in the clear sea, as well as an acquaintance with an interesting culture, go to Crete. Nana Beach 5 * will provide you comfort and quality service.





a brief description of

Crete boasts many scenic spots. Nana Beach 5 * is located just in one of these areas. The territory consists of both multi-storey buildings and small isolated bungalows. The architecture of the buildings is made in a style quite characteristic of the island. On site, as well as in rooms, guests will be provided with all the necessary amenities to ensure a good rest. The last reconstruction is dated 2006.

Location

This hotel is located on the west coast of the famous island of Crete. Nana Beach 5 * is located on the seashore. It is about one and a half kilometers from Stalis and Hersonissos, as well as 5 km from Malia. There is a bus station 100 meters from where you can reach attractions such as:

  • Lasithi Plateau.
  • Knossos palace.
  • The ruins of the Minoan palace.
  • Archaeological Museum in Heraklion.

Hotel accommodation

Travelers should be able to have a good rest, because you need a lot of energy to fully know Crete. Nana Beach 5 * offers its guests 563 rooms, which can be divided into the following categories (with prices based on two adults per week):

  • The standard (1411 euros) is a compact and comfortable room designed to accommodate its adult guests with the possibility of installing an extra bed. Extras include a desk and a wardrobe. The balcony is equipped with garden furniture.
  • The conditions for accommodation in a bungalow (1522 euros) are largely reminiscent of the previous category. The advantage of this option is that the houses are located separately and have their own terrace with a garden.
  • Family apartments (1913 euros) are two rooms with large and one and a half beds. Each bedroom has a private bathroom. In the "adult" room there is access to a balcony.
  • Suite (2771 euros) offers guests comfortable accommodation in an atmosphere of luxury. The bedroom is made in chocolate tones, and the gold elements of the decor will become a bright accent. The living room is large and bright with a panoramic window.

It is important to familiarize yourself with the information about the rooms of the Nana Beach Hotel 4 * hotel in advance. Reviews and prices are one of the most important indicators. The cost is fully consistent with the quality.

Hotel Restaurants

The best source of information about restaurants operating in the territory of Nana Beach Hotel 4 * - reviews of tourists. Both prices and quality of dishes are comparable with the best establishments of the island. So, in this hotel you can visit the following restaurants:

  • Athina Central Restaurant is a huge dining room characterized by luxurious interiors and large seating capacities. A buffet is served here three times a day.
  • Thalassa is a themed fish restaurant located on the seafront. Here you can try seafood prepared in various ways.
  • La Pegola - This is a French restaurant. Here, both traditional dishes for this country, as well as copyrighted masterpieces from chefs are served.
  • Zorbas is a poolside restaurant. It specializes in traditional Greek cuisine, as well as barbecue dishes.
  • Firenze is a luxury establishment serving the most popular Italian dishes. Pizza is especially popular.
  • Poseidon is a seafood restaurant. Its feature can be considered a thematic design in the style of the underwater world.

It is worth noting that visiting all restaurants, except the main one, requires an appointment. In addition, the observance of a dress code is required (it is unacceptable to come to establishments in beach, everyday and too open clothes).





Hotel Bars

If you have chosen for your vacation an option such as Nana Beach 5 * (Greece, Crete), you will not have a shortage of drinks and snacks. Guests are advised to visit the following facilities:

  • Panorama is the main hotel bar. It is located on a panoramic terrace, from which fascinating sea and city landscapes open.
  • Dionissos is one of the favorite establishments both among hotel guests and among local residents. Here you can find a wide range of local wines and special snacks for them.
  • Dolphin is a poolside bar. The stand, located right in the water, will allow you to enjoy your favorite drinks and cocktails, without breaking away from swimming.
  • Poseidon is a snack bar. Here you can find a lot of delicious, satisfying and healthy snacks.
  • Veranda is a rooftop bar located in Thalassa Restaurant. It has a dance floor.
  • Nautilus is a beach bar where guests can enjoy a wide range of refreshing drinks. You can also enjoy grilled dishes here.
  • La Pergola is a popular night club. Here every evening guest DJs perform.
  • Kalipso is an institution focused on children's audiences. Young tourists will be offered sweet carbonated drinks, fruit juices and ice cream here.

Negative comments

But there are also not quite flattering reviews about the Nana Beach 5 * hotel (Crete). Based on them, you can highlight some of the negative aspects of the rest in this hotel. These include the following:

  • even despite the fact that the Internet is paid, it absolutely does not catch;
  • the number of rooms is very dilapidated, with creaking furniture and outdated equipment;
  • the bathroom has a lot of black mold;
  • the playground is very dirty, and many attractions are broken;
  • due to clogged sewage, the drain in the bathroom is very slow;
  • until midnight, music is played throughout;
  • the animation is sluggish and not very interesting;
  • insects are found in the rooms (this problem is especially acute in the bungalow);
  • uncomfortable entry into the sea (there is a high probability of injuring your legs with sharp stones);
  • fruits are served in a restaurant in a cut form (I would like them to be whole);
  • diluted drinks in bars.

General impression

This hotel makes a pretty good impression on travelers. Considering that the main contingent of vacationers is Germans and French, the service is nevertheless tailored for them. Everything is very polite, neat, high-quality, delicate and beautiful. The territory is simply stunning, and the rooms, although not exclusive, but quite decent. But the whole impression can be spoiled by the quality and cost of the Internet. If you want to chat without interruptions on social networks or connect with friends on Skype, it is better to sit in a nearby cafe for a cup of coffee, rather than spend a lot of money on hotel vouchers.
