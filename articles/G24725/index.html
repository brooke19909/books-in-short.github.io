Elk Island

An interesting and exciting adventure for your child will be an excursion to the Elk Island national park. This reserve has become home to two hundred species of birds, fifty species of mammals and thousands of flora. Many who rested here are advised to go with the children on an organized trip to the Elk station, where children can get acquainted with adult moose and their babies.



In addition, children can ride horses, listen to interesting stories about wildlife and take a direct part in environmental activities. This is the variety that the best children's parks in Moscow offer.
