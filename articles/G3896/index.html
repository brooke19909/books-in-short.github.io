Sea tour in the Krasnodar Territory - the village of Blagoveshchenskaya: base, rest, conditions

Holidays on the Black Sea is popular among residents of Russia. The most visited place in the Krasnodar Territory is the village of Blagoveshchenskaya. Base (rest on the peninsula will give an unforgettable experience) - an institution that is in demand among tourists. It is here that you can find housing of different categories and cost. Thanks to this assortment, vacation at sea becomes accessible to almost everyone.

The village has a large number of recreation centers. They belong to different price categories. Let's look at what living conditions and services tourists can expect when they come to a place like the village of Blagoveshchenskaya.

It works only in the summer-autumn season.

Tourists are accommodated in houses (with or without amenities) or in cottages. In the latter there are several rooms. Anyone who is not used to having a rest without all the benefits of civilization can book superior accommodation. A large list of entertainment venues boasts the village of Blagoveshchenskaya. The base, a vacation in which no one calls boring, has its own water park, dance floor, specially equipped area for sports, places for games, a billiard room and table tennis. For vacationers, three meals a day are provided. Those who wish can rent various equipment and inventory.

Carnation

Not far from Anapa (only 30 km) is the village of Blagoveshchenskaya. The recreation center "Carnation" is located directly on the coast.
