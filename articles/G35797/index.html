Grand Hotel Oasis Sunny Beach 4 * (Bulgaria / Sunny Beach): photos and reviews of tourists

How do you feel about Bulgaria? Few modern tourists know that this country was once a popular resort. Gorgeous sandy beaches with a wide coastline, excellent sea and comfortable climate are what attracted tourists from different parts of the world. With such an abundance of sea resorts, which exists today, everyone safely forgot about Bulgaria. But we want to remind you that this is from the budget options, which must be considered.



There are not many resorts in Bulgaria, but all of them are attractive for tourists. Sunny Beach is perhaps the most popular place with a well-developed infrastructure, good service, and a comfortable climate. It is here that beach lovers often come. Recently, a lot of young people have been gathering here, preferring active and eventful leisure. The excursion program here is not the most diverse, but there are a lot of picturesque, really fabulous places.

If we talk about hotels, there are plenty of them. Vacationers will find very modest apartments and chic hotels with a huge landscaped area and excellent service. We offer to evaluate a fairly popular option - Grand Hotel Oasis Sunny Beach 4 *. Reviews of real vacationers will help to form the correct opinion and evaluate the hotel from all sides.

About hotel

So, the Grand Hotel Oasis Sunny Beach 4 * hotel (Bulgaria) is located in the resort center, just 300 meters from the beach. Thus, all infrastructure facilities are located within walking distance from it. This is a fairly large hotel by local standards with 176 rooms of various categories and well-developed infrastructure. It is well suited for youth recreation, vacationers with young children will also like it here.





Population

Check-in is one of the important points to consider when choosing a hotel. The hotel Grand Hotel Oasis Sunny Beach 4 * populate quite quickly, it is noted by almost all tourists. Even if you arrive a little earlier than the designated time period, if there are available rooms, you will receive the room keys within 5-7 minutes and you can begin to relax.

Rooms

The room fund of the Grand Hotel Oasis Apartments 4 * (Sunny Beach) is represented by 176 rooms. For the category of "4 stars" rooms are well equipped. From technical equipment there is air conditioning, TV with satellite channels, telephone. This is quite enough for a comfortable stay. Each room has a private bathroom with shower and a balcony with stunning views of the resort.

The rooms in the Grand Hotel Oasis Sunny Beach 4 * hotel are bright, quite spacious and clean - what you need on vacation. The equipment is working properly, the air conditioner does not make suspicious sounds and noises. Daily maid service is the norm for a hotel of this category. The staff tidies the plumbing, wipes the furniture, the floor, replenishes cosmetic products. There are few tourists who would complain about the dirt in the room and even more so dirty towels with stubborn stains.





Some rooms, in truth, need redecorating. Tourists complain about exfoliating stucco and paint, traces of fungus and mold in the bathroom - all this creates a feeling of discomfort. At the same time, the plumbing is working, no problems and interruptions in the supply of hot water were found.

Nutrition

The hotel offers food: breakfasts, half board and board. Meals can be enjoyed in the hotel restaurant. Of course, you should not expect from Bulgaria an abundance of dishes that you are used to seeing in Turkey and Egypt, but no one will be left hungry here. Breakfast includes eggs, cold cuts, cheese, flavored pastries, fresh vegetables and fruits, and drinks. For lunch and dinner, they always offer meat, fish, seafood - what else can you expect? Everything is tasty and of high quality. The staff is kind, it serves guests quite quickly. Vacationers with children complain about the lack of a children's menu. There is an excellent bar near the pool where bartenders will prepare any cocktail for you. High-quality alcohol, presented in sufficient assortment.

Some tourists still do not recommend overpaying for meals organized according to the all-inclusive system. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel there are enough cafes and restaurants where you can taste the local culinary masterpieces quite inexpensively.

Sports and Entertainment

For an additional fee, all vacationers at the Grand Hotel Oasis Sunny Beach 4 * can visit the fitness center and sauna. This is a great opportunity to tidy the body, relax and rest.

On the territory of the Grand Hotel Oasis Sunny Beach 4 * there is an excellent pool. Of course, most tourists prefer to spend time on the beach, but, for example, in the morning and in the evening it is quite possible to splash around in the pool. They clean it daily, in the morning the water is cool enough, but by lunchtime it warms up to a comfortable temperature.

Fun for kids

Of course, all vacationers with young children care about the availability of conditions for the smallest in the hotel Grand Hotel Oasis Sunny Beach 4 *. Reviews of tourists confirm that the hotel has an excellent playground and sports ground - the kids really like to spend their time here, and their parents can not worry about their safety.

Beach

The sea and the beach are a maximum of 45 minutes walk from the hotel. The beach is sandy, wide enough, equipped with sunbeds and umbrellas, as well as rental points for sports equipment and water activities.





Many tourists note that there is no need to rent sunbeds - it is quite possible to limit yourself to a beach towel or bedspread. If you prefer a beach holiday, you will definitely like Bulgaria. The beach is equipped with rescue towers, the safety of vacationers is fully ensured. This is an ideal place for a family vacation with young children - the depth of sea water is negligible even at a significant distance from the coast. The water is crystal clear, the entry into the sea is gentle and as comfortable as possible.

To summarize

So, if we consider the rooms in the Grand Hotel Oasis Sunny Beach 4 *, standard is a good option for those who decide to spend their holidays in sunny Bulgaria. It has everything you need for a comfortable stay. Again, an excellent location relative to the coast and the infrastructure of the resort is doing its job.

Of course, the hotel is far from those that tourists are used to seeing in Turkey, but this is an affordable option, not without flaws. At the same time, it continues to be attractive to modern tourists. We recommend that you pay your attention to it, take a closer look, because this is the optimal ratio of price and quality of services provided.
