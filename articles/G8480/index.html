Balcony Hotel 3 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang): description, service, reviews

Vietnam is a beautiful country that all fans of unusual nature should visit, there are a considerable number of beautiful places here. One of the most visited cities here is Nha Trang. It has beautiful beaches and a considerable number of architectural objects that attract the eyes of travelers.

In Vietnam, the holiday season lasts from late September to April. It is at this time that the real summer begins here. In Nha Trang in November there is quite arid weather, replacing constant rains and fogs. Also this month, gardens begin to bloom profusely. As for the air temperature, in November it rises sharply to the level of 35-40 degrees and keeps close to it almost until the end of the spring months.

One of the most popular hotels in this region is Balcony Hotel 3 *. Every year a considerable number of tourists who come from Russia have a rest here. Tour operators sell a huge number of permits to this place, since their cost is relatively small, and the quality of the services provided by staff is at their best.

general information

Balcony Hotel 3 * - the place to relax is quite new. Its doors for tourists first opened in 2016. Guests who have visited here note the beauty of the interior that is available in the rooms, and also make in their reviews of the Balcony Hotel 3 * an emphasis on the modern appearance of the buildings from the outside.

The total area on which the hotel is built is small - a little more than 4000 sq.m. It is located not only two large buildings, but also a small garden, which blooms profusely at the peak of the holiday season.

Location

Located Balcony Hotel 3 * in Nha Trang. Many seek to visit this city due to the presence of a large number of beautiful objects and attractions on its territory. Not far from it is the world-famous night market, where vacationers purchase various souvenirs for relatives and friends, as well as original products of Vietnamese production for themselves. Also nearby is a large marine club, nearby you can visit the Tran Phu Park.

The hotel is located on the first coastal line, thanks to which the tourists living in it are provided with walking access to the warm sea.

The nearest airport is at a distance of 45 kilometers, which is quite a lot. That is why in all travel packages that are offered in agencies, the transfer service from the air traffic hub to the territory of the place of rest is mandatory included.

Rooms

Balcony Hotel 3 * offers tourists 88 apartments, which are located in two large modern buildings, the names of which are quite simple: A and B. The entire number of rooms is divided into eight categories, some of which can be combined into one. In particular, this applies to types such as family, deluxe and luxury.

The main highlight of the hotel is that each of its rooms has an individual balcony, from which you can see beautiful views of the sea and mountains, as well as the urban surroundings of the metropolis. As tourists note in their reviews, the view here is simply amazing, they often try to capture it in the photo.

All apartments, regardless of their category, have a private bathroom, well equipped with modern fixtures, as well as all the accessories that are needed to maintain hygiene. Here in the cabinets of their new owners waiting for large terry towels and bathrobes, each guest is also given slippers.

The rooms of the Balcony Hotel 3 * have separate air conditioning, which tourists resting here can use at any time. With their help, you can create the most comfortable conditions that are ideal for any preference. Especially their use is relevant in the hot season.

Nutrition

One of the favorite places for tourists in the Balcony Hotel 3 * is the restaurant, which is located on the ground floor of the main building. Services

The service at the Balcony Hotel 3 * is pretty good, as guests say in their comments left as a result of the rest here. It offers round-the-clock free administration services, representatives of which can be found at the front desk. Room service is also absolutely free of charge at the hotel, an example of which is their daily cleaning and linen change.

For a fee, tourists can rent a conference room, ideal for various events. As a rule, this opportunity is used by guests whose trip is related to doing business.

Attention of tourists is an excellent gym, which you can visit for free, besides it there is a fitness room, which is more to the fair sex.

The infrastructure of the Balcony Hotel 3 * hotel allows tourists to place orders for excursion tours without leaving the hotel. A trip to the sights can be arranged both for a group and individually. To do this, just contact the hotel bureau, which is located on the ground floor of a large building.



Aquazone

All evening and nightly entertainment in the Balcony Hotel 3 * is carried out in the aquazone area, which is located on the roof of one of the hotel buildings. From here, from a bird's eye view, you can observe a beautiful view of the surrounding beauty and enjoy the rays of the sun, comfortably sitting on a sun lounger, some of which are on a large outdoor terrace. In the center of the Balcony's outdoor terrace is a large pool filled with crystal clear water. It has equipment for automatic heating of the filling, as well as its cleaning. On the territory of this place there is also a bar in which vacationers can treat themselves to tasty and unusual cocktails and drinks for every taste.

Prices

Making a description of Balcony Hotel 3 * in their reviews, tourists often talk about the cost of tours to this place. So, on average, a weekly ticket to this hotel costs about 80-90 thousand rubles, which is designed for two, taking into account transportation costs and expenses for renting Superior rooms. Also, in any standard travel package, payment of the airport transfer service is mandatory.

Many tourists, having been here, recommend to their friends to relax in this place, talking about great opportunities for living. It is at this time that you can enjoy water procedures in the open air, as well as bask in the hot sunshine at a time when frost and snow are in Russia.
