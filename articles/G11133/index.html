The best ski resort in Almaty

Thanks to the Asian Winter Games held in Almaty in 2011, most of the sports facilities in the metropolis received a second life. The city hosted the seventh Asian Games, in the framework of which competitions were held in skiing and speed skating, figure skating, biathlon, hockey and ski jumping.

The most dramatic and qualitative changes occurred in the best ski resort of Almaty, Chimbulak. It has acquired a modern look, and its technical equipment today meets the highest European standards. At the same time, the cost of skiing and using the infrastructure of the complex is still pleasantly surprising. The abundance of interesting tracks and accessibility are the main advantages of Chimbulak.





Captured by snowy peaks

The most popular ski resort in Almaty "Chimbulak" is located 25 kilometers from the former capital of the Republic of Kazakhstan. The tracks of the complex are designed for both beginners and professional athletes. Offering ample opportunities for relaxation and fun, it is the main training base of the national team.

The presence of artificial lighting on the tracks "Chimbulaka" suggests the possibility of round-the-clock skiing. The main ski resort of Almaty "Chimbulak" is open regardless of the season. Skiing here begins in the second half of November and ends in May. The rest of the time it serves as a great place for horseback riding, hiking and cycling. It offers excellent conditions for sports tourism, orienteering and mountaineering.

Service and equipment rental

Access to the Chimbulak ski resort in Almaty is provided by season tickets purchased at the box office. There are four-hour, daily and weekly ski passes. Children receive discounts. There is a school of snowboarding and skiing. Perhaps group and individual training.

The local equipment rental has everything you need for a comfortable and safe winter holiday. Rental equipment for a teenager will cost about 3,000 tenge, a set for an adult - 5,000.

Personal belongings can be left in a storage room specially provided for this. The volume of its sections is sufficient to accommodate the luggage of an entire family or group of vacationers. There are small eateries and cafeterias at the base.

Alternative? There is!





Despite the fact that Chimbulak was recognized as the best ski resort in Almaty, city residents warmly respond to another sports center located a few kilometers from the metropolis. This is the ski resort "Akbulak" (Almaty). The spurs of the Soldier Gorge hide it from prying eyes.

In addition to cross-country skiing and downhill skiing, in Akbulak you can freely ride on tubing, for which a separate track is allocated. The vertical drop in these parts is almost a thousand meters. The slopes of the sports base are subject to artificial snowmaking. The tilt angle of the most difficult descent is 55 degrees. Its route partially runs through a pine forest. On weekends, Akbulak hosts mass night skiing.

Starting in November, an equipment rental service has been operating. Equipment from leading brands Salomon, Burton, Rossignol will cost from 5,000 to 10,000 tenge. The cost of a coach starts at five thousand.
