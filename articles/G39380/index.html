Pensions near Moscow with a pool. Places for rest and recreation

Rest in boarding houses near Moscow attracts a large number of tourists, regardless of the time of year. All resorts are characterized by a huge list of services and comfortable rooms. Each of you has a unique opportunity to spend your vacation or mark an important date in a quiet and comfortable place near the capital. For this, pensions near Moscow with a pool are excellent.

Bekasovo

All vacationers are delighted with the relaxing atmosphere that prevails in the pension. Here you can enjoy plenty of forest silence and clean air. You can play billiards (professional tables are available), ride motorbikes and ATVs, have fun in the aquacenter with a swimming pool, jacuzzi and fountains, Finnish steam rooms, hammams. Also at the disposal of vacationers is an excellent sports ground, a rich library, a disco room. Fans of outdoor activities have the opportunity to play paintball. The boarding house SPA center offers the following wellness services: balneotherapy, massage, phytoaromatherapy, thalassotherapy, stone therapy, numerous cosmetic and physiotherapeutic procedures.

Three meals a day, the all-inclusive system works. You can check in either in rooms in hotel buildings or in wooden houses (with a separate kitchen). Cost per day per person - from 4 400 rubles.



Buran

If we consider the boarding houses near Moscow with a swimming pool, then Buran is an excellent option for relaxing with the whole family or in the company of friends. You can improve your health in a salt cave, carbon dioxide baths, SPA capsule, undergo a course of manual sludge mechanical massage, aromatherapy, heat therapy. Qualified specialists will recommend the best program to restore health. The pool with hydromassage and countercurrents is very popular among vacationers.

A ski slope opens every winter. The length of the tracks is three hundred meters. For your stay, you can choose a standard room in a classic building or luxury apartments in a VIP building. A day of rest will cost 3,250 rubles at least.

"Klyazma"

Pensions near Moscow with a swimming pool, located in a forested area, attract those who wish to enjoy the harmony of nature. In the warmer months, there is the opportunity to soak up the sun on a comfortable beach (umbrellas and sunbeds are available for rent). Vacationers actively rent catamarans, jet skis, boats and boats.

In the wellness center of the Klyazma boarding house, you can take an anti-cellulite program, a wellness massage course, visit the pool and infrared sauna. The cost of a daily stay is from six thousand rubles.



"Sunny meadow"

Pensions near Moscow with a pool have a different pricing policy. Solnechnaya Polyana is the most budget-friendly option of all presented. This pension is usually chosen for families with children. There is a swimming pool, playgrounds for sports, as well as paintball and go-kart areas. All vacationers are delighted with the interactive laser shooting range, Finnish saunas and Turkish hammams. Special diet programs, wellness wraps and hydrocolonotherapy are carried out. Accommodation for one person - from 2,400 rubles per day.



Above were described the most popular boarding houses near Moscow. Inexpensive (with a swimming pool - the most popular option) you can relax and improve your health.
