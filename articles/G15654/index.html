The best hotels of Budapest: rating, description and reviews of tourists

Budapest is one of the most beautiful capitals in Europe. A vacation in Hungary is not only a contemplation of architectural monuments and attractions, but also a great opportunity to improve the body and restore strength. Budapest is famous for its thermal springs and hot baths. It is best to choose thermal hotels in Budapest, and holidays in Hungary will leave only positive emotions.

No need to go far in the capital of Hungary to relieve tension and stress, because many hotels in the city have their own thermal pools and baths. This is very convenient for tourists who are frankly too lazy to spend time on the road and do not feel like freezing in the winter on their way to the source. Hotels in the Hungarian capital often offer guests not just relaxation and overnight, but also various services in the field of cosmetology, wellness and spa treatments.

The best thermal hotels in Budapest

The healing waters of the thermal pools contribute to the rapid restoration of physical strength, and the water procedures in thermal baths are one of the important components of the complex of physiotherapeutic procedures.

The list of the best hotels in Budapest with their own thermal pools includes hotels of the famous Danubius chain. This is the most popular hotel chain in Europe, the main feature of which is a wide selection of services in the field of medical and recreational. Thanks to thermal healing springs, spa vacations are very popular in this part of Europe. When choosing a hotel in Budapest, most tourists are guided by the presence of a spa center on its territory and the quality of the services provided there. And their own pools with healing thermal water are an indisputable advantage of hotels in this part of Europe.

Danubius Health Spa Resort Margitsziget 4 *

This is a cozy resort hotel in the center of the Hungarian capital, located on the island of Margit. The hotel has its own large spa, as well as indoor and outdoor pools.

Danubius Health Spa Resort Margitsziget offers its guests a range of services in the field of cosmetology, as well as massage and physiotherapy treatments. In addition, the hotel has everything you need for leisure and entertainment. Chefs at the Platan main restaurant will offer visitors to enjoy gourmet cuisine and a light cocktail on the terrace with stunning views of the park. There is also a caf√©, a lobby bar and a poolside bar on site.

The hotel rooms are equipped with everything necessary for a good holiday. Each is equipped with air conditioning, satellite TV, a work desk, and each guest is provided with a bathrobe. One of the main advantages of the hotel is its unique location. This is a paradise island of peace and tranquility in the midst of city bustle.

Hotel visitors in their reviews highlight the price-quality ratio, as well as note the level of service, hotel infrastructure and the quality of physiotherapy procedures.

Danubius Grand Hotel Margistziget 4 *

Next to the Danubius Health Spa Resort Margitsziget is the luxurious building of its namesake - the Danubius Grand Hotel Margistziget. This is a respectable hotel with a special atmosphere and unique style of the late 19th century.

Guests at the Danubius Grand Hotel Margistziget have access to the spa at a nearby hotel. You can get there through an underground heated passage that connects the hotel with a wellness center. Using the services of the wellness complex is included in the price. For an additional fee, you can use the services of a massage therapist, as well as a range of cosmetic procedures.

Among the advantages of the hotel, guests noted its location, a wide selection of additional services, as well as a pleasant bonus in the form of morning coffee and fresh croissants. Among the minuses, only high prices were noted in restaurants on site.

Danubius Hotel Gellert 4 *

One of the historic hotels in Budapest is another Danubius hotel. This is Gellert, opened in 1918, which is also a historical landmark of Budapest. Its magnificent building is located on the banks of the Danube at the foot of Mount Gellert. This is perhaps one of the most worthy hotels of Budapest in the city center. Business areas are within walking distance.

The Danubius Hotel Gellert is world famous for its Gellert thermal baths. Entrance to the baths is free for guests. The Gellert Hotel stands out for its refined style, and the special atmosphere and design of the rooms leave an unforgettable impression on guests.

The hotel restaurants offer guests a wide selection of international dishes, you can also order quality fine wines from the famous Hungarian cellars. Panorama Restaurant offers beautiful city views.

Danubius Hotel Gellert has several pools, including open-air ones. In summer, an artificial wave pool is available. In the Gellerta baths, you can also use the jacuzzi, sauna, and hot tubs.

Among the advantages of the Danubius Hotel Gellert, visitors noted a convenient location, close proximity to the city center, a good thermal complex. Guests are impressed by the hotel‚Äôs historic building itself, which has retained its original appearance. Among the shortcomings, guests highlighted the old number of rooms.

The Aquincum Budapest 4 *

In the ranking of the best hotels in Budapest there is also a magnificent spa hotel The Aquincum Budapest. The hotel is located in the prestigious Buda district, on the banks of the Danube, opposite the island of Margit, which offers a wonderful view. It is surrounded by old streets, atmospheric bars and restaurants, and also has its own green park. The hotel‚Äôs wellness center offers a range of therapeutic services, thermal pools, hot tubs, a sauna and a steam bath.

The main restaurant, Ambrosia, offers visitors to enjoy authentic Hungarian cuisine. There are also several bars on site where you can taste alcoholic and non-alcoholic cocktails, taste the best Hungarian wines, and also drink a vitamin cocktail after an active workout.

The Aquincum Budapest Hotel has 2 thermal swimming pools. The rooms are elegant and comfortable. The room has high-speed Internet access, satellite TV, individual climate control. Bathrooms are fully equipped with everything you need, a hairdryer and bathrobes are provided for guests.

The Aquincum Budapest Hotel offers pet-friendly accommodation for a fee. This is a great advantage for tourists who travel with their pets.

Cheap and cheerful. Cheap hotels of Budapest

If some tourists travel to Budapest to restore health, physical and moral strength, then other travelers are not afraid to lose their health in the trip and choose a more rich and varied holiday. Consequently, housing is also being looked for more affordable by choosing budget hotels in Budapest.

Those who like to relax in a big way can easily choose a premium hotel without spending a lot of time searching for the best deal on the network. It is enough to send a request to the search engine "Budapest Hotels, Hungary" and the list of first-class hotels is already in sight.

Finding a budget hotel at the best price and at the same time suitable for living is not so simple. But among budget hotels you can find great options.

Passzio Panzio Hotel 3 *

Passzio Panzio Guesthouse is a great option for active curious tourists. The main advantage of the hotel is its location. The hotel is in close proximity to all the attractions of Budapest. The city center can be easily reached both by public and by private transport.

Passzio Panzio is a family-run guest house in a traditional Hungarian style. The hotel has its own parking, which can be used free of charge. The hotel serves a delicious and varied breakfast daily. Free Wi-Fi is also available.

Attila hotel

Attila is a quiet, remote hotel located in a secluded area near the center of Budapest. This hotel is suitable for lovers of a relaxing holiday. To get to the center or nearby attractions, you need to spend at least 15 minutes. The hotel offers breakfast. The rooms have a private bathroom, TV, hairdryer. A refrigerator is provided if necessary.

The surrounding area is ideal for leisurely walks in picturesque places and outdoor activities. Near the hotel you can go fishing.

Full Moon Design Hostel

For lovers of noisy parties, loud music and unbridled fun, Full Moon Design Hostel will open its doors with joy. Full Moon Design Hostel is located in the heart of Budapest. The hotel is located above a music pub where noisy night parties are held. Admission is free for hotel guests. This option is more suitable for young active people who are not disturbed by loud music and noise. This is an ideal place for lovers of live communication and new acquaintances.

Under the roof of Full Moon Design Hostel, a truly international company gathers. There are several supermarkets and restaurants near the hostel. Breakfast at the hotel is available at an additional cost.

Budapest is an amazing resort city that will appeal to every tourist. This is an ideal resort for a very demanding guest, and for an unpretentious traveler, and for an adventurer. The Hungarian capital is pleased to welcome all guests and will gladly open the doors of its luxury hotels and cozy hostels.
