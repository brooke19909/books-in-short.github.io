The best beaches of Vietnam: get acquainted

Vietnam is a country gaining popularity in the field of beach tourism. The coast here is amazingly beautiful. Travel agencies offer many great places to relax. But many before the holidays the question begins to worry, and where in Vietnam are the best beaches. What is the best place to go in this country? Noteworthy places are in the north and in the south, so the choice is quite wide.



Northern beaches of Vietnam

The coast of the northern region is not very popular with guidebook compilers, but it is quite possible to find interesting places for a beach holiday there. The difference between the north and the south and the central region is that locals prefer to relax here. For example, Bai Chai Beach, a northern beach near the capital. If you come to see Vietnam, the white sand beaches interest you as much as the country's cities. So, going to Bai Chai Beach, you can successfully combine a relaxing beach holiday with excursions to the sights of Hanoi. In addition, Halong Bay is noteworthy. The rocky islands surrounding the bay allow you to arrange scenic boat trips, and small beaches are good for sunbathing and swimming a bit, and for watching the amazingly beautiful sunset.



Central beaches of Vietnam

In this part of the country, it is worth visiting China Beach, located between Da Nang and Hoi An. If you go on vacation between May and October, you will certainly enjoy this beach. Pure white sand will delight any tourist, and surf lovers will love the waves. Despite the infamous fame - it was here that the Americans first landed during the war - China Beach is an excellent resort with top-class service. Not far from Hoi An there is another wonderful beach - Lang Ko. This place is included in the thirty best beaches on the planet. The reasons for this appreciation are simple: soft and clean sand, clear sea water, picturesque green mountains and lush tropical vegetation around. It was here that the kings of the Nguyen dynasty built their residences, and this says a lot. Finally, Fukok Island is the place with the best sunsets in Asia. Resorts here are suitable for wallets of all sizes, so this island is prophesied the glory of the new Phuket.





South beaches of Vietnam

It is best to go to the south of the country from December to May. In the summer and autumn months, the rainy season takes place here, associated with constant typhoons and heavy rains. When exploring Vietnam's beaches for a relaxing vacation, try opting for Vung Tau. This is one of the most popular local resorts located near Ho Chi Minh City. No less famous is Nha Trang. This place combines the natural beauty and amenities of a small town located here. In addition, thirty kilometers from here there is a picturesque Dock Let Beach - a secluded beach where you can go in order to relax from the crowd. Here, in Nha Trang, an ideal place for diving. The city has specialized equipment stores. Here it is possible to choose instructors of different qualifications.
