Resort The Byke Old Anchor 3 * (India / Goa): overview, features and interesting facts

India is a relatively inexpensive country for recreation, which offers tourists a whole range of entertainment. Lovers of active pastimes can book excursions to numerous historical and natural monuments. Tourists who prefer a relaxing holiday will enjoy the country's cleanest sandy beaches. South Goa - a state that is just perfect for a beach holiday. There are many budget hotels, one of which is the complex The Byke Resort Goa Old Anchor 3 *. He will be discussed in this article.

Read more about holidays in South Goa

To begin with, it is worth noting that this resort is focused on respectable holidays, so the cost of living here is usually a little higher than in North Goa. At the same time, the beaches here are clean. On the streets, for example, you will definitely not find untidy herds of cows or packs of dogs. Expensive hotels were built everywhere, and tourist infrastructure was created. Most often, elderly travelers from Europe come here, but the Russians have not yet managed to fall in love with the resort. A distinctive feature of South Goa - magnificent white beaches, the purity of which is carefully monitored.

The state is divided into several resort villages. The Byke Old Anchor Resort 3 * hotel is located in the town of Cavelossim, which is focused exclusively on a calm and quiet vacation. Here you will not find cheap bars and noisy night clubs. But the strip of coast along is lined with velvet sand, and the sea was chosen by dolphins, which both adults and children like to watch. Along the beaches there are many restaurants offering fish and seafood dishes. Some establishments may even cook you a dinner of fish that you yourself caught. Other attractions include descent along the Sal River, excursions to the tropics, where you can watch exotic birds and plantations. Often in Cavelossim parties are held with fireworks, performances of magicians and acrobats.

Where is the hotel situated?

As mentioned above, The Byke Old Anchor 3 * hotel is located in Cavelossim. It has a favorable location: on the one hand, its territory is washed by the Arabian Sea, and on the other - the waters of the Sal River. However, the hotel still does not have its own beach. The distance to the public site is 180 m. Only a small road separates it from the hotel. At 500 meters there is a large shopping area where tourists can stock up on food, exotic fruits, clothes and souvenirs.

The nearest international airport is located in the city of Dabolim, which is approximately 35 km away. However, tourists often come to Cavelossim by rail. So, the station is located 11 km from the village. Therefore, as a rule, the road to the hotel does not take tourists a lot of time - they need 1-2 hours to get to their place of rest.

Important hotel information

The Byke Old Anchor 3 * resort complex (Goa, India) was built in 1989. It is a place for a budget holiday, where most often adult couples or youth companies stop. The highlight of the hotel is its main building, built in the shape of a real ship. The remaining buildings are characterized by an unpretentious design - these are small two-story buildings painted in white or beige shades. The hotel area is considered quite large. Most of it consists of a tropical garden with walking paths. There are 9 buildings in the complex. They have 150 rooms, which can accommodate approximately 350 tourists at a time. The last redecoration here was completed in 2015.

Coming here, tourists should pay attention to the rules of settlement. So, registration of guests begins strictly from 12 noon local time. Before entering the room, guests must present a passport and a credit card. In addition, couples planning to stay in the same room together will need to provide the administrator with a marriage certificate, otherwise settlement will be denied. No pets are allowed in the hotel. Eviction after the end of rest starts at 10:00. Please note that the hotel staff does not speak Russian, so tourists who do not speak English should bring a phrasebook with them.

Hotel Rooms

In total, The Byke Old Anchor 3 * hotel in South Goa offers tourists 150 rooms. Each of them is located in a small two-story building and has its own entrance from the street. Most of the rooms are one-room apartments consisting of a bedroom and a bathroom. Additionally, they can be equipped with a balcony or terrace, if the room is located on the ground floor. The standard category is designed for two adults who are accommodated in a double bed. Additionally, one more sleeping place on a sofa or a folding bed can be provided.

For tourists who come to relax with a large family or a friendly company, there are luxurious suites that can accommodate up to four guests. They have two separate bedrooms, as well as a living room and a terrace. The rooms overlook the nearby buildings, road or tropical garden. More about room equipment

Each room of The Byke Old Anchor 3 * hotel in India has not only a standard set of furniture, but also equipment that helps to improve the quality of rest. So, coming here on vacation, tourists can use the following equipment:

What kind of food does the hotel offer tourists?

The Byke Old Anchor (Strictly Vegetarian) 3 * hotel has a unique food system. Coming here, it is worth paying attention to the fact that the complex is completely vegetarian, which means that meat dishes are not served here. Even for a fee. The price usually only includes breakfast, which is served in the main hotel restaurant. It consists of a standard intercontinental set of dishes: cereals, sandwiches, pastries, hot drinks, as well as fresh fruits and vegetables. A buffet is available for guests.

Lunch and dinner are available for a fee. The hotel has a restaurant serving Indian and international cuisine, but you will not find meat here either. In the evenings, tourists are served exclusively by the menu. You can enjoy refreshing drinks or alcoholic cocktails, ice cream and other light snacks at the pool bar or on the beach. There is another bar in the main building, but in its interior it looks more like a cozy cafe where you can not only have a drink, but also have a snack, snacks, desserts or fruits.

Beach and Pool

The Byke Old Anchor 3 * hotel in Goa does not have its own beach area, therefore, when coming here, tourists should be prepared to visit the public coastal area. It is located 180 meters from the complex. To get to it, you need to cross the road. You can relax here for free, but if you want to take a sunbed, you will have to pay for it. There is also a sun terrace by the river, but swimming in its waters is strictly prohibited.

For guests' convenience, the hotel has a spacious outdoor pool filled with fresh water. There is a sun terrace with free sun loungers nearby. However, the water in the pool is not heated, so tourists can swim only in good weather.

Other entertainment options

Despite the fact that the complex The Byke Old Anchor 3 * (Cavelossim) is considered a budget hotel, there are plenty of leisure options that will appeal to any guest. We list the main entertainment available to guests:

  • Ayurvedic center and spa. Provide their services for a fee. Here, tourists can visit not only wellness treatments, but also a jacuzzi, massage room, sauna and gym.
  • Disco-bar, where evening discos for adults are regularly held.
  • A room for board games (such as chess), as well as a mini library. However, the books here are mostly presented in English.
  • Free badminton court.
  • Paid equipment for table tennis, volleyball, billiards, croquet and yoga.
  • A water entertainment center located on the beach next to the hotel.

Children's leisure at the hotel

Tourists with children rarely stay at The Byke Old Anchor 3 * hotel, as it is primarily focused on youth recreation. However, it is allowed to stay with kids of any age. And children under 6 years old can relax in the hotel for free if they sleep in their parents' beds. But there is not much entertainment for children. For them, their own shallow pool is equipped, the rest of the parents will have to organize the leisure of their children themselves.

Positive feedback about The Byke Old Anchor 3 *

This is not to say that this hotel has a good reputation among tourists. Many guests were dissatisfied with the rest in this place, but at the same time they believe that the shortcomings are justified by the low cost of living. In addition, in their reviews they describe many advantages. So, the guests highlighted the following leisure advantages in The Byke Old Anchor 3 *:

  • a large hotel area, which is pleasant to walk in the evenings;
  • Convenient location - near there are shops and the beach;
  • new bedding and linen, including a comfortable hard mattress and pillows;
  • original breakfasts - since the hotel does not serve meat, chefs try to diversify the menu with dishes of fruits and vegetables;
  • free entertainment and discos, however, they are held only in English;
  • An excellent spa in the complex, which offers its services with good discounts.

Negative reviews

Nevertheless, the hotel The Byke Old Anchor 3 * is often criticized. Tourists are not advised to come here, as the following hotel disadvantages spoiled their vacation:

  • the hotel needs major repairs - in the rooms guests found mold and dirt on the walls;
  • neglect of Russian tourists - many say that they are deliberately given bad numbers, and Indians, on the contrary, are settled in rooms with fresh renovations;
  • indifferent staff who do not care about tourists at all;
  • there are a lot of mosquitoes in the rooms, employees almost never clean the rooms normally;
  • air conditioners are very noisy, so sleeping with them is simply impossible;
  • instead of mineral water, employees can bring tap water;
  • there are a lot of insects on the beach and there are sand fleas, the bites of which then itch for a long time.

Is it worth it to relax here?

Thus, The Byke Old Anchor 3 * hotel is a good place for a budget holiday. It has a favorable location, but can not boast of a high level of service. The complex is ideal for youth companies, but for families with small children it is worth choosing a hotel more expensive.
