Playa Park Hotel 3 * (Spain, Costa Dorada): description of rooms, service, reviews

Holidays spent in hot countries leave a lot of good memories. Each person traveling abroad wants to be sure that staying at the resort will be comfortable. For a vacation to go well, you need to consider all the nuances of a future vacation. In this case, disappointments can be avoided.

One of the hospitable countries that welcome tourists from all over the world is Spain. And one of the options for vacation accommodation is the three-star Playa Park Hotel. What can the presented resort complex offer to its guests? This will help to understand the reviews of our compatriots who have been here.

general characteristics

Playa Park Hotel 3 * (Spain, Costa Dorada) is a great option for an unforgettable beach holiday. It is built in a picturesque location on the sea coast. This is a quiet, peaceful area, which has a peaceful rest. Just 100 meters from it is a wonderful beach.



The complex is pointed just 400 meters from the center of Salou, which is the capital of the tourist region of Costa Daurada. Lovers of passive and active recreation will find a lot of entertainment here.

Playa Park 3 * is a separate multi-storey building. There are only 315 rooms of various categories. The popularity of the hotel is increasing every year. This is due to high-quality service and comfortable atmosphere reigning in its walls. Here, those who seek peace and tranquility will like to relax. Noise and fuss are not peculiar to this place. If you wish, you can go to the center. There is plenty of entertainment for every taste.

Orientation

Playa Park Hotel 3 * (Spain, Costa Dorada) organizes a comfortable stay for many categories of travelers. It will appeal to both adults and kids. Also, one of the main profiles of the complex is a service for families with children of different ages. Often youth companies stop here.



Representatives of various nationalities are at the hotel. Therefore, the staff speaks different languages, including Russian. The style of interior design is ordinary. This is the most suitable option for a hotel of the type presented.

Rest in the tourist complex is mainly beach. It will appeal to those vacationers who prefer silence. Noisy companies gather in the city center. There is a lot of entertainment here. The hotel itself is calm. Various programs, the organization of active and passive leisure will appeal to many categories of tourists.

Cost

Playa Park Hotel (Spain) offers its guests rooms of various categories. The cost depends on the type of accommodation you choose. Many of our compatriots turn to various tour operators for help in organizing leisure. In this case, the trip will be more comfortable.



The cost of the trip depends on the features of food, accommodation, season and a set of services. The average tour price is 83-90 thousand rubles. for a double room for 7 nights and 8 days. This includes the cost of flight, accommodation and meals on the type of "All inclusive". Visa processing, tax payment are made separately.

The cost of last-minute tours can be much lower. The minimum duration of the tour is 2 nights and 3 days. Each vacationer can order a ticket for the required number of days. The maximum duration of the tour is 14 nights.

Rooms

A separate consideration is the description of the Playa Park Hotel rooms. Here you can relax in double, triple and quadruple apartments. 221 of 315 rooms have sea views. The cost of living depends on this. Standard rooms can have a view of the courtyard or the sea. There are luxury, spacious apartments. They all have access to the sea. All rooms have a balcony or terrace.



The rooms have all the necessary furniture. Beds can be single or double. For children, special furniture is installed (cribs, high chairs). The rooms are heated during the cold season. Finishing materials, furniture and plumbing new. The accommodation is characterized as comfortable.

The room has satellite TV, telephone, air conditioning. For a fee, you can store valuables in a safe. The bathroom has a shower or bath, washbasin, toilet and hairdryer. Towels are provided. Bed linen is changed 2 times a week. Room cleaning is provided daily.

Nutrition

Food at the Playa Park Hotel is organized according to different systems. The price of the permit may include breakfast only. There is a half board system. But it is most convenient to order food according to the ‚ÄúEverything Included‚Äù type.



The restaurant offers a huge selection of different dishes. A buffet is organized. Breakfast is served from 8 to 10 in the morning. For lunch you need to come from 13:00 to 14:00. Dinner is served from 19:00 to 21:30. The interior is tastefully decorated. It's nice to relax here. Local and international dishes are served. Children can choose a special menu. There are vegetarian dishes, gluten free menus.

There is a cafe-bar on site. It has a spacious terrace. Here you can order refreshments, cocktails. Delicious ice cream and coffee are served. A wide selection of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks.

Services

Paid parking is available on site. There are car rental, taxi services. At Playa Park Hotel, the service is organized at a high level. Check-in at 14:00. You will need to leave before 10:00. The front desk is open around the clock. For luggage there are left-luggage offices, a safe for valuables.



The hotel has elevators. There are equipped rooms for people with disabilities. They are provided with appropriate services. For organizing business meetings, corporate discussions, training, you can rent a conference room. There is also a room for a banquet.

On the territory of Playa Park 3 * you can change the currency, purchase plane tickets, train tickets. You can use photocopy or fax for a fee. There is a tour desk. Prices are much lower than tour operators. A babysitter can be hired for children.

Entertainment

Playa Park Hotel offers a varied stay. You can go to the beach or relax by the outdoor pool. Here you can rent sunbeds and umbrellas for free. There is a solarium. A fitness center is available.

In the evening and in the afternoon animators work. They organize leisure activities for adults and children. A variety of contests, games, performances will allow you to get a lot of positive emotions. There is a play club for children, there is a playground.



You can play mini-golf, billiards, table tennis and volleyball. The necessary equipment is issued free of charge. You can play darts. Bicycles can be rented for a fee. This will allow you to explore the surroundings. Near the hotel there is a golf course.

There is a huge selection of board games, puzzles. Everyone will be able to choose the best occupation for themselves and their family.

Negative reviews

Reviews for Playa Park Hotel are 95% positive. Many vacationers come back here again. However, there are a small number of negative reviews.

They noted insufficient service, careless staff. Animation during the day rarely works. At the reception it is difficult to solve problems. Employees need a consultation with the chief manager. As a result, resolving this situation may drag on for several hours.

Some visitors to the hotel note that the food is not varied, tasteless. Champagne is served every three days. You need to go down the stairs to the beach. This is somewhat tiring.

The furniture in some rooms is old, although most rooms have new beds, wardrobes and plumbing. The staff is not too friendly. The waiters in the restaurant express their dissatisfaction with customers. Although most visitors do not agree with this. There are much more positive reviews.

Positive reviews

Among domestic tourists, the Playa Park Hotel is known as a good, interesting hotel for a relaxing, measured vacation. The staff is friendly, smiling. The staff speak Russian.

The beach is clean, well maintained. The hotel also has many green spaces. Everything is clean. Rooms are cleaned daily. Its quality is very high. There are small lines in the dining room, but the quality of food and drinks is high. A variety of meat and fish dishes, many salads, there is even a vegetarian menu. Delicious cocktails, coffee and ice cream. Nobody goes hungry.

The interior of the rooms is tastefully decorated. There is everything you need. The sea view is especially beautiful from the upper floors. For visitors with children create the necessary conditions. The panorama around the hotel is picturesque. It is conducive to hiking. You can rent any transport to explore nearby attractions. Interesting excursions are organized.

Animation conducts game programs for children, performances for adults. Leisure is varied. For small guests, a large selection of games allows you to have fun and not be bored. But the most successful vacation is for couples, adults and youth companies.

Having considered the features of rest at the Playa Park Hotel, we can say that it is suitable for many categories of tourists. The hotel is calm and comfortable. This contributes to a good rest. Most of the guests are satisfied with the service and come back here again. The hotel is popular among domestic tourists.
