"Nokia Lumia 925": specifications, photos, reviews

Nokia phones are popular and respected in Russia. This is due to their stylish attractive design, high build quality. These devices are also characterized by decent performance, speed, stability in operation. To what extent are these features characteristic of the Nokia Lumia 925 device?





Design and appearance

The case of the device is made of aluminum, the back cover is made of polycarbonate. The dimensions of the device are 70.6x129x8.5 mm, it is one of the thinnest in the Lumia series. It also weighs a little - 139 g. On the front panel there are three touch buttons, next to them is a microphone hole. Above - a voice speaker, an additional camera, motion and lighting sensors, as well as an indicator on which notifications are displayed. There are no functional elements on the left and bottom of the case: they are grouped on the right side of the case. Above all is the volume control key, below is the power button, below it is the camera switch. At the top of the case is an audio jack, a slot for connecting a microUSB cable, as well as a slot for a SIM card. In the back is the main camera with a flash, below is the speaker used in speakerphone mode.

Design and appearance: reviews

What do users say about the design and appearance of the phone? Mobile electronics enthusiasts generally respond positively to the concept proposed by the Finnish brand. According to the owners of the device, the phone case is ergonomic, easy to use, the controls are conveniently located on it. The build quality of the phone is also appreciated by gadget lovers on the positive side.

Display

The next aspect in which we can explore the Lumia 925 is the display. The device is equipped with a screen with a diagonal of 4.5 inches, the resolution is 1280 by 768 pixels. The display is based on AMOLED technology. The density is 331 dpi. The display is protected by durable Gorilla Glass 2, there is an oleophobic coating.

Display: reviews

What are the opinions of users regarding the Lumia 925 display? The characteristics of this component of the device are evaluated by the owners of the device as quite consistent with modern technological trends, especially in terms of the use of AMOLED-matrix. Users note high brightness, screen contrast. Many people like the fact that, if necessary, you can adjust the color settings of the display to individual preferences. Users praise the display for viewing comfort at any viewing angle.

Software

The next significant aspect of "Lumiya 925" is the characteristics of software. The phone is controlled by the Windows Phone operating system in version 8. Of course, in terms of the number of applications and games, this OS is still inferior to iOS and Android. But those that are available at Microsoft's company store work on the phone without any problems. Downloading applications and games, as well as their installation, is carried out quickly (if there is an appropriate Internet channel). The phone has a number of noteworthy applications that are installed by default. Among them - a program for working with a camera, Nokia Smart Cam. With it, you can take pictures, and then process them.





There is a convenient set of navigation programs known under the HERE brand - Maps, Drive and Transit. These three applications can work with the same set of cards, they can also function offline - if the necessary data has already been downloaded to the phone. With the help of these programs, you can look at information on traffic jams, bus traffic, look for the necessary addresses or, for example, get directions.

Many users note the usefulness of the built-in application for buying and playing music tracks. Also, the corresponding songs can be downloaded to your phone. The program has the function of selecting music files for specific styles or years, which are defined by the user.

Soft: reviews

How do fans of mobile electronics software installed on Nokia Lumiya 925 rate it? Phone characteristics in this aspect are evaluated positively in terms of the quality of the software. Of course, many users lack the variety of games and applications that occurs when working with Android and iOS devices. There are, however, mobile electronics enthusiasts who value primarily the speed of a Windows Phone phone. Even with the modest technical characteristics of the device, this OS uses, as a rule, less hardware resources than Android or iOS platforms. As a result, the device works faster, it makes it possible to easily launch games and applications, processing of which phones with similar resources under the control of competing platforms might not be able to handle. However, we can talk about the specifics of hardware separately. What resources does the phone have in this area?





Iron

Having studied the specifics of the software, we will thus study another important aspect of Nokia Lumiya 925 - the technical characteristics in the aspect of hardware. The phone is equipped with a Snapdragon S4 processor, clocked at 1.5 GHz. There is a video processing module Adreno 225. The amount of RAM installed in the device is 1 GB. The internal memory resource is 16 GB. However, there is no way to install additional modules. But the noted 16 GB user who is not too keen on installing "heavy" applications and games, as well as shooting a long video, should be enough.

Sound

The sound components in the phone are of high quality. This determines the excellent speaker volume. Users and professionals positively evaluate the quality of music playback, both using headphones and when listening to tunes through the speaker. It can be noted that the phone is equipped with a built-in equalizer. It also supports Dolby Mobile technology, which significantly improves sound quality. Users note that for comfortable listening to music, the headphones that are included with the device are quite suitable.

Camera

Next in turn is another crucial aspect of the Nokia Lumia 925 phone - camera features. What remarkable facts regarding this hardware component of the device can be noted? First of all, this is a fairly large resolution of the main camera - 8.7 megapixels. The frontal one has a modest indicator - 1.7 megapixels, however, it is impressive against the background of the corresponding components of competing devices. An additional camera can take photos with a resolution of 1280 by 720, or 1280 by 960 pixels, as well as shoot videos with an indicator of 720 pixels. The main camera is equipped with a picture stabilization system. This determines the very high definition of photos. Using the main camera, you can also shoot video, and with a fairly high resolution - 1080 pixels.





Using the button located on the right side of the device, you can launch the built-in application that controls the camera. It is noteworthy that the corresponding control works even if the screen is locked. In shooting mode, the same button can also be used to release the shutter. Thus, the characteristics characteristic of the Nokia Lumia 925 camera look quite competitive. Photos from the device should be quite high quality. Video is similar.

Camera: reviews

But how do users comment on the quality of the camera, how do they relate it to other characteristics characteristic of the Lumia 925 components? Reviews of fans of mobile electronics regarding the functioning of the considered element of the device are enthusiastic. High resolution of the main camera and not at all modest in the additional one, the presence of an optical stabilizer and a good program for working with photos and videos - all this predetermines extremely positive ratings from users. They note a large number of useful functions of the built-in application - such as, for example, choosing the optimal shooting mode. Users rate the quality of photos as high when shooting at any time of the day.

Communications

Let us now consider the technical characteristics of Lumiya 925 in terms of communication resources. The phone supports the main modern communication standards, including 4G, GPS, Glonass, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth in the 3rd version. There is support for contactless payment technology - NFC. All relevant modules, as noted by users and experts in the reviews, work perfectly. Navigation is carried out without much difficulty. As some experts in the field of mobile electronics note, the model under consideration is positioned by the brand as adapted just for those people who have a frequent need for using satellite navigation resources.
