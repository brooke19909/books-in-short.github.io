Akteon Holiday Village 4 * (Cyprus / Paphos / Coral Bay): photos and reviews

One of the elite resorts in Cyprus is the village of Paphos. Here you can not only spend a weekend in the company of friends, but also realize a romantic vacation. The abundance of entertainment, historical attractions and a wide selection of hotels give everyone the opportunity to find what their soul desires. This place attracts travelers from all over the Earth due to its rich history and clear waters of the Mediterranean Sea. The Mediterranean climate with hot summers and warm winters allows tourists to come here all year round.

The hotel complex Akteon Tourist Holiday Village has four stars, which, according to experienced, is quite justified. It offers its guests a warm welcome, fabulous apartments and European level service. Living here will give a lot of positive emotions and unforgettable experiences.

Location

The hotel is located directly off the sea in the village of Chloraka. You can get to the center of Paphos within 10 minutes. The nearest local airport is 16 km away and the way to Larnaca Airport is 145 km. According to vacationers, despite the tiring flights and transfers, such a difficult road is fully justified. Within walking distance are a golf course, bird park, tombs of kings. Nearby squares and walking areas. The lively promenade will allow you to enjoy the starry sky and a noisy party every evening.

general characteristics

Hotel Akteon Holiday Village (Cyprus, Paphos) was erected and opened its doors to accommodate the first travelers in 2002. Global renovation takes place in 2007, however, despite this, the rooms are redecorated annually, which ensures their constant freshness and novelty.

The spacious territory includes a delightful green square and a complex of two-story snow-white buildings, which are located on a rocky shore 10 meters above sea level. Fragrant flowers, lush palm trees, well-groomed paved alleys with seating areas allow you to enjoy the fresh air in a comfortable setting.

General Provisions

Hotel Akteon Holiday Village 4 * (Cyprus, Paphos) does not accommodate pets. No smoking throughout. Payments can be made by credit card. There are rooms designed for people with disabilities. The staff is attentive and polite, speaks English and Greek, greets guests with a smile and positive. It is possible to install an extra bed. Check-in time is standard - 14:00, leaving the chambers should be before noon. Late check-out is possible for an additional fee.

Number of rooms

Incredible luxury apartments await each guest of the Akteon Holiday Village 4 * hotel complex (Cyprus, Paphos). The total number of rooms offered for settlement is 232 units, which are designed for a one-time stay of a maximum of four people. A distinctive feature is the design of each room, which is surprisingly modern and comfortable. The rest of each guest will be filled with homely warmth and a sense of calm thanks to the numerous and relevant elements of the decor.

The floors are covered with laminate. On the walls are sconces and a mirror. The room is filled with light thanks to panoramic windows that overlook the garden or the sea horizons. The color scheme is dominated by all shades of white, which give the chambers of lightness and airiness.

There is access to a balcony or terrace with plastic furniture. Here you can meet the dawn or spend a sunset in a romantic setting.

Tidy maids clean up daily. Linen is changed three times a week. You can order the service "wake-up call", which will wake up at the scheduled time for those who are enveloped in a good sleep. Room Service is available 24 hours a day.

Arrangement

The comfort of the rooms is largely due to their equipment. Comfortable beds allow you to sleep and replenish energy reserves. In addition, the furniture set includes bedside tables, a table, chairs, a wardrobe and a luggage rack. A nice addition is a soft sofa and a kitchen area. It is equipped with everything necessary for cooking and eating. Modern dishes, the latest technology and utensils will recreate the most sophisticated culinary masterpieces.

The presence of an individual air conditioner makes it possible to create a comfortable temperature. You can solve the questions that have arisen with the reception staff using your phone. There is a TV with music channels and a safe, tea set, electric kettle and minibar.

The design of the bathroom surprises vacationers with its modernity and chic. There is a shower, a large mirror, a sink and a hairdryer. Daily personal care products are updated: soap, shampoo, shower gel. A set of towels will delight guests with their softness, and disposable slippers and snow-white bathrobes will make you feel at home.

Supply system

The Akteon Holiday Village 4 * hotel (Paphos) has a restaurant. Here vacationers can have breakfast, lunch and dinner. A buffet is served daily, with a variety of products that delight spa guests. Delicious hot and cold snacks, slices, meat and fish dishes, desserts and fruits can satisfy the hunger even of the most capricious gourmet.

The interior of the institution is made in the national style. On the summer terrace you can enjoy delicious masterpieces and beautiful views of the Mediterranean. Quench your thirst is available in the bar, the consumption of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks is included in the All Inclusive system.

In addition, you can get enough of the meal of your own authorship thanks to the presence of individual kitchen in the apartment. A snack bar is open for visitors, where you can have a light snack and enjoy healthy cocktails or hot drinks. The service is top notch.

Beach

One of the main goals of a visit to a hot country is to regularly visit the beach area. According to the guests, it is not difficult to reach it, since the hotel complex is located on the first coastline. Pure clear waters, endless blue distances and a light sea breeze - this will immerse tourists in the world of relaxation and enjoyment. Sun lovers have sun loungers with soft mattresses. Some of them are located in the shade thanks to umbrellas. There are also showers and changing cabins.

The entrance to the water is gentle, partly rocky, which is unsafe for bathing babies who like to splash around. On the shore, a rescue service is on duty.

In order to saturate the time spent here with vivid emotions, you can visit one of the proposed attractions. The most popular, according to experienced, parasailing and windsurfing. Water scooters are in no less demand. However, thanks to the amazing underwater world, diving has become widespread. Both a beginner and an experienced diver can sink to the bottom of the Mediterranean Sea, get acquainted with the delightful flora and no less impressive fauna. For fans of sports, a volleyball field is equipped.

Aquazone

You can spend time in the swimming pool, which occupies the lion's share of the total area. It has a gradual increase in depth and is filled with fresh water, heated by the sun. It is surrounded by comfortable sun loungers with mattresses and umbrellas. Vacationers, according to their reviews, are delighted with daily water aerobics classes and the opportunity to play water polo. Professional instructors through easy exercises charge travelers with positive and joy.

There is a covered aquazone for the winter and rare weather.

Children's leisure

A calm rest for parents is guaranteed, provided that their baby is happy and smiling. The Akteon Holiday Village complex is positioning itself as a great place for a family vacation. As experienced, this is justified. There is a mini-club where the kids spend their free time swimming in the pool. Their leisure is organized by a professional teacher and a cheerful team of animators. Fun games, sports, outdoor games and much more delight kids.

In the courtyard there is a plastic complex. It includes slides, carousels and swings. Here you can frolic and run, spending the entire supply of energy.

There is an opportunity to use the services of a nanny. Individual supervision of the baby allows parents to relax and enjoy the rest. A baby cot is available on request. The restaurants have high chairs.

Entertainment

The rich leisure and the opportunity to spend time in a variety of ways is what attracts tourists to these parts. For its guests the hotel complex offers sports grounds for playing volleyball, basketball and tennis courts. Here you can play billiards and table tennis, check your accuracy with archery and darts.

The presence of an equipped gym allows you to maintain excellent body shape or lose a couple of extra pounds. There are fitness equipment, treadmills, dumbbells and talented trainers who are ready to help at any moment.

A massage room and wellness treatments at the spa will immerse guests in the world of relaxation and peace.

Live music and animation give daily vacationers a wonderful evening, full of beautiful show program and fun contests.

Additional services

Among the many additional services provided by the Akteon Holiday Village 4 * hotel (Cyprus, Paphos), the most popular transfer to and from the airport. An experienced driver will deliver customers to their destination at the appointed time without delay. For those arriving with their car or renting it at the resort, free parking spaces are provided that do not require prior booking.

The reception is open 24 hours a day. Next to it is a currency exchange and tour desk, whose employees will help determine the choice of an interesting trip to get acquainted with local attractions. Dry cleaning, ironing and laundry require an additional charge. A doctor's call and a first-aid kit are available upon request.

The conference room at the Akteon Holiday Village (Paphos) is equipped with everything you need for meetings, presentations and conferences.

Professional photographers are happy to capture the wonderful moments of vacation guests. Souvenir shops and shops, a beauty salon and a hairdresser are integral components of the hotel complex infrastructure.

Summing up all the above, we can conclude that this hotel complex receives mostly positive reviews. Former hotel guests claim that if they have the opportunity to visit this beautiful city again, they will definitely consider Akteon Holiday Village as an accommodation option.
