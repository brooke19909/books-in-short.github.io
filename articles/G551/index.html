Hotels of Kamennomostsky (Adygea): overview, addresses, reviews

For lovers of relaxation in the mountains, the ideal place is the village of Kamennomostsky, which is located near Maykop, in the foothills of the Caucasus Range and surrounded by truly fabulous nature. The choice for living is huge. Kamennomostsky Hotels will offer you conditions of different levels of comfort, depending on your capabilities and desires.

Hotel "Barskaya Manor"

At Barskaya Manor, guests will find comfortable rooms made from environmental materials. Chopped buildings are distinguished by a special microclimate that allows them to keep cool in the summer and warm in the winter. Meals are organized in the spacious dining room with large wooden tables. The hotel has a picnic area, a sun terrace, an outdoor pool. Those who wish can visit the sauna. Hotel staff organize walking excursions for you, bicycle rental is available in summer, and ski lovers come here in winter. Parking and Wi-Fi are free. For accommodation, guests are offered the following types of rooms:

  • double with two single beds or one double - 2200 rubles per day;
  • Deluxe Double Studio - 3000 rubles per day;
  • Luxury - 3800 rubles per day.

All room rates include breakfast. All rooms are air-conditioned and have a TV. The hotel allows pets at no extra charge. The hotel "Barskaya Usadba" is located in Kamennomostsky at the address: Karl Marx St., 7A.
