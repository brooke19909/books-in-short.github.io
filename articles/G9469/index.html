Trap shooting

Only in the "Black Stones" is a special stand, which is a simulator for connoisseurs of hunting. Only here you can work out the shooting technique and acquire the skills of a real hunter.

Sporting is called target shooting. The target fully imitates a running animal or bird in flight. There is also a booth imitating wild boar hunting. You can simply shoot at targets with a length of 200 m. Sporting is not only a preparation for hunting, but also a good physical activity. After all, here it is necessary to overcome obstacles, and this requires good endurance, self-confidence and good psychological preparation.

Fishing

Strength, dexterity, ingenuity - all this trains paintball. This is an imitation of military operations at a special training ground. Adrenaline and positive emotions are provided to everyone.
