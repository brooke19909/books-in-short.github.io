Dolphinarium in Naberezhnye Chelny - a sea of ​​pleasure and positive

Dolphins are very friendly and cheerful animals. Watching them is a pleasure for both children and adults. In 2006, the first and only dolphinarium was opened in the Republic of Tatarstan. In Naberezhnye Chelny perform artists who have moved from Anapa. Among them are dolphins, sea lions and fur seals. Each of the artists is an individual with his own character, disposition, with his own moods and preferences. But all of them are certainly very talented. They do not just perform tricks invented by trainers. They themselves suggest ideas for new issues. That is, each performance that the dolphinarium in Naberezhnye Chelny demonstrates is the result of the joint work of man and animal. In addition, marine artists also know how to draw. And they do it right during the presentation. And after that, such pictures are acquired at the auction by the audience. For example, the work of the dolphin Savely was once sold for 10 thousand rubles.



In addition, the dolphinarium in Naberezhnye Chelny boasts its own "kindergarten." In addition to adult artists, there are also very young ones who are still too early to go out, or rather, swim out onto the stage. Some dolphins and seals arrived with their mothers or trainers from Anapa, and some were born here.

The Anapa Dolphinarium branch is located at the base of the Dolphin pool in the village. Hydroelectric power station. Now it is refitted and filled with sea water. Here you can not only watch a fascinating performance, but also get to know the marine inhabitants closer, learn about their habits and habits. So, the instructors even advise the audience not to disperse immediately after the end of the main action, but to linger and look at the animals in their ordinary life.



And dolphin therapy courses are also held at the dolphinarium, swimming is practiced with these marine inhabitants. But, of course, with this, a mandatory requirement is to comply with all the recommendations of the instructor. After all, a dolphin is a friendly animal, but still a wild animal that may well bite if it doesn’t like something.

You can also take pictures with marine artists, hold a birthday or wedding photo shoot, and get a video presentation. For the holidays, the dolphinarium in Naberezhnye Chelny gives its viewers gifts in the form of new bright and exciting programs. So, for the New Year, an evening show entitled "The Poseidon Stories" was prepared with acrobats, dancers and, of course, with marine inhabitants. Here he is - a dolphinarium (Naberezhnye Chelny).

Schedule, prices

The dolphinarium hosts both daytime and evening performances. In addition to the main performances, additional ones are occasionally staged. In addition, it is possible for groups to conduct shows on an individual schedule.

As for prices, they vary in the range of 250 - 1000 rubles, depending on the time of day and the age of the audience. There are benefits for daily performances for participants in the Second World War, the Chechen and Afghan wars, for disabled people, orphans from orphanages and boarding schools, and participants in the elimination of ChA.

As already mentioned, this is the only dolphinarium in the Volga region, and therefore the residents of this region should spend time and money and get to know better the friendly artists. After all, this will provide a sea of ​​pleasure and positive emotions. Moreover, many travel companies offer a tour to the dolphinarium (Naberezhnye Chelny).
