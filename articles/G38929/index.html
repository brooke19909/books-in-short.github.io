Apartment prices

Various apartments are provided by the project. Sreda, a residential complex, offers approximately 295 thousand square meters of space for apartments. The complex offers from studios to three-room apartments, ranging in size from 24 to 96 square meters. On the upper floors of the apartment with large balconies and panoramic windows. All apartments are rented out by the developer, which, in turn, allows future owners to significantly save money on repairs and move into apartments that are already fully ready for life.

As for the cost of apartments of the Sreda residential complex (new building), the price per square meter of one-room apartments starts from about 145 thousand rubles. In this case, the most expensive will be the studio, where the cost reaches 200 thousand rubles per meter. Thus, the average one-room apartment will cost about a little more than 5 million rubles.

LCD "Sreda" offers two-room prices a little lower, and they start at 139 thousand rubles per square meter. But three-room apartments are sold at the one-room level at an average cost of about 145 thousand rubles per square meter.
