What is the difference between Android and smartphone? We understand the terms!

Ordinary users choose their phone based on what is displayed on the storefront. Often, the study of a smartphone is limited to viewing the interface and a music player - customers do not have great knowledge about the technical characteristics of the purchased product. Let's increase the level of our own literacy and learn more about how Android differs from a smartphone and about the incorrectness of this question.

Great variety



A smartphone is a cross between a mobile phone and a personal computer. Each of these devices has an operating system (OS). It could be:

  • Open webOS;
  • Windows Phone
  • Android
  • Apple iOS.

The type of operating system is a determining factor when considering how Android differs from a smartphone. To understand how incorrectly the question is posed, consider the types of operating systems. They affect the functions that a smartphone is capable of supporting.

Open webOS

This operating system is the very first one used on smartphones. Now she is found less and less.

Windows Phone (WP)



This MOC was released in 2010 under the leadership of Microsoft. The system also works on the basis of the multitouch function. Its distinguishing feature is Hubs. These are sections uniting the general subject (games, contacts, the Internet and others). Static icons look like "live" tiles. They reflect the status information for each section.

Apple iOS

This type of mobile OS can only be installed on technical products from Apple. The screen and device interface work on the basis of the "multi-touch" function (simultaneous work with 1-3 points of contact). Applications can be installed on iOS in IPA format.

Android



Let us return to the question of how Android differs from a smartphone. Android and IOS systems are the most popular around the world. The first version of this OS was launched in 2008. Subsequently, the developers only improved Android products. Now this system is based on many products of digital technology (game consoles, watches, tablets and smartphones). Android smartphones have the following advantages:

  • have open source software;
  • support multitasking and multi-user mode;
  • widely available and relatively cheap compared to Apple products;
  • Surprise with a bright and convenient interface;
  • Support Wi-Fi, file transfer via Internet, USB, Bluetooth.

The main disadvantage of mobile devices running on the Android OS is the uneconomical battery.

Android or Open webOS

Having dealt with the question of how Android differs from a smartphone, we find out which OS is better: Android or Open webOS? Of course, give preference to the first option, because Google is one of the first companies in the global market. Its products meet absolutely all modern requirements for mobile devices. The cheapest Android smartphone is the Alcatel One Touch Pixi 4007D. Its price is only 1990 rubles.

Instead of a conclusion

It is generally accepted that Apple products are better and more prestigious. However, recently there has been a tendency to increase users of the Android OS.
