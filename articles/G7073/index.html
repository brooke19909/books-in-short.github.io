Hotels on Tverskaya: list, top rated, addresses, rooms, additional services and visitor reviews

Tverskaya is one of the largest streets in the center of Moscow. It extends from the Kremlin to Triumfalnaya Square where it flows into 1st Tverskaya-Yamskaya Street. Once this place was expensive to Tver. And today it is a beautiful, modern and well-maintained street. If you are looking for accommodation in the heart of the capital, hotels on Tverskaya are a good option.

Ritz-Carlton Hotel

A few steps from the Kremlin is the Ritz-Carlton, a luxury hotel on Tverskaya. The institution is located at Tverskaya street, 3. The hotel is surrounded on all sides by historical attractions, and the interior decoration is a clear example of classical architecture of the 19th century. The following benefits are available to guests:

  • Accommodation in one of 334 luxurious rooms (average cost - 40,000 rubles per day).

  • Roof lounge bar with panoramic views of Red Square.

  • Separate club floor with special privileges.

  • 1100 sq. Ft. Conference space m for business events.

  • Personal butler service and concierge service.

  • Facilities for disabled guests.

  • Restaurant author Russian, Italian and French cuisines.

  • SPA center with heated pool, steam room and jacuzzi.

  • Own beauty shop.

  • Laundry, ironing and dry cleaning services.

  • Convenient underground parking.

Reviews

About this hotel on Tverskaya, guests leave such positive reviews:

  • excellent location in the heart of the capital;
  • sumptuous and varied breakfasts;

Hotel "Metropol"

One of the best hotels on Tverskaya is Metropol, the history of which dates back to 1905. The hotel is located at Teatralny Proezd 2. This is a unique combination of historical interior and modern technical equipment. Hotel guests have the following leisure opportunities:

  • Accommodation in rooms with a luxurious historical or modern functional interior (average price - 16,500 per day).
  • Gourmet breakfasts, which include the best dishes of Russian and European cuisines. The meal goes to the sounds of the fountain and harp music.
  • The unique Metropol banquet hall, in which Lenin once delivered his speeches and sang the famous Chaliapin and played the piano by Michael Jackson. The room is decorated in a palace style with a stained-glass roof, massive lamps and marble fountains.
  • Chaliapin Bar is famous not only for its first-class service, but also for its unique interior. This place has repeatedly turned into a pavilion for the filming of historical films.
  • Restaurant SAVVA is named after the founder of the Metropol, Savva Mamontov - patron of the arts and a major industrialist. The institution is located on the ground floor and works under the guidance of the famous chef Andrei Shmakov.
  • Banquet and conference rooms of different capacities and with different design.
  • Organization and holding of weddings, including catering.
