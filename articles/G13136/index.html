Review of mobile phone Nokia 5200

Nokia, a large company, has been supplying modern and functional mobile phones to store shelves for a long time. The lineup includes classic models, sliders, clamshells, smartphones. It is worth paying attention that the manufacturer developed shockproof and waterproof options, as well as musical ones. The Nokia 5200 phone belongs to the latter. The model is positioned as a youth device.

Most often, only top-end expensive devices are equipped with a high-quality player. However, Nokia made an exception by introducing a budget model on the market. Keeping the price low was due to the rejection of some elements. In the model with the index 5200, a low-resolution display is installed, the camera is only 0.3 MP, there are no additional player buttons.

Does this equipment suit the buyer? It is impossible to answer unequivocally, since this moment is purely individual. Given that the cost of the device is much lower than $ 100, then this content can be sacrificed in favor of the quality of the reproduced sound. But the musical model should be, first of all, loud.



Equipment

What comes with the Nokia 5200? The device is packed in a standard box. Inside it there are two tiers. On the top is the phone itself and the battery. Under it, the charger, stereo headset (headphones), miniUSB cable are neatly folded. Also included are instructions, a warranty card and a software disc (installed on a PC).
