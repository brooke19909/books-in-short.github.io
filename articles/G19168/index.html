Prague, Olympic Tristar 3 * - hotel overview, features, services and reviews

Hotel Olympic Tristar in Prague is a city tourist hotel, designed for those who came for a few days to visit the Czech capital. Therefore, guests immediately warn - do not count on the level of comfort of the "five stars", like somewhere else in Turkey.

Its main bonus is its convenient location. But the level of service of the European "three rubles" is observed here. And over time, hotel service has only improved. This is evidenced even by the fact that the rating of the hotel among tourists has increased, especially over the past two years.

Reviews of the Olympik Tristar 3 * hotel (Prague) gave us the opportunity to compose an objective picture for writing this essay.

How to get there

From Prague International Airport to Olympic Tristar 3 * - about 20 kilometers. From the railway station - no more than 5 km.

Tourists arrive in Prague in different ways - both by plane and by train. Fly is the most popular way. Moreover, more than 30 airlines fly daily from Moscow to Prague.

It takes too long to travel by rail from Russia, but from Ukraine it is quite possible. Many book transfers from the train station or airport at the hotel reception. The list of services provides such a paid service.

There are bus tours in Europe. They depart from the west of Russia, from Ukraine, as well as from Belarus. They are taken to the hotel itself.

And those travelers who come on scheduled routes arrive at Florenc. This is a bus station in the center. From there, the hotel can be reached by public transport - metro or tram.

Where is the Olympic Tristar Hotel located in Prague

The traveler reviews are unambiguous - the hotel is in a very convenient location. Next to it is the Invalidovna metro station. Only 10 minutes drive and you are already in the historical center of Prague. And this is taking into account the transitions and transfers, after which you exit at the second stop.

The station is called Florenz. This is the old city. The metro runs until midnight, so you can walk as much as you like. And if you are completely late, then Prague trams work around the clock.

Routes 3, 24 and 8 go to the old city and back. No need to change trains. There are 4 stops from the hotel to the main attractions. In addition, beautiful streets with fabulous beer restaurants lead into the center of the hotel, accessed by road. So the walk will be so enjoyable that you generally will not notice the distance.

Right next to the hotel is the Albert supermarket, where you can buy ready-made food for dinner, as well as purchase transport tickets. It works until 9 pm.

Rooms

Hotel Olympic Tristar (Prague) - large. It provides tourists with 267 rooms with all amenities. Instead of the usual keys for opening doors, electronic cards are used.

The rooms have the usual set of furniture and everything necessary for tourists. Clean well. Plumbing is being cleaned. Vacuumed very carefully.

Soap and cosmetics are reported to the bathroom every day. In the bathroom there is a shower, a mirror, a gel, two towels per person. Plumbing and hairdryer workers.

There is a safe, as well as a paid minibar, but it can be used as a cooler or refrigerator. The air conditioner operates in two modes. Plasma TV. LCD screen. Satellite television with one Russian channel.

The linen is snow-white, the beds are comfortable, the pillows and blankets are very comfortable. Tourists note that in every room there is a sofa. The view on the upper floors opens onto Vitkova Gora.

The rooms have two sockets, and the third - in the bathroom. There are a lot of hangers in the closet. Smoking is not allowed in the rooms. The main building has smaller rooms, while the glass annexes are huge and modern.
