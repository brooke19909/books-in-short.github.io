Guest House by the Sea (Gelendzhik)

The rooms have comfortable furniture, air conditioning, TV. The kitchen is equipped with a stove, kettle and fridge. Residents of the hotel can also eat in the cafe with homemade Russian cuisine.



Guest House at the Sea (Gelendzhik) can offer its guests a sauna, laundry, luggage storage, and parking. There is Wi-Fi. All rooms are decorated in a classic style.

Guest reviews

All hotel guests note good staff. But the reviews regarding the rooms are a bit contradictory. Someone is very satisfied, but someone did not like the furniture and the insufficiently equipped kitchens. The guest house has a small parking lot, because during the season vacationers have to park their cars at the nearest paid car. However, all the small flaws are brightened up by the favorable location and proximity of the sea.

Golden sand

Looking at the guest houses in Gelendzhik near the sea, it is worth recalling the "Golden Sand". The hotel has a good location. To the sandy beach only three to four minutes walk to the pebble - five, the same to the promenade. The center can be reached in a couple of minutes, and the water park - five minutes. The guest house itself is a three-story building five minutes from the new embankment. It has views of the mountains and the sea. The place is quiet, loud music does not bother guests. "Rest by the sea"

Guest house "Rest by the sea" (Gelendzhik) is located very close to the beach (pebble) of the Cape Tolstoy.
