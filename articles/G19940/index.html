Hotel Falcon Resorts 2 * (India, Goa): photos and reviews of tourists

India is one of the most mysterious countries. With its pristine nature and unusual culture, millions of tourists from all over the world dream of becoming acquainted with it. Hotel Falcon Resort offers its guests a budget comfortable stay in the picturesque Calangute.

Hotel Description

Hotel Falcon Resort is a cozy resort hotel located in the city of Calangute, which is located in North Goa. Guests love this establishment for an affordable price, as well as for the high level of professionalism of friendly and welcoming staff.

Despite the fact that in general the technical equipment and furniture in the hotel are in a rather worn condition, a cozy atmosphere is preserved here. The rooms are equipped with everything necessary for a complete and comfortable stay of guests.

Employees of the hotel complex are trying to create all the necessary conditions for tourists to feel at home. The reception is open 24 hours a day. There is also the opportunity to use the services of a tour desk, luggage storage and a modern laundry room.

When staying at the Falcon Resort, be sure to visit the restaurant, working on its territory. Here you can fully experience the originality of traditional Indian cuisine. You can also taste many Chinese and European dishes.





Hotel location

Tourists traveling on vacation to the Falcon Resort Hotel, it is important to familiarize themselves with the features of the location of this hotel complex. So, the remoteness of the institution from the main passenger transport hubs can be described as follows:

  • from Dabolim Airport - 40 km;
  • from Mapus bus station - 10 km;
  • from Tivim railway station - 15 km.

Baga Beach and Aguada Fort are approximately 3 km from the hotel. But the famous Calangute Beach from the hotel will have to walk about 500 meters.
If you want to feel the atmosphere of the city, then you can visit the large Newton supermarket, the Natvara restaurant and the popular pizzeria after only 1 km.

Hotel Rooms

Hotel Falcon Resorts 1 * (India, North Goa, Calangute) is quite small, and its number of rooms is only 30 rooms, located in two-story buildings. The apartments are divided into several categories:

  • Conventional room - a small one-room room decorated in a traditional Indian style.
  • The Deluxe Suite is perhaps the best apartment in the hotel, equipped with a double bed and a large TV.
  • The Superior Room - two-room apartments, which can accommodate a large family or a group of friends.
  • Honeymoon Suites is a small, standard room with a double bed. Its distinguishing feature is a romantic design with a large number of ruffles and pink elements.
  • Budget - the cheapest category of rooms, more reminiscent in design of a military barracks. In a small room there is only a bed and a hanging cupboard.




The overall impression of all rooms may be depressing at first. The reason for this is old worn furniture and a lurid rural interior. Nevertheless, it is in such places that you can feel the atmosphere of real India.

In the rooms

In the apartments that Falcon Resort Calangute 2 * provides its guests with the following features and services:

  • air temperature control system in the form of an air conditioner or fan;
  • orthopedic mattress;

  • each room has a toilet and a bathroom with shower;

Going on vacation to the Falcon Resorts 2 * hotel, it is important to familiarize yourself with some rules of accommodation that are established by the management of this hotel complex:





  • Check-in to hotel rooms starts strictly from noon (early registration will be charged a separate fee of $ 20).
  • On the day of departure, all guests must leave their rooms no later than at 11 o‚Äôclock in the afternoon.
  • The hotel has strict passport control, carried out for the safety of tourists. So, citizens of India must be presented with an identity card (required with a photograph) during registration, and foreign tourists will need a passport and visa.
  • During check-in, each tourist leaves a security deposit (funds may be blocked on a bank plastic card). If during the rest you did not cause material damage to the hotel, this amount will be returned to you.
