Guest House "At Nikiticha", Loo: review and reviews

Loo is just a wonderful southern resort village, which is becoming increasingly popular among domestic vacationers. Just as in Lazarevsky, the tourist infrastructure in this village is developed just fine. Vacationers have the opportunity to visit inexpensive catering establishments, buy wine and barbecue, choose entertainment to their taste and budget.





Of course, there are many inexpensive and at the same time good hotels in this village. Quite popular among vacationers is, for example, the guest house "At Nikitich." Loo - the village is modern and clean. Choosing this hotel for your stay, you can really relax here really well. The rooms in this hotel are quite comfortable, and many additional services are provided to guests.

Guest house location features

There is a guest house "At Nikiticha" (Loo), photos of which are presented on the page, not far from the widely known boarding house "Mountain Air". The coastline is only 300 meters away. Sochi city center is 12 km from the hotel. You can get from the village to the main resort of the country by minibus. Also, many tourists travel to Sochi by train. The station in the village has its own. In addition to the trains, all trains from Russia stopping here, coming to Sochi or Adler.

How to get to the hotel "Nikitich"

Quite a long distance to the station (one and a half kilometers), according to tourists, is some drawback of the Nikitich hotel. Guest house (Loo - a small village) to find, however, will not be difficult, since it was built on the main street. Of course, to overcome the distance from the station to the hotel on foot with suitcases is not the best idea. You can get to the hotel by any minibus following the main street Loo. You need to exit at the stop "Mountain air". Also, many tourists book a transfer at the hotel. This, of course, is the most convenient way. But you will have to pay for delivery to the hotel separately. Of course, you need to arrange a transfer in advance.





Good reviews in the network about this hotel are also available because in the immediate vicinity there is the main attraction of the village - the Aqua-Loo complex. You can visit this water amusement park with the whole family.

Modern Guesthouse U Nikiticha (Loo): Overview

This hotel belongs to the class of two-star. It represents a modern, pleasant architecture four-story building. The hotel area is fenced with a concrete fence painted the same color as the facades of the building itself. The owner of the hotel really is a man whom everyone calls Nikitich. Reviews about the hotel owner on the network are mixed. Some consider him a sincere, kind and sympathetic person, others - not particularly polite. In any case, according to most vacationers, the approach to each guest at the owner of the hotel, like the staff, is individual.

Smoking in most rooms at this hotel is, of course, prohibited. But pets at the request of residents, the owner of the institution can allow to keep in the room. In some cases, guests may be given discounts. You can book rooms at the Nikitich Hotel by phone or via e-mail. Prepayment is 10%. However, in some cases, you can do without it.





Guest House "At Nikitich" (Loo): features of the number of rooms

The rooms in this hotel, according to most guests, are equipped in full accordance with the category of the hotel. That is, if desired, guests have the opportunity to use a TV and refrigerator. The hotel also has air conditioning.

According to many tourists, the guest house "At Nikiticha" (Loo) offers tourists very good rooms. Of the positive aspects, vacationers note primarily the cleanliness of rooms and bedding. This hotel, like most others on the Black Sea coast, is cleaned upon request. Linen at the hotel is changed once every five days. Television in the rooms is satellite.

Guests of this hotel do not say too well about bathrooms and showers. The former are often clogged, and secondly, the water does not drain well. A total of 42 standard rooms are rented at the hotel. The rooms are designed to accommodate one to three guests (plus one extra bed if necessary). Each room includes a balcony.





Dining at the hotel

Having your own dining room is also what I received good reviews from guests at the U Nikitich Guest House (Loo). Description of rooms and infrastructure of this hotel was given by us above. As for the food in the hotel, tourists generally have only positive reviews about this.

Rooms at the Nikiticha Hotel are rented with breakfast, board or half board. Three meals a day in a hotel costs about 600 rubles per day per person. The food in the hotel dining room, judging by the reviews of vacationers, is not bad. The choice of dishes here is quite diverse. However, many tourists consider the portions themselves too small.

In addition to the dining room, the hotel also has a small bar. Here you can order a variety of drinks. The guests are served by a friendly elderly woman.





Hotel Infrastructure

In addition to quite comfortable rooms, the guest house "At Nikiticha" (Loo) offers its guests a large number of various additional amenities. So, for example, on-site guests can use free Wi-Fi. For swimming in the courtyard of the hotel provides a large pool. The water in it is quite clean and warm. Therefore, swimming in the hotel pool, according to many guests, is comfortable.

For children, the Nikitich Hotel has a small playground. Adults, if desired, can sit and relax in the gazebo. Other facilities include karaoke and table tennis. You can iron the linen in the hotel in a separate room specially equipped for this purpose. The hotel also has its own small tour desk.

Opinion of tourists about the service

In general, the guests of Loo respond well about the hotel infrastructure. The only thing, some complaints from tourists are parking. The hotel is under constant video surveillance. However, free but unguarded parking is located, unfortunately, not in the courtyard of the hotel, but away from it. Also, tourists do not leave very good reviews about this hotel due to the lack of an elevator in it.
