Health resorts of Minsk region: review of the best

If you have already managed to visit many Russian resorts and boarding houses, it is time to turn your eyes to the countries of the near abroad. One of such states that border Russia and have a stable political situation is the Republic of Belarus.

The best resorts in Belarus are in the Minsk region. Consider the two leaders in the provision of health-improving services.

Minsk region, sanatorium "Youth": treatment

The sanatorium ‚ÄúYouth‚Äù of the Office of the President of the Republic of Belarus is located only 25 km from the capital, in the village of Ratomka, Minsk region. Nearby is the "Minsk Sea". The departmental affiliation of the sanatorium guarantees the excellent quality of the medical and health services provided and the high qualification of the medical staff.





Here, patients with diseases of the cardiovascular, musculoskeletal, nervous systems, and female genital organs are rehabilitated. Before starting treatment, qualified specialists prescribe a general clinical examination, ultrasound examination of organs, electrocardiography, echoencephalography.

To improve the health of vacationers, a variety of treatment methods are used:

  • physiotherapy;
  • mud therapy;
  • hydrotherapy;
  • hirudotherapy;
  • kinesitherapy;
  • heat therapy;
  • phytotherapy;
  • reflexology;
  • ozone therapy;
  • halotherapy.

Additionally, cosmetic procedures and treatment at the dentist are carried out. As you can see, not every metropolitan clinic or sanatorium can boast of such a list of medical and recreational services provided. Therefore, among the health resorts of Belarus in the Minsk region, ‚ÄúYouth‚Äù is deservedly considered one of the best and most prestigious.

Infrastructure and leisure in the sanatorium "Youth"

Fans of spa treatments will be delighted with the spa center, which is the pride of the resort. Here, Turkish hammam, infrared and Finnish saunas, a jacuzzi, massage room, spa capsule, Indian steam bath are available for vacationers. After visiting the spa, there is a feeling of a complete renewal of the body.

The resort has its own landscaped beach, located on the island of Love, a gym.





Active holidaymakers will splash out their energy while cycling. Those who prefer an unhurried vacation can use the library fund and the billiard room. The tour desk located in the sanatorium organizes fascinating tours with an overview of the sights of Minsk.

Not forgotten and children's leisure. Children, by the way, are taken to the sanatorium starting from the age of 3. For them, classes are organized with the teacher in the playroom, fun games on the playground, swimming in the children's pool.

For guests provided laundry services, luggage storage, parking. On the territory of the sanatorium there are additional food outlets in the form of a restaurant, cafe, bar. There is an ATM, currency exchange, pharmacy kiosk, shops, an Internet cafe, a barbecue area.

Overview of the sanatorium "Sosny", Minsk region

Sanatorium Sosny is located in Myadelsky district, 100 meters from Lake Naroch and 400 meters from Lake Beloe. Around, in accordance with the name of the resort, there are dense pine forests.





This medical institution specializes in the treatment of diseases of the circulatory system, nervous, respiratory (with the exception of bronchial asthma), the genitourinary system, the gastrointestinal tract and the musculoskeletal system.

In the treatment of patients, such wellness methods are used as:

  • speleotherapy;
  • reflexology;
  • phytotherapy;
  • cryotherapy;
  • hydrobalneotherapy;
  • physiotherapy;
  • mud therapy;
  • massage.

Gynecological procedures are also performed here, dental services are provided, and mineral water and inhalations are prescribed.

Before leaving for treatment in the Minsk region, the Sosny sanatorium, check the availability of a spa card with an updated diagnosis and referral for treatment, and for children under the age of 18, supporting documents are required that they were not in contact with infectious patients.





In addition to visiting therapeutic events, vacationers can go to the solarium, sauna, tidy up their appearance at the hairdresser, sunbathe outdoors - on the beach of the sanatorium.

Leisure program in the sanatorium "Pines"

You can have fun in the sanatorium, and the benefits will be not only for health. To do this, incendiary discos, soulful concerts, dance evenings that older travelers like to attend, as well as exciting sports holidays, are organized here. Fans of the cue waiting for a game of Russian billiards.

The sanatorium has a cinema hall with 200 seats, in which you can see the cinema works of Belarusian directors and the latest in world cinema. Reading enthusiasts will find interesting books in the library, as well as periodicals at the newsstand.

Excursion programs will help you learn about the history and see the masterpieces of Belarusian architecture. To this end, trips are organized to Minsk and other cities of the country, including Polotsk, Pastavy, Glubokoe.

Health resorts of Belarus - high quality in everything

The Republic of Belarus is a country whose medicine is based on the same principles as in Russia. Here much attention is paid to the health of the nation. After a terrible environmental catastrophe occurred in 1986 - the accident at the Chernobyl nuclear power plant, the number of pathologies and mortality among the population increased.

Over the past 30 years, many sanatoriums and dispensaries have been opened in Belarus, in which the latest developments in the field of medicine are applied. Services of health resorts in Belarus (Minsk region is leading in this direction) are available not only to residents of the republic, but also to vacationers from other countries of the world.

Come to the sanatorium of the Minsk region! Here you will find not only the high quality of treatment, the wonderful climate of Polesie and the developed infrastructure, but also affordable prices for services!
