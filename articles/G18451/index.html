How to check the balance of the Sberbank card

Any Sberbank card holder periodically faces the need to clarify the balance of funds on it. This is necessary in order to control your expenses, timely identify technical failures (if any), and also pay the loan on time and in full (if the credit card).

Checking the balance of the Sberbank card is not difficult - any client, even the one who holds a credit card for the first time, can handle this. Mastering the basics of using a payment card, it is advisable to familiarize yourself with all the methods of performing the same operations, then it will be easier to choose the best option for yourself.

True, for this, in addition to the card itself, you will need a computer with an Internet connection, as well as a username and password to enter your account (a similar service is available to all bank customers who have opened at least 1 account).
