Phone features

In this part, we will talk about the "Phone Book", "Messages" and "Contacts". For convenience, their shortcuts are located at the bottom of the main desktop. Multimedia features

For some reason, the Samsung Galaxy A3 smartphone got a 1900 mAh battery. Knowing this, at first you expect that he will soon be discharged. In fact, he showed himself to be no worse than devices with a more capacious battery. The fact is that due to the increased technical process (28 nm), the processor power is less. Hence the increased operating time without recharging. In general, with an average load of 4 hours of talk, 2 hours of listening to music and 3 hours of Internet, it‚Äôs enough for about 18 hours of work.

In addition, there is an energy-saving function, when activated, gray colors are turned on, due to which the screen practically does not consume energy. Maximum mode reduces energy consumption by 80%. Thus, in standby mode, the smartphone can last 2-3 days.

Total

What about the Samsung Galaxy A3 SM A300F? The review showed that you should not expect miracles from this device.
