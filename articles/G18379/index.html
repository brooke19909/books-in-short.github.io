"Auto payment" MTS: description and connection of the service

MTS Auto Payment is a convenient opportunity to always stay in touch. Only not all subscribers know how to use it. In addition, it is not always clear how to configure payments. Now we have to learn about all this. In addition, we will learn how to connect and disconnect MTS Auto Payment. To do all this is not as difficult as it might seem at first glance. It is only necessary to know the exact algorithm of actions. Otherwise, the result of working with the service may surprise.



Description

At MTS, the "Auto Payment" service is a great opportunity to always stay with a positive balance. With it, you can forget about the lack of funds. It is enough to make a little configuration and connect options to save yourself from problems.

By the way, "Auto Payment" works with bank cards. In other words, all payments to the SIM-card will occur through transfer from the card. For most subscribers, this approach to solving problems with the account is quite suitable. And so many people want to connect this option. But how to do that? What do you need to connect the "Auto Payment" MTS?

Card connection

By the way, this process can be divided into several parts. Let's start with the most common method. We are talking about cases when the Sberbank auto payment is connected for MTS. What does it mean? Using a Sberbank card, you will automatically replenish the balance of a particular phone number. A very popular scenario.

How to carry out this action? First you need to go through authorization on the Sberbank Online website. After that, select "Payments and Transfers" - "Mobile Communications". Next, pay attention to the list of mobile operators - near most of the images there is an additional signature "Auto payment available." In order not to suffer for a long time with the search for the appropriate team, click on this inscription opposite MTS.



We get into the payment settings. Here you will have to dial the phone number of the subscriber (not necessarily yours), as well as the amount of transfer and settings for charges. For example, you can transfer specific funds on a monthly basis. Confirm your actions. After a while, you will receive a notification about the successful completion of the connection of the service called "MTS Auto Payment" (Sberbank). There is nothing complicated about it.

Through the site

Another option that will suit many users is to use the official MTS page and find the service we need there. It is very convenient to carry out the idea using the Internet.

It is enough to visit the site autopay.mts.ru and follow all the points there. It all starts with filling in the number for which MTS will be paid automatically. Next, select the replenishment frequency. There are only two options, but filling in the appropriate fields is required. Without this, you cannot continue to work with the service.

You can replenish your account according to a schedule or balance. In the first case, you will have to fill in a lot of lines devoted to a specific accrual algorithm: when, how much, how often. But the second one is to indicate, upon reaching how many funds in the mobile account you need to activate the "Auto Payment" (MTS) service. Do not forget that you must write the amount of enrollment. There is nothing difficult about this.

Next, the binding to your bank card is carried out. We fill in the details and confirm all our actions. As soon as you are done, you will receive a notification on the telephone about successful activation of the service.



Advantage

The main advantage of our today's option is that it does not charge a monthly fee when replenishing your account. In truth, it is precisely this factor that forces many to connect themselves to auto payment from MTS.

In addition, this feature is free by default. And you do not have to pay anything for the service. It turns out that our today's offer is a great way to recharge a mobile phone account with a credit card. There is no need to puzzle over the withdrawal of money from the card, there is no need to travel around the city in search of payment terminals, as well as ATMs. The balance will be replenished automatically as soon as money runs out on the room.

Opinion about the service

Now we know how to disable MTS Auto Payment and connect it if necessary. What do subscribers think about this service? Honestly, this opportunity collects mixed opinions. Especially the option of charging funds when reaching a certain figure on the account. After all, it becomes more difficult to control funds on a bank card. In the same way as your communication costs.



Therefore, whether or not to connect MTS Auto Payment or not is up to you to decide for yourself. In any case, you can simply refuse this service at any time. Now we know what Auto Payment is. Subscribers are advised not to immediately refuse this service, but to test it in action for several months.
