How to make a valentine with your own hands: ideas and recommendations

Not so long ago in our country came to the tradition of celebrating the feast of St. Valentine's Day or, as it is otherwise called, Valentine's Day. On this day, it is customary to give loved ones not only gifts, but also original cards, small valentines. Usually these are simple little hearts that are in any stationery store. But after all, on such a day, you want something special, non-standard, to invest a piece of the soul as a gift to your loved one.

How to make a valentine with your own hands in different ways will be discussed later in the article, but first we recall what kind of tradition it is, where it came from and who Valentine is.

A bit of history

The holiday of all lovers, or Valentine's Day, is associated with a very beautiful legend, which tells about the cruelty of the Roman emperor Claudius II, who forbade lovers to marry, wanted men to be free from family, children and served in the emperor's troops. The modest and quiet priest Valentine, under cover of night, performed secret rituals, joining the bonds of marriage of lovers. The authorities learned about the actions of the clergyman and imprisoned him, where he fell in love with the guard's daughter. Before his death, he wrote her a note in which he talked about his feelings. The signature on the letter is Your Valentine.

Even before his execution, his miraculous abilities appeared. Going to execution, Valentine handed a farewell letter to his family through a blind girl, who, after touching a note, received her sight. The event took place on February 14, 269. Since then, many centuries have passed before the tradition of celebration came into the life of people. Only in the 14th century they introduced a holiday in England and gradually began to celebrate in many countries of the world.

On Valentine's Day, it is customary to give valentines to lovers. These are such small gifts, usually in the shape of a heart. The article presents the ideas of valentines, consider some interesting options for congratulating girls and boys, married and couples with children. We will tell you in detail how to make them yourself at home.

Craft from felt

How to make a valentine's DIY felt felt? Very easy. In stores of sewing accessories you need to buy one sheet of red felt. Still need kapron threads, a needle and a filler. You can use cotton wool or synthetic winterizer. On cardboard with a pencil you need to draw a heart template. So that its side details are symmetrical, you can draw only half and cut with scissors, folding the paper in half.

Then the template is transferred to the fabric and cut out along the contours. Do-it-yourself valentine‚Äôs hearts sew together, but not completely. A small incision is left to insert the filler inward. After the synthetic winterizer is beautifully straightened inside the craft, you can complete the seam to the end. The knot is tied and hidden with a needle between the layers of fabric. Such a valentine can be given to a girl on Valentine's Day by a guy in love. This gift, although small, is very hidden and warm to the touch. The girl will be able to carry it in her pocket, constantly remembering her friend. It is possible to present such a heart not by itself, but with a ring located on top of the fabric. After all, any girl in love wants to hear a marriage proposal on such a day.

Love tree

If the couple is married or lives together in a civil marriage, then for the holiday you can decorate a room or bedroom with twigs placed in a vase on a shelf or on a table. Paper valentines are attached to each twig . These are voluminous crafts that are hung on a tree on strings, like Christmas tree decorations. You can create them of any color, combining with the main shade of the holiday - red.

How to make valentine hearts with your own hands? For each craft, you will need to cut several identical hearts according to the template, they should be small in size. Then each part is folded in half and glue is applied to the half with a brush.

Then the parts are fastened together with the side plastered parts. In the middle of the top, they make a hole with an awl and thread a thread tied to a knot. When a sufficient number of volumetric valentines is made, you can start decorating tree branches. Hearts are hung evenly on all branches.

It is necessary to draw the contours of a large heart on a wooden surface. Then, on a line at a close distance from one another, small nails are hammered. Next, we will tell you how to knit a valentine-heart from threads. Here you need to act in a chaotic manner. First, the thread is tied to any of the pins. Then begins the movement of the threads from one nail to another, the opposite, pulling it through the middle. When the winding is dense enough, the end of the thread is tied to the nearest nail.

Paper heart

To make such a valentine out of paper, you need to cut out two rectangles of white and pink. Then, in the middle of each, several cuts must be made. To do this, you first need to round the edges of the parts and fold the shapes in half. Then you need to draw several lines at an equal distance from the fold inward. But they must not be drawn to the end.

When the slices are completed, they begin to stack the rectangles between each other, alternating the stripes in a checkerboard pattern. Weaving begins as follows:

  • Hold the pink part in your left hand and the white in your right.
  • The first strip of the white rectangle is inserted into the first loop of the pink.
  • Bind it through the second loop of the pink piece.
  • So continue to the end of the row.
  • The next strip is passed through the other way round; the pink edge is inserted into the white loop.

When all the strips are stitched together in a checkerboard pattern, then you get such a valentine's card for Valentine's Day, as in the photo above.

Valentine card to husband

A loving wife for the holiday of all lovers can make a very sweet and gentle gift. But this will only work if the couple has a common child. After all, a gift is an imprint of small palms on a salt dough. Such a medallion can be kept as a keepsake for many years, remembering your tender feelings for the child and spouse.

It is not difficult to make such a gift, it is enough to knead the salt dough according to the following recipe:

Everything is thoroughly mixed and the finished dough is rolled out into a 2 cm thick sheet. First, be sure to check that there are no wounds and scratches on the baby's palms, as salt can cause a burning sensation.
