Watermelon Photoshoot: Shooting Ideas

A photo session with watermelons can be quite original, bright and colorful, you just need to decide on the style, and also choose the main direction of shooting. Where is the best shot?

The venue for a photo shoot with watermelons can be absolutely anything, it all depends on personal preferences, but nature is best suited. Bright and rich peel of the berry blends very well with the green background of nature. The presence of a reservoir will add a certain zest and color. Moreover, you can beat the water surface.



The choice of location for a photo shoot with watermelons largely depends on weather factors. The most popular place is considered a studio. Among the main advantages of this choice are the following:

  • nothing will interfere and distract;
  • they will not be able to spoil the shooting adverse weather conditions;
  • there are all the required accessories and attributes;
  • the room is warm, so you can change the images.

The studio always has everything you need to shoot. In addition, it is possible to set high-quality lighting, due to which the photos will be clear and bright.

A great idea would be a family photo shoot with a watermelon, as everyone will be able to contribute their own certain immediacy. These family shots are truly moving, warm and radiant.

What to wear for a photo shoot

To conduct a beautiful and unique photo shoot with watermelons, you need to choose the right outfits and accessories. If you want to repeat the watermelon motifs, you can wear pink-red clothes with green accessories. You can use clothes in neutral colors, it all depends on your own preferences. If the choice is quite complicated, then you can take several images at once.



When choosing clothes, keep a balance, because if the background and color of the dress are green, then everything will merge. Clothing is considered the best option that will allow you to separate yourself from the background.

Photo Shooting Ideas

The implementation of a photo shoot with watermelons is a flight of creativity and imagination. You can use whole watermelons, cut various figures out of them. In addition, if desired, you can cook canapes of berries and fruits, colored fresh.

If at the time of shooting the season is not watermelon, then you can reproduce the red-green shades in the decorations and serving. The original idea will be garlands made of felt.



The ideas of a photo shoot with a watermelon can be quite original, so there is an opportunity to realize all your desires and display emotions. Pictures with watermelon can look original, stylish, fun, funny and even very sexy. Much depends on the chosen method of conducting a photo shoot and style of clothing.

With a watermelon, you can take some interesting portrait photographs. A juicy slice can cover the lower part of the face, as this will allow you to focus on the eyes. You can look original and very bright with a ripe watermelon in your hands.

Children's photo shoot

Children's photo shoot with watermelon does not require additional decorations. Attention must be focused on the child. It will be enough to just put a few whole watermelons as a background and cut one. A slice of watermelon should be given to the baby. And while the child will be busy, enjoying a delicious juicy berry, you can take a lot of interesting, successful photographs.



You can put your child next to or even in a cart with watermelons. Such a photograph will look very beautiful and unusual. If the child is already old enough, then you can give him the opportunity to pose independently.

Pregnant Photoshoots

Photoshoot of a pregnant woman with a watermelon can be quite interesting and unusual, the most important thing is to creatively approach this issue. This is one of the very first ideas that comes to the mind of the photographer and the most pregnant. Its symbolism is quite obvious, since the watermelon is round and very much resembles a tummy. Often, it is accompanied by an original body art, for which a special master is additionally invited.



Very often, a pregnant husband is invited to a photo session. There are certain poses and plots for a couple with a watermelon, in which a family expecting a baby looks very advantageous. The courageous future dad interestingly sets off a romantic and tender mother. If the family already has children, then the photographer will pick up a rather interesting and original story, where the whole family will be present.

You can place a whole watermelon next to the tummy, take a picture with a husband who feeds a watermelon, cut a neat heart out of the pulp and attach it to the tummy. The photographer can only display the tummy, in which case it will turn out very gently and originally.

Original watermelon wedding photo shoot

You can make a wedding photo shoot with a watermelon, which will be very interesting and original. Watermelon theme can be continued, organize a real watermelon wedding. The bride will look luxurious in a white dress with a pink-green bouquet, and the groom with a stylish green scarf in his suit pocket will look very beautiful and interesting against the backdrop of the scenery.

If the wedding is in August-September, then you will not have to look for the details for the photo shoot for a long time, since you can use the watermelon directly. You can feed each other with a ripe berry, which will additionally act as a symbol of sweet life.
