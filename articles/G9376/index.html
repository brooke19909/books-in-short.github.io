Selge Hotel 3 * (Side, Turkey): description, service, reviews

Side is a popular Turkish resort on the Mediterranean coast. His closest neighbors are Alanya and Antalya. The city has excellent conditions for beach and sightseeing holidays. Local beaches have a wide shallow, yellow sand and smooth entry into the water.

Star of the seas





Since the city was founded by the Greeks, everything in it reminds of a bygone era. In gastronomic traditions, the influence of Greek cooks is especially strong. Local taverns serve fish and seafood dishes. Young wine is served, and to it - cheese slices and salads from fresh vegetables.

The lion's share of eateries is family business. They are cozy and bright. In the evenings, live music is played. From the street terraces a bewitching view of the endless expanse of the sea opens. The arbors covered by a vine are buried in flowers.

The service in Side is leisurely. The waiters are friendly and courteous. Life at the resort flows slowly and measuredly.

First impression





Selge Hotel 3 * - city hotel. It is located in the center, occupying the third line. Next to it are bars and restaurants, nightclubs and discos, shopping and entertainment complexes. The nearest international airport is in Antalya. It takes direct flights from Rostov-on-Don, Novosibirsk, Moscow, Omsk, Sochi.

The trip from the stop at the passenger terminal to the entrance to the Selge Hotel 3 * takes about an hour. Taxi drivers charge 4,000 rubles for her. Thrifty travelers ride a minibus. Standard rate - 400 rubles.

The hotel was built in 2001. Residents have fifty comfortable rooms equipped with modern appliances and furniture. They are located on three floors. The rise on them is carried out by a spacious ventilated elevator. Occupied by Selge Hotel 3 * area - 2,000 square meters.

It features a private beach. It is a ten minute walk from the residential building. There are deck chairs and umbrellas on the shore. Hotel customers are provided with free of charge. Rooms and common areas have free Wi-Fi.

Water attractions





The stretch of beach that is assigned to Selge Hotel 3 * is strewn with bright yellow sand. It is wide and its length exceeds 150 meters. Even in the midst of August there is enough free space on it. All will fit! A free shuttle bus runs from the hotel to the coast. Towels are required to be paid a deposit.

The hotel has swimming pools for children and adults. They represent a single aquacomplex. Installed steep slides that work on schedule. In the morning and in the evening long lines line up for them. There are a lot of people who want to slide into the water. Many not only children, but also adults.

The safety of vacationers is monitored by duty instructors. Around the pool there are sun loungers, tables and umbrellas. For towels, a deposit is required. The bar is open. It pours soft drinks, tea and coffee. Light snacks are served. The bistro stops working after 18:00. As soon as it gets dark, the lights and soft lights turn on.

Services





In the lobby of Selge Hotel 3 * (Side) an ATM operates. It serves Visa and MasterCard cards, issues cash. Reception staff for a small tip can help arrange boat, motorcycle, bicycle and car rental. Recommended rental equipment for water sports. They take part in the organization of sightseeing and multi-day excursions.

There is a ticket order to the cinema, dolphinarium and nightclubs. Currency exchange is provided. In Selge Hotel 3 * (Side) there is a laundry. It is allowed to leave luggage for several hours free of charge in the left-luggage office. This option is used by those travelers whose departure date is later than 11:00. This is the recommended time before which you need to free the room. Registration of new customers begins at 14:00 and ends at midnight.

Accommodation Options





Selge Hotel 3 * (Turkey) welcomes tourists of all ages. The hotel is focused on the reception of families with children, couples, seniors. In the evenings, it is quiet and calm. The staff speaks Russian, English and German. Communication problems do not arise. Guests are accommodated in standard rooms. Most of them. Family apartments are also provided.

All rooms have a stable Internet connection. TV set. It is connected to cable TV. You can find Russian federal channels. Balconies offer city or courtyard views. From the windows of Selge Hotel 3 * the beach is not visible. The bathroom has toiletries and toiletries, towels, a large mirror, a wash basin and a shower. Residents use a hairdryer, air conditioning, iron and ironing board.

Additional services that are not included in the price of the hotel are paid separately at the box office. Employees of the accommodation service accept MasterCard and Visa credit cards in cash. Tipping is optional, but welcome. They are asked for the choice of room. On average, the amount of the desired fee for this service is 2,500 rubles.
