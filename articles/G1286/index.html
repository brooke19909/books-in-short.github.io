Petro Sport Hotel - hotel in St. Petersburg: description, rooms and reviews

Petro Sport Hotel boasts comfortable rooms, good infrastructure and high standards of service. Everything is thought out to the smallest detail to satisfy the needs of even the most fastidious guests. This hotel will appeal to both tourists and business people.





Petro Sport Hotel (St. Petersburg): description

St. Petersburg is visited by many tourists and business people, but not everyone wants to stay within the city. Many people choose for themselves such an accommodation option as the Petro Sport Hotel, which is located about 10 km from St. Petersburg, in the village of Yanino. An indisputable advantage is the proximity to the ring road.

The hotel opened its doors for guests in 2006. For guests, there are 99 rooms with all necessary amenities. A special feature of the hotel is its own sports club, where there is a gym and a professional pool. Beautiful nature and a quiet atmosphere make this place ideal for retreats, corporate parties, seminars and just a quiet rural holiday.

  • very modest breakfast;
  • quite often there is no one on duty in the parking lot, and therefore you have to independently look for a person who will open the barrier;

General impression

Going to the Petro Sport Hotel, tourists are waiting not only for a quiet rural setting, but also for the opportunity to visit the sports complex. For many, it comes as a surprise that all related services are provided at an additional cost (this is not indicated when booking). And even parking at the hotel is paid (it would be more logical to include this option in the price, since almost all guests come in private cars). Therefore, if you are not inclined to spend large sums on a country vacation, you will have to be content with beautiful well-groomed territory and fresh air, which is important.
