Do-it-yourself new things from old things, thread preparation

In the wardrobe of any girl there are things that we no longer want to wear. But throwing them away is also no desire. The best way is to dissolve these things in order to bind something new from the resulting threads.



If you dissolve an old thing, then immediately pay attention to the threads. If the threads are old and washed many times, then you should not mess with them. And if the yarn is high-quality and relatively new, then it is quite suitable for you to knit a new thing.

Wash the old thing that you no longer need, and cut carefully at the seams into parts. If there are very old places, then they can be cut out. The knitting details need to find the top, and start to dissolve. Of the old things, you can make quite decent threads, it is best to rewind them later in balls.

Threads thus obtained may break. Knitting from old things and differs in that the threads of them are not very durable. But it is possible to fasten the ends of the threads in the places of breaks by the sea knot.

Yarn alignment

Yarn obtained from old things will be flattened and uneven. Your task is to align it, because from a flattened yarn you will not be able to knit a new thing beautifully. In order to make this yarn suitable for knitting, you need to wind it in skeins, wash it by hand and hang it to air dry.



Old waste items are often made of wool. Woolen threads require a particularly tender attitude towards themselves. They must not be washed in hot water, because they will fall off and shrink. Wash them in warm water. In this case, shampoo for washing hair, a simple washing powder, liquid for wool are often used as detergents. Mustard dissolved in water is also used.

If you washed and dried skeins of yarn, now you need to wind it into balls. Then from these balls you will knit your product. In the case when all the threads you have prepared, you need to decide what can be knitted from old things.

Sweater from old things, the search for thread

During cleaning in the closet, you can find many old things that you no longer need. For example, berets, hats, scarves. They can be dissolved by receiving new threads, and something new can be knitted from them. For example, a sweater.



Making new things from old things with your own hands is relatively easy to do. To knit a sweater for everyday wear, you will not need to know any special knitting techniques. It is enough to understand how the front loops differ from the wrong ones.

To begin, tie a sample measuring approximately 12 by 12 centimeters. You need to create the pattern that you plan to use for your thing. For example, you plan to knit a sweater with an elastic band two by two, so this also knit a sample. Altering old things with your own hands will thus be easy for you.

Sweater from old things, selection of models

If you are not a very experienced knitter, then it is best for you to try knitting sweaters of a not very complex model. New things from old things with your own hands will be easier for you if you do not first take complex patterns and models. If you are knitting for yourself, consider the features of your figure. Girls of high growth with a dense complexion, as a rule, are not suitable for sweaters and cardigans knitted with complex patterns such as braids and bumps. But for a short female figure, such models may well come in handy.



Before knitting, do not forget to make a full-size pattern, so it will be easier for you to navigate. Magazines like Sabrina, Knitting, and others can help you in your search for patterns.

For a beginner knitter, this model of sweater is suitable: after calculating the loops, we knit the required number of loops on the knitting needles, we knit with an elastic band two by two twelve centimeters. Then we pass to the front surface. We knit with an even cloth to the armholes, then we make reductions for the armholes. We knit to the neck, form the neck on the back and front of the sweater, and on the front of it it will be a little deeper. Then the details of the sweater must be steamed and connected to each other using a stitch seam. This seam is made with a special needle with a large eye. Remember to tie the sleeves.

How to knit a lampshade

If you have the opportunity to devote some time to decorating your home, then you can try to create new things from old things with your own hands. When you dissolve old, unnecessary things for you, then you have an idea to create something new. There are threads, and why can they come in handy?

The wardrobe is already full. But there remains a home interior, which also needs to be decorated and made cozy. A knitted lampshade for a lamp is the part that will add charm to your home interior.



To implement such ideas, reworking old things with your own hands will come in handy. So, for lampshades it is better to take old things connected from cotton threads. These threads will not overheat from the light of an electric lamp. It is better if you use this lampshade to decorate a cold bulb.

Crochet lampshade

Old unnecessary things will give you the opportunity to get a lot of thread for your lampshade. Before you start the process of knitting a lampshade, you need to know its size. To do this, make the necessary measurements. It is necessary to measure the circumference of the old lampshade with a centimeter.



Then draw a diagram in the middle of which there will be a circle equal in diameter to the top of the lampshade. Crochet a chain of air loops that will be the base of the lampshade. Then knit according to the pattern. It‚Äôs easy to find a scheme. You can make a lampshade of motifs.

In order for the knitted fabric to be ready for use, it must be starched. To do this, take a glass of cold water and dissolve a spoonful of starch in it, then combine the resulting liquid with boiling water. In the resulting solution, you need to immerse the knitted fabric, then put it on the base of the lampshade and wait until it dries.

Lampshade with knitting needles

The lampshade can also be knitted. It is important that the number of spokes matches the size of the thread. In order to knit the lampshade with knitting needles, you will need to measure the upper and lower circumference of the base and then make a pattern. She will look like a trapezoid.

Before you start knitting a lampshade with knitting needles, you need to knit a small sample with the pattern that you will knit and wash the sample in warm water. Thus, remaking old things with your own hands will become an occupation for you, bringing not only joy, but also benefit.
