Blacksmith Victor Kuznetsov

Despite the fame of his sharp and durable knives in Russia and abroad, one can often hear questions: when and where did Viktor Kuznetsov come into the world? The biography of the blacksmith is simple and intricate at the same time. Viktor Vasilyevich Kuznetsov was born in 1947. Its small homeland is Eastern Siberia. To blacksmithing went a long time.





No fuss

School, army, university (faculty of oriental studies, department of Chinese language), life in a huge, noisy city ... As it turned out, all these and other steps of human existence led the Siberian to the main thing - the blacksmith forge in a small village near Mtsensk. It was there, far from the hustle and bustle of the people, without a TV, with limited access to the Internet that Viktor Kuznetsov, one of the best Russian hand forging specialists, has been living and working for over forty years. It happens that she comes to Moscow to visit her daughter, checks the mail.

Self-development for him is reading up to hundreds of historical and philosophical books annually. Shares the teachings of Lao Tzu. Blacksmithing is primarily a practice. Viktor Vasilievich began working with metal in 1985. He was pushed to action by a simple chisel - a tool for woodwork. Kuznetsov needed it, and he went to the railway forge (FC).

Perfect simplicity

Experience is the son of difficult mistakes. Step by step, Viktor Kuznetsov gained skill. There were fewer defective chisels, then there were none at all. Blacksmithing has become familiar (and at the same time always new). The next fifteen years, Kuznetsov learned to make knives: sharp, durable, uncomplicated in shape.

Art forging did not appeal to him. Although the blacksmith went to the heights of craftsmanship through decorativeness, to which he quickly cooled.
