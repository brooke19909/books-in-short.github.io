How to disable Megafon-TV: information on the service

The option from the Megafon company, through which you can watch television channels on any devices, is provided to subscribers of any telecommunication operators in the country. You can use it wherever there is Internet - wired, WI-FI, mobile. How is the service managed? What are the conditions for its use and are there any restrictions? How to disable Megafon TV? All of these issues will be addressed in the current article.





Service Description

As mentioned earlier, anyone can use the option, regardless of which SIM card of which telecom operator he uses in his mobile device. The cost and terms of connection are the same for Megafon customers as well as for owners of numbers of other companies. In addition, it is not required to purchase any equipment to watch Megafon-TV. You can deactivate the service at any time - without losing money, since the monthly fee is debited daily.

Features of the provision

Before you talk about how to connect and how to disconnect Megafon-TV, you should provide a description of some features of this service.

  • The value of the subscription fee is determined by the package of TV channels that the subscriber will view. In total there are 9 options for packages, each of which contains about a hundred channels. You can choose either one or several options. The cost of a monthly fee for packages can be from one to eight rubles.
  • You can view channels from any device: from TVs, from smartphones, from tablet PCs. You can connect up to five devices to this option. If you try to add another device - the sixth, the system will issue a warning.
  • To watch TV from Megafon, a special plug-in is used - Dune HD (it can be installed by downloading it on the Internet).
  • Channel packages are managed online: you can disable current and connect new ones at any time, without waiting for the end of a specific period.
