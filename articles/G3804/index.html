A little bit about protection

Naturally, like all other multicookers, Redmond is equipped with a four-level security system. If the selected operating mode involves functioning under high pressure, the device will turn on only after the cover is closed. And immediately after installation, your miracle processor will securely fix it. The increased pressure that is created in the working chamber is necessarily relieved by an additional spring valve.

Safe and secure

Moreover, if the temperature exceeds the allowable value, the heating element will be stopped. The device will wait until the pressure reaches the optimum level again.
