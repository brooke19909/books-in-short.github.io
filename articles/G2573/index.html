Luxury Nha Trang 3 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang): hotel description, services, reviews

We invite you to spend a truly unforgettable and in all respects a pleasant vacation in Vietnam, an Asian country friendly to us with an extraordinary history, interesting traditions, exotic nature and sincere hospitality. Among all the local resorts I want to highlight Nha Trang, and among the dozens of its hotels - Luxury Nha Trang 3 *? According to the numerous reviews of tourists who have already visited it, this budget hotel can easily be assigned four stars, as it offers comfortable rooms and impeccable living conditions. We add that Nha Trang stands out among similar Vietnamese resorts for the most favorable climate, therefore, choosing a tour here, you really will give yourself a unique trip.

Location

Luxury Nha Trang 3 * is located in a wonderful place. The geography of Vietnam is not so well known to our compatriots, as, for example, the geography of Turkey. But this is one of the main issues that determine whether a vacation is successful or not. Recall that the territory of Vietnam with a narrow strip 1700 km long borders the eastern end of the Indochina peninsula, washed by the South China Sea. Such a significant length from north to south is ‚Äúguilty‚Äù of the fact that the climatic conditions in the country's resorts are very different. We invite you to the most comfortable resort, where the weather is created not only by the sea, but also by a mountain range that protects the area from adverse air masses.

It is called Nha Trang, or in Vietnamese Nha Trang. By local standards, this is a fairly large city, which is the capital of Khanh Hoa province. If you mentally divide Vietnam into two equal parts, approximately in the middle of the south, Khanh Hoa will be located, and almost in its very center, on the banks of a beautiful bay, Nha Trang is located. Opposite the city, separated by narrow stripes of the sea, are picturesque islands, the most famous of which is Winperl.

Hotel Luxury Nha Trang 3 * was built, you can say, on the coast, a quarter of an hour walk from the city center. Across the road is a hypermarket where everything that the soul wants is sold. Not far from D. Station and the main market, which in 5 minutes takes any taxi. Bars, cafes and entertainment centers are located near the hotel, as well as in the center of Nha Trang. All this diversifies the rest and makes it acceptable in all seasons.

How to get there

It is most convenient to fly from Russia to Kamran Airport, located in the town of the same name, which is 36 km from Nha Trang. There used to be a military base here, but since 2009 it has been operating as a peaceful international airport, receiving flights from dozens of Russian cities in the season. From the airport you can drive to the hotel by transfer of the meeting party or by taxi on your own, which will cost 18 y. e. On a minibus, the cost of the trip will cost about 6.5. e. Travel time takes almost an hour by transfer and half an hour by taxi.



Description

Luxury Nha Trang Hotel 3 * belongs to the urban category, so there is no large territory with a tropical plant garden and developed infrastructure. The hotel was built in 2011, so for now everything is new and working fine in it. There are two buildings on the territory (5 floors each). These buildings are called "A" and "B". Outwardly, both of them are quite stylish and modern, but the internal arrangement varies greatly.

Case "A" is more expensive, and therefore more comfortable. Its rooms offer sea views. Case "B" is a budget. The rooms in it are more modest, and the views from the windows to the neighboring school, the roofs of houses and the city block. Relocation from ‚ÄúB‚Äù to ‚ÄúA‚Äù costs from 150 y. e.

In addition to buildings that occupy the lion's share of the hotel, it has a medium-sized pool. It is surrounded by tropical palm trees and other vegetation, "living" in pots. This greenery creates a cheerful look and feel of being in an overseas resort. Near the hotel there is a busy road without traffic lights, separating it from the beach. In front of the main entrance there is a parking spot for motorbikes and cars.

Infrastructure

Luxury Nha Trang 3 * due to its low stardom cannot boast a rich infrastructure. In the beautifully decorated lobby, decorated with Vietnamese flavor, the reception is located. They speak Vietnamese and a little English. Only the representatives of travel agencies working with Russian tourists know the Russian language. At the reception you can exchange currency, buy a tour, call a taxi, leave luggage for storage, arrange for washing personal items, rent a motobike or bicycle. Prices for this service are the same as in the city.

The hotel offers a conference room with the necessary equipment for work for a business price. And for those who decided to celebrate a festive event in Vietnam, the hotel offers a chic banquet hall.

The toilet rooms, the sizes of which are also very modest, are equipped with a shower (without a cabin), a toilet and a washbasin. Hygiene products, including toothbrushes and toothpaste, are issued on the day of arrival and are replenished daily.

Nutrition

Most often, tourists buy packages with BB meals in Luxury Nha Trang 3 *. The reviews of people about the organization of breakfasts are excellent. They pass in the only restaurant in the hotel. Service for children

A big plus is considered by all parents that this hotel is non-smoking. It is very suitable for kids to stay in it. True, there is no playground and a separate children's pool, but the rooms can accommodate cots, and in the restaurant you can always pick up something digestible for young guests. If they have not reached the age of 10 years, payment for their stay will not be charged.



Adult Service

Since the hotel is located in an excellent location, namely in the center of the resort life of Nha Trang, Luxury Nha Trang 3 * and for adults does not seek to offer a wide range of services. Mostly guests go to have fun in the city. The hotel can spend leisure time in the pool, which is especially true when there is a storm at sea. Around the pool there are always a lot of free sun loungers and umbrellas, but you have to rent or buy towels. In addition, the hotel has a pretty good spa, where quality massage is performed. There is also a fitness room. Animation at the hotel is not provided.

Sea

Vietnam is a country of the subtropical zone, so it is always summer here. But there is also a change of seasons on the coast of the South China Sea, which is fundamentally important to consider when planning a trip to Luxury Nha Trang Hotel 3 *. Nha Trang is famous for its beautiful beaches, one of which lies across the busy road from the hotel. There are no traffic lights on it, but drivers, as a rule, allow pedestrians to cross it. The beach is clean, sandy with large shells and crabs near the coast, spacious, why quite calm. Sunbeds and umbrellas on it are paid, but they are rented for the whole day, so you can leave the beach, for example, for lunch, and return to your sunbed.

The sea here is turquoise blue, the water is warm (even in January it is +22 ¬∞ C, and in summer it is + 28 ¬∞ C). But you should not count on a beach holiday when you arrive in Nha Trang from October to the end of January, as the region covers the season of heavy rains and hurricanes. There are always waves on the sea at this hour, there are even tsunamis, although the water is warm but dirty. But from February you can already bask in the white sand. Vacationers in the summer months the beach gives complete calm to the sea and a lot of sun. So that it does not upset, you must use protective creams.

Additional Information

The hotel in question has many advantages, one of which is the nearby Kamran Airport. It is convenient for traveling with small children. In Nha Trang for a beach holiday, it is better to take tours from February to the end of September. Prices are then much higher, but weather conditions are suitable for the sea and for excursions. Since October, Vietnam has been considered a low season, so prices here are from 2196 rubles per room per night. But on New Year's Eve they are again very high, but tourists can visit an exotic holiday with a carnival and interesting performances.

Guests can spend time on excursions, which are best taken in the Russian Center, located on the waterfront a couple of hundred meters from the hotel.

The most popular ones are a trip to an amusement park on Wingerle Island, where there is an oceanarium and a large water park, on Monkey Island, in the city of Dalat, to waterfalls and to mineral and mud springs, which make it possible to improve your health during the holidays.

They are about 7 km from the hotel. It is more convenient to get by taxi.
