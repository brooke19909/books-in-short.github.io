Popularity



Attractions "Water Slides" are increasingly gaining popularity among buyers and owners of entertainment centers. They are an inflatable high structure that is equipped with a compressor. With it, water is supplied directly to the slide and provides a good and smooth glide. There is an integrated type of model: water slides with an inflatable pool together.

Where can I use them?

The first where construction may be needed is on the beach. There will be a lot of people who want to have fun swimming, as rivers and lakes in cities and suburbs are mostly polluted. A hot summer without the presence of any reservoir is unthinkable. A special design comes to the rescue - water slides, which will serve as entertainment, and save them from the scorching sun.





The second is an entertainment center. Many adults come here with their children. So that the children do not get bored, and the parents do not feel guilty that they temporarily want to distract from the child and devote some time to themselves, again water slides come to the rescue for children, small and older.

The third way is to strengthen the model in an inflatable or stationary pool. Water rides are offered by manufacturers in different variations: this is a florid trench that slides down into the water, and a structure that can fit several people at once, and a model with a slope directly in the water.

Material and quality

Water slides are made of high quality material, due to which the designs:

  • differ in durability and stability;
  • do not give in to burnout;
  • do not fade;
  • do not lose their qualities at high temperatures of water and air;
  • have a long service life.

Usually they are made of bright material. Models for children provide maximum safety and comfortable climbing up. Material for water slides certainly has a quality certificate that complies with international standards. In addition, the design can be washed with ordinary household detergents, without worrying about the preservation of color and brightness of the material.

Scary, fun and wet

Inflatable slides are the most popular type of rides on the water. If you have at least once tried on yourself all the sensations and emotions that you get while rolling down, then you will certainly want to repeat them again. This is evidenced by the water slides of the world, which are built in different countries and in the most bizarre forms: either it is a winding long cobra, then a structure of several floors, or you will be offered to slide down the hill directly into the sea. Undoubtedly, this type of entertainment is one of the most beloved for adults and children.
