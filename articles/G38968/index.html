Amelia Beach Resort Hotel & Spa 5 * (Turkey, Side, Kyzylot): description, service, reviews

In the Turkey region of Side, in the small town of Kyzylot, there is a wonderful tourist area where a large number of travelers who are fans of exotic nature annually relax. Here, throughout the holiday season, guests can enjoy a wonderful hot, but at the same time very mild climate, which allows you to swim in the sunshine daily.

One of the most famous hotels where vacationers come is Amelia Beach Resort Hotel. Tourists claim that relaxing in it is one real pleasure, since it is here that excellent living conditions and a high level of service are offered.



Location

Amelia Beach Resort Hotel & Spa is surrounded by beautiful greenery and gardens. On the one hand, it has access to the Mediterranean Sea, and on the other hand it overlooks the mountain peaks.

This recreation area is located in the town of Kyzylot, where there are practically no places for entertainment, but there are plenty of them on the hotel‚Äôs territory, since you can also visit a disco, restaurant, and, if necessary, the opportunity to organize business meetings and banquets. The sea coast is literally 50 meters from the main building of the hotel, they are separated by the roadway, which can be passed using the underpass.

As for the proximity of transport hubs, the nearest international airport is located at a distance of 80 km (the city of Antalya), it is here that all tourists initially arrive.

Rooms

In total, the hotel has 475 apartments. Due to their number, Amelia Beach Resort Hotel is classified as a major holiday destination. The hotel offers the possibility of placing in the apartments two categories of comfort: "standard" and "family". All of them are concentrated in two buildings, one of which is six-story, and the second is two-story and looks more like a block. Both of them are modern, were built in 2008. In each of them, as well as in the common area of ‚Äã‚ÄãAmelia Beach Resort Hotel & Spa, work is regularly carried out to maintain the beauty of the premises and their comfort.

All apartments have a private bathroom, where the necessary plumbing is installed, as well as a set of bath accessories. In addition, each room has air conditioning, which in a cooler period can create a warm atmosphere in the room, and in hot weather, on the contrary, cool the space.

"Standard"

Standard apartments offer tourists excellent living conditions. In total, there are 300 in their bodies.

Each room has all the necessary furniture, the set of which includes two beds (large double and single), bedside tables, a desk for work, as well as a chair. In addition, tourists have the opportunity to put their things in a wardrobe, which is also available in the room.

With regard to additional features, guests of this level of rooms are provided with an LCD TV with satellite channels, a minibar and a safe, for which a separate daily fee is required.

Among the rooms "standard" there are four that are designed for disabled people. All furniture for them was made according to unique sketches, taking into account all the requirements from people with disabilities. Special attention is paid to plumbing - it is made in full accordance with international standards.



"Family"

Amelia Beach Resort Hotel (Kyzylot) has 177 family rooms. Each of them has all the conditions suitable for living an impressive number of guests - up to 5 people, as well as one child, since it is in such apartments that one children‚Äôs playpen is provided absolutely free of charge.

As for the furniture equipment of this type of rooms, it is practically no different from the "standard" setting. The difference lies in the number of beds, which in the "family" room 4, as an additional can be installed folding design of the European type.

All rooms have one bathroom.

Conditions for children

Parents who come with their children to Amelia Beach Resort Hotel, in the description of conditions and services, often say that it is very comfortable to live with their children. First of all, this is due to the fact that for this category of guests a specially equipped pool is provided in the open area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe recreation area. They also have a well-equipped playground where they can engage in active sports and play with their peers.

Parents note on the positive side the presence in the rooms of the "family" category a free berth for the child in the form of a comfortable crib.

As for food, the hotel restaurants have a menu that contains all the most mouth-watering, wholesome and vibrant dishes that kids will definitely like. There are also special seating areas for them.

In the open air there is a large club for children, which can be visited by guests from 4 to 12 years old. It has a harmonious and very friendly team of animators who can not only look after the kids, but also play with them and watch educational programs. Many Russian tourists note with positive comments that the animators speak Russian, which greatly facilitates communication with them.

Nutrition

According to vacationers, the food at Amelia Beach Resort Hotel is very decent. For a pleasant eating process, several restaurants are arranged here, the main of which is a daily buffet, which serves nutritious and delicious dishes prepared in the best traditions of European and Turkish cuisines. In total, this restaurant is able to combine within its walls 700 people and half as much on the summer terrace.

There are also several other restaurants serving Italian, Chinese and Turkish cuisines. In addition, there is also an institution that specializes in preparing fish dishes - it does not work all year round, but only at the peak of the holiday season - from early June to late September. You can visit any of these establishments only by prior reservation, which is carried out on a paid basis. Once for the entire period of stay at the hotel, you can visit these restaurants for free.



Amelia Beach Resort Hotel has six bars, which are concentrated mainly near the pool, as well as on the beach. One of them is located in the main building. As for the dishes offered, the local menu consists mainly of snacks, as well as a bar card with a wide range of cocktails and drinks containing alcohol, as well as without it.

Spa area

The Amelia Beach Resort Hotel 5 * (Side) has a beautiful spa area, which is often mentioned in their reviews of this place for a vacation by tourists. To provide cosmetic and massage services, exclusively modern technologies are applied here, and business professionals are engaged in the business.

The SPA center has a beautiful thermal complex, which includes a sauna and hammam. Here guests are served by skillful parmeisters. Upon leaving the bath, any guest can cool off in the pool, which is available in the indoor complex. In a niche with water, its automatic heating is installed, which allows guests not to feel discomfort.

The separate room has massage equipment, which guests can also use without payment. In the spa area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe Amelia Beach Resort Hotel 5 * there is a room for cosmetic procedures where masters can do body wraps, peeling, apply a mask and do a number of other manipulations. Such procedures are also done free of charge.

Sport

For sports enthusiasts, the hotel has a large hall where they can exercise their health and figure. It has several dozen simulators that will help keep the body fit. The fair sex is very fond of attending aerobics classes, which are conducted under the guidance of an instructor.

For fans of team sports, there is an excellent football field, basketball and volleyball courts. All necessary equipment can always be taken at special points, and absolutely free of charge. Tennis fans can visit either of the two courts.

Pool

Amelia Beach Resort Hotel has three large pools. They are well equipped and have everything you need for a pleasant and safe stay. Two of them are equipped with high slides for active pastime. Every day they carry out systemic cleaning, so that the water in the niches is constantly crystal clear. Heating here is carried out exclusively due to sunlight.

Around the pools there are a large number of sun loungers, as well as beach umbrellas. Here guests can sit down after water procedures and sunbathe. If desired, anyone has the opportunity to go to the local bar and get there various snacks, specialty ice cream and, of course, bright cocktails and cooling drinks.



Beach

Amelia Beach Resort Hotel (Turkey) has a private beach where tourists can relax on comfortable sun loungers. Here umbrellas and air mattresses are provided for use, if you wish, for an additional fee, you can take a large terry towel.

Guests who wish to enjoy a drink can visit the beach, which is located in the central part of this recreation area. Throughout its territory there are several areas where public toilets, changing rooms and showers are installed, which guests can use for free.

As for entertainment on the beach, here you can engage in active water sports, such as surfing, water skiing, as well as scuba diving under the guidance of an instructor. All these entertainments are provided for a fee. If you wish, you can rent a jet ski, yacht or boat and go on a small cruise in the open sea.



Service

Amelia Beach Resort Hotel provides a range of services. Very popular are those that offer dry cleaning and laundry services. Here you can rent a car without leaving the hotel - a parking space for it will be provided completely free of charge. If a desire arises, guests can take it on a short trip around the neighborhood.

On the ground floor of the main building there is a currency exchange. Not far from it is the doctor‚Äôs office, which is located in the hotel on an ongoing basis. The services of a doctor are provided here as needed, they are paid.



Prices

The cost of the tour at Amelia Beach Resort Hotel & Spa 5 * directly depends on what conditions will be included in its package. So, provided that they stay together in a standard-class apartment for one week, vacationers must pay about 60,000 rubles. As a rule, this price includes a flight from Moscow to Turkey and vice versa, a transfer from an arrival point, room rental, meals, as well as payment of medical insurance. If desired, tourists can include in their package some other services that must be agreed upon in advance during the payment process.

Each tourist should certainly remember that to visit Turkey, a Russian citizen should pre-pay a visa, which costs about $ 80.

As for the team of animators, they receive a huge number of positive comments in their address due to well-coordinated and high-quality work. Parents who relax with their children note the excellent attitude of the staff towards the children, as well as the provision on their part of the most comfortable conditions for their stay.

And finally, about the prices. According to most tourists, they are very acceptable here. That is why most travelers recommend going here to rest for their friends and relatives, and also do not mind returning here more than once, for the next portion of impressions.
