Hotel Klio Apartments 4 * (Gouves, Crete): description, leisure and reviews of tourists

Gouves is one of the popular resort villages of the Greek island of Crete. Every year, many tourists come here from all over the world in search of great beaches, a variety of entertainment and comfortable living in an area with a healthy climate and beautiful nature. The Gouves hotel base offers everyone who wants accommodation for every taste and budget. So, there are both luxurious five-star hotels and more than economical campsites. If you want to spend a vacation in Gouves and are looking for an inexpensive, but comfortable hotel, then we advise you to pay attention to Klio Apartments 4 *. We offer further closer acquaintance with this place, as well as find out whether our compatriots liked the rest here.





Where is

The hotel in question is located on the north coast of an island belonging to Greece. The resort village of Gouves, in which the hotel is located, is small, but has a developed tourist infrastructure. The nearest international airport is located in Heraklion, which is only 17 kilometers from Gouves. So upon arrival in the air harbor, the road to the hotel will take you about half an hour. This is very convenient, especially if you are traveling with small children. City beaches are a few hundred meters from the Klio Aparthotel 4 *. On foot, you can walk to the seashore in just ten minutes. The beaches are sandy and equipped with sunbeds, umbrellas, showers, locker rooms and toilets. You will have to pay for using beach equipment.





Transport

As already mentioned, the nearest airport is in the city of Heraklion, 17 kilometers from the hotel. If you buy a ticket to Crete through a travel agency, then for sure you will be provided with a transfer from the air harbor to the hotel. If you are planning an independent trip, you can get to the hotel by taxi or bus (first to the center of Heraklion, and then to Gouves). You can also book transfers directly at your hotel. Traveling around the island is easy and by bus. However, this type of transportation is convenient only if you plan trips to the nearest settlements. For longer routes, it is recommended to rent a car. In Gouves you will also find bicycles and mopeds for rent.





Klio Apartments 4 * (Crete, Gouves): photos and description

Clio Apartments is a small, cozy, economy-class hotel. The number of rooms here is represented by studios and apartments. Some rooms are suitable for people with reduced mobility. The hotel is not new, but once in several years a large-scale renovation is carried out here. So, the last reconstruction at Klio Apartments was carried out in 2011. The hotel has swimming pools for adults and children, a sun terrace, a restaurant, a bar and a barbecue area. This place is perfect for a quiet and budget holiday for both youth and family tourists with children.

Hotel Policies

Guests arriving at Klio Apartments (Gouves) are checked in after two in the afternoon. If you arrive at the hotel earlier than the specified time, then if you have free rooms, they will populate you immediately. However, in July and August the hotel is often full, as they say, to the eyeballs, so in this case you will have to wait until the previous guests have moved out of the rooms they occupy. On the day you leave your room, you should free up until 12 noon. You can pay at the hotel for accommodation and for additional services with either Visa, Mastercard or American Express cards, or in cash. The hotel is always happy for guests with children of any age. At the same time, babies under two years old are provided with playpens. For extra beds, this is not possible with Klio Apartments (Gouves). The good news for those who love traveling in the company of their pets will be the fact that guests with four-legged pets are allowed in the hotel. However, you should notify the property in advance of your intention to arrive at the hotel with your pet and wait for confirmation from it.

Nutrition

When booking a room at the Clio Apartments Hotel, you can choose one of the following types of food: only breakfast, breakfast and dinner, or all inclusive. The hotel restaurant serves a buffet three times a day.





Sea and beach

All the beaches of Gouves are municipal. Therefore, you will have to pay for using sun loungers and parasols (about 6 euros for two sun loungers and an umbrella). It has everything you need for an excellent holiday: equipment for entertainment on the water, showers, changing rooms, toilets, as well as restaurants and bars. The distance from Klio Apartments 4 * to the nearest beach is several hundred meters. You can walk here in just ten minutes. The beaches here are sandy, with a gentle entry into the water.

Entertainment

The main attraction for hotel guests is relaxing by the pool. It features a spacious sun terrace, and there is also a bar with a wide selection of drinks. Also, hotel guests have the opportunity to spend time in the TV room or game room, sing in karaoke. Several times a week in the evenings, the hotel hosts show programs. Also, experienced travelers recommend that all vacationers in Crete be sure to travel to the sights of this island. You can do this either by bus, or by rented car, or as part of an excursion group.





Infrastructure

Despite the fact that Klio Apartments 4 * is a small hotel, it has everything you need for a comfortable stay and stay. So, the hotel has a restaurant, bar, lounge with free Wi-Fi, parking, as well as a mini market. Also, by contacting the reception, you can rent a car or book an excursion.

Accommodation cost

As for the price of accommodation at the Clio Apartments hotel, it is quite affordable for most travelers. So, a day of accommodation here at the height of the holiday season (July-August) will cost you from 55 euros for two in a studio, from 70 euros for three in apartments and from 78 euros for four in apartments.





Clio Apart Hotel (Crete): reviews of tourists from Russia

In order to make your impression of the hotel in question more complete and accurate, we suggest that you familiarize yourself with the opinions of our compatriots who have been vacationing here this year. Looking ahead, we can say that most of the tourists considered this hotel a great place for a relaxing family vacation on such a wonderful Greek island like Crete. But we offer to understand everything in more detail.

As for the location of Klio, our compatriots considered it quite successful. So, you can walk to the sea in ten minutes. In the vicinity of the hotel there are many shops and shops where you can buy both groceries and souvenirs. Here you will also find taverns and bars. The hotel itself is small and very comfortable. Everything here is conducive to a relaxing holiday. In the evenings, due to the lack of animation, you can fall asleep in silence. In the daytime, for the same reason, there will be no problems with sleeping children.

Our compatriots found the rooms at Klio Apartments spacious and well-furnished. Furniture and appliances are not new, but all in good working condition. If something fails, then after contacting the reception the wizard will quickly fix all the problems. The guests were pleased with the presence in the rooms of all the necessary utensils for cooking and eating food. So many tourists booked accommodation without meals and cooked in their rooms without any problems, buying food in the nearest shops. As for cleaning, we carried out it efficiently and regularly. Also, several times a week, as stated, towels and bed linen were changed.

Opinions of tourists about food in the hotel mostly agree. So, our compatriots found the local breakfasts good, but devoid of variety. As for lunch and dinner, the situation here is better. In addition, lunch and dinner in taverns outside the hotel is much more expensive than in Klio Apartments.

Guest reviews about the hotel staff are also mostly positive. So, all the staff are very friendly and always try to help. Pleased with our compatriots is the fact that many either speak Russian, or at least understand it. So you should not have communication problems here.

As for the local beaches, then tourists are mostly satisfied with them. So, renting beach equipment here is inexpensive. Near the beaches there are plenty of restaurants and bars where you can both have a bite to eat and refresh yourself with your favorite drink.

Experienced tourists recommend that vacationers not spend their entire vacation at the hotel and on the beach, but be sure to travel to Crete. After all, there is a mass of places of interest to visit. To do this, you can rent a car or book a tour. All this can be done, by the way, without going beyond the limits of Clio Apartments.

Summing up, we can say that most of our compatriots who spent their holidays in Klio Apartments 4 * were very pleased with their choice.
