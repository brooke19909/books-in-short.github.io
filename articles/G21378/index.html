"Boomerang" - a beach in Novosibirsk: description, reviews, photos, prices and location on the map

The Novosibirsk region is famous for its excellent summer weather. Already at the end of spring, the region is quite hot, and local residents are trying to get out to rest on the countryside. And what could be better than basking in the gentle rays of the sun and plunging into cool water? Since the beginning of June, the swimming season has already begun in the city, and Novosibirsk, who are alone, and who are companies and families, are sent to the coastal zones. According to official figures, there are 15 beaches in Novosibirsk. The most popular vacation spots for citizens are the following: Zvezda, Butrinsky Grove, Nautilus, Boomerang, Sun City, and, of course, the city beach. Characteristic of the Boomerang complex

The beach is located on the shores of the picturesque Ob Sea and has a length of 1200 meters. Entrance here costs 150 rubles, and more than a hundred parking spaces are represented. However, even taking a walk on foot from the barrier to the water is a real pleasure: birds singing, pine aroma, sun glare ... On the way, you will find many shops selling fragrant popcorn, milkshakes, beer, ice cream and soft drinks.

Entertainment center

We want to note that the Boomerang beach (Novosibirsk) is not just a place where you can sunbathe and swim, it is also a whole entertainment complex. It is located in a suburb of the metropolis, not far from the ObGES. The territory of the complex is huge - as much as 99 hectares. This is a pine forest, and the beach itself, and entertainment infrastructure. In addition, rescuers and a medical service are constantly working here.

It is Boomerang that can provide you the best service. The beach, beautiful nature and all kinds of entertainment make this place very attractive for vacationers. And how much does such a pleasant pastime cost?



Beach "Boomerang" Novosibirsk: price for services

The complex has a sauna, a mini-hotel, a cafe, as well as various kiosks. The beach is guarded, therefore, complete safety is guaranteed for vacationers. All holidaymakers can rent any beach items if they wish. Naturally, such services are paid. For example, for umbrellas you will have to pay 50 rubles, for the same price you can rent volleyballs. You can ride a catamaran for 250 rubles per hour. Are you hungry? Nearby there is a barbecue kiosk (a portion of barbecue costs 150 rubles) and a variety of cafes where food prices are quite reasonable.

Beach visitors

In the summer months, about 20 thousand people of different ages visit the Boomerang entertainment complex every week. The beach, by the way, is very popular not only among local residents, but also among visitors. The audience of this place is quite colorful - Novosibirsk, Berdchane, residents of suburban settlements and more remote regions.





Infrastructure

The territory of the complex is landscaped: everywhere there are drinking fountains and washing machines. The coastline of the beach area is approximately 1200 meters. There is everything that vacationers need: toilets, sun umbrellas, sports grounds for soccer and volleyball. On weekends, promotions and entertainment programs are often held here. As many as 10 thousand people can accommodate at the same time in the Boomerang complex. The beach, various cafes, water activities attract crowds of vacationers here. Young visitors will also not have to be bored: a playground with a slide, a swing, a sandbox is provided for the kids.

Motels and campsites

The complex has a motel with 7 comfortable rooms that are equipped with toilets and showers, TVs. There is also a common lounge and bar.

For lovers of wild relaxation there is a tent camp. This area can accommodate up to 200 tents. Do you prefer camping? 19 very cozy houses are located in a fragrant pine forest, not far from the riverbank. Want to just relax with your company? Then, specially for you, the Boomerang beach (photos are presented in the article) invites you to relax in gazebos with barbecue facilities, benches, electricity and a gas pipeline, which can accommodate up to 30 people at a time.



Cafe

The complex also has several very cozy cafes. We advise you to look at the cafe-bar "Sushi oars!". This is a stylized establishment with a large bar, high-quality commercial equipment, professional lighting and sound equipment, satellite TV and karaoke. The main hall is designed for 80 seats. On the second floor there is a VIP room with excellent views of the Ob Sea and restaurant service. Its capacity is 40 seats.

The entertainment center has several tent cafes and barbecue, which can easily accommodate up to 150 people. In a word, you certainly won't stay hungry if you visit the Boomerang leisure center. The most delicious dishes at affordable prices are the signature hobby of the entertainment complex. Vacationers are also very fond of sitting on the pier in the afternoon or in the evening and admiring the picturesque view of the reservoir.

Winter holidays

We have one more great news - the Boomerang center works not only in the summer, but also in the winter. In winter, a ski base is especially popular. Necessary equipment can be rented. There is still an ice rink. On holidays, it becomes a favorite place of entertainment for all citizens and tourists.





Boomerang complex - everything for your pleasure!

Boomerang is a great place to relax for people of all ages and with any income. By the way, here you can hold a variety of presentations, tastings, promotions, corporate events. In the heart of the entertainment area is the club. It is equipped with high-quality sound and lighting equipment, a large video screen, 8 TVs, satellite antenna. The institution works every day from 22-00, except for an accomplice. Not only local stars, but also celebrities from the capital of Russia often perform here.

Look for the Boomerang beach (Novosibirsk) on the map, collect the straw bag, put on your flip-flops, and go on your adventure!
