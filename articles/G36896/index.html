Skating rink "Pole" (Vladivostok): description and prices

Ice skating always brings a lot of positive emotions and impressions. Sports venues often become favorite places for both children and adults. Skating rink "Pole" (Vladivostok) is waiting for new and regular guests at any time of the year, allowing you to enjoy good ice and comfortable conditions for relaxation. It is always fun and cozy atmosphere.

General information

The indoor ice rink "Pole" in Vladivostok is great for family vacations, meetings with friends or loved ones. Both teenagers and older people often come here. Everyone likes to ride on the ice, feeling light and cool. Guests can come even on hot summers to refresh themselves. This is a definite plus object. If the people who come do not have their skates, then they can rent them. It is also possible to use various sports equipment and skates for young visitors.

Ice at the rink is regularly cleaned while guests relax. If necessary, you can sharpen skates by contacting specialists. For a comfortable stay of visitors there are special locker rooms, showers, left-luggage offices. There is a separate parking space for cars. A doctor is working at the rink. You can relax between skating in the cafe, which is also specially open for guests of the sports facility.

About 150 people can visit the Polyus skating rink (Vladivostok) at a time. There are stands for spectators, as hockey matches regularly take place on ice. About 200 places have been prepared for them. Visitors to the skating rink can consult or ask for help from the trainers who are always present in the building. Adults can bring their children to the arena so that they can learn hockey, figure skating, and short track. Useful sections are specially opened for the younger generation, which from childhood help to harden and improve health.

Address

Skating rink "Pole" (Vladivostok) is located on Ovchinnikova street, building 26, building A. The reference point for the search can be Vera and Nadezhda square and Sorge square, which are very close. There are several ways to get to the rink. You need to get off at the stop called Firsova Street. There are buses to it with numbers 1, 2, 4, 17, 64 and 81. You can also take minibuses No. 12, 40, 85 or 85c.

Prices for the rink "Polyus" (Vladivostok)

One skating session lasts one hour. It will cost visitors 200 rubles. The sports facility itself is open from 10:00 to 21:00. Mass skating starts from 12:00 and lasts until 20:30. But sometimes other events are held at the rink. For example, performances or hockey games. Then on weekdays at the rink you can ride from 11:45 to 12:45. And on Saturday, sessions begin at 14:45 and end at 19:30. On Sunday, the first session begins at 13:45, and the rink closes at 18:30. A more detailed schedule can always be found on the official website of the rink or groups in the social. networks.

Additional Information

Skating rink "Pole" in Vladivostok has certain rules that it is important for guests to know before visiting it.

  • Entrance to the locker room twenty minutes before the start of the session.
  • You can rent your skates or rent them.
  • For using the arena skates, a fee and a security deposit in the form of documents must be paid.
  • It is recommended to leave valuables in the storage room, which is paid.

Visitors are asked to be careful about sports equipment, as well as all equipment. For the safety of mass skating, you need to behave carefully in relation to other visitors. It is not recommended to arrange various kinds of games like catch-ups. This will avoid collisions. Young children should not be alone at the rink. If they are under 14 years old, then they can only come with adults. Also on the rink you can not be with food and without skates. Following these instructions will allow you to spend time comfortably and safely.
