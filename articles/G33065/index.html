Mud volcano Tamani Tizdar

In the Temryuk district (Krasnodar Territory), not far from the village of Za Rodina, in the tract Sinaya Balka, the Azov Hill is located. This is the famous mud volcano. It is called Blue Beam or Tizdar. The second name is associated with the name of the mountain, located a kilometer from this place.

Mount Tizdar rises above sea level by 74 meters and is the beginning of the Golubitsky ridge. Its volcanic origin is evidenced by the fact that deposits of ancient blue clay are located near the water edge. In 1919, the cone of Tizdar volcano collapsed (after an explosive eruption). Now the hill is a truncated cone.

The healing properties of volcanic mud

The mud volcano Tamani Tizdar is known for the healing properties of the erupting bluish mass. What are they? The main therapeutic factor, experts call temperature, or thermal. Under the influence of heat at the place where the dirt is applied, the vessels expand, blood flow increases, and the metabolism in the tissues accelerates. This helps to improve the nutrition of affected skin and muscles, the elimination or reduction of painful and inflammatory symptoms. The second therapeutic factor should be called chemical. The healing effect on the body is due to volatile compounds. Volcanic mud has a pronounced absorbable, anti-inflammatory, analgesic and weak bactericidal action.



Mud treatments increase recovery and immunobiological processes, have a beneficial effect on carbohydrate, protein and water metabolism.

Mud composition

The therapeutic effect is determined by the unique composition of the mud masses, as well as their low mineralization. The crater contains: iodine and boron, biologically active ions of bromine and strontium, zinc and lithium, iron and magnesium, chlorine and calcium, selenium and manganese, humic and naphthenic gases and acids.



The mud volcano Tamani Tizdar is characterized by a constant temperature of healing masses. In summer, the surface of the lake warms up to +20 degrees. There is one more important circumstance. All harmful substances (radionuclides, pesticides, salts of heavy metals) are concentrated in the underground layers of the volcano's mud. Moreover, their number is below standard values.

Mud treatments are successfully combined with bathing in the sea, mineral and sea baths, massage, therapeutic exercises, exercises in the gym (on the recommendation of a doctor), diet, physiotherapy procedures.

Contraindications

They exist and cannot be neglected. Mud therapy is contraindicated in any neoplasm, heart and vascular disease, varicose veins. With caution and with the permission of the attending physician, mud baths can be taken by pregnant women (in the early stages) and children under two years of age.

Taman, a mud volcano: how to get there?

The easiest way is to visit Taman as part of an excursion group. Such trips are now organized by many travel agencies in Krasnodar, Rostov-on-Don, Gelendzhik, Sochi, Anapa and other cities. A comfortable bus will take you directly to your destination.

If you want to go to the volcanoes yourself, then you need to come to Temryuk. Every hour, a shuttle bus number 103 departs from the bus station of this city, next to the village of Za Rodina. Driving on this route starts at 6:30 and ends at 17:50. The "islet of health" is located next to the bus stop, exiting the minibus, you will see a large sign.

Motorists can also visit Taman (mud volcanoes). How to get here by car? To do this, you should move along the highway "Krasnodar - Temryuk". From Temryuk turn right, to the village of Golubitskaya. From her follow the signs to the village of Za Rodina.
