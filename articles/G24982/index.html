Taba resort in Egypt: description, attractions and reviews of tourists

Egypt is a country that attracts tourists from all over the world. Firstly, a large number of monuments of the ancient world are located on the territory of this state. Secondly, Egypt is located on the Red Sea, which is considered warm and clean. Thirdly, here you can take part in exotic entertainments, for example, to ride a camel. In Egypt there are a large number of resorts, one of which is Taba - this will be discussed in this article.

Location

The Taba resort in Egypt is located in the eastern part of the state, on the territory of the Sinai Peninsula. The border with Israel lies very close to this place. Due to the fact that until the end of the 80s of the last century there were territorial conflicts between these countries, you should not walk around the city at night. In extreme cases, you need to bring your passport with you. Terrorist attacks are still being organized in the resort and surrounding areas so far, but there are usually few casualties.

According to tourists reviews, Taba (Egypt) is in an amazing location. The fact is that there is a picturesque bay called Aqaba. Resting in one state, you can see the territory of three more countries: Israel, Jordan and Saudi Arabia.

Climate

The resort is famous for its warm climate without strong winds, since the city is protected from the north by the Sinai Mountains. You can come here at any time of the year. In summer, the air temperature rises to +35 ¬∞ C, the sea warms up to +29 ¬∞ C. In winter it is cooler: the temperature is around +20 ... + 25 ¬∞ C. According to tourists, it is best to come to Taba in the fall, because in this time of the year the sun no longer burns so much, and the water still remains warm.

Hotels

Taba in Egypt is a resort famous for upscale hotels. They are affordable, they have a good level of service, they are comfortable. Most of them are made in the style of the ancient East, which is why the city looks very picturesque.

In the area called Taba Hyats, you can find the best hotels. These include hotel complexes such as Marriott, Hilton and Sofitel. They are located close to each other, nearby is a golf course. The low cost of living in hotels, according to tourists, is due to the family orientation of the resort. However, if you wish, you can find restaurants, bars and clubs. The most popular activities include diving and sailing.

Very often in local hotels you can meet the Israelis. There are several reasons for this. Firstly, rest in Taba is cheaper than in Eilat. Secondly, gambling is prohibited in Israel, while casinos are legalized in Hilton (Taba, Egypt).

Hilton

This comfortable hotel is located on the shores of the Gulf of Aqaba. From the windows of many rooms you can see its panorama. The hotel has a beach on which there is everything necessary for a great holiday. The rooms here are spacious and comfortable, they have minibars, climate control, cable TV. Tourists who choose this hotel note that here you can attend classes at the diving school, go for a massage or relax in the club. In local restaurants you can taste traditional oriental dishes, try Italian food.

Sofitel

Sofitel is another upscale hotel in Taba (Egypt). Reviews about him are only positive. So, tourists note that the rooms offer picturesque views of the bay and the Sinai Mountains. The hotel has a beach, several pools, playgrounds, a casino, and a golf course. You can rent a kayak, bicycles or water skiing. All rooms have en suite bathrooms and spacious balconies.

Beaches

Tourists who at least once visited local beaches, note in their reviews their cleanliness. It is believed that they are the best in Taba: they are environmentally friendly, not polluted, access to the sea is gentle and comfortable, despite the fact that corals grow on the coast. Everyone can go diving. In total, the resort has three diving centers called Werner Lau, Red Sea WaterWorld and Aqua Sport. In them you can get advice from professional divers. The water in Taba is clear and clean, so divers can easily see the most picturesque corners of the amazing underwater world.

Excursions

Holidays in Taba (Egypt) can not be complete without excursions, because near the resort there are a large number of attractions. Almost all tourists visit the monastery of St. Catherine, Mount Moses. Where can I get from Taba?

  • To Israel The tour lasts no more than two days. During this time, tourists can visit magnificent places, such as the Garden of Gethsemane, the Temple of all religions, the Wailing Wall and Mount of Olives. And, of course, you will visit Jerusalem, which attracts travelers from around the world.
  • To Jordan. During the day spent here, you can visit the city of Petra, as well as walk along the Jordan.
  • On the Pharaohs is an island that impresses with its scale and picturesqueness. Around it is a coral reef, which is of great interest to divers. In the distant past, it served as the Phoenician port, and after some time became the property of the Sultan Saladdin. The fortress of Salah El-Din has been preserved on its territory. There is a museum here, so the island will be interesting from a historical point of view.

sights

According to reviews, Taba (Egypt) is famous for its attractions. These include places such as:

  • Zaman Castle, which is now a huge entertainment center.
  • Taba Fjord is a place that does not leave indifferent any diver.
  • White and Colored Canyons are natural attractions that are considered unique.
  • Mount Moses and the monastery of St. Catherine. These two objects are located closest to Taba from all of the above. To get into them, you need to overcome about 180 km.

How to get there

Taba (Egypt) is a fairly popular resort, so many agencies offer tours here. The largest Russian tour operators are among them. To get to Taba, you can take a plane ticket to the local Sharm El Sheikh International Airport. There you will be met by a bus, which in three hours will bring to the resort city.
