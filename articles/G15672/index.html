Why vacationers choose the Alexander Park (Ulyanovsk)

You can relax and feel at the resort without leaving the city. To do this, just visit the Alexander Park (Ulyanovsk). It was equipped with everything necessary so that guests of this urban "oasis" could completely relax.

Description of the park

In 2007, local residents waited for the opening of the promising recreation area "Alexander Park" (Ulyanovsk). The photos show that this complex is located in a picturesque place and is suitable for relaxation at any time of the year.



This territory occupies 15 hectares and is guarded around the clock. Observation cameras are installed around the entire perimeter, and there are security guards who monitor order. The territory is regularly cleaned, so the Alexander Park (Ulyanovsk) resembles a clean green oasis.

The park is like a European resort town with many fountains, sculptures and alleys that are beautifully illuminated at night. In the summer, there are many young people who ride on smooth tracks on skates and skateboards. Strolling through the park, you can stay in a cozy cafe, bar or restaurant. If you wish, you can relax on a bench, which is enough here or in a cozy gazebo. The park has a man-made pond with a paid beach.



Vacationers with kids can go to the Summer restaurant, where there is a playground for children and a cute living area. The park has an equipped children's town.

Free parking spaces are available here.

Hotel in the park

Alexander Park (Ulyanovsk) invites you to relax not only locals, but also guests from other cities. You can comfortably stay in the five-star Hotel Imperial Club Deluxe. It is located in a picturesque place in a park area by the pond. On the territory there is a swimming pool, spa-center, solarium. If you need to conduct a business meeting, the hotel has several conference rooms. The local restaurant is great for organizing banquets. In the exhibition center you can order the exhibition. The hotel has a cozy bar.

Alexander Park (Ulyanovsk): a beach for guests

In the hot season, to have a good time, many come to a beautiful pond. The area for swimming is small in size, but many enjoy spending the whole day here. It is worth noting that the beach is paid, although you do not need to buy a ticket for entering the Alexander Park itself (Ulyanovsk). Prices for relaxing on the beach are low. In 2015, entrance fees had to be paid 200 rubles per person, but upon exit 100 rubles were returned.



When paying for the entrance to the hand, a plastic bracelet is given so that vacationers can calmly go out and return to the beach all day. The number of times is unlimited, but each time you return at the entrance, the bracelet on your hand is checked with magnetic tape. You can only go through the checkpoint, since the place is fenced with a neat wooden fence.

The whole beach is dotted with clean sand mixed with small pebbles. The entrance to the water is comfortable, gentle. In the center of a small pond there is a beautiful fountain. There are buoys in the water for which swimming is not permitted. At a reservoir the rescue workers are constantly on duty.

The atmosphere is completely conducive to relaxation and resembles European modern resorts.

In winter, when the surface of the pond freezes, an ice rink opens here.

Additional amenities on the beach

The beach area is ennobled. There are convenient locker rooms. Large arbors are placed near the fence so that vacationers can comfortably eat and sit. There are several large umbrellas installed throughout the territory. But since there are many visitors, places in the shade are usually occupied. Sun rental is available.

Those who wish can rent a catamaran or a boat and swim to the fountain, which is located in the center of the reservoir.



There is a small snack bar in the fenced area. The assortment includes drinks, french fries, barbecue.

For the rest, dry closets are installed, which are regularly cleaned.

For newlyweds

Alexander Park (Ulyanovsk) - a suitable place for wedding photo shoots. There are beautiful bridges and arbors, which have become a real attribute of modern weddings.

Since 2014, new rules have been in force in the park. Wedding processions can enter the protected area subject to one of the following conditions:

  • If you have ordered a banquet in the restaurant "Dynasty", "Romanovsky" or "Beluga".
  • If you have booked a room or a painting at the Imperial Club Deluxe Hotel.
  • If the buffet is booked in a summer restaurant or park.

Without a reservation, the entrance of the wedding procession to the territory is prohibited.
