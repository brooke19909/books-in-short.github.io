Acacia Palms Resort 4 *: hotel reviews

If you have chosen Goa (India) for your next vacation, and are looking for an option for comfortable accommodation on the coast, then as one of the options we suggest you consider Acacia Palms Resort 4 *.

This hotel is located on the seashore, a few hundred meters from the municipal beach. Margao is 8 km from the property. You can get to it by taxi or by bus. A taxi rank and public transport are located next to the hotel. Colva Airport is fourteen kilometers from the hotel. In the vicinity of Acacia Palms Resort 4 * there are several cafes, restaurants, retail outlets, tour agencies, massage parlors and other tourist infrastructure facilities.

Acacia Palms Resort 4 * (Goa): reviews of Russian travelers

Each experienced tourist will tell you that any trip abroad should be prepared in advance. Especially when it comes to the country and the place where you have not been. After all, this approach will avoid a lot of possible difficulties and problems, and you can seriously save on early hotel booking and buying tickets. Experienced travelers are advised to pay special attention to the choice of hotel. In this process, it is best to focus not only on official information and advice of tour operators, but also on the feedback of those who have recently happened to visit this or that place. After all, this will allow you to learn firsthand about both the pros and cons of a particular hotel. Today we invite you to familiarize yourself with the generalized comments of tourists from Russia regarding their vacation at Acacia Palms Resort 4 *. Reviews of our compatriots about this hotel, I must say, the vast majority are positive. But we offer to learn more about all the moments that new guests will have to face.



Housing stock

In general, the guests of the hotel were satisfied with the rooms. According to our compatriots, the apartments here are spacious, clean, bright. Furniture and appliances can not be called new and modern, but all of good quality and in working condition. If something fails, immediately notify the administrator at the reception. Very soon all problems in your room will be fixed. The large furnished balconies with a pleasant view pleased the tourists. Here you can have a good time contemplating the surroundings and listening to the sound of the surf. Also a big plus, according to vacationers, is the fact that both in the rooms and throughout the hotel there is access to wireless Internet. And you can use Wi-Fi for free. The connection speed is not always high, but in general, our compatriots were satisfied.

Cleaning service

No complaints about the work of the maids of Acacia Palms Resort 4 * (India, Goa) were found in the reviews of Russian travelers. So, according to vacationers, cleaning was carried out regularly and fairly high quality. By the way, the maids in the hotel will drop by your room during the day to clean, and in the evening to find out if you need anything (prepare bed for bed, bring something, pick up trash, etc.) . Towels are changed every day. Bed linen, according to the guests, was updated once a week. Please also note that the bathroom in this hotel does not have bath accessories. Therefore, you have to grab them from home or buy on the spot. There is also no hairdryer in the rooms. But the contents of the sets for making tea and coffee, the maids will replenish daily.

As an additional service, hotel guests can order food in their room at any time (for a fee). But practically none of our compatriots used this opportunity.



Check in, departure

Despite the fact that at this hotel the checkout time (noon) is officially set, according to travelers, it is almost never observed. So, even if you arrive at the hotel early in the morning, then, most likely, you will be settled in the room almost immediately, without condemning you to many hours of waiting. The same goes for departure day. If you plan to leave the hotel only in the evening, then in most cases the hotel staff will allow you to stay in your apartment until the moment you leave. Moreover, the extension of the room will be free. Such an attentive approach to guests, of course, was appreciated by tourists from different countries.

Hotel Acacia Palms Resort 4 * offers its guests two schemes of catering: breakfast only and half board. Most of our compatriots preferred the first option. Also, several types of jam, butter, ketchup, milk, juices, tea and coffee were always available.

You can eat during the day both at the hotel and beyond. Mostly tourists preferred the second option. So, according to them, in the vicinity of the hotel there is a mass of cafes where you can eat tasty and inexpensive. Our compatriots assure that everyone here can find an institution to their taste.



Sea

The hotel is only a couple of hundred meters from the beach. So, as travelers say, you can walk to the sea in just a few minutes with an unhurried step. The beach here is big, clean, fine sand. Resting here, according to tourists, is a pleasure. On the beach there are many cafes and bars where you can eat and order a drink. Also, some of them have at their disposal sun loungers and umbrellas, offer visitors to use free Wi-Fi.
