Multicurrency cards: banks, conditions, opportunities and reviews. Multi-currency card "Sberbank"

Before traveling abroad, you should pre-purchase the currency. What exactly to pay by card or cash is a personal matter for everyone. The problem is that the commission for foreign currency cashless transactions is high, and running around buying dollars in exchangers is inconvenient. Especially for such cases, a new product has been developed - multicurrency cards.



Essence

It is unprofitable to pay with a ruble card abroad. Money is not debited from the account immediately, but after confirmation of the operation. During this time, the exchange rate may change dramatically. You can draw up three cards - ruble, dollar and euro - and pay a service fee on them. But in this case, you have to constantly carry them with you and remember all the PIN codes. This is also inconvenient.

A multi-currency debit card is tied to several (usually three) accounts at once. The choice of currency during the transaction occurs automatically. In the USA, funds will be debited from the dollar account, in Europe, settlements will be made in euros, and in Russia - in rubles. If the account currency differs from the three available, then - depending on the bank rules - the funds from the main account will be debited taking into account the conversion fee.

Multicurrency cards are issued by the international payment systems MasterCard and VISA. There are no problems with the class either. The client can apply for Maestro, Classic, Platinum, Gold, etc.



For whom

Multicurrency cards are very popular among tourists and business people. But such an average plastic does not hurt the average citizen. You can constantly use the account in rubles, and before traveling abroad to buy dollars or euros.

Benefits

  • Universality. When issuing a card for several currencies, it is enough to pay a service fee once, write a reissue application and remember the PIN code.
  • Convenience of calculations. If there are not enough funds on the balance sheet to pay the bill, another currency will be debited taking into account the conversion. If you connect to Internet banking, you can conduct currency transactions online. For payments on the Internet, you can also use multi-currency cards. Banks that provide this product develop interesting offers.

But if the account balance is, say, 1 ruble, 1 dollar and 200 euros, then the amount will be converted. To the existing 1 ruble, a converted dollar will be added at the rate of 1:32. To avoid double conversion, you need to maintain an account balance with the base currency. In the example considered, this is a dollar.

Not all banks issue such cards. Among the existing options, prices vary greatly. Some organizations draw up a card for free, but charge large transaction fees. disadvantages

Multi-currency cards are not beneficial to every client. For two years of service, you will have to pay a minimum of 1000 rubles, which is 2-3 times higher than the same rate for ordinary plastics. Currency exchange is carried out at the bank's commercial rate, which is significantly lower than the market rate.

Reviews

On the forums you can find a large number of user reviews of VTB multicurrency cards. Most of them relate to the exchange rate when converting funds. Consider one example. Conclusion

Customer reviews state that the VTB 24 multicurrency card is a useful tool for frequent operations with dollars and euros. But in order not to overpay on the difference in rates and conversion, it is worth constantly monitoring the balance.
