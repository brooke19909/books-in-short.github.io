This is useful to know! How to check how many minutes are left at MTS?

Most of the modern tariffs presented at MTS require a package of free minutes, within which customers can make calls to the numbers of the MTS mobile operator (or in some cases to all Russian mobile operators). In order not to pay extra money, you must always keep track of the number of minutes remaining. How to do this is analyzed below.

Via the USSD command

How to check how many free minutes are left on MTS without going online? Simply and easily! To do this, it is important to remember the following combinations of USSD commands, allowing you to get the information you need in just 5 seconds. To do this, the client needs to open the call menu on the phone (icon with a mobile handset) and dial the following characters: "*", then "100", then "*", then "1" and "#". After press the call button.

After typing the command, within 5 seconds, an incoming message from the operator will be received on the mobile phone, in which the remainder of the minutes will be indicated. In order to use your personal account on the MTS official website, you must enter a phone number and also create a password. Also, for the convenience of customers, the site has an online consultant window that will quickly answer any questions.
