Pensions and resorts of Nebug: photos and reviews of tourists

The resort village of Nebug, located fifteen kilometers from the city of Tuapse, has a developed tourist infrastructure and annually attracts many vacationers. After all, there are numerous health resorts, pensions and motels. Nebug is rightfully considered one of the best health resorts on the Black Sea.

Sanatorium "Yamal"

The health complex consists of six buildings, two of which are bedrooms, and a separate hotel "Lightning". In dormitories, vacationers can settle in cozy comfortable rooms of such categories as a room with amenities, a studio, and a suite. All rooms are equipped with modern appliances. They have a bathroom with a toilet. Guests go up to the floors with a high-speed elevator.





Beach vacation

Three hundred meters from the buildings is its own small pebble beach, which can be reached by panoramic elevator, walkway or via an underpass. The beach is equipped with changing cabins, showers. Lovers of water recreation are offered rides and boat rental. There is also a diving school.

Treatment and entertainment

Sanatorium "Yamal" (Nebug) specializes in the treatment of a wide range of diseases. In addition to medical procedures, vacationers have the opportunity to visit the pools, which in the health resort there are two, and a bath complex. For sports lovers there are well-equipped sports fields, a football field. Of the entertainment available visiting restaurants, bars. Beer lovers should enjoy a foamy drink made in their own brewery. There is also a night club. Children can have fun in the nursery under the supervision of a caregiver.

For a healthy family vacation, it is still better to choose a sanatorium. Nebug is a true health resort. To improve their well-being, thousands of tourists come here from all over Russia and the neighboring countries.

Reviews about the sanatorium

What do vacationers say? Tourists note the following positive points:

  1. Great food.
  2. Qualified Doctors.
  3. Clean beach.
  4. Developed infrastructure.
  5. Good conditions for children.

Sanatorium "Zorka"

The resort is located in a picturesque park area and receives visitors all year round. Both adults and parents with children can relax in it. A spa camp is available for younger guests.

In the health resort, vacationers are offered a wide selection of treatment options for diseases of the cardiovascular system, digestive organs. Sanatorium "Zorka" (Nebug) is equipped with a complex of modern medical equipment. There are also excellent opportunities for recreation and sports.

The guests are housed in an eight-story building, designed to accommodate two hundred and fifty people. Four stone buildings for one hundred and fifty places were built to accommodate children.



The list of entertainment includes a visit to the sauna, pool, restaurant and bar. For practicing various sports there are equipped playgrounds and a football field. For children there is a children's room, a slot machine room. Kids can also spend time on the playground.

The resort has its own pebble, well-equipped beach. The safety of staying on it and on the territory of the entire complex is ensured by round-the-clock duty of rescuers and professional security. For guests arriving by car, there is free parking. Three meals a day in the local dining room are included in the price.

Sanatorium "Nebug"

Directly in Tuapse is another famous health resort, which bears the same name with the resort village. Sanatorium "Nebug" (Tuapse) offers a range of medical services typical of such institutions. All kinds of entertainment are also available for guests (visiting the pool, saunas, organizing excursions and much more).





A feature of this sanatorium, which distinguishes it from all other similar places of recreation, is the presence of professional animation programs offered daily for vacationers. The resort has its own well-equipped beach, which can be reached by electric car. For all kinds of special occasions there is a modern conference room.

Health Resort "Lightning"

Sanatorium "Lightning" (Nebug) is part of the resort complex "Gazprom Yamal". Vacationers are located in a seven-story building. The housing stock has two hundred and sixty-nine rooms. There are rooms of the first category, studios and suites, as well as two-room apartments. All rooms have a bathroom with a toilet. Studio and luxury rooms have balconies with sea views. All rooms are equipped with modern appliances, air conditioning. For self-catering, there are built-in kitchens. A good mood among vacationers is created due to the presence of aquariums with fish in the apartments.





Guests are offered two meals a day in the Bylina restaurant, organized according to the buffet principle. In addition, guests can visit the restaurant "Galaxy", a nightclub "Legend" and a lobby bar.

The list of entertainments offered for vacationers is similar to the offers of local sanatoriums and includes a standard set in the form of a visit to the pool, sauna, sports in equipped areas.

For those who decided not only to have a good rest, but also to improve their health, you should definitely come to Nebug. Sanatoriums and pensions here are some of the best in the Krasnodar Territory, as many reviews say.

Pension "Black Sea"

The boarding house is located in the center of the village. The stay in it will be remembered by the guests due to the unique atmosphere of coziness and comfort created by the work of qualified personnel. The rooms are decorated in a classic style and feature mostly warm colors.





All apartments are equipped with beautiful and comfortable wooden furniture. A comfortable atmosphere is created thanks to the presence of modern household appliances. Tea facilities are provided free of charge.

Vacationers have the opportunity to do their favorite sports. Children can spend time on the playground. Nearby is the local pebble beach and all kinds of attractions (water park, dolphinarium, ice palace). Excursions are organized for vacationers, animation and show programs are held.

Reviews of tourists indicate that the resort village has a developed infrastructure of recreation and entertainment. Numerous resorts of Nebug provide an extensive range of medical and recreational services. All of them are equipped with the most modern medical equipment, and qualified staff is always ready to provide the necessary assistance or advice. When deciding to purchase a ticket to one of them, it is worth consulting with a doctor.



All kinds of entertainment for vacationers are not the main activity, but serve to achieve the main goal - to strengthen the health of guests. Ample opportunities are available at local resorts for children. Therefore, parents who wish to improve the health of their children can be advised to purchase a ticket to one of the health resorts.

Some resorts of Nebug can be visited year-round.
