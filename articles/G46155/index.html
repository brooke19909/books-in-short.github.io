Five patterns of culottes

Culottes today are an integral attribute of every fashionista. Girls like wide trousers because they do not restrict movement and visually correct the figure. This article contains the 5 most relevant patterns of culottes.

Classic



Pants-culottes, the pattern of which is presented above, can be sewn even by a beginner needlewoman. There is nothing complicated here, but it is impossible to miscalculate the size. If the girl makes the pants a little wider than she planned, then this defect can always be attributed to the style. In general, the wider the culottes, the more feminine they look. Sewing classic pants of this style follows from a dark material. But its density can vary, depending on the season in which it is supposed to wear pants.

Getting to the manufacture of the product. First you need to print a pattern of culottes, and then scale it to fit your parameters. Be sure to immediately determine the length of the pants. This will reduce tissue consumption. We cut out the details and begin to stitch them. First you should make a side seam. Be sure to leave a hole for inserting pockets. Once the legs are ready, they can be sewn together. Now you should stitch the belt and pockets. Last of all, you should hem the legs.

Business



The pattern of culottes of this model is not very different from the classic style. But there is still a difference. It consists in the best fit of the trousers at the waist. Thanks to this, shirts and blouses can be tucked into culottes, and during the meeting the girl will be sure that the clothes look perfect.

How to make such pants? The first step is to scale the pattern on the computer, and then print it. Having decided on the size, you need to cut out the details from the material. Business culottes are sewn mainly from dark-colored suit fabric. If there is any pattern on the material, then it is not pronounced and does not strike the eye at all. When the parts are cut, you can start sewing the product. First you need to sew all the darts in the belt. Then you should collect the legs, laying them in the side seams. Now you need to sew the culottes into a single product, supplement them with a belt and a fly. Be sure to hem the bottom edges of the legs.

Shortened



The pattern of culottes posted above will help the reader to sew the product for summer or autumn holidays. In these pants it is convenient to go for a walk or go for barbecue. They are free, comfortable and have large patch pockets. Sewing such a product is not difficult. If you sew culottes for the summer, give preference to a light material with a bright pattern.

We translate the pattern onto paper and cut out 5 parts for sewing pants and 2 for pockets. The first step is to connect the two parts of the legs. Now you need to sew a pocket on each of them. After that, you should sew the legs together and attach a belt to them. The final action is the filing of the product.

Sports



It is difficult to make a pattern of culottes with your own hands. But to print and fit your size is very simple. So, if you decide to sew a fashionable new thing, you can use the pattern attached above. These trousers will have a sporty look, but going to them is by no means for fitness. In such pants you can go to a club or a restaurant. Fashionable new thing should be sewn their cotton fabric with the addition of synthetics.

Let's get started. The first step is to increase the pattern to the desired size and print it. Now you need to cut out the details. Pockets and bottom linings for pants can be made of a material different from the color of the base. We begin to assemble the product. First we sew the legs. Then you need to grind pockets to them. Now you should sew the legs together. We sew the elastic into the product - and the culottes are ready. According to this pattern, you can sew a less extravagant model. U-turns should be removed and the legs should be hemmed in the classic way. True, in this version, pockets are best cut from the material of the pants, and not from another fabric.

Pantaloons Skirt



Although these trousers are considered to be pants, they look incredibly feminine. Probably because at first glance it is difficult to determine what is worn on the girl. How to sew culottes with your own hands? The pants pattern of this style is attached above. In order to build yourself such a new thing, you will need flying fabric. Moreover, it should be quite dense so as not to glow. We print out the pattern and cut the details along the oblique. Yes, the fabric on the pants will take a lot. If you want to reduce its consumption, make the pants shorter. After all the details are cut, you can start sewing. We grind off the trouser legs and lay the tucks in them. To make this decorative detail look more interesting, iron them a little. Stitch the legs between each other, but leave a place under the zipper. Thanks to her, skirt pants will fit perfectly at the waist. Sew on a belt. If you plan to wear trousers with a belt, then you need to make loops for it, otherwise it is not necessary.
