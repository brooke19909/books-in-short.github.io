Corallia Beach Hotel Apartments (Cyprus / Paphos): description and reviews

Corallia Beach Hotel Apartments is a good 3-star hotel located near the center of Paphos. Many people come here for impressions. Travelers make a choice in favor of this hotel for several reasons. And here we should talk about them in more detail.





Location

Well, this is the first criterion that travelers are guided by when deciding on a hotel. Corallia Beach Hotel Apartments is located on one of the best beaches in Cyprus - on Coral Bay. It is such because its coast is covered with soft and fine sand of light yellow color with a reddish tint. The vast majority of the beaches of Cyprus and Paphos are covered with a mixture of pebbles and shell rock. Many people do not find this surface comfortable.

So the beach, which can be reached in just a minute, is the first reason why the hotel became popular. The second is located in close proximity to the hotel airport. It can be reached in half an hour. And the port of Limassol is only 45 minutes away. Kato Paphos (lower part of the city) is located 12 kilometers from the hotel. And right next door were numerous taverns, souvenir stalls and grocery stores.

Interestingly, the notorious beach is marked with the EU Blue Flag, which is awarded places for cleanliness and good, safe water for swimming.

Services

Corallia Beach Hotel Apartments provides guests with various services. If guests are freezing, then they can offer a central heating connection. Bridal suites, family rooms and facilities for disabled people are also available.

The territory has its own mini-market with food, water and other goods, as well as a souvenir shop. There is also free private parking, car and bicycle rental, luggage storage, tour desk, currency exchange, conference room and a 24-hour reception desk.

In addition to the above, there is dry cleaning, laundry and ironing. They may also offer to clean the guests' shoes.





Recreation and entertainment

Corallia Beach Hotel Apartments has everything that can brighten up leisure travelers. Everyone here will find something to their liking. There is a games room and a library, a pool bar, a beach with sun loungers and umbrellas, a SPA center (beauty and health treatments are available), a massage room, a sauna and a fitness room with all the necessary equipment for a full workout. Many guests enjoy spending time by the outdoor pool or on the terrace.

In addition to all of the above, Corallia Beach Hotel Apartments 4 * provides guests with the opportunity to engage in water sports, snorkelling and diving. Minigolf, horse riding, bowling, cycling, billiards and tennis, fishing, and canoeing are also available. In general, entertainment for every taste.

By the way, in the evening, Corallia Beach Hotel Apartments 4 * (Paphos) hosts various interesting shows and animations. And for children there is a separate playground.

Guests staying

Corallia Beach Hotel Apartments gets mostly positive reviews. The first thing people who have visited here is to note the fact that the staff working in the hotel speak several languages, namely: French, English, Greek, German, Bulgarian, Romanian and, of course, Russian.

Naturally, the location of the hotel was also not disregarded. The beach is understandable, but the guests also liked the fact that there is a bus stop nearby with free buses that take people to the zoo. And along the way you can admire the exotic local vegetation. There is also a stop nearby, from where buses go to Paphos. So for transport accessibility this place also received a plus from the guests.

By the way, guests with children paid attention to the services of nannies, a children's menu, and also that in restaurants, if you ask, they can bring a special chair. Here they treat this with understanding.
