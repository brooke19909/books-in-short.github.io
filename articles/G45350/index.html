Otrada Beach, Odessa: service, reviews

"Ah, Odessa" - these words of admiration from the famous song of Modest Tabachnikov became so popular that they are often used by tourists who choose South Palmyra as their summer vacation destination at sea.

In Odessa there is something to see, but the real attraction is its beaches, which remain clean, well-groomed and hospitable in a large industrial city.

Description of Joy Beach

The coast of Odessa extends for 30 km, 20 of which are sandy beaches. Some of them are artificial bulk coastlines that were built to protect against landslides. The famous "Joy" beach belongs to them. Odessa has only a few coasts of natural origin, including Luzanovka and Chernomorka.

The swimming season in the city lasts from May to September, but seasoned Odessa residents open it from April. One of the most loved by local people and tourists is the beach "Joy" (Odessa). Getting to it will not be difficult even for visitors. It is located just 20 minutes from the train station if you walk. On the fifth tram, you can get to the area of ​​the Musical Comedy Theater. Vodyanogo and get off at the Pirogovskaya stop.





According to the townspeople, the Otrada beach (Odessa) is the cleanest and most well-groomed, therefore, at the height of the season, some vacationers indicate in its reviews its crowdedness as a drawback.

In this part of the coast there is clean fine sand, a gently sloping bottom with a gradual increase in depth, for which families with children love this place.

A rather steep descent leads to the beach, a staircase is built to overcome it, but for lazy or tired visitors there is the opportunity to take advantage of one of the city's attractions: the only cable car.

Highlights of Joy Beach

It is worth sitting in the chair of the cable car on the beach "Joy", as soon as you want to exclaim: "Ah, Odessa!", Such a beautiful view opens onto the bay and the surrounding green zone. Built in 1971, it is considered a real highlight of the city.

The descent begins near the film studio to them. Dovzhenko, well known to all lovers of Soviet cinema (the films Vertical, The Green Van, Cossack Robbers, and many others).





Ticket price is 25 UAH. per person one way, but the trip is worth it:

  • Firstly, delivery directly to the beach takes only 5 minutes.
  • Secondly, during this time you can view the coast and enjoy the sea panorama.
  • Thirdly, this short walk through the air will bring real pleasure to both children and adults.

Another highlight highlighting the beach "Joy" (Odessa) among others, is a large block of yellow stone with the inscription Villa Otrada. Not far from it, an underground river found its way to the surface, which spews its cold clear waters right on the coast. Many residents of Odessa and guests of the city sincerely believe that the stream has powerful energy, which gives a cleansing and healing effect, so it is a pleasure to splash in it, despite the temperature of the water.

Infrastructure and beach prices

Another plus, which is famous for the beach "Joy" (Odessa) - prices. This is one of the most affordable recreational areas by the sea.

Entrance to the coast is free, guests have at their disposal changing cabins, but some of the services are paid:

  • a chaise lounge will cost 50 UAH. in a day;
  • the same price for a beach umbrella;
  • numerous restaurants and cafes offer fast food, soft drinks and beer (barbecue will cost 100 UAH., and a glass of foamy drink from 20 UAH.);
  • entrance to the paid territory costs 50 UAH .;
  • toilet 7 UAH

For reference: 1 hryvnia is 2.43 rubles. Vacationers have at their disposal free sports and children's playgrounds, a first-aid post, shops, bicycle rental (the Otrada beach (Odessa) is included in the “health trails” route), catamarans and many other services and entertainment.





Night life on the coast of Odessa is no less interesting than sunbathing and sea bathing.

Bright Life: Treasure Island

As in all resort towns, on the Odessa coast, life does not stop either day or night in the summer season. On the streets, advertising tables are full of posters with announcements of performances by show business stars in numerous nightclubs on the beaches.

The Treasure Island beach club on Otrada beach in Odessa is always full of visitors thanks to its entertainment programs, delicious cuisine and excellent service.





During the day, vacationers have 4 bars with bar counters located directly on the shore. Here guests are offered cocktails from professional bartenders and a large selection of snacks, while light music and the sound of the surf make the Admiral Benbow bar very popular among people of different ages.

In "Treasure Island" every Thursday, Friday and at the weekend concerts are held with the performance of local and visiting musicians, DJs. Characteristic of this night club is that life in its main building does not calm down with the end of the beach season.

Yacht Club

The famous Yacht Club "Joy" gave yachting a lot of talented skippers, and boat trips in these places were popular even during the time of the Duke de Richelieu. Today the yacht club offers its customers a wide range of services:

  • yacht rental for exploring the Odessa Gulf and the Crimean coast;
  • travel outside Ukraine;
  • boat rental.

Guests of Odessa can use the services of the club from May to September to go on a trip or spend a romantic evening on one of the yachts. A wedding or a photo shoot on the high seas can also become the crown of relaxation in a beautiful city.

Hotels on the beach "Joy"

Not only the clear sea, gentle sun, hot sand and rich nightlife can offer its guests the beach "Joy" (Odessa). Reviews of vacationers note a large selection of hotels located in close proximity to the coast with a pricing policy suitable for people of different incomes. Among them:



  • Hotel "Black Sea" ("Joy") 4 * is located just a hundred meters from the beach of the same name. All 38 rooms of the hotel overlook the Odessa Gulf. In the summer, they are protected by a mosquito net, which allows you to sleep to the sound of the surf. The hotel has a restaurant, bar, tennis court, pool, sauna and a cozy terrace. Room rates from 600 UAH. per day.
  • Hotel "Joy" 5 * has its own beach, which is accessible only to guests. Its 26 rooms are decorated in Italian style. The hotel infrastructure includes 2 restaurants, a bar, a conference room and a beach with a yacht club. The price of accommodation per day is from 1 300 UAH.

The average cost of accommodation in hotels near the beach "Joy" is from 200 UAH. in "Vityaz" and up to 1,500 UAH. in the Mona Lisa.

Reason to return

Odessa is admired by some people, while others have some grumbling nitpicking, but all unanimously claim that it is the most picturesque city in Ukraine. The same reviews sound about the favorite city beach of Odessa "Joy".

Pure water and sand, a mysterious underground river, it is not known where it originates, numerous cozy cafes, bars and shops on the shore, the cable car - all this inspires vacationers.





The negative aspects include its crowded nature and the infrequent appearance of algae near the coast. Otherwise, this is a great place to stay with well-developed infrastructure and at an affordable price. Therefore, the beach "Joy" in Odessa is an occasion to return here on vacation more than once.
