Hotel "Severnaya" (Kirovsk, Murmansk region): photos and reviews

It is worth noting that the Severnaya Hotel (Kirovsk) is one of the best in the region. Here you will get access not only to comfortable rooms, but also to a huge number of infrastructure facilities.

Brief description of the hotel

Hotel Severnaya (Kirovsk) is an excellent option for lovers of comfort. Speaking of rooms, it is worth noting their cozy interior, decorated in bright colors. The furniture is made of dark wood, which, as it were, brings guests back a couple of centuries ago. Nevertheless, the amenities in the apartment are quite modern. This is a TV, and a hairdryer, and a refrigerator, and much more. Thus, in your room you will find everything you need to feel no less comfortable than at home.

In addition to relaxation, guests of any hotel also need entertainment. Ample opportunities in this regard to its guests can provide the hotel "Severnaya" (Kirovsk). You can work out in the modern fitness center, and use the wireless Internet to always be in touch with friends and family. If you need the help of someone from the hotel staff, then you can always contact the reception, which works around the clock.



In order for the rest to be complete, food should also be appropriate. Hotel "Severnaya" (Kirovsk) invites you to its restaurant, where you can try traditional Russian and European dishes. A breakfast buffet awaits you with a huge assortment of delicious and wholesome food.

Hotel Location

Hotel "Severnaya" (Kirovsk, Murmansk region) is located in the city center. Its peculiarity can be considered the fact that in the immediate vicinity there are the most beautiful Khabin mountains, which provides a spectacular and picturesque view from the window. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel there is a shopping and entertainment center where you can enjoy shopping, watching movies and other entertainment. The road to the airport and train station will take you about an hour. And if you come for a thrill, then having overcome only one kilometer, you will be taken to a ski resort.

Hotel Rooms

The Severnaya Hotel (Kirovsk) has a relatively small number of rooms - there are only 41 of them, and they can accommodate about 75 people. So, the following accommodation options are available to you:

  • Standard rooms include 1, 2 and 3-bed accommodation. They are characterized by compactness and minimalism in design. There is also a small work desk (which, however, can be cosmetic), as well as a couple of chairs.
  • The superior apartments have more space and light. In addition, there is upholstered furniture, which serves both for relaxation and for accommodating additional guests.
  • Luxury rooms are the best in this hotel. They consist of two spacious and bright rooms. Taking into account folding soft furniture, this room can accommodate up to four guests.




Guest apartment features

Hotel "Severnaya" in Kirovsk offers its guests to use the following list of amenities that are available in each room:

  • the bathrooms of all apartments are equipped with a shower, an electric hairdryer, a set of bath accessories, as well as disposable cosmetic sets;
  • thanks to the TV, you can enjoy watching several dozens of cable channels in your free time;
  • each room has a landline telephone, which has access to both the city and international lines;
  • for a fee, the maids will fill your minibar daily with alcohol and refreshments;
  • in the refrigerator, your products will always be fresh.




Hotel Infrastructure

The director of the Severnaya hotel (Kirovsk) makes sure that guests feel comfortable not only in their rooms, but in the entire establishment as a whole. For this purpose, the following infrastructure was formed:

  • next to the hotel there is free parking with video surveillance, where each guest can leave his car;
  • so that you do not have to carry heavy bags and suitcases to the upper floors yourself, use the elevator;
  • your celebrations will be held at the highest level if you rent a banquet hall in a hotel;
  • for an additional fee you can visit the business center, where you will have access to all the necessary arsenal of office equipment;
  • for meetings and seminars there is a modern equipped conference room;
  • since there is a ski resort nearby, the obligatory object is a special place for storing equipment;
  • the restaurant offers a huge selection of delicious dishes, and the bar always has a lot of quality alcohol;
  • the entire hotel area is covered by a wireless Internet signal;
  • you can cash out credit cards using ATMs in the lobby;
  • reception around the clock at your service;
  • after a busy day, you will certainly want to take a steam bath in the sauna;
  • You can put things in order by contacting the laundry.




The list of services and services

In this hotel you can use the following services:

  • delivery of dishes from the restaurant to the room (this applies not only to breakfast, but also to orders made from the menu);
  • transportation of guests to the airport or railway station by their own hotel transport;
  • if necessary, you can contact the reception with a request to make copies of documents or send a message using fax;
  • all hotel apartments are connected to the central heating system;
  • a special room is provided for storing bulky luggage;
  • In addition to daily cleaning of the apartments, you can also call the maid in the room additionally.

Negative reviews

Speaking about the Severnaya Hotel, travelers note not only positive aspects, but also some disadvantages:

  • Having settled in the room, do not be surprised if some bulbs do not light;
  • the sconce above the bed is located directly above the head, and therefore it is inconvenient to read and there is always a risk of bumping;
  • although there is a free shuttle service to the mountain, you have to wait so long that it is easier to call a taxi;
  • there are no air conditioners in the rooms, and only a small window opens on the window, and therefore the room is incredibly stuffy;
  • the beds are very small, and therefore, if you have a large physique and high growth, then it will be very uncomfortable to sleep;
  • spiky springs stick out from the mattress;
  • the curtains on the windows are very short (above the window sill), and therefore they let in light and do not give you the opportunity to sleep longer in the morning;
  • the TV works very poorly - the image is snowing, but the sound hisses and is interrupted;
  • incredibly slippery tiles in the hall (it would not hurt to lay tracks);
  • the water from the tap flows very muddy - it would not hurt to clean the pipes.

General impression

Guests of Kirovsk highly value the Severnaya hotel, which is why there are always plenty of guests here, and rooms must be booked in advance. This is largely due to the incredibly convenient location. There are mountains with ski slopes nearby. By the way, the hotel provides a free shuttle to the descent. In addition, it is the central part of the city, which opens up many opportunities for entertainment and business.

The Severnaya Hotel (Kirovsk) provides its guests with excellent food. The restaurant of this restaurant is famous for simply excellent food (even red fish and other delicacies are on the menu). In the mornings, guests can enjoy a rich buffet. This restaurant is liked not only by hotel guests, but also by the townspeople, which in itself is a positive feature. The menu mainly contains European dishes, as well as traditional Russian cuisine.

It should be noted that the title of the best hotel in the region, this institution received a well-deserved. Despite the general simplicity in design, as well as in the list of services provided, it is incredibly comfortable and cozy here. In addition, one cannot help but praise the staff who are ready to help you at any time. Arriving here once, you will certainly want to return to this hotel again.
