Stunning Montenegro ski resorts

Montenegro is famous for its excellent winter resorts, which give the opportunity to go cross-country and mountain skiing. The most popular of them are Kolasin and Zabljak, located at the foot of the most beautiful mountain ranges.



These Montenegro ski resorts provide an excellent opportunity to spend time away from the hustle and bustle of civilization. Here, in addition to snowboarding and skiing, there are countless bars, cafes, restaurants with national and European cuisine, discos. A variety of sports and leisure activities are held at these resorts throughout the season.

When listing the ski resorts of Montenegro, it is necessary to note their location. For example, you can get to the city of Zabljak only by car. But Kolasin is more accessible, it is located next to the railway that connects Belgrade and Podgorica. Of great importance is a fairly advantageous transport connection: scheduled minibuses and a train. From Podgorica to Kolasin, the squad goes a little over an hour.

What is famous for the ski resorts of Montenegro

The largest ski center in the country, which opens the door to all fans of outdoor activities five months a year, is the resort of Zabljak. In this place there are 3 main ski slopes: Yavorovach, Stuots and Savin Kuk. For lovers of cross-country skiing there are tracks 3-12 kilometers long. Montenegro ski resorts reviews have the most favorable. People talk about how they were impressed by amazing places with incredibly beautiful nature and excellently developed service.





The Durmitor Center is located at the foot of the Savin Kuk ridge - one of the most magnificent peaks. Trails for different ages and skill levels are organized here. Most of them were located on the slopes of the ridge. The most difficult track (only experienced skiers can do it) reaches a length of more than 3000 meters and has a height difference of eight hundred meters, which starts almost from the top. For other routes, the length is from one hundred meters to three and a half kilometers. There are also ski slopes designed for children and beginners who want to improve their skills.

The best ski resorts in Montenegro: Kolasin

Kolasin is a small town with a population of about five thousand people. It is surrounded by the mountains of Sinyaevin, Belasitsa, Vuche and Klyuch. On Belasitsa Mountain, the ski season is organized from November to May. Here the snow cover reaches 3 meters, and the grassy slopes make it possible to ski safely with a small thickness of snow.

Reading reviews about the ski resorts of Montenegro, you can also find out that this center is surrounded on all sides by forest and mountains, in other words, it is in a certain hollow, due to which it is protected from the winds, this makes ski entertainment very comfortable.
