Kyzylorda hotels: addresses and reviews

Kyzylorda is a city in Kazakhstan, located on both banks of the Syr Darya River and remote at 830 km from Astana. Until 1862, this settlement was called Fort Petrovsky. Until 1925 it was called Ak-Mosque. Since 1925, the city was given the name Kyzyl-Orda, which in translation into Russian means "Red Army". And since 1997, it became officially called Kyzylorda. City hotels will provide tourists with a warm welcome.
