Gorgeous "African flowers" crochet (master class knitting gift needles)

Many experienced craftswomen say that they love crocheting "African flowers".

These are not literal flowers. So-called parts that can be used to create almost any complex or not very complex thing. These motifs are like mosaic fragments from which magnificent finished products are composed. This article will help you learn how to crochet an "African flower" on your own. The sequence diagrams are presented clearly in the photographs. Tie the needle bar using this adorable pattern.

Crochet an "African flower" (needle bar master class)

It has long been noted that if you create a specific thing, then the work on the development of new technology becomes more fruitful. Skills are acquired faster, and besides, there is no feeling of the gift of time spent for knitting out a sample. We suggest you learn how to make a very pretty needle-bed, which you can easily finish literally in one evening, without even looking up from watching your favorite series. In addition, as you work, you will be able to understand the basic principles of how to crochet "African flowers", and in the future you will be able to decorate your work with these exotic motifs.

  • Prepare a hook of 3.5 mm and the remains of multi-colored yarn.
  • You will also need soft material for filling the needle bed.
