Hotel Nova Park Hotel Side 5 * Turkey: overview, description and reviews of tourists

Tourists who once visited the Nova Park Hotel Side 5 * often wish to return to its walls at least once more. This is due to the fact that this place for recreation provides excellent living conditions, as well as a high level of service, which will certainly bribe any, even the most demanding tourist.



Location Nova Park Hotel Side 5 *

So, more details. Hotel Nova Park Hotel Side 5 * (Turkey / Side) is located almost on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea, in a tropical climate zone, so that its guests can enjoy hot sunny days throughout the rest. The air temperature here is perfectly softened under the influence of the air masses of the Mediterranean - thanks to this, travelers staying here do not languish from the heat.

As for transport links, guests arriving at Antalya International Airport will have to cover a distance of 65 kilometers in order to reach their destination. Near the hotel is located one of the largest entertainment centers in Turkey - the city of Side. Many guests go to this village in order to visit local night clubs, cafes, restaurants, bars and karaoke. In addition, as most of them note, this area is rich in historical and architectural attractions, which are also interesting to study.

Nova Park Hotel Side 5 * was built in 1998, but at the moment its building and territory have a very modern look, which was achieved thanks to reconstruction work carried out in 2017 - at this time the climate system was completely updated and the interior of the rooms was improved. Rooms

In Nova Park Hotel Side 5 * in total, there are 326 comfortable apartments. Each of them has a set of furniture necessary for a comfortable stay, as well as a small amount of electronics. All rooms are divided into two categories: Standard and Family.

All rooms have a private bathroom with a shower, as well as a wash basin and a bathroom. It also comes with full bath amenities, bathrobes, slippers and large bath towels. All rooms, regardless of their categories, have a separate air conditioning, with which you can adjust the temperature in the room to the optimum.

"Standard"

305 apartments in Nova Park Hotel Side 5 * - Standard room. They consist of one room, whose area is 18 square meters. m. All furniture installed in this territory is designed for a couple of guests and, if necessary, one child.

The furniture has a large double bed and one for children, which is designed for children under the age of 12 years - for its provision you do not need to pay extra. Guests can place their things in freestanding cabinets made of dark wood, as well as on the cabinets. If necessary, guests can use the safe, minibar and telephone - their use is paid. Each room has a large plasma TV with satellite channels, as well as a small refrigerator.

Guests of the Standard-class apartments can enjoy beautiful views of the gardens and the sea, which opens from the balcony.

For people with disabilities, the hotel has four rooms that are specially organized based on the characteristics of the disabled. They are furnished with furniture, which was made to order, plumbing is also special.

"Family"

There are not many rooms of the "Family" group in the hotel - only 21. The territory of each of them is 35 square meters. m. and is divided into two separate rooms, which are bedrooms. They are equipped with one double bed of European type, covered with orthopedic mattresses and expensive linens. These rooms accommodate a total of 4 adults. These apartments are ideal for staying with friends or one big family.

As for the furniture equipment of the apartments, it is fully consistent with what is available in the Standard room, but is designed for a larger number of people. The family rooms also have a balcony and personal air conditioning.

Nutrition

Vacationers at Nova Park Hotel Side 5 * (Side) often share a lot of positive impressions received as a result of visiting the catering facilities that are available at the hotel. Beach

The coastline of the hotel is literally 200 meters from its building. In order to get on it, tourists need to overcome the promenade.

Tourists in their comments about the rest at the Nova Park Hotel note that its beach is always clean and beautiful, from here you can admire the beautiful natural landscapes. The entire beach area is covered with fine sand, which is also available at the entrance to the sea. By the way, most travelers are happy to respond to a convenient descent into the water.

There are several dozens of sun loungers throughout the beach area, each of which has large umbrellas. There are also several public toilets and locker rooms. If necessary, vacationers can use showers.

There are also many entertainments along the coastline, moreover, they are designed for different tastes and different needs of guests. Here you can go water skiing or jet skiing, learn surfing skills or take a ride on a rented yacht.
