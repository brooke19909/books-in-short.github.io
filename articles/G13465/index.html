Smartphone design

Reviews of the HTC Desire 620G DUAL SIM indicate that it has much in common with its predecessor, the Desire 610. Both the first gadget and the second case are made of familiar plastic with a good matte finish. The difference is only in size. The 620G screen size is the most common 5 inches, and 610 - 4.7 inches. Hence the difference in the dimensions of smartphones. The newer device has a length and width of 150.1 and 72.7 mm, respectively, with a thickness of 9.6 mm and a weight of 145 grams. But at 610 these parameters are somewhat different: length and width - respectively - 143.1 and 70.5 millimeters, the thickness is still the same 9.6 millimeters, and the weight is 144 grams. Above the display in the 620G are the front camera and a number of sensor elements. Also here is one of the speakers. The second one is located above the bottom of the device. Here is the manufacturer‚Äôs logo and three familiar touch buttons that control the device. A small round hole of the spoken microphone and a microUSB port are displayed on the very bottom edge. On the upper side there is a 3.5 mm port for connecting a wired speaker system. On the back cover, in addition to the manufacturer‚Äôs logo, there is a main camera, an auxiliary microphone, which provides suppression of external noise during a call, and LED backlight.





Processor solution

One of the strengths is rightfully considered the CPU in HTC Desire 620G. Reviews highlight this feature. It contains a real 8-core MT6592, which was developed by MediaTek, the second largest manufacturer of AWP processors. Each of its modules operates in peak load mode at a frequency of 1.7 GHz and is based on the most energy-efficient architecture at the moment - A7. The increased clock frequency in comparison with the base models (1.7 GHz versus 1.3 GHz) provides a normal level of performance in single-threaded applications. Well, in multi-threaded software MT6592 feels just fine. Still, 8 cores make themselves felt. As a result, any, including the most demanding, application will run on this smartphone.

Graphics

A perfect complement to the CPU in HTC Desire 620G DUAL SIM is a graphics adapter. It is equipped with a Mali-450 MP4 video accelerator. Its computing resources, like MT6592, can solve any, even the most complex in terms of hardware problems. The display of this device, as noted earlier, has the most common diagonal of 5 inches to date. At the same time, its resolution is 720x1280 px, that is, the image is not displayed in HD format. The pixel density is sufficient, and individual pixels in the picture are quite difficult to distinguish. The quality of the display is high, the image at angles close to 180 degrees is not distorted. Color reproduction, contrast and brightness, too, do not cause special complaints. But what really can be attributed to the disadvantages of this device is the support of only 2 touches to the surface. That is, if in any toy you need to immediately touch the display with 3 or more fingers, the device will not be able to process the ‚Äúextra" ones from them.





Cameras and their features

The main camera of the smartphone is based on an 8 megapixel sensor. There is also autofocus and an LED backlight system. There are many modes that allow even in the least favorable conditions to achieve acceptable photo quality. As a result, the pictures are really very good on the HTC Desire 620G DUAL SIM. Well, in this case, the video is recorded as 1920x1080 px, that is, in Full HD quality. The front camera is based on a 5 megapixel sensor. Its technical capabilities are quite enough for high-quality selfie and video calls.

Memory

Not bad things are with the memory of HTC Desire 620G. Its operational capacity is 1 GB, of which about 600 MB will be occupied by system processes, and the user can use everything else (that is, about 400 MB) for his needs. The capacity of the internal drive is 8 GB. 3 GB of them is system software, and 5 GB is a place to install user programs and store personal data. If this is not enough, you can install an external flash card with a capacity of up to 128 GB.





Device autonomy

The HTC Desire 620G DUAL SIM has an average level of autonomy compared to competitors. Reviews they point to this feature. The nominal capacity of the complete battery, as noted earlier, is 2100 mAh. If we take into account the 5-inch display and although energy-efficient, but the 8-core CPU, it turns out that it seems like things are not quite good. But the actual operating experience of this device suggests that everything is not so bad as it seems at first glance. In reality, one battery charge lasts for 2 days at an average device load level. At the maximum level, the indicated value will be reduced to one day. Well, with maximum savings, this figure will naturally increase to 3 days.

Software

The HTC Desire 620G operates under the control of the most common OS for mobile gadgets ‚ÄúAndroid‚Äù. The current version of its firmware is 4.4. On top of the system software, the Sense shell was installed from the developer of the HTC device. Unlike previous versions, this modification is significantly simplified and as close as possible to the usual "Android". This is a mid-range device and cannot boast of any unusual set of applications, unlike flagships. Here are preinstalled clients of international social networks, a set of software from Google and familiar mini-applications.





Interfaces

A rich set of communications in the HTC Desire 620G DUAL SIM. Reviews characterize this gadget only from the best side in this regard. Among the wireless methods of data transfer, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, GPS and support for mobile networks of the 2nd and 3rd generation can be distinguished. In turn, wired data transfer can be performed using MicroUSB and a 3.5 mm audio port. This is quite enough for a normal and comfortable data exchange with similar mobile devices or the global web in any volume.





Opinions of owners and experts

A fairly balanced phone with a 5-inch screen and a powerful 8-core processor with enough memory and good autonomy is the HTC Desire 620G. Reviews exactly characterize this device. At the same time, the price of it is only 300 dollars, which is very pleasing to users. Experts say that HTC Desire 620G belongs to the segment of mid-range devices in all respects. Meanwhile, experts leave very positive reviews about him: the device copes well with the tasks assigned to it. So it turns out that this is one of the best devices in its niche, which has practically no weaknesses, and the cost is quite democratic for a device of this level.
