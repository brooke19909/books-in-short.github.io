A welcome accessory in the kitchen - universal juicers

Such juices, rich in trace elements and vitamins, will help to prepare universal juicers. What sets them apart from other kitchen appliances? This article will help you figure this out.





Freshly squeezed juices can be prepared from various fruits and berries. Many people like vegetable drinks, such as carrots, cabbage, and beets. You can get a delicious and aromatic liquid from citrus fruits. In order to always be able to drink a healthy drink made from your favorite fruits or vegetables, we advise you to get a juicer in your kitchen. Such a device can easily cope with hard carrots, and with delicate strawberries.

Universal juicers are of different types. The main distinguishing feature is the centrifugal separator, with which, in fact, the pulp is separated from the liquid.

Cylindrical separator

Universal juicers of this kind help to get a larger (compared to other models) volume of the drink - over 90%. However, this design does not have the function of discarding the pulp. With this device, you can easily prepare tomato juice or drinks from vegetables of dense concentration. However, the big minus of this unit will be that after receiving 2-3 glasses of squeezed liquid, it becomes necessary to clean the separator from the cake.

Conical separator

Universal juicers of this design collect the resulting cake in a special container. However, the yield of the finished drink is not higher than 70%. A large container helps less often clean and remove the cake. It is convenient when processing a large number of fruits.

The power of universal juicers varies from 200 to 800 watts. When choosing such a unit, you should pay attention to the speed controller, because for fruits of different densities it is better to choose the appropriate speed mode. If the speed is too low, juice will turn out to be small, and if too high, the drink will be oversaturated with pulp.

Take a closer look at the centrifuge grid. It is advisable to purchase a device with a stainless steel grill, because parts from softer alloys will quickly fail.

Juicers are divided by productivity. In the West, models that can prepare a couple of glasses of a drink for all family members, for example, for breakfast, are in great demand. Our compatriots love to harvest the juice obtained for the future. Therefore, devices with higher performance will come in handy.

Prices

What else should you know about universal juicers? Prices for these devices range in average from $ 50 to $ 500. Among the manufacturers of low-cost machines can be called the company VITEK. The universal juicer Bosch belongs to the mid-price range. It has more performance and power than cheaper devices.

Elite juicers, such as Bork, have many additional features. Titanium filters in their design can last for many years. The system of turbo blowing strainer allows you to cope with the peel and bones. The device can also squeeze healthy juice from fresh herbs and herbs.
