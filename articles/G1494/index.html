Guest houses, Yeysk: addresses, services, reviews

The ancient Russian city of Yeysk was founded in 1848. The ataman of the Black Sea Cossacks Grigory Rashpil came up with the idea of ​​building the port. She was supported by the Caucasian governor Count M. S. Vorontsov.

Yeysk was widely known as a resort city in the nineties of the last century. It has become famous as a great holiday destination with amazing sandy beaches.



Location

Yeysk is located on the eastern shore of the Taganrog Bay (Sea of ​​Azov), on the peninsula, at the very base of the Yeysk Spit. From the east it is washed by the Gulf of Yeisk, and from the west - Taganrog. The town is the most remote northwestern point of the Krasnodar Territory.

Climatic conditions

The city of Yeysk is characterized by a temperate continental, steppe, coastal climate with mild and low snow winters and hot, dry summers. The average annual temperature is +9.8 ° C. In January, it does not fall below +4 ° C, in July - +24 ° C. 450 mm of precipitation falls annually, relative humidity - 60%.

The beach season begins in May and lasts until the end of October. In summer, due to the shallow sea, the water warms up to +27 ° C, so families with children like to relax here. The beaches are shell, pebble, but mostly sandy, with a width of ten to fifty meters. Storms in summer are extremely rare, the soft breezes soften the heat. Autumn is usually bright and very warm. In spring, the weather is sunny and windy.



Yeisk climate has a beneficial effect on people suffering from diseases of the bronchi and lungs, as well as diseases of the heart and blood vessels. In addition, many allergies recede in Yeysk. For this reason, Yeisk belongs to climatological resorts.

At the beginning of the 20th century, mineral springs were discovered in the city, as well as deposits of silt mud, which in their properties are not inferior to the famous Saki mud. This turned Yeysk not only into a favorite vacation spot, but also into a popular and effective health resort.

Where to stay?

In this city there are hotels and hotels, however guest houses (Yeysk) are the most popular housing format for tourists on vacation. Today, as a rule, this is a new two- or three-story building, which is located on the territory of a private household. Most often it is designed to accommodate from four to ten families at a time.

Today, tourists can choose guest houses (Yeysk) of superior comfort. They are equipped with bathrooms, often have a separate kitchen, the rooms consist of one or two rooms, which are furnished with modern and functional furniture.

Economy class guest houses often have a bathroom on the floor and a common kitchen for cooking. We will present you some of the most popular guest houses (Yeysk), where you can comfortably spend your vacation.

According to vacationers, this guest house is well equipped, conveniently located, suitable for a comfortable stay with children.
