Begonia Nha Trang Hotel, Nha Trang: overview, features, specifications and reviews

Begonia Nha Trang Hotel 3 * hotel is located on Tran Phu Street - the most beautiful in the Vietnamese resort city of Nha Trang. The place is convenient for tourists in that all infrastructure facilities (restaurants, cafes, the beach, night clubs, shops, etc.) are located in the immediate vicinity. Comfortable accommodation in hotel rooms will delight all guests of Nha Trang, without exception.





About hotel

The modern hotel is located on eighteen floors and is ready to offer its guests 120 cozy and comfortable rooms with city or sea views, equipped with air conditioning and high-quality appliances. The interior design is made in a modern style.

A small distance to the beach can be overcome in just a couple of minutes. The road to Kamran Airport, where all international flights arrive, will also be easy and take about half an hour (25 km). Alternatively, close to the Begonia Nha Trang Hotel (within 700 meters), there is a local yacht club and a Vietnamese yachting center.

Arriving at the hotel guests are greeted by friendly staff. Check-in takes place in standard hours. However, with a preliminary request, you can discuss an earlier or later arrival (departure).





Superior rooms

The hotel has 15 rooms of this category. Superior accommodation is possible in two types of rooms: with one large double bed or with two single beds. Room size is 22 square meters, dormer-type windows, maximum capacity - two people.

The room includes: air conditioning, kettle, wireless Internet and cable TV, minibar, hairdryer, alarm clock, safe, bathrobes and slippers, a telephone with an international line. Mineral water, coffee and tea are provided daily for self-catering.

The cost of living in a superior room at the Begonia Nha Trang Hotel (Vietnam) is about 2,700.00 rubles per night.





Deluxe rooms

The hotel has 26 Deluxe rooms with windows overlooking the city or the sea. This accommodation option differs from the previous one in a more luxurious design, interior design. In terms of equipment, they are absolutely similar to Superior rooms.

Room size is 22 square meters, maximum capacity is two people. Guests can choose between a double bed or twin beds. The cost of living is about 4,000.00 rubles per night.





Deluxe rooms with balcony

If you want to enjoy the views around the hotel and breathe in the cool night air, then you will find a Deluxe room with a balcony. There are thirty-seven of them at the Begonia Nha Trang Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang), that is, the overwhelming majority. This accommodation option is very popular.

The standard of equipment is similar to the previous categories. Room size is 30 square meters, maximum capacity is two people. The balcony offers a beautiful view of the city or the sea. Beds are allowed. The cost of one night in Deluxe rooms with a balcony is just over 5,000.00 rubles.

Dining at the hotel

Begonia Nha Trang Hotel already includes breakfast. Guests can enjoy a rich buffet in the early morning hours. The hotel‚Äôs own restaurant is located on the second floor of the building.

It also offers lunch or dinner. The restaurant specializes primarily in national Vietnamese cuisine, rich in seafood, vegetables and rice, and also offers Western European and Oriental dishes.

Hotel infrastructure

In fact, the Begonia Nha Trang Hotel has two facilities that can occupy tourists in their free time: a spacious pool and a modern fitness center. Both of them are located on the highest, eighteenth, floor of the building. One of their main advantages is that you enjoy beautiful views of the city and the sea.

The surface of the pool is 51 square meters. It is ideal for relaxing with friends or family. Unfortunately, for children there is no special department with a small depth. Near the pool is an impromptu bar that offers guests a variety of drinks and light snacks.





In order to keep fit during the holidays, guests of the hotel can visit a small fitness center equipped with modern sports equipment.

Who is the hotel targeted at?

The hotel is located in a separate tall, but narrow building, consisting of 18 floors. He does not have his own territory. Infrastructure and service at the hotel are more focused on adult guests, and to a lesser extent on children.

When staying with a child at Begonia Nha Trang Hotel 3 * an extra bed is not offered. Children under 10 years old are free of charge, but at the same time they should use the facilities offered. There is no children's menu in the restaurant, a games room and highchairs, as well as a special shallow-water compartment in the pool.

The national composition of holidaymakers at the hotel is mixed. Recommendations for choosing this hotel: it is most suitable for couples, companies without children and single tourists who want to combine a beach holiday with a rich excursion program.

Municipal (public) beach

The hotel is small by resort standards, with only 120 rooms. He has no own territory. However, one of the main advantages of the hotel is its location only a few minutes walk from the city beach. It is the most popular place both among tourists and among local residents.

The central beach of Nha Trang is spread along the coast for 7 km, but even taking into account this fact, at the height of the season it can be very crowded. You can get to it from anywhere in the city without any problems. Entrance to the beach is free, sun loungers and umbrellas are available for hire. The territory is quite clean (shore cleaning is carried out daily in the early morning hours). The beach is sandy with a flat bottom, the depth is increasing gradually. The sand is very light, almost white, but with a yellowish tinge.





The central beach of Nha Trang has a well-developed infrastructure. It is designed for a comfortable stay of tourists coming here from all over the world. You can easily find locker rooms, toilets, showers with fresh water, as well as a first-aid post and a rescue tower. In addition, the beach has a huge amount of entertainment that will not let neither adults nor children get bored: water skiing, surfing, a jet ski, a beach volleyball and soccer court, snorkeling, boat trips, massage, etc.

Literally a stone's throw from the hotel is located Phu Dong Water Park. It is not very large, but interesting, designed more for visitors with young children.
