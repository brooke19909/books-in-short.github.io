Naroch, health resort "Priozerny": reviews and photos. Sanatorium on the lake Naroch

Where slender pines rush high into the sky, willows bend their branches to the water itself, and swans nest quietly in the thickets of reeds, one of the most famous resorts, which Belarus is so famous for, is the Priozerny sanatorium.

Location

Situated in the wilderness of coniferous forests on the shores of a picturesque lake, the sanatorium offers unique opportunities for relaxation and treatment far from the stresses of large cities, where loud music is replaced by joyful bird singing, where the air is filled with a thick aroma of tar and healing herbs, and a long healthy sleep provides uniform rustle waves of lake Naroch.



Sanatorium "Priozerny" occupies 12 hectares of the nature reserve "Narochansky". There are no large industrial and agricultural enterprises nearby, the nearest town Myadel is at a distance of 17 kilometers, and the road to the regional center of Molodechno takes at least an hour. All conditions have been created here so that vacationers can enjoy pristine nature, improve their health and restore emotional and emotional balance.

On the shores of Lake Naroch , the Priozerny sanatorium is rightfully considered one of the most modern hospitals in Belarus. Its construction was started in 1986 and was designed for a one-time accommodation of 440 people. Today, two modern seven-story buildings have 274 cozy rooms, and for those who seek solitude and complete tranquility near Lake Naroch, Priozerny offers accommodation in comfortable cottages designed for one to six people.



Number of rooms

Belarusian resorts offer a truly European level of comfort. Each room is furnished with high-quality modern furniture and allows you to stay both one at a time and entire families. In addition to standard beds, a wardrobe and a table, even the simplest one-room apartment provides guests with a refrigerator, an iron, a hairdryer and a set of tableware. The choice of 20 television channels will not let you get bored in case of bad weather.

Single and double occupancy

The superior single room additionally offers its guests a soft armchair and Internet access, and the standard bed is replaced with a wider one. Double rooms differ from single rooms only in size and number of beds. They also include a flat-screen TV and hairdryer in the bathroom, a fridge and kitchenware.



Family rooms

Two-room suite allows you to comfortably accommodate a family of 5 people. In addition to a wide double bed in the bedroom, in the living room there is a folding sofa. Additionally, you can equip two more berths. A reliable safe is designed to store money and valuables, and Wi-Fi provides excellent Internet access from anywhere in the room.

Each of the five cottages is located at a considerable distance from each other. They have two bedrooms with separate bathrooms, a common living room with a compact, fully equipped kitchen, a terrace. Air conditioning will provide coolness and comfort even in the hottest summer days.

In the rooms, bed linen is changed every four days.



Healing procedures

What is especially attractive to Belarusian holiday makers for vacationers is treatment. Belarusians managed to maintain and improve the medical base of their health resorts. Therefore, it is not surprising that almost 50% of visitors are foreign citizens who came to rest on Naroch. The lake with a sanatorium located in the immediate vicinity of its shores is especially popular with residents of Russia, as well as Azerbaijan and Israel.



The medical profile of the sanatorium is diseases of the circulatory, musculoskeletal and nervous systems. Respiratory diseases are also treated successfully. All procedures are carried out in accordance with the exact recommendations of the attending physician, for this it will be useful to take care of the registration of the spa card in advance. Within a day after settlement, the vacationer receives the first medical consultation, a schedule of physiotherapeutic and therapeutic procedures is drawn up, massage, inhalation, water and mud treatment are prescribed.

Additional features

All guests of the sanatorium are recommended morning exercises and swimming in the pool, physiotherapy exercises are prescribed according to indications. If necessary, at any convenient time, you can get advice from an experienced neurologist, urologist, gynecologist. In the treatment of patients both the advanced achievements of medicine are used, and the knowledge accumulated by generations is successfully applied. A reflexology office is working in the sanatorium, and not so long ago, patients got the opportunity to get acquainted with the effect of hirudotherapy. Ozonotherapy shows excellent results in the fight against overweight, contributes to overall recovery and rejuvenation of the body.

A dentist is being consulted all year round, which helps to keep the teeth of the guests of the sanatorium beautiful and healthy.

Wellness complexes

Along with complex treatment, everyone can choose for themselves separate procedures that will help restore strength, raise your mood and increase the overall tone of the body. Since, first of all, Naroch is a lake, one of the cleanest in Europe, Priozernoye pays great attention to hydrotherapy. Every day from morning to evening, the guests of the sanatorium have the opportunity to take coniferous, pearl, coniferous and mineral-pearl baths, expose their shoulders under the jets of Charcot's shower, and bask in a cedar barrel.

A significant healing effect on the body is provided by underwater massage and hydrokinesitherapy, in the complex treatment of joints healing mud and sapropels are actively used, including from the vicinity of Lake Naroch.

The Priozerny sanatorium has several wells on its vast territory that fully cover the health resort's need for mineral water. It is used both for oral administration in diseases of the digestive system, and for taking therapeutic baths.

Spa complex and pool

Complete relaxation and cleansing of the body through the skin will provide vacationers a visit to the spa center of the sanatorium. It has an indoor pool for adults, a children's pool and a mineral water pool.

You can choose from a traditional Russian steam room, loved by many Turkish hammam or the usual Finnish sauna. You can cool quickly in the font or in the ice room.

Cost of rest and treatment

Holidays on Naroch never belonged to the category of the most budgetary. Sanatorium Priozerny, where accommodation prices start from 1,600 Russian rubles, is one of the most acceptable. The cost of a voucher with treatment for one person is from 2200 rubles, subject to accommodation in a double room, secluded accommodation in a cottage will cost a guest of Belarus 9000 rubles per day.

Among the vacationers on the shore of Lake Naroch, the Priozerny sanatorium has an impeccable reputation. The guests of the health resort are satisfied with the size of the rooms and the interior decoration of the rooms, excellent food, including diet, and most importantly - the caring and attentive attitude of medical and service personnel. Of the shortcomings, young people note only the lack of modern animation, but for some, silence and unobtrusive music is the positive side of Priozerny, memories of which forever reside in the hearts of its guests.
