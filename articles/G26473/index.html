Glavstroy: Northern Valley - description of residential complex, reviews

St. Petersburg is the second city in the country, a metropolis with ambitious residents. A high standard of living forces modern construction companies to improve their projects, use the latest innovations, take into account all the wishes and preferences of residents in order to build really worthy residential complexes.

Glavstroy was no exception: Northern Valley is a residential development plan that immediately attracted the attention of residents. Our task is to reveal the secret of its success and popularity, as well as conduct an objective review. Feedback from the first buyers, especially those who have the ability to track each stage of construction, will help in this.





About the complex

LCD "North Valley" ("Glavstroy-SPb") is one of the most ambitious and anticipated projects of integrated housing development, which was based on the rather popular concept of "city in the city" today. These are modern high-rise buildings, built using advanced technologies, with their own infrastructure.

The project of the residential complex "North Valley" from the IC "Glavstroy" provides for the construction of 38 buildings of variable number of storeys and all necessary infrastructure. To date, 30 buildings have been commissioned, the remaining ones are planned to be commissioned in 2021.

Location

To implement such a large-scale project, Glavstroy - LCD "Northern Valley" - chose the Vyborg district of St. Petersburg, spared from harmful industries. Residents of the metropolis are so tired of the noise of the streets, endless streams of cars and endless bustle that they want to feel at home as relaxed and comfortable as possible. That is why the choice fell on the environmentally friendly and somewhat remote from the central part of the area.





At the same time, due to the developed transport network, residents of the new quarter will be able to get to any point of the city both by personal and public transport: the nearest Parnas metro station is within walking distance from the complex. The picturesque Shuvalovsky park with rare plant species and clean air, wide shady alleys and recreation areas is again within walking distance. City registration is guaranteed to every resident of the residential complex "Northern Valley" ("Glavstroy"). Reviews of the first buyers focus on registration, because residents of the metropolis will receive a number of advantages.

Beautification

LCD "Northern Valley" ("Glavstroy-SPb") borders the famous Shuvalovsky Park - a year-round platform for walking and relaxing in the fresh air. In addition, the complex will have its own park area, which will be the pride of all residents.





Infrastructure

In the territory of the North Valley complex, SC Glavstroy, in parallel with the construction of residential buildings, is putting into operation all the necessary infrastructure facilities. Residents of the first houses already use kindergartens, schools, clinics, if necessary, you can always contact the police department. But the delighted residents of the new microdistrict are caused by a well-thought-out system of multi-level parking lots - there are already about 10 of them.

Residents of the complex certainly do not experience a shortage of shops and organization of the service sector, which is also confirmed by numerous reviews. Not far from the metro station are the largest shopping and entertainment complexes: MEGA and Grand Canyon, where residents can make the necessary purchases and have a great time.

Flats

Apartments in the "Northern Valley" from "Glavstroy" impress with a variety of planning solutions - everyone will find an option to their taste. Small cozy studios, comfortable one-room, spacious two- and three-room apartments for large friendly families.





The European experience is the basis for planning decisions - these are exactly the apartments where the cooking area is combined with the dining room and the bedrooms are isolated, they are most popular today. The first residents who arrived at the handed over buildings were pleased with the presence of a spacious wardrobe, which saves space from bulky cabinets and provides an organized storage system. The windows offer stunning views of the Shuvalovsky Park and the city.
