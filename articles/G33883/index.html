Park "Berendeevo kingdom": description

All the figures of fairy-tale heroes that are here can be viewed for several hours. It is interesting to find out what the "Berendeevo Kingdom" park includes. We list its main attractions:

  • Museum of fairy tales. Its feature is its open-air location. You can visit the museum for free. On its territory you can see various fairy-tale and epic heroes. There is a catering establishment selling delicious pancakes and coffee.




  • Interworld. Here you can learn various crafts and learn how to do different interesting things, for example, bake bread or feed pets. Also in this zone you can see real men who protect the Russian land from raids of enemy tribes. These are epic heroes.
  • Glade of worldly wisdom. Here you can recall a large number of Russian folk proverbs and sayings.

"Berendeevo Kingdom" (Kaluga Region): address

It will be interesting to know where this fabulous place is located. In the Moscow region, Serpukhov district there is a village Sidorenki. It is here that the cottage village and the "Berendeevo kingdom" park are located. Many people will be happy to know how best to get here. One of the most convenient options is personal transportation.





If you are a resident of Moscow, then you need to go towards Serpukhov along the Kaluga highway. The distance that will need to be overcome is relatively small - about 140 kilometers. There are longer routes as well as shorter ones. The signpost in the "Berendeevo Kingdom" is located near the road itself, so it is simply impossible to drive past this place.
