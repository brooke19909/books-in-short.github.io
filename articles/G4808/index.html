Resort Lozenets in Bulgaria

Bulgaria is a beautiful country located on the Balkan Peninsula. Recently, it has become one of the most attractive tourist destinations for Russians. With relatively low prices for vouchers, the quality of rest is always on top here. In addition, it is Bulgaria that boasts a variety of offers for tourists. This is a beach holiday on the Black Sea, and ski slopes, and tours of historic cities.

Among the most popular resorts in the country can be called Lozenets, each year meeting hundreds of visitors. Those who choose a resort for their next vacation should definitely pay attention to it. Cozy and quiet, everyone will definitely like it.

Where is Lozenets (Bulgaria) located?

So, more details. The resort L osenets of Bulgaria is located in the southern part of its Black Sea coast. Nearby is Burgas (60 km) and the border with Turkey (30 km). At a distance of 9 km is another wonderful Bulgarian resort Primorsko.

Lozenets is located in a quiet bay very close to the picturesque Strandzha massif. For those looking for a nook for families, this resort is best suited.





History of the resort of Lozenets

The history of the foundation of the resort dates back to 1924. It was then that the village was founded, intended for the recreation of residents from a neighboring village called Valika. The originally formed village was called Emberli. This sonorous name was given to him by the name of the popular grape variety growing here. It was renamed Lozenets a little later.

For a long time, Lozenets was an unremarkable fishing village. The main occupation of the inhabitants was fishing. Local restaurants willingly bought fish and seafood from here.

The village received the status of a popular resort relatively recently. A developed infrastructure appeared here with comfortable hotels, restaurants, clubs and other institutions that will make the rest comfortable and unforgettable.





Weather

This resort welcomes travelers with truly amazing weather conditions. Here you can immediately feel the influence of three seas: Black, Marmara and Aegean. The influence of the mountain ranges is also noticeable, since the town is in close proximity to them.

Summer is quite hot, the thermometer in June-July is kept at around +30 degrees. The sun shines almost all year round, so Lozenets weather is perfect for a beach holiday. The winters at this resort are quite mild. A light breeze blows from the sea, and the mountain range protects from strong winds and slowly gives off heat. In other words, this tourist destination is just fine for those who are going on vacation with the aim to lie in the hot rays of the sun and plunge into the clear waters of the Black Sea.





Nature

Arriving here, tourists will be able to admire the amazing landscapes that open wherever you look. The village and its surroundings are famous for its many natural attractions. Among them is the Bear Hole cave and the largest river in this area is Veleka.

Oasis Camping (Lozenets, Bulgaria) is just 1 km away. And a few kilometers from the village, Silkosia Nature Reserve spread its expanses, the history of which began almost 100 years ago. This paradise is known not only to residents of the country, but also to foreign tourists.

As for the local beaches, this is one of the main reasons for the popularity of Lozenets. All beaches here are exceptionally sandy, and the water is clean and clear.

Relaxation

Everyone who comes to Lozenets, Bulgaria will meet with a wide variety of entertainment. Fishing enthusiasts can enjoy this activity on the banks of the Devil's and Veleka rivers.

Those who prefer a traditional beach holiday can spend time on one of the nearby beaches. There are 6 of them in the district: Central, Oasis, Coral, Small, Bear Hole, Babi Beach. Whatever the choice of travelers, the time spent here will leave only pleasant memories.

From entertainment you can choose:

  • yachting;

  • surfing;

  • water skiing;

  • boat ride;

  • diving;

  • scooters;

  • water parks;

  • beach sports (tennis, volleyball).





Where to stay?

Those who have chosen Bulgaria's Lozenets will always be able to choose the type of housing suitable for the level of comfort and cost. There are several hotels located here. The level of service is quite high, and the price is reasonable.





However, those who already had the good fortune to relax here, most often choose houses and a villa for rent. It is living here that allows you to fully enjoy the beauty of nature and the local flavor.

Are you bored of Turkey and Egypt? Looking for a quiet family-friendly resort? And in that, and in another case, a great solution would be a trip to Lozenets in Bulgaria. Traveler reviews of this resort confirm: there will be a lot of pleasant impressions. Indeed, the country knows how to captivate and conquer the hearts of even the most sophisticated travelers forever.
