Hotel Grand Park Bodrum 5 * (Turkey, Bodrum): description and reviews

Every experienced tourist must have visited Turkey at least once in his life. This country is famous not only for its luxurious resorts, but also for its high-class service and genuine hospitality. And here modern conveniences are organically combined with such a mysterious oriental culture.

In Turkey, dozens of resorts take tourists. One of the famous and popular places is the city of Bodrum. Of course, a traveler who decides to stay here will have a huge selection of hotels. But many people prefer to stay at the Grand Park Bodrum 5 * hotel.

Of course, when planning a vacation, it is worth considering all the factors. What is the Grand Park Bodrum 5 *? Is Bodrum suitable for families? What does the hotel offer in terms of food and accommodation? Is there any entertainment for young people here? The answers to these questions are sought by many travelers.

Where is the hotel located?





For many people who decide to spend a vacation on the seashore, an important factor is the location. Yes, the location is important, because not everyone wants to be in the midst of endless noisy parties or, conversely, spend time away from civilization.

So where to look for the hotel Grand Park Bodrum 5 *? Bodrum, Kadikalesi, a colorful and cozy village near the large resort town - this is where many tourists love to spend time. The scenic coast from the main building is within easy reach.

Bodrum city center is approximately 22 km away. There are many shopping centers, colorful markets, museums, night clubs and many other attractions. A regular bus service runs from the hotel to the central part of the resort. The airport is 55 km away (you can use either the hotel shuttle or taxi).

Infrastructure and Territory

Hotel Grand Park Bodrum 5 * is located on the seafront - its courtyard goes directly to the beach. The complex consists of the main five-story building and the so-called "boutique building" on four floors. The buildings are modern, equipped with an elevator system. There are spacious lounge areas with powerful air conditioning and wireless Internet access.

The hotel territory is quite large - its area is 20,000 square meters. The courtyard is beautiful, as tourists often mention in their reviews - everywhere there are greenery, flowering flower beds, and decorative compositions. By the way, the hotel was opened in 2014, and in 2015 it was renamed Grand Park Bodrum 5 * (before that it was called Yelken Hotel & Spa). Today the hotel is a modern complex with the necessary equipment and all possible amenities.

Hotel Grand Park Bodrum Hotel 5 * (Turkey, Bodrum): room category information

Immediately it is worth saying that the hotel is quite large - it consists of 232 rooms. The choice of travelers are offered rooms of different categories.

  • Standard rooms are 22-25 square meters with carpet. There is a private bathroom, one double or two single beds. Some rooms have a balcony.
  • Deluxe rooms are more spacious rooms (the area is 25-31 square meters) with additional amenities.
  • Superior rooms are spacious rooms with a private balcony. The area is 45-50 square meters. Suitable for 3-4 guests.
  • Disabled rooms are the same standard rooms, but with special equipment that makes the room more comfortable for people with disabilities. All rooms have spacious balconies.

Facilities in the guest rooms





Hotel Grand Park Bodrum by Corendon 5 * is famous for its convenience. The rooms here are really well equipped - soft carpet, large windows (frontal or side views of the coast), private balconies (not in all rooms), elegant design and modern furniture, including a comfortable work desk.

As you know, the modern traveler can not do without the benefits of civilization. And do not worry about this - in each issue there is a set of much-needed household appliances. The standard rooms and superior rooms have a connection to the central air conditioning system. The deluxe rooms are equipped with separate small air conditioning.

Of course, the room has a TV, safe, telephone and minibar. In all areas of the hotel, including living rooms, there is wi-fi.

The bathroom is spacious enough. Residents can use a modern shower and other plumbing. There is a mirror, a powerful hairdryer (stationary), a basic set of hygiene items. The towels are clean and fresh, change them daily. Linen on the bed is replaced 2-3 times a week.

Beach location and water activities





The hotel complex Grand Park Bodrum 5 * is located almost on the seashore - in order to get to the beach, you just need to go down from the terrace. This section of the beach is private, only hotel guests rest here, so you can count on peace and security.

The shore and the entrance to the sea are covered with soft sand, cleaned here regularly. There is a convenient pontoon, although you can enter the water from the shore. Kids enjoy playing on the shallows. There is a lifeguard team on the beach. There is a spacious volleyball court, as well as comfortable sun loungers and umbrellas.

Travelers who prefer a more active pastime will also not be bored. For a small fee, guests can rent a boat, canoe and other types of non-motorized water transport. Worth a ride on a water ski or boat. Tourists have the opportunity to go sailing or experience unforgettable emotions by diving (the sea world is really beautiful here, and the water is clear). By the way, beginners can sign up for training courses and get a diver certificate.

Additional information about the service in the territory





The owners of the Grand Park Bodrum 5 * hotel offer their guests a quality vacation that is unlikely to be overshadowed by any problems. The territory here is large, there is even a private spacious parking lot, which is guarded around the clock. By the way, it is possible to rent transport, whether it be a bicycle or a car, both in a hotel (near the parking lot) and in the city. Many travelers use this service, as it provides an opportunity to explore the surroundings on their own.

Business people often stay at the hotel - they also have all the necessary amenities. Guests can connect to the Internet from anywhere in the hotel. There is also a small business center where you can use office equipment, send or receive a fax.

The hotel has its own medical office, where every day you can find a doctor or nurse. For many tourists this is an important factor. Naturally, the services of medical personnel are paid separately, according to insurance.

Near the reception you can exchange currency (the rate is about the same as in the city). Guests use safe deposit boxes, which are located in the zone of constant video surveillance.

The hotel is often used as a venue for various events, including holidays. That is why hairdressers, stylists, makeup artists and even their own photographers work here.

Leisure: how are the guests having fun?





This hotel is equipped so that tourists, if desired, could fully occupy their leisure time. At any time of the day you can find entertainment to your liking.

In the courtyard there is a large pool - the water here is always clean and cool. Around there is a solarium terrace with sunbeds and umbrellas. This is a kind of center of hotel life. In the morning hours, animators conduct classes in gymnastics or aqua aerobics. Various contests and team games are organized throughout the day, such as water polo or dart throwing competitions. There are two water slides that are suitable for adult tourists.

By the way, there is a small pool for relaxation, equipped with a powerful hydromassage system. There is also an indoor pool, next to which tourists relax in the evenings - the water here is warm, so it is suitable for swimming in cold weather.

The hotel has a large spa complex. For free, guests visit the sauna and hammam (the wonders of a real Turkish bath should be tried at least once). Here, for a reasonable fee, you can indulge in massage treatments - specialists offer not only relaxing, but also wellness treatments. The SPA center also has several cosmetology rooms - visitors are offered masks, massages, various types of peels, etc.

The main building has a games room with various machines - many tourists spend time here. The guests also have a good time playing ping pong or billiards. Guests are welcomed daily at the fitness club, which is equipped with the latest fitness equipment. By the way, local fitness instructors are always ready to conduct a small consultation. During the high season, they also conduct various sports, group training, which can be visited for free.

In the evenings, various performances are organized in the courtyard - guests can enjoy the performance of local groups, as well as dance to live music. Nearby there is a disco, which will please fans of nightly entertainment.

Turkey is a country with an incredibly interesting culture and an extraordinary history. Therefore, do not give up the opportunity to plunge into the local atmosphere. The hotel, as well as in the city, has guides and travel agencies who are happy to plan the most interesting trip in life for you. Prices, by the way, are quite reasonable, especially when you consider that this is a crowded resort.
