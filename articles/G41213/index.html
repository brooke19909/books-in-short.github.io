Tinkoff Bank: reliability rating. Where is Tinkoff Bank in the ranking of banks?

Recently, Tinkoff Bank has been actively advertised. His rating is also growing rapidly, because an increasing number of people are beginning to actively use his services, many of which are really beneficial. However, all this should be described in more detail.





general information

The history of this organization begins in 1994, when the founder of the business, Oleg Tinkov, bought the JSCB Himmashbank. In 2006, the organization was renamed Tinkoff Credit Systems. Today, the bank is called even simpler.

Its main feature is full remote maintenance. Tinkoff Bank, whose rating is high enough, does not have ATMs or retail outlets. But this does not prevent him from being popular.

This is the only online bank in all of Russia. Its main products offered to customers are debit and credit cards, as well as deposits. But this is not the whole list of services. The organization is also engaged in the issuance of combination cards, the issuance of targeted loans (for the purchase of goods in online stores), and advertising targeting. Tinkoff Bank also has a mortgage platform.

He takes 45th place in the ranking. Equity - a little more than 29.2 billion. The amount of its assets is 182.208 billion rubles. And the fact that so many people have entrusted their funds to this organization is the best proof of its reliability.





Interesting to know

Talking about what rating Tinkoff Bank has, one cannot but note the attention to its strategy, as well as the business model.

This organization carries out its work through a high-tech platform. Most attention is paid to the mobile direction. And it is developed excellently, which modern customers like. Therefore, there are more and more people using Tinkoff services. Indeed, through the application you can make quick transfers from card to card (of any bank!), Pay traffic fines and use the MoneyTalk messenger, with which you can send money directly in the chat. In general, modern service.

Over the next five years, the bank plans to become a full-fledged financial supermarket and offer customers both its own services and partner products.

It is important to note that as of September 1, 2016, this organization is the 2nd largest player in the credit card market with a share of 9.7%. The bank's net profit for the first half of 2016 amounted to about 4.4 billion rubles.
