How to knit picks?

With the advent of cold autumn and winter, girls begin to think about how to protect their heads from frost and at the same time look stylish and fashionable. The solution is obvious: you need to knit picks . He is considered to be the most fashionable of

winter hats. She takes her head and does not let it freeze, and fits any style of clothing from jeans with a sports jacket to an elegant dress and coat.

Knitting with a knitting needle of a beret model will not take much time. You just need to be attentive and patient. Accuracy also does not hurt, otherwise you will have to redo the product many times to give it a decent look.

So, before you start knitting, take with knitting needles, get 200 g of yarn warm enough for winter frosts, as well as a couple sets of knitting needles. There are a great many drawings and knitting patterns for berets. The simplest of them is the alternation of the rows of front and back loops. The result is a simple, smooth pattern. However, if the threads you chose are solid, this beret will look boring. Although, if you are a fan of classic models, then this option is perfect for you.

For plain yarn, it is better to choose a suitable pattern from the books.

In this case, attention should be paid to the level of its complexity. If you are a beginner needlewoman, then you will need to practice in advance to knit this figure separately.

The beginning of the beret is almost always knitted with an elastic band - alternating facial and
back loops. In order not to get confused, remember with which loop the row started. For example, if the first row began with the front, then knit the second with the wrong one, etc. Or make sure that the front loops are located above the front ones, and the wrong ones - above the inside ones. Eraser need to tie 8 rows.

To knit with knitting needles further, you need to take knitting needles No. 6 and start the 9th row. The pattern can be selected chess. To do this, tie a certain number of facial loops, for example 5-6, and then the same number of purl stitches. So knit 6

rows, and then swap the front and back loops in places, that is, start with 5-6 back loops, etc. You get a square pattern that looks like a chessboard.

However, before starting to knit, he takes knitting needles in a pattern, that is, after you have finished the elastic, in the 9th row you need to gradually collect 27 loops. Thus,
knit a pattern to 38 rows, then gradually reduce the number of loops with each row.

To finish the product, carefully remove the knitting needles from the loops when there are about 5 of them left, take a gypsy needle, thread the thread, then carefully insert the needle into the loops and tighten. Thus, the beret will be ready for a long and faithful service.

If your hobby is knitting, for children you can also make a couple of berets, while using funny patterns and colored threads. Children will be delighted!
