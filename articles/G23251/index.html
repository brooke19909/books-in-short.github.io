Russian smartphone Yotaphone: user reviews, photos

The time has come when domestic manufacturers are trying to break into the market of cell phones and smartphones. Their products are Yotaphone. Reviews about the model appeared on many Web resources. Users aptly notice the advantages and disadvantages of the device. However, some reviews have no basis. Equipment

The Yotaphone smartphone has an impressive package. It primarily includes the phone itself. Included is a wired headset for talking, as well as listening to music, a charger and a clip. It helps to remove SIM cards from a smartphone. It is worth noting that the charger has the ability to parse. That is, you can pull out the USB cable from it to connect the Yotaphone smartphone directly to a personal computer and synchronize data.





The kit also includes an instruction manual for the device, a napkin, with which you can wipe its screen. In some cases (of course, not all countries have such an opportunity) a discount coupon is provided.

Yotaphone: reviews, photos, history

The smartphone was introduced back in 2012, and more precisely in December. Then the device, which has two screens at once, made a rather strong impression. The thing is that the devices of some companies embodied a similar idea before. They were shown to their designers more than once or twice, but everything ended at this stage and did not reach the implementation. But Russian manufacturers managed to bring their idea to life.

The concept of the smartphone assumed a reduction in power consumption through the use of a second screen. This was to be achieved by displaying information on an alternative screen. At the same time, it turns out that the load from the main screen was removed.

In general, the concept sounded great, attention was focused on it. January 2013 was marked by an exhibition held in Las Vegas. Already on it, the Russian smartphone was recognized as the best novelty in the smartphone industry. Moreover, absolutely no one was confused by the fact that the sale of devices was supposed to start only in the 3rd quarter of 2013. For an innovative approach to the development of telephone devices, the company received thunderous applause and prizes in Barcelona. The buyer can always see the image of these awards on the box from under the Yotaphone.

Development delays

At the same time, Yota Devices believed that the development of a smartphone would take less time than actually happened. As a result, the technical characteristics made it possible to attribute the device to an acceptable price category. This became clear already during the announcement of the device. What caused such a desire of the company to release a smartphone as soon as possible? The thing is that at this time Apple is trying to break into the market, offering accessories for its products that have a similar concept.

However, it would be better if Yota Devices brought the development to mind, since Apple's projects did not appear on store windows. The reason for this was the problem in the development of technical support. Apparently, the company did not manage to quickly resolve these issues.

About the second screen patent

It is worth noting that the concept of the second screen appeared long before Yota Devices announced it. Samsung patents regarding this idea date back to the 2000s. Around the same time, Nokia was developing a similar technology. But what is really important, in reality only the Russian manufacturer manages to implement the plan.

Two companies failed to implement projects due to the fact that the chance of selling such devices was low. But the complexity, which was clearly seen from an engineering point of view, the problems with the technical support for the operation of such a phone - all this only repelled the manufacturers. It turned out that the cost of development and production costs exceeded the return on production.

Now on the mobile phone market, the first place in performance is, of course, not Yotaphone. The Russian manufacturer, however, has achieved exactly one goal in this regard - originality. More on the shelves of similar devices you will not find.

Immediately it is worth saying that the pictures are displayed in high quality. The smartphone has a very nice color reproduction. In sunlight, he also behaves well in this regard.
