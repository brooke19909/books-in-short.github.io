Are you interested in Hurghada hotels? Jasmine is one of them

Many Europeans have long appreciated holidays on the shores of the Red Sea in Egypt. The mild and warm climate all year round, endless sandy beaches, unusual nature, historical architecture - all this attracts tourists. Tourism has long been one of the favorite pastimes of modern people. People go on a trip to get a lot of new experiences, improve their health, chat with family, spend more time with children. Equally important for a good rest and comfortable living conditions.



Built according to European standards and equipped with all necessary hotels in Hurghada. Jasmine is one of them, it was built in 1988. Over time, it was restored, rebuilt. The hotel includes not only the main two-story building, but also several more two-story buildings and a one-story cozy bungalow. Anyone who is interested in Hurghada (Hotel Jasmine) will be pleasantly surprised by the reviews. Rested in the rooms of this hotel are satisfied with everything: food, service and appearance.
