The order of visit

To visit the entertainment complex, you must purchase a plastic card, which will subsequently have to be returned. Together with it you will be given a detailed map of the park with all the entertainment venues and attractions. Galaxy Park prices in Smolensk range from three hundred to six hundred rubles per person. The cost of a one-time visit depends on the day of the week and the duration of the visit. On weekends, Galaxy Park in Smolensk is open until six in the morning and the ticket price is correspondingly higher.

It is worth noting that a plastic card is valid throughout the park, you will have to pay separately only for food and drinks. This fact makes visiting the entertainment complex very convenient.
