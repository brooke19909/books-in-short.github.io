Turkey, Sarp Hotels Belek 4 * hotel. Reviews and Description

Today, many Russian tourists choose Belek (Turkey) as a place to stay . The Sarp Hotels Belek 4 * hotel, the reviews of which we provide below, can be an excellent option for accommodation for those who want to get comfort and wonderful service at an affordable price. We offer a closer look at this hotel.



Location

Sarp Hotels Belek is located just a couple of kilometers from the center of the tourist village of Kadriye. It is only 30 kilometers from the nearest air harbor, located in Antalya. So upon arrival at the airport on the way to the hotel, it will take you no more than half an hour. The hotel in question is 1.3 km from the sea. However, do not worry that you have to overcome this distance on foot. So, regularly from morning to evening, a bus runs between the hotel and its own beach.

Description and photo

Hotel Sarp Hotel 4 * (Belek / Belek) was built in 2014. Its own area is 6 thousand square meters. meters. It consists of a main four-story building, equipped with two elevators. The total housing stock of the hotel consists of 136 standard rooms. Each of the apartments is equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. So, here you will find modern furniture, cable TV, minibar, air conditioning. Each room has a private bathroom with a hairdryer. The supply of bath toiletries is replenished daily. For an extra charge, guests can use the safe and wireless internet access.



Bars and Restaurants

The hotel in question operates on the "ol inclusive" system. Four times a day, chefs serve a buffet in the hotel restaurant. Here, guests can enjoy excellently prepared dishes of Turkish and international cuisines. There are always a lot of sweets and fresh fruits. The hotel has two bars. Guests can also enjoy drinks on the beach. There is also the opportunity to eat during the day.

Entertainment and infrastructure

Sarp Hotel (Kadriye, Belek) has a private beach (shuttle service is available). There are no familiar sun umbrellas, but there is a large comfortable canopy under which you can conveniently sit on a sunbed. By the way, there are always enough sun loungers here, so there is no need to arrive early in the morning and take your place, as is the case in some other hotels.

The hotel itself has two swimming pools and a sun terrace with a bar. You can have a great time here, if for some reason you do not want to go to the beach.

There is no animation at the hotel. However, occasionally parties and shows by guest artists are held. I must say that most vacationers are only happy with this state of affairs.

Perhaps the first thing that many people have mentioned about this hotel is its proximity to the airport. So, according to them, after arrival at the hotel you will be in 20 minutes. This is very convenient if you have a night flight or if you are traveling with small children.
