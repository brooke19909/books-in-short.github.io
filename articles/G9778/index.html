Naberezhnye Chelny - hotels for every taste

The history of the emergence of Naberezhnye Chelny goes back centuries, archaeological excavations indicate the first sites of man before our era and the existence of settlements in the XIV-XV centuries. The modern city was built later on the site of the village of Berezhnye Chelny, which became the center of the grain trade. Naberezhnye Chelny experienced rapid growth after the construction of the Nizhnekamsk Hydroelectric Power Station and large enterprises, including the largest KAMAZ truck and engine plant.

With the increase in industrial production, the city became a major railway, road and aviation hub. The multinationality of the population led to the emergence of a large number of mosques, Orthodox churches and Protestant communities. There are 16 higher educational institutions and branches in Naberezhnye Chelny, and cultural life is represented by 8 theaters, 5 cinemas, museums and galleries. There is a small dolphinarium. Guests, students, business travelers will be warmly welcomed by numerous hotels (Naberezhnye Chelny), the prices for the rooms of which can be selected depending on the wishes and possibilities.
