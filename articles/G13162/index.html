LCD "Victory" (Lobnya): description, reviews

Lobnya is a small town near Moscow, located north of the capital. Construction does not stop here, day or night: new residential complexes and entire microdistricts with comfortable conditions are being rebuilt. What does a modern resident of a megalopolis need, you ask. Tired of the monotony of typical planning decisions, primitive infrastructure, noise and bustle, each of us wants to get a spacious apartment with an improved layout with a beautiful view of the sights of the city, but at the same time get complete isolation from the bustle. Such projects can be safely attributed to the Victory residential complex (Lobnya). Within the framework of this material, using the feedback of the first inhabitants, we will give the complex the most objective assessment and determine how suitable it is for life.





about the project

Four monolithic buildings of varying number of storeys, located in one of the most picturesque and greenest cities near Moscow, are all Victory (Lobnya) residential complexes. Responses of residents, first real estate buyers, mainly affect the benefits of the chosen location. None of them could have imagined that, not losing access to the capital‚Äôs infrastructure, you can feel all the charms of country life, spend free time in the company of friends and relatives in the fresh air, surrounded by trees, and all this without leaving the microdistrict.

For the construction, modern materials and innovative technologies were used, which ultimately made it possible to achieve impeccable quality. Double-chamber double-glazed windows of European manufacture provide the necessary heat and sound insulation properties, all entrances are equipped with high-speed silent elevators and ramps for moving inactive groups of the population.





Architecture

The external design of the facades of houses is made in bright and cheerful colors: green, yellow, burgundy. Even on the grayest and rainy day, with one glance at the new building, the mood rises, the residents of the complex share their impressions. Hanging balconies with carved patterns - the highlight of the project, from the ends of the hulls are decorated with images of flying gulls, snowflakes - I want to look at them endlessly. The project provides for the organization of a small pond, which, as conceived by the developer, should become a favorite place for walking and recreation for all residents.

Location

An excellent place was chosen for the construction of the Victory residential complex (Lobnya). New buildings will appear in the center of residential development, all residents will have access to the necessary social and commercial infrastructure. Rogachevskoe, Dmitrovsk and Leningradskoye shosse lead to the city, through which you can get to Moscow without unnecessary traffic congestion. A well-developed transport network, represented not only by taxis and buses, but also by electric trains, provides mobility even to those residents who do not have a personal car. Public transport is just a 10-minute walk away.

Infrastructure

The developer paid enough attention to the improvement of the territory and the development of the infrastructure of the Victory residential complex (Lobnya). Responses of residents only confirm that living here is comfortable, both for newlyweds and families with young children and senior citizens. In the courtyard of the complex there is a kindergarten, in the near future - the construction of a comprehensive school. On the territory of the complex there are enough playgrounds and sports grounds, recreation areas, walking and bicycle paths.





Residents of the complex certainly do not have a shortage in stores, pharmacies, shopping centers, beauty salons, bank branches. For car owners, several underground parking lots are provided, as well as ground-based guest parking. The territory of the Victory residential complex (Lobnya) is fenced and is under round-the-clock surveillance.

Floor plans

Apartments in the residential complex "Victory" (Lobnya) pleased with a variety of planning solutions. The residents were offered a choice of spacious one-, two-, three- and four-room apartments with large kitchens and walk-in closets. In order that the apartment in the first months of residence does not turn into a warehouse of necessary and unnecessary things, the developer has provided for the presence of special pantries. No more bicycles or shoe boxes in the hallway. All property owners have appreciated the opportunity to rid their apartment of bulky things.





The apartments are handed over to the owner with a pre-finished finish: all surfaces are leveled and fully ready for subsequent decorative finishing, communications are completed, an entrance door and plastic windows are installed.

To summarize

If you are thinking about purchasing your own apartment in the capital, but are limited in funds, pay attention to the Victory residential complex (Lobnya). This is a bright representative of a modern and thought out to the smallest detail complex, combining all the advantages of metropolitan and suburban life.
