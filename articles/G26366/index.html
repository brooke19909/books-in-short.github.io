Grand Panorama Family Sui 4: reviews and description of the hotel in Turkey

Summer is a vacation time, everyone wants to relax a year in advance and, of course, at sea. Our compatriots are increasingly choosing Turkey, or rather Marmaris. This is a real gem of two seas - the Aegean and the Mediterranean. Why do they prefer this resort? Everything is simple: inexpensive, comfortable and fun.





An atmosphere of peace, cosmopolitan charm, golden beaches and endless hospitality - these are the main attributes that attract tourists. The city combines ancient history and modern color. There is something to do, where to go and what to see. The resort is one of the few where carefree and endless celebration reigns. Marmaris has plenty of opportunities for diving, diving schools for beginners are open, underwater excursions and trips are regularly arranged.

A comfortable climate and warm sea create favorable conditions for year-round tourism. The bay in which the city is located is protected by mountain hills. The sea here is almost always calm, clean and warm. Marmaris has many small areas with a good tourism industry, which include Armutalan. It is here that the Grand Panorama Family Sui 4 hotel complex is located, which will be discussed in today's article.

Why did you choose this hotel? Because it is suitable for an economical vacation and is designed for couples with children, the elderly and single travelers. The living conditions here are more than worthy, and the service and maintenance are top notch. Holidaymakers themselves praise the guesthouse and believe that qualified employees work here. Special hospitality is harmoniously interwoven with home comfort and European service.





Description

Grand Panorama Family Sui 4 has been around for 19 years. The building is undergoing regular cosmetic repairs, furniture and interior are updated. The administration has strict requirements for the professionalism of its staff, so there is no rudeness and shamelessness. The hotel rises on a mountain surrounded by a beautiful pine forest. You can get to the complex from the airport of Dalaman (100 km), and then transfer by bus or taxi. A shuttle service is also available for a fee. The central part of Marmaris is only 3 km away, where public transport runs regularly.

Accommodation





The four-story building of the Grand Panorama Family Sui 4 complex (Marmaris / Armutalan) has 86 cozy rooms. All have a balcony with city or forest views.

Private bathrooms have tiled bathrooms with a range of toiletries and toiletries.

The two-room rooms have a seating area with a sofa and a table. Each room has a TV (show Russian and cable channels) and air conditioning. A hairdryer and a telephone are available. On request, children's beds are available. Daily cleaning is provided.

Supply system

The cost of accommodation includes four meals a day "All inclusive". The menu of the Grand Panorama Family Sui 4 includes sausages, dairy products, cereals, meat, fish and fresh vegetables. Breakfast - buffet. During the day, you can visit restaurants and bars located in the local area. There is an Internet cafe where you can enjoy delicious Turkish coffee and sweets.

Infrastructure





Entertainment at the hotel is enough for different tastes and interests. On the territory of the complex Grand Panorama Family Sui 4 (Marmaris) there is a payphone - cards are sold directly at the hotel. There is a tattoo parlor and souvenir shops with a rich assortment of jewelry, silver, clothes and bags.

In the courtyard there is a wonderful outdoor terrace with Internet access, so if you want privacy and silence, you can stay here. It features an outdoor pool with a water slide and a steam room (indoor pool in the building). A massage room is open for all comers. The property has its own fitness center and spa.

The Grand Panorama Family Sui 4 has a medical office, a laundry department and a disco club. Particularly active people will be offered to play table tennis or billiards. Of course, they didn‚Äôt forget about the kids: for the youngest guests of the resort a modern and safe playground and pool are equipped. Children will enjoy the development club, where you can do interesting and informative games.

Coastline



The beach is about 7 km from the building of the complex, where the bus delivers tourists - it takes it in the morning and brings it back in the evening. The shore is clean, sand and gravel, with free amenities (mattresses, umbrellas and sun loungers). The bar is open all day for drinks and snacks. It is forbidden to bring food with you. Nearby there is a municipal beach with benches.

Information about the hostel will help people quickly make a choice. In general, Grand Panorama Family Sui 4 receives positive reviews from tourists. Many liked the service, the friendly atmosphere, the clean rooms and the excellent food. The only drawback, according to guests, is the remoteness from the beach line. For such an amount, it is hardly possible to find a hotel working on the All Inclusive system. Be sure to bring a great mood and positive emotions!
