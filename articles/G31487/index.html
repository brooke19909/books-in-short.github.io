Negative reviews

Not without negative impressions of such an institution as Kirman Hotels Sidera Luxury Spa 5 *. Reviews of tourists highlight such negative points:

  • rocky entry into the sea (you have to buy special protective slippers for your feet or, as an option, swim from the pier);

  • diluted local alcohol on an all-inclusive basis;
