Khadyzhensk city in the Krasnodar Territory: houses, motels and infrastructure

Khadyzhensk in the Krasnodar Territory is a small town in the foothills of the Caucasus in the Absheron region near the river with the interesting name Pshish. Unique sources of iodine-bromine mineral waters were found here, which made these places a tourist center for a relaxing holiday and restoration of health.





Sanatorium "Mineral" in Khadyzhensk, Krasnodar Territory

At the place where mineral water emerged 60 years ago, a sanatorium called Mineral was built. In the territory of the complex of guests and patients of this place rich vegetation pleases, and right behind the gates of the sanatorium a forest begins. In the Mineralny park here and there are benches for relaxation, a pond with a small bridge, a tennis court and gazebos with barbecue facilities for lovers of barbecue.

Prices in the Khadyzhensky sanatorium are quite affordable: a ticket with food and treatment will cost only 3,000 rubles per day. You can rent a room or a house in Khadyzhensk, Krasnodar Territory, and pay only for treatment, which will cost 1000 rubles per day.

Most procedures are associated with treatment with mineral waters:

  • Mineral iodine-bromine baths.
  • Inhalation using medicinal water.
  • Gum irrigation with iodine-bromine waters.
  • Gynecological irrigation with mineral water.
  • Four-chamber bathtubs.
  • Electrophoresis with mineral water.

Such treatment is used for a wide range of health problems. Procedures using Khadyzhensk mineral water helps with:

  • Diseases of the musculoskeletal system and joints.
  • Skin problems.
  • Obesity.
  • Gynecological diseases.
  • Diseases of the nervous system.
  • Gastrointestinal problems.

All procedures are prescribed by a doctor who takes directly to the sanatorium. Also in the Mineral, you can pass a number of tests necessary for a more correct diagnosis and the appointment of appropriate procedures.

The sanatorium in Khadyzhensk of the Krasnodar Territory is a great place to relax and undergo treatment.





How to get to the city

Khadyzhensk is located in the Absheron region of the Krasnodar Territory. The closest airport is in the city of Krasnodar. From here to Khadyzhensk 112 kilometers. You can get there by bus or taxi. If you prefer to travel by train rather than by plane, then there is a railway station in the city through which long-distance trains pass.
