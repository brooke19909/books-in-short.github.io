How to weave a thick bracelet made of rubber bands: instructions, photos

Anyone who is interested in how to weave a thick bracelet made of rubber bands will definitely need the detailed instructions below for making dense, multi-layer jewelry from the famous rubber bands "Fanny Lum". If you wish, you can always take any of the described methods of weaving and develop on its basis your own line of handmade jewelry.





Bracelet "Buds"

Needleworkers and craftsmen are most likely to be interested in information on how to weave very thick bracelets from rubber bands and at the same time make them very beautiful, stylish and fashionable. As you know, flowers never go out of trend - try to make an amazing chain yourself from large round buds. Such an ornament can be worn both as a bracelet, and as beads, and even as a belt.

To create a delightful chain of buds, you need the green and red rubber bands Fanny Lum, a clip, a hook and a small Finger Lum machine.

How it's done





  • Throw on two opposite columns two green rubber bands, twisting them with a figure of eight.
  • From above, throw in pairs two red ‚Äúirises‚Äù on adjacent posts.
  • Dump the green rubber bands into the center of the Finger Lum machine.
  • Put, without twisting, on all four pegs a green rubber band.
  • Throw off all the red rubber bands in the center.
  • Sketch, without twisting, on all four columns one more green "iris".
  • Drop the lower green rubber bands in the center.
  • To finish the job, fold the green loops into two columns and fasten the chain with a clip.

If you want to learn how to weave a thick bracelet from elastic bands so that the buds become more and more with each step, repeat the second step three times, throwing rubber bands of different colors and dropping the lower "irises" to the center.

Bracelet "Wave"





This decoration consists of two multi-colored layers. The instructions presented will help you deal with the steps of its manufacture.

How to weave thick gum bracelets, if you have not purchased a single machine? Oddly enough, for the manufacture of most spectacular decorations from elastic "irises" you do not even need a machine - just a good hook.

So, for the ‚ÄúVolna‚Äù summer bracelet, you will need black, orange, yellow and light pink rubber bands, as well as a clip and, of course, a hook.

Beginning of work

  • Throw a black rubber band onto the hook, twisting it with an eight. Pick up another two black Fanny Lum tools.
  • Throw all the loops off the hook onto them. Loops from a finger also throw on a hook.
  • Pick up two more black ‚Äúirises‚Äù with the tool, drop the loops on them, but now leave the rings that formed on the finger.
  • Throw on the hook three yellow rubber bands.
  • Slide the stitches from your finger onto the hook.
  • Repeat the previous steps until you get the desired length of jewelry. Thread the clip into the black loops on the hook, and then remove them from the tool.

Second layer





If you already know how to weave a thick bracelet made of rubber bands (the scheme of two-layer jewelry, by the way, is quite simple), then you probably already realize that the first stage of manufacturing jewelry was just the creation of the initial blank of the product. Now you can begin to complete the bracelet.

  • Turn the resulting workpiece over and hook the first double black loops onto the hook.
  • Throw on the hook three yellow rubber bands from the workpiece and twist them with a figure eight.
  • Pick up the two black Fanny Lum tools.
  • Throw all the rings from the hook onto them, then put on the loops removed from the finger.
  • Repeat the previous steps until the end of the workpiece.
  • Fold two double loops onto your finger, extending one through the other. Thread the clip into the resulting loop.

Double sided bracelet

How to weave a thick bracelet from elastic bands, if you want to make in it not just two layers, but two front sides? Very simple - follow the instructions of enthusiastic needlewomen, and you will surely succeed.

For the manufacture of two-sided decoration, take white and multi-colored rubber bands, a clip and an ordinary hook.

What's next?

Those interested in how to weave a thick bracelet made of rubber bands will be curious to learn that the creation of such a jewelry does not consist of one, or even two, but of three full stages. So:

  • Weave six blue rubber bands using the basic braid technique.

End of work

The second side of such a bracelet is woven according to the same principle as the second layer in the jewelry called "Wave". Once you have finished processing the entire workpiece, it remains to remove the clips, loosen the loose loops from the hook, weave five blue rubber bands in the base chain and secure the main clip.
