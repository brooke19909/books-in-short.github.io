Where should they stop to create the most favorable first impression of Russia? Of course, in the "Heliopark Old Estate Hotel & Spa 4 *". This is the most presentable and comfortable place in the city.

It has a wonderful location - a stone's throw from the old Kremlin. The Heliopark Hotel in Pskov has the Aristocrat restaurant, the Rublev bar with an open terrace and the Refectory. A buffet breakfast is served every morning from seven to eleven o'clock in the morning. This meal is included in the price.

The hotel accepts orders for the organization of weddings, other celebrations, as well as comprehensive meals for conference participants, since the restaurant hall can accommodate 160 people.
