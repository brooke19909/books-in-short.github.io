How to understand that the iPhone is fully charged?

The joy of acquiring an iPhone is often accompanied by various difficulties and inconveniences of using the gadget. The fact is that this device is significantly different from all other smartphone models. If in the process of using everything it comes to automatism, then in the early days, not everyone can even put the phone on charge. In the article you will find information about the nuances of charging and how to understand that the iPhone is charged.

How to put on charging?

The manufacturer of the device strongly recommends using only original or certified accessories by the developers for the mobile device. You can charge your iPhone as follows:

  • the gadget is connected to the USB cable through the appropriate connector located at the bottom of the screen;
  • the free end is connected to an adapter for an outlet, a computer, or other appropriate accessory (docking station, hub, etc.).

When the cable is connected, the user will notice that charging has begun. Learn how to understand that an iPhone is fully charged by reading the information below.

Signs of the charging process

There are two ways to determine the charging process. Each of them is noted in different situations when the iPhone is on or off:

  1. The smartphone is on. At the time of connecting the cable, a characteristic signal is heard announcing the beginning of charging. If the device is in silent mode, the user will feel short-term vibration. A lightning bolt will appear on the screen next to the battery icon. How to understand that the iPhone is charged? When the process is complete, the image of the lightning will disappear.
  2. The smartphone is turned off. Here the process goes a little differently. If the device is completely discharged, for the first 15-20 minutes it does not show any signs of functioning at all. Users should know that this is not a malfunction - you do not need to try several times to reconnect the charging cable. After this time, an image of an empty battery with a thin red bar at the bottom indicating the minimum charge level should appear on the screen. If the device blinks from time to time, this is within the normal range. After the battery is full, the image will turn green.

In the "iPhone" settings there is a function to display the charge level in percentage terms. If it is installed, to understand that the iPhone is 100 percent charged can be understood by the corresponding indicator. In the photo below you can see that the battery charge is 40%. The lightning bolt is also visible, which indicates the need to connect the device to the network.

How to understand that the smartphone is charging?

In case of blocking the mobile device, when connected to charging, a large image of the battery appears in the center of the screen. After some time, when you press the Home key, you can see that the green part on the battery icon has increased, which means that the iPhone is charging.

If the smartphone is completely discharged, it happens that the screen lights up and immediately goes blank, an empty battery or USB cable is displayed, after some time it is usually activated. If the display remains black and even after a while nothing happens, then there is a malfunction in the charger or the gadget itself.
