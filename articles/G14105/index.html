Fiesta Beach 4 * (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria). Fiesta Beach 4 *: reviews

In the popular Bulgarian resort called Sunny Beach, edged by well-groomed well-maintained beaches, awarded the international Blue Flag certificate for environmental cleanliness and comfort, the elegant Fiesta Beach 4 * hotel is located.

The coastal waters and golden sand spits are kept in perfect condition. The beach line of "Sunny Beach" is covered with many tiny golden grains of sand. The gently sloping coast smoothly descends into the crystal waters of the Black Sea.

The resort area is surrounded by the Balkan mountains, softening the climate to comfortable summer temperatures. Mountain ranges are covered with rare species of vegetation. In the shade of trees and shrubs interspersed with fragrant thickets of flowers, vacationers take refuge from the hot sun and enjoy the magnificent panorama of the surroundings.

Location of the hotel complex

Comfortable hotel Fiesta Beach 4 * (Sunny Beach), ideal for families, borders on the first coastline of the north coast. It is separated from the beach by the sea promenade, where bars and shops are crowded, alternating with attractions.





The lively central quarter of the resort, whose streets are strewn with shops, entertainment venues, bars and restaurants, is very close. Walking to it takes only 10 minutes.

Description of the rooms of the hotel complex

Hotel Fiesta Beach 4 * (Bulgaria), specializing in breakfast and half board (breakfast-dinner, evening drinks - for a fee), was built in 2004. Most recently, it was redecorated. In the main ten-story building there are 146 apartments, a restaurant and a bar offering dishes prepared according to recipes of local and international cuisines.

The territory of the hotel complex is a well-groomed garden and park area with an outdoor pool, which has comfortable sun loungers and umbrellas that protect from burning sun rays. The pool is equipped with a children's section.





The apartments at the Fiesta Hotel are single, double and triple, as well as studios. A baby cot is available upon request. Apartments are cleaned and towels and bed linen changed regularly. All rooms adjoin cozy balconies with picturesque panoramic views of the sea and the park.

The bathrooms in the Fiesta Beach 4 * hotel are equipped with a full set of necessary hygiene items, including a hairdryer. The apartments are air-conditioned. They have a radio, TV and telephone. A safe and minibar are provided for an extra charge.

Entertainment at the Fiesta Hotel

In the comfortable lobby bar of the hotel, located in the lobby of the first floor, guests use free internet, connecting to Wi-Fi. In the evenings, musicians play in the restaurant. The Fiesta Beach hotel complex has a calm and pleasant atmosphere. It does not organize noisy entertainment.

The SPA salon offers therapeutic and relaxing massage sessions . Guests play billiards and work out in the gym. Tourists who prefer to actively relax, have fun in noisy companies, go to the city center, where night clubs, bars and entertainment venues are concentrated.

Beach area

Hotel Fiesta Beach 4 * is located just 80 meters from the clean beach. Flat, with a convenient descent, the shore smoothly enters the warm, crystal clear sea ​​waters. Umbrellas and sunbeds for rent on the beach.





Tourists rent scooters, catamarans and bananas on which they ride, cutting through the expanse of the sea. Vacationers, young and old, with bated breath, descend from the dizzying water slides right into the sea.

Reviews for the Fiesta Hotel

Tourists who visited the Fiesta Beach 4 * hotel claim that it has a comfortable atmosphere. The staff is polite and friendly, perfectly copes with their duties. You do not have to pay extra for the speed of placement and regular cleaning of the room. The apartments are clean, comfortable, cleaned every other day, towels and bed linen are changed on demand. If an extra bed is pre-ordered in the room, it is set before the guests arrive.





Vacationers who choose half board do not buy anything edible outside the hotel complex. They have more than enough tasty, not too spicy and varied dishes provided in the restaurant. It also offers a special menu for children.

Tourists with children who rested at the Fiesta Beach 4 * hotel leave reviews not entirely flattering. They are confused by the lack of clubs, playgrounds and rooms for children's leisure. The hotel has no babysitters or animations. However, this drawback is easily compensated by the fact that the hotel is located near the city center, which is full of entertainment venues. But family tourists are fully satisfied with the beach. Convenient approaches to the sea are ideal even for the tiniest children.

Hotel Fiesta Beach Feature





This hotel has a feature: it belongs to two owners and is divided into two parts. In Fiesta M, the level of service, the comfort of apartments and the quality of food are much higher than in Fiesta Beach. Many tourists directly point out that at the Fiesta Hotel, it’s more likely not a restaurant, but a dining room, where dishes are served on dishes with chips. While in the "Fiesta M" operates a fine restaurant.

From negative reviews, it becomes clear that the quality of service at the Fiesta Hotel falls when almost all apartments are occupied. The staff simply can not cope with its maintenance. Politeness disappears, as if it had never been. Interrogating a change of linen and towels is almost impossible. In general, the Fiesta Beach hotel complex is rated by three stars.
