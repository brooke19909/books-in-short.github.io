Hotel "Lotus", Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk. Description. Reviews

For business people and tourists coming to the administrative center of the Sakhalin Oblast, the issue of their placement is relevant. Hotel "Lotus" in Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk will provide guests with comfortable living conditions. In addition, you will enjoy a friendly welcome and affordable prices.

Description

The location of the Lotus Hotel is the very center of Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk. Near the city administration, the government of the Sakhalin region. The train station can be reached on foot in fifteen minutes. The distance to the airport is ten kilometers. In addition, there is a convenient traffic intersection near the hotel. If you stayed here for a few days, then you can visit the Chekhov literary and art museum, the regional drama theater, take a walk in the park of culture and rest. A grocery store is located on site. The on-site caf√© offers Italian and French cuisine. Wireless internet access is free. There is a free car park. The hotel has 27 rooms. All rooms are equipped with a TV, telephone with intercity and international calls, a refrigerator, air conditioning. The Hotel Lotus is located in Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk at the address: 41 A. Kurilskaya Street

Accommodation

The hotel offers several categories of rooms:

  • single standard second category - 2600 rubles per day;
  • single standard of the first category - 2900 rubles per day;
  • single superior - 3300 rubles per day;
  • standard double with two separate bedrooms - 3800 rubles per day.

Price is for accommodation only. Each room has its own bathroom, toiletries are provided. In the mornings, at the Lotus Hotel in Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk, breakfast is served according to the buffet system at a cost of 100 rubles per person. Upon request, accommodation can be paid taking into account breakfast. For additional accommodation in a single room one adult will need to pay 600 rubles.

Services

To make your stay at the Lotus Hotel in Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk the most comfortable, a number of additional services are provided here:

  • There is a hotel security system.
  • You can use the safe, which is located at the administrator.
  • The hotel will help you navigate around the city.
  • There is a left-luggage office.
  • Laundry and ironing services are available at a surcharge.
  • Upon request, an airport shuttle can be arranged for you. The service is paid.
  • Room service is available.
  • There is a wake-up service for the specified time, a taxi call.
  • The hotel has a shop.
  • Here you can send a fax and make photocopies.

Negative reviews of tourists

A number of reviews of the Lotus Hotel in Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk talk about its shortcomings.

  • The hotel has only one ironing board, and that is located on the ground floor.
  • On the upper floors there is a dance studio. If you plan to relax during the day, you will not succeed. Better book a room on the second floor.
  • Breakfast is very modest. Those who like to eat should eat in the city.
  • Not all rooms have a hairdryer.
  • Some guests did not like the lack of an elevator in the hotel. Heavy suitcases have to be lifted independently on the stairs.
  • There are guests who consider the cost of living in the hotel overpriced.
