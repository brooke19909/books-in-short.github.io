Smartphone LG G Pro 2: it can not be better!

One of the best technical specifications today is the LG G Pro 2. This device has a phenomenal level of performance and is able to solve the most complex problems.





Smartphone Iron

The LG G Pro 2 is based on the high-performance single-chip chip MCM8974 from KUALKOM. It consists of 4 KRAIT 400 architecture cores, which at maximum load are able to operate at a maximum frequency of 2.3 GHz. This is one of the most productive processors on the workstation architecture at the moment. It harmoniously complements the graphics adapter 330 from Adreno. Such hardware resources can easily cope with even the most complex task. No wonder the flagship is the company LG G Pro 2. Reviews of the satisfied owners of this gadget is another confirmation of this.

Housing, screen and camera

The screen size of this smartphone is 5.9 inches. This is one of the largest gadgets on the market. Screen resolution - 1920 pixels by 1080 pixels. That is, the image is displayed in HD quality.

Another important point is that the display is based on a high-quality IPS matrix. The resulting image is bright and saturated. Also, the front part of the device is protected by a particularly durable Gorilla Eye 3 glass, that is, in this case, you can do without a protective film. The back of the smart smartphone is made of textured plastic. This material is resistant to dirt and scratches, but it has one small drawback: it can slip in your hands. If you do not forget about it, then there will be no problems with the operation of the LG G Pro 2. A review of the technical specifications of this smartphone suggests that it has 2 cameras installed right away. One - 2 megapixel, displayed on the front of the smart phone, is designed to make video calls. The second - 13 megapixels - allows you to get pictures and videos of unsurpassed quality. It is also equipped with an image stabilization system and flash for shooting in the dark. Under the camera are the volume swings. An original solution that you will not find so often.

Flagship memory subsystem

Not bad things are with the memory subsystem of LG G Pro 2. Specifications for different versions of the smartphone may vary. But first things first. The RAM in this device is 3 GB. It is enough to run any task. But the integrated flash memory can be 32 GB or 16 GB. This information must be clarified with the seller at the stage of purchase of the device. But even in the most modest version, it is more than enough. If someone thinks it is not enough, then you can increase the amount of memory by installing a TF card in the expansion slot. The maximum size of supported drives is 64 GB.





Information exchange

Many methods of wireless data transmission are equipped with LG G Pro 2. Owner reviews only confirm this statement. There is everything that can be in a device of this class today. First you need to highlight Wi-Fi, capable of transmitting data at speeds up to 100 Mbps. It is perfect for those cases when you need to upload impressive amounts of data to a smart phone. For smaller information, you can also use 2G or 3G. To transfer information to another smartphone or mobile phone, you can use bluetooth or infrared. The latter, although not so often, is still found. For navigation, the gadget is equipped with a universal transmitter that can simultaneously work with navigation systems such as GLONASS and GPS. Therefore, it can be used not only as a smart phone, but also as a navigator for moving around the area. He will not allow you to get lost in any corner of our planet. Universal functions endowed with a micro USB port. With it, you can charge the battery or exchange information with a personal computer. In the latter case, you can perform several operations:

  • Sync contacts with your Google account.
  • Work with your smartphone as a flash drive.
  • Enable the device as a webcam.

At the top of the gadget is a 3.5 mm jack. It is designed to connect external speakers. A fairly high-quality stereo headset comes with the device, and there‚Äôs no need to buy this accessory in addition.





Battery

The LG G Pro 2 smartphone comes complete with a fairly capacious 3200 milliampere / hour battery. Its resource with a mediocre load is enough, as a rule, for 2-3 days. It is simply impossible to achieve a higher indicator with such a diagonal and a similar hardware filling. But watching movies or using the Internet actively can cause the battery to run out in 12-14 hours.

Software

LG G Pro 2 uses one of the most recent versions of Android with an index of 4.4.2 as the OS. In general, the software part of this smartphone is identical to the LG Optimus Flex device with a curved screen and a similar display size. They also installed overseas social networking services and a software package from Google. But for communication in social networks such as Odnoklassniki, My World, and VKontakte, you will have to install the appropriate utilities from the Play Market.





Summary

The LG G Pro 2 smartphone turned out to be almost flawless. One minus that he noticed was that the back cover can slide in your hands under certain circumstances. Judging by the characteristics and reviews of the owners, he has no more weaknesses. Yes, and this can not be considered a drawback. This is more of a remark that can be easily fixed with a regular silicone bumper cover. But in general, this is an ideal smartphone for all those who are not used to denying anything to themselves.
