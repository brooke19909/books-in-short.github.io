Krasnodar Territory, the village of Dzhubga: reviews of tourists, attractions, what to see necessarily

On the shore of the Black Sea Bay there is a small village, buried in the greenery of mixed forests. Dzhubga is considered a traditional resort town that attracts travelers with its charm. Large sandy beaches are never deserted. Travelers come here to relax from different parts of Russia. Reviews about Dzhubga and its attractions are different and therefore, we will figure out who this place is suitable for.

Description of the village

The nature of the Caucasus coast was created under the conditions of the interpenetration of two types of air masses: mountain and sea. A light subtropical background, similar to the Mediterranean, contributed to the emergence of species diversity of the flora, including endemic plants. In the reviews that moved to Dzhubgu, it is noted that even though the summer season is dry and warm, and the winters are warm and damp, it is very comfortable to live and restore health all year round.

Krasnodar region is an appetizing slice of the tourism business. It is possible to relax here, both actively and in full force, so quietly and measuredly. Waterfalls

"Colonel"

They were named in honor of Colonel Babich, who camped on this land in the era of the Caucasian Battle. Later there was a farm that exists today, and its population is 50 people. Spectacular waterfalls are located in the bed of the Kamenisty stream and the largest is 18 meters in height. Their characteristic feature is the fact that they all differ significantly between themselves in volume and configuration. An equipped path leads to them, which runs through a magnificent forest, and then a chain of bridges and stairs begins.

Low waterfalls dry up in the summer months and for this reason experienced travelers recommend choosing a spring in order to admire them at will. Traveler reviews describe their unusually refined cascade of 6 large waterfalls with spotless clear water. The path along the stream fits 10 km, but the brightness and grandeur of the waterfalls are worth it to overcome it completely.

Silver

These are five-meter cascades of falling water, which are located near the village of Bzhid. For this reason, they have another name - "Bzhidsky". The gloss of snow-white limestone through the layers of water forms the impression of the presence of silver. For a thorough inspection, the path is equipped with a ladder.

Tengins

Cascades of water up to 8 m high are located on the Shapsuho River. The most beautiful and popular is the lower waterfall. Above the river, their number reaches 20. A bicycle and cable trail leads to them.

Bigusky

Nature placed them close to the settlements of Bzhid and Teshebs and therefore they were given two more names "Gebius" and "Teshebsky". They reach a height of 15 meters and are located in a beautiful subtropical strip, which is characterized by luxurious vegetation. The path for travelers flows along a path with stairs and bridges on high slopes. There are barbecue facilities and places for relaxation along the line.
