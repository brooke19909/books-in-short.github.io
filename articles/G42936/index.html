Thread decor

To make glasses for the wedding with your own hands in this way will not require any special skills. Even if you are very far from needlework, but celebrate your wedding in eco-style, you still have the opportunity to save on additional decor. Indeed, designer glasses are not cheap. Lace decor

This way of decorating glasses for a wedding with your own hands is very similar to the previous one. Only in this case you need to wind the rope not on the leg, but on the glass itself. Use the same methods for attaching it: a hot gun or PVA glue.

In this decor method, you will also need a piece of lace. It should be glued just below the rope. In order to keep the two layers tighter, lace should be tied with a coarse thread, the ends of which can be tied in a bow.

Now it remains to glue an additional decor. It can be beads, both plastic and wooden. They, by the way, can be worn on the ends of the strings.

Polymer clay figurines

If the bride is a needlewoman, then it will not be a problem for her to make glasses for the wedding with her own hands. We will hold a master class on their manufacture.

The first thing to do is prepare the glasses. We draw on them the beautiful initials of the bride and groom, and frame them with hearts. Now paint over with acrylic paint. Black will be the most readable, and if you use something with sparkles, then be sure that guests will not be able to make out the labels.

When the glass is ready, you can proceed to sculpting the figures. They are made of polymer clay. We sculpt the bride first. A ball of bodily material is rolling - it will be the head. From thin sausages we create a hairstyle, as well as fashion eyes, nose and mouth. Now we make three sausages. One is thicker, the other two are thinner. We fasten them together and attach the head on top. Now we make two more body balls and glue them to the sleeves. To make a skirt it is necessary to roll out a white layer. Cut it into equal strips. Now you should pick up each of them, and twist into a ring. The skirt will consist of three such segments. We glue all the details and the figure of the bride is ready.

Now you should make the groom. We create the head by analogy with the previous one, only you should make a short, not a long hairstyle. Now we sculpt a rectangle and four sausages of black color. Two of them are thinner - these will be hands, and two thicker - these will be legs. Now we connect the parts of the groom. From a white material we cut a triangle and glue it under the neck. It will be a shirt. On it you need to stick a bow tie, assembled from triangles. Roll body and black balls. The corporeal are the arms, and the black are the legs. We collect the figure and send them to the oven for half an hour. Then they can be attached to the glasses.

Painting + application

Rhinestones Decor

Making glasses for a wedding with your own hands will look very impressive if you use something shiny. For example, rhinestones and beads. That it did not look gone, it is necessary to think over a drawing. There is no need to decorate the glass from all sides. Only one face can be made. Do not worry that guests will not see your work. From bright light, rhinestones will shine even through the festive champagne.

How to create decor from rhinestones? We draw a sketch on paper, and then transfer it to glass. Now we glue the rhinestones alternately with the help of a hot gun. We start not from below and not from the side. First of all we glue the biggest details. In our case, this is the central vertical line. When it sits firmly in place, you can move on to the fine decor. We spread waves, flowers or any other pattern according to your drawing.

Such work does not need additional coverage. It makes no sense to varnish it, as in this case the rhinestones will become worse to shine.

Painting

Thinking of making your own wedding glasses for your wedding? The master class below will help you with this.

The first thing to do is draw a sketch on paper. Nowhere without it. What should be the subject? It can be flowers or just beautiful patterns as in our case. Here's the idea. If you put two glasses together, you get a heart. When the sketch is ready, it must be transferred to glass.

The image on the glass should be applied with a wax pencil. When the sketch is ready, you can start painting. Take the contour - this is a paint for glass. It is translucent, silver, gold, with and without sparkles. And now you need to draw a pencil line. An interesting effect is obtained when two types of paint supplement the work. One of them is a convex contour, and the other is flat acrylic. With a regular brush, you can paint over leaves or petals.

When the work is ready, it should be supplemented with decorative details. It can be rhinestones or small beads. The main thing is not to overdo it. The emphasis should remain on the painting, and not on the placer of shiny pebbles.
