Nemo is the only water park in Yeysk and the best in its environs!

The city of Yeysk is only gaining its popularity as a resort center. And this is a great reason to choose it as a place to live during your summer vacation. There are not too many vacationers, and the prices for accommodation and entertainment are affordable. There is also a water park in Yeysk, which means that your vacation in this city will be interesting and complete.

Nemo: water fun for the whole family



The water attraction center is designed for family visits. For the convenience of visitors, the territory of the water park is divided into separate zones, each of which has its own purpose. There is a safe pool with slides for the little ones, rides for teens, extreme entertainment for adults, as well as relaxation areas and an artificial beach. The water park in Yeysk is a place where you can spend all day with pleasure. There are cafes right on the territory of the complex, where you can fully dine with the whole family or have a snack. The abundance and variety of water rides and related entertainment will not let even the most demanding vacationers get bored.

Water park in Yeysk: photos and descriptions of slides



Toddlers from 3 years old can enjoy splashing around in the children's pool, the depth of which is only 60 centimeters. There are also small bright slides, absolutely safe for this tender age. Rules of visit and the exact address of the center of water entertainment

The water park operates in two shifts, each of which is 4 hours. The first session starts at 10.00, the second - at 15.00. You can also purchase a daily subscription, which gives the right to visit the water park from 10.00 to 19.00. The cost of one session (4 hours) for adults is 800 rubles, for children - 700 rubles. A daily subscription costs 1000 rubles for visitors over 14 years old, and 900 rubles for those who have not yet reached this age. Please note that the water park in Yeysk "Nemo" is in the open air, you can visit it only during the holiday season in favorable weather. The exact address of the center of water entertainment: Yeysk, Schmidt Street, possession 6. You can get here by minibus number 4 (stop "Berdyanskaya Street") or number 7 (terminal station "Aquapark").

The water park pleases guests of the resort with a variety of slopes and convenient zoning by age. In general, the water park in Yeysk "Nemo" has positive reviews. However, some visitors complain about long lines and high prices. At the height of the tourist season, especially on weekends, there are really a lot of visitors in the water park.
