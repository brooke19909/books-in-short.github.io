The Sony NEX 7 camera has a body made of magnesium alloys. The device is quite heavy. The device differs from other cameras of the NEX line by a special type of viewfinder, the presence of additional controls, and a navigation key. The camera is equipped with a 7.7 W / h battery. This is a fairly modest figure. Declared by the manufacturer, the battery life of the device is 335 frames. Device Features

Features of the practical use of the device

Let us now examine what are the practical nuances of using a camera. First of all, we note that you can start shooting after about 0.64 after turning on the device. This indicator is probably not the most outstanding - many competing solutions work more efficiently, but quite acceptable for most scenarios of using the camera in practice.

One of the most popular device options is automatic shooting. As a rule, it is carried out in 3 main modes - when recording files in JPG format with minimal compression, when recording data in RAW format, as well as in combined mode - also with the lowest compression level. In accordance with the declared characteristics, the Sony NEX 7 camera can provide an automatic shooting speed of about 10 frames per second. Is this so in practice?

As tests performed by experts show, the device as a whole provides the declared speed of automatic shooting. True, depending on the file format used, the number of frames in one series of automatic photographing will vary. If the pictures are recorded in JPEG format, then there will be 13 frames in the queue, when using the RAW standard - 12, while using both formats at the same time - 11.





Another parameter that is significant from the point of view of the practical operation of the device is the focusing speed. The device in question shows excellent results in the process of measuring it. If you use lenses for Sony NEX 7, operating in the range of 18-55 mm, then it is real to observe the focusing speed in the range of 0.11-0.22 seconds. Many cameras in more expensive segments are slower. In turn, if you use a lens with a constant focus, then the corresponding indicator can be even more impressive - in the range of 0.09-0.13 seconds.

Movie Features

The device in question, like many other devices of this class, can shoot video in Full HD format. At the same time, the user can adjust such parameters as the shutter speed, aperture, sensitivity, white balance, set the necessary focus options. At the same time, work with the appropriate settings can be carried out directly at the moment when the video is being shot. The sound during video recording is fixed in stereo format. It can be recorded using built-in microphones, which, experts say, work very efficiently, and when using external devices. In addition, the sound module of the camera has such a function as suppressing noise that appears in the environment due to strong winds.
