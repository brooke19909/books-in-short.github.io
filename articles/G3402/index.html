Rhodes (Kallithea). How to spend a vacation?

Probably, it is rarely possible now to meet a modern person who would never in his life have heard of such a terrific place as Rhodes (Kallithea). What attracts here annually not even thousands, but hundreds of thousands of tourists from all over the world? Indeed, there is probably something special or even unique in this corner of the globe.

This article will not only tell you what Rhodes (Kallithea) is, what kind of sea is in this area and what weather should be expected in different months of the year, the reader will receive valuable tips on where to go on a tour and how to get to the place destination.

General description of the resort of Rhodes (Kallithea)

On the east coast of the island of Rhodes , the amazing resort of Kallithea is located. Recently, the tourism industry has been actively developing here. And the prerequisites for this factor are indeed many. A healthy climate and a magnificent sea are really wonderful conditions in order to have a great rest at any time of the year.

A large hotel chain is actively operating in this area. Tourists are also always welcome in numerous restaurants and bars. Here, among other things, all the necessary conditions have been created for fans to dance to fiery music.

Many travelers who came to Rhodes (Kallithea) in the summer are interested in the healing springs, which the bay is so rich in. There is also an old hospital. There are many entertainments here: excursions to picturesque bays, leisurely walks along the promenade and visits to amazing caves. The natural conditions of Kallithea are as if specially created for relaxation and enjoyment of the beauty of nature. There is also a great place for diving: the colorful underwater world is ready to give everyone amazing adventures.

Features of relaxation on the beach





Many argue that the main place they want to go on vacation is the beach. Kallithea (Rhodes) is actually a unique territory for relaxation with the whole family or in a cheerful friendly company. It is also an excellent SPA resort, therefore, in search of a long-awaited vacation, a large number of travelers seek here every year.

The beaches are usually crowded. However, in small bays, you can relax and enjoy the quiet rustling of the waves rolling on the shore. The sea off the coast is crystal clear. A beach holiday near Kallithea is perfect for those who love the sea and a relaxing relaxing environment.

The Baths of Kallithea, Rhodes





Local beliefs call this place fabulous, magical, and sometimes even mystical. Why so? The fact is that the thermal springs located along the coast, not in vain attract tourists. The healing properties of thermal waters have long been known.

In Kallithea, by the way, ancient baths have been preserved, designed for taking therapeutic baths and procedures. Their restoration has already been carried out, and therefore today it is possible not only to enjoy their appearance, but also to use it for its intended purpose.

Where to stay? Hotel Examples





Rhodes (Kallithea) can boast of the presence of many hotels in which comfortable conditions are actually created, as they say, for every taste and budget. Rest in an inexpensive hotel is a chance to have a great time. However, a low price is often guaranteed only to those who book a room by contacting the hotel directly.

For example, Faliraki Bay 2 * guarantees inexpensive accommodation. It is popular with those vacationers who prefer a relaxing holiday. The area around the hotel is neat and tidy.

Hotel Apollo Beach 4 * is located near the city, so shops, taverns, clubs and cafes are just 5 minutes on foot. A beautiful landscape and a wide strip of the beach are exactly what you need for young people and tourists who come to rest with their children.

Aldemar Paradise Village 5 * is a hotel that is part of one of the best hotel chains that welcome tourists in Greece. For those who love outdoor activities, all conditions are created here. The developed tourist infrastructure in this hotel ensures the availability of optimal factors for vacationers of any age. I really like spending time in this hotel for young people and couples with children.

What to see in the surroundings





Many interesting things are located near the resort called Kallithea (Rhodes). The reviews of travelers who have been there are another confirmation of this fact.

Tourists with small children are primarily recommended to visit the water park and Luna Park in Faliraki, near Kallithea. In adults, as a rule, it is interesting to visit an ostrich farm, the Lindos Acropolis. Thermal springs are an amazing natural attraction that attracts many tourists. By the way, not everyone knows that the building, where the terms are located, is decorated with a beautiful ancient mosaic.

Transport and modern entertainment





You can get to Kallithea by bus. The journey from Rhodes will be 15 minutes. The road to Faliraki is even shorter. You can, of course, use a rented car.

Many say that there are all conditions for an active fun holiday. Young people are happy to visit local discos, the largest of which, so as not to disturb anyone, is 2 km from the village. The resort offers incendiary dances, amazing horseback riding, go-karting and bowling. Fans of entertainment here really will not be bored.

But for those who are interested in history, there is a chance to get acquainted with the ruins that have remained from the ancient temples built in honor of Zeus and Apollo. You can also visit the nearby town of Afitos with a surprisingly rich ethnographic museum.
