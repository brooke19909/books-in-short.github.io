Hotel Kheops 4 *: photos, prices and reviews of tourists

If you have to choose a place to stay, then you should pay attention to Tunisia. A unique and memorable vacation can be spent in the wonderful hotel complex Kheops Hotel 4 *. Here you will find a beautiful sea, a beautiful underwater world, clean coastlines, an informative excursion program and amazing landscapes. Each vacationer will receive a unique extravaganza of emotions and feelings, have a great time. The hotel will delight vacationers with a high level of service and a favorable atmosphere.



Here it is - Tunisia

Tunisia is a small country located in northern Africa. Most of the population lives on the Mediterranean coast. The southern part of the country can be called almost deserted. The main population - Arabs - are friendly to all tourists.

Do not believe the one who says there is nothing to watch in Tunisia. This is said by those tourists who spent all their time on the beach and were too lazy to see the sights of this country. The Colosseum, which ranks second in size after the Roman one, has been preserved here. You can not only see it, but also make a journey through the underground passages, through the halls where wild animals were kept.

In the mountainous part of Tunisia, the place of Matmata, where the truly native inhabitants - Berbers still live - has managed to preserve their identity. They live in caves and are not only ready to show tourists their homes, but also treat them to green tea, prepared according to local recipes. One cannot but recall Carthage, where excavations are constantly being carried out and more and more exhibits are found that replenish the shelves of museums. The beautiful sea resort of Nabeul, where the Kheops 4 * hotel is located, also retained its originality. Before proceeding to the description of the hotel, I would like to talk a little about the town in order to remove all doubts about the quality of rest in this hotel complex.

Former Neapolis - Nabeul Resort

If you go on vacation to Nabeul, you will be met by a beautiful seaside resort, which is the center of pottery production. In front of the railway station is its symbol - a huge clay jug. In addition, souvenir shops are open all over the city, where vacationers can purchase Nabel craftsmen‚Äôs products made of kaolin clay and covered with blue azure. The most popular are shops on Sidi Barket Street.

At the top of Cape Bon, the small village of Al-Hawaria retained its identity. Here are picturesque grottoes, where locals hunt for falconers.

The best place for shopping is the Souq al-Juma market, where you can buy everything from painting, leather products and perfumes to various spices and exotic fruits.

All hotels are located on the coastal strip. No exception is the Kheops Hotel 4 *. It is at this hotel complex that we will stop our attention.

Hotel location

Kheops 4 * and Les Pyramides 3 * make up a single hotel complex located on the first coastline. It lies north of the city of Hammamet, not far from the central part of Nabeul, just 600 meters away. A small remoteness from nightlife attracts here those who like a relaxing holiday, as well as numerous families with children. The complex is separated from the city of Hammamet by a distance of 10 kilometers, 10 times more is the distance to the city of Monastir. Around are numerous hotels, stretching a whole string along the coastline. The closest are Omar Khayyam and Nabeul.



When choosing a hotel very often mistaken, as there is a hotel with a similar name, located in the nearest city (Hammamet) - Kheops Aqua 4 *. It is also a wonderful hotel, and it is located nearby, but still it is a completely different establishment.

How to get there

Getting to the hotel complex is not difficult. First of all, you need to get into Tunisia itself. Nabeul (Kheops 4 * is located here) is located 76 kilometers from Tunis-Carthage International Airport. From here you can get by rail, taking a ticket to the stop Gare de Nabeul, and then use public transport to get to the nearest stop to the hotel, which is 1 kilometer from the hotel complex. If the luggage is small, you can walk on foot, but if its weight is significant, it is better to use a taxi. Traveling here is relatively inexpensive. So, for example, for $ 3 you can get from one end of the city to the other.

General description of the hotel

Among the hotels located in the city, Kheops 4 * (Tunisia) is very popular among tourists. This excellent hotel is the best suited for an economical vacation in comfortable conditions. An important role is played by the proximity of the beach and the central part of the city, which attracts tourists of various categories and ages to relax.



The hotel itself consists of a six-story building, towering in the central part of the hotel complex. All guest rooms have a unique and elegant design. All rooms have comfortable furniture. For the convenience of holidaymakers, the building has an elevator. There is a terrace in front of the entrance. The hotel is separated from the bungalow of Les Pyramides by a citrus garden, a walk through which will cheer up even the most demanding guest. The hotel placed its first guests in 1989 and has not ceased to please tourists since then.

Number of rooms

For those who came to rest in Tunisia, the Kheops 4 * hotel provides 319 rooms that are equipped with central air conditioning. In this hotel there are only two types of rooms: standard, family. Accommodation options can be different - from 1 to 3 people. The most popular are standard rooms, consisting of a main living room and a bathroom.



The apartments are bright and spacious enough, decorated in soft light colors. Comfortable furniture allows the guest to feel the coziness and comfort inherent in hotels of the corresponding category. All rooms have satellite TV and a telephone, where you can order any service directly to the apartment. A mini fridge is included. The bathroom has a shower and bath, washbasin, toilet, necessary toiletries, bathrobe, towel and hair dryer.

Each room has a balcony, with a magnificent view of the citrus garden and the blue sea behind it. The rooms are always clean, for which cleaning is done every day. Towels are also changed daily.

Nutrition





Unlike similar world-class hotels with a category of 4 *, hotels in Tunisia, in addition to European dishes, offer a large selection of treats prepared according to Arabic recipes. No exception is Kheops 4 * (Nabeul). For his guests, he offers a choice of two types of food: HB (breakfast and dinner) and all inclusive ("all inclusive"). Breakfast and lunch are served in the main restaurant as a buffet. Guests are offered a wide selection of dishes, including more than three types of meat dishes, seafood. A huge selection of fruits and vegetable salads is also pleasing to guests. And the taste of pastries and Arabian sweets is simply impossible to describe in words. Upon prior reservation, breakfast can be served directly in the room. As for dinner, it is ordered according to the menu.

Bars and Restaurants

In addition to the main restaurant, in Kheops 4 * There are a number of other establishments where you can eat and have a great time. The most popular among tourists is the a la carte restaurant, which operates only in the evening. Its doors for vacationers open at 7 pm and delight guests for 3 hours. In addition to the excellent dishes that tourists can try visiting this place, the folklore ensemble here pleases with its music and song, filling the atmosphere with unique notes. There is also a Moorish cafe on site that opens at 2 p.m. and is open until midnight.

There is also a Tunisian restaurant, a snack bar and a poolside bar. It offers quite exquisite dishes, most of which are made from tuna. These are various hot dishes, salads. Even the proposed pastries are cooked with this fish. What else can be noted in these dishes is a huge number of different spices, spices and vegetable oils. A traditional drink is green tea with almonds and mint, which is served with pine nuts. No less popular is coffee with spices and cardamom.

In the daytime, you can also have a meal in the pizzeria. For lovers of nightclubs and discos, there is a disco bar, dancing in which lasts until 2 a.m.

Hotel beach area

Kheops Hotel 4 * has its own beach adjacent to the territory. To get on it, you need to go through the citrus garden and past the bungalow. The sandy beach of the hotel is quite clean. Sun loungers and parasols are provided for vacationers at an additional cost. The towel must be taken, because here it does not give out. Thanks to the convenient entrance to the sea, many families with children relax here. On the beach, hotel guests will not be bored. Various activities are offered here: water skiing, surfing, sailing. There is a "banana" and jet skis. For those who like more relaxing entertainment, you can take a walk on a catamaran and see the beauty of coastal landscapes. And if someone is hungry, then a bar works on the beach, but only in the summer. It is open from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m.

Issue price

The cost of the tour with accommodation in this hotel (at the beginning of December 2014) for 7 nights for two adults is 53 902 rubles. This includes the cost of air tickets in both directions. Vacationers are provided with a standard one-room suite without a specific view from the windows. If you want the same room, but with a sea view, the cost of the tour will increase by 1200-1300 rubles. All prices are for HB packages.



Kheops pricing policy 4 * determined by the comfort of the apartment and has a seasonal dependence. Staying at a hotel during the low season, as well as pre-booking, can save on the cost of trips and spend significantly less.

Territory, infrastructure and services

What else is attractive about the Kheops 4 * hotel? Guest reviews mark a wide selection of diverse services. It has a large pool on the main terrace. For the convenience of guests, sunbeds and umbrellas near it are provided for free. Besides him, there is an indoor pool.



For those who decide to combine leisure with work, an excellently equipped conference room and a business center work. If necessary, you can use the Wi-Fi Internet. Among the transport services, car rental can be noted. With the help of a rental car, you can carry out not only a business trip, but also a sightseeing trip. Additional services include a laundry service and a safety deposit box at the front desk.

The hotel owners did not forget about the little guests. They also have a small pool and a kids club.

Sports and Entertainment

Relaxing at this hotel, guests will not be bored. There are a huge number of various activities and entertainment. For those who monitor their physical fitness even while resting, there is a gym. In addition to it, there are tennis courts, minigolf and table tennis. A variety of entertainment is offered by the water sports center. In addition to everything, an excellent animation team is actively working. They do not let you get bored, arranging all kinds of entertainment events. Particular attention is paid to young guests. In addition to various entertainments, interesting outdoor games are held for them. And so that all vacationers feel comfortable during the rest, a folklore show is held in the evening, and a disco at night.

Guest reviews

And what do tourists say about their stay at the Kheops 4 * hotel (Tunisia)? Reviews vacationers note first of all that the conditions of stay and service at this hotel are fully consistent with the number of stars. Many positive reviews were left regarding the location of the hotel and its proximity to the beach. The citrus orchard did not go unnoticed. He is remembered in every third review.

The excellent Arabian cuisine, delighting guests with its variety and exquisite taste, was also noted with positive comments. The rooms marked with such qualities as cleanliness, cosiness and comfort also earned their plus.

Not without claims. The hotel staff, although friendly, does not have knowledge of the Russian language. In order to communicate with employees, you need at least a minimum knowledge of English or French. In addition, not everyone likes the "too intrusive" group of animators, which completely "does not allow passage."

Nevertheless, there are many more positive comments.
