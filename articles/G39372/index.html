Features vacation. In which countries is it warm in October?

In which countries is it warm in October? Perhaps, many of us had to not only hear this question from our relatives, friends and employees, but also periodically ask ourselves. If so, then let's try to deal with the problem together.

Section 1. In which countries is it warm in October? Relevance of the issue



The second month of autumn in central Russia is almost always rain and a piercing wind. Is it possible to have a good rest if the vacation fell exactly in October? Of course, there are many resorts in the world and, of course, there are those where it is always warm, there is a lot of sun and wonderful evergreens grow.

When choosing an autumn tour, one must be especially careful so that the rest is not spoiled by bad weather. If the choice is successful, then you can emerge from the cold rainy autumn, to be where the beautiful summer is. By the way, there will be much more positive feelings than when you go to hot countries in the summer.

Section 3. In which countries is it warm in October? Sun and sea

Perhaps every student, almost without hesitation, will answer that Thailand, Turkey and Egypt - this is where it is warm in October

In Thailand, prices are fairly low in October . It is wonderful that the rainy season ends in September, so you can have a very good rest in this country. Unimaginably beautiful places and a wonderful clear sea - for everyone who wants to leave from the cloudy cold weather, a trip to Thailand will do just fine.
