How to make a dress from corrugated paper: a master class

Corrugated paper is a very plastic and comfortable material, which is pleasant and easy to work with. Craftsmen using various techniques for creativity are constantly in search of new ideas. Therefore, they have long been aware of the main methods of using crepe paper: to create flowers, bouquets of sweets and huge fluffy balls. Some craftswomen make jewelry and paintings using this material, but still, the technique for making interior gizmos is based primarily on the assembly of flowers. It is from buds that compositions for bouquets and accessories are made.

Leah Griffith: paper dress designer

But few people know that you can make a dress out of corrugated paper. Some foreign designers use this material to create real masterpieces and demonstrate them at fashion shows. For example, the world-famous fashion designer, stylist and photographer Leah Griffith has created a collection of dresses made of corrugated paper, a master class for the manufacture of which she shows in her blog.



Fashion career

The girl became popular thanks to the creation of flowers from corrugated paper: she was so carried away by this occupation and came up with so many unusual techniques that she turned a hobby into a profitable business. Her work is very famous among needlewomen working with this material, and inspire many women to master a new interesting kind of handmade. Leah Griffith worked with various types of paper, including creating wardrobe details from magazines, but began her career with women's clothing using recycled Xerox products.

The process of creating dresses from corrugated paper is very painstaking and time-consuming. Now in the collection of the master more than 40 works. Of course, these works of art cannot be washed and reused. They are created only for shows and photo shoots. Currently, the designer is preparing her own line of corrugated, papyrus and wax paper, inspiring needlewomen for new achievements in creating accessories.



Creating women's dresses from paper

Crepe paper is a very pliable, delicate and versatile material that holds its shape well and gives products a sense of lightness. For a dress with a full skirt, you need to prepare a lot of details similar to flower petals. They can be made in completely different shapes, extended, twisted and bent. Elements are attached with a glue gun to a fabric corset. Of course, you must first come up with a model and develop a sketch. Then patterns are made, as for a regular dress, and the product is assembled according to them.



Dress from corrugated paper for the girl

Sometimes at school or in kindergarten, a child wants to participate in a do-it-yourself dresses contest. Crepe paper is also useful here. As a workpiece, you can use the usual combination or sew the frame yourself. From corrugated paper we make large petals, stretching them to the sides to give a curved shape. A fluffy skirt will need approximately 70 pieces. We attach rows of petals with a stapler or glue gun. For the upper part of the dress, we make roses or other flowers and glue them. The number of buds depends on the size of the product.

Dress for the doll

If this option seems too complicated, but you want to try the technique, you can make a dress of corrugated paper with your own hands for a Barbie doll or a similar toy. Often these figures have problems with the lower body, for example, their legs are lost. Do not rush to throw such a toy. It can be used to create a birthday present or as an unusual piece of furniture in a children's room. You can also attract a child to create the outfit: any girl will be interested in making a dress from corrugated paper with her own hands for her own doll. Such an experience will help children develop creative thinking, determination and perseverance.



DIY doll dresses

The lower part of the body will be covered with a cone made of thick cardboard or foam, on which the figure is fixed with glue. Due to him, the doll will be fixed in an upright position even without legs. In this master class, you will learn how to make a dress from corrugated paper for a doll in one of the simplest and at the same time having many options, techniques: from paper flowers. The material allows you to create beautiful elements without much difficulty. Petals can not even be cut, but simply twisted from a long strip, forming a bud.

You can make flowers resembling roses by folding the paper several times and cutting out the same circles from it: just fasten the blanks in the center with a stapler, spread the petals and the rose is ready! Some masters do not even fasten the parts, but simply tuck the layers into each other, getting a neat bud. If you want to work hard and create a real masterpiece, you should look for patterns of different types of flowers, for example, roses or poppies, and collect them from individual elements. Making doll dresses: a master class

How to make a dress from corrugated paper? As an example, take a simple option. For work, we need:

  • several rolls of multi-colored paper,
  • a piece of cardboard or foam for the base,
  • masking tape
  • scissors,
  • glue gun
  • decorative elements for dress decoration: beads, lace, ribbons,
  • thin wire.

If the doll is without legs, the lower part of the skirt can be covered with a circle cut out of an opaque plastic cover. A dress made of corrugated paper is assembled as follows:

  1. We make a cardboard cone, fixing the edges with masking tape so that there is enough opening for the doll on top.
  2. Cut the blanks for the upper and lower parts of the dress.
  3. We place the doll inside the workpiece and fix it in place with tape.
  4. We fix the bodice. Wrap the skirt blank with a sheet of paper. Decorative elements will be mounted on it.
  5. Stretching the paper on the bottom of the skirt, we make ‚Äúwaves‚Äù.
  6. We make out of paper in tone roses or other flowers, at our discretion.
  7. Glue the blanks to the dress in random order.

A simple example of creating a dress for a doll

You can do even easier and make a skirt of corrugated paper from blanks that will be just folded rectangular pieces. They are made on the principle of origami and can be of different colors, but of the same shape. First you need to make a lot of such modules, and then assemble it on a doll by gluing it to the frame.

It is done like this:

  1. A rectangular blank of paper is folded in half along the long side.
  2. Then cut in half on the short side.
  3. The resulting square is revealed, and the upper corners are bent to the center, as on a paper airplane.
  4. The edges of the lower corners are bent up.
  5. The horizontal bottom bends upward.
  6. The resulting triangle folds in half.

Using these blanks, you can transform the shape of the product by changing the number of elements in each next row. A dress made of corrugated paper, made in this technique, is fixed on the doll using an adhesive gun. It is better not to use PVA glue for this purpose, since the paper gets wet from it and can tear.

Choose a suitable option for yourself and start creating!
