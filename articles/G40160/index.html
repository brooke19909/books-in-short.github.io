Schmitt trigger in electronics

Triggers can often be found in electronic circuits. They participate in the work of many nodes, performing a wide variety of tasks, or can be implemented on different elements, but the principle of their work remains the same. A special case is the so-called Schmitt trigger, which has proven itself in work. This useful modification of the original circuit in a short time gained immense popularity among designers.



There are a variety of options for implementing this idea: a trigger on transistors, operational amplifiers, digital circuits, etc. As an example, consider the Schmitt digital trigger, the principle of which will help us in general to deal with this device.
