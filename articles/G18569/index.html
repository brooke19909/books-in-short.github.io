Whispering Woods by The Verda Reviews

Many travelers dream of going to Goa. Whispering Woods by The Verda is a beautiful modern Indian hotel, which will give you a quality vacation and a lot of positive impressions.





Brief description of the hotel

Whispering Woods by The Verda is a great place for those looking for a vibrant experience and also want to receive a high level of service. Here you will find friendly and qualified staff who are ready at any time to come to your aid and solve problems. You will certainly be pleased with the modern and elegant style of the hotel, as well as the fact that in its courtyard there is a thick shady garden that will protect you from the Indian heat.

Whispering Woods by The Verda (India) invites you to stay in small cozy villas that are decorated in a modern style with traditional Indian notes. The apartments will give you a feeling of absolute comfort, as well as homely warmth and coziness. In the rooms you will have everything you need for life, including a small kitchen, which will allow you to cook your favorite dishes on your own and not spend money on food in restaurants.





Resort Location

Whispering Woods by The Verda is located just one kilometer from the popular, clean Anjuna Beach. Overcoming 9 km, you will find yourself in Calangute - at the very epicenter of resort tourism. You can take the train that leaves from Tivima train station (18 km from the hotel). Goa International Airport is 44 km from the resort.

Hotel Rooms

Whispering Woods by The Verda Resort offers guests one of the 32 guestrooms located in cozy two-story villas. Each of them can have 3, 4 and 5 bedrooms. If you wish, you can rent the whole villa.

Each guest room at Whispering Woods by The Verda Resort features the following amenities:

  • individual air conditioning, which will help you create a comfortable cool climate, which will favorably differ from the Indian heat;
  • thanks to the TV, you can fill your leisure time, staying in your room due to bad weather, or watch entertainment before bedtime;
  • depending on which floor the apartments will be located, you will have a small balcony or your own cozy terrace;
  • in each of the rooms you will be able to use free wireless Internet;
  • as only diet breakfasts are served in the hotel restaurant, travelers should be pleased with the small but functional and well-equipped kitchen in the apartment;
  • You can store the products you bought in a small refrigerator;
  • You can always drink hot tea or coffee, because the rooms are provided with sets for making these drinks;
  • The bathroom is a shower room, combined with a toilet, and there is also a hairdryer, disposable cosmetics and bath accessories.




Resort infrastructure

Whispering Woods by The Verda Resort provides guests the opportunity to take advantage of the following infrastructure facilities:

  • an open pool with clean water that is disinfected with chlorine;
  • You can try unique Indian dishes by visiting the restaurant of this hotel;
  • there is a cozy bar on the territory where pleasant relaxing music is constantly played, and there is also a huge assortment of alcoholic and sweet carbonated drinks;
  • for sports enthusiasts, as well as those who seek to maintain a beautiful and slim figure, there is a gym with the most modern equipment;
  • in the business center you will have the opportunity to use office equipment;
  • for own and rented transport private car parking is provided;
  • in the courtyard of the hotel there is a dense green garden that creates a cool shade;
  • if you stained your clothes, then you can give them to the laundry for laundry;
  • there is wireless Internet throughout the hotel;
  • The reception is open around the clock, and therefore you can contact the hotel staff at any time for questions that arise with you, as well as for solving problems.




Positive reviews

Tourists have long been fond of the Whispering Woods by The Verda Resort and Hotel Complex. Reviews about it consist of a mass of positive comments, as well as thanks and bright impressions:

  • Despite the fact that all the equipment in the room is quite old, it works quite well;
  • the beach is relatively close - about 15 minutes walk from the hotel;
  • You can independently adjust the cleaning schedule, leaving a request for a reception.




Negative reviews

In addition to the positive aspects, there are also some negative aspects of living in this hotel. It is worth highlighting the following:

  • due to the fact that the roads near the hotel are very narrow, bus drivers refuse to go in this direction, and therefore you have to order an expensive taxi;
  • during check-in there are unpleasant moments when the reception staff knowingly gives not very good numbers, hoping for a surcharge for relocation;
  • sometimes in the hotel there are problems with hygiene items, which must be provided during check-in and reported during cleaning, so sometimes you have to buy everything yourself;
  • the bedding is very old;
  • neither towels nor sheets are changed during the whole rest (you have to independently catch the maid so that she fulfills this requirement);
  • the hotel is not connected to the central water supply (tanks are filled by a car that arrives several times a week), and therefore there are situations when there is no water in the tap all day or even two;
  • the rooms have insects (ants, mosquitoes, the worst is large cockroaches);
  • at night or at the time when you leave the room, it is better to close the balcony, as there are cases of theft;
  • the rooms have very poor sound insulation, and therefore the rooms are always very noisy (this is especially true for those who live on the ground floor).

Despite some negative points, we can conclude: the hotel offers quite comfortable living conditions. The hotel is close to the beach, but far enough from the hustle and bustle and busy traffic routes.
