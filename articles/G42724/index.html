Hotel Hydramis Palace Beach Resort 4 * (Crete, Greece): photos and reviews of tourists

Dreaming of an unforgettable Greek vacation, pay attention to such an option as Hydramis Palace Beach Resort. This cozy four-star hotel will provide you with access to quality services, the warm azure sea and many infrastructure facilities.





Location

Hydramis Palace Beach Resort is located in the resort village of Georgioupoli, famous for its clean beaches and beautiful mountain landscapes. This settlement is located between Chania and Rethymnon, the distance to which is respectively 40 km and 15 km. To get to Heraklion International Airport, you will have to overcome about 90 km.





Where to go?

In an incredibly beautiful area rich in attractions is the Hydramis Palace Beach Resort. Resting here, be sure to visit the following objects:

  • Kournas Lake (5 km);
  • healing springs of Argyroupoli (11 km);
  • Ethnographic Museum (12 km);
  • the old fortress of Fortezza (15 km);
  • walking park (15 km);
  • History Museum (15 km);
  • Center for Byzantine Culture (16 km);
  • archaeological museum (16 km);
  • Venetian port (17 km).

Accommodation

At the Hydramis Palace Beach Resort you will live in an atmosphere of home comfort and absolute comfort. Here for your accommodation will be offered the following options:

  • Standard double rooms are decorated in a warm homely manner and are characterized by an area sufficient for a comfortable stay of 2 guests. In addition to beds, there is a dressing table with a mirror, a cozy corner for relaxation, a chest of drawers and other furniture. The furnished balcony will provide you with direct or side views of the sea or the hotel‚Äôs lush garden.
  • One of the double rooms is equipped according to the needs of people with disabilities. The bathroom has safety rails and anti-slip mats. All sockets and switches in the room are located at a convenient height. Well, of course, do not forget about the wide doorways that will allow you to comfortably move in a wheelchair.
  • Spacious family rooms consist of two rooms. The children's bedroom has a pair of beds and a soft sofa. The room for parents is equipped with a king size bed, and also has access to the balcony. An additional condition for comfort is the presence of two bathrooms.
  • The luxurious two-story maisonette is decorated in an elegant manner using natural wood decor. The second level is equipped with a luxurious bedroom, which is adjacent to the dressing room and bathroom. The lower floor is a living room, an additional bedroom and a shower room. The highlight of the room is its own roof terrace, from which you can enjoy picturesque views of the sea and mountains.

Useful information about the hotel

Going to the Hydramis Palace Beach Resort 4 * hotel, you should consider some facts related to the rules of accommodation. So, such moments deserve special attention:

  • check-in and eviction of guests is carried out, respectively, after 2 pm and before noon;
  • Babies up to 2 years old get free extra beds;
  • an extra bed for a child under 6 years old will cost 20 euros, and for an adult - 30 euros per night;
  • only one additional guest is allowed in each room;
  • pets are not allowed;
  • when booking a room, a cash deposit must be paid in non-cash form;
  • payment for services in cash cannot exceed 1,500 euros;
  • children under 12 years old are not allowed in the pool unaccompanied by adults;
  • The cost of using the in-room safe is 3 euros per day.




Positive reviews

Hydramis Palace Beach Resort gives travelers bright moments and pleasant memories. The reviews contain such positive comments:

  • the territory, although small, is very well-groomed and literally immersed in greenery (here you can find olive trees, grapes, and even pomegranates);
  • high-quality cleaning, regardless of the presence or absence of a tip;
  • large restaurant with a huge selection of delicious food;
  • many free sunbeds on the beach, as well as near the pool;
  • the restaurant bakes delicious bread and cakes;
  • good playground with a safe flooring;
  • excellent sandy beach with a good sunset in the sea;
  • in the territory there is a store with affordable prices;
  • trees and shrubs on the territory are carefully looked after, constantly trimmed, giving them a beautiful shape;
  • to replace beach towels, you do not need to go to the reception desk or anywhere else (just leave them in the room, and the maid will do everything herself);
  • there are Russian-speaking employees;
  • upon check-in, guests are given an information sheet detailing the rules of hotel accommodation, as well as tariffs for services;
  • morning yoga classes;
  • a lot of natural marble in the design of the facade of the buildings, as well as in the interior;
  • if you check into the hotel late at night, you will find dinner in the refrigerator of your room;
  • thanks to well-thought-out animation, children are constantly busy with something and do not sit idle;
  • after 23:00 on the territory the music is turned off.

Negative reviews

Unfortunately, no rest can do without negative moments. Speaking about this institution, it is worth noting its disadvantages:

  • the hotel is located quite far from large settlements and tourist infrastructure;
  • the restaurant does not provide a children's menu (and the main assortment includes too many spicy and spicy dishes);
  • wireless Internet can be used only at the reception, but even there its signal is extremely unstable;
  • construction is underway on both sides of the hotel, which not only spoils the appearance, but also creates a lot of noise;
  • the animation is mainly in French;
  • tea is not served during dinner (you have to go for a drink at the bar);
  • waiters are not always courteous with guests;
  • due to the fact that a busy highway runs near the hotel, you will be chased by the noise of cars and the smell of exhaust gases;
  • in the afternoon, a terrible pressure of water in the shower (the situation normalizes only late at night, when everyone was already swimming).

General impression

Perhaps Hydramis Palace Beach Resort (Crete) can be considered one of the best options for budget accommodation in Crete. This is a unique combination of beautiful design, comfortable living conditions, bright but moderate entertainment, quality service and family warmth. As for the location, it can be considered in two ways. On the one hand, remoteness from large cities can be considered a disadvantage. But just such a dislocation provides a quiet and relaxing holiday.

This is a great option for families. During the day, children and parents will be passionate about sea vacations or an animation program, and at night you can fully relax, because you will not be disturbed by loud music. It is also worth paying attention to nutrition. Dishes are prepared deliciously and at home (in the best local traditions). But children's diet dishes on the menu you will not find.
