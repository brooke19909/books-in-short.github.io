Hotel "Park Inn Pulkovskaya" - overview, features and reviews of tourists

Numerous monuments of urban architecture are surrounded by the luxurious Park Inn Pulkovskaya Hotel . Guests of the Northern capital will be able to settle in one of 842 colorful rooms that will fill the rest with an atmosphere of warmth, comfort and coziness.





Location

In a convenient tourist area is the hotel "Park Inn by Radisson Pulkovskaya" . St. Petersburg, Moskovsky District, Victory Square, 1 - the address where you can find this modern and vibrant institution. The location is perfectly characterized by such facts:

  • located a five-minute drive from Pulkovo International Airport (a free shuttle runs regularly between the airport terminal and the hotel);
  • the train station is a 30-minute drive;
  • within walking distance are numerous retail outlets and catering establishments.

What to visit?

St. Petersburg is a real paradise for tourists, because the attractions here are in the hundreds. When staying at the Park Inn Pulkovskaya , be sure to visit such facilities as:

  • Peterhof - the palace and park ensemble of the times of Peter I. Includes monumental buildings, elegant fountains and park areas. This object is under the protection of UNESCO.
  • Tsarskoye Selo is an ideal place for a weekend in the vicinity of the city. Here you can admire the numerous palace buildings, as well as relax in the park area. One of the most attractive objects is the reconstruction of the famous amber room.
  • Victory Square - the location of the famous monument to the Defenders of Leningrad. The memorial complex includes a lot of ground and underground structures that are of historical interest.
  • The park of heroic cities is a complex that includes several churches and historical buildings in memory of the exploits of Soviet soldiers in the Second World War. Also in the park zone grow hundreds of valuable trees.
  • Church of St. Sergius of Radonezh - a temple built in the middle of the Srednerogatsky pond. The construction was designed by the Swiss architect Justus Dahinden.

Hotel "Pulkovskaya Park Inn" cares not only about comfortable living, but also about quality food for guests. For this purpose, the hotel has three establishments:

  • Paulaner is a Bavarian restaurant that specializes in its own brewery. This is one of the best institutions of its kind in St. Petersburg, which is also loved by local residents. The restaurant has a dining room for 600 seats and a banquet area for 100 people. There is also a cozy children's corner. The institution operates as a buffet.

Negative reviews

Such a variant of accommodation as the Park Inn Hotel (St. Petersburg) is not without its drawbacks. Pulkovskaya received such negative reviews:

  • the hygiene kit does not include a toothbrush and toothpaste;

  • unreasonably expensive food at the hotel;
  • lengthy registration procedure, because of which queues can be collected at the reception;
  • 250 rubles are additionally charged for the registration of foreign citizens (although by law this action must be performed by the hotel free of charge);
  • it is unpleasant that at high prices for accommodation for visiting the pool you have to pay extra;

  • not fast enough wireless internet;

  • drinking water is provided in the room only on the day of arrival.
