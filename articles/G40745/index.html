The appearance of the router

On the rear panel there is a WAN port connector (RJ-11 type) for connecting to the Rostelecom provider line and a LAN port connector (RJ-45 type) for connecting a desktop computer (PC). There is also a socket for connecting the power supply and a POWER button to turn it on (ON / OFF). Nearby are the buttons for turning on the WI-FI module, secure connection to a wireless network (WPS), and resetting tinctures to factory settings (RESET).

The non-removable antenna of the WI-FI range has a hinge mechanism that allows you to orient it in different planes.
