LCD "Nekrasovsky" (Dmitrovsky district): customer reviews, photos

Moscow is the capital of the Russian Federation. A huge metropolis in the center of the state. Many come to it to earn more money, many of them remain forever. Prices per square meter of housing in the city are very high. Therefore, more often those who want to buy an apartment look after it outside the city, in the immediate suburbs. LCD "Nekrasovsky" (Dmitrovsky district) just offers such an option for settlement.





A little bit about Dmitrovsky district

In the north of the Moscow region, right on the Dmitrov highway, the city of the same name is located - Dmitrov. It is a medium-sized city with a population of just over sixty thousand people. Dmitrovsky district has a length of more than 70 kilometers from south to north and more than 40 kilometers in width. Over the past fifteen years, its population has been growing steadily, which is associated not only with the natural population growth, but also with the rapid pace of construction of new buildings in the area.





Developers often choose the Dmitrovsky district for the implementation of their projects. After all, it contains all the prospects for development. Nearby is the channel to them. Moscow, Ikshinskoe reservoir, large areas of forest. There are few industrial enterprises that poison the environment.

Location of the complex

LCD "Nekrasovsky" the developer located at approximately the same distance from the Moscow Ring Road and from the city of Dmitrov. About 20-25 kilometers. Thus, the complex is located in the middle, adjacent to the village of Nekrasovsky. Dmitrovsky district belongs to the tourist territory because of its exceptional environmental attractiveness and a large number of historical monuments.





How to get to the complex

LCD "Nekrasovsky" (Dmitrovsky district) is located 26 kilometers from MKAD along Dmitrovsky highway and at a distance of 1.5 kilometers from the Trudovaya railway station. By private car, the complex can be reached in about half an hour. By bus to the metro station "Altufevo" will take about an hour. You can also get to the complex, or rather, to the Trudovaya railway station, from Savelovsky station. Travel time takes less than an hour. The residential complex is located right next to the highway, so finding it is easier than ever.

Description of the complex

What will the Nekrasovsky residential complex (Dmitrovsky district) be when its construction is fully completed in the third quarter of 2017? The project provides for the construction on an individual project of several residential buildings of varying floors from 14 to 17 floors using a monolithic-frame technology. Each house consists of several sections. Only 1140 comfort class apartments. Buildings are being erected in the order of priority: 1, 2 and 3rd. The deadline for the final turn is scheduled for the third quarter of 2017.





The apartments in the residential complex "Nekrasovsky" will feature the most diverse: one-room, two-room and three-room. Their footage is an average of 39 to 80 square meters, depending on the number of rooms. The developer, for the convenience of customers, performs a fine finish and provides future residents with all possible internal communications, including electrical and water distribution. A private boiler house is being built on the territory of the complex, so that residents will not experience inconvenience with interruptions in the supply of heat to the apartments. Infrastructure

The developer designed this residential complex as a comfort class housing, which means that its future residents will be provided with the necessary conditions for a comfortable stay, autonomous infrastructure. Along with residential buildings, a kindergarten, a school, a supermarket, a hotel, a restaurant and a temple will appear in the microdistrict. Residents will be able to receive medical care in their own clinic.





Not a single respectable developer currently designs residential complexes without providing recreation zones, children's and sports grounds and other places for recreation of residents in the project. The Nekrasovsky residential complex (Dmitrovsky district) is no exception, its photos confirm that there is a place for these facilities, and some of them have already been implemented. Besides? It is planned to build a fitness center with a stadium, a fitness center and an ice rink.

Owners of their own vehicles will not leave indifferent a multi-storey parking for up to 240 cars. It is located in the immediate vicinity of the Dmitrov highway, so you do not have to go long to park your car.

Customer reviews about the complex

LCD "Nekrasovsky" (Dmitrovsky district) already has customer reviews in the global network. Even created a special site - a forum with the same name. There, people communicate, find neighbors, and discuss accumulated problems. In some porches there were problems with the elevator. Somewhere there was a flood due to a torn valve.

But for the most part, people are completely satisfied with the housing they have received and are doing the repair work with might and main. At the forum, people communicate, share tips. Of course, the object that has just been commissioned will always have certain flaws. But they are eliminated by the developer or the management company.

Ecological situation of the region

LCD "Nekrasovsky" (Dmitrovsky district), reviews about which are the most diverse, unanimously recognized as the best in terms of ecology. Dmitrovsky district is considered one of the most prestigious areas for housing construction precisely because of its healthy ecology.

Dmitrovskoe highway and the settlements adjacent to it are completely buried in the forest, the green landscape surrounds them from all sides. Not far from the complex is located Ikshinskoye reservoir and channel them. Moscow, where it is nice to swim on a hot summer day or relax with the whole family on the weekend on their shores.

Having settled in the Nekrasovsky residential complex, each buyer will not only acquire life in urban comfortable conditions, but at the same time the complex can be considered a suburban housing. Again and again it will drag you to come back here after a hard working day, for example, in the capital, in order to breathe in full-bodied fragrant fresh air. Moscow cannot boast of this.

Layout and cost of apartments

RC "Nekrasovsky" (Dmitrovsky district) offers apartments from the developer - Intersnab company - in various options and with a different number of rooms: from one to three. Layouts are mostly standard, but there are beveled corners in the rooms. Bathrooms are usually combined. All apartments have balconies.





One-room apartments with kitchens up to 11 square meters, living rooms of the correct proportions. In two-room apartments, the developer designed large corridors for the possible arrangement of wardrobes and redevelopment of bathrooms.

The cost of one square meter of living space starts from 52 thousand rubles. Moreover, one-bedroom apartments are the most expensive. In two-room apartments, the price is already reduced to 49 thousand per square meter. And in the three-room apartments it rises again to 51 thousand rubles per square meter of housing.

Pros and cons

The complex has many advantages, but also many disadvantages. Of the pluses, it is worth noting the inclusion in the project, in addition to residential buildings, of the necessary social infrastructure facilities. As well as their location near the complex, especially the school and kindergarten. An autonomous boiler room is a great indisputable advantage.





As a disadvantage, an insufficient number of parking spaces in the parking lot should be noted. Also, many buyers do not like the fact that the apartments are rented without finishing. Although currently apartments in a similar form are rented by almost every first developer. Because every buyer starts to make repairs anyway.
