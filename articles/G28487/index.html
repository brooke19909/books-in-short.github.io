Philips juicers - quality in your kitchen

Proper nutrition, as you know, always includes fresh juices. If you care about health, then a device for processing fruits and vegetables must be prescribed in your kitchen. Undoubtedly, more attention is drawn to popular brands that have long gained a good reputation in the home appliance market. Therefore , Philips juicers deserve a detailed look. After all, they will help not only those who adhere to a healthy lifestyle, but also amateur gardeners who think out ways to process the crop obtained at their summer cottage. Using juices made with your own hands, you can not without reason be proud of the result of your labors.





The Philips HR 1871 juicer receives a lot of positive feedback. The model is notable for its functions and characteristics. The device with a power of 800 W belongs to the centrifugal type. It has two working speeds and a two and a half liter juice tank. In addition, it has a special capacity. It can hold 1.6 l of pulp, which is ejected automatically. Of the functions that the apparatus possesses, special systems can be noted: direct juice supply, drop-stop. The case of the device is made of stainless steel.

Like all Philips juicers, the model is very convenient in operation. It has an additional function that prevents accidental switching on, which is useful if there are small children in the house. Thanks to the round neck with a width of 80 mm, it is convenient to put fruits into the apparatus for processing. After finishing work, it can be cleaned with a brush, which is included with the juicer. In order for the device to stand steady on the table, there are rubberized legs. A one meter long cord allows the juicer to be easily moved around the kitchen. A special compartment is provided in the apparatus for its storage.





As you can see from customer reviews, Philips juicers are a wonderful and useful purchase for anyone who prefers drinking natural juices of excellent quality. All models work silently, they are convenient and easy to wash. Moreover, devices can cope with products of different hardness: not only soft tomatoes, citrus fruits, but also carrots, beets. The cake comes out only slightly moist, which means that the machine works in good faith. The design in which the device is made, buyers call it very beautiful and concise.





Noteworthy juice squeezer "Philips 1858" - a universal device for making drinks from fruits and vegetables. Its body is made of plastic. The power of the device is 650 watts, it is equipped with a continuous spin system. The device has a two-liter pulp container and a steel microfilter. Thanks to them, the maximum juice volume is obtained, it enters the 1.25 liter tank. In order to add foam to the drink, in the device there is a separator. Buyers are attracted by the compact dimensions of the device, its ergonomics, convenient operation, the presence of a removable container.

Having studied such wonderful household appliances as Philips juicers, you can come to the conclusion about their high quality, functionality and indispensability in a modern kitchen. Any hostess will receive a lot of positive emotions by working with them.
