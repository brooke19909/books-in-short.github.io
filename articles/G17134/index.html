The most popular hotels of Sortavala

The city of Sortavala is one of the most beautiful and ancient cities of Karelia. The city has 3 birth dates, 3 names, at one time there were 3 triangular squares (at the moment there are 2 left). Sortavala differs from other cities by its unique cultural and historical appearance, which was formed by three states: Russia, Finland and Sweden. In this article we will consider the most popular hotels (Sortavala) - inexpensive and for people with good income.

Hotel "Cozy House"

It offers guests of this hotel in Sortavala, the prices for accommodation in which are very adequate - from 2900 rubles, several apartments with Wi-Fi. This apartment has a electric kettle, microwave, TV, dining area, and a bathroom.



Tukhalampi Lake and Wakkolahti Bay are a 15-minute walk away. Ayranne Lake is 2.5 km from here. The distance from the train station to the apartments is 1.5 km.

Cost: from 2900 rubles per day.

Hotel Piipun Piha

Continuing to look at hotels, you can highlight Piipun Piha with free Wi-Fi and a restaurant. The rooms are equipped with a TV, a terrace and a private bathroom with a hairdryer, bidet and shower, cable TV.



In addition, there is a 24-hour reception desk, a bar, a terrace, a tour desk and conference facilities, which not all local hotels can boast of. Sortavala is now attracting many tourists, so such services are very relevant. Ticket service is available.

Cost: from 3000 rubles / day.

Hotel Rantatalo

This Sortavala hotel has a very favorable location - in the very center. Wi-Fi is available. All rooms are equipped with a TV. The well-equipped kitchen includes a fridge and oven. It offers stunning garden views.



At Rantatalo there is a shared sauna, a barbecue area and a garden. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including fishing.

Cost: from 2800 rubles per day.

Hotel "Father Vasily"

But the location of this Sortavala hotel is completely different - it is located on a lake in a quiet location. With a private beach area and a sauna, the guest house is open all year. It offers free Wi-Fi and free parking. The house includes a well-equipped kitchen, 2 living rooms and 4 bedrooms. The bathroom comes with a bath or shower. A DVD player and satellite TV are included.



Products are delivered at an additional cost upon request. On site, guests can enjoy all kinds of outdoor activities - cycling, skiing, horse riding and water sports.

Cost: from 4500 rubles per day.

Hotel Kodikas

The hotel is located right on the shore of Lake Ladoga. It offers well-decorated rooms with shared bathroom facilities, heating, a fireplace and garden views.



This Sortavala hotel has several additional services for guests, thanks to which it has become very popular. The property has a 24-hour front desk, barbecue facilities and a garden. Other facilities include a children's playground and a shared lounge. The hotel territory and surroundings are suitable for various types of recreation, including fishing. Free private parking is available on site for your own car.

Cost: from 3500 rubles per day.

Hotel Lamberg

We continue to consider hotels in Sortavala. Located in the northern part of Lake Ladoga, Lamberg Hotel has a steam bath and a sauna . Guests can rent a bicycle. The famous Valaam Island is a 40-minute drive away.



The cottages and rooms are traditionally furnished and include a TV. Bathrooms are fitted with free toiletries and a shower. The restaurant serves Karelian, Finnish and Russian dishes.

Cost: from 4850 rubles per day.

Hotel "Seasons"

The Four Seasons Hotel is located in Rautalahti, on the shores of Lake Ladoga. The air-conditioned rooms are equipped with a TV, a balcony, a dining area with an electric kettle and a fridge, and a bathroom with a hairdryer and a shower.



The property has a terrace, barbecue facilities, meeting facilities, a shared lounge and a bar. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site, including hiking and fishing. You can leave your car in absolutely free parking.

Cost: from 4000 rubles per day.
