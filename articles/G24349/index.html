Magnificent rest in the rest house "Psou" (Abkhazia)

On the Black Sea coast, in the Republic of Abkhazia, just two kilometers from the Russian border, in the city of Gagra (Tsandrypsh village) is a rest house "Psou". It has its own well-groomed parkland with beautiful palm alleys and magnificent fountains. There are many types of green spaces, among which there are quite rare specimens. The main building of the Psou boarding house (Abkhazia) is an eight-story building located just 30 meters from the beach and surrounded by green cypresses, oleanders and magnolias. And the presence on the territory of their children's town attracts families with children.



Pension "Psou" (Abkhazia) offers the following accommodation for its guests: economy rooms in the summer building, standard, improved. The rooms located in the summer building have partial conveniences, since the shower and the bathroom are designed for all rooms on the floor. The main building has double rooms. It has a bathroom, parquet floors, modern furniture, a fridge and a TV. Each room has a balcony. In addition, the price includes the use of infrastructure and the beach. Families with children move in only if the child has already reached the age of 4. Up to 5 years, it can be settled without room in the room, but with the obligatory food.



Pension "Psou" (Abkhazia) provides 3 meals a day for its guests, organized according to a custom-made system. Guests can enjoy a varied menu including homemade cakes, fruits and vegetables. For eating, a special dining room is located on the seashore. The service is done by the waiters. The cost of food is included in the room rate.



The Black Sea coast is famous for its magnificent beaches. Abkhazia is not an exception (rest house "Psou"). The reviews left by those who have already rested here confirm that the beach here is sandy and pebble and well equipped. In addition to the necessary equipment for recreation, there is a shower, a cafe. For lovers of outdoor activities on the beach there are all kinds of water activities.

Rest house "Psou" (Abkhazia) has a vast territory, which has everything you need for an excellent holiday. It features a summer cinema. There is a private restaurant offering a varied menu of dishes of national and European cuisine. In addition, there is also a cafe. For those who seek to keep themselves in shape even while relaxing, a gym is open. For sports activities there are tennis courts, a playground, billiards. There is a conference room, a long-distance communication point. For those who have arrived in their own car, there is a parking lot. There is a tour desk for those wishing to explore the surroundings. In the evening there is a disco.



Rest in Abkhazia ("Psou") is ideal for families with children and for those who prefer silence and tranquility. It is necessary to pay attention to the fact that the transfer order is carried out in both directions at once and is paid at the cash desk of Arkaim-travel LLC. From midnight until 7.00 in the morning, transfers are not available.
