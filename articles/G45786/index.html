Hotel Hunguest Sun Resort 4 * (Herceg Novi, Montenegro): photos and reviews of tourists

In our article we want to talk about the Hunguest Sun Resort 4 * hotel. It is one of the largest four-star hotels of the famous Hungarian chain. The complex is located on two hectares in a park area on the very shore of the Hercegnovsky Bay.





Hotel Location

Getseg Novi is located in the west of Montenegro, ten kilometers from the Croatian border. This small town is filled with a Mediterranean atmosphere. Nature makes it a unique place in its own way. The northern part of the village is surrounded by peaks, the height of which reaches 1800 meters, and the southern is washed by the Bay of Kotor. The population of the town is only twenty eight thousand. In the very center of Herceg Novi you can see typical very narrow streets, cozy cafes, restaurants, Mediterranean squares.

From early spring to late autumn, a busy life boils on the promenade by the sea. Not far from the city there is a settlement called Igalo, in which the famous thermal-climatic sanatorium with an international reputation works. An hour away by car is Kotor - a city with a world heritage. Also popular among tourists are Budva, the island of Sveti Stefan, Dubrovnik.

Hunguest Sun Resort 4 * hotel, whose length is four hundred meters, is located on the boardwalk on the coast. The complex consists of twenty villas and five buildings, which are buried in dense vegetation. It is not difficult for guests of the country to get to the resort, since the hotel is only twenty-five kilometers from Chilipi Airport (the city of Dubrovnik) and as much from Tivat Airport.

How to get there

For those who plan to drive a car, they will have to get along the motorway leading from the Hungarian-Croatian border - this is approximately 750 kilometers from the border crossing Letenie. You can also use the railway, for example, buy a ticket for the Budapest-Bar train, and then take a bus (88 kilometers) or book a transfer at the hotel. By plane you can fly to two airports (Tivat and Chilipi). As already mentioned, they are equidistant from the hotel.





Hotel Rooms

Hunguest Sun Resort 4 * complex offers its guests the following room types:

  • Classic apartments.
  • Standard room (with extra bed).
  • De luxe apartment with massage shower and private jacuzzi.

Almost all rooms can accommodate another bed upon request. There are also self-catering apartments with a jacuzzi. They can be connected to the villa, representing a good option for families with children.

Complex buildings

Each building of Hunguest Sun Resort 4 * has its own name:

  1. Primorka is the largest separate complex. Surrounded by subtropical lush greenery. The five-story building in its arsenal has 48 "standards" and 4 rooms of the De Luxe class. Most of the apartments have balconies with beautiful sea views. At the same time, 32 rooms are interconnected, and therefore can be a wonderful option for families with children or for companies. The ground floor of the complex is occupied by a Welness center with an infrared sauna, a jacuzzi, a massage parlor, and a solarium. And on the sixth there is a large terrace, perfect for sunbathing.
  2. Jadranka is the most elegant and most equipped building. It has seven De Luxe class rooms. All apartments have a jacuzzi and one with a massage shower. Guestrooms have home cinema systems with state-of-the-art televisions, DVD players and cable TV. On the ground floor of the complex there is an a la carte restaurant with a beautiful spacious terrace and beautiful views of the coast of the Bay of Kotor.
  3. Novlyanka is a small building with sixteen rooms with French balconies (doubles) and wonderful sea views. The building is so conveniently located that it is quite easy to get to the lobby, beach, pool and Welness center from it.
  4. "Lovchen." It is in this building that the main restaurant of the hotel is located, a modern conference hall that seats 150 people. And on the second floor there are five of the best De Luxe rooms with terraces and wonderful views.

Hotel infrastructure

Hunguest Hotel Sun Resort 4 * includes a multifunctional sports ground for basketball, tennis, volleyball. Guests have the opportunity to have fun and play sports actively. In addition to the Welness Center, the hotel has its own beach, on which guests are offered umbrellas and sunbeds. And for supporters of outdoor activities on the coast there is plenty of water entertainment. Especially here, those who are fond of scuba diving will like to relax, since the purest water and rich underwater flora and fauna will not leave anyone indifferent and will give a lot of positive emotions. On the territory of Hunguest Hotel Sun Resort 4 * there are cafes and restaurants by the sea. This will appeal to lovers of vibrant nightlife.

Sports offers

Hunguest Sun Resort 4 * hotel is focused on active people involved in sports. For this reason, there are four tennis courts on the territory of the complex (one with a concrete coating, and three with a clay one) and playgrounds for playing handball, basketball, and volleyball. In addition, guests are offered the opportunity to use the services of the latest gym located in Igalo (this is five kilometers from the hotel).

Discount system

The administration of the Hunguest Sun Resort 4 * hotel provides its guests with a discount system regarding children's accommodation. Children under four years old are not paid. When placing one adult in a double room with a child from four to twelve years, a 30% discount is provided. When paying for all additional services at the hotel for children from four to twelve years old, a 50% discount is used.

Some more opinions
