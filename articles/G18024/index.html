If you want to break out of everyday reality and get into an unforgettable winter tale, then one of the best options to do this is Courchevel. The valley of the ski resort of Courchevel (1850 - 2374 m) is located in the Alps in France, the French referred to as the "Three Valleys" (Les Trois Valles). This fabulously beautiful place has long been considered the most prestigious tourist destination for winter holidays. The resort of Courchevel has more than 600 snow cannons that provide snow cover for the entire winter period (from December to April) and more than 600 km of ski slopes at four different heights (1300, 1550, 1650 and 1850 m above sea level).

An unforgettable vacation in Courchevel is also provided by numerous hotels in the resort, which provide a full range of services so that you feel comfortable and receive a lot of positive emotions. However, getting these very emotions will cost you a certain amount of money. It should be remembered that in Courchevel the higher the hotels are located above sea level, the higher the price policy. But, once at an altitude of 1850 m above sea level, you can take in your eyes the boundless valley of snowy alpine mountains!

Hotel Le Portetta 3 * (Courchevel 1650 hotels)
