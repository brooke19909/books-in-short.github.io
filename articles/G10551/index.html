Hotel Garcia Resort Spa 5 *. Turkey, Fethiye - hotels

Speaking of beach vacations in Turkey, most often mean the Mediterranean beaches of Antalya, which over the past 20 years has become the most popular and popular tourist destination for Russian tourists. However, Europeans, for example, prefer to relax in the resort of Fethiye, which today is perhaps one of the most prestigious and fashionable resorts on the Turkish coast. Beautiful landscapes, gorgeous beaches, developed infrastructure, excellent climate, lots of attractions. In a word, there is everything that tourist Turkey can be proud of. Fethiye, whose hotels are distinguished by their special chic and respectability, offer tourists an extra-class service.

Especially popular among vacationers are the magnificent beaches of the Oludeniz lagoon. Its incredibly turquoise color is striking at first sight. It is mainly visited by tourists who prefer a secluded and relaxing holiday. Many of them come here from year to year and stay either in the same hotels they are used to or are looking for something new. So, for example, in the summer of 2014, 800 meters from Oludeniz, the Garcia Resort Spa Hotel 5 * hotel complex opened its doors to guests in a friendly manner. Those who managed to visit it, in different ways praise the excellent service, exquisite cuisine and amenities of this hotel. In principle, in this area almost all hotels are characterized by a high level of comfort.

Features of rest in Fethiye

The resort got its name in honor of the famous test pilot in Turkey. However, in antiquity, when there was no country like Turkey in the world, the town belonged to Lycia and was called Telmess, which translates from Greek as "land of fires". This place especially attracts lovers of natural beauties, wonderful landscapes, as well as informative vacations. They are offered many interesting excursions, for example, to the remains of the fortress, which was built by the Rhodes knights. However, the most popular place to visit is the tombstones of the Lycian rulers, carved into a sheer cliff. They resemble a city lost in the rocks and amaze with the beauty of its architecture. Particularly noteworthy is the tomb "Garden of Almond Trees", built in the third century BC. e. For lovers of passive relaxation, 5-star Fethiye hotels will provide a huge range of spa services, as well as the possibility of complete relaxation.

Fethiye Hotels

As already noted, this resort has many deluxe hotels. They are perfect for families, they have all the amenities and a wide range of entertainment for all ages. The best hotels on the coast are Jiva Beach Resort 5 *, Club & Hotel Letoonia 5 *, Hillside Beach Club 5 *, and more recently, the 5- star Garcia Resort Spa has joined them. Unlike budget hotels, young people are not particularly keen on this, so a relaxing holiday for tourists is guaranteed. However, this does not mean at all that it is boring and uninteresting, just recreation and entertainment areas are skillfully separated from each other. For example, in the reviews of tourists about the hotel Garcia Resort Spa 5 * you can find both references to well-organized animation and thanks for a quiet and relaxing holiday. Everyone finds what he likes best. And all thanks to the professionally designed interior of the hotel. There are many magnificent hotels among four-star hotels, such as Noa Hotels Oludeniz Resort 4 *, and even among 3-star ones (Dorian 3 *, Flamingo 3 *, etc.).





Oludeniz Bay

This place is unique in its climate. In the bay, beach vacations continue even in the winter months. On the banks of Oludeniz there are more than 50 hotels of various categories. However, in recent years, five-star hotel complexes have been mainly built here. And this is dictated by the demand for them. For example, in 2014, the magnificent Garcia Resort Spa 5 (Fethiye) was commissioned. All beaches in the area are municipal, but for hotel guests who use the services of a beach, sunbeds and umbrellas are provided free of charge. Especially popular are the Cleopatra Coast and Turtle Islands, where you can often find large turtles.

How to get to Fethiye?

Russian tourists who are going on vacation to one of the hotels of this resort need to buy air tickets to Antalya, and from there already get to the right place by car. The distance between the two cities is about 300 km, the cost of a bus ticket is about 10 euros. Buses run every hour. Nevertheless, tourists who bought a tour to the Garcia Resort Spa 5 * hotel may not worry about this, because the price of the ticket most often includes the transfer to the hotel from the airport. On the way you can admire the magnificent natural landscapes, the beauty of the Lycian mountains, covered with dense coniferous forests, etc.



Garcia Resort Spa 5 * (Turkey / Fethiye): general description

This chic five-star hotel was founded in early 2014. It is a hotel complex consisting of a central five-story building and eight 2,3,5-story buildings. On its territory there are also two outdoor pools for adults and one for children, as well as restaurants and cafes, many corners for entertainment.



Location

Garcia Resort Spa 5 * is located on a hillside. From it to the beach is quite far, about 4 kilometers. However, tourists get a free bus there. The hotel is a little less than 1 km from the center of Oludeniz. The nearest airport is the airport terminal of the city of Dalaman, however, as already mentioned, Russian tourists often prefer to fly to Antalya.

Number of rooms

Garcia Resort has 151 rooms in the following categories:

  • standard;
  • improved standard;
  • standard with access to the pool;
  • suites with Jacuzzi;
  • suites;
  • suites with access to the pool.

There are 47 first category rooms in the hotel. Their area is from 23 to 26 square meters. meters. Superior standard rooms are practically no different from just standard ones, but their area is slightly larger - 29-32 square meters. m. They make up the bulk of the hotel rooms. The rooms of the third category are similar to the improved ones, however they have a terrace with direct access to the pool. In suites, instead of showers, there is a jacuzzi, and this is their main advantage. But the suites, which have access to the pool, are smaller in size than usual, which have a living room and bedroom.

Room Description and Service

All rooms, regardless of category, are beautifully furnished and decorated. Some of them have access to the terrace, which opens to the pool, while others have balconies. All rooms are equipped with the latest technology (TVs, refrigerators, air conditioners, telephones), Internet connection. In some of them, as already noted, instead of a bath there is a jacuzzi. Rooms are usually cleaned daily, but linen is changed every other day.



Nutrition

In the Garcia Resort Spa 5 * tourists are offered the service according to the "ultra ol inclusive" system. Guests can use the services of two restaurants and bars. Chefs work hard. This is evidenced by the positive reviews of tourists. Here you can find dishes of international as well as national Turkish cuisine. The main restaurant seats 300 people. The second restaurant is a la carte. At different times of the day, dishes are served here according to a certain menu: from 19.00 to 20.00 - Turkish cuisine, from 20.00 to 22.00 - seafood and fish. From noon to 10 pm, the hotel has a bistro pub. There is also a pool bar, a disco bar and a snack bar in the lobby. Drinks for tourists on this system are served only locally. Expensive drinks and hookah can be ordered at the bar for a fee.

Hotel service

Guests of the Garcia Resort Spa 5 * hotel can use the services of the SPA and wellness center, which includes a gym, hammam, sauna, indoor pools, massage rooms, etc. They include parking, laundry, dry cleaning, medical office, shops, a hairdresser etc.

Entertainment

For vacationers, themed nights are organized in the hotel, animation happens throughout the day, starting with morning exercises. For sports enthusiasts, there are table tennis, darts, water aerobics, billiards, etc.

Services for children

Despite the fact that this hotel has almost everything for an interesting holiday for children, it can not be called children's. Here there is for them a swimming pool, slides, a mini club, a playground with swings, a children's menu, etc. For the smallest, for a fee, you can call a nanny. In the restaurant for them you can find dishes from a special menu, as well as comfortable high chairs for feeding babies.
