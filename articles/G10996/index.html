Hotel "Dynamo", Krasnodar: description, reviews

If you come to the capital of the Krasnodar Territory and you need to stay inexpensively and closer to the city center, the Dynamo Hotel in Krasnodar is at your service. Here you will be offered not only comfortable accommodation, but also a wide range of sports activities.

Description

The hotel is part of the Dynamo sports and fitness complex, which consists of a stadium, a gym, a martial arts hall, a swimming pool and much more. It is located in the central part of the city, within walking distance from the bus station Krasnodar-2. The airport can be reached by public transport in about half an hour.

The hotel has an ATM, a hairdresser and a gift shop. An elevator is available to access the upper floors. The Dynamo Hotel (Krasnodar) has its own cafe, a paid parking lot is equipped for car owners, there is access to wireless Internet. Here people with disabilities will find amenities, you can always turn to qualified medical personnel for help. The business center has everything for conferences. A washing machine and ironing facilities are provided if necessary.

Guests are offered rooms of various levels of comfort. Athletes who come to participate in competitions often live here. The Dynamo Hotel is located in Krasnodar at the address: 190 Krasnaya Street. The reception is open around the clock.

Accommodation

The hotel has 110 rooms; accommodation is possible in a five-bed room or in a single suite. There are several types of rooms in the Dynamo Hotel in Krasnodar:

  • quintuple and triple first category - 800 rubles per day per person, for an additional placement of 480 rubles per person;
  • double of the first category - from 800 to 1350 rubles per day per person, an extra bed will cost from 480 to 810 rubles per day;
  • single of the first category - from 950 to 1750 rubles per day, the cost of an extra bed will be from 570 to 1050 rubles;
  • single of the first category with a TV - 1950 rubles per day, for an extra bed you need to pay 1170 rubles;
  • double suite - from 1650 to 1750 rubles per day per person, the cost of an extra bed is from 990 to 1050 rubles per day.
  • single suite - from 3650 to 4650 rubles per day for one resident, an extra bed will cost from 2190 to 2790 rubles per day.

At the Dynamo Hotel (Krasnodar) smoking is prohibited. Pets are allowed.
