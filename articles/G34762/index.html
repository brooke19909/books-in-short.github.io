Lake Talkas - a paradise in the Urals

Lake Talkas is one of the most beautiful places in the Urals, which attracts a huge number of tourists and just vacationers. It is located on the territory of Bashkortostan on the eastern slopes of the Ural Range. Due to the unusual nature and vibrant landscapes, this place was called the pearl of the Urals, and it truly is!





Specifications

The freshwater lake Talkas is very ancient and was formed as a result of the activity of the volcano; it is located in the Baymak district, only four kilometers from the small village of Tubinsky. On the east side, the lake is framed by the slopes of the Arendyk ridge, whose length is 135 meters and the maximum height is slightly less than a kilometer (987 meters). There is a hill and a small crater on this ridge. In addition, the volcanic origin of the lake is also confirmed by porphyrites, which protrude along the lake to the surface.

The western side of the lake is bounded by an anonymous mountain on which small trees are located. The other two edges of Talkas are gentle and swampy. But the water in it is very clean and clear. While swimming, you can view under water a distance of up to 20 m.
