Guest reviews: Goa is a piece of paradise for everyone

Many have heard about this wonderful place - distant India. From the people who visited there, one can hear regrets about the inability to stay in this paradise for life. Only relatives and friends remaining in their homeland were forced to stand up to their own song in the throat. Let's try to find out what is the matter. So, India, Goa, reviews from tourists.



Everything is perfect here!

These words are often described by vacationers in Goa. Many kilometers of beaches that go to infinity, sunsets, from which the heart contracts, the bright colors of the day, the unique ethnic flavor ... And in addition to the pleasures that please the body and soul, there are also very low prices. And what a kitchen here! Intricate and exotic Indian dishes, shrimps that are larger than a human palm, freshly squeezed juices and even local alcohol - all this causes rave reviews from tourists. Goa makes you forget not only about work and problems, but even about the past and the future. Only the present, only rest - this is true relaxation.

About doubts



Some tourists who have never been to exotic southern countries before, accustomed to the Middle East and Africa, at first doubt. Is it worth it to go, is it not dangerous ... Despite the fact that there is no Turkish "all inclusive" and Egyptian luxury, the rest of Goa is considered by tourists to be unsurpassed. Even simple two-star hotels, without pretense of special comfort, are still clean and pleasant, the staff is responsive, the coast is amazing, and the excursions are more than exciting.

Tourists reviews: Goa - where and how to eat

The food is just great. Almost all vacationers agree that food is tasty and inexpensive. And do not give preference to exclusive restaurants. Cafes on the coast, where locals drop by, are the best choice. Here you will be offered fresh, literally just swam seafood in garlic sauce (it is very famous, by the way, for such a local delicacy as rocky crab, and fresh mango and papaya fresh). In addition, the resort offers not only spicy Indian cuisine, but also dishes of different nations, and vacationers have practically no stomach problems.

Tourists reviews: Goa - sea and coast



Vacationers in winter celebrate a special thrill after a January or February cold to swim in the picturesque clear lagoon, as if in a bathroom (the water temperature sometimes reaches 30 degrees!). Among the good beaches, Arambol is often mentioned. In addition, Goa hosted night bathing, parties in the sand and songs until the morning. Therefore, young people and those who love outdoor activities like it here.

Guest reviews: Goa is a state of mind

Although the road to this beautiful place often goes expensive, many decide to leave here to forget about everything bad. Amid the noise of waves, jungle and waterfalls, the soul is as if reborn. Many are advised to visit the local antiquities, for example, Redi Fort, which leaves the impression of something fabulous and unreal. And the most unforgettable experience is received by those who know how to ride a scooter, fortunately, renting it here costs a penny. They say that you can sell your soul for night skating in Goa. This may be an exaggeration, but tourists admit that their heart remains here forever.
