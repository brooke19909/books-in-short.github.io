Hotel Nilbahir Resort Spa 5 * (Turkey / Side): photos, prices and reviews of tourists

For more than 20 years, Antalya has been one of the most popular tourist destinations for Russian citizens. The Turkish coast of the Mediterranean Sea attracts tourists not only for its fashionable resorts, equipped beaches and excellent climate, but also for a large selection of excursion programs. For several thousand years, representatives of different peoples with a highly developed culture (Byzantines, Hittites, Phrygians, Greeks, Armenians, Assyrians, etc.) lived on this territory, and each of them contributed to the history of the architecture of these places.

Therefore, the main feature of holidays on the Turkish coast is the possibility of combined - beach and sightseeing - tours. From this point of view, the Side resort is especially popular. It is rightly called the architectural and archaeological museum under the starry sky of Turkey. Staying at one of the chic hotels in the city or its environs, for example, in the brand new Nilbahir Resort Hotel SPA 5 *, tourists can combine beach activities with numerous excursions to the unique architectural monuments that these places are full of.

How to get

There is no airport in Side. The nearby one is Antalya Airport Terminal. It takes flights from all over the world. Airplanes from Moscow and St. Petersburg fly here daily, and in the high season from other major cities of the Russian Federation. The flight time from the capital is about three and a half hours. If you have chosen the Nilbahir Resort SPA (Side) hotel for your vacation, then upon arrival in Antalya you will have to make another 65 kilometers by road to Side resort, and from there another 20 kilometers to the hotel complex. However, do not worry, the roads in Turkey are in excellent condition, and these 40-50 minutes will fly by for you unnoticed. A taxi from Antalya to Side will cost 110-115 dollars, and a bus - 10 Turkish liras (4.5 dollars).



Side or Manavgat

The closest large settlement near the hotel is the city of Manavgat, nevertheless, in the Turgids this hotel is marked as Nilbahir Resort Hotel SPA (Side). This is because Manavgat is not a coastal city, while Side is a beach resort with well-developed infrastructure. On this coast there are wonderful sandy and pebble beaches, always clean and well-groomed, and along the coastline there are fashionable hotels. Nilbahir Resort Hotel SPA 5 is located between Side and Alanya. There are not so many hotels in this part of the coast, although on the beaches you can see towers of cranes building new hotel complexes. The beach is separated from the hotel line by a six-lane expressway. Between the hotels there are private cottages.

Leisure activities

Since this hotel is located in an area with underdeveloped infrastructure, guests either have to be content with the entertainment provided by the hotel or go to the nearby cities - Side and Manavgat, where you can have a wonderful time in numerous night clubs, restaurants, discos, etc. But on the territory of the hotel complex you can play billiards (for a fee), table tennis and tennis (for a fee), darts, basketball, boccia, water polo, as well as do water aerobics or work out on fitness machines (for a fee). Children's entertainment

In the lobby of the hotel you can immediately notice a sign on which "MINI-CLUB" is written in large bright letters. This area is intended for children's activities. Here, children will find several slot machines and board games, they also have a children's playground with swings, as well as a paddling pool with slides next to the pool. For a fee, the smallest babysitter can look after. In the daytime, entertainers organize a fun show program for children, as well as contests and quizzes.

Sea and beach

The Mediterranean coast is what Turkey is always proud of. Nilbahir Resort SPA 5 (hotel) has its own beach, which is located only 50 meters from it. However, in order to reach the beach line, tourists need to walk 3-4 minutes through the private sector, then dive into the underground passage, which runs under a six-lane highway, then continue the path between private cottages. In addition, the beach can be called a stretch. Absolutely bare beach with dozens of sun loungers and umbrellas (free). The entrance to the sea is not very convenient, so for the safety of tourists on the water, a plastic pontoon is arranged. For this, tourists express gratitude to the hotel administration. On the beach you can find several water attractions, such as a banana, water skiing, parachute (parasailing). You can also go diving under the supervision of an experienced instructor.



Excursions

If you have chosen to stay at a hotel in Nilbahir Resort Spa (Turkey), which is located 10 km from the city of Manavgat, then you will definitely need to visit the waterfall of the same name with the city. This is truly a bewitching sight. A giant 20-meter (wide) stream falls from a 2-3-meter height directly into the sea. Next to it there are many fish restaurants where you can sit, taste local fish and relax to the sound of a waterfall. By the way, psychologists believe that the noise of falling water is a wonderful antidepressant, so a visit to Manavgat can be regarded as stress therapy. As for excursions to historical sights, the hotel will definitely offer you to visit the city of Side, which is known throughout Turkey as an open-air museum. Here you can see the well-preserved ruins of the ancient Greek city with a real amphitheater, agora, baths, etc. Thousands of vivid impressions will be guaranteed.

Nilbahir Resort SPA 5 *: tourist reviews

The reviews of vacationers in this hotel, left by former guests, indicate that this hotel does not yet correspond to its 5 stars, and that there is much to be improved. Tourists are especially unhappy with the service staff, in particular in connection with housekeeping, as well as the neglect of the beach. Many tourists believe that the price they paid for the tour does not match the quality of service. Moreover, many services are provided to tourists for a fee. The minimum cost of a weekly tour to this hotel (for two people), as already noted, is 38,000 rubles.

Conclusion

Hotel Nilbahir Resort SPA 5 (Turkey / Side), of course, has a wonderful modern dizian and well-developed infrastructure. However, in order for it to correspond to its five-star status, the hotel administration needs to change a lot in the structure of the hotel and the organization of service and leisure, as well as to assemble a conscientious and highly qualified team of employees, which has not yet been done. We hope that by the next beach season all the shortcomings will be eliminated, and in this they will certainly be helped by the reviews of tourists left on various tourist resources. After all, they can point out to the management all the mistakes.
