The best beach resorts in Turkey

To describe all the beach resorts in Turkey, not a whole book will be enough, so we decided to dwell on some of them, the most popular, which means the best. In general, Turkey itself is considered one of the most amazing and beautiful countries, so the rest in this country is unforgettable.



Oludeniz

The best beach resorts in Turkey opens Oludeniz. This is a small village surrounded on all sides by mountains. Its magnificent picturesque landscapes attract tourists from all over the world. The quiet coast of the Mediterranean Sea, a sandy beach, and around the mountains ... You can only imagine this beauty. Calm and solitude reigns everywhere. This place is suitable for couples or for families.

Fethiye

This port town is located near Oludeniz. In its place in antiquity was the city of Telmesos, and its remains, preserved from the fourth century, are the main attractions of this resort area. And, of course, tourists are attracted by the famous bazaar of Fethiye. Here you can not only relax well, but also enjoy eating. This is a favorite place for newlyweds, birthday people or just lovers of idle life.

Kusadasi

Turkey's beach resorts are also located on the shores of the Aegean Sea, among them one of the best is Kusadasi with its famous Ladies Beach. There is incredibly fine sand, its softness and beckons to walk barefoot, which is what thousands of tourists do annually.

Marmaris

This resort attracts guests with the most clear water and the cleanest beaches. For those who are romantic at heart, Cleopatra's beach is open , where newlyweds like to retire. A wonderful oasis, alluring with the beauty of nature. What else do lovers need?

Belek

This resort area is designed for those who value and love luxury and comfort. Chic and shine - this is about Belek. Hotels with five stars, a pine forest with clean air and, of course, the sea. Who can resist such beauty?



Antalya

If you ask citizens of the CIS what beach resorts in Turkey they know, then 99% of respondents will answer: "Antalya!" This resort has received such recognition and popularity because of the large number of luxurious beaches for every taste and budget, and the city itself stretches far across the Mediterranean. Here you can sunbathe on the sand, swim in the sea, plunge into the vibrant nightlife and go shopping, leaving a considerable amount of money in them.

Bodrum

This city is located between the shores of the Mediterranean and Aegean Seas. Pebble, sand, rocky or mixed beaches beckon with their purity, even at night life does not stop. Entertainment in clubs and discos will give an excellent mood. This resort is for people who prefer to relax actively.
