Turkey, Porto Azuro Delta. Residence

The hotel consists of a single five-story building with 213 rooms.

  • 44 Family rooms (maximum 4 people, 40 sq.m.). Family rooms are formed from two separate rooms with an interior door. One of the bedrooms has a bunk bed, the other has a double bed.
