How to make sundress patterns for girls?

Sewing for your beloved daughter is a pleasure. Many may wonder why to do this if clothes can be bought at the store. But the fascinating process where mom and daughter create stunning outfits is difficult to replace with a trip to the store. In this article we will give patterns of sundresses for a girl who can form the basis of parental creativity.





Sundress in the old Russian style

Quite often morning performances are held in kindergartens, in connection with which children need to be dressed in the clothes of our ancestors. Pattern sundress for girls will help mom to make such an outfit. The main thing is to choose the right material. To get a festive look, you should choose a light fabric in a flower. And in order to achieve greater reliability, you should take a dense material that visually looks like a canvas.

To make a pattern, you will need to measure the girl‚Äôs height and chest circumference. Based on these parameters, you need to adjust the figure depicted above to the proportions.

Sewing a sundress will not be a problem. First you need to tailor the front and rear shelves, and then connect the two parts of the product with side seams. The final stage will be the turning of the neck and hem. It is most convenient to make it a purchased oblique trim, but if it is not at hand, you can use a piece of fabric and process the edges with it.





Simple sundress

One of the most elementary ways to create a new thing for your daughter is to sew it in two parts. A sundress pattern for a girl of such a plan is depicted above. You need to choose a fabric that will fit the daughter's color type, and most importantly, will not be a brand. The above image can become a pattern of a warm sundress. In this case, you need to choose the fabric, which includes wool.

You can print the given pattern, and on its basis make your own clothing design. For example, change the round collar to a V-neck or add a collar. You need to transfer the pattern to the material using chalk or soap. You can also use special markers for the fabric. Sewing a sundress is very simple. It is enough to make two side seams and process the neck and hem.





Summer sundress

Such clothes are simply indispensable for hot weather. The pattern of a sundress for the girl is multi-part. To make the product look presentable, for its manufacture you need to take a plain fabric, which will emphasize all the relief seams. You can print the pattern above, or you can draw it by hand. Here you should consider only the growth of the girl. The chest circumference can be any, it will be easy to model directly on the baby. When the pattern is transferred to the fabric, it needs to be cut and stitched in stages. The first step is to make the front shelf. We assemble the part on the drawstring and sew two side planks to it. We sew together all the details of the back. Now you need to process the upper edge of the product and embed elastic in it. After this, you should sew a small bar on the back and shoulder straps. The final action is the processing of the hem. It can be bent or stitched with a zigzag.





Wide sundress

Such clothes will perfectly fit both a thin girl and a full child. The sundress model hides the figure and makes it more fragile. The pattern of children's sundress is given above. It needs to be scaled and adjusted to the growth of the child. Keep in mind that the neck is wide, which means it either needs to be reduced or an elastic band inserted into it. Do not be afraid to make a mistake, because you can always fix the product by slightly adjusting its style. Having cut out the pattern, it needs to be transferred to the fabric. It is advisable to take lightweight material that will be draped in folds. And to have more of them, you need to cut the product along an oblique line. When the parts are ready, you should sew the front and rear shelves on the sides and process the neck and hem. If desired, pockets can be sewn into the side seams.
