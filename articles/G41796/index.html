Hilton Pattaya Hotel: address, room reservation, photos and reviews of vacationers

If you dream of a paradise vacation, pay attention to the Hilton Pattaya Hotel. Here, the charm of Asian exoticism is closely intertwined with a strict and modern European style. Of course, one cannot but note comfortable living conditions and high standards of service.

Location

Hilton Pattaya Hotel is located at 333/101 Beach Road, Moo 9 Nong Prue Banglamung. This part of the city is a favorite among travelers. The 34-story hotel is located above the Central Festival Shopping Center. It is approximately 35 km from Pattaya Airport. Resting here, you can visit the following tourist sites:

  • Memorial Hospital - 0.5 km;
  • diving center - 0.5 km;
  • Museum "Do you want to believe, you want not" - 0.7 km;
  • Walking Street - 1 km.

Number of rooms

To accommodate guests at the Hilton Pattaya Hotel provides 302 comfortable rooms. Comfort categories are as follows:

  1. Deluxe - a spacious room equipped with large or twin beds. There is a desk and a cozy sitting area with upholstered furniture. There is a private balcony.
  2. Executive room - a luxurious two-room suite consisting of a cozy bedroom and a spacious panoramic living room. Due to the fact that the room is located on the 34th floor, one can enjoy beautiful sea views from the windows. This room also has access to the executive lounge.
  3. Suite - a spacious two-room with panoramic windows. The bedroom is equipped with a king-size bed, and the living room has upholstered furniture and a modern multimedia system.
  4. Deluxe Suite - large panoramic room with sea view. There is a bedroom and a living room. The highlight of the room is the balcony with a jacuzzi.
  5. Grand Suite - a room consisting of a bedroom and a living room. There is a dining area. Both rooms have panoramic windows and a balcony with sea views. This room also has access to the executive lounge.
  6. Executive Plus Room - a luxurious room on the 34th floor, consisting of a bright bedroom and a spacious living room. Each room has panoramic windows (including a bathroom). There is a balcony with a jacuzzi.
