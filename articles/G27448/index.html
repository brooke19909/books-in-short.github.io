Chkalovsky beach in Sochi

To get to the Chkalovsky beach (Sochi), you need to go by any means of transport in the direction of Adler to Enlightenment Street. It occupies a stretch of coast from Alleynaya Street to Lazurnaya. Standing in the surf, you can enjoy the view of the mountains, consider dolphins jumping out of the horizon from the water, wave your hand at sailing yachts and catamarans.

You can get to the main entrance through the railway if you get off Enlightenment Street and go to the sea along Pribrezhnoy Lane. If you go to the sanatorium, then the walk will take 10 minutes.

Useful information for vacationers

The visit is free, since Chkalovsky beach is municipal. However, you should not go on vacation without money. Be sure to need a sunbed and parasol, which can be rented for half a day. Price from 500 rub. The shore is pebble, so you can not do without safety shoes and a sunbed. There are practically no natural shelters, so you can protect yourself from the heat only under an umbrella.

All retail outlets are located along the cobblestone promenade, stretching from one to the other edge of Chkalovsky beach. There are also free toilets and showers. There are many attendants: a paramedic, rescuers, cleaners, security guards. Like everywhere in Adler, it offers the services of massage therapists, hairdressers, and tattoo artists.

The depth of the sea near the coast is small. However, you should not lose vigilance. Wide waves, even in calm weather, are easily knocked down. Children and novice swimmers are advised not to neglect lifejackets, which you can buy here in any stall. Breakwaters do not allow the elements to clear up even with a strong wind. And since pebbles are at the bottom of Chkalovsky beach, even in a storm the water remains clear.

What do you need to bring with you?

On-site food products are sold for every taste: fruits, ice cream, drinks, barbecue. Vacationers like to have a bite to eat at the outdoor cafe. Therefore, you can not take food with you. Basic necessities and bathing accessories are presented in a wide range at local shops, including sunscreens. If the filming equipment remained at home - it does not matter. Local photographers make excellent portraits and immediately hand them over to customers.
