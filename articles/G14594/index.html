Hotel Tsagarakis Beach 3 * (Greece, Crete): overview, description and reviews of tourists

Crete is the most popular island tourist resort in Greece. Its shores are washed by the Aegean, Libyan and Ionian seas. The length of the island from east to west is 260 km, and from north to south - about 50 km. There are many beautiful resort towns in Crete. And one of the most visited is the village of Ammoudara. This small village has everything for a comfortable stay for guests of Crete. For example, inexpensive rooms can be rented in Ammudar at Tsagarakis Beach Hotel.

Location Features

This hotel is located in Ammoudar on the seashore. The nearest airport is just 9 km away. You can get from here to Tsagarakis Beach in literally 20-30 minutes. The capital of Crete, Heraklion, is a 10-minute drive away.





Actually, the resort of Ammudar itself is a modern tourist village with a very well-developed infrastructure. There are all kinds of shops, taverns, pharmacies and rental offices work. Relax in this resort, according to many tourists, you can very well. The only drawback of Ammudara, many vacationers consider its proximity to the airport. The noise of approaching and taking off planes can, of course, greatly interfere with rest.

General description of the hotel

Hotel Tsagarakis Beach was built in 1983. It is a three-story building of original architecture. The territory of this hotel has its own. The hotel courtyard is well landscaped and landscaped. Lawns and flowerbeds with exotic plants are all over the territory. The platform in front of the entrance and the paths are faced with paving tiles.

Reviews of tourists this hotel deserved just wonderful. Many guests praise its exterior and interiors. The rooms and corridors of the hotel are decorated using antique household utensils and furniture. Most guests note the special family friendly atmosphere prevailing in this hotel.





Number of rooms

In total, this three-star hotel offers its guests 44 standard rooms. Each room in the hotel is designed for two people. If desired, guests Tsagarakis Beach 3 * (Greece) can book an extra place here. Some rooms have twin beds, some have one double bed. The rooms of this hotel are relatively well equipped. The rooms have:

  • air conditioning;

  • restroom;

  • safe;

  • balcony.

Unfortunately, there are no TVs and refrigerators in the rooms rented out at Tsagarakis Beach . Guests can use the safe only for a fee. Showers in this hotel in each room are individual. If desired, tourists can enjoy free Wi-Fi directly in the apartment.

Most of the guests rented at the Tsagarakis Beach 3 * hotel (Greece, Crete, Heraklion, Ammoudara) praise the rooms. The disadvantages of the hotel tourists include, of course, primarily the lack of a refrigerator and a TV in the room. Available Attractions

Of course, guests of the Tsagarakis Beach 3 * hotel (Crete) have the opportunity to visit the island and many different kinds of interesting places. Most tourists are advised to rent a car for this purpose. This will allow you to explore the maximum number of attractions with great comfort. In addition, car rental in Ammudar is not too expensive.





In the village itself, guests of the hotel can visit, for example, a Cretan knife store or order Jeep Safari. Actually, on the island of Crete, the most popular among tourists are such attractions as:

  • Arkadi Monastery;

  • Knossos palace;

  • cave of Zeus.

The monastery of Arkadi is the national symbol of not only the island of Crete, but also of all Greece. It is located 23 km from the city of Rethymnon, on a mountain whose height is 500 meters above sea level. The most important element of the monastery complex is the Church of the Transfiguration. Also, on the territory of the monastery, tourists have the opportunity to explore the old guest house, refectory, pantry, etc.

Zeus Cave is located on the Lassithi Plateau in the Dikti mountain range. According to legend, it was here that the wife of Kronos, the goddess Rhea, once hid. In this cave, she gave birth to the god Zeus, thereby preventing his father from killing him. For a long time this cave was a place of worship for the god of heaven.
