Nefteyugansk district. Holiday Village "Sail" (Surgut)

Nefteyugansk region has always been famous for its beauty, many untouched forests and crystal clear lakes. What is unique about this region? About it further.

Holiday Village "Sail" (Surgut)

Among the natural beauty of Nefteyugansk is a complex that captivates the hearts of visitors from the first seconds of their stay. Finding a way to it is quite simple. You need to go along the highway that connects Nefteyugansk and Surgut. About halfway there will be a direction indicator, turning along which you can easily get to the complex.





Services

Base "Sail" (Surgut) is open daily. Here you can stay for a long period in cottages and holiday homes. They are equipped so that everyone feels cozy and comfortable. This complex is one of the best of its kind. Developed infrastructure and a variety of services can satisfy the desires of all visitors, regardless of age and interests. The first thing to note is that the zoo is located on the premises of the institution. There are bears here who sweetly ask for passers-by, and many other inhabitants of both our Russian forests and those brought from other countries. You will see here pumas, leopards, wolves, foxes and even a large camel named Mahmud. Employees take great care of their wards, so they are always cheerful and friendly. It should be noted that animals are allowed to feed from their hands - this will especially please the young visitors of the magnificent park. This is not all the services that the Parus recreation center (Surgut) can provide.

There is a beautiful hippodrome, where experienced instructors will teach you and your kids the tricks of horse riding. Ponies are provided for children, and for those who want a more unusual experience, they can provide a camel for riding. For thrill-seekers, the Parus recreation center (Surgut) offers a special program that teaches you how to handle many types of firearms. Here you can visit specially equipped shooting galleries, in which it is possible to shoot both ordinary targets and flying saucers.





Nutrition

I would like to pay special attention to the kitchen, which pleases with its diversity. There are several indoor restaurants and summer areas at the base. Particularly attractive are the bright tents on the lake that invite you to relax in your shadow. The chefs at Sail cook excellently any meat dishes. The ordering waiters will deliver breakfast, dinner or lunch to the summer terrace or room in the house.

Seasonal Fun

Holiday Village "Sail" (Surgut) It offers various recreational activities both in summer and in winter. So, in winter you can ride on snowmobiles, skiing and even a snowy "banana". An ice rink is always open for skate lovers. After staying in the cold, it is best to visit a bathhouse, whose healing pairs drive away the remnants of frost.

As for the summer period, the variety of leisure activities cannot but rejoice. The entertainment programs include traveling with an overnight stay in tents in the open air, boating or a catamaran, riding quad bikes on special tracks.





Well-equipped sports grounds are ideal for all types of outdoor games, from football to tennis, and the crystal clear water in the lakes will cool even on the hottest days of the season.

And this is not a complete description of what the recreation center "Sail" (Surgut) can offer. The site of the complex contains all the necessary information and contact information. Contacting managers, you can find out about all the services, accommodation conditions and cost of stay. Online consultants will help you choose the best vacation program.
