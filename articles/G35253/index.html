Crimea: hotels by the sea. The most popular family vacation spots

In the process of planning your vacation, it is very important to choose a house that will allow you to relax and not cause any inconvenience. This year, a particularly large flow of tourists goes to the Crimea. Hotels by the sea, picturesque sunsets and beautiful nature - all this attracts vacationers. In this article you will learn about the most popular places to stay on the sunny peninsula.

We are going to rest in the Crimea!

Before you book a room in a hotel or boarding house, you need to carefully plan your vacation scenario. After all, this will avoid unforeseen expenses and other surprises. Define a limit on the amount you are willing to spend on your vacation. After all, it will depend on what level of housing you can afford.

But why more and more vacationers prefer Crimea? Hotels on the beach, which are located around the entire peninsula, offer rooms at very affordable prices. In this case, tourists do not need to apply for a visa and change the currency to pay for services.





A mini-hotel in Crimea is an excellent option for a budget tourist

If you are limited in finances, but want to relax on the Black Sea, then this option is right for you. Mini-hotels, as a rule, have no more than 10 rooms of a standard class. All vacationers are offered accommodation at home with a meeting at the airport and escort to the sights of Crimea.

The most popular mini-hotels on the peninsula are the Golden Beach in Feodosia and the Sea Corner in Alushta. Room rate is from 2 to 3 thousand rubles per day. At the same time, all guests receive good discounts when booking for several months.





And if you like a vacation in the wild, then you can take advantage of accommodation in campsites on the beach. In this case, the cost for a place with a tent will be from 150 to 300 rubles per day. However, only trained tourists can afford such an extreme vacation. If you prefer comfort, then we offer you the following options.

Hotel "Legend" (Crimea, Alushta)

Cozy apartment just 6 meters from the beach ... What else can a tourist dream of? The Legenda Hotel, located near Alushta, is one of the most popular holiday destinations for the whole family. After all, here you can enjoy the aroma of the sea breeze around the clock.

For only 3,500 rubles a day, you will get a spacious comfortable room with air conditioning, Wi-Fi, a TV, a bathroom and a wide balcony with amazing views. In addition, getting to the beach is not difficult: just go down to the first floor of the hotel and you have access to the open sea.

The hotel complex has ample parking, so this place will appeal to even tourists. A cafe with European cuisine and sea views will brighten sultry evenings and enjoy the sunset.





"Marine" hotel (Crimea, Yevpatoriya)

The western part of the peninsula is famous for the fact that there are not as many vacationers as on the south coast. However, the majestic cliffs and crystal clear sea will not leave indifferent even the most fastidious tourist.

You can relax in the city of Evpatoria in the hotel with the symbolic name "Marine". The cost of rooms varies from 1700 to 2500 rubles. This amount is very democratic for the Crimean peninsula.

Private hotel is located just 200 meters from the sea. This is due to the fact that the western coast of the peninsula is a high cliff, through which artificial slopes to the sea are built. Only Crimea can boast of such a unique nature.

Hotels by the sea on the west coast is a unique opportunity to combine business with pleasure. After all, there are healing Saki muds that rejuvenate the body and relieve skin diseases. And you can use this natural treatment absolutely free. Simply go to the beach in a city called Saki, which is located just 15 kilometers from Yevpatoriya.





Hotel "Bay of Dreams" (Sevastopol)

The picturesque bay of Laspi, located at the foot of Cape Ayia, is the hallmark of the Crimean peninsula. Hotels by the sea in this unique place, as a rule, are much more expensive than others. If you expect to spend a vacation that will be remembered for a lifetime, then the apartment in the "Bay of Dreams" is just for you.

This elite hotel is located directly on the Black Sea and has its own private beach, which is a great advantage for vacationers. Moreover, a standard double room will cost 7 thousand rubles a day. A luxury room with a wide terrace will cost 25 thousand rubles.

All guests of the "Dream Bay" are provided only with high-quality service, which is famous for the Crimean peninsula. A private hotel is a true example of a premium vacation. Therefore, here you will get only vivid impressions!
