Dolphin Botanic Platinum Hotel, Alanya, Turkey: reviews

Each tourist, considering possible places for recreation, turns his eyes towards Turkey. This, of course, is one of the best options, because here travelers are provided with ideal conditions for a pleasant pastime. Picturesque nature, interesting history, mysterious oriental culture and, of course, perfect service - what could be better?

Of course, on the Turkish coast there are a huge number of hotels. One of the really good places to stay is the Dolphin Botanic Platinum Hotel. Alanya (the hotel is located here) is one of the most popular resorts in Turkey - here every traveler will find something to his liking.

Before booking, of course, it is better to collect all the useful information about this place. What does the Dolphin Botanic Platinum Hotel look like? What do tourists say about him? Should I take children with me? What living conditions can I expect? Answers to these questions are important to many.

Hotel Location Information





When choosing a hotel, you should first of all ask about its location. Proximity to the beach, city center, shopping and entertainment venues is an important factor. So, where is the Dolphin Botanic Platinum hotel complex? Alanya, Okurcalar resort center, Incekum district - this is where you have to spend some unforgettable days.

This is a quiet place almost on the seashore. Nearby are other resort complexes. The town center is about 8 km away. It is easy to overcome by taxi or rented transport. In the central part of tourists, a variety of entertainment awaits, ranging from cafes and shopping centers, ending with water parks and nightclubs. Guests can relax, enjoying the silence and at the same time not feeling divorced from civilization.

Where is the airport?

Of course, the question of the remoteness of airports is of interest to many tourists. Antalya Airport (this is where many travelers fly here) is located about 90 km. Therefore, you will have to spend several hours on the road. On the other hand, you can buy a ticket to Alanya or Side - the distance is no more than 30 km. By the way, the Dolphin Botanical Platinum Alania 5 hotel provides its guests with a comfortable shuttle service.

Description of the territory, information about the infrastructure

Dolphin Botanic Platinum is a relatively new hotel complex, which was opened in 2014. The hotel is a huge six-story building, built in a very original, modern style. Appearance and interior decoration testify to luxury and modern amenities. Four spacious panoramic elevators offer breathtaking views of the surroundings.

What else is worth knowing about the Dolphin Botanic Platinum Hotel? Reviews of former guests indicate that the hotel area is really huge. Everything here is generously decorated with greenery and decorative flowers, palm trees, cacti and other exotic plants. The hotel's courtyard resembles a real botanical garden - travelers are delighted with such beauty and diversity. It is nice to relax here at any time of the day.

Dolphin Botanic Platinum Hotel (Alanya): photo and room information





This is a large hotel in which guests can choose from 447 rooms. It has standard rooms, spacious family rooms and rooms with a high level of comfort. In any case, you can count on modern design, new furniture, a spacious balcony, panoramic windows and, of course, the necessary equipment.

The room has a large plasma TV (there is a connection to satellite TV), a telephone, a convenient safe with a reliable lock, a powerful air conditioning system. It features a minibar with fridge. By the way, it is filled every day with drinking water, beer and soft drinks.

There is also a fairly spacious bathroom with the necessary fixtures. Guests can use the shower, relax in the hot foam bath. Of course, guests can count on towels (they are changed daily) and hygiene items. The maids really come to clean every day (if there are no other instructions from the guests), and they treat their work very responsibly.

Catering scheme for tourists





What does the Dolphin Botanic Platinum Hotel offer? Turkey is famous for its luxurious resorts that provide guests with nutritious meals. This hotel is no exception - residents are offered breakfast, lunch, lunch and dinner.

Three times a day, guests are invited to the spacious hall of the main restaurant, where they are already waiting for the set tables. In their reviews, tourists say that food is on top here. The choice of dishes is simply huge: appetizers, salads, slices. Every day several varieties of meat and fish are served, an abundance of fresh fruits and pastries will delight every tenant.

Non-alcoholic and alcoholic drinks are also free, however, only from a local manufacturer. Some drinks at the bar need to be paid separately.

Restaurants and bars in the hotel area

The territory of the hotel "Dolphin Botanical Platinum Alania 5" is huge. And here there is not only a large dining room with receptions, but also 6 restaurants a la carte. You can enjoy all the delights of Italian, Mexican, Greek, Lebanese, French and Turkish cuisine. Of course, the institutions are equipped with the appropriate charm. You can not only enjoy your favorite dishes, but also completely immerse yourself in the atmosphere of a foreign country. By the way, during the stay, guests can once visit each of the restaurants for free. Naturally, you need to book a table in advance.

There are also several cafes and bars on site, where guests are pleased to offer chilled drinks, cocktails, sandwiches and other light snacks. Be sure to go to the vitamin bar, which serves freshly squeezed juices and healthy cocktails.

Where is the beach located? Conditions for relaxation by the sea





Luxurious sandy beaches - this is exactly what Alanya is famous for. Hotel "Dolphin Botanical Platinum" is located almost on the seashore. The distance to the beach is no more than 100 m. The coast here is clean and beautiful, covered with soft fine sand. The beach strip is wide. By the way, only hotel guests have a rest here.

You can use comfortable sunbeds and mattresses for free. The hotel provides clean and large towels (free of charge, but on bail). Here, tourists have a great time enjoying the sea air, sunbathing and swimming. By the way, the water here is clean, the entrance to the sea is convenient and safe, and large waves rarely rise.

Water activities

On the shore, of course, you can find a lot of ways to spend time actively. A convenient and safe volleyball court is equipped here - various competitions are often organized for guests here.

For a very reasonable price, you can rent a boat, catamaran or other water transport. Travelers get a lot of pleasure doing boat trips, water-skiing and bananas, doing parasailing. The hotel has a diving school - here customers are offered express training courses, as well as equipment rental and tours to the most interesting places on the coast.

Additional services





Of course, guests can count on all possible amenities. Rest should not be overshadowed by small household problems.

Dry cleaning and laundry services are available on site. If necessary, you can also hand over things for ironing.

Free luggage storage is available. For your convenience, a currency exchange office operates (the rate is almost the same as in the city). By the way, credit cards are accepted for payment, which is convenient.

Throughout the building there is Internet access. By the way, the lounge has an air-conditioned relaxation area with comfortable sofas. There is a small souvenir shop where you can buy beautiful gifts with the hotel's branding. In the local mini-market, they sell various household trifles, which are sometimes not enough.

Business Services

Many tourists prefer to combine travel and work. The Dolphin Botanical Platinum hotel complex is ideal for this. To begin with, it is worth saying that you can connect to the Internet throughout the territory - this is a free service, and the connection speed is decent. Some business travelers prefer to spend time in a quiet and comfortable Internet cafe.

You can use the printer, scanner, fax, and other office equipment at your local business center. It is possible to use the services of a secretary (for an additional fee). In addition, the hotel has two conference rooms, which are often rented for seminars, meetings and other business events. Hotel staff copes well with the organization.

Leisure at the hotel - what to do with yourself?





The hotel was created in such a way as to fully organize the leisure of tourists. Here, every guest will find a useful and fun activity. A huge plot of the yard is reserved for the pool. By the way, there is also its own water park with water slides, which operates daily. Comfortable sun loungers and parasols are located nearby on the terrace. There is also a bar where guests are offered refreshments, ice cream and light snacks.

Both children and their parents enjoy the spacious Luna Park with a swing and even their own Ferris wheel.

You can have a good time playing bowling or billiards. On the territory there are several sports fields. There is a private SPA center with a jacuzzi, Turkish bath and massage rooms. For an additional fee, visitors are offered a lot of beauty and wellness treatments. And those travelers who prefer to keep fit, can visit the local fitness center (there are both exercise machines for independent work, and group classes with instructors).

Of course, the hotel has its own animation team. In the daytime, they organize various contests and entertainment events, and in the evenings everyone is invited to watch an interesting show. By the way, interesting activities can be found outside the Dolphin Botanical Platinum Hotel. Turkey is a country with an interesting culture and a lot of attractions. Therefore, do not give up sightseeing holidays. Guides work in the hotel, and there are enough travel agencies in the city organizing trips around the area.

Conditions for relaxing with a child

Modern parents prefer to spend holidays with their children. Is the Dolphin Botanic Platinum hotel complex suitable for this? Alanya is a good place for family vacations, and travelers can count on appropriate conditions at the hotel.

There will be no problems with accommodation - the hotel has spacious family rooms, and workers will deliver a comfortable folding bed to the standard room. Newly made mothers will be happy, because in the hotel you can rent a comfortable stroller. The restaurant offers small guests comfortable high chairs. By the way, there is also a special children's menu.

In terms of entertainment, the hotel also has something to offer. A comfortable and spacious pool is equipped for children, as well as a playground with swings. Children 4-12 years old have fun in a mini club, where animators look after and entertain them. Of course, little tourists have fun on the beach, and the water slides completely enthrall them.

Dolphin Botanical Platinum Hotel (Alanya): reviews of 2017, opinions of former guests





Of course, when choosing a hotel complex, you should pay attention not only to official information, but also to the opinion of former guests. What do they say about the Dolphin Botanic Platinum Hotel (Alanya)?

Guest reviews are positive - tourists really like it here. The territory is large and beautiful, the rooms are comfortable, there are all the necessary amenities, and hotel staff are trying to do everything to make guests feel at home. The food is excellent - the dishes are fresh and served in sufficient quantities, the menu is constantly changing, and homemade cakes will satisfy even the most demanding traveler. The waiters and bartenders at local establishments are polite. By the way, do not refuse to visit a la carte restaurants.

There are plenty of entertainments in the territory - you can always find something to your liking, whether it be pool contests or board games. Animators give all their best in order to create a light and pleasant atmosphere in the hotel. In a word, travelers unanimously recommend the Dolphin Hotel. After all, the service here is at its best, and the cost of living is quite reasonable.
