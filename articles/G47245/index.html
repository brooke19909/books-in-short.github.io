Hotel Bridge Resort 4 * (Russia, Sochi): review, rating, description and reviews

Historically, Sochi has always been considered one of the most popular regions for recreation. Today, this main Russian resort is considered the southern capital of our country. Having become a major financial and cultural center, Sochi has developed rapidly over the past decade.



Overview

In 2012, Forbes called this city the best for business in our country, and this is not at all surprising. By the beginning of the Sochi Olympics, a number of large-scale projects were launched that made the resort infrastructure the most modern, and the amenities for tourists are multifaceted.

People come here not only to idly rest, but also to be treated. The sanatorium and resort infrastructure of Sochi combines many specific wellness methods that are unique to this region with the general procedures used in other resorts. These types of treatment include climatotherapy and balneology, mud therapy, as well as many types of physiotherapy exercises.

Modern Sochi stretches along the eastern coast of the Black Sea. According to some sources, this is the most elongated city in our country. It includes the historical center and neighboring resort towns merged with it. Many of them - Dagomys and Khosta, as well as Krasnaya Polyana, Adler and Matsesta - are famous in themselves.

Museums and sports facilities of the Olympic level, churches, five hundred hotels, including those included in world hotel chains, beaches, entertainment venues, and also the lack of visas, combined with excellent transport links, all this is Sochi, the southern capital, which proud of Russia.



4 * Bridge Resort: rating

This branded hotel complex is located in an ecologically clean resort area - in the Imereti Lowland. The hotel territory is six hectares. The Bridge Resort 4 * was conceived by the creators according to the visual image of the bridge complex connecting all its buildings. Guests are given the opportunity to find themselves in various parts of our planet, while not leaving the hotel grounds. Eleven capitals of the states are presented in thematic buildings of Bridge Resort 4 *. Those who wish can "stop" to choose from Vienna, Sydney, Paris, New York, Istanbul, London, as well as Athens, Rome, Beijing, Tokyo and even Rio de Janeiro.

The hotel complex Bridge Resort Hotel (4 *) is located in close proximity to the famous Olympic Park. The choice of such a unique place allows you to combine a relaxing beach holiday with an active one. Convenient location and nearby transport interchanges, combined with developed infrastructure and minimal distance from significant sightseeing and business facilities make this hotel an ideal choice for a stay in Sochi. Bridge Resort 4 * is located nine kilometers from Adler Station. You can walk to Fisht Stadium in twenty minutes. The international airport is eight kilometers away.
