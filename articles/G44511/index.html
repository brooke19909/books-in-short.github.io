VDNH, hotels: addresses, description of rooms, reviews

Moscow is one of the largest cities in Russia, its capital. People from different countries and cities come here, they need a place to stay. For this, hotels have been created.

Finding good and cheap hotels is difficult enough, especially those that are near the metro station. If you want to stay in Moscow in the VDNH area, the hotels of the capital will be happy to welcome you!

In this article, we will talk about the best hotels in Moscow located in the VDNH area.

Hotel "Golden Ear"

This hotel has only three stars, but during its activities earned a large number of prizes, four times received the Crystal Rook award.



Located at: Yaroslavskaya street, house number 15, Russia, Moscow. Guest registration is available around the clock.

The Golden Ear Hotel is located in six blocks and presents guests of 330 rooms of various classes: luxury, standard, comfort. Each room has a single or double bed, minibar, refrigerator, telephone, TV, Wi-Fi.

This hotel has its own car park. Hotel ratings do not fall below 7 points out of 10. In the VDNH area, hotels are not uncommon, but this hotel is one of the best, as hundreds of visitors claim that it offers fast service, friendly staff, quality cleaning and delicious dishes.

Hotel Cosmos

She has the same 3 stars as the previous one. Located at: Prospekt Mira, house number 150, Russia, Moscow. Registration round the clock.

This renowned hotel offers you nice rooms with a pleasant interior. In any room there is a new TV with many different channels, a Wi-Fi network, a refrigerator, a bathroom with a hairdryer and personal hygiene items.



In addition, there are children's and adult pools, a fitness club, saunas and baths, it is possible to rent conference rooms, video equipment and audio equipment, and the services of a professional secretary are provided.

The Cosmos Hotel (Moscow, VDNH) has 11 restaurants where you can taste dishes of different cuisines. Hotel reviews are conflicting. There are very bad comments, and there are extremely positive ones. Many argue that here everything is always done on time. If any problems arise, the administration tries to eliminate them as soon as possible.

Hotel "ENEA"

This hotel at VDNH (Moscow) has a very good location. The address of this hotel: Kibalchicha street, house number 9, Moscow. In a few minutes you will be able to get to the Kremlin, in a couple of minutes - to the Botanical Garden. Sheremetyevo Airport is about 30 kilometers away.

Each room has a wardrobe, TV, table, chairs, iron and microwave. Standard rooms include a refrigerator, LCD TV, dishes and a bathroom.

In the deluxe rooms, breakfast is included in the total price. The hotel has several cafes and free Wi-Fi. Reviews about this modest establishment are quite controversial. Many strongly recommend staying here, as the administration is always ready to help their customers.

Hotel Oksana

This 3-star hotel is located in Ostankino, one of the nicest areas of the city. It is easy to walk to the metro in a few minutes. Sokolniki Park and the All-Russian Exhibition Center are located nearby.



The hotel itself is located at: Yaroslavskaya street, house 15, building 2. Here everyone has the opportunity to stay with pets. There is also a restaurant with dishes of European and Russian cuisine, a bar.

Free Wi-Fi is available, there is the opportunity to visit the spa and business center, laundry. The hotel provides 24-hour room service. This hotel has a special offer: children under 7 years old with a relative in one room have a rest for free. The reviews are mostly positive. Some, of course, argue that housekeeping is done very early, and the food is not so tasty, but there are many more positive reviews.

By the way, such hotels in the VDNH area are very common, however, the hotels presented in this article are the best.

Hotel "Viva"

This is an extremely pleasant hotel. Located at: Prospekt Mira, house number 105, about a couple of minutes walk from the nearest metro station, check-in is available 24 hours a day.

In the VDNH area, 3-star hotels are often found, however this hotel is considered one of the best (according to tourists' reviews).

Very close is the museum-estate of Count Sheremetev, Ostankino television tower. In addition, the main botanical garden of the Russian Academy of Sciences.



The hotel rooms have free Wi-Fi, a telephone, a TV and a hairdryer. The hotel also has a spa center and cafe. Reviews are contradictory, an equal number of both negative and positive. Many tourists like the look of their rooms. Cleaning is done regularly.

Mini-hotel "Legend"

The hotel is located near the metro station "VDNH". Address: Yaroslavskaya street, house number 8, building number 5, Moscow, Russia. Registration of guests around the clock. A feature of this hotel is the ability to book a program with a city guide.

The classic-style rooms feature an LCD TV, DVD player, minibar and kettle with hairdryer, as well as free Wi-Fi.



Near the hotel there are various cafes, bars and shops. Sheremetyevo Airport is 30 minutes by car. The reviews about this hotel are extremely positive. Hundreds of tourists say that they really enjoyed their stay here, they want to return to Moscow again and stay at this hotel!

Hotel Yaroslavskaya

This hotel has been serving guests for more than 30 years and has 3 star status. She has a very good location: 3 minutes walk to the VDNKh metro station. Acceptable rates are surprising.

The hotel is located at: Yaroslavskaya street, house number 8, building number 1, Moscow, Russia. The hotel includes 90 rooms with affordable Wi-Fi and bathrooms, a TV and a refrigerator. The deluxe rooms have a seating area. Each room has a superb view of the Ostankino Tower, as well as the All-Russian Exhibition Center.

At the entrance there is a cozy cafe, and there is also a sauna, hairdresser, tour desk and luggage storage. This hotel is located next to the All-Russian Exhibition Center and the Cosmonautics Museum, and the city center can be reached in about 20 minutes by bus.



Reviews about this hotel are mostly positive. Many people pay attention to a convenient location near the metro station, to comfortable rooms, the hotel staff is friendly and polite.

Summarize

This article described the main hotels near VDNH. Hotels here are far from rare, there are a lot of them, only the best in terms of price and quality balance are collected in the article. The hotels listed above in the VDNH area will help you make your choice.
