Where to look for a hotel? Location Description





It's no secret that the location of the hotel can be both its advantage and disadvantage. So where is the hotel complex Villa del Sol? A small, but modern and comfortable hotel is located in the resort town of Phan Thiet, almost on the seashore: within walking distance is the picturesque Tien Thanh Beach.

By the way, the distance to the city center is 6 kilometers, it can be easily reached even on foot. Those travelers who are not inclined to take long walks can use public transport or taxi services. By the way, in 10 minutes you can drive to the foot of Ta Ku Mountain, which is also a popular tourist attraction. The road to the airport takes only a few minutes.

Additional information about the hotel territory





Villa del Sol is a modern four-star hotel, which is popular among many foreign tourists. The hotel itself is a small two-story building, built in a modern style. There are several separate villas located in the garden. Travelers themselves note that the hotel is more like a luxurious secluded country house.

There is a large, beautifully arranged area. Here you can stroll through the tropical garden, enjoy the bright colors of exotic plants, relax in the shade of palm trees or walk between neatly trimmed lawns. This is what the hotel complex Villa del Sol looks like. The reviews confirm that it is really pleasant to spend time enjoying the view of the sea and mountains.

Meal plan for guests





It's no secret that one of the most important points is food, because without a good meal, a vacation is unlikely to be complete. So what can the hotel Villa del Sol 4 * offer? Paying for room accommodation, tourists are entitled to daily breakfasts, which are held in the main hotel restaurant in buffet style.

For breakfast you can enjoy traditional European snacks. It also serves warm pancakes, several types of fried eggs, fresh fruits, vegetable dishes, as well as delicious pastries and some Asian dishes.

The rest of the day, guests can also eat at the Vista Almar restaurant, but meals are already prepared to order and paid separately. The menu includes Asian cuisine, as well as dishes from different varieties of fish and seafood.

The hotel also has the Vinedo Bar, which is open until late in the evening. Here, travelers can relax in a relaxed atmosphere, enjoying delicious cocktails, aromatic coffee, spirits and, of course, a pleasant company.

This is exactly what the food scheme looks like on the territory of Villa del Sol (Vietnam). Reviews indicate that the hotel food is very tasty: fresh dishes are always served in sufficient quantities, and the service here is at a high level.

Do not forget that Phan Thiet is a resort, tourist city. There are a large number of cafes and restaurants of various formats, so choosing a place for lunch or dinner is not difficult. Travelers can visit the local market, which sells fresh vegetables, fruits, seafood and even prepared meals.

Conditions for living with a child

Many parents travel with their children. Can I take a child with me to the Villa del Sol 4 * hotel complex? Vietnam, Phan Thiet - a great place to spend a vacation, children like it. According to reviews, the hotel is ideal for a relaxing family holiday by the sea.
