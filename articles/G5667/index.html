Restaurant "Before Dawn" (St. Petersburg): how to get there? Reviews and photos

St. Petersburg is one of the most beautiful cities in the world. A huge number of architectural monuments are concentrated on its territory. And mainly in the center of the city. After all, the whole life of Peter is concentrated here! And therefore it is not surprising that it is in the heart of the Northern capital, among ancient castles and drawbridges across the Neva, that the most original karaoke restaurant is located with a beautiful panoramic view of the city center - “To Dawn”.

This is the best restaurant in St. Petersburg: its original premise (in the form of a three-deck ship) is impressive; the interior is inspiring; service - 100%; and the dishes served are delicious and beautiful in their unique design!

Description

Restaurant "Before Dawn" (St. Petersburg) is atypical in terms of the premises it occupies, because it is located on a ship. These are 3 tiers of magnificent bright space, supplemented by views of the Neva, ancient drawbridges across the river, Strelka of Vasilyevsky Island.





And what is really interesting, every visitor to the establishment from time to time, being in a restaurant, feels himself “floating on the water” - both literally and figuratively: since small currents periodically rock the ship; and looking through the windows, you observe a smooth flow of one era of the city’s life into another.

But all this only gives an additional twist to the best restaurant of St. Petersburg - "To Dawn"!





Each tier is decorated in its own unique style and color.

The first room is in red, black and white.

On the second tier, the entire interior is in beige and greenish tones interspersed with pale blue, which creates a feeling of airiness in space.

The third tier, the uppermost - in transparent pale pink tones. Great for a romantic celebration!





Programs, traditions, facilities

As in any good institution, the Do Dawn restaurant also has its own traditions, chips and event programs so that each guest feels their own uniqueness, individual approach, attentive attitude and cannot be bored while being inside the institution.

From 5 p.m. every day it works as a species restaurant in which it is pleasant to spend time - enjoying the cozy atmosphere, pleasant background music, excellent cuisine, views from the window. Here you can have a great time with your family, with your loved one, also in a friendly company or plan a business meeting.

From 22.00 to 6.00 in the restaurant begins musical events: karaoke, dancing, and on Fridays and Saturdays - concerts of famous artists and music shows with the best DJs.

Every night, exactly at midnight, everyone celebrates the birth of a new day. This has already become a real tradition of the institution. On this occasion, each guest present is honored with a free shot.

And at 2 o'clock one more spectacular event of the city takes place - the cultivation of the Birzhevoy Bridge.

Also, as a entertainment for visitors, a special hookah is offered, which is copyright, and it is equipped with special devices that are no longer in St. Petersburg.

There is such a feature in the restaurant "Before Dawn": each visitor has the opportunity to become the owner of the coveted key from a special cabinet where he can store his alcoholic beverages. All details are with the bartender and the waiters.





Banquet halls

In the favorite restaurant "Do Dawn" in St. Petersburg (he only deserved positive reviews from visitors), three halls and a terrace can be considered as banquet. This place has become already loved by many locals in terms of large-scale events, celebrations (weddings, anniversaries), corporate events.

On the first deck there is a hall with a capacity of 120 people, if a banquet, and 250 people, if a buffet event.

On the second deck, capacity is 250 people (banquet) and 400 people (buffet).

The third deck seats 150 people (banquet) and 250 people (buffet).

The summer terrace has access to the water, there is a pier.

All sound, light, video, music equipment is available. There is also a piano.

And therefore, all the necessary and even the best conditions are created for an unforgettable holiday with a banquet or buffet.

The average cost of a banquet is 1500-3500 rubles per person.
