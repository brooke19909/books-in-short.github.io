Hotel Galaxy (3 *) Hotel, Vietnam, Nha Trang: overview, description, characteristics and reviews of tourists

The city of Nha Trang, proudly called by the Vietnamese its beach capital, is ready to offer tourists accommodation in comfortable and affordable hotels that harmoniously look against the backdrop of beautiful nature. The top ten three-star hotels in the country (in terms of price and quality) includes Galaxy 3 * Hotel. This affordable but comfortable hotel is ideal for travelers who come here for a sandy beach and turquoise sea.

general description

Galaxy 3 Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang) opened its doors to its first visitors in 2014. This is a modern eight-story building with a restaurant, without a pool and its own territory, but the latter is not found in most hotels in Nha Trang.

The friendly reception staff is ready to help their guests at any time. Checking into a hotel, using a left-luggage office, currency exchange, connecting to free Wi-Fi, renting bicycles and cars for independent walks - these and other issues are the responsibility of the hotel staff.





Also, employees are well oriented in their own city and popular tourist sites. Their knowledge and skills will help travelers determine the most interesting excursion routes and book tickets for them.

Location

Only 26 kilometers from the airport in the tourist center of the city is Galaxy Hotel Nha Trang 3 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang). To overcome this short distance, tourists can either use the services of a taxi, or contact the hotel staff for arranging a transfer. In both cases, the service will be paid.

Not far from the hotel is the famous Phu Dong Park (100 m), and even closer - the emerald-colored sea and a magnificent sandy beach (50 m).





Also within walking distance are excellent places for active people to relax, for example, a sailing club.

Popular and interesting sights of the city, which are located in the immediate vicinity of the hotel, include:

  • elegant Tram Huong Tower on April 2nd Square (300 meters from the hotel);
  • Dam market (2100 m);
  • the largest pagoda of the city is Long Son (3000 m);
  • magnificent Chamsky towers Po Nagar (3800 m);
  • The longest cable car in the world over the sea is Vinpearl (4700m).

Number of rooms

Residential fund Galaxy Hotel Nha Trang 3 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang) consists of 65 rooms of varying degrees of comfort.

All rooms have parquet flooring and a classic set of furniture for the hotel. The rooms also have air conditioning, cable TV (some Russian channels are transmitted), individual safe, telephone and minibar.

An additional advantage of the Galaxy 3 Hotel is the availability of a stable wireless Internet signal, not only at the reception, but also in any room. Hotel guests can use Wi-Fi for free.

Also, at no extra charge, guests are provided with:

  • set for making coffee and tea;
  • 2 bottles of drinking water daily.

The en suite bathroom has a shower and offers disposable slippers, free toiletries and a hairdryer.





Maximum two people can be accommodated in each room, extra cots (cribs) are not installed. But it is possible to stay for free in a room with the parents of one young child who will sleep on the beds in the room.

By the level of comfort, hotel rooms are divided into three categories:

  • Superior (35 rooms of 22 sq. M with a small internal window, the view from it is very limited);
  • Deluxe with window (16 rooms of 24 sq. M with a small window to the neighboring building);
  • Deluxe with balcony (14 rooms of 28 square meters each with a small balcony, some of which offer stunning sea views).

Each room is cleaned daily. Linen is changed once a week or upon request.

Nutrition

Galaxy 3 Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang / Vietnam) follows the country's widespread hotel practice, offering its guests only a buffet breakfast, which is included in the price of the night. At other times, the restaurant can also eat deliciously. Using the menu, tourists can order not only international dishes, but also try the local one.





Cook's culinary masterpieces can be eaten not only at a table in a cozy room, but also in your own hotel room. Upon request, guests can arrange the delivery of cooked meals directly to the room.

Positive reviews

When choosing a hotel in an unfamiliar city, you should focus not only on the brochures of tour operators, but also on the reviews of real people who have already been there. Having opened several years ago, Galaxy 3 Hotel 3 * (Vietnam) has already managed to fall in love with tourists from different parts of the world. Below are the reviews about this hotel left by Russian travelers.

The location of the hotel is really excellent: in the center of the tourist area, close to the beach and main attractions. And with this arrangement, the cost of a room per day is quite low.

Check-in is quick. If necessary, hotel employees are ready to selflessly help, for example, with the delivery of luggage to the room or the operation of a safe.

All vacationers note that the hotel employs wonderful and helpful people who, even with a language barrier, try to help tourists.

Free beach towels were found as a pleasant surprise for many, the presence of which is not mentioned in advertising leaflets.





Most tourists agree that the hotel is quite normal for its price category.

Negative reviews

It sometimes happens that for some people the location of the Galaxy 3 Hotel in a busy quarter on a dead end street is an undeniable advantage, while for others it is a disadvantage. In their comments, they complain that it was hard to fall asleep under city noise. But unfortunately, this is the trouble of all the densely populated areas of Nachyang.

There are also complaints from tourists about the rooms, more precisely, about the size of the window and the limited view from it. But this feature of the rooms is inherent only in budget accommodation options. In this regard, you just need to book a room of another category and a limited view from the window will not interfere with a good rest.

Thus, if you plan to relax actively, and use the room only for the night, then Galaxy 3 Hotel 3 * (Vietnam) is a good budget option.
