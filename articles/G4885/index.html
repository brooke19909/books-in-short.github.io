Hotel Konnos Bay Hotel Apartments 3 * (Cyprus / Protaras) - overview, description and reviews of tourists

Trips to Cyprus - a popular tourist destination, which is known largely due to the resort of Ayia Napa. Thousands of party-goers from all over the world come to this town every year with the desire to spend the best vacation in the world. However, Cyprus is not only the bustling Ayia Napa, but also dozens of other, no less wonderful places. So, many travelers have undeservedly forgotten the resort town of Protaras, which has spread its expanses very nearby. Those who dream of a quiet vacation surrounded by fine sand, blue waves and palm trees, simply can not find the best offer.

As a low-cost accommodation option, a small hotel Konnos Bay Hotel Apartments 3 * is perfect. The hotel is suitable for youth, couples with children and active travelers.

Description

Konnos Bay Hotel Apartments is a small hotel with 66 rooms. The hotel rank is 3 stars, so travelers get an excellent opportunity to stay with sufficient comfort for a very reasonable fee.



The hotel building was built in 1990, and the last renovation was carried out in 2009. Despite the fact that the hotel is not one of the newest buildings, the rooms boast fresh repairs, cleanliness and comfort. The administration offers tourists accommodation without meals, on the basis of breakfast or half board (breakfast + dinner).

The successful location of the hotel allows you to combine a quiet holiday with sightseeing programs and active entertainment.

Where is the hotel situated

Konnos Bay Hotel Apartments 3 * is built on a small hill directly on the shore of a secluded beach in Konnos Bay. This gives hotel guests the opportunity to feel comfortable in a quiet place.

On the other hand, large resorts are within easy reach. Therefore, at the first opportunity, you can go to more lively places in search of noisy nightlife and entertainment.

  • Ayia Napa Resort is a 5-minute drive away.
  • Resort Protaras - 10-15 minutes walk.
  • Larnaca Airport is an hour away.
  • Protaras Airport is a 1.5-hour drive.

Konnos Bay Hotel Apartments 3 * is ready to provide vacationers with basic services that will make your stay at the resort convenient and comfortable.



  • 24 hour reception. Reception is available 24 hours a day.

  • Safe. Especially valuable things can be left in it. Offer to use it at an additional cost.
  • Parking. The hotel has a car parking area, which is located in a private hotel area.
  • Cleaning services. Laundry and dry cleaning services are available at an additional cost.
  • Currency exchange. It is very comfortable. You can exchange directly at the hotel - there will be no cash problems.
  • Car rental. This service is useful to vacationers who plan free movement in the vicinity.
  • The Internet. High-speed internet access is available in the hotel‚Äôs public areas and rooms.
  • Fax machine. Business travelers can use this service at any time.

Types of rooms

Konnos Bay Hotel Apartments 3 * in Protaras can be attributed to small hotels. After all, at his disposal only 66 apartments. At the same time, guests at check-in can choose a room with the necessary living conditions.

Studio. This is a compact room designed for 2-3 people. The room is zoned into a bedroom, living room and kitchenette. The studio has a bathroom with a toilet, shower and bath.

One bedroom apartment. Such apartments consist of two separate rooms, one of which is a bedroom, and the other is a living room combined with a kitchenette. It can comfortably accommodate from 2 to 4 people. The bathroom has a washbasin, toilet and shower.

Two-Bedroom Apartment This spacious apartment has 2 double bedrooms and a living room with an equipped kitchenette. There is a bathroom with a toilet, a washbasin and a shower.

Nutrition

Konnos Bay Hotel Apartments 3 * offers guests accommodation without meals. In other words, room rates are shown for accommodation only. Those who wish can pay for meals separately and come to the hotel for breakfast, lunch or dinner.

There is a restaurant serving excellent international cuisine. For cooking, high-quality fresh products are used. Themed evenings are held here periodically, where guests can enjoy Mediterranean cuisine.

At the poolside bar you can enjoy a cold or strong drink and a light snack. It serves juices, great cocktails, snacks and a wide selection of spirits. Meals at the bar are paid separately.



Things to do in Cyprus at Konnos Bay Hotel Apartments

Tourists who decide to stay at the hotel can have a good time. Several types of entertainment are provided for them at once:

  • Pool. It is located right in the courtyard of the hotel with equipped territory. The area is tiled and equipped with sun loungers, mattresses and umbrellas. Even with the maximum occupancy of the hotel, vacationers have enough beach equipment.
  • Billiard tables and tables for table tennis.
  • Darts.
  • Electronic games.
  • Spacious TV room.
  • Great tennis court. Equipment for the game (rackets and balls) is leased for a small fee.




For children

Protaras Konnos Bay Hotel Apartments in Protaras is often visited by holidaymakers with children. For such guests there is everything necessary for a comfortable stay:

  • Free cot in room. The service is available upon request.
  • Playroom. Here children can draw, play board and outdoor games.
  • Equipped playground on site.

Beach

The hotel is located near the beach on a small hill. The road will take no more than 10-15 minutes. However, to reach it, you will need to go down the stairs.



The coastal strip consists of fine sand. They are cleaned here quite often. The crystal clearness of the water and the beach is affected by the small number of tourists vacationing in this part of the island. This is confirmed by the blue flags of the European Union. The entrance to the water is quite gentle and sandy. This makes swimming here as comfortable and safe as possible.

The beach is equipped with sun umbrellas and sun loungers. However, the use of them is paid - the cost is small.

Those who prefer a more active vacation will surely like:

  • boat trips;
  • yachting;
  • Beach volleyball;
  • windsurfing;
  • riding a banana.

What to see

Bored on the beach, tourists at any time can go for a walk in the resort town of Ayia Napa, especially since it is only a few minutes drive to it.

Aquapark. Ayia Napa has a stunning water park that will win the hearts of not only small travelers, but also their parents. Water rides are made in the spirit of the myths of Ancient Greece. And not far away is the famous ship "Black Pearl".

Luna Park. Here you will find entertainment for the whole family, as well as for the most desperate. After all, not everyone dares to ride some of the local attractions here.

Travelers who prefer rich excursion programs can visit:

  • The monastery - it is here that the veil of Jesus is located, which Saint Veronica gave him when he went to Calvary.
  • Museum of Thalassa - all exhibits here are devoted to marine subjects.
  • Park of stone sculptures.

Reviews

If you believe the reviews of tourists who have been here, the Konnos Bay Hotel Apartments 3 * hotel in Protaras boasts an excellent ratio of quality service and low price.



The restaurant offers a fairly large selection of dishes, the quality of food is always on top. Thanks to the frequent cleaning, the rooms are clean and fresh, and the service staff is helpful and smiling.

The only things that tourists traveling here should remember are the distance from the tourist centers and the long road to the beach (getting on the stairs to the afternoon heat is quite tiring). In addition, a trip from the airport will take about an hour. This feature is important for people traveling with young children.

Thus, you can recommend a trip to Protaras Konnos Bay Hotel Apartments 3 * to all those who like a secluded quiet holiday and not too demanding on service. In general, the hotel fully lives up to its 3 star rating thanks to high quality service and courteous staff. In addition, tourists will be pleasantly surprised by the prices for accommodation in this hotel.
