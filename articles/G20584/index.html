Construction of a pattern of vests for girls. Product Assembly Sequence

The vest is one of the most famous wardrobe items that will be a great addition to clothes of absolutely any style. She can be dressed with jeans or formal pants, with a skirt, shorts and breeches. It all depends on the style and material. Patterns of vests for girls are easy to build, and tailoring is not difficult even for those who have no experience.

Materials and Tools

To make a fashionable thing, you will need fabric for the top and lining, a straight-line sewing machine, scissors for cutting material, paper or construction film to make a blank, a centimeter tape, matching threads and buttons. You should also consider decorative elements. Suitable stones, buttons, sewn spikes and stones, floral applications, adhesive rhinestones, zippers on pockets and more.



To make a pattern of vests for girls, you will need to take measurements from the figure of the child: volumes of the chest, waist, hips and neck. Shoulder width, chest height, back and front widths will also be required.

Building blanks by standards

How to sew a vest for a girl with your own hands? Pattern is the basis of the product, which means that for a quality thing you need to make a blank that will fit the size of the child. All measurements taken must be transferred to the drawing in the correct sequence. Then, in accordance with the height of the chest, an additional horizontal line is drawn, on which half the width of the back, armhole and half the width of the front are marked. In the upper corners, they retreat along ¬º of the girth of the neck, shoulder seams are drawn from the obtained point at an angle of 15 degrees. Next is the turn of the armhole, which they draw, connecting the edges of the shoulder seams with the chest line with a smooth line.



T-shirt template design

How to make a vest pattern for a girl in the easiest way? This technique uses a huge number of craftswomen. To create a template, you need a children's t-shirt, suitable in size. It is placed on paper and outlined, excluding the sleeve. After that, it remains to determine the depth of the cut and the average section of the front shelves. This option is perfect for a sports model on a heater. It will only be necessary to add an allowance for sintepon and proceed to the cutting of the fabric. For the classical model, fitting will be required, which is carried out during the fitting.

Cutting and assembly of parts

If it is assumed that the standard patterns of vests for girls will be used for sewing a sports model on insulation (synthetic winterizer), then when transferring the template to the fabric, you will need to give at least 2 cm + allowance for loose seams to process the seams. For other models, they do not. Obtained after building the patterns of vests for girls can be used for cutting the main fabric, lining fabric and insulation.

Assembly of the product begins with sewing off the shoulder seams. Further, the details of the base and lining material are folded face inward and sewn along the armholes and neck. After the product is turned out and the side seams are closed one by one. The last stage is the closing of the bottom sections.



It should be noted that all decorative pockets, stitches and other design elements must be done before connecting the parts. The exception is only voluminous stones and spikes, which will need to be carefully sewn to the upper fabric without affecting the lining.

Making things

Decorative decoration sets the tone for the entire product and determines the style to which the item will relate. How to sew a sports and classic vest for girls with their own hands? Patterns in this case are identical, the only thing you need to pay attention to is the length of the clothes and the design of the bottom. In the classic version, the front shelves are made longer. On a sports sleeveless jacket, the length is either made at the same level or the back is lengthened.

An important role in the decorative finish is played by the accessories used. If the product is fastened with a zipper, then in addition to it you need to make pockets on the same locks. As for color, there are many options. It can be contrasting fasteners or zippers to match. It will also be interesting to look at the option with a multi-colored rainbow snake.



In the classic version, it is best to use buttons on welt or hinged loops. In such models, pockets in the frame will look beautiful.

You can also play with color schemes. For the classics, a black base and a bright pink lining will be an excellent option. Gray and white, beige with red will also be great. Regarding the sports model, there are no restrictions at all. For the top, you can take a colorful raincoat fabric and highlight one of the flowers with a solid zipper.
