Hotel "Kars", Divnomorskoe: infrastructure and hotel reviews

Divnomorskoye is a large resort village in the Krasnodar Territory. Due to its location on the Black Sea, tourism is the main occupation of local residents. Located just twelve kilometers from Gelendzhik, this resort has an excellent ecological environment. Neither in the village itself, nor nearby there are no major highways and industrial enterprises. Comfortable pebble beaches and almost wild natural beauty - all this attracts a huge number of vacationers. The village has a well-developed infrastructure related to tourism and hotel business.

The popular hotel "Kars", Divnomorskoe

Resorts in Divnomorskoye are very popular among young people. Inexpensive private hotels, many night clubs, water parks, attractions, nudist beaches - all this attracts young people. For vacationers of middle and older age, as well as for couples in the village there are pensions, motels and hotels. One of these very popular hotels is the Kars Hotel.



Divnomorskoe is a small settlement, so the hotel is favorably located in its very center. European-style infrastructure

Kars (Divnomorskoe) is a very young hotel complex. It was built several years ago and therefore fully complies with all European standards. The hotel consists of three buildings, in front of which there are pools, and a platform with sun loungers for guests who do not want to go to the sea.
