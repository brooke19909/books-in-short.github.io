LCD "Solar System" (Khimki): description, features

Modern residential complexes rebuilding in Moscow and Moscow Region are an excellent opportunity to purchase comfortable housing in a developed area at a bargain price. If you have been looking for a suitable property option for a long time, we strongly recommend that you pay attention to the Solar System LCD (Khimki). Within this material, we will evaluate all the conditions that potential buyers of real estate can expect.





about the project

The "Solar System" from "Urban Group" is an innovative project with a unique architecture, which simply does not exist today. Here, at the city-planning level, an impeccable quality of construction is laid down, consisting of many small things. Unique solutions that combine the advantages of urban and suburban life, comfort and coziness - something that many customers have appreciated.

Location

LCD "Solar System" (Khimki) is located in the prestigious northwestern region of Moscow Region. Just 4 km in the direction of the Leningradskoye Shosse, in Khimki, a revolutionary complex is being built that will take into account the wishes of all modern customers. Developed transport accessibility allows you to get to anywhere in the capital, both by private car, and by bus, train, minibus. Travel time is 30-40 minutes.





Architecture

In the LCD "Solar System" (Khimki) - a project in which the beauty and luxury of traditional classics are harmoniously combined with the visual comfort of traditional buildings and the advantages of a minimalist style, which together provides the necessary comfort to all residents. Walking along the streets of the complex, residents enjoy bright and spectacular facades with expressive architectural elements, and in the evening they can admire all this beauty framed by bird's-eye lighting.

Security

The security of the complex, the developer paid special attention. All pedestrian paths and walking areas are completely isolated from through traffic. A child traveling to Skoda runs along walking paths, bridges, mini-squares, not meeting intersections and busy highways on its way.

Infrastructure

For the convenience of parents, the first floors of residential buildings are reserved for kindergartens - there are five of them. In a school with their own stadium, children can not only receive the necessary basic knowledge, but also develop their sports skills and spend a lot of time outdoors. In addition, creative workshops, circles and developing sections for children, bank branches, service organizations, beauty salons, cafes, small shops where you can buy everything you need will occupy the first floors of skyscrapers.





Flats

Apartments in the residential complex "Solar System" (Khimki) are represented by a variety of layouts. The complex of cast-brick residential buildings from 3 to 17 floors has become a reflection of the preferences of modern Muscovites. More than 40 plans embodying the modern format of residential development - something that all residents appreciated. In the new residential quarter, the limited edition apartments with the author's layout will be presented for the first time. The chip of the project were apartments with panoramic windows and a view of 180-260 degrees. The complex includes the so-called apartment-offices - a housing format for businessmen and office workers. And, of course, there are quite spacious, bright and comfortable apartments, ideal for families with small children - everyone will find a worthwhile option in every respect.

To summarize

LCD "Solar System" (Khimki) - an elite complex located in a prestigious area near Moscow. Author's architecture, thoughtful layouts, developed infrastructure and advanced technologies are what the developer with an impeccable reputation has emphasized. If you value the comfort of life near the capital, this complex will definitely attract your attention.
