Malaysia, beach vacation: the best places, descriptions, beaches and reviews of tourists

In wet and slushy or in cold and snowy weather it is so pleasant to be on the sandy shore under the gentle sun. For many years, many of our compatriots have preferred to go to warmer countries in winter, for example, to Malaysia. Inexperienced tourists are wondering where in Malaysia a beach holiday will not disappoint in any season? We will try to answer this question in the article.

Why choose Malaysia?

The beaches of this sunny country guarantee unity with marvelous nature, relaxation away from noisy megacities and bustle. Such a trip, especially in winter, will not leave anyone indifferent. Here you will be surrounded by exotic plants, magnificent landscapes, crystal clear turquoise sea.

Many are sure that a beach holiday in Malaysia (reviews confirm this) is a true paradise. Comfortable hotels and a tropical jungle harmoniously combine and form magnificent holiday complexes both in summer and in winter.



Malaysian Resorts

This distant country attracts travelers with its unusual nature, excellent weather, which is always suitable for a beach holiday.

Especially popular among tourists are the islands of Malaysia. Beach vacations on Penang and Langkawi delight with unusually beautiful and well-groomed beaches, luxurious nature and world-class hotels. There is always a nook for those who want to be alone, relax from the hustle and bustle. Many tourists find the mild climate that Malaysia is famous for. A beach holiday with children is especially good in the winter months, when the air does not warm up more than 32 degrees, and fresh winds from the sea allow you not to suffer from unbearable heat.



On the islands of Malaysia there are many coral reefs, rare fish and shellfish are found here. That is why scuba diving enthusiasts like to spend time here.

Wonderful beaches covered with white, black or yellow sand, with exotic trees and small bays - this is a kind of symbol of the country, its visiting card.

In this palm paradise today luxurious hotels with pools that are filled with sea water are built. It is not surprising that today, for many travelers, Malaysia has become a favorite vacation spot. A beach holiday, available in any season, turns the country into a resort of world significance. Especially popular and actively visited are such beaches as:

Malaysia in December

Winter is the best time to go to the fabulous warm Malaysia. Cheerful and always friendly Malaysians, lush greenery of exotic plants, stunning beaches and majestic mountains will instantly disperse your autumn-winter blues, and make your vacation unique and memorable.

A beach vacation in Malaysia in December, as, indeed, in any other winter month, is a common phenomenon. Indeed, at this time it is sunny and very warm (someone even believes that it is hot). The average air temperature reaches +30 degrees.

In December, Malaysia offers a lot of entertainment. Beach holidays are not the only occupation. If you like to explore the big cities and capitals of the states, then the capital of Malaysia (Kuala Lumpur) will certainly interest you. This is a fairly large modern city. At first glance, it strikes with an abundance of greenery, which wonderfully combines with the sights of the capital, and many of them are unique. By the way, beach holidays are also possible in the capital, but we will talk about this a bit later.

And lovers of a relaxing pastime away from civilization are advised to go to the Malaysian islands - Langkawi, Tioman, Penang, Redang.

Of course, for Russians, holidays in December are always associated with the celebration of the New Year, the acquisition of gifts for relatives and friends. The main shopping centers are located in Kuala Lumpur. It should be noted that most recently the capital of the country entered the top ten best cities in the world for shopping.

Malaysia: Beach Holidays in Kuala Lumpur

A young and modern city - the capital of Kuala Lumpur - invites travelers to get acquainted with numerous attractions, and after excursions relax on one of the beaches located nearby.

Closest to the city, and therefore the most visited of them - "Port Dickson". It is 30 km from the capital. The beach is well equipped: there is everything you need to relax - sun loungers, umbrellas, it is possible to rent equipment for water sports.



If tourists agree to spend about an hour on the road to the coast, they can go to the island of Pankgor, which is located north of Kuala Lumpur. Here is one of the best beaches in the country.

From the capital it is quite easy to get to Langkawi and Tioman. These are very beautiful places, so most citizens prefer to relax here.

On the island of Langkawi, diving enthusiasts will enjoy it. They will have a unique opportunity to explore the underwater beauty of the Andaman Sea. And Tioman Island has long been recognized as one of the most beautiful in the world. On this tiny piece of land you can wander through the real jungle. Beach holidays here will appeal to lovers of peace and tranquility.

Malaysia in January

A beach holiday in Malaysia in January has its own characteristics. Here, the air temperature is still quite comfortable (+28 ... +33 ). But there is one inconvenience - very high humidity.

Reviews about the rest in January are enthusiastic descriptions of the colorful capital, its unusual decoration. Indeed, in the middle of winter, Malaysians celebrate the Chinese New Year. True, at any other time, the capital of the country is beautiful.



Malaysia: Beach Holidays

Despite the fact that almost all of the country's beaches are well equipped, some of them are more popular. We will talk about them in this article.

Blue Lagoon

The most picturesque beach on the Perhentian Islands. Crystal clear water, white sand, tropical vegetation and very few people - you will agree, almost perfect vacation. True, there is one drawback - there are no palm trees at all on the beach. It is sometimes called Turtle Bay, because it is not uncommon for turtles to lay eggs here. The bottom and shore are sandy.



Pamgkor

Small island. But here is the best hotel on the Emerald Coast, the Pangkor Laut Resort Hotel, although you can also stay in more affordable accommodation. This beach is perfect for a secluded and relaxing holiday.

Penang

This island and the beach on it is the most popular on the east coast. The rich vegetation is striking in its freshness. There is a mixture of cultural traditions that will surely be of interest to every tourist.

Tioman

The top ten most popular beaches in Malaysia include the island of Tioman. This is a great place. And not only for a lazy vacation on a snow-white shore, but also for practicing various water sports. There are whale sharks. But do not worry - they are completely safe for humans.



Borneo

And this wonderful quiet place is reliably protected from the winds by the Coral Islands. This island has a romantic atmosphere, so the newlyweds love to relax here.

Tourists reviews

Judging by the reviews, rest in this distant country leaves only pleasant memories. And this is despite the fact that some religious traditions have caused certain restrictions for women (referring to the rules for attire when visiting religious buildings and entertainment complexes).

As for the rest, tourists are impressed by the young capital of the country, admires the animal and plant world of Malaysia. In the reviews, a lot of kind words are devoted to Malaysians who are very friendly and delicate towards guests.
