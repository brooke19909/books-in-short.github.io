How to make baubles of ribbons with your own hands

How to make an original friendship bracelet? For this purpose, a variety of

Materials: beads, threads, even soft wire. And you can also make a unique decoration for a friend from bright ribbons. The process is easy, fun and suitable even for beginners in needlework.

Preparation for work

To understand how to make baubles from ribbons, we will consider in order each stage of their weaving. Initially, you need to select several bright stripes from the atlas of the desired length and quantity. The optimal size is one hundred centimeters. Jewelry from satin stripes of contrasting colors looks very impressive. Well, let's get started? Two ribbons must be tied together and fixed on the work surface. It can be, for example, a desk or a soft pillow. As fixing tools, respectively, will be tape or a pin. In time, the whole process of creating jewelry takes several minutes.

How to make a miracle from two ribbons



Consider how to make baubles from ribbons using flat weaving. This option is the most common. First, you need to put two bent in the form of loops strips crosswise. Getting to work. Now you need one ribbon (for example, white) to wrap around another (pink). Then thread the second into the first loop, holding the last, carefully tighten the loop. Now, from the white ribbon, you need to fold the loop and pass the remaining pink ribbon into it. Firmly fixing the first strip, tighten the knot. In this way, we continue weaving until the required length of the bracelet is reached. At the end, you need to loop through the loop and pull both strips in different directions to secure the work. So get baubles of 2 ribbons, bright and beautiful. In conclusion, the ends of the fabric must be treated with fire so that they do not crumble. From tapes, you can create both very simple models, and original ones, even trendy ones. For example, out of three colored or plain satin stripes, you can braid a braid. Such decorative elements as beads, rhinestones, chains, etc. will decorate the bracelet.

Multi-ribbon weaving

There are many ways to make baubles from ribbons. Based on the above described option, you can weave a bracelet of four or even six fabric strips. The principle is followed the same. First, a loop is formed on each ribbon and a knot is tied. Then you need to take turns threading them into each other and gently tighten the first of them. Further weaving is as follows. You should take the tape, which is located below, on the left. It is folded and threaded into a loop located on the right. They will tell you how baubles are made of ribbons, the patterns that the craftswomen share on specialized sites or on the pages of needlework magazines. We present to your attention photos that depict the process itself.





Here's another option for making baubles from ribbons: you can use the macrame weaving technique. To do this, take the satin strip lying on the right and tie a knot on the ribbon nearest to it, pull it to the base. Then repeat and make another one nearby. Similarly, go through all the stripes. Tapes change their position. Now the first of them has become the last, and the second has taken its place. The result is a very interesting bracelet.
