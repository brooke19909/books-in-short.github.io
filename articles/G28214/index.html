Paper airplanes that fly for a very long time: schemes, descriptions and recommendations

Since childhood, we all know how to quickly make an airplane out of paper, and we have done it more than once. This origami method is simple and easy to remember. After a couple of times, you can do it with your eyes closed.

The simplest and most famous paper airplane scheme

Such an aircraft is made of a square sheet of paper, which is folded in half, then the upper edges are bent toward the center. The formed triangle bends, and the edges again bend to the center. Then the leaf bends in half and wings form.





That, in fact, is all. But there is one small drawback of such an aircraft - it almost does not soar and falls in a couple of seconds.

Generational Experience

The question arises - how to make a paper airplane that flies for a long time. This is not difficult, since several generations have improved the well-known scheme, and have greatly succeeded in this. Modern paper airplanes vary greatly in appearance and quality.

Below are different ways to make a paper airplane. Simple schemes will not confuse you, but rather inspire you to continue experimenting. Although, perhaps, they will require more time from you than the form mentioned above.

Super paper plane

Method number one. It is not much different from the one described above, but in this version the aerodynamic qualities are slightly improved, which lengthens the flight time:

  1. Fold a sheet of paper in half.
  2. Bend the corners to the middle.
  3. Turn the sheet over and bend in half.
  4. Fold the triangle to the top.
  5. Change the side of the sheet again.
  6. Bend the two right vertices to the center.
  7. Do the same with the other side.
  8. Bend the resulting plane in half.
  9. Raise the tail and straighten the wings.

This is how paper planes can be made that fly for a very long time. In addition to this obvious advantage, the model looks very impressive. So play on health.

We make a Zilke plane together

Now the next step is number two. It implies the manufacture of a Zilke aircraft. Prepare a sheet of A4 paper, and learn how to make a paper airplane that flies for a long time, following simple tips:

  1. Fold it in half lengthwise.
  2. Mark the middle of the sheet. Fold the upper half in half.
  3. Bend the edges of the resulting rectangle to the middle so that there are a couple of centimeters on each side to the middle.
  4. Turn over a sheet of paper.
  5. Form a small triangle in the upper middle. Bend the entire structure along.
  6. Open the top by folding the paper in two directions.
  7. Fold the edges so that you get wings.




The Zilke aircraft is finished and ready for operation. This was another easy way to quickly make a paper airplane that flies for a long time.

Making the duck together

Now consider the scheme of the aircraft `` Duck '':

  1. Fold a sheet of A4 paper in half along.
  2. Bend the upper ends to the middle.
  3. Turn the sheet over. Bend the side parts to the middle again, and in the upper part you should get a rhombus.
  4. Bend the upper half of the rhombus, as if folding it in half.
  5. Fold the formed triangle with an accordion, and bend the bottom top up.
  6. Now bend the resulting structure in half.
  7. At the final stage, form the wings.




Now you can make paper planes that fly for a long time! The scheme is quite simple and straightforward.

Making the Delta plane together

It is time to make a Delta plane out of paper:

  1. Fold a sheet of A4 paper in half lengthwise. Mark the middle.
  2. Rotate the sheet horizontally.
  3. On one side, draw two parallel lines to the middle, at the same distance.
  4. On the other hand, bend the paper in half to the middle mark.
  5. Bend the lower right corner to the very top line drawn so that a couple of centimeters remain intact at the bottom.
  6. Bend the upper half.
  7. Fold the formed triangle in half.
  8. Fold the structure in half and bend the wings along the marked lines.




As you can see, paper planes that fly for a very long time can be done in different ways. But that is not all. Because you will find several more types of crafts soaring in the air.

  1. You will need a square sheet of paper.
  2. Fold it diagonally to one side, unfold and bend it to the other. Leave in this position.

  4. Now fold this square diagonally.
  5. In the resulting triangle, bend the front and back leaf.
  6. Then bend them under the central triangles so that a small figure remains peeking from below.
  7. Fold the top triangle and tuck it in the middle so that a small top peeks out.
  8. Final touch: Straighten the lower wings and bend the nose.




Here's how to make a paper plane that flies easily for a long time. Enjoy the long flight of your Shuttle.

Making a Gomez Aircraft

Using the recommendations below, you can make a Gomez aircraft:

  1. Fold the sheet in half.
  2. Now bend the upper right corner to the left edge of the paper. Flatten.
  3. Do the same on the other side.
  4. Next, roll up the top so that a triangle forms. The lower part remains unchanged.
  5. Bend the lower right corner to the top.
  6. Turn the left corner inward. It should make a small triangle.
  7. Bend the structure in half and form the wings.




Now you know how to make an airplane out of paper so that it flies far.

What are paper airplanes for?

These simple airplanes schemes will allow you to enjoy the game, and even arrange competitions between different models, finding out who owns the championship in the duration and range of the flight.

Especially this lesson will appeal to boys (and maybe their dads), so teach them how to make winged cars out of paper, and they will be happy. Such classes develop dexterity, accuracy, perseverance, concentration and spatial thinking in children, contribute to the development of imagination. And the prize will be self-made paper planes that fly for a very long time.

Launch airplanes in open space in calm weather. And yet, you can take part in the competition of such crafts, but in this case you need to know that some of the models presented above are prohibited in such events.

There are many other ways to make paper planes that take a very long time to fly. The above are just some of the most effective you can do. However, do not limit yourself to them, try others. And perhaps, over time, you can improve some of the models or come up with a new, more advanced system for their manufacture.

By the way, some paper models of airplanes are capable of making air figures and various tricks. Depending on the type of construction, you will need to launch it strongly and abruptly or smoothly.

In any case, all of the above aircraft will fly for a long time and will give you a lot of pleasure and pleasant experience, especially if you made them yourself.
