Hotel Sunrise 4 * (Rhodes, Greece): overview, description, rooms and reviews of tourists

If you are attracted by the monuments of ancient architecture, but at the same time you want to spend your holidays on the coast, you should definitely take a closer look at Greece. This European resort is dearly loved by many tourists who enjoy a comfortable climate and amazing culture every year. Rhodes is perhaps the most beautiful Greek island, occupying a southern position and washed by the waters of the Aegean and the Mediterranean Sea. Stunning nature, developed infrastructure of the resort, comfortable climate - these are the things that attract many Russians. Of course, the part of the island you, as a tourist, will choose affects a lot of the quality of your vacation. Lindos is a resort that will be appreciated by all lovers and true lovers of a beach holiday. It is here that the best beaches of the island and the warm gentle sea.





Now for the hotels. Greek hotels will definitely not give you unpleasant surprises, their category corresponds to European standards. Tourists can safely focus on the stardom of a potential option. The 4 * hotel will offer good service, most likely, the room will have a hairdryer, a kettle and a set of dishes. Tourists who prefer a 5 * hotel will receive the highest service. Their rooms will be equipped with everything you need, including bath amenities: towels, bathrobes and slippers. Well, everyone who wants to save money can pay attention to 3 * and 2 * hotels. So what to choose? Hotel Sunrise 4 * (Rhodes) is a pretty popular option. It is his review that we offer to all potential vacationers. Reviews of real tourists will be an excellent information base for an objective review.

About hotel

Sunrise Hotel 4 * (Rhodes) is a beach hotel located in the center of Lindos resort on the Mediterranean coast. The coast is only 150 meters, therefore, more and more often this option is chosen by lovers of quality beach holidays. Renovated spacious and bright rooms, a large green area, development infrastructure, rich animation program, all-inclusive meals are what the Sunrise Hotel 4 * (Rhodes) can offer.





It was built back in 1990, in the entire history of its existence, it has managed to establish itself quite well among tourists from different parts of the globe. The hotel was reconstructed and redecorated in the rooms quite recently - in 2013, so all current guests can enjoy the impeccable interior. The hotel is represented by three buildings: the main and 2 additional. Most of the rooms (78) are located in the main building.

Location

Sunrise 4 * (Greece, Rhodes) is located in the Lindos region, namely in the small resort village of Lardos. The airport is 55 km, so the travel time will be short. The hotel is located on the first coastline, so all vacationers can enjoy the sound of the surf and meet the sunrises on the coast.

So, all tourists going to the sea, of course, do not plan to spend all their free time in the room. But if we are talking about beach-type hotels, which is Sunrise 4 * (Rhodes, Lindos), the rooms should be really comfortable, beautiful and spacious. This is not the case when vacationers will spend only night in the room. So what can this option offer us?

Room service

Rooms are cleaned daily, towels are also changed every day, bed linen - every 3 days. Vacationers note that the cleaning is thorough: the staff makes the beds, vacuums and wipes the floor, cleans the plumbing, and wipes the furniture from accumulated dust.

Service

Hotel Sunrise Hotel Rhodes 4 * (Greece, Lindos, Rhodes) offers good service, however, for an additional fee. All vacationers can use high-speed wireless Internet. 1 hour of use will cost tourists 5 euros, 6 hours - 15 euros, 12 hours - 25 euros. The service is not the most budgetary, but also the highest Internet speed. You can pay for the Internet and get a password for it upon check-in at the reception.

A safe and minibar are available at an extra charge. The reception is open around the clock, for all questions of interest you can always contact the courteous and polite hotel staff. Here you can get acquainted with the excursion program of the resort, order an airport shuttle for an additional fee.

Hotel infrastructure

The territory of the hotel Sunrise 4 * (Rhodes) is not so big, but quite well-maintained. Quite often, while relaxing in Rhodes, tourists rent a car - a great opportunity to visit all the legendary and most beautiful places of the resort. So all the hotel guests will be able to use the guarded parking completely free.





Moreover, there is a mini market on the territory where you can buy soft drinks, ice cream, little things you need on vacation, as well as a souvenir shop with the most memorable and valuable souvenirs of the island. Quite often, vacationers spend their evenings in a common TV room, turning it into a full-fledged cinema.

Nutrition

Hotel Sunrise 4 * (Rhodes) offers all-inclusive meals. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served in the central restaurant, and you can have a bite to eat and cool at the poolside bar.

Food is not the most varied, you should not expect Turkish abundance, but everything is very tasty. The quality of the products is on top, which is typical for European resorts. Tourists note that even in season the restaurant never has a line for meat dishes, the assortment is constantly updated, there is no panic and stampede. For breakfast, they give delicious homemade pancakes, pancakes with sour cream and jam. For lunch and dinner, beef, lamb, veal, enough vegetables and fresh fruit. The food is so high quality that there is no need to visit local restaurants. Of course, several times it is worth tasting Mediterranean dishes, going out for a walk around the evening resort. Vacationers enthusiastically describe the restaurant's pastries, and especially delicious pizza. If desired, cooks will prepare pizza with the toppings that you choose yourself.





Baby food is organized for the youngest tourists: here you will always find porridge, diet meat, vegetables and fruits. The restaurant has special high chairs for children. The only thing is that there are not so many of them, therefore, with an abundance of vacationers with children, you will have to take a stool in advance or do it yourself.

Alcoholic beverages are presented in sufficient variety: delicious beer, aromatic wine, spirits do not cause any complaints among tourists at the Sunrise 4 * hotel (Rhodes).

Entertainment on site

All active tourists who are interested in sports can visit the tennis court, play beach volleyball, table tennis, water polo, do aqua aerobics and fitness for free. For an additional fee, the hotel Sunrise 4 * (Rhodes) offers to rent water activities, offered in sufficient abundance and variety. Everyone can visit the hotel's outdoor pool with fresh water, sun loungers and umbrellas are also free of charge.

On the territory there is entertainment for the youngest tourists: an excellent playground, a children's pool, which is why tourists with young children so often relax here.

Beach

If you want to fully enjoy a quality and impeccable beach holiday, pay attention to the Sunrise 4 * hotel (Rhodes island). Lindos, Kiotari - the best regions for beach and sea holidays. It is here that the most beautiful and clean sandy and pebble beaches, crystal clear sea water and stunning nature.





The beach is municipal, the rental of sunbeds and umbrellas is at an additional cost (5 euros). At the reception you can take beach towels, which are required to be returned. The sea is excellent: transparent and warm with a gentle approach and a sandy bottom. The Mediterranean Sea is not in vain considered one of the best for a beach holiday and relaxation with young children.

To summarize

If you are looking forward to an excellent beach holiday and are looking for a hotel with developed infrastructure and amazing living conditions, pay attention to Sunrise 4 * (Rhodes). The hotel is fully consistent with its description, managed to earn a good rating on the most popular tourist portals. That is why it deserves special attention.
