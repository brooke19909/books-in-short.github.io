"Gold ring of Russia. Sights of Tutaev

In the Yaroslavl region, on both banks of the Volga River, the city of Tutaev is located. More than 700 years have passed since its inception, and during this period the city managed to become one of the most interesting places in Russia. After all, the sights of Tutaev are so unusual that they attract the eyes of many tourists.



History of the city

Initially, the city was called Romanov-Borisoglebsky, and only after the revolution did it get its current name. As for his first name, it came from the founder of the city, Prince Roman Vladimirovich, who decided to build his fortress on one of the banks of the Volga. The construction took three years, and in 1283 the construction of Romanov was completed. On the opposite side of the river was located Borisoglebskaya Sloboda, which for a long time was considered a separate province. And only after 500 years, namely in 1822, they became one. And in 1918 the city was renamed in honor of the deceased Red Armyman I.P. Tutaev. During its existence, the city managed to incorporate many amazing traditions. Museums and temples of Tutaev are so interesting and bewitching that they attract visitors from different parts of the country. In addition, the city has a large number of parks, old streets and friendly people.

Cathedrals and temples of Tutaev

On the territory of the city there are a large number of Orthodox churches built in various time periods. Their diversity and beauty will not leave anyone indifferent, which once again proves that the sights of Tutaev may interest more than one category of tourists.



  • Holy Cross Cathedral. The construction of this temple began from the time of Ivan the Terrible and lasted almost a century. The cathedral is a delightful architectural structure, the walls of which are decorated with the faces of the princes Roman, Gleb and Boris.
  • Resurrection Cathedral. Tutaev should be grateful for this popularity among Orthodox pilgrims for this particular building, since in its size and design it has the right to compete with many cathedrals in Moscow and Yaroslavl. It was erected in 1678 as one of the best structures of the Orthodox Church, to this day has retained all its grandeur and pristine beauty.
  • Church of the Intercession. It is one of the oldest buildings in the city. It used to be a man‚Äôs monastery and only in 1771 received church status. One of its features is the presence of the icon of the Virgin under the name "Addition of the mind."

Parks and squares

The tranquility that prevails in parks and squares will help to relax well after a long walk around the city. What other attractions of Tutaev can guests see? "Park of the Soviet period" will take you back to the past, in the days of communism and order. The territory of the park is decorated with sculptures of great leaders, pioneers and symbols of the past tense, all this will inspire a sense of a little nostalgia during a walk. Another interesting place is the museum dedicated to retro cars.

"City Park" is located in the heart of the city and for many years pleases its visitors with the sculpture "Girl with a Scarf". There are several more sculptures here, but it is the figure of the girl that always attracts the eyes of passers-by.



Finally

Sights of Tutaev of the Yaroslavl region will always attract more and more tourists. In addition to their uniqueness, they also bring joy to their visitors. And the kindness and responsiveness of the inhabitants of the city further enchant and complement the impression of what they saw.
