Where to relax in May abroad?

May is a great time to relax. At this time, the choice of tourist destinations increases markedly, but at the same time, the cost of trips, flights is still low. The resorts of Turkey, Thailand, Vietnam, Bulgaria, Montenegro, Israel, Greece and many other countries are ready to meet tourists with warm sun and beautiful weather. Where to relax in May, which direction is better to choose, we will consider in this article.

Thailand

In late April, the main resorts in Thailand (Phuket and Pattaya) officially begin the low season. And this means that tourist trips to these destinations are reduced in price. But this does not mean that you should not go on vacation to Thailand in May. Just the opposite. The weather at this time is comfortable for relaxation, there are rains, but most often at night. The sea is so warm that you do not want to leave it at all. At the same time, there are very few people on the beaches; prices in coastal cafes and restaurants are noticeably lower than in high season. A great option where you can relax inexpensively in May.

But if you still have doubts and are afraid of the possibility of rain, then you should choose the island of Koh Samui for relaxation. The weather here is excellent and the chance of rain is almost zero.

Another option where you can relax in May, especially for those who go on vacation with their children or simply do not like to bother about finding food. Most Turkish hotels operate on the All Inclusive system. So, the cost of the permit will include all the main costs: flight, accommodation, meals, insurance. Turkey is a place where you can inexpensively relax in May abroad.

True, if you really want to swim in the sea, it is better to choose the second half of May. At the beginning of the month, the water in the Mediterranean still does not have time to warm up to a comfortable bathing temperature. Vietnam

Where to relax in Vietnam in May? It is better to choose the resort town of Nha Trang as a place to relax. Rains in Nha Trang in May are very rare, but the sea temperature reaches + 29 ¬∞ . You could even say that this is the best place where you can relax at sea in May in Vietnam.

For those who like to relax more extreme, Fantiet resort is perfect. In May, there are big waves at Phan Thiet, which attracts fans of surfing and windsurfing.

Spain

Spain is especially popular in May. Despite the fact that the air temperature barely exceeds twenty degrees, many tourists get a beautiful uniform tan. However, swimming in the sea, especially in the first half of the month, is unlikely to succeed, since the water has not yet warmed up to a comfortable temperature.

Spain attracts many tourists not only with a beach holiday, but also with a large selection of attractions and excursions. And at the beginning of May there are several holidays in the country: May 2 - City Day in Madrid, May 3 - "Dance of the Peasants" in Santa Cruz de Tenerife, and from May 7 the Big Festival of the Middle Ages begins in Ibiza.

Israel

Another option where you can relax in May is Israel. Resorts of the Mediterranean and the Dead Seas will welcome travelers. And not only those who dream of a beach holiday, but also those who came for an excursion holiday.

In places, the temperature rises to 30 ¬∞ C, but due to cool nights and a refreshing sea breeze it is easy to breathe. Perhaps one of the best places where you can relax at sea abroad in May is very good.

In May, Israel celebrates a holiday - Independence Day. On this day, festive fireworks, a demonstration of military equipment, parades, and concert venues are held throughout Israel. National flags are set everywhere on this day. The holiday is very spectacular and massive.

Greece

May is the beginning of the tourist season in Greece. Many beaches are already open for swimming, and the sea temperature is quite comfortable for swimming. But besides this, Greece is also a country with a rich history and culture. May 18 is the day when all museums are open to all comers.

In early May, the Greeks celebrate workers' Day on a grand scale, and in some cities the Flower Festival is held, which is dedicated to Demeter.

Thus, a vacation in Greece in May will be full of entertainment, but you will not have to refuse a beach vacation.

Montenegro

Located along the coast of the Adriatic Sea, the beautiful country of Montenegro is famous for its resorts. In May in Montenegro, everything is ready for the opening of the holiday season. Cafes and restaurants, clubs are opening. The hotels are ready to welcome guests again. But there are still few tourists at this time, so you can save a lot on your trip.

The average temperature on the Adriatic coast reaches an average of 21 ¬∞ C-25 ¬∞ C, but slight precipitation is possible at the very beginning of the month. Bulgaria

The climate in Bulgaria is very similar to Montenegrin. In May, the weather will be comfortable for visiting excursions and attractions, but a full-fledged beach holiday, most likely, will not work. If the air temperature in places and warms up to 21 ¬∞ C, then the sea is still quite cool. And the nights are still cold enough.

Bulgaria is one of the great options where it is possible to have a cheap vacation in May abroad without any problems.
