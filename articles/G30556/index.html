Pharmaceutical refrigerator "Pozis": instructions, reviews, manufacturer

Pharmaceuticals, hospitals, laboratories need specialized equipment for storing drugs, blood sampling, etc. More stringent requirements are imposed on them, since the slightest deviation from the norm can change the final result. The Pozis refrigerator is recognized and popular among professionals, as it combines quality, reliability, ease of operation and maintenance.

The company's activities are divided into two segments: the first provides customers with high-quality household equipment, the second - reliable refrigeration equipment to ensure the full operation of pharmaceutical enterprises, laboratories, hospitals, etc.

Features of pharmaceutical equipment

According to the Order of the Ministry of Health No. 771 of 10/29/2015, all enterprises working with drugs are required to use special pharmaceutical equipment for the correct storage of drugs. In case of non-compliance with the conditions, the supervisory authorities issue instructions according to which the equipment of inadequate quality must be replaced. Neglect leads to a loss of license.



Storage conditions for drugs are different than the usual food. Medicines react more sensitively to fluctuations in humidity and temperature, negatively tolerate vibration. The well-designed design of specialized refrigerators provides decent protection for samples and medicines. The most popular pharmaceutical equipment on the Russian market is Pozis. Such a demand is due to impeccable quality, attractive price and a full range of certificates that can transfer any, even the most stringent, test without loss.
