Hotel Location

So, the Guesthouse International Hotel 4 *, as we have already said, is located in China, in the city of Sanya, it is located on Hainan Island.

China is a state in East Asia. It is the largest country in the world in terms of population, moreover, it occupies the 3rd place in the territory. China is a great state, it has the status of a superpower in economic terms, it has nuclear weapons and a huge army. Now it has important economic and political importance in the world, absolutely everything is collected and produced here, from small toys to expensive phones, computers and watches.

The island, or in another way the Hainan province, is located in the south of the country, next to the South China Sea. The climate in the province is subequatorial. The main driving force is tropical monsoons, which completely regulate the weather on the island. The temperature here is almost always the same: in winter, the thermometer bars do not rise above +25 degrees, and in the summer they do not fall below +29 degrees. The dry season lasts from 2 to 6 months, it is the period from November-December to March-April. The peak of rains here falls in October, sometimes in June.

Sanya is the southernmost city of the province and country. The main sea resort of China. It is worth noting that there are many beaches and interesting places, by the way, in the town, world events are often held, for example, the Miss World contest.

What types of rooms are available in the hotel?

Let's talk about the appearance of the Guesthouse International Hotel 4 * and its rooms. The hotel is a complex of buildings built in the Chinese style. The hotel is located on a hill, surrounded by many trees, while the beach is just a few minutes walk, because it is located on the first coastline. It offers customers:

  1. Standard double room.
    A small room decorated in warm brown color. It has a large double bed with luxury linen, a white wood table, a sofa. The bathroom has a shower and a large mirror. Windows overlook the picturesque garden. It is especially beautiful in the room in the evening, when it gets dark outside, and the room is lit by the dim light of the lamps.
  2. Deluxe room type.
    Its balcony offers stunning views. This room has two single beds, a dark wood table and a flat-screen TV.
  3. Suite Stylish room decorated in tropical style. It has a full living room with a large sofa, a bedroom with a balcony and beautiful views of the pool, a bathroom with a TV and a large sink.
