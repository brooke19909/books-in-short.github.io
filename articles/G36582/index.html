Skiathos island (Greece): reviews of tourists. Sights of Skiathos

As the long-awaited vacation approaches, the question arises: where is it better to relax? Some prefer rural open spaces and fresh air, others - motels, boarding houses and resorts within our country, and someone is preparing for a trip abroad. Many travelers are attracted to Greece. Skiathos Island is one of the most famous tourist areas of this country. From the article you will learn the history of its occurrence, the main attractions and all types of services provided.



A bit of history

Skiathos Island (Greece) is one of the 4 inhabited islands of the Northern Sporades archipelago. The name of the resort comes from the words skia and athos, which means "the shadow of Athos." According to historical data, the mention of this heavenly place dates back to 478 BC. e. Many trials fell to his lot: the Greco-Persian war, the Macedonian conquest, the occupation by the Venetians, Romans and Turks. And only in 1823 he was able to gain the long-awaited independence. Long enough time about. Skiathos (Greece) was mainly visited only by millionaires. However, the creation of an international airport has contributed to the influx of holidaymakers who cannot boast of having their own yachts.



Favorite places to visit

The capital of. Skiathos (Greece) bears the same name as the island. This port city is the most visited tourist destination, as there are two historical monuments in its vicinity. One of them is the famous Burtzi fortress. It was erected during the period of capture by the Venetians. Currently, the local library, museum and summer theater are located on its territory. Those who wish can visit exhibitions, music concerts and stage productions. The second historical monument is the ruins of the Castro fortress, which was inhabited during the pirate raids. Today, there are only 2 churches.

For example, it is worth noting that the capital is famous for a large number of temples and churches of the old construction. The most famous of them are the monasteries of Our Lady and the Evangelist, which were erected in the XVII and XVIII century, respectively. However, this is far from all the sights of the capital of. Skiathos (Greece). Reviews of many tourists are full of unforgettable impressions from visiting other interesting places.

Main attraction

What else is worth a look? O. Skiathos (Greece) is the birthplace of the famous Greek writer Alexander Papadyamantis. Here he lived, worked and died. After the death of the writer, a museum was opened in the walls of his house, in which to this day his works and personal belongings are kept. This building is located on the main street of the capital, which is named after him.



Greece, Skiathos. Hotels

The resort has many hotels open for tourists. Hotels offer various levels of service. I would like to dwell on some of them. So what does the island of Skiathos (Greece) offer ? Guest reviews are filled with positive impressions of visiting Cape Pounta Villas. Most often, vacationers like to stay here, as the building has a convenient location. Only 3 km from the airport and 2.5 km to the capital of the island. There are two types of villas to choose from: VILLA MARE and VILLA LAGO. Each of them is a two-story building. The first tier is equipped with a living room with a real fireplace, a kitchen with everything you need, a dining room, a closet and an amazing terrace with beautiful views and access to a private pool. On the second floor there are bedrooms, a bathroom and a laundry room. In addition to the pool, the villa has a small garden, an equipped dining area and a barbecue area. For a comfortable stay, the house is equipped with central heating, air conditioning, TV, video player, toiletries, dishwasher and washing machine, dryer. For your personal use, slippers, a bathrobe, ironing board, iron and hairdryer are provided. In addition, in the houses and on-site Wi-Fi. The villas also have access to the sandy municipal beaches, where for a small fee you can use umbrellas and sun loungers. For families with children, paid babysitting services are offered.



Hotel Kassandra Bay Resort

This 5-star hotel is located in the Royal Bay, 3 km from the capital and 4 km from the airport. This place is ideal for romantic getaways and family trips. The hotel has beautiful gardens. In addition, there is direct access to the cleanest, according to the EU, Vasilias beach. Just take into account the moment that this section of the coast is privately owned. The hotel was completely renovated in 2013 and has over 100 stunning rooms and 7 suites. Each room is equipped with telephone, bath (shower), satellite TV, heating, refrigerator, air conditioning, as well as a balcony or terrace with amazing views. For personal purposes, vacationers are available: hairdryer, slippers and towels. In addition, the hotel has its own marina for sea taxis, 2 bars and 3 restaurants. In terms of service, the hotel has private parking, laundry, a conference room that can accommodate 300 people, free Wi-Fi. For the entertainment of customers there is a gym, swimming pools (including children's), filled with sea water, table tennis and spa centers. Also created all the amenities for families with children: playgrounds, chairs in restaurants, paid babysitting services. The private sandy beach is equipped with umbrellas and sun loungers, which can be used for a nominal fee.
