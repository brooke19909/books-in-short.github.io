Pegasos Beach 4 * (Greece / Rhodes island) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

The magnificent Greek island of Rhodes, washed by the waters of two warm seas (Mediterranean and Aegean), has long been chosen by tourists from around the world. This is not at all surprising, because this place can boast of magnificent beaches, a wide hotel base, the presence of many historical and architectural monuments and a number of entertainments for every taste, as well as magnificent nature.

If you decide to choose the island of Rhodes (Greece) for your summer vacation , Pegasos Beach 4 * will be an excellent option for vacation accommodation. We offer you to get to know this hotel more closely, and also to find out whether Russian tourists liked the stay here.

The capital of this wonderful island - the city of Rhodes - is only 13 kilometers from the hotel, so you will get to all its entertainment in just a few minutes. Rhodes Pegasos Beach 4 *: photo and description

"Pegasos Beach" is a fairly large hotel complex, built in a Mediterranean style. It was built in 1985. In subsequent years, restoration and renovation works were regularly carried out here, the last of which was completed in 2012. Therefore, today, despite its age, this hotel is a modern and comfortable place, offering its guests a great vacation. It will appeal to all categories of tourists: families with children of any age, and older people, and fun youth. Comfortable rooms, excellent service, wonderful climate, clear azure sea and wide beach will not leave anyone indifferent.

In total, the hotel has 272 rooms located in several buildings. There are both rooms offering economy-class accommodation, and luxurious, respectively, expensive suites. In addition, the hotel complex has a developed infrastructure (bars, restaurants, swimming pools, sports grounds, etc.), designed to make the rest of the guests as comfortable, diverse and interesting as possible.

Hotel Policies

Like other hotels, the Pegasos Beach 4 * hotel has a checkout time indicated by internal rules that determines the time of arrival and departure of guests. In accordance with it, the arrival of arrivals is carried out after 14:00. However, keep in mind that the administration of the hotel complex is always trying to meet the needs of tourists, and even in case of their early arrival, does everything possible so that they can be accommodated in rooms immediately. However, it is worth paying attention that at the height of the season, when the hotel is almost full, this is impossible. Therefore, you will be asked to wait until the previous tenants vacate the occupied room, and the staff will prepare it properly for check-in.

At this time, you can leave your things in the luggage room and visit a restaurant, bar, have a nice time by the pool, explore the hotel and the surrounding area, or go straight to the beach. On the day of departure, the occupied room should be vacated before noon. When you hand over the keys to the reception, you must also pay for the entire period of your stay at the Pegasos Beach 4 * hotel, as well as for using additional services. Along with cash, plastic cards are also accepted for payment here.

Sea and beach

Despite the fact that Pegasos Beach 4 * is located on the beach, unfortunately, it does not have its own beach. The nearest is in municipal ownership. It is equipped with everything necessary for an excellent pastime: sunbeds, umbrellas, equipment for entertainment and sports on the water. Use of beach equipment is subject to charge. The beach itself is sandy and clean. The entrance to the water is gentle, without stones.





Infrastructure

The hotel complex Pegasos Beach 4 * has everything you need for an excellent holiday for tourists. So, on its territory there is a large outdoor pool with a spacious sun terrace and bars with a wide selection of drinks. People who prefer outdoor activities can play basketball, tennis, ping pong, billiards or darts. Animators work daily at the hotel, and lively shows are held in the evenings.

The hotel has a shop, souvenir shop, beauty salon, laundry. By contacting the reception, you can rent a car, book an excursion in the direction you are interested in, or exchange currency.

For the youngest guests, a children's pool is equipped, as well as a playground. There is also a mini club and a mini disco in the evenings.
