Venus Beldibi Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Beldibi): reviews and photos of tourists from Russia

When planning a vacation on the Mediterranean coast, many tourists wish to combine sightseeing tourism and beach holidays.

The hot sun, clear waters and fresh air will present many resorts in Turkey, one of which is Beldibi. It is located west of Antalya and includes only resort hotels and some natural and historical attractions. A variety of housing options are presented as budget rooms and fashionable apartments. Here you can relax from the hefty shopping centers, city bustle and fast pace of life.

The three-star Venus Beldibi Hotel offers its guests comfortable rooms, a high level of service and guarantees a pleasant pastime for every guest, regardless of age.

The number of rooms includes 113 rooms of the Standard category. Their area reaches 18 square meters, and they differ only in a variety of views. The floors are covered with ceramic tiles. The walls are the work of painters. The order is brought daily, and bed linen is changed three times a week. Neat maids, as noted by tourists, carry out their work conscientiously and efficiently. A wake-up service is available for late wake lovers. Room Service is missing.

Things to do at the resort

You can entertain yourself at this resort with sightseeing. Cute coastal parks full of exotic plants and amazing fragrant flowers attract tourists who want to enjoy nature.
