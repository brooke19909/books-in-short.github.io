Nokia 6131: specifications and reviews

The Nokia 6131 clamshell is a rather elegant phone that has, one might say, an original disclosure mechanism. To open the device, you just need to click on one of the buttons. The device has a huge number of different functions. You can start the list with the camera, continue with communication modules (such as infrared and Bluetooth) and end with support for microSD memory cards.

Introduction





Two color screens are built into the device at once. One is internal, the second, respectively, is external. The first has a good color rendering, the number of colors is 16 million. Nevertheless, certain remarks are made with respect to this component. For example, if the screen is dirty, it will be difficult to clean it. One of the most important shortcomings, by the way, is the weak battery for the Nokia 6131 phone.

Disclosure button

This element is perhaps the most interesting in the design of model 6131. ‚Äã‚ÄãWe can say that this is a kind of highlight of the device. Screen

As we said earlier, the main drawback of the device is the battery for the Nokia 6131 Silver phone. And then what is in the list of advantages of the device? One of the points may well be called a phone display. The Finns in this regard remained true to themselves. The basis of the screen is a TFT-type matrix. It supports 16 million colors. The screen resolution is modest, compared with the devices of the present - only 320 by 240 pixels. But we are talking specifically about the phone, not the smartphone, so everything is in order.

A feature of the display of the 6131 is the lack of a protective glass. The user with his gaze instantly rests against an ordinary liquid crystal matrix. A similar method is used in the manufacture of laptops. This feature has some practical help. More specifically, the lack of a protective glass allows you to fully use the functionality of the screen and convey the saturation of the color gamut.
