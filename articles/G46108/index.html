Pskov is a large city located on the Great River. This is one of the oldest settlements, which is mentioned in the Laurentian Chronicle. Until the 18th century, Pskov was the largest trading and defensive center not only in Russia, but also in Europe. A rich cultural heritage has survived to this day. Naturally, thousands of domestic and foreign tourists seek to visit this city. Hotels in the center of Pskov will provide them with a warm welcome.
