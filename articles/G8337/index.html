What about the phones?

Cell phones have entered our life extremely tightly and harmoniously; moreover, they have become virtually an integral part of it, therefore, to attract attention to them, companies have to show unthinkable ingenuity. One of the most successful advertising tricks back in the days of landline phones was the move "the largest phone in the world." But let's decide on what characteristics we will choose the winner, since everything in this world is relative.

Largest to use

If the main condition is the ability to use a mobile phone in everyday life, then the title "the world's largest phone" gets Huawei Ascend Mate. Honestly, this is a smartphone that looks more like a tablet. Its diagonal is 6.1 inches; the phones have no larger screen yet. However, it is quite convenient to use it, if you neglect the appearance of a person who almost brings a laptop to his ear.
