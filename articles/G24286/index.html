Hotel Park Calitto 3 * Italy, about. Ischia: review, features and reviews

Italy is a country that attracts a large number of tourists from different parts of the world with its picturesque beauties and extraordinary nature. There are many attractions that will certainly become interesting to any traveler.

Ischia Island is a place that is beloved by guests from abroad, because it has beautiful weather all year round, and in the summer you can swim in the waters of the Gulf of Naples, having previously provided for yourself a place near the coast - in a hotel or hotel. One of the most popular local hotels is Park Hotel Calitto (Forio / Panza) 3 *. Relaxing here, tourists can enjoy not only the beauty of the vineyards and the sea, but also the wonderful natural conditions: hot, but not stuffy days, as well as sunlight.

Location

Park Hotel Calitto (Ischia) is located in Italy. From it to the sea coast is within easy reach - it is located practically by the waters of the Gulf of Naples, among beautiful vineyards and flowering gardens. The lively center of Pana is a little less than a kilometer from here, and 56 km to the most important transport hub - Naples International Airport. Just five kilometers from the hotel there is a large international port - in the city of Forio - from which you can also begin your journey through Italian lands.



general information

Park Hotel Calitto (Forio / Panza) 3 * was built a long time ago, in 1981. Since then, repair work has been carried out on its territory several times, and an extreme overhaul of its buildings and territory was completed in 2011. Since then, the hotel has finally acquired a modern look, it has improved some of the accommodation systems.

Rooms

The total number of apartments in Hotel Park Calitto is small - there are only 34 of them, but each is designed according to a design project, it offers beautiful and convenient conditions for tourists. The number of rooms is located in four comfortable buildings.

Hotel rooms, depending on the level of comfort, are divided into two categories: standard and comfort. Each of them has modern comfortable furniture, as well as an individual bathroom with shower and sink. Throughout the Park Hotel Calitto (Forio / Panza) 3 *, Ischia has a modern climate system that allows tourists to independently adjust the temperature in the room based on individual preferences.



Standard category

Apartments of the standard category, which are available at the hotel, are divided into two groups: single and double, depending on the settlement of how many guests they are designed for. Their area is from 11 to 17 square meters. m. Each room of this type has access to a personal furnished balcony with a magnificent view of the flowering garden.

As for the furniture equipment, the standard rooms have the necessary number of beds in the form of beds with orthopedic mattresses, as well as a wardrobe for storing clothes and a dressing table with a chair and a mirror.

Nutrition

Hotel Park Calitto guests have the opportunity to eat right on site, as there is a small restaurant where you can taste deliciously prepared dishes of Italian, Mediterranean and European cuisine. There is also a separately developed diet menu, which is very popular among vacationers. A daily all-inclusive buffet is served.

Tourists can also check out the local snack bar or bar located by the outdoor pool. Here you can order light snacks or salads, as well as drink several types of soft drinks and bright cocktails. Everyone has the opportunity to sit here for a cup of coffee or tea.



Pool

The hotel has an outdoor sun terrace, inside of which there is a small-sized pool filled with crystal clear water. A number of comfortable sun loungers are installed on the terrace, where guests can relax after water procedures under beach umbrellas.

One of the buildings has an indoor pool, which becomes very popular in the cooler season. It has a modern water heating system, which allows you to maintain its optimum temperature, regardless of external factors.



Sports and SPA

Guests who prefer to monitor their own figure and maintain their health are normal, can use the opportunity to visit the gym for free - it is located in the Hotel Park Calitto (Forio / Panza) 3 * building. Simulators are installed here, with the help of which you can work out different muscle groups qualitatively.

Separately, there is a small spa area equipped with a swimming pool, massage room and solarium. Visitors can also relax in the large hot tub.

Beach

Literally 200 meters from the hotel is a large and clean municipal beach, where you can relax in the sun. It is perfectly landscaped for a comfortable stay: here you can use public locker rooms, showers, and also toilets. You can rent air mattresses, sun loungers and towels.

As for entertainment, there are a lot of them. Tourists, staying on the beach, can take a hovercraft or water ski, learn surfing skills under the guidance of an instructor or go on a small cruise on a rented yacht or boat.



Services

For each guest of Hotel Park Calitto, the hotel has a range of services, any of which they can use at any time. In particular, to maintain the cleanliness of the rooms, a room service is working here: professional and very polite maids regularly clean the apartments.

At your service there is a reception desk - reception, behind which you can always find a Russian-speaking administrator. It is here that they can help with the solution of many controversial situations, advise on living conditions and additional features. Here you can also hand over your large luggage to a special left-luggage office, but this type of service is provided for a fee.

Hotel guests have the opportunity to use a paid guarded parking lot, which is located on the territory of the Park Hotel Calitto (S Forio) 3 *, and in their free time, all visitors can stroll through the local flowering garden.
