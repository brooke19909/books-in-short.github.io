Hotel Policies

So, if your room is not yet ready for check-in, you can leave things in the storage room and dine in the restaurant, take a swim in the pool, take a walk around the hotel or go straight to the sea on the beach.

As for the departure from Vera Stone Palace 5 *, you must vacate the occupied room before noon. Upon delivery of the keys, it is also necessary to pay the cost of living.
