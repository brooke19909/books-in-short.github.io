Mobile WiFi router "Beeline". Scope of delivery, purpose, characteristics and setting procedure

The Beeline WiFi router considered within the framework of this material is intended for the implementation of mobile computing networks. This review will be fully devoted to its technical specifications and specifications. An algorithm for setting up this solution will also be provided.

Equipment

Beeline WiFi router delivery list includes the following components:

  1. Mobile router
  2. Warranty card to him.
  3. Accumulator battery.
  4. User's manual.
  5. Power Supply.
  6. Interface cord for connecting to a PC. It is also used when charging the battery.

The only component that is missing in the previous list is a SIM card. But this approach is justified. A potential subscriber can choose the necessary number and tariff plan for himself. But the starter pack must be purchased separately and at an additional cost.

Device design

This router comes in a white case. The operator logo is applied on the top cover. Also next to it is a series of LED indicators. Two buttons are grouped on one of the faces of the device. One of them turns on the device, and the second turns on the WPS mode. There is also a special tray for installing a SIM card. On the bottom cover of the case is the network name and password for access to it by default.

Specifications

This Beeline WiFi router boasts the following technical specifications:

  1. Support for 2G / 3G / 4G cellular networks. The highest data transfer rate can theoretically reach 150 Mbps.
  2. The capacity of the complete battery is 2100 mAh, and one of its charge is enough for 6 hours of battery life.
  3. The Wi-Fi transmitter of this portable solution operates in combined mode and supports almost all 802.11 a / b / g / n standards. The highest speed of information exchange is 150 Mbps.

Setting procedure

A fairly simple sequence of actions must be performed to start operating such an access point. It consists of the following stages:

  1. We remove the device from the cardboard packaging.
  2. After this, you need to get a special tray, put a SIM card in it and put it back.
  3. Next, press the power button and wait for the router to finish loading.
  4. After that, we activate WPS technology simultaneously on the router and the connected device. After that, the connection will be established automatically without user intervention.
  5. Then you can launch the browser and begin to plow the expanses of the "global web."

This is a brief algorithm of how to configure a Beeline WiFi router of this model.

Owner reviews. Current price at the moment

Such a WiFi router for Beeline can be purchased at any branded outlet of this mobile operator. Moreover, its recommended cost is 3289 rubles. Of course, you can find more affordable router models. But with such a purchase, along with the starter pack, the subscriber receives a promotional 200 GB for a period of 14 days.

The advantages of such a network access point, the owners include reliability, high speed, affordable cost and simple setup procedure. The disadvantages of the solution include the white case and strict binding to the equipment of this operator. In the first case, reviews indicate its high grades, and in the second - the impossibility of using other companies in cellular networks.

Finally

The considered Beeline WiFi router is excellent for creating portable networks with access to the global web. It has typical parameters and low cost. Its only significant minus is the link to the equipment of the Beeline company. But there is no escape from him. If you purchase an additional starter pack immediately, you can get 200 GB for 14 days. This is a really nice bonus for the new owner of such a router.
