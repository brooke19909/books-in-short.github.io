The best hotels in Sochi: photos and reviews of tourists

“Probably every person has visited Sochi at least once in his life.” Who does not remember this phrase of the protagonist of the film “Moscow Does Not Believe in Tears”. But there are also people who have not yet been there. Of course, there are many who wish to come to this magical southern city. And Sochi hotels will help you create comfortable conditions for a good rest.

Hotel "Polyana"

The location of the five-star Hotel Polyana, surrounded by the majestic snow-capped peaks of the Caucasus Mountains, is a national park in the village of Krasnaya Polyana. Within walking distance from the hotel there is a water park, ice rink, cinema, many shops and restaurants. Several ski lifts are located within walking distance, at a distance of 300 meters from the "Glade" flows the picturesque river Achips. The hotel is a ski resort, the Black Sea coast is located at a distance of 53 kilometers from it. Four restaurants located on its territory present Russian and European cuisines. The rooms and restaurants offer stunning panoramic mountain views. There are two swimming pools (indoor and outdoor), ski equipment rental is available, lessons at a ski school can be borrowed for an extra fee, and a ski storage is provided. There is a currency exchange office. Tourists with children can take advantage of babysitting services. Parking is free. To accommodate tourists, the Polyana Hotel in Sochi offers accommodation in rooms of several categories:

  • deluxe double with one bed - 12 700 rubles per day, if one person is occupied - 9900 rubles;
  • deluxe double with two beds - 12 900 rubles per day, if one person is occupied - 9900 rubles;
  • double deluxe with a balcony - 14,200 rubles per day, for one person, the price is 12,900 rubles.

Room rate includes breakfast, free Wi-Fi access.

Hotel Globus

Hotel "Globus" in Sochi is located on the Black Sea in the resort village of Loo. The city's sand and pebble beach is a two-minute walk from the hotel. The microdistrict in which the hotel is located is called "Mountain Air". The hotel is a small, cozy four-story building. Parking is free for tourists. There is a dining room, a shop, there is an ATM. Of the nearest attractions at a distance of 1.2 kilometers is the Loo Temple. The hotel has a category of 2 stars. Accommodation in a standard double room will cost vacationers 1800 rubles. Free Wi-Fi access is available.

Hotel "Pearl"

Grand Hotel "Pearl" in Sochi has a category of 4 stars, is the largest cultural, exhibition and business center in the south of the Russian Federation. Located in a snow-white 19-story building. On its own pebble beach 50 meters from the hotel can accommodate about 2000 people at a time. On the beach there are comfortable sun loungers, deck chairs, umbrellas, there are showers and changing rooms, toilets. Various water activities are offered. In the medical center of the hotel you will be consulted by qualified specialists, and there is also the opportunity to carry out diagnostics on the latest equipment. The restaurants feature European, Caucasian and national Kuban cuisine. Near the hotel there is a large swimming pool with heated sea water. Parking is free. The hotel has these types of rooms:

  • double - 9900 rubles per day;
  • double premium - 10 700 rubles per day;
  • double deluxe - 15 600 rubles for one day;
  • Junior Suite - 16,700 rubles per day;
  • Luxury - from 17,700 to 20,500 rubles per day.

Breakfast and dinner are included. Free WI-FI.

Imeretinsky Hotel

Olympic Park is a 20-minute walk from the Imeretinsky Hotel in Sochi. The hotel has a star category. The rooms have balconies. The hotel offers spa and wellness center, sauna and Turkish bath, massage services. There are two pools, the beach is on the first line. In the evenings, leisure programs are organized for holidaymakers. The restaurant serves buffet breakfast. There is a free car park. Pets allowed. Guests are accommodated in comfortable rooms of several categories:

  • double standard (pool view) - 11 100 rubles per day, when placing one person the price will be 9000 rubles;
  • double standard with a sea view - 15 000 rubles per day, if one person settles - 10 200 rubles;
  • double standard on the club floor - 18,100 rubles for one day, for one person - 13,700 rubles;
  • Suite - 22 800 rubles for one day;
  • Suite on the club floor - 27,400 rubles per day.

Breakfast is included. Free Wi-Fi access is available.

Hotel Azimut

The three-star Azimut Hotel in Sochi is a large hotel complex that includes twelve 8-story buildings of modern architecture. You just need a few minutes to walk to the aquarium, dolphinarium, Olympic Park. The fenced area is guarded around the clock. There are beautiful walking paths, indoor pools, several restaurants and bars. There is a beach at a distance of 800 meters from the hotel. Accommodation is offered both in budget rooms and in superior rooms. The price of accommodation, if desired, can include breakfast, half board or full board. Car owners are provided with parking, there is free internet.

Sochi Hotels: Troika Hotel

Hotel "Troika" has a category of 3 stars, located in the ski resort village of Krasnaya Polyana. It features a spa and indoor pool. There is a restaurant, ski storage. Ski equipment can be rented. The distance to the ski lifts is 2 kilometers. The following numbers are offered for accommodation:

  • double standard - 3500 rubles per day;
  • Studio number - 4500 rubles;
  • Luxury - from 5000 to 6000 rubles per day.

Breakfast is included in the room rate, there is free Wi-Fi.

Hotel Gorki Plaza

The three-star hotel “Gorki Plaza”, like other hotels in Sochi, has an interesting architectural solution. It is one of the largest in Krasnaya Polyana. The ski slope is just a short walk away. At a distance of one kilometer there are several ski lifts. Some rooms have balconies and panoramic windows offer beautiful views. The cost of a double standard room is 4860 rubles per day, the most expensive is a deluxe suite - 16700 rubles per day. The price includes breakfast or half board. There is free internet.
