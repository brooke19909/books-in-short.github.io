Anapa, Golden Sands. Rest in Anapa

For many years, most lovers of sea holidays believe that Anapa is the best resort on the Black Sea coast. Golden sands of its beaches, mild climate, unique nature combining steppe and mountain landscapes, warm shallow sea and the largest number of sunny days in Russia - you can sunbathe there for almost 300 days a year - all this attracts a large number of tourists to this resort. In addition, modern Anapa is not only a place for children to relax. Now there are many comfortable hotels, a water park, a variety of restaurants, attractions and other entertainments. Holidays in Anapa will appeal to both adults and children, and lovers to lie quietly on the beach, and active youth.



Resort Location

Anapa is one of the cities of the Krasnodar Territory, located on the Black Sea. It is located at the junction of the Greater Caucasus and the Taman Peninsula. Therefore, wooded foothills there are replaced by steppes and flowering valleys of Taman, and all this is interspersed with sea estuaries. A sandy beach with an arc borders the sea and forms a quiet and beautiful bay. These natural features of Anapa provide a mild climate - it is never very hot there. At the same time, this resort is the sunniest place on the coast, and the swimming season here lasts from May to September. Warm shallow sea, golden soft sand make Anapa an ideal place for families with children.



Wellness properties of the resort

  1. Sea air filled with ozone and iodine has long been valued for its healing properties. But its combination with mountain and steppe air is what Anapa is famous for.
  2. Golden sands have a healing effect. And this is not only a warming effect that helps with rheumatism, arthritis and radiculitis. The composition of Anapa sands is unique - in addition to marine minerals, it includes feldspar and quartz.
  3. The resort has many mineral springs with healing water, which will help with diseases of the cardiovascular system and digestive organs.
  4. Many estuaries and salt lakes are rich in silt sulfide mud, which have beneficial effects on the skin.

All these factors are widely used in sanatoriums and boarding houses of the resort. The most famous of them is Golden Sands. It is located on Pioneer Avenue and offers many wellness treatments.



Resort beaches

This is exactly what attracts Anapa vacationers. The golden sands of its beaches have long been famous for their softness and healing properties. This is because the Kuban River flowed into the sea here earlier. She brought such soft white sand, which is constantly enriched with minerals from sea water. The uniqueness of the beaches of Anapa also lies in the fact that they stretch for many kilometers along the sea. More than 40 of them have only sand, and south of the city there are mostly pebble beaches. These places also have their fans, but it is the sand that attracts most vacationers. This is what Anapa has become famous for all of Russia. Its golden sands have unique healing properties. Enriched with particles of shellfish, seashells and algae, they help with radiculitis, skin diseases and strengthen the immune system. They have so glorified this resort that one of the most famous guest houses in the city is called Golden Sands. The amount of sand is also unique - in some places the beach extends from the sea to a width of up to 600 meters. Huge, sometimes ten-meter dunes are also a tourist attraction, which Anapa is famous for.



Hotel Golden Sands

In recent years, many modern comfortable hotels and private guest houses have been built on the coast. The most famous of them are located in villages near the city - Vityazevo and Dzhemete. This hotel is most popular because, in addition to the name reflecting the main attraction of Anapa, it offers vacationers many amenities and entertainment. The modern three-story building has 50 rooms with comfortable furniture, air conditioning and a shower. The cost of living includes three meals a day, the use of the pool, the Internet and a children's playground. The hotel is conveniently located, and the beautiful sandy beach is only 10 minutes away. Close to the market, shops and a modern water park.



Pension "Golden Sands"

Anapa is also rich in many places to relax that offer wellness treatments. In general, you can improve your health at the resort without a sanatorium, just sunbathing and swimming in the sea is enough. In addition to the life-giving air, warm sand and healing sea water, there are many more healing factors. If you want to not only relax, but also get rid of skin, cardiovascular and nervous diseases, strengthen immunity and improve your condition with asthma and diseases of the musculoskeletal system - lie down on the beach, breathe in healing air and plunge into sea water. However, the Golden Sands sanatorium offers its services not only to sick people. Anapa today is rich in many modern attractions. This is a cinema, a restaurant, a swimming pool, and a sauna. The boarding house is located on the second line of Pioneer Avenue, and it is very close to the sandy beach. Therefore, more and more people are choosing the Golden Sands boarding house. Anapa will offer them a lot of entertainment for a pleasant stay.



Where else to relax

Most sanatoriums and hotels are located on Pioneer Avenue. It stretches for more than 10 kilometers along the sea. Two lines of the avenue are occupied by boarding houses, private hotels and camps. You can choose a vacation for every taste. In addition to the Golden Sands boarding house, you can relax in the hotel, which offers all-inclusive meals, for example, in the Riviera or Victoria. If you prefer home cooking, then visit private hotels such as Sea Breeze and Valencia. In the middle of Pioneer Avenue is the village of Dzhemete. The name translates as "golden sand." It is famous for its wide sandy beaches with high fancy dunes. The clean bottom of the sea allows you to enjoy plenty of bathing. The healing air, healing mud and unique vegetation attract many vacationers. Another village, which is very popular and attracts many tourists to the resort of Anapa, is Vityazevo. Golden sands are here. In addition, comfortable hotels, bars and restaurants, swimming pools and attractions await vacationers.

What does Anapa offer to children?

For a long time this resort is famous as the best place to relax with children. Here, even nature provides everything for kids.

  1. Warm shallow sea, which warms up to 26 degrees. Shallow water stretches for many meters, and the convenient location of the beaches in the bay ensures the absence of strong waves.
  2. The soft fine golden sand covering the beach and the bottom of the sea is very popular with children.

In Anapa, everything has a quiet relaxing holiday with children. Many attractions, a water park, boat trips and a dolphinarium - this will help your children not only enjoy sea bathing and playing on the beach, but also have fun and fun. Many choose private hotels, but the Golden Sands boarding house is best for relaxing with offspring.



What else does this resort offer

It offers guests numerous bars and restaurants. The resort has two water parks, a dolphinarium and an amusement park. Horse and boat trips, excursions to the champagne wine factory and the picturesque Sukko Valley are offered. The resort has an archaeological museum that will familiarize you with the history of the ancient Greek colony of Hermonass and the ancient Slavic principality of Tmutarakan. Anapa has many unique natural attractions, for example, hydrogen sulfide mud volcanoes, the Sukko valley with relic plants. Various hotels and pensions offer spa programs, relaxation in the sauna and swimming in the pool.

Holidays in Anapa will give an unforgettable experience and will appeal to many. Picturesque nature, invigorating air, healing golden sand and mild climate make this resort one of the most popular on the Black Sea coast.
