"Nova Park Hotel", Sharjah, United Arab Emirates: description, reviews

For the past several decades, hospitable Sharjah has been welcomed by tourists from all over the world. The city attracts travelers with its rich culture, an amazing combination of antiquity and modernity, year-round sunny weather and wide opportunities for leisure and business meetings. It is considered one of the safest and most accessible cities for tourists in the UAE and proudly bears the title of the country's cultural capital.

About hotel

Nova Park Hotel (Sharjah) is part of the local Arab group of hotels Mubarak Abdul Aziz Al-Hassawi and is ideally located near commercial areas and popular tourist attractions.

From here you can easily get to Dubai or Sharjah International Airport. The road by car will take no more than 20 minutes.





The hotel is located on the second line and is a nine-story building with a restaurant, indoor pool and fitness center. The first guests arrived here in 1992, since then the hotel complex has been updated several times. Last time - in 2008.

The hotel does not have a private beach or outdoor pool, but guests can use the free pool and beach at Golden Beach Motel.

Number of rooms

Nova Park Hotel (Sharjah, UAE) offers single travelers and families with children to relax in 148 luxurious rooms ranging in size from 32 to 39 square meters with a maximum capacity of up to 4 people.

The institution has rooms of four categories, which differ in the number of rooms and the degree of comfort. The hotel building has the ability to connect to wireless Internet.





All rooms are facing the street, and some have a spacious balcony.

Each room has:

  • TV with a variety of international channels, including Russian;
  • safe;
  • telephone;
  • individually controlled air conditioning;
  • mini bar;
  • kettle.

All rooms have a private bathroom with amenities and a hairdryer. Room cleaning is carried out every day.

Nutrition

"Nova Park Hotel" (Sharjah) offers guests to visit the restaurant of international and Arabian cuisine Al Diwan, where meals can be organized both according to the "buffet" system and in the menu format.





A nice addition to the service are themed evenings regularly held by the restaurant staff. The hotel bar serves gourmet drinks, pastries and light snacks.

Beach

Despite the fact that the country has a decent coastline, many hotels in the UAE do not have their own beaches. Nova Park Hotel (Sharjah) is one of them, but its guests can use the services of the private beach of Golden Beach Motel, which is 5-10 minutes away by transport, at no extra charge. Transfer is provided free of charge, but prior registration is required at the reception.

The sandy beach has a length of about 300 meters, the seabed is convenient for entry.

For a comfortable stay on the coast, guests are offered free use of sun umbrellas, sun loungers, mattresses and beach towels.

In addition to active water activities on the beach, tourists can play volleyball.

Additional service

For a variety of leisure activities, Nova Park Hotel (Sharjah) offers its guests free access to the indoor freshwater pool, which is heated in the winter, relax in the jacuzzi or work out in the fitness center to maintain your physical condition.

Business people can make an appointment in the conference room for up to 40 people. Also at their service is a business center of the hotel, here you can use the Internet and fax.

For travelers traveling by car, the hotel offers free parking. For guests who do not have their own means of transportation, the hotel organizes a free shuttle service to Dubai.

There is also a banquet room for up to 200 people, laundry, dry cleaning and medical staff.

We accept not only cash, but also credit cards: Visa, MasterCard, American Express.

Tourists reviews

Going on vacation in the UAE, many travelers choose the Nova Park Hotel (Sharjah), reviews of which over the past 2-3 years are very positive. In their comments, already rested tourists note the successful location of the hotel, reasonable cost of living, well-organized transfer and friendly staff. The hotel staff speaks only English, in Russian they understand only simple requests.





By cons, many hotel guests include battered rooms and monotonous breakfasts. But if you ask questions to tourists who have just returned from a city called Sharjah, the Nova Park Hotel, whose photo can be found in the article, they will describe well. The staff always goes towards the client, fulfills all his requests, including for checking in or changing the room.

Leaving the hotel, tourists take with them the warmest memories and dream of coming back here again.
