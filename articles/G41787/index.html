LCD "Semitsvet" - business-class housing for those who value comfort

Yekaterinburg is a huge metropolis in the Ural region. The whole is surrounded by forests and lakes. Its development does not stand still, construction is proceeding at a rapid pace. In this rather big city, in the Zheleznodorozhny district, on Tekhnicheskaya street, there is an LCD Semitsvet.





A bit about the city

At a distance of more than one and a half thousand kilometers from the capital, Yekaterinburg is located. The city, which is the largest administrative, cultural, scientific and educational center of the Ural region. In terms of the population, Yekaterinburg holds an honorable fourth place in Russia. He was lost among forests, rivers. On the one hand it borders on the Verkh-Isetsky reservoir, on the other - on Lake Shartash.





Location of the residential complex

LCD "Semitsvet" is located in the Zheleznodorozhny district in the Old Sort at the intersection of Technical and Druzhininskaya streets. Nearby is a large transport artery - Bilimbaevskaya street. Getting to the complex is very simple, the developer Astrainveststroy (Yekaterinburg) took care of this. Public transport is located near the complex. For those who move in their own car, it is very convenient to call in from the Technical street. This street also has an additional tram route. Thus, those residents who move to the residential complex "Semitsvet" will not experience problems with transport.

Description of the complex

Residential complex "Semitsvet" consists of two monolithic houses, each of which rises from the earth to twenty-six floors. The total number of apartments in the houses is five hundred and ten. In the complex, the developer has designed almost all types of apartments: from one-room to three-room. They also have double-chamber windows and entrance doors.





The complex is equipped with telephone systems, radio, television, dispatching elevators, intercom, fire alarm.

LCD "Semitsvet" offers its residents not only business-class apartments, but also equipped courtyard. It will house games, sports and utility venues. Even prudently resolved the issue of garbage collection. And in the local area there is a place for storing bulky waste.

Internal infrastructure

Astrainveststroy (Yekaterinburg) designs and implements truly business-class housing. The company has established itself as a responsible and reliable developer. One of the successful projects is LCD "Semitsvet". Ekaterinburg to a large extent benefited from the erected buildings in a bright, eye-catching color scheme.





What does the internal infrastructure of this residential complex include? First of all, it is a closed courtyard, the security of which is carried out by an access control system. For residents, this is of no small importance, since access to the territory of strangers and stray animals is limited.

The installed video surveillance system additionally ensures the safety of residents of the Semitsvet residential complex (Yekaterinburg). Reviews of its residents confirm this. In addition, car access to the courtyard of the complex is also limited. Children can play on the playgrounds without fear that their balls may fall under cars.

The playground is equipped with safe materials. All slides and swings available in it are provided by the KSIL company. This is the only manufacturer of domestic equipment that has a certificate of compliance with the European standard. And all building materials, respectively, are safe, environmentally friendly, without sharp parts that can injure. Slides are made of stainless steel, the mounting points of the elements are equipped with plastic plugs.

External infrastructure

As in all newly built residential complexes, the first floor is designed non-residential. It subsequently houses various facilities that provide residents of the complex with various social services. It will be both shops and pharmacies.

In addition, the location of the residential complex in one of the districts of the city of Yekaterinburg gives its residents the right to use all its infrastructure. Within walking distance are several schools. For those with little children, there is a kindergarten. Hypermarkets and convenience stores in Zheleznodorozhny, as well as in any other district of the city, cannot be counted. This moment is positively noted by residents.





Across the street from the residential complex is located on the territory of more than twenty hectares, the Seven Klyuchi Park. Taking unhurried walks in it, you can listen to birds sing, woodpeckers knock, and feed the squirrel. Such close proximity of the forested area provides freshness of the inhaled air. Also in the park you can stroll the whole family, have picnics, relax and have fun in the amusement park. In winter, there are also many entertainments, the most important of which is ice skating at the Lokomotiv stadium. Swimming enthusiasts will find an excellent pool there. The location of the complex is very pleasing to the owners of new apartments, which they note in their reviews.
