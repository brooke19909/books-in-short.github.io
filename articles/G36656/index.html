About Lenovo Miix 2 10 Tablet

Lenovo Miix 2 10 is a tablet, which is the next offer from the actively developing Chinese company Lenovo. First, consider how users rate it.

Reviews: about good





About Lenovo Miix 2 10 reviews say the following:

  • It has a good screen with excellent color reproduction;
  • compatible with Windows and "MS Office";
  • has a high data processing speed;
  • weighs a little and is small in size;
  • its price is more than acceptable for most buyers;
  • due to the lack of a fan, it does not make unnecessary noise - quiet;
  • nice looking appearance and neat assembly;
  • good sound from jbl.

Reviews: about bad

Lenovo Miix 2 10 has the following disadvantages:

- It is impossible to change the position of the screen - it is in fact a tablet with a plug-in keyboard, and not a full replacement for the tablet. Long keyboard and mouse insertion times.

- There is no USB 3.0, drives work slowly during copying.

- Since Windows 8 is still a fairly new operating system, there are not enough applications for the touch interface.

- A small amount of memory.

Lenovo Miix 2 10: review

It belongs to the category of transformers, because it is converted into a laptop by installing a keyboard and mouse.





It has a processor with a clock frequency of 1330 MHz. The decent amount of RAM currently available is 2 GB. The internal memory of this device is 64 GB. The pride of the device is its display made using IPS technology. It has a glossy finish and a diagonal of 10.1. Its resolution is 1920 √ó 1200 (Full HD). Like all similar screens, it has excellent viewing angles and increased brightness of 330 cd / m2. In addition, Intel HD Graphics provides good performance and picture on the screen .

As for the communication capabilities, here, we can say a complete box - there is Wi-Fi and Bluetooth of the latest versions. In addition, Lenovo Miix 2 10 is equipped with a variety of connectors that provide it with the ability to connect to various devices and all kinds of data transfer methods. These include a microSD card reader, 2 USB 2.0, Micro-HDMI, Micro-USB, a SIM card slot.





High-quality sound is provided by two speakers, a subwoofer and a JBL.

In addition, the device has 2 cameras - rear and front with a resolution of 5 megapixels and 2 megapixels, which allow you to not only take photos, but also communicate via video chat, as well as participate in web conferences.

The autonomy of the device is provided by a rather capacious 2-cell battery, which holds up to 6800 mAh.

Lenovo Miix 2 10 runs on a 32-bit version of Windows 8.1.

A feature of this tablet is the presence of face recognition software that provides access to a computer. VeriFace application will compare the parameters of your image, having processed the photo saved from the webcam, and will allow you to enter the system. It has the ability to recognize the faces of multiple users.

The docking station is made of magnesium alloy and has a matte finish. This device complements the Lenovo Miix 2 10 hardware keyboard, as well as the woofer, touchpad and additional connectors. The docking station does not have a built-in battery and expandable memory. It also serves as a stand and protects the unit during transport. The device does not allow changes in the angle of the display, which is mounted using special magnets with great grip. Therefore, to extract it, it will be necessary to make sufficient efforts.

The dimensions of this apparatus are only 262 √ó 183x8 mm. It weighs 620 grams, and with the dock keyboard is added to its weight another 440 grams. It turns out only 1060 grams - a little more than a kilogram.

Summary

What can I say, the company once again confirmed its high competitiveness by offering users this solution. This device will be of interest to many buyers, especially those who are studying. It has pretty good autonomy, has good acoustics, a variety of ports and, of course, a high-quality IPS display. The ability to transform provides the ability to use it both as a laptop and as a tablet.

Even considering the fact that the performance of this device is not very large, it is quite enough to solve a large number of tasks. Buy and use, this tablet will not disappoint you.
