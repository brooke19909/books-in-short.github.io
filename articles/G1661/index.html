How to get from Moscow to Konakovo

The distance from Moscow to Konakovo is 147 km. How to get to one of the most picturesque areas of the Tver region?

By car

By car, it is easy and quick to get from Moscow to Konakovo to those who live in the north-west of the capital. On the Leningrad or Novorizhskoe highway you need to get to Klin. If you go along the highway M-9, then the distance between Moscow and Konakovo will be greater. At the same time, there are almost always a lot of trucks on the M-10, and here you can get into a traffic jam near Zelenograd or Solnechnogorsk.

If you start your journey from Moscow to Konakovo along the Novorizhskoye Shosse, you need to go to Leningradskoye in Klin. To do this, turn left at the McDonald's.

If you are traveling Moscow to Konakovo along the Leningradskoye Shosse, turn at the sign with the name of this town in the Tver Region. And he will be right after the village of Shornovo. Then you need to drive along the main street of Konakovo, past the monument to Lenin, where you turn towards Dubna. It is worth remembering: pointers to this city are here. You need to turn after the house located at 69 Svobody Street. Next, go across the bridge to the Dmitrov Gora village, where you will need to turn left at the first turn, towards Fedorovskoye.

You can also get from Moscow to Konakovo by Dmitrovskoye Shosse - by ferry to Dubna. A crossing costs 120 rubles.

By bus

You can also use intercity transport. Buses on the route Moscow - Konakovo run daily. Depart near the metro station Tushinskaya. Ticket price from 250-280 rubles.

By train

You can leave Moscow in Konakovo from the Leningrad station. Trains depart at intervals of one and a half hours. Ticket price - 350-400 rubles. Travel time is two and a half hours.

Recreation center

Holidays in Konakovo will appeal to people with different preferences. There is a huge selection of recreation centers, sanatoriums located close to the so-called Moscow Sea. They are, of course, not in the city itself, but in its environs.

At tourist centers of Konakovsky region, lovers of outdoor activities spend their days off or vacation with pleasure. Here you can swim on a boat or yacht, ride a horse. It is also a great place for fishing enthusiasts.

The most popular recreation centers in Konakovsky district:

  • "Chalet Zavidovo Golf".
  • "Volga Wills".
  • Zavidovo
  • "Lighthouse".

You can diversify your outdoor recreation by visiting the residence of the president "Rus", the reserve "Zavidovo", as well as the sights of this small but beautiful city.

What to see in Konakovo

A little more than 100 years ago, this settlement was a village and was called Kuznetsovo. It was later renamed in honor of one of the revolutionary figures. Holidays in Konakovo will appeal to lovers of excursions. There are many ancient architectural monuments.

Rozhdestvensky Manor is one of the city's attractions. This brick building of Konakovo inherited from Korchev - a city flooded during the construction of the Ivankovo ‚Äã‚Äãreservoir.

The hallmark of the village of Kuznetsova was the faience industry. The old buildings of the factory where the kitchen utensils were made have been preserved here.

Church of the Nativity of the Virgin, which must be visited during a trip to Konakovo, was built in the 14th century. This is one of the oldest temples in the Tver region.

Karacharovo is an old village in Konakovo. At first glance, there is nothing remarkable. However, those who are interested in the history of the Tver region and ancient architectural monuments should visit this village. A huge number of houses built in the XIX century are concentrated here.
