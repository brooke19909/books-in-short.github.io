Vacuum cleaner Samsung SC20F30WA: reviews, features, instructions

The household market is constantly updated with new models of vacuum cleaners. Due to many technical innovations, the opinion that the subject of cleaning with the presence of a bag is an obsolete design is rooted in the minds of buyers. However, this is not always the case, such models are not out of order at all and have their advantages. They also have a vacuum cleaner Samsung SC20F30WA, reviews of which will be discussed in this article.

External characteristic

Like all bag vacuum cleaners, this model is characterized by powerful suction of dust and debris. It works quietly and does not require careful frequent care.

Conducting a review of Samsung SC20F30WA, it can be noted that the vacuum cleaner looks pretty stylish, compact, has a long cord. All this allows him to make unimpeded maneuvers around the room and get dust in less accessible places.

To save the time of the housewives, the manufacturer supplied the vacuum cleaner with a bumper made of soft material. Thanks to this, you can quickly clean the furnished room without worrying about property damage. With such a household item, your furniture will be insured against unnecessary scratches.





Essential Accessories Overview

The good suction capacity of the Samsung SC20F30WA is determined by the presence of a cyclone filter with centrifugal force. Spinning at a certain speed, it draws in dust and larger debris.

Accessories include three nozzles for various types of cleaning and other additional items. During operation, the brush is easily adjustable with respect to the pipe. In addition, it is very convenient to use, because it allows you to make the necessary movements without any tension at any angle.

A removable dust bag is quite voluminous. When it is full, it is easy to replace it with a new one. It is easily removed, the accumulated dust does not spread around the room. Even with timely cleaning, vacuum cleaner bags can be changed once a month.

The appliance is equipped with a reliable HEPA 13 microfilter, which traps allergens. An improved cleaning system keeps the air fresh and promotes healthy breathing.

Equally important is the fact that the unit operates silently. It can be used by older people and those who can not tolerate loud sounds.

There is a special compartment for the cord, with an automatic device for tightening it. For the convenience of cleaning on various surfaces, the vacuum cleaner is equipped with a regulator for changing the position of the villi.

On the case there is a handle designed to transfer the vacuum cleaner to another room. Samsung SC20F30WA reviews say that it is better not to use it during operation, as this causes some inconvenience due to its location. To adjust the power on the housing has a lever.





The role of the cyclone filter

The main objective of this design is high-quality air purification in the room. This type of dust collector frees from frequent cleaning of dust bags. This is important for people with allergies, as they do not have to breathe dusty air.

Due to the formation of a vortex, the filter performs two functions. With a strong swirl of air flow, larger debris is pressed against the walls of the housing. A sponge fixed at the exit traps the smallest particles of dust.

The dust collector is made of hard plastic and is reliable to use. It is also convenient that it is transparent, because this allows you to see the garbage accumulated in it.

The simplicity of the device simplifies its operation and proper care of it.

Its disadvantage is that when it is filled with garbage, it loses suction power. The engine heats up, and you have to turn off the vacuum cleaner.

The design with a cyclone filter cannot perform washing functions.





Reliability in use

Follow the operating instructions as instructed and the Samsung SC20F30WA will last you long and reliably. Using this vacuum cleaner, you can carry out high-quality cleaning in rooms where there are pets. He picks up wool, hair and hair from clothes well.

Existing nozzles allow you to tidy up surfaces of various types. Slit - perfectly copes with cleaning in inaccessible places, crevices and corners. Using a dust brush, you can clean frames and carved objects. The following nozzle is designed to clean carpet, furniture and other soft surfaces.

If you set the minimum suction power, you can clean the lung tissue and other fragile objects. Many housewives thus manage to remove dust from curtains, paintings, lambrequins, tulle curtains and all kinds of decoration items.

To clean excessively soiled carpets and hard places, it is best to turn on maximum power. However, about the Samsung SC20F30WA vacuum cleaner in the reviews, people indicate that it does not have to be turned on with all its might. Half the power is enough to clean the apartment perfectly.

The telescopic tube of the vacuum cleaner is made of reliable material. By moving the adjustment button in the middle, you can adjust the appropriate length, thereby facilitating the cleaning process. Due to the tube, the corrugated hose is kept intact for a longer period.

In order not to constantly change the bags for the vacuum cleaner, you can purchase a disposable dust bag. The design of the model under discussion allows this to be done. Since you still have to buy such accessories separately, it is possible to choose who will like it.





Benefits

This model is considered a budget option. Nevertheless, it should be recognized: the technical characteristics of Samsung SC20F30WA deserve attention. A conventional vacuum cleaner has a power consumption of 200 watts and is designed only for dry cleaning. Endowed with a very capacious dust collector, the capacity of which is 3 liters. At the same time, it has a good dust drawing force - 420 watts. The power cord allows you to use a vacuum cleaner with a diameter of 7 meters.

The compactness of the model significantly saves space for storage. The vacuum cleaner can be placed in the pantry in an upright position.





Instruction manual

Preparing the vacuum cleaner for use and caring for it are as follows:

  • Work elements such as a handle, hose and tubing are connected.
  • After attaching the brush, make sure that it is well fixed on the handle.
  • Immediately before work, you need to clearly adjust the length of the telescopic tube.
  • When sending the vacuum cleaner to the storage place, you need to fix the brush for cleaning the floor well.
  • Do not turn off the vacuum cleaner by the cord.
  • When a red light comes on on the filter indicator, you need to clean all the parts in which debris can accumulate.
  • To replace the dust bag, you need to open the cover on the housing. Then, raise the ring and remove the dust container from the holder. Following the same pattern, you need to install a new bag and close the lid. It must not be allowed to protrude beyond the compartment, because this will affect the rate of absorption.
  • The filters are cleaned by shaking from dust.
  • All nozzles should be cleaned again and the drum checked periodically.
  • The HEPA filter is not washed with water when assembled. It must be disconnected from the handle and the tube and the mesh removed from it. The case is cleaned of dust. And the mesh and container are washed with water and dried well.

The household appliance in question does not need frequent care and does not cause much trouble in operation. Compared with similar models, it has an average cost. Price Samsung SC20F30WA ranges from 6852 to 7490 rubles.





Security measures

As with any technological device, a vacuum cleaner equipped with a cyclone filter has certain requirements for careful use and operating rules.

  • Do not leave children unattended near a vacuum cleaner.
  • Before servicing the inside of the housing, disconnect the plug from the main power source.
  • For longer preservation of the device, it is not recommended to use an additional extension cord.
  • In the event of a breakdown, you should immediately resort to the help of professionals from an official service center.
  • Moving the vacuum cleaner with a hose is not recommended.
  • After cleaning, first turn off the button on the housing, and then remove the plug from the network.
