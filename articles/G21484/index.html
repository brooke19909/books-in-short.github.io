Hotel PGS Hotels Kiris Resort 5 * Turkey, Kemer: reviews

Turkey is an exotic resort that is famous among tourists from Russia and the CIS countries. The territory of this country every year receives a huge number of tourists who like hot weather and a good, quality vacation.

The coastal part of Turkey is divided into several resort areas, one of which is located in Kemer. It also includes the settlements adjacent to this city. Tourists who go on a vacation package always stay for a hotel stay. One of the best in the area is PGS Hotels Kiris. The features of rest in it will be discussed further.

As for the location, according to reviews of PGS Hotels Kiris Resort 5 *, which tourists leave about this place of rest, the hotel is in a very convenient location, not far from the vibrant city center of Kemer, which is famous for having a lot of entertainment places. In addition, they note the excellent location of the hotel relative to the airport, where all tourists arrive - the distance is 65 km.

As for the general natural picture that is available in this place, on the one hand the hotel is surrounded by flowering gardens, on the other - mountains, and from the windows of many apartments you can watch a beautiful view of the Mediterranean Sea.



Rooms

PGS Kiris Hotel 5 * - the hotel is quite large, as its number of rooms includes as many as 800 apartments. Each of them is designed so that tourists would be as comfortable as possible within its walls. There is not only convenient, comfortable furniture, but also electronics, which can also be used.

All rooms are located in a modern seven-story building, which is fully equipped with an excellent air conditioning system, which allows tourists to feel as comfortable as possible in any weather. Also, two small Club buildings are provided for tourists.

The apartments are divided into several categories, depending on the level of comfort: "Standard", "Family" and "Superior". In the category of family-type rooms there are several that are designed to accommodate people with disabilities - here the interior is designed accordingly, taking into account all the needs of this category of guests, the furniture was also made by the personal order of the hotel.

Each room has an individual bathroom, where there is a plumbing and bath accessories needed for a comfortable stay. All guests at PGS Hotels Kiris Resort 5 * are provided with the required number of bath towels, bathrobes, slippers, which are replaced once every three days. In apartments designed for people with disabilities, plumbing is designed taking into account all international standards.

"Standard"

The simplest apartments in terms of equipment - "Standard". They are most popular among couples in love who want to spend time together in an exotic setting. These rooms consist of one room, which is located on an area of ‚Äã‚Äã25 square meters. m

For the most comfortable stay, guests are offered a French bed for two, a pair of bedside tables, as well as a wardrobe. There is also a folding sofa, which can serve as a very comfortable berth for another person.

As for the rest of the equipment, the Standard category apartments have a large plasma TV, on which you can watch your favorite satellite programs. If desired, guests can take the opportunity to store valuables in an electronic safe, as well as a telephone. The rooms have a minibar with an excellent selection of soft drinks, the range of which is updated every day.

"Family"

Positioning itself as a place suitable for family vacations, the hotel offers tourists family-type rooms, where all the conditions for a company up to five people are presented. There is also the possibility of installing a berth for the child - in the form of a crib - "playpen".

The room is located on a large territory - 45 square meters. m, divided into two rooms. Each of them has a sleeping area with appropriate furniture, as well as the necessary electronics. The entire room has one shared balcony with views of the sea, gardens and mountains.

Superior

The most comfortable conditions in PGS Hotels Kiris Resort 5 * are provided in Superior rooms. They are located on a fairly large area, which is divided into one room and a large furnished terrace, which has a jacuzzi. From here, guests can enjoy magnificent views of the surrounding beauty.

As for the furniture of the Superior rooms, there is a large French bed for two and a huge sofa, which can also serve as a berth. There is a soft round sofa on the terrace where you can relax, and there is also a table.



Conditions for children

PGS Hotels Kiris positions itself as a "family" type hotel. First of all, this is confirmed by the presence of apartments of the appropriate type. Also, guests, upon arrival here with young children, can independently verify that for this category of guests all the conditions of safety and good living are created.

Much attention is paid to the placement of children - they are provided with special comfortable cots, which are installed in the room for free. For a fee, parents can rent a stroller - the cost is about $ 5 per day.

On the territory of PGS Hotels Kiris there are several playgrounds on which everything is done in such a way that kids can actively spend free time. There is also a children's pool for them, where everything is done taking into account safety requirements. The main building also has a covered aquazone where small guests can spend their time.

If desired, parents can send their child to a beautiful kids club, where a team consisting of professional animators works. Here they will play with the kids, watch cartoons and educational programs with them. By the way, parents note as a pleasant moment that most of the hotel staff speak excellent Russian, including animators.

If necessary, in PGS Hotels Kiris Resort 5 * you can always use the services of a nanny. They are paid and are priced at $ 30 per hour.



Entertainment

As tourists themselves note, in this place to relax you never have to get bored. Here, according to them, regular show programs are held with the participation of guests and animators. Often there are organized entertainment sports shows, for example, morning relay races near the pool and near the beach.

Every evening, a disco begins on the platform near the pool, the end of which falls on a late night. All this time, clockwork hits of foreign performers, who represent local DJs, have been playing here. Admission to such an event is free.

As for the other types of entertainment, all game fans can go to separate rooms that are equipped for playing billiards, darts, boccia, etc.



Nutrition

Guests of PGS Hotels Kiris Resort 5 * (Turkey), who live in this place on a package tour, can visit the specially organized buffet for them every day, which is located in two restaurants - Vivaldi and Yakamoz. One of them prepares Italian dishes, and the other - Turkish cuisine. As the guests themselves note, the food served here is incredibly tasty and nutritious. It also offers vegetarian options. The administration draws the attention of tourists to the fact that before visiting such institutions you need to book a table for yourself at a certain time in advance.

The hotel also has five luxurious bars, which are located in the pool area, on the beach and in the main building. The largest, which is installed near the pool, can accommodate up to 500 people on its territory at a time. At these establishments you can enjoy drinks as well as snacks.



Pool

PGS Hotels Kiris Resort (Turkey) has a beautiful aquazone, which is located in the open air. It consists of large pools, which in total occupy a rather impressive area - almost 2300 square meters. m. They have two large slides, the visit of which is limited by age criteria (from 7 years).

Around the aquazone there is a beautiful sun terrace where vacationers can spend time in between water procedures, as well as sunbathe. At this place there is a considerable number of sun loungers, with a pedestal for things, as well as umbrellas. Guests who stay here have the opportunity to visit the bar, where you can always enjoy refreshing and bright cocktails, as well as desserts. Guests in their reviews of PGS Hotels Kiris Resort often recommend being here to try signature ice cream, which is made according to a unique recipe.

Sport

The hotel often hosts various sporting events, in which guests take part with great pleasure. For the category of guests who prefer a sporty lifestyle, there are a considerable number of places where you can realize your goals regarding maintaining a healthy mind and a beautiful figure. First of all, this is a gym where both men and women can work out - apparatuses are installed here to create different levels of physical activity. If you wish, you can go to any outdoor area and play basketball, soccer or volleyball. There is also a well-equipped tennis court. All of the above sports can be taught free of charge. Inventory for games is also issued without rent.

However, PGS Hotels Kiris also offers paid sports. In particular, it is windsurfing and water skiing. If desired, you can rent the necessary equipment for immersion in water.

The hotel has a special point where you can rent a bike - on it, tourists usually go to explore local attractions.

SPA

The SPA zone is incredibly popular among guests. You can visit it any day of the week from 8 a.m. to 8 p.m.

There is a large massage room where professional massage therapists can immerse any guest in a state of relaxation. Their services are paid, but, despite this, are in great demand. Next to this place is a large spa, where masters can offer a range of cosmetic services.

The PGS Hotels Kiris Resort's spa area also has a large thermal complex, which can be visited by all fans of the "hot" relaxation. It consists of a sauna and a traditional Turkish bath - hammam. After visiting the steam room, guests can plunge into the pool with warm water, which is located here. It is always filled with crystal clear water, which is automatically heated to a comfortable temperature.



Beach

Very often reviews of PGS Hotels Kiris mention a great beach line that a hotel has - its length is 215 meters. For guests there are also two large, departing to the sea pier, built of wood.

As for the beach itself, it is covered with pebbles and sand, the coast is gentle, which is safe not only for adults, but also for children. Tourists who relax here note the excellent arrangement of this recreation area: there are locker rooms, several showers, as well as dry closets. Almost throughout its territory there are good sun loungers with umbrellas. There is a bar on the beach with a good selection of drinks and snacks.

Along the entire coastline there are many attractions that will appeal to tourists from different locations. If a desire arises, guests can rent any of the gazebos on the beach - the daily cost will be about $ 20 per person.
