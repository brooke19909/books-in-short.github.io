"Hard Rock Hotel" (Pattaya, Thailand): description, photos, reviews

The tastes of travelers are incredibly varied. Someone prefers to visit the purest ocean waters, someone prefers to retire to the mountains, and someone likes a vacation full of various excursions. One of the few resorts, combining a diverse orientation, is Thailand with the mainland settlement - Pattaya. Vacationers of all ages will surely find something to their liking: from the smallest to the elderly.

The choice of holidaymakers is determined by a number of factors: the aroma of exotic plants, developed infrastructure, a non-hot climate and the fresh sea breeze of the Gulf of Thailand - this is why many guests come here. A variety of hotels, from eminent and luxurious to modest and budget, are located along the coast. The four-star Hard Rock Hotel welcomes every visitor from any corner of the globe. It offers high-level service, ergonomic rooms and a cozy atmosphere. Qualified employees, as noted by the guests, every second give a good mood, a lot of positive and a lot of smiles to absolutely all vacationers. In general, the reviews about the Hard Rock Hotel (Pattaya) are mostly positive. How did the hotel deserve such close attention? More about everything in the article.

The good location, which is noted by vacationers, is due to being in the central part of the city, which allows guests, without spending a lot of time, to get to the largest shopping centers.

Beach

Incredibly favorable location of the hotel relative to the coastline. In order to be on the beach, travelers need to overcome a distance of only 100 meters. However, it should be noted that this is a city beach, the infrastructure of which is at the highest level. Rental of sunbeds, umbrellas and beach towels is available for a fee. The order and safety 24 hours a day are watched by rescuers and security service.

The coastline is sandy, the entrance to the waters of the Gulf of Thailand is gentle. It is favorable for swimming and playing kids aground.

Exit to the open sea on a snow-white yacht is not only interesting, but also incredibly romantic. Those who wish to experience a frantic sense of relaxation can use the services of massage therapists who will give each cell of the body a portion of oxygen and new strength.
