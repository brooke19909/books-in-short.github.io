Hotel Mitsis Rodos Village Resort 5 *

Description: Mitsis Rodos Village Resort 5 is located near the resort village of Kiotaki in the east of the island of Rhodes. The town of Lindos is 12 km away, and the international airport is 55 km away.

The Mitsis hotel has a shared beach and grounds with Rodos Maris.

The hotel complex is famous for its ancient Rhodes architecture and exquisite interiors with a unique color scheme. The hotel's terraces offer unrivaled views of the sea, mountain or green fragrant territory with beautiful bizarre pools.

Rooms: Mitsis Rodos Village Resort 5 offers 407 rooms located in the main building and 169 bungalows. Rooms feature furnished balconies or terraces with views of the flowering garden or sea. There are family rooms with two bedrooms and duplex apartments with two bathrooms. The spacious rooms are decorated in a traditional Mediterranean color palette with a predominance of warm, natural tones. The rooms have modern light wood furniture.

The rooms have air conditioning, television, satellite TV, refrigerator, minibar with a selection of soft drinks. A safe is provided for a fee. A hairdryer and toiletries are provided in the guest bathroom.



Beach: A five-minute walk is the beach, where sunshades and sunbeds are provided free of charge.

Meals: Mitsis Rodos Village Resort 5 has an all-inclusive service. Meals are provided in buffet style. Free all-day snacks and local drinks are available.

Lindos Restaurant, which serves breakfast, lunch and dinner, serves international and Mediterranean dishes, draft beers, local wines and juices.

Miramonte Pizzeria accepts a la carte service. Here you can choose Italian dishes that will be cooked before the eyes of vacationers.

From 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. there is a snack bar with an assortment of salads, sandwiches, ice cream, desserts, fruits, hot snacks and drinks.

In the evening, you can relax in the main bar of the hotel, ordering local wines, coffee, tea, draft beer, juices.

From 10 a.m. to midnight, there is a bar-cafe serving sandwiches and drinks. After lunch, guests can enjoy tea or coffee with sweets and pastries.

While relaxing by the pool, there is always the opportunity to have a sandwich, quench your thirst with a refreshing drink, eat ice cream or choose fruits.

In the evening, a bar located in the theater opens its doors.



Tourist info: Mitsis Rodos Village Resort 5 offers vacationers a wide range of services and entertainment. Guests have access to the infrastructure of the neighboring Rodos Maris.

The Mitsis Rodos Village Hotel has parking, car rental, a mini market, a hairdresser, an indoor theater, a games room, a TV room, an Internet cafe, an amphitheater, and a laundry. Tourists have contests, theme nights and music shows.

The hotel has three pools, tennis courts and tables, billiards, darts targets. It offers a game of football, volleyball, water polo.

Guests are invited to the beauty salon of a neighboring hotel.

Reviews: Hotel Mitsis Rodos Village Resort 5, tourist reviews confirm this, is a great place for a beach or family vacation. The hotel has a large intricate area with blue pools and beautiful flowering plants.

Spacious, bright rooms are clean and tidy. Smiling staff with great attention to each guest. The bars and restaurants offer a variety of meat and fish dishes, including grilled ones, a lot of delicious desserts, fruits, wines, juices, cocktails.

Animators organize fun contests and entertaining shows. Many options for outdoor activities and entertainment on the water. On the beach, to which you go exactly 5 minutes, there are always free sunbeds.
