Kleopatra Togan Suit Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Alanya): description, service, reviews

Alanya is a famous atmospheric resort on the Turkish coast of the Mediterranean Sea. Despite the remoteness from the airports, the city is very popular among tourists. Vacationers are called here by the warm sea, beaches marked with the prestigious Blue Flag, as well as affordable prices.

For everyone there is a leisure to their liking, corresponding to the size of their wallet. Fans of secluded relaxation can choose areas remote from party places where you can quietly enjoy cold drinks on the beach. Young people will like spending leisure time in club hotels, hanging out at noisy parties in the resort center or at the marina. For those who find it difficult to imagine a vacation without exciting excursions and trips around the country, there are many historical and cultural attractions in the city and its environs: ancient fortresses, towers, mosques, etc. Natural beauty is represented by caves with underground lakes, stalactites and stalagmites. The article offers information about one of the most popular accommodation options in Alanya - Kleopatra Togan Suit Hotel 3 *.

Most valuable: beaches

Wherever tourists decide to stay in Alanya, whether their choice will fall on the hotel Kleopatra Togan Suit Hotel 3 * or any other hotel, everyone is interested in the issue of beaches.





The local beaches are considered one of the best in Turkey. The water in the beach areas, which guests of the resort have repeatedly mentioned in their reviews, is very clean and transparent, the territory has a well-developed infrastructure, special staff carefully monitor the order. The coast is covered with white sand with a yellowish tint, in some places you can find tiny pebbles and smooth pebbles. Almost everywhere there are locker rooms, rental points for sun loungers and umbrellas, cafes and bars. Guests of Alanya should be prepared for the fact that the prices for drinks and food in beach establishments are many times higher than in stores. Toilets can be used for a fee. Ecological cleanliness and the high level of service of local beaches are periodically marked by the high recognition of the competent commissions and the assignment of the Blue Flag to them.

In Alanya, there are both hotel and municipal beaches. The most beautiful and popular tourists consider Cleopatra, which stretches along the west coast.
