How to charge a phone without a charger: extreme and modern ways

It is becoming more and more difficult for a modern person to live without a cell phone every day. There was even such a joke that he could live without food for a month, without water - for three days, and without a cell phone - 5 minutes. How to charge a phone without a charger?



In the city, the lack of a charger is not a problem. You can almost certainly borrow it from a colleague or ask for help in a communication salon. Many provide similar services for a fee. In the end, you can just buy from them an additional battery or charger. In addition, many modern cell phones are charged via a USB cable from a computer. Yes, and in the most extreme case, you can ask passers-by for help.

True, it is not always the case that the treasured outlet is at hand and around is full of people. A person can go hiking in the forest or mountains, and even just mushrooms and get lost. And then contact is simply vital, and the battery charge may be at zero. And how to charge the phone without a charger in these conditions? In such a situation, even the most extreme methods will do.



The battery of a modern phone is actually the same battery. Therefore, in order to charge it a little, you can heat it in the sun or a piece of hot metal (knife). Another good option is to hit the battery several times on a stone or just ground. Well, the most extreme is to drive a nail into the battery. These methods will allow you to make at least one call to the rescue service or relatives. True, after such executions the battery will have to be thrown away. But its price and the price of human life are not comparable.

But all these methods, of course, are suitable for a very extreme case. It happens, after all, that there are many people, and there seems to be a socket. And you have to look for options on how to charge the phone without a charger. Most often, such situations arise in the train. Since trips are planned in advance, you can prepare.
