Key Documents

At VSK OSAGO (customer reviews confirm this) - a key product.

The main documents involved in the transaction are the contract for the provision of insurance services, insurance policy and payment documents.

The current legislation of the Russian Federation in the field of insurance allows you to apply the contract and the policy or one of these contracts.

That is, the insurance contract, which has an identification number and a date of conclusion, can be applied without issuing an insurance policy, if this is stipulated by the rules of the company.

In another case, a numbered policy is issued on the basis of an insurance contract.

The insurance policy may be individual or collective.

In cases with compulsory motor third-party liability insurance and hull insurance, it is mandatory to have an insurance policy in accordance with the norms of the current legislation of the Russian Federation.

The activities of insurance companies are regulated by the rules of the Federal Law of 11/27/1992 "On insurance business in the Russian Federation".

Features of the occurrence of the insured event

As the reviews confirm, VSK for OSAGO has been working for a long time and successfully. The company has the following rules and regulations.

During the validity period of the insurance contract and policy, the recipient of the service after the occurrence of the insured event has the right to receive a premium. The amount and procedure for receiving the award are stipulated by the terms of the contract.

After the occurrence of the insured event, the insured must submit a package of documents in the prescribed manner, after consideration of which the issue of payment of premium or refusal is accepted.

The insurance company has the right to refuse partially or in full in the payment, if the insured has violated the procedure for notification, collection of documents, terms or other conditions.

The legislation provides for a time limit for appealing against such decisions and a specific legal procedure.

They appeal against decisions to partially or completely refuse to pay the insurance premium by filing a claim with the insurance company or by filing a lawsuit.

The court has the right to cancel the decision of the insurance company on a partial payment or a complete refusal, declaring it illegal. If the court decides to pay in full or increase the amount, this is stipulated in a judicial act, after which the insured has the right to demand the collection of funds.

What measures need to be taken to protect yourself when making an insured event?

  • When concluding an agreement and purchasing an insurance policy, you must carefully read the documents.
  • Keep policy and contract until expiration.
  • At the conclusion of the contract, take a printout with the rules for receiving insurance payment.
  • Timely notify the company representative of the occurrence of the insured event, keeping written evidence of such a notification.
  • Carefully study the stipulated deadlines and the necessary package of documents for circulation.
  • When submitting a package of documents for payment, get a copy of the application with a mark of admission.
  • Keep all correspondence on the insured event with the agent of the company.
  • In no case, until the full payment is made, do not carry out an independent car repair or its alienation in favor of third parties.
  • If necessary, make an independent assessment and submit a report to the insurance company.
  • If in doubt, consult a qualified lawyer in this area.

What to do in case of violation of the payment procedure?

It is important to understand that Russian law stands for the protection of the rights and freedoms of citizens. If you violate the payment procedure, you must go to court.

As practice shows, when considering civil claims upon recovery from an insurance company in favor of legal entities and individuals, the court most often takes the side of the plaintiff if there are no significant violations on his part.

Having appealed to the court, under the MTPL policy, the client of the insurance company collects not only the due payment, but also compensation, interest on borrowed money, penalties, legal expenses, etc. The law allows you to apply to the court not only regarding the amount of payment, but for violation of the deadlines.

After amendments introduced in recent years, insurance legislation has been tightened, and insurance companies are subject to severe penalties for violation of its standards.
