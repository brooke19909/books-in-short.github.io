What is a gadget and widget in a modern computer

From the moment of its creation to the moment of popularization, the computer world has managed to acquire a huge bunch of various terms that an outside listener is now unlikely to understand what a gadget and widget are, why they are, in principle, needed. Terms, despite their similarity, reflect a variety of things. The easiest way to show their difference is on the example of a conventional computer. So, for example, the clock that will be displayed in the upper corner of the desktop screen, or an animated calendar that can be configured according to your own tastes, are widgets for Windows 7. Windows 7 gadgets are devices based on such an operating system. For example, a laptop, tablet or netbook running on such a platform will be called a gadget. Having understood what a gadget and widget are, you can find entire libraries of special applications that simplify the use of a desktop computer.
