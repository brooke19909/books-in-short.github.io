San Remo Hotel 2 *: photos, description and guest reviews

Every year more and more tourists turn their eyes to Cyprus. After all, this is a country with incredibly beautiful nature, interesting history and rich culture. Moreover, here you will find a rich beach holiday. Naturally, on the coast of Larnaca there is a huge number of hotels for every taste. One of them is the hotel complex San Remo Hotel 2 *.

Of course, before the trip, every tourist tries to find interesting information about the chosen place. Where is the hotel located and will you have to go far to the beach? What living conditions can one hope for and are there opportunities for family vacations? What kind of entertainment does the hotel offer? Answers to these questions will be useful to many travelers.

Where is the hotel located? Brief information





For many travelers, the location of the hotel is an important point. The hotel complex san Remo Hotel 2 * is located in one of the quiet areas of the popular resort city of Larnaca.

By the way, the distance to the historic city center with all its historical sights and modern entertainment facilities is only two kilometers. Tourists who do not like long walks can use public transport. But the distance to the beach is only 200 meters - a pedestrian road leads to it. In fact, the location of the hotel can be considered very advantageous.

Hotel Description

San Remo Hotel Larnaca 2 * is a small cozy hotel. It consists of only one multi-storey building, built in a simple but elegant Cypriot style. By the way, not so long ago, equipment was repaired and modernized, and therefore travelers can safely count on all the benefits of modern civilization.

The hotel area is small, but clean and beautiful. Here you can always relax, have a good time and breathe in the healing sea air. Pools, spacious terraces, secluded walking paths and lush, bright flower beds - this situation simply cannot but rejoice.

How many rooms do guests offer?

As already mentioned, the San Remo Hotel 2 * can be considered small compared to others. On its territory there are 69 rooms. These are standard rooms intended for one, two or three tourists (of course, there is the possibility of an extra bed for a child).

There are several superior rooms. By the way, from the windows of most rooms you can enjoy views of the city, the sea and the well-groomed territory of the hotel.

Hotel San Remo Hotel 2 * (Cyprus, Larnaca): photo and brief description of rooms





Of course, comfort is the key to a pleasant stay. Reviews from travelers who have already visited here indicate that all rooms on the hotel are spacious, clean and bright. The atmosphere is straightforward, but pleasant and promotes relaxation. And from the window, as well as sitting on the balcony, you can enjoy views of the city and surroundings.

The room has not only the necessary furniture, but also a set of household appliances. For example, you can spend the evening watching TV (there is access to a satellite dish). A modern air conditioning system will help fight summer heat, and in winter it will warm the air in the room. The phone has a direct dialing function (long-distance and international calls are paid separately).

The room has a minibar, which is regularly filled with drinking water. The remaining drinks are delivered to the room upon request (they are not included in the price). Guests have access to a mini fridge.

The bathroom is clean, equipped with modern fixtures. You can take a shower at any time of the day. Here you will also find toiletries, a hairdryer and, of course, a set of clean towels.

Dining for tourists: what does the hotel offer?





For most travelers, food is an integral part of a truly good vacation. What can the hotel offer in this regard? The cost of living on its territory already includes free breakfast. Each morning, guests are invited to the spacious restaurant, where they are served buffet style.

Reviews say that the choice of dishes is quite large, and the food is served in sufficient quantities, and therefore there is no need to rush anywhere. Dishes are always tasty, hearty and fresh, in a word, guests are satisfied. The rest of the day, alas, the restaurant does not work. But you can have a good time at the local bar. In addition to strong drinks, aromatic coffee and exotic cocktails, snacks and desserts are served here.

You can eat outside the San Remo Hotel 2 *. Larnaca Town can offer the traveler dozens of cozy cafes, lively pizzerias and luxurious restaurants where you can find dishes for every taste and eat, regardless of the time of day.

Beach: distance to the coast and useful information





The warm sea and picturesque beaches - that's why tourists from all over the world come to Cyprus. San Remo Hotel 2 * is located quite conveniently, since the distance to the sea shore is only 200 meters. A few minutes walk along a convenient walking path - and you can already enjoy the warm breeze and enjoy the exotic beauty.

The beach here is sandy, and reviews indicate that the entrance to the sea is convenient and cleared of stones. There is even a small sandbank where children love to dabble. Comfortable sunbeds, soft mattresses, large parasols, comfortable locker rooms and, of course, a team of experienced lifeguards are at your disposal. By the way, on the shore you will find a small colorful bar where you can always get a chilled drink and even eat deliciously.

Water activities for vacationers

Sunbathing, swimming in warm sea waters - this, of course, is a wonderful way to spend a vacation. But many tourists prefer more active and diverse types of recreation. What can the guests of the San Remo Hotel 2 * hotel count on? Larnaca is a resort town, so sports clubs are located on almost every public beach.

Here you can rent a catamaran or a boat, take training courses in scuba diving and windsurfing. Tourists have the opportunity to get a lot of vivid emotions, a jet ski or take a romantic boat trip. Prices are quite reasonable, and extraordinary, pleasant memories are guaranteed.

How to spend leisure time? Entertainment on site





Larnaca is a beautiful city, ideal for any type of vacation. Here you can soak up the beach, go in for sports, go on an excursion, and plunge headlong into the interesting history and vibrant culture of the country.

But is there anything to have fun directly at the hotel? There is a spacious pool in the courtyard for guests to swim and dive. Tourists say that they regularly clean it - the water is always clean, at a comfortable temperature. Nearby there is a spacious terrace with sunbeds and umbrellas, which can be used for free. From time to time, hotel guests organize fun games in the water.

You can visit the TV room, where you will find a large selection of films of different genres. You can have a good time in the company of friends while playing billiards. Travelers who strive to always keep fit can visit the local gym, where there are many effective trainers and, of course, an experienced instructor.

Children's rest: living conditions for the child

Many couples travel with their children. Naturally, relaxing with your child requires some additional amenities. What does the hotel complex San Remo Hotel 2 * offer?

Parents can expect an extra rollaway bed in the room. The hotel does not offer a children's menu. On the other hand, the menu is varied - you can always choose something for the child. As for entertainment, the kids can splash around in a special pool. It is spacious, but small, and therefore you can be sure of the safety of your child. On the territory there are several swings where kids can have fun.

Children can spend their free time in a spacious game room with various attractions, board games, etc. In case parents need to spend a short time, you can use the services of a nanny (not included in the price).

Hotel San Remo Hotel 2 * (Larnaca): traveler reviews





It's no secret that sometimes much more relevant information can be obtained by talking a little with people who have already been fortunate enough to visit a particular place. Do travelers like San Remo Hotel 2 *? Reviews indicate that most of the guests are satisfied with the quality of accommodation and service.

The hotel is small but clean, tidy and comfortable. And thanks to the cordiality of the staff, tourists can really feel at home. The rooms are spacious, cleaned here regularly. Breakfast includes delicious and fresh dishes, and you can have a great time at the local bar. Favorable location, proximity to the beach and the city center are one of the undoubted advantages of the hotel. According to reviews, both youth companies and couples with children feel comfortable here. Tourists recommend the San Remo Hotel 2 * as a good place for a relaxing holiday on the picturesque coast, surrounded by beautiful nature.
