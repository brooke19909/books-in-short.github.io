Lenovo IdeaPad 310-15ISK Notebook PC - owner reviews, features and specifications

The Lenovo product line has a ton of electronic products. This article is dedicated to the Lenovo IdeaPad 310 15ISK laptop and reviews about it. Also, some of the closest analogues will be considered both in price and in characteristics.

Description Lenovo IdeaPad 310 15ISK Notebook

In general, this model has a lot of different modifications. Therefore, when choosing, one should pay attention to both the index of the name and general characteristics.

The laptop case is made of plastic, which "mows" under the metal with its own touches. The material is quite malleable, therefore, most likely, not the most durable, and during careless operation physical damage to the case is possible.





At the bottom of the device there are two covers for quick access to the RAM strips and the hard drive. It will be possible to get to other areas either to specialists of the service center, or independently, having disassembled the case.

On the left side of the device are located:

  • connector for connecting the power adapter;
  • standard VGA output;
  • Ethernet port
  • HDMI
  • USB 3.0, painted in traditional blue;
  • headphone jack;
  • memory card slot.

There are fewer elements on the right:

  • two USB 2.0 ports;
  • drive for reading CDs and DVDs;
  • lock hole.

Screen

The display size is 15.6 inches with a TN matrix. The resolution is small - 1366 by 768 pixels. The coating is glossy, which is why it will be problematic to see something with a large brightness of external sources. The corners are also not particularly pleasing. The response time is not bad, which has a positive effect on watching movies and in games.

"Iron"

Inside the laptop, a Core I7 6500U processor is installed. It has two cores capable of sharing the load into 4 threads. The main frequency is 2.5 GHz. With increased load, it can increase it to 3.1 GHz. The average power is 15 watts. The cache is 4 MB. The processor integrated graphics subsystem HD Graphics 520.

The storage medium is a 1 TB Western Digital hard drive. The speed of rotation is 5400. Not the quietest and most productive medium.





The graphics card is the GeForce 920MX. It has 256 cores and a frequency of 965 MHz. 2 GB of memory on the card. There is support for the latest DirectX 12. When running programs and applications with low resource consumption, it is inactive and everything falls on the shoulders of the graphics adapter built into the processor. With an increase in load, for example in games, 920MX is taken up, the resources of which are much more extensive. As follows from the reviews on the Lenovo IdeaPad 310 15ISK laptop, the performance in more or less modern and popular games is not bad. The default system is Windows 10 Home.

Noise, cooling and autonomy

Fans are not particularly noisy and do not interfere with operation. Cool with quality. Under load, the temperature of the cores and the system is within the permissible norm.

Review Lenovo IdeaPad 310 15ISK Notebook Reviews

Better to start with positive feedback. In general, users are satisfied with the low price. Against this background, both the overall speed of the work and the low noise level look great.





Some users have left quite detailed reviews about the Lenovo IdeaPad 310 15ISK laptop. In it, they indicate a pleasant body material. When used carefully with plastic, no deformation will occur. They also note the sound, which in this model is very good. The heating temperature is tolerable. And for a diagonal of 15 inches, its weight and dimensions are acceptable. Work in the "heavyweights" such as 3D Max and Photoshop did not cause any problems. And many modern games also go quietly on medium settings, and some on high ones.

Negative feedback

Reviews about the price of the Lenovo IdeaPad 310 15ISK are considered mostly positive. But there are those users for whom the price / quality ratio in this model is a bit below the norm. In addition, there are more interesting solutions from competitors.

Almost every negative review about the laptop 15.6 Lenovo IdeaPad 310 15ISK marks its screen. Although it is large, small angles and insufficient brightness seemed critical to someone.





Those who are not used to mobile keyboards will not be familiar with the placement of buttons on this device. This minus is also devoted to several reviews. In particular, this applies to the small size of the Shift button on the right side of the keyboard. Also, the transition keys Home, End and so on are combined on the numeric keypad. Another important drawback, users considered the lack of keyboard backlight.

Some of the reviews on the Lenovo IdeaPad 310 15ISK laptop (silver or another color) say that the battery life is too short, which can be as little as 1.5-2 hours in Internet mode.

In demanding tasks, there is an increase in the noise level from the cooling system, which does not add pluses to the ‚Äúkarma‚Äù of the device. The issue of noise can also include the clearly distinguishable crack of the hard disk. A trifle, but happens to be unnerving.

Someone was not quite happy with the fact that the device had the Windows 10 operating system installed, which, in fact, was included in the price of the laptop.

conclusions

You can highlight the main advantages of this laptop:

  • good performance;
  • the ability to increase the amount of RAM and replace the hard drive;
  • acceptable price;
  • good sound;

Some configurations can be equipped with 128 GB SSDs or their duet with HDD.

Acer Extenza EX2540 has the same screen size. In addition to the main resolution, in 1366 by 768 there are also 1920 by 1080. The processor is dual-core from the i3 or i5 families. The drive is not much different from the hero of the article, except that some modifications of the laptop can use SSD.

Conclusion

In general, the discussion of Lenovo IdeaPad 310 15ISK shows that the laptop is worth the money and solves the tasks.





For everyday work with documents and on the Internet, it is perfect. For some difficult tasks too. Modeling or programming on it is real, however, as a budget option, if there is no way to purchase more expensive hardware.

If the user is not an avid gamer, then you can comfortably play old classic toys on this laptop. For newer ones, you will have to endure the brakes and subsidence of the FPS.
