Pension "Kialasur" (Abkhazia, Sukhumi): description, address, reviews

Abkhazia is a good place to relax. The cleanest lakes, unique parks, indescribable atmosphere of peace will allow you to relax from the harsh working days.

Pension "Kialasur" located in the green zone of Sukhum. This resting place is located next to the arboretum of the capital of Abkhazia. Previously, the boarding house was called "Black Pearl". The complex consists of double family houses. The total number is 64 pieces. Services

The whole range of necessary massage procedures can be done directly on the territory of the Kyalasur boarding house. Abkhazia as the country of the purest mountain rivers and lakes, unique caves and forests has a wide range of excursion programs.

Fans of outdoor activities and sports will find on the territory volleyball courts, designated areas for badminton, table tennis. There you can have a great time with your family.

The beach is less than ten meters from the living area. It is a sand and gravel mixture. The beach has all the necessary equipment, sunbeds, umbrellas. Pension "Kialasur" provides rental scooters, boats and catamarans.

Everyone is invited to ride the rides or take a boat trip to the open sea. In the evening, discos are held.

For younger guests, a playground is organized on site. Children will enjoy playing in the sandbox, ride a slide, swim in a shallow pool.

Fans of the Russian bath are given the opportunity to visit the steam room with a billiard room. The former Black Pearl also has a taxi service and its own gift shop.

Those who wish to fish here will always find a rental of all the necessary equipment (nets, fishing rods, etc.).

Meals are provided at the request of tourists for an additional fee. The guesthouse has cafes and bars.
