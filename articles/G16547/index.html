Brokerage service

Many Russians are interested not so much in the question of whether it is possible to buy shares of Sberbank in principle, but how to gain access to trading on the exchange.

It should be noted that for this there is an institution of brokerage services. Who is a broker and what does he do? This is a legal entity that, on the basis of a license, provides certain types of services, the focus and scope of which, as a rule, are determined individually. How to choose a reliable broker? First of all, pay attention to how many clients he serves and how much money he works with.





Also analyze how long a legal entity has been working in the stock market and what is its level of technical support. As a reliable broker, you can recommend RosEuroBank, Troika Dialog, or Finam.

How the purchase of shares is realized in practice

The procedure for acquiring Sberbank shares is quite simple. First, you decide on the choice of a broker, then conclude a service agreement with him, then open a brokerage account. After that, you can deposit money into it and carry out trading operations in the stock market.
