Duct and cassette air conditioners: what are their advantages

If you are concerned about the noise level, it is better to choose a duct or cassette air conditioner, but this option is only suitable if there are false ceilings in your house. Only such air conditioners are attached to this design. The first block delivers cold through the ducts, the second redistributes it using a grill. Thus, you can forget about the directed flow of cold air, as it happens when using an air conditioner, multi split system or window cooler. The directed air flow from the air conditioner, as you know, can lead to at least frequent colds. There are also such equipment installation options when it is possible to cool an entire apartment with one air conditioner. This eliminates the need to install several multi split systems for cooling a large area. In this case, more powerful or even industrial options are used.





Reliable and economical - what kind of air conditioning?

No less important than the price of air conditioning, when choosing a model is the level of energy consumption and durability. To ensure both, we recommend buying an inverter air conditioner. The fact is that the compressor consumes most of the electricity at startup, and it also wears out faster if it constantly turns on and off. In the case of an inverter air conditioner, it will not turn off permanently. Just when you set the desired temperature, it will work at maximum speed, and then at a minimum, maintaining the specified temperature.
