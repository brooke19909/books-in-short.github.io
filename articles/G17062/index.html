Hotel "Sambia", Zelenogradsk: description and reviews

Sometimes you want to change the situation not only for the holidays, but also for the weekend. For real relaxation, we suggest you visit the Sambia Hotel located in the city of Zelenogradsk, Kaliningrad Region . Zelenogradsk is located on the shores of the Baltic Sea. The hotel was built in 2003, has a category of 4 stars, and got its name from the historical name of the peninsula, where the resort city is located today.





Description

Hotel "Sambia" has its own protected area right on the shores of the Baltic Sea. This is one of the most comfortable hotels in Zelenogradsk, which is located in its very center, at the base of the Curonian Spit national park of Russia . The distance from the hotel to the beach takes only two minutes on foot. The beach is sandy, with a gentle slope into the sea. The hotel complex includes 3 buildings - one 3-storey and two 4-storey. On each floor, guests can relax and mingle in the spacious lounge. All cases are interconnected by transitions. There are no elevators in the buildings.

The hotel has a cozy patio with fountains. Sambia has its own small water park (hotel, Zelenogradsk, photo below). There is a playground for children on site. For those who came to rest in their own car, free secure parking is provided. The total number of rooms of the hotel includes 87 comfortable rooms. Hotel "Sambia" (Zelenogradsk) is located at a distance of 32 kilometers from the center of Kaliningrad, 15 kilometers is the airport of Khrabrovo. The train station can be reached on foot in 10 minutes. Check-in time is 14.00, check-out time is 12.00.

Hotel Sambia (Zelenogradsk): positive reviews

Most of the guests were satisfied with their stay in this comfortable hotel, as evidenced by the most favorable reviews:

  • Of course, all are unanimously noted by the pleasant and friendly staff.
  • Visitors are especially pleased with the rooms located in Building B, as there are large balconies with excellent sea views.
  • Separately, guests note the presence of an indoor pool, which helps a lot in cold weather.
  • The sandy beach is always very clean, the beach area is fenced.
  • As for the restaurant, it has excellent cuisine, a large selection of dishes and drinks. Lunches and dinners can be ordered in the room at any time until 23.00.
  • A very convenient time for breakfast, from 8.00 to 11.00, you can get enough sleep, and then go eat.
  • Also, as a positive moment, vacationers note that for breakfast, instead of the "buffet", you can order dishes of your personal choice.
  • Guests are especially pleased with the festivities that the hotel organizes in the winter season from New Year to Christmas.
  • Very satisfied with the opportunity to go on excursions organized by the hotel staff.
  • Guests also like that the Sambia Hotel (Zelenogradsk) is located very close to the city park, which is a great place for walking.
  • For lovers of cycling on the boardwalk, a special bike path is indicated.




Negative reviews

After visiting the Sambia Hotel (Zelenogradsk), on some points, the guests had a negative opinion:

  • Almost all guests consider the room rates to be overpriced.
  • When guests arrive with large suitcases, the absence of elevators in the buildings is especially felt.
  • Some vacationers believe that the hotel has small rooms.
  • Guests also note that the rooms need to be redecorated.
  • There are some visitors who did not like the fact that room cleaning (the noise of vacuum cleaners) and outdoor work (the operation of lawn mowers) begin too early when some guests are still sleeping.
  • Not very convenient mode of operation of the water park. After every 1.5 hours of work - a half-hour break.
  • The rooms of the first category have poor insulation.

Finally

Despite some shortcomings, many vacationers consider the Sambia Hotel located in the city of Zelenogradsk of the Kaliningrad Region a worthy alternative to foreign vacations. It pleases not only the architecture of the hotel, but also the gorgeous views of the Baltic Sea, as well as the magnificent surroundings of Kaliningrad.
