Portable charger for your equipment

A portable charger is a convenient enough gadget designed to charge a tablet, iPad or netbook. It is great for mobile phones and e-books with 19 V DC or less. You can set manual power in the range from 8.4 V to 19 V, then it is useful for any oversized device: iPod, iPhone, walkie-talkie, camcorder, camera, etc.

The portable charger for the phone consists of a box with a board in which it is possible to insert up to 4 rechargeable batteries. They are designed to change failed in time.





Technical features

There are situations when your favorite phone, smartphone or camera discharges at the wrong time. If in the city it is not difficult to solve this problem, then in nature it will not work to recharge your equipment without a special device. Who doesn't like to spend time outdoors? Only avid workaholics, past whom the brightest moments of life pass. Outdoor enthusiasts should always have in their backpack a special device for equipment that will provide an additional charge of energy.





The portable charger allows you to easily charge your device on the go in two ways: via the power grid or through Powertraveller Solargorilla. Adapters are compatible with all models of netbooks, phones, cameras and other devices. The charge indicator automatically turns off after 5 minutes. The portable charger provides additional energy to the work of the equipment. It has the ability to work at any voltage. The device is equipped with a USB-connector for charging simultaneously with the operation of smaller devices. After 2 minutes of inactivity, it automatically turns off.

Kit

The portable charger comes complete with a neoprene case for transportation and storage, adapters for some models of netbooks, mobile phones, sockets. All technical data are indicated in the instructions for use.

A portable charger is a universal mobile battery that can autonomously provide equipment with additional energy. And this is sometimes very important.





For car enthusiasts who do not want to face the problem of a discharged battery, it is recommended to purchase a portable starter-charger. The device will not allow the motorist to remain without transport in severe frosts when the batteries lose their charge. It has a very simple principle of operation. The device plugs into an outlet with a voltage of 220 volts, converts the current and redirects it to a discharged battery, while providing a large current pulse that can start the engine. Charging will be useful when storing a car battery at home, then the device will help maintain the battery for a long time in working condition.

All portable devices are capable of ensuring the start and operation of equipment in a discharged battery situation.
