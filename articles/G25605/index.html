Riu Palace Bavaro 5 * (Dominican Republic): photos and reviews of tourists

Riu Palace Bavaro 5 * is a beautiful new hotel located on the ocean in the area of ​​Arena Gorda Beach. It is part of the large resort complex Rio Resort, whose territory is covered in a lush tropical garden. The complex consists of a series of three-story bungalows and a main building.

A bit about the hotel ...

Riu Palace Bavaro 5 * opened in 2011. It is located just thirty kilometers from the famous Punta Cana Airport and fifty kilometers from Higuey. The peculiarity of the complex is its location - on the first line of the Arena Gorda beach.

The entire Riu Resort hotel consists of five hotels: Riu Palace Bavaro 5 * (Dominican Republic), Macao 5 *, Naiboa 4 *, Punta Cana 5 *, Club Hotel Riu Bambu 5 *. Guests can use absolutely the entire territory of the huge complex.

Number of rooms

The hotel has 610 rooms in its arsenal. All of them are divided into the following categories:

  1. Junior Suite. The area of ​​such apartments is 41 square meters. The room consists of a living room and bedroom. The windows overlook the beautiful territory of the complex and the neighboring street.
  2. Junior Suite Superior. The room consists of a living room and a sleeping area, its area is 41 square meters. The balcony has a view of the garden.
  3. Suite Jacuzzi. This is a more spacious apartment (64 square meters). This room type is located in the main building. They are equipped with a jacuzzi.

All of the above apartments are located in the main building. On the territory there are still three-story villas, which are built closer to the shore and have a lateral or direct sea view.



The rooms are divided into the following categories:

  1. Junior Suite Pool View (Villas). Has a pool view.
  2. Junior Suite Sea View (Villas). It has a side view of the sea.
  3. Junior Suite Jacuzzi Superior (Villas). Room with a bedroom, a living room and a jacuzzi on the terrace.
  4. Suite Jacuzzi (Villas). The apartments are also equipped with a jacuzzi, but do not have a clear separation between the bedroom and the living room.

All rooms in the Riu Palace Bavaro 5 * hotel are equipped with a hairdryer, minibar, telephone, TV, coffee maker, radio, iron and ironing facilities, safe, fan. All apartments have either a balcony or a terrace. The bathroom has a walk-in shower and spa bath.

Room service is available 24 hours a day.

The Riu Palace Bavaro 5 * hotel (Punta Cana) amazes with its luxury to all guests. He really fully corresponds to the category of five stars. I would like to note that the rooms of the complex are very spacious and beautiful. It can be seen that the hotel itself is new, but at the same time it has a perfectly adjusted service system. So, for example, according to reviews of tourists, checking into a room is very fast and without any hitches, even if you arrived earlier than the specified time. In general, Dominicans amaze with their smile and good nature. They always listen carefully and try to help with something.

Punta Cana Attractions

Punta Cana is a large resort in the Dominican Republic, which is considered the best in this country. It is famous for its whitewashed sandy beaches and beautiful coconut palms along the entire coast. The resort has three large parks, museums. Relaxing in the Riu Palace Bavaro 5 *, you should definitely visit local attractions, as well as numerous and varied excursions.



One of them is the beaches of Bovaro. Their coral sand has one feature, it does not heat up in the sun at all, even on the hottest day. Therefore, on the shore you can relax without a sunbed. These places are almost never disturbed by storms, and the reason for this is the most powerful coral reefs located along the entire coast and protecting it from strong waves. For this reason, this wonderful place 25 kilometers from Punta Cana has long been chosen by the owners of the most expensive hotels in the Dominican Republic. However, fashionable buildings do not spoil the local landscapes at all, they are almost invisible due to thickets of coconut palms.

Fans of underwater beauty should definitely visit boat trips, which include not only skiing along the coast, but also an acquaintance with the tropical fauna and flora. Tourists are particularly delighted with sharks and electric ramps.

Manati Park is very popular among tourists. Tropical thickets are of interest, since you can see the most diverse representatives of the local flora and fauna in them. Here guests can see completely unique birds and animals. Tourists are shown performances involving horses and parrots. But the most interesting entertainment is swimming with a dolphin, which has a healing effect.

In Manati, there is a museum where you can get acquainted with the culture of the local population, including Aboriginal people.

Eyes Ecological Park is home to rare animals. You can walk on its territory on your own, or you can book an excursion with a zoology expert guide who will tell you a lot of interesting facts.



The list of must-see sights worth seeing includes a house museum called Ponce de Leon, built over five centuries ago. Here, tourists can immerse themselves in the long history of island civilization. The author of this complex lived in these places before moving to Puerto Rico, where he subsequently took up a political career.

Sightseeing Tours

Active vacationers will be interested in excursions to islands or cities (for example, you can visit Santo Domingo, Saona Island, the city of artists Altos de Chavon). Fans of boat trips should definitely go on a boat to watch whales. This is an amazingly interesting activity. Near Pointe Cana there is the fishing village of El Cortecito, whose restaurants always serve only fresh fish. Unforgettable impressions will be received by vacationers from immersion in the sea depths. The underwater life of the ocean is striking in the brightness of colors.

In Manati Park, you can visit the very unusual Fun Fung Cave.

In addition, many excursions and entertainment (jeep safaris) are organized for vacationers, among which there are the most unusual: cockfights.

The Dominican Republic has many interesting sights to see. Yes, and there are a lot of entertainments for tourists. Therefore, for all those who are tired of relaxing on the ocean, there is an interesting lesson to their liking.
