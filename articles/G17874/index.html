Rest in the Nizhny Novgorod region. The recreation center "Volga", Nizhny Novgorod region. Holiday homes in the Nizhny Novgorod region

The main pearl of the Volga region is called the Nizhny Novgorod region. Pensions, hotel complexes and suburban hotels of Nizhny Novgorod are becoming increasingly popular among the local population and guests of Central Russia. Everyone will be able to find entertainment to their taste, regardless of how used to while away their leisure.

The most popular options include passive hunting, fishing, hiking and sightseeing tours. As a rule, rest in the Nizhny Novgorod region is versatile, informative and interesting. Almost all resorts offer potential customers several types of entertainment services.





Most citizens mistakenly believe that tourist camps operate only in the warm season, and, accordingly, it is boring there in winter. In fact, holiday homes in the Nizhny Novgorod region are hosted year-round; they are equipped with everything necessary for a quality and versatile pastime.

Even the most demanding and sophisticated visitor will feel like in a fairy tale. In winter, the range of entertainment services is also diverse: snowmobiles, skiing, paintball, ice skating. The cost of living directly depends on the choice of a boarding house: territorial location, conditions, infrastructure and food. People with low incomes will also be able to pick up a lot of suitable objects with ideal conditions. We suggest you familiarize yourself with the best options.

Comfortable recreation center "The Seagull"





The Nizhny Novgorod region is famous for its unique places and objects, such as the suburban complex "The Seagull". It can rightly be called one of the best. The hotel is located right on the banks of the beautiful Oka River, 45 km from the center of Nizhny Novgorod, surrounded by a picturesque pine forest. One location will give a lot of positive emotions, and an ecological clean microclimate will help to heal health. A harmonious combination of developed infrastructure, wildlife and friendly atmosphere sets in a favorable mood. Have fun with friends, family and children.

Accommodation

Rooms of different class are concentrated in two buildings of the complex: "Pine" and "Ship". Absolutely all rooms are made in a modern style, each is equipped with new furniture, a split system and appliances. From the windows, guests can enjoy the thicket and the river. For fastidious and especially demanding tourists, superior rooms are offered, such as "Oriental", "Presidential", "Dandelion".

Services, food and entertainment





Holidays in the Nizhny Novgorod region on the basis of "The Seagull" will be remembered for the atmosphere of the holiday, hospitality and interesting leisure. Active clients will be able to freely visit the sports and entertainment center (opens at the end of May 2014), take bike and horse rides, play collective games. In winter - sledding, ice skating, skiing. In the summer - swim in the river, have picnics, sing karaoke, dance in the club.

Every day fun concerts are held with live music and performances. In the middle of a pine forest, a wooden house was built in the native Russian style with a bathhouse, billiards and karaoke. A fitness center with a pool filled with ozonated water is located on site.

Available infrared saunas, hammam. Created conditions for corporate pastime (conference rooms, meeting rooms). For children, a play area with numerous slides and carousels has been built, there is a separate safe pool and a development club.





As for the power system, it is up to par. Cozy restaurants, cafes and bars are literally scattered around the perimeter of the spacious territory. Friendly staff welcomes guests everywhere. The menu is varied, consists of European, national cuisine. The buffet will surprise with an abundance of confectionery and seafood.

Pension "Dream"

Literally in the bosom of pristine nature is the sanatorium "Dream". The recreation center (Nizhny Novgorod region) is a huge upscale complex with 8 buildings. Convenient location: in a pine forest near the Serezha River, away from the bustle and dusty freeways - attracts a large number of tourists. Attentive service, natural healing measures combined with professionalism and responsiveness of employees - this is a 100% guarantee of full healing from negative emotions and ailments.





Infrastructure and accommodation

Corporate parties are regularly held at the Mechta Sanatorium, seminars, health days, business conferences are held, and New Year holidays are also organized. The recreational and entertainment complex includes several saunas with spacious pools, a gym, table tennis and a solarium. In winter, the following leisure activities are offered: toboggan run, ice arena, ski run.

Tourists are accommodated in comfortable rooms with their own kitchen area (not all). For those who combine leisure with work, technically equipped rooms are offered. At the complete disposal of customers there is a karaoke club, a billiard room, an evening bar. On the ground floor, an art exhibition of Razdumin is regularly held. Rest in the Nizhny Novgorod region in the boarding house "Dream" will not leave anyone indifferent and will be remembered for a long time.





Country resort "Volga"

This is one of the most expensive and elite business centers. Frequent guests of the boarding house are high-ranking officials and businessmen. The recreation center "Volga" (Nizhny Novgorod region) is located 14 km from the central part of the city on the bank of the river. Volga. The sanatorium has its own beach, a health spa, a comfortable hotel, the Lukomorye park, moorings and parking for small vessels, motor ships. Accommodation conditions have been created for different categories of guests. For sophisticated customers will be offered a two-room presidential suite with a jacuzzi. Available budget options for rooms with a balcony and appliances.

For corporate leisure

Good conditions for organizing trainings and negotiations will be offered to business travelers. For these purposes, there are spacious halls of different sizes. In each you will find audio and video equipment, microphones and much more for a quality presentation. If necessary, you can use the services of staff.

Pension Volga - Wellness Center

Experienced and qualified specialists working in this salon will select an individual treatment program. You will be offered hot tubs, a Russian bath and a cedar barrel. Therapeutic procedures include herbal medicine, balneotherapy, thermal procedures, lymphatic drainage, algal and mud wraps.





Those who wish can visit the beauty salon at any time. Rest in the Nizhny Novgorod region, namely in the health center, will give harmony, a sense of peace and complete relaxation. Positioning atmosphere: melodic music, pleasant aroma of essential oils - restores emotional state and relieves stress. The guests will have a unique opportunity to free themselves from negative energy, recharge with positive emotions and come into contact with wildlife.

Recreation centers of the Nizhny Novgorod region reviews from the local population and guests are constantly receiving enthusiastic. Such a trip simply cannot fail to please. Today the pearl of the Volga region at the peak of popularity. From the many options you can choose the most suitable and affordable.
