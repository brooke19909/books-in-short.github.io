New buildings in Sertolovo. Apartments in Sertolovo from the builder (new building)

New Sertolovo buildings have become a worthy alternative to metropolitan complexes. They are not inferior in quality and level of comfort. Of course, many dream of acquiring their own housing in St. Petersburg, but cannot afford such an expensive acquisition. And some are completely tired of the hustle and bustle of the metropolis, exhaust gases and dust and dream of getting an apartment in a suburb of the northern capital. And in that and in another case, an excellent solution to the problem will be Sertolovo new buildings. As part of this material, we will review the most worthy options.

About Sertolovo

Before starting to consider new buildings in Sertolovo from the developer, I want to say a few words about the city, the advantages and disadvantages of living here. So, from a geographical point of view, Sertolovo is a municipality of the Vsevolozhsk district of the Leningrad region. In order to assess the level and quality of life here, it is better to refer to the reviews of those who managed to live in the city for a significant time period.





Benefits

The main and indisputable advantage of the city is ecology: there is a forest, a river nearby, there are no harmful industries in the city, therefore it is ideal for families with small children. Judging by the reviews of residents, in Sertolovo, a good social environment, there are no homeless people on the streets, the level of crime and crime is minimal. In general, it is a quiet and calm town with a developed social infrastructure.

Qualified specialists work in kindergartens and schools, in the city there is an excellent clinic and hospital, as well as a sports and art school. Some residents complain about interruptions in public transport: in the evening it is simply impossible to get to St. Petersburg. But if you have at least one car in your family, the problem is quickly solved.

Real estate in Sertolovo, new buildings in particular, are currently quite in demand. And all because the opportunity arises to purchase an apartment in a picturesque environmentally friendly area, without losing access to infrastructure. And all this with significant savings for your budget.

LCD "New Sertolovo"

I would like to start the review of new buildings in Sertolovo with the residential complex "New Sertolovo", which has become the hallmark of the city. The project is commissioned in five phases, the final stage of construction is planned for the 4th quarter of 2019. The developer is KVS. The new microdistrict, represented by 13 buildings in 4-5 floors, is being built in the north-west of Sertolovo. All houses are built using monolithic brick technology. According to the project, they will line up in the shape of a square, dividing the entire territory into closed courtyards.





The main feature of the project is the park: it will become a favorite place for walking and recreation for all residents of the complex. Two kindergartens, a comprehensive school, playgrounds and sports grounds, walking and bicycle paths will be built on its territory. The developer offered a variety of planning solutions, focusing on the comfort of each resident. The apartments in Sertolovo from the developer in the new buildings turned out to be small, affordable (from 58,000 per m 2 ), but at the same time quite cozy and comfortable. Take a closer look at the complex.

LCD "Clean Stream"

New buildings in Sertolovo (Leningrad Oblast) impress with their diversity. If you value comfort, pay attention to the LCD "Clean Stream", which offers apartments in comfort class. The complex will occupy part of the recreation area. Low-rise buildings, designed in the European style with panoramic glazing - the main feature of the project. This is a great option for those who want to enjoy the benefits of country life, forget about anthill houses.





New Sertolovo buildings for the most part boast excellent ecology. But only in the territory of the residential complex "Clean Stream" does a natural stream flow with clean water. A pond with an equipped promenade will become a favorite place for walking and recreation for all residents of the complex. As for infrastructure, residents will be able to use all the facilities of the city of Sertolovo. The project provides for the availability of its own infrastructure, but its facilities are not included primarily in the construction. All car owners can count on multi-level parking.

The choice of customers are offered studios, one and two bedroom apartments in Sertolovo (new buildings) from 27 to 50 square meters. The combined bathroom helps expand the living space. The cost of real estate in this complex starts at 57,000 per m 2 .

LCD "Golden Domes"

Not far from Vostochno-Vyborgskoye Shosse, in the Chernaya Rechka microdistrict, RosStroyInvest is building an excellent residential complex - Golden Domes. The first phase was commissioned in 2016, and the completion of construction is scheduled for 2019. 30 4-storey buildings erected using monolithic technology are the basis of the project. As an architectural solution, the traditional Russian style with radical elements: bay windows and turrets was chosen.





All the necessary infrastructure facilities will appear on the territory of the complex: kindergartens, a school, a clinic, a shopping and entertainment center, a children's park with attractions, sports grounds with exercise machines, a fitness center. The territory will be ennobled, decorated with flower beds and flower beds. A round-the-clock security and video surveillance system will ensure the safety of every resident.

A choice of buyers are offered one-, two- and three-room apartments from 33 to 73 square meters. The variety of planning decisions is also impressive: some apartments have a trapezoidal shape, others have a rectangular shape with a beveled wall. Apartments in Sertolovo from the builder (new building) are rented with a pre-finished finish, if desired, the builder can make a "turnkey" finish. The cost of apartments starts from 54,000 per m 2 .





LCD "Reflection"

And here is the project of a very young developer - the company "Floors of growth". The 16-story building, represented by three sections, immediately attracted the attention of buyers. Construction is carried out using modern materials and innovative technologies. Each section will be equipped not only with a passenger, but also with a high-speed freight elevator with a loading capacity of up to 700 kg, modern intercoms, as well as a garbage chute with a washing system.





The project does not provide for the construction of infrastructure facilities, but the residents of the complex will certainly not feel their absence. Within walking distance from the house are all the necessary shops, cafes and restaurants, city schools are not overloaded, so arranging children there will not be a problem.

A choice of buyers is offered 414 apartments of European layout. Almost every one of them has a pantry for storing things. The apartments are rented with a pre-finished finish, if the client wishes, the developer can complete the "turnkey" finish. The cost of apartments starts from 56,000 per m 2 .
