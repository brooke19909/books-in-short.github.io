Waterpark "Peterland-2" in St. Petersburg: address, reviews

Waterland "Peterland-2" is equipped with the latest world trends in the entertainment industry. This is a four-story building, which includes a collection of all kinds of fashionable branded clothing stores, restaurants, large children's entertainment centers, cinemas, sports grounds, terraces overlooking the Gulf of Finland. And all this splendor can be found in one place. This is what impresses most visitors to the city, because thanks to such a complex you can have fun in full.





Waterland "Peterland-2" in St. Petersburg is able to surprise every guest with an incredibly interesting interior and fancy design solutions. A comfortable environment will set a person to complete relaxation and receive moral and physical satisfaction. Visitors are also offered a huge selection of all kinds of products.

general description





On the three floors of the building are the most fashionable stores of world brands, namely: H&M, MANGO, Calvin Klein, Guess, Tommy Hilfiger, Lacoste, Cacharel, US Polo, MAC and other equally popular brands. The latest fashion trends around the world can be easily found in these walls.

If guests decided to have a tasty meal and chat with friends, then here you can find great restaurants and cafes. The service is at a high level, and the food is sure to please. Here you can find the cuisines of the best countries of the whole world, under one roof you can find dishes that are enjoyed in different parts of the world. On one floor you can choose for yourself a place that will appeal to any person. It has everything from German restaurants to the world's most popular coffee houses. And, of course, where without a quick snack. On the third floor there is a food court where you can quickly eat and watch the indescribably beautiful panorama of the Gulf of Finland.

Baths and saunas in the water park "Peterland-2"

In the water park you can visit the bathhouse or sauna for every taste. Here they are offered a huge variety. Thus, you can take a steam bath in the Finnish sauna, in the Japanese, Egyptian, Turkish or Russian bath, infrared sauna. This is not the whole list of procedures that a water park can offer.

Bars and Restaurants

On the territory of the wonderful bar "Octopus", which can offer a total of 30 seats. There is also a bistro cafe for another 100 seats. The visitors of the restaurant are offered a huge assortment of fresh drinks, smoothies, non-alcoholic cocktails, as well as a huge amount of fresh juices from a variety of exotic fruits .

Bistro

The menu of this institution provided for the culinary preferences of even the most demanding guests. Here you can watch a huge number of popular salads in classic cooking, there are a variety of snacks, hot dogs, baguette sandwiches and much, much more.

In the warm season, all cafes and restaurants open their summer verandas, terraces overlook the shores of the Gulf of Finland, thanks to this, eating becomes more interesting. The plans of the project include the opening of the beach line, where a huge number of bars and restaurants will be located.



Over the weekend, Peterland 2 offers its guests a variety of entertainment programs. So, you can attend concerts, arrange a real celebration for yourself, participate in many competitions and charity events, enjoy fashion shows, admire exhibitions and presentations, and also become a member of the active life of the entertainment center.

The pearl of this extravaganza is one of the largest thematic water parks in Russia. Unique is the dome of the building itself, which allows visitors to enjoy the sun's rays and sunbathe throughout the year. Here are a large number of various attractions, of varying complexity. In addition to all this, there is even entertainment for diving enthusiasts who can take certain lessons in a specially organized school. You can scuba dive under the supervision of a professional trainer.

Thoughtful events for the youngest guests. Thus, for the kids there are slides that allow you to safely descend, there are numerous installations for creating artificial waves and much more. The territory also has a spa, where you can choose a huge number of procedures and programs for your body, restore strength and improve physical and emotional state.

Peterland is able to satisfy even the most sophisticated adventurers. Here you can relax and find new thrills. Thus, on the territory of the water park there is a huge number of slides that will give a truly thrill. For example, a blue slide. It is she who enjoys great popularity among visitors, since the descent from it occurs from the bottom up. There is an orange slide. It is made in the form of a spiral, and therefore here you can feel all the unpredictability of flight. A green slide is a whole combination of a variety of slides that will also give a thrill. Red Hill - an attraction for true fans of extreme sports, has a large angle.

In addition, there are bars and cafes in the water park. Here you can enjoy healthy cool cocktails and other drinks.

Location





Shopping and entertainment center is located on one of the picturesque corners of the coast of the Gulf of Finland. Nearby is a park where you can spend an unforgettable walk. Thanks to such a good location, you can reach your destination both by air and by water. The administration plans to open a special water area, as well as a helicopter . The address of the water park "Peterland-2" in St. Petersburg: st. Seaside, 72.

As for pricing, a visit for an adult will cost about 1,000 rubles. , for a child 700 rubles. On weekdays, discounts are made here. In addition, for your family and friends you can purchase special gift certificates and present for some kind of holiday.

Why was the water park closed?





Many people ask if the Peterland-2 water park is working or not. Unfortunately, the entertainment complex stopped its work due to the loss-making of the project itself. The water park practically did not bring profit, and the cost of maintaining it exceeded the income. But this is not the only reason why the Peterland 2 water park was closed . In 2012, an accident occurred here and a criminal case was instituted. Such moments negatively affect further development.
