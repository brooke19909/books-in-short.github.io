Wonderful hotel "Ukraine". Moscow address known

The best among the best not only in Russia but also in Europe is Ukraine Hotel, whose address in Moscow is known to many, as it is located in one of the seven famous "Stalinist skyscrapers". Emphasizing the grandeur of the ancient city, this building rises high up over the Moskva River. It is considered a landmark of the capital of the XX century.





The location of the hotel "Ukraine"

In order for each guest who settles in the hotel to enjoy incredibly beautiful views, to see the solemnity and impressiveness of the capital, the Ukraine Hotel (address in Moscow: Kutuzovsky Prospekt 2/1) is located in a very interesting place, which is considered its most important advantage.

Directly past its walls are streaming cars along Novy Arbat and Kutuzovsky Prospekt. Located in the center of the metropolis, in the midst of the Moskva River, in the Dorogomilovo district, the Ukraina Hotel (the address in Kiev is the Kievskaya metro, you can navigate this way) is facing the river with its facade.

From the building down to the shore you can go down an elegant park, where in the 70s of the last century the monument to T. Shevchenko was opened. Hotel guests love to stroll along the beautiful embankment lined with granite, approach the water through comfortable stairs, walk to the Novoarbatsky bridge.

In the immediate vicinity are government buildings, embassies of some countries, shopping and entertainment centers and business centers. In just a few minutes by metro you can get to the Bolshoi Theater, the Tretyakov Gallery, Red Square and the Kremlin.





Some interesting facts

A curious story is the origin of the idea of "Stalin's skyscrapers" in Moscow. It appeared after the return of one of the delegations from New York. They admired this overseas city, its skyscrapers. And then it was decided to build high-rise buildings in Moscow, so to speak, so as not to be left behind.

A team of specialists was created under the direction of Arkady Mordvinov, head of the Academy of Architecture of the Soviet Union. Outstanding and talented Vyacheslav Oltarzhevsky became one of the leading designers. He carefully studied the technology of erecting multi-story buildings, as he was involved in the development and took part in the construction of buildings for temporary residence. The hotel building project in Dorogomilovo (as it was originally planned to call the future hotel) became one of the most interesting things for him.

In 1953, began to develop a project and construction, which involved more than two thousand workers. And on May 25, 1957, a beautiful hotel opened its doors to its first visitors.
