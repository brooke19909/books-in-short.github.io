How to choose a digital oscilloscope. Digital Oscilloscope Benefits

A digital oscilloscope is a device that allows you to monitor electronic signals. With it, scientists can make various measurements of parameters. In this case, the oscillations can be visually monitored or immediately recorded on any medium. Using a special converter, it is additionally possible to study non-electrical processes.

When observing fluctuations, scientists look at the amplitude of the signal, as well as its duration. Old oscilloscopes worked by deflecting a light beam. At the same time, there was a special paper tape for recording vibrations. In turn, the writing element was ink. In modern models, the electron beam is focused on the screen and as a result, a picture with data appears.





Oscilloscope Benefits

First of all, the possibility of freezing the image should be noted. You can do this at any time, which is very convenient. It is also necessary to mention the high accuracy of measurement. At the same time, the bandwidth is quite wide. Regardless of the scan speed, the image on the screen is always clear and bright. Until the starting moment, the signal is already shown on the display.

It is possible to detect impulse noise during operation. Measurement of signal parameters occurs in automatic mode. If necessary, the digital oscilloscope can be connected to a personal computer. You can use a printer to print data. The final signal is processed not only mathematically, but also statistically. Additionally, there are tools for self-diagnosis.

How to choose a quality oscilloscope?

First of all, attention should be paid to the average sampling rate. This parameter must be between 400 - 600 MV / s. Additionally, good oscilloscopes are capable of making equivalent sampling. Screen resolution should be no less than 480 by 234 pixels. The frequency counter is only six-digit. Another device must support CP programming commands. Of the functions of the oscilloscope, you should check the data recording, as well as playback. Additionally, a limit testing program must be installed.
