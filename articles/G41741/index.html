Contactless Bank Card: reviews

Cards with contactless payment technology have been issued in Russia since 2008. However, this banking product is not widely distributed among users. To date, about 50 domestic credit organizations issue such cards. Statistical studies provided data on the availability of contactless bank cards in only 2 million Russians.

general information

Financial and credit organizations strive to keep up with the times, so they offer customers a variety of new products and convenient financial services. Many of them are gaining wide popularity, others are proving to be ineffective. Cards with contactless payment technology have proven their reliability and consistency, which is why they are widely used by customers.

A contactless card is a type of plastic card that is used in an access control system, as well as in payment systems. The peculiarity of the card is that information is read by the device without direct contact. An electromagnetic signal is transmitted between the antennas between the card and the receiver. In the financial sector, this technology has gained widespread popularity. In large cities, many customers pay by contactless credit cards. Making contactless payments is possible using various modern gadgets. The article will provide complete information about the features of contactless technology, as well as its undeniable advantages and some disadvantages.

Technology features

Today, contactless payment technology is used everywhere, but not everyone knows how this system works. The technology is based on the principle of the exchange of electromagnetic waves between the card and the terminal. This allows you to read information from a distance without additional contact and enter a pin code. The RFID standard , which is supported by bank cards, is the most popular way to automatically identify objects. An RFID tag on a bank card includes several microcircuits that process data and an oscillatory circuit.

Due to the electromagnetic signal from the reader, electromagnetic current appears in the antenna and emits a high-frequency signal. NFC always emits an electromagnetic field that feeds the chip on the card for data exchange. The NFC system allows you to determine the physical standard for the interaction of connected devices and a set of protocols. Since the signal exchange takes place at a small distance, the card does not need to be removed from the wallet. If payment is made from a wallet, you need to make sure that there are no other cards with similar technology. Otherwise, payment will be made from the card with which the signal will pass the fastest. A contactless card is becoming more popular, there are different reviews about a bank payment card, however, many users say that this product is simple and convenient to use.

Principle of operation

A special chip with an antenna is built into the contactless bank card, which transmits data about the payment made over the air. In a store that accepts this type of card, a contactless terminal must be installed. To make a payment using a contactless credit card, it is enough to attach "plastic" to the terminal.

Payment takes place instantly, and for the operation you do not need to enter a PIN code (when making purchases up to 1000 rubles). You can issue a bank card in MasterCard (PayPass) or VISA (payWave technology). The first option in the lines of domestic banks is much more common. This banking product is presented in the form of credit, debit, co-branding, classic, premium and standard cards. The terms of service and the purchase price of such a product are determined by the issuing bank.

Card Security

The owners of such cards are protected not only from theft, but also from the banal photographing of "plastic". Plastic cards are protected from theft by screaming. The built-in RFID tag contains data on the expiration date, information on the card number. Unlike conventional plastic cards, a product with contactless technology does not store information about the CVV code. Therefore, to perform subsequent operations, a dynamic one-time CVV is formed.

Despite the fact that contactless "plastic" is protected much better than conventional cards, the Russians stole more than 2 million rubles last year. This is due to the actions of scammers who use home-made RFID readers. Attackers read dynamic CVV from cards from visitors to shopping centers. After that, the scammers created their clones and quickly calculated the received money at their discretion in the nearest stores. The deceived cardholders who tried to pay for the purchase faced the problem of blocking access to the account. This is because the system automatically found a previously used contactless CVV. Useful Tips

In order to ensure the long life of the card and the safety of funds, the owners must comply with the following rules:
