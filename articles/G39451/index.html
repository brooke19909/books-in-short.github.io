Guest Rooms

For those who came to rest in Side, Pimar Beach Resort provides 536 rooms, of which 9 are family rooms, 22 are connection rooms, 36 are suites and 465 are standard. There are 4 rooms equipped for disabled guests. The rooms have carpet, bath or shower, hairdryer. Each room has air conditioning, TV (there is a Russian-language channel), telephone. There is a minibar (for a fee), a safe (for a fee). Each room has a balcony with magnificent views of the beach and pool. A baby cot is available upon request. Cleaning is done every day. Pets are not allowed.



Dining at the hotel

Like most hotels in Side, Pimar Beach Resort provides its guests with meals according to the All Inclusive system , which is valid from 10.00 to midnight. It is produced in the main restaurant of the hotel, and a buffet is served. Imported spirits, ice cream and freshly squeezed juice are available at an additional cost. On request, breakfast can be served directly in the room. In addition, there are 2 more restaurants on site that offer a wide selection of dishes, including Italian, Chinese and Turkish. There is a la carte (advance booking) and 6 bars. In restaurants for little vacationers highchairs are provided.

Beach

Hotel "Pimar Beach" (Side) has its own sand and pebble beach, located immediately at the borders of its territory. Its length is 200 meters. There is a wooden pier. Entrance to the sea is carried out on small pebbles, so swimming is recommended to be carried out in special shoes so as not to injure your legs. Beach equipment is provided free of charge, with the exception of changing beach towels.

Useful information for vacationers

In Side, Pimar Beach Resort is one of those hotels that has excellent infrastructure for a great vacation. For younger guests there are 3 separate pools.
