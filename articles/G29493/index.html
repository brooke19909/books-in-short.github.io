From the Internet

So, the first method that allows you to install Skype on your phone is to download a special installation file to your computer. After that, you will have to drop it on a tablet or smartphone and install it in the system.

An important point here is the compatibility of the program and platform of your phone. In fact, because of this seemingly trifle, users very often encounter the fact that they cannot install Skype on the phone. That is, either the process itself is stopped, or the launch of the application itself.

Go on the Internet and find there a special installation file for your phone model. Now it‚Äôs not so difficult. After that, using a special cord, connect the gadget to the computer, and then download the program to it. Now find it on your smartphone and start the installation. That's all. A few minutes and the process will be completed. True, this is not the only scenario.

Google play

You can also try using a very interesting and simple method. Consider how to install Skype on Android (the phone on this platform is best suited for these purposes). All that is required is to go from the gadget to the Internet and open the Google Play application. This is where you can download yourself a lot of programs, and then install them directly on your smartphone. Convenient, fast and safe.



As soon as you log in, look for Skype. It should be noted immediately that on older platforms you will not be able to carry out the idea. After all, you can install Skype on your phone only if you have Android from version 2.8 and higher. Found what you were looking for? Then click "Download." Wait a while, and then find the installation file. Run it and the installation process will begin. A few minutes - and you can work. Skype is installed on the phone. Nothing difficult, right?

direct link

Our next method is now far from the safest and most harmless. Nevertheless, many users very often use it. This option is based on the transfer of the so-called direct link to download the installation file from the computer directly to the phone.

You will need to find a specialized service, and then find the necessary application on it. Next, get a link to it, and then automatically send to your gadget. By clicking on it from your smartphone, you can safely install Skype on your phone. So this is something like a hybrid of the past two methods. True, now direct links often contain a variety of viruses that do not affect the system of your gadget in the best way. Be careful and do not trust suspicious sites. In addition, remember that the installation of "Skype" is absolutely free.

Use

So, now we will begin the most interesting step. We managed to install Skype on the phone, now we need to make it work. Take your smartphone, and then go into the program. Where exactly it will be located, as a rule, depends on the model of the gadget.



You will see a familiar login window. In it you will have to type your login and password, and then click on "Connection". Before that, look at the settings and check your Internet connection. For Wi-Fi, you will have to enable this option. If you decide to use the Internet from your phone, then you can safely connect to the network immediately.

After logging in, you will find yourself in the familiar contact window. You can choose yourself an interlocutor, and then write messages to him. Video calling as well as voice communication will be configured by default.
