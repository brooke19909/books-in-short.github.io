"Boneko" - humidifiers for health and comfort

Health and well-being often depend directly on the microclimate in the home and workplace. Compliance with the rules is not enough: ventilate the room, air-conditioning or heat. In addition to air temperature, relative humidity is an important parameter. Many people know about this, so in the heating season they try to spray plants more often, do wet cleaning and cover warm batteries with wet towels.





But such a lesson is very troublesome, time-consuming. Therefore, modern manufacturers of HVAC equipment create advanced models of air humidifiers. Boneko is one of the most popular companies in Europe and Russia.

Brief information about the manufacturer

This is a Swiss company that produces climate systems and appliances for the home and businesses. Currently in Russia there are several models at various prices.

Boneco humidifiers have a simple style. They are more suitable for offices and houses with a modern design. The company mainly produces ultrasonic types of devices.

Why do you need a humidifier

So what is a moisturizer for and what can it give a person? As mentioned earlier, indoor air humidity (along with temperature) is important for the human body. In addition, plants and animals also need optimal conditions.





Dry air (less than 40% humidity) can cause the following troubles for people:

  • the appearance of causeless cough;
  • drainage of the mucous membranes of the respiratory tract and eyes;
  • frequent colds;
  • dust allergy;
  • dry skin.

There may be other symptoms. Plants also suffer, especially those that love moisture. Therefore, it is worth considering the “Boneko” air humidifiers and choosing the model that is suitable for the parameters and price.

What moisturizers are there today

Consider popular models on the Russian market. Let's start with the budget U7146. This is a compact humidifier designed to work in rooms with an area of ​​not more than 20 square meters. m. As a reservoir, you can use a regular plastic bottle.

Further, with an average cost (from 5000 to 7000 rubles), you can list:

  • U201A;
  • S200;
  • S250;
  • U600.

These models not only humidify the air in rooms with an area of ​​over 35 square meters. m, but also have the function of aromatization. That is, if desired, you can drip into the water tank a few drops of essential oil for aromatherapy at home. Most models have a backlight.

It is worth mentioning separately about the Boneko air humidifier 7135 (its cost is about 8,000 rubles). This unusual design model is able to cope with its task in a huge room (60 sq. M.).

There are more expensive and powerful humidifiers: S450, U350, U700.

Best and worst reviews

The highest ratings have the previously mentioned 7135. It differs not only in design and practicality, but also in quality. It copes with its task superbly. The device can be turned on for the whole night.





Almost all models have a backlight, a water level indicator and even a built-in digital hygrometer. The Boneko air humidifiers have almost the same reviews:

  • almost silent;
  • copes with the task;
  • uninterrupted.

But there is a major drawback - maintainability. That is, in case of leaks or a malfunction with the device, you will have to purchase a new humidifier.

What to choose?

Often, customers can not decide on a model. It is very important to consider:

  • room / office area;
  • room design;
  • the amount of time for the operation of the device (constantly or only at certain hours);
  • Do you need flavoring?
  • built-in tank or installation of plastic containers.

It often happens that a plastic bottle spoils the appearance of the room, so it is preferable to choose a device with an integrated tank. But if the appearance is not fundamental, then you can buy a budget option.

Boneco humidifiers are one of the most popular among other manufacturers and are in demand.
