Sberbank currency contribution for individuals

Sberbank of Russia offers individuals an impressive amount of deposit programs. You can open an account both in rubles and in foreign currency. Most of the deposits are designed for euros or US dollars, but it is possible to keep less common banknotes - francs, yen and pounds sterling. In the article, we consider the conditions that Sberbank puts forward for deposit programs. Currency deposits, despite a reduced percentage of remuneration, remain popular among the population.

Types of deposits

Sberbank always offers its current and potential customers a wide range of deposits with different conditions. This allows you to open a deposit account for different segments of the population, choosing for yourself the most acceptable program. All existing foreign currency deposits can be divided into three groups:

  1. Urgent - differ in the duration of the transaction. The deposit opens for a short period (from 3 months).
  2. Long-term - imply the conclusion of a contract for a long period. The capital is placed up to 3 years, after which a deposit can be extended.
  3. Online To open them, you do not need to visit the bank, just go to the online office via the Internet and send an application for opening a deposit.

Each of the programs has a number of advantages and disadvantages for a particular client. To place capital with the greatest benefit, you should clearly define the goals and the desired result.

Features and Benefits

The foreign currency deposit at Sberbank for individuals is modern, profitable and convenient. By placing money in a deposit account, the client gets the opportunity not only to save it, but also to win on interest and exchange rate fluctuations. Some programs include replenishment and / or partial withdrawal of funds.





A foreign currency deposit opens up the following possibilities for its owner:

  • receiving money transfers in foreign signs;
  • money conversion;

  • cash withdrawal;

It is convenient to make any deposit actions using online services. It is also envisaged to automatically renew the contract on the same conditions.
