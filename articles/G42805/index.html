Phone Review Fly 449

It seems to be just recently that we enjoyed black and white phones. They were large and not very comfortable. Of course, one should not even talk about their functions. However, technologies are updated every year, and now manufacturers of phones delight us with original models. Great attention is paid to size, design and content.

Smartphones are a kind of mini-computer, which is currently difficult to do without. Thanks to such devices, you can not only make calls and write SMS messages, but also go online, no matter where the person is. One of these new devices is the Fly 449 phone. Its indisputable advantage is that at a fairly low price the device is in no way inferior to more expensive copies. The novelty has relevant technical equipment, a modern design and a large set of different functions. It is worth noting that this particular model can be purchased by children, since its cost, in case of loss or breakdown, will not become critical for parents.

So, let's review the new smartphone brand Fly.



Description

The Fly IQ 449 smartphone is new. The manufacturer equipped it with a dual-core processor, the frequency of which is 1 GHz. The screen is 4 inches in size, with a resolution of 480x800, one camera is 3.2 megapixels and is located on the rear panel. However, it is worth noting that it is of poor quality, there are few color effects, and the image is fuzzy and blurry. The smartphone can use 2 SIM cards.

Color - black and white. The case is a classic type, plastic. The coating is very pleasant to the touch, and, most importantly, that it is not scratched. The back panel is rubberized plastic. Touch buttons. On top there is a micro USB connector, as well as a standard 3.5 mm headphone port.

At the bottom are three touch buttons, equipped with a backlight, and a speaker. Also, the Fly 449 has a compartment for a memory card, located under the back cover. The display is protected by a plastic coating, the sensor is sensitive, but the screen is designed for only two touches. The brightness margin is acceptable, the viewing angles are good, but there is no automatic adjustment sensor.

Performance

This smartphone is equipped with a Spreadtrum SC7710G processor, which has a frequency of 1 GHz and two cores. The amount of internal memory is very small, only 20 MB, so it is best to use RAM in the amount of 512 MB.

The operating system is Android 4.1. Fly has made several improvements, so the phone does an excellent job with all the features. The OS works quickly, without freezes.



Features

At the top of the screen there is a notification bar and with shortcuts to access the main functions of the smartphone. Gallery looks like sorted folders. Fly 449 is designed to work with two SIM cards. To switch them, there is a pop-up menu at the top, in which the necessary operator is selected. In the call log they are displayed at the same time, but if necessary, the report of incoming numbers can be viewed separately. If you make a conversation on one SIM card, and at this time a call comes in to another, then for the caller the subscriber is at that time out of the access zone.

Applications

The smartphone is equipped with a player that is not distinguished by outstanding qualities, there is also a radio. The quality of the program for watching videos is limited in its capabilities, so it is better to use a separate player.

The Fly 449 model has standard applications that can be found on any phone: calendar, alarm clock, calculator, voice recorder, there is a built-in Google Play store, Bluetooth 2.1 and Wi-Fi.

The smartphone can work up to 5 hours in talk mode and up to 200 hours in quiet mode. Battery capacity 1350 mAh.

A few words about the dynamics

The speaker is good, but it is better not to use the volume at the maximum level, as wheezing can occur. The ringtone is loud, but the vibration is almost not felt due to its weakness. The following modes are set for ringing tones: vibro, polyphonic ringtone, and MP3 format.



Equipment

The complete set of the device Fly 449 includes:

  • telephone;
  • battery;
  • charging;
  • USB cable
  • headphones;
  • instructions for the smartphone.
