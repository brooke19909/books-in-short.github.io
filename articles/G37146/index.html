The beauty of the Balkans. Where are the Cursed Mountains?

Have you ever wondered why Montenegro, a country with delightfully beautiful nature, was given such a strange name? It turns out that the mystery lies in the mountains, which, due to the constant accumulation of clouds, appear black. Therefore, the answer to the question of where the Cursed Mountains are located seems quite understandable - of course, in Montenegro.



Here is just a paradise for climbers, near the mountain ranges located ski resorts. The mountains of the mountains, Sinevina, Belasitsa, Komova, Durmitor - all of them attract attention and amaze with beauty, because the mountains in the Balkans have special magnetism. But nevertheless they fade in comparison with the popularity of the Damned Mountains, which in 2010 received the status of a national park. Now part of the mountain in the Plava area is under protection.

Where the Damned Mountains are located is very beautiful, this massif is considered the richest in the Balkans. To date, more than 500 species of plants have been recorded there, and in the Kosovo part of the massif, scientists counted about 180 species of birds, 200 of mushrooms, 130 species of butterflies and 60 endemic species of insects. All this allows us to understand how rich the natural world of Prokletie is.



If we talk about the poorly studied territory of Montenegro, then this mountain is it. Alps of the Balkans - this is also called this massif. Its name evokes various associations and sometimes leads to fantastic thoughts. Where the Cursed Mountains are, at first glance, there is nothing unusual that could indicate some kind of curse or magic. It turns out that the whole thing is in the form of a mountain range, which resembles the sharp fangs of an evil and bloodthirsty creature. That is why legends are often composed that innumerable treasures are reserved in the bowels of the mountains, which will be left only to the brave. Prokletye really stores wealth - these are glaciers created due to centuries-old accumulations of snow, glacial lakes, clean springs with crystal water, waterfalls and mountain rivers.

Many professional climbers dream with their own eyes to see where the Cursed Mountains are. To conquer their peaks is not so simple, and the point here is not at all in height. Massifs amaze with the bizarre nature of their outlines, so only very brave and hardy people with good physical training can conquer them. Everyone who decides to climb to the top of Prokletie will be rewarded with a view of beautiful landscapes.



The Balkan Alps are located on the border of Montenegro, Serbia and Albania. Of particular interest to travelers is the glacial lake Plavskoye, located at the foot of Prokletia. On the territory of Serbia, there is the Monastery of Vysokie Decani, ancient murals have been preserved in it to this day, therefore this building is listed by UNESCO. For all fans of paragliding, mountaineering and just outdoor activities, this is a real paradise. That is why in this corner of Montenegro you can meet tourists from all over Europe. A trip to the Cursed Mountains will bring a lot of positive emotions and impressions. Clean and fresh air, water from crystal springs, beautiful nature will fill the body with strength and vigor.
