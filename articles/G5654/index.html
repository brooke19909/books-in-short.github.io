AEG (washing machine): customer reviews

What reviews does the AEG washing machine receive from buyers? Note that this brand has a good reputation. Domestic consumers have more than once had the opportunity to verify the reliability and quality of this product. However, there are also not very successful models from this manufacturer, which received many complaints from the owners. Let's take a closer look at what AEG brand products are, and also look at reviews of some models.

Features of washing machines

In the model range of the manufacturer AEG there are no budget items. All products belong to the middle segment and premium class. They, of course, differ in content and functionality. However, all devices have a number of undeniable advantages. Let's look at them.

  • Great build.
  • Durable materials.
  • A large assortment. Narrow AEG washing machines are popular. In the reviews, buyers also highlight devices with vertical loading.
  • Long service life. The manufacturer claims that the equipment will work flawlessly for at least 10 years.
  • Durable tank material. It is made of polymer alloy.
  • The wash is of high quality, there are special modes for dealing with stains.
  • High degree of protection.
  • Low noise.
  • Convenience and ease of use.
  • Stylish design.

Consider the disadvantages

Unfortunately, even such quality products have disadvantages. What exactly are users talking about in the comments?

  • Pretty high cost.
  • It may be difficult to obtain spare parts. Most often you have to order them.
  • There are models in the line that are equipped with low-quality seals and bearings. Problems with them arise after 2-3 years of operation.

AEG L574270SL

Let's look at the reviews of the AEG L574270SL washing machine. Like most models of this manufacturer, this instance deserves the attention of buyers. Its cost, of course, is not low (about 45,000 rubles), but the functionality and quality fully justify the price. Up to 6.5 kg can be loaded per wash. Although the device does not belong to a narrow category, it has small dimensions - the height and width are standard, but the depth is only 45 cm. The hatch is located on the front panel.   • for denim items;
  • for down jackets, blankets, pillows;

  • quick wash - the process takes only 20 minutes.

Are there any drawbacks to this model? No weighty! But some comments nevertheless slip through in the reviews. Reviews on the washing machine AEG AMS7000U

In the comments, buyers praised the AMS7000U washing machine very much. It has attracted the attention of many thanks to its original design and quality assembly. Although its price varies within 40 thousand rubles, the device is really worth the money. Owners in the comments focus on the efficiency of the device. Electricity consumption is limited to 0.13 kWh / kg (class A ++), and water - 49 l per cycle. Spinning things up is of high quality, even if the speed is not the highest. The maximum model capability in this mode is 1000 revolutions. According to the owners, this is the only drawback, since for 40 thousand rubles there are already devices developing a speed of 1200 rpm. The rest of the reviews on the AEG AMS7000U washing machine are positive. The model is equipped with perfectly selected automatic programs with optimal values. Users noticed that even having the ability to change the temperature, they do not, because there is simply no need. But besides these points, there is another significant advantage. It's about quiet work. Surprisingly, even when spinning things, the device does not vibrate and does not make loud sounds.

AEG L61470 WDBI

Positive feedback on AEG washing machines is left not only by women, but also by men. And as you know, the latter are not guided by emotions, so you can trust their opinion. In the assortment of this manufacturer there are not only stand-alone instances, but also built-in ones. According to most, the L61470 WDBI is an excellent model. It is quite roomy (7 kg), but not bulky (height - 820 mm, width - 600 mm, and depth - 550 mm). The model has many advantages. Users consider them functionality (drying, a large number of washing programs), a high level of efficiency (class A), and complete protection against leaks. You can also change the temperature of water heating manually, which allows you to gently wash things from different fabrics. In the comments, the owners pay attention to the fact that the manufacturer has provided special modes with which you can save up to 20-25% of electricity.

Unfortunately, there were some shortcomings here. Users noticed that if the drum was heavily filled with things for drying, the result was crumpled and wrinkled laundry. The maximum load in this operating mode should not exceed 4 kg (according to the instructions), but in fact it is better to use 2.5-3 kg per cycle.
