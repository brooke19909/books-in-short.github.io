"Pearl of the Cape", Gelendzhik: Hotel infrastructure and guest reviews

Summer holidays in the resort town of Gelendzhik has always been and remains very popular and in demand. After all, Gelendzhik is summer, relaxation, sun and sea. In the holiday season, the city is visited by more than four million tourists. And this is no accident - every guest here will find entertainment for themselves. Holidays in Gelendzhik can be active or family, extreme, exclusive or budget. Here you can enjoy the warm sea and beautiful landscapes, breathe in the healing air in pine groves, oak and hornbeam forests. A walk along the promenade of Gelendzhik will leave many impressions. There are many entertainment venues (cafes, night clubs, discos and bars). Tourists with children willingly visit water parks, dolphinariums and attractions, excursions.

How to get to Gelendzhik?

Gelendzhik is located in the Krasnodar Territory, in the central part of the Black Sea coast. The distance from Moscow is one and a half thousand kilometers. It can be reached by road on the M4 and M27, there is a railway connection to the cities of Anapa and Novorossiysk. With the beginning of the holiday season, the airport operates in the city. Direct flights from many cities of Russia are accepted here.

Accommodation for tourists

Sanatorium-resort complex is the main branch of the economy of Gelendzhik. More than a hundred sanatoriums of the city receive guests for health and relaxation. More than four thousand hotels, guest houses, boarding houses and campsites offer comfortable living conditions in different price ranges to tourists. Guests of the city are expected in the private sector.
