DIY crafts from ribbons: 4 samples with step by step instructions

Butterfly

To create such a butterfly, you need to pick up three ribbons of different colors. It is better to take dense material so that the product holds its shape well. We begin to collect the insect from the wings. To do this, the tape is folded in turn as follows: the first turn is 6 cm, the second is 8 cm, the last is 10 cm. The edges should be in the center so that the seams are not visible. When all three elements of green, pink and purple are made, we begin the design of the do-it-yourself crafts from ribbons.



A green ribbon is wrapped around the central part in several turns, leaving the edge free for subsequent work on the mustache. The tail also remains free by 5 cm. The cut edge is bent and sewn with a hidden seam inward. It remains to create a mustache. To do this, the front edge is cut in the center and each part is sewn along the ribbon.

Peacock

The method of manufacturing the body of such a beautiful peacock resembles quilling technique. The body is made by twisting two black ribbons in a circle. The free segment will be the neck.
