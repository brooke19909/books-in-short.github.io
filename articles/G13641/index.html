The best hotels in Yekaterinburg in the city center

Yekaterinburg is a city in the Urals, rich in sights and interesting places to visit. In addition, the city has a well-developed infrastructure and a large, medium and small business. Each year, a large number of tourists from different parts of Russia, as well as business people, come to the city. All of them are forced to stay in search of decent housing. Let us further consider the rating of the best hotels in Yekaterinburg in the city center that have the largest number of positive reviews left by guests.

Rating of the best hotels

There is a certain list of the best hotels in Yekaterinburg. All the places of rest that are presented in it offer vacationers excellent conditions for living, as well as excellent infrastructure located right in its building.

According to tourists, the rating of the five best hotels of the city in question includes such as: Novotel, Emerald, Green Park, Onegin and DoubleTree by Hilton. All these places have very high rating indicators according to the reviews of tourists left about them. These and some other hotels of Yekaterinburg will be considered in more detail below.

"Tenet"

This place for rest and overnight is located in the central part of the city, close to its main attractions - 1905 square and the Central stadium.

The rooms of this Ekaterinburg hotel in the city center offer guests excellent conditions for a comfortable stay. Each of them has modern furniture, quality equipment is mounted, and there is also access to the Internet (Wi-Fi). Tenet guests have the opportunity to use a number of additional services, among which the most popular are: breakfast delivery to the room, taxi call and conference room rental.

The cost of living in the apartments offered by the hotel in question is about 5900 rubles per day. This price includes breakfast.

The Tenet Hotel is located at Yekaterinburg, 1A Khokhryakova Street.

"Big Ural"

The hotel in the center of Yekaterinburg "Big Ural" is a place ideally suited for business people. This statement is due to the fact that the place to stay is located in a district of the city from which you can easily get to any business center. In addition, the Big Ural is ready to provide its guests with all the conditions necessary for doing business: there is Wi-Fi in the rooms, a well-equipped conference room, as well as excellent staff who are ready to satisfy any guest's needs.

The hotel is equipped with good furniture and technical equipment (TV, refrigerator). In the building of the Big Urals building there is a wonderful restaurant in which a buffet is served every morning for hotel guests.

Many guests note the positive fact that the hotel sets a small cost for accommodation in apartments - from 1900 rubles per day.

The hotel in question is located in the center of Yekaterinburg at the address: Krasnoarmeyskaya street, 1.

DoubleTree by Hilton

DoubleTree by Hilton is a centrally located hotel. Its guests in their comments tell that the place for rest is within walking distance from the most interesting sights (Opera and Ballet Theater, City Pond) - this is to the liking of many tourists.

The hotel's rooms are recognized by the guests as quite comfortable. It includes apartments of various categories: from Standard to Junior Suite and Suite. The cost of living in them can increase or decrease depending on what level of comfort a guest wants to surround himself with - the price for a room starts at 3,500 rubles per day.

DoubleTree by Hilton offers pretty good service. There is a restaurant, a conference room, Wi-Fi access, as well as many other features.

DoubleTree by Hilton is located at 9A Lenina Avenue.

"Ascension"

Voznesensky Hotel is located in the building of a large city business center of the same name. This location is incredibly convenient for business people, which is probably why businessmen who are in Yekaterinburg staying in it to resolve business issues stay there.

The hotel offers excellent infrastructure and amazing service - many of its guests have noticed in their comments. The restaurant has a restaurant serving European cuisine, as well as the Ballad Bar. For the convenience of business people, Voznesensky provides a meeting room and a conference room.

Not far from the hotel there are city attractions (Opera and Ballet Theater, Church on the Blood, Kharitonovsky Garden), which are not disregarded by the guests.

Hotel "Voznesensky" is located on Mamina-Sibiryaka St., 52.

Novotel

Novotel is a place where, according to tourists, you can relax and fully enjoy the time spent in the beautiful city. The hotel in question is located in the city center, on the street of Engels, 7.

Novotel guests can enjoy the cordial welcome organized by the staff for each guest. It offers well-equipped apartments with individual air conditioning, plasma TVs and a private bathroom. The cost of living in the rooms of the Novotel hotel (Yekaterinburg, center) is from 4,500 rubles per day or more.

As for the infrastructure of the hotel in question, it has a fitness room, restaurant, meeting room and even a hammam.

Emerald

A few minutes from 1905 Square is another popular hotel in the center of Yekaterinburg - Emerald. This vacation spot attracts tourists with its luxurious facilities, which are provided for everyone here. The main building of Emeralda has a large bar, which serves European cuisine. There is also a small conference room and a fitness club that guests can visit.

As for the living conditions in the proposed apartments, they are presented at the highest level. Here, in each apartment, only high-quality modern furniture is installed, as well as a plasma TV, minibar, and the temperature here is regulated by air conditioners. Rooms at Emerald Hotel have private bathrooms. The cost of daily living in the simplest but most comfortable hotel room is from 3100 rubles.

The hotel in question is located in the center of Yekaterinburg (on the city map - near the park of the Youth Palace and the Volkhonka Drama Theater ), at 38 Sacco and Vanzetti Street.

"Green Park"

Located next to the Green Grove Park, the Green Park Hotel is very popular among guests of Yekaterinburg. This place is famous for the fact that cultural objects are concentrated around it - museums and theaters, which tourists like to visit.

The hotel in question in Yekaterinburg (in the center) offers its guests well-equipped rooms that have full furniture for a comfortable stay, as well as appliances, including a TV, air conditioning, a minibar, as well as a telephone and a safe. Green Park guests can freely access Wi-Fi, as well as the services of the restaurant, which serves a buffet every morning.

In reviews of tourists about the Green Park Hotel, it is said that this place for recreation is relatively inexpensive to stay - the daily cost of renting apartments here is from 3,500 rubles.

Onegin

According to most guests, Onegin is the most stylish hotel in Yekaterinburg, which should be settled upon arrival in the city. It is located in a high-rise building located in the center of Yekaterinburg. Hotel address: Rosa Luxemburg street , 49.

Onegin is a hotel that receives the maximum number of positive reviews. Guests say that in this place very comfortable rooms are equipped, equipped with everything that is necessary for short and long stays. All apartments have a bathroom with bath amenities, air conditioning, a TV and an electronic safe. The interior of each room is made in a classic style, using a large amount of velvet and lacquered wood. The cost of daily living in this place is from 3500 rubles.

As for the service, then at the Onegin hotel it is at the highest level. According to tourists, the infrastructure is also well developed: the hotel has a large restaurant, a fitness center, a conference room, as well as a ticket office and a tour desk.

"Atrium"

In the lists of hotels in Yekaterinburg in the center of the "Atrium" often takes a leading position, as this place to relax provides all the necessary conditions that are needed for the most comfortable stay.

The hotel in question has a well-developed infrastructure. Guests note with positive comments the presence of a restaurant in its building, a conference room, as well as free access to the Internet. A fitness center at the Atrium Palace Hotel (Yekaterinburg, center) is also present - judging by many comments, most guests prefer to spend their time in this place. In addition to all this, the infrastructure of the hotel in question provides for the presence in the building of its building of a swimming pool, restaurant, laundry, as well as dry cleaning.

Due to the fact that the hotel has such a well-developed infrastructure, its guests are often business people who are accustomed to value their comfort. Often, star guests and members of sports teams stay here.

The daily cost of living in standard type apartments here is 6,000 rubles, and if we talk about luxury apartments, the rental price will be 10,000 rubles.

"Aurora"

Aurora is a mini-hotel in the center of Yekaterinburg, which offers accommodation within walking distance from the main city attractions. Many comments addressed to this place indicate that its rooms are equipped with everything that is needed for a comfortable pastime. The building itself has a well-developed infrastructure, represented by a restaurant, a bar, a travel agency and a table tennis room.

All apartments of this mini-hotel in the center of Yekaterinburg have air conditioning, a plasma TV with cable channels and a minibar with a safe. Some rooms have private bathrooms, while the rest of the apartments are shared.

The reviews of the hotel in question indicate that the windows of his apartments offer stunning views of the city, as well as some of its attractions: 1905 Square, the Ural State Variety Theater, as well as the Sevastyanov House.

The cost of living in the apartments of this type cannot be classified as low - the daily rent of a room here will cost about 6,000 rubles.

The hotel is located at: Yekaterinburg, Weiner Street, 13.

How to book a room in a hotel in Yekaterinburg (in the center)

The phones by which you can make an apartment reservation in the desired hotel are always indicated on the pages of the official sites of places for recreation. It should be noted that, despite the relatively high cost of living in some hotels, even in them should be booked in advance, since rooms in central hotels are in great demand.

The second booking option is the use of an online form located on the main site or on the intermediary page. However, it should be remembered that in the case of using the second option, you will need to pay a slightly larger amount than the one set by the hotel.
