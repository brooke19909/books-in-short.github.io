Hotel Club Lella Mariam 4 * (Djerba, Tunisia): photos and reviews of tourists

If everyday bustle is tired and there is a desire to have a good rest, then a large number of tourists recommend flying to the island of Djerba in the city of Zarzis (Tunisia). It was built in January 2014.

The main advantage of the hotel is the presence of rooms with increased sound insulation. The apartments are modern, meeting the requirements of even the most avid holidaymakers, furniture (made in the classical style and combined with the overall design).

Going to the window, you can see a wonderful picture of the magical coast, on which there is a magnificent beach only 200 meters from the hotel complex.

A beautiful pool, made in a modern style and a chic tennis court, a wonderful bar with a large assortment of alcoholic drinks - this hotel has everything you need for an unforgettable vacation. The restaurant is famous for its diverse and delicious cuisine, where professional chefs work, whose culinary works can cause real and genuine delight.

The hotel does not forget about people who love sports, for them there is a large assortment of various sports complexes, such as facilities for playing billiards, table tennis, for fitness, and there is also the opportunity to engage in horse riding. After physical activity it will be very pleasant to relax in the sauna. Representatives of the fair sex have the opportunity to visit a beauty salon and a health complex, in which there are various types of massage.





Hotel guests

Club Lella Mariam 4 * hotel (Tunisia, Djerba) was created so that adults and children could enjoy their holidays on the seashore, but for people who visited the hotel for a business meeting, they will always find something to their liking. The main guests of the hotel are families with children, senior citizens. Nationality does not matter for hotel workers; highly qualified staff will welcome any visitor with joy and due hospitality.

Hotel rooms

The hotel has 294 rooms of various formats, the choice of any guest. The variety of price ranges is explained by the capacity and interior of the room itself.

In the hotel there are 3 types of rooms:

  • for non-smokers;
  • soundproofed;
  • family.

The rooms of Club Lella Mariam 4 * hotel necessarily have heating and furniture. To view the landscapes, you can go to the terrace or balcony in the room.

The rooms are equipped with such equipment as TV, ironing facilities, air conditioning, minibar, safe, telephone. In addition, there is a separate bathroom, where there is a bath and shower, toilet, wash basin, fresh towels, bathrobe, hairdryer.

In the hotel room, you can order from the administrator a service such as room service. Room cleaning is daily.

Free Wi-Fi is available in the hotel's common areas.





restaurants and bars

Near the hotel there are many places where vacationers can have a bite to eat, ranging from small bars to an upscale restaurant. The restaurant can pamper you with a variety of food, ranging from endemic dishes to sophisticated gastronomic creations of Asian cuisine. Connoisseurs of fine wines will find here a large number of elite alcoholic beverages to their taste.





Pool and beach

Visitors to the hotel have the opportunity to enjoy their stay in the modern pool, which is located within the hotel complex. I would like to highlight that the water in the pool is notable for its purity, and this is an eloquent proof of the professionalism of the hotel workers. Relaxing by the pool, guests can use umbrellas and mattresses. Near the pool there is a bar where you can find any drinks both to quench your thirst and to cheer you up.

Having overcome the distance of 250 meters, you can find yourself on a magnificent, well-groomed beach. The sandy beach is considered the property of the hotel, so only Club Lella Mariam guests relax there. You should not take sun loungers and umbrellas to the beach, since all this can be taken already in place.





Hotel facilities

Guests who have arrived in their car have the opportunity to leave it in the private parking lot of the hotel complex. And if you did not have the opportunity to exchange money, the administrator at the reception will be happy to help you with this by providing the "currency exchange" service. If the guest needs to receive documents by fax or make a photocopy, then he will again be helped at the reception.

Accumulated dirty things can be taken to the laundry or dry cleaning.

Also, the Club Lella Mariam 4 * hotel provides a car rental service. Thanks to this service, you can independently familiarize yourself with the amazing architecture of the city and the most beautiful landscapes.

The hotel has all the facilities for people with disabilities.

Parents who want to devote a couple of hours to themselves can turn to experienced nannies - they will provide your child with a fun pastime, and you will have a relaxing holiday.

Club Lella Mariam immediately warns that pets are strictly prohibited, even at an additional cost.





Conditions for children

As for the younger generation: they will not be bored at the hotel. For children there is a special pool where they can swim with their peers (this happens under the supervision of not only parents, but also lifeguards).

Club Lella Mariam also has a playground and a games room, where children can have fun and have a good time meeting and making friends with other children relaxing in the hotel.

On the playground, children are entertained by animators, as the hotel provides animation programs.





Entertainment for guests

On the territory of the Club Lella Mariam 4 * hotel, entertainment is offered for guests of various categories. Guests who have just arrived for a business trip can enjoy a massage or Turkish bath, and go to a night club at night. Parents with children can visit the tennis courts, learn to ride a horse, rent bicycles and ride around the city.

There are water activities where you can arrange snorkelling or learn how to windsurf.

Ladies can visit the beauty salon, hairdresser, fitness center or sun terrace.

For guests who do not want to sleep at night, there is an evening program, after which you can go to the disco. There are additional entertainments such as darts, billiards and much more.





Club Lella Mariam 4 *: guest reviews

Reviews of guests who had the opportunity to visit the hotel are mostly positive. Young people are delighted with the abundance of entertainment and the modern night disco. Daytime life is no less entertaining, because there is an opportunity to recover from stormy nightly entertainment. They respond especially well to hotel employees who are ready at any time to provide the necessary services to all visitors.

For an objective assessment, it must be said that in addition to enthusiastic reviews, there is restrained, and in some cases, sharp criticism. Most often, vacationers are outraged due to the strange interior and decoration of the rooms. However, supporters of such claims should visit luxury hotels with sky-high prices in order to compare and understand the advantages and advantages of Club Lella Mariam 4 * (Djerba).

The positive hotel also receives reviews thanks to the sophisticated and incredibly delicious cuisine. Almost all hotel guests with genuine enthusiasm talk about this. The menu in the hotel is represented by a wide variety of different classic dishes. However, gourmets are given the chance to fully satisfy their gastronomic needs. The variety of delicious meat dishes will not leave indifferent fans of hearty food.

The pearl of the hotel complex, according to holidaymakers, is the delightful beach and the warm blue sea located in the immediate vicinity, as well as the opportunity to get umbrellas and sun loungers for free.
