How to sew clothes for a doll? Tips for little craftswomen

What could be more interesting than dolls for a little girl? Beautiful outfits, balls, princes, houses and doll furniture can attract a child into an exciting game for more than one hour. And it is no less interesting to imagine yourself in the role of a famous designer and fashion designer of clothes and to sew amazing things for your darling. How to sew clothes for a doll with your own hands? This question is of interest to many small needlewomen.

What is needed

To create clothes, first of all, shreds, sewing supplies and clothing patterns are needed . For Barbie dolls, it is best to use knitted fabrics that do not require overcasting, although, having got used to it, the details can be sewn with a crochet. If there is a sewing machine in the arsenal, then it will greatly facilitate the sewing process.



How to make patterns

How to make clothes for dolls without a pattern? Of course, you can experiment, but it is better to make patterns of clothes before you start work. Here are some tips for creating them:

- For a fitted dress, the doll‚Äôs body is wrapped with foil for baking and the horizontal line along the chest, sides and back is drawn with a pen directly on the figure. After that, the manicure scissors cut the foil along the line on the back, remove it from the figure and then divide it into parts that are drawn, and trace on paper.

- For leggings or trousers, legs are wrapped with foil, creating peculiar pants on the figure. After they draw the side, inner, front and back seams. Cut the foil begin from top to bottom on the foot of the doll, after the part is removed from the figure and transferred to paper.



- For a fluffy skirt, no special pattern is needed, it will be enough to cut a strip of fabric, sew it, not reaching the top a couple of centimeters for Velcro, and collect it at the waist.

- How to sew clothes for a doll with a sleeve? For this, along with the torso foil, the handle of the pupa is also wrapped. In this case, draw lines along the shoulder, under the arm and on the side through the armpit. The details are removed from the doll according to the same principle, cutting the foil with nail scissors along the drawn line.

Sewing

So, the patterns are ready, it remains to transfer them to paper, add half a centimeter of allowances for the seams and cut them out of the fabric. How to sew clothes for a doll - manually or on a typewriter? If you have experience working on sewing equipment, then, of course, it is better to give preference to such processing, but it is quite possible to do without it. So, to begin with, the details of the product are cut out of the fabric, then they are folded with their faces inward and first the vertical seams are stitched, and then the horizontal ones. If this is a magnificent dress, then first they individually sew a bodice and a skirt, and only then combine them. In products with a sleeve, the shoulders are first sewn, then the sleeves are sewn, and only then the side seams are processed.



Knowing how to sew clothes for a doll, it will be easy to create interesting models, it is enough to understand the process of creating patterns and learn how to make neat seams. Particular attention should be paid to the selection of fabrics, so that the sewn thing looks organically. Just then, the doll will have a stunning wardrobe, which all girlfriends will envy.
