Meteor Stadium in Zhukovsky - Olympic Forge in Athletics

In the south-west of the Moscow region, Zhukovsky is located in a picturesque setting surrounded by forests and fields. From its very foundation, it has been the center of aviation science in Russia. At its test sites, the first tests of the latest developments of Soviet scientists took place.





It is here that every 2 years the most popular MAKS (Moscow Aviation and Space Salon) passes at Ramenskoye Airport , where not only experienced pilots come from all over the world, but also those who create unique equipment and technologies.

The sports component of city life

On the basis of the city-forming enterprise of the Gromov Flight Research Institute (LII) , MAKS is held, and it was the institute that initiated the construction of many socio-cultural and sports facilities in the city. After all, not only science and the show live in the townspeople. Sport is an integral part of their life. It was sport that once gave residents another reason to be proud of their city.

A treadmill, tennis courts, a soccer field, volleyball and basketball courts - this is where it all began. Rebirth

Over time, sports equipment and structures began to deteriorate and even collapse. Then in 2001, the head of the city, Bobovnikov A.P. and Borzakovsky Yu., The coach of the Russian national track and field athletics team, wrote an appeal to the head of the Moscow region B.V. The complete rebirth of the Meteor stadium in Zhukovsky took place in 2005.

Unlimited sports options

The modern stadium "Meteor" in Zhukovsky at ul. Pushkin, house 3 is an excellent multi-purpose sports complex that meets high international standards (IAAF). It is equipped with modern fitness equipment, shells, equipment. There is a stadium for 7000 fans and a playing field of 1200 square meters. m, athletics arena for 4 treadmills, a pool for 6 lanes with water purification without chlorine - ozonation, tennis courts, gyms, a Finnish sauna.

Sport for everyone

Sports sections work free of charge for children, youth and adults. Particular attention is paid to attracting people with disabilities.

Paid services include a visit to the pool, an ice rink in the winter, a gym, and a sports and dance studio.

Children from 7 years old are invited to classes in a sports school.

Among other things, here you can learn to play volleyball, basketball, golf. There are classes in water aerobics and yoga. Training can be both group and individual.

There is a circle of aircraft modeling - it is very convenient to conduct tests on the football field.
