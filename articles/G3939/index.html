Climate in Nha Trang

Beautiful beaches, healing mud and springs - the city of Nha Trang is famous for all this. Vietnam has a variable climate, but here the sea temperature is year-round within the range of 22-28 degrees and rarely changes in any direction.





The air saturated with the aroma of eucalyptus, and complex treatment have a positive effect on people suffering from respiratory diseases, and on those whose immunity requires support. The most inappropriate time for rest is considered to be the period from October to December. The most suitable time for drivers is March-August.

Beach holidays in Nha Trang

Lovers of relaxing by the water mainly gather on the city beach. But there are beaches on the territory of hotels. Seven kilometers of the sandy beach is called the "Mediterranean of Vietnam", and, in fact, in the necklace of the magnificent beaches of Nha Trang is a beautiful gem. The sun shines here all year round and the air temperature is not lower than +26 degrees.

Vietnam, Nha Trang, tours for tourists

The coast of Nha Trang Bay is an ideal place for fishing, swimming and diving. In this place, the waters are almost always calm, and the seafood is striking in its diversity: cuttlefish, shrimp, squid and many other animals. Restaurants in Nha Trang are ubiquitous.





Here you can taste French, Vietnamese, Italian dishes, including seafood. Throughout the city and along the beach line there are a large number of hotels that meet the requirements of customers of any category.

Attractions Nha Trang

Elegant mosaics of Longshon Pagoda and colorful dragons make visiting this place unforgettable. Near the pagoda is a huge (14 meters) Buddha statue. Map of Vietnam Nha Trang defines as a group of islands. There are 71 islands in total in this area in Khanh Hoa province. Over the southern part of the bay, the longest cable car in the world is stretched from the mainland to Hon Che Island . 25 kilometers north of Nha Trang , there is the Baho waterfall, which is born on the top of Khonshon and descends between the rocks to the fields in the village of Fuhyu. The Vietnamese people have many legends about this picturesque waterfall. Mineral springs Thalba is another attraction that Nha Trang is famous for: after all, they visit Vietnam not only for tourism, but also for treatment with the help of thermal, mud and mineral baths.
