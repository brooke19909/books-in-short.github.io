"Emerald City" (Kazan) as if arrived from a fairy tale

Kazan is a city with a population of over one million people. The capital of the Republic of Tatarstan is one of the most beautiful cities in Russia. The Volga River flowing nearby brings its unique flavor to the external appearance of the metropolis. And here the already built residential complex "Emerald City" (Kazan) compares favorably.





Location of the complex

The residential complex (Kazan) was erected in the Volga region. It is almost completely surrounded by transport arteries: the streets of Kul-Gali, Zavoisky, Commissioner Gabishev. On the east side, the elite cottage village Vostochny adjoins the complexes.

Such a good location makes it possible for those who have moved permanently to the Emerald City (Kazan) to not change their usual way of life, place of work, and not feel divorced from civilization. All listed streets are rich in public transport.





Description of the complex and apartments

The Emerald City microdistrict is eleven residential buildings, each comprising 234 apartments of different layouts and areas. Buildings rise from the ground ten floors. The basis is created by a reinforced concrete frame. The modern communications with which the Emerald City is equipped (Kazan) will not allow residents to experience any inconvenience in later life. Modern ventilation will always make the air in the homes fresh, and its own heating units and pumping stations will allow you to not depend on periodic shutdowns of hot water. Also, the pumping station is a guarantee of quick fire fighting in case of fire. So residents should not worry about their safety when buying an apartment here.

Technology equipment

In terms of equipping buildings with new engineering technologies, the complex occupies almost the first place in the city among new buildings. Water meters are installed in the apartments so that residents do not overpay for cash utility bills. The peace and comfort of residents will be guarded by concierges and intercoms. And to their chosen apartments they will be delivered by high-speed silent elevators. Furniture can be lifted using a freight elevator.

The ground floors of the buildings are provided by the project as non-residential. They will accommodate various shops, beauty salons, office premises, pharmacies. All those organizations that are designed to provide residents with comfort and the necessary social services. Entrances to residential and office areas will not overlap.

Infrastructure

The location of the residential complex in the city, which allows its residents to fully use all the amenities and its entire infrastructure. Therefore, the complex is rightfully considered one of the best projects of Kazan developers. Everything is thought out to the smallest detail here. All kinds of amenities are provided not only for apartment owners, but also for tenants and owners of non-residential premises.

Near the microdistrict there are many shopping centers, sports and SPA complexes. For children there are preschool institutions and comprehensive schools. You can spend your leisure time in entertainment centers, bars and restaurants.

Ecological situation

"Emerald City" (Kazan) occupies a rather large territory, so the developer was preoccupied with the problem of landscaping the adjacent courtyard and not only the territory. Planting trees and shrubs will improve the cleanliness of the surrounding air. Residents of the complex will be able to enjoy the clean, delicious air while strolling in the evenings and weekends. All buildings are equipped with their own closed territory, equipped for evening walks, children's and sports facilities.





The developer even foresaw the possibility of moving people with disabilities into homes and created them all the conditions for a comfortable life: ramps, special fixtures in the entrances. Wheelchairs and prams can be stored in designated areas.

Also, when you look at the complex, it immediately becomes clear why it is called the developer "Emerald City". The buildings are emerald-colored and look as if they had come out of a fairy tale. The builder selected the highest quality building materials, which confirms the high degree of environmental safety of buildings.
