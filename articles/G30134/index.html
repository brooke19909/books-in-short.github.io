A little bit about the name of the city

The names of various settlements are always of particular interest. From some of them it is easy to guess what word they came from. However, this does not always happen, sometimes it is impossible to understand the meaning of the name without referring to additional sources. Many people are so accustomed to the name "Perm" and do not think about what it means.

The word itself appeared in ancient times. It is believed that it came from the Veps language. This is one of the branches of the Baltic-Finnish languages. The word means "distant land." Other versions of the origin of the word are also considered. One of them says that it came from the Komi-Permyak words "pere", which means "far", or "parma" (translated as "forest"). Now it becomes clear where the name of the city of Perm came from.

The history of the city of Perm: how did it appear?

This area was inhabited long before the first settlements appeared here. In Perm, many archaeological excavations were carried out, as a result of which more than 100 different objects from different periods were found. In the XVII century, the territory where the city is now located belonged to the Stroganov merchants. There were several settlements, for example, the village of Zaostrovka and a village called Upper Mulla.

The foundation of Perm, as mentioned above, dates back to 1723. It was then that the construction of a smelter began here. I wonder what prerequisites existed here for the foundation of the city? It is known that in 1720 V.N. went here Tatishchev by order of Peter I. This was necessary in order to find a place for the future construction of various plants. He chose a village called Egoshikha, because it was here that there were reserves of copper ore.

Also, the village had a favorable transport location. After a while, the project was approved, and the construction of the plant began here. Thus, it becomes clear when the settlement was founded, and how many years the city of Perm will turn out to be. We also learned what objects were in these places before the appearance of the city itself.

Further history of Perm

Of course, it becomes interesting how the city developed further. Gradually, the village developed. In 1780, Catherine II decided to create a new city next to the existing plant - Perm. After that, the city began to be actively built up, here were built the buildings of various official institutions, roads were laid and much more. Already in 1796, the Perm province was formed , the center of which became Perm.

Since then, the city began to develop even faster. At the end of the 19th century, construction of railways in several directions began here. Around the same time, various institutions related to art, culture and science began to open here. An opera and ballet theater was built, as well as the first cinema.

However, the revolution affected Perm, as well as other cities in Russia. This will be discussed later.

Revolution and Soviet time

As you know, the history of the city of Perm has many events. One of them is the revolution. In 1917, it happened here. On November 8, news came to the city that the October Revolution had begun. Initially, almost all representatives of the City Duma condemned this step. However, after some time, a congress was held at which the establishment of Soviet power was proclaimed.

After this event, the population of the city increased significantly. From 1926 to 1939, the number of inhabitants increased many times, from 84 thousand to 306 thousand people. And by 1970, Perm had become a millionaire city.
