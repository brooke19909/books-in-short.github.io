How to find out the tax on transport tax?

In the event that you own a personal vehicle, you must pay the appropriate taxes in a timely manner. However, most citizens think little about it and remember about them when a certain and rather big amount of overdue obligations is discovered. As a rule, you can see the transport tax debt either by contacting the Federal Tax Service, or by visiting its official website. Moreover, the second option has many advantages.





Resource Benefits

The FTS website allows each taxpayer to create their own personal account, which will store all the necessary information. Thus, filling in the fields "Login" and "Password" on the main page, you get full access to information about your vehicles, as well as the amount of accrued and already paid off payments, the amount of overpayments (if any) and much more. This resource allows you to find out transport tax arrears, as well as make appropriate payments, print receipts and notifications of interest, fill out declarations and track the status of inspections. All of the above activities allow you to do without personal personal communication with tax officials.



Procedure

As mentioned earlier, you can find out the tax on arrears on the website of the Federal Tax Service. However, not all residents of modern cities are confident users of electronic resources, so all necessary actions will be described in detail below. So, for starters, you should go to the Internet resource itself. Then select the tab that matches your category. Do you belong to individuals, individual entrepreneurs or legal entities - each of the above population groups must pay, for example, transport tax 2013. You can find out the debt by following the appropriate link that appears after going to the section. In addition, the official website allows you to create your own personal account, where, as mentioned earlier, at any time you can find a lot of useful information. For example, find out tax arrears.



Payment period

An employee of the tax inspectorate must send a notification no later than 30 days before the completion of the process of paying off monetary obligations. In turn, the terms of payment themselves may vary depending on the region of residence of the vehicle owner. A feature of the above payments is the fact that it is necessary to make appropriate payments for all the time that the taxable item was with the current owner.

Conclusion

Based on the foregoing, one can easily conclude that it is quite simple to find out the tax on transport tax - all the necessary information can be obtained from the annual letter of a registered type. However, from time to time you should check your personal account on the website of the Federal Tax Service to exclude any misunderstandings.
