Palm Beach Palace Sensimar 5 * (Tunisia, Djerba): description of the hotel, services, reviews

Since holidays in Tunisia are popular among our compatriots along with Turkey, in our article we want to talk about one of the hotels on the island of Djerba.

A bit about the hotel ...

Palm Beach Palace Sensimar 5 * is located in the tourist region on the island of Djerba (southeast of Tunisia). Eight kilometers from the hotel is the town of Midoun. The complex has a convenient location, it is quite easy to get to it from the Jerba Zarzis Airport.



The hotel is built surrounded by beautiful beaches and palm trees. Tourists resting in it can visit the center of the town of Houmt Souk, which can be reached in ten minutes by bus or taxi. The convenient location of the complex allows vacationers not only to devote time to the beach and the sea, but also to see local attractions.

The hotel consists of a main building and many bungalows. Palm Beach Palace Sensimar 5 * is part of the Palm Beach Hotels Tunisia hotel chain .

A little bit about the resort ...

Djerba is the southernmost resort of Tunisia. A distinctive feature of this place is the fact that the weather on the island is always a couple of degrees warmer than on the mainland. Djerba is known to vacationers not only for its sandy white beaches, but also for modern good hotels and thalassotherapy centers.

Relaxing at the hotel, bathing procedures can be alternated with guided tours and independent visits to local attractions: the Medina, Fort Bordj el Ekbir, and pottery workshops.



Most Djerba hotels are concentrated along the north and west coasts. There is a small resort area in the south-east of the island, in the town of Medun. You can get to Djerba by ferry (for only ten) or use the services of air services.

Local attractions and activities

In Gellal you can visit the museum of folk traditions, which tells about the life of the native Djerbins. The local history museum on the outskirts of the city is known for its stunning collection of traditional clothes, ceramics, jewelry, wood carvings.

From the island you can go on an excursion to the Sahara, see the Barbary settlements, honeycombs.

At the local Medina you can see a lot of interesting things. Three mosques are located next to it: Jamaa Ettruk, Ash Sheikh, Jemaa el Gorba. On the island is the oldest Jewish village in which there is a 2600-year-old synagogue of Grib.

There is a lot of greenery on Djerba, pink flamingos spend afternoon hours in its shadow. There are an incredible amount of them and they are not at all afraid of people. The island has many underground sources, thanks to them it is rich in vegetation: palm trees, fig, olive, fruit trees. Flocks of marabou live on the sea coast. Djerba is famous not only for its beautiful sandy beaches, but also for its incredibly warm climate and clear waters. The warm sea, washing the island with palm and olive groves, is not subject to temperature fluctuations, so you can sunbathe and swim here all year round.

Sensimar Palm Beach Palace 5 *: reviews

Having a conversation about the hotel, I want to discuss the reviews of tourists who managed to visit such a beautiful place. According to vacationers, the complex is worthy of all praise. It is very conveniently located. If you use a transfer, the road to the hotel takes only half an hour. After the road, the hotel always awaits tourists with a warm and cordial welcome by the staff. Receptionists work very quickly and populate literally within five minutes.



The hotel begins to amaze from the first minutes. The number of rooms is one of the main points of interest to vacationers. What are the numbers in Palm Beach Palace Sensimar 5 *? Deluxe apartments are striking in their beauty, luxury and size. The rooms are very spacious, which creates an atmosphere of lightness and freedom. They are equipped with large four-poster beds, plasma TVs, there are tables with a kettle and necessary accessories. All apartments have separate toilet and bathroom. And in the spacious hallway there is a sofa with a floor lamp. The rooms have large equipped balconies with armchairs, a table, a clothes dryer. The fridge contains cola, fanta, drinking water and mineral water. Stocks are replenished daily.



Rooms are cleaned daily; towels are also changed every day. There are no claims to cleanliness.

The hotel has very good food. A large number of various dishes are served daily in the restaurant: mashed soups, broths, meat, fish, fruits, ice cream, pastries. According to the all-inclusive system, guests are offered local champagne, wine, beer. All drinks are of acceptable quality. For a surcharge, you can order imported alcohol. Once a week (on Thursdays) a "director's dinner" is arranged, which must be attended in certain clothes. The event is very solemn. The local chef deserves special praise, which delights tourists with delights; here you can taste kidneys in sauce, beef tongues, shrimps, mussels, grilled tuna and much more.

The hotel has its own spacious beach with normal sun loungers and reed mushrooms. On the shore there is a shower, a foot washer, a bar and a toilet. The sea is very warm and clean, although sometimes after storms it can bring a lot of algae, but this is nature, nothing can be done about it.

The complex has beautiful pools, the relaxation pool with hydro-massage waterfalls is especially good. It is located near the shore and is equipped with a good bar. There are few people in the pools, everyone has enough space.



The hotel has animators. In the mornings, you can work out with them aerobics or water aerobics, then play darts, volleyball or soccer. In the evenings, guests are invited to an entertainment show (magicians, belly dancing, etc.).

The tourist area is fenced by roadblocks with police and military personnel. The beaches are also patrolled, they are not allowed on the local ones, even merchants should not be approached by tourists. If you are interested in their product, then go to them yourself. At the entrance to the hotel, be sure to check all the cars and bags. Daily checks are even employees. There are also cameras on site.

The hotel is located at some distance from civilization, but here it is always quiet and calm. If you wish, you can visit the nearest attractions, go to Medun and make purchases.
