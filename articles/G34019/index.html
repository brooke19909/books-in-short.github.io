Hotel Marinus (Kabardinka): description, service, reviews

The Marinus Hotel was recently opened in 2009. It is a luxurious building of modern architecture that delights the eye with its exquisite interior. If you need a cozy and quiet place to relax and work, then come to the Marinus Hotel. Kabardinka (the village where the hotel is located) is located in the area of ​​the city of Gelendzhik.

Hotel location

To get to the hotel, tourists need to come to the resort village of Kabardinka. The facility is located near the village center on 77 Collective Street. The Marinus Hotel is located on the Black Sea. Guests can enjoy the services of the restaurant and outdoor pool with a decorative waterfall. An equipped playground is provided for children. The beach is just a ten minute walk away. During the day, tourists enjoy a shady terrace where they can relax and unwind after a trip to the sea. In the evenings, karaoke is available. If you want to spend time outside the hotel, you can go to the Aqua Gallery or Mountain Entertainment Park, located nearby. Inland parking is free.





Arrivals

Hotel Marinus (Kabardinka) offers guests 40 rooms of several categories.

  • "Standard" double - one-room, with an area of ​​16 square meters. The cost, depending on the season, ranges from 2000 to 5200 rubles per day.
  • “Junior Suite” - the room occupies 25 square meters. Price per day from 2600 to 5900 rubles.
  • Room "Suite" - consists of two rooms with a total area of ​​31 square meters. Its cost is from 3000 to 6800 rubles, depending on the season.
  • “Studio” is a large one-room suite occupying an area of ​​31 square meters, the price for it varies, as well as for a suite, from 3,000 to 6,800 rubles.
  • Room "family" - its cost is more expensive, from 5000 to 7300 rubles per day.
  • “Premium Suite” - occupies two rooms with a total area of ​​36 square meters. Such a room costs per day from 3200 to 6800 rubles.
  • Cottage (2-storey townhouse) - its total area is 50 square meters. For rent only in the summer and in the first half of September. It costs from 5900 to 7300 rubles per day.

All rooms include breakfast. Of course, the rooms at the Marinus Hotel, located in the village of Kabardinka , are not cheap . Prices , to be fair, correspond to the quality of services provided and comfort.





Room Amenities

All the necessary amenities in their rooms are offered to guests by the Marinus Hotel. Kabardinka is a seaside village, therefore the authors of the hotel project provided balconies in the rooms so that vacationers could breathe fresh sea air in the evening. All rooms have exquisite natural wood furniture. In addition, the following amenities are provided for visitors:

  • 42-inch plasma TV;
  • a bathroom, which includes a bathtub, a washbasin and a toilet bowl (there is an additional bidet in the "family" room and in the premium suite);
  • telephone, air conditioning, hair dryer;
  • all guests are provided with toiletries, as well as a bathrobe and slippers;
  • The hotel has 24-hour free WIFI.




Fun for kids

Since the location of the Marinus Hotel is the village of Kabardinka , it can be assumed that in the settlement itself there is not much entertainment for children. Therefore, the hotel staff made sure that the children's vacation turned into an unforgettable holiday.

  • In the courtyard of the hotel there is an excellent equipped modern children's playground with slides, swings and gazebos.
  • Near the playground there is a children's pool where children can swim under the supervision of adults. Comfortable sunbeds are provided near the pool.
  • Inside the hotel is a large, beautifully designed, children's room, Winnie the Pooh. Here for children there are many toys, comfortable furniture. You can play on the floor on the carpet with an unusual pattern. And on the ceiling hang amazingly beautiful white-blue lamps made in the shape of balloons. On the walls hang pictures with scenes from favorite fairy tales, and there is also additional lighting. Very nicely curtained windows.
  • On weekends, for children in the courtyard of the hotel held children's parties.

restaurants and bars

The Marinus Hotel takes a lot of care about its guests . The service also includes excellent food at affordable prices.

  • There is a restaurant on site that serves Russian cuisine. Here you can order cold snacks, first and second courses, various drinks. Dishes are presented in a wide assortment, a list of which is given in the menu.
  • A great place to relax in the evening is the Marinus Bar. Here, guests can enjoy exquisite desserts from the hotel chefs, enjoy signature coffee and enjoy special coffee with friends, for example, playing backgammon.
  • Those who wish can smoke a hookah on the summer terrace, and if the evening is cool, they will offer you hot mulled wine.
  • If during your stay at the hotel you want to hold a special event, Cafe Mare will take on all the hassles of organizing it. Whether it will be a birthday, wedding or corporate party, this holiday will be unforgettable for you.




Caring for your health

If you decide to choose the Marinus Hotel for relaxation , the description will pleasantly surprise you with the availability of wellness treatments.

  • The hotel’s cozy courtyard has a beautiful pool with clean water, where you can enjoy swimming.
  • A qualified massage therapist will help you relieve muscle tension in the massage room. If you want to let your body relax and rest, you can go to the masseur by appointment.
  • There is also a modern sauna. Here you can have a great time with friends. In addition to the steam room, there is a backlit pool at your disposal, you can order a separate relaxation room.




Marinus Hotel. Kabardinka. Guest reviews

Reviews of tourists about the rest at the Marinus Hotel are for the most part the most striking.

  • All vacationers are noted by very polite and friendly staff.
  • A pleasant impression is that everything in the hotel corresponds to the description and photos located on the site.
  • The rooms are very nice and clean.
  • In the family suite, vacationers were pleasantly pleased with the presence of a kitchen area.
  • Everyone notes a remarkably clean pool and relaxation area near it with gazebos, where after bathing you can order refreshments and, if desired, food from the restaurant.
  • The restaurant is very tasty, the waiters are impeccably polite.
  • Availability of entertainment for children.

However, there are a number of negative reviews.

  • Some vacationers complain that their rooms were not prepared at the time of check-in, and they had to wait.
  • The number of the Standard category is small in size, and, taking into account the additional space, it became quite crowded in it.
  • Limited number of car parking spaces in the internal parking. The remaining cars were forced to stay outside the hotel.
  • Some guests complained of poor sound insulation between neighboring rooms.




Conclusion

Many vacationers gladly return to the Marinus Hotel, located in the resort village of Kabardinka. Prices for accommodation there, although high, but given the high quality of the services provided, it becomes clear that they are justified. In addition, here you can relax in silence, away from city noise. For guests who want to enjoy the beauty of the Black Sea coast, interesting excursion programs are provided. A hotel room can be booked in advance, if desired. Hotel Marinus (Kabardinka) is always happy for its guests.
