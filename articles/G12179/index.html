Centara Grand West Sands Resort & Villas Phuket 5 * (Thailand, Phuket): hotel description, reviews

Phuket is an amazing and exotic place, very loved by travelers. Many people from all over the world come here. This is one of the most respectable resorts in all of Thailand. And there is a mass of hotels where you can stay. One of the most attractive options is the Centara Grand West Sands Resort.





Briefly about the hotel

This complex was built in 2009. So it is quite new and modern. 5 years ago, the last renovation was carried out, and cosmetic repairs are carried out regularly.

The hotel is located on the sandy beach of Maikao - the best in Phuket. You can reach it in a couple of minutes. The good news is that the hotel has its own private area on the beach. So there is enough space for everyone. Anyway, Maikao is the most beautiful and uninhabited part of the coast. So for this reason, this resort is loved by many.

300 meters from the hotel there is an excellent water park called Splash Jungle. It takes 2-3 minutes to drive to the Sirinat National Park, as much as to the Blue Canyon Country Club. A 10-minute drive takes you to the huge Wat Phra Tong Temple. Still at the same distance is Khao Phra Theo National Park. And the airport is also close. In general, Centara Grand West Sands Resort is located in a place that is advantageous in all plans.

What is available in the hotel?

Centara Grand West Sands Resort is declared as a 5-star hotel, respectively, it has all the amenities that every complex of this level should have on its territory. Naturally, there is free Wi-Fi and private parking, a 24-hour front desk and currency exchange, a tour desk and luggage storage, an ATM and a shared lounge.

People who take their children with them on vacation may not worry that their kids will be bored. There are two small clubs. One is for young children, and the other is for teens. There is also a playground on the street and babysitting services that can sit with the kids in the room while the parents relax.

Centara Grand West Sands Resort also has a dry cleaning service, laundry service, and a business center. On the territory there are several shops (including a souvenir stall) and a mini market. Guests are offered VIP services, transfers (around the city or to the airport), delivery of magazines, drinks and food to the apartments, as well as amenities for people with disabilities. And if guests gather somewhere for the whole day, then they will make packed lunches for them. The staff, by the way, speaks not only Thai, but also English.





For leisure

Centara Grand West Sands Resort Villas Phuket is a resort hotel. Accordingly, guests coming here expect not only a comfortable stay, but also entertainment. However, there is something to keep yourself busy.

There are pools with water slides, a barbecue in the fresh air, a picturesque garden. There is a water park and everything you need for water sports (diving, canoeing, fishing, diving, etc.). You can also rent a bike and go for a drive around the picturesque surroundings.

And the hotel has a modern gym, where you can arrange a full-fledged workout with physical activity. Badminton and volleyball courts are also available.

The Centara Grand West Sands Resort Villas Phuket also has an excellent spa, which you should definitely go at least once.
