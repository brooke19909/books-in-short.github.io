Samodelkin's lessons: how to make a bird feeder from a plastic bottle

Winter is cold, and gentle summer is remembered more and more often. Looking at the frozen birds through the window, you feel a desire to help them. How? Make a manger!

Plastic in hand



A plastic bottle feeder for birds can be made in different ways. We suggest considering several ways.

  • The simplest one looks like this: you will need, in addition to the container itself, wooden spoons with long handles. In the bottle at different levels, make holes and insert spoons. What is the principle of such a bird feeder? From a plastic bottle, the grain should fall into a spoon. Therefore, on one side of the hole, make it slightly wider. And to a cup of a spoon beat a small plate on which birds can sit. Jumping on it, they shake the bottle, and the food will spill out where it should be. Through the funnel, pour the grain into the container and wrap the lid. The last question is how such a bird feeder from a plastic bottle will be attached. With this, it‚Äôs completely simple. Such a bird feeder from a plastic bottle will cost you inexpensively. Therefore, they can be made in unlimited quantities and it is great to support our feathered friends.
