Hotel Obala Plus 3 * (Montenegro, Budvanska Riviera): description, services, reviews

Montenegro is a very glorious, cozy and in a special way native country for a Slavic tourist. Pleasant Mediterranean climate, beautiful landscapes, hospitable, friendly people, quiet, pretty resorts, European service, interesting cultural heritage are significant, but not the most important reasons for such an authoritative status of Montenegrin resorts among Russian travelers. The decisive factor in the popularization of this country among Russian tourists was the fact that Montenegro is one of the very few states in Europe, into the territory of which Russian citizens can enter without a Schengen visa for a period of no more than 30 days. In addition, prices for accommodation, food and other tourist services in Montenegro are very affordable, especially in comparison with pan-European ones.

In terms of tourism, Montenegro is interesting mainly as a beach and ski destination. Medieval sights, breathtaking natural beauties, delicious local cuisine, quality and budget shopping opportunities are considered by visiting travelers as pleasant bonuses, but not as the main goals of the trip. In the south, Montenegro has access to the Adriatic Sea. The length of the beach line is 73 km. The first place in the nomination "The best beaches of Europe" has already been awarded Montenegro several times.





Adriatic coast

The beaches in the south are mostly small-pebble or sand-pebble. Montenegro is rightly considered one of the main directions of ecological tourism on the whole continent. The transparency of sea water in some resorts reaches 35 meters. Weather features of the Adriatic create a clear framework for the high holiday season on this coast. It lasts from May to September. At this time, the water temperature reaches 20-26 ¬∞ C. Naturally, tour operators adjust the organization of charter flights for these periods. Budget tours to Montenegro from Moscow and large Russian cities with charter flights in the summer are bought up like hot cakes. And most decent resort hotels stop booking by the end of June. So you need to take care of booking a tour to the Budva Riviera in advance.

There are two airports in Montenegro: in Podgorica and Tivat. The first airport is the capital, accepting mainly regular international flights. You can get to Podgorica by direct flights of airlines such as Aeroflot, Ural Airlines and S7, but the easiest and cheapest way to use the services of the national carrier Montenegro Airlines.

Tivat Airport is also called the gates of the Adriatic. Tours to Montenegro from Moscow are mainly with flights to this airport. It is located in the south of the country in a convenient proximity to the main resorts of the coast. The distance from the airport to the city of Budva is only 20 km, Becici is 23, Kotor is 7. But given the small size of Montenegro (you can go round and round for a day), you can quickly get to the southern resorts both from Tivat airport and from Podgoritsy.



Obala Plus 3 *, Montenegro, Budva Riviera. general information

This resort, owned by the local Obala chain, is located in the picturesque village of Rafailovici, 5 km from Budva. The hotel has 29 rooms. They are located in the main five-story building of the entire hotel complex. Each room has a direct or side sea view.

The hotel has been operating since 2009. It focuses mainly on budget tourists. Convenient location makes it comfortable for family travelers and for active tourists who like to visit attractions, go on excursions, walks.

The fixed check-in time is 14:00. You need to leave the room on the day of departure until 12:00 .

When checking in, no deposit will be charged.

The hotel does not have facilities for disabled guests.

Russian-speaking staff works at the reception.

Hotel location

Obala Plus 3 * is the main building of the Obala complex. It is 50 meters from Becici public beach.

The hotel complex has a very good location. It is located in the village of Rafailovichi. This is a small, but very cozy, modern and comfortable resort town. It has many cafes, shopping centers and other infrastructure for the maximum comfort of tourists. Being a budget resort, the village of Rafailovichi annually receives huge flows of international tourists, while remaining cozy, flowering, colorful and picturesque.



5 km from Rafailovici is the center of resort life in the south - the city of Budva. The entire coast of the Montenegrin Adriatic is called the Budva Riviera. Budva consists of the New and Old Town. The new city is represented by a picturesque promenade, a large center with many shopping centers, cafes, restaurants, expensive hotels, casinos, night clubs, sports clubs and tennis courts. The Old Town is a historical complex with an architectural ensemble, pretty streets, temples and museums.

Obala Plus 3 * is located 25 km from Tivat Airport and 65 km from the capital Podgorica Airport.
