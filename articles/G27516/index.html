Bead Collars: A Fresh Fresh Trend

Modern fashion almost every season (if not a day) presents us with some surprises. And this applies not only to clothing. Accessories also regularly undergo numerous changes. This time on sale appeared

bead collars. It is worth noting that today this accessory has already become very popular. Such a collar can be anything: metal, made of fabric, beads, with spikes and rhinestones. Some fashionistas prefer an accessory embroidered with sequins.

Is it possible to make a collar of beads? The master class is not only for true professionals in their field. Any fashionista can get this stylish accessory if she does not regret just one free evening.

Many advise using at least a thin base in the form of a fabric. With its help, the future collar will be stronger and more reliable. Making it right out of the beads is a little dangerous. In the process of wearing, the jewelry may well break and simply crumble. By the way, since this is also jewelry, collars should be worn without additional accessories on the neck. Otherwise, it will

the effect of unnecessary details.

So, for starters, draw a collar shape based on. Try to choose it in tone with the beads so that in the spaces between them there will not be a strong tint contrast. In this case, the finished product will look solid. Next, carefully cut the workpiece with scissors, process the edges of the fabric so that the threads do not stick out. Bead collars should look as if made without the use of extraneous materials. The edges can also be fixed with a series of decorations. It all depends on your imagination and preferences. Next, with the help of a needle with a thread, begin to fill the space of the base with the selected beads, rhinestones and sequins. The latter should have special holes. Only in this way can they be attached to the fabric. Of course, there is another option with glue, but in this case the product will turn out to be less reliable. In addition, dropped jewelry will leave an ugly mark on the base, which is almost impossible to get rid of.



Bead collars can also be woven. In this case, you will need the skill of creating various baubles and bracelets. If this is not the case, you should turn to books on beadwork. They will tell you the most successful and durable schemes. In addition, friend-needlewomen can suggest methods for creating such original accessories.

Another option is to adorn the collar of an already unusable shirt. It can be completely cut with large sharp scissors and worn separately.
