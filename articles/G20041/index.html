In anticipation of the New Year: DIY Christmas compositions

New Year is one of the brightest and most beloved holidays among the people. Each nation has its own traditions, but decorating houses is common to everyone. We‚Äôll talk about this.

Candles and balls



Let's start making New Year's compositions. With our own hands, we can realize any idea and fantasy. The main instrument will be candles. Their warm and quivering light is the best suited to create a mysterious cozy atmosphere, which is so characteristic of this holiday. Naturally, your New Year compositions will not do without fir branches and sparkling rain. With your own hands you can bake cookies or cupcakes of the original form - they will come in handy too. Colorful glass balls will complete the picture , in the color of candles or arbitrary shades. For example: on small flat dishes, put 3 low wide (decorative) candles. Between them lay out the legs of the Christmas tree, sprinkled with glittery confetti, and balls. Decorate New Year's compositions with your own hands with rain and place in the center of the laid table, on the sideboard shelf, in different places of the room. It will be great if they can be reflected in the mirrors - then all the surrounding space in the room will take on a fabulous look. Can you imagine how your children will like it? Yes, and you yourself will be satisfied!

The magic of numbers in the light of candles



How else can you make New Year's compositions with your own hands? Put candles in wide glasses, on which you stick on one digit of the coming year. On the legs tie a bow from a decorative ribbon. Put the pyramids of oranges and tangerines in plates next to them - their aroma has long been an unchanging attribute of this family celebration. Insert fir branches between the fruits, lay out the cones, and lay a colored rain on the edges of the plates. When the candles are lit, such New Year's compositions of cones and citrus fruits will help to feel all the joy and sincerity of the holiday. Yes, next to install small figures of Santa Claus or Santa, as well as symbols of the year. This will bring good luck to the house! You can arrange compositions only from candles and gifts of the forest. Acorns, ripe and green cones, dry branches of the original form, branches of mountain ash and viburnum are suitable for this. From the listed materials, make foresters. Put them on a tray, lay out cotton and berries, sprinkle confetti. Set candles nearby. In their trembling light, these so familiar objects will appear in a completely different, mysterious and funny way. It will turn out just great, do not hesitate!


Other materials at hand

No less spectacular and elegant look New Year's compositions of artificial flowers, Christmas decorations and fruits. Take the small wicker baskets. Wrap their handles with synthetic Christmas tree branches and ivy. Weave in the rowan or viburnum brushes, either artificial or real, as you can find. Put shreds of cotton wool, balls, tinsel on the bottom of the baskets. Surely sweets and other sweets. You can red large apples and winter pears. These baskets can be given to children in addition to the main gifts. Just add some more small souvenir toys.

As you can see, creating a miracle with your own hands, turning into wizards is not difficult at all. The main thing is desire and fantasy!
