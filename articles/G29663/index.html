Perpetual magnet motor?

Sometimes you look at web pages, again and again ... And, excuse me, you look exactly like a ram in front of a new gate. It seems that the courtyard is not the 21st century, but the 16th. The very dawn of the era of the transition of mankind from manual production to machine. But at the same time, the sensation is not at all the triumph of reason and science, but the exact opposite. Otherwise, the perpetual motion machine with magnets would not have arisen again and again - an idea whose absurdity has been repeatedly and convincingly proved.

But since any other mechanism works by borrowing energy from outside (which is required by the law of conservation of mechanical energy), it turns out that the perpetual motion machine is powered by energy from nowhere.

The absurdity of this fact, however, is not so obvious as the impossibility of dividing the number by zero. Otherwise, how can we explain the fact that for two and a half centuries both poorly educated artisans and pundits have offered countless designs of perpetual motion machines, among which was a magnet motor? Finally, in 1775, the French Academy of Sciences decided that henceforth projects of perpetual motion machines will not be considered.

The "age" of this historic decree is already three centuries old. Do you think humanity has grown wiser? Judging by the "tutorials" on the sites "How to build a perpetual motion machine with magnets with your own hands" - not at all.
