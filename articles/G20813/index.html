Famous Burj Khalifa Tower and Armani Hotel

The Burj Khalifa is the tallest building ever created by human hands. Located in the resort city of Dubai (United Arab Emirates). The tower soars to the sky at a record 828 meters. This is a multifunctional complex where apartments and apartments, gyms, swimming pools and entertainment facilities, offices and catering facilities are located. And 11 floors out of 163 are occupied by the Armani Hotel in the Burj Khalifa.

Hotel Armani (Burj Khalifa)

The cyclope construction could not fail to attract the attention of hoteliers. Dozens of world-famous companies fought for the right to place a hotel here under their own brand. An example of future success was the nearby Burj Al Arab hotel-sail, located nearby. Ultimately, the best conditions were offered by the Armani firm, specializing in serving wealthy clients.

The hotel in the tower "Burj Khalifa" from the eminent Italian brand opened its doors in late 2010. By the way, interior design was developed with the active participation of maestro Giorgio Armani himself.

Location

When searching for Armani Hotel Dubai, you need to focus on the Burj Khalifa skyscraper. Armani Hotel is located in downtown Dubai in the Downtown area at Burj Khalifa, Sheikh Mohamed Bin Rashid Boulevard. Nearby is the Financial Center Road junction. Three kilometers of "stone jungle" (residential areas, office and banking centers, hotels and restaurants) separate the skyscraper from the Persian Gulf coast.

The main form of transport in the city is a taxi. From the international airport to the tower, the journey by car will take about 20 minutes. Hikers should stick to Burj Khalifa / Dubai Mall Subway Station, 600 meters away from the sea. Using the local metro, you can reach the most interesting places in the city. Just keep in mind that less well-off residents also use it - emigrants from South Asia (India, Bangladesh, Pakistan, etc.). However, the criminal situation in Dubai is safe.
