Hotel Jinene 3 * (Tunisia, Sousse): photos and reviews of tourists

Hotel Jinene 3 (Tunisia) is located in one of the best resorts in the country - Sousse. This city, a suburb of Tunisia, ranks third in the ranking of the most popular places on the Mediterranean coast. This is not only very large, but also one of the oldest cities in the area. Medina, built on the territory of Sousse, is one of the objects protected by the UNESCO World Heritage Site.





In general, it has long been customary to conditionally divide the city of Sousse into two zones, the first of which is a port town with many amazing architectural monuments, and the second is a resort area called Port El Kantaoui, intended for receiving vacationers. The last geographical point is 10 kilometers north of Sousse itself. The port has an artificially created harbor, which connects to the luxurious embankment square.

As for tourist accommodation and a calm, measured rest, in Sousse there is a huge number of various hotel complexes for every taste and budget. On the territory of the city built hotels of any star rating, starting from 3. There are also luxury hotels, which provide an opportunity to relax just a few steps from the sea. Russian tourists love to come to this resort. This is evidenced by numerous reviews of hotels in Tunisia. Jinene 3 *, according to vacationers, is considered one of the best hotels in its category.

It is not necessary to talk about the infrastructure of the city of Sousse from a negative point of view, because there is everything that a beginner or an experienced traveler can only imagine. Within walking distance from the hotel are all kinds of cafes, bars, restaurants, water parks, dolphinariums, clubs and other entertainment venues.

There are spacious golf courses, equipped with everything necessary for exciting battles, and numerous centers of thalassotherapy sessions, and much, much more.

Due to the huge number of nightclubs located in the resort areas of the city, reckless young people who want to have a good dinner go here with pleasure. However, the relatively close location of the city from airports and other transport stops makes the resort popular among couples and among people who have reached retirement age.

Among other things, amazing sandy beaches are recognized as a real visiting card of the resort, the service at which corresponds to the level of quality accepted in Europe. The nearest beach to the hotel Jinene Beach 3 * (Tunisia) is no exception.





Location of the complex

Hotel Jinene 3 * is located in a very convenient point, just 4 kilometers from the city of Sousse in a northerly direction. The Port El Kantaoui area is at the same distance, and a giant golf course is just 3 kilometers away. Monastir Airport is located 25 kilometers from the hotel.

Short description





Outside, Jinene 3 * Hotel (Tunisia, Sousse) is surrounded by a beautiful garden, which is replete with magnificent figures of landscape design and sculptures. The complex has 223 comfortable rooms that are scattered in 2 buildings. Guests are offered both deluxe rooms and suites, and there are also simple economy class offers. In addition, the hotel Jinene Spa 3 * (Tunisia) has rooms for families with small children. It offers views of the garden or the seashore and the beach. Accommodation





The size of the hotel is average, inside there are only 223 rooms. There are rooms for smoking clients and for those who do not suffer from this addiction. Each room has a toilet room with a bath or shower, toilet, sink, towels and all necessary accessories. Some rooms have a loggia or balcony. All apartments have satellite TV and internet. A mini bar is available for a fee. There is air conditioning and a card phone.
