Kirov hotels in Kaluga region: description and reviews

Kirov is a city in the Kaluga region, located on the banks of the Bolva River. In the village there are several industrial enterprises. Also, tourists are attracted by the salt cave "Vita-Breeze" (the first in the area). If you are interested in this locality, the Kirov hotels in the Kaluga Region will provide you with comfortable living conditions.
