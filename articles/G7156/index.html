Children's Health Camp "Krasnaya Gorka" (Yekaterinburg): rest, photos, how to get, reviews

Children's camps are very popular in Russia, they allow the child to spend their holidays cognitively and cheerfully outside the home. Under the guidance of experienced teachers and friendly counselors, adolescents learn to reveal their creative and organizational abilities. In the Sverdlovsk region there is a recreation camp "Krasnaya Gorka", which opened in 1950. Children from different parts of our vast country come here to not only relax, but also to improve their health.

To quickly and successfully adapt to new conditions, numerous games are held here in a game form aimed at interpersonal communication and knowledge of each other's interests. In a relaxed atmosphere, the child opens up, takes initiative and leadership qualities. The most important thing is to interest children and make it clear that they are needed.

Location and travel





It is located DOL "Krasnaya Gorka" 130 km from the city of Kamensk-Uralsky, 130 km from the city of Yekaterinburg on the banks of the picturesque Sinara River. The territory of the institution is surrounded by a pine forest and beautiful landscaped flower beds, which were created by skillful hands of designers.

The unusual Ural nature in combination with the stupefying aroma of coniferous forest captivates and fills with life-giving energy. For the safety of the wards, the entire territory is carefully guarded and illuminated - cameras are installed, a checkpoint has been created.

You can get from Yekaterinburg by yourself on the Tyumen highway. Driving through the city of Kamensk-Uralsky, we turn off to the village of Potaskuev, then we go through the bridge and call into the forest, following the sign, we move to the destination. The price includes a round-trip transfer (check with the administrator for details).

Accommodation





Children's camp "Krasnaya Gorka" provides comfortable accommodation for all children aged 7 to 16 years. The following buildings were built on the adjacent fenced area (all buildings are heated):

- One-storey cottage with rooms for 8 people and a shared bathroom with shower on the floor.

- Two-story and three-story houses with rooms for 4-5 children. Each room has a bathroom with a toilet, there is cabinet furniture with household appliances (kettle, refrigerator).

Employees live in a separate building.

Supply system

Health Camp "Red Hill", a photo of which can be viewed in the article, offers five meals a day. To do this, there is a two-story dining room where highly qualified chefs work, who know how to feed each child healthy, tasty and varied.

On the ground floor there is a phytobar. The institution offers freshly squeezed juices, herbal teas, environmentally friendly and natural products enriched with trace elements for the full development of the child's body.

Sport program





Children's camp "Red Hill" will delight the children with a variety of active games. For wards built an artificial climbing wall. Before the lesson, an introductory briefing on the technique of climbing on a sports equipment is provided for all children. Thanks to the construction, the children learn to control their balance, develop flexibility, dexterity and strength of hands. Children wear protective equipment.

In warm weather, the swimming season opens and rafting on catamarans is organized. During rafting, the guys learn mutual assistance and mutual support. Interesting one-day hiking trails in the local surroundings are arranged with the teacher.

Teenagers will be able to ride a horse across the territory and feed her. Equipped sports grounds for football, paintball and basketball. Those who wish can master shooting from pneumatics and a crossbow at artificial targets.

Entertainment and infrastructure

In the middle of the pine forest there are cozy gazebos with benches and tables for children. Here they gather in whole groups and play chess, checkers, sing songs and talk heart to heart. In season, there is a swimming pool, the second, indoor, is located in the building. Camp "Krasnaya Gorka" has a cinema, a disco club, an auditorium with a stage and equipment. The club has various circles: drawing, dancing, modeling, acting.





Interesting competitions, contests and sports programs await teens. Each event is held with the participation of children and is aimed at instilling in them a love for a healthy and healthy lifestyle. Children will be able to show their talents at a quiz, festival of creativity, in a theatrical performance and a literary and musical program.

Medical service

In addition to recreational activities, the Krasnaya Gorka camp offers wellness treatments at the medical center. Qualified pediatricians work here. Therapeutic rooms are equipped with high-quality equipment and medications. With a disease of the respiratory system, inhalations based on herbal preparations are used. Also, children with circulatory diseases, pathological processes of the nervous system and gastrointestinal disorders are treated here.
