Holidays in Turkey: Royal Palm Resort. Royal Palm Resort: description, number of rooms, reviews

The small resort village of Goynuk (Turkey) on the Mediterranean coast has long been a pilgrimage site for millions of tourists from all over the world and is especially loved by our compatriots. It belongs to the no less famous province of Antalya and is located near the international airport and the city of Kemer. This place is called the Turkish Riviera. Numerous hotels at the foot of the mountains offer their guests first-class relaxation. Among them - Diamonds Club Kemer (formerly Royal Palm Resort). Royal Palm Resort 5 *, of course, it is worth considering, at least as an option for accommodation in the resort. Why? We will talk about this in more detail later, describing all the main advantages of this place and not forgetting about the reviews of travelers who have already visited it.





Royal Palm Resort 5 *: location and description

Diamonds Club Kemer is located on 43 thousand square meters of landscaped territory, 45 km from Antalya International Airport, within walking distance of a bus stop and a motorway, so hotel guests can easily reach nearby cities and attractions on their own or as part of a chosen excursion. As already mentioned, there is a large private territory and access to the sea with a private beach. The hotel was built in 1996, the last time the repair was carried out in 2012. The resort complex consists of one large building with 10 floors and villas of various comfort classes (a total of 140).

Number of rooms

Royal Palm Resort 5 * hotel is designed for a large number of guests. Total number of rooms - 324, including non-smoking and soundproofed. All are beautifully furnished, have smoke detectors, furnished terraces or balconies. There are five types of rooms:

  • Standard Room is the cheapest option. Room size is 23 m2, the main advantage is the view of the sea.
  • Villa Room is very similar to standard rooms, but located in the garden area. Area - within 20-28 m 2 .
  • Garden Room - rooms overlooking the garden with a total area excluding a balcony of 28 m 2 .
  • Family Room consists of two bedrooms connected by a door and one bathroom. Room size is from 40 to 47 m 2 .
  • Corner Room - a corner room with a picturesque view and a spacious balcony, the area is similar to the previous one.




Any accommodation option to choose from can offer you Royal Palm Resort. Royal Palm Resort is committed to providing guests with maximum comfort, high level of service. restaurants and bars

The chefs of the hotel with their team try to combine the most unique and interesting flavors and tastes of world cuisine in order to turn the guests' breakfast, lunch or dinner into a sumptuous meal. High gastronomic art is emphasized by the corresponding interior of the establishments and their design.

Going to Turkey, most tourists prefer to choose the all-inclusive system. She really acts as the most optimal for a long vacation and is largely economical. The ultra all-inclusive power system is what Royal Palm Resort specializes in. Royal Palm Resort 5 * serves a very rich buffet with the most refined and unusual dishes. At Fish Restaurant you will be offered the freshest seafood and fish from the Mediterranean, which will be prepared literally before your eyes.





The hotel offers several facilities where you can relax and sit in silence or in a noisy company. The lobby bar is open 24 hours a day in the spacious lobby of the complex. In the evenings, it plays live music, and visitors are offered cocktails and other drinks (alcoholic and non-alcoholic). From morning until six in the evening there is a beach bar, and from eleven until two in the night there is a disco with all the riches of oriental snacks. All this offers its guests Royal Palm Resort. Reviews about the service are positive. Good cuisine is the hobby of the hotel, a kind of brand that he is trying to keep.

Spa & wellness

Rest at the resort is unthinkable without wellness and relaxing treatments. Turkey knows a lot about this. The hotel offers its guests the services of a fitness center (if necessary, you can contact a doctor and a nurse), a vitamin bar, various spa treatments, a traditional Turkish bath, a jacuzzi and saunas, popular massages, and relaxation rooms. All this is usually included in the price of the permit.

Royal Palm Resort (Turkey) 5 *: tourist reviews





Estimates of travelers not only from our country, but also from all over the world regarding the designated hotel are often contradictory and subjective. In general, we can say that it is not for capricious and pampered guests. More focused on young families, couples, companies. Hotel guests note its not very fresh look, which is not surprising, because it was built 20 years ago, but for all that, the rooms are clean, the cleaning is regular, and the furniture is solid. Negative reviews are offset by marks that the beach is excellent, as are the views around. In general, visitors put not a five, but a solid four to the Royal Palm Resort. Royal Palm Resort involves a budget holiday, but it is not without comfort and quality service, vivid impressions and delicious food, picturesque panoramas around.
