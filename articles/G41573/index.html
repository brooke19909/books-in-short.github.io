Review of the Soviet camera "Viliya"

The camera "Viliya" (USSR) was produced by the Belarusian Optical and Mechanical Company. Several modifications were available to customers, which differed only in terms of exposure. The body and lens are unchanged.

About 3 million units were produced by Viliya-Avto and Viliya cameras.



Technical details

The Viliya-Auto camera has few functions. The manufacturer is BelOMO. It was produced for nine years: from 1974 to 1985. It is a small format camera. To work with pictures, a film of type 135 is used. The frame has a size of 24 √ó 36 mm. Shutter of the central type. Focusing is done manually. A special distance scale is available. The exposition is also exhibited by the photographer himself. Sync contact is used for flash. An optical viewfinder is installed. The total mass of the device is 450 g.



Description of appearance

The shutter button is located rather uncomfortably, but many owners like its move. On the top panel of the device, you can see the flash shoe. Immediately located a special roulette, which is responsible for rewinding the film back. If we talk about the advantages of design and ergonomics, then you need to notice the manual adjustment of shutter speed in the Viliya model. In the previous version of "Auto" this was not.

The side surfaces are made as simple and concise as possible. There are no patterns on them, made in a gray shade. On one of the faces you can notice the presence of a sync terminal connector. There was also a button to open the back cover.

On the right side there is nothing but the same key. There are several options on the bottom surface at once. In the center is a button that is responsible for rewinding. Nearby, to the right and to the left of it, you can notice the frame counter and tripod socket. It is slightly offset to the side. On the back of the camera is a special trigger for fast forward the frame. There is also a viewfinder and a sensitivity scale for the film used.

There is nothing remarkable inside the camera. The standard design that is used in all such cameras. Having opened the back, you can notice the receiving coil and the transport type asterisk.
