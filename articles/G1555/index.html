Hotels in Yasnaya Polyana: description, reviews

One of the main attractions of the Tula region is the estate "Yasnaya Polyana", in which the Russian writer Leo Tolstoy lived and created his great creations. Many tourists come here every year to feel the atmosphere of this place, to touch the history. At the end of an eventful day, you are invited to relax comfortably at the Yasnaya Polyana hotels.

Hotel complex "Yasnaya Polyana"

The hotel is located in a protected area, consists of two buildings - the main five-story building and a two-story VIP building. The Museum-Estate of Leo Tolstoy can be reached from here in just 15 minutes. Meals are organized in your own restaurant, there is a cafe and a bar. Tourists can park their car in free parking. There is a service for organizing excursions, a library, a gift shop. The modern equipped conference room hosts business meetings, seminars and conferences. For lovers of picnics, barbecue areas are arranged, fans of an active lifestyle can play billiards, ride a horse, walk trekking routes. To accommodate guests, the Yasnaya Polyana Hotel in the Tula Region offers the following room categories:

  • single standard - 2200 rubles per day;
  • double standard - 3400 rubles per day;
  • double suite (one-room) - 4400 rubles per day;
  • double suite (two-room) - 4900 rubles per day for two, and 5400 rubles per day for two people.

Room rates include breakfast. The hotel complex is located at: Yasnaya Polyana, Tula region, Tormakhova street, 20.

Park Hotel Grumant

The distance to the museum-estate from the hotel is 3.5 kilometers. This place is preferred by tourists who are interested in a high level of comfort and the maximum number of services at the hotels of Yasnaya Polyana (Tula). Guests can use the services of the wellness center, bowling, indoor pool, indoor and outdoor sports complex, private beach. The hotel has two restaurants serving European cuisine. There is a playground for young tourists. Here you can rent a bicycle, ski equipment. Upon request, an airport shuttle can be arranged for you. The hotel has an ATM, vending machines with food and drinks. A tour desk and ticket service are available. Parking and wireless internet are free. For accommodation, several categories of rooms are offered, including:

  • the most inexpensive, double standard - 3900 rubles per day;
  • the most expensive, two-level studio - 9000 rubles per day.

All rooms include breakfast. The park-hotel "Grumant" is located at: Grumant village, Yasnaya Polyana.

Mini-hotel "Estate Varvarovka"

The inn is located nine kilometers from the Yasnaya Polyana Museum-Estate. On the territory there is a playground for children, a dining area is equipped, a place for picnic lovers is equipped. Guests can leave their vehicles in free parking. Wireless internet is also free. Vacationers have the opportunity to cook in the shared kitchen. You can live with pets, this requires a preliminary request. The following room categories are offered for accommodation:

  • double budget - 2600 rubles per day for two, and 2300 rubles per day for one person;
  • a room with two double beds - from 2300 to 3200 rubles per day, depending on the number of residents;
  • family suite (there is a balcony) - from 2700 to 3600 rubles per day, depending on the number of residents.

Room rates include continental breakfast. located mini-hotel "Estate Varvarovka" at the address: village Varvarovka, st. Skuratovskaya, 10 B.
