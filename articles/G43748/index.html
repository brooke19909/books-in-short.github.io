If you are chasing round-the-clock parties and can not imagine your life without constant movement, then you may not like this place - it's too calm. But if you want to get complete peace and relaxation, then there is probably no better place for this.





Here the family and the couple in love, and the hermit man who is tired of the bustle of the big city, will rest. Many people who visit the Palmira Beach Resort Spa call its villas a paradise. Maybe there really was a garden of Eden somewhere here?

Room Amenities

The level of comfort corresponds to a three-star mark, so all rooms at Palmira Beach Resort Spa 3 * have either a balcony or access to the courtyard. There is a shower, a bathroom is in superior rooms. An oversized double bed will perfectly decorate moments of relaxation in the room.

You will definitely enjoy a minibar, satellite TV, air conditioning and free bottled water. All rooms are equipped with toilets. All rooms of the boarding house are divided by partitions.





Here it is possible to share a person in a room at half price. And this means that you can not necessarily go together or three together (with a child). You can go on vacation and alone.

Rooms are cleaned every day, and towels are changed. There can be no claims to cleanliness!

Spa treatments

The list of procedures is so long that it may seem endless. I would like to try everything several times.

Massage is available everywhere where it is possible to take a lying position: on the beach, in the room, and in the SPA center. The procedures are both relaxing and deeply warming, and healing. Hot stone massage and Thai are also available.

Several types of treatment procedures, such as hydrotherapy and reflexology, are performed here. Available sauna, hammam, spa or mud bath, steam room. In addition, many cosmetic procedures are performed: manicure, pedicure, peeling, and more.

sights

Directly on the private territory of the resort, all types of water sports are available: diving, surfing, windsurfing, kayaking, water skiing and a bike - everything you need for positive and adrenaline. But those who are interested in long walks and historical monuments should get into the city on their own, although a lot of field trips are organized on the spot (at the hotel). And quite often, the Palmira Beach Resort Spa receives positive reviews precisely because amazing places and activities are available here, thanks to which you can decorate your vacation for a loyal price.

White dunes, surrounded by a freshwater pond and a white lake, are covered with low trees. The lake earned its name due to the flowering of lotus on its surface. The unique combination of blue and white colors will create a breathtaking backdrop for photos. And the opportunity to ride a quad bike will slightly warm the blood.

The fabulous creek Suoy Tien is a small river that flows along a miniature grand canyon with red and yellow layers. The main attraction on this excursion is just the waterfall with which the river ends, splashing with myriad drops and forming several rainbows around. Such a landscape will bring only peace.





Climbing Mount Taku is another option to have a good time. The summit is a sacred place for Buddhists. Well, for the lay people there is a cableway and a safe climb lasting 10 minutes.

An amazing trip to the rainforest, where at a height you can hear the creak and the noise of trees, as if these are the sounds of their growth, you cannot but like. On the mountain is a statue of a sleeping Buddha, which the whole world seeks to touch. Touch you too!
