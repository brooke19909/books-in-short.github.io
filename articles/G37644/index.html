This water park is open all year. It is open to absolutely everyone without any age restrictions, whether we are at least a pensioner, at least a small child.

It is in the Limpopo water park in Yekaterinburg that you can relax with a variety of companies, namely a large family, a group of pensioners, a huge friendly company and many others.
