The pearl of the Black Sea resorts - the beautiful city of Sochi promises to soon become even more attractive, thanks to the Absolute residential complex being built right in the center. Sochi, already filled with modern new buildings, will be enriched in another building.





Location of the complex

The Absolute residential complex very successfully found a place in the center of Sochi on Ostrovsky Street. But it will not catch the eye of passers-by, and car traffic will not cause inconvenience, since it is not cross-cutting on the indicated street.

At the same time, residents of the new residential complex will be at the center of all city events. And from the windows of the building you can enjoy the sea, mountains and city life.

Description of the residential complex

The project of the Absolute complex (Sochi) involves the construction of a single-section 21-story building with 224 apartments by the end of the second quarter of 2017. The first five floors will be given to the functioning of various offices, shops and other facilities that provide social services to the population.

The remaining floors will be occupied by happy residents. It also provides for the fencing of the territory of the Absolute residential complex. Sochi cares about the safety of its residents.





In the house, the developer will perform the following work:

  • installation of entrance metal doors;
  • glazing with plastic windows;
  • holding high-speed Internet, digital television.

Communications provided central. And for the convenience of residents, the apartments will be equipped with individual heating systems. In addition, the right to elevate tenants to their apartments is given to three high-speed silent elevators.
