We are going on vacation to the village Utes (Crimea). Description, infrastructure, reviews

The Crimean peninsula is washed by two seas: from the north-east - Azov, from the south and west - Black. Until 2014, administratively related to Ukraine, now it is the territory of the Russian Federation. On the coast of the peninsula there are more than 40 settlements, which are popular resort areas. One of these can be called the village of Utes (Crimea). Reviews of tourists about the service, infrastructure, beach deserve special attention, so we will talk about this in more detail later. In the meantime, let's find out more about the village itself.





Utes Village: Description

This settlement is located on the southern coast of Crimea. This territory of the peninsula is the most popular resort area. The village itself is very small. Its area does not even reach 1 square. km Previously, it had other names - Kuchuk-Lambat, Karasan, and until 1968 - Cozy. Submits to the Alushta City Council. Utes village (Crimea) is inhabited by only 264 people (data for 2014). Of these, about 80% are Russians. A local attraction can be considered the embankment lined with concrete slabs. There are many tourists walking here in the summer season, while locals set up tents with souvenirs. Many vacationers visit the park, which was created back in 1813-1814. Its area reaches almost 8 hectares. Since the 19th century it has been a protected architectural monument.





Economic development

Utes village (Crimea), whose photo can be viewed in the article, develops only in the tourism sector. It is located only ten kilometers from Alushta, which allows visitors to easily get to the chosen vacation spot. Local residents earn only in the swimming season, it was at this time that the largest number of tourists. In the village there are many hotels that are happy to welcome guests.

Rest in the village of Utes is distinguished by its calm and quiet manner. The air here is clean, untagged. The water warms up well. There are free beaches. They are equipped with sun loungers and umbrellas, but places are rented for a fee.

Beaches

Utes village (Crimea) will delight tourists with a variety of beaches. There are pebble, stone (wild) zones, as well as piers. The latter are not particularly popular with vacationers, but here all the same for the convenience of sun loungers. On wild beaches, photoshoots are often arranged on the rocks. The pictures get amazing. On the territory of the sanatorium "Utes" there is an equipped beach. Everything that is needed for a comfortable stay is installed here - changing cabins, deck chairs, sun umbrellas. And on the promenade "Karasan" is a concrete beach, there are also private ones.
