Siam Beach Resort 3 * (Thailand, Koh Chang): room description, service, reviews

Koh Chang Island in Thailand is a picturesque place washed by the waters of the Gulf of Thailand. The word "chang" in translation means "elephant", so many people call this place of rest Elephant Island. Compared to Phuket or Samui, the resort business has begun to develop quite recently in this remote land area. A few years ago, Russian speech was not heard there. Rich people from Bangkok came to rest in this beautiful place, despite the distance of 315 km.

Now this place pleases with many hotels and coastal hotels, one of which was Siam Beach Resort. Tourists love to stay in it because of silence and privacy. The place is part of the marine protected area, so water sports are prohibited there. Surrounding the place are mountains covered with pristine jungles and pristine white sand beaches.

This article will introduce readers to a three-star hotel on the shore of the Gulf of Thailand called Siam Beach Resort, located on the island of Koh Chang. We will also study the reviews that tourists leave after a trip to the island, note the positive and be sure to look for flaws so that you are prepared for any surprises before the trip.

Description

Siam Beach Resort is located on the first line of the beach. This is a small hotel with 84 rooms. It is best to get to it by transfer from Trat Airport, and then by ferry. As mentioned earlier, this is a secluded place. All shops and restaurants are within a 15-minute walk of the hotel. But right from the windows, tourists can enjoy the azure sea.

In addition to rooms in two-story houses, you can rent a separate villa with all amenities with a total area of ‚Äã‚Äã45 m2.

Room Types

The number of rooms Siam Beach Resort is divided into three types:

  • Premium Sea View Beach Front. These are the most expensive rooms, from the door of which tourists step directly onto the white sand of the beach.
  • The Premium Sea View is located in Building D and C, where there are connecting rooms with an interior door between them.
  • Premium Sea View Garden differ from other rooms in the presence of a double bed. These are inexpensive rooms that are located on the second line from the beach. To descend to the sea you need to go through a small bridge.

All rooms are stylishly decorated, however there are rooms from which you can see the sea, and there are windows facing the opposite side.

Garden rooms fell in love with young couples who wanted privacy. Connecting rooms are suitable for large families or groups of friends.

Room conditions

According to visitors' reviews, all rooms are comfortable and stylish, similar to each other. The only difference is the presence of a bathtub or shower, and whether or not there is a TV. In every room of Siam Beach Resort 3 Koh Chang there is the most necessary: ‚Äã‚Äãbeds for rest, air conditioning, wardrobes for storing things, a shower to refresh yourself after a beach holiday.

In more expensive rooms there are plasma TVs, a minibar, a small refrigerator, and a kettle. Each room has a telephone for communication with staff. You can order an alarm service or a meal in the room from the restaurant.

Bathrooms

Plumbing in the hotel is new, modern and clean. The bathrooms are stylish and all toiletries are provided upon arrival. There is a hairdryer and a set of towels.

All bathrooms have a water heater, so water treatments will be pleasant.

In many rooms, instead of a bath, a shower stall, which many even liked. Near the washbasin there is a spacious table for accessories, so that you can conveniently arrange all the cosmetics and hygiene products.

Spacious verandas

Room Description Siam Beach Resort will be incomplete if we do not stop at the wide verandas that are in each room.

It's nice to sit in the evening with a glass of wine or a cup of fragrant tea on natural wicker chairs, feel the freshness of the sea breeze and enjoy the sunset. Nobody will prevent you from being alone with your loved one, because noisy restaurants and shops are in the distance, which attracts silence lovers to this hotel.

Additional services

Despite the beautiful beach within walking distance with free sun loungers and umbrellas, there is a large pool near the hotel. Since Koh Chang is the Elephant Island, there are several statues of elephants on the territory, including the pool. According to visitors' reviews, absolutely everyone had a place to swim and sunbathe, sometimes it was possible to be in complete solitude.

The hotel provides safe and laundry services, an ironing room and a left-luggage office. It is prudent to arrange a transfer from the airport and on the way back, because getting to the hotel is not easy. The path takes a couple of hours, but according to the reviews of tourists who visited this place, it is worth it to get to such a picturesque corner on the farthest island of Thailand.

In those rooms that have a TV, cable channels work, including one Russian. Every year more and more tourists from Russia and the countries of the former Soviet Union come to Thailand, so do not be surprised if a smiling local Russian-speaking girl meets you at the reception.

If you want to travel alone on the island, you can order a rental car directly at the hotel, especially since the parking lot will be free. For lovers of organized trips to the sights at the hotel there is a tour desk where you can join a group of compatriots.

Nutrition

Siam Beach Resort reviews provide an overview of breakfast in the hotel restaurant. Although they are served on a buffet basis, many describe the set of products as traditional for three-star hotels in the world. These are boiled eggs or scrambled eggs, sausages, meat and cheese slices, cereal and pancakes, jam for sandwiches, drinks - tea, coffee, juices. Rice and stewed vegetables are also prepared. Adds a lot of positive fruits.

For lunch and dinner, tourists go to a cafe in the village, which is a 15-minute walk along a sandy beach. Prices are low, and the food is all tasty and fresh. Many heartily ate seafood. There is even a restaurant of Russian cuisine, which (according to reviews) serves excellent borsch.

Opinions of tourists

The quality of service at Siam Beach Resort, as well as in other places of rest, is easy to determine by reading the reviews of tourists who visited there. About the hotel on the island of Koh Chang, opinions of people agree. This is just a wonderful place to relax, where you can stay in nature and enjoy the silence. They note a beautiful and clean beach, warm water of the bay. Many were engaged in diving and admired the beauty of the underwater world of the island.

Among the shortcomings, some noted poor soundproofing in the rooms, tourists were prevented by the sound of the waves falling asleep at night. Many may not agree with this opinion, but this should be taken into account for people with a weak nervous system. It is also advised not to leave any products on the veranda, as it attracts local monkeys at night.

We hope that the information received will help you make a choice in favor of this hotel, because after noisy megalopolises many people want to relax in nature.
