The recreation center "Batiliman" (Crimea): description, service, reviews

An endless number of people come to this place every year who want to relax in an unusual and healthy way. The area is a forest belt saturated with soul-taking species. Walking along the paths along the sea, you can meet a diverse fauna. Of the trees, a detachment of conifers predominates.

Above the burning expanses, majestically stands Mount Kush-Kaya, the translation of the name of which means "bird mountain." Tourists who have been here, strongly recommend climbing the mountain, as it offers the best views, especially it will be useful to people of creative activity.

The landscapes around the Batiliman recreation center, which we will examine in more detail today, will not leave anyone indifferent, they will plunge him into the world of dreams and aesthetic pleasure. This area is very appreciated by photography enthusiasts, artists and divers. Naturalists take advantage of the fact that places here are full of the most exciting imaginations, giving a huge field for creativity. Scuba divers argue that cleaner waters cannot be found on the entire peninsula, so you can dive in the local waters without worrying that the underwater world will be hidden from you. At the bottom of the bay in the tract is a sunken ship, which many diving explore with interest. By the way, the ship is covered with a veil of secrecy, because until now it is not known in which country it began to exist, this puzzle is still being fought for.





Location

The recreation center is located only 35 km from the city of Sevastopol in the bay of Laspi, Batiliman Valley.

As many tourists note in their positive reviews, the administration of the recreation center tried to make the stay on its territory as comfortable as possible. It provides the most comfortable cottages made of wood, located right in the middle of the park. Tourists also have furnished rooms in the buildings of the Batiliman recreation center (Crimea), which are not inferior in terms of comfort to the prestigious apartments of four- and five-star hotels and will please everyone with their convenience.

There are 200 places in all. Even during the high season, no one remains deprived.

Nutrition

The recreation center "Batiliman" often receives positive reviews for catering. The guests here are fed very tasty and satisfying three times a day, and if someone finds the diet insufficient, for an additional fee of 550 r. You can fully eat at any time of the day.

As tourists note, the cuisine menu is far from monotonous. Every day, guests are delighted with the most delicious dishes, while being balanced so that no one experiences overeating or discomfort in the stomach.





Beach

At the recreation center "Batiliman" (Sevastopol) there is a personal well-maintained beach with sand and pebbles, the road to which is only a quarter kilometer from the buildings. Paying for accommodation, vacationers get constant access to the beach, all the entertainment and services at the base.

Water

Guests will always be provided with a stream of water: cold water flows continuously, while hot water is provided by a modern system for converting solar radiation into heat (solar system) and conventional boilers. After swimming, you can use the outdoor shower.





Infrastructure

According to the guests in their positive reviews, the infrastructure here is very developed. For lovers of strong drinks, a bar with a wide variety of choices and, most importantly, located directly above the sea will be a suitable place for this. So the stay here will take place in an extremely picturesque atmosphere. You can relax on the territory of the entire base - equipped places literally at every step. Film lovers will also find something to do - film screenings are held every evening in the open air. The reading room is rich in literature for every taste. You can stretch yourself while playing ping pong, for this all the necessary sports equipment and equipment are provided. In case of injuries or illnesses, a highly qualified doctor will help tourists at the local medical center.





Discount system

If your children are less than five years old, then they can live for free without providing a bed and meals. Optionally, you can purchase a case for 500 rubles per day. You can buy a place in a house without comfort conditions for 300 rubles per day, another place in houses with amenities will cost 400 rubles in 24 hours. At the recreation center "Batiliman" you can relax with children of any age. If tourists arrived in their car, then you can park it in the territory for 100 rubles per day.

Check-in is from 12 noon, check-out is up to 10. Early check-in before check-out time (from 07.00 to 12.00) and late check-out after check-out time (from 12.00 to 20.00) is paid hourly in proportion to the room rate per day (1 hour = room rate / 24 hours) . The recreation center "Batiliman" operates from April to September. Everyone will have time to relax, but it is advisable to pre-book rooms. The telephone number of the Batiliman recreation center can be found on its official website.





How to get there

Public transport is in constant activity, which you can always use after reaching the Laspi Bay stop. However, choosing this method, you will have to take a short walk: you will need to go down to the sea about 3 km straight to the Batiliman tract. It is not difficult to get from Sevastopol by private car. You just need to go along the highway to the city, and from Sevastopol to Yalta to the Batiliman tract.

For maximum convenience, you can use an individual transfer. From the Sevastopol airport, the journey in a comfortable car will cost 2500 rubles.

Having chosen the recreation center "Batiliman", tourists will provide themselves with unforgettable impressions throughout the rest time. The staff will welcome and serve everyone very cordially, and the living conditions will make you feel in comfort, like a home.
