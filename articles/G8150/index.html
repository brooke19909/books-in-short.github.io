The best hotels of Sri Lanka: reviews and photos

Sri Lanka is an amazing place to relax. Most of all tourists are pleased with the prices in local hotels. Moreover, the best hotels in Sri Lanka (five-star) offer prices 1.5 times less than similar hotel complexes in Indonesia or Thailand.

Which hotel to choose?

Sri Lanka hotels offer great opportunities for guests. If you travel there, you can book a room that includes good nutrition, or find a simpler option. All hotels in Ceylon are divided into two groups: colonial-age Sri Lanka hotels (located in the cities of Kandy, Nuwara Eliya, Bandaravela), which create an atmosphere of charm and romantic mood of the past years, as well as more modern hotels located on local islands. In Ceylon, hotels are not divided according to certain classifications, but tour operators according to the principle of the classification system are divided into different groups.

Each hotel has its own unique style, in which traditions and local flavor are combined with natural beauties. Hotels provide guests with various opportunities for hanging out. For example, theme nights, ethnic and cultural shows, fashion viewing, barbecue in the moonlight, fishing, picnic, excursions and more. Some hotels offer water sports: windsurfing, water skiing, sailing, scuba diving, fishing on the high seas.

A striking island near the Indian coast, on which live friendly, pleasant people. In this area the warm sun constantly shines, unique cozy beaches. Staying in the best hotels will make your vacation incredibly enjoyable and your vacation unforgettable. Excellent conditions offers travelers Sri Lanka. Five-star hotels welcome guests all year round. This article will showcase the best placements in Sri Lanka.

Boutique - hotel W15 (Weligama)

Weligama (W15) is a five-star hotel with 10 rooms. Located near the city with the same name. A hotel with a unique design that combines minimalist style and incredible nature. The main advantage of the hotel complex, according to vacationers, is its successful geographical location near the sea coast and away from the bustle of the city. And this opens up opportunities for real relaxation. Themed rooms, with a discreet design, beautiful furniture and household appliances, are equipped with media centers and temperature control systems.

Sri Sharavi Beach Vilas & Spa

The modern Sri Sharavi Beach Vilas & Spa has an excellent spa complex located in the Mirissa - South resort. The hotel area is dotted with forks for guests, there is a restaurant with colorful cuisine, spa treatments. The combination of modern brilliance and new technologies open the hotel in a very favorable light for guests. Cozy villas use not only traditional energy-saving systems, but also solar panels. All rooms are equipped with the necessary for a comfortable stay: furniture, appliances, air conditioning. The villas are located on the first coastline, verandas overlook the ocean. Spending time at breakfast will bring a lot of pleasure from watching the magnificent sunrise.

The hotel restaurant is one of the best in the resort. Gourmets can experience the peak of pleasure from the meals of skilled chefs, dishes of world and local cuisine, in addition - wines from the collection of local wineries. The hotel can hold wedding ceremonies, various family celebrations, corporate events. Excellent living conditions, round-the-clock service make your vacation unforgettable. The guests will remember for a long time the memory of a pleasant time spent among tropical nature.

The high-level hotel Edem Resort 5 * is located on the ocean in the city of Beruwela, near the airport and the capital. It is truly a paradise on the southwestern coast of Ceylon. Reconstruction was carried out in 2013.

Royal Palms 5 *

The hotel is located on the coastline of the Indian Ocean, 74 km from the airport, 40 km from the capital. Nearby is the city of Kalutara. The hotel is recognized as one of the best complexes on the southwest coast. The construction is made in a combination of brick and wood, and all this gives a special atmosphere. Three-story buildings blend in well with the greenery around the jacuzzi pool.

Blue Water Resort 5 *

Since 2013, a new building with 40 rooms has been added. In it you can have a great time with your family, with good friends. Huge rooms, along the entire hotel building a swimming pool, a buffet with delicious food. Near the hotel there is a restaurant with fresh seafood. To get to the city, you can walk 15 minutes.

Cheaper four-star hotels can also offer excellent conditions. The bungalow hotel has been operating since 2012. It is located on the ocean and attracts tourists with the modern benefits of civilization, excellent service. A huge selection of entertainment is provided for guests.

Chaaya Tranz 4 *

Hotel Chaaya Tranz 4 * is located in a picturesque place of Hikkaduwa, on the coast of Ceylon. Advantageous location makes unforgettable pastime in it. Near the hotel there is a small lagoon in which corals are visible on the surface of the water, creating an underwater grove Paradise Isle. The comfortable hotel is surrounded by exotic vegetation. Guests can enjoy a pleasant stay and various activities.

Tangerine Beath 4 *

The complex is located near the Indian Ocean, near the city of Kalutara. The hotel is bordered by Royal Palms Beath, so guests can enjoy the services of both complexes. You can use the pool and park area.

The Sands 4 *

Hotel The Sands 4 * is The Sands By Aitken Spence Hotel (ex. Ramada Resort), which has been updated. It is located near the Culatar. A free hotel in the midst of extraordinary nature for family vacations and corporate events.

Below are descriptions and photos of Sri Lanka hotels in 3 stars.

Villa Ocean 3 *

The hotel is located in Wadduwa, it is 40 kilometers from Colombo, 72 kilometers from the airport. Near the village there is a shop, a nice cafe. It is good to relax with the whole family. Customers can book a room both in the complex itself and nearby in two-story bungalows on the territory itself.

Hibiskus Beach Hotel 3 *

The hotel is located among tropical beauty in the village of Kulutara, 30 km from Colombo. A romantic mood reigns here , and leisure is imbued with the magic of beauty and peace. Being near Colombo, you can visit the shops of the capital, and for a change, look at a couple of nightclubs and discos in five-star hotels. Walking along the beach, you can drop into any restaurant with traditional local cuisine and fresh seafood. And most importantly - there are always few guests at the hotel.

Eva Lanka 3 *

Eva Lanka 3 * is built in the middle of a huge blooming garden. Near the hotel are coral reefs that protect the beach from high ocean waves and attract divers to this place from different parts of the world. The hotel will appeal to those who want to retire, relax from the noisy and annoying city. It is surrounded by tropical nature, located on a hill with excellent views of the ocean. Hotel visitors get the perfect quiet, relaxing break from the bustle of the city.

What do tourists say?

Hotel reviews of Sri Lanka are controversial. Someone speaks of chic conditions and excellent service, while others complain of discomfort. Therefore, before making a choice in favor of a particular institution, it is necessary to correctly identify priorities. The hotels described above have an excellent location and offer good facilities. Perhaps, in comparison with other hotel complexes they are not cheap, but it is better not to save on comfort, even if there is amazing nature and a gentle ocean.
