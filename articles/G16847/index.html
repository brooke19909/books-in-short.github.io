The best hotels in Mozhaisk: addresses, description

Ancient temples and many monuments of Mozhaisk, one of the oldest cities in the Moscow region, have always attracted the attention of tourists. In addition, the city is conveniently located near the Minsk highway leading to Moscow, so travelers often choose it for recreation. And to stay here left a good impression, hotels in Mozhaisk will offer you comfortable rooms and a high level of service.

Upper Miz Hotel

The hotel is located in the village of Miz, eight kilometers from the city and the train station. The Borodino Military History Museum Reserve is just a fifteen minute drive from the hotel. Mozhayskoe reservoir is located within walking distance, where fishing enthusiasts come, as well as the outpost equestrian base, which is why horseback enthusiasts have also chosen this place.

Free on-site parking and Wi-Fi access are available on site. For a surcharge, guests can relax in the sauna. There is no own cafe in the mini-hotel, but within walking distance there is a cafe-bar where you can have a bite to eat, as well as a supermarket where you can buy the necessary products and cook in the shared kitchen equipped with everything you need. For accommodation, rooms of the following categories are offered:

  • double standard - 2000 rubles per day;
  • family - 2000 rubles per day;
  • Suite - 3000 rubles per day for two people, 3500 for three people and 4000 for four.

All rooms are equipped with a TV, a refrigerator, and air-conditioned rooms in the suites. Among the hotels in Mozhaisk, the Upper Miz Hotel has the highest rating. The mini-hotel is located at: Miz, Marfin Broad, Mozhaysk.

Hotel Upper Miz: reviews of tourists

  • At the Upper Miz Hotel, guests praised the location near the Mozhaisk Reservoir.
  • The rooms are bright, comfortable, with a fresh repair.
  • The hotel staff is polite and attentive, populate quickly.
  • The hotel has a large reliable parking behind a concrete fence, the gates open remotely.
  • There is no cafe, but there is a good common kitchen where you can cook something yourself.
  • The family room and suite are pleased to have a kitchenette.
  • The windows offer great views.
  • Most guests consider accommodation here to be the best option compared to other Mozhaysk hotels in the Moscow Region.

Hotel Maria

Mini Hotel Maria is just a 10-minute walk from the city center. Within walking distance there is a supermarket and the city market. To get to a restaurant or cafe, guests need to walk one and a half kilometers. Nearby is the museum of local lore, the Peter and Paul Church, the Chapel of St. Nicholas the Wonderworker. At the hotel, guests can take advantage of free private parking, as well as wireless Internet. On the second floor there is a bright dining room. For accommodation, guests are offered the following room options:

  • double with two single beds - 2300 rubles per day;
  • double deluxe room with a large double bed - 2300 rubles per day.

All rooms are soundproofed, equipped with cable TV, a private bathroom with a hairdryer, and free toiletries. Hotel Maria can be given the second place in the rating among hotels in Mozhaisk. It is located at: st. Communist, building 13.

Hotel Maria: reviews of tourists

  • Guests enjoyed the hotel's convenient location. Driving by car is no problem, parking is right in front of the entrance.
  • Lovely clean and comfortable rooms, bed linens selected with taste. Everything is provided, up to disposable toothbrushes.

  • Two minutes walk from the hotel there are shops, a little further - Sberbank and central street.
  • The hotel is located near the train station, it is very convenient to stop by the passage.
  • Tourists believe that the convenient location, cost of living and wonderful conditions make the mini-hotel "Maria" one of the best options among the hotels in the city of Mozhaisk.

"Station, 27"

Bus and train stations are just a five-minute walk away, and the city center is a 20-minute walk away. Residents of the Vokzalnaya 27 Hotel have the opportunity to relax on the terrace, use the services of free parking and wireless Internet. Several cafes are located nearby. To accommodate guests, several types of rooms are offered:

  • standard - 2000 rubles a day for two and 2600 rubles for three guests;
  • Junior Suite - respectively 2,300 and 2,900 rubles per day;
  • Superior junior suite - 2600 rubles per day for two and 3300 rubles for three residents;
  • place in a four-bed dormitory room - 600 rubles per day;
  • a place in a common eight-bed room - 700 rubles per day.

Most rooms have a TV. Among other hotels in Mozhaisk, "Vokzalnaya 27" stands out for its budget accommodation options. It is located at: st. Vokzalnaya, house 27. You can contact the hotel by phone, whose number is on the official website.

Hotel "Baltic" in Mozhaisk

When checking into the hotel, a security deposit of 500 rubles is required. After checking the condition of the property of the room after the end of the stay, this amount will be refunded. In the morning, the hotel serves breakfast, and there is free parking. If you travel along the Minsk highway, in Mozhaysk at the Baltiyskaya hotel you can relax. The hotel offers several categories of rooms:

  • economy (conveniences on the floor) - from 600 rubles per day for one person;
  • standard with two single beds and an extra bed - from 2000 rubles per day, for an extra bed a fee of 750 rubles is charged;
  • junior suite double - from 2300 rubles per day;
  • double suite - from 2500 rubles per day.

The fee for placement on extra beds in suites and junior suites is 600 rubles per person. For regular customers the hotel has a discount system. The Baltiyskaya Hotel is located at: Mozhaisk, Moscow Region, pos. Red Baltic, house 40.
