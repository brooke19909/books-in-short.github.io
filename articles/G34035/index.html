Lenovo A7600 (Lenovo): specifications and customer reviews

Despite the fact that modern smartphones have become an integral part of many people's lives, tablets are still popular. Especially those with good screen resolution. For example, Lenovo A7600. 10.1 inches allow you to watch movies and videos, surf the Internet and communicate on social networks without straining your eyesight. But this is not all that distinguishes the tablet.



Appearance

In its price category (8-11 thousand rubles), the Lenovo A7600 stands out for its design. Firstly, his back panel is rubberized. And this allows you to conveniently hold the device in your hands, not being afraid that it will slip out with careless movement. It is noteworthy that the back panel is really rubberized, so many owners think that the tablet is very pleasant to the touch. And there are almost no traces on it.

Secondly, the tablet itself is made of high-quality plastic, which does not creak, does not play. This creates the feeling of a monolithic product in the hands, since the upper glass (without an oleophobic coating, unfortunately) smoothly "spreads" over the device. There are no gaps between the screen and the side faces.

Thirdly, the tablet‚Äôs spread is landscape. For a diagonal of 10.1 inches, this is the most optimal and convenient option. Two cameras (external and front) are tailored precisely to landscape screen orientation. Which also adds convenience when using the device.



Cameras: external and frontal

If you choose a tablet according to the parameters, then the quality of shooting should definitely not be in the first place. If only because the devices were originally designed not for this. The front camera of the Lenovo A7600 is presented with a resolution of 2 megapixels. Not the best, but for a tablet this is enough to communicate via video. The external camera acquired a resolution of 5 megapixels, which is also enough to make good photos in good light. Autofocus is not provided for the tablet, but this is not the main thing in devices of this kind.

Screen

Perhaps this is the main advantage, despite the small resolution of 1280x800 pixels. Viewing angles and brightness make the Lenovo A7600 tablet really cool even with such a screen. Firstly, even when installed on a book-case, the device does not lose its clarity. Everything is visible in the same way as in direct viewing. Secondly, in bright sunlight, the brightness of the screen is not lost, like many models in this price category. Everything is clearly visible, which is very important in the summer.



Interfaces

There are not so many of them. It is important to familiarize yourself with them with those who choose the tablet according to the parameters. So, for example, there is a microUSB connector, which is also a USB host. What does it mean? This means that the tablet supports the work of additional accessories. For example, a removable USB flash drive with a microUSB connector. Or any other device through a special adapter. There is also an output for standard size 3.5 mm headphones. But the video output is not provided by the manufacturer, so connecting the tablet to the TV will not work. A microSD card slot is present, which allows you to increase the internal memory up to 32 GB. Unfortunately, a larger volume is not supported. The internal memory is 16 GB, of which only 14 GB is available.



Speakers and sound

Two speakers are located on the front side of the tablet, which provides sound to the user, not from him. But when using it, many owners noted that one of the speakers periodically turns out to be covered with fingers, which degrades the sound. The latter, by the way, is not too good: there is not enough bass. Therefore, some tracks do not sound very interesting. And sometimes even flat and dull. But good headphones can fix the situation, the main thing is that they have bass and the ability to control the equalizer. In this case, even game explosions and pops will seem deafening and realistic.

operating system

The manufacturer installed Android 4.2 on the tablet. For 2015, this is a fairly outdated system, although stable. If desired, the user can independently upgrade the device to a more modern version or flash it. The firmware can be found on the official website of the manufacturer. Programs for the tablet will have to be installed from the "Play Store", although some are already pre-installed. For example, Skype or Kingston Office. You don‚Äôt have to download them already, you can safely use it. Some tablet programs can slow down slightly if you do not upgrade the operating system to a more recent version. This should be considered in advance, even before installation. It is recommended to update the system immediately after purchase, so that using the tablet brings only pleasure.



CPU

It depends on him how well the device will pull games and programs, how stable it will work. And here, it should be noted, everything is not so bad for a budget model. The 1.3 GHz Mediatek MT8121 - although standard for many, is pretty good. Firstly, all games (even WoT) work without lags and brakes. Secondly, when playing videos in both online and offline mode, no noticeable minuses are noted. Everything works clearly and smoothly. Nevertheless, among budget models, the tablet loses slightly to those with a more advanced processor (for example, Qualcomm). On the other hand, the price of devices with such color rendition and screen will be different. Here, Lenovo A7600 wins against its competitors.
