GPS-navigators for cars: selection, instructions, prices

Every motorist strives to ensure maximum safety when driving. Great helpers on the go - GPS navigators for cars. These are modern devices that are versatile and multifunctional. What are the modern navigators and products of which brand to give preference?

What is the point?





The GPS navigator today is a multifunctional device that has an accurate positioning system, so that the exact coordinates and route will be determined on the map of the area. Almost all modern models have voice alerts, so the driver does not need to be distracted while driving. In addition, GPS navigators for cars can have rich functionality that extends the capabilities of these accessories. The essence of their work is as follows:

  • The GPS navigation system includes 24 space satellites that move in the orbits of the Earth. They send radio signals to our planet, which are captured by ground-based devices.
  • After determining the signal, the navigator is able to determine the coordinates of the car and its route.

How to choose a navigator to serve reliably and stably?

First: choose a card

Modern GPS navigators for cars are based on navigation programs. The most popular are Navitel and Garmin. The first program is a software designed for PDAs, communicators and car navigators of a number of brands: Navitel, Prestigio, Explay, Lexand. The program works on the basis of detailed vector maps of cities and regions of Russia, where everything is indicated - from the metro station to the house number, which facilitates orientation in any region.





Garmin equips reliable navigators that are distinguished by build quality, simple menus and ease of use. The cartography of this company is constantly being improved, so you will always be in the know where you are in the most accurate details.

Second: choose a brand and functionality

High-quality GPS navigators for cars can be a reliable assistant in any traffic situation. Note that there are a huge number of brands producing similar products. The same brand offers navigators of different price categories - from budget to top-end. The former are simple, the latter have a complete set of functions. Some of the most sought after features include:

  1. Bluetooth and Hands Free: According to existing standards, talking on a mobile device while driving is prohibited. The built-in Bluetooth module can solve this problem, since you can communicate while driving wirelessly without using your hands. So, the movement will be as safe and comfortable as possible.
  2. Support for memory cards. If the device has a built-in slot for memory cards, then you can record any information into your device at any time.
  3. Entertainment. Video and audio player support are additional features that a GPS navigator can have. The price of such models is, of course, higher, but the functionality is much better.
