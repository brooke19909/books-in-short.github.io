Jomtien Thani Hotel (Thailand, Pattaya): description, photos and reviews of tourists

Thailand is an extremely popular resort among Russian tourists. You can relax in this country all year round. And therefore, to swim in the warm sea and enjoy the magnificent nature, it is not necessary to wait for a summer vacation. You can go to Thailand, for example, in December, February or March. Simply excellent, this resort is suitable for lovers of a relaxing beach or outdoor activities. And, of course, guests who came to Thailand have the opportunity to see many interesting sights.

Since the southern state receives the main income from the tourism business, literally everything is provided for the convenience of holidaymakers. Guests of Thailand can use well-equipped beaches, visit cafes and restaurants with excellent cuisine, have a good time in various kinds of entertainment venues. Well, of course, many comfortable hotels, both budget and expensive, have been built in this country.





Pattaya

This beautiful city among domestic tourists is considered to be one of the best resorts in Thailand. There are many good hotels in Pattaya, located on the east coast, as in many other places in this country. And most of them work including with Russian operators. According to many tourists, one of the most attractive and comfortable hotels in Pattaya of all, accepting Russian guests, is the Jomtien Thani Hotel. This hotel offers its visitors comfortable, not too expensive rooms and excellent service.

Where is the hotel located

This hotel is located in one of the quietest areas of the city - Jomtien. The exact address of the hotel: Pattaya (Pattaya), Jomtien Thani Hotel 3 *, 75/261 Moo 12 Jomtien Beach Road. From Utopao Airport, this hotel is just 40 km away. Of course, this is not too far. Some tourists come to the Thani Hotel to and from Bankoca International Airport. The hotel is 147 km from the property.

Of course, the easiest way to get to the hotel from both of these airports is to book an individual transfer first. Almost all hotels in this country offer a similar service to guests of Thailand. Of course, it is provided for in Jomtien Thani. True, in this case it will be necessary to pay for it separately.





Jomtien Thani Hotel: hotel description

The rooms in this hotel are for rent in one large (11 floors) building, built in 2000. In total, the hotel has 246 comfortable rooms. On the roof of the third floor there is a large, comfortable for swimming, equipped with all necessary pool (with hydromassage). In 2011, this hotel was renovated.

Jomtien Thani Hotel, the photos of the rooms and the territory of which can be seen on this page, has earned good reviews from tourists, including because it is located in close proximity to the sea. In addition, many vacationers consider the coastline itself in this place to be much more convenient for relaxation than in the center of Pattaya.

Number of rooms

The majority of rooms rented at this hotel are Superior (52 Double and 173 Twin rooms). Also, if desired, you can remove Deluxe (17) or Suite (4). Each room has a balcony and a private bathroom. In addition, for the convenience of guests are provided a refrigerator, air conditioning, telephone and TV. All rooms at the hotel are designed for 2 people. But if necessary, by prior arrangement with the administration in each room, you can order two more additional beds.

All rooms have tiled floors. If desired, a hairdryer can be used in the room. Television at the hotel has both satellite and cable. Most tourists keep documents and money in a safe installed directly in the room. There is also a minibar in the rooms at an additional cost. Wi-Fi in all rooms is free.





Hotel infrastructure

The rooms in this hotel, as you can see, are quite comfortable. In any case, they are equipped well. However, Jomtien Thani Hotel 3 * earned good reviews not only for its comfortable apartments. The infrastructure in this hotel is also very well developed. For the convenience of tourists, there are:

  • a restaurant;

  • sauna;

  • bar.

The hotel also has a gym and currency exchange. Most tourists leave their luggage in a special room. For a fee, guests can use the hotel's billiard room or meeting room. The hotel bar is located next to the rooftop pool.

Among other things, the paid services at the hotel include:

  • laundry service;

  • massage;

  • haircut at the hairdresser;

  • visiting a beauty salon.

For a fee, the hotel offers car or conference room rental. A car can be parked directly in front of the hotel. This hotel service is free. In addition to a large lagoon pool, the hotel also has a small paddling pool for children.





Beach

Of course, Russian tourists go to Thailand primarily to sunbathe and swim. The beach from the Jomtien Thani Hotel 3 * (Pattaya) can be reached in just 3 minutes. The sea is across the road from it. Car traffic in the Jomtien area is not too intense. Therefore, the transition from the hotel to the beach via the motorway does not cause any particular inconvenience to vacationers.

Tourists consider the coastal zone located next to this hotel quite comfortable. At least you can sunbathe here with great comfort. However, many guests are advised to go swimming all the same to the island of Koh Lan or somewhere else. The fact is that the water in the sea near the hotel (as, indeed, in most other places in Pattaya), according to tourists, is pretty dirty. In any case, sunbeds and umbrellas on this beach can be rented freely and at not too high cost. Also, vacationers by the sea near the hotel have the opportunity to visit the massage room. Various catering establishments work on the promenade. If you wish, you can have a good lunch in the cafe or sit for a glass of beer in the bar.

Dining at the hotel

A tour with accommodation at the Jomtien Thani Hotel 3 * (Thailand, Pattaya) is usually not too expensive - about 20,000 rubles. dollars per person. You will have to pay a little more for a package with food. Lunches and breakfasts at this hotel are served in the restaurant. The waiters bring the ordered dishes to the tables. That is, there is no buffet system in the hotel. But at the request of the guest, the ordered dish can be brought to the pool. Tourists who leave for an excursion early in the morning are usually packed with breakfast.

Judging by the reviews of the guests, the food at the Jomtien Thani is somewhat monotonous, but quite tasty. If you wish, you can order soup, chicken, yogurt, granola, milk, juice, etc. Many tourists drink coffee in this hotel right in their rooms. Each room has an electric kettle. There used to be coffee bags in the rooms. But recently, the hotel administration has unfortunately refused to provide this free service.





Infrastructure near the hotel

This hotel is called Jomtien Thani Hotel because it is located on Thani Street. Although the Jomtien area is considered one of the quietest, the infrastructure here is very well developed. Near the Jomtien Thani Hotel there are many other hotels, restaurants, cafes, and massage parlors. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel there is a small market (on the other side of the sea). Here you can buy not only fruits and vegetables, but also clothes. There is also a grocery store and currency exchange near the hotel. Those who prefer to shop in large supermarkets have the opportunity to visit the Lotus shopping complex.

Pattaya city center can be reached very quickly from the hotel. For this you need to sit on a tuk-tuk. These small taxis can carry up to 4 passengers at a time. The fare on them is not too high.

Jomtien Thani Hotel 3 *: hotel reviews

The opinion of this hotel among tourists was simply wonderful. Most of the reviews on the network about her are positive. Among the advantages of the hotel, tourists note primarily its convenient location, courtesy and helpfulness of staff, as well as the cleanliness of rooms and corridors. In addition, hotel guests also like the fact that umbrellas and sun loungers by the pool can be used absolutely free. The food in this hotel, according to most vacationers, is very good. The rooms have good furniture and the equipment works just fine.

Many tourists really like this hotel and for the availability of free Wi-Fi in the rooms. In other hotels in Pattaya, a similar service costs about 1200 p. and more per day. The infrastructure near the hotel also suits most of its guests. In particular, many tourists like the fact that next to the Jomtien Thani Hotel 3 * there is a large night market starting at 6 pm. Here, if desired, you can buy very tasty food for ridiculous money. Also, many guests praise this hotel for its beautiful views from its balconies.

The disadvantages of the Thani Hotel tourists include primarily interruptions with hot water in the shower. It would not hurt, according to many guests, to make in the rooms and redecorating. Also, some vacationers consider the minus of the hotel that they take as much as 500 baht for escorting guests to their rooms. Many tourists, among other things, attribute the disadvantages of this hotel to the fact that there is no carpet in the rooms on the floor.

Accommodations

Check-in at the Jomtien Thani hotel is at 14.00, check-out is at 12.00. The hotel reception is open 24 hours a day. Pets are not allowed in the rooms. Smoking is also something that is strictly prohibited both in the corridors and in the rooms of the Jomtien Thani (Pattaya) hotel. Hotel Thani, however, for people who have such a bad habit, also fits very well. The fact is that this hotel has several rooms with ventilation, specially equipped for smoking.

It is worth staying at the Thani Hotel not too expensive. For a week per person, for example, they take from 14 to 21 thousand rubles. Superior room reservations can be canceled freely with a small fine (about 3000 p.). The cost of living in rooms of other types is not refundable. Transfer to this hotel costs about 2 thousand rubles. At the entrance to the hotel you need to leave a deposit of $ 100. Money to tourists is subsequently returned in full. But only after checking the maid's number upon departure.

sights

All sorts of interesting places worthy of visiting tourists, in Pattaya there is simply a huge number. Jomtien abounds with them, as well as the surrounding areas. If desired, hotel residents can go:

  • to the temple of the Big Buddha;

  • to the park of ancient stones;

  • to a crocodile farm;

  • to the village of elephants.

In addition, you can visit the floating market or aquarium of Pattaya, visit the Chinese temple of Viharn Sin. Of course, this is not a complete list of attractions available to visitors of the Jomtien Thani Hotel.





Big Buddha Temple

This attraction is considered one of the most interesting in Pattaya. The Big Buddha Temple Complex is located about 3 km from the hotel and consists of several religious buildings. The center of the composition is a huge Buddha statue mounted on a hilltop. Its height is 15 meters, and the width at the foot is 10 m. This statue was made in 1977. Tours are free.

Park of ancient stones

Including the proximity to this interesting attraction earned good Jomtien Thani Hotel reviews from tourists. This unusual place, in fact, is an open-air museum. Throughout the park, surrounded by tropical vegetation, a huge number of stones of the most bizarre shapes are arranged. Walking in this beautiful place leaves a lot of the most pleasant memories. There are benches everywhere in the park where you can sit and relax while admiring unusual landscapes.

Crocodile farm

This is also a very popular tourist attraction in Pattaya. Crocodiles on this farm are just a huge number - about 60 thousand individuals. A particularly interesting sight for Pattaya guests is the feeding of these reptiles.

The farm also has a small gift shop. Here you can also purchase products from genuine crocodile leather. Of course, various shows with the participation of local pets are arranged on the farm.

Elephant village

This is perhaps the most popular tourist attraction in Pattaya. Many vacationers, including guests of the Thani Hotel, without exaggeration consider the village of elephants to be a truly unique place. This unusual farm was founded in 1973. Today, about 30 animals live in it. Three shows with elephants are organized daily in the village. Animals demonstrate their achievements in painting, acrobatics, playing musical instruments, etc.

Where to buy a tour

Of course, guests of the Jomtien Thani Hotel prefer to visit local attractions along with qualified guides. Excursions in the Jomtien area can be purchased in many places. However, experienced tourists in most cases advise beginners to buy them from Oleg Tour.





Jomtien District: reviews

Opinion about the hotel Jomtien Thani Hotel (Pattaya), therefore, tourists have developed a good one. Guests of Thailand and the town of Jomtien praise. Although tourists consider this resort to be one of the districts of Pattaya, in fact, officially, it is an independent settlement. The erroneous opinion of vacationers about him was formed solely because of its territorial.

But be that as it may, Jomtien is a rather inexpensive and convenient resort, and therefore it is very popular among tourists. All infrastructure here is located no further than 100-200 meters from the coast. Actually, behind the town itself, already undeveloped deserted fields begin. This feature, by the way, also explains the popularity of Jomtien as a resort place. Indeed, in Pattaya itself there are practically no green corners. The people here always rest so much that, as they say, even an apple has nowhere to fall.

Too big supermarkets, expensive restaurants and all kinds of noisy nightlife in Jomtien there. The area is really quite quiet and perfect for a relaxing, including family holiday. There are never even traffic jams here, and in the northern part of the region and generally parallel to the sea there is only a pedestrian path.

Instead of a conclusion

Only one conclusion can be drawn from the foregoing. It may not be the best in Thailand, but still a very good hotel - Jomtien Thani Hotel 3 * (Pattaya, Jomtien). Najomtien, the fashionable district of Pattaya and other similar places are suitable, of course, only for very wealthy tourists. Ordinary Russian citizens of average wealth, of course, it is better to choose housing in Jomtien. The area is quite prestigious, and you can relax here quite inexpensively.
