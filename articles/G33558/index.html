Recreation center, Primorye: "Sidimi" (reviews)

Protected corners, stunning beaches, the sun, a relaxing summer vacation - all this splendor does not have to go far, Russia is rich in natural beauties and wonderful places to relax. Today, in the center of our attention is a recreation center, Primorye, Sidimi. The program includes relaxing on the golden beach sands, exciting excursions to local attractions. But this is far from all. Egypt, Turkey and other popular resorts will not give a tenth of the impressions that can be obtained here. The recreation center (Primorye) "Sidimi" is also the best hiking in the world in the most beautiful protected places, gatherings by the fire. And now let's talk about this recreation center in more detail.



Where is located

In the Hasan district of Primorsky Krai there is a village Bezverkhovo. It is here that Cape Turek and Brinner are located. Almost immediately behind them is the warm and shallow Hek Bay, which has become the best place on the entire coast. Here is based recreation center (Primorye) "Sidimi".

For the location of tourists are numerous private houses and cottages, as well as tent towns. In the beach season, a stunning inflatable water park breaks here. For lovers of water sports there is a rental of catamarans and boats, kayaks, catamarans, surfing and windsurfing. As you can see, the rest here is full of surprises, so if you live in the Far Eastern region, you should definitely visit Sidimi (recreation center, Primorye). How to get to the base, we will tell you right now.



How to get there

The easiest way to set off on your own car. The recreation center (Primorye) "Sidimi" is located about an hour's journey from the city of Vladivostok. On the highway Vladivostok - Khabarovsk, you need to follow until you turn to Razdolnoye, and then you need to drive about 100 km more along the main road.

However, it is best to use river transport if you want to get to Sidimi (recreation center, Primorye). How to get there, we will tell you now. A scheduled boat leaves the pier every day, which takes forty minutes to the place in one hour. On the pier you will be met by a branded bus that will deliver things to the house. It is very convenient to get here on your own boat, yacht or boat.



Living conditions

Probably the biggest attention tourists pay to where they will live during the holidays. Someone is more tolerant of mediocre interiors, others are important every detail. Therefore, it is necessary to collect information in advance so as not to be disappointed.

To accommodate guests there is a three-story brick building. Judging by the reviews of tourists, this is not a luxury hotel, but nevertheless, it is quite comfortable here.

There are a total of 16 Standard rooms. The cost of living in them is 2450 or 2950 rubles, depending on the level of comfort. Among those who come to rest in "Sidimi" (recreation center of Primorye), most of the tourists prefer these numbers. They have: bathroom, hot water, air conditioning, necessary furniture.

For lovers of increased comfort there are seven buildings called "Herringbone". Here, tourists are offered large, quadruple rooms, with a convenient bathroom and shower, air conditioning, upholstered furniture and household appliances, as well as a covered terrace where it is so good to drink tea in the evening. The cost of these rooms is 4450 rubles. per day.

Finally, there are five Fir-tree + buildings on the territory, here the rooms are equipped with more modern furniture. Room rates - 5450 rubles. per day. A great option for relaxing a large and friendly company in Sidimi. The recreation center (Primorye) has all the features to ensure that you get the best vacation.

Beaches

This is the main entertainment for those who came to Sidimi. The recreation center (Primorye), the reviews that are called the best option for summer holidays throughout the Far Eastern Territory, is becoming the choice of most Russians. It is almost always sunny and clear, the air temperature warms up to +29, and water - up to +23 degrees. It is a comfortable temperature for adults and children. The beach here is very good, sandy, about 500 meters. The width of the beach strip is about 40 meters. Judging by the reviews of tourists, it is very clean, they clean the beach every day. There are no algae, there are three rescue towers and a medical center on the shore.

Tourists reviews
