To the Caribbean



We are talking about Puerto Rico, a state that is located on one of the Greater Antilles. It is administered by the United States of America, but has its own Constitution and its own government. Despite the jurisdiction of the United States, Puerto Rico is close in lifestyle to European, not American. Relations with America are common citizenship, defense, and banknotes.

The synthesis of antiquity and modernity

Here, exotic monuments combine medieval monuments with a modern touch of service. From here the recipe of the famous Pina Colada cocktail spread. The country of Puerto Rico is a place where you can touch history and mentally get into the time of sea robbers and slavers. A fairy tale reigns here, in which even an adult can be involved. No one will be indifferent if he takes a walk under the Caribbean tunes, exposing his face to the salty winds and looking at the rocky cliffs that are scattered along the coast of the island.



Tourists from all over the world come here because they know that the impressions will be exciting and unforgettable. The capital of the state is the city of San Juan. As soon as you stand on the ground of its old part, you will see monuments of ancient architecture and museums in front of your eyes. Having crossed the bridge, you will make a leap in time and find yourself in a modern metropolis.

In Puerto Rico, you will get acquainted with Native American culture and visit small and distinctive places: Aybonito - the city of flowers, Adhuntas - the city of the sleeping giant.

Kitchen

The national cuisine of the country is very multifaceted: the Spaniards, African slaves and Native American peoples contributed here. As a result of mixing, Puerto Rican cuisine was formed, which became widely known far beyond its borders.

Beaches and Attractions



The state has many beautiful beaches. There is a place and entertainment for the vacationer with any requirements. For lovers of scuba diving or sea fishing here is a real paradise! Of course, someone will come to enjoy the view of the sandy beaches, but many tourists want to get a lot of wonderful impressions of the cultural heritage of this country. Among the attractions of the country are the following:

  • Castle of Castillo de San Felipe del Morro.
  • El Unco National Forest.
  • Caves of Kamui.
  • Museo de Las Americas.
