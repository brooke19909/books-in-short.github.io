Why the sound does not work on the phone: possible causes and troubleshooting

It is very unpleasant and painful when the mobile phone is silent, especially when you need to listen to some important voicemail messages or videos. Many reasons can lead to such failures. Here, a positive fact for the user is that most of the reasons can be eliminated on their own if you use the tools described below and pre-establish why the sound does not work on the phone.

Preset for everyone

When the sound does not work on the phone, this creates a huge problem, because the user cannot use the device as it should.

Here are some common steps to fix a sound problem:

  1. Check the phone volume, press the increase / decrease buttons.
  2. Reboot the mobile phone, remove the headphones and clean the connector.
  3. Check the position of the vibration sound switch, for this go to settings> click on sound> volume. By setting this option, you can adjust the volume of music, ringtones and notifications.
  4. If the setting is successful, use the device, if not, continue the recovery further and look for a reason explaining why the sound does not work on the phone.
  5. Update the firmware of the device, go to the setting. Click on the ‚Äúabout phone‚Äù section, find ‚Äúsystem update / software update‚Äù and click on it, download the latest version for the phone.
  6. They check the music application and clear the cache, go to the settings, and then click on the clear data tab.
  7. If these actions also did not bring results, then install the SoundAbout application from Google Play for free.

Headphone Mode Failure

This is another reason why the sound does not work on the phone. In this case, the headset gets stuck in headphone mode. Even if the user has disabled them, the icon will not disappear. And you cannot hear music or sound without them. This problem is very common for Samsung / Motorola / LG cell phones.

Elimination Algorithm:

  1. Increase the phone volume to make sure it is not in silent mode.
  2. Plug and unplug headphones several times.
  3. Clean the headphone jack by removing dust / debris from the jack. Sometimes an existing obstruction on the connector can trick the phone into falsely sending a signal that the headset is connected.
  4. Turn the device off and on again.
  5. Restarting will help the device update and fix software errors.

Install a custom application

Did the sound on your phone suddenly stop working while using the app, making a call or watching a video? This is an unpleasant situation, however, because of this, you should not throw the device into the trash.

If the above funds do not give a result, and the phone rings poorly, you can use the additional application "Earphone mode off". Some mobile phones show that the headphones are connected, although in reality this is not so. This application is the solution to such a problem. If the headset is still showing, switch to speaker mode, sound will come from the output speaker.

Silent Android Speakers

Often the reason why the sound does not work on the phone are situations when the Android external / internal speaker stops working suddenly, in this case the device does not make a sound at all. And also during calls to others, they hear nothing. If there is no audibility in the headphones, then this is probably a hardware problem. You need to check the external and internal speaker of Android using the device diagnostic tool.

Diagnostic algorithm - how to find out why there is no sound on the Samsung S4 phone:

  1. Open the hardware kit and enter the key combination * # 7353 # to enter the Device Diagnostic Tool menu.
  2. For an external speaker, touch the Speaker icon. If it works normally, then very loud music coming from the device will be heard.
  3. Click ‚ÄúSpeaker‚Äù again to mute the sound.
  4. For the internal speaker, press the Melody key. If everything is in order with the speaker, music will come from it.
  5. If the device does not pass the test, then the problem is in the software.

In this case, you can use the following tips:

  1. Reboot the phone in the usual way.
  2. Turn off Bluetooth, sometimes the speaker does not work, because the device is connected to this device.
  3. If everything is done correctly, then the sound will appear, if not, then the reason explaining why there is no sound on the phone may be a hardware problem, then you will have to send it for repair.

Restore factory settings

Sometimes users complain that the Android phone turns off automatically during a call or switches to silent or active mode itself. This case may be caused by some third-party applications or a full cache. There are two ways to solve the problem due to which the sound on the phone is lost. Consider them.

Check for sound when the device is in safe mode:

  1. In order to enter safe mode, you must first turn off the phone.
  2. Press and hold the power button again until the question appears whether it is necessary to enter safe mode.
  3. Click on "OK", after which the safe mode will load.
  4. After entering it, the sound mode is checked. If there are no problems, this means that the problem with the speaker is caused by third-party applications, so you need to find the recently installed one and then uninstall it.
  5. Further it is recommended to clear the cache, for this enter the recovery mode on Android.
  6. Then use the volume down button to select the Wipe Cache Partition option from the menu and press the power button to confirm the action.
  7. Once the cleaning process is over, you need to select Reboot system now to reboot the device.

Typically, these two methods can effectively solve the problem due to which there is no sound when they call on the phone. If it failed, you can use the factory reset Android. Before doing this, back up important files on the phone, and then select "Settings"> "Backup and reset"> "Factory data reset"> "Reset phone" to erase all settings on the device.

Soaked apparatus

Sometimes sound problems arise due to the fact that water has entered the speaker of the phone. You can remove this moisture by turning off the device, removing the battery and SIM card and placing them in a container of rice for 24 hours. You can remove residual moisture by carefully blow-drying the hair dryer, making sure that it is at the lowest heating level and at a sufficient distance from the phone to prevent overheating. Continue the operation for no more than a minute.

Further, after there is confidence that the phone and its parts are completely dry, they reboot, perhaps this will fix some malfunctions that were the cause of the problem.

Then make sure that the latest software update is installed on the device. To do this, go to settings> about device> software update> check for updates. Download the latest update for your phone.

If these actions do not bring the proper result, contact the supplier or the place of purchase, as this may be a hardware problem, and it will be necessary to repair or replace the device.
