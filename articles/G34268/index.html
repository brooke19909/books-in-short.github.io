Hotel "Golden Ear" in Moscow: reviews, recommendations, address, contacts, rooms and services

Since 1954, the Golden Ear Hotel has been welcoming guests of Moscow. From the reviews of guests, we can conclude that this is one of the favorite metropolitan establishments among tourists. Convenient location, high standards of service, value for money - all these characteristics make the place attractive for travelers.

Address of the Golden Ear Hotel in Moscow

The hotel in question has an indisputable advantage - a convenient location. It is located in the Northeast district of the capital. The hotel occupies six buildings on Yaroslavskaya Street. The main building is located at Yaroslavskaya street, 15, building 3.

According to reviews of the Golden Ear Hotel in Moscow, its location has the following advantages:

  • 5 minutes walk from VDNH metro station;
  • 10 minutes walk from VDNH;
  • 5 minutes to the Cosmonauts Alley ;
  • a quarter of an hour to the center of Moscow;
  • mass of cultural attractions and infrastructure facilities within walking distance.

How to get to the Golden Ear Hotel in Moscow

Convenient location of the hotel determines good transport accessibility. By public transport, you can get here as follows:

  • Aeroexpress trains run from Vnukovo Airport to the Kiev Station. Next, you should transfer by metro. From the station "Kiev" to "VDNH" about 25 minutes.

  • Aeroexpress runs from Domodedovo Airport to the Paveletsky Train Station. Next you should take the subway. From the station "Paveletskaya" to "VDNH" about 20 minutes.

  • Aeroexpress runs from Sheremetyevo Airport, with links to Belorussky Train Station. Next, you should transfer by metro. From the station "Belorusskaya" to "VDNH" about 18 minutes.

The Golden Ear Hotel in Moscow is located just 450 meters from the South lobby of the VDNH metro station, so you can quickly reach the hotel on foot. You need to get into the city from the side of the last car and go through the underpass. You will reach the hotel in just 5-7 minutes.

What to see

An excellent starting point for exploring Moscow is the Golden Ear Hotel. Photo of the capital inspires travel. Here are the sights closest to the hotel that deserve your attention:

  • VDNH is one of the most beloved and most visited places of residents and guests of Moscow. The Exhibition of Achievements of the National Economy is a large recreational and museum complex, which is annually visited by more than 20 million people. Some cultural and entertainment programs are constantly held here.
  • Moskvarium is a Moscow aquarium where you can get acquainted with all the beauties of the ocean. On an area of ‚Äã‚Äã12 thousand square meters. m inhabited by more than 8,000 marine inhabitants from all over the world. The largest exposition of fish living in Russia is also represented here. There are special pools in which guests can touch some marine inhabitants (crabs, starfish and others).
  • The main botanical garden of the Russian Academy of Sciences is the largest botanical garden in Europe. This is a huge park that amazes with the beauty of landscape design and the variety of plants. The garden is open year-round and is only a couple of minutes walk from the hotel in question.
  • Ostankino television tower is a unique building, one of the key tourist sites in Moscow. From the observation deck, amazingly beautiful views of the Russian capital open. From here you can simultaneously see all the sights of Moscow. Every day, more than 1000 people climb to the observation deck.
  • The Cosmonautics Museum is the largest scientific and technical museum in the world. Here is the whole history of the development of astronautics, from the invention of the first spacesuits to the most modern inventions and achievements. In the halls of the museum are more than 90,000 exhibits.
  • Gorky Film Studio is a legendary place, the oldest Russian film studio, where favorite films and cartoons were created. Over the more than a century-long existence of the film studio, more than 1000 films have seen the light, many of which have become legendary (The Quiet Don, The Dawns Here Are Quiet, Officers, and many others).
  • The monument "Worker and Collective Farm Girl" is a museum and exhibition center, which was recreated and inaugurated in 2010. The building itself was created in 1937. At the same time, more than 1000 people can be in the building of the exhibition complex.
  • Moscow Cathedral Mosque is a striking beauty and scale religious building, the history of which began back in 1904. The building was erected by the architect Nikolai Zhukov at the expense of the Tatar merchant Salikh Erzin. In the Soviet period, this mosque was the only one in the city in which services were held.
  • The Lenin Library is a famous scientific and educational center. The total length of the bookshelves exceeds 275 km. It contains literature and documents (over 45 million units) in all languages ‚Äã‚Äãof the world. Here you can also find rare newspapers, sheet music, maps and even sound recordings.
  • Moscow State University on Sparrow Hills is the oldest Russian university and research center. Of interest is not only the scientific base, but also the building. Or rather, the main building (and there are a total of 600), located on the Sparrow Hills.
  • Old Arbat is the main tourist street in the capital. This is one of the few places where the unique atmosphere of old Moscow has been preserved. Here you can admire the architecture of past eras, as well as enjoy the creativity of street artists and musicians.
  • Moscow Zoo - was founded more than 150 years ago. It all started with fish ponds. Later, the first menagerie appeared, which fell in love with both Muscovites and guests of the capital. The official moment of the opening of the zoo is considered the day when Alexander II brought the elephant here. After that, many followed his example and began to bring animals from around the world here.

Hotel Policies

The hotel "Golden Ear" in Moscow has certain rules of residence. Here are the highlights:

  • The hotel is open 24 hours a day.

  • The working hours of public catering establishments located on the territory are from 07:00 to 23:00.

  • Check-in time at the Golden Ear Hotel in Moscow is 2:00 p.m. Eviction takes place before noon.

  • The client has the right to refuse a guaranteed reservation without any penalties and sanctions the day before the expected arrival before 14:00.

  • In case of untimely cancellation of reservation, a penalty will be charged from the client in the amount of the daily cost of living in the room of the selected category.

  • If you are late for more than a day from the date of the expected arrival, the reservation is canceled and a penalty will be charged from the client in the amount of the daily cost of living in the room of the selected category.

  • In case of non-guaranteed reservation, the reservation is canceled after 18:00 on the day of the expected arrival.

  • Payment for services provided is accepted only in national currency. It is possible to use VISA, MasterCard, Maestro, UnionCard, ChinaUnionpay cards.

  • For late arrivals after 14:00, payment for the room is made in full.

  • Children under 6 years old stay at the hotel free of charge (without meals and without extra bed).

  • Children under 3 years old stay at the hotel for free (with extra bed, without meals).

  • If you evict a predetermined period of residence, payment is made for the actual number of days that the guest has stayed at the hotel. An amount in excess of the indicated value is returned to the guest at the time of eviction.

  • Upon check-in, a deposit is charged from the guest, the rest of which is returned upon eviction.

  • A prerequisite for checking into a hotel is the provision of a valid identity card.

  • Breakfast is provided to the guest from the day after arrival. If the guest wants to have breakfast on the day of check-in, this service will be provided to him at an additional cost.

  • If you want to guarantee the availability of a free room at the Golden Ear Hotel in Moscow, you can book it through the form on the official website, by phone or through special Internet resources.

Unfortunately, there are some drawbacks and shortcomings in the Golden Ear Hotel in Moscow. Traveler reviews will be a warning to you about possible unpleasant surprises. Here are some negative comments worth paying attention to:
