Hotel "Garden Ring" on Prospect Mira: photos and reviews

The modern Garden Ring hotel is not only an object of the Moscow hotel service, which offers tourists excellent rooms at affordable rates (for the center of the capital). In addition, it is still an amazing example of architectural creativity. Those who are in the capital for the first time will definitely like it, but even sophisticated tourists who often visit different countries do not cease to admire it. We will take a closer look at the hotel so that you can make your own opinion about it.



Contact Information

Getting to her is easy, all taxi drivers are familiar with the Garden Ring Hotel. Its address is Prospect Mira, 14. The nearest metro stations are Sukharevskaya, Prospekt Mira and Tsvetnoy Boulevard. You can call the hotel at any time at 8 (800) 777-01-24. In walking distance is the Olympic sports complex, the Sklifosovsky Institute and the Botanical Garden of Moscow State University.

How to get there

The luxurious cladding and the special design of the building are not accidental. The Garden Ring Hotel is located on one of the most important avenues of the city. The building is located in the back of the yard, so round-the-clock traffic on a busy road will not bother you. At the same time, it is very convenient to get from the hotel to any point in Moscow, especially to the historical center. To Red Square - in about 25 minutes on foot, by public transport - even faster. The route through the trolleybus stop on the Garden Ring can be especially interesting, here you take the ring trolleybus and drive along the famous ramparts of Moscow.

It is very convenient that the Garden Ring Hotel is located almost in the city center, which means that you can get to any airport very quickly. If you do not get stuck in traffic, the road will not take more than 30 minutes. If you use the Aeroexpress service, the road will become more comfortable, but a little longer.

Interior and exterior

This is a luxurious six-story building, made in the classical style. The entrance is located in a corner, rounded part of the building. In the spacious lobby with columns you can comfortably relax on the soft sofas, as well as a cup of coffee. There is no cheap shine and pathos of interior decoration, on the contrary, the Garden Ring Hotel is the embodiment of style and grace. Soft, greenish shades and natural materials prevail here: wood and marble. The large and comfortable rooms are decorated mainly in olive shades and furnished with stylish furniture with soft shapes.

Number of rooms

The hotel has 76 one-room, standard rooms, as well as eight spacious suites. In addition, there is one room for people with disabilities. For business people on the sixth floor there is a convenient conference room, which seats 60 people, for conferences and seminars.

First of all, rooms vary in size, huge apartments will cost you the most. Otherwise, they all amaze with a magnificent interior, comfort, coziness and wonderful views from the windows. According to reviews, the atmosphere of each, even the smallest, room corresponds to the highest level of the hotel. Elite modern furniture and excellent appliances will make your stay more comfortable. All apartments have free internet, a tea set and a TV. You can choose a standard or two-room number. In each of them, if desired, you can install a cot.



Room rates

Compared to other business class hotels, the prices here are quite affordable. The average room rate is 6400 per day. A fabulous breakfast buffet is already included in the price, so the morning will be the most memorable moment while you stay here. Double and single rooms practically do not differ from each other in cost. Luxurious suites are a little more expensive. Here the price ranges from 7000 to 7400 rubles.
