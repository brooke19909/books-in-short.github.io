Shopping centers of Orenburg. Description

In our article we will look at the popular shopping centers of Orenburg. These complexes will be named as well as described.

Shopping center "New World". When would you be open? What are the shops in the complex?

This is a four-story shopping center. It was built back in 2008. Its parking is designed for 450 cars. In this complex on the ground floor there is a grocery hypermarket "Family Magnet". Also located there is a store of home goods "Galamart". On the ground floor there is a gallery of clothes, perfumes and shoes. On the second floor are departments with sporting goods. There is also a gallery of shoes, clothes and an electronics supermarket.

The third floor is occupied by a children's entertainment complex and SFK "Territory of fitness", as well as a food court.
