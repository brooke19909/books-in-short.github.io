Sanatorium "Miner" in Prokopyevsk - Kuzbass health resort

One of the largest coal deposits in the world - the Kuznetsk coal basin, or Kuzbass, is located in the southern part of Western Siberia. Most of its mines are located in the Kemerovo region. In 2015, Kuzbass gave Russia 215.8 million tons of coal.

Miners mine coal in difficult conditions: the average depth of the mines ranges from 300 to 900 meters, and the climate in this area is characterized by sharp fluctuations in temperature and intense solar radiation. Working in such conditions throughout the year is not easy, therefore, specialized sanatoriums have been created for the recovery of coal workers. Many of these health facilities are located in the Kemerovo region. Today we will talk about one of them - the Shakhtar sanatorium in the city of Prokopyevsk.

Kuzbass health resort

This resort is located 9 km from the city of Prokopyevsk, Kemerovo region. 20 km separate the sanatorium from another city - Novokuznetsk. The health resort building is surrounded by a picturesque pine-deciduous forest and built on the banks of the Zenkovsky pond. The healing air, silence, remoteness from the bustle of the city and developed infrastructure create excellent conditions for the health of vacationers.

The Kuzbass health resort is especially popular among the miners of Prokopyevsk: in order to get high-quality medical services and have an interesting time, you do not need to travel across the country. Here, of course, there is no coveted sea and white sand beach, but there will be no problems with acclimatization, and the prices for services are quite reasonable.

Sanatorium "Miner" (Prokopyevsk): about treatment

This medical institution is focused on diseases that are most often found in miners. These include, first of all, diseases of the respiratory, musculoskeletal system and connective tissue, circulatory system, digestive and nervous system organs. Here, rehabilitation is carried out for workers who suffered from accidents in mines, as well as the treatment of occupational diseases.

Before starting treatment, qualified specialists conduct a health examination. For this, ultrasound examinations, laboratory tests are prescribed, an electrocardiogram is taken.

To restore the health of vacationers, modern treatment methods are used:

  • speleotherapy;
  • acupuncture;
  • physiotherapy;
  • massotherapy;
  • mud therapy;
  • psychotherapy.

Air massager for legs and hands, antler-steam barrel, hydrotherapy treatments, dentist services are available for vacationers.

Detailed information on the cost of permits is available on the official website of the Shakhtar sanatorium (Prokopyevsk).





Sanatorium infrastructure

The structure of the Shakhtar sanatorium includes a medical and health building, dormitory buildings that can accommodate 400 people, a dining room and a parking lot. Also on the territory of the recreation complex there is a conference hall, cafe, bar, water center.

Vacationers staying at the sanatorium are accommodated in comfortable 2-bed rooms of improved layout. Each of them has 2 single beds, a coffee table, bedside tables, wardrobe, refrigerator, TV, shower, separate bathroom.

The nutrition of miners and their families is dietary and is carried out 4 times a day.





What to do in your free time

Constantly visiting treatment rooms and taking useful procedures will bore even the most disciplined vacationer. In his spare time from treatment, it will be useful to engage in sports that distract from health problems, raise the level of adrenaline in the blood and create a good mood.

All the conditions have been created for playing sports at the Shakhtar sanatorium (Prokopyevsk): there are football and volleyball courts, several tennis courts, a gym, a bowling room, tables for practicing table tennis and billiards. Fans of aesthetics can go dancing in a specially designated area. Of particular interest to children and adults is the opportunity to do paintball, horseback riding.

In the winter months, a ski slope, an ice rink with musical accompaniment are available. A rental point for skates, skiing, snowboarding.

You can have fun by visiting the resort's water center. There is a waterfall, a water slide, a children's pool. The design of an aquacenter deserves separate words: even if a blizzard howls outside, vacationers have the impression that they are in a tropical resort. This is facilitated by walls decorated with bright murals, the presence of evergreens, deck chairs, a tropical bar with exotic cocktails.





Sanatorium Shakhtar (Prokopyevsk): reviews

Vacationers in the sanatorium believe that they did not waste their time and money on a ticket. The quality of treatment, in their words, is at a high level. Well-established leisure activities do not let you get bored in your free time. Nature and clean air near the sanatorium become a real outlet for urban residents.

The Shakhtar Sanatorium in the city of Prokopyevsk, Kemerovo Region, is always glad to see guests and is waiting for them throughout the year!
