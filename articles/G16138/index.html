Delicate and bright flower made of corrugated paper. We will make a gerbera and a rose with our own hands

Flowers made from corrugated paper amaze the imagination with their naturalness and beauty. A floral composition of such roses, tulips or gerberas can revive the interior of any home, bring notes of romance, tenderness and comfort to it.

In this article, we will share with you two magnificent workshops called " corrugated paper flower ". With your own hands, you, even as novice masters, can create really very elegant decorative things. Stock up on the necessary materials and tools, as well as a great mood. So, let's get to work.

Craft for beginners - a flower made of corrugated paper. We will make a bouquet of gerberas with our own hands

In order to make gerberas, you will need the following “ingredients”:

  • corrugated paper of several colors (green, red, purple, pink, etc.);
  • wire;
  • scissors;
  • glue.


First, cut the paper into strips. You can fold it several times to facilitate your work. Then cut from one edge of the strip "fringe". We twist the paper, forming the middle of our gerbera. It is advisable to select paper that is more tender and lighter in color for the core of the flower, and brighter and more saturated for the petals. So you achieve greater realism. Now we take paper of a different color and cut off a strip from it. We also make a frequent “fence” from one of its edges. We apply a strip to the core of the flower so that its lower edge is approximately in the center of the bud. We wind the part, forming beautiful petals. That's all, a flower made of corrugated paper with your own hands is almost made! It remains only to perform the wire winding of the bud and form the stem. Now you need to decorate it with green corrugated paper and, if desired, cut leaves out of it. Using the same principle, make a few more gerberas and create a magnificent bright bouquet.

The perfect material for floral arrangements is corrugated paper. Flowers: master class



Now let's try to create another beautiful flower - a pink rose. For its manufacture, it will be necessary to prepare the following materials:

  • wooden skewers (for barbecue);
  • threads
  • corrugated pink paper for flowers;
  • green floral ribbon;
  • candy in foil.

Our rose will be not only beautiful, but also delicious - we will make its core out of candy. You can present such a flower to a loved one or friend for any celebration. To create crafts, take and fold the paper in several layers. Using scissors, cut out the shape of a petal from it. The workpiece must be pointed below and more rounded on top. Lay out the paper - you should get a "fence" of petals. Now we cut out a number of individual petals from the corrugated paper. After that, with the help of a pencil or knitting needle, twist the edges of the petals, giving them a wave. Now we proceed to the assembly of parts into a single design: we take candy, we wrap it in a strip of corrugated paper. We fix the core with glue on a skewer for skewers. Around the candy we wrap a continuous "fence" of petals, we fix the ends with a thread. Well, now let's add splendor to our pink bud using separate petals. Do not forget to fix them with a thread. After all the petals are attached to the bud, we make out the cup and stem with a green ribbon. Your flower made of corrugated paper with your own hands! Using the same principle, create a few more roses and collect a bouquet from them. Moreover, each bud can be made in a different color, so your product will become very bright and elegant.
