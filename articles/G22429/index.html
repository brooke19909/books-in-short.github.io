History

Initially, this locality functioned mainly as a fortress. So in the past, Vyborg was the most important defense point of nearby territories. Tours from St. Petersburg are carried out in order to provide tourists with the opportunity to get acquainted with powerful and majestic fortifications.

First of all, city guests are invited to the Vyborg castle, built by the Swedes in 1293. It is believed that T. Knutsson, Marshal of Sweden, founded it. Near the citadel, on pl. The Town Hall, which is the main one in the village, you can see its monument. The main tower in the center of the building was named in honor of St. Olav, King of Norway. The height of the structure is 48.6 meters. Above you can see the observation deck, designed to view the surrounding area.





More Attractions

Excursions to Vyborg leave an amazing experience. At the junction of the 15th and 16th centuries a fortress was built here. Subsequently, it was called the Horn. The impressive architectural ensembles of past centuries have survived to this day. Now you can see the towers of the Town Hall and the Round, as well as a bastion called Panzerkaks. Guides talk about the history of their creation, the circumstances that caused the construction of a building. When Empress Anna Ioannovna ruled here, the Annens fortifications were erected.

Thanks to them, Vyborg became an even safer place. A self-guided tour here is a way to slowly walk through the historical center and admire the skilful architectural ensembles built over many centuries. Some of them belong to the Swedish era (up to the 18th century). City development

At the beginning of the 18th century this defense point passed into the possession of the Russian Empire. It was at this moment that the authorities began the construction of several cathedrals, a gostiny dvor, a town hall and other important sights, which tourists today enjoy, taking tours of Vyborg on their own or as part of a group led by a guide.

In 1893, the New Cathedral was erected, which is of central importance in the religious life of the city. It belongs to the neo-Gothic style. This is a rather large and beautiful building. During the Finnish period, many wonderful architectural ensembles were also created here. One of them is the library built in 1935, named after Alvar Aalto. People from all over the world are striving to personally experience this masterpiece of architecture.
