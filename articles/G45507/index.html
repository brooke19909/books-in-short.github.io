Hotel Saint George Palace 4 * (Saint Vlas, Bulgaria): photos and reviews of tourists

St. Vlas is a promising young resort, according to many tourists, perfectly suitable mainly for family vacations. This is located, which has already managed to gain popularity among Russian vacationers, a village just 2 kilometers from the famous Sunny Beach. Despite the fact that St. Vlas is a young resort, the infrastructure here is already very well developed. There are many cafes, shops and entertainment venues in the village, and local hotels are real complexes, in addition to housing, offering tourists just a huge amount of additional amenities. The Saint George Palace 4 * hotel is no exception in this regard.

Location Features

Hotel Saint George Palace is located in one of the quietest areas of St. Vlas. The distance from it to the coastline is only about 250-300 meters. True, tourists have to go down a rather steep path. Actually the village of Sveti Vlas itself, many tourists compare with the quiet sea resorts of Western Europe. The nature in this village is considered by vacationers to be the most favorable on the entire coast of Bulgaria. This village is located on the slope of a large mountain, completely covered with forest. The combination of sea air and the huge amount of oxygen secreted by trees creates a truly unique microclimate in this place of the coast.





Near the hotel Saint George Palace 4 * there are several ski lifts. You can get to them, for example, by taxi or bus. Also nearby are the famous sports resorts of Bansko and Dobrinishte. Therefore, many vacationers find this hotel very suitable not only for the summer beach, but also for winter outdoor activities. Many hotel guests do not even hire a taxi to get to the ski lifts. In the cold season, they simply go in all equipment to the ski run, located about 200 meters from the hotel. Then the athletes descend with a breeze - to the ski lifts.

general description

Actually the Saint George Palace 4 * hotel itself (Sunny Beach) is a four-story building with very unusual architecture. The facades painted in burgundy and white, combined with arched large loggias, give the hotel a completely unique look. This hotel was built recently - in 2010.

The hotel territory, judging by the reviews of tourists, is very well equipped and well landscaped. The courtyard in front of the hotel building and all pedestrian walkways are lined with neat pink tiles that perfectly harmonize with the colors of the building facades. The real decoration of the hotel is a huge pool of an unusual shape. Next to it are light tables and chairs of a summer cafe.

Room facilities

Of course, rooms of any categories in the hotel Saint George Palace 4 * are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay of guests. The hotel rooms include, among other things,:

Guest reviews of hotel rooms

Most tourists find the rooms for rent at the Saint George Palace 4 * Hotel (Bulgaria) quite comfortable and cozy. Furniture and appliances are quite new. And so problems, for example, with the same air conditioners or bed nets, guests never have. Some former residents of the hotel complain only about local boilers. Sometimes they can leak. Also not too thoughtful, many guests consider the layout of the bathrooms in this hotel.

The maids in the hotel are cleaned, according to many tourists, not as often as we would like. But still the rooms in the hotel do not look particularly dirty.

The Internet in the rooms of this hotel, according to many tourists, is rather slow. However, in order to download, for example, a film, its speed is enough. True, it will go to this procedure almost the whole day. Televisions in some rooms have a flash drive connector. Therefore, if desired, guests have the opportunity to watch films brought with them.





Hotel infrastructure

In addition to the pool, on the territory of the Saint George Palace 4 * hotel itself, reviews of which on the Network are mostly only good, tourists can use the following services:

  • currency exchange;

If desired, hotel guests can rent a car directly on its territory. Moreover, in the courtyard of the hotel there is also free parking. Beach Hotels

To the sea, guests of the Saint George Palace 4 * hotel need to go no more than 5-7 minutes. The beach of the hotel, unlike its infrastructure and rooms, has earned, unfortunately, not very good reviews from tourists. It has a small area, and therefore there are always a lot of people. In addition, sunbeds with umbrellas on this beach are very expensive. Concrete breakwaters are installed in the sea here. Their presence, of course, reduces excitement. However, because of the breakwaters in this place, algae are often nailed to the shore.

Tourists praise the beach of the Saint George Palace 4 * hotel mainly for cleanliness and beautiful views from its territory.

Of course, guests of the Saint George Palace Hotel, like any other guests of St. Vlas, have the opportunity to explore various interesting places in this resort.
