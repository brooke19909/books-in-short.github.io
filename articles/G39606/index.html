Unforgettable holidays in Maykop

Every year, millions of tourists select the perfect option for their vacation. The city of Maykop is considered one of the most popular resorts, since every vacationer can find here entertainment for every taste. The capital of the Republic of Adygea pleases its visitors with a warm and cozy atmosphere, magnificent landscapes and a rich entertainment program. Holidays in Maykop will be remembered by the tourist as an incredible and eventful adventure.

Resort Features

If you translate the name of the resort, it turns out that tourists visit the valley of wild apple trees. Locals are proud of Maykop and warmly welcome vacationers. Within the city are numerous historical and archaeological sites, famous culture and recreation parks of Maykop. And the pride of the capital of the republic is the goddess of the Belaya rivers.

Features of rest in Maykop in the warm season are magnificent green landscapes, as well as flowering orchards. The climate of the resort is moderately humid, it is suitable for almost all people. In the city you can not only have fun, but also improve your health in special sanatorium-resort areas.

Region weather conditions

Due to the unique climatic conditions and the location of the city, the summer season begins in May, and warm weather pleases vacationers until the month of October. The average temperature is 22 degrees Celsius. In winter, air temperature is almost always in the range of -4 ... -5 degrees below zero.

Sights of Maykop

Each tourist will be able to find a suitable holiday in Maykop. Fans of history and cultural and entertainment programs are recommended to visit the national museum of Adygea, go to the Oshad barrow, look at the Friendship Square and visit the cathedral mosque of the city.

In addition, vacationers will enjoy the following attractions of the resort:

  • memorials to the Maykop warriors;
  • built 1889-1903;
  • Adyghe Republican Balneological Hospital;
  • Maykop city leisure parks;
  • pass called "Devil's Gate";
  • art galleries of the city;
  • Dolmens of the Deguak Valley.

In addition, hiking tours can be booked in the city, which will introduce tourists to the unique natural monuments, unforgettable landscapes and the special atmosphere of the region. Vacationers will be able to see a huge number of untouched caves, glades, which are undergoing archaeological excavations, mountain peaks, waterfalls and much more.

Nature lovers will enjoy visiting the natural biosphere reserve, which will allow you to plunge into the enchanting atmosphere and improve your health. Water from local sources is healing, it is used to treat and prevent diseases of the gastrointestinal tract.

Leisure

Having looked at the sights of the city of Maykop, you can safely go to the most interesting and extreme places of the resort. The capital of the republic pleases tourists with horseback riding and cycling, and also makes it possible to explore the surroundings by descending along a mountain river.

Holidays in Maykop can be diversified with active sports, namely: parachuting, mountaineering and hang gliding. In addition, fans of active entertainment can book a ride on ATVs, jeeps and mountain bicycles. The resort has a water park and receives visitors a dolphinarium.

The entertainment program will be equally interesting for both adults and children.

Maykop thermal springs

Thermal springs that have a healing effect and attract thousands of tourists every year will help to create an unforgettable vacation in Maykop. In order to get to the treasured place, you just need to choose the desired route or use the bus. Healing springs are located at a distance of 12 km from the city. Having planned a visit, tourists can also visit interesting places located nearby.

There are sanatoriums and hydropathic centers near thermal springs, which offer visitors high-quality procedures and maximum recovery. The choice of services is so great that every vacationer will pick up something unique and relaxing for themselves. In addition, tourists have the opportunity to stay at one of the recreation centers, which are located near the place of rehabilitation.

Accommodation in Maykop

The infrastructure of the city is well developed. The resort has an airport, a railway station and a bus station.

Having reached the capital of the Republic of Adygea, a tourist, as a rule, asks the main question: where to rent comfortable and affordable housing?

Vacationers can stay in Maykop in hotels, mini-hotels , the private sector or settle in the sanatoriums of the city. The leading positions in the ranking of housing of a special type are:

  • tourist complex "Mountain mood";
  • Maykop City Hostel;
  • "Eden".

Also popular are the Biba Hotel, the Shining and Versailles mini-hotels, the Grand Hotel & Spa Maykop.

In order not to worry about where to spend the night upon arrival, it is recommended to book accommodation in advance.

On various sites post thousands of reviews about holidays in Maykop. Vacationers recommend a resort in the capital of the Republic of Adygea for several reasons. Firstly, a wide range of services and entertainment for every taste will not let the tourist get bored. Secondly, everyone should certainly see the sights of the city and swim in the thermal springs.

Maykop is a truly amazing place that will not leave anyone indifferent.
