Premium Banking: Features, Rates, Benefits and Reviews

In addition to the standard service, banks actively offer VIP services. This segment is one of the most profitable, but the principles of work differ from the standard ones. The best service, the most qualified staff, comfortable atmosphere. What does premium banking include and how much does it cost the customer?

What is VIP?

Premium service means an individual approach to the client, providing increased comfort in communication, access to unique products. In this case, the bank assumes the role of financial assistant. Participants in such programs receive all standard services, but on more favorable terms: premium bank cards, personal manager and extraordinary service.





Such a service involves high costs for the organization of work. Accordingly, the selection of potential customers is more stringent.

How to get premium service at the bank?

The conditions for participation in the program for each institution are different. Mainly:

  • the presence of a deposit in the amount of 1 million rubles .;
  • the acquisition of luxury banking products;
  • work in the field of investment: with mutual funds, precious metals, OFBU, etc.

Premium banking is intended primarily for wealthy people who value their time: athletes, politicians, top managers, executives, representatives of show business, etc.

Package of services

Premium banking goes well beyond standard. Its goal is to provide the client with a personal financial assistant who would serve the client quickly and with high quality. At the forefront is the speed of resolving the issue, which should be combined with comfort and a personal approach. The range of services includes:

  • Financial planning: identifying investment needs and building capital.
  • Concierge services - round-the-clock access of customers to the services of booking tickets, hotels, cars, reserving a table in a restaurant, etc.
  • Discount programs for cashless payments.
  • Service with increased comfort. Hotels and airports have special rooms where you can relax, eat and take a shower.




  • Quick (re) card issue. If abroad the client loses his payment instrument, it will be enough for him to simply inform his manager about this, and the order for the registration of a new plastic will go to work.
  • Accompanying services: art banking for the evaluation of art objects, advice on taxation, currency laws, legal support anywhere in the world.

Each customer selects the best banks for premium services for themselves. But if we compare the conditions of two state institutions, then Sberbank has a lower entry threshold, but fewer services. A financial institution focuses on operations with foreign currency.





In Sberbank, there are several managers per consultant. Therefore, queues in service rooms cannot be avoided.

Premium banking at VTB24 appeared earlier than at Sberbank, and has already improved significantly. Managers undergo special training and periodic certification on knowledge of products and job compliance. The financial institution relies on the concierge service popular in Europe. But in fact, the need for it goes by the wayside. Why call a consultant to request a taxi if you can download the mobile application and call the car in one click?
