Location

As mentioned above, the hotel is located in a place called Protaras. This is a small village, very much loved by tourists. In ancient times, the Greek polis of Lefkola was in its place. In our time, Protaras boasts beautiful beaches and excellent infrastructure. In 10 minutes you can drive to the popular Ayia Napa - a rather large and noisy resort. The most convenient way to get to Protaras is from Larnaca Airport - the journey will take only 45 minutes.

The hotel itself is located on a beach called Marlita. His calling card is azure water and golden sand. There are several other excellent beaches within a kilometer from the hotel - Kalamies, Trinity and Sunrise. Attractions such as Ayia Napa Monastery, Cavo Greco National Park, Water World Water Park can be reached in 7-10 minutes. As you can see, in a very convenient location is the hotel Anastasia 4 *. Protaras is a small village, but even here it is important how close the hotel is to the epicenter of entertainment.
