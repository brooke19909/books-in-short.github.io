Where to get inspiration?



If you are just starting to create in this technique, you should not immediately take up large-scale work. Do not forget that frames for one photo and decorative cards are also scrapbooking. Where to start is immediately clear. Try making a small gift envelope or card. If you prefer the design of photos, make a postcard with a photo or draw a picture in a frame. Follow the combination of colors and textures, remember moderation. Do not try to use as many jewelry as possible in one composition. The main thing in scrapbooking is not the number of elements, but how harmoniously they look together. Do not be afraid to use custom jewelry. Individual items can even be crocheted or braided with beads. If such work seems too laborious for you, use ready-made braid and lace, this does not prohibit scrapbooking for beginners. A photo album decorated with ribbons or a fringe of factory production sometimes looks more interesting than a product with handmade jewelry.
