Lermontovo village: reviews recommend

Not far from the village of Tenginka in the Tuapse district is the village of Lermontovo. It is interesting that the poet has never been to these places, although his regiment stood here in fortification. The village of Lermontovo (colloquially - Lermontovka) is located in a very picturesque place: the valley of the Shapsuho River.

Neither sea storms, nor winds on land, nor severe frosts (which is rare in these parts) never block the mouth of the Shapsuho. The river carries its waters into the sea all year round. What is the village of Lermontovo? Reviews about him are very different. For Krasnodar and Rostovites, it is attractive only because you can come here for the weekend: both cities are relatively nearby, and the road to the sea takes only a few hours. Residents of these and other nearby cities do not like Lermontovo. Their reviews indicate that it is always crowded and, as a result, noisy.



However, this does not prevent Rostov residents from coming here often for 2-3 days: the village has excellent infrastructure, many shops, cafes and canteens. Special entertainment for vacationers who come only to plunge into the warm sea water is not required. Therefore, many tourists do not pay attention to the unique location of Lermontovo. Reviews of northerners, Muscovites, residents of other remote regions are more accurate and fair.

Unaffected Geography

If you leave the sea beaches for a while and climb the riverbed, you can see pictures of nature that are amazing. The fact is that civilization here spread only to the village of Tenginka. Everything above is a territory untouched by man.

If Lermontovo (the reviews of all tourists confirm this) is a territory filled with music, the sound of waves, fun beating over the edge, then the Shapsuho Valley is a quiet and peaceful kingdom of nature, wild herbs and flowers.

Rest in Lermontovo-2013

Responses of vacationers who visited the village this year indicate the attention that the district administration pays to its territories. The beaches are much cleaner. Despite the fact that there are fewer tourists, the cafe is not so crowded, and the customers do not accumulate in stores at all. All this happens because in the village there are many new shops and places of recreation, which allowed to unload the crowded town. In general, the village of Lermontovo (Tuapse district) - tourist reviews in this regard are unanimous - has become more attractive. Perhaps the whole thing is in the upcoming Olympics, and maybe the tourist service has become better. Therefore, those who are going to spend the summer in Russia can safely go to Lermontovo without fear. Testimonials from those who have been there indicate that time will not be wasted. From the village you can easily get to any place on the Black Sea coast, go on any excursion. Accommodation here is designed for a wide variety of vacationers. If you wish, you can settle in an economy-class hotel, choose a luxury apartment or find private housing for only 300-400 rubles per day. Naturally, the level of amenities will be different.
