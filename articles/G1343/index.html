Negative reviews

Hotel "Green House" (Tyumen), unfortunately, is not without drawbacks. When planning to stay in this institution, you should know the following:

  • despite the fact that breakfast is held from 7:00 to 10:00, by 9:00 there is practically no food left at the distribution, and the waiters do not replenish its supplies;
  • Wireless Internet is very slow (if you need it for work, then this power is not enough);
  • inflated prices in catering establishments;
  • if you order food in your room, you will have to pay an additional 20% of the amount of the check;
  • due to poor sound insulation you will hear everything that happens in the next room (especially when it comes to the bathroom);
  • ventilation in the bathroom does not work well, because of which there is always a smell of sewage;
  • if you didn‚Äôt have time for breakfast (even for a good reason), then you will remain hungry;
  • there are problems with the provision of documents on the cost of services, which is unacceptable for those who have arrived on a business trip;
  • sometimes there are interruptions in the supply of hot water;
  • the attic rooms have very low sloping ceilings and the windows do not open;
  • the restaurant's menu is quite modest;
  • upon arrival, the reception staff do not inform the guests about all the services provided by the hotel (you have to find out everything yourself);
  • a busy highway runs nearby, and therefore, with the windows open, the noise of passing cars is heard;
  • bedspreads on beds do not wash, and therefore they smell stylishly of dust;
  • there are no salt shakers on tables in the restaurant;
  • constant interruptions in the terminal, due to which there are problems with cashless payments;
  • draft beer in a bar of poor quality (most likely diluted);
  • there is no lamp on the desktop;
  • the refrigerator buzzes a lot during operation;
  • it is impossible to adjust the temperature of the water in the shower (it flows cold or hot);
  • the rooms are not well lit.

Green House captivates with its beautiful architecture and original room design. Here you can plunge into a fabulous atmosphere, feeling like an inhabitant of a doll house. Nevertheless, there are a number of significant drawbacks, considering which, a long stay at the hotel is not recommended.
