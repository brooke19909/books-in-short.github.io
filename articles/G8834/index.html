"Myshkino Compound" - rest in the best Russian traditions

On the banks of the Volga there are many places for a comfortable stay. One of such corners is the tourist complex "Myshkino Podvorye" (Rybinsk). Here, surrounded by a pine-birch forest, there are conditions for an interesting vacation for families with small children. It will also not be boring to fun companies, sports lovers and those guests who prefer silence. At the recreation center, you can order a venue for a celebration for any occasion.

"Myshkino Compound" is an excellent parking lot on a car, water or snowmobile route. If you want to go on excursions in the ancient city of Myshkin, Yaroslavl or Uglich, this recreation center can be a convenient departure point.



The situation on the territory of the complex

The nature of the base impresses with its unspoilt beauty. Pure pine-birch forest fills the air with a healthy aroma, and the Volga River allows you to fully plunge into the atmosphere of relaxation and tranquility. All wooden houses, cottages, baths, a restaurant harmoniously fit into this landscape. All of them are made in the traditional Russian style and do not violate the composition. "Myshkino Compound" skillfully combines virgin nature and a decent level of comfort.

Camp infrastructure



In addition to cottages and buildings, the territory is equipped with additional benefits. There is a restaurant, a club complex, a stage for dance evenings and a kids club. Also built two Russian baths and one Finnish sauna. For leisure activities, there are barbecue facilities, a volleyball and soccer field, mini-golf, an ice rink and a playground. There are other entertainments at the recreation center, for example, tennis, billiards. There is free car parking. In winter you can go skiing or ice skating.

On the banks of the Volga there is a sandy, wild beach. At the boat station camp site you can rent a boat. Paid fishing is also allowed here.

Additional services

For an additional fee at the base, you can access the Internet via Wi-Fi, which is located at the reception. You can also rent many household appliances, such as a TV, iron with board, refrigerator and others. You can rent a high chair at the restaurant. There is also a babysitting service.

Restaurant and Food



It is worth noting that renting a room at Myshkino Podvorye does not include meals. But this does not matter, because there is a cozy restaurant serving Russian rustic cuisine. Local fish and berries that grow in the forest can be considered a special treat here. Here you can taste flavored cakes with blueberries, lingonberries, cranberries. Kulebyaki with meat, pies with cabbage, mushrooms, cheesecakes with cottage cheese and other fresh pastries are also offered.

The restaurant, like the entire recreation center "Myshkino Compound", is made in a rustic Russian style from chopped wood and stone. It starts work in the early morning and is open until 10 pm. It has two rooms for 200 and 80 guests. A buffet and bar are also provided.
