Hotel Blue Sky 4. Kemer. Turkey

The beach complex Blue Sky 4 is conveniently located in the tourist zone of Kirish, 6 kilometers from the popular Turkish resort of Kemer.

Description

The four-story hotel was built in 1997, 400 meters from the sea and the pebble beach. The building is adjacent to a small green area with flowering shrubs, palm trees and a pool area. Nearby is a night club, many shops and bars. It takes about an hour and a half to get to Blue Sky from the airport.



Rooms

The hotel fund has 104 modern rooms, which were renovated in 2009. You can book a standard room, family apartment or a room for disabled guests.

Each room has a balcony, TV, Russian TV channels and air conditioning. For an extra charge, a personal safe or minibar is provided. On the balcony, where there is a clothes dryer and a table with chairs, you can have breakfast and enjoy mountain views.



Rooms are cleaned daily. Linen and towels must be replaced every three days.

Beach

The hotel has a public pebble beach area where you can use sun loungers and awnings for free.

Nutrition

Blue Sky 4 serves customers according to the All Inclsuive scheme from 8 a.m. to 10 p.m. Guests are welcome in the restaurant and on the outdoor terrace. 4 meals are offered, as well as snacks and drinks throughout the day. International and national treats, pastries, fruits, sweets are presented. Bars are open on the beach and pool so that vacationers can always quench their thirst with cold drinks.



Tourist info

Blue Sky 4 is a typical beachfront hotel on the Mediterranean coast of Turkey. Thanks to the established service and good infrastructure, guests are provided with comfortable accommodation and a carefree vacation.

The hotel has a shop and laundry service, car rental and a medical room. The bath complex invites you to a hammam and sauna. The fitness center has a gym and massage rooms. On a cozy territory there are three outdoor pools: a children's one and two large ones, one of which has slides.

The hotel has tennis courts and a volleyball court, a soccer field and billiards tables. A hairdresser and internet cafe are located next to the hotel. You can go on a guided tour at any time.

Reviews

One of the convenient accommodation options in Kirish is the Blue Sky 4 hotel. Reviews from tourists will always help you make the final decision on choosing a hotel.

When booking this hotel, you need to know that it is located in a quiet Turkish village, where the main vacation is the sea and beach activities.



Tourists will have a cordial meeting and a friendly attitude from the staff throughout the entire stay. Guests are accommodated in modest simple rooms, which are cleaned daily and thoroughly.

On the table of the restaurant there is any food, including hot meat dishes, fish, sweets, pastries, greens, a large number of berries and fruits, all kinds of local and imported drinks.

There are no complaints about the animators. In the afternoon, sports classes and competitions are held daily. In the evening, discos, colorful shows and national dance classes are organized.

Blue Sky is a cozy inexpensive hotel designed for unpretentious tourists.
