Holiday Village "Krenitsy" - overview, description, features and reviews of tourists

The beauty and grandeur of Lake Ladoga attract tourists not only from the Leningrad Region, but also from all over our vast country. Ecotourism is very well developed in the surrounding areas; numerous pilgrims seek to visit the holy places of Balaam. It is not surprising that with such a massive attendance of Ladoga there was a great demand for accommodation facilities. Among the numerous tourist complexes, I would like to highlight the recreation center "Krenitsy", which will be discussed in our article.

Lake Ladoga: you need to know

Due to its enormous size (more than 17 thousand km 2 ), it is located immediately in two territorial districts: in the Republic of Karelia and the Leningrad Region. It is the largest freshwater lake in the European part of the continent. On the vast territory around Lake Ladoga, you can see the marvelous diversity of both plant and animal life. The connection with the seas at this lake of glacial origin lies through the Belamor-Baltic Canal. Initially, the lake was called Nebo, it was still known from the chronicles of Nestor of the XII century. The real hydronym appeared due to the formation of a settlement on its shores - Ladoga.

Severe weather conditions (a combination of temperate-continental and temperate-sea climate) absolutely do not affect the attendance of the lake by travelers and vacationers. Leaden heavy clouds that go low above the reservoir, the rare rays of the sun, an almost English nebula and powerful winds - all this creates a unique and attractive image of the northern beauty.





The coastline stretches for 1,500 km, the average depths are about 40-50 m, and the maximum is 200-230 m. The northern part of the lake resembles the Scandinavian countries: there are skerries and fjords, consisting of unusually beautiful granite blocks. In addition, numerous islands forming the archipelago are concentrated in this part of Ladoga. It is here that you can see the largest island of the archipelago - Valaam.

The southern coast is low, territorially refers to the Leningrad region. He is famous for the fact that Peter I himself founded the city of Novaya Ladoga here, and then at the beginning of the XVIII century the entire area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe lake began to belong to the Russian Empire.

Features of the recreation center "Krenitsy"

The Volkhov Bay protrudes into the southern shore of the lake, this place is very picturesque. Three rivers flow into it from different sides: Volkhov, Syas and Voronezhka. The shores are low, overgrown with reeds, shrubs, and forest areas. In the village of Krenitsy you can see the relict juniper grove, famous throughout the region. The air here is clean and healing.





Near the place where the Volkhov River flows into Lake Ladoga, in the village of the same name there is a wonderful recreation center "Krenitsy". This tourist complex is popular not only among residents of the Leningrad region, many visitors from distant regions remain here for some time. It will be interesting for fishermen, outdoor enthusiasts, and lovers of historical and cultural heritage.

The complex is very conveniently located: from Novaya Ladoga to the Krenitsa recreation center is only a few meters, you can get here from St. Petersburg in 2-3 hours. On b / o everything is saturated with Russian identity. An advanced stove, like in the fairy tale about Emelya, drives on wheels, the apartments are decorated with wood, the staff cordially greets their guests with bread and salt.

The recreation center in Krenitsy of the Volkhov region is open all year. Entertainment for every taste and budget can be found for vacationers both in summer and in winter.

Territory

The entire b / o area can be divided into guest accommodation areas, which include small apart-hotels and car camping with trailers, parking for equipment and cars.

The territory of the recreation center "Krenitsy" is very clean and well maintained. In spring and summer, guests can enjoy the beautiful garden, and in autumn you can comfortably sit near the brazier in a wooden gazebo and watch the falling red and yellow leaves.

A beach is equipped on the shore of the lake, sunbathers use sun loungers and deck chairs, umbrellas. The sand on the shore is very small, the entry into the water is gentle - a real paradise for little swimmers. A little away is the pier for boats, boats and other boats.
