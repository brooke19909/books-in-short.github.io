Should I go to Paris in January: weather, shopping, reviews

A trip to Paris in January is an opportunity to see the city in a festive design, beautiful and elegant: light elegant touches in the everyday picture - several bright balls on evergreen Christmas trees, flower arrangements on the parapets of bridges, in shops, cafes. But in the evening and at night, Paris seemed to explode with fantastic highlights and illuminations, acquiring a fabulous look.

We buy tickets Moscow-Paris

Faster and easier to fly to Paris by direct flight from Moscow airport in just 4 hours. But we must be prepared for the fact that due to bad weather flights to Paris in January may be delayed. In addition, a direct flight is the most expensive. Tourists give tips on bargain buying tickets:

  • to take tickets for flights with transfers, which will cost almost two times cheaper than for a direct flight;
  • to purchase tickets of one airline for both flights. If you are late due to weather conditions for a connecting flight, the airline takes care of all further flights to Paris;
  • immediately order round-trip tickets.

Tickets are best purchased in advance by tracking possible discounts and last-minute tours. The cost of tickets depends on the number when the departure is planned:

  • the most expensive tickets to Paris in January - January 2 and 3, the cheapest - January 13 and at the end of the month;
  • from Paris the most expensive - January 5-8, the cheapest - from the middle to the end of the month.

The Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower, which received the second name almost immediately after construction - ‚ÄúIron Lady‚Äù, amazes with festive illumination in the first month of the year, and those who celebrated the New Year in the capital are remembered against the backdrop of a stunning fireworks display. The weather in Paris in January does not always allow you to see the entire 324-meter tower and take the elevator or foot to the observation deck, from where the whole city is in full view. But there is an opportunity to see the openwork structure near it, to skate on the rink (free, round-the-clock), flooded on the site of the tower, located at an altitude of 50 meters from the ground.





The louvre museum

Paris in January is attractive due to the absence or short bursts of museums, the ability to calmly contemplate masterpieces, moving, without rushing, from one exhibit to another. The exception is the Louvre: even in January, you will have to stand in line to get to this museum. But the smile of Mona Lisa and the wings of Nika of Samothrace will reward your expectation.

Notre Dame de Paris

Notre Dame de Paris Cathedral (Notre-Dame-de-Paris) is a Gothic cathedral, striking with the architecture of the building, and stained-glass windows, and interior design. This is an active Catholic church, services are held here, accompanied by the sounds of an ancient organ. The cathedral houses a Christian shrine - the crown of thorns of Jesus Christ.

The main street of the city

The 50-meter Arc de Triomphe was erected by order of Napoleon, and the bas-reliefs decorating it tell of his victories, and the arch itself is one of the most beautiful decorations of Paris. From the Arc de Triomphe, the 2-km Champs Elysees begins, surrounded by parks, shops, cafes. On the Champs Elysees, Parisians traditionally celebrate the New Year, and in January festive illumination still colors the main street of Paris.

January is the month of sales and high fashion.

Twice a year, seasonal monthly sales take place in Paris, when clothes, shoes, accessories, including brand ones, can be bought in almost all stores with significant discounts.

Winter sales begin on the second Wednesday of January, in 2018 they will be held from January 10 to February 13. Traveling tips in Paris in January will help tourists:

  1. So, at the beginning of sales, discounts are minimal - 10-20%, next week - 35-50%, in the third - 70-75%, by the end they reach 80-90%.
  2. It is better to make purchases in the morning and at lunchtime, when there are fewer buyers in stores - after all, fashionistas from different countries purposefully come to sales.
  3. Do not devote weekend shopping to shopping.
  4. Ideal places for shopping, according to tourists' reviews - outlets (shopping centers for sales). The most popular: La Vallee Village (near Disneyland) and Usines Center Paris Nord (near Charles de Gaulle Airport).

In the midst of a winter sale in Paris, an event takes place at which film stars and pop stars come together - High Fashion Week. It shows the trends of the upcoming spring-summer season. But you can get to these shows only with special invitation cards.
