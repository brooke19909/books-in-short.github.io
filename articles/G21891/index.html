Catamaran Hotel (Turkey / Kemer / Beldibi): hotel description and reviews

If the client wants to spend a vacation in exotic conditions, travel agencies often offer a trip to Turkey, to one of its best resorts. Professionals note that in this region the most optimal climate for relaxing in the tropical zone, as well as the most optimal prices.

One of the most popular Turkish resorts is Beldibi, and the Catamaran Resort Hotel 5 * is among the most popular hotels located on its territory.



Location and General Information

The hotel is located in the heart of the tourist region of Turkey - in Beldibi, in the suburbs of Kemer. Not far from it is the airport - literally 45 kilometers away. The Catamaran Hotel is a beachfront hotel, as it is located on the first coastline of the Mediterranean Sea.

This vacation spot has a certain feature that distinguishes it from other resort areas - its building resembles a catamaran, from where, in fact, the name came from. Another feature of the Catamaran Hotel is that its rules prohibit the lodging of single men in apartments.

The hotel was built in 2004. On its territory and in the premises, work is regularly carried out to maintain the general condition - the last time such activity was carried out in 2014. Then some adjustments were made to his work, and the equipment of the rooms was also improved.

Number of rooms

Everything is arranged in a hotel in such a way that absolutely everyone - both adults and children - is comfortable on its territory, so there are several types of apartments that are designed for different needs and different numbers of guests: standard, family, suite, and also prime. In total, 223 rooms are located on the floors of the hotel‚Äôs large building.

All apartments have a private bathroom, where there is a bathroom, shower, as well as a sink. In addition, each guest is provided with bath towels, bathrobes and slippers.

All rooms are equipped with individual air conditioners, with the help of which, if desired, the guest can raise or lower the air temperature in the room based on his own preferences.



Standard

Standard category rooms at the Catamaran Hotel are designed to accommodate two guests, often for staying they are chosen by couples in love who go on a romantic trip. Some rooms are designed for three guests. In the standard of this type there are two beds: one double and a single.



Family

This type of room provides for the settlement of four people and, if necessary, a child under the age of 12 years. These apartments are 45 square meters. m, they are divided into two separate rooms, each of which has two beds, and in one of them - a cot, which is provided on a free basis. There are also several bedside tables and a large wardrobe, in which tourists can store their clothes and personal belongings.

From electronics there is one TV connected to satellite television. Guests also have access to an electronic safe and minibar. There is also a telephone, but for its use it will be necessary to pay at a certain tariff, which can be checked with the administrator at the reception desk.

The territory of the room is divided into two parts: the sleeping and living areas, which are separated by a sliding door. In the living room there is a set of upholstered furniture, which includes a sofa and two magnificent chairs; there is also a glass coffee table and a huge wardrobe. The minibar has a large assortment of drinks, which is updated every day. It includes both alcoholic and non-alcoholic options. An electronic kettle is provided in these apartments, so guests can prepare hot drinks without leaving their room.

Rooms of this category are cleaned daily and linen is changed every three days. Access to the minibar is free.

Living conditions for children

The Catamaran Hotel is declared in all catalogs as a place that is ideal for families. This means that conditions have been created on its territory that will make children staying in its rooms very comfortable and safe.

So, in any apartment, upon request of parents, a baby bed can be delivered - this is completely free (by the way, in family-type apartments it comes in a complete set). If necessary, parents can also rent strollers.

If there is a need to leave the baby for a while, you can use the services of a nanny - they are paid, the tariff is about $ 15 per hour, but the staff includes only professionals who know exactly how to deal with children. Also, for this purpose, you can use a special club where you can take children from 4 to 12 years old - his visit is free, but he works on a certain schedule: from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. with a break from 12:00 to 14:30 .

If desired, the kids can also frolic in a specially equipped pool for them, the depth of which is small.

Entertainment for adults

Guests at the Catamaran Resort Hotel can have fun without leaving the hotel. To ensure that no guest is bored, a well-coordinated team consisting of 15 animators who can find an approach to tourists of different ages works here all day. By the way, as noted by travelers, many guys and girls in this group know the Russian language, which greatly facilitates communication with them for tourists from Russia and the CIS countries.

Every evening on the outdoor area, which is in the hotel near the pool, begins an entertainment program and a party that lasts all night long. At this time, show programs with various fun contests are held here. From morning until lunch, tourists are entertained with sporting events, which also take place near the water area.

Nutrition

For guests who have come to rest on a package tour, meals are organized in a buffet format, which is served daily at local restaurants. The catering facilities at the Catamaran Resort Hotel (Beldibi) are very beautifully furnished and attract guests with their rich views.

There are three good restaurants serving fish, as well as Turkish and Italian cuisine. Before you visit any of them, you need to book a table, the cost of the service is $ 20 per person.

Guests can also visit the four local bars located near the pool and on the beach. They offer some types of snacks and a huge selection of drinks for every taste.



Sport

Many tourists are very pleased that the Catamaran Resort Hotel (Turkey / Kemer) has a beautiful sports hall for them, which has a considerable number of exercise machines. Thus, even on vacation, you can maintain your body in shape. You can visit it for free. There are also several rooms for playing table tennis and billiards.

As for team sports, even without leaving the territory of this recreation area, you can play volleyball, football and basketball - for this, wonderful venues are designed where you can take all the necessary equipment for free.

Fans of extreme sports can go in for water sports (water skiing, surfing), but for this you need to use the paid services of an instructor. The hotel also offers bike hire.

Spa

The spa area is also very popular among holidaymakers at the Catamaran Resort Hotel (Turkey). It is located on a fairly large area and is divided into several independent sections: a swimming pool, a thermal zone and a massage room.

The thermal zone is very loved by guests. It consists of a Turkish bath (hammam) and a sauna. Guests can use the services of parmeisters upon request. After all the procedures, there is the opportunity to relax in the large indoor pool, which is located immediately at the exit of the thermal part. It has an automatic heating system installed, so swimming will be comfortable at any temperature that will stand in the room.





For business

Businessmen who stay for a vacation at the Catamaran Resort Hotel often say in their reviews that there are all conditions for a successful business. There are three well-equipped conference rooms, in each of which you can hold business meetings, negotiate and make presentations. They have modern furniture in the form of large lacquered dark wood tables and leather chairs. Also in any room there is modern technology that will make the event brighter. There is electronics and some office supplies.

Rent of each of these halls is paid separately according to the established price list.

Pool

In the midst of the spa season, a huge number of holidaymakers gather near the pool in an open area, who on sunny days prefer to lie on a deck chair with a glass of cool cocktail. The hotel has two outdoor pools, each of which is 500 and 60 square meters. m. They have a small water park consisting of several slides - tourists can actively spend time on them. A visit to the water zone is possible on any day of the week, but according to a separate schedule: from 7 a.m. to 8 p.m.

Both in one and in the other pool there is no heating, but the water here is always clean, as they are cleaned daily.

Around the pools there is a large sun terrace, on which there are many comfortable sun loungers, as well as umbrellas, thanks to which guests of the Catamaran Hotel (Turkey) have the opportunity to hide from direct exposure to sunlight. Also in the territory of the water zone there is a large bar where a wide assortment of light food and various drinks is presented.



Services

Hotel guests are regularly provided with a number of services that are not included in the price of the ticket. So, the Catamaran Resort Hotel (Turkey, Kemer, Beldibi) has a dry cleaning and laundry service on a regular basis - guests can take their laundry and ironing items here. On the ground floor of the main building there is a doctor‚Äôs office, where a local specialist in the field of medicine can always provide the necessary assistance, but his services are also paid.

Subject to an hourly fee, guests can visit the local computer club, which has the opportunity to play various games, even entire companies. Fans of beauty treatments are always waiting in the beauty salon, as well as in the hairdresser.





All tourists who wish to explore local attractions on their own can rent a car - the hotel has several options. For such a car, a parking space for the entire rental period is attached for free.

Beach

Tourists who wish to relax on the Mediterranean coast can go to the beach, which belongs to the hotel. As already mentioned, the complex was built on the first line, which is very convenient.

The beach of the Catamaran Hotel (Kemer) is always clean, the surrounding nature is very beautiful. The whole territory here is covered with soft fine sand and small pebbles. The descent into the water is gentle, and the depth grows gradually - this contributes to the safe pastime of children on the sea coast.



The entire beach area is well-maintained, it has several public toilets, showers, and locker rooms. On its territory there is also a bar where vacationers can relax and enjoy a cold drink or a bright cocktail. For guests' relaxation, large and very comfortable sun loungers under huge umbrellas are placed throughout the sandy-pebble area - they can be taken for free. Also, if necessary, lovers of swimming in the sea can take for themselves an air mattress - it is also issued completely free.

On the sea coast there is a lot of entertainment for every taste. Here, romantically-minded couples are offered to rent yachts and boats - you can take them on a short trip together in the open sea. For those who want to experience the drive and get an adrenaline rush, there is the opportunity to ride a jet ski or air cushion. Would you like to get acquainted with the underwater world? This can also be done, but only under the guidance of a professional instructor - his services are paid.



Prices

The cost of a ticket to the Catamaran Resort Hotel (Beldibi) is everywhere different and depends on what exactly is included in the package and from which city the tourist will begin his movement. In the event that the tourist will take off by plane from the Moscow airport, the average tour price will be about 55 thousand rubles. It includes a round-trip flight, room rental, meals, insurance premium, as well as a transfer from the airport. Moreover, this cost is calculated for a week of accommodation for two in a standard room.
