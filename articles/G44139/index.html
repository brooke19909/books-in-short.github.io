Hotel "Height 1170" - "Rosa Khutor": description, reviews

Sochi is a unique place where you can have a good rest both in winter and in summer. If you have chosen exactly this direction for your vacation, pay attention to such an option as the hotel "Height 1170". Here reigns cosiness, comfort and friendly atmosphere.





Short hotel description

"Elevation 1170" is a cozy hotel located in the center of a popular resort on the territory of the Olympic Village. Its name clearly reflects its location - it is located at around 1170 meters above sea level.

The hotel, located in the famous resort "Rosa Khutor", is a modern six-story complex with 165 comfortable rooms for tourists. The interior is made in a modern laconic style, and the view of the mountains that opens from the windows will be a pleasant addition to the overall picture. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel there are ski slopes, where both beginners and professionals can try their hand. And even for the smallest there is a separate section of the route.

Hotel "Height 1170" is a legendary place, because it was here in 2014 that the Russian Olympic team was located. Thus, the hotel is literally saturated with the atmosphere of victories and achievements. The overall picture is complemented by clean mountain air and developed sports infrastructure. Guests can enjoy themselves and have a good time in the author's restaurant and a cozy loft bar. After a busy day you can relax in the sauna.

Contacts

The hotel is located at: Russia, Sochi, p. Esto-Sadok, st. Sulimovka, 9. Phone number of the hotel "Height 1170" can be found on the official website of the hotel.

Accommodation

All rooms of the hotel "Height 1170" are planned, decorated and equipped so that the athletes and ordinary tourists in them was comfortable and cozy. To accommodate guests, the following options are provided:

  • The standard double room is 22 square meters. m. The room has two single beds, bedside tables, desk. The combined bathroom is equipped with a shower. It offers views of the Olympic Village or the mountains. You can choose the accommodation option with or without a balcony.
  • Standard Triple Room is 25 square meters. m. The room has three single beds, a desk and a spacious wardrobe. There is also a balcony from which you can see the Olympic village or enjoy panoramic views of the mountains.
  • 45 sqm Family Room m is presented in two versions. The standard consists of two rooms (living room and bedroom), and in the studio these areas are combined in a common space. There are three beds for guests, as well as a folding soft sofa, which can serve for additional accommodation.




Room Amenities

If you have chosen the Vysota 1170 hotel for your relaxation, you can be sure that your room will be as comfortable as at home. This became possible due to the presence of such a list of amenities in the room:

  • combined bathroom with toilet, shower, hairdryer and bath accessories;
  • Wireless Internet signal; TV with cable or satellite channels;
  • small fridge;
  • individually controlled air conditioning;
  • heating system for rest in the winter;
  • smoke detectors;
  • capacious wardrobe;
  • rack for drying clothes;
  • furnished balcony (not in all rooms).




Catering establishments

Hotel "Elevation 1170" ensures that guests have access to a wholesome and tasty meal. For this purpose, the following institutions work:

  • Restaurant "1170" offers guests delicious and hearty breakfasts, which are held through the buffet. This meal is included in the price. In the daytime and in the evening, you can taste exquisite original dishes from the menu. Fans of healthy foods will enjoy a wide range of vegetarian and vegan dishes. Tourists with small children will appreciate the special menu and comfortable furniture. This restaurant is also a great place for banquets and receptions.
  • The Loft Bar "Peak" is a cozy atmospheric establishment that is open from noon to midnight. Here is a wide selection of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, as well as delicious and light snacks. The main advantage of the institution is a panoramic view of the mountain peaks.




Hotel Infrastructure

Hotel "Height 1170" is fully consistent with the needs and expectations of tourists. On the territory of this institution you can use the following infrastructure facilities:

  • storage room for ski equipment;
  • catering establishments;
  • shared lounge with TV;
  • free parking area;
  • facilities for guests with disabilities;
  • Wireless internet throughout.




Service

If you are accustomed to quality service, choose the Rosa Khutor resort . The hotel provides its guests with the following services:

  • free shuttles to the ski lifts run every half hour;
  • organization of business and special occasions;
  • 24-hour reception
  • rental of ski equipment;
  • organization of transfer from the airport and from the railway station;
  • vending machine with drinks, snacks and pastries;
  • buffet breakfast.

Entertainment

Fans of bright and active recreation will certainly enjoy the city of Sochi. "Height 1170" provides its guests with such entertainment opportunities:

  • children's playroom with special furniture and toys;
  • children's ski complex "Egorka";
  • a sauna where you can relax after a busy day;
  • a pool table;
  • karaoke;

  • instructing in mountain sports.




Special offers

Travelers are always attracted by the opportunity to take advantage of promotions. The whole system is developed in the hotel "Height 1170". Hotel 3 * provides its guests with the opportunity to take advantage of these special offers:

  • "Spring exacerbation" is valid all March. Guests who book rooms for this period receive a 15% discount, as well as a free room upgrade (if possible).
  • "To the mountains with friends." When booking three or more rooms for a period of two nights, guests receive as a gift a two-hour rest in the sauna.
  • "Two equals three." When booking a standard room for two nights, the third night is provided as a compliment.

Negative reviews

Many diverse reviews can be found about the hotel 3 * "Height 1170" (Sochi, Krasnaya Polyana). Among them, there are negative ones. Particular attention should be paid to such negative comments:

  • inflated prices for transfers to the airport and train station;
  • no buffet for lunch and dinner;
  • in the bathroom there is no cup for toothbrushes (you just have to put them on a shelf);
  • towels are changed irregularly;
  • the front doors in the rooms are literally "cardboard";
  • the bathroom has an unpleasant smell of dampness;
  • the locker room in the sauna is common (without separate booths), which is not very comfortable;
  • very small balconies, which are closely together for two or three;
  • there are no double rooms, and if you move the separate ones, a hole is formed between them;
  • beds creak strongly at the slightest movement;
  • near the mirror in the bathroom there is no outlet, and therefore you have to touch with a hairdryer;
  • not all rooms have a refrigerator;
  • there is no power outlet near the table on which the electric kettle is located, and therefore you have to rearrange the appliance on the nightstand;
  • uncomfortable little pillows;
  • very cramped rooms;
  • there is no lock or latch on the outside of the balcony door;
  • there is very little space in the ski boot drying room;
  • once a week at night, the fire alarm is turned on, which can interfere with rest;
  • if you do not check into the hotel before six in the evening, the reservation is automatically canceled without notice to the guest;
  • a mirror is only in the bathroom (not full length);
  • poor soundproofing in the rooms (you can hear everything that happens in the hallway);
  • parking is not on site, but a three-minute walk from it;
  • not the best wireless internet speed;
  • despite the fact that smoking is prohibited on the hotel territory, many guests smoke on the balconies, and the employees do not struggle with it (there are not even elementary announcements and a system of fines);
  • uncomfortable spring mattresses on the beds;
  • Slippers and bathrobes are not provided for guests (they can be taken at the reception for an additional fee).




Conclusion

If you want to feel the atmosphere of the Olympic Village, go on vacation to the hotel 3 * "Height 1170" (Krasnaya Polyana, Russia). This is a quiet and comfortable establishment with reasonable accommodation prices, friendly staff, comfortable rooms and an excellent restaurant. Until December 2017, the hotel is closed for major repairs, after which it is planned to upgrade to a four-star status. This will affect prices in the direction of their increase. Nevertheless, the conditions for relaxation promise to become even more comfortable, which will make you fall in love with the Rosa Khutor resort even more.
