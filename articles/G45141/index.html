How to exchange rubles for bitcoins and vice versa?

Predictions are circulating on the Web that bitcoins will soon sink into oblivion, although in fact this is a very liquid product, and it is in demand. Having bought bitcoin today, in a year or two you can earn some money, so many users want to know how to exchange rubles for bitcoins. Just note that there is nothing complicated about this. With the development of modern technology, this is done within a dozen minutes.





Where and how to exchange rubles for bitcoins?

The most obvious place of exchange is the bitcon exchange. There are special cryptocurrency exchanges on the Web where the process of buying bitcoins is simple. And if you are interested in how to exchange bitcoins for rubles in Russia, then this can be done there. True, the exchange of cryptocurrency will be carried out on your WebMoney wallet. Well, money can be easily withdrawn from a wallet to a bank card, but this is not about that now.

Exchange on exchanges

The most popular and oldest exchange is BTC-E. It works with rubles and is trusted by many users. It is BTC-E that has the highest trading volumes, and after hacking the exchange in 2012, all stolen money was returned to the affected users, so you can trust the BTC-E exchange.

Registration here is quick:

  • enter email;
  • login;
  • password;
  • confirm the mail.

Before exchanging rubles for bitcoins, study the systems the exchange works with. You can deposit rubles through Yandex Money, OKPAY and Moneta.ru services. You can also make a deposit in dollars, but only through the exchanges Qiwi, Epese, MoneyPolo, Moneta, Ecoin. After depositing money and exchanging it for bitcoins, you can withdraw the latter only after 72 hours.

Automatic exchangers

It is also profitable to exchange bitcoins for rubles using automatic exchangers. One of the most popular exchangers is Baksman, which works constantly. Can you trust him? Of course. If you have an account with Sberbank, you can exchange rubles for bitcoins using Baksman.





The second most popular and user-friendly is the Xchange exchanger. It also works around the clock and allows you to exchange money using the Yandex Money, VTB24, Alfa Bank, and Gazprom Bank systems.

Less popular is the ProstoCash service, where you can buy and sell bitcoins. The service cooperates with payment systems Perfect Money, Qiwi, ADVCash, Bitcoin, Payeer. To exchange you need a minimum of data.

There are a bunch of other small exchangers, but we will not name them here. Firstly, the mentioned services are more than enough to exchange large and small amounts. Secondly, there is no confidence in the reliability of little-known bitcoin exchange services.





Is it legal to use exchangers?

There is a law of the Russian Federation, according to which all payments between business entities must be made in rubles. But how then are the same bitcoins purchased for dollars? The fact is that when a payment is made in an online store, rubles are converted into dollars and vice versa. Therefore, a currency exchange is made on the territory of the Russian Federation, and the calculation is carried out in rubles. In this case, the foreign seller receives the currency. From the point of view of the legislation of the Russian Federation, the buyer simply exchanges rubles for dollars, and the dollars themselves are removed outside the jurisdiction of the Russian Federation. Bitcoins are just a product that can be bought in this way.

And if you do not know where to exchange bitcoins for rubles, then, based on the above, you can use any exchanger that meets the following requirements:

  1. It is not located in the .ru or . domain zone.
  2. Physically, the site is not located in the territory of the FI.




How to exchange bitcoins for rubles

Depending on which mining system you use, the output can be made in different ways. One of the most popular cryptocurrency projects is Minergate. Most users use this system for cloud mining, and when a certain amount accumulates, the question of withdrawal arises.

One of the most common output methods is not quite standard. No need to use even exchangers. The bottom line is to fund your Forex broker account with bitcoins. Many brokers support this "currency." After replenishment, bitcoins will be converted. You can withdraw them later from the broker's account to your bank card. You will have to pay a certain percentage, but often it is lower than the percentage in exchangers.

Alternatively, you can use exchangers automatically. Bankomat exchange system is one of the most popular and reliable. In order to exchange bitcoins into rubles and withdraw them, for example, to a Qiwi wallet, it is enough to indicate the amount for the exchange in the exchanger, choose a direction and indicate the email address, Qiwi wallet. After confirming the application, Bitcoins will be debited from the account of Minergate (or another cryptocurrency project), and you will receive rubles to the Qiwi wallet. Well, with this system they are easily sent to a bank card.

Webmoney withdrawal

By analogy with the Qiwi-wallet, you can also use the Webmoney wallet. To do this, you need to register in the system, get a formal certificate (confirm your passport details) and open a WMX wallet. From cloud mining systems, bitcoins can be sent immediately to Webmoney system WMX wallets. After that, they change to rubles directly in the system itself and are sent to a bank card. By the way, by analogy, you can do the opposite. That is, change rubles to bitcoins.
