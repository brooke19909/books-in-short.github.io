Hotel Woraburi The Ritz 4 * (Pattaya, Thailand): overview, description and reviews

The city of Pattaya in Thailand is popular among tourists. Not far from Bangkok, and go to the resort from Suvarnabhumi International Airport for a maximum of two hours and a half by bus. Again, Pattaya enjoys the glory of a city full of all Thai joys. Enough there and Asian exotics. As for the sea, the municipal beaches of Pattaya are not among the best. For clean white beaches tourists go to the islands. Well, where do vacationers live? Pattaya is surrounded by luxury five-star hotels. They have a huge territory with gardens. And in the city center, budget hotels settled down. They can be of different stardom: from four to simple guesthouses. This article is dedicated to one such city hotel with 4 * status: Woraburi The Ritz 4 * (Pattaya, Thailand). What are the pros and cons of this hotel? We will try to understand this by analyzing the reviews of tourists.



Hotel Location

Pattaya resort is more than a hundred kilometers south of Bangkok, on the shore of the Gulf of Thailand. Now consider the location of the hotel Woraburi The Ritz 4 *. Pattaya has a simple "chess" division of the streets. The main avenue is Central Street. Perpendicular to him, but parallel to the embankment are streets with the uncomplicated names First, Second and Third. Such a tradition here. Our hotel is located between Second and Third Street, very close to the central avenue. So there is enough urban infrastructure around the hotel. Nearby there is a Big S supermarket. According to reviews, within walking distance there are many restaurants and cafes. An incredibly delicious tom yam soup is being prepared right across the road from the hotel. The municipal beach, as well as the famous Walking Street, can be reached on foot or in tuk-tuk. Also, by public transport you will have to get to the pier to relax on the island closest to the city called Ko Lan.



Number of rooms

Hotel Woraburi The Ritz 4 * (Pattaya) is very new, only recently put into operation. So the furniture, plumbing and even the rooms for guests please with freshness and full service. The hotel rooms are divided into only two categories. The budget is called "Superior Room". All rooms of this category are located on the upper floors of the buildings. A window and a small balcony overlook the pool. The second category of rooms is called intricately: "Duplex pool access". This means that the "Suite" consists of two floors and has direct access to the pool. Naturally, these numbers occupy the first floors of the buildings. What do the reviews say about the internal equipment of the guest rooms? The rooms are very modern, everything is new, clean. The towels that change once a day are also white and fluffy. The room has a new design air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a refrigerator, where a bottle of drinking water is put daily, an electric kettle with cups, and a safe. The bathroom is changed daily hygiene items. The room has a hairdryer.

Many tourists had lunch and dinner at food courts, which are mandatory in all large grocery supermarkets. Do not be afraid to buy food from the so-called "macaques." These are mobile kitchens where you can very cheaply taste freshly baked kebabs or seafood. The price of accommodation in the hotel Woraburi The Ritz 4 * (Pattaya) includes breakfast. Territory

Hotel Woraburi The Ritz 4 * (Pattaya) is a typical city hotel. Therefore, it does not have any territory - land in the center of Pattaya is worth its weight in gold. Between the buildings is a swimming pool with an artificial peninsula, which is seated with palm trees. It is laden with sunbeds. There are no umbrellas there, and they are not needed. As the guests say in their reviews, the sun does not look into the pool at noon. The rest of the day, the shadow of one of the buildings falls on him. This makes the pool only swimming. Many tourists even liked this fact. Usually there are very few people, it seems that this is your individual pool. It is very convenient, having arrived with an excursion, to plunge into its cool waters. The pool is open until eight in the evening and is beautifully illuminated in the dark. By the way, all rooms overlook the courtyard, where it is usually quite quiet.

The buildings of the Woraburi The Ritz 4 * hotel (Pattaya) have an elevator. The hotel has parking, free for guests. Receptionists are fluent in English and are ready to help you at any time of the day. If you arrived very early or want to leave Pattaya in the evening, you can leave your suitcases in the hotel‚Äôs luggage storage room and go on your business lightly. What to see in the resort of Pattaya (Pattaya)

Woraburi The Ritz 4 * is located in the city center, and this makes the hotel a convenient starting point for sightseeing. First of all, you need to visit Walking Street, and preferably in the evening, in the midst of fun. This is "mast si number 1." For the sake of clean beaches and the same sea, you should go to the islands. Mast Sea No. 2 is a floating market in Pattaya. A day should be spent on Jomtien Beach. Reviews say that it is better to buy excursions from street operators. The services they provide are the same as in the travel agency at the hotel, and the prices are much lower. Tourists traveled to the village of elephants, the tiger zoo "Siracha", a farm for growing snakes and crocodiles, tropical park Nong Nooch. For Buddhist meditation, go to the Temple of Truth.
