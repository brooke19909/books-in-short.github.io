Altai Territory: rest. Altai Krai: lakes and recreation centers

Every person needs a rest from time to time. Altai Territory is a real treasury for tourists. After all, this place can offer its guests a lot of entertainment. Here you can swim in the lake, sunbathe on the beach, visit historical sites or even make a real trip to the mountains.

Altai Territory: a brief description





Of course, many tourists are interested in questions about how the rest looks in these territories. Altai Territory is located in the southeastern part of Western Siberia and is part of the Siberian Federal District. In the southeast, these territories border the Altai Republic, and in the north, with the Kemerovo and Novosibirsk regions.

Description of natural conditions

It is immediately worth noting that the Altai Territory is a completely amazing place. Here you will find fast rivers, and large lakes with well-equipped beaches, and mountains, as well as a lot of interesting historical and natural attractions, big cities, resorts and cozy resort villages. That is why an amazing vacation awaits you here. Altai Krai is a land of contrasts, where every traveler, even the most demanding one, will find suitable entertainment for himself.

These territories are rich in various reservoirs. The largest rivers to date are Katun, Ob, Alei, Biya, Charysh and Chumysh. There are also 13 thousand lakes of various sizes. Altai is famous for its rich flora and fauna - there are 320 species of birds, 90 species of mammals, as well as more than 2,000 plants, including relict and endemic species.

Lakes of Altai Territory: beach holidays

Altai - the territory of a thousand lakes. And the waters of most of them have healing properties due to their unique mineral composition. Near large lakes, as a rule, there are small resort centers - here for tourists they organize vacations and offer a rehabilitation program. The Great Spring Lake, as well as the Salt Lake, are very popular.

But Markarol is the largest mountain pond in these territories - it is located in a hollow between mountain ranges at an altitude of 1450 meters above sea level. Another interesting place is Svetloye, or Swan, a lake whose water does not freeze even in winter. By the way, there is a trout farm that will please fishing enthusiasts.

In fact, in Altai there are about 17 thousand lakes of various sizes. And, of course, recreation centers in the Altai Territory offer their guests all possible types of water recreation.

Health centers of Altai





Recreation facilities in the Altai Territory will please even the most fastidious travelers, since here everyone can choose a place that is suitable for the price or level of comfort. In particular, motels and dispensaries are very popular, of which there are really many. For example, in the city of Belokurikha alone there are several health resorts, including Altai-West, Altai Spring, Altai Castle, and Northern Lights.

You will find no less places to relax in Barnaul, the administrative center of Altai. These are the sanatoriums Ob, Barnaulsky, Barnaul, Energetik, Sibir, etc. Each of the above places offers its guests comfortable rooms, various wellness treatments, as well as a lot of entertainment and educational opportunities.

Zavyalovo (Altai Territory): recreation centers and attractions





Of course, in these territories there are many interesting places. And today, vacation in Zavyalova is very popular. Altai Krai here pleases travelers not only with its luxurious nature and excellent living conditions. The territory of the district is crossed by a chain of small lakes, each of which has some unique healing properties.

That is why thousands of tourists from Russia and other countries every year allot time for a vacation in Zavyalova (Altai Territory). Recreation and entertainment here can be combined with the general improvement of the body. After all, Solyonoye Lake is located very nearby, the mineralized waters of which are widely used to treat diseases of internal organs. It also produces healing mud and white clay for wellness and beauty treatments. And, of course, the effect of therapy is enhanced by the clean air of the pine forest.

As for the living conditions in the village of Zavyalovo (Altai Territory), there are recreation facilities here, as well as health centers, state sanatoriums, as well as entertainment complexes. With the help of brine, mineral water, and mud, diseases of the skin, bone, muscle and digestive system are treated here. The most popular places for recreation are the resort complexes "Zavyalovo" and "Power of Lakes", which are located near ponds. Here guests are offered rooms and small cottages in the forest, as well as a huge selection of medical procedures.

Winter holidays and popular ski resorts





If you are interested in skiing, then Altai Territory is the best place for a winter vacation. After all, what could be better than snowy peaks? And the small resort town of Belokurikha is especially popular.

This picturesque place is located in the northern part of the Cherginsky ridge in the southeast of Altai. There are several tracks for tourists. In particular, skiers can go down from the mountains Yakovka and Tserkovka.

What living conditions can Altai Territory offer? The recreation center "Aurora", the sanatorium "Russia", as well as the resort center "Maryino" are happy to open their doors to travelers. Here you will find delicious food, a soft bed and, of course, a lot of entertainment.

Mountain trip





Many fans of the so-called "green" tourism are interested in questions about whether active recreation is possible in these territories. Altai Krai is not only the steppe, plains and lakes, but also high cliffs and mountain ranges. Therefore, every traveler who wants to climb to the top will find a suitable route. And in this case, a zone called Mountain Charysh is especially popular - it is a basin of the river of the same name, surrounded on all sides by mountains and forests. Here you can visit numerous caves, swim in the river, climb any peak you like and, of course, enjoy the breathtaking panorama.

As for housing, here also the choice will be yours - you can sleep in a tent, rent a room in one of the recreation centers (for example, "Zeleny Ostrov" or "Kaimskoye") or use the services of the private sector - local residents will be happy to look for you place for an overnight stay and help you navigate the terrain.

Altai Territory - a treasure for history buffs

Not everyone knows that the first settlements on the territory of Altai appeared 30-40 thousand years ago. And this place can offer guests not only entertainment, but also educational relaxation. Altai Territory has a rich history. For example, those who wish can visit the ancient burial sites, which date back to the IV-II millennium BC.

Naturally, there are more modern monuments of architecture and history. For example, near the village of Topolny there is Denisova Cave, where excavations have been conducted for more than 20 years. Guests will have the opportunity to drive the house-museum of V. Shukshin, visit a lot of museums, not to mention various natural attractions, including rivers, caves, mountains and waterfalls. And the center of Altai, the city of Barnaul, is rich in various monuments of modern architecture, history and culture.
