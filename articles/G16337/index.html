Number of rooms

Le President Aquapark 3 * offers its guests 216 rooms in the main building (five-story) and 170 apartments located in the bungalow. All rooms have telephone, bathroom, TV, hairdryer, air conditioning. The apartments in the main building have sea or garden views.

Two-story bungalows are scattered throughout the territory, some of them are quite far from the main building and the sea. The main advantage of such mini-buildings is silence, since they are distant from the noisy center of the complex.

Dining at the hotel

Le President Aquapark 3 * offers its guests All Inclusive. The hotel has several bars and restaurants serving Tunisian and international dishes.

The main restaurant operates on a buffet basis. Breakfast includes omelettes, eggs, sausages, stewed vegetables, cheeses, sausages, cottage cheese, fresh vegetables, breakfast cereals. In the mornings, the restaurant serves delicious pastries (such as croissants). From drinks you can take tea, cocoa, coffee, instant juices.



For lunch and dinner, guests will find a wider variety of dishes: several types of soups, chicken, liver, tuna and other fish, beef, ducklings, potatoes, pasta, stewed vegetables, national dishes (couscous and others), salads, sliced ‚Äã‚Äãvegetables, olives, pizza. Day and evening drinks are served only in the bar. This is wine, beer, sprite, fanta, cola. All alcohol is locally produced, but of normal quality. Tunisian wines are good, you can choose white, pink or red. The bar also serves cocktails, which are usually prepared based on local Bukh vodka.

The main restaurant also serves fruit: watermelons, melons, peaches.

Hotel infrastructure

Hotel "President" (Tunisia) has a good infrastructure. A currency exchange office operates on its territory. More precisely, money for local dinars can be exchanged at the reception, but if you want to transfer the balances at the end of the vacation again to dollars or euros, then this can only be done at the airport, so knowledgeable tourists recommend changing only small amounts. On the territory of the President Hotel 3 * (Tunisia) there is a mini market, a souvenir shop, a jewelry store, a boutique with all kinds of small things for tourists.



The hotel has free parking for cars, you can rent a car. Reception is open 24 hours a day. The hotel can pre-book a shuttle service. President Hotel (Tunisia) does not accept pets. There is a left-luggage office and a laundry room, and a conference room is provided for business guests. Room safes are available for a fee.

Sports and Entertainment

Hotel Le President Aquapark 3 * has three pools, one of which is designed for children. There are water slides. Opposite the complex is a water park that can be visited by hotel residents. For each tourist, two free visits are provided. Animators conduct daily water aerobics classes in the outdoor pool. In addition, tourists can go windsurfing, play water polo and go water skiing.

Hotel le President Aquapark 3 * has its own spa, where you can enjoy massages and other wellness treatments. The hotel has a hairdresser and a beauty salon. The hotel has daytime and evening animation, which is organized by a team of young people. In the evenings, there is a night club, in which not only guests of the complex are invited, but also local residents, among whom this institution is very popular. Tourists can play darts, futsal, billiards, volleyball, table tennis, mini-golf, visit the games room, tennis court.



On request at the reception, horse riding and camel rides can be arranged for a fee. Near the hotel there is a golf club, which can be visited by fans of this game.

Rest for children

le President Aquapark 3 * (Tunisia) has a separate pool for children. There is a mini club on site for the youngest guests. There is also a playground. The animation team entertains not only adult tourists, but also kids. In the daytime, sports events are organized for the children, and in the evenings a fun mini-disco awaits them. Active animation entertainment is held only during the high season.

Beach complex

le President Aquapark 3 * (Tunisia, Hammamet) was built on the coast itself, which is its main advantage. The distance from the hull to the beach is not more than one hundred meters. To get to the shore, you must cross the road along the sea. But as for the inhabitants of the bungalow, they will have to first go through the territory before they get ashore. It all depends on the location of the buildings. Bungalows are scattered throughout.



The hotel's beach has a fine sand cover, the entry into the sea is gentle and comfortable, you can even say shallow, which is very convenient for kids. There are umbrellas and sun loungers on the shore. In high season, there is a bar serving refreshing drinks.

A bit about the resort

Hammamet, as a resort, is known for its beautiful beaches and the best centers of thalassotherapy. He is considered the most respectable among others in Tunisia. Tourists love the resort because of the opportunity to simultaneously enjoy a beach holiday, get a course of anti-aging treatments and have fun.

The resort area extends for 14 kilometers along the sea coast. Hammamet is conditionally divided into two parts: Hammamet Sud (southern region) and Yasmine-Hammamet (tourist new district). The ancient tourist area is incredibly popular and brings a considerable income to the country. Hammamet was founded more than five centuries ago. Since ancient times, the resort is popular among people. And now there are many different hotels built here, most of which have a four- or five-star level. There are also three-star complexes. The resort has an excellent infrastructure that attracts many guests.

Hammamet is considered the most picturesque and green resort area of ‚Äã‚ÄãTunisia.

The port with the beautiful name Marina is the largest in northern Africa. It is here that the new district of the Yasmine-Hammamet resort originates, which is built in the old style.



Tourists will certainly be interested in wandering around George Sebastian‚Äôs house and garden. The mansion is built surrounded by cypress trees. In the garden there is an amphitheater with modern equipment, in which the International Art Festival is held annually.

Although Hammamet is a more conservative resort than Sousse, it is simply impossible to get bored here. Fans of nightclubs will appreciate the disco area, located ten minutes from Yasmin.
