How to distinguish "iPhone 7" original from a fake: possible ways and useful tips

More recently, defining a fake was quite simple. Only the appearance of the antenna and the shape of the body betrayed a non-original mobile device. Today's fakes are very similar to real smartphones. Therefore, many are interested in how to distinguish the original "iPhone 7" from a fake and not get caught by scammers.

general information

In recent years, scammers have increasingly begun to sell fakes under the guise of real iPhone 7. In this connection, many are interested in how to distinguish the original iPhone 7 from a fake. The non-original mobile device has a similar design and a poor-quality metal case. In more detail about the main differences between the devices - in this article.

What to look for?

There is no official Apple App Store on the fakes menu. If the user previously owned the iPhone, then distinguishing a non-original mobile device will not be difficult. You can turn to knowledgeable friends or to any store where iPhones are on display. The original design and fakes are practically no different, but the content of the "insides" is completely different. When buying a mobile device, the buyer must receive a check. Such a document is proof that the iPhone was not stolen or obtained in another illegal way.

Fake mobile devices have a small step between the case and the screen. It also issues refurbished telephones with the help of aftermarket components. It is also worth paying attention to the thickness of the protective glass on the screen. A fake device is almost twice as thick as the original, and the edges of the device are hemispheres. Fake devices do not have an oleophobic coating, so spots and finger stains appear on the screen after a short use. Original Apple mobile devices are not equipped with an additional memory card slot. Technological features of branded gadgets do not imply the use of two SIM cards.

How to distinguish the original "iPhone 7" from a fake number

The most reliable way to distinguish a fake from the original device is to analyze the serial number. To do this, just select the "General" item in the "Settings" section and select the "About device" inscription. This section contains information about the serial number of the mobile device. This number should be entered on the official Apple website. The user will be provided with information about the warranty period and other data. If the serial number is not recognized, then the device is a fake.

Other definition methods

Consider how else you can distinguish the original device from a fake. You need to be patient and check all the functions in the mobile device. First of all, pay attention to the presence of scuffs and scratches. If the smartphone case has a curved shape, then it is better to refuse such an acquisition. It is important to check the functionality of all connectors and buttons.

Immediately connect your mobile phone to the charger. This will verify the continuity of the charging process and the correctness of its operation. Then you should insert a SIM card and check the Internet. You can record several videos and check the microphone. To check the operation of Touch ID, just enter your fingerprint.

Buyer Tips

There is no single answer to the question of how to distinguish the original "iPhone 7" from a fake. There is only one way out: it is better to buy gadgets in company stores or from authorized dealers of the company.

How to visually identify a fake?

Many potential buyers are interested in how to distinguish the original "iPhone 7" from a fake in appearance. On the front side of the mobile device should be factory film with a tongue for peeling. The smartphone is sold in a cardboard box with a smooth, pleasant to the touch surface. The kit includes: charger, headphones and a white cable. Particular attention should be paid to wires. They should be soft, and there should be no sharp corners on the surface of the headphones. The mobile device has a non-removable back cover.

Fakes often indicate an incorrect brand or model name. The user can connect the mobile device to the PC on which iTunes is installed. During the connection of the original device, the system will detect it and automatically synchronize with it. In the event that the program does not provide information, the smartphone is a fake.

For clarity, the article presents photos of the original and fake "iPhone 7".

In addition, you should pay attention to the camera. In the original mobile device, the camera is protected from any external influences with the help of special glass, there are no gaps, irregularities. The fake is significantly different from the original camera, sometimes a sheet of paper can be squeezed between the lens and the rim of the camera.

Appreciate the design

Not one, even the highest quality fake, can exactly repeat the original design of the "iPhone". Chinese manufacturers have not yet been able to reproduce an exact copy of this device.

This statement applies not only to the housing of the mobile device, but also to technical capabilities. Fakes load programs and applications much longer.

Summary

The number of Chinese iPhone fakes is increasing every year. A few years ago to distinguish fake from the original was quite simple. With careful study, the original and fake iPhone 7 have many differences. Fakes are characterized by a poor-quality image, poor assembly and a curved case.
