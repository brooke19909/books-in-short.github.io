Hotel Linda Seaview Hotel 4 * (Hainan, China): description, services, photos and reviews

Hainan is a Chinese island located in tropical latitudes in the south of the country. It is called "eastern Hawaii." Today, without exaggeration, it is the most popular resort in the Middle Kingdom. By the way, in recent years its popularity has increased in the eyes of Russian tourists, especially those who live in the Trans-Urals. The tourism infrastructure is well developed here, there are many hotels of different categories, especially five-star ones. Of course, there is affordable housing. However, the best option for combining price and quality you will find in 4-star hotels, for example in Linda Seaview Hotel 4 * (China / Hainan / Dadonghai).



A bit about the island

The island is located in the South China Sea. Its coastline is 1,500 kilometers. Lush tropical forests grow in the southern and central parts. There are plantations of bananas, mangoes, tea and coffee, pineapples, and coconuts. Industry on the island, of course, is. However, here environmental standards are strictly observed and all production facilities are removed from the resorts by at least 100 km. There is also strict supervision over the discharge of sewage into the sea. He is simply forbidden. This is why coastal waters are incredibly clean. The island is characterized by a dynamically developing infrastructure of tourism, recreation and entertainment. The beaches are simply gorgeous, clean, well-maintained and well-groomed, and the sand is white and crisp.

How to get to Hainan to relax at the Linda Seaview Hotel 4 * (Hainan / China)

So, you are going to a Chinese island. Airplanes from different cities of Russia fly here. However, charter flights only from Moscow fly directly to Hanyang, and flights from other cities can be connected to one of the cities in China. You can also transfer to a train or bus after arriving at one of the Chinese airports and get to the island by land.



Dadonghai Resort

One of the best resorts on the island is Dadonghai. Its name is translated into Russian as the "Great East Sea." The aborigines called it that way, since a very wonderful picture opens up from the shore to an endless horizon. The resort is located just 4 kilometers from Sanya. It is here, on the second beach line, that the Linda Seaview Hotel 4 * (China / Sanya) is located. Mostly surf lovers come here. The beach fully meets the requirements of athletes - conquerors of waves. But he has one drawback: it is almost always crowded, because he was chosen for relaxation by the Chinese from different regions of the country. All hotels are located on the first and second lines. The aforementioned Linda Seaview Hotel 4 * (China / Hainan Island) is on the second line, nevertheless, it offers a magnificent view of the sea.
