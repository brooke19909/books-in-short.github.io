Vacuum cleaner PUPPYOO D 9002: owner reviews, specifications and features

In today's market you can find less and less models of vacuum cleaners with a bag for collecting garbage. They are gradually becoming a thing of the past. Modern vacuum cleaners are equipped with special technology - Cyclone. The article discusses the representative of such household appliances - a Puppyoo D-9002 vacuum cleaner, reviews and its analogues.

Description of the vacuum cleaner Puppyoo D-9002

The most popular vacuum cleaner models today are robots and models with the Cyclone system. The D-9002 vacuum cleaner is a powerful, modern household appliance that effectively performs all the functions assigned to it.

Consumers prefer bagless vacuum cleaners rather than robots. The second is much more expensive and have less power.

The main advantages of the Puppyoo vacuum cleaner include:

  • HEPA filter. It makes it possible to clean the room from the smallest particles of dust, allergens.
  • The cyclonic principle of operation. Now dust and dirt are collected in a special container due to the use of centrifugal force in the system.
  • Small dimensions and maneuverability. These characteristics are important for cleaning small rooms.
  • Convenience of garbage disposal. All dirt is collected in the container, after cleaning it is enough to simply shake it and wash it. Such a system minimizes the amount of dust, which makes cleaning a vacuum cleaner simple and convenient.

All vacuum cleaners of the Cyclone type are distinguished by their power, the volume of the garbage container, the number of nozzles and the cost.



Specifications

The vacuum cleaner of the D-9002 model does not differ in special characteristics from analogues. It contains all the features of a great home assistant.

The main characteristics of the Puppyoo D-9002 vacuum cleaner:

  • Power: nominal - 1500 watts, maximum - 1700 watts.
  • The volume of the garbage container is 2.5 liters.
  • The length of the cord is 5 meters.
  • Weight including nozzles - 5.9 kg.
  • Telescopic tube.
  • HEPA filtering.
  • Low noise thanks to triple noise canceling technology.
  • High suction power.

The hose of the vacuum cleaner is equipped with a 360 ¬∞ swivel mechanism, which makes it possible to work with a brush from the right side, restricts the hose from twisting and bending.

The cord is pulled to the required length, and reeled up by pressing the right button on the device. The button on the left is needed to turn on / off the power of the vacuum cleaner.

To adjust the power, use the center knob. And the button on top is needed to unfasten the container.



Cost and where to buy

Reviews of the price of the Puppyoo D-9002 vacuum cleaner say that the cost of the device is quite affordable. You can buy goods in online stores that will deliver the vacuum cleaner directly to your home and issue a guarantee, usually for 1 year.

Many make purchases on foreign sites. There is also available to order a vacuum cleaner in question. Its cost without a discount is about 20 thousand rubles. Delivery can be carried out both from China and from warehouses located in Russia. Suppliers promise fast delivery within a maximum of 7 business days from the date of order.

Despite the fact that the vacuum cleaner is manufactured by a Chinese company, customers will be able to be serviced at official service centers. It is also possible to return the goods within 15 days after purchase and forwarding.

Review of the Puppyoo D-9002 vacuum cleaner: what is included in the package

The vacuum cleaner is delivered in a box on which its image and name are printed. The kit has an instruction in English.

The box contains a disassembled vacuum cleaner:

  1. Instrument body. A garbage container is easily pulled out of it, on which a lid is located below for quick cleaning.
  2. Brushes for cleaning and a tool for storing nozzles on the pipe. There are 5 brushes in the set, which are designed for different types of cleaning: floors, carpets, hard-to-reach spots, pillows and mattresses, furniture corners.
  3. Hose. It is equipped with a locking mechanism that allows rotation around its axis. There is a valve on the handle that, when opened, reduces the suction force.
  4. Trumpet. The telescopic design makes it possible to adjust the height of the pipe for any height, which facilitates the cleaning process. Length varies from 49 to 81 cm.
  5. Filter. The set includes two washable filters, the bottom of which is made of foam rubber, and the top is made of material similar to sintepon. The filter is located above the container and can be replaced if damaged.

Also included is a warranty, which is issued for 1 year. A pleasant addition to buyers consider a small brush in the kit, which is designed to clean nozzles.



Nozzles included

Reviews of the Puppyoo D-9002 vacuum cleaner indicate that the complete set of the household appliance is quite multifunctional. It meets all cleaning needs.

The following brushes are supplied with the vacuum cleaner:

  1. Nozzles for hard coatings. Such a brush, thanks to an edge height of 25 mm, can crawl under any furniture and clean the necessary space. Feature is a special mechanism that allows the nozzle to rotate 90 ¬∞ in both directions and up.
  2. Nozzle for cleaning carpet and carpet. The manufacturer describes it as a "cyclone brush", but it is not a turbo nozzle. Feature: it has 24 holes on the bottom and the same on top, 8 slots in the front, which suck in air from the room. This creates the cyclone effect inside the brush.
  3. Anti-mite nozzle. It is considered the most unusual in picking. Designed for cleaning only pillows and mattresses. Thanks to its own built-in filtration system, ticks do not get into the hose and the garbage container. The internal two-stage filter is removable and can be cleaned.
  4. Crevice nozzle. Designed for cleaning dirt in hard to reach places.
  5. Round nozzle that allows you to effectively clean the corners of furniture.

In reviews of the Puppyoo D-9002 vacuum cleaner, customers find it convenient to use a special mount that allows you to store two nozzles on the vacuum cleaner pipe. Such a device facilitates the change of brushes and speeds up the process of cleaning hard-to-reach spots.

Positive reviews

In reviews of the Puppyoo D-9002 vacuum cleaner, price is the most often compared characteristic. Buyers note that the device can be purchased "practically for nothing" if you make purchases during discounts.

Other advantages of a vacuum cleaner are:

  • Compact size and light weight. The device is easy to use even in small rooms to a person with any height and physique.
  • Ease of cleaning. A convenient container allows you to easily dispose of all collected garbage.
  • Multiple nozzles. Brushes that allow cleaning in any corner of the apartment or house.
  • HEPA filtering system. Modern filters that effectively purify the air of harmful substances, allergens and bacteria.
  • The ability to replace filters. Buyers like that all filters in the vacuum cleaner can be replaced with new ones, which can be bought on the official website for a small price.

We can say that reviews about this vacuum cleaner are mostly positive. Buyers are satisfied with the purchased product and can recommend it to their friends and acquaintances.



Negative reviews

Despite the many positive reviews about the Puppyoo D-9002 vacuum cleaner, there are several negative points that arise during the use of the device.

  • Noise level. Although the manufacturer assures that the vacuum cleaner uses a three-stage noise reduction system, buyers believe that the device works the same way as its counterparts. Some consumers made noise measurements with a special UNI-T UT353 instrument. The experiment showed that when operating at maximum power, the noise level is 77 dBa, and at minimum - 72.5 dBa. Evaluation of other models of the same power showed that the noise level varied within 76 dBa.
  • Although the container is quite spacious - 2.5 liters, some buyers, especially pet owners, believe that it was worth buying a model with a more spacious waste bin. Sometimes you have to clean it twice for cleaning.

Negative reviews do not stop people from buying a vacuum cleaner. They believe that, despite the shortcomings, this is a worthy model for their money.

Vacuum cleaner Puppyoo WP968

An analogue of the D-9002 model on the market is the powerful Puppyoo WP968 vacuum cleaner. This model is similar to that considered for the length of the cord, the volume of the dust bag and weight. But it has a lower rated and maximum power.

The vacuum cleaner has 5 nozzles in a set, namely: for removing mites, a combined brush, a brush for animals, brushes universal and for hard surfaces. The latter is equipped with a strip of horse hair, which ensures maximum cleaning of the floor.

The difference from the Puppyoo D-9002 is the inability to control the power level. Customers also complain about the long delivery. Some are not satisfied with the brightness of the model, but a vacuum cleaner is sold in only one color - turquoise.

The cost of a vacuum cleaner is about 18 thousand rubles.



Vacuum cleaner Puppyoo V M611

Many buyers are eager to follow the technology and value the purchase of robotic vacuum cleaners instead of standard models.

The Puppyoo V M611 robot vacuum cleaner is designed for cleaning hard surfaces - linoleum, laminate, parquet - from small debris and dust. The device is equipped with rotating brushes that trap debris and direct it to the dust suction compartment.

The model differs in very small dimensions, weight and the ability to wirelessly work.

Buyers consider the absence of a contact bumper to be positive; it has been replaced with a soft safety bezel. Such an improvement allows the vacuum cleaner not to hit the walls, but to gently bypass them.

The suction power of such a device is 15 watts. And the noise level reaches 60 dB. Power is supplied by a 2200 mAh battery. The device is able to work without interruption up to 2 hours, and the charging time is 6 hours.

You can buy a vacuum cleaner at a cost of about 30 thousand rubles. excluding discounts.



Vacuum cleaner Puppyoo WP9005b

Reviews on the Puppyoo WP9005b vacuum cleaner indicate that this model justifies the declared characteristics. It has significant power, which allows you to quickly clean the room. Disposal of waste occurs quickly and without problems - dirt does not collect in the container.

There are 3 brushes in the kit: regular, round and crevice nozzle. Buyers believe that this is not enough to effectively clean the room, especially from animal hair.

Especially consumers are pleased with the cost of the vacuum cleaner, which without discounts is about 18 thousand rubles.



Summary

The article presented an overview of the Puppyoo D-9002 vacuum cleaner from China, as well as its analogues. It is worth noting that this model is most impressed by the buyer in this price segment. The vacuum cleaner is powerful enough, and its complete set provides effective cleaning of the entire room.

The dimensions of the vacuum cleaner allow you to store it anywhere, and its low weight does not cause discomfort when used. Buyers note that a nice addition is the optional filter in the kit and a brush for cleaning nozzles. Delivery of goods is fast and a guarantee is provided.

Some buyers note a rather high noise level of the vacuum cleaner, although the manufacturer claims to have noise reduction. Also, some are not satisfied with the instruction in English.

Buyers believe that the D-9002 is a quality product, albeit a Chinese manufacturer. The cost of a vacuum cleaner pleases consumers, the opportunity to purchase it at a discount is especially pleasant.
