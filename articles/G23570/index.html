Vest for the boy with knitting needles. Description and recommendations.

Many mothers on parental leave begin to look for something to do that will not only be fun, but also beneficial. So, many women begin to learn knitting. It should be said that knitted things not only look very beautiful and original, they also save the family budget. Any item knitted with needles can be neatly loosened and tied into a slightly larger size. This article will tell you how to knit a vest for a boy. Even beginner needlewomen can cope with this task.

Start: tool selection

Before you start knitting a vest with knitting needles for a boy, it is worth deciding on the material of the threads and the thickness of the knitting needles. Many experienced knitters prefer to work on circular knitting needles, fastened together by fishing line or wire. Such a tool makes the work malleable and does not inadvertently slip the loops from the tool.

It is worth recalling that the thickness of the spokes should not be wider than the selected thread. Otherwise, the work will turn out to be sloppy and careless. After you have decided on the tools for work, dial 10 loops on the knitting needles and knit a few centimeters. You will receive a so-called probe. With it, you can calculate how many loops per one centimeter of knitting.





Loop set

Measure your baby‚Äôs waist and hips. You will need the last value if you are going to knit a long vest for the boy with knitting needles. The scheme for calculating the number of loops is quite simple. You need to multiply the waist circumference by the number of loops that is present in one centimeter of trial work. The resulting number must be divided into two, as the back will be connected separately in front of the vest.





Back

Type the obtained number of loops on the knitting needle and start knitting. Tie 10 centimeters with a double elastic band , and then proceed to knitting a direct fabric. It is better to knit a vest for a boy with knitting without patterns, with the usual stitch, especially if this is your first job.

Knit 20 centimeters, knitting the front side with the front loops, and the wrong side with the wrong ones. In the extreme row on the front side, reduce 7 loops from the edges of the work. This is necessary for subsequent knitting of the sleeves. Knit another 20 centimeters and start closing the loops. It is worth recalling that this work does not endure haste. It is necessary to act carefully, without tightening the thread.

Before

A vest for a boy with knitting needles involves separately knitting the front part. Similar to working with the back, dial the required number of loops on the knitting needles and knit with an elastic band 10 centimeters. After that, do the main part of the work with front stitch. At a height of 40 centimeters from the start of work, close the middle loops to create a cutout. Next, knit the left and right part in turn. When it is tied, close the loops without applying any effort or tightening the thread.





Stitching

In order for your workpiece to acquire a meaningful look, you need to sew the back in front of the side and shoulder seams. They must be done from the wrong side for a more accurate look. Now on sale you can find special needles for knitting yarn. They will help to do this part of the work especially carefully.

There are many options for assembling, but for beginner needlewomen it is better to stop on the simplest method. Alternately grip the loops on the connected parts with a needle and sew them together.

Creating strips

This vest for a boy with knitting needles can be connected both with sewing and with a simple set of new loops.

Sewing on planks

Calculate the circumference of the cutout and dial the required number of loops on circular knitting needles. Knit with a rubber band 5 centimeters and close the loops. Similarly tie the straps for the sleeves. Sew the details, turning the work inside out.

Knotting Planks

Your work will look neater if you knit planks from an already created canvas. To do this, dial the required number of loops directly from the edge of the neck and with the help of circular or toe knitting needles, knit 5 centimeters of elastic. Close the loops carefully so that the edge is free.

Similar to the cutout, dial the loops from the armhole of the sleeves and also tie 5 centimeters. Close the loops without tightening the thread.





Conclusion

Having carefully studied the step-by-step instructions, you can independently knit a vest for the boy with knitting needles. Perhaps for the first time you may have difficulty in working, so try to start with the simplest. Do not handle complex patterns and drawings. Better your work will be simple, but neatly and connected with love.

The heat invested in your labors will always be with your baby. Knit with pleasure!
