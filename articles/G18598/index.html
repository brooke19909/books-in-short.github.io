Santa Claus from beads - beautiful things with your own hands

New Year holidays are the most beloved among children and adults. This is a time of delicious food, fun companies, Christmas tree and gifts under it. Many puzzle over the choice of gifts for friends and relatives. You can go to any nearby supermarket and buy souvenirs, but they are all tired, and made without a soul. We suggest you make unusual gifts with your own hands.

There are many types of needlework: knitting, carving, embroidery and much more. Any needlework is a complex business requiring effort, attentiveness, responsibility and diligence. Weaving is one of the most interesting activities, mastering which you can learn how to create masterpieces.

Santa Claus from beads, Snow Maiden and New Year tree - we will tell you how to make these holiday souvenirs. For work you will need a thin fishing line, multi-colored beads and, most importantly, a good mood. The technique that we will use is called parallel weaving. With its help, you can make not only a souvenir Santa Claus volumetric from beads, but also many other gifts. You can use not only fishing line, but also thin wire, then the figure will be stable. If you prefer a thin fishing line, then the model of the souvenir "Santa Claus beadwork" is performed with the following sequence: the odd rows are woven on the front side of the image, the even rows are on the wrong side.

Volume souvenirs can be obtained using other equipment, the rows must not be folded, but laid out. On the Internet you can find a lot of options for models of what Santa Claus should look like from beads, on specialized sites various schemes of the front and back sides are presented separately, so weaving is much easier.

Try to make a souvenir Santa Claus from beads. Find a simple pattern and start weaving. Take a thin fishing line, a needle and beads of red, white, black and blue. You can use a wider range of colors if you want to make a bright souvenir. Santa Claus from beads weaves is difficult due to the fact that it is necessary to make small details.

It can be voluminous. To do this, collect eight beads, placing them approximately in the middle of the fishing line, then you need to thread four beads with a needle and fix the first row. Thus, you will get volume Santa Claus from beads.

Weaving a Christmas tree from beads. For beginners, a Christmas tree is the best option. It can be made colorful, while weaving is one of the easiest and does not take much time. The Christmas tree is created from the top of the head, which is best made not from ordinary beads, but from a bead in the form of a picture. Finding a Christmas tree scheme is simple, start with small models, then you can make a large Christmas tree and hang it on the wall, you can also put gifts under it.

Weave a snow maiden from beads . When you learn how to weave Santa Claus, you can easily master the weaving of his girlfriend, the Snow Maiden. She weaves the front side, there are a lot of patterns with various patterns and accents. The most difficult part in this process is the manufacture of parts such as crowns, braids, it is better to do them individually and attach them later.

Santa Claus, the Snow Maiden and the Christmas tree - not all New Year's souvenirs. You can master the weaving of snowmen, Christmas balls, other heroes. With beadwork you can create real works of art. This is not only useful, but also incredibly interesting. Give your hobby about an hour a day, and you can forget about worries and problems. Over time, you can learn how to make jewelry from beads, all friends will be happy to receive new beads or a bracelet as a gift. Beadwork is a good business. Hand-made souvenirs are valued expensively, they are always accepted with a bang. Try it and you may have talent.
