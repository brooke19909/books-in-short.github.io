The history of Sofia goes back thousands of years. The territory on which the capital of Bulgaria is located, even in the Neolithic era, was inhabited by primitive people. In the VII century. Sofia received the status of "capital of Bulgaria" only in 1879, after which it gradually began to change its appearance from the Ottoman to European. Fortunately, the Bulgarians are able to preserve historical memory.
