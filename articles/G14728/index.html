How to make a skirt out of a dress: instructions

Probably, every woman in the closet will find a tailored dress that is already tired, and it is a pity to throw it away. Well, you can make a new one out of such a thing. To do this, you do not have to be a jack of all trades. Just a little quick wit, as well as a small supply of knowledge in the field of sewing. So, how to make a skirt out of a dress?



What is required for this

Since making a skirt out of a dress at home is a simple matter, you can turn on imagination and create something original. First you need to prepare all the necessary tools and materials. To work you will need:

  1. Old dress.
  2. Sewing supplies.
  3. Pins
  4. Wide elastic.
  5. Scissors.
  6. Threads matching in color.
  7. Sewing machine.

To work with the material, all necessary measurements should be made. Since you need to carefully make a skirt out of a dress, you should accurately measure the length of the future product, as well as the waist circumference.

How to make a belt

To make a skirt out of a dress, you need to make a belt. Elastic gum is better to take a width of more than 5 centimeters. It‚Äôs worth cutting off all that is superfluous. The length of the elastic should match the waist. In addition, you need to add approximately 2.5 cm for the seam allowance.

The prepared segment must be folded in half, and then stitched along a narrow edge, while departing from the edge by 5 millimeters. That's all - the belt is ready.



How to make a skirt out of a dress

First of all, it is worth calculating the length of the future product. After the measurements, it is worth cutting off everything unnecessary from the dress. If the dress is 70 centimeters long and the skirt should be no more than 60, then 10 centimeters must be cut off. After that, it is worth tearing off the bodice. For this, you can use ordinary scissors. The result should be a rectangle of fabric.

How to make a skirt with an assembly from a dress? Everything is simple. The sewing machine needs to be moved to the longest step. After this, the top of the dress must be sewn immediately in several lines, leaving a fairly long thread behind it. At the end of the work, the skirt should be folded. To do this, pull the thread. The top of the skirt should fit in size to the previously prepared belt.

Final stage

Now it is necessary to sew a blank to the belt, since it is simply impossible to make a skirt out of a dress without elastic. To begin with, it is worth connecting the individual parts of the product with pins. After this, you need to sew the edge of the skirt to the belt using a sewing machine, making a zigzag seam.

In this case, it is necessary to ensure that the entire edge is captured. Otherwise, there is a risk of unraveling. Now it‚Äôs worth removing all the pins. The skirt from the old dress is ready.



In conclusion

Now you know how to make a skirt for a girl from a dress. Photos will help to better imagine what will turn out in the end. In a similar way, you can remake the dress for an adult woman. The main thing is that the style fits. If desired, the product belt can be decorated. To do this, beads, large beads and a bow made of satin ribbon are suitable. If the length of the skirt does not fit, then it can be made to the knee or even shorter.
