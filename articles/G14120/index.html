Porto Platanias Village Resort & Spa 4 * (Greece, Crete): description of rooms, service, reviews

What could be better than relaxing in a spa hotel on the beach? Hard to tell. After all, almost every second dreams about it. Well, this is not so unrealistic. If you want to plunge into luxury, but the budget is somewhat limited, then you can go to the hotel Porto Platanias Village Resort & Spa 4 *, which is located in Crete. Here can not help but like.





Service

Porto Platanias Village Resort & Spa 4 * has everything that should be in a hotel of this level. Here is a short list:

  • 24-hour reception desk.
  • Lobby.
  • Mini market and gift shop.
  • Private free parking.
  • Car rental
  • Currency exchange.
  • Dry cleaning, laundry, ironing.
  • Computer center.
  • Security Service.
  • Luggage storage.

And that, of course, is not all. But special attention should be paid to the list of services provided. These include transfers, newspapers, food and drinks delivery to apartments, VIP-service. There is also a special approach to guests with disabilities (there are special apartments for them), to newlyweds and those traveling with their families.

By the way, the hotel staff deserves special praise. After all, the employees working here speak not only Greek and English, but also German, French, Italian and Russian.





Leisure

No one, being in Porto Platanias Village Resort & Spa 4 *, will not be bored. Right in front of the hotel is a sandy beach with umbrellas and sun loungers, and there is a volleyball court on it. On the territory you can play billiards or tennis, go to the gym.

There is also a SPA center. It has a heated indoor pool, a Turkish bath, a hydrotherapy room with a jacuzzi, a sauna, a contrast shower, an ice fountain, foot baths, and treatment rooms for both cosmetic programs and massages. There are also "suites" in which SPA-service is provided for couples.

And lovers of taking care of themselves will definitely like a beauty salon, where a wide range of services is offered - from manicure and styling to depilation.

Pools and water sports

This should be said separately. Of course, many go to Greece, to Chania, for a beach holiday. But if you do not want to go to the sea - no problem. You can spend time in one of the five pools located in the complex. Or go for a swim in a heated indoor pool. For children, by the way, there are separate pools.

Still, being in Greece, in Chania, you should definitely take time to engage in water sports. There is a professional center dedicated to them, which has all the necessary equipment and highly qualified instructors.

There are also diving, windsurfing, water skiing and sailing schools. If you are not interested in something like this, you can rent a mountain bike, go to the golf course, play soccer, or go horse riding.





Restaurants

There are 4 * five of them in the territory of Porto Platanias Village Resort & Spa. The following establishments:

  • Helios & Eros. This is the main restaurant, where guests can enjoy buffet breakfast, lunch and dinner.
  • Ikaros. This institution regularly hosts theme nights devoted to a particular national cuisine.
  • Island. This is an a-la carte restaurant, which is located near the pool. It offers a wide range of light dishes and a variety of snacks.
  • Vythos. Open in high season. The restaurant also hosts theme nights.
  • Iris. Hall for banquets.

It is important to mention that the restaurants have an evening dress code.

In addition to these institutions, the complex has five more bars. Hermes is located in the lobby, and Island and Atlantis are by the pool. You can find Kochili on the beach, and on the hotel territory, not far from the Ikaros theme restaurant, is the eponymous bar. However, no matter what institution the guest chooses for his pastime, he will be satisfied, as a huge assortment of cocktails and drinks is offered everywhere.
