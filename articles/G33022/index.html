Choose a vacation destination: Sharjah hotels with a private beach

Among the emirates of the UAE, Sharjah is the most original. It is located on the shores of two bays at once - the Persian and Omani. The location on the peninsula determines the enormous length of the beaches. In this third largest emirate of the country, vacation prices are significantly lower than in promoted Dubai. Even five-star hotels with a beach in Sharjah have a price per room lower than similar ones in the neighboring flagship of the tourism sector. But there are some nuances in this emirate. If the UAE has a dry law, then in Sharjah it is very severe. You can not drink alcohol not only on the streets, but also in restaurants and on the beach. The same applies to public morality - topless sunbathing is prohibited.





Access to the beaches

Sharjah is mainly chosen by family tourists who are indifferent to drunken parties. Here are excellent conditions for relaxing with a child. The hotel administration takes into account the wishes of guests to shop and organizes a regular transfer to malls and shopping centers in Dubai. Despite the long coastline, Sharjah hotels with their own beach are quite rare. Most hotels are urban. They offer their guests a free shuttle service to the city park, which has access to the municipal beach. There sunbeds and other ammunition are paid.



Hotels of the "second line", as a rule, have an agreement with their neighbors located on the shore for free admission and servicing of guests. Most popular hotels

Sharjah Grand is located on the very shore of the Persian Gulf. It provides all the conditions for a family holiday, including Russian-speaking nannies and a kids club. If you compare all the hotels of Sharjah with its own beach, Sharjah Grand Hotel 5 * is the largest and most comfortable. More than half of the rooms in it overlook the lagoon. This is one of the oldest hotels in the city. There are tennis courts, and themed weekly themed cuisines of different cuisines of the world serve in the restaurant.

Of the luxury hotels, it is impossible not to mention the Radisson Blue Resort. In the lobby of the hotel there is a living tropical garden with birds and fish. Another excellent complex is the Royal Beach Resort and Spa with five stars. No less popular are the cheaper Sharjah hotels with their own beach. These are the Quartet Lords Beach, Lu-Lou Resort, Carleton, or Coral Beach Resort. They have all the conditions for a quiet, calm and comfortable rest by the sea with the whole family.





Not all Sharjah hotels with a private beach can boast of budget accommodation. However, the Beach Hotel Sharjah is almost the only one awarded with three stars.
