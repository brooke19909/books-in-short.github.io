Among the obvious advantages can be noted the low cost of devices, long battery life and ease of management. It is also worth noting the distinctive operational features of gadgets in the cold. If, for example, a mobile phone with buttons and a large screen appears on the street at minus 40 degrees, then you can still call it. While sensory devices completely refuse to work in such a frost.

Of course, push-button devices lack the capabilities that smartphones have. But a considerable part of consumers prefers just them. A push-button telephone with a large screen for the elderly is becoming almost the best source of communication. Information is clearly visible on it, and mechanical keys are more understandable and convenient for the older generation.
