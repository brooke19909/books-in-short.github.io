Hotel Green Peace Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Alanya): reviews

Green Peace Hotel 3 * in Alanya positions itself as a place ideal for families with children. This is due to the fact that for the category of young travelers there is a wide range of conditions that will make the stay more comfortable for them and their parents.

general information

This vacation spot first opened its doors to tourists in 1992. Currently, it is a fairly large hotel with excellent views and a wonderful atmosphere inside. This result was achieved thanks to a complex of renovations, which are periodically carried out in different areas to maintain a general appearance.





At the moment, on the territory of Green Peace Hotel 3 * in 10,000 m2 there is a large seven-story building, as well as a considerable number of places for entertainment, the main among which is the aquazone.

Location

The hotel is located in one of the largest and busiest resort regions of Turkey - Alanya. Here, throughout the year, there is a rather high air temperature, and the water in the coastal part is warmed to the maximum.

9 kilometers from the location of the Green Peace Hotel 3 * is the lively city center of Alanya, where entertainment for every taste is concentrated: from small bars to large and noisy nightclubs. There is also a water park, which is loved by both adults and children.

Alanya Airport is 30 kilometers from the hotel, and Antalya Air Link is 130 km away.

Rooms

The Green Peace Hotel 3 * (Turkey) fund has 103 rooms, which are presented in one category. Each of them has been modernized, and new furniture and bright lighting have been used in the interior. The apartments offer magnificent views of the sea, mountains and gardens.

Each room has a modern air conditioning, with which you can arrange the most favorable temperature conditions, based on your preferences.

Guests staying here can use the plumbing available in the bathroom adjacent to each apartment. It has not only a place for swimming, but also a sink with a bathroom. Towels and bathrobes can be found in individual drawers. In addition, each tourist receives a set of soap supplies upon arrival.

Furniture in the rooms

All apartments of Green Peace Hotel 3 * have the same furniture set, in which the central place is occupied by a large double bed. Some of them have two single beds, between which stands a stand for a small amount of personal belongings. It has a landline telephone connected to an international communication line - its use is paid.





While in the apartment, tourists can store their clothes in a small wardrobe. There is a separate place for work - a desk and a chair. If necessary, it seems possible to install a safe, the cost of renting it is about 60 rubles. (1 dollar) per day.

Nutrition

Tourists can eat in a beautiful restaurant while staying at this Alanya hotel. At the Green Peace Hotel Alanya 3 * (Alanya), a daily buffet breakfast is served, which serves beautiful and incredibly delicious dishes prepared in the style of European and Turkish cuisine. The all-inclusive program also serves local drinks.





The hotel also has one bar, which is located near the pool. It offers the same menu every day, including drinks and bright cocktails. It also presents snacks and oriental sweets that will appeal to vacationers - they write about this in reviews of Green Peace Hotel 3 * (Alanya).

Services

The hotel guests are offered a wide range of services that can be used for a fee. Especially popular among them is the rental of vehicles, among which are cars and bicycles. A huge number of travelers in their reviews of Green Peace Hotel 3 * say that they took this opportunity to travel in the vicinity. Often, they recommend going to the central part of the village of Mahmutlar, as well as to the center of Alanya, where there are many entertainment places.

Conditions for children

Special attention from the staff is given to those guests who are on the hotel with children. For this category of guests, a number of conditions are provided that can make their stay more comfortable. In particular, reviews often highlight the possibility of free cots in the room. However, this is done only by prior request, as the number of beds of this type is limited.

In catering establishments, a special menu is provided for young guests, as well as special chairs.

If desired, parents have the opportunity to send their child to a small children's club, where his peers will be. Animators work here, who will not only look after the child, but also will entertain him with staged scenes, games and joint viewing of interesting programs and cartoons.

Aquazone

Guests of Green Peace Hotel 3 * (Turkey / Alanya / Mahmutlar) can visit the water zone at any time convenient for them, where an active and useful outdoor recreation under the Turkish sun is offered. It is represented by a large terrace, which is fully lined with sun loungers and huge folding umbrellas. In its very center is a swimming pool filled with fresh, crystal clear water. There is no automated heating system - the water is heated exclusively under the influence of the sun.





Within the aquazone there is a small bar where everyone can enjoy cocktails and snacks. Cold drinks and fruits are in great demand.

SPA

The spa center, which is located in the hotel building, offers the attention of tourists living in it, a wonderful range of thermal and relaxing services. In addition, there is a beauty salon, where several professionals work, able to carry out a number of procedures that affect the general condition of the skin of the body.





In the thermal part there is a sauna and a classic hammam, while in the massage room it is possible to carry out a number of relaxing and therapeutic procedures.

All services offered at the spa are paid and are provided exclusively to hotel guests.

Sport

All those who strive for an ideal figure and lead an active lifestyle, often visit sports fields that are on the open territory of the hotel. All equipment necessary for games can be taken absolutely free of charge in a special paragraph. Often guests play table tennis and beach volleyball.

A huge number of travelers prefer to engage in water sports, a section of which is located within the coastline. To engage in each of them, payment is required for the services of an instructor.

Beach

The hotel has its own beach area along the coastline of the Mediterranean Sea. It says a lot about the rest. Most often, tourists recommend taking with them special shoes, which are provided for entering the water. Such advice is due to the fact that the coastal part is not quite smooth, there are a considerable number of stones within it.

On a free basis, you can use the sun loungers and umbrellas, which are constantly installed on the beach. If desired, each vacationer has the opportunity to take on temporary use air mattresses and beach towels.

Some hotel guests also note that to enter the coastal recreation area you need to bypass the road, which can easily be done through the underpass.

Prices

As for the cost of the ticket, it is given in many reviews of Green Peace Hotel 3 * (Alanya) for 2017. According to vacationers, it is about 55-60 thousand rubles. This price is for a couple of guests who will stay in the apartments of this hotel for seven days. Many of them consider such a price to be quite acceptable, since in addition to renting a room, it implies all possible transportation costs associated with a round-trip flight, as well as a transfer.

Due to the optimal pricing policy in this hotel, combined with good service, many vacationers recommend it to their friends for a romantic vacation or travel with children. An important point is its good location in a quiet, secluded place, across the road from the beach.
