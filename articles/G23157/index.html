Hotel Dream World Aqua 5 * Resort & Spa Turkey: overview, description and reviews

Most tourists go to Turkey to relax in the popular tourist centers Antalya, Marmaris, Kemer, famous all over the world for their excellent service. We suggest you go to the relatively small, but no less famous town of Side, located 75 km from Antalya, to the newest Dream World Aqua 5 * hotel here.

Having arisen 300 years BC, Side has seen a lot in its long century. He had a chance to learn and a fabulous flowering, and complete collapse. Now it is a relatively calm resort town, in which gray-haired antiquity perfectly coexists with amazing modernity.

A vivid example of this is the Dream World Aqua hotel. It has been functioning a little over a year, so everything here sparkles with novelty and purity. Due to its stylish look, the hotel fits perfectly into the colorful view of the city. Designers and designers tried to create a real paradise for tourists here. Let's see what they did.

Tourists have no consensus on the convenience of the Dream World Aqua 5 * location. Side is located on a small peninsula, washed on three sides by the Mediterranean Sea. It is 75 km from the main regional city of Antalya, and only 15 km from the popular Manavgat among tourists. Many people call Side a city-museum, because literally every corner here is covered with legends and recalls the glorious deeds of bygone days.

Here in such a wonderful place in 2015, hospitably opened the doors of Dream World Aqua 5 * for tourists. Its guests can quickly and easily reach all the main attractions, the market, shopping centers, since they are less than 6 km away. From this side, everyone likes the location of the hotel. The nearest airport from Dream World Aqua 5 * is near Antalya. Flights from many Russian cities are carried out here. Rooms

The building of Dream World Aqua Hotel 5 * has 8 floors, which have 385 rooms. All of them in their design combine elements of modern and classical Turkish culture, which makes them unusually comfortable to stay and gives special sophistication. Along with this, the rooms are very bright, spacious and beautiful. Decor elements include large panels on the walls, unusual shaped lamps and art nouveau furniture. The feeling of spaciousness is added by panoramic windows overlooking the pool or the surrounding area.

The category of rooms is one - the standard, but they are one-room, designed for 2, maximum 3 people, and two-room family type, designed for 4, maximum 5 people. All the furniture, appliances and sanitary ware in the rooms are new, the sheets are snow-white, the towels are soft, of excellent quality. Change them daily.

Equipment: air conditioning (it works very quietly), a fridge with a small bar (there is water and drinks in it), a plasma TV with a large screen, a safe, internet (Wi-Fi), an electric kettle with dishes and tea and coffee sets, a telephone, fire detectors and smoke, plus a spacious balcony. The hygiene rooms have a shower, hairdryer, toilet bowl and washbasin. All cosmetics for showering are provided and replenished as needed.

Sea

Without exception, tourists rely on a beach holiday, buying tours to Dream World Aqua 5 * (Turkey). Side, Antalya and other resorts are famous for their wonderful coast and well-groomed beaches. To reach the sea coast, guests of the hotel in question will either have to walk about 20 minutes to it, or drive in free shuttles provided by the hotel in 2-3 minutes. They go quite often - every quarter of an hour, but only until 17-00. This is perhaps the only inconvenience. The beach itself is pretty good, the sand on it is clean, sunbeds and umbrellas are free and in sufficient quantity. There is a bar on the beach where guests can take drinks and snacks for free. The weather in Side is very warm, you can swim in the sea from the end of April until the end of October, and in the summer months the temperature of the water, especially in shallow water, warms up above +26 degrees.
