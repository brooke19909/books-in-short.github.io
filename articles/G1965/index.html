Tax on lottery winnings in Russia: what tax is levied on winnings

Many people regularly participate in different lotteries, hoping to get a big win. To do this, buy special lottery tickets or take part in various promotions conducted by large commercial organizations. But at the same time, all participants must remember the need to pay tax on lottery winnings. It can be paid by the management of the organization that holds the lottery, and the procedure can be implemented by the direct recipient of the prize.

What rate is applied?

The tax on lottery winnings in Russia depends on what amount is given to the winner. It is from this value that a certain fee is paid. It depends on the status of the recipient of money, since the rate is different for residents and non-residents.

The gain is represented by the additional income of the citizen, therefore, personal income tax is paid from him:

  • 13% is levied on people who are residents of the Russian Federation;
  • if the winner is represented by a non-resident, he will have to transfer to the state 30% of the amount received.

Additionally, always 35% is paid with winnings received as a result of participation in advertising games or various contests. In this case, the status of the recipient is not taken into account.

For example, if a Russian person buys a lottery ticket or takes part in gambling or paid contests, then upon receipt of any income he will pay only 13%. If he is a participant in a television show or quiz, then he will have to transfer to the state 35% of the amount received.

Bets are the same for commercial or state lotteries and contests. A high tax rate on lottery winnings of 35% is set for various promotional events held by various shopping centers or shops. With the help of such actions, organizations draw attention to their activities from a large number of potential buyers.

The concept of advertising lottery

It is a stimulating procedure that firms use to increase their income. Most often, such lotteries are presented in the following forms:

  • when making a purchase in the store, a check is issued with which a discount is offered for a future purchase;
  • raffles implemented using mobile phone numbers, moreover, such actions are usually implemented by mobile operators;
  • other tricks with which customers or customers are involved in any unusual game.

Participants in the advertising lottery do not lose any of their funds, but if they get a big win, then they need to get a special certificate from the organizer containing information about the cost of the item received, if it is not presented in cash. Additionally, you need to find out the TIN of the company that issued this prize. Only under such conditions can one correctly calculate and pay tax on lottery winnings.

Features of participation in foreign lotteries

Russian citizens can participate in various draws that are held in foreign countries. In this case, they will have to pay two taxes on profits. They transfer 13% of the amount to the budget of Russia, and also pay the fee stipulated in another country.

In addition, the content of international treaties signed between Russia and other states must be taken into account. In this case, different conditions may apply to avoid double taxation. What is the tax on lottery winnings paid in other countries? The following states can be cited as an example:

  • in Germany no taxes on winnings are levied;
  • in Italy, 6% is paid;
  • in Bulgaria, the state transfers 10% of the income received;
  • in the Czech Republic, the fee is 20%.

Therefore, before you take part in any lottery held in another state, you should decide in advance what amount you will have to pay in the form of tax. In addition, it should be remembered that the timing of payment of fees in other countries is significantly different from the terms used in Russia.

Can I not pay?

The tax on lottery winnings in Russia is considered significant, so many people who are winners of various contests think about using different methods to avoid taxation. It is advisable to transfer the money to the state, as otherwise the citizen will be held administratively liable.

The law clearly states that a fee is paid only with winnings that do not exceed 4 thousand rubles in size. Therefore, if a person receives less than 4 thousand rubles, then you will not have to transfer funds to the treasury. The tax on lottery winnings in Russia has remained unchanged since 2018, so in 2019 it can be 13 or 35%, depending on the type of lottery.

If employees of the Federal Tax Service reveal that a taxpayer has not paid the required amount to the budget, he will be held administratively liable. If income is concealed in excess of 300 thousand rubles, then criminal liability is possible.

When is it paid?

People who receive money as a result of participating in the lottery must understand not only what tax on lottery winnings is paid, but also when this process needs to be completed. Funds are transferred to the budget until July 15 of the year following the year in which the income was received.

For example, if a citizen received a win in 2018, then by July 15, 2019 he must correctly calculate and transfer 13 or 35% of this income.

How to report?

Each winner of various competitions must not only pay the income tax from the lottery winnings in time, but also record the appearance of this income. For this, a 3-personal income tax return is drawn up in a timely manner. It is transferred to the department of the Federal Tax Service at the place of registration, and it is important to provide documentation to this institution by April 30 of the year following the reporting year.

The declaration can be submitted to tax inspectors in the following ways:

  • personal transfer of the document to the employee of the corresponding department of the Federal Tax Service;
  • using the help of a representative, for which you will have to contact a notary in advance to draw up a certified power of attorney;
  • sending a document by mail;
  • the use of an electronic declaration, which is then sent to the Federal Tax Service through special communication channels.

Each taxpayer independently chooses the best method by which he submits a declaration to the Federal Tax Service. At the same time, you must submit a declaration to this institution before April 30 of the year following the reporting year. If there is no documentation in a timely manner in the tax service, then the citizen is held liable.

Winning tax can be transferred in two different ways:

  1. The organizer independently calculates and pays the fee. In this case, the responsibility for the lack of a monetary amount in the Federal Tax Service lies with the company. The winner will not be held accountable in any way. Therefore, he does not even need to know what is the tax amount on the lottery winnings. Punishment for violation

Any person participating in different lotteries must understand whether the lottery winnings are taxed. In this case, there will be no problems with the Federal Tax Service. If a citizen received a prize, then he will have to pay a tax, otherwise he will have to face unpleasant consequences. They are presented in the following forms:

  • if the 3-NDFL declaration is not submitted to the Federal Tax Service within the prescribed time, a penalty of 5% of the correctly calculated tax is paid, and such a penalty is charged for each month of delay;
  • the total amount of the penalty for the declaration cannot exceed 30% of the fee, but it cannot be less than 1 thousand rubles;
  • if at all the citizen avoids paying the fee, then a fine of 20% of the amount of the fee is charged;
  • if there is evidence of intentional evasion, the penalty is increased to 40%.

Additionally, for each day of delay employees of the Federal Tax Service accrue interest. To calculate the penalty, the refinancing rate determined by the Central Bank is taken into account. If a person wins a substantial prize, then the debt will be significant. If its size exceeds 300 thousand rubles, then imprisonment for up to one year may be used as punishment. An additional fine is paid from 100 to 300 thousand rubles.

Therefore, each person who received a prize must responsibly approach the process of paying tax on income received. Otherwise, it will have to face negative consequences.
