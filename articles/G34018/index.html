Hotel Sol Calas de Mallorca 4 * (Spain, Mallorca): description, services, reviews

The huge hotel complex Sol Calas de Mallorca 4 is located in the east of the island of Mallorca, near the village of Porto Cristo (municipality of Manacor). In this area there are a lot of small picturesque bays and secluded sandy beaches. The hotel complex is located on high cliffs. These cliffs are the natural border between the bays of Cala Domingos and Antena. The nearest town to the hotel is Manacor. The capital of the island and the main airport are about seventy kilometers away. To get to the hotel, you need an hour and a half.



Mallorca Bay Resort

This amazing place is located near Manacor and Alcudia. It is extremely picturesque. Dozens of small bays cut the coast, giving travelers the opportunity to enjoy amazing landscapes. The beaches are clean, the water is clear, the sand is golden. The area where the complex Sol Calas de Mallorca 4 * is located - Calas de Mallorca - is calm and pleasant. People come here to be alone with nature and enjoy the sea. Despite some remoteness, Calas de Mallorca is one of the largest and most popular tourist centers of the island.

Territory and what is nearby

Hotel Sol Calas de Mallorca 4 * consists of three multi-storey buildings, equipped with high-speed elevators. Thanks to the convenient location of the buildings, the windows offer stunning views. Each building has its own name. One of them is called "Chihuanuas Salt". It has twelve floors. The second is called Salt Martinez. It is nine-story. There is also a ten-story Balmoral. The hotel buildings are surrounded by a beautiful garden with Mediterranean vegetation. There are many different cozy corners, ladders, arbors and flower beds.

Each building has a special room for watching satellite television on huge screens. Since the hotel is located far from the villages, in the lap of nature, its territory has its own shops. We can say that this three-star hotel is a mini-town for relaxation.



Rooms and guest rooms

In this large resort Sol Calas de Mallorca 4 * as many as five hundred and twelve rooms. Most of the rooms for guests are in the ‚ÄúSol Chihuahuaas‚Äù building. These are two hundred and seventy-two rooms, nine of which are suites. Almost all of them have sea views. The Martinez also stands close to the surface of the water. But the "Balmoral" is located at some distance, by the pool.

Rooms in all buildings are serviced around the clock. Each room has a ceiling fan, terrace or balcony, a good bath or shower, TV with satellite channels and interesting programs. The rooms are air-conditioned only at Martinez. A safe and a refrigerator can be used at an additional cost - these services are not included in the price. The rooms are simple but very functional, and the linen and towels are clean. Hot water is always on any floor. The cleaning is good, regular.



Hotel facilities

At the Hotel Sol Calas de Mallorca 4 * twenty-four hours a day, an administration is hosted. The reception desk is open in the Chihuanuas building. An important hotel service is a car rental, which is difficult to do without if you want to see all the sights of the island, or at least a significant number of them.

At the hotel there is a good parking where you can leave the "iron horse". But if you do not have rights or you do not know how to drive a car, a hotel tour desk works for you. At the hotel you can exchange rubles for euros, put things in storage in a safe in the lobby, wash clothes. All kinds of conferences are often held at this hotel, because it can accommodate a lot of people. Therefore, there are several corresponding rooms with the necessary equipment and a business center.

People who are friends with sports really like this hotel. After all, she offers so many opportunities! These are two volleyball courts, and tennis courts. At the hotel you can not only play darts or table tennis, but also shoot from the bow. And how much is there for children! As many as four clubs for your offspring of different ages - up to four years, up to eight, up to thirteen and for older teens. Everywhere there are specialists, entertainments, afternoon and evening programs, classes and discos. As well as a huge outdoor playground with a variety of swings, devices for active pastime and much more.



Nutrition

Each of the buildings of the complex has its own restaurant, where only guests of this building can go. But tourists rate food at "five plus" in each of them. They are served here by the all inclusive system. Different types of meat (beef, pork, lamb, veal), poultry (chicken, turkey) fish and seafood (mussels, shrimps), fine cheeses, jamon, vegetables, fruits, sweets, ice cream. Lots of baking. Bars of all buildings for the services of hotel residents, regardless of their "registration". Wines, cocktails and spirits are very high quality. Tourists praise the local "champagne" - sparkling wine "Kava".



Where to sunbathe and swim

Any bay that is not far from the hotel - just go down from the cliff - will give you a beautiful sandy beach with clear water. The guests have several options - swim from the rocks and jump from the rocks, walk along the promenade to the shore of Antena Bay or on the steps to Cala Domingos. Get to the nearest beach no more than a quarter of an hour. You can go to distant bays along the shore and then go down, or along the sea. But it will take more time.

And in the Hotel Sol Calas de Mallorca 4 * (Mallorca) there are two air pools - in two buildings, as well as one pond in the room. Guests of any building can go swimming anywhere. They are also allowed to use sun loungers and umbrellas in all three buildings. The pool at Martinez is huge and deep. There is also a children's area. A fitness center with a sauna is open and you can relax and rejuvenate.



How to travel around the island

We have already mentioned how important car rental is to travel in Mallorca. After all, having a car, you can inspect the capital, and all the bays, and caves, and much more. Do not miss the fortress of the fifteenth century - Torre dels Enogistes, where the museum of archeology is now located inside the impressive walls. In addition to visiting nearby sights, experienced tourists are advised to go to Manacor, which is famous all over the world for the famous Majorca artificial pearls. Porto Cristo will conquer you with thousands of its yachts and coastal taverns. And the Dragon Cave, which is nearby, will surprise not only with its bright stalactites and stalagmites, but with a concert and boating on the underground lake. Do not forget about the old Alcudia, which survived the era of the reign of the Moors, the riot of pirates and the cruelty of the Inquisition.

Sol Calas de Mallorca 4 * complex: reviews

Tourists recommend this hotel to other guests. Guests enjoy the cleanliness of the rooms, comfortable beds, fantastic views of the sea and coves, large pools and varied animations, especially for children. Kids here feel happy, and at the same time they do not interfere with their parents' rest. Fine, well-prepared and varied food, just amazing for the Quartet.

Hotel management often invites dancers and singers to entertain the public, especially flamenco performers. Many people call vacation at this resort delightful. It is good to come here to lovers, families with children and travelers with a romantic character.
