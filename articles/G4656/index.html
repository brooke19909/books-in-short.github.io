On the ways to create holiday decorations: make a flat number

Flat jewelry is made faster, since it does not require gluing the frame. To create them, just cut a contour from a sheet of cardboard. In addition, much less flowers are used to decorate it . How to make a volumetric figure from napkins?

It is best to make a figure with which the birthday boy wants to play, rearrange from place to place. To create a three-dimensional figure, you will need more materials and time costs than for making a flat one. But the result will justify the effort spent - the volumetric figure is distinguished by a particularly spectacular appearance.





To create a volumetric figure, the craftsmen are advised to use two identical cardboard blanks. In addition, it is also necessary to cut long strips of cardboard with even edges (of the same width) for gluing the sidewalls. The frame is assembled using a masking tape or a heat gun.

How to make flowers from napkins?

This question is usually faced by those who decided to thoroughly learn how to make a number from napkins in order to please this little birthday with this decoration. Flowers from napkins are usually cut in unlimited quantities. For this:

  • a single-layer napkin is folded four times and divided into four squares;
  • the squares are laid in an even pile and fixed in the center with a stapler or a piece of thin wire;
  • then a square stack is cut so that a circle is obtained;
  • it remains only, not reaching the center, to cut through the edges of the created circle;
  • then they lift the napkin petals and straighten them.




This creates fluffy flowers, which can later be glued to the base using PVA glue. However, needlewomen offer many other options for creating napkin flowers. Some of them can be found later in the article.

The whole process of manufacturing this jewelry consists of three stages. Further, each of them is described in detail.





First we make the flowers

This information will be useful to anyone who wants to know how to make the number ‚Äú1‚Äù from napkins. This stage is very simple, but it takes a lot of time, since a fairly large number of flowers will be needed to create a number. If there is such an opportunity, it is better to involve assistants in this matter.

To create this option, the figures begin to work with the use of red two-layer napkins 33 cm in size. Two flowers are obtained from one such napkin. To make a flower, you should:

  • cut the napkin in half;
  • fold the halves again in half and cut again;
  • fold the two quarters together and fasten them in the middle with a stapler;
  • then a circle is cut out of the square. You can do this by eye;
  • further, starting from the top, each layer in turn compresses each layer;
  • then the flower is expanded, carefully spreading each petal.

To optimize the process, the craftswomen are advised to first make as many such blanks as possible, and then create beautiful flowers from them.

White flowers are also made according to the same principle. If you come across white napkins, which, unlike the red ones, are single-layer and smaller in size, some efforts will have to be made to create the same ones as the red ones. It is necessary to put together three white napkins, put a red square of the required size on top of them and cut a blank along its contour. Then everything is done according to the scheme described above. In total, for the designation of the number ‚Äú1‚Äù, it will be necessary to make 86 red and white colors - 65. This will take about four hours.





Frame

If you want to know how to make a figure out of napkins, you should learn how to make a cardboard frame for it. It is not particularly difficult if a flat figure is created. It will be more difficult for those who are interested in how to make a three-dimensional figure from napkins. For the construction of a three-dimensional frame, two sheets of corrugated cardboard 50 x 30 cm in size will be needed. The front parts of the ‚Äúunit‚Äù are cut out of them. The remaining trim will go to the sides of the digits.

Stages

  • On one of the sheets draw the outline of a figure. The width can be determined by focusing on the number of rows of flowers that need to be glued to it.   • Then cut out one. If the cardboard is very dense and hard, you can use a construction cutter.

  • Then make blanks for the side parts. To do this, measure and cut strips of 9 cm wide from cardboard. Experienced craftsmen advise making them as long as possible - so it will be more convenient to stick them.
