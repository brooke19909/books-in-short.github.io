Kuchugury: hotels for the best vacation

You can relax from the bustle of the city in the small village of Kuchugury. Hotels here are easy to find. For each guest in this place there is a suitable option where you can stay.

Guest houses

In the village of Kuchugury hotels and hotels are very different. Here you can find a cozy guest house or rent a room in one of the hotels. The choice depends on the taste preferences of vacationers.

Fans of warmth, comfort, home furnishings prefer guest houses. Usually they host an average of ten families. One of the most popular options are the following guest houses:



  1. Provence. It is located near Taman Station. It offers free Wi-Fi, a terrace and a barbecue area. For guests arriving in the village by private car, free parking is provided on site. Staying in this place is convenient for those who like to travel with pets. In Provence there are only eight rooms, which allows you to feel the home atmosphere and enjoy your vacation.
  2. "Beloved." This "romantic" guest house combines excellent quality of service, good living conditions and low cost. The rooms of the house have air conditioning, TVs, satellite TV, free parking.
  3. House Tishina. In this house, as well as in the "Beloved", there is air conditioning, parking. It has a sun terrace. Four rooms guarantee peace and quiet while relaxing.

In Kuchugury there are hotels and mini-hotels.





Hotels

Hotel "Lavender Azov" - a place where every guest is welcome. It has bright, comfortable rooms. In the Kuchugury village, hotels are pleasing with affordable prices, and Azov is no exception. There is a small restaurant where cooks prepare amazing seafood dishes.

The name of the hotel is not simple. Here, the scent of lavender soars in the air. Many tourists looking for hotels in Kuchugury, reviews leave positive comments about the hotel "Lavender Azov". They say that every time they feel the scent of lavender, they remember the sea and this unusual place.





Hotels

In the village of Kuchugury, hotels with a swimming pool can also be found. Among them, the hotel "Dauria", located half a kilometer from the sea and one of the best sandy beaches. The hotel is very comfortable, two-story. It has nineteen rooms with beautiful views.

The hotel has an outdoor pool, barbecue facilities. For visitors by car, a free parking place is provided.

In Dauria, the rooms are double and triple. There are two-room and four-bed rooms for family accommodation. Each room has a refrigerator, TV, bathroom and other conditions for a comfortable stay.

Hotel "Kuchugury" is always ready to receive guests of the village. It has thirty rooms with a shared kitchen, there is parking for cars. The nearest beach is a five minute walk.
