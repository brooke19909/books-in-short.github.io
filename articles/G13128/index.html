Popular hotels in Montenegro. Petrovac, Hotel Villa Oliva

Every year, more and more Russians choose Montenegro for holidays. This is a small and very beautiful country on the Adriatic coast. It has a mild climate, 300 kilometers of beaches, ski resorts, amazing nature, many ancient historical monuments, friendly residents. In Montenegro there are places suitable for outdoor enthusiasts, there are also many options for families with children. Many hotels in Montenegro are oriented towards a calm, family vacation . Petrovac is a small cozy city on the Budva Riviera, perfectly suitable for measured relaxation. The city lies surrounded by pine forests and olive groves, on two sides it is surrounded by stone cliffs with lush vegetation. Thanks to this, Petrovac formed its own mild microclimate, which is very useful for humans.

There are no problems with choosing a hotel. The entire resort consists of small hotels and villas, corresponding to various tastes and possibilities of those who come to rest. You can rent a luxury apartment or a whole villa for your family, or you can book a standard room in a regular hotel. Hotels and villas of this resort are high in the ranking representing the best hotels in Montenegro. Petrovac is famous for its small pebble beaches and clear sea. Some hotels have their own plots on the beach, where for guests all services are provided for free. One of these hotels is Villa Oliva. It is worth talking about in more detail.
