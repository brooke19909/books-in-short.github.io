Cheap hotels in Anapa by the sea with pool

Despite the fact that the beach season in the south of our country will not come soon, many people are already thinking about how and where they will spend their summer vacation. Often they opt for Anapa, which is not surprising. It is a beautiful resort town with a mild climate, beautiful nature and well-developed infrastructure.



And there are never problems with living here, especially in recent years, when modern hotels, guest houses, and inexpensive hotels in Anapa appeared. In this article we will introduce you to some of them, and below we will present a list of them:

Hotel "Agat"

Cheap hotels in Anapa by the sea are always popular, so we recommend that you book a room in advance. Today, almost all hotels provide this opportunity. Hotel "Agat" welcomes guests throughout the year. It is located on Pioneer Avenue , two kilometers from the city center . The complex is 350 meters from the well-equipped sandy beach. Near the complex is a dolphinarium, pump room, zoo and oceanarium.



On the territory, in a well-maintained recreation area, there is an outdoor pool, a children's playground and a specially prepared barbecue site. For tourists who arrive in the city on their transport, a parking space is provided. This service is paid separately. The hotel is located in a round-the-clock guarded area.

Rooms are 95 rooms. Each of them has satellite TV, air conditioning, a refrigerator, a fully equipped bathroom with the necessary toiletries. You can come to Agat with children of any age. A cot and high chair are provided for younger guests. Parents can leave their baby in the children's room, where an experienced and attentive teacher will deal with him.

The price of living is from 600 rubles (here and below we indicate the cost of living per person per day).

Hotel "Captain"

If you are interested in cheap hotels in Anapa by the sea, then we recommend that you pay attention to this hotel. Modern and original complex, has a favorable location. It is located on the central promenade, 75 meters from the coast.



From the windows of cozy and comfortable rooms offer magnificent views of the Black Sea. "Captain " is suitable for a relaxing family vacation and for a fun company of friends. The complex includes a hotel with 25 rooms, saunas, cafes, a beauty salon, a tattoo parlor and a gym.

Price - from 1900 rubles.

Hotel Olympus

Olympus is located in the city center, 200 meters from the beach. The four-story building was commissioned in 2005. It contains 32 single - double and triple rooms, furnished with modern furniture and equipped with necessary equipment. Every guest will find a rest here to their taste. The hospitable staff quickly resolves any emerging issues.

Price - from 1000 rubles.

Guest house "Patra"

Park Hotel

This wonderful hotel is located just a hundred meters from the sea coast. Guests will enjoy six categories of rooms, modernly decorated and equipped with modern technology.



You can choose the usual single "standard" or the presidential "suite". Room rates include breakfast, parking and a gym. In addition, you can relax here without parting with your pet, if its weight does not exceed three kilograms.

The price is 1600 rubles.

Cheap hotels in Anapa by the sea ("all inclusive"): "Fairy-3"

The hotel with such a simple name is located four kilometers from the train station, a seven -minute drive from the center of Anapa . Beautiful well-groomed area with shady alleys and comfortable benches, two swimming pools for adults and children, a children's room with toys and a playground - all this contributes to a great vacation.

Vacationers are offered to settle in five-story buildings, in which there are two-level junior suites or "standard" rooms. All-inclusive service. Meals - buffet. The hotel has its own, well-equipped sandy beach , a 10-minute walk from the hotel . On the territory there is a tour desk, massage room, guarded, open, paid parking.



It should be noted that even more rare are inexpensive hotels in Anapa by the sea with a swimming pool (all-inclusive). Holidays in them are more comfortable, so I want more such hotels to appear.
