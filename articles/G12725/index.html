Mackenzie Beach Hotel Apt 3 * (Cyprus, Larnaca): photos and reviews of tourists

Larnaca is one of the largest seaports with a developed infrastructure and excellent location. The port is the third largest in all of Cyprus. The population is about 80 thousand people. The name of the port has its own evolutionary history, for example, before it was known under the name Kition. It is this corner of the globe that can guarantee a wonderful vacation for every taste.

Features of rest in Larnaca

Everyone needs to visit this place in order to try in this life truly all the delights of a good rest. In order to make a family vacation really successful, wonderful conditions are created on the beaches, there are a large number of attractions and other entertainments. In addition to all this, you can make a variety of boat trips and excursions, dive to the seabed and get acquainted with the unknown underwater world. To broaden their horizons, city guests are invited to visit offsite events, during which you can learn a large number of incredible stories. It is on these excursions that one can observe a variety of old cities, ruined ancient temples and palaces - all this is incredibly beautiful and informative. The embankment is a crowded place. Here you can always drop in a cafe, have a tasty meal and chat in a pleasant company.

The city of Larnaca is famous for its history. Today, many tourists are interested in the presence of ancient architectural monuments. All the local diversity, of course, delights travelers. The most popular places of pilgrimage are: the church of St. Lazarus, the ruins of the ancient Cation, the monastery of Stavrovuni , the ancient settlement of Hirokitia.

The sea, which is located in the strip of the city, is not deep, so swimming will be very convenient. This factor is especially true for children, because they love to splash in the water. The beach line is wonderful, the beaches are sandy, so sunbathing is very convenient and comfortable. To rest brought only positive emotions, you need to take care of your stay in advance. A good option for a budget tourist is the Mackenzie Beach Hotel Apartments 3 * hotel. You will find a photo and description of the complex in this article.

general description





This is a modern hotel complex, which has a developed infrastructure and a good location on the beach. Each guest of the hotel can observe a wonderful view from the window and just enjoy the vacation. The Mediterranean coast is especially suitable for relaxation, here you can completely relax and enjoy the sea lagoon. Not far from the hotel is the famous beach called Mackenzie. This beach has been awarded the Blue Flag. This insignia indicates the perfect purity of sand and sea water. Within walking distance is another attraction of this area - a unique monument of church architecture, the Church of St. Lazarus. Nearby is the picturesque promenade of Finikoudes, along which a large number of various cafes and other establishments are located. Near the hotel Mackenzie Beach Hotel Apts 3 * (Larnaca) is the airport, you need to travel only 4 kilometers. This distance can be covered by a shuttle bus that runs regularly, or you can take a taxi.

Interior and exterior





The fund of hotel accommodation Mackenzie Beach Hotel Apt 3 * is 64 rooms of different categories. Each guest can choose for themselves the perfect option that suits the price and aesthetic preferences.

Each room has air conditioning, guests can set for themselves a perfectly comfortable temperature and fully enjoy the rest. In case of cool weather, each room has a heating system. There are separate bathrooms, a hairdryer, all the necessary bath accessories. For those who cannot tolerate tobacco smoke, special rooms are available. These apartments are especially comfortable for allergy sufferers.

Nutrition





On the territory of Mackenzie Beach Hotel Apts 3 * (Cyprus, Larnaca) there are several bars and restaurants that are worth a visit. Each guest can choose for themselves an individual food system. The cost depends on the category of room and board. It can be only breakfast, half board (breakfast and dinner, drinks for an additional fee), full board (breakfast, lunch and dinner, drinks for lunch and dinner are paid extra) or all-inclusive (local food and drinks are free) . Chefs mainly offer Mediterranean delicacies, and you can also try classic European dishes. For breakfast, there is a separate room where it will be most convenient to sit in the morning. In addition to restaurants and cafes, there are several bars on site. So, the Sky Bar Lounge Vanilla, as well as the Sea-breeze grill bar are presented to guests. These establishments will delight vacationers with wonderful light snacks from sea delicacies.
