LCD "Liner": reviews of developers

In Moscow today, a huge number of housing complexes are being built , each of which offers comfortable living conditions. Since the capital is a huge metropolis, the availability of developed infrastructure is a vital point. Today we want to consider the possibility of acquiring housing in LCD "Liner". It was commissioned in 2016. The complex is located in the Khoroshevo-Mnevniki district. Building address - Khodynskoe field.



Total information

This object gains particular popularity among Muscovites precisely because there are metro lines nearby. LCD "Liner" is located a 30-minute walk from the Dynamo metro station, 27 minutes from the Polezhaevskaya station, and from the Sokol station only 4 minutes by public transport. These are modern buildings of varying number of storeys. The difference is from 13 to 15 floors. To date, LCD "Liner" offers the buyer 1-, 2-, 3-, 4-room apartments. They give up without finishing, turnkey. Hire purchase and mortgage.

Additional Information

Obeying modern trends, developers are no longer laying down projects for detached houses. Today it is complexes with a closed courtyard. This is also the LCD "Liner". This is a residential building, built in the form of a quadrangle, with covered parking for 482 places. On the ground floor are offices and cafes.

The project was initially doomed to success, because it was developed by a world-famous architect Hadi Tehrani. The company Inteko CJSC was chosen as the developer. Experts started the construction of the complex in 2014, and on time the facility was ready for delivery. As already mentioned, parking is provided in the house, which means that there will never be a problem for motorists where to leave their vehicle.



The facility was built to ensure the necessary conditions for people with disabilities. From the parking lot of the complex to the apartments you can take the elevators, which greatly facilitates life in a number of situations. In addition, the parking is equipped with modern security and video surveillance systems.

A little bit about the developer

LCD "Liner" was entrusted to the development of the company CJSC "Inteko" on the basis of a serious competition. This is one of the leading Russian developers, which is recognized as an expert in the field of urban studies. The company specializes precisely in the integrated development of the territory for housing construction in Moscow and the regions. Her work is distinguished by an integrated approach. The company carries out a full cycle of work, from the creation of the project to the commissioning and subsequent sale. Moreover, each type of work is performed by professionals at the highest level.

What are the main advantages of Inteko CJSC? The company is constantly in search of innovative technologies and ideas, forms and methods. This allows you to implement projects of any complexity. There are many examples of their work in the city. These are residential complexes "Shuvalovsky", DOMINION, "Garden Quarters", university buildings.



Infrastructure

In the LCD "Liner" apartments are especially popular. Buyers are attracted by the ecology of the area, as well as the presence of all socially significant objects. Literally a few steps away is the Aviapark shopping center and a sports complex. This means that there will be a place for walking, and children do not have to travel far to training.

But what about work? Many are looking for her near her place of residence. This is justified in terms of saving time. 500 meters to the east is Industrial Area No. 7, where the Moscow Machine-Building Plant, warehouse and production facilities operate. South - industrial area number 5. There are car depots and cold storage facilities. Nearby is CHP-16.

There are several schools and kindergartens nearby, so there will be no problems with identifying children either. Reviews LCD "Liner" is described as a convenient complex for a comfortable stay. Easy to get to, nice to see, optimal living conditions. The Khodynskoye field is being built up comprehensively; infrastructure facilities are growing at the same time as houses.



Cultural rest

In the Khoroshevsky district there are several parks, all of them are located within walking distance. This is Birch Grove, Chapaevsky, Petrovsky. The plans - to arrange another park directly in front of the housing complex. As planned, an artificial river will flow through it. A street theater will allow you to spend the evening with benefit to the soul.

In Khoroshevsky district there are a variety of shops and educational institutions. Between the two buildings of the housing complex is a large shopping center Aviapark. There you will find a cinema and more than 50 cafes, hundreds of shops.



How to get there by car

Reviews about LCD "Liner" on Khodynka are very good. People are quite acquired housing and conditions created for them. Many note a convenient location - between the Leningradsky and Khoroshevsky highways. To any of them go no more than 10 minutes.

Let's follow one of the most popular routes in the city. From the metro station "Airport" you have to go along the street Viktorenko 550 meters. Then turn right into the Airport passage, left through Peschanaya Street and leave onto Mikoyan Street.



Cost of housing

It remains to find out the most important thing: how much are apartments or apartments in the Liner residential complex? Here you can buy an apartment of various sizes, ranging from one-room to four-room, respectively - from 29.0 to 137.5 square meters. Various conditions are offered. That is, the apartment can be purchased both without finishing and turnkey. And this includes not only all the necessary work, but also furniture. The cost of one square meter in this case is from 160 thousand rubles. You can buy an apartment not only at 100% payment, but also in a mortgage or installment plan.

The cost of finished apartments in rubles is approximately the following:

  • 1-room - from 5.5 to 7.4 million;
  • 2-bedroom - from 7.9 to 15.0 million;
  • 3-room - from 6.5 to 13.0 million




Instead of a conclusion

LCD "Liner" is characterized not only by its excellent location and good layout of apartments, but also by reasonably affordable prices, especially for the capital. The area is perfect for families with young children. It has everything you need. Green, enclosed courtyard, where strangers are not allowed, sports grounds, convenient access and parking. All apartments have modern finishes. They are spacious and bright, and panoramic windows overlook the parks.

Families with children emphasize the proximity of parks and schools, kindergartens and sports centers. If you are currently looking for housing, then be sure to pay attention to this housing complex. The developer is engaged in direct sales, which means that you will have all the necessary information. Experienced managers will submit documents and conduct a tour for you. In most reviews, the work of the builder is highly appreciated, buyers were satisfied with the service at the stage of the transaction, as well as with the quality of work that becomes clear during the stay in the new apartment.
