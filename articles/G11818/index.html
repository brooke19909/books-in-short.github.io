How to make a house for Barbie

Doll games are the most favorite pastime of most little girls. Elegant dresses and formal suits, beautiful hats and shoes - just a huge number of outfits for all occasions each pet has. Now with a huge selection of all kinds of play houses in children's stores, this is not at all a problem, but not every parent can afford such expensive toys. In this case, it is better to think about how to make a house for Barbie yourself. There are several ways to implement this venture.



First way

The simplest is a house from a cardboard box. Here you do not have to do any complicated calculations and manipulations. It is enough to glue the box over with colored cardboard or wallpaper, cut through the window opening, stick on the frame and, if the size of the box allows, a wall with doors. The next thing is to fill the space of the room with furniture. For comfort in a doll house, you can glue various paintings on the walls in frames from matches, a panorama of the kitchen or another room. By the way, the picture on the entire wall with a balcony or a huge window with a gorgeous view looks interesting. A little landlord can tell you how to make a house more comfortable for Barbie to play. From several cardboard boxes you can make a multi-story building, which will be much more interesting and attractive to look at.



Second way

A more complex option is a wooden or plywood frame. How to make a house for Barbie from plywood? Here you have to stock up with a jigsaw or a nail file, small cloves or a stapler, and also make some calculations. In fact, you need to make the same box with openings for windows. If desired, you can make a roof and a front panel on the canopies, so that the structure in the closed state does not differ from the real building. To understand how to make a house of plywood for Barbie, you need to remember the old mailboxes for parcels that were collected on wooden slats. They will become the connecting links of all the workpieces. Such a house will be much stronger than paper, and you can use aerosol paints to finish it.
