Natural objects

The Holy Nose Peninsula lies some distance from the Baikal Surf zone, but this does not prevent it from being a popular place for hiking. The place is famous for swamps that constantly emit gas. To the west, in the center of the lake, Ushkany islands rise - the rookery of the Baikal seal. Ushkany means "hare." This name was previously given to sea seals.

The archipelago is famous for its unique vegetation: Daurian larch, birch with black bark and many others. The territory is under protection, since 2011 only scientists have been allowed to be on it. You can see the famous seal from the deck of a cruise ship.



On the eastern shore, in relative proximity to the Baikal surf complex, is Spirit Lake. Fish is caught there perfectly, especially crucian carp, which is found in large numbers there. Hiking

A lot of hiking trails are located in the Hamar-Daban mountain range. The Baikal Surf camp site is located far from these places, so a walk can take a whole day, but it's worth it!
