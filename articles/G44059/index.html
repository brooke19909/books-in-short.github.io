Original DIY crafts for the garden

Each of us wants to live comfortably and beautifully. That is why we are trying to make every effort to create an original interior of a house or apartment.

And it‚Äôs much more pleasant to decorate your home not with purchased products, but with those that we can create ourselves. Crafts for the garden can be made from the most common, often junk materials. And the concept and general style depend only on your desires and capabilities.

Everything will go into business: from old tires to wooden pallets, from computer disks to broken dishes. Large-sized crafts for the garden - it can be tables and sunbeds, comfortable chairs from fancy stumps and mini-fountains. From wooden pallets you can make a whole set for relaxation. Alpine slides with miniature bridges and fountains can be created using large stones. Craftsmen also make interesting crafts for the garden from glass bottles. They can serve as "lampshades" for lamps or candles. And from old discs you can make a "bunny catcher ". Plastic bottles are often used as the basis for poufs and light seats.

Everyone probably saw such crafts for the garden, which are made from old tires. It can be miniature sculptures, flowerbeds, and even sandboxes. But even an old bicycle can be used as an original flower stand . Yes, and any other, for example, agricultural machinery, especially a hundred years ago.

Fans of carpentry can make crafts for decorating the garden of natural wood or plywood. For example, the original miniature well. It is not necessary that there be a well beneath it.

Such decor creates a unique atmosphere of comfort in the garden. Even the most ordinary stones or stumps can serve as a source of inspiration. It all depends on the taste of the owner. Some people prefer natural materials. And others, on the contrary, prefer garden crafts made by folk craftsmen, such as sculptures made of plaster or clay, original compositions of flower pots of various sizes.

Lighting plays a special role in your oasis of nature. You can, of course, use solar lamps that are becoming increasingly popular. But much more original and mysterious will look do-it-yourself fixtures. For them, you can take both ordinary electric and LED lamps. But candlesticks made of glass bottles or cans are the simplest garden crafts that can be done in just a few minutes. It is enough to attach a wire for which you can carry a "live light". If there is a desire and opportunity, you can decorate glass using various techniques. Interesting crafts for the garden using decoupage technique look especially cozy and original . With its help it is possible to decorate not only wooden objects, but also ceramic, and glass, and metal. However, when decorating furniture, it is always worth remembering that it is necessary to create such a coating that will be resistant to any weather conditions, including high humidity and temperature extremes.
