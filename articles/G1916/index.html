About hotel

Even at the first acquaintance, there is a noticeable design plan for arranging the territory and the Androthea Hotel Apartments itself. Guest reviews of this hotel indicate its aesthetics. They leave no doubt that worthy living conditions are really created for tourists here.

It looks nice from the front of the building, where a terrace, a swimming pool and, of course, a landscape design of its small territory precede the steps that are convenient for the entrance doors.

The hotel building is based on a fairly high base. With the help of this successful design move, hotel designers got the opportunity to experiment with shapes and volumes. At the same time, the hotel complex looks holistically.





The first floor of Androthea Hotel Apartments has one and a half ceiling height, which is justified for the equipment of common areas: reception, service. It is striking to those who enter the hotel that the interior design of the reception area is originally resolved using the multi-level design of the ceiling panels. An extremely simple geometric pattern is harmoniously repeated on it. (This technique is used by architects when they want to visually increase the volume.)

The color scheme of hotel common areas and apartments (after all, we are talking about an apart-hotel) is not bright, pastel warm tones.
