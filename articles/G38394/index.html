Beach

The hotel has a private sandy beach, 35 meters long, located on the 2nd coastline (120 meters), and a river flows nearby. There is a road between the hotel and the beach, there is an underpass. On the beach, guests can use free sun loungers, mattresses and umbrellas. You can use beach towels for a deposit of $ 10.

How do guests respond to the beach?

Vacationers note the presence on the beach of an excellent gentle entry into the sea. The beach is covered with fine sand, there are no stones, the territory is relatively clean. Many vacationers share that the beach is cramped, there are often not enough sun loungers for everyone, since tourists from a neighboring hotel also relax here. Hours to 9 free places are no longer to be found here, guests are sharing.

The sea is clean, but the river flowing in the vicinity of the beach, many vacationers call it dirty, although there are fish in it. Many guests call the sea simply fabulous. The water in it is very clean, warm and so transparent that the bottom is visible to the very buoys. The fish that swim here can bite, so experienced tourists recommend that tourists not stay in the water, but rather move around.

Vacationers are advised to solve the problem of shortage of sun loungers either by "capturing" the latter early in the morning, or simply, having bathed, go for a walk along the coastline along the bay. It is pleasant (it blows from all sides with a breeze, it is not so hot and the tan lies evenly), and it is good for health. During the walk, guests say, you can admire the crabs running fast on the sand. Crabs try to get back into the sea, but now and then the waves throw them back.
