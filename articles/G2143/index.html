We sew a toy

DIY Christmas bells are easy to make from the remnants of fabric that are in the closet of each needlewoman. The product can be double-sided and fully voluminous. For the first option, the pattern will simply be the shape of a bell; in the second case, you will need several details for the bottom, side surface and top. Templates can be developed independently. The sewing process itself is not particularly difficult:

  1. Details are stacked along the wrong side, leaving a hole for turning out.
  2. After this operation is done directly.
  3. The resulting form is filled with holofiber or other material.
  4. The hole is sutured.
  5. The souvenir is decorated.

Christmas bell made of beads

Such a souvenir can be both flat and voluminous. Usually do the second option. It looks much more spectacular. Both openwork and continuous weaving are used.



The simplest option is done like this:

  1. Make a triangle from the wire, first collecting, for example, 12 beads, and in the last row only 2 (it all depends on the number of rows and, accordingly, the size of the bell).
  2. Continue to execute on the same wire further the same form only in the opposite direction (mirror image), starting with 2 beads and ending with 12.
  3. Fold the resulting bow in half.
  4. Weave on the sides in one piece.

From several such blanks, combined into a single whole, a more voluminous bell will be obtained. Complex options, openwork and patterned, are woven in a circle using special patterns.

Crochet christmas bells

Such products can be, as well as beaded, solid or openwork. The latter look much more attractive. Knitting is performed in a circle - from the top of the bell, where the tongue and pendant are attached, down.



The shape is obtained due to the uniform distribution of additional loops and contractions along the ring. Openwork jewelry should be knitted according to a special pattern with a careful calculation of the pattern loops.

As you can see, there are many ways to make Christmas bells with your own hands. Even a child will cope with simple options. Sophisticated but beautiful will require the help of an adult. In a word, there are opportunities for creativity for everyone: for those who like to sew, knit, weave from beads, perform decoupage, stained glass or applications.
