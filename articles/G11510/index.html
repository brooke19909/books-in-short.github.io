Platinum plastic card of Sberbank of Russia

Sberbank of Russia offers plastic cards, which can be not only debit, but also credit. A plastic card of Sberbank of Russia "Platinum" is issued only for those banking customers who prefer an elite class of service, and also use the card on regular trips abroad. This offer provides a lot of additional features and additional services for owners of such cards.



What features does the plastic card of Sberbank of Russia have?

Sberbank of Russia offers the following options for cardholders:

- withdrawal of credit funds without commission and in large quantities;

- service in the Sberbank Premier zone without queues;

- all issues that appear with the cardholder, he can solve with his personal manager;

- all operations on the card can be performed remotely, using a mobile phone or the Internet;

- a plastic card "Visa" can be opened in any currency, the card can be used in 210 countries;

- holders of platinum cards can use the services of other organizations.



What is a Platinum plastic card, and what are its additional features?

  1. Insurance of each product that was purchased with a card for up to three months is absolutely free. If the product is stolen or damaged, insurance can be up to $ 1,500. For one year, the card holder is entitled to insurance up to 20 thousand dollars.
  2. Double the warranty period from manufacturers for any purchased goods on a card worth more than $ 50 twice. In total, the warranty should be no longer than 24 months.
  3. A plastic card of Sberbank of Russia gives the right to free medical services abroad provided by the International SOS organization.
  4. The Concierge service provides the possibility of booking train and air tickets, hotel rooms, tables in restaurants, seats in concert halls, etc.
  5. Use of free discount programs that are held by companies-
    partners of Sberbank of Russia.

What is a platinum plastic card? How to get it?

In order to issue a platinum card at Sberbank, you need to visit the office of Sberbank Premier, apply for a card and provide a passport. It is also required to fill out a specific form. The card can be obtained by any citizen of Russia who is between the ages of 21 and 65. The only requirement is the availability of mandatory registration in the region where the bank is located.

How is a Sberbank of Russia plastic card issued?

All about fees for servicing platinum cards can be found on the bank's website, in the support service section. It is worth saying that this card is issued in the same way as any other, credit or debit. A card is embossed, as well as a registered one, because a card with such a status cannot be instant. Therefore, it will have to wait a couple of weeks. Good luck getting a card with such a high status!
