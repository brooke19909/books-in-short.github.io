Eucalyptus Grove (boarding house), Abkhazia: reviews

Discover a village with truly pristine nature. This place is different even against the backdrop of the stunningly beautiful nature of Abkhazia, it is special, untouched, very quiet. The heat, which stands here in high season, is not felt at all; it is hidden by a eucalyptus grove. The boarding house, which lurks in its shadow, bears a similar name. Peace and bliss reign here. Despite the fact that the rooms are all full, it seems that there are very few people.

It is called very poetically - "Eucalyptus Grove." The boarding house is extremely well located near the village of Kyndyg, which is only 20 km from the capital (Sukhumi). Accommodation for tourists

Every year, hospitable Abkhazia opens its hospitable embrace. Eucalyptus Grove is a family-type guesthouse, where you can have a good rest with your children. The residential complex here is represented by very cozy two-story cottages, which are lined up along the seashore. Perhaps this type of residence contributes to a sense of privacy.

Tourists are offered cozy double, triple and quadruple rooms. Depending on the number of members of your family, you can definitely choose a decent option. The cost of living here, according to reviews, is reasonably moderate for the Black Sea coast. Depending on the season, you pay from 900 to 1300 rubles per person per day. At the same time, accommodation includes three meals a day, but we will talk about this in more detail below.
