Langepas is a young and harsh city. Where is the city of Langepas located?

Not every Russian is familiar with a young Russian city with a population of about 44,000 people. Where is the city of Langepas located? On the map of the Khanty-Mansi Autonomous Okrug you can see that it stands on the right bank of the Ob River, 430 km from Khanty-Mansiysk, and 930 km from Tyumen. His fate is connected with the start of gas and oil production in the Middle Ob region. It was born as a village of oil workers. The city appeared on the map of Russia on August 15, 1985. Its area is 5951 ha. Langepas is part of the galaxy of cities of LUKOIL.





Langepas flag

Represents a bilateral cloth of a rectangular form of azure color. From below, a green stripe passes through a thin white strip. The ratio of the height of the panel to the width is 1: 2. In the upper left corner is an image of a yellow (gold) squirrel, occupying a fifth of the length.

Climate

Langepas is a city that, by climatic conditions, can be equated to areas of the Far North. It has a sharply continental climate: very cold winters, moderately warm summers. The absolute temperature minimum is registered at -59 ¬∞ C.

Vegetation and wildlife

The city of Langepas, the photo of which you see in our article, is surrounded by mixed taiga, rich fauna and flora. Not far from the city you can meet a bear den. The forest is rich in fur-bearing animals: foxes, muskrats, chipmunks, squirrels - there are a great many of them. By the way, it is the squirrel that is the main symbol of the young city, even its name translates as "squirrel's land". In the swamps you can hunt ducks. In these places, fishing is well developed. More than other types of fish, roach, perch, pike, and muksun are widespread. In some large bodies of water in the Lokosov area, you can see white swans.



European city

The city was built on new projects, taking into account new technologies. It is distinguished from other small northern cities by wide streets, spacious squares. Residential and industrial areas were built taking into account the respect for nature.

Now Langepas includes eight microdistricts, which make up two residential areas. They are separated by the city community center. The city development plan provides for the construction of two more microdistricts in the near future.

Economy

Langepas is a city in which 454 enterprises of various forms of ownership operate, and the active development of medium and small businesses continues. The main share in the volume of industrial production belongs to the Langepasneftegaz TPP, which is part of LUKOIL LLC. Today, this company is developing nine oil fields.



The origin of tradition

An excellent tradition has appeared and has successfully taken root in the city: to celebrate City Day and the Oil Worker Day (a professional holiday for most workers in the city) by completing the construction of new architectural structures. Thus, the school and city stadiums, a water-sports complex, and a mini-football hall were included in the list of recent buildings in recent years. The construction of a modern hospital complex has been completed. Well-equipped medical facility consists of 10 departments. A new kindergarten has begun, which is attended by 280 children. The city of Langepas boasts the first exhibition center, a health center and the Cathedral Mosque. In parallel with social facilities, housing is also being built.

The medicine

To date, the city health network has two independent institutions - the Dental Clinic and the City Hospital, which are well equipped with modern equipment, which allows doctors to introduce the latest methods for the diagnosis and treatment of patients.

Education

Langepas is a city with six educational schools (including a gymnasium). There are all links in the educational chain: kindergartens, schools, professional and secondary technical links, and higher education.



Leisure of citizens

Langepas is a city where much attention is paid to the leisure of its inhabitants. There are 50 well-equipped sports fields and 10 leisure facilities. Each resident of the city can take an active part in public life - 68 public organizations are registered here.

The spiritual life of the townspeople does not stand still. Today, services are held here in the Orthodox Church of the Icon of the Mother of God and in the Muslim Cathedral Mosque.

Orthodox church

Built in 2001. September 7, 2001 the church was consecrated by the Archbishop of Tyumen and Tobolsk Dmitry. In the city of permanent parishioners, about 200 people. 60 people are Sunday school students. In February 2002, a sisterhood named after St. Xenia of Petersburg was created at the parish. Since 2007, the rector of the parish of the church is Priest Valery Basakin. Traditional faiths, such as Islam and Orthodoxy, make a great contribution to the preservation of the traditions of their religions, to the upbringing of the younger generation, and take an active part in the fight against drugs and their trafficking, tobacco smoking and alcoholism.



Langepas Attractions

Despite its youth, the city of Langepas has its own monuments and sights. Their main one is the Square for the memory of WWII soldiers with a memorial complex. Gray-haired veterans and first-graders come here in mournful days for our country to honor the memory of those who, at the cost of their lives, defended our right to life and freedom. People come here in the brightest days of their lives to lay flowers and thank all those who died for their saved lives.

In 2001, the president of the oil company Lukoil presented the city with a monument that was installed in the central square. It is made in the form of a lotus flower and symbolizes the bright future of this city.

Museum and Exhibition Center

It is curious to know that the history of this still very young museum began with the collection of an amateur ethnographer, who for many years had been collecting tools, clothing, and household items from the local population. Currently, more than 200 rare exhibits are stored in the museum. Most recently, the Young Ethnographer circle has opened here.
