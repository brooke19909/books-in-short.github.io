Oulu city, Finland: traveler reviews. Holidays in Finland

For many of us, vacation in Finland is still something exotic, out of the ordinary. Nevertheless, the most advanced tourists have long chosen this corner of the Finnish land, finding for themselves a lot of interesting, informative and fascinating. This article will focus on a city called Oulu (Finland).

Quick reference

The village is located in the center of the province of the same name, which is located in the north-west of the state, in the Gulf of Bothnia of the Baltic Sea. The city is the fifth largest land of Suomi. The city of Oulu was founded in 1605, and therefore it is rightfully considered the oldest in all of Northern Finland. The population is in the range of 140 thousand. Sex ratio: 51% are women, 49% are men.



Climatic conditions

Oulu is a subarctic climate zone, which, in turn, is characterized by sufficiently snowy and cold winters. Summer is warm, but not too long. The average annual temperature is -2.7 degrees Celsius. Over the year, 508 millimeters of rain fall in this tourist center. Moreover, the bulk of them occur in late summer and autumn.

Shopping

Special attention should be paid to the shops of Oulu (Finland). It should be noted that for the local population, trade is an essential part of life. As evidence of this fact, you can specify a monument to the Market Police officer, which is installed on the central trading floor of the city, immediately before the entrance to the shopping arcade. The monument has been mounted for a long time, and now it is not an ordinary statue, but a real symbol of the city. A policeman figurine as a souvenir can be bought on almost every corner, as it is very popular in Finland.



In Oulu, you can find branches of many large retail chains and shopping centers. So, in particular, in the city there was a place for two stores of the Citymarket network, where you can buy goods of various categories: household appliances, clothing, food and more. These stores operate from nine in the morning until nine in the evening from Monday to Friday. On Saturday and Sunday, their doors are open from 9.00 to 18.00.

For those people who value and value their time, the most ideal option is to visit a shopping center called Galleria. This hypermarket contains all the outlets that can attract the attention of buyers. Here you can buy branded clothes and shoes, various souvenirs, perfumes.

Tourists wishing to purchase home furnishings, superior home textiles, furniture or bedding should head to the Jysk store. At this outlet, a wide selection of these things and fairly reasonable prices.

In the very center of Oulu (Finland), Anttila store is located, which is extremely popular among all customers. You can buy here a wide variety of product groups.

If the goal is to buy high-quality equipment, then you should keep track of the Gigantti chain of stores.

Revolutionary low prices can be found in the German Lidl distribution network. Offered goods: coffee, tea, confectionery, clothing for everyone.

Motorists, anglers, lovers of extreme relaxation and hunting should look at the Motonet store.

For athletes and just active people, the doors of Intersport chain stores are open.

A wide range of building materials, special tools and equipment can be found in the K-Rauta hypermarket.

Zeppelin is located in the south of Oulu, a huge shopping center where more than a hundred of the most diverse boutiques have found their place.

As for the bars and pubs of the city, in them you can not only drink a lot of beers and have a meal, but also watch interesting football matches, listen to concerts of various rocker teams.



The city's attractions

In Oulu (Finland) you can not only go shopping, but also visit various places where a tourist can discover and learn something new.

The list of places worthy of attention will look like this:

  • Museum of Art.
  • Turkansaari is an open-air exhibition of wooden architecture.
  • Escurial is a botanical garden. Surprisingly, even tropical trees grow in it, which somehow miraculously survive in the harsh northern climate.
  • The northernmost symphony orchestra on the planet.
  • Holiday Club Oulu Eden is a modern water park, the complex of which includes a restaurant and a hotel.
  • Automobile Museum. It contains exhibits by which it is quite possible to trace the entire history of the automotive industry. The oldest exhibit in the collection is over a hundred years old.
  • Tietmaa is a research museum where all visitors can touch their exhibits with their hands. Any person here can feel themselves in the conditions of the Moon, test themselves as a tightrope walker, check how strong and hardy they are.
  • City Hall.
  • Cathedral.
