How to disable Megafon services and not make a mistake?

When connected to the Megafon network, the company offers its subscribers to try out some of their services. Of this, of course, there are various SMS notifications. But since relatively many of them arrive on the day of connection, most subscribers safely forget about it. As a result, after some time they begin to notice that funds are being debited from the account, and for which it is not clear. And, of course, in this case, customers turn to the help desk or Megafon offices.



Deactivation of services, of course, will be done at the first request of the subscriber. Only it will be necessary to present passport data. But if the passport is not with you, then the consultants will tell you in detail how to independently disconnect Megafon services. Of course, each of them has its own shutdown option, but there is a universal way. However, for starters, it is advisable to find out what services are still connected.

This is most conveniently done using the Service Guide service. The self-service system is connected by default, but to get the password you need to send an SMS with the text "00" to the number 000105. You can view the information of interest and disable the Megafon services in the sections "Options, Services and Tariffs", "Call Forwarding and Call Barring" and "Additional Services". In fact, without leaving your home, at the touch of a button, you can view available services, connect the necessary ones, and disconnect unnecessary ones.



True, sometimes it also happens that the disconnection of Megafon services is not connected with the desire to save. Most often, such requests are made by parents who want to protect their children from inappropriate content. This can be done, of course, with the help of the same Service Guide, when contacting the office or contact center. But you can do it yourself by simply sending an empty SMS to 0500914. If you need to protect your children from accidentally visiting adult-related websites, you can use the Children's Internet service. Moreover, the service is completely free, and you can connect using USSD * 522 #.

Most subscribers, even without understanding the situation, when mentioning a deducted monthly fee , are immediately asked to turn off Megafon's paid services. But this is not always justified. Many of them allow you to save on calls and SMS. Therefore, at first it is advisable to evaluate all the pros and cons of the connected options. You can ask this to do a consultant in the office or call center, or you can do it yourself. To do this, you need to order call details through the Service Guide for the last month. Using the data obtained, compare the cost of calls without a discount and with a discount and a monthly fee. If the subscriber ultimately remains in the black, then there is no point in disabling such a service.



Often, many subscribers after receiving advertising messages connect services, and then forget about it. As a result, it seems to them that money simply disappears from the account. But before being indignant, it is best to contact the operator for clarification. Surely all the missing money will be found, and you will not even need to disable the Megafon services. After all, most of them were invented specifically for the convenience of the company's customers.
