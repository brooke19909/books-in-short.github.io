A little about how to weave a wreath of flowers

Why are girls so drawn to weave wreaths of dandelions that have blossomed in early spring? Perhaps this is how the love of nature is transmitted from generation to generation. And strangely enough, even little girls themselves know how to weave a wreath of flowers of bright sunny hue. Maybe this is a manifestation on a subconscious or gene level of the true nature of the Russian person? Traditions, without a doubt, are of great and important importance in the life of any person. Russia has long been famous for its folk costumes. A mandatory element of a colorful outfit has always been a headdress - a wreath. The Ukrainian one looked the same, which was additionally decorated with long colored ribbons. This part of the wardrobe depended largely on the time of year and the holiday. For example, in the summer, on the longest night that preceded the famous holiday of Ivan Kupala, young girls always made wreaths of fresh flowers for themselves, and then allowed them to swim in the water. Want to make yourself the same or similar jewelry on your head? Then you need to know how to weave a wreath of flowers. This article provides useful tips and a way to make a colorful wardrobe item.

How to make a Ukrainian wreath of flowers with your own hands? Basic rules for selecting a composition

Head jewelry traditionally consisted of twelve different kinds of plants. We list them, specifying the natural properties.



  • Chamomile Symbolizes goodness, tenderness and health.
  • Yarrow . The presence of this unpretentious plant usually means invincibility and rebellion.
  • Hops . Chanted in many legends, symbolizing the mind.
  • Cornflower . This delicate flower personifies youth and beauty.
  • Immortelle, respectively, means health.
  • Lovage . It is considered a love potion.
  • Kalina . The first associations are associated with girlish beauty and purity.
  • Marigolds . The presence of this flower guarantees the black-browed young owner.

  • Periwinkle . It is considered a symbol of a strong family and eternal love.

A simple way to weave a wreath of flowers - yellow dandelions

The stems of plants must be long. Place the first flower horizontally. Attach a second to it at an angle of ninety degrees. Wrap the stem around the base, wrapping a loop that will press the flowers against each other. Attach the second stalk to the first. So attach all the flowers one at a time. The base will gradually thicken by adding new stems.
