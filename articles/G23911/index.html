What are the best beaches for Tuapse to relax

Tourists do not go to this region for entertainment. The locals do not pursue high European service. But the lack of water parks and fashionable five-star hotels more than pays for the amazing southern nature and wonderful coast. As you probably already guessed, we will talk about the Tuapse resort. A photo of the beach already makes you want to go there. The warm Black Sea, natural pebbles and sand, rocks that border the coast, like a turquoise silver rim ... Which of the relaxation areas is best to choose? Let's figure it out.

Almost on any coast of the Tuapse district (with the exception of the very inaccessible ones), you can meet trading stalls and small cafes. Between the two city beaches there is a port and a main pier. The main most visited recreation area within the city is located to the left of the mouth of the Tuapse River. The sand and pebble coating, the entrance is gentle, which is convenient for bathing young children. The beach is protected from the sea by the breakwaters. The infrastructure is very developed here: toilets, changing cabins, showers, lifeguards are on duty during the holiday season. Minus can be called unless crowded.



In the northwestern part of the city beyond the Spider River, there are other, calmer Tuapse beaches. One of them is ‚ÄúSeaside‚Äù. This is a pebble coast, sometimes even rocky, and the sea here has a significant slope. But in July or August you will not have the impression that people are lying like sprats in a bank. By the way, beach service is also present here: there is a rental of catamarans and boats, cabins, several cafes. But if you move on your own transport, you will have to leave the car two hundred meters from the coast in the parking lot.

An unpretentious audience that appreciates solitude, pristine nature and romance, chooses other beaches of Tuapse. First of all, this is Cape Kadosh, as well as the Kiselev rock. True, these coasts are already outside the city, closer to the village of Agoy. Since the coast is rocky, the beaches are rocky, although in some places small pebbles are also found. Due to the presence of reefs and cliffs plumbing into the sea, divers and fans of underwater fishing love this place. However, in the storm, swimming here is uncomfortable and even dangerous.



Tuapse's nudist beaches begin right after Cape Kadosh and continue to the Kiselev cliff. There are boulders here, behind which lovers of sunbathing naked find shelter. By the way, an episode of the film "Diamond Arm" was shot on this coast. There are also places for rest of nudists in the villages of Dzhubga, Dederka and Agoy. However, there is no official nudist beach in Tuapse. If you want to sunbathe topless or even naked, you have to agree to a minimum of amenities and lack of infrastructure.

Sandy beaches in Tuapse are located right behind the Vesna boarding house. Wide - up to 30 meters - coast, protected from excitement by the buns, convenient descent with the help of ladders. However, this paradise is somewhat spoiled by the nearby railway. In order not to hear the sounds of steam locomotives, it is enough to go a little away, there are fewer people, and, accordingly, garbage too. Also, a strip of golden sand lies in the village of Lermontovo (the beach "Golden Coast"), in Novomikhailovsky ("Eaglet") and Dzhubga.
