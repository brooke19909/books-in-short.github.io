Hotel Podostrog Hotel 3 * (Budva, Montenegro): overview, rooms and reviews

Budva is considered the most popular resort in Montenegro. Tourists can find everything here: night clubs with discotheques, restaurants, bars, sports and playgrounds, clean beaches and the warm sea. This seaside town is perfect for active and youth recreation. Budva is also an ancient city that has many historical attractions. If you are looking for a budget hotel, then pay your attention to Hotel Podostrog 3 * (Montenegro). Why choose this hotel?

general information

The hotel opened in 2003. It is a small building surrounded by a small garden. In its only building there are only 60 rooms, so it can be called a small complex. Most often, travelers who spend most of their time outside the hotel stay here. It is also used as a place for a one-night stay. Thrifty tourists also have a rest here. Hotel Podostrog 3 * (Montenegro) is perfect for lovers of a relaxing holiday. When checking into the room, the hotel staff does not charge a deposit. It is worth knowing that smoking in the hotel rooms is strictly prohibited.





Staying here with children, tourists can count on discounts. So, guests from 2 to 12 years pay only 30% of the total cost of living if they sleep in extra beds. If they are placed on the parents' bed, then the discount will be already 50%. As in other European hotels, check-in at the Podostrog Hotel 3 * ( Budva ) starts at 14:00, and ends at 20:00. Pets are strictly prohibited. All guests are charged daily insurance, the cost of which is 0.5 euros. You can pay for accommodation, meals and services with Visa and MasterCard credit cards, as well as in cash.

Location

Hotel Hotel Podostrog 3 * (Montenegro, Budva ) is located on a quiet street near the central part of the city. The distance to it is only 500 meters, so tourists can reach it on foot. Public transport is 200 meters away. From here you can go not only to another part of Budva , but also to other cities of the country. Opposite the hotel is another hotel, and there are many shops, cafes and restaurants nearby. The public beach is just a 5-minute walk away. The hotel and the coast are separated by a small pedestrian road. Distance to the sea is 350 meters.





Near the complex Podostrog Hotel 3 * there are many attractions. For example, the famous church of St. John is only 1.5 km away. Approximately the same amount will have to go through to explore the Budva medieval fortress of St. Mary, whose remains are located right on the coast. The famous Top Hill Club, which is a must-see for nightlife lovers, is 2 km from the hotel. You can get to the hotel from the nearest Tivat airport , 25 km away. There are many taxis and buses to Budva from there. In addition, when making a tour, tourists can pay a transfer to the hotel and back.

Types of rooms

The rooms of the Podostrog Hotel 3 * differ in a small area and simple furnishings. Each apartment has a combined bathroom, equipped with a shower, washbasin and toilet. Some of the rooms also have their own small balcony, which is not furnished. The cleaners clean the living rooms every day, change towels, replenish the bath set, report toilet paper. Bed linen is subject to change every 5 days.

Checking into the hotel, tourists can choose and pay for one of the following types of apartments:

  • Standart room. It consists of a small bedroom and a bathroom. Its area is only 12 square meters. m. Guests can choose between one double or two single beds. It offers views of the surrounding buildings and mountains.
  • Triple room. Suitable for a small family. It differs from previous apartments by the presence of an extra single bed. Its area is slightly larger - 16 square meters. m
  • Junior Suite In total, the hotel features one room from this category. Consists of a bedroom and a living room. There are also two balconies. Tiled flooring. It offers garden views. The area of Junior Suite is 26 square meters. m. An extra bed is a folding sofa.
  • Suite Its area is 16 square meters. m. Windows overlook the garden or parking. The bedroom has a double bed and a sofa bed. The hotel has 3 rooms in this category. Designed for 2-3 adults.




Apartment equipment

This three-star hotel is unlikely to boast of rich facilities. They are equipped with a minimum set of amenities that any guest may need. Each apartment has the following equipment:

  • A small TV of the old model. Cable TV is connected to it, including Russian-language channels.
  • Central air conditioning. It works in the high season, that is, from June to September. The cooling temperature is regulated by the hotel staff. In the cold season, the Hotel Podostrog 3 * hotel (Montenegro, Budva ) includes heating.
  • Direct telephone. You can only make calls to your home country for a fee. Communication with the administration, in contrast, is included in the price.
  • A hairdryer and a set of bath accessories in the bathroom. Guests are offered free shampoo, soap and shower gel.
  • Empty minibar. Drinks are added only for a fee.
  • Wireless Internet. It is provided free of charge, but only works on the first and second floor.




Service and Facilities

As mentioned above, it is precisely with its developed local infrastructure that Budva attracts tourists . Podostrog Hotel 3 * also prepared for its guests a wide selection of services and services that make rest in this place comfortable. We list the main ones:

  • Laundry. It is open daily from 08:00 to 03:00. For a fee, you can not only wash and iron clothes, but also clean shoes.
  • Private car parking. Guests can leave their car here for free after making a reservation.
  • Car rental. It is paid, while gasoline is not included in the rental price.
  • Safe for storing money, documents and valuables. If you are afraid that the hotel staff might steal them, then you can rent it at the reception. Usage fee is charged daily.
  • Wireless and free internet are available in all public areas.
  • Luggage storage.
  • Modern elevator.




Catering

Tourists buying a ticket to the hotel Podostrog Hotel 3 * can pay for one of three food systems: breakfast, half board and full board. Most often, guests choose the first option, and the rest of the time they eat in bars and cafes nearby. All meals are served in the hotel restaurant. In fine weather, visitors can eat on the outdoor terrace. Breakfast is usually served at the buffet, and guests who come for lunch and dinner are served √† la carte. The price also includes hot drinks at breakfast (tea, coffee, cream), as well as mineral water at lunch and dinner. On the ground floor of the hotel there is a bar in which tourists can buy local and imported alcohol.





Entertainment

The Budva Podostrog Hotel 3 * hotel does not have a rich selection of entertainment. As a rule, after breakfast, tourists leave the hotel and return only late in the evening. Nevertheless, if desired, for a fee, they can visit the massage room. The hotel does not have a private pool and sun terrace. At the municipal beach, umbrellas, sun loungers and towels are provided for a fee. In addition, you can visit the water entertainment center, as well as play volleyball or football.

Positive reviews

Guests staying at the Podostrog Hotel 3 *, leave different reviews. As a rule, tourists who are accustomed to budget accommodation are satisfied with the quality of service. They note the following advantages of the hotel:

  • Polite staff who are trying to quickly solve problems. The administrator speaks good Russian.
  • Settled immediately, without waiting for 14:00. If available, tourists can choose their own apartments.
  • Some rooms have modern plasma TVs, which many three-star hotels cannot boast.
  • Tasty and varied food in the restaurant. There are dishes for children and guests suffering from diseases of the gastrointestinal tract.
  • In the parking lot there is always a place where you can put the car.
  • Good soundproofing rooms. With the windows closed, no traffic is heard or noisy neighbors.




Negative reviews

On the contrary, travelers who are accustomed to relaxing in hotels of a higher category remain disappointed with the holidays spent here. Thus, Podostrog Hotel 3 * (Montenegro) gets mixed reviews. We list the disadvantages that tourists usually highlight:

  • Smoking is strictly prohibited at the hotel, but the rooms smell tobacco, which does not disappear even after airing.
  • Plumbing is in poor condition. For some guests, the toilet flush tank did not work, the shower doors did not close properly and taps flowed.
  • Terrible quality of drinks served in the restaurant. In coffee and tea, sediment can be detected. Juices are as if diluted with water to save.
  • The beach is dirty, and a lot of garbage floats in the sea. Nobody removes it, so you have to look for another place for swimming.
  • Little fruit is served for breakfast. Typically, only apples, oranges and bananas are on the menu.
  • Internet speed is very low, and in the room it does not work at all.

Conclusion

If you are looking for a good and inexpensive resort hotel, then the Podostrog Hotel 3 * hotel will be an excellent option for you. The prices here are low, and the quality of the service provided is not inferior to some four-star hotels. It is worth knowing that the complex is not suitable for lazy tourists who prefer to spend time at the hotel. But it will be an ideal option for an overnight stay. Most often here come adults, youth companies. Sometimes tourists meet with children.
