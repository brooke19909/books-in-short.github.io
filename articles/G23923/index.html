Seaside beach in Sochi: description of how to get

Sochi is a large resort city with a huge selection of beaches. Vacationers can sit on a shell, gravel, sand or stones. Those who are used to relax with a high level of comfort should pay attention to paid services. Popular is the beach "Seaside" in Sochi.

Basic information

This beach is perfect for tourists who want to spend a vacation in the central part of the city. The beach is located near the Seaside promenade and the Winter Theater. Its length reaches 700 meters. The plus is that there is no need to pay for admission. If you come with your beach equipment, you will be able to really comfortable rest. It is possible to rent a sunbed, umbrella or a whole bungalow for four people.

The disadvantage of the beach is that pebbles prevail here. Walking here barefoot is not entirely comfortable. Despite this, children run along the promenade without any special shoes and have a great time. The beach is clean and comfortable. Reviews show that here you can relax even with the smallest children.

The beach "Seaside" in Sochi is perfectly equipped. There is a first-aid post, free showers and toilets, and many locker rooms. In the summer, the "Seaside" beach in "Sochi" is crowded. Photos of the embankment zone are impressive.

Paid services

Any beach equipment can be rented. For a deck chair with an umbrella you will have to pay about 500 rubles, a bungalow will cost more than 1000 rubles. For 200-300 rubles you can rent an air mattress.

There are many entertainments on the beach, allowing you to make the rest on the sea bright and unforgettable. These are a variety of inflatable bananas, water slides, trampolines. Adults can ride catamarans, scooters, parachute. The cost of entertainment services starts from 200 rubles. The beach also offers yachting and boat services.

Those who rent a deck chair or bungalow on the beach do not need to sit in the scorching sun all day. Nearby there are many interesting places that you should definitely visit. Sochi's visiting card is the Winter Theater. You can watch an interesting performance with children at any time of the year. The theater has air conditioning.

How to get to the beach?

Nearby is a public transport stop. By bus or minibus you must go to Theater Street, stop "Winter Theater". Two blocks away is the Teatralnaya stop. Public transport runs from it to any areas of the city of Sochi, including the railway station.
