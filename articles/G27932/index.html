Samui in September. Rest and weather

Should I go on vacation on Samui in September? Is it possible to have a good rest on this island in the named month? Let's understand this issue and consider it in detail.

What is the weather like in September?

On Samui in September, the height of the "wet" season. This month there are as many rains as in August. True, in September there are more cloudy days when the blue sky partially hides behind the clouds. For many, such weather conditions are only a joy, since traveling is much easier. Sometimes in September there are light winds on Samui. Precipitation can occur both day and night. But they are usually short-lived - no more than one hour.

What to do in September? Features of rest

Do not think that Samui is bored in September. Tropical rains do not make rest on the island uninteresting or more limited. At this resort, vacation will be a joy in any weather. After all, you can always see the ancient temples of Thailand. Also, no one has canceled a beach holiday and an exciting jungle safari

Relax on the beach: how to spend time, and what to do better?

Now you know what the weather is in Koh Samui in September. Reviews of tourists confirm that a vacation in early autumn, no matter what, this month will be memorable. After all, for example, you can swim in the bay. The water there is still surprisingly warm - about 30 ¬∞ C. It happens that the sea becomes a bright turquoise color, as from the image of magazines.

If waves appear, then it is not so clean. Then the sea becomes noticeably darker.





However, this is the best time for surfers. Turbid water does not upset them. At this time, they can go kiteboarding or surfing. By the way, some tourists specially come here for this - they enter the kiteboard school for a short while and learn mastery.

Is it possible to sunbathe on Samui in September? In the reviews, the girls write that, yes it is possible. Becoming a mulatto while relaxing on Koh Samui will work out without much difficulty. During this period, not so strong ultraviolet radiation. And if you still do not forget to use special creams, then a beautiful tan is provided.

Various excursions and leisure activities

As we already said, in September you can not only lie on the beach on Koh Samui, but also go on excursions. There are a lot of interesting sights. Every tourist should visit the botanical gardens and nature parks. You should also visit the aquarium, a crocodile farm, in zoos.





There are many Buddhist temples on Koh Samui that will not leave you indifferent. Or you can go to the Muslim village, where you will see non-tourist life. Poverty and mountains of garbage are everywhere. But still there is something very beautiful, namely the yellow-green Muslim mosque.

Locals earn their living by fishing. For this reason, the beaches have a huge number of boats anchored. Catch is sold in markets, restaurants and shops.

You can rent a jeep on Koh Samui and go on your personal excursion. Unfortunately, taking a bike will not work, as it rains quite often here. A jeep will be great for a ride on the ring road, stopping at places you like. True, not everyone will like to ride potholes - a similar ride for those who love extreme sports.

In the village of Chaweng a lot of entertainment for young people. Soi Green Mango is the center of nightlife. There are many bars, clubs, as well as discos. Many beloved fast foods, such as McDonald's and Starbuks, also work here. In general, in this place you can have a good time.





True, you should not live in this area. Since if you want to go to bed early, then this will not work, because the music plays here until dawn.

On Fridays, after four in the evening, take a trip to Bophut or Nathon Beach. On this day there are pedestrian streets. That is, you will not meet any bikes and cars, only walking people. In this place, tourists have the opportunity to enjoy the reigning atmosphere of the holiday. Here you can try different goodies and listen to live music. If you get to the very end, you can find yourself by the sea.

I would like to tell you about one wonderful cafe on the beach - Coconut. It is located at Bophut. Incredibly delicious desserts and drinks are served there, prepared, as you might guess from the name, from a real coconut. The cafe has tables with large pillows, where you can comfortably sit down, relax and enjoy the sunset and beautiful views.

Festivals and holidays on Koh Samui

On September 15, the island celebrates Mid-Autumn Festival. Also, a major Jazz festival takes place here one of the weeks of the month. It gathers a lot of musicians and listeners from different countries. Some specially come to Samui in order to get to this holiday.

What are the prices of food and housing?

Holidays in September has another advantage. Due to the fact that precipitation is getting larger, the cost of housing and airline tickets is significantly reduced, compared with August. Fruits and souvenirs are also sold here cheaper than usual. The same applies to various excursions.

Little conclusion

Now you know what the weather is in Koh Samui (Thailand) in September. As you can see, it is difficult to predict it.





There are heavy rains here from time to time, and the sea becomes restless, but this alone can be considered a lack of relaxation. Many, on the contrary, like this weather. Tourists claim that the air then becomes even cleaner.

In September for vacationers there are lots of options for recreation. After all, the sea is still warm, while there is no extreme heat. And the prices for entertainment and goodies are noticeably lower.
