Casco

The main difference between Casco insurance and compulsory motor liability insurance is that the first option makes it possible to repair a car, regardless of who is responsible for the accident. Casco guarantees its owner vehicle repair in almost any situation.

In the Renaissance Insurance company, hull insurance can be assembled from separate modules, like a designer, including only the necessary options, as some may simply not be needed or significantly increase the cost of the product. In the event that the repair cost is more than seventy-five percent of the amount for which the vehicle was insured, the owner will receive a lump-sum compensation in cash. Payment will be limited to the amount of car insurance. This module is the most common and most expensive part of Casco. This is confirmed by the customer reviews of Renaissance Insurance.

Car insurance against theft and theft

Additional equipment insurance

The issuance of additional equipment insurance is valid for parts installed outside the manufacturer. Payment is issued only in case of theft or damage to this equipment, or its individual parts. A comprehensive insurance policy in Renaissance Insurance is now available to many.

Liability insurance

This module makes it possible to expand the limit of customer liability under the insurance policy in the event of a serious accident, as well as damage to several cars at once. The maximum allowed amount in this situation is two and a half million rubles. This option can be used only when buying a hull in the offices of Renaissance Insurance.

Total Cost Insurance

In the event of theft, in situations where the vehicle cannot be repaired, the customer is paid the full insurance cost of the car. The Renaissance organization offers this insurance module exclusively for cars of 2016 and 2017 of release.

Accident insurance

This module involves a cash payment in situations where passengers, the driver of the car received various injuries, became disabled or died as a result of a traffic accident. The payment in this case is calculated as a percentage of the amount for which the injured driver with passengers was insured.
