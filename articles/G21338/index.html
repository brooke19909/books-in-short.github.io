Arkhipo-Osipovka, "Albatross" (hotel complex). Description, reviews

Arkhipo-Osipovka is a resort village that is part of the administrative structure of Gelendzhik. It is located in a beautiful valley surrounded by mountains, between two rivers - Teshebs and Vulan. One of the advantages of Arkhipo-Osipovka is that it is located on the federal highway. That is why many Russians who prefer not to exhaust themselves with a long transfer passing through the winding mountain serpentines leading to other resorts spend their holidays in the hotels of Arhipka, as the people call this village.





All for relaxation

In fact, this is a small town in which there is all the necessary infrastructure for recreation on the beach. In Arkhipovka, the construction of hotels is rapidly developing. Numerous recreation centers, mini-hotels, and inexpensive guest houses are already functioning here. There are year-round sanatoriums with different areas of treatment. The snow-white embankment of the town is built up with cafes, clubs and discos. There are many amazing natural and historical monuments in its vicinity, including the famous dolmens. Prices for holidays here are the same as in the popular Black Sea resorts. And this is not surprising: there is a good ecology that the surrounding mountains and dense mixed forests provide. These are ideal conditions not only for a good rest, but also for the restoration of health. You can relax in the town during the off-season, as its hotels operate all year round, including the Albatros located almost on the embankment of the village of Arkhipo-Osipovka. This hotel complex has gained special popularity among Russians in recent years.
