How to order an online cadastral passport?

Since the advent of information technology, it has become much easier to get any information, including inquiries, reporting and other documentation. At the same time, you can do all this remotely, for example, order via the Internet a cadastral passport while drinking aromatic coffee at the table of your favorite cafe. We will talk about how to do this in our article.





Where and how can I apply for a document?

To date, there are several options for obtaining a cadastral passport. For example, if you have free time, you can contact the Rosreestr yourself. As an option, a visit to the Federal State Registration Service of Cadastre and Cartography makes sense to entrust your loved one, but subject to notarization of the relevant documents.

In addition, it is possible to order a cadastral passport via the Internet by going to the organization’s website and leaving a request there. If you wish, you can submit a request by sending the necessary list of documents by mail or by filling out a special form on the public services website.

What you need to know when making a request for a document?

Before ordering a cadastral passport via the Internet, you need to know a few key points:

  • document transfer service is paid;
  • the cost of services for individuals and legal entities is different;
  • the prices for sending a passport in electronic form and by mail vary;
  • registration and issuance of a passport is carried out within 5 days from the date of processing the application.

What is the cost of state duty for sending a document?

Suppose you decide to order an online cadastral passport. How much will it cost? In this case, the amount of state duty will directly depend on whether you are an ordinary average resident or a private entrepreneur and owner. For example, in the first case, the fee will be only 200, and in the second - 600 rubles.

When ordering a real estate passport in its electronic version you will save a lot of money, because in this case the state duty will cost 150 for the first category of citizens and 300 rubles for the second.





What documents will be needed for registration?

Do not know yet how to order a cadastral passport for an apartment via the Internet? Start by going to the public services website and preparing the following set of documents:

  • applications filled out in a special electronic form;
  • passports
  • original power of attorney for a representative;
  • the original document of ownership of the apartment or land;
  • the original state duty receipt.

We will tell about how to order a cadastral statement via the Internet.

How to order a document for an apartment through the portal of public services?

Do you need a cadastral passport for an apartment? Have you decided to look at the public services portal? In this case, start with the usual registration, after which you will have access to your personal account. So, suppose you went through formal registration and authorization. Next, go to the main page of the portal, scroll down and pay attention to the section (highlighted in blue) under the name "Electronic Services."

At the next stage, before ordering an online cadastral passport, select the third item from the presented menu of icons. It is called “Obtaining information from the State Committee for Civil Protection”.





How to fill in the request form correctly?

After you click on the link of the above icon, you will surely get to the page of the electronic request form. Next, you will need to clarify and select the appropriate data. For example, pay attention to the first paragraph of the form - “Cadastral statement about the property ...”. If you click on the arrow on the right below it, then from the menu that appears, you can select "Cadastral Passport".

To order a cadastral passport via the Internet, select “Section Object”, for example, “Room” (if you mean a document for an apartment). Then indicate the cadastral number or full address of the property. You can also record both options. And finally, after filling out all the necessary columns, select the form and method of providing the data you need. For example, you can get your cadastral passport in the form of:

  • an official document executed on paper, received at any branch of Rosreestr;
  • An electronic document with a link to it;
  • document received by mail.

After that you need to fill in your email, enter the text with the captcha code and go to the next page. And we continue to tell you about how you can order a cadastral passport via the Internet.

What should be indicated in the application form?

After clicking on the link at the end of the form, you will be redirected to a new form in which information about the applicant should be noted. That is, indicate whether you are an individual, an entrepreneur, or belong to a different category of citizens, for example, it can be persons authorized by the court, representatives of state funds and bankruptcy investigation commissions, etc.

Next, you need to mention the following information about yourself:

  • FULL NAME;
  • SNILS (if any);
  • type of document provided (passport);
  • number, series, date of issue of the passport;
  • Passport issued by;
  • contact telephone number.

In the end, it remains only to check the corresponding box and confirm your consent to the processing of data and their transfer to Rosreestr. If you have already filled out the form and fulfilled all the requirements listed above, you just have to check the written, confirm the application and wait for your cadastral passport. You can order via the Internet and other documents if you wish, and even submit a request for registration with the cadastral register.

Checking the correctness of the data entry

At the third stage, you will see a form ready to be sent, where the property will be indicated, address and contact details. All these data must be carefully re-read and verified. In case of inconsistency or error, use the capabilities of the editor and return to what was written. And if everything is indicated correctly, you can sign and send a request or simply forward it without a signature.





If everything is done correctly by you, on the next page you will see a small form with a positive response on registration and an indication of the request number. A link will appear there, allowing you to control the course of actions related to obtaining your cadastral passport for an apartment.

How to order a cadastral passport of a land plot?

In the event that you need to obtain a cadastral passport for the land, the application process must be carried out on the same principle as with an apartment or any other premises. However, when choosing an object, it is worth specifying "Land" and only then fill in all the necessary columns by analogy.





How to check the status of your application?

You can also check the status of the application you sent online. For this purpose, it is necessary to follow the remote control link, enter the number of your request into the empty field, the code from the displayed image and send. As a result, the answer will appear before you: “Your application with No. .... is in the status “Pending payment”. Below will be indicated the amount of state duty, which must be paid before your request is sent for consideration. After that, a reply with a payment code and several payment options will be sent to the mailing address you specified in the application.





Now you know how and where to order a cadastral passport via the Internet.

How to pay the state fee via the Internet?





To pay, you must follow the link left in the email, enter the payment code and select one of the following options:

  • terminals, electronic wallet and Qiwi Bank cards;
  • terminals, cash desks of the Ural Bank for Reconstruction and Development;
  • remote service of Sberbank of Russia;
  • cards and mobile payments of the Tavrichesky Bank;
  • mobile payments and bank cards of the Service Payment service.

In a word, it is possible to obtain a passport for an apartment or a land plot remotely. However, consideration of your application will be completed only after you pay the state fee.
