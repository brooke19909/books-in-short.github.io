River Garden Residence 4 *, 5 * (Turkey / Belek) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Belek is famous for its clear azure sea, beautiful beaches and healing air filled with aromas of pine forests and eucalyptus groves.

When planning a trip, it is always useful to get acquainted with the reviews of experienced travelers to choose the best option. This article provides information about the River Garden Holiday Village 4 * hotel (formerly called River Garden Residence 4 *).

Belek

Belek is located between the ancient city of Side and fashionable Antalya (35 km).

This popular center of international tourism is considered the most respectable, prestigious and comfortable, therefore there are not many noisy young people here, middle-aged tourists, often with children, predominate.

People come to this region to admire the amazing nature of the national reserve, and guests of the River Garden Hotel can relax among the green forests of cypress, cedar, eucalyptus and pine trees.

It is no coincidence that Belek is called "bird paradise." More than a hundred species of rare birds live here, including the symbol of Belek - the famous barn owl or owl "tuto-alba" (the same snow-white owl that brought letters to Harry Potter).

The reserve is famous not only for birds, but also for Caretta-Caretta turtles (meter-long sea turtles with a large head), which have been sailing to Belek's beaches for 100 million years to lay their eggs here.

Belek is a major international golf center, fans of this sport come from many countries here.

Climate and beaches

Belek has a mild Mediterranean subtropical climate. It is dry and hot in summer (38-40 ¬∞ C), wet and warm in winter (15 ¬∞ C).

The sea warms up in summer to 27 ¬∞ C, and in winter maintains a temperature of 17 ¬∞ C.

Tourists living in the Belek River Garden have the opportunity to swim on the beautiful beaches of Belek, which is almost a continuous beach strip for 20 km.

All beaches are clean and even, regularly cleaned, well equipped and covered with sand. There are more blue flags than in Antalya, and magnificent eucalyptus and pine groves descend directly to the sea waves.

Interesting places

Belek and the surrounding areas have many attractions:

  • The ruins of the ancient cities of Aspendos, Perge and Side (half an hour before each).
  • Museums, water parks and entertainment centers of Antalya (30 minutes).
  • Kepryulu Canyon National Park (unique flora and fauna, rare birds and giant turtles, cypress and eucalyptus trees, mountain climbing, rock climbing, horseback riding, rafting and kayaking on a mountain river).
  • Excursions in Pamukalla (hot mineral springs surrounded by snow-white calcareous hills and terraces).
  • Kursunlu National Park with the famous waterfall of the same name.
  • Green Canyon (a picturesque reservoir surrounded by green forests) and Lake and Waterfall Minangaut.
  • Water park "Troy" in the hotel Rixos Premium Belek, built in the form of an ancient city with unusual slides and a dolphinarium.

Hotel

Hotel River Garden 5 (Turkey) was built 5 km from Belek and 40 km from Antalya (transfer from Antalya Airport - 45 minutes).

The hotel complex is the so-called "holiday village" (HV - Holiday Village) and consists of two-storey villas, which accommodate 4 rooms (2 on the ground floor and 2 on the second). Only 69 rooms. The cost of one room is from 9600 rubles per night.





Each cottage has its own pool (140 cm deep). There is a secluded relaxation pool by the river.

The hotel has a large and well-groomed area with its own garden and park.

The staff speaks Russian and English.
