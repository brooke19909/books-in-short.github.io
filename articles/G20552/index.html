Lek Jomtien Hotel (Pattaya, Jomtien): room description, service, reviews

Pattaya as a vacation destination today is chosen by many domestic tourists. The best area in this resort, of course, is Jomtien. There are simply a huge number of good hotels that accept Russian vacationers, including in this part of Pattaya. Most of them deserve very good reviews from tourists. Refers to the number of such hotels and Lek Jomtien Hotel.

Location Features

This complex is located in the south of Pattaya, about 3 km from the famous pedestrian Walking Street. Actually, the Jomtien district itself is famous for its simply well-developed infrastructure. Near the hotel there are many cafes, restaurants, pharmacies, clubs and shops.





Russian tourists arrive who decide to relax in Pattaya, usually at U-Tapao Airport. It will not be difficult for our compatriots to get from it to the hotel. The tour operators working with this hotel usually provide their customers with a free shuttle service right to its gate. Vacationers who book rooms at the Lek Jomtien Hotel on their own can get to her by taxi. Also, such tourists have the opportunity to book a transfer in the hotel (for a fee).

General description of the hotel

The Lek Jomtien Hotel complex belongs to the two-star class. It is a multi-storey building of modern construction. It is considered most suitable for individual tourists, couples without children and elderly vacationers.

This hotel is non-smoking. For adherents of this bad habit, special places are allocated on the territory of the hotel. Pets are not allowed in this hotel. The hotel reception is open 24 hours a day. When checking in with tourists do not take a deposit.





Lek Jomtien complex: room description

Since this is a budget hotel, rooms here are only rented as standard. The rooms for tourists include the following amenities:

  • TV;

  • air conditioning;

  • minibar with fridge.

Of course, like in any other Pattaya hotel, the rooms of Lek Jomtien Hotel, among other things, have individual showers. Guests can dry bathing accessories on the balconies.

Reviews about the number of rooms

The opinion of tourists about this hotel was very good. Hotel rooms are small but very clean and tidy. As many former guests of the Lek Jomtien 2 * hotel note , maids are cleaning daily. Moreover, they relate to their duties very well, regardless of whether they will be left with a tip or not.





The balconies in this hotel are quite large. But they are, unfortunately, not in all rooms. Of course, there are no particularly beautiful views from the windows of the rooms of this budget hotel. Most of them face the walls of neighboring buildings. However, very dense curtains are provided on the windows in the rooms rented out at the hotel. Thanks to their presence, tourists even in the afternoon have the opportunity to sleep in a room in almost complete darkness.

The air conditioners in the rooms work, judging by the reviews of the hotel guests, regularly. Moreover, according to the residents, they create a very comfortable microclimate in the rooms. Air conditioners are placed quite conveniently. At least the tenants did not complain about drafts.

The soundproofing in the hotel rooms, according to many guests, unfortunately, is not too good. Hotel residents can even hear the operation of air conditioners from neighboring rooms and the conversations of other guests. However, this hotel itself is quite quiet. There are no nightclubs and discos nearby. And the main contingent of guests is elderly Europeans. Sometimes such residents bring visitors to the taikas. But at the same time they practically do not drink and usually behave quite quietly. It is because of the hiding places that this hotel is not recommended for tourists with children. In addition, for the small guests in the hotel there are no additional amenities.

Showers in the hotel, according to many residents, are quite convenient for taking water procedures. Toilet paper in the bathrooms is always present. The same goes for shampoo soaps. The maids take out the garbage regularly.





Hotel infrastructure

Good reviews from tourists Lek Jomtien Hotel (Pattaya, Jomtien) for served not only for comfortable rooms. The infrastructure at this hotel, according to many of its former residents, is also quite well developed. Very often, domestic holidaymakers vacationing in Thailand rent cars in this country. If you wish, such guests can leave their car in a free parking lot belonging to the hotel itself. This parking is not guarded. However, there were no cases of theft on her.

There is, of course, a small pool at this hotel. It is located on the third floor of the building. According to many guests, swimming in the hotel pool is quite comfortable. But tourists rarely visit it. After all, the sea is within walking distance of the hotel. Chlorine in the pool complex of the staff, according to many guests, sprinkles quite a lot. Perhaps this helps to avoid the spread of infection (which in a hot climate is quite likely). However, people with sensitive skin should be wary of swimming in the hotel pool. In any case, skin softening creams should be used.

In addition to the pool and parking, the hotel provides for the convenience of tourists:

  • a room for washing clothes, equipped with modern household appliances;

  • baggage storage room;

  • shared lounge with TV;

  • tour agency.





Tourists have the opportunity to return valuables and documents to storage in a safe. For this, a deposit of 2,000 baht (approximately 3,300 Russian rubles) is taken from the guests at the hotel. After eviction from the room, this money is returned to tourists. Guests on the upper floors can take elevators to their rooms.

Hotel infrastructure reviews

Hotel Lek Jomtien offers its guests a decent service , according to many vacationers . The staff at this hotel is very polite. If the tenant has any problems, he can at any time contact the administrator at the reception. All misunderstandings the hotel staff are trying to solve promptly.

In particular, this hotel has earned good guest reviews for its free Wi-Fi throughout. Also, the advantage of the Lek Jomtien Hotel (Pattaya, Jomtien) among tourists is the presence of two Russian-language TV channels at once. In the evening, vacationers can watch some interesting programs. Tourists also include properly functioning elevators to the advantages of this complex.

Breakfast

Despite the fact that the Lek Jomtien Hotel is a two - star hotel , the dining room here has its own. Rooms for rent in this complex with breakfast. The food in the hotel, according to many guests, is tolerable. Special frills in the dining room menu of the hotel is not provided. But the residents do not remain hungry in the morning.

Fruits and vegetables, watermelons and pineapples are what guests of the Lek Jomtien can order in addition to the main courses . The restaurant of this complex can be visited in the morning from 7 to 11 hours . Moreover, even late residents will get fruits almost always. The main menu of the hotel dining room usually includes:

  • fried eggs;

  • ham slice;

  • sausage;

  • boiled eggs;

  • fig.

According to the buffet system, soups, onions, butter, and jam are also served in the dining room.

Beach Hotels

The Lek Jomtien Hotel is located in close proximity to the sea - on a street perpendicular to the shore. In order to get to the beach, hotel guests need to overcome only about 300 meters.

All kinds of cafes, massage parlors, shops, bars are evenly distributed along the coastline opposite the hotel. The sea itself in this place is quite clean. Jomtien Beach is the longest in Pattaya. People on the seafront opposite the hotel are usually quite a lot. The beach is covered with soft sand. So sunbathing here is quite convenient. However, unfortunately, trash is often found in the sand. Therefore, some hotel guests prefer to visit other beaches in Pattaya. For example, Koh Lan on Tien Beach enjoys great popularity among hotel residents.

City infrastructure near the hotel

Thai baht Russian tourists who have settled in this hotel can purchase without any problems. Several profitable exchangers are located in the immediate vicinity of Lek Jomtien. Entertainment tourists resting in the Jomtien area, are also waiting for the most diverse. If desired, hotel guests can visit, for example, bars with dancers, various clubs, massage parlors, cafes with music. Also, residents of the complex have the opportunity to go to the night market and one of the best restaurants in Pattaya, working within walking distance from the gates of the complex.





sights

Of course, tourists who book rooms at the Lek Jomtien Hotel, among other things, can visit all sorts of interesting places in Pattaya. For example, close to this hotel are located:

  • Pattaya Park Tower . This is a very interesting place for Russian tourists is almost a cult. The tower is included in the program of almost all sightseeing tours. On the 55th floor of this building there is an observation deck from which the whole city is led. You can get down from here by cable car - fastened with a cable or in a trailer.

  • Pattaya Aquarium This structure is a hundred-meter underwater tunnel with glass walls. At the entrance to the aquarium there is a swimming pool with marine life, which you can also touch with your hands.

Also nearby the Lek Jomtien 2 * hotel there are such interesting sights as, for example, Pattaya Hill, a race track, a cart-center, etc.
