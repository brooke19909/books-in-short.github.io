Hotel Karmir Resort SPA 5 * (Kemer, Turkey): description, photos and reviews

Five star vacation in a luxury apartment





Karmir Resort SPA Hotel has a total of 299 rooms, of which 255 are standard, 32 are family rooms, 2 are for families with children, 6 are for newlyweds and newlyweds, and four are for people with disabilities. All rooms are cleaned - cleaning is carried out every day, and linen is changed every other day. All rooms are equipped with TVs, air conditioning, telephones. There is also wireless Internet access, a minibar, satellite TV, a telephone, necessary toiletries, and even central heating with an electronic safe. As you can see, the hotel administration made sure that the guests, who decided to stay there, felt at home.
