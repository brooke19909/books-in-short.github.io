How to crochet a triangle - all basic methods

Sometimes a lace product is created by assembling individual (triangular, square, round) elements into a single whole. Each motive is sequentially tied to another, guided by the pattern. Elements can be either plain or knitted from several multi-colored threads. They can be flat or with the content of a knitted flower.

Triangles can also be useful for making toys, such as paws or wings. Shawls, bactuses and scarves are also triangular in shape, and such things can be connected from one triangular piece.

Crocheting a triangle for beginners is not difficult. How to do this, the article will tell. Consider methods of knitting such motifs in various ways.

Triangle with the direction of knitting from the corner

So, how to crochet a triangle from a corner? To do this, dial 2 in. item and the second from the hook knit 3 tbsp. without nak. Next is the increase - its meaning is to knit two tbsp. without nak. in each row in the first and last columns of the previous rows.

Triangles from the bottom edge with different options for reducing loops in each row

The simplest and most commonly used methods are knitting triangles from the bottom up. Before crocheting a triangle, determine the size of its base. Dial the chain from to. n., equal to this length, and continue to work, reducing evenly the loops from the edges. Decreases can be performed from the beginning of the row and ending it, but it is possible only at the end of the rows. The slope or, on the contrary, the steepness of the sides of the resulting motive will depend on these manipulations.
