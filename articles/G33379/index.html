Pension "Gold Coast". Abkhazia

In the Gudauta district near the village of Mgudzyrkhva, there is a recreation center called "Golden Coast" (Abkhazia). It is located just 100 meters from the beach, surrounded by a beautiful tropical park. The health resort was opened in 1988, and until now it is perhaps one of the favorite vacation spots for many tourists.



Description

The boarding house is located in the village of Zolotoy Bereg, 55 km from the border, near the Psou River. The last restoration of the complex was carried out in 2006. The recreation center is located in one of the ecologically clean regions of the country near the park in which exotic plants and trees grow. The resort has a number of wellness programs and events. Health resort "Zolotoy Bereg" (Abkhazia) is located 40 km from Sukhumi and 33 km from Gagra.

Climate and Wellness

The air temperature in this region averages +15 degrees, humidity - within 75%. From July to September, the tourist season begins. In the summer months, the air warms up to +23 degrees, and the breeze blows from the sea almost all the time, bringing freshness and coolness. The humid subtropical climate of the region is the main therapeutic factor used in conducting health programs. Rooms

The recreation center "Golden Coast" (Abkhazia) is a modern four-story building. For leisure tourists comfortable one-room double and triple rooms are provided. They are equipped with all necessary amenities: bathroom, furniture, shower, refrigerator, TV, fan. Some apartments have a balcony.

Beach

Pension "Gold Coast" (Abkhazia) has its own beach (sand and pebble), located just 150 meters from the main building. It is equipped with parasols, sun loungers, showers and changing rooms.

Nutrition

In the dining room, located in a separate building, guests are offered three meals a day. Also in the pension there is a barbecue cafe, where you can enjoy dishes of national or traditional cuisine.
