How to make a goat mask in different ways

Prepare your child for the New Year tree or theatrical performance? Already sewn a suit, but do not know how to make a goat mask? Read the instructions. Choose the right option.

Manufacturing methods

If you are interested in how to make a goat mask , you should first decide how much time you are willing to spend on creating it. If you need to complete the product quickly, choose the easy option. It will look simplified, but it will take literally a few minutes to create it. If you are oriented in advance and can afford long creative work, pay attention to creating a realistic character.





So, you can make a goat mask with your own hands in the following ways:

  • Cut the desired shape from a flat sheet of white or gray paper.
  • Make from paper according to a template that involves folding lines.
  • Glue a more complex three-dimensional shape also according to the scheme.
  • Perform a realistic muzzle using the papier-m√¢ch√© technique.
  • Sew from fabric (usually looks like a hat or partially covers the face).

All presented methods are presented according to the degree of complication. The first option is done quickly, the last two - long enough (not even in one day). As for mounting the product on the head, in any method you can glue or sew the finished mask on a strap, elastic, paper strip, tape.

Materials and Tools

Before you start learning how to make a goat mask, check out the list of accessories you need. It is worth immediately preparing them, and then follow the steps of the instruction. Consider a group of options made of paper, since it is not a mask as such, but a headpiece that is made of fabric. So, you need the following:

  • paper or cardboard;
  • Pencil, eraser, ruler or printed template
  • scissors;
  • glue;
  • paints;
  • brush or sponge (sponge);
  • varnish (optional);
  • additional decorations to create a character (yarn or artificial hair, bow, flower for "hairstyle").

Thus, any of the methods for making paper masks involves the use of the same materials, that is, in the simple version and in the complex, the same thing is needed. You can create a fabulous image by decorating and painting the surface of the mask with a brush.





How to make a goat mask: instructions

Paper-based plastic is a common name for technology that can characterize all methods of manufacturing a product from paper or cardboard, just in some cases a minimum of actions is required, in others - much more. Let's consider a difficult option. The general sequence of work is as follows:

  1. Prepare the template.
  2. Cut out all the elements.
  3. Draw a knitting needle or non-writing ballpoint pen along the fold lines from the wrong side.
  4. Perform folds.
  5. Make cuts where necessary.
  6. Glue all the parts into a three-dimensional structure.
  7. Prepare the paints. For acrylic, you can pre-coat the surface of the product.
  8. To make a goat mask with your own hands more realistic, draw all the elements of the face as natural as possible.
  9. After drying, it is good to varnish the surface. This will give a finished look and harden the paint layer.
  10. Attach an elastic band, ribbon, paper strip, or come up with another convenient way to attach the mask to your head.

All is ready. It's time to try on.





How to make a New Year's goat mask using the papier-m√¢ch√© technique

This method allows you to create an almost completely realistic product that repeats not only the external forms of the animal itself. You can make a mask so that inside it will clearly lie on the surface of your face. The meaning of the technology is that the form is created manually from small pieces of paper glued in layers on top of each other. Usually, a certain preform, for example, from plasticine, is used as the basis. In order for the inner surface to repeat the shapes of your face, you need to put the first layer directly on yourself, after covering your face (except for the eyes, nostrils) with a film or other material.





So, the sequence of work will be as follows:

  1. Roll or cut paper into small pieces.
  2. Prepare the foundation.
  3. Put on it the first layer of glue and pieces of paper (you can pre-mix them).
  4. Leave to dry.
  5. Apply the same number of layers on the same principle to obtain the desired thickness and shape.
  6. When the product is completed, proceed to decorating according to the technology specified in the previous section.
