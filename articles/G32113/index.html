Hotel "Kuban" (Gelendzhik): description of rooms, sea, recreation, reviews

Summer holidays on the Black Sea coast - what could be better? Excellent conditions for spending holidays are offered by the Kuban Hotel. Gelendzhik has long been popular with tourists due to the incredible nature, clean air and many attractions. And if all this is combined with comfortable hotel accommodation, a vacation will definitely succeed!

Basic information

For those planning a cheap vacation in Gelendzhik, the Kuban Hotel is ideal. The guesthouse offers comfortable rooms with the possibility of installing extra beds. This is especially convenient for families. The hotel has a great location. From the windows of the pension you can enjoy the incredible beauty of the Black Sea. The hotel address is Gelendzhik, Bolnichny Lane, Building 3. You can book a room by calling the hotline 8 (800) 245 50 10.



Hotel Kuban invites guests from May 1 to September 30. At this time, the sea is really warm. It is possible to have a great vacation away from your native land. Weather in Gelendzhik in September pleases tourists. While in most cities of Russia thermometers show a temperature of +15 degrees Celsius, there can still be heat. In September, many domestic tourists prefer to go to the Krasnodar Territory.

Pension "Kuban" offers all the conditions for a comfortable stay. Each room has the ability to access the Internet. The hotel territory has cozy playgrounds, an area for sports.

Rooms in the boarding house "Kuban"

When choosing a place to stay, most tourists primarily pay attention to prices. Hotel "Kuban" (Gelendzhik) offers truly loyal conditions for a comfortable stay both independently and as a family. In the economy category, you can rent a double room for only 1200 rubles per day. This will be a cozy apartment with two beds, bedside tables, a spacious refrigerator and air conditioning. For 1400 rubles a day, you will be able to rent a room in the category of "standard" (with bath and toilet).



You can not call too expensive and a room in the category of "luxury". For a daily stay the couple will have to pay 2,000 rudders. For this money, you will be able to get comfortable apartments with a wide double bed, a large hall, cozy upholstered furniture, a wardrobe, a split system and a wide bathroom with a bathtub. You will have to pay 300 rubles per day for an extra sleeping place, regardless of room category.

Meals in a boarding house

Hotel "Kuban" (Gelendzhik) is a place where care for the comfortable rest of tourists. Those who come here on vacation will not have to think about which restaurant can have a better or cheaper lunch. The boarding house offers three meals a day. Reviews show that the chef thinks through the menu to the smallest detail. These are not only domestic, but also foreign dishes that gourmets will like. For cooking, only environmentally friendly products are used.



Guests can have breakfast, lunch and dinner in a cozy room. Meals are included in the price. Those who plan to come on vacation with young children need to order an additional portion in advance. Tourists note that the dishes in the boarding house are great for those who follow a diet. Suitable food for children under three years of age.

Is it possible to imagine a vacation on the Black Sea coast without interesting excursions? Ideal for exploring the sights of early fall. The weather in Gelendzhik in September is conducive to walks. Night life

Hotel "Kuban" 3 * is a favorite place of domestic youth. The low cost of rooms makes it affordable for students on the Black Sea coast. In the summer, huge companies come here who strive to have a bright time and take a break from everyday work. The nightlife in Gelendzhik is really in full swing. Night clubs are conducive to communication and new acquaintances.

Not far from the boarding house is the club "Formula". This is one of the most popular places in Gelendzhik. At night, colorful parties are held here. The club seeks to visit not only tourists, but also locals. Where else can you have so much fun with your company?

It is worth noting that the "Formula" in the summer works both day and night. Especially popular is the pool area. A swimsuit party takes place around the clock. It seems that the celebration begins in May, and ends only in September. In winter, the night club also works for local residents, but only on weekends from 20:00 to 3:00.

Boat trip

How else does the Kuban hotel offer to spend time? Gelendzhik is famous primarily for its incredible sea beauties. Those who have planned a vacation here should definitely make time for a boat trip. The boarding house cooperates with the shipping and tourist company Komandor. Boat trip can be carried out at preferential rates.



You can go on a tour at any time of the day. Evening youth walks are popular among young people. The ship will be able to enjoy an incredibly beautiful sunset. The "company" commander offers walks for avid fishermen. The ship leaves the sea in the early morning. Only far from civilization will it be possible to get a good catch. The woman on board is not a hindrance. Thanks to a family walk, you can combine business with pleasure. While a man plays the role of a fisherman, a woman enjoys incredible sea beauties, basking in the warm rays of ultraviolet radiation.

Safari Park

Many come with their children to the Kuban Hotel (Gelendzhik). Reviews show that the area here is ideal for relaxing small tourists. What is Safari-steam worth? This is a place where animals do not feel restrictions in freedom. They lead a familiar lifestyle, and tourists have the opportunity to watch tigers, bears, wolves and other exotic representatives of the fauna.



"Safari Park" is organized without the usual aviaries. Animals have the opportunity to bask in the sun, sleep on a tree, swim in the lake. Conditions are as close to natural as possible. The park is fenced off from people by a large fence. Thus, observing animals can be absolutely safe.

The park has a large museum "From the depths of centuries and waters." Here are presented historical exhibits that were once found on the territory of the Krasnodar Territory. In addition, there are coins from around the world, models of sailing ships, coral reefs. Kids can visit the mysterious cabin of Captain Nemo. Only here you can see the bottom of the porthole.
