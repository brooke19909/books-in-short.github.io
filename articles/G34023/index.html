The best hotels of the Canary Islands: photos and reviews of tourists

The Canary Archipelago is located in the Atlantic Ocean and consists of seven large islands and several small ones. Hotels in the Canary Islands are very popular with tourists. A unique climate, clean and wide beaches, original local cuisine - now holidays on the islands are available to everyone. For lovers of comfort in the Canaries there are many modern hotels, extreme sportsmen can go in for all kinds of sports, and eco-tourists can visit secluded wildlife spots.

Tenerife - the island of eternal spring

Tenerife is the most popular destination among tourists. Mountains divide the island into two climatic zones. In the north, there is a lot of greenery, a humid climate, and at night it is quite cool. The southern part is sunny; temperature differences are practically not felt.

In Tenerife there are several large resorts where all conditions for a comfortable stay are created. It is here that the most expensive Canary Islands hotels are built:

  • Hotel Gran Bahia Del Duque 5 * in Costa Adeje - is a huge resort complex with spas, gyms, children's mini-clubs and many other entertainments. Guests can stay in standard rooms, villas and luxury mansions.
  • Red Level for Families at Gran Melia Tenerife - considered the best hotel in the Canary Islands. It is located on the ocean and consists of modern villas and deluxe rooms. Impeccable service, delicious cuisine and lots of entertainment attract both couples with children and youth companies to relax in the hotel.
  • Hotel Baobab Suites - Located on the second coastline. Modern rooms in snow-white shades, radiant cleanliness, maximum comfort and convenience, the ability to play sports and shopping - all this makes the Baobab hotel a popular destination among tourists.

Gran Canaria Island - a miniature continent

In Gran Canaria, there are wide beaches with golden sand, magnificent landscapes and many different attractions for tourists. In terms of service in hotels, the island is a worthy competitor to Tenerife. Here are:

  • Bohemia Suites & Spa - Adults Only. The hotel accepts only adult tourists and is located on the second coastline. Heated pool, restaurants, gym, spa, and also a computer in each room - the whole range of services is difficult to list.
  • Hotel Santa Catalina is a luxurious building with spacious rooms. Includes a wellness center, several pools, a tennis court. There are several restaurants on site that serve Mediterranean and national cuisine.
  • Sercotel Hotel Cristina Las Palmas is a city hotel near the port. The hotel is decorated in bright colors. The rooms have city and city beach views. The √† la carte restaurant serves European cuisine.

Canary Island Fuerteventura Hotels

Fuerteventura is an ancient island, the second largest among the Canary Islands. It has no noisy nightclubs, which attracts the attention of tourists who love silence and a relaxing holiday.

  • Sheraton Fuerteventura Golf & Spa Resort is a beachfront hotel. All rooms are air-conditioned and have satellite TV. Guests can enjoy several buffet restaurants. Other activities include a modern tennis court. The spa offers unusual treatments: an ice igloo and a steam room with a hammam.
  • Elba Palace Golf & Vital Hotel - Adults Only is an adult-only hotel. The hotel is built in a traditional Canarian style. Many palm trees, a beautiful courtyard and magnificent architecture contribute to the complete relaxation of the guests.
  • Playitas Villas - holiday villas. Suitable for tourists who are looking for peace and solitude. Each villa has a kitchen and a small swimming pool. On the territory there is a large pool, close to the Olympic size.

Lanzarote - volcano island

Lanzarote has the best beaches of the Canary Islands, which are ideal for families with children. This island is loved by tourists who prefer a nudist vacation.

  • Villa VIK - Hotel Boutique is a small boutique hotel located on the first line near the beach with fine yellow sand, where there is almost never a storm. The rooms are spacious and bright, equipped with the latest technology. The bar serves exotic cocktails and gourmet cuisine.
  • Princesa Yaiza Suite Hotel Resort is a huge tourist complex with six pools, a thalassotherapy center and several restaurants. Each room has a balcony or a spacious terrace. The spa offers chromotherapy and massage treatments. The hotel operates on an all-inclusive basis, and also offers Mexican, Japanese, Italian cuisine.
  • Hesperia Lanzarote Playa Dorada - Only 50 meters to the beach. For guests from deluxe rooms, poolside seats are reserved for the entire rest. The hotel has several restaurants, one of which is open for buffet. Rest at the hotel can be combined with shopping, because the hotel is located near numerous shops and boutiques.

El Hierro - the smallest island

El Hierro is a tiny island with a unique relief. It is here that rare plants grow and endangered species of animals live. The exoticism of this small piece of land attracts travelers from all over the world.

There are no expensive hotels on El Hierro; people come here to enjoy the peace and nature. Tourists are offered to stay in guest houses, in private villas and in small hotels.

  • CR La Asomada is a self-catering rural guest house with barbecue facilities. The house has a bathroom, a terrace and a small garden.
  • Apartment El Apendre, Erese Alto - studio apartments with a kitchen, bathroom and unforgettable ocean views.

La Palma - green island

La Palma is a picturesque island created for a perfect holiday. Exotic birds and butterflies live in the reserve, and you can go diving in secluded bays. The island has an official beach for nudists.

  • Taburiente Playa is a hotel on the second coastline. The patio is decorated with lush vegetation and a pond. The "highlight" of the hotel is the preparation of dishes in the presence of guests. The restaurants serve European and national cuisine. There is a popular diving center near the hotel.
  • Apartamentos Los Rosales - a complex of apartments in a green garden, surrounded by swimming pools. The rooms are simply decorated in light colors.
  • Casas La Principal is a hotel with stunning mountain views. All units include a flat-screen TV. There is a full kitchen with a microwave. Each room has a terrace with garden views.

La Gomera - part of the Canary Archipelago

La Gomera Island is very small, but tourists visit it quite often. There are few beaches on the island; the total length is only about six hundred meters. Most travelers bathe in small bays and bays. The sand on the beaches is black due to its volcanic origin.
