How to choose a tablet for creating graphic images on a computer?

Even the most experienced masters of fine art are often asked quite an urgent question about how to choose a tablet for drawing, without being confused by the variety of available models. Indeed, today there are a great many options. One tablet has a high resolution display, the other is impressive in size, the third stands out at an attractive price. How to get exactly what you need without losing quality and not going broke?



So, you need to choose a tablet. To begin with, it is worth determining in terms of its focus. These devices are divided into two large groups. The first is for true professionals. Models from the second group will suit beginner artists. Therefore, such tablets will differ in many ways. They will have slightly different functions, features, sizes, specifications and, of course, price.

If you need to choose a tablet for a novice artist, then in this case you will be pleasantly surprised by the relatively low cost of the devices. This is due, first of all, to the fact that such models usually do not have sensitivity to the angle of inclination, are equipped with a medium-quality display, and also can not boast of a large number of available levels of depression.

But in order to master the basics of digital drawing, such tablets will be enough.

Properly choosing a professional-level tablet will help you abstract from the price tag. If you need to purchase a really high-quality thing, then you need to prepare for significant costs. But such tablets clearly respond to pressing different strengths and perceive even the smallest change in the angle of the screen.

The size of the model directly depends on the size of the paper on which the drawing should be transferred. Most often, artists choose tablets, focusing on the A4 option. He is also the most popular. However, many professionals claim that it is most convenient to work with a tablet that is tuned to the A5 format.

Be sure to correlate the size of the model with the parameters of the computer monitor. This will help you choose the tablet that most closely matches your existing PC. Otherwise, the image may become very distorted.

The higher the clarity of the display, the more diverse will be the movement of the pen. Therefore, a screen resolution indicator will help you choose a graphics tablet . The higher this parameter, the more movements this model will recognize. This is especially important when it comes to complex drawings, where a variety of shadows play an important role. The standard version is 2000 dpi. It is quite suitable for most artists and designers.

Pay attention to the pen device. Usually it works with a cord that connects to a USB format connector, or on batteries. However, both of these options are not the most convenient. The cord will limit the movement of the user, and the batteries significantly make the entire structure heavier. Therefore, you need to choose exactly the tablet that works on the principle of electromagnetic resonance. In this case, the pen will not need to be fed from additional sources. This option is the most convenient.
