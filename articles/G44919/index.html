Bang Tao beach: photos, description and reviews

Bang Tao Beach (Phuket) with a total length of 8 km is located on the west coast of the island. Despite its advantages, Bang Tao does not belong to the successful choice of a vacation spot for the average tourist. Mostly expensive resort with appropriate rates for accommodation, services. Therefore, the budget tourist is not particularly clear.

Bang Tao Beach

One of the best beaches for family, secluded, single and youth vacations, located in the kingdom of Thailand.

Bang Tao Beach is a huge bay, and the 8-kilometer coastline makes it the second largest in Phuket. It is located in the northwestern part between Pansea Beach and Nai Thon.





Bang Tao is also known as Laguna Beach, as the area surrounding the place was previously used for tin mining. Later, the territory turned into a luxurious "Lagoon Phuket Complex" and is considered the center of the most prestigious villas and hotels.

Just a couple of minutes drive from the beach is the city of Cherng Talay, where the local population still lives on the profits from fishing, agriculture and latex processing.

There is no rental of sun loungers on the beach, as hotels provide them for their guests. But there are a lot of dealers in trinkets and local souvenirs.

Conditional division

The northern part is located closer to the airport and, of course, undeveloped, that is, there is no infrastructure and hotels. Along the coast you can find several lonely cafes, and beyond it is only a road. There are practically no tourists here, so the northern part of Bang Tao is an ideal place for privacy. True, the quality of the beach is worse in comparison with the southern part: a narrow strip of sand, and during tides its width is only a few meters. A place free not only from vacationers, but also from entertainment and sunbeds.





The southern part is located closer to Patong beach and is very different in infrastructure from the northern one. This is where all the cafes, shops, restaurants, hotels and entertainment are located. True, the situation in the southern part is not the same as in the usual resorts. There are no merchants, rental offices or travel agencies. Everything is cultural, rich, civil, and most importantly, clean. Noisy tourist areas familiar to many vacationers are located at the very end of the south and smoothly pass to the territory of Surin beach.

How to get there

Arriving at Bang Tao Beach by local public transport does not work, it remains to call a taxi or rent a car. It is worth considering the high prices of taxi drivers, since the area is expensive. For example, the road from Patong Beach or the airport will cost about 600 baht, and then if you bargain. For 50 baht you can take the local shuttle bus to Surin Beach.

Shopping at Bang Tao Beach

Taking a walk along the beach, you can find several shacks that sell all the accessories necessary for the beach, from clothes to sunglasses.

You will get household items and household utensils near the entrance to the Laguna Complex.

A shop with Asian art, wooden statues of elephants and a handmade Buddha is located on the main road on the way to the beach. Here is the world-famous chain store 7 eleven.

At the entrance to the Laguna Phuket in the Canal Village shopping center there is an opportunity to buy antique goods and designer clothes.

Restaurants on the beach

Laguna Resort occupies the central territory of Bang Tao. There are several excellent restaurants north of Angsana Laguna Resort. Restaurants are among the best, the prices are reasonable, as they are not related to hotels, and the food causes gastronomic delight.

Therefore, if your path passes through Bang Tao Beach, then reviews of tourists recommend visiting restaurants located to the north of the Laguna resort.





Preeda's Kitchen is one of the favorite places for vacationers who want to have a meal right on the sand. And judging by the reviews, the best place in Bang Tao is not to be found. The restaurant serves seafood and Thai dishes. For children, you can order a large hamburger with cheese and chicken with crisp.

A little north of Preeda's Kitchen is the famous Lotus Restaurant , which has been operating for 20 years and has been recognized as the best fish establishment on Bang Tao Beach. Here you can taste seafood such as shrimp, sea bass, lobster. In addition to Thai cuisine, the restaurant's menu also includes European cuisine. The laid-back atmosphere and breathtaking sunset views are ideal for a romantic dinner, where you should definitely choose a barbecue of tiger prawns with garlic and olive sauce as the main dish.

A good selection of restaurants, which are also desirable to visit, are located on the way to the Laguna resort complex.

For example, you can taste the latest Asian and European cuisines at DeDos Restaurant. The institution is open from 6 am to 10 pm.

If you like Indian food, then be sure to check out Kargo Kurry. And next door to it is a restaurant offering international cuisine.

As you can see, Bang Tao Beach is not a place to stay hungry.

Nightlife and weddings

The Laguna Phuket complex has a very beautiful chapel offering a magnificent panoramic view of Bang Tao Beach (photo taken during the wedding ceremony of a couple from Europe). Perhaps this is the perfect place for a romantic wedding ceremony.





As for the nightlife, it mainly boils in luxury 5 * hotels, where there are own bars and restaurants offering guests daily entertainment programs.

But to spend an unforgettable evening, smoothly flowing into the night, it is not necessary to live in a five-star hotel. Bars and restaurants along the coastline are ideal for this idea. By the way, food and drinks here are inexpensive, and as a bonus you get a breathtaking view of the night sea.

A popular place for both locals and tourists is the institution Siam Supper Club. Here you can enjoy a delicious cocktail and listen to jazz music.





Tinlay Place has the Pepper Bar . In this institution on the big screen you can watch the games of your favorite teams, as well as play billiards.

Entertainment

From November to April, Bang Tao Beach provides a wide range of services, including banana, water skiing, paragliding, and kitesurfing. But windsurfing is better to do in the northern part.





On the beach you can enjoy a relaxing Thai massage (cost of 1 hour - 350 baht) and make a manicure.

Children's entertainment: elephant and horse riding along the beach are located on the territory of Phuket Laguna Reading Club, located at the entrance to the Laguna complex.

Between May and October, sailing to Bang Tao Beach can be dangerous. Therefore, always check for the presence of a red flag indicating a danger. As a rule, it is installed at the exit from the Angsana Laguna Resort complex.

  4. Laguna Holiday Club Phuket Resort - is located in the heart of the tropical flora "Laguna Phuket". Nearby are golf courses. 5 * the hotel offers to settle in one of 79 comfortable rooms ranging from 65 to 113 square meters. m
  5. Angsana Laguna Phuket 4 * - for those who are looking for convenience. The hotel is located on an island surrounded by water and has direct access to Bang Tao Beach. In addition, hotel guests can enjoy all the limitless possibilities of the Laguna resort complex for free.

Bang Tao Beach (Phuket): traveler reviews

Despite the fact that the resort is far from cheap, reviews about it are extremely enthusiastic. Tourists noted that Bang Tao Beach combines everything that is needed for a good rest: entertainment, quality service, tasty and varied food, a clean beach, comfortable weather conditions for swimming and sunbathing.
