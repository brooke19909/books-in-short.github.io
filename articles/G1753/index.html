Spa hotels in Tallinn: overview, description, reviews

Tallinn is a beautiful city and a popular resort. On its territory many hotel complexes of different levels have been built. The most popular spa hotels in Tallinn. It is about such institutions that we want to tell in our article.

How to get to Tallinn?

Any tourist is interested in how to get from St. Petersburg to Tallinn. Despite the fact that Estonia has long been a separate country, getting to it from Russia is quite simple. There are several possible options - water, land and air.

The fastest way is a plane. There are direct flights from the Baltic Airlines to St. Petersburg - Tallinn. A ticket will cost an average of 14 thousand rubles. You can’t name an economical flight.

Travel by bus

The distance between cities is so small - 360 km - that bus transportation is the most popular. Tickets for Tallinn by bus from St. Petersburg can be purchased from many companies. Comfortable Lux Express, EcoLines, Simple Express and other buses run along this route. The cost of travel for different carriers is different. On average, a one-way ticket to Tallinn from St. Petersburg will cost from 1,700 rubles. There are many flights to Estonia daily, so tourists have a good choice.

You can also get to Tallinn by sea on the comfortable ferry Princess Anastasia. He flies every four days. The cost of the trip will cost more than 7 thousand rubles, depending on the level of the selected cabin.

The modern resort offers a variety of accommodation options for tourists. Nevertheless, spa hotels in Tallinn are the most popular. They are visited not only by spa residents, but also by the residents themselves, if they want to plunge into the world of spa treatments. In our article we want to review the hotel centers of the city.

If you want to live on the beach, you should pay attention to the Pirita Hotel. The institution is away from the hustle and bustle of the city, and offers a wide range of wellness and medical procedures, as well as cosmetic services. Near the hotel is the Pirita River, which adds a pristine romance to this place.

The three-star complex was built in 1980 in the medieval style, and in 2000 it was completely renovated. Attractions such as Tolstaya Margarita Tower and St. Church are just a 15-minute walk away. Olaf. The distance to the city center is only 5 kilometers.

The hotel consists of 267 apartments of various categories, which are equipped with air conditioning, wireless Internet, TVs, ironing sets, telephones, bathrobes, slippers and other amenities.

The hotel's restaurant serves breakfast and local specialties. The hotel also has a sports bar. The hotel's infrastructure includes parking, a gift shop, a business center and a currency exchange, an indoor pool, a gym, a tennis court, and a fitness center.

Tourists reviews

According to tourists, Pirita is a good spa hotel in Tallinn that deserves attention. A large heated pool makes it very attractive for guests. The rooms are designed in a minimalist style, but equipped with the most necessary. Delicious hearty breakfasts are praised by all visitors to the restaurant.

Near the complex is the coast and two beautiful parks with recreation areas. A bus stop near the hotel allows quick access to the center of Tallinn.

Kalev spa

Many tourists prefer hotels in the center of Tallinn. The old city is very attractive for guests, as cultural objects and shopping centers are located nearby . One such establishment is the Kalev Spa Waterpark. The hotel is a whole complex consisting of a hotel and a water center. It is worth noting that the water park is the largest in northern Estonia. It is noteworthy that hotel guests can use all water bodies, saunas, gyms during all the time of their stay without any restrictions. The price includes delicious and varied breakfasts that you can visit in the hotel restaurant. The buffet will not leave anyone indifferent.

The hotel's spa offers a wide variety of treatments for its visitors. The complex is convenient for tourists who are planning an excursion program in the city. Not far from the hotel is the Dome Cathedral and the Tallinn City Hall.

Kalev Spa Reviews

There are conflicting reviews about this spa hotel in Tallinn. According to tourists, the hotel offers comfortable rooms and decent service. Daily cleaning quality. Ample and tasty breakfasts are also on top.

But regarding the water center, guests have complaints. Visitors note that the water complex can hardly be called a water park. Rather, these are several pools with saunas, baths and spa treatments. The center is visited not only by hotel guests, but also residents of other hotels and local residents.

There is little entertainment for children in the water complex. But adults can swim, visit the baths and soak up the procedures or massage. On weekends, the water center is full, so it is better to visit it on weekdays.

Swissotel (Tallinn)

Near the city center is the Swissotel Tallinn, where you can experience real Swiss hospitality. The hotel is located in the camps of the most high-rise building of the city, from whose windows a stunning view of the Baltic Sea and the surroundings of the old quarters opens.

Tallinn Airport is a 15-minute drive away. The hotel consists of 238 elegant apartments and suites, which were equipped with the latest technology. The rooms will appeal to even the most demanding guests.

The administration prudently provides buffet breakfast for its guests. A wide range of dishes allows you to eat well at the beginning of the day. In addition, the hotel has two more exclusive restaurants and several bars.

The infrastructure of the complex includes an indoor heated pool, steam room, sauna and a good fitness club. The spa is a great place to relax and unwind. A wide range of procedures and massage allows not only to relax, but also just to have a good time. The hotel also has a large business center and banquet facilities. Luxurious interiors pleasantly delight guests.

Swissotel reviews (Tallinn)

Tourists highly value the modern hotel. In their opinion, the institution is worthy of close attention of business guests. The best venue for business events in the city is hard to find. The comfortable rooms are equipped with chic interiors and no less impressive views of Tallinn. The level of service staff is also on top.

Sometimes there are problems with the Internet. Russian-speaking staff is not always present at the reception. And this despite the fact that a third of the guests are Russians. The choice of dishes in restaurants is quite large. The hotel is in a very convenient location for exploring the old town.

The Von Stackelberg Hotel Tallinn

Guests who wish to stay in the Old Town can recommend The Von Stackelberg Hotel Tallinn. Not so long ago, the institution underwent major repairs and now offers guests accommodation in comfortable and very modern rooms.

The hotel is located within the walls of a historic building, built in 1874 as a home for a German aristocrat. The complex is located at the foot of Toompea Mountain, just a few steps from Alexander Nevsky Cathedral and Toompea Castle. In the process of repair work in order to preserve the historical appearance, limestone walls were preserved. Currently, the interiors present modern lighting elements with historical decor.

The room fund of the institution has been updated and presented with comfortable apartments. The rooms have air conditioning, tea and coffee sets, safes, televisions and telephones. The most luxurious apartments are equipped with a jacuzzi.

In the cozy restaurant, visitors can not only have breakfast, but also have a good time. The lobby bar serves delicious cocktails and other drinks. The highlight of the hotel can be called a spa complex, equipped with a Japanese pool treatment rooms.

The Von Stackelberg Hotel Tallinn reviews

According to tourists, the hotel makes a very good impression. Nice rooms and convenient location makes it very convenient to stay. All the sights of the city are within easy reach. The restaurant serves delicious meals. Regular guests praise the spa, which is worth a visit after long walks through the old streets.

Nordic Hotel Forum

Another hotel in the Old Town can be recommended to guests. This is the Nordic Hotel Forum (Tallinn). A comfortable establishment, thought out to the smallest detail, offers a good rest and entertainment. The number of rooms consists of 267 spacious apartments offering the best conditions. The bright rooms are equipped with everything you need for a comfortable stay. In addition to standard amenities, guests are selected incredibly high-quality mattresses, bedding and pillows. The rooms have air conditioning, televisions, safes, minibars and more. Among the apartments you can choose standards, business class, deluxe rooms and suites.

On the ground floor of the hotel is a wonderful restaurant "Monaco". Guests can enjoy a buffet in the morning. The restaurant menu offers an extensive set of dishes. There is a bar in the hotel lobby where you can not only order drinks, but also have a snack during the day.

The hotel provides business events and banquet facilities. The complex has a relaxation center and spa center, each of these institutions will give a lot of pleasant minutes.

Nordic Hotel Forum reviews

Tourists leave slightly conflicting reviews about the hotel. In general, the institution makes a very good impression, but it has certain shortcomings. The number of rooms needs to be updated, as traces of worn-out furniture are noticeable. The showers in the hotel apartments, which are not stalls, but simply a grate in the floor with a curtain, are not impressive either. Such non-modern bathrooms do not fit with the entire appearance of the hotel. The rooms are well isolated, extraneous sounds do not interfere with the rest.

Breakfast in the restaurant is quite decent and plentiful, the choice of buffet dishes is very impressive. You definitely won’t have to leave hungry. The reception staff speaks Russian, which is very convenient. The hotel service is at its best. Guests are highly recommended to visit the spa area. You are guaranteed to get a lot of pleasure, pleasantly and spend time with benefit.
