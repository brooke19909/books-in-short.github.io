How to get on the subway

Visitors to the shopping center should not have questions about how to get to Aviapark by public transport, since there are several options at once. Among the possible ones are taxis, buses, subways, and trolleybuses.

Metro is the most optimal. In 2018, a new metro line was opened for passengers. One of the stations was CSKA, which is located within walking distance to the Aviapark shopping center in the middle of the forested area.

How to get to the Aviapark shopping center by car

Bus as a public transport option

To the question of how to get to Aviapark by bus before the metro station was opened, the answer was unanimous - this is a free shuttle from Begovoy and Dynamo metro stations. However, subsequently, the traffic was changed and free routes were closed for guests of the shopping center. Nevertheless, the decision remains how to get to Aviapark by public buses and fixed-route taxis.

Directly in front of the entrance is the stop of the municipal minibus at number 318, next to the Dynamo metro station.

In the immediate walking distance from the shopping center there are several more stops where buses under the numbers 175 and 818 make an intermediate stop, which is another option for getting to Aviapark.
