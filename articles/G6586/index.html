Ecotropic "Ergaki"

The first journey begins at an altitude of 1300 meters above sea level. The route goes along the trail. Ecotourists enjoy landscapes of picturesque cliffs and alpine meadows. Peaks are clearly visible: Zvezdny (one of the two highest peaks of the natural park) and Bird. The business card of Ergakov is a calm, majestic massif Sleeping Sayan, reminiscent of a giant lying on its back. With imagination, one can discern the proud profile of the leader of the Indians.

Ahead - a cascade of emerald lakes, grasshoppers (stone rivers), narrow canyons of rivers, waterfalls. If you wish, you can come closer to the high mountain bogs, perennial snowfields that feed the fairly large rivers Amyl, Kazyrsuk, Oya, Us. Among the rare beauties, the following stand out:

  • Marble waterfall;
  • the huge Hanging Stone, "stuck" to the rock;
  • Light Lake, bordered by centuries-old cedars.
