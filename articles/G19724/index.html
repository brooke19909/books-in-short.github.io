Recreation facilities on the spit Nazimova - reviews. Rest in the Primorsky Territory

A unique place in Primorye is the Nazimov Spit. This is a narrow sandy strip stretching into the sea for five kilometers. The spit, on which the port of Posyet is located, divides two bays: Pallas and Expeditions.

It is especially good here in the summer. The sea is clean, without vegetation, with sandy beaches and gentle sloping into the water. Snakeheads and crucians are found in nearby ponds, and lotus blooms on the lakes.





Therefore, recreation centers on the Nazimov Spit in the Primorsky Territory are full of tourists all summer.

"Pearl"

This recreation center is located on the Nazimov Spit, just 20 meters from the sea. Designed for families with children (discounts are provided for them).

Accommodation in detached 4-bed houses with one double beds, a mirror, chairs, hangers, a kettle and a fridge. A canopy is attached near the entrance, under which benches and a table are installed.

On the territory of the base, sanitary blocks with toilets and showers (hot water around the clock) and several gazebos with barbecue areas are organized.

Food is not provided, you need to cook yourself. For this, kitchens with everything necessary are open - judging by the reviews, they are very convenient and cozy. Although under the shelter, the houses also have such an opportunity. And there are no shops nearby, so you need to take care of the products in advance.

Clean drinking water is brought to the base territory. Of the additional services, one can single out a permit (by prior agreement) for holidays with pets, seafood delivery and rental of tourist equipment. At the neighboring base there is the opportunity to order entertainment, including various excursions.





The complex operates only in summer, parking is free.

The recreation center "Quiet" on the Nazimov Spit

Located literally by the sea: ten meters. The houses are designed for two, three and four guests. Superior apartments have a kitchen area. Water (cold and hot) is brought into the houses, but toilets and showers are located in the base.

For your convenience, there are separate covered gazebos and places for cooking barbecue.

By prior agreement with the administration, pets are allowed to be brought to the base.

From entertainment, sightseeing trips are available, including to the Marine Reserve, as well as amazing fishing on a freshwater lake (this topic is most often discussed in reviews).

There is no dining room on the territory, you need to take care of food yourself.

As with all recreation centers, on the Nazimov Spit (Primorsky Territory) parking is free.

Ankar Pallada

The base offers three types of accommodation.

  1. VIP Large wooden cottage. Everything is organized in it for complete comfort: a refrigerator, air conditioning, upholstered furniture, double beds, tables, chairs, as well as cable TV. Bedding and cutlery are provided. There is an opportunity to buy one more sleeping place (a comfortable folding bed).
  2. Panel house. This is a free-standing apartment. There are three beds, a hanger, bedside tables, a table, a refrigerator and chairs. There is a canopy at the entrance.
  3. Economy class. Housing without any frills. Only three berths.




All amenities on the street, no dining room.

"At Mikhalych"

There is a recreation center on the Nazimov Spit, 50 meters from the sea. For accommodation, guests are provided with panel houses, lined with clapboard inside. The rooms have beds, chairs and a table, wardrobes.

There are luxury apartments for six guests. They have two rooms with three beds and a large veranda. Three-person houses with one extra bed and an enlarged terrace belong to the same category.

Toilets are rustic. In showers, water is street temperature, hot is served from six to eleven in the evening.

Administration "At Mikhalych" is happy to organize excursions and water activities for its guests.

The recreation center "Raid Pallas" on the Nazimov Spit

The houses are divided into three price categories.

First row.

  • Wooden quadruple apartment with a bedroom and a small kitchen area. It has a gas stove, table and chairs, as well as equipment for cleaning the room.

Near the house there is a canopy with a dining area.



  • Big log house. Designed for six guests plus extra bed. The room is single, divided into two parts. One has a two-story double bed, the other has a dining area. It has a sink, refrigerator, gas stove and countertop. Separately, there is a table and comfortable chairs. You can also dine on the covered veranda.

In the attic, which is raised from the second floor of the bed, there are two berths.

Double house. It has a large bed, plus one folding bed and a bedside table.

Second row.

Two-story wooden log cabin. Can accommodate eight guests. The first and second tiers are identical - a small bedroom and a kitchen. It has a gas hob, fridge and dining table with chairs. Entrance to the second floor from the street. There is also a covered porch with a dining area.

Third row.

Panel houses. Designed for three people (one extra seat). Indoors beds, chairs and a table. A fabric canopy is pulled near the entrance. Under it are benches and a table.

Bed linen, blankets, pillows and dishes for cooking and eating in all the houses at this recreation center (Nazimov spit) are issued upon arrival. Brazier can be obtained from the administration of the complex absolutely free. Toilets and showers are located at the base. In the houses there are sockets for charging mobile gadgets.

Refrigeration and freezing equipment is located in a specially designated area.





For regular customers there is a system of discounts (reviews confirm this information). When renting a house, the rules of residence allow the installation of one tent (for a nominal additional fee).

For outdoor enthusiasts, a volleyball court and a playground for children are organized. Water in a common washbasin in street temperature. In the evening, hot water is supplied to the showers.

Azure Harbor

This recreation center on the Nazimov Spit positions itself as a summer town. On its territory, guests are provided with small panel houses for 3-4 people. The apartment has one metal-plastic window (with mandatory mosquito net), beds, hangers, a mirror, a table and chairs. Bed linen is provided upon check-in. Toilet and showers (with hot and cold water) on site. A volleyball court and a children's playground are equipped for guests.

A store is open at the base, there is the opportunity to order seafood. The administration assists in organizing fishing and trips to protected islands. In walking distance there is a rental point for water equipment.

Marble

Housing on this base is of two categories. Wooden log houses for two or three people and panel three-four-person houses. The apartments have plastic windows with shutters, sockets for recharging gadgets and modern furniture.





For a comfortable pastime, a bathhouse, a volleyball field and a playground are organized.

The administration of the recreation center "Marble" on the Nazimov Spit rents a boat for fishing or boat trips, as well as a rowing boat, scooter and other equipment.

"Sandy shore"

Located just 20 meters from the sea. Triple houses with a large shadow canopy near the entrance. A tent or tent can be installed next to them. Inside the apartment there are 1.5 and 2 double beds, a mirror, a table and chairs. Bed linen is provided upon check-in.

A toilet and showers with hot and cold water at the recreation center "Sandy beach" (Nazimov spit) are located on the territory.

Parking is free.
