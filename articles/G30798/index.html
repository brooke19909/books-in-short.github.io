A bit of history

A choker is the name of a neck jewelry that fits snugly against it. He came into fashion during the reign of Queen Victoria of England. Then it was made of stones, leather and other materials. At some point, the choker was completely forgotten until the hippie generation revived this interest. However, jewelry acquired a slightly different character.

Then tattoos were in fashion. But not everyone was ready to make them for themselves. The pattern of thin fishing line looked like a tattoo, and the girls took up their weaving. Choker tattoos were in fashion in the nineties and zero years. Today, their relevance is back again. Of course, they are not suitable for respectable business people. But show off on the street or in a fun company will be nice and interesting.

So, let's proceed directly to how to weave a choker tattoo. Let's start by preparing everything you need.

Materials for decoration

Before weaving a tattoo choker, you need to cook everything you need for this.

The main thing that is required is a fishing line or any elastic cord. Often a synthetic spandex thread is used as the material. In addition, metal beads are needed to fix the jewelry on the back. But this is not even necessary, since it can simply be tied into a knot.

Previously, everyone preferred a fishing line of dark color. But today they often use colored ones, adding different color beads. In the 21st century, it became fashionable to use headphone wiring for jewelry. However, they must be very thin, otherwise the choker will turn out to be too rude.

We also prepare a clip, which can be purchased at any stationery store, beads for fixing and tweezers. But if they are not at hand, it does not matter, you can do with a regular book for clamping, and instead of beads tie a knot.

Learn how to weave a tattoo choker

You can use two or even more lines if you want to get a more voluminous picture. To determine the desired length of the fishing line, wrap it around the neck three times. Cut one and the second of the same piece.

  1. First we make a loop of one thread.
  2. Then we wrap the second thread around the free end of the first thread.
  3. Actions should be repeated from one side, then from the other side sequentially.
  4. The result is two free ends. A crimp ball is pulled in them and flattened so that it does not bloom. In the absence of a ball, the ends are simply tied.




That's the whole master class. Now you know how to weave a tattoo choker, and you can add your own notes of fantasy to it.

What to wear a choker with?

Given the huge variety of chokers today, they are picked up and worn with different outfits. The exception is, perhaps, a strict classic and business style of clothing.

This decoration can be called a bold decision. It attracts attention, therefore it is very important to maintain a single style so that the choker blends harmoniously with the clothes. It can not be called a universal accessory. But chosen correctly, he perfectly emphasizes the individuality of his owner.





Chokers made of fishing line, steel, wire and leather look great with jeans, tops, shirts and leather jackets. For a romantic style, with dresses, blouses and blouses, fabric accessories with pendants, beads and rhinestones look cute. In the evening, with a deep neckline, long pendants are perfect. Fans of punk and rock also can not do without a choker.
