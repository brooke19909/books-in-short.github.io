Margarita Suite Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Alanya): photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Every year, hundreds of thousands of tourists from all over the world come to Alanya (Turkey). Our compatriots are no exception. This region attracts travelers with excellent beaches, a clean warm sea, a lot of entertainment and the availability of inexpensive but comfortable hotels. If you decide to spend your summer holidays here, then as an option for accommodation, consider the four-star Margarita Suite Hotel. Next we will get to know this hotel complex more closely.





Location

This hotel is located almost in the center of the main resort city of the region - Alanya. The distance to the shores of the Mediterranean Sea is a little less than a kilometer. All the main attractions of the city can be reached in leisurely steps in just 20 minutes. In the vicinity of the hotel there are lots of shops, a market, souvenir shops and other tourist infrastructure facilities.

Transport

The nearest international airport to Margarita Suite Hotel 4 * is located in Antalya. The distance to it is 120 kilometers, and the journey takes about two hours. Gazipasha Air Harbor is also a 30-minute drive from the hotel complex. But this airport accepts only domestic flights. If you buy a ticket through a tour operator, after arrival you will most likely be provided with a transfer to the hotel. If you book yourself, you can get to the hotel by bus or taxi. In addition, it is possible to book a transfer directly to Margarita Suite for an additional fee.





Photo and description of the hotel

Margarita Suite Hotel was launched in 1998. Recent reconstruction work was carried out here in 2011. Also, many tourists who visited here last summer, note a fresh repair in the rooms. Since Margarita Suite is an urban-style hotel, its own territory is small. However, there are four residential buildings, three swimming pools for adults and one ‚Äúpaddling pool‚Äù for the youngest travelers. The housing stock of the hotel complex consists of 143 studio apartments and one-bedroom rooms. On the hotel territory there are: restaurant, bar, parking, shop, laundry. Food is provided on an all-inclusive basis.

Rooms

As already mentioned, Margarita Suite Hotel 4 * has 143 rooms located in four residential buildings. All are equipped with their own air conditioning, TV, safe, fridge, bathroom, kitchenette with necessary utensils and a balcony. Some apartments have Wi-Fi. Rooms are cleaned daily. Sheets and towels are updated several times a week. If necessary, you can order room service.





Prices

As for the cost of living, many tourists considered it very attractive and appropriate to the conditions of accommodation. So, a seven-day stay in this hotel in July will cost you 330 euros (if you choose a studio) or 415 euros (if you book an apartment with one bedroom). Please note that the prices shown do not include meals.

The staff at Margarita Suite Hotel reviews earned for the most part positive. Impressions of a beach holiday and entertainment

Many tourists in their reviews note the fact that the distance from Margarita Suite Appart Hotel 4 * to the beach is not 450 meters (as stated in most hotel descriptions), but all 900 meters. Therefore, this fact should be noted. A bus runs several times a day along the hotel-beach-hotel route, so you can use it if you wish. However, many tourists preferred to overcome this path on foot. On the shore of small pebbles. The beaches are clean and well maintained, the water is clear. There are practically no waves here. Sun beds and umbrellas can be rented at an additional cost. But many travelers bought umbrellas and mats in one of the shops and were located on a free section of the beach for free.

As for other entertainment, there is no animation in Margarita Suite Hotel. Once a week, a Turkish night with live music, belly dancing and fire shows is held on the poolside terrace.
