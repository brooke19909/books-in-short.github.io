Ufa hotels: photos, their features and reviews

The capital of Bashkortostan Ufa is a city attractive for tourists. Also, many people come here for business trips. So that you can relax comfortably after a busy day, Ufa hotels will offer you their services and furnished rooms.

Hotel Azimut

On the main street of the capital of Bashkortostan, October Avenue, the four-star Azimut Hotel is located. Near the hotel there is a central city park. Walking distance to the Russian Academic Drama Theater, Bayram supermarket, cafes and restaurants. The hotel's restaurant uses fresh, seasonal produce to prepare meals. Parking space is paid extra. The hotel has an ATM, vending machines with snacks and drinks. To services of visitors - a library, a fitness center, a gift shop. To accommodate guests, Azimut Hotel in Ufa offers the following room categories:

  • double standard - 2464 rubles per day, if one person lives, the fee will be 2024 rubles;

  • SMART Junior Suite - 4224 rubles, for one person, the fee will be 3784 rubles;
  • Apartments - 4800 rubles per day.

The price includes breakfast, which is served in the hotel buffet. If desired, you can rent a room without breakfast. Free Wi-Fi access is available.

Hilton Hotel

Majestic views of the city and the monument to Salavat Yulaev, which is located five hundred meters from the hotel, come from the windows of the Hilton Hotel in Ufa. You will also need a few minutes to get to the Congress Hall. You can get acquainted with the history of Bashkortostan in the Museum of Archeology and Ethnography, which is also located nearby. There is a playground for children on the street. Parking is paid, there are special parking spaces for people with disabilities. The hotel has a mini-market, an ATM. There is a restaurant and bar, guests can use the services of a fitness center. Among the hotels in Ufa, the Hilton Hotel is one of the most expensive. Accommodation in a double room will cost guests 4480 rubles per day with breakfast and 3360 rubles without breakfast. The cost of a junior suite will be from 12,400 to 14,000 rubles, depending on the number of guests (breakfast is included). There is access to free Wi-Fi.

"The president"

When considering options for accommodation among Ufa hotels, pay attention to the President Hotel, located in the center of the capital of Bashkortostan. Exhibition Center "VDNH-Expo" is located just 10 minutes from it. There are two supermarkets within walking distance. The rooms are well soundproofed and each has a balcony. The restaurant presents Russian, Bashkir and European cuisines. Lunch and dinner are accompanied by live music. Free parking, car wash and tour desk services are provided. Use of the pool and sauna is also free. There are vending machines on site, a playground for children, car rental is available. A breakfast buffet is served in the mornings. There is access to free wireless internet.

Ufa hotels. Hotel "Atola"

Hotel "Atola" has a category of 3 stars. The sports and concert complex Ufa-Arena is a five-minute drive from the hotel. Within walking distance there are also two museums, an opera and ballet theater. Parking at the Atola Hotel is free of charge. There is a restaurant. There is an ATM on site, bike rental and a gift shop are available. Several categories of rooms are offered for accommodation:

  • double standard - 3900 rubles per day;
  • Suite - 11,000 rubles per day;
  • apartments 25 000 rubles for one day.

Breakfast is included. There is free Wi-Fi.

Hotel complex "Bashkiria"

The hotel complex "Bashkiria" is one of those hotels in Ufa, near which there are a large number of attractions. Nearby there is a supermarket, a coffee club. The hotel's restaurant serves Bashkir and European cuisines, and a daily breakfast buffet is served in the morning. The complex has an ATM, parking is free. The cost of living is quite high.
