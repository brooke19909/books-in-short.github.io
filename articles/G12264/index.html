What you need to know when planning a vacation in Livadia, Primorsky Territory

Everyone knows Livadia with its palace, which is located near Yalta on the southern coast of Crimea. But few people know that this resort village has a namesake in the Primorsky Territory of Russia. Rather, this settlement in the Far East was named so because of indirect competition with the Crimean. Say, Livadia Bay in the Primorsky Territory lies at the same geographical latitude as the South Coast. Check if this is so. And what kind of rest is there? How developed is the infrastructure of the Far Eastern village to host holidaymakers? We are investigating this issue as well. Here you will find a brief overview of the recreation centers in the village. We will also talk about the climatic features of the Primorsky Territory. And how to get to Livadia? What is there to see? You will learn about all this from our article.

This village is located in Primorye near the city of Nakhodka. Rather, since 2004 it has been its area. Holidays in Livadia of the Primorsky Territory has several advantages. The first is easy accessibility. From the center of Nakhodka, the resort can be reached in just 50 minutes. Every hour there goes a city bus number 122. Residents of Vladivostok, from which Livadia is separated by 154 kilometers, have the opportunity to come to the resort with a direct bus that leaves daily. Motorists will easily reach the coastal village, moving along the highway from Nakhodka to Uglovoye, and near the village of Dushkino, turning south. From Vladivostok, getting to the seaside resort is even easier. After all, the A188 highway goes through it. The village itself is small. Only 18 thousand people live in it. But every summer 30,000 tourists visit it.



When to go to Livadia of Primorsky Territory

It is important to know the climatic features of this place. As it turned out, geographical latitude is not the main thing. The more southern Primorsky Livadia has a much more severe climate than its Crimean namesake. And all because of the cold current. It causes fogs and drizzle even in June and the first half of July. The cold sea warms up only to +20 degrees even in summer. But in the bay of Livadia - shallow water. Therefore, comfortable bathing temperatures are set there + 23-24 degrees. The best period for a holiday in Livadia of the Primorsky Territory is August. Autumn (September and early October) is also a good time. It is warm, clear weather. But gusty northerly winds can reduce the temperature from +20 to zero in just a couple of days. In addition, the Primorsky Territory is a zone of passage of typhoons. So in the summer, they can ruin your vacation.



What attracts tourists Livadia (Primorsky Territory)

Photos of this resort demonstrate that the places here are very beautiful. The village is surrounded by hills covered with taiga. And on the shore stretched a series of beautiful sandy beaches. There is one and pebble - with snow-white stones, ironed by the sea. The coast here is rugged. In the bays under the names Srednyaya, Rifovaya, Gaydamak there are recreation centers of Livadia in the Primorsky Territory. The tourist infrastructure of the village is well developed. There are many types of water activities on the shore. There is a market, shops, cafes, pharmacies, ATMs and an Orthodox church. In between beach vacations, you can visit the dolphinarium "Center for the Adaptation of the Mammals of the Sea" in Srednaya Bay. There is even a museum in the village, the exposition of which tells about the whaling past of Livadia. Tourists are also attracted by a freshwater lake located in close proximity to the sea.



Recreation centers Livadia in the Primorsky Territory

Previously, there were only a few institutions in the village that hosted organized tourists. Also functioned (and continue to work) children's camps. Recently, the supply of housing has grown significantly. There are new mini-hotels that offer vacationers spacious rooms with all amenities, cottages or simple houses of the economy segment. Among the year-round recreation facilities, one can recommend the "Dream", "Rainbow" and "Estate". The first two offer full board meals. Most recreation centers operate only in the summer months and in September. What were the prices for holidays in Livadia in 2017? If you book accommodation in advance, then you can find discounts up to 40 percent.
