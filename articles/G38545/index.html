What you should know when choosing a vacation in Egypt

  • Do not save on food options.
  • It is strictly forbidden to drink tap water, wash your hands and fruit with it - the commandment of a tourist for a safe holiday in Egypt.
  • Be sure to have special shoes for entering the sea to avoid injuring your legs.
  • It is strictly forbidden to take into the hands of unknown mollusks, other representatives of marine flora and fauna, let alone export them from Egypt.
  • When bathing in the early days, it is recommended to use clothing that covers open areas of the body, in order to avoid severe sunburn. The sun in Egypt is quite aggressive.
  • You should think carefully before ordering excursion services at local offices. The quality and safety conditions in Egypt leave much to be desired.

Compliance with these and other rules of safe rest abroad and in Egypt in particular will allow you to fully enjoy all the advantages of foreign tours and preserve your memories until your next trip.
