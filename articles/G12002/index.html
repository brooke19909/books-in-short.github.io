How to make a rose out of a satin ribbon: tips

In order to decorate your home, you do not have to buy expensive designer items. You can show a little imagination and try to do it yourself. This article talks about how to make a rose out of a satin ribbon .

In every house, somewhere, a piece of this brilliant basma is littered, you can buy it in fabric and accessories stores. It should be noted that it can be of any width, it depends on what you want to get the final result, for example, from a narrow ribbon you will get small buds.



Before you make a rose from a satin ribbon, prepare your workplace. To do this, you need threads in Basme tone, needles, scissors, matches or a lighter. Another nuance is that such roses come in several forms: twisted, pleated, stockroza. Depending on which bud you want, large or not, you need to take a wide or narrow ribbon. We will tell you how to make a small rose out of a satin ribbon.



For her, you can take a tape two and a half centimeters wide. First you need to carefully burn the edges so that the basma does not crumble. The required length is recognized by multiplying its width by twenty. In order to make a rose bud, you need to wrap the edge of the ribbon at an angle of forty-five degrees. Then roll it two times five millimeters. Further, for your convenience, to wrap the bud in tape, hold it in your left hand, and with your right hand, bend the end of the tape at an angle of thirty degrees. So twisting it around the bud, continue until it runs out, or until you get a rose of the right size. In order to be more accurate, occasionally correct it with your fingers, then a beautiful flower will form. After you have finished, you need to fix your rose with a few stitches. Small buds look very nice if there are several pieces nearby.

How to make a rose from a large satin ribbon? For this you need a wide basma. For this type of buds, the tape is not immediately cut to save. As last time, its edge must be treated with a lighter. Fold the basma in half. Hold the crease in your left hand and the rest of the tape in your right. Next, fold its lower tail at an angle of ninety degrees relative to the outside and bend it up. You fold the accordion, so to speak. Repeat these steps until you get a rose of the right size. Holding the ends of the tape, release the rest of the tape. Now, holding one edge, pull the other - this is how the rose forms. To prevent it from breaking up, secure it with a thread.



Jewelry made of satin ribbons is very often used as a decoration for clothes. For example, you can diversify children's clothes with small buds, it will look new and original. Especially now that you know how to make a rose out of a satin ribbon, and it will not be difficult for you to create it. This type of decor can be used to decorate hairstyles. For example, after you have made a bud, take an elastic band and pin it with a special sticker. No one will have such an accessory!
