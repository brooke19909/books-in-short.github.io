How to find out the balance on PrivatBank card without using an ATM

Today, PrivatBank is a leading Ukrainian bank, and its advantages over others lie not only in its leading positions. The Bank has at its disposal a wide infrastructure, so that its customers can get what they need as quickly as possible. In addition, the financial institution provides its users with extensive SMS and Internet banking capabilities, with which you can find out the balance on the PrivatBank card, as well as perform many other operations without leaving your home.

Call the service center

Perhaps the most affordable way to take advantage of the additional features of PrivatBank is to call the service center at the toll free number 3700. You can find out the balance on the PrivatBank card in the service center by calling from the telephone number that was specified during registration. After connecting using the voice prompts of the answering machine, you need to select the item ‚ÄúCheck account status‚Äù. And then enter the last 4 digits of the 16-digit card number whose balance you want to find out. After that, the answering machine will notify you of the balance in your account and other information about it.



A service center is the fastest way to find out the balance on a PrivatBank card. In addition, here you can contact the operator and ask him questions of interest to you, or if necessary, block the card. For example, if you lost her. The main condition for using these services is that you must make a call from a number associated with your card.

SMS banking

If for any reason the above method does not suit you, then an SMS banking system has been developed for this. How to find out the balance of PrivatBank card via SMS? To do this, you just need to send an SMS to number 10060, in the text of which you need to indicate the last 4 digits of 16 indicated on the front side of your "plastic". It costs such an SMS as a regular message, and PrivatBank does not charge any additional fees. By sending an SMS, in a few minutes you will receive a response message in which information about the balance of money on the card will be provided.



Also, using SMS banking, you can replenish the account of any mobile operator in Ukraine, transfer funds to another card and even change the PIN code of your card. You can get a complete list of commands by sending the word HELP to number 10060. However, some basic commands are indicated on the back of most PrivatBank cards. It remains only to recall that this service will only work if the phone number from which you are going to send SMS is tied to your "plastic".

How to find out the balance of PrivatBank card via the Internet

PrivatBank provides the widest opportunities for self-management of an account to users of Internet banking. The "Privat24" service is located at privat24.ua, and for a start you need to register in it. Here you can not do without the phone number attached to your card. Since each time you connect to the service for security, you will need to enter a confirmation code, which will come to your mobile phone in the form of SMS.



If you are having difficulty using this service, you can use the help of an online consultant or contact the service center operator at 3700.

Security

You probably already managed to pay attention to the fact that in order to use the above services, you must have a phone number associated with your card. This is a kind of key to the services of the bank. And with its help there is an opportunity not only to find out the balance on the PrivatBank card, but also to transfer or withdraw funds from it, so be careful and try to limit the access of unauthorized persons to your mobile.

Using PrivatBank services is easy and very convenient, because they were developed in the age of computer technology and optimized so that everyone can handle them.
