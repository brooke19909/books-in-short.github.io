You can get to Natura Park Beach Ecoresort Spa from Punta Cana International Airport in thirty minutes (16 kilometers away). It should be recalled that visitors from those countries with which the Dominican Republic has a visa-free regime established need only a tourist card. Get her at the airport.



Rooms : The beach complex has 510 rooms and apartments distributed in thirteen two-story buildings. In the decoration of each of them used organic materials. Spacious rooms with a balcony or terrace are designed so that guests can enjoy the surrounding landscapes - the sea, lagoon-style pools, a beautiful garden. Standard amenities include air conditioning, cable TV, minibar and fridge, safe and telephone, iron and ironing board.

Beach : It is a site equipped with thatched huts, umbrellas and sun loungers on the stunningly beautiful Playa Bavaro with soft and white sand washed by the aquamarine waters of the warm Caribbean Sea. Internet access in rooms and public areas is available at an additional cost.
