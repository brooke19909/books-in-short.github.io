Coral Beach Rotana Resort Montazah 4

Description: Resort Coral Beach Rotana Resort Montazah, 4 * was built in 1999. The hotel has an unusually convenient location. It is only four kilometers from the international airport and ten minutes from the famous Naama Bay. For tourists, it will not be difficult for a short period of time to get to the city center, which is replete with chic restaurants, nightclubs, and various shops.





Rooms: Hotel Coral Beach Rotana Resort Montazah, 4 * consists of several two- and three-story buildings, in which tourists are offered to stay different types of rooms: 238 standard, four Suites and six apartments. The rooms have all the amenities needed for relaxation: wide soft beds, a TV, a telephone with which you can reach anywhere, air conditioning, a large refrigerator, a bathroom, a powerful hairdryer, a safe for valuable items and papers, as well as mini a bar where travelers will always find a variety of soft drinks. Housekeeping is provided daily.

Meals: On the territory of Coral Beach Rotana Resort Montazah, 4 * guests will find a cozy Mermaid restaurant. In it, a caring and friendly staff offers its customers to try international dishes, which are prepared from the highest quality products. At the Aquarius Pool, Red Lion and Palms Beach bars, guests will enjoy a wide range of alcoholic and soft drinks, as well as mouth-watering snacks. At Moka Cafe, anyone can enjoy delicious cakes and enjoy a delicious cup of tea or coffee.





Beach: Just a hundred meters from Coral Beach Rotana Resort Montazah, 4 * there is a spacious sandy beach. On it, any tourist can use sun loungers and umbrellas for free.

Information for vacationers: Everyone living in the Coral Beach Rotana Resort Montazah has the opportunity to visit a conference room for up to fifty people, an Internet cafe, an outdoor pool, a jacuzzi, a spa, aerobics classes, a sauna, a Turkish bath, and a beauty salon . Within the hotel area you can freely play volleyball, billiards, tennis and table tennis. Guests will be offered plenty of water activities, including snorkeling, diving and much more. For an additional fee, the hotel organizes a variety of excursion programs.

Upon request, any client can enjoy the services of a massage therapist, doctor, nanny, ironing, laundry and dry cleaning.
