DIY panel: ideas, materials, master classes

Thinking of decorating your interior? Make a panel yourself. What can be used as a material for creativity? Yes, anything. You can make a beautiful picture from coffee beans, buttons or leftover skin. Look for ideas for inspiration as well as workshops below.

Money Tree

Have you accumulated a lot of little things? You can make a money tree out of it. This panel will be easy to do with your own hands, but it will look very impressive. If you believe in the teachings of Feng Shui, you can even give the picture a sacred meaning. If you strongly believe that the panel will make you richer, perhaps this is exactly what will happen. It is not in vain that they say so much that thoughts materialize.

How to make such a picture? First of all, you need to build a foundation. The easiest way to do this is to knock together three small boards. Or you can take a whole sheet of plywood. Now you need to draw a sketch. When this stage is completed, arm yourself with a trifle and a glue gun, and create a crown. To make the picture look more interesting, you can plant an application of owls or butterflies on a tree. The barrel must be made of acrylic paste. Be sure to depict ground or grass under a tree. You can complement the composition with various flowers and birds.

Button Panel

If you draw poorly, then you should not think that you will not be able to make interesting decor for the apartment. Show your imagination. Do-it-yourself panel can be made very simple, but to realize the idea effectively. For example, lay out a heart of buttons. As a basis for such a panel, you need to take the canvas. Pull it onto a tablet or subframe, and then tint the fabric with strips. To do this, you can use both oil and regular gouache mixed with glue. We draw the contour of the heart. You need to place it not in the middle of the canvas, but a little with a shift up. In the lower part, you can then make an inscription, but even if you do not plan to do this, you still need to raise your heart slightly above the center. Otherwise, visually your picture will not be balanced. When the silhouette is ready, you need to fill it with red buttons of various shapes and sizes. You can make a fabric backing in the form of the base of the heart so that the striped canvas is not visible.

Couple in love

One of the simplest ideas for making panels with your own hands is to draw a picture and supplement it with paper applications. You only need to come up with a plot. If you do not know how to draw, then you can print the outline of the picture, cut it and circle. The panel will be made on a stretcher, on which a sheet of Whatman paper should be pulled. In its lower part we place a silhouette and paint it with ink. Why ink, and not a marker? Ink does not leave streaks, but ugly strips remain from the marker, and it can also deform the surface of the sheet if you press the felt-tip pen too hard. Now you need to cut hearts from colored paper in pastel colors. Make blanks of different sizes, and fold them in half. Now hearts need to be glued with one side to the panel. We begin to attach the blanks to the upper left corner, and move down diagonally. It remains to draw thin black lines that will visually connect the application and the drawing.

Ballerinas

Is your daughter keen on ballet? Then she will definitely appreciate your do-it-yourself work. The panel on the wall will depict two dancing ballerinas. These will be ink drawings, supplemented with small cuts of tulle. Where to start making crafts? You need to find a stretcher of a suitable size and pull a piece of paper on it. Now you need to apply the image to the sheet. If you draw two ballerinas, be sure to make figures of different sizes. Now paint the contours with black ink. If the layout is unsuccessful, then it is not necessary to start work again. You can simply draw some interesting decorative element in the empty space of the sheet, or make an inscription. Cut two rectangles from tulle and sew them on one side with the needle seam forward. Assemble and attach the skirt as shown in the picture above. Bright panels that can be inserted into the hands of ballerinas, as well as plastic flowers and bows, will help to complement the panel. They can decorate the girl's hairstyle and her skirt.

Peonies

A beautiful mural on the wall with your own hands can be made of fabric, nails, thread and boards. First you need to build a base. Knock down two boards together. Now draw a vase outline on them. In its upper part flowers will be located. How to make peonies with your own hands? A panel of fabric will be made as follows. You draw a petal pattern, and then transfer it to the material. For this purpose, you should choose something subtle, but at the same time holding a shape. Ideal for nylon. If you don‚Äôt have the necessary material, you can starch the one that is. Cut the petals, and then use the hot gun to collect them into buds. Remember that the flowers must be different. Some do more, others do less. Sticks of flowers can be made from wooden skewers wrapped in cloth. Leaves can be made from thick cotton. Take the small nails and drive them into the boards so that they form the silhouette of a vase or can. Glue the flowers to the base, and then wind the threads on the nails.

A pineapple

An interesting panel of threads with your own hands can be done in a matter of hours. All you need to make this craft is the base, nails and threads. As a substrate, it is desirable to use a dense piece of wood. To make it look presentable, you can tint it with varnish or treat it with stain. Now you need to draw a pineapple based on it, and then drive in the nails, as if bordering the image. Be sure to make a jumper between the leaves and the fruit itself. Now you need to take the Iris threads and wrap them in a chaotic manner so that a dense net forms. First, create the bottom of the pineapple, and then, by analogy, the top.

Honeycombs

How to make a panel of fabric or paper in the form of hexagons fashionable this season? This craft can be made in just an hour. You don‚Äôt even have to make much effort. All you have to do is cut the pieces. You can combine different materials. For example, use mesh fabric, shiny paper, and satin ribbons. Cut a lot of blanks. Then you need to make a base. You can take a wooden panel or pull canvas or paper onto a stretcher. Now with the glue gun you need to attach the parts. Here you can fantasize. For example, create a thick mesh at the base, and add more air between the parts above. Or make the top of the panel glossy and the bottom matte.

Shells

If you are very fond of the sea and go to relax in warm countries every year, then probably you have gathered a lot of unusual souvenirs at your place. Observing people, lying on the shore, manage to find interesting shells in the sand. It's a pity to throw them away, so you have to take them home. You can decorate your apartment with such souvenirs, and it is not necessary to put finds on the shelf, you can make a panel of shells. With your own hands to build such an unusual decor will be very simple. After all, it is not necessary to add a complex composition. You can beautifully place the shells on the canvas and stick them in this position. But what if this version of the picture seems too simple? A panel of shells with your own hands can be made very original. For example, you can lay out the outline of a mermaid, make a seascape, or create a picture of the sea shore. Imagine.

Hemp

You can make a decorative panel with your own hands from anything. It is only necessary to show imagination. For example, you can be inspired by nature. If you like to walk through the woods, you can take a saw with you on one of these walks. If you take a long walk, you can definitely find a fallen tree. That is what you need. Spray it on small stumps. From them you can create a variety of shapes that will become the future panel. Before making crafts from hemp, you need to dry the tree well. If desired, you can paint the sections with varnish if you want them to shine, or cover them with stain if you want the tree to get a darker shade. What can be made of hemp? You can create any silhouette. For example, a heart, smiley, wave, spiral, leaf outline, branch or tree. Fasten hemp with each other using wedges. The glue will not hold the heavy structure well.

Leaves

In order to make a panel of leather with your own hands , you will not need either talent or skill. Everything is elementary simple. A craft is made of old things. Cloaks, boots, gloves, or a bag can be used up. You will need to cut straight layers from these things. It is advisable to take skin of different colors. Of course, the panel can be painted, but it is better to immediately take the appropriate material. Now you need to draw a sketch. When there is a clear picture in your head of what you are going to do now, you can begin to work. Take a piece of skin and bend it according to the drawing. Heat the room over the candle flame for several seconds. Cool and proceed to the next crease. Thus, you need to create the whole picture. Fasten leather cuts to the base with a glue gun.

Succulents

If you often make crafts from felt, then you probably should have unnecessary pieces left. In this case, you can make a panel of felt with your own hands. Such an interior decoration will be highly appreciated by your friends. You need to choose the theme of your work based on what color the material is at your disposal. If you have green felt in excess, then you can make panels by depicting succulents that are fashionable today. To assemble the craft, you need to draw a pattern. Draw the leaves, and then cut them out of the material. Now you need to collect cactus plants. Petals need to be sewn together. This is the most reliable way to fasten felt. You can make succulents of different colors and sizes, and then sew them to the canvas and insert this composition into the frame. A panel of felt with your own hands can be considered ready.

Panel of branches

Want to decorate your interior? Do-it-yourself panel master class is presented above. You can assemble such a design in an hour. All you need is branches and paint. You can go with a pruner to the forest or get to the nearest park. Find small torn or dried branches there and cut them. It is advisable to choose branches of the same thickness, and, of course, pay attention to the aesthetic appearance of your material. At home, it will be necessary to dry the cut shoots well. Now you can do drawing. It is necessary to make a template. In our case, this is the heart, you can make a star, a cloud, in general, everything that a vivid imagination tells you. Now fill the template with the branches and cut them to a stencil. It remains to toned the ends of the sticks. Each part must be tied to a thread and put on a common bar, the role of which can be played by a large branch. You can make a solid base for such a decorative panel. With your own hands, things made for the interior are always nice to contemplate in the apartment.
