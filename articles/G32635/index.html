What is a grace period?

Grace period is a time interval during which customers can use a credit card and not pay interest to the bank. At Sberbank, it does not exceed 50 days. If during this period the owner pays for the purchases and manages to make 100% of the amount spent, the bank does not charge interest for use.

Many customers do not know whether it is possible to replenish Sberbank's credit card with parts during the grace period. A financial institution does not impose restrictions on frequency or amounts. Customers have the right to deposit at any time and in any convenient way.

How to calculate the grace period so as not to pay interest?

In order not to overpay for using the bank‚Äôs funds, you need to know when and how to replenish your Sberbank credit card account. To do this, you must be able to calculate the beginning and end of the grace period.

In the calculation, the reporting and payment date are used. The reporting day is the day of issuing a credit card. Payment Date - This is the time you made the purchase.

To maximize the period (50 days), it is recommended to pay by credit card on the date of the report or immediately after it. For example, if you pay 5 days after, it decreases by 5 days, if the purchase is made 14 days later than the report, the period will be 36 days (50 - 14 = 36).

Why payment may not reach?

When repaying a credit card limit of Sberbank, the owner must carefully check the details of the operation, even if it was carried out in the bank through the operator.

There are several reasons why the money did not reach the account of the owner of the credit card.

  1. Incorrect data. Invalid account number, card, bank details.
  2. Incorrect operation, for example, making cash without confirmation, as a result of which the money was returned to the carrier.
  3. The presence of large debt. If the interest for using the funds exceeds the amount of the contribution, the owner of the credit card will not see the balance change. All funds will be used to repay interest, according to the priority of the loan agreement.
  4. The technical error. In this case, the client needs to keep a check indicating the amount, date and time of the transaction. The document is presented at the place of service. For example, if the Sberbank ATM accepted, but did not credit the credit card, the payer must file a claim at the bank‚Äôs office.

The term for resolving the issue takes from 1 to 30 days. In 90% of cases, money is credited within 48 hours after the user's request.
