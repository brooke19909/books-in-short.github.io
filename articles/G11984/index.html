Hostel "Corn", Tver: address, description, reviews and photos

Many tourists at least once face the question - choose an apartment or hostel to stay. In Tver, you can find many apartments, hotels and mini-hotels.

Hostel "Corn" (Tver), how to get to which will be described below, offers comfortable beds at low prices. Guest reviews indicate that the hotel has an excellent value for money service.

Overview

Hostel "Corn" in Tver is famous among hotels for the fact that it was visited by the famous transfer program "Inspector". The host was satisfied with the quality of service and the sanitary condition of the rooms.

The mini-hotel has a special atmosphere of comfort and sincerity. The hotel is located in a multi-storey building and occupies only a few rooms. However, it can comfortably accommodate 40 guests. For groups of more than 10 people, a discount on accommodation is provided here.

In the kitchen, you should wash the dishes after yourself. You cannot take out kitchen utensils into rooms, just like food. It is worth buying food that is stored in the refrigerator for a limited amount. After all, there should be enough space in it for everyone.

On the territory of the hostel, in rooms and sanitary rooms you can not smoke and drink alcohol. Also, while intoxicated, guests are not allowed into the mini-hotel.
