Hotel "Sunrise", Komsomolsk-on-Amur: description, address, reviews, how to get?

If you are looking for the best accommodation option in Komsomolsk-on-Amur, the Voskhod hotel will surely appeal to you. Friendly staff, comfortable accommodation and a host of additional services will make your stay at the hotel complete.

A bit about the city

Komsomolsk-on-Amur is a city on the left bank of the Amur River. From the west, citizens can admire the peaks of the Biadzhal ridge, and from the east - the Sikhote-Alin mountain range. The small mountain river Selinka divides Komsomolsk-on-Amur into the central part and the industrial zone. It is also interesting that in the city there is 500 hectares of pristine taiga.

The development of the city was not uniform. It began with the promenade and the territories around industrial enterprises. The war prevented the completion of the unified city project, and with the advent of peace, Leningrad architects took up some areas and streets, which makes Komsomolsk-on-Amur somewhat similar to the northern capital.

How to get there

The convenient location in the city center attracts tourists to the hotel "Sunrise" in Komsomolsk-on-Amur. Address: Pervostroiteley Avenue, 31.

The easiest way to get here is by taxi, which the hotel staff will kindly order for you. You can also get to the hotel in the following ways:

  • From the train station you can reach your destination with any tram. Get off at the stop "Hotel" Voskhod ". You can also take a walk on Pervostroiteley Avenue.
  • Buses 102 and 104 run from the international airport. Get off at the Sewing Factory stop.
  • From the river station, the hotel can be reached by tram number 2. Get off at the stop "Hotel" Voskhod ".
  • From the bus station to the hotel can be reached by buses number 9, 27 and 104. Get off at the stop "Hotel" Sunrise ".

At the Voskhod Hotel in Komsomolsk-on-Amur, guests can enjoy a full meal in the Escher restaurant. The institution works on such a schedule:
