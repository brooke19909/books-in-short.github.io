The best ski resorts in Abkhazia: description, photos, reviews

The Caucasus is famous for its traditional hospitality, stunning nature and majestic mountains. The most suitable time to visit is autumn and summer, however, winter holidays in the ski resorts of Abkhazia are rapidly gaining popularity. There are no high-quality services yet, but the resort still leaves an unforgettable experience.

Winter holidays in Abkhazia

We offer a walk in the best resorts of Abkhazia.

One of the advantages of visiting Abkhazia in the winter is the reduction in prices for rental housing in the private sector. As for hotels, in the winter there are small discounts.

Women vacationing in Abkhazia love to visit thermal springs and mud baths. With the advent of winter, fewer tourists become available on beaches and hydrogen sulfide springs, which means more comfortable conditions for recreation are created.

Planning an independent trip

If you plan to go on an independent trip through the skiing region of Abkhazia, then you are advised to consider the following points:

  1. Route - it must be made taking into account the overnight stay. Usually hotels are located in cities, but some can be located far from ski routes, so you need to consider how you will get to them.
  2. Equipment - when choosing it, it should be borne in mind that most of the rest at the ski resort you will have to carry it yourself. Taxi prices in the city are quite high, public transport is poorly developed.
  3. Food - problems are unlikely to arise with him. Even in the smallest village there is a cafe that sells local dishes.

Ski resorts of Abkhazia

By visiting them, you will get a lot of positive emotions and unforgettable memories from a winter holiday.

The development of skiing in this region is just beginning. The main thing is that there are all the necessary conditions for it to turn into one of the best ski resorts in the Caucasus - a mountainous area.

Abkhazia has a very favorable location. It is located on one of the slopes that belongs to the Main Caucasian Range, so snow-capped mountain peaks are not uncommon here. As for skiing, the demand for it is very large.

Consider the best ski resort cities of Abkhazia:

  1. The most popular is winter vacations in Sukhumi - the capital of Abkhazia. It has a well-developed infrastructure, which remains the same at any time of the year. Summer restaurants located on the promenade turn into winter ones and still receive visitors. People come here for extreme skiing.
  2. New Athos takes the second place. There is a large selection of ski resorts that provide various services. Highly qualified staff will select a ski route for both beginners and professionals. The resort differs from others in quality services at an affordable price.
  3. Gudauta is inferior to its competitors, but is quite worthy of attention. Not a well-known resort, since there is practically no necessary infrastructure, but here there are always stable prices and few people. There are stunning nature and picturesque mountains. Great for skiing, as snow lays on the mountains for 7 months.

Mountain descents in this country will become especially popular among freeriders who love natural elevations. Extremals are recommended to go to such mountain ranges as Mamzyshha, Arabica, Auathara.

If you are a beginner skier, then it is better for you to descend on more gentle slopes, therefore it is recommended to start acquaintance with this sport from them.

There are no organized slopes here yet, but do not forget that skiing is just starting to develop here. We can say with confidence that after a couple of years the situation will change. By the way, the choice of options for natural descent is simply impressive!

You can go on a ski trip in Abkhazia either with the help of a travel agency or on your own. In the first case, most often the cost of the trip includes not only the road, but also hotel room reservation, meals and compulsory travel insurance.

Benefits of a winter holiday

Compared with the summer bustle and crowded, winter holiday will be more calm and quiet. In addition, it is budget and much more comfortable. You can calmly walk around interesting places of the country, visit the healing baths that are located on the Black Sea coast, and also improve your health in hydrogen sulfide springs.

Separately, it is worth telling about the amazing nature of Abkhazia, which will surprise you with flowering medlar and roses. An evergreen oleander, boxwood and mimosa grows here. What are the aromas of cypress, eucalyptus and laurel. Tourists are pleased with the abundance of fruits, including feijoa, dates, citrus fruits, grapefruit, pears, apples.

The choice is really very large, and prices are quite low.

Winter markets are famous for their choice of different types of fish, which are much richer than summer ones.

Weather

Snow here is rare in winter, and the average temperature practically does not fall below +10 degrees. If the day is bright, then the temperature can rise to +20.

It gets cooler on rainy days, which often happen in February and January. At this time, it is recommended to walk around museums, botanical gardens, visit the zoo, theater and see the sights. Regular guests of Abkhazia recommend visiting the amazingly beautiful Lake Ritsa.

Reviews about the resorts of Abkhazia

With special admiration, tourists talk about the magnificent mountain landscapes, the diversity of flora and fauna.

Many say that the ski resorts of Abkhazia are quiet and calm, there are not a large number of tourists, so you can relax from the daily rush. Most of all positive reviews about the resort of Sukhumi, as besides the mountain peaks there are still many attractions.

If you do not know where to spend your winter vacation and ski, then you should take a closer look at the ski resorts of Abkhazia, reviews of which are mostly positive. Here there is a beautiful nature, and delicious cuisine, and an original culture, and the majestic Caucasian mountains.
