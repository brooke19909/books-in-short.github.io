What is a soundbar and which one to choose? Customer reviews and recommendations

Until recently, music lovers dreamed of putting a home theater in their home. Today, dreams are already different - more modern and technological, for example, a soundbar. What is a soundbar and how to use it?

System features





The soundbar is a soundbar, which is a compact audio system that allows you to enjoy music without massive speakers and subwoofers. The soundbar does not have a large number of speakers, since all the necessary functions are collected in one case. What is a soundbar, an inexperienced user will ask? This is the perfect alternative to your home theater, which takes up little space and allows you to enjoy great sound at optimal volume.

How does it work?

The soundbar attracts attention with its versatility: it can work in tandem with the TV, and as a standalone player, if you connect a USB flash drive to it or insert a music disc. Two types of systems are popular today : active (connection is made to the TV directly) and passive (connection of an AV receiver is required).

To better know what a soundbar is, you need to study its structure. The modern model consists of:

  • player
  • set of speakers;
  • an audio processor that delivers full sound.

As a rule, sound panels can have up to 16 sound speakers, which are located at certain angles, as well as a bass speaker - it acts as a subwoofer. The panel is easy to use, as it can be customized to the needs of each user using the remote control.

Where to put?





If the area does not allow you to put bulky things, for example, home theaters, a soundbar will be a great solution. It is suitable for living rooms, bedrooms or small apartments, where you want to equip a recreation area. Moreover, a universal panel does not exist, so for each room you should choose a specific model. Externally, the panel is an oblong box in which drivers, digital amplifiers, circuits and connectors are mounted, so the sound is reproduced much more clearly. The specially designed housing also serves the purpose of reproducing high-quality sound.

Which is better - a soundbar or a home theater?

Today, various types of speakers are very popular . Which is better? It should be based on your own preferences. Sound panels, for example, attract attention with a variety of design options and playback quality. Moreover, they need not be selected depending on the length of the TV, because you can mount them anywhere. Most often, buyers choose soundbars, because they are cheaper and allow you to save space, which is especially true for small rooms.

Connection Features

Most often, a soundbar for a TV is selected. However, many panels are equipped with one or two digital audio connectors, as well as several analog inputs. All sources are simply connected to the TV, while all content will sound through the panel. Some expensive models have HDMI inputs that allow you to receive multi-channel audio.





Most buyers choose panels to watch TV or movies in high quality. That is why they are so popular. A typical connection option is wireless via Bluetooth, because today most types of equipment support this technology. Control panels through the remote control, which is included.

Installation Features

What is the soundbar, we figured out. Now it remains to understand where it is better to put it. Some are hung on the wall, but you should pay attention to the fact that the panel does not cover the bottom of the TV and its IR sensor. Some panels, by the way, sound better if they are removed from the TV. Wall installation is simple. The only difficulty is to remove the wires so that the interior is aesthetic. An attractive option for many is the hinge mount. This allows you to move the panel in different positions, change the angle of its inclination.

If you dream of a loud sound, then take care to complement the soundbar with a subwoofer. Some models come immediately with it; it attaches to the system wirelessly, making installation simple and convenient. With the location of the subwoofer, you can conjure to get clear and high-quality sound.

Which brand to give preference?





Which soundbar is better? To answer this question, you need to decide for yourself a number of parameters. Remember that the soundbar is an acoustic system, so make the emphasis on power, distortion and frequency when choosing. The final effect is achieved with high-quality bass, so it is important to pay attention to the type of subwoofer. This is the first.

The second is the ability to connect Blu-Ray. This is an additional device, but very necessary, especially if you like to watch movies in excellent quality. That is, thanks to this option, you get a real home theater, only compact and stylish.

The third is manufacturability. Many models are equipped with all conceivable and inconceivable options that are not always needed. Elite panels gives, for example, the ability to integrate via Wi-Fi and support for Smart TV. A modern 3D soundbar can cost 5,000, and 20,000, and 50,000 rubles.
