Ski resort "Lyubogorye" in Veliky Novgorod

Ski resort "Lyubogorye" in the Novgorod region appeared relatively recently. In 2013, construction began by laying the foundation stone for the foundation of the complex. In a matter of years, it has grown into a rather large resort, starting with a test mode launched before the New Year holidays in 2014. Lovers of skiing come here with pleasure . Not only the track attracts visitors, but also sports events.

Resort construction

"Lyubogorye" is located near the regional center of Lyubytino. Since the test launch of the main slope with a length of 1.2 kilometers, the resort has built: access road, cafe, building rental equipment. Clearing of forest stands continued and the installation of power lines was completed, purchased equipment and equipment were installed.

In February 2015, the official opening of the ski resort "Lyubogorye" (pictured above) in the Novgorod region. By the time the ski resort was opened, ski lifts were put into operation and two more slopes were equipped: for cheesecakes - tubing and training. Tubing is a great alternative to children sledding. He has already gained popularity in both adults and children. A fascinating and lively vacation for the whole family.

Currently, five ski runs are in working condition at the Lyubogorye ski resort. The towing lifts work on two, while the main delivery of skiers is carried out on a snowmobile. An order for the supply of equipment for lifts to other routes was made among Russian manufacturers.

The resort has a tradition of holding annual open competitions in alpine skiing and snowboarding.

Resort Location

The slope is in one of the most beautiful places. From its top in front of the skiers reveals the splendor of the panorama of the surroundings. Near the resort there are waterfalls, an open-air museum - a medieval Slavic village, the Church of the Assumption of the Blessed Virgin Mary, built in 1832. Everywhere you can visit and see these attractions, having come on vacation.

Located near Veliky Novgorod, the ski resort "Lyubogorye" is a fabulous place where you can leave the city with the whole family. And if you and your family like a calm village atmosphere - this resort is perfect. Here you can take a break from the crazy rhythm of the city away from problems and work.

Skiers from St. Petersburg who come to the Lyubogorye ski resort for the weekend can not only ride perfectly on the mountain slopes and the ice rink, but also visit the neighboring Veliky Novgorod with its fabulously beautiful Kremlin. The history of this building will be interesting to know, as, by the way, and the history of the whole city.

Residence

Cottages are built for living in the resort area. In addition to paying for the stay, a deposit in the amount of from five to ten thousand rubles is taken at the resort, which is returned after the room is surrendered. This is a kind of property insurance rooms. The cottages have standard rooms for 2 people. If necessary, you can add additional space. Family rooms are designed for four, six, eight or more people. You can add two additional places to them.

In each room, in each house, visitors will be able to feel the comfort of a homely atmosphere. For the smallest, an extra bed is provided in the room so that it is more convenient for parents to monitor the baby.

You can settle in the recreation center "Lyubytino-Khutor". Accommodation on it is carried out in guest houses and a hotel. On weekdays, a 20% discount is provided. The cost of a double room is two thousand rubles, a triple costs two thousand eight hundred, and a guest house with 2 + 2 accommodation costs a little more than three thousand rubles.

Base ski resort for children

A ski slope is provided for children at the ski resort, on which they comfortably learn the basics of winter sports, as well as a convenient lift - a baby lift. In addition, for the smallest there is a playground on the street.

Having played enough in the air, children can have fun in the games room, where there is a TV, and a pool with balls, one of the favorite entertainments of kids. The child will not be bored. Baby sitting during the day, while parents conquer the slopes, will cost 250 rubles.

Ski resort services

For beginners who want to learn to ski, skate or snowboard, there are instructors at the base. They deal directly with an adult or child. To be sure that you will be engaged, you need at least a day to place an order for instruction. Individual lesson costs 800 rubles / person, group 600 rubles / person. Instructors work with beginner skiers on a 400-meter track, with altitude differences of about 40 meters.
