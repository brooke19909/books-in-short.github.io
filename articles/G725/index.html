Hotel Dosinia Luxury Resort Hotel 5 * (Turkey, Kemer): reviews of tourists, photos

Kemer is a resort region in Turkey, located on the Mediterranean coast. It stretched along many kilometers of pebble beaches, to which coniferous tracts almost approached. Steep cliffs provide reliable protection against freezing winds.

Entertainment industry





Holidays in Kemer are chosen by adherents of the All Inclusive program. Tens of thousands of Russians flock to these regions every year. Therefore, the local staff not only understands, but also speaks Russian. The region has all the conditions for a fun and eventful pastime.

Liman Street attracts many tourists with cheap souvenirs and beach accessories. In boutiques and shops there is a huge selection of crafts, magnets, cotton clothes and swimming equipment. The city is famous for its noisy nightlife. It houses the best clubs and discos in the country.

The nearest airport is Antalya. The path from the passenger terminal of the air gate runs along a narrow mountain highway. Travel time does not exceed 50 minutes.

Southern nights





The coast of Kemer is an endless series of multi-story buildings of various levels. Traditionally, the Russians are most in demand in new hotel complexes. Hotel Dosinia Luxury Resort Hotel 5 * in Turkey (photos serve as a clear confirmation of this) was built recently. Its doors opened in 2017.

It offers guests 222 rooms, which occupy five floors. The rise is carried out by silent spacious elevators with forced ventilation. Stair flights are equipped with wide steps. Rooms for guests with reduced mobility are available.

The total area occupied by the complex is 15,000 square meters. Residential buildings are on the second line. A trip to the beach takes about ten minutes. High-speed Internet access is available in rooms and common areas. Connection is automatic and completely free.

At the disposal of Dosinia Luxury Resort Hotel 5 *, whose rating is still being formed, a coastal sandbank. It is owned by the institution. The beach is covered with fine pebbles and imported sand.
