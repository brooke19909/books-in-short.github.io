Aquarium in Bangkok: photo, address, reviews

The aquarium in Bangkok is rightfully considered one of the best and largest in Southeast Asia. In size, it competes with another giant, which is located in Singapore. Despite the fact that it is indoors, not outdoors, its area is huge. The oceanarium occupies more than ten thousand square meters. A huge number of marine inhabitants, including the most exotic, attract adults and children from all over the world. In addition, the aquarium is interactive. Here you can feed fish and animals, take pictures with them and ride on top of a glass-bottomed boat, as well as scuba diving. The Aquarium opened in 2005 and since then it has been steadily included in the list of necessary attractions in Bangkok that you definitely need to see. Let's get to know in more detail what wonders this landmark promises us. After all, reviews about her are almost unanimous - this is the place where you should definitely go to Bangkok and not regret money for it.



Location, opening hours, prices

The aquarium in Bangkok is located on the lower two floors of the largest shopping center in the capital of Thailand, Siam Paragon. This is a gigantic structure that is difficult to get around even for a whole day. There are always many tourists who come to sales. The shopping center also has an excellent food court, there are plenty of other entertainments. At the same time, travelers also visit the Aquarium (Bangkok). Opening hours are convenient for both city residents and travelers. It is open from nine in the morning until ten in the evening. But try not to come under the curtain. At least three to four hours of his time must be devoted to this miracle. The ticket price is almost a thousand baht (for reference: 1 baht is 1.63 rubles). Children will take 710 baht. Often there is a queue at the box office, especially on weekdays when groups of schoolchildren arrive. Therefore, try to take tickets online or in travel companies with small margins. Aquarium in Bangkok: how to get

If you want to get to the Siam Paragon shopping center, you need to take the subway. But not to the underground, but to a special railway that goes over the city. It is called a sky train. Exit should be at the Siam stop. From everywhere, a shopping center is visible from its platforms, at the lower levels of which there is an oceanarium. From the far ends of Bangkok you have to travel with transfers or take a taxi. The history of the shopping center and its underwater world

Siam (Aquarium, Bangkok) was opened on December 9, 2005. It is owned by the Oseanis Australia Group. This is a well-known multinational corporation. She owns similar facilities in China, South Korea, as well as Australia. The underwater miracle that she built in the shopping mall literally translates as "The World of the Siamese Ocean." The shopping center itself, containing not only shops and restaurants, but a huge multiplex cinema, an art gallery and an opera concert hall, was built on the site of the former Intercontinental Hotel.

Aquarium in Bangkok: reviews of tourists

"Siam Ocean World" really does not leave anyone indifferent. This is one of the most visited attractions of the country, which is not related to religion. Impressions after him remain very bright, especially since everything here is equipped with the most modern technologies. The Aquarium itself is designed for the fact that a lot of children go here.
