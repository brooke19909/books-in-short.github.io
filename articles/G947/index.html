Stockholm

The company, in addition to building classic block houses, sometimes allows itself to experiment. In particular, the Stockholm complex can serve as an example of this. It is being built on the banks of the Bolshaya Nevka, and it can be called original due to its design and concept. So, 2 houses that will be included in this complex are made in the shape of a horseshoe. Due to this, a greater number of apartments have river views. Also, this form means that the courtyards for each of the houses are fenced from the outside world by the building itself, which creates a sense of comfort and security. The object belonging to the premium class is going to be commissioned in 2017. The cost of an apartment here starts from 8 million rubles and ends with almost 100 million for five-room apartments.

The advantages of the complex, of course, can be called the presence of a river nearby, the original shape of the houses and the high quality of the materials used. The disadvantages are the high price, as well as the proximity of the busy highway.
