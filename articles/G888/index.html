Guest house "Marina" (Adler): location, number of rooms and reviews

The small seaside town of Adler will be an excellent option for those tourists who want to relax in a quiet place and not spend a lot of money on vacation. It is located near Sochi and Abkhazia, so you can go there at any time. For travelers who prefer budget accommodation, the guest house "Marina" (Adler) opened its doors. What can he offer his future guests?

general information

This small but comfortable hotel began to receive tourists in 2006. She has practically no own territory. On a small plot of land is a five-story complex, painted in beige. In total, the house has 10 rooms, so it is perfect for a quiet family vacation with young children. Children of any age are allowed to check in, and a baby crib can be provided in the room for a fee. Pets are also allowed. However, before buying a tour, you must always notify the hotel of their arrival. Also, the guest house "Marina" (Adler) has the right to charge you an additional payment for this service.





Check-in at the hotel starts at 14:00. On its territory there is a dining room in which tourists can have breakfast, lunch or dinner. You can pay for meals when issuing a ticket (breakfast only, half board). It is worth knowing that the complex does not accept bank cards, so the amount for accommodation will need to be paid in cash. Checkout time at this guest house is noon. Until this time, you must leave your rooms at the end of the holiday.

Home location

The guest house offers guests an advantageous location near the coast. His address: Krasnodar Territory, Adler, st. Enlightenment, 158 V. The complex was built on the main promenade of the city in the private sector. The sea can be reached in just 5 minutes, because it is located 450 meters from the guest house. The local aquarium is 800 meters away, while a water park and a dolphinarium are 2 km away. Tourists can also go to the Olympic Park, which is only 9 km from Adler. You can get to it by bus or taxi. Cafes, restaurants and grocery stores are within walking distance. Near the complex there are several resorts.

Guest house "Marina" (Adler) offers its guests the organization of a transfer for a fee. Its representatives can meet and pick you up by car in Sochi airport, which is 12 km from here. Trains deliver tourists to the nearest Adler train station, which is 4 km away. You can also get to the hotel by intercity bus from nearby cities.

Number of rooms and its equipment

The hotel rooms are equipped with everything necessary for an inexpensive but comfortable stay. The living rooms have a private bathroom with a shower, washbasin and toilet. The rooms are equipped with a TV, to which Russian cable channels are connected. There is also a small refrigerator, individual air conditioning, mosquito net and a set of towels. Cosmetic supplies, by contrast, are not provided.

Hotel Infrastructure

A huge selection of entertainment, cafes, bars and restaurants offers tourists Sochi (Adler). Guest house "Marina" is located near many of them. The complex also offers its guests a range of its own services and services that maintain high-quality accommodation here. We list the main ones:

  • Paid car parking for tourists arriving in their own car. The number of seats on it is limited.
  • Shared kitchen, which is located on the top floor of the building. Here guests can cook their own meals.
  • Free internet throughout the hotel.
  • Shared living room with plasma TV.
  • Playground.
  • Paid sauna.




Guest reviews here

We can say that many tourists liked Adler. St. Enlightenment, in their opinion, is fully focused on their needs. Guests enjoy strolling here in the evenings. The area around the hotel is clean and tidy, and many trees, shrubs and flowers grow around. Tourists who came to the guest house "Marina" (Adler) leave good reviews . They liked the friendly staff, clean rooms with working equipment, as well as the good location and delicious (albeit monotonous) food. Of the minuses, they distinguish poor soundproofing in the rooms, a touristy beach and low Internet speed.





If you want to have a good rest in Adler, without overpaying a lot of money for accommodation, you can safely choose this guest house. It is also perfect for families with children and tourists who prefer a relaxing holiday.
