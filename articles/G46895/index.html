Hotel Sun Village 4 Goa: inexpensive and fun

Description. Hotel Sun Village 4 is conveniently located near the beach of Baga Beach in the North of Goa, beloved by the Russians. People of a respectable age will not like it, and small children will not be very comfortable here. The hotel gathers young people from all over the world and middle-aged people who love an active, fun holiday. The friendly, bustling, cheerful and good-natured atmosphere attracts connoisseurs of music, communication, nightlife.

The panorama around the hotel surprises with its beauty and grandeur. Picturesque hills are interspersed with wild cliffs, and among them lurks are transparent lakes, attracting for their pristine and beautiful.



The road from Sun Village Goa 4 to the beach allows tourists to enjoy pictures of national life, in passing go into the original shops, shops, and enjoy other hotels.

The rooms. The hotel, consisting of 7 multi-storey buildings, has only standard rooms. They are very spacious: each area is at least 20 meters. There are also luxurious verandas from which you can admire garden landscapes, and small balconies overlooking the pool.



The content of the rooms is fully consistent with the declared category. Sun Village 4 has comfortable, equipped with the latest requirements shower rooms with hairdryer, toilets, televisions, split units and fans. Each room has a tea corner.

For a fee, guests are provided with a shower and drinks for the minibar. Rooms are cleaned daily and linen is changed twice a week.

All rooms have two zones: one is a living room, the second is a bedroom.

Beach. Sun Village 4 is located within the third line, it does not have its own beach. Tourists can visit the picturesque municipal beach. Here they can rent towels, sunbeds, tents. There are cafes on the shore, peddlers carry pastries, offer drinks and national dishes.

Nutrition. Tourists vacationing in Sun Village 4 can choose only breakfast or three meals a day according to their favorite All Inclusive system. The hotel constantly has several restaurants specializing in local, continental, Chinese and Mexican cuisines.

There is a barbecue cafe in the open air, there are several bars with local or continental drinks.

Information for vacationers. Infrastructure Sun Village 4 is designed for young people and active middle-aged people. There is an excellent Ayurvedic center where you can improve your health, get rid of stress or adjust your destiny. There is a jacuzzi, massage rooms, a wellness center and a fitness room.



In the evenings, tourists can have fun at the disco, in the afternoon - go shopping or go on excursions.

From November to April, a colorful show program with the participation of local celebrities is held in one of the restaurants on the territory twice a week.

For business people there is a business center, for children there is a pool.

Reviews Guests at Sun Village 4 Goa celebrate the fun and relaxed atmosphere of the hotel. Here you can meet young representatives of other countries, meet your compatriots.

Tasty and varied cuisine is celebrated. Especially tourists are attracted to Indian dishes, but connoisseurs of traditional food are happy to talk about continental cuisine.

The rooms are clean and comfortable, they are spacious and cleaned efficiently and regularly.
