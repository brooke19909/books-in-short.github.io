"Peipsi Compound" (recreation center, Spitsino, Pskov Oblast): recreation, prices, photos and reviews

When planning your vacation or vacation with children, it is not at all necessary to travel outside Russia. Amazing, creative and interesting vacation at any time of the year can be arranged in the Pskov region. Winter and summer tourism in the bosom of nature is highly developed in this region. Comfortable guest houses and hotel complexes provide quality leisure for any budget.

The beauty of the untouched natural landscape, the cleanest air, sand dunes and a lot of active entertainment will allow you to fully enjoy pastime and plunge into the atmosphere of old Russian life. A fascinating journey will give the tourist complex "Peipsi Compound". The recreation center is hugely popular with locals and nonresident guests.

Description





Cozy cottages are compactly located in the picturesque village of Spitsino, near the largest lake in the region called Chudskoye. Chudskoye Compound camp site has a spacious territory with many deciduous trees, centuries-old pines and has its own farm compound. Attractive complex for a romantic weekend, business and family holidays. This is a paradise that has managed to maintain unbridled natural power, life here is serene and peaceful. For body and soul





The Chudskoye Compound complex welcomes guests all year round. The recreation center has a sandy beach and a boat pier. At the disposal of vacationers - water activities (windsurfing, yacht, catamarans), fishing (in winter and summer), as well as sports games. On the shore there are special areas with a stretched net, where everyone can drive into the ball.





One of the buildings has table tennis and billiards. A horse farm is open at the base, you can choose your favorite horse and take a fascinating walk in the local surroundings. Children will be pleased with small and friendly ponies. A passive hunt is proposed in the thicket of the forest for mushrooms and berries. In cold weather, no one will get bored either. The choice of entertainment is rich: skates, skis, sleds, underwater fishing, curling, snowmobiles.
