DIY banana crafts: a master class, description and interesting ideas

Very often I want to decorate the table with something unusual and unique. As a rule, many housewives serve fruit slices for desserts . Crafts made from apples and bananas will make a more original serving of sweet salad. By the way, a great idea is to involve children and other family members in the manufacture of edible figurines. General domestic work, all the more so fascinating, allowing you to show imagination, is very unifying.





Banana Dog

A craft made of bananas in the shape of a dog will look very unusual. Such figures can easily be made with kids. They are not only perfect for decorating the festive table, but also come out winners in any children's competition.

Components for crafts

So, how to make a dog-shaped banana craft? What is required for this? You must have at hand the following tools and materials:

  • Two to three bananas.
  • A couple of peas of black pepper.
  • A few toothpicks.
  • Sharp knife.
  • Marker.




Assembly

Using a marker or a regular fat marker, mark the places where the future dog will have paws. Try to work with a knife very carefully. Children should not trust such work. Make cutouts small and shallow. The paws are best placed at a considerable distance from each other. In this case, the dog will be better standing on the surface of the table or plate. For greater stability and confidence, insert one toothpick into each foot. If they are long, and the dog‚Äôs paws are short, then the wooden stick can be broken in half and carefully inserted into two parts at once. They will not be visible anyway.

The first overseas fruit on which we made the paws should cut off the upper part. Crafts made from bananas are made very easily thanks to the suppleness and softness of the material. Getting to the design of the head. We take the second banana and cut it in half. We mark with a felt-tip pen the places where the ears and eyes will be. Doing banana crafts with your own hands, you can use any materials at hand. As a rule, peas are used to shape the eyes. If not, small cloves are great. To make the dog's ears, it is necessary to cut a piece of banana in the middle and carefully pull out half the pulp. From the remaining peel, cut out the ears. They will hang down, which, in fact, we needed to achieve.

Very often, young mothers, preparing creative work for a competition in a kindergarten or school, ask themselves the question: how to make a banana craft to be durable and survive the transportation and the competition itself ?. Conventional toothpicks come to the rescue. To bond the body and head of the dog you need a decent amount of them. As experts say, you should not skimp. The more sticks in the design, the more stable it will be. A soft banana pulp will hide unnecessary details. Everything, the dog from the banana is ready!





Banana Dolphin

Another banana craft will also look equally impressive on fruit salad . Dolphin can be done in minutes. In this case, the result will exceed all your expectations.

Components for crafts

To make an unusual banana dolphin, you will need:

  • A banana is one thing.
  • Clove - two pieces.
  • The berry is one thing. You can take for crafts hawthorn, cranberries, sea buckthorn - any bright berry.
  • Sharp knife.
  • Marker.

Assembly

To begin with, you should get rid of the ugly and completely unnecessary fruit legs for us to do. Making banana crafts, as a rule, you can use all parts of the fruit. But in this case, we will not need a black leg. The dolphin's body will need one third of the banana. Cut off this part.

Now you need to make the dolphin fins. Using a marker, we outline the places where they will be located. With a sharp knife, carefully cut the peel in the shape of a triangle. Next, we make a small shallow incision on the other side of this geometric figure. Raise this part of the peel. It will serve as a support for the fin. With the help of such an incision, the fin itself should separate some distance from the dolphin's body. At the same time, it will be more voluminous. In the same way we make the second fin. Remember that when making crafts from bananas in the form of animals, you need to observe symmetry with respect to the location of the paws, ears and other parts of the body.



The next stage is the eyes. With the help of an ‚Äúassistant‚Äù - a marker - we make outlines. We put cloves in these places. It remains only to make a mouth. For such a banana craft, we need some bright berry. We cut the remaining tip of the fruit leg in half and insert it inside - it looks as if the animal is holding a ball. The banana dolphin is ready!

Banana island

Crafts from bananas and other fruits are made not only for competitions in kindergarten, but, as we have already noted, for decorating cuts. In this case, we suggest you make this fruit dish completely, the main component of which will be a banana. Ingredients that you will need:

  • Two bananas.
  • One orange or tangerine.
  • Two kiwis.
  • Six to eight grapes.
  • Two kiwis.

Assembly

Two bananas must be peeled from the skins. We leave the whole fruit, the second - we make it a little shorter. These are the future palm trees on the island. On top of the banana trees we need to arrange a sprawling crown. It will be made of kiwi. To do this, they are cut into half rings - long, thin petals are obtained. Then we proceed to the design of palm fruits. In this role, grapes or any other berries that your imagination can think of or which you will be able to fish from the bowels of the refrigerator look great.

Such cutting, in addition to a beautiful and original appearance, has a high practical value. All fruits are finely chopped, so they are convenient to take and eat.
