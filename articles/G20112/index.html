The Caspian Sea invites you to rest (Makhachkala): recreation center "Dolphin", the hotel complex "Jami" and other resorts are looking forward to tourists

The coast of the Caspian Sea is famous not only for its beautiful landscapes, warm water and a large number of vacationers, but also for its recreation facilities. In the Republic of Dagestan, in particular, in its capital, there are a large number of health and entertainment complexes, which every year attract more and more tourists from around the world, especially from Russia, who come to these places for vacation. Makhachkala (recreation center "Odyssey", "Dolphin", "Lighthouse" and others) will be happy to receive and surround the famous Caucasian hospitality of all who respect it with their attention.

The recreation center "Odyssey"

One of the famous recreation centers on the sea coast of Makhachkala is the Odyssey medical and health complex. This hotel is located on the Sulak highway opposite the village of Chapaevo. This holiday home has everything to have a good time with friends. However, entertainment is not the only advantage of this complex. People resting here will receive a positive charge of vivacity and improve their health. The recreation center "Odyssey" (Makhachkala) offers a huge list of services, ranging from delicious breakfasts to pools and fitness centers.

This complex can also boast the presence of its own, equipped with everything necessary, access to the sea, that is, a private beach where no one can interfere with relaxation. On the shore you can not only swim and sunbathe, but also have fun playing volleyball, soccer or other games on the grounds. Comfortably equipped and clean areas will present a real pleasure from the time spent here. That is what a summer vacation should be.

Makhachkala (recreation center "Odyssey") will surprise not only with hospitality. For children, a real "Frigate" is installed on its territory, namely a large game ship, where there are slides, tunnels and other various sports quirks to strengthen the muscles of the child.

Rooms with different levels of comfort and prices are quite diverse, there are both standard and luxury apartments. One of the main advantages of the Odyssey center is, of course, the kitchen, which is suitable for sophisticated gourmets and for lovers to just have a tasty meal. Also, the wellness center can please with the presence of: convenient parking, a Finnish bath, a gym, a phyto-bar, Wi-Fi, etc. Odyssey will become a real paradise on the Caspian Sea for those who want to visit this complex and get the most complete relaxation.

Makhachkala - recreation center "Kizlyar lagoon"

An interesting place to spend a vacation is the recreation center "Kizlyar lagoon". On its territory a hotel with economy and luxury rooms was built. The rooms here are fully equipped. They have: water, a bathroom, a refrigerator, a TV, air conditioning, etc. Also, visitors who want to relax separately from the world can rent special houses that are also equipped with everything necessary. On the territory there is a cafe-dining room, cooks of which will delight you with delicious dishes of national cuisine. Beautiful landscapes, clean air and the Cote d'Azur - all this will help to have a great time, coming to Dagestan on vacation (Makhachkala). The recreation center "Kizlyar lagoon" is exactly what the applicants need.

The recreation center "Lighthouse"

The recreation center "Mayak" is located at: Sulak highway, 2nd Pocket, Makhachkala. The complex consists of two rooms, which are designed for a total of two hundred visitors. The hotel rooms are fully equipped with everything you need. Comfortable furniture, dishes and built-in appliances, bathrooms, Internet, etc. Such equipment is provided in the rooms of the middle class. But in the luxury apartments, everything else will additionally be: a large TV, refrigerator and air conditioning.



The recreation center "Mayak" (Makhachkala) is notable for affordable prices. The cost of a suite starts at just 1,500 rubles per day. Also, in addition to the hotel itself, the complex includes: a shop, a cafe, a playground, a guarded parking lot, a billiard room. Do not forget about the beach, which is fully prepared for relaxation and active games.

Holiday Village "Malibu"

Makhachkala not only hospitably welcomes guests of the city, but also offers to stay in the best places, for example, in the beautiful Malibu hotel complex. The cultural and entertainment center is located directly on 16 km of the Sulak highway - which allows you to get there not only by personal, but also by public transport. The complex consists of the main building and 12 bungalow houses, half of which are with one bedroom, the other with two. Also for large meetings there is a conference room with all the necessary equipment. All rooms and houses are fully equipped with everything necessary. The recreation center "Malibu" (Makhachkala) offers tourists two restaurants, a cafe and a bar, located on the beach by the sea. Every night discos and various events are held. Secured parking, sightseeing tours, laundry services and much more will allow vacationers to relax and have a good time.



Hotel complex "Jami"

The recreation center "Jami" is a large hotel complex, consisting of four spacious three-story buildings, which are located almost on the beach. On its territory there is everything necessary for a complete and comfortable stay: a sauna, billiards, cafe and restaurant, massage rooms, a playground, its own guarded parking lot, as well as an equipped beach with a length of 130 meters. Infrastructure "Jami" has combined all the best. Here, vacationers will enjoy the time spent on the shore of the Caspian Sea.



Holiday Village "Dolphin"

The recreation center "Dolphin" (Makhachkala) is one of the most visited places on the coast of the Caspian Sea. The complex operates during the holiday season, namely from May to the end of October, when the temperature of the air and water is most comfortable for tourists. The hotel complex consists of several buildings, which include buildings with rooms of medium and luxury class. The whole territory is fully protected. The sandy beach is only seventy meters from the complex.



Visitors to the recreation center can be pleased with the fact that they have their own banquet room for 300 people, as well as a sports bar, free internet and a cafe-restaurant.
