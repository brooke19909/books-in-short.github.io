The art of origami: how to make a fighter out of paper

Everyone knows such a hobby as origami. In general, origami is called the art of folding a variety of paper figures. Today it is a fairly common form of art, in addition, it is quite popular. The craftsmanship of this type of art is taught almost everywhere: in kindergarten, at school, in youth centers, in camps and sanatoriums - there are origami circles everywhere. There are even exhibitions and origami competitions for which worthy prizes are awarded. You can learn such art even thanks to the Internet, because now there are many sites on which you can find special schemes, photos and training videos. Those who are engaged in origami know how to fold any animal or how to make a fighter out of paper.

Origami story

As you know, paper was discovered in China. From there it takes its roots in an entertaining hobby. Of course, then they did not even think about how to make a fighter out of paper. At the very beginning, origami was used exclusively for various rituals. An interesting fact is that this type of art was available only to the upper classes, since mastering the technique of this hobby indicated a good tone. This art got to Europe and America only after the Second World War. And later - to Russia. Over time, origami replenished with new figures and schemes, and popularity grew. In general, in the history of origami there are many Japanese names, but Akira Yizizawa is considered one of the main ones. This Japanese master made a very large contribution to the art of origami.

Origami Art Rules

There are several types of folding figures in origami today. If you pay attention to the classic version of this art, then the figures should be made up of a square sheet of paper. At the same time, there is a certain set of conventional signs, with the help of which a pattern of folding a particular figure is drawn. As for these signs, most of them were introduced by a famous Japanese master in the mid-twentieth century. In addition, the sheet for origami should be uniformly colored, and the process itself eliminates the use of glue and scissors. How to make a fighter?

Almost everyone knows the simplest paper folding scheme for a regular airplane. And not everyone knows how to make a fighter out of paper. As a rule, there are various schemes for folding planes, but such an airplane does not always fly well, which often causes children to be upset. There is a correct scheme with which you can accurately show your child how to make a fighter out of paper that flies well.



So, you need only one rectangular paper sheet to make a fighter out of paper. There are different schemes, but, as a rule, they are not much different from each other. So, first you need to lay the sheet vertically, and outline the middle of the sheet by folding it in half. Then you should fold the upper corners of the sheet to this middle and fix it with the help of smoothing movements with your hands. Next you need to bend the resulting acute angle down (so that it becomes lower by about 3-4 cm). After this, the resulting wings should be bent again to the middle. After that, it should be noted about 2-3 cm in different directions from the middle of the fighter and bend the wings at the received marks. In conclusion, you need to bend the so-called stabilizers, which are located on the edge of the aircraft. This is where your origami paper craft is ready. The fighter can be decorated with a variety of icons. For example, it may be red stars. So you got a decent answer to the question of how to make a fighter out of paper.

Origami today

Now this entertaining hobby is full of various types of folding techniques, various schemes, which are divided into light, medium and complex in terms of degree of completion. Well-developed so-called modular origami. In this variety of this art, figures are assembled from several other separate parts, which are also called modules. Each such module is made up of a sheet of paper, rectangular or square - it depends on the scheme.
