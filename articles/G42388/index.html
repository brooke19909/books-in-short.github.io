The best hotels of Egypt (Hurghada): photos, description

There are 15 hotels in the city of Hurghada, and they occupy the first lines among the 25 best hotels in Egypt. To choose where to spend their holidays, many are reviewing a large number of sites and photos of Egyptian hotels in Hurghada. We will make a short excursion around this city.

How to get to Hurghada

Direct flights to Hurghada International Airport can be arranged from most European countries. The best way to get to the hotel from the airport is to take a taxi, preferably, before setting off, agree on a price.

Taxis are not the only means of transport in Hurghada, there is a public one - these are minibuses that run throughout the city.

Acquaintance with the city

The beautiful resort city of Hurghada is located on the Red Sea coast. It fully justifies the choice of those who decided to spend their holidays in one of the hotels in Egypt. In Hurghada, you will be amazed by the clear water of the sea, the golden sand along the coast, the beauty of coral reefs and coastal waters rich in fish. All this attracts tourists from all over the globe. Fans of windsurfing, kayaking and even riding a sea bike will enjoy their outdoor activities.

El Gouna Beach is one of the best beaches in Egypt. It is here that you can very closely see coral reefs, having passed one of the professional diving schools, and go on a boat for day fishing or enjoy a ride on rubber "banana" boats.

sights

If you want to relax from the beach, you can go for a walk in the center of the old city in the Dahar region and wander through its streets. It is worth visiting noisy bazaars and visiting small shops offering all kinds of goods, from sweets to carpets.

By the way, the city is located at a reasonable distance from Cairo, where you can go on a day trip. Or you can divide your vacation between the attractions of Egypt and relaxation at the hotel of Hurghada. Such attractions include the Nile Valley and the Great Pyramids in Giza - the last remaining miracle of the old world. There is also a unique opportunity to take a camel ride through the surrounding desert - a place for calm reflection, especially when you think about the beauty of barren sand dunes.

Hurghada Hotels

Most luxury hotels in Hurghada in Egypt - 5 *. In the photo below, the Grand Plaza Hotel is one of them. All these hotels are located along the coastline overlooking the beautiful blue sea. In each, you can enjoy a relaxing swim in the pool, a game of tennis courts or massage and treatments. Basically, 5 * hotels operate on an all-inclusive basis.

Most hotels have their own restaurants, but there are beach restaurants offering seafood. Their menu includes authentic Egyptian cuisine, including fresh fish, as well as vegetarian dishes prepared with garlic, lemon and Egyptian spices.

Vacationers also have the opportunity to choose accommodation options in hotels where rooms for accommodation and breakfast are provided. These hotels are within walking distance of the beach. Many hotels in Egypt, and Hurghada in particular, are suitable for families with children. They have pools for babies.

We will get to know some of the hotels and the services they provide for vacationers more thoroughly.

Hotel Grand Plaza

This all-inclusive hotel is just a 2-minute walk from the beach. Vacationers are provided with rooms that have air-conditioning and balconies overlooking the picturesque pool or the Red Sea. Each room has everything necessary for a comfortable stay, including satellite TV and WI-FI.

The hotel has wellness facilities and a restaurant overlooking the pool. Meals are provided in the form of a buffet and dinner on the ordered menu.

In Egypt, in Hurghada hotels, guests can enjoy a cup of tea and listen to piano music in the halls designated for this purpose. There is also a peculiar and very fashionable conversation at the Grand Plaza hotel over a cup of tea - Lounge tea ceremony.

Guests can enjoy water sports such as boating and diving at Grand Plaza's private beach. They can relax on the comfortable sun loungers by the pool surrounded by palm trees. Guests can rent bicycles or tennis rackets at the hotel‚Äôs rental desk. For younger guests there is a playground.

The reception staff arranges, if guests wish, car rental and book tickets for flights departing from Hurghada Airport, which is almost next to the hotel. For renting cars, their parking at the hotel is free.

Hotel Hilton Hurghada

Hilton is located just 5 minutes walk from the sea. In a very beautiful bay there is a private sandy beach, whose workers monitor the cleanliness. 3 swimming pools are provided for guests, in one of which a small part is reserved for children. For kids resting with their parents, the hotel has built a couple of playgrounds.

All rooms have access to patios where you can relax while enjoying the view of the landscape surrounding the hotel. The rooms have a marble bathroom. Type of connection: WI-FI and the bar has free internet. Below you can see on the photo the 5 * hotel in Hurghada (Egypt) - Hilton.

Hilton Hurghada has restaurants and bars. The menu has many seafood dishes, Italian and classic international cuisine. Here you can play on the tennis courts or go to the fitness center.

Hotel Titanic Beach Hotel

The description of the hotel in Hurghada (Egypt) Titanic Beach lists all the services that can be provided to vacationers. The rooms are air-conditioned and have a TV with satellite channels. Certain rooms include views of the sea or pool. It features a spa and sauna. An outdoor pool and private beach are on site. Free parking is available. The hotel has a hairdresser.

This hotel is famous for its diving. The underwater sports training center offers diving and windsurfing equipment. There are many wonderful places where you can see the most beautiful underwater world through training. Titanic Beach is 16 km from New Marina and 18 km from Jiftun Island. This is where divers make their dives. Hurghada International Airport is 12 km from the hotel.

Arabia Azur Resort

A good option for a holiday in the Red Sea resort with a scuba diving reef is a 3-minute walk from the beach and a private pier next to the Olympic outdoor pool. The hotel offers rooms with sea view.

All rooms have a bathroom (shower in the bathtub, hairdryer), tiled floors, individual air conditioning, telephone, Wi-Fi (paid), satellite TV, tea and coffee making facilities, safe (free) and a balcony or terrace.

There are 4 restaurants on site serving a variety of dishes, including Italian, Oriental and modern. Cocktails and breakfast buffets are served at the Marina Beach Bar overlooking the Red Sea.

Vacationers can use the sun loungers on the private beach or next to the sports pool. The SPA salon offers body relaxation treatments and a gym. But sunbathing and scuba diving is not the only thing that can be done here. The water sports center's trainers guarantee exciting snorkeling hours, and the health club provides complete relaxation.

All Hurghada hotels offer car rental and free parking on site. The choice of hotels is large, you can always find an option that meets the needs.
