"Cascade"

All guest houses in Khost work to achieve one goal - to provide vacationers with decent living conditions and to do everything so that the time spent in this place leaves the best impression. At Cape Vidnoye is located the guest house "Cascade", which hospitably welcomes all guests and offers them their services. Accommodation is made in rooms of different categories: two- and three-bed economy and two-, three-, four- and five-bed suites. The price per night in them ranges from 1300 to 3000 rubles.

From the balconies of the rooms you can see a beautiful picture of the courtyard with bright flowers and tropical plants.
