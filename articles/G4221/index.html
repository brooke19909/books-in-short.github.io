How to rip jeans on my knees beautifully at home?

For many seasons in a row, ripped jeans occupy a leading position in the world of fashion clothes. They are equally in demand both among women and among men of different age categories. This type of clothing is especially popular among adolescents. Despite the fact that such jeans are not cheap, many fashionistas and women of fashion are eager to purchase them. And some people, having heard that it is possible to remake their jeans without any effort, tearing them in some places, irrevocably ruined things.

The thing is that without knowing some of the subtleties and using special tools, this task cannot be dealt with. In this article, you can get complete information on how to rip jeans on your knees and not ruin them, but rather turn them into a fashionable designer item.

Big and small

Before you start changing your jeans, you need to decide for yourself exactly what size hole on your lap you want. The fact is that there are several types of such processing. They can be conditionally divided into the following:

  • large holes that completely open the knees;
  • small and narrow, visible only when walking;
  • holes draped with threads.




In addition to jeans with holes that open bare legs, there are also those that hide stripes from other fabrics under the holes. Especially popular are such things with guipure fabrics or material with variegated patterns.

But do not forget that no matter what type of hole is chosen to improve your favorite outfit, you need to know the measure in this matter. Otherwise, there is a chance to overdo it and instead of fashionable jeans get a clown robe. The information provided in the article on how to rip jeans on your knees will help prevent a lot of mistakes and will not allow you to become the object of ridicule.

Useful Tips

Making holes on your favorite pants is delicate and requires some rules:

  1. You never need to make cuts in the eye, it is best to make a preliminary fitting and apply marks in the place of the future incision.
  2. Chalk or soap should be used to label the fabric.
  3. The area on the knees is particularly susceptible to stretching, this nuance must be taken into account when forming a section of the required size.
  4. Do not use scissors, as they do not allow you to make straight lines. It is best to use a clerical knife.
  5. Making cuts is only necessary when using the underlay. Without it, you can cut through jeans through a clerical knife.

Knowing how to rip jeans on your knees beautifully, and applying these valuable tips, you can transform your outfit without any help.

Which jeans are better?





Going to decorate their old jeans or recently purchased, with the help of tears, many fashionistas forget to take into account some nuances:

  1. Ripped jeans in the knee area are not suitable for all body types. If a girl has a slim figure and high stature, then she can, in principle, wear jeans with gaps in different places. But chubby ladies with magnificent forms and short stature are better to give preference to this thing with small vertical tears above the knee.
  2. Before making torn knees on jeans, you need to know the features of the material from which they are sewn. Although many people think that any jeans with torn knees look cool, in fact it is not. Pants created using classic jeans with an average level of density are best suited for this.
  3. And finally, I would like to mention the style that is most suitable for such decisions. Too wide cut of trousers will not allow to reveal all the beauty of ripped jeans, since the holes on them will look awkward. Also, it is not necessary to carry out such manipulations with very narrow models, because the fabric on them will deform, which will spoil the original appearance of the outfit. Skinny jeans are considered the most suitable for forming holes in the knees.

Nude knees

Having studied the information on the correct selection of suitable jeans for their further transformation, we proceed to the most important point - the formation of holes.

Narrow strip

This method consists in the formation of narrow holes on the knees. Many people like these ripped jeans because they look more restrained than the previous version. They are preferred to wear natures who want to liberate themselves, but are not yet ready for very large reincarnations.

Making narrow cuts on jeans in the knee area, which would be noticeable only when walking, is very simple. Consider how to rip jeans on their knees with this method, in more detail.





  1. Put on jeans and draw in chalk one thin strip in the center of the patella, on both legs.
  2. Make sure that the work performed is correct.
  3. Remove the trousers and lay them on the table.
  4. Place inside the jeans, under the drawn line, a board.
  5. Cut the line with a sharp clerical knife.
  6. Do the same with the second leg.
  7. Make a small fringe along the edges of the cut using a needle or a fine grater.

As can be seen from the above actions, working with the creation of different types of holes in trousers has similarities. Therefore, you can easily make some jeans with different types of holes for yourself.

Thin web

To make holes on your knees so that they are covered with thin threads is a simple matter, although, at first glance, it seems different. Guided by certain rules for the manufacture of such parts, you can create a thin web on your trousers quite quickly. Consider how to rip jeans on your knees beautifully at home, with a step-by-step guide:

  1. On both legs, in the knees, draw several transverse lines of different lengths.
  2. Place a plate inside each leg under the drawn lines.
  3. Use a sharp clerical knife to make cuts along the drawn lines.
  4. On each resulting strip, use a needle or tweezers to pull out the blue threads, leaving the white ones intact.




If desired, you can make not a full cobweb on jeans, but only a few alternating stripes.

Also, some are concerned about how to tear black jeans on their knees. In fact, making beautiful holes in black trousers is no different from similar work with blue or blue.
