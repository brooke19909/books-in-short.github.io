Hotel "Orchid" (Albena, Bulgaria): overview, description and reviews of tourists

Bulgaria is one of the most popular holiday destinations. On the Black Sea coast of the country there is a huge number of hotels inviting tourists. Among the many resorts and hotels you want to choose the perfect place to relax. A good hotel should be located near the sea, have clean and comfortable rooms, delicious food, as well as a good entertainment program. Let's try to evaluate whether Orchid (Albena) can meet these requirements.

General Hotel Information

Albena resort is located on the picturesque beach of the western Black Sea coast. The tourist area here began to be equipped in the late 1960s, and the Orchid Hotel itself opened its doors to visitors in 1973. The complex consists of three-story and four-story buildings, which generally have about 350 rooms. The last complete reconstruction of the rooms was made in 2003. The best time to visit the hotel is July and August. Warm weather also persists in September, but the intense heat is already subsiding.





Hotel "Orchid" (Albena) is considered a family hotel, which is suitable for couples with children. The buildings are surrounded by numerous trees, and the hotel has its own pool, plenty of places for quiet walks, games and sports grounds. The complex has both single and double rooms. The maximum number of beds for a room is 2 + 2, that is, two parents and two children. The hotel also allows tourists to have pets, but the arrival of your pet must be reported when buying a ticket.

Location

The exact address of the complex: Hotel "Orchid" 3 *, Albena 9620, Bulgaria. The resort is located in the Dobrich region, 30 kilometers from the airport of Varna. The complex is located in the northern part of the resort area on a high hill, which is surrounded by a dense forest park. The beach is 500 meters away. You can get there on foot or on a free bus that leaves the Orchid every half hour. On average, a walk to the beach takes 5-10 minutes. And from a hill, visitors can enjoy beautiful views of the sea and the coast.





Hotel Rooms

Arriving at the Orchid Hotel (Albena), you can stay in a room located in one of two buildings. The complex has 395 rooms of various capacities. Tourists can stay in double rooms with one shared bed, which are supplemented with a folding bed if necessary. There are also rooms with two large beds that adults and their children can sleep on.





The room is a good repair. The walls are painted in beige shades, while bedding and curtains, on the contrary, are bright and colorful. In addition to furniture, each room has a huge window overlooking the coastal forest park or the sea coast. Some rooms also have a sofa, which can become an extra bed. The hotel allows children of any age. Moreover, if the child is not yet 2 years old, the staff will provide you with a cot. The furniture in the room also has a small desk and chair, as well as bedside tables and a TV.

Room features

Hotel "Orchid" 3 * (Bulgaria, Albena) seeks to provide travelers with the most comfortable living conditions. The staff provides daily housekeeping and linen is changed twice a week. The hotel also provides the following amenities for you:

  • A small color TV with satellite channels.
  • Separate air conditioning for each room. True, the supply of cold air is regulated by staff.
  • Individual shower and toilet. Every day it is carefully tidied up. Towels are also changed daily. Shower facilities include toiletries.
  • A landline telephone with which you can reach the administration and hotel staff. Orchid (Albena) conducts its customer service around the clock.
  • On request, staff can provide you with a hairdryer, iron, ironing board and even a small refrigerator. The rooms also have the necessary kitchen equipment.
  • A minibar can also be delivered to the room at an additional cost. Its filling is also made for a certain amount.
  • All rooms have a balcony.
  • The rooms have ceramic tiled floors and carpets are not provided.
  • Upon request at the reception you can also use the electronic safe.

Infrastructure for vacationers

In addition, "Orchid" (Bulgaria, Albena) offers its vacationers many other amenities. We list the main infrastructure facilities and services that you can use:

  • Parking area where you can always leave your car. The only negative is that parking is not guarded.
  • Dry cleaning and laundry services. Carried out at an additional cost.
  • Free Wi-Fi can be found in the hotel lobby, and it also works in the restaurant.
  • The buildings are equipped with elevators, which allows people with disabilities to move on the upper floors.
  • Luggage office. Here you can put large suitcases and bags so as not to clog them in the room.
  • A currency exchanger located at the reception will help you convert money at the current rate.
  • Shop located on site. Here you can buy fresh products, as well as essential goods.
  • Library by the beach, in which there are freely available books in Russian.

Entertainment for tourists

The hotel has many places created for the entertainment of tourists. The entertainment program is aimed at people who love passive relaxation, and for those who cannot sit still for a minute. We list the entertainment that Orchid (Albena) can offer its guests:

  • The hotel has an outdoor pool. Free use of sun loungers and high-speed Internet.
  • Park area surrounding the hotel. There are many walking paths, as well as gazebos where you can sit and talk with friends. Walking through the park, you can see a beautiful view of the sea.
  • Playgrounds for practicing various sports. You can play volleyball, table tennis, chess or backgammon for free on site. For an extra fee, you can play on the tennis court or in the billiards room.
  • Aerobics and water aerobics courses are held by the animators at the pool for those who want to not only relax, but also to tighten their figure.
  • For a small fee, you can use the services of an Internet cafe.
  • Paid massage room.
  • Free animation programs for adults and children, evening discos.
  • Water sports at the beach, available for an extra charge.

What can a hotel offer tourists with children?

Hotel "Orchid" (Bulgaria, Albena) positions itself as a family complex, where you can have fun and safely relax with your children. According to the rules of accommodation, children of any age are allowed to check in. For very young guests, a cot is provided free of charge. But, regardless of the number of children who arrived, the staff allows you to use only one product. The restaurant provides high chairs for free upon request.





Entertainment for children is also enough. The hotel has a children's pool with a safe depth and comfortable temperature. It is also equipped with a children's slide. There is also a playground with a variety of swings. Animators every night hold discos for kids, and in the afternoon they are occupied with various games and contests in a mini-club, which can be visited by children aged 4 to 12 years. For an extra amount, the hotel can also provide you with a babysitter.

Restaurants, bars and catering

For a good rest, you must also have tasty and fresh food. There are two restaurants for gourmets in the hotel: the main one, which serves breakfast and dinner, and the additional one on the beach, where you can have a hearty lunch. Orchid works on an all-inclusive basis. Meal Schedule:

  • breakfast from 7:30 to 10:00 in the morning;
  • lunch from 12:00 to 14:00;
  • dinner from 18:00 to 21:00.




In addition to the main meals, the program also provides thematic evenings of Bulgarian, Italian cuisine, as well as an evening devoted to seafood. In addition to restaurants, tourists can grab a bite to eat at local bars. The snack bar serves pizza, salads, ice cream or fruit. The pool bar can be enjoyed free with local spirits, wine, draft beer and various juices. Imported alcohol and tobacco products are paid. You will also have to pay if you want to drink after 23:00.

Positive reviews

Reviews about the hotel "Orchid" (Albena) must be read by those who decided to go on vacation to Bulgaria. A huge number of tourists visit the complex every summer, therefore, based on their experience, you can understand whether this place is suitable for you or not. The main positive features that distinguish tourists:

  • picturesque nature and ecologically clean area;
  • the presence of an entertainment program for children, a clean children's pool;
  • varied and tasty food, the presence in the menu of meat and a large number of fruits;
  • clean and well maintained beach;
  • helpful staff, quality and clean room cleaning.




Negative reviews

Like any other hotel, Orchid has its drawbacks. Travelers note the following significant errors in service:

  • Overpopulation of the hotel. The beach by the pool is often crowded; restaurants and bars lack tables and chairs.
  • Dirty dishes in the restaurant. Oily traces remain on glasses and plates.
  • Constant noise by the pool. Families with children are not recommended to take rooms with balconies overlooking it.
  • An animation program is provided only for young children.
  • The staircase leading to the sea is not equipped with a descent for prams.
  • The rooms are cold even in warm weather, drafts walk around the room.

The three-star hotel "Orchid" (Albena), reviews of which are highly controversial, is unlikely to surprise you with first-class quality of service. Nevertheless, this is a good budget option for those families who want to spend a vacation on the sea. The hotel will justify the cost of the trip, will delight its visitors with a variety of food and a good animation program. It is worth noting that many families with small children have a rest here, so single people who want to have fun at the hotel will be bored.
