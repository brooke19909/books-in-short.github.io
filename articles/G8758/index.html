Acquaintance with Kusadasi

The city with a population of 73 thousand people is located on the western coast of Turkey. Belongs to Aydin Province. In translation, the name sounds like Bird Island. Near the large land is a small island, where in the old days a large number of migratory birds nested. Now Kusadasi is connected with this place by an unmade road.

The nearby Greek island of Samos was the center of Ionian culture in ancient times. If you wish, you can go on a boat to take a closer look at the remains of a former civilization.
