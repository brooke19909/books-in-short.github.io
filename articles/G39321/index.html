Dip Belts for Belly Dance: DIY

Today, belly dancing is gaining in popularity. Many contemporary beauties want to learn this kind of oriental art. There are many studios where they teach oriental dance. However, here the costume of the dancer will play an important role. In order to choose it, you need to decide on the style. Colors need to be selected not only so that the viewer likes them, but also so that they suit you directly.

From the history

The traditional Arabic costume completely hides the body of the dancer. He leaves open only the feet, hands and face. However, on the stage we seem to be completely different: the outfit consists only of a bodice, belt and skirt. The design of such ensembles was specially developed so that the girls would perform in the cabaret. The outfit was additionally decorated with glass beads, fringe and rhinestones.

The first months of training: how to dress?

Of course, the costumes of oriental dancers are beautiful and spectacular, but if you are just starting to engage in this type of dance, then immediately acquire something like this is completely optional. Remember that in the first months of training, your body will undergo numerous changes. The waist will become more elegant, fat deposits will disappear.





Starting to learn belly dance is best in breeches or leggings and a short T-shirt or top. Further, over time, it will be possible to purchase elements of the outfit for oriental dances, such as, for example, a belt with coins, the ringing of which will create additional motivation and a good mood during training.

How to choose the outfit for oriental dance

Before choosing an outfit for belly dancing, you need to decide on the style. If you want a folk costume, then you will need to display a traditional ensemble in it. If you are a lover of the style of Rax el Sharqi, Oriental and Cabaret, today costumes with tight skirts based on stretch are relevant. For them, as a rule, curly loincloths are used of complex shape, but many do without them at all. But in this case, it will be necessary to embroider the upper part of the skirt with fringed beads or monists. A great addition to the costume will be beautiful jewelry.

Before choosing a costume for belly dancing, you should think about where you are going to use it. At a performance in front of the audience? At a dance competition? In an ordinary workout?

Remember that the image must be chosen to the music and style of dance. This also includes the choice of fabric for the outfit and its color.

Hipbands: Photos, Ideas, and Decor

Belly dancers often use various accessories and attributes for the dance. These can be shawls and scarves, a loincloth, a veil, a saber, a candelabrum, a cane and other accessories. They give the room a unique and zest.





Hipbands and belts for belly dancing are a special part of an oriental costume. Since the belt should sit tight on the hips during the dance, not twist or lose its shape, it is made of dense fabric, which is sewn to a soft and elastic base. The loincloths are embroidered with sequins, beads, beads, and their bottom is decorated with monists or fringe. When the dancer performs the movement with her hips, the monist and fringe rhythmically tinkle and hesitate, riveting all the attention of the viewer.

Belt for oriental dance: fabric, jewelry. Can I do it myself?

Judging by the classical canons, one of the obligatory elements of a costume for oriental dance is a scarf, belt or loincloth. A girl can decorate it with coins or beads, and most often both are used at the same time. During the performance of the dance, they gently ring to the beat of the dancer's movements, creating a sense of peace and magic of the eastern night. The loincloths for oriental dances are the last and complementary touch of your image.

How to use a loincloth for belly dance? Elementary: you just need to tie it around the belt. If you decide to make a scarf for belly dancing on your own, then you should carefully approach its shape, decor and choice of fabric.





Colors can be very diverse: red, blue, yellow, green and others. You can choose a belt for belly dancing in the color of the costume, or you can choose a contrasting shade - this choice remains exclusively with the dancer!

Remember that belts of different sizes behave differently during dance movements. As a rule, the length and width of the product do not exceed one meter.

You can sew a simple scarf with almost no decor, or you can create a richly decorated belt with a lot of sequins, fringe and monists. Additional elements of the costume usually behave very beautifully while performing dance movements, introducing entertainment into it and captivating the viewer.





The shapes available for the loincloth are different: it can be a circle, a rectangle, a semicircle, or a triangle. In order to calculate the most convenient option for yourself, you need to experiment with different models. So you will understand which shape of the belt is most convenient for you.

In most cases, the belt for oriental dance is sewn from light fabrics, such as, for example, chiffon. A leather loincloth will only make the costume heavier and may even interfere with the performance of the dance. A flying transparent fabric, like a chiffon, will not hamper movements, while beautifully fitting the silhouette.

The second most popular fabric for the belt is velvet. Luxurious fabric will emphasize the beauty of the figure and the refined taste of the dancer, as well as create a sense of oriental sophistication. Velvet loincloths look especially advantageous not only because of the soft shine of the fabric, but also shimmering coins brighter against its background.





In addition to these fabrics, for a loincloth you can use organza, stretch, satin, crepe chiffon.

We have already said a lot about the design, but it should be recalled that it is better to make the belt simpler. Remember that an excess of beads and a monist will not only overweight the fabric, but can also spoil the entire appearance of the costume.
