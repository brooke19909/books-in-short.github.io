Torn colored paper application is a fascinating activity for children of any age.

You can attract children to creativity at any age. They are happy to draw, sculpt, glue and cut. Torn colored paper applique is a fascinating kind of children's art. In addition, even at an early age, the child will be able to cope with the task. The main thing is to choose a suitable picture for him.

Materials for work

Torn application of colored paper does not require complex skills, abilities and tools. All that is needed for work is scissors, colored paper, a base sheet, a pencil and glue.





Colored paper of the desired colors is cut into small pieces. Their size can vary from 0.5 to 3 cm. The younger the child, the larger the details should be. Sheets of paper are easy to tear; children can be involved in this activity. They will be happy to do a fun job. As materials for work, you can use the pages of glossy magazines and advertising catalogs. They are great for work.

Performance technique

A drawing that does not contain small parts is applied to the base sheet. For a technique such as torn applique from colored paper, templates can be printed on the Internet or you can take ready-made pictures from children's coloring. It‚Äôs easy to sketch yourself.





The areas that need to be glued are greased with glue, and pieces of paper of a suitable color are laid out on it. The child needs to fill out the outline of the picture to get a picture.

Recommendations for work

For young children who need to be carried away for a short time, you can simply grease the sheet with glue and suggest making a clearing or carpet-plane. So the baby will not worry if it does not get into the circuit.





Younger children can be offered simple pictures. For example, an applique made of paper on the theme "Autumn" is suitable. An adult needs to draw a tree trunk, which is painted over or glued brown. The child sticks leaves from pieces of paper suitable for the theme of colors: red, yellow, orange. We must not forget about the fallen and flying leaves in the air, so that the picture is truly autumn. Such a collage can be done by several children. An application made of paper on the theme "Autumn" will be a decoration of a children's room or a card for any holiday.





Children who already know how to hold scissors in their hands can complicate the task. For work, they can cut sheets of colored paper into small pieces. Templates for working in the technique of torn applique from colored paper can be more complex than for kids. Children need to fill the contours with elements of the same color to make the picture complete. They can be offered to make a bright parrot or create a complex picture consisting of various components. The finer the elements are cut out, the more varied and colorful the torn applique from colored paper can be.

High art

The torn applique technique is used not only in children's works. American artist Derek Goris creates collages from scraps of glamorous magazines. In his hands, torn paper turns into amazing masterpieces in beauty and style. The creations of the master are able to decorate not only the boring interior of the apartment. Many global brands use the work of Goris as an advertisement.

Passion for children of any age

Torn colored paper applications are a great way to keep your kids busy. After all, there is no need to strictly observe the contour or follow a clear form. This is a real flight of imagination and scope for creativity. For work, you can use the remains of colored paper, which was used for applications of a different kind. This is a real waste-free production, because even the smallest pieces go into business. Children of any age create their bright works with pleasure and rapture, and adolescents make more complex collages on the subject. This technique can be combined with a pattern or patch application.





This type of creativity perfectly develops fine motor skills, precise movements, accuracy, attentiveness and perseverance. Toddlers learn to distinguish colors and make up compositions. Torn application is a fascinating and fun activity that can take a little fidget for a long time. Yes, and adults will like it. This is a great way to spend time with children.
