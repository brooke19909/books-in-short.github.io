Is someone embroidering now?

Despite the fact that embroidery is now necessarily included in the comprehensive school curriculum, not everyone knows it well. A rare schoolgirl will be able to make even a small picture with a flower with a needle and thread, not to mention the cross-stitch of cats. Masterfully executed works are now very popular and in great demand. Fashion for embroidered things is back. Of course, only a few will prefer to sit at home when the rest are having fun. However, there are lovers of needlework. And given the fashion for embroidery, modern ladies again took out the hoop from the past, wiped them from dirt and put to use.

Embroidery History

The oldest embroidered items date back to the 5th century BC. Such ancient canvases were found in China, which is considered the ancestor of all embroidery. But they invented embroidery even earlier. Embroidery was still among ancient people. Sewing themselves clothes from skins, ancient people noticed the unevenness of stitches and decided to decorate clothes with such stitches. In China, embroidered on thin silk fabrics, depicting natural motifs.

Ancient embroideries were found on other continents in various countries. Of course, each nation has its own way of mastering this, its own favorite colors, amulets, ornaments and motifs, from embroideries of nature landscapes to embroidery of representatives of the animal world. For example, cats were often embroidered with a cross. These animals were considered sacred, they were often depicted on fabric canvases. We can say that embroidery is one of the most ancient types of needlework.

Where to begin?

Embroidery is not a very simple art, but everyone can learn it. There is not even sexual separation. The world knows the names of many men who perfectly embroider and even put up their paintings for sale and collect exhibitions. In order to learn how to embroider, it will not take a lot of resources. It is enough to have a few things:

  1. Canvas, which you can buy in a fabric store.
  2. Thread floss. They can also be purchased at fabric or craft stores.
  3. Needles for embroidery. They differ from sewing with a blunt end and a large eye.

What is most often embroidered?

Despite the enormous popularity of embroidered shirts and dresses, the most popular are paintings. In the modern world, they have learned to create patterns not only of ornaments. Using different programs, you can create a diagram of your own photo or embroider a picture of a famous artist. Of course, such work is done by experienced craftswomen. Young children begin their work with small animal paintings: puppies, hamsters and parrots. Embroidery of a black cat with a cross is considered very popular.

Cross stitch cats

Cats are a hot topic for the modern world. Cross stitch cats and cats. Increasingly, they find many stories and meanings for them. It is believed that a cat embroidered with a cross brings luck and comfort to the house.

These animals in ancient times were sacred, they were even worshiped and protected. Like mystical animals, they carry the souls of the dead to the next world. Now there is no such belief. But the cat theme is one of the most popular. Such pets are embroidered both according to single patterns and when buying cross-stitch kits with cats. An embroidery kit is much more convenient than a single scheme. It already has everything you need to work. No need to worry about the size of the canvas, it is precisely tailored there for embroidery. The kit also has all the threads. There are exactly as many as required for embroidery or a little more. Search and buy yourself do not have to. Also sets for embroidery are equipped with needles and patterns. Cross stitch with cats is very diverse. You can meet not only colored cute cats or kittens.

What is monochrome embroidery?

There is also the so-called monochrome cross-stitch cats. This is a one-color way of doing work, most often using black. This kind of embroidery is also distinguished by its minimalist plots. Typically, such paintings depict several objects or only their outlines. Monochrome is also considered one of the simplest types of embroidery. It can not be mixed up shades. And counting crosses is easier when they are the same color. This embroidery is easy for beginners or students. There are a lot of motives for such embroidery: figures and shadows of various objects and plants, silhouettes of people or animals.

Among the various types of embroidery, people often choose a cross, not because its sign protects against evil, but because each person can do such creative work. And among the many topics and beautiful pictures, embroidered cats feel themselves win-win. These proud, beautiful, cute and funny pets boldly and confidently took their rightful places in the hearts of a whole planet of people.
