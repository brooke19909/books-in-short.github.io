Punta Cana hotels - on the way to Dominican Republic



Rest in Punta Cana today is preferred by hundreds of tourists. For those who do not know yet, Punta Cana is a beautiful area in the Dominican Republic, spread out in the eastern part of the island of Haiti. The spent vacation in Punta Canna is impossible to forget, as well as what you can see. Today I will tell you about the most popular hotels, because it is from them that the journey through Punta Cana originates.

Punta Cana hotels are diverse and not much alike. True, all of them are united by local, amazingly beautiful nature, chains of beaches, hot weather and a high level of service of guests and the friendliness of all the staff.

Caribe club princess

You can start your vacation in Punta Cana with accommodation at the Caribe Club Princess Hotel, located on the world famous Bavaro Beach. This cozy and cheerful hotel has been dubbed by many tourists a real "hit" of our time. The hotel offers 355 air-conditioned rooms with ceiling fans, balconies, mini-refrigerators, telephones, cable TVs, hair dryers and safes. A golf course is a 10-minute walk away. There are also tennis courts, a sauna, a jacuzzi, a place for diving, beauty salons, 6 restaurants, 4 bars, a disco and a night club. This place is suitable for family and youth recreation, as well as business meetings and conferences. Guided tours of local attractions are organized, and a variety of recreational activities are constantly held.

Sanctuary cap cana

Punta Cana hotels are never empty. Attracts many tourists and Sanctuary Cap Cana , considered one of the few paradises of the Dominican Republic. The hotel territory includes 9.5 km of coastline, most of which is occupied by beaches, one of the best golf clubs on the island, hundreds of cozy rooms, as well as a wide variety of center services. The rooms have different levels of comfort: there are both simpler and real palaces. True, the price of individual rooms bites very much. This place is chosen for their holidays by rich people not only from Haiti, but also from other countries of our land. The variety of local bars and restaurants is amazing. Each vacationer will be able to find what he likes best. For example, there is an Italian restaurant, as well as a gourmet restaurant that prefers original dishes and the opportunity to participate in creative presentations. A spa, hairdresser, jacuzzi, swimming pools, sauna and much more offer their services.

Barcelo bavaro palace

Punta Cana hotels located on Bavaro Beach are integrated into the large Barcelo Bavaro Beach Resort. An integral part of this complex is the Barcelo Bavaro Palace Hotel. If you decide to choose it for your vacation, you will surely be satisfied. The hotel has 594 rooms, each equipped with a double bed, a bathroom with a hairdryer, air conditioning, satellite TV, a telephone and a balcony with beautiful views of the local nature, beach and sea. The hotel has 5 bars, a very interesting cyber cafe, a children's pool with a jacuzzi, a gym, a theater, 2 boutiques, a jewelry salon, a hairdresser, a beauty salon and much more. Hotel guests can prove themselves in the game of golf, become a member of the football team, ride a bike to local attractions. Especially popular are scuba diving, sailing, catamarans and water skiing.
