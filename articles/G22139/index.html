Number of rooms

The hotel offers its guests comfortable accommodation in cozy and spacious apartments. There are 16 of them in the building. There are equipped non-smoking rooms. A loggia is attached to each apartment (35% of rooms with views of the Adriatic Sea).

All rooms have air conditioning and cable TV (with Russian channels). There is also Internet, telephone, TV, a compact bar with drinks (alcohol and juices - for a fee). In the apartments, the room is divided into several zones: a bedroom and a living room. In the latter there is a kitchen corner with all necessary household equipment.





Nutrition

Complex Villa Wumarc Cat C offers tourists standard food conditions: breakfast, half board (two times) and full board (three times). The choice of dishes is quite large. Alcoholic products for a fee, as well as natural juices and ice cream. On the ground floor of the villa there is a cozy bar with a wide range of alcoholic cocktails, snacks and desserts.

Beach

The equipped city beach extends no more than 500 m from Villa Wumarc Cat C. The beach area is well equipped with recreation areas, a rental inventory point (sun loungers, awnings) is open on the shore, there are a number of cafes and bars.

Additional Information

The infrastructure in the hotel is not rich and is mainly focused on picky tourists who come to swim in the sea, sunbathe, attend various excursions, and not spend all their time in the room.
