Titanic Deluxe Bodrum 5 * (Turkey, Bodrum): room description, service, reviews

Turkey is known as one of the most open countries for tourism. Here various conditions are created for a comfortable, interesting and varied pastime of various categories of visitors. One of the worthy options is the Titanic Deluxe Bodrum 5 * hotel. It offers quality service, interesting leisure for its guests.

Each hotel has a specific specialization. In order for the vacation to be organized correctly, you must initially consider all the conditions of accommodation, food and leisure. The reviews of our compatriots who have been here will help to understand this issue.

Room Description

Considering the description of rooms Titanic Deluxe Bodrum 5 *, it should be said that the internal organization may vary significantly depending on the type of apartment.



Each room has the necessary furniture. You can choose pillows. There is a safe (free), the necessary equipment and utensils for making tea and coffee. Nutrition

A variety of exotic dishes is offered to its guests by Turkey. "All inclusive" is the most appropriate system for catering. The hotel has a buffet restaurant. The food is varied. There is a children's menu. However, some dishes are very sharp, salty.



International and local dishes are served. There are vegetarian dishes. Large selection of meat, fish, fruits, desserts. Ice cream is also served on the beach. There is a bar on site. It presents a wide selection of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks. You can order cocktails.

The tourist complex also has a barbecue area. There is a snack bar on the beach. Here you can order beer, wine, various juices, non-alcoholic and alcoholic cocktails. If you want a snack, you can take snacks at the bar.

Service

The service at Titanic Deluxe Bodrum 5 * is diverse. There is a business center for conferences, meetings, corporate trainings. You can use fax, photocopy. There is access to wireless internet.

Shops, a mini market, and a souvenir shop are located on site. You can choose everything you need without leaving the hotel.



Reception is open around the clock. Individual registration is carried out. There are facilities and rooms for people with disabilities. You can rent a car, order a taxi. Laundry service is available.

Tickets can be ordered on site, a luggage room is available. A concierge greets guests at the entrance. Modern elevators deliver visitors to the floor. The staff speaks many languages, including Russian. You can exchange currency on the territory of the tourist complex, order plane or train tickets.

Entertainment

Titanic Deluxe Bodrum 5 * has its own beach. It is close to the hotel. Indoor and outdoor pools are available on site. There is a water park. On the beach you can go diving, water sports, fishing (including fishing). All the necessary equipment is rented.



There is a spa center, Turkish bath, beauty salon, massage room, sauna. A fitness center is available. In the evening and in the afternoon animators work. You can play darts, billiards, tennis and table tennis, board games. There are rental bicycles, sports equipment.

In the evening, you can visit the night club. Interesting excursions are organized. Programs are designed for various categories of tourists. Particular attention is paid to children's leisure. There is a play club for kids, there is a playground. Babysitting services are available for a fee.
