Belek (Turkey) - a great vacation on the Mediterranean coast

Belek (Turkey) is located on the picturesque coast of the Mediterranean Sea, in the province of Antalya. It is considered one of the modern resorts, there are all conditions for a comfortable stay. Not only is it famous for its chic hotels and unique beaches, it is also world famous for its golf center.

Location Features

The city of Belek (Turkey) is located only twenty-five kilometers from Antalya Airport. This picturesque resort is nestled between the Mediterranean Sea and the majestic Taurus Mountains. Belek is surrounded by beautiful pine and eucalyptus forests. It is in this place, in the wild corners of untouched nature, that a giant carriage tortoise is found. Forests are overflowing with unique species of birds, many of them are quite rare on the planet.





Climatic conditions

The climate of Belek (Turkey) is Mediterranean with a subtropical slope. Summer is very dry and hot, winters are warm, but with frequent rains. This area has a huge number of sunny days per year, about three hundred. Thanks to the mountain range and pine forests, the city ​​is protected from cold air currents. In April, the beach season begins in Belek. It continues until the end of October.





Hotels in Belek

In the vicinity of the city there are a huge number of luxury hotels. These are modern buildings with an original design and comfortable conditions. Tours to Turkey, to Belek, are offered by various companies, to go to this city on vacation means to get a lot of positive emotions and impressions.

Entertainment and sports

Holidays in Belek (Turkey) is famous for excellent conditions for lovers of many sports. People come here to go water-skiing, windsurfing and paralyzing. And for divers, the coastal waters of Belek will become a real paradise. These water zones are inhabited by many different marine inhabitants, among them there are turtles and octopuses, jellyfish, squids, dolphins and fur seals. Mysterious underwater caves contain shipwrecks and possibly treasures. Another interesting activity that Belek offers is rafting. All conditions for this water sport are created on the Kapryuchay River. Fans of outdoor activities will be offered to take a ride on bicycles or ATVs.



Souvenirs

Resting in the city of Belek (Turkey), do not forget to purchase souvenirs. At the main bazaar, which is located between Ataturk and Aligentikaya streets, a large selection of various pottery, clothing, souvenirs, jewelry is offered. Be sure to buy a piece of the East, and it will remind you of the bewitching city of Belek (Turkey).

sights

Among the attractions, it is worth noting a unique reserve park where you can do horseback riding, mountain climbing, and just walk in the shade of cypress and eucalyptus trees. Only 25 minutes away are Antalya and Side, where many museums and monuments are concentrated. It will also be interesting to visit the ruins of the ancient cities of Aspendos and Perge.
