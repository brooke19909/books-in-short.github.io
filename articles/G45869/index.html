Frog pattern: sew a funny toy with your own hands

Children are very fond of soft toys. Funny animals made of fabric, fur and felt are favorite attributes in the children's room. Kids play with them, sleep, decorate the walls near the bed with soft toys. Mom-needlewomen are happy to make funny little animals out of fabric for their crumbs.

Frog Sewing Ideas

Among soft toys, school-age children really like unusual animals: dragons, lizards, crocodiles, frogs.

A toy made from a frog pattern has many interesting variations. You can sew pot-bellied Kwaka from the cartoon "Thumbelina" or the unusual Princess Frog from a Russian fairy tale. Funny characters of frogs will become a favorite toy for children or decorate a shelf in a children's room.

Felt toy

Soft toys are voluminous or flat. The second are made of felt and have many uses. A frog made of felt made according to a funny pattern is used as a keychain, pendant, Christmas tree toy, and theater character.

You can sew it in an hour. For the manufacture of toys we will prepare the necessary materials:

  • frog pattern;
  • white chalk;
  • hot glue;
  • scissors, needle;
  • pieces of felt green and red;
  • synthetic winterizer;
  • red and green threads;
  • two buttons (for the eye);
  • bright chintz shreds.

Operating procedure:

  1. We cut out the pattern and transfer it to the fabric.
  2. Cut out the details from felt.
  3. Paired parts need to be fixed, to smooth uneven edges.
  4. A felt toy is sewn from the front, so the seams should be extremely even and neat. Sheathed frog legs, leaving a small hole and filling the inside with a padding polyester.
  5. From a bright plain fabric we cut a circle. We pull it with a thread in a circle, we stuff it with a synthetic winterizer. It turns out a red uneven flagellum. This will be the frog's collar.
  6. Glue eyes on the front side of the head, embroider a smile with red threads.
  7. We attach the second part of the head and carefully sew it. We leave an incision at the bottom of the head and put in the filler.
  8. Sew the upper legs of the "quack". So that the synthetic winterizer easily fills the legs, push it with a pencil.
  9. The following is the assembly of the toy. The upper legs are sewn to the body, closer to the neck. From above we fasten the collar, which covers the place of connection of the legs with the body.
  10. Last sew on the head.
  11. It is not difficult to make a skirt or shirt for a frog from pieces of chintz. A soft toy frog will become even more interesting.
