Cottages in Opaliha, Nizhny Novgorod Region: description, reviews

Cottage villages today are gaining more and more popularity among potential residents and real estate buyers. And all because a private cottage located near the city in an ecologically clean and picturesque place is a great opportunity to enjoy all the delights of country life without depriving yourself of developed urban infrastructure. Opalihi cottages located in a suburb of Nizhny Novgorod, this time fell into our review. We will appreciate the cottages, the level of comfort that all residents can count on, as well as the reviews of all those who have already managed to visit the facility and acquire a dream home.





about the project

Cottages in Opaliha, Nizhny Novgorod Region - a unique opportunity to acquire your own home near the metropolis. The project is based on the format of a house in the village that is so popular these days, combining a number of undeniable advantages. Instead of a three-room apartment with an improved layout, which for many Nizhny Novgorod residents is a real dream, you will get an individual house with a plot of land of 8 acres. This option is a godsend for a large and friendly family, especially if it has small children. The developer opted for a free layout of cottages, which allows each family to equip the interior of the house at their discretion.

Location

For the construction of cottages in Opaliha, a site was chosen near the federal highway M-7, namely a village of the same name, which is located only 11 km from Nizhny Novgorod.





Excellent transport accessibility is one of the main advantages of cottages in Opaliha, Nizhny Novgorod region. Reviews of the first residents of the village and those who managed to visit the construction site emphasize that you can get to it not only by private car, but also by public transport.

Infrastructure of the village

Opalihi cottages are located surrounded by all necessary infrastructure. Residents can use the kindergarten and secondary school of the village of Zhdanovsky. Just 4 km from the cottages - the largest shopping and entertainment center of the city, "Mega". Here you can not only make the necessary purchases, but also have a great time with family and friends.





A good asphalt road is what residents of country houses and villages dream of. So the builder of cottages in Opaliha provided for everything to the smallest detail, paving a new asphalt road from the city to the cottages. Moreover, smooth asphalt paths appeared on the territory of the village, all of them are lit. In the near future, shops, cafes, service organizations will appear on the territory, allowing residents of the village to use the necessary benefits without leaving it.

Communications

When buying a house in the country, it is important to pay special attention to the availability of necessary communications. Cottages in Opaliha are no exception. Reviews of residents who managed to not only visit the construction site, but also live in the rented cottage, are enthusiastic. Residents note that the house has everything that is necessary for a full comfortable life: a gas double-circuit boiler and a water pump ensure uninterrupted supply of hot water to the house and full heating of the cottage in the cold season, a rough water filter provides all residents with clean tap water, each house has an individual sewer system, electrical wiring is carried out, sockets and switches are installed. The developer took over communications management, under his control their normal functioning.





House plans

If you look at the photos of cottages in Opaliha, you will surely fall in love with the project at a glance. The buyers attributed the free planning to the main advantages of the project, they appreciated the opportunity to organize the space at their discretion. A house of 100 square meters with a plot of eight hundred square meters is enough to equip a kitchen, living room, bedrooms, pantry and bathrooms.

Each cottage has been finished: the first tenants emphasize that the plastering and leveling of the walls, the screed are finished without complaints, the surfaces are completely ready for the subsequent decorative decoration.





House price

Cottages in Opalihe with a plot are sold at a price of 3.7 million rubles. The developer offers the opportunity to purchase his dream home not only for cash, but also on mortgage terms. Those who took advantage of the offer note that the conditions are one of the most favorable in the city. The reduced interest rate on the loan is primarily provided by the cooperation of the developer with the largest banks of the city.
