El Mouradi El Menzel (Djerba): hotel description, reviews

Our article may interest those tourists who are planning a vacation in Tunisia. Recently, the resorts of this country have become quite popular due to reasonable prices and a good coast. As a good option for a budget holiday, the El Mouradi El Menzel Djerba hotel can be.

A bit about the hotel

El Mouradi El Menzel Djerba is located on the east coast of Djerba, in a quiet and peaceful place, four kilometers from the town of Agir, six kilometers from the city of Midoun and twenty-two kilometers from the capital of the island of Houmt Souk. It will not be difficult for tourists to get to the hotel, since the distance from Djerba Airport is about 28 kilometers.

The complex is focused not only on family vacations, but also on active youth, so people of completely different ages can spend a good vacation here.



El Mouradi El Menzel Djerba is popular with tourists because of its location, as it is built on the coast. In addition, the hotel belongs to the economy class at a cost. As a rule, each hotel has its own rating based on the ratio of price and quality. So, this indicator at the El Mouradi Djerba Menzel complex is 8.6, which indicates a fairly good level for a four-star establishment.

I would like to note that the hotel will become a wonderful vacation spot for those who seek to move away from the hustle and bustle of the city and spend some time in the lap of nature. Every year the hotel receives many guests, since the complex is impressive in size. In total, the hotel has 670 apartments.

The hotel was built on the seashore in a picturesque park back in 1971, and in 1998 it was completely renovated. Currently, its territory is 33 hectares.

A bit about the resort

We have already mentioned the location of El Mouradi Djerba Menzel (Djerba). Djerba - Tunisia, more precisely, its southernmost point. The island has an area of ‚Äã‚Äã538 km sq., And its coastline stretches for one hundred and twenty kilometers. There are two ways to get to the resort: by ferry from the mainland (the journey takes no more than ten minutes) or by plane to Djerba International Airport.

The island has a long history, now it is not known exactly who first discovered it, but it is believed that this merit belongs to the Phoenicians. For many centuries, the Romans were owned by the Romans, the Carthaginians. Currently, the island is considered the most important fishing center in the whole country. The main port of the island is the capital of Houmt Souk. In its restaurants, tourists can enjoy the freshest and most delicious seafood dishes. Djerba is sometimes called Mediterranean Tahiti because of the endless sandy beaches washed by the warm waters. The island was built up with modern hotels due to its excellent climate. The sea near its shores is always warm and does not have significant temperature fluctuations, so you can swim and sunbathe on local beaches throughout the year. On the one hand, island tourist life is not as active as on the mainland, and on the other, guests can enjoy clean beaches and sea water.



On Djerba, cultures of different nations merged together, for example, Jews live quietly next to Muslims. The island is very beautiful. This is absolutely not a desert area, on the contrary, a huge number of olive groves grow here due to the fact that there are many underground sources on Djerba. And in the southwestern part lies a shady wetland. It is here that you can see huge flocks of flamingos, white herons, gulls.

Local Attractions

If you are resting in El Mouradi Djerba Menzel 4 * (Tunisia), then you should definitely see the most interesting places on the island. Of course, it cannot be said that Djerba is the center of the sights of all Tunisia, but there are beautiful places here, you can see them in between beach vacations. Each hotel has its own guides offering standard excursion programs (prices for them can not be called cheap). However, most attractions can be reached by taxi, public transport or rented car. The advantage of independent trips is the possibility of a calm and leisurely viewing of local beauties, which is not available in group trips.

Many attractions are located in the city of Houmt Souk, located in the north of the island. It is interesting to walk along the streets, look at local shops and the market. In the endless labyrinths of the city you can see three ancient mosques.

On the shore near the city is the fortress of Bordj el Kebir. Once it was a defensive structure, but now it recalls its former greatness.



The main attraction of the island is the synagogue of El Grib. It is the oldest synagogue in all of Africa. It is a place of worship for Jews from around the world. It is located near the village of Riyadh.
