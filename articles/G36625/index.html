Villa Jadran 2 * Montenegro, Budva Riviera: overview, features and reviews of tourists

Villa Jadran 2 * is a small two-star hotel located on the busy street of the city of Budva in Montenegro. The hotel is simple, but comfortable enough, it is perfect for a budget holiday with the whole family.

Hotel Villa Jadran (Montenegro, Budva) is located on 2 lines from the beach, just 200 meters from the Adriatic coast, at the address: Budva, St. 13 Jul 8. If tourists fly to Tivat Airport, then the hotel is only 27 km away, from Podgorica it is 75 km further.

A little about the location

Villa Jadran 2 * in Montenegro is located near the central part of the beautiful and ancient city of Budva. It was founded on the Adriatic coast in Montenegro more than 2500 years ago.

The ancient history of the city is visible to tourists in every stone, building, churches and fortress walls. Arriving in Budva, vacationers not only enjoy beautiful pebble beaches with clear water, but also plunge into the ancient history of this amazing country.





Beautiful rocky mountains covered with prickly shrubs surround the city from the coast. In addition to the beauty of architecture and natural attractions, you can also visit recreational activities. The city is fully adapted for tourist holidays, because for many years Budva has been considered the largest resort place in Montenegro.

Having settled in the Villa Jadran 2 * hotel, you can safely walk along the main attractions of the city, because they are very close to go. There are many shops, bars, cafes in the district.

With children, you can visit the city's water park with many slides and attractions.

Hotel Description

The hotel is a simple brick building with three floors. Hotel rooms at Villa Jadran 2 * total 16. They are designed for accommodation from 2 to 4 people. The rooms are quite spacious, although decorated without any complaints. The hotel has internet access via wireless Wi-Fi, and free for residents.





The hotel does not have its own restaurant, but on the floor there is a spacious and fully equipped kitchen where tourists can prepare food purchased at a supermarket near the hotel. This will significantly save money and will be a salvation for mothers with children. Every experienced traveler knows how difficult it is to find food for children in restaurants and cafes, especially if the baby needs a diet or an allergic child. When it is possible to cook food yourself, you can feel at home.

Paid breakfasts

If you do not want to cook yourself, then you can order breakfast at a hotel Villa Jadran 2 (Montenegro, Budva) for a fee at the time of booking. But they will be cooked in a hotel located next door.





Guests will have to go for breakfast at the LM Budva, 150 meters away. To many, this does not seem very convenient, but for travelers who plan to go on an excursion around the surroundings for a whole day, it is better to have a hearty breakfast before a trip to a neighboring hotel, and not to stand at the stove early in the morning.

Room Quality

All 16 rooms are equipped with the most necessary for a comfortable stay. 14 numbers are represented by type DBL (double room). These are standard one-room double rooms with one double bed.

At Villa Jadran in Montenegro (Budva Riviera) there are also two TRPL rooms, i.e. triple. This is a triple room with single and one double bed.





The room has a TV, electric kettle, refrigerator, hairdryer, small safe. Almost every room has its own bathroom with shower, toilet and washbasin. The floors are tiled.

Almost all rooms have a balcony or terrace. Only one - without a balcony, and in four the bathroom is located on the floor.

Room decor

The interior of the rooms of the hotel Villa Jadran (Montenegro), according to the reviews of tourists staying in the hotel, is not at all impressive. The poor situation does not even correspond to the two-star status. Many were shocked by the old furniture and appliances, comparing it with the situation in Soviet times.



Visitors liked that the refrigerator is not only in the shared kitchen, but also in the rooms, and not small. Things can be safely placed in a large wardrobe. Small personal items are placed in bedside tables. There are enough sockets, you can safely put all the gadgets on charge for the night.

Bathroom

In the bathroom, everything is also simple: the drain tank is plastic, the cheapest, the shower is not separated from the main room by a curtain of not the first freshness. Hotel tourists are given only toilet paper. The rooms are not cleaned every day, towels are changed every 5 days.





According to tourists' reviews, we can conclude that in some rooms there were problems with the plumbing: the shower broke or leaked.

Positive reviews

Villa Jadran has been rated positively for the quality of the rooms. Many people like the presence of a kitchen and a working oven, where tourists cooked baked freshly bought fish. In other hotels this is not possible.

Really like the location of the hotel. You can walk to the coastline in just a few minutes, just cross the road and go to the shore of the Adriatic Sea. Just keep in mind that umbrellas and deck chairs are paid. If you take a set for two people, then this pleasure will cost tourists 5 euros.

If you walk along the coast for about 15 minutes, then you get to the best beach in the city - Morgen.

Near the hotel there is an old market, and the center is convenient to get to. All attractions are nearby: both the fortress walls and the citadel.

Negative reviews

There are a lot of negative reviews about the Villa Jadran 2 hotel: people are unhappy with the furniture, which is constantly falling apart, and the plumbing, which is breaking.

Most of the complaints are about the very poor soundproofing of the rooms. I could hear a neighbor sneezing in the room opposite. All the sounds of street noise were in the room. At night, people could not fall asleep due to music playing on the beach in bars until 3 a.m., even ear plugs did not help.

Most reviews have criticized box spring beds, which are desperately crashing into the backs of sleeping travelers. And the pillows are called wooden.

On the ceiling, lighting in the style of "Illich lamp". Older people will understand this comparison well. But our people would have endured all this normally, if not for the noise outside the window. To regain strength spent on an active day, you need to sleep well. If this is not possible, then nervous exhaustion gradually sets in and rest becomes hell.

conclusions

Before you go on a trip to an unfamiliar place, carefully study the location of the hotel, see photos of experienced tourists. Remember that hotel owners take photos immediately after repair, so in reality you can be very disappointed.

Before choosing a hotel, read the reviews of tourists who have recently been there, experienced all the charms of life in this place and honestly appreciated the hotel. Only then can you book. Even if you agree to some inconvenience for the sake of saving money, then at least you will be mentally prepared for what you see.
