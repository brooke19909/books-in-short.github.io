Smart alarm clock with sleep phases: features, instructions and owner reviews

For those who like to sleep in the mornings and for whom it becomes a whole tragedy to get up early, a great device that has appeared on the market not so long ago is useful. This is a smart alarm clock that makes morning awakening easy and comfortable. It is about this interesting invention of humanity that the article will be discussed.





When is the best time to wake up?

Rest time does not pass monotonously. In a dream, one phase replaces another. The best option, according to scientists, is awakening during the mild phase. If the alarm clock wakes up at a time when the dream is deep, then you may feel bruised and feeling as if there was no night rest at all.

But there is such an option that, having overslept quite a bit, a person is charged with a lot of energy and has a great time all his next day. This, as a rule, occurs precisely because he woke up during the fast phase of sleep. Well, if a person can do it himself. Well, if that does not work out, then an excellent modern device comes to the rescue.





Smart Alarm Clock for Iphone

An alarm clock can be made, for example, as a separate device connected to a gadget on an Android or Iphone. But it can also be built into a mobile device or special so-called fitness bracelets.

If we are talking about a smartphone, then usually you should download special applications for it to activate a smart alarm clock. Android, for example, has a great option called Smart Alarm Clock.





Smart Sleep Time app

You can install Sleep Time on your iPhone. The application operates as follows: an alarm clock is set and placed next to the pillow. The screen should be pointing down. The phone will read the movements of a person and thus determine the onset phase of sleep. When the necessary time approaches for awakening, it is activated. Thus, according to the owners, a smart alarm clock with phases of sleep guarantees a pleasant morning with a sense of good spirits and good mood.

Smart Pillow App

Another great app is Pillow. Sleep tracking is performed using special sensors: a microphone and an accelerometer. In this way, movements during sleep and breathing are tracked. There are paid and free versions of the application. But the main function works in both of them. When the alarm goes off, the volume starts from zero and gradually reaches 70%. If at this time you touch the screen with your hand, the volume will decrease and after a few seconds the sound will disappear. But the mechanism will work again after ten minutes in the same mode.

Smart app Smart Alarm Clock

For Android, as mentioned, you can download Smart Alarm Clock. The application works in much the same way as the options described above. The following functions are available for it:

  • you can independently choose in which phase of sleep you want to wake up;
  • all sounds are recorded;
  • statistics on sleep cycles and their phases;
  • special music is offered for falling asleep, as well as for awakening;
  • weather forecast available.

This application is also intended for Android. He is considered one of the most unsuccessful. Usually a smart alarm clock with phases of sleep, trying to wake up its owner, falls silent and gives you a little more sleep, and then again declares itself. But this is definitely not a description of WakeUp OrDie! Alarm Clock. The device will ring until a green monster in it disappears. And for this, the smartphone needs to be shaken well.

There are practically no settings in this application, the owners note. All that can be done is to set the necessary time, turn on the vibrating function, and also select a smoothly growing melody.
