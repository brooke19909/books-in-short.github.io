Kabanaris Bay - a paradise in the south of Rhodes

Kabanaris Bay is built in the south of Rhodes, in the green of palm trees and blooming bougainvillea, on the first line from the world famous Kiotari beach.





The territory, although small, but completely landscaped. The hotel infrastructure includes mail with telephone and payphone, laundry, luggage storage, concierge services, lobby, dry cleaning. Parking in advance is not necessary. At the 24-hour reception you can arrange a transfer, rent a vehicle, use the depository safe. Those who wish can use the services of a tour desk that organizes affordable tours around the island and the surrounding area.



The hotel has a mini market where you can buy essentials around the clock.

There are many shops in the nearby village of Kiotari, in the evenings you can sit in a pub or Greek tavern, where sirtaki dance and drink local ouzo. The Palace of the Grand Master - attractions of the Old Town of Rhodes, can be reached in half an hour.

The distance to the nearest airport is 55, and to the seaport - 57 kilometers.

Rooms

Housing fund Kabanaris Bay is equipped in three-story snow-white buildings. Standard and family rooms are equipped with everything necessary. There is a telephone, a radio, a minibar, a large makeup mirror, and a work desk. In the combined bathrooms, a bathtub or shower, all the necessary hygiene accessories are constantly updated, a hair dryer works.



Balconies overlooking the Aegean Sea or internal gardens are equipped with a plastic table and chairs.

Ceramic flooring.

Upon request, an iron and ironing board are provided, as well as bed linen.



There are no rooms for wheelchairs . Pets are discussed in advance with the hotel manager. In all public areas you can connect to the Internet for free.

Nutrition

Kabanaris Bay Hotel offers all-inclusive meals to its tourists. Tables are served in the garden or on the outdoor terrace overlooking the blue expanses of the sea. According to this concept, local alcoholic beverages are free of charge.

Alternatively, exotic seafood and Greek cuisine can be ordered √† la carte in the evenings.

There are bars on the beach and next to the pool where you can enjoy freshly made fresh juices.

Beach

Kabanaris Bay Hotel is located next to Kiotari's Blue Flag Beach, which meets all eco-standards. The beach can be reached in three minutes. The entrance to the sea is sometimes pebble, so special shoes are sometimes needed.

Everything you need - umbrellas, towels, trestle beds and mattresses - on the beach is issued at no extra charge.

Additional Information

Kabanaris Bay has a small but always clean outdoor pool, around which there are always plenty of free sun loungers.



Those who wish can play table tennis, volleyball or spend time in the Internet cafe. Those interested in the history of Greece can visit Prasonisi, the neighboring island of Tilos. If desired, they can be accompanied by guides from the tour desk.

Guest reviews

Many who rested in Kabanaris Bay Hotel 3 * speak of him as a great opportunity to relax in Greece at budget prices. Most people like the fact that the hotel was built away from noise and civilization, in a secluded corner, where in some places nature is still wild.

Guests are satisfied with the kitchen and rooms, are grateful for the work of the staff. The only drawback noted by some vacationers are mosquitoes, for the fight with which special means are needed.
