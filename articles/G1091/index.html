Sights of Elektrostal: the history of the city, what to see, photos and reviews

Elektrostal is a fairly large industrial city, which is located near the river Vohonki and almost 40-50 km from the eastern part of Moscow. There are a lot of powerful factories where many residents of Elektrostal work. They combine perfectly with the beautiful green boulevards, streets and parks, bright lawns and flower beds that adorn almost the entire town. Amazing ponds and forests give it picturesqueness. It is worthwhile to learn more about the main attractions of Elektrostal, photos of which are in the article.

History of the city

The city was founded in 1916 thanks to the construction of a metallurgical plant. Earlier on this place was the tract Calm. To begin construction, the labor of local peasants was used. In the middle of 1925, the first railway line was built here, which connected Elektrostal with Moscow. After the onset of 1938, the village turned into a separate city.

It is noteworthy that during the Great Patriotic War, the ammunition that was needed for victory was produced at the electric power plants. In particular, it is worth noting the manufacture of the legendary "Katyusha".

A new stage for the factories of the city began after atomic weapons were invented. The local plant was on the list of the main industries of the nuclear industry. This led to the fact that in 1954 the production of fuel, which was used for the operation of nuclear power plants, was established.

In October 1963, the cultural center "October" was built, and the masters of the Novo-Kramatorsk Plant, named after Stalin, did this. A spacious hall for spectators was arranged here, which could accommodate more than 850 people, as well as a unique rotating stage, so that the actors could change the scenery in a few seconds.

Machine-building plant

This is a fairly large and old production in the city, which specializes in the production of fuel for local gas stations. The plant is part of the structure of the TVEL FC company of the Rosatom state corporation. The general director of the enterprise is O. L. Sedelnikov.

This plant produces nuclear fuel, which operates many nuclear power plants, transport power plants and research reactors, not only in Russia but also in other European countries. The company has quality certificates OHSAS 18001, ISO 14001, ISO 9001.

The history of the plant is presented in the museum of the plant, old photographs of the city streets are shown, there are achievements of workers, as well as photos of significant people, there are many of the most interesting models produced at the engineering plant. Presentations showing the principles of the enterprise are also stored here.

Church of the Righteous John of Kronstadt

This temple - an attraction of Elektrostal - is a single-dome centric church, which is made in the style of Byzantine times. It was erected in the XX century, in the immediate vicinity of St. Andrew's Church. The temple was designed by T.V. Trubnikova and V.A. Drozdov, according to the development of which the iconostasis and interior were exactly designed.

The church plays the role of a baptismal church and belongs to the Moscow diocese. The main shrines of the church are the oldest and most valuable icons of Nikandor of Gorodnoezersky and the great martyr Panteleimon. In addition, particles of the relics of saints are stored here. In the temple services are held only during religious holidays.

City park of rest and culture

The local park - the attraction of Elektrostal - is an ideal place where everyone can stay alone with nature, ride on numerous attractions, and also spend time at the slot machines. In particular, the leisure park is popular in the summer - it is full of families, youth companies and couples in love.

Especially for the summer in 2013, the park was redeveloped, which made it possible to fit more modern playgrounds that were more entertaining and interesting for young visitors. Fans will enjoy the dragon and swans, and trampolines were installed for more active children.

It is noteworthy that the local park has fairly affordable prices: the cost of a ticket for various attractions ranges from 30-100 rubles. Today it is called "Miracle Park", and it is divided into a quieter "Quiet Alley" and "Entertainment Alley", where there is an inexpensive cafe, summer stage, as well as quite a few attractions.

"Movie Gallery"

To display 2D and 3D movies, they use exclusively modern equipment: Dolby Digital Surround EX and MasterImage speakers, as well as huge screens with a special coating. Very comfortable conditions for visitors were created in all cinemas: climate control and soft chairs.
