Hotel "Minima", Kuzminki: address, description, photos and reviews

Hotel "Minima" in Kuzminki is a mini-hotel, which is located just a three-minute walk from the metro station "Kuzminki". In addition, the hotel has its own free parking for guests. It is possible to pay for accommodation not only per day, but also per night or even per hour. The hotel attracts many tourists and travelers with favorable rates for accommodation. All rooms have an eye-pleasing interior, modern appliances, climate control are installed.

Hotel Features

Hotel "Minima" in Kuzminki is ideal to stay in Moscow for a few hours to rest, take a shower, put yourself in order and get a good night's sleep. In addition, this is an optimal and affordable option for travelers and business travelers who come to the capital on business or want to see as many attractions as possible.

Room reservation service is provided around the clock. If necessary, the staff of the Minima Hotel in Kuzminki will call a taxi for you. Each room, without exception, has a shower and a bathroom.

It should be noted that in Moscow there is a whole series of Minima hotels. In addition to Kuzminki, hotels are also located in the vicinity of Belorusskaya, Dynamo, Baumanskaya, Vodny Stadion, and Airport metro stations.

Location

The address of the Minima Hotel in Kuzminki is 113, Volgogradsky Prospekt, Building 5. It is located in the Southeast Administrative District of Moscow. In walking distance - almost any public transport: bus, metro, trolley. That is why the hotel is considered especially convenient for tourists and guests who are interested in having their place of residence geographically located in the southeast or south of Moscow.

Knowing the address of the Minima Hotel in Kuzminki, you can easily navigate in this area. From here it is only 390 meters walk to the Kuzminki metro station, 1.9 km to the Tekstilshchiki station, 2.4 km to the Volzhskaya station.

Good landmarks will be Glory Square, the Moscow Provincial Theater, located nearby. In walking distance - Volgogradsky Prospekt.

You can reserve a room in advance by phone number of the Minima Hotel in Kuzminki. You can also clarify the address in this case, ask how best to get to the hotel.

Important information for travelers: from the hotel - about half an hour by public transport to the Kursky railway station. You can reach Domodedovo and Ostafyevo airports in 40 minutes, and Vnukovo in around 50.
