Complex infrastructure

For the convenience of its elite residents, who, according to the developer, have passed the most severe selection, the Golden Keys residential complex (Moscow) provides for almost complete infrastructure. It begins with its own service operation. There is also equipment for residential buildings with supply and exhaust ventilation, central air conditioning and individual heating point. In addition, on the territory of the complex there are almost all socially significant infrastructure objects: a supermarket, a bank branch, a cafe-restaurant. For recreation and entertainment there is a club, sauna, spa. For sports, there is a well-equipped multifunctional sports complex, which includes a gym, a swimming pool, a football field, and even a tennis court.

Living in such a luxurious complex, as a rule, implies the presence of your own motor vehicle. The benefits of living in complexes

The indicated two LCDs (Golden Keys and Golden Keys 2) are places where it is very pleasant to live. Both the first and second housing estates were conceived as a place exclusively for representatives of the elite of the society, so buying apartments here even with the declared value was not so easy and simple.
