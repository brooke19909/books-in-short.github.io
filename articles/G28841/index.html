Teksta Robot Dog - Baby's Best Friend!

Every child dreams of a pet. Such a desire is not always feasible, because the animals need appropriate care, which is sometimes difficult for parents to provide. Teksta Robot Dog is a novelty in the field of robotic children's toys. The article describes the description of the pet, its features.

Puppy description

Often parents and close relatives do not know what to give the child for the holiday. Among the options for surprise are considered a variety of robots, including animals. The Teksta Robot Dog from Manley Toys is a novelty in the toy market that has interested many parents.

The pet was given the ability to respond to voice and gestures, he also responds to touch, barks, runs and jumps.

The toy is a complete imitation of a pet, and a special programming technology allows it to perform all the actions that are available to a real dog. Namely:

  • move the tail and ears;
  • turn your head;
  • move your paws;
  • run and jump;
  • A feature of the robot is the ability to flip back.

The interactive Teksta robot dog also changes the position of the body, ears, tail, depending on the mood. For example, if you do not play with him for a long time, then the pet begins to feel sad.

The puppy has luminous eyes built-in, according to which it is easy to understand the emotions of the pet: joy, sadness, surprise or tiredness.

Toy characteristics

The Teksta Puppy Robot Dog is completely made of plastic. Management is based on a microprocessor and built-in sensors. Today, the puppy is considered the most innovative toy.

The height of the pet is 19 cm and the body length is 21 cm. It is sold in two colors - white-blue and white-pink.

On the top of the head there are keys for individual customization. The user will be able to:

  • change the color of the eyes of the pet;
  • teach new skills and teams.

Also, programming, pet training can be done through an application installed on a smartphone or tablet.

Complete with a puppy there is a ball that the pet will play, and a bone that he will nibble. To feed the dog, you need to bring the bone with the side on which the arrow is located to the chin and it will light up. To play with your pet, you need to bring the ball to his mouth. Thanks to the magnet, the toy will stick and the dog will start making chewing sounds.

The puppy robot is sold in a transparent cardboard box. In the kit there is an instruction in Russian, a puppy and his toys.

Management is carried out using 4 AA batteries. The stone also needs power - 3 round batteries type AG13 / LR44.

Pet Features

An interactive dog is a very interesting toy not only for children, but also for adults. The pet is in constant motion, requires attention, feeding, training.

Key features of the Teksta Robot Dog include:

  • application of interactive gesture control technology;
  • animation of the movements of a real dog;
  • recognition of voice, gestures and touches;
  • the pet sleeps at night and may cry if it is not paid attention to;
  • the ability to customize the puppy's abilities;
  • the robot changes eye color depending on the mood;
  • can communicate and interact with other Teksta robots.
