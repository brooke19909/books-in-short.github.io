What quality of accommodation and level of comfort can be offered by the Astreas Beach 3 * hotel complex (Protaras)? In their reviews, tourists speak positively about the hotel. Distance to the beach, comfort and entertainment on the beach





What in terms of water recreation can the Astreas Beach 3 * hotel offer? Cyprus, Protaras in particular, is famous for its golden coasts. Only 50 meters away is one of the most beautiful beaches on the island - Fig Three Bay. This is a public site, but the beach line is wide, and even with an abundance of tourists there is enough space not only for relaxation, but also for sports.

You can rent sun loungers with elastic mattresses and umbrellas - it costs only a few euros. By the way, as a bonus, you get the right to use Internet access on the beach for 24 hours. The coast is clean, and the sand is fine and soft. The water is incredibly clear and warm - many tourists prefer to snorkel, exploring the underwater sights and the diversity of the local fauna.

Of course, if you want, you can spend time more actively - go boating or water skiing, do parasailing, try yourself in surfing and scuba diving. On the shore there is even a playground for volleyball and football.

Leisure at the hotel: what to count on?
