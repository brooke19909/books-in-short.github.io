Goncharovsky Park (Moscow): photos and reviews of tourists

On the edge of Moscow, in a beautiful picturesque corner around a natural lake, there is a cozy park of cultural recreation and leisure - Goncharovsky Park. Rustaveli - the street on which it is located. The area in which lush green lands are located has a four-hundred-year history.

Lianozovsky park

The wealthy landowner Prince Kurakin built the estate on the present territory, created a garden and dug a pond, which is today. Once, his house was visited by Krylov, Fonvizin, Rokotov. After the war with the French, there was a lot of destruction, but new houses were gradually rebuilt. And in the nineteenth century, the Moscow businessman Lianozov began to own the manor house, thanks to which a village appeared for Moscow dacha owners Lianozovo.

Park - decoration of the area

To this day, Lianozovsky Park, which adorns the surroundings of the neighborhood with its greenery, is a favorite vacation spot among the indigenous people . A hundred years ago, capital gentlemen led their ladies along the alleys of the park, and now it is a favorite place for modern youth and local residents. It was in this green land that entertaining attractions first appeared. The authorities began to hold mass public events - thanks to this, the park received the status of a state institution.



Rest all year round. Reviews

The territory of Lianozovsky Park is small but cozy - here everyone will find their own way to spend a pleasant time. Someone chooses fun and noisy companies, someone likes to ride interesting rides, and someone prefers to have fun at a disco. Fans of a relaxing holiday will also be able to have a good time on the shore of a pond or visit a program of classical music.

Vacationers say that, regardless of the time of year, the park is in full swing. Various folk festivals are organized here, concerts are organized in which local artists and professionals take part, guests of the recreation area actively participate in games and fun, visitors sing songs, dance and take part in competitions and championships.

The most massive holiday in the park takes place on New Year's Eve - more than three thousand people gather here. All vacationers, unashamedly, put on carnival masks and, drowning with their heads in serpentine, plunge into a whirlpool of fun.

A lot of fun in the recreation area and in the winter, on Maslenitsa. For this holiday, not only locals gather here, but also tourists to participate in the festivities. Here the songs flow, and rituals, contests are held, and the number of pancakes can not be counted! It is noteworthy that both young people and the elderly are taking part in the celebration .

Cleanliness and beauty!

The territory of the entire park is well-groomed and decorated with interesting compositions for planting flower beds. Here is laid a walking street called Lianozovsky Arbat. The ponds are cleared, arbors are set up on the banks, and white swans swim gracefully on the water "glass".

The pride of the park is its unusual fountains, unique and surprisingly beautiful compositions in the form of enchanting ornaments. Water jets of different colors, breaking out from under a water mirror, dance to the music.



Park branch

Having created an unusual corner for relaxing in Lianozovsky Park, local authorities decided to make a branch of this unusual place for entertainment. So Goncharovsky Park appeared. It is located in the Butyrsky district of Moscow.

Updated recreation area, reviews

Vacationers say that now in the recreation area there are paths for cyclists, there is no longer a fence - instead, the park is surrounded by granite benches. The whole territory is well lit at night, the park is landscaped - now there are different representatives of the flora that are pleasing to the eye. For the convenience of hiking, tiled paths are made.

All guests of the park note that four comfortable playgrounds with a special coating were created for young visitors, so that the child does not get hurt when he falls. After a walk, you can enjoy a cup of coffee in the cozy cafes located inside the park.

For athletes, the organizers made a platform with a variety of simulators. A very funny equipment is a circular bike, which involves the occupation of a whole team. For the convenience of vacationers, special loudspeakers are installed on the playgrounds so that you can easily shout to your child or your friend. And, of course, the park has a stage for events.

The pride of the park is squirrel with new aviaries. Vacationers assure that small animals always bring an excellent mood. You can work in the park - for this there is outdoor furniture and Wi-Fi, which is provided absolutely free.

Innovations

More recently, Goncharovsky Park has acquired a "Summer Reading Room", which is located at the entrance to the recreation area. This is a unique and modern innovation - the library in the fresh air. In the park you can relax in peace and quiet while reading a book. For convenience, soft padded stools are provided.

People who have already been here say that the book assortment is quite diverse - this is literature for children, classic works, bestsellers and newspapers. All three months of the summer, the reading room welcomes guests from twelve to twenty hours daily.

Every Saturday at eight in the evening on the site of the Goncharovsky Park for children and their parents will be shown new author's cartoons. For the youngest guests, the organizers made it possible not only to read books, but also to participate in interactive games.

It should be remembered!

While in the park, it is important to comply with the rules established by the administration. Visitors should be in open areas of the recreation area, you can not leave unattended young children, touch objects of suspicion. You should not approach the broken trees, it is not allowed to swim in places where it is forbidden, and to approach wild animals.

The creators of the unique recreation area also took care of retirees who can play chess in a cozy wooden gazebo. In addition, dances will be arranged for the elderly.

Not everyone knows where Goncharovsky Park is located. How to get there? Trolleybuses No. 3, 29, 29k go from the Dmitrovskaya meter to the park. You need to get to the stop "2nd Goncharovsky proezd" or walk - it takes about seven minutes. Goncharovsky Park address has this: st. Rustaveli, 7s1, Butyrsky district.
