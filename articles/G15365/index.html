The Long Beach Resort, Sri Lanka, Koggala: photos and reviews of tourists

Apart-hotel Long Beach Resort (Sri Lanka) is a calm beach hotel, located not very far from picturesque places and interesting sights. People come here to swim in the ocean, travel around the island and study its nature, history and culture. You will get acquainted with the impressions of tourists and their reviews about this hotel in our article.



Sri Lanka Hotels

There are not so many luxury hotels on the island, and they belong to several well-known world-famous chains. They are usually more expensive than in resort countries such as Vietnam or Thailand. Tourists recommend choosing at least the Quartet, because then you can hope for decent service and a green, well-groomed territory. In addition, you should pay particular attention to the location of the hotel relative to the coast, as well as whether the beach is good there. Meals in them are mainly breakfasts. But in some hotels there is an all-inclusive system, or half board. Five and four star hotels are located in the area we are describing near Long Beach. This is Fortress with a good spa center, as well as Koggala Beach. A lot of cheap, inexpensive guesthouses. One of the popular hotels is Long Beach Resort 3 * (Sri Lanka).



Koggala

This resort area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe island is located south of the country's capital, Colombo. It is known throughout the world for its beautiful beach five kilometers long. Different people strive here - both lovers of sunbathing seamlessly, and those who want to spend a honeymoon on the ocean, and those who prefer to rush along the waves on the boards. This area is named after a lake parallel to the ocean and the beach. It also stretches for five to six kilometers along the sea. It is known to tourists due to the many colorful exotic birds living here. Tourists are specially brought here on environmental excursions. From ancient times the lake was chosen by hermits, therefore there are many hermitages and Buddhist temples and shrines. Legends say that Buddhism in Sri Lanka began to spread from this place. It is here, in such a spiritual atmosphere and surrounded by wildlife, the hotel The Long Beach Resort 3 * (Sri Lanka) is located. The largest city in the vicinity is Galle. It is located twelve kilometers from the hotel, and you can go there either by tuk-tuk or by bus. There is an old fortress, a Christian church, which was built by the Danes, and various museums. The most popular of them is dedicated to the Sinhalese folk art.

The hotel area is small, but extremely neat and well maintained. An extraordinary view of the ocean opens from the central staircase. Near the reception there are ponds with carps and small turtles. Nearby is the five-star Fortress Hotel. Long Beach Resort guests go there to take pictures and swim in the pools. Accommodation - tourist tips

The hotel Long Beach Resort (Sri Lanka) has two hundred and eight rooms. They are of different categories - there are suites, improved rooms, standard ones. Most of the rooms are equipped with TV, small fridge, air conditioning. There is an electric kettle, which is very convenient to use in the evenings or in the morning, as well as a safe. But most vacationers are advised to book rooms with sea views. They usually have the best service and are well located. These buildings have a natural grass carpet on which there are wooden deck chairs and you can sunbathe. If the noise of ocean waves does not bother you at night, then you will be very pleased. And the buildings, from which the sea is not visible, overlook the road. Therefore, in these rooms the noise of passing vehicles is often heard. But such rooms are quite tolerable for living.





Children under 12 years old live with their parents for free. The room can put a special bed according to your desire. Cleaned in the room every day. At the same time, they put in the refrigerator half a liter of drinking water per person per day. The rooms are cool and you can relax after the beach and sunbathing.

Beach and sea

The Long Beach Resort 4 * hotel (Sri Lanka) has its own beach, which is a rarity for the island. But keep in mind that this is the ocean, and surfers and those who want to swim in big waves love the Koggaly coast. Therefore, if you come with children, you will have to go to neighboring beaches. Unawatuna is ten minutes away, Mirisa is forty. But there can be jellyfish on this beach. If you do not want to leave the hotel, then a little to the side is the natural lagoon, where you can safely splash around. On the shore near the hotel there are beach fights, which at your request will give you sun loungers and umbrellas. They are located under palm trees, on a green lawn, around which guards are on duty. In addition, the beach itself is very beautiful, spacious, as in the picture. The sun is evil, and we must take care, but in general the temperature is very comfortable. The hotel has two pools where you can swim not only during the day, but also at night. One of them is small, and the second is larger. Reviews about him are positive - swimming there is comfortable and pleasant. Very clean, tidy, and not very many people.



Prices and Shopping

The cost of a hotel room starts from four and a half thousand rubles a day. If you want to get to the neighboring beaches and in the store, use the "tuk-tuk". Drivers will not only take you wherever you need, but they will also wait while you go shopping or bask in the sun. Price - from one hundred rupees. However, excursions and products will be offered right on the beach. For example, large shrimp at five hundred rubles per kilogram, and mango at one hundred. A bottle of water in a convenience store is seventy-five rupees, and a beer in a wine shop is about two hundred and fifty. If you buy an excursion from a tour operator, then two days of travel will cost you $ 275 per person. The same trip bought at a bureau near The Long Beach Resort 3 * hotel (Sri Lanka) will cost one hundred sixty five dollars, and you will be shown waterfalls and beautiful views. When buying fruits, souvenirs, seafood you have to bargain. If you want to buy Ayurvedic cosmetics, then it is better to do this in a pharmacy - there such products are several times cheaper than in specialized stores.

Excursions

As we already wrote, tourists appreciate the Long Beach Resort 4 * hotel (Sri Lanka, Koggala) also because it is an excellent place for independent trips, especially along the southern coast of the island. Surf lovers are advised to head to Weligama and Mirissa. For shopping, Matara and Galle are good. In the last town you can explore the fort, wander around inside, buy souvenirs. And in Matara there is a bus station, from where there is intercity transport. There are three types of buses - red, state and two commercial. There are also express trains. You can get to them anywhere without any problems. In Dondra there is such an object of tourist interest as a fifty-meter-high lighthouse - the southernmost point of Lanka. In Matar - an interesting temple on the island with a bridge. The hotel also has a tour desk. It can be used for longer trips - to Kandy, Sigiriya, Yallu Nature Reserve. But you need to know that usually on such excursions they pick up at night - after all, it takes from three to five hours to go to these beauties. Therefore, guides try to catch it early so that you get there at that time, while it is not too hot. Traveling to Kandy and Sigiriya takes two days, with a stop at the hotel with panoramic views.
