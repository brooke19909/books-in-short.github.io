Resorts of Malaysia. Most popular places

Resorts in Malaysia have been an attractive place for a long-awaited vacation for many years. They beckon with impeccable beaches, beautiful coasts and unsurpassed nature. Of no less interest are the architectural monuments of the country. Let's find out which resorts in Malaysia are the most popular among tourists. We present to your attention the most interesting options.

The best resorts in Malaysia. Kota Kinabalu - a paradise

This city is the business and industrial center of Sabah, on the island of Borneo. It represents the eastern gate to the country. Strolling along the promenade of Kota Kinabalu, you can enjoy a breathtaking view of the endless sea with crystal clear water. It is not surprising that tourism plays one of the leading roles in shaping the state economy. Plus, this resort has a continental climate, which saves tourists from excessive heat and piercing cold. The weather is always good here. It is better to go to Kota Kinabalu from March to September. This time is the so-called dry season, when tourists do not risk spending their entire vacation in the room due to deafening tropical showers. Among the attractions of the resort are the Tunku Abdul Rahman Nature Reserve, Sabah Foundation Building and the State Museum. No less interesting will be a visit to the central market. And the beauty of the local Tanjung Aru beach is out of the question!



Resorts of Malaysia. Kuala Lumpur - the vibrant capital

Kuala Lumpur is not in vain called the city of gardens and lights, because it incredibly combines originality and developed business life. Walking around the capital, you can easily see the ultra-modern skyscraper and a Buddhist temple, which are shared by a couple of hundred meters. Fans of gastronomic tourism are advised to visit at least one of the many open restaurants serving national cuisine. Amazing with their space, modern shopping centers will be able to fully satisfy the needs of even experienced shopaholics. The relatively low cost of goods combined with their excellent quality will not leave anyone indifferent.



Resorts of Malaysia. Langkawi - a mysterious place

This island, part of the archipelago of the same name, represents the most developed and suitable for recreation area. It is famous for its clean air, first-class beaches and unforgettable sea views. The inhabitants of Langkawi will gladly tell you the many legends of this island and show the main places of pilgrimage for tourists.
