Red Energy tariff from MTS: connection features and cost of services

The MTS mobile operator creates many useful communication tariffs for its customers, among which there are packages without the need for payment. That is why a mobile communication client can adjust its costs for services. The MTS "Red Energy" tariff is designed for those who do not recognize the monthly fee. The price of local-oriented calls is the same. Let us consider in more detail all the advantages of using the new package.

Description

The Red Energy tariff introduced by MTS was released in 2018. The main advantage of the new items is a single price for all outgoing calls. The client does not have to torment himself with questions about the money spent on the calls made. For additional services compiled individual rates. SMS messages and the Internet are not in the package. A detailed description of the Red Energy tariff from MTS can be found on the official website of the company.
