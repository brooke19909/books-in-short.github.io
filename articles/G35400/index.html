Hotel Cedriana Djerba 3 * (Tunisia / Djerba): photos and reviews of tourists

Tunisia is currently a popular destination for beach vacations, as it attracts tourists with fairly reasonable prices and a beautiful sea coast. Therefore, in our article we want to talk about one of the economy class hotels on the island of Djerba - Cedriana Djerba 3 *.

A bit about the hotel

Cedriana Djerba 3 * consists of a number of low traditional buildings of white color. The complex is located in the northern part of the island 300 meters from its own beach. The hotel is quite convenient to get to, since the airport is located 4 kilometers in the city of Midoun.



Hotel Cedriana Djerba 3 * was built in 1992, and the last renovation was made in 2015. The complex differs from other institutions in a large selection of recreational activities. It is suitable not only for family vacations, but also for a trip in the company of friends.

Number of rooms

Cedriana Djerba 3 * has 175 rooms for up to five people. All apartments are equipped with TVs, air conditioning and a private bathroom. Each room has a balcony with great views. The apartments are decorated in a modern design creating a comfortable atmosphere.

The hotel offers rooms in the following categories:

  1. Double (77 rooms).
  2. Triple (78 rooms).
  3. Double (76 rooms).
  4. 8 Quad apartments.
  5. Family rooms (18).

Hotel infrastructure

Club Cedriana (Djerba) 3 * has a good infrastructure, due to which the stay at the hotel for tourists becomes incredibly comfortable. The complex has three swimming pools, one of which is for children, and the second is equipped with slides. All ponds are very clean and cleaned daily by employees. The recreation area near the pools is equipped with free sunbeds and umbrellas.

Reception is open 24 hours a day. The attendants will populate you at any time, no matter how late you arrive. Vacationers arriving earlier than the appointed time are also accommodated if available. Near the reception there is an ATM, money can be exchanged directly with employees.



The hotel has a hammam, fitness center, beauty salon, solarium, night club, tennis court and table tennis tables.

Beach complex

Caribbean World Cedriana 3 * (Tunisia, Djerba) has its own beach, 300 meters from the hotel. Many tour operators say that the complex is located on the first line, but in fact it is the second line. However, to go to the beach is not far away - about five minutes. The coast is equipped with sunbeds and umbrellas. The sandy beach is very clean, workers clean it daily. From time to time, algae appear in the water after sea disturbance, which are quickly removed. There is a bar on the beach serving refreshments to guests. Towels for trips to the sea are issued against a deposit returned at the end of the stay.

A bit about the resort

Hotel Cedriana Djerba 3 * (Djerba, Tunisia), as we have already mentioned, is located in the northern part of the island of Djerba, which is the southernmost sea resort of the country. It is always a little warmer here than on the continent. The island is famous for its fine sand beaches, a huge selection of modern hotels for every taste and wealth, as well as the best centers of thalassotherapy.

Sea vacations can be combined with sightseeing of local attractions, which are abundant in Djerba. The capital of the island is Houmt Souk, and the second largest city is Midoun. All other settlements on Djerba are small.



The island is good for a measured and relaxing holiday, the local climate and the coast are great for children. On Djerba you can see blooming lotuses, which are considered its symbol. In addition, the island has interesting places that are worth visiting for tourists.

Weather

Tunisia attracts vacationers primarily for its prices. And Djerba is interesting for tourists in terms of climatic conditions. The island is located in the Bay of Gabes and is close to the Sahara, where additional heat comes from. As a result, its climate is warm and mild, and the swimming season lasts almost the entire year.

The high season begins in April and ends in November. During this period, the air temperature rises to 22 degrees. But from December to March, the thermometer does not drop below 15-17 degrees in the daytime, but at night it can be quite cool (5-7 degrees).

Local Attractions

Relaxing on the island, you can visit the most interesting sights. There is public transport on Djerba, but it usually does not reach the right places, so if you plan to move around the island on your own, it is better to rent a car or take a taxi. Such trips will be much cheaper than tours of tour operators.

Tourists will be interested to see the Gazi Mustafa fortress, which is located in the capital of Houmt Souk. It was built back in the ninth century to protect against pirate raids. The fortress is made of local sandstone and is very beautiful in the rays of the setting or rising sun.



The Jerba Lagoon is a stunningly beautiful place inhabited by pink flamingos. Feathered birds have long chosen salty shallow water in the north-east of the island. In winter, the lagoon is a swaying pink carpet of birds.

The La Griba Synagogue is popular with tourists. It is located in a small village and on the outside is nothing interesting. But her interior is worth a look. In its walls are stored the oldest books and documents.
