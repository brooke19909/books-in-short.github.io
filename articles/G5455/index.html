How to weave rubber bands on a fork: a master class

Today, many children began to get involved in unusual hobbies, such as weaving jewelry, small souvenirs and other crafts using rubber bands. Materials for the manufacture of easy to purchase in any specialized store.

What devices help in work

Together with rubber bands, special machines and forks are sold, with which the production will go much faster. If it is not possible to acquire additional accessories, then you can use your own fingers or take an ordinary cutlery - a fork. And here the question arises: how to weave rubber bands on a fork? The answer is in this article.





What you need for weaving

Before you engage in a fascinating action, you need to make sure that you have at hand the following necessary materials:

  • gum in the required amount;
  • Crochet hook;
  • several special clamps;
  • table fork.

So, everything is ready. It remains only to understand how to weave bracelets on a fork?

Principle of operation





You can find a huge number of schemes for working both simple and complex with a variety of patterns. In the article you can get acquainted with several options for such a hobby.

How to weave rubber bands on a fork: fishtail option

  • First of all, you need to select materials of your favorite color. Put the first gum on the fork with an eight. She is pulled on extreme cloves, then turns over. The result is a pattern in the form of a cross, which is located in the center of the fork. The free edge is worn on the next pair of teeth.
  • With the second gum act as follows: it is pulled on the teeth of the fork from both edges, without affecting the central. Then comes the turn of the third gum.
  • The next step is performed using the hook. The bent end grabs the lowest gum from the edge and is thrown over 2 cloves. It turns out a sagging piece in the middle of the cutlery.
  • Then, similar actions are performed for the second edge. To work more conveniently, it is recommended to shift the loop down.
  • When the time comes to wear the next gum, then on the extreme cloves they will again be in the amount of three pieces. Then they again pick up the lower gum from the edge and throw it through 2 teeth and also leave it in the center. The second edge is also weaved. The action is repeated until the desired length of decoration is set.
  • As soon as the required value is typed, the gum stops adding. Leave a couple of the latter on the cutlery, and each edge of the bottom is displayed in the central part.
  • The last gum has two loops, of which one is made, which remains on the hook. This loop will be needed for the fastener.
  • From the opposite end of the decoration you need to find the loops of the gum that was the very first, and skip the second part of the fastener in them. The work is over - the decoration is ready.

Having figured out how to weave bracelets from rubber bands on a fork, you can move on to the next job.





What else can be done

You can please yourself and your friends with a very interesting decoration option. It will attract the eye not only with an abundance of various bright colors, but also with an unusual technique of execution and will certainly appeal to all those who want to learn how to weave bracelets from rubber bands on a fork.

Option "rainbow"

Here, preference should be given to bright rubber bands, but you must not forget to alternate them during operation.

  • The first gum folds in half to make a figure eight, and only after that it is pulled on a fork (on the middle pair of teeth).
  • The second gum should also be folded with a figure of eight and put on the extreme teeth on either side of the fork.
  • The third, folded like the first and second, is threaded in a similar way onto the second part of the fork, located at the edge.
  • On the teeth located in the middle, you need to wear a gum folded in half, but do not twist it with a figure eight. Using a hook, grab the loop of the lower gum and throw it through the central tooth. The same procedure is done on the second middle clove.

If it is not possible to use a hook, then you can take a toothpick. Thanks to the sharp ends when weaving bracelets from rubber bands on the fork, it is easy to throw the loops.

  • A gum folded in half is again worn on the central teeth, but also not folded by an eight.
  • A series that has already been made is being thrown over a new one.

To get bracelets from elastic bands on the fork, you need to alternate the length: one elastic band in the center and two elastic bands on the sides. To get a rainbow color, excellent colors are used for each row.





At the last stage of jewelry making, you need to transfer the loops from the teeth located at the edges to the central ones. Then throw the gum from the bottom up. Stretch the elastic band folded in half on a pair of central teeth, and string loops from the bottom row on top.

Put on the fastener through the resulting pair of loops. Fasten with the second end of the decoration. To give a neat appearance to the craft, you can straighten all the nodules. This is easy to do - you need to stretch the resulting material in width and length. As you can see from the article, making bracelets from rubber bands on a fork is quite simple.
