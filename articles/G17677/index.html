Matyr reservoir: description, fishing, recreation

During the construction of the metallurgical plant, there was a shortage of water from local rivers, due to the prohibitive capacity in production, the construction of a reservoir solved urgent problems. Currently, water from the reservoir is used by the Lipetsk TPP, as well as for irrigation of crops. Over time, the Matyr reservoir began to be called the Lipetsk Sea.

The population living near the reservoir was relocated due to flooding problems. We are talking about a part of the city of Gryazi, several villages, as well as two settlements that currently no longer exist: Borovskaya and Pakhar's Way. The Matyr reservoir fascinates with its beauty. There is a forest on its shore, next to it there are various complexes for healing and a comfortable rest.

Fishing

The fishermen are especially fond of the surroundings of such a city as Lipetsk. The Matyr reservoir is the leader in the number of fish in the region. Fishermen chose him back in 1970. A huge variety of prey can amaze any spinner. Catfish, silver carp, pike, pike perch, roach, common carp, asp are found in the reservoir.

Of particular interest to fishers are fishing competitions. The organization of such relay races takes place both in summer and in winter. Competitions are city and regional level. In winter, the Russian championship on fishing for mormyshka was previously held; 11 teams took part in it. The attention of tourists is always riveted to this event.

Strict rules must be followed during the championship. Fisheries, as well as law enforcement officers and inspectors monitor compliance with these instructions. There are certain rules, the implementation of which is considered mandatory. It is forbidden to fish for spawning, to get crayfish, to use nets, motor boats, etc. Compliance with these installations allows you to save the flora and fauna of the reservoir.
