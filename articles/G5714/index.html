Card replenishment

From a Sberbank card to Qiwi Wallet, money can be transferred using Internet banking. The user must be registered on the bank's website. For security, a password is entered there, which is sent in SMS.





When the login is successful, you need to find the "Payments and Transactions" section and select the transfer to "Qiwi". Then the wallet number is dialed, then you need to confirm the operation. On weekdays, enrollment is quick, and there may be a delay on holidays and weekends. From Sberbank card to Qiwi Wallet through Sberbank-online money is transferred without commission. This method is considered one of the most convenient and safe.

Using the Kiwi Website

You can send money from a Sberbank card to Qiwi using qiwi.com. You need to go into your account, select the "Top up wallet" section, and then find a way to use the "card". The system will provide information on fees and other conditions.

It is important to choose "replenishment with a Sberbank card", after which it is linked to the account. To do this, fill in all the details that allow you to fully complete the operation:

  • card number;
  • name, surname;
  • validity;
  • CVV2 or CVC2.




To confirm the operation, you need to click "Register". Then, verification and establishment of data is performed, which lasts about a minute. After the operation is completed, it will be clear how to transfer from a Sberbank card to Qiwi. This is done quickly and safely.

There are some rules for using the service. The card must be in the same country as the phone number. The one-time limit on cash is 15,000 rubles, and for foreign cards - 3,000. Daily restrictions for Russians are 50,000 rubles.

Through an ATM

From a Sberbank card to Qiwi, money can be transferred using an ATM. After installing the card in the device, it is important to enter the pin code and perform simple steps:

  • Click "Payments";
  • select "Payment for services";
  • find Electronic Money and QIWI;
  • write the number and amount;
  • check information and complete payment.

After the successful completion of the operation, funds are debited and credited to the necessary details. Money comes in quickly and without commission.
