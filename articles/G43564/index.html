Ultrasonic cavitation is a revolutionary method in aesthetic cosmetology. Exposure to fatty tissue with the help of ultrasound of various frequencies allows you to get rid of extra centimeters and kilograms without the use of radical methods such as liposuction. How does this happen? Ultrasonic waves act on fat cells, destroying cell membranes and promoting the formation of air bubbles. As a result, body fat becomes softer and is eliminated from the body by the lymphatic system. In addition, ultrasound stimulates the production of enzymes indispensable for skin elasticity - collagen and elastin. Thus, the cavitation procedure contributes to:

  • losing weight;
  • increase skin turgor;
  • cellulite treatment;
  • Correction of the most problematic areas of the silhouette.
