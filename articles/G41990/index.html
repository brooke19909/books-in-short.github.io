Hotels in Voronezh: photos and reviews

Travelers, young people, as well as older people who come for a few days to the city of Voronezh, hotels in the city are ready to accept at any time. The offer here is quite diverse: institutions are represented in a very different class.





Hotels in the city of Voronezh can be found for almost any wealth and taste. You can stay in a room for only 1000 rubles, as well as in a hotel room for 10 000. In order not to be mistaken with the choice, we will consider the most popular hotels in Voronezh.

Hotel "Italy"

Let's start the review with an economy hotel. Voronezh gives its guests the opportunity to stay at the Italy Hotel, which is located 5 minutes from the railway station. Near it is Petrovsky Square. Distance to the airport - 18 km.

A continental breakfast is served here in the morning , which can recharge you with positive energy for the whole day. In addition, the institution has a small cafe.

After a productive day in the city, guests can swim in the pool or relax with a game of billiards. A sauna and hammam are also available.

Free internet is available in all public areas of the establishment, so you will always be up to date with all the latest events.

Hotel Italy has 49 rooms that are traditionally decorated. They are equipped with refrigerators and plasma televisions. It features a private bathroom and comfortable beds.

Reading reviews about this hotel, one can understand that many of its guests liked the hospitable staff of the institution and quite comfortable rooms. Some were embarrassed by slightly overpriced prices, but they are justified by the availability of additional services.

Hotel Artik

Artik is located in the village of Otradnoye, near Voronezh. If desired, guests can order a nutritious and tasty breakfast, light snacks or dinner in their room.





For a great stay, guests have at their disposal an excellent Finnish sauna with a large swimming pool, a banquet room and a relaxation room. Outdoor enthusiasts can enjoy a game of billiards. For smokers, the hotel has special smoking areas.

You can always share your experiences with friends and family using the Wi-Fi provided throughout this Voronezh hotel. Free parking is available on site. The front desk is open around the clock.

Guests can book any of the nine comfortable and bright rooms of various categories with private bathrooms, in addition, amenities located on the floor.

Reading reviews of the Artik Hotel, you understand that many visitors to the city are attracted by its interesting design, the presence of a pool, billiards, as well as its excellent location. The disadvantages include only a small number of rooms.

Hotel Valeri Classic

Valeri Classic is a hotel located in the heart of the city, close to the Botanical Garden. The bus stop "Victory Boulevard" is located 400 meters from here, while the distance to the station is only 6 kilometers.





The restaurant serves classic Italian cuisine, while a cup of coffee or a refreshing cocktail is good in the very stylish bar.

The reception is open 24 hours a day, while the friendly staff will help you to resolve all emerging issues. If necessary, you can order a transfer or use the laundry service.

Free Wi-Fi is available at this Voronezh hotel. Free private parking is available for guests of the hotel.

The hotel has 21 air-conditioned rooms with a fridge and plasma TV.

Looking through the reviews about Valeri Classic, you can find out that this place is mainly preferred by business travelers. There are all the necessary conditions for their comfortable living.

Hotel "Natalie-Elite"

"Natalie-Elite" is a hotel located in the city center, near the main railway station. Near the establishment is the temple of the Prophet Samuel and Koltsovsky Boulevard.





The hotel has a restaurant that offers a wide selection of snacks, drinks and delicious hot dishes. If you wish, guests can order a nutritious and hearty breakfast in their room. Enjoy an invigorating cocktail, in addition, it will be good to order light snacks in a cozy, cute snack bar.

You can always share your impressions with friends and family using Wi-Fi. It is provided throughout the hotel in Voronezh. Free parking is available for guests in the surrounding area. The 24-hour mode of operation has a reception.

Guests can book any of the 10 comfortable and bright en suite rooms. All rooms are equipped with a TV, fridge and air conditioning.

Guests say about Natalie-Elite that this is an excellent institution that will suit both tourists interested in attractions and people who come to the city for work. The hotel has a very favorable location. And some guests are delighted with the cuisine of the local restaurant.

Hotel "House of Scientists"

"House of Scientists" is a hotel located 5 minutes from the central bus station of the city. The hotel is located near Moskovsky Prospekt, which allows you to easily reach the necessary point in the city.





Guests will find a fairly cozy cafe, which offers all kinds of dishes of traditional European cuisine. A continental breakfast is served here every morning. In this cafe, the interior is made in African and Alpine style. The hotel has a microwave on every floor.

The sensitive staff of the 24-hour reception desk will provide you with all the necessary information. A business center is available for business guests of this Voronezh hotel. Visitors are offered transfers to / from the airport. In addition, on-site parking is available.

Here you will find 7 modern and comfortable rooms of various categories. All are equipped with Wi-Fi, a telephone, a minibar and a TV. Each room has a private bathroom with toiletries and a hydromassage shower.

Hotel Tokyo

Hotel Tokyo is located in the ecologically clean part of the city in the northwest. The train station and airport are 10 kilometers away. Next to the establishment are the Arena shopping complex and Line hypermarket.





Tokyo Restaurant, which serves breakfast every morning, is open until 19.00. Guests can enjoy a cup of coffee or a refreshing cocktail in the lobby bar.

If you are familiar with regular physical activities, then at your service there is a pool and a gym. In the evening, relax in the sauna or play billiards.

Business services are provided around the clock. You can check your mail using the free Wi-Fi Internet at this Voronezh hotel. There is also a guarded spacious parking lot.

Looking through the reviews about this hotel, we find out that many guests like it for its location, the presence of a gym and pool, excellent cuisine in the restaurant. Among the negative opinions, one can often find dissatisfaction with the speed of the Internet.
