Hotel Lindos Imperial Resort SPA 5 * (Kiotari, Greece): photos and reviews of tourists

Kiotari Bay is usually visited by people who lack privacy. Lindos Imperial Resort SPA 5 * is located in a very quiet and peaceful place. Location

Lindos Imperial Resort SPA 5 * is located on the shore of Kiotari Bay. This is the east coast of Rhodes. It is located at a distance of 55 kilometers from the airport, which is both a plus and a minus.
