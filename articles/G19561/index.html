City of Cherkessk: Hotels, motels and hostels

Cherkessk is a small multinational city with a well-developed infrastructure in the field of sports and education, a booming economy. Today, about 120,000 people live in the capital of Karachay-Cherkessia, while the population is growing all the time.



The ethnic composition of Cherkessk is very diverse - more than 80 peoples live in peaceful neighborhoods, including Circassians, Russians, Nogais, Karachais, and Abazins. Books and newspapers are published in five languages, and local TV shows are also available.

The main driving forces of the economy are city-forming enterprises: refrigeration, rubber and cement plants, automobile production Derways. Specialists are trained at various universities, including the State Technological Academy. The cultural sphere is well developed - there are as many as four national drama theaters and a philharmonic society.

In this article we will find out what hotels Cherkessk offers its guests.

Hotel with the most romantic name

The Edelweiss Hotel (Cherkessk) will gladly offer services related to the temporary accommodation of guests. This establishment offers coziness, comfort, highly qualified staff and first-class service. The number of rooms is represented by 65 rooms of different categories. Some of them are made in a unique style, with a unique interior and in different colors. The rooms are equipped with everything necessary for living.



Not far from here is a restaurant for 300 people. Here you can find delicious dishes of European cuisine.

European style

Hotel "Europe" (Cherkessk) for the first time received guests in 2006, while it has already managed to secure positive reviews from guests and residents of the city. The hotel is very popular with tourists and people visiting the city for work.



The hotel fund includes 32 rooms of different categories. They are equipped with everything necessary for living: a large bed, a snow-white bed, the necessary equipment and additional furniture. Unobtrusive and friendly staff will help you in any matter. In addition, a homely atmosphere, peace and silence are created here.

Hotel "Star Complex"

G. Cherkessk offers hotels for every taste. The Star Complex is a top class hotel with low prices. The hotel has a pool with a jacuzzi, a sauna, a massage room with the necessary equipment, a gym, a cafe.

The facility is located near Victory Park, next to the Government House. The rooms have refrigerators, televisions, telephones, split systems.

Hotel "Grand Caucasus", Cherkessk

City hotels are distinguished by the comfort and hospitality of the staff. The Grand Caucasus complex offers to experience the fusion of Caucasian hospitality and European service. Located in the heart of the cultural and business part of the city, it has a unique style and design.

The rooms are equipped with telephones, equipped with comfortable furniture, plasma TVs, hair dryers, minibars, individual air conditioning. All guests are provided with a free set of hygiene products, a set of towels made of soft white cotton, slippers and bathrobes, which are not all hotels in the city of Cherkessk.

High-level hotels, such as the Grand Caucasus, offer guests: a meeting room; office equipment: copy machine, fax, scanner, personal computers; a restaurant; The Internet.

The hotel also has the Tale of the East restaurant, which offers high-class service and excellent cuisine. European, Caucasian and Oriental dishes are prepared here, music is played, an exciting and fragrant hookah smokes, and a belly dance is performed.

In the center

Hotels in Cherkessk welcome guests throughout the year. This place is no exception. It is located in the very center of the city. A pizzeria restaurant is open here, and room service is also available. It offers guests: laundry, beauty salon, 24-hour security, parking.



85 rooms are available. Each of them has a coffee table, bed, TV, wardrobe, bathroom, refrigerator, hygiene items.

Hotel "Chemist"

Hotel "Chemist" is located in the city center in a common complex with the recreation center "Chemist". It is worth noting that the guests are offered comfortable rooms of several categories.
