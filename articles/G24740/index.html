Camera

Engineers have built two cameras in the Apple iPad mini tablet that deserve to be carefully considered: a front camera with 1.2 MP photo quality and a main 5 MP iSight camera. Prices and modifications

The Apple iPad mini tablet has modifications with three different amounts of memory: 16, 32 and 64 gb. Such gadgets cost significantly less than the usual iPad for us. The Apple iPad mini 16gb tablet has a starting price of $ 329. Such a miracle of technology can be a great birthday present for a child. The Apple iPad mini 32gb tablet will cost $ 429, and the gadget with the most memory will cost you $ 529. If you want to always have the Internet at hand, then you need an iPad mini with an LTE module, for which you will have to pay plus $ 100 to the tablet with the amount of memory you want to purchase.

Functionality

If you are tired of sitting near a power outlet with a charger, then the iPad mini is the best suited for you. And these are not empty sounds, because the tablet can work up to 10 hours when watching videos and actively using the Internet via Wi-Fi, as well as up to 9 hours when surfing the World Wide Web using 3G or 4G.

To summarize

The Apple iPad mini is a great gadget to get you started with Apple. It has everything: a powerful processor, excellent cameras and an amount of RAM, which is quite suitable for a novice user, so you can rest assured that the tablet will correspond exactly to the price you paid for it.
