Ticket price

Jet Airways sells its tickets for flights to all destinations at 3 types of prices.

  • Base cost ("Normal"). Tickets can be booked without prepayment, redeemed and returned with minimal loss, there are discounts for children.
  • The special price is 60-70% of the base, without discounts for children, with half the price lost upon return and without the possibility of booking.
  • Low-cost is a cost of up to 50% of the base. There is no possibility of booking and returning tickets.

The most attractive low cost tickets are cheap flights. However, there is a slight negative. Such a flight does not include luggage, only hand luggage of certain sizes.
