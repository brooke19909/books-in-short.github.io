How to use crafts from cork to decorate the interior?

In today's world, the question of garbage is quite tough, which prevents residents of large cities. Waste processing is not carried out in such volumes as it would be necessary, therefore landfills cover more and more territories.



But if you look around with the master's eye, it turns out that we can use some waste. For example, corks from wine and plastic bottles. The fantasies of people are multifaceted, so there are always interesting solutions. Cork crafts are especially interesting for giving. The only problem may be a slow set of material. Everyone who uses the bottles should be connected here. Particular attention should be paid to the color of the material. The more color shades you collect, the more interesting the crafts from the cork will be .



An interesting solution to the use of these products was tracks. You can post them in several ways. Bright, catchy, they immediately distinguish you from the total mass of summer residents. To create such beauties, a foundation should be prepared. The edges of the track should be laid out empty bottles to get the finished version. Then pour a layer of gravel between them. On top, additionally use sand. The pillow created in this way must be tamped. Now you can upload a picture of the track. The second installation option involves the use of a bonding solution, it is more durable, but has its own difficulties. It is necessary to act quickly until the mixture has hardened.



An even more interesting solution is the craft of bottle caps made in the form of a panel. The created picture can be made out as a separate picture by making a frame for it (it is also possible from plastic bottles). But on any wall they will look original. Corks can be fixed with nails or glue. Which side to use, your intuition will tell you. You can stack from any edge. The shape of the picture may depend on the basis on which you collect it. If this is the wall of the house, it means there is a place to turn around, so there is nothing holding your imagination here, if only there are enough blanks.



Interesting crafts from the cork are obtained in the form of flowers or figures. You can make little men, butterflies, a ladybug or a turtle. Having created an obstacle course for climbing from them, you can be sure that the kids will like it. From traffic jams original handbags, tables, hanging curtains are obtained. You can execute them both from metal blanks and from plastic. Only the connection technology will differ. Plastic can be drilled in the right places, and metal plugs are best twisted.

No less interesting crafts from wine corks. They are used to create an original summer cottage chair or pouf. It turns out very beautifully. Corks are also suitable for making toys. They can decorate umbrellas or rugs.

Cork crafts fit in well with the kids gym complex. Especially if the kids are actively involved. Craftsmen perform whole dresses using this unusual, but so easy to use material. Corks can be used to decorate the kitchen or bathroom. It turns out a safe and original element of decor.
