We plan a vacation on our own

It's no secret that travel agencies also want to eat. They earn their money on extra charges for tours. The logical conclusion: if you want to seriously save, plan your vacation yourself. If you competently approach the matter, you can relax cheaply, and most importantly, qualitatively, and all you need to do is to follow simple rules:

  1. First of all, give a brief overview of the country where you are going to go. If you have friends or acquaintances who have been there, talk to them, take an interest in the specifics of the destination country.
  2. It is mandatory to find out what can be transported to the country. It sounds strange, but sometimes import bans look simply unreasonable, for example, in Singapore it is strictly forbidden to import ordinary chewing gum.
  3. Outside a group without knowledge of the language it will be difficult for you. It is wise to take a trip only if you are fluent in at least English.   6. Remember that early booking will significantly save your money. Find out in advance how and on what you can get to the hotel or hotel where you are going to stay for the night.
