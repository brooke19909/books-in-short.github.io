How to call America is not difficult

Many Russians in distant America have close relatives or acquaintances with whom they periodically have to communicate by phone, since this type of communication is by far the most mobile. Despite the great remoteness of America from Russia, at any time, by calling, we can hear the voice of the person we need from a distant country, because no distance is a barrier to a phone call.

The only thing necessary in this case is that you need to know exactly how to call America from Russia correctly so as not to be mistaken. In general, this is not so difficult to do as it might seem at first glance. If you already know the number of the subscriber located far beyond the ocean, then you also need to find out another country code. This can be done using the directory with information about international telephone codes or find out via the Internet. There are many sites on the pages of which you will always find not only codes of all countries of the world, but also detailed information on how to call America from anywhere in the world.



How is this still done

Here are some tips for those who decide to speak with someone on a different continent. Before calling to America, make sure that your telephone set is in a corrected condition, and that the codes and number of the required subscriber in the USA are at your fingertips.

We start by dialing the number 8 and wait for the beep. After the end of the first signal, you can safely enter the number 10 - this is the international code of the United States of America. Then you need to dial 1, it allows you to determine your further actions, namely, a set of the territorial code of the city.



And here you have to be vigilant, because you can make a mistake. The fact is that in this country the same settlement may have not one, but several territorial codes. For example, New York phone numbers might be 212, 646, and 718.

In conclusion, you only need to dial the subscriber number, usually consisting of seven digits. Thus, to call America, you need the following chain: 8 - (beep) - 10 - 1 - three-digit city code - seven-digit subscriber number.

In order to call a mobile phone in the USA from a landline from Russia, you need this circuit: 8 - dial tone - 10 - 1 - caller number.

Many are also interested in how to call a landline phone from America from Russia to America. In this case, the set should be like this: +1 - city code - subscriber number.

It is much easier to make a call from a mobile phone from Russia to a similar one in the USA. You just need to dial: +1 - the subscriber's number.



The cheapest way to communicate is via Skype, as network technology in the United States is very widespread. Almost 80% of the country's inhabitants have round-the-clock Internet access, both from home and from the office. So you can reach the American at any time of the day if you have a computer next to him.

Now that you know how to call to America, it will not be difficult to call any subscriber . Just remember the above combinations.
