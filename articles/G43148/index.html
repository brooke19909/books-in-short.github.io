How to get from Kazan to Adler and make a route in the opposite direction?

How to get from Kazan to Adler and make a route in the opposite direction? This question excites many people. Each city has its own characteristics. There are several ways to overcome this distance. It is worth talking in more detail about each of them.





Several reasons to visit Kazan

Kazan is the legendary capital of Tatarstan. Her view captivates every tourist. In total, there are five main reasons to come to this place:

  • History buffs must visit all the museums of this wonderful city. Famous exhibits will tell about the most outstanding personalities of the country's events. More tourists will love the Gortalovs mansion, the Tolstoy Museum and the Tukay Museum.
  • Kazan is considered a creative city in which the most gifted people live. On its territory there are several adult and children's theaters of various directions. It is worth buying a ticket to the show.
  • This city has an old pedestrian Bauman street. You can safely walk along it, enjoying the view of beautiful architecture. Vehicles will not bother passersby.
  • Excellent temples were erected in Kazan several centuries ago, and a true connoisseur of art will appreciate these attractions.
  • You should definitely take a picture with interesting sculptures, which are numerous in the city.

In addition, in the big city there is extensive leisure: shopping centers, water parks, cinemas and much more - all this will make your vacation unforgettable!





A few reasons to visit Adler

Adler is a sunny, bright and colorful city by the Black Sea.

Every year, thousands of tourists come to this place from all over the world. There are several reasons to visit this magnificent city:

  • There is always high air temperature in summer: you can swim in warm water, get a beautiful tan and bask in the sun.
  • It is worth taking a look at the clear sea, the view of which is very inspiring.
  • At least from a distance you need to look at the famous mountains, because such a landscape you will not see anywhere else.
  • It will be interesting for an adult and a child to look into the monkey nursery in order to play with these cute creatures.
  • This city has the largest number of fountains.

Travel by bus

There is no direct bus from Kazan to Adler. This is due to the large distance between the named points. There are several options to get to the appointed place this way, but you will have to make at least three transfers. The total travel time will total more than three days. Train is better

The most popular way to overcome the route from Kazan to Adler is by train. A day later, the train sent south from Kazan. The total journey will take approximately 47 hours, five minutes. Of course, this is a lot. But the passenger, choosing this option, can safely sleep, eat, take a walk on the cars, wash and go about their business while driving.

Keep in mind that in summer there is a huge influx of tourists. This suggests that tickets should be purchased in advance. The approximate fare for one person will be from 3,000 to 5,500 rubles.
