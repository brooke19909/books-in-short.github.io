Which smartphone is better to buy: a brief overview

Technological progress is steadily moving forward. And it happens every day. Not so long ago, smartphones entered the mobile device market, which turned out to be much more convenient and enjoyable to use than regular phones. Now most people around the world have smartphones. However, before someone who is just about to purchase this kind of apparatus, the question arises. Which smartphone is better to buy? After all, there are so many, and all are different.

In general, there can be many options.



Let's talk about the main competing operating systems. The first one is iOS. The production of smartphones running on iOS is engaged in Apple. They are called iPhone. One cannot say about these devices that these are just touch phones. Today, the iPhone is the best product in its market, according to many, as it has incredible data protection, versatility and a very smart virtual assistant. Another important factor here is optimization. With medium iron, the iPhone works without any brakes. But there are also disadvantages - limited possibilities of individualization.

Talking about which smartphone is better to buy, you can not ignore the Android operating system. Android smartphones are the main competitors of iOS devices. Most of the smartphones on the market work specifically for Android. Here, personalization options are limited only by the user's imagination. But since the system is open - the probability of infection of the operating system with viruses is much greater.



If the future owner of a smartphone came up with a question about which smartphone is better to buy, and at the same time he needs stability, quality, performance and style from the device, then the iPhone is the right choice. You can offer the Galaxy S3 (S4) or HTC One. However, they usually cost from 25,000 rubles. If the user does not need to play the most advanced toys, and smooth operation does not play an important role, you can choose a device that costs around 8000 - 15000 rubles. An example here is touchscreen mobile phones such as the Galaxy Ace, Huawei U8800, HTC Desire and many others. But there are situations when a person wants a little from a smartphone - make calls, sometimes go online to check mail or an account on a social network. In this case, you can safely take a smartphone, the cost of which varies around 4500 - 7000 rubles. Samsung GT-S6810, Philips W632, LG E400 and the like are suitable here.

To summarize, it is impossible to say with accuracy which smartphone is better to buy. It all depends on the desire of the consumer and financial capabilities. But the overall picture in the article was considered.
