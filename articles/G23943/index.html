Caves Beach Resort 5 * (Hurghada, Egypt): description, photo, reviews of tourists

Housing stock

Caves Beach Resort 5 * (Egypt, Hurghada) has at its disposal 360 rooms, decorated in an original style. They are located in the main building of the hotel. This hotel complex offers apartments in the following categories:

  • standard "pool caves" (40 sq. meters, maximum occupancy - up to four people);
  • "deluxe" with sea view (50 sq. meters, from two to four guests);
  • Sea view (40 square meters, 2-4 people, king size bed);
  • "Royal Pool & Sea Caves" (60 sq. meters, two bedrooms with king size beds, four guests can accommodate as much as possible).

Regardless of the type, all rooms have a TV (satellite and cable channels), a bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer, air conditioning, a minibar, a safe, a kettle and tea and coffee making facilities, a furnished balcony or terrace. In addition, the apartments (as well as throughout the hotel) have wireless internet access. Room service is available 24 hours a day. As for cleaning, it is carried out daily. Towels and bed linen are updated several times a week.

Entertainment

On the territory of Caves Beach Resort 5 * there is a large pool equipped with hydromassage. Next to it there is a sun terrace, a bar with a large selection of drinks for every taste. During the day, animators work at the pool, offering everyone who wants to do water aerobics, gymnastics, yoga, play boccia, water polo, volleyball, etc. In addition, you can play billiards, tennis, diving and wind or kitesurfing.

In the evenings, entertainment shows are held with the participation of both animators and guest artists. However, since the hotel is new, the amphitheater on its territory has not yet been completed. Therefore, the shows are held on an improvised stage. However, it is certain that in a few months the amphitheater will be ready, and guests will be able to enjoy the views, having conveniently located in it.



Reviews of tourists from Russia

Studying the comments of our compatriots, we can note the fact that many chose this hotel largely due to its original design. According to most tourists, they were not disappointed in their decision. So, in their opinion, despite the fact that the Caves Beach Resort 5 * was built recently, and there are still enough imperfections in it, travelers can safely count on a comfortable and memorable vacation.

Many guests note interestingly decorated rooms. By the way, the apartments offered to the guests seemed very spacious and clean. The only minus some of our compatriots considered the fact that the rooms are a bit dark. Of course, this was done to maintain the overall cave style, however, some guests would be glad if a few more light sources were added to the apartments.

Almost all tourists were satisfied with the work of the restaurant in the hotel. According to them, there is a very large selection of well-prepared dishes. At the same time, one could always taste meat, fish, poultry, and, of course, a variety of snacks, salads, fruits, pastries, and sweets.

As for the beach, in general, our compatriots liked it. However, guests express their hope that the construction of the pier will be completed soon, since the sea has yet to go from the shore, and a couple hundred meters must be walked to the depth, which is not very convenient to do along the rocky bottom. But by the way, if you wish, you can use the pier of a neighboring hotel.

As for the staff, there were no serious complaints from the hotel guests in this matter. So, according to tourists, the cleaning was carried out efficiently and regularly, the cooks worked perfectly, the waiters were quick, and the administrators were always polite and attentive.
