Grodno Hotels: Omega, Belarus and Slavia

On the Neman River , one of the famous and ancient cities of Belarus , Grodno, is located. It is considered the cultural capital of the country, it has a unique history and many ancient attractions interesting for travelers.

Interesting places in Grodno

Tourists prefer to visit Grodno in the spring and summer, but even at other times of the year many interesting places await them here. This is a monument city in which ancient architectural structures have been preserved, their historical past is closely connected with the history of Belarus. Often they begin to recognize the city by walking along the pedestrian Sovetskaya Street, which has an old building. Here are souvenir shops and modern cafes and shops.



The old castle, the Church of St. Francis Xavier, the Church of Saints Boris and Gleb, the fire tower, the church, the water towers and the old pharmacy are the most popular places in Grodno. Tourists willingly admire beautiful designer landscapes and sculptures in the Zheliiber Park, study the rich expositions and exhibits of the local Museum of History and Religion.

Where to stay in Grodno?

For accommodation, tourists can choose several options for settlement - rented apartments, hotels or hostels. Grodno hotels offer guests comfortable rooms of different classes and categories, a set of standard or exclusive services. Today, a trip to this city is becoming a popular tourist destination. Therefore, the hotels of Grodno became involved in the competition for guests, the quality of service in them is growing.

For people whose budget is limited, hostels of the city are in demand. Often you can find announcements about renting for the night for tourists or business trip apartments of citizens. Such settlement is popular, but renters of such housing can not be completely sure that the owners of the apartments comply with sanitary standards and basic safety, therefore, most often city guests choose hotels in Grodno. There are several popular hotels in the city that have proven their worth.



Hotel Omega, Grodno

Hotel Omega has earned the category of two stars. It has a convenient location in a quiet and cozy place of the historic center of Grodno. Many attractions are located close to the hotel, which allows its guests to make independent walks around the city and get acquainted with its unique history.

The number of rooms in the Omega Hotel is small - this is 30 quiet and comfortable rooms. There are deluxe rooms and standard single or double apartments. The comfort of the guests is provided by the necessary new furniture and beautiful textiles. The rooms have a bathroom, there is also home appliances - a refrigerator and a modern TV. For the convenience of guests, each room has a telephone.

Check in is done around the clock, the checkout time for payment for accommodation starts at 12:00.

An undeniable advantage for car travelers is the availability of free parking near the hotel. Grodno will reduce travel time, see more interesting and learn more about historical places, if you move around the city by car.



Hotel Belarus

Within walking distance from the city station and its center is the hotel "Belarus". In Grodno it is one of the oldest hotels, the year of its construction - 1968. Guests are offered 188 rooms - first and highest categories, junior suites and suites. They made redecoration, there is modern furniture. In two-room rooms, guests can be accommodated with an extra bed. All of them have their own bathroom with toilet, sink, shower or bath. Household appliances - TV and fridge, radio, telephone - can increase the comfort of the rooms. Wi-Fi is available at an additional cost.



The hotel administration provides paid services:

  • organization of excursions and trips;
  • hairdressing salon;
  • a safe for storing valuables;
  • taxi call and car rental;
  • laundry work.

In the hotel "Belarus" for catering guests have a restaurant, cafe, a bar.

Hotel Slavia

Cosiness and comfort are offered to its guests by the Slavia Hotel (Grodno). It is located in the city center, not far from Sovetskaya Square, and has 24 rooms: for newlyweds, suites and junior suites, standard and superior rooms. There is the possibility of living with people with disabilities.

Pleasant and modern design in the rooms, beautiful and comfortable furniture and plumbing, necessary appliances, air conditioning - all this allows you to have a nice rest on the trip. A workplace with Internet access in the room enables business people to solve many business issues.

The hotel has a restaurant, it offers its visitors dishes of European cuisine, although it is possible to order any dish to the taste of the guest. The halls of the restaurant are decorated with paintings by artists.

The administration of the Slavia Hotel provides a wide range of services - from shoe and clothing cleaning to the organization of cultural events. It often hosts negotiations, business meetings and conferences.



Hotel rates

A very beautiful city is Grodno. Hotels, prices at which are very affordable, hospitably welcome guests. Reviews of tourists and guests indicate the optimal ratio of "price-quality". Depending on the chosen hotel, the cost of living per day can vary from 1000 to 7000 rubles.
