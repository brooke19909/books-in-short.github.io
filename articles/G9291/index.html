The most beautiful places in Ukraine: description, attractions and interesting facts

Traveling is always interesting and informative. In any corner of the world you can find many interesting places that you want to visit. Ukraine is no exception in this regard. From the article you will learn about the most popular routes in this country for tourism lovers.

To do this, we present to your attention the top "10 most beautiful places in Ukraine."





National Dendrological Park

Arboretum Sofiyivka in the city of Uman is the largest in Europe. Here you can admire the huge variety of plants that are constantly carefully looked after, maintaining the beauty of their appearance. Therefore, floral families look according to the canons of the art of landscape design. Beautiful fountains, waterfalls, underground rivers - all this is originally combined with each other and fits perfectly into the surrounding landscape.

The author of the project and the construction manager was the Polish military engineer Ludwig Metel. The construction of the park territory was carried out by the forces of serfs.

The area for the construction of the park is divided by the Kamenka river, there is practically no forest zone on it. And the author of the project successfully used this relief. The construction, by the way, was carried out without developing a preliminary plan.

At the end of work, trees and shrubs were planted in all parts of the park territory. And also erected architectural objects, structures and sculptures decorating it and made in an antique manner.

The park itself and its components are the most beautiful places in Ukraine that are worth visiting, going to this country.

The main compositional dominant here is a number of pools and ponds. The upper area is 8 hectares, and the lower one is about 1.5 hectares. In addition to these reservoirs, there are waterfalls on the territory (one of which has a height of 14 m), cascades, transition locks. An interesting element is the underground river, which is called Acheron. Its total length is 224 m.

Synevyr Lake

This pond is a symbol of the Carpathian mountains. This is the most picturesque and unusual place. It is impossible not to mention it, naming the most beautiful places in western Ukraine. The lake is located at an altitude of 1000 m above sea level, and its depth is 22 meters. In the very center of the surface of the water there is a small island called the "Seabed". The lake is surrounded by the Synevyr National Park, which you can visit to enjoy the picturesque scenery.

The existence of the named reservoir is associated with one legend. She talks about the daughter of the count - Sina. The girl's eyes were bluer than the sky. She had a beloved shepherd Vire. The young man played the flute not far from the place where the beauty was picking flowers. Once young people met, fell in love with each other. Upon learning of this, the rich count ordered the death of Vir. And his people threw the guy on stones from a high rock. When Sina heard about the death of her beloved, she ran to this place and cried. From her tears a lake was formed, in the central part of which the tip of that stone sticks out. And the water in the lake is as blue and clear as the eyes of a beautiful girl.

You can visit Lake Synevyr using various roads. So, from the south to the lake there is a route from Mizhhirya, towards the village of Sloboda. Once a day, a bus runs here from Mezhyhirya.



When compiling a list of the most beautiful places in Ukraine, one cannot but recall this beautiful place.

Khortitsa National Reserve

This attraction of Ukraine is located in the city of Zaporozhye. The reserve belongs to the recreational and educational place of rest. On its territory there are about 63 archaeological sites, as well as unique historical monuments.

The island of Khortytsya is one of the largest on the Dnieper. It combines various natural complexes. Oak forests, meadows, steppes, rocky areas adjoin here. All this contributes to the formation of a unique ecosystem and beautiful landscapes pleasing to the eye.

Offering the most beautiful places to stay in Ukraine for those who are interested in ecotourism and recreational activities, one cannot but recall this island. Hiking, horse riding, cycling, water and underwater activities, as well as rock climbing are held here.

Aktovsky Canyon





A natural complex of unprecedented beauty is located not far from the village of Aktovo, in the Nikolaev region.

It is the only canyon in Europe and frames the valley of the Dead Dead River. The total length of the natural object is 5 km, presenting in miniature an exact copy of the unique canyons of North America.

Odessa catacombs

You can get acquainted with the amazing underground world by visiting this interesting object. The total length of the catacombs is more than 2,500 m. In its length, it was ahead of even the Paris and Roman ones. These man-made caves have witnessed various historical events.

At different times, smugglers, robbers, partisans and revolutionaries used catacombs. In some places, by the way, they are intertwined with natural caves.





Today, catacombs attract a huge number of extreme tourists. It is best to investigate them together with an experienced guide or take a regularly conducted excursion for this.

Shipot Waterfall

If a traveler wants to visit the most beautiful places in Ukraine, it is necessary to see this natural attraction. An interesting waterfall is located on the edge of the village of Pylypets and got its nickname due to a loud noise, like a whisper.

The height of the waterfall is 14 m. The most beautiful cascades, combined with the picturesque surroundings, create a unique sight.

And since the beginning of 1993, the annual Shipot festival has been held near the waterfall, visiting which tourists can spend time in the lap of nature, surrounded by beautiful forests and mountains.

Tunnel of love

This attraction is located between the settlements of Klevan and Orzhev in the north-west of Ukraine. The tunnel is located directly on the railway connecting the two named settlements, one of which has a woodworking plant.





Visiting all the most beautiful places in Ukraine, you can not stop by here. This place attracts newlyweds and romantic couples. There is a belief that if people in love make wishes when they are here, then they will certainly come true, but provided that the love between them is mutual and sincere. The total length of the described attraction is 3 km.

Askania-Nova - Biosphere Reserve

In the Kherson region , 75 kilometers from New Kakhovka, there is a beautiful nature reserve park. Its territory stretches over more than 11,000 ha. Here are collected about 400 varieties of plants. In the center of the reserve is a pond.

Askania-Nova is among the 100 most beautiful places in Ukraine. On its territory, visitors are waiting for beautiful landscapes, photo safaris and a small zoo with unique animals.

Castle in Radomyshl

The object is a historical and cultural attraction of the country. It is located 100 km from Kiev in the city of Radomyshl. In the bowels of the castle you can get acquainted with the authentic interiors of the 17-18 centuries, visit the paper mill, and also see authentic things related to this time.





Denishi

This place is well known to all rock climbing enthusiasts. Denishi is located close to Zhytomyr, near the village of Starokonstantinova. Rocky surfaces lie on the banks of the Teterev River. Picturesque landscapes, clean air, attract many tourists. It is here that it is good to relax with health benefits.

The most beautiful places in Ukraine where you can not only enjoy nature, but also actively relax, include Denishi. The height of the cliffs waiting for those who can conquer them is more than 25 m, so experienced and beginner climbers come here for training or vacation. The tracks are equipped for climbers of any skill level.
