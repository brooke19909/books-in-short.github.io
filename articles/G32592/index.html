How to register an iPhone in iTunes

In any case, it makes sense to register your iPhone in order to be able to recover data from the device in case of theft or damage. Registration does not affect AppleCare plans or the guarantees that accompany your mobile purchase. However, registration speeds up the process of providing support for a smartphone. If iTunes does not recognize your iPhone, you can perform several troubleshooting steps to resolve the issue. After registering with iTunes, you can sync important business documents, applications, and other content from your iPhone.





So how to register an iPhone online

Step 1

Go to Apple's product registration page.

Step 2

Select "Choose your location" in the drop-down menu and find your country.

Step 3

Find the appropriate language from the “Select your preferred language” tab in the drop-down menu.

Step 4

Enter your Apple ID and password in the required fields. Select the Continue button.

Step 5

Press the "Single Product" radio button. Select Continue.

Step 6

Double-click on the iPhone menu. Then select the version of iPhone you are registering. This article provides an example of how to register iPhone 4s, but this process is the same for all modifications. Then - Continue.

Step 7

Enter the serial number of your iPhone. Click Continue, and then click Finish to complete the registration.





Troubleshoot iTunes Recognition Issues

Step 1

Open iTunes, then click the Apple menu and select Check for Updates on Mac. On Windows, select the Help menu and find here "Check for Updates."

Step 2

Check that you are not stuck with foreign objects or dirt inside the iPhone-4g connector (the characteristics of all models and port structures are identical). Make sure that the pins on the computer USB port are not dirty or damaged.

Step 3

Open "Start", in it - "Control Panel" and open "Add or Remove Programs" on a Windows computer. Then check if Apple Mobile Device Support is installed. If it is missing, uninstall iTunes, and then download this service again and install a new copy.

Step 4

Reboot your iPhone and computer. Then connect your iPhone to your computer and try to sync again after starting iTunes again.

Ending

Concluding the instructions on how to register an iPhone, find the serial number by clicking on the Settings menu and then General. Scroll to the appropriate option to view your serial number.





Additional registration for downloading free programs

One of the things that really surprises users is that it is impossible to download applications for free without registering credit card information in the App Store. However, this can be circumvented. Below is an instruction on how to register an iPhone in iTunes without providing payment card information.

Step 1

Open iTunes, in it click on any of the free applications in the App Store.

Step 2

By clicking on the application, you will see a page with a description opens. After that, click on the “Get” button located next to it. This will open a new window. Select Create New Account.

Step 3

You will see a welcome message from iTunes. Click Continue. After that, the terms and conditions of purchases will be displayed. You must check the box “I agree” and proceed.

Step 4

Now enter the required information - a valid email address. After you enter the data, click Continue.

Step 5

You can see that there is a choice of “No” in the names “Payment Method”. Previously, this option was not available. Set the switch to None and enter your personal information and Continue.

Step 6

You will receive a message stating that the confirmation has been sent to the email address provided by you.

Step 7

Check your email, you should receive a confirmation sent to the iTunes Store. In the letter, you will be asked to click on the link to activate the account you just created in the iTunes Store.

When you click on the link, the system will prompt you to enter your account information in the window intended for entering the system. Enter your e-mail and password.

At this point, instructions on how to register an iPhone can be considered complete. You can now log in and start downloading all the applications that are available for free without entering any credit card details.
