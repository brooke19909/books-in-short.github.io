Comfortable hotels in Rome inexpensively

Rome is one of the most visited cities in the world. Many tourists have been there or would like to visit. It is famous for historical sites, architecture, museums.

Any traveler who decides to visit this metropolis asks two questions. The first is how to get there. The second question is where to stay so that it is comfortable, with good conditions and whether it is possible to rent a hotel in Rome inexpensively.

The capital of Italy has two airports where tourists arrive: Fiumicino and Ciampino. The first is more popular than the second.

Fiumicino Airport

Rome Fiumicino International Airport, named after the small town in which it is located, has a second name in honor of the great Italian scientist and artist Leonardo Da Vinci. It is located 35 kilometers from the capital, was founded in 1961. Arriving at it for the first time, you can get confused, since it has 4 terminals and 6 landing zones:

  1. Terminal 1. Used by 14 airlines. Serves domestic flights, medium-range flights, flights in the Schengen area.
  2. Terminal 2. Its 8 airlines use. Of all four, the second is the smallest. Serves flights of low cost airlines: domestic, within the Schengen area and outside it.
  3. Terminal 3. Used by more than 80 airlines. The largest terminal. From it depart, as well as from the second: domestic flights, flights within the Schengen zone and outside it.
  4. Terminal 4. It is used only for flights to the United States and Israel, since these flights require maximum care for the organization of security and a thorough inspection of baggage and passengers. The terminal is relatively small, serving only 950,000 per year. Its purpose was to become separate from the rest, with special systems for registration, baggage handling, passport control.

The transport network of the airport is well organized; getting from it to the city center will not be difficult. However, it should be borne in mind that trains and buses do not run at night.

The three most common ways that tourists use to get to the city:

  1. Railway.
  2. Metro.
  3. Bus.
  4. Taxi.

Airport Hotels

There are several hotels at Rome Airport. This is QC Termeroma Spa and Resort, equipped with a pool and air-conditioned rooms, affordable. B&B Hotel Roma Fiumicino, where you can stay with a pet. Good reviews from tourists deserved the hotel Best Western Hotel Rome Airport. It is located 10 minutes walk from the airport.

Best Western Hotel Rome Airport

The hotel with 50 rooms is a great place for transit tourists. It is equipped with a small pool, free Wi-fi and parking, a fitness center.

There are rooms for non-smokers, for people with disabilities, family rooms. The daily rental price is about $ 75. There are many restaurants nearby, such as Il Ristorantino, Host Restaurant, Contro Corrente, etc.

Tourists can visit several interesting places nearby: the ancient ruins of Porto di Traiano, which can be reached on foot in just 13 minutes, Il Parco di Villa Guglielmi park.

According to vacationers reviews at the Best Western Hotel Rome Airport, the hotel has clean, comfortable rooms. A good restaurant awaits tourists, pleasant communication with staff, good service. Guests note a high level of sound insulation, but the hum of the aircraft, even if it does not interfere with sleep, will still be heard. However, the hotel itself is small, as are the rooms in it. Therefore, it is better to book them in advance.

Cheap hotels in the city center

There are many inexpensive hotels in the center of Rome. Sole Roma in the Parion area is equipped with an internal garden, there is Hotel Regno, where some rooms have terraces.

One of them is located in the vicinity of the Vatican. This 3-star Rome Dei Papi Hotel de Charme is located next to St. Peter's Square and the Vatican Museums (less than 10 minutes walk) in the popular Prati district.

Rome Dei Papi Hotel de Charme
