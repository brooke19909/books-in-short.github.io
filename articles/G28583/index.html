How to choose an air conditioner in the apartment correctly

Now almost any consumer can afford such an acquisition, depending on the needs. Let's try to figure out how to choose an air conditioner in the apartment and not be disappointed.

Modern devices are equipped not only with the function of cooling the air, but also with its heating, ventilation, humidification, etc. Before buying an air conditioner in an apartment, think about what it will be used for, where it should be located, and, of course, how much you plan to spend.



When choosing this equipment, of course, it is worth paying due attention to the opinion of specialists in this profile. They will give practical advice on how to choose an air conditioner in an apartment, what power, equipment, with what additional functions, etc. So, let's begin.

To begin with, let's try to figure out for yourself what air conditioners are. Since you plan to install it in an apartment, consider a class of household. They are divided into monoblock, split systems, multisplit systems.

Monoblock - window and mobile, i.e. floor, consist of one block. The advantages of this type of air conditioner are its low price, ease of installation, and long-term operation. The disadvantages are that, for example, the window system can close part of the window, which will reduce the light transmission in the apartment. They also make a lot of noise, and the power of such structures is usually small.



Split systems are the most popular air conditioners for residential premises. The design of this type consists of two blocks. External is located on the street side, thereby reducing noise in the apartment. The distance between the blocks can reach 20 m. Installing a system of this type is simple, and such designs also have a number of additional functions.

Multisplit systems are more suitable for installation in country houses. Unlike the previous ones, several indoor units can be installed, and they are placed in different rooms. The disadvantage of such a system is the high cost.

How to choose an air conditioner in an apartment at the price and manufacturer? According to these criteria, they can be divided into premium, economy and middle class.

The first is quite high in price and quality. Such an air conditioner will last you a very long time. Manufacturers of brands such as Panasonic, Daikin, Mitsubishi, give a long warranty on service.

Middle class. The set of functions in this category is small, but the device can satisfy any consumer with its functional abilities. Such models are produced by Kentatsu, Fujitsu, McQuay.

Economy class - the cheapest and least functional air conditioners. The main producer is Ballu.
