Hotel Grand Seas Resort Hostmark 4 *, Egypt, Hurghada: overview, description, photos and reviews

The Grand Seas Resort Hostmark 4 * hotel is just an ideal place for those who love peace and quiet, and also appreciate comfort and quality of service.





Hotel Description

The Grand Seas Resort Hostmark 4 * hotel is an example of elegance and comfort. Here, the beauty of the surrounding nature is succinctly combined with the green landscapes of the park, which is located on site. The history of the hotel began in 2002, since then it has supported its high status and good reputation in every way.

The guests of the resort complex are provided with all the necessary amenities to make them feel as comfortable as at home. The rooms have modern technology, they are pleasing to the eye with a cozy and overloaded interior.

As a guest of the Grand Seas Resort Hostmark 4 *, you will not get bored, because you will find a lot of different entertainments. Check out the masterpieces from local chefs at the hotel's restaurants, visit the state-of-the-art fitness center, or relax on an animated show program.





Location

The Grand Seas Resort Hostmark 4 * hotel has a rather advantageous location. The road from Hurghada Airport does not seem tedious to you, because the distance to the hotel will be only 7 km. The city center will have to drive 15 km. You have a unique opportunity to visit the beautiful island of Giftun (16 km), as well as the famous marina (12 km). Nevertheless, the main feature of the location of this hotel is its direct proximity to the sea coast.

Guest rooms

Hurghada attracts many tourists from all over the world . Hurghada Grand Seas Resort Hostmark 4 * can be classified as medium-sized hotels, because in its number of rooms there are 226 rooms, divided into several categories.

The standard category of apartments has 116 rooms. The rooms are designed to accommodate two guests in a large bed or single. Also available is the placement of another guest in an extra bed.

Family rooms are designed for four or more people. They consist of two bedrooms, as well as two separate bathrooms. There is also a set of soft folding furniture, which can serve to accommodate two additional guests. There are 36 such apartments in the hotel.

Junior Suite - these are the best rooms in this hotel. They are quite spacious and can accommodate up to four people (2 on the bed and 2 on the folding sofa). It consists of a bedroom, a living area, and a spacious bathroom.

An important innovation in the hotel is that 11 rooms from the general fund were allocated specifically for non-smoking guests. In each room, regardless of category, the following amenities are available to tourists:

  • a bathroom with a toilet, as well as a bath or shower;
  • hair dryer;
  • split system;
  • TV with cable channels;
  • international and domestic telephone;
  • reliable safe for documents, money and valuables;
  • balcony with wicker furniture (if the room is located on the ground floor, there is access to the terrace).




Hotel infrastructure

The hotel is located at some distance from the resort village of Hurghada. Hurghada Grand Seas Resort Hostmark 4 *, to compensate for this inconvenience, offers its guests a wide range of infrastructure facilities:

  • at any time, reception staff are ready to register new guests or answer questions of interest to vacationers;
  • a conference room for 150 people can be used for its intended purpose, or converted for holding holidays and celebrations;
  • the hotel has a cozy Internet cafe, as well as free Wi-Fi in the reception area;
  • You can tidy yourself up before a romantic dinner at the hairdresser in the hotel;
  • professional dry cleaning and laundry staff will take care of your things;
  • pool with a special section for children;
  • two playgrounds are open for young tourists;
  • a special exchange office operates as well as an ATM and terminals for non-cash payments;
  • You can buy souvenirs in memory of your vacation, as well as buy gifts for friends and relatives in small shops and cozy shops;
  • children can attend a special club where an entertainment program from an animation team is always ready for them;
  • Guests enjoy free parking.




Hotel Restaurants and Bars

Restaurants and bars are the real pride of the Grand Seas Resort Hostmark 4 * hotel (Hurghada). Here guests can visit the following establishments:

  • Marhaba is a huge institution, which can accommodate up to 440 people and acts as the main restaurant of the hotel. It serves both local oriental dishes and those that correspond to the culinary traditions of Italy, America, France and the Mediterranean countries. Guests are offered a sumptuous buffet three times a day. You can also consider thematic dinners dedicated to cuisines from around the world as a pleasant moment.
  • The lobby bar is a calm and comfortable establishment located in the lobby on the ground floor. It serves coffee, tea, desserts, snacks, and a range of spirits.
  • The disco bar invites its guests to pass the night to incendiary music, as well as try many original cocktails.
  • The pool bar provides your comfortable stay. Without leaving the water, you can properly refresh yourself or refresh yourself with an exotic drink.
  • The beach bar offers a huge assortment of drinks and snacks.




Entertainment

Hotel Grand Seas Resort Hostmark 4 * (Hurghada) made sure that its guests did not have to get bored. For this purpose, the following entertainment program was prepared:

  • a large sports center, where all the most modern simulators are assembled, as well as professional trainers;
  • a cozy wellness center where you can take a steam bath in the sauna, relax in the hammam, take a bath with a jacuzzi and get a massage;
  • fresh chlorinated water pool;
  • tennis courts, which are illuminated at night by floodlights;
  • All kinds of marine activities and sports are available on the beach.




Compliments and bonuses

The Grand Seas Resort Hostmark 4 * hotel, as a sign of respect and gratitude to its guests, has developed a loyalty program for them, which includes a number of pleasant compliments.

If you intend to spend a honeymoon at the resort, be sure to provide this information when booking the tour, and also present a marriage certificate at the reception. As a bonus, you will receive a themed room decoration, as well as a fruit basket or a cake of your choice.

Birthdays will not be left without a surprise. Everyone who celebrates their birthday at the hotel will receive a cake. The desire to participate in the bonus program must be reported at the time of booking, as well as during check-in.

Couples who choose to celebrate their wedding anniversary with a vacation in a hotel must observe the same conditions as newlyweds to receive a compliment. The surprise will be a fruit basket or cake.

Arriving at the hotel a second time or more, you find yourself in the base of regular guests. This gives you the right to receive water and fruit upon check-in. But that is not all. Every three days you will be delivered to your room another basket of fruits, as well as a basket of oriental sweets.

Positive reviews

The hotel complex Grand Seas Resort Hostmark 4 * is very popular among tourists. Reviews about it contain a lot of positive points:

  • good animation that just leaves no time for boredom;
  • Spacious, clean rooms with daily housekeeping
  • well-groomed clean area;
  • Most hotel staff speak Russian at a fairly good level;
  • in the dining room there is a huge amount of delicious pastries;
  • many sunbeds on the beach (enough for everyone);
  • despite the fact that check-in is officially carried out at 14:00, in fact you can enter the room much earlier;
  • clean, well-maintained beach with corals and lots of bright exotic fish;
  • the hotel is far from the bustle of the city and noisy infrastructure facilities.




Negative reviews

Many tourists choose Egypt to relax. Grand Seas Resort Hostmark 4 *, despite its popularity, has some gaps in its work:

  • inattentive and arrogant staff;
  • shabby old furniture;
  • not enough sun loungers by the pool;
  • old, worn beach towels;
  • the same type of food;
  • expensive and low-quality Internet (terrible speed for $ 30 per day);
  • noisy air conditioning that keeps you awake at night;
  • the smell of chlorine is too strong in the pool;
  • after delivery of the room keys at 12:00, you no longer have the right to receive hotel services (even just drink water from the cooler);
  • low quality drinks;
  • too shallow sea, in which only children are comfortable to swim;
  • mud in the pool.
