Knitting booties for beginners with knitting needles: features of the process

If you have a baby, you like to needlework, then it will not be difficult for you to make the child look the most beautiful. Independently connected booties for the baby will cost much cheaper than the purchased ones, and the process itself will give you unspeakable pleasure. The fact is that you can realize your imagination. In this article, we will talk about how knitting booties is made for beginners with knitting needles.



It should immediately be noted that there is nothing complicated here. The process is very easy, so even an inexperienced master will cope with it. First you need to choose the thread of the appropriate type and color, as well as prepare the tools for the job. Knitting booties for beginners with knitting needles will be an interesting activity. You need to do this only from natural materials that will not cause discomfort to the baby. The number of threads depends on the size of future shoes, for example, for a three-month-old baby, the length of the product will be about 9 cm.

Pay attention to the knitting needles. Their number should correspond to the type of thread. In principle, the type of knitting needles and their size are indicated on each skein, so no problems should arise. Most often, tools No. 2, 3 and 4 are used. Knitting booties for beginners with knitting needles will be simple. First of all, try to build the cuff of the product. To do this, you will need 4 knitting needles, so try to collect as many loops so that they can be divided equally into each tool.



Knitting booties for beginners with knitting needles is done by the front surface. Subsequently, you can use other schemes. The cuff is knitted with a 1 * 1 elastic band. You will have to do about 12 rows, after which you will need to divide the loops in this way: 11 on the first and third, and 7 on the second and fourth knitting needles. On top of the cuff, you will have to make several crochets that will serve as holes for the string.

Now we turn to knitting the toe. To do this, work with 11 loops on the third spoke. In this case, garter stitching (alternation of the front and back rows) is used. The remaining needles at this stage do not need to be touched. In this way, 18-20 rows should be built.



Knitting booties with knitting needles for beginners is not a difficult task. After the toe should proceed to the creation of the rim of the product. Here at this stage, the remaining pending loops appear in the work. They should be knitted with a garter stitch. As for the number of rows, it all depends on the length of the foot of your baby, so this size is individual.

Lastly, the sole of the product is knitted. It must be connected with the sides during knitting. For work, you should use the front surface. Please note that to connect the elements, you should knit the last loops together. After the product is ready, you can begin to finish it. For example, you can tie a lace that will fit into the holes on the cuff and will serve as a tie. You can also make several flowers with which to decorate the baby‚Äôs first shoes.

After you learn how to knit booties with knitting needles, the knitting pattern can become complicated. For example, you can add various openwork patterns or other thread colors. Good luck
