Tariff MTS "3D Zero": reviews

Those who do not want to pay for communication services during communication can connect themselves to the MTS 3D Zero tariff plan. This offer is available to users of Ukraine. When using it, the subscription fee will be charged only on the days of calls. A similar condition is when using the Internet.

Description

Super MTS "3D Zero" was developed by a mobile operator for Ukraine. With it, customers may not pay for calls to other MTS mobile numbers. In addition, the cost of calls to numbers of other mobile operators is quite acceptable, it is one and amounts to 1.5 UAH. A text message will be paid at 1 hryvnia per pc.





If the subscriber wants to use the Internet, then UAH 5 will be deducted from the balance. every day, and for that price 200 MB of traffic per day is provided. The fee is charged only during the use of the Network. Standard services are automatically connected to the tariff.

Cost

The Super MTS 3D Zero tariff has extremely simple communication rates. If the client does not use the services, then there will be no subscription fee at all. It is not charged even if you make calls to MTS numbers. If calls are made to other mobile numbers, then the monthly fee In the case of additional use of the Internet, the daily mandatory payment will be increased to 5 UAH.





Clients will not pay anything to switch to the offer, but there is a condition. In order to be able to use unlimited, each subscriber must replenish the balance once a month for more than 40 UAH. If there is no replenishment, then it will be possible to call MTS numbers not at 0 kopecks, but at 1 UAH / min.

Call rates to other countries will look as follows:

  1. SMS - 3 UAH.
  2. MMS - 10 UAH.
  3. MMS to Russia - 1 UAH.
  4. Calls to other countries of the world - 1 UAH / min.

the Internet

As mentioned above, the tariff of MTS "3D Zero" does not include Internet traffic, but if a client wants to use the Network, 5 hryvnias for 200 Mb for surfing will be deducted from the balance. The next day 15 UAH will be paid, but 500 Mb of traffic will be credited to the client, and to activate such a package, you will need to enter the service code * 101 * 500 #. Such a package of MTS "3D Zero" is limited.





Features of the offer

The Super MTS 3D Zero tariff is provided according to the following rules:

  1. If not a single call is made from the mobile number, the monthly fee is not charged.
  2. If the client does not go online, the subscription fee for traffic is not charged.
  3. To check the balance of package services, the combination * 101 * 4 # is used.
  4. Unlimited on MTS will work if the user replenishes the balance each month for an amount exceeding 40 hryvnias.
  5. Billing is per minute.

Pros and cons

The advantages of MTS "3D Zero" include full unlimited MTS numbers, as well as the provision of traffic at a low cost. In addition, for many users it is convenient not to pay for communication and the Internet if you do not use it, because not all people need to be constantly online or call. An undoubted advantage is the very low cost of communication with other countries.





The disadvantages of the tariff of MTS "3D Zero" include the need to constantly replenish your balance in order to receive an unlimited package. The high cost of text and multimedia messages to other countries also does not apply to the attractive sides of the tariff plan of the MTS mobile provider.

"3D zero 35"

This proposal was developed for certain regions of the country, but the proposal of MTS "3D Zero 35" has slightly different conditions than in the one described above. It does not provide a connection fee, and all calls are charged the same. Thus, a minute of communication with subscribers of city and mobile operators will cost customers 35 kopecks.





Such an offer does not include a monthly fee, but what is most attractive is that there is no need to make mandatory payments. Perhaps, the advantages of the MTS 3D Zero offer end there.

Many people are wary of the fact that there is no monthly fee in the tariffs, because this means that the offer may have pitfalls, so before connecting, it is recommended to familiarize yourself with the prices of communication services:

  1. Communication with other MTS customers will be paid at 75 kopecks per minute.
  2. Communication with customers of other mobile operators in the country, as well as calls by city phones will cost 1.2 UAH / min.
  3. A text message sent within the network will be 66 kopecks, and outside of it, the cost will also not change.
  4. Internet connection is very high and amounts to 10 UAH / 1 Mb. In this case, it will be more profitable to use additional options.

Why is there such a discrepancy in prices in fact and in the information that the operator provides? The fact is that when replenishing the balance for an amount of 40 hryvnias, the prices change and amount to:

  1. A package with 30 minutes per day is provided inside the network. If the limit is exceeded, the payment will be 35 kopecks.
  2. Communication with subscribers of other mobile operators, as well as calls to landline numbers will be 35 kopecks / min.
  3. You can send 30 free SMS per day, and if the limit is exceeded, then for each sent text message you will have to pay 35 kopecks.
  4. For 1 hryvnia, users will receive 30 Mb of traffic per day.

For the transition you will need to pay 9 hryvnias at the first connection, and the transition itself is carried out by telephone 7722. Before connecting, it is better to check whether the region is among the serviced ones.
