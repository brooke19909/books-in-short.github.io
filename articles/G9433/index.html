What?

The tax rate on the transport tax is undoubtedly important. But what kind of payment are we talking about? As already mentioned, not all citizens of the Russian Federation are associated with such a tax. But only those who own this or that transport.

The thing is that such penalties are annual payments for registering a vehicle with the traffic police. It is important to remember: the transport tax applies only to everything that the engine has. Privileges

We know the transport tax rate. Tax benefits in this regard also take place. And they are distributed individually in each region. After all, the transport tax is considered regional.

Who is considered to be beneficiaries? Of course, the elderly. Elderly people have every right to a kind of discount from the state. Or for a full exemption from payment of transport fees. According to modern laws, pensioners who own a particular vehicle with a motor engine pay only 10% of the total payment. But their benefits must be announced in advance. To do this, it is customary to contact the tax service of your region with a PTS, documents of ownership, a standard form application, a passport, as well as certificates confirming benefits (pension certificate, for example).



Full freedom

Who is not afraid of the transport tax rate? Of course, those who are completely exempt from this payment. Oddly enough, there are such categories of people in the Russian Federation. There are not too many, but they are available.

Who are you referring to? Veterans, Heroes of the USSR, retired military personnel who participated in the hostilities (fought, simply put), disabled people of the 1st and 2nd groups. Also, organizations that transport people with disabilities and repair and create roads of various importance are free from paying transport tax. Families with many children, as a rule, have all the rights to refuse to pay the contribution in question. In such families should be from 3 minor children.
