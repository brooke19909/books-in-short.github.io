Studio headphones: features and best models

Choosing a good headphone is always a difficult task. It is even more difficult to choose studio headphones that are designed to work with sound at a professional level. The market is represented by a mass of models, but their price is clearly not affordable for everyone. Yes, you can save and buy a model from the budget segment, but you should not demand high quality from it. Today we will tell you about the features of studio headphones and look at several popular models.

Features

Studio or monitor headphones are complex and expensive models. A large headband is used for fastening, and large ear pads (burdocks) cover the ears. It uses large-sized membranes that evenly distribute sound and reproduce low and high frequencies. As a rule, they are used at recording studios and radio stations.

Studio headphones can be divided into three types: closed, open, half-closed. The difference between them is the level of sound insulation. If you want to completely get rid of noise, then choose something from a completely closed type. However, in this case, a certain vacuum is created, which from the worst side affects the sound quality. In terms of sound, the open type looks better, but in this case you will hear others, and they will be your musical preferences. A half-closed type becomes a compromise.

Audio-Technica ATH-A550Z





Studio headphones, made in a completely closed type. Provide comfortable listening to your favorite music thanks to a convenient headband and low weight design. Installed two powerful speakers that give a good, deep bass. Studio headphones reproduce sound in the frequency range 5 - 35000 Hz, which allows you to listen to any song. The design received a soft headband, adjustable. Ear pads completely cover the ears, made using foam rubber. Some options are sold with faux leather elements.

To connect to the user's devices, a standard 3-meter cable with Jack 1.8 "at the end is used. In addition to excellent characteristics, the professional studio Audio-Technica ATH-A550Z headphones received a modern style that attracts the attention of buyers. The manufacturer was able to combine good quality and affordable in this model cost.

Beyerdynamic DT 770 PRO





Popular studio headphones, made in a closed type. The manufacturer did its best and produced an almost perfect model at an attractive price. The headphones are distinguished by excellent sound and a reliable design. Reproduces the sound of the widest frequencies. At any volume level shows a clear and deep sound.

Excellent noise reduction allows you to forget about extraneous noise. "Cups" are very tight to the ears, not allowing the design to fly off the head. The design is based on emitters capable of delivering sound in the frequency range 5-35000 Hz. In addition to attractive features, the headphones got a stylish look. The ear pads are removable, they are convenient to clean and change.

A spiral cable is used to connect, the mini-jack is gold-plated. The wire is quite durable and does not fray during operation. Headphones can be folded for carrying.

AKG K271 MKII





The best studio headphones that give great features at an affordable price. The manufacturer made the main emphasis on sound insulation. Moreover, the sound is not heard in both directions - you do not hear others, and they are music from the headphones. A feature that a professional user will like is mute when the device is removed from the head. The model has received special recognition from radio hosts, sound engineers and television workers.

The design is based on electrodynamic emitters with a capacity of 200 mW. The model reproduces sound in the frequency range 16-28000 Hz. It copes well with bass, giving them deep and clean. For connection, a 3-meter cable with gold-plated contacts is used. For convenience, it is made detachable. The weight of the headphones is less than 250 grams. Unfortunately, the design does not add up.

AUDIO-TECHNICA ATH-M20X





Professional headphones that belong to the budget class. We got a convenient design, low weight and good sound insulation. It uses two large speakers that reproduce high-quality, deep sound. It works with a frequency range of 15 - 20,000 Hz. Headphones are fastened with the help of a headband, which is covered with foam. The ear pads are quite large and completely cover the ears, trimmed with artificial leather. They fit snugly to the head, do not fly off.

A standard 3.5 mm jack is used for connection. The wire is long enough, but, unfortunately, does not disconnect. It is covered with material that does not break or wipe. In general, AUDIO-TECHNICA ATH-M20X are a good solution for lovers of high-quality, rich sound.

PIONEER HRM-6





Interesting indoor studio headphones from a well-known manufacturer. Convenient design and powerful speakers allow you to enjoy your favorite music. The range of reproducible frequencies is 5 Hz - 40 kHz. Made of solid plastic, well assembled. Fastened by means of a headband covered with soft foam. The ear pads are large, have an elongated shape. Covered with high quality faux leather. The ears are completely covered and fit snugly to them.

A special cable is used for the connection. In the normal form, its length is 1.2 meters, in the extended one - 3 meters. The wire is disconnected, which adds comfort during transportation. At the ends there are gold-plated jack-type connectors 3.5 mm. In general, the model came out successful, and the cost is at an affordable level.
