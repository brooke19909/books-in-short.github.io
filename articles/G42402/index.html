Simple option

If you are not confident in your abilities, then use the instructions on how to make a box out of paper, taking the finished box as a basis.

To begin with, its surface needs to be cleaned of film and labels. Then they put the lid on the box and draw a line along the board with a pencil. The rest is simple:

  • cut off excess cardboard along this line;
  • put on a cap;
  • at its junction with the trimmed side of the box, stick tape or another adhesive tape;
  • do the same with the inside;
  • when the base of the product is ready, proceed to decorate it.

How to make a box in the form of a chest from paper: what you need

What word comes to mind when they talk about treasure? That's right, "chest"! Therefore, many are interested in the question of how to make a box of paper in the form of an ancient object, where the pirates put the looted treasures.

This will require:

  • thick paper;
  • unnecessary strong box or packing cardboard;
  • long ruler;
  • 3 sheets of thick paper;
  • 1 simple pencil;
  • sharp breadboard knife ;
  • 2 sheets of colored paper;
  • PVA glue;
  • 2 satin ribbons 15 cm long each.




Manufacturing procedure

The paper chest is glued and decorated in the following order:

  • the drawing above is printed;
  • transferred to a piece of fairly dense cardboard;
  • cut out;
  • a detail for the cover is drawn in the form of a semicircle with a radius of 12.4 cm;
  • a line is drawn at a distance of 2 cm from the center;
  • two such parts are cut out;
  • the box is glued according to the first drawing;
  • allowances along the edge of the lid into shallow fringes are cut with scissors and semicircular details are glued;
  • all the walls from the inside are duplicated with cardboard to strengthen the chest;
  • strips are glued, starting from the place of attachment of the box cover to the wall;
  • ornaments are cut out, for example, fasteners, keyhole , etc.

If you like to fold paper figures, then try to make an original souvenir using the origami technique. The box, the scheme of which is presented below, looks unusual and will delight the person to whom you present it.

To make it you need:

  • print 2 sheets of A4 printer paper with patterns, as in the photo, in two different colors, for example, white and purple;
  • cut out;
  • bend the rounded ends of all parts;
  • attach details, alternating colors so that their center coincides.




If everything is done correctly, then you should get a figure resembling a spiral sign of eternity with outgoing rays.

Then from the paper you need to cut a circle of small diameter and stick on the wrong side.

Turn over and start collecting the rounded ends into a new spiral, just like the first time.

When all the parts are woven, sticking a circle is not required, since if the paper is thick enough, the box will not open by itself.
