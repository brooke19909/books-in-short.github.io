Khimki, Tolstoy park: rest for the whole family

In 2017, the Moscow Region Park of Culture and Rest. Leo Tolstoy turns 70 years old. Today it is a huge green area near the canal to them. Moscow and a stunning leisure center.

Cozy silence and outdoor activities

The recreation area is located a stone's throw from Khimki station. Tolstoy Park has endless alleys, thematic lawns, alpine hills, bright flower beds and unusual flower beds. The guests are greeted by magnificent wrought-iron gates, from which the central alley leads to the monument to Leo Tolstoy. A rock garden is nearby. On each of them you can read a quote from the famous works of the classic, whose name is named after the park.



On the coast of the canal there is an equipped beach. On its territory there are free showers, a medical center, a changing area and toilets. Sun loungers and bathing accessories are provided for a fee. The beach has free volleyball and table tennis courts. Visitors can enjoy water activities and a fitness area. Nearby there is a summer cafe and a phytobar.

For those who wish to swim in clean water, there is a closed paid area with two spacious pools surrounded by sun loungers and a bar with a large selection of drinks and snacks.

For private events, family gatherings, banquets, weddings and corporate parties, indoor gazebos are organized on the beach that can accommodate up to 30 guests. By prior arrangement, the gazebos are equipped with musical equipment, plasma panels, refrigeration units and coffee machines. For a fee, you can make an individual order for a catering company, invite a host and develop a scenario for the event. By the way, excellent organizers can be found in the city of Khimki.



Tolstoy Park has several paid areas for barbecue and barbecue with cozy houses.
