How to create a picture of the fabric with your own hands?

How are paintings painted? The most traditional way - they are painted in oil on canvas. But there are other options that can be done by artists who have never held a brush in their hands. For example, magnificent pictures from fabric turn out, the photo shows how they can be stylish, bright, various. Occupation is not only fascinating. The result will turn into an independent element of decor, pour into the design of the apartment and become your subject of pride.





Of the benefits of needlework - the minimum amount of cost for the manufacture of the original work. You will need fabric first. It can be scraps, leftovers. The material that upholstered the furniture or from which the pillows are sewn is suitable. The color scheme is selected individually. It can be in harmony with the design of the room or be in contrasting shades. In order to make pictures of the fabric with your own hands, you will need a stretcher. It can either be purchased ready-made in the store, or made independently. Scissors, a stapler or a hammer with small nails are also required.





The fabric must be delineated and cut to the size of the subframe. In this case, the matter must be pulled as tightly and evenly as possible. Cut the canvas should be subject to allowance, which is equal to the width of the frame. This is necessary in order to hide the edges of the fabric behind it. Then you need to attach to the stretcher with the help of a stapler each side of the matter, while making sure that there are no distortions.

The picture itself is created from pieces of fabric, they can be selected in such a way as to obtain a previously conceived composition. Its elements should initially be marked on the canvas. Then you need to cut out the details of the application and connect them on the prepared canvas with an iron. As a result, you can decorate the kitchen with a still life of your own making or hang a picture with funny dolls in a children's room.





You can make bulk paintings from the fabric with your own hands, this is a more complex and sophisticated option. Such canvases require more thorough preparation and artistic skills. Synthetic fabric for the background (capron, raincoat fabric, silk) will be required. You can realize any idea, it all depends on your imagination. In order to glue the details of the composition, you will need an iron, scissors for cutting elements and a burning machine. To decorate pictures of the fabric with your own hands, stock up with paints and brushes, and beads, foil, and beads can also be useful. In the process of creating the work can not do without needles, threads and glue. Getting started completely coincides with the option described above. That is, the fabric is stretched over the frame, the outline of the future pattern is outlined on it. Then, elements for a future composition are cut out from pieces of colored material and attached to the canvas with an iron. A wire frame and synthetic winterizer will add volume to the picture.
