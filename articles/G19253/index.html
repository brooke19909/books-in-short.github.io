Iberostar Playa de Muro Hotel (Mallorca, Playa de Muro, Spain): Tourist reviews

There are tourists who, during their travels, stay only in chain hotels. High standard of service, proven comfort - and no unpleasant surprises. In addition, avid fans of chain hotels, as a rule, have bonuses for regular customers in the form of discounts or one free night. Lineups such as Hilton, Melia and Hayat have their own hotels in all the key resorts in the world. Sometimes their representation is not limited to one hotel. In some resorts there may be two Hiltons or Sofitels at once. After all, hoteliers are trying to please both budget and picky customers.

This picture is also observed on the famous beach of De Muro, which is located near the city of Alcudia, on the island of Mallorca. There is a four-star hotel Iberostar Playa de Muro and a luxury hotel with almost the same name. Only one word is added to her name - "Village". In this article we will look at both hotels. It makes no sense to compare them. One has four stars, the other five. Accordingly, the prices are more attractive in the first hotel: from ninety-two US dollars per person per night - against one hundred twenty-three in the second. But in both cases tourists are very satisfied, and the reviews about both hotels are extremely positive. After all, this is sunny Mallorca, and legends are made up about rest here. Therefore, the level of service here does not correspond to low prices.

The guest rooms in the four-star hotel are decorated in a modern, laconic European style. Hotel Iberostar Playa de Muro meets all the requirements of a four-star hotel in this chain.
