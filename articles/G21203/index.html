"Golden Bay", Anapa (hotel). Holiday homes in Anapa

If you have a grand event in your life, then mark it royally in a magnificent place called Golden Bay (Anapa). The hotel will appeal to absolutely everyone. Every little thing has been thought out here, so that the guests' relaxation becomes truly unforgettable. This is the most comfortable hotel on the entire Black Sea coast. It combines stunning beauty and comfort so that guests can enjoy every moment spent within its walls.



Where is it located?

You will forever love the Golden Bay holiday home (Anapa). The hotel is located directly in the city, almost on the coastline, just 5 meters from the beach. It has an extremely favorable location, only 60 meters from the seaport of Anapa.

The airport is located quite close, only 18 km from a stunning holiday destination called Golden Bay (Anapa). The hotel attracts tourists with stunning sea views, excellent equipment and excellent cuisine.

If you are planning a trip by rail, then from the station to the place - 9 km. If you book in advance, a shuttle service will be provided. This also applies to meeting guests using air transport.

The main differences from other holiday homes of the Black Sea coast

If you have not yet decided on a place of rest, then you will find Golden Bay (Anapa) - a hotel that is a brilliant combination of coziness, luxury and comfort. This is a real exclusive. It is favorably distinguished from all other hotels by luxurious bungalows. This is an attribute of paradise, starry life, into which you will plunge for the whole time of vacation. Each guest can choose a place to live, as the houses are located in several lines, starting from the shore, where in the evenings you can listen to the sound of the surf. The Golden Bay beach in Anapa is considered one of the cleanest and most comfortable, and you will have to take only a few steps to meet the world of bliss and solar heat.



We choose housing

The most popular is the first line of houses right next to the shore. If you plan to spend the whole vacation sunbathing and swimming, then feel free to choose this option. You will remember the Golden Bay beach in Anapa for a long time. Nearby is the resort yacht club, where you can try yourself in the role of a real captain or just watch how beautiful white yachts glide along the waves.

The second line of houses is located very close, only 15 meters from the coast. But there are other amenities. Each of the houses is surrounded by its own territory, where you can spend quiet family evenings, play with children or arrange gatherings with a barbecue. In addition, there is a two-story, comfortable cottage.

The third line is 30 meters away from the sea, but a huge freshwater pool has been built here for holidaymakers. If you do not like salt baths too much, you can swim here. Not all holiday homes in Anapa offer guests such a large selection of exclusive accommodation.



Arrangement

Each house is made of high-quality pine logs, the appearance of which fits in the most natural way in the beauty of the local landscape. Each house is characterized by the highest level of comfort, equipped with satellite TV, a refrigerator, a split system and a plumbing unit. Pleased with a high level of security, bungalows are equipped with electronic locks. Some primitive image of the houses is further emphasized by handmade rattan furniture. It creates additional comfort.

But the most comfortable place is the terrace. In the evening it is so nice to watch the sun go down over the horizon. It is not for nothing that frequent guests say that this is the best hotel. The Golden Bay (Anapa) will forever remain in your heart.

Beach activities

Everything is arranged to the highest level here. You will be offered to hand over things to convenient lockers and enjoy a relaxing holiday on the coast. It is always amazingly clean and beautiful, there are sunbeds, umbrellas and showers. Very happy about the safety of guests. Experienced lifeguards and security guards are constantly on duty at the beach, a first-aid post is working. Well-groomed area has a good rest. It really seems that you are in paradise: no fuss, only peace and tranquility. Apparently, this is exactly what dreams look like: beautiful Anapa, the beach, the Golden Bay Hotel.

Nutrition

Meals are provided on an all-inclusive basis. You are served three times a day with a buffet with a variety of dishes and snacks, as well as amazing desserts. The abundance of fruit will bring completeness to the meal, and a glass of wine at lunch or dinner will give a good mood. At any other time, you can go to a bar or coffee shop where you will be served with pleasure.



Cost of rest

You can order a package with or without meals. In the latter case, only the breakfast will be included in the price. In addition, the second package does not allow you to use the well-maintained beach and outdoor pool with jacuzzi at no extra charge. You can choose the most convenient option for yourself, before making a ticket to the hotel "Golden Bay" (Anapa). The prices below were valid last season (2014), while new ones are still under development.

Accommodation in beach houses (first line) will cost 16,000 from May to September and 8,000 from December to May. Houses of the second line are no different in cost. In the third line, accommodation is slightly cheaper: 14,000 per season and 6,000 in the winter-spring period. A two-story comfortable cottage will cost 25,000. Prices are calculated with the condition of living two adults and a child up to six years. Extra bed is paid separately: 2500 per day per season and 1000 in the off-season.
