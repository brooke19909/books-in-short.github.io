Radar detector

Police radars are perhaps the most common driver problem on the road. Therefore, the addition of a radar detector to recognize all types of police radars will be an excellent function of the video recorder. This is what the developers did, introducing customers to the perfect device. Neoline X-COP 9000 is capable of recognizing not only radars, but also low-power speed-fixing devices. In addition, a GPS module is built into the DVR, notifying the driver even of complexes that do not emit any signals. Despite modern technological progress, a device with such capabilities is still not so easy to find. The hybrid quickly compares the received data with the database and notifies the owner of the approach to the speed control area.



The detector has four operating modes:

  1. "Town". In this mode, the device switches to a lower sensitivity and cuts off all false positives.
  2. "Track". Sensitivity, on the contrary, increases.
  3. "Turbo". Thanks to him, you can receive a warning about approaching the radar at high speed.
  4. "Intellectual". The hybrid independently changes the sensitivity, given the speed of the car.

Video recording

The photofixing function is quite a rarity in modern DVRs. Some do not cope well with her, while others do not have it at all. But this model does not fit any of these criteria. Neoline X-COP 9000 copes with the function of photographic fixation - it is capable of recording videos in a fairly high resolution (1920x1080) and at a speed of about 30 frames in one second. The settings menu provides for the adjustment of brightness, contrast, sharpness and activation of the dynamic range expansion algorithm, which is a really necessary opportunity.

As a result, the driver can see a high quality image. When viewing the recorded video, all the smallest details will be noticeable: for example, it will be possible to clearly see the car numbers of a car moving nearby, all road signs falling into the field of view of the DVR, as well as the faces of people passing by.

During night shooting, the final image will be no worse. Here, too, vehicle numbers, roadsides and the nearest lighting sources will be clearly visible. The only minus is the not too loud noise that is constantly observed when shooting video at night. Although people usually don‚Äôt need sound recording, the main goal is to capture certain objects. At a distance of about 20-25 meters, all the necessary little things will be visible, and the light coming from lamps, windows or traffic lights will not interfere with shooting.

In addition to all of the above advantages and features, it is also necessary to note one more interesting function. The fact is that the DVR can also perform the alarm operation. That is, if you leave the device in the car at night and turn on the corresponding mode, then the motion sensor will work instantly, and the hybrid will start recording. This is not only a fairly reliable protection against theft, but also a temptation for attackers. After all, with knowledge of such information, the owner of the car and the DVR can detect broken glass and the absence of a radar detector in the morning. And another small advantage is the battery built into the case. Thanks to him, the DVR will be able to shoot while not only within the car, but also at a certain distance from it. For example, in an accident, in addition to the collision of the car itself, you can record a conversation with the inspector, which in the future may come in handy.
