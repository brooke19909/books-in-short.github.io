Pattern of women's shorts: do it yourself

The actual clothes in the heat for most girls and women are tight-fitting shorts. In the summer they are so comfortable to wear, especially if they are short and cute. Why not sew them yourself, because there will be no analogues to your work. But, alas, only a few know how to sew shorts at home. If you are not one of them, do not be discouraged, we will be happy to tell you how to do it right. And the pattern of women's shorts will help in this.

Start





The basis of the pattern can be any previously created model of trousers. It is only worth considering that the pattern of women's shorts will depend entirely on what type of waist you need. But in such a situation, there are not so many options - understated or standard. The classic shorts have a high waistline. In fashion, as you know, a lower landing. It is worth remembering that, if desired, the pattern of women's shorts in the waist line can be omitted. It is not difficult to do this: it is necessary to postpone four or five centimeters down from the waist on the pattern. The very length of the shorts is determined as follows: it is necessary to postpone six to seven centimeters from the step line. After that, you need to separately cut the belt of women's shorts. But before you start cutting directly, be sure to make an increase on the front part so that then there is a fastener with a gap. The width of the increase will be equal to three centimeters, and the length is 15 cm.

Result

As a result of the work, details of the cut will be obtained. These are two front halves and two rear halves. The belt of the front and rear halves will consist of four parts. The resulting pattern of women's shorts is quite easy to sew, because the presence of pockets and other decorative elements and jewelry in this option is not provided.





Stitching

The model you cut is sewn very simply due to the absence of pockets. So, the pattern of women's shorts is sewn as follows:

  1. It is necessary to make a marking and to sew in side seams, allowances. Process and iron them.
  2. It is necessary to mark the stepping seams, allowances. Also process and iron them.
  3. It is necessary to make a basting, after which it will be necessary to grind the middle back seam to the mark of stitching the zipper.
  4. Stitch the zipper "in the frame" in front of women's shorts.
  5. The outer parts of the belt should be reinforced with thermal cloth. An outer belt should be attached to the shorts.
  6. Then we sew the inner parts of the belt along the side seams, folding them face to face with the outer side of the belt (sew along the sides and top).
  7. Turn the belt to the front side. Now tuck the open edge. Sew the belt on all sides.
  8. Sew a hook.
  9. Tuck three to four centimeters shorts at the bottom. Sew.

Conclusion



This is the overview of how the pattern of shorts for women shorts is created, can be considered complete. It will not take you too long to create this item of clothing. Particularly quickly, things will go after good practice, which you will definitely have if you have the proper desire. Good luck in creating wonderful short summer style shorts in which you will look amazing!
