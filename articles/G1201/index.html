How to make a surprise for your beloved do it yourself

We all love gifts and surprises. Someone likes to receive them more, and someone loves to do them, and today we‚Äôll talk about how you can prepare a surprise for your beloved yourself.

We girls always try to give something soft, sweet and often plush, but men love practical gifts. And the principle of "give the person what you yourself would like" is inappropriate here, because we want to surprise our beloved.



With your own hands you can make a very simple and at the same time practical present - daily recognition. To do this, you will need a diary, a pen and a little imagination. I think you have already guessed what the point is. On each page you just need to write why you love your soul mate.

You can do even trickier, but the reaction will have to wait longer. Choose significant dates for you and only on these pages describe your feelings. Imagine the surprise of a young man when, after a couple of months (or maybe more), he sees that it was not a banal notebook, but a love message. In addition, such a gift will be useful, especially if your man is used to planning his day.



One more surprise for a loved one with his own hands will be made very simple too. But he will obviously please your boyfriend in the early gloomy morning when he decides to make himself a cup of coffee. So, you need sugar, some water and heart shapes for ice. Add a little water to the sugar, put it in molds and wait until it dries completely (12-15 hours). Then we put all this in a sugar bowl and wait.

Similarly, you can do with dumplings, for example. It's longer and harder, but we girls are not looking for easy ways. And thus we kill two birds with one stone: we fed and made a little surprise to our beloved with our own hands.

Another rather uncomplicated pleasantness is love coupons. Each coupon can be spent on a specific purpose: breakfast in bed, going to the cinema for a movie chosen by your sweetheart, massage, candlelight dinner, hug time, one day without quarrels, a day without phone calls or the fulfillment of any desire.

This is just a sample list, and you can complement it and upgrade as you like.



If you own graphic editors, then all you need to do is find the right pictures and enter the desire and validity period of the coupon there. This will be a great surprise for a loved one. With your own hands, you can also draw illustrations for coupons if you could not find a printer. This gift is good in that it can be stretched for a couple of weeks.

You can also make a surprise for your beloved do it yourself on the anniversary of a relationship. Recently, girls very often make video presentations from joint photos. However, you can go further and make a real video where the main character is not your soul mate, but you yourself. You can shoot such a video in memorable places for you, for example, where you met, kissed for the first time or held hands. All you need to do is just remember how it all began ... You will succeed!

Most importantly, remember that men are generally not very romantic, and therefore your surprise may not cause a storm of emotions. But despite this, be sure that your loved one will appreciate it!
