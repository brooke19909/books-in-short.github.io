Resorts Tenerife. Las americas

Perhaps there is no person who would not know about the existence of truly paradise Canary Islands in the Atlantic Ocean. The largest of them is Tenerife, which focuses on shopping centers, entertainment venues, and better beaches. The island is also famous for the most striking and eccentric parties, which are constantly arranged here. And the main resort of Tenerife, Las Americas, is located in the very south of the island, and is characterized by a warm climate and an active night and day life. In order to find out all the charms of this place in more detail, we will start with the main one, namely the beaches.



Like all recreation areas of Tenerife, Las Americas is completely covered with fine black volcanic sand. It is with such a natural heritage that the Canaries are famous. This resort is conditionally divided into eight beaches, the infrastructure and equipment on which, in principle, are identical. Being on one of them is free, but you will have to pay for umbrellas, sun loungers and other amenities, and a lot. It is worth noting that among all the resorts on the island of Tenerife, Las Americas is the most visited and prestigious, so the prices for accommodation, food, and other services are very high.



Going to rest on the largest island of the whole Canary archipelago, it is worth knowing in advance that nature has divided it into two parts. The northern part is in front of the volcanic ridge, and this territory has a very rich flora and humid climate. However, this area is not safe from seismic and weather surprises. The southern part of Tenerife, Las Americas in particular, in its landscape resembles a desert, where it is always hot and dry. Coolness can only be found near the ocean. Once in this paradise, everyone understands what real entertainment and fun is. The island has a huge number of clubs for every taste, as well as organized beach disco venues where you can dance Spanish salsa day and night. The island has enough historical attractions that have been built since the conquest of these lands by the Spaniards. Attention deserves the palace of San Miguel, at the court of which knightly tournaments are still held . Every tourist can look at this miracle, having previously bought a ticket for the performance.

In the very center of the resort is the famous Aqualand water park. At this place there are various water attractions for both kids and adults. The map of Las Americas tells you about the other assets and entertainment centers of this area. Tenerife and its natural beauties are famous all over the world, so if you find yourself in this place, be sure to use the terrain or GPS-navigator, so as not to miss anything. Indeed, in order to feel the fullness of local relaxation, you must see both the Spanish colonial buildings and the natural wonders that lie in the local dunes, hills and ocean waves.

Undoubtedly, Tenerife (Las Americas) can be considered the most amazing and truly paradise vacation spot. Photos of this resort are presented in the article, and looking at them, you can already feel the enchanting atmosphere that always reigns there. Therefore, go on vacation to the Canaries without hesitation, and you certainly will not regret it.
