Club Hotel Mira 3 * (Kemer, Turkey): reviews and photos of tourists

It‚Äôs no secret that thousands of our compatriots choose Turkey as their place of rest every year. This country is famous for its hospitality, golden beaches and the atmosphere of ancient history, which, in fact, makes it so popular. On its territory there are a lot of resort cities, large and not very, where hundreds of hotels and luxury resort complexes offer their services. And the city of Kemer is very popular among tourists.

One of the rather popular places in the territory of Kemer is the cozy hotel complex Club Hotel Mira. Many travelers choose this hotel due to the favorable price / quality ratio. So where exactly is the hotel located? What living conditions can I expect? Can I bring a child with me? Where is the beach located? The answers to these questions will be interesting to many readers looking for a place for an unforgettable vacation.

Where is the hotel situated?





Of course, for many travelers, the location of the hotel is important. And the Club Hotel Mira 3 * hotel complex in this regard has some advantage, as it is located in the very center of Kemer, close to all shopping, entertainment venues and interesting sights. The distance to the sea shore is only 250 meters - this distance is easy to overcome on foot.

The equally popular resort town of Antalya is located 45 kilometers from the hotel complex. It is on its territory that the nearest international airport is located, the exact distance to which is 55 kilometers. Nevertheless, you should not worry about such a long distance, since almost all travel agencies, as well as the hotel itself, provide a shuttle service.

Hotel complex Club Hotel Mira 3 * (Kemer): description of the territory and infrastructure

This hotel complex stands out with its thoughtful and convenient infrastructure. Its total area is not so big and is 6500 square meters. Along the perimeter are residential buildings, restaurants and office buildings. The courtyard is reserved for swimming pools. There is a small but very cozy garden with neatly trimmed lawns and curly bushes - it's always a pleasure to spend time here. By the way, the entire territory of the Club Hotel Mira 3 * hotel is decorated with greenery - tall palm trees and spruce trees create such a welcome protection from the sun, and flowering bushes and exotic plants are simply pleasing to the eye.





By the way, the hotel was opened in 1998. But not so long ago, in 2013, a complete reconstruction was carried out - the complex fully meets all international standards. This is a great place for a comfortable and cozy stay.

Description of rooms at the hotel

The hotel complex Club Hotel Mira is considered relatively small - in its territory there are 52 rooms. These are standard spacious rooms, designed for 1-2 adults and a child.

Naturally, the rooms have the necessary set of furniture and household appliances. The floors are carpeted. Some rooms have a balcony or terrace. The air conditioning system, which, by the way, works properly, will be indispensable in the midday heat. You can watch satellite channels on TV, including some Russians. There is a telephone, but the cost of international and intercity calls is not included in the total cost of living - you will need to pay extra separately.





The room comes with a private bathroom with a comfortable and spacious shower and toilet. There is a large wall mirror, sink for washing, a set of personal hygiene products. Naturally, each guest receives a set of towels.

The rooms are cleaned every day, changing towels. Bed linen is changed approximately once a week or at the request of guests.

Food scheme for guests: what's on the menu?

Club Hotel Mira 3 * offers its guests an all-inclusive meal plan, which is the standard for Turkey and many other tourist countries. This means that the price includes full three meals a day, as well as light snacks and Turkish drinks, which can be obtained during the day in bars and cafes.





Is it so good that they feed on the territory of the Club Hotel Mira 3 * hotel complex? Reviews indicate that the food here is quite diverse. Three times a day, guests gather in the main restaurant, where they are served buffet style. The menu has a sufficient number of light snacks, salads, meat and fish dishes. They also offer a fairly large selection of seafood. Pleased with the abundance of fresh fruit. And of course, guests will be offered a selection of fresh pastries, desserts and traditional Turkish sweets. By the way, every day you will find a ‚Äúsurprise from the chef‚Äù - an original main course, which you will surely like.

Other catering facilities on site

Of course, there are other institutions in the complex. Club Hotel Mira offers guests a visit to the lobby bar, where polite staff will offer you light snacks, as well as chilled drinks, tea, coffee and exotic cocktails.

Another bar is located in the courtyard, by the pool. Here you can have a great time on the summer terrace. It is worth noting that the price includes only Turkish-made drinks. For imported products will need to pay extra.

Where is the nearest beach located?





The hotel has its own private plot on the city beach. The distance to it is only 250 meters - it can be easily reached even on foot. Nevertheless, in the morning a small bus runs between the beach and the hotel, the services of which guests can use for free.

The beach, as well as the descent into the sea, is sandy and pebble here, so it is advisable to have special shoes with you. On the shore there are sun loungers and parasols. Beach towel rental is also available.

Are there any conditions for water recreation?

Naturally, lovers of more active recreation will not be bored. The beach has a convenient volleyball court. Here, for an affordable fee, you can rent a catamaran, canoe or boat. There will also be the opportunity to go water skiing or take a short boat trip along the coast.

Hotel complex Club Hotel Mira (Kemer): families with children

Today, many parents travel with their children. Naturally, such tourists are primarily interested in questions about whether there are appropriate conditions on the territory of a hotel. So what can Club Club Mira offer?

In fact, there is not much entertainment for young guests. However, some amenities are still available. In particular, at the request of guests, a cot for the baby or a comfortable folding bed for an older child will be delivered to the room absolutely free of charge. The restaurant has enough comfortable high chairs. There is no special children's menu here. But you should not worry about nutrition, because among the variety of dishes you are sure to pick up something nutritious and useful for the baby. And older children will surely like sweets and homemade ice cream.





As for entertainment, for children there is a special section of the pool. It is spacious enough, but small, and therefore completely safe. Children will be able to dabble merrily under the supervision of parents and staff.

Additional services for travelers

Of course, on the territory of the hotel complex Club Hotel Mira tried to create all the conditions for a comfortable stay. For example, there is a small but well-guarded parking lot, which will come in handy if you travel by your own transport. If desired, and the availability of the necessary documents in the city, you can easily rent a transport, which will greatly facilitate movement and provide an opportunity to independently explore the surroundings.

For a small fee, you can rent a safe from the administrator - here your valuables will be completely safe. Laundry facilities are also available. There is no medical office on the hotel premises, but in case of health problems, employees will definitely call a doctor in your room (his services are paid separately).

Entertainment and leisure

Of course, the hotel has some entertainment that will appeal to travelers. For example, the courtyard is reserved for a large pool. The water here is clean and warm - you can freshen up and swim at any time of the day. Around are sun umbrellas and sun loungers. Here guests sunbathe, relax, get to know each other and just have fun.

By the way, the hotel staff will certainly offer you to visit the hammam. This service is paid separately, but a real Turkish bath is worth the money. Here, professional massage therapists will offer their services, who, judging by the customer reviews, know their business.





You can also spend time in the garden - there are sun loungers, hammocks, comfortable benches and gazebos, where you can relax in the shade or take sun baths. Also in the fresh air there are table tennis tables - fans of this game will be able to have fun.

What else can Club Club Mira offer? Turkey is a country with an incredibly interesting history. Therefore, here you can fully enjoy a sightseeing vacation. On the territory of the hotel you will be helped to choose a suitable tour, to make a route for independent travel and, of course, they will tell you about the most interesting places and give a lot of useful recommendations.

What type of vacation is the hotel suitable for?

The hotel complex Club Hotel Mira 3 * (Kemer) is perfect for almost any type of vacation. According to reviews, couples with children and even elderly people often stop here. But most often this place becomes a haven for youth companies, because in the city center there are many attractions for young people. The hotel is perfect for a budget holiday in decent conditions. Of course, if you prefer to visit luxury resorts where there is not even a need to leave their territory, then this place you will not like. But if you are an inquisitive tourist who prefers outdoor activities, traveling around the surroundings, exciting discoveries and explorations of a new country, then this hotel will come in handy.

Additional Information

It is worth noting that credit cards are accepted at the hotel. This is very convenient as there is no need to travel with a large amount of cash. By the way, you can pay by card in many places of the city, including shopping centers and restaurants.

At the hotel you can not live with animals, so for tourists traveling with pets, this place is not suitable.

Hotel Traveler Reviews

It's no secret that a huge amount of valuable information can be gleaned by asking the opinion of travelers who have already managed to visit one place or another. So what do tourists say about the Club Hotel Mira? Reviews are for the most part positive.

First of all, guests celebrate a beautiful and convenient area, as well as wonderful mountain landscapes. The location is extremely advantageous, since in the center of the tourist city there are a huge number of cafes, shopping malls, markets, shops where you can buy everything you need, have a delicious lunch and have a great time.

As for the rooms, the Club Hotel Mira hotel complex offers pretty good living conditions. The rooms are really spacious. Tourists note that in some rooms the furniture is not new. Nevertheless, all of it is serviceable and in good condition, as well as household appliances. The cleaning is carried out daily, and the employees really work in good faith - the rooms are kept in almost perfect cleanliness.

By the way, guests speak well about employees. Almost all employees to one degree or another speak Russian. The service is attentive, but unobtrusive - here you will be greeted with cordiality and will be ready to help solve any problems.

An important fact is the relatively low cost of living. That is why many tourists prefer the Club Club Mira 3 * hotel complex. Kemer is a great place for a bright, unforgettable vacation on the sea coast.
