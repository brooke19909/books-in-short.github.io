Solomon quotes nowadays

The Jewish king of the Israeli state, Solomon, became famous in his country and abroad thanks to his wisdom and successful rule. For more than thirty centuries, the personality of this statesman and thinker has been overgrown with legends and fabrications. He is considered the wisest of the wisest, but to verify the accuracy of the statements of the Jewish sage is no longer possible. However, the quotes of Solomon to this day are relevant and in demand.

Solomon's biography

The main source of information about the ruler of the Jewish kingdom is the Bible. To this day, disputes about the veracity of this scripture do not subside. However, at present, we still officially accept the fact that such a person lived and rules. The years of his reign date back to about the 10th century BC. e. Solomon was the third king of the Jewish state, before his father David was in power.

Under Solomon, the Jerusalem Temple was built, one of the main religious buildings of the entire Jewish people.

Over the 40 years of King Solomon's rule, the state has flourished and gained power. The wisdom of Solomon helped his country and people live in peace and prosperity.

The main thing is wisdom: acquire wisdom and acquire intelligence with all your possessions.

The king also spoke of love:

Hatred arouses contention, but love covers all sins.

He respected and appreciated the family:

Together, it would be better than one, because if they fall, they will raise one another, but woe, if one falls, and to raise him, there is no other, and even if two are lying, is it warm to them, how can they warm themselves?

A wise wife sets up her house, and a stupid one destroys it with her own hands.

Solomon's quotes are known throughout the world, they contain advice and the simple truth of life, and sometimes a complex philosophical meaning. You can agree with them, you can dispute, but everyone can find something important for themselves. It will be a conversation about love, or about stupidity, about anger or hatred, about the abundance or meaning of life.

Solomon not only speaks of difficult situations in simple words, but also often gives advice. After many years, these wise thoughts from the past and today can help prevent mistakes.

The history of the famous quote

In addition to numerous quotes and wise sayings , many parables are associated with the name of Solomon. One of them is known from Solomon's quote, "Everything Passes." This popular parable is about the famous ring of Solomon.

Once the Jewish ruler turned to the sage, so that he would give him advice on how to cope with difficult life situations. To which the sage gave Solomon a ring where an engraving was made with the words: "Everything will pass." The elder explained that when faced with an ambiguous emotional situation, whether it is joy or anger, you need to remove the ring from your finger and look at the inscription.

In one of these situations, where anger took power over emotions, Solomon took off the ring, and having not received reassurance from the familiar inscription, we were surprised to find another engraving on the inside of the ring, which read: "And this too."

There is even a continuation of this story, when the king "found" another inscription on his deathbed, which read: "Nothing passes."

Today, probably, this is the most famous quotes of Solomon that every person on Earth knows.
