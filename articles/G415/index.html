Pink beach: where is it located? Description

Snow-white beaches, located in the most exotic corners of our planet, have not surprised anyone for a long time. However, there are colorful places in the world that will make you freeze with admiration, and the Bahamian archipelago is just such an exception. Picturesque Harbor, considered an elite holiday destination of the island state, attracts wealthy tourists from around the world like a magnet. They rush here to see with their own eyes the fantastically beautiful pink beach, famous for its unusual color of sand.

Perfect beaches

The unique nature has always been the main attraction of the original archipelago. The Bahamas has been welcoming travelers for many years, giving them memorable happy moments. But the most special among the many islands of the state in the Atlantic Ocean is the famous Harbor, which is known for its beaches. Equipped and great for swimming, they are just perfect in every way.



Natural phenomenon

Pink Beach (Pink Sands Beach) is especially loved by vacationers who are delighted with a wonderful phenomenon, which at first can be attributed to optical illusion. An amazing indescribable picture opens up to the eyes of the guests of the island: endless sky-blue, clear water and outlandish sand. Such magnificence of the virgin nature of Harbor will make even travelers who have seen a lot of things freeze with delight.



What is the reason for the unnatural shade of sand?

Transparent turquoise water also changes its color, and in the light of fading sunlight it becomes the color of a rosy dawn. Such a phenomenon is associated with single-celled insects living on the beach, whose carapace is painted in a delightfully gentle tone. When the surf throws them ashore in huge numbers, the microscopic shells are broken and mixed with sand, giving it a unique shade.

The ocean so carefully grinds the hard shells thrown onto the pink beach (Harbor) that the sand poses no danger to the guests of the archipelago: it is resilient, soft, and it is very pleasant to lie on it. I must say that the water grinds red corals into tiny grains, which also give a peculiar shade along with foraminifera insects.



It is amazing how the color of local attractions changes with the lighting. Under the bright rays of the warm sun, lilac-metallic reflections lie on the world's most beautiful beach of pink sands, and at sunset it impresses with a purple palette.

Elutera beaches

Small Harbor, stretching five kilometers in length, is located next to the island of Eleuthera, and between them is a narrow pier. Those who crave new adventures and experiences will be transported by ferry to a picturesque place famous for its virgin nature and lesser-known pink beaches. With a population of more than seven thousand people, the corner lost in the ocean is practically uninhabited, and only masterpieces created by nature itself receive new guests. Located in the west and east, they surprise a few tourists.
