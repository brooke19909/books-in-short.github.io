Atrium Hotel (Vityazevo, Golden Sands Street, 15): description, services, reviews

High-quality rest and comfortable accommodation will be provided by the Atrium Hotel. Vityazevo is a cozy resort village that will delight you with beautiful beaches and well-developed infrastructure.





A bit about the hotel

Atrium Hotel is a cozy guest house that accepts tourists to relax in the summer. The institution includes two beautiful five-story buildings, made in an elegant architectural manner. Decorative balconies offer city or sea views.

The main feature that the Atrium Hotel (Vityazevo) can be proud of is its luxurious pool, made according to the original design. Located at the height of the second floor, it is well lit by the sun, and also provides an excellent overview of the picturesque surroundings. A convenient location provides quick access to entertainment and other infrastructure facilities.





Location

It is worth noting the advantageous location, which attracts tourists to such an institution as the Atrium Hotel. Vityazevo, Golden Sands Street, 15, is the address where you can find this institution when you come to rest. It is 8 km from Anapa Airport, and 10 km from the train station. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel is the central promenade "Paralia", as well as the popular water park "Olympia".

Accommodation

Tourists will be able to enjoy comfortable living conditions and quality service, choosing Vityazevo for relaxation. Guest houses offer you a comfortable apartment. Atrium Hotel offers the following options for accommodation:

  • Junior suites are comfortable rooms that can accommodate from 2 to 4 guests, depending on the area. It can accommodate in large or twin beds. They also have soft sofas for relaxing.
  • Suite is a luxurious two-room suite, which consists of a bedroom and a living room, interconnected by an internal door. The room has a spacious balcony with sea view.
  • VIP apartments are the best accommodation option in this hotel. The room has two bedrooms (main and additional), each of which is equipped with separate bathrooms. A large living room combines the functions of a relaxation area, a bar and a compact kitchen.

As for the design of rooms, their interior is quite concise and modern. All rooms are designed in the same style.





Room Amenities

If you value comfort, be sure to pay attention to such an accommodation option as Atrium Guest House. Vityazevo will provide you with access to clean beaches and entertainment, and this institution will provide the opportunity to use the following amenities:

  • in all rooms, except those located on the ground floor, there is a balcony with plastic garden furniture and clothesline;
  • air conditioning system that will allow you to take refuge in the room from the southern heat;
  • in the refrigerator you can always store a small food supply;
  • modern TV, which will provide the ability to view cable channels;
  • The combined bathroom has a shower or bath.

Accommodation with children

Atrium Hotel sets differentiated rates for adults and children. So, if you want to relax with the kids, then the following conditions apply to them:

  • 1 child under 5 years old stays in the room for free (without providing an extra bed);
  • children under 12 years old will cost 200 rubles a day;
  • children under 15 years old are accommodated in rooms for 300 rubles per day.

What is included in the price

A lot of services are provided to its guests by the Atrium Hotel. Vityazevo will give you an unforgettable vacation. Paying for accommodation, you will receive the right to use the following service:

  • daily wet cleaning with regular replacement of bath towels and bed linen;
  • use of wireless Internet in the rooms and public areas of the hotel;
  • meeting guests at the airport or at the railway station;
  • entertainment for children (animation, games room, outdoor playground, etc.);
  • swimming in the pool.

Additional services

One of the most popular areas of domestic tourism is Vityazevo. Guest houses provide travelers with all the necessary services for a comfortable stay and a good rest. Speaking about the Atrium Hotel, you can use the following additional service:

  • transfer to the railway station, airport or anywhere else in the village;
  • 24-hour security and video surveillance to ensure the safety of guests;
  • at the reception you can rent a safe deposit box;
  • unplanned replacement of bed linen and bath towels;
  • use of laundry services;
  • organization of excursion programs to get acquainted with local attractions;
  • providing integrated nutrition;
  • a visit to the summer cafe, which specializes in dishes prepared on the grill, room service.




Additional Information

Before you go on vacation to a particular hotel, you should get a number of additional information about his work. So, about this resort you need to know the following information:

  • check-in of newly arrived guests in the rooms starts after 14:00 (early availability is possible if available);
  • on the day of departure, guests are required to leave the room and hand in the keys for reception until noon (late check-out is possible subject to hourly payment);
  • only 1 extra guest is allowed in the room;
  • pets are not allowed in the hotel;
  • Only cash is accepted (terminals for plastic cards are not provided);
  • booking a room in advance, the guest is obliged to make an advance payment as an order confirmation (for this purpose, the administration will contact you by phone number to arrange this opportunity);
  • if the guest stays at the hotel for more than 5 days, he is provided with a free transfer from the airport (railway station) and back;
  • Cots are available for babies under the age of 2 years.

Nevertheless, the convenient location of the hotel compensates for many shortcomings and negative aspects.
