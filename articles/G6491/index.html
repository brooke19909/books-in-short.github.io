China is an incredibly interesting country, it is especially interesting for its culture. That is why visiting China is the dream of many people. I would like to recall from the lessons of geography that the territory of this country is quite large, so the question immediately arises: which city to go to? I hasten to offer an answer: of course, to Shanghai.

Shanghai is the largest city in China and the largest seaport in the world, an important economic and cultural center. In this city several cultures united at once.
