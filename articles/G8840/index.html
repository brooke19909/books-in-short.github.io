How to find out VTB 24 card balance - step-by-step guide

VTB 24 Bank has developed several options for checking the card balance for its customers. In order to choose the most suitable method for yourself, you need to know some of their nuances. How to find out the balance of the VTB 24 card will be described in this article.

What is needed

In order to check the balance of your credit or debit card, you must have:

- Internet connection;

- VTB 24 bank card;

- PIN code to access the card;

- telephone.

Depending on the verification method, you will need to use one or several of the above items at once. For the convenience of cardholders, the bank has provided the simplest and most affordable ways, which will be described below.

The most affordable way that can save time is to check the balance using a mobile phone. In order to find out the balance of the VTB 24 card via SMS, you must first connect this service. If this method was not selected at the conclusion of the contract, then it will need to be additionally included in the list of bank services. To do this, go to the office and ask the manager to dial a command on the phone or find out the code to connect. Naturally, you will need a passport confirming the identity of the person, and, possibly, other documents at the request of a service specialist. You can find out the balance of the VTB 24 card after the account number is linked to the customer number. He will be able to receive information about his condition through SMS. When withdrawing or replenishing your account in cash, notifications will be sent. Thus, you can control the movement of cash on the account and be aware of their amount on the balance sheet. The only drawback of the SMS informing service is that you have to pay for it. Otherwise, it is very convenient and affordable.



ATM

Today there is an alternative way to save your time and effort - this is ATMs. You just need to get to the nearest device and use your card. After identifying the card, you will need to enter the PIN code of the card and select "Account Information". The bank machine will issue a check, which will contain information on the account at VTB 24 Bank.
