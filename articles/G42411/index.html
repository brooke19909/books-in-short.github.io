Culture and Leisure Park in Adler: address, attractions and how to get

How to entertain a child in the evening, having arrived in Adler? The beach is already uncomfortable, but the child really wants to have fun? Then do not waste time and immediately head to the Adler Park of Culture and Rest. You can find out how to get there and what is interesting there below.

Despite the fact that Adler Central Park is more positioned as a place of leisure for families with children, visitors of any age will receive a lot of positive emotions and positive emotions even from ordinary leisurely walks along well-groomed shady alleys.

Almost every adult wants to return to childhood and enjoy all its charms: eat cotton candy, ride fun attractions, dance at a disco. No one leaves here in a bad mood. On the contrary, the park will bring only a sea of ‚Äã‚Äãpleasure and unforgettable impressions. How not to visit such a miracle?

A bit of history

In 1980, a park was founded on a two-hectare land plot. The grand opening took place on May 1 of that year. Adler Recreation Park very quickly won the love of citizens and tourists. Not so long ago, a reconstruction was carried out, thanks to which the paths became more well-groomed and new attractions appeared.

The pride of local florists and landscape designers is the delightful flower beds and chic rose gardens that attract the attention of everyone who comes here. Romantic gazebos entwined with ivy will help you relax and unwind from the daily hustle and bustle of everyday work.

Tired of the rides, you can go to one of the small cafes, have a bite to eat, take a short break, and gain strength before continuing the entertainment. Also in the park of culture and recreation Adler there are fountains with evening illumination, in which even a simple ice cream seems tastier. Do not believe? Check it out for yourself! A huge number of benches for relaxation and always playing beautiful live music will not leave anyone indifferent.

Almost at every step there are figures of dinosaurs. On a hot day, you can go to an artificial pond. There is also a contact zoo, where you can not only look at the animals, but also feed and pet them. Here you will see squirrel, lamb, goat, rabbits, chickens, geese and guinea pigs.

How to get there

The park is located 1.5 km from the city's railway station. Buses go to the park under numbers 60, 117, 125, 125P, 134 and 173. You need to get off at the stop "Sanatorium" Southern Seaside ", and then return a little to the intersection on Romashek Street. If you just arrived and immediately plan to visit the park, you can get on the bus at number 57k, which runs directly from the airport. If you plan to get by car, here is the address of the park of culture and rest in Adler: Romashek street, 1.
