Prazeres Resort 2 *, Candolim, India: hotel description, traveler reviews

Goa is the smallest Indian state. Nevertheless, even its small territory is divided into southern and northern parts. I must say that the unit is not conditional, since both regions are fundamentally different from each other. Goa resorts located in the southern part include fantastic sandy beaches, exotic nature and pompous five-star hotels. In turn, the northern part is literally saturated with an atmosphere of freedom. There are many oriental bazaars, and tourists can participate in beach parties and dances.



Resorts Goa

The coastline of this Indian state is located so that the sun's rays constantly warm the sandy areas for swimming. The beaches bordered by palm trees are stretched in a continuous line along the coast of Goa - Candolim, Vagator, Baga, Anjuna, etc. Parasailing is the most popular form of water entertainment, although diving, sailing and windsurfing are no less common. Here tourists can choose for themselves any affordable housing option: from luxury hotels to affordable guest houses.

The vast majority of Russian travel agencies specializing in this area offer to visit Goa Candolim, Bagi, Calangute or a small Sinkerim. Prices

This popular Indian resort has long been considered a true paradise for downshifters and freelancers. In addition to its obvious advantages - year-round sun and wonderful beaches on the ocean, cheapness is another plus that characterizes holidays in Goa. Prices here depend on the season. From time to time in travel agencies you can buy last-minute tours at a reasonable price. For example, in January, Russians can pay only nineteen and a half thousand rubles for an eight-day stay in a three-star hotel with a flight, visa cost and the breakfast concept. In the high season, for example, on the Christmas holidays, the price doubles.

Since the main cost of the tour falls on air travel, the longer the tourists relax, the cheaper the trip is per day. The average price of a trip to Goa for two for a period of two weeks is about eighty thousand rubles. The price includes the tour itself and expenses are already in place.

Candolim

This resort is ideal for those who prefer a measured comfortable rest away from unnecessary fuss. Candolim - a small village located in the very south of northern Goa - almost forms a whole with Calangute. The resort has many hotels of various categories. Here is the large hotel complex of the Taj Group chain, the five-star Fortune Select Regina, Golden Tulip Goa, offering a range of services comparable to European services. Prices for accommodation are higher than for apartments in other regions of the northern coast of Goa. In addition, there are many three-and two-star hotels in Candolim that are hidden on the shady streets of the resort, for example Prazeres Resort 2 *.

Infrastructure

Among the services provided at Prazeres Resort 2 * (Candolim) there is a small business center, free parking for cars and mopeds, a cozy lobby, a post office, a jewelry store that sells cheap gold items. There is a small market and a souvenir shop on site. India has always been famous for its cheap gold products. If necessary, residents can use the services of a doctor, dry cleaning for a fee.



The reception is open at any time of the day. Visitors are settled quickly, without bureaucratic delays. The registry also has a fax and a depository safe, for which you also need to pay for the rental of cells. Here you can order a taxi, as well as buy a sightseeing tour.

Housing stock

Prazeres Resort 2 * (Goa) consists of a complex of three- and four-story buildings, in which there are one hundred and forty rooms of the categories "standard", "luxury" and "deluxe". It offers beautiful views of the courtyard with garden or pool. All types of rooms have furnished balconies, there are also clothes dryers. Suites and deluxe suites have a kitchenette with all necessary utensils.

Regardless of the categories, all rooms have comfortable furniture, a luggage rack, wardrobes, a split system for air conditioning, a TV, a telephone and a minibar.

An iron and ironing board, as well as an extra folding bed are available upon request. The rooms have ceramic tiles. Bathrooms combined. The hotel offers all hygiene items free of charge. Pets are not allowed according to the rules. For Internet in the room you need to pay separately.

Nutrition

Hotel Prazeres Resort 2 * offers its customers only a "continental breakfast." A buffet is served in the main restaurant. Judging by the reviews, vacationers were pleased with the proposed food concept. In addition, the hotel has another restaurant, working on a reservation. The menu includes international cuisine, although at the request of the client, the chef prepares delicious traditional Indian dishes. There are also two bars: Karibu and Salsa - where you can get drinks and snacks for a fee.



For those who do not want to eat on site, just go beyond it. Nearby is full of inexpensive cafes, there is an excellent restaurant serving seafood. Judging by the reviews, many Russians preferred to buy fish in the market, which sellers cook there.

Beach

To the swimming area from Prazeres Resort 2 * a maximum of fifteen minutes walk. The road passes by numerous shops and cafes, so it is easy to overcome. Sun beds and umbrellas on the beach are paid. Towels can be obtained from the reception, leaving a deposit.

The beach is sandy, the entrance to the water is gentle. Rubber shoes are not required. The famous Calangute Beach is a kilometer away from the hotel. Every half hour a bus leaves for him. The beach bar serves cocktails or refreshments. On this part of the coast, light winds periodically blow, waves rise, after which the ocean throws large shells and starfish onto the sand.

The hotel periodically hosts theme shows, animation comes. For those who combine relaxation with sightseeing, the on-site tour desk offers tours of Candolim and Calangute, Panjim or Mapus. Tourists are provided with a guide and transport. Those who prefer to explore the sights on their own can rent a bike or car in the office located in Prazeres Resort 2 *.
