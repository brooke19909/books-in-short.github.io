Village Beregovoe (Bakhchisaray district, Crimea): location

Beregovoe is a small resort village in the Bakhchisarai district, spread out on the western coast of the Crimean peninsula. Geographers determine the location of its mouth of one of the four rivers flowing into the Kalamitsky Bay of the Black Sea - the Western Bulganak. The village of Beregovoye is 35 km from the district center (Bakhchisaray). The distance to Sevastopol and Simferopol is approximately 45 km, to Evpatoria - about 60 km.

Beregovoye Village: Acquaintance, Vacationers Impressions

This village is widely known among travelers as a quiet seaside resort, characterized by the presence of comfortable sand and pebble beaches. Many argue that it is simply impossible not to fall in love with this place. Upon arrival, guests are pleasantly surprised by the silence, the freshest rural air, deserts and lack of cars.

Description

This small resort, tourists share, is great for families with small children: the local climate and natural conditions are optimal for kids to stay. Guests note the presence in the village of Beregovoy, Bakhchisaray district (Crimea), of wide sandy beaches, shallow at the seaside, which warms up quite quickly, gentle descent into the water without boulders and stones - everything here seems to be created so that both adults and children fully enjoy their vacation welcoming warm sea. Tourists define the resort infrastructure as quite developed - Beregovoe has shops, cafes and many accommodation facilities for every taste.

Features of rest

For those who choose to stay in the Coastal private sector, in most cases they will have to cook their own meals, although, according to reviews, they cook quite well in small tent cafes with affordable prices. In Beregovoy, tourists share, you can taste the most delicious pork sausages that are unlikely to be found anywhere else. Many vacationers here they worthily replace barbecue.

In the evenings, you can drink fresh cow's milk, enjoy the sunset and the starry sky, and wake up in the morning with birdsong. After breakfast, at around 8-9, you should catch time on the beach while the sun is still caressing, not burning, and there is no risk for the northerners to get sunburned.

Beach

When going to Beregovoye for a vacation, it should be noted that there are always very few people on the beach, even if you arrive at the peak of the season (July - August). There are no cheburechki here, neither music nor the noise of voices can be heard from anywhere - on the shore, vacationers are given the opportunity to enjoy exclusively the sound of sea waves. According to reviews, grandmothers with corn and those rarely come across. The beach in Beregovoye (Bakhchisaray district) is remembered for its cleanliness, the presence of a convenient descent to the sea and small pebbles, which make it pleasant to walk barefoot. In the evening, mud is nailed to the sea, and it looks dirty. But in the morning you can endlessly admire its crystal clearness.

The shops

The village has inexpensive shops and a market. As guests assure, the cost of all goods in local stores is pleasantly different from the traditional resort prices, which many call incredibly high. Of course, there are no chain supermarkets familiar to residents of large cities in the village of Beregovoy (Crimea), but you can buy everything you need in existing small, typically rural shops.

Excursions

The only inconvenience for some vacationers, according to them, is that you have to get up at 5 in the morning to travel to Sevastopol from the village of Beregovoy, Bakhchisaray district. The bus goes there every 2-3 hours, you need to spend about an hour on the road. Therefore, those who want to get to the place even in the morning coolness, and not in the sweltering heat, have to become early birds.

Fans of excursions, vacationers tell you, you can go on a trip directly from Beregovoy: in the resort village there are several racks of travel agencies offering tours for every taste. In addition to Sevastopol, you can go on excursions to Yalta, Bakhchisarai. Buses run frequently, during the day you can stroll through the beautiful places of these famous cities.
