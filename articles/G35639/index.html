DIY box for small items: materials and tools

Do-it-yourself box for little things is fast and without any problems. Of course, a variety of caskets can be purchased at the store, but it is much more pleasant to store items in a box made at home.

Beautiful boxes for little things

Everyone in the house has many boxes of household appliances. And although their term has long expired, but they are standing on the balcony, full of various unnecessary things. They will make wonderful boxes for storing small toys or other trifles. Children can also be involved in the work, who will like this creative process.

Everything that is at hand is useful for work. It could be:

  • glue;
  • Scotch;
  • threads
  • beads;
  • felt or other dense fabric;
  • scissors;
  • ruler;
  • beautiful thick paper;
  • any other materials that may be needed in the work.

First, with the help of scissors or an office knife, you need to cut off all that is unnecessary, and often this is the lid. If there are tears and other flimsy places, they must be glued with tape. In addition, you can place a decorative element here: cut a heart on a wall in the corner, using an office knife for these purposes . Inside, you need to paint everything with white acrylic paint, and on the outside the walls and bottom should be glued over with ordinary landscape paper.

Now you need to start designing the product. If you are interested in how to decorate a box for small things with your own hands, then follow the tips described below, and everything will work out.

It can be glued with decorative paper, it will be prettier if one sheet comes out to wrap the whole box. Indentation should be left in height of about 5 cm. Do not forget about the heart - its location is outlined with a pencil and cut out.





Above, in the corners, where there is a supply of paper, you need to make cuts, bend them in and glue them. An additional piece is cut out of decorative paper to the size of the bottom and glued inside.

Now you need all your imagination to originally decorate a box for small things. You can use patterned napkins to decorate the product using decoupage technique. Instead, you can also stick regular patterns on paper. From felt, you need to cut a heart, in the shape of the one that is on the box, decorate it with threads, beads and glue it on top.

If you want to make wooden boxes under a trifle with your own hands, then in the store you can purchase a blank and decorate it yourself using one of the methods described in this article.

What to make from shoe boxes

If you have gathered a lot of boxes of shoes, then do not throw them away, because this is a great material for making a variety of crafts. The extra box provides a huge field for creativity and allows you to turn the unnecessary into something new and original. It is only necessary to arm yourself with scissors, glue, as well as various decorative elements in the form of paper for scrapbooking, fabric, beads, ribbons, etc.

If you adore jewelry and jewelry, but you have nowhere to store them, then a shoe box will be a great way out of this situation. It will differ not only in interesting design, but also in spaciousness. Stronger boxes are suitable for storing medicines and needlework tools.





Matchbox

To make such a product is not at all difficult. If you are interested in the question of how to make a box for small things with your own hands, then prepare 24 matchboxes, the same number of beads, wire, needle, nippers, glue, scrapbooking paper and follow the instructions below.

With a feather, you need to remove the inner sections from the matchboxes. A bead-shaped handle should be attached to each of them. They are fixed using wire. On the inside, the mounting location, as well as the bottom, is sealed with bright paper or felt.

From the cardboard you need to cut the bottom and lid of the box. Next, the sections are inserted back into the boxes and glued them as illustrated in the picture. First of all, the first layer of boxes is glued to the bottom.





After that, it is necessary to cut out the details from scrapbooking paper. They are needed for the decoration of the casket. The box is left to paste and decorate with additional decorative elements. For convenience, you can number each section.

Tin Casket

At home, there are always small items that need to be stored somewhere. And beautiful boxes for storing little things with your own hands can be made from tin cans. Such a capacity will turn out to be quite roomy, and if it is elegantly decorated, it will make a wonderful present for a loved one.

For work, no special materials or tools are needed. Five tin cans, cardboard, scissors, glue, as well as decor elements (beautiful fabric, lace and ribbon) should be prepared.

The first thing you need to do is decorate the jars. To do this, cut out a rectangle from matter and wrap the outer side of the jar with it. From cardboard you need to cut the bottom and paste it with beautiful paper or cloth. With the help of matter, cans are decorated inside.





Now, for the base, two circles with a diameter of about 20 cm are cut out of cardboard, but it all depends on the size of the jars used. From it, you also need to cut out a rectangle, the height of which will be greater than that of cans.

Blanks are also decorated with fabric. Next, the design must be assembled as shown in the illustration. Additionally, the product can be decorated with lace, ribbons, rhinestones. The tape will also be a great choice for making ties.



Original boxes (pyramid)

Such a box for trifles with their own hands is made for packing small gifts. It can be assembled quite quickly, while it does not require gluing.





The scheme of this box consists of a rectangle or square, from the sides of which four triangles extend. In this case, each face should be supplemented with an auxiliary rounded strip.

At the top of each triangle you need to make one hole. All parts are lifted, bent at the base, ribbons are put in holes and a box is tied.





Dressing

A box for little things with your own hands can be done quickly, but it will get a finished look when the master makes it individual, thanks to a variety of decorative elements. And it's not just paper or fabric.

The original box can be made using the most ordinary twine. Everything is very simple: those places where there will be a rope, you need to grease with glue and put the twine in the selected order (circles, curls, etc.). Empty areas can be sprinkled with sparkles, pre-lubricated with glue. Finally, glitter spots are varnished.

Wall reinforcement

Creating boxes with your own hands from cardboard for little things is not so difficult. But for them to last as long as possible, it is advisable to strengthen their walls:

  1. The first step is to prepare a thick cardboard. Structures equal in size to the walls of the casket should be cut out of it.
  2. To make the corrugated board better bend, it is necessary to draw along the fold line with the blunt side of the scissors. It is necessary to make sure that the cardboard fits snugly against the walls, and excess should be cut off.
  3. The workpiece is fixed on the walls. The glue is applied to the entire surface of the cardboard and pressed to the wall for 35-50 seconds.
