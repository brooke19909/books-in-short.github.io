The zoo in Mogilev (Belarus) is one of the most interesting sights of the city

Zoo in Mogilev - what is this place famous for, what is interesting there? This Belarusian attraction is the hallmark of the city. In addition to the wide variety of animals that live there, visitors can find plenty of entertainment for themselves in the complex, including a walk in the park, visiting souvenir shops, riding a mini-train and much more. And most importantly, here you can see the bison, and not one.

A bit of history

One of the most interesting sights of the city of Mogilev, the zoo, is located in a place called Buinichi, which is two kilometers from the city. Even in school textbooks they wrote that at this memorable place in July 1941, Soviet troops of the 388th Infantry Division, as well as police officers, cadets of the NKVD school and 12,000 Mogilev volunteers, defended their native city for 23 days.

Fairy relaxation

If you go from Mogilev in the direction of another Belarusian city, Bobruisk, then you should definitely look to the left. Just two kilometers from the regional center, in the village of Buinichi, there is an impressive natural complex, which includes ethnographic expositions, a inn, a restaurant, a hotel and a zoo. In Mogilev, this is a great place for walking for the whole family.

Visitors have something to see. For many, the real dream is to look at the powerful giants - living bison. The price of the entrance ticket is minimal, but you can walk around the territory as much as you like, even for a whole day, the ethnographic center is paid separately. His visit is really a trip to another reality, reminiscent, rather, of a small fairytale town than a standard tourist attraction.





Zoo in Mogilev (Buinichi): reviews

People come to the zoo not only in order to relax their soul and body, but also to learn something new for themselves and introduce children to old Belarusian traditions. In the "fairy town" is a number of houses, in each of which you can see the results of the work of local artists. You can see such inscriptions as "Potter", "Baker", "Blacksmith", "Straw" and others. Each house is a separate world of a certain type of skill.

A pleasant atmosphere prevails in the local restaurant. Despite the simple name "Tavern", imperial classicism and at the same time an incredible sense of comfort are floating in the air here. Entrance to the zoo is paid separately (3.5 Belarusian rubles). Here you can see a bear, wolf, fox, rabbits, reindeer, camel, ostrich, wild boars and even the Ussuri tiger.

For people who see bison for the first time, this is truly an unforgettable experience. This furry symbol of Belarus actually makes you feel respect for your person. Visitors in the zoo in Mogilev are constantly there, they are attracted by a wide range of entertainment, as there is something to see, where to walk and have a bite to eat. An important factor is prices, they are actually more than affordable.





Mini-rail - an indescribable joy for children

A trip on a small train, which, by the way, also costs 3.5 Belarusian rubles (price for July 2017), is also a local attraction, mainly for children.



During a 10-minute safari, you can take a closer look at bison, sika deer, and roe deer. Children under three years old can ride a train for free, sitting in the arms of their parents or other adults.

Far-reaching plans

Despite all the visible advantages, the park administration is not going to stop there. The plans include events such as:

  • adaptation to the zoo conditions of new species of animals;
  • the construction of a variety of exhibition facilities;
  • the emergence of new hiking trails with the maximum use of the local landscape;
  • construction of arbors, fountains;
  • building a terrarium and buying birds of prey.

Opening hours of the zoo in Mogilev

The address where the zoo is located is Orlovsky Street 1. The college is located nearby, where future forestry specialists study. They take care of nature, create biotechnological facilities, and install feeders for animals. The complex welcomes guests daily. The working hours of the zoo (Mogilev): on weekdays - 10 30 - 21 30 , on weekends - 9 30 - 21 00 .





Great solution for a warm sunny day.

The zoo in Mogilev is a cute park with enclosures for animals, neat paths, cafes, a mini railway, gazebos. There are a lot of animals, birds sing and it is especially pleasant to be in the cool shade of trees on a hot sunny day. This is one of those wonderful places that you should definitely visit in the city.
