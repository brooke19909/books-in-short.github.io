Ways to check the balance of Smart traffic on MTS

MTS SIM card owners who have activated any of the Smart tariff plans have to periodically review their account status. This is not only about the balance, but also about the number of remaining minutes and messages, as well as Internet traffic. Indeed, Smart tariffs imply the presence of packages with services included in the monthly fee. How to check the balance of Smart traffic on MTS? An overview of all possible options for obtaining data will be given in the current article.





General information

Users of the tariff plans of the red-white operator know that Internet packages can be connected to a SIM card separately without activating tariffs from the Smart series. In the event that the Internet is used, provided as part of some additional option, the procedure for the subscriber to obtain information about the remaining megabytes will be slightly different. However, there are universal ways to view account information, they will also be discussed in the current review, along with how to check the rest of the traffic on MTS Smart.

Services requiring an internet connection

The universal methods for monitoring and managing your number, which were mentioned earlier, include:

  • Personal Internet assistant - any MTS client can access it by going to the official resource of the mobile operator (to access data relating to a specific number, you must first register).
  • Application for mobile gadgets - once you install the My MTS application on your mobile device or tablet PC, you can forget about the issues of the series ‚ÄúHow to check the rest of Smart traffic on MTS, etc.

Each of these services is provided only with Internet access and is completely free. By calling the support line, you can get the necessary data in just a few minutes, provided that there is no long queue. Request through a short command

Quite a lot of customers of mobile operators are used to using USSD requests to view account information. Indeed, sending such commands is free, it does not require the Internet to initiate it, you do not need to wait on the line, waiting for the freed contact center employee to view the data by number. At any time, provided that the SIM card is registered in the network, you can not only get the latest balance information, but also check the rest of the traffic on the MTS Smart tariff, as well as other packages and options that are additionally activated on the client‚Äôs number. So, in order to clarify what balance is currently present in packages, you should enter the request * 100 * 1 # - this is a universal command that users of Smart series tariff plans should remember. Indeed, according to the frequency of requests, this is perhaps the most popular - after checking the balance on the number, of course. By entering such a combination, you can simultaneously obtain data on the remains of Internet traffic, as well as on such important packages as minutes and SMS.

Perhaps, for subscribers who use the Internet provided by the Smart tariff plan, a combination will also be of interest, with which you can specify how many gigabytes can be used if add. package. Just in case, we remind you that after the exhaustion of the main traffic, additional is automatically activated on the SIM card. packet with extra megabytes. You cannot check them with the command * 100 * 1 #. To do this, use the command * 111 * 217 #. Of course, remembering so many commands at once is quite difficult. Therefore, it is recommended to save requests in your gadget - then each time you will not have to remember what numbers you need to enter and how to check the rest of Smart traffic on MTS.

Internet provided as part of additional options (not for TP Smart)

For all other cases of refining data regarding traffic balances, the universal command * 217 # is used. It can be used for any option that provides unlimited Internet from MTS.
