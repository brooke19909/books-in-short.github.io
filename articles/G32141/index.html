How to make cat ears with your own hands for any occasion

Recently, very cute little things have come into fashion. Want to be in trend? Learn how to make cat ears with your own hands. This information is also useful for those who need to create a costume for a carnival party!

Bright character





Since you are only making an accessory, it may differ from how real ears look. At a fun evening event you have to shine! So, how to make the rim of "Cat ears" with your own hands at a disco? You will need:

  • Simple black bezel.
  • The cloth.
  • Cardboard.
  • Scissors.
  • Rhinestones or shiny sequins.
  • Black threads.

What do we have to do:

  1. Draw and cut two ears on the cardboard.
  2. Fold the fabric in half and attach the template to the fold, cut.
  3. Insert the bezel in the center of the cut part. Sew the ears at the girth of the rim.
  4. Make cardboard templates a little smaller by trimming the edges by about 2 millimeters.
  5. Insert one template between the two sides of the eyelet. Sew the eyelet around the edge.
  6. Do the same with the other eye.
  7. Now glue stones or sequins to the accessory.

Bright accessory for an evening event is ready!

Barrettes





Few people can walk around the rim all day. This accessory is able to put pressure on the head after some time and cause inconvenience. Therefore, it is worth learning how to make cat ears with your own hands without a rim. What is needed for this:

  • Scissors.
  • Cardboard sheet.
  • Threads.
  • Felt.
  • Simple hair clips.
  • Glue gun or Moment glue.

Working process:

  1. Draw 2 ears patterns on thick cardboard, cut.
  2. According to the patterns, cut parts from felt.
  3. Trim the patterns around the edges. Insert them between the fabric parts, sew the ears.
  4. Glue the bottom of the eyelet with glue and glue it to the hairpin.
  5. To make the ears less boring, experiment with flowers and decorations. You can sew a ribbon along the edge of the accessory, attach rhinestones, sequins, pompons, sew bows at the base.

Done!

Fur ears





How to make cat ears with your own hands as similar as possible to real ones? What is needed for this:

  • Fur.
  • Felt.
  • A simple bezel.
  • Tape.
  • Cardboard.
  • Scissors.
  • Threads.
  • Clay "Moment".

The process itself:

  1. On cardboard, draw and cut an eyelet pattern.
  2. Attach it to the fur and cut 4 parts.
  3. Sew the parts in pairs together.
  4. From cardboard cut a new template for the inside of the eyelet.
  5. Attach it to the felt and cut 2 parts.
  6. Sew the felt to the fur.
  7. Glue each ear to the rim.
  8. Tie two bows from the ribbon and glue them to the base of the eyelet.

Such an accessory is perfect for a stylized event!

Casual option





The daily version of such an accessory should be calmer, it fits any clothes. How to make cat ears do it yourself for everyday wear? Take:

  • Thick wire.
  • Narrow simple bezel.
  • Pliers.
  • Beads that could be strung on a wire.

What do we have to do:

  1. Cut the wire 6 centimeters longer than the ears themselves should be.
  2. String one bead and bend the wire in half, give the shape of cat ears. The bead should be centered.
  3. String the remaining beads without using 3 centimeters from each edge of the wire, bend them to the side (this part will be attached to the rim).
  4. Tie the ears to the rim.

How to make cat ears with your own hands in a slightly different way?





  1. Take two wires and tie to one spot on the rim.
  2. Put a bead on one end, twist both ends together.
  3. Put on another bead and twist it again. Continue to do this until you have created the required length of one half of the eyelet.
  4. Bend the wire, give it the necessary shape and continue to do the second half according to the same principle.
  5. When finished, cut the wire, leaving 3 centimeters to attach to the rim.
  6. One eye is ready, do the second in the same way.

Wear it with pleasure!
