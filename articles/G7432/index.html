Hotel "Old Courtyard", Nalchik: description and reviews

Fans of ecological recreation should pay attention to the hotel "Old Courtyard". Nalchik is famous for its natural beauties, and therefore the location of the hotel in the park area is an indisputable advantage.





a brief description of

Everything that a demanding tourist may need is in the hotel "Old Courtyard" (Nalchik). You will find a swimming pool, slides, gazebos, a sauna and much more in the territory of this institution. Here, a cozy home atmosphere reigns, which is appropriate both during family and during a romantic or active holiday. Being opened in 2003, the hotel has undergone major repairs several times.

This hotel will delight tourists with its authentic design. Its architecture, landscape design and interior design are made in the best traditions of Kabardino-Balkarian culture. And the restaurant "Old Courtyard" (Nalchik) is popular not only among vacationers, but also among the local population. Here you can try dishes, which are a vivid mixture of Caucasian and Russian cuisine.

Additional Information

Going on vacation to a particular hotel, it will not be superfluous to get acquainted with some features and rules of living in it. Regarding this institution, it is worth paying attention to the following information:

  • in this hotel, guests are evicted before 11:30, and after noon the registration of newly arrived guests begins;
  • Children stay in the room free of charge when using existing beds;
  • the cost of an extra bed is 700 rubles per day;
  • the hotel does not provide the possibility of providing cots and other accessories;
  • pets are strictly prohibited;
  • Payment for accommodation and related services is made exclusively in cash;
  • upon eviction, you may be provided with documents for tax reporting (this need to be reported in advance).
