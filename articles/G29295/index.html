All-inclusive Moscow Region hotels with a pool: rating, reviews

Moscow residents simply need to have the opportunity to have a good and full rest. And it is often better to do this not at home, where you still have to do some household chores, but in some hotel or holiday home. Due to the fact that the tourism business is flourishing more and more, not only in certain resort places, but also near ordinary cities, today it is not at all necessary to travel far from home to have a great rest.

In recent years, outside the city of Moscow, a large number of places have appeared, perfectly suitable for relaxation. You can find all-inclusive hotels in the Moscow Region with a swimming pool, various spa services and other entertainments, or you can choose a simpler and cheaper option.

A variety of vacation spots in the suburbs

Such a vacation will save on train or plane tickets, because you can get to any hotel outside of Moscow by your own car, public transport or taxi. And the travel time will be very short, which will not let vacationers get tired of the road.





The territory of the Moscow region is quite extensive, so the choice of places for recreation here is very large. You can find anything here. Pensions and motels, some of which have still retained the Soviet flavor, can offer a simpler and cheaper vacation. Luxurious all-inclusive hotels near the Moscow region with a swimming pool, which can compete with a range of services with popular Egyptian or Turkish hotels, are perfect for a luxurious pastime. Mid-level institutions that provide guests with everything they need during their holidays for quite affordable money are also available here.

Country hotels in the Moscow Region offer a diverse range of services, from providing gazebos for barbecues and boat rental to organizing business meetings and holiday banquets. So, if necessary, in such hotels you can not only have a good rest, but also have some fun or have a great workshop.

All-inclusive system near Moscow

Some hotels in the Moscow Region boast that they work according to the all-inclusive system, well-known to many travelers. There are not many places like this, but each of these hotels deserves special attention.

Those who are accustomed to "all inclusive" in such popular countries among Russians as Egypt or Turkey, should not expect the same list of services in hotels near Moscow, especially for the same money.





All-inclusive Moscow Region hotels with a pool and various spa services usually offer only three meals according to the buffet system, as well as a visit to the pool and fitness center. There may be bars in the hotel that can offer free drinks and snacks for some time. Also, the price may include some additional services, such as sports equipment rental. Below in this article we will compile a rating of Moscow Region hotels operating on an all-inclusive basis.

Best All Inclusive Hotels

As mentioned above, not all hotels located in the Moscow region can boast of an all-inclusive system. Their number, in principle, can be counted on the fingers, but still potential vacationers have plenty to choose from.

The best all-inclusive hotels in the Moscow Region are located in very picturesque places and offer a pretty good range of services included in the price. On their territory you can not only relax, but also take advantage of excellent congress services. These include 4-star hotels such as Solnechny, Les Art Resort, Areal, and Forest Festival Park.

In addition to the expanded all-inclusive system, hotels in the Moscow Region can offer a reduced package of services. For example, in such magnificent 5-star hotels as Mistral and Imperial, the price of any room includes breakfast or breakfast with lunch, as well as the use of swimming pools and gyms.

"Area" in a pine forest

Those who are looking for all-inclusive Moscow Region hotels with a swimming pool should definitely pay attention to the Areal Congress Hotel. It is located very close to Moscow, at a distance of 17 km from the Moscow Ring Road. You can get to it along the Gorky highway. The well-groomed territory of this hotel is surrounded by a beautiful pine forest and is replete with numerous green lawns, and the building itself is made in the style of an alpine village. This is a great place for those who are planning a family vacation away from a noisy metropolis, or for those who want to visit the large wellness center available here. Hotel service and the services available here allow you to organize various corporate events on its territory. Also in the Hotel "Areal" you can arrange a magnificent celebration, which will be remembered for a long time.





Vacationers live in rooms of the following categories: three-room suite, two-room and two-story suites, one-room studios, standard and standard plus. A large company can accommodate in a separate cottage. The cost of living depends on the selected conditions: the hotel offers half board, full board and ultra all inclusive.

Many of those who have visited the Areal Hotel recommend it for relaxation, if you are not afraid of the rather high price tag for accommodation. The most enthusiastic reviews about the territory, services and restaurants. However, some guests did not like the lack of professionalism of the local staff.

"Solar"

Looking through the best hotels of the Moscow Region, pay attention to the Park-Hotel Solnechny. You can find it near the Zhivaginsky ponds and magnificent coniferous forest, if you drive away from Moscow along the Leningrad highway for 74 km. Hotel Solnechny consists of several buildings, built according to the type of European chalets. This hotel offers all its guests an extended all-inclusive package. In addition to traditional breakfasts, lunches and dinners with drinks and snacks throughout the day, guests can use the beach with sun loungers, an excellent sports center and spa complex, bars and equipment for all kinds of games and outdoor activities. The Hotel Solnechny also has excellent opportunities for organizing work events, there is also a special complex for young guests. Accommodation in Solnechnoye is possible in standard and standard plus rooms, junior suites, suites and suites, as well as in cozy cottages.





The guests who visited this hotel note the excellent service and the really functioning all-inclusive system, when even plain water can be found for free, for example, in the lobby. Some complaints are caused by the local restaurant, where tables for 6 people prevail and you have to eat with strangers. Also, the rooms do not provide internet.

"Forest Art Resort"

At about the same distance from the city, only along the Minsk highway, the very popular Les Art Resort Hotel is located, which is ready at any time of the year to accommodate guests in 250 comfortable rooms of different categories, consisting of one room, and in cottages that are separately from the residential building. The local all inclusive system offers not only food with a variety of drinks, but also the use of many entertainment available at the hotel. This includes thermal zones, a swimming pool, an entertainment center for children, a fitness club, billiards, and daily animation for guests of all ages. It is worth noting that for guests staying with children, the rooms provide coloring with pencils, a fairy tale book and a special stool for washing.





In their reviews, guests mostly spoke positively about the rooms and infrastructure of this hotel. However, the proximity of the Moscow concrete ring is annoying for some, as the noise from the cars is well audible.

One of the Foresta hotels

Those who are looking for country hotels in the suburbs of Moscow with an all-inclusive system can go to Forest Festival Park, located in the Chekhov district. It is only 30 km from the capital of Russia, while the place is considered very clean and environmentally friendly. You can come here to relax with friends or family in a beautiful setting or to hold some kind of business or holiday event. A variety of restaurants and bars, two swimming pools, a magnificent spa complex, paintball club, shooting range and gyms - all this can make the relaxation of each guest unforgettable and varied. A professional team of local animators every day will entertain everyone from young to old. For accommodation, guests are offered comfortable rooms, separate summer residences and two luxury villas for the most sophisticated vacation. Prices for rooms with 4 meals included are much more affordable here than in the previous three options.





Vacationers like this place primarily because of the price. However, they are depressed by the very small size of standard rooms, the remoteness of many buildings from the parking lot and the location of the restaurant in a separate building, which is not very convenient, especially in cool weather and with children.

Mistral (hotel and spa)

Want to visit a 5 star hotel? The Moscow region can offer such an option, only the all-inclusive system here will be incomplete. One of these hotels can be found on the banks of the Istra Reservoir. It is called "Mistral." Hotel and SPA in one bottle with its atmosphere and design will make guests forget that they are not far from Moscow, and reminds of France. All rooms have panoramic windows, and the apartments even have their own kitchens, which is very convenient for families. The hotel has a variety of bars and restaurants, a spa center in which guests are offered a wide range of services, a large pool, a gym and a children's room. The price of any room includes breakfast, access to the pool and gym.





"Mistral" is praised for its territory and gorgeous views of the reservoir. However, some complain about rude staff, already outdated furniture, and even broken equipment. And this is with a very impressive price tag for accommodation.

Park Hotel Imperial

Imperial Park Hotel & SPA is considered one of the best hotels in the suburbs of Moscow. And although it does not work with a full-fledged all-inclusive system, it has everything for a complete family vacation and business meetings. Guests will find a wide variety of comfortable rooms of various categories, the price of which includes the use of a sauna, hammam, gym, pool, bowling, breakfast and lunch. You can taste delicious dishes for dinner in one of the hotel restaurants or paint your evening with bright colors in a special karaoke club. For lovers of outdoor activities, the hotel offers billiards, quad biking, fishing, paintball and shooting at the shooting range. Of course, do not forget that true pleasure awaits guests in a unique spa complex. And for young visitors the hotel offers a wonderful playground and a whole games room.





Visitors to the Imperial Park Hotel & SPA Hotel note the good range of services that are available here. However, many were unsatisfied with the food and service at local restaurants.
