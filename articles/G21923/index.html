Ski Trip: Russia

On the territory of Russia there are a large number of nature reserves, unexplored corners and diverse landscapes. Here you can see fields and deserts, dense forests and steppes, mountain peaks and lowlands. All this diversity is more or less mastered by man. And it is not for nothing that our country is considered one of the most interesting around the world. On its territory lies an unimaginable number of hidden places and corners with unspoilt nature.



Alpine skiing in Russia

The development of skiing in Russia began much later, in comparison with other countries. Despite the large amount of snow and the harsh climate with long winters, development was inhibited by the lack of infrastructure. Among the large number of places for skiing, however, places for recreation have begun to appear, which at the moment attract not only residents of the country, but also foreign tourists.



The ski resorts of Russia, the list of which is quite large, are famous not so much for their well-groomed and safe, as for their stunning views and nature, from which it is impossible to take your eyes off. That is why extreme lovers from around the world are eager to get here. Unknown places attract them. And some wildness and primitiveness makes tours to the ski resorts of Russia so popular.

Major resorts

So, in which mountains is the most famous ski resort? Russia is known throughout the world for the Caucasus Mountains, which are a unique combination of natural wealth and beauty. It is in the midst of all this splendor that Elbrus is located - the highest peak in Europe. On the slopes of this giant, more than one ski slope was created, here athletes have the opportunity to hone their skills and observe the surrounding landscapes. The level and equipment of the tracks in this resort are quite high. People with experience ride here, but there are also small tracks for beginners. In the Azau Valley there is a whole series of hotels and cafes, and the slopes themselves are equipped with modern cable cars.

The Caucasus Mountains have sheltered another ski resort. Russia is also famous for it in the circles of lovers and professionals of winter sports. Dombay Valley is a unique place where alpine meadows are adjacent to mountain lakes and mighty pine and fir. Here, at an altitude of 2500 meters, there is all the infrastructure that is necessary for travelers - hotels, cafes, restaurants, equipment rental and comfortable cable cars. The maximum lift here is 3800 meters. The level of difficulty of the tracks varies between medium and high.



In the north of the country, residents also have the opportunity to visit a ski resort. Russia in those places stretches across the northern hills, among which are the Khibiny Mountains. This place is considered the most famous and popular in the region. Khibiny - ski resort (Russia), where you can meet athletes from around the world. The slopes here are gentle and long, which allows people with any level of skiing to feel comfortable.
