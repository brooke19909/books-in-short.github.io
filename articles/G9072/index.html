Charger for iPad, modifications

The charger for the iPad is a very important accessory, without which it is impossible to do for owners of modern iPad2 tablets, since your favorite device will not work without charging.



Types and Modifications

Charging adapters come in many forms. Please note that in order to charge the tablet from Apple, you need a charger for the iPad, which has a current of more than two amperes. It follows that the charging for the iPhone and iPad is different in that on smartphones the current is less than one ampere.

There are various modifications of charging. It is very convenient to use an iPad charger with several USB ports that allow you to charge multiple gadgets at the same time (iPad and iPhone). Devices with a synchronization cable are popular, which saves on consumables. The quality of the charger depends on how long the tablet‚Äôs battery lasts. Indeed, cheap non-original charging is unreliable and will function poorly, since the characteristics of such devices do not always meet the established requirements, are short-lived and can damage the battery.





Basic tips for charging your device

The fastest way to charge your iPad is to use a USB power adapter whose power is 10 watts. It is usually attached to the device. You can charge the iPad (albeit slower) from the computer via high-power USB ports or use the iPhone power adapter. If you connect it to a computer using the standard USB port, it will be able to charge in sleep mode.

When charging the tablet via the USB port, first make sure that the computer is on, because when connected to the turned off PC (in sleep or standby mode), the tablet continues to discharge. When testing prototypes, it was determined that the battery after 1000 charge cycles retained up to 80% of the original capacity. This is a great result. The modern market offers a large number of power sources (external batteries) and all kinds of charges.
