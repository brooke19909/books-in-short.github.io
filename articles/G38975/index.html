Beautiful and tasty bouquets of sweets with their own hands. For beginners - step by step instructions

Sweets are always a good and tasty gift. But presenting a box of chocolates in a bag to a person is so ordinary and banal. Such yummy can be presented in an original and bright way. How? Make candy in an elegant bouquet. It looks amazing! Such a gift 2 in 1: a bouquet and sweets. Moreover, natural flowers can also be included in such a composition. How to learn how to make bouquets of sweets with your own hands? For beginners in this matter, we present the following master class.



Sweet daisies

Now we will turn the candy into a flower with petals. Do you want the first time you get chamomiles, and in general bouquets of sweets? Step-by-step instructions to help you.

To make a sweet composition you will need:

  • cone-shaped sweets;
  • white and green corrugated paper;
  • wrapping;
  • twigs;
  • threads
  • narrow tape;
  • wicker basket;
  • scissors.

So, we begin to make bouquets of sweets with our own hands. For beginners, I would like to note such a detail: if the composition is made of chocolate or jelly sweets, soak them for a while in the refrigerator before work. Then the sweet mass will cool, which will slow down the process of its melting. To correctly make a bouquet of sweets, step by step follow all the steps of this master class.

  1. From corrugated paper, cut out square blanks measuring 5x5 cm. For each sweet flower, you need 5 of these elements. Squeeze each square along the bottom edge. Tighten the top of the part using a scissor motion. So the first chamomile is ready. Perform all the petals in this way.
  2. Attach a twig stalk to each candy. To do this, attach it to the edge of the wrapper (in the place where it is twisted). It turns the stem with the middle of the flower.
  3. On each blank in the same place where the branch is attached, thread the petals with a thread (5-6 pieces).
  4. Cover the wrapping paper in an empty basket, beautifully wrap the edges out. Pour any other sweets in the middle - they will help keep the sweet flowers in an upright position.
  5. Insert the daisies inside the basket. Try to ensure that the entire container is filled with homemade flowers, then they will support each other and will not fall out. You can strengthen the composition with live or artificial plants.
  6. Decorate the composition with paper butterflies, ribbons.

From this description, wonderful bouquets of sweets are made with your own hands.



For beginners, a few more tips

If you make more petals and place them on the candy in a checkerboard pattern (some above, others below), you get a flower that looks like a rose.

To make a sweet tulip, you need to cut an 8x8 cm blank from corrugated paper, put candy in it and twist all four edges at the top of the cone at once.

The paper from which the petals are made can be painted in different shades. The result is stains on the petals, which contributes to the creation of very beautiful candy flowers.

Now you know all the secrets for making sweet compositions. You will definitely be able to make bouquets of sweets with your own hands. For beginners, our master class will serve as a good start in mastering this type of needlework. Good luck in creating delicious gifts!
