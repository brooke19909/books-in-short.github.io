Guest house "Lilia", Lazarevskoye: overview, description and reviews

Holidays in the resort village of Lazarevskoye, which is located in the Krasnodar Territory on the Black Sea coast, is very popular with our compatriots. Here you can find everything for a wonderful pastime, and at affordable prices for everyone.

Many tourists who come here prefer small guest houses in Lazarevsky to luxury hotels. "Lily" is one of such small hotels that welcome every traveler. Here, any traveler can get a wonderful and very inexpensive vacation.

Cozy hotel in Lazarevsky

A nice building made of light brick with a blue roof is occupied by the guest house "Lilia" (Lazarevskoye). On each of the four floors of the hotel there are rooms for tourists.



This place to stay is quite small. On its territory there is no swimming pool with sun loungers and sunshades, a separate playground and sports grounds for active leisure. Due to this, the cost of living in a hotel is very affordable. Moreover, the territory has its own cafe, where guests can eat delicious food at any time for a fee. There is also a small parking lot for personal vehicles, which will certainly please those tourists who prefer to travel by car.

Thanks to this, the guest house "Lilia" (Lazarevskoye) is perfect for those vacationers who do not plan to spend much time on the territory of their hotel, but want to be more at sea or explore local attractions.

Small number of rooms

Guest house "Lilia" (Lazarevskoye) offers its guests relaxation in comfortable rooms of various sizes, which are perfect for both a couple of vacationers and large families.

Among the 40 rooms of the hotel there are standard rooms designed for two, three and four guests. Each room has a private bathroom with shower, air conditioning, refrigerator, TV, bedside tables, table with chairs, wardrobe, electric kettle and balcony. The rooms have the following beds:

- In double guests, either one double bed or two single beds;

- in triple - single and double beds;

- in quadruples - two single and one double bed.





Bed linen is changed in the guest house 7 days after arrival, cleaning - on request. Any guest can use an ironing board and an iron for free, and even a washing machine for a fee.
