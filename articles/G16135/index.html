Hotel Globus 3 * (Rimini, Italy): Travel Reviews

When choosing a hotel for relaxation, it is worth considering many different factors. After all, even a minor flaw can seriously ruin your vacation experience. Traveling to Italy is a dream of many people. There is everything that a picky tourist may need: a warm sea, well-groomed beaches, excellent cuisine and a huge number of attractions. If you decide to choose this country for vacation and are looking for an inexpensive and comfortable tourist complex, then you should pay attention to the Globus 3 * hotel (Rimini).

What can Italy attract tourists with?

Italy is one of the most popular tourist countries. Travelers come here from all over the world and, of course, our compatriots were no exception. This state, which occupies the territory of an entire peninsula, can offer relaxation for any tourist. It offers a good beach holiday, there are a large number of historical and cultural monuments, there are restaurants where you can taste delicious Italian cuisine. There are many shops of well-known brands, where shopping lovers can inexpensively purchase clothes and other goods. And Italy is the most romantic country where many couples prefer to spend their honeymoon.

Hotel location and nearby attractions

Hotel Globus 3 * (Rimini) is located in the largest resort of the Adriatic coast of Italy. Rimini is also considered a shopping center. Not only tourists come here for shopping here, but also businessmen, including Russian ones. In addition, there are many nightclubs and bars that will appeal to youth companies. Fans of sightseeing vacations will be able to appreciate the ancient and medieval monuments located here everywhere. Settling in Rimini, it is worth visiting the following attractions:

  • Miniature Italy Park, located 5 km from Hotel Globus 3 * (Rimini);
  • Tiberius bridge, built in the 1st century BC. e .;
  • the central square of the city is Piazza Cavour;
  • amusement park "Fiabilandia";
  • Museum of Federico Fellini - the most famous director of Italy;
  • Sismondo Castle is an architectural monument of the Renaissance.




The complex itself is located 13 kilometers from the central part of the city. But it is only 50 meters from the beach of La Marina. There are also many restaurants, shops, playgrounds for children and free courts nearby. You can get to the hotel from the Federico Fellini Airport, which is just 16 kilometers from the Globe.

Basic information you need to know about the hotel

Hotel Globus 3 * (Rimini) was built in 1981, and its last redecoration was completed in 2011. The complex has a small four-story building, which offers tourists about 70 rooms. There are also apartments designed specifically for disabled tourists. Check-in is possible from 11:00 until midnight. Vacationers should leave between 8:00 and 10:00. The hotel staff understands the Russian language, and some of them are free to speak it.





The complex allows children of any age. In addition, the cost of paying for the accommodation of small guests is half that of adults. The hotel also allows tourists to come with animals, but you must notify the property in advance when booking and arranging a ticket. It is believed that the complex is designed for a calm and measured rest, so it is suitable for families with children, elderly couples, businessmen and business people.

Number of rooms

The hotel has 71 rooms, 2 of which are suitable for disabled guests. Some apartments also have small balconies. The rooms have a standard set of furniture, including a bed, a bedside table, a small table and an ottoman. The rooms are decorated in a classic minimalist style. The walls are decorated with modern lamps, and flowers are also used as decor.





Four people is the maximum number of people who can stay in one large room of the Globus 3 * hotel (Rimini). There is also a small number of single apartments. The main part is made up of double rooms, into which an extra bed for a child can be delivered upon request. Some rooms have partial sea views. The rooms are tidied daily, linen and towels are changed.

Room features

For a comfortable stay at the Hotel Globus 3 * (Italy, Rimini), the rooms are equipped with many different amenities. The hotel administration seeks to create an atmosphere of comfort in the rooms so that each guest feels at home here. Any traveler staying at this hotel can take advantage of the following amenities:

  • Flat-screen TV, located on the wall. It does not take up much space in the room. It is connected to cable and satellite TV.
  • A personal air conditioner that maintains cool temperatures on hot summer days. The hotel can provide an additional fan upon request. On cold days, heating can be turned on.
  • Empty minibar. Its contents are not included in the price and are paid by guests separately.
  • Safe box for storing valuables, documents and money.
  • Telephone for communication with hotel staff, as well as for making paid local and international calls.
  • An electric kettle with which guests can boil water right in their room.
  • Bathroom with shower and bidet. Free toiletries, towels and a hairdryer are provided.

What services can Globus offer?

Hotel Hotel Globus 3 * (Rimini) cares not only about the comfortable living of its guests, but also about providing them with all the necessary services. Thanks to the developed infrastructure network, the guests of the complex receive a quality vacation at affordable prices. The main amenities that the hotel offers are:

  • Free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel. Tourists can ask for a Wi-Fi password at the reception.
  • Free parking for cars. Cars here are under a canopy and are carefully guarded by hotel staff.
  • For guests of the hotel bicycles for free are provided, with which you can independently travel around the city.
  • There is a 24-hour reception where the administrator of the Globus 3 * hotel (Rimini) will help guests solve problems as soon as possible.
  • Luggage storage and concierge services are available at an extra charge.
  • Currency exchange.
  • Modern elevator.
  • You can use the fax services at the front desk for a small fee or photocopy the necessary documents.




Entertainment for guests

High-quality and varied entertainment is another important component of a good holiday. Therefore, Hotel Globus 3 * (Italy, Rimini) is trying to provide vacationers with a range of possible entertainment. Guests can find activities to ensure a quiet and relaxing holiday, and active entertainment. We list the most popular leisure activities:

  • An outdoor pool located 200 meters from the hotel also has a public beach nearby. It offers free umbrellas and sunbeds.
  • A spa in which tourists can visit the solarium, massage services, Turkish bath and sauna. Here you can relax while immersed in the hot tub.
  • On-site animation staff offers an entertainment program for adults and children.
  • Horse riding lessons can be booked for a fee.
  • Playroom.
  • Organization of excursions to the sights of Rimini.




More about food: restaurants, bars

Choosing a place to stay, tourists pay special attention to the food that the hotel offers. After all, a trip to another country is a great opportunity to treat yourself to delicious dishes and drinks. Hotel Globus 3 * (Rimini), whose rating is quite high, offers tourists several food options:

  • half board;
  • full board ;
  • "all inclusive".




The main restaurant, which operates on site, provides tourists with a wide selection of Italian and European dishes. A daily buffet is served for travelers here. Upon request, the chef can prepare a special diet menu. If you want to relax in a quiet and relaxing environment, you can visit the lounge bar, which offers tourists fine Italian wines, a variety of cocktails (including alcoholic ones), beer, tea, coffee, as well as pastries, desserts and light snacks.

Should I come here with children?

The hotel offers many amenities for parents with children, although it is difficult to call it a family complex. Nevertheless, there are good discounts for children. For example, the Hotel Globus 3 * (Rimini) can check in a child under the age of 5 for free if he sleeps on existing beds. A cot can be provided in the room upon request. Then the child can be issued a 40% discount on accommodation. For babies up to two years old a cradle is provided.

There are few entertainments for children at the hotel. There is an outdoor playground, a mini club. For an extra fee, a children's menu can be prepared at the restaurant. Parents can also hire a paid nanny, who will sit with their child while they are away.

Advantages of the "Globe" according to tourists

None of the descriptions can be compared in accuracy with the opinion of tourists who have previously rested in a hotel. Hotel Globus 3 * (Rimini) reviews have mostly positive. Guests of the complex note its following advantages:

  • Beautiful sea view from the room balcony.
  • Very friendly and unobtrusive staff who speaks good Russian. The hotel administrator is always trying to quickly solve the problem and is happy to answer any questions.
  • The rooms are newly renovated and have good furniture. The rooms are cleaned daily and efficiently, and towels are regularly updated.
  • Within walking distance is the city center. Nearby are many restaurants, cafes and shopping centers.
  • Quiet and peaceful environment. It does not include loud music in the evenings and nights. Sometimes it is a bit noisy from the street.
  • Breakfast and lunch are priced. There is not much variety, but all the dishes are fresh and cooked deliciously. The coffee machine makes very high-quality coffee.
  • The beach is very close. Go to it just 2 minutes.
  • Free Wi-Fi works equally well throughout the complex.




Disadvantages of the "Globe" according to tourists

But Globus 3 * (Rimini) has not only good opinions. Reviews about him are also negative. Tourists note the following disadvantages of the hotel:

  • There is not enough furniture on the balcony. Usually there is one chair there, which is not very convenient if a married couple or a family with children has settled in the room.
  • Air conditioning does not always work.
  • The restaurant primarily serves tourists from Italy. Russian travelers are forced to wait half an hour before they bring food.
  • Lunches and dinners are rather meager and monotonous.
  • A construction site is nearby, which makes a lot of noise in the morning and afternoon.

Should I choose this hotel for your vacation?

Tourist complex Globus Bellariva 3 * Standard (Rimini) is a good budget hotel, which is suitable for a quiet holiday with your family on the Adriatic coast. The quality of the services provided is consistent with the price set by the hotel owners. The beach is very close, and the building has everything you need for a normal vacation.

Of course, if you want to get high-quality service, then it is better to pay attention to more expensive hotels that provide a wider selection of services and entertainment. But for a budget holiday, the Globe will be a great option. It is also very convenient to stay for those tourists who want to travel around the country, and return to the hotel only for the night.
