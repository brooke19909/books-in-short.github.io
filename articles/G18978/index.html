Hotel Agonda Holiday Home 2 *, South Goa: overview, features and reviews of tourists

Holidays in Goa (India) is a cherished dream of many, because local landscapes are permeated with a sense of happiness and paradise life. Snow-white fine sand, bent palms and sea blue are all that we are used to seeing on advertising posters. For travelers who come here in search of a good holiday, a small cozy hotel Agonda Holiday Home 2 * opens its doors. The hotel willingly accommodates single travelers and small groups of vacationers in its rooms. What conditions does the hotel offer and is it worth choosing it as a decent place to stay? Find answers to these questions before the trip.

Hotel Features

Agonda Holiday Home in Agonda is a small hotel, with only 23 rooms. The hotel consists of a central 3-storey building and small cozy houses, made in an original style. There is a peculiarity in local hotels: many hotels in Goa are built on this principle.

This hotel is called one of the oldest in the district - the year of its foundation is considered 1979, however, in 2000, reconstruction was carried out. To date, the hotel boasts updated facilities and fresh renovations.

The hotel area is divided into 2 parts expensive. The yard is quite spacious, with lots of greenery and places to relax.

In India, Agonda Holiday Home is characterized as a two-star hotel offering everything you need for a memorable beach holiday. However, the cost of accommodation here can significantly save on many services that are unnecessary for tourists. These features should definitely be considered when booking rooms.

Hotel Location

This hotel is located next to the small village of Agonda, which is located 9 km north of the popular resort of Palolem.

The city of Panaji (the state capital) is 70 km away.

Dabolim International Airport is 58 km from the hotel, and tourists who want to relax in this part of the country arrive there.

In order to get to the hotel, you can catch a bus at the airport - it runs regularly between small villages and resort areas. Those who want to get as comfortable as possible can take a taxi.

Agonda Holiday Home 2 * hotel is located on the second beach line - about 300 meters from the sea. This is not very convenient, but this figure significantly reduces the cost of hotel accommodation.

This studio has a private entrance . Suitable for two people. The room has 1 double bed, seating area, balcony. The flooring is tiled. The combined bathroom has a toilet, mirror, washbasin, shower or bathtub. There is a kitchenette with kitchenware and a kettle.

Double room standard without air conditioning. The entrance to the room is separate, there is a seating area, 1 double bed, equipped balcony. The room has a combined bathroom with toilet, washbasin, shower, mirror. The walls and floor of the bathroom are tiled; the room has a tiled floor.

Room 2-bed standard with air conditioning. This option mainly differs from the previous offer by the presence of air conditioning. In all other respects, the living conditions remain the same. The room is zoned into a sleeping and living area, has a double bed, wardrobe, seating area, table. The flooring is tiled.

Wooden hut de luxe. This room is a detached small house. It is made in the original style, which makes it cozy and attractive. The walls and ceiling are finished with wood, on the floor - tiles. The hut has a bathroom, double bed, wardrobe, desk, air conditioning, ceiling fan, mosquito net on the window.
Beach huts 2-seater. Such accommodation will be appreciated by those tourists who are looking for in India (Agonda), Agonda Holiday Home 2 mainly a beach holiday. The houses are very close to the water, so the road to the sea will take no more than 5 minutes. It offers guests: a separate entrance to the house, a double bed, a living area, a spacious bathroom.
Villa The most comfortable accommodation for 2 people. Spacious room has a bathroom with shower, double bed, sofa, table, wardrobe, refrigerator, fan.

Nutrition

The hotel offers guests accommodation at the breakfast base. A restaurant and snack bar are located on site. The dishes offered by the chef are international. There are daily dishes for every taste. These are meat products, salads from fresh vegetables, and yogurt.

The snack bar serves a hearty meal throughout the day for a fee. Here, mainly snacks, fast food and drinks.

Cafes and restaurants nearby can help solve the food problem. The cuisine here is very diverse, so there are catering establishments for both meat-eaters and vegetarians. If the task is to eat tasty and inexpensive, it is better to pay attention to small cafes.

There are no large restaurants and nightclubs in Agonda, but lovers of noisy parties can go to the resort of Palolem at any time. Here every evening dozens of entertainment venues open their doors.

What to see and what to do

Agonda Holiday Home 2 is located in the quiet small village of Agonda. Here, the only entertainment is the beach. Fine creaking sand is pleasant to the touch, and coastal waters attract with its cleanliness. You can ride a camel or horse right on the beach for a fee.

Those who are bored of lying under an umbrella can go on an excursion to the ancient fort of Cabo da Rama. This building is located 14 km north-west of Agonda. It gained its celebrity because of the hero of the exile, who lived here with his wife for more than 12 years.

In Agonda there are shops of artisans selling the result of their labor. From here you can take away numerous colorful souvenirs, among which there will be quite popular things and funny trinkets.

Fans of outdoor activities go to the popular and quite noisy resort of Palolem, just a dozen kilometers from Agonda Holiday Home 2, India. Here you can do:

  • diving and snorkeling;
  • canyoning;
  • tracking;
  • go for a boat ride;
  • swim with dolphins (it is better to do it at sunset or at sunrise).

Agonda Holiday Home 2 Hotel Reviews in South Goa

Before booking a room in one or another hotel, travelers try to study the reviews of those who have already managed to relax here. This is a rational approach to business, especially when it comes to reviews of the Agonda Holiday Home 2 * hotel or any other 2-star hotel.

Those who are going to vacation in Goa for the first time in India should know: there are no European luxury hotels of 4 or 5 stars. Most hotels are of 2 * rank and offer guests only the necessary services for a modest fee. The insufficiently high level of service is offset by a luxurious beach holiday.


Service. Despite the limited list of services, the vast majority of vacationers were satisfied with the service. The rooms are clean, clean linen, helpful staff.

The atmosphere of the hotel. The furniture in the rooms (judging by the reviews) is not of the highest quality - among the shortcomings were the lack of a door on the cabinets, a shower pouring in half the rooms, insufficient pressure of water at one time or another.

Nutrition. Despite the low cost of food, tourists are satisfied with the cuisine. According to reviews of Agonda Holiday Home 2, there are French, Indian and other restaurants in the area.

The beach . The beach is just what hundreds of tourists come here every year. According to reviews, a beach holiday is special. The water is clean, the beach is sandy. The only thing worth paying attention to is the strong current, so you should not sail long distances from the coast.

Who can I recommend a hotel in India Agonda Holiday Home 2 in South Goa? First of all, it is suitable for those who appreciate a quiet secluded vacation in picturesque places. Fans of beach vacations will not remain indifferent - the sea and sand here are first-class. Families with children can also safely go to Goa, since all the conditions for a comfortable stay with children are created here. The affordable cost of trips allows you to relax in the popular resort for absolutely everyone.
