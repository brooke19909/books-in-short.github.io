Fans of a relaxing holiday should pay attention to a small country located on the Adriatic coast, called Montenegro. Petrovac - reviews of tourists about holidays in this resort are imbued with only positive emotions. A small cozy town will appeal to lovers of economical holidays.



Description

An economical, high-quality and measured rest alone with nature is what Montenegro prepares for its guests. Petrovac, travelers reviews which recommend this place exclusively for family tourism, is located on the shore of a picturesque bay surrounded by mountain peaks, olive groves and pine forests. The city was founded on the site of the settlement of Lastva in the late 18th - early 19th century. It is located 17 kilometers from Budva and 36 kilometers from the airport near Tivat.

Beach

The city has two magnificent beaches. They are covered with red small pebbles. The beach of Petrovac is very popular among vacationers, on which a small port is located and a beautiful promenade is built. Its length is more than 800 meters. On the promenade, cafes, shops and restaurants are open daily. The beach "Lucice" has a gentle descent into the water. Next to it on a mountainside, surrounded by a pine forest, there is a fish restaurant. Water slides are built on the beach , catamarans, boats, motorcycles are offered.
