Pension "Solnechny", Solnechnogorsk. Reviews, prices, photos

Among tourists, Solnechnogorsk is famous for its many leisure opportunities, including well-equipped pensions and resorts. The best embodiment is the recreation center "Sunny". Solnechnogorsk, therefore, is a haven for travelers.

general information

Not far from the Zhivaginsky pond, on the territory, whose area is 15 hectares, there is a unique four-star hotel "Solnechny". The main difference of the boarding house is that in the immediate vicinity of the protected area there is a forest park zone that grows into a massive coniferous forest.





Pension "Solnechny" (Solnechnogorsk) is presented in the form of a chalet, decorated in a European style. The territory of the sanatorium is under reliable protection, parking for cars of tourists is provided. For a comfortable stay, visitors are provided with a unique landscape area, teeming with sweeping trees, flowers and shrubs.

Hotel Rooms

Rest in Solnechnogorsk, in the boarding house "Solnechny" leaves an indelible impression on tourists. This is due to the fact that it is here that a luxury hotel with comfortable rooms is provided. Each visitor can stay in one of 197 rooms. The price list includes both single and double, and triple rooms.

Each room has a shared bathroom, there is equipment for a comfortable stay: satellite TV, TV, telephone, as well as the necessary furniture (wardrobe, bed, nightstands, mirror). In addition, each room has a minibar in which you can find both alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks.





The minimum cost of a standard room reaches 4600 rubles per day per person. A child's place for a child aged 2 to 12 years will cost 2500 rubles. A nice bonus is the free placement of kids up to 2 years.

Food in the sanatorium

Pension "Solnechny" (Solnechnogorsk) is a unique institution in the Moscow region, where all-inclusive service is offered to all customers. Three meals a day in the sanatorium include breakfast, lunch and dinner. The cost of food is included in the room rate.

The restaurant complex offers buffet meals. The restaurant is open for customers from 8 a.m. to 11 p.m. The menu abounds with dishes of various cuisines, including vegetarian and dietary foods. Each client will be able to choose a dish according to his taste.





In addition, the Solnechny boarding house (Solnechnogorsk) offers its customers to visit other catering establishments, including a lobby bar, grill bar, tea house or sushi bar. And the room-service allows even in the dead of night to order tourists food in the room.

Entertainment in a boarding house

In the city of Solnechnogorsk (Moscow region), tourists are attracted by the colossal opportunities to relax and have fun. A billiard club is offered for guests of the sanatorium, where tables are set for both Russian billiards and the "American".

The luxurious bowling hall leaves no one indifferent. Professional equipment installed in the room will make the pastime not only interesting, but also comfortable. There are 4 game paths and a relaxation area for guests.





There are enormous opportunities for athletes. The pension has a football field, a basketball and volleyball courts, a tennis court, as well as a ping-pong room. The main advantage lies in the fact that most of these opportunities become available to customers with the action of the all-inclusive system.





Bicycle, ski and skate rental

For those who are used to spending time as actively as possible, the guesthouse, located in the city of Solnechnogorsk (Moscow Region), offers rental bicycles, skis and skates. The choice of guests includes both adult models and children. The guesthouse has cycling trails in the most picturesque places. In winter, ski trips are organized. A box with smooth ice is provided for ice skating.

Services for children

Sanatorium "Solnechny" (Solnechnogorsk) offers numerous services for the youngest visitors. In addition to the entertainment and recreational opportunities for children, play areas are organized. In dedicated rooms there are slot machines where children can feel like true racers or supermen. Simulators do not harm health, thereby making the rest not only interesting, but also safe.





For active recreation of children in the park there is a zone for an entertainment complex. Numerous attractions, slides, stairs and houses, a labyrinth filled with many secrets and wonders will make the child's vacation unforgettable and filled with a lot of emotions. The game takes place under the supervision of animators, which allows parents to relax without worrying about the safety of their child.

The animation, which is included in the price at the Park-Hotel Solnechny, does not leave anyone indifferent. The hosts conduct entertaining shows in costume dresses, professional artists and dancers are involved, as well as guests of the recreation center. Cheerful contests, numerous prizes and a lot of fun are what are guaranteed to every guest.

Pension "Sunny" is a unique hotel complex of tourist infrastructure, which will make the rest as comfortable and inexpensive as possible.
