Deposits for retirees: in which banks profitable interest?

Retired people are a special category of investors. Most of them have home savings. Understanding this, banks offer deposits for senior citizens with favorable interest. Bankers enable people to understand that it is better to store funds not at home under the pillow, but in a bank account.

Why is it better to keep money in a bank?

Pensioners are quite trusting people who can often let strangers into their apartment. Those who want to rob a pensioner know where the money is most often with him, and they introduce themselves as employees of a pension fund or charity. The visual purpose of their visit is to provide some assistance to a pensioner, but in reality, after a visit, the latter lose money. Not every pensioner claims such cases to the police, and finding such ‚Äúbenefactors" is then quite difficult, because they change clothes and appearance (hair color, wear dark glasses, etc.). Keeping money in a bank is much more reliable than at home, in addition, the funds can be in safe hands and bring customer income. That is why many financial institutions offer deposits for senior citizens with favorable interest rates.
