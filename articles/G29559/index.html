Hotel Sao Domingos 2 * (India, Goa): photos and reviews of tourists

Goa (India) is a favorite vacation spot of many modern tourists. This is a great resort for lovers of beach holidays, sunny weather reigns here almost all year round. Many tourists note that it was here that they managed to find peace of mind, to distract from life problems and troubles during the holidays.





About the resort

Goa attracts tourists from various corners of the globe with its snow-white beaches and exotic. This is a paradise, a pearl of the west coast of India, in which the exotic and traditions of Western Europe intertwine. The natural diversity, even splendor, strikes the imagination of the most sophisticated tourists. Here you can find impenetrable jungle, mango thickets, huge plantations of spices and spices, enchanting lakes and waterfalls. This is truly a paradise that you do not want to leave. That is why many of those tourists who visit Goa at least once come back here again and again.

About hotels

In Goa, you simply will not find multi-story hotels with a huge surrounding area. All hotels here are not higher than palm trees (9 meters) and not closer than 300 m to the sea. The reason for this is quite strong ebbs and flows, which can cause a lot of discomfort to vacationers. Most often these are small private hotels, nice and cozy houses with an average of 30-40 rooms. There are not so many vacationers in them, which allows you to enjoy peace and tranquility.

All tourists should be careful about the stardom of the chosen hotel - it is far from always true. Yes, of course, there are fashionable hotels here, where the staff is ready to fulfill any client‚Äôs wishes, but there are very, very few of them. Most often, after checking in, tourists encounter dirt in the rooms, old dry furniture, the appalling state of plumbing, and find exotic lizards, spiders and even snakes in their rooms.





That is why before buying a ticket to the agency, find out as much information about the potential hotel option as possible, get acquainted with the reviews on various tourist portals. Only in this case you can spend your vacation in the most comfortable conditions. We want to bring to the attention of all tourists Sao Domingos Hotel Goa 2 *. Reviews of real vacationers will help to make an objective assessment of it and help potential tourists in their choice.

Sao Domingos 2 *: general description

So, Sao domingos 2, according to the description, is a small but quite comfortable hotel near Cavelossim beach. This is a nice place, nestled among majestic palm trees, setting up for the most comfortable, carefree vacation alone with nature. Near the hotel there are quite a lot of small shops and restaurants where you can taste national cuisine. The hotel positions itself as a very budget option, but guarantees hospitality and hospitality. So how is it really?

How to get there

Sao Domingos 2 * is located only 40 km from the airport. Most often, tourists are provided with a transfer to the hotel. If you went on an independent trip, use the services of a taxi. Travel time is 30-40 minutes. Upon arrival, a friendly host meets guests. He does not speak Russian at all, which does not prevent him from taking into account the wishes of vacationers. Settlement takes place within 10-15 minutes: you lay out things, put yourself in order and can go to the beach.

The hospitality and hospitality of the owners of the hotel are noted by all tourists. Indeed, in each review, as far as the lines are devoted to the owners, their desire to fully satisfy all the wishes of vacationers.





Rooms

Of course, going to Goa, it is unlikely that you will spend all your free time in the hotel - you need a room exclusively for sleep and relaxation. Nevertheless, the question of the state of plumbing, living conditions in the rooms excites all tourists, without exception. Goa is an exotic resort, so there is a serious cause for concern.

So, if we look at the Sao Domingos 2 * hotel photos, we will see that they have a nice three-story building that looks like a small country house. Vacationers are invited to stay in one of the 30 small rooms. The rooms have comfortable beds, a wardrobe for storing clothes, a bedside table, satellite TV and, perhaps, the most necessary in the conditions of the sweltering heat - air conditioning. Each room has its own balcony, where you can meet the sunrises and see off the sunsets. On the balcony there are two wicker chairs and a small table.

For a fee, tourists can purchase a password for wireless internet and use a minibar. By the way, about the wireless Internet: almost all the reviews of tourists speak of disgusting signal quality. This is understandable and justified by the location of the hotel. In general, for what to write to relatives and friends through social networks, to share your emotions and impressions from the rest, speed is quite enough.





Let's discuss the condition of the equipment of the Sao Domingos 2 * hotel. Reviews of vacationers most often say that the state of technology is poor. The TV may not work during the whole rest, there may be problems with the climatic equipment, in some rooms the plumbing is out of order. In fact, for Goa hotels - this is the norm. If you, as a tourist, are very annoying, it is better to choose a different hotel or even pay attention to another resort. In fact, for all issues, you can contact the owners of the hotel, they will try to fix the breakdown as quickly as possible.

Hotel Sao Domingos 2 * is unlikely to please you with the beauty of the interior and sophistication - everything here is quite durable, but at the same time allows you to enjoy your vacation. Each room has a private bathroom with a set of towels. By the way, towels are changed daily.

Service

The hotel has problems with equipment, electronics, but as for the service, everything is perfect here. Rooms are cleaned at the first request of vacationers, towels are changed every day, bed linen - according to pollution.





The bathroom does not provide toiletries, so shampoo, soap and other products should be taken with you or purchased in one of the local shops, treat yourself to exotic products.

If you want to go on an excursion, visit local attractions, contact the hotel owners - they will give you valuable recommendations, help you choose an agency that organizes an excursion to Goa. Of course, you can purchase excursions from a tour operator, but their cost is much higher than the cost that street vendors offer.

Hotel Territory

The territory is small, but well-groomed: a lot of greenery, tropical plants that create a pleasant shade and coolness. All vacationers in Sao Domingos Hotel Goa 2 * can bathe in the outdoor pool, use the sun loungers.

The water in the pool is clean, tourists confirm that it is cleaned daily. This is a real expanse for children - here they are ready to spend their whole day diving and swimming.





Some tourists note annoying mosquitoes, mosquitoes. Of course, it is difficult to attribute this feature to the shortcomings of the hotel itself. It is better to stock up on fumigators, pills and protective compounds in advance - then you will ensure yourself a comfortable rest and a good healthy sleep without bites of tropical insects.

Entertainment

For an additional fee, you can rent sports equipment, go fishing, visit the spa treatments. A lively street with local shops, bars and restaurants is nearby. Here you will be offered to taste exotic dishes of national cuisine, enjoy live music and just have a great time. Here you can buy souvenirs. Lovers of nightlife will appreciate the local bars and restaurants.

Sports equipment rental is available on the beach. Among vacationers, water skiing is very popular . This is a fairly budgetary, easy to manage entertainment that allows you to fully enjoy the flavor of local recreation. Professional athletes will help you get on the skis, will introduce you to the main subtleties of management. Here you can rent a catamaran, ride a banana and get acquainted with other, no less bright and extreme water types of entertainment.

Beach and sea

Sao Domingos 2 * Cavelossim is located just 300 meters from the sea. Tourists note a fairly comfortable entry into the water and a gorgeous sandy beach, recognized as one of the best on the coast. That is why families with young children will like it here. The Sal River flows nearby, swimming is prohibited. The public beach, unfortunately, Sao Domingos 2 * (India, Cavelossim) does not have its own beach, but in this case it is not critical.





The beach is clean, the sea is clear and incredibly warm. This is a paradise for beach lovers. If you want to achieve complete relaxation and unity with nature, do not be lazy and go a couple of kilometers to the left. You will be taken to a remote beach where there is no one. This is where you can fully enjoy the sound of the surf, the rustle of leaves in the wind, to be alone with yourself. his thoughts, no distractions.

Nutrition

Hotel Sao Domingos 2 * offers tourists breakfast included in the price. Breakfast is standard for exotic countries: toasts with butter and jam, scrambled eggs or scrambled eggs with bacon, ham, eggs, juice, tea and coffee. The hotel staff is attentive to vacationers, trying to clarify and remember the taste preferences of each and delight him with delicious hearty breakfasts.

To summarize

The hotel is ideal for a relaxing family vacation; young people will also like it here. The optimal ratio of price and quality of services provided is a hallmark of Sao Domingos 2 *. Perhaps, in its price category, this is the best hotel on the coast, secured the support of many tourists. Indeed, the reviews about the hotel are mostly positive. Pleasant and polite staff, comfortable clean rooms, proximity to the sea - this will turn your vacation into a real pleasure and enjoyment.
