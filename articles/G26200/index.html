Golden Holiday 3 * (Vietnam / Nha Trang): description, infrastructure, photo of rooms, reviews

Vietnam is a popular destination for tourists from Russia, who especially like to come here in autumn and winter. This country attracts them not only with its exotic culture and warm sea, but also with budget prices. The resorts of Vietnam have opened a lot of inexpensive hotels, which at the same time offer their guests a fairly high level of service. For example, tourists in Nha Trang often stay at Golden Holiday Hotel Nha Trang 3 *. Where is this hotel located? What numbers does he offer? What can you do while relaxing at the hotel? Did other travelers like it here? We will try to answer these questions in detail below.

Hotel Location

The modern Golden Holiday 3 * complex (Vietnam) is located in the heart of the lively Nha Trang resort. It was built right on the main street, not far from the main city beach. Tourists will be able to reach it in 3-5 minutes. Other complexes are located next to the hotel, so this area has a developed infrastructure. Guests in the reviews note that there are many shops, restaurants and bars near the hotel. In the central part of Nha Trang, there are also several shopping and entertainment centers and attractions. A sailing club is 300 meters from the hotel on the coast. Near the residential building you can find a bus stop. A large water park is 700 meters away from the hotel.

The international airport, which receives flights from Russia, is located in the suburbs of Nha Trang. The distance to it is approximately 35 km. Tourists can get from the airport to the hotel by bus or taxi. Their schedule is specially adjusted to the arriving flights. In addition, when ordering a tour, tourists can pay and transfer. In this case, a special bus will pick them up from the airport, which will quickly deliver people immediately to the hotel building.

general information

Tourists are often afraid to come to budget hotels in Vietnam, as some of them are too old. But the Golden Holiday Hotel Nha 3 * hotel is modern. Its opening took place only in 2014. It does not have a huge territory, and the complex itself cannot be called large. For its guests, it offers only 48 rooms, so even in the high season only about 100-150 tourists live in it. But guests from other countries are attracted by the unique appearance of the hotel. It is decorated in a modern style, and its territory is decorated with a large number of trees, flowers and shrubs. In addition, all the infrastructure facilities necessary for tourists are compactly located on it, including a large outdoor pool with a spacious sun terrace.

The hotel staff does not speak Russian, but they are fluent in English. Registration of guests begins at the allotted time - at 14:00. It continues until midnight. When checking in, tourists should leave their passport with the administrator as a deposit, which they must return after the end of the holiday. The hotel is allowed to stay with children of any age, in addition, they offer discounts. You cannot bring pets with you. Guests must leave their rooms on the last day of rest no later than 13:00.

Hotel Rooms

Since Golden Holiday Hotel 3 * is considered a place for a budget holiday, its rooms cannot boast of a large area and exquisite decoration. There are 48 of them in the hotel, most of the apartments are designed for two adult guests and a small child. When checking in, tourists can choose the following room categories:

  1. Standard. One bedroom apartment with bath. Their area can vary from 21 to 23 m 2 . The windows overlook a corridor decorated with living plants. It also offers superior rooms with an open balcony. It can overlook the city or the mountains, and from the upper floors there is a view of the sea. Tourists can choose between double or single beds.
  2. Suite Spacious apartment of 45 m2. Designed to accommodate two adult guests. There is an open balcony.

An extra rollaway bed is available upon request. There are no facilities for disabled guests at the hotel. All living rooms are cleaned by maids every day. Towels and bedding are also subject to change several times a week.

Room Amenities

All living rooms at Golden Holiday Hotel 3 * are decorated in a contemporary style and feature designer furniture. In addition to it, in the rooms tourists can find all the necessary equipment to make living in them as comfortable as possible. Having settled in this hotel, tourists can take advantage of the following amenities:

  • a small plasma TV, to which not only cable TV, but also a DVD player is connected;
  • individual air conditioning with remote control to control the level of cooling;
  • minibar for soft drinks - it is filled for a fee;
  • a safe where you can leave valuables, money and documents;
  • utensils and products necessary for making tea and coffee;
  • bath accessories, toilet paper, towels and bathrobes in the bathroom;
  • hair dryer and clothes dryer;
  • landline phone.

Infrastructure Golden Holiday Hotel 3 * in Nha Trang

The hotel has a very small territory, so it can not boast of developed infrastructure. It houses only a twelve-story residential building, an outdoor pool and a small garden. Inside the complex, tourists can use the paid laundry, where you can hand over dirty clothes. On the ground floor there is a 24-hour reception desk, where guests can exchange currency, buy a ticket for excursions or entertainment, rent a bicycle. Free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel, but its speed is not too high. There is an elevator in the hotel building, which quickly delivers guests to the desired floor.

On the territory there is a small parking lot where tourists can leave their car for free without booking a seat in advance. The hotel is non-smoking, but there are special smoking rooms for guests.

Supply system

The Golden Holiday 3 * hotel does not offer a large selection of concepts for its guests. The cost of accommodation, as a rule, includes only breakfasts, which are covered in the main restaurant of the hotel. It is located on the 12th floor of the building and also has a terrace with panoramic views of the coast and the surrounding area. Buffet breakfast. The menu includes international dishes: sandwiches, omelets, scrambled eggs, hot drinks, fruits, vegetables, sliced ‚Äã‚Äãsausages and cheese.

For a fee, you can visit the restaurant during the day and evening, where all visitors are already served exclusively by the menu. For lunch and dinner, Asian and Vietnamese dishes are served, as well as seafood and fish. Near the pool there is a small bar where guests can enjoy refreshing drinks, cocktails and ice cream.

Beach and Pool

Golden Holiday Hotel 3 * is located on the second line of the coast, so it does not have its own beach. But the city is only 150 meters from the complex. To get to it, tourists will need to cross a busy road. The beach itself is completely sandy. Entrance to it is free, but you will have to pay separately for the rental of sun loungers and sunshades for each visit. But the hotel staff give guests beach towels. The entrance to the sea is gentle, so you can swim safely even with young children.

The hotel also has an outdoor pool filled with fresh water. It is located directly on the roof of the building, so it offers an excellent view of the sea. There is a sunbathing area near the pool, where tourists can use sun loungers for free.

Entertainment at the hotel

Nha Trang is a busy resort, so tourists rarely spend their free time in hotels, preferring to stroll around local attractions, markets and restaurants. Therefore, the Golden Holiday 3 * complex does not provide its guests with a large selection of entertainment. On the ground floor there is a common relaxation room, which is equipped with comfortable sofas, armchairs and large TVs. Also, guests at the reception can buy a ticket for an excursion or rent equipment for snorkeling or sea fishing.

Vacation with children

And although the Golden Holiday Nha Trang 3 * complex is often chosen by tourists with small children for relaxation, there are not a lot of amenities for them. Upon request, a cradle is provided for guests to rent for free. In the restaurant you can take high chairs for feeding. But there are no entertainments for kids in the complex. Therefore, parents will have to plan their leisure time on their own.

Positive feedback about Golden Holiday Hotel 3 *

It is worth noting that this hotel belongs to the budget category, so it can not boast of a first-class level of service. But tourists who have been vacationing here earlier note in the reviews that the cost of living is consistent with the quality of service. The hotel, of course, has disadvantages, but it also has plenty of advantages. They recommend coming here to picky tourists who want a cheap vacation in Nha Trang. At the same time, the main advantage of the complex is called by its guests its advantageous location, since it is located in the center of the European quarter. After all, next to it is not only the beach, but also restaurants, a night market and shops.

What else did tourists like in Golden Holiday 3 *? In their reviews, they indicate the following advantages of this hotel:

  1. A small but well-groomed and always tidy area. The complex itself looks new and modern.
  2. Varied and delicious breakfasts, which serve a lot of fresh seasonal fruits and vegetables.
  3. Some rooms have beautiful views of the promenade and the sea.
  4. Towels and toiletries are always changed and replenished by the hotel staff.

Negative reviews

Like other budget hotels, this complex has many disadvantages. Picky tourists often call them insignificant and note that they liked the complex. At the same time, other guests write that service deficiencies ruined their long-awaited vacation. Therefore, they recommend choosing another hotel for themselves.

In negative reviews about Golden Holiday 3 *, tourists point to the following hotel disadvantages:

  1. Both day and night it is quite noisy, because the building is being built nearby, and in the evenings there are bars and nightclubs. Therefore, you can not sleep with the windows open.
  2. Poor housekeeping. Guests often complain about other people's hair that can be found on the floor.
  3. The restaurant does not have enough tables for all visitors.
  4. The water in the bathroom runs almost always a little warm, it is extremely rare when it is hot.
  5. Unscrupulous hotel employees who are trying to beg for a tip for any service. If they are not given, then they will fulfill the request of tourists poorly.

Of course, this hotel cannot be called an ideal place to relax, as it has quite a few shortcomings. But if you are looking for an inexpensive option for accommodation in Nha Trang near the sea, then the complex will suit you. It can be recommended to any tourists: youth companies and families with small children.
