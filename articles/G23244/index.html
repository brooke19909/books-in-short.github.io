Attractions of South America: Amazon rainforest, Machu Picchu (photo)

An unmistakable choice of vacation spot is South America. The wettest and greenest continent is truly unique. Only here you can see one of the largest lowlands in the world - the Amazon, the deepest river - the Amazon, the highest waterfall - Angel, the longest land mountains - the Andes. Traveling on the continent of many records will allow you to see not only the already famous natural and cultural-historical sights of South America, but also introduce new ones.

The real kingdom of flora and fauna



Very popular are sea cruises in the country. They provide an opportunity to get acquainted in all its diversity with the culture of many countries of the mainland and its diverse landscapes. What is only the amazingly beautiful Amazon rainforest. The jungle that covers the banks of the "Queen of the rivers" stretches from the foot of the Peruvian Andes and never ceases to amaze with its "floating islands", its splendor and inaccessibility in Bolivia, Ecuador and Brazil. There is no such amazing place, the real kingdom of flora and fauna on Earth.

Waterfalls of the continent

Extraordinarily beautiful waterfalls in South America, the most known of which are considered Angel and Iguazu. Angel is rightly considered the main attraction of Venezuela. This magnificent and at the same time elegant in its simplicity nature miracle can be seen in Canaima National Park. Angel's water stream is overthrown from almost a kilometer high. This is the largest free fall of water, which, even in the air turning into the smallest particles, forms a fog, felt for several kilometers. Waterfall is a favorite place for fans of extreme relaxation, who do not miss the exclusive opportunity to make an unforgettable jump from the edge of the Auyantepuy plateau on a hang glider.

A magnificent series of cascades



A unique sight is the Iguazu, which includes 275 waterfalls that fall from many rocky ledges. A magnificent series of cascades is formed by the waters of the Iguazu and Parana rivers, which separate Brazil and Argentina. In order to feel the greatness of this creation of nature, it is better to take different options of the proposed excursions: from the pedestrian path passing through observation platforms to the most spectacular section of the Devil's Throat cascade, river excursions on boats sailing to the canyon itself, to exploring the waterfall from the air by helicopter.

Noteworthy are many other waterfalls, such as a host of cascades of Peru Gokta, Three Sisters and Umbilla. In Guyana, Marshall, Orinduik and Kayechur are especially magnificent. The mountains of Kanuku, the tropical forest of Ivokrama, the savannah of Rupununi are also unusually beautiful in this country.

The cradle of ancient civilizations

The mainland is distinguished not only by the extraordinary wealth and luxury of nature. It is a kind of cradle of the ancient Inca civilizations, urine, or Mochic, chichba and other tribal Indians, who left behind many unsolved secrets and mysteries of ancient cultures. For a traveler of the country, this part of the world is just a real storehouse of new discoveries. Sights of South America leave unforgettable emotions. Peru, Brazil, Argentina, Ecuador, Chile, Colombia, Venezuela, Bolivia, Paraguay, Uruguay, Guyana, Suriname, Guiana, South Georgia and the South Sandwich Islands, Falkland Islands - each of these countries seems to be saturated with the magic of ancient civilizations and is crammed with countless historical values .

The Lost City



The most famous attraction of the continent is considered Machu Picchu. The lost city, from above in the form of a Condor, is considered one of the religious, cultural and economic strongholds of the great Inca empire. is he It is an excellent example of ancient architecture. Temples, complexes, the famous Inca Observatory, the phenomena of solar initiation, agricultural terraces, the unique drainage system of Machu Picchu and today do not cease to amaze with their grandeur.

Treasures of the Continent

No less peculiar are other sights of South America. In Peru, it is worth visiting the most unusual and ancient city, the capital of the Inca empire of Cuzco. Very close to it is another monument to the civilization of the ancient people of Morai with a large amphitheater. The natural treasures of Peru deserve special attention: the Amazonian jungle, the Andes, in particular the Kolkinsky canyon, the Nazca desert with its mysterious giant images. One of the main tourist attractions of Peru and Bolivia is Lake Titicaca. He is called the cradle of the civilization of Teotihuacan, the Incas, Quechua and other Andean peoples. At the bottom of Titicaca, mysterious terraces and a temple were discovered, erected more than 1,500 years ago. The lake is surrounded by a huge number of legends, the most common of which tells about the underwater city of Wanaku, a kind of Atlantis of South America.

For adventure lovers, Chile is a great discovery. The country is striking in its diversity of mountains, forests, national parks, beaches and lagoons. The mysterious Easter Island, famous for its ancient stone monoliths, is very popular.

Inexhaustible tourist potential

Sights of South America will not leave anyone indifferent. Impenetrable jungle, breathtaking views of the mountains, unusual ancient monuments, the magnificent coastline of the continent, the islands of the Caribbean, the Galapagos Islands, each of which is unique and peculiar, are certainly worthy of the attention of tourists from all over the world. The mainland is also famous for its megacities. The largest cities in South America are able to pleasantly surprise with a combination of modernity and national flavor. In Brazil it is surrounded by the Rio de Janeiro mountain ranges. The cultural center of the country is ideal for relaxation. The most famous sights of the city: Sugarloaf Mountain, from the height of which you can admire stunning views of the city and Guanabara Bay. And also the famous Mount Cristo Redentor Mountain, on which is a huge statue of Jesus.



No less interesting is the shopping center of Brazil, Sao Paulo. It is famous for its theaters, museums, restaurants and, of course, Ibirapuera Park with an incredible number of fountains, beautiful lakes, enchanting shows.

A peculiar attraction of Brazil is the city of Salvador, admiring the unusual location of its neighborhoods and Spanish colonial architecture. This is the most picturesque city in the country, in which the spirit of those distant times still hovers.

The capital of Colombia abounds in sights. Bogot√° is a city of contrasts, famous for its futuristic architecture, vibrant and diverse cultural life, interesting museums, magnificent colonial churches and slums. This unique mixture of poverty and prosperity, expensive cars and pack mules, impoverished neighborhoods and ultra-modern buildings makes Bogot√° the most charming and at the same time chaotic capital of the planet.

The interweaving of modern and past, various architectural styles distinguishes the capital of Venezuela Caracas. The main attractions of the city are the museums area, Los Cabos and Del Este parks, located near the city are the national parks of Avila, Macarao, Guatopo. The capital is considered the best starting point for a trip to Venezuela. The main areas are Margarita Island, the islands of the Los Roques archipelago, Mochima National Park and Morrocoy National Marine Park. From Caracas go on exciting trips along the Llanos savannah, the ridges of the Cordillera de Merida, to Lake Maracaibo and the Canaima National Park, where Angel Falls is located.



Other sights of South America can be seen in the cities of Port Mont, Santiago and Valparaiso in Chile, Ushuaia in Argentina, Georgetown in Guyana, Montevideo in Uruguay, Port Stanley in the Falkland Islands, the Tierra del Fuego archipelago and many others.

Truly in South America lies the inexhaustible tourism potential that has yet to be unleashed. This amazing region of our planet gives exciting sensations and an incredible charge of vital energy. To see with your own eyes the picturesque landscapes, the brightness and originality of national cultures, the diversity of the unique animal and plant world, to approach the feeling of the power of ancient civilizations that have forever gone - it costs a lot.
