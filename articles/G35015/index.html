Greece is a country that offers quality beach and sightseeing vacations at low prices. Coming here, tourists often choose the island of Rhodes for living. If you are looking for a first-class hotel, then you should pay attention to the Atlantica Princess Hotel 4 * complex (Rhodes). Consider what service he can offer his guests.

Location of the complex

The hotel is located in the center of Ialyssos. This is a lively Greek resort that has not lost its own flavor and atmosphere of a provincial town. For tourists there is a good infrastructure. Everywhere you can find small shops, souvenir shops, Greek taverns. The beach often hosts public windsurfing competitions. Travelers who came to rest in August can take part in the Yalisia festival, which is dedicated to Greek history and culture.

Hotel Atlantica Princess 4 * (Rhodes) is located on the second beach line. To get to the city beach, tourists will have to walk about 100 meters. There is a bus stop near the hotel. From here you can reach the capital of Rhodes, which is located 7 km from the resort. Diagoras International Airport is also very close. It is removed at 8 km. You can get to the hotel in half an hour by bus, and by taxi you can get on in 10-15 minutes. There are tourist routes to Ialyssos, a trip in which will cost quite inexpensively.
