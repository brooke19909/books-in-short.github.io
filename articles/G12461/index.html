Aquapark

First of all, both children and adults usually visit the Nautilus water park, which is literally nearby. After a long road and a whole year of work with a minimum of entertainment, the best start to a vacation for the whole family is a trip to one of the largest water complexes on the Black Sea coast, located three hundred meters from the sea. There is enough space for everyone - the water park can simultaneously entertain more than two thousand people.

At the same time, the situation there remains comfortable, conducive to active movement, and all visitors note a thoughtful design, bright greenery, magnificent ponds. Four swimming pools, one of which is for children, six different slides - from ordinary tube-shaped to the most extreme, fountains, geysers, sun loungers in recreation areas, as well as bars and cafes. There are no breaks and days off in the water park.





Dolphinarium

An absolutely new construction in the village is the Lazarevsky Dolphinarium, which was created using the latest technologies: swimming pools with high-quality technical equipment, the coaching staff of professionals, and marine inhabitants have exceptional talents and in general are nice, especially for children. They are not just mammals, all these Pacific walruses, northern sea lions, polar beluga whales and the Black Sea - local - bottlenose dolphins.

These are intelligent, similar in intellect and character close to humans sea creatures. They have a genuine interest in communicating with people, and any person, consciously or unconsciously, will also necessarily reach for them. Because sea animals carry such unsolved secrets as the human ancestral home. Here shows are held, and you can also swim next to these cute animals, get to know them better. The dolphinarium is also within walking distance of the Atlanta Hotel.
