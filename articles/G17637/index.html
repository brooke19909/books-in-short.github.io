Radisson Hotel, St. Petersburg: address, description and reviews

Since ancient times, St. Petersburg has been called the cultural capital of Russia, a city of white nights, beautiful palaces and drawbridges. Many of the world-famous cultural and historical sites located here are attributed to UNESCO heritage. Every year St. Petersburg welcomes millions of tourists coming here with truly Russian hospitality. For their decent accommodation, the city has many hotels of various levels of "star rating". The most popular among visitors are the establishments of the Radisson network, a world-famous hotel brand that provides high-quality 4-5 * service.

Radisson Hotel (St. Petersburg): Acquaintance

The name of this institution immediately pops up in the memory as soon as one of the guests declares his desire to stay in St. Petersburg with comfort and close to the most important historical and cultural monuments. The Radisson Royal Hotel (St. Petersburg) is ideally located on one of the busiest city avenues - Nevsky, considered one of the most popular establishments belonging to the hotel chain known in the city. According to reviews, this Radisson hotel (St. Petersburg) offers excellent conditions for spending leisure time and exploring the sights of the city. Rating - 4.6 points. Category - 5 *. Cost of living: from 12 572 rubles to 151 883 rubles / day. per room.
