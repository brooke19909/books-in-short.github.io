What is baubles: photo and description, basic techniques for creating

Friendship bracelets are called baubles, most often woven from multi-colored threads to a mouline thread . For most people, they are associated with ethnic motives . Such jewelry is common among children and adolescents , but in some styles of clothing , for example, boho, these bracelets are also appropriate and popular .



What are baubles and where did they come from ? It is believed that the founders of the tradition to weave bright bracelets and give them to each other were North American Indians . Representatives of different tribes exchanged similar ornaments to strengthen spiritual ties . There is a similar ritual in hippie culture that used baubles instead of wedding rings . To do this, identical bracelets wove , and the newlyweds put them on to fasten their marriage vows .

What are baubles, photo and description

Previously, each product was created individually and reflected the status and character of a person . The color of the material used for bracelets was also of great importance and contained a certain encrypted message . But now most of the symbolism is lost, only the meanings of some shades and color combinations remain . What are baubles and what is the meaning of them now? This type of jewelry is most often made with one's own hands to present to someone as a sign of friendly disposition . It is not customary to sell or create them for yourself.

The main material used for baubles is mouline thread . But experienced craftsmen are able to weave such bracelets from leather cords , beads, ribbons and weave coins or go talismans . Before you begin , you need to choose a weaving pattern with your favorite pattern . The decoration can be plain or color . Mounting is done in different ways , but most often they leave two tails to tie a bracelet or sew a button .



The main types of nodes

To understand what baubles are made of floss, it is important to understand the varieties of knots for weaving them . It must be remembered that they are all carried out in two stages . There are four types of basic nodes that you should master to create bracelets :

  1. A knot to the right - in this case, the left thread is twisted in the right direction and is threaded into a loop , after which it is tightened tightly and returns to its original position . Then all the steps are repeated - the threads should change places .
  2. Knot to the left - this knot is performed in the reverse way , the right thread is twisted around the left and tightened . The node must be repeated .
  3. Tart to the right - a combination of two nodes . The right knot is weaved first , and then the left one .
  4. Tatting left - first weaves the left , and then - the right .

On the diagrams, each type of node is marked with special icons . In order not to get confused , it is better to sketch a notation for yourself next to the diagram . Weaving is carried out from left to right or from right to left - there is not much difference , the main thing is not to mix the threads .



How to prepare for weaving

To understand what a bauble is , you need to try to make it yourself. It is most convenient to start weaving this type of bracelet on a wide cardboard tablet with a clip . The upper part of the product is fixed with a standard paper clip or with adhesive tape. You can use a pin if the job is done on a soft surface . In order not to accidentally pull the threads , a knot is tied on top . The number of colors used depends on the picture . The length of the thread - on the complexity of the scheme and the planned size of the product . Wide baubles will require more material and it is more difficult to weave , therefore it is better for beginners to start with simple schemes , for example, with an arrow. You need to choose high - quality threads for weaving - it will be more convenient to work with it. It is better to purchase a floss with a large footage. Instead, needlewomen often use cotton yarn in skeins .

Ways of weaving baubles

There are two ways to weave: oblique and straight. The second option is a bit more complicated, but the decorations are more interesting. What are baubles made by oblique weaving? In this case, the leading thread is used, and the rest are braided around it, and all nodes are directed in one direction. To an amateur they will seem the same in both cases, but with this method it is difficult to create an unusual pattern. Most often, for bracelets of friendship, the oblique weaving method is used, when the threads braid each other in turn. It allows you to perform complex multi-colored ornaments.



Sometimes needlewomen are interested in what baubles are from a ribbon and what is their difference from products from a mouline thread. Weaving from satin ribbons is much easier. For them, a method similar to creating a cord is used, while nodules are not delayed. Now, having figured out what baubles are, you can begin to create an unusual bracelet to give it to your friend.
