Hotel Veronica 3 * (Cyprus, Paphos): photos, reviews of tourists

He was especially fond of middle-aged English tourists, so you can imagine the spirit of respectability and regularity that is poured here in the air.

True, both young couples and families with children here also feel quite comfortable, and lovers have fun do not get bored. Together with you, let us listen to guest reviews of this pleasant place of rest and try to get an idea of ‚Äã‚Äãwhat awaits us at the Veronica 3 * hotel (Cyprus).



A little pathos about Paphos

Paphos perfectly corresponds to its name, since the mentioned city is considered an elite resort of this fertile, fabulous island. It is located in a mesmerizing mountainous terrain, similar to the scenery, and descends directly to the shores of the Mediterranean Sea, where, according to legend, once upon a time the beautiful Aphrodite emerged from the foam of the sea ...

Almost everything is connected with her name in the city. Thousands of tourists come here for the sake of the goddess of love, because they believe that walks in this charismatic place fill their feelings with new bright colors and power - and they are not mistaken. By the way, if in the vicinity of Paphos, on the shore, you can find a pebble in the shape of a heart, then a strong and long love can be said to be guaranteed!

And very wealthy people come to rest in this city. True, vacationers with an average income can find decent places for a good rest in the famous resort, for example, Veronica hotel 3 *.

In Paphos and its environs, you can find many cozy bays, secluded beaches, restaurants with excellent cuisine and other details, without which it is difficult to imagine a rich vacation, not to mention the millennium-old ruins and other evidence of history that will delight all lovers of antiquity.

Talk about comfort

Each room at Veronika Hotel has a bathroom, terrace (these rooms are on the ground floor) or a furnished balcony. The rooms are also equipped with air conditioning, central heating, a telephone, a TV (there is a Russian channel), a minibar and a safe (for an extra charge of 2.5 euros and 3 euros, respectively), as well as a hairdryer and a set for making tea and coffee (this is free) .

On the advice of experienced travelers, you should not forget to grab an adapter from your home to recharge your cell phones, as in the hotel you can only get it for a fee of 3 euros.

Hotel Veronica 3 *, the reviews of which are brought to your attention in the article, provides its guests with a hairdresser and laundry (which, you will agree, is very convenient for parents with children), as well as luggage storage. The building is equipped with an elevator that allows you to easily climb to the upper floors, Internet access is also provided. In stock currency exchange. If necessary, guests can order a taxi at the reception or rent a car.

By the way, the hotel has a Russian-speaking staff, which greatly facilitates the lives of our tourists.





A little more about amenities

Rooms are cleaned every day, and towels are changed every 2-3 days. Tourists left grateful reviews about the impeccable honesty and care of the maids.

Opposite the Veronica hotel 3 * (Cyprus), as vacationers suggest, there is an excellent small supermarket. Incidentally, it offers the lowest prices in the vicinity, and here you can always purchase any missing products or essentials. The sellers in it, by the way, speak good Russian (and this is another convenience!).

How is the food organized in Veronica 3 * (Cyprus): reviews

Guests of the hotel are provided with free breakfast, lunch and dinner (they choose the type of their guest house during the design of the ticket). Half board includes breakfast and dinner, and full board includes three meals a day. Living on a half board, if desired, can already separately pay at the hotel 7 euros per day for an additional lunch.

Believe me, the food here is worth it! Tourists in their reviews pay tribute to the skill of the cooks of the Veronica 3 * hotel, emphasizing that the menu always has a choice of several meat, fish dishes (they are especially praised by hotel guests) and soups, and among the side dishes there are steamed vegetables, potatoes, rice, pasta products. Vacationers praise a variety of salads and a dessert consisting of delicious cakes, jellies, pastries, jam (peach is especially impressive for guests), as well as watermelons, melons, oranges and grapes.

Drinks at dinner need to be ordered separately. But their prices are the same as in the surrounding cafes.

Travelers are advised to try the delicious local ice cream, which, incidentally, costs only 1 euro here, while in stores - 2 or 2.5 euros.

Near the hotel there are excellent restaurants offering delicious local dishes and a rich wine list.



Stay conditions for children

Hotel Veronica 3 * offers children a separate cot, special chairs in the restaurant, as well as a children's menu. Those who wish can hire a nanny who will look after the child, while the parents go on a trip or to a restaurant.

For children, there is a playground on site, as well as an equipped pool.

As you drive to a three-star hotel, be sure to bring along shower gels, sunblock and beach towels. And your child should definitely have a bottle of water and a hat, which will protect him from overheating, and you from hassle.

The water in the Mediterranean Sea is quite salty, therefore, so that the skin of the child, and yours, also does not undergo irritation and drying out, be sure to take a shower after sea bathing. At the same time, it would be good for young children to lubricate the places of irritation with a baby cream.



You will definitely enjoy the beach!

Just five hundred meters from the Paphos Veronica 3 * hotel , across the road, there is a small beach with fine pebbles and sand. All vacationers noted its cleanliness and convenient location. In addition, it is protected from high waves by bulk breakwaters.

There is something to admire: through clear transparent water, pebbles at the bottom and small fish are visible, snooping almost at the feet of bathers. By the way, vacationers are very pleased here the lack of algae and large stones at the bottom.

There are water rides on the beach, and sunbeds and umbrellas will be paid (2.5 euros each). The sea here is small enough, which is very welcome vacationers with children and swimmers who are unsure of themselves on the water. If you walk along the coast, to the city beach, then the depth there, which will provide great opportunities for swimming and diving, will be much greater, but the number of people here, alas, will increase.

How can I organize my stay at the hotel?

The hotel has its own restaurant, two bars (one of them is by the pool), a shop. For guests there is a TV lounge, sauna, parking for cars, slot machines.

For an excellent pastime, the Veronica hotel 3 * (Paphos) has a small, but quite roomy pool, equipped with a children's section. Next to it, you can conveniently sit on sunbeds, in the sun or under an umbrella. This service is provided free of charge.

Guests can enjoy massages at an additional cost, use the sauna or soak in the jacuzzi. And to maintain good physical shape, the hotel offers a free gym and a tennis court, which you can play even in the evening, as it is well lit. In addition, you can pay and rent a pool table.

In the evenings, the hotel hosts a disco with an entertainment program. Additionally, vacationers are offered show programs that can cheer up and make your vacation unforgettable.

Excursion programs for different tastes and financial opportunities are also offered.

What is worth visiting while relaxing in Paphos?

As you know, once in this blessed place, you should not sit all day even in the most comfortable Veronica 3 * room (Paphos). Be sure to go on excursions. The most popular attractions (by the number of visits to tourists) are the tombs of kings. Burials here lasted for six centuries. And this solemn place attracts pilgrims.

The ancient settlement of Eroskipos, which means ‚Äúsacred garden‚Äù, will be very interesting for inspection. These are the very gardens of Aphrodite who were witnesses of her ardent love.

No less attractive is the monastery of the Holy Virgin Kykkos, founded in 1100. And the bird park will surely appeal to both adults and children. It regularly hosts bird shows. But besides birds in the park you can see giraffes, kangaroos, guinea pigs and many other animals from different continents.

Enthusiastic about the Sea Safari. This is a trip to the Blue Lagoon, where vacationers are given the opportunity to swim with masks in crystal clear water for an hour.

For lovers of vibrant life in the city there is Bar Street, where the fun begins in the evening. Go there, and the sweet bliss of daytime Cyprus will blow you away like the wind!



You can explore the island without a guide.

If you do not like the required programs offered by the guide, and you have chosen the right company, then you can rent a car to go traveling on your own. As mentioned above, Paphos Veronica 3 * provides the opportunity to rent a car.

One day of rental will cost you about 53 euros. True, you will have to first leave a deposit of 300 euros (this condition applies to all rental offices). But you will be provided with a full tank of gasoline, and you will be able to hit the road on a route compiled just for you! As a huge number of tourists rightly believes, the impressions received on such a trip will be much brighter and more colorful. Well, try it!

By the way, between the Almira and Anabella hotels, not far from Veronika, there is an information center where you can get a map of Paphos and a bus schedule. This will greatly help those who plan to get to know the city on their own. After all, such trips will cost you only 1 euro, but you can consider everything that seems interesting to you in Paphos and take many wonderful pictures that will not let you forget the local beauty in the future.
