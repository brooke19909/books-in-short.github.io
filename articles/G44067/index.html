Quick registration: Yandex.Money. How to use a wallet

Electronic money today has become as commonplace as plastic cards. The only difference is that paying for services and purchases from virtual accounts can be much easier and faster, and most importantly - safer than using a bank card. Even easier is their registration. Yandex.Money is one of the most popular payment systems. How to open a wallet in the system and how to use it? We will learn step by step.

How to get a wallet on Yandex

By the way, the name and surname are needed real, according to the passport, this can be useful when making payments, as well as when restoring access to the wallet in case of loss of control over it. A confirmation code will come to the phone, which must be entered in the appropriate window. You must also specify the email address to which all messages about the status of the account will come. At the same time, the system creates for you a new account to use the account.





Final step

At this stage, it remains to accept the terms of the user agreement, and this completes the registration. Yandex.Money announces the opening of an account on the next page. Here, already in the left menu, there is a wallet number of 18 digits. In the window in the center - congratulations on opening an account and a message that a letter has been sent to the specified e-mail to link it. You will need to go to your mail and confirm the address. Now you have an account with Yandex.Money. Registration is a free service.

How to use a wallet

The balance of the new wallet is still 0 rubles. The account can be replenished in any terminal, it is best in the Euroset or Svyaznoy mobile communication stores - there all operations are performed without commission. On the monitor you need to select the option "Electronic money", then - "Yandex.Money", in the window that opens, enter your wallet number, then deposit money. A message about replenishment of an account will instantly come to your phone and mail.

There is another option - to attach a VISA or MasterCard bank card to the account. This can be done on the same page where the registration took place. Yandex.Money can be replenished from the card at any time. To bind, go to the tab "Bind Map" and complete all the steps. The bank will require a password and confirmation by SMS. You can manage your account through mobile applications.

The best way would be to order a Yandex.Money card on the site. This is not virtual, but a full-fledged MasterCard PayPass premium, which can be paid not only in virtual, but also offline stores. You should place an order on the system page - and the finished name card will be delivered to the user by mail.





What can I pay for Yandex money

The list of possible payments in the system is, without exaggeration, a huge. As soon as registration is confirmed, Yandex money (after replenishing the account, of course) can be used:

  • to pay receipts for taxes, utilities, traffic fines;
  • to pay for telephony, internet, television;
  • for purchases in online stores;
  • for buying games and chatting on social networks;
  • to purchase books, movies, PC programs, antiviruses, etc. on the network;
  • to pay for air and train tickets, tours, hotels;
  • to pay for hosting and domains and much more.

Does the user need a Yandex wallet? The question is rhetorical: of course it is needed! Like everything that makes life easier and more comfortable. The Yandex.Money payment system is one of such opportunities.
