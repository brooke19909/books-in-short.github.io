Hotels in Sudak by the sea: where to stay?

Crimea attracts tourists with picturesque landscapes, a huge number of attractions, fascinating history. A great place to relax is Sudak, famous for reasonable prices, good beaches and a lot of unforgettable experiences after a vacation in this coastal city.





In order to organize recreation, the question arises of finding a hotel in Sudak. On the seashore, fortunately, many hotels are happy to meet and accommodate tourists in their comfortable rooms.

Relax with comfort

Hotel in Sudak on the seashore "Brigantine" surprises vacationers with its unusual location. It is located right on the coast. Any romantic wants to enjoy a cool summer morning and a warm evening, watching the amazing overflow of water. All this becomes possible in this hotel Sudak. In Crimea, on the seashore, you can relax perfectly thanks to the "Brigantine".

Everyone can afford a vacation at this hotel, because prices start at 1800 rubles per night. The view from the window is right on the bay. The central beach, the opportunity to find a wonderful tan at a special platform make tourists stay in this cozy hotel.

Unforgettable vacation in the "Sail" and "Birlik"

Those who wish to have a good time will be interested in offers of rest in Sudak. All-inclusive hotels on the seashore are a wonderful option for lovers of a comfortable stay. The hotel with the poetic name "Sail" is located in the heart of the spa town. The rooms are equipped with everything necessary for an unforgettable vacation (modern furniture, telephone, internet, air conditioning).

The convenient location of the "Sails" will not let you get bored, the children will be delighted with the amusement park, and their parents - from a pleasant evening in the restaurant or on the cypress avenue.
Hotels in Sudak by the sea is a great option for both outdoor enthusiasts and those who prefer to spend time in the hotel, sunbathing by the pool.





Whatever category of tourists the traveler belongs to, the Birlik hotel will not leave him indifferent. At its disposal are suites and standard rooms, the unique interior of which pleasantly surprises. Holidays in Birlik will leave unforgettable impressions thanks to the unique local style. Enjoy the fresh air at any time of the year will allow a small river flowing near the hotel, as well as a large number of lush trees and plants in the courtyard and around the hotel. And snow-white lions at the entrance to the hotel will always guard and maintain a peaceful mood.

A mini-hotel will help you to relax from the city bustle.

An excellent option for those looking for mini-hotels of Sudak on the beach will be the institution Molino. If you do not like noise and a lot of neighbors, the best choice of a place to stay is a small hotel or a hotel with a small number of rooms, but, despite this, with all the amenities. Molino is a short walk from the sea, so you don‚Äôt have to cross the whole city to reach the beach. On the territory of the hotel there is a dining room in which there is a games room - convenient for vacationers with young children.





The mini-hotel is good for company relaxation, comrades can occupy all rooms and spend time with friends. All rooms are comfortably furnished and are designed for a comfortable stay. Prices for one night in Molino start at nine hundred rubles, which will significantly save the family budget.

"Rook" will take to the world of unforgettable impressions

Those who have not yet decided on a hotel choice in Sudak should pay attention to this hotel. Rook has a large selection of rooms. This will allow you to organize your vacation in the best way, because the hotel offers rooms for one, two, three, four or five people.

Four buildings accept tourists; there is also a swimming pool on the territory of Rook - you can swim and sunbathe near the water without leaving a place of rest. In the immediate vicinity are a water park, a free beach. The hotel offers car wash services, which is very convenient for tourists visiting Sudak by car.





A massage room will allow you to relax and improve your health.

Rest for everyone

Another hotel in Sudak on the seashore is Forward. This four-story building is located in a quiet, picturesque place. Modern design and all the amenities make tourists come back here again and again. Along with the usual rooms, Forward offers to spend a vacation in a two-room suite with a kitchen, which is very convenient for long-term relaxation. On the territory of "Forward" there is a gazebo, there is a small fountain. The beach is within walking distance.

Why choose Sudak?

Many hotels are happy to accommodate guests in economy or luxury rooms. Holidays in Sudak will leave pleasant impressions, beautiful photos and memories. This cozy city is surrounded by mountains, filled with natural beauties and attractions. Visitors to the Crimea should stay in this seaside town, where the Genoese fortress, the Choban-Kule tower, the Cave Monastery and many other interesting places that allow you to plunge into history and have fun are located.
