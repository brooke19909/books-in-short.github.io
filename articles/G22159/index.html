Residence

The room fund of the Blue Fish Hotel 4 * hotel (Turkey, Konakli) is represented by apartments of the only category "standard". The interior has a modest modern design. The walls are decorated with paintings, there are vases with flowers. The floors are covered with laminate. The bathroom is tiled. Each room has a balcony, where you can meet the dawn or the upcoming night with a million twinkling stars.

The furniture is presented as a standard set: beds, wardrobe, table, chairs, luggage stand. The presence of an individual air conditioning system, a TV with Russian-language channels, an internal telephone, a safe and Wi-Fi makes staying in the room carefree and comfortable.

The bathroom in the rooms of the Blue Fish Hotel 4 * hotel (Alanya, Konakli) is equipped with a bath or shower, a set of cosmetics and a sink.





Supply system

Dining at the Blue Fish Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya) is carried out according to the buffet system three times a day. The main restaurant has a covered area and an outdoor terrace. The variety of dishes on offer is great: natural vegetables, nutritious meat, fresh fruits and fish products are just a few of the things the guests eat.

According to the all-inclusive concept, the use of all local alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks is included in the price. You can taste them in three bars: in the lobby, on the beach, by the pool. A visit to the first of them is paid. During the day, the snack bar provides an opportunity to satisfy the hunger with light snacks, delicious ice cream or fresh pastries.

Freshly squeezed juices and imported alcohol require additional payment.





Beach

Having familiarized themselves with the venue for a night's sleep, vacationers go to meet the azure waters of the Mediterranean. Blue Fish Hotel (Turkey) is located on the first coastline, a hundred meters from the promenade. In order to achieve the goal, it is necessary to cross the roadway, which can be done using the underpass.

Own sandy beach area stretches a strip of one hundred meters. It is furnished with numerous sun loungers for those who prefer to get a unique tan. However, not everyone is friends with the scorching rays of the sun. In this case, you should sit under an umbrella, which will create a shadow space of the desired area. For the opportunity to soak up the sunbed with a mattress, an additional fee is required. Towel dispenser is missing.

A wooden pier allows you to descend the stairs immediately into the deep spaces. Diving from it is prohibited. Changing cabins, a toilet and showers are part of the beach infrastructure.

For those who want to diversify swimming with a mask, it is possible to go to the open sea on a boat, from which you can immerse yourself in the deep abyss of the Mediterranean Sea. The amazing underwater world and rich embossed nature will give an unforgettable experience from this mini-excursion. Both experienced divers and beginners can visit it.

Among the various water attractions, the most popular are water scooters, circle riding behind a motor boat and windsurfing.





Aquazone

The swimming area is represented by three pools, one of which is equipped with bright and unusual water slides of different structures. Descent from them will give unforgettable emotions to everyone. However, they are open at pre-set hours, which can be found at the reception. Under standard weather conditions, this is before lunch and after.

Nearby is a children's aquacomplex, in the center of which there is a giant octopus. His tentacles act as slides and ladders. A small canopy in the form of a mushroom watered the heads of babies with flowing streams of water. Here they spend the lion's share of their free time, because splashing them give an indescribable delight.

The main pool is located near the main building, surrounded by a sufficient number of sunbeds and umbrellas. There is a fenced children's area where swimming of children is under the constant supervision of adults, which allows everyone to relax at the same time. These pools are unheated.





Leisure of little guests

When planning a summer vacation for their baby, parents choose a hotel, the infrastructure of which will allow their child to gain a lot of new experiences for the upcoming school year. To see your child happy and with a smile on his face is the goal of every adult. The administration of this hotel complex thought out every minute of free time for the guys.

The doors of the daytime kids club are open to visitors between the ages of 4 and 12. It is here that the children will reveal their talents and share their abilities and skills. Having played enough with a variety of toys, designers and kits under the supervision of qualified teachers, they go frolic in the fresh air.

Numerous playgrounds for children of all ages, equipped with slides and swings, ladders and passages, will occupy the kids for a long period. A detached plastic house with lots of sandbox paschiks leads little guests into an indescribable delight. There is also a basketball hoop. The team of animators tirelessly with smiles on their faces gives the kids an amazing tale, the participants of which are the children themselves.

Manege in the room is available on request. The restaurant offers a children's menu and high chairs. Babysitting services are available at an additional cost.





Pastime adults

In addition to shopping and excursions to local attractions, you can spend surprisingly varied time without leaving the borders of the hotel complex. In their free time from sunbathing, and enjoying plenty of splashing in the waters of the Mediterranean Sea, vacationers can visit the library. A calm place and a bewitching atmosphere of silence will allow you to take a trip through the pages of your favorite book and spend your free time.

The presence of billiards, table tennis and darts allows you to compete for victory with an opponent in agility and accuracy.

Not only in the evening, but throughout the day, a cheerful team of animators, consisting of three people, gives a great mood to each guest. Their task is not to let anyone get bored, which, according to tourists, they can easily cope with. Colorful dances, acrobatic stunts, entertaining contests and humorous scenes are just a few that amuse travelers.

The day ends with a disco, entrance to which is paid. Pleasant national music and foreign hits performed by eminent DJs delight holidaymakers every night.

Harmony with oneself lifts the mood for everyone who has visited these procedures.

Sport
