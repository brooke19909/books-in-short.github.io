Crimea, Mezhvodnoe. Travel Vacationers Reviews

Many tourists who prefer a traditional summer vacation on the Black Sea often come to Crimea. Mezhvodnoe, reviews of which you can see in this article, is one of the unique places on this peninsula. To begin with, the village is located in the region of Western Crimea where people settled for more than two and a half thousand years ago. And this means that even in those days, the ancient Greeks realized how good it is to live here. And still these places attract with its outlandish beauty and pleasant climate.



Crimea, Mezhvodnoe. Reviews about the village

This resort town itself is small. Here in the low season, up to a thousand people live. Only fifteen kilometers away is the more famous resort - the Black Sea, the historical center of the region. However, getting here is convenient, including without your own car. From Simferopol minibus can be reached in two hours, and from Evpatoria to get about sixty minutes. Here, first of all, young people and family burdened with children are traveling here. The village stretches along the Yarylgach Bay, which is part of the famous Kerkinitida Bay. Of course, the popularity of this village has led to an increase in the number of vacationers and a rise in prices compared to previous years, but still, many call their vacation magnificent.



Crimea, Mezhvodnoe. Reviews about the sea and beaches

Western Crimea is a strange place. Here, as in Greece, there is everything: huge endless beaches, where even in July you can find a place deserted and clear. At the same time, snorkelling enthusiasts will find bizarre cliffs and secluded bays there. The smell of the steppe is combined with a fresh sea wind, which greatly contributes to healing. For children, the local sand and shallow water are convenient. And young people, of course, prefer steep cliffs and deep "baths". The most important beach of the village is sandy, free and six-kilometer long, because the rest in Crimea is so unique in this area . Mezhvodnoe is famous for its clear water, which is rare for this kind of bottom - and very warm. And in the rocks (they can be reached no more than twenty minutes from any part of the village) you will find fishing, shellfish, crabs, diving and the secrets of the underwater world. After all, water and at a depth is perfectly visible. And there are so many suns in the number of days in a year that both Yalta and Sochi are "envious".



Crimea, Mezhvodnoe. Surroundings Reviews

From the village on foot you can walk to the amazing salt lakes. One of them is of the same name in the bay, and the second is called Dzharylgach. In their chemical composition, they are similar to Saki waters, and their muds have equally healing properties. Since the village is located between the sea and lakes, it is called “Mezhvodnoe”. Crimea, the map of which you will need when traveling, especially if you are on your own transport, reveals here special charms and secrets. Horseback riding tours are organized in the steppe and the countryside. In addition, you can visit the Atlesh reserve, as well as a trip to the legendary Cape Tarkhankut. In the Black Sea, it is recommended to look at the remains of ancient antique ruins.
