Hotel "Christie", Mirny, Yevpatoriya: photos and reviews of tourists

Today it is difficult to find Russian tourists who have never been to Crimea. Why do you need foreign resorts when you are waiting for the enchanting Black Sea, golden beaches and the sun? You can talk a lot about the beauty of European countries and admire the exotic nature, but believe me, here you will enjoy no less. The peninsula of Crimea is stunningly beautiful. Drowning in subtropical vegetation, surrounded by a ridge of the Crimean mountains and a beckoning strip of the sea, it has always attracted lovers of marine leisure. It is very difficult to find a free hotel here in season. Therefore, you need to decide in advance where you will live. Today we will tell you about the Christie Hotel (Mirny).



general description

This is one of the best offers for tourists. Hundreds of people relax here every year, and each of them wants to go back next summer. Hotel "Christie" (Mirny) is modern and comfortable, and it is very conveniently located 30 km from Yevpatoriya. It is located on a spit, between the wonderful Lake Donuzlav and the Black Sea. This is a huge tourist complex, which includes two hotels (4 and 3 stars), as well as cottages. Each of them can be an ideal place for your summer vacation.



Main advantages

No wonder the Hotel Christie (Mirny) received such a high rating. The sea in this place is considered the cleanest in the region. For tourists, there is a private beach located just 50 meters from the hotel. This is not just a sandy patch. Beach infrastructure includes sunshades and deck chairs, a volleyball court and a children's slide with a trampoline, as well as a beach cafe.

All parents are concerned about what the children will do on vacation. Hotel "Christie" (Mirny) completely solves this problem. Animators work with different groups of children from morning to evening. They sculpt and draw, participate in interesting contests. Special attention deserves the hotel kitchen. All dishes are prepared from products grown in this region, which has a positive effect on quality. And of course, one cannot but note the countless opportunities for outdoor activities. This is wind surfing and sailing, sea fishing, diving and boat trips.



Hotel complex "Christie"

It stands out from the majority of hotels, which are abundant here. Hotel "Christie" (Evpatoria, Mirny) includes 54 rooms. The guests are offered standard, junior suites and suites. All are equipped with the latest technology. This air conditioning system, satellite TV, refrigerator. Every tourist has access to the Internet. There is a restaurant with a large selection of dishes for any event. There is an equipped banquet hall, which is ideal for special occasions.

Hotel "Christie" (Mirny village) is equipped with a 24-hour underground parking and a convenience store. Judging by the reviews of regular visitors, this is one of the best places for a relaxing family vacation, as well as lovers of outdoor activities.



Hotel Amenities

Guests check in at 14:00, and check-in is possible at 12:00. The hotel has a tour desk that offers ticketing services. With it, you can choose any excursion route and get to know the city better. Pets are allowed, but the owner must first familiarize themselves with the rules of keeping and walking.

On the ground floor there is a safe where you can leave valuables, as well as luggage storage. A concierge works around the clock to help you deal with any issue, as well as the front desk. At any time of the day or night you will be greeted very cordially.
