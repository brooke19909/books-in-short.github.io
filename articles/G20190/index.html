What to bring from Mexico as a gift to family and friends?

Holidays in Latin America for many seem like a paradise on earth, but sooner or later it is time to decide what to bring from Mexico as a gift to friends and relatives. A huge selection of goods sometimes baffles tourists, because you want to buy everything at once, but the suitcase is not rubber. It should be noted that prices in Mexico do not bite, and here you can buy a lot of high-quality and interesting things for a small fee. It is better not to buy anything at hotels and in nearby shops, the cost of goods there is usually overstated by half, or even more. For souvenirs, you should go to the markets in the city center.

Sombrero can be purchased from straw, velvet or fabric. The poncho is a piece of rectangular-shaped fabric, in the middle there is a small hole for the head. The clothes are woven from colorful yarn, dressing national patterns on it. Poncho will come in handy on long, cold evenings, when you can warm up and remember about a vacation in Latin America.



If you are interested in what to bring from Mexico to a woman as a present, then you should pay attention to blue amber. This is a transparent pebble, in the middle of which there can be small fish or plant branches. Local artisans still make various figures from obsidian, also called "volcanic glass." Unusual products can be purchased in almost every store, you should definitely bargain, sometimes sellers are almost twice as low in price.

What can a man bring from Mexico? No representative of the stronger sex, of course, can resist alcohol. In Mexico, the most popular is tequila, but it is also worth trying mezcal made from fermented agave juice. Many people like Rompope - egg liqueur, which is produced by local nuns. What to bring from Mexico as a gift to yourself or a loved one, if not jewelry. Silver prices will please many; a wide selection of products for every taste is offered here.



Men will certainly love the Aztec knife, whose handle is made of bone or wood in the shape of an eagle's head, and the blade is made of obsidian. If you are interested in what souvenirs to bring to Mexico to friends and acquaintances, then you should pay attention to wooden items inlaid with beads. You can also purchase candles as gifts in special boxes made of various fruits. Their advantage is that they fill the room with the aroma of that very fruit. From the skin of pumpkins you can buy small jugs that will look original in any interior. In Mexico, you can buy a lot of useful, high-quality and simply interesting things. There are souvenirs here for everyone, and low prices will please every shopaholic.
