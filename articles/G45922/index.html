How old are the structures on the Giza Plateau?

It is believed that the age of the Egyptian pyramids on the Giza plateau is 4.5 thousand years. But with the dating of many structures, difficulties arise, since they were partially rebuilt, restored, and therefore even a radiocarbon analysis cannot give absolutely accurate answers. The remaining pyramids were supposedly built during the time of the Old Kingdom - around 2300 BC. e.

To this day, 80 pyramids have been preserved in Egypt, and the most beautiful are those that remained after the fourth dynasty. But since ancient times, only three are considered to be a true Wonder of the World. Their names are known to everyone - the pyramid of Cheops, Chefren and Mykerin. The age of the Cheops pyramid and the other two is about four thousand years, which cannot but surprise.

Pyramids of Mexico

Mexican pyramids are no less impressive and magnificent monument to human architecture and incredibly hard work. To this day, they amaze everyone who saw them, and even at the time of the first discovery, the impression was ten times higher!

They were erected by the Aztecs, Toltecs, Mayans and some other South American peoples. First you need to get a little acquainted with the history of those nationalities that lived here.



The civilization of Cuicuilco bloomed most vividly here. The peak of its maximum power falls on the period from 1500 to 200 BC. Why are we all saying this? The fact is that the largest and most impressive Quicuilco pyramid was built at that time (southern Mexico City). Moreover, the structure is unique, because its cross section ... is round, perfectly fits into the surrounding landscape.

But scientists did not immediately find it. The eruption of the same volcano put an end to the development of civilization in the region, and therefore no other magnificent architectural monuments were found here. What is the difference between the Egyptian and Mexican pyramids?

Many believe that they are almost identical, and due to this they put forward various (in terms of absurdity) theories about the Atlanteans and ‚Äúcelestial descendants‚Äù who fled from the cataclysm, but this is not so. The pyramids of Egypt and Mexico are similar only in appearance (and even then relatively), but in everything else they have a lot of differences.



Firstly, in Egypt, these structures were absolutely smooth, while the Aztecs, Toltecs and Mayans built them stepwise initially. Secondly, the pharaohs considered the pyramids solely as a resting place against earthly troubles, and in Mexico they used the pyramids exclusively as temples, and even carried out very bloody sacrificial rituals there.

Other differences

Thirdly, the tops of structures in South America are completely flat, since it was there that the priests engaged in their bloody work. Moreover, there is also an additional building, which just did the part of the temple and the "slaughterhouse" in combination. In principle, it is also possible to climb to the top of the Egyptian pyramid, but it is impossible to do something there due to the banal lack of space.

Fourth, the age of the Mayan and Egyptian pyramids. In Mexico, almost all of these buildings were built literally at the beginning of our era, while the tombs of the pharaohs were built three to four thousand years BC.

Fans of conspiracy theories may argue that all this is nothing, because the main characteristic of these structures, that is, the pyramidal shape, is the same in all cases. But this is not an argument, since such forms are found in nature, and a gap of several thousand years suggests that the Toltecs or Mayans themselves reached the most convenient form of their temples.

On the basis of what is the age of the pyramids determined?



So how does science determine the age of the Egyptian pyramids and their Mexican "relatives"? Based on radiocarbon analysis, which began to be actively used only in 1984. At that time, Egyptologists examined at least 64 samples of organic substances from the pyramids. Measurements showed that many of the structures on the Giza plateau were 400 years older than previously thought. So, it was reliably established that the Cheops pyramid was built no earlier than 2985 BC. e. This is five centuries more than previously thought! However, this is already enough to refute the version of the "Atlanteans who built these structures tens of thousands of years BC". The age of the pyramids of the pharaohs was much more modest. It should be noted that even radiocarbon analysis posed several new questions for researchers.

So, it is already known for sure that the Chefren pyramid was erected somewhere in the region of 2960. This gives logical reason to assume that its construction was carried out almost simultaneously with the Cheops. It is also possible that this was a separate complex of two structures, to the construction of which the same pharaoh could have had a hand. It would be quite normal to assume that the Pyramid of Mikerin was built somewhere in the next 50 years ...

But radiocarbon analysis showed that it was built no earlier than 2572 BC. e. This one is almost 400 years after the estimated date! But historians have long assumed that these two objects were built together ...
