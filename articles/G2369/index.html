African Village Show (Sukko)

More than one photo album of people vacationing in Anapa is decorated with photos of Africans in a skirt of leaves and with spears in their hands. Previously, they walked along the beach, offering to make a joint photo. And people agree, taking the funniest poses. Here cannibals carry a white man tied to a spear hand and foot, while black slaves raise their beautiful mistress in their arms. This is entertainment for vacationers and a profit for Africans, mostly students working part-time during the holidays.





Unusual service

Nowadays, when people are fed up with all kinds of exotic services, something new is constantly required to attract the attention of the public. Therefore, not far from Anapa created the show "African Village" in Sukko. Professional artists perform in it. This is a friendly and close-knit team called "Rhythms of Africa." In winter, they tour other cities and prepare a new summer program. The village of Sukko, where the improvised village is located, is located 12 kilometers from Anapa.

What will show

How do the artists surprise guests? The program "African Village" (Sukko) includes ethnic dances, songs and playing musical instruments. The rhythm beats on tom-toms. But at the same time, the artists dance to modern fashionable rhythms. They do it so incendiary that many viewers also get up and begin to move in the rhythm of salsa, rock'n'roll, break or pop music.





Affordable art

The internal structure of the village is made in such a way that to stand up and communicate with the artists right during the show "African Village" (Sukko) is not difficult. Spectators are sitting on wooden benches arranged in a semicircle. In the middle is a stage and a bar. Between artists and guests a strip of sand, which serves as a kind of neutral strip. But at the very beginning of a vivid performance, the border is erased. Artists interact with the guests of the village, involve them in their action. They also hold contests and discos.

Eat and have fun

The show "African Village" (Sukko) can be called humorous, there are so many jokes and musical flirtations with the audience. The performance lasts three hours, but the audience does not have time to get tired. During the show, they can eat refreshments from the bar. The menu contains a variety of dishes for every taste. Among them are potatoes, barbecue, kebab and others. There is nothing exotic in them, but the atmosphere itself is conducive to imagine that these dishes are the most African.

Dance language

Although everything happens without words, the audience understands what each member of the tribe does. The language of dance is so expressive. You can see how Africans hunt, eat, relax, communicate with each other. The show begins late at night, so the experience is enriched by the light of colorful lanterns, from which the masks on the artists' faces seem even more bizarre. The dark time of the day allows you to relax and forget about the problems. And when the action ends, the audience is happy to visit a souvenir shop where they can purchase a variety of African souvenirs.

The show is energizing and positive for the whole year, becoming another wonderful memory taken away from the sea.
