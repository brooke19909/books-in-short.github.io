Wishing paper cake is an unusual addition to kind words

Often we encounter a problem: how to choose a good gift to please dear and close people. But even if a gift is selected, it is no less difficult to present it correctly. An interesting and elegant solution to this issue can be a paper cake with wishes made by yourself. It will serve as a packaging for some not very large, but valuable gifts and a repository of parting words and wishes for the hero of the occasion. In this case, no one can doubt your creativity and originality.



Future product shape

How to make a cake with wishes? Firstly, you should decide on the type. It will also be much more convenient if you first draw a diagram of your "confectionery" product.

The first wish cake you can make is a traditional round shaped product divided into several pieces. You choose their number at your discretion, depending on how many wishes you want to write. Preferably, there are at least six segments. After all, the more pieces, the more warm words you can write, the more joy the person to whom you want to present such a surprise will receive. When making a round single-tier cake with wishes, it is necessary to make triangles (pieces) of paper or cardboard. Make one blank as a trial option, and only after that cut out all the other parts, and then fasten them together. The main thing is that each segment can easily be opened and closed.



Making a multi-tiered gift

Cardboard cake with wishes can also be tiered. This look is more suitable for a wedding celebration as a gift to the newlyweds. To create it, you will need several boxes of different sizes, their shape can be absolutely any you like. Boxes can be either purchased at the store or made independently. Particular attention should be paid to design. You can decorate the boxes with beads, fabric or film, sparkles, various ribbons and bows, decorative figures. Carefully think through the details to make your cake look appetizing, like a real one.

Each piece or tier is a box for one wish, which at your discretion can be supplemented with a symbolic gift. Vitamins can embody the wish of health, a sweet life - sweets or chocolate, a card in the form of a heart - love. Think about what your friend is dreaming of and show that the fulfillment of desires is closer than he thinks. To give a cake with wishes of greater value, put real money in one of its fragments. They will become a wish for wealth and another additional gift. You can also prepare small gifts for guests and make a separate piece for each. Try to choose parting words and their corresponding characters based on the individual characteristics and interests of the recipients. Believe me, after such a surprise, no one present will remain indifferent.

Such a beautiful cake with wishes will become an elegant and unusual addition to your gift, as well as the design of kind words about which the recipient will have warm memories for a long time.
