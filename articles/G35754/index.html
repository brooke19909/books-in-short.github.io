What to bring from Yaroslavl: bear

Tourists are traditionally surprised at the abundance of souvenirs depicting a bear. What to bring from Yaroslavl, if not an article glorifying the symbol of the city? An interesting legend is connected with this beast, which is carefully preserved by the townspeople. It says that the village was founded in the place where Yaroslav the Wise killed with his bare hands a huge bear, which was set against him by enemies.





The beast is depicted on various souvenirs, you can choose t-shirts, magnets, calendars, which are abundantly offered by shops and shops. Of course, there are figures created from various materials. The most popular are clay and ceramics. The Teddy Bear Museum is a place where it is easy to buy original figurines depicting the enemy defeated by Yaroslav, as well as appropriate toys for children.

Felt boots ...

What to bring from Yaroslavl as a gift to relatives, colleagues and friends? There were times when only rich people could afford to buy boots. Fortunately, this period is a thing of the past. Today, these shoes are available to everyone. Valenoks from this city are famous for their warmth and convenience, so tourists are well sold out.

Poshekhonsky cheese

What to bring edible from Yaroslavl? Would you like to treat yourself, your family and friends with something tasty? You should pay attention to the famous Poshekhonsky cheese. This product began to be in demand in the second half of the 19th century, since then its popularity has not waned.





Why is cheese called Poshekhonsky, and not otherwise? The product was named after the manufacturer, which is located in the village of Poshekhonye. Cheese is famous for its special sour and spicy taste, which falls in love with almost everyone who tastes it. The product can be eaten in pure form or used in the preparation of various dishes to which it gives a unique taste. It is easy to find Poshekhonsky cheese by looking at local food stores.

The product should not be tried by people who follow a diet, as it is famous for its calorie content. Yaroslavl claim that this product has a healing effect on the skeletal system. It completely lacks synthetic impurities, preservatives.

Embroidery

What souvenirs to bring from Yaroslavl? Embroidery is very popular among city guests, and local craftsmen achieved perfection in this art. Of the patterns, patriotic themes are now the most relevant. Shirts for children and adults, towels, tablecloths, paintings are decorated with embroidery. Gift items are finished with beads, decorated with semiprecious stones.





The cost of souvenirs depends on how much was spent on the materials used in their manufacture. It offers travelers - not only manual (stitch, cross-stitch), but also machine embroidery. Tourists who want to save money should buy such gifts by hand, bypassing souvenir shops.

Yaroslavl porcelain

What can I bring from Yaroslavl as a gift? It is impossible to leave the city without buying at least one elegant product manufactured at the Imperial Porcelain factory. Cups (coffee and tea), plates, vases, jugs of milk - the range is really wide. Of course, there are sculptures that can be miniature or impressive in their dimensions.

Porcelain decorated with cobalt mesh is especially popular . We are talking about an original drawing of cracks that can change colors depending on the lighting. A legend has been associated with the invention of this method, claiming that the work of Imperial Porcelain did not stop even during the war, when the city was deprived of water and electricity. The liquid, without which it is impossible to produce basic material, was extracted from local reservoirs by workers. It was they who drew attention to the cracked ice, through which sparkling snow lenses appeared.

Healing tinctures

Yaroslavl is a city located in the territory of the region, famous for medicinal plants. It is not surprising that the manufacture of medicinal balms for internal and external use has become widespread. For example, the Old Yaroslavl balm, which has been produced since 1989, is very popular with guests of the village and local residents.



The composition of the product contains St. John's wort, blueberries, wormwood, mint. It is famous for its ability to have a strengthening effect on the body. The balm is intended for internal use, it is offered in various containers, which allows travelers to choose the optimal volume.
