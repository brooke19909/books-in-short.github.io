Tver, hotel "Volga": reviews, prices

The ancient Russian city of Tver invariably attracts a large number of tourists coming from all over the country and the world. Its territory is spread out on both sides of the great Volga, the rivers Lazur, Tmak, Solomink, Tvertsa. This is a modern metropolis rich in attractions and interesting events. Hotels in Tver are always ready to receive guests.





Where to stay for a tourist?

Representatives of the tourism business and the guests of the city themselves, when choosing a location, recommend focusing on the purpose of the trip. If this is a business visit, it is better to stay in the center, couples with children will like hotels a bit far from noisy areas, highways, etc.

Below are the best hotels in Tver. In the city center you can contact the indicated places:

  • Hotel "Governor".
  • Guest house "Luidor".
  • A particularly popular place in the city of Tver is the Volga Hotel.
  • For students and low-budget tourists a good hostel "Europe" is open.

Hotel Volga: general information

The reputation of the hotel has developed in such a way that it is rightfully considered the most popular among city guests. The building is well located in the center of Tver near the railway station, administrative, museum objects, galleries.

The structure of the rooms was developed taking into account the wide requests of the hotel guests and is focused on various budget options. In each room, the staff tried to create coziness and comfort, which is confirmed by tourists who visited the city of Tver. Hotel Volga is a place with an unforgettable atmosphere.





Number of rooms

Hotel guests can stay in any of the 158 rooms of the first, second, deluxe category and apartments. You can pay for your stay in cash or bank transfer, the staff is ready to accept Visa and Master Card cards, which all respectable hotels in the city of Tver practice.

The second category of rooms is a single standard.

At the disposal of the guest comes one room and a bathroom, in which a shower cabin is installed. Furniture items are represented by a bed (one and a half), bedside tables. The technical devices in the room are a TV, telephone, refrigerator, hairdryer. Guests can use the dishes. The cost of living, that is, four thousand rubles a day, includes breakfast (buffet).

The first category of rooms is a single standard.

The room is in many ways similar to the previous one with some exceptions: guests are additionally provided with a desk and a kettle. The room itself is more spacious, which affects the cost of living. It is 4250 rubles daily.





Standard rooms for two guests

In one room there are two single beds, a desk, a bedside table. A full bathroom adjoins the room, where a shower cabin is located. Guests can also have breakfast on the buffet system, for which you will have to pay 4750 rubles.

Suite

In this category, the hotel client can choose one of the options provided:

  1. Two-room suite with a bedroom and a living room. In a large room there are armchairs, a sofa, a table, in the other there are two single beds. If desired, one double bed can be installed. In a modern bathroom, hygiene kits are surely laid out.
  2. Two-room suite with a double bed, air conditioning. The living room has a sofa and a table. The bathroom has a jacuzzi.
  3. One room suite with a wide bed, a refrigerator, video player, dishes, hygiene items, two bathrooms.
  4. Suite with an extensive living room (table, dishes, refrigerator, sofa), a spacious bedroom with a wide bed, two bathrooms.
  5. Suite with two bedrooms with wide beds. The rest of the decoration is no different from the previous version.
  6. Two-story suite. On the ground floor there is an extensive living room, on the second floor there is a bedroom.

The cost of living in rooms of this category is 6250 rubles.

Apartments

The rooms are created on an individual project. Some of them are located on two floors, one has a sauna. All rooms are distinguished by original decoration. The cost of living is 11,250 rubles. The price of apartment stay is the lowest in the city of Tver. Hotel Volga provides affordable services.

Additional amenities

Hotel guests can use the following services, which are provided free of charge and for a fee:

  • Parking, which is under 24-hour security.
  • A restaurant.
  • Bar.
  • Dry cleaning and laundry services.
  • Business center, office services with internet access.
  • Shop with souvenirs.
  • Beauty saloon.
  • Sauna.




Where is the hotel complex located?

Any guest of the city can easily find the place where the Volga Hotel is located. Address: Zhelyabova street, house 1. The building is located near the railway station in the historic city center. The hotel welcomes guests around the clock.

Opinion of the guests

The hotel is very popular among tourists, due to its convenient location, reasonable prices and decent service. This is one of the best places in which you can settle in the city of Tver. Guests from all over the world come back here many times.

Next, you should familiarize yourself with what the Volga Hotel deserved reviews among guests. Guests note that this is a good place with a convenient location and delicious food. The staff is distinguished by friendliness, attentiveness and friendliness. But the disadvantages are often attributed to poor sound insulation in standard rooms. Management should resolve this issue. In this hotel you can have a great rest, taste excellent dishes and fully enjoy the city and surrounding attractions.

In the city, the hotel is considered one of the most acceptable accommodation options. The place can be used for family vacations. Guests are offered tasty, wholesome and nutritious food. Accommodation in rooms of any category entitles you to free breakfast. They are organized in buffet style. Despite the abundance of guests, the food is always prepared at the highest level and really very tasty.

The hotel is characterized by updated interiors, but you can feel the "breath" of Soviet times. On the ground floor, upon arrival, there was a faint smell of sewage.

The staff provides inexpensive and high-quality additional services. Some rooms offer stunning vistas. The room is a bit noisy due to the working air conditioner. The hotel is very clean and offers an optimal range of services at an affordable cost.

Hotel "Volga" will appeal to tourists who are unfriendly to the surroundings. For a similar price, this is the best hotel business product available in Tver. Accommodation allows you to experience the urban spirit, conveys the mood of the area, which is important for those who want to imbue with the originality that prevails around them.





The ancient Russian city is not only a storehouse of sights, but also a cultural, business regional center. The city of Tver offers a mass of pleasant emotions and entertainment to residents and guests. The Volga Hotel will help you to stay comfortably at the most affordable cost.
