What is a bellie incident? The history of the concept

The question "what is the bellie incident?" ask almost all readers of books about the First World War. Many are sure that this term is somehow connected precisely with the history of the 20th century, but this is not at all true.

What is a bellie incident ?

The term was used in Roman law. Literally translated from Latin as a "military incident."

Analyzing the historical period before the formation of ancient civilizations, we see that wars at that time began spontaneously. Some tribes attacked others without explaining the reasons. It was just necessary to take possession of the forest area with an abundance of game or to recapture some reservoir with a large number of fish, or replenish your tribe with women who would ensure the continuation of the genus. Long negotiations or the formulation of the causes of war were superfluous.

With the development of civilization, diplomacy is gradually developing. The spontaneous outbreak of war was at least indecent. And the Romans, who waged wars very actively, came up with a very tricky way. Before attacking an objectionable people, they put forward a deliberately impracticable insulting ultimatum. The demands on the enemy were always so absurd that any self-respecting state or tribe could not fulfill the enslaving ultimatum. But Rome needed just that ... Now they could start a war, motivating the attack by refusing to fulfill the ultimatum. In the course of the clarifications, we answered the question "what is the incident of the bell." This is a formal contrived occasion to war.





Casus Bellie in the 20th Century

In the XX century there were several major wars and large-scale NATO operations. The events of August 1, 1914 clearly demonstrated what the Bellie incident was. In the capital of Bosnia , Sarajevo, the Serbian underground member Gabriel Princip committed the assassination of one of the heirs to the Austrian throne, Archduke Ferdinand. After this event, the First World War began.





World War II began in a similar way. Hitler's minions dressed in the uniform of the Polish army and attacked the broadcasting point of a small radio station on the border with Poland and defeated the premises. Hitler blamed Poland for this act.

Also, do not forget about the NATO military operation in Yugoslavia, which took place without the official permission of the UN. NATO members found a reason and began the bombing of the Yugoslav capital.
