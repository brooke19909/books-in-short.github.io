Car radio with navigation and rear view camera: features and reviews

Today, the means that provide comfort and safety behind the wheel, including a car radio with navigation and a rearview camera, have become available to the motorist. The appearance of this device marked a new stage in the development of automotive electronics. It allowed to combine several important functions for the driver in one unit. Manufacturers of auto electronics claim that the car radio with navigation and a rear-view camera "Android", despite its multifunctionality, easily performs its intended purpose. The sound quality on it will satisfy even the most demanding listener.

Before making such a purchase, it is necessary to pay attention to several important points: the advantage of combining several functions in one device, how the car radio with navigation and rear view camera is installed, and what are the features of the products of companies popular in the electronics market.



Advantages of a car radio with GPS

A GPS navigator is indispensable for every driver. Without this device, it is easy to get lost during a long trip through unfamiliar terrain. It helps to navigate in big cities, allows you to avoid serious mistakes in driving: for example, it warns the driver about a one-way road or a sharp turn in front. In addition, knowledge of road works or accidents will help you re-route and save time on the road in a familiar place.

In order to take advantage of these advantages, before it was necessary to buy a separate device or install special programs on a phone or tablet. This required time and effort. In addition, an additional item on the console could restrict vision and distract from the road. Therefore, a car radio with navigation and a rear view camera is a worthy solution to this problem, making life easier for the driver.

This feature is available even for cars of past years, which require only a small device for playing music. Specially for these models, a car radio with navigation and a 1din rear view camera was created. It is inexpensive and quite functional. With its help, both the road and the parking will become safer.



Advantages of a car radio with rear view camera connection function

The rear view camera has recently become not a luxury, but a necessity. This is especially true for large machines. This device allows you to control the situation at the back to avoid a collision due to braking. The rear-view camera also provides invaluable assistance in the parking lot, helping the driver safely place his vehicle for everyone.

Combined car radio with navigation and rear view camera will allow not only to observe what is happening behind the car, but also to record everything. Such a safety net will allow a quicker resolution of the situation in the event of an accident.

Features of car radio installation

First of all, you should pay attention to what features a car radio with navigation and a rear view camera has during installation. In this case, it is necessary to pay attention to several points.

  • You must first install a sensor to transmit the GPS signal. Often it is mounted on the front glass to ensure high-quality communication.
  • The rear view camera is placed on the bumper or trunk. It is also necessary to take care of the safe conduct of electrical wiring to the car radio to transmit high-quality images.
  • One of the wires is connected to the reverse gear, so that the picture from the camera is automatically displayed on the screen of the radio when the gear is turned on to move backward.

Features of car radios from different manufacturers

Car radio installation is approximately the same for cars of different models. However, manufacturers of these devices offer additional options. Models of different firms differ not only in appearance. Now on sale is a wide selection of car radios, different in cost, size, and the presence of additional functions. But when choosing such a device, you need to focus not only on your preferences. The main thing is that the car radio is easily connected to the car.
