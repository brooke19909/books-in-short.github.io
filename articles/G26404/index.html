Sherwood Prize Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Antalya): description, photo, reviews of tourists

Antalya is a symbol of resort Turkey. The region annually attracts hundreds of thousands of Russian tourists. Today it is an important economic center of the country. The city is actively developing. New hotels and entertainment complexes appear on the coast. The streets are covered with open terraces. In the cafe, travelers are served fish and seafood dishes.

Looking for the sun





Sherwood Prize Hotel 3 * is a hotel located in the center of the municipality. It is located in the shade of the spreading trees of the ancient Lara street. The nearest sandy beach is a fifteen minute drive from the residential buildings. An abundance of bistros and eateries is concentrated near the hotel. Antalya's tourist quarter is only six kilometers away.

The main attraction of the suburbs is Duden Waterfall. It is located just 4 km. The road from Antalya International Airport takes about ten minutes by taxi or bus.

Reviews





The Russians were pleased with the location of the hotel. It is located almost in the center of Antalya. Buses run to the beach. But questions arose about the quality of service. The maid was present only from 09:00 to 16:00. If in the evening you need clean linen or a dry towel, the employees will not be able to help. The utility block is locked.

At Sherwood Prize Hotel 3 * fined for any misconduct. Pieces of cheese taken from a common table cost travelers 150 rubles. The thing is that breakfast was not included in the price of their stay. For hot drinks, too, had to pay extra. At the same time, tea and coffee were greatly diluted with water.





But the guests were satisfied with the work of the cleaners. The rooms were cleaned every day, vacuumed, mopped floors. The public areas were clean. The lobby is cozy. The rooms had a picturesque view of the Mediterranean Sea.

Vacationers were saddened by the lack of a playground on the territory. At the same time, the hotel positions itself as a family hotel. Plus, not all employees were benevolent. Some did not even speak English. Some of the apartments are in need of repair.
