When a holiday is at home: how to make flower balls with your own hands

Decorating an apartment for some kind of celebration, you often want to come up with something unusual, original and pleasant. Such details, which would emphasize the festivity of the moment, brought joyful emotions and were remembered for a long time. On New Year's Day you can decorate your home with garlands and rain, for Christmas - with wreaths of fir branches with oranges and bells. And birthday, another wedding anniversary, the arrival of a beloved relative - what to do in such situations? Various kinds of crafts will be the creative solution.

Ball colored



A wonderful fresh idea is to make balls of flowers. Do it yourself, of course, but with the involvement of all or almost all of the household. In order to experience the joy of creativity and the anticipation of surprise together: the sensations will be brighter and crafts will be ready faster. And the rooms decorated in this way will dramatically change, become more elegant, more comfortable, more joyful. So, we are taken for the balls of flowers. With your own hands you can make them both from living plants, and from paper. For example, from soft corrugated or thicker colored paper. Silk or satin ribbons, multi-colored braid, beads and rhinestones, other jewelry will be good as accessories. As improvised items you will need the ball-base itself, paper-corrugation, PVA-glue, scissors, harsh threads or ribbons for hanging. So:

  1. First you need to craft the flower balls themselves. Take an ordinary children's rubber ball, but not very small. It needs to be pasted over with paper (old newspapers) in 8-9 layers. Then carefully divide the newspaper ‚Äúdoll‚Äù into 2 hemispheres with a sharp knife, drill a small hole in the center of one of them and pull the thread or ribbon. Now glue the halves again. Similarly, do as much as you need in quantity, the basis for the balls of flowers. With your own hands, and with the help of your relatives, you will make them quickly and a lot.
  2. From corrugation, make roses, daisies and other flowers that you can. From time to time, try on the base to get the right amount. You can combine different types of paper: velvet, plain color. It is recommended to stick beads, strasses, circles of shiny confetti on the petals. Thanks to such combinations, your flower balls will be more effective. Do it yourself, even if they do not meet factory standards, they will retain your warmth and lively positive energy.
  3. Stick your flower blanks on the ball itself, tightly so that the base shines through as little as possible. Suspend the finished craft in the intended place and start the next one.
  4. What else can be offered is the balloon flowers . For each you need 6 pieces of balls. It is advisable to place contrasting ones in the middle: red, yellow, blue, pink, etc. The shape is round or oval (not long). First you need to inflate by 5, trying to keep the same size. Snap them to each other by applying tails. Then tie the contrasting center. And hang from the ceiling in different places of the room.

It remains to add that it is possible to make balls from natural flowers and plastic, not just paper ones, and flowers from flexible tubular balls.
