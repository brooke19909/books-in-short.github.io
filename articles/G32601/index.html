The best hotels in Fryazino

Fryazino is a small town located just 20 km from Moscow. Residents of the metropolis come here quite often to take a break from the bustle of the city. Fresh air, quiet city streets and stunning nature create excellent conditions for relaxation. This city has long been chosen by fishermen, because the Lyuboseevka River flows here. There are enough architectural monuments, parks and squares to help diversify the leisure of guests. Hotels in Fryazino are quite diverse, but within the framework of this material we will evaluate only the best of them. Guest reviews will help ensure the objectivity of the review and evaluate the conditions that each of them is ready to offer its visitors.

"Town"

I would like to start the review from the Gorodok hotel. It is located in the central part of the city, which is attractive to most visitors. Fryazino hotels (Moscow region) are mostly small, but offer excellent conditions for accommodating tourists with various incomes.





A choice of vacationers are offered rooms of various price categories. Each of them has a set of modern furniture, a TV, air conditioning, a private bathroom with a set of towels and necessary cosmetics. The rooms are small but bright and comfortable. On the territory of the hotel in Fryazino "Gorodok" there is high-speed wireless Internet available to guests absolutely free of charge. For an extra fee, guests can order meals.

The cost of daily living in a hotel starts from 1400 rubles, which is very democratic by Moscow standards.

Address: Fryazino, st. Institutskaya, 12.

Fryazino-M

Looking at hotels in Fryazino (Moscow region), I would like to pay attention to such urban options. Hotel Fryazino-M is located in a residential building, which does not affect the level of comfort provided. This option is mainly aimed at young people, while each visitor will be able to find for themselves the number of the desired category.





Clean, comfortable and bright rooms with a set of necessary modern furniture, with a private bathroom - this is exactly what you need for an overnight stay. The only thing visitors complain about is not enough good sound insulation, sometimes it is quite noisy here. But if such a feature does not bother you at all, feel free to book a room.

The cost of a daily stay in this hotel starts from 1600 rubles.

Address: Fryazino, st. Field, 15.

"Pinery"

A striking representative of a country hotel in Fryazino is the Sosnovy Bor Hotel. It is located 12 km from the city on the territory of the Grebnensky forestry, which makes it so unique. The opportunity to spend several days of your vacation surrounded by forests, breathe fresh air, walk along forest paths is a luxury for the residents of a metropolis, for which they are ready to travel sometimes considerable distances.





The hotel covers an area of ​​7.5 hectares. Here is the main building, a swimming pool, a cottage with a sauna and its own font, several barbecue pavilions, walking paths and places for outdoor recreation. If you want to have a great time in the company of friends and family, enjoy the delights of a country holiday, Sosnovy Bor (hotel in the city of Fryazino) will be an excellent option for you. Few people know that until 2011 the sanatorium was located here, only a few years ago it was converted into a hotel. Against the background of comfortable living conditions, all visitors get access to the pool, sauna, and can attend massage sessions.

The cost of daily living in a room with modern furniture, appliances and a private bathroom is from 3200 rubles. Rooms should be booked in advance, as this hotel is very popular among Muscovites.

Address: Fryazino, village Saburovo.

"On the Field"

Apart-hotel "On Field" is not the most popular option in the city, but has the right to exist. This is an apartment converted into a hotel. There is a living room, a private bathroom and a kitchen where guests can prepare meals. This option is most often chosen by those city guests who come here for a few days.





Guest reviews of the apartments focus on fresh repairs, modern furniture, working appliances, which allows you to fully enjoy the comfort of your stay. And of course, for settlement you do not have to provide your passport details and be closely monitored by the hotel administration.

Address: Fryazino, st. Field, 7.

"Moscow region"

And here is another worthy option - the hostel "Moscow region". Currently, it is such a format of hotels that is very popular, which allows for a nominal fee to remove a bed in a room.





Fresh repairs, modern furniture, new textiles, a fridge, a TV and an electric kettle are standard in-room amenities. The bathroom and toilet are shared, but they have all the necessary conditions for a comfortable reception of the necessary hygiene procedures. You can rent a bed in a hostel for only 800-1000 rubles. You can safely say that this is the most budget option of living.

Address: Fryazino, Prospect Mira, 18.

To summarize

We presented to your attention the best hotels in Fryazino. All of them are so different that each guest of the city can easily choose the option that will satisfy all his requirements and wishes. If you want to spend time outside the city, enjoy the beauty of landscapes, clean air - pay attention to the Sosnovy Bor suburban hotel. A hostel and hotel of a city type is an excellent option for those who do not plan to spend all their free time in a room, but want to live near the city center and have access to all its attractions and entertainment venues. Apartments will surely appeal to those who come on a business trip or stay.

We strongly recommend booking hotels in Fryazino in advance, especially since this can be done in a few minutes. All contact phone numbers can be found on the official websites of the hotels .

We wish you a pleasant stay in Fryazino near Moscow and sincerely hope that our tips and tricks will help you make the right choice.
