Club Titan Hotel 4 * (Alanya, Turkey): description, service, reviews

One of the colorful and picturesque places of Turkey is Alanya. It combines centuries-old history with the modern hotel industry, vibrant night club life and relaxation in secluded bays of the coast. The Club Titan Hotel, which has four stars, offers comfortable accommodation, a high level of service and rich entertainment for everyone who decides to settle here.





general characteristics

The park zone is endowed with amazing beauty: alleys are paved everywhere, the aroma of fragrant flower beds spreads everywhere, tropical palm trees create shady spaces, rescuing holiday-makers from the sun during the hot summer days. Green lawns and shrubs, plant compositions and architectural ensembles complement every open and free corner of the hotel. Last renovation made in 2016.

A considerable 135 km separate this establishment from the international airport of Antalya. Despite this, the annual influx of tourists is constantly growing. It will be comfortable for both young people and families with children, elderly people and people with disabilities. For the latter, special apartments are equipped, the equipment of which will make their stay more complete.

Pets are excluded. Smoking is not allowed in the rooms. Employees speak three international languages.





Accommodation

The design of the proposed apartments is an amazing combination of echoes of an original traditional surroundings with modernity. Their total number is 161 units, and they are represented by the following categories:

  • Standard Room.
  • Family Room.

They differ in capacity, area and views opening from the window. Standard rooms are one-room, while family rooms combine two rooms connected by an adjacent door.

The interior has a unique design in a classic style. The walls are decorated with paintings. The flooring is made of ceramic tiles. The color scheme is a combination of green and blue tones. The walls and ceiling are white, which visually increases the dimensions of the rooms. Each room has a balcony or terrace equipped with exclusive furniture.

Tourists leave grateful reviews in which they praise the cleanliness and order of the hotel. Maids are cleaning every day, and do it responsibly. Room service is paid extra.





Accommodations

According to tourists in their reviews, to ensure a comfortable stay in the apartment has everything you need. In the absence of anything, it is enough to inform the administration about it, and the staff will do everything possible to meet the needs of each guest. Individual air conditioning creates a favorable microclimate. The TV allows you to diversify the evenings spent in the walls of the chambers. There is a telephone and a safe. The latter is leased upon payment of the appropriate amount. The minibar is filled with drinking water upon check-in. Wi-fi is paid.

The furniture combines wrought iron elements with a tree in the color of sea waves and is represented by soft beds, an antique wardrobe, chairs, bedside tables and a table.

The bathroom contains a shower, washbasin, hairdryer and mirror. There is a set of personal hygiene products and a set of towels.





Nutrition

Club Titan Hotel 4 * reviews the positive plan very often gets for catering. The hotel operates on an all-inclusive basis. Meals are in the form of a buffet. Everyone has the right to independently choose their favorite dishes and to independently form their portion. As many tourists note in their reviews, the interior of the main restaurant is modest, but cozy. The cuisine is mainly local and international. In the diet of vacationers there are all kinds of dishes: from meat to desserts. About food tourists respond very positively. They argue that it is difficult to stay hungry here. The food is tasty and nutritious. There are indoor and outdoor terraces, the total capacity of which is designed for 300 people.

You can quench the feeling of hunger during the day at the beach bar, which offers hamburgers, salads, spaghetti, pastries and other snacks. Sweets are served at certain hours. Turkish coffee, elite alcohol - for a fee.





Beach

Distance from Club Titan Hotel 4 * (Alanya) to the coast is 150 meters. The sand and pebble beach is equipped with dressing cabins, showers, umbrellas and sunbeds with soft bedding. The entrance to the crystal clear Mediterranean Sea is gentle. To diversify the time spent here, you can play volleyball on the equipped field, riding a scooter or parachuting after a boat.

Aquazone

Maintaining great shape is greatly influenced by swimming. The improvement of the musculoskeletal system, the development of the muscular system and the improvement of blood circulation are possible due to the presence of a fresh water pool, the depth of which is 180 meters. In its middle is an island with a palm tree, to which a bridge leads. Nearby there is a children's aquazone with a depth of 60 meters. This mutual arrangement allows the children to be under the control of their parents.

An open area for swimming with sunbeds and umbrellas is surrounded. Here you can play water polo or do water aerobics. Uncomplicated exercises from instructors will help to start the day in a good mood and in a cheerful mood, and if necessary, a couple of extra pounds will disappear.





Children's leisure

The Club Titan Hotel 4 * hotel complex, the description of which is presented to your attention in the article, is a great place to relax with the kids. There is everything for every kid to be delighted, smiling and happy. Visiting the kids club is available in the age group from 4 to 12 years. A qualified teacher supervises the order and organizes the leisure of young guests. Drawing, painting, board games, making crafts are just a few things to do here. Sports and contests, active games and watching cartoons in the company of new friends are what will bring new emotions and unforgettable memories to children.

In the park area there is a playground equipped with swings, slides and other equipment, which allows kids to develop physically. It is surrounded by deciduous trees, so it is in the shade.

Feeding chairs in the restaurant - in the public domain. Manege in the room is installed on demand. Using the nanny service, parents entrust their child to a professional teacher, which allows them to fully enjoy a carefree vacation.





Entertainment

Boredom and monotony will not overtake a single guest of the Hotel Club Titan Hotel 4 * (Turkey) , because the range of entertainment offered is quite large. Billiards, table tennis and darts will allow everyone to compete in the skill of accuracy. There is a football field and bike rental. For lovers of constant physical activity, the opportunity to go to the fitness room, which will help maintain beautiful body shape and grace of the figure.

Such institutions as a sauna, a bathhouse, a hammam will be no less entertaining and useful for the body. The spa provides a wide selection of diverse wellness treatments with the aim of improving health, rejuvenating the skin and gaining new strength for future achievements. Massage will give the highest degree of pleasure and relaxation.

Evenings are varied thanks to the fun shows of the animation team. Colorful dances, funny skits, tricks and performances of acrobats are just a few that awaits guests before bedtime. The day ends with a disco, the entrance to which is free, or with a Turkish night.

Additional services

For guests of the Club Titan Hotel All Inclusive 4 * hotel, the opportunity to rent a car is provided, which will allow you to make fascinating excursions to local beauties with comfort and a roof over your head. Doctor's services are paid. Laundry and dry cleaning services are available at the 24-hour front desk. Capture excellent vacation shots and enthusiastic emotions will help a professional photographer. Money exchange is available at the currency exchange office.

Transfer in both directions from the airport is carried out by experienced drivers who will promptly deliver guests to the designated point.

For negotiations, you can use the conference room, which has modern finishes and the necessary equipment.

In general, we can conclude that the Hotel Club Titan Hotel 4 * service offers a high level. Its tourists recommend their acquaintances and friends as an accommodation option when visiting Alanya. Have a nice rest!
