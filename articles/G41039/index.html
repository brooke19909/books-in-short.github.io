Hotel "Arcadia" (Sochi): reviews and photos of tourists

Warm clear sea, gentle sun, picturesque nature and healing climate - all these factors are concentrated in the resorts of Greater Sochi. That is why every year millions of Russians go on vacation to these magnificent places where sea and mountain air merge together and have a beneficial effect on human health.

Resorts in Greater Sochi are very diverse. From the Adler region to Lazarevsky, for one hundred and fifty kilometers along the Black Sea, there are numerous coastal cities and quiet villages that are warmly ready to welcome vacationers all year round.

Anchor Slit

In a quiet environmentally friendly place, in the Lazarevsky district, near the seashore, surrounded by majestic mountains, there is a cozy village with an interesting name Anchor Slot.





Unique landscapes surround this wonderful place. Mountains protect the village from the cold north winds. They are covered with evergreen subtropical forests, numerous mountain streams and waterfalls create a beautiful landscape.

A mixture of sea and mountain air in combination with volatile tropical trees creates a climate that favorably affects the health of people of different ages. Just being here in this atmosphere, you can get rid of numerous ailments and strengthen the immune system.

Holidays in the village of Yakornaya Shchel are preferable to families with children, middle-aged people or those tourists who are tired of noisy cities and simply want to enjoy the sound of the surf, the gentle warm sea and be alone with nature.

For carefree young people who can not imagine their vacation without noisy nightclubs and parties, the village of Yakornaya Shchel is not quite suitable, because there is calm and tranquility. However, there are some good discos here!

Anchor Slot is a small village, but there is a lot of housing here. In addition to several large pensions, rooms are rented in almost every courtyard of the private sector. Private hotels, guest houses and mini-hotels are popular in these places.

How to get there

To get to the Hotel Arcadia in the Anchor Slit, you can use the shuttle service organized by the hotel for a fee.

If you flew to Sochi by plane to Adler Airport, then you need to take a bus or minibus at number 105 and get to the bus station in Sochi.

From the bus station or from the railway station of the city of Sochi there is a bus number 155, which goes to the village of Yakornaya Shchel.

You can also get on the train, in the village there is a railway station.

Hotel "Arcadia", Sochi: reviews of vacationers

Reviews and reviews of hotel guests are the best way to tell us about all the advantages and disadvantages of this place of rest.

There are a lot of opinions about the Arcadia hotel on the forums, which undoubtedly pleases, as it follows that the hotel is popular.

The very location of the hotel and the village cause conflicting comments. Tourists who had an idea of ‚Äã‚Äãthe village and its surroundings, admire the silence and peace. Other vacationers write that in the Anchor Slit there is absolutely nowhere to go, the village is boring and uninteresting.

Fans of a relaxing holiday, as expected, were satisfied with the situation in the village. There is everything necessary for holidaymakers: shops are close, the market is full of fresh fruits, numerous souvenirs, sellers of excursions and several cozy cafes.

The territory of the hotel itself did not satisfy all visitors. Many expected something more. According to hotel guests, the territory is very small.

The rooms do not cause any particular complaints, but the unanimous opinion of almost all vacationers is that there is no refrigerator in the room. Its presence on the floor causes outrage.

Reviews about the children's room are not good. Advertising indicates the presence of games and entertainment, but in fact, in addition to children's drawings, there is practically nothing in it.

The beach of the village is also not to everyone's liking. The promised beach infrastructure did not meet the expectations of many tourists, but a huge amount of free places is an undoubted plus.





For some reason, the promised massage parlor and gym are not all clients found.

Regarding the food in the hotel dining room, reviews are almost all negative. Many customers did not like the dishes.

Hotel guests in their reviews say that the staff is not always friendly, on the contrary, often rude to visitors. Many clients had problems with checking in and booking. Upon arrival at the hotel, the reserved rooms were occupied, and the hotel simply shrugged. Such flaws are very unpleasant.

There are no complaints about cleaning the rooms. The rooms are cleaned almost every day. Linen is changed every three to four days.

Analysis of reviews will highlight the main advantages and disadvantages of the hotel.
