Find out where the best beach vacation in Italy is, spend an unforgettable vacation!

This is surprising, but not a single world nation has succeeded in achieving such success in the ability to live for pleasure as the Italians. Bored is a real crime while traveling in this country, because rest here is an immortal classic, where it is better to have a beach holiday in Italy, offering a wide variety of resorts from where you can visit the great ancient cities.

Arriving at these places, along with other chugging tourists, you can back up the Leaning Tower of Pisa in some original way, bargain with Italians in the Florentine market, and then go to the beaches of the beautiful Tuscan, Amalfi Coast or Lagurian Riviera. It is quite difficult to say where a beach vacation in Italy is better, because the tourist opportunities provided in the country are endless, but several places can be distinguished.

Lagurian Riviera



The pristine nature, the rocky shores breaking to the sea, forming wonderful bays, majestic mountain peaks - this is what attracts the Italian Riviera. The incomparable beauty of the Ligurian coast is conditionally divided into two parts: the Riviera di Levante and the Riviera di Ponente. The first part is famous for the charming resort of Portofino, where it is better to spend a beach holiday in Italy not only on the coast, but also in a national park. Fish abound here, and tourists can easily see dolphins. The Riviera di Ponente is primarily known for the resort of San Remo, where the Italian Song Festival is held. Following him is Rapallo - a large and affordable resort, where the cleanest pebble beaches, azure-colored water and amazingly clean air attract tourists every year.

Tuscan coast



It is these places that are so famous for ancient cities: Livorno, Pisa, Florence, Siena. If a tourist is interested not only to enjoy the sea, but also to learn more about the history of Italy, then the Tuscan coast is a place where the best beach vacation in Italy is incomparably combined with excursions and tours. The coast reaches a length of 20 km, surrounded by pine forests and is called the Versilian Riviera. The main resorts are: Forte dei Marmi, Viareggio, Punta Ala and Livorno.

Amalfi Coast



It is especially difficult to decide where the best beach vacation in Italy is, having visited the cities of Sorrento, Positano and Amalfi. They are located on the Amalfi coast, which is one of the most fabulous, beautiful and unique. There is always a fresh sea wind blowing, the waves are picturesquely breaking on the rocks, and the local landscape and vegetation amaze with a riot of bright colors.

Adriatic coast



Here are located the most popular resort Italian regions. From here it is extremely convenient to go on excursions to Bologna, Venice and other famous cities. Amusement parks located nearby will bring special pleasure to lovers of water attractions. Tourists often enjoy a vacation in the ancient Roman city of Rimini, on sandy beaches. Vacationers who appreciate a quiet pastime, enjoy the music will like Pesaro, where various concerts are regularly held, and August is the time of the Opera Festival. Cattolica is the place where you can magically spend a beach holiday in Italy with children, because the entrance to the water is gentle, on the beaches there is coarse sand. Immerse yourself in a fairy tale, feel the magic and magic of Italy!
