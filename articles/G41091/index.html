Camellia Nha Trang 2 * Hotel (Vietnam, Nha Trang): overview, rooms and reviews of tourists

Camellia Nha Trang 2 * Hotel - two hotels are located under this name, which are located in the very popular Vietnamese resort Nha Trang among Russian tourists. In common parlance, these are the Kamelia 1 and Kamelia 2 hotels.

general information

They can not be called beach, because both are located in the city, a few streets from the sea. But, nevertheless, they offer tourists who come on vacation good housing, service and food for a price that does not bite at all. Probably, therefore, some tour operators advertise them just for spending time at sea.

"Camellia 2" - the hotel Camellia 2 Hotel Nha Trang 2 * - has two and a half stars according to the generally accepted classification, so it can be found under the guise of both "kopecks" and "three rubles". Recently it was renamed so that there was no confusion, and it is called The World Hotel.

Of course, you will not see here magnificent palaces, parks and a vast territory. Everything is budget and compact, but clean and comfortable. And the money that you would pay for a stay in a more expensive hotel can be spent on travel, entertainment or shopping. Let's see what tourists say about these inexpensive options for an exotic vacation, and at the same time find out which one is more popular with travelers. It is also advisable that travelers do not confuse these two hotels, but find out which one suits them best.





Resort Center

Now let's talk about the area where both hotels are located. "Camellia 1" is located in the so-called central sector of the city. As for the Camellia 2 Nha Trang Hotel 2 * hotel (Vietnam, Nha Trang), reviews indicate that it is also not on the outskirts. But the first is still closer to the ‚Äúparty‚Äù part of the city. This is the European quarter, where literally at every step you can meet either a bar, a disco, or a night club.

There are also many hotels designed for spa guests, and a variety of shops, including jewelry. Almost everywhere there are signs in Russian. Despite the central location of the area, the beaches are clean. In the European quarter is also the Nha Trang Cathedral. Two routes of city buses go here - the second and fourth. Riding them is safe; they are comfortable, air-conditioned, with soft seats.

In some ways, the center of Nha Trang, from the point of view of our tourists, is similar to the classic Soviet resorts. Of course, you will not always walk in one European quarter. You will want to look at the Chama towers, and at the villas of Bao Dai - the last emperor, and much more. Excursion bureaus and travel companies are in the hotels themselves and in the surrounding streets. And some attractions can be reached on foot or by bus.





Location, nearby facilities

Hotel Camellia Nha Trang 2 * Hotel is in the heart of the city. Therefore, from the windows, of course, you will not see the sea. It takes forty minutes to get from Kamran Airport to it. Directly opposite the hotel is the Amart supermarket. The price there is cheaper than in the surrounding bazaars. And to get to the Dam market, you will have to drive a few stops - it will take you about ten minutes.

All the sights and iconic places of the city are very close to the hotel. Nearby and public transport. Pharmacies and a market where you can buy fruits are all within walking distance. As for the Camellia 2 Hotel Nha Trang 3 *, it is located a little closer to the beach - at a distance of about two hundred meters. But its location is not very convenient for tourists - there is little infrastructure, shops and cheap cafes. The hotel is far from the so-called European center. True, it is closer to any exotic and Dam market, where you can buy good fruits. It is within walking distance of the hotel.

Food in the "Camellia 1"

The hotel package includes breakfast. They are quite satisfying and dense, although some vacationers complain about the monotony. Guests praise the local Pho soups served in the morning. Most tourists are still happy with breakfast. Great choice - rice, pasta, bacon, vegetables, chicken, sausages, ham, fried eggs. Some guests write in reviews that this is not even breakfast, but a full dinner. There is coffee, tea, packaged juices. For dessert - pastries and two types of fruit, according to the season.

In addition, there are a lot of food outlets near this hotel where you can eat cheaply. The rooms have kettles, so in the evening you can indulge in a hot drink, if desired, or chill it later. Good dinners at the Louisiana restaurant, on the first line, and on the parallel street there is the Cosmos beer bar. There, every fourth mug is a bonus.





Services of Camellia 1

Like many Vietnamese hotels, Camellia Nha Trang 2 * Hotel provides guests with free Wi-Fi. The staff at the reception is open 24 hours a day, so whenever you come, you will be provided with a settlement. Luggage can be stored in the lobby, while the administration rents a car or bicycle. The hotel has a tour desk where you can still book tickets or transfer services. There is a currency exchange at the reception. The hotel accepts families with children of any age to stay, and lodges kids up to four years old for free.
