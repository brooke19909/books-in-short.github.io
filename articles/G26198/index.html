How to make a butterfly on the neck: two easiest ways

In the wardrobe of a stylish mod, of course, there are various ties. Among them, a special place is occupied by male butterflies around the neck, which give the appearance a special elegance, elegance and chic. But it often happens that picking up this item in a store is very difficult to find the right little thing. That color does not suit, then the style. Therefore, involuntarily, many have the question: " How to make a butterfly on the neck with your own hands?" This article presents two options for solving the problem with step-by-step guidance.





How to make a butterfly on the neck in the easiest way?

First take measurements - neck, width and length of the tie. Usually these sizes are 40-42 cm, 10-12 cm and 20-22 cm, respectively. An ordinary rectangular butterfly is sewn as follows:

  1. Cut a blank from the fabric equal to the length and double the width of the product. For a sample, for example, we take standard sizes (finished butterfly - 20x10 cm). Then, given the allowances for seams of 1 cm from all sides, you need to cut a segment 22 cm long and 22 cm wide too.
  2. Fold it in half with the front side inwards and stitch it on both sides.
  3. Turn and straighten the corners.
  4. Insert a blank of dense fabric measuring 20x10 cm.
  5. Sew the second free edge of the butterfly with neat, hidden stitches.
  6. To tighten the middle part of the tie, cut a 6x8 cm rectangle. Roll it into a tube along the length, hiding the unfinished edge inward.
  7. Put the obtained inlay in the middle of the butterfly and form beautiful folds.
  8. Fasten the hem on the wrong side with tight stitches.
  9. Cut a piece of fabric equal to the circumference of the neck and allowances for the fastener. Its width should be approximately 8-10 cm.
  10. Sew it, departing from the edge by 1 cm, and twist, getting a long ribbon.
  11. Attach the butterfly to the finished braid or elastic band.




Butterfly making without laying

Is it possible, and how to make a butterfly on the neck without the use of additional dense materials and a sewing machine? It turns out that having at hand only a piece of fabric, scissors and a needle and thread, you can build a fashion accessory in just half an hour. Note that you should not use too thin materials, such as silk. They will keep shape poorly. But velvet or satin fit perfectly. In extreme cases, take a few pieces left after sewing curtains, if they are suitable in color and have an interesting and extravagant pattern. The resulting tie will keep its shape perfectly due to the fact that when folded in several stages, it turns out to be multi-layered. And in order to put it on conveniently and quickly, sew a piece of a wide elastic band, which the collar will hide. In addition, when choosing this option, you will not need to think about the need to fasten the fastener and handle the free ends.
