Beautiful vertical knitting patterns: options and description

The most perfect of all types of needlework is knitting. Work on most products begins with the implementation of vertical patterns with knitting needles. Collars, cuffs, fringes and other details are most often knitted using a vertical pattern. Such patterns can also be the general background of the entire canvas. Vertical ornaments look great on napkins, bedspreads, rugs, cloaks and other works of skilled craftsmen.

Types of Yarn

For knitting products, it is necessary to apply certain types of yarn. To knit vertical patterns with knitting needles, for each model, depending on its purpose, you need to select the appropriate threads. For light blouses, dresses, napkins, openwork shawls, it is recommended to give preference to thin threads, for sweaters, hats, scarves and other warm things, thicker yarn is used. The following classification of threads:

  • Woolen
  • half-woolen;
  • synthetic;
  • cotton;
  • home-made wool;
  • mohair.

The necessary preparation of threads for knitting

On the eve of the purchased yarn must be prepared for work. In no case can it be immediately rewound into balls and start knitting, since the finished product will be threatened with deformation. Knitted vertical patterns after washing can stretch out a lot, and the product will become several sizes larger than expected. To prevent this from happening, you must do the following procedure:

  1. Pour water thirty-forty degrees into the container.
  2. Put soap in it and use liquid soap.
  3. Beat the foam as much as possible.
  4. Dip the threads in water and wash them gently.
  5. Washing should consist in gentle push-ups.
  6. Rinse the yarn thoroughly in warm water.
  7. Dry completely.
  8. Sew thread into balls.

Knitting from old things

You can make vertical patterns with knitting needles from old knitwear. To do this, it is necessary to separate the pockets, buttons, zippers, collars, all elements of the decor. Then spread the product at the seams to make separate parts. In each detail, find the end of the thread (it is usually located on top near the neck) and completely dissolve the knitting by winding the released threads in skeins. It is necessary to pay attention to the fact that knitwear according to the raglan type open from the bottom up, since knitting started from the collar. Wash the skeins of yarn in the same way with new threads and wind them into balls. A newly knitted model will not yield to beauty an item that was made from new yarn.

Elastic

Simple vertical knitting patterns include elastic. It is used for cuffs of sleeves and socks, the lower edge of the product and knitting the neck. Elastic band can also serve as the main pattern. It is performed by various techniques. It is proposed to consider some of the most common gum.

  1. Plain gum. It knits according to the principle: two front and two wrong loops (you can knit 1 x 1, 3 x 3, etc.). The more loops alternate, the wider the stripes. Follow the next rows as the loops look.
  2. English gum. First row knit alternately with the front and wrong loops. Start the second row with the front loop, then double crochet, remove one loop without knitting. The third and all subsequent ones should be crocheted as follows: the double crochet should be crocheted with the right crochet, then crochet again and remove one loop.
  3. French gum. This vertical knitting pattern in the description looks like this. First, knit two wrong loops, then two crossed front ones (the second loop is knitted first, and then the first). The second row begins with two crossed purl, then two facial loops are knitted. Further, the knitting pattern continues according to a similar principle.
  4. Polish gum. The first row and all subsequent odd rows alternate two front and two wrong loops. Knit even rows with two faces and one wrong side.

Track

This openwork pattern with knitting needles allows even a beginner to knit a vertical track. The pattern is used for such products as a blouse, pullover, cardigan, skirt, etc. Like all vertical stripes, the pattern can be used as a decorative addition to the main knitting. A step-by-step instruction is as follows:

Pigtail strip

A knitting pattern with vertical stripes in the form of a delicate pigtail can be used for any product. The pattern is suitable for cotton thin yarn, as well as for models designed for the cool season. The work is not particularly difficult, so it can be easily mastered even by beginners.
