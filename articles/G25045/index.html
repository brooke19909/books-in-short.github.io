Travel to Nizhny Tagil, recreation: description, services, photos

A wonderful town is Nizhny Tagil. Recreation centers invite everyone who wants to spend a good time away from the village with friends, family or just with nature. Near the city there are many beautiful institutions that are real complexes. Here are the best services. In institutions, you can not only relax and catch fish, but also rent skis and boats. If you wish, there is the opportunity to go to the bathhouse or on an excursion.

On the territory of the recreation center you can find hotels where you will be provided with comfortable rooms, as well as bars, cafes and restaurants, gyms, swimming pools.

"Taiga Ros"

The recreation center "Taiga Ros" is sustained in all traditions of Russia. There are houses of larch and pine. They are great for a full and healthy holiday. In this building, the guest's dream will be pleasant and light. These houses are famous for Nizhny Tagil. Recreation facilities always offer the most comfortable conditions.



To improve your mood, there are routes that allow you to climb the mountain, there is a rope town for children who are more interested in conquering trees, not peaks. People involved in sports are offered to rent bicycles, boats, allowing rafting on the river. In winter, hunting and cross-country skiing and snowmobiles are available for rent. There are cafes and restaurants where Russian dishes are sold. People come here not only for a good rest surrounded by the Ural nature, but also for conducting trainings and seminars.

Lukomorye

Guests can stay in comfortable rooms or in a hunting lodge. Base "Lukomorye" is located on the banks of the pond, which is located next to a city such as Nizhny Tagil. Recreation facilities in this pond are in special demand.

Various celebrations and events are often held here. The institution presented itself in the tourism sector from the best side. The total area is 2.5 hectares. There is a car park. It is provided for free and has a capacity of 20 seats.

Chauzhinka

Where is the most famous and popular recreation center (Nizhny Tagil) located? Chauzhinka is located just 30 km from the city. It is decorated in a traditional style and provides a huge range of services. It should be noted that the recreation center accepts guests all year round. Guest houses are designed for 8-20 residents. If there is extra money, then you can rent a separate room, which accommodates 3-5 people.

Each house is equipped with a lounge and kitchen, have all the amenities. In order to organize a picnic, you can use the gazebo and barbecue. This type of holiday is popular among those who live in a city like Nizhny Tagil. Recreation facilities always have barbecue facilities at their disposal. In winter, you can have a good vacation in a closed gazebo, where there is a fireplace.

On the base you can rent bicycles, boats, catamarans, snowmobiles. Not far from Chauzhinki there is an equipped beach. The minimum cost of a small house starts from 4 thousand rubles., Large - from 8 thousand rubles.
