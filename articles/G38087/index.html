Hotel Santa Helena 3 * (Greece, Rhodes): overview, description, beach, rooms and reviews

Planning a vacation trip to Rhodes (Greece)? Want to combine a rich excursion program with an excellent beach holiday? Would you like to spend too much on an expensive hotel? In this case, be sure to consider the Santa Helena 3 * hotel as a place to stay.



Location

The three-star Hotel Santa Helena is located on the Greek island of Rhodes in the village of Trianta. The distance to the sea is about one kilometer. The neighboring village of Ialyssos is 700 meters away. Here you will find several cafes, bars, shops, supermarkets, a market, tour agencies, car rental offices, etc. The hotel is 7 kilometers from the center of Rhodes . The nearest airport is four kilometers away.



Description

Santa Helena 3 * (Rhodes, Greece) was opened a long time ago. However, in 2013, a large-scale reconstruction was carried out here. So today the hotel offers its guests to stay in one of 22 comfortable and cozy rooms with all amenities. The apartments in the hotel are spacious, bright, nicely decorated. They are equipped with all necessary furniture and household appliances. Free Wi-Fi is also available. So you can always stay in touch with your friends and family who stayed at home. Rooms are cleaned every day. Sheets and towels are updated several times a week. Please note that the use of a safe and air conditioning is possible for a fee.

The hotel area is small, but very well maintained and green. On it is a residential two-story building, a restaurant, a bar, a pool, a terrace with sun loungers, a children's playground. The reception desk at Santa Helena is open 24 hours a day. You can always contact the hotel staff with any question or request. Here, at the reception, you can rent a car for trips around the island or book an excursion in the direction you are interested in.



Santa Helena 3 * (Rhodes): traveler reviews

As you know, perhaps, to everyone who has been abroad, it is very important when planning a trip not to be mistaken in choosing a hotel. After all, a hotel for a vacation becomes for us literally the second home. Moreover, this is true even if you consider yourself to be a lowly tourist category and plan to spend a minimum of time in your room. After all, each of us wants to be able to sleep on a comfortable bed in a cozy clean room, eat deliciously and count on respectful and attentive attitude from the staff.

Therefore, experienced travelers are advised, long before the expected date of the trip, to start collecting information about the hotels of the place where you plan to spend your vacation. Moreover, attention should be paid not only to the official description of the hotels and the advice of tour operators, but also to the reviews left by tourists who have already been here. After all, former guests who have experienced all the pros and cons of a particular hotel, try to share their experience with those who are just planning their vacation.

Thanks to the study of reviews, you will have a more complete and close to the real state of affairs picture of what to expect from a particular hotel. Thus, you will be able to understand whether a particular hotel is suitable for you or whether it is worth continuing to search, so as not to be disappointed in the choice made later.

Today we offer to find out together what impressions our compatriots have left after several days, or even weeks, in Santa Helena 3 *. Reviews of tourists from Russia and other countries of the former USSR, it should be noted, for the most part are positive. Former guests are also ready to advise this hotel to those who plan to stay in Rhodes. But we learn more about everything.

Rating

The average rating for a three-star Santa Helena hotel based on guest ratings is 4 out of a possible five. This suggests that almost all travelers did not regret choosing this hotel. The highest ratings were awarded to room equipment and staff work. Some dissatisfaction was expressed about the remoteness of the hotel from the sea and the monotonous breakfasts.



Location, Territory

According to tourists, Santa Helena Hotel 3 * is located in a calm rural area, away from noisy bars and discos. This is definitely a plus for those who want to be able to take a nap during the day and get a good night's sleep. At the same time, some travelers complained that the distance to the nearest beach is about one kilometer. If this moment is important for you, then perhaps you should pay attention to another hotel. Most of the guests of "Santa Helena" location were quite satisfied. According to them, it‚Äôs even nice to walk to the sea on foot. In addition, tourists note that the hotel is very successful in getting to various attractions of Rhodes. Many vacationers rented a car for several days and independently explored the island, visiting various interesting places and beaches.

As for the territory of the hotel, it is small, but, according to tourists, very well-groomed, green and comfortable. It has an outdoor pool, a terrace for sunbathing with sun loungers, a bar, a children's playground.



Housing stock

Almost all the guests, judging by the comments they left, were very pleased with the 3 * rooms they had proposed at Santa Helena Hotel. So, they are quite spacious, with a pleasant design, comfortable and high-quality furniture and uninterruptedly working appliances. The apartments have everything you need: a TV, a telephone, a hairdryer, air conditioning, a safe, a kettle and a minimum set of dishes, a bathroom, a balcony. Please note that beach towels are not provided here. Therefore, it makes sense to bring them with you or buy on arrival at one of the local stores. Towels for the bathroom can not be removed from the room. As for the shortcomings, some guests expressed dissatisfaction with the fact that the use of air conditioning must be paid extra (about 6 euros per day). However, experienced travelers note that this is a fairly common practice in economy hotels throughout Europe, so there is nothing much to be surprised about. By the way, the use of a safe is also possible at an additional cost.



Nutrition

The cost of living in Santa Helena Hotel 3 * (Rhodes) includes breakfast. They pass in a cafe at the hotel. According to the guests, in the morning you can eat here with bacon, cheese, toast, croissants, eggs, vegetables, granola, juice, milk and coffee. In general, there are no complaints about the quality of the products, but the breakfasts are the same every day. Therefore, those who live in the hotel for more than a week, sometimes prefer to go to the nearest bakery or supermarket in the morning to slightly diversify their diet.

You can also have a snack in the hotel cafe during the day, ordering pizza, a burger, a sandwich, nuggets, french fries, etc. There is also a bar with soft and alcoholic drinks.

According to tourists, 600 meters from the "Santa Helena" there are several cafes with excellent cuisine and reasonable prices. Many enjoyed lunch and dinner there.



Entertainment

Since Santa Helena is a very small hotel, there is no special entertainment program for guests here. The hotel has a swimming pool and sun terrace. Here you can relax if you do not want to go to the beach, or cool off after a day on sightseeing trips. For young travelers, a small playground is equipped in the hotel garden.

In general, according to tourists, the main attractions in Rhodes are beach vacations and sightseeing. Many people advise moving around the island on their own by renting a car. Of course, you can order a guided tour. However, in this case, you will not be able to adjust the route and stop where you want, if this is not included in the travel program.

Sea

As already mentioned, the distance from Santa Helena 3 * to the nearest beach is about a thousand meters. On the road, vacationers took about 20 minutes. As for the beach, it is in municipal ownership here. Sun loungers and parasols are available at an additional cost. If you wish, you can always sit absolutely free on towels or a mat brought with you on a site free of deckchairs. A local umbrella is also available at local stores. Near the beach there are several cafes and bars where you can have a snack during the day and enjoy a drink. Many have free Wi-Fi.
