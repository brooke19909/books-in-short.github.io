Nam Hung 3 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang): hotel infrastructure, room description, service

The attractiveness of tourist Vietnam has already been appreciated by our compatriots. For some time now this direction has been very much sought after by Russians.

Why it is worth having a rest in Vietnam

There are many reasons for this. Firstly, Vietnam, in comparison with other Southeast Asian countries, has a much more developed infrastructure. Secondly, tourists can combine pastime on the beach here with educational excursions. In a word, Vietnam can give a good rest.



Thirdly, for Russians this area is attractive due to its relative cheapness. In local restaurants and cafes it is quite possible to inexpensively and, most importantly, eat deliciously. The most popular of the Vietnamese resorts is Nha Trang. Locals call him - Nha Trang. This place has been a great attraction for a long time. Nha Trang is perfect for both lovers of outdoor activities, combining a visit to the beach with sightseeing, and those who prefer a more measured relaxing holiday, including with young children.

Three Star Resort Hotels

There is enough entertainment in Nha Trang: there are many bars, restaurants and cafes, discos, you can visit massage parlors. And, of course, Nha Trang offers tourists a huge selection of hotels - from incredibly cheap hostels to elegant five-star apartments.

Among them, Nam Hung Hotel occupies its worthy place. The 3-star category assigned to this hotel is fully consistent with the level of service it offers. In general, in Vietnam, hotels of a higher category class prevail. However, in recent years, more and more three-star hotels have begun to appear, which tour operators may well recommend to their tourists.

According to their level of service and room equipment, Vietnamese hotels are most consistent with Thai counterparts. Choosing a hotel to stay in this country, do not look at the classification in force in Turkey or Egypt. In any Vietnamese hotel, the service is much higher, and regardless of its "star". Most local hotels include only breakfast in the price, including Nam Hung Hotel 3 *.



Description

Nam Hung Hotel is located in the center of Nha Trang. There are many shops and cafes nearby. Nearby - four hundred meters away - there is a sailing club and "April 2 Square". Within walking distance are the famous Tram Huong Tower and the huge night market. From Nam Hung 3 * to the famous Nha Trang beach can be reached in just two minutes. Public parking is available near the hotel. The nearest international airport (Cam Ranh) is only thirty kilometers away.

In the immediate vicinity of the Nam Hung 3 * hotel there are many inexpensive cafes, souvenir shops and grocery stores. Three kilometers away is the beautiful Winperl Park. Almost at the same distance, within walking distance is the Long Son Pagoda. Hotel address: 18A Biet Thu, Nha Trang, Nam Hung (3 *).

Hotel infrastructure

Nam Hung has a small conference room equipped with audio and visual equipment. For seminars and conferences, here you can rent any necessary equipment, including fax, computer or laptop, as well as a projector and a screen for presenting presentations. The hotel and its small territory are fully guarded. Reception is open around the clock. Smiling receptionists immediately draw up documents and hand in room keys. If you wish, you can also arrange a transfer here both to the airport and to any other place. At the information desk you can use the depository safe and left-luggage office.

The hotel has a tour desk and currency exchange in the lobby. Air and train tickets are also offered here. Those who wish can receive express check-in and check-out for a fee. However, this service is rarely used by anyone, since in almost all Vietnamese hotels, paperwork is very fast. Nam Hung 3 * is no exception.



Description of rooms

The hotel has sixty eight comfortable beautifully decorated rooms of the following categories: Premier Suite, Superior, Family, Senior and Executive Deluxe. Their area ranges from twenty-one to forty square meters.

The rooms are furnished with new comfortable furniture. They have single and double beds, a wardrobe, armchairs, a large mirror, bedside tables, a desk with chairs. Free residents can use the coffee or tea set. The number of beds and their type depends on the room category. Extras include free bathrobes and slippers.

The rooms are also equipped with a flat-screen TV and DVD player, air conditioning, telephone and a built-in safe for a fee. The service offered in Nam Hung 3 * also includes wireless Internet and an alarm clock service. There are no balconies in front of the rooms. The hotel offers city views.

Bathrooms have a bathtub and shower. Free hairdryer, toiletries, towels. Cleaning and linen change are regular.



Nutrition

The Nam Hung 3 * hotel offers the following schemes: breakfast only (BB) and full board (FB). Residents can visit the √† la carte restaurant or enjoy set lunches. On the territory of the hotel there is a bar that can only be used by residents of Nam Hung 3 *. Au Lac Restaurant offers local and international dishes upon reservation. Most tourists buy tours to this hotel with the continental breakfast concept, since they prefer to eat in inexpensive cafes nearby at a convenient time for them.

Judging by the reviews, right in front of the hotel, literally across the road, there is a very large and inexpensive restaurant. Here, food is served in a buffet style. In just a few thousand Vietnamese dongs you can eat a variety of seafood.

Beach

The hotel is located in one of the most beautiful lagoons of the coast according to UNESCO. The distance to the bathing area is only one hundred meters from Nam Hung 3 * (Nha Trang). Vietnam, in general, is famous for its sandy beaches. The world famous Nha Trang is no exception. This municipal beach in recent years has been completely renovated and equipped. Today it meets the highest criteria. The entrance to the sea is convenient - gentle. Coating - fine white sand. Umbrellas, sunbeds and mattresses can be obtained here by paying separately.

Tourists who are going to buy a tour to this hotel should consider that take into account that from the beginning of November to February in this part of Vietnam an unfavorable swimming season begins. Rescuers are not allowed to enter the water due to high waves.



Additional Information

Nam Hung 3 * (Nha Trang, Vietnam) many tourists choose in order to hold their wedding event in it. The celebration is organized by highly qualified specialists. Newlyweds who come to Nam Hung 3 * for a honeymoon receive a compliment from the administration.

A small pool is located on the territory of Nam Hung. Around him are free sunbeds.

Entertainment

It offers residents a gym and a tour desk working in the lobby of Nam Hung 3 *. Entertainment in this hotel is not numerous, which is probably why elderly tourists, couples traveling with children and business guests often choose it for leisure. The composition of the residents is mixed. Our compatriots Asians and Europeans mainly come here.

But on the beach for lovers of outdoor activities offers a wide variety of entertainment. Those who like to go scuba diving can use the services of specialized diving centers, including those aimed at beginners. A thirty-minute drive from the hotel is the cable car, which runs directly above the sea. On it you can get to the huge water park Winterland, which has many water attractions. Many specially come to Nha Trang to visit this park, its aquarium.



Excursions

The resort has two mineral springs and a mud bath. Guests can rent bicycles in the lobby to explore all local attractions on their own. Convenient location is one of the advantages of Nam Hung 3 * hotel. The reviews of our compatriots indicate that in Nha Trang there are a sufficient number of attractions that are worth seeing.

Tourists are required to buy tours to the towers of the Ponagar Cham, built at the end of the thirteenth century, to the Longshon Pagoda, behind which on the top of a green hill a huge Buddha sits on a lotus flower.

You can go to the largest and most visited by Europeans Cho Dam market. At this bazaar you can buy almost everything, of course, after bargaining.

A short drive from the hotel is Cape Hong Chong or, as it is also called, the Stone Garden. This huge pile of unusual boulders is still of unknown origin, and therefore is considered one of the amazing creations of nature.



Who is this hotel suitable for?

This inexpensive hotel is suitable for those travelers who are not going to lie in their rooms all day holding a TV remote control ... That is how our compatriots speak of the hotel. And although for children, in addition to an extra bed, nothing else is provided for in the infrastructure, however, families often come here. Of course, lovers of luxurious and pathos apartments are unlikely to like it, but you should be aware that the affordable cost of living in a three-star hotel cannot match the service and amenities that local luxury hotels have.

Reviews

Our compatriots speak differently about Nam Hung 3 *. In general, many Russians note the good work of the staff, the daily change of towels, the updating of toiletries and water in the room. However, in some reviews you can see dissatisfaction with the occasional mopping and changing of bed linen. However, almost everything confirms one thing: almost at the first request, the staff of this hotel does all the necessary work. In many reviews, Russians respond with great warmth to the hotel owner and the reception desk staff. Always smiling, friendly, they, although they understand only English, readily solve all problems that arise.

Our compatriots note the presence of many shops around the Nam Hung Hotel. As for nutrition, many people say about breakfast that they, although dense, are pretty monotonous. In addition, some Russians write that they often encountered in the restaurant during dinner with a huge number of Chinese who do not live in the hotel. There are also unfavorable reviews about unsuccessful settlement, or rather, about their unjustified hopes.

Several people express dissatisfaction with the fact that they were accommodated in rooms facing the walls of the neighboring hotel. It should be borne in mind that Nam Hung is not located near the shore, therefore, such situations should be calculated in advance, given this factor when booking. Most of the windows have city views.

There are also reviews of those people who come to this hotel for the first time. Few people note a deterioration in service or an outdated housing stock. The rooms have everything you need for a comfortable stay.
