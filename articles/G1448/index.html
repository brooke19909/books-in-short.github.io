Its main advantages are high functionality and ease of use. Simply plug the appliance into a power outlet, put food in, press a couple of buttons and wait for it to complete. Cooking and defrosting foods

Another mode that is present in most microwave ovens is defrosting. Previously, the products we got from the freezer could melt for hours. For example, a large piece of meat went through a defrosting process for more than one hour. In the microwave, this can be done in just half an hour.

Such a household appliance is not expensive, very compact, and with proper use it can last you for many years. You can learn about precautions when using a microwave oven from the instructions, and we want to tell you about objects that should not be placed in this device.

List of items that should not be put in the microwave

Firstly, do not put eggs in the microwave. It does not matter what they will be: chicken, goose or quail. They simply explode inside the oven. At a minimum, particles of the egg will fly all over the device and stick. Washing them later will be quite difficult. At maximum, the appliance may open or explode. Then the parts of the eggs will fly around the room, and you can be injured by a blow. Worst of all - if the microwave explodes or lights up.

The same thing will happen with tomato, persimmon, watermelon or citrus. The juice inside is very hot, and the product itself will swell. As a result, an explosion will occur. The consequences will be the same as when placing the eggs in the microwave.

The following items from the list are marmalade, soap or chocolate. Many microwave owners try to melt these items in the oven. They will just start to bubble, smoke and burn.

Non microwave items

Discs, plastic dishes or foil. All this is contraindicated to put in the microwave. And even if the material itself from which these things are made does not spark or explode, then after being placed in the microwave they cannot be used. The disk will cease to be working, and harmful elements of plastic dishes run the risk of getting into food and causing poisoning. If you need to use the foil in the microwave for cooking, buy one that is specifically designed for this purpose.
