Park Hotel "Dream", Omsk: description and reviews

For some time now, residents of Omsk have not had to think about where to rest from the hustle and bustle without traveling far beyond the city limits. A thirty minute drive from its center is an amazing island of comfort and silence - Park Hotel Mechta. In Omsk this suburban complex is well known and loved. Most citizens come here as soon as possible. Residents of neighboring settlements also became frequent guests in the picturesque village of Chernoluchye, where the complex is located.



Park Hotel "Dream" in Omsk: description

This is a luxurious suburban complex of European class, located just thirty kilometers from Omsk, in a recreation area near the village of Chernoluchye. The complex has a developed infrastructure, a large selection of entertainment and unlimited opportunities for outdoor activities for both adult guests and children.

The administration and staff of the Mechta Park-Hotel in Omsk interestingly and cheerfully hold corporate evenings on its territory. Vacationers are especially attracted by the fact that the complex is open to visitors throughout the year.

Number of rooms

It is no secret that the key to a great holiday is coziness and comfort in the rooms. The suburban complex Park-Hotel Mechta consists of a main building, five cottages and an entertainment center. At the same time, he can take one hundred and forty vacationers.

For the guests of the complex prepared 36 comfortable rooms in the main building of the hotel, 5 apartments in detached cottages and two VIP-rooms. They are divided into categories: Medium, Mini, Standard, Standard two-room. Get to know them in more detail.

Mini

The rooms of this category have a double bed with a bedside table, a wardrobe for storage, a desk and a chair, a TV, a fridge, a minibar, and an internal telephone. All rooms have bathrooms with shower, washbasin, towels, a set of hygiene products (toothbrush, toothpaste, soap and shampoo).



The room price includes: accommodation, three meals a day according to the "buffet" system, access to the pool at any time of the day, guarded parking lot, stay of the child in the children's room, use of sports grounds (except for the tennis court).

Nutrition

There is a restaurant on site that serves a variety of dishes prepared by highly professional chefs. You will be offered a huge selection of cold snacks, hot dishes, drinks and desserts. Three meals a day are included in the price.

Here you can organize a corporate banquet or a gala dinner. This room is ideal for organizing a family celebration, buffet, anniversary or a fun children's party.



Infrastructure

In the Park-Hotel Mechta (Omsk), in addition to the restaurant, there are two banquet rooms for guests: one is designed for fifty people, and the other for one hundred and forty. Vacationers can visit a Russian bathhouse on wood with birch brooms, essential oils, an oak font with herbs. Guests can relax in the Finnish baths.

The complex has three standard saunas for 6-8 people, as well as a luxury sauna for 10-12 people. Each of them has a mini-pool and a cozy lounge with furniture made of natural wood and a TV. The hotel also has an indoor pool with a hot tub.

Visiting Rules

In the park-hotel "Dream" in Omsk, whose address is Omsk Region, the village of Chernoluchensky, st. Lesnaya, 2, there are rules that all vacationers must follow:

  • on weekends and holidays, the check-in time is fixed;
  • the import of food and drinks is prohibited on the territory of the park-hotel;
  • pets are not allowed;
  • smoking in rooms and playgrounds is prohibited.
