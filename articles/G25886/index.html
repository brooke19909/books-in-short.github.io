Sirene Beach Hotel 4 * (Greece, Rhodes): hotel description, services, reviews

Rhodes is one of the most beautiful Greek islands. And it is not surprising that it is simply very popular, including among Russian tourists. This wonderful resort offers vacationers not only beautiful beaches and clear sea, but also a very well-developed infrastructure. One of the most frequently visited resorts in Rhodes, of course, is Ixia. Here, among other things, there are many very comfortable hotels. For example, Sirene Beach Hotel 4 * (Greece) offers its guests simply excellent rooms. Rhodes - the resort is just excellent, and the tourists who rented rooms at this hotel subsequently respond about their vacation here is actually very good.

Location Features

This hotel is located in the village of Ixia, on the first coastline (35 m from the public beach). In order to get to the sea, guests of the Sirene Beach Hotel 4 * hotel just need to cross the road. Tourists who book rooms at this hotel usually fly to the island at Rhodes Airport. The distance from it to the hotel is only 12 km. Tourists can reach the hotel in just 10-15 minutes. The most convenient way to get to the hotel, many tourists consider hiring a taxi. If you wish, you can also order a transfer at the Sirene Hotel itself.





The island's capital, Rhodes , is just 3 km from the hotel. You can get here from Ixia at any time by public transport. A bus stop is in the immediate vicinity of the hotel. The distance to the harbor from the hotel is 5 km, and to the resort village of Lindos, very popular with tourists, 50 km.

General description of the hotel

Sirene Beach 4 * is a family-run, very comfortable hotel that has earned good reviews from guests. This hotel is a multi-storey building of modern architecture. The hotel area is well equipped and well-maintained. Most tourists believe that this hotel is best suited for a quiet family vacation with children. The hotel belongs to the class of four-star beach.

Smoking in the rooms of this hotel is strictly prohibited. The reception here is open around the clock, which is certainly very convenient for tourists. In most cases, vacationers characterize this hotel as rather modest, but at the same time relatively comfortable. Pets are not allowed. At the entrance, guests must leave some kind of deposit. It could be, for example, a credit card.

Number of rooms

In total, the Sirene Beach Hotel (Rhodes) offers its guests 122 comfortable clean rooms. If desired, you can rent a standard class room or family apartments.





The hotel rooms are designed for 2-4 people. The apartments in the hotel are populated by families with two children or four adults. If necessary, guests of Rhodes can also rent a single room at the Sirene Hotel. For an individual holiday, this hotel, therefore, also sounds very good.

Of course, the rooms of this four-star hotel are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay of guests. The rooms have:

  • TV;

  • telephone;

  • laptop safes and conventional;

  • refrigerators;

  • air conditioners.

Each room has a comfortable desk. Guests can use the dressing room to store outerwear. The floors in the rooms of the Sirene Beach 4 * hotel are covered with high-quality ceramic tiles, which are very comfortable to walk on.

Of course, the shower room in each room of this hotel has an individual. In the bathrooms, in addition to a set of household chemicals for bathing and towels, there are hairdryers. The hotel rooms also have small balconies equipped with outdoor furniture. If desired, you can not only dry beach items, but also admire the beautiful surroundings from here.





Guest room reviews

The rooms in the Sirene Beach Hotel 4 * (Rhodes, Ialyssos, Ixia), like herself, according to most tourists, are rather modest, but actually very comfortable. Television at this hotel is satellite, and guests can watch here, including the Russian first channel. This, many vacationers attribute, of course, to the undoubted advantages of Sirene Beach. In the evening, here you can watch some interesting television programs.

The beds in the rooms of the hotel, according to the guests, are very comfortable, and they are made with clean, well-ironed and new linens. Towels in the hotel are completely changed every day. The maids at the hotel relate to their duties very well. They do room cleaning often and quite thoroughly.

Relate to the advantages of this hotel tourists and the smooth operation of household appliances. Air conditioning in the hotel rooms, judging by the reviews of its former guests, work uninterruptedly. Tourists praise in most cases, and showers. Hot and cold water at the hotel is always there.





Infrastructure

The hotel service itself deserved very good reviews. Guests of the complex, if desired, can visit on its territory:

  • open amphitheater;

  • Internet cafe;

  • Laundry

  • minimarket.

Many tourists who have chosen the island of Rhodes as their vacation destination are looking for a hotel with a parking lot for their stay. Residents of the Sirene Beach Hotel can, among other things, rent a car at a rental point operating on its territory. Parking at this hotel has its own. Of course, the hotel also has a small outdoor pool. If you wish, tourists staying at this hotel can also play table tennis.





Services for children

As already mentioned, most tourists believe that this hotel is best for families. For the convenience of residents with children, almost everything is provided here. Guests have the opportunity, for example, to leave the child for a while with a highly qualified nanny. Of course, there is also a small, well-equipped children's playground on site. In the hotel's dining room, guests have the opportunity to use highchairs as well.

Sirene Beach Hotel 4 * (Rhodes): infrastructure reviews

Opinion actually about the service in this hotel among its guests was also very good. In particular, good reviews on the net about the pool of the Sirene Beach Hotel. The water in it is always clean. Hotel staff wash his bowl very often. The water temperature in the hotel pool, according to most guests, is comfortable. In addition, not too much chlorine is usually poured into it.

Structurally, the hotel pool is divided into two parts - deep and shallow. Therefore, swimming in it is convenient for both adults and children. The only minus of the pool at the Sirene Beach Hotel 4 * hotel guests consider the insufficient number of sun loungers installed nearby. To take one of them, you need to come to the pool early in the morning, after breakfast. Some guests also have sun loungers with umbrellas in the evening.

In addition to a good pool, the pluses of the Sirene Beach Hotel 4 * include the fact that various entertainment events are often organized here. In the evening, for example, in the hotel you can sit in the bar and listen to pleasant music. On Tuesdays, tenants are entertained by singers, and on Thursdays karaoke is available at the hotel. On Saturday, you can join the Greek night at the hotel. Reviews of all these events on the network are mostly only good. You can actually spend a lot of time in this hotel if you wish.

The main drawback of the hotel, tourists believe that for the use of safes and Wi-Fi here you need to pay a fee. Also, some guests refer to the minuses of this hotel and the fact that entertainment events on its territory are arranged too often. This may interfere with sleep for some tourists.

Dining at the hotel

Like most other four-star hotels, Sirene Beach Hotel 4 * operates on an all inclusive system . Most guests praise the dining room at Sirene Hotel . The dishes served here are enjoyed by almost all tourists. Also, the advantages of food at the hotel include the presence of a large number of fruits and vegetables on the menu. Many dishes in the hotel are suitable for children as well. The only thing, according to many guests, the menu in the dining room includes too few cereals. Adult guests are able to order in this hotel as meat dishes, and dairy or vegetable. The variety of drinks in the hotel's dining room is quite varied.






Beach Hotels

Since the Sirene Beach Hotel 4 * is located in close proximity to the sea, tourists have to go to the coastline from its territory for a very short time. In general, hotel guests, even the elderly, spend no more than five minutes on the road in an unhurried step. The only inconvenience in terms of the location of the hotel relative to the beach, its residents consider the need to cross a wide highway. Unfortunately, local motorists are not inferior to tourists going to the beach. In addition, the traffic on the highway, passing in this place parallel to the shore, is quite intense.

In most cases, guests of the Sirene Beach Hotel 4 * visit the free public beach of Ixia. According to many tourists, relaxing on it is quite convenient. The beach of the village is covered with sand mixed with small multi-colored pebbles. The infrastructure on the seashore in Ixia is very well developed. If desired, tourists can rent sun loungers and umbrellas here at a low price, dine in a cafe, visit the shower room, etc.

Actually the sea itself in this place is usually clean and calm. The water in it is not as warm as, for example, in the resorts of India or Thailand, but still has a fairly comfortable temperature. Including for children.

Available Attractions

Of course, guests of the Sirene Beach Hotel, like any other in Rhodes, have the opportunity to visit a lot of interesting places on the island. Very popular attractions located near the village of Ixia, among tourists are:

  • Palace of the Grand Masters.
  • Monastery Filerimos.
  • Rhodes Acropolis.
  • Butterfly Valley.




Guest Recommendations

For sightseeing on the island, experienced tourists are advised to rent a car at the hotel. Of course, in the village of Ixia, as elsewhere in the Greek resorts, you can buy an excursion to any interesting place. Many tourists travel to the sights of the island and simply by public transport. However, in the first case, vacationers usually have to overpay, and in the second, they spend too much time traveling. Therefore, using a rented car can actually be a very convenient way to explore Rhodes and its interesting places.
