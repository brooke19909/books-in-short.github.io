Phuket: beaches, hotels - it's hard to make a mistake in choosing

The beaches are located along the entire coastline of Phuket. The map demonstrates this fact quite colorfully. The most famous of them are comfortably located in the bays of Patong, Karon and Kata. Bangtao Beach is famous for its white sand coast. This is a more secluded and relaxing place to relax. The most populous and noisy with the most developed infrastructure is Patong Beach.

But here you can organize a two-in-one vacation: find a good quiet hotel near the vibrant center of nightlife. In turn, the beaches of Karon and Kata are considered to be elite, secluded, with slightly cleaner water than in Patong. Accordingly, they have a higher cost for living in "local" hotels.

We should also talk about hotels in Thailand. Phuket is not an exception here - sometimes a hotel even at a level of 2-3 * is far superior in quality to similar European star options. The tradition of evaluating quality by the number of stars is European. Certification in Thailand is not officially accepted.



The tour operator, at its discretion, gives the hotel stars. Therefore, a discrepancy in the assessment of the hotel base in Thailand is a common occurrence, and when choosing a hotel, you should pay more attention to the adequate reviews of customers who have been here and the advice of your travel agent.
