How it is more profitable to early repay a mortgage: methods and useful tips

Many mortgage borrowers today are trying to make as much money as possible than is generally supposed. How is it more profitable to early repay a mortgage? It is worth noting that reducing payments or deadlines in different situations has its advantages.

Basic concepts

As part of the payment of such a debt, when a person has extra money, they can be spent on early repayment. But not everything is so simple. Immediately there are a lot of questions about how best to get rid of a loan, reducing the period or amount. When is it better to do it: at the end or at the beginning? Accumulate funds and fully pay them or make small amounts? It is worth noting that the method directly depends on the specific client and his financial situation, as well as income. How confident the person is standing on his feet - this is the main question.

In almost one hundred percent of cases, Russian financial institutions with mortgages operate according to the annuity payment system. What does it mean? Is it profitable to repay a mortgage ahead of schedule in this case? The bottom line is that at first they pay mainly interest on the loan, and then - bank debt. After the prepayment procedure, the credit parameters change, as the amount of debt to the company is being reorganized. Therefore, bank employees have to redo the schedule. Offered:

  1. Reduction of the loan term within the framework of a constant monthly payment.
  2. Reduced transfers, but leave the same number of months of payments.

How to pay off a mortgage ahead of schedule? Reduction of the term or amount is better to choose? Against the background of the first option, the monthly load does not decrease in any way, and the percentage becomes lower due to the short period. The second involves lowering the monthly payment; the family is exempted from the possibly excessive load in the crisis period.

Given that the size of the payment in this case will remain the same, the amount of the overpayment of the banking organization for the loan will become less.

In order to understand how it is more profitable to repay a mortgage ahead of schedule, it is worth considering the situation when a loan of twelve percent in the amount of one million rubles was taken to purchase real estate. Payment begins in March. For example, in September, the borrower is given a premium of sixty thousand, and he decides to pay off some of the mortgage with it.
