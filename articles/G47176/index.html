How much does a cook earn on average in Russia?

A cook is a profession demanded in Russia, therefore it is considered highly paid. Employees are constantly required both in the capital and in other cities. Everywhere there are restaurants, cafes, canteens and other catering establishments. Frequent visitors are busy people. That is why the profession is considered one of the most common. Many people wonder how much the average cook earns?

Earnings in the capital

How much does a cook earn in Moscow? The average monthly income is 30,000 rubles. The salary of the chef will be more, it all depends on the responsibilities and their complexity. Helpers and other kitchen staff get less. The maximum salary for a cook in the capital is 120,000 rubles.

Such rates are valid in state organizations, for example, in canteens. Everyone knows that there is a small wage. But the requirements for employees are low: you need sanknizhka, permission to work, registration.

Commercial enterprises often offer wages of 13,000 rubles or more. The chef needs to purchase products, create menus, cook dishes. The employee must be able to cater to the customers of the institution.

Higher earnings are offered in elite institutions - from 40 to 80,000 rubles. In this case, work experience of 3 years or more is required, knowledge of various cuisines, skills in working with hot and cold shops, and the ability to be based on the tastes of customers. The employee uses new methods of cooking, decorating dishes.

How much does a personal cook earn? The specialist is paid from 20 to 100,000 rubles. The exact amount depends on the type of employment. In addition to high wages, in Moscow, specialists are provided with additional rewards: bonuses, gratuities and other bonuses.

Salaries in other cities

How much does a cook earn in other cities of Russia? It all depends on the type of enterprise and settlement. If you visit sites with vacancies, you can find out that in Novosibirsk specialists are ready to pay from 14 to 60 thousand rubles. The specific amount is determined by qualifications and the amount of work. How much does a cook earn in Yekaterinburg? Employees of this profession are paid from 16 to 45 thousand rubles.

Of course, in the capital, wages are greater than in other cities. This is due to various factors. But in any case, you can get a cook almost everywhere, which cannot be said about many other professions.

Chef salary

How much does a chef earn? In Moscow, such a specialist has an average salary of 65,000 rubles. The amount depends on the prestige of the institution, work experience. The salary is also affected by how the employee improves his skills. In any institution, true professionals are appreciated. If the chef works for a long time, then he has worthy helpers. When a team acts together, such an institution has prospects. Naturally, the salary may become larger over time.

Pastry chef salary

How much does a pastry chef make ? The level of remuneration depends on qualifications, rank. In any institution experienced employees with a diploma are appreciated. The pastry chef has 6 categories, the sixth is considered the most prestigious, providing the opportunity to work in an elite restaurant or cafe.

The salary of a pastry chef may be different. A novice employee does not get much. The average salary in the country is from 20 to 30,000 rubles. In Moscow there is an opportunity to receive about 40,000 rubles, and VIP-masters are offered from 80,000 rubles.

Professionals in the cookery and confectionery business are needed in any locality. If you have all the skills, then you can go to work. It should only be taken into account that in this work there are physical activities, a long stay at the stove. Purposeful person will be able to get a position with a normal salary.

What affects earnings?

The chef has his own responsibilities, in addition to tasty and satisfying feeding clients. He checks the quality of the products that will be used for cooking. Therefore, the cook must be attentive, collected, concentrated. Employees with excellent memory are valued because it is necessary to remember recipes, the number of calories in dishes and the properties of products. It is important to have a developed eye to get quick measurements of products.

Features of the profession are developed sense of smell and taste perception. How much a cook earns is influenced by a wonderful aesthetic taste and spatial imagination. Thanks to these qualities, the employee easily decorates and skillfully presents dishes. Mandatory qualities of a professional include the desire to develop, the desire to search for the best solutions. All of these factors affect wages.

How to become a cook?

Profession get in:

  • culinary technical schools;
  • schools;
  • colleges.

It is important to have a desire to work in this profession. The specialist must love to cook, as well as skillfully handle dishes and other kitchen utensils.

In educational institutions where they receive a profession, strict discipline is in place. Training should be systematic and supervised. In addition to technical schools, there are culinary courses that everyone can attend. Further training is carried out by a scientific organization. They teach professions in many cities of Russia.

Career

As an employee of any other profession, the chef has the opportunity for career growth in his field. After training, a graduate is assigned 2 or 3 categories, and upon successful completion of studies, 4 categories can be obtained. Then he becomes a student of the cook, under whose supervision he will have to work. Then the employee is transferred to assistants.

When the necessary skills are acquired, he can become a cook. In this position, you need to acquire new skills in order to become a chef. And this will require the development of the specialty of a technologist-technologist. To hope for career growth, you need to enter a higher educational institution as a process engineer.

After training, you can get a job. To begin your duties, you must provide a medical book. Other requirements include citizenship, age, experience, and a diploma. Only with the corresponding position can one hope for employment.
