Responsibilities

After completing the training, you can begin to work. The responsibilities of the insurance agent for OSAGO and any other types of insurance include:

  1. Search and attraction of new customers, expansion of our own customer base.
  2. The conclusion of new contracts for all types of insurance and the extension (renegotiation) of existing ones.
  3. Self-assessment of damage - despite the fact that most often this is done by a dedicated employee, each agent must be able to do it on his own.
  4. Strict accounting and preservation of documents - the responsibility for the storage of contributions and forms lies entirely with the agent.
  5. Conducting promotional events - the better the agent can present himself, the more customers he will have.




Of course, the first two points are fundamental, and the income received directly depends on their quality implementation.
