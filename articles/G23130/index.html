What is the best refrigerator: we understand

A refrigerator in any family is the most important household appliance, so owners want it to be functional and reliable. Intensive use around the clock without a break involves a considerable load on all parts, and its impressive size and high cost make it difficult to repair or replace it in case of failure. That is why it is so important to determine which is the best refrigerator. You can offer many recommendations that will help you choose the right unit.



When determining which is the best refrigerator, you should pay attention to the dimensions of the device. To do this, you must first determine the dimensions of the entrance and interior doors so that the purchased device easily passes through them. Too high a refrigerator is not very convenient to use, since usually on the upper shelves there are products that are easy to forget. It is better to choose a wide and low unit. Now manufacturers are offering models whose width is 70, 80 and 90 centimeters instead of the traditional 60, but with every ten centimeters of width, 20% is added to the cost of the device.



When deciding which is the best refrigerator, many of us pay attention to the price, since this factor is also important. The cost depends on the brand, demand, comfort and technical and economic indicators. Here the question arises: "Which company is the best refrigerator?" It is worth noting that the eminent brand adds 30% to the cost, so the products of companies such as Samsung, Electrolux and Bosch are not always affordable for customers.

When determining which is the best refrigerator, you should pay attention to the size and location of the cameras. If you do not store frozen foods for a very long time, and you usually have few of them, then you should consider the option with a small freezer. For those who usually store a lot of frozen vegetables or constantly cook from semi-finished products, we can recommend a device that has the same volume of refrigerating and freezing chambers, while the second is located below. This option is considered more economical, since the unit should not drive the refrigerant high up.

Another important point regarding the question of which is the best refrigerator, belongs to the class of energy consumption. By this is meant the economy class. It is best to purchase a device marked A or A +.

One of the comfort parameters is the control of the refrigerator, carried out in two ways: electronic and electromechanical. The first option involves setting the operating mode by selecting the appropriate one available in the program, and the second by turning the thermostat knob.





When determining which brand of refrigerator is the best, it is worth considering the defrosting system of the device. It can be forced, self-freezing and No Frost system. The second option is simple and effective. Such a system is preferable for vegetables, fruits, fresh products, since the humidity inside the chamber allows them not to dry out. Such a refrigerator can be defrosted a couple of times a year. The No Frost system can only function in the freezer or in both chambers. Such appliances do not need defrosting.
