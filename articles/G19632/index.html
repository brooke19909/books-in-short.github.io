Sewing step by step instructions

What will be needed to make a cocoon-nest for newborns with your own hands?

This accessory can be sewn from any fabric suitable for the baby. It is necessary to prepare 2 cuts with a size of at least 1x0.75 m. In addition, you will need a synthetic winterizer or holofiber for stuffing. It is desirable to make the bottom denser, therefore, using a roll insulation, evaluate its thickness objectively. Perhaps it is better to put it in 2-3 layers for softness? Fill the sides can be rolled up syntepon, but it is better to use holofiber balls. It will take 500-700 grams, depending on the desired density. The lace will go about 2.6 m, and for the tie you need 3 m of rope or tape.

Step-by-step instructions make it easier.

  1. Transfer the pattern to both flaps. Do not forget about seam allowances!
  2. Cut out both parts.
  3. Fold with the sides inward, gently sweep, inserting lace into the seam. Leave the un sewn bottom edge (A) and ends (C).
  4. Sew, twist and steam. Lay the duplicate line at a distance of 1 cm from the edge - the drawstring will be pulled into this gap.
  5. Lay a seam (B).
  6. Cut a holofiber or synthetic winterizer along the outline B. Insert in the middle, pin with pins and make transverse seams.
  7. Stitch Edge A.
  8. Fill the sides through the slots C.
  9. Retract the drawstring and sew the ends.
