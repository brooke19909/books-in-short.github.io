Guest house "Anastasia" (Kabardinka): a description of the facilities and reviews of vacationers

Kabardinka is a small village in the Krasnodar Territory, which is part of the resort city of Gelendzhik. Due to its amazing climate and location, it has been recognized as a resort since the beginning of the last century. Today there are more than 40 large health resorts. And for people who still prefer to relax "savages", in the village there are many private institutions, such as, for example, the guest house "Anastasia" (Kabardinka). Moreover, the name is quite popular. There are several institutions with the same name in the town. Each of them is worth telling in more detail.

Anastasia on Gornaya Street

Some people believe that rest in the private sector is deprived of comfort and normal living conditions. Those who visited the Anastasia Guest House (Kabardinka), located at 62 Gornaya Street, can refute such a statement.





Here in two buildings for visitors there are 14 rooms of different categories:

  • economy;
  • standard;
  • Suite
  • rooms with all amenities.

Depending on the specific room, the room provides sleeping places for two, three and even five people. Guest House Anastasia (Kabardinka) offers its visitors a comprehensive three meals a day in a cozy dining room on the ground floor. Therefore, they do not need to look for a place where they could eat. Although there is a cafe nearby and several grocery stores.

Each room has air conditioning (only in economy the fan is installed), upholstered furniture, a TV and a private bathroom. Guest House Anastasia (Kabardinka) is located near the sea. Just a 15-minute walk from it is equipped with a central beach (pebble).

Opinions of vacationers

Over the entire existence of the institution, a lot of people visited the guest house "Anastasia" (Kabardinka). Reviews about the rest here, almost all were only positive. The owners of the institution made every effort for this.





Most visitors noted the following points:

  1. The surrounding area and the entire patio are simply surrounded by greenery.
  2. Near the house there is a cozy veranda with chairs and swings, where you can have a good free time.
  3. Right next to it is a picturesque pond, in which even small fish swim.
  4. There is a small pool on site. It is very convenient for those who decide not to go to the sea.
  5. The rooms are beautifully decorated and always clean.
  6. Cold and hot water in all rooms is around the clock.
  7. The bathroom works without interruption.
  8. For sports enthusiasts, there is a tennis table in the courtyard.
  9. The hosts can provide barbecue facilities for those who want to cook their own barbecue.
  10. All guests can arrange delivery from the airport or train station for a small fee.

After such a vacation, many customers express a desire to visit this place again.

Anastasia on Stepnaya

There is also on the street Stepnoy guest house "Anastasia" (Kabardinka). It is located in a cozy quiet corner at a distance of 2327 meters from the central promenade. It offers vacationers rooms for accommodation of 2, 3 or 4 people.





Each of them has:

  • necessary furniture (beds, armchair, bedside tables and coffee table);
  • refrigerator;
  • air conditioning system;
  • TV.

Visitors can also rent a two-room suite with a separate kitchen. It can simultaneously accommodate up to 6 people. This room is ideal for families or when guests prefer to cook their own meals. The institution is a three-story house with long shared balconies.

In the courtyard there is a spacious pool around which comfortable sun loungers are installed. Here, during the day, those who for some reason do not want to go to the beach can sunbathe. Guests eat, as a rule, on their own. To do this, there is a kitchen equipped with everything you need for cooking.

And those who do not need this can go to the dining room or cafe located nearby. The food there is quite tolerable. The guest house has its own parking lot, where visitors can park their car for free during their holidays. As practice shows, many people like to spend a vacation in such conditions.

Anastasia on Mira Street

In the Krasnodar Territory there are many interesting places where you can relax. Among them, the guest house Anastasia (Kabardinka) located on Mira Street 26 deserves special attention. Visitors reviews about this establishment are only good.





The hosts offer guests two types of rooms:

  • Junior Suite for 2 and 3 people;
  • two-room suite for 2, 3 or 4 beds.

In each of them there is necessarily:

  • air conditioning;
  • TV with satellite and cable TV;
  • refrigerator;
  • bathroom with shower.

Guests feed on their own. On the ground floor of the building there is a dining room and a cafe-bar. For visitors with children there is a playground on site. It is very important. After all, kids also need to spend their free time somewhere. At the request of customers, the owners provide a barbecue or a special barbecue device. The house has a modem that provides Internet access, which residents can use absolutely free.

Rest in such a place costs 800-3000 rubles per day, depending on the season and the selected room. For many Russians, such conditions are quite acceptable. Perhaps that is why Anastasia on Mira Street in recent years is considered a fairly popular establishment.
