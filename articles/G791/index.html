Hotel "Yaroslavl Compound": address, reviews and photos

The ancient Russian city of Yaroslavl is famous for its beautiful history and historical center, which is lovingly preserved for residents and visitors of the city. It is just an ideal place for walking, so there are always a lot of tourists. A comfortable time free from excursions, you are invited to the hotel "Yaroslavl Compound".

Description

The three-star hotel "Yaroslavl Compound" was opened in August 2010 to the millennium of the city. Its location is the business center of Yaroslavl. The hotel is close to convenient transport interchanges, as well as to the motorway, which is of federal importance and connects Yaroslavl with Moscow, so tourists prefer personal transport. And, importantly, this is very close to the hotel with a modern service station. The on-site restaurant offers Russian and European cuisines, and guests can also enjoy their own pastry delights.

A cash machine is located on site. Free private parking is available for disabled guests. For young guests on the street there is a playground. The historical center is 10 kilometers from the hotel, and you can use the services of a tour desk to organize interesting excursions. The Yaroslavl Compound Hotel is located at 70 Pozharsky Street.

Accommodation for tourists

The hotel consists of 72 rooms, made according to individual design projects. Guests are offered accommodation in several categories of apartments:

  • double rooms with one double or two single beds - 2508 rubles per day for two guests, and 1858 rubles if one person is occupied;
  • in studios with a folding sofa and a large double bed - 3430 rubles per day;
  • in the Suite room - 4038 rubles per day for two people, and 3388 rubles for one guest.

The cost of living at the Yaroslavl Compound Hotel is based on a good breakfast, which is served on a buffet basis. Each room has free Wi-Fi access. Extra beds are not available in the rooms. By prior arrangement, pets are allowed with you.
