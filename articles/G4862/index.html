How to choose a vacuum cleaner for a car?

Such a service as car interior cleaning is currently offered at any car wash, but not every car owner has the opportunity and time to visit it every day. Every driver who cares about his reputation knows the need for daily cleaning of the cabin, because it is known that the condition of the vehicle‚Äôs interior reflects the character of its owner. In this regard, a car vacuum cleaner is an excellent assistant for maintaining order in the cabin and at the same time reputation at the proper level.



What is the difference between car vacuum cleaners and domestic vacuum cleaners? Firstly, by dimensions - a vacuum cleaner for a car has compact dimensions, as well as lower power, which, by the way, directly depends on the dimensions of this device. Therefore, before making a purchase, it is necessary to decide what functions it will be designed to perform. Since for those who decide to purchase a vacuum cleaner for a car, the price is usually not of paramount importance, it makes sense to pay attention to a more expensive model with an improved filtering system and an aquafilter. These functions allow you to save the car interior from allergens and dust.



As for the power supply, the car vacuum cleaner can work both on its own built-in battery, which is charged from the mains using a special adapter, and from the cigarette lighter, while not discharging the car battery. More convenient are models in which both operating options are provided. Operating time varies from fifteen minutes to half an hour.

In the same way as room assistants, car vacuum cleaners are equipped with special dust containers or bags for collecting garbage and dust. Due to the much larger volume, containers are more convenient than bags, so you should give preference to them when choosing, and the transparency of the dust collectors allows you to control their filling and remove accumulated dust and dirt in time.



A variety of nozzles, which are equipped with a vacuum cleaner for a car, can improve the quality of cleaning. Usually the kit includes:

  • crevice nozzle designed for cleaning the most inaccessible places;
  • rounded brushes equipped with a nap for better cleaning of fabric upholstery;
  • flexible hose with a special brush for collecting sand on the cabin floor and in the trunk;
  • a turbo brush with which the salon is cleaned of pile, hair and pet hair;
  • a special nozzle for eliminating spilled liquid and cleaning wet debris, but only those vacuum cleaners that have a wet cleaning function in such components.

As for the opinions of those car owners who managed to purchase a vacuum cleaner for a car, the reviews speak for themselves. Basically, these are words of gratitude to the manufacturer for a successful acquisition.

Of course, it would be a mistake to say that a car vacuum cleaner is a salvation from all types of contaminants that may appear inside your car during its operation, but for daily cleaning, such an assistant is an excellent option to maintain a clean and comfortable stay in the cabin during trips.
