A place for youth recreation - Margadina Hotel, Cyprus

Cyprus is deservedly considered the most relaxing place to relax. It has a moderate temperature, very tasty food, lots of sun, beautiful beaches and almost no crime. Cyprus is a cross between ancient Greece and east noisy Turkey. In addition, many of our compatriots are resting here, they understand the Russian language almost everywhere, and if necessary, it is easy to find a translator.

Ayia Napa attracts youth





In recent years, Ayia Napa has become an attractive resort for young people, where you can find entertainment venues that work all night. These are incendiary dances until the morning, the best DJs in Europe, music for different tastes. Every year young people from Russia, England, Germany, and other countries come here. If you want to combine good rest and vibrant entertainment, then the Margadina Hotel is the best choice for visitors to Cyprus (Ayia Napa) . Although it has only 3 *, it is distinguished by a good location - it is almost the same to go to the beach and to Club Street.
