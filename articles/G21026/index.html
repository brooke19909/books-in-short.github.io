Review "Nokia 3100"

The Nokia company at the time won a lot of fans. Successful decisions, unusual appearance and functionality of products allowed the company to take a leading place. One of the best devices released back in 2003 was the 3100. The attractive design and feature set made the device popular. What is interesting about the device?

Design





I liked the "Nokia 3100" users primarily because of its unusualness. The front part of the device is transparent, this decision of the designers allowed the owners to decorate their device. If necessary, the case is easily changed, since all parts are held on latches. Thus, the user can easily change the appearance and color of his device.

In front of the Nokia 3100 are a display, a speaker and keys. It should be noted that all buttons are rubber and change quite simply. The material of the keys allows you to comfortably work with the device. Fingers do not get tired, and all actions are performed smoothly.

The left side of the device is empty, and the speaker is located on the right. The manufacturer decided to transfer the volume control to the joystick of the device. This is not to say that such a move is successful. During a conversation, controlling the volume is not very convenient. The back side of the device is empty, the only exception is the lever to remove the cover.

The miniature device, due to lightweight transparent plastic, weighs only 86 grams. It‚Äôs convenient to work with a small phone. The mobile phone does not slip out of hands and does not cause discomfort.

Screen





Display "Nokia 3100" square shape. Screen sizes 27.3 by 27.3 mm. This form looks a little strange, but it does not spoil the appearance. The resolution of the small display is only 128 by 128 pixels. Accordingly, the user is struck by the "cubes". If not for this, a miniature screen could be considered quite successful.

The display of the device is quite bright and has in stock 4096 colors. The backlight is soft and practically does not tire the eyes. However, real problems begin in the sun. The screen is very blind and it is almost impossible to make out what is happening on it. Even setting the maximum brightness does not solve the Nokia 3100 problem.

Autonomy

The model 3100 has a removable 850 maH battery. Capacity is enough for four days of work in passive mode. Batteries, when using the Internet and calls, is enough for a day. Even at maximum load, the phone can work up to 12 hours. Since the battery is mostly used up only for the Internet and calls, the charge lasts a long time. The battery charges quickly, in just a few hours, the capacity is fully restored.

Memory

There is a huge problem with the memory in the phone. The user can access a little less than 1 MB. This is enough to save several tunes and pictures. There is no flash drive slot, so the owner will have to carefully consider the memory consumption.

There are many restrictions in 3100. The user can install about six games, and their "weight" should not be more than 64 kilobytes. MMS is also not easy. The device can store only 15 messages weighing no more than 45 kb.

Communication

It supports a GSM network device: 900, 1900 and 1800. To access the Internet, you can use WAP or GPRS of the sixth class. The choice is small, speed too, but for small tasks this is enough.

To download images and tunes, the user can use the Internet or do it using a USB cable. Unfortunately, the infrared port is not provided in the device.

Equipment





Comes with the Nokia 3100 manual, warranty, charging device, battery. In some cases, a USB cable is also included. Although the equipment is small, it is enough. The user only needs to charge the device. If necessary, you can buy a headset for using the radio and a spare case.

Sound

The mobile speaker has good power. At maximum volume, the conversation is heard even at a considerable distance. Of course, it is best to set the sound to half, then the conversation will be comfortable. The speaker does not produce extraneous crackles and noises, the interlocutor is heard clearly. The sound of melodies, calls and alarms is loud. Even on the street, the user will hear the device.

Installing a VGA camera makes it possible to take photos using the Nokia 3100. Since the device‚Äôs memory is not enough to host images, the additional gadget has 8 MB in reserve. About fifty photos can be stored in the camera itself. You can also connect an FM tuner via Pop-port.

Reviews





Many fans of the company preferred the Nokia 3100. The characteristics of the device in 2003 were quite acceptable. Added appeal to the phone and an extensive selection of accessories. In addition to the usual laces and case, the user could equip the device with a VGA camera or a radio tuner.

Unusual appearance also interested users. The reviews left for Nokia 3100 make it clear that the transparent case, which can be decorated, was to the taste of young people. The model can be turned into a bright and catchy device with the help of improvised means.

Functionality also did not go unnoticed. An excellent organizer, access to the network, games and other functions almost equated the device to business class.

Of course, the device has many shortcomings and shortcomings, but the most important problem is the innovation introduced. The transparent case turned out to be very fragile, after a strong blow it cracks.

Total

The output of the device 3100 increased the popularity of the company. Relatively inexpensive and advanced device was greeted by fans with a bang. Stylish appearance, ease of use and high reliability have made the Nokia 3100 a real hit.
