Beginner's guide: how to choose a tablet

A tablet is a relatively new device that combines elements of a smartphone and a personal computer. This technology has a number of positive aspects. Firstly, it is mobility and compactness. It is unlikely that anyone will want to drag their favorite personal computer with them to work or rest, which cannot be said about the tablet. Even compared to a laptop, it looks much smaller. Secondly, it preserves the full functionality inherent in a stationary computer. Using a tablet is very convenient for traveling, using the Internet at work or on the street. Almost every second person now has this device, which allows him to stay up to date with the help of the network. But how to choose a tablet? What to be guided by and what to pay attention to?



Purchase Goals

First of all, you need to decide what the device is purchased for. Most often, a tablet is bought for the following reasons:

  • Emphasize your business position. In this case, it is recommended to purchase a model with a beautiful design that contains all the functions necessary for work.
  • To watch movies and use the tablet as a gaming platform. Here you need to purchase the most powerful model that can maintain a high level of graphics and performance.
  • For multifunctional use, surfing the Internet. For such a case, a budget model that combines all the necessary functions is perfect.

Selection options

Choose a tablet in 2013, guided by the parameters of the device. First of all, you need to pay attention to the screen, or rather its size and image quality. The most acceptable options are 7 and 10.1 inches. The first option is as comfortable and compact as possible, while the second is more suitable for watching movies and game projects. Thus, when answering the question of how to choose a tablet, you first need to study the screen settings. The next important factor is the duration of the device without recharging. Please note that this indicator should be at least 2000 mA / h. Otherwise, the device will turn off after two hours of fruitful work. The name of the manufacturer is another important factor when choosing a tablet. To date, there are two manufacturers that successfully compete with each other: "Samsung" and "Apple". The products of their production are of the highest quality and technological. How to choose a tablet if there are not enough compared parameters? If the above indicators are not enough to finally decide on the choice of device, it is recommended to pay attention to the technical characteristics of the tablet, expressed in processor frequency, graphics card, amount of RAM and a place to store data. How to choose an inexpensive tablet?

It is necessary to watch that the technical characteristics of the device are at an average level. There is no need to purchase a model with 3G, because every tablet has WI-FI. The diagonal of the display in this case should correspond to 7 inches. How to choose a tablet, guided by the above parameters? If you combine all these indicators, combine them and come to a common conclusion, then we can say that the most acceptable option for purchase is the model "Samsung Galaxy Tab 2.0" or "Apple iPad", because they combine all the necessary functions embodied in themselves on the highest level.
