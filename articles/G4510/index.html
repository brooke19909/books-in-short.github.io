"Iron" characteristics of the gadget

Battery and autonomy

Perhaps the autonomy of this unit is a key indicator, speaking in favor of its purchase. Indeed, an impressive battery with the same impressive capacity of 5000 mAh is enough for 2-3 days of using the phone in active mode. You can, without fear that you will be left with a discharged device at a critical moment, use the mobile Internet, navigation, correspond in messengers, play games and listen to music. On average, 30-50 percent of the battery capacity is consumed per day.

However, if this seems not enough, and you plan to use only the telephone network and SMS messages, the mysterious button on the left side, which was mentioned at the beginning of the article, can come into play. She is responsible for enabling power saving mode. When switching, the display brightness decreases to the minimum, all applications are turned off and stopped, the processor goes into low frequency mode, and all networks and functions, except for mobile communications, are turned off.

In this mode, as the manufacturer assures, the smartphone is able to survive up to 5-7 days, depending on the number of hours of conversation. If the phone just lies, according to the manufacturer, in the on state it will be able to spend 29 days without recharging. All this puts the gadget in a favorable light for those who, due to their work responsibilities, must always stay in touch or plan long trips and do not want to miss them.
