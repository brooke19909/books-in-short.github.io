Reviews about hotel Ares City Hotel 3 *, Kemer (Kemer)

Kemer is rightly considered one of the most beautiful natural places in Turkey. The mountain slopes surrounding the resort, densely overgrown with coniferous forests, amazingly clean sea and beaches, a large number of places that can be seen combining vacations with excursions - all this has turned the country into one of the most popular destinations.





Kemer Hotels

Tour operators who want to spend their holidays sparingly, besides seeing a lot of attractions and returning home with purchases, are advised to choose the three-star hotels of this resort. With this arrangement, even despite the low category of the hotel, tourists are guaranteed accommodation in comfortable rooms with good service. In any Turkish hotel, regardless of its stardom, there is always a pool, including for children, as well as the most necessary services that are needed for a comfortable stay.

If the choice of tourists falls on Turkey, in particular on Kemer, then, considering three-star hotels, one should not expect that they will be on the first line of the sea. It is naive to expect check-in in luxurious rooms.

Simple but comfortable rooms, a certain remoteness from the beach, not very developed infrastructure - all this, in general, creates certain inconveniences that characterize three-star hotels. Nevertheless, many Russians choose this option for relaxation, having a great time by the sea, surrounded by mountain slopes, breathing wonderful coniferous air, not limiting yourself on excursions, and gladly visiting many local attractions. One of such establishments, where you can relax economically and at the same time well, is the Ares City Hotel 3 *.



general information

Located almost in the center of Kemer, the former Kami Hotel is fifty-five kilometers from the airport. It is considered one of the best in its category both in the quality of the services offered and in its location. Ares City Hotel 3 * is located just a few minutes walk to the sea. Near the entrance to the territory there is a bus stop. A motorway runs between the beach and the hotel.

Built in 2010, Ares City Hotel 3 * has recently been thoroughly renovated. He is part of the hotel chain of the same name "Ares". Its territory of four and a half acres is a densely planted garden, in which two- and three-story buildings are lost. Guests staying at this hotel can rely on comfortable rooms and quality service.

Infrastructure

Ares City Hotel 3 * (Kemer), positioned by tour operators as a family, designed for beach and sightseeing holidays, despite its low category, offers all the services that are needed. The reception is open around the clock. The territory of Ares City Hotel 3 * is guarded. There is convenient free parking, concierge services, a lobby, a depository safe, as well as a small luggage room for residents. You can order tickets or a transfer from the receptionist, or call a taxi. There is also a tour office where people can buy tours accompanied by a Russian-speaking guide.

Residents of Ares City Hotel 3 * (Kemer) can pay with credit cards, in addition, there is an ATM in the lobby. Those who arrive earlier than the scheduled time for check-in are invited to wait in the lobby, where there is climate control and the ability to connect to the Internet.





Housing stock

Hotel Ares City Hotel 3 * is considered average in size. It has a total of 73 rooms of a standard category. All are soundproofed. There are also non-smoking rooms in a separate building. The furniture is new. Bed linen is changed three times a week, and the premises are cleaned daily at a convenient time for hotel guests.

The rooms have single or double comfortable beds with new mattresses, a large wardrobe, a flat-screen TV, bedside tables, a desk and chairs, a mirror, a refrigerator and a direct dial telephone. Power in sockets - 220 V. There is also a radio and air conditioning with remote control. The rooms have small balconies with pool or garden views.

Types of accommodation in Ares City Hotel 3 * are as follows: single and double rooms with the possibility of adding a folding bed.

Nutrition

The hotel operates on an all-inclusive basis. In the hall of the main restaurant Ares City Hotel 3 * (Kemer), meals are served according to the "buffet" system. The menu includes local and international dishes. In addition to the restaurant, the hotel also has a bar by the pool: here, until eleven in the evening, you can get free drinks from local producers, as well as hot sandwiches and certain types of snacks.





Conditions for children

Tour operators note that Ares City Hotel 3 * (Kemer) is a family-run hotel, which also provides services for young guests. In it you can always hear cheerful children's laughter. There are really conditions for kids here. This is a shallow compartment in the adult pool, and high chairs for feeding in the restaurant, and the possibility of obtaining an extra bed on request. Those who wish can also use the paid service of a qualified babysitter.

Entertainment

The Ares City Hotel 3 * hotel, reviews about staying in the rooms of which are mostly good, offers a fairly extensive list of various services. On its territory a fairly comfortable pool is arranged, and around there are numerous sunbeds and umbrellas. Here you can spend time swimming and sunbathing, with no less comfort than on the seashore. There is a bar by the pool.

The main building has a separate sitting area and a TV lounge. Tourists leave a lot of positive reviews about the work of the tour desk, which has relatively low prices.

A lot of water entertainment is also presented on the beach: volleyball, scooter and catamaran rental , sailing - this is not the whole list of offers.



There is also a special sun terrace on site. Those who wish can take walks in the city or do jogging - jogging.

Beach

Distance to the sea is six hundred meters. The hotel is separated from the beach by a motorway. An underpass is not provided, so children are not recommended to go to the sea on their own. The hotel has its own beach. Its area is four hundred and fifty square meters. The entrance to the water is comfortable, sloping. The beach cover is pebble, so the presence of rubber shoes will come in handy. Umbrellas, as well as sun loungers can be obtained at an additional cost.

Reviews

Judging by the impressions, tourists consider its location to be the biggest plus of this hotel. And although the beach is about fifteen minutes walk, many do not consider this a drawback. In addition, those who do not want to walk can take a bus. Travel time is seven minutes from the gates of Ares City Hotel 3 *. Food reviews are also mostly positive. The food is simple, tasty and varied: everyone remains full. A lot of fish is served, often chicken, a very large amount of greens and vegetables. There are also many fruits and oriental sweets. Those who visited the cafe closest to the hotel or used street fast food are told that for four dollars you can eat delicious shawarma and drink ayran.



Some tourists did not like the number of meat dishes served for lunch. According to them, chicken dominates the menu, while beef is rarely cooked. Nevertheless, judging by the reviews, no one was left hungry.

The conclusion about staying at this hotel is as follows: it is fully consistent with the declared level and will not disappoint with the quality of service.
