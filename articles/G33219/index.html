Pasha S Princess Hotel (Turkey / Kemer / Camyuva): description and reviews

Camyuva is the famous resort region in Turkey, where every year at the height of the spa season an impressive number of tourists gather who want to relax inexpensively, but tastefully surrounded by tropical nature.

One of the most sought after hotels in this region is Pasha's Princess Hotel (Adult Only), which offers four-star accommodation in accordance with international standards.



Location

The hotel, according to holidaymakers, is ideal for those who like to travel alone or for couples who need silence in their vacation. All this is due to the fact that Pasha's Princess Hotel is located in a very calm and beautiful place, located away from the entertainment infrastructure of Kemer (7 km. From the city center), in the small coastal village of Camyuva. It has wonderful fresh air and a great climate. Staying in this place, tourists can fully enjoy the eternal summer, its sunny days.

The nearest airport is far away - 60 kilometers from the hotel. In this regard, the standard travel package without fail includes a transfer service to the destination immediately upon arrival.

general information

The hotel was built in 1997 and since then has undergone several changes that have affected the appearance of its building, as well as technical equipment. To equip the territory and rooms, only modern and high-quality furniture is used, which during the period of the Pasha's Princess Hotel (Kemer) has changed several times to a more comfortable one. One of the last such renovations was made in 2016.

Rooms

Attention tourists at Pasha's Princess Hotel (Turkey) are offered 84 standard apartments. They all consist of one room, and overlook the pool and the interior or the luxurious gardens that surround the hotel.

Each room has an air conditioning system element installed, however, they are not individually controlled, but centrally controlled, cooling is turned on only from two in the morning until five in the evening, that is, in the hottest time. However, despite this, tourists vacationing at the Pasha's Princess Hotel (Kemer), in the reviews say that the system only during these hours is quite enough.

The apartments have private bathrooms, equipped with a plumbing kit, hairdryer and bath accessories, which are issued to each guest individually upon arrival.

Room Interior

According to tourists, all the apartments in the hotel are stylishly and beautifully decorated. Their decoration is made in a classic style, using a large number of white elements. Every day they are cleaned, due to which cleanliness and order are constantly maintained here.

As for furniture, the central place in the room is a French double bed, which has an orthopedic mattress and expensive bedding. For the things of tourists, white wardrobe cabinets made of light wood are provided here, as well as some of the luggage can be placed in the cabinets.

On a free basis, guests can use an electronic safe, a telephone, and a TV that broadcasts satellite channels, many of which are Russian-speaking. If desired, guests can order a minibar in the apartment, however, use of it is paid daily.

The lightness of the overall picture of the interior is given by light textiles, which are available on the huge windows overlooking the balcony.



Hotel Services

Pasha's Princess Hotel (Turkey / Kemer) offers guests a wide range of services that contribute to a comfortable stay. These include laundry and dry cleaning services, as well as ironing services. If desired, guests can visit the medical office for a fee or call a doctor in the room if necessary.

Hairdressing and beauty services are provided on the ground floor of the building. There is also a tour desk where you can order a tour of local attractions, and, as guests note, their cost is much lower than if you include such a service in a travel package with an additional charge.

SPA

This hotel has an excellent spa center, which is divided into several zones. Tourists in reviews of Pasha's Princess Hotel (Adult Only) often share their pleasant impressions that remain after visiting this place. They note that only professionals in their field work in the SPA zone.

There is a large thermal zone, which includes a hammam and sauna. Their visit is completely free, in addition, on each client there are parmeisters who know a lot about relaxing in a hot atmosphere. At the exit of the thermal zone there is a large indoor pool, which is filled with fresh clean water every day, there is also an automatic heating system.

In the hotel part there is a treatment area where services are offered to improve the body as a whole. They can offer various procedures using seaweed, sand, clay, therapeutic mud, as well as various other healing components. For the skin of the whole body, face and neck, a wide range of cosmetic procedures are offered here aimed at rejuvenation. In a separate office, massage services are provided both for the whole body and for its individual sections.

Sport

Fans of an active lifestyle at Pasha's Princess Hotel will certainly find something to their liking. Here, for this category of guests, a gym is equipped with units for working on different parts of the body. Engage in it is completely free.

If desired, tourists can engage in team sports at sites that are equipped on site. Among them there are those that are designed for playing volleyball, soccer and basketball. You can also play table tennis in the special room.



Nutrition

Tourists staying at the Pasha's Princess Hotel can all-inclusively eat at the local restaurant, which serves Turkish and European dishes. Three times a day, guests can visit it during breakfast. lunch and dinner to enjoy delicious cooked food. In reviews of Pashs S Princess Hotel (Kemer), one can often find comments addressed to cooks. In particular, guests recommend tasting local sweets, which can be taken both in the restaurant itself and in any of the two bars that are also in the hotel. The bars serve a wide selection of cocktails, which the restaurant does not have. Among the drinks there is a large selection of both alcoholic and non-alcoholic. If desired, vacationers can enjoy light snacks, sweets and salads.



Aquazone

In the open area there is a small pool with clean water, near which tourists can relax. In order to make their stay as comfortable as possible, there is an excellent sun terrace around, where guests can sunbathe on large wooden sun loungers under beach umbrellas.

If desired, tourists have the opportunity to visit the bar, which is located next to this place - it has a large selection of drinks, often fruit is offered.



Beach

Pasha's Princess Hotel is located on the third beach line from the shores of the Mediterranean Sea, but this fact does not make it less popular among tourists. The coastline is small but quite wide. The whole territory is covered with small pebbles, and descent into the sea, as tourists note, is very convenient. If desired, guests can be accommodated on deckchairs that are made of wood, under large umbrellas, which easily protect against direct sunlight.

Along the coastline there is a lot of entertainment that will appeal to both extremals and fans of a more quiet, relaxing holiday.

Entertainment

According to tourists' reviews, it is impossible to get bored in the hotel, as the small team of cheerful animators who always know what to do with their guests works here all the time. In the daytime, they hold various sporting events in the aquazone, as well as in open air venues, and in the evenings, guests are offered show programs and discos that play music performed by local talented DJs. Entrance to the party is free.

Fans of billiards can visit the special room, where there are several large tables covered with green cloth, and if you want to read a piece, guests can go to the library, which offers a wide selection of literature.

Prices

The cost of the tour at Pasha's Princess Hotel is relatively small: on average, it is about 99,000 rubles, subject to purchase for two. For this price, the purchased package includes not only a weekly room rental, but also a round-trip flight, transfer from the airport to the destination, meals and, of course, a set of necessary medical insurance. Before departure, every tourist should remember that for crossing the Turkish border, citizens of the Russian Federation need to open a visa, which is not included in the package.



Tourists reviews

In their comments, left at the end of a vacation at Pasha's Princess Hotel, tourists often share their impressions. In them they talk about the high level of service and friendliness of the staff. In particular, travelers make a positive emphasis on the work of administrators at the front desk, specifying that they can always answer all questions of interest regarding the provision of services, accommodation or the location of tourist facilities.

As for the design of rooms, it also often attracts the attention of guests, which they respond positively in the comments. Tourists really like the light interior, which looks expensive, as well as comfortable furniture that fits perfectly into the big picture.

As for entertainment, hotel guests note that there are a lot of them in the territory of this complex. In particular, funny discos are held here, there is a beautiful aquazone, near which almost all guests gather in the daytime. And with a lack of impressions, travelers sometimes recommend going for emotions to the central part of Kemer, where the nightlife is in full swing: there are clubs, restaurants, bars, as well as other places where you can have a great time and make new acquaintances.
