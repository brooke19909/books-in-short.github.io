Design, size, controls

The manufacturer officially introduced four color options. Among them, Nokia 6700 Classic of gold color should be highlighted, since this shade pleases with its elegance. Display

The screen of Nokia 6700 Classic is QVGA with a resolution of 240 x 320 pixels. The display displays 16 million colors, the colors are juicy and bright. The light indicator is able to change the backlight, based on external conditions. CABC technology debuted in this unit. Thanks to it, the brightness of the backlight changes based on the ambient lighting and what the phone‚Äôs screen is currently displaying. For example, when viewing Internet resources with a lot of text, the screen becomes muted, the text becomes more contrast. As for games, here, on the contrary, the brightness increases, if only the user is not in absolute darkness. Thus, you can significantly save battery power. Ideally, 50% of energy can be saved in this way. At the same time, working with the screen remains just as comfortable. Forced brightness control in the phone is missing. The screen displays 9 text lines and 3 service lines. The font is readable, quality. With special operating modes, the phone can show up to 16 text lines.





Keyboard

The keypad of Nokia 6700 Classic is slightly buried behind the edge of the case. For the keyboard used one piece of a metal plate. The buttons have a good stroke and are easily pressed. The keyboard feels like you can compare with that of the Nokia 6300. You can quickly get used to it. The backlighting is white, bright and uniform. The navigation key is convenient, it has a built-in indicator light. In case of missed events, it blinks. It is curious that with active backlighting, characters on the screen are created from points with different brightness. This design looks nice.





Battery

The Nokia 6700 Classic phone model received a BL-6Q lithium-ion battery with a capacity of 960 mAh. The manufacturer guarantees 5 hours of offline communication or 300 hours of standby time. Music can be played up to 22 hours, video recording with a maximum quality of about 140 minutes, video playback - 210 minutes. The average battery life is 3 days. A full charge of the battery is achieved in about 2 hours.

Memory

170 MB of free memory are available to the user, which are provided directly by the device itself and are provided for storing any data. Implemented support for microSD memory cards. The corresponding slot is under the cover and the battery needs to be removed to replace the media. In the kit, the user receives a 1 GB card.





USB, Bluetooth

As for the universal port for connecting to a PC, its configuration provides 3 operating modes. In the conditions of Data Storage, the phone‚Äôs memory and drive are visible, no drivers are required, the OS recognizes the phone itself. A special mode of PC Suite provides work with a proprietary application, in this case access to various functions of the device is open, among which backup information is provided. Camera and software

The Nokia 6700 Classic model uses a 5-megapixel module, complemented by autofocus, as well as an LED flash. There are options for the shortcut bar during shooting, it can be configured as you wish. The interface is horizontal. The time to save a photo in any resolution is approximately the same and is 3-4 seconds. Waiting is necessary if you intend to view the image immediately after shooting. When there is no such need, the retention period is reduced to 1-2 seconds. Implemented a variety of color modes. There are 3 effects - Negative, Black & White and Sepia. As for the white balance, by default the camera works well, but there is the possibility to set Fluorescent, Tungsten, Daylight.





The device can create video in several quality options. As for the maximum, it provides a speed of 15 frames with a resolution of 640 x 480 pixels. The maximum duration of one clip is 60 minutes. Video recording mode is characterized by maximum power consumption. When shooting, you can use digital zoom. Sound can be turned off if desired. White balance options and color effects are available. Scene selection includes auto and night shots.

The phone received the S40 platform of the sixth edition. The features include links to FaceBook and other social services. The Nokia Maps 1.2 platform is installed, maps for each of the regions are placed on a memory card. The delivery kit has a monthly navigation license, which includes voice prompts. GPS does not cause any problems. The device features various branded services, including Share, mail, contacts. Windows Messenger and Flickr services are also available. By default, the device has several games and applications, as well as such familiar tools as unit conversion and world time.
