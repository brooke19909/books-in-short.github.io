New buildings in Barnaul: description, reviews

Barnaul is a small provincial town that has begun to be actively built up over the past few years. New residential complexes and entire microdistricts appear, where excellent living conditions are created. Modern construction companies take into account not only the desire of people to live in spacious apartments, but also the need for improvement, development of the district's infrastructure. Moreover, most of the complexes are being built in ecologically clean areas with fresh air and remoteness from the hustle and bustle of endless noise. In the frame of this material, we will consider Barnaul, new buildings of the city, which have already managed to appreciate the first tenants. Our review will be an excellent information base for those who are at the stage of choosing an apartment.

LCD "Seasons"

If you are interested in the comfort class real estate of Barnaul, the Seasons project will surely attract your attention. Modern technologies of a seamless facade make it possible to build strong, reliable, beautiful buildings with enhanced insulation properties. RC "Seasons" is the use of innovative construction technologies, a variety of thoughtful planning solutions, underground parking lots, a closed house area with recreation areas, playgrounds and sports grounds, as well as excellent ecology. The final completion of the project is scheduled for the 4th quarter of 2018.





These are the best new buildings in Barnaul. Prices for turnkey apartments start at 33,000 rubles per square meter. The first buyers who managed to visit the construction site emphasize that all work is carried out efficiently and without violations. The houses will be commissioned on time. They were also pleased with excellent thought-out layouts that allow each resident to feel as comfortable as possible.

LCD "Anton Petrova"

If you are interested in new buildings in the center of Barnaul, in an area with a settled life, surrounded by all necessary infrastructure, then take a look at the Anton Petrova residential complex. The new building was erected in the Leninsky district of the city and commissioned in 2015. Surrounded by kindergartens and schools, this green area is a great option for family living. The house was built on brick-monolithic technology, providing the necessary strength, reliability and durability. For two years of life, the residents did not reveal any violations, the house turned out to be very warm. It pleases with excellent soundproofing properties and stunning views of the sights of the city.





All apartments were commissioned with pre-finishing. Excellent non-standard layouts can satisfy the taste of even the most demanding customer. Today you can buy an apartment with an area of ​​45 square meters with a repair for just 2.1 million rubles.

LCD "Pavlovsky tract"

New building with excellent panoramic views - what could be better? Real estate in Barnaul has never been so modern. These are the reviews shared by the first residents of the new building. A frame-monolithic house in a promising developing area of ​​the city with excellent ecology won the hearts of many. Residents note an excellent transport interchange, thanks to which you can reach any point in the city in just 30 minutes. In the courtyard there is a playground designed for children of different ages, as well as a spacious parking with the possibility of acquiring a place for a car in the property. Apartments in this area will be popular for a long time and will not lose their liquidity.





The complex was commissioned in 2016, the cost of apartments in it today starts from 2.3 million rubles.

LCD "Partizanskaya"

And here is another great offer in Baranaul - a new building in the city center. The business class house with all amenities was commissioned at the end of 2015. All entrances are equipped with high-speed silent elevators, an intercom system and a video surveillance system, which provides the necessary security for all residents.





It is possible to purchase a car place in a residential complex under construction next door, or to rent a parking place there later. This feature pleased all car owners who no longer have to worry about the safety of their cars. The windows offer stunning views, and due to the quality of glazing, noise from busy streets does not reach the residents of the apartments. A variety of planning solutions for every taste is offered. Today you can buy an apartment in the new building of Barnaul for 2.5 million rubles.

To summarize

Who would have imagined that in a small town so high-quality and modern homes are being built! Barnaul, whose new buildings provide apartments for all residents, has really transformed and expanded. Now each resident of the city decides in what conditions his family will live. A variety of planning decisions and the possibility of acquiring real estate on the terms of mortgage lending allow each family to choose the best option, based on wishes and financial opportunities.
