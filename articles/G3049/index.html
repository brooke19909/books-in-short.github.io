Knit a sock with knitting needles - start from the top

After a small sample is connected with the main pattern, calculations are made to help find out the density of knitting, you need to dial the required number of loops. It is important to knit not too tight, but not very weak. The same rule applies to a set of loops. To make it easier to understand the technique of creating a sock, it is better for an example to take 60 loops, which we safely and put on 2 knitting needles. Knitting the second row, we distribute the loops on 4 knitting needles.

So that the sock fits the legs well and does not come down when moving, we knit men's socks with knitting needles, first applying the gum pattern.
