Club Med Phuket Thailand: overview, features and reviews of tourists

When choosing a place for family vacations, a romantic weekend or spending holidays with friends, many stay in Thailand, namely on the largest of its islands - Phuket. A large number of tourists come here every year, because these parts are environmentally friendly, partly wild, but with a developed infrastructure.

The hotel complex Club Med Phuket (Phuket, Thailand), which corresponds to the level of four stars, offers guests comfortable accommodation, varied and rich pastime and a warm welcome. Here everyone will find something to their liking: from the smallest to the more experienced travelers.

General characteristics of the hotel

The history of travelers' accommodation within the walls of the Club Med Phuket 4 * hotel (Phuket Island) dates back to the last millennium. It was opened in 1998, and the last significant renovation was made in 2008. Two-and three-story bungalows are located on a large-scale territory. The distance to the nearest airport is just under 50 km. It will not be possible to stay here with pets, and smoking is allowed only in specially designated places. There are rooms for disabled guests. Deposit at the entrance is not taken.





Accommodation

The total number of rooms in the Club Med Phuket (Kata) hotel is represented by 305 rooms of the following categories:

  • superior;
  • deluxe;
  • suite.

The apartments vary in size, decoration and views. The interior is presented with modern decor, without elaborate elements and high cost. On the floors - ceramic tiles. The color scheme is dominated by orange-golden colors. There is access to a balcony or terrace. French glazing fills the room with light and warm rays of the sun. Housekeeping is provided daily by the maid. There were no complaints about cleanliness and order. Tourists claim that maids are responsible for their work.





Accommodations

Due to the presence of everything necessary for a comfortable stay, the guests of the Club Med Phuket 4 * hotel (Phuket, Karon) feel at home. The furniture is presented as a standard set. There is an individual split system, a TV with Russian channels, a mini fridge, an electric kettle, a telephone and a safe. Two bottles of drinking water are provided daily in each apartment. Iron and ironing board on request. Wi-Fi is free throughout, which is noted by experienced in their reviews as an advantage of this institution. The bathroom is standard, a set of personal hygiene products is located on the sink.

Nutrition

Club Med Phuket often receives positive reviews for catering. The hotel has three meals a day according to the buffet system. This form of serving dishes is convenient for both the administration and the guest, because he himself, having visually examined the assortment, chooses what impressed him. Alcohol, drinks and cocktails in bars are included in the All Inclusive system. Only brand names are paid extra.

The interior of the main restaurant is simple and no frills. There is an outdoor terrace. Eating in the fresh air inspires vacationers and contributes to a calm quenching of hunger. Here you can admire the beauties of the surrounding landscapes.





Aquazone

The swimming area is represented by a huge pool, where there is undoubtedly enough space for everyone. It is surrounded by sunbeds with mattresses and umbrellas. Towels are provided free of charge, there is a shower. The pool is filled with fresh water, zoned for the main and children's aquazone. It does not have a heating system. Swimming as one of the important types of pastimes inspires all guests. It is possible to normalize the work of vital systems of the body, to develop the respiratory apparatus and musculoskeletal system.

The time spent here will not be monotonous. There is the necessary equipment for playing water polo. Daily classes in water aerobics under the guidance of experienced instructors delight swimmers, raising their spirits.





Beach

The hotel is located on the first coastline, just 50 meters from the waters of the Andaman Sea. Such proximity, according to experienced, is to the liking of all holidaymakers. In order to be on the beach, you need to spend less than five minutes, breaking only the roadway. The entrance to the water is gentle, however, the wave sizes can reach a significant size.

The beach area is equipped with sun loungers. Sunbathing and sunbathing is available to everyone, regardless of age. For those who love shade, umbrellas are offered.

Most vacationers, in addition to quietly admiring the sea distances, prefer active leisure on the shore. Here you can play volleyball with your hotel neighbors or go to conquer extreme water sports, the most interesting of which are diving, windsurfing, parachuting for a boat. No less fun is snorkelling. Transparent sea surface allows you to see all the inhabitants of the water abyss.





Sport and beauty

The Club Med Phuket Hotel (Thailand) has a fitness center open all day. Here you can leave a couple of extra pounds, find beautiful body shapes, improve health and raise your mood. Cardiovascular equipment, treadmills, exercise bikes, dumbbells, and much more are what this gym is full of. Qualified trainers will help in drawing up an optimal load plan, which will contribute to physical development and increase immunity.

A visit to the wellness procedures of the SPA salon will allow each visitor to experience an unprecedented feeling of relaxation and relaxation. The use of therapeutic mud and algae has a beneficial effect on recovery. The surge of strength and energy is palpable from the first minutes. Masters of Thai massage will give every cell of the human body a genuine relaxation, giving the skin a fresh look and rejuvenating it.

Sauna and bath are places that are perfect for lovers of high temperatures. You can take a steam bath in the company of friends. There are many positive effects from this event: normalization of metabolic processes, removal of toxins from the body, weight loss and rejuvenation. The age limit is 18+. Anyone younger is not allowed.

The beauty salon is happy to welcome every client. A new image and excellent appearance is guaranteed to all vacationers.

Archery allows you to identify the most accurate shooter. Tennis courts have a hard surface, the services of a coach and the necessary equipment with lighting are paid extra.

One of the unusual pastimes is learning how to cook Thai food. Master classes from cooks will diversify and expand their culinary talents for every traveler.

Toddler Leisure

Club Med Phuket 4 * receives positive reviews, and to some extent even enthusiastic ones, from tourists who came to rest with their children for organizing their leisure. They argue that for good reason this hotel complex is positioning itself as a place for family vacations. The pastime of the kids here will be incredibly intense, driving, vibrant and exciting. Visiting Club Med kids, children from 4 to 10 years old can play various toys, collect the designer, go through a series of master classes under the guidance of a qualified teacher, make crafts and draw. The interior of this institution has both serious training sessions and fun. Throughout the day at the Petit Club Med, small guests can do dancing, gymnastics, and participate in theatrical performances. There are places for daytime sleep, which are paid extra.

For teenagers (guests between the ages of 11 and 17), Password is a place where they can show their creative talents in various fields: video, theater, cinema, dancing, sound mixing.

An incredibly exciting journey for children of all ages is the town of Kids Palace, whose area is 1000 m 2 . There is a rope park, trampoline complexes, modern sports grounds. Club Med Phuket also has a game complex with many slides, labyrinths, ladders and swings. In general, babies will definitely never be bored.

A playpen in the room is available upon request. Feeding chairs in restaurants are freely available.





Additional services

The list of additional, however equally important for guests, services includes the possibility of conducting business negotiations and meetings in the conference room. They are equipped with all necessary equipment, have a design that is conducive to decision-making and comfortable furnishings. Capacity - 80 people.

The tour desk diversifies the busy days at Club Med Phuket. All kinds of excursions to museums and historical sights will give a lot of new emotions and knowledge.

The front desk, whose staff resort to help instantly, is open around the clock. According to numerous reviews, the staff is responsive, friendly and quite friendly. Laundry services are paid upon.

The hotel in question has grocery stores and souvenir shops. A round-trip shuttle is offered with safety for the health of guests. Drivers will deliver guests to the desired point quickly and comfortably.

Professional photographers capture the best shots of an unforgettable vacation. It is possible to exchange money without leaving the borders of the complex.
