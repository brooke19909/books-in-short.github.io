CPU, graphics accelerator and memory subsystem

A very powerful processor solution is used in the Meizu MX4 Pro. Reviews highlight its high level of computing performance. But at the same time, the energy efficiency of the gadget is at an acceptable level. Samsung uses the Exynos 5430 as the central processor unit in this smartphone. Physically, it has 8 cores, but at the same time only 4 of them can be in operation. That is, this chip is based on big.LITTLE technology. Its essence lies in the fact that the CPU consists of 2 groups of cores. 4 computing modules based on the ‚ÄúA7‚Äù architecture (in peak load mode they can accelerate to 1.7 GHz) have a low level of performance (but it is enough to solve most everyday tasks) and have low power consumption. Most of the time this particular computing cluster is in operation. If demanding software is launched, then a high-performance cluster based on the "A15" architecture is activated. There are also 4 cores, but they operate at a frequency of 2 GHz. The result is an ideal balance of performance and power consumption. This smartphone can easily cope with any software. Another powerful link in this device is the graphics adapter - Mali-T628 MP6. He, too, will not have problems launching any, even the most demanding, software. The amount of RAM in this device is always 3 GB. But the capacity of the built-in drive may vary. In the most modest case, its volume is 16 GB. This is, so to speak, the economy version of the flagship. The average modification of the smartphone is called Meizu MX4 Pro 32Gb. It is not difficult to understand from the name, its drive size is 32 GB. Well, the most advanced version of the smartphone is equipped with 64 GB. It should also be noted that there is no expansion slot for installing a flash card, and you will have to be content with what is available.

Autonomy

The capacity of the complete battery is 3350 mAh. Given the diagonal of the touch screen of 5.36 inches and a fairly energy-intensive processor, this value with an average load is enough for 2 days. If you use the device to a minimum, it will turn out to last 3 days on a single charge. But if some serious toy is launched (Asphalt 8, for example), the battery will be discharged in Meizu MX4 Pro in a matter of hours. Reviews They say that in this mode, the smartphone can last a maximum of 4-5 hours.





Communication kit

An excellent set of wired and wireless transmission methods in Meizu MX4 Pro. A review of the technical documentation indicates the presence of such ones as:

  • Support for GSM, 3G and LTE mobile networks.
  • An expanded set of Wi-Fi networks (the device successfully works with a, b, g, n and ac at frequencies of 2.4 GHz or 5 GHz).
  • There is also a bluetooth transmitter, with the latest, 4th generation.
  • One GPS transmitter at the software level can interact with all navigation systems: GPS, BDS and, of course, GLONASS.
  • The microUSB port allows you to quickly exchange information with a PC or charge the battery.
  • To output an audio signal, there is a 3.5 mm port located on the upper edge of the device.
