Samsung Galaxy S4: phone features, reviews and photos

Samsung has long earned credibility among customers and even has its dedicated fans. In the line there are many models of the most diverse equipment. The smartphones of this well-known company are especially popular. In this article we will look at one of the most common models today - Samsung Galaxy S4, the technical characteristics of which are still of interest to many, despite the fact that the phone is not a novelty of the season.



Appearance

The design of the device is an extremely controversial topic. After the model entered the world markets, a lot of complaints to the Korean manufacturer appeared regarding the appearance of the device. The thing is that visually the novelty was practically no different from its predecessor. In fact, this is one of the main problems of the entire series of Samsung smartphones. There is no design at all, all products are made in one style, as if the manufacturer deliberately seeks to anonymize their models. The characteristics of the Samsung Galaxy S4 phone , in terms of external performance, are largely similar to all the device design descriptions from the company. The flagship has simple, streamlined on all sides, uncomplicated shapes. The device is as smooth as sea ‚Äã‚Äãpebbles. Its plastic case and gloss, so unloved by users due to constant scratches, does not deter its acquisition. It's amazing that even despite such a faceless design, Samsung Galaxy S4 (whose technical characteristics cover all the flaws of the case) is in the top of the best-selling models in the world. Sly Korean manufacturers understand as well as you and me that appearance is by no means the most important factor when choosing a phone. The company frankly declares that in the near future it is not going to change its strategy. However, perhaps this should be done, especially with such production capacities. Compared to its predecessor, the Samsung Galaxy S4, whose characteristics are largely identical, has slightly less rounded corners, a slightly wider rim and a different flash location. The dimensions and weight of the device remained unchanged. The innovations include materials: ordinary polycarbonate has replaced ordinary plastic. This is good news, because there is a chance that the new case will be more scratch resistant.

Usability

The keys and connectors of the model remained in place. The side rim framing the display has slightly decreased, which made it possible to fit the characteristics of a larger screen into the same dimensions of the Samsung Galaxy S4. This, of course, is commendable, because the large screen will appeal to everyone, but the sheer size confuses many. It is also encouraging that the back cover remained removable, that is, the user has the opportunity to replace the battery on his own, insert a SIM card without additional devices, etc. Initially, the phone came in two colors (black and white), a little later appeared colored cases, which allows everyone to choose a gadget in their favorite color scheme.

Performance

The hardware platform of the smartphone is based on the powerful SoC Exynos 5410 Octa. It consists of 2 quad-core processors (ARM Cortex - A15 with a frequency of 1.9 GHz and ARM Cortex - A7 with reduced frequencies of 1.2 GHz.). The application proposed by the company is obvious: with a serious load, the first one is used, and energy-efficient ones are replacing the top cores to fulfill those tasks that do not require significant processing power. As for image processing, here Samsung Galaxy S4 (decent graphics performance, as already mentioned) is supported by the specialized graphics core PowerVR SGX 544MP3.

Offline work

The flagship is equipped with a removable battery, which can be extremely necessary, for example, in situations with a swollen battery (which sometimes happens) or in cases of long trips, when it does not hurt to get a second battery.   • Reading. In the conditions of disconnected wireless networks, the phone remains charged for 10.5 hours with automatic page turning.
  • Video. It is possible to play continuously for 10.5 hours, unlike the Samsung Galaxy S4 Mini, the characteristics of which are slightly lower (6 hours of battery life in the specified mode).

Samsung Galaxy S4 Duos (specifications)

In fact, this is a small copy of Galaxy S4. Here, everything is a little smaller: the screen, the case, the camera resolution, and performance. The main advantages of the model are compactness, a more affordable price and, of course, the ability to install two SIM-cards at the same time. This point is often decisive when choosing a particular smartphone. In modern conditions, when you need to be constantly in touch, it is very convenient to have one device and several SIM cards in it. This allows you to save on calls between different operators and not carry several devices with you.

Remarks

With continuous use, the Samsung Galaxy S4 smartphone (the characteristics have been examined in detail) gives problems in the camera, which were subsequently fixed in the new firmware.

Like most powerful phones, heating of the top of the lid is observed, but this is only with severe loads.

There are also some complaints about the Super AMOLED display and the difficulty of using it on a sunny day.
