Kazakhstan, Lake Alakol: recreation facilities, living conditions, prices

Kazakhstan is famous for its unique water resources. On its territory there are a large number of mineral springs. For example, in the south-east of the country there is a lake, the waters of which contain almost all the elements of the famous periodic table. It is called Alakol. Recreation centers, boarding houses and motels are constantly open for people who come here to regain strength and improve their health. The healing properties of the lake have been known since Genghis Khan. He and his army came to the pond to heal the wounds received in battle. Currently, it is treated for disorders of the nervous system, disorders of the musculoskeletal system, skin diseases. It is even known that Lake Alakol can also cleanse radiation . Recreation centers and sanatoriums received famous astronauts who, after a long stay in space, regained their strength and health.

Unfortunately, the infrastructure in this region is not well developed. This is primarily due to the remoteness of the object. For example, from Alma-Ata it will take about 10 hours to get to the lake by car. Outside the recreation areas, finding entertainment is almost impossible. There are also not very many natural attractions in the district. However, even such inconveniences at first sight cannot be compared with the healing properties of Lake Alakol. The recreation centers operating in this area belong to different price categories and provide various living and leisure conditions, which can be found by reading this article.



Koktum

In the center of the village of Koktum there is a recreation center of the same name. At the same time, up to 180 people can live here. The dining room is located in a separate building. The distance to the lake is about a hundred meters. The base specializes in beach vacations, sanatorium and preventive rehabilitation. The administration offers excursion programs in protected areas. For leisure activities there is a billiard room, dance floor, water rides. A massage service and bar are on site. Guests are accommodated in improved comfort cottages and a brick two-story building. The latter provides suites, superior + and standard. The cost of living starts from 4550 tenge (860 rubles).

Alakol





In the village of Akshi on the south coast is the recreation center "Alakol". The price of accommodation for one person per day is about 7000 tenge (1300 rubles). The price already includes three meals a day, which provides a local cafe. In addition, the establishment has a bar with soft drinks. For cooking, only environmentally friendly vegetables grown in greenhouses, which are located directly in the base, are used.

For the rest, guests can settle in comfortable single-story buildings. The rooms are designed for 2-3 people. Hot and cold water is supplied around the clock. The rooms have a TV and air conditioning. Entrance is separate. There are all amenities.

At the base there are conditions for outdoor activities. Those who wish can play badminton, football, basketball. There are also equipped places for tennis and tennis. The beach is equipped, located 400 meters.

"Pelican"

Recently, large-scale work has been carried out to improve the area near Lake Alakol. Recreation centers try to maximize the level of service. A striking example is the Pelican. Here, for 3100 tenge (580 rubles) per day, you can have a great time, and not only relax on the beach, but also improve your health. On the basis of the possibility of three meals a day to choose from guests. It is not included in the price. The territory of 4 hectares is fenced and is under round-the-clock protection. There is a playground and pools for children, a parking lot, a shop, a bar, a sauna, a massage room and a billiard room.

For living, the base offers several options: bungalows, cottages with family, superior, standard and economy rooms. Each of them has a bathroom with hot water.



Aigerim

You can also relax on the northern shore of the lake. Alakol. Recreation centers operating in the area are trying as much as possible to create favorable conditions for their guests. For example, Aigerim. Accommodation in this institution will cost from 3,500 tenge (660 rubles). You can rent wooden houses, standard rooms and VIP cottages. On the territory there is a cafe-restaurant, a Russian bath, a bar. Every evening, music programs, discos are organized. Those who wish are invited to participate in KVN and sports competitions. There is a shooting range, a billiard room, a playground, and slot machines.
