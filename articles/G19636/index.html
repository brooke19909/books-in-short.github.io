Gouves Water Park Holiday Resort 4 * (Crete, Greece, Gouves): hotel description, services, reviews

Gouves Water Park Holiday Resort is a popular tourist destination for many reasons. Everyone knows that in Greece there are a huge number of hotels. However, this hotel is not without reason considered one of the best in terms of combination of price and quality.





Location

Gouves Water Park Holiday Resort is located on Corfu Island. He is very popular. The second largest of all the Ionian Islands and the most northern. And it is here that there is a village called Gouvia. This place is associated with entertainment, fun, clubs and beach parties. However, this is exactly how it is. Therefore, mainly youth companies or people longing for outdoor activities go to Gouvia.

Gouves Water Park Holiday Resort is located in close proximity to the sea - you can walk to the beach in a few minutes. By the way, the airport is not so far from the hotel. It is called "Ioannis Kapodistrias." Not far from Gouvia (which is also called Gouves), Heraklion, as well as Hersonissos, are located. In general, from here you can quickly get to any attractions.

The hotel and all its 260+ rooms look modern, despite the fact that the building was built in 2003. The last restoration was carried out in 2007.





Entertainment

Gouves Water Park Holiday Resort is a great place to be. This hotel is widely known for having its own water park. You should definitely go there at least once. In the water park there are several slides and, of course, pools.

Also, being here, you can do water aerobics, go to the gym, play volleyball or futsal, go to the tennis court or on the mini-golf course. There are many ways to have fun. There are darts, table tennis, an amphitheater, and in the evenings the animation staff conducts various interesting shows and discos.

Here there are all the necessary conditions for the children whom the parents brought with them to rest. Two separate shallow pools, a playground, 2 mini-clubs, as well as animators who amuse kids. By the way, if parents want to be alone, they can call a nanny, but this is a paid service.

The hotel also has a diving school, a billiard room, bicycles (for rent), a massage room and a beauty salon. True, all this is also paid.





Service and Facilities

In Gouves Water Park Holiday Resort 4 * there is everything that tourists may need. There is a private car park and car rental, a small office with fax and photocopying services, currency exchange, a spacious conference room and a laundry with dry cleaning. There is no food delivery, but guests can order packed lunches at the reception if they have plans to travel somewhere for the whole day.

And also in the territory of Gouves Water Park Holiday Resort 4 * there is a mini market. There are shops nearby the hotel, but if you do not want to go anywhere, you can purchase everything you need here.

Finally, when talking about the level of service, attention should be paid to staff. Real professionals work here. And about the level of training of hotel employees says the level of their language skills. Which, by the way, is eight. The hotel has people who speak Russian, Polish, Dutch, German, French and Italian. This is in addition to Greek and English. In general, no one will have problems with the language barrier.

Nutrition

Gouves Park Watersplash Holiday Resort has some great dining options. By the way, the hotel serves all-inclusive guests.

Tourists can visit the hotel restaurant, tavern and pizzeria. In addition, there are also three bars and a cafe on the territory. The system "all inclusive" is valid from 7:30 to 23:00. It includes breakfast with lunch and dinner (buffet), a variety of snacks, ice cream, pastries, coffee with tea, as well as local alcoholic and soft drinks. Freshly squeezed juices and imported alcohol can be offered at an additional cost.
