"Test season" - what does it mean?

As you know, new equipment needs a certain testing period. The immediate plans of the administration include checking the operation of new lifts, the quality of the slopes, the operation of snowmaking and lighting systems, so that for the next season the stay at the resort for guests becomes even more pleasant and comfortable.



What awaits tourists in the season 2015-2016?

In the first season, Terrasky Park (address: Shava, Kstovsky District, Nizhny Novgorod Region) will operate at only 40% of the planned capacity. It offers guests:

  • 7 slopes of varying difficulty (including tracks with night lighting);
  • 1 baby lift and 2 ski lifts
  • training slopes (two);
  • equipment rental;
  • group of instructors;
  • Club "Balamut" for children;
  • Cafe;
  • medical Center;
  • luggage storage;
  • 5 cottages for living.




Future plans

Terraski Park is a ski resort whose development is designed for several years. It should become an interesting and comfortable place to relax, where guests of any age will find something to their liking. The creators of the complex build their work on family values. They understand how important it is to relax in the circle of their loved ones so that all members of the family are happy and satisfied.

To achieve these goals, three recreation areas will be opened in stages over three years. For example, ethno-park, cross-country skiing tracks, tubing park, rope town, "Park of the peoples of the North", extreme park, new comfortable apartments for vacationers.



It is planned that the park will operate throughout the year. Terraski Park will constantly offer tourists new programs and services so that guests receive extremely positive emotions. All vacationers will be charged with positive energy here for a long time.

What is unique about the park?

In a single natural zone, Terraski Park has combined the most popular eco-tourism destinations, which have been actively developing in Russia in recent years - ethno-park, snow-park, eco-park, children's park, extreme park. It should highlight the main directions of the complex:

1. Sports and entertainment:

  • equipped areas for snowboarding, tubing and skiing;
  • sites for the development of agility, coordination and strength for all ages.

2. Cultural and educational:

  • acquaintance with the life and culture of small peoples of the North;
  • communication with sled dogs (club "Malamute");
  • Malamute sledding.

3. Educational:

  • special thematic programs for children and their parents (club "Balamut").

4. Wellness:

  • walking trails for walks;
  • Wellness treatments in the spa center of the park.
