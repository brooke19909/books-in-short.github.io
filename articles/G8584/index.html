Bar "Garage" (Khabarovsk): menu, service, interior

The Garage Bar (Khabarovsk) is a great place to relax with friends and family in a pleasant and warm atmosphere. The bar cordially opens its doors to visitors from 12 noon. Fans of the Mafia game visit the institution on Mondays, where they enjoy a stylish and exciting game. The total capacity of the bar is up to 100 people.





Menu

In the institution you can taste delicious dishes of European as well as traditional Russian cuisine. On weekdays (Monday to Friday), bar guests can enjoy set meals. Despite its shocking and defiant style, the place is perfect for holding a business lunch and various corporate meetings. In the menu you can find such items as:

  • snacks (vegetable, fish and meat rolls, beef tongue and all kinds of cuts and sets);
  • salads (with seafood, meat, poultry);
  • hot snacks (pizza, french fries and fried cheese);
  • soups (hodgepodge, fish soup, pumpkin cream soup, etc.);
  • hot dishes (steaks, fish, barbecue, sausages, etc.).

For large and friendly companies, a set menu has been developed, consisting of several portions of various dishes. To selected goodies you can additionally order spicy sauces for every taste. Wanting to taste both alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, customers can also visit the Garage bar (Khabarovsk). The institution has a convenient location, so visitors can easily get to the bar.





Service

The service of the rock bar is top notch. At each party, a talented photographer is invited to the Garage bar (Khabarovsk), whose photo is bright, dynamic and attractive. Literally every corporate event, birthday and just a theme party are accompanied by original and fun contests, jokes and drive. The waiters will help you decide on the choice of dishes and choose the right drink. Bar guests can enjoy fiery rock music. The Garage bar (Khabarovsk) is equipped with modern professional lighting and sound equipment. The institution has:

  • convenient parking spaces;
  • bar counter (for 10-12 seats);
  • VIP rooms (up to 12 places);
  • main hall (up to 40 seats);
  • concert hall (up to 60 seats).

The rock bar invites its guests to hold family events, corporate parties, New Year's holidays, weddings, anniversaries and birthdays in a warm and friendly atmosphere. There is a specially designed banquet menu. If necessary, guests of the bar can make a reserve of tables and pre-order dishes at a personal meeting with the administrator or by telephone.

Interior

The Garage Bar (Khabarovsk, Volochaevskaya Street, 15) is a stylish establishment that has been welcoming its guests for over 8 years now. The interior is dominated by wooden elements that are organically integrated into the industrial style of the bar. Looking around, you can find a lot of interesting things that attract the attention of guests. Each piece of furniture is thought out and has its own little history. On the walls housed tires from cars and motorbikes, license plates, frames and much more.





Market

Guests of the bar can purchase gift certificates in the Garage, as well as all kinds of souvenir items with the institution's logo, such as:

  • T-shirts for men and women;
  • vinyl stickers on cars;
  • neckerchiefs.
