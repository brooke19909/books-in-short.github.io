How to make crafts from vegetables with your own hands?

An interesting solution that is suitable for the harvest festival, and if you connect imagination, then for other cases, are children's crafts made of vegetables. With your own hands (after a short training session) even a not too skillful child can repeat them. A pleasant moment will be that such a product does not require large financial costs, but it will look quite original.

Let's consider in stages how to make a craft out of vegetables with your own hands. First of all, we look in the refrigerator, basement and other places where we store food. In extreme cases, when we understand what we need, we run off to the store. We turn on the fantasy and think what the available vegetables look like. If imagination is not enough, you can use our tips. Now very little is left - to bring the idea to life!

Ready ideas



Let's try together to figure out what DIY craft projects are the easiest to do. Have the children dream up. They usually do it much better than adults.

The following are ideas for DIY crafts made from vegetables, which are sorted alphabetically (by the first letter of the name of the main component).

Eggplant can be used to make, for example, penguins. For this, it is necessary to cut the side opposite the tail (it will become a beak) at a slight angle so that the bird stands, leaning slightly forward. We clean part of the skin so that the breast is turned out, for the wings we make an incision and bend it a little up. So that the flesh of the vegetable does not darken, places that are not protected by the skin should be rubbed with salt. It remains to make eyes for

this can be used beads. By the way, you can build paws from a piece of carrot. Create some birds, and no one can take their eyes off your craft from vegetables. With your own hands, a small child is unlikely to be able to accurately cut the peel of an eggplant, so be sure to help him.

If you have a head of cauliflower lying in your refrigerator, you can use it to make lambs. From a whole head of cabbage you can make a big animal. If you disassemble it into inflorescences, you get a whole flock. The heads of lambs can be made from olives, and the legs from matches.



Another version of the craft, however, already made from fruits is the head of Baba Yaga. It can be used in various compositions or as a decoration for a room on Halloween. In order to make the head like in the photo, we take a large hard apple, cut off the peel, leaving a little bit at the tail for the hat. With a felt-tip pen, draw facial features , make sure they are large, cut them with a knife. Lower the workpiece for half a minute into a mixture of a glass of lemon juice and a teaspoon of salt. We take out, wipe and place in a warm place. After a week we get a charming face.

Imagine, experiment, and you will certainly make original crafts from vegetables with your own hands. Moreover, it is not necessary to wait for the tasks of teachers. After all, you can make a gift to loved ones - they will definitely like it!
