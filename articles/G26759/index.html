Location

Hotel "Olga" is located in the village of Sheregesh, Kemerovo region. This land is called Mountain Shoria. The name came from the name of the people who previously inhabited this territory - the Shors. And tourists call this area Siberian Switzerland for the beauty of its mountain taiga landscape.

Sheregesh is a popular ski resort where lovers of this sport can go down from any of the four majestic and most beautiful mountain peaks: Mustag, Zelenaya, Kurgan and Utuya. The season here lasts for almost seven months: from November to May.

Another reason why ski enthusiasts choose Sheregesh for their vacation is the extensive network of modern ski lifts - there are 17 of them, there are rope towers, chair lifts and cable cars. Therefore, everyone can choose to their taste!

You can get to such an institution as the Olga Hotel (Sheregesh) from Novokuznetsk, the nearest city where there is an airport, having covered 170 km by bus or taxi.

Another option may be the path through Novosibirsk. You can get to Sheregesh by direct bus or taxi. Novosibirsk and Sheregesh are separated by a distance of 530 kilometers.

From Kemerovo you can get to Sheregesh by taxi or bus.
