Origami ship: different manufacturing methods

You can create an origami ship in many ways, even for a beginner in this business. The finished craft will go to the collection of children's toys or, if the model is complex and unusual, to the shelf for souvenirs.





Simple model

A classic origami ship, the scheme of which is probably familiar to everyone from childhood, is done in a couple of minutes. What to do for those who have forgotten:





  1. A4 sheet fold in half two times.
  2. Flatten. Wrap the top two corners to the fold line.
  3. There are two free edges below, bend them up in opposite directions.
  4. Bend the protruding triangles.
  5. Fold the triangle into a rhombus, the lower corner of which should be open.
  6. Bend free corners in opposite directions up. Again, a triangle turned out, turn it into a rhombus, as in step 5.
  7. Bend the lower corners up and return to the previous position.
  8. Gently pull the top corners to the sides to reveal the boat.

The result was a simple boat!

Motor ship





To get such an origami ship, you need:

  1. Bend the corner of the A4 sheet to the opposite side, cut off the excess. It should make a square.
  2. Bend the square two times in half, then return to its previous position. This was necessary to create fold lines.
  3. Bend the upper corner to the center of the square, then, retreating 2 centimeters from the bend, bend up and retreat 3.5 centimeters, bend down.
  4. Fold the whole sheet in half.
  5. Now bend the bottom of the boat, as shown in the diagram.
  6. It remains to draw the windows.

The motor ship turned out.

Sailboat





How to make origami-ship-sailboat:

  1. Take sheet A4, bend the corner to the opposite side and cut off the excess. It turned out to be a square.
  2. Bend twice in half and return to the previous position.
  3. Bend all four corners to the center.
  4. Bend one of the corners back, retreating about a centimeter. Do the same with the opposite angle, just step back a little more.
  5. Bend the sheet in half in width. Bend both sides toward the center.
  6. Bend the lower corner in any direction.

Sailboat is ready!

Yacht





How to make a yacht out of paper:

  1. Bend the corner of the A4 sheet to the opposite side, cut off the excess. It turned out to be a square.
  2. Bend it diagonally.
  3. Bend one corner to the fold line.
  4. Bend the bottom up.

Even a child can make this simple origami ship.

Dog in a boat





To make an origami ship out of paper with a dog, you need to:

  1. From sheet A4 make a square.
  2. Bend it two times in half, deploy once. The result is a triangle.
  3. Bend one edge at an angle of about 60 degrees from the fold line.
  4. Rotate so that there is a triangle at the top.
  5. Bend the protruding triangle down, and then bend a small part of it inside. The muzzle turned out.
  6. Bend the ears.
  7. Colorize the dog.

Double pipe ship





Working process:

  1. From sheet A4, make a square and fold it two times in half. Expand.
  2. Bend all corners to the center.
  3. Turn the sheet over. Again, bend the corners to the center and flip again.
  4. Repeat the third step.
  5. Turn two opposite diamonds into a square.
  6. Fold the entire sheet in half.
  7. Turn out the edges.

Modular origami





Do you know what modular origami is? In this case, the ship will turn out easy for you. For the rest, a detailed assembly diagram of the modules:

  1. A4 sheet cut into 4 parts. To do this, bend it two times in half and unfold.
  2. Take one of the resulting triangles and bend in half.
  3. Bend the upper corners to the center.
  4. Turn the sheet over, fold up the protruding edges. And stick out the triangles inward between the triangular base and the rectangle.
  5. Fold in half.

The essence of modular origami is to combine different such details to get a three-dimensional figure.

Deck assembly

To get an origami ship from the modules, you need a little less than 1000 blanks of white, green, yellow and pink colors (colors can be changed to your liking). What do we have to do:

  1. For the first row, make 40 green modules and lay them in a circle. Make as many white paper parts and create a second row.
  2. At two opposite edges should be the stern and bow of the ship. In these places, starting from the third row, add two pink modules. A total of 44 parts.
  3. In the fourth row there are 44 white modules.
  4. On the fifth row, again add two modules for the stern and bow. In total there should be 48 yellow details.
  5. From the sixth to the 12th row, alternate white and color modules. 48 for each row.
  6. There should be 36 modules in the thirteenth row. Report a number not completely to the place of the bow of the ship. Inserting the details is backwards compared to how it was done in other rows.

Ship's bow and sail

How to collect a nose:

  1. Report the thirteenth row using 16 modules of different colors (choose your own pattern), of which two details need to be added to the center.
  2. The fourteenth row consists of 14 modules.
  3. Fifteenth and sixteenth row of 11 modules.
  4. There are 12 modules in the seventeenth row, then start decreasing by one part with each row. So, the nose should end at twentieth.
  5. On the edge of the nose, lay the green modules, you should get 40 parts.

How to assemble a sail:

  1. The first row of 13 modules, then increase to the sixth row by one piece.
  2. In the seventh row there are 15 modules, in the eighth - 16, in the ninth - again 15. In this alternation continue until the nineteenth row.
  3. In the twentieth row - 12 modules.
  4. At the twenty-first - 13.
  5. At the twenty-second -12.
  6. In the twenty third - 11.
  7. In the twenty-fourth - 12.
  8. At the twenty fifth - 11.
  9. At the twenty-sixth - 10.
  10. Gather them together back to front.
  11. Roll a cardboard sheet into a tube and fix the edge with glue. The mast turned out.
  12. Bend in the sail and glue to the mast.
  13. Grease the contact points between the sail and the ship.
  14. Make a flag of five modules and stick to the mast.
