"Privat" hotel, Sudak: hotel description, hotel reviews

Sudak is a city located on the southeastern coast of the Crimean peninsula, with a long history, founded in about two hundred and twelve years and described in the monuments of historical literature. This city, having seen many wars, was completely devastated and abandoned by its inhabitants by the eighteenth century. Thirty-three people lived in it. Over the next three centuries, the city grew, developed and became an architectural, winemaking and tourist center. The climate in Sudak is mild, with smooth changes in temperature, there is very little rainfall. The swimming season lasts almost five months.

Sudak - a place of carefree relaxation

Beautiful sand and pebble beaches, a convenient descent to warm water and the splendor of the surrounding landscape attract a large number of tourists from all over the world to the city, for whom careless conditions are created. There are a large number of hotels, hotels and pensions. They offer vacationers comfortable rooms, meals, a variety of entertainment programs and excursions. Some complexes have swimming pools and access to their own equipped beaches.
