Adler hotels with pool by the sea: a description of the best

If you want to spend the summer on the Black Sea, but not get out of the budget, then consider a vacation in Adler. Sea, sun, air - everything is like in Sochi, but much cheaper. The resort town offers you a lot of hotels, new and affordable guest houses, large fashionable complexes, in general, for every taste. Today we are interested in Adler hotels with a pool by the sea.



Pros of relaxation in Adler

Looking at the resort town, you immediately notice its focus on tourists. This is not surprising, because he lives at their expense. This becomes clear by the location of the hotel complexes. It takes quite a long time to choose Adler hotels with a pool by the sea, because there are a lot of offers. Almost all of them are located on the first beach strip.

Adler hotels also have good transport accessibility. They are very convenient to get from the airport and railway station. You can also go from here on excursions to nearby attractions, or even to Krasnaya Polyana. Adler hotels are especially good for skiers. The price tags are much more interesting than next to the ski lifts. At the same time, Adler hotels with a pool by the sea remain attractive throughout the year. In summer - for a beach holiday, and in winter - for a relaxing relaxation. Let's now consider where you can stay so that your vacation will be remembered for the whole year. The list of hotels was compiled according to the reviews of tourists.

Fairytale "Narnia"

Considering Adler hotels with a pool by the sea, I want to note first of all those that have good reviews from regular customers. That is why we are the first to consider this small and very comfortable mini-hotel, which is located in the central part of the resort, on the banks of the Mzymta River embankment . The hotel courtyard goes directly to the picturesque promenade. Nearby there is a large dendrological park, which is the best among its kind.

The hotel with its grandeur and beauty reminds Adler himself . Hotels by the sea with a pool are mostly good, but this is a real gem. The hotel courtyard is the work of the artist. In the center of the courtyard is a swimming pool with several areas, including a separate one, for swimming of children. This pond fits perfectly into the overall composition, drowning in tropical greenery and flowers. You can choose one of five types of rooms: one-room or two-room, suite or apartment. All are equipped with modern split systems, refrigerators and TVs. The hotel provides meals for a fee. The package includes breakfast only. The cost of rest - from 1500 p. (single room), and up to 5500 p. (Suite) per day per person.



Mini-Hotel Mirage

Looking at hotels in Adler near the sea with a pool, I want to highlight this small island of coziness and comfort. It is located at ul. Orthodox, 31. The four-story building offers its guests 17 rooms for living. There is a kitchen for cooking, as well as a dining room. Just 10 minutes away is a pebble beach, promenade and an entertainment center with a water park. The hotel grounds, the green courtyard with barbecue and a gazebo, a children's playground are very pleased. But the most important thing is the heated pool, which pleases lovers of swimming in comfort.

The hotel rooms have modern furniture, air conditioning and TV, refrigerator. Depending on the season, the cost of rest is from 1000 to 3000 rubles per day.

Guest house "Mila"

It is located in Adler, 10 km from Sochi. Guests benefit from terrace. Free private parking is available on site. Each air-conditioned room has a TV and a kettle. Reception is open 24 hours a day. The Winter Sports Palace is just 1.5 km from Milya Guest House. You will find a comfortable room with internet and comfortable furniture. On the territory there is a swimming pool and a recreation area.



Mini-hotel "Yusupov"

Arriving here, you can appreciate how good Adler is in the summer. Hotels by the sea with a swimming pool (it is better to plan vacations in advance, as well as book a ticket) offer comfortable accommodation for a reasonable price. "Yusupov" is located on 37 Fermersky Lane, literally 10 minutes walk from a pebble beach, dolphinarium and oceanarium, water park. Near the guest house there are 4 grocery stores, a Sberbank ATM. That is all that a modern tourist may require.

In the courtyard there is an outdoor pool with sunbeds, a barbecue under a canopy with a seating area, a kitchen and parking, as well as a washing machine. It is very pleasing that the territory is closed and is under video surveillance around the clock. The cost of renting a room is from 1000 to 3000 rubles per room per day, depending on the season.



Seven Winds

Judging by the reviews, this is the best hotel (Sochi, Adler) with a pool by the sea. It is a whole tourist complex, which is two three-story residential complexes, which are located on a large, landscaped area. In the courtyard, paved with paving stones, there is a barbecue area with barbecue and a gazebo, a huge pool.

The hotel has different rooms that will satisfy the needs of any visitor. All are equipped with household appliances and furniture, there is a spacious kitchen. Very conveniently located guesthouse in relation to all the key objects of the resort infrastructure. The embankment is only 1 minute, to the sea - no more than 10 minutes, the center of Adler - 10 minutes, to entertainment venues - 5 minutes walk. Nearby is the shopping center "Mandarin", which has a concert venue and karaoke, a bar and cafes, restaurants. The cost of renting a room is from 2000 to 3500 rubles.



Pension "Emerald"

We continue to consider mini-hotels of Adler by the sea with a pool. "Emerald" is located on the street Guards, 14 / a. It is located just 500 meters from the Black Sea coast. It features a spa and Wi-Fi. The rooms are equipped with air conditioning and a flat-screen TV. The private bathrooms include toiletries. There is a gym and laundry facilities, a pool and a kids club on site. Those who have arrived in their car can leave it in the parking lot. For other guests there is a shuttle service that will take you to the hotel, and to the station or airport. The number of rooms is represented by several categories, which are so cozy and comfortable that they are suitable for a long rest. Meals are on a fee basis, it is possible to order food in the room.



Hotel "Arno"

It is located at: Guards, 3. This is a separate building, consisting of four floors. There are 12 rooms in total, each of which is equipped with everything necessary for living. The pebble beach is a 5-minute walk away, as is the beautiful promenade. The center is only 1 minute, an amusement park 5 minutes. Very close to the market and grocery store.

Meeting guests is included in the price. At your service - a green courtyard and barbecue, gazebo and heated pool. Hotels in Adler with a swimming pool (2017 reviews are confirmation of this) are very popular among tourists. Children love them very much, and parents will not have to go to the beach as often as the children ask. The places are equipped with sun loungers, there is a sauna and a bar. Often guests spend their entire vacation here without going on excursions. On the other hand, if a person is looking for an opportunity to stay in peace and quiet, then this is ideal.
