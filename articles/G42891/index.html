La Mer 3 * (Montenegro, Rafailovici): hotel description, services, reviews

A small seaside village located in the eastern part of the famous two-kilometer golden beach of Becici (Montenegro), surrounded by wonderful Mediterranean nature, is Rafailovici, a popular resort that attracts tourists from all over the world.





In Rafalovichi, many people like the fact that the village has a convenient access from the Adriatic highway, is located in close proximity to the sea and, in addition, it is possible to get from here to Budva on the beautiful seaside promenade. The resort is notable for its dense buildings, but the presence of narrow curved streets descending to the sea, as well as small one- and two-story buildings with open terraces gives it a special cosiness. The main feature of the resort is that all hotels and villas are located here very close to the sea. One of the popular places that tourists choose to place on vacation is La Mer Hotel 3 * (Montenegro).





About the resort

Travelers who choose La Mer 3 * hotel for their stay (Montenegro, Rafailovici) should be aware of the peculiarities of local recreation.

It is known that this resort is mainly chosen by lovers of fish dishes - the region is famous for its fishing, and tourists get the opportunity here to join the difficult, but such a pleasant fishing industry.





In the village there is a restaurant "Three fishermen", extremely popular among vacationers. The golden beach of Becici, on which the resort is located, in 1935 received international recognition (Grand Prix) as the most beautiful beach in Europe. It offers great sports opportunities. Walking amidst subtropical thickets brings many pleasant minutes to vacationers.

Many vacationers come to Rafailovici with children. For young tourists 10-15 years old there are teenage clubs, as well as discos. Rafailovici recommends coming to those who are not afraid of the noise made by laughing teenagers, as well as the inability to rent a car for independent trips around the country.

La Mer 3 * (Montenegro, Rafailovici): Acquaintance

The hotel is located in the south of the Adriatic, in the resort village of Rafailovici, just 100 meters from the sea. Nearby are shops, restaurants and other infrastructure necessary for vacationers.

Hotel La Mer 3 * (Montenegro) belongs to the hotel complex Obala and Obala Plus. The institution was created to provide a comfortable stay: beach, relaxing. It focuses on staying with friendly companies, adult guests, elderly tourists. They love to come here on vacation and families with children.

For the first time La Mer 3 * (Montenegro) received guests in 2012. April, July, August are considered the most popular for recreation. Guests of La Mer 3 * (Montenegro) highly appreciate the accommodation, service and food provided. The hotel is pet friendly. The national composition of the guests in La Mer 3 * (Montenegro) is mixed.





The institution is located in one 3-storey building. The style of interior design is ordinary. It belongs to the category of small hotels.

Hotel La Mer 3 * (Montenegro, Budva) is located in a small cozy village Rafailovici (a resort in the south of the Adriatic coast), 30 km from which Tivat International Airport is located. The hotel is located just 100 meters from the sea, close to shops, restaurants and other infrastructure.

Rafailovici, the village where the La Mer 3 * hotel is located, is the most popular resort of the Budva Riviera. On the south coast of the Adriatic Sea there is no beach more beautiful and larger than the combined beach of Becici - Rafailovici. Throughout (about 3 km), its surface is covered with soft golden sand.

Along the entire beach stretches a picturesque promenade, on which there are numerous hotels, villas, coastal restaurants and cafes. These include the well-known famous Tri Ribara and Porat. Pedestrian tunnels are located at both ends of the promenade. One of them leads to the beaches of Budva. The other is on the clean and sparsely populated Kamenovo beach.
