Hotel Astoria, Anapa: overview, features and reviews of tourists

Not far from the city of Anapa, in the resort town of Vityazevo there is a hotel complex 3 * Astoria (guest house). A beautiful landscaped area with alpine slides, streams, intricate flower beds, evergreens and waterfalls attracts many vacationers. What else is interesting this place of rest?

Location

The complex is located near the beautiful promenade of the local park "Paralia". And although it‚Äôs almost 250 meters from its own beach, there are plenty of pluses at the Astoria Hotel (Anapa). One of them is a huge modern children's sports complex. It was erected opposite the complex under the Gazprom to Children program.





A little to the left of the hotel is Vityaz, the largest bowling alley on the Russian Black Sea coast. Next to it is a large cozy park, especially loved by the guests of the resort. The train station is 10 km from the hotel, and the airport is 8. The hotel can be reached from the city bus station by shuttle bus No. 8 to the Vityaz health-improving complex.

The order of residence

The hotel complex operates year-round. Check-in is allowed from 14 hours, check-out is until 12. To register at the hotel you need to provide a passport, voucher, documents for children. Guests with pets (up to five kilograms) are accepted for an additional fee. An animal passport is required.

The price includes accommodation, use of the pool, beach, show program (three times a week), secured parking, taxi service, children's playroom, on-site entertainment center, recreation areas, apartment cleaning, linen change and Wi-Fi. For an additional fee at the Astoria Hotel (Anapa) it is possible to order breakfast, lunch or dinner (200 rubles), rent a safe deposit box (200 rubles / day), laundry (up to 6 kg - 300 rubles), pets (200 rubles), rent of a Russian bath (up to 6 people - 1,000 rubles), rental of a baby carriage or crib (300 rubles) and other services.

Children

Children are accepted without restrictions. Up to five years old, the child is placed free of charge with paid two adult beds. Food in this case is not provided. Documents for children must be presented. Otherwise, settling with the child will be provided.





Reviews about "Astoria" (Anapa) talk about the good organization of children's activities. The hotel has animators, games rooms, an on-site children's entertainment town and an outdoor pool. In the room for free you can ask for a feeding chair. For a fee, rental of cots and strollers is available.

Number of rooms

The hotel complex has two buildings (five- and four-story) and a total of one hundred rooms. They are designed for vacationers of different income levels, as well as for families and children. Almost everyone has a balcony. There are no elevators in the buildings. The rooms are double rooms of economy class, studio, standard, junior suite and suite. It is also possible to relax in a four-bed cottage. Judging by the description of the Astoria Hotel (Vityazevo), the rooms are cleaned daily, linen and towels are changed every three days, there are clothes dryers.

Standard rooms, or economy

These rooms are located in the basement. Each room has two beds. If desired, one additional can be arranged: a folding sofa or a folding bed (no age limit). The apartments have bedside tables, a refrigerator, TV, air conditioning and split, as well as a convenient workplace. Bathroom - joint, equipment - modern. Rooms of this class are also on the top floor - in the attic.

Junior Suites

Such apartments can accommodate two to three guests. These rooms at Astoria Hotel (Anapa) have a double bed, upholstered furniture, bedside tables, a dressing table and a coffee table, as well as a TV, air conditioning, split and a refrigerator. Bathroom - joint, equipment - modern.

Family apartments

A two-room superior room is also provided. It is designed for four people. One extra seat is organized. One double bed and two single beds or all four single beds and a folding bed are offered as sleeping places. The room has air conditioning, shower, TV, spacious wardrobe, refrigerator and bedside tables.

Studio apartment

They are considered superior rooms. Designed for two people. Visually, the room is divided into two zones: a bedroom (with a large double bed) and a living room. There is a balcony, cabinets, comfortable furniture, a spacious wardrobe, a TV and air conditioning. Bathroom - joint with shower.





Luxury rooms

This category of rooms is two-room. Two or three people can live in them. The room has a double bed, upholstered furniture, bedside tables, a dressing table and a coffee table, as well as a TV, air conditioning, split and a refrigerator. Bathroom - joint, equipment - modern.

Hotel infrastructure

Astoria (Anapa) has its own beach and several indoor barbecue areas, which are completely free for guests of the complex. And also there are several bars, a restaurant, a pizzeria, a dining room, several places for children's games, sports grounds and recreation areas.

Nutrition

A comprehensive menu has been developed; meals are available three times a day. There is also a buffet. Its menu includes a wide range of first and second courses, soft drinks and a sweet table. A separate item of the buffet is baby food. You can have a meal in one of the bars or cafes. The on-site bar and poolside caf√©, first-floor caf√© and summer, restaurants and pizzerias serve Ukrainian, European, Caucasian and Russian cuisines. They work from 8.00 to 23.00.

The main restaurant of the hotel is an ideal place for lovers of healthy and delicious food. The rich atmosphere, impeccable service and first-class cuisine delight guests. The hall has almost full glazing. Soft curtains, covering the rays of the sun falling into the hall, comfortable sofas, wall paintings - all have a relaxed atmosphere and taste delicious dishes.





An evening meal can be enjoyed on the outdoor terrace. Comfortable chairs with high backs, tables covered with painted tablecloths create an atmosphere of relaxation and detachment from the whole world. The menu in the restaurant is of three types: children's, complex and to order. A visit to the restaurant satisfies not only physical hunger, but also saturates the soul with pleasant comfort and cozy conversations.

Banqueting hall

A large, spacious room for up to one hundred people is ideal for weddings, anniversaries, presentations and other events. Helpful waiters, dishes, even the most unusual, musical and light installations, as well as equipment for presenting presentations, will make it possible to organize the ideal event that will be remembered for a long time by guests or business partners. Free Wi-Fi and elite wines will add a twist to both a business lunch and a less formal meeting.

Additional service

The complex has a 24-hour guarded parking lot. There is a self-service domestic room. Iron and ironing board are provided free of charge, laundry services are paid separately. The administration of the Astoria Hotel (Anapa) offers assistance in organizing transfers and excursion services. Guests are offered fascinating trips and trips to unique places of the resort, as well as visits to the dolphinarium and the large Olympia water park. It is possible to invite a nanny or order other childcare services.





High-speed Internet (satellite) and international communication services are available in all hotel buildings. A safe deposit box service is available at the reception, as well as wake-up calls. At rental points, you can rent any household equipment, including an electric kettle, as well as sports and beach equipment.

Entertainment

For guests in the hotel complex "Astoria" in Anapa, a sauna and swimming pools for children and adults are organized. Monday in the pool is a sanitary day. Heating is included in cool weather or at the request of vacationers. For training athletes in the pool, tracks with a depth of 1.5 to 1.8 m are formed, and convenient starting tables are installed. In a shallow area, if desired, an experienced trainer will teach kids to swim. The hotel also has a solarium, billiards, hookah, bowling, a wine and beer shops.

Judging by the reviews of Astoria in Anapa (Vityazevo), a camera with an infrared sauna is especially popular. This procedure is for those who monitor their weight. The room temperature is maintained at 35-55 degrees. This mode warms up the body, provoking the release of excess fluid and toxins. In one session in an infrared camera, you can "lose" up to a kilogram of excess weight.

A large playroom has been prepared for the children, and there is an entertainment town on the street. Safe swing and spring jumpers are installed in it, a large sandbox with cleaned sand, several descents, stairs, horizontal bars and interesting transition bridges are organized.





In the children's room, young guests are waiting for fascinating coloring books, interesting, colorful books and exciting board games. Adults can watch their children from comfortable soft sofas or entrust caregivers to take care of children. It is possible to rent children's bicycles, scooters and rollers. In the daytime, a game show is held for children three times a week. The guys are invited to the fabulous animated journey heroes from children's books.

On the territory of Astoria Guest House (Anapa) there are several tennis courts for guests, as well as ping-pong tables, chess and mini-golf. For outdoor enthusiasts, indoor gymnasiums, volleyball and basketball courts, places for skateboarding and badminton games are organized. Fields for games are covered with a special polymer composition. Wellness facilities are offered by the fitness center, including a Russian bath and massage.

Beach

The beach at the hotel is private and guarded. It is covered with white sand, equipped with sun loungers, umbrellas, toilets, showers and changing cabins. There is a summer cafe, a bar on the beach, a night disco and animators for both children and adults. There is also a beach volleyball court.





At the rental point on the beach and the territory of the Astoria 3 * complex (Anapa), you can rent a quad bike. Trampolines, inflatable slides and other attractions are installed for children.

Environment

Near the Astoria Hotel (Anapa) there is a large entertainment center "Paralia", several restaurants, a Czech amusement park, many cafes, a laser show, discos, a shooting gallery, shops and shopping centers. A cinema and a summer theater are also within walking distance. Pleasantly surprises the hotel guests located near the health and fitness center "Vityaz": a wonderful vacation can be combined with medical procedures (it is quite easy to get a heading to the center).
