Booster - a breathtaking ride

Adrenaline, sensation of flight or weightlessness. What makes people look for these exciting emotions? The ability to experience the indescribable sensations of flight, fear, delight often lead us to a variety of amusement parks and attractions. Adrenaline helps us respond more quickly to danger, overcome weakness or lethargy, and exacerbates all our senses. Recently, much has been said about the benefits of adrenaline in controlling stress.





Thrilling rides contribute to the instant release of adrenaline into the blood. This instantly mobilizes all organs and systems of the body, and also promotes the release of endorphin, which improves mood.

The booster attraction is considered one of the most extreme and scary in the amusement park "Divo Ostrov" in the city of St. Petersburg. This park is located near the Krestovsky Ostrov metro station, in the territory of the Seaside Victory Park. "Wonder Island" was opened in 2003, in honor of the celebration of the 300th anniversary of the city. The park is constantly evolving, and the number of attractions on its territory is constantly growing. Divo Ostrov has received several awards in various competitions and is considered the best amusement park in Russia. The park has many opportunities for an excellent holiday with the whole family and children of different ages. A wide variety of attractions will allow you to choose the necessary level of extremeness for any visitor.





The Booster attraction in St. Petersburg is one of the most extreme and popular among visitors. Adults and children love him for indescribable sensations and adrenaline. The booster attraction is a huge construction similar to a mill. Four special seats are attached at the end of each of their two blades, where passengers sit. With the help of reliable mounts, lovers of extreme sensations are fixed in them and begin flying. A booster ride with a speed of more than 100 km per hour rotates the daredevils, lifting them more than 50 meters from the ground. Moreover, the seats rotate during the flight, allowing passengers to turn upside down and scream with delight!





Many daredevils visit the booster ride! Feedback from visitors after skiing is full of enthusiasm and vivid sensations! The attraction makes several upheavals during one session, which allows you to fully enjoy exciting moments. During the flight, passengers no longer understand where the sky is and where the earth is! Everything merges together in a stream of endless extreme cycle.

There is another reason to visit Booster. The attraction allows visitors to enjoy one of the best views of the city and the Gulf of Finland. Therefore, it is worth visiting it in good sunny weather. Visitors will be able to enjoy the view of St. Petersburg for a few minutes, and then plunge into the cycle of extreme sports and incredible emotions.

It must be remembered that visitors shorter than 140 and taller than 208 cm are not allowed on the booster. The attraction has a platform height of more than 50 meters and rotates the blades at a speed of 9 revolutions per minute. Visit the booster should be all lovers of thrills!
