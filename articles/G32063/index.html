Egypt, Hotel Titanic (SPA and water park): description

Description: if you decide to visit Egypt, the Titanic Hotel may be the ideal option for you. It is located on the picturesque coast of the Red Sea in the popular tourist city of Hurghada.

The original three-story hotel building, erected in 2007, was built in the form of the cruise ship of the same name. On its territory there is a large water park with a pool, a "lazy river", water slides, artificial waves and other attractions, which are famous for all southern countries, including Egypt. Hotel Titanic is only 14 km from the airport and 21 km from the center of Hurghada.

Rooms: the hotel has 608 apartments. Of these, 480 are standard, 83 are family and 40 are Junior Suites.
