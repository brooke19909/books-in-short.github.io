Good resorts in the world. Relaxation. Prices. Reviews

Many people who have worked diligently all year are encouraged by thoughts of an impending vacation. Soon vacation, and you can go to the resorts with sandy beaches. However, even the most sophisticated tourist always seeks to visit the previously unknown corners of the planet. This is where the question arises of what good resorts in the world are worth visiting, and where to relax is not recommended.



Sunny Hawaii

One of the most popular types of recreation are trips to wonderful distant islands, for example Hawaiian. There are a little more than thirty of these islands, but the most popular sea resorts among Russians:

  • Maui
  • Big Island.
  • Oahu.
  • Lanai.

The climate here is very mild, in winter the air temperature never drops below +25, while in the summer it is colder. For tourists who love exotics, waterfalls, rainforests and orchids, there is no better place to relax in the world. Reviews recommend coming to Hawaii in December, January or February. But hot vouchers at low prices, as a rule, are offered from April to the end of November.



Pearls of the Indian Ocean - Maldives

Maldives, scattered in the blue waters of the ocean like pearls, are also good resorts in the world, where many wealthy Russians like to fly on vacation. The price of a voucher for five nights will cost two for 180 to 500 thousand rubles, depending on the hotel and other amenities. It is not surprising that many enthusiastically speak of the Maldives, because it is here that gives the impression of being in a tropical paradise.

Eternal summer, white sand, blue lagoons, fabulous coral reefs and palm trees ... For lovers of exotic marine animals, corners are open where you can see the living inhabitants of the Indian Ocean. All this magnificence cannot but fascinate tourists, forcing them to return to the Maldives again ...

Ibiza is the best vacation for the youth of the whole world!

For young people who are worried not just about the good resorts of the world, but complete freedom and endless celebration, a trip to Ibiza is the perfect solution. Many who have visited this island in Spain, sharing their impressions, note the atmosphere of unlimited freedom, fireworks and fun.

For a price of one hundred thousand rubles per person, you can relax here for two weeks. Rest in Ibiza is simply impossible to forget!

Thailand Resorts

Many inexpensive good resorts of the world are located in Thailand, for example, Phuket, Pattaya and others.

Tourists who dream to relax peacefully with their whole family will be happy to meet the largest Thai island of Phuket. The most favorable time to relax in this first-class resort is from November to March. The island is very rich in beautiful places and attractions, all of them are unlikely to be seen in two weeks, but there is always the option to come to Phuket again.

Pattaya is considered the most famous and most popular tourist resort in Thailand. There is everything that a seeker of adventure or a harmonious holiday can wish for. Most of the coast is occupied by sandy beaches. Here you can find many fashionable hotels. The island is ideal for families and for the nightlife of adventurers.

Famous resort of India - Goa

Absolutely unlike the rest of the country, the Indian resort of Goa occupies more than a hundred kilometers of the ocean. A large number of sandy beaches are always ready to receive tourists from Russia and Europe. The southern part of the coast is considered more expensive and comfortable for a tourist vacation, while the northern part is inhabited mainly by advanced Americans and Europeans.



Resorts of Abkhazia

Not only in distant lands you can relax, bask in the sun or spend your holidays actively, doing water or other sports. The famous resorts of Abkhazia are also great for this and are much closer than the distant islands. The prices for vouchers will also please you - from 25 thousand rubles for one person.

For lovers of outdoor activities suitable Gagra. There will be something to do with the whole family, there is a water park and a hydropathic center. All nearby beaches are pebbled.

Another good resort with clean and fresh air and a good climate is New Athos. According to reviews, the beaches encircle the whole city, so you can go to the sea from anywhere in the city.

Not the most popular resort, but very beautiful - Gudauta. The mild climate, the famous Lake Ritsa, mountains and meadows are not inferior to Alpine beauty. Reviews indicate that here are lower prices for housing and food than in other resorts of Abkhazia, and tourists are much less.

The resort village of Abkhazia Pitsunda is located near Gagra. In this village is the only sandy coast of the country. In addition, you can visit the beautiful relict pine grove and breathe healing air. Other resorts of the Black Sea can not boast of such clean water on the beaches. In addition, the sea is not subject to storms.

Also in Abkhazia there are climatic balneological resorts with pure mineral springs and sea air. For example, the capital of the country is Sukhum. The city is protected from cold masses of air, so the weather here is beautiful all year round. The coast of the city stretches for 10 km.



Russian Resorts

For those who do not want to travel outside their home country, there are Black Sea resorts or the newly attached Crimea peninsula. Here you can relax in the summer without spending a lot of money.

In summer, in these regions, fruits and berries ripen, which grow directly on the streets. In the Krasnodar Territory, there are many options for relaxing with the whole family:

  • Anapa
  • Tuapse.
  • Gelendzhik.
  • Sochi




Anapa is considered an ideal place for children. 40 km of sandy coast are very convenient for the whole family, as they are gentle and sandy. There are also small sections of pebble beaches. Anapa has a large number of water parks, zoos, attractions, oceanariums, dolphinariums, as well as many health-improving children's complexes. If you believe the reviews, in Russia you can not find the best resort for families with children.

It is also popular to travel to Sochi. After all, here is a unique nature, well-developed infrastructure, a large number of health facilities and the cleanest sea in the region. Here you can have a great rest for families with children and outrageous youth. Festivals, the best night clubs, water parks and attractions, aquariums and dolphinariums - all that you can wish for on vacation, tourists can find in Sochi! Among the shortcomings, reviews note high housing and entertainment prices.
