Icheck Inn Jomtien Pattaya (Thailand, Pattaya): photos and reviews of tourists

Among the resorts of Southeast Asia, Thailand is certainly the most popular. Various reasons make Russian tourists eagerly want to spend their long-awaited vacation on this unimaginable tropical patch of the Earth. The many-sided and ambiguous Thailand attracts different categories of tourists: demanding wealthy travelers, Bounty-style vacationers, budget party-goers, extreme travelers, safari and diving enthusiasts, pilgrims, shoppers, sightseeing tourists, spa lovers or courageous sex tourists. It seems that there is no entertainment that can not be tested while relaxing in Thailand.

The regionalization of Thai resorts exactly coincides with their tourist specialization. The colorful metropolis of Bangkok attracts business travelers, lovers of shopping and bustling city breaks, as well as pilgrims. Phuket and Samui seem to be created for the discerning lovers of luxury relaxation, with a selected spa program, delicious food, tropical landscapes, white sands, flat beaches and clear sea. And young, not broad-pocketed, but cheerful, unpretentious and gambling tourists invite bright Pattaya.





About the colorful resort of Pattaya

Pattaya is the most full-blooded and crowded resort in Thailand. However, at the same time and the most budget. Established regular and charter flights between the airports of Russia and Pattaya created the preconditions for an even greater concentration of tourist flows to this already crowded resort. It is a few hours drive from the main capital Suvarnabhumi Airport, which serves most of the international scheduled flights. However, Pattaya also has its own regional air gate - U-Tapao Airport, where many charters from eastern Russian cities fly in the summer.

For all its brightness and attractiveness, Pattaya is hardly suitable for lovers of a classic beach holiday. The sea in this area can hardly be called "clean as a tear," and most of the beaches are very crowded. But for lovers of nightlife, diving, shopping, dating, gastronomic adventures, Pattaya is ideal. To match the entire resort, hotels here are also mostly budget.

How to choose a hotel in Pattaya?

When choosing a property in Pattaya, you should pay attention to its location, price, reviews of tourists and a range of services. The logic of official stellar accreditation in Thailand, or rather the absence of this same logic, puzzles many travelers. In addition, the declared category of the hotel may not be true, to put it mildly, you must be prepared in advance. For example, the Icheck Inn Jomtien Pattaya 3 * hotel has an official 3 * category, but surpasses many 4 * and higher hotels in the selection and quality of services.

The wrong hotel can spoil any vacation, so when choosing a hotel, you should arm yourself with skepticism about the star category and not take the time to collect reviews on specialized portals or from friends who have vacationed in Thailand.
