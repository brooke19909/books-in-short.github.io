Grand Hotel Excelsior 4 * (Italy, Amalfi): description of rooms, service, reviews

The coast of Italy is one of the most favorite places for lovers of quality beach holidays. The Amalfi Coast is particularly attractive. There are many beautiful resort cities, and each has its own special charm. Amalfi (Italy) is the main city on this wonderful coast. There are many historical attractions, which makes the resort attractive not only for fans of lazy relaxation, but also for lovers of educational tourism. Amalfi has about a dozen upscale four- and five-star hotels, and one of them is the Grand Hotel Excelsior 4 *. This hotel will be discussed in our article. We will also tell you about the resort of Amalfi.



What is attractive about Amalfi?

The first thing that catches the eye of tourists in this town is a magnificent cathedral, the walls of which are decorated with Byzantine mosaics. The high ceiling and the Saracen dome are supported by Romanesque columns. Many of the hotels that can be seen along the coast are built in the form of ancient palaces - they are majestic and romantic, but the architecture of modern complexes is also quite attractive. And just such hotel Grand Hotel Excelsior 4 * belongs. Amalfi is a commune with about 6,000 inhabitants. The city is part of the province of Salerno. It is noteworthy that in 1997, the coast of the same name with the city was declared an international heritage by UNESCO. By the way, according to legend, the name of the town came from the name of the mermaid - the beloved wife of Hercules (Hercules). After she died tragically, her demigod husband buried her on this shore.



How to get there

If you bought a tour to the Grand Hotel Excelsior 4 * hotel , then you will have to fly either to Rome, or Sorrento or Salerno. And from there you can get to the resort by bus. The road runs along the coast, and tourists arriving in Italy, even before arriving at their destination, will be able to enjoy the beautiful sea views. By the way, the hotel provides a free shuttle service to guests.

About hotel

Hotel Grand Hotel Excelsior 4 * has a beautiful panoramic location. The 7-story building was built in 1962 in the Art Nouveau style. In addition to the main building, the hotel has several smaller buildings that have different purposes. All of them are buried in the greenery of tropical plants - palm trees and lemon trees, whose aroma spreads throughout the district. In total, the hotel has 104 rooms, and all of them have balconies or terraces overlooking the bay, the azure sea, and this is the biggest plus! The interior of the buildings combines elegance and restraint, there are many elements of decor. The decoration used upscale materials such as precious wood, marble, etc. The whole territory consists of terraces decorated with flower beds, mini-parks, bizarre-shaped pools, which are surrounded by orange and blue umbrellas. The bar is located in a natural grotto and in design resembles a fairy-tale dungeon: uneven rocky arches, reigning twilight, jazz music - all this has a wonderful rest.



Location

Grand Hotel Excelsior 4 * is located 4 km from the center of Amalfi (Italy) and 80 kilometers from the airport. The hotel seems to hang over the bay, so from every point on the territory of the grant hotel offers magnificent views of the sea coast. The hotel buildings are buried in a tropical garden with numerous evergreens and colorful flowers, which during flowering spread a breathtaking aroma. It is always very quiet and calm, and therefore you can completely relax and enjoy your vacation. The beach is just a 10-minute drive, and tourists get there by a free shuttle bus. By the way, shuttle services to the city center are also provided to tourists free of charge.



Room Description Grand Hotel Excelsior 4 *

As already noted, the hotel has 104 rooms of several categories, namely: economy class, superior and deluxe. The classification of apartments is also based on the number of beds in the rooms, as well as the opening view from the window. Consider what amenities are provided in the rooms of each of the above categories.

Economy class

All rooms of this type are double. Their area is 20 square meters. But they come in two types - with one double bed or with two single beds. These rooms are not located in the main building, in a separate building. The entrance there is from the garden. This room type offers a view of the coast. There is also a fairly spacious balcony with outdoor furniture. They are equipped with individual air conditioning, satellite TV, a minibar (fridge), a bathroom with minimal toiletries and a hairdryer.

Superior Double Room

These rooms are 25 square meters. meters. Rooms of this type come with only one double bed, but they have access to the connecting economy double room. In addition to the bed, wardrobe and bedside tables, they also have a desk, additional armchairs. If necessary, a cot can be delivered to the room. Everything else is the same as in the standard double room.



Triple Room with Sea View

Rooms of this category are more spacious - their area is 30 m¬≤. They come in two types: with one double and two single beds and with three single beds. It also has a balcony with great views of the coast. In these rooms, the bathroom and toilet are separate, and the bathroom has a bathtub and shower, hairdryer, free toiletries, a full set of towels.

Suite

These rooms are 45 square meters. They are two-level, like studios. At each level there is a bathroom with a Jacuzzi with hydromassage. Instead of a balcony, there is a spacious terrace overlooking the Amalfi Coast. The room has a bedroom and a living room, a working area with a desk. Everything else is the same as in other apartments.

By the way, the hotel has special non-smoking rooms and for people with disabilities. No smoking throughout. Pets are also not allowed.
