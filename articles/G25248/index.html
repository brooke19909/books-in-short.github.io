Maple Leaf Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang, Vietnam): description of service, reviews

The resorts of Vietnam in terms of the development of tourism infrastructure can even argue with recognized market leaders - India, Egypt, Thailand, Montenegro, etc. For a comfortable stay, there is literally everything here: luxurious beaches, interesting sights, all kinds of entertainment venues.

Perhaps the most popular resort in Vietnam is, of course, Nha Trang. Holidays spent in this southern city will certainly be remembered by tourists for many years. Of course, there are many good hotels in Nha Trang. For example, vacationers can rent good rooms at Maple Leaf Hotel 3 *.





Where is located

This hotel is located almost in the center of the main tourist zone of Nha Trang (500 m). Although the hotel is a city hotel, the distance from it to the seashore is only about 100 m. Not far from this hotel there are several popular tourist attractions - the city sailing club, Tram Huong Tower, Nha Trang night market. Various shops, bars and restaurants are located next to this hotel.

Quite conveniently, this hotel, according to many tourists, is located in relation to the airport. Maple Leaf Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang) vacationers who rented rooms here do not have to get to the hotel for too long. Domestic tourists arrive, who decided to relax in Nha Trang, of course, usually to Kamran airport. This air hub is located only about 25 km from the hotel.

Tourists attribute some of the minuses of this complex to the fact that the transfer buses of tour operators never reach the very entrance to it. A few tens of meters, tourists who rented rooms here have to walk on foot with luggage in their hands. The fact is that the Maple Leaf Hotel is located at the very end of a rather narrow lane. And, unfortunately, the bus simply cannot turn around here. When leaving the hotel, some vacationers give its administrator some money. And those in this case organize the delivery of luggage of guests directly to the place of transfer stop.

Of course, the fact that the bus does not reach this hotel can be attributed to its disadvantages. But some tourists, on the contrary, consider this arrangement a great advantage of the hotel. After all, the hotel is located in the very center of the vibrant tourist life of Nha Trang and at the same time in a very quiet place.

Maple Leaf Hotel Apartment 3 *: general hotel description

This complex took its first guests recently - in 2016. That is, this hotel is currently completely new. From all sides this hotel is surrounded by other Vietnamese complexes. Therefore, the views from the windows of her rooms usually open onto the walls of neighboring hotels. But at the same time, the Maple Leaf building is quite large in comparison with the houses located next to it. Therefore, tourists who check into the rooms of this hotel, starting from the 7th floor, can still enjoy either the city or the sea.





Some rooms at this hotel, unfortunately, do not have windows at all. This is for tourists who decide to rent a house here, you should know. Sometimes in such uncomfortable dark rooms they even populate those vacationers who have booked a higher category of accommodation for themselves in the hotel. In this situation, tourists should definitely defend their rights at the reception. Typically, administrators in order to not have a lot of noise, immediately move vacationers to those rooms that they paid for initially.

When booking and checking in, tourists should bear in mind that the numbering of rooms in this hotel is quite unusual. The first digit in it means the floor. The next two are the actual room number.

Pets, just like smoking, are not allowed in the rooms at Maple Leaf, as in almost any other Vietnamese hotel. Residents of Russia and the CIS countries and Europeans also live in this hotel. A lot of the hotel is populated by the Chinese.

Hotel room reviews

Rented at the Maple Leaf Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang), lodging praises most of its guests. The rooms, as noted by vacationers, in this hotel tourists are offered spacious and bright. When is the best time to arrive at the hotel?

The holiday season in Vietnam lasts almost all year round. So, in fact, tourists can come here to relax at any time convenient for them. However, as in any other resort, the season here is divided into high and low. It is believed that the best weather in Vietnam is from January to August. Weather in Nha Trang in November, late October and early December can still let tourists down. At this time, the rainy season is in Vietnam. In November, for example, this city has 20 times more rainfall than in February. True, the rains at this time in Nha Trang are short-lived. After them, the sun comes out quickly - and again tourists can calmly sunbathe on the beach.
