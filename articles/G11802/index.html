Nabeul, Tunisia: traveler reviews on hotels and places of interest in the city

Tunisia is a popular destination for Russian tourists. Today this is perhaps the only state where the Russians are provided with the most comfortable, high-quality, affordable and, most importantly, safe rest. Recently, it is our compatriots who have put safety at the forefront, trying to avoid crowded resorts.

Nabeul (Tunisia), reviews of which are left by many tourists, can hardly be called a popular resort. This is a very small town, the center of pottery, located just 16 km north of Hammamet. Nevertheless, tourists come here every year who want to spend time away from the hustle and bustle.

If you want to get acquainted with the culture and lifestyle of local residents, Nabeul (Tunisia) will be, perhaps, the ideal option for your vacation. There are very few hotels here, tourists do not violate the usual rhythm of life of the local population, who continues to engage in agriculture and pottery. This is attractive and a small town for travelers.





Who will suit the resort

So, if you want to enjoy peace and quiet, the ideal option is Nabeul (Tunisia). Reviews about the resort of real tourists only confirm that it is a quiet and peaceful place. Some call it a Mediterranean tale. Sights and entertainment are not so many that allows you to spend time in unity with nature. Comfortable hotels offering budget holidays, chic beaches with a wide coastline, proximity to the cultural center of the country - all this makes Nabeul (Tunisia) an attractive place for tourists.

How to get there

Nabeul is located between the two largest airports in the country: Tunisia and Andife. There is another airport - in Monastir, but it is located at a considerable distance. Whatever option you choose for yourself, a bus, train or minibus will help you arrive in Nabeul (Tunisia). Traveler and tourist reviews recommend taking the number 102 shuttle bus serving the Tunis-Nabeul route. The fact is that most tourists fly to this airport. The bus runs with an interval of 30 minutes from 6:30 to 20:30 hours local time. Travel time is 40-50 minutes, the fare is 5 Tunisian dinars.





If we talk about ordinary buses, you can get from the capital to Nabeul by bus number 140 or 104. In this case, the route passes through other cities of the country, due to which the travel time increases to 1.5 hours. If you want to get acquainted with local trains, you can use them. And, of course, a taxi is at your service.

Transport connection of the resort

If you are planning an independent trip, you probably want to know all about the intricacies of moving around the city and its environs. It is convenient to get around the city by taxi. Indeed, no matter how strange it may sound, a taxi is the safest and most efficient way to get from one point to another. We advise you to be extremely careful and watch that the driver turns on the meter during the trip. On average, the cost will be 3-5 dinars.

There is another subtlety that should be known to everyone who chose Nabeul (Tunisia) for their holidays. Traveler reviews tell us that on Fridays, when active trade is developing in the city, taxi drivers ask for twice as much for each trip as on the other days. A little trick: on Fridays, spend the first half of the day on the beach, but it is better to move around the city after lunch, at this time the excitement subsides, and everything returns to normal.

Between Nabeul and Hammamet it is possible to travel by regular regular buses and fixed-route taxis. You can always see the schedule at the bus station.

Climate

Tunisia, the city of Nabeul in particular, has a Mediterranean climate. That is, the winters are warm and the summers are quite hot. Even in winter, the water temperature does not drop below 16 degrees, so you can absolutely safely choose this resort for your vacation. The only thing is that in the winter there are quite severe cold winds here, so in the evening in the city, on the coast in particular, it is quite cool. If your trip is planned for this time of the year, be sure to bring warm clothes with you.

In summer, heat reigns, the average air temperature is 35 degrees, water - 27 degrees. The sea breeze allows you to endure the sweltering heat, which is why the climate of Tunisia is often called comfortable and pleasant.





Beaches

The beaches of Nabeul are comfortable, they delight with a wide coastline, a gentle approach to the sea. Almost all local hotels have their own beaches with sunbeds. The cleanest sea, this continues throughout the year. Local plants create a pleasant shadow from the sweltering summer heat.

The indisputable advantage of Nabeul is the presence of numerous wild beaches, allowing you to fully appreciate the unity with nature.

Hotels

As already noted, Nabeul can hardly be called the center of resort life in the country, so there are very few hotels here. For the most part, these are modest, but rather comfortable hotels, located on the first and second coastlines, that is, almost on the coast. All hotels, of which there are no more than 12 in the city, are arranged in a chaotic manner. If you decide to take a walk from your hotel to the next one, your path will definitely go through urban residential areas, and it will be close. Such is Nabeul. Hotels are predominantly three-star; there are a couple of two- and four-star options. You will definitely not find anything fashionable and luxurious here. Let's talk about the most popular hotels in the city.

Kitchen

Red peppers, tomatoes, local spices and citrus fruits are the pride of the local land. Local housewives will be able to please all the guests of the city with dishes that you will not find in any other city in the country. We strongly recommend at least once visiting a local restaurant or cafe and tasting a national dish - shorba soup with fennel or rice with gravy from orange tree flowers. The dishes are very specific, but they become a reflection of life in this city.

The shops

It has already been noted that Nabeul is the center of the country's pottery. That is why one should not be surprised at the huge jug decorating the square of the railway station. Ceramic products are the pride of the city and its value. That is why every tourist is simply obliged to purchase for himself something from ceramics in one of the local shops. If you are not limited in time, we recommend that you visit one of the workshops and order a souvenir made according to your own sketches and decorated with the author's inscription.





From Nabeul you can bring straw products: all kinds of beds, rugs, baskets and huge chests. All products are made by the best masters of the city and its environs.

On the Friday market, tourists will be able to purchase many souvenirs for relatives and friends, so be sure to choose a time and visit it.

Leisure

Ancient Nabeul was founded in the 5th century BC, until today it has not been preserved in its former guise, as it was destroyed during the Third Punic War. The ancient city was located just 1.5 km from the coast; today, this place has an archaeological park. Here, tourists can enjoy the ruins of the ancient city.





The Archaeological Museum of Nabeul is a favorite attraction for many tourists. It is quite small, but quite interesting. Among the most valuable exhibits can be identified Punic jewelry, antiquities and even objects of the burial of Carthage.

All tourists love to be photographed with huge jugs that embody the specifics of life in Nabeul. One of them is located in the square in front of the railway station.

And here is another attraction of the city - a private museum Dar Zaman. The former provincial house was reserved for him. Here everyone will be able to get acquainted with the life of the inhabitants of the 19th century, to appreciate wedding dresses, household items and furniture.

We advise you to visit the chic orange groves located in the vicinity of the city, as well as vineyards. The top of Cape Bon is decorated with a small fishing village called Al-Hawaria. It has been preserved almost in its original form, therefore it is of particular interest to foreign tourists. The fine sand here gives way to the magnificent grottoes, which have become a haven for numerous crustaceans.

To summarize

Nabeul (Tunisia) is a small town located on the Mediterranean coast. A small number of hotels, a minimum of entertainment do not make it unsuitable for a good rest. Every year, tourists tired of the hustle and bustle and the frantic rhythm of the metropolis come to Nabeul. The hotels here are not fashionable, but quite comfortable. Each vacationer will have a unique chance to be alone with himself, enjoy the local beauties and views. Be sure to get acquainted with the local cuisine, even if you are not a gourmet, as well as the lifestyle of the local population. This will allow you not only to relax at sea, but also to replenish your tourist luggage of knowledge and get to know the country better.
