Who are the hussars?

In order for the child to understand whose costume his mother sews to him, it is necessary to explain to him who the hussars are. These are lightly armed warriors who rode horses. The word "hussar" is Hungarian, since the first such riders appeared in this European country. You can show your child photographs or old paintings depicting a hussar, consider their beautiful shape. A hussar costume is really very attractive. It comes in completely different colors and consists of many parts. Further in the article, we will take a closer look at what elements are in this costume, what is the name of the hussar hat and uniform decoration.

Costume details

Before you start sewing with your own hands a hussar costume for a boy, you need to know what parts this outfit consists of and how they are correctly called. This will allow the child in school or kindergarten to show off knowledge in front of peers.

  • Dolman. A short tunic that had a double row of beautiful gold buttons and buttonholes. They were fastened with special twisted ropes of golden or silver color. Sometimes, embroidery with gold or silver threads was made around each button.




  • Tights. These are tight tight pants. The child can wear simple trousers in black, white or blue (depending on what color of uniform you have in mind), and flashing stripes on the sides with gold or gray satin ribbons.
  • Boots. This costume item can be skipped. It will be enough to wear black shoes or Czech shoes.
  • Mentic. It is like a broken cloak, but warm and trimmed with fur.
  • And, of course, the brightest and most colorful attribute is the hussar hat. Its correct name is a shako. This is a tall, cylindrical-shaped headpiece with a visor and beautiful jewelry. A fluffy feather or pompom was often worn on top. Each army had its own special decorations on the shako, so making a hussar hat is a matter of fantasy for the master.

Necessary for sewing measurements

To sew a jacket or doloman, you will need to measure the length of the sleeve, shoulder, arm circumference, neck circumference, chest and thigh circumference, and the height of the stand-up collar. To sew a hussar hat, you need to measure the circumference of the head and approximately calculate the height of the shako. This is done at the request of the cutter.

If you yourself will be sewing trousers for the hussar outfit, then you will also need such measurements: hip circumference, trouser length, lower leg width, waist circumference.

  • Material for sewing pants. It should be dark and dense fabric. Although you can sew and white leggings.
  • Material for sewing doloman and mentics. Here it is already necessary to select brighter colors of the fabric - red, bright blue, emerald, burgundy, etc. The color scheme may be different.

  • Cardboard for a pattern of headgear.

Variety of shakos

Hussar caps vary in color and height. They can be white, black or in harmony with the color of the uniform. If red is broken, then the shak can also be made red.

In height, they may not be too high - up to 20 cm.

But the decoration differs dramatically. Axelbants can be located along the visor from one edge to the other, from one upper corner of the cap to the opposite middle or to the opposite upper corner. An icon or emblem is attached in the center. You can search the volumetric sticker in gold or silver in the sewing accessories store to resemble the metal emblem of a hussar regiment. To design a hussar hat for the New Year, you can attach a pompom to the center of the child on top of the bottom or attach a fluffy feather.

It is also possible to sew a gold strip on the upper and lower parts of the tulle, on a black background of a shako - a strip in the color of a camisole.

Work order

  1. Make the necessary measurements of the child.
  2. Draw patterns on costume paper and make patterns on the cardboard carnival hats of the hussar.
  3. Transfer patterns to material.
  4. Sew all the seams on a typewriter and attach "zippers" to the pants and jacket.
  5. To do decoration: sew metal gold buttons and accelerating bands, embroider and sew epaulettes on the shoulders.
  6. Before you make a hussar hat, you need to transfer the template to the material and then design a headpiece from cardboard, putting all the pieces together.
  7. Sheathe a cardboard cap with cloth and decorate the headpiece. We will discuss this in more detail later in the article.

The easiest hussar costume

For a boy, you can make a rider outfit with your own hands, without putting much effort. This option is suitable for those mothers who rarely encounter sewing, do not have a sewing machine, special skills and abilities. Such a simple suit consists of black trousers. You can decorate them on the sides with stripes for beauty. To do this, you need to buy a golden ribbon 2 cm wide (the length corresponds to the double length of the trousers plus a couple of sentiments for bending from the bottom and top) and choose the same thread color.

First, the bottom of the pants is hemmed along the seam, so that the tape starts from the inside inside the leg. Then the seam goes to the front side. To avoid wrinkles, it is better to place the workpiece on a flat surface, for example, on a table. Finishing sewing is also necessary on the wrong side, but already on the belt. After completing this procedure, the bottom of the suit will be ready on both sides.

Vest

The role of a doloman can be performed by a vest of red or blue color (the costumes of these riders were of different colors, so that the uniform can be sewn by anyone, even green or burgundy). A white or color turtleneck can be worn underneath. It is advisable to wear the same color under a red vest.

Let's consider in more detail what a vest is. It is sewn from two halves - the front and back, like an ordinary vest, only the neckline needs to be made semicircular and small, like for a sweater. You can sew the front part whole, and make two halves on the back and sew the ‚Äúzipper‚Äù.

The main work will be to decorate the vest. First you need to mark the middle and on both sides symmetrically sew eight beautiful round buttons. They are sewn in pairs at an equal distance from the center line of the front. Next, you need to have a braided rope, twine, a beautiful gold edging that plays the role of accelerants, and sheathed figured around buttons. It can be three loops located down, up and away from the center. Shamrocks look beautiful. Then you will need to connect the buttons together in pairs with a rope. It remains to make epaulettes on the shoulders.

Epaulettes

On the doloman on the shoulders there are epaulettes. These are rounded shoulder straps with fringe. Consider how you can do them yourself. If you have a hussar costume has gold axelbands, then you need to buy a gold edging with a fringe.





The epaulette itself can be made of cardboard, lined with red or gold material. The same beautiful button is sewn on the thin side of the shoulder strap as on the doloman. A fringed hem is sewn along the rounded edge so that the threads hang on the shoulders. You can create some kind of drawing from the edging, as in the photo above.

Costume

If you have the skills of a seamstress and a sewing machine, then you can sew the entire costume of the brave hussar of two elements: doloman and mentica. Usually a suit is worn as follows. Doloman is put on his chest and all buttons are completely fastened, and the mentic is hung on one shoulder, and it hangs on the back of the axel.

When sewing, you just need to make two identical doloman, in the form of a jacket with a collar in the form of a rack. A mentic differs from doloman only in that its edges are sheathed in fur (bottom, neck and sleeves).





The mentic is attached with a rope from the neck under the arm of the opposite hand. Then he will not fall off his shoulder and disturb the child at the holiday.

Pattern

The pattern of the cap of the hussar consists of three separate elements: tulle, bottom and visor. To draw such a pattern, you need to measure the circumference of the head. This will be the length of the body. Having determined by eye what height will be enough for your child to shake, draw a rectangle. Do not forget to leave a couple of centimeters on the hem so that the cardboard shaver can be glued or fastened with a stapler.





Having curled up a tulle around, we outline the circumference of the bottom. Around it you need to draw a lot of triangles to firmly adhere the bottom to the top. A visor is also drawn along the same circle, and triangles are also drawn on its upper part, on which thick PVA glue is subsequently applied. However, before fastening the parts together, according to these templates, you need to transfer the pattern to the fabric from which the shako will be sewn for a brave hussar costume.

The material is connected first by the height of the rectangle, then sewn on the bottom. The visor pattern is transferred to a fabric bent in half. Further, the material is sheathed around it from two sides. Only then the sheathed visor is sewn to the shako.
