How to Transfer Contacts to iPhone with Nokia: Tips

About the ways

In fact, everything is not so difficult. Contacts from Nokia to iPhone are much easier to transfer than it seems. The user has a certain freedom of choice to solve the problem.

You can pay attention to the following tricks:

  • transfer with a SIM card;
  • Using iTunes
  • Work with iCloud and other cloud services.

These are the simplest solutions. Alternatively, you can replace iTunes with iTools. In this case, data transfer will require less effort.

SIM cards

How to transfer contacts to iPhone with Nokia? The first and easiest solution is to use an old SIM card.

What to do under such circumstances? You need to act like this:

  1. Turn off Nokia.
  2. Remove the SIM card from the corresponding telephone.
  3. Insert SIM card into iPhone.
  4. Turn on the apple device.

Nothing more is needed. It remains to open "Settings", "Mail, addresses, calendars" and "Import from SIM". These actions will help transfer contacts from the SIM card to the memory of the "apple" smartphone. Transferring contacts takes a minimum of time. Only this technique helps those who do not plan to change the phone number. Otherwise, it is recommended to use other tricks.

Export from Google

How to transfer contacts to iPhone with Nokia? The following scenario is the unloading of contacts from Google-mail. To do this, you will have to synchronize Nokia with Google.

What exactly will need to be done? The following instructions will help:

  1. Open on Nokia Google.
  2. Log in to your mail.
  3. Go to the "Contacts" section.
  4. Click on the "More" button.
  5. Click on the "Export" line.
  6. Click on vCard.
  7. Save the document to your computer.

This is only part of the entire data transfer process. How to copy contacts to iPhone? Now it remains to correctly transfer the generated document to the "apple" device.



ITunes Help

Let's start by using iTunes. This decision is made most often. How to transfer contacts to iPhone c Nokia?

Once the appropriate data archive is ready, you can proceed with decisive action, namely:

  1. Launch iTunes on your computer. This utility is free. It works with different operating systems.
  2. Connect iPhone to PC. A USB cable is used for this purpose.
  3. Log in to ITUs using AppleAid.
  4. Move contacts to iTunes window.
  5. Select items: "Information", "Contacts" and "Synchronize".

It remains to wait a few minutes. The procedure will end, and the contacts will be downloaded to the "apple" device.

iCloud to the rescue

Contacts are sometimes transferred from Nokia to iPhone using iCloud. To do this, preload the phone book in vCard. How to do this has already been said above.

As soon as the relevant document with contacts is formed, it is recommended to proceed as follows:

  1. Open icloud.com on your PC.
  2. Log in using your Apple ID profile.
  3. Go to the "Contacts" section.
  4. Click on "Settings". The corresponding button looks like a gear.
  5. Click on "Import vCard".
  6. Indicate the path to the location of the prepared telephone book.
  7. Open iPhone and go to iCloud.
  8. Click on the "Sync" button, then open "Contacts".

Usually the last two steps are redundant. Synchronization with "iCloud" occurs automatically. Therefore, the transfer of contacts takes a minimum of time and effort. The main thing is to have an Internet connection on your mobile device. Otherwise, it will not work to realize the idea.



iTools

Thinking about how to transfer contacts to iPhone with Nokia, the user should be prepared for innovative solutions. For example, use third-party applications to translate ideas into reality. Such programs are often referred to as ITUs. This utility is somewhat reminiscent of iTunes, but it is not fully capable of replacing it.

How to copy contacts to iPhone in a similar way? In solving this problem, the following actions will help:

  1. Install iTools on the computer. Both Russian and English versions are now available.
  2. Connect iPhone to computer. It is advisable to do this via a USB cable. Otherwise, errors and malfunctions are not ruled out.
  3. Download contact list with Nokia. We have already been told about this process.
  4. Launch IT Tools.
  5. Go to the "Contacts" section in the left menu.
  6. Drag the prepared phonebook to the corresponding iTools field.

But this is not all possible solutions. Some users are able to transfer the phone book from Nokia to iPhone without additional utilities.

Google sync

Namely, by synchronizing the "apple" device with Google mail. In this case, the data is imported in just a few clicks.



What to do? The following steps are required on the iPhone:

  1. Open device settings.
  2. Select "Mail, addresses, calendars".
  3. Click on the line "Add account".
  4. In the menu that appears, click on the line that says Google.
  5. Provide data from your Google profile. It had previously been used on a Nokia phone.
  6. Click on "Sync".
  7. Put a checkmark next to "Contacts". All other marks should be removed.
  8. Confirm action.

At this point, the Apple device will sync with Google Mail, and Nokia contacts will be copied successfully.
