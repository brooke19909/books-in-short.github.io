"Web Loan": reviews, features, conditions and documents

It is not always possible to borrow money from friends or relatives, but people rarely like to devote someone to their monetary problems. If time permits, you can contact the bank, but more often than not, finances are urgently needed. Despite the fact that people fear microfinance companies, their demand in the banking services market is only increasing. Over the past 4 years, the working capital of recorded MFIs has increased 5 times. The company "Web loan" offers flexible conditions and quick delivery times. Before applying for a loan, it is recommended to evaluate your financial capabilities in order to prevent delays.

Filling out an application

To receive money in a microfinance company, it will take 20-30 minutes of free time. "Web loan" works through the Internet, so the loan application is submitted online.

  1. First you need to register on the site, for this you will need to specify a valid email address and current phone number. A confirmation letter will be sent to the mail, a mobile number will be needed when the application for a web loan is submitted directly.

  3. Also, to get money you need to specify the details. According to customer feedback, "Web loan", it is most convenient to use a card.
  4. The borrower selects the desired amount and term of the microloan. If everything suits him, he should put a tick in the highlighted box and click the "Continue" button. The mark means the consent of the client to the processing of personal data, the use of electronic signatures, methods of interaction and direct debit.

Borrower Requirements

According to customer reviews, Web Loan is a loyal financial organization that is ready to meet customers with a tainted credit history. Therefore, this MFI has a minimum set of requirements for borrowers. A person applying for a microloan should not have open arrears and should be in the age group of 18 to 90 years. If even in the past the client had minor delays in payments, the MFI is ready to turn a blind eye to this.

Also, an individual must have a registered bank card or a personal bank account. It is forbidden to use other people's details for these purposes.

Loan terms

Web Loan has several rates, but the average conditions are the same, as for most MFIs.

  1. Loan term - from 5 to 30 days. You can pay off a loan earlier, but in any case, you will have to pay for the first five days of use.
  2. The first loan is from 1.5 to 15 thousand rubles. Upon repeated appeal, the client can receive up to 30 thousand rubles.
  3. The loan rate is from 0 to 2.3%. The specific percentage depends on the tariff.

Ways to get money

The microfinance company Web Loan has one head ground office, all the rest of its work is online. Therefore, to receive money, you need either a registered bank card or a personal account of any bank. Card can be any payment system. Handing out money is not carried out.

Transferring finance to a bank account can take up to 3 days. Transfer to a plastic card is made instantly. The return of borrowed funds is carried out in the same ways as the issuance.

  1. Transfer by card directly through the Web Loan website, just go to your personal account and click the "Pay off loan" button, then follow the instructions. To pay, you will need access to a mobile phone to which a credit card is attached.
  2. Payment through the bank at the bank details. Before this, it is necessary to clarify the amount for repayment in your personal account. The purpose of payment shall indicate the offer number and the date of issue of the loan.
  3. Through the payment service ROBOKASSA. It allows you to make a payment using a credit card, from a mobile phone account or electronic wallets.

What to do in case of delay?

Even bona fide borrowers may have unforeseen circumstances from time to time. A delay in the "Web loan" will lead to a deterioration in credit history, as the company cooperates with one of the largest bureaus - Equifax.

The client must try to prevent the appearance of overdue debts. If it is not possible to pay the full amount, it is recommended to extend the loan for any convenient period from 7 to 30 days. You can also do this through your personal account, through online payment.

The longer the delay, the worse the credit rating becomes. This means that in the future, the lender may refuse to grant a loan or borrow money on unfavorable conditions for the client.
