Ipanema Beach Park 3 * (Mallorca) - photos, prices and reviews

Mallorca is an island owned by Spain and attracts a large number of people from all over the world. Here are unique beaches and nature that will never leave indifferent any tourist who has visited this stunning island. Deciding to relax in Mallorca, you need to choose the most suitable hotel for yourself. If you do not plan to spend a large amount of money, then a good option might be Ipanema Park Beach 3 *, surrounded by beautiful gardens, fragrant in the mild summer climate of Mallorca.



Hotel building

The hotel itself was built at the end of the twentieth century. The hotel is a complex of two buildings that are connected to each other at an angle of 90 degrees. This arrangement helped elegantly fit the large outdoor pool inside the courtyard. The hotel has 6 floors. Moreover, on the ground floor there are restaurants, bars, entertainment rooms, as well as rooms for working personnel and a kitchen. All other five floors are fully given to the rooms. The location of the hotel can please, as the airport is only 7 km away and 100 meters from the stop. Arenal City Center is half a kilometer from Ipanema Beach Park Hotel. In order to get to the seashore, it is necessary to overcome a distance of 500 meters.



How to get to the hotel

If you have already chosen a hotel, then the second question is how to get to it. The hotel itself, as already mentioned, is located in a small but very beautiful resort town called Arenal. The airport in Mallorca is one. And it is only 7 km away from Ipanema Beach Park. Therefore, getting from the airport to the hotel is not difficult. Regular flights from Moscow and other major cities of the Russian Federation fly to Mallorca. The cost of a flight from the Russian capital to Palma de Mallorca will be approximately 400 US dollars. From the airport, the hotel can be reached by bus or taxi. Doing it on the bus will be cheaper, but less comfortable, especially if you decide to bring a large number of suitcases with things. Taxi will be a more expensive treat. Given that the hotel is only 7 km from the airport, the bus will take you 15 minutes, and the taxi driver will take you 10. The bus stop is located just 100 meters from the entrance to Ipanema Beach Park, so before you choose between convenience and cheapness, you need to weigh everything carefully and think it over.



Hotel Services

Surprisingly, the staff who will meet and serve you on site can speak Russian. This will facilitate the resolution of your questions regarding the hotel. Ipanema Park Beach (Mallorca) offers its guests a shuttle service from Palma de Mallorca airport directly to the door of the hotel. Its cost is insignificant, but you should book a transfer before departure, because otherwise you will have to get to the hotel yourself either by bus or taxi. On site there is a possibility of currency exchange. This type of service is very convenient for many tourists. In order to keep your documents and valuables intact, you can rent a safe. Among other types of rental there is also the opportunity to rent a car. True, there is little sense in this, since the hotel is located only half a kilometer from the center of the town of Arenal, and the beach is within easy reach.



Hotel rooms

In total, the hotel has 96 rooms, which are located in two identical buildings. The hotel management offers its guests only one option of apartments - standard. At the same time, in the room you can find one large double bed, a chest of drawers with a mirror, air conditioning, a TV, as well as a bathroom, which is combined with a toilet. Inside the room you can use wireless internet absolutely free. Also, if you wish, you can fill in the minibar, which is available in the room, but it will only cost extra money. In the bathroom you can find a clean bath towel, as well as a hairdryer to dry your hair after bathing. Going out onto the balcony, you can admire the Mediterranean Sea, as well as the nature, which has remained almost untouched by man. On the bedside table near the bed you can find a phone that is designed to communicate with the reception.

Room rate

The amount for which you will be accommodated at Ipanema Park Beach Hotel depends on the time of booking. The sooner you book a room, the cheaper it can cost you. If you do this one month prior to arrival, then the cost of one night in a three-star hotel in Mallorca will cost settling at 92 US dollars. The price also includes a complimentary breakfast.



Food & Beverage

As mentioned above, breakfast at the hotel is absolutely free. It is served in the form of a buffet, where everyone can choose for themselves the dish that he likes. In general, the hotel is a building of two buildings. So, he has two restaurants and bars. Restaurants Ipanema Park Beach 3 * offer a large assortment of dishes for their guests, but, according to many tourists, forks and spoons are not always enough for everyone, and people have to wait in line. Therefore, it is reasonable to assume that it is better to have dinner outside such a restaurant, somewhere near the hotel. In many institutions of Mallorca and the Arenal in particular, they prepare very tasty dishes, which you then remember for a very long time. In order to taste alcoholic beverages, you must go to the bar of the Ipanema Park Beach Arenal. It offers various types of alcohol, such as wine, whiskey, cognac, beer and even vodka.

Sports and Entertainment

For lovers of active pastime, the hotel has two outdoor pools, the water in which changes daily. If you don't like them, you can always walk to the sandy beach, which is located just a few hundred meters from Ipanema Beach Park. In addition to swimming pools for adults, the hotel also has a children's pool, the water level in which does not exceed half a meter. You can also play billiards and sunbathe in the solarium on site. For fans of computer games there is a slot machine room. True, the entrance to this room is paid. On the beach you can not just sunbathe or swim in the sea, but also enjoy extreme sports. For example, go water skiing or go diving.



Ipanema Park Beach reviews

Vacationers who were able to find the time and go to the stunning beauty of the island with the name Mallorca and settled in this hotel speak about it differently. Some of them consider this hotel to be a very good place to stay, assuring that the number of stars should be at least 4. Other tourists complained that the restaurant did not always have good breakfasts, and the staff did not even try to correct this situation . But all the people who leave their comments about the Ipanema Beach Park hotel agreed that this hotel has a very good location in relation to everything that may be interesting to a vacationer.

In the end, it should be concluded that the hotel has both pluses and minuses. A definite plus is that it is located in close proximity to the airport, which allows you to check into it almost immediately upon arrival. It should also be noted the location of the hotel relative to the center of Arenal and Mallorca beach. If you want to spend most of your time outside the hotel, then as an overnight stay Ipanema Beach Park can be a very decent option.
