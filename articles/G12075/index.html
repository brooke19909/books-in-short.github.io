Digital power meter: overview, specifications, types and reviews

A digital wattmeter is an advanced modification of its analog predecessor. It serves to measure the power in the network, which should not go beyond the limits. Otherwise, a fire and equipment breakdown may occur. At constant current, the power indicator is calculated by multiplying the voltage by the current using an ammeter and a voltmeter. The AC circuit requires special measuring instruments, which include the wattmeter.





Destination

Mostly digital power meters are used in the fields of the electric power industry, mechanical engineering, and in the repair of electrical devices. In everyday life, these devices are used by specialists in electrical engineering, computer equipment, and radio enthusiasts.

Wattmeter features:

  • Testing electrical circuits or their sections.
  • Determining the power of devices.
  • Testing electrical installations by indicator type.
  • Monitoring the operation of electrical equipment.
  • Accounting for the use of electrical energy.

Kinds

Power is measured based on voltage and current data. By the method of measuring and issuing the final information, the devices under consideration are divided into analog and digital wattmeters.

The analog type variant has a block of recording and showing elements. They reveal the active power of a certain section of the circuit. The screen of such a device has a graduated scale and arrow. The divisions of the dial are divided by the power indicator in watts.

Digital modifications measure active and reactive power. The display shows information about voltage, amperage, electricity use per unit of time. The measurement results are displayed on a computer device.





How does it work?

The basic principle of operation of a digital wattmeter, the circuit of which is given above, is a preliminary measurement of voltage and current strength. For this, a current sensor is connected in series to the consuming device, and a voltage indicator is connected in a parallel circuit. These elements are made of thermistors or their analogues (measuring transformers, thermocouples).

The measured parameters are instantly transmitted via an inverter to the internal microprocessor. The power is measured and the results are displayed on the screen and transmitted to external devices.

It is worth noting that electrodynamic devices have a wide range of actions; they work with both direct and alternating current. Inductive devices are used only for AC circuits.

Testing

Consider the operation of a digital power meter by the example of a household modification. After plugging into the outlet, the display shows the time required to measure the power of the consumer. Let's take an LED lamp as it. On the screen when the lamp is off, the indicator 0.4 W (power of the disconnected consumer) is displayed. When the lamp is turned on, the readings change by 10.3 watts. In the column price are zeros, if not specified.

The LED can change the power of the glow. With increasing brightness, the power parameters also increase. When the second mode is activated, two fields (time and kW / hours) are also displayed in the upper part. Since the device worked for less than an hour, zeros are indicated in the time field. In the lower part there is a display of information about how many days a specific consumer was measured.

Next mode: the second field displays the mains voltage and current frequency. The upper part of all modes shows the measurement time. The transition to the next mode is accompanied by current indications in the central part of the screen.

Mode No. 5 displays the minimum power, and on the sixth - its maximum indicator. The cost of electricity is set manually using the buttons. After setting all the parameters, you can measure and calculate the consumption of any home appliance.

Reviews

User feedback on the digital AC wattmeter, the circuit of which is given below, indicates that multifunctional models are preferable, as they have the ability to work with direct and alternating current. However, consumers who need to measure the power of the refrigerator, lamps, other household appliances or meter readings are quite happy with the household modification. Its advantages include compactness and ease of use. Workshops or enterprises recommend the use of more precise variations.





The owners also note that the accuracy of the readings largely depends on the correct temperature regime. They advise choosing a device in comparison with its future destination, price and quality characteristics.
