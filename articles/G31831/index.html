Green Peace Hotel 3 * (Alanya): description of rooms, service, reviews

Our compatriots are very well known for the resort town of Alanya. A huge number of Russians come here, because in this place you can pick up a hotel of any price category. In addition, even ordinary three-star hotel complexes will be able to please tourists with a high level of service and a wide range of entertainment. If you are interested in an inexpensive hotel where you can comfortably relax with the whole family, then we would advise you to consider Green Peace 3 * in Alanya. In the current holiday season, he has received quite a lot of travelers from Russia. Therefore, we decided in today's article to talk in detail about Green Peace Hotel Alanya 3 *. Perhaps this information will help you to get an idea about the hotel complex and make your choice in its favor.





Hotel Description

Green Peace Hotel Alanya 3 * refers to small hotel complexes, it contains only eighty-five rooms. Despite the fact that the hotel has three stars, it is highly appreciated by our compatriots. In addition, they consider it the most suitable in terms of price and quality, which is very important when planning a budget trip.

Built Green Peace 3 * (Turkey) was in the early nineties of the last century and worked for a very long time without renovation. However, over time, the buildings dilapidated, and the rooms began to need updating. Therefore, a year ago, the hotel complex carried out repair work, which had a positive effect on its rating among Mahmutlar hotels.

Where is the hotel situated?

The location of the hotel complex is always important for vacationers. The hotel is approximately five hundred meters from the small resort village of Mahmutlar. Alanya (Turkey) is located nine kilometers from the hotel. This distance cannot be an obstacle for those who want to see the sights of the city or go on a sea excursion. They often depart precisely from the marina of Alanya.

However, keep in mind that most Mahmutlar hotels are very far from the international airport. Three Star Hotel Infrastructure

Going to the Green Peace Hotel Alanya 3 *, be prepared for the fact that its infrastructure is slightly different from what tourists are used to seeing in a five-star hotel. However, vacationers will not experience inconvenience in anything.

On the territory of the hotel complex is a large swimming pool with a section for children. Around the bowl is equipped a relaxation area with a huge number of sun loungers and umbrellas. There is enough space for all hotel guests.

It features a restaurant and two bars. In their reviews, guests often pay attention to describing the variety of dishes and the quality of food. We will also tell the reader about this in the next section of the article.

At the reception, guests can rent a car, and you can leave it in a spacious parking lot. Place on it is provided free of charge and without prior appointment. Therefore, it is often used by vacationers who like to move around the resort in comfort.

In Green Peace 3 * (Turkey), there is a currency exchange office. However, guests come here quite rarely. Indeed, in Turkey you can freely pay with American dollars or pay by credit card.





Beach area

Green Peace Hotel Alanya 3 * has its own beach, consisting of sand and pebbles. It is equipped with sun loungers and umbrellas, but beach towels are not provided at the hotel. The sea in the coastal zone is very clean and transparent. However, the entrance to the water is very inconvenient, on the way of tourists there are large slippery slabs. In windy weather, bathing becomes extremely dangerous and can cause injuries to tourists.

Many write in their reviews that it is worth buying special shoes for the beach. Without it, you risk injuring your legs and ruining your vacation.

Green Peace 3 *: service

For three stars, the service in the hotel complex is provided at a high level. Guests enjoy free Wi-Fi. It works throughout the hotel complex, however, Green Peace 3 * guests (Turkey / Mahmutlar) often write that not all rooms have a stable signal. In some apartments, it barely allows you to access the Internet, so guests have to go down to the restaurant or sit out in the lobby at the reception.

For an additional fee, the hotel will clean up dirty things. They will be given to the laundry or dry cleaning, and then carefully hung in the closet.

If you need the help of a doctor, then know that he is always in place. A professional health professional will quickly examine you and prescribe the necessary medicines that can be purchased at a pharmacy or on site.

What to do on vacation with children?

Most tourists prefer to relax in Turkey with children. Therefore, if you also plan a vacation with a child, then do not forget that in a three-star hotel he may be bored.

The hotel has a playground with a small number of designs. Kids can also spend time in a separate section of the large pool. Here, children do not interfere with adults, but are under their vigilant supervision.

If necessary, the restaurant will bring guests high chairs, but a children's menu is not provided. Therefore, be prepared to feed your child dishes in a shared kitchen.





Rules for staying with children

The hotel can be accommodated with a child of any age. Children under the age of six, sharing the same bed with parents, can stay in the hotel complex absolutely free. The same rule applies to babies up to two years of age who are provided with an extra bed. There is no need to pay extra for their placement.

If your child is from seven to twelve years old, then for him you will need to pay half of the cost of living an adult. Older children staying in an extra bed will cost about seventy percent of the cost of a room for one adult tourist.

If necessary, one extra bed or two childrens beds can be installed in one room. This service is not included in the total cost of living, therefore it is paid at check-out by a separate bill.

Some nuances of checking into a hotel

Please note that pets are not allowed in the hotel complex. They are best left with relatives or friends.

Check-in takes place at two in the afternoon, if you have free apartments you can be settled a little earlier. However, as tourists say, this is extremely rare. There are cases when our compatriots got into their room after three in the afternoon. Check out from the apartment until noon.

If you have booked a hotel online, in some cases it will be impossible to cancel your reservation. Therefore, be careful when planning an independent trip.

Green Peace 3 *: room description

We already mentioned at the beginning of the article that almost all rooms in the hotel complex are standard. Their area does not exceed twenty squares, and three adults can accommodate here.

All rooms are equipped identically. They are equipped with a safe, individual air conditioning, telephone and satellite TV. The floors in the apartments are covered with ceramic tiles, the same coverage is on the balcony.

Bathrooms include a shower and hairdryer. Rooms are cleaned daily; towels are changed as often. However, not all room reviews are positive. Some guests complain of poor sound insulation, dirt and not entirely new bedding. It happens that tourists were accommodated in rooms with an unpleasant smell, which could not be eliminated even after a long airing. Many rooms have views of the sea and green areas. Hotel guests are advised to choose those apartments that overlook the courtyard. They are the most quiet and calm.

Summing up, we can say that the hotel is quite good for budget travelers who are economical about money. For a small amount they get a nice room, good service and the opportunity to swim in the gentle waters of the Mediterranean Sea. If this is your main goal, then feel free to choose this hotel for a relaxing holiday with the whole family.
