Ay Napa (Cyprus) - the best vacation on the island

Over the years, our compatriots, as well as tourists from all over the world, have been happy to relax in the resorts of the hospitable island of Cyprus. They are attracted here by a mild climate, excellent well-groomed beaches, excellent service, and the goodwill of the local population.



In the east of the island is a small but already very popular town - Ai-Napa. Cyprus has many famous and popular resorts, and this city is one of them. As an ancient legend says, once in these places impassable forests grew, in which the local population successfully hunted wild animals. Once a hunter discovered an unfamiliar cave, and in it an ancient icon of the Virgin Mary. People considered the find a good sign, and began to build houses here. Over time, a small fishing village grew up, which they called Ai-Napa, which means "sacred forests."

More recently, only in the 70s of the last century, the tourist center of Ai-Napa received its recognition. Cyprus can be proud of its small but very popular resort. Thousands of tourists from all over the world have a rest here every year. Today it is known as a youth tourist center - noisy, cheerful, not falling asleep for a minute. There are concentrated entertainment venues for every taste - famous taverns, restaurants, bars, nightclubs and discos.



Gorgeous, well-groomed beaches with a convenient gentle beach, a stunning water park with attractions for vacationers of all ages, an amusement park, countless holidays and festivals - all this is Ay-Napa. Cyprus loves its guests and this resort is no exception.

The beach season here begins in April and lasts until October. At this time, the resort is gorgeous, very comfortable weather. Cyprus, Ay-Napa in particular, has a mild Mediterranean climate. The hottest time is from June to September, when the air temperature reaches 32 ¬∞ C during the day and 23 ¬∞ C at night. Water in the sea at this time warms up to 27 degrees.

Tourists always respond with special warmth to the service at the resorts of the island of Cyprus. Ay-Napa, whose hotels are located even on the beaches, fully meet all the requirements of guests. There are many hotels in this small town, and all of them are distinguished by excellent service. For example, the extremely popular hotel, which is located on the most famous beach of Nissi Beach, is Adams Beach Hotel. It has 385 magnificent rooms. Tourists are offered non-smoking rooms, for people with disabilities, luxury suites. The hotel area is well equipped, the quality of services is suitable for families.



However, vacationers are attracted to these places not only by an excellent beach vacation. The pride of the resort is the historical sights of Ay-Napa. Cyprus has many historical, cultural and architectural monuments. Residents of Ay-Napa with awe relate to historical relics located in the city. This is a magnificent sixteenth-century monastery with a marble fountain and Ayas Napas Chapel. Tourists are very attracted by the church of the monastery, which contains the veil of St. Veronica, stretched by her to Christ who was rising to Calvary. Locals recommend visiting the folk museum with a unique collection of tools from the Bronze and Stone Ages, the Church of the Prophet Elijah.
