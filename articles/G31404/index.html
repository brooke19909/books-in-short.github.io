Official employment

The company under study actually offers formal employment. This fact is emphasized by some reviews of the organization as an employer. Accordingly, to get the first work experience in the insurance company "Antal-Insurance" is suitable.



True, you will first have to undergo training. It lasts about a week. Many emphasize that this period of time is not paid.
