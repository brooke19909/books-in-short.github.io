Hotel Cactus Charme Hotel Aquapark 4 * Turkey, Bodrum: photos and reviews of tourists from Russia

The Anatolian coast is already fully developed by Russian tourists. But the resorts of the Aegean, with the exception of large cities, remain a kind of terra incognita for travelers from the Russian Federation. In this article we will consider the hotel Cactus Charme Hotel Aquapark 4 *. It is located in the resort village of Yalikavak. The name is unfamiliar, right? In this article, we will also pay attention to all the tourist "chips" of Yalikavak. The Cactus Hotels & Resorts chain of hotels is very popular. The rating of their representative offices is always at the level of 4.5 points out of five possible. And what will Russian tourists say about the Cactus Sharm Hotel Waterpark Hotel in Yalikavak? Now we find out about it.



Where is the hotel and how to get to it

The Cactus Charme Hotel Aquapark is located just eighteen kilometers from the major city of Bodrum. The nearest international airport (Milas) is located sixty kilometers from Yalikavak. Some tourists also land in Dalaman's air harbor. It is located 180 kilometers from the coast of the Aegean Sea, where Yalikavak is located. Packaged tourists, for whom the transfer price is included in the ticket price, can not puzzle how to get to the resort and the hotel. But for others it will not be a big problem. The main thing is to get to Bodrum. From Dalaman there are intercity buses. From Bodrum to Yalikavak, you can take a taxi or get on "dolmush". The journey takes twenty minutes, and the journey in a minibus costs five lire. In Yalikavak, you should transfer to another dolmush. After all, Cactus Sharm Hotel Waterpark is a country hotel. The bus stop is located right next to the hotel gate, which is very convenient.



Yalikavak: what not to miss on vacation

Despite the fact that Cactus Charme Hotel Aquapark is a resort hotel that has everything you want, tourists strongly recommend that you often get out to the center of the village. And not only because it is full of shops, cafes and restaurants. This old town conquers narrow streets, a beautiful promenade. The Turks themselves consider the beach of Yalikavak the best on the entire Aegean coast. Tangerine gardens and olive groves densely approach the city. There is no special fun at this resort - for discos and other ‚Äúparties‚Äù you need to go to Bodrum. But Yalikavak is able to surprise even sophisticated tourists. Indeed, in his bay sea lions live! Due to the special wind rose, this resort is popular among sailors and lovers of gliding along the crest of waves on the board. The coast due to the gentleness is also ideal for bathing babies. The beaches here are sandy, only occasionally small pebbles can be found when entering the sea. The hallmark of Yalikavak are windmills, visible on the tops of the surrounding hills.

Hotel Territory

Hotel Cactus Charme Hotel Aquapark (Bodrum) was built in 1998. Tourists from Russia report that her territory is quite vast. And most of it is occupied by a spacious lagoon-shaped swimming pool. The hotel itself consists of one building. Due to the peculiarities of the architectural structure, tourists find it difficult to answer how many floors are in it: in places four, and sometimes one. The hull looks like a cluster of white, with blue roofs, houses on a hill. Reviews also mention the flat terrain of the hotel. There are no steep climbs and long stairs in it. But there are no elevators in the building, so people with sore feet should order rooms on the first level. The hotel also has a tennis court, mini-golf course and water park. But the hotel‚Äôs own beach is across the road. To get to it, you need to cross the territory of the adjacent hotel "Cactus Beach."



Number of rooms

Cactus Charme Hotel Aquapark tourists call a small family hotel. It has a total of 54 rooms. They are divided into two categories - standard and family suites. Ordinary rooms can be of different capacities - for one, two or even three people. Accordingly, their areas are different. In general, due to the architectural features of the building, the hotel rooms on the ground floor are larger than on the upper levels. But what pleased the guests was that all the rooms have a direct or side view of the sea. The standards have everything you need for a quality stay: a TV with cable channels, a minibar, a telephone, and a split system. The rooms on the ground floor are adjoined by small terraces, on the upper levels are balconies. The combined bathrooms have both a bathtub and a shower. Standard amenities include a hairdryer. Suites are larger rooms. They are designed to accommodate four people.
