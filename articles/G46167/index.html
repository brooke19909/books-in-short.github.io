DIY tie making - original ideas

A man‚Äôs tie is a thin strip of fabric sewn almost always the same way. The difference is in the colors and width of the product. Now men rarely wear a neck accessory, only for special occasions or for work in offices or institutions with a mandatory dress code.

Therefore, entrepreneurial female representatives drew attention to the ties that were lying in the cabinets. There was an application for such products everywhere. These are elegant dress belts and elaborate collars for blouses, purses and covers for mobile phones, brooches for clothes or coats, bracelets and necklaces.

Jewelry from ties with your own hands can be made very different, you can fantasize, depending on the color and quality of the fabric. It is not necessary to have special skills in order to carve out a decorative element from a thin, beautifully crafted strip of fabric. It is enough to have basic sewing skills and the desire to create an original thing on your own.

In the article, we will consider some interesting options for making jewelry from ties with our own hands with photos. A detailed description of the work on the craft will help to quickly understand how to act, what part of the tie to use, than to fix the finished product on clothes.

Bracelet on the arm

Doing the first option of decorating with ties with your own hands is as easy as shelling pear. This is a bracelet on the hand, to create which it is enough to measure the volume of the brush, cut off an extra piece of fabric from a tie, sew on a button and cut a hole corresponding to its size.

To prevent the threads from falling off at the cutting site, sew a seam over the edge with neat stitches. In the photo above, all stages of the work are clearly visible. Put on the bracelet with a triangular neckline up.

If you have an old metal bent bracelet, then you can update it by making a beautiful jewelry from a tie with your own hands, as in the photo below. If the product is wide, then try to pull the fabric on the base, if it doesn‚Äôt work, then you will have to glue it with a glue gun or transparent moment glue.

Choose a product with an original ornament, then it can be decorated with rhinestones or half beads.

Belt of several ties

To create the next jewelry from men's ties with your own hands, sew the edges of the three products with the inner seam. Triangles at the ends can be left, but it is better to cut and hide the edges by folding the fabric inward.

Wrap the button with the rest of the fabric and decorate it with petals using the kanzashi technique. For each element, prepare a circle of matter, fold it in half twice and pick up the bottom edge of the workpiece with stitches and tighten the thread. Get a petal. Repeat the procedure 4 more times and you can make a flower.

Necklace

If you fold the tie and fold it in an arc, stitching the edges of the fabric, you get an original jewelry in the form of a necklace on the neck. Additionally, decorate the craft with large pearls sewn in a straight line from one side, and you can go surprise your friends.
