Hotel "Amara Dolce Vita" (Turkey): a chic vacation for demanding customers

Spend your holidays in a picturesque corner of sunny Turkey, on the Mediterranean coast in the 5-star Amara Dolce Vita. Here you will be offered a variety of exciting entertainment, from which pleasant memories will remain. Hotel "Amara Dolce Vita" (Turkey) is one of the hundred most prestigious and popular tourist centers in the world. The service meets international quality standards, which is why he was so fond of Europeans.





The guesthouse is located in a beautiful bay in the resort village of Tekirova and is surrounded on all sides by pine forests. The distance from the airport is 75 km. The Amara Dolce Vita Hotel (Turkey) is proud of its clean and wide beach, comfortable rooms and a high level of service. The most demanding and demanding customers are offered accommodation in fully equipped villas with a private pool.

Cosmetic restoration is held here annually. On a huge landscaped area, one 6-storey building (Deluxe Venezia), two 3-storey buildings (Deluxe Capri and Deluxe Portofino), as well as 3- and 4-storey club cottages and as many as eight 2-storey houses (villas) were built. The hotel can be recommended for people who know a lot about quality holidays.





Number of rooms

For guests staying in club villas, a complimentary airport pick-up service includes champagne, flowers and a fruit basket. Customers can use room service. Every day, maids deliver fresh newspapers directly to the door of the villa.

Standard services include: telephone, individual air conditioning, free bar with elite drinks. In addition, there is a TV, a free safe.

Nutrition

Hotel "Amara Dolce Vita" operates on the UAI system (round-the-clock food). The menu is rich in fresh fruits, vegetables, meat dishes and herbs. You can order diet meals. There are 9 restaurants with varied cuisine.





Beach

The highlight of the complex is the sand and pebble beach owned by the hotel. There is a line 600 meters from the building. On the shore there are all the necessary conditions for a favorable and relaxing holiday (mattresses, sunbeds, awnings, showers and changing rooms). A bar with refreshing drinks is open. For vacationers who prefer active leisure, the hotel "Amara Dolce Vita" (Turkey) offers a water entertainment center.

Additional Information

Diverse and multilevel water slides, equipped in the outdoor pool, will cheer up both small and adult. A whole water park is built on the territory of the guesthouse. For sports there is a gym. For sports entertainment there are tennis courts, futsal, a basketball court, a gym.





For a fee you can visit the massage room, wellness center, play billiards and bowling. You will learn to ride horses and shoot from a bow. Hotel "Amara Dolce Vita" (Turkey) invites you to take a steam bath in the Turkish bath and swim in the jacuzzi. In the main building you will find an indoor heated pool filled with sea water. The total number of swimming pools in the hotel is five.

We did not forget about business travelers: for negotiations and meetings - three conference rooms (for 2000 seats). Wi-Fi is available in all public areas. On certain days, concert programs and a disco are organized.

For your children a separate pool with two slides is allocated, a small zoo, a club and a playground are open.

Digest

One of the most popular and tourist developed countries is Turkey. Hotel "Amara Dolce Vita" is a huge world of entertainment, a lot of positive emotions and impressions. In this chic place in every sense, you can relax all year round.

The atmosphere of celebration and comfort reigns in the hotel. There is no time to get bored and think about everyday problems. Fans of a relaxing pastime can splash around on the beach and in the pool, walk along the shady paths of the forest thicket. Active tourists are waiting for unforgettable entertainment for every taste.
