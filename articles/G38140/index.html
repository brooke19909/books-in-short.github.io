How much to fly from Moscow direct flight

A small state lying on the archipelago has about a hundred airports. Practically every more or less inhabited island has its own take-off and landing field. Some of the airports have international status. If we talk about a direct regular flight from Moscow to Manila, then there is simply no such thing. On this route you can fly with transfers - one or even two. But we will talk about connecting and combined flights later. Now we are talking about direct flights. Unfortunately, this is only possible on charter flights. Thus, Russians get to the Philippines only during the tourist season, when travel agencies send chartered planes there. And as a rule, such airliners land by no means in Manila. Traditionally resort islands, such as Mactan Cebu International Airport, become the point of landing. It will be eleven and a half hours to fly such a flight. The liner overcomes a distance of almost eight and a half thousand kilometers.



Cons of charters

So, we found out how much to fly to the Philippines from Moscow by direct irregular flight. But this information is rather theoretical. In practice, charter flights are often delayed. And sometimes for the whole day. Yes, and such air tickets are not cheap. The main problem of charters is that it is quite difficult to get a place on them. After all, the tour operators who chartered the plane, the first thing they want to fill the cabin with "package" passengers, that is, those who bought a ticket from them. Tickets for the charter are thrown into free sale only when it becomes clear: the liner is not fully filled, that is, a few days before departure. Thus, planning your trip with a charter in advance is unlikely to succeed. Another disadvantage is the irregularity of flights. In the summer, during the rainy season, charters do not fly to the Philippines. And finally, it should be noted that such flights bypass the capital of the state, the city of Manila. Since each island of the country has its own airport, charters prefer to land in Bacalod, Angeles City, Basco, Tacloban and other resorts.
