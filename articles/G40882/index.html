Vietnam in February. Weather, vacationers reviews

Vietnam is a tropical country with a pronounced division into seasons. Due to heavy and heavy rains it is impossible to visit throughout the year. The most favorable time for a trip is the dry season. For this reason, knowledgeable tourists choose a trip to Vietnam in February. This month is also famous for the celebration of the Eastern New Year. In the northern part of Vietnam, the weather in February will still be quite cool. Daytime temperatures do not exceed 21 degrees Celsius, and nighttime temperatures hardly reach 12 degrees with a plus sign. But in the southern part, the air temperature this month in some areas warms up to 32 degrees Celsius. There is practically no rain, and if they are, it is rarely and are short-lived.



Vietnam: February trip

The weather in Vietnam in February is not significantly different from the January weather. Already this month, the water temperature in the southern regions warms up to 28 degrees. The warmest city is considered to be Ho Chi Minh City. It is recommended to go here for those who prefer the hot sun and beach holidays. A couple of degrees lower in Nha Trang and Mui Ne. Next is the island of Phu Quoc with its unique nature and unique landscapes. There are practically no winds this month. Among the resorts in the central part of Vietnam, the most attractive for tourists in February are Hue, Da Nang and Hoi An. Here, the water warms up to just 24 degrees. In the northern part it is still cool for a beach holiday, but this does not prevent tourists from getting acquainted with the history and sights of the country.



Holidays and festivals

What else will Vietnam please this month with its guests? Holidays in February, reviews confirm this, allows you to see various national holidays and carnivals, moreover, to become their participant. So, if you decide to go at the beginning of the month, you can still see the carnival procession in honor of the Eastern New Year. Its celebration begins in late January and ends in early February. Those who choose the middle of the month to relax can visit the festival of marble mountains, which is called Kwan in Vietnam. On this day, folk songs are everywhere, exciting performances of the folk opera are held.

What to see in Vietnam in February

Regardless of which part of Vietnam you choose to relax in February, there are many options for an interesting pastime for tourists. For exotic lovers, a visit to the jungle in the central part of the country will be interesting. And not far from the city of Da Nang, there is the Son-Tra Peninsula, which opens up landscapes for guests of amazing beauty. No less attractive is the island of Katba, in the territory of which there is a national park. In addition, it is the largest island in the country. During a trip to Vietnam in February, beautiful architectural structures are especially popular, including ancient pagodas, temple complexes and ancient palaces. Particular attention is recommended to pay to the historic city of Hoi An, located in the province of Quangnam. The sanctuary of Michonne is also located here. The most famous was the Kuti tunnel complex, located 70 kilometers northwest of Ho Chi Minh City. Tourists, in addition, have a great opportunity to see the structure of partisan underground fortifications, to evaluate the perfection of their disguise.



Interesting sights of the country

The weather in Vietnam in February allows you to see all the attractions, which in the country there are quite a lot. The most striking are the tombs of the Nguyen dynasty, located along the river of Spirits. Each of them deserves attention, as it is beautiful in its own way. Phong Nha-Ke Bang Nature Reserve, where the largest cave in the world is located, makes an equally impressive impression on tourists. In the reserve you can see unique stalactites, appreciate the beauty of high mountains and appreciate the beauty and uniqueness of old trees. Considering the most interesting places in the country, one cannot help but recall those located in the city of Hanoi. Particularly noteworthy is the Pillar of One Pillar, which is more than a thousand years old. This is a small Buddhist temple located on the water. In shape, it resembles a lotus flower.

Going on a vacation to Vietnam in February, you can fully appreciate the beauty and many other attractions, including the imperial city of Hue, Halong Bay, the Bay of Fallen Dragons, Tram-Ton Pass, located in the mountain town of Sapa.



What an attractive trip to Vietnam in the first spring month

Vietnam is one of those countries that along with pristine nature has managed to maintain its identity. Its civilization is one of the most ancient on the whole planet. The Vietnamese people are very friendly and hardworking. Holidays in Vietnam in March allow tourists to observe the work of local residents in the rice fields. It is the traditions of hospitality, respect for any guest that make a vacation in Vietnam pleasant and unforgettable. In addition, in March, colorful temple festivals are held in honor of the legendary heroes. These are events such as the festival of the temple of Thang Tam in Wang Tau, the festival of Thau Pagoda, during which a colorful performance of puppets is held, the Giong festival. These spectacles delight all tourists.

It should be noted that in March the weather in different parts of the country is not the same. So, in the northern regions, the night temperature does not exceed 18 degrees Celsius, and the daytime temperature is 20-22. As for the southern outskirts, here the air warms up to 32 degrees Celsius. Therefore, before choosing where to relax in Vietnam, you need to decide what you want to get from this trip.
