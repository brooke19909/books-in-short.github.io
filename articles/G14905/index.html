Electronic levels: description, purpose

Electronic levels, or digital, are devices designed to determine the angle of deviation of elements of various structures from a vertical or horizontal position. Today, these devices are the most popular in the field of installation and construction works.



An electronic level sensor allows you to get very accurate readings while measuring vertical and horizontal surface angles. The slope in such devices is indicated on a special digital display scale and is indicated in degrees, feet, inches and percent. Many electronic levels have a built-in acoustic signal, which is triggered by the following device readings: 00, 450 and 900. This is quite convenient if you need to simultaneously track several objects. Professional digital devices are also equipped with a function that allows you to transfer angle indicators from one surface to another.

Laser electronic levels provide a very accurate result, the builder does not have to rely on his eye for determining the position of the air bubble. Such devices are very useful in the construction of inclined surfaces - stairs or decorative elements - thanks to the function of determining the slope and the level of climb / fall. Structurally, electronic components are located in a separate module. It can be used regardless of level as an independent measuring unit, it can also be inserted into frames of any length.



Another important property of digital levels is the ability to use them as levels, which allows you to determine the height of one point on a plane above another.

Laser electronic levels are specially designed for measuring work both indoors and outdoors of structures under construction. A very convenient function of such a device is the ability to form a laser plane visible to the builder, due to the visibility of which any spectrum of work can be performed without being distracted by measurements. The use of laser levels during construction works significantly increases productivity and increases the results of work performed in the field of guaranteed ensuring the accuracy of the data obtained.

Laser equipment of electronic levels is fully automatic, provided with a self-leveling function. Among other things, there is no need to level the device - just fix the level on a tripod, and he is ready to perform the required operations.



Electronic levels are provided with a protective function from incorrect construction of given planes during an accidental shift of the device or a change in the fixed position. Digital levels are designed to operate in extreme conditions, have protection against moisture and dust.

The electronic level, the price of which, depending on the type of device, ranges from 3,000 to 40,000 rubles, can be equipped with a remote control and a wide variety of auxiliary equipment.
