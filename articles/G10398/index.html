Swissotel Hotel, Sochi: tourist reviews and photos

There is no shortage of good hotels in the all-Russian health resort of Sochi. Here everyone will find housing affordable and affordable. There are chic five-star boutique hotels, chain hotels, motels and pensions. Not to mention the gigantic selection of housing offered by the private sector. In this article, we will consider a front-line resort hotel called the Swissotel Sochi Kamelia Resort Hotel. Anyone who has been to an all-Russian resort before can easily recognize the Intourist boarding house known in Soviet times. But it was reconstructed and updated according to the requirements of modern amenities. Now the hotel is an amazing and harmonious mix of old and new. In this article, we analyzed the reviews of tourists vacationing in a hotel. For a concise analysis of conditions and service, read below. The overall rating awarded to tourists by this hotel is 4.33 out of five possible points.



Location

Swissotel Sochi Kamelia Hotel is built on the front page of the sea and has its own beach. This is very important because many hotels in the resort are located in the "stone jungle", and their customers have a lot of time to get to the coast. And there are fewer free beaches in Sochi than we would like. The hotel address is very simple: 89 Kurortny Prospekt. This is the main transport artery of Greater Sochi. The airport can be reached in ten minutes by car. The main building of the Swissotel was built in the thirties of the last century. The cascade and sculptures of the pioneers still adorn a beautiful vast park, in which evergreen trees and a large number of camellias grow. This flower gave the name to a five-star hotel. A pompous grand staircase leads from Kurortny Prospekt to the main building. And the other facade of the building faces the sea.



The territory of the hotel "Swissotel" (Sochi)

Next to the historical building was built another, modern, modern style. The buildings are located so that as many rooms as possible overlook the sea. All apartments have balconies. These latter are so large that some people in their reviews call them terraces. A large park adjoins the hotel buildings. He is very well-groomed, juicy greens and neat paths pleasing to the eye. The cascade of fountains and sculptures testify to the historical past of the hotel. The hotel complex has several pools with areas for sunbathing and relaxation. There are also playgrounds. Adults can also play. For example, tennis. Courts have excellent coverage. It is only five minutes to walk to the private beach of Swissotel Hotel (Sochi) from the buildings. The territory of the complex is flat, which is suitable for elderly tourists or guests with small children.

Sea, beach and pools

Swissotel Kamelia (Sochi) is a front-line hotel. It has its own beach with bulk sand. Hotel photos and reviews indicate that the entire coastal strip is very well-groomed, paths are laid to the water. The beach is full of lots of sunbeds, so there is no need to take a place in the sun. In addition to the usual sun loungers, there are also luxurious tents where you can accommodate the whole family under a shady canopy. The hotel has three pools. Two of them are for children. One is heated. Water in it always has a temperature of twenty-eight degrees. Around the pools there are also sun loungers and parasols. In the evening, the water is beautifully illuminated, which creates a wonderful romantic atmosphere. The pools are in harmony with the cascade and fountain near the main building.



Hotel Facilities

Swissotel Sochi Kamelia 5 * offers its guests high-class service, worthy of the status of a five-star hotel. Secure parking and a children's playground are on site. For lovers of sports and a healthy lifestyle, there are not only tennis courts, but also a modern fitness room with cardio and weight training equipment. The hotel guests are invited to relax and soak up its spa. There is a dry Finnish sauna, as well as a traditional Russian steam bath. In the center there are six closed cozy rooms for spa rituals with a jacuzzi. There is a hotel club and a kids club. A team of professional animators will spend time with your child, taking him to games, competitions and various master classes. The hotel accepts vacationers with animals.
