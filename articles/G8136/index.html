How to unsubscribe from Yandex.Music: practical tips

Some people think about how to unsubscribe from Yandex.Music. This service is interesting for its features. Especially paid. But sometimes it becomes necessary to abandon it. What to do? How to unsubscribe from Yandex.Music in a particular case? Where it leads? Even a novice user can cope with the task.

Free subscription

How to unsubscribe from Yandex.Music? Reflect on the solution of this issue should those who do not want to pay for the services of the service. But this does not mean that they can not be used.



Yandex.Music has a free subscription and a paid one. In the first case, the application works only in Russia, Ukraine, Belarus and Kazakhstan. A person will be able to listen only to the suggested recommendations and selections.

If there is a free subscription, then there is no need to refuse it. In the case of a paid account, you will have to try a little. After all, Yandex.Music automatically deducts funds for its use from mobile devices. Therefore, you really have to unsubscribe. Otherwise, unnecessary expenses cannot be avoided!

Ways to unsubscribe

How to unsubscribe from Yandex.Music? It all depends on what device the person is dealing with.

Today, you can unsubscribe from the service through iOS and Android. Moreover, in the second case, the rejection of application services is considered:

  • through Google Play;
  • using the "My Account".

We will talk about all these possibilities further. In fact, everything is much simpler than users think. The main thing is to have an Internet connection and a little time.



Via iPhone

How to unsubscribe from Yandex.Music on an iPhone? A similar question worries almost all owners of "apple" phones. It should be noted that the user will be able to refuse the service being studied only when he does this no less than 24 hours before renewing the subscription. Otherwise, the service may deduct funds from the device for another period of use.

How to unsubscribe from Yandex.Music? You can act like this:

  1. Turn on iPhone.
  2. Go to "Settings" - iTunes and AppStore.
  3. Click on the user ID.
  4. Select "View AppleID." Log in if necessary.
  5. Open the "Subscriptions" section.
  6. Click on Yandex.Music.
  7. In the settings, select "Unsubscribe."
  8. Confirm the operation.

Such an algorithm of actions does not always work. After all, if a user has subscribed to the service using iTunes, then they will have to refuse it in the same way.

iTunes and Yandex

What will be required to translate ideas into reality? How to unsubscribe from Yandex.Music? The following trick allows you to cancel the service using iTunes. This technique, as you might guess, works only with "apple" devices.



The guidelines for dropping Yandex.Music on iOS are as follows:

  1. Launch IT.
  2. Log in using AppleID.
  3. Open the "Account" - "View" section.
  4. Click on "Login" / "View".
  5. Scroll to the "Settings" block.
  6. Click on "Manage" near the line "Subscriptions".
  7. Find the desired service and click on the inscription "Edit".
  8. Mark the item "Unsubscribe".

Now you can confirm the action and enjoy the result. The user will refuse to use Yandex.Music on iOS. Disconnection will occur at the end of the paid period. Up to this point, you can use all the features of the service.

On Android

And how to unsubscribe from Yandex.Music on Android? The instructions previously offered to attention in this case lose their relevance. After all, they, as already mentioned, work only with Apple products.

The easiest way is to unsubscribe through the "My Account". This decision will help when the payment for the service was done using a bank card.

In this situation, you will need:

  1. Go to the mentioned service.
  2. Open your profile.
  3. Click on "Manage Subscriptions".
  4. Mark the item "Do not renew."

Quick, easy, convenient. Only Android users most often use Google Play to pay for some services. Then you need to act differently.

Google Play and Subscriptions

I wonder how to unsubscribe from the Yandex.Music subscription through Google Play? This will be no harder to do than with iOS.



When working with Google Play you need:

  1. Open Play Market on your mobile device.
  2. Press the button with several horizontal lines.
  3. Select "Account" - "Subscriptions".
  4. Find Yandex.Music and click on the appropriate line.
  5. Click on the "Cancel" button.

It is important to remember that access to subscriptions will be closed after the end of the paid period. If you simply delete the service and not use it, then the money will still be debited from your mobile phone account. This means that sooner or later you will have to unsubscribe from certain offers.
