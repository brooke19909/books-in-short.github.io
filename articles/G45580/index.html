The history of the castle

The history of the appearance of Castle Vajdahunyad is very interesting, and, probably, it is the only castle in the world that grew out of scenery. The main sensation of the exhibition

The famous architect I. Alpar erected Vaidakhunyad castle, because it was he who invented the miniature copy of the unusual ensemble. A talented master prepared in advance for the opening of the exhibition and visited Transylvania several times, sketching interesting structures.

The architect was running out of time, and due to a lack of funds and time, he realized that he would not be able to build a historic castle from brick. In the end, he created a pavilion from improvised materials, which was called the "construction on chicken legs."
