How to make wood weapons with your own hands

Wood is the simplest and most common material. Many interesting things were made of it, including weapons. And if wooden slings and swords are ancient and not particularly dangerous weapons, then bows and crossbows are still serious and bewitching. And so the question of how to make weapons with your own hands usually comes down to them. Let us dwell on the second, namely, a home-made combat crossbow made of wood.



Idea

A crossbow is a device for launching an arrow or bolt along a guide with the help of a bowstring, stretched by an elastic arc. It consists of a housing with a handle on which an arc is fixed. The design also includes a trigger. The main difficulty lies in the large amount of woodwork and the rather complicated design of this mechanism.

We will need the following for work:

  • case (strong piece of wood about a hand long or a little longer, without knots);
  • arc (several glued pieces of plywood or iron);
  • bowstring (kapron or kevlar strong thread of sufficient length);
  • trigger mechanism (duralumin or wooden);
  • tools from a carpentry workshop;
  • glue.


Housing

We start manufacturing with sawing the case. An arrow groove should be sawn in it from above. An arc will be attached at one end, the other will become a hilt. Approximately one third of the length from the end of the handle, a cavity is made in the housing for the trigger mechanism. The rest of the processing depends on the owner‚Äôs desire - the butt, turning and handle are made individually for those who have decided on their own experience to learn how to make weapons from wood.

Arc

If the arc is made of wood, then the most suitable design is several glued sheets of plywood, from which the arc of the desired shape is sawn and bent. This option is simpler and easier, but the arc will give less effort and may crack. If you take a steel arc, then you will need tools and skills for working with metal. The ideal option is spring steel, it will give the greatest acceleration of the boom. From steel it is necessary to cut the arc of the desired shape and make holes for the bowstring at the ends. After manufacturing, the arc is attached to the body.



Bowstring

Before you make a weapon from a tree, you should pull a bowstring. It is attached to the arc so that it passes right above the body without touching it. Its length is adjustable depending on the size of the ‚Äúlaunch pad‚Äù of the boom and the necessary tension. The main thing is that it should evenly and strongly launch the arrow.

Trigger mechanism

His main task is to seize the stretched bowstring and sharply release it. The structure can vary within a very wide framework: from a simple temple to a complex hook system. The quality of work and the convenience of shooting depend on its structure. All its parts must be strong and reliable, because although weapons are made of wood with their own hands, it really shoots.

Security

Do not forget that the crossbow is not a toy. Before you make a weapon from a tree, you need to fully realize the full responsibility for its use. In addition, it would not hurt to obtain permission to store and use such a device.
