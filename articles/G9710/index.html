Historical city of Pavlovsk, Voronezh region: hotels, attractions, road

Pavlovsk is one of the district centers of the Voronezh region. It is almost famous for nothing, except for a comfortable recreational area, a clean river coast and lots of historical sights. The province does not have to be abandoned, and Pavlovsk sets an example for this.

Where is it and how to get there

The city is located on the banks of the Don River in the southern part of the region at a distance of 154 kilometers from Voronezh. You can get there by car. Travel time is about 2.5 hours. You will have to go along the M-4 federal highway, which means that you can accelerate to the limit of 110 km / h and not worry that the trip will end in a lot of fines.

Public transport also runs from Voronezh to Pavlovsk. The central bus station of the city directs regular flights several times a day. The average ticket price is 250 rubles, travel time is about 3 hours. But in order not to bore yourself with a grueling move, it is better to stay at a Pavlovsk hotel in the Voronezh region in order to be able to wake up in this corner of the departed nobility.

You can tell only about a small part of what has been preserved in the city. The building of a salotopenny factory, a real school, a fire station with bowls, a noble assembly and a merchant club are traces of the past that immerse you in the atmosphere of a real aristocratic county where everyone can feel like a hero of some old Russian book.

Hotel "Don"

In the city of Pavlovsk, Voronezh region, there are simply a huge number of hotels. Especially for the district center with a population of 25,000 people.

The average resident of the region, answering the question of where to stay for a random traveler, from Don Pavlovsk hotels in the Voronezh region will remember first. Her address is st. Gottwald, 4.

The fact is that this institution is located in a historical building and has existed since Soviet times. The interior leaves much to be desired, and in each of the 31 rooms the tourist will find a gloomy interior resembling a Soviet boarding house.

The average price per night for two guests is 1100 rubles. This is the cheapest accommodation option.

Hotel with access to the river

The requirement for a front page in front of the beach looks rather strange for the Black Earth region. But among the hotels in Pavlovsk, Voronezh region, there is one.

For lovers of beach holidays there is a "Guest house on May 1". This is not a hotel in the traditional sense, but a kind of tourist hostel with a shared kitchen, a barbecue area and the ability to access the beach.

Free Wi-Fi is available for guests, as well as closed private parking.

The average price per night for two people is 1200 rubles with breakfast included. This is one of the lowest prices among hotels in Pavlovsk and Voronezh region.

The most expensive option

Grand-Hotel Express (6a Severny Microdistrict) is an option with the most comfortable accommodation.

It offers air-conditioned rooms with plasma TVs in rooms and free Wi-Fi. People traveling by car will appreciate the secure parking.

The accommodation option here is the most expensive among Pavlovsk hotels in the Voronezh region. A pair of two people can spend the night in this hotel and pay 2900 rubles for this. Breakfast is not included.
