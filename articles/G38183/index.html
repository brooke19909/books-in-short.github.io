Grozny hotels: photos and reviews of tourists

Grozny is the capital of the republic of Chechnya. The city is located on the banks of the river. Sunji. He was badly damaged during the war and today is rebuilding again. One of its main attractions is the complex, in which the high-rise dominant is a residential 40-storey building.



Grozny hotels are mainly represented by expensive and luxurious five-star complexes. But there are more modest and cheaper options for accommodation. At the same time, the city airport receives flights from Rostov-on-Don, Moscow, St. Petersburg, Surgut and Mineralnye Vody. You can also get here by bus or train.

The most famous sights of Grozny include: the stadium "Akhmat-Arena" and the mosque "Heart of Chechnya." Tourists can also visit the national museum and theater - State Drama. Nuradilova or Russian Drama. Lermontov.

Hotel "Grozny City"

Grozny City Hotel is located in the central part of Grozny, on the banks of the river. Sunzha. This is the largest hotel complex in the entire North Caucasus. At the same time, the airport is only 10 minutes from here. For business meetings and celebrations, conference and banquet rooms are equipped here. By reading reviews about the institution, you can find out that free Internet is available throughout its territory. For accommodation there is an opportunity to book rooms for every taste. All rooms are very modern and stylishly decorated. The relaxing atmosphere makes you feel as comfortable as at home.



Connoisseurs of hot drinks at the Grozny City Hotel will find the necessary accessories for their independent preparation. Guests will be entertained by satellite TV at any time of the day. Laptops and other valuables can be stored in the safe. There is a large TV and a minibar. The apartments are serviced around the clock. Customers have the opportunity to visit the fitness center, spa, sauna, as well as swim in the large pool. Incredibly tasty dishes are usually cooked in a restaurant. Dome is a local cafe that offers stunning views of the city. Here you can enjoy incredibly delicious desserts.

Hotel "Caucasus"

Hotel "Caucasus" is also located in Grozny. According to reviews, she offers her guests free internet access.



The rooms include a kettle and a large flat-screen TV. The Kavkaz Hotel provides guests with free toiletries and slippers. The front desk is open around the clock.

Hotel "4 Seasons"

The 4 Seasons Hotel will delight you with its European modern design, which combines comfort and simplicity, elegance and homeliness. It all awaits guests. There is a 24-hour front desk and free parking. In addition, a new building has appeared, which is built to high European standards with the obligatory observance of international standards.



People who liked the 4 Seasons Hotel note in their reviews that the friendly and welcoming staff here respect and are responsible for all the wishes of the guests.

Together with the new building, the room fund consists of 60 apartments. They are equipped with everything that is necessary for a comfortable stay of guests.

Hotel Terek

The new four-story hotel "Terek" got its name in honor of the football team. It is located 3 kilometers from the station, as well as 5 kilometers from the airport.



By reading the reviews about this establishment, you can find out that there are deluxe and junior suites. In total, Terek can accommodate guests in 27 rooms.

Mini-Hotel Sputnik

Sputnik Hotel (Grozny) is located near the main attraction of the city - a mosque called the Heart of Chechnya, in one of the most prestigious areas. It will be an excellent choice for all lovers of affordable prices and home comfort (as evidenced by numerous reviews), which is very important for companies when sending employees, as well as for tourists.



There are 1- and 2-bed rooms, the cost of living per day - from 1500 rubles. It should be noted that on the ground floor there is a grocery store. At the request of the guests, a whole package of documents is provided for reporting to the employer regarding residence here.

Hotel "Zama"

In the center of Grozny is located "Zama" (hotel). Winter with its severe frosts, hot summers, off-season - any season is suitable for a wonderful pastime here. You will enjoy excellent living conditions, hospitable staff and high quality service. Room facilities are divided into categories: junior suite, standard, apartments and suites. In this place everyone will find an ideal option, as evidenced by numerous reviews. All rooms have: telephone, cabinet furniture, bathroom and TV.



From here, you can easily get to the city's attractions, such as the National Museum of the Republic and the Drama Theater. At the hotel you can spend your holidays with the whole family, as well as organize a group trip here.

Hotel Arena City

Considering the hotels of Grozny, it is worth highlighting this new five-star hotel, which is successfully located in a green quiet area, not far from the airport and the city center. The entire infrastructure is within walking distance: shops, public transport stops, business and shopping centers, numerous cafes and restaurants. The hotel has a total of 27 rooms of various categories - from a small, at the same time comfortable economy to a two-room spacious suite.



The apartments, which are decorated in an oriental original style, have everything you need for a complete comfortable stay: comfortable and beautiful furniture, large bathrooms with high-quality sanitary ware, towels, bathrobes and all the necessary cosmetics, a mini fridge and a TV, in addition Internet access and satellite TV.

This hotel of Grozny has its own restaurant of Azerbaijani cuisine, called the "Lights of the East." Breakfast is served here in the morning. In addition, as guests of this institution noted in numerous reviews, they also liked the SPA center with a spacious gym, massage rooms and a sauna, laundry, and a huge parking lot with video surveillance.

Hotel Stolitsa Inn

In Grozny there is a mini-hotel with an indoor pool. It is located at the restaurant "Capital". Guests can use the free internet. All rooms include a fridge and TV. All bathrooms include free bathrobes. In addition, each guest has a fan and bed linen, which guests of the hotel are pleased to note in their reviews.



This inn has a terrace and a shared sauna. In addition, there are rooms designed for business conferences. Free parking is available on site.

Hotel Hollywood

Studying the hotels of Grozny, it is impossible not to mention the hotel "Hollywood". Pets are welcome here. For entertaining guests, a bowling alley is provided, there is a private free parking and a restaurant. Free internet is available in the entire hotel.



All rooms are equipped with a kettle, air conditioning and cable TV with a flat-screen TV. Complimentary toiletries, slippers and bathrobes are featured, as evidenced by numerous reviews. A shuttle service can also be arranged.

Hostel Grozny

Hostel Grozny is located in the city of the same name. A snack bar is open around the clock. Judging by the reviews, guests are provided with free parking, as well as the Internet.

The front desk is open around the clock. The hostel also offers bike hire. It is also worth noting that horse riding is popular here.

Hostel Ankor

Hostel Ankor is located in Grozny. Reading reviews of the establishment, you can find out that it offers private parking and free internet.

It offers rooms with shared or private bathroom facilities. Extras include a TV. Guests have access to a shared kitchen.

Apartment on Dudaeva 10

These apartments with free internet are also located in Grozny. Judging by the reviews, the hotel offers good free private parking, and some rooms have a spa bath.

The accommodation has a satellite flat-screen TV. Some rooms include a seating area and air conditioning. The kitchen is equipped with a microwave, oven, coffee machine, dishwasher and kettle. Other amenities include a private bathroom with a bathtub and disposable slippers. Bed linen is included in the price. A free grocery delivery service is available.
