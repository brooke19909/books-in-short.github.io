How to make a tree machine for your child

The question "How to make a machine of wood?" can occur in 2 cases: you need to make a nice gift to your child or make a special copy of the craft in your own collection (or for sale to a collector, but it does not change the essence). In the first case, there are no special requirements for the product, but in the second situation everything is complicated, and you need to try very hard to make really high-quality weapons. However, regardless of the purpose of the machine, the sequence of actions remains the same.

Before making an automatic machine out of wood, you must carefully draw it. To do this, a quality sketch is made. You can do it yourself (for example, draw, if you have such a talent, or use a PC for these purposes with special software). In the simplest case, a finished sketch can be found in specialized literature or on the Internet. If your sketch, then these workpieces must be worked out and made. In this case, it is necessary to fully develop the design of the machine. Each component is necessarily drawn, then the assembly of weapons is analyzed.



At the next stage of how to make an automatic machine out of wood, you need to decide on a set of tools and a workpiece. For rough finishing you will need:

  1. Ax.
  2. Hammer.
  3. Chisel.
  4. Plane.
  5. Hacksaws (one for wood, the second for metal).
  6. Drill.
  7. Construction level.

These are the main tools for pre-processing the workpiece, in addition to which something else may be needed. For final processing a file, a file and a sandpaper are used.





Next, you need to select a workpiece. It is recommended to choose conifers, it is easier to work with them. Usually they take everything with a 10% margin, but for beginners this number is recommended to be increased. If something goes wrong, wood reserves will allow you to adjust the result of the work.





Then, in the process of how to make an automatic machine out of wood, the patterns are transferred to the material, its rough processing is done. Gradually, each of the elements is cut out or cut down. After completely removing all excess parts of the workpiece, you can proceed to grinding. Files are ideally suited for these purposes. Then you can use files. At the final stage, it is recommended to use an emery skin, gradually moving to a smaller one. At the very end, it is best to use a zero number, which allows you to get the most even surface.

Then everything needs to be formalized properly. The tree must be varnished in two layers. In turn, the place of application of the paint is pre-treated with a primer. After applying the first coat of paint, it is necessary to withstand the time that will allow it to dry, and only then continue to design. After drying, the machine is ready, and it can be used for its intended purpose. The above algorithm allows you to make not only a machine made of wood, but also a gun, bow or crossbow. It is universal and, depending on the flight of fantasy, a lot can be done with it.



Most are now on the path of least resistance. People either buy ready-made toy weapons, or go on an even more non-standard path: they think about how to make an automatic machine from Lego, for example. But all this is not entirely correct. Woodwork is an art that is not given to everyone. Therefore, those who make such crafts improve their skills.
