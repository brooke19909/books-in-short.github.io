How many kilometers are from Kazan to Moscow and how to get along the route?

Both our citizens and foreign tourists travel from the capital of Tatarstan to the capital of Russia. No wonder: the route is interesting, popular, and the trip does not take much time, given how many kilometers from Kazan to Moscow are relatively large Russian distances. Flight flight route

Planes of different companies fly from Kazan to Moscow from 02:45 to 22:15. Thus, it is easy to fly away, the choice of tickets is large, and it takes only 1.5 hours or a little more to get there. There is one airport in Kazan. Airplanes arrive in Moscow in any of the available - Sheremetyevo, Vnukovo and Domodedovo. Ticket price depends on various promotions and airline offers. For example, for flights "Victory" the minimum - from 1,500 rubles.
