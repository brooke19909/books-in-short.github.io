It is clear that during the rest tourists spend only a small part of their time in the room. In principle, it is not intended solely for sleep, relaxation and hygiene. Hotel infrastructure

Vacationers quite often spend evenings on the outdoor terrace. It occupies the fourth floor of the building and attracts with stunning views that open from there. Here you can arrange on a deck chair, wrapped in a blanket, and enjoy a starry sky with a glass of aromatic wine.





The hotel has a swimming pool, sunbeds and umbrellas near it are free for hotel guests. A huge drawback is the lack of an elevator - tourists have to carry their luggage to the second, third or fourth floor on their own, which after a long journey becomes a real challenge. The hotel area is not so large and spacious, but it is considered a city-style hotel, so the absence of a huge green garden, a barbecue area can be forgiven.

Nutrition

All vacationers can count on breakfast in the "Nairi" (hotel, Sochi). Breakfast is organized according to the buffet system, it is difficult to call it diverse, but hardly anyone will remain hungry. Usually for breakfast you can see several types of cereals, cereals, milk, cottage cheese, meat and cheese slices, pancakes, coffee, tea and juices to choose from. You can have lunch and dinner in restaurants or cafes - a great opportunity to get acquainted with local cuisine. The kind staff of the hotel will surely tell you the best places in the city where you can have an inexpensive and very tasty meal.

Service

Description of "Nairi" (3 * hotel, Sochi), reviews about it - all this information is useful for potential tourists, as it allows you to evaluate the quality of leisure that he can get. So, the Nairi hotel can offer not only comfortable rooms, a favorable location, but also impeccable service. First of all, all vacationers can count on round-the-clock service - for all questions of interest, you can contact the reception. The hotel has a guarded parking lot, so tourists who come to Sochi in a personal car do not have to worry about the parking space and the safety of their property. Rooms are cleaned daily, towels are changed every other day, bed linen - every 2-3 days.





For an additional fee, the Nairi Hotel (Sochi) will provide a shuttle service to the airport, help organize an individual excursion or hire a driver with a personal car. The friendly staff will book tickets for the plane or train and deliver them to the tourist room. You can always use the laundry services with which you can tidy your things. It is possible to rent a conference room for seminars, trainings, as well as the necessary office equipment (printer, scanner). The hotel offers all conditions for organizing banquets and celebrations.

Entertainment and sports

Hotel "Nairi" is quite small, but on its territory there is enough entertainment for every taste. All vacationers will be able to sit at the outdoor pool - the water in it is changed daily. Sauna is the pride of the hotel, if you choose the Nairi hotel for your stay, be sure to visit it and also enjoy excellent massage from a real professional. The hotel also offers sports equipment rental. All lovers of spearfishing and fishing will be able to order at the reception a boat or a huge yacht for their sea voyage.

Sea and beach

From the hotel to the beach about 10-15 minutes walk. The beach is municipal, pebble, but equipped with everything necessary for an unforgettable vacation. Families with young children often come to the Nairi Hotel, because the sea is clear and the water is clear. There are locker rooms and showers on the beach, a lot of sports equipment and water activities for every taste. Along the promenade, there are small cafes, tents and kiosks where you can have a bite to eat, buy hot corn, soft drinks or ice cream.





Sochi is a great option for those tourists who want to go jet skiing, go diving - here you can not only rent the necessary gear and equipment, but also take the lessons of real professionals. Do not miss such an advantageous offer.

To summarize

The Nairi Hotel (Sochi) is an excellent option for those tourists who appreciate the peace and quiet of relaxation, but want to have access to the resort's infrastructure. You will find friendly and polite staff, amazing service, comfortable, clean and spacious rooms, as well as plenty of opportunities for organizing your leisure time. If you are planning your vacation in summer, autumn or winter, pay attention to the Nairi Hotel - it will surely meet all your expectations.
