Best Beach Hotel Alanya 3 - Turkey for Budget Holders

Best Beach Hotel Alanya is a three-star hotel located in a small but very popular Turkish town called Alanya. Beautiful nature, wonderful beaches - this is why tourists from nearby countries come here.





Hotel location

One of the most important criteria by which visitors usually choose a hotel is its location. Guests of the country want the hotel to be as close to the city center as possible. After all, everyone knows that it is there that all the entertainment venues are concentrated. Well, Best Beach Hotel Alanya 3 is just such a hotel. It is literally within walking distance of the seafront. By the way, the windows of the rooms offer beautiful views of the nearby surroundings - the sea, mountains, tall green trees.

Room Amenities

But the most important thing is not the location of the hotel. And then, what conditions can he offer his guests. Best Beach Hotel Alanya is a mid-range hotel (3 stars), however it provides decent living conditions. The rooms, decorated in a cozy home style, have everything that a person needs for life - soft comfortable beds, TVs with satellite channels, air conditioning and, of course, their own bathroom with hygiene items and a hairdryer.

The cost of living can not be called low, but also high. Double standard room with breakfast included in the amount of payment will cost 19 thousand rubles. It turns out a little less than 10 thousand per person. This is the cost for 7 days and 6 nights. When booking the same room per person, the cost will be about 15 thousand rubles.

There are also triple rooms. Renting such apartments will cost 25 thousand rubles. Breakfast is also included in the price. If you make a reservation for the option with half board, it will be more expensive by 3 thousand.

Every day the rooms are carefully cleaned. There is no need to worry about the cleanliness and order at the Best Beach Hotel Alanya 3 - here they are closely monitored. By the way, if necessary, there are dry cleaning services, they can also be used.
