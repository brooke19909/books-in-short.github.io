Hotel Armas Beach Hotel 4 * (Kemer, Turkey): Tourist reviews

The Turkish resort town of Kemer is visited annually by thousands of Russian tourists. This place for recreation is really very convenient. After all, the infrastructure here is just superbly developed. Of course, many comfortable and not too hotels were built in this town. What hotel should a Russian vacationer choose for a vacation? In this article, we will understand what the Armas Beach Hotel 4 * complex is, how tourists who have already rented rooms here are talking about it.

Location Features

This hotel is located not directly in the city of Kemer, but 1 km from it, right on the Mediterranean coast. In most cases, guests can reach the beach in just a couple of minutes. The nearest airport to Kemer is 55 km away. So, vacationers who have rented rooms at the Armas Beach Hotel 4 * (Kemer, Turkey) can get on a tour operator‚Äôs bus in about 40 minutes.

According to most tourists, such a trip is usually not too tiring. Buses to their customers agencies working with this hotel, in most cases, provide quite comfortable. At least they have air conditioning. Most often, a transfer is suitable for this hotel after two more others in Kemer.





General description of the complex

Accommodation for tourists in the hotel Armas Beach Hotel 4 * in one main building and several additional. Some complaints from tourists is that the latter are located on the hotel across the road. That is, residents of rooms from additional buildings have to get to the sea longer than from the main one. Moreover, on the sites of many tour operators in the description of the hotel only the first coastline is indicated. This, of course, can be considered some misleading.

Actually, the territory of this hotel itself has earned mostly good reviews from vacationers. The courtyard at the Armas Beach Hotel 4 * is compact and quite comfortable for relaxing. All necessary infrastructure elements are located within walking distance. Tourists do not have to roam the territory in search of a shop, dining room or playground. In particular, the courtyard of the hotel, due to its compactness, has earned good reviews from vacationers with children. Losing a child on the territory of this complex is almost impossible. Parents can monitor their children constantly.

The hotel itself looks quite attractive. In any case, the hotel is not old. The hotel was built in 2006. But in 2014 it was reconstructed. At the same time, not only the facade was repaired, but also the rooms, as well as the hall and corridors.





Suitable hotel Armas Beach Gul Hotel 4 *, according to many tourists, it is for families. Pensioners can spend time here with comfort. There are no noisy entertainment venues under the walls of this hotel. And on the very territory of the hotel, any particularly high-profile parties are usually not held. If there is noise here, it is mainly only during animations or due to games of children of other guests.

People from different countries live in the hotel. There are quite a lot of Russians here. So, domestic tourists who rented rooms here will not feel loneliness. The only thing is that from Thursday to Friday there are a lot of Turks in the hotel, who sometimes behave noisily.

Service Reviews

Of course, this hotel, like any other, has its own certain disadvantages. However, with regard to the service here, most tourists agree that it is, of course, at its best. The staff of the Armas Beach Hotel 4 * even consider the most demanding vacationers to be polite and helpful. The administration responds to requests and complaints of guests almost instantly.

Many hotel staff speak Russian. Therefore, if necessary, our compatriots do not have communication problems. Room if desired, tourists can change as they become available. In the event of a breakdown in the hotel, they try to fix household appliances immediately.

Opinion about renting out at Armas Gul Beach Hotel 4 * (Kemer) for vacationers has developed very good. The rooms in this complex are spacious, bright and clean. Towels and linen in the rooms, judging by the reviews, change often. The maids in the rooms are also cleaned quite carefully. If desired, tourists can use the Wi-Fi rooms for a fee. The views from the hotel rooms, according to many tourists, open up simply magnificent. The complex is located in a really very beautiful place.

Vacationers Opinion About Hotel Beach

Good reviews are also available about the sea near this hotel. The water here is clean, warm and clear. The hotel beach itself is also not dirty. There are no too large boulders here, and the entrance to the sea is quite convenient. If desired, you can walk along the beach in ordinary slates or, if the sand is not too hot, even barefoot. The same goes for entering the sea.

There are a lot of sun beds on the beach. Some tourists even note the fact that it is difficult to walk between them. But in any case, all guests occupy the sun loungers since the evening. Sometimes, however, a beach worker simply throws stones that are pressed "marking the place" towels. And then the last blows away. But this does not happen too often.





A feature of the Armas Beach Hotel 4 * (Kemer, Turkey) is that it is allowed on the beach here even at night. In many other Kemer hotels, sea visits are only possible during the day. Many tourists, of course, attribute this feature to the unconditional advantages of the hotel.

Dining at the hotel: reviews

The dining room of the hotel Armas Beach Hotel 4 * most tourists are also very praised. The food here is delicious, and the staff are attentive to the guests. There is usually not too much meat food from beef or pork in the menu of the dining room of this hotel. But at the same time, tourists every day have the opportunity to take dishes from chicken or turkey. Also in the dining room are served all kinds of fresh and quite tasty salads, fruits, vegetables and, of course, all kinds of drinks.

Actually, the restaurant itself, operating on the territory of the hotel, is not too large. And in order to take places in a room equipped with air conditioning, tourists should come for breakfast or lunch early. The only drawback of the hotel dining room is that it serves very few dishes suitable for feeding a child. All the food in the restaurant of the complex is rather fatty and spicy.

In addition to the dining room, this hotel, of course, has a small bar. It is located in the courtyard next to the pool. Here guests can order all kinds of drinks. Also on the territory of the hotel often fry cakes and make ice cream.
