A proven way to install games on iPhone

The iOS operating system is one of the most popular today. It is installed on all Apple devices, including iPhones of any generation. Especially for users, developers created a huge application store, which was called the App Store. It contains a large collection of various games and programs for the iPhone. For ease of search and quick navigation, they are disaggregated into various categories. All of them are carefully scanned by Apple programmers for viruses and files that could harm your device.



You can download any program you like on your phone in one of two ways. The first method, which tells how to install games on the iPhone, involves selecting and downloading applications to the phone directly from a mobile device. In order not to waste traffic, it is advisable to connect to a wireless Internet network. As a rule, its speed is much higher, and the process of downloading a volume application will be much faster.

You need to find the App Store icon on your smartphone. Initially, it is located on the very first screen. Opening the program, we will see a very large list of applications. Here they are sorted by popularity, that is, by the number of downloads. This feature will tell you how to install games on the iPhone only from trusted manufacturers. Quite convenient is the search for applications by category. The App Store separately houses games, office programs, applications for business, sports, entertainment and much more.
