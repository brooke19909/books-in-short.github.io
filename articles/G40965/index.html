Veliky Ustyug hotels: overview, description, rooms and reviews

Until recently, the Great Ustyug was almost unknown among the Russians. However, everything is changing rapidly. In recent years, the development of the project "Great Ustyug - the homeland of Santa Claus" has allowed everyone to plunge into a winter fairy tale. In our country there are not many places where you can relax with your child, and even in the middle of winter. A visit to the estate of Santa Claus is a great opportunity to spend time with the whole family and have fun from the heart. And you can come here only for one day, which is very important for parents who are always busy.





For a fairy tale in Ustyug

Unlike many other places where you can celebrate the New Year, this cozy city impresses with its relative cheapness and a huge variety of recreation programs. Moreover, there are a great many tours: for young people and parents with children, corporate and summer (yes, you were not mistaken, in the midst of summer life is in full swing here too). It is with great pleasure that foreigners who are interested in Russian nature visit it . Therefore, the hotels of Veliky Ustyug are almost never empty. Today we will talk about which of them are the most suitable in order to spend a great winter vacation.

Hotel "Sukhona"

It is located in the historical part of the city, surrounded by a large number of architectural monuments, not far from the embankment and the city residence of Santa Claus. Like many hotels of Veliky Ustyug, Sukhon is designed for family vacations. It accepts children from a very young age. Address: Red Lane, 12.





In total there are about 60 rooms, which differ in size and convenience. The economy category will cost you 600 rubles per night. The room has a full set of furniture, and a shower and toilet on the floor, for every 18 people. There are rooms with partial amenities, that is, a toilet is available in each room, and a shower on the floor is designed for 10 rooms. Cost - 800 rubles per day. The best option is the standard category, costing 1,000 rubles per day. It has modern furniture and sanitary ware, a TV and a refrigerator.

Judging by the reviews, this is an inexpensive but very comfortable hotel. Prices are very affordable, the location is convenient for tourists, and the staff is friendly and helpful. I would like to recommend visiting here at least once.





Hotel Gleden

Hotels of Veliky Ustyug are ready to receive guests every day, at any time of the year. Of course, it is convenient for a tourist to settle near the most interesting objects and attractions. Hotel Gleden (Veliky Ustyug) is located just 2 km from the city center, at the address: 75 Gledenskaya. This is a new and modern building located in a pine forest, within walking distance of a large entertainment center.



There are 35 rooms with modern furniture, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. A single room can be rented for 1,200 rubles, a double room for 1,900 rubles, and a superior room for 2,500 rubles. Room rates include breakfast, free parking with video surveillance, free internet and room service correspondence. The hotels of Veliky Ustyug are a bit like each other: they are relatively small, comfortable, with friendly staff. It is precisely these qualities that characterize tourists at the Gleden Hotel.

Hotel Dvina

Another hotel in the city center that deserves the closest attention. It is located at: st. Krasnaya, 104. Hotel Dvina (Veliky Ustyug) is a unique visiting card of the city. It is located in its historical part, surrounded by architectural monuments, a chain of shops, restaurants and cafes, as well as next to the beautiful promenade. But that is not all. Just five minutes walk separates the hotel from the residence of Santa Claus.



This is a compact and very comfortable hotel that will become a real home for you and your family. This building of the XIX century, restored, but retaining unique features that sets it apart from all the others. Going inside, guests find themselves in a cozy lounge, where polite administrators will offer to choose one of the rooms.

Each room is unforgettable comfort. Everything has been done here to make guests feel at home. Tourists recall the excellent furniture, not artsy, but conducive to relaxation. The rooms have a bathroom and shower, a convenient wash basin and a TV with several channels. Cleaning is carried out regularly, bed linen and a towel are changed every three days. The cost of accommodation starts from 1,400 per night per room, but the pleasure received here is definitely worth it.





Hotel "Fatherland of Santa Claus"

Well, if you go for winter adventures, then immediately in the New Year's fairy tale. Hotels in Veliky Ustyug for the New Year all, as one, are dressed up like towers from a children's book. But there is one here that is the living embodiment of a dream. This is a fabulous hotel located in an amazing location in a picturesque pine forest. And not far from it is the residence of the main character of the New Year, since you will live on the territory of the patrimony.





It offers a range of comfortable cottages made of natural wood, with cozy rooms and equipped kitchens. Along with accommodation, you will be offered a whole range of services. You can leave your car in a guarded parking lot, it is free, but it is guarded 24 hours a day. There is a sauna and a Russian bath with relaxation rooms. Moreover, each of them is unique and is not limited to just a table and drinks. Here you can play table tennis and chess, play a game of checkers and shine with your talents on the pool table. And on a fine day it is better to get together as a family, visiting the Winter Garden and Santa Claus Glacier.

At the same time, children will definitely not be bored. This hotel in Veliky Ustyug is primarily focused on them, and therefore a variety of attractions were invented on the territory of the patrimony, which usually do not go unnoticed. The cost of living depends on the selected room and ranges from 1,700 to 4,000 rubles per room per day (including breakfast). New Year's Eve costs from 3,500 rubles per person, including an entertainment program. Rent of the whole cottage starts at 13 500, and on New Year's Eve - from 22 700 rubles. As you can see, in comparison with other recreation centers, the hotels of Veliky Ustyug seem very attractive. Prices do not seem to be too high, especially given the variety of services.

Iceberg Hotel





This is another great hotel that is pleased to open its doors to you. The Iceberg Hotel (Veliky Ustyug) is a new hotel, which is located near the city center, the homeland of Russian Santa Claus, at the address: ul. A. Uglovsky, d. 3. It offers a four-story landscaped brick building with shops on the ground floor. For a pleasant pastime there is a cafe with 24 seats, decorated in the style of the old city.

Everything for guests





The number of rooms gives tourists the opportunity to choose the most suitable living conditions. Economy rooms are small in size but very comfortable. Maximum they are designed for two tourists. The room has a bed and a TV, a set of dishes and bedside tables. On the floor there is a carpet, which immediately creates an atmosphere of comfort and warmth. The bathroom is combined. The cost of living - from 800 rubles per day in a room. The double junior suite is designed for 3 people, but the basic suite remains the same, so we will not list again. Cost 0 from 1,200 per day. Finally, a suite is a family type room that can accommodate a maximum of 6 people. The cost starts at 2,000 rubles.

Judging by the reviews, this is a very comfortable hotel, which at any time of the year is ready to offer you to stay in one of the rooms and have a good time. The staff is ready to do everything possible to make you like it here.

Instead of a conclusion

Of course, we did not consider all the hotels of Veliky Ustyug. Prices allow you to calculate your vacation budget. Compared to many winter resorts, you can have a great time for a fairly modest amount. The town itself is very quiet, cozy, a real province. Indeed, in a noisy metropolis, Santa Claus has nothing to do. Be sure to come here with the children and give them a winter fairy tale.
