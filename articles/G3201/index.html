Nokia 200: specifications and reviews

The Nokia 200 mobile phone is a model of 2012, with two SIM cards and a full QWERTY keyboard, aimed at teenagers and youth. Price, extensive multimedia features, fashionable design - all this confirms this fact. Read more about the brainchild of the Finnish manufacturer.

Design

The Nokia 200 phone boasts a wide selection of body colors - 8 options for every taste: hot pink, aquamarine, green, soft pink, blue, orange, black and pearl white. The dimensions of the model are 20 x 11.5 x 0.6 cm, weight 105 g.



The case is smoothly rounded and made of nice-to-touch glossy plastic, which does not leave a feeling of cheapness and does not creak in your hands.

No less colorful silicone cases were released for the model to protect them from scratches.

The delivery set is rather modest - the phone itself, a cable for charging, headphones, an instruction manual, and there is no cable for connecting to a computer.

The 2.4-inch QVGA TFT display with a resolution of 320 by 240 pixels looks pretty clear and colorful.

Unfortunately, the Nokia 200 does not have a sensor that adjusts the screen brightness depending on the lighting.

The screen is separated from the keyboard by a strip with navigation buttons and standard buttons for receiving / ending a call.

At the top there is a headphone jack, USB, charging, and on the right - a slot for a SIM card and microSD card.

Keyboard

This is the main feature of the model. The keys are clearly defined and separated, making them easy to find and press. The backlight is quite fierce, so the Nokia 200 is easy to use in the dark. Regular letters are white, and numbers and special characters are orange. The rows of keys are rounded. Fans of social networking and email will quickly learn how to type on the Nokia 200 at high speed.

Numeric keys are duplicated in the center of the keyboard, you can set the speed dialing of certain numbers to numbers from 2 to 9.



"Stuffing" Nokia 200

The characteristics of the phone correspond to its price category.

In the phone book there is a place for 2000 contacts, each of which can contain up to 5 numbers.

The field of entertainment is represented quite widely. So, the user can:

  • play audio and video files in a media player, since a lot of interesting information will fit on a memory card up to 32 GB;
  • take photos with a resolution of up to 1600 by 1200 pixels using a 2 megapixel camera, and also shoot video in quality up to 176 by 144 pixels;
  • listen to the radio with a system for convenient search and storage of station names, as well as record music from the radio;
  • share files via bluetooth.

An interesting option is that the keyboard flashes every minute if you have unread messages or missed calls.

The software platform is based on the popular Series 40 OS, which is characterized by stable operation and a large number of supported games and applications. To view websites using a standard browser from the manufacturer. But Facebook and Twitter are already installed as applications.



Missing Features

Please note that the Nokia 200 does not have:

  • touchscreen;
  • Wi-Fi
  • 3G
  • smart dialing.

Battery

The Nokia 200 is powered by a 1340 mAh battery, which should provide 552 hours or 23 days of standby time or 7 hours of talk time. With the GSM module turned off, you can listen to music continuously for about 52 hours. When connected to a computer via USB, charging does not occur.

Customer reviews

Most Nokia 200 users like it. Of the advantages, buyers note:

  • beautiful design and compact dimensions;
  • quality at an affordable price;
  • good sound when making calls and playing music;
  • two SIM cards, one of which can be quickly changed without rebooting the phone;
  • comfortable keyboard;
  • long battery life from 1 charge.

The disadvantages that this model has (in addition to the missing functions listed above):

  • weak camera, video is almost unsuitable for viewing;
  • You cannot play videos in a browser;
  • multitasking does not work well - each application must be closed before working with another, otherwise the phone may freeze.




To summarize

Nokia 200 has 2 key advantages - a full keyboard and 2 SIM cards. Moreover, both of them are implemented efficiently and work flawlessly.
