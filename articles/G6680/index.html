What are denim backpacks?

How to make a pattern for a backpack

The first thing you need before you start making a backpack out of jeans with your own hands is a pattern. They may be the simplest, without accurate calculations and fitting, but their presence will greatly facilitate the process.

According to the chosen model, it is necessary to draw on paper all the details of the future backpack in accordance with the desired dimensions. The main and mandatory will be the front and back of the bag, valve, straps (if not using a cord or braid). Optionally sized front and side pockets can be designed.





You need to know the dimensions of the future backpack, such as the height of the back and its width at different points (bottom, middle, top). The front of the pattern is a mirror copy of the back. If you make a backpack voluminous, with a bottom, you need to draw wedge-shaped side parts equal in height to the height of the main parts, but having the shape of a wedge, expanding to the bottom. The bottom pattern is constructed so that the long side is equal to the width of the back and front, and the short one fits the wedge in size.

Valves, pockets, straps are designed depending on the size and model of the backpack. When the pattern is ready, it must be attached to the fabric and transferred to it, taking into account allowances for the seams.





How to make a backpack bag in half an hour

It is much easier to sew a backpack bag from jeans with your own hands. The pattern in this case is practically not needed. Such a backpack is sewn from a trouser-leg, and only some details need to be cut separately - handles, straps, a valve.

For sewing bags you need only one line - the bottom seam. To give bulk to the handbag, you need to make two small seams near the side lines, 3-4 cm long, this will help form the bottom. The upper section does not need to be processed, because it is already hemmed. The final step will be the fastening of the straps and straps.





How to decorate

To give the product a final appearance, you need to decorate a backpack made of jeans with your own hands. Patterns for boys imply sharper and more rigorous lines. You can dilute this severity with the help of various decor. These can be special decals. They are attached to the fabric with an iron, they hold very securely, in addition, the variety of such stickers allows you to choose a decoration for every taste.

Leather elements will give the backpack a more sophisticated and graceful appearance. But the use of metal elements will help to create a unique stylish accessory. It can be rivets, spikes, buttons and chains.
