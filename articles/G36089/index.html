Openwork dresses - it's sexy!

Every girl who does not miss the opportunity to purchase a fashionable item can diversify her wardrobe with delicate and light handmade lace dresses. Knitting is a limitless flight of fantasy. Therefore, the fashion for knitted dresses does not end. Designers, without getting tired, create various styles that correspond to fashion trends.

Knitting materials

Having decided on the style of the product and the season in which you will wear it, you must select the appropriate yarn. In summer, a thin thread made of cotton or bamboo fiber is relevant. It is possible to keep lycra, microfiber, silk, viscose, and acrylic into the yarn to keep the product in shape and give color richness.

Crocheted openwork dresses with airy patterns in the waist or neckline area, as well as on the sides of the product, look spectacular. Crochet patterns fit both the main fabric and individual motifs. Openwork is obtained by knitting chains of several air loops. The larger the size of the hook or knitting needles, the more original the outfit will look. The most spectacular is a model created from Irish lace.

In the cold season, it is reasonable to use yarn, which includes merino wool or alpaca. Acrylic also inspires confidence, since lace dresses from this type of yarn are soft, cozy and warm. For knitting winter patterns, hooks or knitting needles are used under the numbers 4, 5, 6.

Dress Models

Stylists offer original styles of hand-knitted dresses that can be worn both everyday and for festive events. Knitted models delight in their shapes and thanks to this, you can create a dress that attracts the eyes and emphasizes the advantages of the figure. In the presented collections there are dresses that freely fall over the figure or hug it.

Also, an openwork can be selected in the model partially or completely fill the canvas. Openwork dresses can have different lengths: from mini to items reaching the floor. Knitted patterns presented by designers have no age restrictions. Both young women of fashion and women of mature age can wear them.

Any style and silhouette of the dress is relevant: it is connected with a lace or a relief pattern, has asymmetry on the sides or a classic straight cut. Particularly noteworthy were knitted knitted dresses with the presence of harnesses, aranes, braids of different widths, intricate weaves. Very relevant style in the style of boho, with flared sleeves and ethnic motifs.

Lightweight summer dresses can be sleeveless and have elegant straps. In strict models, sleeves have different lengths - to the shoulder, elbow or to the hand. Some romantic items with airy patterns fit in with a wide Bat sleeve.

Summer lace dress for girls often begin to knit with a yoke and without sleeves. The hem, as well as the upper part of the children's model is often decorated with patterns in the form of hearts.

How to wear knitted dresses

The dress is the basis of the whole image, which can be complemented with stylish accessories that are harmoniously combined with the model. The main thing is that the details do not obscure the image and do not distract from the main outfit.

The second important element is shoes. Sandals, sandals, or high-heeled shoes go well with lace dresses. For a short flared model, you can pick up moccasins or boots of a man's style. This image will be very interesting and give the image a touch of creativity. Cloth or denim shoes are not recommended. Knitted dresses go well with suede or leather shoes .

The jewelry goes well with dresses in bright colors. It is better to select products from large wooden or leather elements. Stylists do not recommend using flowers in large quantities. Jewelry, shoes and a handbag should be combined with each other. Also with a knitted dress, a wide leather belt looks attractive.

On top of the dress you can throw on any jacket, denim jacket, stoles and scarves, cropped leather jackets.

Where to wear a knitted dress

The choice of outfits for the event depends on the model and the yarn from which they are knitted. Short dresses with lace and lace, knitted from cotton, are perfect for travel and relaxation on the sea coast. Long to the floor with a light train will be appropriate at a dinner party.

Light sundresses not lower than the knee can be worn for a meeting with close friends or for a walk in the park. The image can be supplemented with a large accessory and a thin belt. An outfit of colored threads is appropriate for friendly parties and family holidays.
