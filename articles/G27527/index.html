A place for families

Today, Marmaris, a beautiful resort located in a picturesque bay at the confluence of two seas, is becoming the choice of tourists. This affects the formation of a unique climate and becomes a determining factor for the riot of lush vegetation. It's hard to believe, but until the 80s of the last century there was only a fishing village. But the wonderful nature, climate and gentle sea did not go unnoticed, and soon the first hotel was built here.

Today it is a real gem of Turkey. Hotels are located on the first coastline. There is a small port and a yacht club. Separately, it must be said about Bar Street. This is really a street of bars. It has at least a hundred different establishments with sea views. You can choose any for yourself. Just two kilometers from the city of Marmaris is a beautiful park - hotel D Resort Grand Azur. This is the only institution in the city that has its own territory, a real green park where you can walk and breathe air, swim in the pool and enjoy life.

general description

D Resort Grand Azur is located in the resort town of Marmaris, near the castle of the same name. This is an elegant and very beautiful five star beach hotel. At the same time, the city center is literally ten minutes away, that is, all the shops and restaurants, snack bars and tourist attractions are open to all tourists. The hotel opened its doors to guests twenty-five years ago, but today it is not the same as it was before. In 2013, a complete reconstruction was carried out, after which it was completely renovated, he again began to give people rest.



Transfer for guests

Tourists arrive in Turkey by plane. Especially for their convenience, a shuttle service is organized, that is, guests are met at the airport and delivered to the place. From Antalya Airport to Marmaris is about 227 km, and if you are tired after the flight, then this service will be very useful. It is paid and available upon prior request when booking at D Resort Grand Azur. But if you did not have time or did not think about ordering a transfer, then there is nothing to worry about. At the airport, buses and taxis are always on duty, which will deliver to the place. The only point is that you need to learn a few phrases in English.



Check-In Information

In total, D Resort Grand Azur is designed for 324 rooms. This hotel has only 5 floors. Check-in time is 14:00 and check-out is 12:30. If you need a visa to enter the country, the hotel will provide all the necessary documents for obtaining it. For more information, please contact the administration directly. Upon check-in, a deposit must be paid by credit card or in cash. Be sure to present photo ID. Pets are not allowed, so if you take your pets on a trip, look for another hotel. And here you will be offered free internet throughout the hotel and in the rooms.

D Resort Grand Azur (Marmaris) is perfect for families with and without children. The hotel has its own beach.
