Where is the complex located?





Aqua Hotel Resort Spa is located in one of the most popular areas of Sharm El Sheikh, namely next to Nabq Bay. About 15 km is one of the most famous bays of this resort area called Naama Bay. Sharm El Sheikh International Airport is close - it is only 8 km away. Moreover, the hotel, as well as travel agencies, provides tourists with a free shuttle service, so you should not worry about the road.

By the way, the complex was opened in 2010. In 2012, the Aqua Hotel Resort Spa (ex Top Choice Sharm Bride Resort) was completely renovated. Today, this place meets all standards and is perfect for families.

What do the rooms look like? Photo and description of rooms





The hotel complex has 127 rooms of various categories, in particular, 117 standard rooms, 8 suites, as well as two spacious family rooms. Regardless of category and location, all rooms here are bright, spacious and clean. Some rooms have a private balcony from where you can enjoy views of the garden, the surroundings and the beautiful grounds.

The rooms are comfortably furnished. There is also some household appliances, without which it is difficult to imagine a comfortable stay. For example, in the summer heat you can not do without air conditioning. And you can pass the free evening watching satellite channels (there are some Russians). Also, guests can use the phone. The minibar is filled only at the request of the residents.

In the bathroom, equipped with modern fixtures, you can refresh yourself or relax after an eventful day. There is a toilet, washbasin, toilet mirror, as well as clean towels, shampoos, soap, hair dryer.

What kind of meal plan do guests offer?





For many tourists, the issue of nutrition is important. What does the Aqua Hotel Resort Spa offer? Like almost all hotels in the resorts of Egypt, this one provides meals according to the popular all-inclusive scheme, which provides full meals throughout the day, as well as drinks that can be obtained at one of the local bars. Reviews say that the food here is really good - the dishes are always fresh, and the cook every day tries to diversify the menu. Meat, fish, side dishes, slices, salads, fresh fruits and traditional oriental pastries are served in sufficient quantities to the table.

How far is the beach? Water activities

Immediately it is worth saying that the Aqua Hotel Resort Spa is located on the second coastline - the distance to the beach is about three kilometers. Naturally, a road and a boardwalk leads to the beach, a walk along which will take about 30 minutes. Nevertheless, not all tourists like the prospect of traveling such a long distance every day. That is why the hotel provides guests with a free shuttle to the beach and back. The bus starts to run from 8 in the morning until sunset.

The beach here is public, however not too crowded. There is a pontoon, as well as a calm cleared backwater, where parents with children like to have fun. There are enough sun loungers and umbrellas on the shore, however, you need to pay for their rent, although not too much. Here, tourists can get clean beach towels, and chilled drinks can be bought at the local bar.

Naturally, here vacationers can have fun and engage in active water sports. In particular, you will have the opportunity to swim in boats, catamarans, ride a boat and water ski, try yourself in windsurfing and parasailing. Near the coast there is a large lively coral reef, so tourists like to swim with masks, watching the underwater life. Those who wish to go scuba diving can visit the local club of divers and even take short training courses.

Can I take a child with me? Conditions for children





Hotel Aqua Hotel Resort Spa 5 is primarily designed specifically for family vacations. Of course, there are necessary conditions for a fun pastime for children. First, parents are interested in amenities. Some rooms have soft folding sofas where children can sleep. A rollaway bed is also delivered to the room upon request. In the restaurant you can ask for a chair for feeding, and from the menu you can easily choose dishes that will be useful to the child.

The hotel can offer entertainment. In particular, in the courtyard there are two small, shallow pools in which children of different ages can swim - it is safe here, and the water is always well warmed up. You can have a good time on the playground with swings, fairy houses and sandboxes. A mini-club is constantly working, where your child can have fun in the company of animators, educators and children from all over the world. Children play, engage in dance and art, participate in team games and productions.

Additional service for tourists





The hotel complex Aqua Hotel Resort Spa offers its customers some additional services that are designed to make living on its territory more comfortable. To begin with, it is worth saying that in public areas there is access to the Internet. True, the reviews indicate that the connection speed here by the moments leaves much to be desired. In such cases, you can visit the Internet cafe, where for a small fee you can connect to the World Wide Web.

Laundry facilities and a currency exchange service are constantly on site. There is no medical center as such, but in case of health problems, a doctor is immediately called to the guests (by the way, the necessary medicines can be purchased at a local pharmacy). Guests are also invited to walk around the hotel shops, where they sell various household trifles, essentials, as well as interesting souvenirs.

Hotel Aqua Hotel Resort Spa (Egypt): guest reviews

Everyone knows that a huge amount of interesting information can be obtained by reading the opinions of tourists who are already lucky enough to visit this or that place.
