How to sew a pelmet with your own hands? Do-it-yourself lambrequins: patterns

Decided to slightly update the interior? Want to make clothes for windows? Learn how to sew a pelmet with your own hands. Different types of this decorative element will be suitable for any room, adding sophistication and style to it. In addition, they will easily perform a purely functional task - they will close the curtains and excess space or mask the defects of the window opening.

Types of Lambrequins

This product is a part of the curtain, which gives completeness, and also closes the fasteners and the space of the window opening to the eaves, which may have some defects and spoil the appearance of the room. Lambrequins on windows are hung both as an additional decor for curtains, and as an independent decoration, if the owners prefer lightness and do not like heavy curtains.



The types of these products are as follows:

  • simple;
  • soft
  • hard;
  • combined;
  • curly or openwork.

Each of them has its own cut features, manufacturing techniques, as well as options for suitable materials.

A simple pelmet is made from a cut of curtain fabric with a straight or curly lower part and folds. Everything is going on a special tape or manually.

Soft ones are made of lightweight fabrics and consist of all kinds of decorative elements, which are listed in the next section.

Hard lambrequin is made from curtain fabric glued to non-woven fabric, bando or proclamin. The lower edge can be straight, curly, decorated with braid, bugles, fringe, cord.

Combined products consist of a hard part and several soft elements, alternating in a certain sequence.

Openwork lambrequins are performed at the enterprise by the method of laser cutting of two-layer material. At home, it is possible to make such a product on the basis of a self-adhesive or hot-glue bando. This is a laborious process, but the result is worth the effort.





Product Elements

Tailoring of lambrequins consists of performing all the component parts and combining them into a single whole. This window decoration may include:

  • swagi;
  • flange;
  • cocktail;
  • de frill;
  • tie;
  • bell.

Swags are semicircles in which folds are laid. These are elements of a classic finish that can be symmetrical or asymmetrical. The flap looks like a sag swag. The fabric cloth in this case is thrown over the cornice rod. In the form of a de jabot, the side part of the lambrequin is performed, which has folds and a beveled edge. The long side of the frill is often decorated with twin elements of the chill. A tie is a variant of frill, but here the folds go one on top of the other. A bell or bell is a single fold of a conical shape.

Each component gives beauty to the product. In its manufacture, it is important to correctly calculate the dimensions, choose the right fabric and make a pattern. If no mistakes were made here, sewing lambrequins will not be difficult. If you are just a beginner, try to choose simple models. Gradually, gaining experience, you can cope with complex options.





Tools and materials

Before studying the information on how to sew a pelmet with your own hands, you should familiarize yourself with the list of what you need. In general terms, you need to purchase the following:

  • warp fabric;
  • interlining, bando, lining material;
  • curtain tape;
  • loops (rings) for fastening to a round cornice;
  • threads, needles, pins, scissors;
  • paper and pencil (computer and printer) for the manufacture of patterns and patterns;
  • sewing machine with overlay function (zigzag seam);
  • decoration elements (fringe, cord, bugles, braid).

The desired set is determined in the end by the type of lambrequin and the way it is attached to the cornice.



Why make a lambrequin circuit

When you are going to sew a complex product consisting of several elements, make it a rule to make a sketch on a sheet of paper. It will allow you to see how the product will look, as well as make the necessary calculations. Try to complete several patterns and choose the best one. Designers always work with several options. Drawing is better on a certain scale. A convenient value is 1:10, when in 1 cm on a sheet you have 10 real ones, that is, a three-meter cornice fits into thirty centimeters.

Fabric calculation

If you decide to make lambrequins for the hall (photo below), you will need material for four (three, five) swag, which can go one on top of the other (no less than 1/3).

Calculate the length of each element and determine how much it will overlap the next. This can be done by solving the equation or by drawing a scale drawing as described in the previous section.

For example, the length of the conditional cornice is 3.6 m, you want to arrange 4 swags with overlapping 1/3 of the neighboring one. Three intersections of 1/3 give the length of one swag, that is, 3.6 m should be divided not by 4, but by 3. The length of each swag is 1.2 m.

To make such lambrequins for the hall (the photo is presented above), do the calculations according to the required length and number of swags, scale the pattern or build it yourself in size and sew, observing the technology.

Do-it-yourself lambrequin curtains: patterns

For the simplest options, you do not need templates. A simple or hard lambrequin is easy to make from a cut of rectangular fabric or by making the lower edge curly. Patterns will be needed when performing a soft and combined version.



How to make a lambrequin: master class

If you decide to sew such a product for the first time, you should start with a simple rectangular option - a hard or simple lambrequin with an assembly.

To begin, consider the first method. So, we need to do the following:

  1. From the thermal band cut out the shape of a rectangle with a flat or curly hem without allowances.
  2. Do the same from the curtain fabric, but taking into account the allowances. See how the drawing should fit.
  3. Glue both layers.
  4. Work the edges. You can use a zigzag seam or sew an oblique trim from the inside.
  5. On the front side, they often use braid as a decor, a cord along the contour of the product.
  6. Velcro can be used as an element for attaching to the cornice. Stitch it to the upper edge of the lambrequin from the wrong side. The second part of the tape should be on the ledge.

To make a simple soft pelmet without additional decorative components (swag, peroxide and de jabot), use the following technology:

  1. Perform the necessary calculations. The height of the lambrequin is taken with allowance for seams, the width corresponds to the size of the cornice with the assembly coefficient. With light folds, it is usually 1.5. In the case of a large assembly, the value is taken from 2.5 and higher. Pay attention to how the drawing on the lambrequin will be located.
  2. Cut out the rectangle of the desired size from the fabric.
  3. Work the edges.
  4. Sew the curtain tape onto the top.
  5. Decorate the bottom with braid, cord, fringe.
  6. Assemble by stretching the corresponding threads of the curtain tape.
  7. Hang the pelmet on the ledge.

If you want to make oncoming, bow folds, and not one-sided, you will have to perform more complex calculations, as well as manually lay the folds on an assembly stick. If you have mastered simple options, you can try complex. Such an experience will come in handy when you begin to make soft curtains with a de jabot element.

So, you have learned how to sew a pelmet with your own hands. These products are so diverse and beautiful that you can transform your interior beyond recognition. A special highlight can be a decoration made in the form of a slotted pattern. How to do it, read in the next section.





Making openwork lambrequin

These products are quite new, but have already gained popularity and are in great demand due to their lightness and airiness of the "transparent" pattern. Under production conditions, a pattern is cut out of a two-layer material using a vector (contour) image made in a special computer program. The process is automated.

To make such openwork lambrequins at home, you have to work hard, but the result will pay off. Manufacturing technology will be as follows:

  1. Find or design your own drawing. Choose one that you can make. Consider your experience. If you are a beginner, try not to use small parts. Master a simple ornament. If everything works out, go on to the tricky one.
  2. Take a self-adhesive or thermobando, transfer the pattern to it and cut it out.
  3. Glue in the correct way onto the curtain fabric from the wrong side. If you have a lot of complex parts, you may also need a lining, which can be done using the glue web.
  4. Cut the pattern and sew the edges with a zigzag seam.
  5. Fill all edges with cord or braid (optional).

The masterpiece made by you will decorate the room and will attract the eyes of guests.

You have learned how to sew a pelmet with your own hands. Choose your favorite type of product, do all the necessary calculations, buy fabric and start creating beauty for your interior.
