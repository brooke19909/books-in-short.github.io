Overview of the vacuum cleaner Bosch GL 30 BGL32003

The German quality of technology has long won the hearts of Russians. Bosch is a trademark that has been supplying various equipment to store shelves for a long time. Now you can buy washing machines, mixers, blenders, vacuum cleaners and other appliances. You should not argue about the quality of products of famous brands. It will be at a high level even in the cheapest model of the line.

Currently, every home has a vacuum cleaner. It helps to quickly deal with cleaning. However, their assortment is so large that sometimes it can significantly complicate the choice. Those who choose a dry cleaning machine are advised to pay attention to a vacuum cleaner with a Bosch GL 30 BGL32003 dust collector. According to reviews, the device copes with the tasks. It is easy to operate, high-quality and functional. And the famous Bosch brand is a guarantee of reliability, thanks to which the device will work for a long time without breakdowns.



Short description

Mobility, simplicity, high productivity - the hallmark of the Bosch GL 30 vacuum cleaner range. Compact dimensions are an undeniable advantage. Weight - about 5 kg. This not only greatly facilitates the movement of the device during cleaning, but also allows you to carry it over long distances.

The case of the GL 30 BGL32003 model is made of plastic. Its shape is cylindrical. The manufacturer used beautiful bright colors (red, blue) in the lineup. The lower part is black. The wheels are plastic, internal, there are 4 in total. There is a handle near the suction port, thanks to which the device is easy to carry. For adjusting the power and turning on / off, one digger is responsible. It is located at the top of the case. When you press it, the device turns on, smoothly sets the level of suction power. The Bosch GL 30 BGL32003 vacuum cleaner can work in five modes, which range from minimum to maximum. For the convenience of power control, the manufacturer next to the button displayed all levels. On the other hand is the ventilation grill. The cost of this model is about 9000 rubles.



Specifications

Surprisingly, the Bosch GL 30 BGL32003 machine copes with cleaning along with vacuum cleaners that are equipped with a power of at least 2400 watts, although it consumes only 2000 watts. Has a HiSpin engine. Energy class: D. Parking: vertical and horizontal. Dimensions: 41x29x26 cm. Works from a network of 220 W. The model uses PowerProtect technology. PureAir filters are installed. Bosch GL 30 BGL32003 is equipped with an eight-meter network cable, which is automatically rewound. The cleaning radius reaches 10 m. There is a telescopic tube, three nozzles. Can only be used for dry cleaning. The dust bag is a bag with a capacity of 4 kg. Sucks dust with a power of 300 watts. For ease of use, a bag full indicator is installed, there is a compartment for storing additional nozzles. During operation, it reproduces a perfectly acceptable noise, which reaches almost 80 dB.



Advantages and disadvantages

The Bosch GL 30 BGL32003 vacuum cleaner has many advantages. The most significant is the price-performance ratio. It is also worth noting the suction power, due to which the cleaning is obtained as high quality. A variety of nozzles makes the process of cleaning surfaces in any place, even the most inaccessible, very simple. Also, one cannot fail to note the maneuverability of the device. It is carried out due to wheels and light weight. The capacious dust collector provides a continuous process of cleaning even the largest areas, with an average bag load lasting for several months.

Talking about the advantages, you can not keep silent about the shortcomings of the Bosch GL 30 BGL32003 model. The first thing worth noting is the disposable dust bag included. Fabric will have to be purchased additionally. The filters that the vacuum cleaner is equipped with must be changed once a year. You can save on them if you purchase branded bags of the Bosch brand, as they are included. It is also worth noting the thin plastic housing and the lack of a HEPA filter.



Reviews

So, what is your opinion on the Bosch GL 30 BGL32003? Most buyers respond positively, separately noting that for the money to buy the best device is simply impossible. However, there are complaints. Many noticed that after about six months, power is reduced. The reasons may be different. The most common is dust entering the engine. And if you do not change the filters in time, you will feel an unpleasant odor.
