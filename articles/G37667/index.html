Hotel Outrigger Laguna Phuket Beach Resort 5 * (Thailand): description and photos

Do you want to spend a vacation in Thailand, on the island of Phuket, on the shores of a wonderful clean snow-white beach? Are you accustomed to comfortable accommodation and service of the highest class, so do not consider hotels of economy class? In this case, the five-star Outrigger Laguna Phuket Beach Resort is probably what you need.





Location

This hotel is located in a lagoon on the island of Phuket, on the beach. Patong is 20 kilometers away. Phuket Airport is 25 kilometers away from the hotel. In the vicinity of Outrigger Lagoon there are several restaurants, cafes and shops. The hotel also provides a free shuttle to the city.

Apartments

As already mentioned, the hotel has a total of 255 rooms. All are spacious and pleasantly decorated. Hotel apartments are represented by categories of premium, deluxe, deluxe, club room and villa. Many rooms have great sea views. In apartments of any type you will find comfortable new furniture, a large plasma TV, a refrigerator, tea set, air conditioning, a balcony or terrace, a hairdryer, a bathroom with bathtub and shower, a telephone, a safe.
