Cyprus, "Nelia Beach" - 3 * hotel: reviews, photos, address

It is probably hard to find someone who would not have dreamed of visiting Cyprus. Nelia Beach will open its doors for you if you decide to fulfill your dream. This hotel is incredibly cozy and friendly. One of its main advantages is that it is located right by the sea.





Cyprus, Ayia Napa, Nelia Beach, 3 stars - description

In order for the vacation to be bright and unforgettable, it must be carefully planned in advance, choosing an interesting destination and a good hotel. So, many intend to go to Cyprus during the summer holidays. "Nelia Beach" in this case will be a great option. The hotel offers quite stylish, and most importantly - comfortable rooms, in which you will find everything you need for your vacation. Air conditioning will save you from the summer heat, and to pass the time, you can watch TV. Also a pleasant moment is the presence in the bathroom of a set of high-quality cosmetics for the shower, which will save you from having to take it with you.

Hotel "Nelia Beach" (Cyprus), in addition to a beautiful view from the window and home comfort, can also please you with a developed resort infrastructure. The central place, of course, is occupied by a large blue pool, next to which is a bar. If you prefer an active pastime, then you will certainly be pleased with the presence of a tennis court, sports hall and other interesting opportunities.

Quite interesting and original cuisine is famous for Cyprus. Nelia Beach invites you to its restaurant, where you can get to know more about traditional dishes. In the mornings at this restaurant you will find a buffet with a huge assortment of goodies, among which everyone will find something for himself. Also, you can always order food in your room if you do not have the desire to go down to the restaurant.





Resort Location

After crossing 48 kilometers from Larnaca International Airport, you will find yourself in the resort hotel (Cyprus) Nelia Beach. The address of this institution is Ayia Napa, October 1, 27. This is the central part of the city, and therefore you will have access to a huge number of restaurants, shops and entertainment centers. The popular Limanaki beach is only 100 meters away, while the coast of Fig Three Bay will have to overcome as much as 10 kilometers. A little further is the famous resort of Protaras.

Guest apartments

The gentle sea and vivid entertainment are dreamed by travelers who go to Cyprus. "Nelia Beach" will provide you with comfortable living conditions so that you can have a good rest after a busy resort day. You can choose one of the following accommodation options for yourself at this hotel:

  • The standard room is designed to accommodate two guests in large or single beds. This is a relatively small room, which adjoins the bathroom and balcony, from which you can admire the sea views. If you choose apartments with city views, then this option will cost you a little cheaper.
  • Junior suites are designed as a studio. Here, the whole space, thanks to a successful design solution, is divided into a bedroom and a living room. There is a large double bed, as well as folding furniture for guests. The bathroom is equipped with a bath, and a beautiful seascape opens from the balcony.
  • Executive Suite is ideal for a romantic getaway. It is represented by a bedroom with a large bed, opposite which are huge windows with sea views. There is also a fairly large living room, in which there is a couch and a coffee table. One of the most enjoyable moments can be considered the presence of a hot tub.
  • Family apartments are two large bedrooms, as well as a compact entrance area. Each room has its own bathroom. One of the bedrooms has a balcony, which can overlook both the city and the sea. Taking into account additional places, up to 6 guests can stay here.

It is impossible not to say about the design decision of the apartments of the Nelia Beach Hotel (Cyprus). The photos of the rooms, made by tourists, demonstrate a bright and at the same time restrained interior, made in a modern style.





What facilities are provided in the apartment?

The Nelia Beach Hotel (Cyprus) provides the following basic amenities in its guest apartments:

  • the bathroom, which is equipped with a shower or spa bath, electric hairdryer, as well as all the necessary bath and cosmetic accessories;
  • a small furnished balcony, which can give you an overview of both the city and the azure sea coast;
  • A landline telephone is mainly useful for contacting the reception, but if you want to call abroad, you need to pay for this service in advance;
  • there is a wall-mounted radio that will allow you to listen to music stations (though, preferably, Greek);
  • so that you can relax in this hotel at any time of the year, each room is connected to a central heating system;
  • air conditioning allows you to make a cool oasis from your apartment when the summer heat is outside;
  • satellite television allows you to watch a huge number of Russian-language channels away from your homeland, to always be up to date with the latest news;
  • for a fee you can use a small refrigerator, which can also serve as a minibar;
  • a safe with a combination lock will also be available to you only after making a certain amount of money to pay for this service.




Resort Infrastructure

Hotel "Nelia Beach" will delight its guests with numerous infrastructure facilities:

  • an impressive part of the territory is occupied by a garden that adorns the hotel with green lawns and spreading palm trees, as well as exotic flowers;
  • for those who want to sunbathe or enjoy the beautiful sea views, a fairly spacious terrace is equipped;
  • guests will certainly appreciate the presence of a large clean pool, around which an impressive number of sunbeds, as well as umbrellas to protect from the sun;
  • the restaurant serves delicious dishes of traditional local cuisine, as well as an international menu;
  • a bar located in close proximity to the pool will treat you to delicious cocktails;
  • the fast food cafe serves hamburgers, french fries, pizza, there is a huge selection of salads;
  • paid wireless Internet, the signal of which applies only to public areas;
  • the reception works round the clock, and therefore you can get an instant answer to all your questions;
  • there is a currency exchange office, which saves you from having to look for a bank branch in the city;
  • if you consider that your suitcases occupy too much space in the room, you can hand them over to the left-luggage office;
  • use the laundry service if you have dirty your things;
  • if you have any kind of celebration planned, then a banquet hall will be presented at your service;
  • use the elevator to get to the upper floors without extra effort;
  • due to the presence in the territory of convenience stores, you will always have the opportunity to buy food or hygiene items;
  • For business people, an indisputable advantage will be the availability of a modern conference room in Nelia Beach.




Organization of leisure guests

Hotel "Nelia Beach" will delight its guests with the following opportunities for organizing leisure activities:

  • the steam sauna, which brings emotions to balance, and also helps to relax muscles, is very popular among hotel guests;
  • due to the fact that the hotel has a fitness center, you can even continue your usual workouts while resting, maintaining a fit figure;
  • the massage salon specialists will demonstrate their exceptional skills, as well as a huge number of techniques aimed at relaxation or general recovery;
  • for fans of billiards there is all the necessary equipment;
  • in the garden and near the pool there are ping pong tables;
  • rent a bike and go on an exciting walk through local attractions;
  • young tourists will be able to spend free time on a modern playground;
  • every evening the animation team will organize for you an incendiary entertainment show with concert numbers and competitions;
  • by renting a car, you can devote a day to a trip to neighboring resort towns.

Useful information for guests

It is worthwhile to carefully study the information about the hotels before you go on vacation, for example, to Cyprus. Hotel "Nelia Beach 3 *", like many others, has some rules that apply to accommodation and recreation, namely:

  • When planning a trip, be sure to consider the fact that check-in does not start earlier than at two in the afternoon, but if there are rooms available, then for a fee, this procedure can be accelerated;
  • when leaving your room, you must manage to solve all organizational issues before noon, otherwise you will have to pay for each additional hour spent in the apartment;
  • cot for one child up to two years is free;
  • if a child under 12 years old can be accommodated in existing bedding, then there is no charge for his stay;
  • guests are not allowed to bring animals to the hotel (in case of violation of this rule, the tourist may be refused accommodation, and his reservation canceled);
  • it is advisable to notify the hotel several days before arrival, and do not forget to indicate your wishes regarding the room when booking.




Cyprus, Ayia Napa, Hotel Nelia Beach - positive reviews

Negative reviews

Not only positive, but also negative comments were awarded to the Nelia Beach Hotel (Cyprus). The reviews contain the following negative opinions:

  • the rooms have rather poor sound insulation, and therefore you will hear everything that happens in the yard and in the neighboring rooms;
  • taking into account all the shortcomings of this hotel, then the prices can be considered inflated (they remain so even in the off-season);
  • the hotel is trying to take money from the guests for every little thing (even for the use of the refrigerator an additional fee is charged, which can be found quite rarely);
  • even if you check into an expensive room of a high category, you will not be immune from the fact that construction will be under your windows;
  • as for a hotel of such a high level, wireless Internet could be free (especially since you can use it exclusively in public areas, as an option: go to a nearby cafe, order something to drink and use the Internet absolutely free);
  • due to the fact that gray shades prevail in the interior of the rooms, this does not affect the mood of the guests in the best way;
  • very rarely change bed linen (and even personal requests do not help);
  • in the summer, blankets are removed from the rooms, and therefore, if you freeze, there will be nothing to hide;
  • in order to get towels replaced, you had to persuade the maid for a long time;
  • in order to use the remote control from the TV and air conditioning, you need to leave a cash deposit at the reception;
  • Do not be surprised if, during check-in, they will give you the keys to the room in which someone has been living for a long time;
  • neither a special door, nor at least a curtain is provided in the shower, and therefore, after bathing, water is everywhere;
  • despite the fact that daily evening animation shows are announced, in fact this happens extremely rarely;
  • despite the fact that the hotel is located very close to the sea, due to the irrational layout of the territory you have to make a very big hook.

General impression

A lot of vivid impressions of tourists leaves Cyprus, Ayia Napa. "Nelia Beach" is a fairly popular hotel for travelers. Holidays in Cyprus are quite an expensive pleasure, and this hotel falls into the middle price category. But this only applies to the price of accommodation. Literally for every little thing they will demand money from you. This applies to the Internet, and a safe, and even a refrigerator. Needless to say, even for the remote control from the TV, a security deposit will be required from you. Nevertheless, this accommodation option remains one of the most profitable on the island.

The Nelia Beach Hotel (Cyprus) has recently begun renovations, but most of the rooms have already been completely renovated. The design is modern and interesting enough with original decorative elements. It is also encouraging that the hotel has a fairly developed infrastructure. There is a swimming pool, a gym, a sauna, bicycle rental, and shops. And much more that a modern tourist may need. But perhaps the most important advantage of the hotel is that it is located in close proximity to the beach, because most of the time guests spend swimming in the sea.
