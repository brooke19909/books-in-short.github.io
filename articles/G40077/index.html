Hotel Eleni Holiday Village 4 * (Cyprus / Paphos): photos, reviews

The hotel complex Eleni Holiday Village 4 * (Cyprus) is very popular among those people who like to relax in a presentable place. This hotel is part of the famous Aquasol Hotels chain. And this already speaks of the quality of the services provided and the level of service. However, it is worth talking about all the features of the hotel in more detail.





Location

In a small village called Chloraka, the Eleni Holiday Village 4 * complex is located. Cyprus is rich in interesting places. And one of those is the city of Paphos. And Chloraka is his part. Incidentally, this is an ideal place for a secluded holiday. Here you can enjoy peace and quiet. But also there are a large number of different restaurants, bars and other places for recreation and entertainment.

And the hotel is located on the seashore. The airport can be reached in 20 minutes, which is quite convenient. It is also worth noting that the village is rich in UNESCO monuments, which is also very interesting.

Fun and relaxation

Eleni Holiday Village 4 * (Cyprus) boasts a well-developed infrastructure. This complex has everything that you might need for a good rest. We can say for sure that even if the guests on some day do not want to leave the hotel, they will still find something to do.

It has a picturesque garden and tennis court, a sun terrace and even an internet cafe. By the way, there is even a mini-market on the territory.

Guests can also swim in the outdoor pool and sunbathe on the sun loungers. It is definitely worthwhile to devote time to water sports - volleyball and basketball. By the way, at the hotel there is another swimming pool, indoor, with heating function. And lovers of body and soul relaxation will surely have to taste pastime in the sauna, fitness center or massage.

But that is not all. In addition to the above, the hotel also has a billiard room, darts, table tennis and a video game room. In the evening, guests are entertained by animators.

By the way, if people with children come to the Eleni Holiday Village 4 * hotel (Cyprus, Paphos) , then they can not worry about the fact that their kids will be bored. There is everything that can make the holidays of the smallest guests more comfortable and fun. It has a children's pool, professional animators and a separate playground.

In Paphos Eleni Holiday Village 4 * at a decent level is the service, as well as the quality of services. In fact, the complex has everything that can come in handy. Even a conference room for 300 people and private parking (reservation is not required).

By the way, Chloraka is an ideal place not only for hiking. Many people like to explore local attractions while cycling. There are perfectly flat paths and paths for this.

Other guests are offered the terms of a tour desk and a separate luggage storage room.

By the way, in the territory of Eleni Holiday Village 4 * (Paphos, Cyprus) there is a presentable restaurant where guests can taste delicious dishes of Greek and international cuisine. There is also a huge selection of local wines. While in Cyprus, you should definitely try several types of this drink. After all, he is famous throughout the world for his wines.
