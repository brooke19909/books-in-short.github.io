Hotel "Volga" (Samara): reviews with photos

Comfortable modern hotel "Volga" (Samara) was completely renovated in 2004. The hotel is pleased to offer guests high-level service, affordable prices, convenient transportation. The building is located parallel to the Volga River near the Moskovskaya metro station on Volzhsky Avenue.



The embankment in front of the hotel was completely renovated in 2011. Now it is considered a real calling card of Samara, because it has become even more elegant. Guests of the Volga can easily be convinced of this, because they get to the promenade right from the door of the hotel.

According to guest reviews, the location of the hotel is a big plus, especially in the summer. As for the rooms, even demanding guests speak of impeccable cleanliness, although a bit outdated.

Historical reference

The Volga Hotel (Samara) was built in 1957 on the city embankment. In Soviet times, it became the first hotel in the "Stalinist Empire" style. Almost 60 years have passed, during this time a lot has changed. Today it is a wonderful 3-star business hotel with 225 comfortable spacious rooms, from the windows of which you can see the picturesque landscape of the Zhiguli Mountains and the Volga River. The hotel is within walking distance of such city attractions as:

  • house-museum of M.V. Frunze;
  • Samara Regional Puppet Theater;
  • Samart
  • Theater "Samara Square";
  • Academic Drama Theater. M. Gorky;
  • monument to the heating battery.




In 5 minutes from the tram stop you can reach the Sports Palace and the circus, and in 35 minutes - to Kurumoch Airport. The Zhiguli beer factory is a 10-minute walk away. The city's train station is 6 km away.

Number of rooms

Hotel Volga (Samara) offers accommodation in the following room types:

  • single room "standard";
  • single room "comfort";
  • double room "standard";
  • double room "comfort";
  • business class number.

Each room is equipped with comfortable furniture, a telephone, a refrigerator, Internet access, cable TV, and a bathroom. The fixed price includes breakfast. The hotel operates in the price segment "economy". Convenient location in relation to the main transport hubs, administrative buildings and attractions makes the hotel profitable not only for tourists, but also for businessmen who come on a business trip.





Different categories of vacationers opt for such an option as the Volga Hotel (Samara). Reviews of the hotel can be found very different, businessmen like transport links with all areas of the city, the ability to quickly get to the shopping and business center. Tourists who come to the city of Samara for educational purposes are attracted by the Volga Hotel with its low cost and close proximity to cultural attractions.

Fans of sports and outdoor activities can use the services of the pool and the ice palace of the Air Force Center, located a five-minute walk away. Near the hotel there are nightclubs, restaurants and other entertainment venues.

Food in the restaurant

Each room includes breakfast in the hotel restaurant. In the menu you can choose dishes of traditional Soviet cuisine or dishes prepared according to European and modern Russian recipes. The hotel operates in a retro style, so here the main bias is done in the Soviet era. Room service is available upon prior request.





Their guest reviews suggest that the hotel staff is highly qualified, polite and tidy. As for food, many people like the service of delivering food to the room, since not everyone has time for breakfast. It is enough to warn the manager about your wishes in advance.

The restaurant organizes custom-made banquets, corporate events and other celebrations. It offers a menu with a wide variety of dishes and a good wine list. The restaurant is equipped with a modern stage for performances. In the lobby there is a bar with a large assortment of drinks and light snacks. You can right here comfortably sit on a soft armchair and relax in a relaxed atmosphere.

Some of the guests say that the situation here is a bit outdated, while others understand that this is a peculiar style of a hotel of the era of the Soviet era. A conference room for 25 people is also offered for business negotiations, master classes, and trainings. The hall is well equipped technically, there is office equipment:

  • board for demonstrations;
  • DVD
  • TV.

At the request of the customer, coffee breaks and receptions are organized.

Additional services

The hotel has a 24-hour guarded parking lot. There is also a travel agency, a studio for sewing and repairing clothes, a laundry, a beauty salon and a sauna.

Paid service includes:

  • parking;
  • taxi ordering;
  • round-the-clock ATM;
  • barbershop;
  • ordering tickets;
  • fax, printer;
  • beauty salon;
  • call a doctor;

If necessary, an extra cot is placed in the room. Pets allowed. There is a left-luggage office, a long-distance communication service and a payment terminal. Before heading to the hotel, guests are usually interested in reviews and see illustrations. A wonderful place to stay is the Volga Hotel (Samara)! Photos demonstrate all the coziness and comfort of its premises.

Room reservation

The reception is open 24 hours a day. You can book a room by phone, fax or on the Internet, for this you need to specify the date of arrival and departure. Volga Hotel (Samara) reserves the right to change the range of services without prior notice, therefore it is necessary to clarify any issues of interest at the time of booking.





At the discretion of management, a fee or deposit for additional services may be charged at registration. The amount of the fee is set by the hotel independently. Check-in time is 14:00, check-in earlier than this time is agreed with the hotel. Guests must vacate the room on the day of departure until 12:00, a later time is agreed upon request in advance for an additional fee. As a rule, an hourly fee for staying in a room is taken.

Who is relaxing at the hotel?

The hotel management keeps the level of staff training at its best, the only way to achieve a professional, well-coordinated approach to business. The reputation of the hotel depends on the quality of organized teamwork. First of all, the Volga is chosen by the participants and organizers of international, Russian exhibitions, conferences, congresses.

In the middle segment of hotels, hotels in Samara on the Volga are appreciated. And the Volga Hotel occupies a special position and is a strong competitor due to its flexible pricing policy and comfortable living. Despite its far from young age, the hotel continues to be one of the most sought after. There is never a shortage of customers, there are regular guests who come as if to their home, each time expressing gratitude for the hospitality and leaving positive feedback.
