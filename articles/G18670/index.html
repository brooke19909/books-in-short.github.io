Ayatskoe lake in the Sverdlovsk region: recreation, fishing

Ayatskoye Lake is located 50 km from the city of Yekaterinburg to the north. In the Sverdlovsk region it is one of the largest. It is the source of the river. Ayat. The nearby village is Shaidurikha.

Origin

Ayatskoe Lake (Sverdlovsk Region) was the result of the loading of the river. Ayat from the side of the dam. Flooding ponds that previously existed nearby separately. People settled here during the Mesolithic. The current coast towered over the channels and lakes.





Discovered archaeological finds belonging to several cultures. Ayatskoe Lake is a state landscape reserve. Recently, the rules of conduct have been violated here, which necessitates a closer attention of the authorities. In particular, one of the reasons is the large number of visitors. They are attracted here by the beauty of nature and the rich catch obtained during fishing.

Road

A full rest is received by people who visited the Ayatskoye lake. How can I get to? Visitors move from the Sukhoi Log along the road towards the village of Kurya, heading to Talitsa and the village of Taushkansky. At this point, an asphalt road ends. Tourists move along the ground to the village of Maly Taushkan. This stretch of road is 8 kilometers.

Verkhotursky tract passing buses to the village. Shaiduriz, located at a distance of 70 km from Sverdlovsk and 28 km from Nevyansk. People spend the night with hunters in a hut near the mouth of the river. Shaitan. To get to her, they follow a swampy path of 15 km. They go to Shaitan along an unasphalted road, 15 kilometers going from the Nizhny Tagil highway.





Description

The length of the reservoir is 12 kilometers. It is located in the eastern part of the Ural ridge. The widest part is in the south and north (about 4-6 kilometers). They are separated by an isthmus of 1 km. The highest point is on the south side. From here, visitors see the mountains. Local slopes are swampy.

Ayatskoye lake acts as a flowing reservoir. The Shaitan River with a mouth of 10 meters flows here in the west, the sources of Elnichniy - in the east, Glukhovsky - in the south. Water leaves the storehouse through the riverbed. Ayat (10 m). The limiter is a reinforced concrete dam. The first fortification was built in 1825. Its remains are preserved. Even in summer, the lake is full of water due to the dam. There are small islands. The most popular of them are: Krasny Island (center) and Saint Island (south).

In winter, the Saint is of most interest. The distance between it and the pumping station is 4.5 km. There is a legend according to which, in the 18th century, a miracle worker and a hermit lived here. This fact determined the name of the object.

The bottom is everywhere covered with silt. From vegetation reeds and sedge prevail. A bird settles there. For the convenience of the fishermen, there are floodplains. Holidays in the Sverdlovsk region are the best suited for lovers of life in a tent. Around the dense forest and untouched nature.





Fishing

Fishing in the Sverdlovsk region brings generous results. Regulars of the place manage to get crucian carp, perch, roach, pike, ruff and bream. Visitors are happy with the environment. Waiting with a fishing rod in hand gives pleasant results. Ripus, carp and whitefish are settled here artificially. Visitors stop on each shore of the lake. The most popular areas are backwaters, islands. Here the tributaries flow into the lake.

Characteristics of fishing:

  • The fish caught by fishermen on a visit to the Ayatskoye lake is most often small to medium-sized perch, weighing 1 kilogram scavenger.
  • Roach pecks well.
  • Pike catch is possible.
  • The size of the fish is small.
  • Trophy specimens are rare here.

At the beginning of the last decade, carp were stocked. It breeds poorly due to inappropriate climate. Fishermen still periodically manage to catch large individuals.

Relaxation

This place has good potential as a tourist destination. Beautiful landscape of the mountain range. Holidays in the Sverdlovsk region are pleasant due to their proximity to nature. There are a lot of questions on the agenda that should be addressed in order to ennoble the object. The pond is swamped. Thick bushes formed. The highest hill is Medvezhya, reaching 307 meters. Fishermen and hunters come here every year.





A lot of fish and waterfowl. In the forest surroundings, cranberries and cranberries are harvested. Wandering through the thickets, people meet lynx, moose, hares, squirrels, foxes, roe deer and muskrat. Near Shaitan bears live. The point was not in vain assigned the status of a state reserve. This is a unique natural area in need of care.

Fishing in the Sverdlovsk region on the lake. Ayatsky is not always convenient due to the lack of approaches. Visitors are selected closer by truck, pitching tents. Sandy beaches are not provided. With great comfort, they live in fishing houses. For a membership card, the accommodation fee is 250 rubles, without this document - 350 rubles.

Guest ratings

The state of the reserve will significantly improve the creation of convenient access roads. Tourists overcome puddles and mud over the last two hundred meters. Owners of machines modest in technical capabilities pass the test not without difficulty.

The first impression is negative due to the lack of modern benefits of civilization, insufficient grooming. People who do not so much pursue comfort as enjoy the beauty and naturalness of nature do not complain. Complications appear when moving the boat to the shore from the car. 50 meters she has to be dragged in her hands along a swampy road. There is no control over the machine left out of sight.

Sandy beach, pier and parking are used only by the clients of the recreation center. During waves and strong winds, fish are poorly caught. Hooks occur due to the shallow depth and large amount of grass at the bottom. A good bite here is a matter of chance.





In total, the lake leaves a positive mark in memory due to its size, spaciousness, and the proximity of the forest. Those who are not afraid of the lack of amenities and are ready to go towards wildlife, will definitely like it here. It is important to explore all sides of the lake, find the most suitable for each person. For urban residents, this is a good chance to escape from the hustle and bustle of the day and relax on the shores of a reservoir.
