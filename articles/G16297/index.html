Samsung Galaxy Trend S7390 Smartphone: review, features and reviews

Samsung is the undisputed market leader in mobile devices. The Korean manufacturer regularly produces phones in various price categories. For example, in 2013, the Samsung Galaxy Trend GT-S7390 was released. The device attracted the attention of a domestic buyer, since it was this model that participated in the action held by the mobile operator MTS.



Equipment

The design of the packaging box designers picked up an interesting one. It shows naturalness. The panels are decorated in wood, and the emphasis is not only on color, but also on natural ornamentation. On the front side is a photograph of the smartphone. Above it in the upper left corner is the company logo, decorated in blue tones. Below is the full name of the model. On the side, the buyer can get acquainted with the main characteristics of the Galaxy Trend S7390. Here you can see information about Wi-Fi support, operating system, camera resolution, processor capabilities, communication standards and more.

But what is inside the box? Unfortunately, the manufacturer will not surprise the user with a complete set of equipment. All that is provided is documentation and a charger. Most of the comments were made regarding the lack of accessories. It's about headphones. Also, buyers will have to buy an additional memory card and case.

Design

Samsung Galaxy Trend S7390 is not very different in appearance from other devices of this manufacturer. The case looks quite compact. Its height is 121.5 mm and its width is 63.1 mm. As for the thickness, it is somewhat overestimated, is almost 11 mm. The corners on the case are rounded, a frame runs around the perimeter, which focuses on the front and rear panels. As for the latter, the camera lens and speaker hole are displayed on it. In the center of the cover is the company name.

The front panel has a large touch screen. It is framed by a black frame that expands significantly from the bottom and top. The control panel is designed typically, as for devices of the Korean manufacturer. A mechanical key is displayed in the center, touch buttons on the sides. There is no backlight. Camera

Can I use the Galaxy Trend S7390 as a camera? The characteristics of the camera, unfortunately, will not allow you to take great pictures. Optics is based on a 3 megapixel sensor. Flash and autofocus are not provided by developers. Accordingly, their absence is strongly displayed on the quality of photographs. Of course, you can experiment with the settings, but the end result will not change much. In the camera menu, the user has access to effects, brightness control, white balance and more. If desired, you can switch to the video recording mode. The quality of the videos is average. During recording, you can mute / unmute the sound. The maximum frame rate is 30.

It's time to study the technical specifications of the Galaxy Trend GT-S7390. The performance of the device is not very high. The developers used a single core chipset. Its capabilities are limited by a clock frequency of 1000 MHz. In principle, the device copes with everyday tasks well, but it can hang on games.

The low performance is also evidenced by the small amount of "RAM" - only 512 MB. Naturally, resource-intensive applications cannot be installed on the phone - it simply won‚Äôt pull them. The situation is saved by the support of memory cards. If desired, each owner can install media up to 64 GB.

Samsung's GT-S7390 is based on a modern and fairly popular OS - Android. The manufacturer installed a version of Jelly Bean. Pre-installed software, according to users, is enough. Everything functions quickly and clearly.



Samsung Galaxy Trend S7390: battery specifications

The autonomy of this phone model is provided by a lithium-ion battery. Its type is removable. Capacity is only 1,500 milliamps per hour. How long can gadget owners expect? If you believe the characteristics indicated by the manufacturer, then in standby mode the device is able to work for about 15 days. If you use the gadget as a music player, then one battery charge is enough for 40-42 hours. Those who prefer to talk on the phone for a long time should expect that they will have to charge the battery after 8 hours. With an average load in combined mode, the device lasts 2-3 days.



Firmware

Often information appears on the network that after some time the phone starts to freeze very much, system failures appear. The first thing that comes to the minds of owners in this situation is to run to a service center. In principle, this is the right decision, but advanced users recommend changing the Galaxy Trend S7390 firmware on their own first. No special knowledge is required for this, it is enough just to consistently perform certain actions.

What will be needed for flashing the phone? Of course, the gadget itself and the USB cable. Be sure to charge the battery, as if the update process is interrupted, this can completely damage the device. Install drivers on the computer, download the new firmware and the Odin program.

The order of the actions:

  • Turn on the computer / laptop.
  • Install drivers for the phone.
  • Save the new firmware file to the PC.
  • Download the Odin program. It is specifically designed to change the firmware of mobile devices.
  • Reboot PC.
  • Unzip Odin and extract the firmware files to your desktop.
  • On the phone, activate the option "USB debugging". After that, turn off the device.
  • Transfer it to Dowload Mode by simultaneously pressing the power, home, and volume rocker keys (bottom).
  • Warning should appear on the phone screen.
  • You must confirm the selection of the boot mode by pressing the top of the volume button.
  • Open the Odin program on the PC. The launch is carried out on behalf of the administrator.
  • Without turning on the phone, connect it to the computer using a USB cable.
  • It is important to pay attention that the window with the inscription ID: COM changes color to blue. In this case, you can continue the action.
  • Select the firmware files and press START.
  • Wait until the installation process is complete and only after that you can turn off the phone.

Currently, this model has already been discontinued. In specialized stores, it is not for sale. The only place you can buy a phone is in the secondary market. The price depends on the state of the device.
