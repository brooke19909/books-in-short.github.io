A little bit about the resort ...

Sural Resort Hotel 5 * refers to the famous resort of Side. The glorious past of the famous city is harmoniously combined with modern complexes and unspoiled natural beauty. All this attracts a huge number of tourists from all over the world. Some travelers go to see historical places, others swim in the clear sea and soak up the sun.

In fact, Side is a very small city, but it won the title of one of the busiest resorts in all of Turkey. In addition, it is a real open-air museum. Here are the main archaeological values ‚Äã‚Äãof the country, which are carefully protected by local laws.



Side has gained immense popularity among tourists because of the beautiful sandy beaches located a little to the west of the town. There are always a lot of vacationers. Side Attractions

Relaxing in the Sural Resort 5 * (Side), you definitely need to take such a great opportunity and see all the most famous attractions. As in ancient times, the old part of Side is located on a small part of the peninsula. The city is separated from the mainland by a moat and a wall. Oddly enough, but to this day a lot of different objects have been preserved here.

You can get into the archaeological complex through the perfectly preserved ancient main gates of the Hellenic period, from which there is a central street with columns. Unfortunately, it was from the colonnade that fragments reminiscent of past beauty and grandeur were preserved. Further, this street leads to public baths, it is in their building that the museum is currently located. Behind it is an agora with the ruins of the Tiche and Fortune temple, inside which there is a circular room with twelve columns. In the old days, pirates used this place to trade slaves.

Of course, now it is quite difficult to imagine, but the ancient ruins of the theater, in which fights of gladiators took place, and monumental gates were built in the second century. Once the theater could take up to twenty thousand spectators, it is the largest such building in all of Pamphylia. Next to it is the Roman antique temple of Dionysus, near the entrance to which the restored fountain flaunts.

In Side there are also three temples that have survived to this day, a fountain (an artificial grotto with a real source) and an aqueduct.

Entertainment

Side, as a famous resort, can offer its guests a lot of entertainment. The city has just a huge number of discos that are open until dawn. The most famous of them is Oxide. Shopping enthusiasts can visit jewelry stores, carpet shops, and leather goods. The best traders on Kolonnaya street. Here you can safely shop and are not afraid of fakes.

No less interesting for tourists will be visiting the museum located near the theater. In it you can see various architectural details and elements of Hellenistic times.



Vacationers should definitely go on an excursion (or get on their own) to the amazing Manavgat waterfall, and then try delicious and fresh dishes from trout caught in the local river.

You can also take a taxi to Aspendos and enjoy the Roman amphitheater there. If your vacation falls on June or July, then you should definitely visit one of the performances of the annual festival of opera and ballet.
