Hotel Grand Nar Hotel 4 * (Kemer, Turkey): traveler reviews

The Anatolian coast, and Kemer in particular , have long become a popular holiday destination for Russian tourists. For most travelers, you no longer need to paint the charms and attractions of the Turkish Mediterranean. Many have been here, more than once. Hotels are another matter. After all, sometimes a bad hotel is able to overshadow all the rest. Of course, you can console yourself with the thought that you came to sunbathe and swim in the sea, and not sit and overeat at the hotel. But you must admit: after all, it is better to return from the beach to a clean room with comfortable beds and working equipment, and eat on vacation is tasty and satisfying. In this article we will study one of the hotels in Kemer . It is called Grand Nar Hotel 4 *. What is the level of services, food and rooms at the Grand Nur Hotel? The description of this hotel we built solely on the reviews of tourists who have visited there recently, in the 2017 season.



Location of the hotel Grand Nar Hotel 4 *

Kemer is a resort of the Mediterranean coast of Turkey, which is located 45 kilometers from the capital of the Riviera, Antalya (and its airport). Transfer takes about an hour. For those who have not been to Kemer , let‚Äôs say that the relief is mountainous. The town with its outskirts goes up, and the coastal strip is occupied by hotels with five stars and a beautiful park. The beaches here are mostly pebble, municipal. The hotel we are describing, "Grand Nur 4 *", is located on the outskirts of Kemer . It is easy to find by standing next to the high-rise building of the Grand Viking Hotel. The center of the town , where the mosque, market and bus station are located, is five hundred meters away. But the distance to the beach is twice as much. But as tourists assure of their reviews, a free shuttle runs to the sea six times a day. The hotel has its own beach. Consequently, guests will not need to pay for sunbeds and umbrellas.



Territory

Hotel Grand Nar Hotel 4 * was erected in 2006. Tourists who visited the hotel this summer assure that the rooms have recently been renovated. They were whitewashed, installed new plumbing. The territory of the hotel is small. Its total area is two thousand eight hundred square meters. The hotel consists of one building, elongated in the form of the letter "V" . This building consists of three floors, but for the convenience of guests there are elevators. The reviews mention a beautiful foyer with high ceilings and soft multi-colored sofas. Here, on the ground floor, next to the reception, there is a restaurant. In the interior of the letter "V" there is a swimming pool with a sun terrace adjacent to it. All free space is maximally filled with greenery - lawns, palm trees, flowering bushes. There is a small playground.



Where guests are accommodated

In total there are 85 rooms in the hotel Grand Nar Hotel 4 *. Standard is the only category of guest rooms. The rooms differ only in area (20 or 22 square meters) and, accordingly, in capacity (for two or three people). All guest rooms have private balconies. True, the furniture is plastic there. The balconies offer wonderful views of the courtyard with a pool or pine-covered mountains. If you can choose the rooms, tourists recommend the third floor with a view of the outside of the letter "V" . The area in which the hotel is located is quiet, sleeping, built up with private villas. And near the pool music plays late. The bedrooms are equipped with air conditioning with individual remote control, minibar, TV, telephone. Paid services include the use of a safe. Guests are required to use bottled water on the day of arrival. Tourists do not advise throwing away containers - there is a cooler in the lobby. Satellite TV allows you to watch Russian channels. Flooring - ceramic tiles, which is very nice in the summer. The rooms are cleaned every day, and linen is changed twice a week. Bathrooms feature a hairdryer.



Nutrition

What kind of vacation in Turkey without "all inclusive"? And the Grand Nar Hotel 4 * in Kemer also practices this concept of food. In addition to the three main meals, which are scheduled on a buffet basis, guests are also fed an afternoon snack, which consists of coffee / tea, sweets and light snacks. In addition to the restaurant, the hotel has three bars. Tourists in the reviews warn: the one on the beach is paid. You should also beware of ordering anything after 23:30. Bars in the lobby and pool also go from this time on a paid basis. Local alcohol and cocktails, as well as soft and hot drinks, are freely available from 10 am to 11 pm. But not in bottles! Imported alcohol, fresh juices , smoothies, ice cream, Turkish coffee and hookah are paid.

What tourists say about food

Most of the guests liked the food at the Grand Nar Hotel 4 *. Reviews mention a large amount of greens, fruits and vegetables, which are laid out on the tables at every meal. But meat-eaters will not remain offended. Chicken is often served, not only in the form of goulash, but also baked wings and legs. Fans of Turkish cuisine with nostalgia remember the kyufta , chorba and a variety of sweets, which are prepared by the cooks of the local restaurant. Breakfast here is more monotonous. There are eggs, sausages, bacon, cereals, cheese and sausage slices. The cook bakes pancakes with you and makes an omelet with a variety of toppings.



Beach

Despite the fact that the Grand Nar Hotel 4 * is located one kilometer from the sea, it has its own piece of the coast. Most tourists went to the beach on foot. They note that such a walk gives only positive emotions, because the path passes through such a beautiful area. Back sun- soaked vacationers were taken away by a free bus. He walks six times a day. The beach of the hotel 50 meters long, tourists called well-equipped. There is a shower and a dressing room. Sand and pebble beach is located in the park area of Kemer . Sunbeds and umbrellas are free for hotel guests. But drinks and snacks in the bar are sold for money. Tourists saw only one minus - large pebbles at the entrance to the sea. But this claim is more likely to Kemer than to the hotel. Tourists recommend purchasing coral slippers - they are sold everywhere in the resort. The hotel has two pools - an adult, one and a half meters deep, and a children's one, where the water is knee-deep. Beach towels are not given here.



Hotel Services

Grand Nar Hotel 4 * may well add the word " spa " to its name. After all, the hotel has a Finnish sauna and Turkish hammam. Use by guests of these baths is free and unlimited. But the massage and the traditional Turkish soap peeling - already for the money. Free services include: Wi-Fi in the hotel lobby, gym, table tennis, beach volleyball, backgammon and chess. The hotel also has animation in Russian. Cribs and high chairs, a playground and a shallow pool are provided for children. Before the adult disco for kids, dance music is played. Paid services include massages, billiards, dry cleaning with laundry, water activities on the beach. Reception at the hotel is open around the clock. Its employees speak Russian reasonably well. Currency exchange and car rental are also available.

Grand Nar Hotel 4 * (Kemer): reviews

Tourists liked the polite, helpful and friendly staff. They tried to fulfill any request in a timely and professional manner. But travelers in their reviews warn: this hotel positions itself as a place of quiet family vacation. Therefore, they demand silence there, and partying is not welcome. But if you came to buy a bronze tan, swim in the warm sea and see the beautiful country of Turkey, then this hotel will suit you .
