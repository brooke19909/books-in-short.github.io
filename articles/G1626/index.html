Benefits of a reusable calendar

Compared to traditional paper calendars, the eternal analogue has several advantages:

  1. Shows the exact date and day of the week in any year, can be used until you get bored.

Family option

It is very convenient when you can leave memos for family members on a certain date. You can get rid of notes scattered around the house with instructions using a homemade calendar. In this option, a correctly made workpiece is important. Perpetual calendar manufactured as follows:

  1. A template is made on the computer with the written dates and days of the week. Numbering should go from left to right. The first line is 1-7, the second is 8-14 and so on until the 31st.
  2. It is printed on sheets of the A3 format, it is laminated.
  3. Cuts vertically. You should get columns with dates 1, 8, 15, 22, 29; 2, 9, 16, 23, 30, etc. Days of the week are also cut out.
  4. On the back of the strips, magnets are glued to the double-sided tape. Optimal use of flexible.
  5. Strips with numbers and days of the week are mounted on magnets to the refrigerator in accordance with the current date.

It is convenient to attach notes to magnets to such a calendar. You can write tasks directly on it using a washable marker.

DIY perpetual calendar

You can make a wooden version yourself, without buying blanks. To do this, you must:

  1. A small board.
  2. Whetstones of different sizes (for cubes and dice with months).
  3. Jigsaw.
  4. Paint.
  5. Lacquer.
  6. Glue.
  7. Tracing paper.
  8. Napkin with a pattern.

A stand, a vertical wall, sides for cubes and a "pocket" are cut out from the board. The parts stick together, creating a common foundation. After the glue dries, they can be painted.

The napkin is divided into layers and a pattern is cut out of it. It is applied to a wooden surface, after which it is varnished.



The tracing paper prints the numbers and names of the months, it is desirable that the tone matches the main picture. Carefully cut and glued onto cubes and cubes.
