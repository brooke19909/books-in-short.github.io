Takes for a girl with knitting needles how to knit? Knitting a beret for a girl: description, scheme

An integral part of a women's wardrobe is a familiar and familiar headgear. It is useful in the winter cold, and to create your own image in ordinary, everyday life. Moreover, for any season there are a variety of knitting options. In this article, we will try to figure out how to knit knitting needles for a girl and make sure that this fun process is not only a great opportunity to spend leisure time. It is also a necessary and beautiful gift for your beloved daughter.

Where to start?

To make beret knitting with needles for girls, beginning knitters need to know: no matter what yarn they choose, knitting needles for it should be done in this way - 2: 1. That is, the selected thread folded in half should equal the thickness of the spoke. If you choose the right one, you will get high-quality knitting: the fabric is moderately dense, yarn for work is comfortable, knitting needles are suitable.

Knitting needles for the girl should be knitted from yarn, which is selected depending on the season, season. That is, the time when it is supposed to wear this piece of clothing.



If you think about knitting a light summer beret, then linen or cotton yarn is suitable. If the headpiece is designed for a cool autumn, it is better to choose a semi-synthetic or natural wool. But if it is supposed to be knitted with knitting needles for the girl who she will wear in the winter, perhaps the best choice would be mohair, yarn ‚Äúgrass‚Äù or thread, in which angora is added. Such a beret will be very warm, soft and comfortable.

A simple beret for a young lady. Training

At the age when the little ones begin to walk, they already little by little understand what they like in clothes and accessories. Therefore, a beautiful beret for a girl with knitting needles will be a wonderful gift for a little fashionista.

This beret should be knitted round, choosing a simple pattern. Decreases are done along seven lines.



Designed for a head circumference of 38 (41) cm. When finished, the height of the product should be 22 (25) cm.

In order to knit such a model, you will need: circular knitting needles No. 3 (their length is 40 cm), a set of stocking knitting needles No. 3, pure-wool yarn (50 g per 120 m) - 100 g, a button in the color of the yarn - as a decoration - 1 piece and marker.

The pattern with which the beret will be knitted is the front surface; when knitting round in all rows there should be front loops. Knitting density: 22 loops per 30 rows - equal to 10 by 10 cm.

Sequencing

Knitting a beret with knitting needles for girls is as follows. Knit 84 (90) stitches and knit 5 cm of face around. smooth.

Knit the next row as follows: 2 loops in 1 front, 1 double crochet * - from * to * repeat to the end of the row.

Then again continue the front surface, adding in the next row 1 loop after every third and so 28 times (in turn after the second and third loops 36 times). It turns out 112 (126) loops.



Knit after the cuff 7 (8) cm and set the marker about 16 (18) p. Row seven times. In the same row, you must knit the marked eyelet and the previous one in 1 person .. So, in the work we get 105 (119) eyelets. Decreases should be repeated in each even row another 14 (16) p. Now there are seven loops left. You can break the thread, thread through the remaining loops, tighten, withdraw to the wrong side and secure. Now is the time to sew a button. Children's picks for girls ready.

Gentle beret on a little beauty

It is not difficult to make such a gift to your beloved daughter. You will need knitting needles No. 3, on which 130 loops should be typed and knit from them the usual 1x1 elastic band. The thickness should be about 4-5 centimeters (this is the usual standard) or as your mother decides.

After the elastic is connected, it is necessary to carefully distribute the loops into seven divisions with the wrong loops. The gaps between the sectors must be knitted in three pieces with facial loops.



Knit the second row like this: you need to knit five loops from each loop that is between the divisions. Get 15 stitches.

Now you need to make several rows, after which you can see the pattern of beautiful volumetric leaves.

Tie to the required size, and then evenly reduce the loops: first - purl, and then facial.

After the reduction has been completed, and the product is connected, those loops that remain must be tied with a thread and pulled tight. The thread should be carefully stretched inside the finished beret. To make it more elegant and festive, you can sew a small bells or a beautiful flower.

But as a child will be happy with such a gift, surrounded by the attention, love and care of his mother.
