Refrigerator Indesit SB 185: specifications, description, reviews

Sometimes the need to replace the refrigerator occurs suddenly. A failed old unit can no longer be repaired, and it is impossible to do without such a device in modern families. In such a situation, the time to choose a worthy replacement is disastrously small. In addition, many have a limited budget. But do not forget that this is a purchase for how many years ahead, so the reliability of the refrigerator should be above all. Fortunately, in the modern market there are models for middle-class buyers who perform the functions assigned to them in a quality manner, and the reliability of their work is noted by many already held consumers. One of these refrigerator models is the Indesit SB 185.





Brief information about the manufacturer

Not everyone knows that the history of the founding of the company began in the 30s of the last century. The founder is Italian businessman A. Merlone. However, the company received the familiar name Indesit much later. To date, this brand is produced in conjunction with another well-known, Hotpoint Ariston. Refrigerators are being assembled, including Indesit SB 185, in the Russian city of Lipetsk according to technology and under the control of Italians.

Today, this manufacturer owns about 15% of the consumer market, which already indicates the scale of the entire enterprise. The company won the love of its fans and customers through the use of the latest technology, reasonable pricing and a wide range.

The main characteristics of the model

First of all, the Indesit SB 185 refrigerator will appeal to the fair sex, who concentrate a lot of attention on the external characteristics. The graceful white case will undoubtedly fit into any kitchen interior.



The capacity indicator is about 339 liters, which makes it possible for the family to use up to 5 people. Thanks to easy intuitive management, difficulties with operation will not arise.

This model belongs to class B, so you can not worry about bills for electricity. Indeed, the total amount of consumption per year is not more than 445 kW.

The refrigerator compartment accounts for about 235 liters. The entire space is divided into shelves made of thick and durable glass. Therefore, it is quite possible to put heavy pots in the chamber.

The freezer has a volume of 104 liters. Manual defrosting is extremely rare. Due to the presence of spacious boxes, you can store products of any category and in a fairly large amount. The power of freezing is also quite decent - about 4 kg per day.





Defrost system

The Indesit SB 185 double-chamber refrigerator has a drip defrosting system. It is also called the "crying wall." Such a system does not provide a fan. A small layer of ice that forms on the rear wall of the refrigerator compartment periodically thaws, water flows into the hole provided for this. Through it, liquid enters a container located outside on the back of the refrigerator. The container does not need to be cleaned. During operation of the refrigerator, condensate evaporates. If we analyze the opinions of consumers about the model Indesit SB 185, reviews about this defrosting system are more positive. An ice "coat", as in the old Soviet refrigerators, is not formed here.

The minimum temperature maintained in the freezer is minus 24 ¬∞ C. It is defrosted manually. If a large number of products are loaded into the freezer, you can use the Super Freeze option





It is recommended that refrigerators with a drip defrosting system, including Indesit SB 185, be completely turned off once every 6-8 months to wash and remove the ice that forms in the freezer compartment.

The presence or absence of the ability to change the location of the shelves

The refrigerator compartment has several shelves dividing the common space into uniform parts. However, in any family there is a tall dish that cannot be delivered with such a distribution. Model flaws
