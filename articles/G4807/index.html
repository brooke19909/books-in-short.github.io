How to get from Girona Airport to Barcelona

If you flew to Catalonia on one of the low-cost airlines, then most likely it will land in one of the small hubs. This is Girona Airport. It is not in the city center, so it will be far to the railway station. But from here you can easily get to the Barcelona North Bus Station. It is located almost in the center of the capital of Catalonia. You just need to get on a special bus. It is called Barcelona Bass. His stop is at the exit of the airport to the right.

If you are going to the Girona airport from Barcelona by this type of transport, then you should know: tickets for it in this direction are bought at a separate ticket office. They do not sell them in the cabin. Go down to the first floor of the Barcelona bus station building and you will become the owner of travel documents. The buses themselves run regularly - from platforms 28 and 29 of the Northern Bus Station.

From the center of Barcelona to Girona Airport, as well as back, they get in an hour and a quarter. We can say that this is the only type of bus that you can ride on the route Barcelona - Girona.

How to get to the North Station? It can be easily reached by metro, red line L1, station "Triumphal Arch".





Barcelona - Girona: how to get at night

Trains between the two cities run from seven in the morning until ten in the evening. But if you arrived in Barcelona too late, then you may have problems getting to Girona. The fact is that public transport in Spain generally does not work from midnight to six in the morning. So at this time and do not rely on the train or bus Barcelona - Girona. How to get there all the same at night? There are three options: rented car, transfer or taxi. The latter will cost from one hundred and fifty euros.
