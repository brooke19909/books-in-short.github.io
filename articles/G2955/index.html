"Peshkovo Estate": reviews and photos

"Peshkovo Manor" as a leisure complex received its first guests more than five years ago. In recent years, the construction of the hotel has ended in the estate, in which a public bath has opened, cozy cottages, a cafe with a special menu and several banquet halls have appeared.





Historical roots

The estate is not accidentally mentioned in the name of the complex. Two centuries ago, in these places was the landowner estate of Durnov, a court counselor. He was a representative of the famous noble family. Today, only the outbuilding remained from Durnov’s possessions.

Warm welcome

"Peshkovo Manor" is located in a surprisingly beautiful area of ​​the Moscow region. The best time to visit the complex is summer or autumn. In the middle of autumn, the Peshkovsky Fun festival is held here.

There are two car parks in front of the main entrance and an additional one (near the hotel). They are free and surrounded by video cameras around the perimeter.

For children, a lot of entertainment is thought out here. There is, for example, an organized children's area. But the observation of the life of rabbits is much more amazing for the kids. A children's pool is open in summer.

Cafe pleases with affordable prices and a special "Chekhov" menu. This is often mentioned in reviews. Visitors note that the cuisine is excellent here, there are many goodies. In general, everything is at home, just do not have to cook!

Local Attractions

The first glance at the entrance to the estate falls on a cheerful mill. According to a tradition not so long ago, the newlyweds, making a wish, tie ribbons on the blades.

There is a beautiful stylized cart for a photo shoot, an old outhouse, log cabins, a hotel, baths and much more.

Manor Center - hotel

Outwardly, it is a two-story building, in fact - three floors. In the basement there is a bathhouse.





The rooms, and there are 13, are cozy and large. Each has a TV, sofa, chest of drawers, bedside tables, spare bedding, a set of towels, slippers and soap supplies. There are two rooms in the Standard + room. The bathroom and toilet are separate. The bathroom has a large shower, sink and mirror with lighting.

On the second and third floors, in the halls, there are billiard tables.

Cafe

Seriously eat or eat at the local cafe. The facade of the building and the interior are stylized in Russian antiquity. There are two rooms. Large on the first floor and a little smaller - on the second. "Peshkovo Manor" (Chekhov district) is proud of its cuisine. All meals are prepared using fresh, locally produced farm products. And semi-finished products are made here. Especially appreciated by guests are sausages for frying and pickled kebabs.

Two years ago, a chip appeared in the cafe: "Chekhov's menu." The well-known fact that a famous writer lived and worked in these places was adopted by Peshkovo workers (the estate). Chekhov had special addictions to certain dishes; this is also a well-known fact. Long and serious research in cookbooks, personal notebooks of the cook Anton Pavlovich, his sisters and mother made it possible to create a special menu "What Chekhov ate."





It is composed of dishes that the famous writer preferred. There are, for example, dumplings with cottage cheese and cabbage under the name “This and that”, or tomatoes filled with delicate omelet with bread, indicated on the menu as “Three Sisters”. Tomato soup puree is called “Cook marries”, and potato pancakes - “Summer residents”. They make unique tea “The Seagull” from the Melikhovsky medicinal and spicy herbs, from the local cherries - the compote “The Cherry Orchard”, even bake simple potatoes in a special way, with crackers and walnuts, and they call it “The Man in the Case”.

The cuisine of this institution is so unique that it deservedly hit the Moscow Region gastronomic map. And the chef is preparing here, who has become the "Best in profession" in the Moscow region.

There is a pre-order: by the agreed time, a table is set.

All visitors like Peshkovo Manor, reviews are always enthusiastic. And not only regarding the kitchen.

Separate housing

"Peshkovo Estate" invites large companies to rest. They are resettled in three Russian log cabins standing at some distance from the cafe. They are ready to host companies of up to eight people. The entire first floor is occupied by a living room and a toilet with shower, on the second there are several bedrooms. The living room has a spacious wardrobe, refrigerator, TV, a set of upholstered furniture, a microwave.





Organization of holidays

"Peshkovo Manor" (Chekhov district) is a favorite venue for weddings. There are two banquet halls. In the main all-weather hall, the walls contain numerous photos of the newlyweds in a vintage style and there is a dressing room. The second hall is summer.

On the territory there are several gazebos with a capacity of up to 12 people. If desired, you can book a holiday in a small temporarily installed tent.

There are a lot of reviews about the holidays in Peshkovo. Almost all of them are enthusiastic and laudatory. The guests of the estate say that they again and again wish to return and relax with their souls. Thanks to the chefs and all the staff for the excellent service and delicious food.

Entertainment

For children there is a beautifully organized play area. In the summer, entertaining rabbits run on her lawns.

Adults can relieve stress at a local dash.

"Peshkovo Manor" has two baths on the territory. They are wood burning. Built according to the rules of a real Russian bath. One of them is with a font. The furniture in the baths is made of natural wood and made by hand. The second, attic floor is occupied by simulators.





A public bath (in the hotel building, in the basement), as usual, is waiting for men on Saturday, and the ladies on Sundays.

The leisure complex has a dedicated barbecue picnic area. A large barbecue, gazebo and wooden tables have a wonderful pastime. In the same area there are gun houses for laser tag and paintball. You can play these games, as well as soccer and even volleyball at organized venues outside the estate. There are real buses and wooden shelters on the paintball court. It is equally interesting to play laser tag. The weapon is the most “real”: Kalashnikov assault rifles, blasters and various sniper rifles. Computer software is modern.

A picnic can also be arranged at the pond. There are prepared places for this. A system of ponds appeared in these places before the revolution. By the end of the 90s, there were several terrible puddles in their place. The owners of the estate began their restoration. In one of them you can swim and even ride a boat or catamaran.

Cottage Santa Claus

For family vacations, Peshkovo Manor will also be a good place (photos are presented to the reader in the article). This is especially true for winter holidays. On New Year's days, the estate turns into a magic cottage of Santa Claus.

Near the house in which Santa Claus stopped, there are his enchanted sledges. Children are already in anticipation of delight. The Snow Maiden meets them. While she talks about the magic sleigh, you can take a picture with her. If the baby has already written a letter, it can be given personally to Santa Claus or dropped into a special box. Then the children find themselves in a riddle and wishes of the arbor. Every child gets a prediction here for next year. Then the little guests find themselves in the wonderful house of Santa Claus, drink tea with bagels and listen to funny stories.





Rarity

"Peshkovo Manor" is engaged in the restoration of the oldest building of these places - the outbuilding. This is a building of the early 20th century. Tourists are not allowed into it, but work inside is going on actively.
