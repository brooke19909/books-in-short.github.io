What should be considered before starting work?

Before you start knitting sweaters, you need to choose yarn that is suitable for the season and weather. In the summer, it is better to wear cardigans made of cotton or viscose threads. Silk yarn products with the addition of bamboo threads are also very comfortable to wear. In cold weather, it is advisable to use balls of natural wool or threads mixed with acrylic.

Often, in addition to schemes with a description of a lace cardigan, knitted with needles, patterns are used to sew outerwear. First you need to remove from the drawings all the measurements necessary to calculate the number of loops. Then, from the manufactured sample, find out the density of your knitting and calculate the number of loops for the set, based on the indicator of the width of the bottom of the back.

The classic model begins to knit from the bottom edge, gradually rising to the armholes, and then to the neck. Separately, the shelves and sleeves of the product are knitted, which begin with the formation of cuffs. Further, the sleeves are expanded and knitted according to the pattern. At the end of the work, all the details of the sweater are sewn together.

How to knit a light outfit?

The rest of the article describes how to knit an openwork cardigan with knitting needles according to the schemes with a description. Our example is aimed at women with a chest girth of 104 cm. Knitting description

We start from the back. With smaller needles, dial 115 loops, then knit the first row with the smallest rubber band about 5 cm in height. Then, after every 29 loops, make an increase. Change the knitting needles and knit the main pattern with lace.

Form the armholes at the shelves by closing 8 loops from the beginning of the line. Repeat the knitting of the shoulder bevels in the same way as the description of the creation of the back. To expand the sleeves in each 12 rows of the pattern, add 1 front loop at the edges to form 67 loops on the knitting needles. Then make increases in the 14th row before 77 loops are created. Assembly and strapping

Form the seams on the shoulders, sew the sleeves and make a side seam. Along the edge of the shelves and collar, loop on circular knitting needles and knit 10 rows of garter stitch to form a collar. For the buttons, create holes by closing two loops in their place, and in the next line throw a thread 2 times on the knitting needle. The product is ready.
