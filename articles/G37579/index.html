The principle of operation of the cooler

All air conditioners work on the same principle. Moreover, they are direct relatives of an even more common device in the home , a refrigerator. And they function quite simply. The reasons why the air conditioner does not cool may be the same as the reason the refrigerator does not want to freeze.

All railway guides know a little secret, which makes it possible to provide cold drinks to passengers who wish even without hot appliances even on the hottest afternoon. It is enough to wrap a bottle or a jar in a damp towel and put this combination out of the window. A fast-moving car is in a stream of air, and water evaporates quickly. In this case, thermal energy is removed from glass or aluminum containers. The reverse process is condensation, with it heat is consumed. All refrigeration technology works on this principle, from small bar refrigerators to huge port facilities bearing the same name.





What air conditioning consists of

In order for the air conditioner to work, it must have five operational nodes: an evaporator, a condenser, a compressor, a fan, and a choke. As is clear from the previous section, heat is taken from the room air in the evaporator. But this is not enough, energy must be removed, otherwise, according to the law of its conservation, it will remain in isothermally isolated space. The most common failure

The most common reason that the air conditioner cools poorly (or generally drives warm air, suddenly becoming an ordinary fan) is a refrigerant leak. It is expressed in violation of the integrity of the closed loop, through which an inert gas circulates, becoming a liquid in the capacitor. Actually, nothing is perfect in nature, and sooner or later this happens. In this situation, it is almost impossible to cope with the task yourself. In addition, most likely, it will be necessary to determine the place of the leak (somewhere the refrigerant has disappeared), otherwise the procedure will soon have to be repeated.





Other reasons for inefficient operation

The air filter is very important in the operation of the microclimate system. If it clogs, then not only does the air conditioner not cool, there is a danger of contracting some dangerous virus. A lot of bacilli accumulate in the filter, they fall into the air that the inhabitants of the room breathe. You can change this consumable part yourself.

Still there is a water hammer, incapacitating the system. This happens when you try to turn on the device at a very low temperature (which is generally pointless). In this case, it is not gas that gets into the compressor, as expected, but liquid that has not had time to evaporate. If this happens, you need to contact the service center.
