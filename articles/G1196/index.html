Mackenzie Beach Hotel & Apartments 3 * (Cyprus / Larnaca): description and reviews of tourists

Cyprus is a small island between Egypt and Turkey, located in the Mediterranean Sea, which is considered a win-win destination for a beach holiday. The weather here is always good, and the swimming season lasts a long time, and the flight, if we talk about Moscow, takes only three hours.

In addition to a quality beach holiday, the island has a good hotel base. Each tourist will find an option for himself to his taste and means.





This review presents to your attention a modest and economical hotel in Cyprus called Mackenzie Beach. The hotel complex is a five-story building located on the first coastline, in a very calm and quiet place. The hotel has virtually no territory, and the infrastructure is common with the neighboring Flamingo Hotel.

If you are going on vacation overseas and the sun and do not plan to spend a lot of time in your room, then this hotel is exactly what you are ideally suited for.

Mackenzie Beach Hotel Apts and its infrastructure

For 21 years, the hotel complex has been welcoming tourists from around the world on its territory. Despite its age, the hotel is in excellent condition, its staff carefully monitor the cleanliness and appearance of the complex. About four years ago, the hotel was renovated.





It is important to note that a 5-minute walk from the complex is located Mackenzie Beach, marked with the European Blue Flag.

The hotel has a spacious rooftop pool with sun loungers and an open-air bar. It has its own parking zone, a 24-hour reception, a special room for luggage storage, a banquet hall, a conference room. In addition, in the business center of the hotel, each guest can use the Internet access, receive or send a fax, print or copy documents. Such services will help busy guests combine their vacations with urgent work tasks.

All Mackenzie apartments have a balcony with sea, city or lake views, a kitchenette and a seating area.

Location

The Mackenzie Beach Hotel is located in Larnaca , across the road from the sandy municipal beach, fifteen minutes from the city center. The road from the hotel to the airport takes about 20 minutes, the air harbor is five kilometers away. There are many shops, cafes and restaurants in the immediate vicinity.

Hotel Details

Mackenzie Beach Hotel has prepared for its guests forty-four stylishly decorated rooms, which have all the necessary furniture and appliances for a comfortable stay.





Each room has:

  • air conditioning;
  • TV;
  • safe (paid);
  • balcony;
  • equipped kitchen;
  • bathroom.

The hotel has rooms for disabled guests and non-smoking rooms.

The rooms are cleaned every day, linen and towels are changed three times a week.

To use electrical outlets, you need a special adapter.

Beach

Mackenzie Beach (Larnaca) has a sandy beach of the same name, which was awarded the "Blue Flag". Beach towels are not provided, umbrellas and sun loungers can be rented for a fee. The beach is quite spacious, clean, flat entry into the sea (platform), great for small children.

Catering

The hotel offers its guests a variety of catering systems: all-inclusive, full board, half board and breakfast. Meals are organized on the basis of the buffet. Daily in the main restaurant Mackenzie Beach Hotel Apts 3 *, which has the beautiful name MAIN RESTURANT, tables are served with fresh side dishes, meat dishes, desserts, fruits and pastries. The restaurant is open from 7 a.m. to 10 p.m.

BAR AND GRILL, located next to the lobby, offers Mediterranean cuisine from morning until midnight.

On the fifth floor of the hotel is located VANILLA SKY lounge, which also offers guests a tasty meal and enjoy a gorgeous view.





Entertainment

For entertainment and relaxation at Mackenzie Beach Aparthotel (the hotel does not yet have 4 stars, but three are also of a rather good level) there is a lounge area with a large TV, an outdoor pool, water and beach sports, a games room, a sauna, playground for children. For an additional fee, guests are provided with Internet access, use of the gym, massage room, bicycle rental, water sports center, disco.





Is the hotel suitable for children

Mackenzie Beach Hotel accepts children of all ages. Especially for young travelers, a modern children's playground is installed here, the restaurant has high children's chairs, and upon request, the hotel staff will bring a cot to the room for the child. The hotel also has a highly qualified nanny who is always ready to look after the baby while parents are resting. Babysitting services are paid.

Hotel experience

Opinions of vacationers about the hotel presented in this article often differ. The fact is that some travel companies give Mackenzie Beach a 4- star hotel, but this is not so. The hotel has three stars by European standard, and do not require more from it. It is quite consistent with its stars and value for money. Let's try to look at Mackenzie objectively and understand all its pros and cons.

Advantages of the hotel:

  • The complex has a good location, everything you need - restaurants, shops, the beach, city center, airport - is nearby.
  • Quick check in. Many guests vacationing here note that the hotel has no problems with checking in, the room keys are issued almost immediately upon arrival. At the reception there is a very nice girl who is always happy to help. Any question can be addressed to her, and she will show and tell everything.
  • Spacious rooms with great views. The majority of tourists praise the hotel rooms. According to them, the rooms are spacious, clean, furniture, although not new, but in good condition. If there is no dishes in the kitchen, then upon request they provide all of them. The view from the windows is amazing.
  • Beach and sea. There is an excellent sandy beach right across the road from the hotel. The sea is quite clean, the entrance is very comfortable, smooth, which is a lot of importance for vacationers with children. Three hundred meters from the hotel is the city promenade.




Disadvantages of the hotel:

  • The quality of the Internet connection. According to many vacationers at Mackenzie Beach, the hotel needs to improve the quality of its connection to wireless networks. The Internet almost always works intermittently, which is not very convenient, and for many people this fact, to put it mildly, upsets.
  • Poor cleaning quality. The rooms are poorly cleaned, and it does not interfere with redecorating the rooms and improving sound insulation.
  • Nutrition. Almost all tourists agree that it is better not to take meals at the hotel. According to them, the food is all monotonous, the choice is small, the cooks should also work on the taste.

The cost of trips

As mentioned above, Mackenzie Beach is a reasonably priced vacation option. The cost of trips to this hotel starts from 35 thousand rubles for two adults for seven nights with meals according to the "breakfast" system. If the family goes on vacation with the child, then about 15 thousand rubles will be added to this cost.

When planning a vacation on the island, you should know that entry to its territory is only possible with a visa. It is not a problem to make a visa; its production time is one working day. It should also be noted that you do not need to pay for a visa, for residents of Russia it is completely free.

Holidays in Larnaca

Larnaca is a great resort for people who prefer active entertainment and beach vacations. The most popular beaches in the city are Finikoudes Beach and Mackenzie Beach. Here you can not only swim and sunbathe, but also have fun. These beaches have long been considered centers of outdoor activities, here you can go diving, windsurfing, go for a boat trip, paraglide and more. In the evenings there are always night clubs, colorful bars, festivals and concerts are often held on the coast.

To diversify your vacation, you can visit the island's local attractions: St. Lazarus Church, Larnaca Castle, Kition excavation site. Also interesting places are the museum of owls, the mountain village of the masters of Lefkara, the museum complex Mousio Theasis.





For tourists who come to rest with their families and children, an excursion around the horse farm will be interesting. Here you can ride on olive and orange groves and attend horse riding lessons. In the village of Skarinou there is a donkey farm. On its territory there is a beautiful botanical garden and the oldest olive tree grows, whose age is about a thousand years. Children will also be interested in a boat trip with a glass bottom to the sunken ferry. The wreckage of the ship became an artificial reef, and now they live in amazing marine life. For children from 4 to 12 years old, it will be very interesting to visit the entertainment centers Wow Action Park and Camel-park. On their territory you can ride a roller coaster, jump on trampolines, try your hand at rock climbing and much more.

Nature lovers should drive to the Salt Lake. This is a very picturesque corner, where rare representatives of the fauna - flamingos live.

Conclusion

Judging by the description, Mackenzie Beach Hotel is not a bad budget city hotel. Suitable for unassuming vacationers who love a relaxing holiday and prefer rooms with a kitchen.
