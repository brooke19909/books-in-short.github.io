There are places on Earth, as if specially created in order to delight people with their beauty and leave a part of themselves in their souls forever. Where to stay

It is unique in this country that the people who lived here for several thousand years have preserved nature as it was at the time they first appeared on this earth. Today it is a mecca for tourists from all over the world. Especially popular among travelers is Central Dalmatia. Here you can choose a hotel for every taste and budget. Attractions near the hotel

Too controversial opinions about the hotel from those who rested in Medena Hotel 3 * (Croatia). Reviews of attractions located close to it, on the contrary, are always the most enthusiastic.
