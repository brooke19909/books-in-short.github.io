Bollywood Sea Queen 3 * (India, Goa): reviews, photos

India has always been famous for its magnificent beaches, which is the main criterion for choosing this country for vacation. Especially often, tourists choose South Goa, where you can admire the beautiful landscapes, get acquainted with quite fascinating features of the life of the local population and enjoy plenty of swimming. For living, many prefer the Bollywood Sea Queen 3 * hotel, where you can spend your holidays well. True, it is worth remembering that the hotel is a three-star hotel, and you should not expect a European level of service for guests here.

Hotel location

Hotel Bollywood Sea Queen 3 * is located in the state of India Goa. It is 20 km from Dabolim Airport. The journey takes about half an hour, which largely depends on the driver. Margao, located 7 kilometers from the hotel, has a train station. Many tourists get there by train, and then to the Bollywood Sea Queen 3 * hotel (India). Colva (Colva), the most beautiful beach village of South Goa, is one and a half kilometers from the hotel.

Hotel Description

Hotel Bollywood Sea Queen 3 * (South Goa) is a pretty nice and cute hotel complex located in a small area. It consists of a main and five additional buildings. All two-story rooms, made in the classical style, have a pretty attractive look.



The hotel was built in 1991. Large-scale reconstruction was carried out in 2002. But, according to the guests, the hotel often undergoes repairs and systematically updated rooms.

Territory

Hotel Bollywood Sea Queen 3 * owns a small territory. He greets guests with a pleasant and well-groomed view. Its advantage is a lot of greenery, in which all the buildings are buried. Especially a lot of palm trees around the hotel, which give the overall look a special charm.

Many guests note that the territory is very well looked after. All paths connecting the hotel houses are always clean. Along them grow beautifully trimmed ornamental shrubs. Lawns are also kept impeccable.



The hotel has only one pool. It is very carefully looked after, so the water in it is always in perfect condition. They clean the pool in the morning, for an hour and a half - from 7.30 to 9.00. Sunbeds and umbrellas are issued free of charge, there are no complaints about their shortage.

Description of hotel rooms

Hotel Bollywood Sea Queen 3 * (South Goa) has 55 rooms. You can live on the second floor of the main building or in additional buildings. Among the proposed options, you can choose standard or superior apartments. The 35 standard rooms have a double bed or two single beds. It offers air conditioning and a TV. There is also a telephone in the rooms, but you will have to pay for calls. Some standard rooms do not have a balcony. The bathroom has a shower and necessary hygiene products. Hot water is quite rare, so vacationers have to take a cold shower. A hairdryer is available only upon request.

Superior rooms have a fridge and bathtub. Some also have a small kitchenette where you can make tea or coffee. Pleased guests with housekeeping at the Bollywood Sea Queen 3 * hotel. Reviews of many tourists confirm that it is carried out daily and fairly high quality. Towels and linen are changed 2 times a week. Some people who have a rest here argue that at will and with a good tip, linen is changed daily. The condition of towels and bed linen is not satisfactory: everything is always clean and ironed.

Settlement Features

Very often, tourists who visited the hotel Bollywood Sea Queen 3 * (Goa), warn about the features of settlement. Entrance to the hotel, as well as check-out, is carried out at 12.00. In order not to languish for a long time near the reception, if the arrival did not coincide in time, you can go to the beach and eat in local bars.



Checking in the rooms has another feature that can be read about in the reviews. Often, booking a tour to the Bollywood Sea Queen 3 * hotel (South Goa), tourists choose the standard type of room, and after arriving at the reception they agree on an improved option. For around $ 100, hotel staff provides more spacious rooms with great sea views. If tourists do not intend to part with such an amount of money initially, then they will be given the keys to the apartments located in the most remote building. Often, this move works, because the view from the rooms does not open up to the best, and vacationers for a certain amount can move to more acceptable rooms of the same type or improved.

Infrastructure

At the Bollywood Sea Queen 3 * hotel, guests can contact the reception around the clock. There is a currency exchange and a safe for storing valuable property. You can use it for free. Also in the lobby of the hotel you can connect to the Internet, but often its quality is not impressive.

The hotel has a swimming pool. Part of it is adapted for young guests. There is also a stall where you can buy many different souvenirs, necessary items or even jewelry. According to the guests, the prices in this stall do not differ from the cost of goods in the village of Colva, and if you bargain, then they are lower.

At the hotel, for an additional fee, you can use the laundry, parking, rent a motorcycle or car. For business people, a conference room for up to 70 people is provided.

If necessary, you can use the services of a doctor working at the hotel. For an additional fee, he will provide the necessary medical assistance or advise on issues arising regarding health status.

Power Features

Breakfast is served in the main restaurant of the Bollywood Sea Queen 3 * hotel. Reviews of tourists confirm that the quality of food here is normal. At the choice of the guest, they can cook an omelet, scones or oatmeal. Fruits are also available. Vacationers who choose tours with an all-inclusive food system respond well to lunch and dinner. Here you can order a variety of dishes from a fairly extensive menu.

The tourists respond most warmly to the local necks that are nearby the hotel and on the beach. The food there is varied and very tasty. However, it is worth considering that in some bars extra amounts may appear in the check, so you need to be careful when calculating.



Near the hotel there are many outlets where locals offer to buy various fruits. They sell very tasty pineapples, strawberries, etc. At the request of guests, the restaurant provides dishes on which you can cut and arrange fruit in the room.

Water is dispensed in plastic bottles in the restaurant. She can also stock up at the reception, but for this you need to come with your container.

Beach vacation

Hotel Bollywood Sea Queen 3 * is located on the second line from the sea, the distance to the beach is 150 m. You will have to cross the road along the way to the sea, so guests should be careful. The beach is very beautiful, with silver, almost white sand. Sun loungers and umbrellas can be rented at local necks. They also offer refreshments of very tasty and varied food.

The water is quite warm, but very often there are waves. For young people, this is not a big problem, but those who are older, or children, do not always manage to swim. The water is pretty clear. The beach is also constantly cleaned. Many guests note the presence of stray dogs here. But these animals are quite friendly and do not create problems.



There are always free places on the beach, so guests of the Bollywood Sea Queen 3 * (Colva) hotel can be conveniently accommodated here. Many tourists warn that it is not worth taking pictures with the locals, because they require money for this.

Sports and Entertainment

At the Bollywood Sea Queen 3 * hotel, guests can find many different attractions for themselves. Here they offer to play board games. Billiard fans will have to pay for the opportunity to enjoy the game. Basketball or volleyball can be played on the beach.

Water sports are all paid. There is a center on the beach that rents the necessary equipment. You can swim with a mask and flippers, but there is nothing special to admire. There are almost no fish in the water, and visibility is limited to 15 meters.

The hotel has an Ayurvedic center, where a variety of treatments can be ordered for an additional fee. Professional massage therapists work here. In addition, vacationers will be offered to take a steam bath in a local sauna, which will also cost a certain amount.

As noted by vacationers who arrived from the Bollywood Sea Queen 3 * hotel, the animation is practically absent here. Sometimes the hotel is visited by local groups and entertain guests with quite interesting performances. But it happens once or twice a week.

Conditions for children

The hotel Bollywood Sea Queen 3 * (Goa) when the settlement does not provide a cot. Kids up to 5 years old sleep with their parents. Children over the age of five are allocated a separate berth. The restaurant also lacks a children's menu and no high chairs.

Excursions

Tours, according to tourists, are organized by taxi drivers, always present near the hotel. They offer to visit the local beautiful waterfall. For such an excursion, it‚Äôs not a bad idea to take a camera, because the local landscapes are simply amazing. Here you can even feed the monkeys, after purchasing a couple of bananas or nuts. You will also see plantations where spices are grown. Immediately tourists are offered to ride on elephants.

You can also book boat trips at the hotel. Several diving centers organize swimming, during which there is an opportunity to dive into the water under the watchful eye of an instructor and enjoy the beauty of marine life. Some tourists visit the river with live crocodiles.

Very often, vacationers are offered an excursion to the Apora night market, where you can find everything that your heart desires. But, according to tourists, the prices in this market do not differ from the cost of goods sold in the village of Colva, so paying for a tour is not very profitable.

Hotel staff

One of the advantages of the Bollywood Sea Queen 3 * hotel, the photo of which is located in this article, is a very friendly and friendly staff. Among the employees there are Russian speakers, and since tourists from Russia often relax in this hotel, this greatly simplifies communication. You can discuss any emerging issues with them.



Hotel reviews

Tourists who visited the Bollywood Sea Queen 3 * hotel, leave very different reviews. In the overwhelming majority, they argue that the level of service is consistent with the type of institution. Well, those vacationers who expect any excesses from a three-star hotel will definitely be disappointed.

Many guests complain about the presence of mosquitoes and midges in the rooms. At the reception upon check-in, they give out special plates and sprays, but even they do not save from insects.

Tourists respond very well about cleaners who try to do their job as quickly and efficiently as possible. Also, positive feedback is left about the staff of the main restaurant, where the wishes of the guests are always listened to. It is worth noting that in this hotel, vacationers are not surrounded by the minute attention inherent in European hotels, but guests will be provided with everything necessary for a comfortable stay.

In conclusion, we can conclude that the Bollywood Sea Queen 3 * hotel offers normal conditions for living. Tourists who visited it were satisfied and very often promise to visit it again.
