About the village

The name of the village comes from the Adyghe phrase "gold placers." Some people claim that you can still find grains of gold on the beaches of Jamete. But the real advantage of this place is its unique, healing air and well-maintained beaches. Not all readers know that this place is located near Anapa. It can be reached by regular bus or minibus.
