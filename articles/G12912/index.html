Hotel Nives 3 * (Rimini): photos, prices and reviews of tourists

If you plan to spend an excellent vacation in the Italian resort of Rimini and want to choose an economical hotel for your stay, then the three-star Nives will be a good option. About what awaits guests here, and we will talk further.



Location

Hotel Nives 3 * (Rimini, Italy) confidently boasts a magnificent location. So, it is located just a hundred meters from the magnificent sandy beach in an area called Miramare. Federico Fellini International Airport can be reached in just a few minutes, which is an indisputable advantage when traveling with children. There is also a train station 700 meters from the hotel . From here you can go on a trip to a number of cities in Italy and neighboring countries.

Nives 3 * (Rimini): description, photo

Nives is a small three-star hotel of economy class. It is perfect for those who want to spend a great vacation in one of the most popular sea resorts in Italy and at the same time not to spend much on accommodation. The rooms are comfortable and equipped with everything you need, and the staff is very welcoming and friendly and always ready to help in any situation.



Housing stock

Hotel Nives 3 * (Rimini) has 27 double, triple and quadruple rooms. So here you can comfortably accommodate couples in love, as well as families with children or groups of friends. All rooms are decorated in soothing colors. There is the necessary furniture and new plumbing. All apartments have a balcony. The rooms have a TV, refrigerator. Air conditioning is available at an additional cost. Cleaning is done every day. Sheets are changed once a week.



Infrastructure and entertainment

Hotel Nives 3 * (Rimini) has a 24-hour reception desk. Administrators will always be happy to assist guests in resolving any issue. You can also leave valuables in a safe and use a left-luggage office. The hotel has a restaurant and bar where you can have breakfast and dinner, and enjoy a drink.

Guests can relax on the terrace and play ping pong or billiards.

The beach, as already mentioned, is literally within walking distance. It is well equipped and is sandy. Since the zone is in municipal ownership, you will have to pay for the use of sun loungers and umbrellas (about 15 euros per day). The sea, according to tourists, is very clean, with a gentle entry into the water. On the beach there are playgrounds, changing cabins, a lot of cafes, as well as the opportunity to rent equipment for water activities.

As for additional entertainment, it is convenient to travel from Rimini both in Italy and to neighboring countries, because there is both a railway station and an airport. You can also go on a trip with a rental car.

Prices

The cost in the hotel in question is quite consistent with its category. So, a seven-day accommodation in July in a double room will cost from 28 thousand rubles, in a triple - from 37 thousand, and in a quadruple - 46 thousand.





Nives 3 * (Rimini): reviews of tourists from Russia

Since many people when planning a trip are interested in the opinions of other travelers who have already visited this or that resort, we will give generalized comments from our compatriots regarding their stay in the hotel in question.

According to tourists, "Nives" is a very good option for living while relaxing in Rimini. Here guests will find the best value for money. The biggest plus tourists considered the location of the hotel. So, despite the fact that it is only a 10-minute drive from the airport, aircraft are not heard here. In addition, Nives 3 * (Rimini) is located not on the main street, but in the alley, therefore it is always quite quiet and calm here. Near the hotel there is a supermarket where you can buy a basic set of necessary products and goods. But, of course, the main advantage of the hotel is its proximity to the beach. The path to it takes just a couple of minutes. Tourists also mention that there is a stop near the hotel, from where you can get to all places of interest.

As for food, many travelers only ordered breakfast here. According to them, morning meals are not diverse enough, but no one went hungry. Some guests were also pleased with the fact that if they needed to leave for an excursion early in the morning, then the staff provided breakfast for them ahead of schedule. By the way, as for the staff of Nives 3 * (Rimini), then most of the tourists were satisfied with them. According to them, everyone here is very friendly and always happy to help. All the staff speaks English, and one of the administrators also speaks Russian. The guests did not consider cleaning the rooms to be particularly thorough, but there were no complaints.

As for the rooms, here the reviews of most vacationers also converge. So, our compatriots found the apartments quite acceptable for living. Accommodation conditions here are quite consistent with the level of the European "three rubles". So, the furniture is good, the plumbing is in working order. True, travelers warn that there is no hairdryer, and bath and toilet accessories are provided only upon arrival, afterwards they will have to be bought by ourselves. But let's not forget about the "stardom" of the institution.
