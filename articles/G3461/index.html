Prank from a legal point of view

In Russia, prankers are hooligans, the fight against which is being conducted by the police, guided by articles of the Criminal Code. An offense of such a plan amounts to an insulting harassment of citizens. This is called petty hooliganism. It is very difficult to collect evidence base in such a case, therefore, as a rule, it is not possible to punish prankers.

As for Europe, in this part of the world this is also recognized as a violation of the law, a form of terror. Violators of the order receive an imposing punishment, a fine or criminal prosecution. Therefore, there are few people who want to call for the purpose of drawing.
