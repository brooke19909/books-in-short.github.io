How to make flowers from a napkin (paper and viscose). Workshops for beginners

Crafts from improvised means - this is precisely the direction in the world of needlework today has become very relevant. From the simplest and most affordable materials (paper, fabric, wooden skewers, plastic containers), craftsmen and craftswomen learned to make the most beautiful products. Each house has paper and viscose napkins. This is a fertile material for creating original and beautiful crafts. This article presents two master classes that describe how to make flowers from a napkin. Read the information carefully and try to create such products yourself. Believe me, this activity is very interesting!



Master class "Flower from napkins"

With our own hands we will make a rose. For work, prepare the following items:

  • paper napkins of pink, white or yellow color;
  • scissors;
  • a compass or any round object;
  • stapler with staples;
  • felt-tip pens;
  • pencil.

Process description



How to make flowers from a napkin? To do this, remove 3-6 napkins from the pack and, without unrolling, put them in a stack on top of each other. The more paper material you use, the more magnificent the flower will turn out. On a top napkin, draw a circle with compasses or simply circle a cup or glass. Fasten all the paper blanks in the center with a stapler. Next, cut a circle, capturing all the tiers. With a pink, red or orange felt-tip pen, circle the part around the edge, highlighting the outline. And then everything is simple: slightly lift the tier of paper and squeeze it with your fingers towards the center (to the place where the stapler bracket is located). Thus, complete all the tiers of the paper product. The result is a flower crafts. Such roses can be used for the manufacture of topiary, panels, accessories for carnival costumes. Well, if you glue the stems from the tubes for cocktail or wooden skewers to them, then such flowers can stand in a vase and serve as a decorative decoration for your home.

How to make flowers from napkins (viscose)? Preparatory stage

For work, you need such materials:

  • viscose napkins;
  • green threads (yarn or floss);
  • glue or thermal gun;
  • green felt or colored paper;
  • White paper;
  • pencil;
  • thick wire or wooden stick;
  • bright buttons;
  • scissors.

Craftsmanship stage



On paper, draw a flower pattern in the shape of a camomile. Transfer it to a napkin and cut out two of these parts. A piece of wire (10-15 centimeters) or a skewer wrap with green threads. This blank will become a stalk.

How to make flowers from a napkin? Insert the tip of this part between the viscose flowers and glue them together. Glue a button or two different sizes on the middle of the chamomile, placing them on top of each other. From felt or double-sided green paper, cut out the leaves and attach them to the stalk. Such a flower can be put in a vase or make it the main detail of the crafts in the form of a topiary.

You still can not decide how to make flowers from a napkin? Take our advice: try both of the options above. In addition to the fact that you will receive great pleasure from the process itself, beautiful handmade products will remain in your home that will decorate the interior of your home.
