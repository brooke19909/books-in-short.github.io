Spain. Hotel "Salou Park" - a pearl on the seashore

Spain fascinates with its beautiful natural landscapes, mountain ranges, rocky shores and sandy beaches. The excellent climate, many attractions and the best hotels await tourists on vacation. One of the most prestigious and popular is the Salou Park Hotel. Spain will give an unforgettable experience, an excellent holiday and only positive emotions.

Location

It is rich in luxury resorts and chic tourist complexes in Spain. Hotel "Salou Park" is located in a quiet resort area, near the center of Salou. The airport is 110 kilometers away. A few tens of meters is the beach.



Hotel Description

Spain enjoys its tourists with good service, comfortable conditions and friendliness. The Salou Park Hotel is one of the updated complexes with a modern design and numerous services. This is a nine-story building with chic rooms, there are a total of 218. Most apartments have balconies with sea views. The building has rooms for the disabled. While staying at this hotel, you can use the telephone, safe, hairdryer, TV, and the rooms are equipped with air conditioning and heating. Attentive staff will take care of daily cleaning and changing towels, bed linen is changed twice a week.

Territory

Spain is famous for its exotic nature, olive groves and citrus orchards. Hotel Salou Park has a beautifully landscaped area with green spaces and fragrant flower beds. For those who want to soak up the sun by the pool, there is a sunbathing terrace, and next to the bar you can enjoy cool drinks or sit out with a cup of coffee. Also, guests can relax in the cozy TV room, use the services of bicycle and car rental. There are a currency exchange office, a parking lot, a conference room on the territory, there are doctor services, Internet. A gourmet restaurant with international cuisine invites all vacationers to visit themed evenings, enjoy a wonderful dinner.



For children

One of the best options for families with children is Spain. Hotel "Salou Park" provides all the conditions to make little tourists comfortable. An excellent children's pool will allow you to play and swim safely, and there is also a playground for games on site. And in the mini-club the kids will be entertained with animation programs. There is even a children's disco. The hotel restaurant is ready to offer a varied menu for its young customers.



Services and entertainment

Free services include daytime animation programs, evening entertainment shows, as well as classes in water aerobics, archery, table tennis. Paid services include billiards, internet access, slot machines, car and bicycle rental.
