Black Lake (Zelenograd): we plan a summer vacation

Black Lake (Zelenograd) is one of the few places in Moscow that is suitable for swimming. This unique reservoir is located in the 6th microdistrict of Moscow - Zelenograd. The coastline of the lake is surrounded by forest stands. This gives the local area special comfort. Vacationers feel comfortable in the bosom of pristine nature.

Although Zelenograd belongs to the administrative unit of Moscow, you can only get to it by driving through the entire Moscow region, outside the ring road. To get to Black Lake, you need to retire from Moscow 37 km to the north-west.

Black Lake has an artificial origin. Earlier, in the first half of the twentieth century, there was a peat quarry in these places. And the surrounding areas were swampy. Now here is the beautiful Black Lake (Zelenograd). Photos of this reservoir reveal all the charm of picturesque landscapes.

The reservoir has a peat bottom, because the water is saturated with peat decomposition. It is because of this that it has a dark color. Features

Many are afraid that Black Lake (Zelenograd) has peat deposits at the bottom. However, despite this, it is recognized as favorable for swimming. This is evidenced by the numerous studies of waters that have been carried out in recent years. The water in the lake is clean, there are also a lot of fish here.

The beach is sandy, but its territory is small. Sand was specially introduced to places intended for swimming. The main reason for this decision was that initially the entire coastline consisted of viscous silt. Even nowadays, you can find places that are more like a swamp. Dresin is not there, but it is not recommended to independently move away from the lake. In the forest, special routes are indicated - pedestrian paths.



What awaits vacationers on the lake?

The beach on the Black Lake is equipped according to all the rules. There are playgrounds for children and sports grounds, changing rooms, picnic areas. It is also very pleasant that the pond is surrounded by a beautiful picturesque forest. The local area is a great place for a family day out.

When planning your vacation, you need to remember that in these places the infrastructure is poorly developed. There is no cafe, no entertainment, no other benefits of civilization, but this is what Muscovites attract here. Black Lake (Zelenograd) will allow vacationers to forget about the bustle of the city for a long time and fully enjoy the virgin nature.

It is crowded at any time of the year. In summer, people go here to swim and hide from the heat. In autumn and spring there are many vacationers having picnics. But in winter they come here in order to plunge into the ice hole.

Rescuers are constantly on duty at the lake. Entrance to the territory of the Black Lake is free. Because of this, there are always many visitors, especially on weekends and holidays.



Fishing

The lake is also a favorite place for lovers of quiet fishing. Here, in a small amount, crucians, pikes, perches and gobies (rotan) are found. Fishermen come here throughout the year, the winter fishing pond is especially popular.

Black Lake (Zelenograd): how to get there?

You can get to the Black Lake by metro to the Rechnoy Vokzal stop, and then go by bus No. 400 - the first stop after entering Zelenograd.
