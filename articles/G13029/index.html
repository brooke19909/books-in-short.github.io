Tutorials for beginners

According to Alfa-Direct reviews, training of future traders and investors is doing well. Before starting to trade, each beginner undergoes specialized basic training. In the training course, he learns the basic laws of the financial market, its rules of work, the relationship between bidders.

Stages of basic training:

  1. Studying the terminology of the financial market, without which it is impossible to do. Without knowing all the concepts and terms, the client simply will not understand what is at stake.
  2. Theory. In this section, a beginner studies the laws of the financial market and the interaction between its participants.
  3. The psychology of trading. All future traders should understand the mood of the market in a certain time period and the goals of other players, especially large participants, on which its movements of quotes and any changes in it depend.
  4. Money Management. Correct distribution of capital and financial management allow you to not only increase your funds and earn income, but also minimize losses and risks.
  5. Market forecasting. Each newcomer must learn to independently make an analytical forecast of market movement. This knowledge will allow him to understand in which direction the market is moving, what directions to expect in the future given time period.

Automated trading

Almost all traders trade in the financial market on their own. Recently, however, analysts who have studied statistical data have found that there are more frequent cases of speculators and investors using various advisers, robots and experts to open and close positions. Alfa-Direct customers in most cases leave feedback about trading robots only positive. They note the following benefits:

  • Earnings occur in a passive mode.
  • No need to control transactions.
  • Robots open and close positions independently, as they work according to special algorithms.
  • No need to spend time on an analytical forecast of market movement.

Of course, robots are a very convenient tool for making money in the financial market. However, there is another side to the coin.

The fact is that when choosing an automated program, several factors must be taken into account. The movement on the exchanges is uneven. Periodically, strong impulses form in the market, and then it goes into a calm state. These parameters must be taken into account. In most cases (according to customer reviews of Alfa-Direct), managers help to find the right robot.

Professionals advise newcomers to carefully analyze the market situation and select an automated program option in the terminal that matches the necessary criteria and indicators before choosing a trading adviser. For example, if strong movements occur on the exchange, then you need to use trending robots that work according to trading strategies based on appropriate algorithms. In a quiet market, you need to connect programs for flat movements.

And no doubt, if the client has the opportunity to use a universal robot that can independently determine the direction of the market, trend and impulse movements, their strength, duration, then it is advisable to use just such a program in trading.

Trading Platforms

The brokerage company offers for trading 2 types of trading platforms: Web-terminal and QUIK. From customer reviews of Alfa-Direct, you can understand that the second option is the most convenient, as it has significant advantages, has wider functionality and capabilities.

Customers note the convenience of QUIK for trading and investing. On this platform, you can open and close positions, trade using robots and other automated programs. You can also connect special signals here that will notify the trader about convenient moments for entering the market.

On the QUIK platform, it is very easy to conduct an analytical forecast of market movement, as there are all the necessary tools, technical indicators and even a stock glass showing the volumes of positions of all market participants.
