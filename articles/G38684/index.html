Club Scala Nuova Hotel 3 * (Turkey / Kusadasi): photos and reviews of tourists from Russia

Kusadasi is one of the most beautiful cities in Turkey. Holidaymakers are attracted here by the clear sea, spectacular natural landscapes and, of course, a well-established resort infrastructure. In any case, inexpensive housing in this town will not be difficult to find. Some Russian tour operators, for example, conclude contracts at this resort with the Club Scala Nuova Hotel 3 * hotel.

Hotel location

This three-star complex was built in Kusadasi in about 20 minutes. drive from its center. In the immediate vicinity of Club Scala Nuova Hotel 3 * there are such interesting sights as Ephesus, the city castle, the House of the Virgin.





In relation to the beach, according to tourists, this hotel is also located quite well. At least the residents of this hotel do not have to go too long to the sea . Club Scala Nuova Hotel 3 * also earned good reviews for its proximity to the infrastructure elements of Kusadasi. In just a couple of minutes, hotel guests can walk to large shopping centers and popular cafes of the city . There is a bus stop in the immediate vicinity of the hotel. From here, tourists can get to the center of Kusadasi from just 38 rubles.
