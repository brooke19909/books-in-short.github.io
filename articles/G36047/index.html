Where is the Jazz Loo Park Hotel? Reviews and photos

Holidays by the sea is always a joyful and long-awaited event. Sochi is one of the most popular resorts in the country. Tourists from all regions and even from abroad seek to come here. Where is?

Park Hotel "Jazz Loo" is located near the Sochi National Wildlife Refuge. The surrounding nature is amazing. You can walk for hours, admiring the natural beauty.





Park Hotel "Jazz Loo" is located in the Lazarevsky district of Greater Sochi, in the Krasnodar Territory. The complex is located a 10-minute walk from a well-maintained beach. Hotel address - st. Tallinn 46/48.

A shuttle service is provided from the airport to the hotel, but this service must be agreed upon in advance - at the time of booking the ticket.

Description of the complex

Park Hotel "Jazz Loo" - a modern two-and three-story cottages. The area around them is covered with lawn, which is so nice to walk barefoot. A small but very elegant fountain is built in the center. This is a favorite place for children to play, which often relax in the complex.

Many flowers have been planted on the territory, which delight visitors with their beauty throughout the season. Walking walkways were laid everywhere, benches with iron-forged backs were installed.





The relaxation area has covered gazebos. Here guests can have picnics. The favorite place for tourists is the swing-bench on chains. They are especially liked by couples in love.

Along the sidewalks are small streetlights. In the evening, the area is fully illuminated and offers walks.

Accommodations

Rest in the park-hotel "Jazz Loo" will be remembered for comfort and home-like warm atmosphere in the rooms. The complex features rooms with different levels of comfort:

  • Double "economy". These rooms are located on the ground floor of the building. They have two single beds or one double, wardrobe, TV. There is a refrigerator, air conditioning, hairdryer, waste baskets.
  • Standard Double Rooms with Garden View. Located on the upper floors. The rooms have two single or one double bed. There is a large balcony. The rooms are equipped with air conditioning, flat-screen TV, refrigerators, spacious dressing rooms, hair dryers.
  • This single room has a balcony. It has one 1.5-bed, all the necessary equipment and furniture.
  • Junior Suite This room has a spacious terrace with armchairs and a table. The room has a large double bed and a sofa bed. Also available: desk with chair, wardrobe, TV, air conditioning, fridge, hairdryer.

All rooms are equipped with showers and toilets.





The rooms also have heating and air conditioning. Mosquito nets are installed on the windows. Visitors with small children are provided with safe cots with sides.

Service at the Jazz Loo Park Hotel in Sochi

The rate includes a comprehensive breakfast. Guests can also enjoy free internet in cafes and rooms. Coolers with hot and cold water are installed on each floor, they can be used freely.

The reception staff has a first-aid kit equipped with everything necessary. Also, a medical worker is constantly on duty at the complex. Rooms are cleaned daily. Bed linen is changed every 4-5 days.

Park Hotel Jazz Loo, as mentioned above, offers transfer services from airports and railway stations of Adler, Sochi, Lazarevsky, Loo.

Near a forested mountain zone with terraincourses. There is a special area for yoga. The buildings are equipped with a small gym for outdoor enthusiasts and those who seek to make their body beautiful and slim.

Additional services

The Park Hotel "Jazz Loo" 3 * has an infrared sauna. It can be visited on any day of the week from 10.00 to 22.00. This service is available at an additional cost.

The complex also has its own photo studio. Everyone can order professional shooting here. A set of photographs consists of 10 photographs taken by an experienced specialist.

A cosmetology room is open on site. It carries out various procedures for an additional fee:

  • aromatherapy;
  • comprehensive facial skin care;
  • alginate masks;
  • massages of the cervical-collar zone, face, back and anti-cellulite.

The complex has a billiards room. This service is paid separately. The hotel staff also organizes excursions to "Mountain Eden" and "Paradise pleasure" on the river Loo. In order to see these attractions, you must pay on the spot.





To organize a picnic, guests can rent barbecue grills and skewers. Also at the reception is selling coal and ignition.

Park-hotel "Jazz Loo": reviews

Basically, all comments left on the Internet regarding the operation of the complex are positive. Guests are very pleased with the conditions of stay. The rooms are always clean and comfortable. Maids clean up on time.

In the bulk of the reviews, guests leave laudatory words about the beauty of the hotel. Tourists admire not only the work of the designer working on the landscape, but also the natural beauty of these places.

As for food, visitors note that the food in the restaurant is delicious. The menu here is varied.

Parents who rested with children are grateful to the animators. They say that entertainment programs for boys and girls are interesting and everything is thought out in them, taking into account the different ages of the guys.





Guests are also pleased with the cleanliness of the pool water and they like the fact that it is heated.

Some comments were made regarding regular power outages. The administration gave an answer to such reviews that these were problems not of the hotel, but of the village. But to correct the situation, the complex acquired a personal generator in case such situations arise in the future.
