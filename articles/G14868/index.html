How to make glasses for dolls quickly and beautifully

Recently, with the advent of the amazing Monster High dolls on sale, most children with such a toy have taken a great interest in the creative process of inventing all kinds of things for a full game with a new object of admiration. It concerns not only clothes for such dolls, but also everything that surrounds a person in everyday life, only in a miniature size. Some doll crafts seem at first glance very complex, so not every child can make them on their own. Among the variety of such accessories, tiny glasses occupy an important place.

Doll fashion

Every girl playing with dolls dreams that her plastic girlfriend has all the necessary things that she could brag to her friends. Undoubtedly, all objects of toy life should be in the latest fashion. Therefore, many children are interested in how to make glasses for dolls correctly. So that they look no worse than purchased accessories.

Of course, not all parents know how to make glasses for dolls fashionable and beautiful, so not everyone can help in this matter. Nevertheless, to create them requires very little time and material, which certainly is in every home.

Variety of choices

Before you begin to ponder the question of how to make glasses for dolls, you need to decide on their shape and color. Because the progress of the work and the materials used will depend on this. Glasses can be glamorous, sports, classic and have a lot of additional decorative elements. To make them look stylish and beautiful, you need to skillfully place accents on the product.





As a rule, girls want to have many such accessories for dolls that could fit every outfit of toys. Therefore, it is desirable to learn how to make these items quickly and efficiently. To make the creative process inspirational and easy, it is best to use the instructions with instructions on how to make glasses for dolls in stages at first.

Miracles from clay

Many people know that polymer clay can create original and beautiful things, but few people perceive it as a suitable material for creating a variety of doll glasses. Meanwhile, working with clay is the easiest and fastest among all available techniques for making fashionable toy accessories.





Let's consider in stages one of the ideas that tells about how to make glasses for Monster High dolls from clay. For this it is necessary to prepare the following materials: self-hardening polymer clay of any color, cellophane, varnish, transparent or dark plastic. Working process:

  1. Take the doll and put a piece of cellophane on her face, this is necessary in order to correctly determine the necessary dimensions of the craft quickly.
  2. Roll small pieces of clay into tubes and apply toys to your face, simulating the desired shape of the spectacle frame (without arches).
  3. Then remove the cellophane from the workpiece and lay on a hard surface. Immediately proceed to the formation of the frame by lightly pressing on clay, resulting in a flat figure. All curvatures must be cut with a knife or trimmed with your fingers.
  4. To start working on the temples, for this it is necessary to measure the distance from the edges of the frames of the glasses to the ears of the doll and mold them from the material used. If desired, pieces of thin wire can be placed in the temples.
  5. Next, you need to attach the arms to the frame so that they hold well on the toy. Leave to dry completely.
  6. To make glasses for future glasses, they need to be cut from a plastic bottle of a suitable color, and then gently glued to the frame.
  7. Open the finished product with varnish of a suitable color.
  8. To further decorate the craft, it can be decorated with various clay flowers.

When working with polymer clay, it is necessary to take into account the fact that it hardens very quickly, so all actions must be performed without delay.

Plastic beauty

Sometimes to create interesting and stylish things you can use the materials that are most often thrown away. These items include empty plastic containers of cotton candy or other products. Meanwhile, they can be an excellent blank for doll glasses. Consider how to make glasses for Monster High dolls self-made using recycled materials. To do this, you will need such materials: an empty transparent container, a plastic bottle, scotch tape, nail polish of any color, glue, manicure scissors, a pencil and a sheet of paper.





  1. On a sheet of paper, draw glasses of the desired shape and size (measure the width, height, nose bridge and length of the arches of the glasses on the doll).
  2. Cut the drawing with scissors and stick it on a thin tape.
  3. Next, stick the adhesive tape with the workpiece onto a small piece of a rectangular shape cut from a plastic container.
  4. Then you should cut such glasses, along with the holes for the glasses.
  5. After the work done, it is necessary to proceed to the next stage for the manufacture of glasses. They will not be cut separately, but glued to the back of the glasses frame. To do this, you need to take a piece of rectangular plastic from a dark bottle, attach a blank to it and scratch it with the edge of the scissors along the contour of the glasses. Cut the scratched shape.
  6. Now you should glue the two blanks together with glue, you need to do this carefully so that there are no glasses on the glasses. Before gluing two layers of arches, the lower one must be bent first, and then the outer part should be glued over it. Otherwise, the layers of the arches will diverge.
  7. The final stage will be painting the product with varnish.

In the process of the work, very beautiful glasses are obtained, which can be additionally decorated using several colors of varnish. Also sometimes interspersed with sequins in the frame of the crafts, which gives the product elegance and sophistication.

Openwork weaving

All products created from copper wire look unusually beautiful and stylish. And how to make glasses for Monster High dolls with your own hands, made in this technique, we will consider below:





  1. From pieces of thin copper wire to make a frame for glasses of the desired shape, trying on it to the face of the toy.
  2. When the frame is ready, it should be wrapped in the same material in a spiral.
  3. Then the basis of the crafts with a spiral must be laid on a flat surface and gently pressed, tapping with a small hammer. It will openwork weaving, similar to lace.
  4. The next step will be the manufacture of arches, which must be performed in the same way as the frame of the glasses. Attach them in such a way that the place of fastening is not visible.
  5. The final action is the insertion of glass into the craft frame. You can make them from a plastic bottle in the manner described above.

To make metal glasses look more impressive, they can be complemented with jewelry made of copper wire, which can additionally bedecorated with beads or other small items.
