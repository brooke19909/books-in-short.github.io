Chrissi Amoudia Hotel & Bungalows 4 * ("Chrissy Amoudia 4 *"), Crete, Anissaras, Greece - photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

Chrissi Amoudis Hotel Bungalows 4 is located on the scenic Aegean coast. Here, travelers will find not only decent living conditions, but also excellent cuisine, national color, genuine hospitality, professional service and, of course, the atmosphere of ancient myths that surround the island. It is worth noting that today more and more Russian tourists are interested in additional information about this hotel.

Hotel location Chrissi Amoudia Hotel Bungalows 4





The hotel is located almost on the coast of the Aegean Sea, on the territory of the island of Crete, known among tourists in a village called Anissaras. Blooming gardens, a clear sea and a picturesque rocky coast - in the midst of this luxury is the Chrissi Amoudia Hotel Bungalows 4. Heraklion and the International Airport are located about 25 kilometers from the hotel complex. And quite nearby is the no less popular place among tourists - the city of Hersonissos. The city center can be reached in just 10 minutes.

Brief description of infrastructure and hotel territory





Chrissi Amoudia Hotel Bungalows 4 is located on a hillside surrounded by a lush garden, where it is so pleasant to relax in the shade of trees. The hotel consists of several two-story bungalows decorated in a traditional style. The rest of the territory is occupied by swimming pools, sports fields and other places for entertainment and recreation.

Description of hotel rooms

The hotel has 224 spacious rooms - these are standard rooms of different capacities. Each room has its own balcony (rooms on the second floor) or a spacious terrace, from which you can enjoy views of the garden or the sea shore.





Naturally, the rooms have all the necessary furniture. The air conditioning system will create the most comfortable temperature conditions in the room. There is also a TV connected to a satellite dish. In addition, there is a telephone and a radio. Valuables and documents can be stored in a safe.

The bathroom is also equipped with modern fixtures - here you can take a bath or shower. There is also a wash basin, a mirror, a set of towels and branded hygiene products.

Food for travelers

Naturally, delicious food is an essential attribute of a good rest. And many travelers are primarily interested in what kind of food scheme the Chrissi Amoudia Hotel Bungalows 4 offers.





Greece is a country that is famous all over the world for its exquisite and original cuisine. And here, on the island of Crete, you will have the opportunity to try the most famous national dishes. The hotel operates on an all-inclusive basis. Generous tables are served three times a day in the cozy main restaurant for residents - here you will find light snacks, fruits, fresh salads, as well as meat dishes, seafood and fish. Delicious desserts and ice cream will delight even the most demanding gourmet.

The hotel complex also has a real Greek tavern, decorated in a colorful, national style. It serves traditional Greek food and drinks. In addition, residents can visit the bar located in the lobby of the main building. It works mainly in the evening, and visitors can enjoy breathtaking sea views while sitting on the terrace. Another bar is located by the pool - here residents are also offered light snacks, national drinks and exotic cocktails.

Beach and water activities





A luxurious beach is located just 600 meters from Chrissi Amoudia Hotel Bungalows 4. It is easily accessible on foot. But for the convenience of residents, a tourist bus runs between the hotel and the beach.

Here you will find soft sand and clear sea with convenient descent. On the beach there are sun loungers with soft mattresses and umbrellas. But for their use you have to pay separately, however, the price here is small. Of course, in addition to swimming and sunbathing for travelers, there are other, no less interesting activities. For example, you can ride a boat, take a boat trip along the coast, get a dose of adrenaline, go water-skiing, or enjoy the beauty of the underwater world by visiting the local diving center. In addition, there are volleyball courts on the beach, where you can also have fun.

Conditions for children





Of course, many parents are interested in questions about what conditions for a family vacation the Chrissi Amoudia Hotel Bungalows can offer 4. Beaches, islands - relaxing with children will be a real adventure, because even the smallest guests will have something to do.

To begin with, it is worth noting that, if necessary, a children's or folding bed (for an older child) is sure to be delivered to the room. The rich menu of the restaurant allows you to choose delicious dishes for children, and employees will bring you a baby seat upon request.

In addition, the hotel has a beautiful outdoor children's pool - here your children can have fun in complete safety under the supervision of lifeguards. Children can also spend time in the kids club - professional educators work with them to help organize fun and educational activities at the same time. There is also a playground with slides and swings.

Additional services for hotel guests

Of course, in the hotel complex they tried to create ideal conditions for a carefree vacation. For example, for a small fee, you can take your clothes to the laundry or dry cleaning. For those who want to travel around the island, a car rental point works independently - by the way, transport can be left in the hotel parking.

There is also wireless internet access in public areas. The hotel has a currency exchange. If necessary, you can put things in storage in the administrator safe. The hotel has several shops where you can buy essentials and interesting souvenirs. A very large supermarket is located nearby.

Sports and entertainment for travelers





If you are looking for entertainment and vivid impressions, then this hotel will appeal to you. To begin with, it is worth saying that on its territory there is a large pool, where you can have a great time. Sunbeds, umbrellas and towels are provided free of charge.

Of course, there are entertainment for people who prefer active sports. For example, you can have fun visiting the water slides. There is also a gym. Aerobics classes are regularly held at the hotel, and everyone can take dance lessons. There are spacious sports fields for volleyball, indoor soccer and basketball. Tennis fans will appreciate the local court - rackets and other accessories can be rented here for a nominal fee. The hotel also has table tennis tables and a TV lounge. And in the evenings, the guests are entertained by a team of talented animators who organize funny contests, performances and performances in the national Greek style.

Naturally, this is not all that the Chrissi Amoudia Hotel Bungalows 4 has to offer. Crete is an island full of mysteries and ancient legends. And by visiting the local tour desk, you will have the opportunity to see the most interesting places on the island, including the labyrinth of the Minotaur, the alpine lake, the ruins of ancient cities and temples of the ancient gods, as well as numerous museums. Moreover, in the center of the resort town you will find a lot of pleasant entertainment, shops, noisy markets and, of course, nightclubs.

By the way, if desired, the hotel staff will gladly organize a romantic dinner for you, a celebration of some significant date or even a wedding.

Crete is a great place for a summer vacation. And today, more and more Russian tourists choose the Chrissi Amoudia Hotel Bungalows 4 as their temporary refuge. This hotel is suitable for family, youth, business and, of course, romantic getaways.
