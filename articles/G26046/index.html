Speakers for the phone: how to connect

Those who want to decorate their outdoor recreation or a bike ride with music from their mobile device, probably dream of purchasing a portable speaker for these purposes. Such a column is an external speaker for the phone, which is convenient to carry. It is suitable for playing acoustics from a phone or memory card.

In this article, we will consider how to choose portable speakers, characteristics and types of models, as well as how to connect a speaker to the phone?

Difficulty choosing

When choosing a portable speaker for your phone, you should pay attention to the following characteristics:

  • Manufacturer - first of all, this parameter affects the quality of the column. It is recommended to purchase acoustics from companies that produce only such products. This is guaranteed to give you quality, since such a manufacturer takes care of the brand image and carefully checks the quality.
  • Type of connection. By connection type, the speakers are: wireless - connected via Bluetooth and presenters - are connected via cable to the phone in the headphone jack.
  • Support for memory cards - a portable speaker with support for a USB flash drive or memory card is convenient to use, because the constantly turned on Bluetooth on the phone quickly discharges its battery.
  • The power of the speaker for the phone - this indicator affects how the speakers of the device behave at maximum volume . For example, a speaker with a power above 4 watts will provide high-quality sound at any volume level.
  • Duration of work without recharging and type of power - for the most part, such portable devices have a built-in battery that can be charged via a USB port from a computer or by special charging.

How to connect speakers through the phone?

Wireless speaker connection is easiest to do if both your smartphone and it are equipped with an NFC chip. In this case, just attach the phone to the column, and then the devices are automatically synchronized. You only need to confirm this action by clicking ‚ÄúOK‚Äù.

If NFC is absent in one of the devices, then you will have to make a manual connection. This is done as follows:

  • Turn on the column.
  • Go to your notification bar on your smartphone.
  • Hold down the Bluetooth button.
  • Click on the ‚ÄúSearch‚Äù button.
  • The smartphone will display all the Bluetooth devices that are turned on and currently nearby.
  • Click on the one that matches your bluetooth speaker model for your phone.
  • The system starts the synchronization process.
  • You may be required to enter a digital code, which is located at the bottom of the column. Or you will need to perform some other actions with the device - for example, hold the power button for three seconds.
  • Connection to the column will continue to be carried out automatically, as soon as you turn it on.
