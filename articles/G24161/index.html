Children's sanatorium of Anapa "Globus"

Anapa is a children's resort famous throughout the former Soviet Union. There are many sanatoriums, children's camps, boarding houses, etc. Among all this diversity, the Anapa Globus sanatorium camp stands out. It is located in the first coastal zone of a small resort village called Vityazevo, almost on the Black Sea. All the necessary conditions for a quality rest for children are created here, the territory is landscaped and landscaped.

The territory of the sanatorium and the beach





On the territory of the sanatorium there are two- and three-story comfortable cottages, a medical building, a dining room, an art house, a library, rooms for school and study classes, a sports and game complex, squad gazebos, an indoor playground for general events, a football field, a basketball and volleyball courts , children's cafe, pump room, drinking fountains, dance floor, tennis tables are installed. Two hundred meters from the sanatorium is a sandy beach, guarded and equipped, which has a medical and rescue center, as well as information stands. Sanatorium Anapa "Globe" accommodates children as follows: two to four people in a room, in new buildings. The unit has a toilet, shower, round the clock hot and cold water.

Treatment, medical services and nutrition





The sanatorium treats children according to the following profile: diseases of the musculoskeletal system, respiratory system, nervous system, immunology, endocrinology, pulmonology, allergology, otolaryngology, ophthalmology. Sanatorium Anapa "Globus" provides the following medical services: physiotherapy, massage, therapeutic inhalation, aromatherapy, balanced diet, hydromassage, therapeutic baths, herbal teas, oxygen cocktail. There is a pump room with mineral waters "Anapskaya" and "Semigorskaya". If desired and necessary, it is possible to get advice from a physiotherapist, ophthalmologist, dermatologist, psychotherapist and allergist. They feed the children very well. They are provided with five meals a day, complex meals - breakfast, lunch and dinner, as well as an afternoon snack and a "dream book". The menu is fully balanced, it includes fruits, vegetables, a variety of dishes, dairy products, pastries, healing mineral water, natural juices, the so-called "green table".

Leisure and excursions in the sanatorium

The pedagogical team working here passed a competitive selection. The recreation and entertainment program has a local history and tourist focus and is built taking into account the age of each child. Hiking, sporting events, concerts and discos are regularly held. Children attend a circle of choreography, arts and crafts, courses for young tourists. Sanatorium Anapa "Globe" organizes excursions to various interesting places.
