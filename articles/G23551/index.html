Later years

Until the seventies Orlov worked as an editor at the publishing house "Soviet Writer", he studied the work of poets of the Silver Age, wrote works on the Blok, and until the last days he was engaged in scientific activities.

In 1956-1970, the chief editor of the "Library of the poet" (publishing house "Soviet writer").

Died V.N. Orlov in March 1985. His grave is located in St. Petersburg at the Volkovsky cemetery.

Awards of Vladimir Nikolaevich Orlov

Orlov has been repeatedly noted as a talented literary critic and scientist.

In 1951, he was awarded the prestigious Stalin Prize of the third degree. He received it for the book "Russian Enlighteners of 1790-1800."

The writer has medals received in the Great Patriotic War. These include the medal "For the Defense of Leningrad" and for valiant labor during the war years.
