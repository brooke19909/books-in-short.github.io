Pinnacle Grand Jomtien Resort SPA 4 * (Pattaya, Thailand): photos and reviews of tourists

Pinnacle Grand Jomtien Resort SPA 4 * is a paradise located in the southern part of Pattaya. Here you will feel complete unity with nature, which helps to improve well-being and mood.





Location

The resort Pinnacle Grand Jomtien Resort SPA 4 * is located on the Jomtien coast, 12 kilometers from central Pattaya. In the immediate vicinity are the following tourist sites:

  • Alangkarn Theater (1 km);
  • entertainment center "Mimosa" (2 km);
  • Ocean Marina Yacht Club (3 km);
  • floating market (3 km);
  • national stadium (5 km);
  • Anek Kuson Sala Museum (12 km);
  • ancient temple of Wat Yan (12 km).

Additional Information

Check out hotel policies by choosing a travel destination like Pattaya. Pinnacle Grand Jomtien Resort SPA operates in accordance with the following provisions:

  • check-in of guests starts after 14:00, and check-out is before noon;
  • to confirm the reservation, as well as during registration, a deposit by credit card is required;
  • Check-in is possible only after presenting a valid photo ID;
  • self-registration of guests is possible from the age of 18;
  • pets are strictly prohibited;
  • children under 12 years old are not allowed to visit the fitness center, and the use of the pool is possible only under the supervision of adults;
  • in order to use the massage services, a preliminary appointment is required;
  • if a holiday falls during your vacation, then a gala dinner is necessarily included in the price;
  • when paying for services in non-cash form, an additional fee will be charged from the guests;
  • late check out fee is 500 baht (subject to availability of a sufficient number of rooms);
  • placing an additional guest on a cot or sofa will cost you 660 baht;
  • the cost of breakfast for adults and children is, respectively, 300 baht and 150 baht;
  • newlyweds are given a compliment in the form of a romantic room decoration, fresh fruit and a bottle of champagne.

General impression

The most diverse opinions can be heard about the Pinnacle Grand Jomtien Resort SPA. The rating and reviews allow us to conclude that the impression mainly depends on the purpose of the trip. If you like noisy and vibrant entertainment, then you are definitely not here. Nevertheless, if you are tired of the hustle and bustle of the city, this hotel will be the perfect place to relax. The remoteness from the central part of the city will make your vacation calm and serene (however, get ready for overpriced prices for food and essentials). True, during the days of arrival of Chinese tourists, you will not find in a quiet corner.

The biggest advantage of the hotel is its luxurious garden. It occupies almost the entire territory. Spreading baobabs, tropical palm trees, flowering shrubs, walking paths, artificial ponds, benches - all this pleases the eye and promotes relaxation. But on the beach, unfortunately, not everything is so joyful. From afar, everything is very beautiful and civilized, but upon closer inspection, you will notice a huge amount of garbage that is just buried in the sand. And one more "fat" minus is a lot of strangers in the territory.

Despite all the shortcomings, a rather positive impression is formed about the rest in this hotel. The main reason is a qualified and friendly staff that quickly responds to requests and comments of guests, and is also ready to meet any situation. Of course, the cleaning is lame, but this is not so important, given all the positive aspects.
