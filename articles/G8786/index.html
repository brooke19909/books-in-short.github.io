Services "Tele2": how to disable?

Tele2 is a relatively young Russian telecom operator that stands out from the Big Three with its low-cost tariffs and many services. The operator was famous among fans for never imposing any services on them. Everything was as transparent as possible. Low price remained low. Additional services remained optional. Alas, nothing lasts forever, like the conscience of Russian operators, who love money very much and quietly connect expensive services to subscribers who are slowly draining their hard-earned money from their accounts. In this article, we consider what services the operator offers and how to get rid of them.





Services "Tele2"

Most of the services provided by the operator are almost no different from those offered by other operators. Among them can be identified, for example, automatic payments from a bank card. The Antispam system, which will block the imposed ads for you in SMS messages. Of course, there is the opportunity to pay for the change of the dial tone, where would you go without it (2017 is in the yard, and the service lives and is in demand). Basic things are also available, such as voicemail, message forwarding and tracking of incoming calls.

There are quite controversial things. For example, the service of ensuring anonymity during calls (numbers are hidden from other people), but at the same time there is an option that disables this anonymity, which makes the first function useless. Those who want to have fun can connect the function of changing the voice during a call. Of the useful things, only tracking the user's geolocation can be distinguished. This is convenient if you want to control the movement of the child, for example.





Media Services "Tele2"

In addition to standard communication services and classic services, the operator offers several applications for smartphones, opening up new opportunities for its subscribers. One such application is the Tele2 TV mobile service. This program allows users to watch the latest TV shows and movies without paying for Internet traffic. The second application is Tele2 Own. It was created to track stores and partner organizations that allow users to accumulate a certain cashback on the phone account. When making a purchase at a partner store, you get bonuses that you can then spend on paying for communications, SMS or the Internet. Tele2 is working with the developers of the Zvooq application. Subscribers of the operator do not pay for the traffic spent on listening to music (provided that they bought a Premium subscription).
