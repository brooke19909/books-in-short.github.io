Azura Deluxe Resort & Spa Hotel (Turkey): general description and photo

If you want to relax in a comfortable modern five-star hotel with magnificent views of the azure sea in Turkey, so beloved by domestic tourists, then Azura Deluxe Resort & Spa Hotel may be the suitable option for you.



Location

This hotel is located near the sea in the resort village of Avsallar. Alanya is 22 km away. The tourist capital of the country, Antalya, is a hundred kilometers away. The international airport closest to the hotel is also located here. So after arriving on the way to the hotel, it will take you about two hours.



General information, photo

Azura Deluxe Resort & Spa 5 * (Avsallar, Turkey) is a new modern hotel. It was opened in 2015. The hotel complex consists of a seven-story building. The territory of "Azura Deluxe Resort" is small, its area is 12 thousand square meters. However, there are several restaurants, pools, a spa and much more. The hotel also has its own sandy beach with a large pier.

The housing stock is represented by 330 comfortable rooms. All have a modern design and are equipped with everything necessary.

It is worth noting that this hotel, despite the recent opening, is already appreciated by a large number of tourists from different countries. So, his rating, based on guest ratings, can be called quite high. It is 4.6 points out of five maximum possible. By the way, the majority of holidaymakers here are represented by travelers from Germany. According to our compatriots, Azura Deluxe Resort & Spa is a great place for a family vacation with children, as well as for youth or people aged.



Rooms

As already mentioned, the housing stock of the hotel consists of 330 apartments located in a seven-story main building. The building is equipped with high-speed elevators for the convenience of guests. The hotel rooms are presented in the following categories: deluxe (28 sq. Meters, 2 + 1 people), family rooms (42 sq. Meters, two bedrooms with doors, for a maximum of 4 + 1 people); Superior (terrace, sea view, jacuzzi, area - 34 sq. meters, 2 + 1 people can be accommodated). Some apartments have sea views.

All rooms, according to the guests, are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay during the holidays. So, here there is comfortable new furniture, modern appliances (a large flat-screen TV with several Russian-language channels, a powerful air conditioner that is easy to set up as you like, an electronic safe, a hairdryer, a refrigerator with a minibar), a bathroom, a balcony. Also in all rooms and in the rest of the territory of Azura Deluxe Resort & Spa 5 * tourists can use free Internet. True, according to our compatriots having a rest here, not everyone in the apartment had a Wi-Fi signal of sufficient quality. However, there were no particular complaints about this from their side.



Service, cleaning

As for the staff, in general, the guests of the Azura Deluxe Resort & Spa were completely satisfied with it. So, for example, our compatriots mention that the cleaning of their rooms was of high quality and was carried out regularly. Also, the maids did not forget to change towels and bed linen and replenish the contents of the minibar and personal hygiene products as they were consumed.

The waiters in restaurants are very quick. Despite the fact that there are always a lot of visitors, tables are cleaned quickly and guests do not have to wait. Also, many of our compatriots in their reviews mention the Russian-speaking hotel manager for working with clients. According to them, he is almost always at the reception and is ready to answer any questions and help in solving any problem.
