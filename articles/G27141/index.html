How to get by car from Sergiev Posad to Kalyazin

The distance from Sergiev Posad to Kalyazin is small - a little more than 110 kilometers. It seems that you can overcome it at a good speed in an hour, but this is not so, despite the fact that the road surface on most of the way is very good.

The main problem of the route is traffic jams, especially on weekends in the warm season.

Road in Sergiev Posad

If you decide to take the road on Saturday or Sunday, the first traffic jams will be waiting for you in Sergiev Posad, first at the bend on the necessary Novouglichskoe highway, then at the intersection at the market and the Kapitoliy shopping center and the final one - through the large district railway located on leaving the city. Now, an automobile bridge is being built across the railway lines, which will go to the western detour under construction, so that in a couple of years the problem with congestion will be practically solved.

From Sergiev Posad to Iudino

Finally, having lost in the worst case an hour and a half, you will find yourself on the highway 46K-8400 (Sergiev Posad - Kalyazin - Rybinsk - Cherepovets). There are many villages along the road, so most often the maximum speed will not exceed 60 km / h. Acceleration is not recommended, pedestrian crossings in settlements are equipped with speed bumps, surveillance cameras are installed in some villages (for example, Deulino, Krasnaya Storozhka), traffic police often come across (they especially like to stand in Iudino).
