Excursions

Guests will always find something to do in Marmaris. You can go to the Old Town (it is located in the center of the resort) and visit a 16th-century castle there. Within the walls of this ancient building is now a local museum. He is worth a visit and attention. There you can find as many as five art galleries, as well as ethnographic and archaeological departments. Beautiful photographs from this museum are provided. Fans not to sit in one place can go to a nearby old town called Icmeler. It is located just five kilometers from Marmaris. You can also make a sea voyage to the island of Cleopatra. This interesting place is shrouded in many legends, and its amazing beaches of white sand will not leave anyone indifferent.

If you want to see the unique nature, of course, you need to go on an excursion to the city of Dalyan. Tourists who visit it recommend that you go there without fail, because only there you can see the rock tombs located directly in the steep cliffs.
