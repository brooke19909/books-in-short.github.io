Considering how to glue film on a phone

Cases of modern phones are most often made of plastic, less often - of metal, even less often other types of coatings are used. Screens protect with glass on more expensive models and plastic on cheaper ones. Phone manufacturers are trying to make phone coverage more durable and durable, but no one has been able to release a phone with an absolutely non-scratch coating, and it‚Äôs not profitable for sellers. And how do you want the phone to look new in a month and a year after purchase! And one of the ways to preserve its original appearance is to stick protective films on its body and screen. Next will be described the technology of how to glue the film on the phone.



The protective film on the screen is of two types - universal and specialized, for a specific phone model. It is best to give preference to a film of the second type, since it already has the desired shape, and you don‚Äôt have to cut anything yourself. If you nevertheless bought a universal film, before the sticker you need to attach it to the screen and cut off everything unnecessary with sharp scissors.

First, the phone screen must be cleaned of dust and dirt. This can be done with a microfiber cloth and a special liquid for cleaning displays, there should not be any questions, another thing - how to glue? The film on the phone is glued in two stages: first, remove the first protective layer, revealing the adhesive surface, and apply the film to the phone screen. At this stage, the main thing is to stick the coating evenly and expel all the bubbles, this can be done with a credit card. After you have glued the film as needed, you can remove the second protective layer. Now the film is fixed and it will protect the screen from scratches.





How to glue a film on the phone if you want to protect its case from damage? In this case, one of the best options is pasting it with a carbon film. How to glue carbon film on the phone? This question arises for many who are faced with a similar task for the first time. On the question of how to glue a film on a phone, articles on pasting a film with a car can help a lot, but there are some nuances here.

You will need a carbon film, as well as a stationery knife, hair dryer, solvent. Degrease the surface with a solvent, cut off the required piece of film with a margin on all sides. It is better to paste over the details of the phone separately, if its body is collapsible. For pasting the bends, heat the film with a hairdryer - this way it becomes elastic and it can be pulled. The film must be tucked under the part, otherwise it will come off. Also an important condition for pasting is the removal of air bubbles from under it, for this you can use the same plastic card. Be careful not to cover the microphone and speaker holes with foil when pasting the case, as well as the buttons and connectors of the phone.





Using this technology, you can easily stick a film on an iPhone and on any other phone model. The film coating will preserve the original appearance of your phone, and in case of scratches, the old film can be quickly removed without a trace to stick a new one.
