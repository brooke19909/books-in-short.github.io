Novorossiysk hotels: prices, photos, reviews. Cheap hotels of Novorossiysk

Novorossiysk is located on the Black Sea coast. Here is the largest seaport of Russia in this water area. This city is a huge transport hub, it is visited by hundreds of thousands of people every year, for accommodation of which there are guest houses and hotels in this place.



In this article we will consider the most popular hotels of Novorossiysk. In addition, we can get acquainted with the reviews of people who have already stayed in them.

Hotel "Ocean" is located in the southern region of the Central district of the city in the coastal 500-meter zone of the Black Sea. Nearby is the beach "Sujuk Spit". Near the hotel there is a park area with green spaces and two ponds.

Near the establishment there are bus stops - trolleybus, bus, about ten minibuses pass. Within 200 m there are two paid parking lots.
