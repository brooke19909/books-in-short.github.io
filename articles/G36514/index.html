Features of decorating the finished body with napkins

Most often, as a decorative material for decoration, ordinary paper napkins are used, which are used in the process of a feast. Such material is very cheap, it is easy to work with it, the finished product will turn out spectacular.

How to make the number 3 for a birthday from napkins in accordance with the simplest technology:

  1. Prepare the enclosure. It can be voluminous or flat - it all depends on the idea.
  2. The number of packs of napkins depends on the size of the workpiece. Additionally, it is worth preparing a stapler, scissors and a glue gun.
  3. Pull out a napkin, fold the sheet 1 or 2 more times. Cut a circle from the folded workpiece with a diameter corresponding to the size of the folded napkin.
  4. Cut a circle according to the sketch, previously securing it with a stapler.
  5. Gradually raise all layers of tissue to the bracket. Get half a paper pompom.

Next, figure 3 is formed from birthday wipes. You need to glue each element to the base with a glue gun. If you select several color options for napkins, then you can form a pattern on the surface.

What tools may be needed for decorative decoration

Light and airy birthday number

Another option for making numbers from napkins is also the easiest. You need to purchase 3-4 packs of large paper napkins, pick up the threads in color. How to make a 3-digit birthday number from large napkins:

  1. Fold the canvas in half and cut all the folds.
  2. Fold the pieces in a fan.
  3. In the middle, tie the napkins with a thread.
  4. Spread the edges, lifting them to the place of the ligament.
  5. Fluff the finished pompom.

Next, the elements are connected in such a way as to make a triple. You can cut the shape out of cardboard and stick or attach prepared pompons to it.
