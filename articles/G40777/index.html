Alcatel One Touch Idol X: specifications, reviews, photos. Comparison with competitors

A stylish smartphone with impressive technical specifications is the Alcatel One Touch Idol X. Initially (in 2013) it was positioned as a mid-range solution. But now, 2 years after the start of sales, this device can be safely attributed to the budget class of devices. It is his capabilities and technical parameters that will be described in detail and considered in the proposed material.





Equipment

Typical equipment for this smart phone (as for the device of this manufacturer). There is nothing unusual in it. It includes such accessories and components:

  • The smartphone itself with a 2000 mAh battery.
  • Charger.
  • Entry-level wired stereo headset.
  • Interface cord.
  • Brief operating manual (it also contains the warranty card at the end, on which the seller must put his seal and fill it out completely when buying a device).

The previous list clearly lacks a case. The case of the device is almost completely made of plastic, it is not difficult to damage it, and it will be quite problematic for the owner of this gadget to do without this accessory. Although the front panel of the smartphone is protected by shock-resistant Dragontrail glass, it is recommended to attach a special protective film to the front panel of the device to prevent any kind of damage to it. It is also not included in the package, and this accessory will have to be purchased at an additional cost. Also, owners of a single-sim version of this phone will have to purchase an external flash drive. The phone has only 8 GB of internal memory, and this is clearly not enough for comfortable work today.

Most of the front panel of the gadget is a touch screen. Its diagonal is equal to impressive even by today's standards - 5 inches. The width of the frame on the left and right is only 2.4 mm. Above the screen is the front camera window, there is a speaker and several sensors. A touch-sensitive control panel with backlight is displayed under the display. It consists of three standard buttons. On the underside of the smartphone are harmoniously located: a hole in the earpiece microphone, a USB port socket. The buttons that provide volume control are displayed on the right. If Alcatel One Touch Idol X does not turn on after briefly pressing the lock button (located on the top of the smartphone), then you need to hold it down for 5-15 seconds and wait for the device to turn on. Here, on the upper edge of the device, there is an audio port (3.5 mm). With its help, an audio signal is output to a wired speaker system. It can be either speakers or headphones with a microphone. Device video card

The PowerVR SGX544MP is used as a graphics accelerator in this device. At the time of the start of sales, this video accelerator made it possible to solve all problems without exception. But now more demanding games of the last generation have already appeared, which simply will be launched on it. But at the same time, this graphic solution still allows you to solve most everyday tasks. This is browsing the Internet, and reading books, and playing videos (including movies), and even mid-level toys - all this is possible for Alcatel One Touch Idol X with such a graphics card.





Display

The Alcatel One Touch Idol X is equipped with a 5-inch display. Its resolution is equal to today's impressive standards - 1920 x 1080. It is based on a matrix made using the IPS technology (hence the impeccable color rendering). Well, with the help of OGS technology, the picture on the screen does not distort even at angles that are very close to 180 degrees. Even now, after two years, not every mid-level gadget, not to mention entry-level devices, boasts a display with similar characteristics. In addition to everything else, the screen in this device is protected by Dragontrail glass (an analog of Gorilla Glass), on the surface of which an oleophobic coating is applied (with its help fingerprints do not remain on the front panel of the phone).

Cameras

The Alcatel One Touch Idol X main camera also has impressive parameters. The photos taken with it are of really high quality. She has a 13 megapixel sensor, which is also produced by Sony, a leading manufacturer of "sensitive" elements for phones and cameras. A similar sensor is used in the Samsung Galaxy S4 and Sony Xperia Z. The developers did not forget to equip this gadget with an autofocus system and LED backlight. This camera can record video in 1080p format (with a refresh rate of 30 frames per second). A more modest sensor in the front camera is only 2 megapixels. But this will be quite enough for a selfie or making video calls using the same Skype, for example.





Memory

Now we disassemble the Alcatel One Touch Idol X from the perspective of the memory subsystem. The amount of RAM in different versions of this gadget is always the same and equal to 2 GB. At the same time, system processes occupy about 700 MB. The remaining 1300 MB, the owner of the smartphone can use to run application software. The capacity of the built-in drive in a 2-SIM modification of such an apparatus is 16 GB. There is no slot for installing an external drive. But the single-sim version of this device is equipped with only 8 GB of disk space, but in this case, instead of the slot for the second SIM card, there is a slot for installing a 32 GB flash card. If for some reason the indicated capacity of the integrated drive is not enough, then you can use the "cloud" information storage services, for example, Yandex-drive.

Smartphone autonomy

A fairly modest battery, as by today's standards, is equipped with Alcatel One Touch Idol X. Its characteristics are really not impressive: 2000 mAh based on lithium-ion manufacturing technology. According to the manufacturer, the specified capacity in standby mode should be enough for 320 hours in second-generation cellular networks and 240 hours in 3G mode. In reality, with such a battery and similar technical characteristics, you can calculate in maximum saving mode for 2 days of battery life. If you use this device to the maximum, you will have to charge the battery every night. However, there is no guarantee that he will be able to fine-tune it until the evening. As a result, it is mandatory to additionally purchase an external battery, which can be used with a low charge of the main battery. In this case, your phone will definitely not let you down. But in this case you will have to carry an external battery with you and also control its charge level.





Communications

The communication set of this smartphone includes the following interfaces:

  • Wi-Fi is the primary way to share information with the World Wide Web.
  • Mobile networks of GSM and 3G standards. They allow you to make calls and exchange SMS. Also, with their help, you can organize an Internet connection. In the latter case, it will even be possible to make video calls.
  • The GPS sensor allows you to locate the device with a high degree of accuracy. Also, A-GPS technology can sometimes be used for these purposes.
  • Bluetooth is perfect for those cases when you need to use a wireless headset or exchange data with a similar mobile device.
  • A wired speaker system can be connected to a smartphone using an audio port (3.5 mm).
  • Micro-USB allows you to charge the battery or synchronize the device with a PC.

Software

Smartphone Alcatel One Touch Idol X, as expected, operates under the control of the most popular operating system for various mobile devices - "Android". Its version is 4.2. Of course, this is far from the latest system software to date, but its availability is quite enough to run most applications. The only thing that will not work on this 32-bit version of the operating system is the latest 64-bit applications of the latest generation. On top of the operating system, the proprietary shell of this manufacturer of mobile gadgets is installed. Its interface is quite similar to the similar MIUI shell from "Xiomi". In addition to standard applications (calendar, calculator, organizer "and so on), the installed software includes clients of international social networks (for example, Facebook) and a set of mini-utilities from Google (Gmail +, Chrome, and so on) . Reviews

Alcatel One Touch Idol X has practically no drawbacks. To summarize

At the time of its release, the Alcatel One Touch Idol X was a really attractive purchase. It combined excellent technical parameters and a reasonable price policy. But now for the same money you can buy gadgets with the best options. As a result, buying it at the moment is not entirely advisable. Well, those who managed to purchase it earlier can only use such a perfect device and enjoy it.
