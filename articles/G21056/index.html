How to make an individual TIN - step-by-step description, documents and recommendations

TIN is an important document that every taxpayer should have. Without it, you cannot become an entrepreneur or, for example, get a job. Today we have to figure out how to get a TIN. All the features and nuances of this process will be presented to our attention. What should every citizen of the Russian Federation know about the procedure?





Description

The first step is to understand what kind of document we are talking about. What is a TIN?

Individual taxpayer number - this is how the abbreviation mentioned earlier is decrypted. The document is a sheet of A4 format on which information about the citizen and his individual number in the tax system are written. It is assigned at birth and does not change throughout life. TIN helps tax authorities to quickly search the existing database. Also, a document is required when registering an IP or as a legal entity.

If a citizen does not have a certificate of registration of a taxpayer number, this does not mean that the taxpayer has no number. Quite often, people simply don‚Äôt know him. How can I get a TIN? What does that require?

Where to draw up

For example, find out which authorities deal with this issue. Where to get a TIN?

Today in Russia it is allowed to apply to the following authorities to receive this public service:

  • MFC;
  • district tax authorities;
  • portal of the Federal Tax Service of the Russian Federation.

A duplicate of a tax registration certificate can be issued through the State Services. In practice, most often the document is issued by the tax authorities at the place of registration of the applicant.





Procedure

How can I get a TIN in Russia? A certain algorithm of actions must be observed. There are not many steps; remembering them is easier than it seems.

The procedure for registration of the TIN of an individual as a whole is as follows:

  1. Prepare a specific list of documents. It will be offered to attention below.
  2. Write an application for a number. The finished form can be taken to the Federal Tax Service or make it yourself.
  3. Submit a written request for the assignment of a taxpayer number to a particular organization. Attach prepared documents to the application without fail.
  4. Wait until the paper is ready and pick it up at the Federal Tax Service or MFC.

Now it‚Äôs clear how to get a TIN through the appropriate organization. You have to act a little differently if you want to order a document via the Internet. But the process will still remain extremely simple and understandable.





Internet to help

Modern citizens can get a TIN via the Internet. This will help the site of the Federal Tax Service of the Russian Federation. Having left the electronic application, you can order a document and receive it at the MFC or at the regional tax service.

How to proceed when ordering an individual tax number via the Internet? This will require:

  1. Open the website of the tax service. Choose your region of residence in the upper right corner of the page.
  2. Go to the "Individuals" block.
  3. Select among the services that appeared ‚ÄúSubmission of application for tax registration‚Äù.
  4. Register in the "My Account". To do this, in the fields that appear, you must write an e-mail, a password for authorization, and a full name of a citizen. You also need to dial the code from the image.
  5. Confirm action.
  6. Fill out the application form that appears. It is relevant for those who do not have an electronic signature.
  7. Click on the button ‚ÄúRegister application and forward it to the Federal Tax Service‚Äù.

The application number will appear on the screen. After the TIN is ready, it will be possible to pick up the relevant document from the tax authority at the applicant‚Äôs registration.

Digital signature

But that is not all! You can apply for a TIN in another way for those who have an electronic digital signature. How to act in this case?





It is necessary:

  1. Download and install the application "Taxpayer YUL". It is important that the program is the latest version.
  2. Fill out the application form 2-2-Accounting. If you want to receive the TIN electronically, you must additionally fill out the 3-Accounting request.
  3. Form a transport container with digital signature. There is a separate button for this in the "Taxpayer YL".
  4. Send a request for processing and wait for its verification.

After the actions taken, the citizen will receive an electronic TIN. Is it possible to arrange the TIN differently? No, only in the ways listed. As a rule, ordering a TIN with a digital signature is almost never found in practice.

Documents for TIN (adult)

It should be noted that in order to receive the document under study, certain papers will have to be prepared. TIN is issued to both adults and children. Foreigners can also produce this document. In all situations, the list of attached papers will be different.

You can get an INN for an adult after providing:

  • a completed tax registration application;
  • passports / ID cards.

Nothing more is needed. What other documents can be useful to the population when ordering a TIN in a particular case?

For children

The next situation is ordering a document for children. This operation is not very different from the previously proposed. The procedure will be exactly the same as when ordering a TIN for adults. The difference is only in the documents provided.





How to get a TIN for a child? For this, the Federal Tax Service brings:

  • application (filled out by one of the parents);
  • passport of the applicant parent;
  • minor birth certificate;
  • certificate of registration.

After 14 years, children must provide a passport along with the listed papers. As a rule, at this point, adolescents have the right to independently obtain a TIN. Until the age of 14, the document must be taken away by the parents (legal representatives).

To foreigners

How to get a TIN for an individual who is a foreign citizen? The algorithm of actions is preserved, only the list of documents attached to the request is changed.

In the case of foreigners, you need to bring to the Federal Tax Service:

  • statement;
  • translation of a passport (with notarization);
  • migration card;
  • certificate of registration on the territory of the Russian Federation (registration).

Nothing difficult, special or incomprehensible. Minimum paperwork!

Production time

Production methods

Modern citizens can obtain a certificate of tax registration in several ways. And you can get this document in your hands in different ways.

More precisely, the TIN today is issued:

  • personally;
  • through a representative;
  • on the internet;
  • by mail.

In case of obtaining TIN through representatives, it is necessary to write a power of attorney for this operation. When applying, the authorized person presents not only the application and a copy of the applicant‚Äôs passport, but also his identification card with a power of attorney. Do I have to pay

In Russia, for the production of certain documents it is necessary to pay a state fee. How much does it cost to receive a TIN?

Its primary design is free. Each citizen can issue a TIN without paying a state duty.

For secondary registration (in case of damage or loss of the corresponding certificate), it is necessary to pay a state fee. Today it is 300 rubles. Only after that, the Federal Tax Service will accept an application for the issuance of a duplicate TIN. More precisely, a new document, but with the same taxpayer number.
