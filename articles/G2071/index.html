We go to "MEGA Khimki" on a company bus

Since the shopping center is located outside the city, it is sometimes very difficult to find MEGA Khimki on your own. Not everyone knows how to get to the shopping center, so not everyone you meet can answer your question. The easiest way to get to the mall is with a branded bus belonging to the supermarket. It departs from the metro stations Planernaya, Rechnoy Vokzal, and Mitino. It will not be difficult to find this bus, since it has a plate with the name of the final destination. Using this bus, you will reach directly to the shopping center. It should be noted that from Sunday to Thursday (inclusive), the bus makes flights from 9.00 to 23.00. As for Friday and Saturday, he goes to 00.30.

Regular bus

But a branded supermarket bus is not the only way to get to MEGA Khimki. How to get to this shopping center? You can take the metro to a certain station and transfer to a regular bus or fixed-route taxi that goes to this shopping center. If you reach the metro station "Planernaya", then at the exit you should turn left. Then go forward, without turning anywhere, to the traffic lights, and then turn left again. After 60-70 meters, there is a stop where you need to catch a bus following route No. 959. From this metro station to "MEGA Khimki" can be reached by taxi. To do this, when leaving the subway, turn right. The supermarket is followed by minibuses No. 946,154,980,982,981.



If you are at the Rechnoy Vokzal metro station and want to get to the MEGA Khimki, how to get from this place? Leaving the metro, you need to go straight to the bus stop and take bus number 958. In order to get to the fixed-route taxi, you must go straight to the bus stop and take the minibuses No. 532, 481, 986.

If you have your own transport, how to get there

MEGA Khimki has two entrances: from May 9 and from the Novokurkinskoye highway. If you turn off at 72 km of the Moscow Ring Road onto Molodezhnaya Street towards Kurkinskoye Shosse, then after crossing it you need to go to Novokurkinskoye Shosse. The complex will be located on the right. According to the second option, from the Moscow Ring Road it will be necessary to turn onto the Leningradskoye Shosse, cross the bridge and cross May 9 Street. On the right will be the entrance to the supermarket.
