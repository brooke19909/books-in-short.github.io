The center of green tourism - the Medveditsa River

More and more people are opting for green tourism. What could be more beautiful than to live in the outback, breathe in fresh air, enjoy the sunsets, enjoy fishing and, of course, enjoy ecologically clean homemade food! Rest in the bosom of nature is to the liking of both children and adults. One of the most beautiful places in Russia is the Medveditsa River (Tver Region), or the Medvedikha, as the locals call it. The picturesque banks of this river seem to be designed to relax from the bustle of the city and smog. Everyone can choose a vacation to their liking and a wallet: from camping tent to a comfortable hotel. What is so attracted to the Medveditsa River by domestic and even foreign tourists?



Where to live? We choose housing

Tents

On the banks of the river there are many "wild" tent sites. This way is risky enough, especially for those who are going on vacation with children. An alternative is to consider local campsites within which you can set up camp. Of course, this method is not free (unlike the wild one), but a health center, drinking water, and security are at the service of vacationers. After all, rest in the lap of nature should not only bring joy, but also be safe.





Private Housing

There are several villages on the banks of the river, the inhabitants of which are ready to receive vacationers throughout the holiday season. The price of a home depends on financial capabilities and preferences: you can rent an inexpensive room, or you can also have a comfortable house.

Cottage villages

The cost of a cottage is usually higher. But the package of services is different: parking for cars, television, Internet ... In the villages you can find shopping centers, restaurants with local and European cuisine, as well as many entertainment.

Things to do?

Fishing

One of the most famous and attractive features for tourists is fishing. The Medveditsa River is abundant in fish. However, both local residents and the responsible authorities strictly monitor compliance with applicable laws, keeping the fish population at the proper level and preventing poaching. Special places are reserved for fishing, the seasonality of fishing is strictly observed.



Hunting

The banks of the Bear River, covered with forests, are extremely attractive for hunting lovers. The first hunting season opens in April. It lasts only about a month, so many are in a hurry to visit these places in order to hunt for game birds. Autumn is the season of shooting small ungulate animals and moose. Hunting for duck, pheasant, quail, black grouse and other birds, which the Medveditsa River abounds in, is also allowed at this time. Photos with trophies will be wonderful souvenirs in memory of these places.





Horse rides

Bekas stud farm breeds Bashkir horses. With these quiet and intelligent animals, even children and beginners can easily find a common language. The package of services is quite wide: from riding lessons with a trainer to long walks in the forest.





Silent Hunt

Attract pine forests and mushroom pickers. Many vacationers with pleasure go with experienced mushroom pickers to the thicket. It offers tourists mushroom drying, helping to quickly process and harvest for future forest "harvest".
