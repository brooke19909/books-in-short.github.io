The Greek peninsula of Halkidiki, reviews of tourists about which are distributed around the world, annually invites guests. The convenient location of resorts and infrastructure allows everyone to go to Greece at any time of the year to enjoy a well-deserved vacation.

Where is?





A peninsula with a large number of hotels, beaches and other tourist infrastructure is located south of the famous city of Thessaloniki. The resort consists of three magnificent peninsulas: Sithonia, which has a beautiful landscape and view, Mount Athos, filled with sights, and Kassandra, popular due to the warm climate.

Every year, thousands of travelers from all over the world come to Greece to enjoy the local hospitality, cool winds and the scorching sun. Everyone can read, before going to Halkidiki, the reviews of tourists who independently visited the resorts and were satisfied.

The most common means of transport in the resort of Halkidiki, the reviews of tourists about which are mostly positive, are buses and taxis. In five-star hotels, tourists can use the free shuttle buses that travel between resort towns.

Climate

Halkidiki Peninsula (Greece), whose reviews on the climate of which are ambiguous, is the focus of travelers from May to October, when the holiday season lasts. The average air temperature on the peninsula in May in the afternoon is +24 degrees, in June it is +29 degrees, in July and August - +31, in September it drops to 27 degrees, and in October - to 21.

Cases were repeatedly recorded when in July and August the temperature rose to 35 degrees Celsius. The climate is dry, which is why tourists can easily tolerate the heat. A distinctive feature of the resort - the peninsula is protected by mountains from cold winds, thanks to this the sea behaves as calmly and perfectly warms up. Water temperature in summer ranges from 22 to 29 degrees.
