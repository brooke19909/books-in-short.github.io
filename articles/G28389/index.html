Pattaya hotels: ranking of the best, Michelin stars, quality of rest and vacationers tips

Everyone loves to relax. Rest helps us to relax and distract from everyday problems and fuss. Usually we spend weekends at home with family or friends. Less often we get out to the city for going to the cinema or restaurant. Fortunately, every year we get as many as 28 calendar days that you need to spend wisely. Many people like to spend their vacations outside the territory of their city, but here a good question arises - where can one go?

Usually we consider two areas: Europe and Asia. In the first case, you can enjoy the beauty of architecture, historical monuments, get acquainted with the cuisine and people. In the second case, we get a riot of colors and emotions. In Asia, it is never boring, this region is filled with unusual architecture, unusual food and incredible flavor. This is a different world, bright, memorable and interesting. Where can I go there?

How about Thailand, a state located in the southeast of the region. There you will see the best beaches, the most developed infrastructure and expensive hotels. Today we will talk about the city of Pattaya, which is located southeast of the capital - Bangkok. We have to get acquainted with the best hotels, read reviews and discuss their quality. So let's get started.

Flipper House Hotel is a leading hotel in Pattaya hotel rating reviews. People love affordable prices, delicious breakfasts and a great location.
