Meir Jarr 3 * (Thailand / Phuket): description and reviews of tourists

Phuket is just an amazing resort on the shores of the Andaman Sea! Perhaps he is the best in Southeast Asia. Unlike the democratic Pattaya , this resort is considered the most respectable in Thailand, but this does not mean that it is impossible to find budget hotels here. On the island there are many three-star hotels with quite acceptable service. These include, for example, Meir Jarr 3 * (Thailand / Phuket). Of course, they will not appeal to very demanding tourists, but for people who come to rest not for the sake of the hotel, but for the sake of entertainment and the sea, this is the most suitable option. So feel free to contact the travel agency and arrange a tour to Meir Jarr Hotel 3 *. Believe me , in Phuket you will have something to do, and do not have to spend all day in the room.



Description

As on any island, there are a lot of beaches, but not all of them are equally good. Closer to the boundaries of hotel complexes, the water is not always clear, on the beaches it is crowded, noisy, crowded, but in less crowded places tourists can find beaches with crystal clear water, with such an azure hue that you can‚Äôt understand where the sky ends and where the sea begins. The nature here is incredibly beautiful and pristine, you can completely break away from the bustle of the city and enjoy your vacation. Such beaches are located very close to Meir Jarr Hotel 3 *, and having rented a bike , you can take a ride along the coast in a matter of minutes and choose a part of the coast that you like for sunbathing and swimming in the sea. By the way, it is better to rent a moped at the bike rental point in the hotel, since here you will not be asked to leave a passport as a deposit (as street tenants do), you only have to leave a certain amount as a deposit. At the same time, in the immediate vicinity of the hotel there are entertainment centers, including those of an intimate nature, in no way inferior to those that can be found in Pattaya . This can often be found on Karon Beach . It is there that young people strive. But the beach " Kamala " is perfect for respectable tourists: here the entertainment is more solid, for example, you can witness some enchanting show or a jazz concert.



How to get to Phuket

Despite the fact that the history of the development of tourism on the island dates back only 50 - 55 years , nevertheless, almost from the very moment of the birth of this industry, the government of the country considered it necessary to build airports on the island that would serve as an air gate and receive tourists arriving from all over the world. Imagine only: precisely because access to the island has become more accessible, in just a couple of years this tourist destination has become considered one of the most popular and popular in Southeast Asia.

Air gate

The airport itself is located in the north of the island, and if you move further north from it, you can see how Phuket connects to the mainland through giant bridges - Thepkrasattri and Sarasin . The latter is a pedestrian and peculiar symbol of these places, and cars of different brands rush along the first, bringing here mainly local tourists. There is a ferry service between the island and the mainland resort of Krabi . Some, having arrived in Phuket, first stop at one of the local hotels, and then take the ferry to Krabi and spend the second part of their vacation there. In short, if you bought a ticket to Meir Jarr Hotel 3 * (Phuket), then you will first need to fly to Bangkok, and then transfer to a local airline, and in just a few minutes you will reach the island in a chic setting spend your unforgettable vacation. By the way, from Phuket airport to the Meir Jarr 3 * hotel can be reached by car in 40 - 45 minutes. The hotel itself provides tourists with a transfer for a fee, however, if you are traveling on vacation to Thailand on a package tour , the travel agency serving you will arrange the transportation from the airport to the hotel, and the price will be included in the price of the package .



Meir Jarr 3 *: description and location

This hotel is close to Patong Beach , just a ten minute walk. This means that Meir Jarr 3 * is located on the second beach line. In the immediate vicinity there are huge shopping centers where you can make a wonderful shopping and enjoy delicious food, fresh fresh fruits and exotic fruits in food courts . Nearby there is also a great variety of entertainment centers, nightclubs and even hot spots. A 10-minute drive from the Meir Jarr 3 * hotel is the Thai boxing stadium, and anyone who is not indifferent to martial arts must visit one of the matches. A fascinating sight guaranteed! The coolest feature of this hotel, which is considered its hallmark, is the rooftop pool, which offers incredible beauty of the sea landscape. Many tourists prefer to spend their holidays here, sitting comfortably on sun loungers and enjoying tasty drinks in the pool bar. Many tourists in their reviews of Meir Jarr 3 * say that they often relaxed and sunbathed by this pool, rather than on the beach, which had to go for about 10 minutes. However, there are tourists for whom real relaxation is possible only on the coast, in sea water.



Number of rooms

In total, the Meir Jarr Hotel 3 * (Phuket / Patong ) has 74 elegantly decorated rooms in the following categories: single or double standard without a balcony; Superior standard - also single and double, a little larger than simple rooms and with a balcony; Superior rooms - one-room and two-room, as well as Deluxe - the most comfortable rooms in this hotel.



Room Description and Service

All rooms in the hotel, regardless of category, are equipped with modern furniture, comfortable beds, wardrobes, tables, armchairs or chairs. Of course, all rooms are air-conditioned. Without this, there is no way in Thailand: because of the constant heat and suffocating humidity, you can simply suffocate, especially if the body is not used to such conditions. Some of the rooms, as already noted, have balconies or terraces, while others are missing. All rooms have a bathroom with shower, washbasin, towels, hairdryer and a minimum set of toiletries (soap, shampoo, toilet paper). The suites have a jacuzzi. Among the equipment of the rooms are plasma TVs with satellite channels, a telephone with direct international dialing, a safe for storing valuables, a minibar (also known as a refrigerator), an electric kettle. The beds are very comfortable, bed linens are changed often - every other day, towels if necessary - daily, rooms are also cleaned every day.

Services

At the entrance to the hotel, guests are invited to pack their belongings (if it's winter, then winter outerwear, which is definitely not needed here), pack in a suitcase and hand over to the luggage storage room. Tourists can also use the laundry and ironing services, or they can request an iron for ironing in the room. There is also a currency exchange point, although the rate is overvalued, therefore it is better to perform currency conversion operations outside the hotel. There is free parking for cars rented either at the hotel itself or at rental services . At Meir Jarr 3 * there is also a tour desk, contacting which you can sign up for one or more interesting routes and really (under the guidance of a guide) get acquainted with local attractions.



Nutrition

When applying for a tour package, you can choose one of several food options: breakfast only (BB), half board (HB) or full board (AL). However, they should warn that most tourists note that the food at the hotel, although fresh and quite tasty, is nevertheless monotonous, which , of course, is not quite to the liking of the guests. In addition, Thai cuisine is not suitable for everyone, although, you must admit , it is quite tasty and, most importantly , healthy. And yet, many of the guests take breakfast only, and for the rest of the meals they go outside the hotel, where they eat tasty and varied food. The hotel, including the rooftop pool, has several bars where you can enjoy refreshing as well as invigorating drinks. There is also a coffee shop on site, serving very good oriental coffee.

Entertainment Infrastructure

As noted above, the biggest entertainment for guests of the Meir Jarr 3 * hotel (see photo in the article) is a magnificent pool on the roof of the building with a wonderful panoramic view of the sea. This corner is the most favorite vacation spot for most tourists. Here, on comfortable sun loungers, they are ready to spend almost the whole day and even late evening. After all, in the pool bar you can enjoy very tasty drinks and snacks. The other most visited place in the hotel is the massage room. Massage is almost twice as expensive here as in other places in the city. But the service cannot be compared: everything is in perfect condition, very clean, so you can not be afraid to catch some kind of infection. There are no other attractions, such as a fitness club or spa.

Hotel compliments

For the three categories of tourists, the Meir Jarr 3 * administration has prepared pleasant surprises, which are usually called compliments. So, the newlyweds belong to the first category. Upon presentation of a marriage certificate for the last few months, newlyweds will be presented with a beautifully decorated room with pink petals and swans on the bed, as well as a fruit basket. The second category includes birthday people. They also have a surprise in the form of a shaman or a bottle of another wine. Well, the third category of guests worthy of a compliment are those guests who have stayed here more than once. When driving, they are also waiting for a bottle of wine.

Too much breakfast for them - not normal. But all vyingly praise the location of the Meir Jarr Hotel 3 * we are considering and the gorgeous pool located on its roof.
