The platypus is a soft toy. DIY patterns and sewing - it's just

This article is dedicated to all those who have small children in the family, or just lovers of stuffed animals. I think a soft toy will not leave anyone indifferent. With your own hands, making patterns is not so difficult. But how much joy and pleasant pastime this exciting activity will bring you. Any kid will be pleased with the handmade toy. Patterns for such an undertaking can easily be found in cutting and sewing magazines. I decided to share with you the information I found on how to sew a funny platypus. My choice fell on this particular animal, since it is non-standard for us, and it looks very nice. A great gift for any occasion - a soft toy. With our own hands we will make patterns according to the following instructions.

What do we need?

A cut of fleece (here it is pink) 40x32 cm in size, a velvet rectangle (in the photo is purple with patterns) 40x21 cm, plastic balls (filler) - about 40 grams, black beads for the eyes, strong thread, scissors, glue, a sewing machine.





How is this toy sewn with your own hands?

Patterns are chosen so that any beginner can understand them and easily cope with the task. You can sew the platypus both manually and on a sewing machine. The fabrics are very different, just the fleece is great for such products, as it is very pleasant to the touch and warm. Any child will like such a bright soft toy. With your own hands, cut out patterns and sew on them is a great pleasure.





Step-by-step instruction - do-it-yourself soft toy

We increase the pattern by about 50% so that a completely small platypus does not work out. If necessary, you can stick the pattern on thicker paper, for example, cardboard, then cut it out (it will come in handy for further sewing). Using pins, attach the pattern to the wrong side of the fabric. Cut the workpiece from the fabric. Then stitch the parts in order, leaving a gap of half a centimeter. Before sewing, it is better to sketch the seams or stab with pins so that the fleece does not move to the side, since you will have to sew in a non-straight line. Fill the legs and nose with filler, and then sew them. Do the same with the body of the platypus. Sew black beads in place of the eyes. In the end, you need to stitch all parts of the animal together. Here you are ready to make a soft toy with your own hands. Be sure to save the patterns. You can make several multi-colored platypuses or start sewing them on order, if everything works out very well for you. And this is a very profitable business, since a thing created by one's own hands is always valued much more than a factory one.





Build your collection

After sewing the platypus, you can safely proceed to other animals. It can be a blue dolphin or a red cat with a long mustache and a fluffy tail. This soft toy snake, sewn with your own hands, will become not only the favorite of your children, but also perfectly fit into any interior. You can significantly increase the size of the toy and use it as a designer pillow.
