Small openwork patterns with knitting needles: diagrams, descriptions, photographs of samples

Hand-crocheted at the peak of fashion today. Small openwork patterns made by knitting needles look very nice in them. Schemes, descriptions and step-by-step photographs of the process of their implementation will help novice needlewomen to create exclusive items for themselves and their loved ones.

How is the openwork?

The main elements of knitting are front and back loops, as well as crochets. Each knitter is familiar with them. And if in the second row the crochet is knitted as an independent loop, a small hole is obtained. This is where all the openwork patterns are based.

It is only important to observe the total number of loops in a row. Otherwise, the canvas will expand, which will violate the plans of the master. Therefore, in the row where the yarn is made, some loops are knitted in two or even three together.

Here, too, there are rules. For example, the slope of loops knitted together. In order to correctly perform the pattern, you need to pay attention in the description to the fact that a spoke is introduced into them on the right or left.

Beautifully located holes and the slope of the loops knitted together create the pattern itself. Here are the works made by knitting needles, small openwork patterns with samples, schemes and their descriptions. There are also workshops with step-by-step photographs. But each needlewoman is able to create her own pattern if she understands the openwork knitting algorithm.

It is difficult to understand the features of a small openwork pattern with knitting needles according to the pattern. Step-by-step photos of the process and detailed explanations will help the novice skilled worker.

In even rows you need to throw off the needles. The loops themselves are slightly pulled up. Then they are removed with the right knitting needle without knitting. When all the crochets are dropped, the elongated 5 loops are transferred back to the left knitting needle. And then they make 2 nakida.

Elongated 5 loops are knitted together from left to right front. For beginners, this is not always easy, but with experience everything becomes much easier.

Then again make 2 nakida. This is not at all difficult to do, but attention will be required.

In the odd beginner needlewoman, too, is waiting for difficulty. Need to knit 2 yarn in different ways. The first is behind the front wall.

And the second behind the back. The main thing is to understand the principle and not get confused.
