Address and mode of operation



The Horror Museum in St. Petersburg is located at 86 Marata Street. The exposition is located in the Planet Neptune shopping and entertainment complex, right above the Oceanarium. To visit the attraction, you do not need to make an appointment; excursions are conducted as groups are formed that can go on a mysterious journey one after another with an interval of 15 minutes. The cost of the session is 800-1000 rubles per person. From Monday to Wednesday, the museum is open from 12.00 to 18.00, and from Thursday to Sunday from 11.00 to 21.00.
