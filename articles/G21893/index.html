Hotel Tangerine Beach Hotel 4 * (Sri Lanka, Kalutara): overview, description and reviews of tourists

Many hoteliers place on the same territory, in a well-groomed park, several hotels of different categories. This is done primarily to attract customers to more budget options. So, guests of a four-star hotel have the opportunity to use the entire infrastructure and pools of the "five". We observe this situation in the case of Tangerine Beach 4 * (Sri Lanka, Kalutara). It stands on the same territory as the Royal Palms Beach Hotel 5 *. Since both resorts are managed by the same owner (Tangerine Hotels) and management, guests can use the entire infrastructure, including restaurants, bars, pools, the beach and a night disco club. Of course, this is a tangible plus for tourists staying in the Quartet. After all, they actually use the service of a five-star hotel! And what else can please Tangerine Beach Hotel tourists? We will talk about this in our article. We will give you full information about this resort hotel - we will tell you about its territory, rooms, food, beach, facilities and services. And our main help in the description of the hotel will be the reviews of those who have already had time to relax there. Tourists not only praise the hotel, but also post photos of this place on the networks. And judging by them, you will find a paradise holiday at the Tangerine Beach Hotel. Where is the Tangerine Beach Hotel

The hotel is located on the southwest coast of the island. Although its name is Tangerine Beach Hotel Kalutara, the closest settlement to it is Waskaduwa. But this, by and large, is not a city, but a resort area, which belongs to the Kalutara district. Nearby is another village - Wadduva. And the hotel described by us is located approximately in the middle between Kalutara and the capital of the island state, the city of Colombo. The main international airport, Bandaranayyak, where liners from Russia arrive, is located in Katunayyak, seventy-four kilometers from the hotel. However, reviews claim that the road will take about two hours - because of the way the Sri Lankan drivers drive. By the way, tourists dissuade to rent a car on the island - for the above reason. The capital of Sri Lanka Colombo is only forty kilometers away. Tangerine Beach Hotel is a front line hotel. A private sandy beach with golden sand is only seventy meters away. You will be met by the Indian Ocean with its warm embrace and power. The beach on which the hotel is located is called Wadduwa Beach, and it enjoys the well-deserved fame of the best on the entire southwest coast.

As already mentioned, Tangerine Beach Hotel 4 * shares a fabulous tropical garden with the five-star Royal Palms Beach Hotel. Therefore, the territory is quite vast - almost four hectares. The garden is decorated in Sri Lankan style. And many reviews admire the well-groomed and green territory of the two hotels. Here they can organize wedding ceremonies, as well as arrange the holding of a honeymoon or marriage anniversary. The hotel presents gifts and a compliment to such guests. In this "Quartet" there are only one hundred seventy-nine rooms for guests. All of them are located in a three-story building, decorated in a colonial style. The Duplex Junior Suite is highly praised. These rooms are split-level. On the ground floor there is a living room and a bathroom. Upstairs is a bedroom and a toilet. Above all praise, the chic rooms of the Executive Suite and Lakshari categories. They consist of two rooms (living room and bedroom). They have a terrace or a very spacious balcony with ocean views. But reviews warn: the coast is flat, without lagoons and bays. Therefore, ocean waves roll on the beach serious. If you are a mediocre swimmer or come with children, it is better not to take risks. There are always tuk-tuks on duty at the hotel‚Äôs gate, ready to take you to the nearest lagoon. Here there is quiet and smooth. But for surfers on Wadduwa Beach a real expanse. Although the hotel is positioning itself as a venue for a honeymoon, young guests are also welcome here. Thus, if you have already decided to relax with your children in Tangerine Beach Hotel 4 *, reviews advise you to go to Bentota beach - this is the most beautiful, and most importantly, safe coast of Sri Lanka. Resort Features and Excursions from Tangerine Beach Hotel (Sri Lanka)

Kalutara is a small town that, despite its status as the capital of the district, looks very provincial. But if you lack the hustle and bustle of the metropolis - Colombo is only forty kilometers away, and you can get to it quickly and cheaply by rail or bus. But in Kalutara there is something to see. Even before entering the city, a gigantic stupa of Gangatilaka Vihara appears before the eyes of travelers. This sacred building, in addition to religious significance for Buddhists, has architectural value. It is hollow inside, which is a rarity. You can compare with the Kiev-Pechersk Lavra to Dagob Kalutar Viharu. This is one of the oldest temple complexes in Sri Lanka. It is built on the spot where, after spiritual enlightenment, Buddha Shakyamuni himself preached. Inside, the astonished tourist will find a collection of seventy-four frescoes that depict the scenes of all 550 reincarnations of Prince Siddhartha. In the courtyard of the temple complex, a Bo tree grows. It is also a shrine. After all, it grew out of the appendix of that anemone under which the Buddha gained enlightenment. A sacrifice in the form of flowers or fruits should be offered to the tree in order to find harmony in the soul or fulfillment of a cherished desire. In the south of Kalutara County is the beautiful Bentota Beach. Reviews are advised not to be limited to one city in their sorties. Do not trust the barking guides on duty at the gates of the Tangerine Beach Hotel. Kalutara, or rather, its stores, where rollbacks are promised to such "guides", will be your only place to visit. Hotel guides can take you to the Turtle Farm, to the waterfalls, in Colombo.
