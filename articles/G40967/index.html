HandMade: DIY bead and ribbon bracelets



We girls always want to look our best. Going to work or for a walk, many spend most of the time choosing clothes. Clothes thrown out of the closet lying on a bed in the mountains are a common thing. This is often an indicator that a girl is going to an important event. In such chaos, we often forget about one important detail - accessories. Beads, necklaces, bracelets, rings are an important part of women's wardrobe. Unfortunately, not everyone remembers this, and some deliberately neglect jewelry. But still, any girl should have at least a few such accessories. What if there is no opportunity to purchase all this in expensive stores?

Recently, more than ever, things made by yourself (the so-called handmade) began to be appreciated. Modern fashion shows that do-it- yourself jewelry is more popular among fashionistas than factory ones. And to make such accessories is not difficult at all. The most simple in execution are do-it- yourself bracelets made of beads and ribbons. It may seem that for a girl who has never encountered this, it is impossible to do this. But this is not at all true.

Types of beads

First of all, it is necessary to understand the types of materials for weaving. There are a huge number of different beads, so you need to know at least a little. The simplest is a bead from beads. In addition, there are more expensive beads made of semiprecious or precious stones. Many have heard about Swarovski stones more than once , they are also used in weaving jewelry with their own hands. But such jewelry already belongs to expensive handmade work, so you should start learning with the simplest material.

How to make do-it- yourself bracelets from beads and ribbons?

There are many different bracelets. In this, as in any other business, it is simply necessary to understand. Let's start with one of the easiest bracelets to perform. It is worth noting that such a bracelet is very popular among fashionistas, because it looks very beautiful. For weaving, you will need a satin ribbon, beads, fishing line or silicone thread, a needle. Silicone thread can be found in any store that sells goods for needlework. So, thread a fishing line or thread into a needle, tie the ends. Leaving 20-25 cm from the edge, tie the ribbon in a small knot and start weaving from it. The needle must be inserted in the middle of the tape. Make a small fold of the tape to the size of the beads and stretch the fishing line. Next, we put a bead on a fishing line and again pierce the ribbon, after making a fold. The weaving process is clearly visible in the photo.

So we continue weaving to the desired length of the bracelet. In the end, you need to make a knot of tape directly next to the last bead.


Beaded Necklace



As you can see, any girl can weave bracelets with their own hands from beads and ribbons. Learning how to weave simple bracelets, you can move on to more complex weaving jewelry. You need to find a necklace of beads, choose what you like and get to work. And here, too, it is better to start with simple ones. In addition, master classes with step by step photos are very effective for learning. With the help of such lessons, everyone can learn to weave jewelry. Weaving bracelets with your own hands from beads and ribbons is an opportunity to have exclusive jewelry made in your own style.
