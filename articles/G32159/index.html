How long is a credit card valid? What to do when it ends

Credit cards are issued for a specified period. After the expiration period they are subject to replacement. The owner must know the validity of the credit card to prevent delays. Card reissue is a procedure for replacing a payment instrument. The service is issued upon request of the client or automatically.

What determines the validity period of a credit card?

Credit card conditions vary by bank and type of payment instrument. The period refers to a category that the client cannot influence.

The validity period of a credit card is affected by:

  • Frequency of use. After 6-18 months of daily payment and cash withdrawal, the plastic carrier is damaged. Most of the changes happen with the chip that protects the credit card data. Scratches and microcracks can cause the card to malfunction.
  • Accuracy of the owner. If you constantly drop the card, carry it with a mobile phone or remove it too sharply from the card reader, it may be damaged before the end of the operation period.
  • Type of credit card. Until 2015, plastic carriers were not manufactured with protection in the form of a chip on the front side. Because of this, they were more often subjected to hacking and deformation. If the client has an old-style credit card, without a chip, the risk of damage ahead of time increases.
  • Manufacturing defect. In the case of poor-quality manufacturing of a means of payment, owners complain about the fast fading of the inscriptions and background (within 6 months after receipt), the deformation of the case and protective elements.

If one or more factors are present, the risk of premature wear increases to 50%.

Who sets the period for using a credit card?

Plastic cards are issued by the issuer - a banking organization. The bank determines how long a credit card will be comfortable for customers and offers products with a limit of funds.

The cardholder is not able to influence the standard terms of the product. But the client has the right to reissue a credit card at any time. If the credit card expires, he will receive a plastic carrier with a new period. If there is a technical malfunction, reissue at Sberbank, Rosselkhozbank, VTB 24 is free.

You can get information about the period of use of a plastic card in several ways:

  2. In documents.
  3. By phone contact line of the bank.
  4. On the official site.
  5. In self-service devices.
  6. Via online banking.
  7. In a mobile application.

Information is provided free of charge on the day of contact.

Where is the validity period of a bank card?

Information about the expiration date of a plastic carrier is not confidential. It is located on the front side of the credit card - the bottom line in the center of the card.

The media indicates the expiration of the credit card. The information is presented in digital format "month / year". The last month during which the customer has the opportunity to use a credit card is indicated. After the end of the period, the card is blocked, but the account remains active.

The agreement also indicates the conditions of the credit card: the validity period of the card and the end of the period. In most large banks, for example, Sberbank, Tinkoff, VTB 24, the credit card is valid for 3 years. It does not depend on the product, limit and account holder.

How to find out the term at an ATM?

In the self-service device, the client can get almost all the information about the credit card. These are convenient as the machines operate around the clock.

To find out the validity of a credit card, the owner must use the terminals of his bank. In other devices, limited functionality is available: balance, expense, mobile payment.

To find out when the credit card expires, you need to:

  • insert it into the card reader, enter the code;
  • go to the section on bank cards and deposits;
  • find a credit card account, click on the active line;
  • View information or print a receipt.

Information on the credit card period is provided along with the number, full name customer and contract details. Banks do not charge a service fee.

Getting information with the help of bank employees

If the client cannot independently find out when to change the credit card, he can take a passport, a card and come to the bank branch. The manager will print out the conditions of the card and will orient on the terms of replacement.

Customers of Tinkoff and other online banks can find out information by calling the support service. You will need a passport, credit card and control data (indicated in the application). Without data, operators will not be able to identify the subscriber. Call for customers is free.

What to do if the validity period expires?

The client has 3 options, what to do at the end of the credit card:

  1. Close an account.
  2. Get a new card with a planned reissue.
  3. Make a reissue yourself and get a credit card with a new validity period.

Account closure does not automatically occur. The client must contact the bank branch with a passport in order to terminate the loan agreement. For online banking cards, the operation is performed through the contact center.

Reissue Features

Replacing a bank card with a new one with the same account is called reissue. In the bank, the operation is performed in the presence of a plastic carrier. If the credit card has expired, the client must contact the branch where he received it before. A passport is required with you.

The operation of issuing a card with a new validity period takes no more than 15 minutes. The client signs the receipt of a credit card with a new term, puts a signature on the card (otherwise it may not be accepted at retail outlets).

Re-issuance is issued only to the owner of the credit card or its authorized representative. When obtaining a credit card by a third party, a document confirming the rights of the representative is required. This can be a bank or notarized power of attorney.

When to apply for a replacement plastic carrier?

A planned reissue does not require additional operations to change the term of the card by the client. As soon as the credit card expires, the client receives a notification about the need to replace the card.

In Sberbank, users are notified in 45 days that they will soon need to replace the plastic carrier. Sberbank's credit card is 33 years old. After the period, the client must take a passport and come to the office at the place of opening the credit card.

From 2018, customers of the largest bank in the country can track through when Sberbank Online can track. Opposite the credit card, which expires, a reissue notification will appear. As soon as the card is delivered to the branch, the SMS from number 900 will be sent to the client. Sberbank Online will receive information about the credit card received at the office, and the credit card account will be displayed with a new validity period.

Apply for a replacement card before the deadline. Otherwise, the client runs the risk of missing a scheduled payment, as he will not be able to deposit money to a blocked card in the terminal or through Internet banking.
