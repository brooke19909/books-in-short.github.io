So what is an Apple TV? Review and reviews

The world first heard about Apple TV in 2007. At the Macworld conference, Steve Jobs himself introduced the new product. It would seem that success is inevitable.

Almost 11 years have passed since then. However, in Russia, the prefix did not take root. If practically no one needs to explain what the iPhone is, then not everyone has heard about Apple TV in our country. Even fewer of our compatriots understand what the device is intended for and how it works.

In fact, everything is simple. Most often, this device is used to broadcast content (for example, video in high quality) from a computer or phone to a large television screen.

But only this is not limited to its functions. Apple TV also allows you to transfer content directly from the iTunes application and other similar services. In fact, it makes an ordinary TV ‚Äúsmart‚Äù - it adds Smart-TV capabilities.

The prefix became especially popular when users were convinced that the SMART functions built into most Korean models are far from ideal. Market Launch History and Marketers Mistakes

Initially, the console had a built-in hard drive of only 40 GB. Although the maximum resolution of the video being played did not exceed 720p, this volume was already too small even then. Negative reviews of the updated Apple TV immediately flooded the Internet.

Already at the end of May, the company's marketers realized the mistake and a 160 GB disk was released.

Another significant miscalculation was made. The initial version of the software did not provide for purchases directly in the application. This flaw was corrected as early as 2008. And also based on feedback from Apple TV owners.

A year later, the company completely stopped the production of a 40 GB hard drive. The 160 GB drive seems to have established itself on the positive side. However, already in 2010, the company completely updated the device and refused to use the internal drive as such. This solution 4 times allowed to reduce the dimensions of the device and make it work almost silent.

How did they correct miscalculations?

Instead of a hard drive, the device was equipped with a built-in flash memory of 8 GB, which made it possible to cache the movie being watched. This means that you could pause the movie, and then continue playing - you would not have to download the file again.

Such innovations made the device cheaper. According to research, most users were willing to pay no more than $ 100 for such a device. And in such a simple way, the company managed to lower the price even below this limit.

However, the experiments did not end there. The third version of Apple TV was released in 2012. The company's designers have completely updated the user interface. In addition, the device now supports video with a resolution of 1080p. This version also required significant revision. In 2015, Apple engineers equipped the console with 32 GB or 64 GB of internal flash memory. This opened up great opportunities for storing games and multimedia. In addition, the operating system itself has undergone changes.

The fourth generation of devices saw the light in 2017. 4K video and Dolby Atmos sound allow users to literally "dissolve in parallel reality."

The result, as they say, is evident - positive reviews about the Apple TV were not long in coming.

Set up your Apple TV

It‚Äôs not necessary to call the wizard to set up Apple TV. Apple has specifically designed the interface in such a way that an ordinary user could cope with the setup.

Connecting an Apple TV set-top box to a TV

  1. We connect the HDMI cable to the console and the TV. Only after that we connect the Apple TV to the network.

  2. The Apple logo should appear on the screen. Using the volume control and the button in the middle, use the remote control to select the desired language.

  3. Now you need to connect the console to the Internet. Apple TV automatically scans for available Wi-Fi networks - select the one you need from the list and enter the password. You can connect to the router directly - using a cable.

  4. Now you need to give your consent to the transfer of data to Apple. Personal information is not transmitted. However, you can safely refuse - it will not affect performance in any way.

  5. Install Home Sharing on your computer. This application has a simple and intuitive interface that will make the data transfer process as comfortable as possible.

  6. Now synchronize the PC, TV and set-top box with each other (as a rule, this process happens automatically, you just need to wait until the corresponding message appears).

How to ‚Äúmake friends‚Äù Apple TV and iPhone

In order for everything to work out, you need to use an iPhone with the iOs7 or later operating system. Unfortunately, older versions of the operating system do not support the iBeacon option.

Step-by-step instruction:

  1. Turn on Wi-Fi on the iPhone and connect to the wireless network.

  2. Turn on bluetooth. The corresponding slider is located in the "settings" menu immediately below Wi-Fi.

  3. We bring the iPhone to the console. A pop-up window appears on the screen, click ‚Äúyes‚Äù.

  4. Now you need to log in with your Apple ID. The system will ask a couple of questions, after which data synchronization will begin. The process will not be displayed on the screen - this is normal. Everything runs in the background, you just have to wait.

Connecting Apple TV to a computer

Sometimes you need to update the firmware or restore some data. To do this, you have to connect the Apple TV to the computer. Preferably MacOS is installed on the PC. Windows users will have to additionally install the appropriate application.

The procedure is as follows:

  1. Disable the Apple TV box.

  2. Disconnect the HDMI cable and others.

  3. Using a micro USB cable, connect the set-top box and the computer through the appropriate connector.

  4. Launch the iTunes application on your PC and find it in the Apple TV menu.

How to upgrade firmware?

Everything is simple - this is done through the Option command, then Restore in iTunes. Now the system will ask you to specify the name of the firmware file that you want to install. You need to select the one you need and click Choose - then everything will happen automatically.

An important nuance: you need to pay attention to the firmware file having the .ipsw extension. Download it easily on the Internet.

How to recover data?

In the Apple TV window, select the Restore button. After the system asks you to confirm the operation, click Restore and Update. The procedure will take several minutes.
