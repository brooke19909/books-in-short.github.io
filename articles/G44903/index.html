Hotel Iberotel Makadi Beach 5 *, (Egypt / Hurghada): description, traveler reviews

Fans of splashing around in the gentle waves of the Red Sea, as well as fans of water sports and entertainment, have long been fond of the Makadi Bay resort area. This is where one of the best hotels on the coast is located - Iberotel Makadi Beach.

Hotel Description

The chic, five-star Iberotel Makadi Beach is located on the shores of the Red Sea. Here you will find a huge sandy beach and chic apartments with all necessary amenities. A distinctive feature of this resort complex is its design, which combines traditional Egyptian pretentiousness and practical European laconicism.

Iberotel Makadi Beach offers its visitors a wealth of opportunities for entertainment and relaxation. In connection with the sea location of the hotel, the emphasis, of course, is on water sports and attractions. Particularly noteworthy are the restaurants operating on site, where you can taste delicious dishes from local masters of culinary art.

For people leading a healthy lifestyle, this resort will seem like an ideal place. On the territory of the hotel you will have the opportunity to visit the gym, as well as the wellness center, where you can get a huge range of procedures (massage, sauna and others).





Where is the hotel situated

Iberotel Makadi Beach is located on Makadi Bay, north of the famous resort of Hurghada. The hotel complex is 30 km from the international airport and 37 km from the city center. Only 2 km from the hotel is the largest water park on the coast.





Territory Overview

The territory of the Iberotel Makadi Beach (Makadi Bay) is a real resort town where every tourist will find entertainment to his taste. So, within the resort complex you are presented with:

  • 2 buildings, one of which occupies two floors, and the second - three;
  • 4 chic restaurants where you can not only enjoy full breakfasts, lunches and dinners, but also try gourmet cuisine from professional chefs;
  • 4 bars provide you with plenty of drinks and snacks;
  • On the territory of the hotel guests are available 2 large pools, the water in one of which is heated in the cold season. There is also a children's bathing area;
  • a small well-kept garden with a huge number of exotic plants;
  • a private beach allows guests to enjoy the Red Sea in a quiet secluded atmosphere.

Description of rooms

Guests of Iberotel Makadi Beach (Makadi Bay) are offered for resettlement in two categories of rooms: junior suite and superior. The first category is a one-room apartment of 56 square meters. m, with a maximum capacity of 4 people. Usually the room is used to accommodate two adults with 2 children (large bed, sofa bed, as well as an extra bed).

Room Amenities

The rooms of the Iberotel Makadi Beach Hotel have all the necessary amenities to make guests feel as comfortable and relaxed as at home. So, in each and every apartment of the hotel there is:

  • Personal air conditioning.
  • Balcony with a view of the garden or the sea (and if we are talking about the first floors - a terrace).
  • The TV is connected to a satellite dish, which catches several Russian channels as well.
  • The rooms have a telephone for communication with the administration. All international calls are made only through the operator.
  • The bathroom has a shower. Toiletries and branded cosmetics are also provided.
  • There is a built-in hairdryer.

Special attention deserves the service of this hotel. Rooms are cleaned twice a day, and bed linen and towels are regularly replaced.





Services included in the tour price

Since the Iberotel Makadi Beach hotel has five-star accreditation, the room rate includes a wide range of services, including:

  • free tea and coffee in the room (stocks replenished daily);
  • each room has a safe;

  • from 7 a.m. to 7 p.m. you can use the gym;

  • You can take part in sports games organized by animators;
  • all days of the stay, except the last one, laundry services are provided free of charge;
  • visiting evening entertainment;
  • sauna and jacuzzi in the wellness center;
  • a visit to the tennis court, which is illuminated in the evening;
  • baby strollers or baby-sitters can be provided upon request;
  • catamaran sailing;
  • bathrobe, slippers and cosmetic set for the shower;
  • daily minibar is replenished with soft drinks;
  • use of the private beach area, as well as sunbeds, umbrellas and towels;
  • Each room includes fruit and wine upon arrival.

Positive reviews

Resort hotels care about their reputation, providing guests with high quality service. Iberotel Makadi Beach is no exception. Reviews of this hotel contain many thanks and positive points, among which it is worth noting:

  • good location of the hotel (silence, sea coast, beautiful nature);
  • perfect cleanliness not only in the rooms, but also throughout the resort complex (including the beach);
  • a large amount of greenery in the courtyard of the hotel, which is very important in the conditions of a hot Egyptian climate;
  • clean beach with lots of sunbeds;
  • high-quality nutrition and a wide range of dishes, designed for both gourmets and those who follow a diet;
  • an oriental restaurant located on the top of the tower provides an opportunity not only to taste traditional dishes, but also to enjoy the panoramas of Makadi Bay;
  • in the presence of a large number of available rooms, the administrator can upgrade the room category without any surcharges;
  • a large assortment of fruits on the menu;
  • despite the fact that the hotel was renovated for a long time, all rooms are in excellent condition;
  • the small territory of the Iberotel Makadi Beach hotel (Hurghada) is compensated by the fact that it is combined with several more hotels, so that there is a large space for walking;
  • a large number of outlets near the hotel;
  • convenient entry into the sea and beautiful coral reefs;
  • hotel staff responds quickly to requests and requests;
  • Of particular interest are all kinds of competitions and contests, which are held between different hotels.




Negative reviews

Unfortunately, like any other resort, the Iberotel Makadi Beach 5 * hotel has some negative reviews, namely:

  • unequal attitude towards tourists from different countries;
  • low speed and poor quality of the Internet;
  • almost complete lack of animation;
  • prices for excursions are significantly overpriced.

Bonuses and compliments

Makadi Hotel (Egypt) provides certain categories of guests the opportunity to participate in the bonus program:

  • Newlyweds who indicate when booking a honeymoon the reason for the trip to the hotel are provided with a cake. To confirm their status, it will be necessary to present a document confirming marriage.
  • If your trip coincides with your birthday, then you have the right to receive a birthday cake. In addition to indicating this fact in the process of booking a tour, it is worth a couple of days to remind hotel staff of the upcoming solemn date.
  • Also, guests who have celebrated their wedding anniversary at the hotel are eligible to receive the cake. For this you need to: indicate the moment when booking the tour, on the day of arrival at the hotel, present a copy or the original document of marriage, as well as remind the administration of your celebration for a couple of days.
  • Starting from the second visit to the hotel you will be entered in the base of regular guests. A surprise in the form of fruits and cookies will be waiting for you in the room. With each subsequent visit to your privileges will be added more and more bonuses, such as early check-in or late check-out, souvenirs from the hotel, a romantic table setting in a restaurant and others.

Additional Information

One of the most popular holiday destinations is Egypt, because a huge number of hotels with a good level of service have been working here for a long time. Hurghada Makadi Bay is one of the best hotels in Hurghada. Going there, it is important to know some information about the features of this hotel complex and about the rules of living in it:

  • Check-in and check-out at 14:00 and 12:00, respectively. If you do not have time to hand over the keys and bracelet to the reception desk before the checkout time, then an extra charge will be charged for your excess stay in the room;
  • Pets are not allowed in the hotel.
  • Extra bed for a child under two years of age is provided free of charge, and 50% of the cost of the ticket is charged for placing a bed for older children.
  • If the child under 12 does not need an extra bed, then he stays in the room for free.
  • The largest water park in Egypt is located next to the hotel, the cost of visiting which is not included in the tour, and payment is made separately.
  • When booking a tour, a credit card is required. The same payment instrument should be with you at the time of registration.
  • For every 10 days, guests are given the opportunity to use 150 MB of Internet traffic. After the limit has been reached, you have the opportunity to use paid services.

The Iberotel Makadi Beach 5 * hotel fully justifies its high status. Its level is reflected not only in the chic interior, but also in the quality of guest service. If you want to have a good rest in a quiet calm atmosphere, then this is exactly what you need.
