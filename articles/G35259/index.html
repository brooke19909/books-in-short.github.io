Washing machine "Kandy": reviews. Kandy washing machine: installation, instruction

Many models of Kandy washing machines are compact as well as functional. In general, this company has long been on the market for household appliances and pleases many people. Additionally, the prices of models are quite low, so anyone can choose the right option. Among the shortcomings can be noted poor spin and expensive repair of Kandy washing machines. There are still some problems with the vibration of the washing machine. In this case, the sound may be very loud. All this in some cases creates certain inconveniences for the consumer.





Washing machine installation

During installation, the washing machine must be connected to the water supply for water supply, as well as a sewage system for draining. Additionally, a network will be required to provide the device with electricity. This work can only be done by specialists. First of all, they check the voltage in the network. It should not have any gaps, as well as jumps. Additionally, a fuse in the mains is inspected. The connection is directly through the cable that comes with the washing machine. To do this, the specialist must unscrew the special screws on the rear panel. After that, the insulation is removed, the cable is attached to the terminals.

What errors can be

The most common mistake made by Kandy washing machines is E01. This code indicates the problems associated with the hatch. Most often, the reason is a blocking device. At the same time, it is naturally impossible to use a washing machine. Additionally, the problem may lie in the electronic controller. As a rule, the repair of Kandy washing machines does not take much time.

Code "E02" indicates a flood of water. In this case, a violation of the level can be observed. As a result, too much or too little water may be in the washing machine's tank. In some cases, it is able to not act at all. This problem is related to the bay valve, or electronic controller. Also, a specialist with this breakdown should check the mesh. Sometimes it can become clogged and not let water through. The third most common problem is the code "E03". He speaks about violation of discharge of water. The most common problem is a pump breakdown. Additionally, contact in the connection may be broken. At the same time, water is sometimes drained, but this process takes an extremely long time. In this case, the mistakes of the Kandy washing machine should be corrected in the service center.
