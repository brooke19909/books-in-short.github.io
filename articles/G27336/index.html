Climatic features

The bay is located in the temperate climatic zone, continental type. Throughout the year, the temperature here is favorable. The water area freezes in December, and opens in March. In cold winters, the ice crust formed in the bay reaches 80 cm. The average is 40-50 cm. But in warm winters, the ice layer does not exceed 20 cm. The ice cover is uneven, hummocks often form along the coast and near river mouths.

Animal world

The main wealth of the bay is aquatic biological resources. Recently, there has been a tendency to increase precisely freshwater fish, due to a decrease in the salinity of the reservoir. The most common here are pike perch, crucian carp and perch. Often they are crowded along the coasts and estuaries. In addition, a large number of sturgeon, herring, ram, sabrefish and bream are found in the bay.

There are no large mammals living in the waters of the bay. However, according to archaeological finds that were discovered near Taganrog, these species used to live here. The remains of large and small mammals of the Pleistocene era were found.
