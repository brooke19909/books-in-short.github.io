Difficulty of choosing

Bluetooth data models are significantly cheaper, but this protocol limits the data transmission range to about forty meters. It is preferable to purchase it in cases where the sensor or phone will be at a distance of no more than 40 meters, for example, during winter fishing or using the device on a boat. The transmission of information from the echo sounder via Wi-Fi allows it to be used in any situation, since the range of the most powerful casting spinning is overlapped by the radius of the device.
