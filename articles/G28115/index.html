Magna Graecia Hotel 4 in Greece. Reviews

Magna Graecia Hotel 4 * is a tourist complex located on the island of Corfu. It is located in the small settlement of Agios Marcos (Dassia district), which is very close to the local airport (sixteen kilometers). In this article, we will try to characterize the hotel, based, among other things, on the feedback of its guests. Perhaps this description will be useful to future visitors to the Greek resorts of Corfu.



About the location of the hotel

No wonder this hotel is often found under the name Corfu Magna Graecia Hotel 4 *. After all, Kerkyra (and this is the official name of Corfu) is one of the most popular holiday destinations in Greece. Therefore, the choice of a beach hotel is very important. The hotel is intended for people who want to relax at sea. We can say that it stands almost on the beach, but to get there, you need to cross the road. In addition, a few hundred meters from the hotel are the famous coast of the island, praised by many tourists. This is primarily the beaches of Ipsos and Pirgi. Very close to the hotel are two small supermarkets. There are shops near the beach where you can buy cosmetics made on the basis of natural Greek olive oil and kumquat, as well as tanning products. In some places you can pay by card.



How to get here

You can get to Magna Graecia Hotel 4 by flying directly from Moscow or St. Petersburg, or from Athens. Only charters fly from Russia to Corfu, but it‚Äôs convenient to travel with a group during a special tour. Therefore, if you are traveling on your own, it is better to fly from the capital of Greece. There is an opportunity to come by ferry, but it takes about seven hours. The hotel provides airport transfers. Not far from it there is a bus stop that takes you to the city of Kerkyra in just half an hour. And from there you can already go to any area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe island.

What is here

The hotel was built in 1985, but maintained in excellent condition. It is surrounded by mountains and forests. Magna Graecia Hotel 4 has a garden. It has the main four-story building and several bungalows and outbuildings in which the apartments are located. There are many lawns with olive trees, firs and magnolias. The garden is very nice in hot weather, especially after the hot beach sun. The hotel can hold various events and conferences - for this there is a special room, the so-called banquet hall. Almost throughout the hotel there is coverage that allows you to use the Internet, including in rooms and by the pool. However, it is paid. The price is lower, the longer you buy it.



About accommodation

Magna Graecia Hotel 4 * belongs to the category of large hotels. It consists of one hundred and seventy rooms. There are rooms, as they say, for every taste and budget. There are standard rooms for one, two and three guests, suites and apartments. The rooms are equipped by European standards for beach hotels - there is a bathroom with all the necessary equipment, a hairdryer, a TV, a telephone, access to the World Wide Web, air conditioning, a refrigerator, a balcony ... True, you will have to pay extra for Wi-Fi. The furniture and decoration of the room is beautiful, in dark or beige colors, in bronze and onyx. Tourists are delighted with the magnificent views that open in front of them from the balcony, and the residents of the bungalow also praise the silence. The staff is so helpful and friendly that you can change the number you do not like several times without any additional charge. True, not everyone here speaks Russian. Toddlers under two years old are provided with free cots.



About daily bread

Magna Graecia Hotel 4 * has a restaurant and two bars - in the lobby and by the pool. The lobby snack bar is famous for its cocktails. Pool bar praised for its desserts. The main place where tourists are fed is beautifully and stylishly decorated. Here you can order different types of food: breakfast only, half board or the all-inclusive option. Mostly it serves Mediterranean cuisine with a Greek island flavor, but you can also taste typical European food. Tourists are happy to write about fresh and hot food. They respond very positively to its diversity - a lot of meat and vegetables, fruits and various delicacies are served, including baking and jam. At dinner, offer up to ten types of salads. There is, of course, alcohol: vodka, wine, beer ... Moreover, all this is an order of magnitude better than in Turkey itself. Despite the fact that the hotel (especially during the season) is almost full, the waiters work so smoothly that there are always empty seats, and there is no need to stand in lines. Gourmets can taste the culinary masterpieces of the Etrusco restaurant in the neighboring village of Dassia, marked by a Michelin star.



Magna Graecia Hotel 4 * (Greece) - services for guests

You can take your clothes for cleaning or washing, rent a car, or park your car. The safe is at the reception. The hotel provides many entertainment, sports and recreational activities. At your service and tennis, and billiards, and such a noble occupation, like golf. There are spa and fitness centers where you can not only take a steam bath in the sauna and relax with a massage and jacuzzi, but also sunbathe in the solarium. For children there is a playground. Although this is not Egypt or Turkey, vacationers note very good animation, including Russian-speaking. Live music plays, national evenings are held. The latter are popular with tourists, because the Greeks know how to dance so incendiary. There are many activities for toddlers and teens.



Beach and sea vacations

Why do people usually go to Magna Graecia Hotel 4 *? Corfu is, as already mentioned, a popular seaside resort. Therefore, a beach holiday here is the main goal of tourists. You can choose whether to sunbathe by the pool or take sea baths. There is a special terrace for sunbathing. There are two basins. And to relax on the sea you need to go down to the beach (although you have to go back uphill, but it is good for the body). The coast has a blue flag, and although it is pebble, it has an English lawn where it is convenient to lie. In the sea itself, the bottom is sandy. The beach strip is somewhat narrow, but olives and palm trees grow on it, which creates a natural shadow. The sea is unusually clear. The beach does not belong to this hotel, so sun loungers are paid here. But only before dinner, then you can not pay for them. Beach towels are provided for deposit. Fans of comparing different coasts are attracted to nearby bathing spots. For example, Ipsos with its diving, night clubs and stunning views of Mount Pantokrator. And a few kilometers from here is the beautiful Aqualand water park.

What to look around

In Magna Graecia 4 * guides organize various excursions. Of course, first of all, these are boat trips: barbecue, panoramic around Corfu and a visit to neighboring islands. Tourists are happy with them. Corfu is called an exceptionally beautiful green island, which the scorched Rhodes and Crete cannot compare with. And indeed, the local orange, cypress and lemon groves make the rest completely unique. Magna Graecia Hotel 4 (Corfu) also organizes long-distance travel. From distant excursions, the famous Meteors on the mainland are recommended, although it takes four hours to get there (by ferry and bus). And for the whole day you can go with a guide to Albania. Inexpensive car rental at the hotel allows you to independently visit all the attractions of the island much cheaper. If there is no driver‚Äôs license, but you want to see something interesting, but on a budget, it is recommended to go to Kerkyra by bus. There you can walk, admire the city, sit in a tavern and have a good time. Fortresses, shopping streets, elegant buildings of Venetian times will appeal to anyone. Nearby are Paleokastritsa, Sidari and other pearls of the island.
