Thailand, Pattaya, Garden Sea View Resort 4 *: tourist reviews

Are you planning to relax in the country of smiles - Thailand? Prefer hotels with their own green area and beach? In this case, the Garden Sea View, a four-star hotel that has long been loved by our compatriots, can be a great option for you.



Location

Garden Sea View is located in the northern part of Pattaya, in the Naklua Bay area on Wong Prachan Beach. The city center can be reached in just a quarter of an hour. The nearest airport is 126 kilometers away.

Today it consists of 285 rooms located in several multi-storey buildings, and 64 detached bungalows. The hotel has several restaurants and bars offering a wide selection of Thai and international dishes, as well as drinks for every taste, a beauty salon, a massage parlor, parking, a 24-hour reception, a shop, a souvenir shop, laundry and dry cleaning services, car rental and much more. The hotel has two swimming pools, sun terraces with comfortable sun loungers. Guests can play table tennis or billiards, and also use the gym. On the beach, tourists are offered water activities.

The hotel has a checkout time for check-in and check-out. However, according to tourists who have been here, they rarely adhere to it. Therefore, if you arrive at the hotel early in the morning, if there is an available room for the category you have booked, you will be given the keys to it almost immediately, without condemning you to a long wait. Also note that upon check-in you will be asked to make a deposit. Its amount is 2 thousand baht. On account of the deposit you can order food in the restaurant and hotel bars. Upon delivery, the remaining amount will be returned to you. However, many guests are advised to maximize the deposit spent during the holidays, since you can hardly spend two thousand baht somewhere on the way to the airport, and many do not consider it worthwhile to bring Thai currency home.



Number of rooms

As mentioned above, the Garden Sea View Hotel has 285 rooms and 65 separate bungalows. The apartments here are of the following categories:

  • superior (overlooking the garden, maximum occupancy - 2 + 1 people, area 24 sq. meters);
  • Deluxe with sea view (2 + 1, 24 sq. m);
  • mini-suite (48 sq. meters, 2 + 1 guests, sea or garden view, bedroom and living room, large balcony);
  • a royal suite (two bedrooms, a living room, a jacuzzi, a sea view, 4 + 2 people, an area of ‚Äã‚Äã144 square meters);
  • deluxe (36 sq. meters, sea view, 2 + 1 guests);
  • bungalow (40 sq. meters, garden view, there is a terrace, 2 + 1 people);
  • villas (garden or sea view, 24 sq. meters, maximum accommodation 2 + 1 guests).

Regardless of the category, all rooms have comfortable furniture, air conditioning, TV, telephone, hairdryer, radio, minibar, safe, and a bathroom. Personal hygiene products, bathrobes and slippers are provided. The floors are wooden or carpeted. Rooms are cleaned daily. The maids renew their towels and bed linen several times a week. Room service is available to all guests 24 hours a day.



Pattaya, Garden Sea View: Travelers Opinion

Every experienced tourist knows the importance of carefully planning a trip abroad. This is especially true if you are going on a trip to a country where you have not been before. Also especially careful should be parents who want to spend a vacation abroad with their children. It is very important not to make a mistake in choosing a hotel. After all, for the duration of the rest, the hotel becomes a second home for tourists. And even if you consider yourself an unpretentious person and want to save on accommodation, nevertheless, and you probably want to be able to stay in a clean and comfortable room, eat deliciously and count on excellent service and respectful attitude from the staff. Therefore, experienced travelers are advised to begin collecting information about hotels long before the time of the proposed trip. It should not only read the description of hotels on the official websites and portals of tour operators. So, it is advisable to study more reviews of real people who have already happened to stay in the place you are interested in. Indeed, in their comments, tourists share a lot of useful and truthful information about what they had to face in a particular hotel during their holidays. Thus, you will be able to get a closer to reality idea of ‚Äã‚Äãa specific place and not be mistaken with the choice of hotel. Today we offer to learn together about the opinion that has developed among our compatriots who have visited the four-star Garden Sea View hotel.

So, the Garden Sea View Hotel (Pattaya) receives mostly positive reviews from tourists. So, guests say that living here justifies the money spent. The Garden Sea View hotel also has a very favorable rating. So, the overall rating of this hotel, based on the opinion of guests, is 4 points out of a maximum of five. Thus, most of the travelers who visited here were quite satisfied with their choice. Next, we offer to learn more about how tourists from Russia and other CIS countries describe the hotel in question.



Territory

Tourists, judging by their reviews, were very pleased with this item. So, according to them, the Garden Sea View Hotel (Pattaya) has a very large territory of its own. Which, I must say, is not so common in Thailand. There are a lot of greenery, there are places for walking. True, some of our compatriots say that they have never seen a gardener work in a hotel during their entire stay. Therefore, all the vegetation looks more natural than well-groomed. However, this did not cause any inconvenience to any of the vacationers. By the way, the presence of a spacious private territory with lots of trees and flowers for many travelers has become one of the key points in choosing a hotel. After all, it is much nicer to leave the room to be in the garden than on a noisy city street.

Location

The overwhelming majority of vacationers in their comments indicate that they were very pleased with the location of the hotel. So, it is located far from the noisy center. In addition, the sea and the beach are literally around the corner. It is not difficult to get from the hotel to the city center of Pattaya. Garden Sea View is a ten-minute walk from the street, where tuk-tuks ply for quick access to Walking Street and the waterfront. Some travelers reached the center of Pattaya even on foot. According to them, they were very pleased with the opportunity to make such walks. If you don‚Äôt want to walk a lot , you can always use a taxi or rent a car or motobike. Also in their reviews, many tourists note the delight of having the Temple of Truth near the hotel. This magnificent building can be admired directly from the hotel. You can get to it by taxi or on foot. You will be charged 200 baht for entry. Here, according to our compatriots, there is a Russian-speaking guide who will gladly give you an entertaining tour.



Hotel itself

In general, as already mentioned, guests staying at the hotel were satisfied. However, there were some complaints. So, judging by the reviews of tourists, the Garden Sea View hotel would have a higher, if higher, if the rooms here were in better condition. According to our compatriots, most of the housing stock needs to be renovated and updated. This is especially true of rooms in the bungalow, which many former guests do not advise to settle. As for the furniture, it is, according to vacationers, although not new, but high-quality and in good condition. The same goes for technology. Many liked the availability of a kettle and kitchenware in the rooms, so you can always prepare your favorite drink without leaving the room.

A number of complaints of guests regarding plumbing. So, some complained about a not very comfortable shower (the watering can is fixed on the wall and it is impossible to remove it), others were unhappy with the leaking toilet. For these reasons, a number of guests lowered their ratings for the hotel. Tourists hope that soon such shortcomings will be eliminated.

Otherwise, there are no complaints about the numbers. So, they seemed to the guests quite spacious. Many also note large balconies where you can sit comfortably and have a cup of tea or a glass of wine and enjoy the surroundings.



Staff, service

Our compatriots had no particular complaints about the work of the Garden Sea View employees. So, according to the guests, the rooms were cleaned every day. True, she was not very thorough, but none of the guests delivered any particular inconvenience. Maids also regularly replenished supplies of personal hygiene and drinking water. If suddenly they forgot to bring something, then you can always call the reception and ask to fix the error. As for the change of towels, they were updated every other day, and bed linen - a couple of times a week.

As for the other hotel staff, in general, as our compatriots note, everyone here is quite friendly and welcoming. You will always be helped to resolve any issue.

Restaurants, bars

The Garden Sea View Hotel (Pattaya), the photo of which can be seen in this article, offers its guests meals based on the "breakfast only" system or half board. Also, some tourists chose not to pay in advance for meals at the hotel. So, according to them, in the mornings they could have a cup of coffee in their room and have a snack, for example, with cereals bought in a supermarket, and eat fully later in the city or on the beach. Those who ate in the hotel restaurant in the morning claim that, despite the fact that breakfast continues until 10 in the morning, it is advisable to arrive early, at 7-8. The fact is that despite the fact that cooks always try to replenish the ending dishes, by the end of the meal you may no longer get the tidbits.

As for the menu, almost all the guests were satisfied with it. So, for breakfast you can eat eggs cooked in different variations, bacon, sausages, fresh pastries, sweets, fruits and vegetables. Also, of course, you will be offered several Thai dishes. Moreover, you can choose them in accordance with your taste - sharp or not.

For lunch and dinner, the selection of dishes is also very diverse. According to our compatriots, there are excellent cooks at Garden Sea View Resort (Pattaya) who do not get tired of delighting guests with various delicious dishes of not only Thai but also European cuisine. If you paid only breakfast or half board, then you can always at any time of the day also eat in the hotel restaurant, but for an additional fee. As travelers note, the prices on the menu here, as they say, do not bite. Basically, many tourists still preferred to eat outside the hotel. After all, both in Pattaya itself and in the rest of Thailand, there is no shortage of cafes and restaurants. Moreover, they cook almost everywhere well, and the prices are very reasonable. In addition, experienced travelers are advised not to ignore the shingles (mobile food stalls) on the streets. Also be sure to visit the grocery markets where you can buy fresh fruits and seafood, which you will be prepared here for a small surcharge.

Garden Sea View also has several bars with a wide selection of drinks for every taste.



Infrastructure

The Garden Sea View Hotel (Pattaya, Thailand) has everything you need for a comfortable stay and pastime. So, on its territory there are two outdoor pools. Next to them are equipped with sunbathing terraces with a sufficient number of sun loungers and parasols for all guests. The largest of the pools also has a jacuzzi. According to vacationers, he himself is quite large, with different depths. So it will be comfortable for both confident swimmers and those who prefer to feel the bottom under their feet. There is also a section for children in the pool.

Some guests complained that in the water you can often see fallen leaves and flowers. However, most of the vacationers were not embarrassed. After all, next to the pool there are trees that give the shadow, which is so necessary on a hot day. Therefore, most tourists did not bother a couple of leaves in the water.

There is also an outdoor gym on site. It is equipped only with a roof. Exercise machines here are far from new, however, a magnificent view of the sea opens from the hall. Therefore, many vacationers enjoyed visiting it. And in general, according to experienced travelers, it is difficult to find hotels with a modern large gym in a city like Pattaya.

Garden Sea also offers its visitors laundry and dry cleaning services, visit a beauty salon and an Internet cafe, and rent a car or motorbike. The hotel also has several meeting rooms. In public areas of the hotel, guests can connect to free Wi-Fi. However, a number of tourists expressed the opinion that it would be nice to be able to use wireless Internet in the rooms.

Beach

According to the vast majority of travelers, one of the main advantages of the hotel in question is its location right on the beach. Not all Pattaya hotels can boast of this. In addition, the hotel has its own beach, so guests will not have to pay extra for renting sun loungers and umbrellas. Moreover, the water here, according to tourists, is much cleaner than in other places in Pattaya. In short, the Garden Sea View Hotel offers its guests an excellent opportunity for a beach holiday.

Despite this, many experienced travelers are advised to visit other beaches. True, in Pattaya they for the most part are not very clean. But many tourists are happy to visit the nearby islands with magnificent beaches and clear water. You can get to them from the pier of Pattaya by ferry or boat.

In general, most of our compatriots are sure that Thailand is not suitable for a calm and measured pastime. After all, there is a lot of interesting places that you should definitely visit. Therefore, do not focus only on beach vacations, but travel, discovering this amazing country! Moreover, there are plenty of opportunities for this. So, you can rent a motorbike or a car. You can also always book an excursion to the travel agency or use the services of a taxi and tuk-tuk.
