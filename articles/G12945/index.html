"Antigoni 3", Cyprus - photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

Thousands of Russian tourists relax in Cyprus annually. Tourists with children prefer calmer resorts, and the city of Protaras is a favorite place for such a holiday. Most hotels are family oriented. An example of such a hotel is Antigoni 3. More detailed information about the hotel, reviews and cost of leisure are discussed below.





Resort Information

Protaras is considered one of the most "family" resorts in Cyprus. Especially tourists from Russia love him. The resort is calm and tranquil, most often families with children or aged travelers come here who value serenity and carelessness. Guests of Protaras have a rest from work, city bustle and everyday problems. Come here and those who want to do work - the resort, despite its small size, is prepared for a meeting of businessmen and business people.

Protaras is located near the center of entertainment of Cyprus - the city of Ayia Napa. This is a great solution for those who want to stay in a quiet place, but not far from entertainment venues.

The city of Protaras also attracts tourists because it is a completely new and disheveled resort. More recently, instead of hotels and apartments on the beach there was nothing but shrubs and fig groves.

The climate of Protaras is favorable; the sun shines here almost all year round. However, it is best to come here from May to October, then you can be pleased with sunny days without rain.





Protaras Hotels

The resort welcomes tourists with a large number of modern hotels. Here you can stay in hotels of different categories and classes. There are both luxury hotels on the coast, and modest "treshki" away from the sea. Basically, all apartment hotels are located on the first coastline. Despite the fact that most pensions are located along the seashore, they do not "stand on one another", as in the famous resorts of Turkey. Hotels are isolated from each other, a certain seclusion adds to the indented coast. Hotels are located in the so-called mini-coves, and Antigoni Hotel 3 is no exception.

Briefly about the Antigoni 3 Hotel

An attractive hotel for tourists from Russia can be found in the center of the resort of Protaras. In appearance, the hotel is quite good, the five-story building looks favorable in comparison with less tall buildings. She is in good condition, she does not even need redecorating. Antigoni 3 (Cyprus) is part of the well-known hotel chain Tsokkos Hotels Ltd. This is a famous chain of hotels throughout Cyprus.

The hotel has a small area, a crystal-clear pool in the shape of a lagoon. He fully justifies the number of stars awarded to him.





Where is?

Antigoni 3 (Cyprus) is most often chosen by less demanding and demanding travelers. Most guests of the island tend to stay on the coast closer to the sea, respectively, the requirements for this category of hotels are obviously greater. Hotel Antigoni 3 is located within the city, but close to the sea - just 200 meters from the sandy beach. Also within walking distance is the famous Konnos Bay beach and Fig Grove. Otherwise, it is called the fig tree beach.

It is also convenient that the hotel is located near the airport. The nearest aircraft arrival point is in the neighboring city of Ayia Napa, 40 km from Protaras.





Description of rooms

Hotel Antigoni 3 is located in a small building with 172 rooms spread over 5 floors. All rooms are of a standard type, but this is enough for a comfortable stay. They are divided into:

- IV-standard rooms with a view of the surroundings of Protaras;

- SSV-standard rooms with sea view (side);

- SV-standard rooms with direct sea views.

All rooms have a balcony or terrace, from where you can admire either the sea views or the green decoration of the city.

Modern style and fresh repairs make the rooms especially cozy. All of them are made in bright colors, equipped with high-quality and comfortable furniture (double / single bed, table, bedside table, chairs and armchairs).

The rooms have their own toilet, washbasin and bathroom, some have a shower. Additional amenities include satellite and cable TV, TV, air conditioning, minibar, safe and telephone. Each room has a radio, iron and hairdryer, and an ironing board is available upon request at the reception. Some rooms also have a special kitchenette with coffee / tea making facilities and a mini fridge.

The rooms are cleaned constantly, linen change and maintenance - around the clock.

The rooms are equipped for people with disabilities, there are non-smoking rooms. Unfortunately, pets are not allowed in the hotel rooms and on the hotel premises.

For tourists who are going to stay in Antagoni 3 - check-in usually occurs at 14.00 local time, and you will have to leave early - at 11-12.00.





Hotel infrastructure / services

Many hotels located on the island of Cyprus (Protaras), Antigoni 3 in particular, attract tourists with a variety of services for a comfortable stay. For example, for a fee, in this hotel you can use dry cleaning, a safe, a medical office and laundry, as in other hotels of this category.

The infrastructure of the hotel is of a standard type with two types of pools (open and closed), a 24-hour reception, free parking and currency exchange. The building has two elevators, there is a TV and fax in the lobby, a cozy patio, a tour desk and a library.

Hotel guests can use the services of a doorman, as well as order an airport shuttle. Of course, this can not be done for free.

All public areas are equipped with air conditioning, so staying in a hotel building is always a pleasure. Tourists who need access to the network can use paid Internet / ISDN.

Entertainment and leisure activities

Hotels located on the island of Cyprus (Hotel Antigoni 3, located in the family resort of Protaras is no exception), boast of pristine sandy beaches. Private access to the sea is within walking distance of the hotel. Sun loungers, towels and umbrellas are provided to tourists for a fee, and by the pool on site are free of charge.

In addition to a beach holiday, tourists can have fun in their daily activities by participating in daily shows with music, karaoke, cabaret, bingo and quiz on stage. The hotel offers daytime and evening theme nights. Very often tourists are shown folklore performances. They are called ‚ÄúCypriot nights‚Äù in a different way. In addition, the hotel has a casino where all adult tourists can check their luck and smile of fortune.

On-site guests can not only sunbathe and swim in the pool, but also take part in sporting events and games. Here you can engage in active sports, aerobics, play volleyball and basketball. There is also a tennis court in the hotel building, where fans of this game will be able to compete with each other. In addition, there is a special room for table tennis and video games, as well as a gym, billiards and squash. In general, there is a lot of entertainment for every taste.

Hotel guests can relax by listening to live music that plays every evening, go to a disco or a movie theater.

For an extra fee, you can visit the fitness center, relax in the sauna, jacuzzi, Turkish bath or book a massage. The female half of vacationers will like a visit to the Hercules Wellness Center, where you can put yourself in order and undergo a number of cosmetic procedures.

Hotel guests who do not forget about work even on vacation can use the conference room for negotiations. If desired, vacationers can book a guided tour of the vicinity of Protaras. For the work of the guide and transfer you will need to pay separately.
