Entry-level smartphone Alcatel One Touch Pixi First 4024D: reviews, features, reviews

An affordable and stylish budget phone with very good technical specifications, as for an entry-level gadget - these are all Alcatel One Touch Pixi First 4024D. Owner reviews about him, his strengths and weaknesses will also not be overlooked in this review.





Equipment

Given the accessory to entry-level gadgets, the Alcatel One Touch Pixi First 4024D has pretty good equipment. Reviews only indicate that it lacks a memory card, case and film, which would protect the front panel of the device. The rest of the list of equipment for this phone includes:

  • The smartphone itself.
  • 1450mAh removable battery.
  • An entry-level stereo headset.
  • Charging adapter.
  • User's manual.
  • Warranty insert.
  • Device certification notice.

Design, screen and ergonomics

The Alcatel One Touch Pixi First mobile phone is made in the form of a monoblock. The diagonal of its TFT screen with touch input is 4 inches modest by today's standards. But the resolution is 480x800. The density of dots on the screen is equal to 233ppi in this case, and it is problematic to distinguish a single point on the display surface with the naked eye. Under the screen, as expected, three main control buttons are immediately displayed. Above, above the display on the front panel, the speaker is displayed. The peephole of the second chamber is located in the same place. The control buttons (provide device lock and volume control) are grouped on the right side of the device. On the upper edge are the device‚Äôs wired ports - MicroUSB and a 3.5-mm audio jack. On the bottom of the gadget there is only a conversational microphone. On the back of the device there is a main camera, supplemented by a single LED backlight. There are also loud speakers.





Gadget Hardware Settings

Customer reviews of the Alcatel One Touch Pixi First highlight primarily its very modest technical specifications. The central processing unit "Spectrum 7731" installed in this device boasts the presence of 4 computing units per architecture base under the code designation A7. The clock frequency of each of them at the most intense load can increase to 1.2 GHz. For the majority of undemanding hardware resources of the device, this is quite enough. The amount of RAM in this smart phone is 512 MB. System processes in this case use about 300 MB, and the user, when solving his tasks, can count on the remaining 215 MB. Integrated storage capacity is 4 GB. For the user's needs, only 2 GB is provided, and the rest is pre-installed system software. You can increase the amount of installed memory by installing a memory card, the maximum capacity of which in this case can reach 32 GB. The main camera has a 5 megapixel sensor, and the front camera has a 2 megapixel sensor. In the first case, at a normal level of lighting, very good pictures are obtained. But the capabilities of the second camera on the front panel of the gadget will only be enough for video calls. "Selfies," obtained with its help, will not be of very good quality. A similar situation develops in this case with the so-called "avatars", which most owners of mobile devices do using the front camera. A 2MP sensor for this task is also clearly not enough and their quality will be below average. An impressive set of interfaces is supported by this device. It includes:

  • The main wireless way to receive and send information is Wi-Fi.
  • The device can function in GSM and 3G networks. In the latter case, only a SIM card installed in slot No. 1 can work in 3G networks.
  • To connect a wireless audio headset or exchange data with similar mobile devices, this smartphone is equipped with bluetooth.
  • The navigation capabilities of a mobile phone are implemented using a GPS transmitter.
  • The main wired interface is MicroUSB. Using it, you can connect your smartphone to a PC or charge the battery.
  • To connect external acoustic wire systems, the gadget provides a typical 3.5 mm audio port.

Autonomy

Given the technical parameters and battery capacity, you can be sure that one charge of the latter is enough for exactly 2-3 days of operation of the Alcatel One Touch Pixi First 4024D. Reviews also indicate that in the highest saving mode this value can increase up to 4 days. Well, in the most intensive use mode, this budget class gadget can last a day. On average, it takes about 3 hours to fully charge the battery with a complete charger.

Software component

Reviews on the Alcatel One Touch Pixi First 4024D smartphone highlight the system software of this gadget, which is based on the leading Android software platform for mobile phones. It is not necessary to expect the latest and most current version in this case of this system software. Its version has a lock number "4.4", which is the most optimal for the hardware characteristics of this smartphone.





Reviews and cost of the device

Reviews of the Alcatel One Touch Pixi First 4024D indicate primarily the very modest cost of this device. In most stores, the price of a smartphone of this model is in the range from 3600 - 4000 rubles. But if you wish, you can buy such a phone at a price of 3390 rubles. To accomplish most of the tasks, the hardware and software capabilities of this gadget are enough. There are comments on the autonomy of the device and 512MB of RAM. In the first case, the problem can be easily solved by purchasing an additional external battery. Well, in the second case, you can use the Klin Master optimization package for the Android platform and run it periodically, as the RAM is full.





Summary

A very affordable entry-level solution is the Alcatel One Touch Pixi First 4024D. Reviews highlight its value, which starts at 3,390 rubles at the moment. Otherwise, the technical parameters of this device are very, very modest, but this does not prevent it from solving practically all the tasks that are necessary today without any special problems. Well, if you need something more, then it should already be a mobile gadget not from this price niche.
