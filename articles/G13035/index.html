Oysters 7X 3G review





Perhaps the main advantage of this gadget is the price. Relative to similar devices Oysters 7X 3G costs a penny. But is the price consistent with quality? We will talk about all this in this article.

Design and specifications

As the saying goes "Meet by clothes ..." so we will begin our review with the appearance of the device. Oysters 7X 3G has a completely classic, unremarkable design. Black color, rounded edges - minimalism is evident.

Now, let's move on to the characteristics of the device itself. Oysters 7X 3G is a pretty compact gadget. Small dimensions and weight allow you to take the device anywhere. As for performance, the MediaTek MT8312 processor is on board the device , with a clock frequency of 1.2 GHz. This power is quite enough to read electronic books and perform other simple functions. An Android device is running.





The display size is pretty well matched. On the one hand, the screen is large enough to read electronic books and work with documents. On the other hand, it is quite compact, thanks to which it was possible to make the gadget more convenient, portable.

The gadget runs on an ARM Mali-400 MP GPU. Thanks to him, the tablet is able to quite quickly process graphic information. It is also worth saying a few words about RAM. On board the device about 1 GB of RAM. Pretty mediocre indicator. If you do not overload the tablet with several tasks at the same time, then it will work without lags.

The battery is designed using Li-Pol technology (lithium polymer battery). The capacity is about 3000 mAh. This should be enough for several hours of active use.

It is also worth noting that the tablet has a slot for a SIM card. Therefore, with Oysters 7X 3G it is quite possible to call, read and send SMS.

The Oysters 7X 3G also has two cameras. The camera is at 2 and the front at 0.3 megapixels.

For wireless connectivity, the Oysters 7X 3G has Bluetooth. In addition, there is an interface for USB version 2.0. Also in the case of the device you can notice the headphone output.

Well, in the end it is worth mentioning the main advantage of Oysters 7X 3G. This gadget will cost you only 2600 rubles. It is unlikely that you will be able to find a tablet cheaper.

Oysters 7X 3G reviews

Mostly users are satisfied with the purchase. Many people like the simplicity, ergonomics, low price and functionality of the gadget. However, sometimes users have questions about the firmware and updates. All this can be found on the official website of the manufacturer. Also one more small minus - for Oysters 7X 3G it is quite difficult to find a cover that would fit in size. Perhaps these are all the shortcomings that bother users. The operation of the device among buyers did not cause any complaints.

Conclusion





The Oysters 7X 3G is an excellent budget tablet. The device has mediocre acceptable characteristics. The tablet's power is enough to perform simple actions. In its price range, the Oysters 7X 3G is the best choice. Therefore, if you need an inexpensive and good tablet for simple tasks, then you should seriously think about purchasing this device. In the end, we can say that the Oysters 7X 3G fully justifies its cost.
