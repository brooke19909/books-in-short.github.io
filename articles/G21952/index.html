Some consider the location of the camp site uncomfortable: on the slope leading to Mastryukovsky Lakes, there is a significant difference between the upper and lower points - about 15 m. The complexity of the relief is especially felt by those who came to rest with their children: it is inconvenient to move from a mountain uphill with a stroller. At the camp site, as guests noted, a lot of wasps.

On the territory of the base, according to many vacationers, there is nothing interesting. Since the base is located on a slope, there can be no talk of any tennis or volleyball courts. Service, according to reviews, so-so, the administration is not particularly interested in ensuring a good rest for customers. The reason for this is that the camp site does not belong to private ownership, but belongs to the bearing plant.
