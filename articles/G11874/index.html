Hotels in the south-west of Moscow: list, addresses, reviews

Moscow is the capital of our country, a city of incredible beauty and significance. Every day it is visited by hundreds of thousands of guests, tourists, businessmen. Of course, when planning a trip, you need to take care of the accommodation in advance.

District features

Of course, the choice of hotels in the capital is huge, but in this case, choosing one option or another, you should first of all start from the goals of the trip, as well as the location. So, say, if you decide to enjoy the beauties of the city, get acquainted with all its sights, it is better to be located in the center. For a longer stay, it is worth taking a closer look at the more budget options located in sleeping areas. The south-west of Moscow is quite a profitable place in every respect. There are no problems with transport, because you can get to anywhere in the city using both underground and ground transportation. At the same time, it is relatively quiet, a lot of greenery and vegetation.

"Troparevo"

Continuing the review of hotels in the South-Western Administrative District of Moscow, I would like to mention another excellent option, located near the Troparevo metro station. The hotel of the same name is an excellent option for those who want to enjoy the beautiful view from the window and the freshness of the air even in Moscow. Hotel Troparevo is not just a hotel, but a paradise for a good rest. Comfortable rooms, a spa complex with modern equipment, a sauna and a solarium are all fully accessible for hotel guests. You can entertain yourself by playing billiards. All rooms are equipped with a bathroom with a set of cosmetics, modern furniture, appliances and wireless high-speed Internet.

The hotel has a bar and a restaurant where you can order food and drinks. If you are interested in hotels in the south-west of Moscow, ideal for negotiations and business meetings, Troparevo is definitely suitable for you, because there is a fairly spacious conference room equipped with everything you need, including a modern speaker system. If you are used to relaxing in luxury, consider yourself a special important person, you will definitely like this option. The Rusotel Hotel is what the most honored guests of the capital and foreigners choose for themselves. All hotel staff speaks two languages: Russian and English, which makes the stay here as comfortable as possible.

The luxurious and spacious rooms are decorated in a classic style and will leave a lasting impression on guests. Comfortable beds with new mattresses, a full-fledged working area, a separate bathroom with snow-white towels and a set of necessary cosmetics - this will create a paradise vacation. The hotel has an excellent restaurant where you can taste European and Mediterranean dishes.

The hotel is attractive in terms of location - just 1 km to the Annino metro station. And the view from the city is breathtaking. And all this at a price of 3,000 rubles subject to double occupancy.

Boutique Hotel "De Marie"

If you choose more vibrant areas for your stay, take a look at this option. Boutique Hotel De Marie is within walking distance of the famous and picturesque Vorontsov Park. The highest level of service is noted by all vacationers. The hotel provides ample parking, wireless internet throughout the hotel, a shuttle service, and comfortable rooms decorated in a classic style. Each of them has everything you need for a good rest: a set of furniture, bath accessories, bathrobe and towels, TV, mini fridge. In the morning, guests can enjoy a delicious and satisfying breakfast from the hotel chefs, but you can have lunch and dinner in cafes and restaurants nearby.

The cost of daily living in a hotel starts from 3500 rubles.

Address: Moscow, st. Profsoyuznaya, d. 64, bldg. 2

"Day and night"

Another great hotel option in the south-west of Moscow, namely, within walking distance from the Profsoyuznaya metro station. The windows offer stunning views of the capital, and the rooms have everything you need for a good rest. Space, luxury, comfort - this is what all guests of the Day and Night Hotel focus on. The rooms are cleaned daily, towels are changed every day in the bathroom.

This hotel in the south-west of Moscow is suitable for both travelers and businessmen, because the proximity to the metro station and ground transportation allows you to reach the desired point in the shortest time period. The cost of standard rooms starts from 4000 per day.

Royal

This is another affordable option for a hotel in the south-west of the capital. Hotel Royal is located in the building of the Prince Plaza shopping center on the 6th floor, that is, near the Teply Stan metro station. It offers spacious rooms decorated in a modern style. You can spend the night in them or spend several days getting to know the capital and solving important matters.

Each room has a bathroom with a set of towels and bathrobes, a hairdryer, a TV, a small set of dishes and a kettle. Additional services include a shuttle service to the hotel, high-speed wireless internet, laundry service, luggage storage and free parking.

You can book a room at a price of 2800 rubles per night, subject to double occupancy. A great option for those who are looking for budget housing.

Address: Moscow, st. Profsoyuznaya, d. 129A.
