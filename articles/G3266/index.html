Barcelo Bavaro Beach Hotel, Dominican Republic: overview, description and reviews

The exclusive five-star Barcelo Bavaro Beach Hotel is located on the equally luxurious Bavara Beach in Punta Cana, which is one of the top ten in the world. According to the owners of the elegant complex, you can only imagine it in your dreams. Having opened recently after reconstruction, now it is positioned exclusively as a hotel for adults.





Location

The hotel belongs to the BARCELO PREMIUM group. One of its advantages is its amazing location in a paradise of the country. The international airport is only 25 km, so the road will take a little more than half an hour, to the nearest town Higuei is 60 km, Santo Domingo is 240 km. Barcelo Bavaro Beach Resort is located on the coast and has its own access to Bavara Beach, 3 km long. It is surrounded on all sides by coral reefs, so there are practically no waves, this is the best place for swimming.





Number of rooms

The hotel is relatively new and has recently undergone renovation. The complex includes eleven buildings of three floors, a total of 589 rooms of various categories with all amenities:

  • Superior There are 288 of them, with 52 of them offering a picturesque view of the coast. Room size is 47 m2, maximum capacity is four people. The room has one king size bed or two single beds.
  • Superior Sun Deck (144), of which 27 are overlooking the beach or the garden, the first view is distinguished by the fact that a minibar with snacks and free beach towels are available.
  • Superior Ocean View Club Premium has a larger area, a terrace or a balcony with direct access to the sea.
  • Suite Club Premium is the jewel of the Barcelo Bavaro Beach Hotel (Dominican Republic). The room is located on 94 m2, has a bedroom and a living room, two bathrooms, a jacuzzi and premium club services are included in its price. The maximum number of guests is two. There are five such numbers.




Since last year, the hotel began to offer its customers Plus Room rooms, the price of which includes a one-time visit to the spa (a water therapy complex in a hot tub or shower, thermal pool, sauna or steam bath) lasting 45 minutes, free high-speed wireless Internet, discount for purchases at BWoman and Bman stores of 10%.

Supply system

On the coast of one of the most luxurious beaches in the world, favorable warm weather with soft ocean breezes always reigns. Tourists from all over the world come here to plunge into the blue waters of the ocean, to see its underwater inhabitants, to relax from the bustle of the world. The Barcelo Bavaro Beach Hotel operates under the All Inclusive system, which allows guests to fully enjoy first-class service and world-class gourmet cuisine. In addition to delicious food, the price also includes participation in all daytime activities organized by animators, access to a nightclub, kayaks and pedalos, catamarans on the beach, tennis (table and table), giant chess, billiards and much more.

health and beauty

The complex at Barcelo Bavaro Palace Deluxe features a first-class spa, one of the best in the Dominican Republic. He offers his visitors to open the health side of relaxation at sea and enjoy the full range of procedures. It has its own outdoor and thermal pools with water therapies, massage rooms, a jacuzzi, a steam bath and a sauna, as well as a modern gym equipped with the best simulators.





At the spa in the complex you are guaranteed an individual approach and a program of procedures led by qualified personnel in an atmosphere of relaxing music, scented candles and wonderful natural oils - all that is needed for a perfect vacation.

Barcelo Bavaro Beach Reviews

Having collected all the reviews of tourists who visited the hotel, we can only say one thing: this is heaven on earth. Many say that they did not even expect such a high level, but in reality the complex itself, its territory and mainly the beach are magnificent, even better than in the photographs. Particularly emphasized is its good location, close to the sea, airport, as well as the convenience of ordering excursions. The beach, according to visitors, is very clean, and the sand is white. The water is calm, there are almost no waves, however, there are often algae, and the hotel administration does not always have time to remove them from the surface.

Only at "5+" they appreciate the level of room service, their cleanliness and decor, spacious pool and food quality. Restaurants offer delicious and unique seafood dishes of the highest quality, as well as an abundance of exotic fruits.





Everyone who has visited Barcelo Bavaro Beach, says that the rest in it will be remembered for a lifetime, and most importantly, there remains a desire to return here again and again. The days of the newlyweds who have decided to hold a ceremony on the Caribbean coast will be especially bright and touching. All types of services and the highest quality wedding service at the hotel are at the disposal of everyone.
