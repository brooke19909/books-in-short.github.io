Park named after S.M. Kirov in Kirov: from the outskirts - to the center

Park them. Kirov in Kirov was founded in 1958 at a communist subbotnik, in which workers of city enterprises, school students, and Komsomol members took part. At that time there was an outskirts called the Soloviev Settlement. Fields and ravines overgrown with grass, on which local residents grazed cows and goats. And there they beat keys and springs.

Park bookmark

During the war years, the population of the city doubled, so after the onset of peaceful days, all forces were directed to the restoration of industry and housing. But the time came when people began to think about relaxation, about the improvement of their Kirov. So, on the occasion of the 60th anniversary of the October Revolution, the Kirovites began to transform the Soloviev Settlement, having begun laying the park there. Now it is the center of the city.

First named after the location, namely Solovievsky, in 1974 the park was renamed. He became the namesake of the city in honor of S.M. Kirov, a member of the Politburo.

Landscape park

Created as a recreation area for citizens living in the Leninsky district, the Kirov Park gradually developed and developed, becoming a city-wide leisure venue. Particularly large funds were invested in it in the 1970s, in anticipation of the celebration of the 600th anniversary of the city. Large-scale work has transformed the territory.

It was at this time that a cascade of ponds was built, the circus building was laid, a diorama construction project was developed and began to be implemented. To help builders, city residents often arranged subbotniks, actively transforming the territory. The area was planted with trees, flower beds and paths were arranged, benches and arbors were installed.

The circus, for lack of experience in the construction of such facilities, has become a long-term construction. But in general, the park to them. Kirov in Kirov was transformed before his eyes.
