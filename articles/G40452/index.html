Children's camp Cape of Doom: photos and reviews

The school year ends and a long-awaited vacation awaits the child. Each parent begins to think about the upcoming vacation. Fresh air, sea, hiking, sports - these are the main advantages of a good rest for the child, and most importantly - diversity. When deciding where the child will spend the summer months, you can opt for a camp and choose a more suitable option.

Some parents, of course, are very distrustful of the various children's camps, explaining this by insufficient control over the children, poor nutrition and poor organization of free time in the camps. But there are parents who recall with emotion their vacations spent in the camp, hiking, meeting new people, campfire evenings and guitar songs.



Which camp to choose?

Now it has become easier to make a choice in favor of a children's camp. The Internet has all the information you need, photos, a detailed description, reviews. And after reading the comments, you can make a fairly objective opinion and make the right choice. The main thing is not to be lazy and to study in detail information not only about the camp itself, but also about the teaching staff, their professionalism and qualifications.

Types of Camps

Camps are divided into several types. They are:

  • sanatorium type;
  • Wellness of a general orientation;
  • sports and fitness;
  • profile;
  • thematic;
  • labor.

The camps of the sanatorium type are designed for frequently ill children. Improvement is the main task for the duration of the child's stay. They come to such camps not only for the treatment of any diseases, but also for their prevention. The length of stay is 21 days. And in order to get to such a camp, you need to take a direction in the clinic and collect the necessary package of documents. Absolutely every day the child is scheduled literally in minutes: swimming, procedures, relaxation and activities.





Sports and recreation camps are designed for children who love outdoor activities. Here they are offered a huge selection of sports entertainment. A child can engage in not only his beloved and well-known sport, but also get acquainted with a completely new occupation for him. This can be rowing, and bicycle rally, and fencing. A variety of competitions, contests and activities to do your favorite thing will make your child‚Äôs vacation rich and unforgettable. In such a camp, absolutely every child will definitely find something for himself to his liking.

Profile camps are focused on combining leisure and educational process in game forms. Such specialized camps have a linguistic, artistic or computer direction. The program here is always as intense and intense as possible. This program is suitable for more diligent children who do not get tired of learning and developing.



Labor. These camps are suitable for teenagers. Here, guys can not only make new friends and have fun, but also earn extra money. As a rule, the number of days of stay is chosen by yourself, and it will be possible to arrive even for two days.

Thematic camps present a special program for children. Themes can be completely different for every taste. The plot can be based on one of the famous films, books, or it can be completely fictional. Children are constantly involved in the process of the game, and therefore they certainly will not be bored here.

Cape of Doom. Camp

Cape of Doom is a great example of a theme camp. Here, the child will feel like a real artist. The camp offers a wide range of diverse activities: rehearsals, master classes, meetings with guest musicians, acoustic evenings and concerts.

Here, the child can even organize his own group and go all the way with her from the initial level - rehearsals to the finish line - of his solo concert, surprising his fans.



Classes

One of the main advantages of this direction is the improvement of the sense of rhythm, vocal data, musical notation and other necessary components of the musical world.

The Cape of Doom camp is a wonderful place for the child to open up and test his strength on various musical instruments. The groups conduct classes on playing the acoustic guitar, electric guitar, bass guitar, keyboards and percussion instruments.

In the camp, professionals conduct lectures on the history of music and seminars to promote their own group, work on the image of growing musicians and also help to gain confidence for performing on stage in front of a large number of spectators.

Invited musicians and representatives of rock culture regularly come to the Cape of Rock children's camp, so the stay of children in the camp becomes even more diverse and interesting.



"Cape of Rock" is ready to accept everyone for the whole year. You can completely immerse yourself in the world of live music both in summer, and in winter, and in autumn.

Arriving in large comfortable buses to the camp, children find themselves in an amazing world with a warm, friendly atmosphere, where everyone is respected and appreciated.

A meeting

On the very first day of arrival, children get to an amazing concert, which is arranged by their future teachers. On the same day, children choose with whom they will be engaged throughout the shift. The selected teacher will oversee the child, and monitor his employment and involvement during the stay.

Accommodation

Upon arrival, children are accommodated at will, focusing on age, and live for 2 or 3 people in a room with all amenities. In one building lives from 40 to 50 people.

The number of children in a shift varies from 150 to 300 people in the summer months and in winter, and during off-season holidays from 50 to 150.

You can not worry about food in the camp, it is tasty and completely balanced.

Medical care and safety are provided around the clock.

On the territory of the base there are sleeping buildings, a medical center, a separate dining room, a cinema hall, sports grounds, a hall for discos and game complexes. There are also two football fields, a volleyball court, a pool and gazebos.
