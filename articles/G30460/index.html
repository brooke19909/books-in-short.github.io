Hotel Kassandra Palace Hotel 5 (Greece, Halkidiki, Kassandra). Hotel reviews and photos

The cleanest waters of the Aegean Sea, which wash the coast of the picturesque peninsula of Kassandra, attract tens of thousands of tourists every year. Which traveler would not like to be here at least once? And many manage to carry out their plan. However, you must first plan your trip. The most important point is the choice of hotel. There are a lot of them, but one of the best is considered to be the Kassandra Palace Hotel 5 *, which we will discuss now.





Location

So, the hotel is located in the resort village of Kriopigi, towering on the high eastern coast of the peninsula. It is widely known for its chic snow-white beaches, magnificent pine groves and incredibly beautiful nature.

At a distance of 90 kilometers from Kriopigi is one of the largest Greek cities - Thessaloniki. Macedonia Airport is also located there.

It is important to note that Kassandra Palace Hotel 5 * has its own sandy beach, awarded the Blue Flag for cleanliness. It is spacious and comfortable - it has sun loungers and umbrellas, and towels are provided for all guests.

Service and Facilities

The Kassandra Palace Hotel 5 * hotel has an impressive description. The declared level is 5 stars, and it is the quality of service that best confirms it.

So, on the territory of the complex there is free parking, high-speed Wi-Fi, a 24-hour reception, an exchange office, a tour desk, a left-luggage office. Upon arrival, any guest can use the concierge service. If the guests arrived early in the morning or late at night, an accelerated registration will be carried out. By the way, everyone can order a transfer from the airport.

The hotel also has dry cleaning and laundry services, where guests are also ironed. In addition to the above, on the territory you can find a mini market, shops, car rental and even a church. This is the Church of the Assumption of the Virgin, and wedding ceremonies are held in it.

Also, the hotel provides food delivery to your room and personal babysitter services. If guests do not want to take the child to the mini-club, but want to relax separately, then they can call a nanny in the apartment.





Leisure

Since Kassandra Palace Hotel 5 * is a resort hotel, nobody will be bored being on its territory. On the territory there is a spacious sun terrace and three swimming pools - a large outdoor, children's and indoor heated.

In addition, the hotel has a professional fitness center, two outdoor tennis courts, a billiard room, a sports ground, as well as rental mopeds, scooters and bicycles.

But what definitely should be given a little time, being here on vacation, is water entertainments. An unforgettable experience is guaranteed. You can go diving, snorkelling, water skiing, etc.

For the smallest

Many come to the Kassandra Palace Hotel 5 * with their children. They definitely will not be bored here, because there is a mini-club in which the atmosphere of friendliness and fun reigns. There, the kids are supervised by educators and animators who constantly come up with creative and informative entertainments for them.

The mini-club has a safe playground for games, as well as a center for water sports, where the most fascinating attractions are concentrated.

For teenagers, there is a room with board games, a tennis court and a computer corner with internet access.





SPA center

It opened in Kassandra Palace Hotel 5 * not so long ago. But everyone should visit it. All guests in a relaxing relaxed atmosphere are offered a variety of treatments and a wide range of high-quality aromatherapy products. There is also a sauna, a bathhouse, a jacuzzi, a Sharko shower and a beauty salon where they do everything - from haircuts and styling to nail extensions and eyelashes.

In the SPA center itself, you can go for a massage, sign up for hydrotherapy, try various wraps and scrubs using products based on natural ingredients. It is important to note that although the prices here are affordable, the quality of the services provided in a good sense of the word is impressive.

For business

Among business travelers, Kassandra (Halkidiki, Greece) enjoys considerable popularity . Kassandra Palace Hotel 5 * often becomes the choice of businessmen who came here with the goal of not only relaxing, but also holding an important event.

The hotel has a spacious, modern Aristotle room, which can accommodate about 400 people in a theater setting. It is equipped with a projector, flip charts, a large screen, a TV, a DVD / CD player and a recording device, a powerful speaker system, and wireless microphones. The hall is equipped with central heating and air conditioning, as well as good lighting.

In addition to the above, there are three booths for translators, a separate press room and a cozy cafe-bar, which is an ideal place for a break.

If you want to hold an event in this place, then you need to contact the hotel. In addition to clarifying the conditions, the guest will be offered the services of professional organizers.





Bars and Restaurants

All decent hotels have several good establishments on their territory, and the Kassandra Palace Hotel 5 * located on the Kassandra Peninsula (Halkidiki, Greece) is no exception. There are four of them:

  • The main restaurant. An ideal place for breakfast and dinner in an informal setting. A buffet is served here. And after sunset, the facility hosts theme nights with Mediterranean and Greek dishes.
  • Ammos Tavern. Located near the beach. Guests can enjoy a varied menu including an abundance of seafood and grilled freshly caught fish.
  • Lounge bar. A cozy and calm place where you can order any strong or soft drink, exotic cocktail, freshly ground coffee or fresh. This bar is open 24 hours a day. And they serve here drinks of both local and foreign production.
  • Beach bar. This institution differs from the previous one only in its location.

As the reviews left about the listed institutions show, a solid five can really be put for the hotel catering. The choice is large, and the quality of the dishes is on the level. There is everything - from poultry, meat and fish to salads, fruits, vegetables and sweets.

Before proceeding to the reviews of Kassandra Palace Hotel 5 *, it is worth noting the attention of several nuances that must be taken into account for each person planning to go here to relax.

So, this hotel has a loyal policy towards guests who come with children. For children under the age of 2 years, separate small beds are provided, in addition, accommodation is free for them. But before the trip it is necessary to inform the administration of the age of the children (this information can be indicated in the application for reservation of apartments).
