Hotel The Sailing Bay Beach Resort 4 * (Vietnam, Mui Ne): photos and reviews of tourists

Vietnam is becoming more and more popular with tourists every year. Thousands of travelers from all over the world flock here every year. Some come here with a sincere desire to plunge into an interesting culture and learn the traditions that the country is so rich in. At the same time, one cannot discount incredibly beautiful nature and, of course, miles of picturesque beaches.

One of the most popular resort cities is Phan Thiet. This is a place with developed tourist infrastructure, comfortable hotels and high quality service. One of the good places for spending holidays is considered the hotel complex The Sailing Bay Beach Resort 4 *.

Of course, travel planning involves finding the maximum amount of information. What do the rooms look like and what degree of comfort should you expect? Is the hotel suitable for families with a small child? Is food included in the price? How close is the beach? Answers to these questions are important for many tourists.

Location Description





The Sailing Bay Beach Resort 4 * hotel is located in the popular tourist city of Phan Thiet, in the prestigious Mui Ne. The location of the hotel complex is considered very advantageous. In just a minute you can reach the picturesque beach, and the city center with all its attractions is within walking distance.

The nearest airport is 220 km from the hotel. By the way, the hotel and travel agencies provide guests with a convenient shuttle service, but still it is worth preparing for a long trip. On the other hand, the large train station is 37 km away. There is a public transport stop next to the hotel complex.

It is worth saying that in the area there are enough shops, cafes, restaurants and even a shopping center. And you will have the opportunity to enjoy the sights that the city and its environs are so rich in.

Hotel The Sailing Bay Beach Resort 4 *: hotel description

Of course, every tourist wants to know a little more about what constitutes the territory of the hotel. The hotel complex itself consists of several multi-storey residential buildings. Here, in the building, there are restaurants, lounges, a wellness center and other entertainment facilities.

The hotel occupies a vast territory, part of which is reserved for the eternally green and incredibly picturesque garden. Here, among tall trees and vibrant colors, there are several separate bungalows that are ideal for travelers looking for peace and solitude. The central courtyard of the hotel is reserved for a pool and a large terrace. Emerald green lawns, curly bushes and flower beds planted with bright exotic flowers will delight your eye. The guests claim that the territory here is really beautiful and well-groomed, which can be considered an undoubted plus.

Dining for guests: what to count on?





One can hardly imagine a really good vacation without hearty, tasty food. What does the hotel Dessole Sailing Bay Beach Resort 4 * offer its guests? Immediately it is worth saying that the hotel has a spacious restaurant with a stylish atmosphere and a varied menu.

Guests are free to choose the food scheme convenient for them. For example, you can pay a full three meals a day or just have lunch and dinner in a restaurant. By the way, the most popular scheme among tourists is BB - only breakfast. You also have the right to completely refuse food. This does not mean that you will not be able to use the services of the restaurant, you just have to pay for food each time, according to the bill.

The guests of The Sailing Bay Beach Resort 4 * hotel speak well of local cuisine - the restaurant offers a truly large selection of international and traditional Vietnamese cuisine. You can count on light snacks, salads, fresh vegetables and fruits, several types of side dishes, different varieties of fish and seafood, as well as meat and poultry dishes. There really is plenty to choose from.

Of course, you can eat outside the hotel complex. In just 15-20 minutes you can reach the center of Mui Ne, and here at almost every step there are various cafes, bars, taverns and restaurants where you can eat for a relatively low price and, of course, taste the most famous masterpieces of Vietnamese cuisine.

Where is the beach located?





Vietnam is a country that has something to offer the traveler. There are entertainment and attractions for every taste. And, of course, many tourists arrive in the country, wanting to have a good time on the seashore. What can the hotel Sailing Bay Beach Resort 4 * offer to the guest?

Phan Thiet is a resort town, which is primarily famous for its beautiful well-groomed beaches. The hotel complex is located almost on the coast - its territory smoothly passes into the beach. According to reviews, this is a beautiful place with soft clean sand and comfortable, cleared from the stones of the entrances to the sea.

On the beach, guests can use sun loungers and hide from the sun in the shadow of wide umbrellas. A rescue team is constantly working here, and you can have a snack or a cool drink in the bar.

Water recreation: sports and entertainment

Tourists who prefer more active forms of recreation are unlikely to be disappointed. Near the hotel Sailing Bay Beach Resort Spa 4 * there is a good center for water sports. Here you can take part in a short boat trip without any problems. Travelers can try more extreme types of recreation, including banana or water skiing, parasailing.

In the center you can easily rent water transport, including catamarans, boats, jet skis. There is also a large selection of masks, fins, surfboards and other devices that will help make your stay on the beach more interesting and eventful. In addition, guests love to have fun volleyball competitions at the local sports ground.

To begin with, it's worth talking about some of the amenities that a modern traveler simply needs. For example, guests can leave things in a storage room, which is very convenient. Conducting events on site

How do guests have fun? Leisure





What kind of entertainment can guests of the Dessole Sailing Bay Beach Resort 4 * hotel expect? Phan Thiet is an ideal place for a beach holiday. It is quite natural that travelers spend most of their time on the seashore.

But you can have a good time at the hotel. In the courtyard there is a large pool in which water is cleaned every day. You can relax and sunbathe on the terrace. And the hotel also has a large gym with state-of-the-art equipment - guests can always keep fit.

Conditions for relaxing with a child

Many parents are interested in the question of whether it is possible to take their child with them to the Sailing Bay Beach Resort 4 * hotel. Vietnam is a wonderful place for an easy fun holiday, which is necessary not only for an adult, but also for every child. And at the hotel there are some conditions that help make family living more comfortable.

To begin with, it is worth saying that children under 12 years old live with their parents in the room for free. And in the restaurant you can choose some tasty and familiar dishes for the child. Extra beds are not delivered to the room - the hotel manager will immediately offer you to take more spacious apartments.

An undoubted advantage is the presence of a shallow on the beach - here the kids can have fun in complete safety. Unfortunately, there is no children's animation on the hotel premises. But already in the city there are lots of places where children can play, have fun, learn something new and just have fun.
