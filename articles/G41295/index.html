Architectural solution

The commissioning of the shopping center building was delayed relative to preliminary planning for three years. Despite this, the complex fully meets all modern requirements that apply to the quality of retail space. The shopping center has a professional thought-out concept and competent management.





The architectural and design solution used in the construction of the City Mall Belgorodsky complex is different from other shopping centers that work not only in the market of this city, but also in other settlements of the country. This fact is very attractive to buyers.

The mall is located at two, and in some areas at three levels. Escalators are mounted in such a way as to ensure circular movement of customers in the shopping center. Lifting equipment

The shopping center building has two panoramic and seven freight elevators. In addition, 2 escalators and 4 travelators work to move customers.

Power supply

The Mall City Belgorodsky shopping and entertainment complex uses the most advanced technologies to keep the life support of the complex up to standard. This also applies to power distribution systems. The main elements of the solutions used are the dry transformers used in the complex , manufactured by Schneider Electric. They can not only reduce energy losses, but also occupy a small space in the attached substation.

Based on the devices of the Schneider Electric company, it is possible to dispatch the entire power distribution system and alarm signals to the console. This allows the engineering staff to quickly respond to network problems.

When installing equipment in the shopping center, modern electric meters were installed. They allow you to transfer data directly to the control panel of the entire building.

Air cleaning systems

Air conditioning and ventilation existing in the shopping center are based on the concept of redistribution of heat fluxes. This is an energy-saving modern approach. The central air conditioners, which were purchased from the Rosenberg company, use recycling and recovery systems. This equipment can significantly reduce energy costs. When designing the building, the installation of thermal pumping units was provided. These devices redistribute the excess heat generated in the building, directing it from one room to another to heat water or air.

Automation system

In the City Mall Belgorodsky shopping center, TA Controls controllers are installed. These are the latest generation devices using Longwalks technology. The system is used to manage the engineering communications of the entire shopping and entertainment complex, and it does this automatically. This equipment allows you to reduce or completely prevent possible losses in the event of breakdowns in engineering equipment and improve the quality of customer service.

Fire extinguishing system

When an alarm occurs, modern fine-sprayed water technology is applied . It was developed by Aquamaster. The water fog that this system creates minimizes spoilage in the event of a fire. Using this system allows you to eliminate fire even in switchboards.
