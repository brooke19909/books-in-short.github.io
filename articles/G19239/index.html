Hand-held metal detectors are in demand in any security organizations. With their help, you can quickly detect hidden cold weapons or firearms. Devices differ among themselves in the limiting frequency, supply voltage and dimensions. Some hand-held metal detectors are made with an audible indication. There are also models with vibration alert. It costs on average a good hand-held metal detector in the region of 15 thousand rubles.





How to choose a model?

To select a high-quality metal detector (manual, inspection), first of all, you should evaluate the parameter of its maximum frequency. For a good model, it will be at least 80 kHz. It is best to select models only with an audio indication. Current consumption must leave no more than 5 mA. The minimum permissible operating temperature of hand-held metal detectors fluctuates around -30 degrees. The case for all models is standardly made of plastic. However, it must have a protective coating. Permissible moisture level should be at least 70%. The device weighs approximately 600 g.
