Where to go in Ufa? Where to go in Ufa with a child?

When traveling through the cities of Russia, you should definitely visit Ufa. Here you can see historical monuments near modern buildings, as well as many more interesting places.

Where to go in Ufa? Now consider the list of places. In the center of the city, Gostiny Dvor, located on Verkhnetorgovaya Street, will delight tourists. It will impress guests with its grandeur and scale.

"Lala-Tulip" and a monument to Salavat Yulaev





The hallmark in the form of a modern mosque for Ufa was the building "Lala-Tulip", erected in 1998. Peaks of towers can be seen from almost all places in the city.

Everybody knows where to go to Ufa on their wedding anniversary. According to tradition, flowers are laid on this day at the monument to Salavat Yulaev, erected on November 17, 1967.

Friendship Monument

The symbol of the accession of Bashkiria to the lands of the Russian state is the Friendship Monument, erected in 1965 on the embankment of the White River. Now it is located on Pervomaiskaya Square. Previously, the Kremlin of Ufa was located here, and then the Trinity Church. Buildings were destroyed by fire. The Friendship Monument was built in several stages. First, in 1957 (on the 400th anniversary of accession), a plate was laid on which an inscription was carved. After 8 years, the remaining elements appeared.

Merchant House

The Art Nouveau architectural monument is the merchant house of Chernikov and Kosterin, located in the heart of Ufa. Built in 1907, it housed not offices, but wealthy merchants who visited the city. Later, the building was used as the headquarters of the White Guards of the Urals. In Soviet times, the Ministry of Agriculture, the military commissariat and other departments were located here.

Where to go to Ufa for lovers of the cultural heritage of the region? On Rasuleva street, 4 is the Aksakov Memorial House, which is a branch of the National Museum. The uniqueness of the building is that it was built of larch wood in the 18th century.

National Museum





And, of course, lovers will be interested in the National Museum of the Republic of Bashkortostan itself, which is located on Sovetskaya Street, 14. It was founded in 1864. The exhibits will tell about the life of people at different times, tell about the characteristics of the people, nature and culture. If you wish, you can visit the cinema and listen to a lecture. It will also be interesting for children.

Wondered where to go in the evening? Ufa offers many options. For art lovers, the Bashkir State Academic Drama Theater named after Gafuri and the Opera and Ballet Theater will open their doors. The latter is distinguished by the fact that Rudolf Nuriev began his professional career here. On the contrary, in the memorial complex, there is a monument to Fedor Chaliapin.

In addition to theaters and museums, there are many interesting places to go in Ufa. For example, hockey Ufa-Arena. It hosts various sports tournaments. Where can believers go in Ufa? Parishioners can visit the Cathedral of St. Sergius of Radonezh.

Intellectus

Tourists traveling with their families will be interested in places where to go with a child. Ufa offers to visit the museum of entertaining sciences "Intellectus". In its halls you can learn physics from the practical side. Here, children will be shown Tesla's lightning, sound wave, the properties of the pendulum and other fascinating phenomena. After visiting the museum, physics lessons will become one of the most beloved.

Planetarium and museums





Also, the attention of young travelers is invited to the planetarium, where you can get closer to space open spaces. At the Museum of Archeology and Ethnography, children will be interested in seeing artifacts of gold and ancient jewelry found in the mounds. You can also get acquainted with the atmosphere of the yurt and find out how the tribes lived. The guys will be impressed by a stuffed mammoth full-size and a real meteorite. The Museum of Geology will attract young naturalists. Real wild animals will be able to see live. To do this, you should visit the Forest Museum - the menagerie is located directly on its territory.

Puppet theater, water park and other places of entertainment





Where to go to Ufa to a young spectator? At the Puppet Theater, kids can enjoy watching productions based on the plot of folk tales. The performances are voiced both in Russian and in the Bashkir language. Also worth a visit is the Youth Theater named after Mustam Karim.

Among the attractions there is a Jurassic-style water park called Planet, Magical World Park, rope Gummy. Children can also have fun in the entertainment centers located in different parts of the city.

Now you know where you can have fun with friends and children in Ufa. Enjoy your stay!
