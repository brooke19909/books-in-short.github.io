Seaside Posad - a resort on the Sea of ​​Azov: health and relaxation. Primorsky Posad: recreation centers, conditions, services

With the advent of summer, many people are looking for a good place to relax. Most of the inhabitants of Ukraine prefer to go to the Sea of ​​Azov. There are many resort towns on its coast, but they are all crowded with tourists this season. And what if you want to spend your vacation calmly? Seaside Posad - the perfect solution in this situation! This settlement is located in the Priazovsky district of Zaporizhzhya region. In comparison with other resorts, the prices here are quite low, the air is environmentally friendly, and any noisy objects are almost completely absent.



Briefly about Seaside Posad

Primorsky Posad is a village with a population of not more than 500 people, located in the south of Zaporizhzhya region. Next to it flows the Domuzla River. But she is very swampy in this place. The advantage of the geographical location of this settlement is the Sea of ​​Azov. It attracts many tourists who come here on vacation. Primorsky Posad is located at a considerable distance from major cities, industrial facilities and highways. Due to this arrangement, the air here is clean and untagged.

The climate in the village is temperate continental. Summer is warm, with an average temperature of +22 ° C. In winter, there is practically no severe cold. As a rule, the temperature at this time of the year rarely drops below +6 ° C. No more than 380 mm of precipitation falls during the year. In the summer, clear weather with short-term rain showers is established in Primorsky Posad. In this area there are many healing springs.

Sea coast

Holidays in Seaside Posad are ideal for families with small children. The depth of the sea is small. Only 20 m from the coast does it reach 2 m. The sea warms up by mid-May. Typically, the temperature is around +20 ° C. By July, this figure increases to almost +30 ° C, which makes beach vacations as comfortable as possible. Already in early May, Seaside Posad opens the holiday season, which lasts until September (only 5 months).

The bottom of the sea is shallow, without sharp drops in depth. The beaches are mostly sand with a small amount of small shells. Clay cliffs are found on the coast. During a storm, they blur, giving the water a reddish tint. In order to avoid this, breakwaters made of natural stone were created along the coastline.



Infrastructure

For the convenience of vacationers, the infrastructure is developing on the coast. There are recreation centers, cafes, trade stalls and playgrounds. Thanks to them, tourists who come to these places on vacation will not be bored. Seaside Posad is visited not only for swimming in the sea, but also for healing with mineral waters and basalt mud.

The resort is only at the development stage, so the infrastructure here is poorly developed. In the center there are grocery stores, there is a pharmacy and a post office. The rest of the entertainment facilities are mainly concentrated in boarding houses and recreation centers.

Holiday Village "Fakel" (Seaside Posad)





One of the largest recreation centers in Primorsky Posad is the Fakel. Here, visitors are offered a comprehensive three meals a day, their own beach and secure parking. You can stay in one-room bungalows and rooms of standard class, superior standard, junior suite and suite. All are equipped with the necessary furniture. The suites have a refrigerator, a TV, a bathroom with hot water, which is served around the clock. Bungalows are located directly on the coast, equipped in accordance with the economy class. The recreation center "Fakel" (Primorsky Posad) for children equipped a playroom where they can do crafts and drawing with qualified teachers. There is a first-aid post and a massage room on site. The beach is equipped with sun loungers and beach umbrellas. The use of them is not included in the price, so those who wish will have to pay an additional rent.

Holiday Village "Eldorado" (Seaside Posad)





Also noteworthy is the recently opened recreation center called Eldorado. It is located in the village of Primorsky Posad, directly on the coast of the Sea of ​​Azov. It has its own beach. It offers rooms with new furniture, a TV and a fridge. The interior is made in a classic style with pastel shades. The grounds and rooms are kept clean. On the basis of payment by bank transfer. There is also a store where you can pay by Visa and Mastercard. The whole territory is guarded, there is a parking zone, Wi-Fi is distributed. For children, a playground is organized, there is a volleyball field, rental of beach umbrellas is provided.
