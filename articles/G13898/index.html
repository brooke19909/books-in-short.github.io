Specifications

Specifications Dyson DC41C Origin Extra show this device only with the best hand.





The power of the vacuum cleaner is not regulated on the body, but if you need to reset it, you can use the special button located on the handle of the device. The vacuum cleaner belongs to the usual type. Designed for dry cleaning. Sucks in at a speed of 280 watts. Equipped with motor filter and fine filter HEPA13. The device works without a bag, and all dust is collected in a container, the volume of which is 2 liters.

The control of the device is mechanical. The control system is located on the housing. In the unit there is a function of automatic winding of the cord, foot switch from the "on" - "off" position and vice versa. The cord length is 6.5 meters. The noise level of the device is 83 dB, and the range is 10.3 m. The tube is telescopic and made of plastic. There is a soft bumper. When the device overheats, the auto-off option is triggered. Operation

Instructions Dyson DC41C Origin Extra describes in detail how to use the device. When carrying out cleaning, the unit must be carried by the special handle. When using the device, you must not press the cyclone release button.

Before using the vacuum cleaner, the cord must be unwound to the red mark. After that, the device is connected to power and press the on / off button located on the case. The device is not directly regulated on the body, unlike other vacuum cleaners, so it is quite simple to turn it on. After the surface is cleaned, the device is turned off by pressing the same button as for turning it on.





The device must be turned off and brought upright:

  • before replacing or installing nozzles;
  • before removing the hose and other items for inspection.

Vacuuming should only be carried out with a transparent container installed and appropriate filters. Fine dust present in large quantities should be collected in parts. Do not vacuum up construction debris, as well as sharp and hard objects, this can lead to damage to the unit. The device must not be placed on a table or chair in order to avoid falling.

The included turbo brush, left unattended for some time on delicate surfaces, can damage them. Waxed floors can become dull when exposed to a vacuum cleaner. To change the suction power, pull the switch, which is responsible for reducing this indicator. It is located on the handle of the vacuum cleaner.

Emptying the container

The vacuum cleaner with the Dyson DC41C Origin Extra container is cleaned when the container is full to the max mark. At this point, the vacuum cleaner must be disconnected from the power supply. To remove the container and the cyclone, push the button that is responsible for removing the cyclone all the way. After this manipulation, the handle of the vacuum cleaner, the transparent container and the cyclone will detach. To completely empty the container, press the red button located on the dust container. After dust and dirt are removed, the base of the container is tightly closed until a pronounced click is heard.





All parts of the vacuum cleaner are installed back into the base. When closing the case, press on the handle to transfer the device. The closing of the device will be reported by a characteristic click.

Home appliance care

Use the Dyson vacuum cleaner in accordance with the instructions for use. It should be stored and used in a room where the air temperature does not drop below zero degrees. If the device was brought into the room with frosty air, then it must be warmed to room temperature before use. Wipe the vacuum cleaner only with a dry cloth. Do not use cleaning, lubricating, or polishing agents to care for this unit. From time to time, clean the transparent container and rinse the filter.





To clean the transparent container, the cyclone must be disconnected. To do this, click on the red button located on the tank body. After that, the base of the container will open and a silver button will appear. By clicking on it, the cyclone is removed.

The container is washed only with cold water, without the use of polishing and detergents. It must not be placed in the dishwasher.

The cyclone should be handled with care: do not put it in water and under running water, wipe it with a dry cloth or soft brush. Before installing the container back into the vacuum cleaner, it must dry completely.

The appliance contains a washing filter, which must be inspected regularly for contamination and rinsed with cold water as needed. When washing the filter, water is poured into the open end. Gradually squeeze it. This procedure should be carried out until the water passing through the filter is clean at the outlet. After washing the part, turn it over and lightly tap on any surface. Wring out. Lay on its side and let it dry properly. The filter is then placed back into the Dyson DC41C Origin Extra.

Reviews note that caring for the device does not require significant effort, and events such as washing the filter and container should be carried out no more than once a month.

Safety regulations

The Dyson DC41C Origin Extra Cylindrical Vacuum Cleaner requires certain safety precautions. It should not be given to children and people with disabilities.





The device is intended only for rooms and dry places. It can not be operated outdoors and in high humidity. It is forbidden to use a damaged device, to touch the plug and the power cable with wet hands. Repair of the device and replacement of its parts should be done only by the after-sales service.

Do not pull the cord tightly and keep it near flammable objects. After use, the cable must be wound. Do not carry the unit by the cable, also do not disconnect the vacuum cleaner from the electrical outlet, pulling it on the cord. When cleaning the stairs, the vacuum cleaner should be one step lower. Do not use the appliance for cleaning liquids and burning objects.

By observing safety rules, as well as recommendations for caring for the device, you can significantly extend the life of the unit.
