Description

In terms of general technical characteristics, the Regio slide projector is not too noticeably different from 20th-century filmoscopes. The main differences are only a few futuristic design, great ergonomics and compactness (in comparison).



The Regio slide projector has been manufactured in the People's Republic of China since 2009 by order of one company legally located in Norway. Since this is a film-type device, you can view not only new films on it, but also old Soviet films and cartoons, as well as film photographs.

The material of the outer shell of the slide projector itself is a high-quality non-toxic plastic in two colors: gray and blue. The device itself is very durable, but not shockproof, children over 6 years old can quite use it on their own. But even if it accidentally falls, it will most likely remain intact and operational. Users confirm this fact.

Equipment

The kit, in addition to the Regio slide projector itself, also includes a charger, instructions for use and a spare lamp.



Image quality (due to an old lamp) can be much lower than with a new one. The kit does not contain any small parts that a child can swallow.

Using

If you had a movieoscope in your childhood, it won‚Äôt take a long time to figure out how to use a slide projector. For the rest, an understandable instruction is written below. To start watching a filmstrip using the Regio slide projector, you need to follow a few simple steps.

  1. Find the film you need.
  2. Insert it into a special slot in the housing.
  3. Lock it by scrolling the wheel all the way.
  4. Adjust the sharpness (in slide projectors, as in filmoscopes, there is a sharpness control).
  5. Scroll through the film and enjoy watching.

Especially in order to make it easier for a child to remember how to turn on and off the Regio slide projector, the button is made large enough. It is made in red. There is a smaller button in the overhead projector, it is also red. You need to use it if you want to quickly remove the film from the slide projector without scrolling it. Since the entire operation process is very simple, the child will be able to use a filmoscope without assistance.





In order for you to change the tilt angle of the filmoscope, there is no longer any need to use foreign objects. The slide projector is equipped with a stable adjustable foot. A big plus in using the device is its noiselessness, buyers say.

Unlike previous generation filmoscopes, the Regio slide projector-filmoscope does not heat up, as there are openings for removing excess heat, which means that it does not have a noisy air cooling system that can destroy the whole atmosphere from viewing filmstrips.

The device has a passive cooling system. The Regio slide projector will be able to give you and your family a lot of joy and positive impressions that will remain in your memory for a long time. Then your child will remember these home ideas with the same kind nostalgia as you.

Specifications



The device is designed specifically for watching movies and transparencies. Filmstrips for the Regio slide projector are not included in the package, but you can buy them in the same place where you bought the device. The kit contains from 4 to 6 films, designed for children from 2 to 8 years old inclusive. But as a rule, old film Soviet cartoons are quite suitable. This is what users say.

So, the main technical characteristics:

- The manufacturer gives a factory warranty for up to 12 months. But when ordering, you can buy an additional warranty for up to 24 months.

- Possibility of autonomous work (using four batteries of type D).

- The overhead lamp has a power of 110 lumens, which is a pretty good indicator. Thanks to such a powerful lamp, the image will be bright and clear. The life of the LED lamp itself is approximately 20 thousand hours (in total).

- The size of the image itself is approximately 130 centimeters.

- It is light in weight, approximately 1.45 kilograms.
