Work: difficult but easy

When using the pair trading method (Yudin or in another way), a market participant finds correlated securities. Shares whose price is reduced must be sold, and the money received invested in other securities. This is actually self-hedging. Therefore, this logic is effective even when nothing changes in the market. It is believed that one of the main advantages of pair trading at Forts is independence from external conditions.

With a decrease in the market, stocks whose rate is falling become a source of income. If the market rises, profits can be obtained through those securities whose value is increasing.
