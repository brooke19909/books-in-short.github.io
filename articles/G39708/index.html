Cliff Beach Hotel & Spa 4 *: hotel description, reviews. Holidays in Bulgaria

Holidays in Bulgaria, not far abroad, can be very luxurious. If you stay at a hotel of the appropriate level. In such as Cliff Beach Hotel & Spa 4 *, for example. That would be the right choice. Which will provide a carefree holiday in the most comfortable conditions right on the beach.





Location

Cliff Beach Hotel & Spa 4 * is located in a town called Obzor, which is part of the Nessebar community. This is the Burgas region, to be more precise, a resort located right on the Black Sea. The hotel is surrounded by distinctive Bulgarian villages, picturesque hills and lush green vegetation. The city of Obzor has existed for more than three thousand years, and it began to be considered a resort since 1934.

People who want to combine business with pleasure - relax and improve their health, go to Cliff Beach Hotel & Spa 4 * Staying in Review is helpful for treating asthma and bronchial diseases. Service

In Cliff Beach Hotel & Spa 4 *, as befits a hotel of an appropriate level, there is everything that a tourist needs: starting with free wireless Internet, ending with a private car parking.

In the lobby, guests are greeted by a 24-hour reception desk. If necessary, guests will be issued in an accelerated / individual mode. They can leave their things in a storage room. By the way, there is also a dry cleaning and laundry service in the lobby, where clothes are also ironed.

There is a modern conference room, a business center, an office with a photocopy and fax machine. VIP services are provided, and special suites are available for honeymooners. People with disabilities are offered accommodation in apartments that are fully suited to their specific needs.

In addition to the above, a shuttle service can be provided. But if they need a personal car, no problem. The hotel has a rental point, so you can go right there.

It is also worth noting that the hotel operates the delivery of drinks and food to the room and the preparation of packed lunches with you on the road. By the way, people can find a gift shop on site, which often helps guests who have forgotten about souvenirs.
