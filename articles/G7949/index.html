Hotel Esperos Palace Hotel 4 * (Faliraki, Greece): photos and reviews of tourists

Rhodes is today known in the world as the island of the Crusader Knights, however, its history dates back tens of thousands of years. This is a real diamond of the Mediterranean Sea, which sparkles in the rays of Helios - the god of the sun. According to legend, the Aegean Sea hid Rhodes from prying eyes, and even the gods did not know about its existence. But once the waters parted, and before all appeared a magnificent piece of land, which was worthy of the gods for its beauty. This is the feeling that arises for everyone who first arrives on the island.

Tourists who come to Rhodes to relax in one of the many hotels, for example, in the Esperos Palace Hotel 4 * (Rhodes, Faliraki), flying to the island by plane, experience an aesthetic shock from the breathtaking picture: transparent turquoise sea surface, sometimes covered with snow-white foam; picturesque, beautifully indented shores forming bays; luxurious vegetation, fragrant flowers. The island is also a real open-air museum: ancient artifacts are scattered throughout Rhodes, which delight lovers of antiquity.





Resorts

There are magnificent recreation areas with developed tourist infrastructure along the coastline of Rhodes. Here, amidst lush greenery, chic hotels and hotel complexes, restaurants and taverns, discos and bars whiten. The best resorts of Rhodes are Ialysos, Ischia, Kallithea and Faliraki. The latter is home to the wonderful Esperos Palace Rhodes hotel, which annually hosts thousands of tourists from around the world, including Russia.

How to get there?

The island can be reached by air and water. However, most tourists who travel to Rhodes from Russian cities, of course, use air transport. The airport is located on the west coast, 16 kilometers from the capital. Most flights connecting the island with many countries and cities of the world are charter. There is also internal communication with the Greek capital and many other cities and islands, for example Thessaloniki, Lesbos, Sandorini, Crete, Paros, etc. As for water transport, you can get to Rhodes either by ferry or ferry boat (in Russia we call them ‚Äúrockets‚Äù).

Most of the tourists who come to the island do not stop in the capital, but go to some of the resorts, for example, Faliraki, where the aforementioned four-star comfortable hotel Esperos Palace Hotel 4 * (Rhodes) is located. Tours most often include transfers from the airport to the hotel. But if you arrived on the island on your own, you can take a taxi, however it will cost a lot, or buy a bus ticket, but you will not have a guarantee that it will take you to the door of your hotel.

There is a very good option - car rental. It will cost you from 25 euros per day to 50 (the price depends on the brand of car). In fact, if you came to Rhodes not only to enjoy a wonderful beach holiday, but also to visit the many attractions, the presence of a rented car is what you need. You do not have to buy expensive sightseeing tours, but you can just drive and go wherever you want.





Resort Faliraki

So, you bought a tour to the Esperos Palace Hotel 4 * (Kastoria is a city in which there is another hotel of this chain with the same name, but this article is not about it). So where is he located? As you already understood, it is located in the village of Faliraki. This is a small resort with a population of about 700 people, but in season, and it lasts quite a long time (from late April to late October), the population of the village increases significantly. Faliraki is located on the shores of Kefara Bay.

Holidays at this resort will appeal to tourists of all ages, those who travel alone, young couples craving romance, and even numerous families who come to the island with a large crowd of children. Everyone here will find what he likes best. So, for example, for young people thirsting for noisy nightly entertainment, there are many clubs, discos, bars, etc. For kids - a water park, slides, attractions, a zoo. Fans of shopping can go to large shopping centers or look into small boutiques of branded clothing. You probably know that many Russian tourists come to Greece in order to buy a chic coat of local production at an affordable price. However, acquiring a mantle in Faliraki is not practical.

Beaches of Faliraki

What attracts tourists to this resort? Well, firstly, here, without exaggeration, the most organized beaches on the island, and throughout Greece. Thanks to this, the 3-4 km long Faliraki coastline, divided into several segments, was awarded the EU‚Äôs Blue Flag. The infrastructure of all beaches without exception is perfect. All of them have restaurants, taverns, full beach equipment, clean bathrooms, first-aid posts, kiosks, playgrounds for various sports, children's playgrounds, etc. The vast majority of these beaches are municipal, and only rare are private property. There is also a separate area for nudists.

Esperos Palace Hotel 4 *: general

This magnificent hotel was built in 1985. However, in 2009, it was reconstructed. After that, the hotel received 4 * category and became one of the best among many resort hotels. Today, tourists who come to rest on the shores of the Aegean Sea, can receive service at the highest level. By the way, this is not a single hotel, but one of the members of the Esperos Palace chain. Rhodes, in particular the village of Faliraki, was chosen by the leadership of this hotel chain as one of the most convenient, environmentally friendly places in all of Greece.





According to the brand concept, the interior of the Esperos Palace Hotel 4 * hotel includes elements of aesthetic luxury that are selected with the perfect taste, due to which travelers have the impression that the administration has taken into account all the little things that can provide them with the necessary conditions for a comfortable stay. The complex includes restaurants and bars, a swimming pool, a SPA center, a sports zone, etc. And the water park and zoo located in the immediate vicinity will make the rest of young tourists rich and memorable. By the way, this hotel is famous for organizing various solemn private or corporate events, including weddings, anniversaries, conferences, etc.

Location

Esperos Palace Hotel 4 * is located at the address: 85100, PO Box 202, Faliraki, Rodos, Greece. The distance from Diagoras Airport is about 16 km, from the island's capital - 12 km, and from the center of Filiraki - 2 kilometers. A bus stop is 50 meters from the hotel entrance. ‚ÄúEsperos Palace Rhodes‚Äù is located on the first line on the municipal beach, which means that entry is free for everyone. By the way, for hotel guests, unlike unauthorized visitors, beach equipment is also provided for free.

Number of rooms

Esperos Palace Hotel 4 * is a rather large hotel. It has 248 comfortable rooms in the following categories:

  • Standard with garden or sea view.
  • Comfort overlooking the sea.
  • Suite.
  • Deluxe




Accommodations

Each of the rooms at the Esperos Palace Hotel, regardless of category, is decorated with elegant taste and sophisticated aesthetics. In the design, gentle tones are used that act on the guests peacefully and provide them with maximum comfort. Everyone can choose a room in accordance with their taste, with the concept of convenience, as well as depending on the size of the wallet. For future vacationers: there is not a single room that cannot provide you with coziness and comfort. The quality of service and living conditions promise you a truly comfortable stay in all respects on the island of Rhodes.

Description of rooms standard

The double rooms, which overlook the sea surface, are magnificent, correctly planned and spacious rooms. Three guests can be accommodated here. These standard rooms are slightly more expensive than rooms with garden views. However, it‚Äôs worth it, because for the insignificant surcharge you will have the opportunity to see the amazing picture of the sea every morning.





All standard rooms are equipped with a balcony overlooking either the expanse of water or the garden buried in exotic colors. All are equipped with a comfortable bathroom with shower, scales, hairdryer, make-up mirror, all toiletries, towels, bathrobe and slippers. The rooms have air conditioning and central heating, as the resort operates throughout the year. There is satellite TV, a direct line telephone, free internet, a safe and, of course, a refrigerator, a minibar, which is filled on request (for a fee).

Among the rooms of this category are non-smoking. By the way, a bottle of wine or champagne awaits everyone arriving on the day of check-in.

Comfort rooms

These rooms are simply ideal for those who like to relax in comfort, and also prefer a delicate turquoise color in the interior. They are located on the upper floors of the Esperos Palace Hotel 4 *. From windows the magnificent view of the sea opens. If you settled in such a room, then you will certainly be offered a number of additional services for complete comfort. Unlike standard rooms, there is a separate bathroom. In addition to toiletries there are cosmetics. The rooms have a video player, a set for making tea and coffee, etc.

These rooms can also be served in the evening. On the day of arrival in the minibar, new guests will expect a cold bottle of champagne and mineral water.





Suites and Suites

How do the numbers of these two categories differ from the above? This is a true paradise for people with high demands on comfort. Firstly, the rooms are more spacious than standard ones, and secondly, the furniture is distinguished by sophistication and convenience. The room has original decor elements that will brighten up your stay in the room, and incredibly beautiful sea views open from the windows. Bathrooms have both bathtubs and showers. Everything else is the same as in the comfort rooms.

There is another subcategory - Master Suites. Their decor combines wood, glass and leather. All this together forms a beautiful design that pleases the eye. These rooms consist of two double rooms, connected by a door. They also have a jacuzzi, and of course, a shower, 2 toilets, a dining table and kitchen utensils.

Hotel infrastructure

The Esperos Palace Hotel has a conference room with a capacity of 1000 people. It is equipped with the latest technology, along with it operates a business center (Wi-Fi, fax, photocopy, printer, etc.). There is also a banquet room in which, as a rule, various celebrations are held: weddings, anniversaries, presentations. On the territory of the hotel there is a supermarket, souvenir shop, currency exchange, car rental, parking, laundry and dry cleaning, first-aid post, beauty salon, where you will be provided with hairdressing services, manicures and pedicures. Naturally, all this must be paid separately. If necessary, you can also use the services of a nanny. In a high-class SPA center, tourists are offered massage services, various wraps (chocolate, for example), aromatherapy, etc.

Here is such a service in the hotel Esperos Palace Hotel 4 *. Guest reviews indicate that all services are of the highest quality.

Nutrition

Guests at the Esperos Palace at the hotel are fed more than delicious. For guests, the Ambrosia Restaurant serves international cuisine. There is also an excellent lobby bar, designed for a pleasant pastime, relaxation. Zephyros Tavern offers original Greek vodka snacks. Once a week, the hotel hosts traditional Greek evenings with folk music, dancing, national cuisine and drinks.





Sports activities

For all those who prefer an active holiday, there is a wonderful pool. There are also tennis courts, minigolf, ping pong, billiards, a gym, a children's playground.

Beach

The beach at the hotel is municipal and simply magnificent, although it is not sandy. As already noted, hotel guests enjoy some benefits on the beach. For example, beach towels, sun loungers and parasols are provided free of charge. But water entertainment will have to pay.

Esperos Palace Hotel 4 * (Rhodes): guest reviews

The ratings of those who have already managed to stay at this hotel are different. Some people like food, but some people think it is monotonous. There are people who are grateful to the staff for providing comfort, while others consider some team members to be unscrupulous. Nevertheless, almost everyone is satisfied with the excellent location, as well as the cleanliness of the hotel. There are no complaints about rarely cleaned rooms or infrequently changed towels and bedding. Guests also share their vivid impressions of traditional Greek evenings. By the way, some guests complain about the lack of a menu in Russian. The administration explains this by the fact that guests from Russia are not so frequent here.
