Cyprus Villages Traditional Houses 3 *, Cyprus: description, terms of stay, reviews

Location

Cyprus is a very unusual place. With distinctive streets, architecture and attractions that create a special atmosphere here. In some places even medieval. And a striking example of this is the Cypriot village of Tochni. It is there that the specified hotel is located. The village of Tokhni is a very unusual stone city, in which the production of decorative facade stone was once actively conducted. This fact determines the extraordinary appearance of the village.

The hotel is hard to miss. The hotel complex is a stone house, surrounded by greenery and exotic vegetation. They are located on a hillside. The territory of the complex is spacious, cozy and well-groomed. Guests strolling through it may have the impression that they are in their own village, located inside the town.

The nearest beach is 200 meters away. But you need to go down to him. 7 kilometers from the complex, by the way, is the popular governor's beach. Larnaca Airport is about 35 km away. Not so far, so the location can be called convenient.





Service and Facilities

The stone houses look medieval in an original way, but, as you know, the exterior is deceiving. The complex has everything that is necessary for the tourist - high-speed Wi-Fi, an ATM, a car rental point (parking is also nearby), a souvenir shop, laundry and dry cleaning services, as well as a shuttle service.

Since the area is quite specific, the hotel staff organizes the rental of not only cars, but also bicycles and even horses. Taxis are also better to call at the reception - it's cheaper, there is a special tariff. By the way, the staff speaks German, Greek and English.

What else is there in the hotel?

In addition to the above, Cyprus Villages Traditional Houses 3 * also has its own restaurant, sun terrace and a small swimming pool, the cleanliness and order of which is regularly monitored. It also organizes fishing trips on bicycles and horses, which are especially popular in this area. And also in Cyprus Villages Traditional Houses 3 * there is a small wellness SPA-center with a sauna - it will definitely appeal to lovers of relaxation.

The area is not full of entertainment. Nearby there are several shops and taverns, the epicenter of active life - in Larnaca. But, by the way, people are sent here in order to enjoy the original atmosphere, amazing nature and silence.

What do tourists say?

Now we can note the comments that people who visited Cyprus Villages Traditional Houses 3 * leave about this complex. Reviews are different - there are both positive and negative. But here it is very important to understand that this resort is not for everyone.

Those people who deliberately went to Tochni to this complex to enjoy the originality, unique atmosphere and the feeling that they are in a medieval setting remain satisfied. General impression
