Uninterruptible power supply Ippon Smart Power Pro 1000: description, features and reviews

Ippon is pretty well known to so many users. First of all, thanks to its high-quality uninterruptible power supplies. Since 2002, the manufacturer has been developing and supplying various models of UPSs to the Russian market, which allow for reliable and stable operation of computers and other equipment. It is about one of these models - the Ippon Smart Power Pro 1000 - that will be discussed today.

Introduction

So, Ippon has been producing uninterruptible power supplies for the Russian market since 2002. At the moment, the product line has more than 10 variations of three different types. The Ippon Smart Power Pro 1000 is a Line-Interactive UPS. This means that this model has a built-in stabilizer that can regulate the output voltage.





In addition, the uninterruptible device allows you to connect up to four devices at the same time, which, of course, is a plus. As for battery life, it can reach 30 minutes, but here it all depends on the load. Well, let's look at the UPS in more detail.

Contents of delivery

Ippon Smart Power Pro 1000 is sold in a standard medium sized cardboard box. The packaging design is quite attractive, everywhere there is an uninterrupted image, the main features of the model and technical specifications are indicated.





As for the configuration, inside the box, in addition to UPS, you can find a warranty card, a brief user manual, two network cables for connecting various equipment, for example, system and monitor. There is also a two-way USB cable, one network cable for connecting power to the UPS itself, a pair of RS232 cables, which are already outdated, but are still found on older PCs. The telephone cable with RJ-11 plugs (this is especially for those who have Internet connection via ADSL) and a disk with special software completes the package.





Appearance

The appearance of the UPS Ippon Smart Power Pro 1000 is quite simple and attractive at the same time. The materials that are used here are thick and very high-quality plastic with a nice matte finish. By the way, I would like to immediately say about the weight of the device: a bare UPS, without cables and everything else, only with batteries installed, weighs about 9 kg.





On the front side of the uninterruptible one can find the company logo of the company, two buttons, several indicators and additional icons that indicate different things. The largest button is responsible for turning on the UPS, and the smaller one allows you to enable or disable the sound signal that will be given when the battery level is low, when the lights are turned off, and many other situations. Disabling sound notification is highly discouraged so as not to miss anything.





Further, the lower indicator shows the status of the voltage in the network: if everything is normal, then it lights up in green, if the voltage jumps or the UPS switches to battery operation, it will blink. 4 more indicators that follow indicate the battery level if the battery is running, or the load level when working from the network. The topmost indicator will light up only if it becomes necessary to contact a service center. For example, if the batteries become unusable, the upper indicator will turn red.





On the back side you can find a fan that is needed to remove heat. It does not always work, but only when there is a need. There are also 4 output connectors for connecting devices, 1 connector for a network cable, 1 RS232, 1 USB, 2 RJ-45. There are 2 more connectors for external devices, but they work under the stabilizer. If suddenly the voltage in the network is not stable and deviates more than 25% of the nominal figure, then the power supply with these connectors will be lost. Well, the last thing on the back is a small fuse box.

Specifications

It's time to get acquainted with the technical characteristics of the Ippon Smart Power Pro 1000. Here they are:

  • The type of UPS is interactive (line-interactive).
  • Power 600 watts.
  • Output power 1000 VA.
  • Input voltage 165-275 V.
  • Number of outlets 4.
  • The number of additional outlets 2.
  • Voltage adjustment - yes, automatic.
  • The stabilizer is.
  • Protection against short circuit, overload, telephone line, Internet.
  • Additional connectors USB, RJ45, RS232.
  • Number of batteries 2.
  • The type of battery is lead acid.
  • Battery capacity 7 Ah.
  • Operating time max. 30 minutes.
  • Charging time 22h.

UPS-uninterruptible has pretty good characteristics, but in general they are comparable to similar competitors.

IDB at work

There are no problems connecting the UPS Ippon Smart Power Pro 1000. Everything is extremely clear and simple. The most important thing is to connect the network cable and check how charged the battery is. Recommended charge level - not less than half.





Now that everything is connected, you can check the operation of the Ippon Smart Power Pro 1000. Since the uninterruptible power supply has two stages of stabilization - one to increase and one to decrease - you need to check the operability of each of them. To do this without additional special equipment is not so simple, but nonetheless real. With increased voltage ups copes well, a distinct click is immediately heard. With a decrease in voltage, the situation is absolutely the same. The UPS very well balances the power to the desired level.

Battery life

The Ippon Smart Power Pro 1000 battery allows you to work offline for up to 30 minutes. It all depends on the load. For example, with a standard 20-inch monitor and a system unit of the following configuration: 500 W power supply, 4-core processor, video card with add. powered, HDD 3,500 GB, the uninterruptible battery worked for about 9 minutes from the network, which is generally good. At the same time, the indicator of consumption, judging by the indicators, amounted to approximately +/- 300W.





The battery charging time for 100% was about 24 hours, which, in fact, is a standard indicator.

Battery Replacement

As for replacing the Ippon Smart Power Pro 1000 battery, there will be no problem. Replacement is only necessary if the upper indicator lights up. If this happens, then you can buy 2 branded batteries in Ippon partner stores and make a manual replacement, while disassembling the case. Or you can go to a special workshop if you yourself are reluctant to mess around.

Cost and reviews

Buying a model in black Ippon Smart Power Pro 1000 black at the moment is possible for about 6800-8000 rubles, although there are also offers above 10 thousand.





Reviews of this model show that the uninterruptible is not without flaws. In particular, users note a strong noise from the fan, noise during battery operation, which is emitted by a low-frequency transformer, a long charging time and poor customer support on the Internet. The rest of the model is not satisfactory.

Finally

Ippon Smart Power Pro 1000 is a pretty good uninterruptible power supply, which can easily ensure stable operation of your equipment, despite the bad voltage. Two batteries when turning off the light will allow, slowly, to finish work in the system, and a smart display will always show all the necessary information. Yes, it could not do without cons, but for a very reasonable price and good characteristics, they are not so significant.
