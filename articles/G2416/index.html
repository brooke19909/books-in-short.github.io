Palm Beach 3 * (Kos / Greece): photos, prices and reviews of tourists

The island of Kos, whose name is translated from Greek as "crab", is romantically referred to as "island of dreams" and "floating garden". It is famous for its beautiful beaches, covered with golden sand and surrounded by an abundance of greenery, as well as numerous attractions. But, despite the excellent location, beautiful nature, at all the resorts of the island of Kos, and in particular in Psalidi, very affordable prices.





The main occupation for Russians coming here to relax is savoring Greek cuisine, visiting amazing places and, of course, swimming and sunbathing. However, on the beaches of this paradise you can not only lie idly while taking sun baths, but also engage in windsurfing, kayaking, diving and many other water activities.

Psalids

This resort delights tourists primarily with its superb gardens surrounding the coastal area. The town organically merges with the natural groves of nature. Along almost the entire fantastically rugged coastline with its many bays and capes, many hotels have been built with different levels of comfort. And one of them is Palm Beach Hotel 3 *.



Description

This hotel is just fifty meters from the beach, located in the sandy bay of Psalidi. The resort center, where vibrant nightlife boils, is only twenty minutes walk from it. The traditional Greek village called Astomatos is only fourteen kilometers away. Here you can taste delicious fish caught a few minutes ago in the waters of the Aegean Sea.

The territory on which Palm Beach 3 * (Kos) is located is small. However, there is no free space not planted with surprisingly beautiful flowers or palm trees. Clean paths, nooks under the shade of trees, benches, signs, lighting devices installed everywhere - all this was done to make tourists comfortable not only in the rooms, but also in the fresh air.

Infrastructure



Palm Beach 3 * (Kos), positioned as an inexpensive budget hotel, located a stone's throw from the sea. Despite the low level of stardom, it has everything that is included in the infrastructure of more prestigious hotels. Residents can exchange currency in the main building, cash out money, give clothes to the laundry room, and things to the left-luggage office, which is especially convenient for those tourists who, having passed a room, are waiting for a transfer. The hotel has a cozy lobby, TV room, internet cafe. At the reception you can book a depository safe for expensive items, order a taxi or rent a car or bicycle, as well as arrange a transfer. Here you can reserve a table in the restaurant.





Prices and rules of the hotel

Palm Beach 3 * (Kos), like all other hotels, has pre-established canons that operate on the territory. Check-in time is noon, but this does not mean that guests arriving at any other time will not be served. Clearance, subject to availability, lasts only a few minutes. With true Greek hospitality, tourists are given room keys, a memo of all the services provided at the hotel, and a map of Kos. Greece is rich in sights, and tourists need to know about it.



The cost of living in a double room in the summer starts from six hundred dollars for seven nights with the concept of BB food, while it varies depending on the season, occupancy and availability of charter flights.

Hotel accommodation

Palm Beach Hotel 3 * (Greece) is a small budget hotel. It consists of a group of bungalows two floors high, built in a Mediterranean rustic style. The hotel offers its guests to enjoy a quiet holiday in one of the seventy-three rooms. Simple and unpretentious atmosphere is well thought out and rational.

Each room has comfortable and necessary furniture, which the owners acquired relatively recently. All rooms on the second floor have balconies. Those on the ground floor have terraces. The rooms are equipped with manually controlled air conditioners, there is a telephone, a music center. On the floor are quality ceramic tiles.

Upon request, a TV, an iron with an ironing board, and an additional folding bed and bedding are provided. The combined bathrooms have showers, a hairdryer, and also everything that is necessary for hygiene: gels, shampoos, soap. All of these toiletries and towels are updated daily.



Disabled rooms at Palm Beach 3 * (Kos) are not available. Check in with small pets in advance with the manager. All public areas and rooms can be provided with internet access for a fee.

Nutrition

As with most hotels on the island, in Palm Beach 3 * (Kos), residents are offered a different food concept: only breakfast, half board, full board. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served buffet style in the restaurant.

A rustic snack bar, whose tables are set in the fresh air, took care of the food of tourists. Every day for breakfast you can try more than twenty varieties of dishes, as well as mouth-watering muffins, marmalade and fruits. Snacks and a variety of salads, a large assortment of desserts - this is the restaurant's menu for dinner. In addition, those who wish can use the a la carte service form . Tables must be reserved in advance at the hotel reception.

The hotel has a stylish cocktail bar where you can quench your thirst and have a bite to eat. The institution works almost around the clock.



Beach

Guest reviews

Many of those Russians who have already visited Palm Beach 3 * (Kos) speak of the hotel as a great place, providing an opportunity to relax in Greece in comfort, and at budget prices. Most tourists are happy with the fact that there is a lot of greenery on the territory.

Guests are satisfied with the food and facilities offered. They gratefully respond about the staff. In addition, many people really like the location of the hotel, as well as the animation and a large selection of entertainment on the beach.

The only drawback noted by some vacationers is the lack of a refrigerator in the room, since the proposed food concept suggests that residents will have to purchase some products, especially for travelers with children.
