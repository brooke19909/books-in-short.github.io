Therapeutic profile of the sanatorium "Sochi"

Primorsky (Sochi) accepts people with various diseases of the musculoskeletal system, cardiovascular and peripheral nervous systems. In addition, a urologist, gynecologist, dermatologist, therapist, and nutritionist work in the sanatorium. There is even a dental office. Treatment begins with a diagnosis. Therefore, there is a laboratory at the sanatorium where you can conduct a blood and urine test, undergo an ultrasound scan, gastroscopy and other body tests.

The therapist will prescribe the necessary procedures to the guest, and the dietitian will develop the right nutrition system. The medical building of the Sochi joint sanatorium has a full medical base. Guests can take courses in a variety of bathtubs, including from Matsesta springs, inhalations, irrigation, showers, mineral-based mud therapy from Pyatigorsk Lake Tambukan. By balneological treatment, the potential of the health resort is not exhausted. Experienced masseurs work here, and apparatus physiotherapy is also used. There is also a gym of physiotherapy.

Those who came to Sochi for a beach holiday and general well-being have the opportunity to buy a ticket with payment for breakfast only. And many tourists recommend doing just that. Beach

According to tourists in the reviews, the Primorsky building of the Sochi sanatorium is closest to the sea, on the first line. Just a couple of meters - and you are on a pebble-sand beach. This section of the coast is fenced, belongs to the sanatorium and unauthorized persons are not allowed there. The beach has a length of 500 meters, which allows all guests of the health resort to find a place in the sun or, if desired, in the shade.

Since people come to the sanatorium for treatment, they sunbathe under the supervision of a nurse. In addition to the first-aid post, there is also a lifeguard service. The beach is well equipped, therapeutic sleep is possible under the sound of waves. However, it will be boring for ordinary guests. Numerous water activities are at their disposal.

SPA-center

"Seaside" in the sanatorium "Sochi" (Sochi) welcomes guests all year round. When the Black Sea is stormy or its temperature does not allow swimming, the complex offers its guests a heated indoor freshwater pool. They have a Finnish sauna, a Russian steam bath, an oriental hammam, an individual capsule, a relaxation room. For a fee, guests can soak in pearl, hydrogen sulfide baths, try the effectiveness of healing mud, and relax under the skillful hands of massage therapists.

Documents

If you want to undergo a course of treatment in a sanatorium, and not just relax by the sea, then, in addition to your passport, you need to take other important papers with you. First of all, it should be a medical policy. It is also important to have a referral for spa treatment from your doctor. When you check in, you must present a ticket (voucher for the settlement). If a child is traveling with you, it is necessary to capture a birth certificate, a certificate of vaccinations, a certificate of the sanitary-epidemiological environment and a medical policy.
