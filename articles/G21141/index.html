Sights of Dzhubga: what to see during a vacation on the Black Sea?

Dzhubga is one of the most popular resort villages in the Krasnodar Territory. Every summer a huge number of holidaymakers come here from all over Russia. During the holidays, vacationers manage not only to swim in the sea and enjoy the beauties of the local nature, but also to visit various excursions. What to see in the village, which sights of Dzhubga are considered the most interesting?

Water park "Dzhubga"



In the vicinity of the village there are a lot of natural and historical sites, the inspection of which will give incredible impressions. But nevertheless, the story about the sights of Dzhubga should start with one of the most modern complexes, which every tourist who dreams of visiting the area wants to visit. The village has its own water park, named after the village. It covers an area of ​​20 hectares and can accommodate 2,000 guests at a time. The water park has attractions for adults and children, the territory is landscaped and ideal for relaxing and creating beautiful photographs as a keepsake. If you believe the reviews of tourists who have already been here, it will not be boring to spend the whole day in the center of water entertainment.

Water cascades



One of the most famous attractions of the Tuapse district is Gebius Falls. They are notable for their height (more than impressive for this region). The cascade looks quite picturesque and includes only six waterfalls. The water coming down from the mountains is crystal clear. Bzhidsky waterfalls below Gebius, but you can swim in them. During the walk, tourists will also see the natural cup of “Love and Reconciliation”, into which you can enter and wash off all your sorrows and negativity, as well as have a wonderful rest after a hot day. Nearby are the "desire trees", near which everyone asks for the most intimate. If you are interested in the sights of Dzhubga and the surrounding area, be sure to visit Colonel's Waterfalls. The route during which you can appreciate all their beauty is quite long and difficult. But, despite this fact, cascades of various heights and shapes are regularly visited by a large number of tourists.

Ancient dolmen



There are a lot of ancient religious buildings in the Krasnodar Territory. However, the dolmen located near Dzhubga is special. According to experts, he is more than 5000 years old. The construction is made of stone slabs, has three tiers, one of which today is almost completely gone underground. But here a unique stone courtyard has been preserved, which was once used to limit space during religious ceremonies. If you want to see all the most unusual sights of Dzhubga, be sure to include the dolmen in your excursion program. This burial structure is considered the largest in the entire Caucasus.

Museum "Forest Miracles"

If you drive a car along Chornomorskaya Street in Dzhubga, your attention will probably be attracted by a large dinosaur sculpture. A sign with a mysterious inscription hangs on his neck: "Miracles of the forest." In fact, the stone lizard only invites you to visit the exhibition of wooden sculptures. The collection is private, today it contains more than 1200 sculptures created by the hands of the talented master Gzheljak A. If you mark interesting sights of Dzhubga on the map, do not forget to add the address of the museum to the list: Chornomorskaya street, house 8.

Other interesting places and objects in the village and its environs



The main park of the village is a favorite place for tourists to relax and walk. Here you can see exotic plants and take interesting photos as a keepsake. But there is one truly special among all the trees - this is the relict oak of Hartvis, which is at least 400 years old today. Just imagine what he saw during all this time?

What else to see while relaxing, are there any other interesting sights in the area ? Tuapse and Dzhubga are popular resorts of the Krasnodar Territory, of which a variety of excursions to neighboring cities are offered. But in the pursuit of impressions it is not necessary to go far. Dzhubga has its own lighthouse, it may not look very impressive, but it can easily boast of an interesting and long history. The construction was created in 1954. The first lighthouse at this place was installed back in 1898. Relaxing in the village, you can visit the picturesque Inal Bay. It is located five kilometers from Dzhubga. It is not only remarkable for its beauty, but also for its natural blue clay. Many here bathe and smear with clay, it is believed that these procedures can cure many diseases and contribute to rejuvenation.

Concluding the story about the sights of Dzhubga, I want to say once again that the surroundings of the village are very picturesque. Enough here and various tourist attractions for every taste and budget. Come to rest in Dzhubgu, and you will not regret it!
