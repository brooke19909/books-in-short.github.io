Spain, Costa del Sol: photos and reviews of tourists about the resort

Huge popularity is gaining more and more holidays in Europe. One of the most attractive countries for tourists remains a colorful and sophisticated Spain. Today we will get acquainted with the southern part of the country - the Costa del Sol.

General information

In the very south of the country is a picturesque and sunny coast. Spain, Costa del Sol - the region of the autonomous community of Andalusia, which also includes the settlements of Malaga, located on the coastline.

The coast stretches as much as 300 km. Sandy beaches, slightly diluted with cliffs, which sometimes approach the very edge of the Mediterranean Sea, have picturesque views and a pleasant atmosphere. There are all conditions for a good and comfortable stay. People with any income will be able to find a suitable option.

A distinctive feature of the region is a high average annual temperature of +19 degrees Celsius. But it is worth noting that the cold Atlantic current crosses coastal waters, which is why the water temperature is about 20 degrees above zero. And only in the midst of summer, in July, it can warm up to 23.

Nevertheless, the coast is quite warm and stable weather, 320 sunny days a year. It has a longer swimming season than anywhere else in the country.

The capital of the coast

Malaga is a city and a municipal center, which is part of Andalusia. The city is characterized by an amazing microclimate, which exists thanks to the rocks surrounding it. There are so many sunny days that it seems as if it never rains.





If you do not tolerate the heat, then it is worth remembering that in summer the average air temperature is +30, and the maximum can be higher than 40. In autumn and spring, the climate becomes milder and more pleasant. In the evenings, when it becomes easier to breathe and you can relax from the beaches, it is worth visiting the historical part of the city. Unfortunately, now it is not too large, and one day is enough to see all the sights.

Tourists are lucky enough to see one of the oldest theaters, the history of which began in the 1st century BC. e., and an amazing example of Arabian architecture is the Alcazaba Palace, built in the 9th century. Walking through the labyrinth of narrow courtyards, you can see strange plants, amazing flowers and many fountains.

There is everything to get acquainted with the culture of the country and find out what it is - Spain. Costa del Sol offers a great opportunity to relax and learn a lot.

Torremolinos

In the early sixties there was a real tourist boom. And the first to receive massive flows of people was Torremolinos due to its proximity to the Malaga airport. But today, various tours (Costa del Sol, Spain) offer relaxation in this beautiful city. Cozy hotels from three to four stars, located on the first line, are always ready to receive guests. The proximity of the airport is not noticeable to residents - no noise, because all aircraft land from the other side.

If you look from the sea on the coast of Torremolinos, you can visually divide it into 2 parts: eastern and western. Between them lies a rock.

If you prefer lively beaches, easy hustle and bustle, then Bahondillo will suit you, otherwise you will need Playamar not far from it. It is quite calm and not too crowded here even at the very peak of the season. It is worth noting that this place is perfect for families with children and people who do not differ in swimming skills. The entrance to the water is quite gentle and long.

Whichever option you choose, know: all the beaches are urban, so you do not need to pay for visiting them.





Benalmadena

The next city after Torremolinos is located close enough - so much so that it is difficult to pinpoint the border. Benalmadena looks like a huge balcony hanging over the Mediterranean Sea. This is a beach resort and a mountain resort.

The city center is presented in the original Andalusian style - white stone houses, narrow curved streets. Everything here is in order, always clean and comfortable.

Many eminent people choose holidays in Benalmadena (Spain, Costa del Sol). You may notice that some residents and guests of the city prefer to travel on watercraft. A variety of options are parked at the front doors of their houses: boats, yachts, scooters.

If you are looking for luxury real estate in Spain (Costa del Sol), then this is a suitable option. On the artificially created islands are amazing and fashionable apartments. A two bedroom apartment in the area costs at least one million euros.





Fuengirola

This city is one of the best resorts that Spain (Costa del Sol) offers. Fuengirola is located 35 kilometers from Malaga.

The city is not only a resort area, but also a port for fishing vessels. It was founded by the Phoenicians, but to this day is actively working, supplying marine products to the entire coastal zone.

Experienced tourists are advised to visit the city center and enjoy the beautiful views and color of the country. Children will definitely enjoy a trip to the zoo, where various representatives of the living and plant world are gathered.

The city is an ideal place to relax in the family circle, which is confirmed by the reviews of tourists. The infrastructure is designed in such a way that time flows slowly here. Fans of shopping will appreciate modern supermarkets and large shopping centers, Cort Ingles and Miramar, known throughout the country.





Mijas

Once the city was a small fishing village. But along with the other neighbors, he began to actively develop in the postwar period. Picturesque open spaces attracted not only ordinary tourists, but also real creative personalities. Inspired by the relaxed atmosphere and pleasant lifestyle, the famous artist Salvador Dali created several paintings here. You can take a bird's eye view of the coast by visiting one of the observation platforms.

Among other things, the city is visited for pilgrimage. In an old chapel carved into the rock, parishioners can pray to the holy face of the Virgin Mary.

Unusual and attractive for tourists (their reviews say this) can be a vacation in Costa del Sol (Spain). For example, in Mijas, in order not to bathe in stuffy cars, you can use an exotic mode of transport - donkeys. All animals are specially trained, have a neat appearance.

If you like art, then be sure to check out the Museum of Miniatures. Those who prefer a real sight - the road to fighting with the bulls.





Marbella

Many beautiful and expensive resorts open to tourists Spain. Costa del Sol, Marbella is a truly fashionable, sophisticated and fashionable place. The city stretches for 28 km along the coastline. Beautiful beaches are surrounded by a picturesque marble promenade, along which palm trees grow and the Sierra Blanca rises.

The beaches of this city are considered to be one of the best, and you can relax here comfortably and comfortably. La Concha Mountain plays an important role in creating such popularity, which, like a huge fence, encloses the city and creates a microclimate. The weather here changes very smoothly and softly. In summer, the temperature does not exceed 27 degrees, and in winter, the thermometer columns rarely drop below +15.

Guests and residents of Marbella have the opportunity to choose between paid and city beaches. Here is also located the largest sports port on the entire coast of the Mediterranean - Puerto Banus.





Nerja

Resorts of the Costa del Sol (Spain) provide a choice for people with different incomes. If you are interested in a budget option, then Nerja will be a great choice. Despite the low economic status, the city is distinguished by its friendly residents, beautiful nature and architecture. This place is chosen for solitude and relaxation in the calm atmosphere of cozy beaches and lagoons.

A distinctive feature of the city can be considered a rocky relief, which creates incredible picturesque paintings.

The main attraction of the city are caves. In one of them is stalactite listed in the Guinness Book of Records. Its height is more than forty meters and a width of more than fifteen.

Another feature of the caves is the unique acoustic data. Due to this, the International Music Festival is held here annually, which can be visited in July and August.

Estepona

Despite the huge influx of tourists and constant development, the coast of the Costa del Sol (Spain) has retained one amazing corner, filled with rustic charm and a special touch.

In addition to the beautiful beaches, the city can surprise with its incredibly picturesque National Park and the Los Reales de Sierra Bermeja Nature Reserve. It is here that the only fir forest is located throughout the country.

The climate in the city does not differ from the resorts adjacent to it: mild, subtropical with little rainfall.

For tourists, the eastern part of the city is most interesting. It has excellent infrastructure with a variety of hotels, some of which boast a five-star level.





Manilva and Torrox

Two more wonderful cities are offered to us by the Costa del Sol. Well-developed tourist base, many hotels, chic restaurants and cozy cafe-bars. There are places for walking and sightseeing: churches, ancient castles. There are places for secluded relaxation and bustling city beaches.

A visit to each of all the cities described above will give a lot of unforgettable impressions and help you enjoy your vacation. Comfortable hotels, amazing culture, incredible sunny beaches - all this is offered by Spain (Costa del Sol). Reviews of tourists about these places are filled with pleasant impressions of a successful holiday.
