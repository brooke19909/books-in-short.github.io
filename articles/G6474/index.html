Hardware

For the Nokia Lumiya 710 smartphone, the characteristics of the single-core ARM processor used are standard, as for all budget devices. It is based on 45 nm Qualcomm MSM8255 technology with a clock frequency of 1.4 GHz and Adreno 205 graphics. For the operation of the interface, making calls, sending / receiving messages, the Internet and wireless networks, processor performance is even excessive. The menu works without freezes, smoothly, responsiveness to touch is good. Nokia has always been famous for working out the interface.

The graphics chip is a cut above the predecessor (Adreno 200) and comparable in processing power to the competitor Mali 400. Multimedia content plays correctly, HD video can be watched without conversion to screen resolution. Most games can be played without fear of slowing down the system.

ARM MSM8255 belongs to the second generation (S2) Qualcomm Snapdragon processor family. With it, smartphones support DDR2 memory, HD video recording, 12 megapixel camera, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth.
