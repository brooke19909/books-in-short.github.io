Display

The smartphone screen Xiaomi Redmi Pro 64Gb received no bad reviews. Before us is a good quality display with an AMOLED matrix. Camera

This is the next thing to talk about. As already mentioned, behind us are looking at two camera eyes. Each of them is equipped with a different resolution. The top module received 13 megapixels and support from Sony. At the same time, its aperture has a value of f2.0, and the number of lenses has reached five.



The lower module has got 5 megapixels and Samsung support. It is needed in order to determine the depth of the picture.

Two modules work according to the following principle: the bottom eye analyzes the depth of the image, while the software applies the blur effect to the photo taken from the top module. This technology can be seen in the iPhone 7 Plus, but in the "apple" device it is better developed and almost never fails. The Chinese device does not always work correctly and can blur the wrong area.

In general, the pictures are good. You can capture beautiful landscapes or portraits on Xiaomi Redmi Pro 64Gb. Negative reviews affected only insufficient sharpness. Apparently, this is due to the "unfinished" technology of the two modules. But focus and white balance, on the contrary, were touted. You can record videos in Full HD resolution. During the day, the camera operates at a speed of 30 frames per second, at night this figure drops by half. You can apply the effect of the diaphragm, but it looks unfinished.

The front camera also showed itself well. With a resolution of 5 megapixels, it gives good pictures, it can even determine the age of the user, and almost accurately. Shoots videos in HD.
