Hotel Barcelo Arenas Blancas 4 * (Cuba, Varadero): Tourist reviews

Holidays in Cuba is always a vivid adventure that simply cannot leave anyone indifferent. A special atmosphere, numerous holidays and festivals, hot sun all year round and fascinating excursions - and this is not all that the amazing island of Freedom can offer its guests.

For travelers from many countries, Cuba has become one of the most beloved tourist destinations. Russian-speaking tourists could appreciate the rest here. For vacationers, just ideal conditions were created: a well-developed infrastructure, a lot of hotels and all this at very affordable prices.

Those who want to see Cuba in all its splendor should definitely visit the Barcelo Arenas Blancas 4 * , which is located right on the picturesque beach of Varadero.

Description of the complex

Barcelo Arenas Blancas 4 * - one of the largest hotels that the island boasts. The size of its territory reaches 94,000 square meters. m, the multi-storey buildings of the hotel and two-story bungalows are compactly located on it.





The adjacent area includes green spaces, paved paths, and figured pools, and places for recreation. In short, when creating the landscape, the designers worked wonderfully.

This hotel offers vacationers accommodation in the most convenient all-inclusive system. This means that tourists coming to Cuba will not have to worry about food and entertainment. The helpful hotel staff will do everything for them.

Overhaul was carried out here in 2014, which helped to significantly refresh the hotel and make it even more comfortable and attractive.

Where is the hotel located?

Hotel Barcelo Arenas Blancas 4 Varadero is located in the greenest corner of the coast in the well-known resort of Varadero. The hotel is only 6 km from the city center. There are no permanent residents in the area where the hotel is located. This is a closed resort area for foreigners.

The road to the airport takes only 20-30 minutes, so even families with small children can easily transfer the trip.

Anyone who is used to relaxing in comfort will surely be satisfied with the services offered here.





  • Registration. Travelers can check in at any time of the day or night - the reception is open around the clock.

  • ATM. If necessary, it will be possible to use an ATM to withdraw money, but there will also be no problems with paying with plastic cards.
  • Parking. Cars can be parked in a private car park.
  • The Internet. Throughout the hotel (in public places and in rooms) WI-FI is paid.
  • Car and bicycle rental. If you rent a car, moving around will be much more convenient and faster. A bike ride through the picturesque streets will also leave a pleasant impression.
  • Transfer. To make the trip to Cuba as comfortable as possible, guests can book a transfer. Hotel staff will deliver travelers from and to the airport in a business class car.
  • Dry cleaning and laundry, ironing. Services are available for a fee.
