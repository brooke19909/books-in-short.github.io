The cottage village "Berezovka" in Tolyatti is the perfect solution for those who want to change the urban environment for life in the lap of nature

Tolyatti is a city in the Samara region with a population exceeding 700 thousand people. This is a large industrial center, playing a prominent role not only in the economy of the region, but throughout the country. Many residents of other cities come here for permanent residence. Therefore, the pace of construction is not only not slowing down, but, on the contrary, increasing. Many residents, and visitors alike, prefer not to live in an urban environment, but in the open air. Such a comfortable life they can offer in Tolyatti cottage village "Berezovka."

The location of the village

Where did the developer place his project? Just a few, or rather, about 11 kilometers north of the center of Togliatti along Khryashchevsky highway.

On the same route, residents of the cottage village "Berezovka" in Tolyatti can go directly to the city center. This location provides excellent transport access to the village not only by personal cars, but also by public transport, following to the village of Vyselki or Khryashchevka.

Description of the cottage village

What project did the developer of the cottage village "Berezovka" in Togliatti want to realize? A variety of houses for a comfortable life in the country. Fans of urban conditions will be pleased with three-story apartment buildings. For those who want to combine two lifestyles, an urban environment and a country house, townhouses are presented. In addition, the developer of the cottage village "Berezovka" in Togliatti in his project planned 20 land plots for free development for those who want to live in the house on their own project.

Townhouses and apartments in houses for rent with a fine or rough finish. In the village, the predominance of precisely townhouses is observed. It is in them that all city communications and the opportunity to relax on individual land plots allocated for each apartment are combined.

Infrastructure external and internal

In addition to the variety of residential buildings, the developer also provided for the construction of all the necessary infrastructure for residents. A photo of Berezovka, a cottage village in Tolyatti, indicates that a kindergarten has already been built on its territory. So that residents do not spend time traveling to get necessary food, things or rest outside the village, a shopping complex and sports and leisure facilities were built on its territory. Leisure facilities include a park, public gardens for walks, children's sports and playgrounds, bike paths and a golf course.

The uninterrupted functioning and protection of the village is carried out by the Comfort management company.
