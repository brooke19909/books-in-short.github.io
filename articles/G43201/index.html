Blue Lake (Ufa): a unique natural monument

The nature of the Bashkir region is magnificent. Here, like nowhere else, harmony reigns, attractive and amazing to the ordinary city dweller, accustomed to the constant bustle and rush of the modern metropolis. It is even more wonderful when amazing magical places are preserved very close to cities and highways. Such natural attractions include the Blue Lake near Ufa, which will be discussed in our article.

Reservoir description

Located near the village of Novomusino of the Karmaskalinsky district and located 40 kilometers south of the capital of Bashkiria, the unique spring lake has a clear regular-round shape, which is explained by its formation in a karst funnel.

The bottom of the lake, reminiscent of a funnel, as in many reservoirs of karst origin, is dotted with beating springs. Some of them are particularly powerful. Four springs hit with such force that the water flow per second easily overcomes the milestone of 300 cubic meters.





Features

Scuba divers exploring the bottom claim that under the placer of large stones there is a passage into the karst cavity, but so far the ethno-divers have not reached this riddle. The bottom in the lake is uneven, there are several rather deep (up to 5.5 m) funnels. The presence of ice keys makes the water stably cold, and its high transparency allows you to view the bottom. Blue Lake (Ufa): bird's-eye view

Video and photographs taken at a height of several tens of meters confirm the beauty of these places. The blue lake (Ufa) in the shape of a round saucer strikes with a deep-transparent deep color. A stream flowing from the lake replenishes the Uzen River, which runs nearby, with crystal clear water. On one side, the reservoir is surrounded by low, but touchingly beautiful white limestone cliffs, overgrown with forest vegetation and creating a fabulous lake surroundings. It is not difficult to climb them, and the panorama from above to the lake and the Uzen River is amazing. Modern photographers falling into these places do not miss the opportunity to take unique and very beautiful pictures from the top of the cliff.





Separately about water

The blue clay lining the bottom of the lake gives the water a sparkling color of sky blue, and the beating keys make it crystal-ice and incredibly transparent. During the day, the water changes its color, and sometimes it seems that the lake lives its own life, hidden from the eyes of strangers. Locals are confident in the healing power of spring water, saturated with minerals and active substances of blue clay - a universally recognized natural healer. The townspeople who fall into these places tend to plunge into the crystal cold of azure water, although not everyone is ready for such a feat. Non-freezing even in the most severe frosts, a pond, like a magnet, attracts vacationers. Here it is amazing - Blue Lake. Ufa, the capital of the Bashkir Republic, is located not far from these places, which makes the lake an affordable vacation spot for its residents. Learn how to get to it.





Blue Lake (Ufa): how to get there

Proximity to the Bashkir capital determines the most optimal way. Therefore, the starting point from where they go to such a pearl as Blue Lake is Ufa. She is 40 kilometers from it. From Ufa go to the highway in the direction of Sterlitamak. Upon reaching Novomusino, immediately after the sign, they turn off and cross the village. The lake is located a kilometer from it, that is, it is almost impossible to make a mistake with the direction, since the road, although unpaved, will lead to the intended point.

It is enough just to get to the lake and by public transport. From the Ufa bus station, suburban buses run regularly to the village of Novomusino. By bus, they reach Novomusino, and then walk along the road in a westerly direction to the reservoir. The distance from the stop does not exceed one and a half kilometers.





Bashkir pearl

Non-freezing in winter and very cold even in the hottest summers, the lake attracts many tourists. And although not everyone dares to plunge into crystal-clear water, there are always a lot of lovers of diving trips. Experienced scuba divers enthusiastically describe their underwater journeys: the blue clay that strengthens the slopes and the bottom adds mystery and extraordinary beauty to the lake. Absolute transparency of water allows you to thoroughly examine every unevenness of the bottom, each protrusion, each fold. Divers claim that through the water column you can see the sky, the sun, clouds. And underwater tourists find this a special pleasure.



People come and just sit by the water, relax, take a break from working everyday life. The proximity of the big city makes it possible for many to visit these protected places with enviable regularity. You can only wish tourists not to litter the shores of the reservoir with various garbage. After all, you will certainly want to come back here. We, having told about where the Blue Lake (Ufa) is located, how to get to it and what can be seen there, emphasize once again: the natural complexes of Bashkiria are unique and inimitable, but need to be treated with care.
