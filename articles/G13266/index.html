Description of the city of Honolulu (Hawaii). The capital of the island state of the USA is the small homeland of Barack Obama

Honolulu ... The city with this original and unusual Russian ear name is the capital of Hawaii, the small homeland of Barack Obama. It is the largest in the state. The city is located on the island of Oahu, in its southern part. Honolulu is relatively small, its population is about 400 thousand people. However, there is not only the city, but also the whole island of Oahu, the number of people living here will be about 1 million people. In addition, a large number of tourists rest on the island throughout the calendar year.



Brief description of the capital of Hawaii

The state capital is considered to be a magnificent modern city, a famous tourist center. Every year millions of tourists come here. In the absence of problems with money, life on the island seems like a fairy tale. Magnificent landscapes, an incredible amount of entertainment, restaurants with excellent cuisine. At the same time, here is one of the lowest crime rates in the USA, and the economy is notable for its enviable stability.

A bit of history

If you delve into history, you can find out that people lived on the site of the current Honolulu back in the XI century. Probably, these were the descendants of travelers who moved there from other islands of Polynesia. And in the XIX century, the city has already become a major shopping center, industrial enterprises appeared in it. The United States became interested in Hawaii. With the help of the Americans, the monarchy was overthrown. It was after this that the state of Hawaii entered the United States.



Capital today

Now Honolulu is a financial, business and commercial center, which is important both as a tourist center and as a transportation hub. There are many industrial and commercial enterprises in the city. In addition, sugarcane is actively grown in agriculture. And the famous University of Hawaii is constantly conducting research in the field of astrophysics, medicine and, of course, oceanography.

When tourists come to rest in Hawaii, the capital is the first thing they can see. After all, all transport routes lie only through Honolulu, there are simply no other options. For this reason, the city's airport is one of the busiest in the United States of America. Every year, more than 20 million people use its services.

sights

The city of Honolulu boasts a number of attractions. For example, Waikiki is a resort area, there are many beaches, hotels, shops. A fun nightlife thrives on this territory; the atmosphere of relaxation and celebration is always felt. In addition, one cannot help but recall the palace of Iolani. It has four floors, and electricity and other equipment appeared there earlier than in the White House. In the entire United States, this is the only royal palace, so it is rightfully the pride of the state of Hawaii. The capital is also rich in parks. The city has a large zoo, aquarium, a number of museums.

In general, in Honolulu there is something to see and why to get a lot of unforgettable impressions.

Pearl Harbor Military Base

Near the city, in the center of the Pacific Ocean, is the largest US military base , Pearl Harbor. She became known to the world after the events that occurred at the end of 1941, when Japan, having attacked the base, gave the US a reason to enter the Second World War. Currently, a memorial complex has been created in the base area.
