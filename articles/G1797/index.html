DOSAAF: what is the Voluntary Society for Assisting the Army, Aviation and Navy?

DOSAAF abbreviation - what is it? Surely this question interests everyone who has ever come across this word. This organization celebrated its sixty-fifth anniversary in 2016. In the anniversary year, Chairman Alexander Kolmakov, who is the chairman of DOSAAF, said that by 2020 18 centers will appear in Russia, where active work will be carried out as part of military-patriotic education. In the Soviet Union, a voluntary society for assisting the army, aviation and navy performed slightly different tasks.





A look into the past

During the civil war, a new defense organization appeared in the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, called the Military Scientific Society. For six years - until 1926 - they studied the experience of the Civil War, developed methods for training soldiers. In addition, the specialists of the Military Scientific Society promoted military knowledge among representatives of the working class. At the end of July of the twenty-sixth year of the last century, VNO was renamed the Society for the Assistance to the Defense of the USSR. And six months later, he was connected to the Aviakhim, which included the Society of Friends of the Air Fleet and the Voluntary Society of Friends of Chemical Defense and the Chemical Industry. The new name - AVIAHIM-OCO - changed over time with another: "The Society for Assistance to the Defense and Aviation-Chemical Construction of the USSR". It was this organization that became the forerunner of DOSAAF. In 1948, DOSFLOT, DOSAV and DOSARM, independent organizations, were formed on the basis of society.

DOSAAF USSR: defense and patriotism

The year 1951 was marked by the merger of DOSFLOT, DOSAV and DOSARM in DOSAAF. The new organization was supposed to contribute to the defense capability of the Soviet Union, to prepare workers for the defense of their homeland.





Then DOSAAF, the decoding of which was known to every citizen of the country, performed many tasks:

  • preparing guys for difficult service in the ranks of the Armed Forces;
  • instilled in citizens a love for the Soviet army and a willingness to protect the interests of their native country;
  • engaged in the promotion of military knowledge;
  • trained personnel of technical professions - radio operators, electricians, tractor drivers and motorcyclists;
  • organized various folk lotteries.

GROWTH: brand new, tasks old

With the collapse of the Soviet Union, DOSAAF also ceased to exist. What is ROSTO - this question then worried the inhabitants of the new country. The Russian defense sports and technical organization appeared in Russia at the end of September 1991. The organization trained volunteers (not free of charge) parachuting, shooting, driving. ROSTO specialists organized trips. Under the acronym ROSTO, the organization worked until the end of 2009. And on December 7, the Government of the Russian Federation returned its historical name - DOSAAF.





DOSAAF today

The abbreviation DOSAAF, the decoding of which you already know, today is not just a word. This is one of the most important state projects! Within its framework, Russian citizens over 18 years old can learn air and radio sports, shooting from various types of weapons. DOSAAF branches can be found in almost all cities of our country!

DOSAAF membership: what is it?

DOSAAF of Russia are the true patriots of their Fatherland. Joining the organization is quite simple: you only need a desire and age over 18 years. Accept in DOSAAF and public associations. By paying entrance fees, members of the society get almost unlimited opportunities: classes in sections and circles, attending courses, training in educational institutions - all on a priority basis! Another advantage is the benefits. They are established by the regional offices of DOSAAF.





Organization Objectives

The elimination of the consequences of disasters and natural disasters, the development of sports and vocational training in the military specialties - all this is done by DOSAAF today. What is patriotic education, society experts also know firsthand. Among the state tasks facing them are work with citizens who are in reserve, flight training of cadets. Specialists of technical professions are also trained here. Particular attention is paid to the development of technical creativity. In addition, specialists of the company actively participate in the popularization of physical education.

Today DOSAAF is a real school of patriotism, valor and courage, which includes 601 educational institutions, 116 aviation and 490 sports! Each year, the company produces about six hundred thousand technical specialists. And the real pride of DOSAAF are driving schools. Professional instructors, talented teachers, classes with modern multimedia equipment, convenient race tracks and new cars - all this makes training at DOSAAF driving schools comfortable and high-quality!
