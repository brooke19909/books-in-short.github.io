Foundation History

Of course, different nations have lived on the territory of modern Adler for centuries. However, the date of foundation of the town is still considered 1869. By that time, no more than one hundred people lived on this cape. During the First World War, the nobility began to come to the coast, in evacuation. In this regard, guest houses, restaurants and other entertainment and trade establishments began to be built here. In the Soviet period, localization was affected by these places, and the village gradually began to turn into a city with multi-storey buildings, large hotels and resorts. This process does not stop to this day. Each year, under the starry sky of Adler, thousands of lights of hotel-skyscrapers are lit. So, by the new 2014, right before the opening of the Sochi Olympics, the Azimut Hotel Sochi hotel complex opened its doors to tourists. At this resort, special attention is paid to arranging the beach. After all, most vacationers come here precisely for a quality beach holiday, for water activities, sea mini-trips, etc.
