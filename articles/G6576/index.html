Material properties

Abroad, foamiran is known as FOAM EVA. This abbreviation stands for foamed ethylene vinyl acetate. Foamiran, which is sold in Russia, is mostly made in Iran, which is reflected in its name (FoamIran). Sometimes there are also products of Chinese enterprises.

Today you can buy foamiran of different thicknesses, colors and densities. Although this material has a poorly expressed porous structure, it is characterized by low water permeability, therefore, finished products are not afraid of moisture. In addition, they do not get dirty and do not wrinkle at room and low temperatures.

DIY camomile from foamiran: what you need

Making crafts from this material does not require special tools and devices. It is enough to have a pair of compasses, scissors and an iron on hand to heat parts from foamiran so that you can give them the desired shape. Some masters recommend using a curling iron, as its temperature can be adjusted.

You will also need a ruler, oil pastel and glue (a hot gun or a special composition for working with foamiran will do). You also need to have a pencil and a protractor on hand.





DIY camomile from foamiran: master class

Creating a flower is performed in the following order:

  • the contours of three circles with a diameter of 9 cm are applied to a white foamiran with a compass;
  • cut out the details;
  • on a sheet of yellow fomiaran measure a length of 27 cm;
  • cut a strip 2 cm wide intended for the manufacture of the middle of the flower;
  • fold it in half and glue along the edge;
  • on the bend, a small fringe is cut;
  • twist the workpiece for the core with a spiral, previously lubricating with a small amount of glue;
  • a circle with a diameter of 9 cm is cut out of paper and, using a protractor, cut incisions for petals on it;
  • use the resulting pattern, transfer marks to circles from foamiran and use a toothpick to mark the petals;
  • cut the petals, not reaching the core of 2 mm;
  • slightly warm the tips of the petals with an iron or curling iron and round off, like a chamomile;
  • their bases are tinted with green oil pastel;
  • the place intended for the core is cut into segments (from the center);
  • on the middle from the back, put on the first blank with petals and glue;
  • do the same with two other rows;
  • fixed with glue.




Such a chamomile from foamiran, a master class on the manufacture of which was presented to you in great detail, is suitable for decorating hairpins or a bottle of champagne. It has a flat base and is easy to stick to any surface.

How to create a bouquet

Having mastered the technique of making simple chamomile, you can begin to make a bouquet. To do this, you will need:

  • sheets of foamiran white, yellow and green;
  • wet wipes;
  • wire with a diameter of 0.7 mm;
  • teip tape green;
  • ordinary (for example, direct manicure) and wave scissors;
  • dry pastel;
  • Toothpicks
  • iron;
  • Super-glue "Moment" or a hot gun.




The process of making a bouquet

In order for a chamomile from foamiran (micron see below) to be used to create a bouquet, it is necessary that she has a stalk and leaves. Then it will be easier to combine in compositions or use when designing hairstyles. In addition, the flower itself can be made somewhat differently than the previous workshop offered. Chamomile made of foamiran made in this way will turn out to be more lively and natural. The work is performed in the following sequence:

  • make patterns for future flowers, cutting circles with a diameter of 7, 4, 4.7 and 6 cm by wavy curly scissors ;
  • make incisions towards the center so that the petals come out;
  • wrap a finger with a damp cloth and drive on a dry pastel first green then yellow;
  • tint the middle of the blanks to get the most natural chamomile from foamiran (master class, photo and information on the necessary tools and materials for making flowers without tinting using the Strip method, see below);
  • they apply the petals to the iron, and they themselves are wrapped in the way they like;
  • a strip of yellow foamiran 1.5 cm wide and 20-22 cm long is cut into small fringe, greased with glue and rolled up into a tube;
  • loop at the end of a piece of wire;
  • bend it at right angles;
  • stick on it a core of yellow foamiran;
  • holding the workpiece by the wire, apply for a few seconds to the iron so that the cores open;
  • 3 cm diameter circles are cut out of green foamiran;
  • cut them and heat the ends with a curling iron or iron;
  • cut narrow elongated leaves of different sizes.




Assembly

The master class ‚ÄúChamomile from foamiran‚Äù approaches the most crucial stage. To assemble a bunch of harvested parts, you need:

  • if there is a desire, connect two flowers in a twig, wrapping them together with a teip tape.




Strip method

The next master class ‚ÄúCamomile from foamiran‚Äù offers the creation of a flower in a different technique. The procedure is as follows:

  • take yellow foamiran and cut a strip of 7 cm in length and 1 cm in width;
  • 2 strips of 1.2 cm and 3 cm wide and 9.5 cm and 30 cm wide, respectively, are cut from the same white material;
  • cut the longest strip along the upper edge with denticles, and the shortest strip with a small fringe;
  • they heat the iron and apply all three stripes to it in turn, waiting for them to curl up and fall off;
  • they take a toothpick, drip a little glue on it and begin to wind all the workpieces onto it in turn, starting with the yellow strip;
  • chamomile petals wound with manicure scissors ;
  • break off a toothpick;
  • cut out leaves;
  • apply them to the iron;
  • using a toothpick make veins on a leaf;
  • stick the leaves to the finished camomile.
