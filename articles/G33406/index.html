CTP policy

CTP insurance is mandatory in the Russian Federation. In the Tinkoff Insurance company, the CTP insurance policy has the usual characteristics for this type of insurance: protection only in case of an accident involving another car, low cost (relative to the CASCO insurance policy). The main advantage is simplicity and ease of processing and the absence of queues, because the policy can be issued on the website or by phone with the help of a consultant.





Since the MTPL policy according to the Russian rules is valid only in case of an accident, it is possible to issue an electronic MTPL + CASCO policy at Tinkoff Insurance (JSC). At a cost it will be more expensive, but it will save the owner's money from any unpleasant situations.

Advantages due to which citizens choose to purchase an electronic insurance policy in Tinkoff Insurance (JSC) (reviews are mostly positive):

  • Online format of registration of the policy without waiting in line and loss of time.
  • Settlement of the insured event within 3 days.
  • The ability to find out the cost of the policy through an online calculator.
  • Scans of required documents can be sent via WhatsApp.
  • Policy delivery by courier.
  • Additional services (see below).
  • Favorable conditions when choosing the program CTP + CASCO.




How to apply for a policy

You can familiarize yourself with using the rules on the site how to apply for an TPL insurance (TCO) electronic CTP policy. To apply for an insurance policy, you must collect the following documents:

  • Passport or constituent documents, if the insurance policy is executed by a legal entity.
  • Certificate of registration in the MREO of the State Traffic Safety Inspectorate, PTS, power of attorney (if necessary), contract of sale, gift, etc.
  • Driver's license.
  • A document that confirms the value of the car.
  • The previous insurance contract, if the car is not new and you personally do not insure it for the first time.
  • Statement on the model.

The validity period of the Tinkoff Insurance (CTP) policy is set in the contract. It also prescribes all insurance payments that are possible, and the features of the occurrence of the insured event. If any changes occur on the items specified in the contract (for example, changing the passport of a citizen of the Russian Federation), the car owner must notify the insurer about this within a month, otherwise problems may arise if it is necessary to receive insurance payments.





How to calculate

The cost of the policy can be calculated using the site and the corresponding option "Calculate the cost" on the main page. Calculator "Tinkoff Insurance" CTP, feedback on the work and speed of which is only positive, is quite simple and understandable.

Upon entering the option, a window opens where it is necessary to select the region of use of the vehicle, year of production, make, model and type of transmission (gearbox). After that, you need to fill out the section with information about the owner: driving experience, age and contacts (name, phone and email address), which are needed in order to send a detailed calculation and the final cost of the MTPL policy to the potential insured.
