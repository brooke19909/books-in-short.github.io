Location Sochi

So, before you start a conversation about the most expensive hotels in Sochi, you need to find out more information about the resort.

The first Russian settlements in the city arose in 1838. In the same year, the Russian squadron established a fort here. Since then, the number of residents has gradually grown, and Sochi has slowly developed. Everything changed in 1917 when this town was given the title of city. After this, Sochi began to actively develop in the tourist plan, here begin to build cottages of state leaders.

The city is located at 42 degrees north latitude, which corresponds to the geographical latitude of Nice, Vladivostok. Sochi is located on the northeast coast of the Black Sea. It is worth noting that this settlement is a large economic center of the region. The economy is based on construction, trade, and tourism. In addition, agriculture is actively developing here, for example, the village of Akhintam has many tea plantations.

Sochi is located in the zone of humid subtropics. Most precipitation falls in the winter, most often in the form of rain. Summer is hot and humid. Winter is warm and mild. In the cold season, the thermometer bars on average do not fall below + 6-8 degrees. In summer, the temperature does not rise above +35 degrees.
