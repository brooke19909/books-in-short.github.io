A bit about Ajman

Ramada Hotel Suites Ajman 4 is located in the smallest and poorest emirate in the UAE. Ajman more than compensates for all these "shortcomings" due to the beautiful nature. These are kilometers of beaches with white sand, beautiful palm trees, well-groomed boardwalks for walks. In the cool hours of the day, numerous tourists and local residents hurry to the equipped areas with a barbecue. Comfortable white tables and trestle beds with pale blue umbrellas are located on all embankments.

It is because of the cozy and relaxed atmosphere that this emirate was simply created for tourists to relax. A huge number of the most beautiful hotels in the UAE have been built here, including the best hotel in the country - Kempinski. The hotel, which will be discussed in the article, is located right in the central part of Ajman. Ramada Hotel Suites Ajman 4 is located 500 meters from the beach area, where tourists are delivered free of charge by the hotel bus 6 times a day.





Ajman is interesting not only for beach holidays, but also for interesting museums, the pier of the famous dhow boats, where you can ride, looking ashore from the Persian Gulf.

Hotel Description

Ramada Hotel Suites Ajman 4 UAE Ajman is located in the heart of downtown Ajman. It belongs to the whole Ramada hotel chain, represented by institutions in many countries of the world. The hotel was built in 2008, and has been updated several times already, in 2012 and 2016. The hotel is multi-storey and has three buildings. All 15 floors with their windows look at the surroundings.

The interior of the hotel is impressive immediately, starting from the lobby. Beautiful and comfortable chairs and sofas will brighten up the expectation of visitors. At the entrance there is also an ATM and currency exchange, which is very convenient for foreign guests.





The staff of the Ramada Hotel Suites Ajman 4, according to holidaymakers, is cultural and friendly, they speak quite well several languages, including Russian and English. Any customer requests are carefully listened to and executed, even if it comes to replacing the number with another.

There are 121 such one-room rooms in the hotel. The total area is 54 square meters. m. Nicely decorated rooms have many useful and pleasant furniture items. These are bedside tables, wardrobes, a desk, a wardrobe and a clothes hanger. Room Description One Bedroom Suite

There are 246 such rooms at Ramada Hotel Suites Ajman 4. Area - 74 sq. M. m. The room consists of separate zones: a living room with a dining room, a kitchenette, a bedroom separated by an internal door. The maximum can accommodate four (3 + 1).

Facilities for families with children

For those traveling with young children, the Ramada Hotel Suites Ajman 4 in Ajman provides a free children's bed. Near the indoor pool for adults there is a shallow paddling pool for children. Parents can enjoy swimming in warm water with heating and at the same time look after the child.

You can spend time with the kids on the playground, beautifully designed.





If you want to use your free time by going on an excursion, you can leave your child in the care of a professional nanny. But for such a pleasure you have to pay separately. There are no animators at the hotel. The restaurant has comfortable high chairs for children.

Leisure

Ramada Hotel Suites Ajman 4 offers tourists a lot of sports and active entertainment, both paid and free. First, consider those for which you do not have to fork out.

The heated indoor pool is open year-round. There is a fitness room with a large number of fitness equipment, both for cardio and strength exercises.





After intensive training, you can take a steam bath in the sauna absolutely free. But there is a division by gender. Sauna separately for men and women. You can play billiards. On the sand of the beach area there is a volleyball court where you can organize into teams and play this exciting game.
