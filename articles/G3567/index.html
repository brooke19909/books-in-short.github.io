Hotels in Derbent: hostels and hotel complexes

Derbent is one of the most ancient cities of the Republic of Dagestan. This is one of those resort regions that are unknown to most of the Russians, and yet it is one of the most remarkable places in Russia. Hotels in Derbent vary greatly in cost of living. There are both cheap hostels and luxury hotels for very rich people.





Dagestan Derbent

Derbent is a resort town on the shores of the Caspian Sea. This is a real cultural center, which has preserved many attractions. This city is so ancient that it is included in the list of the oldest existing cities in the world. The atmosphere in it is incredibly special. The city can not be called modern, rather it is a real monument of architecture. Some buildings have a very respectful age. An ordinary walk can allow you to see whole layers of our and not only history. The main architectural monument is a large fortress of the Persian era. Derbent hotels are located in the most prosperous areas, however, some hostels can bring the vacationer closer to the life of citizens.





In Russia, this resort is unpopular. The reason is quite simple. Dagestan is a region of increased danger and one of the most disadvantaged subjects of the Federation. Almost no one wants to relax in Dagestan, and this reluctance in itself causes significant damage to Derbent, because any resort city develops through tourism. The same fact affects the hotels of Derbent.

The large five-star hotels that many Russians are used to are absent. This city can be called a haven for real tourists who prefer to relax not in huge elite hotels, but in small hostels, guest houses and even rent housing with the townspeople. However, this does not mean that there are no luxurious or simply large hotels.

"Europe"

A part of Europe is even in Derbent. Perhaps this is the most famous hotel in Derbent. This is a real hotel kit. You can relax here with your family or with a noisy company. It is located extremely well - just 15 minutes leisurely walk from the city center. The hotel has a very high rating at Bucking and boasts a large number of positive reviews. The minimum cost of one night in the cheapest room is 2600 rubles.

Prices in Derbent

Prices for hotels in Derbent are much lower than at the famous Russian resorts. One night in a hostel on average costs about 500 rubles, and standard rooms of larger hotels on average cost from 2000 to 3500 rubles per night. However, this does not apply to guest houses. Private houses own such houses and, as a rule, they are not designed as hotels. Living in them will cost even cheaper.





"Scarlet Sails"

"Scarlet Sails" is one of the most expensive hotels in Derbent. This hotel has everything a modern traveler can wish for. The hotel is located right by the sea. On its territory there is a private beach on which only hotel guests can relax. The hotel reviews are excellent, but only one drawback is the cost of living. The simplest room will cost 2,800 rubles per night without additional services.
