Sunny italy

This country is a favorite among many. The bright sun, warm climate, lyrical melodious speech, the smell of pasta and pizza, charming Venice - these are the associations that most people have when they hear about Italy. And these ideas are true. It is worth noting that the resort cities of Italy are in great demand.

Take, for example, Rimini. It is necessary to mention it, telling about Italian cities. The list of resorts is actually very large and vast, but Rimini is on the top lines. Picturesque islands

Italy deserves to be told in more detail about it. Capri Island is located in the Gulf of Naples, directly opposite a peninsula such as Sorrento. Picturesque caves, where the path lies across the sea, attract tourists. And of course, many people come here to enjoy the Blue Grotto - the main attraction of the island. By the way, a huge number of legends and myths are associated with this place.

Marettimo is another Italian island. It is located in Sicily. It is magnificent, mainly lovers of silence and admirers of pristine nature go here. What kind of entertainment is there? Sailing and diving are some of the best things to do when you are here. And of course, delicacies. One of the best treats here is lobster pasta.

Ischia Island - it is also worth talking about. Thermal springs - this is its main feature. A huge number of tourists from around the world seek to fly here and swim in the hot springs and try the baths of volcanic mud. And besides, there are beautiful beaches, rich flora (stone oaks, cork trees, cypress trees, olive trees), delicious cuisine and friendly locals.





Greece Attractions

Another wonderful place. One of the leading countries when it comes to the ranking of the most visited maritime states. Indeed, if you want to swim in the sea, enjoy sunbathing, soak in the spirit of antiquity, you should buy a ticket to a country such as Greece. The resort towns of this area are magnificent. What is the island of Rhodes! A legendary place shrouded in legends and myths. What about Corfu? If we talk about the most popular resort cities in Greece, then this island will definitely lead! The unique nature, secluded bays, rocky shores, picturesque mountains and plains, endless golden beaches, olive groves, citrus orchards and the famous Channel of Love (Di Amur) - this cannot but win the hearts of tourists.

In addition, in Greece you can do whatever your heart desires. Horse riding, diving, surfing, golf, sailing yachts, tennis, gourmet delicacies, aromatic coffee, wine tasting, excursions, night clubs, restaurants - there is everything that can interest a modern tourist. So it is not surprising why many people are eager to visit Greece.

Russian resorts

In our mighty country, there are also places in which to rest well. Moreover, people from all over the world come there. The most popular places are the Krasnodar Territory and, more recently, Crimea. The leader in the list of resorts of the Russian Federation, of course, Sochi is a unique city with the Caucasus Mountains, the warm Black Sea, modern shopping centers, interesting sights and much more. Snow lovers can safely go to Krasnaya Polyana in winter, and admirers of sea and sun baths from May are advised to go to Sochi and soak in the southern atmosphere.
