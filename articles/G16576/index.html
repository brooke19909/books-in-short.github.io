Volzhsky city, hotel "Akhtuba": photo, address

If you come to the city of Volzhsky, the hotel "Akhtuba" will provide you with a warm welcome. This is a great option for both tourists and business people.





Hotel Location

The Akhtuba Hotel (Volzhsky) is considered to be quite a popular accommodation option. The address of this institution: 8. Stalingradskaya Street. It is very close to the Volga-Akhtuba floodplain - a popular place for recreation. From Volgograd Airport you can get to the hotel in less than an hour by taxi. If you arrived by train, then bus number 123 will deliver you to the hotel.





Number of rooms

All guests of the city under the name Volzhsky Hotel "Akhtuba" will provide the opportunity for a comfortable stay in rooms of the following categories:

  • Executive Apartment is a luxurious room with a spacious bedroom and a bright and comfortable living room. Guests will enjoy elegant design, as well as a wide range of amenities.
  • Business Suite is one of the best rooms that the Akhtuba Hotel (Volzhsky) can offer you. Photos show a luxurious design in light pastel colors. It has a huge bedroom and a small living room.
  • Studio is a spacious room where a bedroom and a living room are combined in one room. There is also a small kitchen area.
  • Premium apartments do not have a large area, but differ in the original interior. Designer furniture and a good layout create a special atmosphere of comfort.
  • A two-room suite in total can accommodate up to 4 people. In addition to the double bed, there is also a folding sofa, which is used as an extra bed.
  • Standard rooms can be either single or double. The compact area has everything you need for a comfortable stay.
  • Hostel is the most economical option to stay at this hotel. You can stay in a double room with the possibility of sharing.




Services included in the price

In addition to accommodation, a number of services are included in the room price. For all guests arriving in the city of Volzhsky, the Akhtuba Hotel provides the following services absolutely free:

  • wireless internet both in public areas and in rooms;
  • shoe shine using a special device;
  • baggage transfer from the hall to the apartment;
  • use of irons and ironing boards in specially equipped rooms;
  • cold and hot water from a cooler located in the lobby.




Hotel infrastructure

Not only business people, but also tourists come to the city of Volzhsky. Hotel "Akhtuba" will delight guests with the following infrastructure:

  • in the business center, guests can use computer equipment, as well as cable Internet;
  • if you follow your figure, then by all means visit the gym, where there is all the necessary equipment for a full workout;
  • You can relax after a hard day in the sauna;
  • Be sure to use the massage room;
  • so that the suitcases do not take up space in the room, hand them in a luggage room;
  • the hotel has a private parking area with round-the-clock security and video surveillance;
  • several conference rooms for business meetings;
  • ATMs and terminals for paying for services with a plastic card.




Positive reviews

A fairly popular option is the hotel "Akhtuba" (Volzhsky). The guests leave a lot of positive reviews about this institution:

  • very convenient location of the hotel - the central part of the city, good transport interchange nearby;
  • a beautiful view opens from the windows of the rooms that are located on the upper floors (especially at night);
  • if there are free rooms, guests are populated without problems, without waiting for the checkout time;
  • the cost of living is fully consistent with the quality of the services provided;
  • friendly staff, communication with which brightens up all the shortcomings of the hotel;
  • The territory adjacent to the hotel is very beautiful - with fountains, greenery and a playground;
  • the room has a telephone with access to an international line;
  • reception staff honestly warn guests about the condition of rooms and offer better options;
  • good pressure of water in the shower.




Negative reviews

Any hotel has its drawbacks and shortcomings. The Akhtuba Hotel in Volzhsky is no exception. Judging by the reviews of guests, this institution is characterized by the following negative aspects:

  • old elevators that work very slowly (climb stairs faster on foot);
  • very poor breakfasts in the dining room - there is practically no choice, the products are stale;
  • the carpet on the floor in the room is very dirty (it must either be dry cleaned or completely removed);
  • many rooms do not have air conditioning;
  • standard rooms are in terrible condition, and the furniture in them just falling apart;
  • while bathing, water spills throughout the bathroom;
  • sharp corners of furniture are hazardous;
  • the bedding is very dirty;
  • Wireless Internet does not work above the second floor;
  • very uncomfortable layout of the bathroom;
  • hotel lounges are practically not lit, and therefore there is a big risk of falling and getting injured;
  • in the rooms located next to the gym, it is simply impossible to stay due to constant noise and loud music;
  • the hotel does not have a designated smoking area;
  • in winter the rooms are very poorly heated (but heaters partially save the situation);
  • due to poor soundproofing in the rooms, street noise is heard, conversations of neighbors, as well as steps from the corridor;
  • for hot water to flow from the tap, it must first be drained for a few minutes (the higher the floor, the more acute the problem);
  • despite the fact that dinner is until 9 pm, the restaurant is closed much earlier, and therefore there is a risk of remaining hungry in the evening;
  • there is a strong smell of cigarettes in the rooms and corridors (and this despite the fact that smoking is prohibited in the hotel);
  • when the hot water is turned off at the hotel, they do not recalculate downward;
  • use of car parking is paid;
  • old windows in rooms are heavily blown;
  • although the hotel has a satellite dish, there are very few channels;
  • the bathroom floor is very cold and there are no slippers.

General impression

The Volzhsky city is quite attractive for both tourists and business people. Akhtuba Hotel is a popular option for budget accommodation. The main advantage of the hotel is its location in the very center of the city. Nevertheless, there are many more disadvantages than advantages. The furniture in the rooms is very old, the plumbing is leaking, and repairs were carried out only in part of the superior apartments (which, of course, is reflected in the price). There are also some problems with the internet and hot water. That is why this option is not suitable for long stays.
