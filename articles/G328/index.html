The principle of work "Antutu"

The utility tests each individual device using special technical software. The Antutu mobile application works on the Android and iOS platforms, and it is distributed completely free of charge, you can download it from both Google Play and Aityuns.

The priority criterion for evaluation here is the performance of a 3D image or the processing speed of the same 3D model. That is, during testing, the gadget, or rather its set of chipsets, works to the limit of its capabilities, that is, the benchmark loads the device with the most complex graphic polygons. The latter are not so often and not with such intensity used in modern games and applications, but to determine the limit of capabilities of a smartphone - that's it.

The database is replenished with the help of users and service workshops who check their phones in this software.
