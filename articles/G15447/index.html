Credit card with free service and grace period: conditions of registration

Every person at least once in his life had an urgent need for money. In such cases, a credit card with free service may be useful. It can serve as a financial pillow. Read more about how and where to draw up such a financial instrument. Read on.

What's the catch?

In a competitive market, banks are fighting for every customer. Plastic cards have always been a popular product. Therefore, in addition to ordinary credit cards with a monthly service charge, free cards appeared on the market. This product has several advantages, but there are also pitfalls. This can be a percentage for the balance of personal funds in the account, and the minimum amount of monthly replenishment of the account, etc. In order not to overpay the money, you need to study the service contract in detail and not rely only on the words of the manager. Although today, banks are trying to prescribe understandable and transparent conditions so as not to lose customers and not receive negative reviews. In addition, a credit card with free annual service and cash withdrawals is provided free of charge. A financial institution spends money on the production of plastic, which means that it expects a long-term cooperation with the client.





How to issue?

Credit card with free service is provided:

  1. As a special offer for existing customers of the bank. Typically, such proposals come after registration of any other product. For example, successful repayment of an old loan. In such cases, a regular customer is offered to issue a credit card on favorable terms.
  2. During the promotion. During this period, you can issue a product on preferential terms, but for this you need to monitor the news of financial institutions.
  3. Some banks issue credit cards to all customers in a row. Free maintenance is the main advantage of such products. We will discuss them further.

Few numbers

According to statistics, in 2016 the demand for credit cards decreased. According to the Central Bank, on 01.07.15, 223.6 thousand cards were issued, of which 30.7 thousand were credit cards. As of July 1, 2016, a total of 242.4 thousand cards were issued, of which 30.3 thousand were credit cards. Nevertheless, Russians still often use this product.

Credit cards with a grace period allow customers to use the following privileges:

  • receive discounts from partners;
  • draw up a card with an individual design;
  • participate in free insurance programs abroad;
  • pay for transportation;
  • Use PayPass and PayWave technologies.




Annually, banks develop attractive conditions for plastic holders. Today, credit cards with free service and cash back allow customers not only to use borrowed funds, but also to receive a refund from purchases (up to 10%). At the same time, the conditions for granting loans from year to year do not change much.

So, in 2016, a credit card with free service and a grace period was provided on such conditions:

  • Limit - up to 590 thousand rubles.
  • The rate is up to 30%.
  • Maintenance - 500-2000 rubles., Starting from the second year.
  • Free period - up to 60 days from the date of debiting.
  • The minimum monthly payment is 10%.
  • Cash withdrawal - 7%, minimum 300 rubles.
  • Among the bonus programs, the most popular are the accumulation of airline miles and the accrual of points for any purchases.
  • 24 hour access to the account.
  • Sberbank issues instant credit cards with free annual service. In other institutions, clients have to wait at least 2 days.

Key factors

According to a survey by Profi Online Research, when choosing cards with borrowed funds, customers most often pay attention to the following parameters:

  • interest amount;
  • service cost;
  • limit;
  • the presence of a grace period;
  • the presence of deposit ATMs;
  • the possibility of repaying the loan through post offices, Russian Post.
