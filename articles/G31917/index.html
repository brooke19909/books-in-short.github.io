We make watches with our own hands

In order to make your own watch yourself, you will need any vinyl record with the required dimensions. Remove the label. It is best to take a plate with the middle of white, as the red will be quite difficult to interrupt using white.

To make a watch your own hands, you need to try to buy a mechanism in special stores or watchmakers. The standard kit should have a clockwork, hands showing minutes, seconds, hours, washer, central nut.





Prime the plate with white primer from the spray can. It is more correct to apply soil of a universal type or with an alkid content. In addition, by applying white acrylic paint and a sponge, you can paint the plate, but you need to keep in mind that during the application of soil with aerosol, the surface will be more suitable for work. At the end of this, dry the finished work.

Creating a background and a watch with your own hands, mix the paint and apply it on the surface of the plate. To dry.

Further. To make a watch with your own hands, based on a decoupage card, you need to cut a motif. To do this, smear the surface of the plate with PVA glue, wet the card, put it on the glue, do not forget to grease it with glue on top, make sure that each bubble is removed from under the decoupage card, and dry the surface with a hairdryer.





To make an electronic watch with your own hands, you can buy cards of Richelieu, Decomany companies, and cards with a base of rice paper. It is necessary to place the decalcomania sheet in a container filled with water in order to transfer the napkin to the surface and leave it for about five minutes. Next, get a sheet, let it drain water, then put a sheet on the base and translate the drawing. After the sheet is translated, with a spatula you need to get rid of air bubbles. Dry the drawing, then cover it with varnish.



Rice paper must be varnished at least three times. After that, draw a contour, stick the numbers from plastic on the watch with your own hands.

When a hole is glued in the decoupage card, it is possible to make a hole, to do this, insert scissors and turn it several times to make the diameter of the hole larger.

Next, to make a watch with your own hands, you need to insert the clockwork, insert a flat washer and tighten with a nut. In the case where the mechanism is equipped with a loop, it needs to be fixed on the back of the plate. When the loop mechanism is not provided for in the device, you can do it yourself, for this, glue two loops on the Moment glue and tie a wire or rope between them. You can use a sponge so that the arrows are better visible, paint them. Dress each arrow. Since each arrow has a different hole, the order will not be confused.

Insert battery.

It is satisfying that the manufacturing process of such a watch takes approximately four hours. You can apply a primed canvas, unnecessary discs, a wooden palette as the basis for future watches. The clock mechanism can be purchased, or can be borrowed from used watches. There is no need to talk about the methods of decorating the scenery, there are many of them.
