Fishing in Balashov on Khopre

The Khoper River is the largest left tributary of the Don. It flows through the lands of the Volgograd, Voronezh, Saratov and Penza regions of our homeland. The length of the river is 979 km. It takes its beginning in the Penza region. Hoper freezes at the beginning of the first month of winter. Ice holds on the river, as a rule, until mid-April.

About the Khoper River

The unique nature of Prikhoperye has made this waterway a favorite destination for tourists. The bed of a flat river meanders and abounds in old women. The depth of the river is 1-3 meters, in some places it reaches 6-7 meters. The bottom relief is diverse: there are also sandy areas with clay rifts, a rarity and a rocky bottom. The approach to the shore is generally difficult, therefore it is more convenient to fish, rafting on boats. The Khoper River in the 50s of the last century was navigable (from the Don to Balashov) and was distinguished by both the quality and the number of aquatic inhabitants.





In the distant 1970, UNESCO was not without reason considered Khoper the cleanest river in all of Europe. For half a century, the situation, unfortunately, has deteriorated radically. The river, in the recent past, is clean and full-flowing, heavily overgrown and shallow. The Khopra channel within the boundaries of the city of Balashov and the district narrowed almost 3 times. Attempts at cleaning Khopr in the 21st century have been undertaken repeatedly, but have not achieved the desired result. Balashov's utilities and waste from the Arkadak distillery (Arkadak is located 58 km up the Khopr) significantly pollute Khoper. Honestly about fishing in Balashov

Despite all the problems, there is fish in the river and a catch, with a competent approach, is guaranteed. In the thickets of powerful reeds you can catch: bream, carp, carp, crucian carp and ide. In open water, roach often comes across. Of the predators, you can catch pike, zander, catfish, perch and asp. Pike in Khopre is mainly large, from 800 g to 1 kg and above. This applies to the vicinity of Balashov. Since it grows to a size of one or two kilograms in old women, lakes of the floodplain and the terrace of the river, and only after that in the flood it gets into Khoper itself.

A characteristic feature of Khopr is the so-called "labor" fishing in the river Balashov. To catch the coveted large fish, you must try. Previously, there were a lot of fish in the full river Khoper, and serious trophies were not uncommon, but due to the shallowing, a large number of poachers and the influx of ordinary law-abiding fishermen with modern gear and semi-legal feeding of fish, it becomes smaller every year. But this does not mean that she is not here. It just got harder to fish.

A real fisherman is not afraid of difficulties. Difficulties add value to the catch. If you master the skills and techniques of fishing in reservoirs with high attendance of fishermen, then it is quite possible to achieve serious trophies, both in quality and quantity. You just need good gear, professional skills and a reserve of patience.

There are very beautiful places for fishing. Picturesque beaches and coastal landscapes with coniferous and deciduous forests can be admired endlessly. Fishing in Balashov is very diverse and multifaceted. The fact is that Khoper has sections with a very rapid and relatively slow course. In places with a rapid course, you can successfully catch a chub, asp and pike. Catfish are hiding in the pits, and a large pike is waiting for its prey on the brow or exits from the pits. In areas with a slowed flow, perch, scavenger or crucian come across.

Annual fishing in Balashov



Fishing on the Khoper River in Balashov is diverse and depends on the time of year. In early spring, it is good to catch roach and pike. In May, the time has come to catch a chub for various variants of the May beetle.

Moreover, you need to use a variety of gear, you can try, for example, a floating bombard.

Paid fishing in Balashov

In the case of persistent bad luck with a catch, there is always a fallback - paid fishing in Balashov on stocked ponds. There are many such ponds, fish farms that invite fishing on a fee basis. Basically, these are rented, privatized or private ponds in which fish are bred specifically for fishing by decent fishermen. However, such ponds have their own big minus: as a rule, the number and weight of the fish you catch is widely limited by the rules of the owner of the reservoir, and the maximum sizes of the caught individuals are also set. That is, if a fish smaller than the set size is caught on a hook, then the fisherman must release it back home. Let it grow further.

Also, if suddenly luck turns to face you and a good nibble begins, you cannot recklessly continue fishing beyond the limit of weight or the number of fish caught, which the pond management established. In addition, fishing time is often also limited and pre-paid.
