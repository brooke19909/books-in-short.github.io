Hotel Sesin 4 * (Marmaris, Turkey): description, rest and reviews about the hotel

The resort of Marmaris attracts vacationers with a diverse vacation. This territory has long been famous for its beauty: there are magnificent mountains around, on the slopes of which centuries-old pine trees rise.





Hidden in a cozy bay, protected from the winds, the clear water of the Aegean Sea, the hilly coast and the warm sun - everything is created here in order to forget about the daily bustle and relax with friends or family.

Hotel location

Hotel Sesin 4 * - a cozy hotel offering comfortable accommodation for guests. It is located in Marmaris - one of the most beloved resorts for tourists who annually choose Turkey as a place to stay.





Young couples with children come here, and the kids feel great. A gentle entrance to the sea is the key to a safe holiday for children who like to mess around on the beach.

If tourists rest in a noisy youth company, then they will also be interested here. The resort provides a lot of entertainment for party-goers.

Hotel surroundings

Those who are not limited to staying in their room or on the beach will find it useful to find out that Hotel Sesin is located in the heart of the spa town. It is suitable for walking, when you can visit local shops, bar street, walk to Icmeler. Nearby there are large shopping centers. In the nearest market, which is open on Tuesdays, you can buy interesting souvenirs for friends or employees, buy products of local artisans, bargain and feel the charm of an oriental bazaar.

If walking is not interesting, you can use transport. For example, in the vicinity of the hotel there are regular minibuses. You can call a taxi if you wish, but it costs a lot more.

Do you want to diversify your vacation, make it interesting and useful? Hire a bike and ride along the promenade - get a lot of positive emotions! Nearby are nightclubs, discos and bars, so a rich vacation is guaranteed.

Hotel Description

Sesin Hotel 4 * (Marmaris) was built in 1989, but thanks to constant repairs and restorations (the latter was carried out in 2013), the hotel is in excellent condition. The territory is small, but very clean and comfortable. A lot of greenery, flower beds are broken. The main (and only) residential building is a six-story building. Any questions can be asked at the reception, which works round the clock. The total number of rooms is 101, and they are focused on different tastes and requirements:

  • 97 standard rooms (18 m 2 , 3 people, bedroom + bathroom);
  • 2 family room class rooms (36 m 2 , 4 people (3 + 1), bedroom, living room and bathroom);
  • 2 deluxe class rooms (72 m 2 , 6 people (4 + 2), bedroom, living room and bathroom).

Among the proposed accommodation options there is one specially equipped for the disabled. Pets are not allowed in the rooms. The maximum number of residents does not exceed 225 people. Among the rooms in the Sesin Hotel 4 * - standard are considered the most popular, some of them are designed exclusively for non-smoking tourists.

Accommodations

The rooms are equipped with air conditioning, a telephone and a TV with satellite channels, an extra folding bed. The floors in the rooms are carpeted, a cozy panoramic glazing is provided , there is a coffee table and a couple of chairs. Each room has a balcony.





Paid services include the use of the Internet, as well as a minibar and a personal safe, which is installed in the room upon request. Cleaning is done daily, linen is changed once every 2-3 days.

Private bathrooms have a shower or bathtub and hairdryer. It is not necessary to take toiletries with you, because the rooms have everything and you do not need to pay separately. A bathrobe and slippers are provided, and towels are provided.

The rooms are equipped with refrigerators filled with groceries. This service is also paid, but only if tourists are hungry and take something for themselves.

Hotel Dining Options

A full, varied and satisfying menu. The hotel Hotel Sesin 4 * offers four meals a day on an all-inclusive basis: they are fed for breakfast, lunch, lunch and dinner. Meals are organized according to the buffet. As for dishes, hotel guests are most often offered chicken dishes, various side dishes, lots of greens, olives, melons and watermelons, fruits, yogurts, granola, buns with butter, boiled eggs and fried eggs, mashed soups, various types of cheeses.





Sometimes fish and beef dishes are added to the menu. Bars are open all day, where hotel guests will be offered free drinks (with or without alcohol) locally made. Imported drinks, fresh juices and ice cream should be purchased separately.

If you want to diversify your vacation and try dishes of Turkish, Italian or continental cuisine, we advise you to visit the restaurant. Upon request, employees can organize a gala or romantic dinner, laying a festive table near the pool.

Water recreation

The hotel is located on the second line from the beach. On the pebble-sandy shore tourists who live in the Sesin Hotel (Marmaris) can settle down, despite the fact that sun loungers are located on the beach for public recreation. For tourists living in the hotel, it is enough to cross the road and the promenade (the path is no more than 70 meters), take a free umbrella, deck chair and mattress, after which you can relax on the beach.





The beach is not very large, but those who are not resting here for the first time know that you can use places for beach holidays in other hotels. It is enough to lay a towel on an empty seat - and you can calmly sunbathe and enjoy swimming.

Extreme sports

Fans of active pastime should pay attention to bananasailing and parasailing, surfing, boating and canoeing. Various activities, including catamaran, are available on site. If you want to experience new experiences - book water skiing. Especially at the request of customers, a scuba diving school operates at the hotel, where experienced instructors will discover the secrets of diving and teach the wisdom of using diving equipment. The listed services are also classified as paid, which can be ordered at the Sesin 4 * hotel.





Marmaris is considered one of the important Mediterranean centers where interesting cruises and sea voyages are organized. Nearby is the Greek Rhodes and other interesting cities that deserve attention.

What rest is offered at the hotel

In Sesin Hotel 4 * (Marmaris) everything is perfectly thought out. If the beach holiday of the hotel is not attractive and there is no desire to leave the territory, then you can safely swim in the outdoor pool located next to the building. Just occupy your favorite sun lounger and relax surrounded by friends with a glass of a pleasant soft drink.





In the evenings, animators often work near the pool. Especially for tourists, they play the guitars or organize grandiose Turkish nights that do not leave anyone indifferent. Magnificent belly dancing and folk dances, musical evenings where melodic Turkish tunes sound.

If you come to rest with young children, then for kids there is an excellent kids club with special entertainments and games. While parents are resting, the kids are supervised by nannies and carers, play fun.

Additional entertainment and sports

Tourists who follow their figure and are used to constant sports, can visit the fitness center, where there is the necessary sports equipment and fitness equipment. Attentive instructors work here who will help you to make a schedule of classes for the rest and answer all your questions.





Fans of quiet leisure can play a game of table tennis or spend time at the chessboard. These entertainments for the guests of the Sesin 4 * hotel are completely free and are included in the price.

However, the administration made sure that the residents did not get bored and could, if they wanted, order a relaxing massage or visit a local sauna. There are also billiards and air hockey. Payment for using these services is made at a separate rate.

Benefits of staying at a hotel with children

What is attractive for tourists with children resort Marmaris? Sesin 4 * is one of the few hotels in Marmaris where children will be truly comfortable and fun. In the small children's pool, the depth of which does not exceed 0.5 m, the smallest visitors to the hotel always frolic. Adults can easily rest nearby, since a hotel employee is always on duty near the pool and looks after the kids.

Parents of young children are well aware that children simply can not live without movement. Especially for active kids there is a playground with horizontal bars, slides.





The menu at Sesin 4 * is so diverse that parents can easily choose light and nutritious meals for their children. If you will have lunch in a restaurant, then special chairs will be provided for the kids. If necessary, you can order in your room a special crib in which the baby will be comfortable and safe.

Nannies work here, there is a games room for the smallest and an Internet cafe for older children. Animators often organize beach volleyball competitions among children, where all players receive prizes.

Additional services

Frequent guests of Hotel Sesin 4 * become businessmen who come to Marmaris to conduct business negotiations with regular or potential customers. A convenient conference room located in the hotel will allow you to conduct a business meeting most productively. The use of additional demonstration equipment in the hall is for a fee. The maximum number of visitors should not exceed 75 people.

A pleasant addition to the existing entertainment and services can be considered the presence of a library where there are works by classics and contemporary authors. In addition, there is a TV room where you can get together with friends to watch movies and shows together. There is a laundry service, there is free private parking for hotel guests and their guests, you can use postal services and even exchange currency. The most important thing for the staff of the Sesin Hotel 4 * hotel is reviews of grateful tourists who managed to appreciate the local coziness and comfort.

The hotel has a doctor, whose services are absolutely free. Buying a ticket with accommodation, tourists automatically pay for the opportunity to call a doctor if such a need arises. An exception is appeals to private clinics and dentist services - for this they will require a separate fee.

The nearest airport in the vicinity of Sesin Hotel 4 * is Dalaman. Marmaris is located 97 km from it. Transfer should be ordered in advance and paid separately.

Excursions

Since Hotel Sesin 4 * is located within the city, there is a wonderful opportunity to visit the historical center of Marmaris (the old city), which has been perfectly preserved to this day. In addition, they organize trips to Lake Pamukkale, to Ephesus, Prena and Miletus.

In addition, they offer an excursion to Dalyan. This city is considered one of the most mysterious corners of Turkey, it is shrouded in legends, secrets and myths.





Outwardly, Dalyan is very reminiscent of Venice, since it is dotted with many straits and rivers. Tourists also expect an unprecedented treat - a meal of the meat of the rarest blue crab. Nearby are thermal springs and mud baths, which are also worth a visit. Extreme lovers should definitely go on a jeep safari.

In other words, if you are looking for a comfortable and affordable hotel with good cuisine, comfortable living conditions and plenty of entertainment for every taste, then you should definitely pay attention to this hotel.
