Vottovaara is a mountain in Karelia. Information, photo

The mysterious mountain Vottovaara (Karelia) is not among the traditional tourist routes. This place is covered with many secrets and legends. Numerous seids, bizarre-shaped trees, a huge stone pool, ringing silence and a mysterious, special atmosphere leave a lot of vivid impressions.



Location

The second name of Vottovaara is Mount of Death. Where is this facility located? You should look for a landmark in central Karelia, near the villages of Sukkozero and Gimola. Its height is not great (417 meters), which, however, is not so small for this region. From the point of view of researchers, Vottovaara is a mountain in Karelia, which is a large rock with a steep southern slope, which has a width of two kilometers. It stretched from north to south for 7 kilometers. The mountain is located in a remote place, surrounded by swamps and abandoned roads built in Soviet times.



A bit of history

About 9000 years ago, at the place where Vottovaara (a mountain) rises, a rather powerful earthquake occurred, after which a gigantic failure occurred. Thus, an amphitheater appeared in the center, covered with small lakes and rocks.

Scientists of Karelia consider that Vottovaara is a mountain, which is a unique geological monument. But in recent years, it has become of great interest to followers of various mystical trends. As a rule, they claim that Death Mountain (Vottovaara) is a place where evil forces are concentrated. Moreover, many magicians and sorcerers believe that the mountain is a bridge to another world.

Numerous seids that someone had erected on this earth, and gloomy swamps alternating with rocks, as well as chilling stories about shamans living on this mountain, contribute to the emergence of the most incredible legends. Often, local residents talk about allegedly seen in the UFO district.

More likely is the version that this is a creation of the ancient inhabitants of Karelia, who were aware of the unusual properties of the mountain. The slopes of the mysterious mountain are stone terraces covered with moss. Spruce and pine trees alternate here with traces of forest fires and old clearings. The peaks of the mountain are rocky plateaus covered with bizarre-shaped trees of different heights. For example, old, centuries-old pines do not grow here above two meters. And the birches are twisted as if some unknown force had done it.

Seydy

At the top of the mountain, whose area is about six square kilometers, more than 1600 stones. They are arranged in some mysterious order. Scientists involved in the study of this object believe that this is the oldest cult complex - Mount Vottovaara. The information obtained as a result of the work of numerous scientific expeditions suggests that the stones located in this territory are saids. This name was given to cult boulders or rock fragments. They are artificially isolated from the environment. In other words, the seid has clear signs of human exposure.

The densest concentration of seids was recorded on the slopes of the amphitheater, as well as on the highest point of the ridge, as well as on the slopes of the amphitheater. They are located mainly in groups of up to six pieces. The largest of them, weighing more than three tons, are installed on the "legs", more precisely on several smaller stones.



Most of these structures are located on the banks of ancient reservoirs or on cliffs. Seydi surprisingly harmoniously fit into the general relief of the mountain - a somewhat fabulous, disheveled chaos created by an ancient earthquake.

Almost every tourist is amazed at how the trees look on Vottovaara Mountain. Why do they have such a bizarre shape? Look: they are like twisted into knots. Locals explain this anomaly with cold and strong winds at the top. Nevertheless, on Death Mountain, twisted trees grow in the neighborhood of perfectly normal plants.
