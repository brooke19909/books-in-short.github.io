Alanya - the warmest Turkish city located in the south of the country, one hundred and fifty kilometers from Antalya - Russians have chosen for a long time. For children

Eftalia Splash (Alanya) by Russian tour operators is positioned as a family. Sea and beach



Eftalia Splash Resort (Alanya) has its own swimming area. To the beach ten minutes walk. It is separated from the hotel by a motorway, which you need to cross through the underpass. The beach is sandy and pebble, seventy meters long. Many parents believe that special rubber shoes are needed for young children.

Entry into the water is convenient. Sunbeds, umbrellas and towels are available for free at Eftalia Splash. According to reviews, the beach is so good and comfortable that vacationers from many neighboring hotels come here.
