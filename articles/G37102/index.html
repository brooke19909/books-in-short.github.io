Making bouquets is a very interesting and enjoyable activity, because it is about beauty.

- parallel assembly;

- assembly at portbuketnitsa;

Wedding bouquets are most often made using parallel assembly. The essence of this principle is laying flowers with large heads tightly stem to stem and subsequent fixing. In conclusion, the wedding bouquet must be decorated with ribbons, decorative greenery and special elegant paper. An important detail is the selection of shades of colors. At a festive event, only pure and bright colors are appropriate.

The bouquet that is presented to the bride is collected at the portbuketnitsa.
