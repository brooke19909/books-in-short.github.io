Hotel "Sokol" (Moscow): address, prices, photos and reviews of tourists

In the northwest, in a unique historical district of the capital, there is a small cozy hotel. Six centuries ago, at this place was a village named after All Saints. Peter the Great and the Empress Anna Ioannovna stayed in All Saints. Now it is one of the oldest districts of Moscow - Sokol. The memory of the glorious history is kept by the church in honor of All Saints, built in 1683. The church is within walking distance of the hotel of the same name. Sokol Hotel (Moscow) is very convenient for those who have business in the center of the capital.

Hotel Description

Hotel class - 3 stars. The three-story building of the hotel was built in 1935. Initially, it was part of a complex of military camp facilities and was intended to house officers. Overhauled in 2012. A feature of pre-war residential buildings is high ceilings. The interior is decorated with a predominance of beige and light gray shades, which together with stylish furnishings creates an exceptional atmosphere of home comfort. Among the economy class hotels, the Sokol Hotel (Moscow) has the richest long-term experience in servicing customers of this category. The photo of the interior of the rooms is more eloquent than any words and advertising. The administration has found a reasonable compromise between price and quality of services. Number of rooms - 126. Check-in after 14 00 . Checkout time - 12 00 . Pets are not allowed.

The administrator at the reception is 24 hours a day, and rooms are serviced around the clock. Hotel Sokol (Moscow) provides guests with free parking. For an additional fee, luggage storage, a sauna, a swimming pool and billiards can be used. Ironing and repair services, shoe shine are available upon request.
