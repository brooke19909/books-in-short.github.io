Manor Kolomenskoye - a place that will appeal to everyone

Manor Kolomenskoye ... This place attracts not only Muscovites themselves, but also guests of the Russian capital. Why is this happening? And why, at any time of the year and in any weather, it is here that whole crowds of vacationers flock here? Most likely, the secret is that it is here that you can find an activity that will appeal to every guest. Someone comes because of the many exhibitions, someone is interested in history and ancient architecture, and someone just wants to sit in silence in the bosom of nature, enjoying amazing landscapes.

Section 1. Estate Kolomenskoye. general description





Manor Kolomenskoye is a wonderful museum-reserve. Today, the complex of historical and cultural monuments includes 17 architectural objects, 12 of which are part of the royal estate of the 16-19 centuries.

In the Palace of Tsar Alexei Mikhailovich, you can admire the historical interiors dating back to the 17th century. Performances as well as classical music concerts are regularly held in the building of this palace. Kolomenskoye is a manor whose address in the capital is known to almost everyone.

Section 2. The estate Kolomenskoye. History





According to legend, the village of Kolomenskoye was founded by immigrants from Kolomna in very old times. Modern historians believe that they escaped from the army of Batu Khan in the 30s of the XIII century.

The first mention of Kolomensky in written sources dates back to 1236 - Ivan Kalita ordered his estate to be transferred to the heirs of Kolomenskoye near Moscow.

At the time of Aleksei Mikhailovich Kolomenskoye is in full bloom. The unusually beautiful wooden palace built at that time was considered a miracle of wooden architecture. It has not survived to our time, however, according to ancient drawings, a copy of this structure was built.
