Hotel Grand Platon Hotel 4 * (Pieria, Greece): photos and reviews of tourists

In a unique location is the hotel Grand Platon Hotel 4 *. Choosing this hotel for relaxation, you will live almost at the foot of the famous Mount Olympus.





Location

Between two magnificent beaches the Grand Platon Hotel 4 * is located. Paralia Katerini is a great option for lovers of sea holidays. There is a bus stop just 200 meters from the hotel. From here you can get to the following facilities:

  • Mount Olympus (20 km);
  • Katerini main street (7 km);
  • Katerini Polyclinic (20 km);
  • Thessaloniki seaport (70 km);
  • Thessaloniki International Airport (90 km).

Guest rooms

This hotel provides guests with comfortable living conditions. Hotel Grand Platon Hotel 4 * (Pieria) includes the following categories of apartments:

  • Superior 36 sq. M. m is a cozy one-room number. There can be a double or a pair of twin beds. A large mirror is installed above the dressing table. Through a sliding glass door, guests enter their own furnished veranda. At the window there is a pair of soft lounge chairs.
  • Compact Standard Room with twin beds. There is also a desk in the room. A large full-length mirror is installed in the entrance area. On the balcony, in addition to plastic furniture, there is also a dryer for swimwear and towels.
  • The bungalow is suitable for lovers of secluded relaxation. The houses are located near the pool in the shade of a palm garden. The spacious room includes one large and two twin beds. A cosmetic table with a mirror is also provided. Outdoors there is a private terrace surrounded by a garden. It has a pair of deck chairs, as well as plastic chairs, a table.
  • Family rooms consist of two rooms. The adult bedroom has a large bed and a soft sofa. The additional room has two single beds. The room has its own furnished balcony, which can be accessed from each room.
  • Junior Suite is a large two-room suite decorated in calm light colors. The bedroom is equipped with large and one and a half beds. A glass wall is also provided here, through which a panoramic view of the picturesque surroundings is provided. The living room has a seating area from which it is convenient to watch TV. There is a table and 3 chairs for relaxing in the fresh air on the balcony.
  • Luxury is often chosen for a romantic getaway, thanks to its design in chocolate tones using bright red accents. The studio consists of a bedroom and a living area. A large mirror not only performs a practical function, but also makes the room visually more spacious. The bathroom is equipped with a hydromassage bathtub.




Room Amenities

The apartments of the resort complex Grand Platon Hotel 4 * have everything you need for a complete and comfortable stay. So, in their rooms, guests can take advantage of the following list of amenities:

  • centrally controlled air conditioner designed to reduce air temperature;
  • centralized heating system for relaxing in the cold season;
  • satellite television with the ability to view Russian-language channels;
  • telephone used to communicate with the administrator (for an additional fee, access to the international line is possible);
  • small fridge;
  • free wireless signal;
  • a combined bathroom, additionally equipped with bath accessories and a hairdryer;
  • private balcony with plastic chairs.




Infrastructure facilities

The administration of the Grand Platon Hotel 4 * provides access to all the necessary infrastructure facilities on site. The following features are available to guests:

  • reception, where you can get advice on a matter of interest to you at any time;
  • private parking with covered area;
  • a conference room where business meetings can be held, as well as ceremonies and celebrations;
  • free wifi in public places;
  • there is a chapel on the territory;
  • catering establishments;
  • medical center where you can go for first aid;
  • a mini market where products and daily necessities are sold around the clock.




Catering establishments

Delicious and high-quality food will provide its guests with Grand Platon Hotel 4 *. Guests can visit the following catering establishments:

  • Helios is a bar located by the pool. Guests can enjoy a variety of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, as well as cocktails. During the day, you can eat pizza, hamburgers, french fries, as well as enjoy ice cream. Also, this bar is the epicenter of entertainment.
  • Zorbas is a cozy tavern that specializes in traditional Greek cuisine. Guests can also be provided with comprehensive meals, which include international dishes. This establishment prefers local farm products.
  • The lobby bar is a quiet and comfortable place where you can enjoy quality local drinks in a relaxed atmosphere. In the afternoons, you can try fresh pastries with aromatic tea and coffee. Concerts of live instrumental music are held several times a week in the lobby bar.
  • Guests can enjoy cold beer and soft drinks at the beach bar. Here you can also have a snack fast food.




Hotel Facilities

High-quality service will please guests at the staff of Grand Platon Hotel 4 *. Pieria will surprise you with an unforgettable vacation, which will be accompanied by the possibility of obtaining such services:

  • order food from the restaurant to your room;
  • organization of corporate events and celebrations;
  • use of the safe at the reception;
  • use of laundry and shoe shine services;
  • the possibility of staying in a room with pets (by prior arrangement and at an additional cost);
  • use of terminals to pay for services in non-cash form.

Organization of events

Grand Platon Hotel 4 * is perfect for organizing business or special events. Greece will make your events unforgettable. The hotel has a spacious indoor hall, which seats 700 people. Office furniture and appliances can be installed in it, and for holidays and celebrations, the room can be decorated in accordance with your preferences.

Another option for organizing celebrations is a banquet hall with an open terrace for up to 600 people. It is stylized as an ancient estate. The terrace is surrounded by a garden with palm trees and flowers. Given its proximity to the church, this place is great for weddings. A great end to the celebration will be fireworks.

Positive reviews

Vivid impressions and pleasant moments are given to guests by the resort complex Grand Platon Hotel 4 * (Greece). The reviews contain such positive comments:

  • high-quality food with a large selection of delicious dishes;
  • the restaurant menu always has fresh fruits;
  • an iron can be borrowed at the reception for free;
  • the restaurant has a friendly administrator who helps to solve all problems;
  • Wi-Fi is free throughout;
  • there is a lot of greenery in the territory;
  • budget rates for accommodation;
  • The hotel overlooks Mount Olympus;
  • the territory is carefully looked after, and therefore cleanliness and order reign in the courtyard.




Negative reviews

A number of negative aspects travelers may encounter while relaxing in the Grand Platon Hotel 4 *. Reviews contain such negative comments:

  • when you arrive at the hotel you will not be greeted with a friendly smile (communication with the staff is rather dry and cold);
  • when you leave the room and pick up the electronic card, all electrical appliances are turned off (including the refrigerator);
  • to get to the beach, you need to go through a busy roadway without a "zebra" and traffic lights;
  • the hotel does not correspond to its four-star status (this is a maximum of "three");
  • even for those who rest on an all-inclusive basis, ice cream and a snack in the bar are paid;
  • there are a lot of algae on the beach that nobody takes away;
  • extremely inconvenient location (almost in an open field);
  • despite the fact that the beach is far enough, the possibility of transfer is not provided;
  • for guests who did not pay the all-inclusive system, sun loungers on the beach are paid (2 euros per day);
  • not only hotel guests, but also outside tourists can relax on the beach, and therefore it is quite crowded here (especially on weekends);
  • for the use of beach towels you need to leave a deposit of 10 euros;
  • a small TV that is uncomfortable to watch from afar;
  • noisy fridge;
  • the shower curtain is too short, and therefore, during bathing, all the water flows to the floor.

Grand Platon Hotel 4 * is a good option for a budget holiday in Greece. Cozy rooms and quality food make up for the inconvenient location.
