Remi Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya): description, service, reviews

The main advantage of Alanya in comparison with many other Turkish resorts is a warmer climate. In addition, the service in the hotels of this city, judging by the reviews, is offered to tourists better quality than in the hotels of many other coastal settlements of Turkey. For example, well-equipped rooms in this resort are rented at the Remi Hotel 4 *.

Location

This complex is located in the city itself, in Alanya. Its guests can get to the center of this resort in about 30 minutes, that is, residents of the Remi Hotel 4 * hotel can fully use the infrastructure of the city .

Nearby points of interest are attractions such as the city lighthouse and the famous Alanya Castle. The archaeological museum, water park and stadium are also located next to the complex .





The hotel is approximately 50 km from Gazipas Airport. But Russian tourists who rented rooms at the Remi Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya), more often still fly to Antalya. The airport of this city is approximately 130 km from the hotel. Judging by the reviews, tourists have to get from here to the hotel for a long time. The tour operator's transfer bus goes to the hotel for about 2 hours.

In relation to the sea, the hotel Remi Hotel 4 * is located pretty well. The beach is only about 5 minutes walk from its gate.

General description of the hotel

The hotel is hosted by the Remi Hotel 4 * in multi-storey buildings of an unusual southern building. The territory of the complex has its own. Here, travelers can take a dip in the pool or sunbathe in the sun loungers. Parking is located next to the complex. Lease cars rented in Turkey, guests of this recreation center can here for free.

Check-in of tourists at this hotel is registered at three in the afternoon local time, but the reception in the complex is open around the clock. This hotel was built a long time ago. And the repair was last made here in 2008.





Unfortunately, pets are not allowed in the hotel. Guests are not allowed to smoke in the rooms rented in the complex. In addition to the usual, the hotel has rooms for the disabled. Actually, the territory of this complex itself is equipped in such a way that people with disabilities feel here as comfortable as possible.

What is the housing stock of the hotel

In total, 83 rooms are equipped for guests at the Remi Hotel 4 * (Alanya) . In the rooms rented in this hotel, vacationers can use:

  • air conditioning and minibar;
  • safe and TV.

Phones in this hotel are installed not only in the rooms, but also in the showers. The hotel also offers free Wi-Fi. Individual showers for hotel residents include hairdryers.

Tourists about the rooms

Travelers are accommodated in the rooms they booked at the Remi Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya), often even earlier than the estimated time. The rooms in this hotel, judging by the reviews of vacationers, are for rent quite spacious and comfortable. The maids in the apartments of this complex are cleaned daily, but not particularly carefully. However, in any case, the hotel rooms do not look dirty.





The disadvantages of the rooms of the complex tourists include primarily weak Wi-Fi. Also not too good in this hotel are air conditioners. Unfortunately, they cannot create a comfortable microclimate in the hotel rooms.

Lingerie in Remi, judging by the reviews, change often. However, guests often have to remind about the fact that the shower room has run out of gel or shampoo.

The views from the apartments of this hotel offer, as tourists say, not particularly interesting. From the windows of rooms rented at the hotel, vacationers can only admire the garden or pool. Sea from the hotel rooms is not visible.





Infrastructure

On the territory of the hotel, I Remi Hotel 4 * guests can, for example, swim in the pool. The hotel also has its own fitness center. Wheelchair ramps are also available at this hotel. In addition, the hotel has:

  • business room;
  • conference hall;
  • luggage office.

The hotel guests have the opportunity to visit the hairdresser, as well as the sauna or steam bath. In addition, this modern complex has a sports hall as well as a tennis court. For a fee, travelers can go windsurfing or snorkelling.





Reviews of tourists about the infrastructure

The pool in this hotel, as vacationers note, is comfortable. Chlorine in him staff sprinkles a lot. But guests do not consider this a special drawback of the hotel pool. After all, as you know, in Turkey , Coxsackie epidemics occur frequently in recent years. Chlorine is at least some guarantee that the hotel will not have an outbreak of this disease.

Sun loungers at the hotel are free of charge. This tourists, of course, also rank among the advantages of the complex.

Most vacationers believe that this hotel is best suited for those travelers who love peace and quiet. Tourists who are looking for various kinds of entertainment at the resort, in any case, should look for another hotel for themselves in Alanya. There are no noisy fun events at the Remi Hotel for guests. Entertainment is not carried out here. Even on TV, hotel residents can watch only one Russian-language channel. Perhaps only the singing of a mullah through a megaphone in a nearby mosque can break the silence at the hotel.

Some of the shortcomings of the Remi Hotel 4 * hotel (Turkey, Alanya) , its guests consider that its staff practically does not speak Russian. And the hotel workers, unfortunately, do not show special benevolence towards domestic tourists. More or less sincerely in this complex, employees relate only to the Germans. And the thing here is only that guests of this nationality give hotel workers big tips.

In general, tourists read the infrastructure of this hotel quite developed. But still, many vacationers note that for the price they paid for the rooms, the service in Remi Hotel 4 * could be provided with better quality. Some guests even think that in this regard the hotel should not be assigned 4, but only 3 stars.

Rooms for rent in the hotel, of course, with meals. Unfortunately, there are some not very good reviews about the hotel's dining room on the Internet.
