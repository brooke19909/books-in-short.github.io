The price is for 4 *, and the rest is for 5 * - Hurghada Hotel Titanic

Holidays in Egypt are very popular in our country. There are just a lot of offers where exactly to go and where to stay in this tourist Mecca of the whole world, but there are several places and hotels that really deserve attention. One of them is the Hurghada Hotel Titanic, which is a four-story building with 278 rooms. Of these, 220 are standard, 50 family and 8 suites. Standard rooms are equipped with air conditioning, satellite TV, safes.

Having the status of 4 *, the hotel not only corresponds to it, but even surpasses it. This is especially noticeable in terms of nutrition. Already becoming familiar with all inclusive (all inclusive) is completely different from what Egyptian hotels usually offer - for the better, of course. European cuisine is served for breakfast, lunch and dinner, and the buffet provides a wide selection for every taste. The main dishes are served fruit, desserts, in the evening - ice cream. The additional menu also has a pleasant variety, especially fruits. Drinks, including alcohol, can be tasted not only in the restaurant and bars of the hotel, but also on the beach.

The location of the Titanic hotel in Hurghada is noteworthy - the second line from the beach. As a rule, such hotels are not popular, but not in this case. At the disposal of vacationers are provided the beaches of three hotels at once. The distance to the sea is about 800 meters. You can get to it both on foot through the territory of other hotels, and on a special bus that runs at intervals of 5-10 minutes. Sandy and pebble beaches, sunbeds on these are simply necessary, therefore, attentive maintenance staff is always ready to offer an equipped place on the beach and provide soft mattresses to vacationers. What is noteworthy, the service on the beaches is absolutely free. The sea is shallow, clean, corals grow very close to the shore, you can see them by renting a mask with a snorkel.

To compensate for the remote location of the beach, Hurghada Hotel Titanic is equipped with its own water park. Many slides, attractions, pools will appeal to both adult hotel guests and the smallest. At special children's attractions rescuers are constantly on duty, and, sometimes, there are even more of them than bathers. Such an attentive attitude to child safety will not just please parents, it will allow them to remember their childhood and frolic on steeper slides without worrying about their kids.
