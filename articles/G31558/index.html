Holiday Village "Sun City" (Slavyanka) - an unforgettable vacation for the whole family

In the Primorsky Territory, in the Khasansky district, in the beautiful bay of Chirok, the complex for out-of-town recreation "Sun City" is waiting for its guests. The bay is located in the southern part of the Bruce Peninsula and makes it possible to enjoy peace and quiet, to experience unity with pristine nature.

Leisure facilities

The recreation center "Sun City" (Slavyanka) is a cosiness and comfort for the whole family. In the complex you can celebrate any family date, spend a wonderful weekend or school vacation.





There are many entertainments based on. A good volleyball court is equipped, it is also convenient to play badminton on it. Several soccer mini-fields and a play area for toddlers are organized.

On the base you can rent SUP-boards, inflatable and rowing boats, catamarans, kayaks. The beach with fine imported sand is equipped with shadow umbrellas, sunbeds with soft capes.

Here they will teach the basics of snorkeling and control of any boat, learn to fish from a boat. The underwater world in these waters is rich and incomparably beautiful.

Number of rooms

Tourists are accommodated comfortably in the Sun City complex. The recreation center (Slavyanka) provides apartments of eight categories from economy class to luxury.





Alone with nature

The most economical option is a small log house of about 7 m 2 . It has a double bed with a set of linen. Electricity supplied. The toilet and shower are shared outside.

The compact category is a wooden house for two with a private terrace. The bathroom is equipped with a sink, mirror and toilet. It is furnished with a large double bed and a dining table with chairs. A kettle and fridge are also included. Bed linen and two towels are provided. A shower is provided outside.

The best conditions in the region for recreation are offered by the recreation center "Sun City" (Slavyanka). Guests leave the best reviews about compact plus rooms. They belong to the middle price category. The furniture in the apartment has a large double bed, two chairs and a table, there is also a small refrigerator, toilet, sink, shower, mirror. The house is supplied with electricity and hot water, which is heated by solar panels. Outdoor shower.





Well-established life

Number standard is ready to accept four people. It contains a two-story and double bed, a table, four chairs, a mirror, a shower, a toilet, a refrigerator. Guests are provided with a kettle, a towel for each and bedding sets.

For recreation of a large family, the ideal base is the recreation center "Sun City" (Slavyanka). The description of the five-seater number is confirmation of this. Standard plus apartments have two rooms (5 and 14 m2) and a large terrace overlooking the bay. The house has a large bunk and single beds. There is a toilet, mirror, shower, sink, refrigerator and hot water. Guests at check-in receive sets of bed linen, five towels and a kettle.

A cozy stay for two will be good in a comfort class room. The area of ​​the house is 17 m2, the terrace is twice as large. There is a dining table with two chairs, a kitchen area with a microwave, and a kettle. Bathroom with shower, sink, mirror and toilet. There is also a fridge, minibar and Wi-Fi.

For well-off guests

Entertainment

Being in these places? you must definitely visit the state Far Eastern natural marine biosphere reserve. The base organizes excursions both by land and by water. Vacationers visit beautiful islands, mysterious grottoes, golden sand bays with turquoise-casting water, and eared seal and fur seal rookeries.

Several sea routes have been developed. They begin with the recreation center "Sun City" (Slavyanka, Primorsky Territory). One goes around Sibiryakova Island, the second passes near the cliffs of Antipenko Island. In these places, many colonies of starfish and hedgehogs living in underwater reefs. For tourists, an hour-long landing is provided. It is time for a swim and snack. Shadow arbors and a barbecue area with the necessary equipment are organized here, deck chairs are arranged.

One of the most popular walks is an excursion to the island of Zheltukhin. The boat passes through narrow passages formed by rocks. In these places you can observe the life of fur seals, then tourists go to the wild abandoned shore. It still contains the remains of military vehicles of the Great Patriotic War: T-34 tanks, howitzers and tractors.

For romantics there is an hour and a half excursion in the dark to the shores of Vladivostok. The boat enters the beautiful Golden Horn Bay, then into the Eastern Bosphorus Strait , from where the Russian and Golden Bridges are clearly visible.





The recreation center "Sun City" (Slavyanka) organizes fishing in the Sea of ​​Japan. Spinning and snacks as well as bait, the organizer prepares. Flounder, shark and sea ruffes are caught in these places.

A large play area with modern equipment has been prepared for younger guests. Teenagers enjoy spending time on the fields for indoor soccer, beach volleyball and badminton.
