Warm crochet shirt for women: knitting patterns, photo

Increasingly, girls and women choose a neat shirt front instead of a heavy heavy scarf. This accessory is designed to protect its owner as efficiently as possible from frost and cold wind. The specific design resembles the high neck of a warm sweater. Due to the fact that the crocheted knit shirt for women (knitting patterns can be completely different) is worn under a coat, fur coat or jacket, it is extremely comfortable to wear.

How to tie a shirt-front

Mini scarf knit and crocheted. The choice of instrument depends on the preferences and skills of the craftswoman. It should be noted that the fabrics connected with a hook are always a little denser and stiffer. But this is only a plus for a product such as a crochet shirt for women. Knitting patterns can be used completely different: from classic crochet to openwork patterns. The appearance of the shirt-fronts connected in different ways is very different.

Two most popular models can be distinguished:

  • Rectangle with a clasp.
  • Manishka with a neck and a mantle.

Shirtless knitting sequence

The following describes how to crochet such a neck:

  1. Dial a chain of 55-60 air loops (VP).
  2. Knit the first row with single crochet (RLS).
  3. Perform the second row of RLS, winding the hook under the distant "pigtail" of the column of the previous row. When using this technique, a relief scar is formed along the entire row.
  4. 1 VP, 1 PRS, 2 VP, skip two loops in the third, perform RLS, knit RLS to the end of the row. This is how button loops are formed.
  5. Repeat the second row three times.
  6. Repeat the third row.
  7. Repeat the second row three times.
  8. Repeat the third row.
  9. Repeat the second row two times.
  10. Trim the thread and thread the tail. Sew on buttons.

If desired, the finished shirt-front can be tied with one row of RLS or with the "step of the dog".

Knit shirt front with a mantle

It is quite difficult to perform, but also the warmest crochet shirt for women.



Patterns of knitting an openwork border could be any choice of a craftswoman. The photo below offers a good option.



The dense neck consists of 12 single crochet columns and is carried out in a transverse way. Applied alternation of rows:

  • 12 double crochet
  • 7 single crochet, 2 half columns, 3 single crochet.

The rapport is one repetition of the first row and three repetitions of the second. When the neck is ready, strapping is performed along its lower edge. The height of the neck should be at least 15 cm. In some cases, in accordance with individual sizes, knitting a cloth with a width of 18-20 cm may be required. Openwork binding plays a decorative role, so it can be made of any size.
