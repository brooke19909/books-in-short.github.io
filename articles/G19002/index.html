Hotel Mobor Beach Resort 2 * (Goa, India): overview, description and reviews of tourists

India every year is becoming an increasingly popular destination among tourists who want to spend at least a few days on the picturesque beaches of the country. South Goa is famous for the beauty of nature and the coast covered with white soft sand. Of course, many hotels and entire resort complexes were built here. Many experienced travelers recommend choosing the Mobor Beach Resort 2 * hotel as a stopping place.

Of course, before booking you should learn more about the selected hotel. For example, is its location convenient and can a tired traveler count on comfort and hospitality? Is food included in the room rate? Is this a place to relax with a child? This is important information for every tourist.

Locations and its features





Hotel Mobor Beach Resort 2 * has a very favorable location. It is located almost on the seashore, in the territory of the popular resort village of Cavelossim. This is a quiet place, which is mainly chosen by fans of a quiet holiday on the picturesque shore.

It is worth saying right away that there are malls, shops, cafes and even large markets nearby. There is always where to go for a walk, and shopping fans have a good time here. By the way, the nearest airport is located in the city of Dabolim - the distance to it is about 40 km. Travel agencies, as well as the hotel itself, provide customer transfers.

What do the rooms look like? Photo and description of rooms





As already mentioned, this is a small hotel - on its territory there are 38 rooms, namely a spacious studio. All of them consist of a sleeping and living area, as well as a small kitchenette. The family apartments have a bedroom with a door, and the living room has a large and comfortable folding sofa that can replace the bed. By the way, there are also two balconies, and it is furnished with garden furniture, where you can relax at any time of the day.

Naturally, guests will be able to use household appliances, without which it is impossible to imagine a truly comfortable stay. It offers a small air conditioning, as well as a TV with a large panel, on which you can watch mainly cable channels. It can also count on the presence of an electric kettle, a set for making tea and coffee. The kitchen area also has the necessary appliances, including a toaster and microwave. Of course, you will have at your disposal a large set of dishes.

The bathroom is small, but fully equipped, and hot water is available around the clock. By the way, a hairdryer can be obtained at the reception for a small fee.

Can I count on food?





Of course, delicious food is an essential attribute of quality rest. What should a tourist pay for accommodation at the Mobor Beach Hotel 2 *?

Each morning, guests are invited to the local restaurant for breakfast. It passes in the form of a buffet. The menu here is standard - toasts, fruits, oatmeal and granola, fresh fruits and some snacks, as well as tea, coffee and some other drinks.

The rest of the day, guests eat outside the hotel. Near the coast, and in the center of the village there are a huge number of restaurants, cafes and eateries, which offer dishes from around the world. You can enjoy real Indian delicacies or give preference to more familiar, international cuisine.

Where is the beach located? Entertainment and amenities on the beach





Most tourists prefer to relax on the sunny seashore. What can guests of the Mobor Beach Resort 2 * hotel complex count on? Goa, India is famous for its incredibly beautiful beaches and clear waters of the ocean.

This hotel is located not on the coast, but on the third line - the distance to the beach is about one kilometer. A convenient road leads there, which, by the way, passes through a quiet area and the territory of a five-star hotel.

According to reviews, the beach here is very large and clean. There is a pontoon, and the entrance to the sea is convenient. By the way, there are practically no jellyfish, stingrays and other marine animals in the water, so children are happy to bathe here. A team of experienced lifeguards works on the shore. There are locker rooms, and renting deck chairs and umbrellas is quite inexpensive. Of course, at any time you can have a meal in one of the cafes.

Tourists are offered opportunities and more active time, for example, boating, banana or water skiing, sailing, scuba diving, windsurfing (light waves are great for beginners).

Additional service: list of services





Despite its modest size, the Mobor Beach Resort 2 * hotel can offer the tourist some additional services. There is no safe in the room, but you can use one of them at the reception desk for free. Laundry and dry cleaning services are also available, and the cost of washing and ironing is small.

In the hotel you can easily exchange currency at a suitable rate, as well as rent a car, bike or moped, which is convenient, because your own transport greatly simplifies the movement around the neighborhood. It offers free internet. By the way, the hotel has a small but fully equipped conference room, which is used both for business events and for organizing various festivities.

Leisure of guests: how to have fun at the hotel?





Of course, the main attractions are located outside the hotel. South Goa is ideal for sightseeing holidays, as there are many natural and historical attractions. By the way, the hotel has an excursion center where you can get all the necessary information and even get an interesting tour.

As for the hotel itself, there is a small pool in the courtyard. The guests bathe, cool and sunbathe, lying on comfortable sun loungers. This is the center of public life at the hotel - it is always fun here. You can pass the time while playing billiards. And here there is a large library with a good collection of books and periodicals.

Vacation with a child

Of course, parents who are going to relax with a child are interested in the issue of convenience. Is it also possible to take children with me to the Mobor Beach Resort 2 * hotel? Reviews indicate that the hotel is well suited for a family vacation. You should immediately understand that this is a very small hotel, which for the most part is used as an overnight stay. Therefore, you should not count on such entertainment as, for example, a kids club, a pool with slides, etc.

On the other hand, travelers are provided with comfort. A cot can be delivered to the room upon request, and in the restaurant kids are offered highchairs. In addition, for a little money a child can be left with a professional nanny. By the way, the beach is perfect for families with children, and there are several interesting places in the city where parents and their children can have fun.

Hotel Mobor Beach Resort 2 * (Goa): guest reviews





Experienced tourists know that the most useful information can be obtained after talking with people who have already managed to spend some time at the hotel. So what do they say about the Mobor Beach Resort 2 * hotel?

Reviews are mostly positive or neutral. The hotel is really small, but comfortable, and the atmosphere is calm. Employees are always attentive, politely answer questions and, whenever possible, try to quickly solve any problem. The rooms are stylishly decorated, and the equipment works properly, even if it is not new. The road to the beach does not take too much time, and the coast here is really beautiful.

It should be understood that the service and hotels in India look peculiar, and regardless of the number of stars. This place is suitable for tourists who are going to spend the bulk of time on the beach or in the city. By the way, the prices for hotel accommodation are pleasantly pleasing - this is a favorite place for fans of economical holidays who do not want to give up modern comfort.
