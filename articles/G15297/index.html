It is enough to have the necessary materials at hand. Thread, hook and decor. And also know the principles of knitting elongated loops. Even a beginner can really cope with the creation of a product.

Which hook to take?

If you look at the forums of needlewomen, it can be noted: many of them use hook No. 4 for knitting washcloths. However, this is not the only right decision. The device can be taken of any size. The main thing is that it is not too thin and does not have a pointed tip. Well, and besides, it should be convenient for you to hold it in your hands. The same goes for the material the hook is made of.





How to create elongated loops?

The next moment. The most difficult thing that needlewomen may encounter is crocheting the elongated loops for a washcloth for beginners. But the procedure only seems difficult. In fact, it is very simple. It is necessary to wrap the tip of the thumb with yarn when knitting, make a single crochet into the loop of the previous row and remove the thread left. That's all, the loop is ready.

How to tie a single sided washcloth?

Such a product is perhaps the easiest to knit. It is created literally in a couple of hours with good perseverance of the needlewoman. And it can be used for a very long time. At least for a month. A step-by-step instruction for a crocheted washcloth for beginners is as follows:

  1. Dial a chain of 21-32 air loops. The number can be increased if in the end you want to get a wider canvas.
  2. Tie 2-3 rows of Art. non-cash Here you can go in two ways: either tie a chain around, or just go to the final air loop and turn the canvas over.
  3. Tie a series of elongated loops. How to make them is written above.
  4. Knit a row of single crochet posts.
  5. Repeat 1-3 steps until you reach the desired width and length of the washcloth.

Note. If you choose knitting the product round, you must make sure that it does not tighten. To do this, in each row, knit 2 times more loops (2 tbsp. B / n in 1 air of the last row).





How to knit a washcloth in the form of a glove?

And these products are perhaps the most popular, since they can most often be seen on the market. They are created exclusively round-robin, they can either have a department for the thumb, or be without it. In the latter case, crocheting a washcloth for beginners is as follows:

  1. To collect a chain of 50-60 air loops and close it in a circle.
  2. Knit 5-6 rows (or even more) with single crochet.
  3. Run a series of elongated loops.
  4. Knit 1 circle with single crochet.
  5. Repeat steps 3-4 to achieve the desired product height.
  6. Knit as many rows as at the very beginning, single crochet.
  7. Finish work, cut the thread and hide its tip.

This washcloth requires the creation of pens for her. Make them easy, just knit a chain of air loops and connect it to different edges. Do not be afraid that if it is single, it will easily break. Polypropylene yarn is very durable. Her hands just do not break, you can only cut yourself.

If you want a finger to be tied to a glove, just type more loops. Tie the glove to the desired length and leave some space for tying the department. When you finish the whole glove, return to it. Knit 10-15 rows of single crochet stitches and begin rounding by reducing the stitches. Then pull the thread and break it. Hide the scrap in the cloth of the mitten. In this case, you do not need to attach pens.





How to crochet a washcloth in the form of a hedgehog?

If your child does not like to swim, try creating a product for him in the form of a favorite toy, which he himself could rub with soap. In this case, it will be very difficult to get it out of the bath, and tears and crying will be replaced by a smile and laughter. How to crochet a washcloth? Step-by-step instructions for beginners are presented below:

  1. Collect 32 air loops, close them in a circle.
  2. Knit 2-3-4 rows with the usual art. non-cash
  3. Tie a circle with elongated loops.
  4. Repeat steps No. 2-3 another 15 times.
  5. Change one color of polypropylene thread to another.
  6. Knit another 2-3-4 rows of Art. non-cash
  7. Reduce 3-4 loops in each row until a muzzle forms.
  8. Tear off the thread, attach eyes and a spout from threads or other safe materials.

In order for the child to really want to bathe, hedgehog should be placed on a shelf in the bathroom, and not among children's toys. Then any adoption of water procedures will turn into a fascinating activity.





How to make a sponge bob?

SpongeBob is one of the favorite children's cartoon characters. Therefore, if you create a product in the form of it and present it to your son / nephew / friend of the boy, he will be very pleased.   3. Change the color of yarn to white. Knit another 3-4 rows.
  4. Take the yellow thread. Use it until the desired blade length is reached.
  5. To attach eyes, tongue, lips and teeth.

How to tie a frog washcloth?

This is another product that you can create for your child. "The Frog" is very cute, kind and interesting. At will, he can create a completely different expression of the face - funny, sad and so on. A step-by-step instruction for a crocheted washcloth for beginners with a photo is as follows:

  1. To collect 50-52 air loops, turn knitting.
  2. Create 2-3 rows with ordinary single crochet.
  3. Knit 1 row with elongated loops.
  4. Knit another 1 row of Art. non-cash
  5. Repeat steps 3-4 to achieve the desired length.
  6. Finish knitting with a clipping thread.
  7. Attach a loop so that the product can be hung.
  8. Create eyes, mouth and tongue, sew with a needle.

Note! So that the polyester washcloth is softer and does not scratch, before use it should be washed thoroughly with soap, doused with boiling water and allowed to lie down for 5-9 minutes.
