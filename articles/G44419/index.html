Summer is the time for vacations, and many Russians are already starting to think about where to go on vacation. One of the most popular resort cities in our country is Adler. Every year, hundreds of trains bring vacationers here from all over the country. Recently, many trains have been converted to branded ones, and traveling in them has become somewhat more convenient.

We buy tickets

Traveling to the sea is always a pleasure. In order for a good holiday mood not to be spoiled, you should take care of purchasing tickets in advance. There are more than enough people who want to get to Adler by train during the holiday season, and there may simply not be any travel documents for the scheduled day at the box office. Book tickets better at least a month before the trip. Today, if you wish, you can save yourself from standing in line for a long time by ordering a ticket through the Russian Railways website or online banking.





At the time indicated in the travel document, you should arrive at the station, not forgetting to bring your passport with you. He will need to be shown to the conductor along with the ticket. Passengers without a passport are not allowed into the car.

If we talk about the level of comfort, it can be different in different trains. Recently, Russian Railways has purchased many new cars. But there are plenty of old ones - half-rotten. If you want to get to Adler by train in comfort, get a ticket for the company staff. There are no special guarantees that you will reach your destination without hassle in this case, however, there are still much more chances.

Passengers have very good opinions about this comfortable train. The guides in it are polite, and the conditions are pretty good. The shelves in the cars are soft and upholstered not with dermatine, but with fabric. Each compartment has a TV and private radio. Headphones to listen to the latter gives the conductor. There are connection points on both the upper and lower shelves.





It can also be considered very convenient that the cars are equipped not with simple toilets, but with bio. This means that they do not close at stops. In addition, such latrines are usually clean. In the hallways to everything else there are signs that indicate whether the toilet is empty or not. So passengers who decide to get to Adler by train 102 will be completely spared the need to stand in line.
