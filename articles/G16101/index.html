Golovinka: recreation, resort features, prices, accommodation, reviews

There are several settlements with the name Golovinka in Russia - in Kursk, Oryol, Moscow, Rostov regions. Near Sochi, in the Lazarevsky district, there is also its own Golovinka. Rest here is not at all the same as in other resorts of the Black Sea coast. Golovinka has two attractive features: low prices for all services and an amazing atmosphere of complete harmony with oneself and the world. What kind of place is this?

A few words about the village

Named after Yevgeny Golovin, the glorious Russian general, the Sochi Golovinka grew up on the banks of the mountain river Shakh, one of the longest and most full-flowing in the region. Along it stretches the famous tourist route, opened almost 100 years ago. In the 30s of the 19th century, the famous Aivazovsky fought in this area. Golovinka was once a fort. Many Decembrists had a hand in its construction, who in the village put a memorial sign under the planted Rayevsky tulip tree itself - the pride of local residents and a must-see point for visiting all visitors. Clean shady streets, neat private houses immersed in the greenery of gardens, small cozy cafes, hospitable residents - this is what modern Golovinka is. Rest here is calm and measured. The infrastructure of the village is not as rich and diverse as in large cities, but everything you need is available. There is a post office, telephone call center, ATM and a branch of Sberbank, a network of cafes, canteens, a hotel, hairdressers, a hospital, small shops, a supermarket, and a market.





Location and transport

Away from the bright, noisy and slightly ambitious Sochi is Golovinka. Rest here is not disturbed by the roar of cars, loud music, rattling in the bars and discos until morning, and other sounds common to resorts. Nevertheless, the village is not divorced from the world. Highway No. 27 runs through Golovinka, leading to Sochi, Tuapse, Lazarevsky. Adler Airport is located within 70 km from the village, railway station - 35 km, Lazarevsky - 18 km. You can get to Golovinka by train from Adler (get off on the Golovinka platform) or from Art. Sea and beach

Wonderful beach holidays in Golovinka! A photo without words says how spacious it is. There are several beaches in the village. Central - the widest (up to 80 meters), sand and pebble. Entering the water here without boulders, gentle. Approximately in the middle of the beach, the Shah flows into the sea, those who wish can plunge into the cool waters of a mountain river after warm sea water. Nearby there is a cafe, a dining room, numerous retail outlets.

The second beach is the property of the Cote d'Azur sanatorium, but no one is forbidden to be on it. It is narrow (only up to 10 meters a strip of sand and pebbles), but three times longer than the Central. Both are well equipped. Here you can rent an umbrella and a sunbed, swim with a scuba diving, ride a "banana", a catamaran.

There is a wild beach in Golovinka . It is located far from the first two, as those who are called nudists rest there.





Nature and weather

In Golovinka, winters are mild and warm. The air temperature is always positive. This allows multiple trees and shrubs to green all year round. There are 1200 species in the vicinity of the village! In the spring, the village is drowning in white-pink foam and magical aromas of flowering fruit trees. In the fall, approximately from the second decade of September, the velvet season begins. It lasts until about the end of November. Many just at this time like Golovinka. Rest is filled with some kind of enthusiastic pacification and peace of mind. It‚Äôs already fresh in the morning, but during the day it‚Äôs warm enough for sunbathing and swimming (sea water - like fresh milk). The market is full of fruits and vegetables. But even in summer, Golovinka gives excellent rest. Reviews about this resort from those who are not looking for noisy entertainment are always enthusiastic. There is no heat here. Plus 28 (in the shade) - this is the maximum height of the thermometer in July-August.

The natural beauty of Golovinka is not limited only to the plant world. The river Shahe with its many waterfalls, suspension bridges, picturesque bends and rowlocks attracts tourists of all ages to this region.



Where to stay

Pleasant in every way and inexpensive, Golovinka offers relaxation. The private sector is one option. Previously, residents of coastal villages settled holidaymakers wherever they go (remember the film "Be My Husband"). Now the private sector is a comfortable guest house, which offers a wide variety of services. In Golovinka there are a lot of such housing.





There are guest houses where the rooms are suites. They are equipped with all amenities, furnished with new furniture, equipped with modern electrical appliances. For a day they ask from 600 rubles. Aton, Brigantine, Ariadna, Livadia, Blue Lagoon, Watercolor - these cozy private mini-hotels are very popular. Some even have swimming pools and playgrounds, and children are accepted almost everywhere in any age category. In guest houses are simpler, but no less comfortable, prices are lower - from 250 rubles per night. These are institutions such as The Doctor's Away, Katyusha, Alexander, and many others. Almost all the owners deliver vacationers free of charge from the stations (Loo, Lazarevskaya), and from Adler - for an additional fee. Each guest house has a kitchen with a set of dishes, and some can order food from the hosts.
