Green Valley (sanatorium): review of services and reviews about rest and treatment

In this article we will examine in detail the living conditions and treatment in the sanatorium "Green Valley". It is located in the resort area of ​​Tuapse, but quite far from this city, in the resort village of Olginka. Those who have long loved to relax in the Krasnodar Territory claim that this resort was formerly called “Yurga” and was famous for its high level of service. This health resort is located in a picturesque place, in the middle of a relict forest of Pitsunda pine. Belongs to the sanatorium "Green Valley" FSSP Russia. Most vouchers are distributed (at prices below cost) among employees of the Federal Bailiff Service. But if the health resort is not fully filled, anyone can buy a tour package to the sanatorium. And of course, the price of a ticket in this case will be market. Let's see what kind of services the health resort provides.



Where is the sanatorium "Green Valley"

Tuapse is located thirty kilometers from the health resort. Therefore, reviews warn that you will have to take a taxi to the place from the station. The nearest airport is located in Gelendzhik - it's a hundred kilometers away. You can take a bus to the resort village of Olgino, and already from the station to take a taxi - it will be cheaper. One way or another, you can't do without a car. "Green Valley" - a sanatorium, which is located two kilometers from Olgino, and even on a hill (sixty meters above sea level). Do not rush to discard this option and look for another place to relax. If you have respiratory diseases, healing pine air will be a worthy compensation for the difficult road from the beach (three hundred meters). There is a sanatorium at the very border of the Agri Nature Reserve. So, the ecological situation here is the most favorable.



Territory and buildings

Yes, and the Green Valley itself (the sanatorium) is stylized as a protected corner of nature. Relict pines grow everywhere, squirrels and chipmunks run, half-tailed raccoons walk slowly. Not only walking paths, but also medical paths are laid along the forest park zone. Reviews assure that the territory of the sanatorium is quite vast. But even in the most remote corners, cleanliness and order reign. Gardeners and park designers do everything so that the eyes of vacationers delight in some kind of seasonal flowers. In addition to pines, many exotic shrubs and trees grow on the territory, each of them has a nameplate. The sanatorium complex consists of several six-story buildings that fit perfectly into the Mediterranean landscape. The resort has its own outdoor swimming pool.



Rooms and board

Where do holidaymakers settle in the Green Valley health resort? The resort has comfortable standard double rooms (with a loggia, minibar, TV and split system), a suite and apartments. The comfort of the rooms is evidenced by the fact that in 2012 the health resort received a four-star certificate. The cost of living in the Green Valley includes three meals a day. Forget about the healthy, but tasteless food that the waiters bring to you on a common menu. In this health resort, guests are offered a buffet food system, thanks to which you can choose your favorite dishes and eat as many as you want. In addition to the main restaurant “Pension”, there are others in the sanatorium: “Barabulia” (specializing in fish dishes), “Sorrento” and “Kish-Mish” (Italian and Uzbek cuisine). You can spend your evening in the lobby bar, the Snow Valley night cruiser and the Afalina cafe.



Medical Center

Green Valley is a sanatorium specializing in the treatment of diseases of the cardiovascular, nervous, endocrine and digestive systems. Patients suffering from respiratory ailments begin to feel especially well. Adaptive recovery programs allow people who have suffered injuries and operations to get to their feet as soon as possible. The sanatorium has an excellent medical and medical base. Modern high-tech equipment allows you to accurately conduct ultrasound diagnostics, hardware physiotherapy. SPA procedures will help you recover and just relax. Experienced specialists of the medical center make a general, Ayurvedic, floral and stone massage. In the herbal bar you can drink an oxygen cocktail. The resort accepts children of all ages for treatment. In general, the health resort offers about seven hundred types of medical services. And vacationers are very positive about the local treatment and its effect.

Green Valley (sanatorium): reviews

Even if you just want to relax by the sea, a health resort is right for you. It has its own pebble beach, to which only three hundred meters go down. It is equipped with sunbeds and tents. The sanatorium has two pools - an outdoor (heated in the cold season) and indoors. Reviews say that young and healthy people in a health resort are not at all bored. There are sports fields, a tennis court, a four-lane bowling alley, billiards, a DVD movie theater, and a night club. For children there is a playroom and a playground, a maze "Jungle". Vacationers say that you can put your child under the care of a professional educator.

Sanatorium "Green Valley" FSSP of Russia often holds business meetings on its territory. A conference room with the necessary equipment and several meeting rooms are equipped for this.
