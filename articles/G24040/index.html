Hotel Villa Boskovic 2 * (Budva, Montenegro) - overview, features and reviews of tourists

The peculiarity of the resort of Budva, which makes it popular among domestic tourists, is the low cost of accommodation in hotels, food in cafes and goods in stores. Travelers visit this town with a view to relaxing at sea for a very long time. And of course, the infrastructure of this resort is developed simply superbly. The hotels here are in any case quite comfortable. Even in inexpensive hotels, guests are offered comfortable rooms and good service. One of the cheapest and at the same time comfortable hotels in Budva is Villa Boskovic 2 *.

Where is the complex located?

This hotel is located almost in the center of Budva, not far from the government villa Goritsa. The distance from its buildings to the beach is only 200 meters. Blaza Jovanovica, on which this hotel is built, is located away from the main highways. There is no nightlife near the hotel. Therefore, absolutely nothing prevents tourists from resting on the territory of this complex.





Successfully located hotel Villa Boskovic 2 *, according to vacationers, and in relation to the airport. To get to the place, tourists who rented rooms in this complex, upon arrival you need to overcome only 25 km. Usually the road to the hotel from the airport takes less than half an hour for vacationers. The shuttle bus stops at some distance from this hotel. And tourists have to walk about 10 minutes to it. But you should not be afraid of getting lost who decided to rent rooms at this hotel for vacationers. Newly arrived guests are usually met either by the hostess of the hotel itself, or by her sons.

General description of the hotel

They accept guests in this hotel in two buildings - a large main and an additional smaller one. Both villas of the hotel are surrounded by tropical greenery and look simply magnificent. The courtyard at the hotel is small, but judging by the reviews, it is very beautiful and cozy. On all sides, the territory of Vil la Boskovic 2 * is surrounded by a fence. If desired, guests of the hotel can take a walk in the orchard, which is broken on its territory.

Reception at this hotel is open 24 hours a day. Pets are not allowed in the hotel rooms. The tourists in this hotel are usually accommodated in rooms immediately, without waiting for the checkout time.

Hotel rooms

For rent in this complex are several types of rooms. Designed apartments Villa Boskovic 2 * for accommodation:

  • 2 people (17 m 2 );
  • 3 guests (20 or 34 m 2 );
  • 4 guests (36 m 2 ).


Also, tourists at this hotel can rent a mini-house . Its area is 45 m 2 . Equipped mine in Villa Boskovic 2 * (Budva) accommodation:

  • air conditioners;
  • TVs
  • refrigerators.

There are hairdryers in the hotel showers. The rooms are also equipped with kettles.

Opinion of tourists about apartments

In appearance, the degree of convenience and the area rented out at the Hotel Villa Boskovic 2 * rooms vary greatly. However, rooms of all categories of this hotel earned good reviews from guests. The guests of the complex consider it a plus, first of all, although it is old but comfortable furniture. Household appliances at this hotel also set working. Air conditioners in a hotel, for example, do their job well. In addition, they are adjustable in the hotel.

As for cleaning the rooms at the guests of Villa Boskovic 2 * (Montenegro, Budva) there are also no complaints. The apartments in this complex are always clean and comfortable. In time, maids here also change bed linen. The same goes for towels. Throwing them on the floor so that the maid brings new ones is not necessary.

Good reviews are also available regarding the soundproofing of the rooms in this hotel. Guests in each rest center do not interfere with each other's rest.





Tourists consider the shower rooms not too comfortable as some drawbacks in hotel rooms. When bathing, the water in the bathrooms of the complex flows simply onto the floor.

Hotel infrastructure

Hotel Villa Boskovic 2 * is quite small. There are only 5 rooms for rent. In addition, the classiness of this complex is low. Therefore, there are not too many services on the territory of the hotel for its guests. For example, there is no pool in the hotel. But the hotel yard itself, according to vacationers, is planned and decorated very well. The shadow on the hotel creates a large canopy twined with vines. Hotel guests can sit among fruit trees in comfortable chairs at tables.

Villa Boskovic 2 * (Budva) received good reviews from tourists, including for the presence of Wi-Fi on its territory . Judging by the reviews, it catches well in the courtyard of the complex . There are no safes in this hotel either in the rooms or at the reception. However, its guests do not have to worry about the safety of their belongings in the hotel. Theft at this hotel has never happened.

Guests of the complex can park cars on its territory. But the parking here, unfortunately, is very small.

The courtyard of the hotel looks beautiful, according to tourists. Outwardly, it looks like the courtyards of good mini-hotels of Adler or Sochi. The main element of landscape design in the complex is grapes, and flower pots are placed on the territory and on the stairs. All paths in this hotel are covered with beautiful paving tiles. Walking on them is very convenient. In the evenings, the hotel courtyard is lit.





Staff Reviews

The owners of Villa Boskovic Cat B 2 (Montenegro, Budva) are a local family - mother, father and daughter with children. Tourists consider the owners of the hotel to be polite and friendly. The owners of the complex relate to their guests sincerely, trying to create the most pleasant atmosphere for relaxation in the hotel. The same applies to the few employees of the complex. They never show any rudeness towards the guests and try to fulfill their duties well.

Where do guests eat?

Own dining room at the hotel Villa Boskovic 2 * n . But hotel owners can attach their guests to nearby cafes and taverns. In this case, coupons are issued to residents for food. The selection of dishes in the establishments near the hotel for these papers is usually small. But the guests of the Villa Boskovic hotel never go hungry . Yes, and cooks at local cafes, judging by the reviews, are quite tasty. They use fresh and not too cheap products for their dishes.

For example, residents of Villa Boskovic can take in local cafes , for example, sausages, fried eggs, sausages, cereals, milk, etc. They offer taverns in Budva and various salads, vegetables, fruits and drinks to the city guests. In local cafes, holidaymakers are offered puff pastry sweets. Guests of the hotel can buy all kinds of food in a supermarket located near its beach. Very good, for example, smoked pork is sold in this store. Also next to the hotel is the large Zielona Platz market. Hotel guests can purchase semi-finished products in the city on their own in the summer kitchen.





Villa Boskovic 2 *: beach reviews near the hotel

The closest to this hotel is Slovensky beach. Unfortunately, he did not deserve good reviews from tourists. The water in the sea in this place is quite dirty. The coastline here is covered with a mixture of sand and pebbles. Experienced vacationers advise tourists staying at this hotel to go by sea a little further - towards the old Budva. Here is a very convenient beach Morgan. The road to it takes about half an hour or even a little more. But the path to this beach lies in very picturesque places, and therefore, it will not seem especially tiring to the vacationers.

It is at the entrance to Morgan Beach that the famous Montenegrin statue of a ballerina is located. The sea in this place is very clean. There is no rubbish lying on the shore either.

Some guests of the hotel also visit beaches located on the opposite side from the old Budva. This is, above all, Becici, Rafailovici and Kamenny. Relaxing on all these beaches, judging by the reviews of tourists, is convenient.





Excursions

Budva is a resort famous not only for its comfortable beaches and magnificent nature. There are many interesting sights in this city. The two-star Villa Boskovic, of course, does not have its own tour desk . However, all kinds of trips around Budva and its environs are offered to the guests of this complex by their tour operators. Experienced vacationers, however, do not recommend taking mini-tours from them. It will be much cheaper to get an excursion in one of the bureaus working on the beach in Budva itself. Mini-tours are here for vacationers, judging by the reviews, good ones are offered. At the same time, tourists can safely buy excursions at the cheapest cost. On the quality of mini-tours, the small difference in price in different bureaus does not affect.

Instead of a conclusion

Hotel Villa Boskovic 2 * (Montenegro) offers its guests rooms, therefore, quite comfortable. Perhaps the infrastructure in this two-star hotel is developed and not too good. But in relation to the service provided to residents of this hotel, there are no negative reviews on the network.





It is worth staying at this hotel inexpensively. Tourists can relax here with relative comfort.
