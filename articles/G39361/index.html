Hotel Romance Beach Hotel 4 * (Marmaris, Turkey): description and reviews

Romance Beach Hotel 4 is a four-star hotel on the coast of the Turkish city of Marmaris. This is a small town, stretching along the bay, protected from all sides by mountains. From the side of the sea he is saved from strong storms and winds by an nearby island. Marmaris is located in the south-west of Turkey, where two seas meet - the Aegean and the Mediterranean. This wonderful paradise is simply created for a relaxing rest and relaxation. Romance Beach Hotel 4 (Marmaris) is perfect for such a pastime.

After all, what else is needed to have a wonderful vacation - the gentle southern sun, warm sea, light breeze, good living conditions and delicious food. Hotel Interior

The building of the Romance Beach Hotel 4 occupies five floors. At the entrance there is a reception desk where you will be carefully listened and accommodated even in the middle of the night. According to the reviews of tourists who arrived at the hotel at 10 pm, they were not only politely served, but also separately fed dinner, for which they were very grateful because they were on the road for a long time and were terribly tired and hungry.

The lobby of the Romance Beach Hotel 4 Marmaris is impressive at a glance. The snow-white room with its purity blinds your eyes. The large hall has an elongated well shape. On all floors, the aisles to the rooms are located on the balcony surrounding the front door. In the middle, all five floors have no partitions, that is, the fifth ceiling is visible from the first floor.

White chandeliers and beautiful fabric balls descend from the top on metal bars. Upholstered furniture of the same white color complements the composition: sofas and armchairs. A beautiful spiral staircase is also an interesting additional element of the decoration of the hall. Palm trees growing in large square tubs dilute the luster of the paintings on the walls.

A contrasting spot to the snow-white interior of the hall is a delicate shade of glass tiles of the color of the sea wave, which decorated the sections of the floor in the waiting and relaxation areas. For the convenience of guests, the hotel has two elevators.

Romance Beach Hotel 4 (Turkey) has its own territory, fenced with a low fence. This place of relaxation is decorated with numerous cozy sun loungers and an oval pool with clear water. Small palm trees grow around.

Marmaris Water Park is right behind the fence. Some rooms overlook it. Not all tourists are delighted with this, but most of them rate this neighborhood positively. Especially convenient for those tourists who come to the resort with children. No need to travel far to have fun riding a water slide.

Room Categories

The Romance Beach Hotel 4 (Marmaris) has a total of 61 rooms of varying degrees of comfort. All residential areas are cleaned daily, beds and towels are changed twice a week. If necessary, you can exchange it unscheduled by asking the maid on the floor for this. But be prepared that in this hotel there are few Russian-speaking vacationers and the staff does not speak our way, and English is poorly known. Each room, regardless of its category, has air conditioning, a TV, a telephone, a safe for valuables and documents, a minibar, an electric kettle or a coffee maker, and a bathroom. All rooms have showers and hairdryers. On TV you can watch satellite channels, including Russian. There is internet access via free Wi-Fi. There are balconies or terraces in the expensive rooms.

Triple rooms

Seventeen rooms of Romance Beach Hotel 4 Marmaris are designed for three people. The room has one single bed and one double. There is a comfortable armchair and dressing table. All furniture and plumbing are modern, new. The washbasin is made of artificial stone and has an interesting table shape with an oval sink. On it you can sit in plastic chairs and see the beauty of the city and local flora. The design of the rooms is restrained and concise, nothing more. On the wall above each bed there is a sconce; there are small bedside tables. The bathroom is spacious and modern.

Additional hotel services
