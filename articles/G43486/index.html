Hotels in Novgorod the Great: reviews, addresses

Novgorod the Great is a wonderful ancient city, which is located in the north-west of Russia. You can hear a lot of things about Novgorod both from the point of view of culture and history, and from the point of view of an interesting pastime. Almost every street of this city contains a lot of interesting facts, you can always walk here and get a lot of new knowledge. The infrastructure is well developed, hotels in Novgorod the Great are found everywhere: both in the center and on the outskirts, so you can always choose the right option.





A bit about the history of the city

As already mentioned, Veliky Novgorod is a truly ancient city. It is interesting in that, according to many sources, it was here that Russian statehood began to emerge. This fact is undoubtedly very important for the country, therefore Veliky Novgorod is of such interest. It is believed that there was a vocation of Prince Rurik. Then the city gradually began to develop and after some time became the center of Novgorod Rus.

Interestingly, during the time of feudal Russia, the city became the very first free republic. Several centuries later, political independence was lost during the Moscow-Novgorod war, in which Moscow Prince Ivan the Great defeated. The war took place in 1477-1478. Novgorod is one of the few cities that have not been subjected to the Mongol invasion. Of course, he had to pay tribute to the Horde, but most importantly, he managed to preserve the unique cultural monuments. Thus, Novgorod became the only city that could avoid decline in the XI-XII centuries.





Veliky Novgorod nowadays

Despite the fact that the situation is constantly changing, Novgorod is still a large city, which has its own industry, and quite well developed. Despite the economic situation as a whole, its economy is growing steadily. The main sector of the economy of Novgorod is industry. The city has a well-developed market for consumer goods and services, there are many chain grocery stores.

The transport network in Veliky Novgorod functions without any problems. The city is a major transport hub through which several federal roads pass: the Rossiya motorway connects the city with Moscow and St. Petersburg, and the A116 highway connects with Pskov.

The tourism sector of the city is also well developed. Every year more and more tourists visit the hotels of Veliky Novgorod. Hotel addresses can be found in many travel guides. Various city sightseeing tours are also provided.

Culture of Veliky Novgorod

So, the basic information about Novgorod has been considered, and now it's time to move on to the monuments and objects of cultural heritage located in this wonderful city. As you know, there are a huge number of attractions that must be visited.

Many museums, temples and other historical places await tourists every day. Novgorod is famous for its architecture, which has been preserved since the emergence of the city. There are also several theaters and a philharmonic society that have been operating for a long time.

The hotels in Novgorod the Great are quite diverse and are located in different parts, which is very convenient, since each tourist can choose the most suitable option for himself.

It is definitely worth taking a walk through the streets to feel the charm of the city and enjoy its atmosphere.





Hotels in Novgorod the Great: a small overview

Of course, going to this wonderful place, you should think about choosing a suitable place of residence in advance. Tourists are usually fully satisfied with the hotels of Veliky Novgorod, reviews about them are mostly positive.

The largest number of hotels is located in the center, just a few minutes from the Kremlin. The choice of accommodation depends on the personal preferences of tourists and their financial capabilities. Economy hotels in Veliky Novgorod will please you with low prices and good conditions.

You can also find cozy guest houses and small hostels in which payment is much lower than in large hotels. For those who travel to Novgorod with a large company, an excellent accommodation option is in suburban tourist clubs and recreation centers where you can settle in cottages. So, we can say that the hotels in Novgorod the Great are represented in large numbers. It remains only to determine the place of residence.

Hotels in the center

The most convenient option, of course, is to stay in the center, because from here you can easily reach almost all the main attractions of the city. Industry, economics, developed transport networks - this is not the whole list of things that Veliky Novgorod can boast of. Hotels, hotels - in short, everything that makes up the tourism sector is also at a high level.

Hotel Intourist

Indeed, many have heard this famous name. Intourist Hotel in Veliky Novgorod is a large hotel complex that meets all modern standards. Hotel address: st. Great, 16. The guests are offered wonderful cozy single, double and triple rooms of different classes (standard, comfort, business comfort), junior suites and suites. For those who wish, several different apartment options are provided. We can say that this hotel is not focused on a certain budget, here you can find both an inexpensive option for accommodation, and rooms are more expensive. However, the low price does not deprive guests of comfort and service at the highest level.

It is worth noting guest reviews about this hotel. Especially often tourists note the convenience of the location of the hotel. Also of the positive aspects, many note the cleanliness of the rooms and the courtesy of the staff. However, there are also negative aspects, among which guests indicate poor soundproofing of the rooms.





A big plus of the hotel is its location, as it is located in the historical center of Veliky Novgorod, right on the banks of the Volkhov River. From here you can reach the main attractions on foot.

Hotel "Sadko"

Many hotels can boast of Veliky Novgorod. Hotels in the city center are located at every step. One of these hotels is the Sadko Hotel. She has been hosting guests for over 50 years.





The hotel has a huge number of rooms - about 171 rooms. All rooms are clean, comfortable and designed for one or two people. There are also deluxe and junior suites. In addition, the hotel has organized a large number of services, here you can visit the hairdresser, give laundry, use Wi-Fi. The hotel offers free on-site parking. The hotel has a wonderful restaurant in which every tourist can choose the right dish. There is also a spacious hall for banquets and other large events.

Among the positive reviews about the hotel, you can often see that guests are especially pleased with the breakfast and buffet. Tourists also note the good location of the hotel and proximity to the main attractions. Of the negative reviews, it is most often found that some rooms do not have air conditioning.

Hotel address: st. Fedorovsky stream, 16

Hotel Volkhov

Hotel "Volkhov" always attracts the attention of tourists. And this is not surprising, since it combines cosiness, a high level of comfort, and also strikes with its incredible architectural elegance. Its location is really convenient, it is located near the very heart of the city - the Novgorod Kremlin. Reviews about this hotel are extremely positive, there is everything you need for a full stay.





Guests are accommodated in comfortable rooms that are designed for one and two people. It is especially convenient that the hotel has family rooms (7 rooms). These are spacious studios that will comfortably accommodate the whole family. Deluxe and junior suites are also available. The hotel provides a large number of additional services, such as a restaurant, tour services, as well as conference rooms, meeting rooms and much more.

You can often hear rave reviews about this hotel. And this is not surprising, since it is closest to the center. Also, from the positive aspects, hotel guests note the friendliness of the staff and excellent cleaning of the rooms. Of the negative reviews, you can often see the small size of the rooms and the inconvenient location of the outlets in them.

Hotel address: st. Predtechenskaya 24

Hotels in other areas of Novgorod

Naturally, not everyone likes to relax in the city center. Therefore, you can find other solutions, especially knowing how diverse the hotels of Veliky Novgorod are. You can relax inexpensively both in the city itself and in its suburbs.

For example, guest estates located on the banks of the Volkhov River and Lake Ilmen are perfect . Boutique Hotel "Yuryev Compound"

If we talk about hotels that are located farther from the center, then a great option would be to stay at the Yuryevskoye Podvorye hotel. This comfortable hotel is located 3 kilometers from the city center. The hotel is interesting in that it is made in the style of old Russian life. Its location is really very interesting - nearby is the St. George Monastery, as well as the Museum of Wooden Architecture.

With regard to accommodation, offers double rooms (standard and economy), junior suite and suite, apartments are available. The hotel provides a large number of services: free parking, Internet access, restaurant, billiards room and much more.

It is worth considering reviews about this hotel. Of the positive aspects, guests most often note a good location, the beauty of the local nature and a pleasant atmosphere. Of the negative impressions, tourists most often mention the lack of teapots in the rooms and the high prices in the restaurant.

Hotel address: Yuryevskoye Shosse, 6a

Hotel "Windrose"

Windrose is a cozy, quiet hotel, which is also located a little further from the center. However, this does not interfere with its popularity; many tourists stay here. The hotel has 25 rooms, which are comfortably furnished.





For accommodation, double rooms of various classes (economy, standard, semi-standard), as well as junior suites and suites are offered. Reviews about this hotel are mostly good. Particularly positively, hotel guests note the presence of a well-equipped kitchen where you can cook your own meals. However, there are minor flaws, for example, some guests note that some rooms do not catch Wi-Fi.

Hotel address: Novoluchanskaya street, 27.
