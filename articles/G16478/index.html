Holiday Village "Malinka" (Volgodonsk): description, prices, reviews

In recent years, hundreds of new camp sites have grown in Russia, where Russians can rest no worse than in the famous foreign resorts. For example, on the right bank of the Don recently appeared a very beautiful and most comfortable recreation center "Malinka". Volgodonsk, the city where it is located, is very young, but it is developing rapidly, and its population is growing uncontrollably. Now the residents of Volgodonsk and all nearby settlements have a great opportunity to comfortably relax in nature at least every weekend, because their "Malinka", as they say, is close at hand.

Description, infrastructure

Residents of any city get tired of the flow of cars rushing through the streets, from the bustle, from the endless stress at work. Relax and briefly forget about all city problems provides an opportunity recreation center "Malinka". Volgodonsk is a small town with great development prospects. It is located a few hundred meters from the legendary Don, in a very beautiful forested area, on Lesnaya street, house number 5. This area is the outskirts of the city, where there is no noise, no gas, only clean air and peaceful silence. It is more convenient to get here by car, for which you need to move along the 2nd Concrete in the direction of Tsimlyansk, without reaching the bridge over the canal, turn right, follow the bridge and continue to the very gates of the base.

The places here are picturesque. In the shade of maples and birches, like toys, pretty buildings are placed, sports grounds are equipped, alleys are laid. The recreation center "Malinka" (Volgodonsk) in the infrastructure has many different in size and design wooden arbors. All of them are equipped with tables, benches, refrigerators, barbecue facilities and wash basins. For leisure guests on the territory there is a sauna, a bathhouse, a tennis court, a swimming pool, a well-maintained and always clean beach, and a bright and safe playground for children has been built.

Accommodation and prices

Excellent facilities are offered by the Malinka recreation center. Volgodonsk is a city of modern technologies. At the camp site during the construction of buildings also used the currently popular innovations.

So, here all the rooms are made only of environmentally friendly wood. Create a highlight in the design and matched to the tone of the drapery, bedspreads, curtains. In the main building, vacationers are offered rooms of one category - luxury. Their equipment: beautiful furniture, including bedroom and upholstered, refrigerator, heating system, air conditioning, new TV. The hygiene room has a bathtub, as well as a washbasin and toilet bowl, a hairdryer, hygiene products and bathrobes. The cost of such a room per day is 2500 rubles.

Not only private rooms, but also chic two-story cottages for large companies are offered by the Malinka recreation center (Volgodonsk). Prices for them - from 10,000 rubles per day, but you can only rent the first or second floor. Then the cost will be 5000 rubles per day.

The cottages on each floor have two bedrooms, a living room, a kitchen with an electric kettle, microwave, furniture, dishes. The hygiene room has bathtubs with hydromassage, on the second floor there is a balcony, and on the first there is a terrace.

All prices are excluding meals. Guests can cook for themselves or eat in the restaurant of the Malinka base. The cook here is excellent.
