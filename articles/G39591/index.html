Novosibirsk Zaeltsovsky park. general description

Amazing Novosibirsk. Zaeltsovsky park and its history





The history of the park is directly related to the development of the Novosibirsk region of the same name. In 1925, a specially created commission prepared a preliminary redevelopment project for Novonikolaevsk (present-day Novosibirsk), where special attention was paid to the landscaping and conservation of the Zaeltsovskaya forest park zone of the city.

According to the architects' plans, the city should have been decorated with squares and boulevards. For the rest of the townspeople, the Zaeltsovsky Park was organized, where it was planned to build cottages, sports grounds and rest houses.
