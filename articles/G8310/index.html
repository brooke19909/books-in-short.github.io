Do-it-yourself sports shirt from a T-shirt. Description of the manufacturing process:

  2. Cut the trim on the neck and along the lower edge of the product.

  4. Shape the neckline as desired (deep or shallow, V-shaped or round).
  5. Make the back of the product: make a neckline just below the shoulder blades and deepen the neckline for the hands to the middle of the back. You already know how to make a T-shirt from a sports plan T-shirt. Next is an even more interesting alteration option. We suggest you familiarize yourself with a step-by-step description of how to turn a T-shirt into a top. For work, you will need scissors, chalk, a needle, thread, elastic.

How to make a T-shirt from a T-shirt?

  1. Lay the t-shirt on the table and cut it in a straight line along the chest line. You should get a rectangular double canvas.
  2. Tuck the cut edge and sew it so that you can stretch the elastic through it. This can be done either manually or on a typewriter.
  3. Insert an elastic band or braid in the same way into the bottom edge.
  4. From cropped sleeves you can form pockets and sew on the sides of the product.
  5. Pull the elastic bands along the lower and upper edges of the product, fitting them to your shape. That's all, the top shirt is already decorating your body.
