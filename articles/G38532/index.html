Hotel Planeta 5 * (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria): photos and reviews of tourists

For many years, tourists from all over the world prefer to spend their holidays here. One of the most luxurious hotels here is considered to be Planeta 5 *. It stands out among other complexes with its impressive size and original design. But is the appearance consistent with the quality of service? In this article we will analyze what services and entertainment the Planet Hotel (Bulgaria) can offer tourists.

Description

Hotel Planeta 5 * (Sunny Beach) was built relatively recently, in 2006. On the territory of the complex there is one huge ten-story building, in which there are almost 550 rooms. For the convenience of visitors, it is equipped with 7 elevators. The hotel is considered a place for active youth recreation, but despite this, there are a large number of family apartments in the building. Luxurious interiors haunt guests everywhere, even the rooms are very beautifully decorated. The last complete reconstruction of the building was carried out in 2015, and the hotel rooms undergo an annual update





The hotel staff speaks Russian, Bulgarian, English and German, which is very convenient, especially if tourists speak only their native language. Reception is open 24 hours a day. Pets are not allowed in the building. Check-in starts at 14:00, and check-out must take place no later than 12:00. You can come to the hotel with children of any age. Planet operates on an all-inclusive system.

Where is the hotel located?

The Planeta 5 * complex is located in the very center of the famous Bulgarian Riviera (Sunny Beach). The hotel is located on the first line, 70 meters from the public beach site. You can get to the hotel from the airport of Burgas, which is 25 kilometers from the resort. 90 km from the resort there is also a large tourist center - the city of Varna. The territory of Sunny Beach is remote from noisy freeways and railways, so the air here is not very polluted.





Due to its location, the hotel is within walking distance from most restaurants, shops and entertainment venues that fill the resort area. The beach is only 5 minutes away. Large hotels such as Mena Palace and Admiral Plaza are located nearby, and just north of Planet (Sunny Beach) it borders on Jeravi complex. Nearby are an amusement park and a water park.

The hotel offers its guests all-inclusive meals. There is a special diet and vegetarian menu. The system includes breakfast, brunch, lunch and dinner. Organization of children's leisure

Negative hotel reviews and shortcomings

But not only Planeta 5 * (Sunny Beach) is famous for its merits. Reviews are found and not very flattering. However, this is not surprising, because there are no hotels without flaws. Most travelers vacationing in the complex note the following disadvantages:

  • The main restaurant is constantly crowded. There are not enough places, and people are forced to stand and wait until they are free. The administration does not seek to solve the problem.
  • The rooms are cleaned rarely and poorly. Often they just take out the trash. Underwear is changed at best once a week, although it is promised once every 2 days. Towels can also hang in the bathroom for several days. Often a change of linen occurs only after paying a tip.
  • Very noisy environment, which is not suitable for families with children.
  • Poor nutrition. The food is monotonous and quickly bored. Drinks are served in plastic glasses, which does not correspond to the status of a five-star hotel.
  • The Internet is available only in the lobby and it works even there very poorly.
  • Lifts are not enough, so people living on the upper floors have to wait a long time to get into the room.

How much does a hotel stay cost?

Despite the fact that the "Planet" is considered a five-star hotel, living here is not too expensive. The cheapest room will cost you 3,500 rubles per night. The most expensive apartments will cost almost 3 times more expensive - about 9500 per night. Thus, a week-long vacation will cost 30-100 thousand per room in the resort Sunny Beach (Sunny Beach). Planeta 5 * does not have single rooms, so it is better to go there together or in a large company. Then the ticket will cost less and will be approximately 15-50 thousand per person.

Does the Planeta hotel justify the 5 stars stated in the name? Appearance and interior decoration meet high requirements, but the organization of rest does not stand up to criticism. The rooms are poorly cleaned, the restaurant has no seats, the territory is too noisy and crowded. Tourists are not advised to visit this hotel with children, however, for youth, these disadvantages will not be so significant. In addition, the affordable price makes the hotel an attractive option for relaxation, even if you take into account all the shortcomings of the complex.
