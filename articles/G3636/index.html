Primorsky Territory, "Calm Bay" (recreation center). Rest in the Primorsky Territory

The nature of Primorsky Krai is famous for its beauties far beyond the region. Rest in these places will give a lot of pleasant experiences. If you decide to temporarily change the scenery, see the amazing natural scenery, visit the Primorsky Territory. "Calm Bay" is one of the bases where you can stay.

Short address of the recreation center: Primorsky Territory, "Calm Bay." The full postal address is written as: 692928, Primorsky Territory, Nakhodka, Postysheva street, 27 B.

The recreation center is 30 km from the city of Nakhodka. If you live in Vladivostok, the path to this place by road is 206 km. From Khabarovsk - 897 km. You need to go to Nakhodka, then turn to the village of Wrangel. Further, the path will go along a country road to the coast. Then you need to turn to the base, guided by the pointer.

You can pre-arrange with the base staff, they will organize a transfer from the bus station or railway station of the city of Nakhodka to the place.

When you visit the Primorsky Territory, the "Calm Bay" will become your reliable pier for the holidays, on holidays or weekends.

Based on your preferences, you can stay in the coastal or forest part of the base. Coastal is located almost on the beach. There are three two-room, 25 double and 10 two-room wooden houses to choose from.

Entertainment

Outdoor enthusiasts can play and play on the sports field. It offers vacationers table tennis, equipment rental. A town has been built here for children, where they can spend a lot of time.

The beach has a volleyball court. It's nice to play ball running around the white sand court. Also on the beach there are toilets, outdoor showers. In the evening it is no less fun than in the afternoon, as there are discos. The dance floor is indoor, so rain will not interfere with incendiary dances.





The recreation center "Calm Bay" (Primorye) offers rental of tourist equipment. Taking it, you can make long and short walks, go hiking, in order to be able to admire the unique beauties of these places.

At the recreation center, you can unforgettably celebrate any event by renting a banquet hall for 50 people.

Water sports

Fishing - this type of useful pastime will appeal to men. If desired, a boat trip will be organized for you, during which you can not only go fishing, but also ride a banana.

Scuba diving enthusiasts can go diving with an experienced instructor. Costumes and equipment are provided.

Reviews

Those who have already been here share their impressions and talk about how wonderful the rest was. Primorsky Krai is famous for its beauty, Calm Bay is no exception. Experienced tourists note that it is surrounded by picturesque hills that impede the penetration of winds. The sea warms up well in the summer, swimming in clean and clear water, in which every grain of sand is visible, is a pleasure.

Depending on the weather, the water warms up from mid to late summer. Some advise you to go here from early August to early September. In these months, the sea warms up well.

Visitors here note the special beauty of this place. Seeing the blue sea, white sand, bizarre rock formations, it is impossible not to fall in love with such beauty!

Some are worried about the road through the pass, because it rains during the rains. But experienced travelers are reassuring, saying that you can drive. They say that they overcame this barrier even after many days of heavy rains on the front-wheel drive sedan.

Tips also apply to room reservation. They say that it is better to rent one of the houses near the bathhouse. They are far from others, well equipped, and there is just a paradise.
