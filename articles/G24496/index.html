Kansashi elastic band for beginners

Even the simplest hairstyle can be made interesting thanks to the decoration. Do-it-yourself rubber band-kanzashi for a bunch should be liked by fashionistas. After all, with her help, a very easy-to-execute hairstyle becomes festive!

Simple flower

In order for you to get a gum on a bunch using the kanzashi technique, you need to find out how the simplest flowers are made. Next is a description of one of them. What do we have to do:

  1. Take ribbons of two different colors and cut the squares, the sides of which should be equal to 5 centimeters. Need 16 pieces of each color.
  2. Fuse the edges of the squares so that they do not fall apart.
  3. Take one square and bend it diagonally two times. Flame the corner.
  4. Bend a detail of a different color diagonally three times, melt.
  5. Insert the small part inside the large. Correct the edges and melt.
  6. Do the same with the remaining 15 squares.
  7. Cut another 12 parts. And do the same with them, just leave them single layer.
  8. Cut out a circle from cardboard, the diameter of which is 3-4 centimeters.
  9. Stick on a tape of the same color as the large petals.
  10. Glue two-layer petals along the edge, single-layer inside.
  11. Attach a large bead to the center.
  12. You can add more rows with petals.

Make some of these flowers and sew them to a wide elastic band that could be worn on a bunch. If you have thin hair, make smaller flowers.

Roses





Elastic band-kanzashi on a bunch in the form of roses looks very gentle and beautiful. Such an accessory will complement the day look well. What do we have to do:

  1. From a satin ribbon 2.5 centimeters wide, cut 11 strips 6 centimeters long.
  2. Fuse the edges.
  3. Sew both ends of the tape to the bottom and pull the thread.
  4. Only one petal is ready, 54 more need to be made.
  5. To form a core, twist one petal and sew together from the bottom. The result is a tube to which the remaining parts must be glued.
  6. Create the base of the bud by attaching two petals to the core on opposite sides.
  7. Form the second row, it consists of three petals, the third row of five.
  8. From the green satin ribbon, cut out the leaves in the form of a semi-oval. Singe the edges.
  9. Glue about seven leaves to the rose.
  10. Make flowers from the remaining blanks.
  11. Glue to the base to be worn on the bun.
  12. You can decorate roses with stones and organza.

Elastic band-kanzashi on a bunch is ready!

The foundation





The base to which the flowers are attached can be any bauble. Usually they are used as bracelets. Make two flowers and sew along the edges. Glue hairpins or invisibility on the back. To put on this accessory, you just need to wrap the bunch with a bauble and fix it on your hair.

From organza

Such a gum-kanzashi on a bunch is made of organza and satin ribbons. Working process:

  1. Cut squares of organza with a side of five centimeters.
  2. Take one part and bend in half, bend the edges to the center, you should get a rhombus.
  3. Turn the side corners back.
  4. Trim the bottom and melt.
  5. On the first row of one flower you need five petals.
  6. From felt, cut a circle with a diameter of 2-3 centimeters.
  7. Glue the petals onto the base.
  8. On the other side of the felt, glue another five petals, holding them between the details of the first row.
  9. Glue a bead into the center.
  10. Around the middle, paste five small round petals made using the same technique, only from a satin ribbon and smaller in size.
  11. Cut the leaves, melt them around the edges and stretch a little.
  12. Fold the leaf in half and pass over the fire, iron. Also make streaks.
  13. Glue the leaves to the flower.
  14. Make some of these parts and attach them to the gum base.

Done!
