How to change the name of an iPhone: two ways

Each owner of Apple technology, acquiring the long-awaited gadget, does not even know what secrets its "apple" device holds. One of them is the ability to change the name of the iPhone. You will learn how to do this and why this function is needed.

Why is it necessary

Most often, iPhone owners decide to change the name of their gadget in the following situations:

  • if a supported smartphone was purchased;
  • in an effort to be original.

In any case, no matter what causes the person to perform this procedure, the question always arises - how? Change the name of the iPhone, fortunately, you can do it yourself. This is easy enough to do.

How long does it take to rename

In fact, the whole procedure takes no more than a minute, since it is not complicated. To do this, it is enough to clearly follow the instructions below.

What versions of iOS does it work on?

The above function is supported by all available versions of the operating system. No problems should arise, even if the working version of iOS is below the seventh.

Where to change the name of the iPhone?

There are two ways to change it:

  1. Perform this by connecting to a laptop or computer.
  2. Perform the procedure directly in the settings of the phone itself.

How to change iPhone name through computer?

Many owners of "apple" smartphones when trying to change the name on the device in this way make the same common mistake. They connect the gadget to a laptop (or personal computer) and try to rename the iPhone without connecting, but the next time you turn it on, the phone will be called iPhone again. Why it happens?

The error is that you need to perform this procedure using iTunes. It is necessary to observe the following sequence in the work:

  1. Connect the phone to a laptop or computer.
  2. Launch iTunes.
  3. In the sidebar of iTunes, click on the name of the iPhone.
  4. After that, a marker will appear, with which you can rename the gadget.
  5. Next, write a new name.
  6. Press enter.

After a successful operation of this kind, the new name will be visible to Mac OS users, and not just in Windows Explorer. In addition, the phone will be recognized under a new name on all iOS devices. It is worth knowing that any iOS device that also syncs with iTunes (iPod touch, Apple TV, iPod, iPad and others) can be renamed in the same way.
