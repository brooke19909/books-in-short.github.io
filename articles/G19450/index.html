Sanatorium "Ai-Danil" (Yalta, Crimea): official site, reviews of tourists, webcam

The gentle sun, warm sea and stunning landscapes are the dream of any vacationer. In addition to relaxing on the beach, I want to strengthen my health and treat old diseases. Go to hospitable Crimea, where the Ay-Danil sanatorium is located on the Black Sea. An excellent medical base will allow for comprehensive rehabilitation for both children and adults.

Unique nature



Of course, you can relax in the Crimea anywhere, because this peninsula is simply created for a great vacation. But the location of this particular sanatorium is perfect for the health of guests. The hot climate, warm sea and the unique geometry of the mountains (a natural amphitheater as if embracing a hotel) - all these features make you feel very comfortable here. The sanatorium is protected from strong winds, there is practically no storm on the sea. The air is saturated with useful iodine, and the salt level in the water is recognized as the most suitable for a comfortable bath. The symbiosis of all natural features favorably affects the body - this is the main advantage that the sanatorium "Ai-Danil" boasts.

Resort popularity

It should be noted that it is this medical complex that has been holding the palm among similar institutions for many years in a row. And not in vain: in addition to the wonderful conditions of stay in the sanatorium and the optimal rehabilitation program, the hospital has a modern infrastructure that is created according to all European standards. This medical complex is known even abroad, so tourists from all over the world come here to relax. The city of sun and heat - such an affectionate definition has won Yalta. Sanatorium "Ai-Danil" operates in this amazing city, which is surrounded by exotic greenery and pampers with a warm sea.

For which category of patients is a health resort useful?

Of course, everyone can enjoy an excellent vacation in a sanatorium, staying in this institution is especially useful for people with such disorders:

  • Chronic bronchitis, asthma and many other diseases of the respiratory system.
  • Diseases of the cardiovascular system, high blood pressure, heart disease.
  • Disorders of the nervous system.
  • Treatment and prevention of the digestive tract.
  • General healing of the body.

Only highly qualified highly specialized specialists who have vast experience in treating patients are at your service: therapists, pulmonologists, cardiologists, urologists, ENT specialists, chiropractors, neuropathologists, gastroenterologists, physiotherapists, nutritionists and many other doctors. Note that the medical base of this resort is recognized as the best among the health resorts of Crimea. Experienced doctors will help you strengthen the immune system, tell you how to lead the right lifestyle. Here is such a hospitable peninsula of Crimea.

The sanatorium Ai-Danil is located in this picturesque corner, which has long been famous throughout the USSR for its health resorts, because, apart from comfortable living, there is a truly unique healing climate.





Medical and diagnostic base of the resort

Due to the availability of the most modern equipment in the sanatorium, patients receive effective treatment and prevention of diseases. Functional diagnostics is performed using the following methods:

  • Ultrasound of all organs and internal systems of the body.
  • Electrocardiography
  • Hemodynamics.
  • Spirography.
  • Rheumphalography.
  • Monitoring and analysis of all important indicators of the body.
  • Rheovasography of the vessels of the limbs.

In addition, you will have a unique opportunity to undergo a full clinical examination. There are the following programs: diagnosis of hypertension, cardiac disorders, analysis of the liver, study of disorders in the digestive tract, sugar control in diabetes. Doctors will help establish the cause of excess weight, identify abnormalities in the kidneys, and examine the thyroid gland. Many women come here for the qualified treatment of gynecological diseases. Men are no exception - a modern diagnostic base allows you to identify male problems at the earliest stages (prostatitis and many others).

Rehabilitation program at Ai-Danil

The sanatorium (patient reviews confirm this) is a center where you can undergo rehabilitation and quickly recover from serious illnesses. Doctors at the resort claim that they have the most modern methods of rehabilitation programs. Depending on the ailment suffered, an individual recovery program is assigned to the person, which is created by a rhinestone by several professional doctors. In addition, a special treatment package allows you to improve your health as soon as possible, which is very popular with patients.





Massage and hydrotherapy in "Ai-Danil"

Healing mud

Sanatorium Ai-Danil offers its patients a unique procedure with the use of mud from Lake Saki. This composition is a real pride of the peninsula, because it has a unique healing effect. Muds are famous for their properties throughout the world, because they are able to have the following effects:

  • regenerative;
  • anti-inflammatory;
  • resolving;
  • painkiller.

For many years, patients have been healed using this unique gift of nature. Centuries of experience has proven that the mud of Lake Saki heals 100 different ailments! People got rid of gynecological and urological disorders, skin diseases, including chronic psoriasis. The miraculous effect of muds due to their unique composition is determined: volatile substances, microelements, activity of pH media, amino acids, vitamins.





Bathing

Sanatorium Ai-Danil is located on the Black Sea. A pebble beach stretches 340 meters. The distance from residential buildings is about 20-30 meters. The coast is equipped for a good rest: bars, cafes, a disco, sun loungers, a shower, entertaining attractions for children and adults. You can not only soak up the beach, but also have a good rest while admiring the seascape.

For lovers of swimming in the warm waters of well-maintained pools, the health resort has equipped heated outdoor swimming pools and a jacuzzi. Nearby, small fountains are filled with delicate trills.

Rest for the smallest

If you decide to improve your baby, then this resort is perfect for you. The health resort provides a range of preventive measures for children of different ages. In addition, here your child will be diagnosed for the presence of all diseases. The climate of the southern coast of Crimea helps restore the health of babies with respiratory diseases, strengthens weakened immunity, and restores the nervous system.

And, of course, aerotherapy can help a weakened body as much as possible. Healing air, saturated with iodine and specific minerals, cleanses the blood, saturates it with oxygen, makes the body resistant to viral infections. Inhaling a real "bouquet" of volatile air of sea air and beneficial plants, the body is stocked with the necessary chemical compounds that enhance metabolic processes and strengthen the immune system.

For the smallest tourists in the sanatorium a children's pool is arranged, in addition, the child will not be bored here - all kinds of entertainment and attractions will surely be remembered by children.

Entertainment for adults

Sanatorium Ai-Danil (Yalta) is also a real entertainment center, where everyone will find something to their liking. If you are an active person and your goal is to promote health, you can visit daily:

  • A luxurious SPA complex where you can not only relax, but also get a lot of useful procedures.
  • Gym, which is equipped with all the necessary equipment for training.
  • A game of minigolf.
  • Large tennis court.
  • An outdoor pool where you can swim at any time.
  • Football field.
  • Basketball court

After playing sports, you can have fun and relax. In the sanatorium there is a cinema and concert complex in which famous artists regularly perform, game evenings are organized, films and cartoons are shown. If you want to retire with a book, there is a wonderful library in the health resort, which will offer you more than 30,000 volumes of books on various subjects.

In the evening, you can have a delicious dinner in the Ayu-Dag restaurant or visit a small cafe on the coast. On open areas near the sea, you can dance to your favorite music. Well, for lovers of sea sports, special equipment rental is provided.

Tourists reviews



According to vacationers, this sanatorium is a great place for a vacation with the whole family: convenient infrastructure, all conditions for kids, professional treatment and, of course, a climate that suits everyone. The disadvantages include, perhaps, high prices for permits.

Tourists note that the health food is excellent: the menu is hearty and tasty, every day they offer new dishes, there are a lot of fruits and vegetables in the diet. The presence of a webcam on the official website of the sanatorium allows guests to see everything that is happening on the territory of the complex online. There is an opportunity to assess the situation in the hospital before arriving at the place of rest - this service is offered by the hospitable sanatorium Ai-Danil.

How to get to the complex? Tourists note that it is best to arrive in Simferopol by plane or train, and then you can transfer to the Simferopal-Yalta shuttle bus. Go to the Ay-Danil stop, 3 Gurzuf settlement.
