Crochet "Crayfish step" is convenient to use for finishing the neck or bottom of the product and sleeves where the elastic is not provided. Knitting the ‚Äústep of the dog‚Äù is performed as follows: the hook is inserted into the loop of the last edge row to be tied, the loop is pulled out of the thread from the ball and the single crochet is performed by inserting the hook into the loop located nearby, but not on the left, but on the right side. In the course of work, you will see that due to the fact that the hook moves non-standard, the single crochet columns turn out slightly turned out, this creates a raised edge.



To design the edge of the product, crocheting ‚Äúhand step‚Äù can be done using lush columns. To do this, the hook is introduced under the loop of the extreme row, the thread is pulled from the ball, forming a loop. This loop is not knitted, it remains on the hook, double crochet, again insert the hook into the same loop of the extreme row, again pull the loop. On the hook, two loops with an intermediate yarn turned out. Now all the loops are knitted together. The result was a lush column, due to which the tied edge will look even more prominent. Such lush columns from left to right tie the entire edge of the product. The number of loops and crochets on the hook can be from 3 or more. The more loops and crochets strung on the hook, the more magnificent the column will be. One tip: when knitting a fluffy column, it is very important not to tighten the thread, the loops on the hook should be free, otherwise it will be difficult to stretch the thread through them.
