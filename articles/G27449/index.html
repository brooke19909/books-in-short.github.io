The best hotels of Alushta: addresses, description, reviews,

One of the most popular destinations for holidaymakers is Crimea. And how could it be otherwise? After all, here are the best resorts, stunning nature, warm sea, clean air and a large number of attractions. Alushta is one of the beautiful and popular resorts of Crimea. Resting here, you can not only significantly improve your body and get a boost of energy for the whole year, but also have fun. For tourists, a large selection of hotels in Alushta is offered. Accommodation in the best of them is characterized by a high level of comfort and a variety of services for customers.

Alushta Hotels: Features

On the Black Sea coast there are a large number of places to stay. There are private hotels, hotels, holiday homes, motels. What are the most convenient options? We suggest you to choose the best hotels in Alushta. Among the advantages of such institutions, you can list the following:

  • the highest level of service;
  • a large number of entertainment programs for residents;
  • comfortable rooms in which there is everything necessary for living and recreation;
  • a large number of additional features for customers;
  • many hotels are located on the seashore;
  • there is free internet;
  • tasty and varied meals can be included in the price;
  • beautiful room decoration and much more.

Further we suggest you to get acquainted with the best hotels of Alushta.

"Agora"

Fans of European service will be delighted with this place. The five-story building of the Agora Hotel is located in the city center. The interiors use reproductions of famous artists, paintings by local authors, sculptures, murals, Italian furniture and much more. For customers, 16 cozy rooms are provided. They have comfortable beds, air conditioning, refrigerators, televisions. Each room is equipped with a bath and toilet. The price includes a delicious breakfast. On the top floor of the hotel there is a restaurant with interesting views of the sea. Hotel Agora is located on Lenin Street, 5V.

"Sea"

If you are going to relax with your children, then you will certainly enjoy this small hotel on the beach. There are several playgrounds, spas with pools and much more. Professional animators will be engaged with your children, and the restaurant will offer a special menu, the dishes of which they undoubtedly will like. You will live in luxury cottages. Each of them has different room categories. Some have balconies. The address of the Sea Hotel in Alushta is: Professor's Corner microdistrict, Naberezhnaya 25.

"Fairy tale"

If you want to relax from the city noise, enjoying the sea and high quality service, then do not pass this option. Here you can even rent a room with mountain views. The hotel has several catering establishments, a gym, a medical center, a sauna and much more. At the same time, you do not have to pay extra for some services. The price includes three meals a day, classes in the gym, sauna and much more. "Tale" is located at: Chatyrdagskaya street, 2.

Hotel "Crimea"

Beautiful views of the sea and mountains open from the rooms of the hotel "Crimea" in Alushta. There is everything to make your stay comfortable and carefree. The hotel is located near the city promenade. Nearby are local shops, market, pharmacy. You can settle in one of twenty rooms, which have the necessary furniture and appliances. Guests note that the rest here is very comfortable and enjoyable. Address of the hotel "Crimea": Vladimir Khromykh street, 10.
