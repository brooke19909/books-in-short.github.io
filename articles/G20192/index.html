Turkey. Side - amazing beaches and amazing landscapes

The unique Turkish land with picturesque sea coasts and blue lagoons, majestic mountain peaks, pine forests and beautiful waterfalls attracts a huge number of tourists from all over the world. Many developed resort cities have Turkey. Side is one of the most popular holiday destinations on the south coast of the country.

Location

The city of Side is located only 75 kilometers from Antalya and is a modern, highly developed resort with a rich cultural heritage and archaeological sites. You can get to this small but cozy town by bus from Alanya, Antalya and Konya, with a change in Manavgat. In the summer, minibuses from such large cities as Ankara, Istanbul, Izmir often go here.



Resort Climate

Turkey has excellent climatic conditions. Side is characterized by a mild climate with little humidity, this makes it easy to tolerate the summer heat. The most favorable periods for rest are spring and autumn. At this time, the sun does not bake so much.

Side beaches

Turkey is famous for its unique long and picturesque beaches. Side has two main recreation areas, these include western and eastern beaches. The west is characterized by shallow water and a gentle entrance to the sea. This makes it very attractive and popular, especially for families with children. At the height of the season, both beach areas are crowded with tourists. And those who prefer to retire in the lap of nature should go a little further along the coastline. In this place is located one of the most famous turtle beaches of the Mediterranean Sea. On the coast, tourists are offered many different services and entertainment. Here you can go fishing and water sports, including diving.



Attractions and entertainment

Turkey is famous for its rich history and interesting architectural monuments. Side is the center of ancient attractions. Statues and ancient temples of past centuries are preserved here, surrounded by pristine nature. Interesting places to visit are the ancient amphitheater, the temples of Athens and Apollo, the amazing Roman baths and the ruins of the ancient Greek port.

Not far from Side are the legendary ancient cities of Aspendos and Perge, world history was once created here. Walking through the narrow streets of Side with shops and benches will plunge into the real world of the East with its amazing color and charm. Along the coast there are a huge number of restaurants and bars, which prepare excellent dishes of meat and fish. An amazing and unique place is the resort of Side (Turkey). Reviews of tourists about him are only positive. Spend your holidays on the sandy shores of this unsurpassed resort, and it will bring the most wonderful memories. Charming bays, gentle sandy beaches and a high level of service will remember your stay in Side. Turkey will become one of the most vivid memories if you visit this resort.
