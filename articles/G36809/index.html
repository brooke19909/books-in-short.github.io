Pokrovskoye

Spend time in the forest on the lake, not leaving far from Moscow, many citizens dream of. It is easy to do this, as a rest house in the Moscow region called Pokrovskoye provides an opportunity to visit a unique ecosystem.

In addition to buildings, the base has cottages on a clean lake for a different number of guests. The total capacity of the base is 1200 people, for whom the staff arranges an excellent rest all year round.

Cozy rooms, stylish furniture, handmade lamps, exquisite paintings and mirrors create an atmosphere of relaxation in the best resorts. For those who want to spend their holidays in the Moscow Region fishing, Pokrovskoye is a great chance to leave with a good catch.



In addition to a beautiful lake in the woods, this place boasts a lot of entertainment - here are restaurants, bars, gyms, courts, a beauty salon, a sauna with a Russian steam room, a swimming pool, and a platform for ATVs.

Here you can spend your vacation, or you can just unwind with friends over the weekend.

"Green Grove"

A holiday home in the suburbs located in a real forest is a long-awaited dream of many Muscovites. "Green Grove" refers to this type of boarding house. Once there was the estate of Countess Leskova, from which there was a park with alleys of old lindens.



Located just 32 km from MKAD, this recreation center in the suburbs has not only a forest, but also ponds with the nearby river Zlodeika. In the base building there are double rooms of the category "standard", "junior suite" and "luxury".

The price includes three meals a day. Among the proposed activities there are both active types of recreation (paintball, volleyball, exercise machines), as well as visits to the sauna, billiards and a library.

A horse ride is provided for horse lovers, and for those who like to go fishing or rowing, there is a boat station.

Life in the forest near the river, even on weekends, has a beneficial effect on the nervous system and the general physical condition of the body. If time permits, the Green Grove holiday home is a pleasant option to combine business with pleasure.

Aksakov Dawns

For those Muscovites who want to spend their holidays in a sanatorium near Moscow, the most popular Aksakovsky Dawns complex near Moscow is suitable. Located just 25 km from the capital, the resort is located away from the smoke of factory pipes near a reservoir in a mixed forest. Clean air and a unique ecosystem are a tourist attraction in their own right, but you can also get a wide range of services in the sanatorium.



For clients who decide to take care of their health, they offer mud baths, hydrotherapy and physiotherapy, light and electrotherapy, massage, a full computer diagnostics of the body, psychotherapy and a beauty parlor.

Fans of outdoor activities will enjoy horse riding, a gym and a dance hall, badminton and volleyball courts. After treatments and sports, guests can relax in the sauna or sunbathe in the solarium.

Cozy single and double rooms of various categories are furnished with comfortable upholstered furniture. The price includes three meals a day. Here you can recover well after a working week and devote time to yourself or to rest with your family.

Complex Malibu

Not completely familiar, but very entertaining and romantic will be relaxing in the Malibu complex, located on the water. Three buildings of the recreation center "Malibu" are located on the waters of the Pirogovsky reservoir, and one on the shore among the coniferous-deciduous forest.

Located just 20 km from the Moscow Ring Road, this hotel on the water has become a real attraction of the Moscow region. It consists of a "real" pirate ship "Black Pearl", a copy of the ship Jack Sparrow. The illusion of living in the cabin of an old ship does not disappear even with modern equipment.

The Malibu Hotel will delight lovers of the beautiful landscape from the window. Here you can choose a room with a view of the forest or the water, which look good at any time of the year. Numerous entertainments allow you to use "Malibu" for both personal relaxation and corporate events, weddings or anniversaries.

The Nemo building will especially please children, as its interior decoration resembles the submarine of the famous captain.

The hotel has swimming pools, sports and playgrounds, a well-maintained beach on the shore and on the water platform, a bath complex and saunas with pools. There are so many entertainment programs that weekends fly by instantly.

Sanatorium "Valuevo"

The former Musin-Pushkin manor has become a place for the Valuev Clinical Sanatorium, where many Muscovites come for treatment or for the weekend to prevent diseases of the musculoskeletal system, cardiovascular system, allergies and respiratory organs.



The infrastructure of the sanatorium includes:

  • beauty saloon;
  • medical departments;
  • Church of the Protection of the Holy Virgin;
  • gym;
  • basketball and football grounds;
  • tennis court;
  • library;
  • pools and a sauna;
  • salt cave;
  • Cafe.

The resort attracts not only with a beautiful old park on the banks of the Likova River, but also with its proximity to the city (10 km from MKAD) and high-quality medical care. The price includes three meals a day buffet and 2 treatment procedures. For people too busy with work, this is a great opportunity not only to relax close to home, but also to maintain good health.

Pension Oka

For Muscovites who prefer a vacation in the suburbs on weekends away from the capital, the Oka pension located on this river 120 km from Moscow is best.

Here you can enjoy not only pine forest, clean air and a small river, but also get high-quality medical examination and treatment. For entertainment and relaxation there is a cinema hall, a dance hall, a billiard room and fitness equipment, a sauna with a swimming pool and a solarium, places for cooking barbecue, a boat station, a playground and an equipped beach.

The price includes 3 meals a day, three-day treatment, entertainment programs.

Solontsovo

This cottage town on the banks of the Ikshinskoye reservoir will delight fans of outdoor activities. Comfortable cottages in the middle of the forest are designed for a different number of guests, and numerous sports fields will help them to relax and gain strength.



There are 5 courts, a billiard room, football, basketball and volleyball courts, bowling and ice rink, horseback riding and boat rides, fishing. All this will allow the weekend to pass actively and variably.

Pension "Youth"

Not far from the Klyazma River, 40 km from the Moscow Ring Road in the middle of the ancient park, the Yunost boarding house is located. It attracts guests not only with its beautiful linden park and its own beach on the banks of the pond, but also with the fact that it includes three meals a day, visits to the gym, sauna and pool. For a fee, you can undergo cosmetic and therapeutic procedures that will help restore strength and normalize the body.
