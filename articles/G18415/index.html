Accommodation

Toliman is a recreation center (Sysert), which has two hotels on its territory: Gabbiano and Atlantiko.

Gabbiano

The building, located on the forest edge and surrounded by a pine forest. It offers rooms for 2 to 4 people. The cost varies from 3 to 5 thousand rubles.

Atlantico

Business class building combining modern standards and service. It offers single and double rooms, as well as junior suites and studios. The cost per room is from 2.5 thousand rubles.

The cost of accommodation includes access to the gym, which is located on the 3rd floor of the cottage. All rooms of both complexes have comfortable furniture, a toilet, a shower.
