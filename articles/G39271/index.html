Motels in St. Petersburg: overview, prices and photos

St. Petersburg is a cultural capital, so many tourists always rest in this city. For them, the actual issue is the place of residence. It should not be too expensive. Often people need an overnight stay for just an hour or a day. In this case, you can use the services of motels or hostels.

Hotel "Voyage"

Motel Voyage has been operating since 2004. Previously, it had only 20 rooms: they are not large, but comfortable and clean. In 2016, 58 more rooms were added. All of them are beautifully decorated, have modern equipment. Also in the rooms there is quality furniture.

There is a video surveillance system, electronic locks. The price includes meals. If desired, you can go to the cafe or to the shops, which are located near the hotel. Nearby are some of the attractions. The airport is an average of 6 km.

You can come with pets to this motel in St. Petersburg. There is also parking and a luggage storage room. Shuttle services are available at an extra charge. Room service includes food delivery. Laundry or dry cleaning services are allowed. There is a sauna and a meeting room for 12 people. The hotel is non-smoking.

The reviews write that the rooms are quiet - soundproofing at a good level. Delicious breakfasts are also featured. Of the minuses, they notice that the Internet freezes.

Price per day: about 5200 rubles.

Mini-Hotel "Vintage"

This hostel in St. Petersburg is inexpensive and comfortable.

Guests are offered 15 rooms. They are divided into categories. Some have capsule beds. They are surrounded by a dense curtain made of eco-friendly materials. There are also rooms for honeymooners and families.

In the morning, a table is served. True, breakfast will have to pay extra. Judging by the reviews, the food is delicious. Also next to the motel are cafes, bars, restaurants.

For an additional fee, you can order a transfer. There are cameras for storing personal items. The reception is open 24 hours a day. The mini-hotel has internet access.

Cost per day: about 900 rubles.

Hotel "East-West"

This motel is located on the highway Moscow - St. Petersburg.

Breakfast is served in the morning. There is a bar. The staff is polite.

The rooms have everything you need for a comfortable stay. There is a set of bed linen, the bathroom has towels, a hairdryer, a set of hygiene devices. Air conditioning is installed.

There is also a laundry room. There is internet access. Parking is paid - for a place of 2 thousand rubles.

You can rent a room for negotiations. If necessary, the hotel can call an ambulance.

Cost per day: about 6 thousand rubles.

Mini-Hotel Contact

This motel in St. Petersburg is located on Nevsky Prospekt.

The reception is open 24 hours a day. Shuttle services are available at an extra charge. Conducted heating.

No smoking is allowed at this hostel in St. Petersburg.
