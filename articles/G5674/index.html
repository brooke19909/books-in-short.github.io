What does Sim-Lock mean in the name of the Smart Start model from MTS?





Sim-Lock - the function of restricting the operation of a smartphone within a specific mobile operator. This means that the phone or smartphone marked Sim-Lock can not be used with the SIM card of any other operator except the seller. Smartphone MTS Smart Start Sim-Lock is no exception. The gadget will function only when connected to the MTS network. If the buyer does not have the SIM card of the desired operator, he will have to pay extra for the connection, so if you need to buy a smartphone at the price stated by the store, you should choose the seller with whom you have already concluded a contract for the provision of communication services.
