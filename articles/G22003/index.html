General impression of the hotel

Based on all of the above, you can make a certain impression about the hotel Eri Beach Village 4 * (Hersonissos). Reviews of tourists provide an opportunity to conclude that this is a hotel for unpretentious tourists. Many guests complain about food, citing its monotony. Nevertheless, everything is quite tasty and at home. And if you take into account the assortment of dishes served, then you can eat something new every day and arrange your own variety on your own. This is a good option for those who intend to enjoy the sun and sea, and also does not attach much importance to shortcomings. Moreover, the prices for accommodation and related services are very affordable.

Special attention should be paid to the rooms of the Eri Beach Village 4 * hotel (Crete). Reviews of tourists note that the settlement is somewhat reminiscent of a lottery. You can get into a beautiful and bright renovated room, or you can end up in a small room, the last transformations of which were carried out, probably, about 10 years ago. It is also important to understand that at the same time more than 1000 tourists are resting at the hotel. At the same time, the number of sunbeds by the pool and places in the restaurant clearly does not correspond to the number of tourists. And the staff is so small that it is unable to keep track of everything at once.

Many tourists, precisely because of their location on the sea coast, choose the Eri Beach Village 4 * hotel for themselves. Reviews of tourists note that you will be disappointed upon arrival, because the resort has no beach as such. If you want to soak up the sand and swim enough in the sea, you have to go to the territory of a neighboring hotel (fortunately, there is no extra charge for using their beach). Also, due to the fact that there is a bus stop near the hotel, you can easily get anywhere in the island and appreciate all the beaches.
