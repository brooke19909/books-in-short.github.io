Sea Bird Hotel 3 * (Corfu / Greece) - photos, prices, description and reviews

Hotel Sea Bird Hotel 3 (Corfu) is located in the picturesque authentic Greek village, which bears the name Moraitika. From the capital of the island to the hotel about twenty kilometers, and from the international airport - fourteen. This is a small cozy family-run hotel, not at all like the giant palaces in Egypt or Turkey. Three hundred meters from the center of the village. However, it is in such a small hotel that you can feel the spirit of real Greece. He is beautiful, the service is good. Let's see what tourists say about this hotel, and how it fits into the overall picture of the island's hotels.



Corfu

A wonderful marvelous land with emerald greenery, orange and lemon groves, planted with olives and cypresses. All this is the island of Corfu. On the map of Greece, it is located northwest of the continental part of the country. From its east coast, the mainland is visible in fine weather. Respectable people like to relax here. The coastline of the island is about two hundred kilometers. And almost along its entire length there are long beautiful beaches, both sandy and pebble. Each resort of the island has its own specifics. Benitses is preferred by families with children. Dassia - lovers of taverns and bars. Komeno is a place for a luxurious vacation. The southern resort of Agios Georgios is usually liked by vacationers who love water sports. The village of Moraitika, where the Sea Bird Hotel is located, is located near Benitses. Therefore, it is good for spending holidays with the whole family.

Sea Bird: rooms and facilities

The hotel consists of two buildings and is surrounded by a beautiful garden. The restaurant has a terrace, and parking is available near the hotel. There is also an internet cafe on site. The Sea Bird Hotel 3 (Corfu) has seventy-one rooms. They are designed for one, two, three or four guests. The room size also depends on this. The smallest rooms are twenty square meters, the largest are fifty. The rooms are decorated in a traditional Greek style - the white walls are in perfect harmony with the brown furniture. The rooms are air-conditioned, have televisions. All rooms have balconies. You can see the sea or mountains from the windows. There are also rooms facing the patio. They are less noisy. Nutrition

Sea Bird Hotel 3 (Corfu) usually includes breakfast. There are trips with half board. Most often it is a breakfast-dinner. The main restaurant of the hotel, Voula, specializes in Greek and island cuisine. Food for tourists is served according to the buffet system. Everything is very tasty at home. Breakfasts are ordinary European, even improved. They give sausages, eggs, omelets, jams, feta cheese. For dinner - lamb and beef, wonderful fish. The hotel also has two bars - with cocktails by the pool and in the lobby. But if you want to feel the specifics of local food, be sure to purchase white wine, which is made only on the island. Sitting on the balcony of the hotel and having a snack of "Gravera" cheese, you will understand what real life is!



Sea

From the Sea Bird Hotel 3 (Corfu) to the sea, go a little more than a hundred meters on a flat road. There are no slides or stairs that vacationers in Corfu usually complain about. The water is very clean and clear, the sand on the beach is shallow, the entrance is very gentle. The tanning area itself is wide and long, there are many trees in the shade of which you can settle in. The coast is perfect for relaxing with kids. There are many water activities on the beach. On the way to the sea - two supermarkets. Immediately near the beach - a whole promenade with various eateries and taverns, so there is where to have lunch, without departing far from the sea. In addition, the hotel itself has two outdoor pools - an adult and a children's. Around them are sunbeds and umbrellas, there is a sun terrace.



Greece, Corfu: prices

The average cost of rest for two people with a flight to this hotel is about two thousand euros for eleven to thirteen days. It costs six euros per day. But since the island is often windy at night, tourists can assure without it. Rental of sunbeds and umbrellas on the beach - two to three euros per day. If you do not have lunch at the hotel, then a meal will cost you inexpensively, because the portions are huge, and they can be taken at once for several eaters. What tourists say

Vacationers write that the Hotel Sea Bird 3 (Corfu) is a small, clean hotel, with nice staff. Its location is very successful - it is located not far from the center of the village, and from the sea. Very close is a bus stop that takes you to Corfu, and with transplants ride almost the entire island. There is excellent public transport every twenty minutes. The village of Moraitika itself is one of the best places to live in this resort. There is a beautiful sea, and it is also a convenient starting point for travel. The hotel makes it possible to enjoy a not very expensive, but quite decent vacation in Europe.
