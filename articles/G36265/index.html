Trolleybuses of Volgograd: numbers and routes

Volgograd trolleybuses appeared on the streets of the city in 1960 on December 31. Now in the village there are 12 routes. 280 trolleybuses leave for flights; there are only three depots in the city. The fare is 15 rubles. Transportation is carried out by Metroelektrotrans.

The article will help determine which routes function in the city. Here is a photo of the schedule, as well as a brief description of the transport network.

The history of the development of the trolleybus network of Volgograd

Trolleybus routes (Volgograd) began to expand rapidly after the launch of the first. He connected two districts of the city: Krasnooktyabrsky and Central. The first was the main trolleybus fleet. At the moment, it has an index of number 4, is functioning to this day.

Initially, the park consisted of only 20 vehicles, there were 30 drivers. Given that the trolleybus service is open on the last day of December, staff training began in May. The drivers were representatives of the first and second classes of cadets. The training was carried out by specialists who arrived from the city of Saratov. The courses ended a few months before the start of work.

Volgograd trolleybuses eventually increased in number, which is good news. From 20, the number of vehicles increased to 304. The development of the network continues to this day, but the number of cars has decreased. Gradually new routes are opening up. We are talking about 8a and 15a (2003), 18 (2004), 14 (2009), 1 (2008)

Several years ago, Metroelectrotrans held small lotteries and raffles between passengers on trolleybuses and trams. Prizes were tickets valid for only one mode of transport and for only 15 days, as well as special tickets with a protective layer.

In 2008, the long-forgotten route No. 1 was restored. The sequence of flights and the length of flights have not changed. The final stop of the trolleybus (Volgograd) at number 1: "Station" - "Village VGTZ". A year later, the place of departure of the vehicle was changed to the "Children's Center".



Fare in transport

In 2009, the fare on the trolley bus was increased. From 8 rubles, it increased to 10. A month later, local authorities canceled free travel for students of different ages, and a year later the ticket cost 12 rubles. In 2015, it increased to 15 rubles. In 2017, it is planned to introduce a new price - 20 rubles.

To buy a ticket for all types of transport, you need to spend 1 thousand rubles., One - 700 rubles. If desired, you can present a student and purchase a ticket at a reduced cost.

2013 terrorist attack

On December 30, 2013, in the morning at one of the stops of route 15, a trolley bus (Volgograd is a city where this rarely happens) working on a flight was blown up. It happened at the "Kachinsky school" (Dzerzhinsky district). Salon completely burned out. The driver survived, 28 people were injured, 14 were killed. The attack was committed by a suicide bomber. The police opened a criminal case under two articles: 205 and 222. The incident was a tragedy not only of the city, but of the whole country. In that year, several such attacks were committed.
