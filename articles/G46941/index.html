Gray salary

How wages are calculated

As a rule, salaries for security guards are not calculated as monthly salaries. Usually it is formed on the basis of the working hour, respectively its cost. On average, per hour, guards earn from 100-250 p. In this case, everything will depend on how many shifts the employee is ready to withstand. Accordingly, the longer you work, the more you can get.

Based on this, the salary of a security guard in Moscow per day can reach up to 6,000 rubles. But such proposals are extremely rare. Some earn only 2,400 rubles in 24 hours.
