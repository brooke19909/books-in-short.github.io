Hotel "Venus Resort", Vityazevo: reviews, overview, description, rooms and features

Elegant design and comfortable living conditions captivate guests of the resort hotel "Venus Resort" (Vityazevo). Reviews about this institution allow you to make an objective opinion about the rest with all the positive aspects and possible shortcomings.

Hotel Description

A fairly new resort hotel is the "Venus Resort" (Vityazevo). Reviews, however, allow us to conclude that this institution managed to fall in love with tourists. The main secret of the hotel is high-quality service, homeliness, calm atmosphere and very low prices. Here you can have a great rest both alone and with family or friends. Fans of sea holidays especially like this accommodation option as the "Venus Resort" (Vityazevo). Reviews indicate a convenient location on the first coastline. Here you can enjoy the mild climate, clean sandy beach, clear water and fresh air.

Hotel Rooms

Luxurious living conditions are offered to travelers by the Venus Resort (Vityazevo). Reviews note the following accommodation options:

  • Suite is a spacious room, consisting of two comfortable rooms. Given the possibility of installing extra beds, it can accommodate up to 4 guests. The room is dominated by calm pastel colors and natural materials. A pleasant surprise will be a balcony with panoramic views of the sea coast.
  • Studio is a spacious room, which is almost identical in size to a suite. There is a lot of light, open space, as well as several functional areas (bedroom, living room and hallway).
  • Standard rooms can be designed for single, double or triple occupancy. The rooms can be equipped with both single and large beds. You have the opportunity to choose the accommodation option with or without a balcony.
  • Economy, despite its compact size, is equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. Thanks to the ceiling windows there is a lot of light in the daytime, and at night you can admire the stars.

General impression

If you plan to spend your vacation in a place like the Venera Resort Hotel (Vityazevo), the reviews of experienced tourists will be an excellent guide for you. First, it‚Äôs worth mentioning the positive aspects. Given that the hotel is quite new, here you will not find shabby furniture and outdated equipment. Outwardly, everything looks very beautiful and well-groomed. As for nutrition, it is delicious and satisfying at home. Here you will not find culinary masterpieces of a restaurant level, but even those simple dishes that are presented on the menu will leave you a good impression.

If we talk about the negative aspects, then there are also quite a lot of them in the "Venus Resort" (Venera Resort). The hotel in Vityazevo declares the availability of wireless Internet throughout the territory, and in particular in the rooms. Nevertheless, on arrival you will find unpleasant disappointment. The signal is very weak and works only in the lobby area and on the balconies of some rooms. Even if you warn staff in advance that this option is necessary for you, no one will recognize you as having problems. Another unpleasant moment is the constant interruptions in the water supply. To swim, the guests of the upper floors have to wait until the other residents finish their water procedures.

If you want to address any problem or claim to the administration, then you should not count on an early resolution of the problem. Of course, they will carefully listen to you, but everything will remain in its place. If, after repeated communication with the administration, you want to complain to the manager or owner, then most likely you will also not find understanding.
