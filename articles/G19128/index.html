Hotel Sun Hotel Amoudara 3 * (Greece, Crete): photo and reviews of tourists

If you are planning a beach holiday in Europe, then you probably thought about Greece. This is a good option, because there are many resorts in the country, and the prices here are relatively low, especially considering the high quality of service.

The island of Crete is considered to be one of the most interesting and popular resort places in Greece. Here you will find hundreds of large hotels and cozy guest houses where tourists are greeted with genuine hospitality. One of such places is a small hotel Sun Hotel Amoudara. Of course, first you need to get acquainted with all the available information about this hotel.

Hotel Sun Hotel Amoudara (Crete): what tourist rooms look like





The hotel complex is considered relatively small - on its territory there are 58 rooms. For the most part, these are standard double rooms, but there are more spacious rooms. The atmosphere here is simple, but the furniture is solid and comfortable. There are beds, bedside tables, tables, spacious wardrobes, in short, everything you need. And from your own balcony you can enjoy breathtaking views of the mountains and the sea.

Since the life of a modern tourist is difficult to imagine without technology, the hotel has everything you need for a comfortable stay, including powerful air conditioning, a TV and a telephone. For a fee, you can use the fridge, as well as a small but reliable safe.

There is also a small bathroom with a shower, a washbasin and a wall mirror. There is also a small but powerful hair dryer for drying hair. You can count on clean towels that really change daily during a thorough cleaning of the rooms.

Food scheme: what to expect for a traveler





Guests of the Sun Hotel Amoudara 3 hotel complex themselves choose a food plan. Typically, tourists pay for breakfast on site. There is a spacious, cozy restaurant, where guests are invited. The menu, as the guests say, is quite decent - breakfast includes pastries, fresh fruits, salads, light snacks and hot dishes. The restaurant is open during the rest of the day, however, it is cooked to order and bills must be paid separately. The food here is delicious, and the prices are quite normal.

The hotel also has a bar, where guests prefer to spend evenings relaxing with a glass of strong drink. By the way, in this area there are a sufficient number of cafes and taverns where you can eat and dive into the local flavor.

Distance to the beach and amenities by the sea





The hotel complex Sun Hotel Amoudara is located almost on the seashore - the distance is only 200 meters. In a few minutes you can reach one of the most picturesque beaches on the island - a convenient path leads to it.

For a small fee, you can rent a sunbed with an umbrella. There is a sports center on the beach where you can rent a boat, ride a boat or water ski, do various water sports. There is also a bar where tourists are offered chilled drinks, cocktails - you can even have lunch on the incredibly beautiful beach.

According to reviews, the beach here is clean and well maintained, cleaned on the beach daily. The entrance to the sea is convenient, and the bottom is covered with soft sand. Here, both adult tourists and their kids have a great time.

Additional service: list of services for hotel guests





Tourists staying at the Sun Hotel Amoudara can expect some additional services. Here at the hotel you can rent vehicles, including cars and bicycles, which is important for travelers who prefer freedom of movement. By the way, there is a free parking for guests.

You can easily leave your luggage in a special storage room. For valuables, you can get a safe deposit box at the reception. At your service is a small business center where tourists can use computer and office equipment, as well as send a fax. They really tried to create all the conditions for a really good and comfortable stay.

Leisure of tourists: how to spend your free time





At Sun Hotel Amoudara, tourists mostly relax after a day on the beach or in the city. Nevertheless, you can relax here. On the territory there is a small but colorful garden - here guests love to hide from the summer heat and just walk, enjoying the bright colors.

There is also a small pool where water is cleaned almost every day. Near the terrace there are large, comfortable sun loungers and wide umbrellas. Here, tourists relax, take sun baths and just chat, get to know each other.

The hotel has a tour center where travelers are told about the most interesting sights of the island, which is practically the cradle of European civilization. Here you can buy an interesting tour of the surroundings or make a route for an independent trip.
