Speakers Sven MS-1080: specifications and reviews

Sven MS-1080 is a 2.1-format speaker system that for a fairly small price offers the user everything you need to enjoy good, pleasant and high-quality sound. In today's review, we will consider this model in detail, get acquainted with its characteristics, capabilities, reviews and the current price for today. Let's get started!





Description

Review Sven MS-1080 I would like to start with a description of the audio system. This model has been on sale since about 2010 and is quite successfully in demand to this day. The main reasons for its popularity are 3 main factors: price, quality, durability. Yes, it is possible that Sven's acoustics do not catch stars from the sky, but most of their models sound very high-quality for their money and continue to delight their owners for many years.





The type of Sven MS-1080 audio system is 2.1, the total power of the kit is 40 W, which is more than standard for the budget segment, although there are models from other manufacturers with less power, but this is not so important. Another interesting feature of these speakers is the case, which is completely made of MDF. There are plastic elements, but they are few. The design of the model is also quite interesting and will not leave anyone indifferent. Now in more detail about everything and in order.

Contents of delivery

Sven MS-1080 for sale in a rectangular cardboard box. The sizes are average, no carrying handles. On the box you can see the image of the speaker system, the name of the model and read the main characteristics. The design of the packaging is completely standard for Sven, and even the company colors for the flag of Finland are respected, in fact, where did this brand come from in the technology market.





Inside, in addition to the system consisting of two satellites and a subwoofer, there are: a set of RCA cables to 3.5 mm, a set of instructions in several languages, a wired control panel with two RCA plugs and one 3.5 mm jack for connecting to a computer sound card and warranty card. Actually, there is nothing else inside.

The appearance of acoustics

The appearance of the Sven MS-1080 speakers is quite interesting. In general, designers at Sven very often surprised users with their products, or rather the form. There were spheres, and cylinders, and rectangles, and even flat columns resembling a flying saucer. In this case, the acoustics are made in the form of squares. Moreover, everything has been done so compactly that the entire set can be installed at one point, for example, the satellites are simply placed on top of the subwoofer, and the entire set takes up a minimum of space.





As for the colors, Sven MS-1080s are sold only in black, but what catches the eye is a red border around the speaker grids. Such a combination of colors looks very beautiful and to some extent even slightly aggressive, but is the kit really good, we will check a little later.

If we talk about satellites, then on their sides there are no connectors or holes. On the back there is only an output of wires for connecting to a subwoofer, and 4 rubber legs are glued to the bottom so that the speakers better stay in place and do not move anywhere. There is only one option for installing satellites, and it will not work to suspend the speakers. Rather, it can be done, but here the user himself will have to drill holes, look for fasteners, etc.





The only thing that draws attention to the satellites is the front side on which the speakers are mounted. They are covered with a metal mesh with small perforations in the form of circles. A plastic overlay with a red edge and a glossy finish frames the mesh.





Now let's move on to the subwoofer. On its back side is the outlet of the bass reflex pipe. The left part is empty, but on the right in a special recess there are connectors for connecting satellites and connecting a control panel. A little lower is the output of the power cord and the on / off button. Well, of course, there are two controls, one of which is responsible for volume, and the other for bass. I would like to see another twist to adjust the tone, but alas.





And the last - the front of the subwoofer. It housed a huge speaker, which is also covered by a perforated mesh and framed by a plastic, glossy frame with a red edge. In the middle of the net flaunts a shiny Sven logo. By the way, the framing frames on the subwoofer and satellites differ in their shape. For speakers, the frame is made in the shape of an inverse cone, and for a subwoofer, on the contrary, a direct one. It looks a little strange, but not critical.





In conclusion, it is worth saying a few words about the control panel. It is made in the form of a UFO and has exactly the same coloring as the satellites and the subwoofer. At the top is a large volume control knob. An interesting point that is connected with the volume here is the inability to make the volume more than it is set on the subwoofer. In other words, if the volume control on the sub is half, then the controller on the remote control will not be able to make sound above this level. On the one hand, it‚Äôs good, but on the other, it‚Äôs pointless. The remote also has two 3.5 mm jacks for headphones and an external playback device.

Key Specifications

It's time to take a look at the features of the Sven MS-1080. Here, in fact, they are:

  • Type - 2.1.
  • Power - 40 watts.
  • Power speakers - 10 watts (total - 20 watts).
  • Power subwoofer - 20 watts.
  • The frequency range of satellites is 150-20 000 Hz.
  • The subwoofer frequency range is 50-150 Hz.
  • Type of connection - wired, mains powered.
  • Type of control - manual.
  • Connectors and inputs - RCA, 3.5 mm.
  • The size of the speakers of the satellites is 76 mm.
  • The number of speakers on the satellites is 1.
  • The size of the subwoofer speaker is 165 mm.
  • The dimensions of the satellites (W / H / D) are 110/110/120 mm.
  • The dimensions of the subwoofer (W / H / D) are 220/230/268 mm.
  • Weight - 3.95 kg (total).




Sound quality

Now is the time to talk about the sound quality of the Sven MS-1080. In short, the sound is excellent. Not ideal, of course, since there are obvious dips at high frequencies. There is a problem with a low range in the region of 200-400 Hz. This is very audible when watching movies. And one more thing - the position of the acoustics and the listener.





The fact is that depending on the location, the sound quality also changes. If a person is located directly in a straight point in front of the system, then there will be no problems, but if you just deviate a little from the speakers, you will immediately hear changes, and not for the better. It turns out a kind of kit for single-handed students.





User reviews

Basically, the reviews about the Sven MS-1080 are positive, although it still could not do without some cons. So, users note that the speakers have short wires, there is a rumble in the subwoofer at a high enough volume, lack of low frequencies and a problem with high ones, and there is no speaker shielding on the subwoofer, which is why the signals from the mobile phone can be heard without problems. Otherwise, users have no complaints.





Actual value

At the moment, you can buy a set of speakers at a price of 3700-4000 rubles. Just want to note that the price tag is very high. This is explained by the fact that the model is gradually disappearing from the market and not every store is available. It is not recommended to buy these speakers for such a price, since there are many alternatives from both Sven and other manufacturers.

Summary

It's time to take stock of today's review. Sven MS-1080 is a pretty good speaker system that will completely satisfy the tastes and needs of most listeners. If you do not take into account the lack of low and high frequencies and the deterioration of sound when deviating from the radius of the sound, then this model can be called one of the best today. But, unfortunately, a too high price tag does not allow recommending the system for purchase.
