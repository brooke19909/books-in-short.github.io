Best TV: 3D or LCD?

Choosing a new TV can confuse anyone. Indeed, the assortment of models and the latest technologies for the production of household and video equipment make consumers who are not familiar with their distinctive features get lost. For example, how to choose the best TV, what to prefer: LCD or 3D?

Modern models are divided into several types: LCD, with LED-backlight, plasma, 3D and others. They differ in size, case thickness, price category, the presence of additional functions, such as a USB interface, support for Full HD, the ability to access the Internet, built-in video player. Online stores conduct their own research on the popularity of models and create a rating that is built thanks to customer feedback.





Most of the positive reviews and, accordingly, the title of "Best 3D TV", according to consumers, deserves technology from Samsung. Models with a diagonal of forty inches (one hundred and two centimeters) are popular. The TV supports formats such as Full HD and HDTV, which allows you to watch digital television. It is possible to convert 2D to 3D. Other models of the same manufacturer have the following functions: Search All, Social TV, Smart Hub, there is a web browser for accessing the Internet and support for the WI-FI network. The resolution of the best TV is 1920 x 1080. There is an analog and satellite receiver. These TV models, equipped with LED-backlight, are designed to watch TV programs in 3D, for which purpose the special glasses that come with the kit. Buyers are satisfied with the choice and recommend these TV models to their friends.





According to buyers, the best TV from Samsung is a good choice. It gives a beautiful image, especially digital TV channels and in 3D. Consumers like a range of advanced features, such as picture-in-picture. Praise the quality of the glasses that come with the TV. Moreover, it is possible to purchase such an accessory in addition to increase the number of viewers. Also, 3D TVs from LG deserve positive feedback. Buyers say that they like the picture quality in the model, a convenient menu, Magic Remote, and a beautiful design.





If the question arises of how to choose the best LCD TV, then first of all, models with high-quality LED backlight should be considered. This technology is considered more advanced and extends the life of the equipment. Liquid crystal television (LCD) televisions are suitable for placement in small rooms. The size of the diagonal can vary from thirty-two to forty inches. Screen resolution is better to choose at least 800 x 600 pixels. Like 3D, LCD TVs have additional features. For example, a USB port for connecting flash cards. For viewing high quality video, digital outputs HDMI and DVI can be provided. In the category "Best LED TV" will also featured models of Samsung, as a recognized leader in the electronics market. Although buyers and researchers pay tribute to other popular brands: Sony, Toshiba, Acer, Sharp.

The choice of a TV is a subjective matter. After all, when you purchase, you will be guided by your own taste, the technical characteristics of a particular model and financial capabilities. Therefore, for you, the best TV is one that meets the criteria you set.
