The recreation center "Comet" (Vladivostok): how to get to the ski center?

The destination can be reached by car - a free parking space is provided. You should follow the M60 highway (Vladivostok-Khabarovsk) to the stop of the Botanical Garden public transport. See the right turn - turn off. About 8-9 km after the pass, you will see the Comet road sign - move towards the recreation center along the gravel road.

You can also get on the bus. Every day, public transport number 28 runs from Semenovskaya Square. The schedule should be clarified in the information desk. You need to get off at the Military Garrison stop. Then you have to walk about 400-500 meters to the base.

Accommodation options

The recreation center "Comet" (Vladivostok) offers both standard accommodation conditions and premium class for discerning tourists. All buildings harmoniously fit into the natural landscape, decorated in a single wooden style. Daily cleaning and quality service are provided.

Two-storey cottage "Teremok"





Located near the ski slope. Rooms are for rent on the second floor. Toilet and shower are on the floor. In the room you will find comfortable beds, a wardrobe, bedding and hygiene products, as well as a TV. On the ground floor of the building there are recreation areas and a sports shop.

Standard rooms in the house "Hut"

Small rustic house with an open veranda. A cottage was built on a ski training track overlooking a forested area. The room has a TV, a compact refrigerator and the necessary furniture. The bathroom is shared - on the floor.

The hotel complex "The noise is gone"





The three-story building with spacious and comfortable rooms is designed for couples and large companies. Located near the ice rink. The interior is made of environmentally friendly material - wood. Available double and quadruple rooms. There is a private bathroom with a toilet.

Hotel "Country Club"

The recreation center "Comet" (Vladivostok / Shamora) invites you to stay in a comfortable house with an attic, a private terrace and panoramic mountain views (a hot breakfast is provided). It has only four rooms. The apartments are spacious, different categories:

  • Standard room with a bathroom. The room has a desk, a double bed, soft chairs, bedside tables.
  • Junior suite with three separate rooms: two bedrooms and a living room.
  • Corner white room with a bedroom and a living room.
  • Suite with three rooms. There is a personal kitchen area with a bar, appliances and utensils. A separate cot or bed is available upon request.

Three-storey cottage "Outskirts"





It is located at a remote distance from the rest of the houses - near the highway. Standard rooms with general conditions (bathroom, shower).

One-story mansion "Elite"

Located next to the cottage "Outskirts". You can rent the whole house. Suitable for a large company (six-seater).

Winter fun

The main audience of tourists comes with one purpose - to ride a lot of snowboarding, cheesecakes, ice skating, skiing. Three ski slopes with ski lifts were built on the territory . All tracks have different lengths: for beginners and professionals.





For beginners, the recreation center "Comet" (Vladivostok) offers to visit a training slope 300 meters long. If you wish, you can use the services of an experienced instructor. For more experienced people, a more winding track (500 m) is provided. And finally, for real professionals - a difficult slope with sharp turns. It can be used for snowboarding and skiing.

For kids - safe children's tracks. By the way, all ski slopes are cleaned daily for better gliding. In addition, the center has landscaped areas with swings, colorful sandboxes, slides and playgrounds, so that young tourists have something to do.

Rental equipment





The recreation center "Comet" (Vladivostok) in the care of guests constantly improves the territory so that tourists feel comfortable at home. It offers cafeterias, ice rinks, ice slides. Even if you do not have a special winter uniform, it does not matter. The necessary sports equipment will be issued on site.
