Hotel Waldorf Astoria Ras Al Khaimah 5 * AOE, Ras Al Khaimah: photos and reviews

On the coast of the Arabian Sea, a luxurious resort complex Waldorf Astoria Ras Al Khaimah 5 * is located. Here, travelers can immerse themselves in the atmosphere of a real oriental tale, adapted to the most modern international standards of relaxation.





How to get there

In one of the most picturesque places is the hotel Waldorf Astoria Ras Al Khaimah 5 *. Tours usually include a shuttle service. If you prefer to rent a car, you can get to the hotel as follows:

  • From Dubai International Airport you need to go to Emirates Rd, follow the signs for Sharjah. Upon reaching the roundabout, follow the signs for Ras Al Khaimah. It will have to overcome almost 90 km, which will take 50-60 minutes.
  • From Ras Al Khaimah Airport, follow the road leading to Oman. At the first intersection, turn left. At the next, do the same. After passing the golf course, take the roundabout and turn left following the directions for Sheikh Mohammed bin Sulaym. The path equal to 20 km, you will overcome in 15-20 minutes.

Accommodation

Guests of Waldorf Astoria Ras Al Khaimah 5 * can enjoy luxury and comfort in their apartments. Deluxe rooms meet the needs of the most demanding tourists. Travelers can choose one of the following options for accommodation.

The classic room features a flawless design done in fresh colors. The room has a huge bed (or two and a half) and a small seating area. Business people will appreciate the availability of a convenient desk. The bathroom is decorated with natural marble. Guests can soak in the jacuzzi or chill out in the rain shower.

Deluxe is a 76 sqm luxury room. m. Its main feature is a panoramic balcony, from which you can enjoy bewitching views of the Arabian Sea or enjoy the golf course. The living area and work area are separated from the bedroom by a decorative screen. The marble bathroom includes a hydromassage.

Junior Suite is a unique combination of classic and modern interior. The room is decorated in warm caramel colors using traditional oriental elements. The apartments have a cozy bedroom, a spacious living room, as well as a separate dressing room.

Imperial Suite is a spacious suite that occupies 422 square meters. m. The room is located immediately on two floors and has its own staircase that leads to the front door. The apartments have a luxurious bedroom, a private kitchen with appliances and a dining table, a marble bathroom and a large balcony with sea views. The highlight of this room is a huge living room, which combines luxurious classic design and oriental decorative elements. Music lovers will appreciate the presence of their own piano.

Tower Suite - a spacious two-room suite. In the bedroom, in addition to a large bed, there are also upholstered furniture for relaxation and additional accommodation. Passing through the spacious lobby, you will find yourself in a bright living room with a panoramic window. Enjoy a view of the golf course from your private balcony.

restaurants and bars

You can have a good time and eat delicious food in the restaurants and bars of Waldorf Astoria Ras Al Khaimah 5 * . Appreciate the luxurious interior and quality of food in such establishments:

  • Al Fayrouz - Restaurant and bar in the fresh air. The facility is located next to the pool. During the day, you can eat and drink a refreshing drink, and in the evening enjoy the entertainment program.
  • Qasr al Bahar is the main restaurant of the hotel. It works on a buffet system.
  • Lexington Grill is a restaurant that specializes in grilled dishes. You can relax in the cozy room or at the bar. For lovers of secluded surroundings, private rooms are provided.
  • UMI is a Japanese restaurant. The traditional-style establishment is located on the ground floor of the hotel.
  • 17Squared - A molecular restaurant. Here you can try the most unusual dishes from the chefs. The original interior and musical accompaniment emphasize the general atmosphere of the institution.
  • Azure - Italian restaurant. It has a cozy and bright dining room, as well as an outdoor area by the pool.
  • Camelia is a cozy lounge bar located in the lobby area. There is a quiet and calm atmosphere. In addition to drinks and snacks, guests will also enjoy the hookah.

Additional Information

Very popular is the hotel Waldorf Astoria Ras Al Khaimah 5 *. Photos of rooms and public areas attract attention. If you liked this accommodation option, check out a number of additional information before you travel:

  • free cots are available for babies under 2 years old;
  • Extra bed costs 350 AED;
  • only one additional guest is allowed in each room;
  • pets are not allowed;
  • 50% discount in all hotel restaurants for children;
  • A basic wireless Internet package is available for free, and a high-speed connection is available at an additional cost;

  • Guests are charged a tourist tax.

Conclusion

There is not so much entertainment in the hotel, but in order to relax outside, you have to do a rather long and expensive taxi ride.
