Rafee Hotel 2 * (Dubai, United Arab Emirates): description, reviews of tourists

The city of Dubai offers its guests a variety of leisure facilities. To visit this amazing place, it is not necessary to have a huge amount of money. You can relax in pretty good conditions without spending a significant part of your budget at the Rafee Hotel 2 *.

Where is the hotel situated

The city of Dubai will be interesting to visit for every tourist. Only here you can see how magnificent modern skyscrapers coexist peacefully with high-profile Arab markets and mosques. To get acquainted with the vibrant life of the city, you can stay at the Rafee Hotel 2 *, which is located just a 10-minute walk from the central shopping district of Dubai.



Getting to the hotel from the airport is just 15 minutes. The distance is 8 km. The sea is 6.5 km away. Tourists arriving at the Rafee Hotel 2 * are taken to the beach by bus for free.

Hotel Description

The hotel is a 7-story building. It is made in a harmonious combination of modern and Arabic styles. Since Rafee Hotel 2 * is located in the city, it cannot boast of a large territory.



The lobby is quite comfortable. Here you can settle down on comfortable upholstered furniture if delays have occurred with check-in, although arriving guests here are allocated according to numbers rather quickly.

Infrastructure

The administration of the Rafee Hotel 2 * hotel (Dubai, UAE) is working around the clock, which allows guests at any time to contact the reception with questions. But, according to many tourists, the staff practically does not speak Russian, so it will be difficult to achieve mutual understanding.

There is a left-luggage office near the reception desk, where you can place your luggage for free. There is also a tour desk, which can offer a huge list of attractions that you can visit if you wish.



The hotel has a swimming pool, which is located on the roof of the building. Cleanliness of the guests is not satisfactory, since the pool is cleaned regularly. Near the pond there are sunbeds and umbrellas. If you wish, you can relax and sunbathe here.

The hotel has a beauty salon where you can enjoy a massage, make a hairstyle or take a steam bath in the sauna. All services offered by the salon are provided at an additional cost. For money, you can hand over clothes to the laundry, where they will quickly be put in order.

The hotel provides business people with the opportunity to organize meetings or negotiations in the conference room. You can use it only for an additional fee. If necessary, a fax is provided, for the use of which you will have to pay.

About room stock

The hotel Rafee Hotel 2 * (Dubai) offers 215 rooms for check-in. You can choose a standard double room or opt for improved options. According to tourists, the condition of the rooms will not greatly please. The rooms here are quite small, and besides, renovation would not hurt them. The same can be said about furniture, which has a slightly shabby appearance. Often, tourists in their reviews complain about hard and not very comfortable beds, but you can still spend the night here.

Each room has a TV, which guests can use for free. There is also air conditioning. An electronic safe is provided for storing valuables. According to tourists, it is not available in all rooms. If there is no safe in the room, it is better to hand over things to the left-luggage office at the reception.



There is a telephone in every room. International calls on it can only be made at a surcharge. Calls at the reception do not have to pay. The rooms have wired internet. It is provided for use only at a surcharge.

Bathrooms include a shower and hairdryer. Superior rooms have a bathtub. Tourists very often respond not very flattering about the state of plumbing. According to them, taps do not always work here and bathroom door locks may be malfunctioning.

What tourists say about food

Hotel Rafee Hotel 2 * provides its guests with the opportunity to choose the most suitable meal plan: only breakfast or half board. In any case, guests are expected in the main restaurant of the Coffee-shop hotel. According to tourists, the menu offered here is not very diverse, but still some of the dishes on offer can be chosen. Not happy and the quality of the food. According to vacationers who have visited the hotel earlier, a strange smell emanates from some dishes, which indicates a long shelf life of products.



As for the restaurant staff, then also there were some negative reviews. Many tourists point to poorly washed dishes, so before eating, it will be useful to wipe the forks or spoons.

A varied selection of dishes is offered by the Indian restaurant, which operates at the hotel. You can also have a meal in various cafeterias located near the hotel. Many tourists are advised to visit the Afghan restaurant, where you can try delicious kebabs of flavored lamb.

Entertainment

You can spend your free time at the hotel while playing billiards. Tables are available at an additional cost. Sports enthusiasts can work out in the gym, where guests have free access to the hotel.

Young people vacationing at Rafee Hotel 2 * will be interested in spending time in two night clubs. They are located on the ground floor of the room.

Features of a beach holiday at the Rafee Hotel 2 * hotel (United Arab Emirates, Dubai)

Dubai abounds with beautiful beaches. Hotel guests can choose to visit Al Mamzar Beach or Jumeirah Beach Park. Both beaches belong to the city. Vacationers from the hotel provide a free shuttle service to both places of rest. The bus runs only at a certain time, which is better to check directly with the driver, since there were cases when the reception provided erroneous information. If you do not want to be tied to the bus schedule, you can get to the bay by taxi.



Entrance to the beach is paid. For a certain amount of money, here you can take on sunbeds and umbrellas. Tourists are advised to give preference to the beach Jumeirah Beach Park, because it is much better looked after. According to vacationers, on Al Mamzar Beach you can stumble on garbage, which does not allow you to fully enjoy the beach and the sea.

Hotel Rafee Hotel 2 *: tourist reviews

Rafee Hotel is a budget option for a vacation, so many people living here do not recommend waiting for any privileges from the establishment. But still, according to tourists, living conditions here will not appeal to even the most unpretentious guests. Old furniture and condition of bathrooms are not conducive to a pleasant rest.

Also, guests of the hotel criticize the quality of the food offered here. And most of all, tourists do not like the attitude of the staff towards hotel guests, which cannot be called respectful.

Noisy street on which the Rafee Hotel 2 * hotel (UAE, Dubai) is not conducive to a good rest. The city wakes up around three in the morning, and the noise outside the windows will not allow you to enjoy a sweet dream. In the evening, music that rumbles in night clubs will not let you fall asleep for a long time. Guests who rise to the pool can also wake up, although it is not allowed to use it after eight in the evening. The hotel does not very carefully monitor compliance with this rule.

Tourists who visit the hotel recommend that not very demanding young people who spend all their free time on excursions or on the beach settle here. Because of the noise, living with children here will be problematic. Also, the hotel is suitable for a business trip.
