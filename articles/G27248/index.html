Description

If you are looking for an inexpensive but decent hotel in the UAE, then Ibis Styles Dubai Jumeirah 3 * in Dubai is just what you need. The complex is considered one of the most economical hotels in Jumeirah, but it deserves its three stars and even more. The hotel is new, was built in 2015, is a six-story building with spacious rooms and panoramic views. The hotel has 191 rooms, there are three restaurants, an indoor pool, a wellness center and a fitness center. Ibis is open all year and is ready to receive guests at any time.

Location

Ibis Styles Dubai Jumeirah 3 * is considered a city hotel complex, which is located in the business district, near the center of the emirate, near the airport and Jumeirah beach, 40 kilometers from the city of Sharjah. Near the hotel there are many cafes and shops, the road to the main attractions of Dubai takes a little time. The nearest beach is about 30 minutes walk, can be reached using taxi services, the bus does not go to the beach.

Rooms

The hotel is large enough, has 191 comfortable rooms, with all necessary appliances and furniture. According to the type of rooms, rooms are divided into Standard (with a double or two single beds), luxury (with a living room and two single or double beds). All rooms of the Ibis Styles Dubai Jumeirah 3 * hotel (United Arab Emirates, Dubai) have:

  • Air conditioning.
  • TV.
  • Refrigerator.
  • Safe.
  • Ironing facilities.
  • Bathroom.




There are special rooms and services for people with disabilities. Upon check-in, the tourist is required to pay a deposit in case of unplanned costs.

Service

As for the service, many guests of Ibis Styles Dubai Jumeirah 3 * in the reviews note that the hotel justifies its three stars and even deserves more. The service here is excellent, the service is top notch, the staff is very friendly and helpful. It offers rooms for banquets and conferences, business services, free parking for cars, 24-hour front desk, Internet throughout the hotel, luggage storage, transfer, laundry, restaurants, swimming pool, tour desk.





Beach

All beaches in Dubai are comfortable, equipped with showers, dressing rooms and toilets, with fluffy sand and turquoise sea surface. The beach season here lasts all year round, but nevertheless, the period from September to May is considered the most favorable period for water procedures and sunbathing.

The country has both municipal beach areas and private beaches, the entrance to which is carried out for money. The entire coastline here is clean and well-maintained. Umbrellas and sun loungers are offered for a small fee.





One of the favorite places for tourists to rest is Jumeirah Beach, located near the Burj Khalifa Tower. Everything is beautifully decorated here, lawns are spread out everywhere, palm trees grow, there are playgrounds for children and specially designated places for picnics. It is this beach that is considered the closest to Ibis Styles Hotel Dubai Jumeirah 3 *. You can take a taxi from the hotel to it for just 20 dirhams.

For the same amount you can drive from the hotel to the beach Kite Beach, known to many as a kite beach. Here begins the expanse for fans of kitesurfing. Despite the fact that there are no amenities on the beach, it is popular with both Arabs and foreigners. Being in the emirate, you should definitely go to this beach at least once.

For 30 dirhams from the hotel you can reach the second most popular beach in Dubai called Mamzar. The park is beautiful and green, and its main "trick" is a huge pool.

Vacation with children

Many tourists believe that spending time with children in the UAE is not a good option: the winds are constantly blowing, there are no mini-clubs, there are many attractions that may seem uninteresting to them. But still, some may disagree with this conclusion. Children will never be superfluous to bask in the sun, swim in the warm sea and develop culturally. Almost all hotels in the emirates are happy to accept children, and Ibis Styles Hotel Dubai Jumeirah 3 * in Jumeirah is no exception. Here children of any age are allowed to stay, when placing a baby up to 6 years old (without a separate bed), accommodation is provided to him completely free of charge. An extra bed is charged at AED 150 per night. Children under three years old have breakfast at the hotel for free; children under 12 years old require a supplement of half the price. For the entertainment of children, the hotel has an indoor play area. The only thing that can create inconvenience in the hotel is a long road to the beach. But from any situation you can find a way out and get to the sea by taxi or by a rented car.

Prices

The cost of a flight from Moscow and accommodation in this hotel complex starts from 75 thousand rubles for two for a week with breakfast. If you want to have dinner at the hotel, the price increases by about 20 thousand rubles for the entire period of rest.
