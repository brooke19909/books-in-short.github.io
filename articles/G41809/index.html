Northern region of Voronezh: hell on the roads and a million new buildings

The northern district of Voronezh (Moskovsky Prospekt, General Lizyukova, Antonov-Ovseenko, Khodzunov, Victory Boulevard and many other small streets) is a sleeping quarter, which is not an independent intracity formation. It is located close to the center and at the same time is famous for some of the problems that we will now discuss.

Traffic jams

The northern region of Voronezh is famous among the townspeople for its amazing traffic load. To get to work in the center in the morning, local residents have to get stuck in kilometers of traffic jams. On Moskovsky Prospekt, you can travel 1-1.5 hours, breaking about 3-4 kilometers.

When choosing housing, this factor should be taken into account. The road to work and back is a real test, and the "burgundy streets" in the navigator are very depressing, especially if you overslept.

Also in the North is a large private sector with very large houses. It is because of the chic of local housing that this place was called the "valley of the poor." Representatives of the elite of the past two decades and some high-ranking officials live here. It is here that the "northerners" try to go round the traffic jam on Shishkova Street, which irritates the locals very much. On the Korablinov street you can even find a barrier that tacitly reminds us that someday it can be closed once and for all.

Pleasant but dense building

Until recently, the northern district of the city of Voronezh was the outskirts. It is this fact that allowed urban planners to plan residential areas in an integrated manner. If Voronezh is an example of a point development, then the Northern region is continuous housing estates.

Fortunately, it is almost impossible to find single houses here. But there are real fortresses with kindergartens and playgrounds. The presence of sports simulators and recreational areas in the yards is also a normal occurrence.

There are, of course, critics of the development of the Northern region of Voronezh. The most popular residential complexes are concrete-brick jungle without a single tree or shrub, but against the background of Khrushchev, which has expanded in other quarters, you can come to terms with this.

Lack of parking spaces and arson of cars

This is a real feature of the Northern region of Voronezh. Despite the abundance of modern neighborhoods, their tenants in the evenings test their patience, trying to put their car at night.

Another problem is regular hooliganism in the form of arson cars in the yards of the Northern region of Voronezh. The property of residents has been suffering for several years, and a gang of hooligans has not been eliminated so far. It is worth noting that the fashion for pyromania is now a bit quiet, and since 2017, motorists can sleep peacefully.
