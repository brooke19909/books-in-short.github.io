Hotel St. Elias Resort 4 * (Protaras, Cyprus): photos and reviews of tourists

Tourist Cyprus is being actively built up. Along the coast there are modern hotels offering high quality service. One of them was the recently refurbished Louis St. Elias Resort 4 * (Cyprus, Protaras ). Most often, he is chosen for a romantic and family vacation. What can this complex offer its guests?

General Hotel Information

After lengthy repairs and complete renovation, the complex opened in June 2016. It is located on a vast territory, whose area is 40,000 square meters. m. The low-rise building is located on a hill with a beautiful view of Protaras . Hotel building Elias Resort 4 * is surrounded by a lush garden and its own olive grove. For its guests, the complex has prepared 137 spacious rooms that are suitable for both small families and friendly companies.





The administrator starts checking into the rooms no earlier than 14:00. At the end of the rest, tourists must vacate the apartment before noon. No pets allowed.
The city beach is 1.7 km away. For guests organized a free shuttle to the sea and back. You can find out the bus schedule at the reception. Walking to the beach takes about 15 minutes.

Larnaca International Airport is 65 km from the resort. Travelers can rent a car, order a taxi, or pay for a shuttle to get to the hotel. You can walk to the central part of the resort in just 5 minutes. A bus stop is 200 meters from the hotel building. The main orientation of the hotel is considered a family vacation. It is also suitable for honeymooners.

More about rooms and their equipment

Hotel Louis St. Elias Resort 4 * has 137 comfortable rooms, each of which has been recently renovated. The interior of the apartment is decorated in a classic style. Each room has its own spacious balcony with a dining chair and several chairs. It offers amazing views of the surroundings of the resort. Ground floor rooms have terraces. All rooms have tiled floors. Employees clean the rooms daily and change bed linen once a week.

Tourists choosing to relax in Louis St. Elias Resort 4 * ( Protaras ), can be accommodated in the following rooms:

  • Junior Suite It is a studio of 38 square meters. There is a bathroom, a kitchenette and a seating area. No more than three adults can live here.
  • Single Suite Two-room suite consisting of a separate bedroom and a living room in which there is a kitchenette . There is also a combined bathroom. Its area is 51 square meters. m
  • Double Suite. Unlike the previous room, it additionally houses another bedroom. Area - 78 square meters m. At the same time six adults can live here.




In addition to new furniture, the rooms have a flat-screen TV, which is connected to satellite TV, individual air conditioning. A mechanical safe and telephone are provided for a fee. The kitchen has a mini fridge , microwave, porcelain or glass dishes, an electric kettle, tea, coffee and sugar. Guests can make use of a hairdryer, complimentary toiletries and towels in the bathroom.

Local service and facilities

All guests of the St. Elias Resort 4 * ( Protaras ) will be able to appreciate its developed infrastructure. At any time, guests can use the free internet, which is connected in both residential and public areas. There is an outdoor parking lot nearby, and car rental can be arranged at the reception. Reception is open 24 hours a day. You can contact here for all your questions. Also here is a currency exchange.





A paid doctor's office, dry cleaning and laundry services are located on site. Tourists can visit the gift shop, as well as a convenience store that sells food, snacks , alcohol, clothing and shoes for the beach, newspapers and tobacco. Guests can relax in the common lobby. For the convenience of guests there are soft sofas and armchairs, coffee tables, air conditioning and a TV.

Entertainment

For its guests complex St. Elias Resort 4 * (Protaras) has prepared a rich entertainment program. An experienced animation team works for tourists 6 days a week. They hold sporting events, theme nights and competitions for children and adults. Discos, by contrast, are not held. 2 outdoor pools filled with fresh water are on site. There is no heating in them, but there are 3 water slides, as well as free sun loungers and umbrellas. Towels are issued after making a small deposit.





The complex also has its own spa, which is not included in the price. Here guests can visit the wellness treatments for body and face, a sauna and massage services. For outdoor enthusiasts, there are volleyball, basketball, soccer and tennis courts. True, rackets and balls are issued only for an additional fee. Tourists can rent bicycles. There is also a gym offering visitors modern equipment. In the evening, you can play billiards.

Water park and other activities for children

Many couples with children choose Hotel St. Elias Resort 4 * because of the water park , which is located here. All guests of the complex can visit it for free. True, a ride on a hill is only possible for children over six years old. Also, children whose height is below 120 cm are not allowed to visit the water park . There is also a playground and a shallow pool for the kids at the hotel. Children over four years old can visit the mini club , which is open daily from 10:00 to 17:00, including a lunch break.





The guests are offered favorable conditions for living with children. Guestrooms provide cribs, and restaurants have high chairs for feeding. Barriers for kids are located around the perimeter of the hotel. If necessary, you can call the nanny, who for a while will assume the responsibilities of caring for the child.

Catering

The resort complex St. Elias Resort 4 * (Cyprus) offers its guests all-inclusive meals. It includes not only the main meals, but local drinks, including some imported alcohol. Upon request, a special diet or vegetarian menu can be prepared free of charge.
For holidaymakers, the following catering establishments work at the hotel:

  • Restaurant Lemon Tree. All meals are served at the shared and children's buffet. It serves Mediterranean and European cuisine. In fine weather, the verandas and outdoor terrace are open.
  • Restaurant Mezo. It offers an unlimited number of snacks for owners of the all-inclusive system. Chefs prepare Greek dishes - salads, sauces and hot dishes. At the entrance to the restaurant there is also a shopping stall where visitors can buy a variety of cheeses, meat snacks, wines and olive oil.
  • Bar Jacaranda. It is located near the main restaurant and offers guests a wide selection of various soft drinks and cocktails, including alcoholic ones.
  • Pool Bar. Here guests can enjoy refreshing drinks, as well as tea and coffee. Lunch , light snacks, ice cream and other desserts are served to visitors .




Positive feedback from tourists

Hotel St. Elias Resort 4 * is a new complex, so there are not so many reviews about it. But they still help evaluate the local level of service. So, many tourists really liked the rest in this hotel. They describe the following benefits:

  • In the main restaurant, the chef cooks very tasty. Both cereals and mashed soups are served , which you can always feed a small child. Sometimes evenings of Mexican or Greek cuisine are arranged. The food is constantly replenished.
  • The hotel staff is very polite and friendly. Equally well applies to both Russian and English-speaking tourists.
  • Evening programs end early, so they do not interfere with sleeping guests with children and the elderly.
  • From the hotel you can see the fountain show, which begins in the center of Protaras .
  • At the pool you can always find free sun loungers, so you do not need to get up early to occupy them.
  • There are few tables in the restaurant, but the waiters clean the dirty dishes from them very quickly, so there is always free space, even at rush hour.




Negative reviews of tourists

Guests having a rest in hotel St. Elias Resort 4 *, reviews sometimes leave negative. Most often, in their comments, they describe insignificant minuses, which are still worth paying attention to both settling tourists and the hotel administration. According to the guests, the complex has the following disadvantages:

  • Departure from the hotel is not equipped with wheelchair access.
  • The beach is very dirty. Cigarette butts, plastic glasses and other rubbish are constantly lying in the sand.
  • Wi-Fi is slow, especially in the evening. He catches best of all at the reception and at the pool, but here his speed is low.
  • If the room faces the road, the view from the windows may be spoiled by garbage bins standing all over the place, as well as uncleaned building materials.
  • The rooms creak a lot of doors.

Is it worth it to relax here?

St. Elias Resort 4 * is perfect for a relaxing holiday. It can be recommended for tourists with children, elderly couples and even youth companies. Recently, the complex has been completely renovated, so guests can use the new furniture and plumbing in the rooms. A varied and very tasty food is also an important advantage of the hotel.
