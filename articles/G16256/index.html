Nurgush Range: description. How to get to the Nurgush ridge?

One of the majestic mountain ranges of the South Urals is the Nurgush Range, located in the center of the Zyuratkul junction. It is located at a distance of 200 kilometers from Chelyabinsk and 300 km from Yekaterinburg. The nearest settlement is the village of Sibirka (about 7.5 km).

There is still a less poetic version of the name, based on the Turkic-Mongolian language. In this case, the word "nuru" ("nura") means a mountain range.

Geographic location

The ridge is located between the Iremel mountain range and Lake Zyuratkul. It connects with the Urenga and Berry Mountains ranges in the east. The western neighbors are the Uvan and Moskal ranges. Nurgush itself belongs to the central belt of the Southern Urals (Taganai-Yamantaus belt).



From the northeast, the ridge stretches from the city of Lukash to the southwest to the mouth of the Berezyak River. The chain length is approximately 50 kilometers. The average height is 1200 meters, with a maximum height of 1406 meters. The highest point of the ridge is Mount Big Nurgush, which is also the highest point of the Chelyabinsk region.

The mountain range belongs to the territory of the national park, called "Zyuratkul".

Conventionally, the mountain range is divided into 3 parts or ranges: Small Nurgush, Sredny and Big Nurgush.

Description

The Bolshoi Nurgush ridge has the highest monolithic mountains stretching in a chain. Its tops are relatively flat, and the slopes are gentle. The surface consists of many quartzite outcrops that take on a variety of forms: geometric cones and trapezoids, ridges, pillars, walls, and ruined ruins. Slopes and peaks at heights of more than 1000 meters are covered with numerous curums. Stone piles of various sizes are scattered along the slopes (from the smallest to stones weighing about 5 tons).



The slopes of the Nurgush ridge are covered with dark coniferous trees. Further along the height, the taiga gradually turns into a light forest, while some areas of the wasteland are occupied by placers of stones. Here reigns forest-tundra with rare trees and lots of moss. Then the trees disappear. The uppermost part of the Big Nurgush is a mountain tundra. The flattest part of the entire Chelyabinsk region, called the plateau, is located exactly on the top of this ridge. Its surface, covered with moss, stones and lichen, covers an area equal to 9 square kilometers.

Middle Nurgush looks more like a solid wall. Its western slopes are very steep. The heights of its peaks are lower than that of the Great Ridge. Small Nurgush differs in more smoother forms of slopes than in the Middle Range. And its individual peaks reach a height of 1000 meters. The largest animals of the Ural forests are found here - elk and bear. For example, the Urenga and Nurgush ridges are a real bear angle, where animals such as marten, mink, red fox, as well as about 150 species of various birds feel great.





Routes

How to get to the Nurgush ridge? It is quite popular among travelers who want to see the indescribable beauty of the Urals region from a bird's eye view. Ascent is not difficult in itself, and the path goes through interesting picturesque places. The base of the mountain is adorned with fabulous beauty by Lake Zyuratkul, and then the birch forest covers the approaches to the ridge. In addition, tourists passing the slopes of the ridge, with their own eyes, see the taiga, swamps, amazing forms of stone sculptures. There is an attractive silence and absolute peace.

The most favorite route for tourists to the Nurgush ridge is climbing the Big Ridge. Its main attraction is the aforementioned plateau. It is from it that amazing views are opened: in the north is Mount Lukash, belted by Lake Zyuratkul; to the left - Moskal and Zyuratkul ranges; in the west and southwest - the impressive ranges of the Middle and Small Nurgush and the Big Bitch. In the bluish haze, the mountain ranges justify the fabulous name of this part of the Southern Ural Mountains - Sinegorye.



The road to Nurgush

How to get to the Nurgush Range yourself? Due to its easy accessibility, these places can be visited at any time of the year. Good roads have been preserved here since the time of logging. They lead to the other side of the Nurgush ridge.

The most convenient way to the Big Nurgush runs from the M-5 Ural highway. He goes through the village. Siberia and through the former Olympia Cordon (now the Shelter at the Three Peaks of the National Park). Here you can get quite comfortable while relaxing.

You can also use the Yuryuzan-Tyulyuk road to get to Maly Nurgush and Sredny. To do this, follow the sign (to Sibirka) to the old road that goes along the small river Bolshaya Kalagaz through the Olympia cordon (about 3 kilometers). Then in Sibirka you should turn right to the fountain (an object in the form of an icicle is more interesting in winter).

If you don‚Äôt turn, but head straight, you can get to the same Olimpiev cordon, where you can also take a halt, relax, however, the shelters are organized upon reservation.

A path passes through the swamps to Middle Nurgush, if you get to it from the Tyulyuk-Meseda highway.

The road through the Nurgush Range is quite accessible not only for walking, but also for off-road equipment and for bicycle transport. Of course, you can travel here on skis and on foot.
