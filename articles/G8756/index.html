Egypt, Badawia Resort 3 *: a brief description and photos of rooms





The cozy bungalows of the hotel have 100 rooms of different capacities. Most have views of the pool and courtyard, while the rest offer views of the surroundings. Almost every room has access to a secluded terrace or balcony. Here, sitting on comfortable garden furniture, you can breathe fresh air and enjoy wonderful views.

In the room you will find the necessary equipment and furniture. Everything is in good condition. Using the air conditioner, you can maintain a comfortable temperature in the room. You can watch TV and use the phone. There is a small refrigerator where drinks and some products are stored. A minibar is filled at the request of residents, but it is worth noting that this service is paid.

The spacious bathroom has a shower. There is a large mirror, washbasin, and a set of toiletries. Towels are changed every day. It is worth noting that room cleaning is usually carried out at the request of guests.

What kind of food can you count on?





Like most Egyptian hotels, Badawia Resort 3 * provides its guests with full-fledged all-inclusive meals. Three times a day you can count on a buffet in the hotel restaurant. Reviews say that the menu is quite diverse - salads, side dishes, meat dishes, scrambled eggs, pastries, fish, in a word, everything you need for a complete diet. And in local bars you can get free Egyptian-made drinks.

By the way, the city itself also has many cafes, restaurants and bars, where for a very small amount you can eat and enjoy the most famous oriental delicacies. Yes, and shops you can buy fruits and other products. In any case, food will not be a problem here.

Water activities: where is the beach located?





What beach vacation can the Badawia Resort offer? Sharm el Sheikh is famous all over the world for its luxurious beaches. And two of them are very close to the hotel. You can get there on foot, but the road takes 20-30 minutes, which is not always convenient. Therefore, a bus runs from the hotel during the day, delivering residents to the sea and back.

The beach here is very convenient, the entrance to the sea is gradual and thoroughly cleaned of algae. But, since there are corals nearby, tourists are still recommended to wear special shoes. You can use sun loungers, mattresses and parasols. Clean beach towels are provided free of charge at the hotel, but on bail.

Naturally, tourists can have fun and engage in various sports. For example, you can rent a boat or a catamaran, ride a banana, water skiing, go windsurfing, try out the charms of parasailing.

Near the shore there is a large coral reef. Traveler reviews suggest that the underwater life here is incredibly diverse and beautiful. You can snorkel, and those tourists who want to study the local flora and fauna more deeply are engaged in diving. By the way, here you can not only rent equipment, but also take a short training course and get a certificate.
