Royal Court Hotel 4 * (Prague, Czech Republic): hotel description, reviews

Prague is one of the most beautiful European capitals, which is why a massive concentration of tourists in this city, regardless of the season, is quite justified. True connoisseurs of architecture come to Prague who want to enjoy the huge cultural heritage, there are many businessmen who come exclusively for work. The Schengen visa to Prague does not bother tourists, the difficulties associated with its registration, they are ready to overcome all the trials in order to enjoy the beauty and charm of this fabulous city.

Of course, tourists want to stay in an excellent hotel, located close to infrastructure, at the same time affordable. I must admit that Prague offers such a variety of options that every vacationer and inquisitive tourist, if desired, can find an excellent option for a low cost, which will be located in the heart of the city, close to its cultural and historical attractions.

We offer you an overview of one option - Royal Court Hotel 4 * (Prague). Based on the reviews of real vacationers, we will try to evaluate it from all sides. Surely all potential guests are primarily interested in the provided service, infrastructure and type of rooms.





About hotel

Hotel Royal Court Hotel 4 * (Prague) is represented by 6 ground floors and 1 basement. Almost all rooms overlook the courtyard, due to which the rest here is quiet and peaceful. The hotel is ideal for tourists and vacationers who want to be in the center of the city's life, but at the same time removed from the hustle and bustle. The day here is so eventful that at least you want to spend the evening and night in peace. This is a city-style hotel, devoid of the surrounding area, which does not make it worse.

Location

So, the Royal Court Hotel and Spa 4 * participating in the review was highly appreciated for a reason. It is located in the center of the cultural capital of the Czech Republic, in the building of the former Royal Court. The main attraction of the city - Wenceslas Square - is just a 10-minute walk. It is from this place that the tourist route of many guests begins. The nearest metro station named after I.P. Pavlov is just a 5-minute walk away. In the immediate vicinity there are stops of ground public transport, so it is not difficult for hotel guests to get to the necessary places in the city in the shortest possible time.

Location - the main advantage of the hotel, which is surely appreciated by all vacationers.

Number of rooms

The hotel is represented by 45 rooms of economy and superior categories. The difference between the categories is only in the occupied area and the view that opens from the window. If it is so important for you to enjoy the beauties of the city in the morning, to see the red tiled roofs, pay attention to the superior rooms.





The rooms are spacious, bright and homely. Once here, you will not want to leave. The atmosphere is simple, but at the same time stylish and modern, there is not much furniture, but there is everything you need: comfortable beds, a table, nightstands, armchairs, a mirror. Each room has a separate room with all the necessary bath accessories: towels, bathrobes, slippers, shampoo, liquid soap in the dispensers. All vacationers will be pleased with the availability of tea accessories: an electric kettle, a set of dishes, sweets, tea and coffee. It is so nice to return to your room in the evening to have a cup of fragrant tea before bedtime.

Despite the proximity to the center, the rooms are quite quiet and calm, and all thanks to the excellent soundproofing.





Population

Check-in takes place within 5-7 minutes, the staff work very quickly. Even if guests arrive a little earlier than the due date, they will be accommodated if there is a free room.

Infrastructure

Hiking, rich excursion programs in which all the guests of Prague take part, exhaust, take a lot of strength and energy. The only thing that tourists dream about is to take a relaxing bath and lie in a warm bed. But Royal Court Hotel 4 * (Czech Republic) has prepared a surprise - all vacationers can visit the spa located on the hotel territory for an additional fee. Real professionals work here, including real Thai massage therapists, who within an hour will relieve you of all signs of fatigue and charge you with vital energy.





Perhaps the only drawback of the Royal Court Hotel 4 * (Prague) is the very slow elevator. If you are not limited in movement, you can always climb on foot.

Service

If you are looking for a place with excellent service, you will like the Royal Court Hotel 4 *.
