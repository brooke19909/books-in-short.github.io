Entertainment in Gelendzhik: how much to pay?

Gelendzhik is a resort city, so for a fun pastime there is a huge number of different trips, entertainment, places of recreation and all that stuff. Of course, when you come on vacation, you need to understand that you will have to spend a lot more money on entertainment in Gelendzhik, food and housing than in ordinary times. But nevertheless it is very interesting how much entertainment should be postponed when coming to Gelendzhik. Well, an important question: what kind of entertainment is there in Gelendzhik? Let's look at the sights of the city and their prices.

Excursions

In Gelendzhik just amazing nature. This area has long been considered one of the most beautiful areas of the region. Mysterious waterfalls, monuments of architecture and culture, picturesque nature, historical and sacred places.

One of the places to visit is the Pshad Dolmens. These structures are even older than the Egyptian pyramids, and we, the inhabitants of the country, know almost nothing about them.





Excursions can be automobile, walking, sea or even horse riding. Their duration is also different - from several hours to 2-3 days. The cost of such excursions depends on the duration, direction and other factors. On average, the price of a car excursion to one place will cost 800 rubles per adult vacationer.

Parks

Parks are represented by several types of establishments: these are water parks, and amusement parks, and plant parks, and a safari park, and just parks for walking.

If time and money allow, you can visit everything. If both are limited, choose the most interesting. Be sure to climb the cable car to Safari Park, which will delight you with a variety of animals, interesting plants, magnificent views from an altitude of 640 meters above sea level and the city.





Old Park located in Kabardinka is very interesting - every square centimeter of the area is carefully thought out, divided into zones of India, China, the Cossacks and several more thematic zones. The park is very green, interesting, there are competent guides.

Leisure

Those who need active entertainment in Gelendzhik are simply obliged to visit the water park. There are three of them in the city, the largest one is Golden Bay. Judging by the reviews, this park is significantly superior to the other two - “Behemoth” and “Dolphin”, but at the same time there are more queues for attractions and prices are higher.

An excellent choice for outdoor enthusiasts - horseback riding, jet ski rental, paragliding, diving, bike rental, visiting various shooting galleries, paintballs and more.





Fun for kids

The city did not leave children without attention either - entertainment in Gelendzhik for young guests is very diverse. Various carousels, swings, amusement parks at various levels, safaris, dolphinariums, boat trips with animators, swimming pools, carnivals and processions.

No child can resist the performance that dolphins present at the city dolphinarium daily. In the season, these performances take place twice a day, the rest of the time - once.

Entertainment in Gelendzhik for children can be found for every taste and budget. It doesn’t matter in which area of ​​the city you settled - you won’t be bored of children anywhere.





Entertainment for adults

Of course, most of the entertainment program of the city is aimed at adults, solvent vacationers. So, in the daytime and in the evening, the city has bars, restaurants, karaoke clubs, billiards, saunas, discos, nightclubs, cinemas, pools, motels, massages, fitness centers, ATVs, fun skating on the sea, flying over by sea with a parachute and much, much more, which will allow you to spend your vacation fun, quality and informative.

Entertainment in Gelendzhik can be found not only in the city itself, but also in the immediate suburbs. In addition to the already mentioned waterfalls, dolmens, mountain excursions, you can visit the vineyards, where they will organize a tasting of the best local wines.

If you are traveling as a whole company, you can contact special agencies that will help you create a rich rest program that will bring a lot of incredible emotions and will be remembered for a lifetime. Of course, the prices for entertainment in Gelendzhik are quite high, but still this amount of positive emotions is worth it. For example, one hour of horseback riding will cost 500 rubles, without delivery to the place. An hour on the boat - 500 rubles. A visit to the Safari Park for an adult costs 1000 rubles. You can get a taxi to Kabardinka and the Old Park for 350-400 rubles.

In addition to paid entertainment, in Gelendzhik there is a completely free, but the most amazing attraction, for the sake of visiting which, in fact, thousands of tourists flock here every year. This is it - our native Black Sea. It is mysterious, magnificent, amazing. Transparent, clean, fairly salty and very warm. To lie on the sand and splash in transparent waves, you do not need to pay money, but you are unlikely to ever forget about the time spent next to this amazing sea.
