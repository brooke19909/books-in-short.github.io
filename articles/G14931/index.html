"North Star" - Planetarium (Voronezh)

What is the Northern Star in Voronezh? This is a new planetarium that opened in the city in 2013. He instantly fell in love with both children and adults. Therefore, every day everybody, young and old, wants to go there to get into the world of astronomy. There are programs designed for children from 4 years.





All About The Solar System

Everyone who comes to the planetarium (Voronezh) will certainly learn a lot of interesting things about astronomy. It is here that you can take a virtual journey through the solar system, visit every planet. This task is perfectly performed by a program called "Astronomy: 10 steps into the sky." It is held every time the next excursion program for visitors begins.

Show program

The Origin of Life is a unique full-domed program that the planetarium (Voronezh) offers for children of senior and secondary school age. It is a pleasure for adults to watch. After all, each of them becomes a participant in time travel.

Although the time machine has not yet been invented, the North Star planetarium can easily be transported into the virtual space several millennia ago to find out how life was born on our planet.





Immersion story

Each program that the specialists of the Voronezh planetarium prepared is a story with a real dive. Children and adults with much greater interest learn scientific facts, remember interesting things about space, planets and stars. Even for preschoolers, they have their own magical program, which is called "Overflown."

Planetarium Features

Planetarium (Voronezh) is one of the attractions of the city. This place gives everyone extraordinary emotions, a lot of impressions. Not only residents come here, but also guests of the city. The main features of this entertaining planetarium are:

  • Unique digital technology for displaying full-dome programs. Such a compact structure can be placed anywhere. Therefore, field events are possible.
  • A huge space where you can turn around and organize events of various levels.
  • The acoustics in the room are amazing. It is she who helps to create an incredible atmosphere in which I want to learn astronomical secrets.
  • During the session, all visitors are in cozy and comfortable chairs.
