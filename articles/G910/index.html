How to find balance on Yota from various mobile devices

The mobile operator Yota, which also provides Internet services, has appeared on the domestic market relatively recently. However, we hear more and more about him. This means that the popularity of Yota is growing and more subscribers trust this company.





New customers are not yet well acquainted with the terms of use of the SIM card of this operator, and sometimes they have a question: how to find out the balance on Yota? This is what will be discussed in this article, from which you will learn all the ways to check the status of the account.

How to find out the balance on Yota- "sim card"?

The SIM card of this mobile operator and Internet provider can be used in devices such as smartphones, tablets, modems. On each of them, it may be necessary to check the balance. There are several ways to check the status of the account. You can choose the most optimal one after you familiarize yourself with all the options presented below:

  1. Via a mobile device by entering a USSD command. This method is suitable for smartphones and tablets, which have the ability to enter commands of this kind.
  2. Through the Internet. Active users of the global network, this option will probably seem the most attractive. Indeed, through the "Personal Account" you can view not only the status of the account, but also gain access to manage it.
  3. Via the mobile app. This method of checking the balance repeats the previous one with the only difference: viewing the data by number is also done via the Internet, but through a specialized application.

How to find out the balance on Yota in each of these ways, we will consider below. And also we will tell how to replenish the account number.





Balance check via mobile devices

Viewing account status through a USSD request is a quick way to get information. It does not require an internet connection. It is enough to simply enter a certain sequence of characters from the device in which the SIM card of the operator "Iota" is inserted. Mobile devices in this case are smartphones and tablet PCs. The question of how to find out the balance on Yota through the phone is unlikely to arise. But what about the tablet? After all, not every device has functionality for entering characters and sending requests. If there is no such possibility, then you will have to abandon the option of checking the balance through a USSD request. To view the status of the account, the command * 100 # + call button is used. Information will be displayed on the device screen after a few seconds.





Checking the balance on the SIM card used in the modem

If the SIM card is not used in a mobile device, then how do I know the balance on Yota? The modem of this operator does not imply special software for checking the balance and other information by number. In this case, you will have to use the Internet services to see the status of the account. To do this, go to the provider's website and go to your profile (by clicking on the link of the same name). Here you need to register (or log in, possibly through the account of one of the proposed social networks), and you can start viewing the data. By the way, in a similar way, you should check the balance and other information by number via a tablet PC, which does not have the functionality to enter a USSD request.





Balance check via mobile app

How to find out the balance on a Yota-SIM card (USSD command given earlier) through the application? Many subscribers underestimate this method of checking information by number. However, it is no less convenient than the two previous ones. You can download the program through any store available for your device (Marketplace - for devices running Windows Phone, AppStore - for gadgets of the "apple" brand or via PlayMarket - for smartphones and tablets on Android). You can download it for free. Please note that it may not be available for all devices (if you do not find it in the list of available programs, then most likely installation on your smartphone or tablet is impossible). After installation, the application should log in. Information about the status of the account will be displayed on the initial page. View other information through the appropriate sections of the program.

How to replenish the account number "Iota"?

If the question of how to find out the balance on Yota is no longer relevant and the account status information has been received, then most likely you will need to deposit funds. How can this be done for the number of the given operator and provider? There are several options for replenishing an account. In general, they are similar to the methods of paying bills of other mobile operators:

  • ATMs, online banking.
  • Instant payment terminals.
  • Electronic wallets (WebMoney, Yandex.Money, etc.).

You can pay for the services of Yota by any of the options described above. The terms and amount of payment should be specified in the "My Account" or through the mobile application, as well as through the operator‚Äôs customer support line.





Conclusion

In this article, we talked about how to find out the balance on Yota on each of the devices that can use a SIM card (mobile phone, modem, tablet computer), and also provided payment methods for operator services that are not much different from other payment options providers. Do not forget to regularly check the status of your account and make timely payments according to the tariff plan, so as not to be left without the opportunity to go online or make a call at the most crucial moment.
