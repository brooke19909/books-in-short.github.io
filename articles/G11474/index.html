Marsa Alam Hotels: European Resort in Egypt

Marsa Alam is a young resort village in southern Egypt. Now it can already compete with famous resorts such as Hurghada and Sharm el-Sheikh. New four- and five-star hotels have begun to work and began to receive tourists from all over the world.

New resort

Marsa Alam still can not boast of the presence of nightclubs, discos and huge hypermarkets. People come here to enjoy great diving, explore pristine corals, relax on deserted sandy beaches and live in freshly renovated rooms. The resort is called "European" due to the high level of service.

Promising resort

The Hilton Marsa Alam Nubian Resort is a luxury five-star hotel. The rooms are equipped with everything you need. The hotel has outdoor pools, restaurants and cafes. Tourists can visit souvenir shops and enjoy relaxing on the private beach.

Fantasy Resort Marsa Alam is a five-star hotel in Marsa Alam that offers deluxe rooms. The hotel's spa includes fourteen rooms with various treatments. There is a salt-water pool and a lounge area on site. Fantasy Diving Center has professional instructors who will help even beginners to explore the magnificent coral reefs and swim with dolphins.

On the territory there are several restaurants and bars serving confectionery, ice cream, cocktails. The Fantasy Hotel, like many 5-star hotels in Marsa Alam, operates on an all-inclusive basis.

Concord Maureen Beach Resort and Spa Marsa Alam is located in a beautiful garden, which is rare for Egyptian resorts. It offers guests several outdoor swimming pools and well-decorated rooms with all modern amenities. The main restaurant serves buffet meals, while the beach bar serves seafood. The hotel also offers horse riding.

Tourists note that in the new hotels of Marsa Alam, bays are especially good. This is its own special world with unusually beautiful sea animals.

Egypt luxury

Hotels in Marsa Alam (Egypt) are good all without exception. But having been in luxury resorts, it is already impossible to imagine another vacation.

"Jazz Solaya" - is located five kilometers from the airport. This hotel offers luxury comfort. Feel the amenities in a complex with its own beach, restaurants, shops, as well as enjoy diving and world-class cuisine - all this is only in the "Jazz Solaya".

The hotel has three stunning pools, two of which are heated during the winter, and a spa. Concert performances are held daily in the resort's amphitheater.

Guests visit the main all-inclusive restaurant, a la carte restaurants, pool and beach bars.

Utopia Beach Club - a luxury resort complex of the famous hotel chain needs no introduction. Clean blue pools, wellness treatments, round-the-clock service, the best food - all this Utopia Hotel offers its guests.
