Country recreation center "Friendship" in Krasnoyarsk

An important factor when choosing a vacation with children is the proximity to the water. Be it the sea, the river or a small body of water. Many of us live in large cities and over the weekend tend to get out into the countryside, away from the gassed air. The best solution for a family vacation would be a suburban complex.

In order not to spend money on a trip abroad and a long flight, you can choose a hotel or base in a Russian city, for example, Krasnoyarsk. Within the city and beyond, there are many amazing corners with comfortable hotels. The rating of popular complexes includes the recreation center "Friendship" - a former pioneer camp.





It is located not far from the benefits of civilization, in the picturesque village of Esaulovo. Near the Yenisei River flows, surrounded by greenery and tall pine trees. The territory of the base is guarded around the clock, which is a significant advantage. People come to this paradise with children, friends, colleagues. By the way, this is a small rural resort with its own beach, pool, spa center.

Accommodation

Against the background of picturesque natural landscapes, two-story houses and cozy cottages with open terraces were built. The area near the housing stock is landscaped, decorated with green lawns and driveways. Regardless of the choice of accommodation, all rooms are in excellent technical condition.





The rooms have Internet access is free. There is a dedicated telephone line for communication with the administration and the city (for a fee). In the personal use of the guests will be a bathroom with bath facilities. It includes cable TV, kitchenware, an electric kettle and a hairdryer. Each room is equipped with a minibar with free soft drinks.

Daily cleaning is provided. On request, food is delivered directly to the room (for a fee). Children under 12 years old are provided with free accommodation (no extra bed) at the Druzhba recreation center (Krasnoyarsk). Prices depend on the selected category and range from 3500-8000 rubles. per day.

Catering





Guests arriving at the complex overnight receive two meals a day (ordered). The cuisine is European and national. Dishes are always different, made with fresh ingredients. Large selection of food for children. Tourists are served by waiters. Also, the recreation center "Friendship" invites everyone to visit restaurants. There are two of them in the territory. There is a cafeteria with a rich assortment of alcoholic beverages.

Entertainment

The infrastructure of the complex is very diverse. For people of all ages there are classes. The recreation center "Friendship" offers friendly gatherings near the fire. To prevent bad weather from doing so, there are verandas with barbecue and barbecue. Those who are bored with intimate conversations will be able to play billiards, checkers, chess, table tennis.





Summer is a special time when the swimming season opens. Terraces with a hookah are built on the banks of the Yenisei, beach amenities are provided. A steam bath with a pool filled with ice water and a font is open for guests. A true exotic for our compatriots will be bathing in a hot outdoor jacuzzi. You can take such procedures on cold days.

Especially for women, there is a SPA center that provides anti-aging, cleansing and cosmetic procedures. Services include body wraps, including chocolate, full body peeling, anti-cellulite treatments. In particular honor various types of massage.

Leisure for young tourists

The youngest guests will enjoy exciting activities and activities. Separately, a shallow pool was built for children, located next to an adult so that parents could control their children. Water is purified by ultraviolet radiation - it destroys all dangerous bacteria.





The recreation center "Friendship" (Krasnoyarsk), the photo of which is presented in the material, allocated a large area for active classes. Young tourists will be delighted with the labyrinths, carousels, slides, horizontal bars. Bicycles, rollers, scooters are available for rent, on which you can drive around a spacious territory. In winter, skates, cheesecakes, sledges, skis are offered.

A mini-school is open for the manifestation of creative potential. Classes are conducted by professional educators. Your child will learn to draw, sculpt, cut. Kids are shown cartoons. The club has a lot of toys for children of different ages, board educational games.

Event Services

The recreation center "Friendship" is widely known among business travelers, corporate clients. Celebrations, anniversaries, weddings, parties are constantly held here. An individual program and menu is selected for each client.





Tables are served in the spacious restaurant or on the outdoor terrace - at the request of the guests. Business people arriving on business issues will be able to conduct presentations, negotiations and meetings in a conference room for 80 people in comfortable conditions.

What exclamations do guests receive from the Druzhba recreation center (Krasnoyarsk)?

Reviews from most tourists in a positive way. Absolutely everyone was pleased with the territorial location and the presence of the ennobled shore. Despite the presence of pine forest, ticks and mosquitoes are not here, as employees regularly perform pest control.

Cleanliness and comfort reign everywhere. Almost all regional recreation centers create excellent conditions for a pleasant pastime. “Friendship” (Krasnoyarsk) places a great emphasis on children's leisure, which cannot but rejoice. The happy faces of the guys are proof of this.
