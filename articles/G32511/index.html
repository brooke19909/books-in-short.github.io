Kamennomostsky village, "Terem by the river": visitor reviews

Summer, exhausting heat, gassed air - what does every person want at this time of year? Of course, there is a desire to go somewhere in a cool place, enjoy the green vegetation and freshness. Unfortunately, at present in Russia there are not many places where you can enjoy the beauty of nature. For example, the village of Kamennomostsky will give such feelings. "Terem by the river", located on its territory, will allow a person to fully relax, enjoy the vacation as much as possible and preserve a lot of pleasant memories.





Short description of the recreation center

Each person should visit the mountains of Adygea at least once in his life. Only here you can enjoy the healing air and the beauty of the surrounding nature. In some places, even traces of Neanderthals who lived in these parts about 130 thousand years ago have been preserved. The village of Kamennomostsky is distinguished by such beauties. The River Terem is located right in the center of the most popular tourist route.





Visitors can enjoy the murmur of water coming from the Belaya River. A lot of positive emotions will bring a clean sandy beach, the smell of fresh vegetation and the hot sun. In the morning, the chirping of local birds is clearly audible, which also has a good rest.

Guest house "Terem by the river" (Kamennomostsky) will not only bring a lot of positive emotions, but also provide a therapeutic effect. There is a certain relaxation area located near picturesque waterfalls. Many locals come here to do exercises, do yoga or just think about something beautiful. A special microclimate is created by the underworld, Scythian barrows and canyons.

Hotel Services

Hotel "Terem by the river" (Kamennomostsky village) has an incredibly interesting appearance, it is presented in the form of a chopped house with an old design. The staff here welcomes guests at home, there is a free, relaxed atmosphere. Amenities include a spacious sauna and satellite TV. Three meals a day are served at home.





For those who do not have their own car, there is an opportunity to reach their destination by public transport, near the recreation center there is a railway station and several bus stops. Surprisingly, there has never been a problem with cellular communications.

At the recreation center there is the opportunity to engage in an active sport, for example, you can rent sleds, skis or skates.

Types of rooms

Offers an excellent holiday village Kamennomostsky. The Terem by the River provides an opportunity to reserve one of several room options. The simplest (economy) is a standard room designed for two people. It has a mini fridge, modern air conditioning, wireless internet, a comfortable bed, several bedside tables and a TV. Its approximate cost is two thousand rubles a day.





An improved option is a double room with a balcony (standard). From it you can enjoy a beautiful view of the local nature. He will have to pay about three thousand rubles a day.

The most expensive rooms are triple and quadruple. They are comfortable to relax with a large family. There are various options (from economy to luxury). The cost of such a number can reach five thousand rubles a day.

Additional services





There are several additional services that are available to each visitor:

  • First of all, it is a spacious bathhouse with a real Russian steam room. There is an excellent locker room, shower, pool, billiards and a font. For beginners, a free master class is held once a week.
  • Every visitor here likes to cook kebabs in the fresh air. The administration of the establishment provides barbecue facilities; you can also rent a covered or open gazebo.
  • Another advantage is the free parking for all guests.

If the visitor is not satisfied with standard dishes, then he can order any menu for himself from the staff.

Positive reviews





It has already been more than ten years since the creation of the Terem by the River (Kamennomostsky). Reviews during this time were mostly met only positive. Visitors praise the following:

  • The beautiful view from the room is not just pleasant, it is mesmerizing. I would like, without stopping, to look at the local beauty. And such a positive quality has absolutely any room - both standard and junior suites and suites.
  • An atmosphere of coziness, silence and tranquility reigns around, one gets the feeling as if for some time you are moving to a completely different world.
  • We should also talk about the freshness of the air. In the first hours of your stay at the recreation center, it seems so sweet that you want to enjoy it again and again.
  • There is something for everyone to do: for both children and adults, the staff provides a huge amount of sports equipment, entertainment and other useful services.
  • They praise a spacious bathhouse, which can accommodate up to ten people at a time.
  • A large selection of food, including meat, diet and baby.
  • Reasonable prices.

Separately, guests praise the polite staff who seeks to find an approach to each client.
