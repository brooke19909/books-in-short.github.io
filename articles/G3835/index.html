Nikon Coolpix S2800: reviews and overview of features

The Nikon Coolpix S2800 camera, reviews of which were fundamental when writing this review, is considered quite compact. In general, the functionality of the device is enough to get excellent quality images as a result. Due to additional options, this camera will become an indispensable assistant. The device was distinguished by excellent speed and fairly good matrix quality. Often, it is used as a camera on holidays.



Design

The design of the Nikon Coolpix S2800 Red (excellent reviews), like the other color version, is made fairly standard - it is no different from other models of the same company. All outlines are smooth, and the controls have good ergonomics. This device is thin and at the same time pleasant during operation. The thickness of the device is not more than 20 mm. The device can be carried in a pocket and a small handbag. Due to several color options you can find the most suitable for yourself.

Interfaces and Management

On the front side you can find a lens with a flash. The company logo and information, which reveals all the advantages of the device, are also located right there. Holes can be seen just below the lens. They are a microphone. Some modifications designed for girls have a beautiful pattern on the same panel.

The upper edge received a standard appearance. The reviews for the Nikon Coolpix S2800 camera for such a solution are quite excellent. All customers note excellent ergonomics. The power button is conveniently located - it is located in the middle. It is made of metal material. The oval shape itself. A little to the right of it you can find the shutter button. It is distinguished by more overall dimensions. The key is not only responsible for taking photos, but also other functions. The name of this unit is also placed here on the upper end.

Below you can see a special mount, which is necessary to mount the device on a tripod, as well as video output. Under the plug of plastic hides a compartment for the battery and memory card. A belt clip is available on the right side of the Nikon Coolpix S2800. In reviews, consumers praise the manufacturer for such a decision. On the back side, you can directly find all the functionality that a person will have to work with. There is an event indicator on the upper corner, a little further - a special button that is responsible for shooting videos. Below there was a place for a four-position button. In its middle is the standard OK key. In general, it can be called the main one, since it is responsible for managing the device. Below is a key for working with the menu and deleting material. The keys, although small, are easy to use. They all have designations, so it will be difficult to get confused with which for what purpose.



Lens and matrix

The lens has an aperture of F3.2 - F6.5. All customers are satisfied with this choice, as for a digital device such an indicator is impressive. You can shoot at a distance of 26 to 130 mm. There is an optical zoom designed for a five-fold increase.

There are good reviews of the photos taken by the Nikon Coolpix S2800 Black. Their resolution is 5152 √ó 3864 pixels. The matrix perfectly matches its price segment. In general, the manufacturer did not try to invent a bicycle, so everything turned out to be reserved, but at the best level.

The manufacturer has built a large number of modes and effects. The interface is simple, so there is no difficulty with it. The resulting videos are in AVI format. Their speed is 30 frames per minute. There is a built-in microphone.



Screen

Unfortunately, the device does not have its own viewfinder. It is for such cases when it is needed, the display is used. Its diagonal is 2.7 inches. LCD screen It has excellent contrast and hue depth. In the sun it shows itself perfectly, the image is not distorted, glare is not visible.
