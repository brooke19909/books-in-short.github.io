Cameras and their features

An ambiguous situation with this model is formed around the main camera. On the one hand, she has everything in order to receive high-quality pictures and videos. This is a 13 megapixel matrix, and autofocus, and LED backlight. But if the photos are of acceptable quality, then there are certain problems with the video. Most likely, they are associated with the lack of an automatic image stabilization system. This leads to the fact that the frame in the video can be blurry. There is also a front camera. Its main task is to make video calls. She copes with this task without any problems. Moreover, it is based on a 2 megapixel matrix. This is quite enough for high-quality images during video calls.

Memory subsystem

An interesting situation with this model is with the memory subsystem. If everything is clear and unequivocal with RAM - 2 GB, then with the built-in drive options are possible. Initially, 16 GB was integrated in this model. But a little later, a more advanced modification appeared - Lenovo K900 32GB. A review of its technical characteristics indicates that the difference between them is only in the volume of the flash drive. Moreover, it is very significant - 2 times. Accordingly, the cost of the latter is much higher. Therefore, do not forget to check with the seller the amount of installed memory. The situation with memory cards is much worse. There is no slot in this smartphone for their installation. If for some reason 16 GB or 32 GB of internal memory is not enough for you, then you can increase its size only with the help of the OTZ cable and a regular USB flash drive.

The weak link in this premium smartphone is the battery. Its capacity is ‚Äúonly‚Äù 2500 milliamps / hours and this value is most likely due to the thickness of the case at 6.9 mm. Chinese engineers had to choose between the thickness of the smartphone and its autonomy. As a result, the choice was made in favor of the first, and the second parameter faded into the background.
