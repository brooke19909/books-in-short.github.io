Crochet panamas for girls: diagram, description, useful tips

In the summer wardrobe of the child must be present panama. Since the kids are active people, it is not surprising that their clothes and hats quickly get dirty. Therefore, you will not be able to manage with only one panama hat for the entire warm period of the year. But do not rush to buy many of these items of clothing at once. We offer all do-it-yourself mummies with daughters to do this summer accessory with their own hands. Crocheting a panama hat for girls is the topic of our article. It presents a master class in which the entire process of creating a headdress for a little princess is described in detail. The model proposed in the article is simple to implement, so even a novice in this type of needlework can knit it.



Tools and materials

Crocheting a panama hat for girls begins with preparing the necessary accessories for work. We will need a 50-gram package of pink cotton threads and a little white yarn of the same composition, hook number 1.3. To decorate the headdress, prepare small beads, a bow from a satin ribbon.

Master class "Crocheted panama hat for girls." We carry the bottom of the headdress



With a pink thread, dial a chain of 5 stitches. Fasten them in a ring with a connecting column. Next, we knit rows. Their numbering in the further description is indicated by numbers.

  1. Knit all loops with a ring. 3 lifting air loops (each row will begin with them), 11 double crochet columns. Next, connect the first and last loops. We proceed to knitting the second row.
  2. Lifting loops, 1 double crochet in the same place. Next, in each subsequent loop, perform 2 of the same column. As a result, at the end of the row you will get 24 loops, that is, their number will double. You will notice how the circle grows.
  3. 3 air loops. That you completed the climb. In the adjacent loop on the right, tie 2 double crochet stitches. This is necessary so that the circle continues to expand. Perform the same column in the next 3 loops. And so alternate to the end of the row.

The following rows fit in with the same principle with uniform increments. The photo shows a panama scheme for a girl, which is performed by this pattern, with single crochet columns. It clearly shows where to add the loop.



You can continue crocheting a panama hat for girls to the end, but then the product will look simple and boring. We offer you to issue several white stripes on it. This can be done in the fifth and in every fourth row.

Recent additions are made in the 10th row. The bottom of the summer hat is ready for the girl.

Knitting panama: the main part of the product



Follow the next rows with single crochet. Since the increase is no longer done, the canvas will not expand. Having knitted 3-4 rows, you will notice how the product takes the shape of a hat. Perform work in this way until the headgear reaches the desired depth. In order not to miscalculate with the size, it is desirable to try on the product all the time on the daughter‚Äôs head. At the end of the last row, cut the thread, fasten, and hide its tip from the inside of the accessory.

Crochet panamas for girls: decorating

Sew on the last row of columns of beads. Attach a bow to the side of the product. You can decorate the headpiece with other elements: artificial flower, embroidery, knitted applique. As a result, your little daughter-daughter will receive a beautiful and original panama. Enjoy your creative moments!
