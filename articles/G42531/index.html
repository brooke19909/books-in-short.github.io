Hotel "Matryoshka" (Moscow): photos and reviews of tourists

The legendary Russian nesting doll is for foreigners one of the symbols of our beautiful country. It symbolizes Russian comfort and hospitality, the beauty and harmony of Russia. Therefore, it is not surprising that a number of the most popular hotels in different cities of the country have the same name with this toy. This is not only a kind of marketing move, but also the ability to sustain a room in a single style. Such is the Matryoshka Hotel (Moscow). Despite the fairly simple interior, rooms are never empty almost never. Partly this is facilitated by the extremely convenient location of the complex. But he is far from the only one. There is another hotel "Matryoshka": Samara picked up a popular trend. This is not just a hotel, but a real wellness center, which is known and loved not only by the townspeople, but also by guests from neighboring countries. Today we will devote an article to a brief review of these two "nesting dolls", which are modern hotel complexes.



Where is the Matryoshka Hotel (Moscow) located?

You can easily find this hotel. If you have a GPS-navigator or you use the taxi service, the road from the center will not take much time. It is for the location that this hotel is primarily valued by tourists. You can walk to Red Square in just 5 minutes. Hotel Matryoshka (Moscow) is located in the historical center. This building is an architectural value; it was built in 1891 by the merchant Khludov. In ancient times, there were famous Chinese baths. The building itself was completely renovated in 2012 and therefore meets all modern requirements.

Almost within walking distance is the central metro line. Stations "Lubyanka" and "Kuznetsk bridge" will provide the opportunity to easily get to any attractions and travel around the city. The Matryoshka Hotel (Moscow) is a favorite place to stop tourists taking part in cultural and educational excursions along the Golden Ring of Russia.



Number of rooms

This is not a luxury hotel, but you can have a great time here. You will feel how hospitable Moscow can be. Hotel Matryoshka offers its guests more than one hundred rooms for every taste. It is single and family, superior and deluxe. There are separate apartments for non-smokers. Each room is able to create the most comfortable conditions for its guests. The goal of the staff is to surround you with care and attention, so that the days spent here are remembered as the most amazing adventure that Moscow has presented to you. Hotel Matryoshka is waiting for its guests around the clock.



Room Interior

Tourists note that the rooms are small, but very comfortable. Each room has comfortable beds, comfortable furniture and necessary appliances, satellite TV. Hotel "Matryoshka" reviews from grateful tourists constantly collects. All of them especially emphasize cleanliness and comfort, as well as the attentiveness and courtesy of the staff. Each room is equipped with a bathroom and a bathroom with shower, hairdryer and necessary accessories. Depending on the choice of the tourist, administrators can offer an air-conditioned room with a telephone or a minibar, cots and extra beds.

Nutrition

Tasty and healthy breakfasts are offered by the Matryoshka Hotel (Moscow). Reviews emphasize the high quality of the prepared dishes, the neat table setting and the friendly service. The breakfast menu involves several types of snacks (cheese, sausage slices), various cereals, sandwiches, yoghurts, granola, fruits, as well as many different drinks. A nutritious, healthy breakfast provides energy and vigor. During the day, you can easily have a bite to eat in any institution in the capital that you consider appropriate.



Hotel Services

The reception is open 24 hours a day at the reception. He is always ready to assist in any matters, offer a full range of services, help navigate the city, make copies of documents, send a message by fax or mail, take baggage and put valuables in a safe. It is here that one gets the impression of the Matryoshka Hotel. Moscow is able to charm and surround with care and comfort, especially if you choose the right hotel.

Laundry service is available here. She will make sure that the clothes of the guests are always perfectly washed and ironed. For motorists there is a guarded parking lot where you can leave your car. There is free Wi-Fi access throughout the hotel.

Judging by the reviews, this is a great economy class hotel with a great, very convenient location. The building of the old building does not provide sound insulation very well, but you can put up with this drawback. The food is not too varied, but tasty and nutritious.

Cost of rest

All prices are per room per day, breakfast is included. A single room will cost 1700 rubles, a double - 2300. A double room with windows costs from 2600 to 3000 per day, and a superior - 3500. A deluxe room can be rented for 5000 per day. As you can see, prices for the center of the capital are quite affordable.

Hotel "Matryoshka" (Samara)

This is a modern hotel complex, which is located in Samara and is part of the Matryoshka Plaza medical and health center for human ecology. In addition to the hotel, the center includes a number of health and entertainment complexes. Among them are a medical center, a health-rehabilitation complex, a fitness center, a restaurant, entertainment and spa center. Thus, you can satisfy all wishes for a vacation by checking into this hotel.



How to get there

The hotel is located in one of the most beautiful areas, only 9 km from the city. The green park zone pre-sets for a good rest. The airport is 35 km from the hotel, the Sovetskaya metro station is 5 km and the Sportivnaya station is 6 km. The nearest bus stop is about 3 km from the hotel. The friendly hotel "Matryoshka Plaza" offers tourists cozy rooms near stunning sights and museums. The hotel began operating in 2011. It perfectly combines innovative style and impeccable comfort. A nightclub, spa and indoor pool is a partial list of all the pleasures offered to tourists.



Number of rooms

Matryoshka Plaza is a European level of comfort, updated interior and equipment. Everything has been done here to ensure that guests receive maximum comfort from relaxation. Attentive staff provides the highest level of service. Guests are offered 167 rooms, among which there are economy class, standard and comfort apartments. Stylish and comfortable rooms will appeal to guests with any request. They have comfortable beds, a bathroom, a shower with hairdryers and all the necessary toiletries. The rooms have air conditioning and a minibar, telephone and wardrobe. Judging by the reviews of tourists, the rooms are maintained in perfect cleanliness. The hotel offers you additional service, in each room guests are waiting for soft bathrobes and slippers. Room service is available.



Guest meals

Judging by the reviews, the food here is simply amazing. The self-catering kitchen is presented by two restaurants, as well as an excellent pastry shop. Each restaurant is distinguished by its unsurpassed style: New York, Provence, Milk and Chocolate. Each of them offers its own, unique menu, individually compiled and selected for guests. The recommendations of nutritionists are taken into account. Even after the course of treatment, it is possible to arrange the delivery of diet foods to your home so that the diet is maintained as comfortably as possible.

The price includes a full breakfast, the rest of the meals each tourist can order individually, depending on their preferences and doctor's recommendations.



Additional services

The fitness center develops wellness procedures for each vacationer with the participation of medical specialists. This pool and water aerobics, group and individual training, exotic massages and body wraps. In addition, guests are offered a unique European program, rituals in a spa capsule, a gallery of saunas, a sea-water jacuzzi, a style workshop, a cosmetology laboratory, a nail service and a solarium bar.

The Matryoshka Plaza Hotel (Samara) includes a network of bars and restaurants with exclusive cuisine and exotic drinks. In addition, guests are invited to the cinema, billiards and bright, entertaining programs that will not leave anyone indifferent.

Separately, it must be said about children's entertainment. You can come here as a family, your kids will be busy in the children's playroom. While the parents are busy with their procedures, the children will attend developmental, creative and game classes.



Wellness programs

There are more than 20 different programs that are aimed at treating the nervous system and the gastrointestinal tract, musculoskeletal system and genitourinary system, including female and male infertility. In addition, diseases of the heart and blood vessels, ENT organs, and endocrine pathologies are successfully treated here. Medical personnel undergo regular training and certification, which allows us to provide the highest class services. Modern equipment allows you to diagnose quickly and accurately.

Methods of treatment and recovery

The basis of any treatment is a special dietetic food. Here you can get a full range of balneological services, such as showers, baths and irrigation. Very effective is heat therapy. Here is a whole range of similar procedures for which natural resources are supplied from various natural deposits. In addition, doctors will choose physical physiotherapy, spinal traction and massages, physiotherapy exercises, hydrocolonotherapy, and cave and mountain air treatment.

Cost and reviews

The cost of a single room here starts from 2500 rubles. The price includes breakfast. Superior rooms cost 3500 per day, and suites - from 4500. All diagnostic and treatment measures are paid separately. According to reviews, this is an amazing complex that provides an almost endless selection of entertainment and healing procedures.
