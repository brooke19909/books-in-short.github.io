Tour montparnasse

So what to visit first of all for tourists who find themselves in the former Mecca of the creative intelligentsia? The main attraction of the area is the 57-story Montparnasse skyscraper in Paris, the gigantic dimensions of which against the background of the historical center have been constantly criticized. However, the tower is considered the most popular tourist attraction of the capital of France.



Previously, there was a small train station at this place, and as the city grew, it ceased to cope with the increased traffic volumes. On the territory of the complex of buildings dismantled by decision of the authorities in 1972, a monolithic structure of metal and glass grew, from the observation deck of which there is an amazing view of the picturesque city. Montparnasse Tower in Paris, awkwardly towering over the historic district, is 210 meters. Gare montparnasse

Jardin atlantique

On the roof of the Gare Montparnasse is an amazing living area, combining the best achievements of landscape art and engineering technology. Guests should not be afraid that they will miss the train: all announcements in the garden are perfectly audible.
