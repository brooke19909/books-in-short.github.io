Lake Spruce. Rest on Spruce Lake

Despite the fact that the original Bashkir territories are around, Lake Elovoe (Chelyabinsk Region) bears the Russian name. Moreover, this is the only body of water in the vicinity, since others have Finno-Ugric and Turkic names. The lake is located between the cities of Miass and Chebarkul. Spruce is a freshwater pond and is a hydrological monument. The reservoir is located among the Ural Mountains at an altitude of 322 meters above sea level. The lake is round in shape, its area is 3.2 km 2 . In length - more than 2 kilometers, in width - less than 2. The length of the coastline is about 10 kilometers. The greatest depth is 13 meters, with an average of 8. The parameter of water transparency is 4 meters. The volume of liquid is more than 26 million square meters.





Water regime of Lake Elovoe

Lake Elovoe has mixed water supply: underground sources, springs, precipitation and snowmelt. The Gudkovka River flows into the reservoir, and the Elovka flows out. Spruce enters the chain of mountain lakes: Big Miassovo, Terenkul, Small Miassovo, Chebarkul, Small Kisegach, Big Elanchik and Big Kisegach. The banks of the reservoir are mostly steep. Despite its small size, the lake has 3 islands: one large - 15 hectares (this is the largest island in the Chelyabinsk region), and two small ones - 1 ha each. Depending on the weather conditions of the year, ice freezing occurs from the end of October to the end of November, and ice flow - from mid-April to mid-May. The Elovka River flowing from a reservoir connects it with Lake Chebarkul. Spruce - a cozy, quiet and calm lake. However, in inclement weather, waves can rise up to 1 meter. True, this happens infrequently. The lake is perfectly sheltered from winds of all directions and warms up well. This fact determines the long duration of the swimming season.





Fishing on Lake Elovoe

Spruce Lake is attractive not only for local fishermen. Lovers from all over Russia come here to go fishing. In the reservoir live: pike, ripus, crucian carp, chebak, ide, tench, bream, ruff, perch and burbot. Basic maintenance of fish resources is natural spawning. Commercial fishing is not carried out here. In shallow water you can catch only a small chebak and perch. A larger fish is caught from boats throughout the lake. As a rule, they use mugs, ventricles (live bait gear) and spinning. From winter fishing, craftsmen bring a small ruff, perch and chebak. In recent years, the number of fish has increased significantly, which indicates an increasing concentration of nutrients that are its feed.





Recreation centers and sanatoriums on Spruce Lake

Lake Elovoe is one of the most attractive places in the South Urals. It has a stable ecosystem and has been maintaining its structure for more than a century. Even transparency since 1969 (the moment the status of the monument was adopted) has not changed, although in neighboring water bodies it has decreased by an order of magnitude. Rest on Spruce Lake involves two forms: the "savage" and a ticket. The first involves the presence of a tent and other tourist equipment. Because of its small size, Lake Elovoe has a few institutions: the Rodnichok base, the Ural Dawns base, the Elovoe cottage, the Elovoe sanatoriums, the Pine Hill and the UralVO. All recreation areas have their own beaches and good entry into the water, sports and children's playgrounds, a set of boats for skiing and fishing, forest walks and excursions are supposed. Any recreation center (Lake Elovoe) has a full range of entertainment for children: from game rooms to competent animators. Everyone will spend leisure as he wants.





Hydrological monument

Lake Elovoe has a tectonic origin, as evidenced by its rounded shapes. Now a pine-birch forest is growing along the banks . Before the tectonic shift, as a result of which a pond was formed, there were relict forests. They persisted until the middle of the XIX century, judging by the maps and written sources of that time. At present, the fir-trees in whose honor the lake is named cannot be found here. Although at the beginning of the 20th century, according to the notes of the local historian Vladimir Sementovsky, both the spruce and the fir-tree met here. The water in the lake is soft, mineralization depending on the season varies from 161 to 340 mg / liter. It belongs to the hydrocarbonate class, in the transition from soda to sulphate-sodium type.





The mystery of Lake Elovoe

All ponds located in the neighborhood bloom almost all summer, but Lake Elovoe remains clean. Moreover, the concentration of phosphorus and nitrogen compounds is quite high - up to 10 times the threshold of “flowering”. And the reservoir even in the midst of the growing season of blue-green and green algae reaches only the initial stage of the process. Scientists suggest the presence of a powerful flowering inhibitor, which may be manganese, which is found in large quantities in water. It is he who can act as a suppressor of the rapid development of phytoplankton.

Ecology

Lake Elovoye, in spite of everything, still retains its natural parameters. However, the level of atrophy is still growing year by year, as the load on the reservoir increases, especially in the summer (up to 70 thousand tourists come here annually). Driving on motorized swimming means significantly worsens the ecological situation on the lake, since oil products are discharged. Their impact affects the state of fish and vegetation, both aquatic and terrestrial. The number of primitive organisms that filter water has also fallen. There is a risk of lake pollution with algotoxins, which adversely affect living things. All circumstances together can lead to poor water quality.
