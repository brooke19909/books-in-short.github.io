How to make earrings: do it yourself?

The world of jewelry is full of bright colors, brilliant stones and shapes, embodied in the ideal attributes of female attractiveness. It is difficult to imagine a modern girl or woman without the presence of beautiful accessories that emphasize the impeccability of her style. Therefore, in the wardrobe of every representative of the fair sex, there should be many different jewelry suitable for daily dresses. Earrings are especially popular among jewelry, because if you choose them correctly, then other types of jewelry will not be needed. Unfortunately, not every fashionista can afford to buy a large number of high-quality earrings, but they can be easily crafted independently using fairly simple elements. And how to make earrings beautiful, original and flawless is described in the article.

Necessary tools

To start manufacturing various earrings, you must first prepare all the necessary tools and materials. The choice of material for jewelry depends on what kind of crafts you need to do. As a rule, beautiful beads of various sizes, colored feathers, stones, both artificial and natural, and many other decorative elements are selected. To connect the individual parts of the earrings, earrings, rings, warp, pins with and without loops are purchased.

In the creative work associated with the manufacture of earrings, you will have to bend, unbend and cut a lot of wire, as well as metal pins, so you can not do without some tools. These include wire cutters, scissors and pliers. It may be necessary in some cases to increase this list by adding some more tools.

When all the necessary tools and materials are assembled, you need to read the instructions on how to make earrings. As a rule, beginners in this business or in work on crafts with a high level of complexity require a manufacturing manual. People who have certain skills in needlework usually do not need instructions.

Lightness and weightlessness

One of the most popular types of earrings are crafts using beautiful feathers. Accessories made using this type of material give the image lightness and weightlessness. Such jewelry can be made in different variations and fits different outfits. For example, earrings with feathers of delicate colors, golden, silver and glass beads are perfect for an evening dress. A bright and colorful and extraordinary crafts will look great on young women who are in the whirlwind of a youth party. Also, of course, they can be in addition to a light summer sundress, worn for a walk with your loved one. Consider how to make feather earrings that fit many looks and styles:





  1. Prepare two feathers of the same size, ear hooks and clips with holes.
  2. To put on a clip edge of each feather on a clip, having pressed it densely with pliers.
  3. Feathers with clips to attach to svensami.

As can be seen from the description of the progress, the manufacture of such earrings does not require a lot of time. If desired, they can be supplemented with chains of different lengths with beads at the ends. Painting of feathers in different colors or gluing rhinestones and sparkles to them is also practiced.

Flawless style

Many girls are just crazy about earrings in the shape of tassels. Making them will be a little harder than the previous version, but guaranteed to get great jewelry. We'll figure out how to make brush earrings in stages. To do this, you will need the following materials: threads of any color from 100% viscose (used for embroidery), colorless nail polish, ordinary thread for sewing (in tone with embroidery threads), two identical small boxes (for winding threads), sheet A4, clamps for brushes, 2 rings, earrings (2 pcs.), Scissors and a long wooden stick.





Working process:

  1. On the boxes in the center, wrap viscose threads in half of the coil for each.
  2. Take the thread for sewing and bandage it in the middle of the box several times with bundles of wound viscose. This is best done using a needle with a thread that you need to pry between the box and the wound thread.
  3. Next, turn the box over to the other side and pry a ruler between it and the threads. Then it is necessary to cut the wound threads strictly in the middle. Do the same with another instance.
  4. In the process of the work, brushes with a disheveled appearance will turn out. To give them smoothness and silkiness, they will need to hold them over boiling water. In order not to burn yourself, you need to attach the blanks to a wooden stick and keep it that way for several minutes.
  5. The next step will be shaping the product. To do this, step back from the fold of the brush by 1-1.5 cm and rewind the thread around it several times, fixing it with knots. The same work needs to be done with the second craft. The ends of the nodules must be varnished and, after drying, flatten them with your fingers.
  6. Next, you need to start aligning the edges of the brushes. To do this, use a sheet of A4 paper. It is necessary to wrap a brush in it so that its edges peep out. It is best to roll up the paper while doing this. Then you should trim the protruding threads of the brushes and trim the edges.
  7. It remains to do the smallest thing - put on decorative clips for brushes in the field of dressing them with threads. And also attach rings to them for fastening to the sling.

After reviewing the step-by-step guide on how to make brush earrings correctly, you can make additions to them in the future. For example, decorate with beads on chains or beads on threads.

Bead splendor

No less popular than the previous sample, earrings in the form of brushes, which are made of small beads. They can also differ in form and in the manufacturing process. First you need to learn the easiest way to weave such crafts. How to make bead brush earrings in quick and easy execution is described below. For the work you will need such materials: small beads of any color, a needle, a thin ribbon, thread, earrings, decorative clips in the shape of a hat. Procedure:





  1. String the beads on a needle with a thread (5 cm long) and sew to the edge of the ribbon (in length). You need to do this from 15 to 20 columns of beads, which should be sewn alternately, one after another.
  2. Then you need to roll up and sew up the ribbon, resulting in a bead brush.
  3. The protruding tape must be hidden, for this it should be wrapped with beads strung on a thread and fixed.
  4. On top of the brush you need to wear a decorative hat, tightly fixing it.
  5. The final step in the manufacture of earrings is their attachment to the earrings.

Similar earrings collected from multi-colored beads or crystal beads will look very beautiful.

Lace fantasy

To create magnificent earrings with your own hands, it is not necessary to buy expensive materials, because you can also use improvised tools. For example, cut the same lace from unnecessary things and make an exclusive decoration for the ears. Such earrings look simply charming, giving the owner sophistication and innocence. They can be both long and short, but elongated options look more interesting. To make long lace earrings, you will need a minimum of materials and very little time. Namely, two identical laces, a fixative for fabrics, rings and earrings.





Working process:

  1. On the cut lace, apply a special tool to stiffen the fabric and allow the parts to dry.
  2. Then fix the ring on the edge of the lace, do the same with the second copy.
  3. It remains only to attach the rings with lace to the sleeves - and the earrings are completely ready.

These crafts can be further decorated with any materials, although they look charming anyway.

To the rhythm of disco

An unusual option for making earrings with your own hands is the use of foil and sequins. Such jewelry is perfect for young fashionistas and teenagers. How to make earrings correctly using such extraordinary materials is described below:



  1. Two identical balls need to be rolled out of chocolate foil.
  2. Finished beads need to be greased completely with glue and dipped in sparkles.
  3. After drying the first ball of glue on a bead, you need to glue small sequins in the form of stars, hearts and circles, as well as small beads. Let the crafts dry well.
  4. Then you need to pierce the beads with a needle and insert the pins with loops into them.
  5. Next, the beads are attached to the studs.

The most interesting thing in the manufacture of these accessories is that they can be made by a child on their own. Such earrings will not only be a beautiful decoration for the needlewoman, but will also be a great gift to her best friend.

Rich decoration

Among a large number of various earrings, the first place belongs to jewelry with precious stones. But products with semiprecious and artificial stones look no less spectacular. Making earrings from stones is not difficult, you just need to come up with the shape of earrings. Indeed, for some types, blanks with holders are needed, into which stones are simply inserted. But for others, you have to drill holes to fix the rings.





Whatever method for crafts was chosen, it will definitely help to emphasize the magnificence of jewelry. The only drawback to wearing earrings with stones is their weight. Therefore, do not make accessories too heavy, since not all ears can withstand them for a long time.

Originality is the key to attractiveness

The basic rule in needlework is the uniqueness of images and the originality of crafts. Therefore, to create beautiful jewelry, you do not need to make a similar copy of existing jewelry. The most important thing is to come up with and make such earrings that no one has.

All models of the above earrings can be taken as a base, and then modify them, adding various elements of decor. Knowing how to make earrings without flaws, and with which to use tools and decorative material, you have the opportunity to create completely unique crafts for yourself and loved ones.
