How to get a Sberbank card via the Internet?

Sberbank of Russia offers customers various plastic cards. Each of them has its own advantages. They are issued under the Visa, MasterCard and Maestro logos. Now you can arrange plastic not only when visiting the office, but also online. How to get a Sberbank card via the Internet is described in the article.

Can I perform the procedure online? Using modern methods of acquisition, each client can choose the optimal type of payment system. Making a card online is easy. Remote application allows you to issue plastic with an individual design. How to get a Sberbank card? You can do this by visiting the office or online.

Order

Not everyone knows whether it is possible to get a Sberbank plastic card via the Internet? First of all, you need to determine - classic plastic is required or with an individual design. If the second option, then you need to visit the official website of Sberbank, upload a picture with a picture. In addition, you must fill out an application for an invoice. This service is paid. Such plastic costs about 500 rubles, but the price may vary (this can be found on the website).



The application is reviewed in 2-3 business days. During this period, the creation of plastic is not performed. With the approval of the client, the bank sends a request to the employees for the issue. Production takes 45-65 days after a positive decision.

Existing customers with a debit card are provided with credit on favorable terms. Submit an application for it by using a request through your personal account. The answer arrives within 2-3 days. Usually the application is reviewed on the day of filing. And the decision is made during the day.

How to get a Sberbank card? You can visit the bank branch by providing the specialist with documents. An application will be drawn up, after which the client will be asked to wait for a decision. Salary card

This is a standard debit card to which you can transfer money, withdraw it at an ATM. It works like a passbook. There are no credit limits on it, although some accounts provide for the connection of an overdraft (a small allowed loan).



Salary cards are called because they are registered in the salary project of the bank. Usually they are drawn up at a banking institution on behalf of the employing organization after submitting documents. The management enters into an agreement for opening and servicing the card; employees do not need to do anything in such cases. They are given a package with a pin code.

It is impossible to get a Sberbank salary card via the Internet. If a new employee has a debit card, then it can be used as a salary card, but everything is discussed with an accountant.
