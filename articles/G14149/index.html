Beach vacation

For tourists traveling during the swimming season on the coast of Turkey, this issue is more than relevant. Judging by the comments of our compatriots, at Novia Lucida Beach Hotel you can safely count on an excellent beach holiday. So, the distance from the hotel to the sea is only a couple of hundred meters. The beach here is pebble. The entry into the water is the same. Moreover, it is necessary to walk a few meters to the depth, so that children and people who do not know how to swim will be comfortable here. Many tourists in their reviews note that the water even near the coast is very clean. You can even see small fish that strive to lightly bite your leg. According to the guests, the sensations from this are not painful at all, but, on the contrary, pleasant. The hotel does not have its own pier. But many tourists used the pier of the neighboring Xena Hotel.

As for the beach itself, there is a sufficient number of sun loungers and parasols. Moreover, they do not need to be occupied early in the morning, as there are enough sun loungers for everyone. Beachboy will always be happy to move the lounger as you like. By the way, some vacationers in their comments note that they liked to lie directly on the pebbles, laying a towel. By the way, the beach offers a beautiful view: on the one hand the azure sea, and on the other - the hotel and beautiful mountains. Many travelers are pleased to note that it is always clean. After all, on the beach not only ballot boxes are installed in a large number, but also a cleaner works tirelessly.

A small minus was considered by some tourists the fact that used beach towels for clean ones can be exchanged at a not very convenient time (from 8:30 to 10:30). However, most vacationers would like to be able to exchange towels after dinner, on the way from the beach to the room.

Entertainment at the hotel

Many tourists praise the large pool available on the territory of Novia Lucida Beach (Turkey, Kemer). It is equipped with sections of different depths, as well as water slides. So both children and adults can relax here. Nearby there is a sun terrace with lots of sun loungers. However, according to our compatriots, hotel guests occupy almost all the sun loungers early in the morning. Moreover, during the day there are not so many people by the pool. There is also a bar with a wide selection of drinks and snacks.

The hotel is entertained by animators all day. They unobtrusively offer everyone who wants to do aerobics, water aerobics, step, yoga, dance a zumba, shoot archery or an air rifle, take part in funny contests, and also play beach volleyball or darts.

In the evening, entertainment shows are held for hotel guests. This is done by the forces of both animators and guest artists. After the show, a fun disco takes place.



For children

Family tourists claim that the Novia Lucida Beach 5 * hotel (Kemer, Camyuva) has all the conditions for families with children. So, during the day, young tourists enjoyed visiting the mini club, where they were offered various fun and activities in accordance with their age. In the evening, a mini disco is held for children. And during the show program for adults, kids are invited to watch cartoons with the animator to allow parents to relax. Also, family tourists note an excellent playground on the hotel territory and the presence of a children's table in the restaurant.
