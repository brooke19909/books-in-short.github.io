Review of the vacuum cleaner Redmond RV-308

Now the store shelves are filled with Redmond brand products. It has a low price. Naturally, this leads to increased interest from consumers.

Redmond is a Russian brand that sells household appliances. The main specialization is multicookers, however, you can see other appliances, for example, vacuum cleaners. Can I trust the domestic brand? It is definitely impossible to answer this question. This is a personal matter for everyone. However, this brand has one significant advantage - affordable price. Vacuum cleaner Redmond RV-308 is a modern unit, equipped with innovative technologies. Its average price is 10,000 rubles, while models of other brands similar in technical characteristics are more expensive by 3,000-5,000. It is impossible to describe this device in a nutshell, so let's take a closer look at all the main points. And also decide whether the RV-308 model is a profitable purchase.



Description

The Redmond RV-308 vacuum cleaner is externally decorated in a contemporary style. A combination of smooth curved lines looks spectacular. The case is glossy, has a beautiful silver tint. The garbage container is installed at an angle. The suction hose is mounted at the very bottom of the housing. This form gives a masculine and somewhat aggressive look. The rear wheels are large enough, in height reach almost to the very top. Front - hidden, located directly on the bottom panel. The control buttons compare favorably with the general background. At the back there is a special mount for the pipe with a brush. There is also a storage compartment for nozzles.

The Redmond RV-308 model weighs almost 10 kg. Sold in a package with dimensions 55 x 31 x 39.5 cm.



Specifications

This appliance replenishes the ranks of vacuum cleaners without a dust bag . Instead, a special container is installed, the capacity of which is 1.5 liters. It is intended only for dry cleaning. Using the RV-308 is not allowed to collect fluid residues, as this may result in engine damage. The unit emits 78 dB noise during operation.

The engine for the Redmond RV-308 vacuum cleaner is designed for the following indicators: power consumption - 1600 W, suction - 380 W. Allowable voltage: 220-240 V (50-60 Hz). The engine is also protected by three systems: from overheating, temperature control and soft start. There is a telescopic steel tube, the hose is plastic. The length of the power cord is 6 m, the cleaning radius is 10 m. The cable is rewound automatically.

A power adjustment button is located on the chassis. With its help, you can set an individual level: from the minimum to the maximum. Filter types for the Redmond RV-308 vacuum cleaner are HEPA (H10) and multi-stage cyclonic. An ultra-modern MultyCyclone 8 + 1 system is also available. She is responsible for cleaning the air.

Equipment

Before buying any household appliances, it is recommended to check the equipment. Changes may only be made by the manufacturer.

So, what should be included with the Redmond RV-308 device:

  • Vacuum cleaner itself. It is recommended to check the integrity of the case.
  • Instruction and warranty card are the most important documents. When buying, they should be filled by the seller. If this is not done, then in case of failure during the warranty period, free maintenance will be denied, even if the device has a factory defect.
  • Flexible hose and telescopic tube. It is better to immediately check the fastening places in the store (they should ideally fit in diameter).

Dust collector device

The Redmond RV-308 model has a container with a cyclone cleaning system. Directly next to the hose hole is the garbage collection section. A transparent flask is attached to it. On its outer surface there is a button. With its help, you can detach the section in which dust accumulates. There is also a special hole for dirty air.

Inside the flask, a cyclone type filtration system is installed. It consists of several compartments, where initial cleaning takes place, then secondary (for the smallest particles of dust). The flask itself is closed by a lid with locks. It has a hole for clean air. Inside it is a double motor protection filter and a removable panel. After operating the vacuum cleaner, it is recommended to flush these elements.



Preparation for work

Before checking the Redmond RV-308 vacuum cleaner in operation, it is necessary to assemble it correctly. First of all, insert the flexible hose into the suction port. There is a special mount on it, so a characteristic click should sound. Only after it can the connection be considered reliable. Next, the hose connects to a wide part of the tube. And on the opposite - a nozzle is installed.

It is selected depending on the type of surface. Now you need to install the container correctly. In front of the case there is a special recess. A dust collector is placed in it with the bottom to a flexible hose and is pressed down with a slight movement. Be sure to sound a click. It is removed even easier - the button is pressed and rises up by the handle. After that, you can adjust the telescopic tube in height and plug the device into a power outlet.



Redmond RV-308: reviews

It is immediately worth noting that the reviews on the RV-308 are mixed. Many find the pros and cons. The advantages include modern design, low noise, a wide selection of nozzles. As for the shortcomings, there are many claims to quality. Many report that after six months of operation, power is significantly reduced, a sharp smell of dust appears. For some customers, the engine burned out in the second year of operation. Categorically declare that it was a factory marriage, there is no reason.
