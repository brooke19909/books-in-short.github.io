Universal goniometer: GOST, specifications, application and reviews

Universal type goniometers allow you to quickly determine the angle. Modifications with a locking function are also available. Thus, the measurement results can be transferred. Devices with displays of various sizes are produced. It is also important to note that there are models with laser-type designators on the market. Calibration is not required for them. The devices are powered by 3 V batteries. An average quality universal protractor costs around 4,500 rubles.





GOST modifications

GOST 5378-88 defines the basic parameters of universal type goniometers. All models are divided into four classes. They differ in parameters, as well as design features. The first class includes devices with a long nonius. In this case, the ruler may have two scales. The square itself is used with a clamp. The second class includes goniometers with two rulers. In this case, the stopper is located in the middle part.

The square of the model can be of various lengths. The sector is applied on the measuring surface. The third type includes goniometers, which have an auxiliary ruler. Nonius in this case is located with a stopper. The base is small in size. The fourth class includes goniometers in which there is no base and auxiliary ruler. Nonius models are used for long lengths.

Application Models

Goniometers of the first type are actively used in engineering. In this case, the base is small. Goniometers of the second class are in demand among builders. Many modifications come with a bubble level. Third-class models are used in locksmith robots. They are also very convenient in designing. Goniometers of the fourth class are suitable for machine tools and topography. They are also used in the mining field.
