How to make a tulip out of paper. little tricks

Tulips ... Beautiful and delicate spring flowers. They give joy and bring bright colors to our daily lives. Unfortunately, decorating a house with real flowers is not always possible. If you do not know how to make a tulip out of paper, then this article will be a useful guide.



In order to make tulips out of paper, we need:

  • multicolored double-sided paper for petals, stem and leaves;
  • wire for the stalk;
  • glue;
  • scissors.

How to make a tulip out of paper?

First you need to prepare the petals.





Cut six blanks for the petals. They can be any color: yellow, red, blue, pink, violet. Many handmade lovers are also advised to cut tulip petals from paper with a pattern, for example, polka dots, striped, checkered, floral. They will look very stylish. By the way, flowers from bright multi-colored clippings of newspapers or magazines are very unusual. It all depends on the interior, which they will subsequently decorate.

Next, bend the left and right side of the petal with the edges inward.





Apply paper glue to the middle bottom of the petal.

Glue the extreme parts inward to the middle. Do the same with all six petals.
As a result, the petals began to resemble false nails.

Cut out a circle of paper of the same color. In the center of it we make a small hole with a gypsy needle or awl.

Glue the finished petals with the bottom on the diameter of the circle in two layers.





The outer layer should consist of four petals, the inner one of two.

Glued? The bud is ready. Now we take the green paper, cut it into strips about 1 cm wide. We cover the strip with glue and wrap it around the wire, on which we place the bud. Here's how to make a tulip out of paper - easy and simple.





The next step is to cut out the leaves and twist them a little.

In total, we need three sheets for one tulip. Apply glue to the narrow lower inner part of the sheet. Glue the sheets to the stem.
Ready-made flowers from colored paper can be put in a beautiful vase, an old ceramic or clay pot.


If you understand how to make a tulip out of paper, then to build any other flower will not be difficult for you. Large roses made from the same material are also very popular. They can decorate the apartment for any holidays, and make them completely simple.



For this we need:

  • color double-sided paper (necessarily thick);
  • a simple pencil;
  • scissors;
  • glue.

On paper, draw a large petal. Cut it out. By analogy, we make five more of the same, since one paper rose consists of six petals. We twist each a little so that it acquires the necessary rounded shape. On each leaf we make a bottom cut in the middle. Now glue both ends of the petal at all six blanks, and then the petals themselves to each other. It turned out a flower.

Cut out the leaves. Two pieces is enough. Then we make a circle of the desired diameter and paste it inside the flower. Our decoration is ready! She can decorate a sofa, a festive table, furniture.
