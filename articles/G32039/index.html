Despite the fact that the sea water temperature there is slightly lower than in other parts of the country, in the daytime the thermometer rises to +28 ¬∞ C. Evening and night give freshness and coolness.

Coming to Egypt in June, do not rush to poison on excursions to the Giza pyramids. In this area, during the daytime, the temperature can reach +48 ¬∞ C. Postpone sightseeing until the heat ends.

The Aswan resort has a very high humidity level in June, and therefore you should not visit it at this time. Fans of diving, other water sports can use the resort of Dahab. It rains rarely and the weather in June is quite comfortable for a beach holiday - not the hottest air temperature (+32 ¬∞ C) and warm sea (+28 ¬∞ C).

In addition to these resorts, in June you can fully relax in Marsa Alam and Hurghada, where vacationers can visit many entertainment complexes. Entertainment in June

Based on numerous reviews of tourists in Egypt in June, it can be argued that at this time it is more interesting and more fun in large cities - Sharm el-Sheikh, Hurghada, Alexandria and Cairo. Although there is something to do in smaller resorts.

Diving

The most comfortable weather in Egypt in early June or in the middle of the month. In the last decade, the days are getting hotter, and this does not allow you to travel through the desert. Nevertheless, even at this time, trips to Egypt are very popular: the Red Sea is famous for its excellent diving conditions.

Fans of snorkeling and diving have long been attracted by the beauty of the underwater world of this sea, and the resorts have all the conditions for classes: diving stations, diving schools, as well as rental equipment in all hotels. Among the divers around the world, the Blue Hole, located in the Sinai near the resort of Dahab, gained immense popularity. In a vertical well, the underwater descent begins right in the bay, not far from the shore. Divers who once visited Dahab return after diving with full albums of colorful photos and a lot of vivid impressions. However, only divers with wide experience can afford such an adventure.

All hotels have diving centers where, under the guidance of experienced instructors, those who wish can be taught diving. Egypt in June: reviews and recommendations of tourists

A trip to Egypt at any time of the year leaves a lot of pleasant impressions and memories. June in this sense is no exception. However, it should be borne in mind that the weather at this time is very hot, so for elderly people who can‚Äôt tolerate the heat, families with small children should choose another time to visit the country or choose a resort with a cooler air temperature (Alexandria, for example).

Experienced travelers recommend to anyone planning a trip to Egypt in June:

  • Take care of the headgear and sunscreen. Egypt's weather at this time is insidious: the risk of overheating and heat stroke is great.
