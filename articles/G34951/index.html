Lydia Maris Hotel 4 * (Greece / Rhodes island) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Kolymbia is one of the most dynamically developing resort areas of Greece. So, more recently, only a small and inconspicuous fishing village was located here. Today, Kolymbia is becoming a fashionable tourist center with a developed infrastructure (restaurants, bars, taverns, shops, souvenir shops, etc.). In addition, many well-known hotel chains have built their hotel complexes here. Thus, now this place attracts more and more respectable holiday lovers from around the world. If you want to spend an unforgettable vacation in Kolymbia (Rhodes island, Greece), then we suggest paying attention to Lydia Maris Hotel 4 *. Today we will get to know this hotel more closely and find out the opinion of our compatriots who have already stayed here.





Where is

The four-star hotel "Lydia Maris" is located in a very picturesque place of Kolymbia. It is located halfway between the capital of the island - the city of Rhodes - and Lindos. The nearest airport is 28 kilometers away. Thus, after landing on the island of Rhodes, the road to the hotel will take you about half an hour. This is very convenient especially if you go on vacation with young children. Like many hotels in Greece, Lydia Maris Hotel 4 * does not have its own beach. They are mostly municipal, so you will have to pay extra for renting sun loungers, umbrellas and other equipment. The sea is only 350 meters from the hotel. So, having made a pleasant ten-minute walk, you will get the opportunity to relax on a wonderful small-pebble clean beach.





Rhodes Lydia Maris Hotel 4 *: description and photo

The four-star Hotel Lidia Maris, located in Kolymbia (Rhodes Island), was built in 1989. In 2006, a large-scale reconstruction was carried out here. Thus, today it is a modern, beautifully decorated hotel, located in a picturesque place and offering its guests accommodation in comfortable spacious rooms, excellent service and a cozy atmosphere. It works from May to October. This hotel is great for families.

Posting Rules

In accordance with the rules adopted at Lydia Maris Hotel 4 * (Rhodes Island, Kolymbia), guests arriving at the hotel are resettled after 13:00. On the day of departure, your room should be vacated before noon. Payment for accommodation, as well as the use of additional services here you can make both cash and cards of popular payment systems, such as "Visa" and "Mastercard".





Accommodation for family tourists

The administration of the Lydia Maris Hotel is not only always happy for guests with children of different ages, but also offers guests a very flexible pricing policy in this regard. So, to accommodate your children will be provided with children's or extra beds. Moreover, if you place a child under 12 years on an extra bed, then additional payment for this will not be charged. If you come with two or more children, then you will have to pay extra for their accommodation. You must inform the Lydia Maris administration in advance that you need extra beds. At the same time, you will be able to find out the exact amount that it will cost you to stay at the hotel with children.

Pets welcome travelers

This point should be paid to those tourists who are not used to parting with their four-legged pet, even during a trip abroad. Unfortunately, the policy of the Lydia Maris Hotel 4 * hotel (Greece) does not provide for pets on its territory. In this regard, you will either have to find another hotel, or part with your pet on vacation.





Number of rooms

The housing stock of the Lidia Maris Hotel (Rhodes) is represented by 204 comfortable rooms in the following categories: standard, junior suites and suites. They are located in several three-story buildings and bungalows. Rooms of all categories are equipped with modern furniture and household appliances. Each of them has its own bathroom with a bath / shower, hairdryer and necessary toiletries (their supplies are regularly replenished). There is also a TV with satellite channels, air conditioning, minibar, telephone, radio and balcony. Rooms are cleaned every day. Maids renew their bed linen and towels three times a week. All hotel guests have the opportunity to order room service.

restaurants and bars

Dining at Lydia Maris Hotel 4 * (Rhodes Island) is organized on an all-inclusive basis. The hotel's restaurant serves a buffet, served with a variety of first and second courses, snacks and desserts of both national and international cuisine. In addition, during the day you can grab a bite to eat at the hotel's snack bar. The hotel has several bars with a wide selection of both soft and alcoholic drinks.





Beach vacation

As already mentioned, Lydia Maris Hotel 4 * (Rhodes) does not have its own beach. Hotel guests can walk to the nearest pebble beach in literally 5-10 minutes. In addition to sun loungers and parasols, there is also equipment for entertainment and sports on the water. Since the beaches in Kolymbia are municipal, you can use beach equipment here only at an additional cost.

Accommodation cost

The price of accommodation in the rooms of the four-star hotel of Rhodes "Lydia Maris", according to tourists staying here, is fully consistent with its category and the service provided. So, a seven-day accommodation for two at the height of the holiday season, in July and August, will cost from 75 thousand rubles for a standard room, from 80 thousand for a junior suite and from 91 thousand for a suite.





Lydia Maris Hotel 4 *: reviews of tourists from Russia

In order for you to get the most accurate and complete picture of the four-star hotel in Rhodes under the name "Lydia Maris", we offer you to get acquainted with the impressions of our compatriots who this year had a rest here. Looking ahead, we inform that the vast majority of tourists were more than satisfied with their choice and are ready to recommend this hotel to anyone who wants to visit the island of Rhodes.

The travelers liked the hotel rooms very much. According to them, they are very spacious, stylishly decorated and furnished with new furniture. The equipment in the rooms is modern, in working condition. If any malfunctions arise, then the problem will be fixed very quickly. In addition, many tourists note the fact that upon arrival, the staff tries to accommodate arriving guests in the best rooms, whenever possible, without requiring any additional payment. So, many of our compatriots were accommodated in junior suites, although only standard rooms were pre-paid. Such an attitude towards guests by the Lydia Maris Hotel 4 * (Kolymbia) administration was appreciated by tourists. As for the cleaning of the rooms, it was always carried out regularly and carefully. Also, maids changed towels and bed linen three times a week. Satisfied with the travelers and the location of the hotel. According to them, the entire tourism infrastructure is within walking distance.





As for the reviews of vacationers about nutrition, all of them are almost 100% positive. So, according to tourists, the choice of dishes is very wide. And a lot of superbly prepared meat dishes. Many guests of Lidia Maris celebrate delicious pizza, which was cooked in the afternoon in a street-mounted oven. Guests also appreciated the constant availability of fresh fruits and vegetables, a variety of desserts, sweets, pastries and snacks. Travelers enjoyed the drinks served in the hotel bars.

As for the distance from the beach, then, according to our compatriots, it takes no more than ten minutes to go from the Lydia Maris Hotel 4 * to the sea. The beaches here are small pebble and very clean. In general, almost everyone was satisfied with a beach holiday.

But back to the hotel. Since the main contingent of its guests consists of middle-aged Europeans, there is no stormy animation program and noisy entertainment here. However, there are still animators. During the day, they invite guests to do water aerobics, play volleyball or water polo, etc., and in the evenings they hold mini-discos for the youngest travelers. Also on site you can have a great time near the pool. In addition, tourists who visited the local spa were very pleased. According to them, the services here are not cheap, but their quality is beyond praise. In addition, experienced travelers recommend that all vacationers in Rhodes must make trips to the sights of the island. Moreover, it is most convenient to do this on a rental car, especially since the prices here are very reasonable.

As for the staff of the Lydia Maris Hotel, many tourists in their reviews respond very warmly to the staff. So, someone notes the smiling and pleasant maids, someone recalls the friendly and fast-working waiters, someone was very pleased with the receptionists at the reception, and someone was impressed by the local animators. Our compatriots note only one small minus. It consists in the employees' ignorance of the Russian language. This is due to the fact that there are not many Russian-speaking tourists staying at this hotel. However, you can always speak with Lydia Maris Hotel 4 * staff in basic English or German.

Summing up, we can confidently say that in general, the hotel "Lydia Maris" really liked the tourists staying here. According to them, the hotel offers the best combination of price / quality. Many of our compatriots do not exclude the possibility of their returning here. And they will recommend this hotel to their friends and acquaintances.
