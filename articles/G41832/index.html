Cheap hotels in Odessa: addresses, description, reviews

Odessa is a favorite vacation spot on the Black Sea coast. This is not only a popular resort, but also an amazing city full of many interesting sights. Odessa is an atmosphere of joy, comfort, fun, humor and solid positive.





Housing in Odessa

Rest in Odessa is, first of all, comfort and warmth. Such an atmosphere is created not only due to the unique climate and special mood of local residents, but also due to the many comfortable hotels, pensions and rest houses located in the city. Here you can choose housing for every taste and for any money.

Most housing offers come from the center or Primorsky district of Odessa, as these places are most suitable for a quality vacation. In all this diversity you can find cheap hotels in Odessa.

Thanks to the developed transport infrastructure, the hostel guests can always get to any beach and anywhere in the city if they wish. There are many places for car parking.

Eco-hotel "Sliver"

Hostel "Sliver", located in the center of Odessa, on the street. Schepkina, 21, is the first eco-hostel in the city. Its guests have a great opportunity to explore the city - attractions, including Deribasovskaya Street, are within walking distance.



Eco-hostel "Sliver" adheres to the philosophy of healthy and environmentally friendly recreation. Room decoration corresponds to this: natural materials in the interior, pine and linden furniture, cotton and linen bedding, and mattresses are also natural. And for washing and cleaning, the hostel workers use only biodegradable products that do not harm the environment.

It offers guests - 24-hour reception, free Wi-Fi, safe, telephone. The kitchen is equipped with electric stove, fridge, microwave oven, dishes.
Visitors can stay in one of the air-conditioned rooms:

  • shared 10-bed room (with bunk beds, shared bathroom and toilet);
  • 6-bed dormitory room (with bunk beds, shared bathroom and toilet);
  • double standard (with a large 2 double bed and a TV).

Tourists are impressed by the opportunity of a comfortable stay in environmentally friendly conditions. This gives an additional boost of energy.

Hotel "Passage"

The building of the Passage Hotel (Odessa), located in the very center of the city (on Preobrazhenskaya St., 34), is itself one of the main attractions of the city. This beautiful building with exquisite sculptures (a monument of architecture in the Baroque style) was built at the turn of the 19th and 20th centuries and was originally designed as a hotel and shopping center.

Today it is a unique complex in which, in addition to the hotel, there are also a number of shops located in the courtyard under a glass roof.





It offers - 130 air-conditioned rooms. They include a living room, a private balcony, and attached bathrooms with shower and bathtub. Extra beds are not available.

Reception is open around the clock. Guests at the hotel can enjoy a bar or restaurant serving Georgian cuisine.
