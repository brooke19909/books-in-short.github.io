"Mona" - hotel in Lobny: description, features and reviews

Do you need to organize a celebration with a large number of guests or to get together with a close company of friends for a joint holiday? Maybe you need to hold a business event, arrange a photo shoot in the park or just stay with your family? These and many other services are provided by the Mona Boutique Hotel in Lobny.

Briefly about the hotel

"Mona" - a hotel located in the park. It is a four-story building with a glass facade and unique interior design.

It offers - luxurious rooms with a balcony, Italian furniture and modern appliances, restaurants and bars, barbecue places, a summer cafe-terrace, conference rooms. Guests can relax comfortably in the Art Deco lobby or in the fresh air. An elegant park with pretty paths, a fountain, charming benches and gazebos awaits its visitors. So, not far from the capital, guests are immersed in a quiet country life.





The main specialization of the hotel is the organization of weddings and related events:

  • photo shoot in the garden;
  • marriage proposal;
  • acquaintance with parents;
  • hen-party;
  • bachelor party.

The hotel also has special offers for organizing corporate events and business meetings. For conferences, celebrations and exhibitions built "Forum Hall".

Great attention is paid to family vacations. Many surprises are prepared for younger guests:

  • sweets at a meeting;
  • Toys
  • computer games;
  • children's area.

Hotel location

"Mona" is a hotel that is located in a calm, ecologically clean place, 40-50 minutes drive from the center of Moscow. Sheremetyevo International Airport is located nearby . Exact address: Moscow region, Lobnya, Krasnopolianskaya street, 32.

By car, the hotel can be reached via the Moscow-St. Petersburg highway, which was opened in 2014. From city transport, preference should be given not to buses or fixed-route taxis from Moscow metro stations, but to aeroexpress to Sheremetyevo Airport or an electric train to Lobnya station. Then transfer to a local bus or taxi.

Number of rooms

The Mona Country Park Hotel offers its guests spacious and bright rooms, convenient for work and relaxation:

  • Single (15 sq. M.) With one single bed and a convenient place for work.
  • Studio Twin (39 sq. M.) With two single beds, a workplace with individual lighting and a spacious bathroom.
  • Studio King (39 sq. M.) With terrace, king-size double bed and spacious bathroom.
  • Deluxe (45 sq. M.) Has its own terrace with outdoor furniture, a king-size double bed and a spacious bathroom.
  • Junior Suite (55 sq. M.) Is a two-room apartment with access to a summer terrace overlooking the park. There is a king-size double bed, it is possible to install an extra bed.




In addition, each room is equipped with air conditioning, a minibar, a safety deposit box, satellite TV, a coffee maker, a DVD player, a dedicated Internet line and Wi-Fi, a hairdryer, hypoallergenic bedding and toiletries.

At the request of vacationers, a high chair and a playpen are provided for younger guests.

restaurants and bars

"Mona" is a hotel in which there are two restaurants, a bar and a summer terrace :

  • Restaurant Andante. The hotel serves a buffet breakfast in its spacious lounge.
  • Restaurant Mona. Here, guests, sitting on comfortable sofas or traditionally at tables, can appreciate the originality of the preparation of European dishes by local chefs.
  • Bar Da Vinci. Here, to soft music, comfortably sitting in a soft armchair, you can conduct a business conversation, maintain a friendly conversation or relax in solitude.
  • Veranda Dolce. It functions in the warm season. In addition to the standard menu, there are hookahs and barbecue.




Also, the Mona Hotel provides all kinds of restaurant services around the clock, from coffee breaks to gala dinners.

Additional services

The Mona (hotel) offers its guests a number of additional amenities:

  • concierge service;
  • transfer on executive cars;
  • Wi-Fi
  • round-the-clock concierge service;
  • parking;
  • ATM;
  • luggage storage and room service;
  • laundry and dry cleaning;
  • safe deposit boxes at the reception and a safe in the room;
  • a computer with Internet access and other office services;
  • recharging cell phones;
  • conference rooms.




Active guests can visit the neighboring wellness center with a swimming pool or drive to the Sports Palace in Lobny in ten minutes. Also, everyone is invited to go fishing and play golf. Special offers for organizing wedding events and business meetings have been prepared.

Guest reviews

Mona, a hotel whose reviews are mostly positive, is considered one of the best places in the Moscow Region for weddings and corporate parties. It has a superb park, stylish rooms, delicious food and friendly staff. And if there are small incidents in service, then they may well be offset by the beauty and comfort of the hotel complex.
