Where to begin?

To begin with, what can be done from improvised means - paper, scissors, water, ice, plastic cups and much more, where glue is absolutely not needed. You can make a huge number of various figures and interesting paper crafts. Schemes, templates are presented below.





Paper bow

We will need:

  • Three narrow strips of paper - completely different in shades and widths.
  • Wide - 20 cm.
  • Average - 48 cm.
  • Narrow - 46 cm.

To make crafts from white paper without glue, we need to do the following steps:

  1. At the ends of each ribbon, make a V-neckline.
  2. Now gently fold the strips - wide from the bottom, middle - in the middle, narrow - from above.
  3. We combine the centers of the strips and clamp them with a paper clip.
  4. We wrap the narrowest strip in the place of compression and make a bow so that the knot is below the wide strip.
  5. Straighten the bow.
  6. You have a beautiful bow for dressing a gift box.
  7. The ends of the bow are cut at an angle.
  8. That's all, the paperwork without glue is ready!

Candle holders

We all perfectly remember the fairy tale of the Snow Queen. She lived in a beautiful ice palace, which played with different colors. So we decided that we need to plunge into a fairy tale. The exception will be that our candlesticks made of ice will bring you cozy warmth. Let's start manufacturing.

We will need:

  • Forms (you can use cups, plastic containers, etc.).
  • Water.
  • Candles

Manufacture:

  1. Pour water into the molds.
  2. We put them in a cold place. We are waiting for the water in them to freeze somewhere by 80%. Now put our candles in the center.
  3. We look whether the candlesticks are completely frozen. If so, then pour water over the molds so that our ice floes easily come out of them.
  4. Well, the final step. We place our candlesticks on the steps in front of the entrance to the house or on the railing. Enjoy the view of the luminous path to the country of fairy tales.

Snowman sock

In order to make a snowman, we need:

  • White sock (or socks, if you will do several of them).
  • Vata.
  • Thin rope (you can use elastic too).
  • Buttons
  • A piece of red cloth and a piece of orange.
  • Scissors.
  • Thread and corner.

Manufacture:

  1. Fill a sock with cotton just over half. Seal it and tie it on top with a rope. Cut off the remaining tip with scissors, but don‚Äôt throw it away, it will be useful to us, we will make a hat for it from our snowman.
  2. Mentally divide the snowman into 3 parts, and separate the upper, alleged head from the body, bandaging it with a rope. We tie this place with a ‚Äúscarf‚Äù on top, having previously cut a narrow long strip from red fabric.

Instruction:
