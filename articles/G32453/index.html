The best hotel in Rhodes - a comfortable stay

Greece every year attracts an increasing number of guests. Among the many tourist destinations, it is vacation on the Greek islands that is in constant demand. One of the places that vacationers have not lost interest for decades is Rhodes. This island is a real gift for tourists from all over the world who value comfort and coziness. Rhodes is the fourth largest among the islands of Greece and is located in the southeast of the Aegean Sea. It delights tourists with its picturesque nature and mild climate.



Among the wide selection of hotels on this island are presented both luxury apartments with an all-inclusive system, as well as budget options up to mini-hotels, which will help you to save money on traveling around the island and exploring the sights.

The best hotels of Rhodes (5 stars) have received recognition not only in Greece, but throughout the world. In no corner of the earth can you find such a sincere attitude towards tourists, an incendiary atmosphere, and a well-established service sector. Five-star hotels will conquer you with refinement, luxury and fulfillment of all your cherished desires. All that you want to get from the rest, you will find on the island of Rhodes. The best hotels for children are also located here.



Hotels on this island are distinguished by the fact that on their territory there are all kinds of attractions for the smallest vacationers, well-equipped playgrounds, and on Rhodes itself there are the famous water park and Luna Park.

The five-star Aldemar Paradise Royal Mare, all-inclusive, is truly the best hotel in Rhodes. In addition to chic rooms and impeccable service, there is a small but cozy beach, 6 swimming pools, a huge number of restaurants, including a children's one. For kids, too, everything is thought out to the smallest detail: cribs, high chairs (in restaurants), a special menu, clubs, animators, water slides.



Another place that among many vacationing families has come to be known as the "best hotel of Rhodes" is Esperides Beach. The four-star hotel offers not only a good rest for adults, but also a wide range of services for young visitors: a mini club, water slides, a bar with free popcorn and cotton candy. Near the hotel there are amusement parks. Having been here once, you will want to return again and understand why the rest here is really the best for children.

It remains only to choose where to stay, find the best hotel in Rhodes just for you. You will certainly fall in love with this type of relaxation and want to soak up again in the bright sunshine on the beaches of the Mediterranean.
