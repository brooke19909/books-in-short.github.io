Is there a limit to the price of photographic equipment?

If you pick up good modern equipment for work and decide to purchase the most expensive SLR camera in the world, be prepared for the fact that the starting price is not the limit. Any photographer knows how much money he has to invest in his favorite business. We examined only a few models, but often professional lenses are no less than the camera itself.
