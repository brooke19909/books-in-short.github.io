Wonderful 3 * Hotel Lou Lou a Beach Resort

Hotel 3 * Lou Lou a Beach Resort is located in the capital of the United Arab Emirates. Belongs to the Premiere Group of Hotels hotel chain. Located on the first beach line 200 m from the sea. The hotel area is fenced with a high fence with garden plantings. The rooms are comfortable, comfortable. The staff speak Arabic, French, English and Russian.





Location

Lou Lou a Beach Resort Sharjah 3 * is a beautiful hotel located in the United Arab Emirates. It is located 3 km from the capital of the country and 40 km from the airport. The city center can be reached in 20 minutes, from the airport by taxi - in 15 minutes. Check-in time is from 14 hours, check-out time is up to 12 hours. The hotel stands on the first beach line. Distance from the sea - 200 m. The beach is sandy with excellent blue waters of the cleanest Persian Gulf.

Near the hotel there are shopping centers Mega Mall and Al Fardan, where you can buy all the necessary things and souvenirs. Guests at 3 * Lou Lou a Beach Resort can book transfers and visit Sharjah attractions. Al-Nur Mosque, Al-Majaz Embankment, Sahara Center, Sharjah Blue Souk Market and the wonderful Al Mamzar Park are nearby. Guests with young children can visit the Al-Qasbah entertainment complex, the fountain in the Khalid lagoon, and the City Center. Fans of the cultural program can buy tickets to the Museum of Fine Arts, the Center for Fine Arts of the Emirates, the Museum of Islamic Culture.





Description of rooms

Hotel Lou Lou a Beach Resort 3 * has 134 comfortable rooms. Budget tourists are offered cozy rooms with a view of the summer recreation area. You can freshen up in hot weather in a chic swimming pool with a marble fence. More affluent tourists who love chic and wealth can order amazing suites with views of the azure sea. All rooms at 3 * Lou Lou a Beach Resort are equipped with air conditioning, a TV, satellite dish, telephone, radio, safe, minibar with sparkling wines and champagne. Each room has a private bathroom and shower. The chic suites have a private balcony or attic.

Infrastructure

The hotel infrastructure is well developed. On the territory of the service there is a jewelry store, souvenir shop, dry cleaning, business center, free parking for cars. Parents with young children can hire an instructor and visit the swimming pool. For the smallest staff, the hotel has developed a babysitting service. Visitors can use medical, postal, secretarial services.





The staff at 3 * Lou Lou a Beach Resort provides car rental and short-distance and long-distance transfers. Guests are invited to book a trip with and without a driver. Hotel staff provides 24-hour room service and laundry services. On the territory of the service there is a hall that seats more than 700 people. Guests can order the organization of ceremonies, weddings, presentations, banquets. Valuable items are recommended to be deposited in an individual safe at the reception. The most convenient service is currency exchange. According to visitors' reviews, the percentage for cashing out is very low.

Entertainment offered

Hotel visitors can diversify their leisure time by visiting bars and restaurants. Guests can enjoy regional cuisine at the 24-hour Al Khaimah Restaurant. A light meal is recommended at Al Waha Restaurant. The widest selection of drinks and cocktails is offered by the lounge bar. The incomparable sauna, jacuzzi, badminton court, fitness center, windsurfing, outdoor swimming pool - all this covers the excellent hotel Lou Lou Beach Resort 3 * (Sharjah). Guest reviews are only positive. Visitors claim that the service is budget. But, despite this fact, there are no restrictions on entertainment here.





Guests of the hotel are given the opportunity to engage in water sports, take safe walks on catamarans and kayaks, and visit fascinating excursion programs to nearby attractions. Visitors can play billiards, darts, table tennis, beach volleyball around the clock. Girls, taking care of their beauty, can visit the spa-center daily. The service staff offers a wide selection of various cosmetic and massage procedures, gentle body and face care. The main vacation for hotel guests is considered a sandy beach. The blue waters of the Persian Gulf with excellent reefs, motley fish and marine inhabitants will not leave anyone indifferent.
