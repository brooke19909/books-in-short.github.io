Choosing the direction of a tourist trip, each traveler prefers a certain direction. Beach area

Hotel Euphoria Palm Beach Resort (Turkey) is located on the first coastline, directly off the coast. The beach is sand and pebble, the entrance to the water is with a gradual increase in depth. Frolicking and running at the small fry under the supervision of parents is a favorite pastime for kids. The length of its own beach is 200 meters. On this territory there are sun loungers with comfortable mattresses for those who want to get a dark skin color under the scorching sun. And for those who are hiding from the hot heat, there are sun loungers under umbrellas, the use of which is included in the price. It is possible to rent an individual tent for an additional fee.

The coastline infrastructure is quite developed. There are locker rooms, showers and a towel dispenser. Security and rescuers watch over the safety of sunbathers and swimmers. You can escape from the heat by visiting the bar, where local alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks are served free of charge.

For water entertainment, a pier was built that goes far into the sea. Pleasure yachts and boats depart from here. As tourists say, they are incredibly pleased with the opportunity to ride scooters and a banana. Here you can go diving and explore the deep reliefs of the Mediterranean Sea. For outdoor enthusiasts, a football field covered with grass is fenced and a volleyball court is equipped.
