Negative reviews

Going on vacation, it is worth considering that at the hotel you can expect not only positive moments, but also some shortcomings and shortcomings. Unfortunately, Avala Resort & Villas (Montenegro) is no exception. Guest reviews indicate the following:

  • there is practically no ventilation in the corridor, and therefore there is a smell of dampness and the walls are covered with mold;
  • the furniture is very old and shabby, and also obviously has not been cleaned from accumulated dust for a long time;
  • the shower room in the standard room is equipped so that the water not only spills on the bathroom floor, but also enters the bedroom;
  • wireless internet is very slow;
  • there are a lot of cigarette butts on the beach (it would not hurt to install ashtrays or ban smoking altogether);
  • room cleaning is rather weak;
  • in the shower and sink, the drain is clogged, and therefore the water leaves very slowly;
  • despite the fact that the territory of the hotel is relatively quiet, music is playing loudly in a nearby hotel late, which will interfere with your relaxation;
  • if you choose a credit card payment method, then be prepared for the fact that there may be confusion and more funds will be written off from the account than is required;
  • breakfasts are repeated every day (it is especially strange that in the morning they serve salads that have remained in the restaurant since the evening);
  • the pool is very small for such a large number of vacationers, and there are very few umbrellas around it, which creates unpleasant conflict situations between vacationers;
  • there is no ladder to go down to the beach, and therefore you have to get to it along an inconvenient slope;
  • quite often, hotel staff forget to collect pre-ordered lunch boxes for guests;
  • any malfunctions constantly occur with door locks;
  • maids regularly have to remind about the need to clean the room, replace bedding and towels, as well as replenish the cosmetic set;
  • almost all surfaces in the bathroom are covered with fungus;
  • very strong audibility between the rooms, as well as from the corridor;
  • overpriced, if we correlate it with the quality of services and living conditions;
  • a lot of sharp stones on the beach;
  • the wellness center works only a couple of hours in the afternoon, and therefore visiting it is very inconvenient;
  • upon registration, guests are required to make a deposit, and after leaving, they will not remove the lock from the account (in fact, this money is not returned to guests);
  • When booking an airport pick-up and transfer, you cannot be completely sure that your request will be fulfilled.
