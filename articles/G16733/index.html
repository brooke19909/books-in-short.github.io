How to get from Novorossiysk to Perm: methods, cost, features of each vehicle

If you look at the map, you can see that the distance between the cities of Novorossiysk and Perm is a straight line without complex forks. The transport connection between the two points is perfectly established. Both settlements are located in Russia. All this suggests that you can easily get from one point to another. It is important to choose the right vehicle.





The hardest way

The most difficult way to overcome the Novorossiysk-Perm route is with your own car. This is due to the large distance between the two points. It will take more than a day to travel, which, of course, is problematic for any driver.





During the trip you will have to cross large settlements, namely: Rostov-on-Don, Penza, Ulyanovsk, Kazan and Izhevsk.

Throughout the path, there is a quality road, this allows you to keep your car intact.

Features

If you want to get from Novorossiysk to Perm as quickly as possible in this way, then for the duration of the road the two drivers need to unite and take turns driving the vehicle. If this is not possible, then you need to think through all the stops, including a hotel for the night, cafes and toilets. You need to print the card in paper form and bring along modern gadgets that make it easier to go.

Cost

The exact cost of such a trip can only be calculated by the driver, given the road on which he will move, the price of gasoline in his region and the fuel consumption of the car.

The most comfortable way

The most comfortable way to travel is the Novorossiysk - Perm train. Stations are located right in the central part of the city, respectively, you can easily reach them by taxi, car or public transport. The total travel time is 2 days 12 hours. But on such a vehicle, such a gap is not terrible, since you can sleep, have a bite and take a walk in the car.

Fastest way

How can I get from Novorossiysk to Perm in just 11 hours? Only by plane! Unfortunately, there is no direct flight between these two points. List of the most unreasonable options

One can guess that because of the great distance between Novorossiysk and Perm there is no direct bus flight. You can independently invent moving in this way, but you will need to make more than five transfers. The total travel time is 36 hours. It will have to spend more than 3,000 rubles.

Using a taxi is also stupid, since few people agree to move in this direction, and if there is a volunteer, he will have to pay at least 7,000 rubles for a two-way trip.
