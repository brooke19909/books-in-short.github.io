Hotel "Comet" (Vernadsky Avenue) - comfort and coziness all year round

Sometimes one wants to take a break from everyday hustle, breathe clean air, enjoy the peace, feel the peace ... To experience all this, it is not necessary to look for a suitable resort area. In Moscow there are many vacation homes that are a great alternative to expensive resorts and one of them is the Comet Hotel on 16 Vernadsky Avenue.

Where is it and how to get there

In the Western administrative district of the capital of our country, a 16-story building is located - this is the Comet Hotel. Vernadsky Avenue, on which the hotel was built, is 20 km from Vnukovo Airport, which is very convenient for guests of the capital. It is called the Federal State Treasury Institution (FGKU) hotel "Comet" of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of Russia. Every year, under the roof of this institution, accommodation and recreation for employees of the Ministry of Internal Affairs, veterans and their families are organized.



In the current environmental situation, there are not many places of recreation where you can breathe clean air. One of these is the Comet Hotel. Rooms & Facilities

Almost three hundred rooms are offered to vacationers by the Comet Hotel. Moscow is a city of comfort and hospitality and the departmental hotel is trying to meet the spirit of the capital. Guests are conveniently accommodated in:

  • single standard rooms - there is a television, telephone, refrigerator, minibar, air conditioning ;
  • single standard 1st category - renovation was carried out in such rooms, the bathroom and the bathroom are equipped with new plumbing, there is also a TV, telephone, refrigerator, minibar and air conditioning;
  • standard double rooms - double room, has the same amenities as a single room;
  • double standard 1st category - renovated, rooms are equipped with a TV, telephone, air conditioning, minibar, refrigerator, imported fixtures;
  • standard junior suites - they have the same amenities as other standard rooms;
  • junior suites of the 1st category - there is a TV, telephone, air conditioning, refrigerator, renovation is done, new plumbing is installed;
  • studio - the room has a large double bed, upholstered furniture, renovation is done, there is air conditioning, TV, telephone, refrigerator and minibar;
  • suites are two-room comfortable rooms with European-quality repair, a refrigerator and air conditioning. In the living room there is upholstered furniture, in the bedroom there is a double bed.

In all rooms, with the help of a discreet unobtrusive interior, a pleasant and calm atmosphere is created, which contributes to a good rest.

Entertainment and leisure

Hotel "Comet" (Vernadsky Avenue) invites guests to spend time in a restaurant or bar, relax in the sauna, play billiards. All this is located in the complex. Not far from the hotel there is a large and modern cinema "Star", in which vacationers can see the latest cinema. In addition to the cinema, nearby are several entertainment centers, as well as health facilities. Forest park areas adjacent to the hotel give guests the opportunity to take a walk in the fresh air or take an active rest.

Dining at the hotel

For guests, the hotel has a restaurant from 12 noon to 11 pm. Here, guests can hold banquets on the occasion of a family celebration, presentations and receptions. A variety of dishes from Russian and European cuisine is offered by the Comet Hotel. Moscow is the capital of Russia, the best chefs come here, so the dishes offered to guests are prepared with great professionalism. Guests are welcomed by professional VIA, every evening from 7 to 11 hours, vacationers can enjoy their favorite music. The restaurant has a bar, which has a large selection of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks.



The restaurant staff never makes their guests wait - snacks, salads and other cold dishes are served in 20 minutes, hot - for half an hour. In the morning, a varied buffet awaits guests during breakfast. Each guest can order breakfast or lunch directly in the room.

Additional services

The Comet Hotel (Vernadsky Avenue), the address of which we have already indicated, provides guests with the opportunity to hold presentations, business meetings or conferences while on vacation. On the second floor there is a conference room with a capacity of up to 60 people, on the third floor - a conference room accommodates about 100 people. All rooms in the hotel are air-conditioned. For mass events, a multimedia projector and screen can be provided for guests to use. If the conference is long, participants can order a light snack or a full lunch or dinner in the hall. The rent of conference rooms is from 8,000 thousand rubles per day, or from 1,500 rubles per hour, provided that the hall will be used for at least 4 hours.

For added convenience, hotel services include laundry and ironing. Hotel "Comet" successfully combines the conditions for good rest and work.
