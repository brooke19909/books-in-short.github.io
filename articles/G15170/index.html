Travel to Malta



The island of Malta is sandy beaches, narrow streets, ancient towers, huge domes of churches and the spirit of knightly times reigning everywhere. Here tired souls and adventurers will also find refuge. So what do we know about this wonderful place?

Basically the answers are standard: the Mediterranean Sea, the Maltese knights and the famous Maltese cross. But that is far from all. For example, do you know where Malta is located? Not everyone will give the correct answer.

The map on the left will help you understand this. The island of Malta is marked on it by an arrow. This tiny islet is nestled right in the middle of the Mediterranean Sea, slightly south of Italy and north of Libya. It is part of the archipelago along with other, even more miniature places. The island of Malta gave the name to the whole state located on it.

Perfect vacation



On the island of Malta, relaxation will turn into a fairy tale and bliss. Here, serenity is in the air. The sandy beaches of the island are famous all over the world for their unearthly beauty. Lying on the golden sand under the bright Mediterranean sun, you will feel in paradise. You can go diving, plunging into the transparent depths of the sea, and enjoy the millions of colorful fish. Or you can relax on a yacht, sipping a cocktail through a straw and contemplating the beauty surrounding you. Those who crave entertainment will love the Maltese resorts. The nightlife here is in full swing: whole blocks of clubs and discos. In Malta, various festivals and festivals are constantly held, theatrical performances are organized and amazing fireworks explode.

Ancient history



The island of Malta has been shrouded in mystery for centuries and is in no hurry to tell us its ancient history. This tiny island contains a huge number of unique cultural and historical monuments. Mysterious megalithic sanctuaries are more ancient here than the famous Egyptian pyramids. The island of Malta was visited by the Phoenicians, Arabs, and Varangians, and knights, and even Napoleon. World civilizations disappeared, and the island remained to this day. An interesting place for tourists will be the city of the Knights of Valletta. It is under the protection of UNESCO. The streets and squares of the city still preserve the atmosphere of the XVI century. Here you can see the ancient tombs of the knights (in the Palace of the Grand Master), the Great Harbor or take a walk through the gardens of Barracca. A good trip will be a trip to Mdina - the former capital of Malta. In the city you can feel that time seems to have stopped. Go through the Main Gate, look at the palaces, churches and ancient dungeons of this silent city.

Amazing kitchen



After a relaxing break on the coast of the island or an impressive journey, you can appreciate the local cuisine. Try fish dishes and all kinds of delicacies: shrimp, lobster, octopus and other marine life. Taste lamb or pork baked in pots with onions and tomatoes. Do not forget about the local Braggioli dish, these are hot beef rolls stuffed with bacon, eggs, olives and herbs. And, of course, the national dish of Malta is a rabbit with garlic under red wine. Speaking of wine. Local products taste very good and cost much less than imported products. In Malta, you can also try a great draft beer. Refined delicacies of local cuisine will cheer you up.

The island of Malta will undoubtedly become the best place for relaxation and travel, it will give the most wonderful and unique experience.
