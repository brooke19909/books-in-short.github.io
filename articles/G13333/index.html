Knitting owl with knitting needles. Scheme and detailed description of work

Those who learn to knit begin with mastering the technique, learning basic terms and a simple set of loops. Then they try to knit the fabric with the back and front loops. With the acquisition of experience, they master the interweaving of harnesses and braids, and then move on to magnificent ornaments and lace. In this article, we will learn how to knit a very beautiful and mysterious Owl pattern with knitting needles. The scheme will be proposed in the description of the work.





Separate item of the finished product

An owl represents wisdom and patience. Such a pattern will decorate any knitted thing. Owls with large round eyes look spectacular on hats for children. Also, the item can be knitted on women's mittens or a sweater. On men's clothing, this pattern will look no less original and mysterious. When knitting an owl with knitting, front loops knitted on the wrong side are used. This method gives the pattern a bulge, making it look impressive and original.

Description and diagram of an owl with knitting needles

The initial fabric on which the pattern will be located should be knitted with purl loops. Or you can knit a frame of these loops around 2-3 cm around the Owl pattern with knitting needles. The height and width of the element depends on the size of the knitting needles and the density of your knitting. The pattern consists of 14 loops and 32 rows, you sew the wrong edge on your own.

1 row consists of 6 loops knitted in the facial way, 2 wrong, and again 6 facial. Since our owl is symmetrical, try to keep proportions. The right side of the pattern should be mirrored on the left side. All even rows on the wrong side are knitted with the wrong method. 3rd row corresponds to the first, just repeat it. In the 5th strip, remove the first three front loops with an auxiliary tool and lower them for work, then knit 3 loops in the front way, then do the same with the loops removed - you should get a cross. The next 2 loops will be seamy, and then repeat the weaving again, taking into account that the first 3 loops should remain before work and knit at the end of the pattern. According to the scheme, the legs of an owl should be obtained.





Torso and head of the pattern

From the 7th row, the abdomen is formed, so all the odd rows are knitted in the facial way. The 21st row is the beginning of tying out the owl's head and its keen eyes. To do this, take the first loops in the amount of 3 pieces for the work, then knit 4 loops in the front method and 3 loops that were at work. Next, remove the 4 front loops and place them in front of the canvas, knit 3 loops using the front method and return to the missing loops. Following the pattern, we began to knit the head of an owl with knitting needles.

Next, follow the description: knit even rows in the wrong way, odd rows in the front. In the 29th row, repeat the actions of the 21st. Next, we will knit the eyebrows in this way: 30th row - 3 loops in the wrong way, 8 in the front and again 3 in the wrong way; 31st row - 2 facial, 10 purl. and 2 persons .; last, 32th row - 1 out., 12 faces and 1 out. the loop. The scheme of the owl, knitting with needles, is fully completed.

Sew buttons or beads in place of the eye to create a natural effect. You can tie small colored circles resembling pupils. And your owl will have an effect on others.





Openwork owl

The owl ornament can be knitted without the effect of bulge, using crochets and loop cuts. A knitted sweater with an owl on the front part will look very original. This decorative element will decorate a casual blouse, turning it into an elegant thing. The pattern on a product from light yarn looks spectacular.

Nakida in this scheme form the contour of the bird. In order for the canvas not to increase, proportional cuts are made according to the loops applied.
