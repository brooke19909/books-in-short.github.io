General information and infrastructure of the area

The Tolstoy cape (Gelendzhik) district is located within walking distance from the city center. Here are the most luxurious apartments in Gelendzhik, which any tourist can rent for a vacation. You can stay here in hotels, and there are quite a lot of them in the area, or in private houses. There will be no problems with food and entertainment. The thick cape is a place of gathering of restaurants and shops.

Also, tourists will like two equipped beaches of the Cape Tolstoy. One of them is sandy, the other is pebble. Of the entertainment for the whole family, the Dolphin water park is most popular . A quiet, but at the same time an interesting vacation - this is what Gelendzhik can boast of.

The private sector (Cape Thick is a good example) occupies a large part of the city, so it is calm and comfortable here. By the way, vacationers can always be located in one of the private houses, removing it completely or one room.



The main attraction of the area is the lighthouse. Near it there are several cozy pebble beaches. However, not everyone can get to them. Tourists who want to relax here will have to go down the steep stairs. But the beaches are worth it. The water here is very clean and clear. Locals call these beaches "Krucha". There is always a choice, and tourists who settled in the Cape Tolstoy district can go sunbathing on the central beach of Gelendzhik.

More recently, a new microdistrict has opened near the lighthouse - Yuzhnaya Street. Let it be small, but it has many shops, cafes and modern hotels. If you settle on this street, there will be no need to go to the city center, although it can be reached on foot - 20-30 minutes.



Of course, every vacationer should visit the local restaurant "Steeper". It is very popular among tourists. It serves delicious dishes of Russian and Caucasian cuisine.

As for the locals, the bulk of them are hospitable and friendly people. They will always show the way, help you navigate in the city, find the right place, advise you on the choice of a restaurant or a store. Hospitality is a hallmark of the city of Gelendzhik.

The thick cape is a district that attracts nudists. This phenomenon is not uncommon here, so do not be surprised to see a naked man on the beach. Most often, nudists go to sunbathe over the lighthouse, here are the so-called wild beaches. The only drawback of these places is the accumulation of garbage. It is removed only at the beginning and at the end of the season.

Gelendzhik (Cape Thick). Guest houses

A more economical option than relaxing in a hotel is to relax in guest houses, which are also a considerable number in the Tolstoy cape area. In these houses, tourists can cook their own meals. The advantage of guest houses is that they can accommodate not so many guests and allow you to retire from the hustle and bustle of the city.

"Ludiva"

A cozy cottage in a picturesque corner of the city of Gelendzhik. The thick cape is especially famous for its lighthouse. This property is just a 5-minute walk from the guest house. Very close to the market, shops. Here you can relax with the whole family. Guest rooms are equipped with air conditioning, refrigerators and TVs. There is also a comfortable kitchen where you can prepare family dinners.

"Wave"

The guest house is a five-minute walk to the sea coast. It has everything for a comfortable stay: well-appointed rooms with a bath or shower room, a children's room, a barbecue area and a cafe where you can order breakfast, lunch or dinner. You can also cook in the equipped kitchen. The courtyard of the guest house is neat and fully equipped for relaxation. Here you can order individual excursions to the most interesting places of the Black Sea resort.

Mini-hotel "Gottlieb"

Three-story building, in which only 5 rooms. This will provide a calm and quiet rest. Each of the rooms is wonderfully decorated and equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable life. The hotel is located just 150 meters from the rocky seashore, in a very picturesque location. On the territory there is a swimming pool with crystal clear water, a sauna. The pool is equipped with sunbeds. There is also a cafe.

Pine house

Guest house in the Tolstoy cape. Located 100 meters from the pebble and 200 meters from the sandy beach. Very clean and comfortable cottage, which has rooms of different comforts. Nearby everything you need - cafes, shops, banks. In the yard there is a barbecue area, benches for rest. On the ground floor there is a kitchen for self-catering. The administration of the guest house can provide a meeting from the station, organize an excursion. And all this at fairly low prices.
