What is a flexible screen?



As it becomes clear from the very phrase, the screen is called flexible, which does not have a rigid base, one that can be bent without disturbing its performance. This means that such a screen can easily be rolled up into a tube, or even folded in half. The phone, on which such flexible LED screens will stand, can be safely reduced in size by bending in half.
