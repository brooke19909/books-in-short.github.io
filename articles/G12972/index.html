Gebius Falls - one of the greatest natural wonders



As you know, you can watch the water endlessly. And the streams falling from above are just a bewitching sight. Among the excursion routes in the south of our country, waterfalls are especially popular. Gebius - among them. This is one of the most beautiful creations of nature. They got their name by the name of Mount Gebiuss, where they are located at an altitude of about 150-200 meters. However, in different sources you can find other names: Bigius or Teshebsky. They are a cascade of seven waterfalls of different heights and lengths. But the most frequently conducted excursions to three of them - the most picturesque. The highest waterfall is the Devil's Mouth. It reaches a height of 20 meters. A little less - "Lace" with its height of 17 meters. "Vysotsky's Grotto" is named after Vladimir Semenovich Vysotsky, who often rested in the village of Dzhubga and often visited the Gebius waterfalls. Looking at the "Grotto", it seems that jets of water flow from many small round caves. And all because the rock has a porous structure, and the pores are quite large.

Three other waterfalls are not so high: 1.2, 1.4, 1.6 meters. But they are all worthy of attention. Crystal-clear jets, like precious stones, sparkle in the sun. Under each stream there are small water baths where you can cool off after a long walk, so when going on an excursion it is better to take care of a swimsuit or swimming trunks in advance. The nature surrounding Gebius Falls is also magnificent. Photos of these places awaken the desire to immediately find themselves in Gelendzhik, surrounded by an oak-hornbeam forest, to go along the path and pick one or two berries of rose hips, elderberries or blueberries, to see how honeysuckle and scumpy bloom.

Gebius waterfalls are located on the border of the Gelendzhik and Tuapse regions, the highway Krasnodar - Dzhubga. Three kilometers from the village. Arkhipo-Osipovka has a car parking. Here is the entrance to the waterfalls and the beginning of the walking route. Independent travel is good in that you can slowly explore all the beauties, and a large number of people will not interfere. At the very beginning of the route, tourists have to pay an environmental fee. The path itself is well equipped, there are pedestrian bridges, stairs, rope fences, gazebos for recreation. Also, during a walk, you can sit in a cafe and try a barbecue, purchase mountain honey and souvenirs.

But going to the Gebius waterfalls, it is worth considering your equipment. In addition to the already mentioned swimsuit, non-slip shoes are needed, since, despite the fact that the road is well trodden, there are still quite difficult stretches of the path. Still warm jacket and mosquito repellent will not hurt, because most of the trail runs through the forest.
