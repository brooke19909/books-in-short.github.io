How to choose a washing machine: tips and tricks

At the moment, the automatic washing machine is not a luxury, it has become a daily necessity. The market now offers a huge variety of these units. That is why the question of how to choose a washing machine, becomes so relevant. Below will be a few recommendations to buy a suitable option.



How to choose a washing machine

First you need to determine the place of its placement, as well as the size. Here it is worth considering the amount of laundry that will be loaded into it. You can choose the embedded option, or freestanding. The depth of the machine depends on the capacity to load the laundry inside.

The next step when deciding the question "How to choose a washing machine?" is to determine the type of download. Here it is worth saying that it is vertical and frontal. The first option is compact narrow units that can be loaded during the washing process, they are not dangerous for children and pets. Front cars are convenient with a large loading volume, as well as the fact that they allow additional use of the surface for economic purposes. The only drawback is the mandatory availability of space for opening the hatch. All models of embedded machines are exclusively frontal.

To choose a washing machine, it is important to determine the required class of washing, energy consumption and spin. By the washing class you can judge the effectiveness of washing clothes. In this case, the best indicators are A and B. The spin class indicates the degree of humidity of the laundry after washing. The best in this case will be class A and B units. Another important characteristic is the energy consumption class. It depends on the consumption of electrical energy per kilogram of laundry being washed.



Speaking further about how to choose a washing machine, you should decide on the functionality. Units with drying are much more expensive than the others, however, in the absence of space for hanging clothes, this function becomes very significant. All modern machines are equipped with a range of washing programs. The most expensive models have up to 20 programs. Each buyer must independently determine how important this or that function is to him.





Understanding the question of how to choose the right washing machine, it should be said about the spin speed. At 1200 rpm, the laundry will remain almost dry, while at a lower rate, the laundry will remain wet. Different types of laundry require different speeds. Most often, 800-900 revolutions are enough. Typically, cars with higher speeds are significantly more expensive.

It remains to determine the volume of the drum. On most models, it holds 4.5-5 kilograms of laundry. But for a large family, it is better to consider models from the 7-9 kg segment. So you can save energy and time.

Concluding the conversation about how to choose a washing machine, it is worth saying a few words about choosing the right manufacturer. In this matter, you will be assisted by sales managers in the appropriate store. At the moment, products from manufacturers such as Indesit, LG and Samsung are in great demand.
