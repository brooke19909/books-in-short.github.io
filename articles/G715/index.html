Phone "Philips E180": reviews

Nowadays, many have already forgotten what it means to use an ordinary push-button telephone. Indeed, in the era of high technology, the main market for mobile devices is captured by all kinds of modern smartphones, which are used everywhere and can replace a phone, notebook, camera and even a personal computer.

But, despite the abundance of positive features, smartphones are largely inferior to the old push-button cell phones. For example, they are more vulnerable to external influences. They are "awarded" with not the most capacious batteries and not the best antennas for communication. And all so that in case of failure or obsolescence of the device, the owner was in a hurry to give money for a brand-new smartphone in glossy films and with a ‚Äúlifetime quality guarantee‚Äù.

Button mobile phone - an indispensable device

Many will agree that modern gadgets can never be replaced by old-fashioned mobile phones with physical buttons, with small displays, but sometimes with incredible resistance to many external factors. And what is cunning, a small phone that does not know how to connect to the Internet, and which does not have a camera, practically does not consume the battery in standby mode and is able to "live" for several days, or even more.





In this article, we will focus on the Philips E180 phone. The customer reviews of this device are actually mostly positive, and that is why the mobile phone deserves attention.

First impression

When a device of this kind falls into the hands, nostalgia sets in immediately. Of course, this feeling can be attributed, for the most part, to those who were born in the nineties and completely absorbed all the charms of using button devices.

Philips E180 will not be able to replace the smartphone, because it does not have the Internet, applications and other digital joys. But it can be a great accessory and just a handset for communication. This "baby" will not inform you at the unnecessary moment that the battery is low, it is simply unrealistic to wait. And when the time comes for charging, you will have to marvel at how quickly the full battery charge appears on the indicator.

Dimensions and display

Philips E180 is a relatively small phone, and this will play into the hands of those who have their pockets constantly occupied, or if you have to carry it together with a smartphone. Also, the mobile phone can easily fit in a handbag and even in a purse. Dimensions, by the way, are compact: 12.05x5.2x1.65 cm, E180 weighs a little: only 124 grams.





The basic functions embedded in the phone do not require a large display, and here it is only 2.4 inches in size. Its resolution is enough to make it possible to make out pictures and photos that are on the memory card or sent via MMS. There is no camera here, but, in principle, it is not needed.

Sound

There is one more quality in which Philips E180 is good. Owner reviews about the sound of the phone excite the mind. After all, the system allows you to play high-definition audio files in MP3 format, as well as AAC and AMR formats in the case of using voice recorders.

The sound from the spoken speaker is loud and very clear, so many have to turn it down to a minimum in order to be comfortable listening to the other person. Such a good volume is a plus on the one hand, because you can clearly hear what they are talking in the handset, even while in noisy places. On the other hand, the minus is that everyone around you hear the interlocutor‚Äôs speech, so you will have to retire to talk on personal topics.

Device memory

Philips E180 is equipped with a tiny built-in memory, which is enough for stable operation of the phone with standard operating parameters. There is only 64 megabytes of RAM, 128 megabytes of memory for the file system. Of these, only 2.4 megabytes are free, which are available to the user for storing a small number of SMS messages.





If you need to leave a large number of SMS messages or want to listen to music, you can always use the memory expansion slot using flash cards in Micro SD format. The maximum capacity of the memory card supported by the phone is impressive - as much as 32 gigabytes.

Battery

The Philips E180 has a lithium-ion battery that can last a week with active use. According to the manufacturer's data, in standby mode, the device can withstand up to 139 days. In the talk mode, the operating time is reduced by several times and amounts to 48 hours, which, in principle, is not so small. It is worth considering the fact that when the battery is less than 50%, the charge begins to fall much faster.





The Philips E180 phone, reviews about which often make you think about buying it, can also become a charger for your smartphone on the go or where there is no way to access sockets. All you need to do is connect the devices to each other using a special USB cable. The E180 has a special connector for this.

Philips E180: reviews

Even after using a smartphone with a touch screen, getting used to the button device will not be so difficult. For those who have previously used such phones, it will also be easy to learn to write SMS messages and dial a phone number.

The problem may be the lack of connection to a PC, so all the files on a flash card will have to be transferred using a card reader. Also, many people call it a flaw that the USB plug does not attach to the case in any way, which makes it easy to lose.





But all these small flaws are overlapped by the fact that the phone, for example, has two active slots for SIM cards. Contacts from different "SIM cards" will be divided into different groups, which will make it easy to navigate to which of the cards the call comes in. Another important plus for music lovers is the presence of Bluetooth A2DP technology, which will allow you to enjoy music in Bluetooth headphones in stereo.

Based on the above, the Philips E180 phone can be an excellent assistant in the most unforeseen situations. This small gadget is not picky about the conditions of use, it will not break from the first fall. And at any moment you can be sure that he has not disconnected and is always ready to accept an important call or give the opportunity to send an SMS message. And its relatively low price can be a decisive factor when buying.
