The best hotels of Mexico: description, reviews of tourists and photos

Mexico is a mysterious country with a complex history, beautiful nature. She takes the ninth place in the world in popularity among tourists, most of whom come from Canada and America to take advantage of the hospitality of the best hotels in Mexico.

The country is washed by the waters of the Pacific Ocean and the Gulf of California in the west, and in the east by the Caribbean Sea and the Gulf of Mexico. Stunning beaches, exciting excursions attract travelers from distant countries and other continents to the best hotels and beaches of Mexico on the Yucatan, in Acapulco, Puerto Escondido, Puerto Angel. Tourism brings the largest revenues to the country's treasury compared to other industries.

Mexico is hot, so many people come to the country from October to May.

Cancun Resort

Cancun is a large city with a population of almost 1 million people located on Yucatan. Those who prefer a calm sea need to go to hotels north of the city, and surfers to the south, where there are always big waves. The largest underground river in the world Sak-Aktun flows here, which is of great interest to cavers and scientists.
