Niko Elen 3 * (Crete, Greece): description and reviews of tourists

Every year, thousands of Russians go on vacation to the beautiful Greek island of Crete. If you decide to join their ranks, but do not want to spend much on a hotel, then consider Niko Elen 3 * hotel as an option for accommodation.



Location

The small cozy three-star hotel "Niko Helen" is located in Crete in a resort village called Stalida. Hersonnisos is only 9 kilometers away. In this city you will find a lot of entertainment: excellent restaurants, incendiary discos, souvenir shops and shops, etc. Near the hotel there is a stop from where you can leave for any neighboring village. The international airport is located in Heraklion. The distance to it is 30 kilometers. So on arrival you can reach the hotel in just 30-40 minutes.

Niko Elen 3 * (Crete): description

Nico Helen is a small, cozy, economy-class hotel. It has a total of 33 rooms. It cannot be called new, but in 2012 a large-scale reconstruction was carried out here. So, guests here today expect cozy, comfortable and clean rooms. Please note that the hotel Niko Elen 3 * is not allowed guests who come to rest with pets. In addition, smoking is not permitted throughout the hotel. As for the payment, do not forget to bring cash, as the hotel does not accept plastic cards.



Housing and infrastructure

As already mentioned, the hotel has 33 standard double and triple rooms. All are spacious and bright. They provide the necessary furniture. Each apartment has its own bathroom with shower and hairdryer. For an additional fee, guests can use air conditioning, a safe and a fridge. The apartments also have balconies overlooking a picturesque garden with fruit trees. Cleaning is done every day, towels are changed twice a week, and bed linen is updated every seven days.

The territory of Niko Elen 3 * is small, but very green and well-groomed. The hotel offers free internet access. However, according to tourists, Wi-Fi does not catch in all rooms. However, in public areas there are no problems with the Internet .



Nutrition

The price of accommodation in this hotel includes breakfast only. It is standard without hot dishes. You can have lunch and dinner in one of the restaurants located next to the hotel. Here you can taste both European dishes and traditional Greek cuisine.

Entertainment

A big plus of the 3 * Niko Elen Hotel is its proximity to the beach. So, the distance to the sea is only 150 meters. The beaches here are municipal, so you will have to pay extra for renting sunbeds and umbrellas. On the beach, vacationers have everything for water entertainment.

If for some reason you do not want to go to the sea, you can spend time on the sun terrace next to the swimming pool on site.

Turning to the reception, you can rent a car. Experienced travelers strongly recommend that all vacationers in Crete take the opportunity to explore the island themselves. And the easiest way to do this is by renting a car.
