Location Features

The hotel is located on the first coastline, just a three-minute walk from the beach. Also within walking distance is the famous Sharjah Media Center, Khalid Seaport, Al Jazeera Park, Heritage Museum, Aquarium, and Al Khan Promenade.

From the hotel you can quickly get to the Al Bustan, Al Ghurair and Twin Towers shopping centers, the Grand Mosque, the Pontoon Bridge, the textile market, the luminous garden and many other interesting places. Importantly, the international airport is also nearby. Just 11 kilometers in Dubai. Sharjah Airport is a bit longer - about 15 km.
