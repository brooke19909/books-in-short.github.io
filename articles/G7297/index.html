Hotel Olsanka 3 * Prague: description, photos and reviews of tourists

Olsanka is a cozy and beautiful hotel, which is located almost in the center of Prague. Many tourists fell in love with this hotel due to its convenient location. It is 20 km from the airport and 5 km from the train station. There is a tram stop right outside the hotel . Transport runs regularly, so you can get to the city center in less than 10 minutes.

Hotel Olsanka 3 * (Prague): description

The hotel has more than 250 rooms, varied in their content. Always available for guests single and double rooms. For tourists with children there are family rooms. These rooms have two separate blocks for living with a common corridor and shower. For special guests, the hotel has rooms with a terrace with a wonderful view of the city.

Hotel Olsanka 3 * (Prague) receives positive reviews for its excellent service. This is one of the main advantages of the hotel. Friendly staff and quick response to questions arise. If you have any kind of trouble, be sure that the hotel staff will immediately come to your aid. Rooms

All rooms of the hotel in Prague Olsanka 3 * (standard) have everything you need: furniture, LCD-TV with Russian-language channels, radio, telephone and electronic safe. The bathroom is equipped with a shower. All windows from the rooms overlook the beauties of the ancient city.

But tourists who visited the Olsanka 3 * hotel (Prague) leave reviews and a negative plan. They highlight several shortcomings in the rooms:

  • There is no kettle in the rooms. If you are a lover of hot tea, you will have to bring your appliance from home.
  • Poor soundproofing. Many guests complain that even someone is quietly walking along the corridor.
  • The rooms do not have a refrigerator. Therefore, do not leave perishable products in the room.

Rooms with a balcony are located on the top floor of the hotel. The terrace offers stunning views of Prague. In addition, for the convenience of visitors there is a small table with chairs. The special atmosphere of this place predisposes to romantic dates.

Zizkov TV Tower

Just a couple of tram stops from the hotel is the highest attraction in Prague. The tower was built in 1992 according to the project of architect Vaclav Aulicki. This building, with its appearance similar to a spaceship, stands out from the general architecture of the Zizkovsky District. In 2000, it was decided to attach 10 metal statues of two-meter babies to the tower walls. From the side, it seems that babies crawl down the wall of the TV tower. People still argue about whether the city needs such a sight. Nevertheless, the TV tower is very popular among tourists, because its futuristic style is not the most interesting in it.

At an altitude of 93 m there are three viewing platforms. You can climb them using the elevator or on foot. Huge panoramic windows, comfortable sofas and telescopes in which you can view Prague from a bird's eye view - this is what visitors will see when they are upstairs.

A little lower is a chic restaurant. Despite the fact that the prices in the restaurant are higher than in other similar establishments, it is in great demand. After all, the incredible view that opens outside the window impresses with its scope.

Within the walls of the tower is a six-star hotel. True, it contains only one number, the queue for which is scheduled for several months in advance. The main advantage of the room is panoramic windows everywhere. You can lie in the bathroom and watch Prague from top to bottom.

Prague Zoo

One of the most famous zoos in the world is located in Prague. It is easily accessible by tram from the hotel stop.

The zoo has been over 80 years old. On the territory of more than 58 hectares more than 4,500 animals and birds were collected. Large enclosures are designed in such a way that animals feel like in a natural habitat. Each pavilion of the zoo as a separate mainland with its inhabitants and microclimate. For example, here is the "African House" with giraffes and antelopes, as well as the "Jungle of Indonesia" with Sumatran orangutans.

The smallest visitors will be delighted with the cable car, playground and special children's zoo.
