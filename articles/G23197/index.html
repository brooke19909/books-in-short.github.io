Hotel Bon Repos 3 * (Spain, Costa del Maresme, Calella): description of rooms, service, reviews

The tourist destination Russia / Spain is incredibly popular among the inhabitants of our country. Spain is home to all the famous bullfights and flamenco. These places have attracted travelers for more than a dozen years. The leading position among tourists is held by the city with the attractive name Costa del Maresme. This resort is distinguished by its beautiful beaches, incredible sunsets and sunrises. But in addition to the magnificent nature, the city has another indisputable advantage - its proximity to Barcelona.

A great stopover is the Hotel Bon Repos. Here tourists will be served at the highest level, the rest will be truly exceptional and memorable. The hotel is located in the center of the picturesque town of Calella. Every room is cleaned daily, all thanks to the efforts of caring staff.





Each guest of the institution has a unique opportunity in our time - to feel completely happy at least for the rest. You can delight yourself without even leaving your own apartment, for example, sit on the balcony, sipping delicious sangria. Each room has everything you need for a convenient and comfortable life. Perfectly thought-out air conditioning system will protect guests from the heat of the street and give you a restful sleep. Each room has a TV, but in practice, guests use it quite rarely.

Incredibly pleases vacationers with an assortment in the restaurant of the institution. Here, even the most fastidious gourmet is able to find a dish to taste and fully satisfy their food needs. The original highlight of this institution is that every morning guests are served champagne. A sparkling drink is able to cheer up all day, which is a guarantee of a good time. Various themed evenings are regularly held in these walls, which give each guest the opportunity to taste the cuisine from various corners of the world. The fair sex here is waiting for a surprise. Every evening, girls receive a gorgeous rose as a gift.
