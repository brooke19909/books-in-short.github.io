Hotel Valeri Beach Hotel 4 *, Turkey, Kemer: traveler reviews

Choosing a place for a long-awaited trip, many stay at the hot resorts of Turkey. They are attractive for a number of reasons: a favorable climate for healing the body, picturesque nature untouched by human hand, and clear waters of the Mediterranean Sea. Here you can not only enjoy the sun and air baths, but also visit interesting sights: historical ruins, unique museums and monuments of all time. This opportunity attracts tourists who prefer an active pastime. One of such multifunctional corners of Turkish lands is Kemer with its calm atmosphere and hospitable residents.

The four-star hotel complex Valeri Beach Hotel is in demand by vacationers due to its comfortable accommodation and high level of service. Having been here once, you will want to return again.

Accommodation

The room fund of the Valeri Beach Hotel 4 * (Kemer) includes 73 rooms of a single category Standard. Area - 18 square meters. Maximum capacity - 4 people. The interior is classic, the color scheme is dominated by light shades. The walls are decorated with paintings. There is access to a balcony, equipped with a plastic table and chairs. The floors are carpeted. According to the reviews of guests, cleaning is carried out daily, linen is changed twice a week.

The furniture is presented as a standard set: bed, table, chair and wardrobe. The rooms are equipped with individual air conditioning, TV, telephone, hairdryer and minibar.

The bathroom is decorated with ceramic tiles, equipped with a bath or shower, all necessary personal hygiene products.





Satisfying hunger

According to the all-inclusive concept that Valeri Beach Hotel adheres to, buffet meals are available. Each resort three times a day, for breakfast, lunch, dinner, independently chooses their favorite dishes and puts them on a plate in the amount that is necessary to satisfy the hunger. The list of offered masterpieces includes hot dishes, fresh vegetables, seafood, fruits, desserts and much more that will satisfy the gastronomic needs of even the most capricious visitor. According to reviews, it is simply impossible to stay hungry here. All dishes are amazingly delicious and satisfying.

The interior of the main restaurant is warmly decorated, which contributes to a friendly atmosphere and a casual conversation at a meal. Original lamps, numerous sconces, wooden decoration and first-class serving awaken the appetite. There is both an indoor room and an outdoor pool terrace.

In addition, you can eat from the heart in another institution, working on a custom menu. You must pre-book a table and follow the dress code. The bartenders of the two bars, on the beach and by the pool, are pleased to treat travelers with unique and delicious cocktails.

All Inclusive System is valid until 22:00. Fresh vegetables, Turkish coffee, imported alcohol and bottled wine require additional payment.





Beach

Hotel Valeri Beach Hotel is located on the first coastline. In order to get to the coast, it is necessary to overcome a distance of 50 meters. The beach strip is narrow, mainly made of pebbles, lined with sunbeds and awnings. There are showers. Beach towels are free. The Mediterranean Sea forms a picturesque bay surrounded by mountains of the Eastern Taurus. The sea is blue and transparent, calm and deep. Bathing at night is prohibited due to the appearance of unpredictable undercurrents.

Kemer's sea abyss is marked with the EU Blue Flag, which means its perfect cleanliness and bathing safety.

A wide variety of water rides are provided for vacationers. The most fervent are riding a "banana", scooters, from a roller coaster. For those who love extreme sports - diving and parachuting for a boat. The student of the deep sea of ‚Äã‚Äãthe Mediterranean Sea is to everyone's liking who knows how to appreciate nature and unusual inhabitants. Having gone to the open sea on a yacht or boat, plunged into the abyss of the sea surface, travelers experience a series of new emotions and impressions, enthusiasm and inspiration.

Often the purpose of travel is to improve the body and increase immunity. This is possible by visiting the wellness center within the walls of the Valeri Beach Hotel. The local spa will easily restore strength and former freshness to the appearance, joy and tranquility will settle in the soul. The skin is saturated with oxygen, and its texture improves, metabolic processes are activated, extra pounds disappear.

Pastime

For those who are used to daily sports or, having found themselves at the resort, set out to remove the extra couple of kilograms, the doors of the gym are always open. The walls are hung with mirrors, and the equipment is incredibly diverse: from steppers and treadmills to dumbbells and bars. Qualified instructors will help in scheduling individual loads. A fit body, beautiful forms and good health are the result of hard work on yourself.

To diversify the day, in addition to sports exercises, you can play table tennis or billiards. Having competed for victory, players gain new comrades and a wonderful state of mind. The possibility of access to the open sea for fishing attracts avid fishing enthusiasts. Nearby there is an equestrian club where you can book an excursion and ride on horseback through the local scenic expanses.

Additional services

Tourists leave reviews of the positive plan for the provided additional service. The 24-hour front desk offers a range of related services, including ticket sales and tour arrangements. It is proposed to rent a conference room with the possibility of organizing celebrations, equipped with all necessary equipment. Meetings and business negotiations will be held at the highest level.

Those who wish to explore the local attractions with comfort can rent a car. Parking spaces for all motorists are provided free of charge.

Laundry and dry cleaning are paid extra. Doctor's call - on request. A currency exchange office is located within the walls of the hotel complex. Transportation of guests from and to the airport is carried out by professional drivers who will quickly and safely deliver passengers to their destination.

The reception has luggage storage. According to reviews, the staff is always responsive, friendly and polite.
