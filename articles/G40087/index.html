Phone billing. Phone Billing Program

Billing is an automated process that involves professional software. It analyzes how the signal passes through each cell, then it is sorted and calculated, what will be the cost of services for a particular owner. Phone billing forms the payment for telephone communication. After the call, he writes off as much money as indicated in your tariff plan.





Billing systems

Mobile phone billing is a complex process that requires modern, high-quality and powerful tools. Software, banking and legal support are the components of new technologies. It is because of this that the phone billing program exists only in large companies that are engaged in electronic commerce and, of course, in regional cellular companies.

The function of this new system is that it calculates the price for mobile services separately for each person. Programs store information about the time when the user called, the duration and other characteristics of conversations for a certain period (for a year or six months). Phone billing involves professional software that is used in the accounting calculations of telecommunications operators.

Load distribution

As a rule, mobile operators cooperate with each other. They install one station and use it together, without spending additional funds on software and hardware. Usually in each city one operator sets up a separate tower for himself. In rural areas and on highways, several companies use one. The owner of this mobile station will be alone, but other operators pay him a rent, its cost is calculated based on the number of calls.

Billing systems are also involved in Internet traffic. In this case, the subscriber will be charged at a separate rate. Each company has a different way. In one it is necessary to pay for the time spent on the Internet, in the other - for transmitted and received information packages.





Billing Functions

Phone billing is based on a fairly simple principle. The switch node records all the data, namely: the duration and characteristics of the conversation. Then all the information received is sent to the central station, where the calculation is made.

The software, which is installed in the central office, processes all data and monitors regulations, tariffs and prices. Phone billing contains all the information about the user (tariff, availability of bonuses, prices in certain areas).





These modern databases store information about all payments of each client. This allows you to make a withdrawal calculation. At billing stations, high-quality and powerful computers are installed that allow you to make settlements very quickly. Due to this, at the request of subscribers about the status of the account almost instantly the result comes.

The software also monitors the status of user accounts. The system has the ability to deactivate the subscriber. If a person has not replenished his account for a long time and does not use the services of a mobile company, then he is simply turned off.

As you may have guessed, due to billing of mobile phones, a message pops up saying that the system cannot establish a connection with a zero account. If you replenish the balance of the phone, you can immediately use the services, the system automatically connects them.

Most mobile operators create a feedback function. It also controls cell phone billing. With its help, by going to your personal account, you can replenish, disconnect, connect various services, change your tariff and much more.

If you go to the site of your mobile operator, you can see your actions for the last month due to phone billing. You can see who you were talking with, how long and how much money was withdrawn from you for this.
