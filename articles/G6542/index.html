Hotels in Cherepovets: addresses, description, reviews

Every day a large number of visiting people visit the city of Cherepovets. Administratively, it belongs to the Vologda Oblast. This region is one of the largest settlements. The infrastructure is well developed here. And hotels in Cherepovets can quite compete with the capital. In this article, we will consider all the institutions that received the most positive reviews.

"Azalea"

Not far from the train station is the Azalea Hotel (3.2 km). In time, this journey will take no more than 20 minutes. You can get to the center, having overcome only 6.2 km. Given that there is a bus stop near the hotel, getting to this part of the city will not be a problem. People who travel by plane in order to get to the "Azalea" overcome 16 km, in time it will take about 2 hours. It is also useful to know the exact location of the hotel. Her address: Cherepovets, Krasnodontsev street, house number 126. Guests will be accommodated in one of the proposed rooms of the category comfort, luxury, standard and superior suite. The minimum daily price is 1,500 rubles. for one person. All rooms are equipped with necessary appliances and furniture. The bathroom has a shower and toilet. The superior deluxe room has a jacuzzi, towels and personal hygiene products. The cost of living in this room is 4200 rubles.

Hotel visitors can visit the cafe or bar. Dry cleaning and laundry services are available. If desired, they can use an ironing board and an iron. Anyone traveling with valuable luggage is advised to rent a safe or a left-luggage office. Wi-Fi is free in every corner.



"Vintage"

On Sovetsky Avenue, in building No. 47, there is an excellent Vintage Hotel. It has a total of 12 rooms. It is located almost in the center of the city. The railway station is less than 3 km away. For accommodation, single and double rooms are standard and comfort. The minimum cost per person is 2400 rubles. For this price, a guest can rent a room with furniture, air conditioning and a refrigerator. The bathroom must be equipped with a bathrobe, disposable slippers and a hairdryer. The rooms of this hotel in Cherepovets are cleaned every day. On a five-point scale, visitors were rated above 4 points by criteria such as location, quality of sleep, service, cleanliness. They also noticed that in this hotel the most ideal is the combination of quality of service and price. You can pay for hotel services by transferring funds to a bank account or in cash.

Hotel Monarch

In the city of Cherepovets, on Detskaya Street, at number 11, there is an excellent guest house. It is called the "Monarch." The airport is very far - about 20 km, the journey will take more than 2 hours. But to the city center - only 10-15 minutes. For accommodation, standard, deluxe, comfort rooms are provided. Prices start at 3600 rubles. For this price, a single room is equipped with all necessary furniture and a TV. There is access to Wi-Fi. In the bathroom, guests will find a hairdryer, jacuzzi, towels, bathrobe and even slippers. Be sure to provide the presence of cosmetics: shampoo, soap, shower gel and more. The most expensive room that Monarch Hotel provides is a deluxe room with a private entrance. Spent the night in it will cost 19,000 rubles. It is designed for two guests. There is a sauna on site for visitors, a steam bath is offered in the Turkish bath, a plunge in the pool and a visit to the spa. Cleaning is done every day. There is an additional service - transfer, its cost is not included in the payment. For almost all criteria, this hotel received a high score. "Victoria"

More recently, the new Victoria Hotel has opened. It is located 3 km from the center and the train station, and the journey takes about 20 minutes. Tourists are offered double, triple and quadruple economy rooms and a single standard. The minimum cost of living, which is 1300 rubles., Meals are not included. The hotel is located at the address: Cherepovets, P. Okinina street, house number 9. While staying at the hotel, guests can use the laundry and dry cleaning services. The reception is open around the clock, taxi calls are free.

Guest house "Voyage"

On Ustyuzhenskaya street, in the house number 9, there is a guest house called "Voyage". Here you can rent a bed. A day, its cost will cost 350 rubles. Also offers a double room categories Single, Double, Twin. Their price varies from 700 to 2600 rubles. The most expensive rooms are considered family. They have a double bed, there is all the necessary furniture. A set of bed linen is provided. There are lamps on each side of the bed, there is Wi-Fi, a TV and a kettle in the room. The bathroom comes with a mirror, towels and a hairdryer.

Guest house "Voyage" offers to use the parking lot, a shared kitchen. On-site facilities include billiards and table tennis. Sewing and ironing facilities are provided free of charge. If necessary, a taxi is called at the reception.





Voskresenskaya

In the center of Cherepovets, on Sovetskiy Avenue, at number 82, is located the hotel "Voskresenskaya". Visitors liked the cleanliness, location and service most. There are four categories of rooms in the hotel. It opened 11 years ago. Here you can rent these rooms: economy single, quadruple suite, standard double, studio (up to 3 people). The cost for one day varies from 1980 to 4680 rubles. Wi-Fi is free. The rooms are equipped with air conditioning and a safety deposit box. Guests with private cars are invited to use the parking lot. Among the additional services the hotel provides the following services: laundry, tour desk, ticket sales, transfer, fax.





"Unity"

Hotel Unity is located in the city center, on Lomonosov Street, at number 19. The most visitors liked the cleanliness of the establishment, convenient location and qualified staff. Visitors will be accommodated in 26 rooms that meet the economy, standard, premium, studio class. There is also a single superior category. This room has two rooms - a bedroom and a living room. The cost of living for one day with breakfast is 2800 rubles. The living room has a fridge, desk, sofa and TV. And in the bedroom there is a double bed and bedside tables. The bathroom is equipped with a shower, sink, toilet. If you want to order an additional breakfast, then you will have to pay 150 rubles for it. This hotel has a VIP conference room. It accommodates up to 15 people. Cost per hour - 300 rubles. Residents of Unity can use the pool, Wi-Fi, visit a restaurant or bar. On the territory there is a guarded parking lot, which is under 24-hour video surveillance.





"Peter the Great"

According to feedback from visitors to Cherepovets, the Peter the Great Hotel received high ratings. Address: intersection of Lenin and Naberezhnaya streets, house number 3. It has been operating for 3 years. There are only 53 rooms, which are represented by categories of standard, studio, superior and comfort. The minimum cost of living with breakfast will cost 2070 rubles, the maximum - 3330 rubles. Hotel services can be paid by bank transfer, in cash or by bank card at the terminal. In the family rooms there is the possibility of placing an extra bed. A day it will be necessary to pay in the amount of 600 rubles.

Tourists are provided with an additional service, which includes laundry, transfers, flower delivery, booking tickets, business center services. Businessmen can rent a conference room. It has a projector, screen, internet. For tourists who travel with a lot of luggage, luggage storage is provided. While at the hotel, you can use free Wi-Fi and parking spaces for private cars.





Rendezvous

In the center of the city there is a small guest house Rendezvous. In total, 15 rooms are offered here. The cost of a single - 1200 rubles. per day, double - 1400 rubles. They all have a bathroom equipped with a toilet, disposable cosmetics, slippers. The furniture offers beds, bedside tables, a table and chairs. They also have a TV. Many tourists who visited the Rendezvous praised the convenient location. The distance to the station is only two kilometers. Unfortunately, visitors did not really like the service, cleanliness and quality of sleep.





It is worth noting that not all hotels in Cherepovets allow pets, unlike this guest house. By prior agreement on the price, you can stay in Rendezvous with your favorite pet. The hotel has Wi-Fi, parking. Reception is open 24 hours a day. Payment is accepted only in cash. This guest house is strictly non-smoking.
