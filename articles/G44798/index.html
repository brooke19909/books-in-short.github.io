Rest at Golden Horn Hotel (Bulgaria): general information

Increasingly, Russian citizens are choosing to relax in Bulgaria, or rather, the Golden Sands resort. The choice is obvious. Reasonable prices, golden sandy beaches, unique architecture and hospitality of the local population - all this contributes to an unforgettable vacation and recovery. Bulgaria offers a range of positive emotions, a lot of interesting sightseeing tours and fun activities.





The Golden Sands resort is preferred by couples with children, as exciting activities are offered for young tourists: amusement parks with bouncy castles and water slides, electronic games and meetings with cartoon characters. Almost all hotel complexes are located near the sea coast, surrounded by a tropical garden.

The hotels have play areas for toddlers and recreational facilities for adults. One of these guest houses is Golden Horn (Bulgaria). This is a paradise in the center of the plant park. Its owners are a married couple, which creates an atmosphere of comfort and domestic warmth in the hotel. The quality of services and service meets European criteria.

For its dear guests, the administration creates comfortable conditions for a serene pastime. Such a harmonious combination of "price-quality" and attracts people from different countries and cities. Near the Golden Horn 3 Hotel (Bulgaria) there is a bus stop and a regular bus service to Albena and Varna. The infrastructure is replete with numerous entertainment centers, shopping malls and restaurants for tourists with a pretentious taste.

Number of rooms

A five-story building with an elevator is built on a beautiful territory. It has rooms of different comforts from standard to luxury class. Each room has air conditioning and Internet access (chargeable). Each room has a balcony. The rooms are furnished with good quality wooden furniture. A compact fridge and kettle are included.



There is a TV, landline telephone for communication with the administration and radio. A hairdryer (upon request) and hygiene products are provided free of charge. For an additional fee, you can use the bar. Golden Horn Hotel (Bulgaria) provides daily cleaning. Children are provided with a cot.

Infrastructure

WI-FI is available throughout. For private cars parking is provided. In the front desk there is a safe where you can leave valuables and not worry about their safety. There is a left-luggage office and laundry at an affordable cost. The building has a medical office, an exchange office and a tour desk.

At the Golden Horn 3 Hotel (Bulgaria), you can undergo wellness treatments, make a hairstyle or manicure in the SPA salon. To maintain fitness, a gym with a fitness instructor is provided. You can soak up the artificial beach and swim in the clear water in the outdoor pool. In free use canopies and chaise lounges.





For games and active leisure there is table tennis, darts, billiards, beach volleyball and water activities (catamarans, boats, banana). It offers - car rental and a television lounge with a rich collection of films. The administration of the Golden Horn complex (Bulgaria) did not forget about children's leisure.

Parents do not have to independently invent entertainment and activities for their children. The caring owners of the hotel tried to do everything possible to make the children fun and comfortable. For them a shallow pool, playground with slides and swings. In the evening there is a disco with dances and competitions. If necessary, you can rent a stroller.

Nutrition





Full three meals a day offers a three-star pension Golden Horn (Bulgaria / Golden Sands). The price includes unlimited drinks (non-alcoholic and alcoholic). Breakfast is buffet style. The menu includes dishes of national and European cuisines. The hotel has a restaurant and a lobby bar.

Beach

Golden Horn complex (Bulgaria) does not have its own land. The public beach is a 10-minute walk from the residential building. A comfortable bus runs regularly to the coast (free of charge). You can also get your own way through the underpass. Beach attributes are not issued to tourists, they can be rented on the coast.

You can buy a "burning" tour at an attractive price in the off-season. The hotel complex Golden Horn (Bulgaria) reviews from tourists are mostly enthusiastic. If you expect luxurious conditions and royal service, then opt for a more expensive hotel. And for those who appreciate coziness, comfort, goodwill and prefer to spend money on interesting tours, this complex is the best suited.
