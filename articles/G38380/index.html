Priozersk. Sights of a unique city

The city of Priozersk is located in the Leningrad Region. Thousands of tourists visit it annually, among them both Russians and foreigners. This place has gained its popularity due to clean air and unique natural conditions. Priozersk, whose attractions attract numerous travelers, cordially welcomes its guests. Here you can see both excursion groups and tourists traveling on their own. The last useful map of Priozersk with attractions. It can be purchased in this city at any Rospechat kiosk.



Having visited Priozersk, you can see unique buildings, interesting monuments and ancient fortresses. Description of all interesting places is worth starting with the main tourist attraction.

Priozersk. Sights. Fortress Korela

This fortress is located on the banks of a turbulent river called Vuoksa. The ancient building is framed by the majestic pine forests of the Karelian Isthmus. It served as a place uniting the Karelian tribes back in the 13th century. Near this fortress, battles for land and power often took place. Today it is an interesting monument of architecture and culture of our ancestors. A museum is open in the fortress. By visiting it, you can learn about all the vicissitudes of relations between Veliky Novgorod and Sweden. In antiquity, Korela played the role not only of military construction. The fortress was also a prison. To this day, the rooms in which the criminals were serving their sentences have been preserved in it. The guides will tell you numerous legends associated with this place, as well as introduce the history of the ancestors - the Karelian people.

Priozersk. Sights. Church of the Nativity of the Blessed Virgin Mary

A place sacred for Orthodox believers is located in a square in the city center. From far away its blue domes and golden crosses are visible. Citizens get married and baptize children in this temple, and services are regularly held there. The building was erected back in 1847.

Priozersk, Leningrad region. Sights. Central park

This place is a natural landmark of the city. The park is located near Kalinin and Lenin streets. Century-old trees and giant boulders delight numerous travelers and residents of the city. Only on the foot bridge can you reach the park located on the Rocky Island.



Priozersk. Sights. Kirche

The Lutheran Church, located on Leningradskaya Street, was built in 1930. It was designed by the famous Helsinki architect Armas Eleal Lindgren. According to his plans, the structure was to resemble an ancient fortress thanks to the gray walls rushing up. Currently, tours of the building are not carried out, since several years ago there was a fire in it. The facade of the church was restored quite quickly, but tourists are no longer allowed inside.
