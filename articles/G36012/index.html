Soloniki (Lazarevskoye): attractions, recreation, reviews

The rest here is a real paradise for those who prefer a quiet beach coast to a crowded quiet coast. Soloniki - this is the name of a small-length village located in the city of Big Sochi of the Krasnodar Territory, on the territory of the Lazarevsky District. Resorts characterize it as a quiet secluded place where you can fully and efficiently relax. According to vacationers, Soloniki (Lazarevskoye) is one of the few places on the Black Sea coast that is the best suited for those who come to catch their breath after a frantic race in the metropolis.





Geography, nature

The village is located in the lower river. Tsushvadzh. This area is buried in luxurious subtropical vegetation, the air here is rich in oxygen and saturated with medicinal pine volatile. The climate is humid, subtropical. Most precipitation falls in the winter.

In the center of the settlement, the mountain river Tsuskhvadzh was formed with a powder. Soloniki in Lazarevsky are spread out on both its branches. The village is part of the Sochi National Reserve. From all sides the resort is surrounded by natural attractions and picturesque landscapes. Lovers of contemplating the beauty of pristine nature call Soloniki in Lazarevsky a great place to relax. On three sides, the village is surrounded by the Caucasus mountains, on the south it borders on the Black Sea. Nearby is a dense pine forest. The air here is unusually clean and fresh. There is no industry in the vicinity.

According to reviews, Soloniki in Lazarevsky is one of the few Black Sea resorts with perfect ecology. The only source of minor atmospheric emissions is the railway passing through the territory of the village.

Description

In the resort village constantly lives about a thousand people. More recently, this place was little known, but recently it is gaining more and more popularity and is quite actively developing. Those who want to relax here should know that there are only two streets in the village: Soloniki and Tikhoretskaya. Before booking accommodation, it would be useful to inquire about its location.

Infrastructure

In Soloniki, as vacationers assure, there is everything necessary for a pleasant pastime. According to reviews, there are no any special entertainments in the local resort. Yes, they are not needed, many believe. Most of the tourists who come here appreciate first of all the opportunity to admire the beautiful nature, enjoy the silence, enjoy plenty of delicious, healthy food and visit the forest or mountains, get acquainted with the sights.

Distance, transport, how to get?

The resort village is located 6 km from Lazarevsky (a popular resort), 240 km from the capital of the Kuban Krasnodar , 63 km from the center of Sochi. Trains and trains in Soloniki do not stop. They follow further towards the Volkonki railway station (3 km). Those who plan to travel by train to Soloniki should buy a ticket to either Volkonka or Lazarevsky. The distance from both settlements to the village is equally insignificant.

A-147 passes through the territory of the resort village - a highway connecting Soloniki with other of us. points of the edge. It goes onto the M-4 Don (federal highway), along which tourists come from remote regions of the country. To get to the Soloniki by car, you need to follow to Dzhubga (Tuapse district), then turn onto A-147, drive through the resort of Lazarevskoye. Then to the destination you need to overcome another six kilometers.

Features of rest

Soloniki vacationers call a wonderful resort. One of its advantages is its low population. There is no noise, queues, crowds of tourists do not scurry through the streets, it is difficult to imagine traffic jams. The atmosphere of Solonikov entirely contributes to a relaxing holiday surrounded by pristine nature. Tourists love Thessaloniki primarily for its clean and spacious pebble beaches, warm sea, in which there are no sharp drops in depth, low cost of living compared to other resorts of the Krasnodar Territory.

What to do with yourself?

Moreover, according to many reviewers, in the village. Soloniki is not at all bored. There are catamaran rental points on the beaches, vacationers are given the opportunity to ride on water tablets and bananas, and visit interesting excursions. Many people remember with pleasure the time spent at this resort.

Soloniki (Lazarevskoye): beach

The coast of the Soloniki differs in a relatively small extent. But there is enough space for everyone coming to rest. There is only one public beach in the village, its length is 500 m. The surface is partially covered with pebbles, partially with sand and pebbles. According to reviews, the depth in the sea is growing quite smoothly. There are children's entertainment. There are also several small private beaches on the coast. In addition, nudists chose places for themselves among the stunning nature of the Solonikov (Lazarevskoye).

Rest in Soloniki is considered healing. In addition to plants that produce essential oils that are beneficial for the body, these places are also famous for healing water. Where to eat?

One of the characteristic features of the resort, which many attribute to its advantages, is the availability of fairly low prices for accommodation and food. Soloniki vacationers are among the most inexpensive Black Sea resorts.

Catering establishments in the village are represented in small numbers, but for vacationers who come here annually, they are quite enough. In one of the inexpensive cafes or dining rooms available on site, you can have a pretty tight meal. According to reviews, the food here is very tasty, the cost of breakfast, lunch and dinner is quite affordable. You can have breakfast for 150 rubles, lunch (4 dishes) - for 359-400 rubles.

Accommodation

The most popular accommodation option in Soloniki (Lazarevskoye) is the private sector. It is represented by mini-hotels, guest houses, turnkey houses, vacationers also rent rooms in private houses. The authors of the reviews claim that in Soloniki, without any problems, everyone can find housing to their taste, corresponding to their financial capabilities.

The private sector in Soloniki is chosen not only to save money. Such objects have a number of advantages over other accommodation options: a limited number of residents, the presence of a patio drowning in greenery. According to vacationers, you can spend pleasant evening gatherings in a cozy gazebo or at the barbecue. Children are provided with a playground, which greatly enriches their leisure time.

How to save on housing?

For those who plan to save on living, experienced vacationers are advised to pay attention to economy class rooms in private hotels and guest houses. Here, for visitors, the minimum comfort is ensured (a common bathroom and a small living area), but the owners will not require a large fee from the guest. Economy rooms are mainly used for overnight stays, and not for a comfortable stay. The cost of living in economy class rooms in the private sector of Solonikov in Lazarevsky is called by many ridiculous.

To relax in comfort

For those who want to relax in a quality manner and not overpay, it is better to opt for a more profitable accommodation option, for example, standard rooms that have all the necessary amenities.
