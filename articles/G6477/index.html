The most effective snake repeller

Almost everyone is afraid of snakes. This fear is laid down at the genetic level. Moreover, he is justified. Snakes move almost silently and are able to penetrate even the smallest gap. Especially dangerous is that they can bite. Even a non-poisonous snake can be fatal to humans . When it is bitten, toxins accumulated in the mouth fall into the blood. These substances, which are the result of the decomposition of the animals that the snake fed on, kill, like cobra venom.



How to Prevent a Snake Bite

There is an opinion that in order not to endanger oneself, one should avoid encounters with this reptile. Most often, the snake bites the one who just steps on it. However, her actions are unintentional. But there are times when even a viper suddenly becomes aggressive and attacks for no apparent reason. Man has always tried to invent a snake repeller. There are a lot of tips. For example, plant garlic beds around the perimeter of the infield or lay a circle of paraffin balls, etc. However, all these methods are absolutely useless.

Herbicides and nitrate are extremely unpleasant for reptiles. These drugs work as a snake repeller, if they processed the area around the perimeter. The enemies of these reptiles are some dogs and cats. Hedgehogs catch them.

Modern snake repeller





Reptiles perfectly feel the slightest vibration of the soil layers. Surely many have read that snakes leave the area they lived in before a volcanic eruption or an earthquake. This effect occurs due to low-frequency soil vibration. The modern snake repeller works on the same principle. The device emits low-frequency pulses.

Structurally, the ultrasonic snake repeller is a device, the material for the manufacture of which is lightweight strong metal or plastic. In a pointed cylindrical housing are batteries and electronic filling. There are more advanced models. In addition to the built-in battery, they also have solar panels, which ensures the device continuous operation for many years.

Snake Repeller "LS 107"



Outwardly, such a device is similar to the one used to drive moles out of the infield. The ultrasonic device "LS 107" carries out its work at a frequency of 350-450 Hz. Vibration of the device repels snakes from a plot of up to three hundred square meters. Reptiles perceive sound vibration signals emitted by LS 107 as dangerous. This makes them leave their habitat. It is worth remembering that when using this repeller, no poisons and reagents are used. Thus, no harm is done to humans or animals.

The device has a unique innovative circuit. It has energy-saving features. This factor allows you to use the repeller on one set of batteries for two years, if its operation will be carried out in warm seasons.
