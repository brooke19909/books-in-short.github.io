La Pineda, Spain: a paradise for vacationers

One of the youngest, nevertheless very comfortable and pleasant, is the seaside resort of La Pineda (Spain). It is located near Tarragona, in Catalonia, and is now a very visited and popular holiday destination on the Costa Daurada. There are entertainment centers, and shops, and markets, as well as many cafes and restaurants, but despite all this, the town is always quiet and calm. That is why families with children most often prefer a vacation in La Pineda.



Spain has long since gained the popularity of a world-class seaside resort, which is why people from all over the world now come here to spend their vacations. Each nation has its own favorite resort, and La Pineda is just the place where a huge number of tourists from Russia go. Here, most of the service is designed for small travelers: many water parks, paddling pools, children's restaurants and shops. If you spend your vacation with your baby right here, he will never forget these paradise days in La Pineda.

The city of La Pineda itself does not have any historical monuments or architectural masterpieces on its territory, so if you are a lover of the beautiful, go to nearby Tarragona. In ancient times, it was a Roman province on the Iberian Peninsula, therefore, an ancient aqueduct, an amphitheater and the Scipio burial place on the sea coast have been preserved here. Unfortunately, the city of La Pineda (Spain) boasts more modern and less luxurious buildings.
