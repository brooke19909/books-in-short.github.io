A variety of crafts from do-it-yourself napkins

It happens that you want to do something yourself or with a child, but as you think, how many materials are needed, so the desire disappears. A characteristic feature of our time is the absence of any framework and restrictions. You can do anything and from anything, for example, crafts from napkins. With your own hands, having minimal skills, you can create something unusual and creative yourself or with a child. First, a little about the materials.



Napkins for creativity can be very different. Paper white or color can be used to make flowers. Very original flowers are obtained in this way, in addition, they are very simple in execution. From flowers you can make a Christmas wreath on the door, a topiary or a whole picture, or you can just collect them in a beautiful bouquet and put on a table or a stand, transforming your interior. Using paper flowers attached to a wire, you can surprise everyone with new napkin rings.



On New Year and Christmas, I want to make the house fabulous and incredibly beautiful. And here, too, simple napkins will come to the rescue, from which you can cut snowflakes on the windows, make huge snow-white flowers and arrange them in vases, hang them on a Christmas tree or strengthen them in another way. DIY crafts from napkins will decorate the house for any holiday and celebration. This is a very grateful material, and even if some errors were made during the work, everything will be easily corrected.





Crafts from do-it-yourself napkins are also good because a masterpiece can be made from very ordinary and inexpensive material. A very fashionable and successfully developing direction in needlework is decoupage. Napkins play a major role here. There are special decoupage cards, and you can print out your favorite pictures, but the easiest way is to pick up napkins with the right pattern in the store. Decoupage is a very old, one might say, ancient art, the development of which is now gaining momentum. Using this technique, you can easily transform your furniture, watches, dishes and other interior items. Especially exquisite decoupage looks in combination with mosaics, for example, eggshells or embossed patterns applied with special materials. Before proceeding with the decoupage of a particular surface, it must be properly prepared, cleaned, sanded, primed or leveled. It is better to make the background for gluing the picture white, and the surface on which there will be no pattern can be tinted after gluing the main motive. Next, the picture is covered with a layer of glue for decoupage, sanded, varnished and sanded again. So continue until the picture completely merges with the surface. Another product can be artificially aged by applying craquelure varnish.





Cloth napkins are also a great material for creativity. They can be not just put on a plate, curled up or folded four times, but made of them beautiful flowers or figures. It’s also a great finger workout. Some kind of origami, just made of fabric. By the way, motifs of embroidered napkins can be used for decoupage.

DIY do-it-yourself napkin crafts are a quick and very enjoyable way to make your interior individual. This way you can save a lot of money and not buy new things, but make the old ones look different.
