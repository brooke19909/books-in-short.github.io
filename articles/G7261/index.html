Holiday Village "Aivengo" in Kirov: description and reviews

If you want to spend a pleasant weekend in the country, pay attention to the recreation center "Aivengo" in Kirov. A vast landscaped area, comfortable accommodation, beautiful nature, quality service - there is everything you need for a good rest.

Location

The location of the institution in question attracts with the beauty of nature and good transport accessibility. To get to the recreation center "Aivengo" from Kirov, the route needs to be laid along the Soviet highway towards the Bystrica river. You will need to drive only 27 km. Nearby is the Admiral Festival Park. In order not to get lost along the way, enter the coordinates 58.368029, 49.541982 in the navigator.

Accommodation options

At the recreation center "Aivengo" you will feel as comfortable as at home. Accommodation options provided for guests:

  • Standard is a compact room with a bunk bed that can accommodate up to three people. The cost of single accommodation is 1,200 rubles, for the second and third guests, respectively, plus 800 and 500 rubles.

  • Aivengo - a spacious bright room with a large bed. There is also a kitchen area with a microwave. Single and double accommodation will cost 3,000 rubles, for the third and fourth guest will have to pay 800 rubles.

  • VIP - a spacious two-room suite with a bedroom and a living room. The bathroom, in addition to the shower, is equipped with a hydromassage bathtub. The cost of single - double occupancy is 5 800 rubles.

  • House number 2 is a compact house with a pair of large and one single bed, as well as a sofa bed. There is a kitchen with a stove, microwave and fridge. Price - 4 800 rubles per day.

  • House No. 3, 4 - a house consisting of two independent parts. Each of them has a large bed, three sofas, a kitchen area and a bathroom. Cost - 5,600 rubles per piece.

  • House number 5 is a spacious house for 15 people with two sofas, four double and three single beds. There is a kitchen area and a bathroom with a shower. Cost - 12,000 rubles per day.

Service list

The recreation center "Aivengo" in Kirov provides travelers with ample opportunities for recreation. Guests can use the following services:

  • Russian billiards (250 rubles per hour);
  • banquet room for 14 people with a TV, music center and karaoke;
  • indoor winter pavilions for 20 people with a barbecue oven and music center;
  • street arbors;
  • barbecue facilities on the street;
  • computer room with laptops and game consoles;
  • rental of sports equipment (skis, poles, boots, cheesecakes and so on);
  • organization of business, corporate and holiday events;
  • three meals a day for 900 rubles per person;
  • bath complex (sauna, pool, jacuzzi, water slide, relaxation room) - from 1 900 rubles per hour;
  • Russian bath (from 700 rubles per hour).

Positive reviews

About the recreation center "Aivengo" in Kirov, you can hear a number of positive reviews. Customers note the following:

  • responsible approach to organizing events;
  • a good sauna with billiards, karaoke and a pool;
  • nice room decoration;
  • clean air and silence;
  • many barbecues and gazebos in the fresh air;
  • excellent food - everything is very tasty, large portions;
  • pleasant and helpful staff;
  • new and perfectly clean bedding;
  • comfortable orthopedic mattresses on beds;
  • quality pillows and blankets.

Negative reviews

The reviews about the recreation center "Aivengo" in Kirov also contain some comments. Namely:

  • the rooms are cool enough, unheated floors;
  • the lack of a normal road makes it difficult to access the base in bad weather;
  • in a large house for 15 people, there is only one bathroom;
  • in standard rooms there is no utensils (it is in the shared kitchen, which employees forget to warn about);
  • only one foot towel is issued per room, which is not entirely hygienic;
  • high prices for accommodation and other services.
