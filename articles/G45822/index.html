Crochet. Bolero for beginners

A good breakdown for a novice skilled worker will be crocheting a bolero: this is a small thing, so the result of the work can be estimated a few days after the start of work. At the same time, a variety of models and patterns will allow you to choose the option for every taste.



Knitting according to the pattern

Knowing the conventions and the ability to read the pattern makes crocheting easier. Bolero most often has the form of a wedge starting from the neck. Product length, pattern and finish depends on the model, type of yarn and your desire. The most suitable option for beginners is the scheme according to which the bolero is knitted in one piece. The main thing is to clearly follow the notation and correctly perform each element, then the result will live up to expectations. Typically, a pattern consists of rapports - repeating sections. The construction of the scheme should take into account that the rapports must fit in whole, otherwise the pattern will look torn, which will ruin the look of the whole product.



Choosing a ready-made pattern is an important step if you decide to master crocheting a bolero. Schemes in print and electronic publications are usually published together with a photograph of the finished model, which greatly facilitates the perception. It is important to consider that in order to achieve a reference result, it is necessary to strictly comply with all the recommendations of the author of the publication regarding the thickness and composition of the yarn, hook number and procedure.



Crochet bolero as described

To work on the description, it is not necessary to know the notation of the loops in the diagram. It is enough to clearly follow the steps that are given in the text. On the one hand, this facilitates the process, but on the other, it is more difficult to detect an error in the process of work, it is easier to get lost and get confused. Also, inaccurate formulations of the author of the description are sometimes failed. In principle, crocheting a bolero is not so difficult, so it is quite possible to perform it according to the description.



Knitting bolero for girls up to a year

It is known that the less voluminous a thing, the faster you will be able to complete knitting. That is why it is best for beginners to train their skill on children's clothing. The work is quick, the little things are cute, so there is an excitement to knit again and again.



The following is a description by which you can make bolero for babies up to a year. Larger sizes fit in the same way, you only need to take measurements from the model and focus on them when knitting, periodically trying on the product if possible. A distinctive feature of this model is a simple pattern that will look interesting on a small child, it is better for older girls to choose a more complicated picture.



Sequencing

  1. Prepare a sample. To do this, knit 10 stitches and knit 1 row with single crochet. Measure the resulting fragment, this is useful for calculating the number of loops needed to start knitting.
  2. Take measurements. Take a child's T-shirt or blouse and measure the length of the neck: this size is worth orienting.
  3. Calculate the number of loops by dividing the length of the neck by the length of the sample and multiplying by 10. So many loops must be dialed.
  4. Start knitting with a chain, knit the second row with single crochet and measure again - it is better to detect an error in the calculations immediately. If the beginning of the product is equal to the length of the neck - continue to work.
  5. The next row is crocheted with columns. In order for the bolero to expand, knit 2 columns in every fifth loop.
  6. Knit in the same way 6-7 cm, in each second row making additions.
  7. Lay the bolero on a flat surface, folding it in such a way that it will be worn. The place of the future tie on the neck should be in front. Now measure 6 cm from each edge, and at the place of the mark, tie the front and back part with a thread. So you will form the sleeves and determine the further front of work.
  8. Next, knit only the section that was inside the marks. The length of the front and back is selected individually. When you reach the required size, finish the job.
  9. Make ties from chains of air loops and attach them to the edges of the neck. Further, the bolero can be trimmed with any decorative elements, or you can wear it like that.
