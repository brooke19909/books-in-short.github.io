Is the consolidated budget a set of budgets of all levels or a way of state influence on the system of market relations?



One of the main conditions for the full-fledged existence of the modern state and our country, in particular with the market system of economic relations, of course, is taxpayer funds. In the XXI century, this factor can be called the main. Although some circumstances are still relevant, such as national or religious affiliation, the culture of society and the ethnic territories inhabited by one or another people. However, without the necessary amount of funds withdrawn from its own population, a national idea and honest leadership, such a country can be called simply a union of ethnic, religious, territorial or other affiliation. Resources, initially aimed at providing the state, form the budget. They are combined at the regional level in the consolidated budget of the region, form the budgets of the constituent entities of the Russian Federation and the consolidated budget of the Russian Federation in the long run. Thus, observing the exact hierarchy, the country's treasury is replenished. A state with significant capital, highly qualified leadership and a national idea will be a prosperous and prosperous homeland for its own people.

Consolidated Budget Revenues



Funds aimed at replenishing the treasury have a significant number of sources, ministries, managers and consumers. And the consolidated budget is already that common fund, which brings together all sources of replenishment of the treasury at all levels for further use by the country's leadership in the process of satisfying the various needs of our beautiful homeland. From an economic point of view, this term refers not only to the national tool for consolidating the country's resources, but also applies in other areas where this method of calculating and selling funds is necessary. Thus, the consolidated budget is also an element of the economic activity of any business entity. It provides stability to the financial functioning of both small business and entire states. In a nationwide concept consolidated budget is a kind of basket in which all sorts of state fees, taxes, licenses, excise taxes and other funds collected by representatives of the executive branch fall. She shares three main sources of revenue:

  • federal budget;
  • budgets of the constituent entities of the Russian Federation;
  • budgets of state non-budget funds.

Government spending



The market system of economic relations today is the most effective means of exchanging values, constantly wanting to minimize the influence of the state administration on the exchange process itself, but it is still not able to fully satisfy the needs of society without government intervention and requires some adjustment. And the consolidated budget is the full amount of funds withdrawn aimed at ensuring a certain adjustment to the market system of economic relations in order to more closely meet the needs of the entire Russian society.
