Where and when is it better to go to Vietnam?

Before you take a vacation and buy tickets to the resort, you need to decide which type of vacation is preferable for both the tourist and his family (if he is not traveling alone). Rest can be informative, beach, entertaining or mixed, when a person can have fun and diversify the trip with interesting excursions, visits to entertainment venues and a stay on the beach. In this regard, Vietnam provides a great choice.

When it is better to go on vacation to this country, it depends on what the tourist expects from the trip and which city he is going to. In Vietnam, the terrain is very diverse, in addition, the country has a considerable length from north to south, so at the same time different weather conditions reign in opposite corners of the state. The trip will bring only joyful emotions and good memories, if the rest is not overshadowed by constant rains. When thinking about when it is better to go to Vietnam, you should opt for the dry season. In the south, it lasts from December to April, and in the north - in May, June, September and October.

For many, the concept of when it is better to go to Vietnam does not mean good climatic conditions, but the cost of tours. During the rains you can also have a very good rest, during this period there are not so many travelers, hotels are half empty and make significant discounts to customers. In addition, you should not think that there are heavy rains all day, rainfall is short, and you can easily wait in a cafe or visit museums and other educational places in order to get to know Vietnam better.

Where it is better to go depends on the desired vacation. If you need a beach, it is preferable to go to Phan Thiet. This is a beautiful seaside resort with sand dunes, the city has a huge number of hotels and restaurants, where they prepare amazing seafood dishes. For lovers of educational holidays, it is best to go to Hanoi - the capital of Vietnam, as it is the most colorful city from the entire Asian region. Tourists will notice a peculiar combination of West and East, will be able to wander along the spacious streets, admire the ancient colonial villas, visit near quiet lakes and relax in the bosom of amazing nature.
