Credit cards Renaissance Credit

Renaissance Credit financial institution demonstrates to its customers the continuous improvement of its services. A significant place among the programs of this bank is the design and issuance of credit cards. Registration of such a service usually takes no more than 15 minutes, which provides the organization with great popularity among the population of the Russian Federation.

Credit cards "Renaissance Credit" allow you to take an interest-free loan up to 300 thousand rubles. Within 55 days it can be repaid without additional fees and charges, but after this period the borrower will have to service the loan with an interest rate of up to 40% per annum. In addition, the card makes it possible to repay loans through the services of "Eleksnet" and salons "Euroset". Also a nice bonus is the Cash back function, which allows you to return up to 10% of the funds spent in the supermarket. To do this, it is enough to pay by bank transfer at any partner store of a financial institution, and after a while, part of the funds spent will be returned back to the account to the owner. Credit cards Renaissance Credit is an excellent tool for a quick loan with a shortage of funds. They allow you to take a decent amount at any time, simply by using the ATM of any financial institution. In addition, any card has a revolving credit option. This means that when paying off current debt, money will again be available for loan.

How to get a Renaissance Bank credit card



To obtain and issue a credit card, a client needs to visit a bank branch. To receive such a service, he must meet the following requirements:

  • age from 20 to 70 years;
  • Valid passport of a citizen of the Russian Federation;
  • bank account to which you will need to attach the card. Renaissance Credit provides the service of opening an account immediately upon application for a credit card;
  • valid registration near one of the bank branches.

Renaissance Credit credit cards are represented by several types:

  • nominal - on which the name and surname of the owner are present;
  • express - differs from the previous one in the absence of a customer name;
  • transport - credit cards of "Renaissance Credit" of this type allow you to pay for travel in the subway and public transport.
