Conference organization

At times, there is a need to communicate with several interlocutors, so that everyone can hear the other and can make his speech. To do this, run a program called Skype. After that, the people with whom the conversation will be conducted are determined. A list of contacts, friends or colleagues is visible on the left side of the program window. Among them, several participants are selected. You need to hold down the Ctrl key and click on the selected participants from the list. Then in the toolbar click on the "Conference" button. A line will appear at the bottom of the window, where it will be written who was invited to the conversation. Above, accordingly, nicknames and avatars of already added participants will be displayed. Now, after selecting the participants, you can release the button and proceed to communication. As you can see, the instructions on how to make a conference on Skype are very simple.





What can be done in such a conference?

By combining users, you can write in a chat located at the very bottom of the window. Printed messages will arrive simultaneously to all contacts added to the conference. Using chat can be absolutely any number of contacts that you want to see in the conversation. Calling is the second option.





Other ways of communication

In addition, there is the option of making a conference with feedback in Skype. Each participant will be able to hear the other and speak. To do this, there is a hotkey in the "Call Group" section. Do not forget that communication in audio format will be convenient with a small number of people - no more than 10. If a desire or circumstances require more people, it is better to use special software.

How to make a conference in Skype for a collegial conversation with the host

If there is a need to discuss serious issues in a collegial community, then a certain organization and leading person of the conference is required. This is all so that the interlocutor does not interrupt another person and everyone has the opportunity to listen to others. The leader is precisely what leads. The organizer creates the conference itself, invites people, and at the end of the speech of one, passes the word to the next. Thanks to this, business conferences are more efficient.

In order to follow the instructions on how to make a conference of this type on Skype, you need to choose a moderator and send all previously asked questions by e-mail. To eliminate unnecessary noise (children's screaming, outside conversations, the sound of pressing a key), which distracts the speaker from thinking, you need to turn off the microphones for participants who are not currently speaking. To do this, click the microphone button below the photo of the participant. Such a rule will have a very positive effect on the overall discipline and cohesion of the entire team.
