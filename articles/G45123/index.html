Knitting socks on two knitting need for creativity

New technologies and working methods sooner or later come to any field of activity. This helps to speed up, simplify and make your favorite activity more diverse. Knitting socks on two knitting needles is no exception. Many women and even men know how to knit, and with pleasure will make warm new socks for their near and dear ones. Previously, this work was carried out using five knitting needles. Today, the imagination and ingenuity of man allow this lesson to be simplified. What is the advantage of knitting socks on two knitting needles?



Firstly, there is no need to move the loops located on the four knitting needles, which speeds up the work. Secondly, there are no sharp ends of five spokes. Acceleration is also achieved by knitting loops instead of replacing another knitting needle. An overlooked tool often falls out. You can also knit two socks in parallel, having the same knitting needles and two skeins of yarn.



For beginners, to start knitting socks on two knitting needles, you will have to visit the store of accessories and yarn. For socks, woolly winter threads are best suited. If clean wool is to your liking, you should know that after washing the product a large number of spoils spoiling the appearance will appear. Knitting needles must be purchased with the number 3.5. And always with fishing line at the ends, since the non-working part of the loops moves on it.



The following model of socks is very simple in execution. They are easy to bandage if torn. Knitting socks on two knitting needles is a rather painstaking but interesting affair. Not everyone can make a quality product the first time, but the following tips will be useful to everyone.



Before starting work, you should carefully calculate everything correctly. In advance, you need to measure the volume of the leg at the ankle. Then take a control sample of the future sock and calculate the density of knitting according to the pattern. From which it turns out how much you need to dial loops. Their number should be even, and it is necessary to round up.



Start knitting on two needles as follows. First, a garter belt is knitted from the heel to the ankle. Loops are put together on knitted needles. Then they must be divided in half and determine the center. Knit should be handkerchief, making in each row nakida before and after the central loop. Thus, a part of the sock is produced, starting from raising the legs and ending with the thumb. The number of rows is determined by applying the product to the leg. At the next stage, knitting of socks is done in a hosiery method. So it is necessary to do 6 rows. Then again, go to the garter stitch, while you need to start reducing the loops. This is done through a series: at the beginning, at the end and in the middle of the sample, before and after the central loop. Immediately reduced by 4 loops over 8 rows. At the final stage, you need to close the last row in any way. Then the canvas is folded in half and with the help of a kettel seam, the bottom of the toe and its side are connected. The product is ready.



It can be decorated with original embroidery, decorated with ornaments or intricate applications. The resulting socks will look very good on the feet of babies. And if they are decorated with contrasting or matching pom-poms and lace in the form of ruffles, this will be a great addition to the children's outfit.



In order to further create your own models, decorated with bright ornaments and drawings, to do your own original things, you need to master the step-by-step knitting of socks with knitting needles. Whether the work will begin with the sock, or with the heel - it does not matter. Any knitting technique is useful for further work.

Some people find this activity boring and inexpressive. But a creative person will even do such an ordinary work as knitting socks on two knitting needles for himself original and unique.
