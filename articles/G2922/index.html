Rest in Kabardino-Balkaria: Lake Shadhurei and Gedmishkh Falls

The terrain of Kabardino-Balkaria is amazing. Ranges of the Greater Caucasus pass through the territory of the republic. On the northwest side is the beautiful Shadhurei Lake. And to be more precise, there are two of these reservoirs. The first is most often called Upper or, given its size, Big. The second is a little lower, and that is why it is called Lower or Small, since its area is less by 0.2 square meters. km It should be said that earlier one more of these lakes was included, but due to unexplained reasons, it completely became shallow at the beginning of the 20th century. Currently, only the basin has remained in its place.

Shadhurei Lakes are a popular tourist attraction. Currently, a large number of tourists come here to enjoy the unearthly landscapes. It is also worth noting that this area was used to shoot the popular film "Sannikov Land." You can recognize the Lower Lake in the scene of a deer sacrifice, and near the Upper Lake in the film, the shaman conducts his rituals.

Shadhurei Lake is located at an altitude of more than 1000 m above sea level. Five kilometers from it is the village of Kamennomostskoye of the Zolsky district.

Flora and fauna

On the steep slopes that surround these ponds, beautiful alpine meadows spread. But from the west you can see low forest plantations. Wild animals such as a fox, a wolf, a bear, a wild boar live here and you can even meet a deer. And from the birds this area was chosen by pheasants, tits, quail and others.

Near the Big Lake you can see the traces of fishermen. After all, this is not surprising, since a lot of fish are found in the pond (grass carp, silver carp, etc.). There are several types of algae in the lakes that fill the water with oxygen, creating favorable conditions for underwater animals.





Shadhurei Lake: how to get there?

In order to get to the local attractions, you need to take the direction to the village of Kamennomostskoye. You can overcome this distance both on a personal car and on a bus. If you follow from Pyatigorsk, then you need to get to Nalchik and transfer there to any minibus that moves in the necessary direction.

For example, you can take a flight to Khabaz. This bus passes through Kamennomostskoe. And from here it is already very easy to get to the Shadhurei lakes. How to get to them, you can ask any local resident. Moreover, if necessary, they will be happy to take you to your destination. The path lies along a dirt road, which means that it is very important to take into account weather conditions, since sometimes it is very eroded and it is difficult to overcome this distance even on an SUV.
