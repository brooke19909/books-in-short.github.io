Needlework lessons. We knit a sweater with knitting needles

Knitted sweaters are real "magic wands" in the cold season. This garment is convenient and practical, it can be worn with a skirt, trousers, dress. You can do this thing with different patterns of ordinary and textured yarn. But today it has become very fashionable to wear things made with a thick thread. And sweaters in this case is no exception. Linking such a product is easy. Since bulk yarn and knitting needles of a large number are used in the work, the manufacturing process of the sweater is quite fast. In this article we will tell you how to knit a sweater with knitting needles. For beginner needlewomen, recommendations on the choice of yarn and a tool for work are presented. So, we study the information and recharge with inspiration.





We knit a sweater with knitting needles. Preparation of materials

We will make the product from thick yarn. What kind of thread do we need? When choosing this material, carefully study the label. It shows the weight of the skein, the composition of the yarn and the number of meters in 100 or 50 grams. To knit our model, you need an alpaca / acrylic thread (100 m / 50 g). If you want the product to turn out a little fluffy, take another mohair pail (280 m / 50 g), but then do the work with a double thread.



Since we knit a sweater with knitting needles from bulk yarn, that means we need a large number of knitting needles - No. 15. For work, prepare two sets of tools: socks and circular ones on a fishing line.

The size of the sweatshirt made according to this description is 36. You can increase it if you initially dial more loops.

Description of the knitting process

Details of the front and back are first performed by a continuous canvas. To do this, we collect 58 loops and then we knit the jacket with knitting needles with front stitch 47 centimeters in height. Now we divide the part in half - we will finish each part separately.

Back. We knit 29 loops directly until the product reaches a height of 66 centimeters. Now close the loops.

Before. We carry out similarly the details of the back to a height of 60 cm. Next we form the neck: close the central 9 loops and knit each part separately. To form the roundness of the neck, close another 2 times in each 2nd row with 1 loop. When the product reaches a height of 66 centimeters, we finish the work.

Sleeves. We collect 26 loops and knit with front stitch 46 cm in straight and reverse rows. It turns a rectangular canvas. Hinges close in one row. Perform the second sleeve in the same way.

Assembly of parts into a single whole product



We knit a sweater with knitting needles, and it's time to sew parts of it. But first, moisten the parts and place them to dry on a table covered with a towel. The first to perform seams along the lines of the shoulders. Next, we sew in the sleeves. Getting to the binding of the collar. We raise the loops on the knitting needles along the neckline, skipping every 4th. We knit the front stitch in a height of 20 centimeters, after which we finish the work. Next, we sew the product along the side lines. We hide all the ends of the threads on the wrong side.

Knitted sweaters with knitting needles, a confirmation of this, radiate warmth and comfort. A similar thing, done by yourself, will surely become the most beloved in your wardrobe. I wish you success and smooth loops!
