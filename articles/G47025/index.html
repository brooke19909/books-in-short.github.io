Holiday Village "Pine Forest" (Lipetsk): description

Lipetsk is one of the most beautiful cities in the southern part of Russia. Posh squares and parks, lots of attractions - you thought you knew everything about the city? We suggest you go to the recreation center "Sosnovy Bor". Lipetsk and its environs will open up for you completely on the other hand, you will get the opportunity not only to stay in the clean fresh air, but also to enjoy the range of procedures and impeccable service.





About base

Surely many of you, as a child, went with your parents all summer to a camp site or recreation center at a factory or large enterprise. So, we offer you to plunge headlong into that very pleasant atmosphere of not the most comfortable, but truly sincere relaxation with songs accompanied by a guitar by the fire, hiking and active games in the fresh air.

Sosnovy Bor (Lipetsk) is a recreation center located in the midst of majestic pine trees, filling the air with a pleasant and useful coniferous aroma. The essential oils contained in these plants are prescribed for people with diseases of the respiratory system, cardiovascular system. This gives every reason to call Pine Forest a full-fledged open-air health resort.

Location

The Sosnovy Bor base (Lipetsk) is located in a picturesque place, on the banks of the Matyr reservoir, in the village of Yarlukovo, near Lipetsk. A plot of 6 hectares has been allocated for the territory of this resting place. It is enough to accommodate and ensure a comfortable stay for all guests.

Here you will find several summer houses located at a comfortable distance to each other, a common residential building, as well as several gazebos, showers, rental points for sports equipment and equipment.





How to get there

If you cannot go to the sea, a full-fledged alternative for you will be a vacation at the recreation center "Sosnovy Bor" (Lipetsk). How to get to the place for those who plan to visit it for the first time? The recreation center is located only 20 km from the city in the village of Yarlukovo. You can get to the place both by private car, and by public transport, departing according to the schedule from the city bus station.

Terms of Accommodation

If you chose the Sosnovy Bor camp site (Lipetsk) for your vacation, you will be offered to settle in small but quite comfortable summer houses for 4, 8, 12, 14 and 16 people. Of the furniture in the houses there is only a bed and a bedside table where you can place a minimal set of personal items. Connoisseurs of increased comfort can stay in comfortable double rooms in the main building. Apart from a standard set of furniture, such apartments have a TV, an electric kettle and wireless high-speed Internet.





Nutrition

As vacationers assure, it is better to eat in a cafe, ordering dishes according to the menu. But amateurs and true connoisseurs of "wild" rest can do their own cooking, using the barbecue grill provided completely free of charge. True, for this it is necessary to stock up on provisions for the entire stay in the recreation center. In the nearest village there is a store where, if necessary, you can purchase some goods. In addition, locals will be happy to offer you homemade eggs and dairy products.

Beach

The recreation center "Sosnovy Bor" is located on the banks of the Matyr reservoir. This is a great place for a full beach holiday. A wide coastline with fine clean sand, equipped with sunbeds and umbrellas - that is what lovers of water and solar procedures need.





In summer, the beach is always pulled up, allowing outdoor enthusiasts to enjoy a team volleyball game.

Entertainment

On the territory of the recreation center, all the necessary conditions for excellent recreation and leisure activities are created: bike rental, badminton rental are organized, equipment for playing table tennis, football and volleyball is provided.

Security

The territory of the recreation center is under 24-hour security and is equipped with a video surveillance system. For guests arriving by car, free secure parking is provided.





Booking conditions and prices

In the summer, the recreation center is quite in demand, so it is better to plan your vacation here in advance. To do this, it is better to book a house or room in advance. The cost of daily living primarily depends on the selected category and starts from 400 rubles. It includes accommodation, using a shower, as well as a barbecue. Food in the cafe, rental of sports equipment is paid separately.

To summarize

Not sure where to spend your vacation? Tired of the hustle and bustle of the city, want to be in a truly paradise? Pay attention to the recreation center "Sosnovy Bor" (Lipetsk).
