Simple and clear procedure for setting up the Megafon Internet

This article describes the steps for setting up the Megafon Internet on any mobile phone or smartphone. Performing everything step by step, you can easily configure any mobile gadget.





Service activation

First you need to activate the ability to transfer data. This can be done in one of the following two ways: by calling the operator or visiting the regional site of this mobile communication. In the first case, we call the toll-free number of the Megafon service center 0500. Then, following the instructions of the autoinformer, you need to connect to the operator. After that, we ask him to activate the ability to transfer data for a specific number. In some cases, the operator may require you to provide passport data (documents are best prepared in advance) or provide a secret word. After successful completion of this procedure, an appropriate short text message should come. In the second case, you need a computer or laptop. There should also be a separate connection to the global web. First, send a short message to number 0351 with the text internet (the format of the letter case in this case does not matter). In response, within five minutes, a password should come to access the Personal Account system. Then you need to start the browser. Using a search engine (for example, Rambler or Yandex), we find the local site of the Megafon operator and go to it. Then we go to the ‚ÄúMy Account‚Äù (enter the phone number in the international format in the login field, and the password was received earlier in the message) and activate this service in the corresponding section of the system. Next, you need to wait for the message. Which of these two options to choose depends on the capabilities and preferences of the subscriber. The easiest way is to use only a telephone: you do not need additional equipment (laptop or personal computer) and there is no need to separately connect it to the global web. This completes the preset. Megafon Internet now provides a data service for your phone number. Next, you need to properly configure the mobile device.

When you turn on any mobile device for the first time and register it on the mobile operator‚Äôs network, the search for possible automatic settings begins. Once they are found, they are sent to a mobile phone or smartphone. In the future, after the Megafon Internet settings are accepted, they must be saved and set by default. After that, it is recommended to perform a complete reboot of the device. That is, turn it off by pressing the appropriate button and then turn it on. For smartphones, you also need to activate the data transfer mode in the settings. Then you can proceed immediately to the testing operation, which will be further described.



Test

After the automatic settings of the Megafon Internet are accepted and activated, we enable data transfer in the menu. We launch any of the browsers installed on the gadget. Then in the address bar we enter the site mail.ru, for example. If everything is done correctly, then the requested page will open almost immediately. Do not forget that this service is paid, and in the absence of funds in your mobile account is turned off automatically. Therefore, it is recommended to replenish it in advance in order to avoid possible troubles associated with this. If the automatic settings are accepted and saved, the account balance is positive, and the site does not open in the browser, then proceed to the next step.

Call operator

Another way to get automatic settings is to call the operator. To do this, we dial the same number 0500. Following the instructions of the autoinformer, we connect to the call center operator. Then we ask him to send to our phone number settings for connecting to the Internet. This may require passport data or a secret word. Also do not forget to indicate the model of a mobile phone or smartphone. After that, the operator will send them to you, and within five minutes they will have to come. Then it‚Äôs enough to save and install them by default. Then it is recommended to perform a complete reboot of the device using the previously given method. At the next stage, we proceed to testing, which was described in the previous section. If the site mail.ru has opened, then everything is ready. Otherwise, we proceed to the next step.



Mobile Operator Site

Another possible way to obtain the necessary parameters for accessing the global web is the regional website of the mobile operator. To do this, according to the previously described methodology, we go to it. Then open the "My Account". In it you need to find the section "Network Settings". Next, indicate the phone model here and send the necessary parameters. After the manipulations, you need to restart your mobile phone or smartphone. Next, turn on the ability to transfer data to the Settings menu (only for smartphones). At the final stage, we test the Internet according to the previously described methodology.
