Liapades Beach Hotel Studios 3 *: description, recreation and reviews of tourists

If you want to spend your summer vacation on an amazing green island surrounded by the azure sea, then come to Corfu (Greece). You will find a calm measured rest away from city noise. Hotels here are for every taste and budget. If you are looking for a budget comfortable hotel, then consider Liapades Beach Hotel Studios 3 * as an option. About what is offered to tourists here, and we will talk further.

Location

This hotel is located right on the seafront in the small resort village of Liapades (Corfu Island, Greece). Kerkyra International Airport is only 21 kilometers from the hotel, so on arrival you can reach Liapades Beach in just 30-40 minutes. This is very convenient if you travel with young children. In the vicinity of the hotel there are restaurants, shops, car rental and tour desk. Also nearby is a bus stop with links to various cities on the island.





Description and photo of the hotel

Liapades Beach Hotel Studios 3 * is a hotel built in the traditional Greek style. It is located in a green quiet place surrounded by olive groves and is ideal for a measured family vacation away from the noise of the big city and the bustle accompanying it.

The hotel is open from April to October. Here tourists are offered comfortable accommodation and a high level of service. The hotel has forty rooms of various categories (standard, family and studio), which are located in several buildings. Each room has a private bathroom with shower, TV, air conditioning, refrigerator, safe, telephone, terrace or balcony. Some apartments also have a kitchenette with a set of dishes. Rooms are cleaned daily; linen and towels are changed twice a week.

As for meals, when booking a room in Liapades Beach 3 * you can choose either breakfast or half board (includes breakfast + dinner). The hotel also has a tavern where you can have a meal during the day for an extra charge. Near the pool there is a bar with a large selection of drinks.

Just a hundred meters from the hotel there is a magnificent sand and pebble beach. It is in municipal ownership, so you will have to pay for the rental of sun loungers and parasols. On site you can have a good time by the pool.





Reviews of Russian tourists about Liapades Beach Hotel Studios 3 *

As you know, today more and more people who are in the process of planning a trip to a particular country, when choosing a hotel, are largely guided by the comments of travelers who have already visited it. Therefore, we decided to make your task a little easier and give generalized reviews of our compatriots who stayed during their holidays at the three-star Liapades Beach Hotel (Corfu, Greece).

Looking ahead a bit, we note that most tourists were quite satisfied with their choice. According to them, this hotel represents an optimal ratio of price and quality, and also more than corresponds to its category. But we learn more about everything.





Impressions of travelers about the hotel itself, about the territory and location

A big plus of this hotel, many tourists considered its location. So, she is close to the beach in a calm green place. Here is a very beautiful beach and clear sea. Near the hotel there are shops where you can buy everything you need. It takes only about forty minutes to get from the airport to the hotel, so the road will not be a burden. The territory of Liapades Beach Hotel Studios 3 * is quite large, green, shady and very well-groomed.

As for the rooms, according to our compatriots, they are spacious and equipped with everything necessary. Tourists note a fresh repair, as well as good condition of furniture and appliances. Some rooms have a kitchenette and kitchenware. All apartments have safes, which can be used free of charge. With hot water, according to the guests, there are no interruptions. Bathrooms are spacious enough, but showers seemed cramped to vacationers. However, this did not cause any problems.

Rooms are cleaned every day except Sunday. Towels are refreshed once every three days, and linen once a week.





Guest comments on meals

According to tourists, special culinary delights in this hotel should not be expected. However, the food here is always very tasty and satisfying. Breakfast is quite varied, several dishes are offered for dinner. By the way, for some vacationers, evening meals seemed a bit long. This is due to the fact that dishes are served in turn, and the whole meal as a result lasts about two hours.

You can eat outside the hotel. So, in the vicinity there are many taverns where they cook excellently. Experienced travelers also advise those who are first in Greece to keep in mind that the portions here are very large. Therefore, having ordered one serving of a dish or salad, you are likely to be able to eat together.

Guest opinions about staff

According to travelers, all the staff at Liapades Beach Hotel Studios are very friendly, friendly and helpful people. Also almost always the hotel is its owner, who will be happy to answer all questions and help in solving any problems. However, our compatriots pay attention to the fact that the staff here does not speak Russian. This is due to the fact that there are few tourists from our country at the hotel. So you have to explain in basic English or German.





Impressions of a beach holiday and entertainment

It should be noted that all tourists, without exception, were very pleased with their beach holidays. So, according to them, the beach closest to the hotel is sand and gravel. It is small, but there are not so many vacationers here, so there is enough space for everyone. For the rental of sunbeds and umbrellas you will need to pay (about eight euros per day). But you can sunbathe, sitting on your own mats or towels. The sea is very clear, turquoise. Swimming here is a pleasure. Due to the fact that there is a bay in the area, the sea is always calm. In the vicinity there are several wild beaches with picturesque grottoes.

As for entertainment at Liapades Beach Hotel, this hotel is designed for tourists who prefer a calm and measured rest. There are no noisy animations and night discos. The entire entertainment program is limited to swimming in the pool and visiting the Greek evenings with songs and dances once a week. In addition, experienced travelers are advised not to forget that the island of Corfu is rich in many interesting sights that you should definitely visit. This can be done both as part of the tour group, and independently, by renting a car.
