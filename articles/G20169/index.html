Sindbad Beach 4 - An Unforgettable Vacation

Description: Sindbad Beach 4 - a hotel of the European level. It is located 10 kilometers from the city of Hurghada. It is 5 kilometers from the international airport. The hotel is popular not only among Russian tourists, but also among the French, Italians, and Belgians.

Vacationers are waiting for a huge amount of entertainment. For those planning a vacation with their children, there is an excellent mini water park on site. In addition, the organization of boat trips, including a boat trip, a dive in a submarine, a walk to the coral islands.


Sindbad Beach 4 was opened in 1992. It is a complex of buildings, including several 2-story buildings and a number of 1-story bungalows. The territory has many green spaces.

Rooms: Sindbad Beach 4 offers 260 comfortable rooms, including 154 Standard Room, 16 Suite and 90 Superior Room. Each has a shower or bath, hairdryer. Free toiletries are provided during your stay. Air conditioning, cable TV (there are Russian channels), telephone. There is a minibar in the room, but it is filled at an additional cost.

There is a free safe, desk for work, TV. Service round the clock. Perfect cleanliness is maintained through daily cleaning. Bed linen is changed every two days.

Each room has a balcony or terrace.

Meals: Sindbad Beach 4 offers the following types of meals to its vacationers : Half Board, Club All Inclusive, All Inclusive. Half Board includes two meals a day plus drinks (for a fee). All Inclusive consists of three meals a day in the form of a buffet. Plus, snacks in the hotel bars throughout the day, all kinds of local drinks. Club All Inclusive includes three meals a day. As for dinner, you can visit the main restaurant of the hotel (buffet) or, upon reservation, dine √† la carte in any restaurant located on site.



The hotel has five restaurants, a poolside bar, a Vienna cafe, an Alladin cafe, a lobby bar, a disco bar and a hookah bar. There is a beer bar on the beach.

Beach: The hotel has its own sandy beach, which is 350 meters long. Admission is free for hotel guests. The entrance to the sea is even.

Additional information: On the territory of Sindbad Beach there is a small water park, a water sports center, a heated outdoor pool, and a laundry room (for a fee). There is free parking for cars, dry cleaning. You can rent a car to explore the surroundings and attractions. There is animation. Among paid sporting activities, surfing, bowling, diving school, volleyball, darts.

For those who are trying to be irresistible during their holidays , there is a hairdresser, solarium, jacuzzi, massage room, sauna, bathhouse.



Reviews: First of all, Sinbad Beach is a fairly comfortable hotel. This is evidenced by its popularity among European tourists. Secondly, it has a very convenient location: proximity to the sea, beautiful surrounding views, clean beach.

The service staff is very polite with the guests. Always ready to help and answer your questions. The territory is full of all kinds of entertainment.
