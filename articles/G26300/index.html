Malia Bay Beach Hotel & Bungalows 3 *: reviews, photos

Malia Bay Beach Hotel & Bungalows 3 * is a resort complex, which is a cozy bungalow located in the shade of a blooming green garden. Here you can enjoy all the delights of relaxation on the shores of the warm Aegean Sea. This beautiful area will give you the opportunity to plunge into the atmosphere of silence and tranquility, as well as relax from the bustle of the city and the frantic pace of life.

Short hotel description

The resort and hotel complex Malia Bay Beach Hotel & Bungalows 3 * is a combination of several separate cozy bungalows, whose residents have direct access to the sea coast. All apartments have furnished balconies to enjoy the scenery and fresh air. Speaking of the interior, it is worth saying that it is both calm and bright, made in the style of modern laconicism.

It is worth noting the well-organized resort infrastructure of the Malia Bay Beach Hotel & Bungalows 3 * hotel. Here there is a swimming pool, sun loungers with protective awnings, and children's playgrounds, and much more. It is worth noting that the guests of this hotel can get all the necessary services without leaving its limits.

One of the most famous features of Greece is its unusual cuisine. You can get acquainted with all culinary traditions in the restaurant of the Malia Bay Beach Hotel & Bungalows 3 * resort hotel. A buffet is served here for breakfast, lunch and dinner, and the rest of the time you have the opportunity to place orders on the menu and enjoy them on the outdoor terrace overlooking the azure sea.





Where is the hotel located

Malia Bay 3 * Resort is located in the northern part of Crete on the outskirts of Malia, which is becoming more and more popular with tourists every year. The location of this hotel compares favorably with the fact that the famous Minoan palace and other masterpieces of ancient architecture are located nearby. If you want to get to know Crete better, you can visit the capital Heraklion (35 km), Hersonissos (7 km), as well as the most famous resort of Agios Nikolaos (25 km). You will also have the opportunity to book a transfer to the airport.





Number of rooms of the hotel Malia Bay 3 *

Comfortable apartments - this is one of the most important moments by which you can make an impression of a hotel. Staying at Malia Bay 3 * (Greece), you can choose one of the following options:

  • Small double rooms with a large bed are often chosen by newlyweds and romantic couples for themselves. There is not enough living space. Nevertheless, this drawback is fully compensated by the gorgeous views from the window that open up to you sea or mountain landscapes, as well as the sprawling greenery of the garden.
  • Triple apartments have a double and one and a half beds, as well as a small soft sofa, which acts as an extra bed. Usually this type of accommodation is chosen by small families or groups of friends.
  • Family apartments have two rooms and are designed to accommodate four people. One bedroom has a double bed. There is also a pair of soft chairs. Another bedroom can be equipped with a bunk or a pair of one and a half beds for children.

The stylish design of the Malia Bay Beach Hotel & Bungalows 3 * apartments deserves special attention. Photos taken by travelers demonstrate a restrained minimalist style, complemented by original decorative elements.





Apartment Amenities

In all rooms of the Malia Bay Beach Hotel & Bungalows 3 * hotel (Greece), for your comfort, the following amenities are provided:

  • ceiling fan, which is designed to save the residents of the room from the summer heat;
  • individual air conditioning is provided in the apartments, but using it becomes possible only if an additional payment is made;
  • you will have access to your own balcony or terrace, which provides comfortable garden furniture, as well as accessories for drying clothes;
  • You can use a landline telephone both for communication with the administration and for making calls abroad;
  • subject to an additional payment, you can use a small refrigerator;
  • depending on the category of apartments, your bathroom will be equipped with a large bathtub or a modern shower;
  • so that you can quickly dry your hair or do styling, a hairdryer is provided;
  • there is a large plasma TV that broadcasts many satellite channels (most of them are musical);
  • for an additional fee, you will be given a key to the safe in which you can store your valuables;
  • bath accessories, as well as individual cosmetic sets.




Infrastructure resort Malia Bay Beach Hotel & Bungalows 3 * (Crete)

In addition to comfort and cleanliness in the rooms, the thoughtful resort infrastructure of the hotel is also of great importance. Great attention is paid to this issue in Malia Bay 3 *. Reviews of tourists note the presence of the following infrastructure facilities in the resort complex:

  • a large garden with tropical plants that surrounds all the buildings and bungalows belonging to this hotel;
  • There are two open terraces, one of which is intended for sunbathing, and the second is equipped with comfortable chairs and tables;

  • The resort has several shops where you can buy souvenirs, food and drinks, as well as basic necessities.

Opportunities for wellness and outdoor activities

If you are used to lead an active lifestyle, and carefully monitor your health, then you are advised to use the following services of the Malia Bay Beach Hotel & Bungalows 3 * ai:

  • outdoor swimming pool, which also has a heating function in case of cold weather;

Additional services

Additionally, at the Malia Bay Beach Hotel & Bungalows 3 * hotel (Greece, Crete) you can count on receiving the following services:

  • It is possible to order a transfer from or to the airport on the day of departure (to be paid separately);
  • the hotel has its own service for renting cars, mopeds or bicycles, which gives you the opportunity to choose the right vehicle for yourself;
  • travel agency staff will organize excursions for you according to pre-designed or individual programs;
  • every evening, animators organize incendiary entertaining shows for hotel guests with concert numbers and competitions;
  • the opportunity to come to rest with your pet (however, there are some restrictions on the size and weight of the animal);
  • there is a safe at the reception where you can leave your valuables for the duration of your absence at the hotel.

Resort Policies

Going on vacation to the resort hotel Malia Bay Beach Hotel & Bungalows 3 * (Greece), be sure to check out some of the rules that govern accommodation in it:

  • all operations related to the registration of guests, as well as their eviction, are carried out accordingly strictly after 14:00 and before noon;
  • Children under 12 years old are provided with special cots or folding beds;
  • the cost of an extra bed for adult guests is 30% of the cost of single occupancy;
  • it is possible to use bank plastic cards to pay for the services provided;
  • Each newly arrived guest is charged a certain amount of money as a security deposit, which you can pick up upon eviction if you have not caused material damage to the hotel.

It is recommended that you familiarize yourself with these rules at the planning stage of your trip, so that during check-in, as well as during the rest, there are no misunderstandings or additional questions.





Positive hotel reviews

Many guests return again and again to the Malia Bay Beach Hotel & Bungalows 3 * resort (Crete). Reviews of tourists indicate that the rest in this hotel is accompanied by a lot of positive aspects, namely:

  • the territory is very clean and well-groomed, decorated with a huge number of plants and flowers, as well as original decorative elements;
  • the pool is large and clean with a special section for bathing children;
  • a lot of sunbeds and umbrellas, so there are always free places near the pool and on the beach, even if the hotel is full;
  • quite a lot of living space in the apartment;
  • if you open the window, the automatic shutdown system is activated in the air conditioner;
  • good snow-white new towels;
  • very polite and helpful staff who try their best to help guests in solving a wide variety of problems;
  • cleaning is very clean even without a tip (and if children live in the room, the maids leave sweets for them);
  • very close by is a mini market where you can buy products, hygiene items and other necessary goods at fairly reasonable prices;
  • for lunch, as well as for dinner, you can have unlimited quantities of ice cream of various types;
  • very good breakfast menu;
  • The restaurant provides comfortable high chairs for children.




Negative hotel reviews

It is worth noting that, in addition to the positive aspects, there are also a number of significant shortcomings in the operation of the Malia Bay Beach Hotel & Bungalows 3 *. Traveler reviews are accompanied by the following comments:

  • the territory of the hotel is incredibly small, and therefore the noise from all the events that take place on the stage in the evening is heard in all rooms;
  • a road passes through the hotel territory (despite the fact that traffic on it is not very intense, there is some discomfort);
  • the beds are small enough, because of which it is not very comfortable for people with large stature to sleep on them;
  • too little light in the rooms, which is why it is not just gloomy, but also rather damp;
  • due to the fact that there are no mosquito nets on the windows, a huge number of mosquitoes get into the rooms, which neither fumigators, nor sprays, nor other insect repellents take;
  • from cosmetics and hygiene items, only soap is put in the bathroom;
  • there are no beach towels, and therefore you have to take baths to the sea or bring with you from home;
  • the Internet is paid and quite expensive (some guests bought one card for two and used it together without informing the reception);
  • not very good quality fruit at the buffet; lack of a special menu for children;
  • when you consider that the hotel has a huge number of families with children, the playground is incredibly small and does not differ in a large number of attractions;
  • the hotel is located far enough from the central part of the resort town, and therefore there are practically no entertainment or walking spots near it;
  • for sunbeds and umbrellas that are on the beach, you have to pay extra, which is very strange for a hotel that has its own coastal zone;
  • disgusting quality not only in alcoholic beverages, but also in juices and refreshing cocktails (bartenders dilute everything with water);
  • not only is it impossible to find a quiet place throughout the hotel, but the walls of the rooms do not provide any sound insulation.

conclusions

Traveler reviews about this institution are mostly positive, but they can not do without comments. Perhaps the most enjoyable moment is the unlimited ice cream, which is provided to guests after lunch and after dinner.

The hotel area is very small and incredibly beautiful. But there are many shortcomings in it, one of which is the roadway, which runs between the buildings. It is also worth noting that in the whole hotel there is practically no quiet corner. There is a pool, there is a scene where animators work, there are office rooms. And all this constantly makes noise.

The rooms are quite spacious, but at the same time they are noticeably lacking in light. Also a huge problem are local mosquitoes, which are absolutely resistant to any insect repellent. It is also worth paying attention to the fact that the furniture and appliances in the rooms are very outdated and decently worn out, and therefore require replacement. Although all these shortcomings are fully compensated by the low cost that is charged for accommodation.
