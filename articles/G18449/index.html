Cancun hotels: Best Caribbean Coast Vacations

Cancun Resort is one of the top ten in the world . It is located on the Yucatan Peninsula. Growing up from a fishing village, Cancun is today an international resort. The local climate is almost perfect: the average annual temperature is 30-32 degrees Celsius.

The Caribbean coast - these are great opportunities for relaxing beach and educational. Cancun hotels stretching along the coast offer the most comfortable conditions and all the services for a great vacation at the highest level. The resort infrastructure is very developed. Local entertainment is added to the standard entertainment - corrida. Here you can find a huge number of bars, restaurants, discos and entertainment centers.

Rest in Cancun is not possible without visiting local attractions. A two-hour drive from Cancun is Chichen Itza, the capital of the Mayan empire , which has become history today. Reverence and admiration encompasses when you personally meet with the Kukulkan pyramid.

Mysterious pyramids, the ruins of a vanished civilization, exotic nature, national sombreros are particles of Mexico, memories of which will remain with you in vivid photos.

Cancun hotel reviews are usually only flattering. The most popular hotels in Cancun are a real fairy tale, regardless of their "star".

Fiesta Americana Grand Coral Beach (5 stars )

This is a modern, chic hotel, considered central in Cancun. Everything shines here like in a fairy tale. The hotel staff is first-class, qualified and polite. The suites are surprisingly large and comfortable. However, the standard rooms are equipped with all modern amenities. Some rooms have sea views. Here you will be provided with a full range of hotel services and will provide all the conditions for an upscale vacation.

In the hotel you will find an excellent spa center, the latest equipped gym and tennis court, a beautiful pool and a magnificent amazingly clean and beautiful beach.

The hotel stretches along the promenade, which is very picturesque and romantic. All hotels in Cancun are very well and harmoniously fit into the surrounding nature.

Guests can enjoy local and French dishes at the local restaurant. Breakfast in the restaurant is free of charge on the basis of the "buffet".

Radisson Hotel Hacienda Cancun (5 stars )

  The hotel is located in a residential area in the city center, very close to local attractions. In addition, shopping centers, Tulum Avenue, and the financial district are located next to the hotel.

The hotel has all the amenities that modern upscale hotels have, including wireless Internet and car rental. The hotel has a chic swimming pool and a fitness center.

Guests enjoy a free shuttle service to the beach, which the hotel provides. The beaches here are magnificent in beauty and well-groomed. They provide the opportunity not only to take sun baths, but also provide recreational activities.

The Ritz-Carlton, Cancun (5 stars )

This is perhaps the best option that all Cancun hotels can offer. It is located away from the routes of mass movement of tourists and city noise.

Here you will enjoy impeccable service, magnificent, simply chic, rooms with all amenities. Here you will find air conditioning and even ironing boards, as well as all the equipment that vacationers may need.

The hotel is surrounded by magnificent landscapes that make the rest especially enjoyable. The beach of the hotel is almost a local attraction due to the level of grooming and the amazing beauty of its design.

The hotel has a restaurant and bar. A fitness center is located on site. Additional services include car rental, child care services.

Hilton Cancun Golf & Spa Resort (3 stars )

The hotel is located in the resort area by the sea. The design of the hotel is Mexican; in architecture, elements inherited from the Mayan culture are visible . This is a family-friendly hotel where there will be no noisy parties.

You will be provided with good service and comfortable rooms. The rooms here are standard and deluxe, equipped with every comfort. The hotel has a swimming pool. A large number of restaurants are available at reasonable prices.

From the hotel you can easily reach the main attractions of the city.

In the morning dining room you can enjoy a breakfast buffet. Day and evening you can visit the restaurant with first-class cuisine.

Holiday Inn Express Cancun Zona Hotelera (3 stars )

This hotel is located in the center of Cancun Recreation Area.

It features a fitness room with a lot of cardiovascular equipment, an outdoor pool, free breakfasts, and wireless Internet.

All hotels in Cancun have a very high level of service and practically do not cause complaints from tourists.
