Hotels of Melitopol

The city of Melitopol has several hotels. They are distinguished from each other by the number of rooms, their arrangement and technical equipment, the convenience of location relative to transport highways and the city center. Melitopol hotels offer their guests a different set of services, the living conditions in them differ in the level of comfort. Prices that are set in hotels vary widely and range from two hundred to one thousand three hundred rubles per person per day.

Some hotel establishments have their own websites on the Internet, which allows potential guests to visually see and compare the conditions offered to them. Melitopol hotels - the conditions in them, comfort and service - guests like.

The hotel complex "Tower" in Melitopol

In the city center is a fairly new hotel complex "Tower". It is decorated in a medieval style. Guests will be offered comfort, coziness and various entertainments.



Hotel Tower is small, consists of only four rooms, presented in the luxury and junior suites, they can accommodate two people. The arrangement and design of the rooms comply with European standards. Each room has comfortable furniture and there is the possibility of organizing an extra bed. Each is equipped with air conditioning, a refrigerator, a TV, free Wi-Fi, a minibar and a safe. The bathroom with shower or bath has a year-round independent hot water supply, guests will be offered a hairdryer.

Guests can relax after a trip or a business meeting in the sauna. Visiting the fireplace room and bar, playing billiards will allow you to spend your leisure time. The chefs of the cafe will offer you to taste home cooking, dishes from the menu can be ordered in the room.

The presence of a guarded parking lot, a conference room with equipment necessary for events of various kinds, a banquet room significantly increases the level of comfort.





Hotel "South"

At the exit of the city in a southerly direction is a motel of the same name. Guests are offered fourteen suites, junior suites and economy. They can double and triple occupancy.

The suites are equipped in accordance with standards, there is an extra bed. Junior suites have two single beds, an extra bed is not provided. Economy rooms do not have their own bathroom, amenities are located on the floor.





Hotel "South" has its own cafe-bar, it works around the clock. Guests can relax and unwind in the local sauna. For the convenience of guests with their own vehicles, the hotel offers secure parking.
