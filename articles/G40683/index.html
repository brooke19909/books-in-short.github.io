Roh. What is an opportunity to relax in comfort or to be deceived?

When choosing a hotel, tourists usually know in advance in which room they will live, based on their personal preferences and financial capabilities. But to date, the hotel industry has introduced a new method of accommodation for customers called Run of House, which can be translated as "escape from home." Let's figure it out. ROH: what is it - the opportunity to relax in comfortable conditions for a low fee or just a publicity stunt?



Choosing a room to stay should always be responsible. When choosing Room ROH, the client must be guaranteed accommodation in a standard room or in the best apartments of the hotel, which are not reserved and not occupied at the time of registration. Typically, this type of booking for the tourist has more pros than cons. Firstly, for a low fee, he can relax in more comfortable conditions than he could afford. Secondly, when booking a large number of rooms it is not necessary to specify the features of each room. You can simply book, for example, 100 seats, and upon arrival the groups at the reception will be given the keys to the best available ROH rooms. What kind of rooms they will be is unknown, but in any case you will win, because this type of booking is the most cost-effective. Thirdly, you do not lose anything. If your goal is to book the cheapest option at a hotel, then it is better to choose a ROH number.



Of course, this method of booking a room has its own pitfalls. Firstly, when choosing this type of accommodation, the hotel guests will not know in advance in which room they will be placed. This will be known only upon arrival at the hotel. In such cases, you need to prepare for the worst. If you are satisfied with the simplest option in a hotel, then feel free to agree to this offer. Customer dissatisfaction will not be taken into account, since the living conditions should have been known to the guest in advance. Secondly, some unscrupulous hotels, even with the availability of the best category, can still accommodate a guest in a standard room. In such cases, you need to assert your rights - you can complain to the administration, throw a scandal. And in some countries, mainly Asian, you can even give a small bribe. Whether this is a great option or a false marketing move depends on the hotel that hosts the guests. In order not to fall for the bait of such negligent enterprises and relax in good conditions, it is necessary to adopt a few tips.

  • Only select trusted hotels, look for reviews on online forums. The hotel must have a good reputation. Fortunately, today anyone can speak on the pages of the World Wide Web about this or that organization.
  • Always find out the conditions of accommodation in the cheapest room.
  • If you want a hotel room of a class up, avoid peak tourist seasons.
  • You cannot choose the level of comfort of the room, but be sure to indicate the type of accommodation:

- ROH single means providing the best room available to accommodate one person.

- ROH double - a room with one large bed for two people.

- ROH twin - a room with two separate single beds. That is, the guest should be offered the best option in the Twin category.

Choose modern hotel booking options, such as ROH. What is it? This is a great vacation with the possibility of obtaining a high comfort room for a low fee. Subject to choosing a good hotel and, of course, the season. Have a nice rest!
