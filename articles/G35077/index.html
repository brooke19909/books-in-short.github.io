Safari park, Lipetsk region. Lipetsk Safari Park

Have you ever been to a real safari park? We are sure that most of our readers will answer negatively. In this case, we invite you to make a trip to Zadonsk (Lipetsk region). Safari Park, whose area is five hundred hectares, opened here in 2007.



Kudykina Gora

This is the name of one of the most visited and popular attractions not only in the Zadonsky district, but throughout Russia. The Kudykina Gora complex is an open-air Lipetsk Safari Park, where sheep and horses graze quite freely, ostriches and guinea fowls, buffalos and musk ox live. Visitors to the park enjoy watching deer and yaks, kangaroos and camels.

Safari Park (Lipetsk Region) is an amazing place for families. On its territory, you can even spend all day outdoors with young children, look at a wide variety of animals, and relax from the noise of the city.

The reserve is located on the very banks of the Don River and meets its guests throughout the year.

Safari Park in the summer

Undoubtedly, this time is most suitable for visiting this amazing place. Together with the children you will find yourself in the fabulous City of Masters, you can ride in an old carriage in a farm where animals are kept. Surprisingly, donkeys, horses, and poultry familiar to all of us coexist very well next to llamas and kangaroos, yaks and camels, ostriches and red deers.



A playground, horse riding, trampolines and a bright circus performance are organized for young visitors to the park. This amazing place is a safari park loved by children and adults. Are you interested in the ticket price? We hasten to please you. Entrance to the territory is free. Only voluntary donations for the development of the park are accepted. This project was created as a charity and at the beginning of its activities was completely free. But now there are some additional paid services - excursions, trampolines, carriage rides.

Holidays in Safari Park

The creators of this unique natural complex build their work in such a way that their guests have the opportunity in the park to hold corporate holidays, children's birthdays. If you have a desire to visit Safari Park (Lipetsk region) with a small group of friends, you need to make an appointment with the administrators in advance.

Picnics

The park is in constant development. New interesting objects are being built. Last year, several food outlets appeared in the park, and the construction of the ship-cafe was completed. Here you will be offered vegetable salads, fresh rolls, sausages or meatballs with rice garnish, pancakes, dumplings and other dishes that children enjoy eating.



Previously, many visitors had picnics here at the pond or in any other quiet place. Today there was a paid porch with barbecue, right on the banks of the Don. However, we consider it necessary to recall - do not forget to bring garbage bags with you, so that after your holiday to restore complete order. Of course, visiting a cafe or renting a veranda is not necessary at all, because in the park area free tables are plentiful.

Tips for guests

The safari park is open for four days a week, except Mondays, Tuesdays and Thursdays, from ten to twenty hours. On its territory it is strictly forbidden to smoke, drink alcohol (including beer).
