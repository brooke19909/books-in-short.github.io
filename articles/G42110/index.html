Laser epilator Rio Salon Laser: reviews, description, specifications

Today, various devices and techniques are used to combat vegetation on the body. Laser hair removal is very popular. This is most often a salon procedure. However, it can be done at home. For this, the Rio Salon Laser epilator is used. Reviews about the model and its main qualities will be discussed later. What is laser hair removal?

We learned about laser radiation relatively recently - in the last century. Laser is a device that creates a narrow beam of intense high-power light. In a short time, such devices have become very widespread in many areas of human activity.

Initially, laser hair removal was performed only in beauty salons. Everything has changed since the moment when the unique portable Rio Salon Laser epilator appeared on the world market, with which you can carry out hair removal procedures without leaving your home.

Manufacturing company

Home laser epilator Rio Salon Laser was developed by the famous company RIO DEZAC (UK).

The manufacturer began its activities about thirty years ago. Today, it is widely popular in the world market due to its innovative products and respect for the environment.

The principle of operation of the device

Laser epilator "Rio" is a small device. It emits a narrow and directional stream of light. Melanin (pigment), which is contained in the hair, absorbs the energy of such radiation. This causes the heating of its follicle (root). As a result of such exposure, they are destroyed. As a result, the procedure helps to slow down or completely stop hair growth.

Such hair removal is used only to remove dark hairs on the skin of a light shade. They have enough pigment. For light, gray, red and fluffy hair, this procedure is absolutely ineffective, since the energy from the laser is not concentrated in the follicle. In this case, the Rio Salon Laser will not produce the expected effect. Customer reviews whose hair does not have enough melanin are negative.

If you have a strong tan or having a dark skin tone from nature, you should also not hope for a high result. It should be remembered that laser hair removal is a procedure that is carried out in courses consisting of 6-10 sessions. They need to be carried out once a month. A single application will not give a result. Therefore, before using an epilator of this type, it is necessary to carefully study the instructions for the maximum result.

The device acts on each hair individually, destroying the rod from the very base. This allows you to achieve a lasting positive effect. The device has five power levels, which makes it possible to get the best result in accordance with the existing features of the skin type.

Unlike waxing and shugaring, laser is pleasant and painless. True, this process is slower and more laborious. However, a laser epilator with a scanning function speeds up this process by several times. The device can find and process up to 60 hairs simultaneously.
