Vincci Resort Taj Sultan (Hammamet, Tunisia). Photos and reviews of tourists

Tunisia, along with Egypt, has for many years been one of the most popular African countries among tourists from Russia and the CIS. This is because the holiday season lasts all year round, and these states are not inferior to, for example, Turkey with magnificent beaches, the presence of a mass of attractions and a wide hotel base. As for Tunisia, one of the most popular regions of this country is Hammamet. It is considered a European-class resort and is famous for its luxurious hotels and magnificent beaches. If it is here that you decide to spend your vacation, then as a place to stay we suggest you consider the five-star Vincci Resort Taj Sultan. Today we will tell you in detail about what is offered to guests here, and also find out what impressions our compatriots have left from this hotel.





Location

The five-star Vinci Resort Taj Sultan Hotel is conveniently located ten kilometers from the center of Hammamet, on the very shore of the sea. This resort has long gained the fame of one of the most respectable in Tunisia. Due to the fact that the hotel is far from the city center, guests have the opportunity to relax in peace and quiet. However, if you have a desire to have a noisy and fun time, the resort is replete with bars, discos and nightclubs. You just have to choose an institution to your taste. In addition, many tourists come to Hammamet not only for a beach holiday, but also for anti-aging and wellness treatments, which this resort is famous for. As for airports, as a rule, the planes of travelers staying at the Vincci Resort Taj Sultan 5 * land either in the air harbor of Monastir (105 km from Hammamet) or in the capital of Tunisia (68 km from Hammamet). Thus, the path to the hotel will take you from one to two hours. 48 kilometers from Hammamet there is another airport located in Enfid. However, it is not as popular as the aforementioned air harbors. If you buy a tour through a travel agency, then you will most likely be provided with a transfer upon arrival. Otherwise, you can get to Hammamet by train or intercity bus. In addition, the Taj Sultan Hotel arranges transfers for its guests at an additional cost.





Vincci Resort Taj Sultan 5 *: photo and description

This five-star hotel complex was erected in 2003. It belongs to a hotel chain called Vincci. Here, Arabian luxury blends harmoniously with French charm and elegance. Arriving here, you will plunge into the atmosphere of beauty, glamor, leisurely and impeccable service. The hotel includes a large three-story building and two adjacent blocks. In total there are 252 comfortable rooms. By the way, most rooms have sea views. As for the private territory of the Taj Sultan Hotel, its area is 4 thousand square meters. Most of it is occupied by picturesque gardens. The hotel complex has restaurants, bars, sports fields and equipment, a rich entertainment program, a beauty salon, a luxurious beach and much more. Here you can have a great time both tourists traveling with children, as well as young people, as well as older people.

Posting Rules

The five-star hotel complex Vincci Resort Taj Sultan (Tunisia), like most other hotels, has a set time for arrival and departure of guests. So, the settlement of new guests is carried out in the afternoon. If you arrive at the hotel earlier than the specified time, then if you have available rooms of the category you have booked, you will be settled immediately. If the previous guests have not vacated the rooms, you will have to wait a bit. However, there is no need to be in the lobby at the reception, as you can leave your things in the storage room and swim in the pool or the sea, have a snack, take a walk around the territory, etc. On the day of departure, leave your room until 10 a.m. At the same time, you will be asked to hand over the key to the room and pay for the time you live and use additional services. You can pay here as cash, and cards "Visa", "American Express" or "Mastercard". If you have already fully paid for your accommodation to the tour operator, then upon leaving you will only have to pay for additional services.





Accommodation for tourists traveling with children

Vincci Resort Taj Sultan 5 * (Hammamet, Tunisia) allows guests with children of any age. Moreover, if in the occupied room you place your baby up to two years old on the additionally provided arena, then you will not have to pay extra for this. If your room will accommodate one or two children from 2 to 12 years old on extra beds, then for each of them you will need to pay 30 euros per day. Please note that no more than two children or adults can be accommodated in each room. If you need such a service, be sure to notify the administration of Vinci Resort in advance and wait for confirmation from it.

Accommodation for tourists traveling with pets

This item will be very important for those people who are used to going on foreign holidays in the company of their four-legged pets. So, according to the rules of the Vincci Resort Taj Sultan, pets are not allowed on site. Therefore, you will either have to choose another hotel from the pet-friendly category for accommodation, or part with your pet for the holidays.





Number of rooms

This hotel consists of a three-story building and two additional blocks adjacent to it. In total there are 252 rooms in the following categories: standard double, triple and quadruple, family, as well as junior suites and suites. The standard rooms are equipped with air conditioning, television (satellite TV), telephone, separate bathroom and toilet, minibar, as well as a balcony or terrace. For a fee, a safe and Wi-Fi access are available. Most rooms have sea views. Family rooms of the Vincci Resort Taj Sultan 5 * hotel differ from the standard ones in a larger area, zoned space (sleeping and living areas), the availability of extra beds and bathrobes in the bathroom. Junior suites, in addition to everything available in standard rooms, have a larger area, separate bedroom and two bathrooms. Suite Suites differ from Junior in the presence of two separate bedrooms. Regardless of the category of the occupied room, all guests of the Taj Sultan hotel complex can order a room service. Rooms are cleaned daily, and towels and bed linen are regularly changed.

Sea and beach

The five-star Vincci Resort Taj Sultan is located directly on the seafront and has its own wonderful sandy beach. It has everything you need for an excellent holiday: deck chairs, gazebos, parasols, equipment for sports and entertainment on the water. In addition, there is a snack bar "Le Simbad" on the beach, where you can not only enjoy refreshments or alcoholic drinks, but also a snack.

Entertainment

Vincci Resort Taj Sultan has three pools for adults (one indoor and two outdoor), as well as a pool for the smallest. Near them there are sun terraces with sun loungers and parasols. In addition, there is a bar with a wide selection of drinks for every taste. Fans of water entertainment can ride on a water slide.

Fans of outdoor activities are invited to play beach volleyball, tennis or table tennis, squash, soccer or billiards, shoot archery, visit the gym and aerobics classes. The hotel has daytime and evening entertainment, daily discos and a game room.

For those who want to relax, there is a spa and beauty salon where you can visit the sauna, jacuzzi, conduct a number of skin and body treatments, as well as make a hairstyle, manicure and pedicure.

For the youngest guests of the Vincci Resort Taj Sultan 5 * hotel (Hammamet, Tunisia) a playground, a children's pool, and a mini club are also equipped.





Cost of living

As for the prices for accommodation in this hotel, they are fully consistent with its level of stardom. Also note that early bookings can save you a lot. So, with regard to cost, a seven-day stay in August will cost you from 56 thousand rubles for a standard double room with sea view, from 64 thousand rubles for a honeymoon suite, from 70 thousand rubles for a standard triple room and from 85 thousand rubles for a family number.
