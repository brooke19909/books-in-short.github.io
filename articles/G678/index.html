Apollo Beach Hotel 4 * (Greece, Rhodes): reviews, description, photo

An unforgettable Greek vacation awaits guests of the resort complex Apollo Beach Hotel 4 *. High-quality service, comfortable rooms and beautiful nature create a heavenly atmosphere.





Brief description of the hotel

An unforgettable Greek vacation will give travelers Apollo Beach Hotel 4 *. O. Rhodes, Faliraki is a fairly popular tourist destination. This institution was opened back in 1975, and therefore, certain service traditions have already formed here. Thanks to major repairs carried out in 2005, the hotel took on a modern look. On the territory of two five-story buildings and numerous infrastructure facilities for recreation and entertainment.

If you like to go on excursions and get acquainted with new sights, pay attention to the Apollo Beach Hotel 4 *. Faliraki has a large number of tourist sites. Resting here, be sure to visit such places:

  • Anthony Quin's Sea Bay - 3 km;
  • water park - 3 km;
  • Kalythei thermal springs - 5 km;
  • Acropolis of Ialysos - 8 km;
  • Filerimos - 8 km;
  • Rodini National Park - 10 km;

  • Knights Avenue - 12 km;

  • Mandraki seaport - 12 km;
  • deer sculpture complex - 12 km.

Hotel location

Only 400 meters from the main street of the resort is the Apollo Beach Hotel 4 *. O. Rhodes, Faliraki, Kallithea, 85105 - the address where travelers can find this hotel. 5 km from the hotel there is a modern golf course. If you want to walk around Rhodes, you will have to overcome about 15 km. The international airport is 17 km away.

Number of rooms

Living conditions in the Apollo Beach Hotel 4 * hotel differ in cosiness and comfort. In this institution, guests can stay in rooms of the following categories:

  • Double standard room allows guests to stay on two single beds, which can be moved or installed separately. There is also a desk and a seating area with soft chairs. The room has a large full-length mirror. The spacious, furnished balcony offers luxurious sea views.
  • Single standard room is quite compact in size. The guest can sit on one and a half orthopedic beds. Near the window there is an upholstered lounge chair with a coffee table and a floor lamp for reading. Features a private furnished balcony.

Negative reviews

Rest in such a resort institution as Apollo Beach Hotel 4 * (Rhodes) is not without drawbacks . Reviews note such negative points:

  • on the municipal beach, which is located next to the hotel, a lot of garbage (bottles, butts and other things);
  • food is quite monotonous, and the menu is repeated every day;
  • Considering that 90% of vacationers are European pensioners, it can be boring in the territory, because the entire entertainment program is tailored specifically for them;
  • the beds are rather narrow and short, and therefore large and tall people will be uncomfortable to sleep on them;
  • next to the hotel there are several nightclubs in which music is constantly playing loudly;
  • rather old furnishings in the rooms;
  • the use of wireless Internet is paid (about 5 euros for 2 hours);
  • poor sound insulation in the rooms;
  • on the municipal beach for the use of sunbeds and umbrellas you need to pay extra;
  • animation is practically absent;
  • water in the pool smells strongly of bleach;
  • the air conditioner works very weakly and does not save at all from the heat;
  • it is useless to handle any problems at the reception (they will listen to you, but no action will follow);
  • if guests urgently need medicines, then they are not given a first-aid kit, but it is recommended that they go to the pharmacy;
  • for unknown reasons, employees are reluctant to call an ambulance if one of the guests is ill;
  • Only those who have previously submitted an application can take part in the Greek theme evening (no one tells guests about this need only);
  • maids do not take tips left on the bed (if you want better cleaning, you need to personally give them money);
  • guests cannot extend their stay on the day of departure, even at an additional cost;
  • the restaurant serves stale vegetables that have long lost their presentation.

Fans of a beautiful and colorful holiday should pay attention to the Apollo Beach Hotel 4 *. O. Rhodes is a unique resort where you will enjoy the beautiful nature and lots of attractions. It is worth noting the location of the hotel in the park area, which provides beauty and comfort. Nevertheless, the outdated atmosphere and noise from neighboring entertainment venues can somewhat spoil the overall picture.
