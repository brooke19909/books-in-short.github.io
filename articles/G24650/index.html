How to make a lace napkin

Weaving a napkin from beads is not a very difficult, but time-consuming process. In order for the final result to please the skilled worker and enthuse others, it is necessary to observe several simple, but very necessary rules:

  • The color scheme should be carefully thought out. The right combination of colors makes the thing more attractive. Also, do not use only one or two colors. Excess color is also undesirable, as the pattern scheme is simply lost.
  • The material must be of high quality. Quality beads not only shine brighter, it is also the same size. Beads of different shapes can deform a thing, ruining the final picture.
  • The fishing line should be strong and flexible enough. In the case of low-quality fishing line, the napkin made of beads will change over time. If the thread is fragile, then it may break in the middle of the work. You can also use threads for knitting.
  • A special bead needle simplifies work. Thanks to this improvised tool, things will go faster, and it will be much more convenient for you.




Variety of schemes

Anyone can weave bead napkins. Patterns of napkins made of beads are clear even to a beginner. There are a great many of them. For beginners, the Valentine card scheme is perfect. To create a lace napkin, red and beige (optional white) beads are taken. Any openwork bead napkin is woven from the middle. For this product, you need to crochet a napkin with a diameter of 8.5 cm. After this, the edge of the base is divided into 27 identical segments. Borders are marked with pins or auxiliary threads. At these points it is necessary to fix the bead net. A certain number of beads is collected on each thread, after which neighboring threads are connected. According to this scheme, the remaining beads are strung. The last heart (optimally make nine hearts) is fastened by threading the thread through the first bead at the beginning of the net.
