How to make a box of wood

You can make the box yourself or use an old product as a workpiece, which must first be cleaned of varnish and paint, sanded and polished. How to make a box yourself:

  1. Take an ordinary rail, the width of which will be the height of the walls of the product (you can use the casing of a suitable size).
  2. Cut the slats into the parts that the box will consist of. If its length is 18 cm, then there are two such parts. Still need two side parts (10 cm long). How to make a jewelry box?

The box can be equipped with a decorative lock and a mirror that can be glued to the lid. You can cut thin partitions that divide the bottom of the box into compartments. About how to make a box for storing rings and earrings, read below:

  1. Twist some velvet rollers.
  2. Lay them on the bottom of the box so that they fit snugly against each other. They can be fixed with a stapler and glued to the base of the box.
  3. Now you can store any jewelry. They will be fixed, and you can easily find them. Jewelry will not be scratched, chipped together.
