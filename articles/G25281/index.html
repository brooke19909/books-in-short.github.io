Opening of the year in the Tula region: the first ice palace in Tula

Already more than one generation of Tulaks annually looks forward to the onset of winter and the opening of city ice rinks in order to go ice skating or play hockey with friends. Traditional winter sports remained seasonal for residents of the region. But now the situation has changed, because in 2015, on the Day of the city, its residents received such a gorgeous gift - a new ice palace was opened in Tula.

Winter sports are available year-round!



On September 10, 2015, in Tula, on the territory of Rogozhinsky Park, the grand opening of the first indoor ice arena in the city took place. The ceremony was attended by the governor of the Tula region V. Gruzdev, famous hockey players, as well as senior officials and representatives of the main commercial investor of the project - the Tropic company. The ice palace in Tula began to be built in 2014. Such a quick implementation of the project is the result of cooperation between the state and private partners. The total project cost is estimated at 200 million rubles. The ice palace in Tula was opened by all the rules, with a symbolic throwing of the puck and the holding of the first friendly match.

The working hours of the sports complex and information for visitors



Any tula can test the artificial ice of the first indoor arena in the city. The ice palace in Tula is recruiting into the figure-skating training groups and the hockey section for children of different ages. Free skating sessions are also available, which can be accessed without an appointment. The complex has a skate rental service, all sizes are available. There is also a gym and a sports cafe. Where is the ice palace in Tula? The address of the sports complex: Demyanova street, 26B. Today this object is unique and unique in the city. In the entire Tula region, previously there was only one ice arena in the regional center - the city of Novomoskovsk, now the Tula people can skate, watch skaters and hockey matches year-round.
