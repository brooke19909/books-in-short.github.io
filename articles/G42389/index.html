How many countries in Europe are dwarf states?

The number of countries in Europe is 65, of which 9 are dependent territories, 6 are unrecognized republics. There are also several so-called dwarf states on European territory. Having independence, they sometimes have no hospitals, no educational institutions, or other infrastructure, but they are developing steadily, mainly due to tourism. How many countries in Europe have this status? At least six: Andorra, Vatican, Liechtenstein, Monaco, Malta and San Marino. Sometimes Luxembourg is added to this list.
