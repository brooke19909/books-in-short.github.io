Hotel My Hotel Garden Beach 3 * Tunisia, Monastir: reviews

Thanks to its pleasant climate, smooth beaches, warm Mediterranean Sea, rich recreational resources and traditions, Tunisia spa is becoming one of the leaders in the tourist market of the Mediterranean region.

And before the cultural heritage of Tunisia, even industry leaders, Turkey and Egypt, take off their hats. The fact is that in the past centuries, someone tried to conquer the stretch of land, which is now inhabited by the citizens of Tunisia, and many even succeeded. So, in ancient times, the ancient city of Carthage, founded by the Phoenicians, flourished on this territory. Then it was completely destroyed by the Roman conquerors. Tunisia was long under French rule, until the Arabs took over government. Long colonization left unique imprints in the cultural life of the country. And in our time, almost every such "imprint" has become a popular sightseeing object.



The most popular tourist business card in Tunisia is the ruins of the ancient city of Carthage, and in El Jame there is an ancient amphitheater - the architectural dominant of the country, the hero of posters and tourist photos. It is not inferior in magnitude and size to the Roman Colosseum, but not so "promoted". And in every tourist city there are local attractions.

Hotel Location

The hotel is very well located, 11 km from the ancient city of Monastir and 12 km from the city of Sousse in the village of Skanes. Such a location is convenient in all respects. Big cities are almost nearby (7-10 minutes by taxi or bus), you can go, visit cult tourist sites, monuments of large ancient settlements. What interesting places are located in the vicinity of the hotel?

15 km from the hotel is the center of the old town of Monastir - Medina. Architectural monuments such as the Grand Mosque, the Museum of National Art, temples, Ribat deserve a cursory or careful inspection.

The mausoleum of the first president of Tunisia Habib Bourguiba is another architectural business card of the city. The building has a magnificent view: with a golden dome, wide alleys, groves.

Fans of thalassotherapy, clubs and evening entertainment have a direct road to Sousse. This resort is relatively more expensive than Monastir or Skanes. It is popular among young people, Europeans, lovers of spa, outdoor activities and nightlife.

Outdoor enthusiasts can also choose what to do in the Skanes area. The surrounding area has 18-hole international golf courses.

There are also many ranches in the region where horse riding enthusiasts can indulge. Horseback riding along the coast from Monastir to Sousse can also be enjoyed if you visit one of several riding schools.

Another popular activity in the Skanes area is diving. At depths, it is convenient to observe the small motley marine fauna, reefs, underwater landscapes.
