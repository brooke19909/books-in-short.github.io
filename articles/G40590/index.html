Owls from felt: we make original crafts quickly and simply

Everyone loves little cute things. Consider how souvenir owls are made of felt. The job description will help you make the original little thing simply and quickly.





Different souvenir options

The purpose of the toy depends on the size of the toy. Very small owls made of felt can be used as pendants on the phone. Also, small-sized products are often connected to keys in the form of key rings. Such stylish little things will make these personal things noticeable and certainly will not let you get lost. If the product is medium in size, it can be presented to someone as a souvenir. A variety of animals look very original on a Christmas tree or in flower pots. It is necessary to increase the size a little more, and you will already receive a stylish soft toy. It is important to carefully perform the work and fill the product with a fairly tight filler to maintain shape. As a result, it will be simply impossible to believe that a wonderful owl is made with felt from felt and other available materials at home, and not bought in a store. The largest souvenir models are sofa cushions. To do this, simply enlarge the pattern of small gizmos to the desired scale and sew the product in the usual way. The final stage of processing will be embossed seam trim.

Product Model Selection

At this stage, you can show your creative inclinations in full. Fantasizing and experimenting with materials, original and unique crafts are easily obtained. Want to make a phone case, then the product can be in the form of a pocket with a valve. Are you planning to decorate the Christmas tree with crafts? Make voluminous owls, filling them with a synthetic winterizer. Such small birds look wonderful in the composition of autumn compositions from leaves and natural materials. You can also "seat" a souvenir on an improvised tree made by yourself from beads or in the style of origami. As you can see, it all depends on how the craft will be used. Therefore, it is very important to choose the right combination of several shades that will be in harmony with the overall composition. First, practice making small crafts. An owl is made of felt with your own hands very simply and quickly. What materials are better to use and how to carry out work in stages? You will learn more about this later in this article.





Necessary materials

Of course, a variety of materials are used to obtain the product. It can be hard paper, thick fabric or knitted fabric. But let's look at what it takes to make a small felt owl. Trim remaining after sewing other products will be used. Take a felt of several colors, semi-rigid or soft in its structure. Surely contrasting in tone and fairly strong threads will be required. In the absence of special materials, for example, synthetic winterizer, you can cut old small knitted items into small pieces.





Stages of cutting owls from felt

If you do not have the required sample, you can draw the blank yourself. To do this, first try to depict on paper a general view of the souvenir with all the details as it will look ready-made. Then move separately additional finishing preparations - wings, head, beak, etc. Usually there is a small size owl made of felt. A pattern of all the details for this reason can easily fit on a pair of sheets of a school notebook. You can copy an image directly from a computer monitor by printing it to a printer. If necessary, change the scale of the pattern. When cutting, apply the details of the patterns to the felt and trace a piece of soap or a simple pencil. Additional seam allowances are not necessary.





Manufacture

It is advisable to cut the felt owl blanks with sharp scissors. After all, when all parts are connected, the edge will not be processed, that is, no bending of the material is required. The seam goes directly along the very edge, capturing with stitches no more than 0.5 cm. First we connect the details of the body. Through a small hole we fill the product with light material. After that, we form wings, which we similarly make voluminous. You can simulate plumage by making parallel strips of stitches on them with contrasting threads. Then, prepared eyes in the form of a three-layer structure with a bright button in the center are mounted on the body. In conclusion, we decorate the owl with a beak and decoration accessories in the form of hearts, flowers or other funny ornaments. When using an owl as a keychain, be sure to attach a loop between the ears. Or you can stick a long wooden stick to the bottom of the craft for installation in a flower pot. A wonderful souvenir is ready!
