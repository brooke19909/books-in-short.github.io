Crochet for beginners sweaters: schemes, descriptions, tips



Crocheted products strike with beauty and grace, especially if they are made by the hands of real craftswomen. These things are comparable to works of art, and putting on such a masterpiece, you can immediately find yourself in the spotlight. Mastering the crochet technique is easy. Improving experience and practice, a novice craftswoman can eventually become a real pro in needlework.

How to choose the right crochet hook

For beginners, it is important to immediately learn the basic rule: you should select a hook for the thickness of the yarn, and yarn - for the pattern of the selected pattern. It happens the other way around: the fashionista liked the yarn, and she wanted to knit a unique thing under it. In this embodiment, the hook is bought under the thickness of the thread, and then the circuit is already selected. Crocheting for beginners sweaters with a complex pattern can be an impossible task and discourage the desire to continue needlework. You should always practice with simple patterns, gradually mastering new, more complex patterns. This is the only way to achieve true mastery.



Hooks come in various lengths and thicknesses and have their own number. Quite often, the yarn label indicates which hook number it is intended for. Otherwise, you must independently determine the size. It is important that the thickness of the head of the knitting tool be equal to the double thickness of the folded yarn. For knitting some products, this rule does not apply, then the thickness of the hook and yarn can be the same. In the process of knitting, using several types of tools will help to better understand the details.

The choice of yarn does not endure fuss

Openwork crocheting sweaters can bring real pleasure from work, especially if the visual image of the product, which the knitter is guided by, reflects an exact copy of what she holds in her hands.



The color of the yarn may differ from what is mentioned in the description. Basically, the craftswomen choose the product according to the drawing, and the shades are selected according to their style and the characteristic features of their appearance.

The quality and uniqueness of work depends little on the color of the yarn. It is important to choose the right thread texture. For knitting wear items it is better to use natural yarn, it is somewhat more expensive, but if you create a masterpiece, then it should correspond to its status in all respects. Synthetic thread is most often used for knitting toys, decor elements. Specialized stores can offer a choice of yarn of all possible colors and any quality.

In order to learn crochet for beginners in a short time and without special difficulties, sweaters should not be taken as a practical example. It is easier to train on a single piece of canvas that does not have a special purpose, which can then be dissolved or stored as a sample. Text job description



It is not worth guessing which model to knit a product, textual or schematic - at the beginning of the knowledge of technology, both options are important. If you build up some experience, over time, one pattern scheme will be enough for needlewomen, but a text designation is never superfluous. In addition, it reflects all the main numbers: the number of loops in the figure, rows in the rapport, the junction of the individual elements. With such an allowance, there is no need to re-count the loops once again.

Focusing on the diagram and text description, it is easier to learn crochet. A jacket for a girl, which can be chosen as the first independent product, will be a good start to the practical implementation of the theory studied. When everything is at hand: two job descriptions, a tool, yarn and a model for which the product is knitted, work is faster. Over time, you can begin to create clothes for yourself.

How to read the drawing scheme correctly

In the process of studying and practical development of the knitting technique, it is important for a novice master to remember and consolidate all the information received on patterns and methods of work.

Prior to introducing the concept of "rapport" into the description of schemes, a drawing could consist of several sheets in which each element of the pattern was drawn with all the details and with the real number of rows. This made it difficult to crochet for beginners sweaters - the most popular element of clothing. The description was too long, and the master could get confused in the ranks. Now it‚Äôs enough to be able to read the rapport pattern and repeat it as many times as necessary.

The scheme is read from bottom to top and from left to right, if only the front (even) rows are indicated. With a more detailed drawing, when a description of the back rows is required, they are read from right to left. If the scheme requires special reading, additionally the arrows indicate the direction of knitting. This may seem complicated only for beginners. After accumulating some experience, the process of studying the circuit will cease to cause difficulties.

Knitting workshops



It is not difficult to master the crochet technique yourself, but this process can take quite a lot of time. Nowadays, you can learn everything from quality manuals and video programs. Only this is a bit wrong. To become a true master, it will still be more correct to work out with a professional. Learning from someone is always more convenient and faster. In addition, the training master will transfer to the students his own achievements, which cannot be found in the manuals, will clearly show knitting techniques, as they say, on the fingers.

And if you already have some initial experience in mastering the knitting technique, it is enough to visit narrowly targeted master classes, such as, for example, crocheting sweatshirts. If you want to learn how to knit more serious things, you can agree with the teacher about a private lesson.

Both fashionable and beautiful

Crocheted products are unique and inimitable, even if the basics of ancient drawings were used to create them , because each needlewoman will definitely bring something special to the existing schemes: she uses other connections of the main elements, replaces some nodes with others or something else. To meet two absolutely identical things is almost impossible. Crocheting for beginners sweaters according to a well-learned pattern can be repeated with another yarn with a different shade as a fixing practice. Firstly, the scheme will be studied in detail, and the creation of the second and subsequent models of time will require much less. Secondly, repetition is the mother of learning, which means that having done one operation several times, it will be much easier to remember. Thirdly, creating the next product, the master will definitely want to bring something of his own to the drawing - this can turn out to be a unique thing in all respects.
