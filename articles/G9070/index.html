What will surprise the world famous Ibiza tourists? Attractions not to be missed

Located in the Mediterranean Sea, a fabulously beautiful island, part of the Balearic archipelago, is famous for its clubs, which are considered the best in the world, and luxurious beaches with funky parties. To a greater extent, the resort attracts young people, but older people will also find places worthy of attention.

A resort where life is in full swing

The island of Ibiza, whose attractions will pleasantly surprise all vacationers, welcomes guests from March to November, but even in the winter months the flow of tourists does not stop. They say that the resort with its vibrant nightlife is full of vibrant colors and that it never sleeps, and this is true: there is round-the-clock fun and the atmosphere of a constant holiday. I must say that in Ibiza there are many architectural monuments, which all travelers will be interested in getting to know.





The capital of the sunny resort is the city of the same name, which attracts tourists with its entertainment center and well-preserved historical center.

So, what will attract the multifaceted Ibiza to foreign visitors? Sights, photos of which are placed in advertising booklets of travel agencies, will appeal to different categories of visitors.

Cathedral of the Immaculate Virgin Mary

In the old part of the island's capital is one of the most magnificent Catholic cathedrals. The patroness of the resort is Virgin Mary, and it is not surprising that it was in her honor that a temple was built in the XIII century, impressive in its forms. A truly breathtaking view of the monument of medieval architecture opens from the sea, and tourists who go on an excursion aspirate talk about how beautiful the hospitable Ibiza is. Sights are devoid of frank luxury, but even this minimalism will make the hearts of guests of the island beat faster. There is also an unusual museum-sacristy, which will surprise you with unique collections.

One of the oldest necropolises

The well-preserved necropolis of the Phoenician-Punic culture, founded even before our era, is proud of the charming Ibiza. Sights (photos and descriptions are presented in the article) cause genuine curiosity: only here there are about three thousand of the oldest burials of noble Carthaginians. Now this sacred place has received the status of an archaeological reserve protected by UNESCO, and you can get inside Puig das Mulins for free.





Antique settlement

It will seem incredible, but Ibiza, whose attractions are protected by local authorities, can surprise with a unique architectural ensemble, which reached the descendants in good condition. The settlement, which appeared in the Moorish era, consists of seven residential houses with underground labyrinths and two towers. The fact is that the island was often attacked by pirates, and residents tried to protect themselves by erecting defenses. The historical significance of the ancient village of Balafia, preserved in excellent condition, must not be underestimated.

Nature reserve

Ibiza, whose attractions are world famous, welcomes tourists who have traveled long distances just to enjoy the exotic nature. Ses Salines Park is considered one of the most beautiful corners of the island. Underground kingdom

Can-Mars Cave was once used by pirates as a warehouse where they kept the loot. Tourists who visited the amazing natural monument admit that they are impressed by the unique beauty of the underground labyrinth. By the way, visitors will be able to see the cave signs that smugglers left in order not to get lost in the dark grottoes. This natural museum will amaze you with an underground waterfall, transparent lakes, colonies of calcite formations, which have the most amazing forms.





Of course, in one article it is impossible to describe the main attractions of Ibiza. Photos and reviews of tourists indicate that the island with a rich past is famous not only for entertainment, but also for monuments of ancient civilizations and beautiful natural areas. The resort, hosting travelers year-round, will give a good mood and unforgettable emotions.
