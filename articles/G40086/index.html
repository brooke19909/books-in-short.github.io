Description of the sanatorium "White Sea" (Arkhangelsk)

In the resort area of ​​the southeastern shore of Lake Smerdye, which is 41 km from the city of Arkhangelsk, the White Sea resort invites everyone who wants to relax and improve their health.

Brief information about the boarding house





Sanatorium "Belomorye" (Arkhangelsk) was founded in 1978. It occupies a large territory with an area of ​​up to 22 hectares. Around the residential and medical buildings there is a coniferous forest. And just 50 meters away is the large Smerdje Lake.

Sanatorium "Belomorye" accepts for treatment and rest adults and families with children from four years old. Since 1998, a year-round children's health camp has been operating in the boarding house.

Number of rooms





According to information provided by the official website, the resort “Belomorye” (Arkhangelsk) is able to accept up to 290 people. The room stock of the boarding house consists of 24 single economy-type apartments, 130 standard double, 8 two-room double superior and 2 two-room luxury. The rooms are a small entrance hall, bedroom, bathroom with shower and a loggia. The suites still have a living room.

All rooms have modern furniture necessary for a comfortable stay (single or double bed, table, chairs, bedside tables, wardrobe, dressing room for outerwear), as well as an electric kettle and a TV. Refrigerators are only available in superior apartments and suites. Bathrooms are equipped with a shower.

For children there is a separate whole floor. There are double and triple rooms with all amenities.

Prices for tickets to the resort "Belomorye" (Arkhangelsk) are from 1200 rubles per day. For example, the cost of living in a double room of the “standard” category will cost 4580 rubles per day for two (as of 2017).

Services and services provided in the boarding house





In the sanatorium "Belomorye" (Arkhangelsk), everything has been done so that vacationers spend their holidays in comfort. Therefore, guests are offered:

  • transfer;
  • free parking;
  • laundry service;
  • Ironing room
  • a pharmacy on site;
  • wired internet in the business center, where you can still use the fax;
  • banqueting hall;
  • conference room for 30 seats;
  • library
  • cinema hall;
  • sports equipment rental;
  • organization of excursions;
  • Cafe.

The reception is open around the clock.

Four meals a day are provided in a cafe on the territory of the sanatorium. The menu here is extensive and includes various diet dishes from fish, meat, fresh vegetables and fruits, pastries. All made with fresh produce. Individual meals are available upon reservation.

Among the active entertainments, guests will find a gym, an indoor pool with mineral water, tennis, sports grounds for playing volleyball, basketball, football, table tennis, billiards and skiing in winter. From time to time, the boarding house organizes poetic and musical evenings for guests. Organization of fishing is also possible.

Fascinating excursions are held in the historical and memorial museum of M.V. Lomonosov, the museum of wooden architecture Malye Korely, the Antonievo-Siy ​​monastery, as well as the city of Severodvinsk.

Therapeutic measures





The following treatment options are available to holidaymakers at the Belomorye sanatorium:

  • hardware physiotherapy (muscle electrostimulation, electrophoresis of drugs, UHF therapy, UV radiation, galvanization and so on);
  • balneotherapy (various types of baths and showers, as well as irrigation);
  • mud applications;
  • paraffin and ozokerite applications;
  • various inhalations;
  • music and aromatherapy;
  • phytotherapy;
  • physiotherapy;
  • nutrition and more.

Nine doctors are responsible for prescribing the procedures, and 38 nurses are responsible for carrying them out. Among the medical staff there are certified specialists in the field of restorative medicine, cardiology, neurology, pediatrics, dentistry, gastroenterology, endocrinology, there are a pulmonologist, psychotherapist, occupational pathologist, therapist, physiotherapist, physical therapy and functional diagnostics doctor.

Among the wellness programs available to holidaymakers of Belomorya are spa treatments, a set of procedures to reduce weight, improve the condition of joints, the nervous system, increase the immunity of adults and children, as well as measures aimed at combating such a bad habit as smoking.

Duration of treatment varies from two weeks to 24 days.

Location

Sanatorium "Belomorye" is located at: Arkhangelsk region, Primorsky district, village of Belomorye, 20. You can get to the boarding house both by private car or by public transport. By car you should go along the Vologda highway to the sign "Belomorye" (somewhere after 41 km). From the turn to the sanatorium you need to go only 500 meters.

Buses to the pension "Belomorye" depart from the Sea River Station of the city of Arkhangelsk. Take the route number 163. He rides three times a day. From the railway station runs minibus number 54. From Talagi Airport can be reached by bus number 12.
