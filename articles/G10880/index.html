Hotel Sunset Beach Resort (Pattaya, Thailand): photos, description, reviews

It's no secret that one of the most popular resorts in Thailand is Pattaya. This place attracts tourists from all over the world, because the city can offer a rich beach holiday, comfortable accommodation and relatively low prices. This is where the Sunset Beach Resort hotel is located. So what do travelers say about this place?

What does the hotel area look like? Location





Small but very beautiful Sunset Village Beach Resort is located almost on the seashore, in a quiet resort area, about 15 minutes from the vibrant center of Pattaya. It consists of separate bungalows, which will surely appeal to fans of a relaxing holiday surrounded by nature. All buildings are constructed from environmentally friendly materials in a traditional Thai style. The hotel area resembles a blooming garden. By the way, the famous Crystal Lake is located nearby. You need to drive to the airport for about twenty minutes, so no one should worry about a long trip. By the way, three times a day a bus runs from the hotel to the city center, which is very convenient.

Hotel Sunset Village Beach Resort 3: photo and description of rooms





The hotel is considered relatively small, as it consists of 59 rooms. All are located in small, secluded cottages scattered across a tropical garden. The rooms here are spacious and clean. The modern style of their interior is combined with Thai traditions. Moreover, the rooms are decorated using exclusively natural materials and fabrics. There is an outdoor terrace where you can enjoy peace and fresh air. By the way, some rooms have direct access directly to the beach.

Of course, you can count on the necessary household appliances, including an air conditioning system, a telephone with the ability to dial directly, a safe with a combination lock. You can skip the evening watching satellite channels on a large-screen TV. There is a rather large minibar with a fridge, which is great for storing drinks and some products. By the way, drinking water is replenished every day.

The bathroom with modern equipment and a spacious shower cabin will also please you. You can count on a daily change of towels. For your convenience, there is a hair dryer and a set of hygiene products. Some cottages also have an outdoor shower.

Meals: can I eat on site?





Many tourists are interested in the issue of nutrition. When paying for your stay at Sunset Village Beach Resort, guests are entitled to daily breakfast. You will be invited to the cozy hotel restaurant Sea Terrace, located almost on the seashore. The choice of dishes for breakfast is very good - there are fruits, vegetable dishes, pancakes, pastries, soups, cereals, sometimes light meats are served.

Naturally, you can have dinner or lunch here at the local restaurant, but for an additional fee (albeit a small one). Guests can enjoy Thai and international dishes. You can have a good time in the luxuriously decorated Coco D'Amur Bar, which offers not only drinks and cocktails, but also snacks.

You can eat in the city - there are a lot of cafes, restaurants and pizzerias. There are even several Russian establishments with a more familiar menu and good service.

Where is the beach?





Hotel Sunset Beach Resort has its own stretch of coast - the beach is located in close proximity, you can walk to it in a few minutes. The sea here is clean, and the entrance is convenient, although you should be careful, because not all the bottom is cleared of stones. Since the beach is private, only guests are allowed to enter it. It is always calm, and there are enough sun loungers with umbrellas. You will also be given clean beach towels.

If you are interested in a more active pastime, then you should visit one of the public beaches of Pattaya, where you can engage in water sports.

Additional service, leisure and entertainment for tourists





Hotel Sunset Beach Resort is designed for a relaxing comfortable stay. In the courtyard there is a pool, and around it there is a sufficient number of sun loungers and umbrellas, on which you can relax at any time of the day. Guests can also expect a sauna and a real Thai massage. Golf fans will not be bored, as there is a special field near the hotel.

Almost throughout the territory there is access to the Internet, and the connection speed is pretty good. There is also a business center with a conference room where you can use a printer, fax and other equipment. The hotel staff is happy to organize various events.

Also, guests often use the services of a local tour desk, since Thailand is an incredibly interesting country that has something to offer the traveler.

Hotel Sunset Beach Resort 3: what do guests say?

Of course, reviews are a source of very valuable information. So what do travelers say about this hotel? Hotel Sunset Beach Resort is a picturesque place that is ideal for a relaxing, comfortable and leisurely vacation. There are no crowds or city noise, only the soft sound of the sea and the bright colors of nature.

The rooms are spacious and clean, stand out with their original design. Cleaned up here all the time. Guests note that towels and hygiene products are replenished every day. Nutrition is beyond praise, as the choice of dishes is large, and the food is always fresh and tasty. The undoubted advantage is the presence of its own private beach, without merchants and the noise of the crowd.

The city center is located at some distance, but you can get there at any convenient time. Accommodation prices are relatively affordable, especially considering the level of comfort and proximity to the sea.
