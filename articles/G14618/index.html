Hotel "Kronon Park" (Grodno): description, infrastructure, rooms, photos

This small hotel is one of the most expensive luxury establishments in the city of Grodno. Suggested price range: from 10 657 rub. According to reviews, the Kronon Park Hotel (Grodno), located in the very center of Pyshek (a picturesque forest), combines decent comfort, an exquisite classic interior and high-quality service. The hotel building is surrounded by a small park with comfortable walking paths. Guests here enjoy the tranquility and silence, broken only by the singing of birds and the murmur of water in the fountain. The city center is a 10-minute drive away.

Number of rooms

For accommodation of guests at the Kronon Park Hotel (Grodno, 19 Pyshki tract), 15 comfortable rooms are offered that belong to different categories:

  • standard;
  • business;
  • classic;
  • VIP

The cost of a weekly stay in the room: from 61,470 rubles.

Room features

The design of each of the rooms at the Kronon Park Hotel in Grodno is distinguished by its unique style and color scheme. All rooms for guests are equipped with the best Italian furniture, accessories and plumbing. Residents can use:

  • a huge balcony with a wonderful view;
  • television with satellite channels;
  • high speed internet;
  • minibar with fridge;
  • air conditioning;
  • work table;
  • direct dial telephone;
  • a bathroom (guests are provided with a bath or shower, hairdryer and toiletries.

Hotel infrastructure

The amenities of the hotel are ensured by the availability of a number of amenities. Guests can use the hotel:

  • the garden;
  • spacious lounge with fireplace;
  • conference room;
  • business center.

Service

Residents can use for free:

  • round-the-clock service;
  • internet access.

Paid guests can use:

  • parking lot;
  • transport to the railway station and airport;
  • luggage room;
  • room service;
  • banquets and meetings organization service;
  • service organization of excursions.

For entertainment and sports, the hotel provides residents with:

  • a sauna;
  • pool (indoor).

For the convenience of young guests, the hotel has cots.

Residents call the prices at the Kronon Park Hotel (Grodno) quite acceptable, adequate.

Catering

Guests of Kronon Park can eat at the buffet, bar or restaurant (continental breakfasts are provided). In addition, the hotel also has a full board system.

According to reviews, residents are very pleased with the hotel's excellent restaurant, located on the 1st floor of the complex. The institution consists of 2 halls with a capacity of 40 and 65 people. In the first room, the solemn and majestic atmosphere, provided by the design using expensive furniture, candelabra and beautiful tablecloths, is noteworthy. The second room is more elegant, airy and romantic. In it, visitors note the presence of huge panoramic windows overlooking the forest. The restaurant's menu offers dishes of national Belarusian and European cuisines.

In the stylish and elegant bar located in the lobby, guests note the presence of a huge fireplace. The atmosphere in the bar is called pleasant and relaxing. Visitors are offered a wide selection of drinks, snacks and cocktails.
