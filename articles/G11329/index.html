Hotel Side Star Elegance 5 * (Turkey, Side): reviews

If you are used to relaxing in chain hotels where you know the high standard of service and services; if you are a connoisseur of "old-style" hotels; and if you are looking for a place to stay in Side (Turkey), then we can recommend Side Star Elegance 5 * to you. Until the fall of 2013, it was called Hotel Asteria Side 5 *. And now, despite the change of sign, he belongs to the Asteria Hotels chain. It should be said that this hotel was the first five-star hotel in the resort of Side. It was built in 1987. But the last large-scale renovation took place at the hotel quite recently, in 2015. What is this five star hotel? We will cover this in this article. The information base was not only the materials provided by the hotelier himself, but also the reviews of those tourists who had already visited there. First, let's say the following. Location

The exact address of the Side Star Elegance 5 * hotel is: Turkey, the Anatolian coast, Side, Kumkoy district, the first line of the Mediterranean Sea. The nearest airport (Antalya) is 65 kilometers away. Reviews say the transfer takes an hour and a half. The center of Side is two and a half kilometers away. Kumkoy is actually a suburb of the resort. The Side Star Elegance Hotel is located next to similar Fives. Its neighbors are the Melas Resort and Horus Paradise hotels. Reviews give the location of the hotel five points. On the one hand, close to the city. If you wish, you can walk along the promenade. On the other hand, quiet and calm, a lot of greenery. To the beach go from two to five minutes. The transport connection with Side is simply wonderful. Near the hotel there are many shops, souvenir shops, there is even a pharmacy.

Territory

Side's first five-star Side Star Elegance hotel grabbed a huge chunk of the coast from the city. Its area is 42 thousand square meters. Many reviews mention that the terrain is uneven. But multi-level terrain is not a problem for weak legs. The Hotel Star Elegance offers all kinds of elevators, mini-funiculars and escalators. Many reviews are devoted to the interesting architecture of the three buildings. The entire territory is not revealed to the guest immediately, but gradually. Every day brings new discoveries - transitions, hidden arbors, gazebos ... The territory is perfectly landscaped. A lot of shade. The buildings - the main seven-story building and two additional five-level buildings - fit perfectly into the hilly landscape. There are scattered pools, tennis courts, an amphitheater, a la carte restaurants and other infrastructure. Many reviews call Star Elegance a hotel with a water park, while others give it the characterization of a SPA hotel.

Nutrition Reviews

Nearly all tourists who have visited Side Star Elegance in the past two to three years praise sumptuous dining. Unlike most Turkish all-inclusive resort hotels, where tourists are fed with semi-finished kebabs, they serve lamb, shrimp, fried and grilled fish, as well as other delicacies. If for medical reasons you need a diet, they will bring you dishes without sugar, gluten, lactose, etc. Vegetarians say that you can always choose something to your taste. The waiters are quick. In the hall of the main restaurant you can always find a free table - both in the air-conditioned hall and on the veranda. To visit a la carte, you need to book your visit at the reception. Tourists praise the cuisine of Turkish and fish restaurants.

Beach

Side Star Elegance Hotel has a private beach. Between him and the buildings runs a promenade. The beach is covered with natural sand. According to reviews, it is small (only seventy meters), but wide. All space is lined with sunbeds and umbrellas. There are quite a lot of them, and if you come to the beach before ten in the morning, finding a place at the water's edge will not be a problem. The descent into the water is gradual, which is good for bathing children. The hotel provides beach towels.



Pools, mini water park

Despite the fact that the Hotel Side Star Elegance is positioning itself as a place of family relaxation, there is such a new feature for Turkey as Relax Pool. Only adults (18+) are allowed into this pool. This is the place to take a break from children's cries and splashes. Towels are issued from 9 am at the upper and lower pools. And you need to know this when going to the beach. You can still sunbathe in any place on the vast territory of the hotel, in the shade and in the sun - both on sunbeds and in hammocks. For little children have their own pools. For those who feel cold at + 26 ¬∞ C, there is an indoor tank. It would be incorrect to call two slides an aquapark, but teenagers like them, especially the one that is a continuous pipe. These rides work on schedule. There are separate pools at the Deluxe rooms - it is quiet and calm.

Vacation with children

Side Star Elegance 5 * hotel does everything to attract tourists traveling to Turkey with the whole family. For small guests, everything is thought out to the smallest detail. Starting with the fact that tourists with children are lodged on the ground floor of the first building so that they do not have to deal with the nuances of the hilly landscape of the hotel. The restaurant does not have a clear meal plan for babies. But there are high chairs for feeding babies. When you check in, you can even rent out a pram - for free. Children over four and under twelve years old can be bailed to children's animators. Teenagers are entertained in sports sections: they play with water polo, darts and the like outdoor fun. Parents who take care of their babies themselves can take them to frolic in a shaded, soft-covered playground.



Infrastructure at Side Star Elegance

Reviews claim that the hotel's services are consistent with its five-star status. Romservice is free here. The hotel has a spa center. Guests can take a steam bath in the Finnish sauna, Turkish hammam or Russian steam bath. But you will have to pay for massage services. Wi-Fi is in all rooms, its speed is excellent. The service of access to the World Wide Web is free. But network games in Internet cafes, as well as billiards with bowling, do not belong to this list. If you like tennis, bring your own rackets and ball. The hotel has a beautiful court. You will have to pay only for the rental of ammunition and evening lighting. The hotel is friendly to sports lovers. One of the pools is used for different types of "activity": there are classes in water aerobics, then a game of water polo. For adults, there is a team of animators. Very often, every other day, the hotel invites you to perform professional dancers and other artists. The lobby bar offers live music in the evenings.
