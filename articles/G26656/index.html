Belair Beach Hotel (Greece / Rhodes / Ixia): hotel description and reviews

It is not surprising that tens of thousands of tourists from different parts of our planet come here every year. But before the trip, each of them is faced with the problem of choosing a hotel. About location

As already mentioned, Belair Beach Hotel is very well located. Only 2-4 kilometers separates the hotel from local attractions. These include Rodini Park, the Acropolis of Rhodes, the Temple of Apollo, Aegean University, Diagoras Stadium, Clock Tower, as well as the Palace of the Grand Masters, Knights Street, the Archaeological Museum, Mandraki Port and much more.

Transport links here are also well developed. Opposite the hotel there is a bus stop, from where you can take a minibus to the city center in just 15 minutes. The airport, by the way, is also located nearby. Rhodes international airport Diagoras is less than ten minutes away.

But the most important thing is that the hotel is 50 meters from its own beach, equipped with sun loungers and umbrellas. Interestingly, the sandy and pebble coast itself stretches for 5 kilometers.

There are many ways to diversify your leisure time at Belair Beach Hotel. Here is what to do here:

  • Billiard room.

  • Water sports on the beach: windsurfing, diving, canoeing.

I would like to say a little more about the latter. Ixia is a paradise for surfers. For them, all conditions are created here, first of all, by nature itself. It is easy to catch a wave here, since the sea is constantly raging, and the winds do not stop blowing at all.

It is in Ixia that there are three large windsurfing centers - Windserfers World, Surfer's Paradise and Pro Center. Interestingly, there are Russian-speaking instructors. Those wishing to come here to study this sport should take this fact into account. And it is better in this case to go in April, May, September or October. A good time for beginners - a comparative "calm" reigns at sea, convenient for acquiring the basic skills for conquering waves.

Helpful information

When preparing to fly to Greece at the Belair Beach Hotel, it is important to consider several nuances. So, check-in starts at 14:00, as in many other hotels. Check out lasts until noon. This information is useful to consider when choosing a flight.

With children, accommodation is allowed here. Moreover, for children under two years of age it is free. Cots will even be provided for them.

Now about remote booking. When implementing it, you will need to provide personal credit card details. The hotel accepts American Express, Visa and Maestro. The used card is presented upon check-in along with the passport.

By the way, according to the law, cash payments cannot exceed 500 euros. To find out how to pay for your stay if the final bill is a larger amount, you need to contact the administration.
