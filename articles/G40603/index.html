Abkhazia, Gagra, "Midel-Gagra" (boarding house): description, photos, rest and reviews

Hotel "Midel" (Gagra) - the former "House of Creativity of the USSR Writers Union" Literary Fund. At one time, such celebrities as V. Vysotsky, B. Akhmadulina, E. Evtushenko, R. Gamzatov, F. Iskander and other singers, poets, writers rested here. Now it is a boarding house, which is glad to accept everyone.





Location

The boarding house is located in a picturesque place, near the foot of the Gagra Range, on the Black Sea coast. From the windows of the buildings you can see not only water open spaces, but also mysterious gorges, tropical palm trees and oleanders, majestic mountains. The location near the sea contributed to the formation of a unique climate in this place. The beach is only 50-150 m from the buildings.

Near Adler is the Midel boarding house. Gagra is only 41 km away. The Russian-Abkhaz border is 26 km from the boarding house.

The full address of the Holiday House: Gagra, Nart Avenue, house 141. Those who come here by their own transport can use the free parking located in the pension.

From the airport or railway station you can take a minibus to the border. From here, after checking the documents, a regular bus will take you to the Midel stop (Gagra). Photos will help to see the location of the boarding house and beautiful natural landscapes around it.





You can pre-order a transfer from the railway station, airport to the boarding house. Today the cost of this service for 1 person is 500 rubles. No baggage charges apply. Hotel staff will meet you at the entrance, spend in the room.

The price of trips already includes 3 meals a day. The menu is designed for seven days. That is, during the week every day, new hearty dishes are prepared for you. In addition to them, Abkhazian wine and fresh fruits are served to vacationers.

Beach and water activities

As mentioned above, the sea is located in close proximity to the hotel. Vacationers say that sometimes it seems as if the waves are beating on the balcony, the impressions of this are unforgettable. It has its own beach, covered with small pebbles with a fenced area, a guard and a lifeguard. Sun beds and umbrellas are provided free of charge on the beach of the city of Gagra, the "Midel" hotel.





You can go diving with an experienced instructor. Equipment and equipment is available for a fee. It will be nice to ride a catamaran (pedal boat) or take a boat trip.

Beauty, leisure

The pension has a beauty salon, where for a fee you can use any of the services provided. Also, guests of the hotel can enjoy various types of massage.

In the evening, you can visit the disco, which is also located on the territory of the Rest House at the address: city of Gagra, "Midel". The guesthouse is open until 24 hours. As vacationers say, the sounds of music subside at this time, not a minute later. Therefore, those living in nearby rooms can fully rest and sleep at night.

Fans of table tennis will find in the boarding house everything necessary for practicing this sport.

Wi-Fi is available throughout the holiday home.





Excursion programs will help to spend your vacation time is not only useful, fun, but also interesting. You will learn a lot of new things, see local beauties and get a lot of impressions that will remain with you for a long time.

Midel (Gagra): reviews

Vacationers who visited this hotel speak positively about nature, rooms, staff. The administration is friendly, in the morning it offers to brew fresh coffee. As for food, one of the tourists who visited Christmas at the company of friends and girlfriends here notes that they prepare a delicious, home-style menu, a varied menu. During the time they were there, it never repeated.

The excellent attitude of the staff towards the vacationers is noted by many guests of the boarding house. They say that next to the Holiday House there are not only many cafes, but also shops, stalls, a post office, a water park, which is interesting for both children and adults.

Tourists say that the country's amazing beauty is Abkhazia. "Midel" (Gagra) is located in a region with lush nature. They advise those who love a relaxing holiday, a clean sea and not spoiled by the service of 5-star hotels to visit this place. In addition, in a boarding house you can relax cheaper than in luxury expensive hotels abroad.

Almost all speak well of the sea, they say that compared to the beaches of some other cities, it is crystal clear and very clean. On the shore, you can catch crabs, which sometimes walk in these places.
