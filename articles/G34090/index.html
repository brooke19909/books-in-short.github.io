CHC Memory Boutique Hotel 3 * (Greece, Crete, Hersonissos): description of rooms, services, reviews

Crete is the largest Greek island washed by three seas at once. Here the sun almost always shines and warm weather reigns - even in December-January at a temperature of 15-16 degrees you can swim. Many people decide to spend their holidays here, but first choose a hotel in which they would be provided with maximum comfort. Well, there are a lot of good hotels in Crete, but I would like to mention CHC Memory Boutique Hotel 3 * with special attention.





general information

This budget but cozy hotel is located in Hersonissos - a resort town located between the Aegean and Mediterranean Seas. The location at the CHC Memory Boutique Hotel 3 * hotel is good - even the airport in Heraklion is relatively close (about 25-30 kilometers).

Even from the hotel you can quickly walk to the promenade and, accordingly, the beaches. A bus stop is in close proximity to the building. So guests do not need to use a taxi - you can save by riding on public transport.

By the way, there are several interesting sights nearby. At 400 meters from the hotel is an aquarium called the Aqua World Aquarium. At 2 kilometers - Lychnostatis Folk Art Museum. A 5-minute drive is the Crete Golf Club. In general, there are many interesting places here - every connoisseur of Cretan attractions will confirm this fact.

What is in the hotel?

CHC Memory Boutique Hotel is a 3-star hotel, the main purpose of which is to provide guests with maximum comfort during their stay. In fact, this is the concept of boutique hotels. So it is not suitable for those tourists who want to get maximum entertainment without leaving the hotel. Here you can brighten up your leisure time, just relaxing on the sun terrace and swimming in the pool. There is also a television room.

But on the territory of CHC Memory Boutique Hotel 3 * there is free Wi-Fi. There is public parking nearby. There is a 24-hour reception in the lobby, and there you can also find a camera for storing things, a point with a photocopy machine and a fax machine, as well as a laundry room where guests are not only washed, but also ironed.

Accommodation options

The main thing that interests people who are going to the city of Hersonissos for a vacation is the apartments. The hotel has 38 rooms. The cheapest option is a 2-bed apartment "Promo". Their area is 16 square meters. m. Inside there are two separate beds, access to a spacious balcony with a view of the surroundings, air conditioning, a safe and a wide-screen TV with satellite channels. There is also a plumbing unit. It has a bath, shower, toilet, hairdryer and free hygiene items.

A weekly stay in this room for two people will be ~ 38,000 rubles. This is taking into account the all-inclusive service, 13% VAT and city tax (0.5%). Three adult guests will have to pay approximately 50 tons. And if you compare prices with the rates of other hotels, you can understand why it is very popular.
