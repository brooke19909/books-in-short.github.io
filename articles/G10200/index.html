Nizhny Novgorod Hotels: list, addresses, photos, reviews

Hotel Marins Park Hotel, Nizhny Novgorod

The hotel is considered one of the largest in the entire Volga region. It is located in the heart of the city at the famous Nizhny Novgorod fair. Convenient location allows you to quickly and inexpensively get from all transport points. From the train station you can even walk.

Each guest of the hotel can take advantage of a free breakfast consisting of delicious and healthy dishes. And all this is completely free. The hotel has a cozy cafe and restaurant where you can hold a business meeting or a romantic date.

This is the best place for a business trip. The hotel has 7 conference rooms where you can organize a business event for a small fee and even hold an international conference.

Comfortable clean rooms are fully equipped with everything necessary for living and working. Always friendly staff provides round-the-clock European service.

The Congress Hotel Marins Park rightfully deserves the status of the best hotel in Nizhny Novgorod. Guest reviews are extremely positive.

Prices - from 2900 rubles per person per day.

The hotel is located on the street. Sovetskaya, 12, m. "Moscow".

Hotel "Sail"

The hotel is located in the historic district of Nizhny Novgorod, on Ilyinskaya Street, which, in turn, is famous for its many attractions. For those who visit the city for the purpose of relaxation, the best place to stay is simply not found. The hotel is relatively young. In 2007, a complete reconstruction of the building was carried out, thanks to which this place has gained popularity among famous artists, athletes, musicians and businessmen.

The hotel has 55 comfortable rooms with various levels of comfort. There are standard, luxury and VIP rooms. All rooms are contemporary in style and equipped with new furniture. Each guest is offered a soft bed, refrigerator, telephone, air conditioning, work area, all bath accessories. A complimentary buffet breakfast is included.

Within 10 minutes from the hotel, guests will find the main street of the city, where there are many attractions, cultural centers, cafes, restaurants and shops.

Check-in time is up to 12 noon. Earlier check-in requires coordination and may be additionally paid. Prices - from 2800 rubles per person per day.

The Parus Hotel has earned the special status of the most welcoming hotel in Nizhny Novgorod. The list goes on with a no less decent hotel.

Hotel City Hotel Sova

The hotel is quite new. Its doors opened only a few years ago, but he had already managed to gain recognition among sophisticated guests. The European quality standard combined with Russian soulfulness do not leave indifferent any visitor. The hotel is located in the center of Nizhny Novgorod, near the train station. Convenient transport interchange allows guests to easily get anywhere in the city.

The hotel has 50 comfortable rooms, 37 of which are standard. The rooms are equipped with everything necessary for a good rest. There is one large or two single beds, TV, telephone, Internet, desk, air conditioning, shower, toilet and all necessary hygiene products.

Check in at the hotel from 14.00, check out until 12.00.

Address: st. Vaneeva, d. 121.

Hotel "Azimut"

The hotel occupies a place in the business center of the city. The hotel offers amazing views of Alexander Nevsky Cathedral, Kanavinsky Bridge and the Nizhny Novgorod Fair. You can also contemplate the beautiful view of the confluence of two rivers, the Volga and the Ob. In just 10 minutes you can reach the main street. Public transport stops are a 5-minute walk away.

The rooms have everything you need. Modern furniture and household appliances are present in every room, regardless of its category. The apartments also have a fully equipped kitchen.

Children under 6 years stay free with parents. The price includes breakfast.

Address: st. Zalomova, 2.

Nizhny Novgorod hotels offer their services not only to people with high incomes. A simple tourist or a person who has come on a business trip can afford to rent a house in an inexpensive hostel or mini-hotel.

Budget accommodation options in Nizhny Novgorod are represented by the following hotels according to consumer ratings.

Hotel "Astra"

The hotel is located in the city center near the largest medical center "Stork". Nearby are also the main business centers. Despite the relatively low cost of living, the hotel offers its customers 14 superior rooms. The price includes breakfast, tea, coffee, Internet, and a parking space. On the territory there is a cozy cafe with a varied menu. Guests can also order a transfer, use the safe, book tickets or excursions. Pet lovers can stay with them for a fee. Smokers can use the designated smoking area.

The cost of hotel accommodation is from 15 euros per day per room.

The hotel is located on the street. Guzhevoy, d. 48.

Hotel "Ilyinka"

The hotel is located in the historical center of Nizhny Novgorod. In walking distance guests are given the opportunity to visit the Church of the Assumption of the Blessed Virgin Mary and Elijah the Prophet, as well as the house of Peter the Great. And this is not all the attractions that are available to hotel guests for review. Only 50 meters from the hotel offers scenic views of the natural heritage of the city. The Volga and Ob rivers merge together. You can also visit the famous Nizhny Novgorod Fair and take a walk along the largest pedestrian street of the city - Bolshaya Pokrovskaya.

The hotel itself is located in an elite three-story building and occupies two floors there. Guests are offered comfortable single and double deluxe and junior suites. The suites are equipped with Jacuzzi bathtubs and hydromassage showers. Throughout the hotel you can use the Internet.

Room rate - from 32 euros per day.

Address: Krutoy Lane, 6V.

Hotel "Start"

The hotel is located near the metro station "Petrel". The rooms are decorated in a simple classic style. Each room has a TV and air conditioning. Within a 10-minute walk is the picturesque Sormovsky Park. There are also many cafes near the hotel.

Throughout the hotel guests can use the free Internet.

Hotel prices are quite affordable, from 1600 rubles per room.

The hotel is located at: st. Lindovskaya, d. 60.
