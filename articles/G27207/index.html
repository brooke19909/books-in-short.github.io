It is important for all tourists to know where in November the warm sea

Many people, despite the fact that summer ends in August, easily extend this season by visiting warm countries. However, in order for the vacation to really work out and you do not fall into the zone of cool temperatures, it is important to know in which month where it is warm and where it is not. In the article we will try to figure out where in November there is a warm sea, which will strengthen our strength and prepare for the upcoming New Year holidays.



Very often they leave for a vacation to the azure surf in November, when the desire to return the summer just passed is sometimes simply irresistible. The most relevant country at this time is Egypt, which you can get into quickly and without any special undertakings. The main thing is to choose a resort that is located as far south as possible, since in the northern cities of this ancient mysterious country it begins to cool with the onset of winter.

A trip to Thailand may turn out to be extraordinary and memorable. If you are looking for a place where the sea is warm in November, then this exotic island country is ideal for you. Here you can lie on a white sand on the island of Phuket, plunge into crazy shopping and entertainment in Pattaya, or learn the basics of windsurfing on the Krabi Islands. There are no cold weather in Thailand, so you can visit its beaches all year round.

For lovers of distant travel, the answer to the question of where the warm sea is in November is simple - in Central America. Here your attention is given a very wide, and most importantly - colorful selection. Among the states located in the Caribbean, the queen is Mexico and its unforgettable Cancun. Here you can plunge into the Mayan Riviera, as well as visit the world's only underwater museum. More budget options in this region will be the island states - Guatemala, Jamaica, Panama, Dominican Republic.



If you are looking for where the warm sea is in November, the city you can die for is Miami. Of course, the prices there are somewhat high, but it has everything: mirrored skyscrapers, chic villas with pools, palm trees and beautiful tropical plants, and, of course, a warm ocean surf. And if you do not like the hustle and bustle, then the suburban beaches of Miami will be a real paradise for you.

This is where in November the warm sea really is on the islands that are scattered across the oceans of our planet. When choosing such resorts, always focus on the political system established in them. Hawaii is another state of America, so it is always fun and noisy there, and the service, of course, is at the highest level. Seychelles - an independent state near Africa. This is a Catholic country, and there are not imposed any strict rules of conduct on their guests. All those archipelagos that are located in the waters of the Indian Ocean, as a rule, are Buddhist states.





The place where the warm sea in November is the Middle East. The choice of resorts here is very wide - the UAE, Israel, Iran. Just when leaving for the home of Scheherazade, remember the dress code - here they are extremely scrupulous about the appearance of women. Although on site you can always relax and take a walk in your usual attire.

The warm sea in November is a real fairy tale, which, although it does not last long, is remembered forever. Many people dream of leaving for warm countries from a cold and rainy city. Today it is possible to fulfill these desires.
