How to crochet bulk flowers

If you like to do needlework, be sure to try crocheting three-dimensional flowers. They turn out very beautiful. Such decor will look spectacular on the subject of your wardrobe or will be able to make a pleasant highlight in the interior.

Knitting flowers

If you have already tried yourself in this type of needlework, then take any remaining yarn and a working tool of the number that is. To begin with, it is better to try to make flowers according to the schemes (they are given below) from single-color threads.

When the pattern is mastered, proceed to the combination of yarn in different colors. You make the middle, for example, yellow, the first row of petals - light pink, the second - bright, etc. This allows you to get a wide variety of options when using the same initial scheme.

When the principle of work is understood, proceed to creativity - self-development of templates and methods of manufacturing elements. You can collect a bouquet of them to decorate the interior. You will definitely be carried away by this business so that crocheting volumetric flowers turns into an everyday hobby and a pleasant pastime.

Where can I use manufactured items

Crochet voluminous flowers are made in different ways, they will always find application.

There are two ways to use it:

  1. Decoration of the finished thing (homemade or purchased).
  2. Making an independent souvenir or item of clothing.

You can decorate with knitted flowers as follows:

  • caps and hats;
  • Scarves
  • dresses, sweaters;
  • booties;
  • bags;
  • curtains.

You can make of flowers:

  • belt;
  • jewelry (pendants, earrings, brooches);




  • a blouse;
  • scarf;
  • cover;
  • panel;
  • a bouquet for a vase or decorative filling of the container.

Choose any option you like. From a large number of even identical elements made according to one scheme, spectacular products are obtained.





Crochet volumetric flower (simple option)

Three methods of knitting flowers are often used:

  1. In a circle according to the principle of napkins.
  2. Consistently making each petal (also around the circumference).
  3. Making an openwork strip, which then gathers on a thread in a spiral in a three-dimensional form.

To get a viola (pansies), you need to knit sequentially first three petals, then the remaining two.





Crochet volumetric flowers: a bouquet for a vase

As the templates for the manufacture of elements for any composition, the above schemes are suitable.





It is enough to complete a certain number of blanks, tie the leaves, sepals, take the sticks or wire for the stems, wrapped with threads of the corresponding shade, assemble everything into one whole, and the crocheted three-dimensional flower is ready.

Using the scheme of roses from the previous section, you can make a bouquet of carnations on the same principle. In this case, the workpiece strip will be longer, and it should not be twisted in a spiral, but in a more complex way, to imitate the texture of the clove. In addition, it is not necessary to tie the petals, as for a rose. The main thing is to understand the principle of making a double flower.

Similar to knitting the viols from the previous section is creating a lily or chamomile.

The principle of operation is that each petal is sequentially executed. All of them fit together one thread. The middle is made separately and sewn on top.





Two options differ in the shape of the petals and the centers. The principle of creation is one.
