St johns wood

The most popular of all areas of the city. It is attractive because it is located in the very center and excellent transport links are built next to it.

The richest people in the city live here. Among them are the owners of the largest banks, shops and shopping centers. Many top managers, lawyers, and diplomats dream of living in this place, trying to build an excellent career in London.

The area is also suitable for couples with children. There are private schools, kindergartens, shops and restaurants. Interestingly, St. John's Wood has the most famous American school, so quite a lot of Americans live here.

Greenwich District

One of the most prestigious areas of London. Located in the eastern part of the city. Of course, everyone knows that this place is famous for the fact that it is here that the zero point of longitude begins.

In addition, in this wonderful quiet area is the Greenwich Observatory, which the whole world knows. The place is also famous for its quiet and cozy parks.

It is believed that absolutely every tourist should visit this area. But unfortunately, it is not at all close to the center, and the road to it will take more than an hour and a half.

Islington

This area is considered ideal for people working in the banking and public sectors. He is adored by the indigenous people of London.

Tower bridge

This area is named after the bridge located in it. The Tower Bridge is believed to be one of London's most popular attractions.

Of course, all the property located near this place is very expensive. As they say, it is in the Tower Bridge that you can feel the atmosphere of the rhythm of life in London, while at the same time see its ancient part.

From here you can easily walk to the most famous buildings of the city. In addition, there are many office buildings, which is not surprising. After all, London is a business city. Mostly well-known television channels and print media are based here.

In addition, the Tower Bridge has plenty of nice bars, pubs and restaurants.

As for residential real estate, there are very few of them, since most of them were bought by well-known world companies. We can say that this area is one of the most business districts of London.

Paddington

The area is located in the very center of London. It is mainly inhabited by bankers and businessmen, since many people in this profession are constantly on the move, and here the railway station is close. It is from here that trains depart to all the main cities of England. In addition, aeroexpress trains to the main and largest UK airport are based at this station.

City

One of the most ancient areas. It was here that the capital of Great Britain was once born. In modern times, it is the largest global financial center, respectively, one of the most prestigious areas of London. By the way, he does not obey royal authority.

Five office workers work five days a week in this area, as many companies are based here. In addition, the City has a sufficient number of restaurants and bars. Many workers like to relax here in the evenings.

But practically no one lives here, on weekends few people can be found on the streets.
