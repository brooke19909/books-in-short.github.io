How to prepare for a studio photo shoot: best ideas and recommendations for clients

Everyone who is going to capture some important event in life, or make a romantic gift for their soulmate, is eager to find out how to prepare for a photo shoot in the studio. However, few people know that almost half of the success depends on thorough preliminary preparation. So it turns out, in many respects the result of the photo shoot will depend not on the photographer, but on yourself. That is why it is so important to read this article in advance, carefully follow all the tips and tricks.

General tips

There are several general recommendations on how to prepare for a photo shoot in the studio, they are suitable for any occasion, for whatever reason you decide to go to film.

First of all, you should choose a good photographer. Be sure to check out his portfolio first. Always remember that photography is also an art. Therefore, the pictures should not only be outwardly attractive, but also cling to you for the living. Even if you don‚Äôt fully understand what exactly you liked in these photos. This is the case when you should trust your emotions and your sixth sense.

Do not pay attention to discounts and profitable promotions. They can only confuse you: you will pay a little less, and in return you will not receive any pleasure and will only be disappointed with the result.

In advance, you should consider the duration and location for shooting, as well as its concept. For this, you may even have to first meet with the photographer to discuss all the points. As a rule, several filming spaces are equipped in the pavilions. Therefore, it does not hurt to imagine in advance what scenery you will be posing.

clothing

Finally, one of the most important points is the clothes. Among the tips on how to prepare for a photo shoot in the studio, you will always find recommendations that your outfit must be combined in style and color. For example, if a girl comes to shoot in a red dress and a young man in a bright green shirt, one should not expect anything good from the photos.

Clothes should be selected noble colors. They should be pleasing to the eye and not irritate. Refrain from drawings, cages and vibrant prints, it is better that dresses and shirts are plain. Everything causing will attract extra attention, distracting from the main thing - your face.

Among the recommendations to clients how to prepare for a photo session in the studio, there are often tips to choose pastel shades. They look very good in the pictures.

Be careful about choosing rich colors. In some cases, they emphasize the image of the model, but with the same success they can destroy it. The option is especially losing when the only bright spot appears in the whole image.

Color

When preparing for a photo shoot, pay special attention to color. The maximum allowable amount is three chromatic. Recall that seven colors of the rainbow and their derivatives are chromatic. Pale shades are also considered chromatic, only they are less saturated. Gray, neutral white and black are considered achromatic, so you can safely add them to this list.

Handle black with care. His perception is multifaceted, in this case a lot will depend on the shape and texture of your clothes.

Of course, you don't have to dress up for a photo shoot. If you come in a boiling-white t-shirt and blue jeans that accentuate your figure, the pictures will be just perfect. However, if an elegant dress has long been hanging in your closet, then this is the case when you should wear it.

The main thing is not to dress, following the principle of choosing all the best that is in the closet. Remember that your outfit must be combined with the style and costume of the other participants in the photo shoot.

The best option: to bring along several costumes, and to change clothes several times during the photo shoot.

Accessories

One of the main tips on how to prepare for a photo shoot in the studio is that you definitely need to bring some accessories with you. In this moment, you can rely on your imagination: colored scarves, hats, umbrellas, glasses, identical T-shirts, fruits, lollipops, balloons, colorful candies.

Of course, this does not mean that in all the pictures you will be with these attributes. But several of these frames greatly diversify the shooting. The accessories that you take with you are best thought out in advance. For example, you can draw inspiration by looking at photo sessions of famous artists, paying attention to bright and original ideas.

Mood

In addition, such original accessories are sure to cheer you up. Good mood is another factor that guarantees you success.

You must want to go on set. If you have a family photo shoot planned, and one of the children is categorically against it, do not force it. The master will be able to provide ideal conditions and light, but he will not be able to do anything with a sour and displeased expression on his face.

An emotional attitude should be positive. Only in this way you can get the desired result.

Appearance

In how to prepare for a photo shoot in the studio, your appearance always plays a big role. It should look almost perfect.

Gentle peels, exfoliating scrubs, moisturizing masks will benefit. But don't overdo it. Remember that brushing a pimple in the editor is much simpler than the ‚Äúred star" that you pick out all over your forehead, trying to squeeze it out.

These tips apply to men. Weathered lips, flaky skin and untidy unshaven will ruin even the best shot. At the same time, refuse especially ‚Äúvigorous‚Äù cosmetic procedures, especially on the eve of shooting.

Use sunburn with extreme caution. When the face indicates that the girl spent a week at sea, and her neck was ashen white, one should not expect anything good from such a survey. Pay attention to your hands. Both men and women are advised to do moisturizing procedures and a neat manicure.

It is better to get rid of glasses. When shooting lenses, glare of different colors is discarded, as a result, multi-colored spots may appear on your face. If glasses are part of your image, remove the glass, leaving only the frame. Such a trick is used even by Hollywood stars.

Hairstyle and smile

Hairstyle should be as simple as possible. Among the tips on how to prepare for a photo shoot in the studio for a girl, masters most often recommend slightly curled loose hair that looks as feminine as possible. Discard the sparkles in hairspray. They shimmer beautifully only in life, and in the pictures they look like dandruff.

Sign up to the dentist for professional whitening a couple of weeks before the photo shoot. Nothing can decorate you like a bright and open smile.

Girls will need makeup. For help, it is better to contact a professional makeup artist who will help to hide all the flaws. If you put your face in order on your own, be sure to apply a tinting base to even out skin color and remove all irregularities. The color of the neck, face and visible part of the chest should be the same. Eyebrows and eyes are recommended to be emphasized, but not too carried away with it.

Family portraits

Always a joyful and exciting event - shooting a large family with children. You should think in advance how to prepare for a photo shoot in the studio with a child. If the baby is still sleeping during the day, you need to consider these periods in order to get into the "window" when he will be in a good mood. If the child is tired, awake for a long time and is naughty, shooting is better not to start.

Prepare the baby in advance, interested in something. Tell him something unusual about the profession of a photographer so that a stranger does not scare him. Do not forget about incentive prizes during the photo shoot. It does not hurt to grab drinks and small snacks.

Thinking about how to prepare for a photo shoot in the studio as a family, be sure to bring your favorite baby toys with you. So you can not only distract his attention, but also make him pleased when he looks through the pictures many years later and sees his favorite things on them.

Think in advance what you could do at the photo shoot. This is the best option how to prepare for a family photo shoot in the studio. For example, if a child likes it when you read it, bring along a blanket or blanket and a large book.

Romantic shooting

Before the wedding and romantic shootings, the models are especially worried, tormented by how to prepare for a photo shoot in the studio. The best ideas are worth their weight in gold, because these are the pictures that you will be reviewing after many years, showing your grown children.

Try on a wedding dress in advance. It should sit perfectly. Make sure you can walk in it without difficulty, without stepping on the hem. It is better to take a pair of stockings with you, as they are often torn.

It is better to tune in to a romantic photo shoot in advance. For this, each couple can have their own ritual: a walk around their favorite places, small souvenirs and surprises, a relaxing massage, a picnic on the eve with a glass of wine. It will be useful to watch a romantic movie. In this case, one should pay attention not so much to the plot as to the picture. This is a great option how to prepare for a photo shoot in the studio. The poses that you spy on the main characters, then you can use in your pictures. Remember how the characters hold hands, kiss each other, stroke on the cheek. If this is a good movie, then the director thought over all these details in advance. So they will be original and highly artistic.

Pregnancy Tips

Girls are especially kind to filming on the eve of the birth of a child. Therefore, it is especially important to know how to prepare for a pregnant studio photo shoot in the studio.

It‚Äôs best to go on set at 28-30 weeks. By this time, the belly will be neatly rounded, while you still have enough strength to withstand the shooting, which, as a rule, lasts at least an hour.

In such photos, knitwear looks good. For example, sweaters, dresses, jumpers. They tend to stretch, which means that there is a good chance that they will fit, even though your sizes have increased. Knitted items will be especially relevant on the eve of the New Year holidays, when clothes correspond to the season.

Another option is stretch clothing. T-shirts, dresses, topics T-shirt organically take the form of your body. Good idea to use flowers. They will add brightness to the photos, organically complement the image of a young woman.

Shooting day

Be sure to get enough sleep on the eve of shooting, you should have the look of a well-rested person. Be sure to eat, hungry models get tired quickly, there is a desire to finish everything faster, in such a situation it is difficult to count on a romantic mood.

To relax, it does not hurt to take a flask with you. A sip of cognac or a glass of wine can liberate a person in a few moments, adding originality to the pictures.

Come on time, and preferably in advance, at least a quarter of an hour, laying a little time on any force majeure that may arise.
