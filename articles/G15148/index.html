Guest House "Villa Laguna" (Gelendzhik, Russia): overview, description and reviews of tourists

There is a place in the resort of Gelendzhik, perfect in every way. Good environmental conditions, silence, tranquility and at the same time developed urban infrastructure make this area very popular among holidaymakers. It is called "Blue Bay" (Gelendzhik). Villa Laguna is a guest house located in the heart of this elite area of ​​the Black Sea resort. This article will be devoted to his review. To make a description of the mini-hotel, we analyzed the reviews of those tourists who happened to spend their holidays in one of the rooms of the “Villa Laguna”.



Where is Villa Laguna located (guest house, 3 *)

Gelendzhik, the microdistrict "Blue Bay", st. Kiparisova, 2 - this is the address of this mini-hotel. Despite the fact that the central part of the resort is quite far away, tourists did not call this a problem. There is a bus stop right outside the walls of Villa Laguna Guest House. A trip to the center takes about fifteen minutes. Kiparisova street is very quiet and calm, built up with private houses. "Villa Laguna" is easy to find, it stands out by its very appearance. She stands on the corner where the road makes a turn. This is a three-story building in cream color with beautiful brickwork. It was built in the fourteenth year, and its novelty pleases the eye. It takes only ten minutes to get here from Gelendzhik Airport . If you arrive at the resort by train, then the trip from the Novorossiysk train station will take less than an hour.



Territory

"Villa Laguna" (Gelendzhik) consists of one residential building on three floors. The fourth level is adapted for a solarium. It is good to take sunbaths after you refresh yourself in the pool. Incidentally, it is located in front of the house. The building of the mini-hotel is built in the shape of the letter "G". Half of the rooms thus have a shared balcony overlooking the pool. If you like silence, reviews of tourists are advised to choose a room with windows on the road. This highway is infrequently used, and good nights are guaranteed. And companies can sit by the pool, the noise of which is amplified by the echo of the courtyard. Tourists characterize the territory of the guest house as well-groomed, clean. In the courtyard and on the roof-solarium there are barbecue facilities, where for a fee you can cook yourself a barbecue or grilled dish. Spiral staircases with forged railings lead to the upper floors. These rooms open onto a shared balcony. Guests occupying rooms on the lower floor have priority individual access to the pool.



Number of rooms. Categories

Mini-hotel "Villa Laguna" (Gelendzhik) has thirty-four rooms, equipped in such a way that guests feel comfortable and cozy in them. The rooms are divided into categories. The lowest of them is the “standard economy”. The room is designed for double or triple occupancy. It has one double and one single bed. “Economies” are located in the basement of the building. The next step in the rank of the room stock of the Villa Laguna guest house is the standard without a balcony. With the same area as the “house-keeper” (eighteen square meters), filling is better there. The name "standard with a balcony" speaks for itself. Junior Suite consists of a bedroom and a seating area. This room is twenty-six square meters. It adjoins an extensive balcony with sea views. The room has additional amenities: mini fridge, kettle, microwave. There are two types of suites in the guest house. The first is ordinary, and the second is with a kitchenette. There are also apartments in the hotel. Fans of luxury vacations can choose the usual one, with a balcony, consisting of a kitchen, a bedroom, a living room and a bathroom. But there is something more abrupt in Villa Lagoon. This is a duplex apartment. Its total area is 74 square meters. m. On the ground floor there is an entrance hall and a spacious bathroom with a washing machine and bathtub. On the upper level there is a bedroom and a living room. The latter has a full kitchen with a dining area, a sofa, armchairs and a plasma TV. This apartment features panoramic sea views.



Room features

We have already considered the premises of the highest categories. But even in the usual "standards" and even in the "economy" the guests of the Villa Laguna guesthouse (Gelendzhik) feel quite comfortable. The lowest category room has a central climate control system, mini fridge, LCD TV, hallway with mirror, coffee table, wardrobe. The bathroom "economy", "standards" and "deluxe" has a shower. Cleaned in rooms every day. There were no complaints about the quality of the maids. On the fifth day, bed linen is changed. Tourists say that if some kind of breakdown occurs in the rooms, it is quickly eliminated.



Nutrition

Guest house "Villa Laguna" (Gelendzhik) reviews are described as BB ("Bed and Breakfast"). But breakfast is not always included in the price, but only in the summer and in the velvet season. In May and October you can order morning meals, including room service. Breakfast is served in the cafe “Laguna”, which is located in a dormitory building. Reviews of tourists claim that the set of dishes for a morning meal is quite large. Nobody went hungry. The inn offers a half or full board for a fee. Cafe chefs specialize in European cuisine. Reviews mention delicious strudel, Viennese beef steak. The portions are large, the dishes are delicious. Prices are average at the resort. Blue Bay is a quiet sleeping area. There are not many cafes there. Therefore, reviews of tourists recommend to lodge in your hotel - convenient, tasty, cheap, safe.



Pool

Not a single photograph of the territory of the Villa Laguna guesthouse (Gelendzhik) taken by tourists is complete without this reservoir of turquoise water. The hotel's swimming pool is spacious. It is designed as a lagoon and has flowing shapes. In addition, given that the pool is in the shade, it is heated. Tourists consider this a huge plus. But this is not all the advantages of the pool. It has the functions of a jacuzzi (hydromassage). And calcite deposits in the cervical region of the spine will break the cascade. At the fence separating the guest house from the neighboring building, there are several sunbeds. But you can sunbathe on them only at noon, when the sun is at its zenith. It will be more comfortable to settle in a rooftop solarium. There are also sunbeds and canopies. The pool, as the administration of the mini-hotel assures, is cleaned with a special modern means, so that the smell of bleach is not felt.

Sea

To the public beach to go the most leisurely step for about seven minutes. According to reviews, the guest house "Villa Laguna" (Gelendzhik) is located almost on the shore. This pebble beach is well protected from storms by capes, so there are no waves. It is quite equipped - there are changing cabins, you can rent sunbeds. Pebbles are medium in size, shallow at the edge of the sea. There are many people on the beach, but this does not affect the quality of the water. After all, the "Blue Bay" was and remains the most environmentally friendly area of ​​the resort of Gelendzhik. There are activities on the beach such as riding a banana or catamarans. Boat trips and other recreational activities are arranged. Reviews do not recommend going to the left or right of the public beach to the “wild” places, because they are not cleaned there, and you can step on the glass.



Service and Facilities

Hotel "Villa Laguna" (Gelendzhik) positions itself as a three-star hotel. In the basement of the building there is a garage where guests can park their car for a fee. And near the guest house there is parking, which is under 24-hour video surveillance. There you can park your car for free. Of the services, the reviews recall the Internet access included in the room rate. But if you have a “bad life without Wi-Fi,” tourists recommend booking a room on the ground floor - the only router is at the reception. Speaking of front desk. The girls there are so kind that they will help you book excursions around the city and the surrounding area of ​​Gelendzhik, organize fishing on a yacht, horseback riding or a ride on ATVs. At the reception there is also a luggage storage room - free for guests. The most popular service at the bed & breakfast is the wood-heated sauna with private pool and relaxation room. This service, like bicycle rental, is paid. But you can absolutely freely take table tennis equipment from the administration. For a fee, you can take the clothes to the laundry and order a transfer to any point in the Krasnodar Territory.

Family facilities

Many people know that this is a children's resort - Gelendzhik. Hotel "Villa Laguna" has provided for its youngest guests a shallow area in the swimming pool, as well as a games room filled with toys. But there is no mini-club in the hotel, and children can play there under the supervision of their parents. The breakfast included in the price always consists of some kind of dish that is useful to the growing body. There are milk porridges, cereals, and pancakes. Children under the age of five can stay at the hotel for free, provided that they do not eat in the cafe. Breakfast and other meals require additional funds.

Prices

Tourists claim that "Villa Laguna 3 *" (Gelendzhik) meets its value. In the 2016 season, prices were affordable there. The whole issue of Econom in May and October cost two thousand rubles. And in the midst of the season, its cost reached 3200 rubles. The most popular category of rooms in a mini-hotel is "standard". Without a balcony, it cost from 2,200 to three and a half thousand rubles a day. The presence of an add-on outside the window increased its cost by four hundred rubles a day. As for the "apartments", the price is high at any time. In the low season, it is five, and in the high - six and a half thousand rubles per day.

"Villa Laguna" Gelendzhik: reviews

Tourists were pleased with their stay at the mini-hotel. They praised its location relative to the sea. Everyone liked the heated pool and wood-fired sauna. The rooms are nicely decorated and clean. The staff is friendly, trying to please everything. The cafe is very tasty food. Many independent tourists, having tried the cuisine at local catering points, opted for the dining room of Villa Laguna. Most of the tourists who wrote reviews did not visit the guesthouse for the first time. They claim that when they visit again, they are entitled to a ten percent discount.
