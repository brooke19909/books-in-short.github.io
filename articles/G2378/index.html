5 Common Beginner Mistakes

Life insurance is unreliable.

It is possible to reduce risks in long-term financing if you choose a company that adheres to a conservative approach to investments: it invests reserves in government securities and corporate bonds.

Life insurance is an investment, and only then protection.

This service aims to reduce risks in case of health problems. Although endowment insurance allows you to generate some capital, but making a profit is not the main goal. This service is financially attractive due to tax deduction and income indexation.


The only thing you should focus on is the amount of the insurance premium.

When choosing a program, several factors must be taken into account: the purpose of the investment (protection or accumulation), a set of risks, and the duration of the contract. Depending on these parameters, the optimal product is selected.

It is better to buy an insurance program with a minimum set of risks.

Such products provide for the payment of reimbursement as a result of death or in the event that the client has lived to the end of the term of the policy. They cannot demonstrate all the benefits of the service or financially protect a person in the event of injury, disability.

Outdated information.

Once a year, you need to revise the contract to ensure the accuracy and correctness of the data. If life circumstances have changed (marriage, divorce, childbirth, increased income), then you need to adjust the program so that the insurance amount can cover the financial needs of families. By the way, untimely updating of data very often serves as a legitimate refusal to pay compensation. This is especially true in cases where the beneficiary has not been changed (for example, as a result of divorce of spouses).
