Poseidon Beach on the Sea of ​​Azov: general characteristics and reviews

A very popular holiday destination is the Sea of ​​Azov for many Russians. Sandy beaches, warm clear water, developed infrastructure - all this attracts tourists here. And especially the Taman Peninsula, where there are many unusual and interesting vacation spots. One of them is Poseidon Beach. This is a whole tourist complex, offering tourists not only relaxation, swimming in the sea, but also a lot of entertainment and even recreation.

general characteristics

The Taman Peninsula has long attracted tourists. It attracts tourists here mainly the sea, which warms up quickly, and long sandy beaches. All this territory is well-groomed and ennobled, although not all beaches are private. But one of the private stands out among others for its unusualness. It is decorated in the ancient Greek style. This is Poseidon Beach . The address of this tourist complex can be easily found in any brochure: Temryuk district, the village of Peresyp. The beach is located in the most visited place of the Taman Peninsula - near Peresyp.

This is the best vacation spot on the Sea of ​​Azov. It is impossible to drive past this beach - snow-white columns and antique statues are visible from afar. This place tells the story of the Taman Peninsula, which was connected with Ancient Greece. In addition to a beautifully landscaped area, Poseidon beach attracts tourists with a developed infrastructure. Here you can not only relax, but also have fun.



How to get to Poseidon Beach

Quite often, excursions come to this tourist complex. Mud pools with healing mud and the nearby ostrich farm are especially attractive for tourists. The tour program also often includes visits to the sturgeon farm, lotus valley and the Tizdar mud volcano. Vacationers from all over the Taman Peninsula, the Kuban and even from the Crimea go to Peresyp. And a must-see place for any excursion is Poseidon Beach. After all, here is the only oceanarium in the vicinity.

But a lot of vacationers come to Azov on their own. It is easy to find out the address of Poseidon beach: Temryuk district, Peresyp village. The complex itself is located at the entrance to the village, on Kalabadka street, which runs along a narrow strip between the Sea of ​​Azov and Kutepov estuary. The most convenient way to get to the beach is by car. Entrance to the territory is paid, but arriving in the morning and paying 400 rubles, you can stay for the whole day.

You can also get by public transport or taxi. First, in any way you need to get to Temryuk. There are buses from all directions: from the Crimea, Krasnodar, Novorossiysk. Shuttles from Temryuk to Peresyp. The beach itself is located near the village, but you can ask the driver to stop on the track near the entrance, as the bus passes by the beach.



Beach infrastructure

The tourist complex "Poseidon" is suitable for families. The main thing that attracts tourists here is, of course, the beach itself. It is wide, sandy-shell, equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. The approach to the sea is shallow, near the coast the depth is very shallow. Therefore, this beach is an ideal place for families with children. The water is quite clean and warms up very quickly, especially since the Taman Peninsula is a very sunny and warm place.

The beach offers vacationers such services that are included in the price of the entrance ticket or are paid separately:

  • umbrellas with sunbeds and benches for relaxation;
  • left-luggage offices;
  • showers and toilets;
  • locker rooms;
  • small zoo corner;
  • playground with rides;
  • inflatable slides, bananas and catamarans;
  • first-aid post and rescuers;
  • cozy and inexpensive cafe.

Wellness treatments

The main thing that attracts tourists to the Poseidon beach is the spa treatments. The cost of any of them is 250 rubles. There is no mud volcano here, but therapeutic mud is imported directly from Tizdar. There are two swimming pools on the beach for mud baths, beautifully surrounded by Greek columns. This healing volcanic mud has a unique effect on the body: rejuvenates, relieves pain, removes toxins, stimulates metabolic processes.

In addition to mud baths, the beach offers a unique procedure - wine and beer baths. They also have a healing effect on the body. Immersed for 15 minutes in a wooden barrel with natural red wine, you can improve well-being. Such baths accelerate metabolic processes, have a beneficial effect on the skin, well relax and relieve stress.



Beach activities

To fully enjoy all the attractions of the Taman Peninsula and Poseidon Beach, it is better to book a tour. But you can visit interesting places on the territory of the tourist complex itself and next to it yourself. What kind of entertainment does the Poseidon beach offer?

  • Wine tasting is the hallmark of the Taman Peninsula. It is proposed to try exquisite Fanagor wines, and what you like is to buy.
  • The oceanarium, a visit to which costs 350 rubles, although small, but interesting.
  • An ostrich farm near the beach provides an opportunity for everyone to not only get acquainted with the life of these amazing birds, but also to feed them with their hands.
  • In the evenings, on the beach, tourists are offered the entertaining folklore program "Kuban Vareniki". On it you can not only learn a lot of interesting things about the life of the Kuban Cossacks, but also try their national dishes.

Reviews about Poseidon Beach

Vacationers leave a lot of positive reviews about this place. Many people like a well-maintained beach with clean sand and warm water. This is mainly written by tourists with children. It is an ideal place to relax for toddlers. But many people resting on the Sea of ​​Azov come here specifically to take mud or wine baths. They note that from such procedures amazing feelings remain.
