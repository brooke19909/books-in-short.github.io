Dzhubga: Hotels, motels and hostels. Photos and reviews

Have you decided to choose the village of Dzhubga for your vacation ? The hotels available on its territory will offer the best conditions for relaxation. However, before deciding on one of them, it is important to learn as much as possible about the option you like.





Hotel "Arcadia"

Do you dream of a quality and comfortable vacation on the very shore of the Black Sea, away from city noise? Then there is no better option for accommodation than the Arcadia Hotel (Dzhubga). At a distance of 350 m from the hotel there is a luxurious beach, within walking distance are an aqua park, a bus station, a market, numerous cafes and shops.

As for the territory of the hotel itself, it is also able to please the guests of Arcadia. There is a swimming pool, billiards, playground, table tennis, Wi-Fi zone, gazebos and barbecue facilities. And if you get bored of active pastime among the many vacationers, you can retire to the library.

Vacationers can stay in one of the 25 rooms of the hotel. Settlement is made by 2, 3 or 4 people. There are no single rooms. All rooms are equipped with a bathroom with a shower, a toilet and a washbasin, a split system, a TV, necessary furniture and a refrigerator. Three meals a day are offered in the dining room at the hotel, which seats up to ninety people.





Hotel "Gosteev". general description

Will Dzhubga be your summer vacation destination? The hotels in this village will be able to offer the best living conditions. So, "Gosteev" is a modern four-story building with a luxurious adjoining territory, on which is a swimming pool.

Three meals a day are organized in the hotel dining room, there is a bar. It is also pleasing to vacationers that there is a modern water park near the hotel, and you will have to spend no more than ten minutes on the road to the beach if you go at a slow pace.

Guest house "Turquoise"

Convenient location is the first thing that attracts tourists to the Turquoise guest house. To get to it, you need to move along the federal highway, which passes through the village of Dzhubga. The Korona Hotel, located near the road, is an excellent landmark. Having traveled about 100 meters ahead of the Crown, you will need to turn left and overcome another 200 meters. On the left side is a light green house with a luxurious territory, buried in flowers and trees. The youngest guests are invited to play in the sandbox or on the specially equipped playground. Adults at this time will be able to fry the barbecue and enjoy its great taste, admiring the stunning views of the sea, which opens from the gazebo.

The hotel rooms are designed for 2-5 people. Each of them is equipped with a bathroom, new furniture, comfortable beds and air conditioning. There is a kitchen on the floor where guests of Biryuzy can cook for themselves and their family. Hotel Hestia

"Hestia" is a hotel with all the conditions for an upscale holiday. Architecture, level of equipment and service - everything here is at the highest level. The sandy and pebble beach of Dzhubgi , 800 meters long, is located 300 meters from the hotel, so that at any time you can be on the seashore in a few minutes, leaving the Hotel Hestia. Dzhubga has a magnificent water park, which is also located next to the hotel. For those traveling on their own cars, Hestia offers a free parking equipped with a video surveillance system.

For accommodation, the hotel has one- and two-room "Suites", as well as apartments of the "Deluxe" class. Guests will not need anything. As for food, the hotel chef delights guests with delicious dishes in the morning or throughout the day - at the request of guests. The hotel also has all the conditions for preparing aromatic barbecue and cozy places for eating it, accompanied by local wine.





Amazing corner

Many great places for photographs can be offered by Dzhubga. Hotels often have rooms with windows or balconies that offer wonderful views of the village and the sea. On the territory of most hotels there are many flowers, trees, gazebos, swings and benches.

The length of the sand and pebble beach of Dzhubga is more than eight hundred meters, and here everyone can enjoy the gentle southern sun. In addition, there are many opportunities for active pastime on the beach. Slides, water rides, catamarans, etc. provide an unforgettable vacation.

Vacationers reviews about hotels in Dzhubga

Today, many beach lovers from Russia and neighboring countries have already managed to appreciate all the advantages that Dzhubga has. Hotels vying with each other offer the best conditions for spending summer vacations. Nevertheless, summarizing the reviews of tourists, we can say that some hotels met their expectations, while others did not.





So, most travelers praise the Arcadia Hotel. They mention its green area, spacious rooms, delicious food and attentive staff. As for the Gosteev hotel, tourists disagree about it. Some believe that more worthy options can be found if Dzhubga became the place for a summer vacation. The hotels of this village in terms of beauty of the territory, comfort of rooms, cleanliness and location, as a rule, are on the same level as Gostev, but some of them can offer better food. Vacationers at this hotel complain about small portions, insufficient amounts of meat and fish.

As for Hestia, it is worth noting that some vacationers complain about the rare cleaning of the room and the lack of dishes in the kitchen. Although the rest of the guests are satisfied.

Conclusion

Hotel reviews may not be the same, as all people have different requests. Therefore, choosing one or another option, it is worth being guided not only by the opinions of vacationers, but also by your own requirements for a comfortable stay. And let your vacation in Dzhubga become unforgettable!
