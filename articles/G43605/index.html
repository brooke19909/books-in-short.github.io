Hotel Almiros Apartments 3 *, Corfu: overview and reviews

Corfu is the most popular of all the Ionian islands of Greece. It attracts vacationers not only enchanting nature, many historical attractions and clean beaches. This island is also popular with tourists all over the world, thanks to the well-developed infrastructure of local resorts. To rent good housing in Corfu, if necessary, is absolutely no problem. For example, good rooms for rent on the island in Almiros Apartments 3 *.

Hotel Location Features

The largest settlement closest to this hotel is Corfu. The distance between the hotel Almiros Apartments 3 * and this resort is 39 km. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel there is also a quite popular resort village of Acharavi.





Russian tourists usually fly to Greece, of course, by plane. The nearest international airport is 40 km away. Typically, domestic tour operators provide their customers who have bought permits with accommodation in this hotel, a shuttle bus. Therefore, a trip to the hotel from the airport should not cause any special problems. If you wish, you can also get to the complex and take a taxi.

Dining at the hotel

Rooms for rent at Almiros Apartments 3 * hotels (Corfu, Acharavi) , unfortunately, without breakfast or a guesthouse. There is no private dining room at this hotel. But, as already mentioned, each room has its own kitchen. So, hotel residents can cook on their own. There are absolutely all conditions for this. If necessary, tourists can buy products nearby. There is a small supermarket directly opposite the hotel. True, judging by the reviews of vacationers, the products here are somewhat expensive. Therefore, those tourists who want to save money should visit the Dilos hypermarket in Acharavi. It sells meat, milk, cereals and other products at affordable prices.





To get to Acharavi, if desired, you can, including walking. In this case, the journey will take about 25 minutes. However, the road to this busy resort town from the hotel leads, unfortunately, not too convenient for people to travel. It is not equipped with sidewalks and has a small width. At the same time, cars can ride on it at a rather high speed. In addition, on this road there are many areas unlit in the evenings. So, walking to Acharavi from the hotel, among other things, may also be unsafe.

To stay at Almiros Apartments 3 * hotel as comfortable as possible, experienced tourists advise beginners to rent a car in Acharavi. This service is worth it here, like everywhere in Greece, very inexpensive. If there is a car, the residents of the hotel will be able not only to quickly purchase all the necessary cooking products, but also to see many interesting sights about. Corfu

Those tourists who do not want to spend time on vacation cooking can eat at any of the many cafes in Acharavi.

Where can I rent a car?

This is of course inexpensive. However, rental offices in Acharavi are usually asked to make, among other things, a deposit of $ 300. This is a tourist worth a visit to Almiros Apartments 3 * . After renting a rented car, the pledge, judging by the reviews of vacationers, returns in all offices without any problems.
