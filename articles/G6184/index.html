How to make a dog costume for a child?

Traditionally, at New Year's morning performances in kindergarten or in elementary school, teachers and educators arrange performances with children, the characters of which are animals, including dogs, with their children.

If your child has such a role, then he will need the appropriate outfit. And do not rush to look for a salon where you can rent a dog costume. With your own hands for a child to make it is quite simple, just be patient and turn on your imagination.

What is required

The dog costume should consist of:

  • shirts;
  • spacious trousers with a tail;
  • hats with ears;
  • fur vest.

This will require:

  • a simple, opaque fabric for trousers and a hat, for example, gabardine light brown, beige or light gray;
  • satin or crepe satin for the design of the dog‚Äôs ears and appliqu√©;
  • faux fur color matching the shade of gabardine, from which the vest, tail, ears and trim will be sewn;
  • satin ribbon for collar;
  • wide elastic;
  • bugles and sequins for decorating the edges of the applique.




Where to begin

To make a dog costume, you will need a sewing machine and basic patterns of a children's vest (jacket) and trousers (shorts).

Next, using patterns, cut out the necessary details. It should turn out:

  • 2 shelves;
  • the back of the vest;
  • 2 parts of the front and back of the trousers.

They are sewn together, as usual, and cuts are made so that the suit looks neat and you are not ashamed of your work.

We sew a dog costume: the procedure for making a fur vest

For sewing this part of the outfit, dogs proceed as follows:

  • carefully so that the pile is not damaged, they cut the fur with sharp scissors;
  • grind shoulder and side seams;
  • the sides, the bottom of the vest and the neckline are tucked and hemmed on a typewriter or manually;
  • sew a secret button to the shelves;
  • make a loop for the clasp.

If you sew well, you can independently make a tunic with sleeves of the simplest cut. Otherwise, you can use a shirt, t-shirt or turtleneck of a suitable color, even white, but always without drawings or inscriptions.

Trousers

As for the bottom, it is made as follows:

  • grind side seams of trouser parts;
  • ‚Äúbones‚Äù are cut out of a fabric of contrasting color;

  • if there is a desire, the edges are decorated with sequins;
  • grind stepping seams, and then, putting the leg in one in the other, also the middle one;
  • make a drawstring along the upper section of the trousers;
  • the bottom is tucked and hemmed;
  • an elastic tape (elastic band) is inserted into the drawstring.

Tail

When the trousers are ready, the dog's costume needs to be supplemented with the necessary accessory. It is a tail that can be made from the remains of fur. In order to make it, perform a series of actions:

  • take a rectangular piece, fold it with fur inward along the length and sew it into a pipe;
  • turn out and manually, using a needle and thread, give the workpiece the shape of a tail;
  • sew in the back of the trousers.
