Often tourists come to this ancient city with a very specific purpose - to get acquainted with its sights. Indeed, there is something to see here: this is the ancient Kremlin and Trinity Cathedral, the Gremyachaya Tower and Dovmontov city, as well as a huge number of museums and galleries. There is a famous philharmonic society and a planetarium, so if you are interested in the history of Russia, it is best to start studying it from this city. This means that you will need to study the hotels in Pskov in order to choose a decent place to stay.
