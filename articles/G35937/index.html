Who can take a loan?

Any resident of the city who has a constant source of income and work experience of at least three months can take a loan. The borrower must be 18 years old. How to get a loan?

Where to get a loan in Omsk is easy to find out. Now let's talk about how to do it right and what documents will be needed. To obtain a cash loan, a prerequisite is the presence of a passport of a citizen of the Russian Federation. You can contact the bank in person or fill out an application on the site. Experts recommend doing this online and submitting requests to several banks at once.

In addition to a passport, a second document of your choice is required to obtain a loan. To increase the chances of approval, you can submit additional documents to the bank. Additional documents may include:

- certificate of ownership of property (real estate or TCP), this is useful when applying for a loan against bail;
