DIY pulse chargers: schemes, instructions, reviews

Sometimes the battery in the car discharges very quickly. As a result, you have to use various devices in order to start the car. Today, pulsed chargers are very popular. The main producers are considered to be Sonar and Bosch.

However, some people cannot afford to buy these devices because they are expensive. In such a situation, you can try to assemble a model yourself. In order to understand pulse charging, you need to look at the standard circuit of the device.

Scheme of a conventional charging model

Circuit of pulse chargers for car batteries include a transformer with a magnetic circuit, as well as transistors. To adjust the voltage, regulators are used that are connected to the modulators. Also, the pulse charger circuit includes special triggers. Their main task is to increase voltage stability. There are clips on the charger for connecting the device. Electricity itself is supplied through a cable.





6 V device: diagram and instructions

Making a 6V pulse charger yourself is quite simple. For this purpose, a small platform is being constructed for the transformer. It is also necessary to prepare insulators in advance. Directly the transformer is often used power type. The conductivity of the current on average is 6 microns. It is also important to note that the system is able to cope with increased negative resistance. Oscillators are used pulse type.

For normal operation of the device, a linear tetrode is also required. It should be selected with a lining. Some experts strongly recommend the use of filters. Thus, it is possible to stabilize the voltage when the overloads in the network exceed the mark of 20 V. In operation, the instruction of the pulse charger is very simple. To connect the device, clamps are required. In this case, plug the plug into a power outlet.

How to make a charger on 10 V?

Pulse charger circuits for car batteries include step-down transformers. To begin the assembly of the model should be with the search for a quality transformer. In this case, a powerful magnetic circuit is required. Still in the circuit of pulse chargers for batteries include insulators. Many experts install regulators with modulators. Thus, the input voltage index can be reduced or increased. In this case, much depends on the power of the car battery.

Directly tetrodes are used only with plates. Resistors are used expansion type. Some modifications have triggers. These elements allow you to cope with the short-wave interference that occurs in an AC network with a sharp increase in the clock frequency.





12 V Model Reviews

Impulse chargers for 12 V batteries are in great demand these days. If you believe the reviews of experts, then step-down transformers are used to assemble the model. The oscillator in this case will be required with high current conductivity. It is also important to note that only trim triggers are suitable for models.

Tetrodes, in turn, are of a linear type. The parameter of permissible overload in devices does not exceed 15 watts. The rated current index is on average 4 A. Magnetic cores for models are installed behind transformers. Especially for them, it is necessary to choose high-quality insulators. To connect the charger you need clamps. If you believe the experts, you should consider that it will be quite difficult to make them yourself.





Single Phase Modifications

You can make a single-phase pulse charger yourself on the basis of a step-down transformer. Regulators are also used to assemble them. Modulators in this case are suitable only for a switched type. Triggers are installed directly with insulators. Some experts also recommend the use of rubber pads.

Tetrodes are selected with high bandwidth. Regulators are mounted above the modulator. In this case, three resistors are required. They must withstand the rated voltage at around 10 V. Metal clamps will be needed to connect the prior.

Biphasic devices

A two-phase automatic pulse charger is pretty easy to assemble. However, in this situation, you can not do without a power transformer. Also, only expansion resistors are used for assembly. The input voltage in the network, as a rule, does not exceed 12 V. Thyristors for models are used with insulators. The modulator is mounted directly on the lining. The controller in this case is suitable for the rotary type. Magnetic cores are used to overcome interference. Devices of this type are connected through a wire. From a 220 V network, they can also work. To connect to batteries, clamps are required.

Reviews of three-phase modification

Three-phase pulse charger expert reviews are good. The advantage of models is that they can withstand more overload. Magnetic cores in this case are installed with a conductivity of 6 microns. Linear resistors are used to stabilize the output voltage. In some cases, code analogs are also installed. However, their service life is not long.

It is also important to note that the voltage limit in the devices should be regulated using modulators. They are installed right behind the transformers. Trigger triggers are used to overcome magnetic interference. Many experts recommend installing filters to assemble chargers. These elements will help to significantly reduce the negative resistance parameter in the circuit.

Model with transformer KU2

Charging equipment with transformer KU5

Dual Choke Model

Chargers (pulse) with dual chokes allow you to use more than two modulators. Thus, it is possible to install digital voltage regulators.
