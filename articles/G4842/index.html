So, to make this composition, you need to take pebbles 10-15 cm in diameter. It will take not so much time to make several of these bugs - no more than two or three hours. Wash pebbles and dry before starting work. To make a ladybug from stone, use an acrylic primer that dries quickly and perfectly hides all the bumps. It can be found in a construction or art store or made independently. To do this, you will need to mix water and PVA glue in a one to one proportion. Depending on the material chosen, one or two coats of primer will be required. When it dries, you should paint the ladybug made of stone with white acrylic paint , diluting it with a small amount of water. The next step will be the drawing of the contour of the future pattern. A simple pencil is suitable for this. Now you can move on to the most interesting - murals.

Painting





Paint best with acrylic paints. Brushes fit both natural and synthetic. To draw wings, you need red paint. When they dry, paint them a second time. This must be done to make the picture look more vivid. We paint over the head and that part of the body that is hidden by wings with black paint. Then we draw her points and contour lines between the wings. To make the ladybug made of stone even closer to the original, we apply blue streaks on white paint, which can be obtained by mixing white and blue. Then draw the face. For the design of the eyes, choose green or blue, for the mouth - white, and do not forget about the cilia! Now the ladybug has become like a fun cartoon character.
