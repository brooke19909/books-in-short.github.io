Hotel complex "Mira"

The Mira Hotel in Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk is considered one of the most fashionable. On its territory there is a swimming pool and even a solarium, plus all other infrastructure facilities designed to fulfill any whim of customers.

Meals are served in the luxurious Pavarotti Restaurant. Here you can taste dishes of not only Russian, but also Italian cuisine. White furniture, which is furnished with an institution, sets up a calm cozy atmosphere. For a bite to eat, guests can check out the snack bar and lounge area, which also serves light drinks. The Mira Hotel can be found at Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk, Mira Ave., 255.

Belka hotel

The Belka Hotel in Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk is a unique hotel complex, the project of which belongs to Japanese architects. The hotel is built of wood. The hotel is located in the city center near the main attractions. The train station is just a 10-minute walk away. The airport is only 20 minutes away. 2 km from the hotel there is a ski slope.

The hotel accommodation fund consists of standard rooms, family apartments, suites and rooms for honeymooners. Each is equipped with sleeping places, comfortable furniture.

The hotel provides its customers a variety of services. You can enjoy delicious dishes of Russian, European and Japanese cuisine in a local cafe, which is usually called a tavern. You can also visit the sauna with several pools, a Finnish bath, aromatherapy, a relaxation room and delicious food. The complex provides its services phytocenter.

This hotel complex tourists fell in love with for its unusual atmosphere. Wood trim creates a warm, cozy atmosphere and contributes to relaxation and good rest. The location of the hotel is also considered its important advantage in the eyes of tourists.

Belka Hotel is located at: Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk, st. Khabarovskaya, 29 B.

Hotel "Gagarin"

The three-star hotel "Gagarin" (Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk) is an ideal option for relaxing couples and business people. It has an excellent location in the very center of the city. All kinds of offices, cultural facilities, shopping centers are located nearby.

The rooms are combined European and Oriental style. The categories of rooms are different, but each of them has a minibar, TV, telephone, dressing room, two tables, a bed and a bathroom with all the necessary accessories. The hotel has several places to eat. It offers a restaurant with traditional cuisine, a cafe "Wasabi", a karaoke bar. Refined natures like to taste cocktails in the unusual bar "133" with an elegant design. A conference room is available for business meetings.

About Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk Gagarin Hotel leave a lot of positive reviews. Many business people prefer to stay in this hotel, as there is everything you need for a short stay. For families, this hotel is also suitable. It has good food, comfortable rooms and good service.

Hotel "Gagarin" is located at: st. Komsomolskaya, 133.

The hotel complex Pacific Plaza

Hotel Plaza (Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk) is considered the most popular in the city. It is located on the main street, within walking distance of the local administration and the main theater. Nearby is the railway station and airport. The rooms offer fabulous views of the mountain peaks and the city.

The choice of rooms in the hotel is replete with variety. The hotel complex has 149 fully equipped comfortable rooms of various categories. Here you can find both a budget standard and a luxurious suite.

In each room you can count on a comfortable bed with high-quality linens. The bathroom has toiletries, a hairdryer and a bathrobe. The rooms are locked with a combination lock. Guests can count on round-the-clock service. The security system also works continuously. Hotel guests can contact the administration at any time for any question. With special cordiality and attention of visitors, workers of all infrastructure objects are greeted. An iron and ironing board are available on request, as well as direct food to your room. Car owners can park their vehicles in the parking lot for free. A fitness room and a VIP lounge are also included.

The Pacific Plaza Hotel in Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk has several restaurants and bars. The main restaurant serves a buffet three times a day. You can order dishes of world cuisines. The restaurant "Valentine" offers a wide selection of wines, as well as all kinds of goodies. Guests can enjoy light snacks at the lounge bar and enjoy a variety of cocktails. In the lobby there is a lobby bar where guests can enjoy fresh pastries and hot drinks.

From the reviews of visitors to the hotel, we can conclude that this place is loved for its accessibility, good location, excellent service and beautiful views.

The Pacific Plaza Hotel is located at 172 Mira Ave.
