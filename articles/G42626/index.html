Sochi, "Marine distance" (park hotel). Tourists reviews

In the light of recent world events, domestic resorts are becoming more and more in demand every year. The Black Sea coast of the Krasnodar Territory is especially popular, because there is everything that tourists need for a good rest: a warm and gentle sea, picturesque nature, developed infrastructure and a huge selection of hospitable guest houses, hotels, hotels and resorts.

Rest in the village of Golovinka

The small cozy village of Golovinka is one of the resorts of the well-known Greater Sochi. Golovinka is located forty kilometers from the city of Sochi and belongs to the Lazarevsky district. If you look at the map, you can see that the village is located on both sides of the Shakha river, which descends from the mountains and flows into the Black Sea.





Golovinka is a quiet and calm village. Every year it becomes more and more popular. The infrastructure is developing, new hotels are being built, the territory is being improved. However, this place remains clean and peaceful. There is no fuss, dirt and noise of big cities. Harmony with nature and a relaxing holiday are the main characteristics of a local holiday.

Wide pebble beaches, clear sea, crystal mountain air and pristine wilderness - these are the factors for which tourists come here. Fans of nightclubs, restaurants and beach parties, perhaps, need to choose a resort with a more developed infrastructure, especially since there are many such in the Krasnodar Territory.

Golovinka is suitable for a quiet family vacation. The local population is famous for its hospitality and friendliness.

Another, no less important advantage of vacation in this village is the cost of housing, which is much lower than in more developed resort cities.

Morskaya Dal - hotel in the village of Golovinka

In the quiet and cozy village of Golovinka, Morskaya Dal boarding house (park hotel) is waiting for vacationers. It is located near the sea. The shore in this place is steep and high, so the boarding house is located on a hill with a beautiful seascape.





Park Hotel Morskaya Dal (Sochi) It has a large territory (more than four hectares) and is literally surrounded by greenery. Vacationers in the guest complex can enjoy the subtropical vegetation, which is represented here in abundance.

The boarding house is open all year round according to the All Inclusive system.

Accommodation for vacationers

Morskaya Dal (Park Hotel) provides for the simultaneous accommodation of one hundred people vacationers.

The hotel has comfortable rooms of different categories and capacities. The hotel was completely renovated in the last two years, so the interior of the rooms is modern and stylish.

Park Hotel Morskaya Dal (Sochi) is a hotel complex, as it consists of several two-story and one-story buildings.





The main number of rooms is concentrated in the main two-story main building - twenty-four rooms. There are also two two-story cottages and one one-story - they also have rooms for vacationers. Several rooms are located in the main building.

Park Hotel Morskaya Dal (Lazarevskoye, Sochi) is one of the few hotels that has undergone a complete renovation and today operates on the principle of "All Inclusive", which is so loved by all tourists.

  • entertainment;

Distance to the sea, beach

Morskaya Dal is a park hotel that located from the sea at a distance of about ten minutes of a leisurely walk. You can go down to the sea along a picturesque shady alley that lies among the majestic centuries-old trees, slender pines, tropical shrubs. The air is saturated with coniferous phytoncides, which is a kind of climatotherapy.

The distance from the hotel to the sea is about five hundred meters, but the road is not annoying, but rather inspiring.





The beach in the village, like on the entire Sochi coast, is stone, wide. Pebbles are small and soft. The sea is clean.

Morskaya Dal Hotel offers beach equipment. On the beach there are bathrooms and locker rooms.

Park Hotel Morskaya Dal: reviews of vacationers

There are quite a lot of reviews about it, which indicates the popularity of this vacation spot. Most likely, this is because the hotel operates on the All Inclusive system, which is familiar to Russians for their holidays in Turkey and Egypt. For our country, this principle of operation of boarding houses is quite new and is just starting to gain momentum. Naturally, vacationers are in a hurry to share their impressions, and the hotel administration, in turn, can draw conclusions for itself by reading the wishes and comments of customers.





Park Hotel Morskaya Dal (Golovinka), reviews of which are quite versatile, new. He works this year only the first season. Hence, perhaps, some flaws. The reconstruction work was not completed on time, at the beginning of the season there were still workers and builders in the territory, creating inconvenience to some vacationers, who, in turn, were quick to express their negativity, leaving a review. Park Hotel Morskaya Dal (Sochi), according to many tourists vacationing in June 2016, was not ready to receive vacationers. The rooms had minor defects, the rooms were not properly washed after the same repair, some furniture was understaffed.

It is worth saying that those vacationers who rested in late July, in August or in September, mostly leave good reviews about their stay in this hotel. Yes, of course, there are some negative points, but in general there are much more positive reviews.

Positive reviews

In 2016, the Morskaya Dal Park Hotel (Sochi) turned out to be a rather popular vacation spot for Russians. Reviews of tourists allow us to note the following advantages of this resort complex:

  • new fresh repair in the rooms, nice design;
  • comfortable modern furniture, comfortable beds;
  • high-quality linen, appliances;
  • hearty and tasty food;
  • a good assortment of alcoholic beverages (wine, champagne, beer);
  • quality work of waiters;
  • the territory of the hotel is also well-groomed - many flowers and trees, coolness, fresh air;
  • there is a pool;
  • friendly friendly staff;
  • excellent animation for children and adults (almost all tourists note the work of the animator);
  • a good beach - wide, there was enough space for everyone;
  • reasonable prices for holidays;
  • interesting excursion routes;
  • television - more than 200 channels.




These are the main positive points about which vacationers spoke. Of course, there were complaints about the kitchen and everything else, but judging by the majority, the conclusions are the same.

Almost all vacationers were delighted with the animators, especially those who were on vacation with children. Very interesting programs, games, contests were offered, and evening discos did not let the hotel guests get bored.

Negative reviews

Of course, it is worth noting the disadvantages of this resort complex, which, like any vacation spot, are also available. Based on the reviews of some vacationers, we can say about the following negative points:

  • construction work, hotel unavailability for the season;
  • some tourists are unhappy with the rise from the sea;
  • lack of sunbeds and umbrellas on the beach;
  • there are no places for smoking and, as a result, they smoke everywhere;
  • the kitchen is monotonous;
  • poorly arranged cleaning staff service;
  • lack of Internet in the territory (only in the administrative building);
  • the All Inclusive system works until 20-00, later - all for money;
  • far from shops, village infrastructure;
  • in the evenings mosquitoes bother, but there are no mosquito nets (fumigators and sprays do not eliminate the problem).

Judging by the negative reviews about such a vacation spot as Park Hotel Morskaya Dal (Krasnodar Territory, Sochi), almost all the problems here are due to inexperience and shortcomings of both staff and the administration. Almost all the disadvantages can be eliminated if desired. Also, perhaps, the requirements of vacationers are somewhat exaggerated, since this guest house is still at the level of "three stars", and not 5. Accordingly, the prices in it are for three.

In general, Sochi, Golovinka, Park Hotel Morskaya Dal leave a positive impression of the rest. Silence, comfort, pacification are the most valuable advantages of the hotel. In the future, the hotel administration promises to develop this place of rest, it is hoped that this will not greatly affect the pricing policy.
