Ski resort "Abzakovo": hotels, reviews

Abzakovo is a ski resort in Bashkiria. But his fame spreads far beyond the borders of this region. First of all, Abzakovo attracts tourists with a well-developed infrastructure. There are wonderful slopes, modern lifts, many hotels and cafes. There are ski schools and tracks for snowboarders at the resort. And for those who do not ski and do not feel the need for it, there is a water park. In this article we will give a brief overview of the Abzakovo hotels.

Should I stop directly at the resort? The reviews say that in the village, whose population is only 1330 people, there are many hotels of completely different price segments. So every tourist can choose a hotel for their own means. In the description of the hotels, we first of all focused on the reviews of guests. Based on their praises and complaints, we have compiled a rating of the best hotels. But first, a few words about the resort itself.



Where is Abzakovo located

This resort is located in the east of Bashkortostan near the border with the Chelyabinsk region on the spurs of the Southern Urals. From all sides Abzakovo is surrounded by the Halmaurdy, Kirkty and Bikembet mountain ranges, which determine the unique climatic features of this amazing place. And the mountain river Kulsagadi creates the opportunity to arrange an excellent vacation in the resort in the summer. In addition to fishing, rafting is popular here.

It should be said that the resort is a success in any season. Therefore, the Abzakovo hotels are open year-round. One of the advantages of the resort is its transport accessibility. This settlement of Beloretsky district (Bashkortostan) can be easily reached from Magnitogorsk. To the resort from this city 60 kilometers. Moreover, right from the Magnitogorsk airport to the ski complex, buses and minibuses ply. You can get to the resort and by train. You should go to the Novoabzakovo station. And finally, a good road leads from Ufa, the capital of Bashkiria, directly to the resort.

Hotel Overview

A large selection of housing is offered in the village itself. Naturally, the closer the mini-hotel or guest house is located to the ski lifts, the more expensive it is. There are no hostels, as such. Chain hotels and luxury five-star hotels too. The entire supply of housing is kept within 3-4 *. A feature of the resort is that almost all hotels in Abzakovo have rooms of different categories. Thus, in the same hotel you can find economy class accommodation for students who come to ski, and suites for discerning customers who like to relax with maximum comfort. The resort can also find apartments, that is, rooms with a small kitchen. This is an excellent choice of housing, because in this way you can significantly save on food.
