How to connect roaming in Crimea?

To date, mobile operators in the Crimea do not function as they do throughout Russia. Perhaps this is due to the fear of sanctions, but providers such as Beeline, Megafon and Tele2 do not work throughout the peninsula. It is neither possible to buy their SIM cards, nor to use the tariffs and services offered to subscribers located in other regions of the Russian Federation. As soon as the holder of such a card is in the territory of Crimea, intranet roaming is automatically connected, due to which the cost of communication increases very significantly. But is there any way to save? How to connect roaming in Crimea without extra costs? To do this, you can use the services of other operators, including local ones.





On the territory of Crimea, their own providers operate - Volna, K-Telecom (aka Win Mobile), Sevmobile and Krymtelecom. At the same time, Sevmobile works well exclusively within Sevastopol and its environs. In addition, it is present in the Crimea and "MTS", albeit with some features. What to choose?

Roaming "MTS" in the Crimea

As already noted, this operator fully works throughout the peninsula. Therefore, you can not only take advantage of an existing connection, but also purchase a local SIM card. When you connect on the spot, the subscriber will have the opportunity to set certain tariffs that will enable him to travel profitably throughout Russia.

It is not profitable to use MTS roaming in Crimea because the local SIM card of this operator offers several attractive tariff plans. The region where this provider is connected in Crimea is the Krasnodar Territory and the Republic of Adygea. As elsewhere in the country, there are currently a number of Smart tariffs that offer a certain number of unlimited calls to MTS within the region, Internet traffic and SMS packages.





Tariffs "MTS" in Crimea

For those who use the phone minimally, the Smart Mini tariff plan is suitable, which will cost only 200 rubles. It includes the following package of services: 1 GB of Internet traffic, a thousand minutes for on-net calls, including outside the region, as well as 200 SMS to local MTS SIM cards. Within the region, calls to SIM cards of other providers will cost one and a half rubles per minute, while outside they will cost 12 minutes.

If you plan to make calls not only to MTS subscribers, then it is better to opt for Smart or Smart Unlimited packages, which involve cheaper network calls. The first will cost 350 rubles a month, the second will cost a little more than 12 rubles a day in the first month of connection and 15 rubles a day for subsequent use.

Replace roaming in the Crimea will help the local tariff "Guest", which assumes free incoming calls and outgoing to SIM cards of the same tariff. Any other calls within the region, as well as to MTS subscribers throughout Russia, will cost one and a half rubles per minute. However, the Internet is not included in this package. If you use a home SIM card "MTS"

If you decide not to purchase a SIM card with a local tariff and use roaming in the Crimea, how much will it cost?

If you are connected to one of the Smart packages (except for Mini), most of the services are valid throughout Russia. At the same time, in roaming, 15 rubles will be debited from your account daily. Calls within the package will be free, as well as Internet traffic. On top of the package, calls will cost 2 rubles per minute to the home region and five rubles in Russia, SMS - 3.80 rubles.

It is also possible to activate the service "Everywhere at home", which is available at almost all tariffs, including archival ones. The exception is VIP-2014 and Business without Borders plans. Connection of the service will cost 30 rubles, and use will cost seven rubles a day. Calls in Russia will cost three rubles per minute, and all incoming calls will be free. In addition, this service gives the subscriber a hundred SMS for free.

For MegaFon subscribers

Internet Services

As already noted, in some cases it is more advantageous not to connect roaming in the Crimea, but to purchase a local SIM card. This is especially true for active Internet users.

The highest quality 3G and 4G services are present at the local provider WIN mobile. Also, 3G-communication throughout the republic is represented by Krymtelecom, and Sevmobile within Sevastopol. When roaming is connected, all providers begin to work through the above operators.
