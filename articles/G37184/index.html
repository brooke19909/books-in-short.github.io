Pension "Yuzhny" (Sochi): description, rooms, services

Guests from all over the world are invited by the Yuzhny boarding house. Sochi has always been and remains one of the favorite places to relax, because the city is located in the southern part of Russia. The resort is famous for its mild Mediterranean climate, which has a beneficial effect on health and does not require long acclimatization. Recreational boarding house "South" (Sochi) is located on the territory where you can year-round see the flowering of a wide variety of plants brought from distant corners of the planet, for example, Australia, Africa or even America. In the boarding house you can see palm trees, conifers (spruce and thuja, pine and fir, junipers, etc.) and various deciduous trees.

  • South-2.

Absolutely all guest rooms are cozy and comfortable, have modern furnishings, a working air conditioning system, and are also equipped with a safe, fridge, hairdryer, telephone, minimal set of dishes and satellite TV, bath accessories.

Recreation and entertainment

Pension Yuzhny (Sochi) offers its guests a rest on a well-maintained beach, and if you wish, you can take walks on a fast boat, ride a jet ski, catamaran or just visit an amusement park. The wellness center has an indoor pool, which has underwater lighting and waterfalls.




At leisure, you are invited to play bowling in the modern hall or visit the billiard room, where you can try your hand at the American pool or the Russian version of the game. For lovers of a relaxing holiday, there is a cinema that is equipped with professional sound and projection equipment, so you can enjoy volumetric sound effects and high-quality picture in soft chairs. Pension Yuzhny (Sochi) has its own library with a wide catalog of books of various genres.
