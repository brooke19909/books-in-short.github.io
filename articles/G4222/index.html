Pattern of animals from felt. Original ideas for making toys

The world of childhood in modern times is replete with various toys and entertainment. Nevertheless, the child is happy when his mother works to create a gift for her child. He observes the process of making his beloved animal and looks forward to picking it up. The pattern of animals from felt is done simply. In this article, we will consider various patterns and types of products from this material, since needlewomen pay special attention to it.





Why felt

This material allows you to create many different products, such as educational books, toys, appliques for clothes, pillows, and this is not a complete list of ideas. The soft zoo is especially popular. Below is a pattern of animals from felt, which can be used in the manufacture of children's toys.

The density of this material varies from thin to very dense. In the process, the edges of the felt do not crumble, which greatly reduces the production time. From a sheet of fabric with high density it is enough to cut one piece and decorate as desired. In addition, felt has bright rainbow colors and their derivative shades.

From a thin material, a bulk product can be sewn. The pattern of animals from felt is good because it has a minimum of details and allows you to create bright toys in a short time.





Variety of products

Those who are just trying to sew felt toys can start with a simple flat product, consisting of one part or two identical stacked with each other. The edge treated with a manual decorative seam will look more original. To create such toys you will need a pattern of animals from felt with one element. It is enough to print a template or transfer the picture from the screen to a sheet of paper, then copy the image to felt and cut. You can sew on button eyes, embroider the features of the face, and the toy is ready. If you place a filler between the two duplicated parts and sew the edges together, then your product will gain volume.

Often felt toys are sewn when creating a finger puppet theater. In this case, the lower part of the product is not stitched, leaving a hole for the finger. And also do not stuff.

Another way to develop children's memory and thinking is to create an alphabet. At the same time, not only letters are sewn, but animals are created in a pair for each character. For example: F - giraffe, L - fox, C - elephant and so on.





What is the difficulty

Those who have mastered working with felt can try to create toys from several parts. In the process of sewing these products, the elements are superimposed on each other and sewn alternately. Often in the manufacture of such toys a dense felt of various colors is used. Filler in this case is not applied.

If you stick a magnet on the back of the toy, you get an original decoration on the refrigerator. You can make each detail of one animal separately and sew buttons tightly. The child himself can assemble the product by attaching paws or a tail to the body.





Skillful needlewomen come up with various puzzles depicting animals. This option is more complicated than the previous ones, as it requires perseverance and accuracy. Nevertheless, it has its own originality and is aimed at developing attentiveness and fine motor skills in children.





If you are interested in animals from felt (patterns, patterns and images of which are given in the article), proceed safely to their creation, starting with light elements. Gradually, you will master this type of needlework and will be able to make your own unique toys.
