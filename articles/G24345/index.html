How to make a carnival musketeer costume for a boy with your own hands?

Musketeers are brave and courageous characters who are always ready to help those in need. In their arsenal there is a saber, musketeers on a horse move. In general, this is a rather interesting and independent image.

Headdress

How can you imagine a musketeer without a hat? No way, so be sure to focus on this when you sew a musketeer costume for a boy with your own hands. The photo below shows the chic musketeer's hat.





As you can see, this is a classic hat with wide sides and one feature. This feature is a pen, so we recommend choosing this option. Ready-made hats can usually be purchased at the store or perhaps you have old adult hats. You can use what you find, the main thing is to add a pen. Ostrich, goose and swan feathers are best suited for this option. Fix the feather on the side of the hat with silicone hot glue or sew with thread.

Another option for decorating a hat is to depict a coat of arms or cross on it. This can be done in the form of a patch, which is cut out of a dense material, in a contrasting color. It is best to fix the finished patch with a thread and a needle.
