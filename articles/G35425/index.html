Eden Roc Resort 4 * (Greece, Rhodes): hotel description and reviews

Many people consider Greece an ideal place to relax. It is warm even in winter, here you can find unusually interesting sights and, of course, quite modest prices for everything in these places. And if you find a good hotel with an attractive value for money, you can arrange an unforgettable vacation. Well, just such a "convenient" hotel complex is Eden Roc Resort 4 *.





Briefly about the hotel

This hotel was built over 45 years ago. But, despite the fact that her "age" is quite large, she is modern. And the rooms, and the hall, and the area - everything looks attractive, stylish and new. After all, restorations here were carried out regularly, as well as cosmetic repairs.

Eden Roc Resort 4 * is located in a place called Kallithea (or, as it is also called, Kallithea). This is one of the most popular resorts in Rhodes. The location is good - you can get to the airport in about 15 minutes. It is close enough to get there quickly, but not so much that you can hear airplanes (which is a hindrance for many, so it's a plus).

The hotel itself, consisting of 356 rooms of various categories, is located in close proximity to a pebble beach with convenient entry into the water. There are umbrellas and deck chairs.

And nearby (at a distance of 2-5 kilometers) there are various interesting sights. For example, St. Francis Park, a water park, a clock tower, Knights Street, the Palace of the Grand Masters, etc. There is a bus stop 700 meters from the hotel, so you can get to it from anywhere.





Services

In Eden Roc Resort 4 * created the ideal conditions for relaxation. There is everything that you might need, ranging from free Wi-Fi to free parking next to the complex.

The reception here is round-the-clock, there is a room where guests can leave their luggage, and currency exchange. There is a playground on the street. There is also a play area in the room. Nanny services, by the way, are also offered here.

There is a dry cleaning service, laundry service, an office with a photocopy and fax machine, and even a conference room. A mini market and a small souvenir shop are located on site. Guests can also rent a car, for this you need to contact the reception - the issue will be resolved quickly. By the way, the staff here is not only friendly, but also multilingual. The hotel employs people who are native speakers of 9 different languages! Among them are Czech, German, Greek, English, French, Italian, Polish, Slovak and, most importantly, Russian.

It is also worth noting that the Eden Roc Hotel has several rooms designed for people with disabilities.

On the beach, where guests go most often, entertainment shows are held every evening. You can also rent equipment designed for water sports. Many guests enjoy windsurfing, canoeing and skiing, snorkelling and snorkelling.

About nutrition

Eden Roc Resort Hotel 4 *, like every good hotel, has its own restaurant, bar and tavern. When booking a room, guests can order breakfast, half board or all inclusive.

The main restaurant offers amazing sea views. At this establishment you can taste Greek and international dishes. The tavern is located on the seafront - it serves national cuisine. By the way, for dinner, guests should come in clothes that match the dress code. For women, these are light dresses or other evening wear, and for men, trousers and a shirt.

There are also several bars. Champagne wines, spirits, exotic cocktails and cold drinks are served there.

According to hotel guests who have visited these establishments, they will not be able to stay hungry here. The dishes are delicious, fragrant, always fresh. And there is everything - an abundance of various meat (poultry, lamb, pork, beef, etc.), seafood, vegetables, side dishes and fruits.
