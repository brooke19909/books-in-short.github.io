Absolut Bank: deposits of individuals

Absolut Bank has been operating for over 20 years. Over the years, it has received high marks on long-term deposits. So, investing your own money in this institution is not only profitable, but also reliable. Perhaps, it is for this reason that the great demand for their products is explained.





Absolut Bank: deposits of individuals (main offers)

The financial institution in question practices the adoption of personal funds of citizens under different conditions. Some advantageously differ in high interest rates, others in the possibility of capitalization, additional replenishment of the deposit, etc. It is impossible to say exactly which proposal will be the most successful in a particular case. Therefore, each individual will individually determine what will appeal to him. So, Absolut Bank, whose deposits differ in many advantages, offers the following products to its customers:

  1. "Absolute maximum +".
  2. Rastivklad.
  3. "Progressive Income."

"Absolute maximum +"

This is one of the products that Absolut Bank offers. Deposits of individuals are accepted in this case under the following conditions:

  • The investment currency is rubles, dollars and euros.
  • Interest rate - 9.75% per annum.
  • The volume of the deposit is from 30,000 rubles (1,000 dollars / euro).
  • The term of the deposit is 3-36 months.
  • Capitalization of funds is not provided.
  • Partial replenishment or withdrawal of funds from the investment is not allowed.
  • Payments are made in several ways.
  • Extension of the investment period - in automatic mode.

If the potential client is satisfied with such conditions, then he will have to do the following:

  1. Fill out an application on the company's website.
  2. Wait for a call from a bank employee.
  3. During the conversation, indicate the desire to invest in this particular type of product.
  4. Conclude an agreement through a special online service or by contacting the nearest branch of the institution.




Rastivklad

The next product that Absolut Bank offers is deposits at 7.25% per annum. The rest of the conditions for investing own funds are as follows:

  • The valid currency type is rubles, dollars, and euros.
  • The investment amount is from 10,000 rubles (in foreign currency - from 1,000 dollars / euro).
  • Deposit term - 1-3 months or 2 years (for deposits in foreign currency - 1 year).
  • Capitalization of funds is provided.
  • Addition of a contribution is allowed, as well as a partial withdrawal of funds.
  • Payments are made once a month in various ways.
  • The extension of the contract is automatic.




"Progressive Income"

The last offer from Absolut Bank is deposits with differentiated interest rates up to 11% per annum. Other conditions of this product can be found below:

  1. The investment currency is Russian rubles.
  2. The term of the deposit is 1 year.
  3. Capitalization of funds is provided.
  4. Addition of contribution is allowed (only until mid-term).
  5. Partial withdrawals are not provided.
  6. Payments are made monthly.
  7. Extension of attachment - automatic.

As you can see, Absolut Bank with its various proposals will be able to satisfy the requirements of each potential investor. And thanks to their special online system, it is possible to conclude an agreement on long-term investment of funds in a short time and without even leaving home.
