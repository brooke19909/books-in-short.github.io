Samsung Alpha: smartphone features and reviews. Samsung Galaxy Alpha

An impeccable combination of a small body and a high level of smartphone performance is Samsung Alpha. The characteristics of this device at the software and hardware levels allow today to solve problems of any level of complexity. And a similar situation will continue over the next 2-3 years. This phone will be considered in great detail in the framework of the material.





Background of the appearance of the smartphone

Until recently, Apple released high-performance smartphones with a small diagonal display. But the South Korean giant produced flagships only with a large diagonal. But with the release of the iPhone 6, the situation has changed dramatically. It was immediately presented 2 devices on the same hardware and software platform. The difference between them lies in the diagonal of the screen, which in the first case is 4.7 inches (a more modest device) and 5.5 inches (iPhone 6 Plus). As a result, the South Korean company had to be rebuilt, and in addition to the S5, a gadget such as Samsung Alpha appeared on sale. Characteristics (reviews confirm this) they are almost identical. The difference between these devices is the diagonal of the display and the cost of the device. So it turns out that this smart phone owes its appearance to Apple's bold experiments.

Technical characteristics allow us to attribute this gadget to the premium class, but in appearance you can not say right away. CPU and its features

A very productive processor solution is used in Samsung Alpha. The characteristics of Exynos 5 Octa 5430 are as follows: 8 cores based on the "A7" architecture divided into 2 computing clusters. The first of them operates at a frequency of 1.3 GHz and is used in cases where there is no need to use the maximum computing resources of the smartphone. Also in this case, the maximum level of battery saving is ensured. The second cluster is turned on when demanding applications are launched (Asphalt 8 can be taken as an example) and it operates at a frequency of 1.8 GHz. The computing capabilities of this chip are quite enough to solve a wide range of problems.

Memory

The memory subsystem in Samsung Galaxy Alpha is perfectly implemented. Her characteristics are as follows:

  • The amount of RAM is 2 GB. Somewhere around 1 GB of them is occupied by system processes (operating system) and the TouchViz software shell. The rest is dedicated to solving user problems, and this is quite enough even to run such resource-intensive applications as Asphalt 8.
  • The capacity of the internal drive is 32 GB. And this is more than enough to install new applications and store personal data.

But there is no slot for installing an external flash card. Therefore, with a lack of integrated 32 GB, you will have to use cloud services. But such an approach is justified - if the device breaks down, the most important information will be saved and it will not be difficult to restore it.

Battery

One of the most controversial issues is the battery capacity at Samsung Galaxy Alpha. Her characteristics are really modest. A capacity of 1860 mAh at best will allow the owner of this device to stretch for 1-2 days on an average charge. Theoretically, this value can be increased to 3 days, but at the same time, the smartphone will turn into just a regular ‚Äúdialer‚Äù. Well, if you play the same Asphalt 8 or a similar toy on this device, then the battery will last for 8-12 hours.





But, on the other hand, the developers wanted to get a device with a small thickness and low weight. As a result, they did it, and their autonomy remained at an acceptable level.

Interfaces

An impressive communication set of interfaces is implemented in Samsung Galaxy Alpha. Specifications indicate the following list:

  • The device can work in all types of mobile networks, including LTE.
  • The Wai Fai transmitter is built-in, capable of also exchanging information with any wireless router at the moment.
  • There is also a "Bluetooth" version 4.0.
  • For navigation, the device is equipped with one ZhPS transmitter, which, in addition to the standard ZhPS network, can also determine the location using GLONASS satellites.
  • The main wireless interface is microUSB, the version of which in this device is 3.0.
  • Another wired port is a 3.5-mm audio port, designed to output and input audio information.
