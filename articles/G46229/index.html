Modern smart TVs - what is it?

Modern household video equipment is developing rapidly. Therefore, fans of new products pay attention to such models of television receivers that are equipped with the Smart TV function. What is it and what is the advantage of such devices?

For a long time, TVs that only showed films or television programs without other features were more familiar. Computer technology is increasingly confident about themselves. The fusion of the two systems has occurred recently. New technology has made a real revolution. The first manufacturer of new video equipment was Samsung.



When similar devices appeared in 2011 - television receivers with Smart TV, what it is, even the manufacturers themselves could not decide. Of the additional features that such devices possess, one can name access to social networks, a browser, and email. You can play games or view photos. In some ways, it resembles a tablet or smartphone. The differences include the lack of RAM on the TV.





Thus, an explanation of the term Smart TV can be found . What it is, they explain simply: it is a TV that allows you to access the Internet. There is even an industry standard for the new hybrid - HbbTV. Of the useful features that such models of household video equipment have, one can call an online video library. To use it, you need to download the movie you are interested in from the Internet and watch it on TV. Also, the owner of a new generation TV, which is akin to the World Wide Web, becomes a user of multimedia services from the home network. For example, you can view photos, videos, or play music.

Samsung, a well-known manufacturer of consumer electronics, manufactures D6530 / 6510 models - these are 3D Smart TVs that provide their owner with the following services:

  • Quick access to sites using a web browser that supports Flash and is adapted for wide screen.
  • The Search All function provides the ability to search on all devices connected to the TV, as well as on the Internet.
  • You can simultaneously watch television programs using the Social TV service to follow news and messages on social networks.
  • The TV is connected to the Internet using the built-in WI-FI adapter.
  • 3D technology provides a realistic, three-dimensional image.

Another well-known company - LG, produces a Led Smart TV, it has a diagonal of forty-two inches. Supports Full HD format, Internet connection via WI-FI. The device has child protection, Skype, allows you to record video on a USB drive.
