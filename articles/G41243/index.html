How to make a flower out of paper quickly and easily

How to make a flower out of paper simply and quickly? Crepe paper is well known to the older generation, as large flowers were prepared for all the parades, which were an obligatory attribute of Soviet holidays. They decorated squares, halls, streets. In kindergartens, children even made costumes for performances from such material. Since this paper has the ability to stretch, and from it you can make beautiful creations. There was a time when crepe paper disappeared from the shelves. But now she has again gained popularity, she has appeared in all stationery stores, in stores for artists and in special craft circles. It is sold in rolls.



How to make a flower out of paper

It is hard to believe that these chic bouquets are made of paper. They look like they are alive. We offer you a small description of how to make a flower out of paper.

1. Start with the simplest flower

Cut strips 8 cm wide, 25-30 cm long. Fold them with an accordion, round the edges. Pull the middle wire. Now begin to reveal each layer of corollas and form a flower. You can curl the edges a bit with a pencil . Glue the finished craft to where you need it. For example, on a gift box. Now you can teach your kids how to make a flower out of paper. Each child will find this process fascinating and interesting, even if the result is not so beautiful.

2. To know how to make a voluminous flower out of paper, try this option.



Cut a strip of crepe paper 6-8 cm wide, 30 cm long. Fold it with an accordion 5 cm wide. Scissor round one edge as shown in the picture, and start wrapping it in a roll. Deepen the leaves a little with your thumb inward (for a half-open bud) or on the outside for an opened flower. Curl the edges of the leaves with a pencil or a toothpick. Where necessary - extend the petals so that they naturally fit. You got a voluminous rose. Cut the green leaves. Cut 1 cm narrow strips of green crepe paper and begin to wrap the wire on which the rose rests. Glue the prepared leaflets to it. Such a rose can be put in a vase. If you cut the wire to the base of the bud, you get a flat base. In this form, it can be glued to a surface, for example, on a vase, on a box, on a bag.
