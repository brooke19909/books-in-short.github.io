Introducing iPad mini: gadget specifications and features

So, iPad mini is a gadget that is a tablet mini-computer developed by Apple, which was announced on October 23, 2012. This is the fifth product in the line of iPads, which has a reduced size of 7.9 inches (in contrast to the standard 9.7). In iPad mini, the specifications are similar to the internal specifications of the second model, including screen resolution. The gadget was introduced on November 2, 2012, and immediately appeared on sale.



The tablet model under consideration is available with iOS 6.0 firmware. It can be used as a modem for other devices, allowing them to connect to the Internet via Wi-Fi, via Bluetooth or USB, and also access the AppStore (digital application platform for iOS).

IPad mini features

The technical characteristics of the gadget are such that they allow you to use the following pre-installed programs: Siri, Safari, Mail, Itunes. In addition, the user has at his disposal photographs, videos and music, maps, notes, a calendar, GameCenter, PhotoBooth and contacts. Like all other iOS devices, it can sync data from a Mac or PC using iTunes. In addition, iOS 5 and later are equipped with Icloud. Despite the fact that the tablet is not designed for phone calls over a cellular network, users can use a headset or built-in speaker and microphone and make calls using software like Skype. The device has an additional Ibooks application, which serves to read books and other EPUB format content downloaded from iBookstore.



Appearance and functionality of the iPad mini. Specifications

The gadget has four buttons and a switch located on the sides, including the "Home" button next to the display (returns the user to the main screen). Other keys are located on the right, side and top, and put the device into sleep mode, and also adjust the sound volume. The functionality of the software-controlled switch, in turn, varies depending on the software version. All models can be connected to a local wireless network using Wi-Fi. The gadget is available with built-in memory of 16, 32 or 64 GB, without the possibility of its expansion. In other words, the specifications in iPad mini are such that the ability to connect and read SD cards is present, but it can only be used to transfer photos and videos.



The device has similar parameters with the iPad of the second generation. In both cases, the display has a resolution of 1024 x 768, but in the mini version, due to the smaller screen size , the pixel density is higher (163 PPI compared to 132 PPI). Unlike version 2, the device has cameras of 5 megapixels and 1.2 megapixels. However, some advantages remain with the Apple iPad mini - the technical characteristics of this gadget are more advanced. Its audio processor is similar to the fourth-generation tablet, which allows the mini to use Siri and voice dictation.



In general, reviews about this device were positive around the world. However, most reviewers praised the size of the gadget, its design and the availability of applications, while criticizing the power connector and the lack of expandable memory. Separately, iPad mini WIFI cellular is mentioned as a positive feature of the device, allowing you to use various sources of connection to wireless networks around the world. As you can see, in the modern market, the tablet most competes with Kindle Fire HD, Google Nexus 7, as well as Barnes & Noble Nook HD.
