Banknote exchange procedure

If the operator agrees to exchange a trifle for banknotes in Sberbank, then in this case some preparatory measures need to be made. The exchange algorithm itself is extremely simple.

First of all, the client writes an application and indicates in it all his passport data, as well as the amount of the amount he wants to exchange. After that, the bank employee must count all the coins and confirm this operation with his signature and seal, which are put on the application. At the next stage, the client is given cash.
