Adler, hotel "Svetlana": description and reviews of guests

In the summer, Adler resort is visited by thousands of tourists who decide to relax on the Black Sea. This popular southern city has many cafes, restaurants, entertainment venues and, of course, comfortable hotels. Where is the hotel situated

The guests of the Svetlana complex need to go to the beach no more than 2-3 minutes. Many of the rooms on the top floors of this hotel offer wonderful sea views. The complex is located at Adler, Bogdan Khmelnitsky, 58a. Hotel "Svetlana" is located in close proximity to the market, a large shopping center and a billiard room.





Long-distance trains are the main form of transport on which tourists come to Adler. Svetlana is a private hotel located very close to the railway station. This hotel is 3.7 km from the property. You can get to the hotel by minibus (Nos. 117, 60, etc.) or by bus (Nos. 125, 56). A transfer from the railway station or airport and to the hotel itself is offered to tourists.

What is a complex

The Svetlana Hotel (Adler) accepts tourists in one four-story building with an attic. The courtyard of the complex is covered with beautiful pink paving tiles. Therefore, traveling around it is very convenient for tourists. Most vacationers find the hotel grounds attractive and comfortable. In the courtyard of the hotel there are flowerbeds with exotic plants everywhere, southern trees planted, rest corners arranged. The hotel's interiors are decorated in pastel colors pleasing to the eye.

Rooms in this complex can be rented both for a very short period (up to 4 days) and for a long period (more than a month). Children to the hotel "Svetlana" are accepted at any age. Unfortunately, rooms for disabled people in this complex are not provided. Pets are not allowed in the hotel. From the documents for checking into this hotel, only a passport is required.





Housing for rent

If desired, guests of the resort can rent a standard room for 2-4 people in the Svetlana Recreation Center. The complex also has several two-room suites. All rooms for rent in the hotel have:

  • air conditioners;
  • refrigerators;
  • TV sets
  • electric kettles.

Beach items can be dried on the balcony. Bathrooms at this hotel are available in all rooms.

Guest reviews about rooms

Opinion about renting a house in the guest house "Svetlana" at his guests was very good. The hotel rooms, according to many tourists, are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. Maids are cleaned in rooms with the declared frequency. Therefore, the rooms always look neat and comfortable.

Residents consider it a drawback of this hotel that its owners may refuse tourists to check into pre-booked rooms. This is usually motivated by a large influx of guests. In the event of such a situation, vacationers at the hotel, among other things, often offer not too good rooms in return and are asked to wait for the release of those that have been booked.





Hotel infrastructure: reviews

The following services are offered to guests at the Svetlana Hotel:

  • kitchen;
  • barbecue with skewers;
  • iron and ironing board on the floor.

Reviews of tourists the hotel infrastructure has earned good. The service here, according to many vacationers, is offered simply excellent. Many tourists praise the reviews and staff of this hotel. The owners of the hotel and its staff are friendly and helpful to their residents.

All resorts in the summer are noisy and very crowded. Adler is no exception. Hotel "Svetlana", however, is located in this village in a rather calm area. The center and its rooms are always very quiet. This, of course, many vacationers also attribute to the advantages of the complex. Many vacationers believe that this hotel is very well suited for living, including with children.

Some tourists come by car to the spa town of Adler. Hotel Svetlana offers such residents, among other things, parking places (free).





Dining at the hotel

Unfortunately, there is no private dining room at the Svetlana Hotel. However, its guests have the opportunity to cook lunches, breakfasts and dinners on their own. Especially for this purpose, the hotel provides summer kitchen. Here residents can use stoves, pots, pans, knives, etc.

Actually for eating, the hotel provides a small summer kitchen, equipped with tables and chairs. In order to allow guests to warm up cooked dishes at any time, the kitchen corner is supplemented with a microwave.

Beach near the hotel

The proximity to the sea is what, among other things, the Svetlana hotel has earned good reviews from tourists. Adler, st. Bogdan Khmelnitsky, 58a, as already mentioned - this is her exact address. Unfortunately, this small complex does not have its own beach. Visitors to the hotel are citywide. Reviews about this beach among tourists, as well as about the complex itself, are mostly good. The coastline in this place is covered with small pebbles. The entrance to the sea here is quite convenient and no dirty rivers flow into it nearby.





Excursions

A huge number of various interesting sights is what distinguishes Big Sochi and Adler. Hotel "Svetlana" - the complex is not particularly large and expensive. But if desired, tourists can purchase here, including some interesting trip. The hotel collaborates with agencies offering excursions, for example, to Krasnaya Polyana, to Abkhazia, Sochi, etc.
