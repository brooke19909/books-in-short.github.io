What can be made of natural material? Crafts from natural materials

Autumn time is rich in wonderful gifts of nature. Some of them delight us with culinary delights, because you can prepare delicious dishes from a variety of vegetables and fruits, while others, in the form of multicolor foliage, pardon your eyes with their bright colors. Usually, all this beauty almost instantly falls with a variegated blanket to the ground, which is why it quickly loses its attractiveness. Unfortunately, few people know what can be made of natural material, thereby prolonging its life. But crafts from leaflets, cones and chestnuts are not only funny figures and applications (although how can one imagine childhood without these cute little things?), But also an original and really beautiful decor of housing.

So what can be made of natural material? It all depends on what the craft is for. In today's article, we will consider more than one version of excellent products that can be created independently using only available means at hand. Many of them are children's crafts, for example, a variety of animals made of natural material, toys and even cartoon characters and computer games. But it can also be unusual trees, topiary, unique panels and compositions that will delight and remind you of a wonderful autumn season in the cold winter.

It is impossible to describe all those masterpieces created by talented needlewomen from natural material. Autumn bouquet or photo frame, original vase or decorative figurine, Christmas toy or picture - the imagination of talented artists is simply limitless. Moreover, their work does not stop at soulless interior items, stretching much further. For example, to arrange a children's photo shoot using ordinary grass, leaves, grains and maple seeds. Have you ever imagined that you can make such beauty out of a natural material? Of course, the final result will appeal not only to adults, but also to the children themselves, whom you can attach to all the stages of creating a photo collage, from collecting plants to decorating their own pictures.





Transformation of flora into fauna

To begin with, we will tell you how to make a variety of animals from natural material. First of all, we distinguish between types of crafts. These can be applications that are created using leaves and small elements. Such material is usually glued to the base - a cardboard sheet, cloth or wooden board with glue stick or PVA. A similar composition is flat and looks like a picture.

With the help of leaves and blades of grass, you can create a variety of representatives of the animal world, the main thing is to choose the right material according to colors, size and texture. Craft "come to life", if it is supplemented with a picture, making the beast face, tail, or even prey. Miniature details can also be created from natural material by cutting leaves in some places to remove excess. It‚Äôs better to glue the elements on the schematic image of the animal, so it will be easier to maintain symmetry and select individual parts of the figure.





However, there is another type of crafts in the form of animals. This may be a bulk composition of natural material. Most often, nuts, acorns, cones and chestnuts are used. The body and head are made of them, and matches, twigs, floristic wire or plasticine serve as legs, paws, neck. To do such crafts from natural materials quickly and beautifully, you can even use ready-made animal figures, if only they fit organically into the general concept.

Winged miracle

Another favorite of children and adults is a round-eyed owl. This bird can be made in a variety of ways: from a piece of bark, feathers, thread, leaves. We will demonstrate to our readers a wonderful and very simple craftsmanship. Such an owl made of natural material consists of a pine cone, several acorn hats, ‚Äúhelicopters‚Äù taken from ash or maple, hop cones and a couple of pieces of bark. In order to assemble the craft, you need to stock up with good glue - it can be ‚ÄúMoment‚Äù, ‚ÄúSecond‚Äù or silicone for a hot gun.





Such an owl is made of natural material very quickly:

  • you need to prepare the details by selecting a lush bump, disconnecting the caps from the acorns and picking up small pieces of bark;
  • the material must be cleaned of dirt and dried well so that the glue reliably connects the parts of the owl;
  • on the sides to the bump-body should stick a bark - these will be wings;
  • in the upper part with the help of acorn caps we form eyes, and above them we attach maple seeds - these are eyebrows;
  • the final touch is a little bump that will become a beak.

In such a simple way, we get a wonderful bird from natural material, which will not be difficult to get in any city park.





Autumn landscape

Talking about crafts, it is impossible to ignore such a kind of creativity as application. A little higher, we have already talked about how, with the help of leaves, you can lay out figures of animals and birds, but what else can be done from natural material? An excellent craft would be a fantasy landscape in which one leaf turns into a whole tree, and their combination - into a real dense forest. To compose such a composition, it is very important to choose the ideal material, because it will need to be glued to the paper as a whole, and all the flaws will be visible on the sheet when laid out. With the help of such an application, one can clearly demonstrate the whole riot of colors of a withering nature and organically place leaves taken from different trees.

Autumn vegetation also receives an unusual embodiment in the form of representatives of the underwater world. From miniature oblong and oval leaves, very plausible flocks of nimble fish are obtained, more material can easily be turned into pot-bellied crucian carp, and different blades of grass and thin branches will make up the flora that grows at the bottom of the sea. Making the composition inside the cardboard box, you get a wonderful "aquarium".





Simple but tasteful

The purpose of the work is to create a specific plot, it can be a village house, a forest landscape, a river, an apiary, a shed with firewood or a small wagon. Successful combination

A distinctive feature of autumn is that at this time of year nature itself offers us such paints that perfectly combine with each other. Looking at the riot of colors outside the window, I want to take a piece of this happiness to my home. To cope with such a task is easier than simple, you just need to make an autumn bouquet of natural material. Whatever you add to this composition, it will look beautiful. It is also encouraging that such decoration can decorate a house not only inside but also outside. So, from leaves, cones and beautiful twigs, you can make door wreaths, flower arrangements and street bouquets that will look great in pot-bellied pumpkin vases.

Autumn flowers are a separate topic for conversation. Nature, preparing for a long winter, pleases with a stunning abundance of chrysanthemums, oak, asters and dahlias. Their magnificent colorful corollas combine perfectly with a variety of natural materials. The classic composition can be generously flavored with luxurious maple leaves, a crimson branch of wild grapes or a twig of yellow-lettuce willow. Bushy plants with late elastic berries (dog rose, hawthorn, mountain ash) will be an excellent and especially relevant addition.

A bouquet of autumn leaves and flowers may be small, crowning one small vase, but a multi-tiered composition will look no less appropriate. When composing it, it is important to observe the proportions, making each new "floor" slightly smaller than the previous one.





Seasonal Decor

Finally, we will share with readers the ideas of the original autumn crafts, with which you can decorate the house, make it a little more comfortable and warmer. The multi-colored foliage on the trees will not please for long, it will fall off with the first frost, but it is very simple to make the view from the window not so dull and gray. To do this, you just need to string small, but always beautiful leaflets on long threads or a fishing line in the form of garlands that decorate window openings.

Golden autumn can also be "brought" home in the form of elegant candlesticks or vases. For this, a jar of an interesting shape or an ordinary transparent glass needs to be pasted over in a circle with colored leaves. Freshly collected material will not lie on the vessel beautifully, for a start it will need to be dried a little so that the elasticity leaves the sheet and it becomes more malleable. You need to work with ordinary PVA, it will perfectly fix the decor on the glass, and after drying it will become transparent. In addition, this type of glue is not afraid of the heat of a burning candle inside a glass.
