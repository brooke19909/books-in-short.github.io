Embroidery on clothing or the magic of cross stitching

Since ancient times, things with patterns embroidered with a cross are famous for their homeliness, naturalness and unusual design that attracts people around. Modern women are increasingly buying standard things and redoing them themselves by flashing a thing with a cross or beads. Besides the fact that such clothes turn out to be fashionable, they also become exclusive, because by buying an ordinary inexpensive dress, you can make a masterpiece out of it, selling it can earn two, or even three times as much. Embroidery on clothes looks bright, individual and expensive.

Basically, this magic of transforming clothes from ordinary gray things into bright things is available to everyone. To do this, it is enough to have the initial skills of cross-stitching and beads in the usual outline.

Patterned interior

A few centuries ago, women sought to decorate the interior of the house with embroidered pillowcases, sheets, towels and even table napkins. Cross stitching filled almost every home. A well-educated girl was considered to be one who knew how to embroider, knit, cook. Many ordinary women made a living by weaving and embroidering with a cross, stitch and other types of tablecloths, napkins, towels, bedding and clothes. These items were exported to bazaars and fairs, where they were bought by wealthy citizens. And skilled craftswomen were always provided with orders, since the desire to decorate your home and wear beautiful clothes was inherent in women at all times.

Handmade embroidery

Initially, cross-stitch on clothing, which was decorated with almost everything: women's and men's shirts, trousers, skirts and dresses, was very popular. In those days there was no such clothing that did not have at least a few flowers executed with cross stitching. Such things were considered ceremonial and wore mainly for mass celebrations. Some needlewomen traded their products in local bazaars. Buyers increasingly became ladies who did not have the desire and time to learn the craft of needlewomen.

Progress has come - popularity has gone





Over time, embroidery on clothes began to be performed not only by hand, but also by machines that did it several tens of times faster. Despite the great popularity of machine sewing, manual work, which looks more elegant and unique, is still highly appreciated in the modern world. For their machine work, designers usually choose rustic motifs - images of flowers, animals or birds. Such drawings have long been appreciated. And now modern clothes can no longer do without them. Despite the popularity of the style, whole collections with embroidery, which combines naturalness and avant-garde, have been developed for young people. These may be animals, but they are already more vibrant and lively; birds on the background of beautiful landscapes; beauty of nature.

Hobbies or magic?

Many people think that embroidery on clothes is nothing but real magic. Experienced needlewomen manage to make something out of a regular plain sweater or dress, and it all starts with an elementary attachment of a picture to a fabric. The embroidery process itself does not occur on the "bare" product, you need a picture. After finishing work, the attached piece of paper or canvas with a pattern mystically disappears, leaving a beautiful ornament in its place.

According to old-timers, embroidery on clothes has a sacred meaning, which in reality can protect a person from evil spirits and the evil eye. In addition, ordinary patterns can not only give protection to the owner of a shirt or dress, but also seriously harm. The strongest ornament is the one located on the collar and cuffs. Not for nothing that girls have long been embroidering shirts with their narrowed just in the neck and wrists.

Ribbon sewing: fashionable and beautiful

Recently, embroidery with ribbons on clothes is very relevant. This method is quite well known, and some believe that it is even more colorful and interesting than ordinary sewing on clothes. In fact, this is so: ribbon embroidery is really gaining popularity due to its colorful and large-scale shapes. Increasingly, ribbon sewing is used to decorate dresses, especially wedding skirts and shirts. Basically, ribbon embroidery is intended for women's clothing, since it is not common for men to wear something like that. Shiny satin ribbons, the craftswomen create real masterpieces, whole flower glades on elegant clothes.

Should I wear?

During the purchase of shirts with a unique ornament, you need to pay attention to the uniformity of stitches and the accuracy of the ornament, which embroidered on clothes. Photos of such products are usually not true, so buying online is a risky undertaking. It is better to spend a little more money, go shopping, but find something really worthwhile. It is also worthwhile to study the reviews on the work of handy maids before buying something remotely.
