Hotel Seashell Resort Spa

The sea, the sun, the beach, the silence and the light breath of wind - this is a real summer vacation. Some people like to travel to Europe, some to the cool mountains, and some to the Seashell Resort Spa. What is this new complex? Why do many tourists choose the Resort Spa hotel in Turkey?



Location

Seashell Resort Spa is a new unique complex, which was opened for tourists recently, in 2013. It is located in a beautiful and cozy historical place in Turkey, in a small village near the sea - Evrenseki. Why exactly here? The village is a beautiful, unique combination of amazing nature, pleasant climate and a great past. The hotel kindly provides its services to all who wish to relax in comfort. It is located near the airport in Antalya, and 8 km from Side, where you can walk and relax. Resort Spa is famous for its beach: a long coastline allows you to make amazing romantic walks in the evening and jogging in the morning. In addition, a gentle and shallow entrance to the warm sea will not create discomfort.



Interior and exterior

Since the Seashell Resort Spa opened recently, it can be assumed that the design in the hotel is modern, comfortable, cozy. In fact, 283 rooms are provided for lovers of luxury and comfort! Modern technology and design, a calm and pleasant atmosphere, elegance in every item - this is what Seashell Resort Spa 5. Turkey has always been famous for its hospitality, so caring staff will help brighten up your stay here. The exterior of this place is just as beautiful and will not disappoint you unequivocally. Amazing Mediterranean gardens, pools, lawns, the appearance of houses - this is real beauty!
