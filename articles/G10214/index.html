Village Novomikhaylovsky (Tuapse district) - rest for every taste

What is the resort area Novomikhailovskaya (Tuapse district)? This is the village of the same name and several villages, farms and auls - Psebe, Plyakho, Olginka and Podhrebtovoe. It is located between Cape Gryaznova and the tract Wide slit. This place is known all over the country, since from the time of the Soviet Union there was at first a pioneer camp, and then the Orlyonok Children's Leisure Center. By the way, local residents, speaking of their village, prefer the name "Novomikhailovka".

The name of the future resort was given in the nineteenth century. Before that, it was called by the name of the river flowing through it - Nechepsuho, and in its place in ancient times was the Greek settlement of Nikopsia. There are good beaches, the open sea and accommodation, which will suit different categories of tourists - both budget and accustomed to luxury.



Where is located

Novomikhailovsky resort (Tuapse district) is located forty kilometers from the city of Tuapse. From the regional center - Krasnodar - it is separated by 135 km. If you are coming here in your car, tourists are advised to use the Don highway. You need to go through Rostov-on-Don, Krasnodar and Dzhubga. Another option is the Tuapse-Novorossiysk highway. By plane it is better to arrive in Krasnodar, and then get by bus. He travels to the village for about three hours. By train, travelers usually arrive in Tuapse, and then also follow the bus. Sometimes hotels and private owners offer a shuttle service to your accommodation. But this must be decided in each case with the owners.



Climate

You can often hear that the Novomikhailovsky resort of the Tuapse district is located in ideal conditions. It is surrounded by green hills covered with subtropical forest. They protect the village from cold winds from high mountains. Therefore, the climate here is very good, similar to the Mediterranean. Winters are mild and not very cold, summers are not too hot, and you can swim for six months. Probably, such climatic conditions led to the fact that the village. Novomikhailovsky (Tuapse district) officially acquired the status of a resort since 1966. The high season here lasts from June to the end of September, although, of course, vacationers come here at other times. The water warms up quickly and remains warm all season. The sun shines almost 250 days a year.

Beaches

The coast of the Novomikhailovsky resort (Tuapse district) is suitable for tourists who can swim and love the depth. The beaches are very long and wide. In size, they are somewhat inferior only to the famous coast in the village of Lazarevsky. Pebble beaches themselves, where small pebbles are often mixed with sand. They are comfortable and equipped. There are many water activities - skiing, catamarans, spearfishing, diving, fishing. The sea is shallow, the depth begins immediately. By the way, doctors believe that it is small pebbles that are healthier than sand. Therefore, the local beaches are good not only for relaxation, but also for healing. The coast is sometimes steep, and the most convenient descent for children is located near the mouth of the Nechepsuho River, around those places where it flows into the sea.



Entertainment and infrastructure

This resort has become known since the sixties of the last century. Then the living conditions and entertainment were quite ascetic. A lot of time has passed since then. Now the village of Novomikhailovsky Tuapse district has a whole industry of fun. Both young people and couples come here with pleasure. Despite the remoteness from the big city, the village boasts the presence of bars, discos and nightclubs. They are located on the promenade on the left side of the bay. But there are more than enough ordinary cafes and dining rooms with Caucasian cuisine here. Here you can have lunch, order dinner, and even listen to live music. There are many tour agencies that offer trips, trekking, boat trips and extreme tourism. The beautiful promenade is located in the tourist complex "Tornado", where there is a well-groomed park and a pond with fish. There is also a diving center. And from this base you can climb the stone stairs to the observation deck and the church of St. Nicholas.



Housing

The village of Novomikhailovsky (Tuapse district) overlooks the sea with a small tip. Here are the most luxurious hotels and expensive guest houses. Their balconies offer stunning views of the sea, and the beach is right behind the wall of the room. However, the prices here are appropriate. But the main part of the village stretches from the sea to the mountains along the Nechepsuho River. From its center to the sea a little more than two kilometers. There are housing offers in the private sector, but there are also many multi-storey buildings. Therefore, in the village you can rent a whole separate apartment. Especially this option is suitable for those who go to rest in a large company. The price of housing is mainly determined not so much by amenities as by proximity to the sea. But there are no housing options too far from the water - you will have to walk a maximum of ten to fifteen minutes. In addition to proposals for private apartments, houses and rooms, in the village of Novomikhaylovsky in the Tuapse district there are also hotels, pensions and hostels with meals. And for budget tourists who have arrived in their car, special car campings are provided. Prices at this resort both for housing and for food, tours, cafes and canteens are quite low. Obviously, all these factors determined the popularity of Novomikhailovsky among Russian tourists.
