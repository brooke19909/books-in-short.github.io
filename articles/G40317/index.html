Road by bus

In the first section of the article, we talked about three ways. So, the Kiev-Kherson bus travels exactly on the third path. This shuttle bus runs every day. He leaves Kherson in the morning at 7.10. The first stop in Nikolaev will last 10 minutes and at 8.20 the bus leaves in the direction of the city of Bashtanka. The journey to this point will take a little more than an hour. At 10.15, departure from the New Bug will take place in the direction of Bobrinets (Kirovograd region). After Bobrinets (departure from there at 11.45) the bus will stop in Kirovograd. Here passengers can relax for 20 minutes from a tiring long trip. Next, the Kiev-Kherson bus passes Aleksandrovka, Smela of Cherkasy region, Korsun-Shevchenkovsky, Kagarlyk and arrives at the central bus station of Kiev at 18.50.
