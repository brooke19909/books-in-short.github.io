Utopia World (Turkey) - one of the most outstanding hotels in Alanya

The leading place in the travel industry has always been Turkey. Each year, it lures a huge number of tourists with its oriental motifs, a Hamam bathhouse, hot discos and pristine beaches. Many people have already appreciated the Utopia World Hotel (Turkey). Reviews are proof of this. And this is not surprising, since the hotel is striking in its beauty. It has a large well-groomed territory with numerous plantings, and a magnificent panorama opens from the windows.





Hotel Territory

Utopia World (Turkey) is, first of all, a magnificent building of architecture, including the impregnable mystery of the castle, cozy bungalows, a modern water park, sports grounds and, of course, endless steps and stairs that can level all the consequences of the buffet. Well, since we are talking about food, it is worth noting that the food is excellent here. A huge and varied selection of dishes will not leave indifferent even the most fastidious.



Utopia World Hotel (Turkey) has 4 restaurants - one main and 3 a la carte: Italian, Turkish and Far Eastern. Before you visit one of the restaurants a la carte, you must first sign up. In addition, the hotel has 8 bars and gozleme, where they cook delicious Turkish cakes.

Entertainment for guests

Utopia World (Turkey) has its own sand and pebble beach, which is 150 meters from the hotel. Its length is 250 m. There are 2 berths and a special platform where guests can sunbathe. Sun loungers, umbrellas, mattresses and towels are provided free of charge. For especially sports there are kayaks, water skiing, scooters, bananas, diving and other paid entertainment.

Near the hotel there is a water park. It holds 12 water slides, 4 of which are built specifically for children. There are 3 swimming pools and rafting 300 meters long. Also, guests are offered a fitness center, volleyball court, futsal, Turkish bath, sauna, playground, children's buffet, amphitheater, cinema, shops and much more. There is a Russian-language animation. Every day, show programs, children's discos and various games for children are held.





Hotel Rooms

Utopia World (Turkey) includes 595 rooms, all rooms are spacious. Many apartments have sea views. There are non-smoking rooms, 2 rooms for people with disabilities. Some people prefer to live not in villas, but in the main building, explaining that the distance from the building to the restaurant and the beach is less.

The standard room has a balcony or terrace, bath (shower), toilet, safe, wireless Internet, TV, air conditioning, hairdryer, telephone, minibar. They clean the rooms every day, change bed linen 2 times a week.

Utopia World (hotel, Turkey) boasts a high level of service, although sometimes you can see complaints about the incorrect behavior of the staff. Not always quickly, as it would be desirable, the settlement occurs. However, the vast majority of reviews are positive, and many tourists would like to visit this place again.
