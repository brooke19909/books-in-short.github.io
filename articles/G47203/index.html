Hannover: Germany as it is

Hannover on the map of Germany can not be found right away. The country is full of large cities, each of which is unlike the others and is distinguished by its sights and features. Hanover is located on the banks of the River Layne. This city is famous for its beautiful architecture, parks. It is filled with the dynamics of life. It has many exhibition centers.



Tourists have many reasons to visit Hanover. Germany attracts with its beauty, architectural diversity and rich history. During the war years, many cities were destroyed. The architecture of some has not been fully restored. A similar fate overtook Hanover. The city was almost destroyed, only the most beautiful buildings that had important cultural and historical significance were restored.

Hannover (Germany) is very convenient for hiking. Leaving the station building, the tourist immediately gets on the pavement, where a red line is drawn. If you walk along it, you can visit the 36 most famous places in the city. The route to these attractions is well thought out.

First of all, you can explore the surroundings and the city itself from the observation deck of the city hall. Her building is located on the banks of the pond. The panorama of the city from here is beautiful. This is a very romantic place. Inside the town hall, everyone can see photographs of Hanover in different periods of history.



Further, the line leads to the Opera Square, where the magnificent opera building, erected in the spirit of neoclassicism, is located. There are also several modern shopping centers. The main crossroads of the city is the Krepke Square. This place plays a huge role in the life of citizens. It is here that rallies and festivities are held, processions of various kinds begin from here. In the square it is worth paying attention to one of the main attractions of Hanover - the Wind Rose. It is a pointer that shows the distance to other cities in Europe and the world.

Hannover (Germany) is also famous for the Sprengel Museum. On display are his paintings by famous artists - Malevich, Kurt Schwiters, Picasso.

Germany (Hanover in particular) is known throughout the world as a country with developed services and modern technologies. For the townspeople created the most comfortable living conditions. A well-developed public transport system, metro lines , banks, insurance companies and various government organizations - all this allows residents and guests of the city to enjoy scientific achievements and innovations.



Hannover (Germany) is a major center of international trade. Here are stores of famous brands of clothing, as well as novice designers and fashion designers. Thanks to numerous international exhibitions, there is always a large influx of tourists in the city. Meetings, congresses and seminars are held here annually. Their main topics are new technologies and developments in industry, computer and information technologies, and the automotive industry. These events are of great international importance. They annually bring together thousands of specialists.
