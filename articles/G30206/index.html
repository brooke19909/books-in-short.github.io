Details on how to insert a SIM card into the "iPhone"

Those who received a gift or bought their first "iPhone", when starting to work with it, face a serious problem. At first glance, it is not known where to insert the SIM card in it. In ordinary smartphones, it is installed under the battery, and newly-minted users have no idea how to get into the compartment where it is located. Also, many have heard that standard SIM cards in this smartphone are not suitable. Therefore, the question of how to insert a SIM card into the "iPhone" should be considered in more detail.

Types of used SIM cards



Depending on the generation, various types of SIM cards are used in the Apple smartphone. Currently, on SIM cards you can find perforation to make it more compact. Thus, you can reduce its size to microsim, which is used in smartphones 4 series and below. Before inserting a SIM card into the "iPhone", read the user manual. Usually in it you can find information about the required type of card. The Apple 5 and 6 series smartphones use an even smaller SIM card, which the manufacturer calls nanosim. Its difference from the previous version is a more compact size by reducing the amount of plastic around the chip, as well as reducing its thickness.

The greatest difficulties await the owners of more advanced devices of the 5th and 6th series. To insert a SIM card in iPhone 5, you need to trim it as much as possible. It is quite difficult to do it yourself, since it is very easy to damage the chip. It is also necessary to grind a bit of plastic thickness with sandpaper. Users of "iPhone" when trimming the card themselves are advised to leave more plastic around the chip than necessary, and then adjust the size using a file.

How to insert a SIM card into the "iPhone": instructions



In order to install the SIM card in a special slot, you need to find the key in the kit, with which it is possible to remove the SIM card holder. To do this, you need to insert the key into the hole on the case, press, and then the card slot will slide out. Similarly, you can insert a SIM card into iPhone 6, the only difference is the location of the compartment under the card. What to do if there is no special key in the telephone set



Many users are faced with a situation where the key to install a SIM card is lost. The fact is that this is a very small accessory and it is easy to drop it out of the box and lose it, for example, when checking the device in the store. It is easy to replace the special key with a regular paper clip, which must be slightly folded. It is worth opening the compartment carefully so as not to damage the device.
