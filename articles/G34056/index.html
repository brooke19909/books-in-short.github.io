History and structure

Already on December 14, the railways of Ukraine received their own administration. A truly historic event. In 1998, the Charter of Railways of Ukraine was adopted. A rigorous document regulating the work of the enterprise.

Southern Railway of Ukraine

The largest Ukrainian division. Management of the road is located in Kharkov.



The year of the beginning of work is 1869. The total length of the tracks exceeds three thousand kilometers. Functioning takes place within the borders of Kharkov, Sumy, Poltava, Chernihiv and Kirovograd regions of Ukraine.

The direction is an important link with the Russian railways. Transit traffic exceeds sixty percent of total freight traffic.

The number of serviced enterprises exceeds three thousand. Among which are such giants as the Poltava Mining and Processing Plant, an oil refinery and automobile plants in the city of Kremenchug, Kharkov Tractor, Kryukovskoye Carriage Building Enterprise.





Electrification work began in 1956. The total number of stations is nearly three hundred.

It began its activities in 1873. Territory - southeast of Ukraine.

It has 244 stations. The total length of the tracks exceeds three thousand kilometers. The main freight traffic - iron ore, coal, equipment, grain.
