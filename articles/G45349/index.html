How to easily transfer contacts from phone to computer

Copying data to the backup from a mobile to a computer is a way to protect yourself from all sorts of troubles associated with the loss of information in case of loss of a mobile phone. It does not matter that this is a breakdown of the device, its loss or theft. The result is always the same. If the phone is lost, all information that was on it is automatically lost.

Modern mobile, as a rule, come with a software disk. It contains programs for working with the phone - for editing music files, for connecting to the Internet, and also for backing up data from a mobile phone to a computer. But with all the advantages of such programs, they all have one drawback. They are not universal, i.e. designed to work with any one phone model or with a small range of models. Thus, when replacing the phone, you also need to automatically change the software for working with it. And this is not entirely convenient.



It is much more convenient to work with a universal program that understands any phone.

Transferring a phone book using a disk

Copy contacts from phone

How to copy contacts from phone to computer? This is straightforward. We use other means

How to transfer contacts from phone to computer using Google service? It is even easier. Another way to copy contacts from your phone to your computer is to use the Google cloud service. AirDroid Program
