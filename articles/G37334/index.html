Backgammon. Rules of the game

Backgammon is a very ancient and addictive game. One of the main and common options are long backgammon. They are recognized by the international community as a sports discipline, and official tournaments have been held for several years now.

Not many can boast not so much a good game of backgammon, but simply the ability to play, knowledge of individual rules. Here are the basic conditions, having mastered which, you can already quite competently play long backgammon and subsequently achieve a decent level.

The goal of the game in this type of backgammon is to complete a full circle with all the checkers (counterclockwise), enter the house (the last quarter of the board for movement) and throw them away before the enemy.

It is designed for two players. The tournament takes place on a special board, which is divided into left and right parts, where 15 pieces are located, each player having a different color. The right to the first move is determined by throwing out one dawn (dice), the one who scores more starts first. If the number of points is equal, then throw the dawn again.

In the game "long backgammon", the rules make it possible to declare the opponent throwing invalid by saying the word "kestum" during the throw. When throwing, it is necessary that both tricks fall and lie steadily on one face in one half of the board. If this does not happen, then the throw is repeated.
