Aquapark "Equator" in Krasnodar: description, services

Water park "Equator" in Krasnodar is located almost on the shores of Lake Stara Kuban. Residents of the city with joy families and fun companies actively relax, swimming and riding on different slides. This is a whole complex with modern equipment occupying 7.5 hectares of coastal zone. This is an open place, so the water park operates only in the warm season, from the second half of May to the end of September, depending on weather conditions.

For people who do not have the opportunity or time to go to sea, such a replacement meets all expectations. In the article, we will consider what services the Equator water park in Krasnodar offers visitors, what attractions are available for children and adults, how much the ticket and additional features will cost. Poolside relaxation

In the "Equator" of Krasnodar, the price includes a stay on the rides all day. An adult ticket will cost 800 rubles, and a children's ticket is half cheaper. But this is only on weekdays, when there are fewer people. At the weekend, you have to pay accordingly - 1000 and 500 rubles. Rent an inflatable raft or circle - 200 rubles, but still take a deposit - 300.

Visitors can not only ride the slides, but also swim and sunbathe near the five pools. One is the most popular among children and adults, called the Wave. There you can ride on rubber rings or simply jump over the crests of the wave.

The rest are equipped with fountains and small waterfalls, under the jets of which you can do jet massage.

Around all pools are sun loungers with towels and sun umbrellas. You can lie down, relax and enjoy a refreshing drink.

Entertainment

The water park "Equator" in Krasnodar operates from 11:00 to 21:00. In the summer, often in the evenings, fun shows, foam parties, discos with invited popular disc jockeys are held. You can order a table for corporate meetings, professional events. There were times when people celebrated a wedding at Equator.

For lovers of "hot" there is an erotic club with a strip show. In the afternoon, training in water aerobics is organized. Those who wish can play billiards, participate in a competition for the best karaoke singer. Wet football matches are sometimes held.
