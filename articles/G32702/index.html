Zvezda Beach (Novosibirsk): description, photos, reviews

Soon the long-awaited summer will come, I believe that wonderful warm days await us ahead. It is no secret that both adults and children strive with all their hearts to use them in their entirety: to sunbathe, swim, ride a boat or a catamaran. Novosibirsk and its environs have a considerable number of beaches with varying degrees of amenities. According to the ratings based on the results of the polls, the most popular among residents of the metropolis is Zvezda Beach (Novosibirsk), in which a decent level of amenities is combined with accessibility. The object is attractive primarily because it has the opportunity to offer everyone a rental of gazebos with barbecue facilities, where you can spend a weekend or a holiday in the lap of nature. Zvezda Beach (Novosibirsk) is especially popular as a holiday destination for companies and families with children.





About the opening of the season

Vacationers are pleased with the free and free entry. Zvezda Beach (Novosibirsk), the photo report from which can be seen in the article, opens with the onset of calendar summer. But even in the warm months of spring (in April and May), the townspeople come here just for a walk. Usually the swimming season opens here on the 15th of June. Previously, the beach receives a special permit, which takes into account many factors (results of a survey of the bottom, water level, analysis of aquatic microflora, verification of the presence of a first-aid post and rescue station). Officially, the beach is called open after a special permit confirms that all indicators are normal.





Acquaintance

Zvezda Beach (Novosibirsk), the photo of which you see in the article, is located on the shore of the Ob Sea, in Akademgorodok, not far from the pine forest. And although, according to reviews, getting to this place without a car is not very easy, due to the fact that a bus stop is a couple of kilometers away, there are always a lot of tourists here during the season.

Vacationers highly appreciate the beach "Star" (Novosibirsk). Reviews characterize it as a fairly clean place. It is spacious here, vacationers share, there are many beautiful stone boulders. Those who wish can ride a banana or a catamaran.

Zvezda Beach (Novosibirsk): description

A little further away is the recreation park of the same name, which has many amenities for spending leisure time - swings, gazebos, a playground, football and volleyball courts, several cafes and paved walkways.

Zvezda Beach (Novosibirsk) is equipped with a paid comfortable toilet; the authors rate this as a plus in terms of ensuring cleanliness on the territory. Entrance to the beach by car is also paid (fare 250 rubles). Entrance for pedestrians is provided for free. Having left the car in the parking lot in front of the entrance (free of charge), vacationers need to walk about 600 m more to the entrance.

This object is the official beach of Novosibirsk, which has a rescue tower, a medical center and security.

Where to eat and relax?

In summer, on the beach there are stationary cafes, kiosks selling ice cream, soft drinks and barbecue. And of course, vacationers are given the opportunity to cook their own meals on the grill, for which you need to rent a gazebo.





Lounge

Beach lovers are invited to relax in the comfortable beach lounge located on the beach.





On the elegant individual covered terraces equipped with wicker furniture, those who wish can hide from the burning sun and relax in sun loungers with soft drinks. This vacation is chosen by people who value comfort. Capacity - 4-5 people.

Rates

A luggage storage and rental service are also available on the beach. Here you can get equipment for sports, sun loungers, umbrellas and other beach items, as well as leave valuables and charge the phone. Rent price:

  • sunbed - 100 rubles per hour;
  • chaise lounge chair - 100 rubles / 2 hours;
  • ball - 50 rubles;
  • catamaran - 500 rubles / 1 hour, 300 rubles / 30 minutes;
  • lounge - 300 rubles per hour.

Charging the phone costs 30 rubles. Using a left-luggage office - 100 rubles.

Nuances

It is known that entry to the territory of Zvezda beach by car is paid. A special check confirms the right to drive and park the vehicle until 9.00 the next day. Parking services are paid according to the tariffs of NEOKOM-SERVICE LLC (management company), which are valid at the time of their provision. For official carriers - "Blues", "Elite", "Mark" (taxi) travel is free.

For playing sports

One of the most exciting activities that make corporate vacations memorable and just an extra reason to meet friends is beach soccer - a beautiful and exciting sight that does not allow anyone to get bored. Since 2008, in the Zvezda recreation park, you can also stretch yourself at the beach soccer stadium, which fully complies with all the stipulated standards. The stadium has not only a field, but also a platform for 200 spectators. For fans of sports and active leisure, as well as those who want to hold a tournament or just play, equipment rental is provided. Rent price:

  • basketball, volleyball, soccer balls - 250 rubles per hour per unit;
  • basketball and volleyball courts, including shields, net, layer. chairs, rings, awnings - 400 rubles per hour;
  • stadium for beach soccer - 1700 rubles / 2 hours.

After the game, athletes can relax on the hot beach sand and in the cool waters of the Ob Sea.

Zvezda, beach (Novosibirsk): how to get there

The closest public transport to the Zvezda beach is the Mayak Cinema (816 m), and you can walk from it in 11 minutes. The beach can also be reached by buses No. 23, 36, 48, by minibus No. 6.7.

Moreover:

  • From the metro station "Marshala Pokryshkina" to the beach "Star" can be reached by shuttle bus number 11, 6. The road will take 74 minutes.
  • By shuttle bus number 36.41 - 77 min.
  • From the 2nd building of NSTU on bus number 6, 41 can be reached in 67 minutes.
  • From the October (Communal) bridge can be reached by bus. No. 41, minibus No. 6 for 66 minutes.
  • By bus No. 44, 45.48 - in 71 minutes.

Impressions of vacationers

Visitors claim that it is beautiful here even in winter. Beach "Star" is called the coolest place for parties.





Many people really like it: the beach is considered stylish and very comfortable. Among the advantages of the beach include: a large territory, convenient parking, excellent location and plenty of entertainment, gazebos, cafes, clean sand, warm sea, wooden parapet at the entrance. The disadvantages are the high cost of parking and the crowded beach.
