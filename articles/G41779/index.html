A place where it is interesting to go with children is the Contact Zoo (Perm)

Not only children, but also adults love animals. Especially not enough communication with cute animals for those who live in a big city. The contact zoo (Perm), which is located in the Iceberg Modern shopping center, fills the emotional deficit of communication with wildlife in adults and young Permians.





You can iron!

The Contact Zoo (Perm) was named "Hakuna Matata", becoming a place where visitors can not only enjoy the cute animals, but pet and treat them delicious. Both adults and kids like the fact that you can take photos with your favorite pets at the zoo.





Many visitors immediately became regulars at the zoo. After all, an hour and a half spent next to the animals give an emotional charge for the entire working week. At the same time, communication with smaller brothers is completely safe even for the smallest children - the zoo employees help to take the animal in their hands so as not to cause inconvenience to him, and show how to stroke them correctly.





Traveling to the zoo is also a way to enrich your knowledge of nature. Employees talk about the habits and characteristics of eating animals, how to properly maintain and raise them.

Who lives in the zoo?

Contact Zoo (Perm) "Hakuna Matata" has become home to the cheerful charming raccoons Bonnie and Clyde. Raccoons are still young, but, as the zoo owners hope, over time, when they grow up, they will be able to start a family and give Perm a lot of pretty raccoons.





Meerkats are surprising for those who see them for the first time - these are amazing desert animals similar to aliens. Meerkats Jisa and Futya are happy to advertise their contact zoo (Perm), easily communicating with visitors. It's hard to resist their charm!





The African pygmy hedgehog, unlike the Russian ones, has not sharp needles, but hard bristles. The white furry belly of the hedgehog raises exclamations in children and their parents. There is also a distant hedgehog's relative in the zoo - porcupine, whose long, up to 40 cm, needles are nothing more than wool, which changed during evolution.

But in chinchillas, the hair is soft and tender. However, if the chinchilla is frightened, it will begin to drop wool in bunches to blind the enemy, and will try to escape. Therefore, employees of the contact zoo are asked not to make noise next to her.





The biggest dormouse in the zoo is the ferret. He sleeps for 17-20 hours in a row, and during sleep he can be picked up, touched and stroked - he will not wake up.

A dwarf pig and a Cameroonian goat, without shyness, go for a treat, and the bravest guests of the zoo can hold in their hands a Madagascar cockroach, an Achatina snail or a bird-spider named Flower.





The royal python King does not refuse to communicate.





Who else lives at the zoo? Guinea pigs and rabbits, turtles and chipmunks - and they are glad to see visitors every day. More than 20 species of animals are represented in the Perm Contact Zoo. Beautiful healthy animals infect with their vitality and give positive emotions.

Fun for kids

Hakuna Matata, a contact zoo (Perm, Iceberg shopping center) has prepared a variety of activities for young visitors. Children can play on a specially equipped playground where there is everything that the kids like so much: a slide, a dry pool, a vine, which is so fun to swing on, and much more.





It is in the zoo that you can visit the exhibition of various pets every month and make yourself a true friend. They are organized by Perm breeders.

How to get there

Contact Zoo (Perm), whose address is simple: st. Popova, 22, shopping center "Iceberg", works every day from 10 to 21 hours. It should be noted that children under three years of age and disabled can visit here for free, and for large families and pensioners a 50% discount is provided.
