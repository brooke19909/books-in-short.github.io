How to transfer MTS points: options and methods

The company - the mobile operator MTS offers bonus services. This is characteristic not only for them, but also for other major mobile operators in the Russian Federation. This article will focus on the loyalty program. It includes not only the ability to accumulate points for paying for mobile services, but also spend them. But how to transfer MTS points to bonuses, you will learn further from the article. However, it is important to note one thing: this can be done not only with cash, but also with goods, services, and so on.

MTS Cashback

Using this method, you can easily transfer your points for paying for cellular services in real money. You just have to connect to this bonus program. It is worth noting that not only those who have their own SIM card from MTS, but also those who are the lucky owner of the home Internet or telephone, as well as a TV and router from MTS, can participate in it. Only those who own satellite TV do not participate. How to transfer MTS points? Now it will be about this.

So how to connect to the bonus program for the return of funds for payment of MTS mobile services? You just need to follow any of these action algorithms:

  1. Fill out the form on the official network site - bonus.ssl.mts.ru. This is the best way, because for it you will receive 50 bonus points. A trifle, however very nice.
  2. Next, you need to enter a telephone request by dialing * 111 * 455 * 1 #. You will send a USSD command in response to which information will be sent to you.
  3. Then you need to send SMS. To number 4555 write any Russian word and forward it.
  4. Call the official technical support service at 0890 and ask the operator to connect you this loyalty program. This method is the longest and most nervous.
  5. Get an official MTS Money plastic card, which will officially offer you a cashback when making purchases or paying for services.
  6. Make a purchase in the official offline store or MTS salon, and then inform the seller of his mobile phone number, to which he will be required to credit bonus points.

How to transfer MTS points? Very easy: get cashback from paying for mobile services. However, this can only be done after you connect this loyalty program.

Where to spend

MTS mobile and mobile operator offers you an extensive range of rewards for your accumulation of bonus points under the loyalty program. The most important of them, of course, is a discount on purchases in official MTS stores. However, if you do not need this, you can simply and very easily and quickly spend points for additional services. It is worth noting that the discount on goods is calculated in this way: ten points are equal to one ruble of the discount. For 10,000 points you will be given a discount of exactly one thousand Russian rubles. It is worth emphasizing that this discount can be accrued not only for ordinary goods (telephones, headphones, etc.), but also for ordinary communication services that you will connect or pay in the MTS official store.

Just go to the official MTS website and select the desired section. Then go to your account: enter your username and password. Next, just select the desired service and purchase it. If you are interested in a more thorough consultation, then on the official website there is information and an algorithm of actions for spending points.   3. If you are comfortable, use the filters to search for new products.

How to pay

Of course, if you decide to spend your points, initially you need to register on the site and log in to it. Next, you need to find the goods that you are going to pay, and begin the procedure. After that, enter your phone number to confirm that you have bonus points in your account. The next step is to pay for the goods with points by pressing only one button. Points will be deducted from your account and goods will arrive on your phone. If this is a discount, a code will come, if it is a service, it will connect to your phone number.

It is worth noting that transferring MTS points to another number is easy. Just go to the desired section on the MTS official website, enter the subscriber number and the amount of bonuses.

From this article, it became clear how to transfer MTS points into money. But how to turn them into services, we have not figured out yet. Therefore, information on this will follow.

Rewards

For 99 points you will receive only 30 minutes of calls. However, this applies only to the numbers of the mobile operator MTS. You can connect this service via a USSD call or on the operator‚Äôs official website.

For 149 points you will receive the same service, but not for 30 minutes, but for the whole hour. It costs, of course, not so expensive, and you will also be more tempted to buy exactly an hour, since in this way you save as much as 50 points.

SMS

For as many as 229 points, you can buy yourself fifty free SMS to the number of any mobile operator. Of course, such a service will cost much more than calls to mobile phone numbers, but for those who send messages, this is very suitable.

For 400 points of MTS, you will get the same service, but it will not be in half a hundred SMS, but in a full hundred. By purchasing this service you save as much as 50 points.

Hooter

For the extra 75 points that may remain in your account after some expensive purchase, you can purchase the Good-Ok service. With its help, all subscribers of any mobile operators will be able to call you and, until you answer, hear any trending music. This is very relaxing before an important call and, of course, uplifting.

Discounts in stores

If you have accumulated from 5,000 to 40,000 points, you are offered discounts on goods in MTS stores. It is worth recalling that one ruble discount on goods - ten points. Therefore, with 40,000 bonuses, you can reduce costs by as much as four thousand Russian rubles. It is completely justified.
