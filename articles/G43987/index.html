Biography and personal life of actress Maria Ilyina

In Russia there are many wonderful actors who know how to transform and get used to their role completely. One of these actresses is Maria Ilyina. Get to know her better.

Biography of Maria Ilyina

The young actress was born on August 31, 1991 in Moscow. She has a younger sister who is fourteen years old. She graduated from the prestigious Theater University named after B.V. Shchukin in 2013. She has a Slavic appearance, large blue eyes that captivate everyone. Her height is 165 cm, according to the horoscope is Virgo. He prefers to act in such movie genres as comedy, detective and melodrama. Photos of Maria Ilyina are distributed all over the Internet, she also puts them on social networks.



The future actress always dreamed of making a career in the field of acting, but was afraid to even start, as she was not confident in her abilities. But when she found out that her friends were submitting documents to the B.V. Shchukin Theater School, she could not resist and decided to take a chance. And she did quite unexpectedly for herself.

With her talent for acting, the ability to get used to the role and convey all the feelings of the heroines, she struck all the teachers, especially the course leader P. Lyubimtsev. So she entered the university, and her completely new life began.



Even during her studies at the university, she began to receive invitations to participate in various projects and shows, but the girl preferred not to be distracted from her studies and devoted all her time to completing assignments from her teachers. But after completing her studies in 2013, she began to accept invitations to participate in films and shows where she needed a bright appearance and good acting talent. Fortunately, Ilyina Maria Alexandrovna had all of this in abundance.

But, as happens with many aspiring actors, Maria has not yet received the main role. All the heroines she played had a bright charisma and some kind of inner strength, but obviously this was not enough to get the main role. Her heroines were somewhat similar to Maria Ilyina herself. They all could truly love and believe in people. Mary herself was waiting for her only one with whom she would share fate.

Filmography

For the first time on the screens of the country appeared in 2010, when she played a role in the film "Christmas Trees". In 2014, she starred in the 1st season of the 32nd series of the series "Rosehip Aroma" in the role of Nadia. In the 1st season of the fourth series of the series "Spring Exacerbation", Maria appears in the role of Alena. Personal life

Maria Ilyina married Anton Astapenko in 2016, who was her best friend in the past. She changed her last name to her husband's last name. In April (the 29th), she had a daughter, whom she named Vasilisa. Both Anton and Maria are madly in love with their girl and wish her a happy future.
