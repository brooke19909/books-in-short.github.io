Yalutorovsk hotels: addresses, room reservation, services, prices, guest reviews

The small provincial town of Yalutorovsk in the Tyumen region is famous for its history. After all, it was here that the Decembrists once served a link. A lot of tourists come here to visit the Yalutorovsky prison, where you can plunge into the past of our country. And so that after a busy day of excursions you can relax in a comfortable and cozy atmosphere, you are invited to the hotel Yalutorovsk.

Hotel Siberia

The small mini-hotel Sibir occupies the second floor of a two-story building in the city center and is attractive to tourists primarily for its convenient location. The train station and bus station are a five-minute walk away. Within walking distance from the hotel there are two supermarkets, several cafes, a central market. Guests enjoy free parking and free Wi-Fi access. Corridors and halls are equipped with CCTV cameras. Laundry services are available at an additional cost. To accommodate guests, several categories of rooms are offered:

  • single standard - 1200 rubles per day;
  • single with a bathroom - 1650 rubles per day;
  • double economy - 1700 rubles per day;
  • double with a bathroom - 1700 rubles a day;
  • triple classic - 2550 rubles per day.

Each room has a refrigerator, TV, electric kettle, dishes. All rooms are provided with bottled drinking water. At the Sibir Hotel three days prior to arrival, cancellation is free of charge. The hotel is located in Yalutorovsk of the Tyumen region at the address: Lenin street, house 56.

Hotel "Siberia", reviews of tourists

Guests share their impressions of their stay in the hotel in their reviews.

  • At the Sibir mini-hotel, guests appreciated above all its convenient location in the very center of the city, near the railway and bus stations.
  • The rooms are small and clean, the furniture is standard, cleaning is done every day.
  • Guestrooms provide bottled drinking water.
  • Unfortunately, some of the rooms have amenities on the floor.
  • Some guests believe that for a rather modest living conditions the cost is too high.

Travel tips - if you have chosen the Sibir Hotel among the hotels in Yalutorovsk, book your rooms in advance.

Hotel "Retro"

This hotel is also located in the center of Yalutorovsk, is a building of classical architecture. The hotel has its own restaurant and cafe. Within walking distance is a supermarket. Parking spaces are provided free of charge, and there is free WI-FI throughout. Laundry and ironing services are available at an extra charge. The hotel restaurant will help you organize a banquet. Pets are allowed by prior arrangement. Accommodation for guests is offered in the following rooms:

  • single standard - 2625 rubles per day;
  • double budget - 2675 rubles per day for accommodation of two people, 2350 rubles per day if one person lives;
  • triple improved - from 2800 to 3600 rubles per day, depending on the number of residents;
  • in junior suites - from 4200 to 5500 rubles per day, depending on how many people are accommodated in the room;
  • in apartments - from 4600 to 5900 rubles per day, respectively;
  • in a family room - 4410 rubles for three people and 4725 rubles for one person.

All room rates are inclusive of breakfast, which is served in a buffet restaurant. All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, telephone, TV, free toiletries. At the Retro Hotel, three days before arrival, cancellation is free of charge. The hotel is located in Yalutorovsk of the Tyumen region at the address: Lenin street, house 19.

Hotel "Aragats Plaza"

If you are interested in hotels in Yalutorovsk on the P402 highway, the Aragats Plaza Hotel is located at a distance of two kilometers from the city center. The hotel has free secure parking, free Wi-Fi access. You can order a transfer. It has its own restaurant and bar. Tour services are available. Each room has its own original interior. The following room categories are offered to accommodate guests:

  • standard - 2200 rubles per day;
  • Junior Suite - 3000 rubles per day;
  • Luxury - 5000 rubles per day.

The price includes breakfast. All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, TV, fridge and telephone. In order to book a room, please contact the administrator by phone. The Aragats Plaza Hotel is located in Yalutorovsk at the address: Severnaya Street, 100.
