Hotel "Villa Jasmine" (Kabardinka)

Hotel Villa Jasmine (Kabardinka) is located in a quiet and comfortable recreation area near a pine forest and an orchard. Not far from it is the famous "Old Park". Comfortable rooms, a wonderful location, excellent cuisine, polite staff will provide an unforgettable and pleasant stay at the Villa Jasmine Hotel.

Description

"Villa Jasmine" (Kabardinka) is located away from the federal highway. The village‚Äôs central beach is only two hundred meters away. Such an arrangement makes this place quiet and calm: the noise of cars and the loud music of evening discos on the waterfront do not bother. The sea and air here are always clean, since there is not a single large industrial enterprise and port nearby.





The hotel Villa Jasmine (Kabardinka) offers its guests high-class service. It offers early booking and shuttle services. The cost of leisure at the hotel includes breakfast, the use of a parking lot, barbecue facilities, hammocks for relaxation, a pool and a playground for children. There is no restaurant or cafe on site, guests eat on a cozy veranda-dining room. To order, they prepare a full lunch and dinner. Vacationers also have access to laundry facilities.

Throughout the season, the hotel Villa Jasmine (Kabardinka) organizes excursion programs. 10 km from the village is a large resort town of Gelendzhik, where there are entertainment for every age and taste.

Number of rooms and living conditions

To accommodate guests, Villa Jasmine (Kabardinka) has 23 comfortable rooms, the interior of which is made in a modern style. Room categories are as follows:

  • One-room double "Standard".
  • Two-room "Suite".
  • Three-room "Penthouse".

The standard room has all the necessary amenities. It is equipped with a double bed, bedside tables, an armchair, a wardrobe for storage of linen and a dressing table with a mirror, a plasma TV. The bathroom is in the room.

Room "Suite" - two-room. The bedroom is equipped in the same way as the Standard room. The lounge has a sofa, and a chest of drawers is for storing things. It also has air conditioning, a fridge and a safety deposit box. Both the bedroom and the living room have a balcony and satellite TV.

The most comfortable for living is a three-room "Penthouse". The room is equipped with cozy sofas, ottomans and a dressing table, beautiful mirrors and a small coffee table.





The highlight of the room is two bathrooms in the bedrooms, one of them has a jacuzzi. The third room is a kitchenette with a sink, fridge, microwave, kettle and cooking surface.

Each room has a small balcony with beautiful wicker furniture (a table and two chairs). Rooms are cleaned daily, linen and towels are changed every three days or more often (upon request).
