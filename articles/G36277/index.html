Lazarevskoye, hotel "Surf": reviews of tourists

Are you planning a vacation on the Black Sea coast and are considering which place to choose? A great option for a vacation would be the village "Lazarevskoye", hotel "Priboy". Why is this hotel so attractive, read below.





Description

The location on the first beach line, comfortable rooms, a restaurant with delicious cuisine, excellent wireless Internet, a chic swimming pool, a wide range of services are just some of the advantages that the Priboy Hotel, Lazarevskoye boasts. Reviews from tourists for these and some other reasons, she deserves, mostly positive.

At your service there are rooms of different levels of comfort, a beach with different types of entertainment, a heated pool, a backlight and even a waterfall, a tour desk, restaurants and cafes, a billiard room and a beauty salon.

Who should relax in such an amazing place as the village "Lazarevskoye", the hotel "Surf"? First of all, for travelers who are not accustomed to sacrifice their comfort even on vacation, but who know how to "count" their money, appreciate the proximity of the clear sea, love outdoor activities and excellent service. Families with children here will enjoy a large selection of entertainment for children, and older tourists - healthy sea and mountain air, as well as a mild climate.

Hotel "Surf" (Lazarevskoye), the photo of which you see below, is located only 10 meters from the Central Embankment. Nearby are an amusement park, a concert hall "Coliseum" and the Center for National Cultures. A fifteen minute leisurely walk from the hotel you will see the water park "Nautilus" - a great vacation spot for children and adults.





Hotel "Surf" (Lazarevskoye) in Russia is located in a surprisingly picturesque place, surrounded by mountain and sea landscapes, as well as in the bright colors of tropical plants. All year round you can stroll and enjoy the beauty of the nature of the Black Sea coast.

Terms of accommodation

For tourists who want to relax in a place such as the village "Lazarevskoye", the hotel "Priboy", the following rules apply: check-in at two in the afternoon, check-out at eleven in the morning. Tourists reviews

Guests at this hotel respond mostly to him in a positive way. But there are exceptions. Below are the impressions of people vacationing in a place like the Priboy Hotel (Lazarevskoye).

Reviews of tourists about what they liked in the hotel:

  • Daily cleaning and towel change.
  • Excellent service, polite staff, always ready to help.
  • Great views from the rooms.
  • Convenient location - proximity to the sea, entertainment places and shops.
  • Spacious balconies in all rooms.
  • Delicious cuisine in the restaurants and cafes at the hotel.
  • Warm floors in the bathroom - especially nice if you come not in high season.
  • Affordable prices for accommodation.

And the cons of the hotel, which guests noted:

  • There is no private parking directly at the hotel, there is paid parking for guests, but it is difficult to find.
