Choosing an album for photos

Photo albums come in three forms:

- with plastic windows for pictures;

- with cardboard sheets on which you can stick photos of different sizes;

- with magnetized sheets.

The first view is the most convenient and practical, but excludes the possibility of arranging a memorable album by making inscriptions under the pictures. If you prefer the second view, this will allow you to fully use your imagination, sticking pictures of different sizes in different locations and commenting on events. Magnetic sheets will also help show creativity by creating an album. With your own hands, you can arrange the original thing without applying glue and corners. But over time, the magnetic sheet is scratched, while the photos come off. Therefore, if you want to make a beautiful and truly valuable thing for a long time, you should take a regular album with cardboard sheets as the basis for work.
