Hotel "Alexander", Montenegro. Rating of hotels in Montenegro

Currently, Montenegro, the photo of which is presented in the article, is one of the most popular resort destinations among domestic tourists. Over the past few years, the number of vacationers here has increased significantly. The many advantages of this place make tourists come back here again and again.



General information

The warm Adriatic Sea captivated the hearts of many travelers. The coast boasts magnificent nature and wonderful beaches. Currently, the tourism sector of the country is actively developing. The selection of suitable vacation spots is truly huge (this is indicated by the rating of Montenegro hotels ). Luxury lovers can find private apartments or luxury villas. As a budget option, you can consider pensions and cheap hotels.

Pricing aspects

Montenegro is known for the affordable cost of all services. Prices are significantly lower than in other domestic resorts. At the same time, the quality of service is maintained at a decent level. It is the combination of the above advantages that makes this place so attractive for Russians. For example, a three-star hotel located in Budva will cost 50-100 euros per night. Ulcinj prices are even lower.
