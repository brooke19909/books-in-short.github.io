Aldemar Royal Mare Suites (Greece, Crete, Hersonissos): description of the hotel and territory, reviews

A luxurious and relaxing stay will give travelers the Aldemar Royal Mare Suites. The most modern equipment, many opportunities for entertainment, as well as quality and friendly service - all this will make your vacation unforgettable.





What the hotel offers

Aldemar Royal Mare Suites is not for nothing called a paradise, because his philosophy is to care about the pleasure of guests. Here you can experience the famous Cretan hospitality. As if merging with the picturesque surroundings, the hotel is surrounded by green gardens. You can have a good time on the well-kept beach or swim in the refreshing turquoise pools. This hotel has a gym, tennis courts and other opportunities for outdoor activities. A good option for adherents of a healthy lifestyle. And, of course, one cannot fail to mention the thalasso center, which is considered one of the best in the world.





Where is the hotel

Aldemar Royal Mare Suites Resort is located in the resort village of Anissaras, about 3 km from the city of Hersonissos, located on the northern coast of Crete. It is worth noting that next to the hotel there is a bus stop that provides regular connections with the cities of Heraklion (25 km) and Agios Nikolaos (30 km).

Resort Rooms

Comfortable living conditions are offered by this hotel. A good and comfortable room is an integral part of a quality stay. The following accommodation options are available to guests:

  • From the windows of the double standard room you can enjoy views of the luxurious green garden or the azure sea. Guests can stay in a large or a pair of single beds. For relaxation, a soft sofa is provided. There is a private balcony (or terrace, if we are talking about the ground floor).
  • Double VIP rooms consist of a large bright bedroom and a cozy living room. Their main feature is the availability of access to the pool, which is shared by a block of four rooms (there are sunbeds and umbrellas for the number of guests).
  • Junior suites are spacious rooms, designed as a studio. The bedroom is separated from the living room by a decorative partition. Through the veranda you can go to the shared pool block.
  • VIP-Suite in a spacious square combines a bedroom and a living room. Opposite the bed is a sliding glass door that leads to the veranda. If the room is located on the ground floor, through it you will be taken to a shared pool. If we talk about the upper levels, then through the glass windows you can enjoy the sea. The bathroom has a jacuzzi.
  • The presidential apartments in their layout and equipment resemble a junior suite. The main difference is the presence of a private pool, as well as close proximity to the beach.




Amenities at Guest Apartments

One of the best in Crete is considered the Aldemar Royal Mare Suites hotel. Tours are very popular, Vedas here guests can feel real home comfort. This is achieved by the following amenities in the rooms:

  • luxurious bathroom equipped with shower or jacuzzi;
  • a minibar, which is filled for an additional fee (guests often use it as a refrigerator);
  • a safe with a combination lock for storing your money, documents and other valuables;
  • TV with cable or satellite channels;
  • a player for viewing video from laser discs;
  • a phone that will provide you with a connection with the reception, as well as access to an international line;
  • a kettle as well as a coffee maker that provide you with round-the-clock access to hot drinks.




Supply system

Resort Aldemar Royal Mare Suites 5 * offers guests a choice of four food systems. You can choose exactly those conditions that will satisfy all your needs:

  • The Golden All Inclusive package includes three meals a day, as well as alcoholic and refreshing drinks throughout the day. A distinctive feature of this program can be considered that for your dinner you can choose one of seven restaurants serving dishes of cuisines of different nations of the world.
  • Full board is a traditional buffet that is served three times a day. Meals are provided in the main dining room.
  • The full board "VIP" is an ideal option for those guests who are accustomed to luxury. The most exquisite dishes and delicacies will be present in your diet. The hotel staff will satisfy your every whim. For example, you can create your own menu or order dinner on the beach.
  • Half board is suitable for active tourists who spend little time at the hotel. It includes breakfast and dinner.




restaurants and bars

Experienced chefs working in the restaurants of the Aldemar Royal Mare Suites 5 * hotel (Crete) will be able to satisfy the most demanding gourmets. To verify this personally visit the following establishments:

  • Restaurant "El Greco" invites you to enjoy delicious dishes prepared according to traditional Cretan recipes. The colorful atmosphere is maintained thanks to the sophisticated interior, as well as the flowering courtyard.
  • Symposio is the main restaurant that serves as the dining room. It is here that guests can enjoy a variety of dishes presented at the buffet.
  • Restaurant "Kandia" is an ideal place for adherents of a healthy diet. It offers a wide range of dietary and vegetarian dishes that are distinguished by exquisite tastes.
  • Restaurant "Albatross" is located on the seashore. Here you can try culinary masterpieces made from fresh fish and other seafood.
  • Nautilus is a cozy beach bar. He specializes in Mexican cuisine. You will enjoy the colorful interior and fiery music.
  • Dionissos is a gourmet French restaurant. To experience all the charms of the Provence cuisine, complement your meal with aromatic collection wine.

Negative hotel reviews of Aldemar Royal Mare Suites (Greece, Crete, Hersonissos)

It is worth noting that an objective impression of a resort can be made only on the basis of traveler reviews. Holidays in Aldemar Royal Mare is a cherished dream of many tourists. Nevertheless, in order not to be disappointed, you should know about some of the negative characteristics of this hotel:

  • the furniture and plumbing in the rooms are a bit dated, and therefore require replacement;
  • going into the sea a few meters, you will find that the bottom is strewn with sharp stones;
  • maids do not pay any attention to balconies and terraces during cleaning;
  • for many guests, the dress code in the restaurant comes as a surprise (if you do not have the appropriate clothing, that is, the possibility of delivering food to your room);
  • in the sand on the beach there are a lot of cigarette butts, candy wrappers and other garbage;
  • there are a lot of ants in the rooms (especially the problem is relevant for the first floor);
  • guests of neighboring hotels are allowed on the territory of a private beach, which often causes crowds and conflicts;
  • old metal deck chairs;
  • lamb dishes are poorly pickled, and therefore practically do not chew;
  • there is practically no animation at the hotel (with the exception of entertainment for children and live music in the evenings);
  • ordering a room with a double bed, you are likely to get a couple shifted;
  • shabby bathrobes;
  • very slippery and cold tiles on the bathroom floor;
  • there are always strong waves on the hotel beach (it is possible to find a quieter place, but the entrance there is paid);
  • there is no normal bar on the beach (of all amenities - a tent with water and beer);
  • paid tea, coffee and drinking water in the restaurant (although you can go down to the bar for free drinks);
  • slippery tiles by the pool;

  • Carpets in the corridors of the hotel almost never vacuum.
