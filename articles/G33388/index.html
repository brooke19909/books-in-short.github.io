Hotel Celuisma Cabarete 3 *, Cabarete, Dominican Republic: overview, description and reviews of tourists

Cabarete Beach House ex Celuisma Cabarete 3 * hotel is located in the north of the Dominican Republic, near the city of Cabarete. The distance from the airport to the hotel is about 17 km.

Hotel Description

The hotel has 9 buildings, in which there are 170 rooms designed for two, three or five guests. The smallest room is 20 square meters. m, and the largest - 56 square meters. m. Hotel Celuisma Cabarete 3 * ("Playa Dorada") does not allow children under 18 years of age. The hotel does not allow pets. Upon entry to the hotel requires the presence of an identity document and a credit card. Check-in starts at 15.00 local time.

Service

All tourists who visited the Celuisma Cabarete hotel emphasize the good attitude to the vacationers by the entire hotel staff. Friendly administrators always meet guests at the reception. The only thing worth highlighting is that there are no Russian-speaking staff at the hotel, so if you do not know English, it is recommended that you learn the most common phrases and phrases before you travel to Celuisma Cabarete.





At the reception you can order a service such as a transfer, its cost must be checked with the administrator. The rooms are cleaned every day, towels, hygiene items are changed.

Rooms

All rooms at the Celuisma Cabarete 3 * hotel (Dominican Republic, Puerto Plata) have a balcony, which is very important in conditions of high average daily temperatures and high humidity.

Each hotel room has the following:

  • one or two beds;
  • bathroom;
  • air conditioning;
  • safe;
  • mini fridge;
  • TV with cable TV.




Tourists who visited the Celuisma Cabarete Hotel note the fact that there is no Wi-Fi in the rooms, they do not even provide such a service here for a fee.

Nutrition

Hotel Celuisma Cabarete 3 * (Dominican Republic) offers food in the following categories: "breakfast only" and "all inclusive". Breakfast in the main restaurant starts at 7.30 in the morning. Between lunch and dinner, guests can enjoy local and European dishes. In addition to the main restaurant, the hotel has 2 more establishments of European, local and Thai cuisines. To spend an evening in one of these restaurants, you need to reserve a table in advance.

At the pool for the convenience of holidaymakers is a cafe-bar. In it you can try alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks of local and imported luxury. The restaurant's menu has various snacks and salads, fruits, as well as desserts of its own production. Appetizers include sausages, cheeses and barbecue. The pool bar is open until 21.30.





Tourists who visited the hotel Celuisma Cabarete 3 *, reviews leave mostly positive. They note that the food in the main restaurant is very good, varied, always something new appears. With such a large assortment of food, every tourist finds a dish to his liking.

Hotel Territory

The hotel has 2 outdoor pools, one of them has a bar. In one of the buildings there is a gym in which everyone can use the new simulators and professional advice of experienced trainers. The spa is very popular among tourists. Experienced massage therapists offer more than 10 types of various massages; face and body skin care services are also presented. The Celuisma Cabarete has a beauty center where experienced hairdressers, stylists and make-up artists will help you choose the look for any event.

For horse lovers, the hotel has organized such activities as horse riding. Even if you have never ridden, experienced instructors will help you with this. Horse riding is not only exotic, but also informative, because during skiing tourists will find themselves in the most beautiful places of the Dominican Republic and get to know more closely its history and sights.





In one of the buildings of the hotel there is a souvenir shop where all tourists can buy small memorabilia with the symbols of the Dominican Republic. Many visitors to the hotel come here because of the windsurfing center, which is located on the beach. At the service of tourists in the center there are always instructors who are ready to help beginner athletes. Here you can also rent all the necessary equipment and equipment.

Beach

The hotel has its own beach, which is located 50 meters from the buildings. White sand, azure water and coconut palms are the main natural attractions of the coastline. On the beach there are always sunbeds and umbrellas for vacationers. If you are a lover of beach sports, then for you there is a volleyball court. There are no places for changing clothes on the beach, so wearing a bathing suit is better in advance.

Attractions nearby

  • Cabarete. This small settlement used to be a haven for fishermen. Now it is a city with developed infrastructure. Such a transformation, Cabarete owes its natural location. Due to the fact that there are no natural breakwaters in the vicinity of the city, this place is very popular among windsurfers and kayserfers. For tourists, hotels, bars, restaurants and night clubs were built on the territory of the city. During the day in Cabarete you can wander through the quiet streets, looking at the local flavor and trying exotic dishes in the cafe, and at night in the city a huge number of places of entertainment are opened, where everyone can dance in the fiery rhythms of rumba and salsa, as well as try Dominican rum.




  • El Choco National Park. The reserve area is more than 77 square meters. m. On this territory, you can meet more than 900 species of exotic plants, 40 species of birds and bats. Here it will be interesting not only to a person who is fond of nature, but also to those who love adventure and risk. Tourists who visited this park strongly recommend using the services of a professional guide (at least for the first visit), since the territory of the reserve contains a large number of different paths, which are easy to get confused in. In addition, the guide tells a lot of interesting information about plants and animals that live in this area. The main attraction of the park is the cave complex, which houses underground pools. This place is shrouded in mystery and mystery, because it is not in vain that so many legends go about it.

Travel tips from Celuisma Cabarete

In order for the rest in the hotel we are considering not to leave unpleasant impressions and the vacation not to be completely ruined, before the trip, you should ask about the reviews of the former guests and take into account some of their tips:

  • at the hotel, none of the staff speaks Russian, so if you are not good at foreign languages, you should learn the most common phrases;
  • the hotel does not have Wi-Fi, tourists are informed about this in advance;
  • evening entertainment at the hotel is rare, so it’s better to leave for Cabarete for a “nightlife”;
  • if you are going to a hotel for surfing, it is better to choose the period from February to March - at this time the waves on the beach are the strongest;




  • it is better not to turn off the air conditioning in the room, as it will be impossible to stay in it because of strong stuffiness;
  • at the entrance to the hotel, all guests require a passport and a credit card;
  • parking on the territory of the complex is completely free, here you can leave your car for any period;
  • at the reception you can order all the tours you are interested in.

In general, the hotel Celuisma Cabarete 3 * many tourists recommend considering as a possible accommodation option. Have a nice rest!
