A little bit about the company

The company "Electrolux" was established in Sweden in 1908. But then it was also called Elektomechanika and was engaged in the production of vacuum cleaners. During the ubiquitous distribution of electricity, the company cooperated with another Swedish manufacturer, called Lux, who was engaged in the production of gas lamps. But at the beginning of the 20th century, nobody needed such products. As a result, the companies launched the first compact vacuum cleaner in tandem. This baby weighed only 14 kilograms.

The full merger took place in 1919. Since then, the manufacturer began to call himself "Electrolux". The company launches its first refrigerator in 1928. And at that time, the unit made a real revolution. The first washing machine was created in 1959. It was a real breakthrough. Since then, Electrolux has become the first company in terms of the production of household appliances. To this day, the products of the manufacturer are in quite large and steady demand. A striking example is the Electrolux EWT1066EDW washing machine. We will begin a review of it right now. And the first in line will be the design and appearance of the machine.
