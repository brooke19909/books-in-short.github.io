Extreme Park in Mariupol - a wonderful vacation for the whole family

"Extreme Park" Mariupol for more than 14 years is a favorite vacation spot for residents and visitors. It is interesting for both adults and children - the organizers of the park took care of everyone, so it's hard to say what age category it is mainly oriented to.

What is attractive about Extreme Park of Mariupol?

The atmosphere of the holiday always reigns in the park: music plays, in the evening spotlights, lights and illumination are lit. Animators in costumes of cartoon heroes walk around the territory and guarantee visitors a good mood. You can have fun in your free time not only on weekends, Extreme Park in Mariupol is open even on weekdays (except Mondays) from April to October. Exact dates depend on weather conditions and vary slightly each year. So, if it is warm autumn, the park is open until the first days of November.



The park regularly hosts festive events. For example, Ivan Kupala Day, Happy Ice Cream Celebration, City Day and many others.

The leisure center is located in a very convenient location - just 10-15 minutes from the city center. Nearby is the picturesque river Kalchik and there is a very beautiful and beautifully landscaped park of culture and recreation named after N.A. Gurov. In 2016, the construction of a zoo began in its vicinity. In the past few years, a kart track has been operating around the territory of the leisure center.

In the "Extreme Park" of Mariupol, there are 14 modern attractions that were brought to the city from Russia, Italy and the Netherlands. All of them meet safety standards and pass annual technical inspection. The park management does not lag behind the new trends in the entertainment industry, so some attractions satisfy the needs of the active part of the population in extreme recreation.





The pricing policy of the park is accessible to wide layers of the population, and compared to other similar Ukrainian places of recreation, the cost of tickets for attractions is quite low.

Park area and infrastructure

Every year, "Extreme Park" Mariupol is visited by approximately 600,000 people. Residents of the city are very fond of this place and show visitors to the city as one of the main attractions.





The territory of Extreme Park is very clean, well-groomed and beautiful. In addition to the attractions, there is a magnificent free playground with colorful swings and carousels. Starving vacationers can have a snack or try barbecue in a cafe. There are several dry closets.

There are shopping pavilions in the park where you can buy ice cream, soft drinks, cotton candy and popcorn, as well as souvenir products. Nearby there is a parking lot with lots of parking spaces for visitors.

Park rides and entrance fees

All attractions can be divided into the following categories:

  • for children weighing up to 30 kg (height above 80 cm);
  • for minors up to 50 kg;
  • for adults and children weighing more than 50 kg (above 100 or 120 cm);
  • extreme.

The most visited attractions are the Ferris Wheel (a little over 30 m high) and the Wild Slides. Children like bumper boats, trains, Flower Carousel and Lazy River.

Fans of extreme relaxation can visit the "Free Fall Tower" and Space Gone, which are breathtaking even for adults.



Prices in the "Extreme Park" Mariupol are quite affordable: the entrance ticket costs 3 UAH., For children's attractions - 10-15 UAH., For adults - 15-25 UAH. There is the possibility of acquiring package offers that include the cost of several specific attractions.

Reviews

Residents look forward to the end of April - beginning of May, when the Extreme Park opens in Mariupol. It impresses most visitors. Many people come here regularly, take their children and rest with pleasure.

On sites and forums, visitors enthusiastically share with each other the vivid impressions experienced while riding the rides. Some are afraid and hesitate to visit, for example, "Lumping". Others, on the contrary, several times "test their strength" on the "Wild Train" or "Tower".

Younger and older children also love Extreme Park in Mariupol. This is the main entertainment of the guys on the weekend. According to the reviews of parents, many children try to behave themselves all week, so that later mothers and fathers take them to the park.
