Car receiver Sony DSX-A30E: specifications, manuals and reviews

When you drive in a car, nothing improves your mood like beautiful music. Radio Sony DSX-A30E is able to give unforgettable moments of acoustic accompaniment. It has a stylish and modern design. It looks presentable in any car and has all the functions necessary for a good radio.

Sony DSX-A30E: Specifications

This radio tape recorder has an RDS tuner with auto-tuning, the range parameters of which are FM / MW / SW. You can connect a USB drive to the device from which MP3 / WMA / AAC formats are played . The device supports ID3 tags.

The car radio is equipped with an eight-character liquid crystal display. There is support for Russian text, front input AUX and USB, as well as an RCA output for the preamplifier, as well as a three-band graphic equalizer. The device can be controlled both by buttons from the panel, and from the remote control.

The product runs on a 12 V car battery. The external panel is removable and the backlight is green. The maximum power of the sound channel is 50 watts.

Instrument parameters (width / height / depth): 17850120 mm. Product weight - about 0.7 kg.

The radio remote control RM-X211 comes with the radio, as well as the parts necessary for installing and connecting this equipment. Made in black color device Sony DSX-A30E.

How to turn on bluetooth? This question is asked by many users of this radio. The answer is simple: there is no bluetooth in this device, so you cannot turn it on.

Device Care





Car receiver Sony DSX-A30E does not require any special care. Sometimes it is necessary to change the old lithium battery on the remote control to a new CR2025, since over time it discharges and control of the remote control becomes impossible. An improper battery must not be used, as there is a danger of explosion. The lithium battery should be stored out of the reach of children.

To make contact with the device more reliable, wipe the battery with a dry cloth. When placing the battery in the remote control, the polarity must be observed. Do not take the battery with tweezers or other metal devices, this can cause a short circuit, and it should also be kept away from heat sources.

Failures in the operation of the device can be caused by contamination of the contacts that connect the front panel to the device case. To clean the contacts, you need to remove the front panel and wipe them with cotton. During this process, the ignition must be turned off. Do not touch the contacts with your fingers or metal products.

Precautionary measures





If the machine has warmed up while standing in a hot place, the Sony DSX-A30E cannot be turned on until the cabin has cooled down. Do not leave a removable front panel, as well as portable audio devices inside the car, as strong heat from the sun can damage them. When the device is functioning, an antenna with an electric drive automatically goes out to improve the quality of the reproduced sound. The radio should be protected from moisture.
