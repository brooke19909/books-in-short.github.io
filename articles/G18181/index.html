The history of the castle

Many people, looking at such beauty, think about who could even come up with such a brilliant architectural project? Well, the history of such structures is always interesting. Hohenzollern Castle was built in the 11th century. The building is pronounced Gothic style. Peaked roofs, upward battlements of towers, high walls, the chapel of St. Michael - he cannot but attract attention. By the way, Hohenzollern is fully consistent with all those ideas about knightly castles that the people had in the 19th century.

Of course, the interior of Hohenzollern is as luxurious as its appearance. The walls are lined with natural wood of precious noble species, the ceilings are painted with amazing patterns, the massive and majestic columns are made of marble ... All this looks really rich and tasteful. And the elegant interior is completed by elegant drapery and coffered ceilings. Needless to say, Hohenzollern Castle is a real gem of all of Prussia.

Popularity

Hohenzollern has become incredibly popular with tourists, and among locals too. Today, about 300 thousand people travel to these places every year. By the way, for 12 years the castle was closed to visitors - from 1978 to 1990. The fact is that then there was a strong earthquake, and the structure was damaged. But today it is still majestically towering on a hill.
