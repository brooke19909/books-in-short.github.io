history of the company

In 1975, Vittorio Merloni, an Italian, created the company Merloni Elettrodomestici. Ariston equipment, characterized by excellent quality and relatively low price, quickly became a leader in the Italian market. Gradually, it began to spread throughout Europe, and then - to be delivered to our country. It should be noted that the Indesit brand also belongs to this company, so that all the information that is available in the article can rightfully be applied to this technology.

Buyers immediately noticed the new manufacturer, and soon the first reviews appeared. The Ariston refrigerator, they claimed, belonged to the middle segment of household appliances: at a relatively low cost, it performed all its functions perfectly, but it also had some drawbacks.

Soon there were even production in our country. The Ariston refrigerator is good for this - spare parts for it are not taken across half the world, but are delivered from several domestic plants.

What is the information in the official instructions?

  1. Cold air in the chambers is distributed extremely evenly. This contributes not only to better food safety, but also to minimal energy consumption, since the compressor will turn on much less frequently.

Cons Options Full No Frost

  1. Since technology involves constant air circulation, products can dry out faster. This is especially true just for vegetables and fruits.
  2. The freezer for refrigerators of this type is less than about 20% (compared with conventional models), since all the necessary equipment takes up a lot of usable space.
  3. During operation, an increased noise level may be observed , since the sound of the compressor is superimposed on the noise of the running fan.
  4. Because of this, in some cases, energy consumption can increase significantly.
  5. Of course, this kind of equipment will cost significantly more than ordinary products.

A quick temperature recovery after opening the door is also not an indicator, since in many respects this characteristic depends on the perfection of the heat-insulating layer, which is becoming cheaper and more functional every year. In addition, many experts agree that conventional refrigeration chambers can maintain a lower temperature longer when the power is turned off, since they do not have channels for supplying air inside.
