Hotel on about. Phuket YK Patong Resort 3 *: tourist reviews, description and beach

A tourist destination such as Thailand is becoming increasingly popular, and travelers with a small budget choose the YK Patong Resort 3 * hotel for themselves. The reviews of the guests of this hotel allow you to compile a complete and objective picture of impressions about it.





General impressions of the hotel

More and more tourists, talking about traveling to Thailand, opt for YK Patong Resort 3 *. Guest reviews focus on the fact that the hotel offers a fairly decent level of relaxation. There is also a small outdoor pool directly on the roof, as well as a traditional Thai restaurant and a small bar with all kinds of alcoholic and carbonated sugary drinks.

Particular attention should be paid to the rooms of YK Patong Resort 3 *. Reviews say that although they reflect the long history and dilapidation of the hotel, they are in a perfectly livable condition. They have all the amenities a modern traveler needs, as well as a balcony from which you can look at the vibrant Thai streets, getting to know the local color and the rich rhythm of life.





Hotel Location

Hotel YK Patong Resort is located in Phuket, in the central part of it. In the area there are many different shops, catering establishments with local cuisine, as well as markets and flea markets. Just a 5-minute leisurely walk from the hotel lies a beautiful beach. The hotel is approximately 45 km from Phuket International Airport .

Guest apartments

In any hotel, one of the main indicators of the level of service is the rooms. This also applies to YK Patong Resort 3 *. Guest reviews focus on the fact that the decoration of the apartments is quite unsightly, and all rooms need at least cosmetic repairs.

The hotel can offer you the following accommodation options:

  • Standard is a double room with a king size bed. The room is quite small, so there‚Äôs no reason to talk about the possibility of placing an extra bed. This room has a balcony with a rope for drying towels and linen.
  • The superior room is a cross between a suite and a standard apartment. It is slightly larger in area than the previous category. Here, in addition to beds, there are also chairs, as well as a desk. A balcony in this room is also provided.
  • "Deluxe" offers you to stay on a double king bed or a pair of single. Thanks to the design solution, the room has a living room area, as well as a small corner that acts as a kitchen. On the balcony, in addition to drying for linen, there is also plastic furniture.




What is in the rooms

Sufficiently comfortable conditions for their guests are offered by the YK Patong Resort 3 * hotel. Reviews say that in each of the hotel rooms you will be available such amenities:

  • cable television (there is one Russian-language program in the package), for broadcasting which is used, although an old, but quite modern TV;
  • a telephone, thanks to which you can contact the reception at any time if you have any questions (international communication is also provided);
  • since there are some stereotypes about theft in Thai hotels, in order to dispel all doubts, a safe is provided in the room;
  • after you make a certain amount of money established by the tariff, you will be able to receive alcoholic and sweet refreshing drinks daily in your minibar;
  • Each room has individual air conditioning, as well as a ceiling fan so that you can create a comfortable microclimate in your apartment;
  • the bathroom has all the necessary bath accessories, as well as a small set of disposable cosmetics for showering.




Hotel service

An important moment when choosing a hotel complex for recreation is the list of services and services that are provided in it. Speaking about this hotel, it is worth saying that the following features will be available to you absolutely free:

  • at the maid on your floor, at any time convenient for you, you can borrow an iron, a hairdryer, as well as an electric kettle;
  • despite the fact that the hotel does not have its own beach, and guests go to the public, however, they are provided absolutely free towels for this purpose;
  • You can at any time enjoy swimming in the pool, which is located on the roof of the hotel;
  • The price of the tour includes a hearty and tasty breakfast;
  • You can count on the participation of reception staff in resolving issues of interest to you;
  • The apartments are thoroughly cleaned every day.

The hotel also provides a number of paid services to its guests:

  • laundry and ironing;
  • use of computer equipment in an Internet cafe;
  • filling the minibar;
  • the provision of a number of services in the room (for example, the delivery of food, dishes from a restaurant, the press, etc.);
  • transfer to the desired point on the island;
  • massage services and spa treatments.

Among other things, you should be prepared to leave $ 100 as a deposit, which will be returned to you upon eviction from the hotel.





Service Ratings

It is important to know not only about the list, but also about the quality of the services provided at the YK Patong Resort hotel (Thailand, Patong). The reviews of tourists helped to make a rating in which the hotel services were evaluated on a five-point scale:

  • The infrastructure of the bottom hotel complex was rated 3.8. The guests considered the area around it to be insufficiently landscaped, as well as the restaurant and the bar caused complaints.
  • Service earned a rating of 3.8. It is affected by the almost complete absence of any entertainment at the hotel. Nevertheless, the work of the staff and the quality of the Internet connection are rated quite high.
  • 3.8 was also rated for the condition of the rooms. Particularly noted is the quality of ventilation. But the worn-out state of furniture and poor sound insulation somewhat underestimated the score.
  • Since this hotel does not provide any infrastructure for young tourists, this item was rated 2.9. Especially many complaints were caused by the lack of a special children's menu in the restaurant;
  • The highest score - 4.1 points - was awarded the location of the hotel. It is located in the city center, and therefore there are many infrastructure facilities next to it.




YK Patong Resort 3 * (Thailand, Phuket): hotel reviews

The following positive moments of rest in this hotel can be distinguished:

  • budget rates for accommodation;
  • convenient location of the hotel relative to urban and tourist sites;
  • The rooms have everything you need to ensure a minimum level of comfort;
  • An interesting feature of the hotel is the rooftop pool (although, due to the security partitions, the panorama of the city is practically not visible).




Negative comments

Reviews of tourists about the hotel YK Patong Resort 3 * (Phuket), in addition to the positive aspects, also contain a number of negative comments:

  • the negative side of the central location of the hotel can be considered the noise that penetrates from the streets into rooms (this problem is especially acute at night);
  • breaking key fraud (always ask the reception staff to help you open the number for the first time);
  • even if you have booked a room in advance, this is not a guarantee that it will be free on the day of your arrival;
  • rudeness of staff (especially from the Russian-speaking employee);
  • there is no light at night in the corridors of the hotel, so there is a high probability of injury.
