How to find out if a loan is approved or not? Options and methods

Sometimes such situations happen that the deadlines for obtaining approval expire, but information has not been received. This article discusses options for how to act in such situations. Who is denied credit?

How to find out if a loan is approved or not? There are situations when a bank refuses to receive a loan. How to find out which banks approve the loan? Below are the most common causes of negative decisions:

  • Personal criteria of the bank. Some banks have been working with clients from 21 years old, while others - only older than 28 years. Many banks refuse to lend to customers over 65.
  • A large load of loans. If your monthly payments on any type of loan is about 50%, then be prepared to refuse.
  • Lack of citizenship or registration.
  • Bad credit history. It is stored in the BCI for 10 years. The manager may be afraid to approve you a new loan.
  • Informal employment. Your income must necessarily be documented.
  • Low salary. If your monthly income is less than 15,000 rubles, then you will be denied a loan.
