Halkidiki, Kassandra: resort specifics

The hotel we are describing is located in the village of Loutra. This is in Greece, on the peninsula of Halkidiki. A few words should be said about this fabulous place. In ancient times, it was called Pallini or Flegra. And when in the fourth century BC, King Kassander (brother-in-law of Alexander the Great) founded a settlement and port here, the peninsula was named after him. On the very southern edge, the village of Agia Paraskevi is perched. It is famous for the fact that near it, right in the middle of a pine forest there is a thermal spring, which has no analogues in the properties of healing water. So, the Aphrodite Hotel (Halkidiki, Kassandra) is located five kilometers from the village of Agia Paraskevi, but only seventy meters from its spa. Territory

Hotel Aphroditi Hotel 3 * was opened back in nineteen seventy. The last renovation took place in 2007. This is a very small hotel with a welcoming, almost family atmosphere. It consists of a residential building, a swimming pool with fresh water, a sun terrace with shade canopies and a bar. The hotel is located on a flat top of a low hill. The restaurant's terrace offers enchanting views of the mountains covered in pine forests. And the water surface of the Aegean Sea can be admired directly from their rooms. In good weather, according to reviews, the holy Mount Athos is visible, and across the bay - the neighboring "finger" of Halkidiki - the Sithonia peninsula.

Dining at the Aphroditi Hotel 3 * (Greece, Halkidiki) and reviews about it

Breakfast is already included in the room rate. Morning meals always represent the same set of dishes. This is a continental breakfast, that is, the choice is not very large. There are boiled eggs, scrambled eggs, cheese, toast, cereal, butter, jams. Breakfast is buffet style. The staff does not follow who takes how much, and you certainly will not leave hungry. For twelve euros per day, you can buy dinner at the hotel. And evening meals are fundamentally different from morning ones. They are very diverse, rich, with gourmet Greek and European cuisines. They give both fish and meat. The food is very tasty, and tourists recommend having dinner at the Aphroditi Hotel 3 *. In the restaurant you can eat both in the hall and on the flower-covered terrace. In the village of Loutra there is only one supermarket, but there are many cafes and taverns.



Beach and Pool

Reviews say that on the second line is Aphroditi Hotel 3 *. The road to the sea will take about five minutes, but you will have to go back up. This is the only inconvenience of the hotel. But on the sandy beach of the village of Loutra, vacationers will feel like kings of the coast. There are very few people here. There are no loud music, rattling water scooters, bananas, parachutes and screaming children. But the beach is very equipped: there are changing cabins, showers. Sun beds and umbrellas are paid there. Near the equipped beach there is a "wild". There is no one there at all. Expanse for lovers of privacy. Along the shore, you can walk to the small church of Panagia Faneromeni, which preserved ancient frescoes and contains a miraculous icon. And within walking distance from the hotel "Aphrodite" is the famous hydropathic center Agia Paraskeva. In addition to medicinal baths, you can swim in the indoor and outdoor thermal tanks with hydrogen sulfide, visit the sauna, hammam, jacuzzi and undergo a massage. The hotel has a swimming pool with a children's section.
