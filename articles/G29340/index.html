Princess of Kos Hotel 4 * (Greece / Kos island): photos, prices and reviews

Kos Island ... Beaches, unique nature, beautiful landscapes and clean ecology ... This is one of the paradise in the Aegean Sea and the third largest island in Greece. Recently, rest on the island of Kos has become very popular among tourists, including Russian.

Sandy and pebble beaches of this corner are buried in verdure, combining with castles and historical sights. The modern life of the city of the same name and the infrastructure that the hotels of the island of Kos have become an indisputable advantage when choosing a vacation on its territory.

Due to the beauty of nature, the island was called the "garden" of the Aegean. This serene corner harmoniously combines the patriarchal spirit of antiquity of Greece and the most modern conditions for spending a good rest.





general information

Today, this destination is considered one of the most accessible tourist routes. In the south of the island of Kos are the resorts of Kamari and Kardamena, famous for their sandy beaches and absolutely calm sea, as a result of which the water here is clear and clean. On the southwest coast, in Kefalos Bay, there are ideal places for windsurfing or kitesurfing. Not only professionals come here, but also beginner athletes.

In the east direction, near the capital of Kos, is the resort of Psalidi. Its coast is pebbled, but the sea here is emerald in color and crystal clear. There, just a ten-minute drive away, there are thermal springs.

Holidays on the island of Kos are especially popular in resorts such as Marmari, Tigaki and Mastichari, whose sandy beaches stretch for many kilometers. Due to the constant wind and small waves, the north of the island is ideal for practicing wave riding and windsurfing.





How to get there

A direct flight to the island of Kos is possible only from May to October, when charter flights from St. Petersburg and Moscow begin. Frequency of flights - once a week. However, in the high season - from June to the end of September - another flight is added. The duration of the flight from the capital to this Greek resort is three and a half hours. In all the other months, Kos must be reached via Athens.

Island beaches

Most of them are fully equipped. The beaches of Kos are very different - long and very small, with golden, white or volcanic black sand, pebbled coating and picturesque bays. In shallow water in some places there are natural "jacuzzi". Here, millions of bubbles rise from the bottom. Some beaches are covered with healing black sand. And the coast of Psalid is rich in thermal springs, in which there is a lot of sulfur.

Situated forty kilometers from the island's capital, the resort of Kefalos is considered one of the most picturesque. True, the sea here is colder than in other places, but this factor is fully compensated by local attractions and preserved authentic architecture, including ancient windmills.





Where to stay

Kos island hotels have a variety of price categories: from comfortable expensive apartments to budget hotels. Inexpensive offer tourists rooms for thirty or forty euros per person. Those who want to book a room with an additional set of services are recommended to stay in 3- or 4-star hotels.

In general, this direction is now popular among other resort places, and the reason for this is very affordable prices on the island of Kos. There are three and a half hundred hotels in this picturesque corner, so there will be plenty to choose from.

Comfortable sandy beaches that meet in winter flamingos, a wide variety of flora, a plane tree, which Hippocrates planted at the walls of the Johannite fortress, ancient temples, a favorable mild climate ... All together create excellent conditions for spending a good rest.





Kos island. 4 star hotels

To fully immerse yourself in the atmosphere of a carefree life, you need to choose comfortable living conditions. They are offered by four- and five-star hotels of the island. And if the latter are somewhat expensive, then the 4 * category is the most optimal choice. There are eight hotels at this level in Kos. Built in the style of the palaces of the Greek patricians, they are distinguished by a good level of service and a developed entertainment infrastructure.

These are typical beach hotels with several categories of comfortable rooms overlooking the sea or the city - family, suites or cozy bungalows. Prices for accommodation in these hotels range from seven to twenty thousand rubles. per day per person.

Princess of Kos Hotel 4 *

Located on the beautiful beach of the northern coast of the island of Kos, near the picturesque village of Mastichari, this hotel is convenient because it is only eight kilometers from the local airport. Tourists who come here to rest with their children are very pleased with this fact, because the kids do not get tired of a long drive. Five hundred meters away, there is a bus stop. The center of the island is thirty kilometers away. Hotel Princess of Kos is positioned primarily as a calm family.





Infrastructure

The area on which the hotel is built has an elongated shape and stretches to the very sea. It is fully landscaped. A lot of roses, palm trees, well-groomed paths - everything is very nice and cozy. Princess of Kos Hotel has all the necessary infrastructure to make tourists comfortable and interesting to relax. For the convenience of guests, the reception is open 24 hours a day, which is especially good for arriving at the hotel during off-hours. If you encounter problems requiring immediate resolution, you can always contact the on-duty administrator who will do everything to help customers.

This four-star hotel has its own parking, the use of which is free for guests. There, at the registration desk, you can rent a car without worrying about the parking lot. Number of rooms

Princess of Kos Hotel 4 * built in Aegean style. Its buildings are surrounded by beautiful gardens and vibrant flowers. From the windows, tourists can enjoy views of the Aegean Sea.

The hotel has two hundred and sixty-three rooms in the following categories:

  • standard single, double and triple with sea and garden views;
  • one and two bedroom apartments.

In 2008, a complete renovation of furniture and sanitary ware was made. The rooms are decorated in soothing colors, the standard equipment is as follows: direct dial telephone, TV, work desk and a large makeup mirror, split air conditioning with manual control, single or double beds with bedside tables, fridge. Picturesque paintings and sconces are hung on the walls. The furniture is made in panel style.





The apartments also have comfortable balconies overlooking the azure sea or the green garden. There are also chairs and a coffee table. The doors to the balcony are silent sliding.

On the floor - non-slip ceramic tiles. In the combined bathrooms, new plumbing - washbasin, shower, toilet. Superior rooms include bathrobes and slippers. A standard set of accessories for personal hygiene and a hairdryer are required. Safe on request. The cost is two euros per day.

Upon arrival at the apartment, the Princess of Kos 4 * hotel gives guests a bouquet of flowers and fresh fruits.

Room cleaning - daily, maids come in the morning when residents have breakfast in the restaurant. Towels, of which workers make funny little animals, change every other day, and bed linen twice a week. If necessary, you can use the laundry and ironing (for a fee).





Nutrition

Princess of Kos Hotel (Greece) offers all-inclusive meals. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served in one of the two restaurants. Serving - buffet style.

Guests can enjoy dishes from the rich Mediterranean menu on the outdoor terrace of the restaurants. You can pre-book a table at the registration desk.

In addition, the hotel has three bars: on the beach and by the pools.

There is also the possibility of living with meals according to the concept of "continental breakfast", but this is not very popular among tourists, since the independent search for places to eat is not only time-consuming, but also more expensive.





In the evenings, the spacious, beautiful restaurant, decorated in blue and blue tones, in harmony with the Aegean Sea, a few meters away, serves Greek or international dishes. Here, delicious seafood - squid, octopus, as well as a traditional barbecue with vegetables. Guests can enjoy refreshing drinks and a quick snack at the beach bar, while cocktails, juices and desserts can be enjoyed at the pools. At four o'clock in the afternoon, children are given a delicious ice cream. The all-inclusive concept also includes free locally-made drinks - wine, beer, ouzo, metaxa, etc.

Sea and beach

Princess of Kos (Greece) is on the first line. The hotel has its own one hundred meter long beach, which ends with the complex. Only a couple of minutes of walking to the water, no need to cross the road. The beach is sandy, with a gentle comfortable entry into the water. Here you can use sun loungers, parasols and towels. The beach offers a wide variety of water sports. There is also a water cooler, shower room and toilets.





Pools

Right behind the beach area are two swimming pools with sunbeds and umbrellas and bars. Everything necessary for living in Princess of Kos 4 (Kos) is free of charge. Those who are disturbed by small waves at sea, which in the north of the island are far from rare, prefer to swim and sunbathe in fresh pools, separated by a bridge.

Sports and Entertainment

Princess of Kos (Greece) invites its tourists to visit the gym, play table tennis or beach volleyball. On the ground floor of the main building there is a TV room.

Tennis courts and pool tables - paid entertainment. However, there is no need to pay for archery, gymnastics, and water aerobics.

Those who wish can rent a bicycle from the reception and travel around the area on their own. There is also an open air theater.

There is a water sports center on the beach. Licensed instructors help windsurf.

Twice a week a disco is organized, there is also animation.

Themed evenings and shows are periodically held. The open theater has karaoke. Those who wish can take Greek dance lessons.





Little vacationers

For children, the Princess of Kos hotel organizes fascinating leisure activities: animators are constantly engaged with them. In the club, kids draw, paint, play.

Those who wish are offered a highchair in the restaurant and extra bed in the room.

There is also an outdoor playground with slides, as well as a shallow compartment in the pool.

Excursions

The convenient location of Princess of Kos Hotel 4 * makes it easy to travel around the island. You can take sightseeing or sea excursions with a trip to Rhodes or Leros, stop by in Turkish Bodrum. The cost of tours - from fifty euros. Near the hotel, about three kilometers away, there is a water park and a picturesque garden with peacocks.

Many tourists must book a sightseeing tour, which includes a trip to the ruins of Asklepion, where healed by Hippocrates, Kos - the island's capital, the fortress of the knights - ion exchangers. Many tours include visits to mountain villages, lunch at the tavern and overstocking with souvenirs. For those who want to save money, it is optimal to visit the Agora market, where you can buy metaxa, honey, olive oil, and various sweets.





Reviews

Objective information about the Princess of Kos Hotel 4 * hotel can be obtained from subjective ratings of those vacationers who stayed at this hotel. Many travel sites advertise a particular resort always only on the positive side, which is not always true. However, it is difficult to hear negative reviews about this hotel. Almost all travelers in their comments note delicious cuisine, excellent sea and the surrounding nature. The food in the restaurant is always more than enough. Seafood is served twice a week: squid, octopus, mussels, shrimp. Fish on the table is present every day. The same goes for meat: lamb, pork and beef. The only drawback, according to tourists, is the meager variety of fruits - on the menu, as a rule, only grapes and apples. Reviews with notes of discontent mainly relate to waves. But the Aegean Sea has never been particularly calm.





The service deserved a lot of positive evaluations - the staff is always helpful, polite, helpful and helpful. If customers have any questions, they can be addressed to almost any hotel employee. A lot of positive feedback about the infrastructure and the service offered.

There are no special complaints from travelers about the pools either. Many of them note the fact that the tanks are cleaned daily. However, some were disappointed that it was impossible to find free sunbeds on the sun terrace after breakfast. Therefore, tourists who want to spend time at the pools had to get up early to take loungers for their families. In general, most Russian vacationers are satisfied with their stay at the Princess of Kos Hotel 4 *, located on the Greek island of Kos. They do not exclude the possibility that they will return here again. Many are happy to recommend it to their friends and family as a good family hotel.
