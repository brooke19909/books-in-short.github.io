Climate in Northern Cyprus

The Mediterranean climate in Northern Cyprus makes it a great place to relax. In summer, the air temperature is around 30 degrees, and winters are mild and warm. Water warms up to 28 degrees in the summer, and with the onset of cold weather its temperature does not drop below 18 degrees.

The developed infrastructure and a network of comfortable hotels in Northern Cyprus allow you to spend time using all modern conveniences, enjoy swimming, beach vacations, and excursions. Staying on the seashore helps to restore strength. It is useful for people with diseases of the respiratory system, suffering from physical inactivity, helps to restore the nervous system and increase immunity. Humidity is low, about 60%. In hotels of Northern Cyprus you can not only relax, but also perfectly improve your health.

What is eaten in Cyprus?

Food in Cyprus is diverse, here you can taste traditional Turkish and Greek dishes. Cypriot cuisine is famous for meat recipes, which mainly use lamb and poultry. The meat is grilled, stewed in pots.

In Cyprus, a lot of vegetables are grown, so many dishes are prepared with eggplant and tomatoes, beans, and a variety of spices are used. Garlic and herbs, which are added during cooking, add flavor and taste.

According to tourists reviews of Northern Cyprus, there are many dishes that are worth a try.

The famous Cypriot meze consists of 20-30 dishes, which are served in small portions.

The meat is often cooked on skewers, a dish called souvla. Be sure to try and onion. These are sausages stuffed with pork and lamb, soaked in wine, smoked, dried, served fried with salads and vegetables.

They also like to cook delicacies from migratory birds, the capture of which is forbidden, but it is not so easy for local residents to abandon their habits.

For centuries, the best recipes of the Mediterranean countries, which in turn dominated this island, poured into the Cypriot folk cuisine.
