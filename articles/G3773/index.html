Precautionary measures

Any electronics requires careful handling. PocketBook 640 is not an exception. The instruction for this device puts forward a number of requirements that are mandatory for compliance during operation:

  • Do not expose the device to contact with water or a humid environment;
  • the device must be operated carefully enough, avoiding excessive pressure on any of its parts;
  • storage of an electronic book is prohibited, as well as its operation near sources of electromagnetic radiation or ultraviolet rays;
