Women's sweater with deers: knitting pattern and description

Jacquard is a crochet or knitting technique that creates a pattern. Most often, jacquard consists of two colors and includes an ornament of repeating geometric and floral motifs, which can be supplemented with large inserts from images with animals.

Description of work

Dimensions of the future finished knitted product: XS. The parameters of the finished jumper:

  • width is 82 cm;
  • length - 64 cm;
  • sleeve length - 47 cm.

To work, you will need Sisu yarn for knitting - wool + nylon, 160 m - 50 g. You need 350 g of a white shade, another 100 g of blue, as well as circular knitting needles No. 2, No. 3,5. The estimated density of loops for knitting: 27 p. = 10 cm.

Knitting in front, back looks like this:

  1. On knitting needles No. 2, you need to dial 220 loops with a white thread.
  2. Rubber band according to the 2x2 scheme (2 front, then 2 wrong) knit 6 cm.
  3. Now it is necessary to switch to knitting needles No. 3. 41 cm is knitted with the front stitch. After that, the loops are divided in half on the front and back of 100 loops.
  4. Then between the loops close 10 loops for the armhole. The hinges are put off.




Sleeves:

  • Initially, 52 loops of white thread are put on knitting needles No. 2.
  • In a circle knit elastic 2x2, length 6cm.




  • Now they switch to knitting needles No. 3. In the 1st row, 5 loops are evenly added. It should get 57 loops.
  • Knit with the front surface according to the drawing.
  • To expand the sleeves, 28 loops are added evenly. It is necessary to add 1 loop each. Do this in every 10th row, strictly on each side of the 1st loop.
  • After 47 cm there should be 85 loops on the knitting needles. After this, the first loop, as well as 5 loops symmetrically to each side of it (only 11 loops are obtained), are closed for the armhole.
  • The remaining 74 loops are set aside.

Yoke:

  1. On circular loops No. 3, all deferred loops are thrown - 348.
  2. In the fifth row, loops are evenly reduced (a total of 28 loops). It remains 320.
  3. Next, they knit according to the figure of a deer for a coquette.
  4. Having tied to the desired size, the middle 22 loops are removed from the auxiliary knitting needle (along the front and back). And the remaining loops are knitted with the front / back rotary rows to the end.
  5. As a result, there must be 172 stitches on the knitting needles, including a piece on the auxiliary knitting needles.
  6. Now fits the cuffs. When it is connected according to the scheme, 52 loops are uniformly reduced and transferred to knitting needles No. 2.
  7. The collar is knitted with a 2x2 elastic band (16 cm).
  8. The hinges close and sew sleeves into the armholes.




The result is a female sweater with deer. The knitting pattern can be applied to any pattern. The main thing is to remember that decreasing and adding should be done so that the pattern does not move. The first loops, which then hide under the seam, are great for this.

Female raglan knitting

Raglan knitting is a universal technique, which is especially recommended for beginners to master. The advantage of raglan is that with correctly made calculations there is no need to stitch such a product, and such a sweater fits perfectly in the figure. What is most remarkable, it does not matter at all whether it will be male or female sweater with deers - the knitting pattern for raglan knitting is always the same.

Knit raglan is best on circular knitting needles. In the first 2 rows, only one front loop is used. When the row is closed in a ring, mark the line for raglan. Depending on the size, it is necessary to divide all knitting into different zones. You can divide the loops using a contrasting thread or special markers. Threads are divided into 4 parts - back, 2 shoulders, in front.

Be sure to knit a "sprout". It is needed in order for the canvas to sit exactly on the back. Most often, the parameters of the sprout are 3-7 cm. The increase in loops should be done on both sides of the raglan line. To do this, in each row on the front side to the side of the raglan line, a yarn is made. Raglan is knitted so much until the jacket reaches the width necessary in the back. Then they go to the sleeves. It is advisable to knit them separately. Elastic knitting ends.

A raglan sweater for women knits in time as much as a man. The difference in work is manifested only in the knitting of the sleeves. The female model allows knitting a straight sleeve with a 2x2 elastic on the cuffs, the male model assumes uniform decreases and additions, otherwise the sleeve will not sit on the figure.

For convenience, you can remove the hinges from which the raglan will be tied, remove it on auxiliary knitting needles. Then the knitting will continue in a circle and there will be no need to sew the sleeves.

Features of work

A raglan sweater, female or male, is particularly affected by uneven thread pulling. And this should be taken into account.
