CreditPlus: reviews of borrowers. Credit Plus

A loan issued in a few minutes is a useful service. It is used by many people who find themselves in a difficult financial situation. This service is provided by a huge number of legal entities, Internet services. One of them is CreditPlus. This service has been around the world for about 2 years. The period of existence is not so long, but during this time a lot of loans were provided to people who applied, a huge amount of good reviews about CreditPlus accumulated, a positive reputation was formed.

Legality of activity

At CreditPlus, people who want to get a loan as quickly as possible can apply without fear. This online service belongs to the microfinance company Ecofinance. It conducts its activities in accordance with the laws of Russia. In 2015, the company was included in the register of microfinance organizations and received all the necessary documents to enable it to work in the loan market.

Internet service is reliable. CreditPlus, according to employees, guarantees the observance of the rights of borrowers, confidentiality. All information entered on the site is protected. The microfinance organization notes that the personal data of borrowers are not transmitted to third parties.





Ease of sending an application and receiving money

To obtain a loan, new customers are required to fill out a small application form on the microfinance organization's website. It includes 4 sections in the CreditPlus internet service:

  • contacts (phone number, email address);
  • passport data;
  • additional information;
  • confirmation of the entered information.

For those cases when there are difficulties with filling out and sending the application, CreditPlus has provided an exit. On the page where the profile is located, you can simply leave your personal phone number. From CreditPlus, employees always call customers back and help with clearance.

Applications are accepted and reviewed around the clock. Clients will find out about the final decision almost instantly. In case of refusal, users of the Internet service are informed about the possibility of resending the application in a few months. With a positive decision, the money is immediately sent to customers on plastic cards, bank accounts. Cash is also available through Contact and Leader systems.





Personal Account Overview

All clients of the Internet service have a personal account. It is created when you first contact the microfinance organization. The personal account is convenient and functional. It displays the amount of the active loan, accrued bonuses. The personal account also allows customers to independently use the most interesting and necessary services for them:

  • extend the term of use of borrowed funds;
  • get new loans;
  • transfer bonuses for timely payment of loans in cash;
  • Invite friends to the microfinance organization website and receive additional bonuses for this.

Entrance to the personal account of CreditPlus, according to the feedback of borrowers, is carried out after specifying the login and password. The username is the mobile phone number, and the password is the combination of numbers indicated during registration. For cases when access to your personal account is lost, a recovery procedure is provided. Customers are only asked to provide a mobile phone number. A temporary password is sent to it, allowing you to enter your personal account.

Bonus accrual

Loans from CreditPlus are beneficial for customers, because there is a bonus program in the online service. Its essence is to accrue bonuses to the account of your personal account in the amount of up to 10% of the principal loan amount. To receive them, one condition is required - timely repayment of borrowed funds.

The bonus to the client account is received automatically upon repayment of the loan on time. Accrued rubles may be used as follows:

  • withdraw to a plastic card, bank account or through payment systems to receive cash;
  • spend on payment of the next loan.




Support service

In order for customers to be able to receive answers to emerging questions, ‚ÄúCredit Plus‚Äù has created a support service. There is a hotline telephone to contact her. Calls from all regions of Russia are free. The support service employs qualified specialists. They can advise on any issue and help to sort out the problems that arise. The advantage of a support service is its work schedule. Specialists are in touch 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. This schedule allows customers to quickly receive answers and advice.

If it is not possible to call, you can send your question through the feedback form. It contains 2 fields: in the first field, the client describes the problem, and in the second - indicates the email address. It is to her that the answer comes in the future.





Positive reviews

Some customers have a good opinion about the CreditPlus service, because a microfinance organization working through the Internet has certain advantages. Firstly, loans are issued as quickly as possible on the site. Users only need to fill out a questionnaire, indicating in it reliable information about themselves, their work. Income certificates, guarantors, collateral are not needed.

Secondly, any citizen of Russia can apply to Credit Plus. The region of residence does not play any role for several reasons:

  1. The company does not work in any particular subject of our country. MFIs do not have an office. All applications are accepted through the CreditPlus website and by phone.
  2. Ways to get money are available to all people. Payment systems work in all cities of the country, and almost every person has plastic cards.

Thirdly, CreditPlus offers a convenient and useful service - auto-prolongation. Quite often, customers forget that they need to repay the loan. When they remember the return date, they realize that they are facing an overdue period. In such situations, CreditPlus does not spoil the credit history. The loan is automatically extended by 10 days. This service is paid. The extension cost is 20% of the loan body.





Microfinance Organization Criticism

Customers leave negative feedback about CreditPlus for several reasons. One of them is associated with an age restriction. A loan can only be issued to persons over 22 years of age. Another reason for the appearance of negative reviews is that some customers do not receive money after approval of the application. In such cases, the microfinance organization asks people to contact support.

Users also leave negative reviews due to the fact that the company, in their opinion, is cheating in calculating bonuses (Cashback). In fact, this is not so. Bonuses are always accrued for timely repayment of the loan. They can be absent only if technical problems arise on the site.

CreditPlus Debtors Reviews

Customers who did not pay the loan on time leave different reviews. Some people testify that microfinance organization specialists are not very worried about delays - they call the phone number indicated on the questionnaire once a week or send an email to their personal e-mail.

There are completely opposite reviews. In them, debtors indicate that company employees behave unacceptably. They constantly call with threats to bring to justice, start writing on social networks to friends, threaten to transfer the debt received from CreditPlus to collectors. Specialists also refuse to installment and freeze interest.





In conclusion, it is worth noting that the percentage of approval is quite high in the online service CreditPlus. MFI seeks to help people, provides funds for any purpose (to solve crisis problems, shopping, travel, entertainment). But in some cases, customers are refused. The reason for the negative decision may be the presence of a very bad credit history, the indication in the application form of inaccurate information about yourself, and the lack of positive motivation to repay the loan.
