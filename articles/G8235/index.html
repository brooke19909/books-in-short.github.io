How to get there

Varadero - the famous beach where our hotel is located - is located in the province of Matanzas, on the Ikakos Peninsula. This is 20 kilometers of continuous sandy coast. Havana is 135 kilometers from there. It is not too far to drive from the Varadero international hub to the Islazul Punta Blanca 2 * hotel. As a rule, tourist groups come here with a transfer. Islazul Punta Blanca is most often either the first or second in line. So you get quickly and comfortably. Only charters fly to Varadero from Russia. Regular flights are possible from Moscow to Havana. From there you can get the bus carrier "Viazul". Flights to Varadero depart four times a day. You need to go about three and a half hours. Planes fly from St. Petersburg with a change in Vienna or Frankfurt am Main, and then directly to Varadero.

Territory and rooms

The hotel is located in a tropical garden, and at the same time stands right on the ocean. The whole territory is surrounded by greenery, coconut palms are everywhere, as on brochures. Around it is very picturesque and well-groomed. Hotel Islazul Punta Blanca 2 * is not too big. It has only 21 rooms. They are located in small two-story buildings, white with blue or cream with a red edging. All rooms are of the standard type. There is a usual set for relaxation and comfort - in-room amenities, bathroom with hairdryer, satellite TV, air conditioning ... Plumbing is good, new, in working condition. Hot water without interruption. There is one Russian channel on television, the rest are American. There are two holes in the sockets, not three, so an adapter is not needed. On the second floor there are balconies, on the first floor there are terraces. There is a problem with the Internet, as in many places in Cuba. But in the neighboring hotel "Puntarena" you can buy a card with a username and password. Sometimes guests are accommodated not in the Punta Blanca, but in the Carey Club, if there are no places. And without any surcharges.



Services

On the territory of the Islazul Punta Blanca 2 * hotel (Varadero) there is a store where, for special tourist money - cookies - you can buy different beach things, water and snacks. There is also a laundry service, where guests rent contaminated clothing. If you suddenly have any health problems, you can invite a doctor. And tourists recommend taking valuables to the administration. For this, there is a special safe at the reception. The hotel has Russian-speaking staff. When you arrive, you are greeted, and the suitcases are brought to the room. The waiters and other staff are friendly, unobtrusive, do not ask for a tip, they will always be interested in how you are doing. There is a disco at the hotel with Cuban songs and music, but it is organized once or twice a week. Animation is at the Karey Club Hotel. There are a lot of sports activities on site. There is a gym, volleyball and tennis courts, and billiards.

Beach

The resort complex of Varadero is a real protected tourist city. The police are at your service everywhere. The safety of tourists is provided round the clock. The ocean from the hotel Islazul Punta Blanca 2 * (Varadero, Cuba) is only 50 meters! Water can be accessed directly from the lobby. Own beach, sunbeds and umbrellas are free. True, the sand is not snow-white, but grayish. But the reason for this is the smallest seashells crushed by the sea, which are mixed with it. The water is clear, clean, everything is visible to the smallest detail. A beach snack bar is located right by the pool. So, if the ocean has wind or waves, you can swim here, and even with a view of the sea. There are plenty of water activities at the beach. They are free for hotel guests. Beach towels can be taken at the reception of the Karey Club Hotel, at the animator. The water in the oceans is cool in the morning, but vacationers quickly get used to it. And after lunch, I do not want to get out of it. There are very few people on the beach, many even had the feeling that they were there alone.



What to see

One of the most popular excursions from the Islazul Punta Blanca 2 * hotel (Cuba) - to the island of Blanca. It is not very far from the hotel. You are being taken on a boat. There is white sand on the beach, and starfish float in the ocean. The island is surrounded by corals and is very good for snorkelling. They also take excursions to Havana, to a crocodile farm, to the Indian village and to the Caribbean Sea, which includes a visit to the beach. You can go to the capital of Cuba on your own, by bus. There will be enough time to leave after breakfast and return for dinner. Moreover, the stop is right next to the hotel. Excursions are better not to take from tour operators, but individual. It is both cheaper and more interesting. Near the resort is the Varaikakos reserve with karst caves. Tours are also organized there, although they can be visited on their own.
