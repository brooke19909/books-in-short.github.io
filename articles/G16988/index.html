Life insurance

Depending on how the savings are made, classic life insurance is divided into two varieties: funded and risk. It should be noted that the normative definition of these concepts is extremely conditional and is based on the established practice of using these terms.

Risky

Life insurance is a financial protection for the family of the insured in the event that a person dies under certain circumstances, and this is clearly stated in the contract of the RGS company. Feedback on this service is quite controversial, since many people either did not receive payments at all, or received them after quite lengthy proceedings, which is also far from the most pleasant moment.

If necessary, such contracts may also include insurance against any diseases or accidents, disability, and the occurrence of fatal diseases. In this case, there will not be any excessive savings, because all the money paid to the client ultimately goes to cover these risks, as well as all kinds of expenses from the insurance company. At the same time, the fact that in this case any payments after the end of the insurance period through the CSG is completely excluded is equally important. Reviews are often left by people who tried to get their money at the end of the term, despite the fact that this is not provided for by this type of insurance at all.

According to employees of the UK, the most common option today is emergency insurance in case of death of the insured person.



Cumulative

Endowment insurance provides for the gradual formation of savings, and at the same time, it is worth noting that it constantly includes a risk component, which provides for the payment of the indicated amount of funds in the event of death or any other cases provided for in the drawn up agreement.

Today, the most common option is the so-called mixed life insurance, when in case of death of the insured person or at the end of the term (if the insured survives to this point), in accordance with the agreement, the specified amount is paid by Rosgosstrakh. Offices in Moscow and other cities most often offer just such agreements. Judging by the reviews, this type of insurance has been popular since the days of the USSR, but it is worth saying that at that time it also provided for compulsory insurance against any accidents.

Features

For this insurance option, the following features are characteristic:

  • a fairly long period, which allows you to gradually form an impressive amount of savings in accordance with the contract, despite the fact that the contributions are relatively small;
  • guaranteed accrual of a certain return on invested savings in accordance with the contract, as well as in certain cases, additional investment income may be provided.

Due to these characteristics, insurance reserves for this contract will constantly grow throughout the term. Experts argue that if the contract is terminated on an ongoing basis, a person has the opportunity to get good money as a redemption amount - it is enough to come to the CSG. Branches provide such payments, regardless of location.

It is worth noting the fact that accumulative insurance includes any agreements that provide for survival payments, including also various pension insurance contracts. But at the same time, lawyers advise to pay attention to the fact that lifetime death insurance in itself is also considered to be cumulative, although it does not provide for the inclusion in the contract of a case of surviving until a certain date. Reserves, in accordance with such a document, will constantly grow throughout the entire insurance period, and they will reach their maximum only at its end.



The timing

With regard to timing classification, there is short-term and long-term insurance.

In fact, there are no clear boundaries for this division, but the situation is somewhat different in the contracts of Rosgosstrakh.

Offices in Moscow are located almost on every street, but, regardless of which one you turn to, they will tell you that short-term ones are policies that are issued for one year, but in fact it often happens that insurance life for three or even five years is also called short-term. This is necessary in order to separate them from other types of agreements that are concluded for several decades.

The funded option in itself is long-term, regardless of the features of the policy. At the same time, risk insurance in the overwhelming majority of cases is short-term, but in practice there are often situations when it is issued in case of death for 10-15 or even 25-30 years.

Voluntary pension insurance

In the CSG, this type of insurance is an accumulation program, with which a person can completely independently form a future pension for himself.

Such programs are part of pension systems in most countries of the modern world, providing citizens with the opportunity to independently provide for their old age. It is worth noting that such systems already depend not on the financial capabilities of the state, but directly on the capabilities and desires of the citizen himself.



UK employees say that there are several options for how funds can be disbursed when applying for pension insurance:

  • one-time payment after a person reaches retirement age;
  • lifelong allowance;
  • lifetime pension with a guaranteed payment period.

The last option has one feature. If a person dies before the end of the specified period, the payment of the amount continues in favor of his heirs until the time specified in the policy expires.

It is worth noting the fact that in the CSG insurance of this type can have a very different cost, and it depends directly on a number of factors.
