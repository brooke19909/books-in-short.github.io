Guest house "Athens" (Kabardinka): description, photos, reviews of tourists

After the New Year holidays, many of us begin to think about vacation. The most popular type of vacation among Russians is a trip to the sea. Every year, millions of residents of our country go to the resorts of the Krasnodar Territory, because in these places there is everything for a good rest: a clean and warm sea, wonderful nature, a healing climate, all kinds of entertainment and a huge selection of housing.

Kabardinka Village

Fifteen kilometers from Gelendzhik in a cozy mountain valley on the banks of the Tsemess bay is located the picturesque village of Kabardinka. This town is famous for its unique climatic conditions. Mountains surround Kabardinka from three sides, protecting it from winds and bad weather. The slopes of the mountains are covered with juniper forests, which infiltrate the air with coniferous phytoncides, useful for human health.





In Soviet times, a children's resort was actively developing in Kabardinka: sanatoriums for rest and treatment of children, children's camps and recreation centers were built. A shallow clean bay and wide beaches are ideal for the health of the younger generation.

Today Kabardinka is one of the most prestigious resorts. Here are located modern sanatorium-resort complexes, which operate all year round. The infrastructure of this small village is well developed. Tourists will not be bored, because the village has a lot of entertainment: a beautiful promenade with many cafes and restaurants, an amusement park, a dino park and many other interesting places.

Guest house Athens

Kabardinka, the review of which we began with climate and infrastructure, is a quiet and calm village, in which the private sector predominates. Therefore, the most popular among holidaymakers are private guest houses.

Rest in the guest house is a great opportunity to relax in a home environment, in wonderful conditions and at an affordable price.

Among the many guest houses in Kabardinka, pay attention to the guest house "Athens", which is located in the central part of the village.





Athens Guest House (Kabardinka) is a three-story modern hotel-type building in which rooms for vacationers are concentrated. It has its ennobled territory - a beautiful green courtyard, where there is a parking place, a place for barbecue, beautiful flower beds and a gazebo for relaxing.

Athens Guest House (Kabardinka), as mentioned above, is located in the center of the village, so the road to the sea will take about fifteen minutes on foot. Near the house there are shops, a market, canteens and cafes.

Accommodation for vacationers

Athens Guest House (Kabardinka) offers standard double and triple rooms for relaxation. There are also several family rooms that can accommodate up to six people.

Each standard room has a toilet and shower, comfortable modern beds, necessary furniture, TV, mini fridge and air conditioning. Some rooms have a balcony.





The rooms are renovated in a European style, calm pastel colors prevail. The rooms are cozy and very clean.

Athens Guest House (Kabardinka) is an ideal combination of price and quality. A double room in a hotel, depending on the season, will cost from 900 to 1900 rubles per day, a triple room from 900 to 2700 rubles, and a family room with its own kitchen - from 1700 to 4500 rubles.

Guest reviews

Reviews about the guest house, which are many on the Internet, were pleasantly pleased. Without exception, guests note the hospitality and tact of the owners of the guest house and a wonderful attitude towards vacationers. Here are attentive to all the requests and wishes of the guests. Hospitality is very important, and grateful vacationers go to friendly owners from year to year, as to their relatives or friends.

Athens Guest House (Kabardinka), reviews of which are simply magnificent, are worthy of the attention of tourists and are able to provide the most comfortable conditions for a good rest.
