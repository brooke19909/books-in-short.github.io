Hotel Description

The chic hotel Side La Grande Resort & Spa 5 * offers its guests to immerse themselves in the traditions of Turkish hospitality, which perfectly fit into the modern architecture of the hotel buildings. It will be comfortable for absolutely everyone, regardless of age and preferences regarding relaxation.

The heart of the Side La Grande Resort & Spa 5 * hotel is its luxurious modern spa, where only the best masters work - true professionals in their field. Be sure to take a steam bath in a Finnish bathhouse or a traditional Turkish hammam, and also do not forget about massages, aromatherapy sessions and other procedures that will help you immerse yourself in the atmosphere of relaxation and rest.

While you will enjoy your vacation, the chefs of the hotel restaurants will take care of your hearty and quality food. But you should not be limited to only a buffet. Having visited the a la carte restaurant chain, you can appreciate Italian, French, traditional Turkish and many, many more dishes.

They did not forget about the youngest guests at the hotel. They will certainly enjoy the entertainment in the children's mini-club, a pool with slides and a spacious playground.





Hotel location

Side La Grande Resort & Spa 5 * hotel is located in the picturesque area of ‚Äã‚ÄãKumkey, near Side (its center is only 7 km from the hotel). The airport closest to the resort is located in Antalya - you will have to drive 55 km to it. And most importantly, the hotel is located just 300 meters from the azure sea coast.

Guest rooms

Depending on how many guests arrive, the Side La Grande Resort & Spa 5 * hotel (Turkey) can offer them accommodation in family or standard rooms. They differ mainly in their capacity. In total, the hotel has 387 apartments that can accommodate 774 guests.

Comfortable standard rooms consist of a small, but quite comfortable and bright room. Some of them have views of the pool, and the windows of the rest open the picturesque surroundings of the Kumkey region. The room has a bathroom with toilet and shower, a hairdryer is a must. A pleasant moment is the presence of a small balcony.

Family rooms differ from standard rooms by the presence of two bedrooms, each of which adjoins a separate bathroom. One of the rooms has a king-size bed, and the second is equipped with single beds. Additional guest accommodation is also possible. If guests wish, both rooms can be equipped with single beds. As in the previous case, the room has a balcony.

All rooms of Side La Grande Resort & Spa 5 * are made in a simple and modern style. Photos of tourists reflect a good design decision both in terms of aesthetics and in terms of functionality.





Apartment Amenities

All rooms of the Side La Grande Resort & Spa 5 * hotel (Side) are decorated in a modern style and have all the necessary characteristics and amenities to make guests feel comfortable at home. The apartments have the following attributes:

  • one or two bathrooms with shower and toilet; there is also a hairdryer, large mirror, toiletries, bathrobe and slippers;
  • the room has a built-in electronic safe;
  • modern TV with satellite channels, as well as the ability to play video from a flash drive and other external media;
  • mini-bar, which is filled daily with drinking water and a minimum set of soft drinks;
  • telephone with connection to the international communication line;
  • air conditioning system connected to a common hotel;
  • balcony.

Supply system

The Side La Grande Resort & Spa 5 * hotel offers its guests meals according to all the beloved all-inclusive system. This refers to the provision of the following services:

  • breakfast (early and late), lunch, and dinner, which are held in the main restaurant in buffet style (a pleasant moment is that in the morning guests are served fresh orange juice);
  • drinks and snacks are available all day in all hotel bars and by the pool;
  • on the beach you can eat traditional Turkish gozleme cakes with fresh Ayran and other drinks;
  • a children's buffet is open all day, as well as a cafe with a diet menu;
  • at night, snacks and drinks are available in the lobby bar, as well as in the main restaurant;
  • visiting one of the hotel's restaurants during the holidays is available absolutely free, subject to prior reservation.

The all-inclusive system does not include foreign and elite alcohol, as well as wine.

Hotel Restaurants

In addition to the main restaurant, where the main meal of tourists is made, several more establishments operate on the territory of the hotel, which are classified as a la carte:

  • La Grande is a unique restaurant that offers guests an amazing symbiosis of Italian and French cuisines. Here you can try both traditional dishes for these peoples, and innovative masterpieces in fusion style from the chef.
  • The Far Eastern restaurant will introduce you to all the traditions and subtleties of national Chinese cuisine. A colorful interior and musical accompaniment will help you immerse yourself in a mysterious atmosphere.
  • The Mediterranean restaurant is a real kingdom of seafood. Here you can see both quite traditional dishes and real culinary masterpieces from the most exotic ingredients. It is worth noting that they are prepared here only from the freshest products that were caught from the sea only in the morning.

All hotel restaurants (except the main one) open to visitors in the evening. It is worth noting that important point that dinner in one of the establishments is possible only by appointment. It is worth to book a table in advance at the reception.





Free services and entertainment

The price of the tour to the Side La Grande Resort & Spa 5 * hotel (Turkey, Side) includes the opportunity to use the following services and entertainment for free:

  • participation in the animation program, as well as viewing entertainment shows;

  • visiting the sauna, as well as the Turkish hammam.




Paid services and entertainment

For an additional fee, the hotel offers the following entertainment and leisure opportunities:
