Hotel Aqua Azur 4 * (Bulgaria): photos and reviews

Bulgaria is a popular country among tourists from Russia. It offers an inexpensive vacation, which is characterized by high quality service. The Black Sea coast attracts travelers who love the clean sea, fresh mountain air and wellness services. Tourists often stay at the Aqua Azur 4 * hotel, which is located in the quiet resort of St. Constantine and Elena.

Important hotel information

The construction of the hotel was completed in 2005. On a small territory there is a huge eight-story building, which offers 166 comfortable rooms for tourists. The Aqua Azur 4 * complex (Bulgaria, St. Konstantin) is perfect for measured relaxation, so it can be recommended for families with children. Pets are strictly prohibited. The hotel staff does not speak Russian well, but it understands Bulgarian and English well. Check-in starts at 14:00 local time. You will have to leave the hotel after the rest no later than 12:00. Facilities for disabled guests are provided. Smoking is strictly prohibited on site.





The tourist complex is located on the coast of the village of St. Constantine and Helena, which is the oldest seaside resort in Bulgaria. It is removed from its central part by half a kilometer. The beach is about 150 meters away. It is also located near the largest tourist centers of the country. Varna is 8 km from the hotel, while Golden Sands Resort is 10 km away. The road to the hotel from the airport, 20 km away from it, will take no more than an hour.

In addition to furniture, each apartment has additional equipment:

  • A modern TV with cable TV. Russian-language channels are available for viewing.
  • Telephone. Used to communicate with the administrator at the reception. He can also call guests in the morning and wake them up at the appointed time.
  • Room service. Tourists can order breakfast, drinks or newspapers to their room for an additional fee.

Supply system

Tourists staying at the Aqua Azur 4 * hotel (Bulgaria) can choose one of the following catering concepts:

  • Breakfast. The price includes only a morning meal. European cuisine is served: scrambled eggs, scrambled eggs, sausages, ham, cheese, fresh pastries, cereals, fruits, as well as tea and coffee, which can be diluted with milk.
  • Half board. Includes breakfast and lunch. During the day, tourists can enjoy local cuisine. The menu contains several types of side dishes, meat, fish, salads from fresh vegetables.
  • "All inclusive". This option offers free meals throughout the day, as well as local spirits and soft drinks.




Meals are provided in the main restaurant, which seats 150 people. Hotel guests can enjoy a buffet here. For a bite to eat, guests can check out the lobby bar, which serves light fare, freshly baked pastries, desserts, and fruits. The pool bar offers refreshing drinks, alcoholic cocktails, freshly squeezed juices or ice cream.

Hotel infrastructure

Hotel "Aqua Azur" offers its guests a range of services that is consistent with the status of a four-star hotel. Tourists can use the following infrastructure facilities:

  • a 24-hour reception and an administrator who can even help solve problems arising even at night;
  • currency exchange point;
  • public parking nearby; free of charge;
  • paid car rental, where you can travel around the country;
  • Wi-Fi is free in all public areas, and you can also use a computer with wired internet;
  • organization of meetings and conferences in the business center;
  • souvenir shop;
  • a left-luggage office where tourists can leave luggage, large bags and suitcases;
  • safe at the reception for guests who are worried about the safety of their property;
  • a laundry room for guests to hand in dirty clothes;
  • shared living room with soft sofas and TV.




Entertainment program of the complex

For its guests, the Aqua Azur 4 * hotel (Bulgaria) has prepared not only an extensive service that helps to make a better vacation, but also a wide variety of entertainment. The following leisure activities are offered to tourists:

  • an outdoor fresh water pool, next to which is a sun terrace, equipped with free sunbeds and umbrellas;
  • indoor heated pool, where tourists can swim in bad weather;
  • water sports and entertainment center on the beach;
  • spa and wellness center offering relaxing massage courses, hammam, sauna, solarium, jacuzzi, mud wraps for face and body;
  • organization of excursions;
  • equipment for sports games: badminton, volleyball, table tennis, archery;
  • alley for hiking;
  • billiard room.




Organization of children's leisure

A good place for a family holiday is Bulgaria. Hotel Aqua Azur 4 * has prepared for tourists with children a good system of discounts on accommodation. For example, children under 11 years old can relax in the hotel for free, provided that they are accommodated in an extra bed. For babies, a cradle is delivered to the room upon request. The restaurant provides high chairs and a special children's menu can also be prepared. There is not much entertainment here. There is a shallow pool designed for swimming for younger guests. There is also a playground equipped with swings, slides, a sandbox and toys.





Hotel Advantages

Often the reviews of previous guests are a decisive factor when choosing a place to stay. The tourist complex Aqua Azur 4 * (Bulgaria) does not have the best reputation, because the opinions of guests are not always positive. First, consider the advantages of the hotel:

  • Clean rooms that are carefully cleaned every day. They wipe the dust from the cabinets, wash the floors well, clean the sink, bath and toilets.
  • Smiling and friendly staff who politely answer questions and quickly solve problems.
  • Very warm and calm sea. The entrance to it is smooth, so it is well suited for swimming with children.
  • Only a few minutes to the beach. The hotel and the sea are separated by a small staircase and a road.
  • A very beautiful sea view opens from the balcony.
  • Standard but hearty breakfasts. Well suited for children.




Hotel Disadvantages

Nevertheless, the hotel Aqua Azur 4 * (Bulgaria) has negative reviews. According to tourists, there are serious drawbacks that can ruin a vacation. They believe that the hotel management should pay attention to the following disadvantages:

  • The hotel has water problems. For several days she was not at all, so I had to wash my face with water from the cooler.
  • There is no Internet either in rooms or in public places. It works intermittently only at the front desk.
  • Bad housekeeping. The pool is always dirty tiles, on the beach there are a lot of cigarette butts, packets of chips, plastic glasses and other garbage.
  • Poorly washed dishes in a restaurant. In September, the playground does not work, so the hotel has no entertainment for children at all.
  • There are no sun loungers and towels on the beach. You have to buy them yourself.

Should I go here on vacation?

If the listed disadvantages of the hotel did not seem significant to you, then Aqua Azur (Bulgaria) will be a good place for you to relax. It is designed for a family vacation, so couples with children, as well as elderly tourists, will like it here. In general, the hotel offers a budget vacation at good prices that are consistent with the quality of service. Although some travelers still believe that it is more likely a three-star rather than a four-star complex.
