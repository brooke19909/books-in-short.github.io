Hotel Aurum Moon Hotel 5 * (Turkey, Didim): photos, reviews

One of the best resorts in Turkey, Didim, stretches along the coast of the island of the same name, which is washed by the blue waters of the Aegean Sea. It was here that the 5-star hotel complex Aurum Moon Hotel 5 * was recently built.





Location

Surrounded by palm trees, a pristine beach and ancient monuments, the hotel invites all lovers of a relaxing family vacation, active tourists who are looking for water adventures, curious travelers who want to get to know the sights closer, and newlyweds for whom a honeymoon trip on the Aegean coast will become a vivid and memorable experience.

The uniqueness of this resort is an ancient settlement, the historical traces of which are scattered throughout the Didim area. For a long time, this region was only a small coastal village, and only at the end of the 20th century, through the joint efforts of authorities and investors, the resort began to rapidly transform and grew to an international level. Today it is a young but rapidly developing competitor to the nearby and bustling cities of Marmaris and Fethiye.

Hotel info

Quite compact, without unnecessary and catchy details in the interior, Aurum Moon Hotel 5 * is made in traditionally light colors, setting up for a pleasant and relaxing holiday. A small but picturesque area has a pleasant walk in the shade of tropical trees.

For maximum convenience, vacationers right on the beach there is one of the bars where in the middle of a hot day you can order refreshments or return to the deck chair with your favorite cocktail - enjoy the sea waves.

The private beach area is ideal for small vacationers. The coast is quite shallow, and a smooth sandy entrance to the water allows you to keep children within sight. Sophisticated gourmets will remember the diverse Mediterranean cuisine, active tourists will be impressed by trying out water sports, children will always be occupied with games and attentive animation, and young couples will find solitude and tranquility for two. Absolutely every visitor staying at Aurum Moon Hotel 5 * (Didim) will determine his occupation and spend the best days of rest in maximum comfort.
