Rethymno Sunset 3 * (Greece / Crete) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Most often, tourists vacationing in Crete prefer to settle in "treshki". It is inexpensive and very convenient. Such hotels are usually located near the sea, have excellent cuisine, offer many services, as well as rooms with all amenities. A typical hotel of this kind is a tourist complex, which was previously named Golden Sun. Rethymno Sunset 3 * (as this hotel has been called since 2013) is located in a busy tourist location, on the second beach line. For many Russian travelers traveling to Greece, it has become a place of comfortable and pleasant stay. Here you can enjoy beautiful views of the mountains and the sea, as well as experience the surprisingly authentic atmosphere of the Cretan heartland. After all, this island is unusually beautiful.

Where is the hotel Rethymno Sunset Hotel 3 *

The hotel is located in the center of northern Crete, in the tourist village of Adelianos Kampos, near the city of Rethymnon (five kilometers). This resort itself is perfectly visible from the windows of the hotel. The center of the village, which is actually the beach suburb of Rethymno, is six hundred meters from the hotel. Walk to the sea for about a quarter of an hour. The path runs through olive groves and a number of cafes and other establishments. Like many Cretan hotels, Rethymno Sunset 3 * (Greece) is located on a hill, which provides stunningly beautiful views from the hotel, especially at sunset. And the distance from the main streets of the village guarantees the guests the silence necessary for relaxation. Near the hotel there is a bus stop that you can use to get to the center of Rethymnon. There are many cafes and snack bars on the promenade and beach, all of which are within walking distance. Supermarkets and shops are also found almost every hundred meters. Therefore, it is nice to walk here, especially in the evenings.
