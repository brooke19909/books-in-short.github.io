Washing machine "Ariston": malfunctions and their elimination. Ariston washing machine: possible malfunctions, DIY repair

No hostess can imagine herself without an automatic washing machine. The assortment range allows you to choose a model that is most suitable for dimensions, technical capabilities, and in accordance with the available budget. One of the popular brands is Ariston. The model range meets all modern requirements: reliability, ergonomics, profitability. But even this equipment is prone to breakage. What are the possible malfunctions of the Ariston washing machine? Let's take a closer look.

Advantages of Ariston washing machines

There are a number of advantages of equipment of this brand:

  • Profitability. The consumption of electricity and water is minimized.
  • Ergonomics Convenience in operation and beautiful design.
  • Wide range. The ability to select the best equipment in the right price segment.
  • Availability of service, constant availability of spare parts for all models.
  • The software can be adjusted to your needs.

As you can see, Ariston washing machines have all the advantages of modern household appliances.

Equipment device

Any washing machine consists of several parts:

  • electronic control unit;
  • heating element - TEN;
  • electric motor;
  • drain pump - pump;
  • water supply system;
  • bearings and seals;
  • sensors.

This is an incomplete list, but it reflects the essence of functioning.

In these parts, the most common causes of malfunction of the Ariston washing machine. Below we consider them in more detail.

Major breakdowns

Service life directly depends on how the Ariston washing machine is used. Faults and their elimination bring a lot of discomfort to the owners, but nevertheless, basically all the problems come from improper operation. Consumers do not take into account the hardness of the water, make improper installation and connection.

According to the reviews of repair organizations, the main malfunctions of Ariston washing machines are as follows:

  • Water pump. It is worth noting that this weak spot is characteristic not only for this brand, but also for other manufacturers.
  • Weak mechanical fastening of doors, drum, top cover.
  • Hose integrity. Most often it suffers at the junction with the drum. Directly depends on the hardness of the water.
  • Control module errors.

These disadvantages are the Ariston washing machine. Faults and their elimination do not cause difficulties for the masters, since all spare parts are in constant access. Let us consider in more detail each type of breakdown and how to fix it.

water pump

The Ariston washing machine helps to track down and resolve malfunctions using the instructions in which there is a table with error decoding. In such cases, check the connection of the controller with the pump, the pump itself (voltage in it), the operability of the sensor, and the absence of clogging in the drain system. If a cause is found, it should be corrected; if not, they will replace the pump.

Mechanical fasteners

When loosening the door or cover, tighten the fasteners. To monitor the weakening of the drum will help increased noise and vibration during operation. It is important to timely respond to this problem, which was identified by the Ariston washing machine. Faults and their elimination of such a plan will not take much time and will not cost a penny. But if you miss the situation, you may need major repairs, which will cost a round sum.

Hose integrity

The appearance of water on the floor indicates such a breakdown of the Ariston washing machine. Faults and their elimination of this type bring a lot of trouble. Repair begins with a search for a leak. For this, not only hoses are inspected, but also the places of their fastening.





There are situations when the pipe is clogged and flies from the place of water supply. In such cases, blockage should be removed and the hose should be secured back. If the integrity of the rubber tubes is violated, then they are replaced with new ones. Such malfunctions can lead to a complete malfunction of the machine. Water falling on the electronic unit, electric motor, sensors, relays, incapacitates them.

Control Module Errors

How does the Ariston washing machine behave in such situations? Possible malfunctions blinking of lamps will indicate.

In this case, the following errors pop up: F06 (failure of the buttons), F09 (failure of the volatile memory), F12 (broken circuit between the display and the controller, F18 (incorrect operation of the microprocessor). The solutions will be as follows:

  • F06 - check the serviceability of the buttons, their contact. Very often, with such breakdowns, the control unit is completely changed.
  • F09 - replace the controller or make firmware microchips, which is responsible for the memory.
  • F12 - check the connection, if necessary, replace parts.
  • F18 - to eliminate the code, change the controller.

The Ariston washing machine does not differ in special structural difficulties. Do-it-yourself repairs can be easily repaired. What other breakdowns can occur?

Tubular electric heater (TEN)

The following errors are typical for breakdowns of such a plan:

  • F03 - malfunction of the sensor that monitors the operating temperature of the heating element. The contacts between the controller and the sensor are checked, the sensor itself for serviceability. Replaced if necessary.
  • F07 - TEN is not immersed in water when the equipment is running. The contact between the sensor responsible for the water level and the controller is checked, the serviceability of both. If necessary, the defective element is replaced.

With such errors, the health of the heater itself is checked using a multimeter.

Another series of malfunctions

Part of the damage may occur due to any initial.

For example, if water leaks from the drainage system or its intake, electrical and electronic contacts can be flooded, which can damage any system.

The occurrence of vibration during the destruction of bearings can displace the axis of the drum, which will also lead to overhaul. An unstable supply of electricity is the reason for the failure of the electronic control unit.

Troubleshooting

First of all, it is necessary to correctly install the equipment. It must be sure to level. With proper installation, the Ariston washing machine (the malfunctions and the elimination of which we examined) will rarely disappoint with breakdowns. In this case, the service life will increase. How will the Ariston washing machine (vertical loading) behave when mounting and connecting out of level? Faults that arise are the displacement and deformation of the axis. Such repairs do not fall under warranty, and the owner will have to incur significant costs for it. The installation of filter systems for water supply and the use of special tools will help prevent many malfunctions, including the formation of scale and its harmful effects on the heater and other systems.

For washing machines, a separate cable is provided, which should be designed for the power consumption of the equipment, and in the shield - a separate machine. Mandatory grounding device. Do not load laundry weighing more than the recommended instructions. This may result in failure of the drum fixing systems. The consequence is a major overhaul.
Whatever the malfunction of the Ariston washing machine, do-it-yourself repairs should begin by studying the instructions, which contain a diagram of the device and a table of operation errors. Another important point, if the equipment is under warranty, it is better to contact a service center. If you are sure that the breakdown occurred as a result of improper operation, then you can do it yourself. This will significantly reduce time and financial costs. And the constant availability of spare parts will help you quickly find the right part for any model.
