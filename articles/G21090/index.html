Cost and income estimates in bb workspace electronic budgeting system

Estimates are a fairly common term that occurs in various areas of human life, and therefore, even in the field of economic activity has several contexts. In its most general form, the estimate of expenses and incomes is a calculation, a documented financial plan, on the basis of which an economic entity manages the capital of an enterprise or company. In addition, the cost and income estimates serve as a necessary tool for controlling the expenditure of financial resources. Estimates can be drawn up to finance both the projects as a whole and the production of some works, which are separate links or stages of the production process.

As a rule, budgeting is the main document of the basic budget of an enterprise or company, therefore, very often in the economic literature and practice the process of budgeting is called budgeting. In this sense, the estimate of expenses and incomes reflects in detail the entire operational economic activity of the enterprise, company or individual entrepreneur. At the same time, it shows how rational the work is being done to cover the costs of production activities with the income that an economic entity receives after selling its products.

When drafting this document, it is necessary to accurately determine and indicate limits for all major budget-forming areas (items) of income and expenses, correctly identify the sources of reserves and savings, taking into account these indicators, optimize budget parameters with the amount of tax deductions and carry out a rather large list of calculation procedures and analysis.
