Hotel Kemer Dream Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Kemer): traveler reviews

An incredible place for a long-awaited vacation or summer vacation is the amazing hot Turkey, which annually receives guests from all over the world. The mild climate, clean air, gorgeous mountain scenery and the opportunity to get enough of new emotions and impressions attract tourists to these parts from year to year. Here you can find accommodation for every taste: from luxury apartments to budget options. The doors of the four-star Dream Beach Resort in Kemer are open year-round for guests who want to enjoy their vacation. A lot of entertainment, comfortable conditions and high-level service, as experienced, are the main advantages of this institution.

Features of accommodation

As with most hotels in Turkey, sharing with pets is not allowed. Smoking is allowed only in designated areas. Three rooms are equipped with everything necessary for people with disabilities. The staff speaks three languages: English, Russian and Turkish. This simplifies communication and resolving emerging issues. It is possible to install an extra bed. Check-in at the Kemer Dream Hotel (Kemer) starts at 14:00, while leaving the apartment is necessary before 12:00.

Number of rooms

The total number of rooms offered for check-in is 335 units. Among them there are two categories: Standard and Family. All of them are one-room. The smallest dimensions are 18 square meters, while the family rooms are more spacious - 35 squares. They differ in the opening views from the windows. Maximum capacity is 4 people.

The design of the room at the Kemer Dream Hotel 4 * has a classic focus. All elements of decor are modest, but at the same time incredibly harmonious and ergonomic. Such an interior promotes rest and relaxation. The main color palette incorporates white, golden and blue shades. The feeling of spaciousness is achieved due to the high white ceilings and French glazing, thanks to which the first rays of the sun penetrate inside and wake guests with their warmth. Wall sconces illuminate the room in the dark. The floors are laminated. There is access to a balcony equipped with plastic furniture.

Housekeeping is provided by the responsible maids daily. Linen is changed three times a week. A wake-up service is available.

Arrangement

The complete set of furniture set is primitive. It includes double beds, a desk, a chair, a spacious wardrobe, a luggage stand. A large mirror is located above the countertop, which also contribute to a visual increase in space. The apartments are equipped with individual air conditioning, regulation of which is available to everyone. The TV allows you to brighten up evenings by watching your favorite TV show thanks to the presence of Russian-language channels. The phone provides instant communication with the reception, whose employees instantly solve emerging issues.

Use of the safe is a paid service. Wireless Internet access - Wi-Fi - is available throughout and does not require additional charges. There is a minibar, which is daily replenished with drinking and mineral water. All rooms are equipped with a modern fire safety system.

The bathroom is finished with high quality tiles. There is a shower, hairdryer, bathtub and mirror. A complete set of personal care products is updated daily. Marble countertops add sophistication to the interior. The number of white towels complies with the standards.

As many guests note in their positive reviews about the Kemer Dream Hotel 4 *, cosiness and comfort reign here. Everything around sparkles with cleanliness. The administration of the hotel complex makes every effort to ensure that each guest feels at home.

Supply system

The current All-Inclusive program includes a five-time meal. The serving form is a buffet, which is served according to a predetermined schedule. Everyone chooses and imposes their favorite food on their own. No quantity limits.

The abundance of served dishes and their taste features impress all guests, which was noted by guests in their reviews of Kemer Dream Hotel. Local culinary experts invest a piece of their soul in every masterpiece. The interior of the main restaurant is classic, decorated in bright colors. Numerous spotlights and embossed ceilings add gloss to the design. Wooden furniture, individual serving and the luminosity of the hall are the main advantages of this establishment.

You can quench your thirst at the poolside bar or snack bar. A variety of soft alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks will allow everyone to choose what their soul desires.

Toddler Leisure

Not only adult relaxation will be filled with a lot of positive emotions. Hotel Kemer Dream Hotel 4 * takes care of the stay of its young guests. This fact is often noted by grateful parents in their positive reviews. The kids will be able to enjoy the annual vacation here in full. The doors of the children's mini-club are open for them. Entertainment
