Fascinating "Underwater World" - Kabardinka Aquarium

15 km from the bustling Gelendzhik, between two mountain ranges on the Black Sea, a hospitable Kabardinka is conveniently located.

Couples with children come here who prefer a relaxing beach holiday. But children cannot sit still for a long time. Need some fun.

In addition to the beach

The most popular places with interactive games or contact zoos. Some hotels and guest yards have their own contact zoos. To visit a sea resort and not see a live shark is the height of connivance!

Located in the city center near public transport, the aquarium fits in the small building of the Underwater World complex with large facade windows.

Inside the underwater world

For the convenience of visitors, all inhabitants of the Kabardinka Aquarium were resettled in three different thematic zones.

It should be noted that the interior is made in muted colors, everywhere soft backlight. It creates a very real feeling that you are walking along the seabed, and around the aquariums lit with ultraviolet light with marine inhabitants.

In the first hall of the Kabardinka Aquarium, you can get acquainted with the freshwater inhabitants of water bodies: majestic pikes, large and important catfish, etc.

The second room is inhabited by sea corals, sea sponges, stars, exotic motley fish and a reef shark - small but real.

In a darkened atmosphere with a mysterious backlight, it is so exciting to watch the fantastic dances of the marine inhabitants: calm stingrays, aggressive piranhas, lightning-fast morays.

The third hall of the Kabardinka Aquarium is inhabited by seals, huge snakes, lizards, chameleons and crocodiles. There is also a mini zoo with chinchillas, rabbits and other small pets with which you can play with kids.

The tour will take no more than an hour, but a great mood and a lot of emotions for the kids will be provided.

You can independently wander between the exhibits and find out everything from the information plates, or you can book an excursion with a professional ichthyologist.

How to get to the Underwater World

The Aquarium of Kabardinka is located at: Krasnodar Territory, Gelendzhik urban district, Kabardinka village, Revolutionary street, 71.

You can get by shuttle taxi to the stop "Avtostaniya".

Opening hours: from 10-00 to 22-00 hours.

Tickets cost 400 rubles, children under 7 years pass free of charge.
