Hotel Mersoy Exclusive Aqua Resort 4 * (Marmaris, Turkey): traveler reviews

When choosing a place for a long-awaited vacation or annual family vacation, the preferences of each trip participant are taken into account. Someone wants a bright night partying, someone is interested in a variety of excursions and acquaintance with new sights, and for someone, the main purpose of the trip is a dark-skinned tan and recovery of the body.

A multidisciplinary resort combining different facets of relaxation is Marmaris, which is located on the Mediterranean coast of Turkey. Fresh and clean air, beautiful hilly landscapes attract tourists from all over the world. Centuries-old trees perform a healing function, allowing you to increase the immunity of each guest in these parts.

Beach

There is no private beach area at the hotel. Vacationers are invited to visit the municipal beach, which is separated from the hotel complex by the roadway. There is no underpass. The entrance to the sea is gentle and sandy. This causes the greatest delight in kids, because frolic and swimming at the shallow depth gives them a lot of positive emotions and memorable impressions. The infrastructure is at a high level. There are changing cabins and showers. Rescuers are absent.

Despite the constant crowding, everyone can rent a deck chair with an umbrella. Beach towels are provided on a fee basis.

In order for the pastime on the beach to be eventful, tourists are provided with a wide selection of various entertainments. Many people prefer playing volleyball, some ride water scooters and a banana, while others prefer more extreme leisure activities.

One of the popular activities is diving. Everyone can sink to the bottom of the Mediterranean Sea and enjoy the unique underwater world with their own eyes.
