Tour "Bulgaria Golden Sands" - a pantry of health and relaxation.

Taking a plane ticket to Varna, and then taking another 25 minutes ride by car, you get to the famous Bulgarian resort Golden Sands.

Golden Sands resort was created in the sixties of the last century near the large Bulgarian resort town of Varna. It is distinguished by the amazing golden color of quartz sand on the Black Sea coast and the pristine beauty of the national park. Golden Sands is marked with the international Blue Flag, which means cleanliness and environmental friendliness.

Among the wide range of tourist services in Bulgaria, the tour "Bulgaria Golden Sands" is one of the most popular.

Tour "Bulgaria Golden Sands" is a modern tourism infrastructure that allows you to enjoy the magnificent climate and the gentle warm sea. Tourists of different ages come here, and every vacationer receives what he is looking for.

Initially, the Golden Sands resort was planned not only as a place for a beach holiday, but also as a health resort for the treatment of respiratory organs, the musculoskeletal system, and the nervous system. The resort has sanatoriums, modern wellness centers, and boarding houses. Here, mineral waters, healing mud, phyto-therapy, massages, and beekeeping products are effectively used to restore the body. Undoubtedly, the time spent in the resort of Golden Sands is a pantry of health for more than one year.

The Bulgaria Golden Sands tour is a great opportunity to spend an active vacation. Water activities are presented at the resort quite widely - diving and windsurfing, jet skis and sea trams, water skiing and yachts. Lovers of outdoor activities on the beach expect no less interesting activities. These are wonderful sports complexes with volleyball and basketball courts, tennis courts, pools, horseback riding trails. Extremists will enjoy deltoplane or skydiving.

The nightlife of the resort is uninterrupted fun, many restaurants, cafes, and entertainment programs.

However, vouchers "Bulgaria Golden Sands" are in demand, primarily for families with children. Every mother dreams of a safe sea coast. In Golden Sands - a shallow sandy beach stretched for several kilometers in length and one hundred meters in width, the absence of ebbs and flows, a clear sea. The climate of the resort is perfect for the children's body and helps to strengthen it. A unique infrastructure has been created for children in Golden Sands; any cafe has children's menus with surprises and gifts. Children's camps are very popular in Bulgaria, providing children with a fascinating and informative vacation. In the sanatoriums of the Golden Sands resort, special rehabilitation programs for children have been developed.

Holidays in the Golden Sands resort can be combined with excursions to the sea capital of Bulgaria - the beautiful city of Varna. Among the attractions of Varna are the Cathedral of the Holy Assumption of the Theotokos, the Regional History Museum, the Archaeological and Ethnographic Museums, the Museum of the History of Varna, the Museum of Natural History, the Aquarium and the only Dolphinarium on the Balkan Peninsula .

In addition, shopping in Varna will bring a lot of pleasure to Russian tourists. It is quite affordable that you can dress fashionably, purchase leather and jewelry, and interesting ceramics. On the central pedestrian street of Varna there are shopping centers, boutiques, small shops where tourists are given the opportunity to buy goods from world-famous companies, as well as unique author's national souvenirs.

Golden Sands is a visa-free vacation acceptable for Russian tourists, the absence of a language barrier, a kindred Slavic culture (unlike resorts in the East). However, Bulgaria is a European country that strives for high international standards of service.
