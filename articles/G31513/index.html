Panasonic TX-32ER250ZZ TV: owner reviews, specifications and features

At the same time affordable and high-quality multimedia entry-level solution is the Panasonic TX-32ER250ZZ TV. The reviews of its owners, technical specifications and the procedure for setting up this device will be discussed in this article.

This information will allow a potential buyer to find out the capabilities of such a multimedia center and determine how it meets its requirements.







Manufacturer, positioning, equipment

Panasonic is a Japanese manufacturer of electronic equipment. Moreover, until recently, only premium solutions were present in the assortment of its products. Now this company has decided to take a very bold step and has released mid-range and even entry-level devices.

The Panasonic TX-32ER250ZZ TV belongs to the last group. The reviews of experts and owners once again confirm this positioning.

Modest technical specifications and low cost in this case act as additional arguments. The package bundle for this solution is as follows:

  • TV.
  • Remote Control.
  • Complete support.
  • User manual supplemented with a warranty card.
  • A set of batteries for the control panel.

It should also be noted that the devices of this brand were mainly manufactured in Japan. But now the factories of this company can be found in China or even Belarus. And it is precisely at the production facilities of the Horizont factory that this TV model is assembled.

Acoustic subsystem

High-quality acoustic subsystem is part of the Panasonic TX-32DR300ZZ TV. Owner reviews focus on the availability of support for the V-Audio Surround function, the activation of which can significantly improve the sound quality of the TV shows being watched.

As expected, this subsystem includes 2 separate speakers. The rated power of each of them is 5 watts, and in total this allows the owner of such a multimedia center to count on 10 watts of sound.

Connection Methods

A modest, but at the same time, sufficient list of communications boasts the Panasonic TX-32ER250ZZ TV considered in this article.

A review of the capabilities of this model indicates the presence of an abbreviated list of ports. The Japanese manufacturer included the following in this list:

  1. The number of digital HDMI ports is two. One of them can be used to connect a mini-PC. And the second one, if necessary, can be used to obtain a satellite signal from a modern model of the receiver.
  2. But the USB connector in this case is just one. Therefore, the owner will have to make a choice as to what to use it for. Either it will be used to power the mini-PC, or it will be used when playing movies or music from external storage devices.
  3. One 3.5 mm audio output for outputting sound to external speakers or headphones.
  4. There is also one D-Sub port, which can only be used to receive images from a personal computer. In this case, this TV will turn into a conventional oversized digital monitor.
  5. In addition to this, there is also one RCA port. It is used in cases where it is necessary to display an image from satellite receivers that are outdated by today's standards.




Setting procedure: instruction

The Panasonic TX-32ER250ZZ TV is set up very easily and simply. The instruction in this case includes the following steps:

  1. Assembly of the TV. Removing all transport mounts.
  2. Installation at the place of use. The supply of communications.
  3. Turn on the device.
  4. Activate the setup menu, search for all channels in automatic mode.
  5. At the end of the last stage, you must save the list.

After that, the device in question is ready for use. Again, there is no need to perform an additional step to configure the Smart TV function in this case, for the reason that this software option is not provided in this TV.
