I wonder how to make a trap?

Human life is inseparable from the life of various animals and insects. But it often happens that some living things are inherently pests and cause damage. How to deal with them?





Wasps

Often it wasps that do great harm to a person‚Äôs garden and garden. They are predators, feed not only on fruits and plants, damaging them, but also on beneficial insects. It is worth noting that since no one from wildlife can resist the wasps, people have to do this. You can figure out how to make a trap for these insects. To do this, you need only a two-liter plastic bottle, a stationery knife, a stapler and a rope. The bottle itself is cut into two parts so that the bottom is significantly larger than the top. The neck is turned over and inserted with the hole down. The top of the bottle is fixed with a stapler. A thread or rope is needed in order to hang a trap, for example, from a tree. Everything, it remains only to the bottom pour water mixed with sugar or honey. If there are honey bees nearby, it is better to dilute the water with vinegar. Wasps react to it in the desired way.

Fish

How to make a trap at home for fish? You can use the same principle as in the trap on wasps. Only here it is better to take a larger plastic container, for example a five-liter bottle. The fish will swim in the middle through the neck-hole, but simply cannot get out.





Flies

Advice on how to make a fly trap at home will also be helpful. After all, these insects often annoy a person in the summer. So, you can make special adhesive tapes. To do this, you need strips of paper, and as an adhesive, you can take linseed oil, rosin or pine resin, as a bait - honey. In a water bath, 300 grams of rosin is melted, mixed with linseed oil (200 grams) and 60 grams of honey. The mixture is applied to paper tape and suspended from the ceiling.

Mice





How to make a mouse trap? There may be several options. The first one is probably the simplest. It is necessary to take a half-liter jar and put it with its neck down, on one side propping it with a coin. Inside the container, you can put a piece of fat as a bait. The trap is ready! The next way to make a mouse trap. To do this, you need a two-liter bottle. It is necessary to cut off the top with the neck, and from below make cuts that look like sharp petals that will bend inward. In the middle of the mousetrap you need to put the bait. It is also important not to forget to leave a hole so that the mouse can get inside. That's all. The animal, getting into the middle of the can, cannot get out of it, because curved sharp ends will interfere with this. And another option for how to make a trap with your own hands for a small rodent. So, you need to take a high bucket and a roll of toilet paper or paper towels. From this roll you need to make a groove (first cut it into two parts, then glue it along with tape), which is placed on the edge of the table so that the side where the bait is located is slightly in the air. The mouse will go after the bait and fall into a bucket, which will be located directly under the tunnel. But she simply cannot get out of a high bucket.
