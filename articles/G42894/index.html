Residential complex in Sochi "Bald Mountain"

Sochi city. With this name, warm memories of previously spent vacations immediately emerge. I want to drop everything and leave for this beautiful city, where the warm Black Sea, beautiful mountains and clean air. Spend in Sochi just a couple of weeks, wandering along its narrow streets, which suddenly rise up, then rapidly fall. You breathe this mountain air and by the end of the rest you are charged with positive energy for the whole year.

Sochi city and its districts

The city is located near the mountains. On the one hand, they help protect Sochi from cold winds, and on the other, they create difficulties in the form of mountainous terrain. The city is divided into four districts, these are Lazarevsky, Khostinsky, Central and Adler. In the Khostinsky district on the border with the Central is Lysaya Gora, whose height is 140 meters. She received this name thanks to industrialization. Once upon a time, a forest covered the top of the mountain, and during the construction of Vereshchaginsky dachas it was completely cut down. In Sochi, Lysaya Gora is famous for the Arboretum, it is one of the most beautiful places, and the Svetlana microdistrict and the Sobolevka village are located on the mountain.

Mountain history

During the Caucasus War, the mountain was the property of the Ubykh feudal lords. In 1870, the territory was divided between Khudyakov Sergey Nikolaevich and the Vereshchagin brothers. Vereshchagins bought the north-western slope, and the south-eastern one, respectively, went to Khudyakov, who in 1889 founded the Arboretum park. The brothers sold their land, they later divided it into pieces and sold it again. So in Sochi, the upper Bald Mountain began to gradually take on its current form. Today this place attracts a large number of tourists and vacationers.

The village of Sobolevka

Most of the village is located in a cozy evergreen valley, near the Vereshchaginka River. At the moment, about 3,000 people are constantly living in the village. The village received development in the 90s, at first there were several dozen private houses, now it is a full-fledged microdistrict with its own infrastructure and borders. In the city of Sochi, Lysaya Gora is a fairly popular place, but Sobolevka does not enjoy such fame. Silence and calm are the main advantage of this place.

Due to the location on the mountainside, a magnificent panorama opens up from the village on the Black Sea coast. For vacationers, Sobolevka is unpopular, since it is far from the beach, but this place is ideal for permanent residence.

Comfortable housing

On Lysaya Gora there is a microdistrict - "Svetlana". It is located close to the center, and the Arboretum is on its side. It consists of 14 streets, and also runs the federal highway. And there is a street in Sochi. The bald mountain, which is located just in its upper part. On it, the old panel five-story buildings are adjacent to new buildings. The area has good infrastructure: there are two kindergartens, a secondary school, a medical center and various shops. Housing here is in good demand. Part of the development of the Svetlana microdistrict, which is closer to the sea, is very popular among vacationers. They are attracted by the circus, promenade and city center.

Place for outdoor activities

Vacationers in Sochi in various mini-hotels and rest houses noted that the place is ideal for active holidays. The beauty of these places fascinates with its views, from the Arboretum and from the sea the air is very clean. You can climb the cable car to the very top of the mountain, then walk down the well-groomed paths, admiring the subtropical vegetation. In Sochi, on Lysaya Gora, the streets are fan-shaped. If you are planning to come to rest in Sochi, be sure to visit Lysaya Gora.
