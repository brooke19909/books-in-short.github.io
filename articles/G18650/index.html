Hotels in Svetlogorsk (Kaliningrad Oblast): addresses, descriptions, reviews

Svetlogorsk is a resort city in the Kaliningrad region. This is one of the main tourist and cultural centers of the Baltic seaside. It has everything you need for relaxation: the azure sea, clean forest air and developed infrastructure. Svetlogorsk hotels in the Kaliningrad region will provide you with a warm welcome, comfortable accommodation and high quality service.

Guest house "Flamingo"

Flamingo is a hotel built in 2005. It is located in the very center of the city (Gorky Street, 12), in close proximity to the beach. The train station is 5 minutes from the hotel. Here you will find a large green garden and barbecue areas, as well as rental boats for fishing or walking along the lake. You can feel the atmosphere of home warmth and comfort in the fireplace room, and you can taste the local cuisine in the hotel restaurant.

As tourists say in their reviews, this is a wonderful place for events, because the staff provides all possible assistance in their organization. The opportunity to relax with pets is also noted.

Rooms at Flamingo Guest House

"Flamingo" - a hotel in which tourists are available to accommodate 10 rooms of the following categories:

  • The standard rooms are equipped with large or single beds, a desk and clothes hangers. It offers double accommodation or single with the possibility of sharing.
  • Superior rooms differ from the standard ones not only in their larger area, but also in the interior decoration. Separately, it is worth noting the presence of a terrace with furniture and a clothes dryer.
  • Deluxe rooms consist of two rooms and can accommodate up to 4 people. The spacious bedroom has a large bed, and the cozy living room is equipped with folding soft furniture.

Svetlogorsk hotels in the Kaliningrad region are distinguished by cosiness and hospitality. All Flamingo rooms have a minibar, TV and landline telephone. Thanks to daily cleaning, your apartment will always be clean and bright.





Hotel "Olympus"

The Olimp Hotel (Kaliningradsky Prospekt 72-b) amazes its guests with its unusual architecture. A distinctive feature of the residential building is that it is almost completely made of glass. The hotel complex also includes the following infrastructure:

  • Lobby bar, where you can drink your favorite drinks and listen to live music;
  • the elegant restaurant offers a wide selection of international dishes;
  • professional indoor tennis courts where you can enjoy the game in any weather;
  • modern gym;
  • indoor and outdoor heated pools;
  • billiard club;
  • Wellness center where you can visit the sauna and have a massage;
  • Private car parking with 24 hour security.

Accommodation at the Olympus Hotel

Hotel "Olympus" offers its guests single and double occupancy in standard rooms, as well as luxury apartments. Guests can use air conditioning, a fridge, satellite TV, a long distance telephone, a hairdryer, and free Wi-Fi.

Reviews mark the cleanliness of the rooms, as well as the professionalism and courtesy of the hotel staff. Of the shortcomings, it is worth noting that not only guests, but also outsiders can use the pool.

Hotel "Rus"

"Rus" (Vereshchagin Street, 10) is a favorite place of cultural figures, politicians, athletes and other celebrities who come to Svetlogorsk. A beautiful hotel surrounded by a birch grove is located on the very coast of the Baltic Sea. The modern institution operates in accordance with the highest standards of European service.

Special attention should be paid to the restaurant "Rus". The hotel can be proud of its chef, who prepares original and refined dishes. They come to try not only the hotel guests, but also the locals. Also, guest reviews especially note the sauna and the work of massage therapists. "Rus" is a hotel that has its own beach, as well as a large sunbathing area located directly on the roof of the building. This accommodation is sure to appeal to those guests who like to travel with their pets.





The number of rooms of the hotel "Rus"

This hotel can overshadow many hotels in Svetlogorsk, Kaliningrad region. The following room categories are available for travelers:

  • Standard is a simple yet elegant room. All the necessary amenities for living are compactly located on a small area: a double bed, a dressing table, a TV stand and much more. Thanks to the warm carpet you can walk barefoot on the floor without fear of catching a cold. This room has a private balcony from where you can enjoy picturesque views of the Baltic Sea.
  • Premium is a superior room. In addition to the spacious area, it is worth noting the original design and warm colors. The room has a large mirror cabinet, which, in addition to the practical function, also performs a decorative (visual expansion of the space).
  • Entering the Superior apartment, you find yourself in a bright living room with leather furniture. Behind the sliding doors is a luxurious bedroom, which has access to the balcony.
  • Presidential Suite is an example of luxury and comfort. It has a cozy bedroom, and its own study, and a huge living room with dining table, leather sofa and fireplace.

Hotel "Navigator"

Guest House "Navigator" (Svetlogorsk, Moskovskaya street, 8) is a cozy establishment located in a park area, literally 5 minutes from the railway station. Here you can enjoy quality service, fresh coniferous air, as well as the gentle Baltic Sea. The beach is about 500 meters away.

If you are a fan of outdoor activities and a healthy lifestyle, then by all means take the opportunity to rent a bicycle. After a walk, visit the steam sauna, which will improve your well-being and appearance. You can also take advantage of such necessary services as free internet and secure parking.





Pension "Baltika"

If you want to relax on the beach, then pay attention to the boarding house "Baltika" (Svetlogorsk, Vereshchagin Street, 8). The history of this institution began in 1984. Since then, the hotel has undergone reconstruction twice (in 1997 and 2006). Here, guests can take advantage of the following features:

  • visit to the gym with modern sports equipment;
  • a beauty salon where haircuts, manicures, and all kinds of caring procedures are provided;
  • a treatment center where you can be examined and improve your health;
  • tennis court and ping pong tables;
  • places for a picnic;
  • playgrounds and games rooms

You can purchase a ticket that includes accommodation with full board, as well as include spa treatment.

The guests of the boarding house celebrate its good location, as well as delicious and hearty breakfasts. The rooms are large enough with a comfortable layout. But the whole impression of the rest can spoil the construction site.

"Villa Gotland"

This is a small hotel with a cozy, chamber atmosphere, which opened recently - in 2015. It is located at 37 Lenin Street. Its "trick" is the interior, decorated in a traditional Scandinavian style.

Judging by the reviews of tourists, then, despite the fact that the railway station and bus station are very close by, the hotel is very quiet. You will hear only the sound of trees and the surf.

Each of the 12 guestrooms offers the following amenities:

  • a safe for storing cash, documents and valuables;
  • a minibar with your favorite drinks;
  • a loggia or a balcony with furniture and a clothes dryer;
  • electric hair dryer;
  • combined bathroom equipped with shower or bathtub.

The guests of the Villa Gotland hotel can use the following services:

  • good food in the dining room of the hotel (you can limit yourself to breakfast);
  • rental of bicycles and other sports equipment;
  • organization of picnics and barbecue in the fresh air;
  • holding ceremonial and business events;
  • game room (billiards, checkers, dominoes and other entertainments);
  • the opportunity to undergo a medical examination and treatment in neighboring sanatoriums.




Art Hotel Lumiere

Art Hotel "Lumiere" (Svetlogorsk, Lermontovsky Lane, 2a) is a unique establishment, the hallmark of which is an original, stylish design. Externally, the hotel building resembles a fabulous house with a stepped roof, decorative wrought-iron balconies and wooden beams. Interior decoration is a unique combination of classic and modern. It is worth noting that each of the 20 rooms is individually decorated and is a real work of design art.

This hotel provides guests with the opportunity to conduct business and special occasions. You can also have a picnic in the fresh air or join a walk through local attractions organized by hotel staff. To relax both mentally and physically, you can visit the traditional Turkish hammam, Finnish bath, and aromatic sauna. A pleasant end to the procedures will be a massage.

Hotel guests can take advantage of services such as wireless Internet, free parking, a transfer from the airport (train station) and back, as well as a laundry service. Foreign citizens are provided with a translator. If you are traveling to a hotel with children, please consider taking advantage of the babysitting service.

In general, reviews about this hotel are positive. Guests are satisfied with the location, the quality of service, and the cleanliness, and the work of the restaurant.





"Grand Palace"

The Grand Palace Hotel is located on Beregovy Lane 2. This option will appeal to those who value luxury and sophistication. Guests have access to indoor and outdoor pools, access to the wellness center, spa, gym and private beauty salon.

Judging by the reviews, the price of accommodation is quite consistent with the quality. Guests celebrate delicious and hearty breakfasts, as well as smiling staff and a convenient location. But the negative reviews are mainly related to the presence of unauthorized persons in the territory and the inaction of the guard.





Svetlogorsk hotels in the Kaliningrad region will provide travelers with an unforgettable vacation.
