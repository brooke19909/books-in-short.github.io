Hotel "Poseidon" Vityazevo: overview, rooms and reviews of tourists

In anticipation of the holidays, each of us begins to think about where he would like to spend it. Modern opportunities allow tourists to travel the world, discover new countries and even continents. That is why modern vacationers are already hard to surprise with something. Asian and European resorts are open to them, but do not forget that in Russia there are enough luxurious places. Of course, today it is quite difficult to compare Russian seaside resorts with European ones, but tourism is on the rise, so in the next few years the situation may change dramatically. Holidays in the resorts of the Krasnodar Territory can be excellent, which is confirmed by numerous reviews of tourists. The main thing is to decide on the place and accommodation option.





Vityazevo - Anapa resort located just 10 km from the city. The status of the village should not scare away potential vacationers, because there is no less entertainment here than in Anapa itself. At the same time, tourists can stay away from the hustle and bustle of the city and fully enjoy the peace and quiet. It remains only to determine the hotel - there are enough of them, each endowed with a certain qualification. We offer you to pay attention to the hotel "Poseidon" (Vityazevo), located in the heart of the resort village. It remains only to understand what he can offer modern vacationers. Reviews of real tourists will become the basis for a detailed and, most importantly, objective review.

Location

So, if you are looking for a hotel located in the very center of the resort, you will definitely like the Poseidon Hotel (Vityazevo). Reviews of real vacationers emphasize how advantageous it occupies: within walking distance from the hotel is the main attraction of the resort - Vityazevsky pump room. A unique mineral spring with healing water that allows you to improve your health. Just 400 meters away is the largest resort in the resort, Aquamarine, offering a full range of wellness treatments. And, of course, all vacationers will be pleased with the proximity to the coast that Vityazevo can offer, the Poseidon Hotel (Mira, 221 B - this is his address) in particular. Just 5 minutes walk, and you will find yourself on an excellent sandy beach, equipped with everything necessary for an excellent sea holiday.





Territory

The hotel, represented by four comfortable buildings, is quite beautiful: everything is literally buried in verdure. In addition, all vacationers will be able to take a dip in the outdoor pool, sunbathe near it, sitting on comfortable sun loungers. The pool is large enough (7 meters in length), so it is suitable for swimming for both adults and children.

Here, right on the territory of the hotel, there is a playground for its guests, represented by a green lawn, barbecue and a gazebo. In the evenings, a lot of people gather here, vacationers share their emotions from the rest, get to know each other and just have a great time in friendly company.

Accommodation

The attention of vacationers who choose the Poseidon Palace Hotel (Vityazevo) is offered 26 comfortable rooms of the category "Suite". At the same time, the hotel can accommodate up to 70 vacationers. By local standards, the hotel is quite large, spacious and really suitable for a beach holiday on the Black Sea.

Rooms and their equipment

Hotel "Poseidon" 3 * (Vityazevo) offers excellent conditions. Each room has one double or several single beds (depending on the selected room category), as well as an armchair bed, bedside tables, a spacious wardrobe, a coffee table and a dressing table. Moreover, all vacationers can use a TV with satellite channels. For maintaining the optimal microclimate inside the room meets modern climatic equipment - split system. Tourists note that this is a really powerful air conditioner, in a matter of moments cooling the air in the room to a predetermined temperature. A hotel bathroom with good plumbing, a set of towels and cosmetics is the dream of every vacationer. Some rooms have an excellent balcony with stunning views of the coast and the surrounding countryside.





Tourists sometimes complain about the cold floor in the room and the bathroom, which is why you have to use slippers and wear warm wool socks. This item must be taken into account when planning your vacation. Moreover, the hotel administration needs to pay due attention to the sewer system, which, according to tourists, is quite often clogged, especially in the season, which leads to a number of inconveniences for vacationers.

Infrastructure

The hotel has a spacious guarded parking lot, which can be used by all vacationers. Guests will no longer have to look for a parking space and worry about the safety of their car.





Service

Rooms are cleaned daily. Hotel "Poseidon" (Vityazevo) values ‚Äã‚Äãits reputation, so it tries to ensure that all sanitary requirements are met. Cleaning is done at a convenient time for vacationers, most often during their absence: furniture is cleaned, the floor in the room and bathroom is cleaned, plumbing is cleaned, the room is ventilated, towels and bed linen are changed. That is why the room is always fresh and clean.

The reception is open around the clock: vacationers can always contact the friendly staff with their questions and suggestions. At the information desk you can get a map of the resort, get acquainted with a rich excursion program. Here you can take a shuttle to the airport or train station.

On the territory of the hotel, all vacationers will be able to use the wireless high-speed Internet for free. The signal catches perfectly even in the rooms of the basement.





Nutrition

Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served in the hotel restaurant. All vacationers can count on dishes of Greek and European cuisine. Here you can always try dishes cooked at the stake - the highlight of the hotel. Meals are not included in the price of the hotel, some vacationers cite fairly high prices, therefore, they recommend eating in local cafes and dining rooms along the coast - there are plenty of them here.

Sea and beach

If you want to fully enjoy a beach holiday, you will definitely like the beaches that Anapa (Vityazevo) can offer . The Poseidon Hotel does not have its own beach, but the coast is within easy reach. The sea here is clean and transparent, shallow, due to which it warms up quickly enough. A wide coastline, warm fine sand create excellent conditions for a beach holiday, including with young children. Hotel "Poseidon" 2 * (Vityazevo) is located just 5-7 minutes walk from the beach. The swimming season here begins in May and lasts until the middle, and sometimes the end of October. The beach is equipped with sunbeds, umbrellas, there are open rental points for sports equipment and equipment for water sports - everything you need for a quality and interesting beach holiday.





To summarize

Hotel "Poseidon" (Vityazevo) will be an excellent option for those tourists who want to spend their holidays away from the hustle and bustle of the city. Occupying a fairly favorable position relative to the coast and all the attractions of the resort, the hotel attracts many tourists and vacationers. Of course, the hotel is not without flaws, but it is all solvable. That is why we strongly recommend that you pay attention to this option if you decide to spend your vacation in Anapa and its environs.
