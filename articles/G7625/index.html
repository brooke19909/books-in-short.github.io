Traveling to the cities of Russia. Vologda - St. Petersburg

If you have planned a trip to the cities of Russia, go first to St. Petersburg. This is one of the most beautiful cities, which is located in close proximity to Vologda, the cultural capital of Russia. If you want the journey to begin with positive emotions, then the direction Vologda - St. Petersburg will be a great solution. Find for yourself that trip option that will be as comfortable as possible.

Travel from Vologda to St. Petersburg

What could be better than a trip with family and close friends to the beautiful city of St. Petersburg! How many interesting things are ahead of you: ancient architecture, huge shopping centers, movie theaters, many new emotions and impressions. The direction Vologda - St. Petersburg is very popular, which is why you can get there by any means. There are comfortable and fast trains, regular buses are provided. If you are a good driver, it will not be a problem for you to get to the cultural capital by car.



Be sure to bring along a photo and video camera if you have planned a trip in the direction of Vologda - St. Petersburg. The distance between the cities is small, the road offers a gorgeous view, along the way you can stop for a snack and arrange a small photo shoot in nature. The camera you have to continue: to capture the unprecedented beauty of old St. Petersburg.

Be sure to provide a stopping place if you are not traveling with friends. In St. Petersburg, a huge number of hotels for every taste, mini-hotels. You can also rent an apartment for rent. They are easy to book online.

Car ride

The most convenient way to get from Vologda to St. Petersburg is by car. Well, if the car will be attended by two drivers. They will be able to change each other if one of them gets tired. The distance is relatively small (660 kilometers), about 8 hours, but without a shift driver driving this amount of time is difficult to overcome.



The Vologda - St. Petersburg trip by car is quite economical: not so much money will be spent on gas, and you can take a snack with you or stop in a roadside cafe.

You need to prepare for the trip in advance, put in the car all the most necessary things. Although the roads are good and unbroken, you need to be prepared for everything. Be sure to prepare a first-aid kit, warm clothes, take more money. Learn in advance the route Vologda - St. Petersburg. The map and navigator are your best travel assistants. If the equipment fails you at a crucial moment, the card will become a backup option.

The main advantage of traveling by car is that if you are tired, want to stretch your legs, sit in a cafe or take a walk through the forest, then at any time you can stop and take a break from the road, breathe fresh air, run around, spread a bedspread and lie down a bit . You can also arrange a small snack in nature.

Train travel

Almost all people since childhood love trains, probably this has some kind of romance. The train remains one of the most comfortable and fastest means of transportation.



There are reserved seats and a business compartment in a standard carriage. You can have a snack or a cup of coffee. If the trip is at night, you can go to bed no matter what time you arrive at your final destination. For half an hour, the conductor will check whether everyone is ready to exit.

Trains in the direction of Vologda - St. Petersburg run quite often. You can safely choose the appropriate option and arrive at your destination at a convenient time. The cost of tickets varies from 1000 to 6000 rubles per seat.

White Nights Train

The Vologda - St. Petersburg route runs the daily branded White Nights train. It differs from other vehicles in its decoration and high class. The name comes from the phenomenon of the same name. St. Petersburg is famous for its white nights. All napkins, bedding and embroidery are Vologda products. The train has wagons with seats reserved seats and coupes, as well as business class and luxury.



In addition, some cars have a shower, and an iron with ironing board is provided. Conductors offer passengers tea and coffee, as well as sweets for free throughout the trip. There is also a restaurant car on the train.

To St. Petersburg by bus

If you opted for a bus, check the time and place of departure in advance. Buses travel on a strict schedule, just like trains. Take books, magazines, or board games with you on the go.



On the route Vologda - St. Petersburg, the bus is in transit from 8 to 12 hours. Therefore, calculate the time of arrival if you are traveling with children, it is best to book a hotel or find a place to stop in advance.

Travel more often

Plan your vacation for a trip to St. Petersburg for the summer, at which time the city is unusually beautiful. Warm weather, greenery in numerous parks, the ability to swim along the Neva on a water tram - this is the minimum that you can please yourself on a sunny day. Come see the city in other seasons. In St. Petersburg, you will always find what to do, depending on your hobbies.

Get ready for the trip in advance, consider all the options. Warm clothes, umbrellas, money for additional expenses - all this will not be superfluous in your trip. And if you have already visited St. Petersburg once, you will definitely want to return to this city again.
