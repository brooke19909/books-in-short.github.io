Bus station "Tushino": how to get there?

The Tushino bus station is the only transport company in Moscow that provides passenger transportation in the Riga direction to settlements:

  • Volokolamsk, Shakhovskaya and other cities of the Moscow region in a north-west direction;
  • Rzhev, Torzhok, Ostashkov and other cities of the Tver region;
  • Pskov;
  • Riga;
  • Tallinn;
  • Vilnius.

There are several ways to get to the Tushino bus station: by metro, train, bus, and personal transport.

Location

Bus station address: Moscow, Stratonavtov passage , 41. The nearest metro station is Tushinskaya. The object is located quite conveniently, since the distance from the lobby of the metro station, as well as from the railway station, is small (no more than 3-5 minutes walk).

Metro

If you plan to go to the Tushino bus station by metro, you should focus on the Tushinskaya station, located in the northwestern part of not only the subway, but also the capital.





To make it convenient to travel with a minimum number of transfers, you should focus on the Tagansko-Krasnopresnenskaya line. It is on it that Tushinskaya is located. If you go from the south or from the center, you need to board the first carriage. Upon arrival at your destination, climb the escalator to the transition, exit towards the bus station. Then walk along the sidewalk along the final bus to the gray one-story building of the bus station.

By train

You are lucky if you live or work in such a way that it is convenient to use Riga trains, as the Tushino bus station is very close to the platform and station of the same name. How to get, for example, from Nakhabino or Istra? Very simple. You need to buy a ticket to Tushino. Next, you should focus on the exit to the passage of the Stratonauts.

On a personal car

Tushino is located in close proximity to the Moscow Ring Road. Entry and exit is convenient from Krasnogorsk on the M9 highway (Moscow-Riga), that is, along Volokolamsk highway.





If you go from the center of Moscow, you should move along the Leningradskoye Shosse to the intersection of Alabyan and Baltiyskaya Streets, then follow straight towards Volokolamskoye Shosse until you turn to Tushinskaya Sq. Follow it to the bus station.

Ground transportation

Residents of the Tushino and Pokrovskoye-Streshnevo districts can get to the Tushino bus station by local buses: 210, 266, 614, 930. As well as routes: 2, 400, 741, 777.

As you can see, you can get to Tushino bus station by any means of transport without any problems. Therefore, feel free to choose the most convenient way for you. Have a good trip!
