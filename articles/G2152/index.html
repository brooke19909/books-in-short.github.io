Porfi Beach Hotel 3 * (Greece, Halkidiki): description, rating, photos and reviews

Greece is a democratic combination of sea holidays with an extensive excursion program. In the north of the country is the region of Halkidiki, popular with tourists. It is ideal for a relaxing family vacation. Even in hot summers, coastal resorts are surrounded by greenery, and tourists can always diversify their vacation by going on an excursion to the sights of ancient Greek history. There are plenty of accommodation options available for travelers. Expensive hotels are often located along the coast, but you can choose a budget place to stay if you wish. One of them is the budget complex Porfi Beach Hotel 3 *. You can see the photo, reviews and its description in this article.

More about hotel location

Often, budget hotels are located on the second and third lines of the sea, which means that the road to the beach every day for tourists will take at least 10-15 minutes. Sometimes the savings are worth it, but for some people this way can be difficult. For example, elderly tourists or families with small children. However, the Porfi Beach Hotel 3 * in Sithonia is located right on the seafront, which is considered a rarity for budget hotels. You can reach the private beach in just 2-3 minutes. The hotel is always quiet and peaceful, as there are no restaurants and bars nearby. Residential buildings are surrounded by a dense park. In the immediate vicinity of the complex are other hotels. A bus stop is 50 meters away. From here, tourists can get to the nearest villages - Metamorfosi and Nikiti. Both of them are about 3 km from the hotel.

The nearest airport is in the vicinity of Thessaloniki. It is there that flights from Moscow and St. Petersburg most often land. The easiest way to get from the airport is if you paid the transfer to the hotel in advance. You can also take a bus or taxi. As a rule, the path to the hotel takes about 1-1.5 hours.

basic information

It is worth noting that in the rating Porfi Beach Hotel 3 * primordially occupies high places, and tourists often call it one of the best budget hotels in Halkidiki. But they also note that this is still an inexpensive hotel, so you should not count on first-class quality of service. However, if you want to find an inexpensive accommodation option for a relaxing holiday at sea, then this complex is perfect for you. In the reviews of tourists, this hotel often receives a rating of 8/10 from its guests, which is a high indicator. In the ranking of the best hotels in Sithonia, the hotel ranks 18th out of 47, giving way to more expensive complexes.

The complex itself opened its doors to tourists back in 1991 and since then has been hosting guests from all over the world every year. Every few years, rooms and infrastructure facilities are regularly repaired. So, the last renovation of the premises was completed in 2017. The hotel is located in a green area right on the beach. Tourists can stay in the main building or in four additional smaller buildings. The hotel itself can hardly be called a crowded place to stay. It offers guests a total of 97 rooms, most of which are standard.

Hotel check-in rules are quite familiar. You can come here on holiday with children of any age. Children under 2 years old get discounts on accommodation. Pets are not allowed in the hotel. Upon registration, it is necessary to present an identity card. You can pay by credit card or cash. It is worth noting that some of the hotel staff speaks not only English, but also Russian.

Room Amenities

Despite the fact that the Porfi Beach Hotel 3 * hotel (Halkidiki, Sithonia, Nikiti) is considered a place for inexpensive accommodation, its rooms do not look outdated. On the contrary, they made modern repairs, and they are also equipped with all the necessary amenities for a comfortable stay. So, guests are offered a set of new and comfortable furniture. All rooms have single or double beds, bedside tables and wardrobes, a seating area, a table with a mirror for easy makeup. On the balcony there are sun loungers and a coffee table.

In addition to furniture, the rooms also have a set of additional equipment. It includes:

  • a television connected to satellite television;
  • individual air conditioning with remote control;
  • small refrigerator without filling;
  • built-in hairdryer in the bathroom;
  • a telephone that can be used both for communication with the administration and for international calls.

There is no separate safe in the rooms, but tourists can leave their valuables, documents and money in a paid cell at the reception. All rooms are serviced daily. Towels and bed linen at the hotel are changed at least 3 times a week.

Infrastructure and service

Since the resort complex Porfi Beach Hotel 3 * is a budget place to relax, you can not count on a first-class level of service. But the most necessary infrastructure facilities on its territory are still open. A 24-hour reception desk is open for guests, where an administrator is always present. He quickly solves the existing problems, and also tells you where you can relax nearby. In addition, at the counter you can exchange your currency for a local one or arrange a car rental. Here you can also hand over your luggage to a special storage room or leave valuables in a paid safe. At your request, the administrator is ready to call a doctor in the room around the clock. However, his services are not included in the price and are not covered by insurance.

Laundry facilities are available on site, but laundry is charged separately. But tourists can leave their car for free in a guarded parking lot near the complex. It is not necessary to reserve a place in advance. Wireless internet is available for a fee and only in public areas. In the reviews, tourists indicate that its speed is very low. There is also a small mini market where you can buy snacks, drinks and souvenirs, as well as beachwear and shoes. You can do your hair and makeup, as well as take a course of anti-aging and wellness treatments for your face at a local beauty salon.

Nutrition Concepts

Immediately it is worth noting that the Porfi Beach Hotel 3 * does not offer the all-inclusive concept, beloved by the Russians. Most often, tourists prefer to include only breakfast in the price, and the rest of the time they have lunch and dinner on their own. But if you wish, you can also pay for evening meals, as half board is available for guests. All breakfasts and dinners are served in the main hotel restaurant. A buffet is served here. Most of the dishes relate to European and Mediterranean cuisine.

Also on the territory of the Porfi Beach Hotel 3 * in Greece there is a tavern serving seafood and Greek dishes. In fine weather, you can dine on the outdoor terrace, located next to the pool. There is also a bar, but all drinks and snacks are available for a fee.

Beach holidays at the hotel

According to tourists, the main advantage of relaxing at the Porfi Beach Hotel 3 * (Halkidiki, Sithonia) is the presence of its own beach. Indeed, often budget complexes do not have a separate access to the sea, and guests are forced to visit public places. The beach near the hotel is small, but completely sandy, so tourists can walk on the sand without shoes. The entrance to the sea is gentle and even, which is very pleasant for guests with small children. The bottom here is also sandy, but sometimes there is sharp pebble. Therefore, before bathing, it is better to wear shoes. The beach itself is fully equipped for a comfortable stay. For a fee, tourists are provided with sun loungers and sun umbrellas. But you can always sit for free directly in the sand on a towel.

If you do not want to swim in the sea, then you can always relax near the outdoor pool. It is filled with fresh water, but it does not heat up even in cold weather. Near the pool there is a spacious recreation area. Here, tourists can also sunbathe on sun loungers or mattresses. Beach towels are provided at the reception. They are free. Tourists also do not need to pay a deposit.

Other entertainment options

In high season, the Porfi Beach Hotel 3 * (Greece, Sithonia, Halkidiki) has an animation team that entertains tourists. You can also visit the water entertainment center located on the beach. For a fee, tourists can enjoy a motor boat, water ski or banana ride. The beach also has a volleyball net in the sand. A tennis court is on site. Rental of rackets and balls, as well as the inclusion of lighting is not included in the price. Tennis lessons are also paid separately. Tourists can play basketball for free. In the evening, a billiard room opens. Darts equipment can be rented for a fee.

Hotel criticism

Reviews about Porfi Beach Hotel 3 * tourists leave mostly positive. But some guests did not like the hotel stay. They indicate that their expectations from the rest were completely not met, therefore they do not recommend other tourists to settle here. Other guests note that the hotel is fully consistent with the asking price, although it has a number of minor flaws. In their reviews, guests recommend that hotel owners eliminate the following service deficiencies:

  • the rooms on the ground floor are in need of repair, therefore it is better to move into the rooms located above;
  • all apartments here are quite dark, so even in the afternoon you have to turn on the lamp;
  • the sea near the hotel is very dirty, so tourists do not recommend swimming in it;
  • there are no infrastructure facilities near the complex, so you have to take a bus every time and go to the center, which is not very convenient;
  • the hotel is allowed to smoke throughout its territory, so it smells of tobacco smoke everywhere, which tourists with small children did not like.

Thus, we can say that the Porfi Beach Hotel 3 * in Greece is a good option for those tourists who want a cheap vacation at sea. And although it has its drawbacks, it still stands out positively among other three-star complexes. It can be recommended to tourists who come with young children. Also, people who prefer a quiet and measured rest will like it here.
