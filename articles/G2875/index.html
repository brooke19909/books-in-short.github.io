Rest in Rimini: reviews of tourists

Rimini is a beautiful resort town located on the chic Adriatic coast. It is famous for its contrasts - here the ancient Roman and medieval buildings harmoniously coexist with modern hotels built in the Art Nouveau style.





Attractions Rimini

Reviews of tourists confirm the popularity of this Italian resort. There are more than one and a half thousand hotels, a huge number of villas and apartments ready to open their doors for guests from any country in the world. The city is proud of its many kilometers of golden sandy beaches. Not a single traveler will be left indifferent by dozens of discos, chic restaurants, bars, trendy shops and water amusement parks. Hospitable Italy will find suitable entertainment for any guest. Rimini, reviews of which are very flattering, will surprise with its bizarre architecture. Here, ancient Roman buildings are colorfully combined with such treasures of the Middle Ages, such as, for example, the Malatesta Temple.





Shopping in Rimini

Guest reviews of the city cannot ignore the opportunity to go to the most famous European stores. Most fashion boutiques are located along the beautiful Adriatic coast, on the city's promenade. The most fashionable shops are concentrated in the heart of the city, on the streets of Garibaldi, Corso Augusto, Gambalunga and on Tre Marteri square. Shops work the same way as in Europe - usually from ten in the morning to eight in the evening, they close twice a day for a break. Travelers should remember that on Sunday, many of the boutiques are closed.

Rimini beaches

Reviews of beach vacations at this famous Italian resort are also impressive. The coastline of the city with the beautiful name La Marina stretches for fifteen kilometers along the coast. Golden sand beaches are in excellent condition. Mostly guests of the resort can use paid beaches, although there are several free sites where there are various cafes, cozy restaurants, and beautiful beach bars.





Cuisine in Rimini

Reviews about the cuisine of this beautiful resort are full of romance and emotions. Here, traditional Italian restaurants with famous Parmesan, Parma ham, Bologna sausage and balsamic vinegar are combined with Canadian, Asian, Indian and Japanese cuisines. In the best restaurants of Rimini: Lo Squero, Da Guido, Il Lurido and Marinelli - you can taste delicious pasta Barilla and fragrant Piadina cakes, which are traditionally baked exclusively in this region. An average dinner at the Rimini restaurant will cost about ten euros. In some restaurants you can be offered complex dinners, the price of which includes a glass of chic Italian wine. By the way, you can conduct a tasting of local drinks at the famous Tenuta del Monsinore winery in Rimini, the reviews of which are the most enthusiastic. Rest in this beautiful old Italian town, the warm sea, fashionable shops, chic beaches, modern restaurants and hospitable hotels will be remembered for your whole life.
