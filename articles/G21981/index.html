Bank "Renaissance Credit": reviews of experts and ordinary consumers



The financial structure, better known as Renaissance Credit Bank, reviews of which can be found on the pages of many periodicals, was founded almost 14 years ago, but it got its name only in 2003. From the very beginning, this organization was focused on issuing various consumer loans, however, with the growth of the structure, the range of its services was significantly expanded. Today it is a large bank with more than five million customers. The loan products provided by him consist in the issuance of targeted and non-targeted loans, plastic cards and car loans.

Representative offices of this company can be found in more than 60 regions of the Russian Federation. On the website of this financial institution you can always see where the nearest Renaissance Credit bank is located. The addresses of the offices of all representative offices are listed in the corresponding section of the website. In addition, the bank cooperates with the widest retail chains in Russia, which increases the number of its representative offices by more than 20,000. The simplicity of the design of services has made this company very popular among the country's population.

Renaissance Credit - services



The top of popularity among all the bank's services has long been taking credit cards. Renaissance Credit Bank, reviews of which are usually benevolent, has a very soft policy in selecting clients. To receive plastic, the client must meet several criteria:

  • Be between the ages of 21 and 65
  • bring with you a valid passport of a citizen of the Russian Federation in your name;
  • fill out a special application form;
  • specify your work and mobile phones.

Making a credit card usually lasts from 15 minutes to an hour. The maximum amount that can be borrowed is 300 thousand rubles, and the grace period for using the card is 55 days. After this period, the borrower will have to service the loan at an interest rate of 40% per annum. At the same time, the minimum amount that must be paid each month will be 10% of the amount owed. If you have questions about the maintenance of the program, you can not visit the bank "Renaissance Credit". The hotline, whose phone can be found in any booklet of the company, is always ready to help in a difficult situation. Just dial the phone number and an experienced specialist will give your advice on the issue.
