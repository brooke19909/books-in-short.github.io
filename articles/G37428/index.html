Cole Rice Tips

The travel photographer has developed as many as 15 tips for those who cannot afford to buy an expensive professional camera and can only take photos on their mobile phones. The information below will help you find answers to the question of how to make a beautiful photo:

A lot of applications on smartphones have these functions, and they can be used to make the photo better. You can also use filters to add warm tones to the picture.
  • Vignette. This technique is one of Cole's most beloved. Its meaning is that it darkens the photo at the borders, while brightening the middle, which makes the photo much brighter.
  • The effect of 50 percent. It consists in editing the photo as you like best, and then reduce all changes by 50%. The meaning is very simple: your photo will look more natural.
