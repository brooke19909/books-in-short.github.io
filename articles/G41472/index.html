Banks in Kostanay: addresses, opening hours and contact numbers

Information about the banks of Kostanay, their addresses and work schedule is available in many sources: search engines, corporate sites, contact centers and so on. However, the search can be tedious, and the information provided on a third-party resource may not always be accurate. The operating hours, addresses and telephone number of Halyk Bank in Kostanay, Sberbank, ATF Bank and other credit organizations are available for review in this article. All data is taken from the official websites of institutions.

What banks are there in Kostanay?

The city has 46 service offices of thirteen commercial banks of the Republic of Kazakhstan. The greatest availability of branches falls in Kostanay to Halyk Bank and Kaspi Bank. The city has subsidiaries of foreign banks: VTB, Sberbank of Russia, Home Credit Bank and Alfa Bank. In Kostanay, there is also a territorial branch of the National Bank of Kazakhstan and offices of the Housing Construction Savings Bank of the Republic of Kazakhstan.

Weekdays and holidays

Almost half of all banks in Kostanay work according to the usual five-day schedule. Branches of other credit organizations also function on Saturday. Only one bank has doors open for customers all seven days a week - at Kaspi Bank. The opening hours of some departments may differ from the above: for example, there may be a moving schedule, lunch break or extended working day. More accurate information about the operating hours of a given structural unit must be clarified on the bank's website or in the contact center of a credit institution.

For the convenience of customers in Kostanay there are self-service banking devices working around the clock: ATMs and information and payment terminals. With the help of them you can withdraw money from payment cards, replenish your current account, pay off a loan, make a transfer and so on. The list and location of such devices is on the websites of banks and electronic maps of Kostanay.

Legal entities service

The business hours of business units in almost all of the above banks are the same: from Monday to Friday, departments for working with legal entities operate from nine in the morning until six in the evening, Saturday and Sunday are considered weekend. The exception is SB Sberbank of Russia JSC and Halyk Bank JSC: in Kostanay, business representatives can be served in these organizations on Saturday from nine in the morning until two in the afternoon.

In conclusion

Almost all commercial banks registered in Kazakhstan offer their services in Kostanay, with the exception of small and highly specialized credit organizations. On the territory of this northern city there are 13 banks, including 45 branches and branches. Given the size of this administrative center and the number of its population, the availability of bank branches, terminals and ATMs is very high. Comfortable service conditions in Kostanai branches are provided for representatives of small and medium-sized businesses. Due to the flexible work schedule and the absence of days off at some banks, Kostanay customers can choose for themselves the most convenient time and place of service.
