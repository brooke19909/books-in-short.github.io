Francesco Carrozzini: biography and career

One of the well-established directors to date is Francesco Carrozzini. Young and talented, he released about a dozen short films that were presented at various film festivals.

Francesco Carrozzini. Biography

September 9, 1982 in Italy in the provincial town of Monza (a suburb of Milan) was born Francesco Carrozzini. The biography of little Francesco is not too large, today little is known. Father was the chief editor of Vogue magazine. His mother, Franca Sozzani, who wrote several books about fashion, was the winner of art awards.





Francesco Carrozzini from childhood was surrounded by musicians, artists and actors. As a teenager, he and his comrades began to shoot his first short films. Francesco was so keen on directing that in 1999 he moved to Los Angeles to study directing at the University of California. Then Carrozzini returned to Milan and studied philosophy at a local university. After some time, the young man left for New York, where he is now.

Francesco had an affair with singer Lana Del Rey. For the first time in public, they were seen in the summer of 2014. According to media reports, the couple broke up, but there were no official statements on termination of the relationship.
