Hotels in Astrakhan: reviews, photos, phone numbers, addresses

Every year, thousands of tourists come to the hospitable city of Astrakhan. Someone is brought here by official affairs, someone wants to relax on the Volga, and someone is interested in the history of this ancient city. To comfortably resettle their guests, the city authorities have built and continue to build hotels. Today there are more than thirty of them, including mini-hotels and guest houses.

It should be noted that the hotels in Astrakhan are all very different - in terms of comfort, service, cost of living, location. Most of them provide not only comfortable accommodation, but also the opportunity for business meetings and negotiations. Today we will introduce some hotels of Astrakhan to you. We will indicate their addresses in the description. We hope that you will be able to choose the suitable option for accommodation if you plan to visit this wonderful city in the near future.

"Victoria Palace": 3, Red Embankment, tel. (8512) -39-48-01

If you list the most popular and famous hotels in Astrakhan, then you should start with this one. The hotel is located in the business part of the city. It occupies an eight-story building and offers visitors 62 well-equipped rooms, an opportunity for fruitful work and relaxation.



Hotel rooms meet modern European standards. They are equipped with modern comfortable furniture, air conditioning, work desks, showers, plasma panels, lightproof curtains. Not all hotels in Astrakhan can boast of such a convenient location. In the city center next to the hotel there are restaurants, cafes, shops, cinemas.

The hotel has a round-the-clock surveillance system, which ensures the safety of guests. In front of the building there is a spacious (guarded) parking.

Services

In addition to the modern elegant room stock, the hotel guests are invited to take advantage of the hotel:

  • conference room;
  • a restaurant;
  • a lobby bar;
  • massage room;
  • Laundry
  • gift shop;
  • meeting room;
  • a beauty parlor.

Guests at the Victoria Palace Hotel can rent a car or use the shuttle service. Here you will be offered to visit a fascinating city tour.

Reviews

Most visitors to this hotel note its very convenient location. Comfortable rooms. Many visitors note that not all hotels in Astrakhan allow residents to stay in rooms with their pets. At the Victoria Palace Hotel, this is allowed. One disadvantage of this hotel is the slow service in the restaurant.
