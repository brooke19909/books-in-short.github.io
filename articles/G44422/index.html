Rest: from Samara to Turkey. Flight "Samara-Turkey". Holidays in Turkey: prices

If you contact the travel agency with a request to choose an option that the whole family will like, then most likely the manager will offer the most popular vacation option. There are a number of flights from Samara to Turkey that will deliver directly to the magical coast, to the world of the warm sea, the gentle sun and fruit trees growing right along the streets. I want to come back here again and again, each time experiencing a whole rainbow of emotions.





To the resort from Samara

Everyone has heard about the beautiful Turkish beaches and the highest level of service, remember more about the unique monuments of architecture and art - and it will become clear where to go on vacation. Thousands of tourists fly annually from Samara to Turkey. You will discover hundreds of ways to have fun: exciting excursions, incredibly varied shopping, a yacht trip, swimming in the sea, diving and much more. Depending on your capabilities, Turkish coasts can offer hotels from economy class, accessible to most Russians, to luxury villas and residences.

Holidays for every taste

Depending on your preferences, you can choose your own, individual holiday. Those who want to improve their health fly from Samara to Turkey. Here, for this, all conditions are created. Each hotel has swimming pools with heated water, with sea or regular, which can be used at any time. A variety of spa centers will offer anti-stress programs, so important in our rhythm of life. For men and women, there are short-term courses on rejuvenation and weight loss. To come from the resort, having thrown off a couple of extra pounds, is this not a dream? Add to this the abundance of fresh fruits and vegetables, mild climate, warm sea and fresh air. Not being sanatoriums, local resorts have a healing effect.





Beach lovers can enjoy the warm sun throughout the entire period. For those who like outdoor activities, it is worth flying from Samara to Turkey every year. Sea sports: sailing, windsurfing, diving and many others are widely represented here. For youth companies conduct a large number of adventure tours and incendiary discos. Parents with children can relax properly, instructing the nannies and animators to look after the kids.

Fans and connoisseurs of history, legends and ancient architecture will be interested in informative guided tours. And landscapes and amazing landscapes delight absolutely everyone. Mountains and plains, forests and gardens, sea and sand, sunrises and sunsets - this can not be described, you just need to see.

Choose the time of year

On the coast of the Mediterranean or Aegean, it is always good, but the influx of tourists is noted from March to November. In spring and autumn, at the lowest prices you will discover incomparable Turkey. In September, you can leave Samara for a ten-day vacation for just $ 500. In terms of weather conditions, this month is the most comfortable. There is no intense heat, so numerous excursions are only a pleasure. The temperature of air and water is the same, about 27 degrees. For tourists from Samara, autumn is the best time to relax, as the summer heat is too unusual.





Turkey Resorts in the Mediterranean Sea

The only vehicle delivering tourists to their destination is an airplane. Samara-Turkey, it is a very popular route today. Direct flights are operated by Orenburg Airlines, and the final destination is Alania. Other resorts can only be reached with a change, for example, via Moscow. This resort town is characterized by economical hotels with comfortable conditions, without unnecessary luxury. Tourists note excellent service and good food. The downside is the distance from the airport.

The second economy resort is Side. The distance from the airport is compensated by "luxury" hotels at affordable prices. Antalya closes the top three, an excellent resort, but the entrance to the sea is uncomfortable, immediately a steep cliff. For families with children is not very suitable.

The most expensive and luxurious areas of Turkey are the resorts of Belek and Kundu. They are the youngest on the coast, equipped with modern infrastructure. Here are luxury hotels, sandy beaches with a smooth deepening into the sea, the town is close to the airport, if you are used to luxury, then here you are.

For lovers of affordable shopping and cheap excursions, Turkey opens its doors. Kemer from Samara receives the largest number of tourists. This resort is located in the coastal strip between the sea and the mountains. Mountains protect from the winds, and the sea guarantees the mildest climate. Beach lovers should know that they will have to lie on pebbles. The beaches near the hotels are covered with sand, and entering the sea is still pebbled. In Kemer, nightlife is in full swing, there are a huge number of clubs where celebrities come to concerts very often.





Aegean Resorts

They are not as often heard as the above. You will be offered to visit Pigal Beach in the form of an excursion tour, no matter which resort you choose to stay. This is a modern equipped beach that offers countless opportunities for water sports. Recreation complex with pools, tennis courts, bars, gyms allows everyone to spend time with benefit for the soul.

If you are a fan of noisy companies, then your choice of Bodrum is the capital of nightlife in Turkey, the most vibrant city. This is the best place for connoisseurs and lovers of water sports, surfing and diving.

On the peninsula is one of the most beautiful resorts - Marmaris. He is considered the best among all the others. The European image and romantic landscapes attract family people here. The resort is located in a bay with three islands. A large number of secluded and uninhabited bays makes it possible to spend the day in the lap of nature with the most important person.

There are beautifully designed kilometer-long beaches, and the water in the sea is completely clear, turquoise. Growing palm trees emphasize the beauty and exoticism of these places.





Tours to Turkey

Each travel company in Samara will gladly make an offer to spend its holidays here. Because this is a win-win option that will appeal to all family members and will be affordable for most residents of the city. Departure from Samara to Turkey is carried out daily. Air transportation on a given route is performed by ten airlines:

  1. NordWind operates flights every day in the summer and every 10 days in the spring and fall. Arrival Point - Antalya.
  2. Orenburg Airlines, daily from May to September, to Antalya.
  3. Ural Airlines, seven days a month to Antalya.
  4. Utair, Abakan Avia, Transaero, two, three flights a month to Antalya.
  5. Ural Airlines, flight with landing in Bodrum, once every 10 days.
  6. NordWind, Orenburg Airlines, Ural Airlines, landing in Dalaman, once every 10 days.

These are far from all air travel options in the Samara-Turkey direction. Direct flights take about 3 hours. With a change through Moscow, you can use the services of Aeroflot Airlines. Charter flights

Most tourists from Samara prefer their regular. The reason is simple: they are 35-75% cheaper. The average price tag for a regular carrier ticket is at the level of 30 thousand rubles, and the tourist tariff does not exceed 20 thousand. An exception may be the New Year holidays. In addition, a charter flight is always a direct flight, without transfers, which saves time.

An experienced travel agency manager will help you choose the Samara-Turkey flight and will be able to make a number of profitable offers. Especially if the season is coming to an end and last minute packages are available.





Ride with children

Turkey is the best holiday destination for a young family with kids. Each hotel is equipped with a playground; there, the baby will not be bored for sure. A well-developed animation service and a full staff of caring nannies will allow parents to fully enjoy their vacation. Separately, it should be noted that it is necessary to choose the autumn months, when the air temperature is close to the usual one - this will help the child more easily transfer rest in Turkey. Prices for tickets at this time are much lower, which is very important for a young family. If in the season a tour for 7 days can be purchased for $ 1,000 or more, then in September-October it can be purchased for $ 500-700.

The cost depends on the chosen resort. The most economical are Alanya, Antalya, Kemer. The number of stars at your hotel will also affect the amount you pay. I must say that the service here is wonderful everywhere, from a modest 3 stars to suites.

Turkish cuisine

It is quite sophisticated and at the same time not too exotic, which all guests like. Both vegans and meat lovers will find a dish to their taste here. The abundance of vegetables, herbs, fish, baked in many ways meat and, of course, sweets will delight the whole family and even small sweet ones. The most popular kebab is meat, fish or poultry baked over an open fire.
