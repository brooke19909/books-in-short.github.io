Bosch WLG 20260 OE washing machine: specifications, comparison with competitors and reviews

Currently, the daily work of people is greatly facilitated by innovative technologies. For example, the Bosch WLG 20260 OE washing machine performs tasks with minimal human involvement. It is enough to choose a certain mode and after a few minutes just take out clean linen.

Every domestic customer is familiar with the Bosch trademark. Over the entire period of activity, she has earned an excellent reputation. The equipment sold under this brand has been working without breakdowns for a long time. The manufacturer applies modern technologies and high-quality materials that significantly extend the operational life of devices. The company pays particular attention to functionality. The Bosch WLG 20260 model (reviews below) is equipped with a large number of programs, intelligent control, and has a high energy efficiency class. And also, importantly, there is an economical washing mode. The price of such a model is 20,000-23,000 rubles. Of course, there are cheaper options for lesser-known brands, but German quality is worth the money.

So, let's figure out what advantages and disadvantages this model of a washing machine has, and also conduct a comparative analysis with competing options.



Short description

Bosch WLG 20260 OE is an automated instrument equipped with intelligent control. Case height - 85 cm, legs adjustable, removable cover. The standard width is 60 cm. This model of the washing machine is narrow, its depth is only 45 cm. The hatch for loading clothes is located on the front panel. The manufacturer does not recommend using the top cover, but if you place light objects (powder, conditioner, cosmetics) on it, then they will not harm the washing machine.

The instructions that come with the device contain information on technical specifications. The most important is the classification of the efficiency of the device: washing - A, energy consumption - A (0.19 kW * h / kg), spin - C (1000 rpm). The washing machine is designed for 5 kg - approximately two sets of bed linen and several towels. Such a load is quite enough for a family of 3-5 people. Water consumption per wash is 45 liters.



Design features

The case of Bosch WLG 20260 OE is coated with white enamel. The design is made in a classic style. The control panel and powder compartment are a rectangle. On the box there is a special hole for the hand, above it is the name of the brand and a series of models. It is divided into three cells: on the sides - for powder (preliminary and main wash), in the middle - for air conditioning. In the center is a digital display and control buttons (temperature selection, Eco, spin speed, etc.). On the right side there is a regulator with which you can select a specific washing mode. Between them is the operation indicator, which glows in red, and the power button (pause). The control panel is informative. It’s easy to understand, since all the buttons are signed.



Modes

The Bosch WLG 20260 OE washing machine is equipped with 16 automatic modes. The main ones are designed for certain types of fabrics.

  • Cotton. Maximum load of linen. It is possible to use pre-wash and intensive wash options. Temperature condition: from cold to 90 °. Spin speed - up to 1000 rpm, if necessary, can be reduced.
  • Mixed underwear. You can fill the drum no more than half - the optimal weight is 2 kg. The temperature is selected from cold to 40 °. The spin speed is programmed to a maximum (1000 rpm).
  • Thin Linen / Silk. Incomplete loading - 2 kg. Temperature range - cold, 30 °, 40 °. The spin level is medium (600, 800 rpm).
  • Wool. It is recommended to wash no more than 2 kg in one cycle. The optimum temperature is 30 ° - 40 °. Spin and rinse mode - with stops, speed - no more than 800 rpm.
  • Synthetics. The weight of the load is 2.5 kg. Temperature mode: cold, 30 °, 40 °. Spin speed - maximum.
  • Jeans. Drum filling - 2 kg. It is not recommended to wash at temperatures above 40 °, it can be reduced to 30 °. The laundry is squeezed out at 800 and 1000 rpm.
  • Shirts / Blouses. Wash things in a delicate way. Loading - 2 kg. Temperature - 40 °, 60 °. Spin - 800 rpm.
  • Outerwear. Weight - 2 kg. Temperature - 30 ° or 40 ° (depending on the type of fabric). Squeeze no more than 800 rpm. You can use a super rinse.
  • Night wash. Loading of linen - 2 kg. The temperature range is cold and 40 °. Spin and beep are disabled by default.
  • Kids' things. The load on this mode is maximum. Permissible temperature - 40 °, 60 °. Spin at maximum speed.
  • Express wash 30 ´ / 15 ´. It is used for a small amount of linen (1 kg). The range of water heating is 30 °, 40 °. Spin speed - 800, 1000 rpm.

In each described mode, you can use the timer functions, spin off.



Additional features

The Bosch WLG 20260 OE has additional options.

  • Spin - change the speed of rotation of the drum in one minute. Valid values ​​are 400, 600, 800, 1000 revolutions.
  • Drain - the machine drains the water without rinsing and spinning.
  • Rinse - an option that includes an additional rinse with spin.

Benefits

The Bosch WLG 20260 OE model, like many other devices, has a number of significant advantages. The main ones are:

  • Modern design.
  • Convenient control panel.
  • A sufficient number of automatic modes.
  • Low noise during operation.
  • Alert by beep.
  • Correction of temperature and spin speed.
  • It perfectly erases and rinses linen.
  • Compact size.
  • Removable cover for installation.
  • Convenient box for powder.
  • There is no vibration.
  • The presence of leakage protection.




disadvantages

The Bosch WLG 20260 OE model has practically no shortcomings. The only thing that was noticed was minor flaws.

  • Poor filter on the drain hose.
  • Whistle at high speeds during the spin cycle.
  • Water remains on the hatch seal after washing.
  • The sound notification does not turn off until you close the door or turn off the device from the outlet.
  • Weak castle, quickly crashes.




Competitors

Bosch WLG 20260 OE can compete only with models from brands such as Electrolux, LG, Gorenje, Samsung.

  • Electrolux EWS1052NDU - 5 kg washing machine. Equipped with touch screen display. Dimensions: 60 × 85 × 38 cm. Washing modes - 15. Efficiency: washing - class A, electricity consumption - class A ++ , spin - class C. The maximum rotation speed of the drum is 1000 rpm. Noise level: during washing - 58 dB, during spin - 76 dB. Unlike the Bosch model, a quick program takes 20 minutes. Equipped with Fuzzy Logic technology. Cost - 19000-21000 rubles.
  • LG FH0C3LD. Dimensions of the device: 60 × 85 × 44 cm. Maximum load - 5 kg. Automatic programs - 9. Noise during operation - 55 dB (for washing), 76 dB (for spinning). The express program is designed for 30 minutes. There is a diagnosis of SmartDiagnosis, the adjustment system is 6 motion. Classification: energy consumption and washing - A, spin - B. Price range: from 19,000 to 23,000 rubles.
  • The Gorenje W529S is rated for the same loading weight as the Bosch WLG 20260 OE. Customer reviews indicate that these devices are very similar. Dimensions of the model of the Gorenje trademark: 60 × 44 × 85 cm. The number of programs - 15. There are delicate modes. The spin speed is higher than that of the Bosch model - 1200 rpm. Water consumption - 49 liters. The energy consumption class is A ++ , the wash cycle is A, the spin cycle is B. The spin cycle is quieter, producing a sound that equals 66 dB. You can only change the temperature yourself. Sold in the same price category as the Bosch washing machine.
  • Samsung WF60F1R0E2WDUA may well compete with the described model. Up to 6 kg of laundry can be washed in one cycle. The control is mechanical, there is a display. The programmed modes are 6. Dimensions: 60 × 45 × 85 cm. Noise during the spin cycle is equal to 77 dB. In this mode, the drum rotates at a speed of 1200 rpm. Electricity consumption corresponds to class A ++ . The cost is approximately the same as that of the Bosch model.

Washer Bosch WLG 20260: reviews

So, let's now find out the owners opinion on the WLG 20260 OE model. Were they not disappointed after several years of operation? After reviewing the reviews, we can confidently say that more than 90% of buyers were completely satisfied with the purchase. What was especially emphasized? First of all, on the functionality. Fast washing, which takes only 15 minutes, has appealed to many. The remaining programs are programmed with optimal temperature and spin speed. It is possible to wash delicate fabrics without fear of damaging the texture. The control panel is quite convenient and intuitive. All buttons have information. Programs are not encoded in numbers, which many owners like.

But what disappointed buyers was the lock on the door. Many argue that problems with it begin after a few months of operation. Otherwise, there are no complaints about the operation of the washing machine.
