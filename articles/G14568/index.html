Core technology

German Liebher refrigerators are a treasury of various innovations that help to improve the quality of food storage. The corporation offers the following technologies in its numerous models:

BioFresh is a refrigerator system in which you can adjust the temperature and humidity level inside the equipment. This function allows you to choose the optimal conditions for storing different types of food. For meat, fish and milk, a low level of moisture is more suitable, while for fruits and vegetables it is high. Thanks to this technology, you can increase the shelf life of products at least three times. In addition, this system does not allow food to freeze completely, which allows you to save all the necessary vitamins, minerals and taste.

Other companies offer a similar technology, calling it a "freshness zone" and others, but the author of this innovation is undoubtedly Liebher. Refrigerators, reviews of which are only enthusiastic, are presented in a wide range and have additional functions.

CoolPlus is a system that protects the refrigerator from decreasing ambient temperature. This technology is used in single compressor models. The system operates in such a way that when the ambient temperature decreases, the compressor of the device starts to work intermittently. This is reflected in the climate of the camera. This technology allows you to extend the compressor. This is achieved due to the presence of a sensor in the freezer, which responds to changes in ambient temperature and thereby regulates the level of activity of the compressor.

Door sliding is a door installation technology in which it connects directly to a furniture niche. This function uses the built- in Liebher refrigerator .

Door on door is a technology in which the furniture door is directly connected to the refrigerator.

FrostControl is a system that protects products in the event of power outages. The technology captures the initial temperature values ‚Äã‚Äãuntil the power is turned off and holds them for as long as possible.

NoFrost is a popular cooling system that avoids the formation of frost and ice on the walls of the chamber. This is achieved by special air circulation, in which moisture leaves the refrigerator. At the same time, all products are blown with cool air, which allows to qualitatively increase the shelf life without freezing to the camera machines.





SmartFreeze is a special refrigerator cooling technology that allows you to quickly freeze large volumes of products.

SuperCool - a special mode of rapid cooling, in which the temperature in the chamber for 6 hours is reduced to +2 degrees. After that, the technical device automatically goes into normal operation. This function is used in cases where you need to quickly cool a large number of products as quickly as possible. With this technology, the temperature level of those products that are already inside the equipment does not increase.

SuperFrost - superfast freezing of food in the freezer. This function allows you to cool a large amount of food at temperatures from -32 to -38 degrees. Freezing in this mode allows you to better maintain quality indicators of products.

Repair of refrigerators "Liebher"

The technical equipment of this company has high quality indicators and, accordingly, a long period of operation. But, like any other device, it can break down and then will need some repair.

A complete replacement of the Liebher refrigerator is required in very rare cases. Most often, the problem is the deterioration of the components or in the failure of the electronics.



The main possible malfunctions of the Liebher refrigerator:

  • problems with the electronic board;
  • malfunction of the temperature sensor;
  • compressor failure;
  • freon leakage;
  • malfunction of heaters and fuses.

Maintenance

In order to prevent breakdown and subsequent repair of Liebher refrigerators, care must be taken to timely diagnose technical equipment and eliminate minor malfunctions. Most often, this process is based on the following points:

  • elimination of high moisture in the system;
  • solution to the problem of freon leakage;
  • replacement thermostat;
  • removal of high levels of refrigerant;
  • door suspension;
  • diagnostics of electric fan.

In most cases, the master can repair the damage directly on site. The most common problem is a malfunction of the control unit. The reason for this may be a normal short circuit or fluctuations in the supply of electricity.

The most difficult problem that Liebher owners of refrigeration equipment may encounter is an evaporator leak in the internal structure of the cabinet. This is the only malfunction that must be fixed in the workshop. A qualified craftsman will solve all other problems directly at home at the owner of the refrigerator.

It is important to entrust the repair work to a professional who will accurately determine the cause of the breakdown and will be able to fix it as soon as possible. If you try to independently solve this problem or entrust it to a person without special knowledge and experience, you can only aggravate the situation. This will entail additional financial and time costs.

As a preventive measure, the following diagnostic steps are recommended:

  • reliability check of compressor and equipment shock absorbers;
  • cleaning the capacitor and its capacity;
  • comprehensive cleaning of the drainage hole;
  • adjustment of thermostat indicators;
  • diagnostics and inspection of the air circulation system;
  • check voltage
