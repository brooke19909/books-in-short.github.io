Holidays in the city of Narvik in Norway

Narvik in Norway is a small town located in the northern part of the country. It is located on a peninsula surrounded by fjords and mountains. Due to its picturesque location and the famous ski resort, many tourists come to Narvik.

History of the city

The official date of foundation of Narvik in Norway is 1902. Initially, it was a port city, and unlike many cities that played an important role in establishing trade relations, it remains one of the leading transport hubs in the country. The port of Narvika is the center of transport and logistics development in Norway.

The harbor in this city is not covered by ice and is protected from the wind, which makes the port of the city one of the most important in the country. And thanks to the Gulf Stream, tourists can enjoy the mild climate. A lot of cargo passes through the port of Narvik, most of which is ore. But due to its good location, other types of goods are also delivered. From Narvik, ore is sent to all countries of the world.

Winter fun

One of the main attractions of Narvik is the ski resort of Narvikfjell. It creates ideal conditions for winter sports. In total there are 75 descents, the total length of which is 20 km. Narvikfjell is the best ski resort not only in Norway but throughout Scandinavia.

In addition to constant snow cover and excellent conditions, this ski resort is known for having risen to the observation deck, you can enjoy amazing views of the ocean. Narvikfjell has a ski school with professional instructors. Visitors can also rent sports equipment. The ski season lasts from late November to May, but it is best to come from February to March.

Other attractions in the city

Tours to Norway are becoming increasingly popular. People from all over the world are increasingly attracted by the mysterious and majestic beauty of this country. In Narvik, in addition to winter sports, tourists can also climb, ride a mountain bike and go fishing.

The city has a unique attraction - cave paintings. Vacationers can find them using the map for tourists or following the signs. Drawings with images of people and animals are made on a stone, which is located on the street. All visitors to this Norwegian town visit this archaeological site.

In Narvik in Norway, there is also the northernmost zoo in the world, which is called Polar. It is located in the Salangsdalen Valley, which can be reached by bus. In the Polar Zoo you can see wolves, brown bears, wolverines, lynxes and other animals of the Far North.

There are several bars and restaurants in the city of Narvik where you can taste delicious Scandinavian dishes and play bowling. The most famous restaurant in the city is located on the observation deck at an altitude of 656 m. You can get there by the Narvikfjella lift, which also works in summer. Restaurant visitors can enjoy not only delicious dishes, but also beautiful views from the observation deck. Also in the city there are night clubs.

Shopping & Hotels

Where to stay in Narvik in Norway? You can stay in comfortable hotels for a reasonable price. Almost all hotels have restaurants where you can also get acquainted with the peculiarities of Scandinavian cuisine.

There is a shopping center in Narvik where tourists can buy all the necessary goods and purchase souvenirs. There is a Vinmonopol liquor store in the same shopping center, but its work schedule differs from the work of other stores of the shopping center: it is open from Monday to Saturday. From alcoholic drinks it is worth getting "Aquavit".

Most often they buy souvenirs related to the theme of the Vikings and trolls, which occupy a special place in Scandinavian folklore. Often purchased and fishing gear. Of course, everyone knows about the famous Scandinavian sweaters, but keep in mind that their cost is quite high. Some also bring gastronomic gifts - seafood, meat and cheese.

Climate features

Despite the fact that Narvik is very close to the North Pole, the weather there is not too harsh. This is due to the influence of the warm Gulf Stream. In winter, the number of sunny days becomes very small, but you can observe such a unique natural phenomenon as the northern lights.

Winter in those parts is mild, the temperature in Narvik is from -5 to -10 degrees Celsius, and sometimes above zero. From the second half of May until the end of July, the city begins a period of white nights. The climate of Narvik adds even more attractiveness among tourists.

Tours to Norway are popular due to the fact that more and more people are interested in winter sports. Many are attracted by the beauty of the North. Narvik is a great opportunity to visit the famous ski resort and enjoy magnificent views.
