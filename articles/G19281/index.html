Ilya Ulyanov - the great Russian enlightener of the 19th century: biography, family, achievements

Ulyanov Ilya Nikolaevich - the great Russian statesman in the field of education of the 19th century.

He made a significant contribution to the development of public education in the country, initiated a number of major endeavors in the field of education. Thanks to him, innovative forms of training were introduced in educational institutions, and teachers themselves began to take qualification courses. Teaching professionals began to engage in professional teachers.

The childhood of Ilya Ulyanov

July 14, 1831 in the family of a fugitive peasant from the Nizhny Novgorod province, who settled in Astrakhan, Ilya Ulyanov was born.





His father, Nikolai Vasilievich, a peasant of the landowner Brekhov of the Nizhny Novgorod province, having not received his freedom, fled to the Astrakhan province in 1791 . In 1797, he was freed under conditions of compulsory residence in the region. Since that time, Nikolai Vasilievich begins to master tailoring craft, enters the tailors workshop.

At five, Ilya lost his father. The whole burden of care fell on the elder brother of Ilya Vasily, who remained the only breadwinner in the family.

And yet, the absence of his father was not a disaster for the boy, since Vasily fully replaced his parent. From an early age, Ilya Ulyanov proved to be a capable student. Having made an exception, he is admitted to the Astrakhan men's gymnasium, which he graduates in 1850, becoming the first gymnasium student in the history of the educational institution to receive a silver medal.

Student years

Ilya Ulyanov, whose biography began with difficult events and facts (the absence of a breadwinner, a large family), still did not abandon his desire for knowledge.

In 1850, he entered Kazan University at the Faculty of Physics and Mathematics. The young man was very lucky: the educational institution was headed by the outstanding scientist N. I. Lobachevsky, who was distinguished by progressive views on pedagogy, science and society. Thanks to him, the views of young Ilya Nikolaevich were formed.

As a student, the young man studied at the meteorological and astronomical observatory. This contributed to the fact that Ulyanov I. In 1854, he graduated from the university.

The beginning of pedagogical activity

In mid-1855, a young scientist was appointed teacher of mathematics and physics at the Penza Nobility Institute.





Here Ulyanov continues meteorological observations by order of his teacher N. I. Lobachevsky.

In fact, Penza for I. N. Ulyanov was the beginning of his independent activity in pedagogy, science, society. Here Ulyanov Ilya proved himself to be a highly qualified teacher and educator. He owns a number of educational initiatives.

At the same time, the work gave him managerial skills, which were developed in subsequent years.





In Penza, Ulyanov I.N. meets Maria Alexandrovna Blank, who becomes his wife, who later gave him six children.





In 1863 they moved to Nizhny Novgorod, where the head of the family received the post of senior teacher of mathematics and physics in the male gymnasium. At the same time, he is conducting teaching and educational work in other educational institutions. At the same time, he takes a creative approach to the educational process. Gradually, he formed his own pedagogical system and views on education.

Ulyanov's activities in the field of public education

In 1869, Ilya Ulyanov was appointed the inspector of public schools of the Simbirsk province by the Ministry of Public Education , and after 5 years - the director of public schools. The latter appointment has expanded the capabilities of an innovative teacher.

Director Ulyanov first of all personally got acquainted with the state of schools. It was deplorable: out of 421 schools, only 89 worked, and more than a third of the teachers were not professionals, they were replaced by parish priests. Zemstvo power was demonstratively inactive.

Energetic and selfless I.N. Ulyanov was able to win over the progressive circles of the province. Soon, the Simbirsk province was among the best in the field of public education.

Achievements of I. N. Ulyanov in the field of public education

Ilya Ulyanov, whose achievements in the field of public education arouse deep respect for him among all progressive people of the past and present, carried out gigantic work to improve the education system in Russia.

Under his leadership, in 1872, the Poretsky Teachers' Seminary was opened, which prepared a whole galaxy of Ulyanovsk teachers. Professional teachers came to schools.

In the Middle Volga region, for the first time, a whole network of schools was created for Mordovian, Chuvash and Tatar children. Moreover, training was conducted in their native language.

In the province, the number of educational institutions has increased. Only the number of Chuvash schools was brought to thirty-eight. There are more than two hundred new buildings for educational institutions.

Archives confirm that Ilya Nikolayevich donated personal funds to new schools, the publication of textbooks.

In 1823, for failure to pay taxes and other duties, he falls into the Vedomosti on the Astrakhan bourgeoisie, but already under the name Ulyanov. From this time on, he is always referred to as Ulyanov.

Finally

January 24, 1886 Ulyanov Ilya Nikolaevich died suddenly, whose biography is filled with noble deeds in the name of public education. His memory is immortalized by a bust in Ulyanovsk.





Years will pass, but the contribution of the great enlightener of the nineteenth century I. N. Ulyanov will remain an enduring value for Russia.
