DIY Pareo is the perfect complement to your beach wardrobe

If you want to look irresistible on the beach, then you definitely need to take care of a beautiful pareo that would look perfect with a bathing suit on the beach podium. This is a great addition to a swimsuit. It is light, not constraining movements. That is why many fashionistas choose it when going to the beach.

Pareo, sewn with your own hands, will make you irresistible and unique. With it, you can easily emphasize the beautiful lines of the figure or hide flaws.

Process of creation

To sew pareos with your own hands, you will need a piece of fabric (about a meter and a half), threads, a sewing machine and, of course, a good mood!

This will not be difficult, since this thing is a rectangular or square piece of fabric. You can choose both airy chiffon and soft flowing silk. It is important to determine the size of the product so that you can make the most of its capabilities (tie like a dress, skirt, pants, etc.). Then you need to process the edges of the fabric on a sewing machine, and the beach outfit will be ready.





To make the pareo with your own hands even more interesting, you can glue rhinestones along the contour of the pattern (for example, a picture of a rose or a heart) or you can draw batik patterns on a plain fabric with your own hands, which will perfectly complement the beach outfit.

How to tie it beautifully?

How to sew pareo with your own hands, we have already figured out. As you can see, this is easy. Now we describe how to tie a pareo to the beach. There are many options.

It is always necessary to take into account the peculiarities of the figure before choosing one of the methods of tying a beach scarf. So, for women who want to hide their lush hips or a small tummy, it is better to tie it at the waistline. Girls who want to visually increase their height, it is better to use a scarf as a long skirt on the floor. Small breasts can be hidden under light tissue tied in a knot on the chest.

There are many interesting options for making a pareo. The photos below will help you decide on a particular method.





You can easily tie a beautiful top. For a top with an open back, you need to tie the two ends around the neck, stretch them down the waist, then tie with the other two ends of the pareo on the hips.

A great option - pants made of pareo. They can be easily made of two pareos, preferably identical in color. The first must be tied on the hips, the ends are tied around the ankles, the second in the same way is tied around the hips and fixed on the second ankle.

A regular rectangle of fabric can help out in a situation if you do not have a swimsuit on hand. On the neck it is necessary to tie the two ends of the pareo, then stretch them between the legs and, enveloping the buttocks with fabric, tie a beautiful knot on the front waist.





As you can see, pareo is an ideal assistant in creating a charming beach look for all occasions. Properly tied pareo will make your figure irresistible, and hand-sewn will be the most striking element in your summer wardrobe!
