The recreation center "Golden Beach" (Peresyp) - description, infrastructure, reviews of tourists

On the Azov coast of the Krasnodar Territory there are a lot of beautiful resort places where you can relax well and fully. The mild climate of the Sea of ​​Azov, the gentle beach and warm water create ideal conditions for tourists.

Rest in Peresyp

Peresyp - a resort on the Sea of ​​Azov in southern Russia. On two sides Peresyp is surrounded by water: on one side the sea, on the other - the waters of the Gulf of Akhtanizovsky. This unusual area is very attractive for tourists. The embankment is located on a narrow sand strip, the landscape is unique and very interesting.

The resort is far from city noise and fussy roads. Perfect for a quiet, inexpensive vacation with children, for lovers of peace and unity with nature.

There are no noisy night discos, expensive restaurants and attractions. The main attraction of the village - clean soft shallow beaches and sea surface.

Holiday Village "Golden Beach"

Rash is today considered a developing resort. The main accommodation offered is private guest houses, recreation centers and mini-pensions. Every year, the housing stock of the village is expanding, improving and creating comfortable conditions for tourists.

The entertainment complex “Golden Beach” (Peresyp) is one of the largest resort establishments in the village. This recreation center is located on the very shore of the sea. On the territory there is a five-story hotel that resembles an old fairytale castle. The hotel has 120 rooms. Cozy standard and deluxe rooms are waiting for their customers.





The complex is built in the form of a large amusement park right on the beach. The beach in the base, as in the whole Peresyp, is sandy, the bottom is shallow, the sea is shallow and warms up very well. "Golden Beach" (Peresyp) has its own rescue station, a medical center.

For lovers of camping on the territory of the "Golden Beach" there is a car camping.

A tourist complex feature is a font filled with healing mud from a volcano. Healing mud containing boron, iodine, bromine and calcium have a healing effect on the human body. The so-called Greek mud baths are very popular among tourists recreation.

Entertainment at the recreation center "Golden Beach"

For thrill-seekers on the territory of the recreation center "Golden Beach" (Peresyp) there are water attractions - several steep inflatable slides. Extreme lovers can ride the sea on a banana, a tablet, fly into the air with a parachute and go water skiing.





A great opportunity to entertain yourself is to undergo wine spa procedures that will help cleanse the skin, improve the functioning of internal organs and increase vitality. During the procedures, the body is saturated with minerals, immunity increases. In addition to wine baths, Golden Beach (Peresyp) will offer you to visit a tasting of products of the Panageya trademark, which specializes in the production of wines from grapes grown on Taman land.





On the territory of the complex "Golden Beach" (Peresyp) there is a cozy cafe where you can order a comprehensive lunch, drink coffee or eat ice cream or barbecue. Refinement to the “Golden Beach” is given by professional musicians who will perform familiar tunes and give you an unforgettable vacation.

Guest reviews

Tourists in their reviews rather flattering talk about the recreation center "Golden Beach" (Peresyp). The reviews are mostly positive. Vacationers note the good work of the staff of the entire complex. The beach itself is a highlight of the recreation center. It is wide, clean and very comfortable.

The lack of vacationers consider the lack of a swimming pool in the resort complex.

From the reviews of vacationers, we can conclude that the recreation center "Golden Beach" is worthy of attention, everyone will find entertainment to their liking.
