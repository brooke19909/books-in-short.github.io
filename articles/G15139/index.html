Do-it-yourself cute angel made of cotton pads

Jewelry in the form of angels is very cute. They are ideal for interior decoration, gifts, cards for the New Year, Christmas, Easter and many other holidays. A very convenient material for creating angels are cotton pads. Try to attract a child to the creative activities, this will be a fun and useful joint pastime. Take a look at a few workshops and learn how to make an angel from cotton pads with your own hands.

What do you need to create a little crafts "Angel"?

Such decoration will be a wonderful addition to a bedroom for girls or a holiday card. To make an angel with your own hands from cotton pads, you need to prepare the following materials:

  • packing cotton pads;
  • a spool of white cotton thread;
  • glue that quickly hardens;
  • rhinestones;
  • decorative threads;
  • scissors.

The necessary materials will be found in the house of each mistress.





Master class on creating an angel from cotton pads

The decoration will appeal not only to households, but also to guests. An angel from cotton pads will turn out beautiful: making it yourself is not difficult with your own hands, if you follow the detailed instructions:

  1. Remove the outer layer from both sides of the cotton pad. Roll the inner layer of cotton into a small ball and wrap it in one of the removed upper layers of the disc. Fasten with white thread.
  2. Straighten the edges and cut with scissors in a wave-like fashion.
  3. Having bent the other part of the disk, glue the two corners of the semicircle with glue.
  4. Glue the wings to the body of the angel.
  5. At the last stage, we proceed to decorate the crafts. It all depends on your imagination. Rhinestones, silver and gold threads, sequins, sparkles - everything will be appropriate. But the obligatory attribute is, of course, the halo. It can be made from interweaving silver or gold threads or by curving a thin wire with a ringlet.

If such toys are planned to be used to decorate the interior or Christmas tree, then such crafts should be made with a special loop. Do-it-yourself angels from cotton pads can then be hung on any household item.

Christmas tree toy "Angel"

Different can be angels from cotton pads. With your own hands, you can make them step by step in just one hour. This will require the following tools:

  • snow-white cardboard;
  • packing cotton pads;
  • yarn of wool brought together yellow and white;
  • plastic bead;
  • thin aluminum wire;
  • pencils, scissors, superglue;
  • silver nail polish.

If the components are not enough, you can buy them at the nearest store.





Step-by-step instruction:

  1. First you need to make the basis for the torso. On a sheet of white cardboard we draw a semicircle of the required size, cut out and glue it so that a cone forms.
  2. On the resulting frame in rows we glue cotton pads, starting from the bottom. Before the procedure, they should be divided into two thinner halves. Each subsequent row is overlapped in relation to the previous row. In this way, you need to glue the entire body to the very top.
  3. In order to make a head, you need to grease the bead with glue and wrap it tightly with white threads of wool. You will get a small ball.
  4. Angel needs to attach a hairstyle. Woolen threads of light yellow color are a little ‚Äútousled‚Äù. And with glue we fix the angel on the head.
  5. For the manufacture of wings, you also need a cardboard base. We cover them, like the body, with cotton wool disks: in the direction from the periphery to the center. Pre-tear the discs into pieces, simulating feathers.
  6. From two cotton pads we form cones and fix them with glue. These will be the sleeves of the angel, which should be trimmed with scissors.

After all the details have dried, you can collect an angel. First, glue the head, wings, arms to the body. We form a nimbus ring from the wire and fix it above the head. Do-it-yourself angel made of cotton pads can be decorated with beads and sparkles.

Another option

For its manufacture will need the following materials:

  • boxes with cotton pads;
  • beads, rhinestones, beads;
  • white cotton threads;
  • glue gun;
  • scissors.




Preparation method:

  1. From one cotton pad to make two thinner, dividing it along the fibers. Insert a bead or a ball of any light material into one of the halves and tie it with a white thread, forming the head of an angel and wings.
  2. Cut the other part of the cotton pad and collapse the sleeves of the white robe of the angel. Fix them with glue.
  3. Just like the first cotton pad, we share the second. From these two circles we add two triangles. It will be the robe of an angel.
  4. From the inside of one triangle we fix with glue the sleeves of an angel hoodie. From above we fasten the head with wings.
  5. Using glue we connect the two halves of the body.
  6. The final touch is decorative ornaments of an angel. From a golden thread you turn a halo, from beads, sequins and rhinestones make a shining robe.

Easter decoration

For such an angel you will need tools:

  • one cotton pad.
  • white silk threads.
  • a small bead.
  • zigzag scissors.
  • toothpicks.
  • Super glue.
  • gold thread.




Performance:

  1. Divide the disk into two layers.
  2. Tie a bead in one piece.
  3. Use zigzag scissors to make denticles along the edge of the disc.
  4. Bend the other part of the disk in half.
  5. Take one and the other edge and wrap up to half the distance without reaching the center, and then wrap in the center.
  6. Lubricate part of the toothpick with glue and fix it inside the resulting package. This will be the torso.
  7. Glue the torso and wings together with glue.
  8. We twist nimbus and jewelry with gold threads. DIY angel from cotton pads is ready!

The workshops use very simple and accessible materials, and the lessons are interesting and uncomplicated. Children and parents will enjoy the joint production of these original angels from ordinary cotton cosmetic discs.
