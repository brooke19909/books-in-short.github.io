Samodelkin's lessons: how to make a fortuneteller from paper

If you are bored, there is nothing special to do, but you want to occupy yourself with something interesting, try to tell fortunes. At the same time, you will find out what tomorrow prepares for you. How? We’ll talk about this now!

Fortune Teller



Read carefully about how to make a fortune teller from paper. Take a regular album sheet. It is rectangular in shape, and you have to turn it into a square. To do this, measure the length of the short side with a ruler, and set aside the same number of centimeters on the larger one. Do this from the top and bottom edges of the sheet. Connect the marked points with a line. Cut off the excess strip. So, the first stage of learning how to make a fortuneteller out of paper, you have passed, proceed to the second. Fold all 4 corners of the square inward so that they connect in the middle of the sheet. What did you do? Yes, also a square, only smaller. Rotate the paper with the smooth side and repeat the corners. The second stage is over. The third important point on how to make a fortuneteller out of paper: expand the sheet and arrange the numbers on the inner sides, starting from 1 and ending with 8. And on the outside write the transcripts of what each number can mean. Not necessarily serious, you can playful: you are going to have fun! Fold your figure again, put it on the thumb and forefinger of both hands, ask questions of interest to you, choose numbers and read out the answers! Well, figured out how to make a fortuneteller out of paper in this way? Then read on!

Fortune telling on figures



Another original method of studying the future looks like this: take a dozen small leaves. On each draw one image: the silhouette of a female, male, bird, cat, as well as a tree and a flower. And inanimate objects: a fence, a house, a door, a river. You can colorize, but this is not necessary. Such a fortuneteller made of paper with his own hands is quite effective in black and white. The main thing is that from the side of the “shirt” the pattern does not appear through. Now the interpretation of the images:

  • The symbol "woman" indicates high demands on oneself, people, deeds, the fulfillment of desires and life in general. Lower the bar a little, then your aspirations will come true. Just beware of false and deceitful people who, alas, are in your close circle.
  • The symbol "man" is more positive. He points to reliable protection in personal and public affairs. And your desires will come true, however, with the help of outsiders.
  • But the drawing “bird” is a symbol of ephemeral hopes and Manilov’s dreams. And if he fell out during fortune-telling, fantasize as much as you like, but do not invent anything concrete, there will be no sense! However, with great perseverance, something can happen.
  • The drawing “home” indicates the stability and monotony of your daily life, which will last for some time. Improve your life, strengthen family, family ties - here you will be lucky.
  • The image of a “tree” is a difficult financial question; it will loom in the near future. If your desire is not connected with money, then fortunetelling gives the go-ahead.
  • If you have been tortured by all sorts of difficulties and troubles, and you want to know when they will be completed, and the “fence” pattern has appeared, calm down, problems will soon be resolved in a way convenient for you. Just call for help from your comrades, then things will go smoothly.
  • Figure "cat": this animal is always independent, walks by itself, and therefore do not rely too much on the execution of the conjecture. But you personally obviously do not occupy the strength of will and character. This is also good!
  • Did you get the “flower-seven-flower” picture? A good sign that you will be lucky soon. Although you are not safe from intrigues and changes in your personal life. Therefore, the fortuneteller of paper, the scheme of which is considered here, warns you to be careful.
  • The drawing “river” indicates a possible long road: a trip, your trip or the arrival of relatives. And if you are guessing at a desire, believe: it will be fulfilled, only you should try.
  • The picture “door” has fallen - that means, get ready for new active deeds, and the desire will take on real forms, just show enterprise!

How does all this work: put the leaflet-drawings in a bag, make a wish or formulate a question quite clearly and pull out the picture. Treat it and do not get too upset if something does not meet your expectation!
