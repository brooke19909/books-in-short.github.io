Beach Holidays: Entertainment & Amenity Information





What in terms of water entertainment can the hotel complex Richmond Ephesus Resort 5 * offer? Kusadasi is a resort famous for its luxurious beaches and clear waters. The hotel, by the way, has its own stretch of coast, which is only 50 meters away - the hotel courtyard overlooks almost the sea.

The beach is covered with soft fine sand, and the entrance to the sea is very convenient. Depth increases gradually, so children feel quite comfortable here. The hotel offers loungers with comfortable mattresses, as well as umbrellas that protect from the sun. You can ask for a large beach towel in the main building.

Of course, there are plenty of entertainment for travelers. There are boats and catamarans, boats and jet skis, the rent of which does not cost so much. Guests can enjoy boat or water skiing at an extra charge. You will always have the opportunity to try parasailing or take some lessons from experienced windsurfers. Near the coast there is a diving school where you can rent equipment or take a quick training course and get the appropriate certificate.
