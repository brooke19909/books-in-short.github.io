Ski base "Polazna" - an active recreation center

Ski base "Polazna" is located on the banks of the Kama River near Perm. The active recreation center on the territory of the reserve "Lunezh mountains" was opened in 2004. The ski base "Polazna" works all year round.





Accommodation options

The recreation center provides accommodation in the buildings of the bath complex, wooden houses and tents. We will tell you more about them:

  • The rooms of the bath complex are well-maintained. They are well heated. Here you can find everything you need for a comfortable stay. The rooms are designed for 2, 4, 6 people.
  • Wooden houses. They can accommodate seven to eight people at a time. The rooms have a cozy atmosphere and pleasant interior. Those who wish can fry the barbecue on the grill.
  • Tent Camp Ideal for lovers of romance. Here you can order a single or double tent.

The price for accommodation varies: on weekends the cost is slightly higher.

Winter fun

Ski base "Polazna" has its own ski slopes, which have a different level of difficulty. There are tracks for snowmobiling, flat skiing and snow tubing. The snowboard park is equipped according to modern requirements and standards. The ski season in the recreation center is extended by a system of snow cannons. In the evening, the tracks are well lit. Music is heard all around their perimeter. A ski rental service is available for tourists.





Summer fun

In summer, guests of the recreation center can make a fascinating rafting on the Kama River in a boat, rent a catamaran or an ATV. There is also a specially equipped area for playing paintball and sports. Those who wish can go fishing or sightseeing in the picturesque surroundings. In summer, they organize extensive exciting programs for guests that are designed for family or corporate vacations.

Children's entertainment

The complex has a playground. There is also a cable car for kids. Guys can shoot at the shooting range or play paintball. There is also a sports school for the Olympic reserve, where skiing and snowboarding trainings are held.

other services

Guests of the ski resort can use the cafe, tavern and bar, rent a summer veranda, and take a steam bath in the Russian bath. On the territory of the base you can organize a festive event, hold a wedding, corporate party or graduation for schoolchildren.

For tourists there is a rental of sporting equipment, travel backpacks, safety vests, alloy boats , catamarans, scooters and ATVs. At the request of the guest, a guided tour can be organized. In the surrounding areas there is a parking lot of primitive man and the "stone city".





Security

Ski base "Polazna" has its own medical center, where qualified doctors work. On the tracks for help, you can contact an experienced instructor. And the professionals accompanying excursions and rafting have the necessary skills in providing first aid. For cars in the territory there is a spacious guarded parking lot.

How to get there

The city where "Polazna" is located is Perm. The ski resort is located at a distance of 45 km from the city. This is a very beautiful place that Perm Region can be proud of . In the Dobryansky district, pos. Konstantinovka is located the ski base "Polazna". How to get from the city? For these purposes, a fixed-route taxi, regular bus or own transport is suitable.
