How to make fluffy slime: the best recipes

Fluffy slime (from the English fluffy slime - fluffy mucus) is called an elastic material that can deform from light pressure. The popularity of the jelly-shaped toy, popularly referred to as the lizun, mainly ensures its extensibility and "obedience". So, colliding with the wall, the slime takes a flat shape, starting from it - spherical.





We present to your attention a selection of fluffy slime recipes. The list of tools necessary for its preparation is as follows:

  • a container made of plastic or glass, where the components will be mixed;
  • a spatula or wooden stick that is needed to mix food;
  • gouache or food coloring to give the original color to the slime;
  • plastic bag.

Classic recipe for making a fluffy slime

Having prepared the work surface and materials such as polyvinyl alcohol, sodium boric acid and water, proceed to create a jelly-like substance:

  1. Pour into a container, the surface of which is enameled, part of a pack of polyvinyl alcohol.
  2. Add water, gently stirring with a stick.
  3. Put the water-powder mixture on a slow fire. Future lizun should boil for 45 minutes. To avoid burning, mix the solution regularly.
  4. In a separate container filled with clean warm water, add two tablespoons of sodium boric acid. After crystals appear, strain the mixture.
  5. After cooling the solution of powder and water, add to it a filtered mixture in a ratio of 3: 1. Add food coloring.

In the eyes, the solution will acquire a jelly-like consistency resembling mucus. As you can see, making a fluffy slime without glue is a quick and easy process.





But what if there is no polyvinyl alcohol at hand? The following recipe will tell you how to make a fluffy slice based on PVA glue.

Making a glue toy

The main difficulty that needlewomen encounter when preparing glue at home based on glue is proportions. Therefore, be careful:

  1. Combine a quarter cup of water with a similar volume of PVA glue. Mix well.
  2. Mix in a solution one tablespoon of sodium borate powder, previously dissolved in water. Add dye.
  3. As an analogue of powdered sodium borate, you can use a 4% solution of sodium tetraborate, in which you do not need to add water.

That's all! The slime is ready to serve you for two weeks.

Klee-starch slime

There are many variations of making a funny toy. However, not everyone can find the necessary ingredients at home. So, how to make a fluffy slime without unnecessary trips to the pharmacy? The simplest recipe without the use of sodium tetraborate / borate:

  1. Dilute starch with water by mixing it with a small amount of PVA glue.
  2. Pour one third of the glass with liquid starch into a plastic bag, adding a few drops of dye.
  3. Pour ¬º of a glass of thick PVA glue into starch.
  4. Stir the solution thoroughly until an elastic lump forms.
  5. After removing the lizun from the bag, allow the liquid to drain.

Environmentally friendly option

How to make a fluffy slime safe for a small child? A plastic toy for children over the age of 3 years is prepared according to the following principle:

  1. In 150 ml of heated water, add 20 g of shampoo, mix.
  2. Stirring the solution, introduce flour into it until the consistency of the mixture resembles an elastic dough.
  3. At the stage of adding flour, color the future slime with gouache or food coloring.
  4. After cooling the dough in the refrigerator, rinse the slice with cold water.

Now you know how to make a fluffy slime without fear for the health of the child.





Recently, the slime is rapidly gaining the love of not only babies, but also their parents. Perhaps the main advantage of the jelly-shaped toy is its accessibility: you can find a fluffy slime in the nearest children's store, or you can cook it yourself!
