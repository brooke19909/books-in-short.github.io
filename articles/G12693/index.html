The best mini-hotels of St. Petersburg: description

As soon as they do not call St. Petersburg. The city plays the role of the cultural capital of Russia. It is compared with Venice. He is rightfully considered the most ambitious brainchild of Peter.

The population of the modern metropolis will very soon reach 5,000,000 inhabitants. The city has a huge number of museums, architectural monuments, art galleries, palaces and theaters.

Wandering wind

And in St. Petersburg, there are about seventy rivers and forty islands that form numerous canals and tributaries. Almost six hundred bridges were thrown across their channels. Twenty crossings are bred daily.

Most often, they arrive in the metropolis from Moscow. Travelers are accepted by the Pulkovo International Airport, railway stations and bus stations. Guests are accommodated in mini-hotels of St. Petersburg.

The fastest and most convenient way to get from the Russian capital to St. Petersburg is to take the Sapsan high-speed train. Ticket prices vary. The minimum cost is 1,000 rubles. On the day of departure, the fare can increase up to 8,000.

Trips to St. Petersburg are popular not only among travelers in the central regions of Russia, but also among tourists from the northern regions of our country. The city is well known and loved by the residents of Murmansk, Arkhangelsk and Tyumen.

The hotel is recognized as the best mini-hotel in St. Petersburg. Located just ten minutes leisurely walk from Nevsky Prospekt. It offers visitors - comfortable rooms, a lobby, a hall for meetings and conferences, a sauna. The price includes unlimited internet connection and delicious breakfast.
