Firstly, one can not fail to note the favorable location of Karon View Resort 2 and its proximity to all interesting places for tourists with the diverse preferences that the city has to offer. Description of rooms. All rooms and bungalows at this compact hotel have all the essentials.

modern amenities to ensure that travelers stay as comfortable as possible. They are equipped with central air conditioning, linen is changed once a week, the floors in the rooms sparkle with polished parquet. Each room has a paid minibar. A safe is provided for storing documents or valuables. Guestrooms have pay Internet and telephones.

The rooms are cleaned daily, each of them has a shower, washbasin and toilet. The standard rooms here are called Superior. Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel.

Beach. The Karon View Resort 2 hotel is located within walking distance of a two-kilometer long sandy beach with umbrellas, sunbeds and mattresses. Towels are available for a fee.
