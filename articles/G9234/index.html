Will Russian smartphones conquer the world?

Russian technologies in the field of communications do not stand still. Moreover, they ambitiously declare themselves in the world market. Even the leaders got around

What is so hooked in this device? Is the Russian smartphone with two screens loved only for the design? First things first. Perhaps no one will deny that Russian developers in the field of communications have always had interesting thoughts and ideas. Lots of useful features

Russian smartphones from YotaPhone are able to carry out their work on the basis of Android (Jelly Bean 4.2). The processor is "charged" with 2 GB of RAM. However, the built-in memory did not disappoint. It is no worse than that of similar instances - 32/64 GB. Among other things, the "beginner" was equipped with a pair of cameras and the ability to support LTE, 3G and GSM standards. By the way, with all this, Russian smartphones organically combine an e-reader at the same time. The screen, located on the back of the device, using the "electronic ink" system, is able to work continuously. In this case, the display consumes a minimum of energy. Due to this, the battery of the mobile device lasts a very long time. As already mentioned, the design was pleasantly struck. It looks original, functional and at the same time quite compact. The public is skeptical of Russian creation. He is perceived more as a kind of momentary fashion trend, which will soon pass, giving way to the real sharks of the mobile world - Nokia, Samsung and Iphone. However, the inclinations of the Russian mobile device are good, so let's not rush to conclusions. Perhaps this device will be able to make a technological leap and become a trendsetter in the world of smart phones. But will it be so - time will tell.
