Norway hotels: overview, description, reviews

Homeland of the fjords, northern lights and ice. A country of harsh landscapes and ski resorts. It is, of course, about Norway. Here, real heroes are born who are not afraid of any weather conditions.

If you are a lover of bright emotions and you are interested in an exotic vacation, then you should pay attention to a variety of hotels in Norway. The number of options is impressive, you can stay both in populated areas and in uninhabited places in the mountains.

Exotic vacation

An unusual hotel in Norway, a tree hotel, Tree Hotel, provides an excellent opportunity to fulfill the childhood dream of a tree house. The owners are the Lindwall couple, who were inspired to create such a hotel after watching the movie. In the future, it is planned to build another twenty houses, but so far there are five. They are made in completely different styles, which makes the whole hotel very individual and interesting.

The rooms are equipped with everything necessary for life, you can stay in them as one person or a couple. The cost of living for one day is 129 euros per person and 179 euros for two.

The owners of the hotel all year round provide tourists with a variety of entertainment, in the summer they entertain tourists with horseback riding, fishing, and in winter with snow-sledding and skiing.

Basecamp ship in the ice

Basecamp Ship in the Ice - the hotel is located right in the ship. In summer, the schooner provides services for walking in nearby waters, and during frosts she is left right at one of the fjords, where she freezes and from that moment the hotel is considered open. On the territory of the ship are 10 rooms with all necessary conditions. You can get to the hotel by purchasing a tour from Basecamp. In winter, you can ride a dog sled and enjoy the northern lights.
