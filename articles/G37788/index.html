Infrastructure of the future complex

According to the ideas of Spanish architects, the Spanish Quarter should become a separate small town. Customer reviews emphasize that LCD infrastructure will be rich and diverse. In total, it is planned to build 40 houses in height from 6 to 15 floors. They will be located in small blocks with cozy courtyards. A pedestrian boulevard will be built between the blocks, on both sides of which cafes, restaurants, various offices and shops will open. For children in the "Spanish Quarters" it is planned to build two kindergartens and a school for 1200 places, and the distance from them to all buildings will be approximately the same. For lovers of outdoor activities in the complex will build a fitness club, swimming pool, ice rink, jogging and cycling paths, tennis court, multiple sports grounds.

The infrastructure will also include shops, a clinic, an entertainment center.

The design of the territory of the residential complex ‚ÄúSpanish Quarters‚Äù is planned to be made beautiful and enjoyable for residents. Here trees, flowering shrubs will be planted, palisades and flowerbeds will be smashed, benches, small architectural forms will be set up, and colorful playgrounds will be created for kids.

Many interest holders are interested in parking. They are going to make it underground in several levels. It is planned that 2500 cars will be found here. In addition, business class houses will also have small underground parking lots.

Ground parking in the complex is not provided.

Design

Architects have proposed building in a complex of 40 buildings of average number of storeys, at least 6, at most 15 floors. All houses and apartments in the Spanish Quarters will be of two classes - ‚Äúbusiness‚Äù and ‚Äúcomfort‚Äù. The difference is the internal layout, the height of the ceilings, the price, the design of the MOS finishes. In addition, underground parking is provided in business class homes. In buildings of any class, it is planned to make spacious halls with a height of 4.5 meters and a concierge room, install elevators (OTIS company), one each in six-story buildings and two (passenger and cargo) in fifteen-story buildings. All buildings are erected in a monolithic way, but the bearing walls in them will not be supports. Pylons will take on this role, which will make it possible to create individual layouts in the apartment. Only kitchens, baths and bathrooms (places where water is supplied) will be delivered ready-made, that is, without subsequent redevelopment. The external walls are planned to be installed in cellular concrete, which is distinguished by good vapor permeability, high thermal insulation, and ease of installation. Unfortunately, one of the disadvantages of such a material is its high sound permeability and moisture capacity, but builders will cover these blocks with porcelain stoneware, clinker tiles, textured plaster.
