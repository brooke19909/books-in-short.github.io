Rest in Bashkiria: recreation centers "Mirror Carp", "Yamashla" and others. A brief description of the services

A well-spent vacation is the key to success. There is a misconception that the best resorts of the modern world are in Europe and on exotic islands, but this is far from the case. Fans of skiing, fishing and other types of outdoor activities should choose a vacation in Bashkiria. Recreation centers accept tourists all year round, providing quality services and comfortable conditions that can please even the most demanding vacationers.

Holiday Village "Mirror Carp"

The base is located in a picturesque corner of nature near the capital of the republic of the city of Ufa, only 40 km. The number of rooms of the camp site allows you to relax with a company of up to 20 people. For the convenience of guests on the territory are located both small houses with a capacity of up to 4 people, and cottages for a large company. Having visited these places, you can fully appreciate the rest in Bashkiria. Recreation facilities, in particular, Mirror Carp, take care of their reputation, trying to meet modern requirements.



There are 10 small houses on the territory of the holiday home, each equipped with a small bathhouse and a bathroom. This accommodation is ideal for families. For a larger number of guests, there are 2 cottages with 16 beds each (additionally equipped with their own banquet rooms) and one for 20 (equipped with a separate kitchen, karaoke and a billiard room).

The recreation center "Yamashla"

When choosing a vacation in Bashkiria (recreation centers are designed for different consumers), you should pay attention to the Yamashlinskoe reservoir. On its shores is a tourist complex "Yamashla", surrounded by beautiful nature. Not far from the base are the most important attractions of the resort.

"Yamashla" combines such a variety of options for relaxation and resettlement of guests that will appeal to any visitor. For lovers of comfortable living on the territory of the base there is a hotel, in the room stock of which there are both “deluxe”, “junior suite” and “economy” rooms, as well as rooms of the “hostel” type for 6-10 people. The guests, who belong to the adherents of wild rest, can settle down on the territory of the tent camp, equipped with comfortable army tents for 8 people, or set your own, any capacity. “Yamashla” can easily lead the rating “Best recreation centers in Bashkiria”.



In the summer, the administration rents all the necessary equipment for active pastime on the water, as well as diving and fishing, in winter you can ride snowmobiles and skiing. Tourism lovers are invited to go along mountain trails and get to the caves, enjoying the surrounding nature.

The recreation center "Ural-Tau"

The recreation center "Ural-Tau" is located near the resort of Abazkovo, the most popular in Bashkortostan. Relatively small, but at the same time a cozy resting place. Prices are more than acceptable, which attracts many tourists every year.

Accommodation is possible both in the main building of the camp site, which has 11 rooms, and in a separate guest house, where guests will be offered improved living conditions. Also, for convenience, there is a cafe on the territory where you can agree on a full 3 meals a day, and even use the vegetarian menu. It is worth noting that not all recreation centers in Bashkiria provide such services. Reviews about Ural-Tau are only positive. Many visitors note improved living conditions, a large list of excursions, year-round operation.



Among the entertainment, “Ural-Tau” offers active types of winter recreation, including snowboarding and skiing, and then you can take a steam bath in a Russian bath. In summer, horseback riding and a rich excursion program are available.

The recreation center "Three gudgeons"

The base "Three gudgeons" is located in the forest on the banks of the Pavlovsk reservoir, has all the necessary attributes for a comfortable rest for its visitors. Guest accommodation is an enviable variety. For living, the company built a cottage "Youth", which has in its arsenal several 3- and 4-bed rooms. And for those who come to spend time with their family, houses with the same name - "Family" will do. Also, for a large company provides a "luxury", which can accommodate up to 14 people in complete comfort.

Among the activities in the summer, guests can enjoy shooting at dashes, fishing, water skiing and motorbiking, as well as sports games.

In winter, guests can enjoy ice fishing, skiing, snowmobiling and snowboarding, take a steam bath in a Russian bath, or spend time in a cozy local cafe with excellent cuisine.



Each tourist will be amazed by the variety of vacations in Bashkiria. Recreation facilities provide entertainment for every taste, so you will not be bored here. Come here on vacation and you won’t regret it!
