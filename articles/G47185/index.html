Hotel AA Grand Oasis Resort 4 * (Egypt, Sharm El Sheikh): photos and reviews of tourists

Sharm el Sheikh is an amazing resort city. This pearl of Egypt attracts travelers from all over the world. Tourists will appreciate the benefits of relaxing in this magnificent place. Among the many local hotels that welcome guests, AA Grand Oasis Resort 4 * offers its services. Based on the reviews left by people who were vacationing at this hotel, we can conclude that the guests here are waiting for a pleasant and exciting pastime.

AA Grand Oasis Resort 4 *: description

In addition to its convenient location, the hotel pleases guests with its architecture. All buildings of the complex are made in the Arabic style. At first glance, the buildings you can feel the harmony in every detail: in the smoothed corners, snow-white domes, majestic arches. The lobby of the hotel looks no less attractive. This place allows you to feel like a guest who arrived at the magnificent palace of the Caliph. The stained glass dome gives a special charm to the interior, thanks to which the room is filled with soft light. In each element, the unsurpassed skill of the architects who created this man-made miracle is felt.



Hotel AA Grand Oasis Resort 4 * (ex Tropicana) was opened in 2002. The last reconstruction was carried out here in 2011, and since then the hotel has pleased its guests with extraordinary beauty, giving a feeling of home comfort.

Where is?

Hotel AA Grand Oasis Resort 4 * is very close to the airport: the distance is only 2 km. From its territory, you can watch the takeoff and landing of aircraft, which insanely pleases children. The old town is also nearby, it can be reached within a few minutes. Those wishing to visit Naama Bay will have to overcome about 10 km. You can get there by minibus or take a taxi.

The hotel is located on the picturesque coast of the Red Sea in a fairly quiet corner. There are excellent conditions for relaxing family people who want to enjoy the peace and beauty of the local area.

Beach vacation

A clean and well-maintained beach is one of the main attractions of the AA Grand Oasis Resort 4 * hotel. Photos of this amazingly beautiful place will appreciate its charm. Guests are very pleased with the clean and warm water of the Red Sea, where you can watch a myriad of colorful fish or see the beauty of coral reefs.

On the beach you can always find free sun loungers and mattresses, which are provided here for free. You can also take umbrellas and towels. Walking along the beach and entering the water is recommended in aquatic baths, as there is a risk of injuring yourself on corals.



For the convenience of vacationers, a pontoon was built, which provides access to considerable depth. Diving enthusiasts will not have to go through the water, bumping into corals.

Near the shore it is very convenient for kids to swim. There are no deep places, and parents may not be puzzled by the safety issues of their children. Moreover, vacationers are always under the watchful eye of lifeguards.
