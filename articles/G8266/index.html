How to remove the protective glass from the iPhone: recommendations

The main function of the protective glass is the preservation of the phone from shock, cracks and scratches. This element takes care of everything. Over time, it becomes unusable because it loses an attractive appearance. Because of this, the level of protection of the device is reduced. To replace it, you must first learn how to remove the protective glass from the iPhone. This procedure is simple. It is enough to follow simple rules.

Why is a replacement required?

Before you familiarize yourself with how to remove the protective glass from the iPhone 6 and 5, you need to familiarize yourself with the reasons for the replacement:

  • The appearance of the phone deteriorates.
  • The protective functions of the device are lost.
  • If you want to change to a new glass.




When the phone has a new protective glass, the device looks neat. There are special accessories for the iPhone that are perfect in size. But you can use universal ones that need to be glued correctly.

Safety precautions

One important nuance must be taken into account before removing the protective glass from the iPhone 5 or 6. Since the surface of the screen is perfectly smooth, the glass is fixed to it securely. It is held not only by glue, but also by electrostatic forces. Two surfaces reliably adjoin, and therefore glass cannot be so easily removed. It must be tucked with a fingernail. If you use another object for this purpose, for example, a knife, that is, the probability of damage to the display.

The sensor is mounted on the body with double-sided tape or glue. Their strength is less compared to the touchscreen and protective glass. Disassembly Features
