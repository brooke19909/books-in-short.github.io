Ras Al Khaimah Resort: Travel Reviews, Features and Attractions

In our article we want to talk about one of the resorts of the UAE. Reviews from tourists about Ras al-Khaimah will help us make up our minds about this wonderful place that our compatriots loved.

A little bit about the resort ...

Ras al-Khaimah is the country's northernmost emirate, located about an hour from Dubai. The resort is incredibly picturesque. On its territory, the mountains come very close to the coast, creating stunning landscapes. The city of Ras al-Khaimah is the capital of the emirate, which the strait divides into two parts connected by a bridge. According to tourists' reviews of Ras al-Khaimah, the city is filled with beautiful attractions, most of which are concentrated in the western part. But in the eastern part there is an international airport. Surprisingly, in this emirate, unlike others, there is no prohibition.



It is worth noting that the tourist guides somehow the city is undeservedly deprived of attention. And by the way, the local nature is incredibly picturesque, and the climate is quite mild, as evidenced by the numerous reviews of tourists. Ras al-Khaimah at first may seem dilapidated and dusty, but it is not so. Resort hotels are able to offer wonderful relaxation and pristine beaches.

How to get to the resort?

According to tourists' reviews of Ras al-Khaimah, getting to the resort is not at all difficult. There is an international airport from which you can quickly get to the place of rest by ordering a transfer in advance. The Emirate connects the new modern highway with other regions, so Dubai can be reached in just an hour. By bus from the city you can reach Sharjah, Dubai, Ajamanu and other places. Taxi services are very developed in the emirate, there is no other type of public transport.

History of the city

The history of the city is quite old. At first, there was a powerful fort called Julfar, mentions of which date back to the year 695. In general, the first settlements in these parts were still three thousand years ago. Archaeologists have found entire settlements that date back to the Iron Age. The history of the region cannot be called calm.



For centuries, pirates raided the land. For this reason, these places on the Persian Gulf coast were called the Pirate Coast. The history of the emirate is inextricably linked with another emirate called Sharjah. Once Ras al-Khaimah was a very prosperous region, and therefore attracted the attention of other countries. Since the year, the emirate came under British rule and became part of the UAE only in 1792.

Climate Ras Al Khaimah

According to tourists' reviews of Ras Al Khaimah (UAE), the resort's climate can be attributed to a tropical view. It is very soft and comfortable. Summer is the hottest time of the year. At this time, the temperature can rise above +35 degrees. But the weather is greatly influenced by the proximity of the mountains, which give coolness. Therefore, the climate of the emirate is more mild. The wonderful weather conditions attract many holidaymakers in Ras Al Khaimah. Reviews of tourists about the resort let me recommend it for a good vacation.

Emirate nature

According to tourists, a vacation in Ras al-Khaimah allows you to fully enjoy the beautiful nature of the emirate. The city itself is located on the coast of the Persian Gulf. And from the east, the Hajjar Mountains approach it, which are famous for its picturesque waterfalls, riverbeds and canyons. Peregrine falcons live in the territory of the emirate, which themselves are a tourist attraction, as they are considered the fastest animals in the world.





Near the city there is a stunning water reserve. Thousands of birds stop on its territory in mangrove forests. Among them, the most popular among tourists are beautiful pink flamingos.

The local waters are home to many small exotic inhabitants. Among them are mackerel, marlin, tuna, sailboat, baracuda and others. It is worth recognizing that the construction of artificial islands negatively affected the ecology of the bay.

Local kitchen

Reviews of tourists about holidays in Ras al-Khaimah are always associated with local cuisine. And she is really unusual here. This is due to the fact that the city is incredibly multinational, which could not but affect the dishes. In Ras al-Khaimah, there are nearby establishments with Arabic cuisine, Japanese, Chinese, Indian, Mexican, Italian and others. The largest and most famous restaurants offer an international menu. Seafood is popular in the city, as in any seaside resort.



As we have already mentioned, the emirate has one important difference from other places in the country. Here they are loyal to alcohol. Moreover, large restaurants offer visitors good wine lists. Tourists are allowed to drink any alcoholic beverages. In local bars you can order not only beer, but also something stronger.

Beaches

For any tourist who goes to a beach resort, one of the most important points is the quality of the beaches. The emirate has not so many hotels and almost all of them have their own beautiful beaches. The water temperature is simply fabulous and attracts any spa person to swim, even if you are not a fan of water procedures. The gentle sea and soft little white sand is a paradise for tourists. Local beaches are not only beautiful. But also very comfortable. They are always spacious and have a lot of space, there is absolutely no feeling of overcrowding. Resort Attractions

If you plan to spend a vacation in the emirates, then you will probably want to see something interesting in an exotic country, as evidenced by the reviews of tourists. You can travel endlessly in the UAE (emirate of Ras al-Khaimah), and still you will certainly want to come back here again to admire the beauties of the country. Nature itself is one of the main attractions of the emirate.



As a family vacation, you should go to the water park "Iceland", which will give a lot of positive emotions. The park is a kind of continuation of the mountains and strikingly fits into the local landscape. The most popular attraction among tourists is an artificial waterfall, whose height is 37 meters. In the park you can also admire a pond with exotic fish, swim in a variety of pools with reefs and ride a roller coaster. According to the spa, this is an amazing adventure that is certainly worth experiencing.

Fort Al Husen houses the National Museum. The ancient fortress was built in the eighteenth century. The complex has survived many historical events and has now lost its direct purpose. Currently, within its walls is an exposition of the museum, represented by archaeological finds and ancient Arabic weapons.

It is no less interesting to visit the museum of Ahmed bin Majid, which contains all kinds of models of Arab boats, pearl catchers equipment, as well as a large pearl collection.



According to tourists' reviews of excursions in Ras al-Khaimah, it can be said that the most popular places to visit are hot springs. They are located in an Arab village, near the foot of the Hajar Mountains. Khatt Springs offers stunning views of the date palm valley. A trip to the hot springs gives all spa guests relaxation and enjoy pleasant spa treatments. The healing waters of mineral springs are filled with useful substances that are beneficial for the body and help in the treatment of certain ailments. It is believed that bathing in hot springs is the best peeling, after which you will feel just refreshed.
