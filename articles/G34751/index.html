Smartphone BlackBerry 9700 review: description, specifications and reviews

BlackBerry phones are traditionally perceived in the community of users and experts as technological, stylish and functional devices. Many of the devices that the brand launched on the market several years ago are quite capable of solving modern user problems by their characteristics. Among them - the BlackBerry Bold 9700 phone, which was released in 2009, but is still in demand. What are its main advantages? What do users say about the experience of using its capabilities?





Phone Features

For starters, let's examine the main characteristics of the BlackBerry 9700 phone in terms of hardware, as well as its main functions. This device has:

- GSM and WCDMA modules, operating within the framework of basic communication standards;

- a display with a diagonal of 2.6 inches in TFT format, with a resolution of 480 by 360 pixels and a color depth of up to 65 thousand shades;

- battery with a capacity of 1.5 thousand mAh;

- case in the size of 109 x 60 x 14 mm;

- Intel XScale processor, operating at a frequency of 624 MHz;

- RAM module with a capacity of 128 MB;

- 256 MB flash memory module;

- slot for connecting additional memory cards in microSD format up to 16 GB;

- a camera with a resolution of 3.2 MP with digital zoom, which allows you to shoot with a resolution of photos in 2048 by 1536 pixels; equipped with a flash, image stabilizer, as well as autofocus;

- GPS navigator with support for the A-GPS option.

The phone runs on the BlackBerry OS operating system in version 5.0, which has a wide range of applications installed - in particular, for editing pictures and documents.





Device features

The main user functions of the BlackBerry 9700 device:

- playback of polyphonic and MP3-tunes;

- text input using T9;

- sending messages, e-mail;

- vibration alert;

- voice dialing;

- support for Java programs;

- recording videos on camera;

- record sound on the recorder.

Display

The phone has a 2.44-inch display. It belongs to the category of TFT displays, has a resolution of 480 by 360 pixels and is capable of displaying 65 thousand colors. According to many users and experts who have compiled a review of the BlackBerry Bold 9700 smartphone, the phone‚Äôs screen is sufficiently technological, it provides a smooth picture with almost imperceptible image pixelation. In principle, despite the fact that the phone is equipped with a rather conservative TFT display, the quality of the image formed on it is comparable to what characterizes modern screens using AMOLED and even SuperAMOLED technology. Among the most notable hardware components of the phone is the backlight, the brightness of which varies depending on the specific level of illumination measured by a special light sensor.
