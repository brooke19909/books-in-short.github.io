Tactile sensations

At first glance, the Alcatel One Touch Star smartphone looks expensive, no different from higher-level devices. The smartphone has all the necessary function keys, on the upper edge is a power button and a screen lock. Connectors for the charger, headset and USB cable are located on the bottom of the device. The back cover of the Alcatel One Touch smartphone is made of cheap plastic, and scratches and dirt may appear on it, although the texture of the device is matte. To remove the cover, you must make every effort, because the mounts securely hold it. There is a battery and a slot for two SIM cards.
