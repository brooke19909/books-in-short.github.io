Hotel Riviera Nessebar 3 * (Nessebar, Bulgaria): overview, description, rooms and reviews

Russia has a long pre-perestroika friendship with Bulgaria. It is easy to guess the reasons for its occurrence: the beauty of nature, many excursions, hospitable people or the warm sea ... Most hotels are noticeably refreshed after renovation, and this is noted by many tourists. Fortunately, while the country has not turned into an expensive tourist destination. So for a budget vacation and an interesting "excursion" you should definitely go to the Sunny Beach resort.

Briefly about Nessebar

One of the oldest European cities, founded more than 5 centuries ago. The resort is located on a rocky peninsula and is conditionally divided into 2 territories. Most hotels, including Riviera Nessebar 3 *, are located in the new part, and the historical center falls on the old one.

Most of the city was subjected to natural disasters - earthquakes. Only ruins of aqueducts, towers, ancient walls and temples remind of former glory. On the old streets of Nessebar you can see the stone bases and protruding wooden bay windows. But it is these architectural ruins that cause great interest among tourists staying at the Riviera Nessebar 3 * hotel (Bulgaria). Nessebar, judging by the reviews, amazes with the magical combination of medieval churches and Renaissance houses. All this has turned the city into a tourist pilgrimage center.

Also, the advantage of the resort is associated with the relatively inexpensive cost of permits. Private 2 * and 3 * hotels will save on accommodation without compromising comfort.

Riviera Nessebar 3 * (Bulgaria / Nessebar)

Sunny Beach is a 7-kilometer beach located north of Burgas. Every year he receives the blue flag for a reason, since the tourist complex is located away from highways, railways and big cities.

It is worth noting that Sunny Beach is the largest Bulgarian resort, on the territory of which more than 800 hotels are built, including the Riviera Nessebar 3 *, so it is very busy here in high seasons.





The tourist complex is ideal for families with children: a shallow bottom and a calm shallow bay, many children's swings, playgrounds, pools and entertainment areas, where discos, competitions and games with prizes are often held. Just 10 km from the resort in 2003, an aquapark opened. In addition, all hotels are built along the coast, so the descent to the sea is carried out without stairs.

Location of hotel Riviera Nessebar 3 * (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria)

Only a few dozen hotels belong to the 3 * category. According to tourists, these include the Riviera Nessebar 3 * hotel, located between Ravda and Nessebar and only 50 meters from the beach. Interestingly, the hotels in Sunny Beach do not have an address (even on official websites), only the location map is indicated.





For a pleasant walk from the Riviera Nessebar to the nearby Sunny Beach, you can rent a bicycle. And 500 meters away is the Nesebr Water Park. The nearest bus stop is one minute walk.

Hotel kitchen

Since tourism brings good profit to the state, they pay special attention to its development. The quality of service, favorable location, interesting "excursion", beautiful flora, comfortable rooms and delicious food - all this serves as the main bait of all tourists.

In the Riviera Nessebar 3 * hotel (Nessebar) three meals a day on the principle of a buffet. Vacationers note that the food is not diverse, but it is difficult to call it bad. Such tourists prefer to have lunch and dinner in cafes and restaurants, since there are quite a lot of them near the hotel. The average cost of lunch is almost the same, so for the entire time you can visit many establishments and try many national dishes.





The picky guests say that the Riviera Nessebar 3 * hotel has a good selection of dishes, but it is recommended to alternate them so that they do not repeat. The only negative - often served cooled food.

In the hotel there is a restaurant and bar, where you will be offered to taste the national dishes of Bulgarian cuisine, as well as original dishes from the chef.

Hotel beach

The coast of Sunny Beach annually receives a blue flag of quality, which means that here you will find the purity of sand, the beauty of nature, the level of service and developed infrastructure.

And this is not surprising, because those who stop at Riviera Nessebar 3 * (Bulgaria) have a view of 7 kilometers of clean sand and sand dunes. This amazing seascape will leave few indifferent.

In the season there are a lot of people. As a result, there is a crowd of tourists on the beach, the water becomes slightly muddy. This is not like many visitors to the hotel.

The beach located on the territory of the hotel is free, but you will have to spend 8-12 leva on an umbrella and a sunbed. Access to the toilet is possible only for money.

Compared to the rest of Bulgaria's beaches, this one is very clean, calm and quiet. Therefore, tourists of a mature age love to come here who want to relax from the city bustle and vibrant youth life.

Vacation with children

As for entertainment, young tourists here are definitely not bored. Well-equipped playgrounds are located both on the hotel territory and along the coast of the beach. On the promenade you can also jump on trampolines and ride from the inflatable slides.





A great place for families with children is a visit to the water park, located 500 meters from the hotel. Another entertainment can be a train, whose path starts from the central alley and passes through the north of Sunny Beach. Equally interesting will be a ride on horses and in a carriage.

For teens their own entertainment. Rental offices will offer young people to rent bicycles, segways, electric cars, ATVs and scooters.

Rest will not do without discos. In the hotel "Riviera Nessebar 3 *" this type of entertainment is considered a mandatory program.





In order not to make a mistake in choosing a place to stay with children, the guests of the Nessebar Riviera are better to get acquainted with useful information in advance:

  1. The southern part is the closest to the ancient Nessebar. Main feature: the presence of sand dunes.
  2. The central part is the noisiest place where most restaurants, shops, and cafes are concentrated. It will certainly be interesting for students to go here.
  3. The northern part is the most peaceful place, it is here that the 3 * Riviera Nessebar hotel is located. The mountain slopes make this place unusually picturesque.

Where to go on an excursion

The ancient museum-city of Nessebar is the main pearl of Bulgaria, which has preserved its beauty to this day. It is located on a rocky peninsula north of Burgas. This place is a must-see if you come to Riviera Nessebar.

After many years of research, historians have proven that the history of Nessebar dates back to at least five millennia: every year archaeologists find traces of ancient civilizations and cultures that once inhabited the peninsula. In 1956, Nessebar was declared a museum city, and since 1983 it has been under the auspices of the UNESCO organization.

The old part is a real miracle that a few centuries ago went under water. Now in its place there is land size 850X300 km, where there are 40 temples. You can enjoy the views of the "underwater Nessebar" from a boat in clear weather.

Opinions of vacationers about the hotel

What do tourists think about the rest in Riviera Nessebar 3 * (Bulgaria)? Reviews about the hotel, despite the advantages listed above, were mixed. Most tourists were disappointed with food, so they preferred to dine in the nearby restaurants and cafes.

A lot of flattering reviews was said about the staff and the hostess of the hotel. And also vacationers emphasized that all employees are fluent in Russian, do their job quickly and efficiently.

There are few reviews about the pool, since almost everyone preferred to swim in the warm sea. Therefore, this part of the hotel is the calmest.





Some tourists shared a trick on the Internet. According to them, in rooms the quality of communication is much worse than in a restaurant, by the pool and at the reception.

A few words in conclusion

Judging by the reviews of tourists, Sunny Beach is a place worth visiting if only to enjoy a calm atmosphere, soak up the sun, walk barefoot on the soft sand of a 7-kilometer beach, climb the hotel terrace and meet the sunset there, make " tourist pilgrimage "to the city-museum of Nessebar and marvel at the beauty of architectural buildings.
