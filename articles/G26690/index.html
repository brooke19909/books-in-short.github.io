Anti-radar "Crunch": instructions, reviews

Anti-radar "Crunch" - a special type of radar detectors produced by a company specializing in the production of audio equipment. Audio Products Inc first appeared in the Russian markets in 1997, providing affordable and high-quality acoustics. Having gained the reputation and trust of customers, the company expanded its product line by launching the production of radar detectors.





Advantages of Crunch Radar Detectors

The main advantage of this brand of radar detectors is its affordable cost, coupled with the innovative technologies used to create them. Gadgets are sold both through automobile and radio electronics stores, and through online stores.

The Crunch anti-radar can not only record the location of modern police radars, but also notes the presence of laser guns and similar devices, the action of which is aimed at detecting anti-radars.

The maximum range of Crunch radar detectors is 5 kilometers. The effectiveness of gadgets is proven in practice, as well as their quality: devices rarely fail.

Cost of Crunch Anti-Radiators





The price of the Crunch anti-radar varies depending on the specific model and functionality, but the minimum is 1700 rubles. Despite the fact that the latest models of radar detectors released are more expensive, their prices are much lower in comparison with similar devices manufactured by other companies.

The lineup

The company is constantly improving its line of anti-radar models. The latest released radar detectors are equipped with digital processors with a discriminator, so that their efficiency increases significantly. Anti-radar "Crunch" quietly tolerates temperature changes, does not require grounding and can operate autonomously from a power source.

Anti-radar "Crunch" (213B)

The Black 21 series of radar detectors first appeared on sale several years ago. In the category of budget devices, they quickly took a leading position. To detect the radars and lidars used by the traffic police, a novelty was used - the Crunch anti-radar (213B).





The instruction for this model reported that the device is capable of fixing police pulsed devices. The anti-radar functionality also includes the ability to detect conventional radar signals. The option of circular determination of laser signals allows you to capture and fix the location of the lidars used by traffic police.

Disadvantages of Crunch Radar (213B)

The gadget calmly registers simple and outdated radar models and monitors the driver's compliance with the speed limit, but modern lidars and radars can cause difficulties for him. Anti-radar Crunch does not record the latest models of complexes for fixing violations. These are the systems "Avtodoriya" and "Arrow". Despite this, it is possible to use a radar detector in places where traffic police officers have not yet used the latest developments in the field of devices that monitor compliance with the high-speed regime.

Functional features of the radar detector

The anti-radar captures the signal of radars and lidars at a distance sufficient to reduce the speed to an acceptable value.

The driver is informed about the detected devices through sound and light signals. The display contains LEDs whose flashing frequency increases as you approach the fixed radar. Sound notification works similarly: for different frequency ranges, a certain tonality is established, which also allows you to approximately determine the power of radio emission.





The information content of the Crunch radar (213B) allows the car driver to receive the necessary data in a timely manner. In addition, the motorist can manually configure the alert system.

Switching the sensitivity modes allows you to avoid interference and "garbage" radio signals on the air.

The performance of the radar detector is checked after each of its inclusion. If an error is detected, the car driver is notified of this.

Due to the affordable price, wide functionality and popularity, the model began to be faked quite often, so the original radar detector can only be purchased from an official manufacturer.

Anti-radar "Crunch" (224B)

The main advantages of the device can be called high efficiency and performance, speed in detecting radar. All models in the series are equipped with Samsung processors, thanks to which gadgets can compete with similar foreign devices. The reviews left on the Crunch anti-radar (224B) confirm its popularity and trust from motorists.





The radar detector was developed specifically for Russia and is able to quickly and accurately determine the location of various speed meters operating in all radio frequency ranges accepted in the country. The device is capable of fixing mobile and stationary traffic police radars, radars operating on instant and short-pulse signals. Laser devices that detect speed violations are detected in the circular sector. VG-2 type radar direction finders are also fixed by the gadget.

Anti-jamming is an important function of a good radar detector. The effective and reliable hardware of the Crunch device (224V) allows you to avoid clogging the air with third-party interference and noise, reducing their level to the minimum possible. The installed filter ignores impulses that are similar to radio signals from radars and do not constitute a hazard, and does not allow the warning system to falsely trigger. Protection against radio interference and false signals provides the choice of sensitivity mode.

Radar detector "Crunch 2130"

For devices capable of fixing radar and lidar of the traffic police, certain requirements are imposed: the clarity and accuracy of fixing the traffic police devices and an affordable price. Anti-radar "Crunch 2130" meets all the wishes of motorists, is of high quality and reasonable cost. The model is part of the Regular series, all the instruments in which quickly, accurately and accurately fix police radars.





Almost all modern radar detectors are capable of catching radio signals in all ranges. The reviews left on the Crunch anti-radar confirm the fact that the gadget copes with its technical minimum perfectly: it fixes radars, direction finders like VG-2, lidars.

The functional features of the device allow you to catch not only standard Doppler radars, but also more difficult to fix the device. The gadget perfectly catches such devices as short-pulse radars and working on the basis of one-time radio signals that are very popular with the traffic police. Sources of laser signals are very accurately located by him. Errors in identifying such devices are extremely rare, so the radar detector justifies its popularity.
