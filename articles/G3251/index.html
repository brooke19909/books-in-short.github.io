Metro "Petrovsky Park". Third Interchange Station

The opening of the Petrovsky Park metro is scheduled for August 2017. At this station, passengers traveling along the Zamoskvoretskaya line towards the north will be able to transfer to the Third interchange circuit. Several times the opening was postponed to a later date. According to the original plan, the Petrovsky Park metro station was supposed to be commissioned in 2015.





What is another line for?

Thirty years ago, the metropolitan metro scheme had a completely different look. She was less branched, and each of its branches is not so long. But even then, the plan for the construction of the chord line was under consideration. For several reasons, this project was rejected.

Today, the construction of stations that are part of the Third Interchange Circuit (TPK) is quite active. In 2017, the opening of five new stations is planned. In 2018, the construction of the metro Lefortovo, Rubtsovskaya, Sheremetyevskaya should be completed. What is the Third Interchange Circuit for? What will give the residents of Moscow the opening of new stations? We give an example.

A person who is not far from the Kuntsevskaya metro station can get to the Savelovsky shopping center using ground transportation. There is another way ‚Äî by metro. But in this case, he will have to pass three stops along the Arbat-Pokrovskaya line, then go to Koltsevaya and get off at the Novoslobodskaya station. It will pass another three minutes along the Serpukhov-Timiryazevskaya line and only after that will it be in the shopping center (from the subway a transition to the shopping center is carried out). Thus, he will spend half an hour on the road. At the same time, he will make three transitions from one branch to another, which is rather tedious. The opening of stations of the Third Interchange Circuit will reduce this route by half.





Metro Petrovsky Park, as well as several other stations under construction, will connect Kuntsevskaya and Savelovskaya. Each resident of the capital has at least once encountered such a problem: you should get to the object, which is geographically close, but get by land transport for a long time due to traffic congestion, and you will have to make several junctions on the subway and go through many stations. With the completion of the Third Interchange Circuit, such inconvenience will be eliminated.

Features

The Petrovsky Park metro station, which will open in late summer 2017, is being built according to a standard project. What the pavilion will look like can be seen in the photo below. In the construction of stations of the Third Interchange Circuit, decorative design is minimized. Not an exception and the metro station "Petrovsky Park".

sights

Metro station "Petrovsky Park" will, of course, perform not only a transfer function. It is being built in an area where there are many attractions. And after the opening of new stations, they can be quickly reached from anywhere in Moscow.

Among the interesting places located near the metro under construction is a park, in whose honor the station is named. The park complex was created at the end of the XVIII century. This is one of the historical monuments of Moscow. Not far from the metro station of the same name, which will open soon, the Dynamo Stadium is also located .
