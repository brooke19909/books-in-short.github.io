Hotels

Travelers come to Leukerbad (Switzerland) throughout the year, so the tourism infrastructure is developed here at the highest level. The resort has 28 hotels, which can accommodate 1400 people, and 1700 apartments, campsites and chalets, which simultaneously accommodate 6900 tourists.

Among the hotels, the Haus Cristal hotel is located within walking distance from the resort center. According to reviews, here you can rent comfortable apartments with beautiful views of the mountains. If you prefer this hotel, you will receive a Leukerbad Card Plus card upon check-in, using which you can use public transport for free and use the Sportarena complex.

Another popular hotel, Kur & Ferienhaus Volksheilbad, offers rooms with balconies and TVs. The windows offer panoramic views of the snowy mountain peaks. This hotel also offers a Leukerbad Card Plus. Attractions and entertainment

The resort of Leukerbad (Switzerland) has a large number of entertainment venues and attractions, as tourists write about in their reviews. How to get there

The resort in Switzerland can be reached by plane, which lands at Geneva Airport. After that, you need to catch a train to Lek-Susten, and there you can buy a bus ticket to the tourist village. After about 2.5 hours you will arrive at your place. Leukerbad is a 2.5-hour drive from Bern, 3 from Geneva, 5 from Milan. Experienced travelers are advised to get to Zurich Airport, where you can take a train or bus to the resort.
