How to check the remaining minutes on "Tele2": which way to choose?

For active subscribers who prefer to always be aware of what is happening on their number, there are several verification options. Check the rest of the minutes on "Tele2" on your own

So, if you decide to independently receive information about what your tariff is, how much traffic has already been spent on it, minutes have been spoken within the set limit and text messages have been sent, then the following options will be very interesting for you. Since different lines of tariff plans are used in different regions of the country, we offer information on TP, implying the included amount of communication services.





Useful USSD-requests in order to check the rest of minutes on the Tele2 number:

  • Black (TP): * 155 * 0 #.
  • Violet (TP): * 116 * 17 #.
  • Other TP: * 116 * 20 #.

After typing any of the given list of commands, information about how many minutes are left to be used will be sent as a text message. Such a notification will arrive almost immediately after the necessary request is entered on the screen of the mobile device and sent.

In addition to the USSD request, you can use the web portal and the mobile application in order to check the remaining minutes on Tele2.
