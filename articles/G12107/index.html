Black Lake, Montenegro: description, relaxation, photo

Lake Crno Jezero is located in Montenegro. It is the most famous in the country, located in the park Durmitor. This lake is located at an altitude of more than 1.4 thousand meters above sea level.



Hydronym

The name of the lake in translation into Russian means "black". It received such a hydronym because of the dark green color of water. However, this does not mean that the pond is dirty, it just reflects conifers growing around. These forests surround Crno Jezero so densely that its surface appears black. That is why it is called Black Lake.

Briefly about the main thing

Black Lake is glacial. It is considered one of the most beautiful in this region and the largest among similar reservoirs of Montenegro. The water in it is not at all cloudy, but crystal clear, and therefore, in calm weather, you can easily see the bottom. The lake itself and the surrounding area have gained worldwide fame thanks to its incredible beauty and grandeur, which are described in the works of many writers. Now this place is quite popular not only among residents of Montenegro itself, but also among visiting tourists from other countries.



Characteristic

Black Lake (Montenegro) is located at the foot of the Bear Mountain. You can drive from the center of Zabljak in just a few minutes (distance of about 3 km). If you look at this pond from a height, you can easily see that it consists of two lakes: the Big and the Small. They are interconnected by a narrow channel 60 m long. Local legend

The local population has one interesting legend, which is associated with the Black Lake. It is believed that one saint, when walking through this area, was unjustly accused of theft, and all the villagers pursued him. In punishment for this, he sent a flood to them, as a result of which the village was under the Small Lake, and the surrounding area - under the Big.



Neighborhood

As it was written above, Black Lake (Montenegro) is surrounded by dense forests, which are reflected in its waters, making them dark. The conifers themselves in the lower parts of the trunks are covered with moss, and wild berries grow at their foot.

Around the lake is Durmitor Park, which has many paths for cycling and hiking. Many mountain streams flow into the reservoir, the largest of which is Mlinsky. In spring, when the snow begins to melt, they turn into beautiful waterfalls.

Black Lake (Montenegro) is not the only body of water in these places. In the park you can see others, however, they are much smaller, but also very beautiful.

Rest on the lake

The discrepancy is about 4 ¬∞ C. However, this does not stop tourists. They come here not only to ride boats and sunbathe, but also to swim.

For a good rest, all the necessary conditions are created here - arbors, benches are set, even a local restaurant functions, to which a specially constructed hiking trail leads. This food service serves a variety of national dishes. There are signs throughout Durmitor Park that make it impossible to get lost or go astray.

Fishing on the Black Lake in Montenegro also deserves attention. Trout, of various species, lives in large quantities in the reservoir. This is a lake, California, river. You can catch it only on artificial baits. The unique thing is that even sitting on a boat, you can see how a fish swims to the hook - so clear water in the lake. Fishing is paid, and it is advisable to discuss all the nuances with the caretaker in advance.

In these places, tourists are offered a varied recreation program. Of course, in the summer season what could be nicer than sitting on the banks of a clean reservoir. However, those who prefer an active holiday, it is recommended to visit one of the highest mountains - Bobotov-Kuk, next to which is Black Lake (Montenegro). The height of the peak is 2523 m. And in the winter, tourists are invited to visit the Ice Cave, which is also located near the lake.
