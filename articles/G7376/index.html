"Haide" (insurance company): useful information for potential customers

The world is so unpredictable that no one can guarantee a person the safety of his life and health, safety in any circumstances of his movable and immovable property, etc. Moreover, legal entities can also not be sure that nothing threatens their business and something could hardly happen to him. Realizing all these points, today people prefer not to take risks, but turn to various insurance companies that offer a variety of insurance programs of their choice. One of these companies is JSC "Gaide".

General information about the insurance company "Hyde"

Haide is an open joint stock company that appeared on the Russian insurance market in 1993. The company's shares are held by 32 shareholders, among which there are both legal and physical persons. The General Director is Mrs. Tatyana Mikhailovna Gaide. The amount of the authorized capital of the company is 120 million rubles. Despite the fact that the Hyde (insurance company) was formed in times very difficult for the Russian economy, it managed to overcome inflation and its consequences, continued to work and develop in a country where bureaucracy and corruption flourished and defended its position in fierce competition with huge the number of other insurance companies.





At the moment, "Haide" is one of the most successful companies and has its own technical service station, a modern medical center, a round-the-clock information call center, emergency commissioners service. It operates in Moscow, as well as in the Ural and Northwest regions. In St. Petersburg, there are already 22 branches of Haide. Millions of residents of the cultural capital of Russia know the address of the main office: insurance company "Hyde", St. Petersburg, Khersonskaya street, 39A. Haide cooperates with a number of leading insurance companies and reinsurance agencies in the world, including Korean RE, Polish RE, Fortis Corporate Insurance, GEN RE and others.
