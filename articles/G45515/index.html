Housing prices

As already mentioned, this is one of the most prestigious areas, so real estate prices here are higher than in others. In the old housing stock, in five-story houses, one-room apartments cost between 1700000-1900000 rubles. For a two-room apartment you will have to pay 2100000-2600000, the price of a three-room apartment is 2400000-2900000 rubles. In a nine-story building, the cost is slightly higher, by 200-300 thousand rubles. The average price per square meter in a new building is 45-50 thousand, in business-class houses - from 70 to 90 thousand rubles. Townhouse at the construction stage will cost about seven million rubles.

Promising area

In Krasnodar, the Festival Microdistrict is considered one of the most promising. It has all the necessary infrastructure, well-established transport links. In this area there are many free lands that once belonged to the Solnechny state farm, which allows for comprehensive development.
