Gelendzhik, hotels by the sea: addresses, description, services

Gelendzhik is considered one of the most comfortable resorts of the Black Sea coast of Russia, and this is not difficult to verify. It is a city of sun, fun and relaxation. The climate on this stretch of coast is a mix of continental and highland. For more than half a year the sun shines here, and from late March to late October you can sunbathe on the beach, but for the right bathing temperature the sea warms up only by mid-May and lasts until mid-autumn. There is no that sweltering humidity that is observed in other Black Sea resorts, for example, in Tuapse, Lazarevsky, Loo or Sochi. Every year during the beach season, about 4 million tourists choose Gelendzhik as a vacation spot. Hotels by the sea or on the slopes of the mountains surrounding the city are always ready to receive and accommodate everyone.



Rest in Gelendzhik

In this city you will feel a special flavor, which is created due to the sun's heat, bright colors and various vegetation: juniper and pine groves, Lebanese cedars, the oldest and largest of which grows in the courtyard of the summer house of the Russian writer Korolenko. Here you can also find rare Pitsunda pines, oaks and hornbeams, boxwood and laurel bushes. The city and its surroundings are shrouded in an incredible, slightly bitter and brackish aroma. This is noteworthy Gelendzhik. Hotels by the sea will allow tourists to admire the beautiful scenery of the bay, in which you can see the white sails of yachts gliding along the azure water, and with luck, the ups and downs of dolphins that live off the coast. They say that the air here is able to heal and gives a lot of positive emotions and good mood. That is why the inhabitants of Gelendzhik are so kind and hospitable. They are determined to ensure that all guests of the city are satisfied with their rest and remember for a long time the time spent here.



Infrastructure

Gelendzhik is a resort with a well-developed infrastructure, with a large number of entertainment facilities: restaurants, cafes, bars, taverns, discos, nightclubs, which are mainly located along the famous city promenade. Fans of lazy relaxation can also come to Gelendzhik. Hotels by the sea with a swimming pool are an ideal place for those who come here to enjoy plenty of peace and the southern sun. The city has a dolphinarium, several beautiful water parks, one of which is the largest on the entire Black Sea coast, a wonderful amusement park for both adults and children. By the way, he is practically in no way inferior to Disneyland. There is also a cable car that allows you to enjoy the bird's eye view of the surroundings of the city and the safari park. Wine lovers can go for a tasting in the cellar of the winery and try local wines to the sounds of the violin.

Natural beauty

Picnic lovers can go on excursions to the numerous waterfalls located on the Zhane River and form natural fonts, where you can swim, relax under the murmur of mountain streams and the sound of falling water. In the suburbs there is Mirror Lake, where you can fish, ride catamarans or boats, sunbathe, comfortably sitting on the trestle beds and sunbeds, in a word, get a real relaxation, and return to Gelendzhik in the evening. Hotels by the sea in the evenings offer tourists a wonderful view of the quay glistening from the light of lanterns and silver water, illuminated by a bright southern moon and pink lights. Believe me, the picture is simply amazing and worthy of the brush of a landscape painter.

Where to stay?

Gelendzhik can offer many options for accommodation : hotels by the sea, mini-hotels and guesthouses. This sea town is located in a rather large bay in the form of a horseshoe. Buildings - private houses, motels, boarding houses, hotels and hotels - are located in the form of an amphitheater. Row after row they descend to the water. Of course, many of the tourists want to relax closer to the beach, and therefore the prices of coastal hotels are much higher than those at the foot of the mountains.
