"Green Island" in Berdsk - park and recreation center

Along the coast of the Ob Sea, as the inhabitants of Berdsk often call the Novosibirsk Reservoir, there is a vast park, which at one time was called "Green Island". It perfectly describes what opens to the eyes of guests who wish to spend their weekend here.

How to get there

The recreation park "Green Island" in Berdsk is assigned to number 9/1 in the microdistrict with the same name. You can get here by any type of urban public transport, as well as on your car. At the entrance there are comfortable parking. From the city center to the park go no more than 10 minutes.

The Berdsky sanatorium adjoins the green zone to the right, and the Berdskaya Spit reserve is located even further. This location is very good in terms of choosing a vacation program. After all, you can stay in a sanatorium, and spend time outdoors on the beach in a park or enjoying the beauty of the reserve.

What does the Green Island base in Berdsk offer?

The territory of the complex is a pine forest, in which a lot of interesting things for children and adults are hidden. First of all, these are comfortable double wooden houses. They can comfortably spend several days. Rent will cost 1.5 thousand rubles. per day.

There are all conditions for family celebrations, corporate parties, birthdays, weddings, etc. A variety of arbors are dispersed at a great distance from each other. The smallest can accommodate 10 people, and they can be rented for 1.5 thousand rubles. in a day. There are large tents for 40 guests in the Green Island park in Berdsk, but their price is also higher - 5 thousand rubles. per day.

You can relax with maximum comfort in the gazebo with a Russian or Finnish bathhouse, next to which there are mangal zones. You can choose from an hourly or daily rental.

The popularity of the Green Island park in Berdsk does not fall all year round. When the reservoir fetters ice and covers the snow, the service of renting snowmobiles becomes popular. For 3.5 thousand rubles. You can ride for an hour, getting incomparable pleasure. Ski and skate rental is also available.

Sports fields are dispersed throughout the complex. Admission is free all year round. You can play football, paintball, volleyball, laser tag to choose from. It is here that every year sporting events are held, organized with the participation of schoolchildren, professional athletes and just fans of a healthy lifestyle.

Beach on the shore of the reservoir

There is access to the coastal zone from almost anywhere in the Green Island park in Berdsk. The beach is sandy, there are no stones, but trunks of trees that have fallen into the water often come to the surf. This is not surprising, because almost the entire coast is densely covered with pine forest, due to which there is an unusually clean and "tasty" air.

The bottom is safe, and even near the surf shallow. This is one of the reasons why people loved to come here for a swim and sunbathe with their young children. Kids can splurge freely in shallow water, without fear, a stone's throw from the coast to go headlong into the water. But, of course, adults need to look after the kids very carefully.

What should you worry about before going to the park?

Entrance to the territory of the Green Island park in Berdsk is paid year-round. Cost in 2018 - 50 rubles. per person, for children - free of charge. And then it all depends on how exactly visitors want to spend their time. Often guests are located in free areas with tents and barbecue facilities. In this case, the administration will charge a fee for the rental of space and equipment. In other words, money must be taken with you.

Given that for the most part the Green Island park in Berdsk is a forest belt, here you can encounter all the "charms" of outdoor recreation. So, it is worth taking care of the supply of water with you, a snack for the whole company, as well as insect repellents. If there is a desire to fry shish kebabs, then it is not necessary to carry a barbecue grill, but a means for ignition, coal, and meat - this must be bought in advance. The park has its own bar and cafe, where you can have lunch or dinner if you wish.

Although Novosibirsk is far from the south, you can sunbathe on the beach at the height of summer for your pleasure. And this means that not only swimwear will be useful, but also towels, panamas, and flip flops for swimming. For the kids, put sandcastle toys in the bag. It is clean here, so they will certainly enjoy such entertainment.
