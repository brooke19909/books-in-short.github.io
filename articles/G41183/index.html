5 * Hotels: Hurghada Marriott Beach Resort, Egypt, Hurghada. Hotel description, reviews

Egypt is a country visited by thousands of tourists every year. It's no secret that one of the most popular resort areas is Hurghada. On the territory of the city, as well as in its vicinity, there are many 5 * category hotels. Hurghada Marriott Beach Resort is one of such hotel complexes. Here tourists are offered comfortable rooms, good food, a picturesque beach and, of course, high-class service. So what is this place and how is it spoken of?

Location Description





First of all, travelers are interested in the question of exactly where the Marriott Beach Resort 5 * hotel is located. Hurghada, or rather, the picturesque coast of the Red Sea - it was here that a large five-star hotel complex was built. By the way, its location can be considered very successful. For example, the distance to the old city is only 8 km - it is easy to overcome it by taxi or bus, and in this part of the resort there really is something to do. About 5 km away is the center of Sakkala. The distance to the international airport is also quite small at 4 km - the hotel guests do not have to worry about the long journey.

Hotel complex "Marriott" (Egypt): territory and infrastructure

The large hotel occupies a rather impressive territory, which faces directly to the sea. The hotel consists of a large six-story complex, built in an interesting modern style. The courtyard is reserved for swimming pools, sports fields, terraces for relaxation and entertainment. According to reviews, the territory here is really beautiful - everywhere palm trees, ornamental bushes, flowering flower beds, interesting compositions. It is regularly cleaned, so spending time at the hotel is always a pleasure. By the way, the hotel is part of the famous Marriott chain (Hurghada), which over the years managed to create an excellent reputation for itself. After all, guests are offered only quality service and comfortable conditions.

What do the numbers look like? Photo and short description





Hotel 5 * Hurghada Marriott Beach Resort offers customers check-in in one of 283 spacious rooms. There are rooms of various categories, including:

  • Standard rooms /
  • Suites /
  • Venus Suites (large bedroom, separate living room with large windows and panoramic views, two bathrooms) /
  • Cupid Suites (consist of a bedroom with a door and a spacious living room with upholstered furniture and a dining area) /
  • Neptune Suites (consist of a bedroom and a living room, and each room has a TV and a minibar, and there are also two separate bathrooms);
  • There are several rooms with a high degree of comfort and the necessary equipment for relaxing people with disabilities.

Regardless of which category you choose, you can count on your room to be spacious, bright, clean, furnished with the necessary amount of modern, comfortable furniture. Some rooms have a private balcony or a private private terrace with comfortable furniture, which overlooks the hotel grounds, the sea and the surrounding area.

The rooms are equipped with modern technology, because without it a truly comfortable rest is hardly possible. Guests can independently maintain comfortable temperature conditions in the room using a convenient air conditioning system. In your free time you can relax watching TV (some Russian channels show). There is a telephone, as well as a safe, which is used to store small valuables and documents. Each room has constant, high-speed Internet access, which is very important for many travelers. There is also a minibar, but in standard rooms its filling is paid separately and only at the request of residents.

The spacious, shining cleanliness of the bathroom will delight you. There is a bathtub, you can take a shower. There is a toilet mirror, washbasin, bidet. Guests can use a hairdryer to dry their hair. Also, each guest is given a set of clean towels, which are changed daily. Of course, in the bathroom you will find soap, shampoos and other hygiene products. Reviews from tourists indicate that the rooms are really cleaned every day and do it very well - no one will have to complain about cleanliness.

Where is the beach? Rest of tourists on the seashore

What beach holidays can the Marriott Hotel offer? Hurghada is a city famous for its picturesque beaches and warm sea. The hotel owns its own coastal area, which is located very close by - you can walk to it in a few minutes. The beach here is clean, covered with soft sand. There is a small backwater where children bathe, but you can swim in the depths. On the beach you can relax and have fun.





Naturally, there are other ways to have fun. For example, many tourists like to snorkel, because the underwater world here is bright, beautiful and interesting. On the shore you can rent a boat or a catamaran, ride a boat, banana, water skiing. It is no secret that one of the attractions of the Red Sea is the numerous coral reefs. Accordingly, scuba diving is a popular activity among tourists . At the hotel you can take short training courses, rent equipment and book a trip to the most interesting places.

Vacation with children: are there suitable conditions?

Many people prefer to relax with the whole family, along with children. What can the Marriott Hotel offer in this regard? Egypt is a wonderful place where you should definitely take your child. On the territory of this hotel complex, he will easily find himself a fun activity. In terms of amenities, a rollaway bed, a chair in the restaurant and a sufficient selection of meals for children are guaranteed.





Yes, and there are ways to have fun. A shallow pool is specially equipped for children, where the water always warms up well. Many children prefer to spend daytime in a mini club, where together with animators and other children they can play, engage in art, take part in productions, etc. In the evening, a fun mini-disco is organized. The hotel also has an excellent playground with swings and slides, where you can also have a good time.

Does the hotel offer additional services?

Of course, guests are entitled to additional services. The hotel has ample parking, and you can easily rent a car in the city. A currency exchange office, laundry service, dry cleaning and an Internet cafe are constantly working. In case of health problems, you can contact a medical center.

The hotel has a decent business center with a small conference room. By the way, the local amphitheater is used not only for evening performances. Seminars, business events, festivities and even weddings are often organized here.

Leisure of guests: how to have fun at the hotel?





Of course, tourists have something to do, without leaving the hotel. For example, many people prefer to spend time near a large pool, around which are sun loungers with soft mattresses. Here, by the way, classes in water aerobics are regularly held, as well as fun competitions in water polo. During the day, other events are held in which residents of the Marriot Beach Resort 5 * hotel can participate. Reviews indicate that here you can sign up for morning exercises, and yoga, and dance lessons.

There are also tennis courts where fans of this sport can have a good time. There is also an equipped squash court. Tourists have fun and chat with each other while playing billiards and table tennis. You will have the opportunity to visit the gym, as well as the sauna, bath, jacuzzi, ice pool. The hotel has several experienced massage therapists.

Animation is available, and quite good. And in the evenings, exciting shows are organized at the local amphitheater, where there really is something to see. Of course, do not forget about excursions, because Egypt is a country that is ideal for traveling and studying history.
