Nahariya (Israel): an oasis surrounded by greenery

Israel can rightfully boast of popularity among tourists. Many travelers choose this country for their holidays. Some come here to immerse themselves in the ancient history of the country, others - to improve their health on the shores of the Dead Sea, and still others enjoy relaxing on the beaches of the Red and Mediterranean Sea.

A bit of history

Special attention in this country deserves the city of Nahariya, located on the Mediterranean coast. Israel as a young state has its own new history. The relatively young city of Nahariya was founded in 1935 by a group of enthusiasts led by Dr. Suskin.



Having acquired a large plot of land from Arab landowners, they dreamed of organizing an original farming here. And such vigorous activity meant the development of local infrastructure. But the dream did not come true, because the high-quality products of Jewish farmers were replaced by cheap Arab vegetables.

Then the immigrants, not despairing, began to develop resort holidays here with the same activity. The first customers to come to the new boarding houses were the British, who were fascinated by the mild climate and the warmth of the Mediterranean Sea.

Location

Mediterranean Nahariya (Israel) freely spreads on the flat terrain in the northern part of the country. From the northern border of Israel with Lebanon to Nahariya 9 km. And from Haifa, the distance to the city is 34 km.

You can take the bus from Ben Gurion Airport (Tel Aviv). Travel time takes about 2 hours, and a ticket costs 50 shekels.

sights

Today Nahariya (Israel) is a fairly popular destination among foreign travelers. Developed tourist infrastructure and well-maintained beaches - all these factors attract tourists to Nahariya. Israel is known for its many resorts, but this city is one of the highest levels in demand among tourists.



Travelers with great interest go on excursions in the suburbs of Nahariya. Climbing Mount Rosh HaNikra, you can admire the magnificent picture of the sea coast. As the locals joke: "from this point you can see all of Israel."

Today on Mount Rosh HaNikra, a nature reserve has been opened with a tourist attraction - karst grotto caves. It should be noted that the presence of these caves significantly affects the tourist flow to Nahariya. Israel is rich in various attractions, but the local cave grottoes are especially popular among travelers.

The main attractions of the city also include the castle of the Crusaders Montfort. It is in ruined condition, but its central knight's hall is well preserved. Montfort Fortress is a national monument. Admission to its territory is free.

In the city itself, you can visit such attractions: the remains of the Khan's fortress (2200 BC), the Byzantine temple (64 BC) and the Phoenician fortress. Vacationers with interest visit the local museum of archeology and modern art. Entrance to the museum is free.

Climate and weather

Many tourists are attracted by Nahariya (Israel) because the mild climate of the Mediterranean prevails here. It is rainy in winter, but relatively warm, and summers are hot and sunny.



In summer, the weather in Nahariya (Israel) pampers tourists with hot and sunny days. The swimming season lasts from early May to late September. The air temperature in the summer season stably keeps above +25 degrees. But the heat, due to the refreshing sea breeze, is much easier to tolerate. Precipitation falls from December to the end of February.

Tourists reviews

Rest in this place is not only a sightseeing. The city of Nahariya (Israel) is famous for its amazing beaches. Traveler reviews about them are very different, but most of all about Ahziv beach.



This beach is famous for its warm lagoons, where you can relax with young children. A club with a pool was built near the beach. There are all conditions for surfing and diving.

Many women leave their admiring reviews about shopping in this city. Shopping trips here turn into a pleasure. Local shops have a wide variety of products. Leather goods and cosmetics with Dead Sea minerals are especially valued here.

It should be noted that many couples who leave their enthusiastic reviews about this place come to Nahariya to spend their honeymoon. Newlyweds can order an unforgettable photo shoot here, conduct the wedding process and order a festive hall to celebrate their family holiday with friends.

Local hotels

Coming here, tourists have a wide selection of accommodation. A large assortment of rooms is offered by local hotels. Nahariya (Israel) is known for its hotels.

The three best hotels in Nahariya are headed by :

  1. Shtarkman Erna Boutique Hotel;
  2. Sea Life Hotel;
  3. Madison Hotel Nahariya.

According to tourist reviews, Shtarkman Erna Boutique Hotel is located close to the sea, hotel staff offers various excursions. For cars there is a private parking.



Sea Life Hotel is located 50 meters from the sea. The rooms are spacious and bright. Good and varied breakfasts. The city center is a few minutes by car.

Madison Hotel Nahariya is suitable for family vacations and business trips. The hotel has good food. All rooms have internet.

Almost all year round the city of Nahariya is buried in greenery and flowers. Israel as a whole is known for its arid climate, so for many visitors it is surprising to find a green oasis here. Having rested here once, many tourists then tend to come here again.
