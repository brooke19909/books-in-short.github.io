LCD "European Park": reviews, photos, layout of apartments

Volgograd is a large Russian city with a developed infrastructure. At present, quite a few residential complexes and new landscaped areas are being built on its territory, but it is sometimes very difficult to make a choice in favor of one of them. We want to introduce a new project - LCD "European Park". Reviews of real customers will help to make the most objective review, identify all the strengths and weaknesses of the complex. The review will take place according to the main criteria: location, infrastructure, transport accessibility, layout and prices. First stage houses have already been commissioned, so residents of the microdistrict have already formed their own opinions. We are interested to know how their expectations were met.





about the project

LCD "European Park" is represented by a rather large and well-known developer "KVARTSTROY". His activity began back in 2003, the developer managed to implement several excellent projects, but did not stop there. The company has excellent facilities not only in Volgograd, but also in Moscow. To date, the developer has managed to establish the work of all structural divisions. The high pace of construction and compliance with all standards have earned an impeccable reputation for the company. A separate milestone in the development of the enterprise was the Park Evropeisk residential complex . This is a comfort class housing project that has no analogue in the city. That is why he attracted so much attention. The first phase of four ten-story houses was commissioned in 2014. About 1,500 people bought an apartment here, becoming happy owners of excellent real estate. The project was supported and approved not only by residents, but also by representatives of the administration.





New buildings are being erected using monolithic technology, which speeds up the construction process. The facades of the houses are lined with blocks of cellular concrete, which gives the building not only strength and durability, but also excellent insulation properties. In such a house it will be warm and cozy on cold winter evenings, at the same time as this cool, hot summer. The decorative finish is done with plaster and facade paints.

District Infrastructure

Let us together evaluate the infrastructure that the Park Evropeisky residential complex has. Responses of residents of the complex are mostly positive. The developer paid great attention to the improvement of the complex. Modern playgrounds and sports grounds will appear here - a paradise for children. Transport accessibility

At the construction stage, the district could not boast of a developed transport network, but by the time the object was commissioned, many new bus routes appeared, which allows residents of the complex to easily reach any point in the city. The public transport stop is located right behind the territory of the European Park LCD.

Layout

Of course, many are interested in what apartments are offered to residents of the Park Evropeisky residential complex. The layout of the apartments corresponds to traditional European projects. In general, the developer took the experience of European colleagues as the basis of the entire project, which is reflected in the title. He offered small apartments with a variety of planning solutions. If you had the opportunity to evaluate the layout of European apartments, you must remember that they imply the presence of isolated rooms for sleeping and relaxing and a single space in the kitchen, dining room and guest area. Most often, a small but comfortable kitchen is separated from the living room by a bar. It was this idea that formed the basis of the entire project. All apartments turned out to be small, but rather cozy, comfortable, and, accordingly, more affordable for buyers. Even a young family can afford an apartment here, which is just beginning to live a separate independent life and so far cannot buy a spacious apartment.





Finish

All apartments in the LCD "European Park" for rent turnkey. This again repeats the European concept. The developer decided to save the residents from a lengthy and expensive repair by presenting finished apartments with decoration. On the Internet, there are enough negative reviews regarding the decoration itself. In fact, many people really consider her wasted means, because in most cases everything has to be redone. The developer took into account the negative experience of his colleagues and decided to attract professional architects and designers to work on the project. They developed a unified concept of decoration, preferring calm, even neutral tones, which will become an excellent background. For decoration, high-quality finishing materials were used: laminate of class 32, non-woven wallpaper for painting, two-chamber double-glazed windows, plumbing made in Europe. All residents who received the keys to their apartment, note that the decoration is so high quality that they really do not want to change anything. They just brought furniture, added some touches, decor items.
