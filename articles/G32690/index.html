How to check the balance of another Beeline subscriber?

To ensure that the user is always in touch with his close people, friends and relatives, the Beeline operator offers its customers to activate a special service. With it, you can find out the balance of another subscriber. This will allow not only to always be aware of the state of the room, but also to come to the aid in a timely manner when the amount of funds in the account tends to zero. How to use this option and is it available to all customers? This will be discussed in detail in the current article.
