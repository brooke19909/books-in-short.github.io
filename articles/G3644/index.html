Hotel "Alyosha Popovich", Yaroslavl: address, description of rooms, reviews

By the beginning of the last century, Yaroslavl was already considered one of the largest cities in Central Russia. Industry is well developed here, in particular chemical and textile. However, this did not prevent him from becoming one of the most beautiful cities on the Volga.



Yaroslavl city

In literature, the city was once called "Russian Florence", "Russian Switzerland." Many Muscovites willingly bought summer cottages and summer estates here, located on the picturesque banks of the river. However, today not only residents of the capital come to Yaroslavl, the city has become one of the main pearls of the Russian Golden Ring, so many tourists come here every year.

Hotel "Alyosha Popovich"

Yaroslavl is a city where today it is not difficult to find a good hotel. The choice of a good hotel depends on many factors, including the area or the proposed infrastructure. "Alyosha Popovich Dvor" was built in the historical center of the city. It is considered one of the best options for living in terms of price / quality ratio.





This tourist complex is a project that has been implemented as part of a tourism development program. The idea of ‚Äã‚Äãits discovery arose in 2012, when the 1150th anniversary of the city of Rostov the Great in the Yaroslavl Region, the birthplace of Alesha Popovich, was celebrated. The essence of the concept is that guests staying in the complex can not only have a good rest, but also visit the world of Russian epics and fairy tales, ancient legends. There is even a version that the epic hero, in whose honor the hotel is named, carried his military service exactly at the place where the hotel is now located.

"Alyosha Popovich Dvor" was built in close proximity to the Yaroslavl Kremlin. This stylized hotel, which is especially often chosen by foreigners, allows guests to plunge into the world of Russian fairy tales and epics for several days.

Its three-story building looks like princely mansions. Apparently, therefore, those foreigners who come to the city and want to get to know Russian culture better choose this mini-hotel. Every tourist will be able to feel himself a hero of a fairy tale, the choice of which will be the hotel "Alyosha Popovich".

Yaroslavl, being the capital of the Golden Ring tour, annually receives several tens of thousands of tourists, including foreigners, who are primarily attracted by the originality and convenient location of this hotel. The folk-style hotel is a five-minute walk from the Kremlin. Five hundred meters there is a bus stop. The railway station is three kilometers away.





Address, how to get

From the bus station, the hotel can be reached by shuttle bus to the Transfiguration Monastery stop. Hotel address: Yaroslavl, st. Pervomaiskaya, 55. You can get from the train station to the Alyosha Popovich Dvor hotel by trolleybus number 5 and 9 or bus number 33, which goes to Epiphany Square. From the last to the three-story hotel building, only five meters.

Near the hotel there are many attractions, including the Transfiguration Monastery (250 m), the Church of the Epiphany, the Assumption Cathedral, the Church of Elijah the Prophet, the Yaroslavl Museum-Reserve. The city center is only five minutes walk. The airport is eighteen kilometers away.

Infrastructure

This hotel, the level of which is equivalent to a three-star, has a beauty salon, left-luggage office, laundry, conference room. The infrastructure also includes a souvenir kiosk and a 24-hour ironing room, a museum-theater with free one-time access for hotel guests and a health center. Reception works without interruption. Check-in is possible at any time of the day. Here you can pay for a transfer or order a taxi.

Late check-out, shops, bridal suites, designated smoking areas, heating, newspapers on request, water coolers on each floor and shoe shine are also provided at the hotel.





Business Services

A special place in the infrastructure is occupied by the conference hall, which the Alyosha Popovich hotel offers. Yaroslavl is a city that is not only a famous tourist destination, but also a business center. It often hosts various forums and congresses, symposia and other events. For such business events, the hotel provides a modern conference room with a maximum capacity of up to one hundred participants. It is located on the attic third floor of the hotel. Like the hotel itself, the conference room also features original design. Its walls with imitation of brickwork depict many city attractions.

The cost of renting a room includes air conditioning, the use of a flipchart and a screen, and the provision of office supplies. For a fee, you can rent audio equipment, including microphones or a mixing console, multimedia projector and widescreen TV.

The conference room provides various furniture arrangements based on the format of a particular event. It has a street entrance.

For children

On the territory of the Alesha Popovich Hotel there are many amenities for young guests: a children's playroom, a mini club, highchairs in restaurants. Children under the age of five are allowed to stay at the hotel with their parents free of charge, subject to the use of beds in the room. Children under the age of twelve are provided with an extra bed for 600 rubles per night. Older children can get a berth in the form of a folding chair for 1000 rubles.

Hotel Policies

Check-in time at the Alesha Popovich Dvor Hotel is 14:00, and the check-out time is set at noon. If you leave the room beyond the checkout time, an additional fee will be charged from the client. From twelve days to six in the evening - hourly, from eighteen hours to midnight - half the fee per day, and beyond this time - for the whole day. For stays of no more than twenty-four hours, the full price of the room is taken from the client, regardless of the time of arrival.
