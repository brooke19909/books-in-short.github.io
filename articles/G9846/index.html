Craft a bear from cones: a master class

Creative activities with a child are always interesting and exciting. If you like to make crafts together from natural material and are looking for new ideas, a bear made of cones will be a great option for you. These souvenirs can be made so unusual and original that they will be an excellent gift for grandparents, and can also decorate any interior.

Crafts from natural material: the benefits of such creativity

It is very useful for a child to engage in any kind of artistic skill. It develops fine motor skills, imagination, imagination, the ability to follow the instructions of an adult. Working with natural materials has several advantages:

  • everything the child makes is natural and therefore safe for health;
  • collecting and preparing cones, acorns, leaves, flowers can be turned into a fascinating cognitive process;
  • These materials provide great opportunities for creativity.

The child is suitable for any crafts of cones: bear, hedgehog, fox, mouse. Children love to make animals. In addition, animals are the heroes of folk tales. Of these heroes, which the baby made with his own hands, you can arrange a whole theater.



Preparation of materials

All elements of natural origin must be collected in dry weather. It is advisable to let them lie down a bit in the room until completely dry. After a while, a moist, fresh spruce cone will turn from a thin to a large one that has opened. This must be taken into account. If it is necessary to keep the form, coating is applied with an aqueous solution of wood glue. In a few days, modeling will begin. Everything that you find in nature should not have visible damage: holes, dents, mold, rot. High-quality materials will allow the craft to maintain an attractive appearance for a long time.

Cones, acorns and everything else is better to sort and sort right away. Smooth, neat should be used in its entirety, and those where there are not enough scales, hats, can be disassembled into parts and used separately. If you need a bear made of cones, acorn caps or pine scales will work as ears.

Material combination

In any artwork, the technique of contrast looks spectacular: color, texture, shape, size. This is one of the methods of composition with which harmony is achieved. A bear from cones and plasticine, threads, balls, grains, nuts will look good. You can use chestnuts, acorns, sticks, matches as additional elements. Good combination of natural textures and artificial. For example, you buy eyes or a nose in a handicraft store ready-made plastic, designed for soft toys. They will give your little animal a certain facial expression and facial expressions. Although they can be made from plasticine, peppercorns, pebbles. In a word, you need to show imagination, and then your bear from the cones will turn out magnificent.

Fir cones

Tips common to all the works were said above, now specific options and methods for their manufacture will be considered: how to make a bear out of cones of spruce, pine, their combinations. You can make a beast of large size or in the form of a miniature desktop souvenir. Very simple samples will be offered, which even a small child will cope on his own, others will require joint efforts with an adult.

Any work begins with the preparation of everything necessary. In the illustration, where the crafts made of cones are presented, the bear is made rather simplified. It is made using material collected from under the tree.





To cope with such work, you will need to follow these steps:

  1. Find bumps in the forest or in the park (one large for the body, the second smaller for the head, four medium-sized for the paws and two identical for the ears).
  2. Collect all the elements as shown in the picture. They can be fixed with glue, wire or pins, on the heads of which again all parts are glued.
  3. Decorate the product with a ribbon.

Craft number 2

And if you go to the fir tree for a long time? How to make a bear out of cones? Pine trees are often planted right in the park and in the adjacent area. Use this material. It also makes wonderful souvenirs.





Such a bear can be obtained in several ways. Option 1 includes the following steps:

  1. Build a body of several large elements.
  2. Attach a head from one bump to it.
  3. Ears are made of pre-separated flakes.
  4. For paws, take also two elements.
  5. You make pompons from white threads: round for the muzzle and cut in shape for the feet and ears. They are obtained by winding yarn on a cardboard blank in the form of a ring, then cutting along the perimeter and pulling it into a ball. This method can be obtained in any form.
  6. Attach pompons with glue or wire to the right places.
  7. Set eyes, nose. Decorate with a scarf.

Option 2 is made using a completely different technology. Such a bear is done very easily from cones with his own hands. Take an old soft toy and glue it with pine scales, leaving fluffy paws, the inside of the ears and the muzzle.

The following is the simplest of cones. Photo clearly demonstrates this. Such a craft can build even a baby. The cardboard form can be cut as a whole or in separate elements. In the first case, two bumps are used for the body on both sides of the workpiece. In the second embodiment, the legs and head are inserted between the scales and glued.





Beautiful combination

If you do not feel a lack of materials, feel free to combine them. You will get the same cheerful bear from cones, a sweet couple or the whole family.

So do the following:

  1. Take a good big fir cone for the body.
  2. You attach to it a pine, which has opened in a flat form, as a head.
  3. Muzzle and ears get from the caps of acorns.
  4. Eyes and nose are made of peppercorns.
  5. Four small pine cones are used for paws. Try to strengthen them so that the craft is stable.

Bear made of cones and plasticine

In the previous examples, the elements were connected using glue or wire. The same can be done with plasticine. The most important thing is to use the appropriate color so that it is as invisible as possible among brown scales. Try to press the elements tightly against each other and take a sufficient amount of clay.



By the way, it can also be used as a decor. Then choose any color. From it make a muzzle, eyes, ears, jewelry.

Fashion teddy bear

If you have already decided to go in for creativity and are going to make souvenirs for your pleasure or as gifts to relatives and friends, try dressing your hero in bright things. Items in his wardrobe can be crocheted or knitted, sewn, or even used clothes for dolls.





Ideas for decorating are very different. Let it be a bear made of cones, dressed as Santa Claus or Snow Maiden. Great gift for the New Year. It is not necessary to dress the souvenir from head to toe. It is important not to overdo it and preserve the beauty of natural material. It will be enough cap of Santa Claus or the crown of the Snow Maiden.

If you want to make a gift to one of your friends or relatives, decorate the bear with the element of clothing that is most characteristic of this person. An original hat, scarf or bow will be quite enough.

Polar bear

In addition to decorating with additional things, it is worth using the magic of color. Take plasticine in bright shades or even paint to create a specific image of your hero. If you paint a bump in white color from the crafts presented in the photo, you get a polar bear.



You can also make a panda bear, clown, artist. It all depends on your imagination.
