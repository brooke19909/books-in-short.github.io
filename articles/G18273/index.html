Making Harry's Tie

The image of Harry can be recreated, going to a New Year's party, Halloween celebration, children's party or costume party. Let's start creating the image of a wizard with the simplest thing - a tie. Without this modest little thing, Harry Potter's costume would not look at all. You need to find a completely red tie. You can also use a regular tie. But in this case you will have to devote more time, since it will be necessary to wrap it with a red cloth. Still need a piece of yellow material.





The fabric is well ironed. It is best to use moistened cotton fabric. Through it, you can iron all the elements of Harry's costume. A rectangle equal to the length of the tie is cut out of red material. From the wrong side, it must be hemmed manually. Then the tie is ironed again. The seam is sewn on a typewriter. To create a Harry Potter costume with your own hands, small strips of no more than 2 cm wide are cut from yellow material. They are fastened diagonally from the top of the right side to the bottom of the left. Must be at least 6-7 strips. After that, the tie is ironed again. A knot is tied. On the node, the strip should be attached at a different angle or directed in the opposite direction. This will create a credibility effect.

Invisible Mantle of the Famous Sorcerer

The invisibility cloak is what Harry Potter costume is unthinkable without. The pattern of this subject is not very complicated. To create it, you will need a back, front, hood and two sleeves. But this element of the costume is the most voluminous. A large flap of black fabric will be required. He ironed again. Sewing from crumpled fabric is strongly discouraged. You will also need scissors, threads, a needle, a sewing machine, chalk and a voluminous sheet of paper. It should be noted that if the suit is sewn on an adult, then the fabric should be at least two meters in length - to full height. If the Harry Potter costume is for children and is designed for a small child, then you can take less matter.





For greater effect, you can take the lining fabric in red. From paper on a full scale we draw a pattern of all the details. We lay out the paper on black material and circle it with chalk. It should be noted that it is necessary to leave 2-3 cm for the allowance and hemming of the product. Scissors cut out all the details. Then they are gathered together and manually sewn with thread. Iron the seams with iron and sew on a typewriter. Then we repeat the action identically with red material. When both parts are ready, they are connected and hemmed manually. The seams are ironed and sewn. The bottom of the product does not need to be sewn. Through this cut, you need to turn the Harry Potter costume on the front side. Only after this we sew the bottom. Ironing again. The result is an excellent gown with a beautiful red lining. For convenience, you can add a belt.

Wizard's sweater

You can use any school sweater to create a Harry Potter costume for a boy. The main thing is that it is black or gray. You can also use a regular turtleneck. On the product along the V-shaped line, cut the neck. The recess should be at least 5 cm for a child and 6-12 cm for an adult. The seam is processed and ironed. Then it is sewn on a typewriter. To create the image of a full school uniform, you can use ordinary dark trousers and a white shirt.





Distinctive emblem and scar

The easiest way to create a logo is to print it on a printer. You can use thick cardboard. Paper with an emblem is glued to the base and attached to the mantle. Also in specialized services you can order a printout of the badge on the fabric or on the sticker. The logo background should be green. Below you can use the inscription of the faculty. To ensure that the emblem is attached to the product most firmly, the mantle can be left overnight under pressure. Then the glue will take better. Self-adhesive paper can be used for scarring. Draw a zipper on it. Gently cut with scissors.

Glasses and magic wand

To fully recreate the Harry Potter costume, glasses are needed. They must be round. You can use purchased, but you can make them yourself. To do this, you need a dense wire and pliers. It is necessary to gently bend the edges and give the wire the desired shape. We solder. Coat the joints with silicone or liquid plastic. After that, the metal is coated with black paint or varnish. The product is left to dry overnight.





To make the final touch to the Harry Potter costume, you need to make a magic wand with your own hands . To do this, you can use a straight piece of wood or a dowel. To create a relief effect, the tree is coated with silicone glue in a spiral. Then let dry and apply silver paint from the spray gun. After complete drying, varnish. Harry Potter costume is ready.
