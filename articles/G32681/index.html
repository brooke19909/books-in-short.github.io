Starwood Hotels and Resorts Worldwide. One of the largest hotel chains

Chain hotels have gained immense popularity among tourists. Many look for familiar names when booking a room away from home. Indeed, the hotel brand is a quality mark, a guarantee of level and comfort. Many hotel chains are enterprises with many years (and sometimes even centuries) of history. Starwood Hotels and Resorts Worldwide is a name familiar to everyone.

A bit of history. How did the brand appear?

You can write books and make films about the history and appearance of Starwood. This is a story about how to find benefits even in times of crisis, how to think outside the box and take reasonable risks.

Founded in 1991 against the backdrop of the American crisis of the 80s, by the end of the 90s it became the largest hotel chain of the time. The company was allowed to conquer the market by purchasing the Sheraton hotel chain. For her, the company competed no more no less with Hilton.

Now under the brand name of Starwood Hotel there are more than 1300 hotels in 100 countries of the world. Starwood is a conglomerate. He manages 11 hotel chains from budget to luxury, designed for different target audiences.

The flagship project of the company

With Sheraton in the 90s, the story of the unprecedented success of Starwood Hotels began. The first hotel of this chain was purchased back in 1937 in the city of Springfield. Changing the name was then economically unprofitable, so all subsequent acquisitions were renamed. And so the Sheraton network was born.

SPG. Starwood Hotels Loyalty Program

The Starwood Preferred Guest membership card (Starwood privileged guest) gives hotel guests even more benefits. This program is perhaps the best in its class. It consists of three levels, the transition to each of them depends on the number of nights spent in hotels.

Becoming a member of the Strawood Hotel family is simple and free. More than 1300 hotels in 100 countries will be in your pocket on one plastic card. Exclusive offers, discounts, bonus nights and much, much more.

For the convenience of guests, mobile applications have been developed that allow you to quickly book, cancel, check the balance, order additional services. SPG even offers its own Visa-based credit card for even greater convenience of settlements and accumulation of bonus points.
