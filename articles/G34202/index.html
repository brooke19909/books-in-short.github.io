Fortress history

The fortress in W√ºrzburg began to be built in 1200. At that time, a conflict was brewing between the townspeople and Archbishop Konrad von Querfurt, and he decided to build a "solid house" for himself on Marienberg Hill. The erected citadel became the seat of government and a fortification. The fortress tower and part of the palace were preserved from that construction.
