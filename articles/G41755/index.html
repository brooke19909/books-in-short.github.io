What is an individual investment account? How to open an individual investment account?

Since 2015, a new investment system has begun to operate in Russia, the profits of which are completely exempt from taxation. Brokers did not stand aside and are ready to offer their customers additional conditions for deposits in an individual investment account. What is it, you will learn from this article.

Definition

An individual investment account (IIA) can be opened by any individual resident of the Russian Federation in a brokerage company. The owner contributes funds to it, which he can use to complete transactions himself, or transfer them to trust. Such accounts have a special taxation regime. Features:

1. A resident may open only one individual investment account.

2. The maximum amount of investments is 400,000 rubles / year.

3. Time limits: 3-5 years.

4. An individual investment account can be replenished, partial withdrawal is not provided.

5. If the contract is broken ahead of schedule (before the expiration of 36 months), then the client loses all tax benefits.

6. Funds can be invested in the Central Bank, futures.

7. Accounts can be transferred between brokers.





Taxation options

According to IIS, there are two schemes for obtaining benefits:

1. The tax base is reduced by the amount that was deposited to the account for the year. This option may be of interest to people who receive the main income in the form of wages. Compensation occurs at the end of the year in the form of a deduction, after the submission of a 3-personal income tax return.

Example. The taxable income of an employee is 500 thousand rubles. From it personal income tax will be paid in the amount of 65 thousand rubles. (thirteen %). If during these 12 months a person contributes the same amount to the IIS, then the tax base will be recounted to him, having returned the personal income tax. This option will work, even if the investment brought a small (up to 13%) loss over the year. This scheme is not available to entrepreneurs on a simplified taxation scheme, for pensioners and the unemployed.

The deduction algorithm is similar to the one used, for example, when buying an apartment. The man acquired housing. Starting next year, he begins to collect documents for a tax deduction, which is calculated on the basis of the salary specified in the 2-NDFL certificate. Only the amount of tax paid in the same reporting period when the apartment was purchased is refundable.





A similar scheme works with IMS. Only help should be provided to the broker. Tax authorities recalculate a person‚Äôs salary for the previous year. If it does not exceed 400,000 rubles, then 13% will be returned from all income (i.e., 52 thousand rubles). To confirm these operations, you will need to provide a certificate of employment, which clearly indicates the amount of tax paid for the previous reporting period. So for customers who receive an "unofficial", such a scheme is not beneficial.

2. Full exemption from tax on income from IIA. This option is suitable for active investors.

Example. A private entrepreneur decided to invest 400,000 rubles in a brokerage company. The yield is 30% of the invested amount. For 3 years he will receive 360 ‚Äã‚Äãthousand rubles. Example. During 2015-2017, the client contributed 400 thousand rubles to the IIS. And then he sold and bought the Central Bank with the same money. After three years, the balance amounted to 200,000 rubles, and profit - 800,000. After 36 months, the client decided to close an individual investment account. Since the second taxation scheme was chosen, the state will exempt it from paying personal income tax in the amount of 800 x 0.13 = 104 thousand rubles. In order to get a benefit, you need to provide the broker with a certificate that there were no deductions of the first type for the previous 36 months.

The client can independently choose a tax scheme before investing, but it is impossible to combine them during the term. For people who have no idea about the work of the exchange, IIS is an improved investment option. Affordable amount, there is no need to develop well in the market, the ability to earn income that exceeds bank deposits, tax benefits - all these factors make this tool attractive enough for the population. For speculators, an individual investment account is an additional opportunity to optimize their investment portfolio.

Individual Investment Account 2015: how to open

You need to contact a brokerage company, which is a professional participant in the stock market. The form of ownership must be AO or LLC. Most often it is AMC with large banks. IIS is used to segregate assets placed in the stock market. This scheme is a bit like an IRA. But if the latter provides for the possibility of withdrawing funds only upon reaching a pension return, then in IIS the time is limited to 36 months. The obligation to withhold taxes is imposed on the broker. Income from such operations is calculated separately. That is, it is impossible to reissue a current account with a broker for IIS.
