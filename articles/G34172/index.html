Camera





Much more interesting is the situation in this category. The smartphone we are talking about today is equipped with two cameras: the main and the front. It is very interesting that the phone, whose cost is estimated at about eight thousand, had a built-in lens that takes pictures with a resolution of thirteen megapixels.

But due to the fact that the section is slightly recessed inside the case, the lighting turns out to be uneven, which leads to the appearance of greenish marks left in the photographs taken. If you often shoot in low light conditions, take this fact into account. The rest of the photos are of good quality for a device of this class. Now let's talk about the front camera. Something special cannot be said about her. She makes good photos, but this is typical for lenses with a quality of 1.6 megapixels. So nothing new.
