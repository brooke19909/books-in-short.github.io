Phone story

What can be said about how the 6700 model was created in general? We are now seeing the Finnish manufacturer on the other side. After Microsoft bought them, Nokia devices gradually changed their names. However, this does not change the essence of the matter: internal competition is sometimes more acute for the manufacturer than external. Extremely suspicious is the fact that two devices for the same price have differing technical characteristics and compete with each other.

This unpleasant trend has roots in the distant past of the company. The Nokia 6700 model is also associated with it. Its creation was due to the fact that it was necessary to interrupt the success of the sale of the Nokia 6300, which at one time became a bestseller. In parallel, a device is created with the code 6303. And these three models begin to compete with each other, forming a fancy triangle.
