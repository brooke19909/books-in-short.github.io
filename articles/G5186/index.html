We create cosiness in the house: we learn to crochet a plaid

Hand knitted blanket is beautiful, warm and original. Such a product will be a worthy decoration of the interior of your home. How to crochet a plaid is the topic of this article. Here, the attention of needlewomen presents tips on choosing materials for work and how to perform a similar item. Read, remember, get inspired.



The purpose of the product and the selection of threads for its implementation

Before you start crocheting a plaid, let's decide why and for whom it is needed? This thing can serve as a blanket on a sofa or chair and perform a purely aesthetic function. Then you can take synthetic yarn. She looks very good in the finished product, things connected from it are durable. If the blanket will be used as a blanket, then for its manufacture, take a natural thread: cotton, peeled wool, linen, alpaca. This thread is pleasant to the body, does not cause skin irritation and allergies. It is important to remember that it is desirable to carry out a plaid for the baby from yarn, on the packaging of which is the label "baby". Such a thread undergoes additional processing and purification, it is very soft and delicate.



Beginners in this direction of needlework should know how to choose the right tool for the yarn in order to knit blankets. Crocheted beautiful products will be obtained only if its number matches the thickness of the thread. In order not to make a mistake with the choice, carefully study the label on the skeins. The size of the hook for working with this type of thread is indicated there.

Learning to crochet a plaid . Ways of doing

This product can be connected by two methods: a whole canvas and motives. The first method is quite simple: pick up a chain of air loops of the right size and make a direct canvas with any selected pattern. When the blanket reaches your desired length, fasten the thread. Next, bind the entire product in several rows.

It is much more interesting to make a plaid of motives. We will consider the technology of its manufacture in more detail.

Knitted blanket - simple and beautiful!

Elements for making a plaid can be of various shapes: squares, rectangles, penta- and hexagons, long stripes. You can see examples of such products in the photo.



How to crochet a plaid from individual elements? First, the necessary number of motives is tied. Then they all combine into one whole product. This action can be performed by stitching or crocheting the connecting posts. So that the product does not stretch and has a finished look, you should draw its edges. On all sides, strapping is carried out, regular or curly ("fans", "cloves").

Crocheted baby rugs are especially good. Schemes of such elements are presented to your attention in the photographs. A blanket knitted from such colored particles is sure to become your child's favorite.



Conclusion

Knitting a plaid of motifs is a good solution in order to use the remnants of yarn. But for knitting multi-colored squares and flowers - this is what you need. Gather the remnants of the strings, sort them by composition and thickness, and start your work. The process of creating a knitted blanket is very exciting. Loop by loop, and you do not have time to look around, how to do this work in a few evenings. Let the needlework process bring you an excellent result in the form of a cozy and beautiful blanket!
