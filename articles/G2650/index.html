Conditions for obtaining

Almost all banks where you can get a loan in Yekaterinburg put forward standard conditions for receiving funds. They are the presence of:

What documents are needed

To obtain a loan at any financial institution, the borrower must provide employees with a specific set of documents. In general, it is standard, but sometimes the bank may request additional documents for a more thorough analysis of the solvency of the potential borrower.

The standard package includes:

  • original passport of a citizen of the Russian Federation;
  • to request information about a client from the credit history bureau, the bank will need SNILS;
  • less often, banks are asked to provide a certificate that will serve as proof of income (it can be compiled according to 2NDFL or the form of the bank).

Additional documents for obtaining a loan are:

  • TIN;
  • driver's license;
  • certificate of ownership (real estate or vehicle);
  • a copy of the work book certified by the seal of the employer;
  • international passport.

If there is a last document, then it can be provided as an additional one, which will serve as confirmation of solvency. However, it should be borne in mind that it should have marks on going abroad over the past six or twelve months.
