Interesting historical facts. Historical facts about Russia

Historical events and facts are very informative and interesting. They give us a unique opportunity to understand what is happening in a given period of development of human society, nations and countries. Almost all peoples have interesting historical facts. Russia has a lot of them. This is easily explained by the rich centuries-old past of our country. Interesting about modern Russia





About the rulers

Since the beginning of the reign of Nicholas the First in 1825, the rulers in our country have alternated on the principle of "bald - hairy." This pattern has survived to this day.

About television

In 1992, the chiming clock on television on New Year's Eve was delayed for one minute.

About money

The double-headed eagle on coins is not the emblem of the country, but the emblem of the Bank of Russia.

Scientific historical fact

The only time traveler in the world lives in Russia . This person is Sergey Krikalev. He spent more than 800 hours in space, moving at high speed. According to the theory of relativity, time slows down at high speeds. It was estimated that the astronaut returned to Earth 0.02 seconds younger.

About laws

In 1994, the government passed a law prohibiting dogs from barking from 23.00 to 7.00. This law is valid now, but only in Moscow. It is also noteworthy that the legislative act does not spell out what punishment the offender will incur.

Facts from Geography

The RF is almost twice as large as the United States. St. Petersburg metro is the deepest in the whole world. The Trans-Siberian Railway connects the capital and the city of Vladivostok and is the longest railway line in the world. Siberian taiga - 8% of terrestrial land.
