Installation

General tips:

  1. Position the drain hose to a minimum height of 60 centimeters. Relevant tips are also provided in the manual for connecting household appliances. Do not ignore them.
  2. Pay attention only to built-in washing machines under the countertop. The first model you like is not suitable for this.
  3. Installation should never be done on a pedestal, no matter how attractive it may look.
  4. Remove the transport bolts before starting work.
  5. Do not conduct any actions yourself if you are not confident in your abilities. It is better to contact the master of the service center.
