Anapa vacation. "Carnation" (recreation center): description, prices and reviews

Anapa is considered one of the oldest settlements in Russia. More than 25 centuries ago, it was part of the Bosporus kingdom. Here lived the Genoese, Circassians, Turks, Greeks, it was in these places that the Silk Road passed.

Now many tourists from different cities of our country are coming to Anapa to admire the rocky shores, landscapes of mud hills and relic juniper forests. It has a unique climate, about ten types of healing mud and mineral waters, and Anapa doctors treat many diseases. This city, the coast of which consists of sandy and pebble beaches, awarded the UN award, is recognized as one of the best balneological and climatic resorts in the world.





Anapa is considered a place for families and children. A huge number of hotels, more than 170 pensions, resorts and tourist centers where you can get an excellent vacation are concentrated here.

Gvozdika, a recreation center located at Blagoveshchenskaya station, gladly opens the door to everyone who wants to spend their holidays in this wonderful city.

Description

The recreation center "Gvozdika", the photo of which is presented below, is within walking distance from the beach. To accommodate vacationers, there are rooms of various categories located in panel, brick and wooden houses. It offers several playgrounds, playgrounds and sports grounds, rental, 3 meals a day. For a fee, the base staff organizes a variety of excursion programs in a comfortable minibus.





Location

The camp site is located on the Black Sea coast, in the village of Blagoveshchensk on a picturesque peninsula, 30 km from the base is the city of Anapa. The recreation center "Gvozdika" has its own large beach, there is a clear sea and healing air. In the immediate vicinity are the Kiziltash and Vityazevsky estuaries with unique formations, rich in mud, with healing properties.

Accommodation

To accommodate guests who want to spend their holidays comfortably, Gvozdika (recreation center) offers:

  • Superior rooms with a refrigerator, TV, air conditioning. Also in the room there are necessary furniture, a shower and a bathroom.
  • Wooden log cabins with all the comforts of home.
  • The rooms are economy, the amenities are one for the entire block (toilet and shower on the floor).

When booking, guests can choose any type of accommodation.

Cons of rest

Some guests of the recreation center claim that the rooms in the houses are not clean enough, the furniture needs to be replaced, sometimes insects can be found in the room. The soundproofing in the rooms leaves much to be desired; if your neighbors are noisy, it is unlikely to fall asleep. There are tourists who give a low rating to the sea. It happens that there are a lot of algae on the shore, jellyfish swim in the water, but, as a rule, this happens after a storm and waves.
