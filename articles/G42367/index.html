Boutique Hotel "Mona" - a great vacation at an affordable price

Mona Boutique Hotel is a great place to relax. The complex is located near Moscow. The road will not bring problems and inconveniences. The vast territory of the hotel is striking in its beauty and splendor. Interesting landscape design, a musical fountain, cozy terraces - all this makes the complex popular among vacationers, honeymooners, businessmen. What other highlights the hotel has reserved for visitors, we will tell in the article.

Fountain - hotel decoration

Boutique Hotel "Mona" has its own zest, which captivates visitors. This is undoubtedly a singing fountain. 35 jets of water create an unusual picture that fascinates and delights. At night, the fountain comes to life. Under the musical accompaniment, the color of water, the strength of the blows change.

Especially newlyweds like this element of decor. They are happy to choose a terrace located near the fountain for their first wedding dance.

Mona is a boutique hotel, reviews of which are exceptionally positive. Guests are satisfied with the condition of the number of rooms, level of service, well-groomed green area. Many people choose the complex for large-scale events and celebrations.
