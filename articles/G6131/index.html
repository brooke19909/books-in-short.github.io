Specifications

So, what can please us with Pioneer AVH-X1500DVD? The characteristics of such a device suggest that it may well provide high-quality sound in the passenger compartment (if there is an acoustic system of the appropriate level). Judge for yourself. The DAC features are such that it provides full 24-bit sound with a sampling frequency of 96,000 Hz. This is pretty decent. The power of the amplifier is 50 watts on 4 channels. In other words, to use this particular radio, it is not necessary to have an additional amplifier or receiver.





Plus, a color touch screen with a diagonal of 6 inches, its own operating system and all kinds of sound enhancers. For example, the radio tape recorder can dynamically correct the range of compressed audio files (such as MP3). This alone makes the device very advanced. Pioneer AVH-X1500DVD is definitely one of the best in its class. And it is recognized even by competitors. But the main thing here is the price. For reasonable money, the user receives a full-fledged multimedia center. What is bad?

Appearance and design

The appearance of this gadget is a bit unusual. It is especially interesting that the mechanical control buttons are adjacent to the touch buttons. This step helped optimize ergonomics. However, the buttons are very small. To get into them when driving is almost impossible. In the reviews, many users regret that there are no special notches on the physical buttons. In general, the design impression is good. The radio tape recorder is designed so that it easily fits into any interior. To the advantage of such versatility is the fact that the device has a choice of 216 backlight colors. Thus, you can choose a color that matches the original highlight of the car.





The radio is made in a glossy case. But this is not particularly important, since almost all the space is occupied by the touch screen. The front panel is non-removable. And this means that such an interesting device will attract the attention of night bullies. So you have to leave the car in the garage. In general, the design of the Pioneer AVH-X1500DVD is very different from other products of this class. Other manufacturers are trying to make the control completely touch. But the manufacturing company decided that physical buttons would come in handy too. And it turned out to be right.

Radio functionality

For its class, the radio has quite a few functions. Support for USB playback and CD-DVD playback are fairly standard options. They won‚Äôt surprise anyone now. But iPhone support is a completely different level. It is also possible to use the steering wheel buttons as control elements of the radio. But this requires a special adapter. Car radio installation

This is a very interesting process. The fact is that the dimensions of a unique radio are very different from the standard. Therefore, before installation, you will have to take care of purchasing the front panel for your car under the DIN 2 format. This is necessary, since it will not work to cut the right hole with a file. Next, you will need to buy a set of necessary adapters (for the steering wheel buttons to work as radio controls). An adapter for connecting the radio connector to ISO is already included.





After installing the panel with an extended slot for the radio, it is enough to connect all the necessary wires and "stick" the radio into place until it clicks. The head unit is installed. But before fixing it tightly, it will not be superfluous to check its performance. If all is well, then you can push the radio into a click. In principle, the installation is simple. This is the whole point of such a product as the Pioneer AVH-X1500DVD car radio. The user manual, by the way, also has a detailed description of the installation process. So problems should not arise.
