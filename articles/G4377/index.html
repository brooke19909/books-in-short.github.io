Phulin Resort 3 *, Phuket: reviews, photos, a detailed description of the rest in Phulin Resort 3 *

Karon is probably the best beach in Phuket. It combines all the best that is on the beaches of the island. Here you will find nightclubs and discos, restaurants and shops. There are hotels across the road from the beach. Among them is Phulin Resort 3 * (Phuket Island), which we will talk about today. Ready? Then we begin!

Small hotel with a welcoming soul

Phulin Resort 3 * is quite small, it has 79 rooms, as well as everything you need for a great holiday.



Rest at the hotel begins with the reception. Hotel guests are greeted in a cozy lounge by always polite and smiling Thais. Suitcases are delivered to the room as if by magic, and the settlement procedure lasts just a couple of minutes.

Comfort and cleanliness

The hotel rooms are cozy and clean, they have everything you need for a great holiday: comfortable modern furniture, clean linens, tables and mirrors, a hairdryer and a minibar, a coffee machine. Also, the rooms have air conditioning, you can set a comfortable temperature for yourself.

In the lobby of the hotel Phulin Resort 3 * (Phuket, Thailand) there is free Wi-Fi. If you are lucky and the room is above the lobby bar, you can use the Internet without even going down to the lobby. You can buy a local SIM card with a package of unlimited Internet, it will please with high speed.

The adjacent bathroom sparkles with cleanliness. Bath accessories - soap, shampoo, shower gel, cotton buds, toilet paper - are replenished every day.

Linen is changed daily; friendly maids also clean rooms every day. By the way, even in every room there are towels for a beach holiday.

The best start to a day at Phulin Resort is to plunge into the pool, then wait for breakfast on a sun lounger in a sun lounger . The pool is small, but it is enough for a morning swim or evening gatherings with a glass of wine.

The hotel serves breakfast only. It includes an abundance of seasonal Thai fruits, both familiar to Europeans (watermelon, banana, pineapple), and exotic mangosteen, rambutans, carambola. It also serves noodles, rice, vegetable salads, pancakes with honey and various syrups, omelettes, cereals with milk. If you want a different, familiar meal, you can cook it in deluxe rooms. There is a small kitchen with the necessary utensils: microwave, refrigerator, toaster, kettle, dishes. Tea, coffee and sugar are regularly replenished by maids.



Remarkably complement the cozy rooms of the hotel small balconies. Here, in comfortable chairs, it is so pleasant to relax with a cup of coffee or tea, with a glass of soft drink or your favorite wine and contemplate the emerald green of the jungle covering the mountains or the well-kept courtyard of the hotel with a swimming pool. Drinks can be ordered at the hotel bar or bought at a store located next to the Phulin Resort 3 * hotel (Phuket), a photo of which, by the way, is presented in this article. Prices do not differ. The great advantage of the hotel is that it is located away from the noisy central streets, but at the same time, all the necessary infrastructure is within walking distance.

The hotel area is small, but very well-groomed and green, with many cozy corners, flower beds and comfortable benches.

The hotel does not have an elevator, but the hotel itself is far from a skyscraper, so its absence does not burden and does not create big problems.

Hotel excursions

Having settled in the hotel, you should definitely take a free sightseeing tour provided by Phulin Resort, during which you can take a sightseeing tour of the island and visit the latex, pearl factory, honey factory, tea shop, learn the secrets of Thai medicine and buy your favorite souvenirs.



The island's signature souvenirs are tin and pearl products, which were mined here from ancient times, as well as scarves painted using the batik technique. The hotel guide accompanying excursions will tell about the history of the island and traditional crafts.

Deep sea

Phuket Island is a mecca for diving enthusiasts. The sea surrounding the island is inhabited by a rich and diverse underwater world. There are also some wrecks near the island. It is here that you can feel like a real explorer of the deep sea. From the Phulin Resort 3 * hotel (Phuket), reviews of which are very positive, excursions are organized to the Similan Islands, the islands of Koh Tachai and Koh Bon, to the Richelieu rock, where whale sharks and stingrays are circling, as well as to the Cruise Cruise ship, which lies at a depth of 30 meters.

Architectural masterpieces

Thailand is a country of temple Buddhist architecture. Guests of the Phulin Resort 3 * hotel will be offered trips to the Tiger Temple, Wat Sirey Temple, which are located nearby. All temples necessarily have their own legend and their own history, which are associated with the island and with Buddhist beliefs and ideas about life. It is also worth visiting the local mining museums and Tai Hua, and on the way to learn a lot of interesting things about the history and traditions of Thai culture from a guided tour.



In the evening, you can go to night shows, discos or visit Magic Carnival, which combines a variety of genres: circus numbers, live music and fiery dances, and then dine in one of the selected restaurants, paying tribute to national Thai gastronomic hits.

Snow-white Karon

The beach closest to the hotel is Karon. As the guests of the island say, neither Patong nor Kate can compare with the purity of water and the snow-white sand on Karon. The beach can be reached by shuttle, which runs from the hotel to the beach and back, or you can take a leisurely walk along the green streets along endless benches with local souvenirs, pharmacies and clothing stores.

On the way to the hotel, we recommend choosing a local restaurant and a snack. Here guests are always welcome, and the menu is very diverse. You definitely need to try fresh seafood, which will be cooked right here, in front of visitors, and traditional Thai dishes that combine the seemingly most incompatible tastes. They are both sweet and salty, sour and spicy.



Freshly caught squids fried with garlic and served with sweet sauce and chicken stewed in a sauce with cashew nuts and vegetables will not leave anyone indifferent. Well, it‚Äôs impossible to refrain from trying exotic fruits for Europeans.

Magnetic comfort of the hotel

Hotel Phulin Resort 3 *, of course, is deprived of the splendor of luxury world-class hotels. He has a very small area, but well-groomed, cozy and beautiful. There is no water park and other entertainment for children. But it has the main thing: friendly and polite staff, ready to instantly fulfill any request, a clean comfortable bed, comfortable rooms furnished with comfortable furniture, a delicious plentiful breakfast, a magnificent view from the windows that open to the jungle or the pool.

The hotel is located in a quiet location, close to noisy nightlife and the best Karon beach in Phuket with snow-white creaking sand and a gentle entrance to the sea.

Restaurants, shops, night clubs, discos, the most beautiful monuments of temple architecture - all of them are located very close. And if you want to see more, immerse yourself in Thai culture and history, try more entertainments and experience more adventures and positive emotions, you can always order any excursion from friendly hotel guides.



Rest in this hotel will surely turn out to be unforgettable, it will again and again beckon and call to itself!
