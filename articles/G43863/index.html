How to recharge a card without a card: the most profitable options for transferring money

Credit and debit cards are a universal way to make payments. With the help of cards you can purchase services and goods, make payments in favor of others, cash out money, use your money abroad. The most popular among the population are plastic products of Sberbank. Using their example, you can consider the most popular ways to recharge a card if the card itself is not available. However, such methods of transferring finances to card accounts are also relevant for products of other banking organizations - Alfa-Bank, VTB, Home Credit, Tinkoff and others.

Cash transfer

So, how to recharge a card without a card? One way to replenish a card account is to deposit cash. You can carry out such an operation by replenishing not only your cards, but also the cards of third parties. Such operations can be performed directly at the bank that issued the card, as well as at any other third-party service or organization that provides the ability to make online payments using electronic systems.

Third Party Banks

How to recharge a card without a card, it is important to find out in advance. You can transfer money by contacting any commercial financial institution. The procedure will be similar to that carried out at the bank that issued the card. You do not need to open an account for this; a bank employee will conduct a transaction on a payment order. You will need to provide your passport details, plastic product number, name and surname.

In some cases, the procedure for crediting funds only by card number may not be possible. Then this will require a card account number. In addition to him, the employee will need to provide the full details of the bank that issued the card.

Money will be received after such a transfer to the account within 3 days. The commission is appointed by the bank that sends the money, and therefore its size must first be checked with the employee. The recipient will not pay anything from the card - money will be credited to his account minus a commission fee.

Transfer through the post office of Russia

Still how to recharge a card without a card? The authority to make money transfers also exists with the central postal operator. The obvious minus of this method of replenishing a card is the duration of replenishment. Money transferred through Russian Post will be credited to your account within 7 days. In addition, for the transfer you will have to pay 1.5% commission on the amount of payment. Moreover, the minimum size of the additional payment is 40 rubles.

To complete the transfer, you will need to present the details of the plastic and passport. You will also need to fill out a standard form. A postal worker will take cash and issue a receipt confirming payment. You can make a transfer absolutely at any post office, not only at the place of registration.

Wire transfer

How to replenish a Sberbank card without a card by wire transfer?

Thanks to the development of modern technology, the classic use of paper money is becoming less and less common. Mutual settlements are increasingly made using bank cards or electronic money.

This method provides additional protection against theft or loss of cash. All funds stored on any electronic medium are protected by additional PIN codes and passwords. Ideally, access to them is available only to the holder of the card or electronic wallet.
