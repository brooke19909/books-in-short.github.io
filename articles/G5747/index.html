DIY lightning brooches - master class with explanations and photos

If you are thinking about how to make a brooch with your own hands, then pay your attention to crafts from zippers. Among old things it is easy to find unnecessary "snakes" that can be used to make beautiful jewelry. Often craftsmen make flowers and insects, hearts and birds, fruits and berries. Some people prefer abstractions from geometric shapes.

How to make zipper brooches with your own hands, we will consider in more detail in our article. Crafts from products with metal teeth are spectacular, but often plastic clasps are also used. The basis for drawing a pattern laid out from strips with teeth is felt. It is easy to purchase in small sheets at any sewing accessories store. The range of colors is so large that you can choose the right shade for any outfit.

Crafts made on denim and using wool for felting look interesting. There are brooch options only with zippers, but crafts with colorful fabric inserts look beautiful. Additionally, the decoration can be decorated with rhinestones or pebbles in the frame, sew or paste half beads or metal rivets.

Beautiful flower

To work on the brooch below, you will need the following materials:

  • black metal clasp with large links;
  • an orange "snake" with small metal teeth;
  • needle with an orange thread;
  • glue gun;
  • black felt circle;
  • safety pin.

Before you start working on a zipper brooch with your own hands, cut off the fabric with scissors and scorch the entire strip with a candle so that the threads do not crumble. On a prepared black felt circle, glue a coiled large strip of lightning with hot glue. Cut the orange clasp into equal lengths, wrap it with loops and sew in the center with threads selected to match the fabric. When you collect a lot of petals, wash them around the middle in a circle. Pin a safety pin on the back, you can put a brooch on a blouse.
