Hotel Zante Imperial Beach Hotel 4 * (Greece / Zakynthos Island): photos, reviews

Greece is one of the most popular tourist countries in Europe. This is where thousands of tourists annually seek to relax on the picturesque beaches and plunge into the mesmerizing atmosphere of ancient civilizations and fabulous myths. One of the famous beach holiday destinations is the island of Zakynthos, which pleases its guests with incredibly beautiful landscapes and high-quality service. It is here, almost at the very shore, that the Zante Imperial Beach Hotel 4 * hotel complex is located.

Of course, when planning a trip, it is worth considering a number of important factors. For example, is the hotel location convenient and can a tourist count on cleanliness and comfort? Do guests offer food? Is this a place to relax with a child?

Hotel Zante Imperial Beach Hotel 4 *: photo and room information





This is a medium sized hotel with 142 rooms. Of course, guests themselves are free to choose the category and level of comfort in the rooms. Reviews from most tourists suggest that the rooms are spacious and bright. Not everywhere there is a fresh repair, but you can count on modern amenities and cleanliness.

At your service is a standard set of furniture: comfortable beds, sofas, spacious wardrobes, bedside tables and tables (in family rooms there are additional furnishings). Guests are provided with working equipment, without which, of course, it is difficult to imagine real comfort. You can relax in the evening while watching satellite channels. There is a small but powerful air conditioner that is simply indispensable in the summer heat. A safe is available for a small fee. Ironing facilities are delivered to the room upon request. And some rooms even have their own kitchenette with cooking appliances and utensils. By the way, all rooms have private balconies with views of the lagoon.

The private bathroom is small, but with good plumbing and a constant supply of water. There is a toilet, washbasin, towels (changed every day) and some hygiene items. Wet cleaning of the rooms is daily.

What kind of food can a tourist count on?





Food is the part without which the vacation is unlikely to become full. What should the guests of the Zante Imperial Beach Hotel 4 * count on? Reviews say that the guests are fed decently. The hotel operates on a convenient all-inclusive system, which gives you the right to three daily meals, snacks and drinks throughout the day (the only exception is the beach bar, where you have to pay the bill).

The menu here is pretty good: they offer both international and local cuisine. You will find light snacks, salads, fruits, meat, fish and seafood dishes, as well as side dishes, fresh pastries and, of course, drinks.

Beach and water recreation: amenities and entertainment





Of course, most tourists come to the island, wanting to have a good time on the seashore. The hotel complex Zante Imperial Beach Hotel 4 * is located on a hillside, its territory faces directly to the sea. You can go down the stone steps. In addition, the funicular goes to the foot. There is also an elevator, which can also be used.

Sun beds and umbrellas on the beach are paid, but the price includes one drink, which can be obtained here, in the bar. Reviews indicate that the coast here is clean and beautiful, covered with fine white sand, and it is cleaned here every day. The entrance to the water is convenient, the depth increases gradually, there is a site suitable for bathing children.

Of course, tourists can count on a more active holiday. For an additional fee you can go boating, boat, banana, water skiing, sailing or even windsurfing. In the evenings, various shows and parties are often held on the beach - no one will be bored.

There is also a luggage storage room. The hotel has a professional doctor who will certainly help in case of health problems. In hotel stores you can buy interesting souvenirs and household trifles.

How to spend your free time? Leisure of guests





Most of the time, travelers, of course, spend on the beach or in the city. Nevertheless, you can have fun and relax on site. In the courtyard there is a spacious swimming pool with clean water, several water slides are working, where adults and children have fun.

You can have a good time at the local tennis courts. The hotel also has billiards and ping pong tables. There is also a mini golf course. The hotel has a fitness center: a gym is equipped with modern fitness equipment. The center has several professional instructors who, incidentally, from time to time organize group sports classes. Massage rooms are located nearby, where, by the way, brilliant specialists also work. In the hall there is a large TV room with a good collection of films.

Entertainment can be found outside the hotel Zante Imperial Beach Hotel 4 *. Greece is a camp with an amazing history and a mass of ancient historical sights. The island is perfect for excursions.

Conditions for a family holiday: is it worth taking a child with you?





For tourists who plan to spend a vacation with their children, the issue of comfort becomes very important. So what to count on when staying at the Zante Imperial Beach Hotel 4 *? As already mentioned, the hotel has spacious family rooms with a separate kitchen for 4-5 people. In the restaurant, you can count not only on the high chair, but also on the availability of special dishes for the child.

As for entertainment, here the hotel has something to offer. In particular, there is a kids club on the territory where kids can have fun, jump on trampolines, take part in group games, in short, have a great time. For younger guests there is a playground with swings, a room with slot machines, as well as a shallow pool. For a small fee, you can use the services of a professional babysitter.
