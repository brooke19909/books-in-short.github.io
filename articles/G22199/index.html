Travel to the UAE: reviews of tourists

Popular world resort countries include the United Arab Emirates. But what attracts vacationers to this hot zone with desert landscapes? At first glance, an unattractive country is fraught with many interesting places that you can discover for yourself by visiting the UAE. Responses of tourists to a large extent reflect the extraordinary delight and warm impressions received when visiting this amazing state. The cleanest sandy beaches and refreshing sea waters, ancient sights that cause genuine interest - this is an incomplete list of those things for which it is worth visiting this desert state.

Climate and geography





This country is a federation of seven emirates: Dubai, Ras al-Khaimah, Ajman, Sharjah, Umm al-Quwain, Fujairah and Abu Dhabi. The name of the latter is the capital city of the UAE. Reviews of tourists about this amazing metropolis are different: someone is struck by the combination of urban landscapes with images of ancient minarets and small alleys, and there are those who are just crazy about the vast expanses of limitless deserts.

Emirates occupy the northeast of the Arabian Peninsula, their shores are washed by the waters of the Persian Gulf. The state of Oman borders them on the east side, and Saudi Arabia adjoins the south and west. The climate here is hot - tropical dry. Precipitation is approximately 100 mm per year. The most optimal temperature for relaxation is observed in winter - about 23 ° C. In the summer, about 40–45 ° , in open places the thermometer reaches 50 ° .

sights





One of the main attractions of this country, many consider the territory of the UAE. Reviews of tourists about this unique corner of the Earth contain such a comparison: “Like fairy-tale landscapes from the magic books of Hauft, sheikhs, minarets, sands and the sea appear before travelers in the sands of Arabia ...”

The UAE has many cultural places worth visiting. For example, the capital city is famous for its ancient mosques, which are known for their rich historical past. During excursions, tourists are usually invited to visit museums, take yacht trips, and relaxing on the beaches is taken for granted here.

Useful Tips





For those who decide to visit the UAE, the reviews of tourists are important. It’s also better to take note of a few tips with this content:

  • If you want to go diving and other sports in the open sea, it is better to go to Fujairah. Here you can admire the most beautiful coral formations, make a swim in the wave and get a lot of pleasant experiences.
  • For those who want to improve their health, it would be nice to visit Ajman, where springs with healing mineral waters are concentrated.
  • You can also make a cruise to all the Emirates. In this case, travel in a carriage compartment costs 4 dichrams (about $ 1). You can also rent a car. Holidays in the UAE (reviews of tourists about this region are almost always positive) are very interesting if you choose a suitable route for yourself.
  • Ras al-Khaimah is the land of hot springs, here you can also strengthen your health.
  • Despite the fact that the influence of European countries in this state is already quite tangible, still you do not need to relax. No smoking or drinking alcohol in public places. There is also a dress code: you can sunbathe only at the pool in bathing suits, you can’t walk in open dresses and so on.

You can relax in the UAE, the reviews of tourists (Sharjah, note, is considered the most international city, since the first church of the Holy Apostle Philip was built here in the region ) have many recommendations that differ and diverge in direction. But, of course, it’s better to see once a hundred times, it’s better to see once, so if possible, the whole family should get out into this country - so amazing and extraordinary.
