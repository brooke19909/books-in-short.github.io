Almaty hotels: overview, description, rating

The city of Alma-Ata is the largest city in Kazakhstan. Therefore, a large number of tourists and vacationers come here. Consider the most popular options that every traveler needs to know.

Rating of institutions:

  1. Hotel "Alma".
  2. Hotel "Edelweiss".
  3. Hotel Renion Hills.
  4. Renion Zyliha Hotel.
  5. Resident Hotel.
  6. Hotel Mildom.
  7. Hotel "Aksunkar".
  8. Hostel Amigo.
  9. Guest house "Visit".
  10. Hotel "Continental".

Hotel "Alma"

Average price per day: 4 thousand rubles.

This hotel in Almaty (Kazakhstan) is located in the city center. Near it there are sights and a traffic intersection. The rooms are comfortable, the interior is decorated in a classic style. The beds are comfortable. There is a minibar, modern appliances, air conditioning, a bathroom. There is access to the Internet. Guests are invited to eat in the restaurants. You can find dishes of both local and European cuisine. The bar has a large number of drinks. In the morning a buffet is served. To conduct a business meeting, lecture or seminar, you can use the conference room. It accommodates up to 120 people, while equipped with all necessary equipment.

Parking is free, sauna, pool, internet and gym too. For a game of billiards, you will have to pay about a thousand.

Hotel "Edelweiss"

Average price per day: 1500 rubles.

In the rating of Almaty hotels, this institution takes the second place. The city center is only 10 minutes away. The rooms are comfortable and equipped to the highest standard. There is a car park where guests are provided with free parking, there is a sauna, billiards and internet. The rooms are both cheap and expensive. Family apartments are also available. All are equipped with everything necessary. Meals are provided in the restaurant. Breakfast is hearty and delicious. Thanks to the large menu, various celebrations can be held here. The airport is 19 km from the hotel, and the train station is 20 km away.

You can come with pets, but this nuance must first be discussed with the hotel. The common living room has a TV. Rooms are cleaned every day. Upon request, staff will wake the guest at a specific time. Reception is open 24 hours a day. There is a laundry room.

Hotel Renion Hills

Average price per day: 4500 rubles.

This hotel in Alma-Ata provides shuttle service, laundry and so on. Numbers of various categories. All of them are comfortable and cozy. There is a restaurant on site. It serves a variety of cuisines. Nearby are cafes, shops and attractions. The train station can be reached in half an hour, a little more time will be needed to arrive at the airport.

Renion Zyliha Hotel

Average price per day: 3 thousand rubles.

Rest in Almaty promises to be unforgettable if you spend it in this hotel. It is located near the entire infrastructure of the city. There is free parking and internet. The rooms are clean and comfortable. They are decorated in a classic style. The bathroom, which is individual for each room, has a hairdryer, slippers, bathrobe and other accessories. The room has a minibar, TV, air conditioning. There is a bar and a cafe on the ground floor of the hotel. Breakfast is served here in the morning.

Shuttle services are available at an extra charge. Satellite TV is connected. Laundry service is available.

Resident hotel

Average price per day: 2500 rubles.

For guests who come to rest in Almaty, parking, a washing machine, a shuttle service, TV and ironing facilities are provided. Reception is open 24 hours a day. Numbers - 26 pieces. All of them are well prepared for settlement. They made repairs. The bathroom includes toiletries, bathrobes and towels. You do not have to pay extra for breakfast, as it is included in the price. Sauna can accommodate up to 10 people. Near the hotel are the mosque and the Ascension Cathedral. The airport from the hotel can be reached in 15-20 minutes.

For the sauna you will need to pay 1 thousand rubles. Equipped smoking area. Rooms are cleaned every day. There is a fax and camera for storing things. The shared living room has a TV. The staff speaks Russian and English.

Hotel Mildom

Average price per day at Mildom Hotel: 4 thousand rubles.

The hotel positions itself as a home hostel. Settlement occurs around the clock. There is internet. Since the hotel is located near the Medeo complex, it is in demand. The rooms have a toilet, shower and sink. A TV is installed, there is heating and air conditioning. There is all the necessary furniture. The beds are comfortable. In the complex there is a cafe, restaurant. You can order room service or food delivery. Chefs are familiar with local cuisine, Russian, Caucasian and European. You can order diet meals. For businessmen, they offer a small conference room where you can hold an event, briefing or meeting. A gym, shops and supermarkets are located next to the hotel.

There is a terrace, parking. The hotel provides skiing opportunities. Every day the rooms are cleaned. Smoking areas are equipped. For a separate price, you can "activate" VIP services. It is free to hand over shoes for cleaning. The rooms are soundproofed. Near the hotel there are exchangers where you can change the currency to local. Bicycle rental is allowed.

Hotel "Aksunkar"

Average price per day: 2500 rubles.

This Almaty hotel is located near the airport. There is air conditioning and internet. There is a bar. Parking is available for guests. Some rooms have a seating area. Installed TVs are connected to the satellite. Some rooms have a private bathroom and some have a shared bathroom. The reception is open 24 hours a day.

On the territory there is a bar, ATM. The shared living room has a TV. Equipped smoking area. Rooms are cleaned every day. A transfer service is available. There is an elevator. A hairdresser is open.

Hostel Amigo

Average price per day: 1 thousand rubles.

On the territory of this hotel in Almaty there is parking. It is free for all guests. The front desk is open 24/7. A ski resort is 19 km away. The State Museum is 1.2 km away. The airport is 14 km away.

There is a vending machine that sells drinks. Shared kitchen and living room are equipped with everything necessary. Laundry open. There is a fax. Cameras for storing personal belongings and luggage are installed. Non-smoking rooms are available. Shuttle services are available at an extra charge.

Guest house "Visit"

Average price per day: 1500 rubles.

This hotel in Almaty allows you to come with pets. There is a TV, air conditioning, kettle. Internet is free. The reception is open 24 hours a day. A sports complex is 20 km away and a ski resort is 23 km away. The airport is 17 km away.

There is a garden, parking, a shared kitchen. Conducted air conditioning and heating. You can order food in the room. There are places for smoking. Ironing and laundry services are available. The check-in and check-out process can be accelerated if desired. The staff speaks not only Russian and English, but also German.

Hotel "Continental"

Average price per day: 1 thousand rubles.

Another hotel in Almaty, it is located in the business district. Guests can play billiards or use the sauna. The rooms are comfortable, as there is everything you need. The bathroom includes a hairdryer, slippers, bathrobe and towels. Meals are not included in the price, so you have to visit a local restaurant or cafe. On the ground floor, a night club is open where you can have fun and drink. Nearby is an entertainment center. The train station is only 10 minutes, 30 minutes to the airport.
