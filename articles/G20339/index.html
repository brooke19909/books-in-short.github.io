Holidays in Tunisia: Best Travel Deals

Holidays in Tunisia will especially appeal to those who like to combine the tranquility of beach bliss with active excursion programs. The local hotels offer decent service for a very reasonable fee.



Tours in Tunisia
If you have ready documents for traveling abroad, then you can not just book a tour program, but also take advantage of a special offer and purchase last-minute trips to Tunisia. At the same time, the quality of your vacation will not be different from ordinary tours, and the low cost will be a pleasant gift for you on vacation.

To travel to Tunisia, a visa is not required, therefore, the preparation of all necessary documents does not take much time. In addition, the flight to the resting place itself is quite short, which makes the trip completely tireless. Last-minute tours to Tunisia are almost always available during the season, so they are easy to find and purchase.

You can book tours at the offices of travel agencies or using the highly relevant and convenient offer of booking vouchers via the Internet by sending an application for a tour online. The latter option is absolutely reliable, so resorting to it, you will save a lot of time. about the country

Tunisia is located in North Africa, in the very center of the Mediterranean. Beautiful nature with stunningly picturesque bays, groves of orange trees, palm trees and jasmine will amaze you with a harmonious combination with endless sands.

Tunisia is kilometers of clean beaches, rich ancient history and interesting sightseeing trips. Here you will visit the ancient cities that preserve the history of Roman civilization, personally see the streets of Ancient Carthage, can get acquainted with the historical ruins of the Amphitheater, the mausoleum of the Kharasanids, the Capitol, walk through magnificent palaces and look into the Byzantine churches. Holidays in Tunisia will not leave you indifferent.

The country is religious, but Muslim bans do not very much leave an imprint on everyday life. The local currency is Tunisian dinars, which are strictly forbidden to be exported from the country. Most of the population speaks Arabic and French. Time in Tunisia is two hours behind Moscow.

Holiday Conditions

Tunisia hotels are modern and very comfortable. They are a new building of the European type. Most hotels are located on the first coastline, has a landscaped landscaped area. Many hotels offer vacationers medical and beauty treatments such as massage, mud therapy and the famous thalassotherapy. The last type of service here is of very high quality, and its prices are much lower than European ones.

The level of service is quite worthy, higher than in Egypt, but so far inferior to the European one. However, not enough to feel any discomfort. Rest in Tunisia is truly comfortable and pacifying.

The attitude of local residents towards tourists is much friendlier and more peaceful than in other Arab countries.

Here you will be offered a unique journey through the sands of the mystical Sahara on a camel ride. You can go surfing in Tunisia, as well as play golf quite European.



Tunisian Resorts

Holidays in Tunisia are best spent on the island of Djerba or in the town of Zarzis, which are considered the best resorts in the whole Mediterranean. Amazingly clean beaches, a clear sea, beautiful streets attract thousands of tourists here every year.

These and other resorts of the country are in tourist demand all year round. Active beach vacations begin from the end of March and continue through November, however, even in the winter, tourists come here with pleasure in order to relax and restore their health by visiting thalassotherapy centers.
