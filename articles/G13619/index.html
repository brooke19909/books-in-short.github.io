Stork Hotel 2 *: description, traveler reviews and price

The Greek island of Crete has long been loved by tourists from all over the world. Our compatriots are no exception to this rule. Travelers here are attracted by the magnificent nature, the cleanest sea, developed infrastructure, the presence of a mass of interesting historical sights, as well as hospitable residents. Hotels here can be found for every taste and size of the wallet. If you plan to stay in a comfortable place, but at the same time want to save some money, then the Stork Hotel 2 * hotel will be an excellent option for your stay. Location

The two-star Hotel Stork is located on the northern coast of the Greek island of Crete. It is located almost in the center of the resort town of Amoudara and eight kilometers from the large city of Heraklion. It is also close to Nikos Kazandakis International Airport (only 14 kilometers away). So upon arrival at the air harbor you can reach Stork Hotel 2 * in just 15-20 minutes. This will especially please travelers on holiday with young children. The hotel is only 350 meters from the nearest sandy beach. So you can reach the sea in just a few minutes.

Stork Hotel 2 * (Crete, Amoudara): description

Despite the fact that this hotel is not new, in 2014 there was a partial renovation. Thanks to what today guests expect comfortable accommodation in clean and comfortable rooms. It features an outdoor pool with sun loungers, a bar and a 24-hour reception. The hotel is great for an economy class vacation.



Housing stock

In total, the hotel has 40 rooms. There are both double apartments (possibly single occupancy), as well as rooms designed for up to four guests. All are decorated in a pleasant style and equipped with everything necessary. The rooms have a TV, refrigerator, wireless Internet, a balcony, a private bathroom. Air conditioning and a safe are also available at an extra cost. For the convenience of guests, the hotel is equipped with an elevator.



Accommodation cost

Since the Stork Hotel 2 * hotel belongs to the category of economy class, many can afford to stay in it. So, the cost of accommodation for 10 days in July here is from 25 thousand rubles for a double room, from 34 thousand rubles for apartments for four and from 17 thousand rubles for a room for one. Prices include breakfast. For lunch and dinner, guests can find taverns and cafes in the vicinity of the hotel.

Stork Hotel 2 *: reviews of tourists from Russia

Since today most travelers, before booking a room in a particular hotel, study not only its official description and listen to the recommendations of travel agents, but also try to independently find the comments of people who have already been here, we will make this process a bit easier for you by giving a general opinion of our compatriots staying during their holidays at the Stork Hotel (Amoudara, Crete). First of all, we can say that the vast majority of tourists really liked it. In their opinion, the hotel is fully consistent with its value. But first things first.



As for the rooms in the Stork Hotel 2 *, they are small, but there is enough space here. They are decorated simply but nicely. Furniture and plumbing are in good condition. Everything works without interruption. Tourists also note that not all bathrooms are equipped with showers. Some apartments have a simple tray with a curtain. This is due to the fact that the restoration of the hotel, which began last year, has not yet covered all the rooms. But in any case, such a detail did not cause any complaints. There were no interruptions in water, according to travelers. Rooms were cleaned every day. However, according to our compatriots, it cannot be called too thorough.

Speaking generally about the hotel, many travelers characterize it as homely and very comfortable. Guests enjoyed spending time by the pool and in the bar. By the way, the guests really liked the pool. Since it is big enough and deep. However, there is also a small section designed for children.

Regarding breakfast at Stork Hotel 2 * (Crete), the majority of guests agreed that the food here is quite tasty. Despite the fact that the range of products offered is not very large, but there is always great pastries, yogurts, cheeses, sausages, fruits, etc. In addition, every day the choice is slightly different.

As for the location, the guests were satisfied with it. So, in the vicinity of the hotel there are lots of taverns and cafes where you can have very tasty lunch and dinner. There are also many small shops and souvenir shops. A couple of kilometers from the Stork Hotel (accessible by bus) there is a large supermarket where you can purchase everything you need.

The beach, according to our compatriots, can be reached in 10-15 minutes. It is equipped with everything necessary.

And, of course, most experienced travelers advise all vacationers in Crete not to limit themselves to beach vacations, but to travel to interesting places. Moreover, according to them, it is better to do this by renting a car.
