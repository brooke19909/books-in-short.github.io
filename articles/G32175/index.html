Hotel "Novotel" in Sheremetyevo

As soon as a guest crosses the threshold of the Novotel Hotel in Sheremetyevo, street anxiety and buzz instantly disappear. The hotel is located very close to Sheremetyevo Airport. The huge building attracts the eye with its original facade, made in a modern style. The interior of the rooms, hall and restaurant is thought out to the smallest detail, because the hotel receives guests from all over the world. High level service, modernly equipped rooms, a varied menu of the restaurant and bar await travelers at Novotel in Sheremetyevo.





Guest rooms

The hotel has 493 comfortable rooms that are equipped with an LCD TV, safe, iron and ironing board, as well as a paid minibar. Spacious rooms have high sound and heat insulation properties. For the convenience of your stay, guests can use the free WI-FI.

Standard rooms are comfortable and clean rooms with comfortable sleeping places. Hotel guests are offered chilled water, and if desired, you can order tea or coffee in your room. A bathrobe and slippers are provided.





Superior rooms feature larger beds. Also in the rooms there are small sofas, and in the working area there is a computer desk. For a certain price, you can order a special cot for a child. The bathroom includes all toiletries, clean towels, a bathrobe and slippers. Each bathroom is equipped with a hairdryer and a mirror.





Deluxe rooms are the largest rooms in comparison with others. The room consists of several rooms that perfectly zone the space. The suite has the necessary utensils, as well as a Nespresso coffee maker and a kettle for self-service drinks. For a comfortable stay, the room has an alarm clock, air conditioning.





Halls for conferences and celebrations

The Novotel Hotel in Sheremetyevo has 6 conference rooms that allow holding meetings, corporate meetings and banquets. The halls are equipped with the latest audio and video equipment. The Novotel Hotel in Sheremetyevo offers the following services: high speed Internet, printing and copying of documents, simultaneous translation for foreign guests, services of a loader, screen and overhead projector.





Services

For the safety of Novotel visitors, each room, regardless of the convenience category, is equipped with doors with locks with a rotary handle, sprinklers, eyes, and the rooms are equipped with a fire system and emergency exit lights. The hotel has 24 hour room security.
