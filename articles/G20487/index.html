Rating and reviews about hotel GM Doc Let Beach Resort 4 *

If you want to spend a vacation on the sea, in a quiet place, away from the noise of a big city, then as a place to relax, we suggest you consider the four- star GM Doc Let Beach Resort in Vietnam.



Location

This hotel is located in the southern part of Van Fong Bay. Nha Trang is 45 kilometers away. Kamran Airport is an hour and a half drive away. The hotel provides a free shuttle to Nha Trang twice a week. If you want to use it, you must inform the reception in advance.



Description

This hotel is called by many tourists who have been here a great place for a quiet measured rest with family or friends. It has a not very large, but well-groomed green area. The housing stock of the hotel is located in six villas. In total there are 98 deluxe rooms with a view of the garden, pool or sea. The apartments are spacious and equipped with everything necessary (furniture, TV, minibar, bathroom, air conditioning, tea set, balcony). Throughout the hotel guests can use free Wi-Fi.

The hotel has a large outdoor pool, next to which there is a sun terrace with sun loungers and parasols. Guests have access to the local spa and gym. There is a restaurant on the beach serving breakfast, lunch and dinner. There is also a bar with a wide selection of drinks and snacks.



GM Doc Let Beach Resort & Spa (Nha Trang): Russian traveler reviews

Many seasoned tourists, while preparing for the next trip abroad, try to collect as much information as possible about the proposed vacation destination. After all, this allows you to avoid a lot of problems and difficulties. Particular attention should be paid to the choice of the hotel in which you plan to stay during your overseas vacation. Indeed, for sure, even unpretentious tourists would like to be able to live in a comfortable room with everything you need. Therefore, travelers do not spare time and effort to search and study the reviews of those who have already happened to visit this or that place. This allows you to form a closer to reality view of a particular hotel. To save time, we bring to your attention the generalized impressions of tourists from Russia about their vacation in the four-star hotel GM Doc Let Beach Resort (Vietnam, Nha Trang). Ahead of events, we note that the rating for this hotel is quite high and is 4.1 points out of 5 possible. This suggests that the vast majority of guests were satisfied with their choice. But we learn more about the opinions of our compatriots.



Rooms

In general, our compatriots liked the apartments in this hotel. According to them, they are all very spacious, pleasantly decorated. All rooms have furnished balconies. The apartments are located in six villas. Tourists note that they all have a good location. The only thing, some guests would not advise settling in the first villa, since it is located near the road. If you sleep lightly, then the noise of cars can disturb you somewhat. The rest, according to vacationers, everywhere in the hotel is quite quiet and calm, so nothing will interfere with your vacation, day or night.

As for the equipment of the rooms, our compatriots assure that they have everything that is needed. True, furniture and appliances cannot be called new and modern. However, everything is quite high quality and in working condition. If something fails, then report it to the reception, and the problem will be fixed very quickly. A big plus, according to many guests, is the fact that throughout the GM Doc Let Beach Resort and Spa there is the ability to use wireless Internet. Moreover, Wi-Fi here is free, and the connection speed, according to our compatriots, is usually quite high.



Cleaning service

Judging by the comments they left, the guests had no particular complaints about the work of the hotel maids. So, according to our compatriots, they cleaned here regularly and fairly high quality. Maids renewed towels every day, and bed linen a couple of times a week. If necessary, you could ask to replace the laundry at any time.

As for the staff as a whole, the guests say that the hotel staff is very welcoming, friendly and attentive. The main words and phrases in Russian are known by most of the employees. With the rest, you can easily communicate in basic English.

Sea
