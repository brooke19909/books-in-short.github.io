Climate

If you purchased a tour to Velaa Private Island Maldives, then you are naturally interested in what the weather is like. I must say that it is never cold here. The climate of the Maldives is warm and humid, but not hot. The maximum air temperature reaches +32 degrees, but due to humidity it is very difficult to breathe. As for water, it almost always warms up to +26 degrees. It rains from May to October. This is the period of the southwest monsoon. From November to February, when the northeastern monsoon dominates, it is less humid. In a word, it is best to go on holiday to the Maldives just when it is winter.
