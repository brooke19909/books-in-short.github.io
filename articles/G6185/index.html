Guangzhou Safari Park: an unusual zoo

Guangzhou is the largest city in South China, famous for its huge number of attractions and places of interest to visit. One of them is the only Guangzhou safari park - Chimelong. It has a huge area - about 130 hectares, which is inhabited by more than 300 species of various animals.

What makes Chimelong Safari Park special?

Guangzhou safari park itself is more like a huge reserve, here animals are not kept in cages, but enjoy life behind fences in large enclosures.

The zoo is divided into two zones: the first is a safari zone, where visitors are transported around the territory in peculiar steam locomotives consisting of several small wagons; here you can also rent a special car or drive your own, having made an independent trip; the second is the pedestrian zone.

The safari zone is divided into parts according to the natural regions of animal habitat. Here you can not only see animals living in the open air from very close range, but even touch and feed some of them.

Safari area

On the territory of this zone animals are kept practically without barriers. Some of them can be found freely moving along the road, for example, kangaroos or llamas.

Near each animal, the engine stops for a couple of minutes so that tourists can take pictures, but getting out of transport is prohibited. It is best to take places in the last carriage - this will provide an opportunity for an additional review.

The habitats of carnivorous and large animals are fenced by a moat and tensioned wire.

In the safari zone you can see elephants, camels, tigers, lions, cheetahs, hippos, bears, rhinos, giraffes, storks and other large species of animals and birds.

A trip on the route of one tram takes about half an hour, but to see all the animals, you may need several of these trips.

Pedestrian zone

The most numerous inhabitants of the territory are monkeys. They can be seen not only in aviaries, but also climbing trees above their heads. You can also buy food for them and feed.

The next largest are birds, especially parrots. Very interesting to visit at home for newborns. Here you can see the recently hatched chicks, monkey babies, tiger cubs, cubs and others. Also see the animals that are in the safari zone, but here they are in aviaries, and almost any of them can be fed.

For children there is a zone with pets - goats, hens, horses and others. There are rides nearby.

Zoo show

Another difference between the Guangzhou Safari Park and the regular zoo is that it hosts various animal shows:

  • tigers;
  • elephants;
  • birds
  • hippos;
  • monkeys and others.

The program includes not only feeding animals, but also performing some tricks, dances and performances.

In the park you can see a mountain of fruits and flowers and much more.

Guangzhou Safari Park: how to get there?

Where it is located? There are two ways to get to Guangzhou Zoo:

  • by bus: routes No. 288, 288A, 301, 301A, 304, 305 and 309 - you need to get off at the Xiangjiang Zoo Station stop;
  • by metro: line 3, Hanxi Changlong station.

It is more convenient to get on the subway, both from Guangzhou itself and directly from the airport or Guangzhou East Railway Station. There passes a direct branch, following to the desired station. At each station you can find a detailed diagram of the nearby territory. You need to proceed to exit E. Every 15 minutes there is a free shuttle that goes directly to the entrance to the zoo. You can not wait for the shuttle, but take a walk on foot - the road will take only 10 minutes.

Opening hours and ticket prices

Entrance to the zoo is open every day from 9:30 to 18:00.

Tickets for adults are 250 yuan and 175 yuan for children. This is about 2400 and 1600 rubles, respectively. If the day of visiting the park coincides with any of the holidays, then the price of tickets will be slightly higher - 300 yuan for adults and 210 yuan for children. Translated into rubles - 2900 and 2000. For children whose height does not exceed 1 m, admission is free. Prices are relevant for the summer of 2018.

What to see in Guangzhou

Urgent question of the tourist. Suppose you have already visited Guangzhou Safari Park and Chimelong Complex. The latter, by the way, includes not only the zoo, but also a water park, an amusement park, an international circus, a separate bird park. Now you may have a question about what to see in Guangzhou yet. The main attractions of this city include:

  1. The central square of HUACHENG SQUARE is a green alley more than 1.5 kilometers long, located among skyscrapers in the very center of the city. There are a large number of other buildings recommended for visiting: Opera and Museum of Guangzhou, a library, an underground shopping center and others. Ideal for lovers of walks and modern architecture.
  2. Shamyan Island is a historical monument of the period when this territory was a European colony.
  3. Chen Family Academy is an example of 19th century architecture. It contains various works of art - ivory sculptures and statues, made by famous masters of their craft.
  4. Yuan is an unusual building.
  5. Sun Yat-sen Memorial - built in honor of the father of the Chinese revolution.
  6. Dafo Temple is one of several famous Buddhist temples.
  7. Guangzhou TV Tower is China 's tallest building.
  8. Lotus mountains are a very picturesque place with ancient buildings and unique statues.

Guangzhou is a beautiful city with an amazing safari park and other interesting places.
