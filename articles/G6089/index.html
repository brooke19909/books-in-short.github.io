Oven "Electrolux"

A gas or electric stove is a constant attribute of the kitchen. It is important for housewives who devote a lot of time to cooking food to choose the right equipment, take into account its efficiency, ease of use, and functionality. A good choice may be the oven "Electrolux", which will occupy an important place in the kitchen.

Specifications

The electric oven, manufactured by Electrolux, is a built-in kitchen equipment, it is multifunctional, equipped with a ring heating element. An oven provides a professional level of cleanliness. And you do not need to use special scrapers and cleaning products. This is possible thanks to a special self-cleaning button, which allows you to quickly remove food residues from the stove. The oven itself can remind you with a signal that it needs to be cleaned.





Every housewife will be pleased by purchasing the Electrolux oven, because it allows you to show culinary talents, equipped with convenient touch controls. The menu contains ninety recipes, help, there are eighteen heating modes. On the LCD display you can monitor the temperature, power level and cooking time. The oven allows you to choose the most optimal cooking program for you and start it with one button "My program". In addition, it is possible to independently create your own programs. The volume of the oven is seventy-four liters. Doors open smoothly and silently.





Using the oven "Electrolux", you can get amazing dishes that will be perfectly baked from all sides. Moreover, you can put the cooked dish at any level of the oven, it will warm up evenly, thanks to the fan and the air duct. To the listed advantages of the oven, you can add its energy efficiency, in which it even surpasses class A appliances in energy consumption. This effect is facilitated by the presence of excellent thermal insulation in the inner chamber, which allows it to retain heat, as well as a door with laminated glass. Due to this, the oven "Electrolux" receives additional protection that allows you to save heat. Using the device is very convenient and safe. After all, his door during cooking remains cool and does not burn his hands. If there are children in the family, there is no fear that they will be able to turn on the oven on their own without the knowledge of adults. There is a special function "Protection against children." The oven of the Electrolux company also has additional functions that allow, for example, to determine the degree of readiness of a dish. For this purpose, a thermal probe is used.
