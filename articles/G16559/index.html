Panorama of Anapa - a view familiar to many

The picturesque panorama of Anapa is familiar to many residents of our country - almost everyone rested there, at least once. In the Soviet Union, this resort was extremely popular for holidays with children. In its vicinity there were many pioneer camps, which attracted children from all over the country.

The resort city is well located at the junction of the foothills of the Caucasus and the Kuban fields. It is here that nature is rich in small picturesque hills and beautiful flowering fields. The panorama of Anapa is complemented by beautiful sandy beaches, where guests of the city and its residents have a great time. Anapa is located on the shore of a cozy bay, the water in which warms up well, and a shallow depth allows you to safely spend time at sea. The total length of sand and pebble beaches is more than forty kilometers.





A huge number of boarding houses, camps, recreation facilities are located in the resort city of Anapa. The panorama map of the surroundings is also rich in sanatoriums in which guests from all over the country can improve their health. The Tambukan Peninsula has long been famous for its local mud, whose healing properties have a positive effect on diseases of the musculoskeletal system. In recent years, resort attendance has been growing steadily. More and more people come to Anapa with their whole family to enjoy their vacation on the Black Sea.

In addition to excellent beach vacations and restoration of health, for tourists there are many interesting places both in the vicinity and in the city of Anapa. The street panorama conceals many interesting restaurants and institutions with local cuisine and amazing seafood dishes. Small shops offer a large selection of souvenirs. The city market is famous for its low prices and an excellent assortment of vegetables and fruits filled with sun and vitamins.





Anapa's panorama would not have been complete without the main attraction and favorite place of local residents and visitors - Anapa lighthouse on the embankment. Here, loving girls and boys are very fond of spending time attracted by the stunning views and indescribable marine atmosphere.
