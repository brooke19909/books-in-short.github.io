Hotel "Orbita" (Minsk): address, description, reviews

Hotel "Orbita" (Minsk) was built in 1991. Outwardly, it looks like a high-rise building of the 90s, built in the style of constructivism (elongated windows, flat roof). Architects designed spacious living spaces in it. They were repaired on time, modernized, cleaned.





The number of rooms of the 14-story building totals 276. The interior design of a well-groomed hotel is much more interesting than the design of its facade and the back. The hotel staff adheres to high standards of service guests. The level of service and periodic updating of internal design, communications, office equipment and plumbing contributed to the assignment of a three-star level to this hotel in 2005.

Location

The guests of Belarus who came for excursions, as well as those sent by their organizations to its capital, have long loved the Orbita Hotel (Minsk). The address of this comfortable and hospitable hotel is 39, Pushkin Ave., Belarus, 220092, Belarus. If you find the hotel on the map, you will see that it is located in the western part of the city, near the Pushkinskaya metro station

About hotel

This purely urban hotel is one of the iconic buildings in the vicinity of the Pushkinskaya metro station. The hotel complex looks harmonious with the surrounding buildings. At its core, it is a comfortable portal to the tourist city of Minsk. Hotel "Orbit" is able to comfortably accommodate more than 400 people in its rooms.

The hotel has created comfortable conditions based on a wide variety of client tastes and preferences. For the comfort of some tourists, privacy is desirable, others, on the contrary, want to feel in the center of a busy metropolis. The first to recommend more quiet rooms, which overlook the courtyard. For the second, the most desirable rooms are the upper floors, from whose windows a stunning cityscape opens.





Hotel "Orbita" (Minsk) is an inexpensive hotel, but providing its guests with rooms from economy to VIP class. Tourists in their reviews mark it as the most attractive in the "three stars" category by the criterion of "price / quality".
