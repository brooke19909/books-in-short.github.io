Market "Gardener": how to get from different points of Moscow

One of the largest modern shopping complexes in Moscow is the market "Gardener". Here is a huge assortment of various goods, a wide selection of items from leading Russian and foreign manufacturers, moreover, at the lowest possible prices. You can buy goods here both at retail and in bulk. Every day, hundreds of people seek to visit the market "Gardener". Not everyone knows how to get to this place from various points in Moscow. But before dwelling on this issue, it is recommended that you familiarize yourself with the shopping center in more detail.



Market description

Shopping complex "Gardener" consists of three main sectors. The first is the clothing market. In this sector, you can purchase shoes, clothes, wedding dresses and all kinds of accessories. A wide selection of fur and leather products is offered here. The second sector offers products for gardeners and gardeners. In this part of the market you can buy seedlings of fruit trees and shrubs, all kinds of seeds of vegetables and flowers. Along with the known seedlings, rare exotic plants are offered here. In winter, fir and pine are traded. The third sector sells various birds, animals, fish and mammals. At this site, you can purchase all the necessary funds to care for them. In addition, in the market you can buy various building materials. Despite the wide popularity and popularity that the market "Gardener" has, how to get to it, many do not know. It should be noted right away that it is located on the 14th km of the Moscow Ring Road on the Upper Fields.

Market "Gardener": how to get there using public transport

The shopping complex can be reached by bus or taxi from five metro stations, including Bratislavskaya. A minibus number 165 and number 10 passes here. In addition, there is a bus passing the station that takes you directly to the market. If you need to go from the Vykhino metro station, you need to take the minibus No. 136 or take the bus following route No. 956. In addition, there is a free bus from Vykhino to the market, the stop of which is not difficult to find. If you exit the subway and become facing the square, then a bus to the left is left to exit. When there is no bus, a barker is working loudly here, who invites people to get to Sadovod free of charge. The interval between departures is 20-30 minutes, depending on how quickly the empty seats fill up in the transport. You need to know that free flights are only available until 19.00.



But what if you are at the Domodedovo metro station and you need to visit the Sadovod (market)? How to get from this station? A minibus taxi number 165 passes here, which goes directly to the shopping center (stop opposite).

You can also get to the market from the Kuzminki metro station. Here runs a flight of several fixed-route taxis and buses. So, if you turn left and then left again in the underpass, then at the exit you can easily see the area near which the McDonald's rises. Near it is a stop for minibuses. You need to take a minibus number 875. If you go a little ahead, to the first bus stop, you can catch a bus following route number 655. From the Kuzminka metro station, take bus number 955, which goes to the Mega Belaya Dacha restaurant , across the street from which there is a "Gardener". Minibus number 866 follows this route.

From the Lyublino metro station, you can also get to the Sadovod market. How to get from this point in Moscow? You can take a minibus number 553, number 315 and number 27. In addition, a free shuttle bus runs from the station to the shopping center.



How to get to the market by car?

For those who need to purchase bulk items in large quantities, a wide choice is provided by the Sadovod clothing market. How to get to it by public transport, you already know. But the market can be reached by car. The shopping complex has a single passage, which is located on the MKAD side. If you go from the place where the Moscow Ring Road intersects with Volgogradsky Prospekt, then the entrance to the market is located after the MKAD junction with Verkhnyaya Polya Street (inner side). If you call in at the place where the Moscow Ring Road intersects with Kashirsky Highway, then this will be the outside. You must go the same way as the first option.
