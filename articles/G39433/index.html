Sardinia, Alghero: attractions, weather, beaches, hotels

Holidays in Sardinia - a real adventure, exciting and unforgettable. This paradise is ready to open its arms to all those who like to bask on the beach, go hiking or cycling, go in for water sports and make excursions to the most wonderful corners of the planet.

Every year more and more tourists come here who want to spend at least a few days in a wonderful paradise. The island offers many resort areas to choose from and a huge amount of entertainment.

Island of sardinia

What is Sardinia? This is the island of the Mediterranean, the island part of Italy, which is famous for its mild climate, almost two hundred meters of beaches and comfortable rest. The northeastern part of the island is a prestigious area where the houses and villas of the most famous and wealthy people from around the world are located.



According to an ancient and very beautiful story, the island of Sardinia appeared from the footprint of the great Creator when he stepped into the water. The resulting piece of land had a bizarre shape, and unusually beautiful nature appeared on its surface: white sandy beaches, flower fields, crystal clear lakes and lush greenery. That is how this corner appears before all those who decide to visit it. Alghero is on the list of cities that you should definitely visit on the island.

Alghero City, Sardinia: Location

Alghero is located in the province of Sassari, its population reaches 44 thousand people. This is one of the main cities of Sardinia, as there is an international airport.

The coastline of the city is called Riviera del Corallo - "Coral Riviera". Here lives a large colony of red corals, which made the city of Alghero famous far beyond Italy. The collection and sale of corals are of great importance in the life of Sardinia - both economic and cultural.

Weather

Alghero extends on the very shore of the sea, so the climate here is quite mild, and two capes (Punto Giglio and Capo Caccia) reliably protect the city from strong winds.

In winter, the weather in Alghero is quite warm: the average temperature often stops at -10 o C. In summer, the thermometer rises to +22 o C.

The nature of the city and its environs

The rich landscape is represented by cliffs with sharp and flat stones, sandy beaches, luxurious Mediterranean vegetation. Vast olive groves grow around the town.

The territory of Cape Capo Caccio is popular due to the large number of caves and gorges, most of which are still poorly studied. The most famous of them is the cave of Neptune with hundreds of stalactites and stalagmites. The territory of this cape has recently turned into a reserve.



The shores of Sardinia in Alghero are in great demand among divers. Diving allows you to see the incredible beauty of the underwater world: corals, eels, moray eels, huge lobsters and many other underwater inhabitants. Even the most sophisticated divers will surely want to see with their own eyes the Nereus Grotto - the largest sea cave in all of Europe.

Historical Monuments

After exploring the natural beauties of the island, be sure to visit the famous historical sites that will tell you about the rich cultural heritage of Sardinia.

  • Cathedral of Alghero. This building is located in the historic city center and is considered its main building. It is often called the symbol and main attraction of Alghero. The cathedral was built in the XVI century. The building is made in the style of the late Renaissance, and the interior is fascinating and delights with its grandeur and luxury.

  • Church of St. Michael. The famous Chiesa di San Michele, like the previous cathedral, is located in the historic city center. It was first mentioned in the XIV century, and now has become one of the local attractions. Its hallmark is an octagonal dome, fully covered with bright colored ceramic tiles.




  • Nurag Palmavera (megalithic complex). A few kilometers from the city of Alghero is a monument of the Bronze and Iron Age. The oldest part of this building is the central limestone tower.

Beaches Alghero

A beach holiday is one of the main reasons why thousands of tourists come to Sardinia every year. Sassari Province (where Alghero is located) is famous for its luxurious beaches. Italians and residents of European countries like to come here. Quite often you can meet Russian-speaking travelers here, as many tour operators are actively promoting this tourist destination.

There are several beaches on the Coral Riviera in Alghero.

  • Spiaggia del Lido. Central city beach. The coast here is covered with fine pure golden sand, the water is crystal clear. This option is perfect for families with children - the entrance to the water is gentle.

  • Maria Pia. This beach is located somewhat south of the previous one. Here, too, you can relax on the fine sand. This place is suitable for those who want to relax without fuss, savages. However, there are well-equipped swimming areas.

  • Burantin. This beach is 5 km north of Alghero. In addition to fine golden sand and warm waves, Burantin is famous for its bizarre shape, very reminiscent of the heart.

Regardless of which beach the tourists choose, the excellent mood and a lot of entertainment are guaranteed. On the beach you can go yachting, go boating or jet skiing, scuba diving, and try surfing. There are many bars on the beaches where you can enjoy a cocktail and have a bite to eat.



Here you can rent equipment for water sports. Moreover, an experienced instructor will certainly teach everyone to dive or surf. Even if the vacationer is going to stand on the board for the first time, he will certainly expect success and a lot of impressions.

Entertainment

After relaxing on the beach and trips to the sights, tourists will surely look for what to see in Alghero yet. There is enough entertainment here. For a family trip, it is recommended to visit Luna Park, which is located on the territory of the port.

Children will be delighted with going to the aquarium - this wonderful museum of fauna is located in the city. Here you will see huge turtles, moray eels, poisonous stone fish, sharks and many more unusual tropical fish.

You can have a great time on horseback riding, traveling on a yacht or canoe.

There are also entertainment for adults. On Sulis Square there are many night clubs and incendiary night discos. Particular attention should be paid to the clubs: Maracaibo Cocktail Bar and Bar Gilbert. Here you can not only have fun, but also try great cocktails.

Alghero Hotels

Before deciding where to stay in Alghero, select your preferred comfort level and calculate your budget. Remember that the cost of living is affected by the distance to the beach and the city center. It is also important what features of the hotel the hotel offers (all-inclusive, breakfast only, no meals).



If the task is to relax inexpensively, it makes sense to choose three-star hotels in Sardinia in Alghero.

  • So, accommodation at Hotel Villa Piras is estimated at about 90 euros. This hotel is located in a quiet area, only a 20-minute walk to the city center.

  • Hotel Domominore also offers budget rooms (about 80 euros). This option is suitable for those who want to settle near the airport. In this case, it may be inconvenient to have a long distance to the city center.

  • Hotel Pa Blanc offers rooms with an en-suite bathroom and a shared kitchen. This option of accommodation will appeal to couples with children, because in the kitchen you can always cook a suitable dish for the child.

More comfortable rooms are offered by 4-star hotels. In these apartments, the cost per night varies between 130-170 euros. Among the most popular:

  • Hotel Corte Rosada;

  • Hotel Portoconte;

  • Hotel Oasis (located near Marina Pia Beach).

Anyone who is accustomed to relax with chic will surely appreciate 5-star hotels with top-level service. The price of accommodation per night is from 190 euros. The list of such hotels included:

  • Carlos V Hotel;

  • Villa Las Tronas Hotel & Spa.

As a rule, such hotels offer all-inclusive services, so guests are unlikely to lack anything.



Holidays in Alghero (as well as throughout the island of Sardinia) can be called a real trip to paradise. In addition to the mild climate and breathtaking landscapes, tourists will find entertainment for every taste and budget.
