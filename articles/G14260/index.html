Making an original and beautiful gift - champagne and candy pineapple

Sweets and champagne are a traditional gift set. This set is good in that it can be presented to both women and men for any occasion. But you must admit that giving just a bag with a bottle of sparkling wine and a box of sweets is so banal, simple and not at all original. A gift should not only please, but also pleasantly surprise. In this article, we will reveal the secret of how to turn a classic gift set into an interesting, beautiful and unique surprise. Today we will learn how to make pineapple from champagne and sweets. This product can become not only an exclusive present, but also the main decoration of the festive table. It looks elegant, spectacular and rich. Are we getting started?



Learning to make pineapple from sweets and champagne: we prepare the necessary materials

To complete the composition we need:

  • a bottle of champagne;
  • sweets in yellow or golden wrappers with one flat side (in the form of a hemisphere or pyramid);
  • green and yellow corrugated paper;
  • double-sided tape;
  • yellow satin or paper ribbon;
  • transparent wrapping paper;
  • brown or yellow sisal fiber;
  • scissors.




Decoration of sweets

Want to learn how to make champagne and candy pineapple? Read further instructions. We unfold a roll of yellow corrugated paper and cut a rectangle of 20 centimeters wide. We fold it twice in half, so that a square is obtained. Cut the part along the fold lines. As a result, we have square blanks. On the flat side of the candy we attach a small piece of double-sided tape. Then glue the sweetness in the center of one paper blank. We make out all the candies in the same way. Now we fix the pieces of adhesive tape on the back of the squares. Sweet items are decorated.

Bottle decoration

We continue to perform the composition "Pineapple from champagne and sweets." We proceed to gluing the bottle with sweet blanks. We begin to do this from the bottom of the tank. On paper parts, remove the protective layer from the adhesive tape and fasten them close to each other in a circle. Thus we decorate the entire bottle, right up to the neck. Glue the candies tightly one next to the other, while twisting the edges of the paper squares.

Making greens

To make a pineapple from a bottle of champagne look realistic, you need to attach leaves. For these purposes we will use green corrugated paper. Cut a large rectangle and wrap it around the neck of the bottle in several layers. Next, we perform oblong-shaped leaves with a pointed one end. Using double-sided tape, we fasten these parts to the bottom edge. The inner leaves are placed close to the neck of the container, and the outer ones are slightly bent to the sides.

The final stage in the manufacture of the composition

The place where the last row of sweets ends and the foliage begins to fasten, we decorate with sisal fiber, wrapping it around the bottle.

From transparent wrapping paper we cut a large blank of a square shape. We spread it on the table, in the center we put our "pineapple". Turn the edges of the paper up and align. We bandage them with a ribbon, forming a package around the composition. We tie a beautiful bow. Champagne and candy pineapple is ready!

Caramels as a basis for composition

This product can be decorated with sweets of a different shape. In this part of the article we will tell you how to decorate a container with ordinary caramels. For work, we need a thin wire (floristic or plain). We will connect sweets with it. To do this, cut the wire into pieces of 5-6 centimeters. We twist the "tails" of two sweet elements. Then we fasten the third one to the second candy and so on. We form a chain of caramels. Its length should correspond to the circumference of the bottle along the bottom edge. When the sweet garland is ready, we attach it to the container. To do this, glue a strip of double-sided tape (in a circle) on the bottom of the bottle. Next, remove the second protective layer from it and catch sweets. In the same way, we carry out the second chain of sweets and fasten it. Thus, we decorate the bottle with caramel to the very top. We form foliage in the same way as described above.

Packing: what options can be?



We are talking about the technology of making the composition "Pineapple" from sweets. How to do it, you already know, and will be able to do it yourself. But so that the original gift made of sweets and champagne does not lose its presentable appearance during transportation, make sure that the packaging is reliable. At the beginning of the article, one of the ways to design a composition in transparent wrapping paper is already described. We suggest you familiarize yourself with one more option, maybe you will like it more. In order to not only reliably, but also beautifully arrange the product packaging, you will need a basket with low sides and a green floral grid. From the last we cut out a large rectangle and put it in a basket. In the center we put the composition. Raise the edges of the grid to the top of the pineapple and easily grab them with a ribbon. In the basket, if the place allows, you can put bright fruits: tangerines, bananas, lemons. Having come to visit and handing a gift to the hero of the occasion, you just have to watch his eyes fill with delight as he unties the ribbon.
