Apatity city, hotels: description, reviews of tourists and photos

In the center of the Kola Peninsula, between the Khibiny Mountains and Lake Imandra , the city of Apatity is located. Mountains in Apatity - the most important object of natural attractions. For lovers of skiing and skiing there are wonderful slopes, for fans of mountaineering there are many mountaineering routes of varying degrees of difficulty. The massif itself, like a box with gems, stores about 500 minerals. The second beautiful natural attraction is Imandra - a large lake with many islands. This is a great place for fishing, kayaking, and in winter, tourists like to arrange kite races and enjoy the northern lights. For those who wish to have a good time in this wonderful city, there are several hotels in which there is everything necessary for a comfortable stay.

Hotel Amethyst

Built in 1976, the Amethyst Hotel is waiting for its guests at ul. Lenin, house 3. "Amethyst" is considered one of the most popular hotels in Apatity. The hotel can accommodate both in single rooms, the cost of 1400 rubles per day, and in double rooms, the cost of a night in which starts from 2275 rubles. By the degree of comfort, all rooms of the Amethyst Hotel in Apatity are divided into standard, standard + and comfort. For guests there are discount systems for early booking, for the duration of the stay, as well as special price offers for groups of 10 people or more. The hotel offers beauty services, a medical center and a photo salon. Free parking is available. The reception is open 24 hours a day, non-cash and cash payment methods are possible, Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel. Pets are allowed upon request.

Reviews of tourists about this hotel in Apatity are mostly positive. Guests note the quality of service, reasonable prices. Among the shortcomings are usually called the outdated style of room decoration. Nevertheless, it is one of the best hotels in Apatity of the Murmansk region.

Hotel "Severnaya"

On the central square of the city of Kirovsk, surrounded by the Khibiny Mountains, is the Severnaya Hotel. Apatity-1 is the nearest railway station to this Arctic center of skiers.

87 comfortable rooms equipped with everything necessary for a stay are waiting for their guests. The cost starts from 2400 rubles per day. The hotel has a 24-hour front desk, free Wi-Fi in every room. You can also use the services of a fitness center, beauty salon, laundry and spend an unforgettable evening in the restaurant, tasting European dishes. Only good reviews can be heard about this place, especially the guests note the friendly staff and delicious cuisine in the restaurant.

Hostel Sheri

From the inexpensive segment of Apatity hotels, you can consider the Sheri hostel. Here tourists are waiting for single rooms at a cost of 1100 rubles, double rooms - from 750 rubles per person, quadruple rooms - from 450 rubles per person and six-bed rooms - from 350 rubles. Guests receive a discount system. Wi-Fi is available throughout the hostel. A bathroom, a relaxation room, a kitchen and a dining room are shared, there are all the necessary equipment for cooking. Usually, guests have an idea about the hostel format, its features, but in any case, the quality of the rooms and the level of service has not been canceled. Visitors in their reviews note the friendliness and attentiveness of the staff, the corresponding price-quality ratio.

Mini-Hotel "Belorechka"

In a picturesque place near the Museum of the European North, a mini-hotel was built. For its guests, the administration has provided the following types of rooms: single with a cost of 950 rubles per day, double with a cost of 1660 rubles for two people. The rooms are equipped with everything necessary for relaxation, Wi-Fi is available on site, there is parking. Guests have the opportunity to order food and drinks, as well as breakfast in the room. There is a 24-hour front desk, car rental and shuttle services. Reviews about the mini-hotel are positive, guests note the availability of prices, highly qualified staff, as well as clean and comfortable rooms.
