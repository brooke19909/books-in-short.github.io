What is a journey? Hiking. Leisure. By car in Europe

Traveling is what accompanies humanity since the formation of the world. Throughout history, people have been in constant motion: they wanted to find a better corner, make new friends, establish economic contacts with distant countries. Today, others have been added to these tasks. What is a journey, why is a person drawn to uncharted shores, where to start an inexperienced tourist? About all this and even more - in this article.

Theoretical base

In order to answer the question of what travel is and how to prepare for it, it is necessary to delve into the meaning of the definition itself. According to him, travel is moving on land or water with the goal of studying, learning the unknown, general development, cultural enrichment. Local tasks include sports training, establishing diplomatic and friendly contacts, visiting educational places, etc.





Travel has long been a feature that has allowed people to build a full-fledged picture of the surrounding reality. Information about the surface of the planet, information about the economic structure, culture, traditions, history, population of various countries - all these are the achievements of travel and the fruits of travelers.

Golden ring (route): why you should choose and how can you go?

Traveling along the Golden Ring is an opportunity to touch the national history of Russia. The route covers cities, each of which occupies a special niche in the process of development of the state. Many of them feature attractions that are protected by an international organization of UNESCO. In addition, the trip helps to remove the fuss, get rid of the tension imposed by life in the metropolis, and plunge into the leisurely existence of the Russian provinces.

Routes in Europe

Today you can go on a trip not only in Russia, but also by car ... in Europe! To do this, you must first take care of obtaining medical insurance, international driver's licenses, as well as a special visa at the embassy of the country where entry will be made initially; subsequently, there will be no restrictions on movement between states within the European Union.





In order to go by car in Europe, you also need to plan a competent route in advance. The most popular among tourists are, for example, the following:

  • Russia, Belarus, Poland, Czech Republic, Austria. The total length of the route is 5170 km, stops in 6 cities (Minsk, Brest, Vienna, Klagenfurt, Prague, Warsaw).
  • The route from Moscow to Berlin with a length of the road of about 2000 km (you can travel for 25-30 hours, but with stops for rest, sightseeing - 4-5 days one way); Crossing 4 borders: Belarusian, Czech, Polish, German.

Regardless of where the trip will be made, the trip should be planned and thought out. It is recommended that you familiarize yourself with the laws and national characteristics of the countries where you will be entered.

Hiking





This is one of the best options to start exploring the world of travel. If there is still not enough experience and skills to travel long distances, hiking with a gradual increase in the length of the route and the complication of conditions is what will help to gain confidence in your abilities. You can start with regular trips overnight to the forest (the main thing is to make sure the chosen place is safe), and continue at the intersections of mountain ranges and hilly areas of the Urals, the Murmansk region, the Caucasus and other regions.
