Night club "Planet" (Kirov): description, address, reviews

The youth of the city of Kirov, like in many other areas of our country, loves to visit nightclubs. After all, here you can not only have a good time, but also meet interesting people. One of the most visited places in the city is the Planet Club. Description, address, opening hours, reviews about this institution - all this you can find in the article.

Club "Planet" (Kirov): description

If this is your first time in the city and you want to have an unforgettable time, then pay attention to this place. Club "Planet" occupies three floors. What is there only:

  • Stage with a dance floor.
  • The hall where you can play billiards.
  • Several bars where you can try a variety of cocktails.
  • A karaoke hall where you can choose and sing your favorite song.
  • Comfortable corners with upholstered furniture for a more relaxing holiday.
  • Large dance hall and much more.

A lot of customers like to relax near the bar. And there are a lot of reasons for this. We list only a few: here you can meet interesting people, try delicious cocktails and other alcoholic drinks, chat with the bartender and much more.

Usually in nightclubs the music sounds quite specific. Anywhere, but not at the Planet nightclub. Regular customers are well aware that here you can listen not only to modern domestic and foreign hits, but also the popular hits of the 1990-2000s.

Visitor Information

  • Club "Planet" in Kirov is located at: Moskovskaya street, 102 V.
  • From Monday to Friday the institution is open: from 09.00-20.00; Saturday and Sunday - 09.00-17.00.

Night club "Planet" (Kirov): reviews

About the institution leave a large number of comments on the expanses of the World Wide Web. We will get acquainted with some positive opinions of visitors:

  • The coolest and most fun parties are held here.
  • Delicious food at affordable prices.
  • The club "Planet" has created ideal conditions for a great holiday.
  • Modern sound equipment in karaoke allows you to brilliantly perform your favorite musical composition.
  • Pleasant interiors contribute to creating a comfortable environment for communication.

Among the shortcomings, some customers sometimes note:

  • Inconvenient work schedule.
  • Rudeness to visitors from individual customers.
  • Bad service

Fortunately, there are more positive reviews about visiting the Planet nightclub on the Internet. So feel free to take your friends, stock up on a good mood and go hang out in one of the most visited places in the city.
