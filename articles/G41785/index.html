Hotel "Victoria" (Irkutsk): address, description of rooms, service, reviews

Guests of the city of Irkutsk will certainly be interested in hotels where you can stay during your trip. The modern city is able to offer tourists comfortable accommodation and good service. One of the institutions worth paying attention to is the Victoria Hotel (Irkutsk). It is about her that will be discussed in our article.

A bit about the hotel

Currently, the city has a wide selection of hotels for every taste. But each guest prefers institutions located in the center, as it is very convenient and makes it possible not to spend extra time traveling in transport. In close proximity to the center is the Victoria Hotel (Irkutsk). The hotel is one of the inexpensive establishments that offer accommodation in good rooms at affordable prices.

The address of the Victoria Hotel in Irkutsk is 1. Bogdan Khmelnitsky Street, 1. The convenient location of the establishment is difficult to overestimate. On the one hand, the city center is within walking distance, and on the other, the hotel is in a quiet courtyard, so guests will not be disturbed by the noise and transport. In addition, just a few kilometers from the hotel are the most interesting objects of the city, which will interest not only business people, but also tourists: places of recreation, attractions, shopping centers.

Transfer Organization

Getting from the airport or train station to the Victoria Hotel in Irkutsk by taxi is possible in just fifteen minutes. The hotel is located in the historical district of the city, therefore it is convenient for accommodating all categories of guests, regardless of the purpose of arrival. Tourists should not worry about how to get to the Victoria Hotel in Irkutsk, as one of the services of the establishment is to provide a transfer for guests.

Catering

The Victoria Hotel in Irkutsk has one of the most comfortable restaurants in the city of the same name. The restaurant serves Russian and international cuisine. The restaurant offers two cozy, well-equipped rooms. Within its walls you can spend a pleasant evening with your family, friends, business partners or a soul mate. The staff of the institution accepts applications for all kinds of celebrations. Two of its rooms can accommodate 25 people.

The restaurant also serves buffet breakfast. And at lunchtime, complex meals are provided. In the evening, the institution takes orders on the menu.

Hotel Location Reviews

Continuing the review of the hotel, I want to pay attention to the reviews of tourists who have already managed to visit it. It is worth noting that the hotel belongs to budgetary institutions. According to guests, it is consistent with the ratio of "price-quality". Experienced tourists note high prices for accommodation in hotels in the city, so Victoria is a great compromise if you are planning a budget trip. For a reasonable fee, you get good apartments and decent service.

According to reviews, the Victoria Hotel in Irkutsk has its advantages and disadvantages. One of the main advantages of the institution is its convenient location. The hotel is located next to the main street of the city, so getting to the most important objects or places is not ore. In addition, public transport stops are nearby. The hotel building is located in the courtyard, so the noise from the streets does not prevent guests from relaxing.

Another important advantage of the institution is the parking. It is very relevant, since there is no place to park a car in the nearest streets due to the traffic congestion in the city center. The presence of parking will be appreciated by all guests arriving by car. The weather in Irkutsk in May already allows the first tourists who want to see the beauty of the region to come together. Therefore, it is recommended to book hotel rooms in advance. Otherwise, you may not find available apartments upon arrival. The hotel is in great demand at any time of the year due to its budget prices.

Reviews about the number of rooms

The hotel is not so vast and diverse. Available apartments are presented in only a few categories. According to reviews, the rooms are quite comfortable for living, because they have everything that guests may need. The furniture in the rooms is not very new, but it does its job. Some tourists note problems with plumbing. The rest of the rooms can be a convenient place to stay during a business trip or vacation. Bed linen and towels are in decent quality apartments.

Maids clean and update detergents daily. Not all apartments can boast a good view from the window, since the hotel is located in the courtyards, but this is not so fundamental. The rooms have good internet access, which is very important for guests.

Nutrition Reviews

There is a restaurant in the hotel building where you can eat without going outside. Buffet breakfast is included. The range of food in the morning seems scarce to some guests. Nevertheless, early breakfasts are very convenient for those guests who come for work or plan to leave. At the buffet there is cutting, pastries, several types of jam, tea, instant coffee, yogurt, kefir, juice, bread and butter, fruit slices. Visitors can also order a hot dish, such as scrambled eggs or porridge, which is served literally for five to seven minutes. But brewed coffee is not included in the buffet, it must be paid separately.

In the daytime, the restaurant offers its visitors a set menu or lunch. You can also spend a good evening at the institution if you do not want to go out. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel there are many cafes and restaurants that are also worth a visit.

Instead of an afterword

The Victoria Hotel, according to reviews, occupies a worthy place in its price category and is very in demand among the guests of Irkutsk. The city is very popular among tourists because it has many attractions. Weather in Irkutsk in May (during the day the temperature reaches +15 degrees) allows you to visit Lake Baikal and other interesting places. It is spring and summer that many travelers consider the most convenient time to travel to Irkutsk. Therefore, rooms in local hotels should be booked in advance due to increased demand, especially for budget institutions, which are not so many in the city. Hotel "Victoria" is the best choice in terms of price-quality ratio.
