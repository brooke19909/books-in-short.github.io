Holiday Village "Zaglyaglyadie" (Krasnoyarsk): description and reviews

Our article will be interesting to those who like to relax in the fresh air, away from the city and the bustle. We suggest you to go to the recreation center "Zaglyadlenie" near Krasnoyarsk. Clean air and beautiful nature - this is what you can enjoy while relaxing at the base.

Location

The recreation center "Zaglyadlenie" (Krasnoyarsk) is located in the village of Glyaden, Emelyanovsk district, just 50 kilometers from Krasnoyarsk. The complex is located in a forest and consists of a mini-hotel and ten houses. The real pride of the base is the restaurant, made in the Russian style. The chef of the establishment offers amazing dishes.

On the territory of the base there are several gazebos with barbecue areas, where guests can spend time in a fun company and prepare delicious dishes.

The recreation center "Zaglyazhenie" (Krasnoyarsk) has a beautiful landscaped area where you can have a great time. The complex will certainly appeal to those people who want to have a good time in the lap of nature. If you are a supporter of luxury hotels with the highest level of service, then such a recreation center will not suit you. But supporters of active pastime here will like it. In general, the nature of the Emelyanovsk region is amazingly beautiful, and therefore has a good rest.

Mini Hotel

As we already mentioned, on the territory of the recreation center "Zaglyadnye" (Krasnoyarsk) there is a mini-hotel that can accommodate a small group of tourists. The hotel can accommodate no more than 20 people. It has a large relaxation room and its own kitchen. The cost of one person is 750 rubles per day.



On the territory of the recreation center "Zaglyadlenie" (Krasnoyarsk) there are three such mini-hotels (their capacity is 20, 21 and 12 people). The cost of living of guests during the New Year holidays increases and amounts to 2000 rubles per day.

Houses

A description of the Zaglyazhenie recreation center would be incomplete if we do not recall that there are still ten houses on its territory. Seven of them are equipped with two bedrooms, and three - one. All buildings are placed in two rows.

Wooden houses can be safely called comfortable. Each of them has a lounge, a kitchen area, consisting of a dining room and the kitchen itself (the kitchen has a sink, electric kettle, sink and stove). The houses have all the amenities, as well as digital TV.

Prices at the recreation center "Zaglyazhlenie" are quite affordable. Renting a house with two bedrooms per day costs 2,000 rubles on weekdays, and 5,000 rubles on holidays and weekends. Cottages with one bedroom are slightly cheaper - 2500/4000 rubles per day. During the New Year holidays, rental housing is rapidly increasing to 10,000 rubles per day.

The restaurant of the complex

Rest at the Zaglyazhenie recreation center gives you the opportunity to visit the restaurant of the complex, where you can spend a pleasant evening arranging friendly gatherings, dates, corporate parties or birthday celebrations.

If you like homemade Russian cuisine, then you will certainly enjoy the restaurant. The chic interiors of the restaurant, made in the Russian style, are especially impressive. A real fireplace creates a special atmosphere.



The restaurant menu contains Russian dishes. Here you can taste dumplings, pancakes, dumplings, cabbage soup, salmon and much more.

The relaxed and relaxed atmosphere of the establishment sets up relaxation and complete relaxation. An interesting fact is that in the restaurant you can not only have a pleasant evening, but also organize a celebration in the Russian style.

Russian bathhouse

Outdoor recreation is simply inconceivable without a real Russian bathhouse. It is made in the best traditions. The bath is heated with birch firewood. The steam room gives health and relaxation. A visit to the bath will give you a boost of energy and vitality. The air temperature in the steam room reaches 90-120 degrees. The real herbal tea, which is prepared on herbs collected on its own site, will complement the pleasant experience of visiting the bathhouse. According to tourists, even a single visit to the steam room gives a healthy sleep.

The complex has several saunas of different capacities: for 12 and for 6 people.

Horse rides

Emelyanovsk district has beautiful natural places. While relaxing at the base, guests have the opportunity to ride horses. This ancient entertainment has been of interest to people since the very first animals were tamed. Communication with horses allows not only to relax, but also to have fun.



The staff offers horseback riding, tours to the stable, as well as the organization of romantic events, weddings and more. As you know, horseback riding is a great opportunity to recharge your batteries. Such entertainment is available to guests of any age.

In the village of Glyaden you can not only ride a horse, but also engage in equestrian sports more professionally. If you have never previously been in the saddle, then for you instructors can organize a horseback ride accompanied by an instructor.

Entertainment based

Outdoor recreation is primarily picnics in nature. On the territory of the base for this there is everything you need. Guests enjoy comfortable gazebos with a view of the forest. Around them are equipped with mangal areas, allowing you to cook on a fire. Pergolas have night lighting. It is also possible to connect audio equipment.
