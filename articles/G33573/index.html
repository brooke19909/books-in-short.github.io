IR port for smartphone: what it is, how to use it. Infrared port

IR is an abbreviation for the phrase infrared port. The name clearly indicates the technology by which this device is implemented. The first prototypes of infrared ports appeared in the 1990s. The port is implemented as a receiver and transmitter of light waves in the infrared range, and this is light with a wavelength of 850 to 880 nanometers. This range is beyond the spectrum of radiation visible to humans, therefore it does not interfere and is convenient for use. Infrared radiation from the infrared port is absolutely harmless, so you can not think about the consequences of its use.

As previously used infrared

In the era of push-button phones without an operating system, when a very small percentage of the population had the Internet, the infrared port along with bluetooth technology became the main way to distribute information between devices. It worked like this: you had to select a file on your device, and then in the options, select transfer via infrared. Then two phones were laid out next to each other at a distance of a centimeter, or less. Now it was only necessary to wait until the process of transmitting information through the port was completed. The transmission speed and the amount of information transmitted were very low.





Decline in popularity

Soon, bluetooth and wireless Internet began to develop much faster and replaced the infrared port. But they did not forget about him, but on the contrary, found a new, more logical application. More precisely, the well-forgotten old. The fact is that, a long time ago, the infrared range is used in control panels for household appliances. Given the trend of equipping smartphones with more and more features, it is not surprising that the infrared port also fell into some smartphone models. One of the popular representatives of such devices was Xiaomi Redmi Note 2 - a Chinese smartphone of the mid-price range.

Configure the infrared port in the smartphone

The program for the IR port Mi Remote is configured without much difficulty. Let's look at this process step by step:

  1. Make sure you have an Internet connection on your device.
  2. Turn on the device for which you want to configure control via infrared.
  3. In Mi Remote, click the plus sign in the upper right corner of the screen.

After that, a special window will open in which you will need to select the type of device to be connected and the brand of the manufacturer, then select the name of the device.





The next step will be the selection of remote control codes for the device to which you are trying to connect. Do not be afraid, you are not required to select the code manually, you just need to point the smartphone towards the desired device, and then press the button in Mi Remote. After receiving a response signal - stop pinching the button on the smartphone and confirm your intentions in the Mi Remote menu bar. Sometimes there may be cases that such an operation will have to be repeated several times. On average, three iterations are needed for a successful join. That's all, now you can easily and simply control all the devices in a smart home.

IR Applications

The advantage of an open operating system is that third-party manufacturers can absolutely safely develop and offer their applications for working with the hardware of the device. Infrared port was no exception, so now you can find many programs in Google Play Market, the essence of which is to use a universal remote control on your smartphone via infrared.

You definitely may need one of these third-party applications if you want, for example, to connect to some rare model of TV, or a smart lighting system.

ASmart Remote IR is the best third-party application for the IR port at the moment. It is universal, so you can easily pick up codes and connect to your air conditioner, remotely control the operation of a webcam, projector and so on.





In addition to this application, the AnyMote Universal Remote program behaves pretty well in work. In it you can combine on one screen the control buttons of various devices, creating your universal remote. Agree, this greatly simplifies life. With such an application, the loss of a standard remote control, or its failure, will cease to be an urgent problem. The special IFTTT service in this application will help you create multi-level action scenarios. This means that pressing one button can solve several of your daily tasks at once. The application supports almost 900 thousand devices to connect.
