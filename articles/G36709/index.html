North Goa, Baga. Reviews of Hotels Goa (Baga)

Clean sandy beaches, warm sea, favorable weather conditions, beautiful landscapes, local color, exotic cuisine - all this attracts tourists to North Goa. Baga is a small village in an ecologically clean Indian state, known for its good location, good service, a large selection of affordable housing, beautiful nature.



A river flows into the sea, flowing into the sea, and here lies a wonderful beach surrounded by cliffs. Travelers usually associate North Goa with noisy crowded streets, confusion in shops, and hustle and bumps on the beaches, but Baga stands out among such towns for a relaxed atmosphere.

Sights of Bagi

Goa is famous for many interesting places, Baga was no exception, there is also something to see here. The town has a huge number of fabulously beautiful corners where you can retire and meditate, looking at the surrounding splendor. Of the architectural sights, the Bom Viagem Monastery should be distinguished, but a more amazing view opens to the side of this building. If you pass a hill overgrown with walnut trees along a narrow path, then healing springs will open before your eyes. Locals claim that bathing in these waters heals not only the body, but also the soul.



Near the same hill is Chor Biam, a huge black rock. There is a legend according to which under the mountain countless treasures of ships crashed in the Arabian Sea were buried. As evidence, rusted artifacts are scattered in this area: deck bells, anchors, guns. To the south of the beach are spread rice fields of amazing beauty . Also, tourists like to walk along the bridge over the river, it is interesting for its strange shape.

Local kitchen

Tourists have never had problems with food in Goa. Baga provides travelers with a huge selection of dishes from different cuisines of the world. The resort has a large number of bars, cafes, restaurants located both in the center of the town and near hotels and not far from the beach. The prices here are reasonable, in Italian or French restaurants you can pre-book dinner by candlelight. For a snack, many opt for pizzerias or ordinary eateries on the terraces, where they prepare a wonderful expresso. Some restaurants have a fish barbecue in the evening and at night, and a wide selection of local dishes are also available.

Entertainment



Most of Goa's beaches boast an impressive length and width, Baga has a relatively small sand strip, but it is surrounded by a fabulously beautiful landscape - a cape overgrown with forest. Here, of course, travelers seeking peace and quiet will enjoy it. Active tourists who are not prone to meditation will also not be bored. Like many other resorts of Goa, Baga offers vacationers water skiing, windsurfing, snorkelling, diving.

Children like to swim in the local river, and women catch oysters there. During the S√£o Jo√£o holiday, an interesting river festival called Sangodd takes place in Baga. Thanks to its good location, you can go from here to any corner of North Goa. Tourists mainly choose excursions to the spice plantations, elephant rides, night trips to the jungle.

Housing choice



The main center of tourism in India is Goa. Baga reviews are positive due to the wide choice of housing in a wide price range. It should be noted that in this resort town there are practically no 5-star hotels, only 4-star hotels, only tourists are invited to stay in 3-2-star hotels. Although lodging in Baga is not too rich in stars, it attracts travelers with its availability, convenient location and decent service.

Baga hotel prices

Goa hotels offer affordable prices, for example, you can stay in a 5-star hotel with all the amenities and high-class service for only $ 70. The price of 4-star housing starts at $ 40-50. Baga offers a wide selection of 3-star hotels with a convenient location and good service, and you can book a room for only $ 30. If the convenience for the tourist is not the main thing, and the hotel is needed only to spend the night there, then it is not worth overpaying, quite decent apartments can be rented for only $ 15.

Hotel reviews of tourists



The atmosphere of peace, joy, goodwill and hospitality reigns in Goa. Baga receives mostly positive reviews from travelers, because although this resort cannot boast of luxury apartments, even 2-star hotels try to please the client. Beira Mar 2 *, located 300 m from the beach, deserved a lot of good feedback. It provides excellent economy class accommodation, the rooms have everything you need for relaxation: a fan, a music center, a safe, a bathroom, a balcony. But the most important thing that tourists distinguish is the friendliness of the staff.

Hotel Colonia Santa Maria 3 * is located on the first line near the airport. Just 100 meters away are Goa's largest discos - Mambo and Titos. All rooms are equipped with a bedroom and a living room. The location, food and service of this hotel receive the highest ratings from travelers.

Abalone Resorts 2 * captivates with good service, convenient and comfortable rooms. The hotel is mainly designed for couples and youth. On the territory there is a pool, sun loungers near it are free. The disadvantages include the remoteness of the beach, about 2 km to go, but such a walk is only a pleasure, because when you look at beautiful landscapes, time flies by unnoticed.

Hotel Sun City 4 * can be considered a new building, it was opened only in 2008. The rooms are offered with all amenities, there are two restaurants with Indian and European cuisine on the territory , there is a free bar near the pool. The beach is 500 meters away, but there is something to do near the hotel: sauna, gym, billiards, table tennis, spa, massage and much more. Everyone who wants to spend an unforgettable vacation should go to the address - India, Goa, Baga.
