Washing machine instruction manual: what should I pay special attention to?



So, you have got a brand new washing machine. Do you want her to serve you as long as possible, but do not know how to do this? Everything is quite simple. To do this, it is important to observe several rules, which are contained in the brochure of the manufacturer under the name "Operating Instructions for the washing machine".

It should be noted that the modern unit for washing clothes is a qualitatively designed mechanism programmed for any desired mode, through which dirt on clothes is eliminated.

Often, housewives think that the instruction manual for the washing machine is a simple formality, so they do not attach due importance to this document. And this is their big mistake, because non-compliance with the requirements set forth in the above brochure very often leads to damage to household appliances. Remember that the instruction manual of the washing machine is the key to the maximum operation of the equipment. Among other things, precautions cannot be ignored by removing dirt from clothing mechanically. There is another weighty argument in favor of the fact that clothes should be washed according to the rules. The instruction manual for the washing machine will enable you to avoid various types of breakdowns of household appliances for as long as possible, which is very important for the family budget, since calling a master and repairing is not a cheap pleasure.

So what should you pay attention to?

Correct installation



Normal operation of the washing machine is not possible without proper installation. Household appliances should be placed on a flat surface. Do not forget to properly connect all hoses to the inlet and outlet communications through valves and gaskets.

Also check the ability to shut off water - the need for this often arises. It will be useful to mount a filter on the inlet water stream, which will soften the liquid and remove large particles and rust from it. Such prudence will make it possible to increase the life of the internal mechanisms of the unit. It is recommended to install household appliances without any distortions in order to minimize the risk of vibration and breakage of the main bearing. Also correctly install the adjusting screws located at the bottom of the unit. The power outlet must be located as far as possible from the moisture source so that electrical discharges do not occur.

In general, it is better to entrust the installation of the washing machine to a qualified specialist. For example, the instruction manual for the Beko washing machine is exactly what this provides.



Type and quantity of linen

To reduce noise vibration and adjust the quality of washing, experts recommend combining large and small things, for example, underwear and a duvet cover.

If you have the opportunity to weigh the laundry before washing, then do not ignore it. The instructions for the washing machine almost always contain a table that helps housewives not to overload the equipment. On clothing labels, you will always find information on what temperature conditions should be washed. If you do not follow these recommendations, the fabric may deteriorate or lose color. Always empty the contents of your pockets before washing, so that no foreign objects can enter the washing unit, which may cause household appliances to simply fail. Do not store dirty laundry inside the machine for very long.

Powder

There are no clear recommendations on which powder is ideal for washing. Of course, for washing machines should use the composition marked "Automatic". Do not forget to stock up in advance with special tools that prevent the formation of scale in the units.



Drain and Gulf Water

Remember that the machine on the bay must be connected to cold water, preferably with a special filter.

During the washing period, do not use the cold water tap, which is adjacent to the machine. In this case, the drain cock should be located as close to the floor as possible so that the water can easily come out.

Purity

Be sure to keep household appliances clean. Regularly remove plaque and particles of dirt from the tray for loading washing powder, ventilate and wipe the drum so that mold and rust do not form on it. Clean the rubber bands of the laundry cover. At intervals of 1 time in 6 months, check the operation of the filters and all nodes of the pipeline.

Remember that the life of the washing machine (on average, it is from two to eight years) directly depends on how carefully you treated your equipment and observed the rules for its technical operation.
