"Losevo Park" - a decent vacation in the Leningrad region

Leningrad region is one of the largest territorial-administrative units of Russia. Has common borders with Finland and Estonia. In the west it breaks off with the waters of the Gulf of Finland. It occupies more than 83 thousand square kilometers. It has many unique natural and architectural monuments, recreational areas. One of the most popular holiday destinations is Priozersky district.

Losevo Village

Until 1948, the village had a purely Finnish name - Kiviniemi. Before the advent of Soviet power, it was a Finnish village. In the 40s belonged to the Karelian-Finnish Soviet Republic. In 1945 it became part of the Leningrad Region.

The village is located on the banks of the Vuoksa River, therefore it attracts many vacationers, especially in Losevo Park. In addition, several children's health camps and recreation centers for families and friendly companies are located on the shore of the reservoir.

At the Losevsky rapids themselves, there are many competitions in water tourism and rowing slalom.





The recreation center "Losevo Park"

The base is located only 80 kilometers from the northern capital of our country. The village itself is located in one of the most picturesque places on the Karelian Isthmus near the banks of the Vuoksa River.

The entire territory of the institution is guarded around the clock, so you do not need to worry about your own safety, loved ones and about personal property.

For people who want to get as close as possible to the surrounding nature, the recreation center offers accommodation in a campsite, in tents (from 100 rubles per 1 visitor per day), on the very bank of the river. Such vacationers also do not need to worry about safety and leisure, all services can be used: from the gazebo with barbecue facilities to bike rental. There is also the possibility of visiting the shower room, for a fee.

Leisure and entertainment

On the territory of the institution there are many sports fields. Here you can play paintball or go to the bathhouse. Losevo Park offers boat trips along the Vuoksa River.

Rest for active people

Do not think that paintball is only for young people. Separate teams are formed for different levels of training. For beginners, teams are created in which everyone can feel the imitation of real "military" operations, feel the excitement and try themselves as a strategist. Experienced players can play according to a specific scenario, all actions are as close as possible to the "combat", with interesting missions and strategic tasks. For children and women there are special suits that minimize the impact force of the ball. No need to worry, there is no rubbish and debris on the paintball field in Losevo Park, so the possibility of injuries is minimized.

For lovers of enjoying the natural landscape, boats are available for hire. The Leningrad region has always been famous for its fabulous landscapes. All rental equipment is regularly checked and no breakdowns will occur during the boat trip. For everyone, excursions along the Losevsky rapids are organized on an ongoing basis. You can take masks with you and dive plenty, naturally, if it's not winter. And vacationers who want to retire can cross to a remote island and organize a picnic for themselves.

Those who are not afraid of water and want to test themselves are offered rafting on rafts along the Losevsky rapids, before which they will be instructed and life jackets will be issued.





Health benefits

Naturally, what a vacation without a real Russian bath. In "Losevo Park" there is an opportunity to experience all the advantages of a Russian steam room, which are located on a fenced area with barbecue facilities, a gazebo and a toilet. This is where you can relax as much as possible. It cleanses the skin, respiratory system and removes excess salt from the body.





Events for every taste

Rest in "Losevo Park" is not only a bathhouse and paintball. In the third building there are many slot machines. There is also a room for children, where they can draw, play with toys and ride a scooter. For fans of board games organized zone in the 2nd and 4th buildings.

Do not forget about fishing lovers. For such people, there is all the equipment that you can rent. And fishing on the Vuoksa River may surprise not even with the quantity, but with the diversity of the catch.

Losevo Park is not only an opportunity to contemplate the surrounding beauties, but it is also interesting to spend time competing with your friends or marking significant dates, even the New Year. By the way, to meet the upcoming 2018, the doors of the recreation center are open to all comers.
