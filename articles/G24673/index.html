Resort Location

Before talking about hotels in Mui Ne (Vietnam), you need to figure out where it is located.

This city is part of the Phan Thiet agglomeration, which is located on the shores of the South China Sea, about 200 km from the major Vietnamese city of Ho Chi Minh City. The resort itself is located on the eponymous coastal cape. It is also called the "Vietnam Tourism Capital". By the way, the city is insanely popular among Russian tourists.

So, it is worth noting that in Mui Ne there is always about the same air temperature, it ranges from +30 to +35 degrees. If you do not want to go in the rainy season, then the period from May to October is not very suitable for you. Best months to travel: February, March, January. It is worth noting that the water temperature here is always about the same, it ranges from +25 to +30 degrees.

Unfortunately, in Mui Ne and in the vicinity there is no airport, so you have to get from Ho Chi Minh City. The most convenient, but at the same time expensive option is a taxi, it will cost 70-120 dollars (4,500-7,800 rubles). The most popular and fairly budgetary way is a bus, a trip on it will cost $ 10 (650 rubles). If you love boat trips, you can get there by boat.
