How to get to "Sheremetyevo" the shortest way?

The question of how to get to Sheremetyevo is relevant for both Muscovites and numerous guests of the capital. But if a visitor is often simply unknown the way there, then the indigenous inhabitants of the capital are interested in how to get to the airport with minimal loss of time and nerves.

How to get to the airport "Sheremetyevo"

The main air gate of the country is located about ten kilometers from the Moscow Ring Road along the Leningradskoye Shosse, in the territory of the Khimki urban district. But getting here is far from always easy.

Answering the question of how to get to Sheremetyevo, one has to take into account many factors of Moscow life. First of all, traffic jams should be attributed to them, which are quite frequent on the Leningradskoye Shosse and during peak hours all traffic along it will be paralyzed for a long time. And at the end of the week, when many rush out of town, the situation is much worse than on a normal weekday. Based on these unfortunate circumstances, you should give preference to the railway when answering the question of how to get to "Sheremetyevo". Traveling here by Aeroexpress train from Belorussky Train Station will take a little more than half an hour.
With a standard ticket price of 320 rubles, such a trip will save a lot of nervous energy. Perhaps this is the only guaranteed way not to get stuck in traffic and not be late for the flight. Traveling in a business class in a luxury car will cost 900 rubles. For those who have to travel to Sheremetyevo often, there is an extensive system of discounts and bonuses. But, making a choice in favor of Aeroexpress, it should be borne in mind that it is impossible to get to the terminals of airports C and B. It is necessary to get to these objects with a transfer to a special bus that will take you there for free.

You can use city public transport. It will cost much cheaper. But it is best to go by bus or taxi if there is a sufficiently large margin of time before the departure of the flight, when there is no question of how to get to Sheremetyevo as quickly as possible. The easiest way to catch them is at the final stops at the metro stations Rechnoy Vokzal and Planernaya. Routes operate around the clock and go to Sheremetyevo with an interval of ten minutes to half an hour, depending on the time of day. The fare varies significantly (from 28 to 70 rubles). A taxi ride to the "Sheremetyevo" from the center will take half an hour and will cost one and a half thousand rubles. But this is in the absence of traffic jams, which are not only at night.





Can I get to the airport by metro?

Not yet. Currently, you have to choose exclusively between land modes of transport, when you have to look for a way to get to Sheremetyevo. The metro is planned to be held there only in a very distant future. At the moment, it is in the design stage. We are talking about the so-called "easy metro", the line of which should connect the city of Khimki with Moscow. The terminal station of the line should be located in Sheremetyevo.
