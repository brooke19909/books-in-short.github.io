On the very shore of the sea, near the village of Okurcalar, there is a cottage complex - a country hotel "Justinian Club". Alania, like Managavt, is a couple of tens of kilometers from it. The last repair was completed there last year. The hotel is extremely popular among Russians, and therefore our compatriots represent the vast majority of vacationers there. Moreover, the hotel has a royal holiday.



Hotel Justiniano Club, Alanya. Rooms & Facilities

This hotel has only one building (seven floors) called Jasmine. Most of the rooms are located in small bungalow houses scattered around a picturesque park. Standard rooms accommodate two to three people, and the "superior" are designed for 3-4 guests. There are also family rooms, consisting of two rooms. There five can settle down at once. The rooms are air-conditioned, all with balconies and amenities. The hotel itself has a bar where you can smoke a hookah, and a shopping center with many shops. Hotel Justiniano Club, Alanya. Beach and water treatments, entertainment for children

Guests can use not only the indoor and outdoor pools, but also water slides, as well as a spa center with a sauna, hammam and thalassotherapy treatments. A lot of interesting programs for children. Hotel Justiniano Club, Alanya. Reviews

Guests like the rooms and the fact that many of the rooms have at least a side view of the sea. Some guests complain that it is far to go on excursions, but the hotel is so self-sufficient that most of it is enough for its entertainment, beach relaxation and an unrealistically beautiful garden with pomegranates, palm trees, dates and evening paths illuminated in the evening. The nearest village can be reached on foot - its mosque and bazaar are visible directly from the windows of the Jasmine building. The sea is very clean, the beach is well maintained, and the rest is very good and fun, especially for families with children.
