Cooking Fried Eggs





Plasticine food for dolls can be simple and complex - it all depends on how old the girl who "cooks" her. Kids are usually offered to make simple "sausages", "cucumbers" or "tomatoes." Older girls make sandwiches and cakes. But there is a dish that is perfect for playing girls of any age. This is scrambled eggs. "Cooking" it is extremely simple.

Cooking Technology:

1. From white plasticine to mold a flat cake of arbitrary shape.

2. Make a small cake out of yellow plasticine.

3. Put the yellow cake on white and crush it. You can decorate the fried eggs with a sprig of "greenery", molded with your own hands. To do this, a piece of green plasticine is rolled into a thin sausage, squeezed so that it becomes flat, and various strokes are made with the stack.

Making sausage sandwiches





In addition to scrambled eggs, a couple of sandwiches can be served for a doll for breakfast. Such plasticine food will appeal to any doll.

Cooking Technology:
