How to make a poppy from a cloth with your own hands in different ways

Want to update your clothes, hairstyle or create an original decor for the interior? Make a poppy from a cloth with your own hands. It will turn out to be very natural and beautiful, therefore it is suitable for creating a bouquet, as well as for a souvenir or fashion accessory.

Fabric flowers

Floral motifs always remain popular both in interior decoration and in creating a feminine image that is associated with a beautiful flower. If you are engaged in needlework, then you know that often there are small shreds of fabric that are a pity to throw out. So that they do not lie idle in the closet, find them a great use. Make a poppy from a cloth with your own hands.

It can be used to create a bouquet in a vase, brooches, hairpins, headband, decoration for curtains or a gift box. Application to the created decor, you can come up with the most diverse.

Materials and Tools

If you immediately have a question about how to make a poppy from a cloth with your own hands, and you think that it is too complicated, the answer is: no, simple, and no complicated devices will be required. There are several ways, but the sequence of actions is similar. Depending on which manufacturing option you choose, you will need different materials to create a center, petals, stamens. The list is as follows:

  • Synthetic or natural fabric (for which purpose it is indicated in the relevant instructions) or satin ribbons.
  • Pattern of petals (you can draw on paper yourself or print a finished template from the Internet).
  • Scissors.
  • Needle.
  • Threads to create stamens are black or brown, as well as to match the petals and the middle.
  • Wire and corrugated paper.
  • Semolina or spangles for decoration of stamens.
  • Bead, beads for the middle.
  • Candle.
  • Gelatin and a patch of organza (used in the latter method).
  • Glue or thermal gun.
  • Basis for brooches, hair clips, bezel.

As you can see, to make a poppy from a cloth with your own hands, you do not need anything complicated. Everything is either already at your place, or it is easy to buy at a needlework store. Above is the entire set of materials in case you decide to try to create a flower in different ways. You can choose the right one based on what you already have in stock.





Manufacturing technology

Creating a flower by any method will consist of several stages:

  1. Preparation of petals and leaves.
  2. Performing the middle.
  3. Making stamens.
  4. Connection of all elements and attachment to the base (hairpin, etc.).

There will be differences in each option, but overall the work is similar.

How to make a poppy from a cloth with your own hands: instructions. Easy way

So, we will consider how to make a poppy from a fabric with our own hands quickly.
The sequence of work is as follows:

  1. Take a piece of red or pink synthetic fabric or a 5 cm wide satin ribbon and cut the petals according to the patterns.
  2. At each part, melt the edges above the candle flame and, until the fabric has cooled, stretch the shape of the petals with your fingers. They will not be flat, but with volume.
  3. Wrap the stamen threads on your fingers in the form of a figure eight or on a piece of cardboard (you can take a plastic card) with a ring. Remove the skein from the base and tie in the middle. Trim the edges evenly.
  4. Dip the ends of the threads in glue and then in decoy or sequins. Leave to dry.
  5. As a middle, use a bead that is glued to the stamens.
  6. Connect all the petals in layers (internal to each other, then the middle, etc.), glue the middle to the stamens, and fix it on the back of the hairpin on the back side. If you are making a flower for a vase, you need to make sepals, leaves and a stalk of wire wrapped in green corrugated paper.

This is a quick way to create a product, because instead of the middle, a bead is taken and there is no frame.



DIY poppies from fabric: a workshop on creating hairpins

You can try to make this accessory using sophisticated technology. A wire frame will be used for each element, and the center will be created separately independently. The sequence of work is as follows:

  1. As in the previous method, take a synthetic fabric, cut out the petals and work on the candle flame.
  2. Cut the wire into elements according to the number of flower parts (petals, middle, stamen harvesting) with a length equal to at least a petal, but better longer to make it convenient to work. If you are going to create a flower on the stem, take the height taking into account the stem to the maximum (measure the depth of the vase).
  3. Apply glue to each piece of wire and wrap strips (not more than 5 mm) of corrugated paper. It is better to take in tone with the elements or white.
  4. Fasten the resulting frame elements with the petals with glue (this is faster) or threads (neater) to match the fabric.
  5. Make stamens, as in the previous method, only use a wire blank to pull a bunch of threads.
  6. Take a circle of green cloth, sew it around the perimeter and pull it into a bag. Fill it with cotton, padding polyester, or whatever you have. Secure with thread. Rewind a piece of wire to form a box. Instead of a mug of green fabric, a lump of green wool is used for felting.
  7. Combine all the details with glue into a single whole, forming a stalk, or cutting off excess pieces of the frame.
  8. You can make several flowers of different sizes or shades and glue the floral composition on the base (hairpin).




The work is completed. So in different ways you can make poppies from a cloth with your own hands. A master class, a detailed description of actions and materials will help to realize any option easily.

Corrugated poppy gelatinized poppy seeds

This method is different in that you need a natural fabric. Cotton, satin, silk or chiffon are suitable. An organza flap will also be required. With it, corrugation is performed, creating a raised surface of the petals.





To make a poppy flower from a cloth with your own hands in this way, follow these steps:

  1. Dilute a tablespoon of gelatin in water.
  2. After 40 minutes, heat without bringing the composition to a boil.
  3. Dip the petal cloth in the solution.
  4. Remove and dry the treated flap. He became firmer.
  5. Cut the petals, and then each separately folded in half, put in an organza flap, which is twisted clockwise with one hand, holding the second blank on the table surface.

After this treatment, the petal will acquire an interesting texture. Do the rest using any of the above technologies.

So, you saw how you can make a poppy from a cloth with your own hands in different ways. Choose the appropriate option for the manufacture of parts and their assembly. Create gorgeous floral decorations.
