St. Petersburg, "Vvedensky" hotel: photos and reviews of tourists

Each guest of St. Petersburg, naturally, is worried about where he is best to stay. Of course, the Northern capital offers many comfortable hotels, but the four-star Vvedensky Hotel, a photo and a detailed description of which you can see in this article, is worthy of the attention of everyone who wants to keep the most pleasant memories of the trip.

So, welcome to the Vvedensky Hotel!



Hotel Location Benefits

The impressive nine-story building of the gymnasium of the XIX century., in which the Vvedensky hotel is located, belongs to the architectural monuments of federal significance. It is located in the heart of the Petrograd side, in its green zone. The hotel is surrounded by many shops, expensive boutiques and cozy restaurants.

Thanks to the good location of the hotel, you can build a very interesting and at the same time, a tireless tourist route. After all, the Hermitage Museum, the Summer Garden, the House of Peter the Great, the Peter and Paul Fortress, Alexander Park, the Winter Palace, Palace Square and many other places of tourist pilgrimage are nearby. This location can be very convenient if there is a need to accommodate your guests or business partners.

The hotel is easily accessible from the Petrogradskaya, Gorkovskaya or Chkalovskaya metro stations, it is only a 15-minute walk from them.

Classification of rooms at the Vvedensky Hotel

Visiting hotel offers 158 comfortable premium rooms. In addition, for the convenience of guests arriving at the Vvedensky Hotel, there is a restaurant, cafe and well-equipped halls for business meetings and conferences. Several rooms are adapted to the needs of people with disabilities.

The hotel has 136 standard rooms, 11 - Superior Comfort, 6 - Deluxe, 3 - Suite, one room of Business Suite and Royal Suite. A brief description of these apartments will be given below.

Once again about the rooms provided by the Vvedensky Hotel

Entering each of the rooms that Vvedensky Hotel offers, you will be pleased with their restrained but very cozy atmosphere. Dark wood furniture, carpets, the whole well-designed design give a feeling of peace, reliability and respectability. Incidentally, each room has a minibar and safe, as well as free internet access. In addition, a telephone is provided in all rooms, and from Monday to Friday, residents are offered a free daily newspaper.

Air conditioning, orthopedic mattresses and hypoallergenic bedding ensure a relaxing and healthy sleep.

Bathrooms kept in sterile cleanliness are equipped not only with the bathtub itself, but with a shower and bidet. A hairdryer and free toiletries are provided for use, and bathrobes and slippers are provided upon request.

The hotel has where to relax and eat deliciously

What is nice to note, the price level here is in full accordance with the level of comfort. Rich tasty breakfasts of the "buffet" type (their price is included in the payment for accommodation), which are provided by the Vvedensky Hotel (St. Petersburg) from 7 to 10 am, energize guests for the whole day.



On the ground floor of the hotel there is a light elegant restaurant La Restaurant, which is a kind of gastronomic heart of the entire hotel complex. It is able to accommodate up to 70 vacationers, offering dishes of both Russian and European cuisine.

On the second floor of this building there is an excellent Italian cafe "Biscotto", offering a wide selection of Tuscan cuisine and homemade desserts from pastry chef Nikolayeva.

Near the entrance to the hotel there is a lobby bar, which, according to guests, can be proud of its bartender and delicious aromatic coffee, which offers fragrant fresh pastries from its own bakery. The landscape of Bolshoi Avenue outside the window and comfortable soft chairs will help the visitor to relax after a trip or business meetings and negotiations and have a good rest.

Hotel "Vvedensky": reviews

The hotel staff, according to numerous guests, is always worried about the comfort of the hotel guests. And this, you see, does honor to any hotel.

Ladies positively evaluate the beauty salon of Ekaterina Garus, located on the second floor of the hotel, as well as a wellness center that includes two Finnish saunas, a Turkish hammam, 2 jacuzzis, a massage room and a gym. By the way, from 7:00 to 23:00 the gym is free here.

Judging by the reviews, the friendly staff who speaks English and is always ready to help does not leave visitors indifferent. The hotel concierges organize city tours at your request, as well as ordering and delivery of rail or air tickets. They can provide information on cultural events in the city and book tickets for official meetings.



Hotel Vvedensky - a great place for business meetings

For businessmen who come to St. Petersburg, the Vvedensky Hotel can be a great place for conferences and business meetings. To do this, he has all the modern conference facilities that allow them to be held at a high level.

The Vvedensky conference hall will be able to accommodate about 200 guests, and the Blok conference hall, which was so named after the great Russian poet who studied at the Vvedensky gymnasium at one time, can accommodate 120 people. Two meeting rooms for 10 people each, equipped with comfortable leather chairs and a round table, will be a great place to successfully resolve all issues.

And in order to betray the meetings and negotiations with a friendly atmosphere, participants are offered coffee breaks, and in the case of protracted events, complex lunches or dinners. The successful outcome of these meetings can be noted with a festive buffet reception.

Additional hotel services

Those who came to the Vvedensky Hotel (St. Petersburg) with their own car will be pleased to know that the hotel has its own underground parking. However, due to the small number of places in it, it is worth worrying about booking them in advance.

For those who need a car, but do not have one, the hotel provides rental cars.

Dry cleaning and laundry services are available for all hotel guests. Ironing and shoe shine services are also provided.

The hotel has a currency exchange and an ATM, as well as a gift shop and a tour desk. A separate room is provided for storing luggage for arrivals. And check-in and check-out are carried out individually. In addition, the reception is open 24 hours a day.

The undoubted amenities include the fact that guests can order breakfast in their room, and pay for services with a credit card.



Hotel Vvedensky - a good choice!

If you take into account the reviews left by guests who visited the Vvedensky Hotel, it will become obvious that this hotel is a good choice for a person with good taste and striving for comfort and respectability.

The spirit of aristocracy, brought from the 19th century, combined with the most modern interior details and service options, will help any guest of the Northern capital feel so comfortable here that they will turn this hotel into their permanent address for new visits to St. Petersburg.
