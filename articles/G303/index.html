Hotel Emotions By Hodelpa 4 * (Juan Dolio, Dominican Republic): description, service, reviews

Juan Dolio is a very popular tourist destination. This small resort town, located along the coast, attracts tens of thousands of tourists every year. Here you can relax and recharge with vivid impressions. But before you go on vacation, every tourist should choose a hotel for temporary residence. Well, if you want to stay in a complex with first-class service, reasonable prices and an all-inclusive system, you should opt for Emotions By Hodelpa 4 *. This complex is in great demand among tourists, and there are many reasons for this.





Service and Facilities

Emotions By Hodelpa 4 * has everything that guests may need - from free high-speed Wi-Fi to private parking, which is available on request.

The reception is open around the clock, so individual check-in and check-out are available here for everyone. There is an exchange office, laundry and dry cleaning with ironing, a small business center, a conference room, 2 meeting rooms, as well as room service and newspapers. In addition to the above, VIP services are provided.

It is important to note that Emotions By Hodelpa 4 * is designed for adult guests. No children are allowed. But for the rest of the tourists, ideal comfortable conditions are created here. There are even rooms for people with disabilities in which everything is adapted to their requirements (even there is a special convenient shower).





For leisure

In Emotions By Hodelpa 4 * everyone will find something to their liking. Of course, most of the guests spend all their time on a private private beach, enjoying the purest loose sand, the gentle sun and warm azure water. But besides this, the hotel has a fitness center, an outdoor tennis court, a sports field, bicycle rental, its own night club, a wellness center and a billiard room. There is even a wine cellar and water sports facilities.

But special attention deserves the spa at Emotions By Hodelpa 4 * (Juan-Dolio). It offers a full range of services: deep muscle massage, stone therapy, reflexology, mud baths, a sauna, body wraps, treatments using scrubs, aromatic oils and much more. A detailed list of the pleasures offered can be found on an individual basis or upon arrival at the hotel.





Restaurants

Emotions By Hodelpa 4 * (Juan-Dolio) has several elite establishments where guests can indulge in delicacies, delicacies and a variety of drinks.

Breakfast, lunch and dinner are held according to the "buffet" in two restaurants at the same time. One of them is called Tropico, and the other is Ergo's. They are open from 6:00 to 10:00, from 12:00 to 15:00, and also from 19:00 to 22:00. There is no dress code, but you should not go there barefoot or dressed in a swimsuit.

A restaurant called Oregano will appeal to lovers of fusion cuisine. It is open in the evening, and to have dinner there, you need to book a table the day before. By the way, the institution has a dress code. And lovers of Italian cuisine are definitely worth a look at Amici restaurant, which specializes in it. Seafood lovers, in turn, will like Figaro, located here. And if you want to taste Mexican cuisine, then you should go to the Lolita restaurant.

Corporate and social events, gala awards and, of course, weddings are often organized and held here. Future newlyweds are offered several service packages at all. The most economical rooms

It should be noted that the Emotions By Hodelpa 4 * hotel (Dominican Republic) offers guests apartments at prices quite reasonable for this level of service. The most economical option is the so-called superior rooms of 30 square meters. m

They are stylish soundproofed apartments with one huge king-size bed and floor-to-ceiling windows. Inside, there is everything necessary for comfort: a widescreen TV, a safe, a climate system, ironing facilities, a trempel, a minibar, a coffee machine, a dressing room and even an alarm clock. Naturally, there is a bathroom with a shower, hairdryer and other hygiene items.

How much does it cost to stay in such a room? In June, 7 days for two people will cost about 60-65 thousand rubles (including all inclusive). But in addition you will have to pay a tourist fee (18% of the amount) and city tax (10%).





Luxury apartments

Also in the fund of the Emotions By Hodelpa 4 * hotel (Dominican Republic) there are so-called club Essential rooms. They are decorated in a more elegant style and have all the previously mentioned amenities. But, in addition, these apartments have access to a private pool and a private entrance to the room. Also, people who book them are guaranteed access to the executive lounge. They are also provided with slippers and bathrobes. And the beds, by the way, in such rooms are elongated (more than 2 meters).

The cost of these apartments, of course, is higher than that mentioned above. 7 days will cost two guests about 100-115 thousand rubles.

By the way, all issues related to the view from the window must be decided in advance, even when booking. Not all windows offer sea views. There are rooms with garden and pool views. Therefore, if this aspect is fundamentally important, it is necessary to indicate the wishes in the reservation specially reserved for this when booking. But you need to consider that apartments with sea views are usually more expensive than others.





Impressions of guests

In the end, I would like to draw attention to the reviews that people who have already visited there who leave about their holidays at the Emotions By Hodelpa 4 * hotel.

Naturally, the vast majority of comments are positive. Not only those travelers who were counting on a peaceful stay in a quiet and secluded place are not very satisfied. This is not here. This hotel has a party atmosphere, which is not surprising, given the resort specifics of this complex.

People who returned from the Dominican Republic, enthusiastically describe the fabulous indoor beach, high quality service, tact and level of service of the staff.

Also, almost all guests in their reviews respond with nostalgia about the food and drinks served in the hotel restaurants. Everything is incredibly tasty, so the all-inclusive service is fully justified.

Naturally, no one leaves without attention the amazing view from the windows of the apartments. The numbers in reality are fully consistent with the declared description and photos. The furniture is new and comfortable, the inside is always clean and tidy, which is the merit of the maids who tidy up the rooms daily. Linen, like towels with bathrobes, also change regularly.

To summarize, we can confidently say that this hotel will be the perfect vacation spot for people who dream of an unforgettable vacation on the Caribbean Sea. It is worth going for unrestrained fun, entertainment, relaxation and gastronomic pleasures. Surely you will not have to regret your choice.
