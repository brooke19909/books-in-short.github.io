Coral Hills Marsa Alam 5 * (Egypt): reviews and photos

From time to time, every person wants to relax somewhere on the sunny seashore. And today, many tourists choose the Egyptian city of Hurghada as their place of vacation. It is here that the luxury resort complex Coral Hills Marsa Alam 5 * is located. Where exactly is the hotel located and what conditions does it offer to foreign tourists? What reviews do travelers have about this place? Is it suitable for the rest of the family? These are important questions, the answers to which will be of interest to many readers.

Hotel complex Coral Hills Marsa Alam 5 * (Egypt, Hurghada): room description





This hotel complex can be considered quite large - on its territory there are 339 rooms. For the most part, these are standard rooms, but there are also spacious family rooms, as well as separate villas with several bedrooms, a kitchen and a study. Depending on your requirements and capabilities, employees will surely help you choose the most comfortable place.

All rooms are spacious with a private balcony with views of the hotel and the sea. Of course, in the room you will find the necessary amount of furniture and household appliances. In particular, there is an individual air conditioning system, as well as a telephone with direct dial function (long-distance and international calls are paid separately). Valuables can be stored in a small safe. The room has a minibar, but it is filled with drinking water only on the day of settlement - you must pay for any other drinks separately. There is a TV.

The bathroom has a comfortable shower, as well as a hairdryer for drying hair, a toilet, a wall mirror, a set of hygiene items and towels.

Dining





Like most Egyptian hotels, the hotel complex Coral Hills Marsa Alam 5 * offers its guests an all-inclusive scheme. Buying a tour, you get a full three meals a day, snacks in bars throughout the day, as well as juices and drinks from the Egyptian manufacturer. According to reviews, the food at the hotel is really at a high level - guests are served buffet style with a large selection of dishes, snacks, salads, fresh fruit and real oriental desserts.

In addition, you will have the opportunity to visit the wonderful Italian restaurant Hills Food Court - you will have the right to a free visit once a week, but only by reservation. In the courtyard there is also a grill restaurant where you can enjoy delicious dishes prepared on fire. The pool, lobby and beach have bars serving light snacks and drinks.

Water recreation and beach





Coral Hills Marsa Alam 5 * hotel has its own beach area, which is located in close proximity to residential buildings. Only guests can relax on the shore, which is very convenient. There are enough sun loungers with mattresses, as well as sun umbrellas.

The beach here is covered with soft golden sand, cleaned regularly. Sunset at sea is convenient, but since the coral reef is located very close , tourists are advised to use special shoes. There is a volleyball court on the beach, as well as a water sports center. Outdoor enthusiasts will have the opportunity to ride a boat, boat, catamaran, banana, water skiing, etc. If you wish, you can use the services of the diving center at any convenient time, where you rent the equipment you need to dive, and also conduct a course diving training.

Additional hotel service





For many Russian tourists, it is Hurghada that becomes the vacation destination . Hotels "5 stars", of course, offer a range of additional services that help make your vacation more comfortable. And this hotel is no exception.

It is worth saying that the hotel has a laundry service. If you have your own or rented transport, you can leave it in a private parking lot. There is also internet access in rooms and public areas, but this service is paid. There is no medical office at the hotel, but if necessary, a doctor will be called to you. The hotel also offers to visit several shops and souvenir shops. You will have the opportunity to use the services of a business center where there are computers and other equipment. The hotel also has a spacious conference room, which is suitable for both business events and various celebrations.

Hotel Coral Hills Resort Marsa Alam 5 * (Hurghada): entertainment and leisure for tourists

Of course, on site you can really have fun. For example, there are two large outdoor pools with sun loungers and parasols, where guests can relax and unwind. Also, the Coral Hills Marsa Alam 5 * hotel offers its guests to visit the wellness center, where you can visit the sauna or Turkish bath, sign up for several massage sessions or relax in the jacuzzi.





There is a small but well-equipped gym. There are also sports fields for indoor soccer and volleyball. There is also a tour desk where you can help pick up an interesting tour of the surroundings. By the way, guests can go to picturesque mangroves once for free. You can ride a quad bike next to the hotel. And of course, in the daytime and evening, the guests are entertained by a team of animators.

Conditions for relaxing with children

Many tourists are interested in the question of whether it is possible to relax on the territory of the Coral Hills Marsa Alam 5 * hotel with a child. Of course, this hotel complex is perfectly adapted for family vacations.

For starters, it's worth talking about amenities. A room for a child will be delivered to the room upon request, and in the restaurant you can always ask for a high chair for feeding. By the way, in the main restaurant there is a specially equipped children's corner where children can dine in the company of peers. Of course, there is a special menu.

Your child in the hotel will not be bored. In the courtyard there is a safe and spacious children's pool. The guys can also have a great time in the mini-club, where teachers and animators will be engaged with them. There is also a playground with swings, which the children simply adore. And the hotel constantly hosts various classes for children, sports competitions, art lessons, dancing, etc. In short, your child will definitely not be bored.

Hotel Traveler Reviews

Many people are primarily interested in questions about what tourists who have already managed to visit the Coral Hills Marsa Alam 5 * hotel are saying. The reviews are for the most part positive, although there are some negative comments. Indeed, the rooms here are spacious and you can enjoy sea views from the window. Near the hotel there is a calm bay with coral islands, where most tourists spend time.





The food at the hotel is at a decent level - there is a large selection of snacks, meat dishes, as well as fresh fruits and vegetable salads. The hotel has several shops where you can buy various little things and souvenirs. Perhaps the problem in this case is that not all staff understand the Russian language, although there are several employees with whom you can talk.

Of course, one of the most popular areas today is Hurghada. 5 star hotels are usually quite expensive. But this hotel is perfect for a budget holiday on the beach, which, in fact, makes it so popular.
