Zaozernoe (Crimea) - reviews on the wonderful holiday

Among the many places for recreation, one of the most popular is the resort suburb of Yevpatoriya - the village of Zaozernoe (Crimea). Reviews of this village confirm that it is a great place, suitable for both family vacations and youth companies. It is here that you can not only relax perfectly at sea, but also combine relaxation with the treatment of various diseases, including the musculoskeletal system. In addition, you can take advantage of the general health program of the whole organism, because in the health resorts of this resort village all procedures are carried out using mud from Lake Moynaki.



This village was formed in 1861. This happened due to the need to rebuild the Light Beacon, which can still be seen today. It rises in the southwestern part of Yevpatoriya. This resort town is located just a few kilometers from the village of Zaozernoe (Crimea). Reviews show that getting to it is not difficult, since the village and the city are connected by regular transport links.

The village itself is a pretty cozy village. On the first coastline there are pensions, children's health camps and sanatoriums of the village Zaozernoe (Crimea). Reviews show that here you can combine relaxation with treatment. All resorts have a powerful medical base, qualified specialists. Numerous shops and cafes are concentrated on the next lane, which makes it possible to eat independently. In grocery stores, there is a fairly large selection that can satisfy the wishes of every tourist. Next come the private mini-pensions, and then the private sector. Private hotels are modern complexes for a comfortable stay, offering a number of additional services. And the private sector of the village is suitable for those who prefer a more economical and independently planned vacation.



A significant role in choosing a place to relax is played by the beaches. The coastal strip of the Kalamitsky Bay is strewn with a layer of clean and fine sand. That is why the beaches of the village Zaozernoe (Crimea) are extremely positive. The sand here warms up quite quickly and by noon very often heats up to 50 degrees. The beach has a smooth descent to the sea. Thanks to the shallow water of the coastal strip, the water here is very warm. The tides are practically not observed here. The choice of beach areas in Zaozernoye is quite large. There are both equipped and well-equipped beaches, and deserted, where lovers of remote recreation seek.



Holidays in Zaozernoye (Crimea) - reviews confirm this - this is not only spending time on the beach or taking medical procedures. On the outskirts of the village is located the entertainment center Foton, where various concerts, festivals and all kinds of shows are held.

There are several night clubs in the village, where you will be offered various show programs, there is a disco. Those who will rest here in August, if desired, will be able to get to the international festival of rock music. Half an hour from the village there is a beautiful water park "Banana Republic Aquaparkos". For lovers of electronic music in the village of Popovka, which is located at a distance of 25 kilometers from Zaozerny, the Quasantip Republic project is presented, where the best DJs in the world gather.

Fans of kite surfing and windsurfing will also get a wonderful vacation, going to the Crimea. Zaozernoye, reviews of which describe all the attractive sides of this village, is located near Lake Donuzlav. It is here that sports entertainment fans seek.
