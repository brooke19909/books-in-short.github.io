Bodensee Hotel 3 * (Konyaalti, Antalya, Turkey): description, leisure and reviews of tourists

Taking a trip to the sea, especially to foreign resorts, many vacationers begin to look for places in which they will be offered everything that they are looking for for a comfortable vacation. And first of all it concerns hotels.

Konyaalti

This is not a city. And not even a village. Konyaalti is the most famous region of Antalya. It is famous primarily for its beaches and five-star hotels, among which there are branches of many hotel chains. Many foreigners choose the area not only for holidays, but also in order to purchase real estate here. Indeed, Konyaalti has a very developed infrastructure with hospitals, numerous institutions, supermarkets and restaurants. There are also cultural centers, in which, for example, in Ataturk Kultur Merkezi, musical evenings are organized every week; The pyramid in which Oktoberfest is held - a beer celebration; aquarium; dolphinarium; chic tennis clubs; Beach Park. Nevertheless, many come here to relax.

Actually, mainly for this reason many Russian families come here every year, who, having fallen in love with this place, come again and again, and in many cases they acquire real estate here.

The resort is famous for its main beach located in Beach Park. He has been awarded the Blue Flag for cleanliness and excellent service. Since 2014, everything that is provided here is free. This is sunbeds, umbrellas, and the ability to use a shower or toilet, changing cabins. This and other municipal beaches are used by those who stay at Konyaalti hotels, including the three-star Bodensee Hotel 3 *.

general information



Many tourists who choose this hotel for their holidays are primarily attracted by its location. Bodensee Hotel 3 * (Antalya, Konyaalti) is located in the center of the area, very close to the sea. But most importantly, the airport is only fifteen kilometers away, so the transfer is very fast. This is especially convenient for those tourists who come to rest with young children.

The three-story, pink-painted building of this small hotel is visible from afar. It is completely surrounded by greenery. The territory of Bodensee Hotel 3 * is small, but thoughtful and well-maintained. Everywhere there are flowers, palm trees and fruit trees with benches in the canopy.

Infrastructure

Being a three-star, Bodensee Hotel 3 * (Konyaalti) offers its guests a fairly developed infrastructure. Dry cleaning is located in its only building, there is a medical room (service is paid), a depository safe, an ATM and a left-luggage office.

At his always open registration desk you can order a taxi, arrange a transfer, if this was not done when buying a ticket.

rules

In Bodensee Hotel 3 * the same rules apply that are established in any other hotel. Check-in and check-out are carried out at a specified time. However, if tourists arrived at the hotel before the scheduled time, if they had free rooms, they would be given keys immediately. When leaving, you need to pay for all those services whose cost was not included in the ticket.

Housing stock



One building with thirty-eight rooms, which can accommodate up to eighty people at a time, has recently been renovated. The rooms have everything that is needed for living - new furniture, including a wardrobe, bedside tables, comfortable beds with mattresses, a dressing table, a large mirror. The TV catches satellite channels, manually adjustable air conditioning. On the floor - ceramic tiles.

The rooms are decorated in soothing colors, and the curtains are matched to them. Some of them have small furnished balconies.

Bathrooms are also thoroughly renovated. They have a shower room. All supplies and towels are updated daily. Despite the fact that the building has only three floors, it has an elevator. There are smoking rooms. Accommodation with animals must be discussed with the administration in advance.

Nutrition

The hotel serves residents on the concepts of "full board" and "half board". Tables are served as buffets. Those who prefer to eat on the menu must pre-book a table at the registration desk.

In addition to the restaurant, the hotel has a poolside bar where vacationers on an all-inclusive basis receive free drinks from Turkish producers and snacks. For imported alcohol you need to pay extra.

Beach

The sea in the Konyaalti area is always clean. From Bodensee Hotel 3 * (Turkey) to the beach is only ten minutes walk, although in a straight line the hotel is located quite close to the coast. The registry warns in advance about the need to purchase special shoes, since the coating in the nearest urban bathing area is pebble. Many of those staying at the Bodensee Hotel prefer to drive to the famous Beach Park beach, where everything is provided for free, and the sand is covered, and the entrance to the water is gentle. You can get to it by bus in ten minutes.



Additional Information

Many of those who chose Bodensee Hotel 3 * for relaxation are satisfied with its location, which is very convenient not only for exploring the surroundings, but also for shopping. There are a lot of inexpensive shops around, there is an excellent tour desk right next to the hotel gates. Here you can buy a boat trip, diving with an instructor, a trip to the church of St. Nicholas, the zoo, mosques and even the ancient city of Olympos.

On the territory of Bodensee Hotel 3 * there is a small but always clean pool. There are no problems with sun loungers located around this mini-hotel. There is also a bar. For those who love outdoor activities, the beach offers diving, pedalos, bananas and other activities.

Prices



Many tourists traveling to Turkey are primarily interested in hotel prices. Bodensee Hotel 3 * refers to the economy class. For two weeks of rest with meals (only breakfast and dinner), transfer, departure from Moscow for two you need to pay about thirty-five thousand. But if you buy tickets in advance, then in the high season you can relax at a lower cost.

Reviews

Since Russians do not often stay at this hotel, there are not so many impressions left about our stay in our compatriots. Nevertheless, Bodensee Hotel 3 * reviews are mostly positive.

It is positioned as an ideal place for those who prefer a relaxing holiday, combined with excursions and swimming in the sea. True, some did not like the scarcity of the menu, especially fruit, but tourists compensated for this by buying everything they needed in the market, where prices are quite low.



There are also reviews that say that the food was hearty and very tasty. Apparently, this factor is associated with the occupancy of the hotel.

Another plus is the location of Bodensee Hotel: proximity to the airport and beach, restaurants and shops.

Many recommend the hotel to those who are interested in the financial side of their vacation, as well as for those who just sleep in the room and spend the rest of their time either on the beach or on excursions.
