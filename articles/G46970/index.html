Hotel "Old Rostov" (Rostov-on-Don): description, photos and reviews

If you are planning a trip to Rostov-on-Don, pay attention to such an accommodation option as the Old Rostov Hotel. A convenient location and quality service will leave you with the most pleasant impression of your stay.





"Old Rostov", hotel: address

The hotel is located at: Rostov-on-Don, st. Turgenevskaya 32. Convenient location attracts tourists and business people hotel "Old Rostov". How to get to her? From the main bus station you need to walk to the railway station and take the minibus No. 38, 78, 90 or 99. After reaching the Turgenevskaya street stop, walk about 100 meters on foot.

Description of rooms

Hotel "Old Rostov" provides its guests with comfortable living conditions. You can choose one of the following accommodation options:

  • Single rooms are ideal for business travelers and lovers of independent travel. The categories "Economy", "Standard" and "Attic", which differ in area and equipment, will be offered to your attention. The cost of living ranges from 1500 to 4000 rubles.
  • Double occupancy is possible in economical and standard rooms. The first version has only 2 single beds, and the second provides an additional sofa. The cost of living is from 2500 to 3500 rubles.
  • Triple rooms are located in the attic. There are 3 single beds, next to which there is a nightstand. Ceiling windows provide soft, natural light. The cost of living is from 3300 rubles.
  • The quadruple attic rooms with sloping ceilings are equipped with single beds for the number of guests. There is a small dining table. A large window allows a lot of light to pass through and provides a good view. The cost of living is from 4000 rubles.
  • Studio is the best room in this hotel. There is not only the necessary furniture for living, but even a pool table. In addition to the shower, a bathtub with hydromassage is also provided. The functional areas of the bedroom and living room are separated by blackout curtains. The cost of living is 5000 rubles.

Hotel Policies

Hotel "Old Rostov" operates in accordance with certain rules, which is recommended to familiarize each guest. It is worth paying special attention to the following points:

  • the checkout time at the hotel is considered to be noon;
  • Check-in from midnight to 10:00 is guaranteed only to those guests who previously booked a room;
  • upon registration, a full prepayment is required for the entire planned period of residence;
  • There is no charge for children under 5 years old;
  • a prerequisite for hotel accommodation is the presence of a passport (in the absence of this document, employees have the right to refuse to accommodate);
  • late eviction before 17:00 will be charged hourly, until midnight - 50%, and after midnight - the full cost of the daily stay in the room;
  • it is forbidden to give room keys to strangers;
  • pets are not allowed in the hotel;
  • in case of smoke or flooding, as well as in violation of public order, the administration reserves the right to visit the room without the consent of the guest.

Negative reviews

So that unpleasant moments do not spoil your vacation, it is worthwhile to prepare in advance for the possible shortcomings that characterize the Old Rostov Hotel. Reviews of tourists say the following:

  • in terms of lighting fixtures, there are no bulbs in the rooms (at the same time, contacting the reception does not solve the problem);
  • the rooms have a smell of dampness;
  • interruptions in hot water supply;
  • in a five-story building, the elevator travels only up to 4 floors; guards in the parking lot behave boorishly with guests;
  • there is no curtain in the bathroom, which is why during bathing the whole floor is flooded with water;
  • plumbing needs repair or replacement (faucets are leaking);
  • poor sound insulation (noise from the street, from the corridor and neighboring rooms interferes with the rest);
  • when entering the buildings there are not enough ramps (it is inconvenient to carry suitcases);
  • there is no shelf above the bathroom where cosmetics could be put;
  • few free sockets (if there is more than one guest in the room, you have to charge the gadgets in turn);
