Where is the Odintsovo Pension Fund located?

Few citizens, before reaching retirement age, think about what the Pension Fund is, how it works, and why do we need a pension. Meanwhile, each citizen of the Russian Federation has SNILS as one of the required documents.

SNILS - control and accounting or guarantee

The insurance number of an individual personal account is the account of a future pensioner. This account allows the state to streamline information about working citizens, as well as what amounts the employer pays to the employee‚Äôs account.

However, SNILS allows not only to take into account accumulations. This is perhaps the only document containing a unique digital combination that stores all the personal data of a citizen of the country. It is issued on a personal application (but more often mothers issue it to their children at a minor age) and is subject to replacement only in exceptional cases.

The route known to every resident of the city

Of the nearly two million pensioners in the Moscow Region, more than fifty thousand registered are in Odintsovo. The Pension Fund makes sure that the cost of living corresponds to the average pension in the region. However, not all citizens know that they are entitled to additional benefits in the form of a set of social services or additional payments. You can learn more about this by contacting the Odintsovo Pension Fund.





How to get to the local branch of the Pension Fund? The easiest and most reliable way for senior citizens is to travel by public transport. A minibus or a bus with the number "2" pass by the stop "Marinka". You need to get off at this stop and go to the opposite side of the street. A ten-minute walk on foot will lead you to your destination - on 10a Lyuba Novoselova Street.

In Odintsovo, the Pension Fund operates on weekdays from 9 to 18 hours, with a lunch break from 13:00 to 13:45.

Internet alternative to queues

However, the Odintsovo Pension Fund is always crowded. For a long time, part of the services provided to the population by the state can be obtained via the Internet. Today, older people, having reached retirement age, do not want to just stay at home. They are actively mastering computer technology, using resources that significantly facilitate the lives of citizens. Indeed, through the Internet portal you can get advice, make an appointment, order the necessary information.





Retirement age is not a sentence. This is just a new stage in life, which means for many people the free time that they can devote to their family, grandchildren, as well as themselves.

Who else needs a Pension Fund

But not only older people apply to the Odintsovo Pension Fund. Maternity capital, which has been helping Russian families in raising children since 2007, is also registered at this institution. State assistance consists in compensating part of the expenses of a family that raises two or more children to improve housing conditions, education, pay for treatment of mother and child, or for the purpose of pension savings for mothers.

The Pension Fund in Odintsovo, as well as in other cities and regions of the Russian Federation, not only keeps records of working citizens and their accumulations, but also carries out consulting activities. After all, along with state bodies, the accumulations of citizens are also managed by non-state pension funds.



Pension savings are indexed in the state fund at low rates. Therefore, transferring your funds to a non-state pension fund , you can not only save them reliably, but also significantly increase them. However, it is very important to choose the right fund that will be responsible for the future of citizens who have entrusted their fate to him.
