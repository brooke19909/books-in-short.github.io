Hotel "Altai", Barnaul: address, description of rooms, services

Are you planning to work in Barnaul or just want to change the atmosphere and relax? Do not want to spend a lot of money on accommodation, but at the same time want the hotel to have the necessary amenities? An excellent option in this case could be the Altai Hotel (Barnaul). Why stay here, read below.

Short description





Hotel Altai is a 3-star inexpensive hotel, successfully located in the central part of the city and located in a building that is a monument of Soviet pre-war architecture - it was built in 1941. Moreover, inside the hotel does not look old at all - it was completely renovated in 1990. The hotel will delight guests with bright comfortable rooms, a wide range of delicious dishes in the restaurant, cafe and dining room, a small wellness center, including a sauna and pool, a beauty salon with a hairdresser, free Wi-Fi, a small guarded park and a wide range of services.

Here, business travelers who come here to a conference, training or seminar, as well as people idly resting, will feel great here. Families with children in the hotel will also like it - the kids are welcome here and accept them from any age. And people who are not used to leaving pets in the apartment while they are on a trip will like that pets can be taken with them - the hotel allows pets.

Hotel location

The hotel has an excellent location: the very center of Barnaul and seven minutes walk to the embankment of the Ob River. To get to anywhere in the city, you need only ten minutes to go to the bus stop "House of Unions". Barnaul International Airport is twenty-five kilometers from the hotel, and only four to the railway station.





Being in such an area as the center of Barnaul also means that within walking distance there are many attractions of the city: Nagorny Park, Rodina Cinema, Drama Theater, Dynamo Stadium, City Museum of Local Lore, Culture and Leisure Park, Nikolskaya Church and many others. Near the hotel there are a large number of shopping and entertainment centers, shops, restaurants and cafes.

Number of rooms

Hotel "Altai" (Barnaul) offers seventy rooms of various levels of comfort to accommodate guests. You can choose any of five categories: one-room "Economy", "Standard", "Studio", "Suite" and two-room "Comfort". The rooms can accommodate from one to three people at a time. Each room is decorated in a unique style and exquisitely decorated with vintage paintings and carpets. The rooms are cleaned daily, so the rooms are always clean, towels and linen are changed every few days.





Economy

This variety of rooms offers single, double and triple rooms. The rooms have all the amenities: one, two or three single beds, a wardrobe, a desk, a telephone, wooden furniture, a bedside table, a minibar, a TV with satellite broadcasting.

A bathroom with shower and sink is located on the floor, which is typical in this hotel only for rooms of this category.

Standard

This room type has the same types of room amenities as the previous one. The exception is the presence of a single bed. Rooms of this category can be designed to accommodate one, two or three people.





Each room has a bathroom with shower and sink, towels, cosmetic and hygiene items.

Comfort

This is a two-room double room for the most convenient accommodation of guests: it includes a living room and a bedroom. There are two single beds, upholstered furniture, bedside tables, satellite TV, minibar, telephone and other amenities that are presented in the rooms of the previous categories.

A bathroom with a shower, a washbasin, hygiene and cosmetics, a bathrobe and slippers are in every room.





Studio

This is the largest variety of rooms in size. It can accommodate from one to two people. The rooms have a double bed, upholstered furniture, wardrobe, bedside tables and other necessary amenities. Equipped bathroom is in each room. The stylish and sophisticated interior of this category of rooms will allow you to feel truly at home here and spend an unforgettable vacation in the hotel.

Suite

These rooms are often used to accommodate honeymooners. They are the most comfortable and beautifully decorated. For guests there is a double bed, a minibar, a telephone, upholstered furniture and other amenities presented in all categories. The rooms have a bathroom with shower, sink, hygiene and cosmetics, bathrobe and slippers. The rooms are luxuriously renovated and decorated with expensive carpets.





Nutrition

The hotel has a restaurant with delicious dishes of European and Russian cuisine. Breakfast at the hotel is included in the price of trips in all categories of rooms, except the economy. For guests of this variety, it is possible to order breakfast for a fee. This meal is buffet style.

If you want to have lunch or dinner at the hotel restaurant, it is easy to arrange for a small surcharge. There is also the option of delivering breakfast or food and drinks directly to your room. If you plan to leave on business or for an excursion during breakfast, or if you leave the hotel before it is held, you will be provided with a packed lunch with you.

You can taste light snacks or cold drinks in the hotel bar, this is a favorite place to meet friends or business partners over a cup of coffee and delicious pastries. There is also a dining room around the clock where you can have a tasty and inexpensive meal.





And if you want to try dishes in other institutions, doing this is not difficult. Since the hotel is located in the central part of Barnaul, there are many cafes, restaurants, canteens and shops nearby. In a word, everyone will be able to find an institution to their taste and wallet.

Additional services

Hotel "Altai" (Barnaul) provides a wide range of services for its guests. These include:

  • Free secure parking - for tourists who love to travel by car.
  • Tour desk - so you can immerse yourself in the history of the city.
  • Luggage room - so you are sure of the safety of your belongings.
  • The wellness center, including a sauna and a swimming pool, is for guests who are used to watching their figure even on vacation and on business trips.
  • Transfer to the train station or airport - so you can comfortably get to the right place, especially if you have a lot of things.
  • Transfer to anywhere in the city and order a taxi - if you need to quickly get to the desired point.
  • Beauty Salon - for those who are used to looking great even when traveling.
  • Faster departure or entry - when there is absolutely no time left.
  • Laundry and ironing - so you always look neat and do not bring stale clothes home.

For children

Hotel "Altai" (Barnaul) accepts children from any age. Accommodation of children under five years of age in existing bedding is free of charge. Cots are not available at the hotel.

For business travel

If you want to hold a training, conference or other business event at the hotel, the administration of this hotel will be happy to help you with the organization. There is a conference room with the necessary equipment, photocopying and faxing services are provided. And after a successful business event, you can arrange a festive buffet reception in the banquet hall.

For travelers who come here on a business trip, the hotel is ready to provide documents for reporting or paying taxes.

Posting Rules

Hotel "Altai" (Barnaul), the price of accommodation in which is one of the most affordable in the city, sets the following rules: check-in from 14:00, check-out - until 12:00. Room reservation can be carried out both through the Internet, on the official website or partner sites, and by phone or on the spot, in fact. Cancellation conditions will depend on the room category you choose.

The cost of living for children over five years of age or adults in an extra bed is 600 rubles per day. The maximum number of extra beds in a room is one.

If you want to come to the hotel with pets, you must first coordinate this fact with the hotel staff. The service may be charged.

Contact Information

You can call and clarify the information you are interested in about such a place as the Altai Hotel (Barnaul). Hotel phone: (3852) 23-92-47. By this number you can also book a room and find out about current promotions and offers.

How to get there

Even if for the first time in the city, driving to the hotel on your own is not difficult. Hotel "Altai" (Barnaul), whose address is Lenin Avenue, house 24, is located in the center of traffic intersections near the stop "House of Unions". You can get to it by sitting on any of the following buses: the first, thirty-ninth, thirty-fifth, nineteenth, fifty-fifth, tenth, etc.

You can also take the first or seventh trolleybus or the sixth, eleventh, thirty-fourth, twenty-seventh or one hundred and forty-fourth shuttle buses.

So, if you want to have a cheap and comfortable rest in Barnaul or go on a business trip to this city, the Altai Hotel will help to realize this idea. There is everything for you to spend your leisure time with comfort, but at the same time, the trip will not harm your finances.
