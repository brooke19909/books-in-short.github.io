Rock Water Bay Beach Resort Spa 3 *: reviews

Before buying a ticket offered by the tour operator, it is worthwhile to study the reviews of those people who have already managed to go to this resort. It should be noted that the hotel collected a large number of both positive and sharply negative comments. This significantly affected the rating of Rock Water Bay Beach Resort Resort 3 *. However, everything should be found out in order.



The road to the hotel from the airport. This is the main drawback that can greatly spoil the impression of this place. The road takes about 5-6 hours. It will be especially difficult for families with small children.

Arrangement of rooms. Many say that the apartments are equipped quite stylishly. At the same time, some indicate that the hotel is not new, but with fairly solid furniture.

The view from the window did not leave anyone indifferent. The panorama really opens wonderful.

Regarding food, reviews about Rock Water Bay Beach Resort Spa were very divided. Some say that it is too monotonous. Others argue that for a meal without any frills it is quite good. Asian dishes are somewhat modernized to European ones. There are practically no hot spices.

The beach deserves a separate topic, since it is in search of a unique place to relax under the sun of relaxation that most tourists come here. In addition, at some distance from the hotel there are other beaches, more suitable for a comfortable pastime.

Local attractions were noted by many travelers. In their opinion, there is something to see in the vicinity of the hotel. Thus, the hotel is quite suitable for active tourists.
