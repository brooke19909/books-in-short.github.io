Elias Beach Hotel 4 * (Limassol, Cyprus): description of rooms, services, reviews

The very beautiful Greek-Turkish island of Cyprus fell in love with our tourists for its mild climate, beautiful beaches, the original culture of the Cypriots, which is a mix of Greek and Turkish traditions. There are many wonderful hotels that delight European comfort and Cypriot hospitality, but I especially want to highlight the Elias Beach Hotel 4 *. Limassol, the city where it was built, is considered the second in terms of the number of indigenous people and almost the first in the number of tourists arriving here annually. Despite this, there is no airport in Limassol, and a large international seaport accepts ships from some Asian countries and the largest Greek islands. With other settlements in Cyprus, it is connected only by roads.

There is another interesting feature that is useful to know for those who choose a hotel for their vacation, which sounds in our opinion like Elias Beach. Limassol is a city where a lot of Russian-speaking citizens live. There are even numerous restaurants, cafes and shops where the service is conducted in Russian. This annoys some of our compatriots, but helps someone to feel at home in a distant land. In any case, even those who do not know either Greek or English will not succeed in getting lost in Limassol, as there will always be people on the streets who perfectly understand Russian speech. The same applies to the hotel staff, although the bulk of its guests are English. What other features and surprises await tourists at the Elias Beach Hotel? Limassol and the whole island of Cyprus what will be remembered and what will surprise? To answer these questions, we invite you to a virtual walk around the city and the hotel.

Location

Cyprus, as many are well aware, is divided by a green line into the Greek and Turkish parts. The first went to the southwest, the second to the northeast. It is on the Greek half in the beautiful and warmest bay of Akrotiri in Cyprus that the city of Limassol is located . Elias Beach 4 * was built not in the center, but on the outskirts, where there is less noise and fuss, but more beautiful nature and peace.

But everyone who wants to have a bright and eventful time in one of the resort‚Äôs nightclubs or just stare at the central areas of the city can easily get there by local minibuses or buses. The distance will be approximately 12 km, and the travel time is no more than 15 minutes. You can also go to the center by taxi, which is plentiful in Limassol. They are even called at the reception of the hotel for guests upon request, but not every tourist will like their prices. Those who do not want to go somewhere can entertain themselves with walks along the promenade, which passes just next to the Elias Beach Hotel 4 *. Limassol is proud of many of its attractions, and the promenade is one of them. It not only offers fantastic views of the sea, but also offers wonderful corners of nature, where you can relax. There are cafes, restaurants, shops on the promenade, so everyone will find something for the soul here.

How to get there

The island of Cyprus is mainly hit by plane. Where to fly: to Paphos or to Larnaca (there are flights from Russia to the airports of these cities), it does not make any difference for Elias Beach Hotel 4 * guests. Limassol is approximately the same distance from both, with an average of 65 km or 30 minutes by bus. The only difference is that planes from Moscow, Krasnoyarsk, Krasnodar and Nizhny Novgorod fly to Paphos, and from Moscow and St. Petersburg to Larnaca. There are also flights from Kiev. Regardless of which city the flight was made to, tourists in Cyprus are met by the tour operator of the company where the ticket was purchased, forwarded to the transfer point and delivered to the hotel.





general description

Only positive feedback from tourists about Elias Beach Hotel 4 *. Limassol welcomes them with the hot southern sun, and the hotel staff with warm Cretan hospitality. Check-in, if you managed to clean the room after the previous guests, is carried out here immediately upon arrival. Sometimes you have to wait a bit, but not more than 30 minutes. In any case, there are no strictly allotted check-in hours. The hotel area is not very large. All its buildings and structures are compactly located on 1.2 hectares among palm trees, evergreen shrubs, flowers and emerald green, neatly trimmed lawns. Tennis fans will surely enjoy the tennis courts lit in the evening, while squash lovers will enjoy the well-equipped squash courts.

It is noteworthy that Elias Beach is a hotel with free parking, provided that the car is rented from the administration. In addition to cars, you can rent bicycles here. But the highlight of the hotel is not the rental of vehicles, but the wonderful pools. There are two: indoor with a jacuzzi and a three-level outdoor with a spacious sun terrace around. But for business events, Elias Beach (Limassol) is great. Its conference rooms, designed for a different number of participants, are equipped with high-quality modern equipment. Guests are served by multilingual staff, and during the breaks between conferences or meetings, a buffet is organized.



Accommodation

All rooms are concentrated in a stylish seven-story building, equipped with modern elevators. The view from the windows can be on the territory, the city or the sea, which is desirable to specify when ordering a tour, since after placement, the change of rooms is carried out in rare cases. It is noteworthy that the design of each room is exclusive, everything, including curtains, the color of upholstered furniture, bedspreads on beds, is made in a certain color scheme. It always creates a feeling of comfort and good mood. Some vacationers mention in the reviews that the maids, although they clean very well, but not always in the morning, because they do not have time. Sometimes they arrive after dinner, when the guests have already settled down for a day's rest. This is perhaps the only drawback of their work.

The room categories at Elias Beach are standard and superior. Their area is from 32 to 35 squares. Equipment - beds, wardrobe, table, chairs, easy chair, TV, central air conditioning (works almost silently), TV with paid and free channels, safe (paid for in the standards), hairdryer, multi-channel radio. Sets of summer furniture are installed on the balconies.

The categories of two-room rooms in the hotel are: junior suite, family suite (designed to accommodate up to 4 persons), superior suite. Their area is from 39 to 65 squares. The layout includes a bedroom, a living room and a hygiene room. The equipment of two-room rooms is the same as one-room, with the exception of the living room, furnished with comfortable upholstered furniture. The hygiene rooms in all rooms are equipped with a bath or shower (with a curtain), a toilet and a washbasin. Personal hygiene products are issued on the day of arrival and are regularly replenished. Towels that guests have thrown to the floor (such rules) are changed by the maids on the same day, and pillowcases and sheets are changed every other day.

Leisure outside

Since far from the city center is Elias Beach, its guests need to get to all interesting places and attractions by road. You can use regular buses, minibuses, taxis, but the best option is sightseeing tours. Limassol is located approximately at the same distance between the ancient cities - the small states of Kourion and Amathus - one in the east of Limassol, the other in the west. Now these are city-museums, which are interesting and informative to visit.

Lovers of outdoor activities in Limassol are waiting for night clubs, taverns, bars with live music, shops where all goods are of high quality but expensive. Those hotel guests who are new to Cyprus for the first time can diversify their holiday by visiting many historical monuments of the island, such as ancient Paphos, Hirokitiyu near Larnaca, the medieval Kolossi castle and many others. A boat trip with a swim in the sea and a disco is also popular. But you need to consider that the yacht starts from the city of Ayia Napa, so it will take you more than 2 hours to get to it.

Nevertheless, Elias Beach is more preferable for those who come here for the sake of the sea and a calm, measured rest. Near the hotel there is a promenade where you can easily find a good restaurant, a relatively inexpensive bar, there is even a park and a zoo with unique and funny inhabitants. It is especially pleasant to walk on the promenade in the evenings when the heat subsides and you can admire the sunset with rapture. However, even the most lazy hotel guests are unlikely to deny themselves the pleasure of attending the Limassol festivals if they come to rest during their conduct. In February, a large-scale carnival dedicated to the end of winter is held here, in May - a flower festival, in June - an exotic pagan festival of Shakespeare's Floods and Nights, in July - a beer festival, in August - an Aphrodite festival, and finally, in September - a wine festival, when everyone is invited to try for free the best wines of Cyprus. Riding enthusiasts will also be interested in relaxing at the hotel, as there is an equestrian club just 7 km away. As you can see, there is no time to miss the guests of Elias Beach.



Sea

A beach holiday is what most Russians travel to the Mediterranean for and the most important entertainment for all Elias Beach 4 * vacationers. Cyprus, Limassol especially, because it is located in the most beautiful bay of the island, famous for its clean and well-groomed beaches.
