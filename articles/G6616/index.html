Inventory: what is it, features of carrying out, necessary forms and acts

All accountants are aware that inventory is an indispensable and strictly necessary measure for accounting. No less information about him is possessed by those who are responsible for managing divisions, departments, and the company as a whole. Individual specialists from different departments can take part in the inventory. At some enterprises, this is the responsibility of the special department of material support.

general information

Any accountant can tell that the inventory is a strictly limited sequence of events, each of which is extremely important for the result. The main task of the audit is to make sure that all the material assets that the company has on paper are present in material form.

From year to year, such an audit should be carried out both on the financial obligations of the company and on its property. It is necessary to control fixed assets, other assets. The frequency of inspections, the nuances of their organization, the list of objects that need to be clarified, should be formulated by the leading personnel of the enterprise. It is necessary to create an internal situation, confirm it with an accounting policy. They must issue a thematic order confirming this situation.

The laws of our country also say that the inventory is a necessary and important event, without which it is impossible to do at the enterprise. A special law was passed, published under number 402. It clarifies the situations requiring an inventory without fail.

The legislation obliges to do such a check if the company is preparing to hand over annual reports of accountants, if it is planned to implement it, to transfer some objects under a sale or lease agreement. An inventory is needed if the person responsible for the storage of items changes, if theft is revealed, if something was used incorrectly or deteriorated. The procedure is prescribed for structural changes, emergency.

Nuances and forms

Any manager of an enterprise knows that an inventory is not only an event that is planned in advance, but also an event that can be organized suddenly if the preconditions have arisen. In particular, an unscheduled check is carried out if it is discovered that the client was deceived.

The main condition for organizing the event is the will of the management staff. Checks can be arranged at any time. It is required if unaccounted goods are produced, there is an unplanned sale. A sudden inventory is needed if management wants to make sure that the person responsible for a certain sector of the enterprise is competent.

Responsibility for organizing the inventory rests with the commission. It should have three employees or more people. The head issues an order, which establishes a list of workers included in the commission. She acts constantly.

If there is a need, additional highly specialized personnel are introduced into the group. Each check is carried out with the involvement of all members of the commission. If someone is absent, the outcome of the event is invalidated. Responsible personnel are also required to be present at the audit. Without his participation, the results are not valid.

Event Definition

A monthly, annual inventory and carried out with a different period is an event that involves checking the availability of certain values, monetary obligations. It is produced for a specific date. The obtained indicators are compared with information from accounting documents, discrepancies are revealed.

Inventory allows you to control the safety of material wealth. There are several types of events. Frequency allows you to divide cases into planned, scheduled, and sudden. The first is strictly required and necessary when preparing reports for the year. The sudden is carried out according to the secret schedule of the managing staff or at an unpredictable moment, if there is a reason.

Material, financial inventory conducted outside the well-known schedule, is aimed at identifying the responsibility of employees to fulfill their obligations. Such a check should be sudden for someone who is responsible for the property ex officio - this is the main condition of the event. According to the results, they conclude how competent a person is, whether he faithfully performs his work. Unscheduled events are needed if appropriate circumstances arise. In this case, employees can take the initiative and invite the management staff to organize an inventory.

Many or few?

It is customary to identify complete and partial, selective, forms of inventory. The former involve events in which they recount, compare everything that the enterprise owns. With such a check, there are no exceptions and cannot be. It is possible to organize a partial inventory. For her, it is determined which groups of property are subject to verification, and it is with them that they work. The sample form involves the allocation of positions from a group of goods, items, assets to be checked.

If a check of the second or third type is organized, it was possible to detect some discrepancy, not without a full check. The sample form is most often organized during a pause between the main reconciliation events. These measures allow you to take control of the safety of tangible assets. Managers can track how well accounting is kept.

Who is responsible?

Accounting, inventory - the responsibility of qualified personnel. A special commission is determined by an order issued by the chief manager of the enterprise. It is permanent. Whatever inventory is planned to be carried out in the company, this commission is obliged to be present at the event in full force. She is responsible for the control between inventories.

If the scope of the planned audit is very large, you can create separate specialized commissions. This organization of the workflow makes it possible for a strictly agreed time to check the entire company. This is especially convenient if different sections of the company are far apart from each other. In particular, if a legal entity has structural divisions, the formation of working commissions cannot be dispensed with. In some cases, the optimal solution is the formation of a single group. This works during the current scan, after which it expires.

Targets and goals

Carrying out, proper execution of the inventory, analysis of the results of this event is strictly necessary to clarify how the actual property of the company corresponds to information about it in the reporting documentation. The task of the commission is to determine whether there are objects that were not taken into account, and to calculate how much material is actually consumed in the production process. Inventory (accounting) should identify all shortcomings, detect excess, make sure that compliance with operational standards and rules in relation to property. The commission is responsible for the initial inspection.

Accounting is implemented through recounting, measuring, ascertaining weight. Inventory objects must be checked strictly in those places where they are located in the norm of the work process. During the inspection, you need to find out everything spoiled. Identify that it is no longer suitable for use, as well as materials, items, products whose expiration date has expired.

Key Rules

For an inventory to succeed, it must be continuous. Responsible staff must be in place at all times. The audit must be sudden for the person responsible for the audited property. Only the commission approved by the management has the right to carry it out.

The procedure for organizing an inventory in our country is determined by official orders, decrees, which are issued on behalf of the Ministry of Finance. It is necessary to be guided by the provisions that establish accounting rules, as well as local documents adopted within the enterprise for rationing work.

Among the most important, it is worth noting the order of the Ministry of Finance adopted in the 99th, published under the 49th number. The fact of the need for an inventory is enshrined in the 402nd Federal Law on Accounting. If some aspects of the workflow are not spelled out in these laws, if there is no information in other state documents, you need to adopt a local act and make a provision in the accounting policy.

Inventory and revision

These two events have certain differences. Both of them are held in order to check the movement of property, so sometimes they are confused. The main difference is the purpose of verification. The inventory is aimed at establishing a distinction between the real state of affairs and recorded on paper. Such a check is needed in order to remove errors from accounting led by accounting. An audit, in turn, is required to identify transactions that are conducted in defiance of the law and without reason. The audit is aimed at assessing the appropriateness of the measures, at determining their compliance with the standards.
