Pioneer receivers: reviews, overview, specifications, settings

In those days, when the USSR existed, the word "pioneer" for people who lived on opposite sides of the iron curtain built then was perceived in completely different ways. Residents of socialist countries so called girls and boys who were members of a children's public organization. But in the United States and Western European countries they called a skilled and courageous person, a successful pioneer. In this sense, the Japanese company Pioneer has always positioned itself, preferring to be at the forefront of technological excellence and technological progress. Over its long history, which began on the first day of 1938, this company was engaged in the production of various types of equipment. She owns many palm trees associated with the introduction of advanced technologies in production.





Among the many diverse types of equipment to its consumers, Pioneer offers devices called receivers. What kind of equipment is this, what is it for, and what are its technical characteristics? Let's try to figure this out.

Reasons for popularity

AV receivers are amplifiers in which the built-in receiver is located. For the first time, such devices appeared on the consumer market in the middle of the last century. However, only in recent years have they gained particular popularity in the field of Hi-Fi electronics. The fact is, this technique has become an essential component and the central link of any kind of audio systems, including high-end home theaters, gaming or multimedia complexes.

What are these devices?

The reason for the high popularity of AV receivers is their all-consuming versatility. The gadget has numerous functions, working as:

  • switching center and power amplifier in a multi-channel acoustics system;
  • preamp for analog signals;
  • source selector (from DVD and BluRay to computer);
  • network device;
  • phonoprojector to a turntable;
  • AV processor with digital decoders HD-formats;
  • multi-room system designed to sound several rooms at once;
  • an equalizer with automatic tuning when using a measuring microphone;
  • dock with iPod connectivity.




In connection with such a variety of functions, the choice of such a complex electronic device is not an easy task. At the same time, many experts on similar equipment and inexperienced customers prefer to buy Pioneer receivers. Reviews of the equipment manufactured by this global electronics manufacturer indicate the reliability and quality of the products of the Japanese company.

Device functionality

Before buying, the buyer should understand the technical characteristics that Pioneer receivers possess. The reviews of experts confirm that this technique is the most advanced and is intended for digital broadcasting. At the same time, she copes with her task perfectly, representing a complex hardware complex, which includes a number of modules that perform certain special functions.

In general, the receiver is used to receive and convert signals received by the monitor of the broadcasting device, which may be a TV screen, PC display, etc.

The AV receiver is a functional multi-channel amplifier. It has a built-in radio tuner, as well as a processor designed for spatial sound, which decodes various audio formats, namely:

  • DTS
  • Dolby Digital and others.

A similar function is carried out while switching audio and video signals from various sources.





Modern AV receivers have a large list of connectors required for connection, namely:

  • compositional video and stereo - RCA;
  • component for video;
  • multi-channel video and audio jack HDMI;
  • coaxial and optical, designed for multi-channel sound.

The number and variety of connectors can be different and depends on the class of the device, its functionality, as well as the price category.

Audio calibration

The Pioneer digital receiver is endowed with special features. They are created due to the fact that Pioneer specialists were able to make their own technological discovery. They created a multi-channel acoustics calibration. Ideally, such a technology allows optimal tuning of indicators necessary for a particular room in which listening to audio recordings. In this regard, mostly positive receivers receive Pioneer reviews. Indeed, such a characteristic is their clear advantage over similar products of other brands. Today, depending on the specific function, the company uses 3 levels of such technology:

  1. MCACC, working to measure the size and level of the speaker, as well as the distance to the subject. In this case, a specific autonomous sound adjustment is carried out. Such a system is able to independently make settings based on the information it receives. This allows you to get the highest quality sound effects. At the same time, Pioneer receivers are also endowed with the function of phase control. This allows you to configure the delay of sounds and their equalization in accordance with the phase of other channels.
  2. Advaced MCACC, capable of 3D calibration, as well as measurements at more accurate readings. This is made possible by using the Auto Phase time axis. In addition, this technology allows for the additional Control Plus function, which provides automatic compensation and real-time phase analysis between the main signals. The Standing Wave control function reduces unnecessary sound resonance.
  3. MCACC Pro. This technology is considered the most advanced. It went beyond the functions described above and includes a correction and phase analyzer. At the same time, it becomes possible to group the indicators of the delay characteristics, which are possible due to imperfect filtering systems located in the loudspeaker. At the same time, the adjustment process is activated, providing a smooth stream of reproduced sounds between different channels. The technology provides for the implementation of the tuning function necessary for dual amplifiers, as well as adjusting the position of the speaker in increments of 1 mm. It is worth noting that MCACC Pro works for all channels as a setting for the phase difference existing in the original audio recording coming from the speakers.

Video playback

The Pioneer VSX AV receiver of almost the entire range created by the manufacturer is capable of supporting 4K / 24p / 4: 4: 4 / 36- and 4K / 60p / 4: 4: 4/24-bit signals. As a result, the device allows you to turn on the palette of bright sounds. Each of the devices on the Pioneer VSX AV receiver converts 4K video signals to consistent ones.

In addition to other advantages, the video converter provided in the design of the device can be noted. This element converts a composite video signal into a component one.





He is also able to convert any of the existing types of analog video signals with HDMI, that is, when you connect the following:

  • PC;
  • DVD
  • Blu-ray
  • media player;
  • video cameras;
  • game consoles.

It is worth considering that this function is endowed with models that are in a high price category.

Well, if the Pioneer receiver does not turn on, then you may need to replace the power board due to drying out of the electric capacitors.
