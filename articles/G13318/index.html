Hotel Green Gold Hotel 4 * (Turkey): overview, description and reviews of tourists

A wonderful hotel in Turkey, which is suitable for a budget holiday with the whole family, is called the Green Gold Hotel. It is it that is often recommended by representatives of travel agencies to those tourists who wish to relax in silence, surrounded by beautiful nature and close to the sea.

general information

Green Gold Hotel (certified municipal) was built in 2012, due to which its territory at the moment looks very fresh, the building is quite modern, and the technical equipment of the apartments is at a high level. It is located in a relatively small area - 6200 square meters. m, where developers managed to place a large seven-story building, as well as a garden and a luxurious pool. As tourists say in their reviews of Green Gold Park Hotel, everything is combined quite compactly, but very harmoniously and elegantly.



Location

The hotel is located in one of the most famous resort areas of Turkey - Kusadasi, in the small village of Guzelchamly, which is visited by several thousand tourists every year, including from the CIS countries. There is a rather considerable distance between this place and the center of Kusadasi - 20 kilometers. The distance to the airport is even larger - 110 km. That is why every ticket that travelers purchase, without fail, includes a transfer from the airport. As guests of the hotel note in their reviews about the rest, transportation of a guest from the arrival point to the hotel is carried out quite quickly and in a very comfortable minibus with air conditioning, which is especially important for hot places.

Rooms

At the Green Gold Hotel guests are equipped with exactly 100 apartments, which offer the best conditions for living in the entire district. All available rooms are divided into two categories: standard and family.

All apartments have air conditioning, which can be regulated by the guests themselves. Thanks to this feature, guests staying in rooms do not lose sight of the heat, adjusting the optimal climatic conditions for themselves, regardless of the weather on the street.

Each room also has a private bathroom with all amenities. From the plumbing, a bathtub, a bathroom and a modern shower are obligatory installed here. There is also additional furniture, presented by drawers and cabinets. They include the bath amenities provided for guests, as well as slippers, several bathrobes and towels.

Standard

The rooms belonging to this category are located on a small area - 20 square meters. m. They are all arranged for a comfortable stay of three people, but most often their guests are couples of lovers who visited Turkey for a romantic trip. All apartments of this type consist of one room, each has a private balcony, from where guests can watch a beautiful view of the environment.

Among the furniture, the central place here is occupied by a large double bed, which is installed complete with a single bed. Near each of them there is a small pedestal, one of which has a landline telephone. There is also a place to store tourists clothes. In their free time, they can watch their favorite TV shows on a plasma TV connected to a satellite broadcasting system.



Family

The rooms that belong to this group are located on 30 square meters. m, which are divided into two rooms. At Green Gold Park Hotel (Seliger), family-type rooms can accommodate four people at once. For them, each room has one double bed with orthopedic mattresses, as well as furniture for storing things - here a large wardrobe, divided into shelves, is designed for this. One of the rooms has an excellent working area, represented by a table made of light wood, and a comfortable chair.

One of the rooms has a plasma TV. If necessary, guests can use the safe, telephone and minibar, however this is paid.

This type of apartment has individual balconies with a pair of armchairs and a table - as guests note, you can relax here while admiring the views of the mountains and gardens.

Conditions for children

At the Green Gold Hotel (certified municipal) in Kusadasi, special attention from the administration is given to those guests who come with children. As our compatriots note, this category of tourists is best to be accommodated in family-class apartments, where a cot for a child can be installed on a free basis - for this you need to make a preliminary request.

During the day, children can spend time in a small pool, which is specially equipped for small guests, in compliance with all requirements regarding their safety.

When visiting guests with a child in any catering establishment at Green Gold Hotel, a special comfortable chair is provided for the baby.

Nutrition

Often, hotel guests note in their comments how good food is provided here. All guests who live on an all-inclusive basis can become participants of the buffet every day, which is held three times a day. According to travelers, it offers nutritious and very bright dishes prepared in the best Turkish traditions. There are several food options traditional for European cuisine.



Tourists recommend paying great attention to the assortment of drinks and cocktails, which is available in the bar. According to them, the choice here is truly unique, and bartenders with their art can surprise any gourmet. There is also a menu in which there is a selection of snacks and desserts.

For business

Often, hotel guests are people who come to Turkey on a business trip. In this case, they need to find a place to conduct a business meeting, negotiations or presentation. For such purposes, a conference room is ideally equipped on the ground floor of the Green Gold Hotel. Its area can accommodate up to 150 people at a time, for which there is an appropriate number of seats, as well as tables. The hall is well equipped with modern technical facilities, among which there is light, sound and video equipment. If necessary, stationery can be provided.

Before using the conference room for personal purposes, it must be rented. Rent is paid hourly.



Infrastructure

A doctor is on duty around the clock at Green Gold Hotel (Turkey) - his office is located on the ground floor of the hotel. If necessary, guests can use its services, all of them are paid. Not far from this office there is a currency exchange, as well as an ATM, where you can withdraw cash from plastic cards.

If desired, tourists can use the services of dry cleaning or laundry, as well as ironing - they are also provided only on a fee basis.

You can rent a car or bike at the hotel. Tourists who have been here recommend using such vehicles for independent travel to local attractions, which are very numerous in Kusadasi. They can also go in search of entertainment in the central part of the region, where there are many nightclubs, restaurants, bars, karaoke and other places for entertainment.



Beach

Despite the fact that the Green Gold Hotel (Kusadasi) is not on the first coastline, the hotel has its own plot on the coastline. Its entire territory is covered with sand and pebbles, which, as guests note, are quite safe for children. The descent into the water is gentle enough, which also pleases the parents of the kids.

Sun loungers and umbrellas are arranged in several rows throughout the beach - here tourists can relax and sunbathe for free in between swimming in the sea. Here you can also find locker rooms, showers and a public toilet, which can be used at any time.

Tourists vacationing here often note that when leaving the hotel you need to take a beach towel with you, since they are not provided on the beach.

Reviews

Tourists who rested in a hotel are often advised to spend their holidays here to their subscribers on social networks and relatives. On the Internet you can find a lot of reviews about this place, in most cases bright photos are attached to them, which capture the moments of travelers' stay here, and also reflect the reigning atmosphere.



In their reviews of the hotel, tourists often talk about the decent service that is provided here. They say that the hotel staff is very helpful with guests, they always try to satisfy all their needs. Most of the administrators who are at the reception 24 hours a day speak Russian well, which greatly facilitates communication with them for tourists from the CIS countries.



Travelers pay special attention to the prices at which they are offered trips to the hotel. As a rule, a weekly stay for two in a standard room costs about 45,000 rubles. According to many, this is quite an affordable price, especially considering that it includes a round-trip flight and transfer by a comfortable car with air conditioning installed in it.
