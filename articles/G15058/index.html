Where is the five-star Mardan Palace?

Mardan Palace hotel 5 * is conveniently located on the Mediterranean coast in the resort village of Kundu. Antalya city center is 25 kilometers away. The hotel complex is only fifteen kilometers away from the nearest air harbor. Therefore, upon arrival, you can get to Mardan Palace 5 * Kundu in just half an hour. This is an undeniable plus, especially if you go on vacation with young children. The hotel is also located on the first coastline and has its own clean sandy and equipped with all necessary beach.

Hotel Policies

In accordance with the accommodation rules adopted in Mardan Palace Antalya 5 *, the check-in of newly staying guests is carried out after two in the afternoon. However, if you arrive at the hotel earlier, prudent staff will do their best to provide you with a booked room right away. As for discharge, on the day of departure you must vacate the room before noon. Also, if necessary, you can extend your stay in the hotel complex for several hours, but you will have to pay extra for this. When you check out of the hotel, you must pay for accommodation and use of additional services. If you have already paid the accommodation agency to the travel agency in advance, then you will only have to pay for the additional service if you used it. Payment can be made in cash or with plastic cards.

In the Mardan Palace Antalya 5 * hotel children of any age are allowed. Moreover, if a baby up to two years old will be placed in your room on a crib, then you will not have to pay for his accommodation. Also free is a child under six years old in the parents' room, where an extra bed is provided. Please note that each room can accommodate no more than one extra or baby bed.

As for those who like to travel in the company of their four-legged pets, we are forced to disappoint them. So, according to the rules of Mardan Palace 5 * (Antalya), pets are not allowed in the hotel.

Nutrition

Meals at the Mardan Palace 5 * hotel (Turkey) are carried out according to the "all inclusive", "ultra all inclusive" and "full board" systems. The hotel complex has eleven restaurants, ten of which are a la carte, and fourteen bars. Buffet breakfast and brunch are served at Bosphoros Restaurant. For lunch and dinner, as well as a snack during the day, you can in several restaurants by making an order from the menu. For hotel guests, the best chefs prepare dishes of French, Japanese, Thai, Mediterranean, Russian and Turkish cuisines. In restaurants you can also order vegetarian dishes.





Sea beach

Hotel Mardan Palace 5 * has its own large and equipped with all necessary sandy beach with a gentle entry into the sea. Here you will find comfortable sun loungers, mattresses and umbrellas. Also on the beach there is a bar where you can order your favorite alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks.

Infrastructure

The five-star hotel complex Mardan Palace has at its disposal everything necessary for a great holiday infrastructure. So, here you have at your service a 24-hour front desk, a spacious parking lot for guests arriving at the hotel with their own car or wanting to rent a car, laundry, dry cleaning, mail, room service. Also at the hotel complex you can exchange currency, buy everything you need for your vacation, as well as various souvenirs in a number of shops, visit the spa, hairdresser, and beauty salon. If you want to book various sightseeing tours, Mardan Palace 5 * will be happy to help you with this.

For fans of swimming in the pool, the Mardan Palace hotel complex will be a real find. So, there are two indoor pools (one with sea water), as well as several large outdoor pools. One of them is made in the form of an underwater reef, filled with sea water and inhabited by exotic fish. Several pools are equipped with slides. There are also children's pools.

The hotel has plenty of opportunities for outdoor activities. Here you can play volleyball, tennis and table tennis, squash, bowling, basketball, visit the gym and aerobics classes. The hotel also has everything you need for water sports. Animators work all day in the hotel complex, and in the evenings entertainment shows and discos are held. There are a number of recreational activities designed for the youngest guests of the hotel.





Cost of living

Since Mardan Palace is a truly luxurious five-star hotel, the cost of living is not low here. So, a five-day accommodation here will cost you in the amount of two thousand dollars.
