Hotel Azuro 3 * (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria): photos, reviews

One of the world-recognized and favorite tourist destinations of the Black Sea resorts is located in Bulgaria. It's about Sunny Beach. For decades, it has been growing in popularity and deservedly awarded the blue flag. This resort complex was built along the coast of the sea bay with a wonderful well-groomed strip of golden sandy beaches stretching for 10 kilometers.





Many hotel buildings in the central part of Sunny Beach were erected in Soviet times. However, today they are quite modern and modernized. Basically, their resort niche is a solid economical vacation available for the vast majority of tourists.

It is fair to say that Sunny Beach has been developing in the direction of elite relaxation for the past two decades. Tourists who want to pay for additional comfort, choose the northern part of the resort, built after 1991, characterized by expensive four- and five-star modern hotels.

But back to the first group of inexpensive hotels. These include, in particular, the subject of this article - Azuro 3 * (Sunny Beach). Photos of this popular hotel complex are presented in this article. It is located near the airport - at a distance of a 20-minute autotransfer. Ordering a taxi here is quite simple, you can do it even using the Internet.

One of Bulgaria's favorite hotels

Why do many tourists, having once relaxed in it, for the next holiday season again choose Azuro 3 * (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria)? Their reviews indicate the excellent price / quality ratio of spa services provided by hotel staff.





The hotel complex was completely renovated in 2013. This refers to internal design, communications, infrastructure, equipment of client rooms, furniture. Thanks to such changes, the possibility of a good economy-rest is provided. Here, the administration has excellently adjusted the food of guests. A beach holiday is clearly beyond the scope of three stars.

Rooms

Absolutely different rooms make up the Azuro 3 (Sunny Beach) housing stock: from small families to large families (with two bedrooms and a living room). Tourists in their reviews characterize housing conditions as a solid fourth. This is a solid economy class: good repair, new inexpensive furniture, working appliances, decent basic amenities, a cozy balcony that does not cause discomfort in the bathroom. The hotel rooms offer a spectacular view of the sea. Each of them has air conditioning, TV with Russian channels, minibar. Those who wish to use the safe receive this service at the reception. Shopping

Guests of the Azuro 3 (Sunny Beach) hotel most often use the opportunity to make purchases in the shops of Burgas. The corresponding bus route, which takes only half an hour, is completely tireless. There are two options for the road:

  • With a change in Nessebar (regular buses No. 1 and No. 5, respectively).
  • Direct flight Sunny Beach - Burgas (shuttle bus).

The main shopping street of the city is Aleksandrovskaya. There are many shops selling light industry products. The product is mainly Turkish. There are many fakes "under the company." The Indian clothing store has earned good reviews. The fabrics here are natural, solid. Tourists also mention Tria shopping center, where there is a Paradox cosmetics store, clothing stores Triumph, Salsa.

After strolling through the shops of Alexandrovskaya Street, tourists, as a rule, go for a more serious shopping in the supermarket Burgas Plaza. It should be reached by bus. The road takes 25 minutes. The world's leading brands are worthily represented here: Marc & Spencer, Hugo Boss, Kenvelo, New Yorker, Stefanel, Benneton, Mango, Mothecare, Esprit, LC Waikiki and others.
