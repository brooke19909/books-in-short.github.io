Park Inn in Kazan: address, room selection, reservation, quality of service, additional services and visitor reviews

In this review, we will talk about one of the best hotel complexes located in the capital of Tatarstan, a large scientific center of Russia, the city of Kazan.

Kazan is a multiconfessional city, where, in addition to mosques, there are Orthodox churches, a Catholic chapel, a Lutheran church, and a Jewish synagogue. The city is considered the cradle of the culture of the Tatar people. The Kazan Kremlin is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Here is the second largest Grand Concert Hall, many theaters, museums, a water park, all-Russian and international events are held.

Kazan is considered the leading tourist destination in Russia. She hospitably welcomes travelers not only from the Russian Federation, but also from other countries of the world. Over a million people come here every year, and all of them are looking for a worthy accommodation facility.

Park Inn

The international hotel complex Park Inn (Kazan), the photo of which is presented in the article, offers its customers many services and excellent service at a European level. There is Internet access throughout the hotel, guarded parking, equipped gym, restaurant. For business people there are several rooms for negotiations and important meetings.

The hotel has a level of 4 *, consists of one eight-story building. It was opened in the fall of 2010. Refers to a hotel chain called Park Inn by Radisson.

Location

Park Inn (Kazan) is located in the heart of the city with a rich historical and cultural heritage, in close proximity to the main central streets and cultural attractions. A few steps from the hotel is the Opera and Ballet Theater, a stadium, shopping centers. The Kazan Kremlin is less than four kilometers from the hotel. The international airport is 24 kilometers away. A bus stop is 200 meters away.

Exact hotel address: Lesgaft street, building number 7.

Service and Facilities

Park Inn complex (Kazan) offers its guests excellent service at the proper level. Among the main services provided by the hotel:

  • comfortable rooms;
  • four conference rooms;
  • gym;
  • trendy bar and restaurant;
  • wireless Internet;
  • car parking for three dozen cars;
  • transfer to / from the airport;
  • staff speaking five languages;
  • non-smoking rooms;
  • accommodation with pets.

In addition, the hotel has a luggage storage, ATM, tour desk. Reception is open 24 hours a day.

Dry cleaning and laundry services are available for a fee.

Placements

The Park Inn By Radisson (Kazan) offers its guests 151 comfortable category rooms: standard (132 rooms), luxury (3 rooms), business (16 rooms). In addition, the hotel has two rooms for people with disabilities and specially equipped adjoining apartments.

Each room is equipped with:

  • individual air conditioning;
  • functional working area;
  • satellite tv;
  • the internet;
  • mini-bar;
  • shower;
  • safe.

All rooms have a pleasant calm interior in warm colors. Slippers and a bottle of mineral water are provided for each client. Superior rooms have coffee / tea making facilities, bathrobes, soft drinks.

Cost of living

Standard rooms are offered for 3,450 rubles per day.

Business class rooms will cost 5 thousand 200 rubles per day.

Accommodation in a luxury apartment costs 9 thousand 800 rubles.

For children

Park Inn (Kazan) allows children of any age. Children under 12 years old stay free of charge. Baby cots are available for children up to three years old. The hotel restaurant has a children's menu. For a fee, it is possible to use childcare services.

Reviews about Park Inn (Kazan)

Many consider this hotel to be an excellent option for couples and people visiting the city for business meetings.

The complex is highly appreciated for its location, peace and quiet. For many guests, high-quality and high-speed Wi-Fi plays an important role. Guests who arrived at the Park Inn (Kazan) by car will appreciate the ample parking.

In general terms, judging by the reviews, the hotel has a modern and beautiful interior, cleanliness and order are everywhere, the rooms are spacious with new furniture, comfortable beds and good plumbing. Shower accessories, slippers and a bathrobe are a nice bonus. Guests also note that the hotel has good fitness and spa centers. Breakfast is a buffet with a national corner, the food is delicious and varied. The hotel staff is very friendly, the girls at the reception are always friendly and smiling, they help to solve any problems of the guests. Each client can take a city map and a map from them, where the hotel surroundings, interesting buildings and places within walking distance are marked. It is very convenient that any hotel room can be booked on the Internet, just fill in the necessary columns on the official website of the complex.

As for the cost of rooms in the Park Inn, it is, of course, not the lowest, but quite democratic for a four-star hotel.

Attractions near the hotel

Despite the main purpose of your visit to Kazan, while relaxing in the Park Inn hotel, you should definitely take the opportunity to visit the sights of the city, which are within walking distance from the complex. Namely:

  • house-museum of Lenin;
  • Kazan Arboretum;
  • Pokrovsky's house;
  • Church of St. Barsanuphius;
  • Museum Bucky Urmanche;
  • Shamovskie gate.

Children of Kazan will also like it very much. Not far from the hotel there is a zoological garden and exotarium, where young tourists should be very interested. Many are also advised to visit Butterfly Park and Family Park. And, of course, do not disregard the water park "Riviera".

Conclusion

Park Inn in Kazan is a modern city hotel of a decent standard. The main number of guests are young people and girls who came to the city on vacation or on a business trip. The main advantage of the complex is its location away from city noise and close to the main attractions, shopping centers and places for recreation.
