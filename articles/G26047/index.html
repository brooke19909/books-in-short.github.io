Specifications and reviews: Nikon Coolpix L830

A lot of fun and innovative things happened in the world of cameras. For example, superzoom (optical magnification of 20 or more), compared to the clearly inferior digital zoom of smartphones, is excellent. And there is nothing to compare. And what can we say about the Coolpix L830 camera costing $ 300 with a huge 34x zoom and an equivalent zoom of 22.5-765 mm! And this is not the largest focal length that can be found in such devices.

Features and Design

You can forget about wearing this camera in your trouser pocket. Like all superzoom, the L830 is voluminous - its weight is 508 g, and its dimensions are 111 x 76 x 91 mm. But at the same time, there is no inconvenience when carrying, and it fits well into the pockets of the coat. The camera is available in black, red and plum design with a beautifully textured holder.

Nikon Coolpix L830 Black owners praise the Nikkor lens with a decent maximum aperture of f / 3-5.9 and 34x zoom. At 22.5 mm, it is perfect for shooting landscapes, and can also instantly switch to shooting the top of the Empire State Building, birds on trees and everything that your heart desires.





On the top panel is the really necessary thing - a stereo microphone. This is a step forward compared to the new Canon and Nikon cameras, capable of recording high-definition monaural video. The L830 not only shoots in 1080 / 30p mode, but also records two-channel audio, which is widely appreciated by many reviews.

Nikon Coolpix L830 is equipped with such necessary things as a powerful retractable flash, speaker, power and shutter buttons, surrounded by a zoom switch. For added stability to the left of the lens, there is also a lever for switching between the minimum and maximum focal lengths. The handle is quite deep and the fingers fit well on it, but this is individual, and everyone should hold the camera in his hands to decide whether it is comfortable in his hands. For some, this is unacceptable, but for the average amateur, it will probably not be a problem.





On the back there is a 3-inch tiltable LCD monitor with 921 thousand pixels. Most of the time it works fine, but in strong sunlight there may be problems with its high reflectivity. By default, the brightness is set to 3 out of 6, so when shooting outdoors, you should make sure that the settings are correct - as many reviews advise.

The Nikon Coolpix L830 on the back has a textured thumb place, a button with a red mark for shooting video, and four buttons around the joystick with an OK button in the center. There are buttons for changing modes, playback, calling up the menu and deleting. The remaining keys provide access to flash modes, exposure compensation, macro and timer.

On the right is the power input and a small compartment for USB and HDMI. Below is a compartment for four AA batteries and an SD card. The L830 does not have Wi-Fi, but the device accepts optional Eye-Fi cards for network connections.





What is in the box?

The kit, in addition to the camera, includes a belt, a lens cap with a cord and a USB cable. Nikon also provides 4 alkaline batteries so that you can start shooting immediately without waiting for the battery to charge. Buying a set of lithium batteries makes sense, since it will be possible to take up to 1180 shots, compared with 390 with alkaline. There is also a quick user guide. To save money, Nikon does not include a software CD, but ViewNX2 can always be downloaded from the manufacturer‚Äôs website.

Performance and Usage

Nikon Coolpix L830 camera reviews by owners are called a real compact when it comes to sensor size. It uses a 16MP 1 / 2.3-inch image sensor, which is much smaller than the Micro Four Thirds and APS-C chips. Again, the buyer receives what he pays for. For example, the highly prized Sony RX10 with a 1-inch sensor costs $ 1,000 more!

Reviews Nikon Coolpix L830 called a camera that shows decent results, but nothing more. You can‚Äôt expect excellent performance from the tiny chip in less than optimal ambient lighting conditions. However, despite this, the L830 allows you to get more successful shots than vice versa. Photos are crisp, with nice, realistic colors. Flash portraits come out best, as the face recognition system does an excellent job of shooting at weddings and other social events.





Lens

Reviews Nikon Coolpix L830 is praised for its wonderful 34x zoom. You can quickly switch from wide-angle to extreme telephoto. But it should be remembered that with such an increase, the subject of photography is easy to lose sight of by slightly moving the camera. The best condition for such a survey is stability. A monopod or tripod would be ideal, but any solid support could do. The real plus is the Hybryd VR system, which combines lens shift and electronic vibration reduction. Nikon offers an enhanced 68x digital zoom, but is best avoided to reduce noise.

Functionality

The L830 is a compact camera. It is enough to press the green auto button, and there will be four options: auto, smart portrait, special effects (11 options), scenes (18 options) and a simple car. In automatic mode, it is possible to adjust ISO, white balance, resolution, color and AF area.





Video

The video shot on the Nikon Coolpix L830 Red, the reviews of the owners are called uneven. It takes a few seconds for the camera to adjust the exposure, and in dim lighting there are difficulties with focusing. But the lens mechanism works silently, which is convenient when recording audio. As with photographing, everything will be fine as long as there is enough sunlight.

Photosensitivity

The ISO 125-3200 range is only a small part of what is offered by many modern models. But, given the size of the sensor, this can only be rejoiced. Color reproduction is accurate, and digital noise is acceptable up to ISO 400, with pretty good 800 results.

Verdict

Nikon Coolpix L830 reviews by professionals are called a camera with the makings of a more advanced camera, which in reality is a good compact with a super zoom lens. It is suitable for amateurs striving for something more than a regular smartphone, but will not impress those who used higher-class equipment. But at a price of $ 300, the camera does not try to be something that a small sensor will only interfere with.
