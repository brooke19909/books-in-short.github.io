Bluetooth headphone rating for phone: top best, customer reviews

Every year, the 3.5 mm connector is less and less relevant. Therefore, wireless headphones are becoming an increasingly important purchase. However, there are more and more of them every month. Choosing the right pair of devices can be much more complicated because of this. The bluetooth headphone rating in this article will help the reader understand which model is best for him. Several categories of devices will be presented.

Choosing on-ear headphones or earplugs, vacuum ones, in-ear earphones is a moot point. There should always be an individual approach related to the ease of use of each form factor by an individual. Once you have established what you need, then you can decide whether wireless is the best option for you.

If you prefer flying over long distances, be sure to use on-ear headphones with the ANC (active noise reduction) function. If you are actively involved in sports, then designer models may be more convenient.

After all this, you will need to find the model that fits into your budget. Some devices have an overpriced price tag, and this does not always indicate a better build or sound quality.

This article will help you make the right choice. Below is a ranking of bluetooth headphones that span different segments and shapes: from excellent ANC noise canceling devices to low-cost and sports kits.

Currently, buyers mostly leave a lot of positive reviews about the model Sony WH-1000XM3. These headphones are the leader in the ranking. However, their form may not suit someone. The main reason is convenience - wireless headphones offer unprecedented freedom from tangled cables, not to mention the connectors on the gadgets. What to look for when buying

The first question when buying a pair of wireless headphones: why are they needed and how much should they cost? High cost does not always provide stable performance and better materials. If you purchase such a product for the sake of innovation and popularity, it is better to consider gadgets at a price not exceeding 10 thousand rubles.

Bluetooth in-ear headphones are a great choice for sports and are often waterproof for outdoor workouts (not to mention sweat). However, it is important to consider the structure of the auricle. Therefore, it is necessary to check whether additional pads and inserts of different sizes are included with such devices.

Overhead models have become the most popular for everyday use. Such headphones are usually a little smaller and cheaper, but by design they are not always the most comfortable, especially for those who wear glasses. This form, according to reviews of many buyers, is convenient, but with prolonged listening and lack of ventilation, the ears can become very hot.

Battery life is another factor that ranges from more than 20 hours in larger headphones to just three hours in fully wireless models. They are good for most trips to work, but not so convenient for long trips.

For the best sound quality, look for support for aptX or aptX HD (Sony offers its own solution called LDAC). The lack of additional noise ensures the best playback of tracks, and also allows you to escape from the hustle and bustle of loud conversations.
