What water park in Astana to choose for your vacation. Overview of water parks in the capital of Kazakhstan

In the summer heat, everyone dreams of a vacation on the sea coast. And in the cold winter, one wants to travel somewhere to Bali or Thailand, soak up the white sand under the rustle of waves in the shade of palm trees. There is no sea in Astana. But there are as many as three water parks! Which one to choose for your vacation? In this article, we describe each of the three water parks in Astana. And the choice is yours.

For the capital of the country, three water parks - this may be not enough. For example, in Almaty there are more such institutions. But it should be taken into account with what futuristic scope Astana was built. And it is also reflected in the city‚Äôs water parks. Oasis Beach Club

This is a completely new and so far the only water park in Astana in the open air. The emphasis here is on a relaxing beach holiday. This is an alternative to the not-so-clean Ishim River and Taldykel Lake in the Kazakh capital. Thus, the slides here to regret are few. And they are intended primarily for babies. But in abundance there are sun loungers and beach umbrellas - free for visitors. Also included in the price of the ticket is the use of rubber rings and mattresses. And even sunglasses can be given to you if you forgot yours at home. Do not worry if the weather is cool outside. Pool water is heated to comfortable temperatures. The purification systems here are of the latest generation - you will not feel any smell of bleach. In addition to filters, once a day the tanks are also freed from small floating debris. There are three pools in the Oasis Beach Club: for the smallest (20 centimeters deep), children's (one and a half meters) and adult (2.5 m).



Central water park in Astana: cost, location, opening hours

This place for summer holidays is located in the Central Park of Culture and Rest. Getting to it is easy. It is located on the corner of Gogol and Baribaev streets. The park is officially called "Astana", but locals often call it "Gorky's Name". This water park in Astana works seven days a week. From Tuesday to Sunday, it opens at 11 am. And only on Mondays in the water park a short working day. He opens the door to the first visitors at 14:00. And the institution always completes its work at the same time - at eight o‚Äôclock in the evening. The ticket price depends on whether it is a weekday or a weekend. In the first case, it costs five thousand tenge (900 rubles), and in the second - seven (1270 rubles). The price is for visitors who have crossed the age limit of twelve years. Kids who are not yet three years old, the passage to the water park is free, but subject to the accompaniment of adults. Children from 3 to 12 years old must pay three thousand tenge (550 rubles) to the cashier on weekdays and four (725 rubles) on weekends.





Reviews about the Oasis Beach Club

Visitors claim that this water park in Astana is a great alternative to spending time on the banks of a river or lake. Water is pure, sun beds - as much as you want. On the territory there are playgrounds, a place for beach volleyball, a cafe with affordable prices. The water park is new, and every year there are attractions. In mid-2017, there are already eight slides for adults. Plus countless mushrooms, fountains and other entertainment for the youngest visitors. However, one should not forget that the pools are in the open. In the rain or cold, your stay there will not seem so pleasant.
