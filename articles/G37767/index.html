Sanatoriums of the Urals with a pool and mud therapy





It's no secret that the Urals is one of the main pearls of the Russian Federation. The Urals are a real treasury of natural resources, which are also healing sources - for those who want to improve or maintain their health here. In this region, a huge amount of all kinds of mineral waters, and many sanatoriums in the Urals with a pool use these waters for swimming and hydrotherapy directly from sources. There are more than four hundred health resorts here, let's look at some of them.

The best sanatoriums of the Urals

The most famous and popular are the resort of Ust-Kachka with the Obukhovsky sanatorium, erected on the hydrocarbonate-chloride-sodium mineral water deposit, the blazing Mount Yangan-Tau and the Rodon resorts of Uvildy, Chelyabinsk Region, Lipovsky Sverdlovsk Region.



Since water with a high content of iodine and bromine is quite common in this region, sanatoriums and health resorts did not fail to take advantage of this. And now, at the sources of these thermal waters near Khanty-Mansiysk, the four-star sanatorium (Wellness Hotel) "Ugra Valley" and the sanatoriums of the Urals with a swimming pool in the Tyumen and Sverdlovsk regions (for example, the resort of Tavda) have opened their doors.

Sanatoriums of the Urals by regions

- Sverdlovsk region. Mud therapy with sapropelic mud from the lakes Kurtuguz, Moltaevo, Akhmanka, Maly and Bolshoi Taraskul, and the Gorbunovsky field is actively used here.

- The Chelyabinsk region provides mud treatment services from the Khommutinsky group of lakes, in particular, at the Bagrovsky Mineral Waters resort.

- The Kurgan region is famous for the resorts built on the inland lakes Medvezhye and Gorky, the main healing factors of which are sulfide silt mud and brine.

- In the Orenburg region , the Sol-Iletsk resort, which was built on the mud deposit of salt lakes, unique in nature, has been successfully operating and taking treatment for several years.

In addition to mud treatment, a wide range of services is provided by almost all sanatoriums in the Urals, their prices can be found on the official websites of health resorts. So, for example, the boarding house "Karagaysky Krai" offers trips worth from 1615 rubles per person per day (with treatment and food). "Uvilda" will cost 20 rubles more, and in the sanatorium "Kisegach" the initial price per person starts from 1740 rubles per day. In many resorts there is a flexible system of all kinds of discounts, promotions are held.

Range of sanatorium services

Ural sanatoriums with a swimming pool can offer not only water procedures and mud therapy that has become popular. Almost in each of them you will be offered massage and physiotherapy, physical education and exercise therapy, a balanced balanced diet.

In addition, the sanatoriums were erected in the most beautiful places with favorable environmental conditions, the administration takes care of the comfort of the rooms and the organization of the entertainment program so that the rest becomes not only useful, but also interesting. Sanatoriums of the Urals with a swimming pool accept whole families for recovery, including with young children.
