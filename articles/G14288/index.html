The Orange House Hotel (India, Goa): Travel Photos and Reviews

If there is Paradise in the world, then it can be found in India, in the smallest and most comfortable state of Goa. Each traveler will find here picturesque landscapes, and gentle beaches, and the refreshing breeze of the Arabian Sea, and, of course, hospitable residents. The Orange House Hotel is an ideal place that combines all the charms of Indian nature and quality service. Let's get acquainted in detail with its features.





Location

Currently, India is quite a popular country in terms of tourism. This is due to many factors: rich natural resources, religious centers, oriental practices and much more. It is not surprising that every year more and more hotels open here, which are ready not only at affordable prices to accommodate tourists, but also offer them original entertainment programs. The Orange House 1 * is no exception.

North Goa (India) is a picturesque place with a subequatorial climate. The southwestern part of it has access to the sea. It is here, in the village of Anjuna, that the hotel is located. You can get to it by taxi or bus from the airport, located near the village of Dabolim. The distance is approximately 45 km. This information is relevant for those who travel on their own. And the cost of tourist tickets, as a rule, includes a transfer from the airport and back.

Description

Orange House Hotel was founded in 2013 and got its name for a simple reason. It has a bright color finish, which stands out sharply in the general landscape. Although tourists are inclined to a different version, believing that such an unusual name corresponds to its home environment.

The two-story building has an area of ‚Äã‚Äã250 square meters and includes only 12 comfortable rooms. Thanks to its modest territorial scale and spaciousness, this service has received 1 * status. The rest of the service is quite high quality.



The surrounding area is well maintained. In general, The Orange House has created an atmosphere of comfort, cleanliness and pleasant relaxation. You can get acquainted in more detail with the atmosphere and nuances of the service with the help of travel sites. Recently, tours to The Orange House have become increasingly popular. Photos, price nets, conditions of arrival, accommodation and a range of services - all this can be seen and found out in advance.

Rooms

The hotel has several types of rooms. Here couples or a group of friends can relax in comfort. For this, double and connecting rooms with one double and two single beds are provided. The rooms are modestly but tastefully furnished. The interior continues the sunny theme of the hotel.

Each air-conditioned room is equipped with a fridge, cable TV, free Wi-Fi, a telephone, a balcony and a bathroom with shower. Unfortunately, tourists vacationing with children, the hotel cannot offer lullabies and cribs. But the service of an additional guest in the room is valid.

The windows of the rooms offer a magical view of the square, palm trees, the sea and the beach. Cleaning and linen change in the rooms is carried out with a frequency convenient for guests.



Services

The list of hotel services for one star is quite wide. The Orange House includes laundry, free parking, a 24-hour reception, an internet room, scooter rentals for walking or moving around the village. Near the hotel you can find a bank, an evening market, working only on Saturdays, a supermarket and, of course, the beach.

The Orange House does not have a restaurant or bar, but you can taste delicious national and European dishes in the many cafes located near the hotel. Also, some rooms are equipped with a kitchen and everything necessary for it (utensils, kettle). If you have the necessary products, without leaving the room, you can cook yourself something tasty or arrange an evening tea party on the balcony. Or all this can be transferred to the ground floor in a specially equipped breakfast room.

Entertainment

The entertainment program of the Indian service The Orange House includes active tourist excursions, hiking, wellness practices (yoga, mandala). You can also sign up for exciting marine activities (sea kayaking, diving, etc.). For people who first encountered extreme sports, training workshops are provided.



Beaches

There is no specific beach area that The Orange House would have. India from the southwestern part is washed by the Arabian Sea. The coastline here stretches for tens of kilometers. It is conditionally divided into private and public areas. Therefore, tourists who relax at the hotel can freely visit the village beach of Anjuna or the private Baga and Vagator. There are bars, cafes, umbrellas and deck chairs for rent, lifeguards and boat services on duty.
