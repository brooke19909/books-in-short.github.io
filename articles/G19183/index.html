How to choose a dehumidifier for the home. Dehumidifiers household: reviews, prices

The optimal humidity mode in the house is needed not only to create a comfortable microclimate. Excessive condensate levels can be harmful and even dangerous. For example, excess moisture contributes to the deterioration of furniture and finishing coatings, and also acts as a favorable environment for the development of mold and fungus. It cannot be said that the fight against humidity is more difficult than with dry air, but in rainy regions you cannot do without special equipment. In the normalization of microclimate indicators, household air dehumidifiers that can be used in residential or utility rooms are effective. Manufacturers offer options for such devices with different characteristics, so you should initially familiarize yourself with the operating parameters of the devices.

Key selection criteria





To begin with, it will be useful to determine the principle of operation of the dehumidifier. There are three types of devices: action-based assimilation, condensation-based and adsorption models. It is for the home that condensate collectors are considered an acceptable option. Next, you should determine the power. The time during which household dehumidifiers completely harmonize the microclimate, depriving the room of excess moisture, depends on this indicator. The optimal level is 400-500 watts. Conclusion





The latest models of modern dehumidifiers are equipped with precise sensors for tracking microclimate indicators, automatic adjustment systems, various indicators and ergonomic handles. However, do not forget that some devices recycle air, not replacing it with street air. This is an important point of choice, since a dehumidifier for a home should either renew the air itself, or work in conjunction with ventilation. It should also be noted that climatic equipment is very demanding on maintenance. Hours of air filtration will certainly lead to contamination of the exhaust and intake grilles. Accordingly, once a month or even more often it is necessary to carry out a full cleaning of the device in order to extend its service life.
