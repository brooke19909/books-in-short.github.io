A brokerage company is ... Brokerage companies of Russia: rating

A brokerage company is an organization that provides intermediary services in the market. Such firms can be found in various highly specialized areas. For example, in exchange trading, insurance, shipping, and so on.



Stock Market Intermediaries

A stock brokerage company is a professional trading participant. This company concludes transactions at the expense and on behalf of the client who hired it. A private investor, as well as a large organization or a fund, can act as a customer. The benefit that customers receive is that investors who do not have the necessary permissions participate in the auction. Technical assistance to the client is provided by a brokerage company. This, among other things, allows the investor to keep abreast of the latest news, to know the nuances of the current market situation. The intermediary, thus, helps the client to make profitable transactions.

Insurance and leasing

Intermediary functions in these areas of activity can be combined, since the tasks that are solved in them are similar. In this case, the purpose of the brokerage company is to ensure that the insurance or leasing services provider contacts the client. In some cases, the intermediary receives a discount from organizations. This, in turn, allows him to make better offers to customers than if they contacted a leasing or insurance company directly.

How to choose an intermediary?

There are so many deals on the market today. Existing high demand creates a corresponding supply. Consequently, a variety of brokerage companies are present in the market. Reviews of intermediaries are one of the key selection criteria. It is desirable, of course, that the opinion was from real, and best of all, familiar people, business partners. An important point is the period during which brokerage companies operate. The rating of intermediaries operating in the market, as a rule, also contains the main technical and financial indicators of the organization. These, in particular, include access to analytics and the size of the commission. In margin trading, the key parameters, on the basis of which the client decides to cooperate with a broker, are the minimum deposit and leverage. Undoubtedly, an organization providing intermediary services must be licensed.



Work specifics

Before making certain operations with assets, a credit brokerage company carries out its own analysis of the military, political, economic situation in the state. The organization studies the tax policy, finds out what is the country's attitude towards investors. Applications are accepted by brokers in electronic and paper form. Telephone conversations are also available (calls are usually recorded). A necessary condition for cooperation is an agreement with a broker. It is either in electronic or paper form. The document must clearly state the terms of cooperation. In particular, the site on which the auction will be conducted, the procedure and timing of the transfer of money to the client, the report form and so on are indicated.

Comparison criteria

Existing brokerage companies can be compared according to the following criteria:

  1. Year of foundation.
  2. Reliability rating.
  3. Trade turnover for all customers of the organization. This criterion allows you to determine the place that the company actually takes in the investment field.
  4. The degree of development of the regional network.
  5. Level of information support (does the company provide training courses, is there daily analytics, a personal consultant, are online quotes visible, is it possible to register a demo account).
  6. The amount of overhead. In particular, the commission for intermediary services, the percentage for crediting and withdrawing funds, the price of depository services, and the cost of software are estimated.

The most important point is the last. Often, it is the size of the overhead that determines the choice of a broker.

Banks

The above companies are mainly brokerage. For them, mediation is the main activity. A number of banks operate in Russia, which also provide brokerage services. This business acts as an accompanying one for them. It is believed that companies that directly provide brokerage services work better, since this is their main activity. However, this is not quite true. In practice, the bank's commission for intermediary services is much lower than that of brokers. This is explained by the fact that they do not need to additionally finance offices and train employees to work in the market. This can be done by existing bank employees. Specialized investment companies need to spend money on this - part of their direct income. Therefore, their commission is higher than that of the bank.
