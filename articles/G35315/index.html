Hotel Ban Nam Mao Resort 3 *, Thailand, Pattaya. Overview, description and reviews of tourists

Hotel Ban Nam Mao Resort 3 * is not quite ordinary for Pattaya. True, she is not by the sea, but not in a noisy alley. Here you can find the same comfort and silence as in island resorts such as Koh Samui or Koh Phangan. If you want a family, relaxing or romantic getaway in a crazy resort like Pattaya, then choose Bannammao Resort Pattaya. An overview and description of the service at this hotel, as well as analysis of guest reviews, can be found in this article.



Suburbs of South Pattaya

The hotel we are describing is located near Jomtien Beach. In fact, this is a separate village, but reviews of the Bannammao Resort 3 * hotel (Pattaya) describe it as a resort area. There is some kind of homespun truth in this, since the center of the resort has grown and many surrounding villages have already entered there. This happened with the Jomtien Beach area. Unlike the rest of Pattaya, this is a very family resort. Here are wonderful conditions for families with children. This area stretches along the Gulf of Thailand. The resort infrastructure is located very close to the sea and beaches. There are many green corners and almost bucolic places.

Quiet, especially in the evenings. There are no shows or loud discos. But a lot of not very expensive hotels, hostels and guesthouses. There is a night market, many small shops and lots of restaurants. Therefore, those who choose to vacation in South Pattaya should think what they need - peace of mind or nightlife. If you want to wander around Woking Street every night, then you have to go there by transport. And if your goal is a serene pastime on the beach, then these places are just for you! Some of the sights of Pattaya are located near Jomtien. This is a floating market, a dinosaur park and a big Buddha.

How to get there

From Bangkok to the hotel Ban Nam Mao Resort 3 * (Thailand, Pattaya) you need to get three hours by bus or transfer. Most public transport from the capital stops near the North Road. From there, you can get to the hotel with a tuk-tuk for about forty baht. There is a special bus from Bangkok that stops right on Beach Road. It costs about one hundred and fifty baht. Tourists who do not have a very large number of bags and suitcases can ride a motobike with a breeze.



Location and what's nearby

If you want to visit the center of Pattaya and plunge into its entertainment and nightlife, then get there in just ten minutes by transport. A free bus runs from the Ban Nam Mao Resort 3 * to the city, which greatly facilitates the cares of guests and does not burden their wallet. It delivers guests to South Pattaya. Not very far from the hotel are some interesting sights. The Big Buddha statue is on Khao Chi Chan Hill, only a quarter-hour drive from the hotel, and the Phoenix Golf Club is ten minutes away. True, the hotel does not stand right on the beach, but, on the other hand, this determines its seclusion.

Not far from the Bannammao Resort hotel (Thailand, Pattaya) there is a Seven-Ileven chain store and several cafes with affordable prices. Fifteen minutes on foot is the Ambassador Hotel complex. There are many gift shops and fruit shops.



Territory

Hotel Ban Nam Mao Resort 3 * as if immersed in the lush greenery of a tropical garden. There are two buildings and a bungalow. Paths are laid to them. There are many decorative ponds in the garden. The hotel is surrounded by private houses. Squirrels jump on trees, tropical birds sing. Tourists call the hotel garden a paradise in the midst of the bustle of the city. Everything is very well maintained, and the hottest time is best spent here, in this oasis.



Rooms

Hotel Ban Nam Mao Resort 3 * is quite small. There are only twenty rooms. A choice of guests they are offered to settle in a bungalow or double standard. All rooms are air-conditioned, have their own bathrooms with bathtubs or showers. From cosmetic products give shampoo and shower gel. There is a TV with satellite channels. He catches even one Russian. Each room has a balcony or terrace. They are very large, and there are two chairs and a table.

The rooms catch free wifi, but it is best to use it at the reception. There is also a minibar, but only water is free there. The rooms are spacious. The floor is tiled, there is a mirror, a coffee table, two armchairs. Bungalows consist of two rooms with a common terrace, and filling, as in the building. Living there is better because there are few neighbors and no unpleasant surprises. Check in the rooms immediately upon arrival, without delay. No deposits are taken. Sheets and towels are very clean, without stains. The cleaning is good: the floors are washed not only in the rooms, but also on the balconies. There is no hairdryer in the bathroom, so you need to take your own if you want to have a good hairstyle. The plumbing is serviceable, there is always hot water, without interruptions.



Services

The management of the hotel Ban Nam Mao Resort 3 * (Pattaya) offers guests many services and entertainment. The staff provides transportation from and to the airport, room service and food delivery. Soiled clothes are washed and ironed, and you can also put them in dry cleaning. The hotel has a massage parlor. The staff is very friendly. Beach towels are given out - they can be taken at the counter opposite the reception. There is also a boiler with boiling water: in the evenings, guests make themselves tea. There is a safe in the lobby where you can hand over valuables, but it only works until eight in the evening. There are no thefts in the rooms. Tourists claim that they often forgot there tablets, money, phones, and everything was in place.



Nutrition

Ban Nam Mao Resort 3 * (Thailand, Pattaya) has its own restaurant. In addition, the hotel has two more bars, one of which is by the pool. Room rates include breakfast. To use this service, you need to present a special coupon in the restaurant. They are issued upon settlement. The breakfast menu includes one hot dish to choose from (soup, omelet, fried eggs), and vegetables, fruits, toasts, tea, coffee are served in buffet style. But if you want to have lunch or dinner, you will always be fed. They will cook even at midnight, if you wish.

Among the neighboring cafes, tourists advise the institution "Play". They include Russian-language music, and the owners create a cozy atmosphere. There are inexpensive establishments with good food on the beach. In the evenings, you can eat at the night market near the Ambassador complex. And in the very north of the resort, on the territory of the Pattaya Park Hotel, there is a restaurant with the best view of Pattaya.

Beach vacation

To the sea from the hotel to walk about ten minutes. The nearest beach to the resort is Jomtien. This is one of the best beaches in Pattaya. It is equipped, clean, there are sun loungers, chairs, umbrellas. They do a good massage here. Tourists say that if you already have a swim in Pattaya, then it is here. No noise from cars and scooters, beautiful sunsets, not crowded. The coast is long and wide, and in the very north of the resort is almost empty. Only in the distance you will observe the stone jungle and skyscrapers of the center of Pattaya and rejoice that you stayed here. Rental sunbeds paid. But if you buy at least something in coastal cafes, such as beer, you can sit on their sun loungers under umbrellas all day.

The hotel has an outdoor pool where you can take spa treatments, in particular, sit in the jacuzzi. For children, a special swimming area is equipped. The pool is open until late at night. Those who are too lazy to go to the sea relax on free sunbeds and bask in the warmest whirlpool. By the way, if you come to the beach at the Ambassador Hotel, you can swim in their huge pool right on the sea.



Excursions

One of the most popular destinations for hotel guests is the Blue Lagoon. This is the beach of the former military base in the Ambassador area with crystal clear water of amazing color and paradise sand. This was one of the first resorts to open in Pattaya. Many tourists visiting the hotel prefer island tours. From here you can go to Koh Samet, enjoy a kind of corner of paradise life. Travelers praise the Princess Island, which is especially good for divers. Near it is excellent clear water and a rich underwater world. Well, just to swim, it is better to take a ferry to Koh Lan. To do this, you need to get to the Big C store on a white tuk-tuk, then on blue to Woking Street, and there is a seaport nearby. From there to Koh Lan carry thirty baht. There is a beautiful clean beach, good water and small waves.

Bannammao Resort 3 * (Pattaya): prices

The cost of a hotel room starts at one thousand seven hundred rubles per night. Eating at a hotel restaurant costs about fifty baht per meal. It can be ordered directly to your room for the same money. The hotel bar is not very expensive cocktails. They cost sixty baht. Hot drinks - thirty each. Tourists are advised to take American dollars in large bills - fifty and one hundred each. So it‚Äôs more profitable to change to baht.

At the night market in the center, cheap food and clothing. At the fruit market you can buy fresh pineapples for fifteen baht apiece. Local exotic fruits, leather bags and clutches, cosmetics (creams, balms), rum are brought from Pattaya. Massage on the beach costs about three hundred baht, and in the city center - two hundred.

If you buy guided tours, tourists do not recommend taking them at the hotel. Better to use local stalls. You will pay one and a half to two times less, and you will ride on the same bus as those who gave more money. A taxi to the "Blue Lagoon" carries for 400-500 baht, so it is more profitable to go to a military beach in company. Tourists say that it is cheaper to travel with a local driver. Entrance to this beach costs 100 baht, and a taxi driver will buy you tickets at a special discount for Thais.

If you use tuk-tuk, never give a specific address, just press the button to exit to the right place. Otherwise, the driver will consider that you hire him as a taxi and take it at exorbitant prices.

Ban Nam Mao Resort 3 *: reviews

Guests praise this hotel, naming its main advantages - silence, greenery around and the proximity of the sea. The atmosphere in the hotel is very comfortable and homely. When tourists are in the center and see how noisy and crowded there, they are doubly happy that they chose this hotel. Many even call it amazing. It is best to take either bungalows or rooms with pool views.

The hotel is very suitable for relaxing in a calm and relaxing environment. When tourists compare the Ban Nam Mao hotel with the Ambassador, they say that the latter is too crowded and looks like an anthill. And here people go not for parties, but to a completely different Pattaya - quiet and comfortable. Among the vacationers are decent Russians, British and Germans.

Tourists also wrote that in Jomtien you can make a good shopping. At the intersection of Sukhumvit and Tepprazit, there are several shopping centers, including Tesco Lotus, Home Work and Metro. Prices there are calculated for local residents, so you can buy different products cheaper than in the center of Pattaya.
