History of the invention of TVs

The first device for receiving images was invented by a German scientist in 1884. This device did not even remotely resemble the usual TVs. And only in 1906 a device with a twenty-line screen was introduced. Its size was only 3 x 3 cm.

The country saw the first Soviet television in 1932. Compared to modern models, it seems very primitive. However, at that time this unit was considered an unattainable dream. After that, the production of TVs increased significantly, and almost every year some innovations were introduced.

Modern models

Currently, every year technical innovations appear that make our life more comfortable, brighter and more interesting. The technique is becoming better and more perfect. Manufacturers are constantly increasing its functionality, changing sizes and design. These changes have affected TVs. Their specifications have improved significantly. The UltraHD 4K format has appeared, so the image has become more clear and detailed.

Since the invention of the television, many changes have been made. Viewers can now enjoy watching videos with superior sound and realistic images. Modern monitors allow you to watch movies in 3D-format. Today's TV models are designed not only to watch movies, but also to access the Internet, because they are equipped with a Smart-TV system.
