Features of Marmaris: nature, climate

The resort is located in the subtropical zone and is surrounded by mountains that close a small bay from strong winds. Snow-white sandy beaches do not know the storms, but away from the coast strong winds can raise big waves. In general, the climate can be described as mild and dry. The sea warms up already in early May, just at this time the beach season begins here, which lasts until October. Keep in mind that the Aegean is a little cooler than the Mediterranean. The maximum water temperature is +26 o C. The hottest and driest months occur in the middle of summer (July and August). At this time, you need to use creams with the maximum protection factor. People who suffer from high blood pressure are advised to come on holiday in Marmaris in other months.





Three wonderful islands are located in the bay, on which spreading pine trees grow. The nature of the resort fascinates with its beauty: these are mountain landscapes, forest groves, flowering shrubs, exotic trees and palm trees. In parks, they often offer to make an interesting sightseeing tour on a horse. Along the quiet coast you can take a yacht ride and see from the sea a beautiful bay with clean beaches, mountains covered with bright green vegetation.
