Open a bank account for an individual entrepreneur. Which bank to open a current account for IP?

Private entrepreneurs can use a checking account, but this is not necessary. It may be required for business activities. It is necessary to open a current account for an individual entrepreneur in a bank if there will be a large turnover of non-cash funds in work. Which bank is preferable to issue it will be described in the article.

Why is it needed?

It is necessary to open a current account for individual entrepreneurs in a bank for business development. This is especially important if the implementation is planned, the purchase of goods. By law, the entrepreneur is prohibited from paying or receiving for goods more than 100 thousand rubles under 1 contract.

Large businessmen also need cashless payments. If you open a checking account for an individual entrepreneur in a bank, this will allow you to work with large counterparties. Cash flow of more than 100 thousand rubles should go through the account. Settlement and cash procedures, remote servicing, and currency control are conducted with him.



How to open?

After opening a bank account, it must be indicated in the tax account and registered. If the organization is not notified in a timely manner, a fine may be imposed. Documents that the entrepreneur received must be transferred to the tax office. If necessary, it can be closed. This procedure is also not complicated.

Documents

To open a current account for an individual entrepreneur in a bank, the following documents are needed:

  • passport;
  • IP registration paper;
  • an identification number;
  • certificate of tax;
  • special license;
  • statements.

This is a standard list of documents, but additional papers may be required. It all depends on the requirements of the institution.
