Samara hostels on the Volga: photos and reviews of tourists

Samara hostels and regions are very popular, local residents periodically come there to spend time with benefit, enjoy their vacation and forget about all the accumulated issues that need to be addressed. A large number of tourist organizations allow vacationers to easily choose the most suitable of them by price and location.

How many total recreation facilities in the Samara region?





In total, there are about 40 recreational facilities in the region. Samara hostels and regions differ from each other in their location and cost of recreation. Some of them are available only in the summer season, while others are ready to accept vacationers even in winter, however, the cost of rest in the cold season is significantly increased.

In the summer, the issue price ranges from 300 to 6,000 rubles per day, it all depends on which base you go to and which set of services you choose for yourself. Some recreational facilities differ from each other in service: somewhere, it is high-quality, and at some bases it is worth seriously working on it.

"Pines"

Chalet "Sosenki" (Samara) is located on the banks of the small river Sok - it is only 60 kilometers from the regional capital. Pine forest, clean air and scenic views form all the conditions necessary for relaxation. The base works all year round, in the summer it can take up to 370 vacationers, in the winter - 80.

The base has a rich infrastructure, there is even a mini-cinema, and for young tourists there is a playground with a lot of attractions. In summer, there is a beach and a cafe. Vacationers are offered a large number of additional services that will be performed by staff quickly and efficiently.

Chalet "Sosenki" (Samara) offers its guests a rest in the houses of the "Lux" category, two-story cottages, summer houses and a hotel. The cost of living will be from 520 to 2100 rubles per day, depending on the option chosen. If desired, you can also take a ticket for 14 days. Getting to the base is very simple: you need to take an electric train in the direction of the Zhigulevskoe Sea and get off at the 154 kilometer station.

"Rook"





Chalet "Rook" (Samara) is located on the right bank of the Volga, near Pervomaisky Spusk. Tourists are offered two-story and three-story cottages for 4 and 8 places, respectively, a hotel and residential complexes, where you can buy rooms at relatively inexpensive prices. All the proposed premises are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay: there are TVs, upholstered furniture, refrigerators, equipped bathrooms, etc.

Judging by the reviews, one of the important advantages of Rook is that children under 5 years old can stay for free if they do not need a berth. The second positive nuance: vacationers are offered a large number of different entertainments: baths, a sports ground, a zoo, sports equipment rental. Children can relax in the creativity room with animators.

The cost of living here is slightly higher than at other recreational facilities, in addition, it changes almost every month. In October 2015, the Radya camp site (Samara) raised prices again: now rest here costs from 2 to 6 thousand rubles per day, depending on the chosen room. You can get to the base using water transport from the Kinap marina or by car.

"Lighthouse"





Mayak Chalet (Samara) positions itself as an eco-hotel where you can have a good weekend or vacation. The facility is located 35 kilometers from Samara and operates year-round. There are all necessary conditions for recreation, you can even take children with you - electric cars and bicycles are always available for them, there is a children's menu in the local restaurant, and in winter you can rent skis.

A magnificent view of the Zhiguli Mountains is one of the highlights of the camp site, which has been popular with residents of the region for more than five years. There are also Russian and Finnish baths, 24-hour security, and boat parking is available in winter and summer. If desired, you can arrange three meals a day diet type.

Mayak Chalet (Samara) is one of the few where there is a "floating house" that can be rented. The cost of living per day can be clarified by contacting the telephone number: +7 (846) 332-32-79. To get to the base, you will first need to get to the village of Kurumoch, which is located 40 kilometers from Samara, then turn off the regional highway and drive a few kilometers through the village of Vlast Labor.

"Rainbow"

If you are interested in low-budget camp sites in Samara and its environs, you should pay attention to the "Rainbow", which is 60 kilometers from the regional capital. This recreation center operates only in the summertime and can accommodate up to 170 people at a time. Vacationers are offered a choice of two-story and one-story towers, wooden buildings and summer houses.

The cost of living here ranges from 200 to 3000 thousand rubles per day. Guests can use the sauna, sports and gymnasiums, dance floor, animators are responsible for children's activities. You can get to the base through the village of Pribrezhny, located on the route of the highway "M5".

Away from Samara





Those who seek solitude and want to forget about the bustle of the city should think about visiting camp sites in Samara and the Samara region. One of them is the Park Hotel. The facility is located 120 kilometers from the regional capital and territorially already belongs to the city of Togliatti. Beautiful view of the Volga, opening from here, makes you forget about everything.

Guests are invited to stay in a six-story building; in total, the hotel is designed for 127 guests. On the 1st floor there is a conference room, an Internet cafe, a phytobar and other infrastructure. If you wish, you can go on an excursion to Samara Luka, this service is inexpensive. The cost of living in a hotel ranges from 2000 to 6000 rubles per day. You can get here from the bus station in Togliatti by bus and trolley.

"Russia"

It is also worth considering the Samara camp site on the Volga, among which stands out "Russia", located opposite the Studenny ravine in the national park "Samarskaya Luka". Here holidaymakers are offered a choice of cottages for 6 and 16 people, as well as summer houses for 2, 4 and 6 people.

The most requested months are July and August. At this time, the price of rest ranges from 700 to 1200 rubles per day, the daily rent of a house for 6 or 16 people is 5500 and 15000 rubles, respectively, meals are paid separately.

"Upper Forest"





The beauty of these places and the fresh river air - this is what makes tourists carefully choose camp sites on the Volga. Samara and its remoteness from noisy vacation spots here also play a big role. Near the city is the base "Upper Bor", designed for 71 vacationers. It is here that lovers of extreme relaxation often go, since here you can ride all-terrain vehicles in the vicinity.

Athletes will also feel comfortable here, as they are invited to spend their free time in the gym, play a game of badminton or billiards, and also play tennis. The cost of living ranges from 1,500 to 9,000 thousand rubles, however, as evidenced by the reviews, the prices are worth it. The base is located only 40 kilometers from Samara, you can get there by electric train, you will only need to get to the station "Mastryukovo".

"Aviator"

If we look at Samara camp sites on the banks of the Volga, we can come across Aviator, a place for rest, located near the village of Volzhsky. Near the sandy beach are wooden log cabins and guest houses. Many tourists come here because of the relatively inexpensive prices and the opportunity to admire the tranquility of the Volga - in this place the water almost always behaves calmly.

It offers vacationers rental of household and water equipment, as well as sports equipment. Accommodation prices here are based on the fact that the houses are booked by entire companies. The reservation for one room costs from 10 to 15 thousand rubles per day, while for each additional place you will have to pay another 500 rubles.

"Spark"





If you do not want to relax in the city, it is best to go to campsites on the Volga: Samara is literally surrounded by similar institutions. One of these is the Iskra, built near the village of Podgora on the river bank. The base is located in the Samara Luka National Park and has its own sandy beach.

Here you can rent a summer house of various capacities and categories ("A", "B" and "C"). The base is mainly focused on water recreation, so there is a lot of relevant infrastructure: boats, catamarans, kayaks, bananas, etc. The prices for daily living here range from 500 to 1100 rubles at the peak season (June-August) , in the remaining months, prices are slightly lower. Meals are not included in the price and amounts to 580 rubles per day.

Reviews

Samara hostels, reviews of which are mostly positive, are very popular among the local population. Most often, city dwellers go on holiday to Sosenki, Rook, Mayak and Verkhny Bor, and each time they are satisfied with a high culture of service, quality service and constantly updated infrastructure.

Some guests of camp sites located far from Samara note a lack of staff, which negatively affects the overall quality of service. On the territory of bases built far from major highways, the administrator can perform the functions of a maid and a porter, which, according to the guests, is not entirely correct.

As it was before?





Earlier in the region there were more recreational facilities. Especially popular was the Bereg camp site (Samara), with dedicated buses going there twice a day. Unfortunately, in the late 1990s it ceased to be in demand, and therefore it was closed.

In total, about three dozen bases that previously existed in the region were closed. The reasons for this were very different: undeveloped infrastructure, remoteness from settlements, poor service. Some properties were purchased by the new owners and began to generate revenue again after repair and re-equipment.

Conclusion

Samara camps are mostly open year-round, but there are those that close for the winter. Before planning a vacation, it is best to clarify the working hours of the base, the cost and living conditions, since these parameters can change periodically. Also, be sure to specify what conditions are offered for children if you plan to relax with the whole family.

Reviews say that it is best to go on vacation in early June, as it is at this time of year that tourist centers offer a large number of discounts and last-minute packages that you can take advantage of.
