Description and location

AMAKS Tourist Hotel is located near the bus station, so tourists and business people who come to the capital of Bashkortostan give preference to it. There are two banquet rooms. Each is designed for 40 people.

If tourists arrived by car, they are offered private guarded parking at the AMAKS Tourist Hotel in Ufa. In a small library located in the lobby of the hotel, you will find interesting literature for yourself, and in the store you will get memorable souvenirs. Within walking distance from the hotel there is a restaurant and a supermarket. Several cafes and a market are within walking distance. Each room has new modern furniture and a private balcony.
