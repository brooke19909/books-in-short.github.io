How to take a screenshot on "ZTE": instructions

All smartphones on the Android operating system use the same tools and functionality. Most of the controls and interface match each other, regardless of the manufacturer of the smartphone. The same can be said about the ZTE company and its devices. Mobile phones from this manufacturer are controlled by the Android OS, so the function key combinations are identical to the original ones. Let's see how to take screenshots on the "ZTE".

What is a screen?

A convenient feature appeared in the version of "Android 4" and older. To quickly capture the contents of the screen, you can use only two keys, after which the phone will need only a few seconds to save the image in a folder with other photos and screenshots.

Devices "ZTE" do not differ from other similar smartphones in functionality. You can also take a screenshot on ZTE with a couple of taps. To do this, use the instructions provided.

Screenshot Guide

To get started, open the image or application on the screen that you must take. Next, hold down the on / off key and volume down button at the same time. If you did everything correctly, a notification will appear on the display to save the image to the gallery. Otherwise, you will see a volume indicator on the screen, just lock the phone. If you did not succeed the first time, then repeat the operation after the volume indicator disappears from the screen. Creating a screenshot is accompanied by a flash on the screen and the characteristic shutter sounds of the camera.

Third party programs
