The territory of the base is ennobled, clean. All tracks are lined with stone. On the street are gazebos and barbecue facilities.

Residence

Those who come to the recreation center can stay in a hotel room or in one of the guest houses (there are three in total).





A two-story wooden house can accommodate eight people (maximum). The cost of living per day is twelve thousand rubles. There is a kitchen, a shower room, a bathroom, necessary furniture, a music center, a jacuzzi, etc.

There is another one-story house, which is also designed for a maximum of eight people. It has everything the same as in the first housing option, in addition to a jacuzzi. Cost per day - ten thousand.

A two-story house on the water will intervene up to 10 people. Price - 15 thousand rubles a day.

There are also hotel rooms:

  • double suite (3000 rubles per day);
  • double junior suite (2500 rubles per day);
  • standard (1000 r. per day);

Manor "At Albert." Nutrition

How much does food cost? Its price is 1200 rubles. For this money, the guest is offered breakfast, lunch (buffet).

The restaurant has a grill, author's cuisine, a bar, billiards and a summer terrace. The menu here is varied. There are main dishes, soups, desserts, meat and more. The restaurant also has a banquet.

Services and entertainment

The estate has an equipped bath complex. It has a font with ice water, a bath, a heated tub and a jacuzzi. There is tea, brooms, honey and bathrobes. The services of the bath complex cost three thousand rubles per hour.

Manor "Alberta" also offers excellent conditions for riding a scooter, fishing.





For those who love outdoor activities, there are plenty of options. For example, you can fly on a hang-glider. The cost of such a service is one and a half thousand rubles in ten minutes. You can rent boats here; on average, you will have to pay 2500 rubles for half an hour of use. There is also the opportunity to ride a jet ski. For 10 minutes you should pay five hundred rubles. You can also rent:

  • 2-seater catamaran (three hundred rubles / half an hour);
  • a kayak (three hundred rubles / hour);
  • a wooden boat with oars, designed for six people (five hundred rubles / hour);
  • plastic boat (800-100 rubles per hour).

Conclusion

Now you know how interesting the estate "Albert" on Pavlovka is. As you can see, this is a good recreation center, which can be visited both with friends and family. There is plenty of entertainment for every taste.
