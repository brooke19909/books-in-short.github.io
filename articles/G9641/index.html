Paphos, Kissos Hotel 3 *: reviews, ratings, photos

A huge tourist destination such as Paphos is very popular. Kissos Hotel 3 * was built in the 1980s. Then it included only 72 numbers. Now their number has increased to 144, including superior apartments.





Location

The convenient location attracts travelers the resort complex Kissos Hotel 3 * (Paphos). The beach is only 300 meters away. The nearest shopping center is a 10-minute walk away. Resting in this institution, guests should visit the following tourist sites:

  • archaeological site of the ancient tombs of the Greek kings (1 km);
  • Metropolis of Paphos (1 km);
  • Dionysus House (1 km);
  • Church of St. Solomon (1 km);
  • St. Paul's Church with columns (1 km);
  • Paphos defensive fortress (2 km);
  • Marchideo Theater (2 km);
  • Paphos water park (4 km);
  • golf course (7 km).

  • The coffee shop offers a huge selection of pastries and fresh pastries. A wide assortment of coffee and tea is also available here.
