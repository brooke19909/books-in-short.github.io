Building your own home is always accompanied by many questions. The most basic of them is financial. Of all the existing materials, the foam block has positively proven itself in all respects. It is not too expensive, practical and durable. Those who choose it inevitably face the question of how much it costs to build a house from foam blocks. Foundation

Since foam blocks are a fairly light material, there is no particular need to make a solid foundation. Lightweight enough with mandatory reinforcing ligament. Ideally, even at the stage of the project, it is necessary to clearly distinguish between where and what size the premises will be, in order to make a foundation along their perimeter. This will give the structure strength and durability. Think about it before you build a house out of foam blocks. The cost of this will not increase much.



Roof frame

What height do the roof - a purely individual question. So, for example, you can make high, with the expectation that there will be an attic or, on the contrary, a small one. The cost of building a house from foam blocks implies that about 5% of the amount will go to the roof frame. Depending on the price of lumber in your region, it may take from 3,000 to 10,000 rubles.

House 9 * 8. How to calculate the amount?

It is noteworthy that the last figure for the foundation is slightly higher - 42. Given the fact that the minimum width for the solid structure is 2 meters. The cost of building a house from foam blocks consists of the price of the foundation, roofing frame, basement and walls. The latter - net of windows and doors. No house can be put on a bare foundation. Requires at least a base of 5-6 bricks high. This will take about 40 thousand rubles. Please note that this is without the cost of work. Typically, builders take about 30-50% of the price of the material.

Further calculation of the cost of a house of foam blocks depends on the number of floors of your building and the height of the walls. It is optimal to make three-meter ceilings. Solution

What you can not do without in building a house is a cement-sand mortar. It must be not only done correctly, but also acquired everything you need. So, for example, the cost of one bag of cement will cost 200-300 rubles. A large-scale construction such as a house will require about 100 bags. Given the foundation. For example, such a building requires at least 1 ton of good sifted sand. Buy it or carry it yourself - your business. On average, 1 ton of sand costs 2-3 thousand rubles. Insulation

After the ‚Äúbox‚Äù under the house is ready, she needs to settle down. Foam blocks - this is a material that has a small, but quite noticeable percentage of shrinkage. To proceed with the internal and external decoration, it is necessary to give the house about a year to upset. During this time, under his weight, he will take the final form, height.

When wondering how much it costs to build a house from foam blocks, include such an article in the estimate as a heater. Selecting it is a problem. First of all, because there are so many varieties. For a house of foam blocks, it is better to choose either polystyrene foam, or angelin. If everything is clear with the first: it is lightweight, does not let air through, and is durable - then the second needs to be discussed separately. Angelin is a roll insulation that works according to the principle of a thermos: it does not give up heat from the inside, it does not let in cold from the outside. In addition, it is moisture resistant, easy to install (mounted on a construction stapler or self-tapping screws), durable. The cost of materials does not differ much: for one meter about 30-50 rubles, depending on the thickness.



Interior decoration

Perhaps, everything is limited solely by your desires and imagination. Traditionally, gypsum board is used as a finishing material for walls . Firstly, it is durable. Secondly, it is easy to install. Thirdly, they can make not only straight and smooth walls, but also various kinds of niches, levels, even arches. The only drawback is the fragility of the material. But this is not so significant, despite the fact that you are going to build a house from foam blocks. The average price per sheet of ordinary drywall varies in the region of 200-300 rubles, moisture resistant (for the bathroom, for example) - 50-100 rubles more. The amount of material depends on the size of the house and the number of storeys. It is recommended to purchase drywall in batches for each room with a small margin of one or two sheets.

Naturally, a frame will also be required for wall decoration. No matter how smooth the walls were. Metal or wood is your business. The cost is not much different: from 100 to 300 rubles per linear meter of staff.

Pay particular attention to the rough floor. If a large load is meant (for example, a huge aquarium in the room), then it is more advisable to make a concrete screed. It is expensive and long, but durable. The services of workers in this case amount to at least 50% of the cost of materials. If there is no need to make a screed, then you can trust the good old floors from the boards. Remember, the smoother the rough floor, the easier it is to lay the floor covering in the future. Ceiling coating can be absolutely anything. There are no specific tips, and the financial side depends only on your capabilities.
