Popular hotels in Nizhnevartovsk

What hotels in Nizhnevartovsk are worth visiting for city guests? Where is it better to stay when going here on a tourist trip or on a business trip? Let's look at those that deserve the attention of visitors.

Samotlor





Hotel Samotlor (Nizhnevartovsk) is located in a picturesque park area, almost at the embankment of the Ob River. You can find it at the address: street 60 years of October, house 2b.

The guests of the restaurant expect bright, comfortable rooms decorated in a classic style. Each room is equipped with air conditioning and cable TV. The rooms have a private bathroom and a minibar. In addition, guests have access to free Wi-Fi.

The hotel reception is open 24 hours a day. Here you can not only rent a room, but also order food and drinks, take a bathrobe, sign up for a beauty salon, sauna, massage room. There is a billiard room on site.

By the way, other hotels in Nizhnevartovsk do not have such a convenient location for city guests, as Samotlor is located just a 15-minute drive from the railway station and 5 km from the international airport.

"Hope"





Hotel Nadezhda (Nizhnevartovsk) is located a few hundred meters from the central railway station of the city. You can get here from the airport by car, spending no more than 20 minutes on a trip.

The hotel has 104 rooms of various classes and individual price categories. All rooms, without exception, have televisions with a wide package of satellite channels. The rooms are equipped with private bathrooms, where guests are provided with free bathrobes and hygiene items. Most apartments have air conditioning and a fridge.

Meals at the hotel can be arranged by ordering breakfast in the room, or by going to the restaurant located on the territory of the complex. Walking for groceries to the nearest store will take about 5 minutes.

Hotel Nadezhda (Nizhnevartovsk) provides guests with free access to Wi-Fi. Moreover, customers can leave their own car in a free secure parking lot.

You can find the Nadezhda Hotel at 39 Severnaya Street.

"The Pearl of Siberia"





We continue to survey the popular hotels of Nizhnevartovsk. Next on our list will be considered an institution called the "Pearl of Siberia." This hotel with modern developed infrastructure is located at a distance of 1.5 km from the central point of the city, on the banks of the Ob River.

Just a choice of guests here are 20 rooms. The hotel has both relatively cheap single and double rooms of the standard class, as well as luxury apartments with enhanced amenities that will satisfy the needs of any tourist.

Customers are offered a free service, which includes free Wi-Fi access, round-the-clock service at the front desk, the ability to call institution staff in the room, and morning wake-up upon client's request.

At the hotel are:

  • conference room for up to 30 people;
  • a restaurant and buffet where you can organize meals at affordable prices;
  • gym;
  • sauna;
  • massage rooms;
  • billiard room.

"Ob"





The Ob Hotel (Nizhnevartovsk) is located at 60 years of October Street, 2g. The facility is located within walking distance from the embankment of the river of the same name.

A choice of customers offers 42 rooms of various levels of comfort and price category. The hotel has a 24-hour reception. Guests are given a free parking space in the car park, which is adjacent to the complex.

The hotel rooms are decorated in warm, pleasant colors. A large flat-screen TV awaits guests in every room of the restaurant. The rooms have a private bathroom.

Every morning, the staff organizes a complimentary buffet breakfast. You can also eat at the local restaurant, where, for a moderate fee, visitors are served popular European dishes.

"Birch"

Listing the popular hotels of Nizhnevartovsk, in the end we note the institution called "Birch", which has the status of the most inexpensive place in the city where you can rent a room. You can find the hotel at 13 Industrialnaya Street.

The rooms are located in several separate buildings. The first two have all the necessary amenities for a comfortable stay. The rooms that are located in the third building are the cheapest. Here basic amenities are on the floor.

For guests of the establishment, a cafe is open around the clock, with which you can organize good nutrition. The chefs of the restaurant prepare popular dishes of domestic and European cuisine. As well as for accommodation in rooms, the prices in the cafe are very affordable.
