Kitchen processor. Browse Popular Models

The time when several household appliances were in the kitchen at once to facilitate the work of the hostess passes. They are replaced by a kitchen processor - a universal assistant that can perform all the necessary functions. With its compact size, it does not require large space, and therefore is organic even in a small kitchen. Thanks to many different attachments, it fully replaces the mixer, blender, grater, meat grinder and juicer.
