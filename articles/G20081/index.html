Pensions in the Leningrad Region - the best choice for recovery



The modern pace of life is associated with all sorts of stresses. Most residents of megacities suffer from chronic fatigue, which is exacerbated by the lack of necessary physical activity and poor nutrition. At the same time, two days off per week, as a rule, is not enough to fully restore strength. For this reason, it is very important from time to time to have a good rest, and preferably, away from city noise and in the fresh air. The ideal solution in this case is a trip to one of the medical institutions. It will help to restore physical well-being, increase vitality and strengthen the nervous system.

Rest in the Leningrad region



Preventive clinics and pensions in the Leningrad Region are located in the most beautiful places in Russia with good ecology. Some of them are located on the shores of the famous Gulf of Finland among the dense pine forests, others are on the shores of Lake Ladoga, surrounded by the Karelian Isthmus. At the same time, they all have a high level of comfort and excellent service. Pensions in the Leningrad Region are a unique opportunity to combine communication with nature and living in modern complexes. Numerous health resorts of this region are perfect for both individual and group recreation, restoration of physical health and mental strength.

The benefits of a vacation in the Leningrad region

The main advantages of carrying out preventive or outpatient treatment in one of the boarding houses of the Leningrad Region, first of all, are the absence of acclimatization and long flights, which are very exhausting for any organism. In addition, there is no need to draw up a package of documents required for traveling abroad. Also, the usual language environment and proximity to the city can be called advantages, which makes it possible to draw up a diverse cultural program and visit a large number of attractions in St. Petersburg. It should be said that all pensions in the Leningrad Region offer today truly European service and a high level of comfort for their guests.

Pension "Petrel"



The medical-improving institution "Burevestnik" is located on the Karelian Isthmus, forty kilometers from St. Petersburg and three hundred meters from the Gulf of Finland, in the village of Repino. Sea air, rich natural landscape and clear Lake Shchuchye - this is what this place is famous for. The main service of this medical institution is providing recreational activities for adults and children. Corporate events and the organization of various sports competitions are also offered by the Petrel Sanatorium. The boarding house (the Leningrad Region has this for it), among other things, offers a comfortable beach holiday on the bay for all comers.

Pension "Baltiets"

A health resort called Baltiets, like the Burevestnik sanatorium, is located in one of the most colorful places of the Karelian Isthmus - in the ecologically clean village of Repino.





This institution is a modern complex that provides its guests with a full range of hotel and wellness services. The latter include physiotherapy (magnetotherapy, laser therapy, aromatherapy, inhalation, light therapy), massage and a sauna. Physical therapy is also available for guests of the resort "Baltiets". The boarding house (Leningrad Region, or rather its climate allows it) in the winter season offers its guests the opportunity to ski and ice-skate. Corporate clients are offered to hold conferences, seminars, trainings, as well as wedding ceremonies and holiday banquets. In addition, like all pensions in the Leningrad Region with a swimming pool, Baltiets offers everyone to attend aqua aerobics classes with a professional instructor.
