Hotel TTC Hotel Premium Phan Thiet 4 * - overview, features and reviews of tourists

Choosing a place for the upcoming trip, you need to decide on the main focus of the vacation: it will be beautiful sunbathing and a dark-skinned tan or trips to museums and attractions. And only a few resorts are able to combine these two aspects, complemented by comfortable accommodation for every taste and affordable prices. Such is the city of Phan Thiet - located in the south of Thailand.

This resort is famous for its amazingly luxurious nature, well-groomed beaches and a developed infrastructure of entertainment. The most favorable period for visiting is from April to October. Rainfall is less likely at this time. The coastline varies in its purpose from the bustling nightlife to a great place for extreme sports. For those who prefer privacy, there is a corner on the wild and deserted beaches. A sense of calm and peace will settle in the soul of vacationers.

The four-star TTC Hotel Premium Phan Thiet (ex. Park Diamond) welcomes every guest. A warm welcome, quality service and comfortable accommodation are guaranteed to every traveler who comes to this castle. An overview of the hotel TTC Hotel Premium Phan Thiet 4 * is presented to your attention below.





general characteristics

The hotel complex TTC Hotel Premium Phan Thiet 4 * (Vietnam) was erected in 2004. From this moment on, the number of vacationers staying annually is constantly increasing. The last renovation was held in 2017.

However, the Russians do not always want to go here, since a long flight is tiring. The location of 200 km from the nearest international airport is also not very convenient, as it provides a long journey to the hotel by taxi for four hours.

Phan Thiet TTC Hotel Premium Phan Thiet has a total area of ​​13,500 square meters. The territory is represented by a single building of fourteen floors, equipped with four elevators, a spacious aquazone and an amazing park area. The latter is distinguished by numerous palm trees, tropical plants, beautiful landscape constructions, original ponds and fragrant flowers.

Pets cannot be accommodated. Smoking in rooms is prohibited. There are apartments for people with disabilities that are equipped with all necessary amenities. The staff is friendly and unobtrusive, speaks two languages: Vietnamese and English. It is acceptable to place an extra bed. Payments are made by cards of international banking systems.





Number of rooms

The total number of rooms proposed for settlement is 171 units. All of them belong to the luxury class of various variations. The interior is made in a simple modern style. The area of ​​the apartment is spacious and roomy. The floors are covered with laminate or carpet. There is access to a balcony where you can meet the early rays of the sun or spend a sunset without leaving the room. The walls are decorated with paintings, there is a large amount of greenery in the form of spreading flowers.

The furniture is represented by a standard set: a double bed, a wardrobe, a desk, chairs and bedside tables.

The presence of an individual air conditioning system allows you to feel comfortable regardless of the temperature outside the window. A TV with Russian channels will brighten up a monotonous evening by watching your favorite TV shows and news. An internal telephone helps you get in touch with the front desk and instantly solve problems. The safe will allow you to relax carefree, saving important documents and gadgets in a safe place.

The presence of an electric kettle and tea set is to the liking of each guest. Wi-Fi - available throughout. Iron and ironing board are available upon request.

The bathroom is decorated with ceramics, equipped with a shower or bath, sink and hairdryer. There are all necessary personal hygiene products, slippers and a cosmetic set. The number of towels complies with the international standard.

Housekeeping is carried out daily by responsible maids. Bed linen is changed three times a week. It is possible to use the room service.





Supply system

The hotel management allows guests checking in to choose their own diet:

  • Breakfast.
  • Half board.
  • Boarding house.

Those who choose the all-inclusive system are offered three meals a day according to the buffet system. Each vacationer with the buffet method of serving dishes has the opportunity to choose what is right for his soul in the amount that he is ready to consume. The main restaurant has a delightful design: multi-level relief ceilings, spotlights and columns. There is a closed area and an outdoor terrace. Preference is given to international and international cuisine, in the dishes of which local chefs put a piece of their soul.





Beach

The hotel complex is located in the first coastline at a distance of 50 meters from the coast. The main purpose of a trip to the resort of most vacationers is to get an amazing tan. For this, there is everything you need. The beach is mostly sandy, the entrance to the water is gentle. Many sun loungers with comfortable soft bedding, umbrellas and towels are included in the price and do not require additional charges.

For activists and those who are simply not used to sitting in one place and want to get emotions to the maximum, there are extreme sports such as windsurfing, diving, parachuting for a boat. More relaxed activities include snorkeling and banana riding. Daily volleyball court gathers friendly companies that relentlessly strive for victory.

Aquazone

The main and only pool of the TTC Hotel Premium Phan Thiet 4 * hotel (Phan Thiet) is located in front of the main building near the children's building. A good neighborhood allows a small child to be under the constant supervision of parents. Unheated pool, has a gradual increase in depth. Sun loungers and umbrellas allow you to soak under the scorching rays of the sun throughout the day. The towel dispenser is free of charge.





For children

Reviews about TTC Hotel Premium Phan Thiet (Vietnam) are mostly enthusiastic, especially guests enjoy the organization of children's leisure. The hotel belongs to the category of family vacations. The presence of a shallow swimming area is to the liking of every baby. Swimming not only develops coordination, the respiratory system and the musculoskeletal system, but also gives them an unprecedented sense of joy and happiness.

There is an opportunity to use the babysitting service. A professional teacher will take the kid’s free time with various educational games and cognitive activities.

The restaurant provides high chairs and a children's menu. Cots in the room on request.





Sport

Most holidaymakers prefer not only to relax, but also to play sports regularly, because improving the body is a set of activities, and a good rest is a change in activities. For lovers of a healthy lifestyle, there is an equipped fitness room, hung with wall mirrors. A lot of trainers, treadmills and sports equipment will satisfy the needs of absolutely everyone who decides to lose extra pounds and keep their bodies in perfect shape. Professional instructors will make a schedule of individual loads and hold training sessions, which anyone can visit.

For fans of tennis there is an equipped court with a hard surface. Equipment and necessary equipment, as well as lighting in the dark, are paid extra.





SPA center

A wellness center in which strength is quickly restored, stress disappears, beauty and youth return to the body, peace of mind is gained, everyone can visit. To the sounds of calm music and flickering candles, each visitor will experience true pleasure. The procedures are carried out using sea water, mud and algae. The result is a beautiful fresh look and a great mood.

Massage performed by Vietnamese professionals is aimed primarily at the effect of bioenergy points on the body. The unique national technique of execution has an integrated approach, in which the joints of the muscles are involved, blood circulation is improved and muscle tension is eliminated.

A visit to the sauna will also have a positive effect in increasing immunity and human health.

The beauty salon, which will be engaged in cosmetic services for spa guests, regardless of gender, will appreciate the opportunity to create a new image and amazing style.





Diving

Diving into bottomless deeps in this area has the lowest prices. However, it is no less interesting and organized at a high level. A large number of places for descent under water were concentrated here. In addition to the incredible variety of corals, here you can find mollusks, octopuses, squids and a number of other inhabitants.

The most popular period for diving is the time from October to April. The most common places are Muren beach, coral reef "Rainbow", as well as the many islands that surround the resort town.

Shopping

Shopping is a favorite activity of all tourists. In Vietnam, due to the low prices of goods, this process is addictive. Free pounds of luggage disappear before our eyes. Souvenirs, coffee and sauces - something that often does not go through customs due to significant advantages.





Additional services

In positive reviews about TTC Hotel Premium Phan Thiet 4 *, the quality of the services provided is often noted. The presence of conference rooms allows businessmen not only to enjoy a serene vacation, but also, if necessary, to organize business events in available meeting places. To do this, there is all the necessary equipment: projectors, screens, computers, comfortable furniture and a unique interior that can quickly change the relaxing state to an assembled look. Banquet facilities are used to celebrate special occasions.

The transfer is paid extra: quickly and safely, vacationers can make numerous trips by ordering this service at the reception. The 24-hour front desk offers concierge services, a tour desk, currency exchange and luggage storage.

For those who have rented a car or arrived with their four-wheeled friend, free, illuminated parking is offered.

The program for newlyweds, which is available only to newlyweds upon presentation of a marriage certificate, includes romantic decoration of the room, the installation of candles, rose petals and a bottle of champagne as a gift.

Room Service is available around the clock; you can order press delivery in your native language. The hotel has a Russian-speaking staff.

Laundry and ironing services are not included in the ticket price and are paid additionally upon the fact.

In general, summing up all the above, we can conclude that reviews of the TTC Hotel Premium Phan Thiet (Phan Thiet) hotel are extremely rare negative. Basically, tourists are satisfied with the quality of the services provided, the attitude of all staff to their work and the cost. TTC Hotel Premium Phan Thiet offers affordable prices (from 2000 to 5000 rubles for accommodation in a standard room) for people with an average income. Many tourists recommend considering the hotel as an accommodation option during their stay in Phan Thiet.
