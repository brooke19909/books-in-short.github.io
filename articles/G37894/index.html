Wall-mounted washing machine: pros and cons, overview

Household appliance manufacturers regularly market devices with new features, design features, or unexpected designs. Unfortunately, not all innovations really indicate a transition to the next level of progress and bring tangible benefits to housewives. Nevertheless, pleasant replenishment in the market does happen. Not so long ago, a wall-mounted washing machine appeared, which is characterized by a wide range of technical and operational features. We can say that this is a fundamentally new approach to doing washing, since such models have little in common with classical equipment of this kind.

Features of wall mounted washing machines





The installation method, unusual for a washing machine, is only an external difference, which, however, also surprises. To this, you can immediately add small sizes. The unit has a very modest size, for which it receives the prefix "mini" in some lines. If we talk about the features of the internal filling, which is equipped with a wall-mounted washing machine, then the absence of a pump and a pump for pumping water will come to the fore. Otherwise, the set of components and the configuration of their interaction duplicate the device of traditional design, but only in a reduced form.

It is also worth noting the various approaches to the embodiment of machines of this type from manufacturers. The segment is still being formed, and there are not so many companies represented in it, but certain branches in the model lines are already being traced. The first and most successful development of this type is the Daewoo wall-mounted washing machine in the DWD series.

The advantages of wall construction





This is the case when, to discover the benefits, just look at the technique. All the advantages that arise from the compact size, fully apply to machines of this type. Among them, it can be noted easy installation, energy efficiency, saving space in the room and ease of transportation. In addition, wall-mounted models have a spectacular design. True, in the line of full-fledged aggregates with such frills today you will hardly surprise anyone. A confirmation of this is the Electrolux washing machine of the latest series, made in different colors. The advantages of mini-cars include the support of modern software with electronics. Despite their small size, models of this type combine the full range of necessary options and operating modes.

Cons of wall construction





One way or another, wall-mounted models are physically unable to provide high quality wash. This is not a sentence at all, but today the maximum class of quality of work of such models is B. It is quite possible that in the future this level will increase, as has already happened with the characteristics of spin and rinse. But if, as a basic function, the wall-mounted washing machine can develop further, then the drum volume will remain the same. Minor changes, again, are not excluded, but the mounting method in the form of wall mounts will not allow manufacturers to significantly increase capacity. Therefore, the load volume of 3-4 kg is the maximum for machines of this class.

Another disadvantage of the new models is the cost. To begin with, it should be noted that models in the traditional form factor with modern technical fillings are estimated at 10-15 thousand rubles. It is difficult to say how much the capabilities of such models exceed the technological advantages that wall-mounted washing machines have. The price of such modifications is about 16-20 thousand rubles.

User reviews of wall design





The first owners very harmoniously evaluate the design characteristics. Ease of handling the controls, a wide selection of washing modes, compactness and ease of maintenance are noted. Separately, many housewives note the savings. This also applies to minimal installation costs, the low level of electricity that a wall-mounted washing machine consumes. Reviews with criticism are also found and note inconvenience when downloading things. Rather, the placement process itself is quite ergonomic, however, you can lay almost half as much laundry per wash as with conventional models.

Comparison with conventional models

Indisputable advantages of mini-units over classic cars include space saving, minimizing noise and vibration, more advanced electronic stuffing, efficiency and ergonomics. If we talk about the advantages of conventional equipment of this type, then, for example, the mentioned Electrolux washing machine for the same money can provide a higher quality of washing. Moreover, at one time it will be possible to complete a full cycle with laying in the drum volume twice the capacity of the Daewoo model. As you can see, in both cases there are advantages and weaknesses.

Conclusion





An example of a new washing machine design demonstrates that modern technology does not guarantee in every sense the perfect solution to problems. Rather, they expand the range of possibilities of existing developments, sometimes sacrificing some of the advantages of traditional ideas. For example, a wall-mounted washing machine has shown that laundry care can also be performed without connecting a bulky equipment that rattles and vibrates during operation. You can also wash with a device that is pleasant to handle, which features a stylish design and convenient digital control. Another thing is that the quality of the result will not be the best, and you will have to spend extra time on a large amount of laundry. On the other hand, the development of the segment began not so long ago, and in the near future, perhaps, the wall structure will turn into a product that is more advanced in technical and operational capabilities. Such models have already shown significant progress in terms of electronic filling.
