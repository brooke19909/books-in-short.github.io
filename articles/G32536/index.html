The best hotels of Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk

About 200,000 people live in Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk, while the city is the administrative center of the entire Sakhalin region , famous for its pristine volcanic landscapes and rich wildlife.





For 40 years, the city was in the possession of Japan, and now in one of the surviving Japanese buildings is a large museum. Local residents in the summer park take refuge in the shade of numerous beer tents, while tourists travel away from the city to enjoy the amazing views and the natural beauty of the island.

In this article we will look at the popular hotels of Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk, which everyone can accommodate.

Gagarin

Hotel "Gagarin" (Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk) has a favorable geographical position, as there is an airport a few minutes from here. At the same time, judging by the reviews, the hotel is very comfortable and quiet. A huge range of services offered can satisfy the most demanding guests.



Experienced staff will surround you with care and attention. Here are different categories of rooms: standard, apartments and suites. Each of them is equipped with all necessary appliances and ergonomic modern furniture.

"Fisherman"

Hotel Rybak (Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk) is a self-supporting enterprise that is part of the Union of Fishing Collective Farms.



This is a comfortable hotel, which is located in the city center, near two stations, a service center, a city administration building, a cinema, an art museum, a number of cafes and restaurants.

Judging by the reviews, the guests of the restaurant are waiting for comfortable rooms. At the same time, the building is equipped with hot water supply throughout the year and autonomous energy supply.

"Anniversary"

The modern three-story hotel Yubileinaya (Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk) opened its doors in 2004. It is located in the ecologically clean part of the city, within the borders of the park named after Gagarin and not far from the STK "Mountain Air".



Here, guests are provided with 39 spacious comfortable rooms. The windows of the establishment offer a picturesque view, as evidenced by numerous reviews. On the territory there is a guarded free parking, a cafe-bar. For business guests there is a business center and a conference room. In addition, there is a swimming pool, sauna, bath. Responsive and friendly staff, your stay here will make you feel at home.

"Panorama"

The hotel is located in the city center with stunning views of the west and east. The rooms of this Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk hotel are located from the fourth to the eighth floor with a rich variety of services on the remaining floors: cafe, cinema center, beauty salon, sports club, bar, pool, conference room, shops, sauna.

Judging by the reviews, the rooms are presented in several categories, while each is made in a unique style and with absolute convenience for guests.

Imperial Palace

Hotel Imperial Palace is located in the city center. For accommodation, it offers 43 comfortable rooms. This is a hotel complex with professional service and comfortable rooms, as evidenced by the reviews. It has an excellent transport interchange, therefore, suitable for business people and tourists.



This Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk hotel offers its guests a cafe, a conference room, a karaoke room, a restaurant, a sauna, and a fitness room. The best cooks work in the restaurant and cafe, which means that the dishes here are very tasty.

"Ruby"

At the moment, the Rubin Hotel is considered perhaps one of the best in the city. High quality services, impeccable reputation, impeccable service - all this makes it a leader among similar institutions. The hotel offers its guests 33 rooms.

Each of them has a kitchenette, which is very popular with all residents. Guests of this Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk hotel can have a good time and enjoy gourmet cuisine in the Mishka club, while the Respect beauty salon provides effective and modern body and face care procedures, as evidenced by numerous reviews.

Sakhalin-Sapporo

In the very center of Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk there is the Sakhalin-Sapporo Hotel. It offers guests 80 comfortable rooms of various categories.



Judging by the reviews, there is a wonderful restaurant with very tasty cuisine, while for sports fans there is a gym, a conference room for business guests. The hotel will welcome you with a good-natured and helpful staff.

Hotel "Squirrel"

This is the first hotel in the city, created by Finnish technology in a wooden style. It has 3 floors. Alpine-style rooms. The hotel is located in the business center near the leisure park, shopping center, museum of local lore, station.

There is a room specially equipped for honeymooners. Judging by the reviews, for people who want to relax in the evening, there is the opportunity to use the services of a sauna. There is also a restaurant on site that serves gourmet and delicious dishes.
