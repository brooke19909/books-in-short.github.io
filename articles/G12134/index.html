Sea of ​​Azov, recreation in Dolzhanskaya: recreation facilities, conditions, prices

Krasnodar Territory is not deprived of the attention of tourists. People come here to sunbathe on the beach, plunge into the warm, pleasant water, take boat trips or other water transport, and have fun on the rides.

At the beginning of the summer season, everyone thinks where to spend their holidays. In Dolzhansk recreation center for every taste. They are usually located off the coast.



Features of rest at the station. Dolzhanskaya

For those who have not been here before, it will be useful to know that Dolzhanskaya station is located in the place where the Sea of ​​Azov and the Bay of Taganrog combine their waters. In these territories you can relax away from civilization. Visitors enjoy peace and quiet, starry sky, clear sea, warm rays of the sun. Time flows slowly, slowly. Everyone who wants to spend a vacation on the sandy Long Spit of the Sea of ​​Azov needs to choose a vacation in Dolzhanskaya. Recreation centers offer their guests sand and shell beaches, entertainment programs, sports grounds, children's equipped towns. For accommodation, one- and two-story houses, rooms in hotel complexes of different price categories are provided. Cheap include economy-class housing without amenities. Visitors can eat both in cafeterias and in cooking. For this, each recreation center has specially equipped kitchens.

Let's look at the popular recreation centers operating in the area.

South

The recreation center "South" in Dolzhanskaya will delight tourists with bright colors that were used to design the territory. There are also a lot of flower beds and flower beds with beautiful plants. These places are popular with windsurfers. As you know, Taganrog Bay is rarely calm. The waves are almost every day. Vacationers need to know that the flowing river Don makes the water of the bay yellowish. This, of course, can somewhat upset beach lovers. That is why it is better to choose a place where the bay connects to the Sea of ​​Azov. Here the beaches are mostly sandy, the water is clear, the depths are small.

During the rest, tourists can visit the Russian bath, swim in the pool. For fans of the active game are offered sites for badminton, volleyball, football. In the evenings, discos are held. Rest in Dolzhanskaya will not be boring. Recreation centers are trying to create such conditions that guests were comfortable, cozy and fun.



Guests can eat in the dining room. The menu is varied, the dishes are delicious. Three meals a day for one person per day will cost about 300 rubles. Those who wish to cook on their own can use the equipped kitchens, which are located on site. For accommodation provided rooms of different levels of comfort, with a capacity of 3 to 4 people. The most expensive will be rooms with all amenities. They are equipped with air conditioning, a bathroom and furniture. Standard rooms do not have a toilet and a bathroom. Guests can use the shared bathroom, which is located on the territory. The cost of this number varies from 330 to 880 rubles. Those who prefer to live with amenities will have to pay from 990 to 1320 rubles per day.

"Breeze"

Spit Dolgaya - Priazovye nature reserve. Those who want to relax here are advised to choose the Veterok base. It is located on the Taganrog Bay. Its coast is only 15 meters. Beach lovers will appreciate the comfortable warm water and gentle sunbeams.

Housing of this recreation center in Art. Dolzhanskaya, as they say, for every taste. Tourists are offered two-story houses with and without amenities. Entrance to the first and second floor is separate. The rooms can accommodate from two to four people. The minimum room rate in May and September is 290 rubles, and from June to August the price rises to 450 rubles.

The entire territory of the base is densely planted with flowers and trees. Visitors are invited to use the barbecue area, library, concert hall, sauna. For those who can not imagine themselves without doing sports, they equipped a gym, a basketball court and a tennis court.



"Ruby"

“Rubin” is a recreation center in the village of Dolzhanskaya. It is located in a forest. The distance to the sea is just over 300 meters. The recreation center covers an area of ​​5.3 hectares. Visitors are resettled in cottages, two of which are brick and one wooden. All are equipped with air conditioning, a TV, and a bathroom. The minimum cost of living from September to October is 1400 rubles, from June to August - 1700 rubles. Tourists receive a complimentary breakfast for this price. Saturated and fun will be a vacation in Dolzhanskaya. Recreation centers located in this region offer a wide range of additional services. “Ruby” is no exception. The administration organizes excursions, parachute flights, horseback riding and hiking, fishing. On the territory there are equipped sports and playgrounds, a parking lot and a billiard room.



"Albatross"

In Dolzhanskaya there is another popular recreation center, it is called "Albatross". To the coast you need to overcome only 50 meters. On the beach there are sun loungers and umbrellas, attractions are installed. Accommodation is possible in standard rooms equipped with everything necessary. The minimum cost per person from 1000 rubles. Toddlers under 5 years of age are accommodated free of charge, children under 14 years old receive a 50 percent discount.
