Iberotel Palace 5 *, Sharm El Sheikh: description, photos, reviews

If you prefer a comfortable, calm and measured rest and choose Egypt as a place for spending your holidays, Sharm El Sheikh, Iberotel Palace 5 * may be a suitable option. That this hotel offers, and we will talk further.



Location

The five-star Iberotel Palace Hotel is located in the most popular resort of the Red Sea - Sharm El Sheikh. It is located in the Old Town area within walking distance of the Old Market. Naam Bay is 7 kilometers away and the airport is 20 kilometers away. The hotel is located on the seashore and has at its disposal a large private sandy beach.

Iberotel Palace 5 * (Egypt, Sharm El Sheikh): description, photo

A feature of this hotel is the fact that only guests over 18 years old can stay here. Therefore, if you plan to go on vacation with your children, then you should look for another hotel to accommodate.

Iberotel Palace opened its doors for the first time almost 20 years ago, in 1996. The last large-scale reconstruction was carried out in 2012. The hotel's housing stock is represented by 263 rooms of the categories Superior, Deluxe, Suite, Junior Suite and Royal Suite.

The hotel complex has several swimming pools, sun terraces, bars, restaurants (the main one with a buffet and several a la carte), spa, gym, sports grounds, parking, beauty salon, internet cafe, diving -center, laundry, dry cleaning, physician's office and much more.



Housing stock

As previously noted, the hotel has at its disposal 263 rooms. They are located in four three-story buildings. According to tourists who had previously rested at Iberotel Palace 5 * (Sharm El Sheikh), the location of the rooms is the most successful in the blocks located closer to the reception. Therefore, experienced travelers are advised to check in these apartments if possible.

In general, regardless of the category, all rooms are very spacious (from 36 sq. Meters), bright, with a fresh repair. They are furnished with modern furniture and appliances. There is a large flat-screen TV with cable channels (including those broadcasting in Russian), a refrigerator, a safe, air conditioning, a bathroom with a hairdryer, and a balcony or terrace with a pleasant view. Rooms are cleaned daily and towels are changed. Maids update their bedding several times a week. At any time, guests have the opportunity to use the room service.



Cost

As for the price of accommodation in the five-star Iberotel Palace, according to the Russians who have been here, it is quite consistent with the category and level of service at the hotel. So, accommodation for two for seven days will cost you from 59 thousand rubles for a superior room, from 63 thousand rubles for a superior room with a partial sea view, from 94 thousand rubles for a junior suite, from 110 thousand rubles for a representative suite and from 256 thousand rubles for a royal suite. The cost of living in all cases includes meals on the ol inclusive system.
