The Light 2 Hotel 3 * (Vietnam / Nha Trang) - photos, prices and reviews

Nha Trang has been called for a long time no other than the beach capital of Vietnam. Moreover, he carries this title more than deservedly. Indeed, here absolutely every tourist can find entertainment to his taste. So, someone is attracted to Nha Trang with magnificent sea views and excellent beaches, someone - to the presence of interesting historical monuments, and someone arrives here in search of nightly entertainment and for surfing. The hotel base of this city will not disappoint you. It is represented by both fashionable hotels and simpler establishments.

So, if you, as well as many other tourists from different parts of the world, plan to spend an unforgettable vacation in Nha Trang and at the same time looking for a budget, but comfortable option for accommodation, then take a look at the The Light 2 Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang). Today we will take a closer look at this hotel and look at what impressions our compatriots have already stayed here.





The Light 2 Hotel 3 * on the map

This hotel boasts a very good location. So, it is located on a busy street with a lot of restaurants and shops, almost in the center of Nha Trang. So that all the facilities of the entertainment infrastructure of the tourist capital of Vietnam will be at your fingertips. Hotel guests can walk to the beach in just three minutes. There is an airport in the vicinity of Nha Trang. You can get from it to the hotel in 40 minutes. You can do this either by taxi or by ordering a transfer to the hotel.

Description and photo of the hotel

The Light 2 Hotel 3 * is a small hotel (only 50 rooms) with a very pleasant and relaxing atmosphere. It has recently been renovated, so you are waiting for updated and equipped with all necessary apartments. The hotel has a restaurant, bar, car rental, tour desk and laundry service. In addition, here you can use the services of a business center. The hotel also provides internet access. This hotel will surely appeal to young people who have arrived in Vietnam in search of noisy and stormy entertainment, as well as family people with children who prefer to relax on the beach and travel to places of interest.





Hotel Policies

As in many other hotels (both Vietnam and other countries of the world), check-in of staying guests at The Light 2 Hotel 3 * is carried out after two in the afternoon. On the last day of your stay, the occupied apartments must be vacated before noon. However, if your flight is in the evening, and the hotel has available rooms, then you will most likely be extended for several hours, without requiring any additional payment. You can pay here both in cash and with plastic cards.

Additional Information

The hotel allows guests with children. Moreover, if you place a child under nine years old on the sleeping places available in your apartment, you will not have to pay extra for this. You can also ask for an extra bed. This service at The Light 2 Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang) is paid. Please note that you must inform the hotel in advance about your need for an extra bed and wait for confirmation. As for guests with pets, the hotel policy does not allow their accommodation. Therefore, if you want to go to Nha Trang in the company of your pet, then you will have to find another hotel in the Pets Friend category.





Rooms

The housing stock of The Light 2 Hotel 3 * (Vietnam) is represented by 50 apartments located in a multi-storey building equipped with an elevator. The rooms here are of the following categories: standard, superior and deluxe. Please note that most standard rooms do not have windows. Balconies are only available in deluxe rooms. Regardless of the type, all hotel rooms are equipped with a private bathroom with a hairdryer and a variety of bath and toiletries, stocks of which are replenished daily, TV (there is a Russian channel), air conditioning, telephone, refrigerator. Throughout the hotel you can use free Wi-Fi. Rooms are cleaned and towels are changed every day. Maids change bedding twice a week. In addition, hotel guests always have the opportunity to order room service and use the concierge service.





Cost of living

Room rates at The Light 2 Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang, Vietnam) can be called very attractive with confidence. So, for example, a seven-day stay in this hotel in January will cost you 236 euros for a standard double room, 261 euros for a superior room and 305 euros for a deluxe room. Prices shown include breakfast in the hotel restaurant.

The Light 2 Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang): reviews of Russian tourists

Since so many people planning a trip abroad today try to get acquainted with the opinions of compatriots who have already stayed in one place or another, we bring to your attention the generalized comments of travelers who spent their holidays this year in this hotel. Immediately make a reservation that most tourists liked this hotel. They do not regret the money spent and claim that this is a good place to stay in Nha Trang. But we will understand in more detail.





Comments from travelers about the hotel itself

As for the rooms of The Light 2 Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang), then, according to our compatriots, they are consistent with the description. So, experienced travelers are advised to pay attention to the fact that most of the standard rooms do not have windows. By the way, this is a feature not only of this hotel, but also of many other hotels in Vietnam. At the same time, air conditioning and fans are installed in the rooms, so there will be no shortage of fresh air. If you want to get a room with a window, then book a deluxe room. The suites also have balconies. The rooms themselves are small but very clean and comfortable. It has everything you need: an electric kettle, a refrigerator, a hairdryer. Also, the guests were pleasantly surprised by the fact that the maids tirelessly on a daily basis update the bathroom supplies of toiletries (razors, cotton buds, shower gels, shampoos, toothbrushes, etc.). According to them, such a service is rarely seen even in five-star hotels, not to mention three-star ones. Rooms were also cleaned and towels changed every day. Bed linen was changed about once a week. Another tip from those who have already visited this hotel is to recommend, if possible, checking into rooms located on the upper floors. In this case, noise from neighboring establishments will not reach your apartment.





Comments from our compatriots on the location of the hotel

Travelers also liked the location of The Light 2 Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang). The reviews of our compatriots boil down to the fact that the hotel is located in the very center of the resort capital of Vietnam. Therefore, all the entertainment and the entire infrastructure of this city for guests will be at hand. In addition, in its vicinity there are many restaurants and bars where you can eat very tasty and cheap enough or have a good time with a glass of your favorite drink. Our compatriots also liked the proximity of the hotel to the beach. According to them, you can reach it literally in five minutes at a slow pace. It is convenient from here to make trips to the sights and other settlements of Vietnam. By the way, experienced travelers are advised to use a gray taxi. They have the most attractive prices. You can also rent a motorbike. However, it is recommended to drive very carefully, as traffic in Vietnam is very chaotic, and many drivers simply ignore both the rules and the traffic signals. For the same reason, you should be very careful when crossing the road.

Impression of tourists about food and staff

Guests at The Light 2 Hotel 3 * are offered breakfast only. Moreover, they are quite tasty and varied. So, you can taste cereals, pastries, salads, sausages, and even soups. Some tourists considered the breakfast time a little inconvenience - from 6 a.m. to 9 a.m. Therefore, those who like to sleep longer were sometimes late for a meal. On the other hand, the need for early awakening contributed to the fact that the vacation did not take place in bed (you can sleep at home), but on the beach, on walks or trips to interesting places in this amazing country.

As for the hotel staff, our compatriots left mostly positive impressions about it. According to them, the employees here are very welcoming and pleasant people, and also do an excellent job with their duties. Also note that almost none of the hotel staff speaks Russian. However, you can always explain in basic English.





Reviews of tourists about beach vacations and entertainment

The vast majority of our compatriots were very pleased with their beach holidays in Nha Trang. Moreover, The Light 2 Hotel 3 * is located just a five-minute walk from the coastline. According to tourists, here you will find the cleanest fine sand and warm clear water. In addition, the beaches have everything you need for water sports. Since the beaches in Nha Trang are municipal, renting sun loungers and umbrellas here will require an additional charge. Experienced travelers also recommend, if possible, at least a couple of times to travel outside Nha Trang. Indeed, in the vicinity of this city there are also magnificent beaches, characterized by snow-white sand.

As for entertainment, the hotel in question in this vein cannot offer anything special to its guests. The only thing you can book here is a tour, rent a motorbike, and use free Wi-Fi.
