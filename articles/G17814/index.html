Mobile Dolphinarium in Kazan

A visit to the dolphinarium is an event that leaves a lasting impression and gives a lot of positive emotions. For adults and children, the marine animal show will be an exciting and interesting performance. A trip to the dolphinarium will be a great gift for a child on his birthday or on the occasion of a holiday. Young visitors, especially for the first time, are extremely interested in watching the tricks and tricks that unusual artists show.



Dolphinarium in Kazan is a tour of the unique Moscow mobile dolphinarium. He arrived in the city at the end of December and stayed here all winter, so the residents of the city have the opportunity to see the show with their own eyes more than once. The composition of the troupe, which will represent the dolphinarium in Kazan, is as follows: Black Sea dolphins, charming fur seals and a unique white whale. It is this marine mammal that will become the highlight of the program - not every city provides the opportunity to see it.

For all visitors to the dolphinarium, information will be interesting that communication with dolphins is not only pleasant, but also useful. It is scientifically proven that they have a positive effect on the human psyche, allow you to recover faster from stress and heal mental wounds. Dolphins can even cure diseases! So you can combine business with pleasure!

The Dolphinarium in Kazan for all visitors provides a unique opportunity to look closely at their pets, touch them, take a memorable photo. There are no words to describe the joy of children who reverently and enthusiastically communicate with marine mammals.



Dolphinarium in Kazan is equipped in such a way that visitors can feel as comfortable as possible. The territory is heated using special systems, and a constant temperature is maintained at about 17 degrees. Thanks to this, both visitors and pets feel as comfortable as possible, which means that performances can be held throughout the winter. The capacity of the dolphinarium is 570 people.

The program of performances, which was prepared by its employees, includes demonstrations of marine animals, exciting contests and unforgettable shows. All visitors have the opportunity to see with their own eyes what incredible things marine mammals can do. The general composition of the troupe is 1 white whale, 3 fur seals and 3 Black Sea dolphins.

Ticket price

The touring time of the mobile dolphinarium in Kazan is limited. In this regard, pre-sale of tickets is open, which can be purchased at the box office of the city. Dolphinarium in Kazan (entry prices start at 500 rubles) is available for most residents. The upper ceiling of prices reaches 1000 rubles. For this money, visitors get the opportunity to watch shows lasting 50 minutes.



The organizers are sure that there are many who want to visit the dolphinarium in Kazan. The address at which it is located is the territory near the Kazan Arena stadium. There is a covered room, which will receive guests until spring.
