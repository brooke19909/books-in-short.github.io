The best hotels of Gelendzhik: reviews and photos

Gelendzhik is a resort city. It attracts tourists with its amazing embankment, one of the most colorful and longest in Russia, the Blue Bay with clean mountain and sea air, which has a healing and restorative effect on the body, undermined by an endless series of stresses and burdens of life. Thousands of tourists come here. Therefore, it is important to book a hotel room in the sunny city in advance.

The best hotels of Gelendzhik will be presented in this article.
