How to make a rose from foil: a master class

Flowers - this, of course, is beautiful, a charming bouquet of roses is especially mesmerizing. But fresh flowers are not as durable as we would like, they quickly dry out, losing their presentable appearance. You can decorate the house with a beautiful, sophisticated bouquet of roses from foil. A properly designed composition will delight the owner for a long time. This is an incredibly beautiful element of home decor and a nice gift that will never fade.

We will be happy to share with you a master class on how to make a rose out of foil. Foil is a pleasant silver color. Flower buds are guaranteed to be beautiful.

Material

Before you make a rose from foil, prepare the material and a place to work. Foil is a fragile material, it must be cut very carefully, so it is better to use a blade or a sharp clerical knife, a ruler, and protect the workplace with thick cardboard or a chipboard board.

The foil already has a beautiful color, but some craftswomen prefer to paint ready-made roses to give them a dull color. Prepare a spray can of the desired color, if desired. Variety of roses can be suitable sparkles, beads, beads.

How to make a flower - a rose from foil

Let's start creating these wonderful colors. First, prepare the foil strip. Measure 50-60 cm from the roll. Putting the ruler on the mark, draw a blade along it.

Fold the line like this: place the foil with the shiny side down, fold the bottom so that the end of the line is in the middle. On the sides of the foil sheet, make lateral folds inwards of about 1-2 cm. Then wrap the upper part to the middle.

Now proceed to the formation of the flower. How to make a rose from foil? Roll each strip of foil in a spiral to form a flower bud. Since the foil is a soft, plastic and malleable material, it is able to take any shape. Therefore, in the process of spiral formation, bend either inward or outward segment, forming light waves.

From the remainder of the segment, make a flower leg, tightly twisting the foil. If you want to make a stable bouquet, substitute a thin stick or a dense wire under the bud.

Form several flowers to create a composition. Finished can be decorated, as already mentioned, with paints, spray cans, various sparkles and beads.

Here's how to make a foil rose in a few minutes. The process of creating is so fast that in a short time you can create a whole bunch of these attractive flowers.

The formation of a bouquet

Ready-made buds can be used as you like: they decorate gift boxes, complement compositions, but we suggest you collect a bouquet. As mentioned above, to make the flowers more stable, take a thick wire or stick. With the remnants of foil on the buds, connect the wire and roses. Cut some more foil and decorate the stem. Gather the bunch in a bunch, add more elements, these can be various branches for decoration, covered with silver, dry twigs and flowers.

Tie the bouquet with a bow or put in a suitable vase. Here's how to make a foil rose with your own hands.
