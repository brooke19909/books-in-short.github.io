Hotels in Kingisepp: overview, rating, addresses and photos

The small town with the name of Kingisepp, which is a little difficult for a Russian, is located near the border with Estonia, in the Leningrad Region. He acquired his current name in the early 20s of the 20th century in honor of the revolutionary Victor Kingisepp - an Estonian by birth. Previously, the place was called Yamburg. Its turbulent history spans 6 centuries, and its modest originality amazes visitors.

Kingisepp is a chamber town, lives a little less than 50 thousand people. A significant part is built up with new skyscrapers and houses of the Soviet era with the rarest intersperses of old buildings from the late XIX - early XX centuries. Almost all historical buildings were destroyed in the fierce battles of the Second World War, including one of the rare gems - the Catherine Church.
