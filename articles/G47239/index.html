5 * Hotels: Stella Makadi Garden Resort (Egypt, Makadi). Hotel description, reviews

The new and rapidly developing resort region of Makadi is built up with class 4 and 5 hotels. Stella Makadi Garden Resort is one of the model hotels in the area. It is new, modern. Built in 2011. The hotel is located on the second line from the sea. It has a common territory with another hotel of the same chain - Stella Makadi Beach Resort. Therefore, checking into one hotel, you actually get at your disposal as many as two.



Makadi

The former deserted desert has now flourished with an excellent tourist infrastructure. And all thanks to the beautiful sea, huge beaches, colorful fish and unspoiled nature. To develop this area began in the nineties of the last century, after Hurghada. But since there were no settlements of their own, this district was administratively assigned to the central resort. There are still not very many Russian-speaking tourists here, but Europeans like the resort. It is good to relax with children here, quietly, calmly. Very clean sea, along which you can walk for kilometers. Connoisseurs of diving and snorkeling love Makadi. There are coral reefs near almost every beach, and they are not very far from the coast. If you make brief conclusions, we can say that the Makadi district is devoid of the fuss and noisy entertainment characteristic of central Hurghada. But he has everything else, for which people come to Egypt. Stella Makadi Garden Resort 5 is located in an area where only status hotels are being built. It will provide you with a measured rest to the taste of tourists of any nationality and cultural characteristics.



What are 5 class hotels here

Stella Makadi Garden Resort is a prime example of a hotel that, like beads in a necklace, is strung on the coast of Makadi Bay. The hotels here are practically all new and of a class not lower than the Quartet. Most of them have access to the water from a pier or pontoon, and only Azur hotels have sandy beaches. The area in Makadi is windy, and all hotels have protective screens for vacationers. "Fives" in this resort area correspond to their classification. They are fully capable of providing you not only comfortable, but also an elite rest. Five-star Makadi hotels, as a rule, are designed for European tourists, and therefore you should expect a very high level of service from them. Each of them is a mini-town with its own infrastructure, has a unique architectural style and a special fabulous atmosphere. As a rule, outwardly these hotels resemble the palaces of the Egyptian pharaohs. And inside they are equipped quite conveniently and in accordance with all the requirements of spoiled travelers. Not without reason, many of them, arriving in Makadi, claim that they did not expect such a high service from such a country as Egypt. Stella Makadi Gardens Resort 5 has everything described above: a park and shops, animation and the beach, first-class food and relaxation. It has already been said that the hotel is not directly on the beach, and its buildings are six hundred meters from the sea. Water recreation

Stella Makadi Garden Resort 5 provides guests with the opportunity to use the beach of the Beach Resort Hotel. This is ten minutes at a slow pace through the park. Or take the hotel bus - and there will be no problems. It runs from the beach to the main building. The coast is well-groomed, with white sand, about half a kilometer in length. On the beach - pontoons, sunbeds, umbrellas, toilets and showers. Very clean and well cleaned. Tourists claim that the sea is simply incredible, especially in the morning. Stingrays swim to the reef near the beach, you can see moray eels, turtles, octopuses. It is inconvenient to enter water from sand. Going down to the sea is necessary only from the pontoons. But you can swim with a mask - a lot of fish. There are two slopes on the main pier - one just for swimming, and the second at the end rests on a reef with corals and fish. A diving center is open on the beach, and there are also many water activities: banana, parasailing, skiing. Volleyball is played on the beach, and daytime animation seduces tourists here. But the pools in the hotel are worth it to visit them at least once. There are four reservoirs with water slides. They completely imitate swimming in the sea - there are even artificial waves.
