Absolut Real Estate Company: reviews of employees and customers

The vast majority of people who would like to purchase real estate, turn to companies-developers. However, unfortunately, not every developer can boast an excellent reputation. Often, equity holders are dissatisfied with the delay in building construction, the quality of work performed and materials used, communication with company managers and non-fulfillment of other promises made by the developer. Therefore, finding a suitable developer is extremely difficult.

But what if you want to get a job in a similar company? You should familiarize yourself with a lot of information in advance. For example, for many it matters what reputation the company in which he plans to get a job has earned among customers, as well as counterparties in the market. Among other things, it would be nice to know from a reliable source whether the organization is properly fulfilling its obligations to workers. For example, does it pay wages on time and in full? And does its level correspond to the duties that you have to fulfill? Is it easy to let employees go on legal annual leave? Will you be officially employed? What skills and personal qualities will you need in order to successfully continue to work in the organization in question? How does the leadership punish its wards for mistakes, inaccuracies in work, violation of labor discipline? All this is of great importance.

In this article, the Absolute Real Estate Agency (Belgorod) will be examined in detail. Employee reviews are of particular interest. However, it is important to study other issues. What do customer reviews say about Absolute Real Estate? What are the features of work in the organization in question? You can get answers by carefully reading this article.

Positive customer feedback

But less is written about successful deals, because they think about something completely different. So it happened in the case of Absolute Real Estate. There are positive reviews, but not many. Negative customer feedback

What else do you leave about Absolute Real Estate? Among the negative aspects, customers distinguish the following:

  • Short reservation periods - only two weeks. If you do not manage to get a mortgage during this time, then your reservation fee will burn, as practice shows (despite the guarantee of returning 75% of the amount).

So describe the work of the company "Absolute Real Estate" reviews of some customers. Trust them or not, you need to decide for yourself.

Conclusion

How to decide whether you should become part of the team of the organization in question? It is important to carefully examine what the opinions of customers, contractors, and experts say about the Absolute Real Estate company. Such independent opinions will help to decide on employment.

In any case, even if it seems to you that you are satisfied with all the requirements of a potential employer, and that you are ideally suited to your chosen position, take the time to carefully study the working conditions. If the proposed schedule is new to you, think carefully about whether you will be comfortable working on it, whether it will become exhausting.

No matter what decision you make, remember how important it is to leave truthful and objective reviews about your employers.
