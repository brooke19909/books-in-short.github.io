Hat for the girl: make it yourself

At any time of the year, each child should have a suitable hat. In summer, this is a beautiful panama hat that will protect the baby from the scorching rays of the sun. In winter, a warm hat for the girl will protect the child from the cold. Today, the range of hats designed for young women is wide and very diverse. However, it is often almost impossible to choose the right model. Either the right size is not there, the quality is in serious doubt, or it just doesn‚Äôt work to pick up a hat that matches the color of outerwear.



But such problems are not known to those who make clothes for their child on their own. In this case, to acquire fabric or yarn of the required quality is not difficult. In addition, you can choose not only the right color, but also the shade of the future product. If desired, you can even use melange yarn to wear the same headdress for a child in combination with different clothes.



In addition, the hat for the girl can be decorated in various ways. For this purpose, you can use purchased lace or flowers and other elements made with various techniques using your own hands. This will ensure that the outerwear and winter or autumn hat for the girl will be made in the same style. Most likely, it will not work in the store to find a suitable option. In addition, the product you create will be one of a kind. After all, a hat is made in one copy and for a certain size.

If the headpiece is sewn, then you can choose two different materials. The first will be used as a lining. This fabric should be subject to more stringent requirements. If the quality is low, the baby will experience discomfort while wearing things. The top layer is also desirable to be made of natural material. However, if desired, any other suitable material may be used.



A hat for a girl can also be knitted. You can also use crochet or knitting needles. In the first case, you get a beautiful openwork product in which it will not be hot even during very intense heat. A crocheted hat can also be knitted for the winter season. For this purpose, it is necessary to choose a fairly dense pattern. If desired, you can also knit a hat simply with crochets. However, given that the hat is intended for the girl, it must be properly decorated. Items decorated with flowers and leaflets crocheted using the Irish lace technique look very attractive. If a summer hat is made, it is better to choose a beautiful lace and a thinner hook. In this case, the panama will keep its shape well.

Often, knitted hats for girls are made using knitting needles. In this case, it is possible to get a thin hat. If you choose cotton, then this will be the best option for the summer. By purchasing wool, you can knit a warm hat for the winter. In this case, it is desirable to make a two-layer headdress for maximum protection from the cold.
