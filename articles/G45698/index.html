Costa Blu Hotel 4 * (Corfu, Greece): description, leisure and reviews

Costa Blu Hotel 4 is located on a picturesque hill in the eastern region of Corfu in Greece. Due to the fact that the hotel is located on a hill, the windows of the rooms offer a wonderful view of the sea. The hotel itself is literally immersed in greenery, because many trees, flowers and shrubs grow on its territory.





Vacationers often admit that it seems that you are traveling on a huge cruise ship, which slowly cuts through the sea water.

Neighborhood

Tourists who stay at Costa Blu Hotel are strongly recommended to take a walk around the island and get acquainted with its attractions.





Moreover, you do not need to go far - many ancient architectural structures are 3-10 km from the hotel. The nearest airport is 9 km away. Corfu city center is 11 km away.

Sports & Entertainment

Keeping yourself in good shape is best done at the fitness center or gym located on the Costa Blu Hotel 4 (Greece). There is an outdoor pool, sun loungers and umbrellas are located nearby, and delicious cocktails or fresh drinks can be prepared at the nearest bar.





There is a massage room (a massage course is ordered in advance), there is a sauna and a bath. Paid tennis courts function, coach services are ordered separately.

As for connecting to the Internet, WI-FI costs tourists 20 EUR per day or 10 EUR per hour on public terms.

Beach holiday

Costa Blu Hotel (Corfu) is located in close proximity to the sea, on the first line. There is a private sandy beach, vacationers are provided with sun loungers and umbrellas. There are few people resting and not enough space. Of course, some tourists are looking for just such a vacation, but the condition of the beach is quite satisfactory. According to vacationers, near the hotel there is an unequipped beach. But the equipped place for a beach holiday is located 500 meters from the hotel. There is also a shower and a dressing room. Water Sports Center is 800 meters from the property.

Catering services

On the territory of the Costa Blu Hotel (Greece) For breakfast and dinner they offer buffet meals, lunch - half board. There is a restaurant, there are 2 bars. In the vicinity of the hotel there are many eateries where you can have a tasty and nutritious snack.





The main restaurant serves international and Greek cuisine. The bar on the veranda offers evening shows. There is a cozy bar in the lobby where you can enjoy good drinks and snacks. In the caf√© in the garden you can enjoy coffee, tea and cakes.

Family holidays with children

Costa Blu Hotel has everything for the smallest vacationers: a platform for active games, a shallow children's pool, you can use the services of a nanny (for a fee). The restaurant has the opportunity to order dishes from the children's menu, upon request, the staff will bring a high chair.

Additional services

There is a conference room where you can organize events for 150 people. This is a very convenient service for those who are planning a corporate vacation or are going to negotiate with partners. There is a dry cleaning service and laundry facilities, so clothing is easy to put in order. Free parking and luggage storage are located on site.

There is a beauty salon for vacationers, there is a hairdresser. The reception is open around the clock, here you can exchange currency. It should be noted that this hotel is not intended for hectic pastime, but everyone who chooses a relaxing holiday, get a real pleasure.

Internal regulations and rules of residence

Accommodation at Costa Blu Hotel starts at 13.00. On the day of departure, you need to pack in advance and leave the room before 12.00 (this is necessary so that the staff has time to change the bedclothes and put the room in order to check in for the next vacationer). The hotel allows children with any age to stay, and for children who are not yet 2 years old, a cot and accommodation are provided for free.

The rooms can accommodate extra beds (beds are reserved in advance). The service requires payment in the amount of 30 to 50% of the room rate per day for 1 guest. No more than one extra bed in the room. It is forbidden to live with pets. The hotel accepts MasterCard, Visa plastic cards.

Sightseeing vacation

Greece has long been considered the cradle of the history of mankind. On the island of Corfu, many attractions have been preserved, each of which deserves special attention of tourists.





The hotel can book tour services and visit many interesting places. These are the Byzantine Museum, the Museum of Asian Art, the Monastery of Our Lady of Platitera, the Regent's Palace and the Museum of Kapodistrias. Be sure to visit Gasturi and look at the palace of Achilleion.





It is worth visiting the fortresses of the Middle Ages that in Kassiopia, Paleokastritsa and Gardika, go by boat to the island of Pondikonisi, where an amazing church of the 13th century has been preserved.

A bus stop is located near the hotel, 50 meters away, and you can master any direction yourself.

Tourists reviews

Hotel Costa Blu Hotel is in line with the specified level of service. Tourists note that the road from the airport takes no more than 40 minutes. A sufficiently detailed briefing is carried out for a good rest.

They categorically do not recommend agreeing to a promoter due to the fact that they are located practically in semi-basement rooms, and when the water is drained, there is some smell, increased humidity and even mosquitoes.

In general, tourists pay attention to the excellent location of the hotel, a clean pool, as well as friendly and friendly staff. The food is diverse, the products are always fresh. During a walk, it is recommended to visit the town of Benitses, which takes 10 minutes on foot. Despite the good food at the hotel, vacationers prefer to try the local cuisine, which is offered in many restaurants in the area.
