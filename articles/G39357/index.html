Hotel "Kaleidoscope", St. Petersburg: address, description of rooms, service, reviews

Guests of the Northern capital of Russia prefer to choose places to stay in the city center. Close to major attractions and memorials. Kaleidoscope Hotels (Saint Petersburg) are known for their design and good location. Nearby are many restaurants, bars and clubs. Guests can spend a comfortable time in the rooms and see the city.

Location

The Kaleidoscope hotel chain is known throughout Russia and Europe. The organizers of this hotel complex are the spouses Elena and Eugene. The couple loves to travel, so they try to take into account all the shortcomings that they saw in other hotels in their hotels. Everything is aimed at ensuring that guests can relax and not need anything. Staying at The Faces Kaleidoscope should be at home.

Attractions and landmarks nearby

All Kaleidoscope hotels are concentrated in the city center. Kaleidoscope hotels: service and accommodation

Guests are offered several options for rooms of different cost and capacity. Moreover, they differ in their size and number of people to stay. Conditions and comfort are the same throughout the hotel chain.

The hotel provides an opportunity for all guests early booking rooms. At the same time, the reservation can be canceled without charging a penalty for 5 days. You can cancel it later on condition that you pay the daily rate.

Check-in at Kaleidoscope hotels, reviews of which will be lower, is possible only for citizens who have reached the age of 18. Children and adolescents can only stay with the parents or legal representatives (by notarized power of attorney).

Check-in time is 14:00 (with the consent of the administration and availability of places, earlier check-in is acceptable). Guaranteed early check-in is also possible provided that 50% of the cost of the first day is paid. Eviction - 12:00. A later eviction (from 12:00 to 23:00) is possible subject to payment of 50% of the cost of a day's stay or 100% of the payment for eviction after 23:00.

Children under 12 stay free at the hotel, however breakfast is charged extra. Foreign citizens can take advantage of visa support (1,500 rubles per call).

Safety regulations

In the hotels "Kaleidoscope" (St. Petersburg) they are doing everything possible to ensure that guests stay as comfortable and safe as possible. Visitors can use the safe for valuables (all information from administrators). It is forbidden to give the room key to unauthorized persons. The hotel does not allow pets, as well as smoking and drinking alcohol. An additional cleaning fee for the room in which they smoked is 5,000 rubles.

Do not disturb guests from other rooms and disturb public order. It is not allowed to store prohibited substances and objects in rooms.

Service and facilities

Each room is serviced daily. Guests can inform the receptionist and ongoing cleaning will be carried out as necessary. All apartments have their own kitchenette with the necessary set of appliances and utensils for cooking and heating food. Also, guests are provided with several types of tea and coffee (the set is replenished during cleaning).

The bathroom, which is combined with the toilet, has towels and a set of cosmetics for all residents. If necessary, the kit is replenished during the stay.

Air conditioning, satellite TV and internet are included in the range of services provided by the hotel to all guests. All this is complemented by a hairdryer and ironing facilities. If necessary, wash items, guests should contact the administrator. Laundry service is available for a fee.

Hotels "Kaleidoscope" (St. Petersburg) include food (breakfast) in the price. During the stay of children at the hotel, breakfast is paid separately (for children under 12 years old). If necessary, guests can refuse this service.

Guests are given the opportunity to hold holidays and celebrations at the hotel. To do this, it is necessary to inform the administrator about your special holiday and the room will be decorated by this time.
