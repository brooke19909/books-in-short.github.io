Setting up digital channels on a Samsung TV. Universal instruction

This article will describe the universal instructions for setting up digital channels on a Samsung TV of any model. This is a fairly simple operation; performing the manipulations outlined in the future will not be difficult. Recommendations will also be given as to which connection method is most optimal in which case.

Commutation

Any tuning of digital channels on a Samsung TV starts with the fact that it is fully assembled and then switched. That is, at the first stage, the TV must be removed from the package. Then assemble and install on site. Then you need to connect the power cord to the connector of the multimedia device at one end, and with a plug to the power supply network. Then we bring the signal wire and connect it to the input labeled ANT. Again, the signal wire can provide information from the antenna or from the equipment of any cable provider.

Software setting

Further, the setting of digital channels should be implemented at the software level. Then you need to go to the "Settings" menu and select "Channels". Next, the auto search mode is activated by selecting the item of the same name in the menu. Before starting it, you need to specify only the source of the television programs. It can be either an antenna or a cable terminal. Next, the system will automatically search and find all available programs. At the end of this stage, you will only need to save the list.

Testing

At the final stage of setting up digital channels on a Samsung TV of any modification, you need to check the list of TV programs and make sure that they are working. To do this, just go through all the broadcasts and see the quality of their pictures. As a rule, there are no problems with digital television and everything is set up with minimal human involvement. After that, you can watch various channels.

Conclusion

As part of this material, a brief instruction on setting up digital channels on a Samsung TV of any modern modification is presented. Cable programs are best viewed in cities. But the wireless reception of television programs is uncontested in rural areas. It is based on this condition that it is recommended to choose a broadcast source.
