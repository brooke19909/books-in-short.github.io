Instructions on how to make blood for Halloween

Recently in Russia it has become fashionable to hold the Halloween holiday. In honor of him, parties are organized at which guests dress up in various scary costumes, for example, the insidious vampire or witch. In order for the event to look as natural and intimidating as possible, various decorations are needed, of which the most basic part is blood. Accordingly, there is a problem how to make blood on Halloween, and it is rather difficult to solve it.



Indeed, in order to create a convincing image of an evil vampire, it is not necessary to have real blood. Yes, in joke shops, on shelves, it can also be found, but it is not always convenient to use. But nothing is impossible! There are many ways to make an imitation of blood: capillary, venous and arterial, curled and fresh, non-food and food, drying and not. Halloween blood should be one in one as natural, stored for a long time, easy to wash, do not harm your health. Ideally, a pleasant taste is desired. Without it, a holiday and not a holiday at all, so let's look at several options for its preparation.

Now, how to make sugar blood for Halloween based on sugar syrup. You will need the following ingredients: sugar syrup - half a liter, red food coloring - two teaspoons, distilled water - a quarter cup, sifted flour / corn starch - a quarter cup.





First of all, we prepare the syrup. To do this, take: distilled water - 150 ml, sugar - 350 grams, citric acid - 2/3 teaspoon, soda - 1/4 teaspoon.

We heat water, dissolve sugar and bring to a boil. Then add citric acid and cook for 45 minutes on low heat, covering it with a lid. Remove from heat, cool a little and add baking soda. We are waiting for 8-10 minutes and remove the foam. We mix the syrup with the elements that remain, and that's it - the blood is ready. A very simple way and it looks very natural.
