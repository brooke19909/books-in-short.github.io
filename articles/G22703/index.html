Indesit washing machine: an example of Italian quality

What determines the price of goods?

Many international companies are focusing their efforts on creating the most popular household appliances. Among them, the Indesit Italian company holds a strong position. This company has become famous for producing high-quality and relatively inexpensive products. A separate position in the classification of household appliances produced by the organization is occupied by Indesit washing machines. Prices for these products vary and depend on several parameters. Firstly, from the selected model. It is known that the more functions included in the equipment, the more expensive it is. Secondly - from the place of production. It is clear that the device made in Italy will cost more than the Chinese counterpart.

The Indesit washing machine can have either front load of laundry or vertical. The first lineup is wider and has several more varieties than the second. The reason for this is the high level of consumer demand. In turn, the first and second categories of equipment have their own classes. Another point buyers pay attention to is price. It is noteworthy that no matter how much we would like to purchase goods cheaper, a clear understanding of the fact that quality products cannot be cheap is hidden in the "margins" of the mind.





Front-loading models

A relatively inexpensive, high-quality and compact option is the Indesit WISN 82 washing machine. Loading of linen into this machine is done frontally. For a small price (about 250 cu) you will receive an aggregate with the best possible functions. Of course, it would be nice if the maximum spin speed was at least approximately 1000 rpm. But alas, this is only possible in more expensive models. This automatic machine allows you to easily load up to 5 kg of dry laundry into the drum. In this case, the cleaning and washing mode is selected from the available 11 programs. It is noteworthy that this inexpensive device has an option for silk, delicate spinning and even sports shoes. The Indesit washing machine WISN 82 is equipped with the Fuzzy Logic intellectual function, thanks to which the operation of the equipment is automatically optimized depending on the amount of laundry loaded into it.

A more advanced and more expensive option is the IWSC 6105. It belongs to the category of narrow devices. The depth of the apparatus is 45 cm. The height and width are the same as most models produced by this company: 80 cm and 60 cm. The described option is equipped with 16 programs at a maximum spin speed of 1000 rpm. At the same time, in one washing cycle, up to 6 kg of laundry can be loaded into the drum of the washing machine. It is noteworthy that this model, like many others, is equipped with a special function to save electricity and water. Any errors of the Indesit IWSC 6105 washing machine are described in detail in the instruction manual, which is an invariable component of the equipment package.





The second category of equipment

Some people prefer vertical laundry machines. One of such models is the Indesit washing machine WITP 1021. In terms of overall dimensions, this option can be attributed to small and compact machines. Its height is 80 cm, depth - 40 cm and width 60 cm. At the same time, this washing machine is equipped with foam control functions, leakage protection and others. The cost of this model is within 280 cu
