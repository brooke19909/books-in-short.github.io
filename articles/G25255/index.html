Hotel Handakas Hotel 2 * Greece, Heraklion, Amoudara: reviews

Amoudara is a famous resort village located 8 km west of Heraklion. The resort is popular with tourists due to the abundance of spacious sandy beaches and attractions, proximity to the capital, a wealth of infrastructure and a colorful palette of placements. Among the guests of Amudara, a significant proportion are our compatriots. Those who choose to relax at this resort can stay in a wide variety of hotels: Amoudara has both luxury spa complexes and inexpensive hotels. As one of the popular budget options, tourists recommend the Handakas Hotel 2 *.

Acquaintance

According to reviews, Handakas Hotel 2 * is a home-like cozy place. The hotel is suitable for those who want to directly get acquainted with the local way of life. The authors mostly respond positively to the owners of the institution, recommending them as welcoming, kind hosts. In the evenings, guests can gather with their friends for conversations in the living room or by the pool, sit quietly and relax after a day's walks. The restaurant here always offers delicious and varied food. According to tourists, Handakas Hotel 2 * in Crete is a hotel that cannot be judged only by the number of stars in the category. Many hope to return here again.

Location

Budget hotel Handakas Hotel 2 * (address: Crete, Greece, Gazi, Heraklion, Amoudara, September 3) is located 300 meters from the beach (sandy) of Amoudara, surrounded by beautiful green vineyards (in the green zone of Amoudara Lasithiou). A distance of 200 m separates the property from bars, restaurants and supermarkets. A bus station is 200 meters away. From here, buses run to Heraklion, as well as the Heraklion port and airport. According to tourists, the hotel is located a 15-minute drive from Nikos Kazantzakis Airport (12 km), 138 km from Chania Airport.

The property is 5.5 km from attractions such as Kules Fortress, Theodoros Vardinoyanis Stadium and Venetian Walls. Guests can easily get from here to the center of Amoudara Lasithiou, 5 km from the hotel. The beach is located 300 m (2nd line). The hotel and the beach are separated by a road.

Handakas Hotel 2 *: description

The institution belongs to the type of hotels. Consists of one storey building. The size of the hotel allows it to be classified as small (it has less than 50 rooms).

According to reviews, guests get the feeling that they live with some distant relatives. Always in the living room, someone sits on a sofa and watches TV. Staff of old age, wearing badges is not accepted here. Treating guests is very simple, homely. According to reviews, the staff are at the reception almost in slippers and with a friendly smile, observe how the guests go into the room. Only a few of the staff know English, no one understands Russian. Rooms are cleaned approximately every two days, but it does not get any cleaner. Vacationers say that employees can easily enter a room not only for cleaning. For example, a letter from a guide can be transmitted by laying it on a bed in the absence of a guest.





Breakfast vacationers call meager, but admit that for a week of rest they do not have time to get bored. You can walk to the sea in about 10 minutes. You can spend up to five minutes on the road to the bus stop.

The rooms on the ground floor are hidden in pomegranate bushes, in the higher rooms you have to pay extra for using air conditioning. There is no refrigerator in the rooms. Wifi works well. After midnight, you can only enter the hotel by calling the entrance.

How do vacationers rate the service?

Hotel vacationers are called cozy. It belongs to a married couple of advanced age. All staff are also old people, the exception is the girl administrator. Due to age or in accordance with the peculiarities of the mentality, but the cleaning of the hotel is poor, vacationers say. Tourists note that on the balcony in the room you can see the dirty plastic chairs that guests have to wipe themselves with wet wipes. Sunbeds by the pool are also dirty, they do not rub, so many do not risk lying on them, but guests still use the pool, since they clean it. According to the rules, towels and bed linen should be changed every three days, in fact they are changed 1 time per week, vacationers say.

Hotel guests in their reviews share that the girl-administrator answers all questions. The owner is described as a friendly person, but only at those moments when there is no hostess, his wife. You can ask him for a hairdryer and an iron. The authors of the reviews describe the hostess as an unpleasant person who allows herself to yell at guests, look with hostility, and not greet them. The guests try to ignore it, communicate mainly with the owner, all requests are addressed to him.

How do guests respond about food?

According to reviews, for breakfast, guests are offered cheese, eggs, sausage, cottage cheese, bread, granola and cookies (all kinds of dishes - one for each type). Beach

The sandy beach is 300 meters from Handakas Hotel 2 * (Greece, Heraklion, Amoudara). Vacationers say that there are always waves at sea, many like it, but they do not recommend resting with small children here. The bottom of the sea is sandy, there are no hedgehogs. Sun beds on the beach are paid (you need to pay 2 euros or purchase drinks).
