Knitted openwork knitting needles. How to learn to knit openwork products?

Regardless of the weather changing throughout the year, a woman tries to look elegant, bright and attractive. One of the most exquisite things worn in the warm season is outerwear knitted from knitting needles in a lace style. This product goes well with any item from the wardrobe. The pattern makes it airy and adds charm to its owner. The variety of models that can be created with openwork knitting needles is very wide. That allows you to choose an option that emphasizes the merits of your figure.





Model variations

Knitted with delicate knitting needles when creating summer blouses and tops. Tunics with a pattern can be either wraparound or sweaters. Tops can have not just straps, but decorative additions covering the shoulders. The length of the sleeves when knitting openwork can vary from both the shortest and the standard size. Light and airy cardigans visually correct the proportions of the figure, making it graceful and elegant. As a fastener, you can use a brooch, a thin belt from the same yarn, a button or not to smell at all. A long cardigan with knitting openwork, gives the appearance of luxury and nobility. Jackets, blouses and other products are made by separate parts, round, across or the whole canvas with sewn sleeves.





Variety of patterns

Knitted thin openwork with knitting needles can complement the fabric with a separately created element. And also a full product can be made from openwork ornaments. Openwork knitting is very popular among experienced craftswomen and beginner needlewomen. Knowing the basic techniques of knitting loops, you can understand any pattern and create a truly chic product. Knitted summer openwork knitting needles can contain fans, floral and geometric patterns, braids, openwork paths, bumps, a chess pattern, waves, twigs, leaves, spikelets, a net and many other knitting elements in their rapport. Depending on the model, patterns allow you to knit romantic items for the festive evening, as well as laconic styles for work or a business meeting. Openwork patterns do not require additional decor details, as the decoration can make the pattern heavier. Therefore, a simple openwork knitting is enough to apply the outfit during a cultural event, presentation or anniversary.





Material used

For knitting summer lace with knitting needles, needlewomen use thin yarn without defects and knots. Knitted clothes made of cotton, acrylic, linen and viscose are well worn and pleasant to the body. Various variations in the composition and combination of threads in a percentage ratio are permissible. Dark colors of yarn will be inappropriate, as they will not convey the whole expressiveness of the pattern.

In addition to threads, when knitting openwork patterns, straight or circular knitting needles of the appropriate size are used. When creating a drawing, the main thing is not to go astray while reading the diagram. In order not to be mistaken, special row counters are used. The needlewomen also knit braid elements with the help of auxiliary devices, on which several loops are temporarily removed. If necessary, markers marking the end and beginning of rapport may be needed. Also hooks for tying and stitching parts.





Legend

Patterns connected with an openwork method are made with the help of yarn, several loops knitted together, and various variations of the facial loops with the wrong ones by reducing, crossing and adding them. There are simple patterns whose rapport contains two or four loops. There are complex ornaments containing more than a dozen loops and several rows up. The number of crochets determines the structure of the canvas. Their alternation helps to understand how airy the product will be.
