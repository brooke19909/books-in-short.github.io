The best hotels of Limassol. Cyprus

On the island of Cyprus you will find many wonderful resorts with excellent hotels that meet all European standards. Limassol is considered one of the most popular resorts. It is here that there are many historical and cultural monuments, as well as clean sandy beaches, near which the hotels of Limassol are located. You will remember Cyprus forever! Outside the city you will find the Troodos Mountains, which are popular with tourists both in summer and in winter.



Limassol impresses with a huge variety of hotels, known for their high level of service and professional, friendly staff. Almost all Limassol hotels are famous for a large number of restaurants, taverns and bars. Cyprus also offers many nightly entertainment venues. In addition, you can enjoy excellent Cypriot cuisine, sitting in a small restaurant right on the picturesque coast. The combination of a beautiful sea, a carefree holiday and the greatest cultural and historical center of Europe - all this is Cyprus.

Limassol: 5 star hotels

One of the best hotels of this type is Londa. It is located on the seashore, which avoids the hustle and bustle of the streets, and at the same time, the infrastructure is only a few meters away. Comfortable rooms with everything you need will win your favor, and the well-equipped beach will confirm your right choice in favor of this hotel. The hotel also offers several bars, an excellent restaurant, a conference room, a SPA center, a sauna, a swimming pool, a gym, and much more for a comfortable stay.



Almost all Limassol hotels (Cyprus, this is different) that operate on an all-inclusive system, offer such a wide range of services that it is perhaps difficult to imagine the best service. Such hotels have everything for a relaxing holiday for adults, as well as for fun for children.

Cyprus, Limassol. 4 star hotels

If you cannot afford a five-star hotel, choose one of the four-star hotels, which are a bit inferior in the range of services, but also offer comfortable rooms, great beaches and a wonderful quiet holiday by the sea. For example, the Park Beach Hotel hospitably offers you air-conditioned rooms, a sandy beach, a pool for children, a chic restaurant (which, among other things, offers a children's menu), a beauty salon for lovely ladies, and a massage room.



Another worth noting is a hotel like Mediterranean, which impresses with its fashionability. A large walking alley, close proximity to the city center, a huge selection of rooms (the largest of which can accommodate 2 adults and 2 children), tropical gardens surrounding the hotel - all this will not leave you indifferent!

No matter what amount you have, by whatever criteria you choose a place, whether you are traveling alone, with friends or with children, you will always find rest to your taste by choosing hotels in Limassol. Cyprus will certainly leave the most pleasant and unforgettable memories. This is a place where you want to return again and again, because it is impossible to see all the beauty of the island in one trip.
