Krasnodar reservoir: recreation, fishing and construction history

The Krasnodar reservoir is an artificial reservoir on the Kuban River in the Republic of Adygea and the Krasnodar Territory of Russia. It is the largest in the North Caucasus.



Eyes of a tourist

The first thing you pay attention to when you first get into these places is the huge mass of water that hangs over the city. A feeling of bewilderment immediately arises: how are people not afraid to live here? Driving along the dam through the spillway, which is chained to smooth concrete shores, one can observe a bewitching foamy element, striking against an iron gate, like a beast who wants to break out of the shackles on a plain stretching before it. The height of the water here is noticeably higher than life hovering below, on the plain. More recently, the dam of the Krasnodar reservoir was protected by military equipment, for example, an armored personnel carrier was located above the spillway, now it is not visible. Antiquities of the Kuban Sea

It is much safer to inspect the Krasnodar reservoir from the side of the Starokorsunskaya station, there is no longer a dam here, but there is a free approach to the water. In winter, the water level drops so much that sandbanks form. The remains of the washed banks rise like fantastic ruins, gazing at the Krasnodar reservoir. Rest in these places will bring you an unforgettable experience. Locals love to fish here, and if they are lucky, then antique vessels that occasionally wash waves from a powerful cultural layer from Starokorsunskaya to the former river bed towards Ust-Labinsk. People are attracted not only by fishing on the Krasnodar reservoir, but also by black archeology. However, the police are also on the alert, annual raids bring a rich harvest of detained red-handed diggers of all stripes. Every spring, the earth under the blows of waves falls off the coast, exposing the layers of the past millennia, most of them the waves carry with them to the bottom of the reservoir. Local residents of Starokorsunskaya tell how in one spring a huge, completely whole amphora fell from a cliff into the water. Through the eyes of archaeologists

Aslan Tov (Adyghe archaeologist) has been working on the plain part of Adygea and on the banks of the Krasnodar reservoir for more than thirty years. He talks about the fact that in 1999-2003, together with a group of archaeologists from France, he explored burial grounds, ancient settlements, settlements and mounds on the southern shore of the Krasnodar reservoir. Out of the whole range of objects, only twelve settlements belonged to the Maikop culture. Thanks to the equipment brought by the French side, it was possible to find out that this culture is a thousand years older than previously thought. The famous archaeologist A. Leskov brought the leadership of Adygea to the discovered artifacts to show officials what wealth is under their feet. And what? As a result, the French delegation curtailed its work and left. The leader of the expedition, Bertil Lionne, was indignant that he had come here to investigate antiquities, and not to sponsor officials of various levels. Here is such a harsh reality ...

What is at the bottom?

The construction of the Krasnodar reservoir led to the flooding of a vast territory - 420 square kilometers. Twenty villages and villages and even part of Krasnodar were flooded. People were forcibly displaced to new places. Many did not want to move, and this is understandable, because many generations of their families lived on this earth. About fifty cemeteries came under flooding. Most of them were pre-flooded with concrete. So the Krasnodar reservoir caused a lot of curses from the locals.



Construction problem

From the day of its construction, the object has been a source of complaints. In addition to the flooding of settlements, arable land, archaeological sites, burial grounds, etc., it creates a number of serious inconveniences for the region. This is an increase in the level of groundwater, waterlogging of territories, a change in the microclimate and, most importantly, the danger of flooding. After all, this monumental structure is located in a seismically dangerous area; over the past few years, five average earthquakes have been recorded here. According to experts, the dam will be able to withstand shocks of 4-5 points. In this regard, many are interested in the technical condition of the Krasnodar reservoir.

It's time to do something!

The Minister of Defense of the Russian Federation, Sergei Shoigu, also expresses serious concerns about this facility. Indeed, having examined the reservoirs of the Krasnodar Territory, a special commission came to the conclusion that it was necessary to repair this particular dam. During its existence, there has never been a major overhaul, the infrastructure of the facility is in very poor condition. The most alarming among specialists is the five hundred meter length. On it, in a concrete coating, gaps from 20 to 50 centimeters formed. Today, EMERCOM of Russia recognized the Krasnodar reservoir as a dangerous facility and took it under its constant control.



Krasnodar reservoir: rest

Despite the above problems, this water body is a popular holiday destination for many residents not only of the Krasnodar Territory, but also of other regions of Russia. The climate here is hot, steppe. The reservoir and the Kuban River are a kind of boundary: on one side of the steppe, and on the other - plateaus and mountains. In summer, the temperature here reaches a mark of plus 35 degrees Celsius, and in winter it often drops below zero. The resort town of Krasnodar is located on the shore of the reservoir. Tourists can admire the architecture of buildings of the nineteenth century, in addition, there are a lot of interesting monuments, parks, beaches, entertainment centers. Krasnodar is the capital of the Kuban Cossacks, so here literally everything is saturated with the spirit of this culture. Vacationers will be offered delicious Cossack cuisine, show programs with dancing. In the city and on the coast of the reservoir there are many hotels, pensions and recreation centers.

Beaches and attractions of Krasnodar

There are two official beaches in the city. The first of them is called Old Kuban, it is located on Parusnaya street. The beach is fully equipped and equipped, in addition, there is a water park and entertainment center, which are very popular. The second beach is located near the CHP. Thanks to this neighborhood, it has less popularity, despite the fact that it is also equipped and meets all the wishes of vacationers.

Tourists often call Krasnodar "Russian Paris". He deserved such an epithet for its lush greenery, fountains and squares, as well as an abundance of open summer cafes on shady streets and alleys. It is pleasant to walk around this city, admiring the numerous monuments and ancient architecture of its central part. Tourists are advised to visit the Catherine Square (a monument to Catherine the Second is installed here), see the St. Catherine Cathedral, the monument to Aurora, see the Alexander Triumphal Arch, admire the finest work of the openwork Shukhov Tower. Outside the city you can visit mud volcanoes, rocks and gorges, waterfalls and dolmens.

Krasnodar reservoir: fishing

This pond is very popular among fishing enthusiasts. From the shore, mainly they are catching bream, ram, sabrefish, crucian carp, rudd, roach, perch; large bream (it is represented by several age groups), zander, catfish and asp are fishing from the water. In addition, the barbel, gudgeon, chub, silver carp, bleak live in the Krasnodar reservoir.





From the beginning of June to November, at the entrance to the reservoir at dawn, the sound of numerous quacks is heard with which the fishermen hit the water. These are somniks. The depths in the places of their fishing range from four to fifteen meters. Som stands here at the edge of the old channel of the Kuban River, which is now hidden by the thickness of the waters of the artificial sea. The distance between the boats is 50-60 meters. There are practically no beginners among them. Usually they hunt alone, each of them catches catfish here for several years.
