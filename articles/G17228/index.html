Kineshma City: Hotels. Overview, description, number of rooms, reviews

Ivanovo region is considered one of the most beautiful in Russia. It is here, in the Volga regions, that thousands of Russians come to rest every year. The hotel business in this area is very well developed; you are sure to find a suitable guesthouse, hotel or hotel. Today we want to tell you about the small town of Kineshma. The hotels here are inexpensive and very comfortable, today we will talk about them in more detail.



What's interesting here

Kineshma is a city in Russia, a large administrative center of the Kineshma district of the Ivanovo region. A large port on the Volga, so for the first time you can visit it by cruising along the rivers of Russia. The places here are really amazing. Russian hut

But what else can be called a hotel complex in the old Russian city? It has long been famous for the unique beauty of the Volga expanses, beautiful temples and clean streets of Kineshma. Hotels not only emphasize the color of this city, but are also the guardians of the ancient traditions of this land.

Entering from the main entrance, you find yourself in a small museum-teahouse, where you can get acquainted with the life of peasants. You immediately pay attention to the friendliness and courtesy of the waiters and all staff. The hotel itself is small, it consists of only 9 rooms. Of these, four are "standards", 3 euro rooms and 2 two-room suites. Surely you are interested in the cost of rest, which a visitor to a city called Kineshma can count on. There are different hotels here, so we will give the average rates in each of them. A double "standard" per day will cost 1800 rubles, a double room "euro" 2250 rubles per day, and a two-room "suite" 3300 rubles. Extra bed is paid in the amount of 500 rubles.



Entertainment

Kineshma has long been distinguished by warmth and cordiality. Hotels very well convey this national trait of the Russian people, meeting guests very warmly. In addition to standard services (hotel, restaurant, sauna), there is an exciting animation program. Everyone can live a day in the past era. Tourists dress up in national costumes, get acquainted with household items, collect brushwood and help melt the stove, dine according to all the rules of a peasant meal.

Not every hotel can boast of such vivid and informative shows. The city of Kineshma is the place where everyone really feels himself to be a Russian person on his land. Address: Volzhsky Boulevard, 1a. Judging by the reviews, this is the brightest hotel where everyone is welcome. Cozy interior, excellent cuisine, guests become regular the first time.



Hotel "Sadko"

If your trip is not connected with vacation, then animation programs may not interest you. In this case, pay attention to the small but very cozy guest houses, which are quite a lot in the city of Kineshma. Hotel "Sadko" is one of the best representatives of its class. The two-story hotel complex includes everything you need for a good rest. This cafe and bar, a large banquet room and 22 hotel rooms. The whole complex is designed for 35 people. A shopping center and a market, a stadium and a sports complex are 400 meters away. The parking is guarded, which means that your car will be under reliable supervision. The complex is located at ul. Schorsa, 1a.

Number of rooms

There are various options for your attention: suites and junior suites, with and without amenities. All rooms have a TV and fridge, hairdryer and iron. The cost of living is quite low, especially when you consider that breakfast is already included in the price. Single room costs from 600 rubles per day. Double room 1100 rubles per day, triple - 1200 rubles.

If you like comfort and coziness, then choose a two-room suite. The cost is 1000 per person per day. Judging by the reviews, this is a good place to spend the night. It is quiet and comfortable, and the rest is very inexpensive.



SPA Volga

In the center of the city is another wonderful hotel. By the way, if you are traveling with pets, you will be very welcome. Here they practice cohabitation of pets with the owners, there are special places for walking.

SPA-Volga Hotel (Kineshma) offers competitive prices, a night here costs from 450 rubles. For a budget tourist this is a great option, especially when you consider that it is homely here. The hotel is located at Plekhanovsky proezd, 1.

The hotel shares smoking and non-smoking rooms, which is very practical. In the surrounding area there is an excellent garden and terrace, where it is so nice to drink coffee. Reception is open 24 hours a day. Guests are offered free internet access. Since this is a "SPA hotel", you need to take time on this side of the holiday. Judging by the reviews, here you can perfectly go to the sauna and swim in the pool. There is a jacuzzi, sauna, solarium, hammam and massage room.

For entertainment, there is a large TV in the lobby bar, where you can enjoy a refreshing drink. A picnic area and everything you need for a barbecue will allow you to spend the evening just great. And the children's playground will allow not to miss the smallest members of your family.

Reviews say this hotel combines two in one. On the one hand, this is a great option for a budget holiday when you need to spend the night. And on the other hand, you can spend an amazing weekend here, soaking up the spa.

"Central"

As the name implies, the Central Hotel (Kineshma) is located very conveniently, directly in the city center. This is one of the oldest hotels in the city, but there is very little information in tourist guides about it. It is located at ul. Volzhsky Boulevard, 11. This is an excellent option for prudent tourists, since there are always a lot of free rooms of economy class. There are 36 rooms in total, the hotel is designed to accommodate 80 tourists. The rooms have everything you need: furniture, appliances, a bathroom. Judging by the reviews, this is a simple Soviet-style hotel.



Hotel "Peaceful pier"

Wonderful hotel in Kineshma, which is located in the center, very close to the river station, not far from the main attractions of the city. Only one kilometer separates the hotel from the railway and bus stations. This is a special hotel, which is located on the water. The number of rooms includes only 42 rooms, each of which is equipped with a climate control system. Each room has a large LCD TV, refrigerator. All rooms are equipped with necessary toiletries.

The cost of living here is not the lowest - from 1899 rubles per room per night, but the level of comfort is worth it to stop here at least once. A stunning and very elegant restaurant will offer you so many fantastic dishes that it will be very difficult to stay. The restaurant is open around the clock. On Saturdays, there are bright entertainment events.

The spa complex is rightfully considered one of the best. It offers a comfortable Finnish sauna and a Russian bath. To relax after a busy day, you can book a massage. Judging by the reviews, this is not the most affordable, but very comfortable hotel that will appeal to every tourist.



Cultural and entertainment center "Vega"

A stunning and very comfortable hotel that is perfect for families. If you have a triumph, then here you will be helped to organize it at the highest level. Children's birthdays, weddings and anniversaries - everything will be very fun. The hotel will allow you to spend time in comfort, cozy rooms with a double sofa bed, TV, as well as a bathroom and shower will cost you 1,700 rubles.



The banquet hall is waiting for its guests on the most amazing evenings. Live music, theme parties and fun discos await you. And if you want to relax, then welcome to the SPA center. Lovers of warming are waiting for a real birch bathhouse on wood. You can take a steam bath with fragrant birch and oak brooms, use aromatic oils, and then plunge into a large pool.

At your service is a Russian steam room, a whole set of accessories (brooms, hats for a steam room), a heated outdoor pool. A great menu, great beer and snacks are available for you and your guests. A large fitness center and gazebos for relaxation complete the picture, there really is an activity for everyone.

If you are planning a vacation, be sure to visit the ancient city of Kineshma. There are different hotels here, you can choose the option that suits you based on your own capabilities, the goals of your trip, as well as the composition of the company with which you are traveling.
