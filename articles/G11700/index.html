Bad Reichenhall: attractions, hotels, reviews

The spa town of Bad Reichenhall is located in southern Bavaria. In the Middle Ages, salt was actively mined here, and today it is one of the most famous health resorts in Germany. Favorable climate, mineral springs and picturesque natural landscapes - what else is needed for a comfortable stay and effective healing?

The history of the spa town of Bad Reichenhall



According to the assumptions of scientists, once on the site of the modern resort of Bad Reichenhall was the sea. The oldest archaeological finds made in this area date back to 2600-2300 years BC. At that time, in the south of Bavaria there was a city, which was a major trading center.

Mineral springs were discovered in 696 by Saint Rupert. Since then, the city has been developing as a health resort and commercial and industrial center.

Throughout the history of the city, there was an active mining of unique alpine salt. The beneficial properties of this natural resource were almost immediately studied and described by local pharmacists and alchemists. Regular guests of the health resort were royal people, as well as many representatives of wealthy and noble European families.

Since 1990, Bad Reichenhall has been the state resort of Bavaria. Many ancient buildings and real historical monuments have survived to this day. In this area, all conditions are created for tourists who want to relax and improve their health. The resort has modern clinics and hotels of various levels of comfort.

General Resort Information



Any city that has existed for more than 5000 years deserves attention due to its rich history and architectural sights that have survived to this day. So why not combine business with pleasure and spend your vacation in Bad Reichenhall?

In this Bavarian resort you can not only visit interesting excursions, but also improve your health. You can relax in the famous health resort with full comfort year-round. The resort has clinics and motels with various treatment profiles. Tourists without special medical conditions can stay in local hotels and choose spa-procedures or wellness programs.

The resort has a huge number of mineral fountains (more than 50 pieces), near which you can take inhalations naturally. The real local pride is the Gradiver, an open-type inhaler that is 13 meters high. The historical building is considered the largest in the world and is stable today.

Resort Attractions



Holidays in Bad Reichenhall will not be boring, because in this resort and in the vicinity an incredible amount of historical attractions. The life of the city has always been inextricably linked with the salt industry. You can learn more about how salt was mined in these parts by visiting the museum located in the old saltworks - Alte Saline. This is a historical monument of industrial architecture, the building was built in 1834.

If there is enough time, it makes sense to walk around the old upper city. Here you can see the ruins of the ancient walls and the only surviving of the fourteen defensive towers. In the upper city, many architectural monuments of various periods have been preserved.
