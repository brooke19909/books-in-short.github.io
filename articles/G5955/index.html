Children's cotton "Pekhorka": description and reviews

Children's cotton "Pekhorka" is a high-quality thread for knitting products for kids and not only. High quality yarn has earned the love and recognition of the craftswomen for a large shade map and a relatively low price. In addition, it is always nice to support a domestic manufacturer.

Pehor factory

The yarn-making factory has a notable history and dates back to 1905. Just think, she‚Äôs over a hundred years old. Naturally, during this time, product quality has reached the highest level and the threads are not inferior in quality to expensive foreign counterparts.

The factory was built on the outskirts of Moscow, in a village called Zhilino. Its founders are the Baskinna brothers. Initially, the enterprise was created as a cloth factory, but later the owners brought in equipment for the manufacture of yarn. It received its name from the name of the Pekhorka River flowing nearby .





Types of Yarn

Pekhorka produces more than one hundred articles of yarn. Today, the factory works with the following types of raw materials:

  • cotton;
  • viscose;
  • alpaca;
  • wool;
  • acrylic.

Yarn has a large gamut of shades. Manufacturers use only high-quality imported dyes. Children's cotton "Pekhorka" is dyed with raw materials, which undergo many tests and have a natural basis, because dyes should not fade and cause allergies in babies.

Environmentally friendly production

The factory produces section dyeing threads, as well as flossed, fashioned and melange yarn. The manufacturer is fighting for the preservation of the environment - production waste is recycled, and high-quality batting is obtained.

Best for kids

Children's cotton "Pekhorka" is the pride of the factory. For its manufacture using first-class raw materials, which undergoes special processing. Cotton threads are tightly twisted, so that the products are soft and delicate, and the knitting is even and elastic.

Yarn "Pehorka" - children's cotton, which is used to create things for children of all ages. It is especially pleasant to knit things for newborns from it, because the crumb wants to put on something unusually tender, like a cloud. Children's cotton "Pekhorka" is suitable for knitting hats, booties, overalls, sweaters, socks and much more. Many needlewomen choose it for the manufacture of crocheted or knitted toys.

Things created from mercerized cotton well tolerate a large number of washes, do not stretch, and have a pleasant, delicate texture. It should be noted that the Pekhorka cotton for children is more suitable for products of the spring-summer season.
