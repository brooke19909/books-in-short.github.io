Hotel The Residence Family & Fun 4 * (Greece, Rhodes): overview, description and reviews of tourists

Rhodes is an island shrouded in ancient legends and secrets. But besides this, the traveler is waiting for the picturesque rocky cliffs, beautiful beaches and, of course, the gentle sea. There are many hotels on the coast of the island that are ready to receive tourists. And one of the good options is considered the hotel The Residence Family Fun 4 *.

Of course, before booking it is worth asking some important questions. For example, it is worth knowing where exactly the hotel is located and what distance to the beach will need to be overcome? Do they offer food and comfort here? What is the opinion of tourists who have already spent several days here?

Location Features and Territory Information





Location is an important factor when choosing a hotel. Where to look for the resort complex The Residence Family Fun 4 *? Ialyssos, a small tourist town located on the seashore, is where the hotel is located. It is worth saying that its location is quite advantageous, because from here it is easy to get not only to the beach, but also to the main attractions of the island, including the Temple of Apollo, the oceanarium, the archaeological museum and the Rhodes Acropolis. All of them are located about 10-15 minutes drive.

Near the hotel there are small shops, cafes and taverns. By the way, the distance to the airport can be covered in about 20-25 minutes, so you should not be afraid of a long journey.

The Residence Family Fun 4 * hotel itself consists of a large four-story building, built in the shape of the letter "P". In the courtyard there is a pool and a terrace for relaxation. The sight of tourists is pleased with the evergreen, flowering garden, which is always pleasant to stroll through. The hotel area is well maintained, clean and beautiful.

Hotel The Residence Family Fun 4 * (Rhodes, Greece): a description and photos of rooms





The hotel offers tourists check in one of 92 rooms. These are spacious apartments, which consist of a bedroom, a living area (there are upholstered furniture and a table), as well as a kitchen corner.

Of course, the room has the necessary equipment for a comfortable stay. In particular, you can watch TV, use the modern climate control system. There is also a telephone and a safe. In the kitchen corner there is the necessary equipment and utensils for cooking, including a microwave, refrigerator, electric kettle, coffee maker, a small stove.

The bathroom is small, but it has everything you need: toilet, washbasin, shower, large wall mirror. By the way, maids change towels every day and replenish stocks of soap, shampoos and other hygiene items.

Can I count on food?





Of course, when planning a vacation you need to ask about the conditions of food, because this is an important factor. The Residence Family Fun 4 * hotel provides its guests with daily breakfasts, which are held in a local restaurant. According to reviews, the menu is not very varied, but there is still something to choose from - fresh buns, toasts, fried eggs, salads and light snacks, fruits and, of course, drinks.

The rest of the day you can eat in the same restaurant, but each time paying the bill. The hotel has its own bar with a pretty impressive selection of drinks. Also nearby are several good cafes where you can have a delicious lunch. And do not forget that you can cook in your room, and there is a grocery store on the hotel premises.

Convenience and entertainment on the beach





Of course, arriving on the island, travelers expect to spend at least a few days on the beach. What can the hotel offer The Residence Family Fun Hotel 4 *? The hotel is located very close to the sea - you can reach the beach in a few minutes by leisurely walk.

The coast here is covered with sand and small pebbles. According to reviews, the beach is clean, it is cleaned every day. The sea is relatively calm, although sometimes you can observe light waves. On land, one of the rescuers is constantly present.

Since this is a public beach, rent for sun loungers and sun umbrellas must be paid separately (prices are quite reasonable). Nearby is a bar where you can have a bite or refreshment. Of course, on the beach you can rent a boat, ride a boat or water ski, swim with a mask, go windsurfing and other water sports.

Additional service: what should a tourist count on?





Having settled at The Residence Family Fun 4 * hotel, tourists can count on some additional services that make their stay more comfortable. The front desk, as well as room service, is open around the clock. Here you can exchange currency.

Parking is available near the residential building, which is very convenient, because most tourists travel around the island in rented vehicles, which, of course, need to be left somewhere. By the way, rent a bike, bike or car is not difficult. Travelers will certainly appreciate the availability of Internet access throughout the hotel.

Leisure of guests: what to do on the territory of the hotel?





Most guests prefer spending time on the beach or exploring local attractions. In the courtyard, guests relax while lying on comfortable sun loungers. Here you can not only sunbathe, but also refresh yourself in the pool. By the way, for guests there are small water slides, where not only adults, but also kids have fun.

By the way, for children, the hotel has a games room with a variety of toys and attractions, where children of different ages have a great time. A mini club also operates daily - animators and professional educators play with children here.

Adult tourists have fun playing billiards. And at your service is a tour desk, where polite staff will help you find an interesting tour of the island, because the rest does not end outside the Residence Family Fun 4 * hotel. Greece is a country with an amazing history. The sights of this state are really worth a look.

The Residence Family Fun Hotel 4 * Hotel: traveler reviews

Communication with a person who has already visited a particular place can clarify the big picture. The Residence Family Fun 4 * hotel is popular among tourists, because here guests are offered modern comfort and are surrounded by real home comfort.

The rooms are spacious and fully equipped with good appliances. An undoubted advantage is the presence of their own kitchen, which makes it possible to cook on their own. Pleasant is the fact that the rooms are cleaned daily, and the staff here are very hospitable and helpful (basically everyone communicates in English).

It has easy access to the beach. And there are many legendary sights nearby, which is very convenient. Moreover, the price of accommodation is quite reasonable, especially considering the decent conditions. Tourists recommend the hotel for a fun beach and a busy sightseeing vacation.
