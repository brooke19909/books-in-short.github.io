How to get to the hotel?

Tourists can always arrange a transfer to Linda Sea View Hotel 4 *. The guests' reviews regarding it are very mixed, so some still recommend getting to the complex on their own, especially since the path does not differ in complexity. From the airport, Sanya city center can be reached by express bus. In addition, the shuttle bus at number 8 keeps its way through the Sanyavan district and the central part of the city and goes to Dudonghai zone, where our hotel is located. The cost of public transport is low. But a taxi from the airport to the bay will cost 40 CNY.

Those tourists who plan to see the resort need to know some of the nuances of local transport. There are taxis, buses, tourist minibuses and rickshaws. There are many buses in the center. But the most convenient transportation option is still a taxi. It can be called by phone or found near the hotel. But it is worth remembering that local taxi drivers do not speak any foreign languages. Therefore, it is better to ask the receptionist to write on paper the exact name of the place where you plan to go.



But tourist minibuses are good for small companies and families who plan to visit several attractions during the day. You can independently agree on the cost of the trip with the driver. Such an excursion will cost you much cheaper than a standard tour with a guide at the hotel.

Well, the most exotic form of transport is rickshaws and rickshaws. The price of such services is always negotiable.

Linda Sea View Hotel 4 * (Hainan): reviews

Speaking about the hotel, I would definitely like to discuss the reviews of tourists who had a rest here. According to guests, this complex is fully related to budget vacation spots. The hotel is by no means suitable for vacationers who are accustomed to increased comfort and count on an active entertainment program.

But the hotel will appeal to thrifty tourists who are limited in funds or simply do not want to overpay for any additional benefits. The hotel is an acceptable option for those who plan to actively visit the beach and see the most interesting places of the resort.



Hotel apartments are very different. As for the settlement, then, according to the guests, this is a matter of chance. You can get a good number, or you can completely inappropriate. All apartments are different. The best are those that have a view from the windows of the sea. If you don‚Äôt like your room, then you should discuss this issue with the guide, since the reception does not understand Russian at all, and there are also problems with English. Sanya has enough beaches. Each of them is interesting in its own way and has features. Which one to choose for relaxation is up to you.
