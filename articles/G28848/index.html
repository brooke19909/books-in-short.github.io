Biography and thoughts of the richest people in the world - an instruction for those who want to be a billionaire





It is difficult to find a person who does not want to live in abundance. And if some have to work daily in sweat to earn a penny, then others gave fortunes in the form of an inheritance.

1. Wealth without happiness - empty ringing of coins

Unfortunately, most of us are not spoiled of fate, and there is no end in sight to our daily troubles. But many rich people created their first capital thanks to their inventiveness, the proper use of their talents, on which their entire biography was built. And the thoughts of the richest people in the world, as time shows, are united in one thing - to work as much as possible with the mind and not be lazy.

The famous French writer and philosopher Stendhal believed that man does not live on earth in order to become rich, but in order to become happy. To achieve high prosperity does not mean to find peace and the joy of life. Direct proof of this is the biographies of the world's richest people. Many billionaires, drowning in luxury, still remained lonely and miserable.

It was he who expressed the idea that intellectual property has a shelf life of bananas.
