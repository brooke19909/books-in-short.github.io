Stylish set - do-it-yourself hat and snood from knitwear

A snood and a knitted hat are just such things that it takes a lot of time to sew. In an hour, you can quite manage. You can sew a kit for a child or an adult. Patterns are built in the same way, only the fit is in accordance with the measurements taken. In the article you will learn how to sew a hat and a snood out of knitwear and you will understand that it is very simple.





We sew snud: fast cut, fast tailoring

Snood is the same collar, but in a modern interpretation. It is used not only to keep warm, but also to create a specific image. The choice of fabrics is quite large, so it is easy to meet the needs of both children and adults. A knitted set of a hat and a scarf-snood, made with one's own hands, will be doubly pleasing. A product of our own performance is already an occasion to be proud of ourselves.



When the fabric is matched, you can get to work. It is necessary to carve a rectangle with a width of 1.50 m by 50 cm.

All knitted fabrics are elastic, and they stretch well, and arrows can also form on them. When the fabric is made of thick threads, until the seams are processed, it is better not to stretch it around the edges. Otherwise, arrows will appear that will be clearly visible on the product. If the knitwear is thin, then the edges quickly curl into a tube. To make it easier to stitch such parts, they need to be chipped with pins.

Two lines, and snood is ready

Doing hats and snacks from knitwear with your own hands will not take very long. The resulting piece of fabric is folded with the front side inwards along the length, fixed with pins and sewn. The result should be a long pipe with open edges. The product must be turned to the front side.

To grind side seams, it is necessary to fold the snood in half, but in such a way that its second part is inside. When the product is correctly folded, the front side will again be inside.





The edges of the product must be chopped with pins and sewn one centimeter from the edge of the edge. This leaves part of the seam unstitched, about 10 centimeters.





We turn the product through this hole. Now all the seams remained inside the product.





The remaining hole can be closed with a blind seam manually or stitched on a typewriter, then a small scar will remain at the place of the stitch.





We proceed to the next stage: do-it-yourself knit hat and snood will be ready in just a few minutes.

If you can find a tight knit with a large viscous, then you can make a snood in one layer. In this case, you need to make only one small seam.





Sew a hat from knitwear

Many people do not like to wear hats, citing the fact that they are not suitable. In fact, you just need to choose the option that suits you and the color and model. You can also learn how to sew a hat from knitwear with your own hands. The pattern is built simply, no additional knowledge is required even for those who will sew the product for the first time.





It is necessary to measure the girth of the head and figure out how high the hat should be. It can be made dense all over the head, or it can be lengthened in the back of the head and sew some kind of decorative element - a pompom, brush, etc. .. From the measurement, which indicates the girth of the head, we take 2-4 cm. then the product will be very stretched when worn and will not fit tightly on the head. And the desired cap height should be multiplied by two.

The finished part for sewing the cap will be equal to: head circumference multiplied by the double height of the cap. After the pattern, you will get a rectangular piece of fabric, with which we will continue to work.

Quick sewing knitted hats

First of all, stitch the longest seam, and then turn the product up to half so that the wrong side and the seam remain inside.

A hat and a snood from knitwear with their own hands are sewn at the beginning at the same time, only the completion of the work is different. In this case, the product remains turned inside out. It is necessary to lay out the fabric in such a way that the seam is in the middle, flatten and stitch all 4 layers along the edge (on the crown) together with one line.

Now, to the seam, which is located in the center, you need to turn the fabric on both sides and once again line all the layers of fabric together, now there are 8. Now the edge (8 layers) can be processed immediately in one fell swoop and turned out. The knit hat is ready.

Other hat options from the same pattern

There are still options for how you can beat the same hat. Until the moment when the cap is inverted to half, everything is done the same way. But in this case, you need to process the edges in a circle before closing the slice. Then take all the slices in your hand, tie a tight thread around the edge (as a bag is tied) and turn inside out. The node will remain inside.

You can make the same knot on the front side, but then tie it not with a thread, but with a beautiful lace (leather or fabric). If you spend a little time, then a hat and snood from knitwear with your own hands will diversify your wardrobe.

From dense knitwear you can sew a hat in one layer. On the sides and at the top, one line is laid, and the product is ready. The work is all nothing, but the look is so original!





You can also sew a hat from knitwear with a lapel, then when constructing a pattern, you need to add double width to the lapel to the length of the fabric. The hat in the finished form will be long, but when it turns up, everything will fall into place, and it will just fit. On the lapel you can sew a label.
