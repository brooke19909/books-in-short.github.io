Hit of the season: satin ribbon hair clips

Fashion is a fickle lady, she changes day by day. That is why individuality plays a big role, and how exactly you will achieve it is not so important! Some resort to the help of specialists, others try to prove themselves. This article will be interesting for both. Speech today will be about hairpins made of satin ribbons. Of course, you can buy them, but we remember that the main thing is a highlight!



Such jewelry goes well with a variety of hairstyles. Hairpins made of satin ribbons will organically fit into the everyday look and will be a great addition to the festive styling.

Working with satin ribbons is a painstaking work that requires perseverance and imagination, but the result is amazing. In addition, we can always make an accessory that is perfect for the chosen outfit.

To begin, let's figure out how to make bows from satin ribbons with your own hands. Here is all we need:

- hairpins bought at the store;

- lace;

- a narrow tape of a suitable shade;

- needle with thread;

- lighter;

- decorative elements: beads, sequins, rhinestones, etc .;

- Moment-Crystal glue or any other that does not leave traces;

- 30 minutes of free time and, of course, inspiration.



I think from the master class presented below it will become clear how to make wonderful hair clips from satin ribbons.

This accessory is perfect for older girls. For little fashionistas, you can make bow hairpins from rep ribbons. We will use the same materials (with the exception of ribbons). For this decoration we need rep ribbons of different widths and shades.



For the main bow, take 2 pieces of tape: their width should be approximately 2.5 cm and length 14 cm. Note that the rep ribbon is very loose, therefore, before starting to make a bow, walk along the edges of the lighter.

Then we glue our melted billets into rings, wait for the glue to completely dry and tighten with a thread their middle. Next, we collect one large bow from two blanks. To do this, glue them together, and for strength we fasten with a thread.

Now let's make the second bow: we fold the ribbon 1.5 cm wide and about 6 cm long in half so that its ends converge in the middle. In the center, we sew and tighten our workpiece, forming a neat bow out of it. We will cover the seams with a narrow ribbon - we will wrap our bow, and we will sew its ends from the wrong side.

While our large bow dries, we will decorate the hairpin with a ribbon in tone.

Now we have the components of a fashion accessory ready. All that remains for us is to connect our workpieces. We glue the bows on top of one another, and then we fix all this beauty on a hairpin and wait for it to dry completely. Now you can safely give jewelry to your princess!

Hairpins made of satin ribbons - a simple and, no doubt, a good way to emphasize individuality.

We hope that our article will help not only mothers who try to instill a sense of style for their crumbs and give a piece of the holiday on a boring gray day, but also girls who are in search of a suitable style and a worthy addition to their image!
