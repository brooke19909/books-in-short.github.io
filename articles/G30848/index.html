Explay X5 - a modern affordable smartphone

Smartphones quickly and firmly entered the lives of ordinary mortals, having gained wild popularity due to wide functionality. The latest development - Explay X5 2014 release - is a modern communicator that fully meets the needs of any user. The Android 4.2 classic operating system for smartphones with an average price policy, combined with fairly high technical indicators, makes this model reliable, productive, and multifunctional.

Specifications



Explay X5 battery capacity of 2000 mAh allows you to work with your smartphone for more than a day without recharging. But, as with any communicator, it is not recommended to allow the battery to completely discharge to zero. The size of the device provides comfortable work with the display. Dimensions of the model - 74 x 138.69 x 10.4, weight - only 156 grams. A powerful processor for this class and RAM expand the capabilities of the smartphone to a level that several years ago seemed generally fantastic and impossible - today this tale is available to everyone. Built-in memory - 4 GB, Bluetooth 3.0 and Wi-Fi, which have become the standard for all smartphones, meet all the requirements of both an experienced user and a novice, and if you wish, you can increase the memory with an additional card - this is useful, in particular, for fans large collections of music that are always at hand.

Opportunities

Smartphone Explay X5 has a convenient function - support for 2 SIM-cards. In the modern world, this is a necessity that often saves time, nerves and money in accounts. Equipped with a flash and autofocus, an 8-megapixel camera guarantees decent quality pictures that can compete with simple digital soap dishes. Explay X5 also has a front sensor to simplify the creation of selfies, which are now in fashion among young people. True, this device is worse than the main one, and there is no flash in it. The headphone output is standard - 3.5, and picking them to taste is not difficult. There are also functions for listening to the FM frequencies of the radio and the light sensor, which simplifies the work with the smartphone in an environment with unstable lighting. The microUSB connector is used to connect the Explay X5 to other equipment (such as a PC) and to a charger.

Pleasant trifles



The kit comes with 3 color panels, which are selected by the user according to their mood. Design themes meet the needs of a simple and experienced user, but if you want, of course, it will not be difficult to download a convenient style for yourself. Adequate price Explay X5 (from 7 thousand rubles) is an undoubted advantage of this model of smartphone.
