Green areas giving peace

Around the casino there are wonderful French gardens with exotic plants, and from the side of the turquoise sea there are sun-drenched terraces. Monte Carlo, whose attractions have made the city look unforgettable, is famous for its beautiful green areas - great places for finding harmony.

The Japanese Garden became the real work of landscape architecture , created so that residents and visitors of the city could fully enjoy the solitude with nature, get rid of problems and be in silence. The picturesque area was laid out in memory of Grace Kelly at the behest of her husband, Prince Rainier III. Everything in this breathing calm place is subordinated to the philosophy of Zen, leading a person to enlightenment. The murmuring waterfalls, transparent ponds with fish, magnificent rock gardens, small houses seem to be saturated with Japanese symbolism.





Royal Palace

After visiting the casino and finding peace in green areas, tourists go on to get acquainted with a small Monte Carlo. All the sights of the city, of course, cannot be mentioned in one article, but it is impossible to pass by the grandiose structure that originally served as a defensive fortress.

The current residence of the princely family, built on a 60-meter cliff, was home to Prince Rainier III of the Grimaldi dynasty and the beautiful Grace Kelly, who became the princess of Monaco. The royal palace, surprisingly modest in appearance, is partially open to visitors. A museum is located in one wing of the residence, and rulers of the state have lived in the other for over 700 years. Tourists admire the rich interiors, covered with gilding, paintings of famous masters, luxurious carpets, bright frescoes.





In addition, travelers gather in the square in front of the palace, where a ceremonial change of the guard is held. The regularly held ceremony remains for a long time in the memory of those who arrived in search of new experiences in the fabulous Monte Carlo. Sights, photos of which can be found in every guide to Monaco, captivate with elegance and sophistication.

Oceanographic Museum

The capital of Monaco is called the treasury of ancient architectural objects that have a certain value for society. Monte Carlo, whose attractions and cultural objects are of interest to tourists, is famous not only for casinos and comfortable hotels, but also for museums with unique exhibits. All guests of the city consider it their duty to visit a true paradise for lovers of the underwater world.

The Oceanographic Museum, founded by Prince Albert I, is visited annually by about one million people. An architectural masterpiece that has grown in the rock rises above the city, and tourists traveling by ship, from afar see a snow-white building with a huge collection of underwater inhabitants. All visitors are delighted with the real submarine at the entrance, on which the team of the famous traveler Cousteau explored the ocean.
