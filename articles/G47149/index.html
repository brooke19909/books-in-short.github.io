Troubleshooting methods

One way or another, during the operation of the (narrow) Hotpoint Ariston RSM 601 W washing machine, various kinds of malfunctions can occur. Not always they are associated with a literal breakdown. The vast majority of them can be easily fixed. The main malfunctions and, accordingly, some methods for their elimination are described below:

  • The device does not turn on:
    • blackout in the house;
    • the machine is not plugged in.
  • There is a continuous set and discharge of water:
    • the drain hose is not at the correct height;
    • the edge of the drain hose is placed in the water;
    • Incorrect debugging of the wall drain hole.
  • A leak:
    • incorrectly fixed water hose;
    • incorrectly fixed drain hose;
    • detergent dispenser too clogged.
  • Excessive foam formation:
    • too much washing powder in the dispenser;
    • the powder is not suitable for washing with a washing machine.
  • Failure of drain and spin functions:
    • clogged drain water;
    • bend of a drain hose;
    • under the selected mode, drain or spin is not provided.
  • Spin vibration too strong:
    • uneven installation of the device;
    • the machine is sandwiched between furniture or walls.
  • There is no water intake:
    • the hose is bent;
    • the tap is closed (water);
    • lack of water in the house;
    • low pressure in the water supply;
    • the start button is not pressed;
    • lack of connection of a water hose to the crane.
  • Anomaly code on display:
    • turn off the device, wait a minute and turn it on again.
  • The wash cycle does not start:
    • the start button was not pressed;
    • tightly closed manhole cover;
    • delayed start programming;
    • water tap shut off.

Having carefully understood the essence of the problem, you can easily fix it. The main thing is to pay attention to what the instruction on its use says about the Hotpoint Ariston RSM 601 W washing machine. This will help extend the life of the device.
