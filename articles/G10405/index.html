Hotel 5 * Xperience Sea Breeze Resort, Sharm El Sheikh: reviews, photos

Sharm el Sheikh is located along the picturesque bays of the Red Sea, rich in bright corals with many inhabitants of all colors of the rainbow. This is a holiday city, a city of entertainment, a city of rest, than it resembles European Mediterranean resorts, but with a romantic flair of Arabian tales and short stories from The Thousand and One Nights.

And now we will discuss in detail the hotel 5 * Xperience Sea Breeze Resort. Ready? Then we begin!

Sea breeze

In Sharm el-Sheikh there are many hotels for every taste, and among them there are not even two identical ones. 5 * Xperience Sea Breeze Resort Hotel (5 stars) was opened in 2011 on the Red Sea. Everyone can climb the roof of the hotel and contemplate the peaceful azure expanse.



Tiran Restaurant's terrace offers wonderful views of Tiran Island. You can enjoy it for days sitting in the comfortable armchairs of the restaurant. You should definitely go on a one-day excursion to the island, which is organized by the hotel itself.

It has everything to fully enjoy diving and snorkeling. Around the island there are four coral reefs with many colorful colorful inhabitants. The excursion is superbly organized. A comfortable yacht will deliver guests to the island. You can rent diving equipment and discover the uncharted beauty of the Red Sea.

Hotel Bliss

It is so nice to sybarit on the beach with a cocktail while taking sun baths. Five hotel pools allow you to enjoy swimming and relaxation in comfortable sun loungers when the sea is low tide.

The hotel staff will do everything so that the rest is filled with pleasant moments. Children will find something to their liking in the club and in the pools, where funny animators will not let them miss you for a minute. The Egyptians are very fond of children, and the kids here are always fun and interesting.

Adults will be pleased to relax in the sauna or hot tub, go through several sessions of relaxing massage.

Massage at the hotel is done by very good specialists. Their work both relaxes and tones at the same time. After the massage, visitors are sure to recommend relaxing and enjoying a cup of freshly brewed aromatic tea.



Also in the hotel you can plunge into the amazing world of the Red Sea. Along the hotel beach is a coral bay, where you can swim with scuba diving or snorkelling. Impressions are indescribable - you get the feeling that you are in the stunning beauty of a tropical aquarium.
The hotel, despite its privacy and intimacy, is located only 5 kilometers from Sharm el-Sheikh Airport.

Gastronomic symphony of Xperience Sea Breeze

The hotel restaurant offers an abundance of gourmet dishes. City restaurants do not compare with the gastronomic symphony of appetizers, main dishes and desserts that the hotel chefs offer.

Here, delicious aromatic vegetables and fruits, spicy herbs are served in abundance. The guests of the restaurant like fula, bean dishes, tender mutton, grilled, stewed with vegetables, pilaf or beshbarmak, tazhin, kebab kebab and much more.



The hammam-maxi, the pigeon stuffed with rice and spices, cooked on charcoal, and the fish fried with tomatoes, paprika and spices will not leave indifferent guests.

Nearby Institutions

To feel the oriental flavor, you can go on an evening shopping to the Old Market, take a walk through the Tiran shopping centers, Il Mercato, and visit Soho Square. Here you can buy t-shirts, towels made from natural Egyptian cotton, fairly high-quality fakes of different brands: sunglasses, polo shirts, bags, sneakers, wallets and suitcases. The most important thing is to remember that this is shopping in an oriental way, you must bargain, which will reduce the initial price by a third, or even half.

On Soho Square, you can go to the ice rink and try different sorts of ice cream and soft drinks in the ice bar.

But despite all the charm of walking around the city, I still want to return to a clean, comfortable hotel to fully experience the evening relaxation to the romantic solo electric guitar and entertainment shows at the hotel.



It is better to start the morning at Xperience Sea Breeze Resort 5 * (Sharm El Sheikh) with a swim towards the Shark Bay and contemplate the colorful flickering of the inhabitants of the coral reef. After breakfast, it's time for active games. Animators will offer something for everyone who wants to not only enjoy the sun and the sea.

Hotel Tours

They go to Sinai just over the sea, for amazing nature, for corals, which are abundant here. The Xperience Sea Breeze Resort 5 * hotel, tours in which will please every tourist, offers a varied vacation and sightseeing trips for every taste: to Ras Mohammed and to the Pharaoh island, to the Nabq National Park and to Giza, Luxor, Cairo.

All excursions, wherever they are conducted, are always thought out to the smallest detail and give only pleasure to tourists who have come to rest.

In the Ras Muhammad National Park, on the beaches you can watch white storks and slender gazelles, wander along the sand dunes and see the mountains that the wind created from petrified sand, swim with the fish and enjoy a coral multi-color fairy tale.

On the rocky island of Pharaoh in the Gulf of Aqaba, you can touch the frozen history. The ancient outpost of the Crusaders, built in the 12th century, has been preserved in its original form. And around the island there is a beautiful coral reef with many fish and colored algae.



Nabq National Park, which is located halfway between the two resorts, Dahab and Sharm El Sheikh, is famous for its mangroves, exotic plants and rich in wildlife, salt marshes and sand dunes.
The hotel also offers tours abroad of Egypt, the most popular are trips to Jerusalem and Petra.

Cozy bliss rooms

The hotel rooms are cleaned every day and are pleased with cleanliness and freshness. Elegant room decoration allows you to forget about the hustle and bustle of everyday life and relax in the hot afternoon.

Thanks to the perfectly thought-out concept of the hotel, Xperience Sea Breeze Resort 5 * (Sharm El Sheikh) is known far beyond Egypt. At this place you can meet guests from all over Europe. Many visitors return to this place again and again to spend an unforgettable vacation full of impressions and pleasant events, which indicates its high reputation.

Every year the 5 * Xperience Sea Breeze Resort hotel in Sharm El Sheikh becomes more comfortable and more beautiful and magnetically attracts guests who want to taste all the pleasures of a holiday on the Red Sea.
