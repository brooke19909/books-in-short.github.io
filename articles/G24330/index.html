Hotel ClubHotel Riu Tequila 5 * in Mexico: review, description and reviews

ClubHotel Riu Tequila 5 * - a five-star complex located on the Yucatan Peninsula in Mexico, has earned extremely good reviews from Russian tourists. Almost all guests who have visited the hotel recommend it to other guests. This beach complex, in their opinion, is good for a romantic getaway, and for staying with children, and for excursions. Let us consider in more detail what are the bonuses of this hotel for Russian travelers and why does it attract them so much. The hotel belongs to the famous Rio Hotels and Resorts line.



Playa del carmen

Once it was the most common Mexican fishing village 70 kilometers from Cancun. Now she was chosen by lovers of the sun and the sea. And all thanks to the white, similar to flour, not only in color, but also in the structure of the sand. No wonder the ClubHotel Riu Tequila 5 * (Mexico, Playa del Carmen) is visited by tourists eager for a beach holiday, as well as fans of beautiful nature, national parks and clear water. This resort is located in the middle of the famous coastal strip of the Riviera Maya. Its main street is the so-called Fifth Avenue, where all kinds of shops and souvenir shops are located, as well as restaurants, clubs and bars. And near the pier there is another favorite place for tourists - Plaza Mayor. There you can see the Indian ritual dance, which in ancient times appealed to the fertility gods. It is performed on a high pillar. But keep in mind that alcohol cannot be drunk on the street here.

Location

ClubHotel Riu Tequila 5 * hotel is located eight hundred meters from the beach. It is surrounded by a palm grove. The resort town of Playa del Carmen, to which she administratively belongs, is a little more than three kilometers from her. Therefore, getting to it is easy. Next to this hotel are four other hotels of the same chain, they stand right on the beach. Although the hotel is not too far from the airport, the transfer takes a little over an hour. In Playa del Carmen, if you walk along the sea, you can get in any half an hour.



Territory

ClubHotel Riu Tequila 5 * hotel actually stands in a beautiful palm grove. On its territory there is parking for guests who arrived with their own or rented cars. So that hotel guests do not feel the urgent need to go beyond, there are a number of shops right in the lobby. The architecture of the hotel is very colorful and expressive, reminiscent of Native American and Mexican villages with elements of Mayan and Aztec temples. The lobby is especially gorgeous. And the infrastructure itself was created like a real town. Moreover, there is a church, a square, and hacienda. Everything is very beautiful and comfortable. On the territory live small local animals - giant guinea pigs and raccoon noses, which are constantly begging. In short, the real jungle. Two residential buildings are located around the pool, and in the center is a restaurant.



Number of rooms

ClubHotel Riu Tequila 5 * hotel is quite large. It has 664 rooms. These are mainly rooms for two or three guests of a standard type, as well as junior suites. The rooms have almost everything you need for a good stay - satellite TV, a safe, a stocked minibar, comfortable wide beds, an ironing board with an iron, and many towels of different sizes. The bathroom has a hairdryer. The rooms are air-conditioned, but if such a method of cooling the air causes problems for someone, there is a traditional fan on the ceiling. They clean the rooms every day and make a variety of figures from towels. The rooms themselves are decorated very colorful, in the Mexican style, and in the minibar the bottles with alcohol are turned upside down, each of them has a special tap for dosing. They give local rum, white and golden tequila, as well as vodka, constantly replenish the contents. When checking in, they put on bracelets, so you can walk around the territory, eat and swim in the pools. But the room is provided only from three in the afternoon, this is the Mexican hotel system. Outlets at the hotel are Mexican. But in the bathroom there is also an ordinary plug, so you can do without an adapter.

Service and entertainment

The ClubHotel Riu Tequila 5 * hotel has five conference rooms of different capacities for seminars and business meetings. Reception and reception are open 24 hours a day. ClubHotel Riu Tequila: Beach & Pools

The hotel is on the second line from the sea, across the road. He shares a beach with another hotel - Rio Yucatan. You can get there on a small electric car with six seats, or you can walk on a beautiful alley or on the territory of other hotels. The beach is huge, the color of the water is amazing - blue and turquoise. The sand is coral, snow-white, almost always cool. There are a lot of sunbeds on the beach, nobody takes them in advance. There are no umbrellas; vacationers lie in the shade of palm trees. But you can‚Äôt come to the coast around the clock. The beach is closed from eleven in the evening until seven in the morning. Often there are waves. The entrance is very gentle. Beach towels are changed once a day, for free. You can dine at the restaurants of the neighboring hotel. The hotel has two swimming pools, one of which is designed for children. The second, for adults, is divided into three parts. One for free swimming, the second for animation, where they do water polo and aqua aerobics, and the third with a jacuzzi. It is there that one of the bars is surrounded by water, you can swim there, and you will be poured a cocktail, alcohol or a soft drink, as you wish.



Excursions

Organized sightseeing trips from ClubHotel Riu Tequila 5 * in Mexico are very expensive, around $ 130 per person. And the difference between excursions from tour operators or local street bureaus is not too big. But if you decide to explore the surroundings, it is best to start with boat trips to the local lagoons and bays. This tour lasts seven to eight hours and includes lunch. And you can choose an evening cruise to Cancun and to the island of Mujeres, where you will find a night disco. Not far from Playa are the mysterious Mayan cities - Chichen Itza and Tulum - with their temples, bas-reliefs and step pyramids. A trip to Shkaret National Park, where you can swim with dolphins, travel along the underground river and explore the amazing caves where butterflies live, will leave you an unforgettable experience.
