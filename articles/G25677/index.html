PGS Hotels Casadel Sol 4. Description, reviews

Description: PGS Hotels Casadel Sol 4 is located next to the white beaches of Kata, which stretch along the western coast of Phuket, 20 km from the island's capital and an hour from the airport. Around there are many bars, shops and nightclubs.



The resort complex, built in a typical Thai style, invites romantic couples, tourists with children, youth companies.

Rooms: PGS Hotels Casadel Sol 4 has 181 rooms, most of which belong to the deluxe and superior categories. In addition, there are family rooms, apartments with a jacuzzi and access to a private pool.

All rooms have balconies and superb views of the landscaped green area with pool. The rooms have air conditioning, electronic safe and kettle. This family room has a refrigerator and kitchenette. Beach towels and umbrellas are provided.

The Pool Access ground floor apartment has a pool that can be accessed directly from the room.

Meals: Guests at PGS Hotels Casadel Sol 4 will enjoy an all-inclusive service with buffet breakfast, dinner and √† la carte lunch.

The traditional Thai restaurant serves classic and gourmet national dishes and modern international dishes. Free of charge throughout the day offer coffee, water, alcohol of local producers. Freshly squeezed juices, imported bottled wines and packaged ice cream are additionally charged.

The cozy lounge bar is a popular place to chat with friends over an exotic cocktail. Relaxing by the pool is doubly pleasant when there is a bar nearby with soft drinks, ice cream, fruits and sandwiches.

Beach: Relaxation is offered on a city sandy beach, a stone's throw from the hotel. Sunbeds and awnings are available for a fee.



Additional Information: PGS Casadel Sol provides guests with comfortable accommodation and a wide range of leisure activities. Domestic services and a taxi call are offered, there is a medical office and on-site parking, wireless Internet and massage services.

Children's service includes a special menu and children's chairs, cribs and strollers, playgrounds and a shallow pool. It is possible to leave the child under the supervision of a nanny.

The choice of entertainment will suit any tourist. These are darts and billiards, origami and Thai cuisine classes , gala dinners and karaoke evenings, movies and board games. On the territory there are three pools and water slides.

Digest: One of the main advantages of the hotel is its proximity to the sea. The snow-white beach with fine sand impresses with its beauty and cleanliness.

Five minutes walk to the beach on the way you can find many shops, bars and cafes. Phuket's nightlife is just a 20-30 minute drive away.

PGS Hotels Casadel Sol 4 has its own landscaped area and several decorative ponds with a bar directly in the water. Rooms are cleaned and towels are changed daily. The animators and staff at the reception work great.

The food is incredibly plentiful and delicious. Both traditional European dishes and local exotic dishes are offered. The bars have a large selection of tropical cocktails.

You can safely go to Casadel Sol with children for whom interesting animation is organized, a swimming pool with non-slip descent is equipped, a special menu is offered.
