Nokia 8910i: how to distinguish a fake

Pay attention to the housing. The fact is that the Chinese prefer not to create new parts, but use old models. A titanium case is not used in fakes, so it will seem different to the touch. The original part is matte and rough, painted according to a special technology, the nuances of which are classified.

Due to the fact that the case is not made correctly, the fake device feels like plastic. The paint is applied in a thick layer and it is easy to notice that its color and texture is very different from the original version. There is no feeling that there are metal parts in the Nokia 8910i.

The original comes with gray letters on the keyboard. If the purchased device has white and convex keys, then you can be sure - the front panel is fake.

Sometimes consumers claim that a non-original phone is one that has an additional alphabet in addition to Latin letters on the keyboard. However, this nuance cannot be considered an indicator of fake.
