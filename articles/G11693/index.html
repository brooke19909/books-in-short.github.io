Geographical position

Cyprus on the world map is a small part of the land located in the northeast of the Mediterranean Sea. The island is located at the junction of the sea routes of Syria, Egypt and Turkey. The closest to Cyprus is Turkey - 70 km, from Egypt to Cyprus - 350 km, to Syria - 103 km. With its view on the map, the island resembles a lizard.

Territorial division of the island

97.3% of the territory of the island of Cyprus belongs to the Republic of Cyprus, 2.7% - a British exclave, the territory of military bases. In fact, the situation is different. 36% of the territory after the invasion of Turkish troops in 1974 began to belong to the self-proclaimed TRNC (Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus). The legitimate authorities own 59%, 2.3% - the demarcation zone, which divides the island and is protected by the UN Armed Forces. The population of the Republic of Cyprus is 866,000. The main language is Greek. In addition, English and Turkish are used.

Phantom State

Northern Cyprus (TRNC) is a state recognized only by Turkey. It occupies the third part of the island of Cyprus. For the world community and the EU, Northern Cyprus is an ethno-political problem that needs to be addressed immediately.



Northern Cyprus is an absolutely safe, tourist destination. TRNC is an expensive resort and designed for organized tourists. Unique ancient monuments of the time of the Crusaders, magnificent landscapes, mild climate - all this attracts tourists from all over the world. It is best to visit Northern Cyprus in the spring - at this time the valleys of the island bloom with emerald green grass, numerous plants bloom in the mountains. There are few tourists at this time, and there is an opportunity to enjoy the beauty of waking nature.

Cyprus Resorts

This fabulous island has many great resorts. All of them have their own characteristics and are designed for different groups of vacationers. Today we will tell you about them, and you will understand which one is most suitable for you.

Ayia Napa

If you prefer to visit nightclubs, discos during your holidays, then this place is right for you. Its daytime appearance is misleading. At first it may seem that this is a quiet and calm town, lost between plantations of fruits and vegetables. With dusk, the city literally lights up with neon. Nightclubs and discos, restaurants and pubs are full of youth. And in the morning, all vacationers will again enjoy the silence and magnificent white sand beaches.

You should not think that a holiday in Cyprus, the photo of which you can see in our article, is just aimless lying on the beach. Everywhere there are conditions for playing sports.



Protaras

Golden sandy beaches surrounded by cliffs that frame small bays. Here you can admire the very picturesque sunrises and sunsets. This resort will appeal to lovers and connoisseurs of nature, which did not touch the hand of man. Here romantics and happy owners of large families will feel comfortable.

Protaras is a fairly young resort located near Ayia Napa. Here, visitors will find a clear sea. Your children will quickly find friends on the beach. The hotel provides early evening meals for younger guests and babysitting services. In a word, this is a resort for families.

Larnaca

If you prefer to live in a small town, you like to walk along the promenades and visit the many shops, we recommend that you go to Larnaca. A small port town with a population of 40 thousand people is very cheerful and cozy. In ancient times, it was the largest port in the country, and today it is a haven for magnificent yachts. Well-groomed, beautiful promenade with spreading palm trees, numerous bars and restaurants, as well as fishing taverns - a great place for walking.

Larnaca was built on the site of the ancient kingdom of Kitium, founded by the grandson of the legendary Noah - Kittim. The name of Larnaca can be translated as "the city of St. Lazarus." Lazarus resurrected by Christ came here and became the very first bishop of this island.





Limassol

The beaches in Cyprus are perhaps the best in this resort. Active people will like to relax here - they love numerous trips and excursions around the island, a variety of nightly entertainment. This is a port and a major tourist center.

The population of the city is 135 thousand people. Without a doubt, the most visited resort of the island of Cyprus. Rest, hotels, food system - everything here is at the highest level. Limassol - the wine center of Cyprus. Every year you can admire fun carnivals, take part in wine festivals, where the best specialists in this field treat everyone for a week with their products.

Pathos

This resort is for those who value antiquity, museum exhibits, ancient legends and ancient monuments most of all. Holidays in Paphos combines the historical area and the amenities of a modern resort. In summer, this resort is the coolest place on the island. A relict pine forest has been preserved here. According to the Cypriots, walks in it help with various diseases and prolong life.

Cyprus in August

Summer has exceeded its half. The hottest month is just around the corner. Many people, naturally, are interested in the question, what is Cyprus in August? In the last month of summer, the weather in Cyprus is almost no different from July - it is still hot and dry. Daytime temperatures reach +40 degrees, and sometimes higher, at night a little more than +20. More comfortable weather in the mountains. It is not worth waiting for rains in Cyprus in August - this is a rather rare occurrence. And there are practically no cloudy days. However, due to the abundance of greenery and the sea breeze, the heat is well tolerated by the bulk of vacationers.

Do not go to the beach from 12 to 16 hours. This time is best spent at the indoor pool. In August, the water in the sea warms to such an extent that it does not cool even at night, so vacationers bathe at any time of the day.

These are not the most favorable weather conditions do not prevent August from becoming the height of the holiday season in Cyprus. Vacationers from all over the world fill the hotels and beaches of the island. But parents planning a vacation with their children should choose May or September. The fact is that kids are much harder than adults to withstand extreme heat.

Cyprus Attractions

Holidays in Cyprus, photos of the most picturesque places of which you can see in our article, in addition to a wonderful holiday on well-maintained beaches, it can be very informative. The island of Cyprus has a long and interesting history, so there are many unique historical and architectural monuments.

The tombs of the kings are underground burials of enormous size - several hundred meters. They are a copy of the houses in which these kings once lived. The most interesting and valuable things are collected in the dungeon - these are crosses, household utensils, drawings. To our great regret, today only two tombs have remained unplanned.

Archbishop's palace

Cypriots are sure that the most beautiful building on the island is this magnificent palace, built in 1955-1960. There are several museums on the territory - Folk Art, Byzantine, Art Gallery.

Kolossi Castle

It is located ten kilometers west of Limassol. It was built from yellow limestone at the very beginning of the thirteenth century, by order of the King of Jerusalem. This is a unique monument of military architecture. It was rebuilt in the middle of the fifteenth century, and since then its appearance has not changed.

Aphrodite's Bath

Located near Paphos. According to ancient legend, Aphrodite had fun with Dionysus here. This monument can only be reached on foot. According to legend, the water in the bathhouse gives eternal youth.

Amathus

This is an antique policy, whose history goes back more than a thousand years. He is a silent witness to the history of the island. Surely this place will interest fans of Greek mythology.

Eroskipos

This ancient village is located near Paphos. Translated from Greek, the name can be translated as "sacred garden." According to legend, it was here that the gardens of Aphrodite bloomed.





Archaeological Museum

The oldest museum on the island, located in Nicosia. Its expositions occupy fourteen halls. To get acquainted with them even fluently, it will take a lot of time.
