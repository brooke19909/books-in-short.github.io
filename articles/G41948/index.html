The village of Vardane (Sochi): attractions, infrastructure, housing

With the onset of the swimming season, a huge number of tourists from Russia flock to the south of our wonderful country, namely to the numerous resorts of Greater Sochi. Tropical Mediterranean climate, wonderful nature, clean gentle Black Sea, picturesque mountains, architectural and natural monuments - there is everything you need for a real good rest.

Rest in the village of Vardan

Sochi is the summer capital of Russia. This glamorous noisy resort, unfortunately, can not afford everyone. But the numerous villages around Sochi are in no way inferior to the famous city.





In the Lazarevsky district of Greater Sochi in the valley of the Buu mountain river on the Black Sea coast, there is a cozy village of Vardane. This fairly developed and modern resort offers excellent conditions for relaxation.

The village of Vardane (Sochi) is perfect for tourists who prefer to spend their holidays in a quiet place, in good conditions, at an affordable price, enjoying the natural beauty and silence.

How to get there

The village of Vardane is located thirty kilometers from the city of Sochi and about thirty-five kilometers from the regional center - the village of Lazarevsky.

Intercity trains do not stop in the village, the nearest Loo station is five kilometers away. From there, it is not difficult to get to Vardane, very often homeowners offer a free shuttle service from Loo Station.





Electric trains run regularly from Sochi to Vardan, the journey will take about an hour.

There is no direct train from the Adler airport to the village, so you will first need to get to the Adler railway station, and then by train to the village itself.

Where to stay?

The main type of housing in the village of Vardan is private hotels, pensions and guest houses. Locals tried their best, so tourists have something to choose from. The competition among homeowners is very great, so everyone is trying to create ideal conditions and provide vacationers with optimal service. The village of Vardane (Sochi) is famous for its genuine Caucasian hospitality and good naturedness of local residents.





Guest houses in the village usually have several floors and nice architecture. All pensions have their own territory, parking for a small number of cars. In the courtyard, as a rule, there is a barbecue area, a children's playroom, and gazebos for relaxing.

The rooms are usually standard and economy. They differ in the availability of amenities in the room. Tourists are offered a standard set of furniture, a TV, a refrigerator, air conditioning or a fan.

Be sure that each guest house has a kitchen for self-catering. Some guest houses have a food ordering service and small cafes on site.

Infrastructure of the village

The resort infrastructure of the village is quite developed. There is a small promenade with cafes and bars. It is believed that there is not a lot of entertainment here, compared to the larger cities of the Krasnodar Territory, but no one is bored.

Restaurants near the sea, small tents with different snacks, the air is saturated with the aroma of barbecue. Numerous souvenir shops delight tourists with products of local craftsmen.





The beach provides water activities: slides, bananas and boat trips on boats.

Fans of excursions also did not go unnoticed and numerous excursion bureaus offer their services.

In general, rest in Vardan (Sochi) will appeal to lovers of a quiet budget vacation, peace and solitude with nature, whose beauty in the village is simply indescribable.
