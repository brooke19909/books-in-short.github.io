Alushta in history

Alushta is a Ukrainian city full of its remarkable places and memorable events. Its history began with the emperor Justinian, who built a fortification called Aluston, which is reminiscent of the three Greek towers that have survived to this day, despite the destruction and inexorable time: Alushta went through several wars, the Ottoman conquest and occupation by the Germans.

By the way, it was here that they shot the good old film about Shurik's adventures with the participation of Natalia Varley, the film "Three Plus Two" and many other films. Climate Alushta

In summer, Alushta is a true subtropic with a temperature of about 25 degrees Celsius. Winter is mild and the temperature rarely drops below zero. There is not much rainfall, air humidity is about 70%. The swimming season in Alushta is quite long: from May to mid-October (however, children should swim from mid-June to the end of August). The water temperature averages at least + 20C. Therefore, a vacation in Alushta will give you many pleasant moments.
