How to mold plasticine mixers. Modeling lesson for the smallest

One of the most useful activities for a child is considered to be modeling. She directly affects the development through the fingers of the baby, and it is also a fascinating and exciting hobby. How to make smeshariki from plasticine and what is needed for this?

To start modeling, you need to take clay, a stack, a wire and a board. The best option for this would be a special plasticine that can be baked in the oven. When such a material hardens, it becomes, like plastic, hard.

Next, we will tell you how to mold plasticine mixers in stages. First of all, select the character that you will master. Start with your child's favorite. Basically, the modeling technique is the same for all cartoon characters. Start by kneading clay. When you do this, fashion a torso ball. Its size depends on the size of the future Smesharik character.

Eyes and nose

Eyes are made of white balls. They need to be flattened slightly when fastened to the body. Then stick on them two smaller black balls as pupils. If you sculpt Krosh or Hedgehog, make a nose out of black circles. Losyash, Nyusha and Kopatych have noses of a different shape, size and color. From thin strips of plasticine make eyebrows and a mouth. To make beaks for Sovunya, Pina or Kar-Karych, take a plasticine ball of a suitable size, stick to the body and give the desired shape to the stack.



We continue to create

How to sculpt Smeshariki from plasticine further, you will learn if you read the article. Legs, wings, hands for our heroes must first be made from pre-prepared plasticine sausages, and then, using wires, attach to the body. In this case, they will not fall off during baking. In addition, thanks to the wire, the figures will become stronger and more stable. The ears of Krosh and the horns of Losyash are attached according to the same principle.

Character Identity

How to mold smeshariki from plasticine so that they are as similar as possible to the cartoon characters? Very simple. Do not forget while sculpting about the individual details of each character in the cartoon Smeshariki.

For the Hedgehog, it will be glasses and needles. Make the first of the strips, but the manufacture of the second is more complicated. First roll up small balls and attach them to your back, giving them a pyramid shape.

The main detail for Barash is the horns. We make them from thin sausages. We make the hat for Kopatych like this: roll two pancakes of various sizes and stick one on top of the other, stack the shape.

For Nyusha, it is necessary to blind a flower and fix it on the head. We attach Kar-Karychu a bow tie on his neck, Sovunye we make knitted pointed hat from plasticine.



Conclusion

From this article, you learned how to mold plasticine mixers. When you bake a stuck character in the oven, it will become strong and will bring joy to your child for a long time.
