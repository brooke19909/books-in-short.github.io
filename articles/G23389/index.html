Where it is better to relax with children in Spain: tips for tourists

Spain is a beautiful country that offers a wonderful beach holiday. When planning their vacation, each married couple seeks to find a good place where you can spend time with kids. The article talks about where it is better to relax with children in Spain.

Features of holidays in Spain

In anticipation of the summer, many tourists are considering where to go on vacation. One of the most popular destinations is Spain. Travelers are attracted not only by its stunning sea coast, but also by its rich cultural heritage and numerous entertainments. The country is interesting not only because of the rest on the sea beach. There are many attractions and outstanding places that every self-respecting person should see. If you plan to spend a vacation with the whole family, the question always arises: where is it better to relax with children in Spain?

Many experts recommend choosing resorts on the Mediterranean and Balearic seas for the first trip. It can be anywhere from the Costa del Maresme to the Costa Blanca.

Many parents prefer all-inclusive hotels. Holidays in Spain with children according to this concept is hardly possible. This is not Cyprus or Turkey, which specialize in all inclusive. Prices for tours to Spain are higher, and therefore the demand for them is slightly lower. Most often, tourists are offered 3-4 star hotels and apartment hotels. The latter option is most relevant for those travelers who plan to relax in Spain with children. Where is it better to stay: in a hotel or apartment? Experienced tourists recommend choosing apartments, if you are interested in the proper nutrition of your baby.

The advantage of the apartments is that they consist of several rooms, a bathroom and a private kitchenette. Therefore, you will have the opportunity to cook your own food. The presence of a kitchen saves the parents of the youngest guests, as well as children who follow a diet.

If you do not want to stand by the stove during your holidays, you can visit the restaurant. But do not hope that when ordering dishes from the children's menu you will bring healthy dietary food. Most likely, you will be served french fries and sausages. Europeans have a peculiar understanding about the proper nutrition of children, which causes resentment among our compatriots. Agree that not every mother wants to feed her baby with such harmful products. Therefore, the kitchen in the room is the only option to feed your child.

Malgart de Mar

A good vacation with children on the sea in Spain offers Malart de Mar. Quiet and calm resort town consists of two parts: historical and tourist. The local coast is covered with small pebbles. As you approach the nearby resort of Santa Susanna, there is more sand on the beaches. A gentle entry makes the region convenient for families with children. If you are tired of an idle holiday on the beach, you can stroll along the old part of the town and sit in a local cafe. Tourists recommend visiting the famous Marineland water park. This is the best place to relax with the whole family. The territory of the water park has a zoo, swimming pools, water slides and a dolphinarium.

For families with children in Spain at the sea, it is better to choose hotels focused on family vacations. In Malart de Mar, tourists recommend paying attention to the HTOP Palanmar hotel. It is located just 50 meters from the beach. The institution offers active children's animation. The hotel rooms, although simple, are not without charm.

Another hotel recommended by tourists is Ibersol Sorra D'Or. This is one of the few institutions in Spain that works on the concept of "all inclusive." The hotel is located 25 meters from the coast and offers an animation program for kids.

Of no less interest is the Amaraigua Hotel, built on the coast itself. It has direct sea access, which makes it possible not to waste time on walks. The hotel has a good kids club.

Canary Islands

When wondering where to relax in the summer with children in Spain, you should pay attention to the Canary Islands. Many parents think this is the perfect place for toddlers. For example, Tenerife is always warm, but not hot. Family-friendly hotels have playgrounds. In children's clubs animators work. The only drawback is that activists do not know Russian. Kids can be taken to Loro Park, where you can see a stunning show with sea lions, killer whales, dolphins and parrots.

Gran Canaria is no less attractive. The island is the second largest on the Canary Archipelago, but at the same time no less beautiful than Tenerife. Local hotels have animators and children's pools. And the famous Aqualand water park will allow you to have fun.

Costa blanca

It is difficult to make a choice between the numerous resorts of Spain. Where is it better to relax with children? It is difficult to give a definite answer to the question. The best time to relax

If we sorted out the question of where to go on vacation with children in Spain, it remains to find out what time to choose for a vacation. Tourists note that the sea warms faster off the coast of Mallorca and in the Costa Blanca. A little later - in the Costa Dorada. But in the Costa del Sol, water can be cold even in the middle of summer. This feature of the resort is caused by cold undercurrents. The best time to relax with children is the period from late June to early October. In August, the Spanish resorts are very hot. In addition, this is the most expensive period for rest.

But in the Canary Islands you can relax throughout the year. True, in winter the water in the sea is cool (+17 degrees). At this time, children prefer to splash around in the pool. But in the spring and autumn in the Canaries, the weather is ideal for relaxation.

Instead of an afterword

Among the many resorts in Spain, you can choose from many wonderful places for families with children. Tenerife, Mallorca and the Costa Dorada enjoy constant success among tourists. All resort regions are good in their own way and will find their admirers. Choosing a place of rest is based on your own preferences and wishes.
