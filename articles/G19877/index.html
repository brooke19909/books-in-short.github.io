Hotel Park Avenue Resort 2 * (India / Goa): traveler reviews

Mysterious India attracts millions of tourists from all over the world. The contrasting combination of poverty and luxury, natural wonders and mountains of garbage makes you think about a lot. Goa can be considered one of the most heavenly corners of India. It is here that the Park Avenue Resort 2 * hotel is located, which also leaves a very mixed impression among tourists.

Where is the hotel located

Park Avenue Resort 2 * is a miniature hotel, which is located near the famous Calangute Beach along the highway of the same name. The hotel is quite far from the main transport links. To get to Dabolim International Airport, you will have to overcome 45 kilometers, to Tivim Train Station - 20, and Mapus Bus Station - 8 km. The sea coast from the hotel is only 500 meters. In addition, within walking distance from the hotel there are many infrastructure objects: shops, cafes, restaurants, clubs.





Hotel Description

Externally, the Resort Park Avenue Calangute 2 * hotel looks rather modest, and the equipment of its rooms at the first glance at it disheartens. Nevertheless, tourists have long been fond of this hotel, because it is located in the most advantageous place of the city of Calangute - in terms of accessibility of infrastructure facilities. In addition, it is worth noting the fact that the owners of the hotel care about the development of its service. There is a cafe, bar, laundry, library, tour desk and many more things that are so necessary for tourists.





Number of rooms

Park Avenue Resort 2 * hotel offers its guests one of 60 rooms, which are located in 5 small buildings. It is worth saying that all rooms can be classified as double standard, although judging by the reviews of tourists, many of the rooms do not even reach the economy class.

Each room is equipped with a TV, a bathroom, and a minibar. Some rooms also have air conditioning and a private balcony, however, and such accommodation will cost a little more.





Hotel Services

The unsightly view of the Park Avenue Resort 2 hotel * owners and staff are trying as they can to compensate for the wide range of services provided to tourists. So, the service available at the hotel includes:

  • the ability to deliver drinks and food directly to your room;
  • dry cleaning and laundry services, as well as ironing of customers' clothes;
  • it is possible to rent a car relatively cheaply;
  • there is a left-luggage office where it is possible to hand over bulky suitcases;
  • there is a tour desk where you can get advice and book a trip to the main attractions of Goa with an experienced guide;

Entertainment

Resort Park Avenue offers fewer activities for leisure travelers. The main one is a small but clean and well-kept swimming pool located in the courtyard. There are also catering facilities on site. The bar offers a wide selection of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, but in the restaurant you can familiarize yourself in detail with the features of local cuisine.





Hotel surroundings

The resort Park Avenue can compensate for the lack of visibility with its well-developed infrastructure that extends beyond its borders. Going outside the hotel gate, you can visit the sights of Calangute, such as:

  • Gurudwara Kila Taragarh Sahib Temple, which is characterized by a modest but quite impressive architecture.
  • A large local bazaar where you can feel the local flavor. Here, in huge quantities, spices, souvenirs, masterpieces of folk art are presented.
  • The famous Calangute Beach, where you can enjoy pristine nature;
  • Saligao Church is one of the most striking architectural monuments in all of North Goa.
  • Subodha Kerkar is a famous art gallery where you can enjoy traditional Indian art.

What you need to know when checking into a hotel

One of the most inexpensive hotels can be considered Resort Park Avenue 2 *. North Goa is a fairly popular tourist destination, so finding an inexpensive overnight stay is not so simple. However, if you are fortunate enough to book a room in advance, you should know some of the features of this hotel:

  • On the day of arrival, you can get into your room no earlier than noon, but on the day of departure you must leave the room no later than 10 a.m. A two-hour window is given to hotel employees to put everything in order by the arrival of the following guests.
  • In no case is pets allowed in the room. This is due to the peculiarities of the local flora and fauna, as well as to the sanitary and epidemiological conditions characteristic of India.
  • The hotel accepts bank plastic cards of international payment systems. It is worth noting that when booking a room, the hotel reserves the right to block a certain amount in your account until your arrival and check-in.




For those traveling with children

Due to its specific characteristics, India is not the best place to travel with children. Nevertheless, for those who even take their kids with them on a long trip, Resort Park Avenue Hotel tries to create the most comfortable conditions. So, if you come to Goa with children, then you should know the following features of this hotel:

  • The hotel allows children with absolutely any age (even the smallest).
  • If you arrived at the hotel with a child under the age of 6 years and you do not need an extra bed for him, then you will not have to pay for it.
  • If you need an extra bed, then be prepared that for it you will have to lay out 300 rupees per night.
  • It is not allowed to add more than one bed in each room. This is due to the fact that the dimensions of the room simply do not allow to exceed the established limit.
  • There are no cots provided by the hotel by default. In some cases, such a service is possible if the agreement is made in advance (still at the stage of booking).
  • Even if you have agreed in advance on the need for extra beds or cots, the initial payment will only be charged for standard room equipment. For everything else, you will have to pay on arrival at the hotel.

Tourists reviews

Already many tourists managed to visit the Resort Park Avenue 2 * hotel. Reviews about him are rather mixed. Among the positive aspects, the following can be distinguished:

  • the efforts of the hotel staff and management to create the most complete infrastructure for their customers;
  • friendly attitude of hotel workers towards tourists;
  • convenient location where all the main attractions are within walking distance;
  • round-the-clock supply of hot water;
  • delicious homemade breakfasts;
  • affordable price;
  • well maintained clean area.

Nevertheless, you can find negative reviews that note the following points:

  • The hotel staff speaks only Hindi and English;
  • you cannot come with a pet;
  • the hotel is practically unsuitable for children;
  • gray linen (sometimes dirty);
  • very high noise levels in and around the hotel;
  • poor condition of the rooms (old furniture and lack of repair);
  • the dominance of insects and lizards;
  • in many rooms the kitchen is locked;
  • valuables are dangerous to leave in the room.

Hotel "Resort Park Avenue 2 *" (North Goa, Calangute) is a rather ambiguous place that can cause completely different feelings and impressions. We can only say with confidence that this hotel is not suitable for lovers of a comfortable stay on the All-Inclusive system. Picky tourists who come to India to get an unforgettable experience of local color and attractions stop here.
