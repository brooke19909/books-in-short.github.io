How to connect a fitness bracelet to your phone: step-by-step instructions

Now it has become fashionable to pay attention to your health. However, this is rather not a fashion, but a necessity. The daily norm of steps of an average person should be 10,000, but with sedentary office work it is quite problematic to control it. In this regard, the popularity of fitness bracelets is gaining momentum. The gadget is useful not only to people from the world of sports, but also to everyone who is not indifferent to their own health and wants to track physical activity indicators.

The fitness tracker displays data such as heart rate, steps taken, calories burned, and sleep quality. Fitness tracker is a real find for mobile people and those who want to be active. For greater convenience, you can connect it to a mobile phone. How to connect a fitness bracelet to your phone: detailed instructions are presented in this article.

Chinese-made fitness trackers

To pair the tracker with the phone you need to go to the "settings" by clicking on the three points in the upper right corner. In the menu that opens, select Mi Band. Other functions

You can also connect the detection function if you removed the bracelet from your hand and cannot find it. Click on the Locate Band: after that you will hear the vibration of your device. How to connect a fitness bracelet to a Samsung phone

The South Korean manufacturer is also not far behind the Chinese: the fitness tracker Gear Fit 2 is able to compete with many. This bracelet is an excellent assistant in training and in everyday life. How to connect the bracelet to the Galaxy device:

  1. In the Galaxy app store download Samsung Gear. If the program has been used for a long time, you must update it to the latest version.
  2. Following the instructions on the smartphone display, install additional plugins for the fitness bracelet.
  3. At the end of the installation, check the boxes next to all installed applications, notifications from which you would like to receive on your fitness bracelet.
  4. You can always update applications in the "Get new applications" section. All updates are available there.

Users often ask themselves: is it possible to connect a fitness bracelet to a phone on Android and how to do it? The bracelet is installed on any mobile running on this platform. The only requirement is that the version of Android should not be lower than 4.4:

  1. First, install the Samsung Gear program, after downloading it in the application store.

The only negative: you will have to additionally install the S Health, Samsung Accessory Service and Gear Fit 2 Plugin applications, if one of them is not installed, this may affect the correct functioning of the fitness bracelet.

If everything is done correctly, the tracker will be ready to use.
