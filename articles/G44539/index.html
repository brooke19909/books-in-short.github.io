Cyprotel Faliraki resort 4 * (Greece / Rodos island) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

Rhodes Island is an excellent place to relax. It is here that the Cyprotel Faliraki Resort 4 hotel is located. And today, many tourists, in particular Russian travelers, are interested in questions about where this hotel is located and what leisure conditions it offers.

Where is the hotel situated? Brief infrastructure description





Cyprotel Faliraki Resort 4 is located on the east coast of the Greek island of Rhodes. The center of the picturesque resort village of Falikari is only 1 km from the hotel complex - you can walk here. By the way, there is a bus stop near the hotel. Using public transport, you can travel around the entire county. The nearest airport is 17 kilometers away.

The hotel is a large complex, whose area is 12 thousand square meters. On its territory are picturesque residential buildings built in the traditional Greek style. The hotel itself is located on a slope, so it offers a beautiful view of the coast. Moreover, residential buildings are surrounded by a flowering garden. The hotel is open from April to October.

Description of rooms

Hotel Coprotel Faliraki Resort 4 can be considered quite large - it consists of 356 rooms. There are rooms of various capacities, with different levels of comfort, as well as several rooms for people with disabilities.





Each room has a balcony with garden furniture, which overlooks the courtyard or flowering garden. Of household appliances, there is a TV, air conditioning and a fan, as well as a direct dial telephone, a small refrigerator for drinks, as well as a radio and a safe.

The bathroom is also fully equipped with modern fixtures. There is a spacious shower, washbasin, towels, branded hotel cosmetics and, of course, round-the-clock hot and cold water.

What food does the hotel Cyprotel Faliraki Resort 4 (Greece) offer?





Reviews about the food on site are mostly positive. This complex operates on an all-inclusive system, which gives tourists the right to three meals a day, local drinks and snacks throughout the day. For guests, tables are served in the main restaurant of the hotel - here they are offered international dishes, as well as a separate table with traditional Greek dishes. Moreover, themed evenings are organized three times a week dedicated to the traditions and cuisine of a particular country.

By the way, guests have lunch on the outdoor terrace by the pool. There is also a bar serving chilled and spirits, as well as tea and coffee. You can eat in the snack bar or tavern, where the menu includes wood-baked pizza, various varieties of local ice cream and traditional Greek drinks.

Water and beach vacations for travelers





The beach is about 1000 meters from the hotel. It can be reached on foot along a footpath. In addition, in the morning guests are delivered to the beach by a tourist train. The coast will amaze you with clean and soft white sand and soft sea waves. Descent into the sea is convenient and safe. There are sun loungers and umbrellas on the shore, for the use of which you need to pay separately. There are also changing areas and showers with fresh water.

Near the beach there is a tavern with an excellent menu, where for a little money you can have a great lunch. Tourists can also enjoy water sports, including water skiing, boating, beach volleyball , etc.

Conditions for staying with children





Of course, on the territory of the Cyprotel Faliraki Resort 4 all the conditions for an interesting children's vacation are created. As for amenities, parents of a small child will definitely be offered a cot and a high chair in the restaurant. As for baby food and some other accessories for babies, they can be purchased at the nearest supermarket.

In the courtyard of the hotel there is a large pool for children, where children can have a great time. And not so long ago, small water slides were equipped here, where children and their parents can have fun. A mini-club also works, where professional educators work with the guys. You can have a great time at the playground. And do not forget about animation - fun contests and performances for children are guaranteed.

Additional services on site

Of course, the owners of the hotel tried to do everything so that the rest of foreign tourists was as comfortable as possible. For example, there is a spacious parking lot. A bike rental service is also available on site - this type of transport is convenient if you intend to explore the surroundings.

There is also a currency exchange office. The hotel also offers to visit the local souvenir shop and jewelry store, where you can buy gifts for yourself and loved ones, as well as jewelry in the traditional Greek style. In public areas there is free internet access.

Entertainment for hotel guests





What recreational facilities does the Cyprotel Faliraki Resort 4 offer? The photo shows that almost the entire courtyard of the complex is occupied by a large swimming pool, around which there are sun loungers and umbrellas. By the way, in the mornings, water aerobics classes are held here, as well as water polo competitions.

There are also mini-golf courses, as well as a paved tennis court, which is illuminated in the evening (for a small fee). Guests can also try archery, play table tennis or have a great time with friends at the pool table. There is also a large volleyball court. And those tourists who wish to keep fit can use the local gym. In addition, animators are entertained throughout the day.

Of course, there are other attractions that Cyprotel Faliraki Resort 4 can offer. Greece is a country with an ancient history. And the island of Rhodes is no exception, because there is something to see. The hotel tour desk can help you choose a route for sightseeing. For example, you can visit the monastery of St. Amos, walk through the former center of the ancient world, the city of Lindos, take a walk along the legendary Butterfly Valley, visit the Rhodes fortress, etc. In short, a vacation at this hotel can be a real adventure for any traveler.

Cyprotel Faliraki Resort 4 Hotel (Rhodes): traveler reviews

It is worth noting that this hotel is very popular among Europeans. But over the past few years, the number of Russian tourists who want to spend a vacation at Cyprotel Faliraki Resort 4 has also increased. Reviews about it are mostly positive. First of all, they note real cosiness and sincere hospitality of the inhabitants of the island. Living conditions are more than worthy. The only disadvantages are the beach, which is not too close. On the other hand, a relatively affordable price is an obvious advantage, since a day of staying at the hotel costs about 3,000 rubles per person.

Moreover, here you will find a lot of entertainment. Parents having a rest with children will also be pleased, as the guys really have something to do. Separately, it is worth mentioning the team of animators who really do their job really well - throughout the day they organize various shows, competitions and performances that will appeal to both children and adults.
