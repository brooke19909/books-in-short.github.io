Hotel "Lonisera", Turkey: features of rest. Tourists reviews

Hotel Lonisera (Turkey) adorns the Mediterranean coast in an area called Turkler. Its recreational opportunities are determined by the mild Mediterranean sanatorium climate of Fugla Bay. For vacationers on the compact cozy Fugla Peninsula (of the same name with the bay), the most modern hotel infrastructure has been created, comfortable terraces leading to the beach coast have been planned. This is a four star first line hotel with a decent reputation. "Turkey, Alanya, Hotel Lonisera" - these words adorn the brochures of many domestic and European tour operators. This hotel complex is really preferred by many frugal Europeans (especially pragmatic Germans) sophisticated in a comfortable stay, and it is affordable for vacationers from the former CIS countries.

The Turkish subtropics, where the hotel is located, are ideal for relaxation. The rich history of the country is also interesting for vacationers, and the production of souvenirs for tourists is a major business, significant for the country's economy.





Location

The local hotel and resort business has a harmoniously built logistics. Indeed, unlike the evolutionary development of European resorts, the state of Turkey developed its tourism industry meaningfully and plannedly.

Alanya - "Lonisera." The distance between them is 20 kilometers. Antalya Airport is 98 kilometers from the hotel. Around the hotel complex - an impressive retail and restaurant infrastructure. Nearby are the ancient attractions of Asia Minor, as the Country of the Star and the Crescent was once called: Alanya Arkeoloji Muzesi, Damlatas Caves, Ornek Sinemalari.

While staying at the hotel, you can travel using three modes of transport: the most economical - Dolmush minibuses (price 1.5 lira), an ordinary taxi, or by renting a car.

Recreational potential

Hotel "Lonisera" (Turkey) uses man-made recreational facilities for recreation of its guests, and not only the unique natural opportunities of the Mediterranean. Perhaps that is why its comfortable sandy beach is quite extensive and almost never crowded. Many of the guests prefer to relax by the pool (and there are 10 of them here). The area occupied by this hotel complex is quite extensive - 90 thousand m 2 . It has a well-groomed orchard with terraces, an amphitheater, a small zoo, shops, restaurants and bars. He is in great demand among couples. It is quiet, spacious. The staff is polite and attentive. Abundant and tasty food for vacationers on the all inclusive system. A boring animation program, ample opportunities for outdoor activities and sports. This is the Lonicera Hotel. Turkey, being a rather popular resort, of course, is rich in hotels of various classes, but this complex is special.





Hotel

Vacationers are met by four buildings (five-, three-story and two four-story). They hospitably provide guests with 552 rooms, including 66 family and 37 luxury. From the side of the facade, all buildings are made in one style, differing from one another only in the color of the balconies.

Each room is equipped with a balcony, bathroom, toilet. How comfortable are standard rooms? Of the furniture in them - a mirror, a bedside table, a table, two beds, a wardrobe. There is a complete toilet and bathroom. There is a safe in the closet. The key to it can be taken at the reception (service - paid). The balcony is equipped with a plastic table and chairs. Residents of the rooms use household appliances: refrigerator, air conditioning, TV, telephone. The staff at the Lonisera Hotel (Turkey) change bed linen and towels every three days.

The open-air restaurant provides meals for up to 450 guests. Indoor restaurants have, respectively, 600 and 300 people. Also, catering facilities for vacationers include a seafood restaurant A La Carte with 100 seats and Panorama for gala dinners and banquets (200 seats). During the summer holiday season, the main catering establishment operates on an all inclusive system.

Resorts have the opportunity to try national Turkish cuisine in the huts equipped by the pool. Hotel Lonisera Word (Turkey) diversifies its food system, giving vacationers the opportunity to have a meal anywhere on its territory, thanks to five bars. Three of them are located by the pools and one at a time, respectively, in the lobby and at the disco.



If we talk about the diet, it is quite convenient and democratic: from 7:00 to 10:00, vacationers have breakfast, from 12:00 to 15:00 - have lunch, and from 18:00 to 22:00 - have dinner. Pools

Hotel "Lonisera Word" (Turkey) cordially provides its guests with swimming pools with adjacent infrastructure. Beach

The four-star Lonisera is a hotel in Turkey, modeled on traditional European resorts with its balanced hotel infrastructure. In Turkey - Haggle

The Turkish market should be a bargain. This is a national tradition. So, you went to the seller (the sellers are all as one - men) and inquired about the value of the item you liked. He looks at the clouds and calls you a truly cloudy price that has nothing to do with reality. Next - the word is yours. An important point! You should only voice your price when you are exactly buying a product. ‚ÄúJust like that‚Äù is not allowed, it is against the rules and Turkish culture. Do not buy - it is better to remain silent.

Suppose you decide to buy. Then call the price half as much as your opponent voiced. He allegedly takes a break, runs to "confer" with his manager. That is, it depicts a stormy contractual and thought process. Its result is a confidential message from the seller that the manager (solely out of respect for you) agreed to sell the goods, only asks to increase the payment by 15%. You add 5-7% to your amount. Seller agrees. Both you and he are satisfied. This is Turkish trade.
