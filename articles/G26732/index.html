How to put money on the phone with a Sberbank card? Tips & Tricks

With the advent of modern technology, many things have become much more convenient to do, without even leaving your home. In addition, with the increase in competition in the field of banking services, many offers for customers have appeared. One of them is to replenish the balance of your phone. How to put money on the phone with a Sberbank card will be described in this article.

Convenience Services

As mentioned above, to replenish the account of a mobile phone, there is not even a need to leave the borders of your own home. Everyone will agree that it is convenient. But there are other options when you need to seek help from an ATM. There are several ways to help transfer money from a card to a phone. It is enough to choose the most suitable one, and the subscriber will be in touch again. This option can help out in many situations, for example on a business trip.



The main ways to put money on your phone with a Sberbank card

The most busy or advanced Internet users can use the Sberbank-online service. Only first you need to connect the Internet banking system. To do this, you will need to personally go to the office of the credit institution and get instructions or advice from the manager. After all the procedures, the client will be able to quickly and easily transfer money to the phone from the card via the Internet.

If it is not possible to replenish the balance through a worldwide network, then you can use an ATM, a special payment device or a mobile phone. In the latter case, the Mobile Bank service should also be activated.



Instruction manual

So, in order to put money on the phone with a Sberbank card, you must first decide on the chosen method. In the event that the client decided to choose an ATM or a special payment device, then he just needs to insert his card into the department and enter the pin code. To replenish the balance via the Internet, you must go to your personal account in the Sberbank-online system. After that, enter the login and password issued by the manager in the office. Next, select the ‚ÄúPayments‚Äù menu and your mobile operator. As in an ATM, you will need to enter a phone number and the amount to be replenished.

If there is no Internet or ATM nearby, then you can use the phone. To transfer money, you need to activate the Mobile Bank service. How to do this, was said above. Next, you will need to send a message to the number 900, in the text of the letter indicate the amount, for example 200. It should be borne in mind that the money will only go to the number whose SIM card was attached to a bank card. If you want to transfer a sum of money to another subscriber, you need to send SMS to number 900 with the text PHONE 8 ********** 200.



To complete the payment process, you will need to send the code to the number 900 received from Sberbank.

Other features

Sberbank provides its customers with many services. For example, you can not only transfer money to a phone from a bank card, but also from card to card. This option is very useful and convenient, especially when the owner of another card is on a trip and he runs out of money. To help a person in such a situation, it is enough to enter his mobile phone number, and the bank card number will be determined automatically. The only condition is the availability of the connected Mobile Bank service. So, to send money, you need to dial the number 900 and the text of the message containing the number in the format 903 *******, and indicate the amount of transfer, for example 500 (limitation - 8000 rubles). In response, an SMS from Sberbank will need to check the recipient's data, namely his first name, middle name, and also the letter that the surname begins with. The bank must send a code, which should be entered in the response SMS, and send it in confirmation of the operation. The whole procedure takes less than one minute. That is why this service will save a lot of time for both customers.



Last Tips

Thus, it becomes clear that there is nothing complicated in putting money to the phone from a Sberbank card. It is enough to connect the necessary service packages and enjoy the convenience. More than five million users have already appreciated this quality.

In order not to forget your bank card in a payment terminal or ATM, you must definitely pick it up after all transactions have been completed. As a rule, the device itself beeps. This should be done, because cases of fraud have become more frequent.

In the event that there is no opportunity or time to personally contact the bank to connect any service, you can do this using a terminal or ATM.
