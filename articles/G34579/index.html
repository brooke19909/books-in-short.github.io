How to make a notebook from a notebook with your own hands

It can be difficult to find a diary that will fully meet all the requirements for design and content. In this case, it is not at all difficult to make a notebook with your own hands, especially since it can become a wonderful and exclusive gift.



For the manufacture of a notebook, you can use only improvised materials without spending money on purchases. There are many instructions telling how to make a notebook out of a notebook, this activity can turn into a hobby and bring pleasure and a new experience.

Notebook from sheets of notebook: step-by-step instructions for manufacturing

To understand how to make a notebook out of sheets of a notebook, you need to familiarize yourself with the master class in detail. In addition to notebook sheets, you will need colored paper, thick cardboard, a needle with threads, stationery clips, ribbons, silicone sealant (Moment glue) and various decorative elements.



  1. You need to take notebooks, their number depends on the desired thickness of the notebook, remove the covers from them and put them in one flat pile.
  2. Next, you need to make markings near the root of the future notebook, indicating six points equidistant from each other. You can make holes with a file or other convenient tool.
  3. You need to put the tape and sew on it the first notebook on the holes made. The thread is not cut, but other notebooks are sewn from above on top of the holes made. Finally, you need to fix the thread with a knot.
  4. Next, the notebook is pressed using stationery clips and the strap is glued with sealant. They need to fill each click and fix the root more tightly with clips.
  5. While the main part of the notebook dries, you can make bookends (sheets connecting the cover and sheets) using colored cardboard or scrapbooking paper.
  6. Now the matter is left behind the cover, it is made of thick cardboard and decorated in accordance with your preferences.

Design and filling out a notebook

When thinking about how to make a notebook out of a notebook, one should not forget about personal notes. But how to fill it out so that the design is original and reflects the identity of its owner. In this case, there are many options.

The first step is to pick up a bright ribbon, which will become a bookmark. On the first page, you can characterize the conventions that will be used later when filling out the notebook.

At the end of the diary, you can take a few pages of useful tips. And of course, no one canceled various thematic drawings, stickers that can be placed on the pages.

If you are interested in how to make an alphabetical notebook from a notebook, then this is not so difficult. To do this, you just need to manually make the alphabetical cutting down, so the diary will become more practical.

Notebooks in the style of Moleskine Cahiers

"Moleskin Kaye" are thin notebooks with a cover of multi-colored cardboard. These flexible and comfortable notebooks are a great alternative to regular notebooks.



A great analogue of a classic notebook can be done in just an hour. To do this, you need a notebook, decorative elements for the cover. Of the tools useful scissors and a ruler.

If you are thinking about how to make a notebook out of a notebook, 12 sheets will be enough. Notebook sheets need to be cut to the desired size, the cover can be strengthened with thick colored cardboard or scrapbooking sheets. Now the Moleskin-style notebook is ready.

Notebook with removable blocks

Very often blank notebooks remain in a notebook; they can be cut and a small notebook can be made. This master class will tell you how to make a notebook from a notebook or notebook sheets with your own hands. Its main feature is that the sheets will be removable, which is very practical.



Of the materials you will need cardboard, notebook sheets or a notebook, PVA glue or double-sided tape, a thread with a needle and laces. The first step is to make a cover of thick cardboard, if desired, it can be covered with cloth. In the cover you need to make several holes and fix the lace, on which the notebook sheets will be attached.

Functional and practical notebook is ready. In this case, you can independently adjust the thickness of the diary, and the cover can last more than one semester.

How to age vintage note pad sheets

Vintage style is very popular today, this also applies to notebooks. But how to give an improvised diary an aged look? To do this, you can use sheets with the effect of antiquity.

To make such a paper is not at all difficult, you just need to dip it into a tea solution. The more tea leaves you use, the yellower the paper will be. Notebook sheets are dipped into the solution for several minutes, then dried and ironed.
