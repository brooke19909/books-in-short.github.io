Water park "Dolphin" in Nebug: attractions, prices, reviews

During a summer vacation at a seaside resort, I really want to not only lie on the beach and swim, but also get new experiences, and maybe even a dose of adrenaline. Why not head to the water entertainment center? One of the favorite places to relax among tourists in the Tuapse district is the Dolphin water park. What is remarkable for this complex of water entertainments?

General information about the water attraction complex



In the village of Nebug, his water park opened in 1997. The complex covers an area of ​​more than 3 hectares, on which are located pools and water attractions for visitors of various ages. Is it safe to visit the water park "Dolphin", which will soon celebrate its twentieth anniversary? Of course, yes, the complex regularly passes all the necessary checks, and if necessary, minor repairs are done there. In 2004, during the voluntary certification of recreational centers, the water park received category “A” - an assessment of the 5 * entertainment center. Amusement Leisure Worldwide, a well-established manufacturer of water attractions from Canada, is installed in the water entertainment complex.

Attractions for every taste and age



Many families visit the water park primarily for their own children. The water entertainment complex in Nebug will delight young visitors with a dedicated entertainment area. This is a shallow safe pool. In the children's area there are slides, game towns and other attractions. Water park "Dolphin" offers interesting entertainment for adults. The most extreme are Black Hole, Kamikaze-Abyss and Triple Pigtail. For those who are afraid to ride on the highest slides, there are also ordinary direct open structures.

Infrastructure and other entertainment



Water Park "Dolphin" in Nebug boasts a high level of service. Everything is created here for the comfort of guests. When buying a ticket, each visitor receives a special bracelet with a built-in electronic sensor. Using this accessory, you can open and close your own locker in the locker room. Having changed clothes and having left all valuable things, it is possible to go to attractions and pools. The complex has recreation areas - artificial beaches with sun loungers. Water park "Dolphin" invites its guests to visit one of the two restaurants, which are also part of the complex. Here you can buy refreshments, snacks or dine with the whole family. Especially for the youngest visitors to the complex, a separate children's menu has been developed. Unlike many other similar establishments, "Dolphin" does not close in the evening, at 22:00 foam parties begin here . At night, all attractions work, and the complex is illuminated by multi-colored lasers. Fireworks and incendiary dance music create a festive mood. Moreover, each party is held according to the original program, which means that even for a short vacation, you can go to a foamy disco two or more times. In the daytime, animation programs for children are held.

Visiting Rules

The water entertainment complex is designed for family visits. Each adult accompanying a child under 16 years old must constantly monitor his ward during his stay at the water park. Minor children in the water sports complex must always be in life jackets or arm ruffles. One adult can accompany no more than two children up to 120 centimeters tall. Water park "Dolphin" offers its visitors flexible prices and various rates. Children's tickets should be purchased for visitors shorter than 140 centimeters. Also, it is precisely the growth that determines the admissibility of descent from adult attractions.

How much does it cost to go to the water park?



The complex of water attractions has several rates. Entrance tickets can be purchased for a fixed time or for the whole day. However, keep in mind that in the second option it is impossible to leave the territory. Water park "Dolphin" prices for their services are acceptable. Entrance ticket for an adult costs from 500 rubles, the cost of visiting for children is from 300 rubles. Children under 3 years old can visit the water park for free with an adult accompanying person. Food and drinks in the cafe are paid separately according to the menu. Attention: it is forbidden to bring your food and snacks to the territory of the water park. The exception is drinking water and baby food for the smallest.

Waterpark "Dolphin" (Nebug): visitor reviews



This water entertainment complex is very popular among tourists due to the picturesque landscape. On its territory there are a lot of greenery and decorative sculptures. So while relaxing in this place, do not forget to take beautiful photos as a keepsake. Does this water entertainment center have flaws? According to many visitors, local restaurants have unreasonably high prices. Many tourists also do not like that when buying a ticket for the whole day you can not leave the territory of the complex. But still, if you are staying for a while in the village of Nebug, the Dolphin water park should definitely be visited. This complex is especially liked by children, kids are ready to frolic in water at least all day. A variety of slides will also delight adults. But adolescents of small stature, who should not attend adult attractions, can become bored here. Be sure to consider this fact when planning family leisure activities.
