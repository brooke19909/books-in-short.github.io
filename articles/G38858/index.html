Hotel Rajan (Egypt) - pleasure is included in the price

The five-star Rajan Hotel (Egypt) is located on the first line, near the picturesque bay of the Red Sea - Sharks Bay (Shark Bay), known for its fabulous coral reefs. Immediately after the three hotel beaches, a unique reef kingdom begins. This picturesque place is framed by the mountain ranges of the Sinai desert, gradually turning to the coast into rocky dunes, and then into gorgeous beaches. In a great location, the Rajana Hotel (Egypt) was built, the photo below demonstrates this.





Egyptian investment companies spend huge amounts of money on developing the infrastructure of the Sharm el-Sheikh resort (where our hotel is located). The boarding house architecture of this unique health resort city consists of low-rise buildings - hotels, luxury villas, residences, mansions, town houses. The territory is well-groomed, landscaped. Around - pools, artificial waterfalls. It's hard to believe, but in the 70s of the last century Sharm el-Sheikh was just a fishing village. Its current glory is created by well-groomed hotels, as if transferred here from Europe. Hotel Rajan (Egypt) is famous for its plentiful, varied menu. Judge for yourselves: the food of vacationers is organized by the main restaurant Capitol Restaurant (regional and international dishes), the Baywatch Restaurant (grill, salads); Italian Restaurant - Italian cuisine; French Restaurant - French cuisine; Seafood Restaurant - Seafood. Hotel Rajan (Egypt) provides comfortable rooms with balconies. Guests can use 599 rooms, of which 32 are single suites and 12 are family suites. Room equipment includes a TV, minibar, refrigerator, telephone, air conditioning.
