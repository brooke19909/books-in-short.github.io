Rooms

This hotel does not belong to large ones. It has only sixty eight rooms. They are small but refined, with all the necessary amenities for a modern tourist. Describing the hotel Agelia Beach (Crete), reviews mention that the rooms are air-conditioned, television with satellite, including Russian, channels. The rooms in the hotel are equipped with a refrigerator, there is Wi-Fi. They give a bathrobe and slippers. Some rooms have no balconies. They are considered economical and surrendered by stock. There are good discounts on them. Room size is twenty-five square meters. The view from them opens on the territory of the hotel or on the sea (partially on the pool). There are family rooms measuring thirty-five meters. They have a living room, separated from the bedroom by a sliding door. Fourteen rooms have access to private pools, where you can swim day and night.



Services

Tourists are satisfied with the service at the Agelia Beach Hotel (Crete). Reviews mention that you can rent a car here and park it for free. There is a currency exchange, you can get information about the surroundings, a tour desk is open. Since the hotel operates on an all inclusive system, the cost of many services has already been paid. Sports and fitness rooms await active tourists. Animators also invite vacationers to do aerobics, aqua gym, beach volleyball. Free Internet is available not only in the rooms, but also in the lobby, restaurants and poolside. Many services for children - highchairs, menus for kids, a mini club. And those who want to have fun in the evenings are invited to karaoke. There are many paid services. These are billiards, barbecue, evenings with invited stars, live music and Greek dances. Sauna, wellness center and beach discos are also at an additional cost.

Sea, pools

The private beach with golden sand is a highlight of the Agelia Beach 4 * hotel (Crete). Reviews of vacationers tell us that the entrance to the water here is good, gentle and safe for children. It is located just a hundred meters from the hotel buildings. It is the presence of a private beach that gives the bonus that sunbeds and umbrellas are free there, unlike most hotels in the Cretan coast. Towels are issued on a special card on bail. You can pick them up at the reception. The sea near Rethymnon is not very violent, waves are rare. These shores are also famous for the fact that the famous Cretan Caretta turtles lay their eggs here. The hotel has four swimming pools (one for children). Around them are sun loungers, umbrellas and tables.
