Hotels in the center of Perm: names, overview, addresses, reservations and reviews

Perm is the largest industrial city in Russia. It has a zoo, a planetarium, museums, art galleries and a philharmonic. Many tourists come to the city in order to get acquainted with its life, art and culture.

Guests are offered to stay in Perm hotels in the center, as well as rooms on the periphery. Each tourist has the opportunity to choose a place taking into account personal preferences. However, visitors are not always willing to live on the outskirts of the village. Comfortable conditions and proximity to the center are some of the important criteria for tourists. Some are looking for hotels near attractions, while others are interested in proximity to public transport or train stations.

Travel Tips

In the center of Perm, hotels are located on several streets and at different distances from the main facilities. Vacationers need to determine the area where they would like to live while visiting the city.

It is also worth describing for yourself a number of criteria that a hostel or hotel should meet. For example, the number of rooms, amenities and the availability of a kitchen. Price also matters, and for some tourists it becomes a determining factor.

Hotel "Flowers"

The hotel is located within walking distance of the city center. Nearby there are shops, cafes and restaurants. For hotel guests there is a parking lot, which is fenced with a barrier. Laundry or room service is available. The hotel has a restaurant serving European cuisine.

The hotel rooms are of various comforts. From standard to fully equipped suites. The apartment is spacious and bright. A lot of bright accents in the form of floral prints or unusual curtains. All rooms have private bathrooms with showers and air conditioning.

Guests speak well of this place. In their comments they say that the hotel is very nice and comfortable. The rooms are spacious and have everything you need. In some bathrooms, instead of showers there are bathtubs - this sometimes causes discomfort.

Address: Permskaya street, 56. The cost of living is from 1,500 rubles per day. You can reserve rooms without advance payment. Guests rate this place 8 points.

"Prikamye"

Hotel "Prikamye" (Perm) provides guests with the opportunity to use the safe, parking, fitness room, as well as put in the room an extra bed for a child (crib). The hotel has an ATM, bar and restaurant.

Meals are served on a buffet basis, however, if necessary (for an additional fee), guests can order dishes from the menu.

The interior of the rooms is classic and discreet. Carpet on the floor and light walls. Spacious beds and necessary furniture are at guests' disposal. The bathroom has a shower cabin and the necessary shower accessories.

Guests speak well of the hotel. They believe that this is a very convenient and comfortable place to relax. The complex itself provides everything necessary for living, and its convenient location allows you to easily get to the main attractions of the city.

Address: Komsomolsky prospekt street, 27. This is the very center of the city, from where it is convenient to walk to the main memorial sites and museums. The cost of living is from 2 500 rubles per day, rooms with increased comfort will cost guests a little more. Booking is available both on the official website and by phone.

Hotel "Ural"

This is one of the largest centers where tourists and guests of Perm can sleep for the night. Along the hotel building there are small shops, communication salons, catering establishments and ATMs.

On-site guests can use the Turkish bath, massage, fitness center and spa. Also on the ground floor of the hotel opens its doors to a beauty salon and hairdresser. For younger guests there is a kids club and a special breakfast menu.

The interior of the apartment reminds many of the Soviet era. Strict stylistic design, classic style and color scheme of rooms - all this has the same perception. Some tourists note that when visiting the hotel it seems that he was in an expensive elite hotel of the Soviet era.

Guest reviews about staying in this place are ambiguous. Some did not like the confusion with the rooms (instead of the studio they settled into the usual standard). Other guests say that these are the best suitable conditions in the very center of the city. Clean, comfortable and comfortable. Polite staff, but without a "twinkle in the eye." The apartments are comfortable, everything you need is available. Furniture is relatively new, plumbing too. Many recommend a hotel for a stay.

Address: Lenin street, house 58. The cost of living is from 2 800 rubles per day. The hotel has apartments with different levels of comfort. Tourists can book a suitable room for a certain date on the official website or by phone.

"Pearl"

This hotel is located at 65 Gagarina Street, lit. A. The cost of living from 1,500 rubles per day. Room reservation is made by phone. Near the planetarium and the circus.

The hotel staff is working around the clock and is ready to answer all questions. The spacious rooms have furniture, a fridge, a TV, a bed, and a bathrobe and towels. Bathroom and everything you need from cosmetics in each room.

The interior of the rooms is European. Light walls are in harmony with the beautiful curtains covering the windows. Small bedside tables and lamps on the walls above the bed are made for the convenience of guests.

Hotel "Pearl" (Perm) received good reviews from guests. In the morning, a delicious but simple breakfast is served in the common room. The hotel is clean and comfortable. The staff is helpful but sometimes too intrusive. The hotel is centrally located in a quiet location. If you do not need clubs and parties, then this area is ideal for a relaxing holiday. The price category suits all guests.

Hotel "New Star"

The most interesting hotel of the city is located at 38 Zvezda Street, 38, lit. B. You can walk to the center for 5-6 minutes, or you can look into Gorky Park, located nearby. The cost of hotel accommodation is from 3,500 rubles. Room reservation round-the-clock both on the website and by phone.

Hotel Description

Outside, the building looks like a high-rise building in New York. Large stained glass windows, height and embossment - all this speaks of European design. The name of the hotel, which is highlighted in the dark, allows guests to freely find this place. Indeed, the best hotels in Perm in the city center should look like this, New Star really meets all modern criteria.

The hotel presents itself as a European hotel. The interior and service are consistent. Spacious bright rooms in gloss and luxury. An interesting design solution for each room and a spacious lounge are features of the hotel.

Modern repairs and new furniture, convenient location and high quality service - all this brings this hotel closer to European standards. The presence of a safe, parking and a large gym makes this place even more attractive for tourists. Not all hotels in the center of Perm can boast of such a gym.

The hotel can accommodate in rooms of different comforts - from business to luxury suites. On the ground floor there is a restaurant serving breakfast and European cuisine. The large lobby has a lobby bar where guests can relax around the clock.

Guest reviews about the hotel are good. Some believe that this is the best place in Perm, where you can inexpensively stay for the night. Overshadowed by this whole good picture is only a meager breakfast. Guests in the reviews note that it is far from European, and the restaurant menu needs to be expanded. In Perm, hotels in the city center should provide the opportunity for a wider selection of dishes.

In their reviews, guests are unequivocal about the rooms at the New Star Hotel. All of them say that the apartments are excellent and large. The hand of a designer is felt. The furniture is new and comfortable. TV and Internet are also at a high level.

Hotel "Profsoyuznaya" (Perm)

The hotel has a 24-hour front desk and friendly staff. It features a safety deposit box and car parking. In the morning, a continental breakfast is served, and the restaurant offers any choice from the menu.

The interior of the rooms is classic in soothing colors. The rooms are small but have everything you need. A sleeping place with bedside tables, small lamps, a TV, a fridge and a bathroom. Free high speed internet is available. Almost all hotels in the center of Perm provide free access to the network to their guests.

Address: May 9, house 21. Cost of living from 2,000 rubles per day. If you walk along the streets, you will find yourself on the embankment of the Kama River, which you need to visit for all the guests of Perm. From this point of the city it is convenient to get to the Academic Theater and the center. Booking a room is simple and convenient (by phone or online).

Reviews on "Trade Union"

Guests in their reviews comment that this hotel meets all the requirements for price and quality of service. Always friendly staff who helps and prompts if necessary. A wonderful and hearty breakfast, along with comfortable accommodation, this is indicated by almost all tourists.

Of the minuses, hotel guests find only that the building does not have an elevator, although it has only two floors. Sometimes it is difficult to lift your suitcases. Proximity to the main sites and attractions of the city is another plus of relaxation in the "Trade Union".

Perm: Hotels, hotels (conclusion)

Many come to this welcoming village for business or sightseeing. There are city residents who require temporary accommodation with comfortable conditions. Hotels in the center of Perm are in demand among tourists. Many people want to settle closer to the embankment or the theater; transport routes (stations, airport) are important to others. It is important that all hotels provide guests with transfer and taxi services to anywhere in the city. Many recreational facilities are equipped with gyms and wellness centers. Such a variety of services enables tourists to choose a comfortable holiday for themselves, being in the city of Perm.
