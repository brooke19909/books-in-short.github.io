Glory Park

Do not forget to go to the most interesting places in Kiev with a camera in order to capture the incredible beauty forever. This park is not just an oasis with green alleys, but also important historical sites. Here you will see the Memorial, erected in memory of the victims of the famine, the Stela above the eternal flame, the Alley of military glory with three monuments to hero pilots. From the river side, the park has four delightful viewing platforms that are interconnected by alleys. Interesting places of Kiev for tourists, and for local residents, are not limited to this.



Vladimir hill

Taking a walk here, you can see the monument to Vladimir the Baptist, a delightfully beautiful water cascade, look at all the beauty from the observation deck, relax in the gazebo. Park bridge

This unusual building has three names: Devil's Bridge, Park Bridge, the bridge of lovers.

The first name can be called very successful and accurate. You yourself will see this as soon as you step on the bridge. The plank flooring on it is so fragile that it threatens to fall right under you at any moment. Many parents even threaten to take their naughty children here, they say, under the liars, the bridge will fail.

The second name is absolutely justified. Indeed, without this bridge, the park zone, which consists of Khreschaty and Mariinsky parks, would simply form 2 separate sections.





And why did they call the place the bridge of lovers? Because there was a tradition on the wedding day to hang locks and ribbons here as symbols of eternal love. And many more are recognized here in love for each other. Such interesting places of Kiev for young people will be entertaining both from a historical point of view and from a romantic one.
