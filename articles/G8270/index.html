Riviera Beach Resort & Spa 5 *, Nha Trang, Vietnam: hotel description, reviews

More and more often, tourists choose Vietnam as a resting place. After all, this country boasts a warm climate, long sandy beaches and the unspoiled beauty of nature. Naturally, over the past few years, many new hotels have been opened here. One of them is Riviera Beach Resort Spa 5 *. Before the trip, every tourist tries to get as much information as possible about this hotel. So is it worth stopping here?

Location, photo and description of the hotel





This hotel is located in a small resort village called Nha Trang. Cam Ranh Airport is approximately 11 km away. Riviera Beach Resort Spa 5 * - a picturesque hotel complex, whose area is approximately 100 thousand square meters. The hotel consists of cozy two-story bungalows, so guests can count on peace and quiet. By the way, the distance to the center of the famous tourist city of Nha Trang is 27 km - it can be easily and quickly reached by car. The hotel, by the way, was opened in 2015 and is still under expansion.

Cam Ranh, Riviera Beach Resort Spa 5 *: description and photos of rooms





Of course, tourists are interested in the question of what living conditions can be expected. The hotel has 70 rooms, although the hotel is still under expansion. All rooms are bright, spacious, with large windows and quality finishes. Furniture, towels, linens - everything is new.

There is also a set of household appliances. Large-screen TVs include satellite and local channels. There is also a telephone, radio, safe with a secure lock. The air conditioning system with a convenient control panel allows you to create a comfortable temperature in the room. There is a minibar, which is filled with drinking water daily, a mini fridge. There is a set for making fragrant tea and coffee. Guests enjoy free internet access.

The bathroom is equipped with modern new fixtures. You will have the opportunity to relax in a warm bath or quickly refresh yourself in the shower. There is a wall mirror, washbasin, toilet. In addition, you will find a set of hygiene products, including soap, shampoos, lotions - their supplies are replenished every day. Each guest gets a set of towels, a soft bathrobe and slippers.

The rooms are cleaned twice a day, and therefore no one will have to complain about the cleanliness of the rooms. Lingerie is changed twice a week.

Dining at the hotel





Booking places in the Riviera Beach Resort Spa 5 * hotel, tourists themselves can choose the appropriate meal plan. For example, you can only have breakfast at the hotel or pay for a full meal. In any case, they are waiting for you in the restaurant, which offers a huge selection of international, Vietnamese and Asian dishes. According to reviews, the dishes are always fresh and tasty, and the portions are large.

The hotel also has a bar with a stylish atmosphere and a relaxed atmosphere. Here you can sit with friends, relax with a glass of strong drink or enjoy aromatic coffee. By the way, you can eat in the city - there are cafes, bars and restaurants for every taste.

Hotel Riviera Beach Resort Spa 5 (Nha Trang): are there any conditions for a beach holiday?





Naturally, one of the reasons for such a high popularity of this country is the warm sea. What kind of rest can the guests of the Riviera Beach Resort Spa 5 * hotel expect? Nha Trang is famous for its clean beaches. One of them is located just 20 meters from the hotel territory - you will live right on the seashore.

The beach is well maintained, covered with soft white sand. Sunset at sea is comfortable, gradual. The coastal zone is cleared of algae, but the animal world is quite interesting. You can use comfortable sunbeds with mattresses, cool under the lovely thatched umbrellas. It is worth noting that all the furniture and appliances are new, as the hotel has recently opened.

Additional hotel service





Riviera Beach Resort Spa 5 * - a luxurious hotel complex, which is famous for its hospitality and a high level of comfort. There is everything in order to save guests from everyday problems.

Laundry and dry cleaning services are constantly available here. By the way, upon request, iron and ironing board are delivered to the room absolutely free of charge. You can also visit the gift shop where they sell original things, because you should bring a piece of Vietnam with you home.

There is internet access throughout the hotel. According to reviews, the connection speed is quite high. If necessary, you can visit the business center, where there are computers, fax, printer and other equipment. The hotel has a good library that you can use. There is also a conference room with the necessary equipment. Hotel staff can independently organize seminars, business events, holidays and even weddings.

Also, a free bus to the city regularly runs from the hotel, so you definitely will not feel divorced from life. And it is much more pleasant to relax in the evenings, enjoying the sounds of nature, and not the city noise.

Leisure and entertainment travelers

What in terms of entertainment can the Riviera Beach Resort Spa 5 * hotel offer? Vietnam is a good place to spend a vacation. It has everything you need for a pleasant, leisurely pastime.

In the courtyard there is a large pool, next to which there are comfortable sun loungers. There is also a small bar where you can always order a refreshing drink. You can have a good time on the tennis court. There is also a specially equipped area for yoga and tai chi. You can steam up in the sauna and go through several sessions of really good massage. Do not forget about excursions, as in the vicinity there are many interesting places where tourists have a good time.

We should also talk about amenities for children. For example, you can count on a high chair in a restaurant and an extra bed in your room. There is also a large, comfortable pool where children of different ages can splash around, even kids. Your child can have a good time in a mini-club, where there are many interesting toys and, of course, a professional nanny. Hotel Riviera Beach Resort Spa 5 * is perfect for family holidays.

Guest reviews about the hotel





Naturally, you should first find out what exactly tourists think about this place, who have already managed to spend some time here. What do they think about the hotel Riviera Beach Resort Spa 5 *? Reviews are exclusively positive. The hotel is new, with an interesting design and modern design. The territory is large and green, the rooms are spacious. Cleaned here twice a day. By the way, according to reviews, the service is at the highest level - all employees are polite and smiling, devoted to their work.

The food is very good - the dishes are always fresh and tasty, and the portions are large. The hotel provides guests with a free shuttle to the city, where you can walk, eat, have fun shopping. In short, this hotel is perfect for a relaxing beach holiday in the bosom of incredibly beautiful nature.
