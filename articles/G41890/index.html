Federation Tower: Height of Federation Tower

In Moscow, on the street Krasnopresnenskaya (embankment), a huge fantastic structure was built, striking not only with its grandeur, but also with the image of something amazingly cosmic, airy. This is one of the buildings of the most modern district of the capital - the Federation Tower. Its height is 95 floors.

Federation Tower is one of the most ambitious projects of the well-known company Mirax Group in recent years. This is a magnificent business center, distinguished by due quality and thoughtfulness of all implemented solutions using the most modern technical innovations.

Federation Tower: height, description

Federation Tower is located on the territory of Moscow City. In Europe, it is one of the highest modern buildings.

Not so familiar with the appearance of Moscow is such a height of the Federation Tower. "Moscow City" includes two skyscrapers "East" and "West", united by a common six-story stylobate. The floors of this grandiose complex housed beautiful elite apartments (residential) and modern offices in their premises.



The entire structure covers an area of ​​about 10,000 square meters. meters. The project area (total) is 443,000 square meters. meters.

The height of the "Federation Tower" in Moscow under the name "West" is 243 meters. The tower with 95 floors Vostok has a height of about 374 meters. This is one of the highest skyscrapers not only in Russia but also in Europe. It should be noted here that even in the tallest building on the planet “Burj Khalifa” today there are 10 less elevators than in the Russian Federation Tower, in which there are 67 of them.

Impressive and magnificent "Federation Tower". The height with a spire, the construction of which was abandoned over time, would be 506 meters. According to the project, it was supposed to be “Tower C”.

Location

The building is located on the 13th section of Moscow City, the most modern metropolitan business center. The nearest metro stations in this territory are Mezhdunarodnaya, Delovoy Tsentr and Vystavochnaya. The height of this unique structure allows you to see it from almost all sections of the city of Moscow.



Builders, clearance

The builders of the project are ZAO Federation Tower. The project itself was developed by architect Sergei Choban, together with his German counterpart Peter Schweger.

The project constructor is the company Thornton Tomasetti (American), which at one time was engaged in work on projects of 6 buildings, which were included in the list of the 10th World's Tallest Buildings. Also, this company was developing a project for the huge Kingdom Tower, which is now being built in Saudi Arabia. This building after the construction will be the highest on the planet. Over 1000 meters tower height. Compared to her, the “Federation” is a baby.





The contractor is a well-known company Renaissanse Construction (Turkey). On her account more than 500 structures and buildings, the total area of ​​which amounted to about 15 million square meters. meters in many countries: Libya, Austria, Turkmenistan, Switzerland, Russia, Belarus, etc.

The glazing of the facades of the complex was handled by the Chinese concern Yuanda, which is the world leader in the production of materials for these types of glass work.

Also, Australian, German, Swiss and other foreign companies took part in the construction.

From the history of construction

The laying of the West tower of the Moscow modern complex began back in 2006. Its construction was completed in 2008.

The Vostok Tower began to be built in 2007. It was built up to the 91st floor in 2014 (the height of Federation Tower at that time reached 343 meters). In December 2014, all the monolithic tower erection works were completed. Full glazing of the tower ended in the spring of 2015.



All engineering work was completed by the end of 2015 (December). The planned deadline for the completion of all work and commissioning of the complex is 2016 (summer).

Terrain infrastructure

The magnificent infrastructure of the business center is quite unique and diverse. The dimensions and height of the Federation Tower make it possible to accommodate many organizations for effective work and good quality rest.

For successful entrepreneurs, there are numerous excellent premium offices here . High-speed ultra-modern escalators and elevators provide quick access to various sites and institutions of the grand complex.

Offices and a shopping gallery are located in the stylobate zone of the structure. The main part of the Federation Tower territories is occupied by business offices: in the western tower - from 1 to 46 floors, in the eastern - 1-60 and 63-68 floors (magnificent sky-offices). 287 thousand square meters meters - the total area of ​​offices.

The 61st floor of the West tower is occupied by the Sky Club, and the 62nd floor is the Sixty restaurant. On the upper floors of the Federation Tower are luxury residential apartments. In the building "East" from 90 to 95 floors are platinum apartments. From the 95th floor of the building, beautiful panoramic views of the city are presented.

Apartments

The area of ​​all residential apartments in total is 78,000 thousand square meters. meters. The building of the complex "West" has apartments with an area of ​​80 to 350 square meters. meters with 3.5 meter ceilings.

The East Tower has residential apartments, the total area of ​​which is from 80 to 2300 (this is the territory of a whole floor) square. meters, with a ceiling height of 5.5 m.
Federation Tower is a state-of-the-art skyscraper with residential apartments in the very center of the Moscow City business zone. The height of the Federation Tower (Moscow City) is quite impressive.



In conclusion about the transport accessibility of the Federation Tower

Thanks to a well thought out location, access to the complex is ensured from all sides of the city. You can get to it through the Krasnogvardeisky passage, the Third Transport Ring, Krasnopresnenskaya embankment and Kutuzovsky Prospekt. Just three minutes from the complex there is Mezhdunarodnaya metro station.
