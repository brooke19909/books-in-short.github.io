Positive reviews

Many travelers are attracted to the Golden Ring Hotel (Moscow). Reviews about this institution are mostly positive:

  • the windows of the rooms open beautiful metropolitan landscapes (and from the upper floors the panoramas are simply fabulous);
  • cleaning, replacement of towels and linen is done every day;
  • incredibly convenient location - a few steps from the Arbat, as well as near the metro station;
  • just wonderful furniture - everything is new and orthopedic;
  • all cosmetics in the bathroom are reported daily (even if previous supplies have not yet run out);
  • daily in the minibar put a bottle of drinking water for each guest;
  • the bathroom has a huge mirror that is highlighted;
  • upon request, the maid will provide you with an iron and ironing board;
  • impressive luxurious classic decoration of the hotel;
  • quite affordable rates, as for a five-star hotel;
  • the bathroom is very spacious and bright;
  • friendly staff in the cafe;
  • The assortment of breakfast is quite varied.




Negative reviews

Guests highlight the following negative aspects of the hotel:

  • breakfasts are quite expensive, and therefore it is better to eat in the cafe next door;
  • disgusting soundproofing in the rooms;
  • the registration procedure, as well as the eviction from the hotel, was very lengthy;
  • the pedestrian approach to the hotel is almost completely blocked by a fence, which is very inconvenient;
  • hard pillows on the beds;
  • the staff behaves in a rather peculiar way with guests (dismissive, and sometimes even rude);
  • parking near the hotel is paid, and security is not very reliable;
  • get ready for the fact that you will bring your suitcases to your room on your own (although this is simply unacceptable for a five-star hotel).

General impression

It is worth noting that the Golden Ring Hotel externally produces the most favorable impression. Inside, everything is furnished in accordance with the classical style, and there are also luxurious decorative elements. Also pleased with the new high-quality furniture and modern appliances. Of the positive aspects, it is also worth noting the central location (in close proximity to the famous Arbat and other tourist sites). Nevertheless, despite the external beauty, lack of professionalism in service. The staff lacks basic politeness, and the reception staff sometimes behave arrogantly. The director of the Golden Ring Hotel (Moscow) should pay attention to this. And all this in a five-star hotel, where the cost of living is not so low. Speaking of price, it is very overpriced in terms of nutrition. Do not spend money on expensive breakfasts. in neighboring cafes and restaurants you can eat deliciously, and the bill will be about half as small.
