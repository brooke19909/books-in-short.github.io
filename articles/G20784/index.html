Fabric tulip workshop: four options

For craftsmen, the craftsmen use completely different materials: natural, scrap, fabric and satin ribbons, felt and lace. Many people like to decorate a room with flowers, making volumetric pictures from them or creating individually, for the subsequent decoration of a bouquet in a vase. In the article, we will consider what tulips can be made of fabric with your own hands, a master class on the manufacture of four different types of them. The presented single-piece flowers can be put in a vase, given to loved ones or thus to arrange gift wrapping. Also find out how to create a picture of "Tulips" from satin ribbons.

Voluminous buds

In order to sew such beautiful tulip buds from fabric, it is necessary to choose the fabric of the same color for the flowers themselves. It may have a small pattern, but the main background should be the same. For the stem and leaves, you need to choose a light green dense fabric, preferably such that its edges do not crumble. Felt will do. But you can also make them from satin fabric, but you will have to sew two patterns together on the wrong side and then turn them on the front side, ironing each part separately. If you have a lot of free time and a sewing machine, then this option is also valid.





A fabric tulip, the master class of which is presented in the article, is easy to do. It is necessary to draw a template on cardboard, so that later on it will make the same patterns of all colors. The pattern resembles a drop in shape. On the one hand, we see a semicircle that gradually narrows, and on the other, it turns into a sharp corner. Further, circling the contours of the pattern on the fabric with chalk, we cut out two patterns for each bud. On the wrong side, we outline a sharp edge, leaving the semicircle not sewn. Then the product is turned on the front side. In the hole in the formed pocket, you need to put filler. It can be cotton wool or synthetic winterizer. Then the parts are sewn together with the inner seam, but not to the end. Leave a small hole for the stem. It is sewn from two thin strips of fabric. In order for the flowers to stand in a vase, you need to insert a wire or a thin twig wrapped in cotton wool or sintepon inside. The stem is inserted into the tulip hole and sutured. It remains to sew a sheet, and a tulip made of fabric with your own hands is ready.

Stick flowers

A master class of a fabric tulip, which stands in a vase with a thin stick, read on. It is necessary to choose compatible colors for cotton fabric. It can be plain colors, or you can use a fabric with a different pattern. This is a matter of your imagination.





Sewing such tulips is easy. You also need to draw a template. Even the children in the garden can draw the shape of the tulip flower , so we will not tell adult masters how to portray it. Do not forget that you still need to leave at least 0.5 cm of fabric for allowances. Further, on the wrong side, we sew the part along the contours, leaving the bottom not sewn up. This is done in order to insert the filler. To fill in the corners of the flower, use the wand. When all the flowers are sewn, it remains to put them on sharp sticks. Instead of leaves, you can tie a bow from a satin ribbon of green color on them.

Rectangular Tulips

The next tulip fabric workshop is dedicated to a different cut. Rectangles are cut from the fabric according to the pattern, which are stitched on the sides. It turns out a pipe made of fabric. The upper part of the flower consists of three or four corners of the fabric, which are sewn in the middle.





The bottom of the flower after filling with synthetic winterizer is pulled together with a dense thread around the stem. Leaves and stems are made according to the same method as in the first description, so we will not repeat. Some craftswomen in the middle of the top so that the junction of several seams is not visible, sew a bead or pebbles.

Ribbon painting

A workshop of a tulip made of fabric for a picture is simple. You need to bend and rotate the usual red satin ribbon several times, creating the desired number of petals. Bottom and top they are sewn to the fabric background.





Stems are ribbons sewn from the bottom and top by folding the ribbons in the center. The stem is carried out by wrapping the tape on the wire. As you can see, creating masterpieces is easy. Get down to business, you will surely succeed!
