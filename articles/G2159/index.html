We are going to rest on the Blue Lakes of the Leningrad Region

Leningrad region is rich in various natural beauties. Everyone will find rest to their liking. Especially a lot of ponds in this region. According to various sources in the region, there are from 1800 to 41 600 lakes. The difference in calculations can be justified by the fact that the title "lake" was assigned depending on the area.

Thus, the Blue Lakes of the Leningrad Region are one of the very many reservoirs in the region. They got their name because the oval shape of the lakes and the trees surrounding it form a kind of mirror in which the sky is reflected. In clear weather, the water really turns blue, even azure. Most of the tourists who visited these places leave only positive feedback about the lake and try to return here again.

Rest on the lake

Marchenkovsky lakes are quite popular among tourists. Moreover, people come to them both in winter and in summer. In the warm season, tourists who like to soak up the sun, as well as children from the nearby Blue Lake Children's Health Camp, relax on the pond. In summer, a large number of people gather here.

The pond is also popular with fishermen. Blue lakes of the Leningrad region are inhabited by perch and roach. Cancers sometimes occur.

For divers, the Blue Lakes (Leningrad Oblast) are quite interesting. In some parts of the reservoir you can find a flooded landfill. Also, professionals have repeatedly found cars and even underwater mines. There is a flooded grotto here.

The floating island is popular . Enthusiasts are trying to make a real underwater training ground at this place in order to maintain the interest of visitors to dive in such reservoirs as the Blue Lakes of the Leningrad Region.

Around the same time, those interested in extreme fishing, using spearguns in their craft, begin to appear. They say that the catch here is pretty good.

Why are there so many tourists?

Fishermen and divers explain their interest in coming to the lake in the winter mainly with two things. Firstly, in the winter there are no children on the lake whose safety may be at risk during extreme fishing. Secondly, in winter the water on the lake is more transparent than in summer. In the warm season, visibility under water is reduced due to the flowering of the lake.

To summarize

About twenty minutes from the lake there is a ski base. Thus, after winter fishing or scuba diving, you can warm up by skiing. In general, we can say that the lake is popular among tourists all year round.

Everyone here is able to fully relax both physically and emotionally.
