Only a small part of what can be made from a bottle

Every year, 29 billion plastic bottles are produced in the world. Seeing these monstrous figures, a person who cares about his descendants and really evaluates his abilities will ask himself the question: "What can be made from a bottle that has been decomposing for almost 1000 years, that is, in fact, can be considered eternal?" Recycle containers, making them useful or just beautiful thing, everyone can do. So we will at least make the planet cleaner, and the interior of the house or cottage - more attractive.



What will you do first?

It turns out that there are a lot of caring and creative people in the world. Used plastic bottles make creative and useful things: pencil cases, needle cases, piggy banks, vases and frames, garlands, mobile phone cases, bags, brooms, flower pots. Among the most ambitious projects are a yacht and a floating island with a house. Bottles are built into the walls of buildings and make the basis of real furniture. In addition, ordinary plastic containers can be turned into an amazing interior element: various animal and insect figures, panels, children's toys and furniture for them, candle holders and other original decor items will please the eye. And you can decorate the porch and the cottage with colorful "eternal" flowers. Dare, because if we do not, our grandchildren will get only such flowers, and only plastic bees will sit on them.

Its screen curtain, made of the bottoms of plastic bottles, perfectly copes with the role of a partition and will serve you for many years. By the way, Michel specializes in practical eco-art. To make a contribution to a noble cause and create a curtain, you will need:

  • a lot of empty plastic bottles;
  • stationery knife;
  • wire for beads;
  • clean sand;
  • pan.

Step-by-step instruction:

To get an interesting and practical element of decor for a summer residence, you need to know exactly what can be made from a bottle, except how to throw it in a landfill? And most importantly - how to do it.

1) From a cleanly washed bottle, drain the remaining water and cut the bottom with a clerical or breadboard knife.

2) Scissors carefully trim the edges.

3) Fill the pan with sand, put on a fire and heat it.

4) Immerse the edge of the plastic bottle in hot sand for a few seconds. When it melts, you will see how the edge has become even and smooth.

5) String all the parts onto the wire. By the way, it can be replaced with fishing line.

In the same way it is possible to make curtains on the entrance country door or windows. Another tip: add a little brightness to the curtain. Multi-colored spots (bottoms of colored bottles), laid out in a pattern, enliven the room.

What can be done from plastic bottles to give more? Chandeliers and lampshades for fixtures, containers for seeds and seedlings, garden figures, irrigation and watering devices, a greenhouse and even a vertical flower bed!



And further

Do not take a long time to look for what can be made from a bottle. Turn on imagination and create masterpieces that have no analogues yet. Just remember to share your ideas with humanity later.
