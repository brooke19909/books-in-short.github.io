Light origami for children and adults

It happens that a child has nothing to occupy. Try to teach him how to fold light origami paper. We hope that this will seriously interest your baby. In addition, such an activity develops fine motor skills of hands, teaches patience, attentiveness, indirectly introduces the big world around. Children love to make something with their own hands. And adults, helping them, are pleasantly distracted from everyday affairs - so quietly and the evening will fly by!





Light origami: making the boat of our childhood

  1. Fold the usual sheet in half.
  2. Bend the upper corners to the middle.
  3. Bend the top layer of paper at the bottom rectangle.
  4. We repeat on the other hand.
  5. We bend these triangles inward and on the left and on the right.
  6. We repeat on the other hand.
  7. Pull the middle of the boat in different directions to combine the angles.
  8. Bend the lower triangle up, repeat on the other side.
  9. Pull, bending the bottom up.
  10. Spread it out - the boat is ready!

As a result, the child is satisfied, and you remembered the skills you had long forgotten from your childhood.
