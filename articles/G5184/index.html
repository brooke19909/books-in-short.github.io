Quantum human teleportation

Quantum is a very small indivisible particle in physics. Recently, many scientists have been experimenting precisely with the movement of these particles in time and space. If you manage to move a small particle, then everything else will turn out as well. More recently, Chinese and Canadian scientists have managed to teleport encoded information in particles of light.
