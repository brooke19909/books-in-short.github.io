City of St. Vlas, hotel "Sveti Vlas" (Bulgaria): reviews, description, attractions

The Black Sea coast, like a magnet, annually attracts millions of tourists. That is why numerous hotels are located throughout it. Each of them offers original rides, useful entertainment and provides truly exquisite service. One of these is the resort center of Sveti Vlas (Bulgaria). We will try to find out more about him, as well as the description and location.



About city

Before talking about the hotel, it is necessary to devote a few words to the city itself. It is located at the foot of the Horn of the Old Planina on the south side, in the famous Sunny Beach for many tourists. Its history goes back to antiquity. And the city received its name Sveti Vlas (or St. Vlas) in honor of the Slavic god Veles, the patron saint of trade. It seems that this saint still helps residents. So the city from year to year is becoming more popular. Although the status of a seaside resort he received in 1963. And one of his young services is the 3 * Hotel Sveti Vlas.

Bulgaria (reviews about hospitality do not need confirmation) is famous not only for its picturesque nature and healing climate, but also for its rich historical heritage.

sights

The settlement of St. Vlas was founded in the II century by the Thracians. Today, there are no longer preserved ancient monuments of architecture. However, not far from it is the town of Nessebar. In it you can visit the Old Metropolitan Church dating back to the 5th century. Its walls still keep the tradition that the church was erected on the ruins of the temple of Apollo. Four centuries younger is the New Metropolitan Church of St. Stefan. The ancient structure captures the achievements of Bulgarian and Byzantine architecture. We have survived the frescoes and carved iconostasis, the throne and the department of the bishop, preserved from the era of the Bulgarian Renaissance.



You can also visit the Church of St. Paraskevi and Christ Pantokratov. Their base dates back to 13-14 centuries. Both buildings have lavishly decorated facades using glazed ceramics and white stone. All these sights can be visited on your own or take excursions that Sveti Vlas offers to its guests (hotel, Bulgaria). Reviews about him are said to be one of the most attractive services in Sunny Beach.

Description

The hotel with the same name of the city is a modern low-rise building in several buildings. An elevator is provided for a comfortable lift. The interior can be called restrained, but very sophisticated, made in a marine style. The 48 comfortable rooms are equipped with air conditioning, satellite television, refrigerators, telephones with intercom, radio, pay Internet and minibars. Each room has a balcony-terrace and a bathroom. Perhaps sharing. An extra bed or baby cot is provided for this. Rooms are cleaned daily, towels are changed every three days, and bed linen once a week. Allowed guests with pets (up to 3 kg).

The hotel is located in a remote part of the city from the beach, approximately 500 meters. However, a walk to the sea may not seem boring. She is brightened up by the original assortment of shops and shops that are rich in Sveti Vlas, Bulgaria. Guest reviews, however, point out other advantages of this resort. For example, a modern amphitheater or marina of Marina Dinevi. The last structure is quite impressive in size and holds about 300 yachts. Boat trips are one of the most popular attractions in Sunny Beach.



You can get to the hotel from Burgas Airport, the capital of the region, located 35 km to the south. You can use a bus or taxi for this.

Services

The list of services that the Sveti Vlas hotel offers includes paid parking, a safe for storing documents and other valuables at the reception. Medical assistance is also available on site.

Among the activities there is an outdoor pool for children and adults. Sun loungers and parasols are provided free of charge. The pool has a bar with a rich menu of drinks.

Meals for the rest can be arranged in the hotel restaurant. At breakfast time there is a buffet. By the way, a special menu is provided for children, plus special chairs. Or you can use the services of local cafes and restaurants. However, it is difficult not to appreciate the variety of menus, the refined taste and originality of dishes that Sveti Vlas (hotel) can be quite proud of.



Bulgaria, reviews of tourists have repeatedly confirmed this, it is famous for its excellent cuisine, both national and clever use of European recipes.

Reviews

Sea, sun, beach - many world resorts can boast of such resources. But what attracts Russian tourists to rest in Bulgaria, "Sveti Vlas"? Reviews of experienced travelers note a number of advantages of local resorts. We share the most significant of them.

pros

  • The first item marks the hospitality and diligence of the hotel staff. Sometimes tourists stay at night or in the early morning, so it is very important to quickly accommodate by numbers and further service. Hotel Sveti Vlas 3 * (Bulgaria) reviews characterize as one of the most convenient and worthy services of Sunny Beach. In addition, the hotel is located in a quiet area with picturesque and well-groomed adjacent territory.
  • In second place was the cleanliness of the territory and hotel rooms. Cleaning and linen change in accordance with the rules. Given the fact that there are always not a lot of guests, the staff tries to serve everyone in good faith.




  • The next advantage is the kitchen of the Sveti Vlas hotel (Bulgaria). Reviews note a variety of menus and quality service. If you wish, you can arrange a romantic dinner on the balcony-terrace in the room. The restaurant staff will help with serving and meals.
  • And, finally, Russian tourists liked the picturesque view of the green reliefs and the sea from the rooms of the Sveti Vlas Hotel (Bulgaria). Reviews Dinevi Marina are noted as a particularly attractive place to relax. Walking along the waves of the Black Sea, fresh air, modern technology and the friendly sun have turned it into a local attraction.

Minuses

Despite the high quality of service, Russian tourists still found a couple of shortcomings:

  • The hotel, as mentioned, is located in a quiet area, and therefore there are no noisy entertainments like open discos and clubs. Rest in the hotel "Sveti Vlas" (Bulgaria) reviews of tourists relate to the family, complete harmony and comfort. And for young people, the choice of this service is unlikely to become permanent.
  • The lack of equipped beach is also saddening. Sun loungers, umbrellas and private shops with drinks are all that can be found on it. The rest of the entertainment is provided by the yacht club.




In general, the hotel "Sveti Vlas" is consistent with the status of three stars and has affordable prices for a comfortable stay on the Black Sea.
