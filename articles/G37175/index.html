The Emerald Cove Koh Chang 5 * (Thailand, about. Chang): description, photos and reviews of tourists

Thailand in recent years is gaining great popularity among tourists from Russia, tired of the traditional resorts of Turkey and Egypt. This exotic country attracts travelers with its original culture, pristine nature and unique cuisine. And here you can find the cleanest sandy beaches and ancient historical sights. At the same time, you can rent a hotel room for a small amount. However, for lovers of respectable holidays in Thailand there are luxury hotels. One of them is The Emerald Cove Koh Chang Hotel complex. We will tell you more about it in this article.

Features of rest on the island of Chang

Choosing the resorts of Thailand for the upcoming vacation, it is worth focusing primarily on its focus. So, the island of Chang is perfect for a relaxing and secluded vacation, lovers of pristine nature come here. However, he is completely unsuitable for nightlife. There are no bars and clubs, huge shopping centers, attractions and other entertainment infrastructure facilities. Therefore, the Emerald Cove Koh Chang 5 * hotel is more focused on a calm and measured rest.

Chang is the country's second largest island. Tourists, as a rule, initially fly to Bangkok, and from there they get to the resort. The easiest way is to transfer domestic lines to the plane. Flights depart from the airport 2-3 times a day. The average journey time is about 1 hour. You can also get on the ferry. To do this, you must first leave the capital to the marina. The road to it will take about 4-5 hours, and the journey by ferry will take tourists about 40 minutes.

The most popular beaches of the resort are located on the west coast of the island. There is much more developed infrastructure. For tourists, there are many restaurants and cafes serving local cuisine. There are also small shops, and souvenir shops, as well as massage parlors and tour desks. If necessary, you can visit the hospital, police station and branches of the local bank. Coming here, be sure to visit the local waterfalls: Klong Plu, Klong Nueng and the Congo cascade. Of the attractions, it is worth noting the famous elephant village, as well as Chinese and Buddhist temples.

General Hotel Information

The respectable complex The Emerald Cove Koh Chang 5 * is ideal for a relaxing holiday with your family. It was built in 2004 on the seashore and is located on a vast territory, whose area is 16,000 square meters. m. There are several buildings decorated in Thai style, an Olympic pool, a massage parlor and other infrastructure. Tropical trees, shrubs and flowers grow throughout the hotel. Tourists can enjoy nature while strolling along the cozy walking paths. The hotel has its own beach area. The hotel offers 165 rooms, differing in size and facilities.





Coming here, it is worth knowing that the local staff does not speak Russian. Most employees understand English, German or Chinese well. Upon check-in, guests will need an identity document with a photograph, as well as a security deposit for each number - 5,000 Thai baht (about 9,000 rubles). It is fully refunded after the end of the holiday and check the apartment. You can deposit it in cash or with a bank card.

Check-in is from 14:00 until midnight. However, if necessary, tourists can register ahead of schedule if the hotel has available rooms. You need to free your apartment on the last day of your vacation before noon. It is forbidden to bring any pets with you.

Location and nearby attractions

The complex Emerald Cove Resort Koh Chang 5 * is located on the west coast of the island of Chang, which is famous for the best sandy beaches and developed infrastructure. Since it was built right on the seashore, the road to the beach takes only a few minutes. To get to the pier, tourists will have to spend about half an hour. The hotel is in a quiet and secluded place, so you can‚Äôt quickly reach the central part of the resort.

The nearest airport is in Trat Province and is 25 km from the hotel. But most often tourists get to the Emerald Cove Koh Chang 5 * by ferry, which is moored to the pier. As a rule, the price includes a transfer, so vacationers do not have problems finding the way to the hotel.

There is a supermarket 200 meters from the complex, and a half kilometer away is a local restaurant and bar. Also, guests often visit the following attractions:

  • Klong Plu Waterfall (3.2 km);

Hotel Rooms

Amari Emerald Cove Koh Chang 5 * apartments have a large area and luxurious European-style interiors. All hotel rooms were completely renovated in 2014. 24-hour room service is available upon request. Housekeepers daily clean the premises, as well as change linens and towels. If necessary, tourists are provided with apartments for the disabled and non-smokers.

The housing stock of the hotel is represented by the following categories of apartments:

  • Deluxe Room - rooms designed for two adults and a child. They consist of a bedroom, a separate bathroom and an open terrace. Room size is 40 m2, and the balcony is 10 m2. Most of the apartments have mountain views, but rooms with side sea views are available for a fee.
  • Suite - spacious rooms in which 4 adult tourists can stay at once. They consist of a living room and a bedroom, connected by a common door, two separate bathrooms and two terraces. However, some apartments do not have balconies. The total area is 106 square meters. m. Windows offer panoramic views of the mountains or the sea.




Some of the Deluxe and Suite rooms are interconnected by interior doors, allowing tourists to settle in the rooms with two and three bedrooms.

Beach and Pool

The hotel Emerald Cove Koh Chang 5 * Ko Chang has its own stretch of coast, which is approximately 100 meters away. The beach is completely sandy, while it is regularly tidied up and thrown away garbage, so you can relax here even with young children. The sea is also clean, and the entrance to it is gentle. In addition, there is a coral reef near the hotel, so you can not only swim, but also go diving on the beach. All guests of the complex can use sun loungers and parasols free of charge, as well as take beach towels at the reception. Outsiders are strictly forbidden to enter the hotel.





The Olympic swimming pool with a length of about 50 meters is another pride of the complex. It is filled with fresh water, which is regularly cleaned. It is decorated with a massive figure of a Buddhist deity, and part of the reservoir is equipped with a jacuzzi. Near the pool there is a sunbathing platform, on which deck chairs with umbrellas are located. They can also be used for free. It works daily from 06:00 until midnight, so you can not only sunbathe here, but also lie down under the starry sky.

SPA treatments and sports

The Emerald Cove Koh Chang Hotel is designed for tourists who prefer a relaxing holiday, so there are many options for measured leisure. Separately, it is worth highlighting the spa, which is located directly on the coast of the sea. For a fee, vacationers can get a Thai massage course. However, one visit per hour is free. Also at the hotel for guests conducted group classes in yoga, water gymnastics and Thai boxing. You can also visit the gym at any time.

There are many options here for outdoor activities. For example, tourists can go diving, but they will have to pay separately for equipment and lessons. Playgrounds for playing beach volleyball and table tennis are open. Sailing equipment can be rented on the beach.

Other entertainment options

The Emerald Cove Koh Chang complex does not have its own entertainment program. Coming here, some tourists are disappointed by the lack of animation. Night discos are also not held here, therefore, in the dark, on the hotel territory, as a rule, it is very quiet.

Guests can relax in the games room, which houses computers and video game consoles. For tourists who are tired of the heat, there is a reading corner, equipped with soft sofas and air conditioning. True, the literature there is presented mainly in Thai, Chinese and English, so it will be difficult for guests from Russia to find a suitable book there.

Accommodation conditions with children

The island of Chang is perfect for family vacations, as it is remote from resorts famous for its vibrant nightlife. The Emerald Cove Koh Chang often hosts families with young children, who are always welcome here. Toddlers under 2 years old are accommodated in cots; they are provided free of charge.
