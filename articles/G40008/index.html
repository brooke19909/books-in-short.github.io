Side is a favorite resort of Russians. It is located on the Mediterranean coast. Located between Antalya and Alanya. It differs from other settlements of the country by a special measured life. Welcome!





Side's main industry is beach holidays. The city has dozens of hotels of various levels. Side Miami Beach Hotel is the best option for discerning and economical travelers. It was erected in 2002. The date of the last renovation is 2014. The total area occupied by the complex exceeds 4800 square meters. The hotel consists of two residential buildings. Number of rooms - 100.

The institution is focused on the reception of families with children and on the placement of youth companies. To the beach no more than three minutes walk.
