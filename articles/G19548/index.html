Climate and ecology

Dolzhanskaya spit is far from the sea. Due to its geographical location, the climate there is temperate continental. The sea heated during the summer softens the winter cold, and in summer the heat is not so much felt here. The sea breeze refreshes the heat. There are no large industrial enterprises in the entire okrug, so the ecological situation on Dolzhanskaya Spit is the most favorable. This natural formation almost closes the Taganrog Bay.

Between Dolzhanskaya (Russian Federation) and Belosarayskaya (Ukraine) braids - only forty kilometers. The water off the coast of the natural embankment is very clean, especially from the open sea. Dolzhanskaya village was founded in 1848 by immigrants from the banks of the Dnieper. Ukrainian Cossacks built a cozy village at the base of the spit, engaged in gardening and fishing. And now the population of the village is only incomplete eight thousand people. Therefore, in the peak season, the village may seem too crowded. Dolzhanskaya Spit beaches

The natural dune, which gradually rose above the surface of the water, was formed not only by the removal of alluvium into the sea by the mighty Don River. Tidal phenomena threw many broken shells onto the sandy shore. Dolzhanskaya Spit stretches for nine and a half kilometers. Its beaches are becoming deserted as they move to the edge. When you reach a place where the braid becomes very narrow, you can see a strange phenomenon. If on the one hand the sea rolls high waves ashore, on the other hand there is complete calm. Therefore, the Long Spit is so popular with windsurfers. Here you can always find the right wind and the right wave.

The beaches of the spit are composed of small shell rock mixed with river sand. Such a mineral composition makes staying on the shore not only enjoyable, but also beneficial for health. At sea, people sometimes find cures for healing mud. Therefore, you can often meet vacationers, smeared from head to toe in black and blue mass.



Leisure facilities

As already mentioned above, the village of Dolzhanskaya is located at the very beginning of the spit, on the mainland. It is a triangle with a pointed end. Therefore, in the village all housing, one way or another, is located close to the sea. But if you want to hear the sound of waves, and from two sides, you only have one place to look for housing - Dolzhanskaya Spit.

Rest in the village, as we see, is very diverse. Those who cannot live without modern amenities find housing in the village. And those who want to retire from the noisy crowds of vacationers will find nature untouched by civilization on the Long Spit. On the dune, made of shell rock, there are at least ten campsites. At the very edge of the spit, as its continuation with a dotted line, there are several islands. They live only seabirds. Great place for a romantic beach holiday.
