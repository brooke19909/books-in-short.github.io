Hotel Description Asia Hotel 3

Description: The popularity of Asia Hotel 3 is due to its convenient location and reasonable prices with very good service. The center of Bangkok, where traffic stops, shopping and office buildings are concentrated, can be considered an ideal place for businessmen, shopping lovers, as well as tourists who come for the purpose of cultural relaxation and independent acquaintance with the city.



Rooms: 650 rooms of several categories are located in a ten-story building of modern architecture. The rooms and suites with a classic design are equipped with all standard amenities according to the class of the hotel.

The rooms of Asia Hotel 3 include a seating area, flat- screen TV, minibar with lots of drinks, a fridge, a marble bathroom, a separate tap with drinking water, bathrobes.

Meals: The hotel can book apartments with the type of food "Breakfast" or "Half Board". The hotel has four restaurants of different gastronomic trends. The classic Thai restaurant is a favorite place for guests and citizens to dine. Rio Grill invites you to taste Brazilian treats, in Chinese they serve culinary delights of different provinces of China, Vietnamese delicacies with original taste are presented in Saigon Vietnamese.

In the evenings, Asia Hotel 3 guests gather in the lounge cafe to enjoy an exotic cocktail and listen to live music. During the day, you can always sit with a cup of coffee in the cozy Crystal Bar.



Additional information: The main vacation spot at the hotel is two outdoor pools with free sun loungers and beach towels. Other facilities include a tennis court and gym, spa and sauna. Traditionally offer sessions of the famous Thai massage. In the evenings, guests can enjoy discos.

Business services include the provision of meeting rooms and equipment, service and technical support for events. The hotel provides meeting rooms at various levels, equipped with an office center with photocopying and other equipment.

Guests of Asia Hotel Bangkok are provided with household services: hairdresser, dry cleaning, laundry. The hotel has a gift shop, travel agency, currency exchange, luggage storage, ATM, smoking area, wireless Internet connection. Free parking is available near the hotel.



There is preferential accommodation for children under 12 years old and the ability to take one extra or cot in the room. But the pet will have to be left at home - the hotel does not accept guests with animals.

Reviews: According to the feedback of tourists who had to stay at Asia Hotel 3, we can conclude that the hotel is in great demand among Russian travelers, due to the affordable price and ideal location in Bangkok, which especially attracts shop-lovers.

Near the hotel there is a metro station that allows you to quickly move around the city. Opposite there are numerous grocery stores and small Thai restaurants with amazing cuisine, and large shopping centers can be reached on foot in 5-7 minutes.

The hotel offers reasonable accommodation and good service at reasonable prices. It is believed that the Hotel Asia is the best choice for an unassuming client with average material capabilities.
