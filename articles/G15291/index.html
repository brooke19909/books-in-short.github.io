VON Resort Golden Coast 5 * (Turkey / Side): reviews, photos, hotel description

Side is just the perfect place for lovers of sea holidays. Hotel VON Resort Golden Coast. The gentle Turkish sun and high standards of service are all that is needed for an unforgettable vacation.





Hotel info

Resort VON Resort Golden Coast fully justifies its high five-star status. This hotel can be called the standard of quality service and comfort. Great attention is paid to equipping the rooms with all the necessary amenities for relaxation, as well as to the continuous development of infrastructure.

VON Resort Golden Coast has a wealth of gourmet opportunities to get to know the local specialties. You also have the opportunity to improve your health using traditional Turkish procedures.





Hotel location

VON Resort Golden Coast is located 12 km from Side in the incredibly scenic Cholakli area. The airport is 55 km away. The remoteness of the hotel from a number of settlements can be described as follows:

  • from Antalya - 65 km;
  • from Manavgat - 17 km;
  • from Side - 10 km;
  • from Aspendos - 25 km;
  • from Belek - 20 km.




Guest rooms at the hotel

Hotel VON Resort Golden Coast 5 * has a number of rooms in the amount of 404 comfortable apartments, which are distinguished by a gentle and calm corporate design. It is important that 4 rooms out of the total number are specially adapted for people with disabilities.

You can choose for yourself a number corresponding to one of the presented categories:

  • Standard rooms have several varieties that are designed to accommodate two or three guests. The room may have a double bed or several single beds. The possibility of additional placement on a cot or on a soft sofa is also allowed.
  • Family rooms have two possible configurations. The first type of apartment involves placing a king size bed in one room. Behind the decorative partition there is a children's area, in which there is a two-level children's bed. The second configuration implies the availability of separate rooms for adults and small tourists. So, in one of them is a large double bed. It also has a bathroom and a balcony. The second room is equipped with two single beds for children.
  • The suites are in many respects reminiscent of a two-room family, but have a number of distinctive features. In the VON Resort Golden Coast 5 * hotel these apartments have two bathrooms, as well as a spacious furnished terrace with a magnificent view of the clean, well-kept pool or the sea coast.




In the rooms

Each room is equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay at the VON Resort Golden Coast 5 * hotel. Photos taken by tourists who have been here earlier confirm the high level of living conditions. So, in each room of this hotel there are the following amenities:

  • a bathroom with a toilet and a shower cabin (some apartments have a bathtub);
  • electric hair dryer;
  • a telephone that has round-the-clock direct communication with the administration, as well as access to an international line;
  • TV, which shows many international satellite channels, including Russian;
  • mini bar;
  • electronic safe with a reliable combination lock;
  • the air conditioning system is tied to a common hotel;
  • Each room has a balcony or a large terrace.

Hotel Restaurants

A country with one of the most specific and unusual cuisines in the world is Turkey. VON Resort Golden Coast confirms its high level thanks to the professional work of chefs and restaurant staff. Hotel guests are invited to visit the following facilities:

  • Saffron is the main establishment of this hotel, where guests are invited to breakfast (early and late), lunch and dinner. The buffet format presents a real abundance of traditional Turkish cuisine and European culinary traditions.
  • "Kaftan Kebab" is a cozy restaurant where you can get acquainted with all the subtleties and features of the national fish soup. Here you can find the best Turkish dishes and drinks.
  • Beluga is the best place to try fish. Here it is always fresh and prepared only according to the most sophisticated recipes.
  • Haseki is an oriental restaurant. The interior, the menu, the music, the spicy aromas - all this helps you to immerse yourself in the heady fairytale atmosphere. Here you can order a hookah.
  • Longorn is a true meat lover's paradise. The menu of this restaurant presents a huge variety of steaks, as well as original sauces and drinks to them.

Hotel guests should keep in mind that visiting all restaurants except the main one is available only with prior reservation of the table.





Cafe on site

On the territory of the resort and hotel complex VON Resort Golden Coast 5 * (Side), guests have the opportunity to visit many cozy cafes where you can have a tasty snack at any time of the day. Dishes for every taste are presented in the following establishments:

  • L'atelier du chef is a snack bar that works especially for those who cannot fall asleep on an empty stomach. Throughout the night, this establishment treats its guests with delicious, but at the same time, light dishes.
  • Sydonia - opens the door for hotel guests in the afternoon. This snack bar works in a buffet format and offers a huge number of delicious snacks prepared according to original recipes.
  • Sofra is an incredible place in its atmosphere, which is always happy to treat its visitors with traditional Turkish gozleme cakes. Drinking this incredibly delicious dish is certainly worth a fresh Ayran.
  • Fragaria is a cozy patisserie. Here you can taste not only traditional Turkish sweets, but also enjoy all your favorite cream cakes and pastries. A great addition to such a sweet picture will be a huge assortment of varieties of tea and coffee.
  • "Aquapark" is a fast food cafe that operates in close proximity to a water attraction park. You can eat without stopping from entertainment.

Hotel infrastructure

Every year, millions of tourists from all over the world visit the resort town of Side. Description of the hotel VON Resort Golden Coast would be incomplete without mentioning the rich infrastructure of this resort complex, which includes the following facilities:

  • several conference rooms of different capacities, which can be equipped and decorated according to your needs;
  • a large spa treatment center, after visiting which you will feel completely updated;
  • You can find the necessary information and contact friends and family using the services of an Internet cafe;
  • a modern sports hall provides ample opportunities for those who monitor their health and figure;
  • at the hairdresser you can radically change your image;
  • Having visited the numerous shops operating on the territory of the hotel, you can purchase souvenirs, leather and fur products, as well as jewelry;
  • The attention of adults and young tourists is represented by 4 freshwater pools, two of which are open, and two are located in the spa;
  • 300 meters long beach equipped with umbrellas and sunbeds, the entrance to the water is extremely convenient and will appeal to both adults and children.

Negative reviews

Not without flaws even in such a prestigious hotel as VON Resort Golden Coast. Guest reviews contain a number of comments:

  • Despite the high status of the hotel, guests are not provided with slippers;
  • insufficient number of waiters who simply do not have time to serve all the guests;
  • poor connection quality and Internet speed (even subject to surcharge);
  • towels issued on the beach, mostly faded, washed, with holes and patches;
  • too strong smell of bleach in the indoor pool;
  • there are no fresh juices in the dining room (instead of them are instant powder drinks);
  • diluted alcohol.
