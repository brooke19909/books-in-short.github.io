How to make palm from plastic bottles for home decoration

Often there are situations when too many unnecessary plastic bottles accumulate in the house. Someone prefers to just throw them away. But, if you have the time and ability, you can turn them into something colorful that will diversify the interior of your home and cause the envy of friends. If you are a lover of exotic, then you will be interested to learn how to make a palm tree from plastic bottles.



You will need:

- a strong pin of about one and a half to two meters long and with a diameter slightly smaller than the neck of accumulated plastic bottles;

- three strong metal pins thirty to forty centimeters long each (depending on the size of your palm, these can be welding rods, quality knitting needles from umbrellas or long knitting needles);

- wire or wire for fastening the structure;

- plastic bottles. It is better to take two-liter, green for the leaves and brown for the trunk. Only the size of the resulting palm depends on the amount;

- if a wooden pole - short nails with a hammer;

- scissors;

- an awl or puncher with a thin drill.

Let's move on to how to make a palm tree out of bottles. It does not require much effort or unusual skills.



Basic training

There are many ways to make palm from plastic bottles. Perhaps the simplest and fastest is to string them through the neck on top of each other. First, wash the building material and remove the labels.

Beginning of production - leaves

Take the green bottles. They need to cut the bottom, and then cut them lengthwise into three parts, from the bottom to the neck, leaving to stay on the dense part of the neck with a thread.
