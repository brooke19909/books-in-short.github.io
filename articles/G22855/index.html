Costs

For trips to Europe, you should open a currency card in dollars or the United States. Ruble Visa Classic and MasterCard Standart abroad will accept. But the purchase will cost more due to conversion rates. What it is? If the invoice is issued in international currency (dollar, euro), the amount will be converted into rubles at the exchange rate of St. Petersburg. If the goods are paid in Swiss francs, then the figure received will once again be converted to the account currency. A commission is charged for each such transaction.

Do not also forget about the exchange rate difference. At the time of purchase, the amount in the account is blocked. Write-off occurs when the bank receives a confirmation of the transaction. The time difference takes several days, during which the course changes.

For the withdrawal of foreign currency from the ruble account, the commission is charged by the issuing bank that issued the card and the partner who serviced it. There are also limits on cashing money, which should be clarified before traveling abroad.

Security

Cases of plastic fraud are common, but extremely rare. The attendants are not able to duplicate the data. All operations take place in front of the user. The Priorbank currency card has an electronic chip, PIN and CVV codes. Additionally, users can activate the SMS-informing service on the movement of funds, impose restrictions on debiting amounts from the account during the day or in a certain country, etc. In theory, the bank is obliged to refund the stolen amount if the client has notified about the theft of money within 24 hours receiving SMS.
