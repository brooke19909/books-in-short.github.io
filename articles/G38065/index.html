Samui Island Beach Resort Hotel 3 * (Thailand, Samui): photos and reviews of tourists

Thailand is a country that attracts travelers with nature of incredible beauty and unique sights lurking in mysteries. On its territory there is a considerable number of resort areas with hotels in which travelers relax all year round. You can buy tickets to them in almost any travel agency - for this, it is enough to have the necessary amount with you, as well as an understanding of what exactly is expected from the vacation.

One of the most popular hotels in Thailand is Samui Island Beach Resort Hotel 3 *. Here, every vacationer can feel the excellent level of service, as well as relax in complete comfort and coziness.

general information

Samui Island Beach Resort Hotel 3 * in the ratings among hotels in Thailand often takes positions close to the leading ones. Its level, in accordance with international standards, is rated by three stars, but many tourists from Russia and the CIS countries very often talk about the fact that living conditions can be easily estimated at 4 *.

The hotel was built in 2005 and thanks to a short time of its existence at the moment it has a modern look, which is constantly supported by the restoration and repair work. During their implementation, not only the appearance of the hotel is always improved, it also replaces some elements of technical equipment with a more modern one.



Location

Samui Island Beach Resort Hotel 3 * is located on the territory of Chaweng Beach on the picturesque island of Samui. The place where the hotel is built is quite favorable in terms of climate, since it is here that you can relax throughout the year. However, many travelers in their reviews of Samui Island Beach Resort Hotel 3 * recommend going here from December to March. According to them, the conditions prevailing on the island during this period are the most optimal for a beach holiday. So, in the winter season you can enjoy the maximum number of sunny days and hot weather, significantly mitigated by the moist air, the flows of which come from the ocean.

On the photo of Samui Island Beach Resort Hotel 3 * you can see that the hotel is surrounded by beautiful natural landscapes that can captivate tourists from different regions of the world with their beauty. There are tropical gardens that delight travelers throughout the year, and on the other hand offers magnificent views of the vast oceanic expanses.

Not far from this place is the Big Buddha and Hin ta Hin Yai - world famous historical monuments. Five kilometers from the hotel there is a small fishing village where you can go for inexpensive shopping - locals can sell not only exotic seafood, but also original souvenirs.

The nearest airport is five kilometers from the island. All tourists who arrive in it are forced to get to the place of rest by water. That is why the hotel offers a paid transfer service for each ticket, within the framework of which a motor boat delivers guests to their destination.

Rooms

The Samui Island Beach Resort Hotel 3 * has a small fund of rooms - it consists of 62 apartments, which are ready to receive guests at any time in their comfortable conditions. Depending on the options offered, they are divided into several groups: superior, deluxe and family suite. In addition, their part is located in separately built bungalows and villas - they are located separately from the main four-story building, in which apartments of the first categories are located. Separate building is reserved for rooms of the family suite and deluxe groups.

All apartments have a bathroom equipped with a set of modern sanitary ware. All of them are combined with bathrooms and contain in their small area a place for bathing (a bathtub with a jacuzzi or shower), as well as a washbasin. There are also small cabinets in which the guests are waiting for bath accessories, designed for each individually. Among them are bathrobes, towels, slippers, and soap products needed to maintain hygiene.

Guests of all apartments can use the climate equipment with the possibility of individual control. Thanks to their presence, each vacationer has the opportunity to independently configure the most suitable temperature conditions for themselves in the room, regardless of the weather on the street.

All apartments have a private balcony from which you can watch the surroundings of the hotel.
