What is a smart apartment: features of the layout, living space, the pros and cons

Smart home sales announcements today are not uncommon. Despite the fact that the term appeared relatively recently, it managed to become an integral part of modern reality in a short time. What is it, and how is the living space in a smart style different from the usual?

Definition

Translated from English smart - smart, smart, clever. A distinctive feature of the "smart" interior is the rational use of space and its functionality.

Differences between smart apartments and ordinary housing are noticeable at the planning stage of the house. In most cases, developers in the design try to cover a large number of square meters, resulting in elongated rooms with a single window. Such a layout does not allow you to conveniently arrange furniture, and the space needs sunlight. Such apartments are characterized by a large number of panoramic windows located along the facade.

Layout

Designers of smart apartments in Kiev and other cities are engaged in the development of designers who put themselves in the place of future residents: they determine a convenient place to place a washing machine, a mirror, the number of squares for a berth. This smart apartment does not require reworking the project and already has a high-quality finish, which eliminates the need for repairs.

When planning a smart apartment, as a rule, furniture of standard sizes is taken into account. Many developers offer to buy an apartment in Kiev, already equipped with furniture. For those buyers who have a limited budget, such offers can determine the real cost of housing without spending money on an independent home environment. Built-in furniture is an integral part of a smart apartment, allowing not only to save free space, but also creating an attractive interior. Bulky cabinets-walls have long sunk into oblivion.

Disadvantages of acquiring smart housing

It is impossible to say that such smart apartments are ideal: the main pitfall is the lack of free space for a large family. For one person or couple, such a property is the best option.

According to experts of real estate companies, the disadvantage of smart apartments is cramped. Disadvantages of smart housing:

  • the cost per square meter is more expensive than in ordinary apartments;
  • if the property is in a non-residential fund, you will have to pay utility bills at a commercial rate with the addition of a land tax;

Despite all the disadvantages, it is worth noting that such smart apartments are popular and quite in demand.

The benefits of smart housing

In addition to the minuses, it is worth considering the advantages of smart apartments:

  • minimum cost of housing in the real estate market;
  • apartments are mostly located in the new building, which is important;
  • minimal losses of electricity and heat, savings on utilities;
  • housing quickly pays off when it is rented out;
  • lack of partitions visually increases free space
  • saving on repair work;
  • alternative to rental housing.
