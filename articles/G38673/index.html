The waterfall is one of the most attractive sight for travelers around the world. The power and majesty of the overthrowing element has a bewitching effect on people.

Among the splendor of the Caucasus Mountains, in the vicinity of the sunny resort town of Kislovodsk, in the picturesque gorge of the Alikonovka River, one of the most beautiful natural phenomena, Honey Falls, can be observed.



Caucasian legends

Beautiful names of these natural wonders owe to ancient legends. According to local legends, once among the rocks of the waterfall in the hollows and crevices, wild bees built honeycombs, collecting nectar from honey plants, which are rich in the slopes of the gorge. Torrential rains and spring floods washed away the hives, staining the rocks with amber droplets. The water became sweet, and the gorge was filled with a delicate aroma of honey. The bees died out long ago, unable to withstand the harsh winters, but the name remained.

According to legend, once the daughter of a noble prince, contrary to the will of her father, fell in love with the common shepherd Ali Konov. In honor of the young shepherd, the river is also named, as well as the settlement, which was formed later. Honey Falls: how to get there?

Traveling to the amazing lands of Kislovodsk is not so difficult. On the highway, the distance from Pyatigorsk to Kislovodsk is 43 km, which can be overcome in forty minutes. Also, electric trains go there every hour (journey time is 57 minutes), the departure point of which is the city of Pyatigorsk. Honey waterfalls are located about 17 km from Kislovodsk, near the village of Krasny Kurgan. This path can be done on foot or by car, passing the border with Karachay-Cherkessia. Do not worry about this. No one will check documents. Pointers with the inscription "Honeyfalls" can be found immediately after the sign "Karazhaevsky Konzavod". They will help you navigate among the mountain landscapes. The top of snowy Elbrus adorns the green panorama of this region. A road tape leads through the field and descends into the gorge Alikonovka. The view offers an incredibly picturesque panorama: the walls of the Dolomites from one edge of the tract and thickets of hazel among the rocks and lowlands - on the other side.



Honey Walk

Sightseeing of famous sights - a group of waterfalls of the Alikonovsky tract, begins with a descent through an arch made of stones. You need to go into the narrow aisle along the stairs between the rocks, the so-called "Mother-in-Ribs."

At first, tourists see the second largest (6 meters) Zhemchuzhny waterfall. Snow-white large splashes of this powerful source, sparkling in the sun, like strands of pearls, undoubtedly delight the eye. Crystal clear, this wide cascade resembles the magnificent skirt of a bride's wedding dress!

Then, the Secret Waterfall continues the acquaintance with the sights, which quietly paved the way under a huge boulder. In some sources, it is called "Girl's Braids" (for its interwoven jets of three streams).

Further, the path leads to the Snake waterfall, which, meandering, slides along a steep rock on the other side of the boulder, playing loudly with cold spray.

Walking to the biggest waterfall

A bridge has been built at the largest cascade, on which you can walk to experience the chilling freshness of crystal splashes and take stunning photos of Honey Falls, which are beautiful at any time of the year! In the summer, they will give coolness on a sultry day and cheer up their "music." In winter, tourists are struck by bizarre compositions of frozen power and mysterious silence. Autumn with its colors brings a new breath to the picturesque panoramas of nature. And this has its own charm. In addition, in autumn, as in spring, water flows are fuller and more impressive.



The healing power of the elements

The stream, dropping from a height, hits a stone bed, above which clouds of water dust form. Sunlight, playing with crystal droplets, draws rainbow paintings. On a bright cloudless day, you can see dozens of rainbows. Their beauty has healing power. The magic drops of a falling veil under the rays of the sun create electric charges. So, Honey Falls with cool air, saturated with negatively charged ions, have a miraculous effect on the human body, nourishing vigor and magical energy. It is very good for restoring strength and strengthening the immune and nervous systems.
