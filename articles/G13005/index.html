Vietnam (Nha Trang): reviews of tourists about a wonderful and healing holiday

One of the most visited resorts in South Vietnam is Nha Trang. The city is located 1260 kilometers from Hanoi and 450 kilometers from Ho Chi Minh City. There is little rainfall both in winter and in summer, with the exception of the first half of October. The climate and landscape are reminiscent of the Mediterranean. Those who choose a vacation at this resort will receive a lot of unforgettable impressions from a trip to Vietnam (Nha Trang). Reviews of tourists show that it is an ideal place for lovers of outdoor activities. In addition, there is an extraordinary beauty of the turquoise sea, many islands with coral reefs, excellent sandy beaches and an interesting underwater world.



How to get to this resort? The easiest way is air travel. If you fly from Hanoi Airport, it will take 1 hour 40 minutes, and if you fly from Ho Chi Minh City - 1 hour. Vietnam (Nha Trang), reviews of tourists confirm this, has long been popular not only among local residents, but also among European tourists. And in recent years, he has gained great popularity among Russian tourists. Due to the fact that their number is very large, a visa is issued at the Nha Trang airport upon arrival. In addition, the city has many Russian-speaking travel agencies, shops, hotels and cafes, which will not be difficult to find, since all the signs are duplicated in Russian.



But a beach holiday and a trip to shops and restaurants is not all that you can do when you come to rest in Vietnam (Nha Trang). Reviews from tourists in 2013 show that several excellent diving centers work here for city guests. Guests of the city can visit the longest cable car that connects the resort and the nearby island of Hong Che, which has an excellent amusement park for adults and children. And inside an artificial mountain there is a night disco.

To accommodate guests, hotels of various categories are offered, many of which have their own massage parlor and SPA complexes. The beaches of the resort are mostly municipal. The exceptions are those that belong to hotels with a category of 5 stars. Unlike public beaches, they are fenced and equipped with everything necessary.



And this is not all that Vietnam (Nha Trang) is famous for. Reviews of tourists show that this resort is also therapeutic. The city has a beautiful clinic offering treatment with mud and mineral water Thapba. The unique combination of biologically active organics, trace elements, salt solution under the influence of heat has an antibacterial, anti-inflammatory and cleansing effect on the human body. A mud treatment activates the body's defenses and boosts immunity.



What else attracts tourists to Vietnam (Nha Trang)? Reviews of tourists tell about the many attractions that you can see in the city during your vacation. So, a few kilometers from the resort, on the hill are the Tyam towers of Po Nagar, the construction of which dates back to the reign of the principalities of Champa. The most popular attraction of the city is the Longshoib Pagoda, built at the end of the 19th century. Here, tourists can explore the beautiful sanctuaries decorated with dragons. Behind the pagoda, you can see a huge white Buddha sitting on a lotus flower. Another structure that is recommended to see is the Nha Trang Catholic Cathedral, located near the railway station.

In addition to the above, there is something else that can be done for a change, having arrived in Vietnam (Nha Trang) - excursions. Reviews of tourists show that the most popular is an excursion to the Institute of Oceanography. No less impressive is the trip to Cape Hongchong, located 3.5 km from the resort. In addition, various trips are made to nearby islands.
