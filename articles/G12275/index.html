Romeo Beach Hotel 3 * (Kemer, Turkey): hotel description and reviews of tourists

One of the most popular resorts on the Turkish coast, both among Russians and among people from other countries, is Kemer. Travelers here are attracted by a well-developed tourist infrastructure with an abundance of restaurants, bars, nightclubs, discos, shops, hotels, etc., as well as magnificent beaches and the clear azure sea. If you want to stay in the very center of Kemer, within walking distance of all the attractions offered here, and are looking for an economical and comfortable accommodation option with your own beach, then pay attention to Romeo Beach Hotel 3 *. We offer today to learn more about this hotel, as well as find out what impressions were left with our compatriots who had already been here.





Where is

This hotel boasts a very advantageous location. So, it is located just one kilometer from the center of a large resort city of Turkey - Kemer. Thus, all the various objects of tourist infrastructure for hotel guests are located within walking distance. The hotel has at its disposal a private pebble beach. It is only 200 meters away. The beach here is small. However, the hotel can not be called large, so everyone can easily fit on the available sun loungers.

How to get there

The closest harbor to Romeo Beach Hotel is near Antalya. The distance to it is 55 kilometers. Thus, after arriving at Antalya Airport, the path to the hotel will take you just over an hour. If you travel with the assistance of a travel agency, then after boarding you will probably be offered a transfer to the hotel on a comfortable bus with a guide that tells about the history, culture and features of Turkey in general, and Kemer in particular. If you send on a trip yourself, you can get to your hotel by bus. However, there is no direct bus route between Antalya Airport and Kemer. Therefore, first you have to get to the center of Antalya, where you can catch a bus to Kemer. If this option is not suitable for you, then there is always the opportunity to use the services of a taxi or book a transfer at the hotel.





"Romeo Beach Hotel" (Kemer): photos and description

This three-star hotel was built in 2008. In 2012, a large repair was carried out here. The hotel consists of a modern, elegant five-story building, equipped with an elevator, and includes 30 comfortable rooms. The area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe hotel's own territory is 930 square meters. meters. Here, tourists can enjoy the restaurant, terrace, garden, TV lounge and shop. In addition, a couple of minutes walk is the hotel's own beach.

Hotel Policies

As in most other hotels from different countries, check-in for arriving tourists is carried out after two in the afternoon. If you arrive earlier than the specified time (for example, in the case of an early flight arrival time), then the administrators will not make you wait in vain and, if there are free rooms, will populate immediately. If, at the time of your early arrival, rooms have not yet been vacated by previous guests, then you will have to wait. Doing this in the lobby is not necessary at all: you can leave your suitcases at the reception and relax in the hotel garden, take a walk in its surroundings or go directly to the beach. On the last day of your stay at Romeo Beach Hotel, you should leave your room before noon. At the same time, you will be asked to pay for accommodation and additional services. This hotel accepts cash payments as well as American Express, Mastercard and Visa cards.





Family tourists

This hotel is always warmly welcomed by tourists staying on vacation with their children of all ages. Moreover, if you place a child under four years old in your room on existing beds or a baby up to two years old in a crib, you do not have to pay extra for this. Please note that this room cannot accommodate extra beds. If you need a playpen for a child under two years old, then it is recommended that you inform the hotel in advance and wait for confirmation from it.





Tourists with pets

If you belong to the category of people who are used to taking your four-legged pet with you everywhere, including on trips abroad, then this information will not be superfluous for you. So, in accordance with the rules of Romeo Beach Hotel, pets are strictly prohibited on its territory. Therefore, you will either have to part with your pet for a vacation in Turkey, or try to find another hotel with a more loyal policy for tourists traveling with our smaller brothers.

Number of rooms

As already mentioned, the hotel's housing stock is represented by 30 rooms located in a modern five-story building equipped with an elevator. All of them are stylishly decorated and equipped with all necessary high-quality furniture and fixtures. Each room has its own bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer, air conditioning, a small refrigerator, a TV with satellite channels (including Russian channels), a telephone and a safe. Some rooms also have balconies. Rooms are cleaned every day. Maids renew linen and towels twice a week.





Nutrition

When booking a room at Romeo Beach Hotel, you can choose the right meal plan for you: half board or all inclusive. Three times a day a buffet is served in the hotel restaurant, which presents a variety of dishes of both national Turkish and international cuisine. The hotel also has a bar with a wide selection of drinks for every taste.

Beach vacation

As already mentioned, the hotel in question has its own beach. It is pebble and is only a couple of hundred meters from the hotel. There are sun loungers and parasols, the use of which is free for hotel guests.





Entertainment and infrastructure

Despite the fact that Romeo Beach is a very small hotel, it has everything you need for a comfortable stay. So, the reception desk is available around the clock. Turning to the administrator, you can order a taxi, transfer, excursion. The hotel also provides room service. The entire hotel has free Wi-Fi.
