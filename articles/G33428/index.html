Residential complex "Birch Alley"

In the picturesque Ostankino district of Moscow, a residential complex (LCD) "Birch Alley" is being built. The quarter of comfortable apartments is located near the Yauza River Valley natural park, Sviblovo estate and the main botanical garden of the Russian Academy of Sciences between Yaroslavl and Altufevsk highway.

A bit of history

In 2015, the developer of the residential complex ‚ÄúBirch Alley‚Äù entrusted the sale of its apartments to a third-party company. But in the spring of 2017, it was decided to sell the apartments on their own. Previously, the complex was renamed the "Legendary Quarter on the Birch Alley."

Each building is named. Moreover, it was the name of the famous athlete (and even now alive).

Construction stages

According to the documentation, there are only two of them. The first will be the delivery of an eighteen-story building with the name of water polo player Evgeny Grishin. Its settlement will begin at the end of the winter of 2018.

The second phase of the construction of the Birch Alley complex should be completed by September 2019. By this time, three buildings with 18 floors and one seventeen-story building will be built.

Technology

Houses are being built using modern monolithic technology. The facades are made ventilated, and the upper floors have panoramic glazing. On the front walls of buildings thought-out boxes for external units of split systems.

Each building is planned to be equipped with a modern engineering system, the work of which will be carried out automatically, but under the control of the dispatch service. Each apartment is supposed to have internet, television and telephone. For personal cars of tenants reserved underground parking.





Observation is a mandatory attribute of the LCD "Birch Alley". It will be around the clock, which will ensure the necessary level of security for residents of the complex.

Interesting and unconventional landscape ideas have already been thought out for the courtyard. Paths are planned to be made both pedestrian and bicycle.

You can choose apartments, not only by cost and footage. The developer offers other attractive options. For example, studio apartments are designed as a small area (32 m 2 ), and quite significant (51 m 2 ). Three-room apartments win thanks to a successful layout.

Social sphere

The Botanichesky Sad metro station, the closest to the complex, is just 10-12 minutes of leisurely walk. There is also a light metro in the area. She walks from the VDNH station. The completion of the construction of the Small Ring Station of the Moscow Railway is also planned for commissioning the first building. It is planned as a single transportation hub. A school, a kindergarten and several chain stores are also within walking distance. If there is a need for global purchases, then you can visit the center of the "Golden Babylon". To him by car go no more than 10 minutes.





On the first and second floors of the residential complex "Birch Alley" will be located gyms, cafes, hairdressers, shopping centers, etc.

By the end of the summer of 2019, it is planned to open a fitness center in the residential complex. But the hallmark of the quarter will be the Center for Water Sports. It will have many sections for children and adolescents, as well as a sports school of the Dynamo water polo club, a large pool and an arena for international competitions.

The organizers of the Center offer children to study for the first year without paying a fee, but further benefits will be provided only to young athletes who show outstanding results. The rest will have to pay the full cost of classes.

The territory of the "Birch Alley" is planned to be closed, so an electronic transmission system will be organized.
