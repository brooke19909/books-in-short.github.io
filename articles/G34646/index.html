Muine Resort 2 * (Vietnam). Reviews, photos

It's no secret that the resort area of ​​Phan Thiet is considered one of the most popular tourist destinations in Vietnam. Of course, there are a sufficient number of hotels of various categories here - each traveler will be able to find something suitable. And a pretty good place is a small hotel Muine Resort 2 *. So where exactly is the hotel located? What conditions can be expected? What do other travelers say about this place?

Hotel Muine Resort 2 * on the map: location

It is immediately worth noting that this hotel complex has a very advantageous location. Hotel Muine Resort 2 * is located on the first coastline just a few meters from the beach in the northern part of the resort area of ​​Muine. The center of Phan Thiet with all its attractions can be reached in fifteen minutes. The international airport is located in Ho Chi Minh City about 200 kilometers away. But do not worry about the distance, since the hotel, like most travel companies, provides a shuttle service.





Brief description of the territory and infrastructure

The hotel complex Muine Resort 2 * has its own small territory. Despite its relatively modest size, it is well-groomed, planted with flowers, shrubs, palm trees and other exotic plants - in short, the surrounding beauty of nature pleases the eye.

Approximately in the center there is a multi-storey building, where a restaurant, bar and other office rooms are located. Throughout the hotel there are small cozy one-story bungalows and two-story villas. Thus, peace and personal space will be provided to you.





Description of rooms for guests

This hotel is considered relatively small - there are 58 standard rooms. One-story bungalows are designed for co-residence of 2-3 people. The villas are two-story and have several bedrooms, they are perfect for people relaxing in a large company or family.

The rooms here are cozy, bright, with large windows. The floor is covered with quality laminate. Some rooms have a private balcony with garden or beach views, depending on the location of the building. Naturally, the room is fully furnished - there is a bed, a table, chairs, bedside tables, a wardrobe, a mirror, in a word, everything you need.





As for household appliances, guests are also provided with everything necessary. Each room is equipped with a modern air conditioning system, which helps to escape from the heat. In addition, there is a TV on which you can watch cable and satellite channels (popular music, as well as English). There is a telephone. The minibar in the room is replenished upon request, but this service is not included in the price. Room service is available around the clock, but again paid separately.

The bathroom has a spacious shower, sink, toilet, mirror. Each tenant is given a set of towels, hygiene and cosmetics, the stock of which is replenished regularly. The rooms are cleaned very carefully - the maids come every day and do an excellent job.

Power scheme

Immediately it is worth saying that the Muine Resort 2 * hotel offers its guests a one-time meal of the BB (bed and breakfast) type. Generous buffets are served every morning in the main restaurant, where everyone is free to choose their own food. It should be noted that the choice of dishes here is really large. For example, you can enjoy real Vietnamese culinary masterpieces or give preference to more familiar international cuisine. In addition, the hotel has a good bar serving refreshing drinks and exotic cocktails, as well as an outdoor barbecue area.





In any case, you should not worry about nutrition. On the coast there are lots of cafes and restaurants where you can have a decent lunch at a very affordable price. In addition, you can buy products at the supermarket or visit the city center.

Beach and water holidays on the coast

Of course, most tourists are interested in high-quality rest on the sea. And this hotel offers its own beach, which can be reached on foot in 2-3 minutes. The coast here is clean, well-groomed, covered with soft sand. The entrance to the sea is also sandy and comfortable. Stranded is suitable for bathing children.





Sunshades and wooden deckchairs with mattresses are arranged in rows on the shore - guests can use them for free. It is worth noting that this beach is quite secluded, so outsiders are unlikely to bother you.

If you are interested in a more active vacation at sea, then on the coast you will have the opportunity to ride a catamaran, yacht, boat, boat, water skiing, etc. By the way, this part of Vietnam is a real paradise for windsurfers, as the waves here are amazing .

Additional services

Despite the fact that the Muine Resort 2 * hotel is often called an ordinary guest house, there is everything you need to make a tourist vacation truly unforgettable. You do not have to worry about clean clothes - laundry and dry cleaning are constantly working at the hotel. Of course, these services are paid separately, but they are more than available.

There is also a small free parking for people traveling with their own vehicles. By the way, if you like to explore places on your own, then you can rent a car, motorcycle or some other type of transport at the hotel, which will greatly accelerate your movements.





Things unnecessary during your stay here can be taken to the left luggage room. The hotel has a currency exchange office, so you will not have problems with local money. An equally important advantage is the availability of constant access to the Internet, which in our time is very important. By the way, this service is completely free, and if necessary, you can even rent a computer for a while.

Leisure and entertainment

What entertainment does the hotel complex Muine Resort 2 * offer? Phan Thiet is a city famous for its hospitality, rich culture and ancient history. Therefore, you definitely will not be bored.

To begin with, it is worth noting that in the courtyard there is a wonderful pool where you can swim and refresh yourself. Sunbeds and umbrellas are placed here. If you wish, you can visit the billiard room - this service is paid separately.

Do not forget that in the resort area there are a lot of health centers and spa salons, where for a small fee you can relax and improve your health. What else can Phan Thiet offer? Excursions are a favorite way of spending time among many tourists. And, of course, in the vicinity there really is something to see. For example, just a few kilometers away is the ancient magical spring of Suoy Tien. In any case, the staff of the hotel tour desk will be happy to help you choose and buy a tour or make a route for an independent trip.

Hotel complex Muine Resort 2 *, Vietnam: tourist reviews

One of the most important aspects for people going on a trip is the opinion of tourists who have already managed to use the services of a hotel. It is worth noting that the reviews about this hotel are exclusively positive. Indeed, this complex has gathered all that Phan Thiet has to offer: the sea, a clean beach, interesting excursions, genuine hospitality, as well as delicious food and the breathtaking beauty of nature.





First of all, travelers note the convenience of the rooms - despite the fact that the rooms are not too large, they are extremely comfortable and clean. The food is also excellent - a large selection of dishes will not leave anyone hungry. A definite advantage is the proximity of the beach and, of course, the presence of its own pool. The hotel staff is always ready to help - by the way, they are fluent in English. In addition, the cost of living in the hotel is relatively affordable, especially considering the services provided. Relaxation in the bungalow will really appeal to fans of a quiet pastime in the lap of nature.
