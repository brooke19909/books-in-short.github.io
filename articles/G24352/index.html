How to remove a virus from your phone: a review of the best programs

Although viruses appeared immediately after the creation of computer systems and the Internet, they have become widespread in the last decade. Then it became clear that removing the virus is not always a matter of minutes. Sometimes you have to spend a lot of resources to overcome one small malicious program.

And if computers now become more resistant to such virtual attacks, then phones are not so confidently protected from viruses. Especially when it comes to more budget models in which security is rarely thought of.

Symptoms

Usually, users think about how to remove the virus from the phone at the moment when the device starts to periodically display errors and crashes. The first wake-up call will be the denial of access to some personal files. The task of most viruses is to redirect personal data to the destination. Therefore, they endlessly "rustle" through your folders in search of the necessary information.

The next problem that may make you think about how to remove the virus is a decrease in battery power. In this case, two outcomes: you noticed that the smartphone began to discharge many times faster, or the battery volume as a whole changed. In the first case, it becomes clear that the system has third-party programs that take a lot of charge. In the second - it is likely that malicious software has reduced the maximum battery charge.

One of the common variants of the manifestation of viruses in the phone is spontaneous downloading of something. The smartphone starts to install some files on its own, or launch a browser with advertising sites. Banners are constantly popping up right on your desktop. In this case, you definitely need to tackle the extraction of malicious software from the system.

Where do they come from?

Before, just in case, finding out how to remove a virus from Android, it‚Äôs better to figure out where it gets into the system. Many users believe that malware can get on the phone only through third-party programs on the Internet. That is, if you use the Play Market, then you will not encounter a similar problem.

In fact, this is an erroneous opinion. Unfortunately, recently all the software that gets to the Play Market is not checked. Of course, there are special programs that have been approved by many users. But if you install any other utility, you may encounter just such a problem.

But, of course, most often viruses come from the Internet. You went to some site to read an article, there you noticed an interesting link to another article, by clicking on which a download began. In this case, anything can happen. The phone starts to open all programs at once or starts the removal of the entire system. Or maybe the virus has quietly settled in the root of the folders and will gradually harm personal data.

Malicious software could settle on your phone because of a wicked joke of friends who threw a link on a social network to download such a program. Therefore, it is very important to be careful and once again double-check all third-party resources.

Programs

If for some reason the above methods are not suitable for you, you can try installing antivirus on your phone. The only thing in this case, it is important not to download more viruses with the programs. To do this, it is important to use only the proven utilities that are on the Play Markete.

Manual removal

Of course, this is the most desperate option, but sometimes it helps to cope with annoying malware. Only suitable if you understand the source of the virus.

Suppose that you installed a program or game and noticed that advertising banners began to appear actively with it. Perhaps problems with access to files have begun. Try uninstalling this program, preferably right away. It is better to remove it either from the Play Marketa, or from the "Applications" system menu.
