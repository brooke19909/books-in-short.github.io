Virgin Islands: extravagant vacation

The Virgin Islands are American possessions located in the waters of the Caribbean Sea , 120 km from the Puerto Rico Peninsula. The total territorial extent of these estates is 355 m2. On the territory are the largest islands: St. John, St. Thomas, Water Island and Santa Cruz, as well as a large number of small reefs and uninhabited islets of unusual virgin beauty.

You can travel to the US Virgin Islands at any time of the year - even climate (average monthly temperature + 22 C even in winter), an abundance of pristine nature, untouched by human activity make this place more and more popular. For those tourists who prefer the beauties of marine life, a wide range of entertainment is offered: windsurfing, sea fishing, diving, boat trips along the shores of the Caribbean Sea.

For sports people, excellent conditions have been created for versatile and active recreation: yachting, upscale golf courses, badminton, football, polo and much more. A tourist trip to the Virgin Islands is the best option for a beach holiday. Local sandy beaches (about 40) are considered the cleanest in the world. Tourism here is flourishing every year, the leisure infrastructure is developing, almost a third of the local inhabitants work in all the entertainment and cultural institutions of the island.

US Virgin Islands - a vacation for extravagant people. Here you can watch crab races, take part in costume carnivals, interesting and unusual rituals, festivals, take an excursion route to the beautiful and uninhabited Bucks Peninsula. First-class service in restaurants and hotels awaits you, you will get tremendous pleasure from shopping at local shops and will be delighted with the beauties of picturesque hills, bays with clear transparent water and cozy bays.

The Virgin Islands are also rich in history and cultural heritage, there is something to see and what to visit. For example, the fairly large island of St. Thomas is modern, comfortable and chic. While on St. Thomas, you should see the Charlotte Amalie Market Square, Fort Christian, Mount St. Peter and Blackbird Castle. Be sure to walk around exotic shops and shops, visit trendy nightclubs and just feel the classic American atmosphere. In the Caribbean, this is one of the most visited places, the tourism industry is quite developed here, so guests from all over the world come here.

If you want privacy with nature and want to have a good time, then come to St. John's Island. On its territory is the National Park, which is under federal protection. This island is a model of ecotourism in the Caribbean.

Between the two islands is the picturesque island of Santa Cruz. Here you will find a little bit of everything and entertainment, and detachment, and immersion in regional culture - this is a universal place of relaxation for those who want to combine business with pleasure. On the island you can visit the colonial town of Christiansted, the cleanest beaches with soft sand and the Kruzan winery.

The following activities are offered for tourists: twin-engine flight, diving, boat and yacht trips, sport fishing, windsurfing. The Virgin Islands is a great opportunity to pamper yourself with excellent service and a host of entertainment services.
