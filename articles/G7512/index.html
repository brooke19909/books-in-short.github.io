Short description

Blue Wave is a boarding house located in one of the most beautiful parts of the Crimean resort of Alushta, near the city embankment and Seaside Park. Here, not only the healing air filled with the aroma of juniper, but also a treatment center with many procedures located nearby helps to improve the body.

The hotel consists of two seven- and nine-story buildings, each of which has sleeping rooms. In addition, there is an own dining room with three meals a day, a bar, a gym, a sauna, a heated indoor pool, sports grounds, a dance floor, billiards, a library - everything you need to relax your body and soul. For guests who come here by car or plan to rent a car for sightseeing of Crimea, the pension has free parking.





Who is the Blue Wave Hotel for (Alushta) for? For families with children who want a budget vacation, but do not sacrifice comfort; for older people who enjoy walking in shady parks and wellness treatments; for those who follow their figure, because there are so many sports equipment, and the hotel food is described as healthy and even dietary. And, of course, for those who have long dreamed of visiting the peninsula, but previously there was no opportunity.

Location

The Blue Wave Hotel (Alushta) is located in the eastern part of the city, at a distance of three hundred meters from the Central Embankment. The road to it goes through a cool, lighted tunnel, which is so pleasant to walk on a hot day.

Beach

Blue Wave is a guesthouse that has its own pebble beach. The latter is equipped with the necessary amenities: changing rooms, a shower, wooden sunbeds, a first-aid post, umbrellas. Sheds may be subject to a surcharge.

Number of rooms

The Blue Wave Hotel (Alushta) offers one hundred thirty-five rooms of various levels of comfort. The following categories are distinguished among them: double and triple economy, double and triple standard, double comfort, as well as double junior suites and suites, consisting of two spacious rooms.





Each room has a TV, refrigerator, separate or combined beds. Starting from the standard category, rooms include a balcony with outdoor furniture. In the junior suite and suite you will find upholstered furniture and air conditioning. Each room has its own combined bathroom.





Please note that hot water is not supplied around the clock, as it is heated in your own boiler room. The serving time can vary: from seven or six in the morning to nine or eleven in the evening.

The health resort is located at the Golden Ear Hotel, which is four hundred meters from the Blue Wave Hotel (Alushta). For children

The Blue Wave Hotel (Crimea) is one of the best places on the peninsula for families with children. Young guests are welcome here from any age, a playground, a games room are organized for them, and the menu in the dining room is designed in such a way that all the food, with rare exceptions, is perfect for them.

Walking in the fresh air with the aromas of pine needles, sea and mountains, along with therapeutic procedures will strengthen the immunity of the kids, and sports games and communication with peers will allow you to not get bored of older children.

Terms of accommodation

The following rules apply at the hotel: check-in and check-out must be done no later than eight in the morning. Please note that a tourist fee is required upon arrival - it is one percent of the total cost of the stay.

Children under three years old without extra bed and meals are free of charge. Extra beds for adults can be in all types of rooms, except for a double junior suite in the first building and triple accommodation options.

Of the documents required a common passport, ticket and insurance policy. For children, grab a birth certificate and health certificate.

It is important!

If you plan to visit the treatment center, you need to bring a spa card with you, since you cannot apply for it on the spot. If you want to carry out treatment for a child, a card is also needed.

Weather in Alushta: when is the best time to go?

It seems that they have decided on the hotel, but everyone knows the fact that there is such a thing as a tourist season, and someone wants to find more hot sunny days, and someone wants more comfortable and cooler weather. The weather in Alushta during the swimming season varies significantly: if in May-June this is on average twenty-twenty three degrees (such conditions will be good for pensioners and young children, only in the latter case it is better to go a little later, when the sea warms up), then July-August - twenty-six to twenty-eight heat. August and the beginning of September are the highest tourist season, which means the most expensive vacation.





On average, a trip to a hotel in the Alushta Blue Wave resort costs from three thousand rubles per night in a standard room in June-July, for an improved one - from three and a half thousand, junior suite - from four, luxury - from five. In high season room rates per night can rise by one and a half to two thousand rubles.

Tourists reviews

Hotel "Blue Wave" (Alushta) reviews from travelers receive both positive and negative. The pluses include the proximity to the beach, comfortable rooms with a good repair, effective treatment procedures, reasonable prices, polite and helpful staff, daily cleaning, beautiful views, the availability of entertainment in the vicinity, a clean and well-groomed area.

Among the minuses, tourists noted the following: very small portions in the dining room, a lot of people on the beach who are not hotel guests, because of which there may not be enough sun loungers, poor sound insulation and an old tunnel leading to the beach.
