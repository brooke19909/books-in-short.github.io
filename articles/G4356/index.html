Phuket in October: tourist reviews

If paradise existed on Earth, it would be like Thailand. Its islands, and the whole country as a whole, are a very special world, in which everyone who comes here falls in love. Phuket is one of the largest and most popular islands in the country, where people from all over the world come.

For those who are coming here for the first time, the question arises of what Phuket is in October, whether it is worth going. The reviews of those who were in this place in the offseason give the most truthful picture of what tourists expect at this time of year.

Phuket Location

This island is the southern province of Thailand, and bridges connect it to the mainland. Its location at all times has been advantageous for travelers and merchants, since everyone who has traveled from India or to China has stayed here.

This Thai province includes 33 islands, the largest of which is Phuket. Nowadays, everything has been done here for a great vacation, from hotels and their infrastructures to various entertainments, festivals and shops.





One of the attractions of the island is its climate, for which travelers cross half the world.

Seasons on the island

Many tourists are attracted to Phuket in October. Reviews of vacationers say that at this time you can buy tickets at the lowest prices. Why are such prices in this period?

There are no distinct seasons in Phuket, as is noticeable in other countries. They can conditionally be divided into a dry period and a rainy one. It is believed that the first lasts from November to April, and the second from May until October.





The tropical monsoon climate of this area can manifest itself in the form of prolonged rains for several days in a row (July and September) or short-term rains in the morning, as, for example, Phuket looks like in October. Reviews of tourists recommend coming here on vacation from the middle of the month, since in the first half more frequent rains are possible, although their intensity differs from year to year.

Wise Thais hang red flags on the beaches if swimming in the sea can be life-threatening. This is due to the formation of strong undercurrents, therefore, deciding to visit Phuket in October (tourists' comments warn about this), you should carefully monitor possible warnings. In extreme cases, you can always have a good time near the hotel pool.

What Phuket looks like in October

This island is unlikely to disappoint travelers even on a rainy day. The richness of greenery and flowers growing right on the streets of fruit trees washed with clean rainwater, a riot of aromas - all this will make an unforgettable trip to Phuket in October. Reviews of vacationers note that even this month you can find a lot of entertainment for yourself and take the time to get to know the island and its inhabitants.

For example, a farm for growing orchids has not left anyone indifferent. It is located in a greenhouse, which may not be enough for a whole day to inspect all exhibits. Here orchids are grown for any color and purse. In general, Phuket is a paradise for lovers of exotic plants, as in numerous shops you can buy inexpensive copies of a few centimeters in height, as well as real tropical giants.





Holidays in Phuket in October (reviews of tourists say this) must be combined with a visit to local waterfalls. They are especially beautiful after the rainy season, as the rivers become more full-flowing, and the waterfalls themselves are more powerful.

Even the rainy season does not stop exotic lovers from getting new experiences. Thai culture and the ability to organize holidays in a quality manner make it possible to have a great time in Phuket. Here travelers are expected in any weather:

  • Butterfly Museum, where you can see an extensive collection of these exotic insects of completely different sizes and colors. Their gluttonous mouths do not close for a minute, and if you are lucky, you can see the birth of a new beautiful butterfly.

Extreme sports in October

October also attracted rafting enthusiasts to Phuket. It is during this period, when the rains practically stop, and the rivers after them are still full of water, you can get real pleasure from the descent to the raft.





Rafting in Phuket is available not only to experienced rafters, but also to beginners. The most popular route of various difficulty levels is located in Phang Nga. Under the guidance of experienced coaches, everyone can try this extreme sport. Groups are formed depending on the level of training of people in them.

The rafting program includes not only a trip along a bustling river, but also a bungee jump from a cliff above a beautiful lake for the most daring. The average cost per adult will be from 1000 baht, which also includes a visit to the SPA with fish, elephant trekking, lunch (buffet) and much more.

Holiday Prices

Often, the decisive incentive for tourists is prices to spend their holidays in Phuket in October. Their reviews report that during this period it is possible to combine the low cost of a ticket and the main events with the already established weather.

For example, a ticket for 15 days will cost only $ 1,300 per person. As many travelers who have visited the island during this period note, by the end of the month there is an increase in prices for everything from food to souvenirs and excursions.

Phuket is recognized as the most expensive resort in Thailand, so for those who wish to relax here inexpensively October will be the best option for saving money.

Pros of rest in Phuket in October

If we summarize all the reviews of tourists who visited the island in October, then among them we can distinguish a number of advantages of such a vacation:

  • Firstly, quite low prices literally on everything. What is important, while the quality of service does not decrease.
  • Secondly, this lack of heat. Since it begins to rain mostly at night, tourists have wonderful warm days at their disposal, which can be spent both on the beach (with the sun) and on numerous excursions (in cloudy weather).
  • Thirdly, the warm sea and the absence of storm waves allows both adults and children to swim hours to fly.
  • Fourthly, there are still not so many vacationers, so there is no crowding either in the dining rooms or during sightseeing.

Thus, October, according to travelers, especially its second half, is a great time for a quality vacation in Phuket at an affordable price.
