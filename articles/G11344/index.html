Suntopia Hydros Club. Description, reviews

Description: Suntopia Hydros Club - a hotel of category HV-1 - built on the Mediterranean coast against the backdrop of the beautiful Taurus Mountains, a kilometer from the center of the Turkish city of Kemer. Nearby are many places where you can actively have fun, including restaurants, nightclubs, etc.





The hotel area - 65 thousand square meters - is completely landscaped with pine groves, pomegranate and tangerine trees, under which peacocks walk.

The developed infrastructure includes several shops, laundry, a small conference room, dry cleaning, currency exchange, medical office, small lobby, elevator, concierge services, and a beauty salon.

The registration desk is open around the clock. Here you can arrange a transfer.

The hotel has its own parking lot, which reservation is not necessary in advance.

The distance to the nearest airport is 75 kilometers.

Rooms: All suites at Suntopia Hydros Club Standard, Family, and Garden View are all in one four-story building and 21st bungalows. Their total number is 372.

All rooms are furnished with the necessary modern furniture, a flat-screen TV, a work desk, a large makeup mirror, a telephone, a tea table and chairs, a safe and a minibar.

Balconies overlook the sea or the patio, terraces in front of the bungalows are furnished.

Combined bathrooms include hand dryers, hair dryers, and hygiene products.

Some apartments have bunk beds. Upon request, you can get an iron in the room, the service "auto-wake up".





All rooms are air-conditioned and connected to the heating system.

Pets are not welcome.

Meals: Suntopia Hydros Club offers all-inclusive meals. Buffets are served at Olympos main restaurant. Three others, including G√ºne≈ü with Asian cuisine, are available upon reservation. There is also a special children's restaurant for up to 25 kids.

Guests for the entire stay can once a day dine on the menu.

The hotel also has three bars: by the pool, lobby bar and on the beach.

Beach: Suntopia Hydros has its own, a hundred meters from the main building. There is a pier and a pontoon. Coating - pebble, large. Everything you need for tanning - umbrellas, sunbeds, mattresses and deck chairs - is free. There are comfortable showers.

Additional information: For the youngest holidaymakers, Suntopia Hydros Club offers a children's pool, a comfortable playground, a mini club and babysitting services on request. At the request of parents, a cot can be installed in the room.





Adults can use the indoor and two outdoor pools, water slides, as well as a Turkish bath, massage room. If you wish, you can rent a pedalo, play darts, tennis, mini-golf, billiards, do archery, aerobics.

The hotel has windsurfing courses as well as many motorized water activities.

Reviews: The hotel is located near many other hotels such as Kemer Hotel, Olympos, etc.

Guests are satisfied with everything: food with lots of fruits, tidy rooms, and service. Particularly impressive is the territory, completely green and thought out to the smallest detail.

The hotel is suitable for families.
