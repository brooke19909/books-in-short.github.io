The Norwegian Tony Finger in the early 90s became famous for making funny dolls that were made from unusable scraps, shreds and other improvised materials. The pattern of a rag girl named Tilda went around the whole world, and any toys made in this technique began to be called tildes.

Cute plush animals and little men become a welcome gift for family and friends. It is customary to dress beautiful and warm toys in the maximum number of accessories. Simple tildes made of colorful chintz are just as incredible cute.

Preparing the workplace

To make a lamb tilde you will need:

  • finished paper pattern of a suitable scale;
  • pieces of fabric of the appropriate size;
  • scissors;
  • pencil;
  • thread, needle, glue, sewing pins;
  • filler material;
  • decoration elements: ribbons, ribbons, beads, glass beads.

First you need to prepare a place for creativity: clean the work surface, put all accessories in prominent places, adjust the lighting.

Sew tilde sheep Lamb should begin after cutting out all the details from the fabric. To do this, a paper drawing of a cut is carefully cut out along the contour. The fabric is folded the required number of times, the required part of the pattern is applied and pinned with sewing pins, studs. Pencil outlines the details and draws allowances from 0.5 to 1 cm, depending on the size of the toy.

For the manufacture of large-sized toys, the received parts will need to be processed on an overlock. It is better to stitch such a toy on a typewriter, the stitches will be even and beautiful, the seam will be strong, and the sheep itself will have a neat appearance.

All fabric parts must be "baited" - assembled on a thread or fastened with sewing nails at the edges. The assembly order does not matter.

It is most convenient to start with the torso. To do this, you need to stitch together the two body parts together, leaving a few centimeters incomplete. It is necessary to turn the resulting pillow inside out and fill with the selected material, then complete the seam on the front side.

If the toy of the sheep will consist of two such pillows, then repeat all the steps with the remaining patterns of the body. Fasten both parts of the body with a thread, for greater strength, you can use a small amount of glue.
