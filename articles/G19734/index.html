Brandy Ocean View Hotel 3 * (Vietnam / Nha Trang): photos and reviews

The Vietnamese city of Nha Trang has become a resort for a very long time - even in the days of the French Indochina. But domestic vacationers discovered this southern city only a couple of decades ago. And immediately appreciated all its unconditional advantages. In any case, renting inexpensive and at the same time quite comfortable housing in Nha Trang is absolutely easy. For example, not too demanding tourists can stay in this city at the Brandy Ocean View Hotel 3 *. Reviews from vacationers, this complex has earned quite good.

Hotel Features

As you know, the star rating of hotels in Vietnam is somewhat European. Service in this country is offered to tourists in any case of less quality. Therefore, many tourists who rent rooms in three-star hotels in Nha Trang are afraid upon arrival to see tattered walls, old furniture and miserable interiors. Fortunately, this does not apply to the Brandy Ocean View Hotel 3 * . This modern city hotel in Nha Trang was built relatively recently - in 2013. Therefore, the furniture and appliances in the rooms are still quite new and comfortable. The rooms, halls and corridors in the hotel, according to vacationers who have already managed to live here, look pretty neat and beautiful.





Reception at this hotel operates around the clock. Pets are not allowed in the hotel. Only tourists who have reached the age of 18 are registered here. Smoking in the hotel rooms is, of course, impossible. For additional services on the territory of Brandy Ocean View Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang), its guests can also be paid with cards.

Where is the hotel situated

Location - this, according to tourists, is one of the main advantages of the Brandy Ocean hotel. This complex is located 25 meters from the sea. For city hotels in Nha Trang, such proximity to the ocean is rare. Most of the complexes in this city are far from the sea. Hotel Brandy Ocean guests can walk to the beach in just a couple of minutes.

Tourists, of course, can get from Kamran Airport to the hotel quite quickly. This air hub is not too far from the city. In any case, more than 30-40 minutes, the trip from the airport to the hotel on a transfer tour operator‚Äôs bus does not usually take vacationers.

Tourists also consider the proximity of bus stops and many other infrastructure elements of Nha Trang to the advantages of the location of this hotel. Very close to this complex are also the Gorky Nha Trang park and the city water park. This hotel guests, of course, are also attributed to its undoubted advantages.

Living conditions in this hotel for tourists, judging by the reviews, are quite tolerable. The rooms here are small, but comfortable enough. Cleaned the maids in the hotel often. Hotel guests do not have to use dirty towels either.





The furniture in the Brandy Ocean View Hotel 3 * (in the photo it can be seen) , according to vacationers, is quite comfortable. Judging by the reviews, travelers sleep in hotel beds comfortably. Residents reviews about the service

For unpretentious vacationers, according to tourists, staying at Brandy Ocean View Hotel 3 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang) can be quite comfortable. The absence of a pool, most tourists consider, of course, a minus of this hotel. However, this hotel is located in the vicinity of the famous Gorky Park. Here, among other things, several convenient free pools are equipped. So guests of Brandy Ocean still have the opportunity to swim in fresh water .





The availability of laundry is, according to vacationers, of course, a plus of this hotel. But some tourists do not recommend washing clothes at the Brandy Ocean Hotel . This service is here, unfortunately, is relatively expensive. In the city, if you wish, you can easily find laundry and cheaper.

The fact that the hotel has parking is travelers, of course, also relate to its advantages. Most of the three-star Nha Trang hotels allow guests to park on their territory for free.

A big plus of this hotel, many vacationers consider the availability of WiFi throughout its territory. Brandy Ocean guests are free to use the network . Beach Hotels

The description of the hotel Brandy Ocean View Hotel 3 * given above allows us to judge it as being quite convenient for living. The infrastructure here, of course, is not well developed. But the rooms in this hotel are for rent, judging by the reviews, comfortable. The beach of the hotel travelers consider simply delightful. The guests of the complex can get here , as already mentioned, in just a couple of minutes. True, in order to go ashore, residents of the complex need to cross a rather busy highway, which is worth a closer look .

Actually, on the very beach of the hotel, the sea is always calm, warm and very clean. On the coastline here, too, judging by the reviews, there is no rubbish. The only drawback of the hotel regarding the beach is that the guests here are given towels only for a fee. This service in the hotel costs about 20 rubles a day with our money. This, of course, is not particularly expensive. But still, many three-star hotels in Nha Trang provide this service to their guests free of charge and even without a deposit. Of course, Brandy Ocean loses to such hotels in this regard.

Among other things, many tourists recommend that guests of the Brandy Ocean View Hotel 3 * should definitely visit Winperl Beach in Nha Trang. The sand on the coastline is snowy white, very soft and clean. This beach is usually an unforgettable experience for tourists.
