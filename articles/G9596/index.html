And what wonderful photos you can take here! They will warm the soul of a house in Russia in the cold season. On this island you can book an excursion from many places in Greece for the whole day, however, if you want to completely immerse yourself in the atmosphere of ancient Zakynthos, you can go on vacation here for the whole term. One of the best hotels is the Mabely Grand Hotel due to its characteristics that are ideal for a good holiday.

Argassi

The most developed tourist center of Zakynthos with excellent infrastructure, created specifically for the needs of vacationers, is the resort of Argassi. It is beautifully situated at the foot of Mount Skopos in a gorgeous green valley. It is easy to get here by bus from any city. During your mini-tour you can look into the Church of Our Lady of Skopiotis on the top of the mountain or visit the ruins of an ancient bridge with an arch as high as fifteen meters. In addition, shopaholics will be incredibly happy with the variety of shops and souvenir shops, dotted on every corner.

However, you should be careful with the prices, since at the height of the season they can be inflated about once every ten.

A bit about the island

Stunningly beautiful icons, frescoes of the Zakynthian school, known throughout the world, and museums filled with treasures are offered to guests of Zakynthos. Unfortunately, an earthquake in the twentieth century destroyed many architectural monuments that no one will ever see in their previous form (only after restoration). One of the characteristic sights is Keri Caves with stunning emerald-colored water (you can even swim there).

The azure grotto with arches will amaze with its grandeur and once again show the beauty and power of nature. A professionally organized photo session with a wide selection of entourage elements (from dresses to natural landscapes) will preserve an unforgettable palette of sensual beauty of the island. This offer is especially relevant for event tourism (wedding, anniversary).

Aquapark

Water Village is the largest water park in Greece near the city of Zante. Terrestrial attractions, water slides, children's cars, pools for every taste (jacuzzi, lagoon), free parking, shops, cafes and restaurants - all this wealth spreads over 40,000 square meters. Admission is inexpensive - for an adult a little more than twenty euros, and for a child - about fifteen. However, vivid memories and many creative photographs are provided.

Travel Tips

From Zakynthos, the following interesting gizmos can be brought to memory of yourself, family, friends and close relatives:

  • Sweets. Nougat, pastille (mantolato) with a variety of additives (in the form of nuts) is traditional for the island, therefore, having bought it, you will not lose.
  • Food (other than sweets). Extra virgin olive oil, cheeses, honey, different types of wines.
  • Jewelry. Gold (both yellow and white), silver jewelry and precious stones. All products are certified there, so you can not be afraid of fakes.
