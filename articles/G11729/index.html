Hotel Nam Hai Hotel 3 * (Vietnam): description and photos

If you consider Mui Ne (Vietnam) as a place to stay and are looking for a budget but comfortable hotel near the sea, then pay attention to the three-star Nam Hai Hotel. Perhaps it is exactly what you need.



Location

The undoubted advantage of the hotel in question, tourists who have been here, call its location. So it is located just a three-minute walk from the beach. You can reach the coast either through a narrow street or through the territory of neighboring hotels (if the guards do not mind). In the vicinity of Nam Hai there are several cafes, bars, karaoke, shops, supermarkets, massage parlors, etc. The grocery market can be reached on foot in about half an hour. There is a public transport stop next to the hotel with buses to Phan Thiet. The distance to Ho Chi Minh Airport is just over 200 kilometers. Therefore, the road from it takes about four hours.



Description

Nam Hai Hotel (Vietnam, Mui Ne) is a small city hotel. He has no own territory. It is a multi-storey building equipped for the convenience of staff and tourists with an elevator building with a rooftop pool. The housing stock of the hotel consists of 72 comfortable rooms, some of which have magnificent sea views. It offers guests a restaurant where you can taste both dishes of national cuisine, as well as the more familiar cuisine and deliciously cooked seafood. The hotel also has a massage parlor, laundry and dry cleaning, parking. The reception desk is open around the clock.

Our compatriots, leaving reviews about Nam Hai Hotel, declare that the hotel is quite consistent both in price and in the three-star category. This is also indicated by a rating based on guest ratings. So, it is 4.2 points out of five.

Rooms

As already mentioned, the hotel in question has 72 comfortable rooms. They are represented by the following categories:

  • Superior (area 26-28 sq. M, maximum occupancy - two adults and a child under seven years old).
  • Premier (27 sq. M, view of the golf courses or the sea, maximum occupancy 2 + 1 guests).
  • Deluxe Ocean View (32 sq. M., Living room, two windows with direct sea views).
  • Family (53 sq. M., Two rooms, two bathrooms, partial sea view, four adults and one child can accommodate as much as possible).
  • Five-bed apartment (86 sq. M, two bedrooms, a living room, a dining room and a kitchen, direct sea views, two bathrooms with a bathtub and a Jacuzzi; five adults and two children can accommodate as much as possible).

Regardless of type, all rooms are equipped with comfortable furniture, flat-screen TV with cable channels, telephone, refrigerator, air conditioning, hairdryer, tea set. Please note that there are no balconies in the rooms. It offers free Wi-Fi. However, according to our compatriots, he “catches” well not in all numbers.

Apartments are cleaned every day. Maids renew towels several times a week. Also, as necessary, they replenish supplies of personal hygiene products.



Nutrition

Hotel rates include breakfast only. They are held in buffet style from 7:30 to 10:30 in the morning. In general, our compatriots found breakfast acceptable. So, in the morning here you can eat omelet or scrambled eggs, soup fo, ham, vegetables, fruits, pastries and sweets. During the day you can also eat in the hotel restaurant. Cooking here, according to tourists, is very good.

Beach vacation

As already noted, the indisputable advantage of Nam Hai Hotel (Mui Ne, Phan Thiet) is its proximity to the sea. The beach is municipal. Therefore, for the rental of sunbeds and umbrellas will need to pay. There are no palm trees on the beach . The sand here is shallow and fairly clean. There are often waves at sea, but many tourists are only happy about this fact.
