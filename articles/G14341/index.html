Turkey, Kamelya Holiday Village HV-1 - photos, prices and reviews

Turkish resorts attract lovers of both VIP-rest and economy class. In addition to the fact that this country offers a diverse beach holiday, it is a mecca for shopaholics, gourmets and extreme sports lovers.

The coast of the country is diverse. Each resort region is famous for its features and attractions. Antalya destination is especially popular among tourists.



Regions

Kemer, named after the mythological character Chimera, is famous for its mountain landscapes, unique air with the smell of pine needles and eucalyptus and pebble beaches. If you are a lover of large pebbles and are looking for a hotel with a large flowering, green area, pay attention to the Champion Holiday Village HV-1 hotel in Beldibi.

Belek, located in a protected nature reserve, boasts clean air, VIP-class hotels, golden sandy beaches and the opportunity to get to know the local inhabitants - turtles, which are common in the area and go ashore to lay their eggs. A large number of them can be seen near the Attaleia Holiday Village HV-1.

Alanya is without a doubt the best place for children. Shallow sea and gentle sand attract families with kids here. And the air, filled with aromas of cedar, pine, fills the body with vigor and energy. In this region there are many club hotels with stunning territories, a variety of activities for both children and adults, for example, Eftalia Village Hotel HV-1.



Side is known not only for its historical sights, but also for the longest (ten-month) holiday season. Comfortable temperature here lasts from the middle of spring to the end of autumn. It is possible to swim in the warm sea even in November. Sandy beaches and gentle sea approaches are ideal for children, and young people will also have something to do here.

Side

This region consists of several tourist areas: Kumkoy, located just 3 km from the city; Titreengel - 7 km east of Side; Manavgat - located in the valley of the river of the same name; Colakli is the most remote (14 km) from the center, but no less popular area with beaches of golden sand and popular hotels of the most famous chains.

Kamelya Holiday Village HV-1 is one of them.

A family room of two rooms of 25 and 18 square meters, separated by a screen, can accommodate a maximum of 4 people (there are 1 double and 2 ordinary single beds).

Beach and sea

"Camellia" is located just 300 meters from the Mediterranean Sea. A wide sandy private beach with a length of 550 meters is equipped with umbrellas, sunbeds, mattresses. Towels are provided there. There is also the Beach Bar, where you can enjoy alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m., and have a snack from noon to 6 p.m. If you are a fan of the Aegean coast of Turkey, but would like to relax in a hotel of this class, you can advise taking a closer look at Omer Holiday Village HV-1 (in Kusadasi), which has a developed infrastructure and has a beautiful large territory.



Infrastructure

On the territory of the hotel are located: Internet cafes, exchangers, shops, beauty salon, first-aid post, dry cleaning, parking. It is also possible to rent a car or take a bus to the city center. All these services are provided for money.

Entertainment and sports

On the territory there are 3 outdoor pools and 1 large indoor. Everyone can visit the fitness center with fitness equipment and the spa, where you can relax in the jacuzzi, Turkish bath or use the services of a massage therapist for an extra charge.

Kamelya Holiday Village HV-1 will have something to do with active youth and people with a sporting lifestyle. Here they are at their service: a tennis court (rental of accessories, lighting and private lessons for a fee), table tennis, volleyball, a place for playing darts, a football field, aerobics and aqua aerobics, mini-golf, a water park with 7 slides, 3 of which are for children.

Nutrition

The Kamelya World Hotels complex has 6 a la carte restaurants offering Italian, Mexican, Mediterranean (paid), Chinese, fish and local Turkish cuisine with barbecue. Guests at Kamelya Holiday Village HV-1 can visit one of these restaurants once a week.

The main hotel restaurant, bright and bewitching with its fabulous design, invites guests to an indoor air-conditioned room or an outdoor terrace for breakfast, lunch and dinner from 7 a.m. to 9.30 p.m.



The number of bars in Kamelia will also impress fans of such establishments and is a record for hotels of this class on the Turkish coast. So, in the Champion Holiday Village HV-1 hotel, located near Kemer (in the village of Beldibi) there are only three bars.

The opinion of vacationers about the hotel

Numerous positive reviews from Russian tourists suggest the high level of this hotel. Among the qualities that do not leave them indifferent and cause a desire to return, they call:

  1. Very beautiful and large area located between the three hotels.
  2. A varied diet.
  3. Nice, big beach.
  4. Polite service staff and usually quick resettlement. Difficulties can arise only during the high season, when there may be a shortage of available rooms.
  5. The opportunity to use many services on the territory of the five-star hotels "Celine" and "Furla", accommodation in which is much more expensive than in "Camellia".
  6. An excellent team of animators who do not let bored adults as well as children, unobtrusively drawing them into sports or dance entertainment.

Of the shortcomings of the hotel, old rooms are most often noted, but the administration successfully copes with this problem, as the number of rooms is updated there every year, new buildings are being built in place of the oldest. Due to the large number of vacationers at the height of the season, there may be a shortage of sunbeds on the beach or by the pool, but the staff is trying to solve such problems quickly.

Prices

The cost of rest at this hotel depends on the month when you want to relax there and the number of nights spent there. The longer the rest, the lower the price. So, the approximate prices for a room per day, subject to 2 adults staying in it, in 2014 were (in May, October): $ 260-300 for Delux, Junior Family Room - $ 350-380, Suite Room - $ 450-520. At the height of the tourist season, the cost can increase by 50-100%. Rest in a standard room will be cheaper and will cost $ 150-300.

If you are a fan of self-rest, you can book a room directly on the hotel website and book a transfer from the airport there.
