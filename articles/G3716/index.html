The ideal location for active tourists is Signature Hotel Al Barsha 4 *. UAE, Dubai is a fast-paced resort with plenty to see. Here are the main attractions to visit in this city:

  • Burj Khalifa is the tallest tower in the world. Inside the skyscraper are hundreds of restaurants, shops and entertainment centers.

  • Dubai Marina Mall is a huge shopping center with a large number of stores. At affordable prices, you can buy a lot of branded goods.

additional information

Some organizational issues need to be familiarized with travelers who plan to visit Signature Hotel Al Barsha 4 *. Reviews of tourists focus on the following points:

  • New guests are checked in from 14.00 to midnight;
  • formalities associated with departure are decided from 11.00 to noon;
  • subject to availability for an additional fee, early check-in or late check-out is possible;
  • children under the age of 6 stay in the room free of charge, provided that they do not need an extra bed;
  • the cost of additional accommodation is 180 dirhams per night;
  • children under 2 years old are provided with free cots (this service must be ordered in advance in the Special Requests section, as the amount of furniture is limited);
  • the room cannot accommodate more than one crib or extra bed;
  • no pets allowed;
  • it is possible to pay for received services with bank plastic cards;
  • if the name indicated on the credit card does not coincide with the name indicated on the identity card, the guest will have to pay in cash the amount covering the reservation;
  • self-registration of guests is possible only from 18 years old;
  • Check-in is only possible with an identity card with a photo and a personal credit card.

Negative reviews

Do not forget that any hotel has its drawbacks. This statement is true for Signature Hotel Al Barsha 4 * (Dubai). Reviews of tourists indicate such shortcomings:

  • the pool is very small, it does not fit everyone (in addition, it is open only until six in the evening);
  • the hotel does not provide a shuttle service to the beach (this option cannot be ordered even at an additional cost);
  • not the best quality of cleaning (in some cases you have to wait for the maid on your own and point her to the most dirty places in the room);
  • disgusting sound insulation in the hotel (even with the windows and doors closed, the noise of the carriageway and music from the nightclub are heard);
  • almost every day the hotel undergoes some kind of repair work, and therefore you have to listen to the drone of drills and the sound of hammers (this happens in the afternoon, when guests usually want to relax);
  • newly arrived tourists are not immune to being accommodated in an uncleaned room (only clean bed linen testifies to the visit of the maids);
  • not the best quality food in the main restaurant (the dishes are monotonous, the assortment is rather modest);
  • the rooms and all other rooms of the hotel have long been in need of redecoration (the walls are frayed, scratched and even stained in spots).
