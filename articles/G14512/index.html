About Services

The Palmery Resort and Spa 4 * (Phuket, Kata Beach, Thailand) has earned the status of a good hotel with excellent service and polite staff. The vast majority of tourists who have already been here claim that everything is just that. Well, you should list all the services offered by the hotel to make sure of this.

In addition to an outdoor swimming pool, the complex has a picturesque garden and terrace. The hotel itself has an excellent fitness center as well as a spa. There you can undergo anti-aging and wellness treatments or visit the massage room.

The complex has an excellent restaurant serving a variety of dishes. There is also a bar with quality alcoholic drinks. Also, people who come to rest in their own car are offered a place in free private parking (you do not even need to make a preliminary request).

And finally, the service. He is really top notch! It has everything: a tour desk, ticket service, babysitting, concierge, etc. There is also a conference and banquet room. Reservation

This hotel is one of the most popular in Thailand. Therefore, if you want to stay here, you should reserve a room in advance. Better - two months before the trip.

Check-in is from two in the afternoon, and check-out is before noon. In general, everything is traditional. The hotel can be accommodated with children, and if the child is less than 4 years old, then he can even live for free. If he is more adult, then you will have to pay 1000 TNV per night. The same thing if tourists decided to take another person (friend, friend, etc.) with them. This is approximately 2100 rubles. Upon arrival, guests will have to show their credit card, the number of which they indicated when booking.

In general, the hotel is excellent - with good service, excellent food, minimum prices and polite staff. Great option for a positive vacation!
