AnTuTu Benchmark - what is it?

When studying information about smartphones, one can often hear that a special test is used to evaluate them. It is called AnTuTu Benchmark. The essence of this utility is to analyze the main parameters of the device. At the end of testing, which can take about 10 minutes, information with scores and a global rating appears in front of the user. It is on its basis that the rating is compiled. Xiaomi smartphones in AnTuTu have also been tested. However, you need to know one caveat - performance results may vary depending on the version of the program, as well as on the specific model of the device. But they will differ slightly.

The rating, which is presented below, will help determine the best phone by technical characteristics. In this test, of course, such moments as design, dimensions of the smartphone, type of body materials, optimal balance of filling and cost are not taken into account.

How did this model attract the attention of users? Of course, the characteristics. Camera resolution is quite enough for this price segment: the main one is 13 megapixels, the front one is 5 megapixels.

Now consider what, in the opinion of users, is an advantage, and also find out what is said in the negative reviews. Deficiencies, as such, were not found.
