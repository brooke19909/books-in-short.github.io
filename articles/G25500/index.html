Obzor (Bulgaria): weather, recreation and resort reviews

Convenient geographical location, natural conditions and a fascinating historical past have made Bulgaria one of the most socially and economically developed countries of the Balkan Peninsula. It is not surprising that this country is popular with tourists at any time of the year.

Its most famous resorts are Albena, Golden Sands, Sunny Beach, Sunny Day, Balchik, Kranevo, Varna, St. Vlas, Nessebar, Obzor. Bulgaria is incredibly rich in picturesque landscapes and endless golden beaches, original culture, famous sights and delicious national cuisine that will turn the head of any person.

Fairytale city



Sixty kilometers south of Varna is the small town of Obzor. It is located on the very shore of the Black Sea near the descent of the Stara Planina mountain range. Thirty kilometers from Obzor, the ancient city of Nessebar is located, the resort village of Pomorie lies a little further, and only twenty five kilometers the life of the resort "Sunny Beach" is seething. This is a resort with a beach strip of six kilometers, stretching from Cape St. Atanas to the southern cape of Mona Petra.

The freshness of the sea and the beauty of the mountains attract thousands of tourists of all ages to the fabulous city of Obzor. Bulgaria generously bestows hospitality and goodwill on every vacationer.

History of the city

The ancient town of Obzor has existed for more than three thousand years. Initially, it was settled by the Thracians. During the reign of the wise Greeks, he received the name Navlochos, and was subsequently renamed the city of the Sun - Heliopolis. When the Romans came to Obzor, the construction of the temple of Jupiter begins. From this moment the city bears the name Theopolis - the city of God. And today, the townspeople, as well as visitors, can observe the remains of this magnificent structure.

In the Middle Ages, the Bulgarian military fortress Koziak was built in the city. After the utter destruction by the Ottoman forces, the village of Gezeken appeared on its territory. And only in 1935 the city received its current name Obzor. Bulgaria from that moment opened up new prospects for its development.

Climatic and weather conditions



A popular seaside resort Obzor known among holidaymakers as the Bulgarian pearl of the Black Sea coast. Due to the proximity of the sea and mountains, the air here is filled with incredible freshness and amazing smells.

But although the city has a temperate continental climate, the air temperature in it is always higher than in Golden Sands, Albena or Varna. In the summer months, it reaches 28-30 degrees above zero, heating the water to the state of fresh milk. Therefore, the main rest time falls from May to October.

Due to the special climatic and natural conditions, the weather in Obzor makes the vacationers happy and allows you to enjoy the warmth of the sea and the sun from the heart.

Sights and historical places

As in any settlement, the Obzor has its own interesting and significant places. So, the most visited are the ancient ruins and sights of the Middle Ages. This is the Temple of Jupiter, and the Koziak fortress built by the Romans, and ancient columns and capitals located in the Central Park of the city. Tourists and the ruins of the Heliopolis fortress never go unnoticed.



Places such as the lighthouse on Cape Emin, the Orthodox Church, and the House of Culture are often visited with interest. The latter houses the Chitalishte library, which has over seventeen thousand books. Also, various recreational activities are periodically held at the House of Culture: theater performances, concerts and more. Many of them involve children of primary school age.

Interesting excursions

What just does not attract a welcoming and sunny Bulgaria? Rest in the Review can not even be imagined without catchy excursions. Slow walks around the city with a tour of its inimitable ancient sights, field trips to neighboring resort areas. You must definitely feel the atmosphere of ancient Sozopol, see the beauty of the streets of Varna and conquer Nessebar, lying on an unusually beautiful small peninsula.

Being not far from the Obzor, the truly Bulgarian villages of Bayala and Emon are also attracting guests. Here you can fully experience and feel the national flavor of Bulgaria.

Transport and hotels of Obzor

When planning a trip, you need to consider the transport features in the city of Obzor. Bulgaria today has numerous means of transportation. But, given the relatively small territory of the Review, it is possible to move around on foot. Of course, there are also regular buses here, with which you can get to Sozopol, Varna and Nessebar. Water transport, such as a catamaran or a jet ski, is also very popular .



In addition to the private sector, in Obzor there is a hotel base to accommodate many vacationers. It includes both overhauled ancient buildings and recently constructed hotels. Their number is increasing every year, but the prices for settlement and service, as before, delight vacationers. The most popular hotels among tourists are Casablanca, Miramar, Nanev, ObzorCity, Elegance, Iberostar Luna Bay, Starfish, Obzor Beach, Paraiso Beach, Tudi.

Both rest and treatment

The interweaving of history and modernity, the beauty of nature, friendly residents - this is what Bulgaria is most remembered for by vacationers. Obzor plays an important role in this. It is one of the most environmentally friendly places on the Black Sea coast, because the distance to the nearest enterprise is at least sixty kilometers.



For many tourists this is a quiet and comfortable town. Its endless sandy beaches, coniferous forests, mineral springs with healing sulfuric water, mountain streams and small silver waterfalls are the main advantages of the resort. That is why the Review is well suited for those suffering from bronchial and asthma-bronchial diseases.

Guest reviews

When asked about the impression that the rest in Obzor (Bulgaria) leaves in the shower, the reviews are almost always positive. But for everyone, a stay in this country and at this resort is remembered in its own way.

For some, Obzor is a cozy and quiet place where it is good and inexpensive to relax with your children. Amusement parks, children's carting, playgrounds and pools, water parks and convenient entry into the sea make pastime unusual for both children and their parents. And on the central square in the city, children's festivals take place every summer.



Many guests of the city express their sincere enthusiasm when purchasing Bulgarian wines, ripe fruits and vegetables, as well as leather and flax products made by local artisans. Delicious pastries and dairy products at grocery stalls are an extraordinary success among tourists. It should be noted that in comparison with many resorts in Bulgaria , Obzor has a very low price level.

For some, Obzor is a great place for relaxation and unrestrained entertainment. The presence of many shopping centers, restaurants and bars, cafes and bistros, shops, nightclubs is a priority for active youth. True gourmets can taste the culinary masterpieces of national cuisine and other delights in coastal inexpensive restaurants.

For most tourists, after a wonderful vacation, the town of Obzor (Bulgaria) remains in memory for a long time. Photos taken in the most beautiful and interesting places will not let you forget about a great time.
