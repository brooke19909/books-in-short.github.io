Samara city, hotels: addresses, phone numbers, reviews

The longest embankment in the country, the largest square and the tallest building of the railway station in Europe - Samara is considered the real champion.

Hotels, concert halls, theaters, museums, cinemas, galleries, squares and parks, architectural monuments - the largest city in the Middle Volga region is proud of its cultural life. Samara is the seventh largest metropolis in Russia in terms of population .



For those planning a short trip to Samara, we have compiled a list of required events:

  1. Walk on the boat. You can see the magnificent Volga with the start of navigation in May.
  2. Excursion to the bunker of Stalin. The presence of underground structures in Samara has generated a huge number of urban legends. Rumors of tunnels under the Volga, a reserve command center and other underground facilities are incredibly popular, but, unfortunately, have no evidence.
  3. Buying souvenirs. It is simply impossible to leave Samara empty-handed. Ceramics and clay toys, caskets and national costumes, as well as colorful decorative magnets - travelers have a really great choice.
