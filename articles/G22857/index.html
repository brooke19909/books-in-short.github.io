How to get to Moscow from Monino and back

Monino, one of the many villages that make up the Moscow region, is not far from Moscow. How it is more convenient and easier to get from the village to the capital and back, what transport options exist - more about this in this article.

Distance between Moscow and Monino

Monino is a small green urban-type settlement located in the Schelkovo district.

Monino is 37 km away from Moscow in a straight line. The road stretched for 41 km, railway tracks - for 72 km.

Many Monino residents work in the capital, making a daily round trip. Muscovites, on the contrary, come to the village for a weekend to relax, fry kebabs, take a walk in the forest and visit a museum dedicated to the development of domestic aviation.

Train station Monino

The easiest way is to use the services of Russian Railways. You can go from Monino to Moscow by train daily. Electric transport operates seven days a week, and on weekends and holidays additional trains are launched.

Monino railway station belongs to the Yaroslavl direction of the popular Mytishchi - Fryazevo line. The station is an intermediate.

The train station in Monino is located on the street. Railway, 1b. There are terminals at the station where you can quickly purchase tickets, at the ticket office you can pay by card. But we must take into account that the ticket offices do not work around the clock.

The box office building is located on the side platform, there are also 2 island platforms that are connected by underground and ground passages. Turnstiles are installed for ticket control.

From Monino to Moscow by train

Electric trains from Monino station arrive at the Yaroslavsky railway station of the capital, being on the way 1 h 10 min.

The first flight from Monino to Moscow leaves at 04:18, the last at 23:19. The interval of movement of the trains allows you to choose a convenient time: the trains depart in about half an hour. 40 trains depart daily.

On weekends, additional trains are launched at 10:50, 11:25, 11:52, 12:41. The flight at 19:44 is valid only on weekdays.

The Monino train to Moscow makes 10 stops.

From the capital to Monino by electric train

From the capital it is also convenient to get to Monino by train. The first flight leaves from Yaroslavsky railway station at 06:19, the last - at 01:22. Electric trains run at half-hour intervals. Additional trains are launched on weekends, these are the following flights:

  • 11:54;
  • 12:33;
  • 12:56.

Only on working days the train runs at 19:29.

Ticket price

You can get from Moscow to Monino for a small amount - 132 p.

You can save and buy a subscription for 10-90 trips. It should be borne in mind that the subscription has time limits and can be designed only for travel on weekdays or have a validity period of 1 month.

On the road by car

The distance from Moscow to Monino by road is 40 km. Approximately you can get to the capital from the village in 40 minutes, but the speed of movement depends on traffic jams, which are not uncommon in this direction.

They leave the capital along the Enthusiasts highway (E-22), go to Balashikha, then the road changes its marking and becomes the Gorky highway (M-7). Car congestion occurs here most often in the morning and evening on weekdays, Saturday mornings and Sunday evenings.

To get off the M-7 to Monino, you need to go to the stele with the name of the village and after 500 m turn in the opposite direction, drive again 500 m and turn right. Near the entrance is the building of the former checkpoint.

Bus service

From Monino bus station to the capital, daily, except weekends, bus number 362 leaves. It will take 1 hour 10 minutes to get there. The bus arrives in Moscow at st. m. "Schelkovskaya". Flight schedule: daily from 06:30, then at 07:40 and 08:20. At 09:40 a new bus leaves, followed by an hourly interval, at 10:40, 11:20, 13:00, 14:00, 14:40, 16:15 and 17:00 there are buses, and the last flight is at 6 p.m.

On weekends, there are additional flights at 07:10, 08:40 and 09:45. Convenient time at 12:05 and 13:40. Towards evening, there are buses at 16:00, 17:30 and 18:40.

On weekdays, additional buses depart, starting from 07:10, at 08:55 and 10:05. Daytime additional at 12:15, 13:30 and 15:50, evening at 17:30, 19:00.

It is not difficult to get from Monino to Moscow, it is just as easy to get from the capital to a nearby village on any day of the week.
