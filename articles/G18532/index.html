Botany Beach Resort Hotel, Pattaya, Thailand: Travel Photos and Reviews

The Kingdom of Thailand is one of the most attractive countries for a pleasant and unforgettable vacation. It is here that you can admire the magnificent tropical flora and see a lot of interesting things. Choosing a place to spend your holidays, you should pay attention to the hotel Botany Beach Resort. They provide excellent conditions for living. At the same time, tourists do not have to spend excessive money, because tours to the hotel can be purchased at a very attractive cost.

Hotel location

Botany Beach Resort is located near the famous resort city of Pattaya. It is buried in a tropical garden, so it will be very pleasant for lovers of wildlife to stay here. Remoteness from the city has many advantages, as well as disadvantages. Those who wish to spend time in peace and quiet will seem like a real paradise. There is no strange smell that hovers through the streets of Pattaya. But for fans of entertainment, a vacation at the Botany Beach Resort may seem a little boring, because discos or other entertainment programs are not provided here.





Tourists arriving at Bangkok Airport will have to get to the hotel for about two and a half hours, since the distance is 160 km. Guests landing at the U-Tapao Airport, which is only 15 km away, arrive much faster.

Hotel Description

Botany Beach Resort is a hotel complex, which consists of three-story buildings, villas and bungalows. All buildings are almost buried in luxurious gardens that will amaze guests with their cleanliness and grooming. Although the territory is significant, here you will not have to face dirt, garbage or uncut grass. Everywhere there are paths, tiled, which allows you to walk throughout the territory and enjoy the birdsong. You can admire here with bizarre figures made of ornamental plants, small ponds with vegetation and unusually bright colors that fill the whole neighborhood with marvelous aromas.





All hotel buildings are designed in Thai style. From the first minutes of their stay, hotel guests can familiarize themselves with Thai architecture. The interior of the hotel, where there are many decorative elements of national style, will be no less interesting for vacationers.

Description of rooms

Among the 185 rooms offered, guests can choose between standard rooms or superior apartments. Each room can accommodate a maximum of 3 people. The rooms are decorated in Thai style. They are quite spacious and furnished with beautiful furniture, which creates a feeling of home comfort. The inherent dampness of many such establishments due to the humid climate is not a problem here even in bathrooms.

Each room has a balcony or terrace where you can sit with a cup of tea or coffee. The rooms, located in one-story buildings, have small verandas, also equipped with chairs and a table.





The room has all the necessary furniture. There is where to sit, where to put the brought things. Evenings can be spent watching TV, on which several Russian channels are broadcast. Guests will not have to worry about the safety of valuables that can be stored in the electronic safe available in each room. It also provides the opportunity to use the phone. Internet is available in all rooms, and you do not have to pay extra for its use.

The rooms have powerful individual air conditioners, thanks to which the guests will be guaranteed the most comfortable temperature in the rooms. Guests at the Botany Beach Resort 4 * hotel can use the minibar available in the rooms, but you will have to pay for drinks. The minibar is always filled with fresh drinks. Every day they put water here, which guests can use for free.

Housekeeping does not disappoint vacationers. After it, perfect purity reigns everywhere. Linen is changed every day or on request. In Thailand, they are very careful about natural resources, so they are very happy if the guests show understanding and themselves notify the staff about the need to change clothes.

Nutrition

Despite the fact that far from all tourists like Thai cuisine , the hotel guests respond positively to the quality of food. Here even the most demanding people will be able to choose the right dishes for themselves, since the proposed menu is quite diverse. Guests do not complain about the excessive use of spices, which is typical of Thai cuisine. Among the dishes, you can choose options that will appeal to even young hotel guests. Very happy tourists and a wide variety of exotic fruits that are served at the table. Here you can also drink quite tasty tea or coffee.





Guests of the Botany Beach Resort (Pattaya) are provided with free breakfast only. Guests will have to pay for lunch and dinner. You can eat here in two restaurants offering European, Russian and Thai dishes. It also serves delicious seafood dishes.

Outside the hotel, guests can find various cafeterias where you can have a tasty and not very expensive meal. Nearby is a fruit market, where you can stock up on low prices with a variety of exotic.

Beach vacation

Hotel Botany Beach Resort 4 * (Pattaya) has its own sandy beach. Guests speak about him very well. Cleanliness always reigns here, which is unusual for most Pattaya beaches. Pleased with holidaymakers and the purity of the water. Often, people who have been on holiday near the Gulf of Thailand, complain about water pollution. But near Jomtien Beach, the situation is different. The entrance to the water is quite convenient. Near the coast is quite shallow, which allows children to enjoy swimming. The distance to deeper places is small, so adults can swim enough here.





Guests at the Botany Beach Resort enjoy free use of sun loungers and parasols at the beach. Free sunbeds can be found even in the midst of the tourist season, when all hotel rooms are populated.

Infrastructure

In many travel agencies, the hotel prescribed Botany Beach Resort 3 *. Reviews of tourists confirm that this hotel complex is fully consistent with four stars. It offers a variety of services that will allow guests to feel in fairly comfortable conditions. Administrators are present at the counter around the clock, which allows vacationers to address emerging issues at any time. The staff does not speak Russian, but in every way tries to understand the problems of the guests and quickly fix them.





The hotel organizes a shuttle service to the city by bus or taxi at the request of tourists. In the lobby there is a bank branch in which you can exchange currency. Hotel guests can also use a luggage room.

The hotel has a shop where tourists can stock up on various souvenirs. Laundry and dry cleaning services are available at an additional cost. People who want to organize a business meeting or negotiations are provided with a conference room, the rental of which will cost a certain amount.

Entertainment

Hotel Botany Beach Resort can not boast of a lot of entertainment, but still guests will not have to be bored here. In the fitness center, vacationers can exercise in modern fitness equipment. Thai massage, which is done outdoors, will allow you to relax and improve your health. Such a service is inexpensive, but it gives a lot of positive. Guests can play table tennis for free.





A lot of interesting things can be seen during excursions, of which a huge number are offered. Tourists can choose to visit various islands, where the beauty of nature will leave a lasting impression. Vacationers are also given the opportunity to visit Bangkok or Pattaya and see the sights of these cities.

Botany Beach Resort Hotel: traveler reviews

Almost all the tourists who happened to spend their vacations at the hotel are very pleased with this place and are going to pay a second visit here. Most of all, guests like the staff who will come to the rescue in any situation. The hotel staff always treat guests with respect and goodwill.

Many guests are amazed at the beauty of the hotel gardens. Very good tourists speak about the condition of the rooms, service and food. Here, only the most picky vacationers will be able to identify any flaws. At Botany Beach Resort, people of any age can relax.
