Holiday Village "Admiral" (Tyumen): description and address

The recreation center "Admiral" (Tyumen) is perfect for recreation of various types. People who are tired of the bustle of the city love to come here. In the country club, they are waiting for a lot of excellent offers for good leisure. The place is ideal for holding a wide variety of celebrations. Landscaped area, beautiful views, a beautiful lake - all this allows you to make any holiday spectacular and unforgettable. Guests book an institution for their events not only in the warm season, but also in the winter. Indeed, in the snow, the base also looks simply amazing.

general information

The recreation center "Admiral" in Tyumen has a sufficient number of cottages on the territory. They can be removed both for relaxing on weekends or vacations, and for accommodating guests before the holiday. Each house is designed in accordance with all the rules and has everything necessary for a comfortable stay. In total there are four cottages at the base, which can accommodate about 20 people. Inside, guests will find several bedrooms, a living room, bathrooms, there is a Finnish sauna. The gorgeous view of Lake Andreevskoye makes any vacation even more enjoyable and unique. The place of the club was chosen very well right next to the water area. And the proximity to the city allows guests to quickly reach their destination.

Meals at the recreation center "Admiral" Tyumen takes place in a restaurant. Guests are offered business lunches, receptions, barbecue, buffet. On the territory of the club you can hold picnics, as well as engage in extreme sports. There are excellent specialists here who will help guests learn how to climb a climbing wall and paraglide. You can also rent cheesecakes, banana, water skis, ATVs, jet skis and much more.

Base Address

Guests can reach their destination in a variety of ways. Therefore, it is important to know the exact address of the Admiral country club: Borovsky village, Lesnoy lane, building 4, building 1. It is convenient for some guests to come directly by private car, while other visitors can get to the railway station called Lake Andreevskoye. From there, the institution is quite close. Since the recreation center "Admiral" (Tyumen) is located in the urban village of Borovsky, you can also get here by bus number 153. He goes every few hours.

Additional features

The country club is always ready to offer a lot of options for a variety of events. At the recreation center "Admiral" (Tyumen) are corporate events, family celebrations, holidays for children. Very often people choose this place for their main day - a wedding. They are offered a choice of several tents. For a summer wedding there is a room for 500 people. But most of all, guests like the all-weather tent, which is located on the water. It is suitable to accommodate about 280 people. In the article you can see a photo of the recreation center "Admiral" (Tyumen) and the tent itself. It seems to soar above the water and looks both externally and internally simply excellent.

On the territory of the base, photo shoots are often held. There is a beautiful pier and green lawns. Guests often rent a suitable room for their events. Also in the institution regularly organize concerts, festivals. The premises of the base are also ordered for sporting events.
