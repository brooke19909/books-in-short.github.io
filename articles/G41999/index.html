Hotel Triton Garden Hotel 3 * (Greece, Crete): overview, description and reviews of tourists

Triton Garden Hotel 3 * is a resort hotel located on the island of Crete. This is a seasonal hotel that welcomes vacationers from April to October, when you can sunbathe and swim. Tourists coming to Crete like this hotel. It has a fairly convenient location. The rooms are large and comfortable. The staff is welcoming and welcoming. Therefore, reviews about the hotel are mostly positive.



Heraklion, Malia

Triton Garden Hotel 3 * (Malia) is located near the resort town, on the site of which, in ancient times, was the port of the legendary King Minos. And two kilometers from the town of Malia - the ruins of the palace, which is a contemporary of Knossos. It is no less interesting than this famous archaeological site. Unlike the latter, the Malian Palace was not reconstructed, and all the ruins are preserved in their original form. The town is also quite attractive. Picturesque old quarters start from the large church of St. Nectarios. There you can see the Venetian temple of St. Nicholas and St. Demetrios. The church of the same name, which is also very old there. The modern resort is very popular among young people around the world, thanks to its dance floors, discos and nightclubs. How to get and what's nearby

If you fly by plane, then be aware that the airport, where air ships land from different countries, is thirty-two kilometers from the Triton Garden Hotel 3 *. A shuttle will take you from the terminal to the bus station. And from Heraklion to Malia every hour a little bus goes. Near the hotel and the sea, and the center of the town - shops, restaurants, nightlife. To walk to any point about five to seven minutes. A stone's throw from the hotel is a bus stop. From here, as already mentioned, you can get to Heraklion (in an hour) and Hersonissos (in about ten minutes). Night clubs are also very close. By the way, they work in such a way that you can go into several at once and come back. You are given special bracelets that you show during face control.



Territory and service

The building of the Triton Garden Hotel 3 * is built in a style traditional for Greek architecture. It is surrounded by a garden, which is reflected in the name of the hotel. The area is very well maintained. The hotel consists of several buildings separated by a road. The hotel administration, as well as room service, are available around the clock. There is a car park at the hotel. You can rent a car or bike on site. In the lobby there is a safe where guests put valuables. The hotel has several shops, a beauty salon, and laundry. If you are celebrating a birthday, wedding or celebrating a round date at the hotel, a professional photographer is at your service. Throughout the territory it catches Wi-Fi. Other activities at the hotel include billiards and table tennis. If you come with a small child, they will put a cot in your room at your request and even invite a nanny.

Sea holiday

The hotel, you can say, is almost on the beach. Sea from distant buildings across the road. To a beautiful sandy beach no more than a few tens of meters along a shady street with cafes and shops. The coast here is public, as on the whole island. So you can choose not only the nearest beach, but also at any other hotel. To each of them to go five to eight minutes, looking in which direction. The water everywhere is very clean, transparent, the entrance to the water is gentle. Children are very comfortable and safe. No wonder the local coast is considered the best in Crete. Sun loungers and umbrellas are paid, cost about four euros per day. A little east of the hotel is Potamos Beach, where you can swim with a mask and look at the marine fauna. In general, the coast, divided into different sections, stretches in this area for a distance of about four kilometers, from the town of Malia to the village of Stalida. Between them runs a minibus. Any beach in the area has a Blue Flag. At the hotel Triton Garden Hotel 3 * (Crete, Heraklion) there are two swimming pools - air and indoor.
