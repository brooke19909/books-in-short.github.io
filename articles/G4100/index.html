Description of rooms

Comfortable living conditions - this is one of the most important conditions for a good rest. In Port River Hotel & Spa 5 * you will be offered the following accommodation options:

  • Standard room is a spacious bright room. Some of the rooms are equipped with a double bed, while the rest are additionally equipped with one and a half. There is a dressing table and an easy chair for rest. You can admire the surroundings from your own balcony.
  • The family room consists of two spacious rooms, each of which has its own bathroom and a balcony. The children's bedroom is equipped with one and a half beds and folding chairs. The second room is equipped with a large bed and a dressing table with a large mirror.
