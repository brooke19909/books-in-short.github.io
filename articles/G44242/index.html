Hotel "Caucasus", Adler: reviews and photos

The hotel "Kavkaz" pleases its guests with its original modern design, high standards of service and comfortable living conditions. Adler will leave you with the most vivid impressions, thanks to the beautiful nature, hot weather and vibrant entertainment.

Short hotel description

If you dream of an unforgettable vacation by the sea, then pay attention to the Kavkaz Hotel, located in an ecologically clean area. It is worth noting the original modern architecture of the building of the original spiral shape.

There are 57 comfortable rooms for guests, most of which are equipped with balconies. The price includes hearty breakfasts, the use of car parking, as well as a taxi call at any convenient time. If you are interested in this option, then book your apartment before heading to the Kavkaz Hotel (Adler). Phone: +7 (862) 260-82-84.

Special attention deserves the restaurant "Caucasus", designed for 40 people. In the cozy room you can enjoy traditional Russian dishes, as well as masterpieces of Caucasian cuisine.





Where is the hotel situated

The advantageous location is characterized by the hotel "Caucasus" in Adler. Nizhneimeretinskaya Bay, 161 - the address of this institution. The distance to the beach is only 30 meters. The journey to the airport and train station takes about 20 minutes. Also nearby is a bus station from where you can quickly get to anywhere in Adler, as well as in neighboring cities.





Hotel Rooms

If you value comfort, then the Caucasus Hotel is just perfect for you. Adler will give you even more vivid impressions if you can fully relax and regain your strength in guest rooms of the following categories:

  • Standard rooms offer comfortable accommodation for two guests. The spacious room has a sleeping place, dressing table, wardrobe with a full-length mirror and other necessary furniture. A panoramic window deserves special attention, which opens up picturesque views of the city. The combined bathroom is equipped with a bathtub and washbasin.
  • Studio is a spacious apartment that can accommodate up to 4 guests. In the bedroom area there is a French bed and a dressing table. The living room is represented by a seating area and a dressing table. Due to the angular arrangement of the room, two of its windows offer views of different areas of the city.
  • Luxury apartments consist of a comfortable living room, which is combined with an entrance hall, as well as a spacious bedroom with a large bed. A sofa bed and an armchair can accommodate additional guests. In total, up to 5 guests can stay in the room.
  • The suite is a spacious room, which in size and functionality resembles the suite that the Caucasus Hotel (Adler) offers. Safe, designer furniture, air conditioning, refrigerator, TV - this is not a complete list of amenities that are provided in two spacious rooms. A feature of this category of apartments is the presence of a balcony with a beautiful sea view.
  • Spacious attic rooms are great for family use. The bedroom has large and small beds, and the living room is equipped with folding soft furniture. The sloping ceiling and the windows in it create a special atmosphere. The bathroom is equipped with a bath.

Separately, it is worth noting the interior design of the rooms that the Kavkaz Hotel (Adler) offers its guests. Photos of travelers demonstrate restrained style, warm colors and home comfort.

Hotel "Caucasus" (Adler): reviews, positive aspects

A huge amount of useful information can be gleaned from the reviews that tourists leave on specialized resources. It is worth noting a number of positive characteristics of the hotel:

  • the hotel is quite new, and therefore the repair and furniture are in good condition;
  • the windows are kept in perfect cleanliness, and therefore the landscapes will seem even more beautiful to you;
  • the hotel is very conveniently located both relative to the sea and relative to infrastructure facilities;
  • very tasty food in a restaurant at a relatively low price;
  • happy with the availability of an elevator in the hotel (but it works very slowly);
  • nearby - a large comfortable beach;
  • new linens and towels that changed regularly without requests or reminders;
  • spacious and comfortable bathroom; pleasant classic interior of the rooms, done in calm warm colors;
  • on the balconies there is a clothes dryer and towels, and therefore there is no feeling of dampness in the room;
  • each floor has an iron and ironing board for general use;
  • You can rent a bike without leaving a deposit.




Negative hotel reviews

Unfortunately, the Kavkaz Hotel is characterized not only by positive aspects, but also by significant shortcomings. Reviews of tourists contain a number of negative comments:

  • rather meager breakfast menu;
  • some catering workers serve guests very leisurely;
  • the lobby bar is open only in the evening;
  • some maids are not very conscientious about cleaning the rooms;
  • the hotel does not have its own adjacent territory, and therefore - there are no benches, no walking paths, or a playground;
  • in the rooms, whose windows face the street, it is simply impossible to sleep from round-the-clock noise, which is heard even through tightly closed windows;
  • on the site adjacent to the hotel there is a large pit with green foul-smelling water (there even frogs wound up);
  • the hotel does not have its own beach, and on public beds and other equipment are very expensive (from 200 rubles per person);
  • it was rather irrational to lay a light carpet in the room - it is very dirty, and apparently no one tried to clean it;
  • in the rooms that are next to the restaurant, there is a buzz from the exhaust pipe;
  • door jambs are located very low in interior doorways, which is inconvenient for tall people (you have to bend down all the time so as not to hit your head);
  • very noisy air conditioning that keeps you awake at night (and turning it off is also not possible due to the extreme heat);
  • hairdryer can be taken only on request at the reception;
  • inflated transfer prices - it will be much cheaper to call a taxi;
  • the TV shows only 9 channels (and those of poor quality);
  • old and creaking baby cots;
  • neither tables nor chairs are provided on the balconies;
  • in the mornings in the lobby and some rooms of the hotel there is a strong smell of food from the restaurant;
  • despite the fact that the furniture is quite new, it is already slightly worn;
  • the staff does not respond in any way to guest comments on malfunctions and breakdowns in rooms and requests to eliminate them;
  • Before laying out things in a closet, it is better to wipe it from the inside, otherwise there is a risk of staining your clothes.

General impression

Tourists have long been fond of the Kavkaz Hotel in Adler. Reviews note that its main advantage is its favorable location relative to the sea, urban infrastructure and tourist facilities. The bay where the hotel is located is considered environmentally friendly, and the water in this area is the cleanest in the whole city.

Speaking about the hotel itself, it is worth noting its novelty and attractive design. Guests especially note the availability of an elevator, which is especially important for relaxing with young children and the elderly. Nevertheless, the impression may be spoiled by the untidy adjacent territory, as well as the constant noise from the restaurant and neighboring establishments.
