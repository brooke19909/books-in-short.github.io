Hotel Iliada Beach Hotel 4 * (Protaras, Cyprus): photos and reviews of tourists

Many tourists who want to spend a few days basking in the sun and enjoying the waves of the sea choose Cyprus as a temporary haven. This island nation has something to offer tourists. By the way, one of the most popular resorts in the country is the city of Protaras. It is here, almost in the very center, that the Iliada Beach Hotel is located.

Of course, an experienced tourist before booking tries to get the most information available. Can a hotel location be considered profitable? Are there any conditions for a family holiday with a child? What are the impressions of the tourists who have already managed to spend several days at the hotel?

Where to look for a hotel and what does the hotel complex Iliada Beach Hotel 4 * (Protaras) look like?





The first point that you should pay attention to is the location of the hotel. In fact, Iliada Beach Hotel 4 * boasts a really convenient location, because the seaside is within easy reach. The front lawn goes directly to the beach.

By the way, this is a popular tourist area in the center of Protaras - within walking distance there will be all the entertainment and sights of the city, as well as numerous cafes and shops. Travelers usually land at Larnaca Airport, which is 60 km away. By the way, you can count on a convenient transfer, and the road does not take much time.

The hotel itself consists of a large five-story building, built in a modern style. The hotel area is not too big, but it is comfortably furnished and beautifully decorated - you will even have your own Mediterranean garden for walking.

Photo and description of rooms





Iliada Beach Hotel has 112 rooms. Most of these are standard rooms. Depending on the location, windows or balconies have sea or garden views. Their area, by the way, is 22 square meters. There are suites as well as more spacious family apartments with a separate living room.

According to reviews, the rooms are very clean. Everything is arranged simply, but elegantly - there is a set of necessary furniture and, of course, household appliances. On TV, guests can watch their favorite satellite channels. Tourists are also offered a safe, radio, telephone (the number can be dialed directly), as well as a small refrigerator (it is also a minibar). In the summer, guests use individual air conditioning, and in the cold periods of the year they include central heating.

The bathroom will delight you with a spacious bathtub and a modern shower. There is a toilet, washbasin and mirror, as well as clean towels (changed daily) and a hairdryer.

Food scheme: what should a traveler expect?





Food is an important element of a really good vacation. So what does the Iliada Beach Hotel offer the guest? When booking a tourist, he chooses a scheme convenient for himself, whether it is only breakfast or three meals a day, wholesome meals. By the way, the services of local restaurants can be used even without advance payment, but in this case, after each visit you will be presented with a check.

The cozy Argo Restaurant serves breakfast every day. In the evenings, guests are offered a generous buffet and the opportunity to try traditional Cypriot and Greek dishes. You can also visit the √† la carte restaurant Eleni, where guests are offered a wide selection of international and local cuisine, as well as a grill menu.

Thalassaki Bar Tables are located on the terrace, right by the pool. Here, guests can not only order drinks, but also enjoy homemade ice cream, desserts and light snacks. Armonia Cocktail Bar offers freshly baked pastries, aromatic coffee and a wide variety of cocktails.

You can eat outside the Iliada Beach Hotel. Protaras is a large resort city. Cafes and restaurants here are located literally at every step, so there are no problems with food.

Distance to the sea, amenities and entertainment on the beach





Most tourists want to spend their holidays enjoying the beauties of nature and relaxing on the seashore. What does the Iliada Beach Hotel offer? Corfu, Crete and, of course, Cyprus are considered the most popular islands with very picturesque beaches. And, as already mentioned, the hotel is located almost on the coast - the distance to a private beach covered with soft golden sand is not more than 50 meters. Moreover, the lawn of the hotel smoothly passes to a private coastline, and there is no fence here. Therefore, tourists can use the sun loungers and umbrellas that belong to the hotel for free. By the way, you can also get a beach towel here, but only on bail.

Traveler reviews say that the beach here is really very beautiful, the entrance to the sea is convenient, and the waves are small, which allows you to swim, and is also suitable for beginners who want to go windsurfing. By the way, there are many entertainments here - you can not only swim, but also swim with a mask, go water-skiing and boating, parasailing, and play beach volleyball. There is simply no time left to miss here.

Are additional services provided?

Entertainment and leisure: how do tourists spend time?





According to reviews, most travelers prefer spending time in the city or on the beach. But you can have some good fun in the hotel itself. In the courtyard there is a swimming pool, and nearby, on a neatly trimmed lawn there are sun loungers - guests can sunbathe, enjoying the breathtaking seascape.

From time to time, group sporting events are held here, including morning exercises, aerobics, and dancing. Nearby are tennis courts. You can pass the time while playing ping pong. And in the evenings, the hotel often plays live music, and animators often delight with fun and fiery performances. A popular activity for travelers is cycling around the area. And the hotel will tell you about the most interesting sights of the country and even help you choose an excursion tour.

Family holidays: living conditions with a child

The Iliada Beach Hotel complex is suitable for families with children, because here families are offered additional amenities, including spacious four-bed rooms, extra beds, high chairs in the restaurant. By the way, the menu here is quite large and includes simple, healthy food that is suitable for a child of any age.

Kids feel great on the beach, splashing in the warm sea waves. On the other hand, children often relax with their parents at the hotel, because for them there is a small pool. By the way, the hotel has babysitters who look after children, and for a relatively small fee.

A set of necessary equipment is available, and the rooms are cleaned well enough.

In the restaurant, tourists can always not only have a tasty meal, but also enjoy a calm, homely atmosphere. The food is tasty, and the selection of dishes is good. A huge advantage is the proximity to the beach and reasonable accommodation prices. Tourists recommend Iliada Beach Hotel. Cyprus is a place that can give a bright, unforgettable vacation.
