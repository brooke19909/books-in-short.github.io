Reviews: Blanes (Spain), or One Hour Rest

As you know, the entire coast of Spain is divided into resort areas. Each of them is characterized by a unique climate, type of coast, and water temperature regime. For example, the Costa Brava (in translation "coast of the brave") is very similar to the Crimean rocky coast, with many secluded coves with pebbles and sand. The water here quickly warms up, but also cools quickly, so the beach season on this coast lasts until the beginning of October.

But located just a few kilometers south of the Costa Maresme zone ("sea coast") is a sharp contrast with its neighbor. Wide sandy beaches, a flat landscape (mountains recede deep into the mainland), a sharp sunset in the sea are characteristic features of this section of the country. You can swim and sunbathe here until the end of October. The town of Blanes is officially located on the Costa Brava. But he is the southernmost settlement of this area. Description of the resort and reviews: Blanes, Spain

Most resorts of the Costa Brava were unlucky: in order to get to them, you need to change from a train to a bus. Blanes is "lucky" in this regard. There is a direct train from Barcelona. As the mountains begin further, in Blanes the railway line ends. Tourists arriving in Spain at the Girona airport can get to the resort by regular bus. What about accommodation and what are the local hotels? Blanes (Spain) reviews describe in very different ways.
