Sea, sun, summer, beach ... This is a unique place with an ideal climate. In connection with the recent political events, tourists have not decreased a bit. Quite the contrary! Those who were once captivated by local beauties return again and again to their favorite places.

The best seaside vacation spot in Russia is Crimea. He is ready to receive tourists throughout the year. Of course, the swimming season lasts from May to October. But even in winter, a tourist will find something to have fun on the peninsula. The list of attractions is simply impressive:

  • Golden Sands in Feodosia.
  • The Swallow's Nest is a legendary architectural structure.
  • The bays of Cape Fiolent are a picturesque place.
  • Massandra Palace Museum.
  • Cave city Mangup (Middle Ages).
  • Marble cave Chatyr-Dag.

Crimea is an ideal place to relax with the whole family. For children there is a lot of entertainment.

But for active and courageous youth, we recommend visiting Cape Tarkanhun. Surfers and divers gather here every summer. And the highest peak of the Ai-Petri Peninsula in winter is a paradise for skiers and snowboarders.

Of course, not all places for recreation are listed. It all depends on your time, desires and material means. And to see everything, everyone needs to come here again and again. And every time the peninsula will surprise you.

Top 12 interesting destinations for holidays in Russia

In order not to throw hard-earned money to the wind, we will consider vacation options in Russia. The ranking of the best places, landscapes, attractions and cultures is presented below.

1. Karelia

The Republic of Karelia is considered one of the most beautiful places in Russia. This unique region is located in the northwestern part of the country. Often families come here from all over Russia to spend their holidays. And not by chance. In Karelia there are many beautiful picturesque waterfalls, lakes, powerful forests.

The largest settlement is Ruskeala. It is in this place that an amazing park is located, which was built of marble.

Extreme lovers will also find something to do here. You can go diving.

2. Kabardino-Balkaria

This is a small North Caucasian republic, which is striking in its features and picturesqueness. It is in this place - the highest point in Europe - Elbrus. Therefore, tourists come here from all over the world.

But this is not one attraction of Kabardino-Balkaria. What is the Cherek gorge with its blue lakes, the Chegem waterfalls, the Baksan gorge leading to Elbrus - and these places of rest in Russia need to be seen firsthand.

You can plunge into greatness, breathe in full with fresh healing air in the capital of the republic - Nalchik.

Be sure to visit one of the oldest volcanoes of Mingi-Tau, the eternal mountain of wisdom, the national park and the tract of Jily-Su. Of course, the vacation will not be complete if you do not climb the sacred Elbrus.

3. Mountain Ingushetia

Arriving in this country, every tourist as if plunges into a real fairy tale. Unique mountain landscapes, original monuments, towers, ancient legends and myths. Ingushetia is a ‚Äútime machine‚Äù country that allows you to return a thousand years ago.

The real treasure of this mountainous country is the unique towers and temples. The largest tower community is Egical. It is called a ghost town. Tagim is also the oldest village, but the "younger brother" of Egical. Vovnushki - a place of military and majestic towers that once inflicted death on opponents.

But the diamond of Ingushetia is the ancient temple of Christians - Thaba-Erda. However, the history of its creation is far from Orthodox.

Ingushetia is like another planet in which time stops and takes your breath away from the local beauties.

4. Kamchatka

The best places to stay in Russia are located in Kamchatka. This is one of the most interesting places in his native country. Travelers from all over the world dream of getting here.

Kamchatka is not a Crimean resort. Most likely, rest here will be active-extreme. Due to the wild and harsh nature, Kamchatka has its own special appeal. Volcanoes, geyser valleys, canyons, blue and acid lakes are the most picturesque and beautiful places on the peninsula.

Be sure to go fishing and try the real Kamchatka caviar! Many tourists bathe in the Paratunka thermal springs. And if you come to Kamchatka in the winter, don‚Äôt be afraid and from the bottom of your heart ride in a team with a husky.

And for dessert - the Komandorski Islands, which seem to be lost in Kamchatka. Here is a place for solitude with oneself, nature and thoughts. Where else can you feel this?

5. Baikal

How much is hidden in this word! This is the most picturesque and mysterious lake on the whole planet. Great Baikal - more than 20 million years. Can you imagine? It is the deepest lake in the whole world. Moreover, the most unique and precious in it is 19% of fresh water reserves.

You can talk about the nature of Baikal for hours without a break. No matter where you go, you won‚Äôt be disappointed anywhere. You will fall in love with Baikal!

Be sure to visit the largest island of Baikal - Olkhon. Do not forget to admire the legendary Shaman-rock, to taste delicious omul in the village of Listvyanka. Did you know that in one of the places of power on Lake Baikal you need to make the most cherished desires?

Baikal is so huge in area that it will not be possible to see all the beauty in one trip. But, having been here once, you will definitely come back again.

6. The Republic of Komi

One of the best places to stay in Russia is concentrated in the Komi Republic. However, not every tourist dares to come here on vacation. There are no ski resorts, resorts. The Komi Republic is harsh and wild nature, powerful forests untouched by man, pristine lakes - romance that is not understood by everyone.

One of the main attractions is the weathering pillars on the Man-Pupu-Ner plateau. There are seven of them, and the height is up to 42 meters. In 2008, this attraction was included in the list of seven wonders of Russia.

You should definitely visit one of the largest reserves in our country - "Yugyd Va". A huge territory with wildlife that has not been touched by man. Pristine and majestic beauty is simply amazing.

If you are brave and hardy, you cannot live without extreme sports, then conquer hard-to-reach mountain peaks. The highest points of the Ural Mountains are Manaraga and Narodnaya.

7. The Golden Ring

This is a whole group of 12 ancient cities. This list was created back in the days of the Soviet Union to attract foreign tourists. Vladimir, Ivanovo, Pereslavl-Zalessky, Kostroma, Rostov, Sergiev Posad, Suzdal and Yaroslavl. It is these best places to stay in Russia that reveal the history and culture of the great and mighty Russia.

8. Curonian Spit, Kaliningrad Region

Sandy spit of amazing beauty is located on the coast of the Baltic Sea and the Curonian Lagoon. This narrow and long strip of land separates the bay from the sea and extends from Zelenogradsk to Lithuania.

The unusual name of the braid is not accidental. The ancient Curonian tribes lived here before the colonization of Prussia. In 2000, this unique natural phenomenon was listed by UNESCO.

9. Lake Seliger, Tver and Novgorod regions

This is another place that needs no introduction. Seliger is also called Ostashkovsky lake. Because on the lake shore is the city of Ostashkov.

Seliger has more than 160 islands. The largest of them is Khachin.

The lake lies at an altitude of 205 m above sea level. It has glacial roots. Because of this, he has a peculiar form. This is not an ordinary lake, but rather looks like a chain of lakes, which are connected by 110 channels. This is one of the best places to stay in Russia.

10. Eastern Siberia

Sayans in the East and West - two mountain systems - the best places of rest in the south of Russia.

Western Sayan up to 3971 m high is Mount Mongun-Taiga. It is the largest peak in Eastern Siberia. On the slopes - mountain taiga, which goes into the tundra.

Western Sayan is located near Altai. The highest point of its ridge is Kyzyl-Taiga Mountain. Steep slopes, rugged terrain, vast areas of placers of stone - this is what is worth seeing firsthand.

Almost everywhere in the Sayan mountains there are spruce-cedar-fir forests. In Eastern Siberia, a rich flora and fauna.

The largest city, which is located in the Sayan Mountains, is Krasnoyarsk. These are the best places to stay in September. Russia will surprise you!

11. Volcano Krenitsyna, Sakhalin Oblast

This active volcano impresses with its grandeur. It is located on the island of Onekotan of the Great Kuril Ridge.

Krenitsyna volcano is recognized as the largest two-tier volcano. Its height is 1324 m. This volcano is called a ‚Äúvolcano in a volcano‚Äù because of its unusual shape.

His cone rises in the form of an island. Around the lake itself are the camps of the ancient Tao-Rusyr caldera.

The volcano is on the list of beautiful places in Russia for relaxation and impressions.

It is known from history that the volcano erupted only once - in 1952.

12. Ivolginsky datsan. Republic of Buryatia, the village of Verhnaya Ivolga

This largest Buddhist monastery complex is the center of the Buddhist traditional Sangha in the Russian Federation. Ivolginsky datsan is one of the brightest historical buildings. It is located in the village of Upper Oriole, which is near Ulan-Ude.

Even if you have a different faith, then you just have to look at this greatest architectural structure.

The best places to stay in Russia in winter

The proud North Ossetia is famous for its greatest mountains. Here is another world with its own culture, foundations, customs and mores. Vladikavkaz, the capital of the republic, welcomes tourists with crystal clear air, mountain peaks and the seething water of the formidable Terek. This unique city will be remembered for a long time. An indelible impression will make on you the symbol of North Ossetia, an old male deity - a monument to Uastirdzhi. It rises above the Transcaucasian Highway like a horseman who flies out of the mountain and freezes over the road. Zeygalan waterfall, the city of the dead Dargavs, the legendary towers of Kurt and Tag, the unique and majestic Kuratin gorge - all this must be seen with your own eyes!

Mountain Altai ... Translated from the Turkic language, "Altan" means "golden". Can not argue with that! Unique nature, literally golden natural places, mountain ranges, turbulent rivers, placers of deep caves and seething hands, cave paintings that preserve history and wisdom - and this is all about Altai.

This is the place of legends of the ancient Scythians. If you believe the belief, it is in this place that the crown of everything is hidden - the entrance to Shambhala. This unique place you need to visit without rushing. Gallop to go and put a check mark will not work. Altai is a thoughtful and serious place. If you open your soul to him, he will answer in the same way - he will give mesmerizing places, cast a spell on you, and settle down in your heart forever.

Be sure to visit the harsh North Chuysky ridge. Here are fabulous glaciers and stunning views. The highest point in Altai is Belukha Mountain.

It is saturated with mysticism and attracts with its power. If you want to tickle your nerves, then go along the suspension bridge leading to the island of Patmos. Visit the granite cliffs near Belokurikha, which are connected by an interesting legend. They are called the "four brothers." And here is why, you will find out for yourself.
