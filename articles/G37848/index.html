How on Beeline to connect the "Promised payment" yourself?

Even the most conscientious customers of mobile operators sometimes do not have the opportunity to timely deposit funds to their account. Situations can be different: someone just does not have time to visit the office, someone has turned off the Internet and you can’t transfer a payment from a credit card and so on. In order to maintain loyalty, almost all mobile operators offer their customers to use special services in such situations. In the Beeline company, it has the name "Trust payment". Using this service, you can stay in touch for several days without replenishing your account balance. How to connect the “Promised payment” on Beeline and what are the conditions for its use, we will consider in this article.





To whom is the “Trust payment” service provided?

The ability to receive funds from Beeline is provided to all current subscribers (individuals) who are eligible for the following conditions:

  • the period of use of the SIM card should be at least 2 months (for some tariffs this threshold is slightly higher - at least 3 months);
  • expenses for cellular services (calls, Internet, etc. opportunities) for the last 2 months should be at least 50 rubles.

Customers of the company who have been using communication services for several months and have corresponding expenses are entitled to activate the promised Beeline payment. At the same time, the phone acts as a tool for connecting this option. Using certain combinations of symbols, you can get funds into your account almost instantly. Also, the possibility of obtaining such a "loan" is provided via the Internet, namely on the official website of the mobile operator. Here on the page of the service "Trust payment" you just need to specify the phone number.





Terms of Service

Like any service that provides additional opportunities for the client, “Trust payment” has a number of conditions and features. Consider them below.

Trust amount

For each customer of Beeline, there is a personal amount that can be issued to him “on credit”. Its calculation is carried out mainly on the basis of the amount of expenses for a person’s mobile communication services over the past three months. The more money a mobile operator receives, the greater the amount he is ready to provide as a trust payment. How on Beeline to connect the “Promised payment” if less than 50 rubles were spent on mobile services for the specified period? Activation of "Trust payment" in this case will be impossible, since the minimum amount of expenses should be at least 50 rubles. Thus, if the client spends up to 70 rubles on communication over the past three months, then the amount of 30 rubles will be available to him as a trust payment. For other subscribers, the following data applies:

  • up to 400 rubles - 100 rubles;
  • up to 700 rubles - 200 rubles;
  • up to a thousand rubles - 250 rubles;
  • over 1000 rubles - 500 rubles.




Fixed payment for using the service

“Promised payment” (“Beeline”), the command for activation of which will be given below, is connected for an additional fee. Its cancellation occurs after the expiration of the service - that is, after three days.

How to connect the “Promised payment” on Beeline?

Activation of the service for Beeline customers can be carried out using one of the above options.

1. Via mobile phone:

  • entering a USSD command - * 141 #;
  • a call to the automatic auto-information system (AVR) at number 064012.

In the first case, you must enter the specified combination on your phone and follow the prompts of the system. It is preliminary recommended to clarify how much you can get to your account. To do this, dial the sequence of characters: * 141 * 7 #.

In the second case, the activation of the service is possible by calling 064012. Here you can also specify the size of the "Promised Payment" and activate it immediately.





2. Through a customer service specialist at number 0611. If the subscriber is interested in the question of how to activate the Promised Payment on Beeline, he should know in advance that the service of a technical support employee will cost an additional charge of 30 rubles from the account. Thus, the activation of “Trust payment” instead of 15 rubles will cost 45 (an additional fee is charged for the services of a specialist company).

How to activate the "Promised payment" on "Beeline" via the Internet?

Having access to the Internet, you can also activate the "Trust payment". To do this, go to the Beeline official portal, log in to your account and activate the service. If the subscriber does not have the ability or desire to enter account information, then you can simply go to the main menu of the site and find "Trust payment". Enter your number on the page of this service. Activation of the service will occur in a few minutes.

Validity

“Promised payment” (“Beeline”), the command for activation of which was given earlier, is connected for 3 days. At the same time, the subscriber has the opportunity to suspend the service at any time. To do this, simply replenish the account: if the amount that covers the payment received (including the amount for connecting the service - rubles) is received on the balance, then the full repayment of the debt will occur.





Conclusion

The service "Trust payment" is able to help out the subscriber in any situation when it is not possible to deposit funds into the account, but it is necessary to use communication services. To activate it, just enter the code "Promised payment" ("Beeline") or activate it in any other way.
