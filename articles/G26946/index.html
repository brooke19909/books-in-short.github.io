Pension "Tatyana", Koblevo: photos and reviews

Where to go to relax? Surely each of you asked this question. Moreover, many want to combine both leisure and recreation, and it is imperative that it is inexpensive. Is this possible? Yes, if you are considering a vacation option in the village of Koblevo, in the Black Sea. This is an opportunity to enjoy a wonderful vacation and restore health. There is no industry, and pine forest creates optimal climatic conditions.



Resort Features

It is here, on the luxurious coast, the Tatiana boarding house is located. Koblevo is a clean beach and small shell rock. The sea in these places is not deep, thanks to which it warms up quickly. It is an ideal place to relax with young children. Here everyone can find entertainment to their liking. Attractions and carousels, discos and cafes, bars. For amateurs and connoisseurs of history it will be very interesting to make excursions to Nikolaev. Here you can see the excavations of the ancient Greek colony of Olbia, where the ruins of the fortifications have been preserved. Throughout the year you are invited to the pension "Tatiana". Koblevo is great in any weather.



Location

If you decide to spend your vacation here, then you will definitely not regret it. Pension "Tatyana" (Koblevo) is located in the most picturesque corner of the village. The distance to the beach is only 300 meters. There is a wide strip of fine sand with a gentle entrance to the sea. The total length is 5 km. But special attention deserves the territory of the boarding house. The area is huge, about 1.5 hectares. It is very clean and well maintained, with numerous flower beds and gazebos, squares and lawns. Here you can walk for days, and it will not be boring. A neat four-story building was erected for tourists. Separately built dining room. All rooms are air-conditioned.

How to get there

An unforgettable vacation awaits you here at any time of the year. Koblevo (boarding house "Tatyana") is the best place for those who travel by car. It is located in the designated village, on Kurortny Avenue. Most tourists come here from Odessa. For them, the optimal route runs along the Odessa-Nikolaev highway. Upon reaching the village, take the road T1516. Having reached almost to the end, turn onto Nikolaevskaya Street, then to Vostochnaya, and from here you will already get directly to Kurortny Avenue. The distance from Odessa to Koblevo is only 55 km, that is, it is an ideal place for a weekend tour.

If you do not have a personal car - it does not matter. Regular buses, minibuses and taxis go to the resort village. It is enough to get to Odessa, and then the road itself will tell you an option.



Boarding house services

There is a basic package, which is paid along with a ticket. This accommodation is in comfortable rooms with hot and cold water. There is a safe on the ground floor of the hotel. All tourists can use the parking, luggage storage, sports and children's playgrounds. On the territory there are many gazebos that can be used as desired.

Additionally, there are paid services at the request of the client. These are water rides and rental yachts or small boats. The huge water park will not let children get bored, and adults can spend time interestingly and profitably. To do this, there is a tour desk that offers a huge number of interesting tours.



Residence

Pension "Tatiana" (Koblevo) provides cozy, comfortable rooms, consisting of double or triple rooms. All are equipped with beds and bedside tables, a table and chairs. For the convenience of guests there is a clothes rack, TV and fridge, a balcony and a bathroom. The interior itself is nothing special, the walls are either painted or upholstered with clapboard.

Short description

In total, the boarding house is designed to accommodate 330 tourists. For this, there is a four-story brick building. It is equipped with centralized hot water, which is supplied around the clock. Standard rooms on the ground floor are designed for two visitors. Inside there is a balcony and all amenities (shower, toilet and wash basin). A small fridge and TV complete the picture. Area - 14 m2. This is the best choice for a couple who decided to visit the pension "Tatyana" (Koblevo). Reviews (2016) show that not everyone wants to relax in five-star hotels. Many tourists have enough cleanliness and comfort that prevail here. In addition, there is simply no time to be in the room, there are too many interesting things around.



For the whole family

Triple rooms of the standard type are located on the upper floors. Here, to other delights, the opportunity to view the surroundings and drink coffee in the fresh air in the evening is added. The rooms have all the amenities, appliances and new furniture. At the choice of tourists, it can be white or brown. Guests note that this is an excellent guesthouse for relaxation without any complaints. The rooms are not too large, very reminiscent of the Soviet past. But there is everything you need in order to sleep and relax. It is very convenient that the on-duty group of house masters is constantly working here. If a sink clogged, a pipe leaked, or another trouble happened, feel free to call.

A huge plus is Euro windows and a mosquito net. The estuary is nearby, only 30 meters, so the insects get out in the evenings. Repair and its innovations came in handy. Superior rooms

They are located on the third floor. These are rooms with an increased area, so they are perfect for a large family and just for lovers of comfort. There is a spacious balcony, large plastic windows with mosquito nets, an enlarged bathroom.

Many tourists are interested in how organized the holiday in Koblevo is. Reviews say that, despite all the advantages, it remains a Soviet resort in essence. The service can not be called bad, but the friendliness and warmth inherent in European recreation areas are clearly not enough. The second minus is people smoking on balconies. However, this applies not only to this resort. After all, since there are no specially designated rooms for this, you have to put up with this state of affairs.

Cost of living

This is a great option for a budget holiday. Pension Tatyana (Koblevo, 2016) invites tourists to spend their vacations in accordance with their wishes. The price depends on the month of residence, however it is quite low. In general, varies from 215 to 320 UAH (about 520-770 rubles) per day per person with meals. If you plan to have lunch while walking in the surroundings, you can take a package without meals. In this case, the price is even more interesting, from 60 to 160 UAH (about 150-380 rubles). Children under 5 years old stay free of charge, and from 5 to 12 a discount applies. The sooner you decide to get a ticket - the better. When booking in advance, there is an interesting system of bonuses and discounts.



Catering for tourists

Judging by the reviews - just great. A delicious porridge is prepared for breakfast, hot and very tasty. The soups are delicious, not every cafe can offer such a quality. Sandwiches, snacks, once a week red caviar, fruits and vegetables - that is, no one will remain hungry. But rations for the tour are very modest. In the set is a bun, water and juice.

Tourists reviews

Clean sea, fresh air, comfortable rooms and delicious food - this is what distinguishes the Tatiana boarding house in Koblevo. Photos taken here delight in high brightness and contrast. They will please you with pleasant memories for a long time. Compared to other recreation facilities, where they drink alcohol and smoke barbecue without any control, it is very quiet, comfortable and decent here. Great nature and the ability to go on excursions - this is an added bonus to your vacation. Be sure to come here with the whole family.
