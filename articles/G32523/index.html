Nose hair trimmer: principle of operation, types

And if hairs stick out from the nose or ears, for example, then the beautiful is not enough. But everything is fixable. Modern technologies have found application here, a compact and easy-to-use device has appeared. Let's see what a machine for cutting hair in the nose (trimmer) is, whether it is effective, what is the mechanism of its work.

Appearance

Panasonic ER-430K

The device is small in size, looks like a thickened handle. On the body of each model is a button on and off. The rest is nothing special. Different nose clippers can vary in shape, color, and even size.

Principle of operation

Trimmers appeared a long time ago, but before they were very primitive and not quite convenient to use. The very first models were powered by hands. One hand held the device, and the other rotated a special handle to engage the blades in this way. Then came more advanced models, but also mechanical ones. For the blades to start working in them, you need to press on a special device all the time. Also not the best option, although it will cost much cheaper than an electric model.



Speaking of the latter. A battery-powered nose hair trimmer is the best option. It is enough to turn on the device and arrange the working part with blades in the nose. The result will be instant. The cost of such devices ranges from 10-30 dollars. On sale you can find more expensive models, the equipment of which implies the presence of additional nozzles, for example, in order to smooth the eyebrows, remove hair on the neck, etc.

Benefits

The hair clipper in the nose is a great appliance for home use. Agree, removing hair from the nose with scissors or tweezers is not very convenient, and it hurts. The trimmer, especially the electric one, works quickly, which means that the pain will be felt much less. In addition, this machine is safe to use. Many models have a backlight, which is also convenient, you can see where the hairs are located and whether they are well removed.

Philips NT8110

Hair clipper: reviews

What can I say? After analyzing a certain number of reviews, it becomes obvious that the device is good, there are practically no complaints about its operation. Of course, a lot can depend on the model, but basically most of them do their job well. Hair is removed qualitatively, grow back more slowly. But do not forget that the trimmer cannot remove hair once and for all, i.e. periodically the procedure will have to be repeated. But the machine for cutting hair in the nose does not take much time for its use, moreover, you can always take it with you even on long trips.
