The recreation center "Poseidon" (Berdyansk): description and reviews

Tourist shelter "Poseidon" (Berdyansk) is located in one of the most environmentally friendly places in the resort. This is a local reserve. It is called the Berdyansk scythe, and this recreation center is located in its far corner. Despite the fact that the reserve is remote from the city, there are comfortable housing, a mini-zoo with wild animals, water fun, and a dolphinarium on its territory. There are many estuaries around with healing water and healing mud. And children will like the new water park "Cape of Good Hope" with many slides. Administratively, this area is divided into three zones - Near, Middle and Far. Throughout the scythe stretches the only street - Makarova. The recreation center "Poseidon" (Berdyansk) is located on it. Therefore, getting lost here is almost impossible.



Territory and services

Base "Poseidon" (Berdyansk) is a few two-story wooden cottages. They are located in close proximity to the sea. The territory of the base is not too big, but very well-groomed, clean and incredibly beautifully decorated in the style of a fabulous village. She is fenced and guarded. Everywhere there are many wooden arbors. Tourists who have been here write that flowers and conifers are planted everywhere, and the administration is very concerned about the comfort of vacationers. You can park the car in a guarded parking lot, play tennis. Since Berdyansk has been developing as a children's resort for many decades, the tourist shelter has many services for kids. This is a modern playground, and a small zoo. The Internet works not only in the territory of the base, it also catches the network in rooms. Tourists praise the masseur base for diligence and professionalism.



Number of rooms

Among the materials from which the buildings of the Poseidon Hotel (Berdyansk) are built, there is not only wood, but also granite. Thanks to this, in all rooms of the mini-hotel it is quite cool even in the heat. However, all rooms are air-conditioned. Premises for guests at the base are rooms consisting of one or two rooms, depending on the number of residents. Everywhere there is a TV with satellite channels. Hot and cold water constantly. For comfort, all rooms can be classified as junior suites and higher. Vacationers write that the main highlight of this holiday is a dream with an open window to the sound of waves. Indeed, to the sea no more than seventy meters from the farthest number!

Nutrition

On the basis of "Poseidon" (Berdyansk) there is a cafe where you can eat on the custom menu. A shared kitchen also allows people to cook for themselves products purchased at a store or market. Tourists praise the cooks for delicious lunches and dinners. The food is homely, quality, varied. The dishes of Ukrainian, Tatar and European cuisines dominate. You can order a special menu for children. There are barbecue grills for outdoor barbecue. There is a bar where they make good cocktails.



What tourists say about sea vacations

Mini-hotel "Poseidon" (Berdyansk) not only stands on the first line, but also has its own beach. He, like everywhere else on the spit, is huge and amazing. The sea is clean, the coast with a fine white sand. Here, the zone of personal comfort is perfectly felt. The beach is equipped with comfortable sun loungers and awnings. A lot of water activities - riding a scooter, an inflatable banana, parachuting. There are several stalls on the beach selling drinks and ice cream. Nearby is one of the oldest lighthouses in Ukraine, which still indicates the way to ships. Neither youth nor more mature people are bored here. In addition, not only more and more Ukrainians come to this resort, but also vacationers from abroad. Moreover, the base administration is trying to improve the service every year.
