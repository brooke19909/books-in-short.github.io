Intelligent machine

Fuzzy Logic functions - what is it in practice? The system of built-in sensors and sensors, thanks to which the volume of linen, the level of pollution of things, water hardness and many other initial parameters are estimated. These sensors send information to the microprocessor, which, on the basis of the data received, sets the device to a program that takes into account the duration of the wash, the degree of heating, the flow rate of the powder, the amount of water rinsed, the spin power, and the operating mode as a whole.

Fuzzy Logic in the washing machine is a system of built-in sensors that will analyze the level of soiling of the laundry, and the microprocessor of the device will calculate the duration of the wash. In addition to the level of contamination, touch sensors will help establish the nature of the contamination, the composition of the fabric and the hardness of the water in order to choose the optimal, gentle operation.

We can say that the technology fully controls the washing machine using several initial parameters. In combination, they make it possible to operate with hundreds of mode options (!), This is a different automatic machine with Fuzzy Logic from a simple automatic device. And between it and the model a semiautomatic device (low- class washing machine ) runs through the abyss.



Why is Fuzzy Logic technology often compared to feminine logic?

In order to catch the similarity of the fuzzy logic and behavior of a woman, it is necessary to restore to the smallest detail the process of creating any dish in the kitchen by her. For example, during the preparation of the dough, an experienced hostess will never immediately take the amount of flour exactly according to the recipe, because she perfectly understands that the optimal amount depends on many circumstances. A woman will take as much product as needed to get the desired density of the dough, adding flour several times during the batch, if necessary, to achieve the best result. The same principle is at the forefront of the Fuzzy Logic function. What is it then, if not female logic?



Strengths and Profitability

The work of devices with Fuzzy Logic gives significant advantages:

  • reduced washing time as a result of accurate assessment of the level of contamination;
  • reduction in water consumption due to preliminary determination of the weight of things and control over foaming;
  • minimum energy consumption due to a decrease in the volume of water for heating;
  • full automation of the washing process, which eliminates the need to manually set the spin speed, duration of work, the number of rinses, the desired temperature of the water, etc. (for a person, the decision is made by a ‚Äúsmart‚Äù machine);
  • saving powders, laundry bleaches and other products that use washing machines.
