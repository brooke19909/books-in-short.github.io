Hotels in Tyumen: a list with addresses and phone numbers, reviews

Tyumen is the first Russian city to appear in Siberia. Today, this city can be safely called the concentration of the country's oil and gas industry, because there are a lot of industries associated with oil production and its refining. Those who have to travel on business trips, or those who just want to get acquainted with this Siberian city, are sure to find out about Tyumen hotels, the list with addresses and phone numbers of which is very extensive.

The abundance of churches in Tyumen

On the territory of modern Tyumen there are not only industrial plants. A large number of cultural and architectural monuments have been preserved here, some of which arose even before Siberia entered Russia. Here is a large complex of the Trinity Monastery, which was formerly called Preobrazhensky, it was founded in 1616. It includes beautiful cathedrals and churches, walls of stone and the chambers of the abbots. Near the bend of the Tura River there is the Ascension Church with a bell tower, erected in 1739, and not far from it are the buildings belonging to the convent, founded in 1601. Here, in the cemetery, there is the church-chapel of All Saints and the rotunda. In general, there are a lot of ancient churches and cathedrals in the city, so if you are interested in Orthodox buildings, then you definitely need to come here.





What else to see in Tyumen

Of course, there are also enough monuments of urban architecture. They are: the former city government, which now houses the museum of local lore, the guest house, which functioned in the 30s of the XIX century, the post office (beginning of the XIX century). Of particular importance are the wooden buildings of Tyumen, preserved from the late XIX - early XX centuries.

The Tyumen Museum of Fine Arts is well known in the city, the year of foundation of which was 1957. It contains a magnificent collection of sculptures, paintings and works of applied art by both Russian and foreign masters.

The variety of hotels in Tyumen

To find hotels of Tyumen that are suitable for any tourist, the list of which is very diverse, is very easy. After all, there are both simple hotels and hotels that are assigned 3 or 4 stars.

Among the hotels that are assigned 4 stars, one can distinguish the following: business hotel Eurasia and Best Western Plus Spasskaya. Of the three-star hotels in the city, you should pay attention to the Vostok and Cote d'Azur hotels.





If tourists prefer cheap hotels, there are some in Tyumen. For example, you can stay in the "Moscow Courtyard" or in the "Aurora", which has everything you need for a simple holiday.

Information about the above Tyumen hotels, telephones and their addresses can be found below in this article. Thanks to this information, any future guest of this Siberian city will surely find a hotel to his liking and affordable.

Business Hotel Eurasia

For some tourists it may not be easy to choose a hotel in Tyumen. In the city center, for example, there are a number of good hotels, each of which is attractive in its own way. Business Hotel Eurasia is one of these places located at 20. Sovetskaya Street. It offers guests not only comfortable accommodation, but also a modern business center with equipped conference rooms. There are also a variety of restaurants and bars. There are 87 rooms, they are all very spacious. Guests can choose from eight different categories, among which there are various standards and suites, comforts, studios and even a special wedding room. The price tag here starts from 4900 rubles. for the standard "atrium" and ends with 15,000 rubles. for a business suite.





Visitors to this hotel in Tyumen leave reviews mostly enthusiastic. The hotel is praised by almost everyone, even admire the free minibar in the room. However, some guests noted that room prices could be lower.

Best Western Plus Spasskaya

The name of this hotel in Tyumen speaks of its belonging to an international hotel chain. It is located in the city center and boasts excellent service and amazing comfortable rooms. It has its own pastry shop, swimming pool, sauna and gym. Guests are surrounded by a very sophisticated interior both in the rooms and throughout the hotel. A multilingual staff will make any foreign guest stay comfortable.

To get to the Spasskaya Hotel, one must come to 2a Lenin Street. Here, tourists are waiting for well-equipped with the latest technology rooms of such categories as standard, comfort, twin, business, luxury and presidential. A day in a standard room will cost 4,500 rubles, and the most expensive presidential number will cost 18,000 rubles.

Reviews about this hotel are beyond praise. Guests praise both rooms and conference facilities. However, the prices in this place are considered by many to be unreasonably high.





Acceptable three star hotels

For those who are not looking for pompous luxury, but also do not want to relax in cheap and simple rooms without additional amenities, there are three-star hotels in Tyumen with quite adequate and reasonable prices for accommodation.

In hotels such as Vostok and Cote d'Azur, guests will find not only comfortable and clean rooms, but also additional services such as a sauna, own restaurant and bars.

Cheap accommodation in Tyumen

If you are one of those tourists who do not need luxury and expensive rooms, then hotels in Tyumen economy class - this is what is perfect for you. Guests will not find gyms and pools in them, but they can count on simple, cozy rooms and very reasonable prices.

Such places are well located if you plan a lot of excursions or walks around the city. Also, this is a great option for those who find themselves in the city passing through or simply do not have a lot of money.

Economy Hotel Moscow Courtyard

Inexpensive hotels in Tyumen are easy to find. Therefore, if you do not need luxury, then feel free to go to the Moskovsky Dvorik Hotel, which is located at 37 Moskovsky Trakt Street, Building 1. Only 10 minutes from the railway station and 25 from the airport.

For little money in this hotel you can get great accommodation without any frills. This option is very suitable for some tourists, however, sometimes they complain about the very poor soundproofing in the rooms.

Aurora - inexpensive rooms and apartments

Looking for cheap hotels in Tyumen? Aurora is a great option for you - a hotel in which there are rooms for different numbers of vacationers, as well as separate apartments. Here guests are offered: free Wi-Fi and additional discounts on hot springs and access to fitness centers. Daily cleaning of the premises, a shared kitchen with the necessary equipment, a special place for storing luggage - all this awaits vacationers in the Aurora. Employees will be happy to answer all additional questions by phone: 8-3452-692348.

This place is recommended as a great budget option. The rooms have new furniture, very warm and comfortable. Judging by the reviews, the Aurora hotel is more like a hostel, as it is positioned on some sites, but the price-quality ratio is fully justified.
