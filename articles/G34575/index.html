Sianovsky quarries: reviews of excursions, photos

Syanovsky quarries are limestone cavities of natural and artificial origin. Syanas are partially formed by karst cavities and funnels formed under the influence of natural phenomena.

The main caves arose due to the extraction of snow-white limestone. White stone was extracted from here from the 17th to the 20th century. The stone, shining with whiteness, was used for the construction of Moscow. From this limestone, runways were built at Domodedovo Airport. By the 40s of the last century, the Syanovsky quarries were closed.

Historical facts

The interest of tourists in abandoned mines erupted in the 70s of the last century. The activity of researchers, their powerful pilgrimage to the caves worried the authorities. By their order in 1974, the entrance to the caves was bricked up with concrete.





A group of old cavers - the pioneers of Xiang - discovered the caves 14 years later, in 1988. The names of the daredevils are inscribed on the wall in one of the grottoes. Currently, these quarries are a popular excursion site near Moscow. Up to a hundred people enter the caves on weekends.

general description

It is believed that the Syanovsky quarries, photos of which are made by tourists who descended into them, reach a length of approximately 70 kilometers. Cavers currently studied tunnels with a length of about 30 kilometers. Their height varies, reaching 0.4-3.5 meters. Moreover, the total depth of calcareous rocks is 25-30 meters.

The entrance to the caves is uncomfortable, it is narrow and constantly in wet mud due to the rains that flood it. Rains do not penetrate deep into the catacombs; there is no such slimy dirt as at the entrance. The miniature grotto, which is located next to the entrance - the place of the general meeting of cavers.

It contains the cave Journal, reminiscent of a ship. Catacomb researchers, before being sent to the caves and after returning from them, enter their names (logins) in a huge notebook, accompanying them with witty inscriptions and funny drawings.

The journal reflects the statistics of visits. Thanks to him, it is always known how many tourists explore the Syanovsky quarries. The speleologists' reviews confirm that by opening the Journal, at any time you can find out if any group is being delayed beyond measure in the cave labyrinth. Perhaps she should already be contacted, to find out the situation in which people are, and if necessary, to help.

The Syanovsky quarries are dotted with natural funnels, legendary grottoes, tunnels into which each visitor penetrates, little-known drifts, caves that are under excavation, and those passages that are still tightly blocked up. Some of them have become popular attractions.

Passages from the natural karst - the famous grotto of Gromov - lead to the caves "Smoking-room" and "Bottle". Laz is named after the speleologist Gromov. Tunnel "Pocket"

"Pocket" is a cleft that all cavers "love". The tourists' comment about the grotto is this: the passage through the tunnel is a dream at night. Memories of him excite the imagination, causing an involuntary laugh. A gaping hole is terrifying even when viewed. Down the tunnel, all the daredevils fly with a whistle, and when climbing they have to make incredible efforts. This grotto must be passed to everyone who falls into the Syanovsky quarries.

Grotto "Master and Margarita"

The cave arch, shining with a moire glow, is decorated with a fresco "Margarita on a broomstick" and a cross hollowed out in the wall. Here, under the cross, a tiny candlelight is lit, when the fire goes out, a shadow slowly creeps onto the cross, creating an infernal picture.

Eyes Xiang

Venus Grotto (Eyes of the Caves) is a narrow vertical cleft located between two tunnels. The floor level here varies from different sides. On the one hand, the floor is about 1.5 meters higher. It is believed that only girls with a very slender figure are able to penetrate into the narrow gap.
