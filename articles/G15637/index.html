Solim Inn Hotel 3 * (Turkey / Kemer) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists





Kirish is another resort town that can be found in Turkey. It is very difficult to call this place a city, because the infrastructure is not very developed there, and in the evenings Kirish turns into a quiet and calm seaside paradise. People often come here to take a break from the hustle and bustle of big cities. However, despite the fact that you will not find noisy clubs and large luxurious restaurants here, in the town next to Kemer there are several very decent hotels where you can always stay if you have such a desire. One is the Solim Inn Hotel 3 *, ready to impress guests with its service and proximity to the sea.

Solim Inn Hotel 3 * (Kemer Kiris) is located in the immediate vicinity of a fairly large city called Kemer. If you go by taxi, then the Solim Inn Hotel 3 * can be reached in just 40-45 minutes.

Room service

A very important factor when choosing a hotel is how well and conscientiously the guide treats the room service it provides to its settlers. Solim Inn Hotel 3 * will provide tourists who have expressed a desire to spend their holidays here with all the necessary amenities. The hotel rooms are very comfortable and guests will always feel at home. Each room has a telephone for communication with maintenance personnel in case of any problems or desires. For those who have settled, they also have the opportunity to use a TV, cable and satellite TV are installed, which is absolutely free. Each room has a personal safe. However, in order to use it, you must pay an additional fee for its rental. The minibar in the room is empty, but when paying, you can ask to fill it, based on your preferences in drinks. Each room has a private bathroom with hairdryers and bath towels as a bonus. In order for tourists to be able to cool after the scorching Turkish sun, each room has a cooling air conditioner.



Rates

The hotel Solim Inn Hotel 3 * Kiris has 88 rooms. All of them are standard and designed either for 2 persons or for 3. Depending on how many people will live in the room, the cost of living in it differs. A standard all-inclusive room for two people will cost guests $ 45, while for three people one room will cost $ 65. Rooms where only breakfast is free are cheaper. One such room for two people costs $ 37. In general, the cost of living in the room of the Solim Inn Hotel 3 * is very reasonable, and an additional charge may be subject to a minibar, a safe and car rental.

Dining at the hotel and beyond

Solim Inn Hotel 3 * (Kemer) does not have a large restaurant. Its functions here are performed by the buffet, which provides tourists with breakfast, lunch and dinner. Breakfast is usually buffet style, where you can find a wide variety of snacks and light meals, which are usually eaten in the morning. As drinks, you can choose ordinary clean water, mineral water, still drinks and juices, as well as alcohol. For lunch and dinner, more substantial food is offered in the form of soups and hot. For lovers of alcoholic beverages, there is a bar where you can taste vodka, beer, gin, wine and much more. In Kirish there are not many restaurants and cafes outside the hotels, so if you want to have dinner somewhere in the city, then it is better to go to Kemer. There you can find a large number of establishments offering various national dishes of fish, beef and lamb. For lunch, you can head to the local bistro and taste the famous Turkish durum there. This dish is traditionally Turkish, and the shawarma that is cooked in Russian cafes is not near taste and quality.

What to see near the hotel?

As practice shows, Kirish is a small and quiet town where Solim Inn Hotel 3 * is located. Reviews of tourists indicate that there are few places for a stormy pastime, and even discos and clubs are somehow too cozy. However, the natural landscape of the city is simply breathtaking. You can take the road to the cliff that separates Kirish and Kemer, while enjoying stunning views of the Mediterranean Sea.

If you want to see a large number of attractions, it is better to go to neighboring Kemer or, even better, to not too distant Antalya. In these cities there are several very interesting places that will be remembered for a very long period. Not far from Kirish there is another city - Phaselis, where you can find very interesting sights, the history of which goes back to ancient Greece.

If you read the reviews of people about the Solim Inn Hotel 3 *, then most of them are positive and mainly depend on the room where they were accommodated.
