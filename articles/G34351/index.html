Fabric flowers: how to make and where to put it

A flower is a perfect creation of nature in which everything is exquisite, beautiful, elegant. Each petal is as if carved by a special master of beautiful affairs, and then neatly inserted into a thin stalk, so that as a result a beautiful creation of nature is born, which can cause admiration in children and adults, in men and women, regardless of preferences and occupation.

Perhaps this is the reason for the exorbitant love of flowers, this explains the addiction of all creative personalities to mention flowers in their creations. How many paintings were written with the image of flowers, how many verses saluted the delicate creations of nature, how many sculptors took the clarity of the lines of a delicate plant as a basis, even designers from all over the world are inspired by looking at lovely bouquets.

Why not add more colors to your daily life. Putting daily bouquets of fresh flowers is not very right, because for this they need to be cut and the period of their flowering state limited. Therefore, flowers from fabric come to replace them . How to make bouquets of artificial flowers can be easily learned from special literature. So many sources are devoted to creating bouquets of fabric flowers that it will take too long to list them.

How to make roses from fabric or tulips, but to make sure that they do not differ from living ones? Everything is very simple! It is enough to make them yourself, giving them a piece of yourself. After all, creating beauty is not only useful, but also pleasant. So, flowers from fabric. How to make them tastefully, so you can be proud of your creation later. This requires shreds of fabric, preferably of different textures: satin, brocade, organza, and so on. Imagine a paradise flower that seems to play with the sun, shimmering on its rays. Create delicate and delicate petals for the future flower and fasten them all at the base. This will create a lush flower. Let your bouquet have different flowers made of fabric. How to make them is already clear. The main thing is not to forget about the combination of colors, because if there are conflicting shades in the bouquet, the whole work will be a failure.

When all flowers from fabric are ready, how to make stems to create a bouquet? To do this, you can use ordinary twigs or plastic sticks, on the tip of which flowers are attached. Then they are wrapped in green cloth and decorated with leaves. Do not take too long stems, a small and neat bouquet seems more preferable.

The bouquet of flowers is almost ready, it remains only to fasten it into a single whole. A bow seems to be a great way to hold flowers together and decorate your bouquet even more. How to make bows from fabric, no need to explain. Oh, they all know how to tie skillfully.

For a more sophisticated bouquet, you can add sparkle and radiance, which is possible with the help of rhinestones, beads and sparkles. To do this, apply a little glue to the flower parts and place rhinestones there or sprinkle with sparkles.

And in order for the bouquet, like a real one to give a wonderful aroma, you can spray your favorite perfume on it, which will spread throughout the room with gentle notes and give a pleasant mood.

With such charming bouquets you can decorate your home, as well as give them to charming women who are madly in love with flowers. But only such a bouquet will be much more valuable, because for many months it will delight the eye and give magical memories of the past holiday in the hope of receiving such a charming bouquet again.

There is nothing more valuable than positive emotions, and such a bouquet will give them daily. Let the flowers bloom in each room, and there is nothing wrong with the fact that their petals are made of colorful matter that can spill over the sun for years and congratulate you on the new day.
