The best children's health camp in Anapa. Anapa Children's Camps Overview

Summer is the time for rest and recovery. Many parents do not have the opportunity to relax the whole summer with their children. But you really want the child to spend this time as useful and fun as possible!

Children's camp is the best solution





Sending your child to the children's recreation camp in Anapa is a great solution. There is both the sea and the sun, and many new friends. For children, this is the perfect vacation. Still, it's better to have fun with your peers than to be bored with adults. In addition, you can also improve your health.

In Anapa, there are many children's recreation camps. Each of them has its own characteristics and offers its own conditions. Anapa Children's Health Camps annually receives thousands of children not only from Russia, but also from neighboring countries. For children, this is a good reason to meet new friends.

Below will be described the most popular health camps in Anapa.

DSOL "Vita"

Sanatorium-health complex "Vita" takes visitors from 7 to 17 years. The camp is located at: Anapa, p. Vityazevo. The capacity of the camp is 1400 people.

The complex is located on a huge fenced area of ‚Äã‚Äã16 hectares. There are sleeping quarters, an indoor pool, a sanatorium base, a gym, a game area, a playground for discos, a theater, a cinema, a huge sports ground where you can play basketball or football, a tennis court, a football field, rooms for various circles, saunas, cafes and shops with groceries and other children's goods.

General terms

One room can accommodate from 2 to 4 children. The rooms are fully furnished. There are beds, bedside tables, curtains, wallpaper on the walls and linoleum on the floor. A combined bathroom is in each room.

The detachment consists of 30 people with two counselors and a tutor.

Wet cleaning is carried out in the rooms every day, linen is changed once every 7 days. Water - around the clock.

The food in the camp is excellent. Children are fed 5 times a day. The menu includes a variety of fruits, homemade cakes, vegetables and pastries. The cozy spacious dining room has everything you need for eating.

But the main asset of the camps in Anapa is, of course, the sea. The beach of the Vita complex is 100 meters from the sanatorium. On the beach there are medical and rescue posts, fountains with drinking water. The beach is under guard.

Entertainment

In the camp, children can participate in the interesting interactive game "Republic of Vitaly". This is a kind of state model. Here everyone can become the president, deputy or mayor of the city. There is a stock exchange in the republic where you can get a job. Children can spend the money they earn on goods sold in stores in the camp or go on an excursion. Also available for visiting 10 circles. There is also its own television studio, and an art studio. Children can attend a dance, theater studio, as well as sports sections.

The main value of the Vita complex is sanatorium rehabilitation. On the basis of the camp, diagnostics are carried out on modern equipment.





Appointments are issued after the child is examined by the pediatrician of the sanatorium.

Change - 21 days. Prices for 2016 - from 38,500 to 41,800, depending on the month.

The price includes accommodation, meals, entertainment, wellness treatments, disco, attendance at clubs, souvenirs.

Additionally paid travel, transfer, visit to the water park, dolphinarium, excursions, cafes.

Health complex "Energetik"





Children's camp "Energetik" (Anapa) is located in the valley of a beautiful river
Sukko. It is 14 km from Anapa. The camp accommodates 1200 children from 7 to 15 years.

Each shift in this camp is unique. So that children do not get tired of the same program, counselors constantly change them. Children get the impression that they were in the camp for the first time.

Offers from "Energy"





Children's camp "Energetik" (Anapa) offers its guests to go on interesting hikes, try themselves as an actor, designer, artist and just participate in exciting holidays, fun concerts and lush fairs. Children love such entertainments very much and actively take part in them.

Each child is given special attention, which allows him to fully reveal his potential and creative abilities.

Health camp in Anapa "Energetik" also offers spa treatment according to the standard of the resort. Treatment is carried out according to the following profiles: ENT diseases, nervous disorders, problems with the musculoskeletal system. The base is equipped with modern devices.

Infrastructure

"Energetik" has its own beach with small pebbles, to which only 600 m. The beach is equipped with changing cabins, sun awnings and bathrooms. Visiting the beach - 2 times a day for 2 hours. The entrance to the sea is gentle. The place for swimming is limited by buoys. Children on the beach are constantly watched by counselors, lifeguards and nurses.

The camp has a whole sports complex with showers and locker rooms, consisting of a large gym for volleyball, tennis courts and a football field.

In the evening, children can go to a disco, fully equipped for entertainment.

The rooms are bright and very comfortable. One room accommodates 3 to 6 people.

5 meals a day. The menu consists of wholesome healthy dishes. Always eat fruits and vegetables. For additional recommendations, you can contact a nutritionist who takes into account personal preferences and nutritional characteristics individually for each child.

Wellness center "Premiere"





Camp "Premiere" (Anapa) is another worthy place where you can send your child for a health holiday. Compared with previous options, the camp occupies a smaller area, only 6 hectares. It is located in the very center of the resort area. The picturesque territory of the camp instantly attracts attention. Children here are completely safe, as the camp is guarded around the clock.

Conditions of stay

There are 5 buildings for living. The comfortable rooms accommodate 4 to 6 people. Each child has its own bed, bedside table. Shower and toilet - in every room. Camp in Anapa is the best choice for children's recreation.

The dining room is in a separate building. It looks a little like the usual dining room. There are air conditioners, and the tables are served by real waiters. Children are fed 5 times a day.

Camp Premiere (Anapa) is not just a children's camp. Here, every child can become healthier. The camp specializes in the treatment of the respiratory system, skin diseases and the nervous system. You can also take part in two wellness programs to increase immunity and strengthen posture. Experienced doctors individually prescribe diets, physiotherapy, massages, inhalations, and oxygen cocktails.

200 meters from the sea. Camp "Premiere" (Anapa) has its own beach. For outsiders, the beach is closed. On the beach there are tents, toilets and even sun loungers for relaxation.

In the store on the camp site you can purchase the necessary goods. There is a games area and a cafe. The camp is equipped with sports fields for various sports. Learned children can visit the library. It is possible to wash things in the laundry room.

Entertainment

Camp in Anapa is not only relaxation, but also fun. Particular attention is paid to the cultural and entertainment program for young guests. Every day, concerts, fun contests and other recreational activities are held in the camp, of which the children themselves are participants. Adults only help them. At certain times, children can attend developing circles and sections. Among the 40 directions, here for each child there is something interesting.

On the basis of the camp, the program "Man of the 21st Century" operates, in which every child can take part. As part of this program, children learn to communicate freely in their native and foreign languages, work on a PC, and provide first aid in critical situations.

For an extra fee, you can go on exciting excursions. One of them (survey) is included in the price of the permit, which is 36800 rubles per person. The price also includes accommodation, meals and a cultural and entertainment program.

Anapa Children's Health Camps are represented by another center - "Change", located in the picturesque Sukko Valley. Nearby is the Big Utrish Nature Reserve.

Wellness complex "Change"

Smena (children's camp, Anapa) is a whole recreation complex for young guests, working all year round.





The location itself allows us to talk about the unique healing climate of these places. Just staying here, the child is already recovering.

Camp "Change" (Anapa) operates year-round. It consists of two branches: "Reserve" and "Southern". Children are accepted from 7 to 17 years. Group rides are accompanied by discounts. Settlement of 2 to 5 people in a room. On each floor there is a recreation area with upholstered furniture and a TV.

Camp "Change" (Anapa) offers children meals according to a certified program. Feed them 3 to 5 times a day. Menus are developed by specialists according to the individual age characteristics of a growing child's body. Therefore, the menu contains a variety of vegetables and fruits.





These are the most popular Anapa camps. Prices may seem a little overpriced, but it is fully justified, since the conditions in these institutions are at the highest level.
