Sony ST27i phone: features and reviews

The Sony Xperia ST27i phone, which will be discussed today, is presented by the Japanese developer as a youth solution and is located in the budget segment. The device cannot boast prohibitively high technical characteristics, in comparison even with its direct and closest competitors. Nevertheless, the device managed to occupy a favorable position in the market, and it gained really impressive popularity. This suggests that there is something enchanting in this once new smartphone market. Well, if so, let's take a look at the smartphone in detail. So get to know Sony Xperia Go.

Screen





Today we will not do as usual. We will not give technical specifications at the beginning, but we will do it at the end of the review in order to draw a line under everything that will be said. But first things first. What do we have in this case? Before us lies the Sony Xperia GO ST27I. The diagonal of the screen is 3.5 inches, as in the good old fourth iPhone. Resolution especially does not pamper users, just something 480 by 320 pixels. I would like to note that with such a budget we have fairly good viewing angles. In parallel, a good margin of brightness can be noted so that you can read the text in sunlight.

In general, users speak out for the fact that for its class the device has not only a good screen, but, it can be said, an optimal one. By the way, a special coating is applied to the display. It makes it possible to work with it even if there is water on the screen itself. This is useful to those people who often work with smartphones with wet hands. So to speak, very impatient people. A great solution for regular use near the sea. Interestingly, this model stands out from its segment through the use of mineral glass. For a state employee this is unusual.

Hardware





Sometimes there are cases when the Sony ST27I does not turn on. In part, this problem is connected with the hardware of the device. But what is it here? Immediately make a reservation: on board the smartphone there is an operating system of the Android family. Its version is 2.3.7. A number of changes have been made to the shell. Specialists of the Japanese company worked on them. The changes affected the design, as well as the interface as a whole. And now from general words to specific names.

CPU





As a chipset, the NovaThor U8500 fixture is installed. It works with two cores of the Cortex A9 generation. The clock frequency of the processor cores is one gigahertz. The amount of RAM is small - only 512 MB. As we can see, everything here is within the framework of budget rules. Another thing is preinstalled software. This is really a reason for a separate discussion.

Lockscreen





A well-designed lock slider. When using the Sony Xperia ST27I, you will have to make long movements. Having left to right, we can unlock the device from the screen. If we draw from right to left, then activate the camera. At the bottom, by default, four icons are located at once. How are they represented? The first icon is a folder with additional programs. They can be used by people leading an active lifestyle. The second is the transition to the company store. The third is to open the text message menu. Fourth - to open the menu with calls.

Features





It should be noted that there are branded floating wallpapers here. They are pretty enough. By default, they are blue. However, the user has the right to decide what shade they should be. The range of active programs is represented by a compass, speedometer, FigureRunner utility, a program for using the flash as a flashlight, and another utility from Adidas. It is intended for training. What awaits us besides this software?

Additional programs and widgets





There are not so few of them in the Sony ST27i. For example, there is a widget that allows you to control wireless interfaces, as well as change the brightness level, activate flight mode, enable and disable navigation and other similar functions. Its appearance is quite good, the Japanese worked on this, as they say, to fame.

The second widget allows you to see what weather awaits us, not only today, but tomorrow, as well as the entire next week. Perhaps an hourly break to view the most accurate weather forecast. By default, there is help, which is periodically displayed on the screen. It contains tips that allow the owner of the phone to optimize its work through the use of chips with the battery. Help, in general, will be useful for people who have just begun to master the operating system of the Android family.

Do I need a news feed?





Sony ST27I, the characteristics of which we will give in this article, is equipped with a program called TimeEscape. It displays a tape of actions of users whom the owner of the phone previously added as a friend. To be honest, there is no practical sense from this utility as such. Nevertheless, certain categories of users may well be interested in it. The program Music Unlimited will not only allow you to listen to music, but also download it to your device. In terms of watching the video, it is useless. Which, by the way, can already be understood from the name of the application.

Form style

I would also like to talk about the music player, since we got to this topic. It is decorated in the best traditions of a Japanese company. Using the interface, files can be quickly transferred, as well as transferred to a playlist, where the user has the right to add the necessary tracks or compositions.

If the buyer understands the equalizer settings, then he will be able to please himself with high-quality and surround sound, since the equalizer itself is present in the settings of the multimedia player built into this unit. Using a special add-on, you can increase the speaker volume. This is a very useful add-on because the speakers themselves cannot be called loud. Rather, it is more about colloquial. We can say that the conversational speaker with the quality of voice transmission of the interlocutor is doing well.

A service called Unlimited, which is built into the multimedia player, allows you to find information about the artist of the currently playing song. Information will be found using third-party services such as YouTube or Wikipedia. Sony Xperia Go ST27i, whose specifications will be given at the end of today's review, is well suited for listening to music. In this area, among the analogues of the budget segment, it is probably the leader. Actually, that's why the smartphone was presented as a youth solution. You can buy this model for 7 thousand rubles.

Tired of music? No problem! You can use an analog radio. The list is quickly selected, it can be edited manually. The radio is also designed in a Sony style.
