Mesh Knitting Basics

Fillet knitting in its essence imitates the lace that our grandmothers did. Only crocheting simple stories are created faster, and creation schemes are much more understandable and simpler. For this type of creativity, monochrome cross-stitch patterns are suitable. Although professionals have learned how to create color patterns.

The sirloin crochet pattern consists of empty and filled with columns of cells. A chain of air loops fits the size of the product as standard, which in the next rows is filled with cells by alternating air loops and double crochets.

The size and density of the mesh will depend on the thickness of the thread, the size of the hook, the number of crochets and the correct execution of the technique. Loose knitting makes the product larger, but shapeless, and tight knitting creates neat miniature patterns that hold their shape. When fillet knitting clothes is very important. When creating decorative elements of delicate work, take the hook half less than the thickness of the thread.

Where filet mesh is used

In addition to the usual filet grid (squares), there is a tulle pattern, where there is also an alternation of columns with loops, but a larger number. That is, in a regular grid, the columns alternate through two loops (in a filled square, instead of loops, the columns).

And in the tulle pattern there is a ‚Äúdouble‚Äù alternation: 1) the column is replaced by five loops; 2) the usual square is replaced by five loops that are attached to the middle of the arch of the previous row. It turns out a kind of oval mesh.

The fillet pattern, despite its simplicity, is used when knitting napkins, tablecloths, curtains, tunics, tops, sweaters, dresses, paintings, collars, bags, bedspreads, pillowcases.

Previously, the loin mesh was only one-color, while the main pattern could be made with double crochet or empty cells. Since such products require lining, due to the contrasting color, the craftswoman emphasized either the background or the pattern. Now the crochet patterns of the sirloin net remained the same, but the masters adapted themselves to immediately create multi-colored patterns.

How to knit according to the pattern

Embroidery patterns are good for their variety. For knitting paintings, panels or napkins, it is better to choose monochrome plots or having two colors. The pattern for cross-stitch is read as follows: an empty cage corresponds to a column and two air loops, a filled square corresponds to four columns. Consider a scheme with a spider, the plot of which is suitable for any product (napkins, panels, shirts, tablecloths).

Only for clothing, the border can not be knitted, and a spider with a cobweb can be made from empty squares. That is, first there is a double crochet, then two air loops and again a double crochet. In this case, the shirt or dress will be made of solid columns, and the picture is transparent, then no lining is necessary.

For panels, ‚Äúreverse‚Äù fillet crochet is used. The napkin patterns in this case are read in the forward and backward directions. That is, you start to knit from left to right, turn the work over and go from right to left. For these purposes, ‚Äúblack‚Äù cells are knitted with crochets, and the background - alternating columns with air loops.

Knitting sweaters

Coarse mesh models are worn over shirts. If you choose a contrasting color, you can see the picture brighter. Such sweaters, tunics, vests knit quickly, literally in a couple of days, since the pattern is small, and the squares can be knitted large (a column with two crochets and four air loops).

When knitting sweaters, make a pattern, apply it strictly on it. Note that the first row starts with a solid row of columns. From it up will go knitting sweaters, and down - frill. A large flower is located on the front shelf, and all other details are made with a net (meaning filet crochet).

Sweater schemes can be modified. To do this, perform the drawing with empty cells, and knit the entire sweater in solid squares from the columns. Appearance will be different. Try to connect a small square with a flower in a different style and attach to the shirt, immediately understand what you like more. Then grind the shelves, knit the sleeves, and in the last turn, work on the product strapping.



Combination of filet pattern with a different pattern

The fillet grid shines through, because whole things require lining. But with a combination of different patterns, you can knit a skirt on a girl without additional material. The principle of operation is simple. Knit twenty centimeters from the waist centimeters. Switch to fillet crochet (tablecloth or bedspread patterns can be taken as a basis, since the grid will be the background, and not the main pattern, like a sweater).

Once you have reached the desired length of the product, begin to knit shuttlecocks. They should be wide, like a bedspread or pillowcase. Mentally divide the loin mesh in half (this will be the middle). Now, from the beginning of the pattern (immediately after the belt), you are tying the first shuttlecock. The next tier begins in the middle, and the last begins immediately after the end of the grid.

Thus, due to shuttlecocks, voids are covered. Another option for knitting a skirt without a lining is to use a grid like a sweater (description of the scheme above) with double columns. And as a decoration, use separately knitted elements (flowers, leaves) or stretch multi-colored satin ribbons. With a little imagination, you can get the original model.

Brief Summary

Fillet pattern, despite its simplicity, for beginners can be quite moody. The product may not keep the shape of the squares, which is possible due to poor density of knitting, improper selection of threads, crochet hook, number of crochets. It is better to practice on small napkins or knit ten-centimeter samples before work.

To create a form, the craftswomen use a double grid. This is also filet crochet. Schemes of a tablecloth, bedspreads, clothes in this case involve the alternation of two double crochet and two air loops. Due to the double columns, the product shines through less and holds its shape well. This tip will allow beginners to create neat handmade items in a short period.
