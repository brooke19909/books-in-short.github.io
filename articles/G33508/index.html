Do you know how to sew lambrequins with your own hands?

Elegant drapery made of fabric on the window or door can bring sophistication and sophistication to any interior. It can be a separate decorative element or a combination with curtains. As you understand, today we will talk about how to sew lambrequins with our own hands. This is not difficult if everything is done consistently and accurately.



Where to begin

Before you learn how to sew lambrequins with your own hands, decide which sample you need. Then make your choice of fabric. You will need it for the base, lining and edging. Seamstresses with experience recommend choosing a lightweight but dense fabric for the base so that it drapes well in the product. Choose a color and combination with a curtain to your liking.

Now you need to determine how much tissue you need. It depends on what tasks you set for the labret:

  • if you need to visually reduce the high window, then you need an elongated sample;
  • magnificent voluminous drapery will visually expand the window.

How will the lambrequin be attached

It can be mounted on a cornice or on a bar fixed above the cornice. In the first case, the execution is much simpler. It involves the use of curtain tape. In order to fix the lambrequin to the bar, you need to use "Velcro". One part is sewn to the product, and the second is nailed or glued to the bar. In this case, the window size is visually increased .

We cut fabric

Often, beginning seamstresses ask a question about how to sew a lambrequin yourself, and whether a pattern is needed for this. Of course, nothing is worth doing by eye. In accordance with the style of your model, make a pattern template. For this you can use glued newspaper sheets. Make all the details separately (base, lining, piping) and number them. From the front and lining material, cut the details. For seams, leave allowances of about two centimeters. The fringing strip is equal to the length of the cornice, plus a small margin so that you can tuck it on the sides.



We assemble the product

If you want to know how to sew lambrequins with your own hands correctly, you must remember that you cannot start scribbling them on a typewriter without rough sweeping or pinning off parts.

So, fold the main fabric and lining with the front side inwards and fasten with pins. Sew the side and bottom seams on a typewriter. The top of the drapery remains open. Notches can be made on allowances - this will allow the fabric to lie down better. Sew the selected border over the top, and then iron all the seams and stitches well. The finished product should be turned to the front side and ironed well. On the bottom, the lambriken can be decorated with fringe, frill or drape with a special braid.

Decorative elements

Many women are interested in: how to sew lambrequins with your own hands, so that the product looks original. To do this, you can use decorative elements that will support the basic style of the interior of the room. For example, in the children's room, make an application (a typewriter, the sun, a butterfly, flowers), give it density using a doubler and fix it on a lambrequin. Thus you will revive any room.



Today you learned how to sew a soft pelmet. We hope that our tips will help you design your window exactly the way you dreamed.
