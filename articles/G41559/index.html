TV diagonals affect image quality

Today it is no longer possible to imagine the life of a Russian family without a TV. He, however, and the sofa became almost a member of the family. With this electronic device they share joy when their favorite team wins, and he also becomes a witness of disappointment and frustration when the opposite happens. Therefore, the choice of a TV should be taken seriously and responsibly. But which one is better to choose? How do TV diagonals affect image quality?



Or maybe more?

First decide which part of the room you want to place it in. Then decide whether he will stand on the counter or whether it is better to hang it on the wall. And, finally, it's time to start almost to the main thing. It is necessary to decide which TV diagonals will be most comfortable for watching your favorite movies and programs. Keep in mind that the common expression that the larger the size, the better, respectively, is not true at all. This parameter is very individual and you need to be guided by your own comfort.



How to choose the diagonal of the TV?

Typically, there are two factors that influence the choice of size of an LCD TV screen. First of all, this is the distance that you are far from the electronic device, and the resolution of the transmitted image on the screen. The larger the latter, the smaller the distance between you and the TV. You can, without sacrificing quality, both move away from the screen and look at it almost closely. And, of course, the smaller the number of pixels, the better it is to choose the diagonal of the TV more compactly, because otherwise poor picture quality will appear. You can check it yourself when buying an LCD TV. Just go as close as possible to the picture with low image quality. You yourself will see all the flaws. But if you move away from the electronic device, then the errors will disappear from you.



All about the same

For example, if we consider a TV with a diagonal of 32 inches, then for transmitting a high-quality signal with 625 lines, the most optimal is the distance from the viewer to the screen, equal to 96 inches (or approximately 2.5 meters). With this removal, you will not notice small inaccuracies in the image, and the picture will be high-quality and saturated. If the device produces 720 lines, then in this case the best distance is about 1.9 meters. Large diagonal TVs with Full-HD quality can be watched from a distance of 1.5 meters. This is the minimum at which you will not notice any distortion of the transmitted picture. When viewing from a distance exceeding the calculated, a noticeable difference between electronic devices having different resolutions will not be visible. Thus, if you see that you have a distance of 2.2 meters from the sofa to the cabinet on which the TV is located, then a diagonal of up to 37 inches will be the best. In the case of more space, you can choose a device with other sizes. As can be seen from our short review, TV diagonals play a huge role. Image quality and comfortable viewing depend on them.
