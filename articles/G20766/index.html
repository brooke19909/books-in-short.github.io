Equipment

What can not be saved yet? For example, on technology. Now technologies are developing rapidly and rapidly. But many still try to buy appliances (especially kitchen and household) for a long time. Cosmetics

Savings Rules

In order not to constantly have to deny yourself something, you just need to learn how to distribute the budget. And then you will not need to think about saving. But few have such skills. Nevertheless, they can be learned.

To get started, write on a piece of paper all your current expenses. Start with the most necessary - travel, products (full list), utility bills and so on.
