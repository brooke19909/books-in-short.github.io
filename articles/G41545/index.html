Where to go in Volgograd? Good advice for tourists

Arriving in new cities, it is always worth sketching at least the minimum route so that, guided by it, you can learn the most interesting places and streets. The courageous city of Volgograd is famous for its military monuments. When planning a study trip, you need to find out in advance where to go in Volgograd with your acquaintances or friends.

To fully plunge into the historically saturated city, you must initially go through all the memorials and memorable places. Many citizens will tell you where to go in Volgograd, the sights of primary importance here are familiar even to the smallest residents.

Mamayev Kurgan





The most landmark in the city is Mamaev Kurgan. A grand multi-level monument erected at the central height of Russia tells its guests the story of bloody battles for Stalingrad. Therefore, determining where to go with children in Volgograd, it is worth making Kurgan in the first place. The indisputable historical significance and emotional burden of this place will reveal for children the value of human life, the importance of unity and cohesion. Due to the scale and significance of the Kurgan ensemble, the previously studied dates and concepts come to life in the heads of children, giving new ground for thought.

In the hall of military glory, an eternal flame burns daily , the glare of which strengthens the majesty of this place in the history of all mankind. An equally significant place in the city of Volgograd is the indestructible panorama museum "Battle of Stalingrad." The museum also allocated a place in its pents for a hall dedicated to the losses of the Soviet army in the bloody Afghan war.





On the basis of the panorama museum, a "concert and theater lounge", the first modern theater of Volgograd with a homely atmosphere and a colorful manner of conducting light and sound performances, was also created. Each viewer has the opportunity to spend an evening in the "living room" at their own elegant table. The refreshment of each evening is worked out in the theme of a given mood on the stage, which, undoubtedly, adds a touch of fabulousness to the evening.

Youth Theater





Where can I go in Volgograd and have fun with the whole family? You can visit the Youth Theater. An advantageous feature of this theater is the complete absence of a barrier between the audience and the cast. After major repairs, the building lost its stage, which allows viewers to plunge into the action and not feel like simple observers. The capacity of the innovative theater is 82 seats. Thanks to this, everyone will be accommodated with optimal convenience.

Zoo

Where to go to nature lovers in Volgograd? In the city several years ago, a large-scale constantly operating touching zoo opened . It has two halls: a farm room with a full range of rural animals (from chickens and rabbits to beautiful ostriches and amadins), and an exotic one filled with overseas animals (chameleons, iguanas, crocodiles and even snakes). All residents of the touching zoo of Volgograd can be safely stroked, fed and even photographed. During contact with any animal, children watch the staff.

Paintball club

Where to go in Volgograd lovers of adrenaline racing? Such people will like the Colosseum paintball club. The qualitative difference of the institution is that, in addition to standard sites such as forests and urban mounds, customers are provided with an unfinished two-level pool building, which is ideal for strategies and action games. The length of stay in the pool is about 3.5 hours, which allows you to play enough with friends to shoot. Also, customers are offered thoughtful game scenarios where all three platforms are connected, which complicates the cross-country ability and adds intrigue to the process.

Conclusion

We figured out where to go in Volgograd. As you can see, this city is rich in interesting places and events.
