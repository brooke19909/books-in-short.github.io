How to make flowers from beads for decoration or as a separate item

Flowers and trees made of beads are stunning products that amaze with their beauty, grace, craftsmanship and subtlety of execution. The most interesting thing is that the craftswoman, starting to weave, begins to see living plants in a new way, to notice the details, the smallest shades and characteristics of each flower and leaf. The uniqueness of the flora first opens when a person tries to repeat her masterpieces to some extent.





I must say that various flowers from beads, the photos of which are not very difficult to make, can easily be done independently. Moreover, novice bead lovers are able not only to repeat the tried and tested schemes, but also add to them something their own, unique and inimitable, using imagination and skillful hands.





Craftswomen who want to learn how to make flowers from beads, finished products can not only put on the shelf and decorate the room, but also present the product as a gift. Moreover, it can be either a separate composition, or jewelry attached to a brooch, hairpin, headdress or clothes. Some craftsmen even weave earrings, necklaces and bracelets in the shape of flowers.

Beadwork is a real art, which, with all its apparent simplicity, requires a lot of patience, perseverance and good vision. In addition to beauty, beadwork also attracts with its usefulness for development: children engaged in this type of needlework develop fine motor skills and, therefore, thinking.





From these small beads it is possible to weave anything, any fantasies and flowers: even simple ones, even with many leaves and petals. How to make flowers from beads? You need to start with the very foundation and center - with the pestle.

For weaving, you will need to purchase beads in several colors - those that are suitable for a pattern or an invented image. Suppose you can take white, green and blue shades. In addition, you need a wire (or fishing line, which is more like it), a ribbon and a flower stand.

How to make flowers from beads? First of all, you need to get ready for work. To do this, a light canvas spreads on the table so as not to lose the beads. It can be a simple handkerchief or towel. The right amount of beads is poured onto the fabric. Now you can get to work.

Weaving begins with four beads being collected on the wire, then the wire is twisted, and two more beads are collected. That's all, the pestle is ready. Now flower petals are made. To do this, six beads are gathered, twisted, the same number of beads is taken again, and the whole process is repeated again and again until a petal is gathered in a circle. Then the next petal is woven from the same wire (there are five in total).

Then several more colors are performed, connected together, and the whole composition is obtained. This can also be done from the fishing line, with or without a needle, as you wish. And how to make flowers from beads in another way? Pretty simple: take a fishing line or kapron thread, depending on the purpose of the product. The fishing line is stronger, and from the thread the product is more accurate and even. Here you need to focus on whether the product will be worn and used or stand on a shelf.

Having cut off a piece of thread or fishing line that is necessary along the length (it is not necessary to take a very large one, it will get confused and interfere), you can get to work. It is better to print and hold the weaving pattern in front of you, it is more convenient. Everything else depends on patience and imagination.
