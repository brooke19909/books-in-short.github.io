Sheraton Hotel in Kaluga: address, description of rooms, service, reviews

When planning a trip to Kaluga, each tourist will be interested in the offered accommodation options. The modern city offers a large selection of hotels at various levels. Among the local institutions it is worth highlighting the Sheraton Hotel (Kaluga). It will be discussed about him in our article. The status of the best institution in the city makes you get to know the complex more closely.

A bit about the hotel ...

On a business trip and on a tourist trip, each of us seeks to find the most convenient place to live. Whatever the purpose of your visit, it is most convenient to settle in the central part of the city. The selection of hotels in Kaluga is very large. Here everyone can find accommodation to taste. We suggest paying attention to the Sheraton hotel in Kaluga. It is located in the business and cultural center of the city, so there is no need to spend time on long trips.

The hotel is located at: st. Academician Korolev, 16. He has been hosting guests since 2014. Near the hotel there are some local attractions, including the Museum of Craft, Architecture and Life. You can get to the airport from the hotel in half an hour, and to the station in 15 minutes. There are bus stops near the hotel. The Sheraton Hotel in Kaluga is the only five-star hotel with a stunning design.

Number of rooms

The Sheraton Hotel in Kaluga offers its guests 174 rooms. All of them are made in a modern style. A rich number of rooms is able to satisfy the wishes of the most demanding customers. In the hotel you can book not only standard apartments, but also superior rooms.

The description of the Sheraton hotel rooms allows you to assess their comfort level. The institution's apartments are represented by rooms of the following categories:

  1. Standards are air-conditioned double rooms with a minibar. The room can have two beds or one double bed. The cost of living in a room per day is 6200 rubles.
  2. The studio apartment consists of a bedroom with panoramic views of Kaluga and a living room. The cost of living is 9900 rubles per day.
  3. Deluxe rooms have a living room and are designed to accommodate three guests. The cost of apartments per day is 7150 rubles.
  4. Executive Suite is equipped with additional services.

It is worth noting that all the apartments of the hotel are equipped with mini-refrigerators, work desks, computers and wireless Internet. All rooms have city views. Each unit is equipped with a private bathroom with bathrobes, a hairdryer, slippers and detergents. The rooms have kitchens equipped with coffee makers, kettles and dishwashers. Also in the rooms there are TVs, safes, air conditioning, ironing facilities and other amenities. Some have direct pool access. The hotel is non-smoking.

Catering

Meals at the Sheraton Hotel are possible not only in the mornings. In the morning, guests can enjoy buffet breakfast. During the day, La Grillage Restaurant is open for guests, which specializes in oriental and Mediterranean cuisine. You can drink beer at the Hubble Bar.

It is worth noting that in the immediate vicinity of the hotel you will find many places where you can have a good time and eat.

Infrastructure

The Four Points By Sheraton Hotel in Kaluga has a spa area, which consists of a small pool, two saunas and a jacuzzi. In addition, the hotel has a banquet hall and parking. The hotel has a hairdresser.

Attendants accommodate guests at any time of the day or night.

Behavior rules

Four Points by Sheraton in Kaluga requests to book apartments in advance. The hotel pays attention to the fact that the establishment is focused on non-smoking guests. Therefore, according to the internal rules of the hotel complex, smoking in rooms and in public areas is strictly prohibited. Staff reserves the right to levy a fine if smoking is detected in the apartment.

The hotel allows children of all ages. If the child is under six years old and does not require a separate bed, his hotel accommodation may be free of charge. For older children or guests who require an extra bed, an additional fee of two thousand rubles per night will be charged. An extra bed is available upon request.

The hotel welcomes pets. Pets are allowed subject to prior arrangement. For the stay of the animal in the apartment, the administration of the institution may charge a fee. This point needs to be clarified during the pre-booking process.
