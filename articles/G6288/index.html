All areas of Izhevsk: description and features

The city of Izhevsk is the largest place in the Volga region. It has excellently developed commercial, industrial and cultural activities. This is a fairly large settlement; in 2017, more than 650 thousand people were registered in it. Many tourists come to this place in order to enjoy the natural, architectural and historical sights. Where to go first? Each district of Izhevsk deserves special attention.

Ustinovsky





The leader in terms of population is the Ustinovsky district in Izhevsk. Today, about 137400 people live in it. In addition to local residents, there is always a huge number of tourists. In total, there are three main places that are worth visiting:

  • Almost all of the space is occupied by one of the most famous - Izhevsk Plant. Many city residents work here. For tourists, they offer a fascinating tour with a walk through industrial workshops.
  • Not far from the Petrovsky shopping center is a unique monument built in honor of a dog named Zvezdochka. Each person, no doubt, should be photographed against his background.
  • In the same place is an old airport, which has retained its appearance in the pre-war era.




Ustinovsky district in Izhevsk is divided into two territories: a sleeping area and an entertainment center. In the first area there are panel houses, excellent vegetation and a deserted road. In a word, it is an ideal place for older people and parents with young children. In second place, a huge number of shopping centers, nightclubs and high-rise buildings are organized - a lot of entertainment for every taste is presented for the young generation.

October





Silver in number can be deservedly given to the Oktyabrsky district of Izhevsk. To date, 135,017 people are registered on its territory. Here you can always meet a huge number of students, and it is not surprising, because the most prestigious institute of the city is located on its territory. There are several interesting places:

  • A large zoo, the inhabitants of which are the most unusual species of animals.
  • Museum dedicated to the famous Kalashnikov.
  • It is mandatory to take a walk along the Central Square, its view simply cannot but bewitch.
  • The embankment also has incredible beauty.
  • For art lovers, delightful performances are organized every week at the Opera and Ballet Theater.

Visitors will be interested to live in the unusual apartments that prevail in the area. They have a layout of the distant 30s. It is worth noting, it is noticeably different from modern new buildings.

May Day and Lenin

Almost the same number (126,000 people) are Pervomaisky and Leninsky district in Izhevsk. Both of these places can be called sleeping areas, they are always quiet, calm and safe.

In the Pervomaisky district there are small houses, modest shops and playgrounds. Only in one place can you see a modern-looking shopping center with beautiful lighting. The main attraction of this district is the Alexander Nevsky Cathedral.





In the Leninsky district, you should go to the Tatar bazaar, where you can buy quality products, souvenirs and household items. Here, as in other areas of Izhevsk, mainly small residential buildings are observed. The only attraction is the train station, which has an ancient design.

Industrial

The smallest is the Industrial District of Izhevsk, its number barely exceeds 120,000 people. Locals sincerely call it a hot spot, since there is an incredibly fertile land with which they get a good harvest. Basically it is filled with five-story houses, on its territory there are no ancient barracks and modern high-rises. In the afternoon you can see people running on the bus, grandmothers sitting at the porches, and mothers walking with strollers. At night, the area is noticeably empty.





The main attraction is the Ice Palace, which hosts city competitions, concerts and holiday parties.

Other areas

In total, five main districts of Izhevsk can be distinguished. In addition to them, there are also several remote settlements, also related to the territory of this city. These are:

  • Varaksino.
  • Polygon.
  • New Greenhouses.
  • Volozhka.
  • Brigade Aircraft.

About 100,000 people live in each of these villages. They are rich in small dictations, outlets and summer cottages. Unfortunately, outstanding attractions in these objects are not observed.

Izhevsk is a real asset in the territory of the Russian Federation. It, like all cities, is divided into several small territories. It is worth visiting each district in order to have the opportunity to study in more detail the features of this settlement.
