Children's hat with ears, knitting needles: step-by-step description

A hat is a must-have accessory for the cold season. Today, do-it-yourself products are especially popular. They are unique, attractive, they store the energy of warmth, because the needlewoman puts her soul into her work. To knit a baby hat with ears, knitting, means to create a fashionable element of clothing in which your baby will be cute and attractive. Such products can be additionally decorated with beads, decorative buttons or other knitted elements. In such a cap you can safely conduct a photo shoot.





Features of a baby hat

Knitted, a baby hat with ears protects your baby from piercing winds. The child will not pull off the headdress, because it is fixed on the head with attractive ties. It is important for the child that all products are appropriate for their size. This is especially true for hats. After all, each baby has its own complexion, and finding the right option is just as difficult as for an adult. A small hat will not cover the forehead and nape well, and a large hat can be blown, especially in the ears. And this can lead to constant otitis media and colds.

If the mother makes a little needlework, then it will not be difficult for her to independently knit a baby hat with ears of the appropriate size. In addition, things created with your own hands are characterized by a special warmth, which means that your baby will be comfortable and warm even in severe frosts.

We decided on the yarn, now you need to choose the appropriate size of the needles. To do this, we will knit a sample with several options for knitting needles, we will choose the suitable one. Now create a pattern with the selected knitting needles with a pattern that will make the entire hat. We make appropriate measurements on it. Remember that this item is required, because the main thing is to correctly calculate the number of loops.

Knitting hats

Consider an example of knitting a children's hat with ears. You will need about 100 g of yarn, 50 g each in a skein. Knitting is done with 2.5 mm knitting needles. You can use both circular and hosiery.

Getting started with the caps ears:

  • To do this, gain 7 loops, and knit several rows, adding 1 loop in each row at the edges.
  • When there are 23 loops on the knitting needles, knitting must be postponed. It is approximately 6.5 cm tall.
  • The last row should be wrong side.

By the same principle, we knit the 2nd ear. After the chgeo, you need to connect 2 ears to each other, forming the back of the cap:

  • for this, we knit the front row of the 1st ear;
  • we collect 12 loops on the knitting needles and knit the front side of the 2nd ear.




At this stage, the front of the baby hat with ears to knitting should be formed. The scheme of the future drawing should already be prepared, because very soon it will come in handy:

  • There are 5 loops on the knitting needles. Now we knit another 8 rows of caps, adding 1 loop in a row. This will result in a beautiful transition from the ears to the front of the product. And behind these rows will reliably cover the neck of the child.
  • Now there are 66 loops on the needles.
  • In the 9th row, we knit 66 loops and dial 22 more. At this stage, knitting is closed in a circle and switched to circular knitting needles.
  • We knit a fabric 10.5 cm high. This is enough to knit a baby hat with knitting needles for a baby up to 1 year old.

Now begin to make the reduction of loops. To do this, the number of loops on the knitting needles should be divided into 8 wedges. We reduce at the beginning of each wedge, knitting 2 loops together. Let's say there are 88 loops in a hat. So, in 1 wedge - 11 loops. Reductions are performed as follows:

  • 1 time, 1 loop is removed in each 4th row.
  • Once reduced by 1 loop in every 2nd row.

As a result of such reductions, 16 loops will remain on the knitting needles. Now we knit every 2 loops together, and the remaining 8 loops are simply tightened together with a working thread. From the wrong side, carefully hide the thread, fix it.





Now we twist 2 cords for ties. The edges of the cap can be crocheted, without crochet. Sew on ties, pompom, beads.

Conclusion

To create a chic, attractive hat for your crumbs, you should prepare some yarn, knitting needles and a good mood. Believe me, your baby will be comfortable and warm. If you want to tie a hat on the fierce winter frosts, experts advise to make a lining of warm fleece.
