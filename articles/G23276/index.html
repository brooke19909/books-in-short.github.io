How to transfer money from MTS to "Tele2": step-by-step instructions

Today it is difficult to find a person without a mobile phone; moreover, usually one person is a subscriber to several mobile operators. This is convenient, since each of them has its own advantages. In this regard, situations began to arise more often when there was no money on one issue, and there was a lot of money on the other. How to be in this situation? How to transfer money from MTS to Tele2 (or to another operator)? Is it possible? Look for answers in the article.

Money transfers

Mobile operators quickly adapt to modern realities, therefore they have the service "Money transfers". It is possible to transfer money from subscriber to subscriber not only not in Russia, but also in the CIS countries, in Georgia.

There are three options for transferring from the MTS operator:

  • Cash transfer.
  • Transfer to a bank card.
  • Transfer to a mobile phone.

Of all these functions, we are only interested in transferring to a mobile phone, so we click on the desired link. We get into the next section, where a transfer to the number of four main operators is proposed. Take a look at the screenshot.





As you can see, it is possible to transfer money from MTS to Tele2. Such a service is provided by operators for their subscribers. In order to use it, it is enough to have Internet access from any device. How to transfer money from MTS to "Tele2"? Let's get acquainted with the service in more detail.

Translation step by step

Since within the framework of this article we are interested in the question of how to transfer money from MTS to Tele2, we click on the corresponding icon.

In the screenshot you see a table that will load after you click on the icon you need. It is small and does not require a lot of information.





How to transfer from MTS to Tele2? Fill in the data: phone number, amount for transfer. By default, there is a checkmark in the table that the transfer should be done from the phone number, if you wish, you can rearrange it and transfer from a bank card.

After all the necessary fields have been filled, click the "Next" button. In order to be able to continue the operation, you will need to transfer to your personal account. This will require a phone number and password. If you forgot your password, you can make a request and the password will come to you in the form of SMS.

To complete the transfer, an SMS will be sent to your phone number with instructions. Following her, you can finish the operation. Now you have the knowledge of how to transfer funds from MTS to Tele2. Similarly, the transfer to the numbers of all other operators is performed.

Cost and terms

Of course, the translation service is provided for a fee. In addition, there are a number of restrictions on how to transfer money from MTS to Tele2.

  1. The maximum transfer amount is one thousand rubles.
  2. It is permissible to perform no more than five transfers per day.
  3. The balance of the MTS phone number from which you intend to make a transfer must have an amount sufficient to pay for the transfer and the commission for it.

In addition, there is a commission, which is 10% of the amount transferred. Exactly the same commission is charged when transferring to numbers of other operators. An exception is made only for transfers between MTS subscribers, in this case they do not charge a commission. So, there is a way how to transfer money from MTS to "Tele2", but in fact it is not very profitable, although convenient.

You can perform operations not only from a desktop computer. Smartphone owners will be able to make transfers too, for this there is a mobile version of the site. The time required to complete the operation will be no more than five minutes.
