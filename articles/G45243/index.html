Indesit WISL 102 washing machine overview

What place do washing machines take in people's lives? Many will not hesitate to answer: "The main thing!". Modern automatic models minimized human participation in this process. Now you do not need to constantly monitor the washing, just put the laundry in the drum and turn on the appliance. Thanks to intelligent control, the washing machine itself will complete all the necessary processes within a certain time.

Surprisingly, for a high-quality result, it is not necessary to purchase very expensive models. It is recommended that you pay attention to the Indesit trademark. She has long been known to the domestic buyer. Each appliance has everything you need - protection against leaks, optimal washing conditions, self-diagnosis. The products of this brand replenish the segment of budget equipment. This is exactly what the Indesit WISL 102 model is. You should not expect solid advantages from it. She, like many other representatives of the budget segment, has disadvantages, but they are fully compensated by the cost of 15,000 rubles. Let's take a closer look at this device. The information in the article will help each buyer make certain conclusions.



Short description

Indesit WISL 102 is a washing machine with a fully automated process. Her body is covered in white paint. This spraying is quite high quality, any dirt can be easily removed with a damp cloth. The sunroof for loading linen is located on the front panel, which corresponds to the front type. The door is equipped with a convenient handle. The control panel is simple. There are two mechanical regulators on it. One is responsible for choosing the temperature, the second for washing modes. Six buttons are located above them, thanks to which additional functions are activated. There is no display. LED indicators allow you to follow the steps of washing. On the left is a box for detergents. It has a decryption of programs. It is divided into three compartments: two for the main and prewash powder , one for the air conditioner. The design of the drum is created in compliance with innovative technologies. The elastic band on the hatch is of high quality; after washing, water does not remain in it. The top cover is removable, which allows you to embed the device under the countertop.



Specifications

Indesit WISL 102 is electronically controlled. Case dimensions: 85 √ó 60 √ó 40 cm. Drum capacity - 5 kg, its volume - 40 liters. The assembly of the device is carried out in Russia. What classes of work efficiency does this model correspond to? Almost one liter of water is consumed in one full wash cycle. Electricity consumption - 0.15 kWh / kg. The maximum rotation speed of the drum during the spin cycle is 1000 revolutions, which corresponds to class C. Indesit WISL 102 is an economical instrument. This is confirmed by the high class of washing and energy consumption - A.

Modes for washing are divided by type of fabric. The first five are for cotton, from 6 to 9 for synthetics, programs 10 and 11 are used for wool, silk and other delicate types. Also, the model is equipped with additional options: drain without spinning, wash without wrinkles. Gentle rinse and spin modes deserve special attention.

Security systems

The Indesit WISL 102 washing machine has partial leakage protection in the form of a housing. It is also equipped with a drum imbalance control. This system automatically monitors the uniform distribution of linen during washing. If it is violated, the device stops the process and reports an error using a special combination of glowing lights. Important for such washing machines is the level of foaming. This model is equipped with sensors that monitor its level.



Common problems

Unfortunately, all devices tend to break. The Indesit WISL 102 model is no exception. The instructions that come with the kit contain information about the most common problems. Let's take a quick look at them.

  • Failure of the heating element. This problem occurs due to contact with water. TEN breaks due to the formation of scale on it. To prevent breakage, it is recommended to periodically clean it with special tools or use a water softener during washing.
  • Problems with the electric motor. The company supplies the devices with a high-quality motor, but its sensor is its weak point. Many buyers report frequent capacitor problems. Only replacing the part with a new one will save.
  • Problems with the surge protector. Unfortunately, there are lots of factory-owned washing machines. Most often, it manifests itself in the first three years of operation.
