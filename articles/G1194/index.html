About the complex

LCD “Live! In Rybatskiy ”is a complex development, a whole block of houses and objects of social and trade infrastructure. Construction is underway in the Ust-Slavyanka microdistrict. In parallel with the buildings of multi-storey buildings, of which there should be more than fifty in total, the construction of two large schools, four kindergartens, and also a medical center will go here. It is planned to equip several underground parking lots, as well as guest parking lots in the courtyards of each house. The first floors in all buildings will be given for commercial facilities. These will be pharmacies, shops, hairdressers, dry cleaners, bank offices, etc. Housing belongs to the comfort class.

Apartments in the new building will be presented in all types - from studios to large three-room apartments. So choose housing to taste in the new LCD “Live! In Rybatsky, it’s not difficult.



Construction progress

Work began in 2015. Since the project is large-scale, construction will go in six stages. The first includes the construction of three buildings for 2,400 apartments. It is planned to put them into operation at the end of 2017. A year later, the developer promises to hand over the buildings of the second stage. At this stage, the progress of construction work is in full accordance with the schedule. Already today apartments in the LCD "Live! In Rybatskiy ”can be purchased on an equity basis and at the lowest cost.
