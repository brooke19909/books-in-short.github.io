Guest house "Emerald" (Adler, ulitsa Kirpichnaya, d. 24): reviews and photos

Guest house "Emerald" (Adler) is a private hotel located half a kilometer from the sea. It is one of the most popular among tourists who come to relax in this resort village, which is part of Greater Sochi. The hotel opened in 2014 and underwent a complete renovation last year, so the rooms are all new, including plumbing.





Adler

This resort village is located south of Sochi and is almost a fifteen-minute drive to Abkhazia. From the latter it is separated by the Psou River. And it borders with the regions of Sochi along another water stream - Kudepsta. There are many sanatoriums and boarding houses since Soviet times, and the last Olympics in 2014 gave the resort a new impetus in development. Modern roads were built here, infrastructure was updated, and many hotels and restaurants were opened. The cheapest way to go here is in early autumn. Holidays in Adler in September will not only please you with an extension of summer, but also with a great offer of housing and a drop in prices.

How to get there

Most travelers arriving to rest in the Krasnodar Territory fly here by plane. You can use the Adler Airport directly , or Sochi. In this case, you will arrive at the resort by express. The Emerald Guest House (Adler), the address of which we provide in this article, is easily accessible both from the station and from the airport. A lot of transport goes here, and there is a stop near the mini-hotel. Regular bus number 100 leaves from Adler Train Station to the guest house. The stop where you need to get off is called Brick Street. If you arrived by plane, you can get from Adler Airport by bus number 105. In this case, you need to get off at the Vodokanal stop.





Territory and what is nearby

Emerald Guest House (Adler) is located in a 17-storey building with an elevator. Therefore, lovers of beautiful views can order rooms upstairs. Amazing landscapes open to the eyes of guests, starting from the sixth floor. From there you can see the sea and the famous Olympic Park. On the territory of the guesthouse there is a parking lot. Nearby is the entire infrastructure of the resort village, with its cafes, bars, restaurants, shops (including round-the-clock) and entertainment. And the children will be pleased with the dolphinarium and the Culture and Leisure Park with a wide variety of attractions. Kirpichnaya Street, where this hotel is located, is located in the very center of Adler, about a kilometer from the train station and four from the airport.





Rooms

The rooms for guests at the disposal of the Guest House "Emerald" (Adler) are divided into several categories. First of all, these are standard air-conditioned studios with one or two beds, bedside tables and a coffee table, microwave and electric kettle. Each room has a TV, fridge, ironing board and iron. For cooking, a special place with a minimum of utensils is allocated. The room has a bathroom with shower, where the necessary cosmetics are left, as well as a hairdryer.

Family rooms are designed for three to four people. In addition to a large bedroom and a living area with a sofa bed, this room has a kitchen with a complete set, a dining table.

The most expensive and luxurious in this hotel are apartments. There are three whole bedrooms, each of which has a TV. In rooms of any category you can use the wireless Internet, which is included in the price.

Tourists write that the rooms are very clean and tidy, it is pleasant to be in them. Wi-Fi works fine. Apartments and family rooms have air conditioning not only in the bedrooms, but also in the kitchen. Huge balconies have a clothes dryer. Constantly hot and cold water with good pressure. In total, the hotel has about fifty rooms.





Food and Services

Guest house "Emerald" (Adler, Brick, 24) provides guests with breakfast, but they are paid separately from accommodation. The private hotel has a Lermontov bar and a restaurant where you can order a diet menu if you wish. Tourists praise rolls and sushi, which are delivered directly to the room at great discounts.

The administration helps arrange transfers from the train station, but such a service is paid. A mini club is opened for young guests, where they are entertained by professionals. And sports enthusiasts can do fitness and visit the gym and spa with massage services. By the way, you can come here with pets, which is important for owners of dogs and cats who can not part with them even on vacation.

The hotel has its own travel agency and a tour guide. It is possible to wash and iron clothes, make a beautiful hairstyle or get a haircut.





Beach and water recreation

Walking to the beach from the guest house on foot is quite far - about half an hour, and not too convenient. But next to it is a bus stop. Public transport in Adler drives well. And to get to the beach from the hotel by bus no more than five minutes - just one stop. The beach closest to the hotel, which can be reached on foot, is not too crowded, but not equipped. And the places on the sea coast where the bus goes are quite well-maintained, but there are a lot of them. But you can sunbathe right at the hotel. On the roof of this multi-story leisure complex, which is the Emerald Guest House (Adler), there is a paid pool and a solarium.





Excursions

The fastest and most convenient way to see the surroundings is to use the Swallow express train. He quickly and comfortably takes you from the Adler railway station to the Olympic Park and Sochi. Boat trips for one or two hours are also popular here. Most of these excursions are allowed to dive directly from the deck of the ship.

To go to Sukhum, Krasnaya Polyana or Tuapse, you need to rent a car. And if you are not a fan of independent travel, use the local tour desk. After all, Adler is an excellent starting point for visiting many attractions in the vicinity of both the village itself and Sochi. Lovers of wandering in the mountains and looking for protected trails will especially like here.

It is convenient from this mini-hotel to visit Abkhazia - Gagra, Pitsunda. Do not forget about interesting places located in the immediate vicinity, such as the Matsesta hydrogen sulfide source.
