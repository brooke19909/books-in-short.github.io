Hotel GM Doc Let Beach Resort & Spa 4 *: overview, description, features and reviews

The Vietnamese GM Doc Let Beach Resort & Spa 4 * hotel is one of the best hotels in the Nha Trang resort region. It is located in a place called Ninh Hoa. The hotel attracts tourists with a private beach, an excellent pool, and free Wi-Fi. It is completely new - built in the year before last. The hotel is specifically designed for the relaxation and relaxation of families with children. The place is good, quiet, located about fifty kilometers from the center of Nha Trang. You can get there by hotel transfer or two local buses with a transfer. Moreover, unlike Mui Ne, it is not long to go. But in GM Doc Let Beach Resort & Spa 4 * , those who crave peace above all are especially eager. This country hotel gives tourists a sense of comfort and silence that you will not find in the city noise of Nha Trang.



Ninh Hoa

GM Doc Let Beach Resort & Spa (Vietnam) is located right on the grounds of this small fishing village. She is administratively assigned to Nha Trang. Its peculiarity is that this village is part of a long strip of beaches under the general name "Zok Let." This area is called the "Vietnam Bounty." There are gorgeous beaches, picturesque lagoons, sand, fine as flour, and blue water. Many tourists vacationing in Nha Trang come here only on an excursion to spend half a day. And those lucky ones who chose the GM Doc Let Beach Resort & Spa for their holidays can spend here all the time. Say that in ten to twelve days, relaxation at the sea will bore you, and want some noisy entertainment? And this is not a problem. After all, Nha Trang is not far away, and the hotel organizes special tours there, not for money, but for free. So in any case, those who stay here win.



Location, Territory

GM Doc Let Beach Resort & Spa is a colonial-style hotel . Six white low-rise two-story houses with columns are scattered in the middle of a tropical garden. There are no elevators, but they are not needed. Your luggage will be brought to the rooms by employees. The territory is very green, well-groomed. Many fountains, benches, places for privacy. There is a mini zoo that children like. Huge butterflies of various colors fly everywhere. Along the shore, you can walk around the surroundings and look at the local flavor, as well as discover many cheap and attractive places for lunch and dinner. Five kilometers from the hotel is the famous White Sand Beach. And 3 km away is a local market where you can buy fruits cheaply. Rooms

Now let's move on to the description of where tourists who come to GM Doc Let Beach Resort & Spa lodge. Reviews say that for Vietnam the hotel is simply luxurious. It provides beautiful and comfortable air-conditioned rooms. Views from them open different - to the sea, to the mountains, to the pool or the garden. The rooms catches "wi-fi." It is free both in the rooms and in the hotel lobby. The hotel has ninety-eight rooms. The rooms have good new furniture, excellent plumbing. There are many pleasant things - a hypoallergenic bed, slippers for guests, a safe. The bathroom is modern, deep. Bottled water is provided in the refrigerator every day for drinking. The size of the rooms is huge, there are small balconies.



Nutrition

GM Doc Let Beach Resort & Spa has a restaurant and bar. The price of the room, as a rule, includes a rich breakfast buffet, approximately as in the Thai "fours." The food is hearty and delicious. The lobby has a bar with very attractive prices. Tourists recommend it. In the Zok Let restaurant you can have lunch and dinner. It stands directly on the ocean, and guests enjoy a magnificent view while eating. Therefore, some tours offer guests the "full board" service. Opposite the hotel there are many cafes with cheap seafood, so those tourists who believe that eating in a hotel restaurant can eat dinner can have dinner there. Many dine on the beach, not taking their breaks. Many cafes perfectly cook lobsters, and they serve fresh juices, fruits, salads. Bread is free. Services

On-site parking for cars, bicycle rental is available. No wonder this hotel is called GM Doc Let Beach Resort & Spa 4 *. There is a wellness center equipped with all modern equipment. His workers are professional, experienced specialists. Here you will be offered original Ayurvedic treatments, for example, massage using hot stones, face masks, aromatherapy. If you come with small children and want to leave them in your room, a nanny will look after them for an additional fee. Dry cleaning, laundry. Near the pool there is a small gym. You can book excursions at the hotel and even get married - the hotel specializes in services for newlyweds. Several times a week, guests are taken to Nha Trang for free - departure at nine in the morning, and return at three in the afternoon. The hotel staff is exceptionally friendly, and the hotel itself is very clean.

Beach, sea, pools and other water activities

The hotel coast is private, closed. There are sun loungers, but not with umbrellas, but with special "houses".





All comers have enough space, nothing needs to be occupied in advance. On the beach there are a lot of offers for water sports and entertainment - kayak rental, own boat. If desired, organize fishing. You can snorkel or even dive. Massagers are on duty at the beach. The sea here is excellent - clean, transparent, without waves. The sand is white, as in an advertisement. The entrance is very gentle, ideal for young children. Palm trees grow on the shore, many arbors. There are swing benches. Both children and adults love them. Tours from city hotels of Nha Trang are even brought here. For bathing in the sea and sunbathing, beach towels (for painting) are issued. At the hotel, in the very center of the park, there is a large and deep pool - and it is open not seasonally, but year-round. The water in it warms up well. There is a sunbathing terrace near the pool, and in the evening it is beautifully illuminated. Perhaps water recreation is the main thing that tourists appreciate at the GM Doc Let Beach Resort & Spa. The reviews claim that if you want to spend time relaxing on the white sand and swimming in the beautiful, warm sea, away from the hustle and bustle of all worldly concerns, then this is for you.
