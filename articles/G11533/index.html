About ship paper

The toy folding algorithm is simple. First you need to determine what to create the craft from, and then how to make it.

A paper boat is made of strong, fairly thin paper, without the use of glue. Scissors may only be needed to prepare the sheet. Felt pens are needed to finish important elements on the boat. The paper should be clean, not wrinkled, resistant to folding. Well suited for work:

Step by step: design a toy

The most popular paper boats are made according to the classical scheme. The assembly algorithm assumes a sequence of the following actions:

  1. Put a rectangular sheet of paper in front of you vertically and fold in half on yourself. The fold line will divide it across.
  2. It is necessary to determine its central point. To do this, without turning, fold the workpiece again in half and straighten it straight away. The center and midline are indicated. The position of the workpiece - free edges down.
  3. Bend the top towards the midline towards you.
  4. Bend the lower part of the workpiece: the upper layer is forward and upward (towards itself), the lower layer is backward, respectively. In this case, bend the small angles along the edges of the first layer back. Get a triangular pocket.
  5. Open it and flatten it.
  6. Fold up the free edge of the resulting square.
  7. Turn the workpiece over and repeat the same. Carefully smooth all kink lines.
  8. Open the pocket, connect the lower free corners and flatten.
  9. Get the shape by pulling the top corners.
  10. Fix the boat. The product is ready.

How to fold a ship

Another simple paper boat awaits its embodiment. Assembly instruction is easy. For this cute steamer it is better to take colored double-sided paper.

  1. Fold and unfold the paper in half diagonally in both directions.
  2. Work with the top of the paper according to the scheme.
  3. Bend the top layer of the workpiece along the dashed line.
  4. Unscrew the tip of the paper along the dashed line.
  5. Fold the resulting creation in half.
  6. Fold the paper along the dotted line on both sides. This is an external reverse fold.
  7. Rotate the model in the desired direction.
  8. To fix and decorate the finished steamer (add windows, anchor name of the ship).

The seaworthiness of the boats can be improved by slightly adding foam to the bottom.
