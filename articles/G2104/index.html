Mon Reve 3 * (Rimini, Italy): description, photo, reviews of tourists

Many travelers from around the world consider Rimini the best resort in Italy. After all, there is a well-developed infrastructure, magnificent beaches, as well as hotels and entertainment for every taste and budget. If you decide to spend your vacation in this seaside town, but are looking for a budget option for accommodation, the Mon Reve 3 * hotel may be a suitable option. You can learn more about what guests are offered by the hotel from this article. We will also find out what impression tourists have after staying here.



Location

The 3-star small Hotel Mon Reve is located in the resort town of Rimini, in an area called Torre Pedrera. The hotel is only a few tens of meters from the beach. In the vicinity of Mon Reve 3 * there are a lot of cafes, restaurants, bars, shops, tour agencies, etc. As for transport links, it takes only a quarter of an hour to get from the nearest airport to Rimini. The train station is a five-minute drive from the hotel. Therefore, it is very convenient to travel from here to other cities in Italy.



Mon Reve 3 * (Rimini): description, photo

This small but cozy hotel was built in the last century. A global reconstruction was carried out here several years ago. So today, guests are offered accommodation in modernly decorated and equipped rooms. In addition to the housing stock, the hotel has a restaurant with a wide selection of national dishes, a bar, and a parking lot with video surveillance. The good news for tourists traveling with pets will be the fact that in Mon Reve 3 * guests with four-legged pets are always welcome. However, if you plan to arrive in the company of a furry friend, you must notify the hotel in advance and receive confirmation.

Housing stock

Mon Reve 3 * (Rimini) - a small hotel. So, it consists of only 17 rooms located in a building equipped with an elevator. Each room has comfortable beds, air conditioning, and a bathroom. A hairdryer is also available upon request. It is possible to install a cot.



Mon Reve 3 *: tourist reviews

In order for people planning a trip to have a more complete and clear picture of this hotel, we suggest that you familiarize yourself with the generalized reviews of travelers who have already visited Mon Reve. So, in general, our compatriots characterize the hotel as a fairly modest place. However, it would be wrong to count on some excesses and delicacies at dinner for little money, so the guests have no particular complaints.

Moreover, the rooms in the hotel, although small, are very comfortable, they have everything that a traveler needs to relax. Plumbing is working properly, the beds are comfortable. Rooms are cleaned regularly.

The staff of Mon Reve (Torre Pedrera) 3 * left a very good impression on vacationers. So, all the hotel staff are very friendly and always ready to help. Please note that the majority of guests here are Italians themselves. Therefore, the staff at the hotel does not speak Russian. However, in order to explain, basic knowledge of the English language is quite enough.

As for food, it, according to tourists, is quite simple here. However, the quality and taste of the dishes are simply excellent.

Of course, travelers considered the location of the Mon Reve a big plus. After all, the nearest beach can be reached in just a couple of minutes. By the way, the beaches here are municipal, and not owned by hotels, as in Turkey. Therefore, the rental of umbrellas and sunbeds will have to pay extra (about 5-6 euros).

In general, this hotel is perfect for young and active tourists who prefer to spend the whole day on the beach or travel to interesting places, and return to the hotel only for the night.
