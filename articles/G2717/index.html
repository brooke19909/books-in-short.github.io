Recreation area "Meshcherskoe"

The entry into operation of the existing urban recreation infrastructure should be considered only as the first step taken in the right direction. Muscovites have long needed such places of rest. Their number was significantly behind the minimum necessary. Many who have had a chance to spend a hot Sunday evening here have a reasonable question: why is this all created just now, and not much earlier? It cannot be said that the Meshchersky recreation area would require extremely large financial investments in its creation, maintenance in a dignified form and further development.

So the first experience of translating such a recreational project into reality should be recognized as positive. This is confirmed by everyone who happened to visit the Meshcherskoye. They liked the beach, and there are not so many similar places within Moscow.





Any positive urban development experience deserves to be developed and further extended both to other areas of the capital and to the near Moscow region. A fairly stringent control over the state of the water and the forested area surrounding the Meshchersky Pond should also be attributed to the positive experience. In both this and last years, the Meshchersky recreation area was officially certified by the relevant supervisory authorities. The water in the pond is recognized as suitable for swimming without any restrictions. Further monitoring of the reservoir and the ecological state of the adjacent territory will be conducted in the most rigorous manner.



Meshchersky recreation area: how to get to it from different directions

The easiest way to get to the recreation area is via the MKAD; the congress is located at the 49th kilometer. If you use public transport - trolleybuses of routes 10 and 18 and bus 781 to the stop "Lake Meshchersky". And of course, fixed-route taxis. From the metro station "Slavyansky Boulevard" a minibus 753 will deliver you to the destination. From the metro station "Kievskaya" - a minibus 500. It is quite possible to get from the platform of the Kiev station by train, you should get off at the Vostryakovo stop. And if you go from the Moscow region, from Peredelkino, you should use the bus of route 809.
