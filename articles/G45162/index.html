general information

The first advantage of GHT Balmes Hotel 3 * is its location. The hotel is almost at arm's length from the sea! And the sandy beach of Calella is not just good. It can be called legendary. It extends over two kilometers along. Moreover, for many years it has been adorned with the Blue Flag - a quality mark of European beaches.

Another plus is the relatively close airport. Aeroport de Girona is approximately a 30-40 minute drive from the hotel. It takes about an hour to get to Barcelona Airport.

It is also worth noting that from Calella you can quickly get to such famous beaches as Lloret, Santa Cristina and Fenals.

Service

He is at GHT Balmes Hotel 3 * on top. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel, there is private parking (if you need a place, you must book it in advance), the reception is open 24/7, the lobby has a currency exchange, a left-luggage office and an excursion desk.

I would also like to note that GHT Balmes Hotel 3 * is a multilingual hotel. They speak Catalan, German, Italian, Spanish, French and English.





Entertainment

Most people come here for a beach holiday. But, if you do not want to leave the hotel, you will still succeed in diversifying your leisure time. And this applies to both adults and the youngest guests.

There is a sun terrace, outdoor swimming pools (including a children's one, with a slide), a sports ground, a playroom for babies, a jacuzzi, bike rental, table tennis, a billiard room, and a golf course nearby.

On the beach, in turn, you can rent equipment for water sports. Canoeing, for example, try windsurfing, or just go snorkelling. While staying in Spain in the summer, you should definitely try any of the above - an unforgettable experience is provided.

In the evening, all guests are invited to the show. The animation staff arranges really interesting and varied performances.

Nutrition

GHT Balmes Hotel 3 * (Calella) does not work on an all-inclusive system - guests booking their apartments can choose their own meal option. The hotel's restaurant serves breakfast and dinner. The main specialization of the institution is Mediterranean cuisine.

People who rested in this hotel, note the diversity of the assortment. Every day a new dish appears in the diet, but what is stable is a huge selection of fresh vegetables and fruits. And so there is everything - ready-made salads, two or three dishes of chicken, fish and meat, all kinds of pickles, cheese slices, soups, pastries, sometimes they even serve chocolate fondue.

Most guests only order breakfast, without dinner. Because there are countless shops and markets nearby with a huge assortment of products and low prices. There are also taverns and restaurants where you can try different Mediterranean delicacies.

Booking and hotel policies

If you want to stay at GHT Balmes Hotel 3 * (Calella), you need to remember that check-in at this hotel starts at 14:00 and lasts until midnight. Departure, in turn, starts at 7:00 and ends at noon. Therefore, flights must be selected appropriate. Of course, guests will not be denied check-in if they arrive earlier, but there may simply not be places - the hotel is popular and it rarely has empty rooms. So you have to wait until the previous guests are evicted, and the room will be prepared for the arrival of new guests.

Please also be aware that this hotel does not accommodate extra beds. But then pets are allowed! However, if a person intends to come with a pet, you need to clarify this point when booking.

By the way, you will also need to specify credit card details - this is a guarantee of booking. Suitable "Visa" or "Master Card."
