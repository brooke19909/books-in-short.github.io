Hilton Long Beach Hotel (Hurghada / Egypt)

The Hilton Long Beach (Hurghada) solar complex has long been a favorite vacation spot for Russian citizens. And this is not surprising, because the friendly staff of the hotel is attentive to its guests and takes genuine care. Guests can get to the hotel in half an hour from Hurghada Airport.





The resort is notable for its affordable price and special festive atmosphere. Tourists are given the opportunity to enjoy diving and surfing. Convenient location allows people to freely visit the majestic and outstanding sights of the city, such as the Temple of St. Paul and Anthony, the Roman settlement of Claudianus, the ancient Coptic monastery and much more. You can have fun and have fun at the Titanic Water Park. Hilton Long Beach Guesthouse (Hurghada) is a real exotic paradise where even the most demanding customers will appreciate the excellent service and quality of service.

Rest at the hotel will give an unforgettable experience, a charge of emotions and vivacity. The guesthouse is designed for a category of people of different ages. It will be comfortable for families with children, active and young companies, as well as business travelers. There is a modern conference room and banquet facilities on site. Private parking also provides free parking.





Number of rooms

All rooms are equipped with air conditioning and modern furniture. Some rooms have seating areas. There is wireless Internet and a telephone. Also on the premises there is a TV with Russian television, a fridge, a bar with drinks and a microwave.

In personal use equipped bathroom, where you will find bath products and a hairdryer. This room has a balcony with panoramic views of the landscaped garden or outdoor pool. Cleaning is carried out regularly in the premises, and a change of bed is scheduled.

Nutrition

The Hilton Long Beach 4 resort complex (Hurghada) operates according to the European all-inclusive system, which includes breakfasts, full lunches and delicious dinners. At any time, guests can enjoy alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, ice cream. For lovers of Italian and Oriental dishes, two restaurants are open - entrance without reservation.





Vegetarians and children are offered a separate menu. The building has a cafe and a disco bar. You can enjoy a cooling cocktail or freshly squeezed juice in the pool bar or on the beach.

Beach

In walking distance stretches the coastline (60 m). The beach strip is quite wide and sandy. Beach facilities are free of charge. A water entertainment center and sports equipment rental are open. There is a platform for outdoor games.

Additional Information

Hilton Long Beach Guest House (Hurghada) has a tour agency. Here you will be offered inexpensive and interesting routes in Egypt, also fishing or boat trips. Active tourists will be delighted with the variety of sports activities. You can sunbathe and relax in the artificially created lagoon, located on the territory of the house.





A team of experienced animators works throughout the day, and dance shows and programs are organized in the evenings. It features a beauty salon, gym, hairdresser and horse riding courses. Souvenir shops and shops are located on site. Hilton Long Beach (Hurghada) offers a wellness center and sauna.

For your children there are playgrounds, a development club, a variety of games under the guidance of teachers and animators. There is also a disco for kids with fun contests and jokes.
