Weaknesses

The first tenants have already started receiving utility bills. And here one significant drawback was revealed - high tariffs. And this is despite the fact that counters are installed in each apartment. Currently, a house management company is trying to solve this problem.

Here is another omission - at the present time the management company does not allow changing the Internet provider. The one that is offered does not cope with the needs of residents, therefore, on their initiative, signatures were collected for making changes and the possibility of attracting other companies.

There are problems with the organization of the social infrastructure of the Emerald Hills residential complex. Reviews confirm that getting a child to a local kindergarten or school is quite problematic. If a private kindergarten were built on the territory of the complex, this would at least partially solve the existing problem.

Residents complain of traffic jams, especially in the mornings and evenings. It must be admitted that this feature is characteristic of almost all areas of Moscow and the Moscow region. In this case, a wide transport network helps minimize congestion.
