Hotel Poseidon Resort Hotel 5 * (Loutraki, Greece): overview, description, rooms and reviews

Greece is an amazing country. Rich in sights and interesting cities. A huge number of travelers go here every year. And all of them before the trip are primarily determined by the resort and the hotel. Many decide to stay at Poseidon Resort. general information

Poseidon Resort is located in Loutraki. This city is located on the shores of the Gulf of Corinth. He is also known under the shorter name Loutraki. It is very popular with tourists. And all because precisely here there are healing mineral springs.

The hotel is not in Loutraki itself, but 2 kilometers from it. Also, in just an hour you can get to Athens and, accordingly, to the international airport. It is also convenient to get to famous sights from here. Many decide to go to Epidaurus, the famous temple of Asclepius and the ruins of an ancient theater. It takes about 45 minutes to get to it. And you should definitely visit Lake Vouliagmeni, at a depth of 50-100 meters of which thermal springs beat.

But most importantly, the Poseidon Resort has its own pebble beach, which is within easy reach. Everything that is there (mattresses, chaise lounges, umbrellas and towels) is absolutely free.





Service

Poseidon Resort is a 5-star hotel, and the service in it is appropriate. Organized 18-hour room service and fresh newspapers, there is a laundry with dry cleaning, 24-hour reception provides express check-out and registration in accelerated mode. Administrative support can be provided to guests at any time - the hotel has a guest relations department. The complex even has a medical office. Doctors can also be called to the room if something serious happens.

Private parking is available on site. If you need a transfer, it will be provided. Want to drive your own car? Not a problem, there is a rental. It is also worth noting that the hotel organizes catering for private events.

If people took their children with them to rest, then they may not worry about their pastime. For kids, an appropriate leisure program is organized here. In addition, children will always be under the supervision of educators and animators. If you want someone to follow the child in the room, then you can use the services of the babysitter.





Leisure

In Poseidon Resort 5 * no one will be bored. Here everyone will find entertainment to their taste! Here you can take the time to futsal, ping-pong, volleyball, archery and basketball. There are four professional tennis courts on site. Fans of extreme sports will definitely enjoy water skiing, yachting and jet skiing, as well as windsurfing. You can go climbing outside the complex.

There is also a 25-meter freshwater pool on site. It is not simple, but with five lanes for sports swimming. There is also one heated indoor pool. And one separate, shallow - especially for children.

SPA salon

Each self-respecting hotel has its own beauty and health center on site. Poseidon Resort 5 * is no exception. The spa at this hotel is called Armonia. And here a huge number of services and procedures are provided, the full list of which amounts to dozens of items. However, it is worth listing at least some of them.

Here they make a wide variety of massages. For legs, arms, back, whole body and even face. Particularly popular is the aroma massage Plaisir Des Sens. It perfectly relaxes, because the procedure also involves peeling with essential oils.

Egyptian bath, mud baths and masks, chocolate wraps, thalassotherapy (using sea water), treatment with lavender, aromatic oils and herbs are also popular here. Many are recorded on face cleansing, skin reconstruction and procedures with hesperidin (a bioflavonoid that ensures long life of the body's cells).

In the SPA-center of the hotel Poseidon Resort Hotel 5 * you can even book a whole wellness course. With thalassotherapy, chocolate wraps, peeling and many other procedures.





Business service

The Poseidon Resort Hotel 5 * has a modern business center, ideally created for conferences. It was built in 2004, and since then there are regularly held corporate events of various kinds. The center itself, by the way, consists of nine halls. The largest accommodates 450 people, and the smallest - only ten. The center also has facilities for holding coffee breaks and cocktail meetings.

There is still a summer amphitheater on the territory, oriented to 900 seats. This place is often used for presentations, awards and various performances.

The halls are equipped with wireless high-speed Internet, autonomous air conditioning and heating, professional technical support, natural and artificial lighting and a sound reinforcement system. All rooms are finished with soundproofing material.

By the way, weddings are often held in the halls. The hotel has real professionals in the organization of celebrations and banquet managers. Many potential newlyweds turn to them in order to arrange the best holiday in their life.





restaurants and bars

The hotel Poseidon Resort (Greece) has several culinary establishments corresponding to the level of 5 stars. The first thing worth noting is the attention of the Nafsika restaurant. Its windows offer amazing views of the sea, and it is here that exquisite dishes from the chef are served.

Poseidon Grill Restaurant has an outdoor terrace and serves barbecue and grilled meats, as well as light snacks and traditional delicacies.

Gourmets will definitely enjoy the Triaina Tavern, located next to a small bay and yacht marina. After all, it serves Greek cuisine, seafood and fish. At Amphitrione, with panoramic views of the sea and mountains, guests are served buffet style. It serves breakfast, lunch and dinner.

The last restaurant, called "Neptune", operates only in the winter season. Here guests are greeted by modern furnishings, delicious food and pool views.

The number of bars at the Poseidon Resort Hotel is 4. Loutraki is a city rich in various cafes and taverns, but why go somewhere if there are many places with a wide assortment in the hotel? All bars serve snacks, light meals, soft drinks and strong drinks, as well as exotic cocktails.

Reviews

And finally, a few words about the comments made by people who stayed at this hotel. The hotel makes a good impression - it is located near the sea, surrounded by lushly flowering vegetation. The staff is polite and welcoming - despite the fact that check-in starts at 14:00, guests can be accommodated earlier if they arrived earlier. But subject to availability.

The apartments are clean, cleaning is done every day, as is the replacement of towels. And water, as experienced guests assure, you can drink at least from the tap - it is clean and tasty. No wonder, after all, Loutraki is famous for its thermal springs. And the tap water here is mineral, which many tourists find surprising.

The food is excellent - everyone will find something that will appeal to him. A lot of different dishes, lots of fruits, vegetables, seafood and desserts. There is even a separate kosher corner! And a few diet and vegetable dishes. So no one will remain hungry.

The hotel itself is quiet. An ideal place for a relaxing holiday. It may be noisy except on a summer weekend - on Saturdays and Sundays, Greeks rest here. By the way, this once again proves a decent level of service at the hotel. If locals come to a hotel in Greece, then it is really good.
