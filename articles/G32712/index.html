Camp "Star" in Tsivilsk for a wonderful holiday for children

In the summer, all parents think about where to send the child to rest. The Zvezdny camp in Tsivilsk is an excellent option that will satisfy even the most demanding mothers and fathers. In this recreation facility, children can recover and gain strength. In addition, a program thought out to the smallest detail will provide a fun pastime. Emotions will charge boys and girls for the next year.





Where is the Zvezdny camp in Tsivilsk

There is a summer school for children in an oak grove. Nearby there is a river, on the banks of which children take sunbaths and can be bought accompanied by professional physical instructors and counselors. Photos of the Zvezdny camp in Tsivilsk open the curtain and indicate that the setting is picturesque and environmentally friendly.

The camp is located 3 kilometers from the city of Tsivilsk. You can get to the children's summer institution independently, by car or public transport. It also offers a transfer of children from the city of Cheboksary right to the gates of the camp.

Camp infrastructure

The Zvezdny camp in Tsivilsk has a total territory of more than 20 hectares. Most of this area is occupied by a grove and meadows. That is, in addition to having a great time, boys and girls will be able to breathe clean air and enjoy nature while they are in the camp.





On the territory of DOL there are:

  • Residential buildings in the rooms of which children live. There are three buildings on the territory.
  • Administration. This is a medical building, and a laundry room, and a shower room.
  • Also on the territory there is a dining room, which simultaneously accommodates almost 500 people.
  • There is a stadium where sporting events and various competitions take place.
  • For evening programs there is an equipped dance floor.
  • There is also a room in which creative classes take place.
  • Boiler room, which provides a constant supply of warm water.
  • There are also water towers and power lines in the area.

In addition to the above buildings and premises, on the territory of the Zvezdny camp in Tsivilsk there are many places for children's entertainment. Therefore, boys and girls will always find where to spend leisure time and have fun.

Entertainment in the camp "Star"

The DOL program is thought out almost every minute. Each child will find for himself what is most interesting. Therefore, do not worry that the son or daughter will be bored or they will have nothing to do. Team spirit, fun in a large company of like-minded people will provide a wonderful holiday for children of all ages and characters. Almost all positive reviews about the Zvezdny camp in Tsivilsk. This means that children have positive memories, and parents are satisfied with the level of service and the variety of entertainment programs for boys and girls. Among the entertainment for children in the camp "Star" is invited to:

  • Outdoor swimming pools.
  • There are treadmills at the stadium.
  • Also, the stadium has everything that is needed for sports fans:
  • Football fields (3 pieces).
  • Volleyball courts as much as 7.
  • There is also a basketball court.
  • There is also a campfire site on site. Welcome and farewell events are held here at the beginning and end of the shift.
  • The entertainment club accommodates at the same time more than 470 people. This place hosts performances on the equipped stage, there is also a dressing room so that participants can reincarnate. Those who wish can visit the library in the club.




  • The dance floor is also equipped with a stage and a place for dancing, resting boys and girls.
  • Playgrounds for children will have fun with new friends in a relaxed atmosphere. On the territory of the sites there are many gazebos in which events are held between units and within units.

Both boys and girls, and small, and adult children, will surely find fascinating entertainment for themselves. By visiting the site of the Zvezdny camp in Tsivilsk, you can see photos that show the whole diversity of pastimes for children of different ages.

Camp conditions for children

Resting in the Zvezdny camp, the children live in buildings consisting of two floors. In each room from 4 to 6 people. Toilet, shower with hot and cold water, washbasins are located on the floors. All units have places for drying shoes and clothes.

In children's rooms there is such furniture:

  • Beds in the number of children living in the room.
  • Bedside tables for storing personal hygiene items and things.
  • Each room also has a wardrobe in which boys and girls can put clothes and shoes.




Each squad on the floor has a games room in which there is a TV, benches, tables for playing tennis.

Camp Zvezdny has everything you need for a good rest. Therefore, mothers and fathers should pay attention to this institution. Children will be delighted with such a pastime.
