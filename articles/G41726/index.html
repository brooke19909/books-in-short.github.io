How to get the "promised payment" on a megaphone?

It is not always possible to replenish the account of your mobile phone when the balance approaches zero or has already reached it. How to stay in touch without replenishment? The answer is simple - to borrow from your operator, for example, to activate the "promised payment" service on a megaphone.

To whom is the service available?

Everyone knows that trust must be earned. You can get a loan for mobile services only if the subscriber has been connected to the network for more than three months. In this case, the number should be used regularly throughout the connection time. Also, in order to receive the promised payment on Megafon, you must spend a certain amount on a monthly basis. If the total monthly expenses for all operator services do not exceed 50 rubles, in the best case they will give a subscriber a loan of half of this amount.



The more you spend, the more debt you can claim. If the monthly expenses for cellular communication do not exceed one hundred rubles, a trust payment is unlikely to be more than 50. Those who spend about 300 rubles every month can count on a loan of up to 100 rubles. The maximum amount credited to the account under the "promised payment" program on the Megaphone is 250 rubles. To receive it, you need to spend more than 500 rubles a month . How to get the promised payment Megafon-Moscow?



The easiest way to order the service is to type the command from the call menu of the mobile phone: asterisk, 106, grille, and press the send call key. It is noteworthy that this combination works even with a negative balance. If you want to borrow the amount less than the maximum possible, after the lattice you should enter the desired number of monetary units in rubles (a two- or three-digit number) and, having typed another lattice, send. After processing the request, a message appears on the phone screen about the provision or inability to provide a loan.

You can connect the promised payment on Megaphone by sending a message to the service number 0006. In SMS, you can specify the desired payment amount or send it empty. With a negative balance, sending SMS is not possible. Another way to activate the service is to connect via a call to 0006 or 0500. Following the prompts of the autoinformer, you can also get detailed information about the loan and the conditions for its provision. If you need the promised payment on Megaphone, and the balance is less than zero, you can use the company's online service. To do this, visit the operator‚Äôs official website, select the region and enter the Service Guide system, and then select the service of interest.

With a negative balance, you can send free SMS to other subscribers with a request to call back or replenish your account. The number of such messages per day is limited. If you need to replenish the account of a loved one, and only your own mobile phone is at hand, you can use the transfer of funds within the network.
