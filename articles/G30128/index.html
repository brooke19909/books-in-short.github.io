Three different ways telling how to make a mask at home.

Very often, parents face the problem of how to make a mask for their little one for the holiday or to participate in a theatrical performance. The easiest way, of course, is to go to the store and buy what you need. However, it happens that suddenly on sale there is no desired model. There are all kinds of masks: a wolf, a bear, a hare, and a spider-man, but there is no turtle mask needed. Or just a little money and you have to save. Or you want to make such an exclusive and original costume that no one will have!

In these cases, there is no other way out than to make a mask with your own hands. But this, in fact, is completely and uncomplicated! But it is very entertaining and interesting. Moreover, the baby will certainly take an active part in this matter, which will make it possible to become even a little closer to him: nothing brings people closer than joint labor impulses.

There are many tutorials on how to make a mask. The simplest of them is a mask-cap or mask-crown. A corona mask needs cardboard, scissors, paints and an elastic band to hold it. The muzzle of the desired animal or fantastic creature is depicted on cardboard, cut out, and an elastic band is sewn along its edges. This mask is worn on the forehead above the eyes, like a crown.

Now you can briefly tell how to make a mask-cap. It should be sewn from fabric. To do this, you will need special patterns, a filler such as chopped foam rubber, buttons for the eyes. After stitching the main parts, the filler should be very tightly filled into the nose (upper jaw) so that it is straight and does not sag, sew on the ears, eyes and nose pipette. It is proposed to make the teeth out of white shoe insoles: it looks scary, but the child cannot hurt anyone with soft fangs.
