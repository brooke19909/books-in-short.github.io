Mitsis Family Village 4 * (Greece, Kos island): hotel description, services, reviews

Mitsis Family Village is a family-run hotel complex located in one of the most popular resorts in Greece. The hotel is widely known for its comfortable rooms, reasonable prices and, of course, the all-inclusive service. However, he has many other advantages.





About hotel

The hotel was built in 1981, and the last restoration was carried out in 2005. Mitsis Family Village is located on the island of Kos, in its southeastern part, right near Mount Dikeos. A 5-minute drive is one of the most popular resorts of this place - Kardamena. It is famous for its pristine sandy beach, which stretches for three kilometers along the coast. Kardamena can be reached from the hotel by the hotel shuttle bus. In half an hour you can walk to the city center. And the airport is 10 minutes away by car.

But the most important thing is the pebble beach belonging to the hotel. Most tourists go here for a beach holiday, so this clarification is important. In addition, there is a very convenient entrance, and the depth does not start right away, which makes the beach ideal for families with small children.

Also nearby are various attractions. For example, Asklepion is an ancient temple built on three levels. You should also look at the fortress of St. John and admire the nearby plane tree of Hippocrates, which, according to legends, was planted by the healer himself.

Bars and Restaurants

At the very beginning, Mitsis Family Village serves its guests an all-inclusive system. And this, in addition to breakfast, lunch and dinner, there are also snacks, soft drinks, soft drinks and strong drinks (both imported and local). And in unlimited quantities.

There is a main restaurant - there guests can enjoy a meal from five "buffet". By the way, dishes are prepared in the presence of guests. Next to the restaurant (near the registration desk) is the main bar, open from 10 am until midnight. This place welcomes guests with a cool, pleasant environment, as well as stunning views of the Aegean Sea. Here you can order anything - from coffee, tea and juices to gin, whiskey and rum. Vodka, tequila, ouzo, brandy, exotic cocktails - this is also available.

There is also a bar on the beach. But it works only until 17:30. There you can taste various drinks, wine, beer, as well as hot dogs, sandwiches, muffins, donuts and other treats. The third bar is located by the pool. They serve all the same, as well as delicious ice cream.

The last bar available is theatrical. Open from 21:00 to 23:00. This is an ideal place for people who want to not only have a drink and a snack, but also watch an entertaining performance.





About Services

In Mitsis Family Village 4 * everything is created for the comfort of guests. So, for example, if guests are going to leave somewhere for the whole day (for an excursion, for example), they will prepare lunches with them. Only for this you need to go to the reception on the evening of the previous day (until 20:00) and make an order for it.

If the guests arrived at the hotel late, then without dinner they will not stay. Especially for them, a variety of soups, salads, sweets and drinks are offered.

On the territory of Mitsis Family Village 4 * there is a mini market, jewelry store, TV-salon, car rental, dry cleaning with laundry, currency exchange and parking. Also, guests have the opportunity to take advantage of flower delivery, transfer to the beach or call a doctor. There are also specialists in the hotel dealing with children (nannies). Many guests who have come to rest with the kids leave their carers in care.

Leisure

While in Mitsis Family Village (Kos), no one will be bored. Here, once a week, a vibrant Greek show is held, in which a local dance group and a folk music orchestra participate. Disco evenings are also organized here.

There are six outdoor pools that lead directly out of the rooms. This is one of the highlights of the hotel. By the way, there is also a swimming pool with a water attraction for children. For adults, there are two slides.

The hotel also has two tennis courts (only equipment can be obtained on deposit). There is a full-fledged fitness center, a billiard room, a volleyball court, canoe rental and a water sports club near the sea. By the way, you can play beach soccer there. Pony and horse riding are available at an additional cost.

And while staying at the hotel, you should definitely take diving lessons and visit a night club called Zone. Only there, drinks are available at an additional cost.





For children

In addition to the above conditions, for the kids the hotel has a mini club. There children are engaged in specially trained and trained personnel. Kids from 4 to 11 years old are accepted there.

The hotel also has a separate children's playground. For kids, they often organize an entertainment program with dances and songs. There is even a room with computer and video games, as well as Internet access.

It should be noted that for very young children (infants), the administration provides high seats in the restaurant and cradles in the room. And the beach for them is good with a comfortable surface and an extremely successful entrance to the sea. Plus, there is shallow water, in which it is convenient for parents to bathe their kids. In general, you can safely come to the Mitsis Family Village 4 * hotel (Kos, Greece) with children. For their rest, all conditions are created here.

Booking and accommodation

From 14:00 the check-in begins, and until 12:00 the departure lasts. In principle, this is not news - this rule is valid in almost all hotels located in such a wonderful country like Greece. The reviews make sure that if there are free and tidy rooms, check-in can be done earlier. However, all this is discussed individually.

Guests with children under the age of 2 years stay free of charge (provide a cot). If he is older than two, but younger than 12, the same rule applies. If necessary, provide an extra bed. But! If there is another child of the same age, then he will have to pay extra 25% of the total cost. Has someone else grown up come with guests? Then it will be necessary to pay 35% of the total cost.

By the way, if you need an extra bed, you must warn the administration in advance. Management will have to notify guests of their decision.

What else is worth knowing

However, this is on the one hand even good - there are not so many vacationers, as in summer, more space. At night, the beaches are generally empty. And very romantic.

By the way, it is definitely worth doing diving here (fortunately, the hotel provides such an opportunity). Or at least buy glasses and swim underwater. There are a lot of different animals in the sea. You can even feed the fish by hand, as experienced tourists claim.
