Hotels Thailand Pattaya - City Coast

Pattaya is a resort city on the east coast, at a distance of 165 kilometers from Bangkok, southeast of the capital of Thailand. In Pattaya, which is one of the most significant tourist centers of the country, more than five million tourists come every year. A lot of guests come to the country from Russia, and last but not least they are attracted by their comfort. Hotels in Thailand Pattaya is glad to meet its guests with warm smiles and affectionate comfortable beaches.

While walking around the city, you somehow do not believe that once, long before my birth, Pattaya was a tiny village in which ordinary people lived, the main occupation of which providing food and clothing to their families was fishing. But in 1960, several wealthy families from Bangkok organized a resort in this village with excellent beaches, and gradually a modern city was built on the site of the village with good opportunities for very stormy entertainment. Now Pattaya is a resort city for families, and there is a place for children too.

The cost of living here is low, and the level of infrastructure is close to the western one, so in Pattaya there are so many tourists from Russia, Scandinavia and Eastern Europe, and most of them come here recently with whole families.
