Pemar Beach Hotel (Turkey / Side): description and reviews

Turkish hotels from year to year offer tourists excellent service at low prices. Therefore, the resorts of this country are very popular among residents of Russia and the CIS countries. Side is a small town located on the Mediterranean coast of Turkey. For a respectable stay, the five-star Pemar Beach Hotel is perfect here. We will tell you more about it in this article.

More about the resort

Side is a popular but small resort located on the Mediterranean coast. Often include nearby villages, for example, Manavgat or Aspendos. Side is located between two other major tourist areas - Alanya and Antalya. Travelers come here in search of a quiet and relaxing beach holiday, which is customary to combine here with a tour of ancient antique ruins and other excursion programs. Diving, by contrast, is not very well developed in the resort, but tourists can look at the French ship, which sank off the nearby shores during World War II.

From July to September, the only art festival in Turkey is held at the Aspendos Amphitheater. Opera and theater groups come here from all over the country to present their performances to tourists.

Side, like other Mediterranean resorts, has a warm climate. The high season begins here in mid-April and lasts until the end of September. The abundance of seafood in local restaurants is another hallmark that gourmet tourists will love.

The coastal line in Side is fully built up with hotels, guest houses and apartments. All of them, as a rule, are located very close to the sea, so you can get to it in a few minutes. Side Pemar Beach Hotel belongs to an impressive five-star complex, which offers its guests a high level of service.

The last renovation of all premises here was carried out in 2011. Tourists can accommodate in a huge seven-story building with two wings. In total, 534 comfortable rooms are equipped in it. The complex can hardly be called quiet and comfortable. In high season, more than 1000 guests can stay here.





The hotel has a lot of Russian-speaking staff. For guests organized a transfer from the airport to the hotel and back. Often tourists with small children, couples, elderly people rest here. Pets are strictly prohibited.

Hotel Location

The description of the hotel Pemar Beach Hotel will be incomplete without indicating a detailed location, because it is often an important factor when choosing a place to stay. It is worth noting that the complex is built on the first coastline, so the distance to the beach is about 100 meters. You can reach it in just a few minutes. Other hotel complexes are also located next to the hotel. Nearby there is a grocery and hardware store, there is a gas station.

The hotel itself is located in the small village of Okudzhalar. The central part of Side is 19 km away. You can get to it by taxi or public transport.

The nearest airport is located near the resort of Antalya. The distance to it is about 90 km. Tourists get to the hotel in an average of 2-3 hours.

Pemar Beach Hotel: description of rooms

All rooms presented in the hotel are standard. They are designed to accommodate two adults, to which the child can additionally be hooked. However, for large families, there are adjacent apartments - rooms that are interconnected by an internal door. Smoking is strictly prohibited in the living area. For violation of this rule, tourists may be fined.





There are 4 rooms at Pemar Beach Hotel in Turkey, specially equipped for wheelchair travelers. The standard apartments are 27 square meters. m. They all have a spacious open balcony, and the hotel windows either overlook the nearby surroundings and the road, or the beach and the sea. As a rule, the latter category costs a little more. Rooms have either laminate or carpet flooring. Each of them has a separate bathroom equipped with a bath or shower.

Every apartment is cleaned daily by maids, who also take out garbage and add drinks to the minibar. Sheets and towels are changed 3 times a week.

Room features

The tourist complex Pemar Beach Hotel, the photo of which can be seen in this article, offers its guests not only standard room furniture (2 single beds, wardrobes, a desk and dining table, chairs, bedside tables), but also additional amenities. We list the main ones:

  • central air conditioning - works on a certain schedule from May to October and is regulated directly by the hotel staff;
  • TV (some rooms may be equipped with old models), to which many cable channels are connected, including in Russian;
  • a minibar, which is replenished daily with non-carbonated drinking water, and the remaining drinks are provided only for a fee;
  • a hairdryer and toiletries in the bathroom;
  • safe, which you can use for free;
  • a phone that can be used to communicate with the administration, and for international calls;
  • 24-hour room service, including food and drink delivery, is available for a fee.

Hotel catering

Like many other tourist resorts, Pemar Beach Hotel offers all-inclusive meals. Between 10:00 a.m. and 02:00 a.m., guests can order free soft drinks and local alcohol. Ice cream is also served during the day. Freshly squeezed juices and imported alcohol are provided for a fee. All drinks are poured into glasses.

The main restaurant of the hotel is open every day - a buffet is served here. In fine weather, meals are also served on the outdoor terrace. The menu offers a large number of dishes of Turkish, Mediterranean and European cuisine. On the table there is always meat, fish, fresh fruits and vegetables, desserts and ice cream. Special diet menus are available upon request for free.





You can have lunch or a meal at the hotel in the following catering establishments:

  • lobby bar - open from morning until midnight;
  • terrace bar - open only in fine weather;
  • round-the-clock pool bar - serves soft drinks, cocktails, ice cream and fruits;
  • disco bar - open only during night discos;
  • beach bar - located on the coast, it also serves only drinks, ice cream and light snacks.

Beach and Pool

Hotel Pemar Beach Resort has its own stretch of coastline, which is about 200 meters long. The beach here is sandy and pebble, and when entering the sea large stones can come across, so you need to wear special shoes so as not to get hurt. Guests enjoy free use of sun loungers, parasols, towels and mattresses. The beach is also equipped with a wooden pier 50 meters long.

The complex also has 3 outdoor pools, the water in which is not heated. The largest area is 1490 square meters. m, average - 300 square meters. m, and small - 244 square meters. m. In good weather, 4 water slides, designed for swimming adults and children, work in the morning and in the afternoon. Near the pools there is also a sunbathing area with umbrellas and sunbeds.





In winter, you can swim in the indoor pool, whose area is only 100 square meters. m. However, it is not heated additionally, so the water in it will still not be too warm.

Sports and SPA

The Pemar Beach Hotel complex in Okurcalar not only offers traditional beach vacations. There are also a lot of options for active and recreational activities. However, most of them can be used for free, since their cost is included in the stay.

For example, in the SPA-salon operating on the territory of the hotel, by appointment, you can visit the sauna and Turkish bath. Paid only massage services.





A distinctive feature of the hotel is the presence of its own amusement park, which operates in the evenings in fine weather. Attractions are completely free.

If you love sports, you will surely like the following entertainment options:

  • basketball court;
  • football field - you can play on it only for a fee;
  • gym;
  • table and classic tennis - you only have to pay for court lighting and racket rental;
  • billiards room;
  • Beach volleyball;
  • mini golf.

Free discos are also held several times a week. The hotel has a Russian-speaking animation team that organizes entertainment programs for adults and children.

Service and Facilities

For a comfortable stay in the complex Pemar Beach Hotel has a developed infrastructure. Employees will always help with the solution of the arisen problems. Guests can use the following services and facilities:

  • amphitheater;
  • doctor's office;
  • business center with two conference rooms;
  • beauty salon and hairdresser - provide their services for a fee;
  • cosmetics, beachwear and souvenir shops;
  • currency exchange and ATM for cash withdrawal;
  • car parking; car rental is also available at the reception;
  • paid laundry.

In addition, in public places you can connect to free Wi-Fi, but its speed is not too high.

Conditions for relaxing with children

Pemar Beach Hotel complex often comes with tourists with young children. An extra bed is provided for free in the room. The restaurant has the opportunity to take a chair for feeding, as well as request a children's menu. For a fee, you can rent a stroller or hire a nanny.





Children have their own shallow pool and playground. You can send your child to the mini club, where Russian-speaking staff works. It works every day from 10:00 to 17:00 with a lunch break.

Positive feedback about Pemar Beach Hotel

Despite the wide variety of services and entertainment offered, the hotel described does not have a very good reputation. Tourists in reviews often note a lot of shortcomings and are not recommended to relax here. However, they do not forget about the merits, pointing to the following advantages:

  • a huge, but always tidied territory, which hosts a tropical garden;
  • own spacious beach, where there are always free sunbeds;
  • convenient access to the sea, which is suitable for small children;
  • varied food: a large number of fresh pastries are served for breakfast, several types of meat and fish for lunch and dinner;
  • polite staff who speaks good Russian;
  • tidy rooms with a classic repair - sometimes tourists are offered a free choice of rooms with sea views.




Negative feedback from guests

Many do not recommend coming to this hotel, believing that it does not correspond to five stars. As arguments, tourists indicate the following serious drawbacks:

  • the hotel often smells bad sewer;
  • The rooms are equipped with old beds with uncomfortable mattresses, which are impossible to sleep on;
  • the restaurant does not have enough tables for all guests, so you often have to stand and wait until the place becomes available;
  • rather monotonous menu, sometimes baked pastries are found;
  • not all air conditioners work.

Thus, we can say that Pemar Beach Hotel will be a good option for family and beach holidays. However, it does not reach other five-star resort complexes, which affects its price. It is much lower here, therefore, despite all the shortcomings, tourists still choose this hotel for relaxation.
