Why do we need interest

Any financial institution will never work at a loss for itself, therefore, creating credit products even on the most favorable conditions for the client, the bank will not offend itself. Why are there commissions and interest for using cards?

Firstly, servicing ATMs and paying rent is not cheap, so many banks charge interest on money withdrawals, and they can reach about 10% for some organizations. Secondly, issuing and servicing a card also requires cash costs from the bank, therefore, usually a financial institution charges a fee for either issuing a card or servicing it in the form of a monthly or annual fee.





But banks compensate for these nuances by the fact that the percentage is not taken when paying for purchases or housing and communal services, as well as for operations in online stores and when paying for mobile.

In addition, all banks offer interest-free periods for using credit cards. They can be from 50 to 100 days. If the debt is repaid within the prescribed period, the bank does not charge a fee for using the money.

Some tricks

For those who constantly use a credit card, the question ‚Äúis it possible to withdraw money from a credit card‚Äù is quite relevant.

And apparently, it was such users who came up with several tricky ways how to avoid large commissions and interest for cashing out money.





Here is some of them:

  1. Purchase any item in the store. According to the law on consumer rights, goods can be returned within two weeks without explanation. Having paid for the goods with a credit card, the return of the amount can be made in cash. Thus, the percentage for the purchase is not withdrawn, and after a while there is cash in your pocket.
  2. Make a purchase in the online store. When paying for a purchase with a credit card, the bank does not withdraw interest. Then after a while you can cancel the order and request a refund to the debit card. But in this case, you will have to wait up to five business days.
  3. Invite a friend to use a credit card for purchase. In this case, a friend or acquaintance of the owner of the credit card pays for his purchase using the proposed credit card. Accordingly, the cardholder receives cash in an equivalent amount.
  4. Payment by mobile phone. Topping up a mobile account with a credit card occurs without interest. Then you can withdraw this money to a regular card. But here you need to remember that the mobile operator can take interest on their services. It turns out that the money will leave the credit card without commission, but the operator will have to pay.

In addition to these tricky methods, there are probably more options on how to withdraw money from a credit card with a minimum percentage or no at all, but nothing is known about them yet.

Terms of registration

When issuing a card, it is better to check with the bank manager whether it is possible to withdraw money from a credit card and what percentage will be charged for this so that there are no surprises in the future.





The conditions for issuing a credit card are quite simple:

  • The borrower can visit the bank office or fill out an online application for a card.
  • The age of the borrower must be from 21 years to 70 years.
  • You must have a steady income.
  • The borrower must have a residence permit in the region where he takes the card.
  • It is necessary to provide the documents requested by the bank. This is usually a passport and a second identity document. If the amount on the card is large, the bank may request a statement of income.

In general, as can be seen from the requirements, they are similar to those presented when applying for any loan.
