Modern cross-stitch pillows - great opportunities for creativity

A way of realizing creative potential, which has a long history, is cross stitching of pillows, which are an exclusive addition to the interior and an original gift on the occasion of the celebration.



Like any other human handiwork, such creativity corresponds to certain rules. Basically, they are subject only to a graphic image made using symbols (color or black and white diagram) and the process of preparing for work. The main requirement is a correctly selected base, needle and thread. As a rule, cross-stitching of pillows is carried out on a canvas of various weaving densities. The thickness of the colored threads and the size of the needle depend on the size of the cell formed at the intersection of the threads of the web.

For those for whom cross-stitching of pillows is a completely new occupation, it is worth paying attention to ready-made kits available in large quantities. Their use greatly facilitates the process of creating various products.



When choosing a kit for needlework, you need to pay attention to the form and size of the finished work specified in the instructions: how much do they correspond to what you want? Since all the material necessary for the work is already selected, and the pattern for cross-stitch corresponds to its quantity in a proportional ratio. The main lesson is preceded by a small preparatory work:

  • preparation of threads - division into the required number;
  • processing the edges of the canvas - bending and overcasting;
  • meshing - 10x10 tissue cells are equal to one square in the drawing diagram;
  • determination of the center of the picture on a graphic image and on a fabric basis;
  • determination of the number of additions of threads for specific areas of work;
  • hoop refill.


Having a certain skill in this type of creativity, you can independently make a selection of materials. There are also no strict rules in this matter, however, there are a number of preferences under which cross-stitching of pillows will bring more joy and pleasure, since the process itself will be less time-consuming and more productive. The hoop is best used wooden or plastic with a screw to adjust the canvas tension. The average size of the needle with a large eye and a rounded point is an ideal option for almost any fabric. The type of thread is selected based on the selected pattern and the result that you want to get. Aida threads will give a glossy gloss to the product, volume and softness - threads with the addition of Riolis wool. The Gamma cotton floss helps to highlight the outline of the picture or individual details .

For self-selection of schemes, you can use special printed products. They are contained there already in finished form, indicating the type of threads, color palette and with graphic symbols. However, free cross stitch patterns created using the PM editor program are currently available. Thanks to its functional set, it is quite simple to create the necessary schemes for a photograph or an arbitrary drawing.
