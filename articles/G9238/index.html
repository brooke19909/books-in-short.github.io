Abu Dhabi Hotels - A Fairy Tale Travel

The main city of the United Arab Emirates is a tourist paradise called Abu Dhabi. It is also the capital of the emirate with the same name. Many beautiful and unique mosques in the city center, stunning fountains located on the promenade, for tourists the number of impressions here is just off the scale. Vacationers have every opportunity to enjoy a full and interesting vacation in Abu Dhabi. They are attracted by the purest sea waters and beautiful beaches, all the delights of practicing water sports.

Abu Dhabi hotels provide luxury, comfort and traditional oriental hospitality. Every year a huge number of tourists come here and everyone can find a hotel of their own liking. The best reviews about Abu Dhabi hotels are left by tourists who prefer first-class vacations and live in five-star hotels. From a large list of such hotels, the Sheraton Abu Dhabi Hotel, Emirates Palace, Le Meridien Royal Abu Dhabi, Radisson Blu Hotel Abu Dhabi Yas Island and many other high-level hotels can be distinguished.

Abu Dhabi heads the Emirates Palace hotels. Its building is an example of exquisite oriental architecture, it is literally a palace from an oriental tale. In the very center of the palace stands a majestic dome decorated with colored mosaics. Around the main dome there are more than a hundred small domes decorated with gold spiers. The color scheme of the amazing building makes it possible to imagine yourself in the middle of the desert and see all its colors. As if in the middle of a vast desert, the hotel has a beautiful and cozy green oasis. The hotel has 342 rooms, the bulk of the deluxe category, 20 restaurants and bars, which include Iranian, Lebanese, Italian, Japanese, Chinese, Latin American and international cuisines.

There is a night club, two modern spa centers, two swimming pools, tennis courts and a golf course, a banquet hall, a huge conference hall for 1200 people, a business center with modern equipment and a helipad. On the top floor of the hotel there are 6 rooms in which only royalties from Middle Eastern countries can live. The hotel has the most modern technology, the rooms are equipped with plasma panels with a diagonal of 125 centimeters, in the suites there are panels with a size of 150 centimeters, they can be used not only as a TV, but also as a computer monitor. Television programs can be recorded in memory and rewound if necessary. Specially for the hotel, 1,002 Swarowsky chandeliers were manufactured and 10 employees clean them daily.

Abu Dhabi five star hotels also represents the Sheraton Abu Dhabi Resort. It can use an adult pool, a children's pool, a wide variety of water activities, a sauna and a beauty salon. Tennis courts and a playground are on site. There is a business center and many other indispensable attributes of a modern upscale holiday. The luxurious and cozy rooms have air conditioning, a bathtub, bidet, satellite TV and a personal safe. This one of the best hotels has recently undergone reconstruction. The hotel has 26 deluxe rooms, 3 exquisite royal suites. Most of the rooms have a magnificent view of the sea, for business people on the sixth floor there are rooms with additional services. Of course, Abu Dhabi hotels are some of the best in the Middle East. No wonder many tourists agree that a trip here is like a fairy tale "A Thousand and One Nights."
