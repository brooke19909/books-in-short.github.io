Hotel Location

So, the description of Sheraton Sharjah Beach Resort SPA must begin with the location. As soon as we find out where the hotel is located, it will be easier for us to understand how advantageous the location is.

Sheraton Sharjah Beach Resort is located in United Arab Emirates. The UAE is a state located in the Middle East. It consists of seven emirates, each of which has a monarchical form of government. The UAE is located in the southwestern part of Asia, the eastern part of the Arabian Peninsula. It is washed by the waters of the Persian and Oman gulfs. One of the seven emirates is called Sharjah. This is where our hotel is located. Sharjah is located in the east of the country. This is a fairly developed part of the state, but there are strict morals. That is why most party-goers are going to have fun in the neighboring city of Dubai. Alcohol in this emirate is available only in some hotel bars. Moreover, tourists here should not appear in shorts, short dresses in public places, men should always wear something upstairs. The enforcement of this rule is constantly monitored by the morale police.

The beach season here lasts all year. Better time to relax is from October to May. It is during this period that there is no intense heat. By the way, sandstorms sometimes occur in Sharjah, most often in the spring. Fortunately, they quickly end without causing much damage to the rest.
