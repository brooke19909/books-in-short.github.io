Bread machine Panasonic SD ZB2512: description and reviews

The article will focus on one of the makers of the manufacturer Panasonic. The company has long been a leader in this field. There are many different models in the line, which are recommended to be looked at by potential buyers. For example, Panasonic SD ZB2512. The device will meet all expectations and will please every housewife.





Brief characteristics

For the breadmaker to work, 550 watts of power is needed. The loaf at the end will weigh 600, 800 grams or 1 kg. It depends on the selected mode and form. Among the available functions there is a timer, pause, the ability to choose the size of the bread and the color of the crust. However, the last two options are not relevant in all programs.

Management is thanks to touch buttons. There is an LCD display. The timer allows you to make a delay of no more than 13 hours. When the device is turned off, the settings are saved in the device memory.

The total weight of the Panasonic SD ZB2512 is 7.5 kg. The manufacturer gives a guarantee of 1 year. The average cost is 10 thousand rubles.

Of the available programs, one should note the ability to bake various types of bread, kneading several types of dough, as well as making jam and fruit in syrup.

Description

Model SD ZB2512 - the best bread machine, according to consumers. It has improved features and several new options.

An interesting nuance of this device is that it received two dispensers. The first is necessary so that the device serves additional ingredients (raisins, nuts, tomatoes). Moreover, the device does this at the end of the batch of dough, which allows not to spoil the color of the finished product. The second dispenser serves to add yeast. Sometimes even experienced and professional chefs can put this ingredient in oil or salt, which then prevents the dough from rising. That is why a dispenser is used. The Panasonic SD ZB2512 bread machine will add yeast to the kneaded mass, which will allow them to perform their function perfectly.

The container of the device received a non-stick coating. It guarantees excellent cooking. Moreover, this "bucket" is fully resistant to scratches and damage. Inside there are two special ledges. They are necessary for better kneading the dough. Therefore, the hostess will not have to constantly look into the device to check the status of the process. Thanks to the thick walls of the appliance, heating will be even. The form is strong, so it will not break if dropped.



Functionality

The Panasonic SD ZB2512 bread machine has two completely new features that will please any housewife. We are talking about the option of cooking bread with filling, as well as low-yeast dough. When working with the second program, yeast really needs much less, the amount is reduced by about half. In this case, the product is very tasty. The function is designed for a long period of time during which the dough rises. This is what allows you to achieve the most successful result. The program can run from an hour to 4 hours 50 minutes.

Among the advantages of the Panasonic SD ZB2512 device are a set of programs, the presence of a dispenser, and a large number of functions for creating a test.

The disadvantages include the fact that when cooking a certain type of product, you can not choose the size and color of the crust.





Reviews

Reviews about the device are quite good. Many write that he fully justifies the price. What good consumers say? Delicious products, perfectly kneaded dough, a steel case that requires minimal maintenance, many programs.

Of the minuses, consumers notice the dispensers are too loud, as well as overly fragile plastic inserts.
