Hotel Phulin Resort The 3 * (Thailand / Phuket): description, reviews and photos

Phulin Resort The 3 * is a beautiful and cozy hotel with comfortable rooms and a huge number of services.





Hotel Overview

Resort Phulin Resort The 3 * offers you to live in a bright and comfortable apartment. Bright and unusual decor is successfully complemented by simple wooden furniture. The room also provides many amenities that will make your stay at the hotel as comfortable as possible so that you feel as comfortable as at home.

The Phulin Resort The 3 * hotel provides its guests with a wide variety of services. Among them, the most popular is massage, which is done by masters in accordance with local medical traditions. Also, while in Vietnam, you should definitely visit its sights, in which the tour desk will provide all possible assistance.

Special attention deserves the restaurant of the hotel Phulin Resort The 3 *. The emphasis here is on international cuisine, so that everyone can find something to their taste. Separately provided menu with traditional local dishes. All guests can use the food delivery service directly to the apartment.





Hotel Location

Resort Phulin Resort 3 * on the map of Phuket is central. The famous Karon Beach is a short walk away. And if you ride 10 minutes by bus, you can be on the coast of Patong. The journey from Phuket International Airport to the hotel takes less than 45 minutes.





Hotel Rooms

The Phulin Resort 3 * hotel (Thailand, Phuket, Karon Beach) has 79 guest rooms that can be divided into several groups:

  • Standard room means double occupancy. This is a spacious bedroom with a king size bed or a pair of one and a half.
  • Double deluxe is a huge apartment, which includes both a bedroom and a living room. Also for the convenience of guests there is a small area with kitchen utensils, dishes and appliances.
  • Another modification of the deluxe rooms is located on the ground floor. Its difference from other apartments and the main feature is that from the veranda you can go directly to the pool.




Room facilities and amenities

Resort hotel number 1 The Phulin Resort 3 * cares about the comfort and convenience of its guests. For this purpose, the hotel apartments were equipped with the following list of amenities:

  • individual air conditioning, which, regardless of the vagaries of the weather, will help you maintain a comfortable climate;
  • the TV is connected to a satellite dish, so you have the opportunity to watch dozens of entertainment channels, among which are Russian-speaking;
  • you can take discs with your favorite movies with you, because the room has a player for them;
  • every day your minibar will be replenished with drinking water, and for an additional fee you will receive your favorite alcoholic or carbonated drinks;
  • the bathroom has a shower, hairdryer, as well as a set of bath and hygiene items;
  • so that you do not worry about the safety of your valuables, you are given the opportunity to store them in a safe.

Resort Infrastructure

Phulin Resort has the following infrastructure facilities:

  • Guests can use the swimming pool, which is located in the courtyard of the hotel;
  • You can try local unusual dishes in the restaurant, where the best chefs work;
  • to see all the sights and find out interesting facts about their history, use the services of a bureau for organizing sightseeing tours;
  • You can get the most effective cosmetic or medical procedures in the modern spa;
  • in the shade of palm trees there is a cozy playground with lots of swings, attractions and a sandbox;
  • you have the opportunity to undergo a medical examination or call a doctor in the hotel;
  • You can use both wireless and cable Internet;
  • if you stained your things, then quickly and accurately remove stains in a dry cleaning or laundry;
  • in order to travel around all the interesting places and surrounding settlements on your own, you can rent a car, and leave it in a guarded parking lot for free at night;
  • at the round-the-clock reception you will be offered to exchange currency for a small commission percentage.




additional information

The See Fulin Resort Hotel has a number of internal rules that govern the stay and relaxation of guests:

  • You can register at the reception desk and check into your room from two in the afternoon until midnight;
  • you need to return the keys to the reception from noon to one in the afternoon on the day you leave the hotel;
  • Children under 12 years old are charged 200 baht for beds in the room;
  • Free accommodation is available only for babies up to 2 years old with the possibility of placing a baby cot;
  • an extra bed (regardless of whether the child will live on it or an adult) costs 100 baht per night;
  • each room cannot accommodate more than one extra bed or cot;
  • The hotel does not allow pets;
  • You can use a plastic card to pay with the hotel for the services received.




Positive reviews

Resort The Phulin Resort 3 * (Phuket, Karon Beach) leaves tourists with a lot of positive impressions, which is expressed in numerous approving comments:

  • the beach is not far enough (about 15-20 minutes if you walk in walking steps;
  • all guests are absolutely free to bring suitcases to their rooms (although they will not refuse a tip);
  • the rooms are very spacious and bright, the walls are also quite cheerful;
  • in the lobby area there is a cooler with boiling water, which partially compensates for the lack of an electric kettle in the rooms;
  • the hotel area, although small, but very well maintained, and it is nice to be here;
  • daily replacement of towels is made, as well as separate ones for going to the beach;
  • the pool is small, but it is very clean, and the water in it gives a feeling of freshness (almost does not smell of bleach);
  • there is a Russian-speaking girl at the reception, which greatly simplifies the process of solving all problems and issues;
  • In the vicinity there are a lot of shops, cafes and shops.




Negative reviews

After staying at this hotel, tourists have the following comments:

  • a very modest breakfast menu (there is practically nothing), moreover, it is held too early;
  • the air conditioners in the rooms are noisy, and therefore very old (but it is not possible to turn it off at night due to the extreme heat);
  • during check-in, you have to leave a deposit of $ 100 (or the equivalent of this amount in any other currency);
  • very old furniture and plumbing in the rooms (water is constantly dripping from the shower and taps);
  • in half of the apartments the hair dryer turned out to be inoperative;
  • the hotel has very thin walls, and therefore poor sound insulation (not only the noise of the street is heard, but also everything that happens in the neighboring apartments);
  • there is no sidewalk on the way to the sea, so you have to make a big circle so as not to go along the roadway;
  • rooms are cleaned quite rarely and very poorly (even if you hang a special plate on the door handle, this still does not guarantee that the maid will come to your room);
