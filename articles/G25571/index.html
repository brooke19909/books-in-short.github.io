Aquarium in Valencia, Spain: address, opening hours, how to get

In the world there are a huge number of places that deserve special attention. For example, one of the most beautiful attractions is the Aquarium of Valencia. What is he famous for? How to get to it? What objects are worth paying attention to? The answers to all these important questions can be found in this article.





Short description of the institution

The aquarium in Valencia (Spain) is part of the legendary project "City of Science and Art", created in 2009. Today it is the largest in Europe, because its total area noticeably exceeds 100 thousand square meters.





The first thing that catches your eye is the appearance of the entertainment complex. The organizers made sure that he looked original, making it in a futuristic style. Two small towers are interconnected by transparent bridges and underground tunnels.

The creators have been working on this project for more than 15 years. Everything needs to be designed to the smallest detail. The result was not long in coming! People are very pleased to be in this place. They have the feeling that they are really under water.

Getting to know the entertainment center

Entering the entertainment complex, the visitor sees the box office. The staff tries to serve each client very quickly, so there are practically no long lines. If you wish, you can buy the right to visit via the Internet, but it does not apply to a seasonal 10 percent discount.





After receiving the ticket, the visitor must go to the turnstile. Then he finds himself in a spacious hall, in which are located colorful photographs of the oceanarium in Valencia. You can choose for yourself the most interesting objects and go to their study. If you have difficulty traveling in this place or if you want to get more detailed information about each object, then you should use the services of a guide. On this site, several souvenir and grocery stores are organized, as well as a left-luggage office.

A few reasons to visit the aquarium

Each person, at least once in his life, should visit the aquarium in Valencia. There may be several reasons for visiting it:





  1. Here you can feel like a part of the underwater world. Unique design will give the most pleasant emotions. In some places, photography is allowed, which will capture these moments for a long memory.
  2. Today, more than 500 species of the most diverse marine life live in this place, unique individuals are brought from all over the globe.
  3. It is worth noting that all organisms do not exist in artificial conditions. Visitors will not have to watch how they suffer. The organizers made sure that seawater was comfortable in the water chambers.
  4. A small museum is organized on the territory of this institution, in which you can get acquainted with the unique flora.
  5. At this place, brochures are sold in several languages, which will allow everyone to better get acquainted with the main objects of this place.

The administration of the entertainment complex is very polite to each of its visitors. At the entrance, a national drink is completely free - orchata, created from chufa tubers. Many people come to this place just to enjoy its taste.

Tropical part





As mentioned earlier, the aquarium in Valencia is divided into two main components. To a greater extent, visitors love the tropical component of the complex, in which there is a temperate sea. You can get closer to this place only by walking along the bridge passing through the crystal clear pool. In appearance, the building looks like a small ball. But do not despair, most of the space is located exactly underground. A person will experience the real pleasure of visiting this part of the entertainment center. He will go through a tunnel, the length of which reaches 70 meters, and from all sides of it will be a variety of fish species. In addition, the natural sound of water, splashing and the sound of the sea will be heard from all sides. Perhaps he will even have a sense of fear, because the overall picture will be very similar to reality!

Mediterranean part

At the exit from the first part of the aquarium you can see a mesh ball, similar to a dome. Its height reaches the border of 26 meters. A person can come to this place in order to get acquainted with individuals that live in swamps, reed beds or in small water sources.





It also has a long tunnel with cameras of various sizes installed on its sides. If the visitor has difficulties with which object is presented in front of them, then he can read about him the description presented in the photo below. If necessary, you can turn on the backlight.

In this place there is an imitation of a destroyed pirate boat, it is believed that real treasures are stored in it.

Show organization





The real happiness is to get to the legendary show in the best oceanarium in the world! Each season a unique project is organized in which dolphins perform. Visitors can sit near the pool in order to look at unusual art involving animals. In addition, the host may ask several people to go on stage so that they have a chance to touch the beautiful animal world!

How to get there

There are many reasons to visit the Aquarium of Valencia. How to get to this place? The most reliable way to do this is by using the services of a guide. Collective tours to this place are organized several times a year. If a person decided to do this on his own, then he must pave the following route:

  • Get in any way to Moscow or St. Petersburg.
  • Take a flight to the airport of Spain.
  • Go down the subway and get to one of the stations: Tarongers, Xativa or Maritim-Serreria.
  • At the exit from it will be a bus stop. It is required to take the route number 15, 95, 190a, 190b or 190c.
  • Only a few meters are left to the designated place, which can be overcome on foot.

Tourists are advised to use taxi services.

The approximate cost will be from 10 to 25 euros. In the evening, tariff prices increase markedly.
