Hotel Resort De Alturas 4 *, Goa, India: photos and reviews of tourists

If you plan to relax in Goa (India) and are looking for a comfortable hotel near the sea, then as a suitable option we suggest you consider Resort De Alturas 4 *.



Location

Resort De Alturas is located in the northern part of Goa, in the resort village of Candolim. The nearest beach is 600 meters away. The hotel is 40 kilometers from the airport, and 23 kilometers from the train station. In the vicinity of Resort De Alturas 4 * there are several cafes, restaurants, bars, shops, supermarkets, massage parlors, tour agencies, exchange offices, etc.

Hotel Resort De Alturas 4 *: reviews of Russian tourists

Any experienced traveler will tell you that it is very important to start preparing in advance for any trip abroad. Especially when it comes to the long-awaited vacation. After all, such an approach will allow you to plan everything more carefully and avoid misunderstandings and difficulties during your vacation. Seasoned tourists are advised to pay great attention to the issue of choosing a hotel. Indeed, even if you consider yourself to be unpretentious to the living conditions of a person and do not plan to spend a lot of time in the hotel, for sure you will want to return to a clean and comfortable room every day with comfortable furniture and everything you need. Therefore, many travelers spend hours studying the reviews of those who have already visited a particular place. Indeed, in their comments, former guests try to mention not only the pluses, but also the minuses that you may have to face in each particular hotel. In order to save your time and energy, we suggest that you familiarize yourself with the already summarized reviews of Russian tourists who recently stayed in a four-star hotel in Goa Resort De Alturas. Immediately, we note that almost all of our compatriots were very pleased with this hotel. But we learn about everything in more detail.



Housing stock

Judging by the reviews of our compatriots, the rooms of De Alturas Resort 4 * (Goa, India) did not disappoint them. So, the guests found the apartments not very large, but spacious enough for comfortable accommodation, as well as nicely decorated, clean and comfortable. In addition, each room has everything that guests may require. Many were pleased with the availability of free safes. In addition, a big plus, according to our compatriots, is the fact that throughout the hotel, including apartments, there is access to wireless Internet. Moreover, Wi-Fi can be used for free, and the connection speed is usually quite good. So you can always be in touch with your friends and family, staying at home.





Cleaning

Tourists did not have any complaints about the work of the maids of this hotel. According to our compatriots, they cleaned the rooms quite well, on a daily basis. Towels and bed linen were renewed every other day. As expenditures, the maids replenished the contents of the set for making coffee and tea, as well as supplies of bath accessories. In addition, bottled drinking water will be brought to your room every day. In addition, hotel guests note that very often maids erected amusing figures of towels and flower petals on their beds. For such attention, many were not sorry to periodically leave a tip.

Check in, departure

Accommodation of newly arrived guests in the rooms of the Resort De Alturas 4 * hotel (North Goa, Candolim) intended for them, in accordance with internal rules, is made after two in the afternoon. However, according to our compatriots, if you arrive at the hotel in the morning, you can ask to be accommodated as soon as possible by paying about $ 10-20 for this service. As a rule, there are no difficulties with this, so you do not have to wait a few hours.

As for the day of departure, the occupied apartments should be vacated before noon. If your departure from the hotel is planned later, then you can leave things in storage in the luggage room and continue to relax. For an additional fee, you can extend your stay by several hours.



Nutrition

The cost of living in this hotel includes breakfast only. As Russian tourists say in their reviews, in the morning you can eat porridge, pastries, local dishes, scrambled eggs, scrambled eggs or boiled eggs, jam, coffee, tea, juice. You can also always enjoy fresh fruits. Especially guests liked pineapples, papaya and watermelons.

You can also have lunch and dinner in the restaurant De Alturas Resort 4 * (India, Goa). Here you can sample local specialties and seafood. Also, if desired, it is possible to order lunch or dinner in the room.

However, many guests of the Resort De Alturas preferred to eat outside the hotel during the day. So, according to them, in its vicinity there are many cafes and restaurants where they cook very tasty, and the prices are very reasonable. Moreover, you can find establishments with both local and international, as well as Russian cuisine. So, some of our compatriots were glad to diversify their menu with familiar dishes like dumplings or borsch.

Please note: experienced travelers warn that in all establishments when ordering drinks, you should ask that you do not add ice to them. The fact is that it is made here from running water, which contains bacteria that can cause intestinal upsets. Therefore, in order to avoid possible excesses, do not forget about such a feature.





Beach

According to our compatriots, from the Hotel Resort De Alturas you can walk to the sea in about 10 minutes. The path runs through the city streets, where you can buy something you need and, if you wish, have a bite to eat. The beach here is quite large, with a small pleasant yellow sand. Entering shallow water. However, as tourists note, the sea here is often turbulent, but many loved to frolic in the waves.

As for the beach, it is regularly cleaned, so it is always clean here. Here you can stay in any place you like by simply laying a mat on the sand. However, many tourists preferred to rent sun loungers. And you can stay next to one of the beach cafes. If you order a drink here, you can use the sunbed and umbrella for free. In addition, you can connect to Wi-Fi, and you will not have to go far for refreshments.
