Crochet beanie: schemes, description and interesting models

Today, knitting has become so popular that not only women, but many men are well versed in handicrafts. Depending on their preferences, they order or buy ready-made sweaters, hats, pullovers, scarves and many other things.



Eccentricity versus classic

Along with classic, simple wardrobe items, craftswomen are increasingly receiving orders for various non-standard products. For example, knitted crowns for babies, mittens in the form of hedgehogs, sweaters with the image of deers, as well as adult and children's animal hats crocheted. These accessories are a stylized head of some real or fictional animal.

Crocheted animal hat can be equipped with eyes and nose. Some models provide only an opening instead of a muzzle, so the person who put on the hat transforms into the selected beast.

As prototypes, the craftswomen choose the most diverse representatives of the animal world: foxes, wolves, dinosaurs, giraffes, cockerels, raccoons, rams, as well as all kinds of cartoon or fictional monsters. Below are some interesting animal hats and recommendations regarding the manufacturing process.

What yarn to choose

To get a durable and warm crochet animal hat, you need to choose the right materials. First of all, the yarn should be high in wool: at least 50%. Otherwise, the crochet beanie will be beautiful, but non-functional. Do not use a fully acrylic thread, as synthetic products can be hot, but they will not give real heat.



If we are talking about knitting for children, then we need to pay close attention not only to the percentage of wool in the yarn, but also to the origin of the raw material. Sheep wool can be stiff and prickly. Some manufacturers have learned how to handle the thread very well and achieve that it becomes completely soft. However, this is the exception rather than the rule.

If you need a crochet animal beanie, the best choice is merino wool. This yarn around the world is recognized as the softest and highest quality.

This is done in order to protect the neck from wind and frost.

Universal animal hat

As a rule, most of the unique knitted hats are created by talented (and moderately eccentric) designers who are in no hurry to give out patterns for free and make life easier for those who want to duplicate their creation.

You can bring your favorite model to life by mastering basic knitting skills and understanding how classic hats are formed. Next, the beast hat will be considered, the scheme of which is an improved semicircular hat.



For its manufacture, you can use yarn of any thickness. The diagram shows the calculation for double crochet (CCH). The main principle is that 12 CCHs are knitted in the first row, and in each subsequent additions are also made at 12 points.

If the craftswoman wants to get a denser canvas, she should use columns without a crochet (RLS). Here, the algorithm of work will be slightly different: the first row is 6 sc, and the addition of new elements, respectively, at six points.


Features of work

In order for the bottom of the cap to be formed correctly, you must adhere to the above description. Places for adding columns can be marked with plastic markers. When the diameter of the bottom is 1-2 cm narrower than required, you need to knit one row without additions. In the next row, follow the basic instruction (introduce new elements at 6 or 12 points), then repeat the series again without additions and the expanding series. Now you can continue to work without tying out new columns.

The indicated sequence (alternating rows) is necessary in order for the cap to have smooth outlines. Otherwise, if, after intensive expansion, we proceed to knitting an even web, an ugly sharp transition may form.

If the usual patterns and patterns have already become boring and bored, you can apply the "bushes" pattern. He will give the product a slight effect of lace.



The part of the cap that will cover the ears can be made denser by applying, for example, single crochet posts.

How to tie ears

Each decorative ear consists of two parts: external and internal. The first one will be slightly larger and should be knitted from the main color yarn. The second, respectively, is smaller and it needs material in a contrasting color. The algorithm for manufacturing a large part:

  • Run a chain of 7 air loops (VP).
  • Knit in each of them according to the sc.
  • In the 8th loop, perform 3 sc.
  • From the back of the chain also knit 7 sc.
  • At the end of the part, knit 3 sc in one loop of the base.

Further, all the rows will be circular: in the beginning you should make an air loop, and in the end - the connecting column.

Two elements should be knitted into each loop of the base: either RLS or CCH. For the formation of a narrow edge, it is necessary to use RLS, and when moving to the manufacture of the wide side, one cannot do without higher CCH.



The size of the parts is regulated by performing one or another number of rows. For example, a small eye can be connected from two, and a large eye from three rows. If three levels of CCH is too much, the last row is performed by RLS. Parts that are tied with threads of different thicknesses will have a different size: thin yarn is suitable for making a small ear, and thick yarn is for a large one. In this case, the number of rows and loops in the elements will be the same.
