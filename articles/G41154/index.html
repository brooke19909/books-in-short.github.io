How to make a fire costume for a children's party

To participate in a children's matinee, toddlers often require various costumes that are suitable for the plot and theme. If a Russian folk tale is played out, then often it has the role of fire. Read about how to make a fire suit with your own hands in our article today.

Fire suit for a boy

If you are going to make a fire outfit for a boy, then you will not need so much materials and time to make it. Instructions on how to make a fire suit for a boy, below.

  1. Take a Whatman paper and draw a flame on it with a simple pencil. You will need one big fire for the abdomen, two for the legs and one more in the form of a mask. Do not forget about the holes for the eyes and nose.
  2. Paint the painted details with orange paint. Red paint can be edged or painted over individual fragments.
  3. Cut out all the images of fire.
  4. Since you already made the main elements of the fire suit, you will need black and plain clothes. Ideally, black trousers and a thin cotton long-sleeved T-shirt. Sew one of the lights carefully onto the belly of the T-shirt. This is best done with orange thread.
  5. Sew the lights to the bottom of the legs in the same way.
  6. On both sides of the mask, make holes and, having rubber band, tie knots. The fire suit is ready!

Fire outfit for girls

In fact, you can make a fire suit from any materials that can be found in every home. For example, to make an outfit for a girl, you can use an old skirt or sweater. Of course, their color should be either red or orange. Make such a suit is not difficult.

  1. Take orange and red tulle, sew small pieces to the skirt belt so that each individual element creates a sharp end. When walking, such a fiery belt will develop and create the illusion of a real fire.
  2. In the same way, you can decorate any blouse. Only here you will also need satin ribbons of the corresponding colors. Cut small pieces of ribbons and cut one of the sides in the form of a triangle. Then sew the ribbons one edge to the neck of the shirt, as well as to its lower edge.
  3. From the red thin felt, cut out the image of fire. From orange, a similar image is smaller. Sew or glue the second to the first. And carefully sew the fire to a skirt or dress.
  4. Satin ribbons can be used to make hand jewelry. Create small felt lights as described in point 3, wash them on small pieces of red ribbon. Tie a decorated ribbon on each hand with bows.

Making a fire costume is not at all difficult. You can come up with many ideas yourself, just by including your imagination and imagination.
