Hotel Nautilus Barbati 3 * (Corfu Island, Greece): overview, description and reviews

Greece is a country with an amazing history that is literally saturated with the atmosphere of ancient myths. Of course, thousands of tourists come here every year, wishing to have a pleasant and interesting time.

When it comes to relaxing on the seashore, most travelers prefer to spend time on one of the secluded islands. And the island of Corfu is considered to be quite popular, the magnificent landscapes and romantic atmosphere of which attract a huge number of tourists. Of course, there are a sufficient number of hotels and pensions on the shore, one of which is the Nautilus Barbati 3 * hotel.

Of course, when choosing a vacation destination, an experienced traveler pays attention to a lot of factors. For example, it is important to find out exactly where the Hotel Nautilus Barbati 3 * hotel complex is located and what conditions the guest can expect? Is there a transport interchange nearby and are there meals available for guests? Is the hotel suitable for families with a child? The answers to these questions are important.

Hotel Location Features





This hotel complex is worth looking for on the northeast coast of Corfu. Nautilus Barbati 3 * is built on a rocky slope of the coast, in a quiet resort area, where mostly travelers live. By the way, the hotel does not work all year round, but only from April to October.

The famous resort of Ipsos is just 2 km away. The large city of Corfu is approximately 15 km away. The same distance will have to be overcome to the international airport. Naturally, guests can count on a transfer. And here is a good traffic intersection. Using public transport, the hotel offers easy access to almost every corner of the island.

Hotel Nautilus Barbati 3 * (Greece, Corfu): features, infrastructure





The hotel complex occupies a fairly large territory. It consists of four separate multi-story buildings and a large courtyard. The hotel is built almost on a hillside, which descends and breaks off above the sea. The cliffs are equipped with terraces for relaxation and sunbathing.

The territory itself, according to reviews, is very beautiful - residential buildings are literally buried in verdure. Tourists can enjoy the bright colors of thermophilic plants, relax in the shade of perennial cypress trees and, of course, enjoy the seascape.

What rooms offer guests?

Hotel Nautilus Barbati 3 * is not too big - on its territory there are 64 rooms. For the most part, guests are offered standard rooms designed for two people. On the other hand, there are more spacious rooms where 3 or even 4 guests can freely stay. Of course, there are several family rooms that are perfect for parents with children.

Photo and detailed information about living rooms





Naturally, comfort is an important part of relaxation. What can the guests of the Nautilus Barbati 3 * hotel count on? Reviews of tourists say that you can really have a good time here. All rooms are quite spacious and bright, and from the windows (or balcony) you can enjoy views of the picturesque cliffs and the sea, or a no less beautiful garden with a pool. Of course, the rooms have the necessary furniture, including beds, tables, chairs, bedside tables and large wardrobes.

There is also a basic set of household appliances that will make living here as comfortable as possible. For example, you can create a pleasant temperature in the room - you will have individual air conditioning and a fan at your disposal. You can shorten the time while watching TV, however, by and large, Greek and English-speaking channels are broadcast here. There is a small safe with an electronic lock, as well as a telephone with the ability to dial directly.

The bathroom is spacious enough - you can relax in the foam bath or cool off in the shower. There is a toilet, washbasin and wall mirror. Guests are provided with hygiene products, as well as shampoo, soap and some other cosmetics. Of course, you will be provided with clean towels that will be changed daily during cleaning.

Can I count on food at the hotel?





Nutrition is an important point on which the quality of rest depends on a lot. What can guests of the Nautilus Barbati 3 * hotel count on? Corfu is a place where hotels primarily provide a BB scheme, which includes complimentary breakfasts.

Meals are organized at the local restaurant. Here you will find a calm atmosphere, stylish design, calm atmosphere and, of course, delicious, freshly prepared dishes. The reviews confirm that the menu is quite diverse - no one leaves the hungry restaurant. Beach holidays: distance, amenities and entertainment





As already mentioned, the hotel complex Nautilus Barbati 3 * is located on a hillside. Here practically in the rocks there are wooden terraces. They have comfortable sun loungers, and there are umbrellas under which you can hide from the sun. By the way, everyone has enough sun loungers even with the maximum occupancy of the hotel. You can go downstairs to the water and swim, although the depth here is quite large and is more likely for experienced swimmers.

In about 15 minutes you can walk to the wonderful, wide Barbati Beach. This is a private stretch of the coast, so there are always enough tourists here. For a small fee, you can rent sunbeds and umbrellas for the whole day. According to reviews, the entrance to the sea is convenient, and the bottom is completely cleared of stones. A rescue team is working on the shore.

Of course, here you can engage in water sports and enjoy a more active holiday. Travelers have the opportunity to ride a boat or water ski, make a short trip on a boat or yacht, go windsurfing or sailing, swim with a mask, or take part in one of the beach parties. In short, on the beach you can have fun from the heart.
