3 * Hotels: Apk Resort & Spa (Thailand / Phuket). Photos and reviews of tourists

Thailand is by far one of the most popular resort destinations in Asia. Warm sea, exotic fruits, unconventional customs ... Everyone comes here to find something of their own or to see the world in all its diversity. On about. Phuket has many different hotels with 3 stars. Apk Resort & Spa is a good option for a budget holiday. For those who came to the country primarily for their impressions, this is a great option.





Description

Apk Resort & Spa (Phuket) - is a solid "C grade". Let there be no such luxury as in five-star hotels, but the rooms will surprise you with their neatness, cleanliness and accuracy. The hotel is located in the very center of the city, while the sea is within walking distance.

Evening life on about. Phuket is not subsiding. As soon as the sun sets over the horizon, a noisy party begins in the city. Someone wants to join this celebration of life, someone prefers to hide in a hotel. In any case, settling in the 3 * Apk Resort & Spa hotel, you can realize any of your dreams.





Where is

The location of the hotel is very comfortable. It is located on the outskirts of the city, but very close to Patong beach, which is quite convenient for vacationers. Across the road is a huge shopping center. There you can find not only souvenirs and gifts, but also everyday goods. In the evenings, traders of various foods are located near the shopping center. You can buy yourself something delicious for dinner for little money.

There are a lot of different discos and night bars around. Life in the evening is very lively.

Go to the beach for 10-15 minutes unhurried step. All vacationers noted that the location of the hotel is very good, even despite the fact that it is not on the first line.

Number of rooms

In total, the 3 * Apk Resort & Spa hotel has 99 rooms, which are scattered in two buildings. The first building presents the following options:

- Superior Room - a spacious room in which, if desired, there can be one double bed or two, but smaller ones. There is a balcony with beautiful mountain views.

- Deluxe Room - has a slightly larger area, but also has a balcony. Almost all rooms of this type overlook the mountains.

- Executive Room - a large spacious room with a lounge and a living room. It has a balcony-terrace and a jacuzzi. In the evenings it is pleasant to watch the sunset behind the mountains, sitting in a comfortable chair on the balcony.



In building No. 2, the rooms are almost the same, but with some changes:

- Standard Room - the simplest and most common room. There is no balcony in it, and all the windows face the neighboring building.

- Deluxe Room. In this building, not all rooms of this category have a balcony. But at the same time they are good, spacious and comfortable.

- Superior Room. Family type rooms. The layout is considered improved, the balcony overlooks the mountains.

- Mini Suite - suites. Equipped with new furniture and appliances, very large. There is a huge balcony and jacuzzi.

The rooms are equipped as standard: air conditioning, refrigerator, telephone, cable (where there are even two Russian channels), hairdryer. Depending on the type of room, there may be a French balcony with a beautiful view. The cheapest rooms face the wall of a neighboring hotel. But as tourists say, if you ask, you can change the number.

Logging in using an electronic card. An unusual addition in some rooms is the air conditioning system, which is individually regulated.

There is a minibar with tea and coffee. Vacationers note that the hotel has excellent soundproofing. When construction work was going on nearby, no noise bothered the guests. And the music from neighboring discos also does not interfere. And this is a very good plus for the hotel.

Hotel facilities

The hotel Apk Resort & Spa 3 * (Phuket, Patong) has two pools. One is on the roof, the other is on the ground floor of one of the buildings. The roof terrace is very beautiful. If you come here in the evening, you will see an unusual view of the sunset. Tourists like to relax near the pools. It is nice and comfortable here.

Paid laundry facilities are available on site. Due to the high humidity, linen on the balcony dries for a long time, so tourists recommend using the service.

Free internet with Wi-Fi access is on the ground floor. The speed is good, so you can sit on social networks calmly.

Nutrition

In its basic concept, the hotel offers breakfast only. Lunch and dinner are paid separately. But you don‚Äôt have to eat right here, around it is full of other options, and you should get acquainted with the local cuisine. Consider the nutrition in Apk Resort and Spa 3 * (Phuket) reviews.

At first, almost all tourists expect mid-range dining from a three-star hotel. But the hotel in this regard surprises its guests. Breakfast is very hearty and plentiful. Meal time is from 7 to 10 in the morning. No matter what time you come, full tables of all kinds of goodies will always be waiting for you.

Portions here do not give out plates, as happened in other hotels. Buffet system. Come and choose what you like. A lot of meat, side dishes, there are omelets and salads. Fruits are always present in large quantities.

To attract guests for dinner, the hotel thought out a good system. If you order one serving of hot, you can take soup, salad, vegetables, fruits and dessert for free in addition. And do it until you are full. The prices are very reasonable.

Another interesting observation of tourists is that the restaurant always plays live music.





Room Reviews

Impressions about the rest in Apk Resort & Spa 3 * (Thailand, Phuket, Patong) among tourists are only positive. The rooms are all working: appliances, cable, communication. There were even no complaints about the hairdryer, as is usually the case. Very clean and comfortable. The furniture is not old, but not new. Superior rooms even have a certain style. By the way, the rooms are decorated in the French style.

Cleaning is done every day. Towels are changed daily and bed linen every three days. But if you ask, they will do it more often. A beach towel can be taken at the reception by appointment. Return in the evening. The rooms have safes.

No cockroaches, bugs, or other living creatures were noticed by the guests. Sound insulation, as already written, is pretty good.





Beach Reviews

Apk Resort & Spa 3 * does not have its own beach. Closest to Patong. This is a place of urban relaxation, and therefore it is always crowded. The sea is dirty and does not cause much sympathy among many tourists. You can swim and even need to, but there are other options in the area.

So, guests from Thailand from Russia note that they often went to Paradise beach. It can be reached by transport, the road will take about 10 minutes. But it is much cleaner, while for some reason there are much less Russians there.

Very beautiful beaches on the islands. If you want to see the country and its rich nature, feel free to go on a trip. By the way, the hotel offers guide services, but they all do not speak Russian. Tourists are advised not to buy tours from locals, but rather order online through those who understand your language.

Objective opinion

When you read reviews about the hotel Apk Resort & Spa 3 * (Thailand), there are both positive and negative opinions. Someone was very lucky, and everything went perfectly, someone counted on more, but his expectations were far from reality, someone objectively perceived a three-star service. In general, the opinions of people were divided. In this regard, it is worth creating your own objective impression, which can be created only by visiting the hotel personally.

But while you save money and pack your bags, we will help you.

So. Why do people go to Thailand? First of all, for new exotic experiences. If you are going to spend your vacation lying on a sun lounger near the pool, and animators should dance around you around the clock, then you are not here. Your path is Egypt or Turkey.

Thailand needs to be felt. Everyone who wants to come here must definitely get acquainted with an extraordinary culture and bewitching tropical forests. You can‚Äôt spend the whole day in a hotel, on the contrary, you need to come there only to spend the night.

So for the overnight stay and hearty breakfast of tourists visiting Thailand, the Apk Resort & Spa 3 * hotel is the best suited. Comfortable rooms, comfortable clean bed, TV, evening dinner on the balcony with a beautiful view of the hills - what else does a tired tourist need for a day?

If you want to get an impression of the country, see as much as possible, choose this three-star hotel. This is the ideal ratio of quality service and low cost.





The main advantages of the hotel 3 * Apk Resort & Spa

1. Good location. Indeed, the hotel is perfectly placed. There is very little to go to the sea, and nearby are shopping centers, a market and entertainment complexes.

2. Comfortable rooms. This criterion is always the main one when choosing a tour. Therefore, we can safely say that in this hotel you can always count on high-quality daily cleaning, clean towels and a soft bed.

3. Nutrition. Although the hotel does not offer All Inclusive, the food is good enough. By paying inexpensively, you will surely remain happy and well-fed.

4. Lovely rooftop pool with amazing scenery. Relax after an active day will help its successful completion. See how convenient Apk Resort & Spa 3 * is in this regard. Hotel photos confirm the veracity of these words.





Conclusion

It is not always necessary to strive for luxury. Hotel Apk Resort & Spa 3 * in Thailand is an excellent choice for a budget tour. It has everything to feel comfortable and safe. At any time, you can have a hearty meal, take a walk around the city or visit a guided tour. Enjoy the country and let only positive emotions from rest remain.
