Hotel Acacia by Bin Majid Hotels Resorts 4 * (United Arab Emirates): what does the territory look like?

This hotel is a large multi-story building, built in an interesting style. In fact, the hotel is a model of modernity. Here you will find a pleasant atmosphere, stylish design and new equipment. The complex has a very small territory. The courtyard has a large pool and terrace, and there is ample free parking next to the building.

Hotel Acacia by Bin Majid Hotels Resorts 4 *: photo and description of rooms





The hotel provides its guests with everything that may be needed for a relaxing holiday, not associated with various problems. On its territory there are 374 rooms. What categories of rooms are there in the Acacia Hotel by Bin Majid Hotels Resorts 4 *?

  • standard rooms;
  • one bedroom suite;
  • two bedrooms suite;
  • junior suites;

Regardless of the room you got, you can count on a spacious, bright room with large windows, modern and stylish furnishings. Some rooms have views of the hotel's courtyard, while others have views of the surroundings and the sea. Balconies are not available in all rooms.

Of course, the hotel provides guests with all the necessary household appliances. Pleasant clean spacious bathroom with modern high-quality fixtures. Here you can always take a shower. The hotel provides clean towels as well as toiletries including shampoos, soaps, gels and lotions.

What kind of food do tourists offer?





When paying for accommodation at the Acacia Hotel by Bin Majid Hotels Resort, tourists themselves are free to choose a convenient food system. For example, you can only pay for breakfast, two meals a day, the all-inclusive scheme, or even refuse food. In any case, even if you did not pay for food, you can always have a bite to eat at a local restaurant or cafe, and for a relatively small amount.

You can have a good time in the main hotel restaurant Al Nakhla. In the morning, they offer traditional continental breakfasts. For lunch and dinner, the menu is more varied - guests can enjoy the exquisite taste of dishes of international, oriental and Mediterranean cuisine. By the way, there is an open-air terrace with a wonderful view of the surroundings and the territory of the hotel itself.

There is a bar Flamingo, which will delight you with a stylish atmosphere, delicious drinks and a huge selection of cigars. By the way, this establishment works almost until night. On the ground floor there is a small cozy cafe where they offer only the best varieties of aromatic coffee and, of course, delicious oriental pastries.

Naturally, you can eat outside the hotel. For example, across the road there is a small restaurant where they cook deliciously and speak Russian.

Where is the beach?





As already mentioned, Acacia Hotel by Bin Majid Hotels Resorts is located near the seashore. The beach is just 300 meters from the hotel building - you can walk here in a few minutes. The coast, as well as the entry into the sea, is covered with sand (it is cleaned regularly). On the other hand, to get special beach shoes still does not hurt.

On the shore there are sunbeds with soft mattresses, sun umbrellas, a rental point for clean beach towels. Sunset at sea is gradual, convenient, there is a backwater where kids like to swim. Of course, tourists can engage in more active types of recreation, such as boating, boat or yacht.

Leisure: how do hotel guests spend their time?

Despite the fact that Acacia Hotel by Bin Majid Hotels Resort is a city hotel, you can also have a good time on its territory. For example, in the courtyard there is a large swimming pool with a heating system. Sun loungers are located on the terrace - here you can sunbathe, relax and just chat with other guests.





The hotel offers a visit to the Ozone spa, where customers are offered to relax in the jacuzzi, undergo a course of therapeutic and wellness massage, sign up for various anti-aging treatments for face and body. There is also a beauty salon where extremely talented stylists and cosmetologists work.

Oxygen Gym gym is constantly working with a huge amount of modern equipment, including many cardiovascular equipment. There is also a sauna where you can warm up your muscles before training and relax after it.

There is a cigar room where men like to relax for a pleasant conversation. Guests can visit a night club or sing in the karaoke bar. There is no animation here, but dances and other recreational activities are often arranged in the evenings. The hotel has a tour agency. Here you will learn about the most popular and interesting places, they will help you make the best route for an independent trip or purchase a ready-made tour.
