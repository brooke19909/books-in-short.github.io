Title

There are many rumors about the appearance of such an unusual name. Rumor has it that after the locals of this okrug and villages fled and survived after the Tatar attacks and raids, they laid the first bricks of the city. Once a carriage with Catherine II herself drove past the village, and she accidentally dropped her necklace. After which the town was named Ozherelyevo.

In fact, everything is much simpler, the settlement received its sonorous name thanks to the name of the first head of Ozherelyev. So the city was called the Necklace of the Moscow region. The transition from one word to another is very likely due to the reduction of sounds, which very often happens in spoken Russian.
