Sizing

Asking the question of how to tie the tracks as slippers, you first need to decide on the size of them. How to do it right? There is a very simple way that our grandmothers used:

  • Stand with one foot on a sheet of A4 paper.
  • Circle the outline of your foot with a pen or pencil.
  • Now you need to measure the distance between the most protruding points from the bottom and top of the foot in the resulting figure with a centimeter ruler. Usually it is on the second or first finger (each person has a different way).
  • After measurements, we determine the size. Usually the 36th corresponds to a foot length of about 23 cm. Sizes 37-38 correspond to a mark of 24 cm. And the 40th will be equal to 25 centimeters.

How to knit slippers, knitting needles

For beginners it will not be difficult to make mini-socks for adults with their own hands, which will warm not only the legs, but also the soul. After all, everyone knows that a thing made by oneself is much nicer to wear and give to loved ones.

So, let's look at how to make short homemade socks. The threads for this product can be of any color and from a suitable material (which yarn is most suitable, described above). In this workshop, you will learn how to tie tracks as slippers for sizes 38-39. If you or the person for whom you are knitting has a larger or smaller size, simply increase the number of loops (+1 size = +2 loops, and -1 size = - 2 loops). In order to knit slippers, you need about 100 grams of yarn.





So:

  • We collect 56 loops on the needles.
  • The first row: we knit 27 facial loops, after which we make a crochet (extra loop). To do this, simply thread the needle. Then we knit 2 front loops, again we crochet and we knit 27 loops. Do not forget that the first loop in the row is not knitted, but simply removed on the knitting needle, and the last is always knitted as the wrong one. This rule must be observed when knitting any thing.
  • Second row: we knit all the loops wrong.
  • Third row: we knit 28 front loops, then we make 2 crochets, we knit 2 front loops and we crochet again, then we again knit 28 loops.
  • Fourth row: we knit all the loops as purl.

In this order, tie another 10 rows. In the center of the front row, do not forget to make increases (double crochet, then 2 facial and double crochet). 15 row tie without increments. 16 row contains only the wrong loops. 17 row tie to the seventh loop of the front, not reaching the middle.

Getting started knitting the soles of our track

On an extra knitting needle, dial 14 loops and knit as follows: remove 1 loop (we already know that the first loop in a row is always removed and does not knit), then knit 12 facial loops, 1 loop from the sidewall and 1 central knit together.





Now flip your knitting to the other side. Gradually collecting from the sidewalls of the loop, continue to knit. There must be 14 stitches on your knitting needles. Tie them in a 16 row pattern (described above). Tie (sew) the footprints to the sidewalls.

2 way

We present to your attention one more master class explaining how to knit slippers-footprints with knitting needles, for beginners. It is lightweight and easy to understand. Handkerchief is the main pattern in this description. Its essence lies in the fact that all rows are knitted with facial loops. Knitting begins with the heel of the product. For slippers of size 39, dial 44 loops and tie the desired number of rows to close the heel.



After that we tie the foot of the product, gradually reducing the loops on both sides closer to the toe. Next, grab the loop around the edge of the slipper and begin to knit its sides to the desired height. Now the canvas can be closed by knitting and removing loops from the knitting needles.
