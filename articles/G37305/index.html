Arkhyz, Lake of Love. Description, photos and reviews

In the world there are a huge number of incredibly beautiful corners of nature, among which is this fabulous place, located among the high mountains.

One of the most romantic places in nature is an amazing lake, hidden high in the mountains. This very small natural reservoir with a heart shape is one of the best attractions of Arkhyz. Lake of Love is its name. The turquoise and clear water sparkling in the sun symbolizes the depth and purity of this most tender feeling.



general information

To admire this beautiful lake, tourists have to go a long way, but it is worth it (below is information about where Lake of Love is located).

In Karachai, the name of the lake sounds like Suuk-Dzhurek, which translates as "cold heart." This is possibly due to the fact that the water in it is quite cool. On the map, this lake is not indicated at all, because its size is very small, and it is often called Bezymyanny.

People come here, making a considerable and difficult journey, not only tourists - nature lovers, but also couples in love for a romantic date. These places are especially beautiful in the spring and summer, when the blue lake is surrounded by picturesque landscapes of bright greenery. Flowing into the lake in thin streams of water from high mountain glaciers. Trout splashes in the waters of this beautiful reservoir.

Where is the lake of love?

This small pond is located on the slope of the Morg-Syrty ridge (2500 meters above sea level). This place belongs to the Zelenchuksky district of the Karachay-Cherkess Republic and is located near the village of Arkhyz. This area is located in the mountains of the Western Caucasus.



This fabulous place belongs to the Teberdinsky reserve. The lake is a resting zone of a protected natural site, so passage there is officially prohibited.

Routes to the lake

Unofficially (with the permission of the forester), some make their way to Lake Love (Arkhyz) on a walking route. Reviews about the trip for lovers of romantic travel are the most enthusiastic. Of course, this road is not easy, because you need to climb approximately to a height of 2500 meters. The village of Arkhyz itself is at a level of 1400 meters, which means that the entire trip from this point is more than a kilometer in height.

It should be noted that guides before the trip advise holding a pinch of salt behind the cheek so that the ascent is easier to carry. This helps get rid of thirst, but water is still needed along the way.

The most popular trip to the Lake of Love (Arkhyz) is horseback riding. The equestrian route is easier on foot, and the romance of such a walk does not leave indifferent even the most inveterate skeptics. In total, the tour takes about 8-9 hours. During the whole journey you can enjoy the picturesque views of the majestic mountains, flowering colorful meadows. The contrast of snow and greenery, the singing of birds and the flutter of multi-colored butterflies are striking in the campaign.

There is a good sign - when you come to the lake, you should definitely give him a coin. This will help maintain and strengthen love. According to other signs, lovers need to drink water from the lake or swim in it.



About the nature of the surroundings

The mountains of Arkhyz are majestic and fabulously beautiful. Lake of Love and other magnificent natural attractions attract tourists here - lovers of a romantic form of relaxation. There are a lot of places for outdoor recreation.

The journey begins to the lake from the village of Arkhyz. The road first goes along the gorge of the river. Kizgich. The first 3 kilometers of the path is a convenient forest trail, then there is a long climb to the pass, called the Arkhyz saddle (2000 m). A wide trail goes through a dense forest, covered with centuries-old firs, the shadow of which slightly facilitates the path. After the forest, subalpine meadows appear, from where the village is visible below. Arkhyz also offers a view of Abishir-Akhub (ridge) and Morg-Syrty, in which the lake is located. Ahead is Mount Red. Half an hour from this place is a picturesque natural reservoir, similar in shape to a heart. The lake is located at an altitude of 2347 meters.



Conclusion

According to the reviews of travelers who visited these places, there is practically nothing to see in Arkhyz itself. In this village there are some services for those who take independent walks in the local mountains. It offers hiking and horseback riding, trips to the reserve zone, river rafting, etc. It turns out that the most remarkable and beautiful place in Arkhyz is Lake of Love.
