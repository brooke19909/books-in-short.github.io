"Aquamarine", 2 * boarding house (Sochi, Hosta): review, description and reviews

Hosta is a unique Black Sea resort, very popular with domestic vacationers. Due to its location, the average annual air temperature in this town is even 1 degree higher than in Sochi. In fact, Khosta is the warmest resort of the Russian Federation. Of course, the tourist infrastructure in this village is developed just superbly. There is also a huge number of hotels here. For example, the Aquamarine boarding house offers its guests a good service.

Location Features

This hotel is located in close proximity to the hosta beaches. To get to the coastline from its territory, tourists have no more than 3 minutes. Khosta Train Station is a 15-minute walk from the hotel. Of course, this is pretty far. With luggage for tourists arriving by train to the city, going to the hotel will most likely not be very convenient. Therefore, in most cases, vacationers who have booked rooms in this complex get here by taxi, which you can hire directly in the square in front of the station.





A good location in relation to the beaches is, therefore, what distinguishes "Aquamarine", a 2 * pension. Sochi, Adler, Hosta reviews of tourists regarding the development of infrastructure have earned very good. But housing in all these resorts, of course, should be chosen as close to the sea as possible. The disadvantages of the location of the boarding house "Aquamarine", many tourists include the proximity of railways. Because of this, the rooms of the complex are often noisy.

200 meters from the center "Aquamarine" is the medical complex "Matsesta". If desired, the guests of the boarding house can attend the procedures of balneotherapy and physiotherapy (a spa card is required).

general description
