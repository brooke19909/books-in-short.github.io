What is forex trading? How to play in the Forex market

With the development of technological progress, many people found it convenient to make money remotely by working at home in front of their personal computer. Today you can start earning even without special knowledge or big money capital.

The popular Forex exchange provides anyone who wants to start trading with just a few dollars in their personal account.

Forex: what and how it works

Different world organizations constantly need to change the currency for international operations. In this they are helped by the Forex market, which is rightfully the largest of the existing world markets. On its sites, more money is exchanged daily than on the markets of the whole world combined. Forex daily turnover of more than five trillion US dollars.

The name of the Forex exchange comes from the expression "forex exchange", which means "foreign exchange". Money is traded on this site at free quotes. The price of the world currency is constantly changing, and traders timely sell and buy it. This whole process is the Forex market.

Forex trading takes place only in online mode, since this market is completely virtual.

Forex Appearance

Forex appeared as an international platform in 1971, and has been expanding rapidly since then. In 1977, daily transactions at the amount of 5,000,000 US dollars were made at Forex. According to experts, in 2020 the market will gain a daily turnover of 10 trillion.

Traders from all over the world today participate in Forex trading, their number is constantly growing.

Forex Members

The main participants are, of course, banks. It was created at one time precisely for interbank trade. Over time, the list of participants has changed and now they include:

  • central and commercial banks;
  • investment companies;
  • traders
  • Insurance companies;
  • pension funds;
  • international companies involved in import and export;
  • dealers and brokers.

All participants can participate in the Forex market actively or passively.

Active participants are those who influence pricing. These are banks and brokers.

Passive participants - those who conclude deals at the prices offered by active participants. These are investment funds, companies related to import and export, as well as private investors who work through dealing centers.

Forex Market Relations

Since Forex works continuously, everyone can join its work at any time of the day or night. On this site, market trade relations are regulated, both between large companies and between states. This happens precisely due to the trading participants.

Understanding the principles of Forex is not difficult. However, in order to understand all the nuances and versatility of this trading system, to learn how to fully use the financial instruments of the site, it will take years. The guarantee of successful Forex earnings is only a clear and clear understanding of all the processes taking place on the site.

Difference between Forex and Stock Exchange

Many people know what stock trading means and what the stock exchange is like. Cryptocurrency trading is somewhat different from buying / selling shares, but not everyone understands what Forex is.

Stocks on the stock exchange are constantly monitored. On Forex, they mostly trade the most popular currencies. You can, of course, deal with rare, more exotic currencies. But the largest bets are placed on the world's leading currencies. These are Euro, Dollar (USD), British Pound (GBP), Japanese Yen (JPY), Australian Dollar (AUD), New Zealand (NZD) and Canadian Dollar (CAD), Swiss Franc (CHD).

Unlike Forex, on a stock exchange you can conduct transactions at a certain time only on a specific exchange (such as Nyse), and the most inconvenient - only within a few hours a day. You can trade Forex currency in real time around the clock. At the same time, there is no need to even think about on which exchange each lot is traded - the Forex system itself selects the most profitable sites and in this sense, trading in this market is very convenient and comfortable.

Since Forex uses loans when concluding trade transactions, having invested even a small amount, for example $ 100, a trader can count on the purchase of specific currency pairs in an amount up to 2000 units. This is the principle of margin trading.

In practice, this is expressed by the fact that if the price of the selected pair changes by only 0.0001, the result can be a very substantial profit from the investments made. At the same time, the purchase / sale is very fast, and the volumes are incredibly large.

Forex Exchange receives its commission from all transactions. It makes up the difference between sales and purchase prices. Naturally, the purchase price is always at least slightly higher.

Thus, the Forex exchange does not own physical shares or other documents accompanying currency trading. The system maintains the buy / sell status of each trader and receives a reward for this.

Unlike the stock market, Forex is not managed by participants.

Forex Basics for Beginners

Forex is one of the pillars of the movement of financial investments between states in world trade. With his participation, the international process of buying / selling (exchanging) currencies is much simpler, and this mainly attracts large banks. Various companies constantly pay import to other countries with the currency of these states. For such an operation, it is required to convert (convert) the national currency to the required one.

Individuals working at Forex at their own risk and risk only with their own money can only participate in transactions made by large participants. However, their personal contribution is not so significant. That is, the market provides an opportunity for those who understand what Forex is to trade currency at the price that is currently the most profitable.

When a private trader sells a currency, such as an American dollar, then it will be exchanged for another, such as the Japanese yen. The components of this procedure are called "pair". So, GBPJPY is a pair in which British pounds are traded in exchange for Japanese yen. Since Forex uses credit instruments, with this trade a trader sells or buys this pair at a price equivalent to 1.5000 per GBPJPY (pair).

That is, the similarity of Forex with the stock exchange is that there is trading in shares, and here in pairs.

Forex Concepts and Tools

  • The base currency is the currency in which the trader is interested, and he usually trades with it. For example, in the pair USDEUR - USD is the base, and EUR is the quote ("counter-currency") currency.
  • Leverage (or leverage) is a loan from a broker, thanks to which you can trade funds that exceed the balance by hundreds of times. For example, having only $ 100, you can buy 10,000 EURUSD, using the necessary leverage.
  • Indicators are special tools with which technical analysis is carried out. Forex indicators are: trend, oscillators and auxiliary.
  • Lot - the so-called unit of trade. Distinguish: Standard lot of 1,000,000 points; Mini lot of 10,000 points; Micro lots at 1000 pips.
  • Open positions are orders that are not yet closed.
  • Point or Pip (mark) - the minimum price applicable to the quote of a currency pair. Most currencies have a pip equal to 0.0001.
  • Selling price (bid) - the price at which a pair can be sold.
  • Short position - a parameter meaning that the order is placed in the hope that the price of the currency pair will fall.
  • Margin is the difference between the sale price and the purchase price.
  • Stop-loss is an undesirable price in its direction, upon reaching which the order will be automatically closed to avoid further losses.
  • Spread is one of the important concepts. This is the measured in points difference between the sale and purchase price of a pair over a period of time. Spreads are fixed - constant; fixed with the possibility of expansion - under certain conditions it can expand; floating - a variable value depending on the state of the market.
  • SWAP is the transition of an open trading position to the next trading day (day). It can be both positive and negative. There are trading accounts that are not charged with swaps. Sometimes there are days on which trading companies set up triple swaps. This needs to be known in advance on the broker's websites with which the trader works. Understanding the meaning of SWAPs, you can trade using the so-called SWAP Forex strategies. With this trade, orders are opened and kept open for days, months, and even years. Of course, for this you should choose currency pairs that have the most favorable positive results.
