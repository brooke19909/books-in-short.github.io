Responsibilities of holidaymakers

Before talking about places for barbecue in Moscow, I would like to recall the basic rules. First, you must comply with established rules of conduct, and secondly, fire safety rules.

  • Drinking alcohol is strictly prohibited.
  • It is forbidden to make a fire outside specially equipped areas.
  • Illegal felling of trees and their use as firewood is punishable by a fine.
  • The brazier can be installed at a height of at least 30 cm from the ground so as not to damage the roots of plants.
  • Open fire is prohibited; charcoal can only be used.
  • All garbage after it is necessary to clean.

All places for barbecue in Moscow are regularly supervised by special services. Violation of these rules leads to administrative liability and fines in the amount of 5,000 to 300,000 rubles.
