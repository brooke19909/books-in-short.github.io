Melia Sharm Hotel 5 *: reviews, photos

Surely everyone knows that Egypt is today one of the most popular resort countries in the world. In particular, the city of Sharm el-Sheikh enjoys special demand among tourists. This is where the luxury hotel called Melia Sharm Hotel is located. So what does this place look like? What living conditions should I expect? What reviews do former guests leave? This information will be of interest to many travelers.

Location Information





The spacious Melia Sharm Hotel 5 * hotel complex is located almost on the very shore of the Red Sea, namely on the picturesque Sinai Peninsula, in the territory of the famous resort of Sharm El Sheikh. Just a few steps away are the calm waters of Montazah Ras Nasrany Bay, where guests can enjoy all kinds of water activities.

By the way, the Sharm el-Sheikh International Airport is located only 7 kilometers from the hotel complex - you will get there in 10-15 minutes, which is very convenient. About the same distance is Naama Bay, a well-known tourist destination.

Hotel complex Melia Sharm Hotel 5 *: photo and description of rooms





Immediately I must say that, regardless of category, the hotel will be waiting for you in a clean comfortable room with a view of the sea or the surrounding area. Each room has a balcony or a private terrace with wicker furniture, where you can relax, enjoying the bright colors of nature and clean sea air.

The rooms are stylishly furnished, the furniture is new and comfortable. Of course, guests are entitled to a set of necessary household appliances, without which it is difficult to imagine a truly comfortable pastime. You can regulate the temperature in the room using the air conditioner. You can also relax while watching satellite channels, as the room has a TV. Guests can use the safe for free. There is also a minibar with a refrigerator, which is regularly filled with drinking and soda water - all other drinks are delivered upon request of the client, they are not included in the price.

Of course, the room comes with a toilet and a bathroom, where you can take a shower at any time of the day. Here you will find a large cosmetic mirror, a powerful stationary hair dryer for drying hair, as well as a set of towels and a set of hygiene products.

The rooms are cleaned every day, bed linen is changed on request. Towels are changed after cleaning.

Meal plan

As is customary in tourist Egypt, this hotel operates on an all-inclusive basis - guests are provided with good nutrition. Three times a day, guests are gathered in the main restaurant of the hotel. Incidentally, it is located very conveniently, you can easily and quickly get here from anywhere in the complex, even if you are not settled in the building near the dining room in the Melia Sharm Hotel. There is a sufficient number of tables, even considering periods of maximum occupancy. By the way, the restaurant has an outdoor terrace where you can eat while enjoying breathtaking views of the Red Sea. According to reviews, the menu here is quite diverse.





Naturally, you can visit other institutions. For example, the Melia Sharm Hotel invites its guests to an Italian restaurant √† la carte Aqua. Tourists also like the O-Grill restaurant, located by the pool, which offers a huge selection of grilled dishes. In addition, during the day you can get chilled drinks, coffee, tea, light snacks and alcoholic drinks in one of the seven hotel bars. By the way, there is a disco bar, which works at night, as well as a cigar room.

Beach and water activities for tourists

As already mentioned, the territory of the Melia Sharm Hotel hotel complex goes directly to the picturesque little bay. The hotel owns a private section of the beach with sun loungers and wicker straw umbrellas. There is a long pier, as well as a beach bar.





Near the coast there is a lively coral reef, which can be considered a local attraction. On the other hand, this means that you can enter the sea only in special shoes.

There is an opportunity to engage in more active types of recreation. This coast is simply designed for scuba diving, as the underwater life here is rich, colorful and vibrant. The hotel has a diving center where you can rent the necessary equipment. In addition, you can take a training course.

Also, guests have the opportunity to rent a boat or a catamaran, ride a boat, water ski or do other water sports.

Is it possible to come to the hotel with a child?





Since today many parents travel with their children, they are interested in the availability of appropriate amenities on the territory of a hotel. So what can the Melia Sharm Hotel 5 * offer? Guest reviews indicate that this place is well adapted for living with a child.

In particular, in the courtyard there is a special children's pool where children can splash and sunbathe. There is also a well-equipped playground with slides, sandpits, various swings. In addition, during the day, children are entertained by funny animators who organize various competitions and team events.

Additional services offered by the hotel

Of course, the Melia Sharm Hotel is a great place to relax, not overshadowed by domestic and organizational problems. To begin with, it is worth noting that there is a currency exchange, so the likelihood of problems with money is minimized. The hotel also has a laundry and dry cleaning service, which you can use for a moderate fee.

Here you can rent a car and some other means of transport, which is convenient for tourists who prefer to explore the territory on their own. Here you can order a taxi or organize a trip in a limousine.

And the hotel has a medical office. Doctor's services are paid separately, but a specialist will help you in case of health problems.

Entertainment and leisure guests





You are unlikely to be bored at this hotel. To begin with, it is worth saying that in the courtyard there are four swimming pools, as well as a large terrace for sunbathing. There are plenty of sun loungers and parasols in place - you can swim, relax and just have fun having a pleasant conversation.

Those who wish can visit the gym, which is equipped with new, modern equipment. If you wish, you can conduct several classes with a professional instructor who will select the most effective load for you. Tourists can visit the sauna and jacuzzi, as well as the Turkish bath. There is also a good beauty salon where professional hairdressers and cosmetologists will offer their services.

The hotel also has tennis courts, next to which you can rent balls and rackets. There is also a billiard room. Do not forget that the hotel has a team of talented animators who entertain guests throughout the day and arrange interesting performances in the evenings.

What else can the Melia Sharm Hotel 5 * complex offer? Sharm El Sheikh is a large tourist area with a lot of attractions. Here you can easily find suitable excursions and turn your vacation into a real adventure in the land of the pharaohs.
