Panasonic: manufacturing country, product overview, reviews

Every company that becomes known as a major manufacturer of equipment goes a long way. Sometimes corporations suffer from a lack of customers, sometimes they experience incredible success. A huge contribution is made by the founder, who, together with his brainchild, experiences all the ups and downs.

Konosuke Matsushite had to overcome a difficult path . This is the founder of Panasonic, whose production country is Japan. In 1918, he decided to create a corporation that would be engaged in the development and production of equipment.

Founder

But any story of the company begins with its founder. Panasonic, whose manufacturing country is Japan, was created, as already noted, in 1918. Then Matsushita turned 24 years old. It is unlikely that the young man assumed that in the future his brainchild would turn into a corporation known for its technology.

Konosuke was born in 1894. His family was not poor, as they owned small property. Matsushita had seven brothers and sisters. But at some point, the well-being of the family was shaken, and at 9 years old the boy had to go to work. Because of this, he was deprived of a good education.

One of Konosuke's first works was a service at a bicycle store. Even then, the father predicted that his son would soon be able to hire people with education. But while Matsushita was fond of electronics and various inventions. Working in one of the leading companies at that time, he dreamed of starting his own business. So there was a small company, "Matsushita Danki." Then no one knew about Panasonic, whose country of manufacture was Konosuke's homeland.

About company

At the moment, Panasonic Corporation is a large corporation originally from Japan, which is engaged in the production of household appliances and electronic goods. The headquarters of the company is located in Osaka. In 2011, it was one of the 50 most successful corporations in the world.

Few people know, but the company got its current name only 10 years ago. Until that moment, she was named after her founder, Matsushita Electric Industrial Co., Ltd. But Panasonic is the brand name of the company.
