Landscapes of the Gulf of Finland

Forests and crystal clear water in the bay, fresh air will not leave anyone indifferent. In winter, you can rent a snowmobile, and in summer a quad bike, boat or catamaran. In winter, slush and mud in the cities, and chic drifts outside the city. Rest on the Gulf of Finland is interesting at any time of the year. Children (and not only) are happy to play snowballs and blind a snowman. In summer, they can splash around on the shore of the bay, since it is shallow.





The beaches of the Gulf of Finland are very attractive for families with small children. There is a gentle descent without drops. Gulf of Finland fishing





And what kind of fishing in the Gulf of Finland ... At any time of the day there is the opportunity to catch pike, bream, perch, perch, burbot, roach, smelt. Moreover, one of the best places for fishing is Shelter Bay, located on the island of West Birch. There is a very strong undercurrent. The rocky bottom with sharp changes, underwater rocky outcrops and rocky embankments help pikes to successfully hide from fishermen. There are not many people who want to go fishing because of the little known location. But the fish is not frightened and, with due dexterity and skill, is very well caught.

Ideal fishing from a boat to spinning. Pikes and zander are quite large, so it is advisable to take the rods strong and powerful, such as a three-meter throw "cartoon", an assembled power coil with a braid of at least 0.22 mm and strong carbines with a margin. Of the lures, jerkbaits, spinner baits are good.

Popular place

The most popular fishing spot is the strait between the island of Vortex and the Kiperort Peninsula. The rocky bottom with a pronounced topography, oxygen-enriched water make this place attractive for fishing. One of the minuses is too many fishermen.

There is also a great opportunity to hunt waterfowl. Gulf of Finland Housing





Along the entire Gulf of Finland there are many hotels, private houses, cottages in which you can stay. Moreover, prices vary depending on the presence or absence of amenities, the number of visitors, seasonality. Alternatively, you can take a tent, friends, family, guitar, meat and go to the shore of the bay, where no one is there. Arrange an unforgettable weekend with an overnight stay, morning fishing and an ear on a fire. And it will come out very inexpensively. If the means allow, or you are a truly urban resident who is used to relaxing with all the amenities, then you have a huge offer of cottages, mini-hotels. There, of course, the rest will come out a little more expensive, but you do not have to cook at the stake, feed the mosquitoes, and also all the beauty will be at your disposal. Rest on the Gulf of Finland will not leave anyone indifferent, and you will come back here again and again.
