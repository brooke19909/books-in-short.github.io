La Fleur Samsung GT-S5230: characteristics, instructions, description and reviews

More recently, touch displays seemed like a supernova, and no one could have thought that they would enter our daily lives so quickly. If previously only premium-class devices were equipped with them, now any, even budget, smartphone model has a touch screen. However, a similar approach was seen at one time with support for such an audio format as MP3. Why all this? Moreover, the Samsung La Fleur GT-S5230, the characteristics of which will be given in this review, refers to this particular type of devices.

Main technical parameters





The device works in second generation cellular networks. The diagonal of the screen is three inches. The device is equipped with one camera module, its resolution is 3.2 megapixels. The amount of internal memory for storing user information is 50 megabytes. There is the possibility of expansion through the use of external microSD drives up to 8 gigabytes. A lithium-ion battery with a capacity of 1000 milliampere-hours was used as a source of battery life. The smartphone is presented in a classic form factor.

Device History





We used to draw a line of demarcation between phones and smartphones just at the moment when the first devices were equipped with touch displays. However, this is not entirely accurate. The fact is that smartphones have their own operating systems with a wide range of distinctive functionalities. But the model we are talking about today is nothing more than a phone with a touch screen. At one time, LG released a device that literally undermined the inaccessibility of touch phones for a huge mass of buyers. It was a model KR500. Actually, for a South Korean company this has become a reference point. The creation of the Samsung La Fleur GT-S5230 phone in 2011, a description of which the reader can meet in today's review, was a symmetrical response to the LG promotion of the device under the name KR500.

Exterior





Phone Samsung La Fleur GT-S5230 can be called compact. It is this opinion that arises when you first familiarize yourself with the model. Most likely, this feeling is created due to the fact that the body is quite thin. People who decided to try using the device for a long time were surprised at how light it is. Most likely, many of them expected that the touch display will increase the size, but in reality everything turned out differently. There is no need to talk about management: it is convenient to use the phone with one hand and two.

Materials of manufacture





The manufacturing material for La Fleur Samsung GT-S5230 is mainly plastic. It is not very high-quality, fingerprinting the back or front panel will not be difficult. However, a fancy pattern is applied to the lid, and thanks to this, the prints are not striking. But here's the problem: what to do with the front panel? The pattern is not applied to it. And we have no choice but to endure or simply not pay attention to this lack of attention. Other few elements are made of metal. This, in particular, the insert under the display, as well as the framing of the front panel.

Controls





The La Fleur Samsung GT-S5230 not many controls. Among them, there are traditional keys. They are intended, as you might have guessed, to receive or reset a voice call, go back, take a picture with the camera active, and also lock the screen. Of course, it could not do without a paired key designed to adjust the volume or sound mode. These controls are located under the screen of the device, as well as on its side faces. The communication connector is located under the volume buttons.

Resolution and quality





La Fleur Samsung GT-S5230 Display Resolution is 240 by 400 pixels. The picture is displayed as WQVGA. For mid-range phones that are equipped with touchscreens, this resolution is becoming standard. Color reproduction is at a level, the shades look quite natural. Of course, the display is not the best of those that have competitors in the segment. The brightness is sufficient when working indoors, but the screen will definitely fade when switching to bright sunlight.
