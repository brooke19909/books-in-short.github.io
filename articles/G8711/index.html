Side Sungate Hotel 5 * (Turkey, Side): rating, description, reviews

Turkey is one of the tourist destinations beloved by Russians and residents of other countries. This is absolutely natural, because rest in the country involves:

  • absence of problems with visas - special entry documents for tourists for staying in the country up to 60 days are not required;
  • chic natural conditions for every taste - in the country and endless sandy beaches and mountains;
  • a convenient all-inclusive meal system with a wide range of dishes and drinks from local and other popular cuisines of the world;
  • developed hotel infrastructure, serious competition between them, which encourages staff to try to be better;
  • a wide range of souvenirs, leather and fur products on the shelves of markets and shops at affordable prices;
  • a wide range of prices with options for offers for tourists with different material conditions ;
  • the duration of the season from May to October inclusive;
  • A variety of interesting and affordable excursion programs.

The country is divided into provinces, each of which is characterized by its own characteristics of climate, cultural traditions, cuisine and color. One of the most visited provinces is Antalya, and the cities are Side.

General information about Side

The climate for this city is Mediterranean, with hot, dry summers and mild winters. Side is interesting from a historical point of view, because in the past it was often in danger, which influenced the curious fence of this place today. In the historical center you can see the amphitheater, which accommodated twenty thousand spectators and is considered the largest in the country. The beaches of the city are safe due to its shallow depth, which is ideal for families with children.

The western part of the city will appeal to those wishing to spend their holidays calmly and quietly. For lovers of nightlife, there is an assortment of nightclubs for every taste with theme parties and simply high-quality music and alcohol. With absolute certainty, we can say that Side is a universal resort city in which every tourist will find entertainment for everyone. One of the noteworthy vacationing hotels is Side Sungate Hotel 5 *.

General impressions of the hotel and rooms

Check-in takes place at 14:00. Tourists who have arrived before this time are sent to eat and explore the territory, which is important - they do not insist on a tip, as is often the case in Turkey. According to most tourists, although the area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe Side Sungate Hotel Spa 5 * is small, it‚Äôs cozy and has everything you need for a comfortable stay: pools, bars, a lawn with a well-kept lawn and much more. The rooms are clean and spacious, mostly with balconies, the views of the sea and the hotel are quite pleasant. Rooms with terraces have sun loungers, which is very convenient for an afternoon break with a view of the nature. Plumbing and appliances are in good condition, a minibar and regular stocks of coffee / tea are replenished. Some tourists complained about the carelessness of the presence of cosmetics in the bathroom and their late replenishment.

Internet quality and speed are satisfactory.

Guests note a pleasant neighborhood of Turks and Europeans in the hotel and a low percentage of Russian-speaking vacationers.





Dining at the hotel

Most vacationers were satisfied with the assortment of buffet dishes and their quality. It is important that the menu at the Side Sungate Hotel 5 * hotel (Turkey) is varied and new dishes are offered daily. A variety of meat, cheeses, desserts and cream soups delighted guests. Always offered fish, different types of potatoes, pasta and lots of fruits and vegetables. As the dishes are consumed, their supplies are replenished. The nutrition of the hotel has earned positive ratings for the availability of various options for how to feed children of different ages. Parents leave grateful feedback addressed to the cooks and administration. There are some minor remarks regarding the over-sharpness of the dishes, but they are not critical. A very important factor is the lack of queues for receiving dishes. But, as tourists say, everyone has enough food. Nobody will be hungry here.

Among the tourists who rested in the hotel during the peak season, there were complaints about the failed dinner in a la carte restaurants due to the maximum occupancy of the hotel and the lines.





Opinions about the sea and the beach

The hotel is located on the second coastline, so you need to walk 150 meters to the sea. Some families covered this distance in two minutes, others in eight to ten, slowly enjoying the promenade. Along the way there are a lot of plants forming a shadow, almost no traffic, the path is convenient. The beach of the hotel is sandy, clean (beachboy regularly removes garbage during the day), but small. Sun beds and umbrellas are always available, towels are provided. A very convenient service is plastic storage rooms on the coast, where you can put valuables and safely go to sea with a key on your bracelet.

There is no unequivocal opinion of the guests on the fullness of the beach: one had a lot of space, free sunbeds and few people even with 100% occupancy, others were crowded during periods when some of the rooms were empty. Due to its shallow depth and gentle entrance, the beach is considered ideal for children. The sea is clean and gentle, no complaints about the low water temperature were recorded even from tourists resting in the velvet season.





Hotel staff

Initially, the Side Sungate 5 * hotel is focused on European tourists, so Russian-speaking employees are rarely found on staff. But they all speak good English, so the language barrier can be overcome by knowing this language. The hotel staff are modest and unobtrusive, as noted above, do not insist on a tip. Service in bars and restaurants is highly rated by tourists. Turks treat children very warmly, according to tourists' reviews, after a request, the staff brought toys for the sandbox to children on the beach.

In general, the staff is attentive and helpful.





Entertainment

According to tourists, the day and evening animation of the hotel is of high quality. There are exercises, sports classes in different directions on the lawn, beach and pool in the afternoon and fun shows in the evening. Most of the children of tourists were satisfied with discos. In general, the hotel is characterized as quiet, calm and family. For a good club holiday, it is better to choose any of the nightlife on the city's promenade. Right next to the hotel is the central street of the city, where you can find a large bazaar with Turkish flavor and many catering establishments with delicious desserts and hookahs.

Summing up, it should be recognized that objectively the level of the hotel is high and basically corresponds to the 5 * category. No sharply negative reviews were noticed. Many guests expressed a desire to rest again at Side Sungate Hotel 5 *. Tours here can be purchased by tourists of all age categories. Everyone will find something to their liking. Have a nice rest!
