Crochet lessons: jacket

The jacket has long been one of the favorite inhabitants of the wardrobe, especially among women. Its definition and place of application are so wide that they make it the most practical and comfortable to wear piece of clothing.

A jacket is a cropped jacket. But in modern fashion there are short and long wardrobe items with a wide variety of cuts, necklines, fasteners, pockets made of various materials. The most comfortable - knitted or crocheted jacket. It can be very warm (almost a jacket) or, conversely, very light, summer.

Body jacket

The jacket is so versatile that it can be suitable for women with any type of figure, it is important to choose the right style.





Apple

The main feature of this type is a wide waist, therefore, the jacket should be loose, expanding better downward and not short.

Pear

It has lush hips combined with a pronounced waist and small breasts. The most winning jacket will be a volumetric one from above with a length either above or below the middle of the thigh.

Hourglass

A figure that has equal volumes in the chest and hips with a thin waist. The fitted models of jackets with belts or decorative elements in the belt area will do. If at the same time there is excess weight, it is worth choosing denser fabrics, semi-fitted style and dark colors.

Rectangle

Those who do not have a pronounced waist are well suited for long jackets without a marked waist line or, conversely, with a pronounced waist.

Jacket Material

Jackets are sewn from a wide variety of materials - from leather to denim. Now things made on their own have become very popular, and many fashionistas take on a hook, knitting needle or sewing machine. Crocheting a jacket is probably the easiest and fastest. You can get a novelty in the wardrobe in just a couple of days. Even a simple scheme for beginners with due diligence will give a completely worthy and interesting pattern. In addition, if you crochet an openwork jacket, it will look much more elegant than your fellow knitted on knitting needles.

You can practice by crocheting a summer jacket for a child (daughter, goddaughter or niece). There is no need to be smart with models or styles. Make the jacket plain. The most universal is white, it is perfect for summer and will be combined with any colors in the wardrobe of a child. If you try and do not overdo it with patterns, you can get a great gift. Small size will also play in your favor at the initial stage.

How to learn to crochet a jacket





We will learn how to crochet a jacket with a pattern of motifs. A motive is a kind of block that fits separately (flower, leaf, ornament, etc.). After all the blocks according to the scheme are connected, they assemble the product.





Size :

  • breast volume 92 cm;
  • length 64 cm;
  • sleeve 40 cm.

To work, you need :

  • yarn 100% cotton 400 g;
  • hook number 1.5;
  • 5 buttons.

Knitting density :

  • one outlet - 7 x 7 cm.

Openwork motif :

  • socket.

Description of work

This model for the convenience of beginner needlewomen can be made from individual motives, and combine them after knitting according to the scheme.

Tie the finished product with the "Edge" pattern. It is performed as follows:

  • first and second rows: knit double crochet;
  • third row: knit three single crochet, 2 stitches, so alternate to the end of the row.

For the front shelf, knit five button loops from the air. P.
