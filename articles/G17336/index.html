Kherson hotels of economy class near the bus station. List

Residents of Ukraine, Russia or other countries of the world can come to Kherson for different purposes. History buffs visit this village in order to explore its many attractions. Applicants go to this city for admission to institutes, business travelers - for business purposes. And, of course, all these citizens would be interested to know which inexpensive Kherson hotels are the best. There are a lot of good economy hotels in this village. Some of these complexes are located including near the bus station.

Hotel list

Most Kherson hotels offer tourists quite comfortable and at the same time not too expensive rooms.





Near the bus station, visitors can rent housing, for example, in such complexes as:

  1. Ninel . This cheap hotel is located about 2.8 km from the station. The most inexpensive room here costs only about 350 rubles per day.
  2. Versailles This complex is located a little further from the bus station - about 3.3 km. The rooms here are more expensive than in Ninel - about 990 p. per night.
  3. Welcome Home is about the same distance from the train station as Versailles (3.5 km). The cost of housing in this hotel starts at 1100 p.
  4. The compass . This hotel is located 4 km from the bus station. There are numbers in it from 950 p. per night.
  5. Non Stop Hotel is only about a kilometer from the bus station. Rooms here for rent at a price of 900 r. per night.

The rooms all of these Kherson hotels offer tourists comfortable and also inexpensive.





Hotel Ninel: description and reviews

This large hotel complex is located on 22. Belinsky Street. If you wish, you can rent a standard, superior or luxury room. The price of accommodation rented at the hotel includes breakfast. The rooms of the Ninel Hotel are equipped with a TV, telephone, bar. Also in all rooms of the center are air-conditioned.

Shower rooms, even in standard apartments, are individual in the hotel. In the superior rooms, tourists, among other things, can use coffee tables, dressing tables and safes, and in the suites there is also a sofa with armchairs. On the territory of the hotel for guests there are several pools, saunas, a restaurant, a cafe, etc.

Reviews of tourists about this hotel on the network are different. Some visitors consider it extremely inconvenient and express a lot of complaints regarding the service provided here. According to other guests of Kherson, in order to spend a couple of nights here, the hotel is quite suitable. Moreover, the rooms here are much cheaper than in any other complex of the city, located next to the bus station.

Kherson hotels: Versailles

The exact address of this hotel: st. Crimean, 93. The Versailles complex offers several tens of luxury rooms to the guests of Kherson. It is worth living in them relatively inexpensively. Therefore, this hotel can be categorized as budget. In addition to air conditioning, the rooms rented here include a TV, telephone, armchair and shower. If desired, residents can access the Internet in their rooms using the Wi-Fi network. The hotel has a restaurant, recreational facilities and barbecue, free parking. Guests can also play billiards.





Like Ninel, the reviews this hotel received from residents were not particularly good. Of course, the rooms rented in it for real suites do not pull. However, living in this hotel, according to many visitors, is still a little more convenient than in Ninel. In any case, the Internet at the Versailles Hotel works well, and the staff here are very polite.

Economy Kherson Hotels: Welcome Home

This hotel is located at ul. Tchaikovsky, d. 60. If desired, visitors can rent here standard or comfort class rooms. Guests at the Welcome Home are allowed to stay with pets (by prior arrangement). All rooms are classically decorated and equipped with modern TVs and air conditioning. Showers and balconies are available in all rented rooms. For the convenience of the ambassadors, the hotel, among other things, provides for a small dining area, equipped with everything necessary for self-cooking.

Kherson hotels can be comfortable or not very. The Welcome Home Hotel praises most of its former guests. The advantages of this complex are, first of all, that its residents can use Wi-Fi, billiard room and sauna for free. The staff of this hotel, like its rooms, tourists in most cases praise. The disadvantages of the hotel, its guests consider mainly only the lack of signs (the hotel is difficult to find).





"Compass"

This complex is located in Kherson at the address: st. Petrenko, d. 25 (in the building of the sports center). For guests of the city, the Compass Hotel offers air-conditioned standard, deluxe or junior suites. If necessary, visitors can use the rented rooms with a desk, wardrobe, TV, telephone, hairdryer and safe. The hotel has a restaurant, a gym, a hammam with a swimming pool, a massage parlor and a solarium for guests.

All hotels of Kherson, the addresses of which were given above, according to tourists, have a number, albeit small, but still disadvantages. About the Compass Hotel, there are practically no negative reviews on the network. The rooms here are spacious and comfortable, the furniture is new, and the appliances are working. Very nice to their guests are the staff of this hotel.
