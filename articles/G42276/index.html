DIY Jewelry Organizer: Ideas and Materials

Small things are often lost, especially for various jewelry and jewelry. Small earrings, thin rings often roll into unknown corners of the apartment. And chains, beads and necklaces! They are always confused. I wanted to throw a chain around my neck, you take it out, and from the casket a rope from tangled beads, bracelets, catched earrings stretches behind it. What you need is an organizer for jewelry, this is a convenient device, an assistant in the storage of jewelry, and beautiful decor on a shelf. We have accumulated several ideas for creating it ourselves.

Jewelry storage

Most often, we store our jewelry in one box or leave expensive jewelry presented to us in a gift box, but what about inexpensive jewelry and simple beads? Of course, you can purchase a simple and convenient organizer in the store and pack your treasures in compartments, but what about creating something exclusive? Let's make a DIY jewelry organizer together. There are so many interesting ideas: from twigs, an organizer dress, in the form of a casket, and even from wine corks.

Let's get acquainted with the first master class.

Tree for decoration

On a small installation of twigs, it will be simple and convenient to store your chains, beads and other jewelry jewelry. Prices for such jewelry holders are sky-high, let‚Äôs do it yourself from improvised materials, saving a little.

Wood as a material is inexhaustible possibilities and ideas.

To create an organizer you will need:

  • branches
  • wire;
  • varnish;
  • sandpaper;
  • glue for wood;
  • secateurs;
  • knife.

Process of creation

To make the organizer with our own hands, we need to stock up on branches. Take a large branch, you can branched, as a basis for a tree, as well as a couple of small, dense, strong.

Using a secateurs, remove excess processes from the branches, with a knife, peel the bark from the branches and process the ends.

In order not to chop a splinter, the installation should be smooth, so polish the branches.

If you want to make your tree colored, then you should color the branches now, if not, then proceed to assembly. Connect the branches together, forming a tree as you like, fasten the parts with glue and wrap the joints with wire.

Leave the organizer for several hours, the glue should harden. Then coat the entire craft with varnish and let dry completely.

Such a tree can be placed on the wall, placed in a vase, or made a stand so that it stands smoothly and firmly on a shelf.

If you took thick branches, you are unlikely to be able to place earrings on them, for this, make loops of thick wire, color them in the appropriate color and hang them in pairs on the branches. Earrings can also be placed on them.

Wine cork board

Such an organizer, like a tree, can be hung on a wall or placed near a mirror. Wine cork home decorations look very cool. In order to create this board, you will need:

  • wine corks;
  • frame;
  • cardboard;
  • hooks;
  • glue;
  • knife.

The number of traffic jams depends on what size frame you have chosen. Let's see how to make a cork board so that all of your jewelry can be placed on it.

Board creation

Put the frame on the cardboard, circle it and cut it, this will be the basis on which the corks must be glued.

Corks from wine put in a pan and pour water, boil. This is necessary in order to get rid of the smell of wine, from stains, as well as make them softer, because we need to cut them.

When the corks have cooled, take a board and chop them. Cut them as you wish, along or in circles, it all depends on what pattern you want to lay out.

Yes, plugs can be cut along, laid out with a brick, or divided into colors, cut into circles and lay out a simple image. When cutting in circles, adhere to at least 1 cm thickness.

Having cut the cork, we proceed to gluing. Roughly arrange the pieces on the cardboard, arrange them in the order that you like best, and then gradually glue the parts, firmly pressing them together.

When the cork board has dried, it can be decorated. Paint, add a picture to the frame. Drive small cloves into the board or place hooks for jewelry so that any jewelry can be placed.

This cork board is universal, among other things, it can be used as a note board, leaving notes or photos under the button. A convenient organizer for DIY jewelry is made.

Dress for jewelry

What an interesting way to store jewelry! Such a convenient organizer for jewelry remotely resembles a cover for clothes. It can also be placed near the mirror, hung on a hook, and hung in a closet, laying out all your jewelry in separate pockets. It is also an excellent cover for various trifles, for example for buttons, spools of thread.

What do we need to sew a dress organizer for jewelry?

  • strong hanger;
  • dense fabric;
  • vinyl film;
  • braid.

As well as scissors, threads, a sewing machine.

Dress making

We measure the width of the hanger, add 5 cm for allowance. This will be the width of the dress. Choose the length of your choice. We cut a rectangle according to the parameters. Fold the parts in half face inward. Attach the hanger and circle it. Cut out the office.

Cut the vinyl into strips of different widths to fit different decorations. We decorate the upper edge with braid. To make the pockets voluminous and place more objects in them, make folds on vinyl tapes and sew them to form pockets.

On a fabric basis, mark with soap or chalk where the pockets of the appropriate width will be.

Sew the base, leaving space at the neck of the product for the hanger, then place the vinyl tapes and sew them. Dress the dress itself with braid around the edges. You can give the shape of a dress or any other thing to your fabric piece. Depending on what thing you did, decorate it, a dress, for example, can be decorated with lace.

Insert the hanger. You can sew a cut for it or decorate it with a braid, forming a throat for the dress.

Dress-organizer for jewelry with your own hands made and ready to use, lay out your treasures.
