Pricing

Private sector prices in Pitsunda fluctuate depending not only on the month, but also on the time of year. The fact is that there are many who want to come here in the autumn-winter period. Well, here the prices are quite affordable. But for the coming summer, it is worth asking in advance.

There are budget options - for students. These are rooms from 600 rubles per day, then you can find the economic options of small apartments - up to 1000 rubles per day.
