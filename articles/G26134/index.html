Rest in the green grove of Samara

How often do we strive to the Far Far Away without noticing that there are amazing places of rest near us. Sun, water, sandy beaches. Nature gave Samara everything so that you can have a great break from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. Not for nothing that from time immemorial people have chosen this place.

Legends of Toad Island

Historians say that in 1357 on the banks of the Volga, on the road to Batu-Saray, the great Russian hierarch Alexy stopped. During the halt, he visited the cave of the hermit known in those places. History keeps the secret of conversation. But there is evidence that after the meeting the great ascetic predicted the appearance of a big city at this place.

The construction of the fortress and the conduct of hydraulic engineering events changed the course of the great river. The once steppe left bank of the Volga near the mouth of the Samara River turned into a large island. Over the years, the water changed its course, the channels broke the island into smaller sections. Spring floods caused new areas of sand, river braids appeared. Along with this, the names changed. The island of Veal, Cow, Podzhabny - all these are the names of once one land.

Today, a strip of mighty trees and sandy beaches on the opposite bank are clearly visible from the Samara embankment . The green grove of Samara is a favorite vacation spot on Rozhdestvensky Island, the largest part of that ancient land.

Tourist paradise

In summer, the Green Grove of Samara gives thousands of tourists daily, lovers of beach holidays. The island lives in pristine nature. There are no generally accepted urban communications. No gas, light. But the wildlife, wonderful lakes and a golden beach. In Samara, Green Grove is a place of pilgrimage for lovers to be close to nature, relax without the bustle of the city.

Numerous tourist centers and resorts are scattered along the Volga coast. The green grove of Samara offers magnificent panoramic views. And also developed tourist routes in the reserve "Samara Luka".
