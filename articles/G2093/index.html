Hotel Xperience Kiroseiz Park Land 5 * (Hotel Hperens Kiroseiz Park Land 5), Naama Bay, Egypt. Booking, Prices, Photos

Description: Xperience Kiroseiz Park Land 5 is located on the coast of the warm Red Sea, in the heart of the resort area in Egypt. This fashionable hotel is distinguished by the fact that it has two separate territories: one of them has a comfortable multi-story hotel, and the other has a complex of two-story villas.

The airport is approximately 20 kilometers (or fifteen minutes by bus). A trip to the ancient pyramids in the capital of Egypt - Cairo - will take five hours by bus or one hour by plane.





Rooms: The hotel offers accommodation in 700 rooms, both standard and superior, with air conditioning, terraces or balconies. As well as 22 modern bungalows near the beach.

The rooms have a shower or bath with a hairdryer, telephone, refrigerator, cable TV with Russian channels, a small safe and a minibar.

Linen is changed every three days, towels (including beach towels) are changed every day, rooms are cleaned daily, room service is provided.

Beach: Own, sandy, with coral reefs. Xperience Kiroseiz Park Land 5 is located on the third line, to the beach more than 1000 meters. Tourists are constantly driven to the sea by a free hotel bus.





Meals: On the territory of the hotel, every tourist can find his favorite dishes and drinks. After all, there are 14 restaurants and bars.

Xperience Kiroseiz Park Land 5 offers a menu of European, Egyptian and international dishes.

Pharon - the main restaurant of the hotel, open from morning until evening. Saddle Bar - open in the evening. It offers drinks and cocktails, as well as Mexican dishes.

In Chez Pascal, service is carried out according to the à la carte system, it is necessary to arrange a reservation at the front desk to reserve a table.

Dolce Vita - Italian restaurant serving pizza, sandwiches and snacks.

Bars are located next to the pools, in the lobby, on the beach. In addition to alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, Starlight Disco also hosts a disco every evening.





Information for vacationers: The staff works harmoniously and quickly. There are several shops with souvenirs and goods for a beach holiday, laundry, ATM, hairdresser, dry cleaning. Doctor services are available upon request.

Three swimming pools, two of them heated, a water park - all this offers tourists Xperience Kiroseiz Parkland 5. Reviews of little vacationers about children's slides are the most enthusiastic. You can play volleyball, table or tennis on the court, go to the movies.

In the evenings, a disco is held, animation near the pools - in the daytime.

A large conference room for four hundred people is provided for conferences and business sessions.

For children, Xperience Kiroseiz Park Land 5 has two pools, a playground, a club. The club has two programs for children from 4 to 8 years and older. Babysitting, cots and chairs in the restaurant are available upon prior request.

Reviews: Most travelers have spotted an excellent service and food at Xperience Kiroseiz Park Land 5. Reviews are mostly positive. Some like the sandy beach and coral reefs, while others spend most of their time in the sun loungers by the pool. Others are delighted with the relaxation area with an ozone bath, jacuzzi, steam room, sauna and massage.
