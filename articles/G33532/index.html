Muine Ocean Resort & Spa (Phan Thiet) was built in 1998. However, in 2011 there was a reconstruction. So today, guests expect comfortable rooms with freshly renovated and stylish interiors. Housing stock

As mentioned above, the hotel in question has 35 comfortable rooms. They are located in small bungalows and villas. All apartments have a stylish design and are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay.
