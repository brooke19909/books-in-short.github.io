Do-it-yourself chiffon skirt? Easy!



At all times, skirts and dresses made the female silhouette more elegant, emphasizing the dignity and beauty of the figure. They are especially good if sewn from flying and transparent fabrics. In addition, such an airy dress, a chiffon skirt with an elastic band or on a yoke with a zipper are ideal clothes for the summer heat. The length can be very different, here you need to proceed from your own preferences. It is worth noting that the length of the maxi will hide the full hips, and small folds at the waist slightly conceal the convex tummy. But short fluffy skirts are suitable for owners of slender legs. Such a thing is simply required for a women's wardrobe, since it is universal. If the skirt is made of plain fabric, it will look great with a motley T-shirt with a simple cut. If, on the contrary, with prints or ornaments, it is better to give preference to a calm top of a neutral color. This combination can be perfect casual or holiday wear.

So, with what it is clear to wear a skirt, they also decided on the length, but here where to get it? And here are two options - either buy or sew on your own. Everything is clear with the purchase - go to the store, choose, buy - and enjoy the new thing, but with the second option everything is a little more complicated, but the result is worth it. What are the advantages of self-tailoring?

  • A self-sewn chiffon skirt will come out much cheaper than purchased.
  • A huge selection of fabrics significantly exceeds the ready-made models presented in the markets and in stores.
  • With individual sewing, you can adjust the number of folds near the elastic and the length of the skirt or make it multilayer.

The pattern of a summer skirt from chiffon is built very simply, for it you do not need to take a large number of measurements and do a lot of calculations. It is enough to measure the volume of the hips, waist and length of the product itself. To make the skirt look good, it is better to make it from two canvases with oblique cuts up. This will reduce the number of folds near the elastic for a good fit. Do-it-yourself chiffon skirt is sewn very easily and quickly. The main thing is not to rush, to properly cut the fabric and process the seams.



It should be remembered that such a thin material can not be cut into several layers, this will lead to skewing of the fabric. Also, so that the skirt, made of chiffon with your own hands, looks neat, you need to properly process all the slices. For internal, reverse sections, use overlock or zigzag stitching or double-turn the fabric with overlapping stitches. To finish the bottom of the skirt, a Moscow seam is used or the edge is overlocked and, turning inside out, a line is laid close to the edge.

A self-made chiffon summer skirt can become a real highlight of a women's wardrobe. It can be on a wide elastic band with embedded folds or tiered with a zipper. It happens with a yoke made of a denser fabric, with a mini-petticoat and with a maxi-length of the upper layer of transparent fabric. A single-layer skirt made of so-called crepe-chiffon, gathered on an elastic band to a height of about 15 cm with a distance between the stitches of 1 cm, will look great. Which model of skirt you take, and this flying fabric will always make the figure more feminine and graceful.
