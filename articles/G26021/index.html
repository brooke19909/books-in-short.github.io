LCD "Optimist", Pervouralsk - review, developer, layout and reviews

Pervouralsk is a small provincial town in the Sverdlovsk region. This is not the cultural and historical capital of the Urals, but the city where the majority of the population is occupied by the mining and processing industries. Currently, the village is actively developing, being built up, new residential complexes appear. Due to the established and developed transport network, you can reach Yekaterinburg in just 40-50 minutes.

LCD "Optimist" in Pervouralsk is a unique residential development project that immediately attracted the attention of critics and those who have long been thinking about their own apartment. As part of this material, we will try to evaluate the complex and the conditions that it offers modern, quite picky and demanding residents.





about the project

Residential complex "Optimist" - a project of mass development of a new neighborhood. The first stage is represented by a three-sectional house of variable number of storeys for 210 economy class apartments. The construction area is fenced, isolated from through traffic.

Modern technologies and innovations were laid down in the Optimist LCD project in Pervouralsk. Reviews of the first buyers focus on the autonomous heating system. Thanks to it, residents can control comfort in the apartment and save on utility bills. All entrances are equipped with high-speed elevators that operate silently, as well as ramps for moving low-mobility categories. The complex is due to be completed in the 4th quarter of 2017.
