Murat Hotel 3 * (Kemer, Turkey): hotel description and reviews of tourists

In the historical area, at the foot of the mountains, is one of the favorite places for Russians to relax. This is Kemer, where thousands of our compatriots come annually. The resort, located on the Mediterranean, is famous not only for its wonderful beaches, but also for the variety of landscapes. Around the numerous hotels of this small Turkish town there are many mountain ranges covered with pine forests. The coast of this resort is indented with quiet and cozy bays.

The slopes of the mountains approach the very sea. The air smells of pine and orange aromas, and sandy or pebble beaches washed by clean and clear warm water are crowded all year round.
