Getting a loan from a bank is a procedure during which a borrower sometimes has to pay certain types of commissions and also enter into a loan insurance agreement. If the entire amount of the debt is repaid ahead of schedule, the borrower has the opportunity to receive a refund of insurance on the loan. There are several ways to do this.

Situation in the structure

Not so long ago, almost all banks introduced new obligations for borrowers in the form of health, life and property insurance. Now, when drawing up a loan or a loan, a bank client must sign an insurance contract. Because of this, there is a need for additional funds. But the legality of compulsory insurance is sometimes a big question. And if you know this, then it is quite possible to get a loan insurance return. The law currently in force allows this to be done.





Loan protection

Banking institutions insure the amount of the loan in order to protect themselves and get back the funds issued to the client, in any circumstances. Current legislation suggests that health and life insurance is voluntary, but collateral must be protected. In fact, many banks impose absolutely all types of insurance on customers, however, in the case of a vehicle or real estate, which act as collateral for a bank, this procedure is also quite beneficial for the borrowers themselves. If unforeseen circumstances arise in the form of partial destruction or complete destruction of the house, then the insurance company will have to close the rest of the debt. However, quite often, such a contribution acts as an expensive and redundant option, and repayment of loan insurance is not always available.





If the borrower is confident that he will be able to repay the loan soon, then such an additional service will only take away his money. It is worth agreeing to conclude an agreement with the insurance company only in those situations when a loan is taken for a long period and there is a great risk of loss of solvency in the event of job loss due to job cuts or health loss. These are insured events, so you can expect that the company will be reimbursed cash.

Contract execution

When a borrower receives a loan, quite often various commissions are paid, about which he was not notified in advance. Often he has to pay the so-called insurance amount. If there was no such agreement, then you can contact the financial institution with a written application for the return of insurance on the loan. If the bank agrees, the borrower will receive his funds. If you do not agree, then you can file a lawsuit. Credit insurance will be refunded upon resolution of the case in favor of the borrower.

It indicates the amount of the policy, and also lists the points regarding the possibilities of returning the insurance amount in different situations, in particular if the contract is terminated. The borrower has the right to offer certain and essential terms of the contract on which he is ready to assume the obligations.





Subjects of the contract

  1. Health and life of the person to whom the borrowed funds are issued.
  2. Real estate that is acquired under a mortgage program, and then acts as collateral for a pledge. It should be noted that the return of mortgage insurance has certain features, which will be described later.
  3. A vehicle that was purchased with a car loan.

Such types of insurance are intended to give the client the opportunity to pay off credit debt in a situation when an insured event occurs.

Refusal to pay insurance

The company may refuse to pay the client the amount of insurance due to him, referring to certain circumstances:

- Skipping application deadline. Usually it is one month from the moment of the insured event, unless otherwise specified in the contract. If it is impossible to write a statement for some reason, then an employee of the company should be notified.

- The application does not contain the required data: the number of the contract and the date of its conclusion, information about the insured person, and there is no date of occurrence of the insured event or its circumstances.

- Documents on the fact of an accident, necessary for payment, are not attached.

The package of documents may have a different composition depending on the type of insured event:

  • the onset of disability involves the provision of a medical certificate and an extract from the patient's card;
  • the death of the insured person requires the provision of an appropriate certificate from the registry office.
