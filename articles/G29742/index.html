Shershnevskoe reservoir: description, rest, photo

Shershnevskoe reservoir was created in the 60s in the city of Chelyabinsk. It stretches to the Sosnovsky district. The name was given to him in honor of the village, which is located next to an artificial reservoir. It was assumed that the reservoir will be a source of water for Chelyabinsk itself, the surrounding areas and towns.



Reservoir characteristic

The size of the Shershnevskoe reservoir is average, as its length is 18 km. In the widest places, it spreads over 4 km; the surface area is approximately 40 square meters. km As for the depth, the minimum is about 5 m, and the maximum is 14 m. The bottom in its structure is different - silt, sand in places. In these areas, chernozem and meadow soils are found. The shore of the reservoir is smooth, with a relief plan and mainly hills with vegetation predominate on it.

Infrastructure and the natural environment

Roads are laid along almost the entire length of the coast. Multi-apartment buildings, country houses, ordinary one-story residential buildings are being built, and there are also boron, gardens, vegetable gardens and other households near the reservoir.

The Shershnevskoe reservoir along its banks has a rich nature, which is represented by many familiar trees, such as birch trees, maples, poplars and low-growing willows of several species. Shrubs also firmly settled near the reservoir and annually bear fruit with such types of berries as dogrose and hawthorn. Black elderberry and broomberry supplement edible plants with their some uselessness for visiting city citizens, but please them with their beauty and splendor in the spring.



Rest on a reservoir

In the northern part of the reservoir there is a city beach for general use and a beach for nudists. A bunch of beautiful umbrellas from the sun and sunbeds attract and attract vacationers. The southern part of the "sheltered" in its territory water attractions. There are parking lots for cars, places for rest with the arrangement of barbecues and a smokehouse - barbecue or smoked fish are an integral part of cultural leisure.

You can rent a room or even a house for the weekend. Shershnevskoe reservoir - a reservoir on the banks of which there are always places for tourists. Beautiful and equipped gazebos on the beaches are considered very convenient for spending holidays during the holidays both in summer and in winter, because they are open to the penetration of the wind. This allows you to escape from the heat or from the cold in winter.

Water adventures are possible here on catamarans, jet skis, boats and yachts, which makes vacation in the Chelyabinsk region in the summer unforgettable. In winter, skiing on the frozen mirror of the reservoir will be very interesting.

Reservoir problems

Shershnevskoe reservoir (Chelyabinsk) is equipped on the site of the flooded Mikhailovsky farm, where there were many houses, farms, gardens and even a plant. The remnants of water-damaged wooden buildings often nailed to the shores earlier, which created a displacement and reformation of the coastal zone of the reservoir. This also interfered with the fishermen, creating inconvenience in their movement on boats and rafts, which simply hooked on logs and boards sticking out of the water, sometimes intertwined with bushes growing on the banks.

Now the coast is relatively clean, there are no obstacles to the free and comfortable rest of tourists. The environmental situation is under control, the common areas are carefully protected. In the zone of water attractions for children, everything is provided for protection against danger, even special sand was brought there, and the bathtubs for babies are filled with purified water from microbes.



Security

If we consider in detail the security situation on the beaches of the reservoir, the situation is as follows. Since this place is considered a resort, the precautionary measures are observed 100%. Rescuers are on duty on motor boats and boats, which, in the case of a swim, swimming for buoys, will be able to return them to the beach, help to get out of the water, or even simply advise vacationers about possible places for recreation and swimming.

The vigilance, honesty and benevolence of the local population are amazing. Therefore, if the Shershnevskoe reservoir is considered as a possible place to spend a vacation or go on vacation, you can not worry about safety.
