Hotel Pierre Anne 3 * (Cyprus, Ayia Napa): description, photos and reviews of tourists

Cyprus can hardly be called a budget holiday destination, but it is perfect for tourists who have already studied up and down other popular resorts, for example, Turkey or Egypt. This island is located in the Mediterranean Sea and has a hot climate, natural mountain landscapes, as well as a rich heritage of ancient culture. If desired, tourists can always find inexpensive hotels for recreation that offer quite acceptable conditions for living. For example, in Ayia Napa there is an inexpensive resort complex Pierre Anne Beach Hotel 3 *. But is it worth saving on vacation? Let us consider in more detail what rooms this hotel offers, as well as its infrastructure, food concepts and entertainment. Holidays in Ayia Napa

Hotel Pierre Anne 3 * is located in Ayia Napa - a large resort city in the southeast of Cyprus. Tourists come here in search of a clean coast with soft, almost velvet sand. He gained his popularity back in the 80s, and not only among tourists, but also among believers. It is believed that the Byzantine icon of the Blessed Virgin Mary was found in the forests of Ayia Napa. In its place a monastery was built, which has been perfectly preserved to this day. This resort is considered one of the best on the island, moreover, the Cypriots themselves rest here. It is aimed at outdoor enthusiasts, youth companies, as many bars and nightclubs are open here. But families with children and older couples can easily find something to do.

What attracts tourists to the resort of Ayia Napa? First of all, with its beaches. Many of them here received the Blue Flag - an award that is given only to the cleanest sections of the coast. In addition, the beaches are well developed tourist infrastructure. Travelers can go diving, ride a motor boat, catamaran, water ski or surf. The coast is fully equipped - showers, toilets, changing rooms are installed everywhere. The entrance to the sea here is very convenient, flat and gentle, therefore safe for swimming with young children.

Ayia Napa is perfect for gastronomic tourism. Restaurants and cafes are open throughout the city, serving delicious international and local cuisine. If desired, you can visit a luxurious restaurant or limited to a budget tavern. Those who like to study the culture and historical heritage of the host country can be advised to visit the ancient monastery mentioned above, as well as the tombs of the ancient period. The Museum of the Sea is also open in the city, where you can get acquainted with the inhabitants of coastal waters and the history of shipbuilding in Cyprus. Tourists with young children can visit the water park or an amusement park with carousels, a ferris wheel and trampolines.

Where is the hotel situated?

Thus, tourists coming to Ayia Napa will definitely not get bored. It is important to choose a hotel with a convenient location to quickly get to the beach and the city center without losing precious time. As a rule, expensive complexes are built on the coast, so it is quite difficult to find an inexpensive hotel near the sea. However, Pierre Anne Beach 3 * boasts a good location. Only a small pedestrian road separates it from the coast. Therefore, you can walk to the beach in just 5 minutes. At the same time, the hotel is removed from the central part of the resort, where nightclubs are located. Consequently, the nights at Pierre Anne 3 * are quite quiet. The distance to the center is 500 meters, so to get there, you can walk along the picturesque streets of the city.

Another advantage of the location of the hotel is its small distance from the airport. It is located in the city of Larnaca, which is 50 km from Ayia Napa. Tourists can reach the hotel in about 1 hour by bus or taxi. If desired, guests can also pay for a paid transfer, and then the hotel will take care of picking you up from the airport and delivering you to the place of rest as comfortably as possible. The famous Ayia Napa Monastery is 500 meters away. Within walking distance there is also a large supermarket, museum and information desk, where you can learn more about the resort or buy an excursion.

More about the hotel itself

Hotel Pierre Anne Hotel 3 * (Cyprus, Ayia Napa) began to receive tourists immediately after its opening in 1989. It occupies an ennobled and landscaped territory almost on the coast itself. Its area is about 23,000 square meters. The hotel is built in a classic Mediterranean style. It is distinguished by the simplicity of design and the abundance of light shades in the design. The white color in which the buildings are painted perfectly sets off the greenery of the landscape surrounding the hotel. In total, the hotel has 189 budget rooms for single and double occupancy. For families there are comfortable and spacious superior apartments.

In total, the hotel has 4 residential buildings:

  • Building No. 1 - a five-story building, where 67 rooms are located;
  • building number 2 - a small two-story building, accommodating 8 superior rooms (some of them have their own separate entrance);
  • Building No. 3 - a three-story building with 41 rooms;
  • Building No. 4 is another five-story building with 73 more living rooms.

Also on the territory of the Pierre Anne 3 * hotel (Cyprus) you can see a spacious pool with a sunbathing area and several public buildings, including a restaurant with an outdoor terrace.

Registration and Eviction Rules

Before checking into the room, each future guest must fill out a number of supporting documents. It cannot be said that the Pierre Anne 3 * hotel (Cyprus, Ayia Napa) is distinguished by any individual requirements for them. So, all tourists must present to the administrator receiving them, an identification card (required with a photograph) and a credit card. The hotel accepts Visa and MasterCard. For guests arriving from the CIS countries, Russian-speaking employees help fill out the documents.

Registration here starts at 14:00 local time. However, if necessary, timely notification and availability of rooms, employees can check in guests in the early morning and night. The hotel is loyal to families with children - you can come here with kids of any age. But to rest with the animals you have to find another place, because they are not allowed to come here with them. Another important rule - eviction from the room is carried out strictly before noon. However, you can stay on site for some time, for example, if your flight to the airport is scheduled for the evening.

Arriving for a vacation at the Pierre Anne Hotel 3 * hotel (Ayia Napa), you should not count on spacious and expensively furnished apartments. However, all rooms here are newly renovated and furnished with new furniture. Budget rooms, designed for one or two people, look, of course, modest - walls painted in beige, the absence of decor elements, tiles on the floor. But for a fee you can purchase a room with sea view.

Superior rooms of the Pierre Anne 3 * hotel (Ayia Napa) are designed for 3-4 adults. They are a bit more spacious, their area is 30 square meters. m. Maids clean the apartments every day, while guests are outside the living rooms. They change bed linen only once a week, as do towels in the bathroom. For guests with reduced mobility, rooms are equipped with low sinks, wide doorways and a spacious bathtub. In total, the hotel has 5 rooms for such guests. It is also worth noting that all rooms are strictly non-smoking.

Arriving on vacation, tourists, as a rule, expect to see in rooms not only a standard set of furniture consisting of beds and wardrobes, but also a certain set of amenities. What kind of food does the hotel offer tourists?

Buying a ticket to the Pierre Anne Beach Hotel 3 * (Cyprus), tourists also pay two meals a day - half board, which includes only breakfast and dinner. Guests can dine either in the city or in the hotel restaurant, but for a fee. As a rule, tourists most often choose the first option, because there are many inexpensive cafes near the complex. The main restaurant operates on a specific schedule. So, tomorrow starts at 7:00 and lasts until 09:30. And dinner - from 19:00 to 21:00. Alcoholic drinks are not included in the price, so they need to be paid separately. Breakfast and lunch are served at the shared buffet, and the menu includes several types of meat, sausages, fruits and vegetables. Several times a week they serve fish and seafood. Vegetarian food is also free.

Another restaurant located on the territory of the hotel is called En Plo; a bar of the same name is also open on its territory. Every day from 10:00 to 21:00 (the bar is open until 23:00) here you can try international and national Cypriot cuisine. All services, of course, are provided for a fee.

Infrastructure facilities in the complex

Developed infrastructure is one of the main factors affecting the level of service provided. Pierre Anne Beach 3 * (Cyprus) will not surprise you with a huge selection of facilities and services offered, however, everything you need for a good vacation will provide. For example, for tourists traveling by car, there is private parking. You can use it for free. Guests can also rent a car directly at the hotel. For lovers of a healthy lifestyle, bike rental is available. The 24-hour reception offers currency exchange services, registration of excursions. Here you can call a taxi to the hotel, copy documents, connect the Internet. Wi-Fi in the common lobby and bar, by the way, is free of charge. At the reception you can use wardrobes, a luggage storage room and rent a separate safe.

If necessary, you can call a doctor at the hotel, but his services are not covered by travel insurance.

Beach holidays at the hotel

As a rule, tourists choose the hotel Pierre Anne 3 * (Ayia Napa) for a beach holiday by the sea. Therefore, the main entertainment in the complex is focused on this particular leisure option. Of course, guests spend most of their time on the beach. The hotel has access to the urban stretch of coast, equipped for a comfortable stay. It is only 50 meters away, and a pedestrian road separates it from the complex. The entrance to the sea is rocky and sandy, so you need to swim carefully so as not to get into the underwater pit. The beach has numerous sun loungers and parasols, which can be rented for a fee. Here you can sign up for diving courses, go windsurfing, go skiing or play beach volleyball. Most of the services, of course, are provided for a separate amount, but the entrance to the beach itself is free.

If there are too many people on the coast or you just do not want to go anywhere, then you can relax next to the pool. Its area is 550 square meters. m. Therefore, you can swim without any hesitation in movement. It is filled with fresh water that does not heat up. The pool is open every day from early morning until 19:00. Next to it are sun loungers and parasols. Unlike the beach, tourists do not need to pay for them.

What else can you do at the Pierre Anne Hotel 3 *?

It is worth noting that the hotel offers a small selection of entertainment for tourists who love outdoor activities. They can rent a bike for a fee. On a special area equipped on the grass, you can play volleyball. Equipment for playing tennis is provided only for a deposit that will be returned to you after the return of the racquets and balls intact. A distinctive feature of Pierre Anne Hotel 3 * can also be called the presence of its own spa, as they are not often found in hotels of this category. True, it is worth noting that all the services provided here are strictly paid. In the evening, tourists also like to play billiards, in a special room with tables.

Is it possible to come here with children?

Hotel Pierre Anne 3 * accepts tourists even with the youngest children. The age at which settlement begins is unlimited. For children under 12 years of age, accommodation in the complex is completely free if they sleep in their parents' beds. For babies up to two years, a cradle can be provided upon request. You can use it for free, but their number is strictly limited, so they are not always enough for all guests. Other amenities include a separate children's menu in the main restaurant (for example, milk porridge is served for breakfast), as well as provided high chairs. There is no separate entertainment program for kids at the hotel. However, for their comfortable and safe swimming, a separate shallow pool with fresh water is provided.

Positive feedback about Pierre Anne 3 *

And although this hotel positions itself as a budget vacation spot, it often receives good reviews. Tourists, as a rule, are satisfied with the rest in this place, although they note the presence of some shortcomings that can be ignored, given the low cost of living. Many of them even recommend the Pierre Anne 3 * hotel, indicating the following advantages as arguments:

  • excellent location - in 5 minutes you can walk to a supermarket and a bus stop, and across the road there is a theme park;
  • a bottle of local wine is usually provided as a gift to guests;
  • even the inexpensive rooms are in good condition - they are newly renovated;
  • polite staff who speaks excellent English, some employees also understand Russian;
  • The hotel has calm tourists from Europe or local residents, so you will not find drunk holidaymakers who make noise and behave indecently on the territory.

Negative feedback about this hotel

Of course, like any other hotel, Pierre Anne 3 * could not please absolutely all the guests. Some of them believe that the complex has significant shortcomings, which may spoil the impression of a vacation. Therefore, in their reviews before the trip, they recommend paying attention to the following disadvantages of the hotel:

  • the beach near the hotel is always crowded, so even for a fee, getting a deck chair is quite problematic;
  • monotonous breakfasts - usually serve the same set of dishes, which quickly get boring during the rest;
  • even for a fee, a very slow Internet connection is provided, which barely copes with downloading social networks;
  • not all rooms are in good condition - some guests come across cramped rooms with old repairs, which the administrator refused to change;
  • the entrance to the sea near the beach is rocky, so you have to swim exclusively in shoes.

Who would like a vacation at this hotel?

After reviewing the description of the Pierre Anne 3 * hotel (Cyprus) and traveler reviews, we can conclude that it is well suited for a budget vacation. Due to its favorable location, it will be comfortable for youth companies and guests with small children to relax. It can also be recommended to lovers of a relaxing holiday and those who prefer to spend most of the time on the beach. But tourists who love nightlife will not be bored here, because clubs and bars can be reached on foot in a short time.
