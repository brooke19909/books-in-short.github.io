Great place for recreation and fishing - Shchuchye Lake (Komarovo)

In a quiet secluded corner of the Karelian Isthmus there is an amazing Shchuchye lake (Komarovo). The map below shows its location and may be useful to those who first want to visit these wonderful places.

It is very important to pay attention to the fact that there are several lakes with a similar name. They are located in Buryatia, Sverdlovsk region of Russia, Kazakhstan. Our reservoir is located in the village of Komarovo, Leningrad Region. The area here is so beautiful that it simply attracts those who are tired of the bustle of the city and seeks to relax their soul and body.

Scenery

On clear days, everything is fine here - swamp grass blooms , an emerald carpet of moss spreads under your feet, and agile lizards run in the grass. True virgin nature. The crystal water surface is surrounded by a wonderful landscape created by a glacier, and the forests are rich in delicious berries and mushrooms.

The shores of the lake are peaty or sandy. The reservoir is surrounded by centuries-old fir trees. The bottom is sandy and extremely clean; there is an undercurrent, but not strong.



Sanctuary "Lake Shchuchye" - protection of virgin nature

Shchuchye Lake (Komarovo) is assigned to those territories that are under special protection. This is a natural monument. In 2011, the Shchuchye Lake Nature Reserve was established in order to preserve unique landscapes, endangered species of plants and animals.

The infrastructure is well-maintained, and the nearby territory of the reservoir is free from transport. There are many picnic areas and fun outdoor games. Equipped paths for cycling enthusiasts. And, of course, there are a lot of fish here - amber pike , trout, roach. In a word, a paradise for fishing lovers. Just plunging into the cool water, stepping your foot on the soft green grass, breathing fresh air with your full chest, you can feel like part of this beautiful nature.

This landscape is an ideal place for a family holiday. And for those who want to be alone with nature, you can retire to the forest thickets. It is important that Shchuchye Lake (Komarovo) is a paradise for people with different desires. Here everyone will find something personally for themselves.

Flora and fauna

Wildlife is striking in its uniqueness. Here you can meet the endangered representatives of the fauna and flora, listed in the Red Book. Exceptional specimens for the Karelian Isthmus include Kozelec low and sedge spherical. You can also find plants unusual for this area, for example, oak oak and common hazel. In addition to simple shrubs and grassy species, in the swamps there is an English sundew and a white waiting list. Very often you can see a pale white water lily.
