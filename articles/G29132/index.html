Hammamet is one of the most attractive tourist resorts in Tunisia. It is located on the Mediterranean coast, and the most famous thalassotherapy centers are located here.

How to get there

Like other cities in Tunisia, Russians get to Hammamet mainly by air. For example, you can first fly from Moscow to the Hammamet-Enfida airport, located 48 km from the city, and then transfer to bus number 106. Moreover, traveling to the resort by municipal vehicles will cost travelers relatively cheap - somewhere within 5 Tunisian dinars .

Hammamet: thalassotherapy

Tunisia has for many years been considered a great place to organize a health holiday. In particular, Tunisian SPA centers and hospitals where thalassotherapy methods are practiced are known far beyond the borders of North Africa. The basis of this method of therapy is a proven medical fact: the penetration of sea water ions through the skin restores the electrolyte composition of blood plasma and helps to increase human immunity. In addition, the Hammamet thalassocenters practice effective body wraps with healing mud and seaweed. Moreover, there are own hospitals and spa rooms at almost all major resort hotels. Especially known is Africa‚Äôs largest thalassocenter at the four-star Nahravess Hotel, which has 90 massage rooms and about a dozen swimming pools with sea water.
