Golden Rock Beach Hotel 5 * (Marmaris, Turkey): description, service, reviews

On the sea coast, lying opposite the island of Rhodes at the confluence of two seas - the Mediterranean and the Aegean, is the modern city of Marmaris. This place was inhabited in ancient times, when the city was called Fiskos and was an important trading port on the Aegean in the 6th century BC. e. It was conquered more than once by different nations, until in the 15th century the Ottomans captured it, which gave it the name Marmaris. The ancient fortress erected in 1522 testifies to that time.

Today, Marmaris, due to its climate and location on the picturesque shore of a beautiful bay, is considered a real gem of Turkey.

Marmaris is a tourist resort city with a population of 28 thousand people, which for the season takes about two million vacationers from around the world.
Marmaris is located in the province of Mugla. It is considered the best resort in Turkey and the most "European". Marmaris is famous for its nightlife, an abundance of bars and clubs and is recognized as the most dancing in this country. In its harbor, Netsel Marina, there are always more than three thousand snow-white yachts and pleasure boats at the joke.

In Marmaris, Europeans rest, mainly the British, Dutch and Germans. Foreigners have chosen this resort because of its undeniable advantages over other places of rest on the sea. It is climate, sea and atmosphere. The climate is excellent, because even at a temperature of +50 degrees a person does not experience sweltering heat. The sea is always calm, since the city is located in a bay: it is fenced off from the open sea by an island and a peninsula. And the atmosphere creates a feature of the resort: in this place there is the famous BarStreet Marmaris street. At least hundreds of bars, nightclubs, and restaurants are open here, creating an aura of celebration and fun. The main goal of the guests is a bright vacation in these entertainment venues. Night life does not subside until the morning.

Marmaris Bay is the largest in the world. The embankment along it has a length of 12 kilometers. It is a favorite place for walking guests of the city.

For lovers of beach holidays in Marmaris, there are extensive beaches. The main one is urban; it stretches along the entire promenade. The sea is warm and clean, the beach is sandy with small inclusions of pebbles. Near the beach are numerous bars and cafes.

Marmaris is the capital of Eastern Mediterranean sailing and the center of Turkish yachting. It hosts the annual sailing festival in May, and in the fall sailing races. Tourists have the opportunity to charter a yacht or boat for several hours or weeks and go on a sea voyage.

For diving enthusiasts, Marmaris is an ideal place. There are more than 60 points for diving and exploring the flora and fauna of the Aegean Sea. Divers can explore all kinds of artifacts that have been resting on the seabed for many centuries. It is very popular among experienced divers to dive at the cliff of Jazih, where the depth is from 3 to 30 meters and there are no undercurrents. Marmaris is also known for its diving schools with reliable equipment.

Turkish resorts are famous for their exquisite and delicious cuisine. And Marmaris is no exception. Bars, cafes and restaurants are crowded along the promenade, where it is never empty. They serve seafood, national Turkish, as well as unusual dishes, such as fried ice cream for dessert. Gourmet gourmets will appreciate the fine cuisine of Marmaris. The city has a large group of elite and exotic restaurants. Among them, the leader in the demonstration of national cuisine - Samdan restaurant. He works day and night. English cuisine is a priority at The Creat Corner Restaurant. Exotic lovers will love the Happy Brothers Chinese restaurant. And many other attractive establishments with interesting menus can be found in Marmaris.

Marmaris is a holiday city, a city for young people to relax. No wonder he was nicknamed "Turkish Ibiza." It has a lot of economy class hotels, created exclusively for young people who love night parties. Therefore, Marmaris hotels are in great demand among young people around the world. Recently, Russian tourists began to visit this resort, but so far there are not as many as other foreigners. You will not hear Russian speech in the crowd.

Youth hotels in Marmaris have a wide variety of entertainment. These include bars and discos, swimming pools and gyms, baths, saunas, numerous fun on the water, water park, etc.

Young people often choose club hotels. They are reasonably priced and have a more developed infrastructure.

The most popular youth hotels have a 5 * category. They stand on the first coastline. Service in them on the system of "ultra all inclusive" and is aimed at the implementation of active youth recreation.

The nightlife in Marmaris begins with the sunset, when tourists leave the hotels on the promenade for bars, clubs where you can enjoy live music, appreciate famous and novice DJs and dance. Disco are also in the afternoon on the beach, which suits Kikka Beach.

The most convenient locations for youth hotels are in the center of Marmaris or in the Cityler area. One such hotel is Golden Rock Beach Hotel.
