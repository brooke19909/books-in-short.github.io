Poseidonia Beach (Limassol) - one of the best hotels in Cyprus

In order for the rest to be complete, accommodation plays an important role. For those who have chosen Cyprus (Limassol) for their holidays , the four-star Poseidonia Beach hotel will be one of the best places to stay. It rises in the bay of Limassol, between the suburbs of Limassol Yermasoy and Amatu. The hotel is surrounded by magnificent gardens on both sides. From Larnaca Airport it is separated by a distance of 70 kilometers, and to the airport of Paphos by 10 kilometers less. The central part of the city is only 4 kilometers away, and there is a bus stop right outside the hotel . Three superb golf courses are an hour away. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel there is an amusement park and 2 beautiful water parks.



Hotel Poseidonia Beach (Limassol) itself is a seven-story building. For accommodation of guests 138 comfortable rooms are provided, among which 84 have a sea view. So, for vacationers in the building there are 110 double standard rooms with garden views, 20 family rooms with garden or mountain views, 2 presidential and 6 studios. All have bathrooms, a hairdryer, a direct dial telephone, color satellite television and air conditioning. There is a minibar and a balcony. Ironing board is available upon request. There is adjustable heating. Accessible rooms are available.



For its guests the hotel Poseidonia Beach (Limassol) provides meals on the All inclusive system in the main restaurant "Poseidon". It is located in the basement and has superb views of the Mediterranean Sea. A special feature of the food in this hotel is that each dinner is dedicated to a separate kitchen. So, during the rest you can try the dishes of French, Cypriot, Italian cuisine. It passes under the bewitching sounds of a piano. In addition to the restaurant, the Aegean coffee shop is located in the garden, where guests can enjoy a delicious and delicious breakfast. Guests can enjoy refreshing cocktails at the lobby bar.

Poseidonia Beach (Limassol) not has its own beach. Municipal beach is 150 meters away. The entrance to the sea is gentle, so this place is ideal for swimming not only adults, but also small vacationers.

Hotel Poseidonia Beach (Limassol) has a well-developed infrastructure. On its territory there is a swimming pool for adults and separately for young vacationers. For those who have arrived or rented their own car, parking is available. To store valuables at the reception there is a safe. In addition, you can use the services of a doctor, laundry, dry cleaning. The hotel has various shops, a currency exchange. For those who came not only to relax, but also to solve their work tasks, a conference room is available, which seats 225 people.



If you have chosen Cyprus (Limassol) for your holidays, Poseidonia Beach is the hotel where you will not be bored. Here is a wonderful animation that will not disregard a single guest. For those who want to work a cinema. For those who care about their health and beauty, the hotel has a gym, fitness center and spa. If necessary, you can use the services of a hairdresser. After an active pastime, you can relax in the sauna or jacuzzi, as well as use the services of a qualified massage therapist. In the evening, a disco is organized for guests, passing to live music. There is karaoke, a night club. In addition, throughout the day you can try various sports, including on the water.
