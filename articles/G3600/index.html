Popular night clubs in Minsk: rating

Almost every night , Minsk nightclubs open their doors to citizens and tourists. Some of these institutions have been operating for many years, while others have appeared recently. In these clubs you can have fun until the morning with friends. In addition, in such institutions you can make new friends.

In our article we will consider the popular nightclubs in Minsk, the rating will be a little lower. It will present the best institutions that are distinguished by a special chic.

"Max Show" - enchanting places for young people

If we talk about the best night clubs in Minsk, then, of course, the Max Show establishment belongs to them. It has been working for more than twenty years. There is everything to spend fun and bright evenings. The institution conducts interesting show programs. Various artists are performing. The club operates seven days a week.

The main color in the interior of the institution is red. Bar counters, sofas and wall decorations - all this is done in such a range.

The lighting is unobtrusive, the most striking element is the central bar. Comfortable tables are arranged in a circle.





The cuisine in the institution is European. The menu also has cocktails and alcoholic drinks. In the evening you can smoke a hookah.

The club also has a VIP lounge. Themed parties may surprise guests. There are often bartender shows and DJ sets.

OVERtime - a new club where everyone can relax

Continuing to describe the nightclubs of Minsk, it is worth talking about OVERtime. It was built at the end of 2014.





The institution was created by professionals. Guests can feel safe, because there is face control and a dress code. Here you can have fun from the heart. The club has three recreation areas, a stage for performances, a lobby bar, bar counters. Here you can find a good company and have fun.

Jack club

The institution is located in the city center, waiting for guests seven days a week. The menu is interesting, and the cocktail and wine lists are quite diverse. In the institution after nine in the evening live music. Often the best cover groups of the country perform here. The club works like a cafe. A special menu is available from twelve to four in the evening.

Club "Yula"

Previously, the institution had the other name "Radio B-Hey" and R-Club. The club opened in the nineties of the last century. But to this day the Belarusian party-goers visit the club. From nine in the morning until 16:00 the institution works like a cafe. Here you can have breakfast or lunch with a pleasant musical accompaniment. And on Friday and Saturday, it's good to relax in fun parties. The institution has various famous music artists.

RICH CAT - a club for those who love cats

Describing the nightclubs of Minsk, let's talk about the RICH CAT establishment. It features a sophisticated, original design with cat statues. The music format is quite original. Here, as a rule, tracks of such directions as POP, R'n'B, as well as Mash up sound. Popular artists and DJs are performing here.





The design is very thought out. It was designed in such a way that visitors were comfortable during chamber events and large concerts. The interior is eclectic. The central place in the decor is occupied by a cat (club symbol). Especially for this institution, elegant white figures were developed and manufactured.

Art programs in the institution are thought out to the smallest detail. For guests, dancers, show ballet, cover bands and other artists perform. Both themed and private parties are held.

The institution operates from eleven in the evening until six in the morning. Entrance for girls is free, guys will need to pay 200,000 rubles. There is a deposit system for ordering tables, and in three types (female, male and VIP).

Bogema live

What other popular Minsk nightclubs are there? For example, Bogema Live. This is a karaoke club. The institution has a very sophisticated interior. The music format is quite original. The atmosphere here is truly festive. In the institution, each guest will feel like a real star.

The Bogema Live menu is quite large, there are dishes of Belarusian, European and Japanese cuisines. A wide range of alcoholic drinks and cocktails will delight visitors.

Club AUroom

The facility is located in the business center. The club occupies two floors at once. Sometimes the institution is called the Golden Room. Probably because the club is decorated in oriental motifs. There is a comfortable sitting area. In the institution you can relax both together and a noisy company. Open from Thursday to Saturday from six in the evening.
