The Caribbean World Monastir Hotel (Tunisia) was created for a good mood

This Caribean World hotel is located in Monastir, not very far from the airport, as well as from the nearby famous resort, Sousse. Its location is very good, primarily based on climatic conditions - there is a lot of sun, but it is not evil, but very soft. The Caribian World Monastir Hotel (Tunisia) is very popular because of its construction - its original buildings and bungalows stretched along the sea through an area planted with tropical trees and beautiful flowers. This huge park, designed in the same architectural style, together with pools and buildings for housing, occupies as many as twenty-three hectares!



Hotel Caribbean World Monastir (Tunisia). Rooms and rooms

In total, the hotel has 610 rooms, where two, three or four guests can stay. They have individual air conditioning, and depending on the season, the room can be cooled or heated. Everywhere there are balconies with beautiful views of the sea or the park. Rooms of different sizes - in a smaller bungalow, in larger buildings. The hotel is loved by tourists from Western Europe and the USA - it is of high quality and not very expensive.

Caribian World Monastir Hotel (Tunisia) - eat and have fun



This "four", in addition to the "all inclusive" system, entertains guests with gastronomic shows. Breakfast, lunch and dinner, having tasted Tunisian and international cuisine, can be in two main restaurants. There are six other establishments specializing in various menus - Mexican, Italian, Chinese, Indian. There is also a special restaurant for grilled dishes. It has four bars, one of which is by the pool, and the other is on the beach. You will not be allowed to forget that you have come to Tunisia. The Caribian World Monastir Hotel offers an aroma of an oriental tale, which can be felt in the special Moorish Cafe. Having lounged on pillows, they drink tea and coffee, as well as smoke hookah.

Caribian World Monastir Hotel (Tunisia) - sea or pools?



The hotel itself is located almost on the beach. However, visitors are especially impressed by its pools. They are originally designed, in perfect harmony with the general style of the hotel and the garden, and also offer a lot of entertainment. The main pool is simply gigantic - its area is more than two kilometers, in addition, it is filled with sea water. The Caribbean World Monastir Hotel is known throughout Tunisia for its water activities. In the main pool there is a huge toboggan and picturesque waterfalls. Moreover, the hotel also boasts a water park. Slides, an island for sunbathing, an aquabar, where you have to go on special bridges - you can spend a whole day here and completely forget about the sea. There are two more outdoor pools, including a children's one - and also of considerable size.

This is just a small fraction of what you can tell about this hotel. But there is still great animation, for children and adults, a SPA center with the famous procedures for wrapping with algae, playing sports, dancing, riding horses, camels and ATVs - you will not list everything.
