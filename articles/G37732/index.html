Park Inn Hotel (Sochi): description, contacts, reviews

The hotel "Park Inn" is characterized by luxurious design and quality service. Sochi is a popular resort, but your place of residence is an important factor influencing the impression. With this hotel you can be sure that the memories of the rest will be only positive and vivid.

Brief description of the resort hotel

If you are planning a vacation by the sea, then pay attention to a hotel such as Park Inn. Sochi will open for you from a new unexpected side, if you will be surrounded by home comfort and quality service. Here you will find services such as a swimming pool, private parking, free internet and much more. Importantly, pets can be accommodated at the hotel.





One of the main advantages of the Park Inn Hotel (Sochi) is its guest apartments. All are decorated in bright colors. This provides guests with a good mood. With regard to amenities, it provides all the necessary furniture, appliances, as well as an equipped bathroom. It is important that a safe is installed in the room, and therefore you can be absolutely calm for the safety of your valuables.

As for food, here the Park Inn Hotel will be able to please the guests. Sochi is famous for its huge number of restaurants, but this hotel has one of the best establishments. It offers not only local, but also international cuisine. In addition, you can always order your favorite dishes directly to your room.

Facilities at the guest apartment

In all guest apartments at the Park Inn by Radisson Sochi City Center, guests have the opportunity to use the following list of amenities:

  • free extra bed for one child;
  • high-speed wireless Internet access to which is provided for free;
  • sets for making tea and coffee (stocks of bags of drinks and sugar are replenished every day);




  • the bathroom has an electric hairdryer, bath accessories (towels, bathrobe and slippers), as well as an individual cosmetic set;
  • modern LCD TV, which provides high-quality image transmission of satellite channels;
  • air conditioning system, thanks to which you can maintain a comfortable microclimate in your room;
  • a safe with an electronic combination lock will ensure the safety of your belongings;
  • full-fledged workplace;
  • electronic door lock.

Resort Infrastructure

In this hotel you will have access to the following resort infrastructure facilities:

  • a large gym equipped with many fitness equipment, as well as a fitness room;
  • a huge luxurious restaurant where you can try both traditional Russian and international dishes (room service is possible);
  • in the bar, in addition to alcoholic drinks, you will be offered juices, coffee and tea, as well as a huge selection of light snacks;
  • in the lobby bar you can enjoy your favorite drinks in a relaxed atmosphere;
  • near the hotel building is equipped with a private parking zone, the use of which requires a reservation;




  • reception staff at any time of the day or night are ready to help you;
  • if you want to go on a fascinating tour, then be sure to contact the tourist office;
  • on the ground floor (in the lobby) there is an ATM through which you can withdraw cash from your cards;
  • if you have valuable or too bulky luggage, then it can be taken to the left-luggage office;
  • the conference room has everything you need for your business events to be held at the highest level;
  • in the business center you will have access to a wide range of office equipment;
  • if you have planned a gala event or celebration, then you should definitely use the beautifully designed banquet hall.

Useful information for guests

It will be useful for guests to read the following information regarding the hotel:

  • the registration procedure for new arrivals starts after two in the afternoon;
  • on the day of departure before noon you must leave your room and hand in the key to the reception;
  • one child in an extra bed is free;
  • a folding bed for a second child or an adult guest will cost 1200 rubles per day;
  • for an additional fee you can stay with your pets at the Park Inn Hotel (Sochi); telephone for information clarification +7 (862) 296 82 28;
  • registration of children is possible only after presenting a birth certificate;




  • foreign citizens are required to present not only a passport, but also a valid migration card upon arrival;
  • the minibar is replenished absolutely free for guests staying in deluxe rooms;
  • It is worth noting that a number of points that are indicated by you in the column "Special wishes" may require an additional payment.

Positive reviews

Many tourists prefer the Park Inn resort hotel (Sochi). Guest reviews mark a number of positive aspects of this accommodation option:

  • a very convenient location both in terms of transport interchange, and in relation to resort infrastructure facilities;
  • good sound insulation between rooms;
  • pleasant and friendly staff that promptly responds to all your requests and wishes;
  • perfect cleanliness of the rooms;
  • Towels are changed every day during cleaning.

Negative reviews

Of the negative aspects of living in this hotel, the following can be distinguished:

  • near the station square, but because in the morning it is very noisy here;
  • the set of bath accessories does not include either shower caps or toothbrushes;
  • very small selection of products for breakfast;
  • uncomfortable hard pillows;




  • too weak water pressure in the bathroom (this problem is especially acute for the upper floors);
  • the hotel really lacks a pool, because it is located far enough from the sea.

General impression

It is worth noting that the Park Inn Hotel (address: 56/3 Gorky St., Sochi, Krasnodar Territory, Russia) is a rather attractive accommodation option. It offers beautiful and comfortable rooms, as well as qualified staff who will satisfy any of your whims. The assortment and quality of dishes in the restaurant deserves special attention. Nevertheless, there are some disadvantages here. The hotel is a considerable distance from the sea. However, there is no swimming pool. It is also worth noting the proximity of the train station, which is why in rooms with windows overlooking the square, it is quite noisy (especially in the mornings).
