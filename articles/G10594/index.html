Lush crochet flower: knitting pattern and description

In order to crochet voluminous lush flowers, you need to prepare:

  • Yarn of several types.

  • Gypsy knitting needle (there are plastic and metal).

All these materials and tools are needed to make a magnificent flower pattern. Crocheting to complete the decoration is not difficult. As you can see, there are not too many tools.

Description of work

We begin to knit a flower with lush crochet columns. The article will provide step-by-step instructions. The scheme and description of the lush flower crocheted, are of interest to many needlewomen.

First you need to form a small ring consisting of five air loops (if desired, the ring can be made larger by increasing the number of loops):

  1. The first circle is an air loop. We knit twelve single crochet.
  2. Now we take a thread of another color you like. The second circle is a connecting column or half-column in the first air loop. We knit four air loops.
  3. Now you need to make a crochet. Insert the hook into the same loop. Again we make a crochet and stretch this loop to the height of four loops.
  4. Repeat this pattern again. Then we do it with the next two loops.
  5. Then we double crochet and stretch the loop through all the loops made. So we made the first petal. Then we make three air loops and a connecting column.
  6. We repeat the work according to this pattern and knit another five petals. We fix the thread and hide it.

A lush flower with volumetric petals is ready.

How to crochet a rose: description of the scheme

A rose is the queen of all colors. It attracts us with its beauty and aroma. But everyone knows perfectly well that any flower cannot always please with its color. And therefore, you can tie such a beauty yourself.

So let's get started. Initially, you need to dial an arbitrary number of air loops with a hook, but not less than a hundred.

How to make lush flowers crochet? The knitting pattern of roses is described below:

  • Row 1 - Work begins with six lift air loops. Next - four air loops, two double crochets with twelve loops, two air loops. Then we skip four loops of the main chain. In the fifth loop, knit two double crochet, four air loops, two double crochet. And repeat this until the end of the row.
  • Row 2 - begins with six lifting loops. In an arch of four air loops of the previous row you need to knit: two double crochets, four air loops, four double crochets. Knit this way to the end of the row.
  • Row 3 - take a thread of a different color. We start with six air lifting loops. In the first arch of four air loops we knit six double crochet. In the next eight arches consisting of four air loops, knit twelve double crochets. And the other eight arches - knit sixteen columns with a crochet. There are twelve arches left. In them we knit twenty double crochets. At the end, the thread should be fastened and cut.

Now you need to neatly fasten all layers from the wrong side and sew.

That's all! Rose is ready. Now it can be attached to a hair clip, to a purse or dress as a decoration. Or attach a leg to it and put in a vase.

We knit a camomile. Description of work

Daisies are very lovely and delicate flowers. And it is very nice to look at their unique buds. Of course, I want a bouquet of daisies to please us in the cold season.

Description of work is given below:

  1. First you need to make an air loop and fix it. Then we collect a chain of twenty air loops. They should be connected in a circle. After you need to knit all the petals in a circle.
  2. For a petal, we collect a chain of eighteen air loops. Then in the second loop of the chain we knit two half columns. The next thing we do is knit another ten single crochet stitches. The two stitches that are left need to be knitted as follows: the next is knitted with a half-column, and the second with a single crochet. The resulting petal is fixed in a circle of ten loops.
  3. We knit the rest of the petals in the same way as the first. After we finished work, we proceed to the middle.
  4. In order to make the middle, you need to dial an air loop. Then we knit four air loops. After, in the first loop, we knit twelve single crochet columns. Closed the ring. The first row is ready.
  5. In the second row of the middle, we knit four more columns in each of the twelve columns. It should be 24.
  6. In the third row, you need to add loops through one single crochet. It should be eighteen loops. We cut the thread. Sew the middle to the finished petals.

Chamomile is ready!

Lush crocheted sunflower. Description and scheme of work

Sunflower is a perky flower, bright and sunny. And it can be connected very easily.

Such work will bring only pleasure and joy to others. How to crochet a lush flower? The scheme is presented below.

For work, you need to use a hook of 1.6 mm. The threads should be thick. This is necessary so that the knitting is tight and does not stretch.

To tie the petal, you need to dial six air loops and knit four rows with a single crochet.

In the fifth row, you need to add one column on both sides and knit four more rows.

In the last row in the center you need to knit three crochet columns: the first with one, the second with two, the third with one. Thanks to this method, the top of the leaf will be pointed.

At the end of the work, the petal itself must be tied along the contour with a single crochet.

The first petal is ready. But the work is not over yet. Such petals need eleven more. That is, there should be twelve in all.

Such petals will be arranged in a circle in two rows. But first you need to connect the two central circles. They will keep the petals.

Now we sew the petals to the finished mug - one on four sides. And then everyone else.

The sunflower is almost ready. Now you need to sew the second middle (circle). You just need to leave a small hole. In this slot you need to put a synthetic winterizer, and then sew it up. Thanks to this, our sunflower will be voluminous.

If desired, you can attach a leg and put in a pot. The sunflower is ready!

Crocheted cloves: diagram and description

Everyone knows that on May 9th it is customary to give cloves to veterans. But what if such a gift lasts forever and never fades? Probably many will agree that this is a very good option.

The flower itself is knitted in one piece. First you need to dial nine air loops. After closing them in a ring. Next, make three lifting loops. They need to be performed at the beginning of each row instead of one double crochet.

Next, you need to connect twenty columns with a crochet inside the ring. It turns out only 21. It is necessary to knit one connecting column in the third loop of lifting. Thus, each row should be completed.

The next row must be knitted according to the same pattern. Only need to increase the number of loops three times.

After you need to tie another six rows and in each should make an increase. Thanks to this, the flower will grow rapidly.

For the stem, take a green thread and a stick (old pencil, tube, and so on). Lubricate with glue and carefully wrap the stick with thread. At the end, fix the thread with glue. The stalk is ready.

Next, you need to make a sepal. For him, we make a sliding loop and after knitting eight crochet stitches. In the next row, you need to make additions through one or two loops.

The next row goes without increments. In the third row, we increase through two loops. And we simply knit two rows. Next, you need to connect the stem and sepals, and also attach the bud itself. The clove is ready. If desired, you can make a whole bouquet.

How to tie a tulip. Description of work

Tulip is a flower of spring. Bouquets of tulips are always presented to girls for the holiday of March 8. But what if such a flower never fades?

Such a flower can be connected very simply and quickly. To do this, make a sliding loop. You need to knit eight single crochet into it. In the next three rows, six increments must be made. In this way we increase the future tulip bud.

After you need to knit ten or eleven rows (the more rows, the longer the bud) without increments.

The finished part must be filled with padding polyester. And the more you put it, the denser the bud will be. In order to form a bud, you need to sew four sides of the part with the thread with which you knitted it. The bud is ready! It remains only to attach a leg and a leaf.

Knit a lily. Scheme and Description

Everyone knows that a lily is a very delicate and pleasant flower. Each needlewoman can tie it. And not just as a gift, but also as a hair ornament.

To tie a flower, you need to dial a chain of crochets of twenty loops. In the first row, we begin to knit from the third loop from the hook. We collect one loop with a single crochet, four loops with a double crochet, nine loops with a double crochet, three loops with a crochet, one single crochet.

In the second row, we turn the work and continue: one air loop, two single crochet, two double crochet, four double crochet, three double crochet, two double crochet, two single crochet, the last but one loop air chain.

In the third row we knit in a circle. Two double crochet stitches, two double crochet stitches, three double crochet stitches, four double crochet stitches, two double crochet stitches, two double crochet stitches, three single crochet stitches In one flower - six petals, for each tie the right amount.

In order for the petals to get the desired shape, a thin wire should be passed along their edges. Next, you need to collect all the petals in a bud, while leaving a small hole for the stamens.

For stamens, you need to take a thin wire. Its length should be approximately 25 centimeters. We divide the wire into the desired amount (about five) and wrap it with thread.

We collect all the parts of the bud into a single whole, form. Lily is ready!

Crocheted chrysanthemum

Knitted chrysanthemum can be used as a brooch for clothes or as a key chain for keys, a handbag and so on.

In order to make it, you need to tie a chain of air loops about fifty to sixty centimeters long. Next, we knit aerial arches, consisting of six loops, while missing three loops.

After, on each arch of three air loops, you need to connect three petals. You should do about ten to fifteen loops.

After the petals are tied and the thread is fixed, you need to twist the arches so that a flower is obtained. Sew it with a needle and thread.

Optionally, you can tie the leaves. Chrysanthemum is ready!
