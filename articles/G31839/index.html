Description of the recreation center "Bonfire" (Penza)

Just five minutes drive from Penza there is a recreation center "Bonfire". Despite the small distance from the city, a forest stretches around the camp site, allowing you to relax from the noise surrounded by relaxing sounds of nature.

brief information





The recreation center "Bonfire" (Penza) is a place to relax with family or friends. On the territory there are several houses, as well as barbecue facilities, gazebos, various entertainments. In addition to traditional gazebos with barbecue grills, they offer to enjoy a sauna and a swimming pool. Also at the recreation center "Bonfire" constantly organize various holidays.

Accommodation options





All guests of the campfire recreation center (Penza) are offered to stay in one of the houses:

  • "Terem." It is a large three-story house that can accommodate up to 30 people. There are two lounges with double beds, a living room with a TV and stereo system, a bathroom, as well as a sauna. Around the house there is a well-equipped porch, where there is a barbecue and a large table with chairs. Inside the house there is still a clair with water and a small kitchen area with a microwave and refrigerator.
  • VIP-house "Beatrice." This is a small house for a romantic weekend. It contains a bedroom with a double bed, a living room with a soft sofa, armchairs, a coffee table, a TV and a decorative fireplace, as well as a bathroom with a luxurious bath and shower. There is also a small kitchen area with a microwave and a set of dishes. On the veranda there is a barbecue.

Prices at the recreation center "Bonfire" (Penza) for the rental of houses start at 8,000 rubles per day. This is the cost of accommodation in the Beatrice Cottage. Renting a Teremok house with a company of up to 20 people will cost 16,000 rubles per day.

Services and entertainment available





The following services are available to vacationers:

  • free parking;
  • sports entertainment;
  • organization of celebrations;
  • rental of gazebos with barbecue facilities;
  • bath and sauna;
  • sports equipment rental;
  • nutrition.

The campfire recreation center (Penza) offers its guests the following types of entertainment:

  • paintball;
  • football and volleyball matches;
  • table tennis;
  • walks in the woods;
  • dance floor with mirrored walls;
  • a playground.

Also, vacationers can visit the house "Raus", where there is a sauna and a swimming pool with hydromassage. Around the cottage there is a furnished terrace. This house can comfortably accommodate up to 25 people at a time.

Teremok Cottage has a sauna with aromatic oils, a banquet room and karaoke. In the house "Razdolie" in addition to the bath there are two pools: with cold and warm water.

Throughout the camp "Koster" are wooden arbors with barbecue. They provide everything you need for a comfortable stay: skewers, a sink for washing hands, lighting, sockets. There are six arbors. They are designed for vacation companies from 10 to 80 people.

The administration of the recreation center also offers its services in organizing various celebrations and events (for example, weddings, birthdays, anniversaries, corporate parties and so on) for up to 100 guests. This can be done in the form of a banquet or buffet.

A pleasant bonus for holidaymakers is that they cook delicious dishes at the Koster recreation center. So, guests have the opportunity to order pilaf or hodgepodge for a company of up to 25 people or more.

Reviews about the recreation center "Bonfire" (Penza)



We list the main advantages and disadvantages of this place, noted vacationers:

  1. The base has a very good location.
  2. The territory is well-groomed and clean, a large number of garbage cans are provided to maintain order.
  3. A great place to relax or holiday, designed for no more than one day.
  4. Poor water supply. It flows either cold or very hot. The pressure is very weak.
  5. The Beatrice Cottage can actually accommodate a company of four. Although on the site of the recreation center it is indicated that the house is designed for six.
  6. It is pleasant to spend holidays organized by the administration of the recreation center (for example, Shrovetide, New Year's Eve and so on). Here, they not only hire a host, but also organize various contests with raffle of valuable prizes. There is also a great banquet.
  7. Administrators help to realize any idea. For example, organize a romantic evening with a soulmate (turn on music, prepare champagne and fruits, decorate the house with rose petals).
  8. Pleased with the opportunity to order food on site. Pilaf and hodgepodge are delicious.
  9. Pools, steam rooms, saunas - everything is clean.
  10. All the interiors of the houses are thought out to the smallest detail. The rooms are made in the same style and do not contain anything superfluous.
  11. If necessary, at the base they give out for free barbecue utensils: barbecue, dishes, cauldrons, skewers.
  12. Coal, firewood and ignition can be bought on site.
  13. There is a separate playground for children. Animators also work.

Location

There is a tourist camp "Fire" at the address: Penza city, Akhunsky crossing, 1. You can get there by private car along the road leading to the village of Zarechny. This may be Cordon Groundhog Street or Neutral.
