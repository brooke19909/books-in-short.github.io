"Stalfond": reviews of grateful investors

Just a couple of decades ago, not all young people thought about their own future - pensions. But today, when state reforms contradict each other, it is almost impossible to hope for social security of the state.

Therefore, many in recent years have invested in the "Stalfond", reviews of which testify to the reliability of this private pension fund.

Currently, in Russia the NPF market has great prospects for development, therefore, today the number of organizations of this type is growing rapidly. However, as in any other market segment, customers most often prefer organizations that are already time-tested, such as NPF Stalfond. Reviews (for fifteen years of existence) about this organization are better than any advertising campaign, as they are based on real indicators and opinions of the society. Indeed, according to data released at conferences, Stalfond NPF has steadily increased its annual income over the past ten years. And the opening of new representative offices in many cities of the Russian Federation indicates that this non-state pension fund does not stop at the achieved results, but develops and expands the boundaries of its activities.



It is also worth noting that as early as fifteen years ago, when the Stalfond NPF was founded, only participants in the pension program of the Severstal industrial group of enterprises could give feedback. Today, anyone who thinks of tomorrow can be the client of this fund.

In addition to the gratitude of the depositors, Stalfond earned NPF reviews and partner companies that help to increase the welfare of customers. By choosing only organizations with an impeccable reputation and a high level of reliability for cooperation, this pension fund was able to significantly increase the amount of invested funds of depositors.

The same clients who are willing not only to receive a stable income from investing their pension, but also wish to receive additional financial resources, NPF Stalfond offers closer cooperation. So, any investor can become an agent of this fund to attract customers. For this, the company conducts many trainings on the regulatory and legislative framework of pension provision so that each employee can provide advice at the proper level. This opportunity has allowed many people to secure a decent level of income today, as evidenced by the "Stalfond" reviews.



Deputies and doctors, directors and teachers, builders and engineers ... All of these people with different income levels and social status have one thing in common - they laid a reliable foundation for their secured old age today, becoming a client of Stalfond. Reviews of these people about many years of cooperation speak only about one thing - they took the right step to their own well-being. Perhaps it is worth following their example?
