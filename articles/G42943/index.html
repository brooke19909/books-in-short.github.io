How to reset iPhone to factory settings using iTunes?

Apple, an American company, takes a leading position in the mobile phone market, but still plays a significant role in this sector. For the sake of fairness, this manufacturer releases a considerable number of high-quality devices on the world stage that fully justify their exorbitant cost. This is primarily about optimized software and features of the operating system. The architecture of these two areas (let's call them that) is built in such a way that Apple devices can compete with devices that are more powerful in terms of filling. But no matter how simplified the operating system, but not everyone understands it. Therefore, it is often possible to meet user questions about how to reset an iPhone to factory settings. We will talk about this problem (can it be called that?) And about solutions in this article.

The reasons leading to the need for factory “reset”





Many users who are wondering how to reset the iPhone to factory settings will probably decide to do this due to software failures. There are some other reasons that we will not talk about nonetheless. But what each owner of the device running the IOS operating system should understand is that, having reset to the factory settings, he will lose absolutely all the information stored on the device before this operation. We are talking, in particular, about the contacts stored on the smartphone, about multimedia files and similar materials. Therefore, before you are interested in the question “how to reset iPhone to factory settings”, it is better to take a look at how to make backup copies of files. This will help you avoid losing important data forever. Meanwhile, we move on to the story of how to reset the iPhone to factory settings.

Using iTunes





Probably not all users who ask how to reset iPhone 5 to the factory settings know that for this you can use the standard "iTunes". It is on the use of this service that one of the methods for returning the device to its factory settings is based. Its advantage is that in a similar way to the recovery point you can return any device manufactured by Apple, whether it is an iPod or an iPad. Once again, we remind the user that during the procedure of returning to the factory settings, all the contents of the device will be deleted without the possibility of subsequent restoration. The black list includes all types of multimedia files, as well as phone contacts and calendar data.

How to reset iPhone 4S to factory settings using "iTunes". Sequencing





Initially, the device owner must make sure that the service has been updated in advance to the latest version. If this is not so, then we strongly recommend updating “iTunes” before the process of returning the device to the factory settings is activated. The next step is to back up the files. They will need to be created and transferred to a personal computer, after synchronizing the operation of these two devices with each other. If there is nothing to “save” from the device, then you can simply skip this step. Next, you will need to disable something in the engineering menu. More specifically, deactivation must be applied to the Find iPhone feature. Actually, the preparatory part of the operation is over.

Return to default settings

To complete the action, connect the iPhone to a personal computer. After displaying the device in the “iTunes” program, we go to the tab called “Overview”. There we click on “Restore”. It remains to confirm that you agree to the activation of the restoration of the factory settings and give the green light to completely delete the data available on the smartphone. Next, the operation will continue, and the user just needs to wait until the device reboots.
