Paradise for a skier is very close - the resort of Abzakovo.

The location characterized by the ski resort of Abzakovo is the eastern slopes of one of the most picturesque southern Ural ranges, namely Krykty-Tau. This place is located near the Shaitan Mountain, whose height reaches 820 m, 60 km from the city of Magnitogorsk, as well as 3 km from the Novo-Abzakovo station. For many years, the resort of Abzakovo has been a favorite place for residents of Magnitogorsk to relax, but in recent years it has become so popular that it can be considered a resort of the all-Russian level.
