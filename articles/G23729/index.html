How to get from Milan to Verona: an overview of the most popular modes of transport

Milan and Verona are Italian cities, the distance between which can be covered by train or bus. How to get from Milan to Verona, choosing the most convenient form of transport? To do this, you need to know the advantages and disadvantages of all possible methods.

A bit about cities

Milan is the capital of northern Italy. This city attracts tourists from all over the world. It is considered the most expensive city in Italy and practically in no way inferior to London and Paris. In Milan, there are fashion boutiques, business centers, amazing sights. You should definitely visit the Sforza castle and the church of Santa Maria delle Grazia, which is decorated with the fresco of Leonardo da Vinci "The Last Supper". Milan is a symbol of modern Italy.

Verona, on the other hand, is a town that gained world fame thanks to the tragedy of the brilliant poet and playwright William Shakespeare "Romeo and Juliet." The historic center of Verona is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The city is famous for its rich history, bewitching sights and ancient churches. All this awaits you on the Milan - Verona route. How to get from one city to another?

By bus FlixBus

Give preference to this vehicle if you want to save. The cheapest trip will be on a FlixBus bus, it will cost 9.9 euros (about 730 rubles). Tourists have the opportunity to choose the most convenient flights, as buses run daily from dawn until the evening. Routes are direct, which means you don‚Äôt have to make transfers in other Italian cities.

How to get from Milan to Verona by FlixBus bus? To do this, you need to come to Lampugnano Station and purchase tickets. After 2 hours you will find yourself in Verona. Transport arrives at a stop called Porta Nuova. All routes except one are direct.

You can take a bus at the airport of Milan. The transport leaves at 11:55 from Malpensa, and arrives in Verona at 18:45. In addition, you will find a transplant in a city called Modena. It takes no more than 1 hour. The cost of such a flight is 21 euros (about 1,500 rubles).

By bus Baltour

Another popular way to get from Milan to Verona is by Baltour bus. Direct flights are operated several times a week. Transport departs from Milano Lampugnano at 7:15 and arrives in Verona after almost 4 hours. If you prefer a route with a change in Bologna, then the travel time will be about 7 hours. The cost of a ticket for a Baltour bus reaches 19 euros (1400 rubles) or more. Club card holders can get a small discount.

On the bus "Eurolines"

When advising how to get from Milan to Verona, experienced tourists note the convenience of Euroline buses. This transport is equipped with a multimedia system, air conditioning. There is a lot of space near the seats, so you can stretch your legs if you wish. The backs are comfortable, they can be adjusted. Buses depart from Bus Station Lampugnano at 7:15, and arrive in Verona, at Dietro Chiesa, at 11:15 the same day. The ticket will cost 15 euros (1100 rubles).

By train

This type of transport is the most popular in Italy. He is preferred because of speed and comfort. The route Milan - Verona daily runs at least 50 trains. In Milan there is a central train station called Milano Centrale, from where the movement begins. Arrives transport to Porta Nuova Station, a 15-minute walk from Verona city center.

By train Frecciarossa

There are several ways to get from Milan to Verona by train. The fastest way you will overcome the route on the transport company Frecciarossa. Travel time by high-speed train is about 1 hour, as the vehicle accelerates to 200 km / h. Since flights are made several times a day, it will not be difficult to choose the most convenient departure time. Trains run about 2 times per hour - from 6:45 to 20:45. A ticket can be purchased for 14.9 euros (1100 rubles) at a fare called Super Economy. At the base rate, you will have to pay from 25 euros (about 1850 rubles) to get on high-speed transport.

By train Regionale

If you have free time, you can save a little and take a trip on a regional train, which is on the way for about 2 hours. Electric trains run daily from 6:25 to 00:15. They depart from the Milano Centrale platform once an hour. Depending on the class of car, the ticket price will be 12.75 or 18.9 euros (942-1400 rubles, respectively)

Since the distance between the cities is small, you can overcome it in an hour and a half. The advantage of traveling by car is the ability to stop at picnics, visit interesting places and change the route depending on your wishes.

By plane

How to get from Milan to Verona? You can use the services of a local airline called Alitalia. Travel time is about 3.5 hours, including a transfer in Rome. It takes 1 hour 15 minutes. Airplanes depart from Linate and arrive at Villafranca Airport. To purchase a ticket, you must pay 128 euros (approximately 9500 rubles).

Thus, going on a trip to Italy, you can choose the most convenient way to travel around the country. Among the available means of transport are cars and buses, trains and planes. Each of them has advantages and disadvantages, so when choosing, you need to focus on your wishes and, of course, the budget.
