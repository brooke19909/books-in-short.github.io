Dinskaya stanitsa of the Krasnodar Territory: description, population, schools

Krasnodar Territory is one of the most prosperous regions of our country. Krasnodar is considered the economic and tourist center of Russia. It is this fertile land that an increasing number of people choose for permanent residence. There are many developed comfortable farms and villages. One of the most promising and developing is considered the village of Dinskaya in the Krasnodar Territory. It is about her that this article will be discussed.

A bit of history

This territory - Don Kuren - was granted to the Kuban Cossacks in 1794 by Catherine II, after the defeat on the land of the Kuban Zaporizhzhya section. Over time, the kuren began to be called Dinskaya, and in 1842 this territory became known as the Dinskaya village.





A lot of tests fell on the Dynch. They survived the revolution, collectivization, civil and domestic war. In 1942, in August, the village was occupied by German invaders. In February 1943, the Soviet troops expelled the Nazis from these lands. In memory of the fallen soldiers, a memorial was created with the names of 253 fallen Dincans. The T-34 tank was installed as a tribute to the soldiers-liberators during the Second World War.

Immediately after the liberation of the village, the population began to restore the national economy. In the middle of the 20th century, many different enterprises were created in the village of Dinskaya: a bakery, factories (sugar and canneries), a confectionery factory, and ATP. Out of several small collective farms, one large farm was created. Several new schools, a book house, and a department store were built.

Dinskaya village of Krasnodar Territory: description. Location

Consider where this village is located, what infrastructure and population it has.

Dinskaya is located at a distance of 30 kilometers northeast of the regional capital - the city of Krasnodar, on the banks of the Kochet River. The village is the administrative municipal center of the Dinsky district. The postal code for Dinskaya is 353200.





Message

Not more than 4 kilometers from the village is the federal highway M-4 Don. There are also two highways of regional significance - Krasnodar - Yeysk and Temryuk - Krasnodar - Kropotkin. There is also a railway connection (through the Dinskoy district).

Population

In 1959, the population of the village was only 11,180 people. Currently (according to 2016), the population of the village of Dinskaya is 36 thousand inhabitants. About 90% of the inhabitants of the village are Russians; there are Armenians and Ukrainians here. Due to the proximity to Krasnodar and the developed infrastructure, the village is chosen for permanent residence by immigrants from other regions who want to improve their climatic conditions.





Dinskaya village: work

The main part of the economy of the region is agriculture, as well as the processing of agricultural products. In the Dinsky district, livestock and poultry farming are well developed. In addition, the following crops are cultivated here: corn, sunflower, cereals, sugar beets. Such enterprises as the Yuzhnaya Zvezda confectionery factory, sugar and canneries, and the Dinskaya meat-packing factory operate and produce products that are known throughout the country.





Among industrial enterprises, the following can be noted:

  • "Yugtruboplast";
  • Krasnodar Compressor Plant;
  • building materials plant;
  • "Rosslav".

A specialist of any qualification will be able to easily find work in any of these enterprises. You can get acquainted with the vacancies on the official website of the Dinsky district employment center.

Educational institutions. Kindergarten

In the village of Dinskaya there are public and private kindergartens, four schools, and a technical school.

As for any area, a kindergarten in Dinskaya is a necessary institution. Due to the fact that during the perestroika period many gardens were closed and reconstructed, and the birth rate, on the contrary, rose, today there is a deficit of this structure. In the village there is a kindergarten No. 4 MBDOU. Located on Mira Street 4.
