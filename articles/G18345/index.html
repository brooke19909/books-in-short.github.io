Pension "Breeze Deluxe", Zatoka: photos, reviews

How to spend a summer vacation? Worth to go to the sea! And it is not necessary to go far abroad! Great scenic spots are very close by. A lot of good reviews can be heard about the Breeze Deluxe boarding house. Zatoka is famous for its neat beaches and fun activities for different categories of tourists.

Basic information about the base

Hotel "Breeze Deluxe" (Zatoka) appeared relatively recently. The cottage town was built only in 2013. Despite the short history, many people prefer to spend their summer holidays here. The hotel offers rooms that are suitable for families with young children, as well as for youthful noisy companies. The boarding house itself is located only 40 km from Odessa. In your free time you can visit famous sights.



Base "Breeze Deluxe" (Zatoka) is famous for its well-developed infrastructure and a large range of services. It is on the territory of the boarding house that the largest pool in Zatoka is located. Vacationers can have a great time near the hotel. A great alternative for those who do not want to spoil the condition of the skin and hair in salty sea water. Additionally, in the territory of the base there is a parking lot, a children's town, a gym, a restaurant and a bar. A medical center is open around the clock, where vacationers can be provided with qualified assistance in a timely manner.

Rooms and prices

You can book rooms for your stay in one of the two-story cottages or in the central building. It is possible to rent one-room or two-room apartments. The cost of the room will depend on the season. In June, the price of one person in a standard room without meals will be 1200 rubles. In July and August, 1800 rubles will have to be paid for the same conditions. The standard room can accommodate two adults. For a child over three years old, you will have to pay 650 rubles at any time.

The most popular are standard rooms in the central building located on the fourth floor. The windows offer a charming view of the huge swimming pool of an interesting shape, as well as the sea promenade.



For family holidays, two-room standard rooms are more suitable. The cost of such apartments in June will be 5500 rubles (for four adults). In July and August you will have to pay 8,000 rubles. Surcharge for a child, as in the case of a one-room number, will be 650 rubles.

Offers everything for a comfortable stay base "Breeze Deluxe" (Zatoka). Reviews show that the rooms are well maintained. Each of them has a plasma TV, comfortable beds, a safe, a minimum set of dishes, a bathroom. Guests have the opportunity to leave their car for free at the 24-hour guarded parking lot. Wi-Fi is available throughout.

For those who are used to relax with increased comfort, the Breeze Deluxe boarding house (Zatoka) is also suitable. Everyone has the opportunity to rent a two-story cottage with all amenities. Inside there is a kitchen-studio, a bathroom, two bedrooms, air conditioning, a safe, a washing machine. The balcony offers sea views. The daily rental of the cottage in June will be 7500 rubles. In July and August you will have to pay 10,500 rubles. The house is designed for the simultaneous residence of four people. For each child older than three years, 650 rubles must be paid.

All rooms, regardless of category, have daily cleaning and linen changes. Hot water is available around the clock. Children under 12 years old receive a discount of 20% of the daily price of apartments.

Nutrition

Few people want to cook their own food, coming to rest in the influx. Breeze Deluxe offers various solutions to the problem. On the territory there is a dining room where you can order three meals a day (cost - 500 rubles per day). It is possible to go only for breakfast (125 rubles), lunch (200 rubles) or dinner (180 rubles). In this case, the order should be pre-arranged in the evening.



A comprehensive meal in the restaurant "Castle", located in the boarding house, is another way to solve the problem. It is possible to choose a dish to taste. The work uses only high-quality products. Reviews show that the menu also contains special dishes for young children.

Recreation for adults

Summer vacation is not only about spending time on the beach. For adults, there are many different leisure opportunities. For those who are used to watching their figure all the time and staying in good shape, a gym will come to the rescue. It functions from early morning until late evening. There is also a tennis room on site. Not only professionals, but also amateurs can have a great time. Sports equipment is offered absolutely free of charge.

On the territory of the pension you can celebrate a birthday or other important event. The restaurant menu should be agreed in advance. Barbecue lovers have the opportunity to rent barbecue, skewers and other necessary accessories for an excellent holiday in the company of friends and relatives. Pension "Breeze Deluxe" (Zatoka) is a place where everyone feels at home.

Children's leisure

On the territory of the boarding house, everything is thought out as well for the safe rest of the kids. The bright children's town with slides and swings attracts attention. It will be interesting here for kids at any age. But to leave preschoolers unattended is still undesirable.



If parents need to leave for an event, you can use the services of a kids club. Here, under the supervision of educators and animators, the kids have a great time. They play in the fresh air, sculpt from plasticine, draw. It is possible to order a children's birthday at the restaurant "Castle".

Beach vacation

Be that as it may, summer vacation is associated primarily with the beach. The boarding house itself is located in close proximity to the promenade. The base has its own well-kept sandy beach. For an additional fee, anyone can rent a deck chair, a parasol, an air mattress. There is a cafe with ice cream and cold drinks on site.



The beach of the Breeze Deluxe boarding house (Zatoka) is a place for active recreation. A lot of entertainment is offered. You can ride a "banana", go down the water slide, jump on a trampoline, fly a glider over the waves.

Evening entertainment

How to spend a cozy summer evening in the resort town? Near the boarding house there are many entertainment venues that operate until late in the evening. Young people can visit one of the disco bars on the waterfront. There is also a karaoke club. On the seashore there are fun attractions that will give a lot of vivid emotions not only to children, but also to adults.

An interesting evening leisure offers to organize a boarding house "Breeze Deluxe" (Zatoka). Reviews show that you can have fun with your family and friends without leaving the base. In the evening, a special program with live music is offered to guests of the Zamok restaurant. Most of all, older tourists prefer this kind of vacation.

Excursions

The resort town offers a variety of spa programs. They will be useful for tourists of various age categories. The most popular is a sightseeing tour of Odessa. From Zatoka to the regional center can be reached in just 40-50 minutes on a comfortable bus. Tourists will be able to visit historical places, walk along the Potemkin Stairs, enjoy the beauty of the maritime station and take a lot of vivid photos.

Families with young children should definitely visit the Nemo Dolphinarium, which is also located in Odessa. Enchanting show of the European level will give a lot of unforgettable impressions. Young people can attend a romantic evening performance. The cost of tickets to the dolphinarium starts from 750 rubles. For an additional fee, you can swim or take pictures with dolphins.

Reservation

To be sure that your vacation will be held at a high level, you should pre-book a house or a room for a stay in a boarding house. This can be done by calling: +380 98 66 33 111. Calling is worth a month before the proposed vacation. To book a room, it is worth making a deposit, which is equal to the cost of two days of stay.



It should be prepared for the fact that during the settlement you will have to make a deposit in the amount of 1200 rubles. If the room is delivered in the same condition in which the guests received it, the money will be returned. Accommodation in rooms is carried out after 13:00, eviction - after 10:00.

Transfer

Unfortunately, they will not be able to get to Zatoka by direct train. Guests of the Breeze Deluxe boarding house are offered a comfortable transfer from Odessa. Employees meet tourists right next to the train car. A car for 6 passenger seats is offered. The cost of the service is 2000 rubles. It will be possible to get to the recreation center in 50-60 minutes.
