Cottage Village "Nikolin Klyuch": location, description, reviews

About one and a half thousand kilometers separate the capital of the Urals - Yekaterinburg - from the capital of Russia - Moscow. The harsh climate of the Ural Mountains does not suit everyone. Therefore, immigrants from other regions of the country are not so much seeking here. Due to the fact that Yekaterinburg is the largest industrial center, its residents increasingly prefer to move to housing outside the city in order to live in more favorable environmental conditions. Such an opportunity is offered by Nikolin Klyuch, a cottage village at some distance from the metropolis.

A bit about the city

Yekaterinburg takes its place in the top five largest cities in Russia in terms of population. More than one and a half million people chose him as their permanent residence. This is due in many respects to the large number of functioning industrial, manufacturing, manufacturing enterprises that provide jobs to thousands of citizens. Here are the headquarters of the country's largest industrial concerns. This is a city with a well-developed infrastructure, the ability to get from it to anywhere in Russia, but with a very harsh climate.

Location

The village of Nikolin Klyuch in Yekaterinburg is located at a distance of 25 kilometers from the city along the Chelyabinsk tract. A little further down the road is the village of Kashino. Immediately at the traffic intersection, turn right onto Pervomaiskaya Street. After about 400 meters, turn right into the cottage village. In total, a trip to Nikolina Klyuch takes no more than 20 minutes from the center of Yekaterinburg.

Infrastructure of the village

"Nikolin Klyuch" is located at a fairly close distance from Yekaterinburg. Therefore, residents, especially those who have their own cars, will not feel restrained in the opportunity to take advantage of the rich assortment of social infrastructure in the city. Those who are not car owners can easily reach the city by public transport: a bus that runs from the village of Kashino during the day with an interval of about 20 minutes.

In the village itself, a medical center and several retail and office premises are provided. The main infrastructure that residents of the village of Nikolin Klyuch can use is located in the neighboring village of Kashino. There is a school, a kindergarten, a clinic, a temple and shops.
