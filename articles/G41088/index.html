We have a rest for real: hotel Amc Azur Grand Resort 5 *

Egypt, Hurghada, the Red Sea coast! It is always sunny here, and many kilometers of sandy beaches are washed by the warm sea. In these parts, surfing and diving can be practiced at any time of the year. And thanks to the constant breeze blowing from the sea, and the dry Egyptian climate, the heat in these places is quite easily tolerated. Therefore, you can spend the whole day on the beach without harming your health. And this resort has about 150 hotels. Amc Azur Grand Resort 5 * is not among the last among them.





And this hotel is located almost on the seashore, not far from the city center and the airport. The complex covers an area of ​​12 thousand square meters and can simultaneously accommodate up to 1000 guests. And this completely new hotel, which began operating in 2010, has already managed to fall in love with tourists.

The 395 rooms at Amc Azur Grand Resort are housed in its main 5-story building. Of these, 19 belong to the category of Suite. These are 51.5 square rooms with a living room and a bedroom. The capacity of this room is 3 + 1 people. Another 25 rooms are Family Room. This room has a bedroom and a bathroom, and can also accommodate up to 4 people. Well, the rest of the hotel rooms are standard - Standard Room. They are small, with an area of ​​27.5 squares, accommodating up to 3 people.

All rooms are equipped with central air conditioning at a comfortable temperature. There is also a telephone and TV with 3 Russian channels. Each room has a safety deposit box and a minibar, which is filled daily with free water and soft drinks. There is Wi-Fi here, but it is paid. The nice thing is that all rooms have balconies, and that cleaning and linen change is done daily.

Amc Azur Grand Resort has a sandy beach. Its length is 200 meters. And you can enter the sea from a pontoon 350 meters long or on sand from the shore. Also here, hotel residents can use the shower, beach towels, sun loungers, umbrellas and mattresses for free.





Food system in hotel Amc Azur Grand Resort - All Inciusive. It includes lunches, breakfasts and buffet dinners. This system also includes free snacks, ice cream and Egyptian-made drinks served in the hotel bars. And tourists eat in the main restaurant, which seats 450 people. In addition to this restaurant, there are several more bars. This is a lobby bar, shisha bar, disco bar, another bar is located on the beach, and another by the pool.

Amc Azur Grand Resort has the most modern infrastructure. On its territory there is a disco, an amphitheater, a library, a TV room and a large (1000 sq. M.) Outdoor pool. There is also an exchanger, a shopping center, a laundry, a hairdresser, a first-aid post and a conference room. The main building of the complex itself is equipped with elevators (6 pieces), and there is paid Wi-Fi in the lobby.





Tourists are waiting for a variety of free entertainment. These are billiards and surfing, ping pong and beach volleyball, canoeing and darts. There is also a free fitness center, daily live music and animation in Russian. And for an extra charge, tourists can rent a car, visit the SPA-salon, ride a sailboat or a boat, and also parasail.

Amc Azur Grand Resort 5 has the most favorable reviews. Tourists like this new and beautiful hotel with excellent interior and food for every taste. In the evenings, a piano is played in the hotel lobby, and a violin and guitar are in the restaurant. The bars have a lot of free drinks: vodka, rum, gin, cognac, whiskey, 2 types of beer, 3 types of wine and many cocktails. In addition, there are nice staff and decent rooms.
