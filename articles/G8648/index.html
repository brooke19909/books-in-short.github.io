Hotel "Marina Mandarin" in Singapore: photos and reviews

Singapore is one of those cities where everything is the same. For example, the most modern, unusual and expensive entertainment complex is Sky Park, as well as the best zoo in the world, the most interesting incredibly enchanting music and light show, which uses fireworks and water jets ... This list can be continued for a long time - the range of entertainment is practically inexhaustible. It is designed for representatives of any age with different tastes and preferences.

And where can a tourist stay here who would like to immerse himself in an atmosphere of entertainment, extravaganza and celebration during his vacation? The number of hotels ready to provide comfortable rooms for vacationers is huge. One of them is Marina Mandarin. Singapore is located near the sea. Most hotels, including Marina Mandarin, were built as close to the shore as possible so that guests staying in them could enjoy breathtaking views of the sea and the city's panoramas.





Hotel Description

Hotel Marina Mandarin is the epitome of Singaporean hospitality, elegance and luxury. The location is the city center, so you can quickly get to any significant Singapore objects. Nearby is the exhibition center, which serves as the main venue for important congresses and meetings. Its name is Suntec Convention & Exhibition Center. Nearby is the largest shopping center Marina Square, and opposite - the workshop of fine arts Esplanade. Well, since visitors are usually interested in entertainment, they are also within easy reach - the Clarke Quay and Boat Quay areas are within walking distance. In 7-10 minutes you can walk to the Esplanade Theater, Ferris Wheel, Suntec City Convention Center, National Museum, Chinatown, City Hall Metro and many other beautiful sights and important sites of this wonderful city.

The institution occupies 21 floors, which housed 575 elegant rooms. The huge panoramic windows offer a delightful picture. The outlines of the city, the view of the river - this is what many prefer to admire from their comfortable rooms. But still, most guests want to see Marina Bay from the windows of the Marina Mandarin Hotel: this allows you to admire not only amazing sunsets, but also the reflection of city lights on the water surface at night.





Facility Rules

As in all hotel establishments, the hotel "Marina Mandarin" set the time of departure (12.00) and arrival (14.00). You can stay here with children of any age. Toddlers up to two years old who need a separate bed will stay free of charge. For older children, who usually sleep with their parents, you also do not need to pay. But if they need a separate place, they will have to pay SGD 80 per night. The maximum number of extra beds is one. It is provided only if a preliminary request has been made and the administration of the institution has confirmed that it will provide this service.

Payment will be required during your stay at the establishment. It is not included in the total price. Cards accepted: JCB, Diners Club, MasterCard, Visa, American Express.
