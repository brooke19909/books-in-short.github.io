DIY DIY crafts for the garden

Love for the earth is inherent in the Russian people at the genetic level. Therefore, everyone who has their own country house or cottage, tries with all his might to ennoble the land, to make it beautiful and unusual. Especially interesting are estates in which do-it- yourself crafts are used to decorate the territory. The flight of unbridled imagination pushes craftsmen to create such peculiar and interesting objects that can sometimes be called unique works of folk art.



DIY crafts for the garden are sometimes made from such waste materials that one can only be surprised at the skill and unbridled imagination of their creators. Simple improvised means, a minimum of financial costs and one creative and
deya - this is the standard recipe for every garden craft, about which, if you set a goal, you can publish a whole folio filled with amazing photographs, one more beautiful than the other.

What today do owners of unusual sites make original crafts with their own hands for the garden? Yes, what will come of Everything is used - from natural materials to various household waste. So, for example, many people are fond of crafts from already used plastic bottles. Well, folk craftsmen just don't make them: from the original flower pots to the personal oasis with palm trees.

And do-it-yourself crafts for the garden from old metal barrels that many hide in the farthest corners of the site or even bury it in the ground? A little imagination, a brush, multi-colored paints - and water for irrigation can be stored not in a rusty container, but in the original container that decorates the garden. Many craftsmen turn banal stones, which only clutter up the territory, into picturesque sculptures, and old used automobile tires into designer planters and swans.

Very interesting and original DIY crafts for the garden from ordinary mounting foam. Obedient to work, it allows you to create any sculptures that, after staining, have a beautiful and unusual look. Those happy homeowners who know how to work with wood create do-it-yourself crafts for the garden in a traditional style. These are all kinds of houses, carts, mills, benches, sculptures of fairy-tale heroes or animals. Folk craftsmen also do not ignore the vine, from which they build chic wattle-fence, all kinds of chests, flower pots and flower baskets.



The fantasy of DIY do-it-yourselfers is simply inexhaustible. Old rubber boots and boots, bicycle wheels, hay, straw, gypsum are used. Some even contrive to turn a banal garden scarecrow into a decoration of the site, while exotic butterflies fly in the garden of the most fierce followers of folk art, hedgehogs, hares, frogs sit under each bush or fairy-tale gnomes rest. As the saying goes, no matter what the craftsman amuses himself, the main thing is that do-it-yourself crafts for the garden please the envious neighborly look and the soul of the owner.
