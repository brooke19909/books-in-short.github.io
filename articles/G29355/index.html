Fly Era Style 3: specifications. Fly Era Style 3 - model overview

Fly Era Style 3 is one of the latest models from this manufacturer. Its technical specifications, parameters and characteristics are what will be discussed in this review. It appeared on sale in September this year and differs from its competitors in a fairly affordable cost.





CPU and its characteristics

At the heart of Fly Era Style 3 The MTK6582M single-chip system from the Chinese developer MediaTEC lies. It consists of four revision cores "A7", which in peak load mode operate at a clock frequency of 1.3 GHz. Of course, you can not expect unrivaled performance from him. But to solve most real problems, this is quite enough. If there is no load on the CPU, the clock frequency is reduced, idle cores are turned off. In principle, for a mid-range device, such a processor is more than enough. From this we can conclude that this smartphone model is focused on this particular niche. Based on this, we will consider the remaining parameters of Fly Era Style 3.





Graphics subsystem

The graphics subsystem is implemented using the Mali 400MP2 graphics adapter. This is another plus of Fly Era Style 3. Reviews of the new owners of this smartphone only confirm this statement. The most productive MTK solutions are equipped with such a graphics card (for example, MTK 6592 with 8 cores on board). Its performance is enough to solve a wide range of tasks. The screen size of this smartphone is four and a half inches and can handle up to five touches at the same time. At the same time, its resolution is 854 pixels in length and 480 in width. Its optical density is 228 PPI. Of course, I would like more resolution, but this will be enough for normal and comfortable work. The type of matrix used in this smart phone is IPS, which is capable of displaying more than 16 million colors. All this allows you to get a bright and rich picture on the screen of the gadget. In addition, this smartphone supports a special technology - OZHS. Its essence is that there is no air gap between the screen itself and the sensor. This allows you to significantly improve the quality of the picture. This technology so far only works in more expensive smartphones, and in budget devices it can be found only in this model.

But this can be understood by looking at the technical characteristics of this gadget. First of all, you need to allocate RAM, which is installed here only 512 megabytes. Camera

Phone Fly Era Style 3 is equipped with two cameras. One of them - in 5 megapixels - is located on the back cover of the device. It also has an LED flash for night shooting. For higher quality, you can use the autofocus technology supported by this unit. This is enough to get high-quality images. It also supports high-resolution video recording - 1920 pixels by 1080 pixels. The second camera - 0.3 megapixels - is designed to make video calls in networks of the third or fourth generation. Another possible way to use it is to communicate using special programs (say, skype).





Connection

A rich set of communications from Fly Era Style 3. Reviews only confirm this. First of all, it is worth highlighting Wi-Fi, which allows you to very quickly receive data from the global web. To connect other mobile devices of this class in this smartphone is integrated bluetooth. To charge the battery, as well as to connect to a PC, a standard USB connector is used for most of these devices. There is also a 3.5 mm jack for connecting mobile speakers or headphones. In addition to this, a GPS navigation system is supported - its transmitter is installed. If necessary, you can use the A-GPS system, which is also supported by this device, to more accurately determine the location. Another plus of this smartphone is support for third-generation networks. With the appropriate coverage, the data exchange rate grows significantly. A definite disadvantage is the lack of an infrared transmitter. But they are so rarely used in practice that this can not be called a serious problem.

Battery

Another weak link is the battery of the Fly Era Style 3. Overview without the characteristics of this component will not be complete. The battery capacity in this device is only 1650 milliamps / hours. With a minimum load on the phone, this is enough for 2 days of work. But in case of intensive use it can be stretched for 6 hours. It is unlikely that the owners of this gadget will load it so much. Therefore, this cannot be considered a significant drawback.





Housing

In the lower part there are three key buttons: the menu, the main screen and the previous window. At the top are the headphone jacks and the charging socket (you can also connect to a PC with it). The second, for conversation, is located above the touch screen. At the bottom is a small microphone hole. Three body colors are available: white, black and blue.

Software

From the position of the operating system, it compares favorably with competitors of Fly Era Style 3. Summary

In addition to the small amount of RAM, there are no more serious flaws in Fly Era Style 3. Price while it is today a little less than five thousand rubles.
