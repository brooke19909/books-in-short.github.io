Network failure

If "iPhone 4" does not catch the network (like any other gadget), it is likely that the reason for this was a malfunction in the mobile operator. That is, it is he who has a failure. Accordingly, all subscribers will not be able to use the connection.

Viruses

Do you have a network search on your iPhone constantly? Try to analyze your past actions. If previously the connection worked fine, but then suddenly it suddenly began to fail, then something went wrong.
