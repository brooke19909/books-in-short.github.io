Short biography of Vasily Stalin

A very interesting figure is the youngest son of the leader of the peoples, Vasily Stalin. Biography, photos of him are available for review in this publication. Vasily was born in 1921 in Moscow. His mother was Nadezhda Alliluyeva. After her suicide, he was brought up by the head of the Stalinist guard Vlasik. Biography of Vasily Stalin says that he graduated from the Crimean Kachin aviation school, and then Lipetsk higher aviation courses.

Biography of Vasily Stalin tells us that he was a member of the Second World War. As a pilot, he made 27 sorties and shot down two German aircraft. He started the war in the captain's rank. For a while, Vasily was an inspector of the Red Army aviation. In 1943 he was appointed commander of an air regiment. In May of that year, Vasily was removed from office on the orders of his father for "revelry and drunkenness." The biography of Vasily Stalin says that in 1944-1945 he commanded first one and then another air division. During the Second World War he was awarded the orders of Suvorov, the Red Banner (twice), Alexander Nevsky. He headed the equestrian federation of the Union. With his light hand, projects for the construction of equestrian institutions and bases were approved, and the best breeds of racehorses were also bred.
