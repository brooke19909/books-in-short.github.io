Hotel Basilica Holiday Resort 3 * (Cyprus): photos and reviews of tourists, rooms, services, location

Paphos is a city that is famous for its ancient traditions, a huge number of historical attractions, as well as a mild climate and warm sea. Therefore, it is not surprising that every year thousands of tourists visit the city. Naturally, all travelers are warmly welcomed here, providing them with all the conditions for a decent vacation. Quite popular today is the hotel complex Basilica Holiday Resort 3 *. Where is the hotel situated? What living conditions await tourists? Is it possible to relax here with the whole family? The answers to these questions will be of interest to many readers on vacation.

Where is the hotel situated?





The location of the hotel complex Basilica Holiday Resort 3 * - Paphos, one of the most popular and famous cities of the country. The hotel is famous for its advantageous location. You can get to the city center by taxi or bus (by the way, the bus stop is just a few meters from the hotel). The beach is only 250 meters away. The nearest international airport is 10 km away, which is also quite convenient. Hotel Basilica Holiday Resort 3 *: photo and description of rooms





Of course, part of a good beach holiday is comfort. So what rooms does the hotel complex Basilica Holiday Resort 3 * offer? Cyprus, Paphos - an excellent place, which is famous for its hospitality. And in this hotel you will find not only a warm welcome, but also spacious, comfortable rooms of various categories. In total there are 105 rooms. Each standard room consists of a small living room, kitchenette, as well as one, two or three bedrooms. There are also double studios - rooms with a high level of comfort.

Regardless of the category, these rooms are perfect for relaxation. There is access to a terrace or balcony, from where you can admire the garden, as well as views of the pool or the city. The floors are covered with ceramic tiles, the furniture is in good condition. From household appliances in the room there is a TV (some music and one Russian channel are shown here), as well as a telephone and air conditioning. The kitchen area has a microwave and electric kettle, as well as a set of dishes.

The bathroom is quite spacious and equipped with everything necessary. There is a shower, bathtub, sink, mirror, as well as a set of personal hygiene products and a set of clean towels. Here you will find a hair dryer.

It is worth noting that the rooms on the territory of the Basilica Holiday Resort 3 * are in perfect condition - they are cleaned daily, and the maids are honest.

Meal plan for hotel guests





Tourists who are going to relax on the territory of the Basilica Holiday Resort 3 * hotel are offered a choice of several meal plans - either BB (breakfast), HB (breakfast and dinner), as well as all-inclusive (three meals a day, light snacks, and drinks from a local manufacturer).

Three meals a day are served in the main restaurant. According to reviews, the food here is quite diverse - snacks, side dishes, meat, vegetables and fish dishes, as well as a large number of desserts and fresh fruits. By the way, if you paid only for breakfast, you will still have the opportunity to have dinner or lunch at a restaurant for a relatively low cost.

The lobby bar serves drinks, as well as grilled snacks and dishes. In addition, on the promenade, as in the city itself, there are lots of restaurants, cafes and bars, so you are sure to pick something up to your liking. And next to the hotel there is a supermarket where you can also buy groceries.

Beach and water activities





As already mentioned, the beach is located just 250 meters from the hotel - it is easily accessible on foot. This place is intended for public use. By the way, almost all the beaches in Paphos are municipal - only five-star luxury hotels have their own, private sections of the coast.

The coast is rocky, but the descent into the sea is quite convenient. On the coastal shallows children can swim. There are sun loungers and umbrellas on the beach - you have to pay for the rental, but it costs not so much. Of course, everyone will be able to ride catamarans, water skiing, a boat, a boat, etc.

Additional service for tourists

The hotel complex Basilica Holiday Resort 3 * is a great place for a simple but comfortable stay. Of course, the hotel offers some services that will make your stay here more convenient.

To begin with, it is worth saying that there is its own currency exchange point. Dry cleaning and laundry are constantly working, which is sometimes very convenient. There is a small parking with security. By the way, if you do not have your own transport, then in the city you can easily rent a car, motorcycle or bicycle. Valuables can be deposited in the safe at reception. If you have health problems, contact one of the employees - they will call you a doctor, whose services are paid separately.

It has access to the Internet in public places of recreation, but for its use you need to pay extra.

Entertainment and leisure





The hotel has a fairly spacious pool around which umbrellas and sunbeds are placed - you can have a good time here, chat with friends, play water polo or simply take a sunbath.

There is also a room for billiards, table tennis. If you wish, you can relax in the jacuzzi and visit the local sauna. During the beach season, the hotel has animators who entertain guests throughout the day. And in the local amphitheater, exciting show programs are organized.

What else can the hotel Basilica Holiday Resort 3 * offer? Paphos is a city of legends and history. This place is perfect for those travelers who like to combine a beach holiday with various educational activities. At the hotel or with a tour operator you can buy a tour, make a travel route around the city. For example, not far from the hotel complex there are such bright sights as the Catacombs of St. Solomonia, the Ethnographic Museum, the Royal Tombs, as well as the Archaeological Museum of Paphos.

Are there any conditions for relaxing with children?

Many tourists wonder whether it is possible to take a child with them to Cyprus. 3 * Basilica Holiday Resort provides some services for parents traveling with children. To begin with, it is worth noting that staying at the hotel for kids up to two years old is completely free.





The hotel has a small but well-equipped playground with swings, where children can have fun in company with their parents. In addition, in the morning a children's club begins to work. Here, children will be entertained by animators and nannies - children will be able to spend time interestingly and profitably, doing drawing, needlework, sports, team games, etc.

If you suddenly need to leave your child for a short time, a professional nanny works at the hotel, who will sit with the baby for a relatively small fee, providing you with the necessary time.
