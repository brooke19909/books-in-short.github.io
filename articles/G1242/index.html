User manual

So that the washing machine does not break down over the entire service life and is characterized by high performance, it is important to follow the rules that are set out in the instructions.   • Only a service center specialist should install the machine.
  • It is important to use only original hoses and accessories for installation.
  • Do not place heavy objects on the equipment.
  • It is not recommended to open the container intended for powder during the washing process.
  • It should be remembered that at high washing temperatures the door may be hot.
  • If you open the door with the use of force, then an automatic sensor will fail, which monitors the closure density.
  • Cover the machine with plastic or other material. The technique must dry in natural conditions.

Tips for smart housewives

Quite economical, in terms of spending resources, is a narrow washing machine Haier HW60-BP12758. Reviews show that with its acquisition, you can significantly reduce payments for utilities. But so that the technique really justifies itself, the manufacturer gives some tips, which are also given in the instructions:

  • To improve the quality of washing, it is important to choose the right program and not exceed the amount of laundry. But do not use too much detergent.

Terms of Use

Before using the Haier HW60-BP12758 washing machine, the instructions for it must be studied. It is important not to forget to remove the transport bolts in order to avoid shaking the equipment and its breakdown. Next, the master sets up the machine and adjusts the legs for greater stability. To work, you must select the appropriate program. The designations of the buttons and their functionality will be considered further:

  • On off. Button to turn on and off the equipment.
  • I-time. You can manually select the desired washing time. However, the spin function does not apply this option.
  • Spin speed. Using this button you can set the desired spin level, even if the program provides for a slightly different one.
  • Rinsing. It will be needed for an additional cycle if the main one is not arranged for a number of reasons. Often used when washing children's things.

Sometimes, especially at night, an audible signal interferes with the sound at the end of the wash. It can be turned off. To do this, press the i-time button and simultaneously spin. Hold for 3 seconds to achieve the effect. To cancel the operation, all actions must be repeated.
