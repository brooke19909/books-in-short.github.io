Hotel Houda Golf Beach Club 3 * (Tunisia / Monastir): photos and reviews of tourists

Monastir in Tunisia is one of the most famous port cities. There is a huge number of beautiful hotels and beaches that will not leave indifferent any vacationer. Each beach in the outskirts of Tunisia and the city itself is equipped with its own equipment rental point, as well as a school where experienced instructors teach the basics of diving. On any beach, there are restaurants and cafes that have dishes prepared with seafood and all kinds of fish.

Local Attractions




If we talk about what interesting places you should definitely visit in Monastir, first of all we will talk about the famous fortress wall. It is located very close to the port city. The Bugrib Mausoleum and Mosque are located in the same area. The museum of Islamic culture with ancient exhibits is very popular, among which the manuscripts of that time are considered the most amazing. The whole hall of this museum is reserved for clay dishes, and the most ancient exhibit is the astrolabe of 927.

The central part of the city is famous for the location of the mausoleum there, where the whole couple of Bourguib is buried. The tomb from the beginning to the end is decorated with marble and precious stones. In 1980, golden domes and a minaret were added to the museum.

Recreation and Sports





Near the port city is the largest diving training center, where competitions of various levels are held. Not far from this place the visitors are offered interesting and extremely exciting boat trips. Those who are literally crazy about this type of sports entertainment like golf should definitely pay a visit to the new field, which was opened only 2 years ago. In order for connoisseurs of this entertainment to be accommodated with the greatest possible comfort, the Houda Golf Beach Club 3 * hotel was built, adjacent to the golf club.

Description





The hotel is close to the previously mentioned golf course and is located between Sousse and Monastir. The three-star hotel is equipped with its own beach, equipped according to all the laws of the tourist business. Each room in this hotel (even economy class) has a chic view from the window (onto a flowering garden or the sea shore). All apartments have a climate control system, TV, refrigerator, Internet, combined bathroom. The hotel also has several pools with jacuzzi and water massage functions. Near each of them is a specially equipped relaxation area with sun loungers and other arsenal for comfortable relaxation. Among other things, the Houda Golf Beach Club 3 * hotel offers the services of an integrated SPA-center with various types of massage. Monastir's central area is 12 kilometers from the hotel. The total number of rooms is 516 pieces.

Reviews about the hotel Houda Golf Beach Club 3 *

Many tourists note that although the hotel has about 3 stars, it does not bother to relax in comfort. The distance from the hotel to the airport and back is overcome in just 20 minutes, which has a very positive effect on the overall experience. But not only this is attractive hotel Houda Golf Beach Club 3 *. Guest reviews also indicate comfortable living conditions.

Tourists note that rooms with views of the tennis court are in fact no worse than rooms that offer beautiful views of the sea or the flowering garden.

As for the television in the room, only 1 Russian channel is supported, but it is enough to find out all the news. The floor in all rooms is made of tiles, so that any sounds on the floor are audible, but when the noise stops, you can quite sleep.

In addition to the Russian channel, a television antenna catches 2 English-language channels and 2 channels in French.

For lovers of extreme relaxation and children there are slides and water transport. One of the slides is designed for bathing babies, and the other three are for adults.

Beach area reviews

The seashore pleases with cleanliness, the sea itself is also almost perfectly clean. There are practically no storms, so you can not be afraid for your life. A beautiful and very comfortable sightseeing yacht with a highly educated guide carries the tourists around the sea. True, all tours are voiced in German, English or French, so it will be a little difficult to understand what they are talking about. Such language preferences are highly justified, because this area was previously considered one of the colonies of France.

The sun is scorching, therefore it is better to go to the beach either early in the morning, or after lunch, or even completely in the late evening, when it is already dark. You should definitely bring sunglasses and cream with you for a proper tan, clothes should not be dark, but light to reflect sunlight. For better protection on the beach, it is better to be under an umbrella, which, incidentally, can be taken near the hotel. The location of the hotel is very quiet and calm, but at the same time vibrant.

The surrounding area. Guest comments

Of children's entertainment, water aerobics and water ball games are very popular, which are conducted under the guidance of experienced instructors. And here, people with even a small budget can fully experience all the delights of a good rest with lots of entertainment.

Having once visited this country, not a single tourist will remain indifferent and will definitely want to come to Tunisia more than once, because this place is simply fascinating, you just have to look at the sea. The pier near the hotel is very long and wide. By the way, sometimes you can see dolphins swimming peacefully among holidaymakers. Little children will like this fact very much, because dolphins are very friendly and are not able to offend a person. On the contrary, these mammals always saved from sharks, which is recorded even in history.

In general, Tunisia is a wonderful country for families. It has everything that can satisfy the needs of both adults and children. In addition, rest here can be less costly than staying at domestic resorts.
