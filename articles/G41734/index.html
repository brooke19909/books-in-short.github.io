Hotel Golden Coast Resort & Spa 4 * (Vietnam, Phan Thiet): photos and reviews of tourists

In recent years, Vietnam has become increasingly popular with travelers who want to relax on the seashore. This exotic country can offer not only picturesque white beaches, but also decent living conditions, genuine hospitality, rich culture and history. One of the popular hotels is the luxury complex Golden Coast Resort Spa, located in the private sector. Guests here can count on a relaxing holiday surrounded by modern luxury and breathtaking natural beauty.

Golden Coast Resort Hotel Spa consists of several residential buildings and separate secluded bungalows. The territory here is large and is a real work of art - around palm trees, exotic thickets, spacious lawns, bright flower beds. Staying here brings real pleasure.

What do hotel rooms look like?





Of course, for many travelers, the main issue is the quality of accommodation. What does the Golden Coast Resort Spa offer to the tourist? On its territory there are 79 rooms, including:

  • Standard rooms.
  • Superior rooms (overlooking the garden or the beach).
  • Deluxe rooms (spacious rooms with sea or courtyard views).
  • Beach bungalows.
  • Family Villa (there is direct access to the beach).

All rooms are clean, well maintained, spacious and bright. Here you will find large comfortable beds, as well as other necessary furniture. In addition, the rooms are equipped with the necessary equipment, without which a truly comfortable stay is hard to imagine. In the evenings, you can watch satellite channels on the big screen TV. You can create a comfortable temperature using an air conditioning system and a fan. There is a telephone and constant access to the Internet. Guests can also use the safe for free. Drinking water is regularly replenished in the minibar (other drinks - only for a fee). You will also receive everything you need for making coffee and tea.

The room adjoins the bathroom, equipped with a shower and modern fixtures. Clean towels and hygiene products are replenished every day during scheduled cleaning. Residents are provided with a hair dryer.

Relax on the beach: are there any opportunities?





As already mentioned, the Golden Coast Resort Spa is located almost on the coast - getting to the beach is not difficult. It is incredibly beautiful here, covered with white sand and, of course, clean (employees regularly clean its territory). There is a gradual entry into the sea, the water is almost always warm. Guests can relax on comfortable sun loungers with mattresses. They are also given clean beach towels, which, by the way, are regularly changed.

Of course, on the beach you can do some water sports, boating, boats and canoeing. Sometimes fans of sailing and windsurfing come here. The city has a scuba diving club. The weather, by the way, depends on the season, sometimes in the afternoon waves rise.

Range of additional services





Naturally, guests can count on an additional service that will make living here more convenient. Dry cleaning available. You can use the services of a currency exchange office. The hotel has a good parking lot, and you can easily rent transport at the hotel (if you have the necessary documents, of course).

Almost throughout the territory there is access to the Internet network. The hotel also provides a spacious conference room that seats about 200 people - here you can hold not only business, but festive events.

How do guests relax? Leisure and entertainment

Although the hotel is located in a secluded area, there are some activities on site. For example, you can relax next to the pool, which is a kind of center of hotel life. Nearby, by the way, is a section for bathing children. Even your child will be able to have a great time on the playground with a swing or in the playroom, where the teacher always remains with the children.

It features a fitness center with a fully equipped gym. You can also sing karaoke, play table tennis, visit the bathhouse, and relax during a real Thai massage. This is not all that Vietnam offers to the traveler . Hotel Golden Coast Resort Spa 4 * has its own travel agency, where you can choose and purchase an interesting sightseeing tour of the surrounding area. Every traveler knows that a lot of valuable information can be obtained by talking to people who have already managed to visit a particular place. How did tourists remember Golden Coast Resort Spa?
