Hotel Titanic Aquapark Resort (Egypt, Hurghada): description, reviews, photos

Egyptian Hurghada is the largest resort in the country. The warm sea and the bright sun, magnificent coral reefs, oriental hospitality - this is a holiday that awaits tourists here all year round!



Relaxation

Due to the constant breeze blowing from the sea and low humidity even at a rather high temperature, the heat in Hurghada is easily tolerated even by elderly people, and the midday heat, unlike other seaside resorts, does not exhaust at all. The local climate is favorable for tourists of all ages.

Hurghada, many call the window into the country of the sun and the exotic. Trips here help Russians plunge into a fairy tale. Thanks to the magnificent beaches, the sea, amazing cleanliness and rich underwater world, as well as the amazing cultural heritage, many people call Hurghada holidays an exciting journey combined with a pleasant pastime.

The resort has many large tourist complexes with a vast territory adjacent to the water. In addition, many budget hotels have been built within the city. In the north of Hurghada, they are adjacent to the coast, famous for its coral reefs.



4 Star Hotels

Not only in this resort, but also everywhere in Egypt, four-star hotel complexes are the most popular accommodation option for Russians, and at any time of the year. The climate in the region allows you to swim in the sea not only in summer but also in winter. Of course, in December or February (not a high season), many resort complexes are not completely overcrowded, so tourists are offered ‚Äúhot‚Äù packages that allow not only a good rest, but also significant savings on the price of accommodation.

Holidays in Hurghada are often chosen by lovers of an active type of leisure. Many hotel complexes have their own water parks and centers for water sports entertainment. Here tourists can enjoy diving and windsurfing, boat trips, snorkeling, sea tours to nearby islands and more. Traditionally, most hotels offer high-quality animation - beach parties and dance shows, fun shows for children. One of such complexes is Titanic Aquapark Resort 4 *.

general information

Like many other hotels in Hurghada, it has not only its own beach, but also a large water park. The hotel complex is located just eight kilometers from the airport, which is a big plus: in addition to being convenient for young children who do not tolerate transfers, those who do not meet can get to the Titanic Aquapark Resort by taxi without spending large sums on moving . The city center is only twenty minutes away.



Having reached the Titanic Aquapark Resort 4 * hotel, tourists pay attention primarily to its appearance. Architecture and landscape design are in the same original style. The hotel is stylized as a huge cruise ship. It was built in 2005 and is part of the PrimaSol hotel chain.

Infrastructure

Titanic Aquapark Resort 4 * (Hurghada) offers a lot of services. This is a modern conference room for one hundred and fifty people, a lobby where visitors can relax after a transfer before checking in, a left-luggage office, a currency exchange office, an ATM, coffee machines in the lobby, a supermarket where you can buy everything you need, several kiosks , including souvenir.

The hotel has its own free parking. Tourists do not need to pre-book places.

At the 24-hour reception desk you can propagate documents, receive or send a fax. There is also the possibility of renting a cell in a depository safe. Luggage can be left in the luggage storage room before check-in or after check-in. At the reception you can rent a vehicle to explore the local attractions on your own. For services provided by laundry, dry cleaning, a doctor, you need to pay extra. The hotel also has a beauty salon and concierge services are available.

Nutrition

Titanic Aquapark Resort 4 * (Egypt, Hurghada) works on the principle of All Inclusive. This means that tourists do not need to worry about their meals, as in the main restaurant they are served daily buffet breakfast, lunch and dinner. The concept also includes snacks and local drinks at the pool and beach bar, as well as tea, instant coffee and pastries in the lobby bar. For ice cream vacationers need to pay. There is also a special menu - freshly squeezed juices.



For children

Hotel Titanic Aquapark Resort 4 * (Hurghada) by tour operators is positioned as a place where you can comfortably and carefree relax with children. Therefore, a lot is provided for the infrastructure for small customers. Upon request, parents can get a baby cot in the room, they will make an application for a special menu for the baby at the receptionist upon arrival. The restaurant offers free high chairs convenient for feeding. There is also a mini club and, of course, a pool with low slides in the water park.

Beach

A few years ago, tourists buying a ticket to the Titanic Aquapark Resort used a swimming area belonging to the neighboring Ali Baba complex. But after a major reform, the hotel has its own private beach, intended only for its guests. Since the Titanic Aquapark Resort is built on the second line and is somewhat distant from the sea, a bus daily takes tourists to the bathing area from 9 a.m. to 6 p.m. every fifteen minutes. There is a bar on the beach where, according to the all-inclusive concept, you can eat and get drinks from local producers. Umbrellas with sunbeds, mattresses, as well as beach towels are free for hotel guests. The beach is sandy and pebble, so rubber slippers are welcome.

Additional Information

There are several reasons why many choose the Titanic Aquapark Resort 4 * hotel. Hurghada is famous for its rich underwater world, so those who come to the Red Sea for diving are often staying at the hotel. Accommodation prices at Titanic Aquapark Resort are quite affordable amid the services offered and the rich infrastructure.

On the territory of the hotel there is a wellness center, there is a sauna, a beauty salon, a steam room, a jacuzzi, a massage room (paid service). In addition, guests can play billiards or table tennis, and spend time in the gym.

Titanic Aquapark Resort, reviews of which are mostly positive, offers good animation, including for children. Many experienced tourists say that in Egypt there are few hotels where there is such a high-quality service. In the evenings, live music plays at the amphitheater, discos are organized. There is also an internet cafe where fans can play video games online.

Water activities

Lots of water activities and on the beach. There is a diving center where you can rent equipment, as well as use the services of a licensed instructor. This also applies to kiting (surfing with a parachute), as well as riding a scooter or catamaran, boat trips, etc.

Already from the name of this hotel you can understand that it has a water park, which is especially popular with young visitors who can ride for hours on the hills. Reviews

Many Russians chose this hotel complex due to the fact that there is a lot of entertainment for children. This criterion is especially relevant for people planning a family vacation. All tourists are united in their opinion: the water park of the hotel is one of the best in Egypt. A lot of positive reviews about the beach, especially about coral reefs of amazing beauty. As for the numbers, no one has any complaints. Tourists are satisfied with the area occupied by the premises, and how unobtrusively and neatly they are cleaned. Many good words have been said about the work of the staff.

Most Russians highly appreciated the food provided at the hotel: a rich buffet is always served in the main restaurant. Here you can find not only an abundance of vegetables and fruits, but also meat dishes from beef, fish, chicken and lamb with a variety of side dishes. The only thing that some tourists did not like was the monotony of soups.

The Russians call another drawback that in the territory of the Titanic Aquapark Resort 4 * (Hurghada) there is no restaurant working according to the preliminary menu. However, this defect is fully compensated by numerous bars where you can quickly have a bite to eat (for example, on the beach or in the lobby).

As for the sea, it is wonderful in Hurghada. The beach, although somewhat remote from the hotel, is great for children. The sea in this part of the coast is rather shallow. The road to the beach by bus takes a maximum of three minutes, although some guests, judging by the reviews, preferred to walk on foot, crossing the territory of two neighboring hotels.

As for the infrastructure around the Titanic Aquapark Resort, there are several shops literally at the entrance, including one with fixed prices. A twenty-minute walk is a large shopping center where you can buy groceries, including fresh fruit.

Many Russians in their reviews say that they had a wonderful holiday in this hotel, so they advise choosing it. And as a bonus, get good memories for the whole year.
