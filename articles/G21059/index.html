Amut Lake, Khabarovsk Krai: description, attractions and reviews

High in the mountains in the Khabarovsk Territory, near the village of Gorny, is Lake Amut. Just 60 kilometers away is the large city of Komsomolsk-on-Amur, and here - picturesque nature and clean air. Amut is one of the cleanest reservoirs in the world. In terms of transparency, it is not inferior to Lake Baikal. This beautiful place all year round attracts a large number of tourists from around the world. They go to enjoy the clean mountain air and amazing landscapes.



The origin and characteristics of Lake Amut

According to scientists, this reservoir was formed as a result of a landslide that blocked the river. Thus formed Lake Amut. Khabarovsk Territory can no longer boast of such a clean reservoir. Its average depth is 12 - 15 meters, and the maximum depth is about 70 meters. And the total area of ​​the lake is 450 by 130 meters.

As a result of rapid water exchange, this pond warms up very slowly. The best time for swimming in Lake Amut is mid-July and until the end of August. During this period, the water warms up to 17 - 18 degrees Celsius.

For the same reason, Lake Amut is not rich in fish. She simply has nothing to eat. But the nature around the lake fascinates with the beauty of even a sophisticated tourist and traveler.



Pond attractions

Amut Lake is part of the Jerginsky Reserve. A distinctive feature of this place is pristine, untouched nature. Evergreen centuries-old fir trees surround the lake from all sides. They are especially beautiful in the winter season.

Most of the guests who visit Lake Amut in the Khabarovsk Territory throughout almost the whole year are residents of the Far East. They always are not averse to distract from the bustle of the city and relax in the fresh mountain air. Even Buddhist monks come here who claim that the mountain climate of the lake has a beneficial effect not only on physical health, but also on the state of mind.

Even 10 years ago, the distinctive attraction of this reservoir was the nondescript gray trailers that were located on its shores. And now here is the Amut Snow Lake camp site, equipped according to international standards. Therefore, even more tourists began to come to Lake Amut, Khabarovsk Territory.



Amut Snow Lake Camp

You can always come to relax in this place. Amut Snow Lake Chalet is waiting for guests throughout the year. At an altitude of 1300 meters above sea level, a beautiful view of Lake Amut. The camp site is conveniently located in the middle mountains of the Khabarovsk Territory. This is a very convenient place for sports and sightseeing. Of course, it is advisable to plan a vacation on Lake Amut in advance. If you want to enjoy the lake in the summertime, in order to swim in the cleanest water, then choose the time of year from mid-July to the end of August.

But the Amut Snow Lake camp site is especially attractive in the winter season. In addition to skiing, on the base of Amut Snow Lake you can go snowboarding and biathlon. It often hosts zonal, regional and international competitions. The pride of this camp site is Julia Chapalova, who became the Olympic champion. It was here that she began climbing the Olympic pedestal.

Lifts operate along mountain routes. And on the ski runs themselves - the rammer Ratrak. In addition, you can use the services of a professional trainer, as well as rent the necessary equipment.

In addition to sports recreation, on the basis of Amut Snow Lake you can relax and have fun:

  • visit a cozy karaoke bar;
  • play billiards;
  • dance in a disco;
  • rent a barbecue;
  • Take a steam bath in the bath, which is designed for 15 people.

On the territory of the sports and tourist base there are two hotels, as well as cottages of different categories. An economy class cottage for 6 people will cost you 12 thousand rubles a day, and renting a six-bed hotel room - 7200 rubles a day.

At this camp site there are various cafes and restaurants with Chinese and Russian cuisine. A large Chinese restaurant is designed for about 100 people, and a Russian restaurant is slightly smaller. In addition, you can order a balanced individual three-time menu for only 700 rubles.



This is a winter vacation spot

Lovers of skiing on the lake are always waiting for the cold weather. Beautiful snowy ski and ski slopes will leave you with only the best impressions of the lake and its bewitching nature. Everyone can relax here, regardless of sports training. In winter, various ski slopes are equipped for tourists and athletes:

  • ski track for athletes with a length of 5 and 7.5 kilometers;
  • a simple track for athletes - lovers 2 km long;
  • walking trail of 1.5 kilometers.

A special place is occupied by a New Year's holiday at the Amut Snow Lake sports and tourist base. Many wish to spend the New Year holidays in the bosom of untouched nature. In addition, a festive program with the participation of Santa Claus and a company of New Year's characters is provided.



Amut Lake, Khabarovsk Territory: how to get

To get to this place, first of all, you need to get to Komsomolsk-on-Amur. From there, take a bus to the village of Gorny or Solnechny. And from there it is recommended to transfer to an SUV, since a mountainous rocky road is laid to the lake. You can, of course, walk a distance of about 7 - 10 kilometers and walk or use a bicycle. But, despite such difficulties, Lake Amut attracts many tourists all year round.

Reviews about this place

You can find a lot of reviews about Amut Lake and its unique Amut Snow Lake camp site. And they are all very controversial. Almost all the reviews speak of the magnificent nature, the purest mountain air, a wonderful sports vacation, especially in the winter season. But the reviews regarding the camp site are quite contradictory. Many argue that the service at the camp site leaves much to be desired, while others, on the contrary, are delighted with a comfortable stay. Most reviews say that you can spend a day at the camp site, but staying overnight is not advisable. Therefore, everyone must decide how to relax.
