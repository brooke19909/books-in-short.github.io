All about what is WMZ in WebMoney

The development of the Internet as a communication network leads to the fact that each inhabitant of the planet will ultimately be calculated by electronic currency. It goes without saying that users will need to become the owner of settlement wallets. Already today they are provided by Internet payment systems.

WebMoney - a simple, convenient service

One of the most popular services is WebMoney. In this system, you can register several types of electronic wallets. But first of all, any user should familiarize themselves with what WMZ is in WebMoney, WMR, WMU, WME, which means the twelve-digit wallet number and their prefixes in the form of letters R or Z. Almost all the information is presented on the official website, but the resource not very convenient interface. It can be quite difficult for a beginner to deal with such a volume of new information. Therefore, this article provides explanations for some concepts.

What is WMZ at WebMoney



First of all, a new user of a payment system must go through a simple registration procedure and log in via a link sent by e-mail. These steps are enough to become the owner of the first wallet. In WebMoney you can add as many of them with how many types of currency equivalents the system works. At this stage, it is worth understanding what WMZ, WME, WMR, WMU are. These abbreviations are nothing more than the designation of the equivalent of various world currencies.

The first two letters do not mean anything other than the name of the WebMoney payment system itself. The last unit of the abbreviation indicates the type of currency: Z - dollar, E - euro, U - hryvnia and so on. New website users often confuse wallet names with equivalents. There is no big trouble in this, but these concepts are better to share.

After registering by default, WebMoney creates one of the types of wallets and assigns it a twelve-digit number. WMZ currency can be transferred to the account with the prefix Z, WMR - R, WMU - U and so on.

Currency exchange, where to do it

System users often create multiple wallets for each type of equivalent. And, as a rule, the question of how and where to exchange electronic currency becomes no less acute than the question: what is WMZ?





One of the exchange methods is the WebMoney exchange machine. To use it is elementary. To do this, go to the "Wallet" section on the site. Among the proposed operation icons, select "Exchange". Further, the system will offer to set in two lines the exchange of what currency the client is going to implement. The last step is to click the "Exchange" icon.

This method is the easiest, but has some limitations. The WebMoney machine offers an exchange of only WMZ to WMR and vice versa. No other currency equivalent works here.

Why and where to buy electronic equivalents

What is WMZ? This question is asked by people who first came to the WebMoney website, buyers of some online stores, online game players who were offered to pay with just such signs. Indeed, today a large number of services and commercial sites cooperate with this payment system. No exception - portals offering jobs to freelancers. WebMoney is a convenient service that significantly saves time. This payment system is used by many, and its popularity will grow.





Not only people who make money on the network can have Z, R, U, E wallets and funds on them. Almost everyone can own electronic currency. There are some fairly simple ways to fund your account. One of them is the acquisition of WebMoney cards. In some cities, their list is available on the official website of the system, you can place an order for delivery of a payment card to your home or office. Then you need to enter her number in your account in the section "Top up account". People who do not live in cities served by WebMoney should not despair. Cards are successfully sold through a network of dealers through the means of Internet sites.
