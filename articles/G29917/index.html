Festival Riviera Resorts 4 * (Egypt, Hurghada): hotel description, photos and reviews

If you plan to go on vacation to Egypt and consider Hurghada hotels as a place to stay, Festival Riviera Resorts 4 * can be an excellent option for a budget and comfortable holiday on the very shore of the Red Sea.



Location

This four-star hotel is located fifteen kilometers from the center of the resort city of Hurghada. The nearest airport is twenty kilometers away, while El Gouna is ten kilometers away.

Egypt, Hurghada, Festival Riviera Resort 4 *: general description, photo

The hotel complex "Festival Riviera Resort" began to work in 2008. It consists of two large five-story buildings, one of which is located on the first coastline, and the other on the second. The territories are interconnected by an underground passage. In total, the hotel has 434 comfortable rooms of standard, club and family categories. There are also apartments specially equipped for people with disabilities.

Festival Riviera Resort (Egypt) has several swimming pools (including for children), sun terraces, bars, a restaurant, a health club, a mini club, sports grounds, a diving center, a windsurfing school, a beauty salon, and a gym hall and much more. The hotel complex has at its disposal a large sandy beach with comfortable sun loungers and a bar.

Housing stock

As for the rooms offered to the guests, then in general they were all satisfied. However, as some tourists note, in most cases by default you will not get apartments with sea views. If you want such an option, you must inform the reception administrator at the check-in desk in advance. As a rule, you will be given a room with a sea view for a fee in a very small amount - about ten dollars. In general, our compatriots found the apartments in Festival Riviera Resorts 4 * spacious, stylishly decorated, bright and pleasant. The furniture here is quite new, the technology is modern. Everything works without interruption. If something fails, then after a call to the reception the problem will be fixed as soon as possible.



Check-in, cleaning

As tourists note in their reviews, they have no particular complaints about the work of the maids at the hotel. So, cleaning is done regularly and fairly high quality. From time to time, maids make funny figures from towels and sheets. Kids especially like this.

As for the settlement, according to our compatriots, in most cases, newly arrived tourists have to wait for the checkout time. The fact is that Festival Riviera Resorts 4 * is a fairly popular hotel, so sometimes it is almost 100% full. So, new arrivals sometimes have to wait until the previous tenants have left and their maids have properly prepared their rooms. But if you arrive at the hotel early in the morning and there will be free apartments of the category you have booked, the administrator will do everything possible to make you check in as early as possible. Nutrition

This issue did not cause much disagreement, as is the case with many hotels in Egypt and other countries. Guests note a large selection of different dishes from meat, poultry, fish. For breakfast, tourists are offered cheese and sausage slices, cereals, cereals, differently prepared eggs and much more. There is no shortage of drinks. There are several bars on site.



Sea

Many tourists really liked the beach belonging to Festival Riviera Resorts 4 * (Hurghada). According to them, it is quite large and sandy. It is regularly cleaned, so you will not find garbage in the form of plastic cups and cigarette butts here. There is a bar on the beach where you can either order a drink or have a burger, sandwich or pizza snack if you are hungry during the day. Entry into the water is gentle, there are practically no stones at the bottom, so many tourists could do without special rubber shoes without any problems. At low tide, in order to get to the depths, you have to overcome a decent distance in shallow water. However, there is no such problem during high tide.

The beach of the Festival Riviera Resorts 4 * hotel is equipped with a large number of comfortable sun loungers and parasols. There is enough space for everyone here, so there is no need to get up early in the morning and run ashore to take a seat. At any time of the day, coming to the beach, you can find free sunbeds.

Entertainment at the hotel

The hotel complex has several swimming pools with sun terraces and bars. In the daytime, a team of animators works near them. They offer a very diverse entertainment program, including water aerobics, step aerobics, zumba, darts, water polo, dancing, yoga, fun contests, beach volleyball, tennis and much more. So here you will not get bored. In the evenings, entertaining shows and discos are held for hotel guests.

Since the Festival Riviera Resort Hurghada 4 * hotel is always happy for families with children of different ages, for young guests there are all conditions for a comfortable stay and fun entertainment. So, during the day, kids can visit the mini club. Here, the animators will offer them educational activities and entertainment in accordance with age. After dinner, a mini-disco is held, where young tourists can dance to children's songs.
