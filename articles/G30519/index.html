How to make flowers from plastic bottles simple and beautiful

For mankind, the topic of garbage disposal has always been and will be relevant. That is why the opportunity to turn the scrap into something useful or beautiful is so attractive. Among the many options, the idea of how to make flowers from plastic bottles is especially popular right now .



Such flowers often decorate playgrounds, urban courtyards and rural estates. You can meet them in apartments - as an ornament of flower pots and just an element of decor. They delight in their diversity and unusualness. Meanwhile, to create a flower from plastic bottles with your own hands is not at all difficult. The main thing in this business is desire and some experience. If at first your own imagination seems insufficient, you can use the ideas of others as a model. Having mastered the technology, you will create on your own, getting great pleasure from it.

If you are determined to learn how to make flowers from plastic bottles, you should learn it in practice. You will need empty bottles of different colors and sizes, wire or branches for the stem, acrylic paints and brushes, a knife and scissors. Sometimes this is enough. But in some cases, you may also need a candle, hairdryer, or a pot of boiling water. It depends on what shape of flowers you plan to make.

First, consider how to make a small flower. This is the simplest craft from a plastic bottle. A white container made from milk or yogurt is perfect for her - without additional painting, you can easily get a camomile. But, of course, dishes of any other color will do. You will still understand how to make flowers from plastic bottles.

Having determined what size flower you want to receive, carefully cut the bottom of the bottle. Decide on the number and shape of the petals. It is best to draw them, and then cut them along the contour. Cuts are made right down to the neck.



Now bend the petals, give them the desired shape. The easiest way is to bend the petal using a jet of hot air from a hairdryer. In the same way, you can slightly or very randomly twist the petals, give them a wavy shape. In some cases (for example, in the manufacture of rose petals) it is more convenient to use a candle. But then there is a risk of melting the workpiece. It is worth accumulating some experience before using open fire.

If you need to tighten the petals tightly (as, for example, in the locust), it is easier to use another method. Wrap the petal with a roll and secure with a paper clip. After that, lower the workpiece into hot (almost boiling) water for 10 seconds. The plastic will have enough of this time to take the shape you need.



Actually, the base of the flower is ready! It remains to come up with a middle for him. You can make it from foam, foam or even a plastic bag, twisting it like a rose. The stem can be made of wire wrapped in cloth or a narrow strip of plastic from a green bottle. In the part of the neck where the thread is located, make a hole in the diameter of the stem, place a flower on it and tighten the lid so that the parts are pressed against each other. Another option is to fix the flower on a real branch. Maybe for this you even choose a living tree?

No matter how you use your craft, now you know how to make flowers from plastic bottles. To create a flower of a different shape, a different size or color, you just need to carefully look at the sample. Over time, you will be able to create bouquets of these masterpieces. And let there be more beauty in this world, and less garbage.
