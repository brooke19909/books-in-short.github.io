Hotel "Rus" (Adler, Russia): description and reviews of tourists

Adler district of Sochi is one of the most picturesque and attractive seaside resorts. Here you can find a hotel for every taste and budget. One of the most attractive places to relax is considered the 3-star hotel "Rus." Adler has always been popular with vacationers, and this small hotel is no exception.

Hotel location

Address at which the Rus Hotel is located : Adler , 1/3 Tauride . Along the village where the hotel is located, there is a wide beach strip. The village is located near the border with Abkhazia. The location of the hotel is well developed infrastructure. In addition to numerous catering establishments, shops and a market, there is a park with activities for children. In addition, not far from the village is the Olympic Park, the only one of its kind in Russia. The hotel occupies a four-story building with very beautiful architecture. There is an upper terrace where you can relax and sunbathe. The terrace offers wonderful views of the Caucasus Mountains. Opposite the hotel there is a convenient free parking for 15 cars. Walk to the sea for about ten minutes. Pebble beach, wide and very clean. There are no breakwaters in the beach. For those who wish, the hotel has a small pool.





Accommodation for vacationers

In total, 32 rooms are provided for tourists, including 28 one-room standard and four deluxe rooms. The hotel can accommodate 80 people at a time. Standard rooms have three types.

  • Eight ground floor rooms without a balcony. The room is small, its area is 15 square meters. Its cost, depending on the season, ranges from 1,500 to 2,000 rubles per day.
  • Twelve rooms of the same area on the second and third floors with a balcony. The fee for such a number is from 1600 to 2200 rubles.

Room Amenities

All for the convenience of holidaymakers has a hotel "Rus". Adler is attractive not only as a beautiful resort. Everything is provided here to make tourists feel at home. Each room of type "Standard" provides:

  • shower and bathroom;
  • TV;
  • air conditioning;
  • refrigerator;
  • mini bar;
  • telephone (for both internal and external communications).

All rooms are equipped with comfortable furniture. Apartment Suite has a bathroom equipped with modern fixtures. As for the furniture, here, among other things, in the living room there is a comfortable folding leather sofa and a coffee table. In all rooms for the convenience of visitors there is free WI-FI.





Nutrition

Meals do not include the Rus Hotel in the price . Adler is rich in its producers of quality products, so the hotel has its own dining room and cafe-bar, where you can eat cheaply. Food is organized as follows:

  • complex meals (breakfast, lunch and dinner, respectively, at 100, 170 and 150 rubles);
  • there is a system of discounts on food for children for breakfast and lunch (50% from 3 to 7 years old, and 25% from 7 to 12);
  • You can order the preparation of dishes to order.

In addition, not far from the hotel there are a large number of cafes, bars, restaurants, many establishments with home cooking.
