Grand Zaman Garden Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Alanya / Alanya Center): photos and reviews of tourists

Grand Zaman Garden Hotel 4 is a small hotel in the central part of the Turkish city of Alanya. The hotel windows overlook the Mediterranean coast on one side and the green slopes of the mountain on the other. The main attractions of the city are just 15 minutes walk.

Alanya is a beautiful old town, which has an old fortress, a medieval castle, Ataturk Square and the famous Red Tower. Tourists living in the Grand Zaman Garden Hotel 4 also delightfully describe not only the architectural, but also the natural beauties of these picturesque places.





Visitors to Alanya are simply obliged to visit the beautiful Damlatash Cave, which is located 1 km from the hotel. There is another famous Dim cave, but you need to get to it by minibuses 11 km. It is worth the attention of nature lovers and the Dim River tract, to which the distance is slightly less - 7.2 km.

The city is also famous for its harbor with yacht parking. You can rent one of them and enjoy the beauty of the picturesque coast from the sea.

Just 150 meters from the hotel complex is the famous Cleopatra beach. In addition to the beach area, you can sunbathe in comfortable sun loungers by the pool. But more on that later, and now let's turn our attention to the description of the hotel Grand Zaman Garden Hotel 4.

Interior decoration

Entering the hotel lobby, you would think that you find yourself in some magnificent palace. The bright large hall sparkles with a thousand lights that shine from the polished marble floor with beautiful ornaments. The ivory color in the interior beautifully echoes with additional chocolate elements. On the walls are beautiful panels with Turkish patterns.





For visitors, a lounge corner is arranged in the lobby with round original armchairs, also light chocolate in color. You can sit there, wait for the guide or surf the Internet via free Wi-Fi, although numerous reviews of Grand Zaman Garden Hotel 4 say that the wireless network catches the signal perfectly in the rooms.

The hotel is modern, as it was built in 2006, and the last reconstruction was carried out in 2013. The bathrooms, the interior were updated, the mattresses and furniture were changed to new samples.

Hotel Description

Hotel Grand Zaman Garden Hotel 4 includes two buildings. One building has 8 floors and is called the Main Building. Another is a five-story building called Anex. In the main building, a porter at the reception desk is working round the clock for tourists. For travelers with their own vehicles, there is a free car park on site, only you need to notify the administrator in advance or mark this item in your hotel reservation via the Internet or travel agent.

You can arrange car rental directly at the hotel. Also in the lobby there is an Internet cafe where you can copy photos to a USB flash drive, work on a computer, print photocopies or send a fax. Of course, such services are paid extra.

Nutrition

The Grand Zaman Garden Hotel 4 has an all-inclusive meal plan. There are tables both in the restaurant and in the cafe in the fresh air, during the day you can have a snack near the pool. Some tourists write that even after a walk in Alanya until 4 in the morning they, having arrived at the hotel, were able to eat. Reviews about the quality of food are very different. Some tourists praise the variety of dishes, beautiful design and presentation, while others write that in 10 days the food is tired of the order, as it was constantly monotonous. But all unanimously praise the Turkish desserts, they say that they are very tasty and they are served in a huge amount.





For those who want to try something exotic or buy home Turkish famous spices, the market with everything you need is at a distance of 200 meters from the place of residence of tourists. A supermarket is in the same vicinity. This convenience was noted by many guests in their reviews.

Bathrooms

All rooms at Grand Zaman Garden Hotel 4 have a private bathroom with a washbasin, toilet and shower. The rooms are decorated in light beige colors. The tile is modern and beautiful.





Plumbing is all new, without complaints from the settlers. Free toiletries are provided: soap, shampoo, shower gel. You can use a hairdryer. It is provided free of charge in all bathrooms.

Territory

The hotel occupies a small area with two swimming pools with fresh water without heating. One large - 150 square meters. m, the other - less - 70 square meters. m. In the outdoor cafe next to you right next to the water you can refresh yourself with soft drinks, eat ice cream and snacks. There are also three water slides that attract tourists with children.





Very close is a pebble and sandy beach. However, sun loungers, umbrellas on them are paid. In the data about the hotel it is recorded that towels are given out for free, but in the reviews people indicate the opposite, that they require 4 euros for them, which outraged absolutely all hotel guests.

This inconvenience is compensated by the proximity of the Alanya water park with numerous water attractions. Let's see what else to do at Grand Zaman Garden Hotel 4.

Description of entertainment

For sports enthusiasts, right at the hotel you can work out for free on the simulators in the fitness room. If you carefully read the reviews of people, you can understand that the hall cannot boast of a variety of shells, but the most necessary is available.





After classes, you can go to the Finnish sauna or Turkish hammam, but for an additional fee.

You can also play billiards or table tennis (also for money). In the sand there are conditions for playing beach volleyball. For children, animators often organize fun activities.

Many tourists in their reviews emphasize that in such a hotel it is better to book rooms for people with children or senior citizens, as well as for those who just want to relax from the noise. The hotel is quiet, loud discos and screams of tipsy you will not hear.

Additional services

The hotel has a beauty salon and massage room, you can make a hairstyle or cut your hair at the hairdresser. A beautiful spacious SPA center with a solarium is equipped.





Items that need cleaning can be taken to the laundry room. Naturally, for all the additional services you have to fork out separately.

Also, if necessary, you can consult a doctor. For children there is a playground, a children's pool and a mini club. Kids in the restaurant will be provided with extra high chairs.

There are shops on site. We accept Visa and MasterCard credit cards.
