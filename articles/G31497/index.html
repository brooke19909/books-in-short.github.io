What is a roof?

Many seek to acquire roofing from the Pokroff Center. What kind of element is this? The roof consists of such parts:

  • rafter system;
  • vapor barrier layer;
  • insulation;
  • waterproofing film;
  • wind protection;
  • roofing material laid on the roof, and other components.

Together, this forms the so-called roofing cake. If an error occurred while laying one of the layers, it can lead to a decrease in the service life of the roof or a weakening of its performance. A properly installed roof structure protects the building from heat loss and condensation in the under-roof space.

In fact, a competent device of the roof of the building is the key to its effective and long-term operation. Therefore, to perform all the necessary work requires a professional team, whose confident actions will allow you not to think about replacing materials or repair for many years.
