Gmini MagicBook T6LHD: reviews. Gmini MagicBook T6LHD: specs, instruction manual

The Gmini MagicBook T6LHD e-book reader is a six-inch backlit reader introduced on the mobile technology market at the end of 2013. The screen of the gadget works on a relatively new and interesting technology - E-Ink Pearl HD FrontLight, which managed to get a lot of positive feedback from users.





So, the subject of today's article is the Gmini MagicBook T6LHD reader. A review of the characteristics, advantages and disadvantages of the model, as well as the opinions of experts in this field and the reviews of the device owners - this and other interesting information will be presented to your attention further.

Equipment

The gadget is packed in a small box of cardboard, where you can see the image of the reader itself and its most ‚Äúdelicious‚Äù characteristics. On the back of the package there is a detailed specification, a separate block is reserved for the most flattering reviews. The Gmini MagicBook T6LHD occupies the lion's share of the space, and related accessories are neatly laid out around the perimeter. Contents of delivery:

The case for an e-book looks modest, but the universal dimensions of the gadget allow you to choose any other one you like, even from clean leather or flexible plastic. There are no specific holes in the protection, so any orientation of the cover (horizontal or vertical) is suitable.

Appearance

Gmini MagicBook T6LHD e-book is made of soft-touch rubberized plastic. The front side has a glossy design, and the rear is matte. It feels like velvet to the touch. The corners of the gadget are noticeably rounded, and in appearance it looks more like an oval than a rectangle.





As for the build quality, here users leave exclusively positive reviews. Gmini MagicBook T6LHD does not creak, crunches, and does not break even with a strong pressure on the case, and all structural parts are tightly and competently fitted to each other. The device looks solid and serious, and if you turn it in your hands, you feel that you are holding a solid device that does not give away cheapness.

Most of the front panel of the Gmini MagicBook T6LHD (6 inches) is occupied by a display. The brand logo flaunts above it. The bottom frame is reserved for function keys: a navigation joystick with the buttons "OK", "Back", "Context menu", "Home" and on / off the backlight of the gadget.

On both sides of the device there are duplicate swing pages. Users have repeatedly thanked the manufacturer for such a well-thought-out move and left positive reviews on this subject. Gmini MagicBook T6LHD turned out to be universal for both left-handed people and right-handed people, and protective accessories, like the same case, are easy to pick up.

The overall user experience of the design and appearance is generally positive. There are no elements that cut the overall style or annoy the owner. Everything is done in calm and universal colors.

Ergonomics

Thanks to its dimensions (179 x 123 x 11 mm), the gadget is convenient to hold even in one hand, and due to the pronounced roundness of the corners it fits almost perfectly in the palm of your hand. As mentioned above, the rear of the case has a matte finish, so the device does not slip and does not try to fall out of hand.

All function keys are pressed easily, but with due elasticity and a distinctive click. At first, there may be difficulties in the perception of buttons due to duplicated rockers for turning pages, but users say that you quickly get used to it, so that the discomfort disappears after an hour or two.

Control

Despite the full touch control, all the functionality is duplicated by mechanical buttons, which does not infringe on the rights of either fans of modern tapas or traditional ‚Äúkeyboardists‚Äù. The owners in their reviews have repeatedly thanked the developer for such a constructive approach.

The weight of the device is 230 grams, which is slightly more than that of similar devices, but the reader almost does not feel in your hand as something heavy and tiring, so any discomfort during operation, according to users, does not arise. The case noticeably aggravates the gadget, but protection from all sides and a more attractive appearance is a clear plus.

Screen

Naturally, the main and decisive factor for the purchase of this kind of technology remains the screen and picture quality. E-Ink Pearl HD technology has been used in readers since the end of 2011 and has proven itself in gadgets of this kind.





One of the main features is the fact that the design does not emit its own light, nor does it flicker, which has an extremely positive effect on vision, especially with prolonged use of the device. In addition, the output image is similar to a paper sheet, which is also comfortably perceived by the reader.

The contrast level at a more than acceptable level is 12 to 1. Rare competitors in this segment can boast of such a ratio (the most common - 7 to 1). This level allows you to easily read almost any font with a resolution of 1024 x 768 pixels.

Backlight

If you like to read in the dark, then by pressing a special key you can turn on the backlight. More modest readers working on electronic ink are deprived of this function, but there is also a fly in the ointment: the device discharges much faster when the backlight is on.

This mode gives the screen a pronounced blue tint, which you get used to pretty quickly. The backlight is evenly distributed, so that no unlit areas or stripes are observed. The level can be adjusted on a scale from 1 to 8. The first step is almost invisible even in the dark and does not blind at all, transmitting a diffuse and soft glow. The latter is pronounced, and you can use it, perhaps, as a flashlight, and not as a surroundings for reading. For a comfortable pastime in the dark, the fifth or even fourth step is quite enough.

If you read the user reviews, you can find out that for most people when the backlight is working, their eyes get tired much faster than with fluorescent lights, so this functionality will be useful, as they say, in a tent or when the power is turned off.

Changing the picture on the screen does not happen instantly, as it is organized, for example, on smartphones and tablets. But this not only does not interfere with reading, but on the contrary helps to calm the eyes. The gadget's interface does not slow down at all and almost immediately opens large libraries. Naturally, on the ‚ÄúLes Miserables‚Äù of Hugo (5 volumes) or on ‚ÄúPeople of Good Will‚Äù of Farigul (27 volumes), the device will stumble and think for a while.

Users in their reviews say that they are completely satisfied with the characteristics of the screen. For the reader, the proposed matrix capabilities and the output image are the very thing. The gadget does not lose paper media at all: the high definition of the image, the view under the ‚Äúsheet canvas‚Äù and the presence of backlighting are pleasing.

Work impressions

The gadget can read books aloud, but with one nuance unpleasant for the domestic consumer - only in English and Chinese. The same amateur firmware can correct this defect, but the voice assistant does not always work correctly, and it reads strictly defined fonts.

Pleased with the abundance of codecs. In addition to the usual TXT, FB2 and RTF formats, the device recognizes the mobile extensions MOBI and EPUB, as well as the rare PRC, TRC, SNM and OEB. Moreover, no problems arise with the ‚Äúheavy‚Äù PDF format. Everything that the reader "eats" is displayed properly and without any problems, if the file itself does not.

Additional features

As for some additional features, judging by the reviews, there are practically none of them. Yes, and expect from the reader something like a video or radio is not necessary. Users note that the gadget loses music quite tolerably, although small drawdowns occur if an audio file in high bitrate is caught. Pictures, like photographs, are displayed in black and white without any hue or saturation.

In general, the device copes with its purpose 100%, and for other charms, refer to the shelf with tablets and smartphones.

Offline work

Matrices of this plan, working on electronic ink technology, are extremely energy efficient, because the main energy consumption occurs when turning pages and working with navigation.



Battery life can be calculated, if not months, then weeks - for sure. Our respondent was no exception, and field trials showed that even when reading up to five hours a day, the device will ask for an outlet no more than once every three weeks. If you constantly use the backlight at the middle step, then the battery life will be reduced by about half.

Summarizing

Gmini MagicBook T6LHD (price about 6000 rubles) has almost all the advantages of gadgets in its segment. The model is perfect for even the most picky users and those who like to read in the dark. The clarity of the picture and the excellent balance of contrast will not strain your eyesight, and the smooth operation of the interface will provide a comfortable pastime for your favorite book.

Here you can add a completely affordable price tag: similar venerable competitors cost one or two thousand more, having at the same time similar characteristics, and sometimes inferior in some respects to our respondent.

The main advantages of the Gmini MagicBook T6LHD reader (judging by the reviews):

  • relatively low price tag;
  • high-quality screen with good contrast;
  • backlight;
  • large set of codecs;
  • excellent assembly and materials used;
  • regular stock software updates and sensible support;
  • Convenient and practical case included.

Disadvantages:

  • navigation buttons are sometimes accidentally pressed while reading;
  • the reader is too heavy for this kind of technology;
  • low accuracy of the sensor.
