About climate

The subtropical city is in a unique location. Directly at the Anapa Bay, the foothills of the Caucasus begin and the steppe of the Taman Peninsula goes to it. This geographical location of Anapa contributes to reduced air humidity and an increase in the number of sunny days, which are actually more here than the average in Crimea. This combination creates a unique beauty. In the Anapa region, juniper, elm, wild olive, and thuja later cultivated and planted in other regions later grow in their natural habitat.




The Mediterranean climate here is characterized by mild summers and mild winters. The swimming season in Anapa begins in mid-May and lasts until the end of October.
