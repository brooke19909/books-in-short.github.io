Gran Canaria island: where it is, photo and description, weather, attractions, reviews of tourists

If you consider yourself an avid traveler, then you probably dreamed of visiting the Canary Islands. The most popular destination of the archipelago is the island of Gran Canaria. Its tourist centers are well developed infrastructure. Numerous tourists come here for a beach holiday and vibrant nightlife. Resorts of the island specialize in water activities, sea excursions, windsurfing and other activities. You definitely will not be bored here.

Description and geographical location

Where is Gran Canaria located? The island is located in the south of the Canary Archipelago. He is the third largest. He owes his appearance to volcanoes. That is why the largest of them is a local attraction and a visiting card of the island. Nieves towers over the ocean, its peak in the cool months is covered with a white cap, which makes it possible for locals to not forget about the existence of snow.

Gran Canaria has the largest population on the archipelago. Very often it is called a continent in miniature, since there are many interesting things on its territory. Before the arrival of the Spanish colonialists on the island, the Guanche tribes lived here. Then Gran Caria turned into a colony. And over time, it became a remote, but very attractive province of Spain.

Its capital is the city of Las Palmas. It is a multinational, very fun and noisy place, famous for annual carnivals and cultural festivals. The capital is located in the north-east of the island. It is considered one of the largest cities in Spain. In the south of the island are mainly resorts, which are the basis of the economy, and in the center are mountainous areas with the remains of an ancient pine forest.

The climate of the region

All tourists, without exception, are interested in the question of what the weather is like on the island of Gran Canaria. It is worth saying that the territory of the region is divided into two climatic zones by mountain ranges. Each of them is dominated by its own winds, on which the weather depends. The northern part of the island is influenced by the trade winds, which bring fog and coolness. It rains more often and there is never any heat.

The southern part of the island is warmed by African winds. Climatic features also influenced the local flora. Lush vegetation prevails in the northern regions, while the southern regions are more reminiscent of the steppes. Sometimes dust storms even happen here. Throughout the year, the island has approximately the same weather. There is no pronounced change of seasons.

For recreation, tourists prefer the southern part of the island of Gran Canaria. Water off the coast is suitable for swimming throughout the year. Park Tamadaba

One of the main attractions of the island of Gran Canaria is the Tamadaba National Park. Its territory reaches 7500 ha. The park is located in the north-east of the island near the town of Agaite. In 1987, Tamadaba acquired the status of a UNESCO Biosphere Reserve. Unique plants, shrubs and other plants grow on its territory, which are not found anywhere else on the planet. No less unique are the animals living in the reserve park. Of greatest interest to tourists are the famous Canary pines, whose needles reach a length of 30 cm.

Not only the flora and fauna of the park is interesting, but also the most beautiful views of the ocean and surroundings that open from the heights. Numerous paths for guests to walk on bicycles and on foot are laid around the property. Those who wish can also stay here in tents for several days.

Botanical Garden

Among the attractions of the island of Gran Canaria (Canary Islands) it is worth paying attention to the botanical garden. The locals are very proud of him. The unique place is called Viera and Clavijo. It appeared in 1952 thanks to the efforts of the Swedish botanist Eric Sventenius. On the mountainsides near Las Palmas, he laid a future oasis in which he wanted to grow plants growing throughout the archipelago.

Currently, the garden covers 27 hectares. On its territory there are beautiful paths that visitors can walk along and admire flowers, shrubs and trees. The main attraction of the garden is the laurel forest with the rarest trees. Canary pines, alleys with dragon trees, as well as an unusual garden of giant cacti are no less attractive. Here you can also see the remains of the Guanche sanctuary.

Columbus House Museum

One of the most visited places by tourists on about. Gran Canaria (Canary Islands, photo given in the article) is the city of Las Palmas. On its territory there are attractions, among which it is worth paying attention to the Columbus House Museum. The historical monument is a three-story building in the style of colonial architecture of the fifteenth century. This building underwent major reconstruction once in 1777. In fact, the house was never owned by Columbus. The building served as the residence of the governor of the island, who kindly provided apartments for the great traveler.

In the courtyard of the building is the Museum of the Age of Discovery. He has been working since 1952 and talks about the history of sea voyages of different times. The museum has five exhibits. Some of them tell about the development of navigation before and after Columbus. In the twelve rooms you can see real maritime exhibits and quality renovations. In one of the rooms you can admire the voluminous model of Gran Canaria. The local halls also feature rare paintings provided by the Prado Museum. The museum is very interesting to visit. According to tourists, its exposition helps to learn a lot about the history of the island, about navigation and discoveries of different eras.

Palmitos Park

The park is located in the south of the island and is considered the largest thematic oasis. It is simultaneously a thematic reserve, an open zoo and an entertainment complex. Its territory is 20 ha, more than 15 thousand tropical plants grow on it, about 150 species of animals and birds live. The park features thematic exhibitions of insects, animals and fish. Here you can watch shows with the participation of four-legged artists. Especially tourists like the performance of parrots in the open. In addition, guests have the opportunity to watch shows with dolphins, as well as swim with them.
