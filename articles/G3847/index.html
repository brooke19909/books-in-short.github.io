Side Temple Hotel Hotel 3 * Turkey, Side: reviews

Side Temple Hotel is a great place for family and youth recreation, designed for those who appreciate quality service and stay in a great atmosphere. Here, each guest is surrounded by beautiful exotic nature, unusual for a simple Russian citizen. That is why tourists from the CIS countries often become guests of this place.

brief information

The hotel was built relatively recently - in 2009. Due to the short time of existence, its territory has a fresh, modern look, the buildings look fresh and harmonious with the entire surrounding area. Here, work is carried out every year aimed at maintaining the general condition of the resting place, as well as improving the conditions. Recently, a major renovation was made, during which the interior was updated in the apartment.

The total area of Side Temple Hotel 3 * (Side) is small - it is only 6000 sq.m. It houses buildings where all available rooms are concentrated, as well as places for recreation and entertainment, one of the most striking of which is the aquazone. Throughout the hotel there is a green tropical garden, which during the spa period is completely covered by abundant flowering.



Location

The hotel is in a very beautiful place, surrounded by vibrant nature and a large number of attractions. It is located almost in the center of the largest resort region of Turkey - Side, literally a kilometer from it. This place is surrounded by a huge number of entertainment places that attract the attention of tourists. Many of them are very fond of visiting nightclubs and restaurants that are located in the district.

The coastline is located almost near the hotel - at a distance of 100 meters from its main building. Guests of Side Temple Hotel 3 * (Turkey / Side) often in their comments about the rest share the fact that they visit the beach almost every day, which, according to them, is perfectly landscaped.

As for the nodes of air communication, the main one is the airport of the city of Antalya, to which all tourists from different countries arrive. It is located at a distance of 65 km from the town of Side.



Rooms

The hotel has a fairly small number of rooms - there are only 45 apartments. All of them are located in modern buildings, of which there are five in the territory of Side Temple Hotel. All rooms presented to the attention of the guests are divided into two categories: "Standard" and "Family".

In each type of room, there is a mandatory need for modern climatic equipment, presented by modern air conditioners installed during one of their last renovations. Guests in their reviews of Side Temple Hotel often note that their installation is an excellent solution, since with the help of such equipment guests can always provide for themselves the most comfortable temperature conditions, based on individual preferences.

Each apartment also has a private bathroom, which has everything you need to maintain hygiene. In addition to plumbing, it has a bath kit, which includes a pair of bathrobes, as many large terry towels, as well as soap accessories.

Conditions for children

In reviews of guests often noted that the hotel is perfect for families. Especially for those who prefer to spend their holidays with children. Side Temple Hotel provides some facilities that make children and their parents more comfortable on site.

One of them - the possibility of free cots in the room - it is ideal for sleeping a child aged 4 to 12 years. Also, the provision of individual places is carried out in public catering establishments - here for children there are special comfortable chairs.

In the open area, small guests can visit the small pool, which is equipped with everything necessary for the safety of their stay.

Entertainment

In Side Temple Hotel 3 * (Turkey / Side) there are several opportunities for entertaining tourists, thanks to which they do not have to be bored while relaxing. One of them is playing beach volleyball on a specially equipped area, which is available on the hotel‚Äôs private territory along the coastline.

Another favorite activity for guests is a visit to the massage room. According to them, the real professionals work here, who are able to immerse the client in a state of complete relaxation. All services that are provided here are paid, but this fact does not make them less popular.

Recently, evening programs have begun to be held at the Side Temple Hotel 3 * hotel. In the reviews of 2017, tourists began to celebrate bright shows called "Turkish Night". They are held once a week and gather at the site of almost all the guests. This is a very spectacular event, which leaves a lot of pleasant impressions.

Nutrition

Most of the guest comments indicate that the Side Temple Hotel offers fairly good buffet meals, which are served in the large restaurant for guests arriving on a package tour. According to them, quite original and nutritious dishes with excellent taste are served here. You can visit this institution on any day of the week from 8 a.m. to 8:30 p.m.

Apart from the restaurant there are also two bars, where the assortment contains a huge number of cocktails and drinks, among which there are both non-alcoholic and alcoholic ones. Local lemonades are in special demand here. Also, in the bars you can taste original snacks and real oriental sweets.



Car rental

All hotel guests have the opportunity to rent a car. Experienced travelers recommend that you definitely take this opportunity and go by car on an independent tour of the local attractions of Side and its environs.

Rent is provided by the day; in the case of using this service, a parking space for a vehicle is issued absolutely free.

Aquazone

Travelers admit that while staying at the hotel a huge part of their time, they spend on the outdoor sun terrace, which is perfectly arranged for relaxation under the hot sun and for water treatments.

Here, guests can comfortably sit on the sun loungers that are installed around the entire perimeter of the site. Near them there are large folding umbrellas, which during the particularly active action of sunlight can be used. Towels are provided to tourists for free. If desired, guests can head to the bar and chill out with a delicious cocktail.

In the very center of the terrace is a small pool with very clean water. Its area is 225 sq.m. As guests note, the water in it is constantly warm, but it is heated with the help of sunlight, which causes problems in the winter.

Visiting the aquazone is possible according to a certain regime: from 8 a.m. to 6 p.m.



Prices

A fairly affordable pricing policy operates at the Side Temple Hotel. In the reviews of 2017, many tourists shared the prices for vouchers that were installed after the renovation - on average they are about 22,000 rubles. As usual, this tour includes expenses related to the flight, transfer service and accommodation for a week together in a Standard room. Such tours include the all-inclusive concept, in which guests can eat in the local restaurant from the buffet.



Impressions about the rest in such a place among tourists remain mostly pleasant. They often recommend visiting his friends and relatives, talking about decent service and rather comfortable rooms in which a cozy atmosphere reigns.
