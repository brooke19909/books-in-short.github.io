Where is the hotel located

Numa Beach Hotel 5 * (Turkey) administratively belongs to the village of Avsallar. It is located 21 kilometers west of Alanya. The difference between Avsallar and other Turkish resorts is that it is always several degrees higher than in neighboring areas. A unique microclimate is created by hills and a bay protecting Avsallar from the north winds. Here even small, but no less sweet bananas ripen.

Getting to the hotel will not be difficult. After all, Avsallar is located on the highway that connects Alanya with Side. The last resort with the ruins of the ancient city is located forty kilometers from the hotel. Daily on this route mini-buses "dolmushi" ply. You can get from Alanya to Avsallar in half an hour. Antalya International Airport is one hundred and forty kilometers. Tourists should prepare for a two-hour shuttle. But if your plane lands at Gazipasa (Alanya) airport, then the distance from the ramp to the hotel will be only 60 km. Numa Beach Hotel 5 *, is located on the very shore of the Mediterranean Sea and has its own beach. It is located on the territory of the ancient port (II century BC. E.).

Numa Beach Hotel 5 *, as befits the Turkish "five", offers its guests spacious, bright rooms, equipped with everything necessary for a quality stay. Beach, Pools

Numa Beach Hotel 5 * reviews are called a hotel on the first line. From the building, guests will have to go to the beach for about a minute. The hotel has its own piece of coast.
