How to make a frog from a plastic bottle easy and simple?



Fans of various kinds of non-standard crafts probably wonder about how to make a frog out of a plastic bottle on their own. Usually this is either a figurine that will decorate the courtyard or summer cottage, or casket. There are other options for creating such crafts, but they are much more complicated. Therefore, we will not focus on them, but focus on what is the simplest and easiest. It is the two indicated options that relate to those.

Raw materials and tools

So how to make a frog out of a plastic bottle? First you need to ensure that you have all the right tools and materials at hand. First, you will definitely need two plastic containers with a volume of 2 liters. Better if they are green. If there are none, then others can be coated with paint. Surely you need tape. Also at hand should be scissors or a knife, a green insulating tape, marker and ruler. When creating a box, zipper is added to this list, which will close it, paper applications in the form of large eyes. The figurine needs three more containers of 0.5 liter capacity and a backing. Also, the eyes on this list will not be superfluous.



Figurine for the yard

Usually this craft is called the "frog princess from a plastic bottle." This name very accurately characterizes it. It really reminds everyone of the famous fairy-tale hero, which is located at your playground or homestead. The algorithm for its manufacture is as follows. At the first stage, the lower part of each of the 2-liter containers is cut off. The distance from the bottom should be 7-8 cm. At the place of the cut, they are applied to each other. It is imperative that the teeth from the lower and upper parts are opposite each other. All this is wrapped with tape. Appliques in the form of eyes are glued to the two front legs.

A crown is cut from one half-liter capacity. You can use for this as its conical section, and a straight line - at the discretion of the author. All this is glued on top with adhesive tape. If desired, it can be pre-coated with yellow (for plausibility).

Then the remaining 2 containers are used. They cut out the conical part between the lid and the straight section. From above it is wrapped with a green insulating tape. Next, the basis is drawn up. It is best done from a metal sheet, painted with blue paint and added on top of a green spot, for example (a sheet of lily). In the middle of the last is the figurine itself, made in advance. To the left and to the right of it, conical trimming is set - these are the paws that are located at the edges. This is done with liquid paint (in this case, it plays the role of glue). After everything is dry, the craft is ready. This is the first way to make a frog out of a plastic bottle, over.



Casket

The initial two components in this method are similar - these are the two lower parts of 2-liter containers. Lightning is installed inside and it is fixed with a thin wire, over which an insulating tape is wound. Next, the eyes are glued (not necessarily on the legs, as in the previous case). Then check the zipper with the help of closing and opening. That's it - the box is ready, and we have considered yet another way of making a frog out of a plastic bottle. It is very simple and well suited for our fashionistas. In such a box, you can store various jewelry and other jewelry.

Conclusion

It is very easy to make a frog from a plastic bottle. A photo of her is given in the article. It is important to understand one thing - in this material two of the most common ways to create such crafts are indicated, relying on which (and supplementing them with your imagination), you can get something special.
