Sports Palace in Izhevsk: address and opening hours

Sports facilities are open in almost every city. They motivate the population to lead an active lifestyle, and also allow you to spend your free time interestingly. So, the sports palace in Izhevsk is open both for adults and for the younger generation. Here you can find for yourself not only a good hobby, but also try to start a career as an athlete since childhood.

General information

The sports complex is well known to the townspeople, as many visit the pool in it. The Sports Palace (Izhevsk) has the largest bowl in the city. Depth varies from 1 to 5 meters. In total, the pool has 6 tracks 25 meters long. To purify water, special filters and chlorine are used. Visitors can come to regular swimming sessions, as well as relax with the whole family. Little children swim with their parents.

Adults need to bring a certificate from their GP about their health status. Kids up to the age of ten will need a certificate from a pediatrician, as well as test results. For students and schoolchildren there is a preferential system for entrance tickets. Residents of Izhevsk respond very positively about the pool and mark its service among the rest.

In addition to swimming training in the sports palace, you can enroll in a sports school. Children are trained by professional trainers. The institution regularly hosts competitions, to which many spectators come. They are attended by young athletes who pre-apply for participation. They also require health certificates. The sports school itself has been open since 2000.

In addition to swimming, for children in the sports palace (Izhevsk) there are other sections. Among them are art and gymnastics. There are separate activities for boys. Many children attend a school of drafts, where games are played in Russian and international drafts. The club opened the club "Tourist". In it, girls and boys are taught different types of gymnastics. For some schools in Izhevsk in the center there are basketball classes.

Sports Palace (Izhevsk): address

The sports facility is located on Sovetskaya Street, building No. 35. The working hours are as follows: from Monday to Friday from 7:30 to 22:00, on Saturday and Sunday from 9:00 to 21:00.

How to get there

You can find the sports palace in Izhevsk quite quickly. Nearby is a stop called the "Sports Palace". The following routes go to it:

  1. Trolleybuses 2, 2d, 4, 4d, 14.
  2. Buses 28, 40, 79, 281.
  3. Minibuses 10, 68.

It is located next to sports facilities. Nearby are the Central Stadium, the Zenit Sports Complex and the Izhstal Ice Palace.
