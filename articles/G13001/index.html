Bus station "Darnitsa" in Kiev: where is it, how to get there

The transport infrastructure of the capital of Ukraine, Kiev, is well developed. Road trips can be made to any ends. In addition to the central bus station, from where, of course, the most destinations are available, there are several more. One of them is a small bus station "Darnitsa", located in the Dnieper region.

Where exactly is this small bus station, in what directions you can go from it and other useful details will tell you right now.





Where is it and how to get there?

The Darnitsa bus station is located in the Dnieper district, on Gagarin Avenue, 1. Tram tracks run very close, just a few meters from the station platforms, and many bus lines stop nearby.

The bus station is located just in the middle between the two metro stations of the red line - Chernigovskaya and Lesnoy. The time to reach the destination from both stations will require the same amount. So choose the one to which it will be more convenient and faster to get to you.





You can go from Chernigovskaya metro station to the bus station in the following way. Having come to the surface from the side of the Novus supermarket, we go around it. We go along the bus stop (we see directions to Pereyaslav-Khmelnitsky, Cherkasy and others). If you walk quite a bit, you will already see a small building of the bus station "Darnitsa".

From the metro station "Lesnaya" to the bus station you can go as follows. You need to exit the metro from the side of the Bazar shopping pavilion and the Mayak shopping center. Then we go on the right side along the tram tracks. Literally in 10 minutes we will see the building with a sign "Bus station Darnitsa".

Departure directions

So we found out where the Darnitsa bus station is located (Kiev). It is the starting point for Chernihiv, Sumy directions. So, buses run from here to such famous cities as Slavutich, Chigirin, Glukhov, Oster, Kremechug, Kanev, Varva, Senkovka and others.

The routes pass through many small and large villages. At the request of passengers, drivers are ready to drop you off at stops in these settlements.

Every 5-15 minutes (depending on the route), buses leave from the bus station. So, for example, in the Brovary direction to the villages of Gogolev, Svetilnoye, Rusanov, Ploskoye, you can leave almost every hour, sometimes more often. The last buses in this direction leave at 20:45.

The reliability of regular buses is trustworthy. In extremely rare cases, one of a number of buses in a particular direction may not leave at the appointed time.

Mode of operation

The bus station operates daily without changes in the opening-closing mode. So, you can buy tickets at the box office from 6 a.m. to 20:50 p.m. In the small building of the bus station you can wait for your route.

On some buses that pass villages and villages of the Kiev region, tickets at the box office are not for sale. You can pay for the fare directly on the bus. Departure is made from the platforms of the bus station, which have the appropriate plates.





Summary

If you are on the Left Bank, for example in the Darnitsky and Dnieper regions, then the Darnitsa bus station is located very close to you. Where is she and how to get there, we tried to tell you as accurately as possible, taking into account practical details. Choose the desired route and go on a trip or on business with joy!

Chernihiv, Sumy, Cherkasy, many smaller cities and very small villages - there is an opportunity to get to these points from here. So far, the destination is Kiev, Left Bank, Dniprovskyi district. Here, at the terminal stations of the metro of the red line, is the bus station "Darnitsa". For business or guests feel free to go from here on the road! Enjoy your trips and wish you always be on time!
