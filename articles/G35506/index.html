Application `` Dog '' do it yourself

Applique classes are recommended for children from two years old, as they perfectly develop fine motor skills, accuracy, and creative skills.

Introduction to this type of creativity should start with simple pictures of geometric shapes, gradually folding them into flowers and animals.

One of the simplest pictures for children 3 years old is the application `` Dog ''. There are many different ways and methods of applications with which you can make a dog or puppy.

Let us consider in more detail how to perform several of them.

What is required for the application

You will need such materials and tools:

  • disposable plate;
  • a set of colored paper (brown, black, pink);
  • scissors;
  • glue;
  • glued eyes, but this is not necessary (although for small children this will greatly simplify the task).

Glue the dog together

To make your Dog paper applique beautiful, follow these guidelines.

  1. Fold the brown paper in half and cut a pear-like shape. There should be two such pieces. These will be the ears of the dog.
  2. Cut out three brown spots of irregular shape. You can even have different colors.
  3. Next, cut a large oval from black paper. It will be a nose and a small pink semicircle is a tongue.
  4. Now glue all parts of the muzzle onto a plate. Draw the mouth and glue the eyes. Eyes can be hooked ready, or you can independently cut them out of paper of the color you want.

So the application `` Dog '' is ready.

Alternatively, it is possible to form a picture not according to separate geometric shapes, but as a whole image.

To do this, draw a dog silhouette on paper and cut it out. If you are not good at drawing our four-legged brothers, this is not a big problem, just print a dog template (preferably on cardboard). Cut it out and circle on paper the desired color.

This template is useful to you, so that in the future you will get more than one application `` Dog ''. You can use this form, or find any other.



Next, just stick the cut dog and create some kind of surrounding background for it in the picture. Stick best on a solid base, such as cardboard.

Glue the dog out of geometric shapes

But for young children it will be more useful and interesting to make a picture of small parts, like a puzzle. Therefore, here is another way to apply on this topic.

  1. Cut two identical circles of dark brown paper.
  2. Cut two more circles from a lighter shade of brown. Cut them in half.
  3. Cut two ovals (eyes) and a small circle (nose) from black paper.
  4. Glue the first two circles together, as if making a snowman.
  5. Glue two halves of the circle like ears, and one like the tail.
  6. Make a face by gluing eyes and nose. Draw your mouth.

The application `` Dog '' is completed. Everything is very easy and simple. Children will be interested in making puppies on their own or under your strict guidance.

Over time, you can try other ways of applying a four-legged friend. The main thing is not to be afraid to experiment, and do not do everything according to the pattern. Make your own adjustments, as in any other creative activity.
