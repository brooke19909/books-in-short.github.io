Rest on the Black Sea (Ochakov): recreation facilities, services, prices

The southern city of Ochakov (Ukraine, Mykolaiv Oblast) is washed by the Black Sea and attracts tourists with the steppe natural landscape of protected areas, free sandy beaches stretching to infinity. On a sunny day, the whole family can visit the water park, small in size, but very clean, which Ochakov is proud of. Recreation facilities also deserve the attention of tourists, since everything was built here with an emphasis on maximum comfort.

Why Mr. Ochakov?

More than 17 thousand tourists come to relax on the Black Sea in the city of Ochakovo annually. The resort town has numerous cafes, markets, shops and even a diving club. Various excursion programs can be booked on site: hiking trips to the Kinburn Spit reserve , fishing off the island of Berezan, a trip to the historical and archaeological museum "Olbia" - a city abandoned by the Greeks in the 4th century AD.

Features of the resort area

The incredibly calm and safe Black Sea is an indisputable advantage for tourists who decide to visit Ochakov. Recreation centers in these parts are located in close proximity to the coastal zone. For those who like to observe the raging natural elements, this area is unlikely to be suitable, since the waves here rarely violate the smooth sea surface. The advantage of the resort is not only the golden sand on the beach, but also the sandy bottom of the bay. Both children and adults are pleased to wander barefoot through the water without fear of sharp pebbles and hard pebbles.

The cost of housing in the private sector of Ochakovo starts from 40 hryvnias per place and from 200 hryvnias for a small house, designed for 3 people (daily rates). In the city limits there are also many small tourist camps and pensions.

"Black Sea"

The main resort life is concentrated on the Black Sea spit (Ochakov). Recreation facilities ("Chernomorka", "Troyanda", "Talisman" and others) are located in this area. On the one hand, it is washed by the Black Sea, and on the other, by the Berezansky estuary. Locals call this coastal region of Chernomorka. Here, 5 km from Ochakovo, there are many bars, restaurants, discos, as well as tourist sites, designed for any wealth and taste.

Inexpensive rest offers the recreation center "Chernomorka", located at the very end of the Black Sea spit. On a spacious landscaped area, vacationers are waiting for more than 200 cottages worth from 50 hryvnia per day. Cooking is done in several kitchens with gas and refrigerators. You can also order meals in the dining room or bars.

The recreation center "Chernomorka" near Ochakovo has one of the largest and cleanest sandy beaches with attractions and sea activities. The safety of vacationers is monitored by a rescue station, where you can rent boats, catamarans or ride on boats.
