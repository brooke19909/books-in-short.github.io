Determine your tax tax debt

Our country has a well-regulated tax system. Moreover, tax revenues are the main source of revenue for the state budget at all levels. Accordingly, inspections are scrupulous in tracking the timeliness of the payment of mandatory payments and, in the event of debt formation, begin the enforcement process. That is why it is so important to independently monitor the formation of own taxes debts and pay them off in a timely manner.

Currently, the Federal Tax Service is constantly developing all kinds of innovations that make it easier and more comfortable for the taxpayer to interact with tax authorities. Answers to most of the questions that interest ordinary citizens can be quickly found on the official website of the service, without wasting time on long lines in territorial divisions. For example, using the Internet resource of the tax service of your region, you can find out the tax debt under the TIN.
To begin with, we will determine what a TIN is and where it can be taken. The abbreviation TIN is deciphered as follows - an individual taxpayer number. In our country, it is assigned to each taxpayer and is required to be received. In order to have a TIN, you must contact the inspectorate at your place of registration and fill out the necessary form. TIN will be issued in the form of a certificate certified by the seal and signature of the head of inspection. For the first time in their life, individuals receive TIN certificates absolutely free of charge. In case of loss of this document, for its restoration it will be necessary to pay a certain amount of state duty.

So, back to the question of how you can determine your tax debt under the TIN. The easiest way is to turn to the information Internet resource of the tax service of your region. Then in the window that appears will reflect the amount of tax debt, if any. You can also get acquainted with the contacts of the tax office, where you are registered as a taxpayer.

Thus, if you do not agree with the existing tax arrears, you have the opportunity to contact your inspection either by e-mail, or by phone, or by going there in person. In any case, tax inspectors will prepare a complete answer to all your questions and provide a receipt for paying off the existing debt.
If you do not have access to the Internet, or if you want to resolve all issues immediately on the spot, you can immediately contact the inspection in person or by phone. Tax inspectors will perform their duties in the same manner. The only thing worth remembering is that specialists will gather all the necessary information about the taxpayer much faster, provided that they will first know his TIN. Therefore, the combination of these numbers is still worth knowing when contacting the tax office.

Thus, now you know how to find out TIN debts. This procedure is absolutely not long and not laborious. Therefore, it is preferable to still spend a few minutes to find out your tax ID tax debt than to learn about its existence much later, by receiving a message about this from the bailiffs or justices of the peace.
