Guest House "Diana" in Adler, st. Enlightenment, 154 a: reviews

Krasnodar Territory is a tourist region. Residents from different parts of the country come here on vacation. Guest house "Diana" in Adler awaits visitors all year round. There are all conditions for a comfortable and interesting stay. Hotel owners value every guest and try to organize a holiday in the most comfortable conditions.

Where is it located and how does it work?

Guest house "Diana" in Adler on the street. Enlightenment is located 10 minutes from the spa town and 3 minutes from the beach. The hotel is located on the first coastal line of the Black Sea.





The owners of the hotel are waiting for guests throughout the year. Book a room in advance and make a prepayment. Photos of rooms on the Internet, according to guest reviews, are fully true.

Vacation with children

It accepts families with children of any age of the Diana State Duma in Adler (Enlightenment, 154). Visitor reviews are mostly positive. The hosts know how to find contact with any child, and after a rest, young visitors have only pleasant memories from communicating with them.

The beach is very close to the hotel, so the road to it will not hamper either parents or children. Near the hotel there are many interesting entertainment places that will appeal to all vacationers.

Accommodations

"Diana" in Adler is a 4-storey hotel with different rooms in terms of comfort. On the lower floors are rooms of a higher class. On the upper rooms are "economy".

Inside the building there is a spiral staircase. The corridors are decorated in white, which visually creates a large amount of space.





Vacationers can pre-book one of their favorite rooms:

  • "economy" for 3 people - one double and single bed, TV, shower and toilet on the floor, split system;
  • "economy" for 4 people - double and two single beds, TV, air conditioning, bathroom and shower on the floor;
  • "rooms with conveniences" for 2,3,4 people - equipped with upholstered furniture, TV, air conditioning, refrigerators, toilet and shower in the rooms, some have a private balcony;
  • bungalow rooms for 3 and 4 people - double and single beds, TV, air conditioning, shower and toilet, fridge, small enclosed courtyard for families living in the bungalow;
  • two-room suite in a separate block on the ground floor - equipped with all necessary furniture, own equipped kitchen, bathroom and shower, TV, air conditioning, ironing board with iron in the lobby, private parking, accommodation for up to 8 people;
  • rooms with amenities for 3 and 4 people, located on the 1st and 2nd floors - have a high level of comfort, modern wooden furniture, air conditioning, TV, refrigerator, shower and toilet, private balcony.




For an additional payment in all rooms one more sleeping place can be provided. Vacationers who live in a bungalow have the opportunity in the evenings to sit on a separate courtyard in a comfortable place.

Features of the hotel "Diana" in Adler

The hotel occupies a fairly large area. There is a spacious courtyard, which is as green as possible. Many varied flower beds pleasantly surprise travelers. The guest house was built in 2009, so the building is in excellent condition. All communications are in good condition, and virtually no interruptions in water supply.

For holidaymakers who come by private car, parking is equipped. There are no noisy entertainment venues near the hotel, therefore, in the evening and at night, the peace of tourists is not disturbed.

There is a modern kitchen in the backyard of the complex. There are several sets of dishes, a gas oven, two refrigerators and a microwave oven. An area for eating is organized - comfortable tables and chairs, TV. On this territory, neighbors by numbers often spend friendly gatherings, and their communication develops into real friendship.





On the territory there are small places for relaxation under sun umbrellas. In their shade, you can hide from the heat of the day, relax in the fresh air after an active stay at sea. With pets vacationers are not accepted.

Service

Common toilets, kitchens and the yard are cleaned daily by staff. The rooms of the governess restore order at the request of vacationers. The rooms have all the necessary equipment, so guests can do the cleaning themselves if desired.





It is possible to use a washing machine for an additional fee. The owners of the complex provide a shuttle service. At any time of the day, vacationers will be greeted for an additional fee by car at the airport or at the railway station.

Beach

The seashore is 150 meters from Diana in Adler. The road will take about 5-10 minutes on an asphalt road. Along the way there are trading stalls with souvenirs and goods for a comfortable stay.

The beach has separate sections with sand, equipped with breakwaters. Basically, it has large and small pebbles. Cleaning is carried out daily. It operates rental of sun loungers and parasols.

Along the shore there are small cafes where you can hide in the shade, eat or drink a glass of cold drink. On the beach there are all kinds of attractions and entertainments:

  • trampolines;
  • waterslides;
  • riding on bananas and tablets;
  • scuba diving club;
  • boats for walking on the sea;
  • ATVs.

On the shore there is a daily rescue service.
