Review of the bread maker Panasonic SD-2511 WTS

To constantly have flavored bread at home is not a problem at present. And for this you do not need to run to the store, but rather buy a Panasonic bread maker. Devices of this manufacturer are famous for excellent quality, reliability, long life. The whole process of making bread is fully automated. A person only needs to fill up the necessary ingredients according to the recipe and include the appropriate program.

The lineup of this brand is quite large. One of the best copies is the Panasonic SD-2511. What is the peculiarity of this bread machine? Why did she win the love of many buyers?



Short description

This model is available in white and black. The design looks classic notes. The case is outwardly inconspicuous, there is a laconicism and even somewhat minimalism. Panasonic SD-2511 is a device with dimensions of 25.6 √ó 38.9 √ó 38.2 cm, designed for baking weighing 1 kg. Forms for bread - loaves. The uniqueness of the breadmaker is that it is possible to choose the color of the crust.

The case is cylindrical, made of plastic. The control buttons are on the top panel. There is also a display that displays information about processes and modes. The lid is very comfortable, everything is thought out for loading products. The network cable one meter long can be folded into a special compartment. This model prepares baked goods with filling thanks to the presence of a dispenser.



Advantages of Panasonic SD-2511

What are the advantages of this model? It is worth noting that there are practically no negative reviews on the network, which indicates high quality and excellent functionality. So, let's look at the interesting model SD-2511.

  • Build quality. The famous Panasonic brand proudly holds the highest level. There are no creaks, cracks. Good quality materials.
  • Bakery products. Bread and pastry are excellent. The bottom does not stick, the crust is fragrant and crispy. In the recipes that come with the Panasonic SD-2511 WTS bread machine, the optimal ratio of all proportions is observed.
  • Ease of Management. The panel where the buttons are located is touch. There is a transcript on the cover. Everything is signed, even a child can figure it out.
  • Easy care. Important: when working with such devices, all surfaces and baking dishes can be cleaned quickly and simply.
  • A large number of programs. There are a total of 14. The machine kneads the dough, working with several types, for example, yeast-free, wheat, gluten-free, rye. It is also possible to cook muffins, jam. The device is equipped with an accelerated baking mode.
  • The presence of a timer and a temperature sensor. These options are quite important, as the bread will always be hot and baked by a certain time.
  • Price. At present, the Panasonic SD-2511 model can be purchased on average for 12-15 thousand rubles.
