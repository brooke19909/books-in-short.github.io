Such different patterns of elephants

Sometimes you want to make some souvenir for a loved one with your own hands. But something stops all the time. Either there is no pattern, then the material is not suitable. In fact, everything is simple: there will be a desire, and an opportunity will appear. In this article we will show very simple patterns of an elephant, according to which even a beginner needlewoman can sew a toy.

Brooch



Do you think the silhouette of an elephant can only be made of felt? Nothing like this. A brooch can be made from anything. For example, from plastic. To build such an ornament, you will need a pattern of an elephant. It‚Äôs easy to draw. Draw a circle and transform it into a cartoon animal as shown in the picture above. Having come up with a pattern, you can start creating the product. We roll out white plastic, cut out the silhouette of the animal from it on a pre-prepared basis. Now you need to decorate the decoration. You should start from the back and smoothly move down. You can decorate a brooch with either strips of plastic of a different color, or beads, rhinestones and beads. The main thing here is not to get carried away and not to forget to make the animal an eye. When the work is done, it must be baked in the oven, and a pin attached to the finished product.

Pillow toy



Today it‚Äôs fashionable to do utilitarian things. Therefore, we want to bring to your attention a pattern of an elephant, which can be used not only for room decor, but also as a pillow. Sewing such a toy is very easy. You need to find a suitable fabric, preferably a cheerful color. But you should focus not only on your taste preferences, but also on the interior of the room for which a pillow is created. We proceed to the creation of the product. First of all, you need to print the picture above, or make an elephant pattern with your own hands. We place on the material two parts of the body, one for the abdomen, and four for the ear. It is worth remembering that paired patterns should always be mirrored. We pass to tailoring. The first step is to sew the side parts with the stomach. Then connect the back together, leaving a small hole. A toy should be turned out through it and stuffed with a padding polyester. Now you need to sew the ears together and sew them to the toy. Optionally, you can detail the pillow by adding her eyes and tail.

Seated elephant



Even a child of 10-12 years old can sew such a toy. To make a figure, you need a pattern. The elephant will be multi-detail. A suitable fabric should be selected. Toys made of fur look interesting, but if it is not available, then you can use drapes or fleece. We print out the picture, which is attached above, and then we begin to create. First of all, you need to collect your head. We grind together parts with a trunk and a wedge, which will be in the back of the head. Now we turn the head and stuff it with a padding polyester. Then proceed to the manufacture of the body. We grind tucks and sew two parts together. Do not forget to leave a hole to turn out and fill the body. Now you can sew on two finished parts of the toy. It remains to supplement the elephant with its paws. Details of the two forelimbs are stitched together. But heels will need to be inserted into the hind legs. Therefore, you first need to sew the hind legs to the sole, and only then grind the details of the leg. All limbs should be sewn into place. The last thing to do is to attach ears and eyes to the elephant.

Decorative toy



Such a thing is most often used as a sachet. Pattern toy elephants is very simple. Any silhouette is drawn and mirrored. Now you need to find fabric with a fun print and breathe life into the contour of the animal. We sew the two parts together and stuff them with either odorous grass, such as thyme, or synthetic winterizer. Sew a hole. For greater resemblance to the original, you need to attach ears and a tail to the toy. Eyes are optional, but with them the elephant will look more expressive.
