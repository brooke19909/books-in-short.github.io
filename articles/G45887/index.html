Grand Ideal Premium Hotel 5 * (Marmaris, Turkey): photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

Marmaris today is one of the most popular resorts in the Republic of Turkey. It attracts tourists of various nationalities with its magnificent climate, excellent beaches, calm warm sea, picturesque nature and, of course, the presence of a lot of entertainment and hotels for every taste.
