Resort InterContinental Pattaya Resort 5 *: description of rooms, service, additional services

Thailand has for many years held the title of one of the most popular destinations among Russians. You can relax here at any time of the year. But most often, tourists rush here in the winter, when the local resorts have sunny and very hot weather. In Thailand, there are several coastal cities that have their own tourist focus.

But Pattaya is considered a universal resort. Youth companies can stay here in an inexpensive hotel located in the city. And families with children often choose luxurious complexes built right on the coast of the sea. For example, a positive reputation among tourists has the resort InterContinental Pattaya 5 *, considered one of the best in Pattaya. It is about him that we will tell in more detail in the article below.

Features of rest in Pattaya

Pattaya is a lively resort where life boils at any time of the year. It is considered an inexpensive place to stay. Therefore, tourists often flock here, trying to save on a ticket. Its significant advantage is its proximity to Bangkok and a major international airport.

Many tourists planning a vacation in winter are interested in the weather in Pattaya in February and January. These months are considered the peak season of the holidays. Then, in mid-March, the rainy season begins. It is still hot at this time, but high humidity and cloudiness can ruin the long-awaited vacation. But in February, the weather in Pattaya is often sunny and hot. In the afternoon, the air temperature rises to 33-35 degrees, and the sea itself warms up to 27 degrees. It gets a little cooler at night. The temperature can drop to 25 degrees.

Pattaya is conditionally divided into several large areas, each of which has its own focus. The northern part is suitable for a democratic holiday. There are a lot of cheap housing, cafes and bars, but not much entertainment. Central Pattaya is an ideal place for active tourists. It is here that the whole resort life is seething. A huge number of shops, bars, nightclubs and massage parlors are open everywhere. But in this part of the resort, a lot of people always rest, and not all of them behave decently.

If you want to find a quiet place to stay, then choose southern Pattaya in Thailand. This is where the hotel described in the article is located. It is here that tourists most often come with young children. However, the cost of living in this area is significantly higher than in the north. Often, it is here that the respectable 5-star hotels of Pattaya are located.

Where exactly is the hotel located?

Pattaya Resort is located on the east coast of the Gulf of Thailand in Thailand. It used to be a fishing village, but now local beaches are considered one of the best in the country. The hotel itself is built right on the coast, so you can get to the sea in just 5 minutes. It is noteworthy that all residential buildings here are built on a hill. Therefore, tourists have to constantly go down and climb the numerous stairs.

It has already been noted above that the hotel was built in the southern part of Pattaya, therefore it is quiet and peaceful here, day and night. The resort center is approximately 4 km away. You can get to it by tuk-tuk, taxi or rented transport. Near the hotel there are practically no large infrastructure facilities: restaurants, bars and shops. From almost all sides, it is surrounded by other 5 stars Pattaya hotels. Among them are such well-known complexes as Royal Cliff Beach Hotel and RCG Suites.

The nearest international airport is located near Bangkok. The distance to it is approximately 90 km. This is where most of the Moscow-Pattaya flights fly. After landing, tourists get to the resort by bus or taxi. The flight itself takes more than 8 hours.

basic information

This five-star complex was built in 2005. But do not think that the hotel has an outdated room fund. In the absence of an influx of visitors, cosmetic repairs are regularly held here. The last of them was completed in 2016.

The hotel itself occupies a large territory located on a hill near the seashore. Its area is about 25,000 m 2 . All of it is almost completely planted with tropical trees, shrubs and flowers. For tourists, 7 four-story and 10 two-story residential buildings have been built here. For secluded relaxation you can rent a separate villa. There are only 2 on the hotel territory. At the same time, more than 350 tourists can stay in the complex, who are accommodated in 156 comfortable rooms.

When buying a ticket, you can pay a paid transfer. Then, after the flight on the route Moscow - Pattaya, a comfortable bus equipped with air conditioning will pick you up from the airport. He will take you directly to the hotel. Upon check-in, an identification card and a credit card are required. The complex accepts tourists with children of any age, but registration with pets is strictly prohibited. Check-in starts at 15:00. After the end of the vacation, the living rooms must be vacated no later than 12:00 local time.

Additional amenities in the rooms

All rooms of the InterContinental Pattaya Resort 5 * complex are equipped with high-quality and comfortable furniture. The bedroom has comfortable single or double beds. In the room you can find wardrobes, bedside tables, a desk and chairs. All rooms have an open balcony or terrace if located on the ground floor. They are equipped with a separate dining set, as well as a clothes dryer.

Each room has additional equipment and appliances. Most of them are provided free of charge, but some amenities are not included in the price. So, tourists can take advantage of:

  • a plasma TV to which a set of satellite television is connected;
  • individual air conditioning with remote control;
  • a safe for storing valuables, documents and money;
  • a minibar and a refrigerator for drinks (filling is provided for a fee);
  • wireless internet;
  • a set for making hot drinks, which includes an electric kettle and dishes;
  • ironing facilities, including iron;
  • DVD player and wired telephone;
  • hair dryer.

All guests are provided with a set of bath accessories. It includes not only shampoo and shower gel, but also a moisturizing lotion, hat, toothpaste brush. Men are given a separate shaving kit. All hygiene items are replenished daily. Housekeeping is provided daily. During her maids also change towels and bed linen.

More on Catering

When paying for your stay at the InterContinental Pattaya Resort, you can choose one of three catering systems:

  • BB - only morning meals;
  • HB - half board, which includes breakfast and lunch;
  • FB - full board with full three meals a day.

All meals are served in the main restaurant. He begins to work from 06:00. In the morning a buffet is served for its visitors. Day and evening tourists are served on a fixed menu. It is worth noting that drinks are not included in the price, therefore they will have to be paid separately.

Only a bottle of drinking water is brought to your room daily for free. You can buy drinks and cocktails in two bars, one of which is located on the beach. In addition, two restaurants specializing in international, Thai and Asian cuisine are open in the territory. Their visit is also paid separately.

Service and extras at InterContinental Pattaya Resort

This five-star hotel has its own developed infrastructure. Most of the services are included in the price, but some of them are provided for a fee. Tourists can take advantage of the following infrastructure facilities:

  • 24-hour reception, which also provides currency exchange;
  • car parking;
  • a computer corner where Internet access is provided;
  • a lounge equipped with a TV;
  • ATM;
  • safes for storing valuables.

The hotel provides a number of additional services. For example, for a fee, tourists can rent a car or take their clothes to the laundry. For businessmen, the hotel has a conference center, consisting of 6 rooms. They can accommodate from 30 to 200 delegates.

Beach and Pools

Private beach is a significant advantage of this hotel. Its length is about 100 meters. The coast itself is completely sandy, but the entrance to the sea is rocky, so before swimming it is recommended to wear special shoes so as not to cut yourself. Free access for tourists to beach loungers and umbrellas, showers and toilets.

If you do not want to swim in the sea, you can swim in one of the three pools located on the hotel premises. All of them are filled with fresh water. Next to them is also equipped with a terrace with sunbeds, umbrellas and mattresses.

Entertainment

Often, tourists come to the InterContinental Pattaya Resort hotel in search of a calm and measured rest on the sea. Therefore, a lot of entertainment is not here. At any time, tourists can visit the gym, the entrance to which is free.

The hotel has a spacious spa, but its services are not included in the price. It offers a huge selection of beauty and wellness services, including the famous Thai massage, as well as access to the sauna and steam room.

There are practically no sports grounds on the hotel territory, but if you wish, you can sign up for paid golf lessons. They are held on the field, located near the complex. There is no animation and entertainment program on the hotel territory, so tourists will have to plan their vacation on their own.

Conditions for accommodating children

As a rule, the InterContinental Pattaya Resort complex is a favorite vacation spot for tourists with small children. They are attracted to a favorable location, because it is located in a calm area, where it is always quiet at night and there are no drunken youth walking late through the streets.

The hotel accepts tourists with kids of all ages. If you come to rest with a baby, then when you check in, you can ask to bring a separate crib to the room. It is provided for a fee, and their number is limited, so it is better to notify the administration in advance.

As expected, the restaurant has high chairs for feeding. The administrator can also call you a nanny at your request, but her services are not included in the price.

The hotel offers for its young guests a standard set of entertainment. A separate shallow pool is equipped for them, which is filled with fresh water. The hotel has a playground and a children's room. There is also a mini club in season, which accepts all children from 2 to 11 years old.

Hotel criticism

But the hotel also has its drawbacks. In their reviews, tourists note that the service at the InterContinental Pattaya Resort is not ideal, unfortunately. Therefore, before the trip, they recommend that you familiarize yourself with the negative feedback so as not to spoil your planned vacation. What did the tourists most dislike about this hotel? In the reviews, they indicate the following disadvantages of local service:

  • Lack of infrastructure facilities near the hotel. Yes, it is close to the sea, but you can‚Äôt get a walk along the promenade or go to a restaurant here. To do this, you will have to go to the center. Fortunately, every day free shuttles run to him.
  • The hotel rooms are very dark even during the day. There are few lamps in the rooms, so they do not save. Especially severe lack of light in the bathroom. Tourists complain that in the dark it is difficult to shave and paint.
