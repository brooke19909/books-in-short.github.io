What is an iPod? For the uninitiated

This article focuses on one of Apple's products, the iPod portable player. The manufacturer is quite famous. But what is an iPod? Why is it better than players of the same class? What are its advantages and disadvantages?



If we turn to history, it will become quite understandable why the question: "What is an iPod?" - Many music lovers do not arise in principle. Apple Corporation launched the release of players since 2001. And the first in this lineup was the iPod "Shuffle". The main feature of it was the presence of a hard disk containing thousands of songs. Outwardly, he was distinguished by a control mechanism. It was a disk on the front of the device. Over time, new generations of Apple players from mechanical control switched to touch. Such an external feature has done its job in brand recognition. What is an iPod? This is a player with control in the form of a wheel, circle or disk, as some users call it. Minimalism and classicism are the appearance of iPods. Photos of him, widespread thanks to the advertising campaign, well reflect these stylish trends.

However, every Apple portable audio device with all

external similarity has distinctive features. This is size, weight, color, and memory size. Such a variety allows any user to choose an iPod that is suitable for any characteristics. The price for each device remains approximately the same.

Answering the question: "What is an iPod?" - I want to note that this sound-reproducing product of the Apple Corporation, unlike its other devices, can be synchronized not only with a computer, but also with music centers and car radios. Which makes it not only a player, but also a device for storing, storing and carrying music files. Moreover, such a carrier accommodates up to 160 GB of information.
