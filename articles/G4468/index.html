Craft from disposable do-it-yourself spoons

Today, no one is surprised by the use of disposable tableware. But it‚Äôs a shame to throw out spoons, forks, plates and glasses half an hour after the meal. There are many options to extend their life. Let them please some more time. The only question is how to make from them such decorative products that will delight others.

Decorate the wall





The easiest option: decorate the wall in the kitchen with such spoons. The amount will depend on the size of the wall and the desire of the owner of the kitchen. You will need to take plastic disposable spoons, purchase acrylic paint with a brush, glue and a wooden tablet.

The selection of the color palette and the location of the spoons on the tablet depends on the imagination of the one who will undertake the implementation of such an unusual wall panel.

Vase





If the hostess of the apartment really likes to have fresh flowers on the table every day, then the unusual craft from disposable spoons will look very original. A beautiful flower vase is very easy to perform. You need to take a can, plastic spoons, scissors, glue, acrylic paint and a brush for applying paint. Now you need to do the bank. It can be painted by choosing the right color shade. Cut off the upper part from plastic spoons, apply paint to these blanks in the tone of a tin painted can. The dried parts of the spoons must be glued to the jar. As the fantasy tells: inward or outward with the convex side of the blanks.

Clock

It turns out a beautiful craft from disposable spoons in the form of watches, placed in the sun. You will need 250 pieces of plastic spoons, scissors, superglue, polystyrene, watches, bolts with a screwdriver, a clerical knife. First you need to cut a circle with a diameter of 45 centimeters with the help of an office knife from polystyrene foam. If there is a desire, you can perform crafts of a larger diameter or smaller.

Mark the center of the circle in which the watch will then be placed. Cut off the upper main parts of the spoons. Now it follows, starting from the edge of the circle, moving towards the center, glue the spoons so that the foam would be completely closed. Spoons can be left the same white and natural, or you can give them a different color scheme. Before you place the watch, you need to prepare a hole in the foam on the back side, which will help to easily change the battery in the watch. The watch can be replaced with a photograph or a mirror.

Christmas tree

Very often they make crafts from disposable spoons for New Year's contests and exhibitions. In this case, you can advise making an unusual Christmas tree. At the end of the work, you can paint it green and attach miniature toys to it.

And before you begin, you will need 100 plastic spoons, a paper cone, glue, as always, scissors and acrylic paint with a brush. Release the spoons from the handles using scissors, stick the top parts of the spoons in a checkerboard pattern on the bottom of the cone, moving to the top of the head.

Craft from disposable spoons can serve well for gardeners. From such disposable tableware bright labels for beds are obtained with the designation of those plants that inhabit the beds.

Garland of flowers





Craft from disposable spoons can help out when decorating a scene for a home holiday. A garland of flowers resembling spring flowers will look gorgeous.

To perform such crafts from disposable spoons, you need to take the best multi-colored spoons of pink, yellow and blue. You will also need dense cardboard or fiberboard, a brush, paint, pompons made of woolen thread, glue and a thick thread on which the whole garland will hang. Cut out three equal-sized triangles from cardboard, each of which can be painted. Spoons are divided into two parts: the handle and the convex part. The second parts will serve as the petals of spring plants. One flower consists of six petals. In each triangle, determine the middle and glue in a circle all the second parts of the spoons. Add a colored wool pompom to the center of each flower. In the triangles, make holes and pass through them the prepared thick thread. The garland is ready. The number of triangles and colors can be increased. It all depends on the area to be decorated.

Musical instruments

Crafts from disposable spoons with their own hands can help mom create for her baby a musical instrument for a noise orchestra. What is most often used by children in these activities? Drums, harmonica, children's pipes. A child with maracas in his hand will look extraordinary and will cause a lot of enthusiastic looks. How to make such a toy? There is nothing easier. You need to take a plastic egg from kinder surprise, two plastic spoons, scotch tape and rice. The box from under the kinder must be filled with rice in a small amount, closed, hold the container between the spoons and pasted with colored tape.
