general information





The doors of the Surgutneftegas sanatorium (Lermontovo), the photo of which can be seen above, were first opened in 1976. Over all these years, more than 220 thousand people have visited the dispensary, of which about 45 thousand are children.

Today, the Surgutneftegas sanatorium is a modern health resort that has a powerful production base. All guests of the boarding house have full treatment, food and an unforgettable vacation.

Number of rooms





The guests of the Surgutneftegaz sanatorium are offered to stay in the following rooms:

  • one-room single rooms of the first category (area 12 sq. m);
  • one-room double for the first category (area 12 sq. m);
  • two-room double suites (area 24 sq. m).

In total, the number of rooms of the Surgutneftegas sanatorium totals 389 apartments. The rooms are located in two seven-story buildings.

All apartments have single or double beds, bedside tables, wardrobes, tables and chairs, as well as televisions, telephones, small refrigerators and an electric kettle. Additionally, the rooms include bathrobes, towels, slippers, sewing kits, personal hygiene kits, dishes, and a hairdryer. The apartments are equipped with air conditioning. All rooms are equipped with a shower.

Around the residential buildings extends a large well-groomed area with flower beds and places for relaxation.
