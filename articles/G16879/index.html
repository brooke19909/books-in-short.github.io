Actress and Clowness Natalia Fisson

A bright, smiling, attractive woman looks at us from a photograph. This is a famous actress Natalia Fisson. In her life there was a place of love, joy, success. But a bereavement awaited Natalia in 2014. This winter, her beloved husband died, with whom she went a long life. The role of the wife of a famous director, perhaps, was central to her life. And she, thanks to her energy and cheerfulness, was for Vadim Fisson a support in the difficult moments of his life.





Disease is not a hindrance

Not everyone knows that he lacked both legs. But this is true. He lost the first due to a serious illness, even before he met his future wife. Not to lose heart after his second leg was taken from him, Natalia Fisson helped. When this happened, they had been married for several years. Thanks to her care, Vadim graduated from LGITMIK, in which she studied. Only Natalia Fisson studied at the acting department, and her husband at the directing.

Way to dream

The first place of work of the actress was the theater "Lyceum". She worked in it from 1981 to 1985. Then, wanting to receive a special education, which she had not had before, Natalia Fisson entered the Leningrad State Institute of Theater, Music and Cinematography. In it, the actress graduated from graduate school in the direction of the stage movement. Those who are close to the world of humor know who Natalia Fisson is. Her biography was replenished with interesting facts about professional activity precisely thanks to perseverance and determination. These qualities helped her to receive many awards and recognition of talent.





Granite science

Fisson Natalia Vladimirovna was born in Leningrad. This happened on May 11, 1964. In order to develop her skills, she studied a lot, and graduated not only from the institute in Leningrad, but also from the Brodway Dance Center in America in 1996, and the 1997 Clown School Fillip Golie in England.
