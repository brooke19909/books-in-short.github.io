Beautiful floral pattern

A great activity for any woman is knitting. The fantasy pattern presented now consists of only 24 loops. It fits in this way:

1st row: two loops knitted on the wrong side, rapport 1 (make a broach, remove one loop, then knit the next one and stretch a loop through it that was removed, double crochet and two facial), simple broach, 4 facial, five double crochets, again 4 facial two knit facial together, rapport 1, 2 wrong.

2nd row: a pair of facial loops, rapport 1 * (sew two loops at once on the back, double crochet, two back), knit two loops together on the wrong, 3 wrong, 3 wrong, now knit one wrong from each yarn, 3 wrong, knit crossed wrong side two loops, rapport 1 *.

3rd row: two purl stitches, rapport 1, make 1 simple broach, a pair of facial stitches, (one front + double crochet) x five times, two front stitches, two stitches immediately knit the front stitch, repeat 1, 2 wrong stitches.

4th row: a pair of faces, a rapport of 1 *, knit two loops at the same time, 12 purl, together with a crossed back, knit two loops, a rapport of 1 *, 2 faces.

5th row: two purl, rapport 1, 1 regular broach, 10 facial, two loops to knit the front, rapport 1, two purl.

6th and 8th rows: two facial, rapport 1 *, 12 facial, rapport 1 *, two facial.

7th row: two purl, rapport 1, 12 purl, rapport 1, 2 purl.

Pigtail
