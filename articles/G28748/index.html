Rest in Koktebel: reviews of vacationers

According to vacationers, vacation in Koktebel is a real pleasure, which is associated with a wonderful atmosphere of warmth and constant fun. As tourists note, in this town there are a large number of interesting places that will be interesting to visit for representatives of different age categories. Often, whole families come to this village, since many consider Koktebel to be the ideal place for families with children.

What features does rest in Koktebel have? Where can I go and what to see? About all this further.

general information

In reviews of Koktebel, it is often said about the favorable location of the village. The village is located in a very picturesque place located in the eastern part of the Crimean peninsula. Its nature is a harmonious combination of beautiful hills, blue and insanely warm sea, as well as original vegetation. The nature inherent in these places was praised in their works of literature and painting by many famous poets, writers and artists.

What can be said about the nature prevailing in the village? Reviews of Koktebel often tell about the warm water washing the coast of the town, as well as the large number of sunny days in the year that can be observed while staying here. This is especially true of the height of the holiday season - the summer period.

Beach vacation

A huge number of tourists prefer to visit Koktebel in the summer, when the air temperature rises slightly above 30 degrees and the Black Sea waters warm up to the optimal rate for swimming for both adults and children. It is a beach holiday that attracts travelers from all over Russia in this place. And this, I must say, is not at all surprising.

The beaches of Koktebel are famous for their shallow water. As experienced tourists say in their comments about staying on vacation here, the real depth here begins a hundred meters from the coast. Such shallow water, where the water quickly warms up, allows you to have a wonderful vacation with the kids, without worrying much about their safety.

As practice shows, in the midst of the holiday season, the beach strip of Koktebel is completely occupied by tourists. That is why many travelers prefer to organize their stay in private pensions, which have their own beaches closed to strangers.

Among the beaches there are several that are especially popular. Among them is Surf, which is located within walking distance from the famous volcano Kara-Dag. However, as tourists note in their reviews of the beaches of Koktebel, the infrastructure is not very well developed, so if you want to have a snack, you will have to leave your place and go to a cafe located outside its territory. However, despite this, a large number of vacationers Surf attracts with its clear water, picturesque landscape, as well as the ability to find unique gems.

Among the popular beaches, pebble is also often mentioned, which is located opposite the Voloshin House-Museum - a local attraction. It runs along the Embankment, where throughout the day and all night the music does not calm down, travelers have fun and relax. Fans of outdoor activities here will be very interesting, since the beach offers a large number of extreme and other types of entertainment.

In addition to the aforementioned beaches, tourists very often recommend visiting sites located near the boarding houses "Papaya", "Blue Bay", as well as "Primorye".

In reviews of Koktebel, it is often noted that fans of an ideal tan of the whole body have the opportunity to visit a nudist beach, which is located at the foot of Junge hill, which is at the very end of the embankment.

sights

Almost all tourists who organize their holidays in Koktebel, in their reviews tell that in this village there are a large number of places where you can go. Many of them are ideal for a family trip with a child.

What sights of the town attract the most travelers? In reviews of vacationers in Koktebel in 2017, a visit to a vintage wine factory is often recommended, where guests are offered a tasting of the best drinks made from fruits grown in local vineyards. You can also book a whole tour here with a large team, where the guide will tell interesting stories. If desired, tourists can buy for themselves several collectible copies of a delicious and insanely aromatic drink.

A lot of travelers are attracted by the water park, which is located next to the aquarium. Both of these places are very popular among both adults and children. Families often spend the whole day here, having fun on water slides in the open.

Reviews about Koktebel very often offer recommendations on visiting the dolphinarium. Here, according to tourists, you can not only watch an interesting performance with dolphins and seals, but also swim with them in the same pool or take a joint photo.

Fans of wildlife are advised to visit the famous Karadag reserve, in the territory of which there is an extinct volcano of the same name. However, in the comments, they also note that an independent excursion to such a place is impossible - it is necessary to organize it as a whole group. In addition, it is suitable exclusively for the strongest and most enduring, since the tourist's path lies through a large number of ascents and descents, and the total length of the road is a little over 7 kilometers.

As you know, one of the most popular attractions of Koktebel is the Golden Gate, which are located in the sea. They can also be visited, however, access to the location of the historical monument is possible only by boat, which is available for rent.

Private holiday

Almost all tourists who plan their vacation in this village think about where to stay. Many vacationers in reviews of Koktebel recommend renting rooms in the private sector. In the midst of the holiday season in the town, you can rent a small house or a separate room - it all depends on the preferences of the guests, as well as on their number.

As practice shows, a large company is best to stay in a house somewhere near the sea. It is also convenient for families with small children. Some of the advantages of this type of housing are noticed by travelers who have repeatedly rested with a child. They say that, having your own kitchen, which is in the private sector, you can independently prepare delicious food for your baby, being confident in its freshness and quality.

Couples or single travelers most often prefer to stay in private rooms, which are also in the private sector. In reviews of vacationers about Koktebel it is said that this option is quite inexpensive - a day of living here costs about 300 rubles per person.

Hotel rest

Fans of leisure on the all-inclusive basis are offered accommodation in local hotels, which are also numerous in the city. Among them, there are several popular ones that were highlighted based on reviews about Koktebel left by travelers. Among these are Narlen, Cool Breeze, and Liana. All these places have one thing in common: they offer a high level of service, comfortable living conditions, as well as close proximity to the best beaches of the town.

As for the cost, it is much higher than that set in the private sector, but this does not frighten travelers at all. Due to the popularity of local hotels, it is recommended to book rooms long before the opening of the holiday season.

Sanatoriums

As you know, the air in Koktebel is very clean and pleasant. In addition, it is filled with a large number of minerals useful for the human respiratory organs. Due to these properties, it has a therapeutic effect on people.

In connection with this feature of the town, many travelers from different parts of Russia wish to improve their health while staying in this region. In order for the process to go with the maximum benefit for the body, many of them prefer to stay in sanatoriums, where, in addition to clean and healthy air, a host of other wellness procedures are offered.

How to choose the most suitable sanatorium for yourself? First of all, it is necessary to build on the treatment of which problem is necessary - each place specializes in a separate area. In addition, you need to understand what living conditions are required, and choose the ideal place for yourself, depending on the level of service, frequency of food and, in the end, according to reviews that can be found on social networks or on thematic sites.

Vacation with children

Many tourists who plan a summer trip to seaside cities with their children should pay attention to what conditions for the relaxation of the smallest tourists are available here.

On a trip to Koktebel with children in the reviews you can find different opinions. In particular, most tourists define this locality as a rather noisy place where parties and colorful events are constantly held. In this regard, it is not recommended to go here with a very young child.

However, if the trip is planned with a more or less adult child, and the parents themselves are not confused by the presence of constant noise around, then Koktebel is a great place for children's pastime. The positive reviews of many parents say that it is here that you can find great beaches that are distinguished by their picturesque and shallow water. In addition, it is often said that cleaning areas are regularly cleaned by special services, which is the safety of vacationers, including children.

Among other things, according to parents, in Koktebel there are a huge number of places for children's recreation. Vivid examples of such are the water park, dolphinarium and a large aquarium, where kids will certainly learn a lot for themselves. Among other things, they often notice the presence of a friendly attitude of boarding house workers to guests with children, as well as the presence of good animation in places for recreation.

Beach activities

In reviews of Koktebel (Crimea), it is constantly said that in this village there is a huge amount of entertainment that is ideal for people with different tastes and needs. Many of them are located along the beach line, where in the midst of the spa season there is the largest concentration of guests from different parts of the country.

So, fans of boring relaxation, being here, have a great opportunity to fly with a paraglider or to make a parachute jump. Some fans of this extreme extreme very often make kayaking around Chameleon or Kara-Dag.

Fans of excursions often make hiking trips to the Kara-Dag valley, as well as swimming in the direction of the Golden Gate or simply to the open sea.

In addition to all this, parties and interesting festivals are often held on the beach strip, in which a large number of guests take part in the town. On many photos attached to reviews about Koktebel, one can easily notice the emotional atmosphere that prevails here during their holding.

Medical Items

The infrastructure of Koktebel is quite well developed - this is very often noted by tourists in their comments. As they say, on the territory of the settlement there are several medical centers that guests of the town can contact if an unpleasant incident happens to him or their companions.

Some of them are located in the boarding houses ("Primorye", "Blue Bay"). In addition, tourists also have the opportunity to visit a mobile emergency room. In the nearby city of Feodosia, which is located a few kilometers from Koktebel, there are two hospitals, one of which is general, and the second one is for children. If necessary, they can also be contacted. As they say in some reviews about Koktebel (Crimea), wide-profile specialists are available here who can help in difficult situations.

How to get there

How to get to this wonderful village? Some reviews of vacationers about Koktebel (Crimea) say that you can easily get here by bus, which travel from the cities of Crimea. In the midst of the holiday season, direct bus lines to Feodosia from some cities of Russia (Lipetsk, Bryansk, Novorossiysk, Stavropol) move from where many modes of transport can just get to your destination - it will cost about 50 rubles.

You can also make your trip by car or by using the services of private carriers. It should also be remembered that such a pleasure is much more expensive than a bus ride, however, you can get to the right place in this way much faster and more comfortably.

Reviews of Koktebel often express regrets that there is currently no railway connection with Feodosia, from where tourists used to get to Koktebel by shuttle bus. However, this problem will be solved with the completion of the construction of the Crimean bridge.
