Sanatorium "Hope" (Novocheboksarsk): how to relax and be treated here and how much does it cost?

The banks of the great Volga River are interesting not only for their amazing nature and tourist attractions. Here you can improve your health and return home from vacation with a completely different person. For this, a health resort was built 10 kilometers from the capital of Chuvashia under the name of the sanatorium "Nadezhda" (Novocheboksarsk). This is one of the best complexes of this kind in the Volga region. There is a sanatorium right on the riverbank, has a large territory - as many as six hectares. It is planted with fir trees, cedars and larch trees. There are many places for walking, as well as an apple orchard. Below we will tell you about how to get to the sanatorium "Nadezhda" (Novocheboksarsk), as well as what services it provides to its customers.

Accommodation

The resort is surrounded by a protected forest. To get here, you first need to get by bus, plane or train to the city of Cheboksary. From the station or from the airport you need to go further by regular bus. He will take you to the city of Novocheboksarsk, where the health resort is administratively assigned. And from there you will be taken by a business bus. He runs on a schedule three times a day on a specific route. And with your own car you can just get to 46, Naberezhnaya Street. People who come to rest at the Nadezhda sanatorium (Novocheboksarsk) in the main building are lodging. This is a three-story brick building, which can accommodate 80 people at a time. The rooms are designed for accommodation from one to four guests. Depending on the number of residents, they consist of 1-2 rooms. Near the main building there are two others located a hundred meters from it. The second building is well-maintained, it even has a sauna and a psychological relief room. It is designed for 25 people. In the first building, there are mainly 3-4-bed rooms with shared facilities. 60 guests can live there at the same time. The furniture in the rooms is functional, working. Cold and hot water round the clock. There are not only standard rooms, but also superior rooms, including apartments and suites.

Treatment

Recreation of vacationers in the sanatorium "Nadezhda" (Novocheboksarsk) is carried out using various procedures based on their own mineral springs. These are iodine-bromine waters that enter the hospital from a depth of almost a kilometer. They have a beneficial effect on metabolism, soothe the nervous system, stop inflammatory processes and strengthen the entire body. The sanatorium also treats cardiovascular and pulmonary diseases, disorders of the gastrointestinal tract, and helps with problems with joints and spine. There are programs for arthritis sufferers and diabetics. For allergy sufferers, a visit to the salt mine is provided. The pride of the sanatorium "Nadezhda" (Novocheboksarsk) is a mineral water pool. It works on schedule. There is also a large swimming pool. Its length is 25 meters. There are four lanes for swimmers. The package includes visits to water procedures in the morning and at lunchtime. The remaining sessions are paid.

Services

Throughout the resort "Nadezhda" (Novocheboksarsk) laid walking paths, which can also go straight down to the water. In the main building there is a dining room. There are also comfortably furnished halls where you can relax. In the main building there is a free wifi. In the dining room of the sanatorium for guests, five meals a day are established, based on the development of a dietitian. Each vacationer has his own program. There is pleasant music in the dining room; it is beautifully and tastefully decorated. The menu includes dishes of Russian national cuisine, fresh pastries, seasonal fruits and berries. In this health resort you can not only be treated, but also carry out the prevention of diseases or just relax. Many programs have been developed here for children and adults, especially those who come just for the weekend. You can play billiards and exercise on the tennis court, work out in the gym, just sunbathe on the beach on the banks of the Volga. At the health resort, a children's camp is also organized.
