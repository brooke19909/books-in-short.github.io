Comfortable hotels in Alushta will help every guest to forget about daily worries and will make the time spent in this place a true holiday. Thanks to the infrastructure and excellent service of the hotels, the warm sea and the hot sun of the peninsula will become even more pleasant.



Today, an increasing number of domestic tourists are interested in Crimea. Alushta (hotels by the sea are ready to receive guests year-round) was no exception. The cost of hotel rooms here varies from 900 to 10,000 rubles per day per person. The following factors affect the price: season, room level, additional services, distance from the sea, time of booking a hotel located in the resort of Alushta. Hotels by the sea (prices in some of them are indicated in this article) offer guests suites, junior suites, standard, economy class. Accommodation in the midst of the season will always be much more expensive.
