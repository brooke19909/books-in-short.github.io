How to get from Moscow to Dmitrov?

There are many ways to get from Moscow to Dmitrov. The most basic vehicles are car, train, bus and plane. Before you familiarize yourself with each flight in detail, you should understand why residents of the Russian capital travel to this glorious city.

Features of the city Dmitrov

Mostly people come from Moscow to Dmitrov in order to visit their relatives, friends and relatives. Many people visit this place for the sake of traveling, because there are many attractions here:

  • The first thing that every tourist should see is the ancient Dmitrov Kremlin. This greatest building was built in the 11th century. To this day, he managed to maintain his pristine appearance.
  • A real symbol of the city is a monument to Yuri Dolgorukov. The place for its construction in 2001 was not chosen by chance. It was on this territory that the king was predicted the birth of the long-awaited heir.
  • After the marriage is registered, all newlyweds will certainly go to the happy bridge. The husband must transfer the newly made spouse through him. Horseshoes are installed on both sides of it as a symbol of success and wealth, one of them is directed up, and the second is down.




  • The city has a small Arbat, where evenings of local residents take place in the evenings. At the entrance to this street you can see an unusual monument "Merchant and Merchant". The best sculptures of the city worked on this project.
  • Be sure to lay flowers on the T-34 tank, rich in historical legends.

The magnificent city of Dmitrov is famous for its nature. In the summer it is fabulously beautiful. Every resident of Moscow and other cities must visit this peaceful corner of the Earth.

Train

The reasons forcing a person to move from one city to another can be distinguished many. The first thing a traveler needs to take care of is choosing the right vehicle. The distance from Moscow to Dmitrov is only 65 kilometers. The best way to overcome this path is by train. A few meters from Savelovskaya metro station is Savelovsky station. At his ticket office you can buy a ticket to Dmitrov. Flights are operated up to 10 times a day. The total travel time to the appointed place is 1 hour 25 minutes.





An express train leaves the same place three times a day. It will allow a person to reach the appointed place in just one hour. Many residents do not use this method because of the high cost of travel.





Car

Knowing how many kilometers are from Moscow to Dmitrov, it is easy to guess that you can make a trip by personal transport. The first thing to do is to leave the territory of the Moscow Ring Road. Further, focusing on the arrows with directions, you need to enter Dmitrovskoe highway. This track is convenient and comfortable for each driver: fresh asphalt is laid on it, there is a wide strip and bright markings. Along its perimeter, gas stations are installed . On a direct road you need to get to Yakhroma, after which you should cross the roundabout. After it there will be signs with a direction to Dmitrov, on them you can easily get to the appointed place.





Bus

From this settlement, many people travel to the capital every day to work. Therefore, several times a day, bus runs from Moscow to Dmitrov and vice versa.





The first thing to do is get to the Altufevo metro station. At the exit from it you can see a stop. It is from her that the bus number 401 is sent, the next one to this city. He arrives at the local station. The total road time is 1 hour 10 minutes. Provided that the transport will move non-stop. However, in the morning and evening time, large traffic jams are created on the highway, so this time period can increase several times.

By plane





Many people who prefer not to spend time on the road are wondering if there is a possibility of a flight from Moscow to Dmitrov. Based on the fact that the distance between these two settlements is less than 100 kilometers, the answer suggests itself. It is worth considering that there are no airports near a small town, so you will not be able to move in the air with any kind of transfer.

Conclusion

Each person is incredibly curious, he likes to explore new places, change the familiar environment and occasionally leave the walls of his home. Near the capital of our homeland, there are many small cities that attract with their nature, attractions and extraordinary history. That is what Dmitrov is. A person who has been here at least once in his life has noticed that there is some special atmosphere in him that gives relaxation, tranquility and serenity.

What a blessing that metropolitan people have the opportunity to come for a walk from Moscow to Dmitrov, and this can be done on any convenient type of vehicle.
