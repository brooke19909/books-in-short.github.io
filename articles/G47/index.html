History of Aluston Fortress

During the reign of Emperor Justinian I, one of the most attractive structures on the Crimean peninsula, called Aluston, was erected. The fortress, which has existed for several centuries, has formed a large city around itself. He received the name Alushta and proudly bears this name to this day. Unfortunately, by the 21st century only ruins and huge boulders remained from the fortress, but several centuries ago the powerful walls of the building reliably defended their inhabitants.
