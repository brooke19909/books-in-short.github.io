Vityazevo

This small village, part of Anapa, is located 11 kilometers from its center. It is always quiet and calm, there is no noise and fuss. Over the past few years, dozens of guest houses, hotels and hotels that meet European standards have been built in Vityazevo, and one of them is the Dolce Vita guesthouse. Vityazevo annually receives tens of thousands of tourists from all over Russia and the CIS countries. Here they find everything that can be found abroad, just at very low prices compared to foreign counterparts.
