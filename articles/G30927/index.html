The best hotels of Rome: luxury, comfort and courtesy

Each person is completed the best. Therefore, when visiting the capital of Italy, you should pay attention to the best hotels in Rome, which welcome their guests with regal comfort and aristocratic sophistication.

When deciding on a trip to Rome, do not forget that only staying in a hotel complex of the highest class, you can get a comfortable stay worthy of kings. Therefore, the best hotels in Rome meet their guests not only with luxurious rooms, but also with a range of additional services that cannot leave indifferent lovers of real relaxation.





One of the best hotels in Italy is considered to be the five-star Gran Melia Rom. Hotels in the center of Rome attract visitors with interesting architecture, excellent service and personalized additional services. This complex spreads over 7,000 square meters and is surrounded by a delightful garden with artificial ponds. Each luxurious room, furnished with the latest trends in modern design (without changing the traditions of the past), offers unforgettable views of the Vatican, Via Venete and the Castle of St. Angel. Guests can use the services of the Roman-style spa, fitness center with individual trainers and a hydromassage pool. Dinner at the restaurant will be remembered not only for first-class cuisine, but also for its magnificent interior, which allows you to go back to the time of the Roman Caesars.

Cavalieri Waldorf Astoria, located on the hill of Montemario, like all the best hotels in Rome, amazes with luxurious decoration, combining both modern trends and tribute to antiquity.





In addition to the expensive materials used in the decor, the main attraction is the originals of paintings by famous authors, located both in the lobby of the building and in the rooms.

Regardless of the category of the room, the five-star hotel offers its guests an extensive list of services for relaxation and recreation. From the windows you can see the Old Town and get acquainted with the sights of the Vatican. The highlight of the hotel is the restaurant La Pergola, made in the ancient Roman style and offering tastes of Mediterranean and continental cuisine. La Pergola offers the rarest wines.

At the beginning of the 20th century, Carl Buzili Vichi tried to design such a hotel complex so that the best hotels in Rome were equal to him. And for more than a century, Ambasciatori Palace has been a place of concentration of sophistication, luxury and sophistication. In this five-star hotel, each room is decorated with expensive fabrics, precious woods, stone sculptures and special decor items, preserved in pristine condition since 1903. For the guests there are all kinds of new technology, allowing you to make the rest as comfortable as possible.





Each dish served in the restaurant is made according to old Italian recipes, so it allows you to feel the taste of dishes of old Italy. An excellent complement to them will be an extensive collection of Italian and French wines.

If, when visiting the capital of Italy, you want to choose cheap hotels in Rome, it is best to opt for the three-star Arcadia Hotel, from which you can get to the sights of the Eternal City in 15 minutes. This is the optimal ratio of price, quality and convenience. Hotel Caravel will welcome its guests not only with comfortable rooms, affordable prices, but also with friendly staff. Tre Fontane offers comfortable accommodation in modern rooms, as well as authentic Italian cuisine.

The selection of hotels in the Eternal City is extensive. And each of them is unique in its own way. Based on several criteria, you can choose the best option and enjoy your vacation in beautiful Rome, which keeps many secrets.
