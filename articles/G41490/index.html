Autumn decor. DIY decor for the interior

Autumn decor attracts primarily by the fact that it causes a feeling of cosiness and warmth, pleases the eye with calm tones, an abundance of natural materials and textures ... This style is very fond of designers and decorators, because thanks to it you can easily create a truly atmospheric interior.





It is also important that even beginners in decorating rooms can easily create real art objects.

Autumn decor: images, materials, colors, textures

Natural material is often used to create charming autumn looks. Of course, thoughts about buds, chestnuts and acorns familiar from childhood immediately come to mind, however, other gifts of nature have long been in the hands of designers. Heavy hazel beads, curled acacia pods, pot-bellied pumpkins, amber clusters of mountain ash, dried flowers and, of course, colorful leaves of all stripes - all this is suitable for creativity. You can create your own autumn decor with wood material: branches, pieces of bark, beautiful saw cuts, coniferous paws. In this style, the colors of autumnal nature dominate. Gold and scarlet, muffled greens, sunny yellow, amber-orange, coffee and chocolate - such a gamut is most characteristic.





But do not think that decor elements in the autumn style are just crafts from the gifts of nature. In our work, vintage lace, burlap and gunny, pieces of genuine leather, organza, ribbons may come in handy ... At the same time, we stock up on decoupage napkins with the appropriate plot, wrapping paper, and sheets of music notebooks. Often, corks from wine bottles, cereals and grains, sand, colored salt and much more are used for decoration. Knitted and homespun elements are used for decoration. The minimum set of necessary tools for needlework will help to create autumn interior decor with your own hands . Therefore, you should stock up with scissors, pliers, a small file, a set of needles, tape, glue gun, brushes. The most important thing is to give free rein to your imagination and taste and not be afraid of experiments.

Interior in autumn style

To emphasize the species, a calm color scheme is used to decorate the rooms. Most often, the calm color of the walls prevails, which is complemented by expressive details. It can be wooden and forged elements reminiscent of a country house or a hunting lodge. Often, autumn interior decor is emphasized by the use of real or decorative fireplaces. Wicker furniture made of twigs or rattan will also be appropriate.





Decor Elements for Autumn Interior

Details make the mood! What autumn decor of the room can do without warm plaids with tassels, patchwork pillows, candlesticks with thick candles and wicker wreaths? It is these little things that emphasize style and allow the designer to realize ideas as much as possible. Things created with one's own hand and not bought in a store will be especially valuable. The ideas of autumn decor are often inspired by nature itself.

Autumn in the camera lens

The beauty of nature is requested on the pages of photo albums. They say that autumn is coming for kids, for young lovers, and for those who ... Autumn decor for a photo shoot is often complemented by the same decor elements that are used in the interior. Although nature itself often dominates! To create autumn photos, just go out with the camera to the nearest park. But professional photographers often create whole worlds against the backdrop of crimson foliage. To do this, on the nature, podiums with the appropriate props are installed: couches, old suitcases, gramophones ... The shoulders of models are wrapped in shawls and fur boas, and lush chrysanthemums and asters rest in their hands. Kids love to be photographed with wooden carts, straw bogeys, wicker baskets. Organically fit into the photo and animals: dogs, ponies, squirrels.





You can create autumn decor for a photo shoot in the studio. For this, use stepladders, swing on the ropes, rocking chairs, hammocks. Special backgrounds popular nowadays can be useful, thanks to which the photographer can easily recreate the atmosphere of a cozy terrace covered with golden foliage, or a cozy cafe in which it is so good to hide from autumn rain.

Children will like to pose in high rubber boots, sort through garden tools, and of course, toss up armfuls of carved maple leaves.

We help children to create

The development of creative abilities of children is greatly facilitated by the joint creation of small handmade masterpieces with them. Better to start with a simple one. For example, here from such candlesticks for which the decor is used with autumn leaves.





To do this, you will need maple leaves, freshly collected for a walk , glass jars, PVA glue. Even a baby 3-4 years old will be able to grease the leaves with glue on their own. Pressing them to the jar will also be interesting. For such crafts, textured ropes, lace, twine, strands with beads, ribbons can come in handy. Such elements will not only decorate the craft, but also additionally fix the leaves.

It remains only to revive the jars with candles. And be sure to remind the child of safety with fire.

Autumn decorative elements in clothes and jewelry

Updated the interior - take on the wardrobe! Today you can meet the gifts of autumn not only on lampshades and frames for photographs. Why not save the delicate September flowers? An epoxy resin is useful for this, with which you can make live cabochons, which later become rings, earrings, gerdans, brooches ... Necklaces made of bright orange beads look healthy in autumn, similar to clusters of mountain ash.





Autumn decor is also used in clothes. For example, it can be shown in the decoration of the hem of the dress with embroidered oak leaves. The application on the neck of a blouse looks charming, the motive of which can be, for example, small ruddy pumpkins.

DIY do-it-yourself decor in everyday life





Holidays deserve special attention. For example, for Halloween you can decorate a porch or a loggia with pumpkins. Although in general the autumn decor in the house does not require a special occasion. As do not require him and good memories. After all, golden autumn is a wonderful time, which is always a pleasure to plunge into.
