Villa Bellevue Apartment 3 * (Greece / Crete) - photo and reviews of tourists

One of the most popular holiday destinations in Greece is the fabulous Crete. It is visited annually by the army of tourists, considering it a paradise. The modern infrastructure of the island's hotels, in which living conditions are oriented towards any income, attract here those who are looking for not only fashionable apartments, but also cozy family pensions and very modest campsites.

You can get to Crete only by air or by sea. Almost the entire coast of this island is dotted with picturesque bays and fishing harbors. All the main tourist resort areas of Crete - Chania, Iraklio, Rethymnon, etc. - are perfect for both youth and family vacations. In addition, tours to the largest Greek island are chosen by those who want to join the centuries-old history, see the Knossos Palace or the Samarya Gorge, go on a fascinating cruise to Dia or Santorini, look at the Archaeological Museum, as well as the Lassithi Valley, where you can take professional sommeliers conduct a wine tasting.

Infrastructure

Bellevue Villa Apartments 3 (Crete) offers a fairly large list of services. Housing stock

In total, Villa Bellevue Apartment 3 (Crete) has eighteen rooms of the following categories: apartments, studios and suites with balconies overlooking the sea or the surrounding mountains. The bungalows are built in a Mediterranean style typical of the region. In addition to traditional furniture - beds, a wardrobe, nightstands, a TV, a refrigerator, tables and chairs - the rooms have a kitchen area with all the necessary attributes for cooking, including a kettle and dishes.





Bathrooms combined. There is also a hairdryer and slippers. Non-smoking apartments are also available. Pets must be arranged with the property in advance.

Nutrition

Villa Bellevue Apartment 3 offers residents half board. Twice a day, a buffet is served in the restaurant, which exhibits many salads, several types of hot dishes, fruits and pastries. You can have dinner both in the tavern located on the territory of the apart-hotel and in the village where you can listen to live music, dance and enjoy wonderful Greek dishes.

Beach

The distance to the sea is about a thousand meters. Hotel Bellevue Villa Apartment 3 (Greece) is built on the third beach line. Those who wish can walk to the sea. Since the hotel is built on a hill, it is not difficult to get down to the coast. But for tourists with young children and the elderly, a paid bus leaves the hotel three times a day. You can return to Villa Bellevue Apartment 3 on the same shuttle.



There is gray sand on the beach. Trestle beds, mattresses and umbrellas are paid. The entrance to the sea is convenient for children, so the presence of special rubber bathing shoes is not necessary. Those who are not satisfied with the municipal beach of Agia Pelagia can go to the coast of Ligarya Beach, where, judging by the reviews, there are fewer swimmers, and the coastline is wider.

Entertainment

The hotel has a small pool. The staff cleans it daily. Around a lot of free sunbeds. For those who do not want to go down to the sea, it will be convenient to sunbathe on the terrace. However, there are many entertainments on the beach for young people, among them diving.
