Where to spend a free evening? How to entertain a child? Or maybe you want to go to a good movie theater or tickle your nerves at the rides? You are invited to the largest entertainment center - Gorky Park. Kharkov is rightly called a green city, however, the Gorky complex is considered the largest corner of nature.

A bit of history

This entertainment center begins its history back in 1893. Then the first trees were planted, they became the foundation of the future park zone. In the 19th century, the square was named Nikolaevsky; it did not enter the territory of the city. The grand opening of the park was made in 1907, by which time the small trees turned into majestic plants, which formed this corner of wildlife.

The idea of ‚Äã‚Äãcreating a green complex belonged to the townspeople - they wanted to equip a zone where they could have a comfortable rest. By the joint efforts of the authorities and local residents, about 98 hectares of forest stands were planted. Long alleys, separated by plants, converged in a single center, which looked like a circular platform at the very entrance to the park. Subsequently, the complex was named in honor of the writer M. Gorky.





The fate of the beloved park

Locals love their park very much and relate to its history with trepidation. He became a real symbol of the people's victory over the troubles and tribulations of different times. The complex survived the Great Patriotic War, although most of the buildings were destroyed. But the townspeople did not abandon their favorite vacation spot: volunteers and local authorities jointly restored and restored Gorky Park. Kharkov has always been famous for its dedicated people who created wonderful monuments of wildlife. A colonnade was erected at the entrance, which was designed by E. Svyatchenko and A. Krynkina. At the end of the central alley, a large beautiful fountain was built. The construction gave cool to guests in the hot summer days. Nowadays, the park is thriving and hosts thousands of tourists and locals every day. The complex has completely changed its appearance: a modern entertainment center has turned into a real Disneyland in Kharkov!

The wonderful metamorphoses that the green corner of nature has undergone have been brought to life by city mayor G. Kernes. The modern metropolis of entertainment for adults and children - this was Gorky Park. Attractions: Gorky Park, Kharkov

This wonderful entertainment complex is known throughout Ukraine. Want to get into the real local Disneyland? Go to Gorky Park. Kharkov is able to surprise its guests with the most exciting rides. For example, the popular Ferris wheel reaches 55 m in height - this is the largest Ferris Wheel in Ukraine. When you are at maximum height, you will have stunning views of the city. It feels like you're floating in the air. Roller coasters are suitable for extreme sports - trailers rise to a height of 20 m and fall down at a speed of up to 60 km / h! The length of the route is 400 m.

What about entertainment that makes even daredevils nervous? This is an attraction called the Catapult. Kharkov (Gorky Park) has prepared a real surprise for you: a person is placed in a reliable booth, and the mechanism throws such a device as a cannon. A lot of impressions, and adrenaline just rolls over.





Only for the daredevils

The park is equipped with unique attractions that evoke strong emotions among its visitors. Here are the most popular:

  • Crazy Bench is an attraction for those who want to soar into the sky. The platform is equipped with benches with mounts, it soars up and at a tremendous speed begins to rotate in different directions.
  • Air Flight Rise is perhaps the most exciting attraction in the entire park. Small planes rotate around an axis at speeds up to 6 revolutions per minute! Additionally, the mini-board rotates 360 degrees in a vertical plane. Ready to experience congestion before 3G? A similar attraction exists only in New York.
  • Carousel chain "Arabia" - such an entertainment only for daredevils. You will be raised to a dizzying height of 28 m. You can not only enjoy the feeling of flying, but also enjoy the beautiful view of the park.
  • "Somersault" - and such entertainment requires special endurance. Two gondolas rotate at a tremendous speed of 360 degrees, you take off at 18 m and see how the sky and earth change places.
  • "Tower of the fall." If you are ready to climb 40 m, and then quickly fly towards the ground, be sure to visit this attraction. But remember: the sensations are very strong, they do not suit the faint of heart.




House of Horror

The pride of the entertainment center is the modern room of fear. This is one of the attractions that once enjoyed particular popularity. In Soviet times there was also a kind of prototype - a room of laughter. Unfortunately, today there are fewer and fewer such attractions. The room of fear (Gorky Park) will help to recall past times. Kharkov has become one of the few cities in which a complex has appeared that is not inferior in equipment to the best European counterparts. "House of Horrors" is an attraction that is made in the form of an old abandoned mansion where you can meet the most terrible monsters. Legend has it: in the old days, this estate was owned by Professor A. Kozlov, who practiced black magic. But during his experiments, the scientist made a mistake and ... disappeared without a trace. And dark powers, ghosts and even zombies took possession of the mansion. So if you are going to visit this room of fear, be prepared for scary adventures and chilling encounters with fairy-tale characters.
