"Plants vs. Zombies". How to create a poster from a plasticine popular game

Among gamers there are quite a few of those that ‚Äúate the dog‚Äù in the fight against zombies. It turns out that one of the effective means in the war against the living dead is plants.

This bold statement is proved by the popular among fans of all ages arcade game with elements of a strategy that is addressed to children six and older. It is called Plants vs. Zombies. How to mold her heroes from plasticine?

It's simple

At the end of the work, the author and the audience will not see a trivial picture on paper, but an original volumetric poster with your favorite characters.

But they need to be placed on any basis. Black cardboard is offered here. It is the color of darkness from where the living dead creep out. And he is the color of the land on which their enemies grow: flowers, fruits, mushrooms and so on.

We cut cardboard according to the format of the standard frame, if one is planned in the future. In any case, you need to determine the size of the future product and prepare the appropriate amount of clay for it.

Sculpt from plasticine "Zombies against plants"

Let this work be called that way, because each work should have a name (and preferably an original one). This sound reflects who attacked whom in the plot of the game.

There are a great many representatives of flora and monsters in the game. For the poster, let's take the most characteristic and recognizable characters: Cutie Sunflower and Common Zombie.

Let us depict their joint portrait in several steps:

  1. From gray plasticine we make a zombie head blank. On the cake, we push the eye sockets and mouth with our fingers.
  2. We make the center of the Sunflower from the same brown cake.
  3. Roll out a long, fairly thick yellow tourniquet. We bend it with a short flight of stairs and wrap it around the center of the Sunflower. These will be the petals.
  4. We place the finished parts on the base, slightly pressing down so that they stick and do not fall off.
  5. We will prepare small parts for the ghoul:
  • 2 convex white cakes for the eyes;
  • 1 lilac flat oval for the tongue;
  • 4 jagged white rectangles for teeth.

All this is firmly attached (by pressure) to the base of the character‚Äôs head.

6. Apply the last small details to the scary head:

  • pupils and nostrils of black small balls;
  • fine black flagella wrinkles;
  • rare greenish hair from pieces of a thin flagellum.

7. We are finishing work on the plant. Sculpt and fix:

  • 2 black oval eyes;
  • 4 white highlights on the pupils;
  • purple thread of a smiling mouth.

Creative result

Blinding Plants Against Zombies, as it turned out, is not so difficult. So, the poster is ready. It can be placed on the wall next to other fan pictures of this theme. Moreover, it will effectively stand out with its originality of execution.

In addition, the time of creative work, even taken away from your favorite game, will clearly bring the author much more benefit.
