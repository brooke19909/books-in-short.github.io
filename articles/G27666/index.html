Environmental goals and environmental objectives. Goals and objectives of environmental monitoring

The main environmental goals and environmental tasks are to create an information system that allows you to obtain truthful information about the state of the environment, changes in biotic and physical components under the influence of anthropogenic and natural factors.

Tasks solved by environmental monitoring

Let us find out the main directions associated with these studies. Goals and objectives of environmental monitoring :

  • obtaining primary information, its processing, systematization, analysis, creating a database;
  • processing and providing information in the form of maps, charts, tables;
  • modernization and improvement of methods for obtaining primary information;
  • assessment of the real state of the environment, as well as a forecast for the near future;
  • identification of the causes of the probable and observed phenomena of such a state;
  • Efficiency of providing the required information to interested parties.

The goals and objectives of environmental monitoring, noted above, contribute to the adoption of operational decisions in the field of environmental management. Forecast is necessary for the development of solutions designed for a long time period.

Basis for organizing environmental monitoring

This activity is based on three basic principles:

  • systematic;
  • complexity;
  • unification.

These parameters contribute to the construction of an environmental monitoring system, imply the use of certain constituent components:

  • selection of monitoring objects and verified characteristics;
  • creation of special observation points;
  • systematization of the obtained data, analysis and assessment of the environmental situation ;
  • consideration of information and solutions obtained in order to find ways to eliminate problems in the environmental situation.

Environmental Monitoring Framework

It is based on the main four blocks:

  • database;
  • analytical unit;
  • information block;
  • control system.

An integral part of the environmental monitoring system is considered the OS state management system, because information about the real environment and trends is considered the basis for creating a special environmental policy, as well as for planning the economic and social development of certain territories.

The goals and objectives of environmental policy are the preservation of the unique natural environment, the improvement of the culture of human communication with nature.





Application of information obtained during monitoring

Environmental goals and environmental tasks performed during monitoring allow executive authorities to receive information for work:

  • planning activities aimed at reducing environmental pollution;
  • selection of priority areas of activity;
  • monitoring and evaluating the rationality of a variety of environmental measures;
  • development of temporary measures to reduce pollution in those territories where excesses of maximum permissible concentrations have been identified;
  • checking compliance with the standards and norms of quality of the natural object;
  • obtaining information for the organization of scientific research;
  • the introduction of relevant legislation.

The economic aspect is an important point in developing a monitoring system. During the research, the conformity of the techniques and methods selected for monitoring with the tasks assigned to employees is assessed. Thanks to the results obtained, it is possible to adjust the state policy in the field of environmental protection.

Comprehensive monitoring

What are the main goals and objectives of environmental impact assessment? There are special programs that require comprehensive monitoring to comprehensively assess the various problems associated with environmental pollution. What does integrated monitoring include?

  1. Studying global impacts on the earth‚Äôs surface, for example, analyzing the problems associated with the release of carbon dioxide and halocarbon compounds into the atmosphere.

The scale of the environmental monitoring system

Monitoring is carried out both on a small territory and on a global scale (throughout the globe). Based on the data obtained, the general state of the biosphere is analyzed.





National monitoring

It means carrying out a research system in one country. The goals and objectives of this type of environmental review are to obtain information and assess the state of the OS in the interests of national security. If pollution levels increase in some cities or industrial areas, local measures are taken to eliminate the identified problem.





Environmental education in educational institutions

Due to the relevance of the problems associated with the pollution of natural territories with chemicals, the modern education system also pays special attention to the formation of the foundations of an ecological culture among the younger generation. The guys, under the guidance of their mentor, are engaged in their own monitoring studies, solve certain problems related to the deterioration of the quality of drinking water. The goals and objectives of the environmental project depend on what problem the student wants to study, consider, analyze. For example, you can evaluate the quality of drinking water in different places of the same locality, find out the existing difficulties, offer your own ways to eliminate them. The goals and objectives of environmental education are regulated by federal educational standards of the second generation, aimed at fostering a tolerant attitude towards nature in the younger generation. Many preschool institutions choose ecology as the main direction of their activity. What are the main goals, objectives and environmental education in the DOE? Kids get acquainted with the rules of behavior in the forest, study rare plants, learn to distinguish between useful and poisonous flowers, mushrooms.





Environmental law

Due to the fact that unauthorized emissions of harmful chemicals by large industrial enterprises into the atmosphere have recently become more frequent, issues of legal protection of nature from harmful human effects have become urgent. The goals and objectives of environmental law are regulated by Russian law. It provides for liability for violations of environmental management rules, indicates all measures that may be taken against violators. Large chemical industries pay special attention to the creation of high-quality treatment facilities that prevent the penetration of toxic substances into the air and water resources. What are the environmental goals and objectives of the enterprise that oversees the activities of chemical plants? Samples of water, soil, air are taken at certain times, according to the results of quantitative and qualitative analysis, the compliance of the samples with acceptable standards is determined. If serious violations are detected, an environmental audit is carried out . Its goals and objectives depend on the specifics of the organization, but they mainly concern verification of compliance with all security requirements. If the obtained results indicate the presence of serious problems, the issue of initiating a criminal case on the violation of environmental legislation is being resolved.





Conclusion

Environmental monitoring is an integral part of ensuring the safety of people living on our planet. That is why every state is trying to find material resources for organizing research aimed at solving environmental problems. In order for the goals and objectives of environmental management to be consistent with the country's policy, there is such a direction as environmental policy. This branch of law involves certain regulatory acts that stipulate all requirements for the activities of large companies and enterprises, as well as those liability measures that will be taken against them in case of violation of regulatory acts. In order for the younger generation to have formed an ecological culture, special training courses in ecology are already being introduced at the pre-school and school level. In the classroom, children learn to respect the environment, rationally use natural resources. The new federal educational standards of the second generation suggest the formation in students of the desire for independent study of the basics of environmental management, environmental research and monitoring.
