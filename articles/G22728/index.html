Condtrol XLiner Laser Level

The XLiner DUO self-leveling professional laser level builds vertical and horizontal laser planes. The device was made in an original and reliable case, the design of which is strikingly different from other models of Condtrol levels.

Design

The body of the laser level Condtrol XLiner is made in the form of a rectangular trapezoid. On the sides are rubber dampers that protect the level from mechanical damage and preserve its functionality. The large support area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe laser level increases its stability on flat surfaces.

Instrument batteries are located at the bottom of the chassis. To create the battery cover, thin plastic was used, which is impractical: the latch, like the cover itself, easily breaks when the compartment is opened. Despite this, it closes very tightly, not passing dust, dirt and other garbage.

Condtrol XLiner has an IP54 degree of protection according to international standards, which is also characteristic of other types of lasers.





Compensator Type

The ADA laser level, like other levels of this class, is equipped with a pendulum compensator based on magnetic dampers. Installing a damper takes 2 seconds even after a strong shake of the device.

When the operating range of the compensator is exceeded, the level starts flashing at intervals of 1 second, notifying the user about the need to install the device on a more even surface. The lock of the pendulum compensator is reliable, which is most important when changing places of work and frequent transportation of the device.

Activate and switch modes

Condtrol DUO laser level control is simple and intuitive. The device is fully controlled by two membrane keys located on the top panel. On the lower side of the device there is a switch with which the compensator is locked.

All keys are numbered for user convenience. The number of lines activated by pressing the power key is displayed by the corresponding numbering:

  • Number 1 - inclusion of a vertical plane with a single press.
  • Number 2 - inclusion of a horizontal plane when pressed twice.
  • number 3 - simultaneous projection of horizontal and vertical when pressed three times.

The right key starts the operating mode with the receiver. It is used when working on the street in clear sunny weather at a distance of up to 200 meters, when it is not possible to visually determine the location of the line.

Indicators for enabling and disabling the receiver operation mode and for removing / setting the compensator lock are located on the level panel.





Laser Level Functions

The Condtrol XLiner DUO has a laser type that allows you to build lines with a large scan.

Battery saving is realized due to the function of separate inclusion of laser planes.

The range of operation of the level without a receiver, according to the manufacturer, is 50 meters. Reviews on Condtrol laser levels refute this information: in good light or on the street at this distance the laser beam is simply not visible.

The lock indicator lights up after moving the compensator lock switch, which allows you to carry out all work in an inclined plane without a signal from the device about the loss of horizon. At the same time, the compensator is protected from shaking. The compensator is blocked by shifting the switch to its original position.

Visually, the laser lines of the level on a sunny day are almost impossible to consider, especially at a great distance. In this situation, the operating mode with the receiver is activated, which allows you to determine the location of the laser.

The Condtrol XLiner DUO laser level is equipped with standard 1/4 and 5/8 inch threads, so you can attach the device to any tripod, pole or other wall mounts.
