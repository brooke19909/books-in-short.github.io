Hotel "Summer", Tuapse: overview, description, rooms and reviews of tourists

What season do we like the most? Of course, summer! Sea, sun, beach - these are the three components of a great summer vacation. And if you also stay in an amazing place, then the rest will be paradise. If the choice fell on the Black Sea coast and you prefer a vacation in Tuapse, the Summer Hotel will be a wonderful solution.

Hotel location

The Summer Hotel is located in the very center of sunny Tuapse, not far from the Central Beach, along Gagarina Street. Such a location is the main advantage of the hotel - everything is just a few meters from the sea. The hotel is located in a modern building, there are all the amenities necessary for a comfortable summer vacation. All vacationers leave exclusively positive reviews about this place, about the level of service and maintenance.

Design of the Summer Hotel

The hotel building is an example of modern style: it is a small rectangular building with panoramic windows overlooking a delightful beach. The interior design can be described as eclectic: a combination of modern elements, such as minimalist furniture of the correct form, simple layout and elegant decorative objects (chandeliers, paintings, mirrors in openwork frames) make the hotel really interesting and charming.

The most beautiful and magnificent place in the hotel is the restaurant. The spacious hall with high ceilings and panoramic windows is designed in the best restaurant traditions. Walls and floors are white - nothing distracts from eating food. But in order to somehow dilute this whiteness, the designers decided to hang burgundy curtains on the windows. The furniture in the hall is massive, wooden, which contrasts perfectly, again, with the lightness and airiness of the hall. In this room I want to be for a long time.

The corridors are also designed very reasonably: the color scheme is calm, using natural wood colors and natural materials (of course, wood). The rooms open onto a cozy terrace in similar traditions, but there are also very comfortable armchairs here: you can sit in the evening and enjoy the stars.





Rooms at the Summer Hotel

Since the Summer Hotel (Tuapse) is three-star, there are several categories of rooms. If you want to relax with chic, then you need to book a suite "Star". It is very spacious, one-bedroom, and is located on two levels. The furniture here has a large double bed, bedside tables, a wardrobe. The room also has a TV, DVD player, split system and refrigerator. Convenient and comfortable.

The next category of rooms is luxury. This number consists of two rooms - conditionally a bedroom and a living room. There is also a double bed, a sofa bed, a wardrobe, a chest of drawers, bedside tables, an LCD TV, a split system and a refrigerator. The rooms of this category are furnished in a romantic style, the designers used muted tones to decorate the room, which creates an unusually cozy and delicate atmosphere.





The Summer Hotel (Tuapse) also offers studios: there is a relaxation area and a bedroom. A TV, air conditioning and refrigerator are also present. The main advantage of this room is the windows, they overlook the sea. In the evening, you can open the curtains and listen to the sound of the surf.

Architects and designers of the hotel took care of the room for the newlyweds. A very romantic atmosphere is created here. The room is decorated mainly in light pastel colors, bright flashy elements are completely absent here. Being in such a room is a pleasure.

Rooms of the 1st category are presented in two types - with a double bed or with two single beds. These rooms have a TV, air conditioning and refrigerator. The bathroom is usually one in two adjacent rooms. These rooms are suitable for middle-income guests, it is cozy and has everything you need.

Of course, the hotel owners took care of the availability of economy class rooms in their hotel: these are six-bed rooms, where, as a rule, the company of young people are accommodated. It also has everything you need.
