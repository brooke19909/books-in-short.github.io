Sea Aquarium on Chistye Prudy: amazing exposition, exclusive aquariums and extraordinary inhabitants

Many Muscovites and visitors to the city are familiar with the striking Moskvarium at VDNKh. In this article we want to talk about its more modest, but no less interesting alternative - the "Sea Aquarium" on Chistye Prudy. Let's look at its amazing features, extraordinary inhabitants, and also get acquainted with other interesting information.

Sea Aquarium on Chistye Prudy: Acquaintance

The Oceanarium on Chistoprudny Boulevard is the largest coral reef in our country , which has been cultivated for more than 10 years! Sharks, stingrays, octopuses, girl fish, doctor shrimp, surgeon fish and more than 1000 fish and 500 species of other inhabitants swim slowly in their natural conditions.





"Marine Aquarium" is also the first oceanarium of the capital. It was opened in 2000. In addition to exploring the rich underwater world, visitors are expected to:

  • moray show;
  • Shark Lunch show
  • petting zoo;
  • individual and group excursion services;
  • holding children's parties.

On 800 m 2 of space filled with 300 aquariums, visitors have a unique opportunity to see conditions as close to natural as possible:

  • tropic coral reefs;
  • Amazon basin;
  • Tanganyika, Malawi - water bodies located in Africa;
  • pelagic and shelf zones of tropical oceans, etc.

The six-meter aquarium, which accurately reproduces the picture of the seabed, also attracts guests. The unusual design is striking: the walls, floor, ceiling - these are all the walls of a huge aquarium. It seems as though people are walking inside a fabulous underwater kingdom.

Now let's see how to get to the Sea Aquarium and how to find it.

Contact Information

The Marine Aquarium at Chistye Prudy is open to guests any day of the week from 10:00 to 20:00. Be careful - the ticket office closes half an hour before the completion of the institution.

The exact address of the aquarium is: 14, Chistoprudny Blvd., 3. It can be easily reached from several metro stations at once:

  • "Turgenevskaya";
  • "China town";
  • Kursk
  • "Clean Ponds".




The spectacular dinner show of bloodthirsty sharks starts at 18:30 only twice a week - on Tuesday and Friday. Tickets for this unforgettable sight are available at the box office from 17:30.
