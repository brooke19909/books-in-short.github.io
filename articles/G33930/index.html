Krizantem Katya Hotel in Alanya

Description. Built on the first line from the sea, Krizantem Katya Hotel consists of three buildings. The hotel is only two kilometers away from Alanya. The last major reconstruction was carried out in 2008.



The area around the hotel, although not very large, is fully equipped and green.

Its infrastructure includes a shopping arcade, parking (without prior reservation), libraries, dry cleaning, a medical office, a currency exchange, a beauty salon, a television room, a conference room, as well as a small banquet room for any special occasions.



You can arrange a shuttle service at the 24-hour front desk, as well as use a car rental desk or order a taxi. There is also a depository safe.

The distance to the nearest airport can be covered in an hour and a half.

Number of rooms. Krizantem Katya Hotel apartments are located in three six-story buildings and have the following categories: 165 standard - 15 square meters. meters, and 34 family with a hall and a separate bedroom - 55 square meters. m. The maximum number of residents - 5 people.

All rooms have a small laptop safe, a TV (with several Russian channels), all the necessary furniture, a large makeup mirror, a work desk, and a paid minibar.



In the well-equipped bathrooms, in addition to all the necessary hygiene items, there is also a hair dryer.

All rooms are equipped with individually adjustable air conditioning, ceramic flooring on the floor. If desired, you can get extra bedding and an iron.

Half of the rooms overlook the sea, balconies are comfortably furnished. Two elevators work.

On the ground floor in the main building there are conditions for guests in wheelchairs.

Internet access is available in all public areas.

Nutrition. Krizantem Katya Hotel offers an all-inclusive service. The buffet is served in the main restaurant, there is the opportunity to dine on the outdoor terrace.

Another restaurant, international, operates on a preliminary reservation system. There are also three poolside bars, a lobby bar and a beach vending machine.

Beach. Krizantem Katya Hotel has a private beach. It can be reached through the underpass under the motorway. Distance to the sea - 50 meters.

On the beach, except mattresses and beach towels, everything is free. The water has very small pebbles, but special shoes are not required.

Additional Information. Krizantem Hotel 4 offers its little guests a playground equipped on soft lawn grass, Russian-speaking babysitting services on request, a baby cot in the room, as well as a mini disco and interesting animation. For them, there are two small sections near the adult pools, of which there are only three on the territory of the hotel.



It offers adults water slides, a gym with fitness equipment, a fitness room, table tennis, chess, a massage room, a disco and animation.

Guest reviews. Krizantem Katya Hotel, Alanya, Turkey is a comfortable hotel that fully corresponds to its category. The guests are satisfied with the quantity and quality of food, the cleanliness of the rooms, the responsiveness of the staff.
