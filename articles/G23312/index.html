"Corsair" (Adler, 3 * hotel): services and reviews

If you plan to spend a vacation by the sea, then pay attention to the hotel "Corsair" (Adler). The hotel was built relatively recently. Nevertheless, it has already formed its own traditions and standards of quality service.





Resort Summary

If you are tired of living in rooms with shabby furniture and old plumbing, then pay attention to the Corsair Hotel (Adler). The hotel was built relatively recently, and therefore you will not have such problems. All rooms are comfortable and quite nicely decorated. At the same time, you will have your own balcony from which you can enjoy city views, as well as incredibly beautiful local nature.

The combination of several residential buildings is a hotel "Corsair" (Adler). The hotel has, albeit a small, but incredibly cozy area. The well-kept garden will be a great place for walking, and thanks to the interesting decor you can take a lot of original photos here. You can always take a dip in the clean outdoor pool or soak up the sun in the sun loungers on the terrace.

Special attention deserves the restaurant, which operates on the territory of the hotel "Corsair" (Adler). The hotel gives you the opportunity to try a wide variety of European dishes in the original interpretation of the chef. In addition, you can order comprehensive meals here, coordinating all the features of the menu individually.





Resort Location

Quite a favorable location boasts a hotel "Corsair". Adler is a fairly large city, and this hotel is located in its central part. From here to the sea coast you will need to walk for about 5 minutes. Approximately the same time will take the road to the train station. If you arrived at the resort by air, then note that the distance from the airport to the hotel is about 5 kilometers.

Resort Hotel Rooms

"Corsair" (3 * hotel, Adler) offers you the following accommodation options:

  • Standard apartments offer guests both single and double occupancy. The room is a bedroom. There is also a small entrance area through which you can enter the bathroom equipped with a shower.
  • Mini-suites are superior apartments, which are characterized by an increased area. Due to this, it becomes possible to install in the room not only a bed, but also a pair of soft armchairs that form the living room area. Also in these apartments you will have access to a private balcony.
  • Luxury - these are large two-room rooms, which are often chosen for family vacations. Here, accommodation is made not only on beds, but also on extra beds in the form of folding upholstered furniture.
  • For a romantic getaway, an accommodation option like a boudoir is suitable. It is generally similar to a mini-suite. Nevertheless, the difference is that the bathroom is equipped with a hydromassage bathtub.




It is worth paying attention to the interior of the rooms that the Corsar Hotel (Adler) offers its guests. Photos of the apartments demonstrate minimalism, warm shades of the interior and homeliness.





Facilities at the guest apartment

"Corsair" (3 * hotel, Adler, Hosta) offers its guests to use the following amenities in the apartment:

  • depending on the category of the room, your bathroom will be equipped with a modern shower or bathtub with the possibility of hydromassage;
  • a wide-screen TV, thanks to which you can pass the time watching dozens of satellite channels;
  • almost all rooms have their own access to the balcony, on which there is plastic furniture, as well as a special device for drying clothes and towels;
  • thanks to the presence of a refrigerator in the room, the products you bought will remain fresh for a long time;
  • the presence of a landline phone gives you the opportunity, without leaving the number, to contact the administration, as well as make a call to another city;
  • individual air conditioning system is responsible for creating a comfortable microclimate in the room;
  • bath accessories and a cosmetic set in the bathroom.

Positive feedback about the Corsair Hotel (Adler)

Hotel "Corsair" (Adler): reviews, shortcomings

The most significant shortcomings can be considered the following:

  • staff may refuse early check-in even if the hotel is completely empty;
  • during registration, employees do not consider it necessary to provide a password for wireless Internet (you have to go down to the reception desk again to clarify this issue);
  • in spite of the fact that coolers are in the corridors, there is no water in them;
  • there is no electric kettle in the room;
  • if you want to order comprehensive food in a restaurant, then be prepared for the fact that it will be meager and rather monotonous.

General impression

It is worth noting that the resort hotel "Corsair" makes some tourists an ambiguous impression. On the one hand, these are spacious, bright, comfortable and clean rooms, as well as a beautiful and well-groomed area (albeit small). On the other hand, here they clearly save on trifles. Nevertheless, if you are looking for a comfortable option for living in the city center, as well as near the sea, then this hotel is quite suitable for you.
