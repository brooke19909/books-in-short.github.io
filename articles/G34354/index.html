Victory Park: attractions, catamarans and other entertainment

The Moscow region of St. Petersburg is considered one of the most attractive for living due to many factors, including a large number of green spaces. Victory Park, located next to the metro station of the same name, has long been a favorite vacation spot for residents of surrounding houses and neighborhoods. The landscape of the park is decorated in a regular style, and many ponds give it special significance in both winter and summer. Straight paths, neat lawns, mighty trees with shady crowns make the garden a great place to relax on a hot summer day. Victory Park offers several entertainment options for every taste. Attractions, catamarans, cycling and boating - all this is available for adults and children during opening hours. And tents with ice cream and cotton candy can please children with their favorite treat.

A great way to have a good day off is to visit Victory Park. Attractions located on its territory are designed to entertain people of different ages and preferences. In accordance with this, there are several zones - a nursery, for the whole family and a complex for lovers of extreme sensations.

In the children's part, the smallest visitors will find entertainment for themselves. This area is designed to maximize interest and attract kids. A variety of swings and carousels, as well as a slide and even a children's steam locomotive, can captivate children for a long time. But this is not all that Victory Park is attractive for. Attractions for the smallest are complemented by a playground and water fun. In addition, on weekends, funny and colorful clowns walk around the territory of the square, who organize funny performances and competitions.



Entertainment for people of all ages is located in the family zone, which Victory Park is famous for. Attractions here are designed for both children and adults who are not averse to ride on the carousels and remember childhood. In addition, there is a shooting range, where you can check the accuracy and hardness of your hands and win a memory prize.

The third section of the square - extreme - mainly attracts young people who do not deprive Victory Park of their attention. Attractions, breathtaking, allow you to escape, experience unforgettable sensations and feel the surge of strength and energy that gives an adrenaline rush into the blood. One of the most popular funs is the classic Roller Coaster, while the more unusual ones are Hip-Hop and Kamikaze. They allow young people to spend leisure time fun and interesting.



It is especially worth noting the availability of recreation in such a beautiful place as Victory Park. Attractions, the prices of which do not exceed 120 rubles, will help to spend time with pleasure, saving the family budget. Sports grounds for playing basketball and other outdoor games, morning jogging paths and comfortable benches for relaxing and reading books make this oasis in the city, Victory Park, indispensable. Attractions, photos against the backdrop of beautiful landscapes and flower beds, fresh air and silence in the midst of a bustling city will give each visitor a bright and memorable day off.
