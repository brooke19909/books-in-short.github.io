FSK "Leader": reviews about the company, the best projects

The company involved in the construction and sale of large commercial and residential facilities, FGC Leader, reviews of which can be found on a variety of network resources, is also a multidisciplinary developer working in addition to Moscow in various regions of Russia: in Krasnodar, Kaluga, in the Leningrad region, in the suburbs. The holding has structures of various profiles, and therefore has the ability to independently carry out absolutely the entire cycle of work from design and construction to the turnkey delivery of the facility.

All projects that are in the work of FGC "Leader", reviews are numerous and varied. Firstly, it is noted in them that innovative technologies are almost always used during construction, planning decisions are optimal, and the infrastructure component is deeply thought out. Also, reviews of FGC "Leader" in a fairly large amount are present on behalf of the employees of this company, which contains explanations, tips, answers to questions from customers on the site.

As the company develops vigorous activity in absolutely any housing construction plan, as well as in any of its segments, many questions come up. General information

One of the most successful and dynamically developing companies is considered FGC "Leader". Ratings

In 2016, the company FGC Leader was in the top ten rating of housing developers in the country (national association). The RBC-500 rating put Leader Corporation in third place among the most successful developers in Russia. A year earlier, it was the twentieth place (the rating of the fifty fastest growing companies in the Russian Federation, and everything was considered here - not just the real estate field).

In 2016, the volume of ongoing construction that the company is conducting has allowed it to occupy the thirteenth position in Russia among the two hundred largest developers. This rating was compiled by RASK. Forbes Magazine, which publishes data on company income and investments, has added FGC Leader to the list of the two hundred largest private corporations in Russia, where 60% of the proceeds go to general contracts and 38% to investments, but it is indicated that the turnover in investment activity is growing very quickly (the peak was in 2012 - 71%).





Head of corporation

Even in reviews, there is often a question that may seem insufficiently correct.
