How to connect SMS package on Megafon? What packages are provided by the operator?

For people who prefer to communicate via text messaging, Megafon has developed several options for options. A subscriber can choose the most suitable SMS package in terms of volume - 100, 300/600 - or activate the option that allows sending 100 messages daily for free. What options are provided by the operator and how to connect the SMS package on Megafon? We will talk about this in detail in this article.





Types of SMS options

Depending on the needs and desires of the subscriber, you can choose one of four offers: package S, M, L and package XL. The first three options include the volume of 100/300/600 text messages provided for one billing period (per month), the fourth, for more outgoing subscribers, 100 messages per day.

The S, M, L and XL packages are also distinguished by the periodicity of charging the subscription fee: for 100 messages per day, the subscriber will have to pay a certain amount of money daily, while for other options payment will be made in full for the billing period upon activation and after 30 days.





For what cost can I connect an additional SMS package to Megafon?

Consider separately packages from S to L and the XL option. The first three, which the operator offers, implies a one-time write-off of funds at the time when the option is connected, in the amount of 75 rubles / 150 rubles / 240 rubles. respectively. The next time a new write-off (also in full) will be made in a month, that is, at the end of the billing period.

SMS package (Megafon) XL is activated for free, the subscription fee is not charged when connected, but is distributed by the days of the billing period. Thus, the daily payment will be only 9 rubles (a month this option will cost the client 270 rubles).

Terms of Use

Before telling how to connect the SMS package on Megafon, I would like to draw subscribers' attention to some features of these options:

  • it is impossible to activate two of the above options at the same time (therefore, if the number is currently connected, for example, package S, and you need to replace it with package M, then you must first disable the first and only after that activate the desired option);
  • balances on packages do not transfer to a new billing period, regardless of the number of packages; upon the first date of the new period, the message counter is reset and the starting volume of the service is provided);
  • You can send messages as part of the options from Megafon to the numbers of any mobile operators (only in your region; when sending SMS to another city, tariffication will be made in accordance with the tariff plan)
  • You can use messages included in the package only while in your (home) area; when roaming in the country and abroad, tariffing is carried out in accordance with the TP, while the charging of the subscription fee is not suspended).




How to connect SMS package on Megafon?

Activation of any of the packages considered in the article can be carried out through an online assistant (access to which can be obtained by going to the official resource of the mobile operator). In the list of available options for connection, you should find the offer of interest and activate it using the corresponding button. In the absence of the Internet or the impossibility of its use, you can contact a specialist of the operator‚Äôs contact center and ask to connect the selected option. However, it is likely that the specialist will write off an additional 30 rubles from his number. This amount will not be debited when connected independently.

How to connect the SMS package to Megafon from the phone? You can also become the owner of one of the SMS packages from a mobile device. To do this, dial the following combination on the number: * 572 * <the number corresponding to the SMS option> #, and then make a call.

  • SMS package S (100 messages per month) - number 1.
  • SMS package M (100 messages per month) - number 2.
  • SMS L package (100 messages per month) - number 3.
  • SMS XL package (100 messages per day) - number 4.




How to disable message packages?

How to connect the SMS package on Megafon, we found out earlier, I would also like to provide a description of possible options for disabling the service:

  • through the Internet assistant (in the list of options activated on the number, find the desired one and click the "Disable" button);
  • through a contact center specialist;
  • by entering a request on the mobile device * 572 * 0 # (disables any of the options considered).
