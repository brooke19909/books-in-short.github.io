"Rocco": a club in Ivanovo - a place for a fun and comfortable night's rest

The night club "Rocco", which is located in the city of Ivanovo, is not just an institution for relaxation and dancing. This is the place where celebrations are held: weddings, birthdays, graduations, corporate parties, banquets. The features of this club institution are described in sections of the article.

Rocco Club in Ivanovo: Feature

The club is considered a place that is perfect for a comfortable, fiery and fun holiday. Beautiful interior, modern music, a dance floor - all this makes it popular among young people.

In addition to incendiary parties, there are many festive events. The participants of the festival are provided with all the necessary musical and lighting equipment.

Rocco is an institution that is considered one of the famous clubs in Ivanovo. It is so famous thanks to the good work of the staff and the high quality of service. In addition, the institution is characterized by inexpensive prices for dishes and services.

Rocco Nightclub in Ivanovo is located at Pushkin Square, Building 2. It is located in the Palace of Arts. The institution is open on Fridays and Saturdays from eleven in the evening until six in the morning.

Halls





For guests, there are several types of rooms. For parties and celebrations, customers are provided with the following types of rooms:

  1. Big Hall - a room with a spacious dance floor. Modern music plays here, various entertainment events and performances are held. This room is very popular among visitors to Rocco, a club in Ivanovo.
  2. The Small Hall is a room with a comfortable and warm atmosphere. Here, guests can have fun and enjoy listening to fashionable music. For visitors work DJs and bartenders.
  3. Lounge - a lounge where you can relax in a relaxed atmosphere, comfortably sitting on a soft sofa. This room is perfect for celebration, and for a noisy party in a friendly company. The lighting is dim, the music is unobtrusive.
  4. Superior room. There is a separate bathroom. The in-house bar serves quality drinks. The hall is conveniently located, so guests always see what is happening on the stage.

Celebrations

At the Rocco club in Ivanovo, employees are always ready to help guests organize a special event. Here you can hold any event from your birthday in a small circle of family or friends to a large corporate party. The halls of the club can accommodate from forty to one hundred and fifty people. For each solemn event, a host is selected, an entertainment program is drawn up. Guests are invited to watch the performances of famous artists, listen to fashionable DJs, break away to the rhythms of modern music on the dance floor.

The cost of renting a hall varies from 2000 to 6000 rubles. The menu for the solemn event will cost guests about 1700 rubles. Party participants receive a discount on alcoholic beverages and free music and lighting equipment.

Assortment of food and drinks

In the institution "Rocco", a club in Ivanovo, visitors are offered a wide selection of European dishes. The menu here is about the following:

  1. Cold appetizers of meat, fish and cheese.
  2. Dishes on the grill.
  3. Salads from vegetables, meat, poultry, greens.
  4. Burgers
  5. Beef Steaks.
  6. Various desserts.
  7. Fresh juice.
  8. Alcohol (wine, champagne, beer, vodka, gin, rum).
  9. Coffee Tea.
  10. Different types of hookahs.
  11. Non alcoholic cocktails.




The average cost of food and drinks in an institution varies from five hundred to seven hundred rubles.

Customer opinions about the club

We can safely say that the reviews about this institution are rather contradictory. Some visitors believe that Rocco (a club in Ivanovo) is a great and stylish place for a night's rest. They claim that this place has a cozy atmosphere, great music and a beautiful interior.



Other customers are not at all enthusiastic about the club. They say that the institution does poor cleaning, the dance floor is uncomfortable. Some visitors were disappointed by the impolite behavior and rude attitude of security guards. In addition, there are guests who claim that the club lacks a normal air conditioning system. Many say that the quality and choice of music leaves much to be desired.

Rocco is far from the only place for night rest in the city. The clubs of Ivanovo also include Iceberg, Bridge, Atlant, Lounge and many others.
