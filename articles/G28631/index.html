"Bee honeycombs" (small cells): instructions for implementation

We start knitting this version of the pattern from the wrong side, that is, unpaired rows will form the inner side of the product, and paired ones - the front.

1 row: remove one loop (edge), * 1 front loop, remove 1 loop and double crochet *. Repeat the pattern to the last loop from the * - * icons.

2 row: (outer side of the product): hem (remove), * double crochet, without knitting, transfer from one knitting needle to another (right), 2 front loops *. Repeat the looping from the * - * icons to the last loop in the row.

3 row: hem, * 1 the back loop to be removed on another knitting needle, make a crochet, next loop, together with the crochet made in the previous row, knit the front *. From * to * repeat the design of the pattern to the end of the row.

4 row: hem, * 2 loops are knitted with the front, the yarn, without knitting, is transferred from the left knitting needle to the right *. Repeat this alternation of loops to the end of the row.

5 row: hem, * 1 knit a buttonhole together with the yarn made earlier, the front one behind the front wall, remove the wrong loop 1, make a yarn *. Repeat pattern from * to *.

6 row: hem, * yarn overlay from one knitting needle to another, without knitting, 2 front loops *. Repeat the loop sequence from the * - * icons to the end of the row.

7 row: we knit as well as 3 row, etc.





Large honeycomb pattern. We knit a sample

We knit the first row with facial loops, and the second - with the wrong ones.

3 row: 4 facial loops, 2 loops simply removed without knitting, while the thread is at work. Repeat this sequence to the end of the row.

4 row: 4 purl loops, 2 loops to remove without knitting, while the thread is before work.

5th, as well as 7th rows to perform as the 3rd. And knit the 6th and 8th rows, as the 4th. Knit the 9th row with purl stitches, and the 10th row with knit stitches.

11 row: 1 front loop, * 2 to remove, without knitting (the thread is at work), 4 front *, repeat * - * until the last loop, 1 front.

12 row: 1 wrong loop, * 2 to remove from one knitting needle to another (the thread is before work), 4 wrong ones *, repeat from signs * - * to the last chain, 1 wrong side.

The 13th, as well as the 15th rows are performed as the 11th. And the 14th and 16th rows are knitted in the same way as the 12th. One important tip for needlewomen

These options for the picture belong to the group of patterns with the loops removed. Knit them should be weak. The thread stretching along the wrong side of the work cannot be tightened, otherwise the fabric will turn out shapeless and compressed.
