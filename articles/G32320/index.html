Scorpion from beads: sketch, weaving pattern. Beading lessons for beginners

Beadwork is a fascinating and interesting activity. There are a lot of ways and options for creating various figures of animals and insects. For example, a scorpion made from beads is a work not so difficult to perform, a beginner can easily do it.

The material in the article is aimed at supporting and explaining in detail the points at which difficulty or difficulty may arise in the process of work. Beadwork lessons for beginners are more productive in simple and uncomplicated tasks. Follow the chronology of the description and do everything leisurely, carefully studying the sequence and features of the work.

Scorpion bead

Scorpion weaving scheme is quite simple in its execution.





The length of the wire, which will subsequently be used in the process, must be at least 150 centimeters. A bead made of scorpion will be created using the voluminous weaving technique.
The tiers of beads are divided into two levels - upper and lower. The upper part is the back of the scorpion. Accordingly, the lower tier will form a segmented elongated abdomen of the insect.

Your task is to understand correctly how a scorpion is gathered from beads. Sketching a future product is a key part of the work. Using a sketch on paper, you mark the number, position and color of the beads.



If the description of the ready-made set is used, then the picture is not needed. Proceed directly to the assembly.

Necessary materials and tools

Before you begin to learn beadwork lessons for beginners, you will need to prepare the following materials:

  • beads of various brown shades (brown);
  • black beads;
  • yellow or orange beads - for the eyes;
  • for firmware - thin fishing line;
  • wire with a diameter of about 0.2 millimeters.

Some recommendations

It is advisable to use large-sized beads in weaving. Colors can be chosen at your discretion and taste. Most often, beads of brown, red, yellow, orange and gold are used for weaving scorpions. But you can use any other colors and shades, thereby giving your product a unique and originality.

Scorpio: sketch from beads and assembly sequence





First row

  • Type the beads on the wire in the following sequence: one black, two brown and one black again. Then three brown beads. They form a tier that will be located below. Place the assembled set of beads in the middle of the thread.
  • The end of the wire, on which a black bead is strung, pass through three beads of a different shade on the opposite side of the base.
  • The wire must be tightened. The result was the lower and upper balls of the first line.
  • The next step is to work on the scorpion claws. Put five black beads and eleven beads of a different shade on the thread.
  • Pass the end of the thread through four black beads in the opposite direction. In this case, the last bead must be held.
  • Tighten the wire.
  • At the same end of the wire, type five beads of the same color.
  • It is necessary to hold the final bead and pass the wire in the opposite direction through the four beads. Next, pass the thread through the eleven beads of a different color.
  • Tighten the thread. Beadwork scorpion claws can be considered completed.
  • In the same way, you need to make a second claw.

Second row

  • First you need to do the first level. Put the beads on the wire in the following order: one brown, two yellow, one brown. After this, pass it through the previously typed beads. Bend the wire so that the beads are parallel to the upper ball of the previous line.
  • For the lower level, you must use five brown beads. Bend the thread so that the beads lie parallel to the upper ball.

Third row

  • To weave the upper tier, you need to take six pieces of brown beads.
  • For paws, you will need to dial eight beads of a different shade on one end of the wire.
  • The most extreme should be kept. In this case, pass the same end of the base through seven black beads in the opposite direction.
  • Tie the wire. You should get a foot.
  • Using the other side of the wire, weave the other foot.
  • To create a tier below the third line you need to apply six brown beads.

Fourth row

  • Weave six brown beads for the top ball of the craft and the same for the level that fits below.

Fifth row

He is very similar to the third row.

  • It is necessary to make a tier, which will be located on top, from brown beads - 6 pieces. Next, weave paws of 10 black beads.
  • Weave the bottom line of 6 brown beads.

Sixth row

The pattern of formation of this line is similar to the previous row.

  • Weave the top of six brown beads.
  • Next, make the paws. To make each of them you need 12 black beads.

Seventh row

  • The row, which is placed on top, has six brown beads.
  • For the lower tier, six beads of the same color are needed.

Eighth row

  • It will take five brown beads for the upper part of the scorpion and four beads for weaving the bottom.

Ninth row

  • It will take four brown beads to weave the upper tier.
  • For the bottom row, three beads of the same color should be used.

Tenth row

The scheme is the same as in the ninth row.

  • To create the top line, use three brown beads.
  • The lower tier also needs three beads.

Eleventh row

  • Weave the upper tier of three brown beads.
  • To weave the lower level, you will need two beads of the same color.

Your volumetric weaving of scorpion beads is almost complete. Let's proceed to the second part.

Tail manufacturing sketch

In the manufacturing process of the tail section, it is necessary to use the conventional parallel weaving technique. Lines should be placed one after another.

Weave five lines of two beads. Following are five, respectively, one black bead.

Fasten the thread in the following way: pass it through the previous line, twisting together the two ends of the base.





Last step

In the final, you need to flash the product with fishing line. Tighten the bead scorpion to hold its shape.





Use the usual firmware technique.

The acquired experience will make it possible to create completely new and original bead products.
