Hotel "Captain" on the Embankment

Excellent conditions for relaxation are provided by tourists at the Captain Hotel. Anapa will give you even more vivid impressions thanks to comfortable living conditions and quality service. The hotel was built in 2006. Nevertheless, its number of rooms and the entire infrastructure are in excellent condition. It is also worth noting that the windows of all rooms offer an incredibly beautiful Black Sea landscape.

In 2011, the hotel opened its own beach, where you can safely and without fuss enjoy a sea holiday. There are all the necessary conditions - changing rooms, toilet, shower, sun loungers and parasols. For the youngest tourists, a mini-pool with sea water is equipped.

Location

The location of this resort hotel is Anapa (embankment). "Captain" - a hotel, which is a couple of minutes walk from the sea. There is a bay with a port and a parking for yachts right in front of the hull, which gives you the opportunity to regularly take boat trips. Also, hotel guests will be interested in visiting the nearby Gorgippia Museum, which stores unique archaeological finds. The road to the city center, as well as the main transport hubs, will take only a few minutes.

Services for hotel guests

If you choose a vacation in Anapa, the hotel "Captain" can offer you the following services:

  • in the gym you can use the most modern simulators, as well as get advice from a qualified trainer (it is worth noting that classes for men and women are held on different days);
  • you can relax in a classic steam room or undergo an aromatherapy session in a phyto-sauna;
  • You can take a steam bath in a Russian bath in compliance with all traditions;
  • Wi-Fi is available throughout (access password is provided free of charge);
  • the hotel has a saltwater pool;
  • the use of car parking with 24-hour security is available at an additional cost;
  • you can use the laundry service if you have dirty or wrinkled clothes;
  • reception staff can order a taxi for you at any time of the day;
  • You can order a transfer service from the airport (from the train station) and back.

Mini Hotel

The hotel has recently opened a mini-hotel "Captain". Anapa boasts thickets of juniper and fir, and the building is almost completely made of these materials. That is why hundreds of adherents of a healthy lifestyle come here every year, as well as those tourists who have problems with the respiratory tract.

The mini-hotel has only 20 rooms, but they all overlook the Black Sea. In the mornings, you can admire swarms of dolphins passing by, and in the evenings - yachts passing by. It is also worth noting the round-the-clock operation of a heated pool with sea water.

  • Room of the second category differs from previous apartments from the view from the window. From the balcony you can admire the main street of the city.

Rules of residence

Hotel "Captain" (Anapa) operates in accordance with the following rules of accommodation:

  • Reception and check-in is carried out around the clock;
  • registration is carried out only after presentation of the passport;
  • to check in the room for children under 14 years old, you must provide a birth certificate, as well as a power of attorney (if the child arrived at the hotel with accompanying persons);
  • Payment for accommodation and related services can be made in cash and non-cash;
  • The hotel does not offer the possibility of pets;
  • on the day of departure, guests must leave the room before noon (late check-out is charged extra);
  • for accommodation in a room less than 24 hours, the cost of a full day is charged;
  • in summer, to confirm the reservation, an advance payment is required in the amount of the daily cost of living;
  • in case of systematic violation of the rules and public order, the hotel administration has the right to terminate the accommodation contract unilaterally;
  • things forgotten by guests are stored in the hotel for 6 months (if we are talking about valuables or large sums of money, then the period is extended to 1 year);
  • smoking is allowed exclusively on the balconies.

General impression

Hotel "Captain" on Pioneer Avenue is a good option for budget accommodation. The hotel is located in the city center near many infrastructure facilities. But to go far enough to the sea. As for the hotel "Captain" on the Embankment, this is an institution with a claim to luxury. It is located in close proximity to the sea and delights guests with beautiful views from the windows of the rooms.
