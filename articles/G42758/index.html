The most interesting sights of Oryol

The eagle bears the proud title of a city ​​of military glory. In 1566, it was erected precisely to ensure the protection and protection of the territory of the Moscow principality from raids by enemies. Over the entire history of its existence, the city has repeatedly experienced the hardships of hostilities, troops were held here and battles were fought with the Crimean Tatars. The modern sights of Orel have largely preserved the memory of past times and military operations in the district.

This city was founded as a fortress at the confluence of two rivers. The favorable climate and large fertile lands made its surroundings attractive for the lives of many noblemen. The main attractions of Orel are the apartment museums and estates of famous and talented writers who have made a great contribution to the development of literature.



During the construction of the city, the banks of the Oka, which divides it into two parts, were not lined in strict embankments. They remained steep and steep, with city beaches and tree planting, giving a picturesque view to all the surroundings. Interesting monuments and the indescribable atmosphere created by the merchant architecture of the buildings have been preserved in the city center.

The main attractions of Oryol can begin to be seen from the place of the city. Currently, there is a small square with a memorial. It was installed in honor of the 400th anniversary of the city. The key dates in the history of the Eagle are carved on it. Not far from this memorial place is the oldest temple in the city. Initially, the Epiphany Cathedral was a wooden building, but it was destroyed during a fire. After that, the cathedral was built of stone. At the moment, it is one of the oldest stone buildings in the city of Oryol. Sights, whose photos are striking in their beauty, include an ancient carved iconostasis. This cathedral is also famous for its icons, many of which are considered miraculous.





The Assumption Monastery is also interesting to visit. Not all buildings have been preserved here to our time, but a visit to the territory will certainly impress you. All visitors to the monastery are treated to delicious artesian water, cool and healthy.

The main attractions of Orel are associated with famous writers whose estates and apartments have survived to this day. The city houses the Turgenev Museum, which was founded in honor of the 100th anniversary of the birth of the famous writer.

On one of the high banks of the river is an interesting reserve, which is called the "Noble Nest". It was here, according to popular belief, that the estate was located where the main actions took place, described in the novel of the same name by Turgenev. Today you can walk around the park, sit in the gazebo, enjoy the magnificent view from the observation deck.



Numerous tourists and townspeople will be interested in the events that are going to be held on Oryol City Day 2013. The holiday program is distinguished by its diversity and diversity.
