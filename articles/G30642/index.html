Rest in Myskhako: reviews of tourists

With the advent of summer, everyone is eager to leave away from the bustle of the city. But if someone‚Äôs rest is limited to work in the country, then many will enjoy going to the sea. Modern bases require a lot of money from tourists, and not everyone can afford such a pleasure. For tourists who want to have a good rest and not spend a lot of money, there are places where they can go with the whole family. One of these places is the village of Myskhako, reviews of vacationers about him are positive. This is a Caucasian resort, which attracts more and more tourists every year.

What is the village of Myskhako

Myskhako is a small village near Novorossiysk. Here you can relax and save a little on paying for housing. Other microdistricts and towns of Novorossiysk are crowded with various hotels, hotels, and recreation centers. You will not find this in Myskhako. Here, the entire coast is built up with private cottages and houses. Tourists are offered to rent a whole cottage or a separate room.

All houses are located within a 2-minute walk to the sea. It is very comfortable. It cannot be said that Myskhako is a budget village. But you still manage to save some money. Many tourists, having heard reviews about their holidays in Myskhako, travel here with large companies and rent houses for a very long time. The rooms have everything you might need during your vacation. If you are still in doubt, then read the reviews of vacationers about the rest in Myskhako, many even post photos.





How to find

Myskhako village is the south of Novorossiysk. To get to the city center from there, you have to overcome six kilometers. The cottage village is conveniently located on the eastern slope of Mount Koldun. This is a relatively low mountain at 447 meters. If you want to enjoy the local nature, then visit Cape Myskhako, which is located in the west of the village. This is an incredible natural beauty that attracts a look again and again. From this height you can admire the Tsemess Bay and the city of Novorossiysk itself.

You can get here in any way convenient for you:

  • By rail. From many cities of Russia there is a direct train to Novorossiysk.
  • Through the air. There is no airport in the city, but if you fly to Anapa, you can easily get to the right place.
  • By sea. In summer, they launch special flights for tourists, which allow you to get to the vacation spot from any coastal city.
  • By car. Many choose this particular mode of transportation. To get to Novorossiysk, you should follow the M25-M4 highway.

When you get to the city, you can take the bus that will bring you to Myskhako, or hire a taxi.





General information about the village

As already mentioned, Myskhako is a small cottage village, which belongs to the Novorossiysk region of the Krasnodar Territory. Private houses prevail here. The coast has just begun to be built up, but today the population is eight thousand people. Many come here to relax and rent cottages for a certain period. But someone buys these cottages to use as a summer residence or rent to vacationers and earn money on it.

Of course, the village is famous not only for its beautiful views. The pride of this village is a winery. Vineyards have appeared here since ancient times. They were planted in 1869. This happened almost immediately after the accession of this area to the Russian Empire. The wine produced at this plant is named after this place. Myskhako is produced here - wine, reviews of which are heard on all beaches and in all restaurants of the coast. Since not only local vacationers, but also those tourists who live on the same coast can enjoy its pleasant taste.

If you still cannot decide where to go, feel free to go to Myskhako. Here you can enjoy stunning nature, swim in the transparent sea, sunbathe on clean and low-filled beaches. And the highlight of your holiday will be wine, which is produced at a local factory. Not a single vacationer has yet brought reviews from the village of Myskhako that would not have attracted his friends and acquaintances.





Leisure

Young people can easily get to the city to visit a nightclub or other entertainment venue. Tourists with children can also find something to their liking in the city, for example, a cinema. Those who do not go on holiday during the swimming season can attend the young grape festival, which takes place in November. This is another exciting sight and the opportunity to taste the young wine, which in a few years will change its taste. Perhaps this will be an occasion to re-visit this place.

Even without all kinds of excursions and entertainment, you can spend time with benefit for yourself. The incredible nature, the cleanest sea and the complete absence of city bustle and noise - what else does a tired tourist need on vacation.





Beaches

First of all, Myskhako is the sea, the reviews of which are the most positive, but to get into the water, you need to go through the beach. Local beaches can be divided into three zones. The first is the central beach, which is covered with pebbles. Here you can find a variety of water activities and other services for tourists. The entrance to the sea here is very convenient, so the majority of tourists converge on this beach. This creates some inconvenience, since the length is only 350 meters. The central beach belongs to the eastern part of the cape.

Followed by wild beaches. Fewer people gather here, so the sea is much cleaner and calmer, and on the beach you can quickly find a suitable place. They are also pebbled, but there are places where the sea meets the mountain, and the coast is more rocky and steep. Such places are very fond of extreme sportsmen who like to jump from rocks. Although this is a rather dangerous pleasure.

The third zone is small beaches that are located in the settlement area and are practically not equipped.





Winery "Myskhako"

This is one of the main attractions of Novorossiysk. A visit to the winery is included in the route of many excursions that are held here. Once here, you can enjoy the open views of the plant and its vineyards, the fruits of which are used to make local wine. This plant is considered one of the oldest and largest in the region.

Then the tour continues in the cellars, where the wines are stored with many years of aging. Such always cost a lot of money. This factory even produces ice wine, which most likely not everyone has heard of. It is made from grapes that are frozen right on the vine. The best wine of this kind can be tasted only by Canadian producers.

The tour ends with a tasting. Tourists are offered several types of wines for tasting, you can buy them for your own collection. An interesting fact is that at this plant they make wines that are delivered to the Kremlin. And the president of the Russian Federation himself prefers a drink that is produced at the Myskhako winery.

Based on this, it can be concluded that the main souvenir brought from Myskhako will be a bottle of your favorite drink from the largest winery in the region.
