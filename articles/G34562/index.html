Important differences

At first glance at such scarves, it seems that they look exactly the same, no different, in fact, no different. But is it really so? The most important hallmark is size. But in single-turn options there are differences. The knitter knits her product as a simple straight scarf, and after the end of work she sews it with a knitted seam into a ring. Work stages

How to knit a scarf collar for men when using any pattern? We need knitting needles number six and seven for work, yarn should have about 300 g. 180 needles are knitted on knitting needles No. 6 (circular), a marker must be put at the end of the row. Now knitting can be knitted and knitted in a circle. On such knitting needles, six rows must be knitted, constantly alternating: a series of purl stitches, a number of facial loops. It turns out a garter stitch.





Knit one row with the front loops. Next, knitting needles No. 7 come on. One row is knitted on them. Now the scarf can be knitted according to any chosen pattern to the height that is needed. Even rows should be knitted strictly according to the pattern.

Next, knit one row with facial loops using knitting needles No. 7. Change to knitting needles No. 6 and knit a row with facial loops. The six rows that go afterwards need to be knitted, doing one at a time - a row of facial and a row of purl stitches (this is a garter stitch). Now you need to close the loop. The scarf is ready.

241 loops are drawn on the knitting needles, everything is in a circle, and then it fits in according to the scheme:

  • first row - only facial loops;
  • second row - knitting two loops together with the front (the right loop should go over the left), double crochet *, repeat exactly the same from * to * to the last loop, one front;
  • third row - all loops are knitted wrong;
  • the fourth row - we make one front, double crochet, remove one loop without knitting, one front, throw on the knitted loop * removed, repeat from * to * until the end of the loops on the knitting needle.




All the rest of the work consists of knitting rows to the width that the craftswoman needs.
