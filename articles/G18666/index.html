DIY cap pattern

For some girls, picking a hat is an unusually difficult task. That model does not fit, then the size. And sometimes the hat seems to suit you, but its design is not very happy. How to find a way out? Sew a hat yourself. Caps patterns you can find below.





Simple hat

It is very simple to make a pattern for the caps of such a style. To make it, you need artificial or natural fur. Before proceeding to the manufacture of caps, you need to measure the length of the head circumference. For accuracy, use a soft meter. Next, you can start drawing a pattern of hats made of fur. By analogy with the sample, you need to build such a geometric figure, the bottom of which will consist of a rectangle, and the top - of four triangles. If your sizes differ from the above, then you should draw a hat pattern with your own hands. If the size is the same, you can simply print the image on the correct scale. Now you should draw a pattern on the back of the fur. This is best done with soap or chalk. These materials can be easily removed even without washing. Finishing a hat is no problem. It is necessary to combine the back seam and sew the triangles so that they all join at the center.





Wide hat with fur trim

Sewing such a hat is not difficult. The cap pattern shown above is one rectangle and 6 more details. From what to sew a hat - it's up to you. It is advisable to use natural fur and leather, but you can replace them with any synthetic materials. You need to start sewing a hat by cutting out 6 parts. They are sewn together in a circle. In order for the product to be worn even in the most severe cold, it should be made of fabric or tie a lining. It remains only to cut the fur trim and sew it on the headpiece. The pattern of hats made of fur can be modified. For example, add more wedges or make them a little smaller. Each girl must decide individually, taking into account her parameters and personal preferences.





Turban hat

Such a hat is suitable for girls with an oriental appearance. The cap pattern consists of one part. Measure the circumference of your head and, based on this figure, modify the drawing. If you are afraid to make a mistake, you can take measurements from your hat that suits you exactly. Now you need to choose the right fabric. The turban looks good in both bright and neutral colors. But you should not choose something printable, otherwise the embossed seams that serve as the main decoration of the headgear will simply be invisible. We draw a pattern of a female hat on the back of the material with chalk. Now it needs to be cut. The next step is to assemble the headgear. We sew all the tucks so that they fit on the back of the head at one point. Now you need to close this place with a decorative overlay, and the hat will be ready.





Parody of snood

Today it is fashionable to wear not hats, but scarves. But in severe frost, such a product does not protect against wind and does not heat well. Alternatively, you can sew a snood hat. It looks like a swirling scarf, but it warms perfectly and is not blown by the wind. You can make it from fur, from fleece or even knit. It is advisable to collect such a hat from two fabrics. Moreover, the lining may be in a contrasting color relative to the top. So it will be even more interesting. When the material is purchased, and you tuned in, you can begin to make patterns for women's hats. We print the picture attached above, redraw it at the correct scale. Now you should cut two parts from each material. Do not forget that one part will be the same as on the pattern, and the second should be its mirror image. Now you need to sew the details of the top and bottom, and then connect them to each other. The last action is sewing a large button and making a hinge for it.





Ushanka

Today it is difficult to meet a man in a hat of a similar style. But the girls just love earflaps. These hats look provocatively and are perfect for both everyday wear and a tracksuit. How to sew a hat that can be worn in a feast, and in the world? You need to print the pattern. It can be scaled in advance on a computer in any graphics program. If you do not have the necessary skills - it does not matter. It is allowed to independently draw a pattern in the right size. Now transfer the pattern to fabric or fur. To make the headgear look more interesting, you should make it colorful. For example, combine pink and white. We cut out all the details and start stitching one by one. First you need to connect the side parts with a long wedge, and then sew everything else. To make the product look strict, you should not sew on the ties, but in a child or adolescent version, they will be quite appropriate. Instead of regular shoelaces, you can sew braid caps or pompons on your ears.
