Choose the best hotel in Turkey for a vacation with a child

Turkey is famous all over the world for its amazing nature, beautiful beaches and picturesque landscapes. Tourists, attracted by the mild climate, the opportunity to touch the secrets of ancient cities and the beauty of olive groves, often ask: "Recommend a good hotel in Turkey."





The catch is that it is almost impossible to give a universal answer to this question, since everyone has different tastes and financial possibilities. In addition, someone travels alone and prefers to stay in low-cost hotels, while someone travels as a family, is looking for the best hotel in Turkey to relax with a child and agrees only to a five-star hotel and the all inclusive system . So, before choosing a suitable hotel, you need to decide which type of vacation is preferable for you or your family.

The best hotels of Turkey for vacation

For those who prefer to combine relaxation with sports, Alanya is the perfect resort for the Turkish Riviera, which is gaining popularity among lemon and orange gardens on the shores of the warm Mediterranean Sea. The main advantage of this resort center is that on its territory inexpensive and comfortable guest houses coexist perfectly with luxurious complexes. Staying here, you can try your hand at extreme sports such as trekking, mountain climbing and rafting.





Families traveling with children try to find the best hotel in Turkey for families with children. Of course, it also depends on personal preferences and financial capabilities. But in any case, it should be the most comfortable and safe place for children.

What could be more attractive to children than a large, modern amusement park or water park? In this case, the best hotel in Turkey for families with a child is the one located within walking distance from the most popular amusement parks:

  • Tatilla Park is located near Istanbul and is rightly called the best Turkish Disneyland. In terms of size, it is in first place in Europe and fourth in the world. Both children and adults can find here entertainment to their liking.

  • Aqualand are endless expanses of water and breathtaking rides. Entertaining slides, a complex of pools for the smallest and many bridges, slides and lakes of various depths. In addition, on the territory of Aqualand there is the most impressive dolphinarium of Turkey Dolphiland - a park of marine trained animals.



Choosing the best hotel in Turkey for a vacation with a child, it is best to pay attention to those that have their own beach, and the bottom of the sea is sandy, since many children do not like large pebbles under their bare feet.

When you are planning a vacation to Turkey with children, do not forget about the need to collect a children's first-aid kit. Despite the large number of pharmacies working even at night, problems can always arise when choosing medicines, since all the usual medicines are called differently in this country. When traveling with a child, it is better to foresee all possible surprises in advance and be prepared for them.
