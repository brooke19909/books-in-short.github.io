Trip to Peterhof on a meteor

The famous Peterhof is a unique historical monument. Literally translated from German means "Peter's court". Everyone who once visited the Northern capital, visited Peterhof. This museum is located on the shores of the Gulf of Finland. It has a luxurious park complex created during the reign of Peter I. This place will not leave anyone indifferent! Excursions to Peterhof on a meteor are very interesting.





Cultural Heritage on the Gulf of Finland

On the vast territory of the complex there are a wide variety of fountains. They work in the summer. Close with the onset of autumn. Huge statues, covered with gilding, sparkle in the rays of the summer sun. However, at the end of October, these most beautiful statues are hidden for the winter from the eyes of visitors. Peter the Great Palace is located not in the center of St. Petersburg, but outside the city. There are many ways to get to the "Russian Versailles".

Driving a car is sometimes long and tiring. And what can you see when traveling exclusively on the highway ?! Nothing special. The train route also does not promise anything exciting. But there is one type of transport in St. Petersburg that will ride you through the water with a breeze, bypassing all traffic jams! We present to your attention a meteor! This is the fastest and most reliable way to get to the palace, but also the most expensive. Do not spare money to ride on it. After all, the meteor route passes through various historical monuments of the city. A walk in the fresh air along the St. Petersburg river will be remembered for a lifetime. A trip to Peterhof on a meteor is fantastic!





Purchasing a ticket for a meteor trip

Buy a ticket for this type of transport is not difficult. They are sold on online sites, as well as at the box office of the city. The trip takes about 30 minutes. The first meteor sets off, usually at 10:00. The latter leaves the Gulf of Finland for St. Petersburg at 18:00. When buying a ticket, you need to tell the cashier what time you plan to leave the palace and park complex. The intervals of movement of motor ships for each company are different. Basically, they range from 15 to 60 minutes. Please note that the meteor movement is strongly tied to weather conditions. With a strong and squally wind, they usually do not swim. In St. Petersburg, there are several companies selling tickets for motor ships. But prices are about the same everywhere. A trip to Peterhof on a meteor (2014) was 500 rubles one way. Two years later, in 2016, ticket prices rose. Now their cost is 750 rubles or 1300 rubles if you are going there and back. There are benefits for certain categories of citizens. So, a ticket to Peterhof on a meteor is sold at a discount to pensioners, veterans, children, disabled people and students. Important! A ticket to a meteor does not include a visit to Peterhof. A tour of the palace must be paid separately on site.





Where do the ships depart

Meteors set sail from two embankments - Palace and English. Slowly, the boat unfolds in the waters of the Neva and gradually picks up speed, leaving behind St. Isaac's Cathedral and Vasilievsky Island. Features of the meteor inside

Inside the body of the ship is divided into three compartments. Each corresponds to the classes: first, second, third. The higher the category, the more expensive the ticket. The first class, the most comfortable, is designed on the principle of "minimum seats - maximum space." Looking through a panoramic window, you will feel like a captain of a ship. The second class is arranged similarly, only there are more seats. The third (economy) is the cheapest. He is less comfortable. There is vibration in this class. Cradles not much. But before the trip, do not eat tightly.

Between the compartments there are observation platforms with low sides. On a sunny day, you will certainly be wearing glasses. On the observation deck, the wind blows them in a split second. Places here are occupied on the principle of "where I managed, I got there." A meteor arrives at the pier located nearby the Lower Park of the palace. Many people think that this is the fastest way to get to Peterhof.





Climate features in St. Petersburg

St. Petersburg has its own climatic features. The weather is moody like nowhere. If in the morning the sun was shining in the sky, then after some time it may begin to rain: torrential or drizzling from dawn to dusk. Stock up with durable raincoats, because in Peterhof there is nowhere to hide and hide from the rain. By the way, on the territory there are restaurants with high prices on the menu. Therefore, if you plan a long walk for several hours, then be sure to bring rations and drinks with you. But, despite the vagaries of nature and a certain high cost, a trip to Peterhof on a meteor is an unforgettable and fascinating pastime in the cultural capital of our state!
