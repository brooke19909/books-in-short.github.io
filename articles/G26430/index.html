Want to have a great rest? Picturesque Benitses (Corfu) is waiting for tourists!

The most northern island of Greece, distinguished by its natural beauty, has been popular with tourists for many years. It has everything that can satisfy the most demanding traveler: warm sea, gentle sun, many architectural attractions.

Many centuries ago, the island was ruled by the Roman state and inherited a pretty resort, which began to develop rapidly from the late 70s of the last century. The name Benitses (Corfu) is translated as "a corner rich in water." Indeed, there has never been a shortage of life-giving moisture.

Some facts about the resort

The colorful Benitses (Corfu), whose photo causes an irresistible desire to get to know him, is one of the main tourist centers of Greece. An ancient settlement, known since antiquity, is located 12 kilometers from the island's capital. The resort, located between the old harbor and the lush green hills, stretches for several kilometers along the coast. After the advent of the modern port, built specifically for tourist boats, the appearance of the busy village has changed radically.



Paradise for vacationers

The quiet place is ideal for a relaxing holiday, and it is not in vain that couples with children love it, as well as lovers of solitude with nature. Initially, Benitses in Corfu was incredibly popular with young people having fun parties on the beach, but the locals, tired of the noise, did everything to ban them, and now the resort is calm and quiet. There is practically no night club life, and those who are used to daily parties should pay attention to other Greek resorts.

The miniature village boasts a developed infrastructure: luxury hotels, cozy taverns and restaurants, numerous souvenir shops and shops attract thousands of guests all year round.

Beach vacation

Of course, in sunny Greece, beach vacations take first place. Benitses (Corfu) boasts clear water and clean sand. In addition, there are a lot of algae in the sea, which are the first sign of its purity. Posidonia, growing at the very bottom, has the unique ability to filter water, thus reducing the content of harmful substances.



The resort's beach, which received the Blue Flag Award for the excellent quality of the services provided, is equipped with everything necessary for outdoor activities.

What else to do?

Tourists who want to get acquainted with the unique sights of Benitses (Corfu) should visit the ancient quarters located at the end of the village, the ruins of Roman terms built before our era, the maritime museum, which contains thousands of shells and other inhabitants of the sea.

Fans of fresh air will appreciate the hiking trails of different difficulty levels leading to the mountains.

Hotels for every taste

Numerous hotels in Benitses (Corfu) accept tourists all year round. As vacationers admit, no problems with housing arise.



Potamaki Beach Hotel is a five-story beachfront building with simple rooms. In addition, there is a freshwater pool and a huge terrace with sun loungers, sun umbrellas and showers.

Apartments Corfu Sun Sea Side is a whole complex, conveniently located on the seafront and offering the best value for money. Spacious apartments, a furnished balcony, a kitchen with everything you need, free parking nearby will appeal to those who are not used to saving on vacation.

Hotel Kaiser Bridge is a luxury hotel with wonderful panoramas. All rooms have balconies overlooking the Ionian Sea, delightful mountains or the lush garden. Just 10 meters away is the beach, which cannot but please the guests.

Benitses (Corfu): reviews

According to many tourists who had a good rest in the village, they chose it because of its convenient location: it is not far from the capital of the island and interesting sights from here. There is a port and a parking for yachts, so everyone can order an excursion and go on an exciting adventure.

A wonderful corner pleasantly surprises with the cost of accommodation. Amazing natural beauty leaves no one indifferent, and legends go about an excellent beach vacation among tourists.

Recently discovered in the resort, the ruins of ancient Roman baths with perfectly preserved mosaics attract archeology lovers here.



The landscapes of the original Benitses (Corfu, Greece), which is the embodiment of our ideas about paradise, are created for the enjoyment of the eyes. The hospitable resort has a unique face - sunny and colorful. And it is not surprising that tourists who have visited here once are sure to return.
