Hotel Calamander Unawatuna Beach 4 * (Sri Lanka): photos and reviews of tourists

Calamander Unawatuna Beach 4 * is a unique hotel. Exotic Sri Lanka will not leave indifferent any traveler. And this hotel will create a special mysterious atmosphere.

Hotel Features

The main feature of the resort complex Calamander Unawatuna Beach 4 * is its design solution. First of all, it is worth paying attention to three unique pools, namely:

  • a pool with glass sides, which offers a magnificent view of the Indian Ocean (is the longest in the Asia-Pacific region);
  • The world's first red water pool;
  • a children's pool decorated with imitation of a turtle shell made of Italian mosaic.

Also, you can not ignore the main restaurant Calamander Unawatuna Beach 4 * . This is a kind of imitation of the royal auditorium Magul, which is located on Mount Kandy. The main feature of the institution is a massive solid wood roof, which is the largest in Sri Lanka. The restaurant is located right on Unawatuna Beach.

As for the Calamander Unawatuna Beach 4 * rooms, they are made in a minimalist style, which can be considered appropriate against the background of the overall luxury of the establishment. The main emphasis is placed on natural lighting, for which there are large windows. All furniture is made of natural mahogany. The design is dominated by natural materials.
