Hospitable Turkey: Belpinar Hotel in Kemer

The hot southern sun, the smell of coniferous forests and citrus orchards, the gentle waves of the Mediterranean Sea, the hospitality of local residents - all this gives southern Turkey, Kemer. Hotel Belpinar is a real gem of this resort. This is a club hotel, not in vain it was called Palmariva Club Belpinar 4 * until the beginning of 2013. To be more precise, it is located not in Kemer, but in the village of Beldibi. Getting to the city is not difficult - after all, minibuses (here they are called dolmushi) stop every quarter of an hour right at the gate of the hotel.



What is this four-star hotel "Belpinar" (Turkey)? The photo clearly shows that the vast territory is divided into two zones: quiet, sleeping, where there are three multi-story buildings, and active, entertaining. Between them lies a walking alley and an underground tunnel. All rooms are categorized into standard doubles, Family Room and Junior Suit, offering chic views of the sea or the majestic mountains. Each room has its own balcony. The room equipment is fully consistent with the four-star category: there is air conditioning, a minibar, an LSD-TV, and a hairdryer.

Going on a trip, forget about diets - it's Turkey! The Belpinar Hotel invites you to enjoy the ultra all-inclusive food system, which means that you can eat and eat from early morning until late in the evening. By the way, all alcoholic drinks of local production, as well as five types of imported wine are also available to guests free of charge. In addition to the main restaurant, there is also a separate restaurant with Turkish cuisine in the territory , where A La Carte is served , but visiting it is already for a fee.
