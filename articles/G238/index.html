Poodle Artemon - a costume for a masquerade based on the fairy tale "Golden Key"

What outfit to choose for a children's party? If all the ideas seem banal to you and nothing new comes to mind, turn to fairytales known from childhood for help. Everyone knows the story of the wooden boy Pinocchio - "Golden Key". One of the characters in this fairy tale is the poodle Artemon, the costume of this hero is ideal for any boy!

How to make a fancy dress with your own hands?



Factory-made carnival outfits are sometimes quite expensive. Why spend so much money on a ‚Äúdisposable‚Äù costume if you can do it yourself? You do not even need to be a professional seamstress. Character Artemon costume has a fairly simple. In the tale, he is described as a big black poodle. Pick up basic clothing in dark colors. It is very good if you have a black fur vest. From scraps of fur or pieces, you can sew small cuffs worn on the wrists and ankles. Do not forget to make the same tail, long enough with a pompom on the tip, like a real poodle. If you make an Artemon costume with your own hands from the clothes that you plan to wear later, all the masquerade details should be made removable. For example, the tail will hold well on a knitted belt or elastic band.

Important Image Details



Any poodle boasts lush curly ears. No exception to this rule and the fabulous Artemon. The costume can be supplemented with a hat with ears. They are easiest to make from fur or fluffy threads for knitting. Sew such ears to any finished black hat or rim. If you do not have such materials, try making a head carnival out of paper. Cut two ears of a suitable size, glue their outer sides with paper strips cut with a fringe from a sheet of black. Separately, cut out a strip of thick paper and glue a ring from it along the circumference of the head. Attach the ears to the resulting workpiece. A blue or red satin bow around the neck is an accessory that Artemon constantly wore in the fairy tale. The costume for the masquerade of this character will be appropriate to complement with such an ornament. From the same tape, you can make small bows and attach them to fur cuffs.
