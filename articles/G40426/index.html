Guest houses Lermontovo (Krasnodar Territory): description, addresses, prices

The popular resort village of Lermontovo is located at the mouth of the Shapsuho River, on the Black Sea. The famous resort of Dzhubga is located just four kilometers from this village.

It is a well-developed modern resort. On its territory there are many boarding houses, recreation centers, hotels and guest houses located almost on the coast. Here you can relax in large entertainment complexes, improve your health in health centers, enjoy the magnificent nature in the parks.



Climatic conditions

The climate in Lermontovo is subtropical, hot. The village is surrounded by mountains covered with dense coniferous forests, so the air here is filled with aromas of pine needles and is considered very useful. In summer, it warms up to +29 ¬∞ C, and water - up to +25 ¬∞ C. The holiday season begins in late May and continues until early October. In some years, daytime September temperatures are higher than June temperatures. Late autumn and winter in Lermontovo are dank and rainy, resort life freezes.

Where to stay?

In recent years, more and more vacationers prefer guest houses in Lermontovo. Prices for accommodation in them are usually lower than in hotels and pensions, the service is excellent. Many such establishments include meals in the price.

Guest houses (Lermontovo) are located 10-15 minutes walk from the sea coast. For the most part, these are new buildings with spacious areas, swimming pools, car parking. The owners are trying to provide guests with comfortable living conditions, to create maximum comfort.
