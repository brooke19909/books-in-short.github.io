Privat bank transfers from Russia to Ukraine: features. Is it possible to transfer money from Russia to Ukraine on a PrivatBank card

It is far from a secret that relations between Russia and Ukraine have never been simple. This is due to many reasons, for the most part artificially and illegally created. However, no one will dissolve relations between relatives living in one territory and working in another. So you have to look for ways out of the situation, especially regarding monetary issues. For example, Privat Bank helps to make transfers from Russia to Ukraine. How easy it is to perform such operations, we study further.





What are the options for transferring funds?

Currently, there are several options for transferring funds to Ukraine. For example, you can do this by bank transfer and pay in cash at the bank's cash desk or at a specialized service manager. Moreover, the funds you send can be credited very quickly to the card or bank account of the recipient. Or they can be cashed through the bank cash desk, presenting a code number and passport.

According to the same system, the Ukrainian PrivatBank also works. Money transfer from Russia to Ukraine is carried out in compliance with the internal policy and staffing of the recipient credit organization.





Instant transfers from MoneyGram

If you prefer cash transfers, you can always choose one of the most suitable options. For example, it could be MoneyGram urgent international money transfer system.

Its main advantage is that it is not necessary to open an account to complete a payment transaction. And the time of crediting funds takes no more than 10 minutes. During this time, your money will go to Privat Bank. Transfers from Russia to Ukraine using this service are quick and flawless.





Fast money transfer to the account

One of the most common options for transferring funds from one account to another is SWIFT (international non-cash payments), which are supported by Russian credit organizations and the Ukrainian Privat Bank. Transfers from Russia to Ukraine using this service are carried out in any branch of a domestic bank.

They are reliable, have a high degree of protection and a high transfer rate of funds transferred. However, these transfers are fraught with a rather high commission. This is especially true when you want to transfer money from Russia to Ukraine. PrivatBank in this case will be a foreign credit organization. This means that you will have to pay 1-3% commission for the services of the domestic bank of the sender.





How to send a SWIFT payment?

In order to transfer funds using SWIFT transfer, you just need to bring your passport with you and go to the nearest bank branch. Further, it remains to go to the cashier, to inform about their desire to transfer money from Russia to Ukraine (to Privatbank, for example), give a passport.

After that, it is enough to indicate the surname and name of the recipient, as well as the SWIFT code of the banks (sender and recipient). Most often, the cashier or operator of your department fills in all of these boxes. And, finally, it remains only to make the necessary amount, taking into account the size of the commission, to confirm the desire to make the transfer and communicate its details to its recipient.

Do transfers work through Western Union?

The Western Union international system is currently operating and payments are being processed. In order to make such a transfer, you must come with a passport to the nearest bank or organization that supports such transactions.

Further, it remains only to indicate the surname and name of the recipient, deposit money taking into account the commission (not more than $ 3, less often 1.7% of the amount) and find out the secret code. After instant crediting of funds, do not forget to inform its recipient.
