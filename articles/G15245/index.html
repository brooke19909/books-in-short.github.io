"Mountain Residence" - an apart-hotel in Sochi (Krasnaya Polyana). Description, reviews

For bright summer and active winter vacations, the Mountain Residence hotel is ideally suited. Here you can fully enjoy the comfort, quality service and, of course, incredibly beautiful surrounding landscapes.





Location

If you want to enjoy the beautiful mountain scenery and enjoy all the modern benefits during your vacation, you should pay attention to the hotel "Mountain Residence". Krasnaya Polyana, 81/4 Estonian Street - the address of the resort. It is about an hour from Adler Train Station. As for Sochi International Airport , the hotel is almost 50 km away from it. The nearest ski lift is just 300 meters from the hotel.

Tourist sites

A lot of interesting places can be visited by guests of the Mountain Residence Hotel. Krasnaya Polyana is rich in sights, among which it is worth noting the following tourist sites:

  • lift "Mountain carousel" (300 m);
  • Mzymta River (500 m);
  • Gazprom lift (1 km);
  • carousel-8 (2 km);
  • cable car (2 km);
  • sledding center (2 km);
  • complex "Russian hills" (3 km);
  • ski resort "Rosa Khutor" (3 km);
  • ski complex "Laura" (5 km).

Accommodation and prices

Comfortable rooms decorated in calm pastel colors await guests at the Mountain Residence. The following accommodation options are available to travelers:

room Description Price (per day, in rubles)
Suite A cozy two-room suite consists of a bedroom, as well as a small bright living room. Guests can stay in large or twin beds. For relaxation, as well as for an extra guest, a soft sofa is provided. from 2500
Apartments The apartment is a functional room, the hallmark of which is the presence of its own kitchen area. It is separated from the living space by a bar. Beds and a soft sofa are provided for a comfortable stay. from 4000
Two-room apartment This room differs from the previous accommodation option by the presence of two separate bedrooms. One of them has a French bed, and the other a pair of one and a half. from 6000
Family apartments This option is designed to accommodate 5 guests. It has 3 separate bedrooms, as well as a living room combined with the kitchen area. The highlight of the room can be considered the presence of a decorative fireplace. from 7500

The price includes a continental breakfast, as well as wireless internet and private parking. It is also worth noting that the rooms of the Residence Mountain Hotel are decorated in a modern, simple style. And warm shades contribute to the fact that the rooms have a calm and cozy atmosphere.





In the rooms

Everything that a modern tourist may need for a good rest is in the rooms of the Mountain Residence hotel. Sochi will please you with a warm welcome, and in your apartments you can take advantage of such amenities:

  • individual air conditioning for cooling the air temperature in the room;
  • centralized heating system for relaxing in the winter;
  • refrigerator for storing food stocks;
  • kitchenette with appliances and utensils for cooking;
  • a tea set;
  • combined bathroom with a set of bath accessories and a built-in electric hairdryer.




Hotel Restaurant

Quality rest is impossible without tasty and nutritious nutrition. The restaurant "Mountain Residence" is responsible for this area. The cozy dining room is decorated in a classic style. The interior is dominated by luxurious golden shades, as well as dark wood. The atmosphere of warmth and homeliness of the restaurant gives a decorative fireplace.

The main specialization of the restaurant is Russian cuisine. However, the menu also contains European dishes. A good addition to delicious food will be quality wine or hot mulled wine. Fine dining desserts will be a great end to the meal. The dining room of the restaurant is designed for 60 people. Thus, this room is ideal for celebrations and gala banquets.

Helpful information

Before going to the hotel, guests should check out some useful information. So, the following information can be considered the most significant:

  • registration of newly arrived guests is carried out around the clock, and eviction - before noon;
  • upon check-in, guests are required to pay a deposit in cash or non-cash;
  • registration is possible only with a passport;
  • Self check-in at the hotel is only from 18 years old;
  • in one room no more than 2 children under 7 years old can stay for free;
  • Pets are only allowed if you have notified the hotel in advance.

Negative reviews

Apart-hotel "Mountain Residence" is characterized by a number of shortcomings. The most significant are the following:

  • windows and balcony doors do not close tightly, and therefore they allow noise and cold air from the street to pass through;
  • the rooms are very smoky;
  • in the rooms there are not enough lighting fixtures, in addition, they emit unpleasant cold light;
  • there is no microwave in the dining room, and therefore, if you arrive at the end of breakfast, you will have to eat cold dishes;
  • in the evening you can hear loud music that comes from a neighboring night club;
  • the entrance to the hotel is not cleared, and therefore you will have to overcome snowdrifts;
  • lunch and dinner in a restaurant must be ordered and paid in advance, which somewhat restricts freedom of movement;
  • maids do not make beds during cleaning;
  • there is no microwave in the kitchen corner;
  • constant power outages;
  • in numbers there are no knives and a corkscrew;
  • Wireless Internet is slow
  • in the bathroom, the hood does not work well, which leads to the formation of mold and the smell of dampness;
  • if you did not come for a paid breakfast, lunch or dinner, you will not only not be returned the funds, but also will not give the food intended for you;
  • meager dinners;
  • numbers are locked with a regular mechanical lock, and not electronic;
  • regardless of the number of guests staying in the room, only one key is issued;
  • despite the fact that the birthday is promised a bonus, the staff does not perform this function;
  • housekeeping is carried out in the afternoon;
  • maids knock on the room, ignoring the Do Not Disturb sign;
  • in the bathroom there are few shelves for cosmetic accessories;
  • redecoration is required in the corridors;
  • low temperature in the hammam;
  • cold water in the pool;
  • breakfast starts at 08:00, which is late enough for active tourists.

Despite the external gloss of the Residence Mountain hotel, there are a huge number of minor flaws that staff are not even trying to eliminate. Nevertheless, if you do not pay attention to details, we can say that this institution will provide you with comfortable living conditions and a lot of opportunities for entertainment.
