Hotel Art Village Club: suburban complex in Moscow. Reviews and photos

It is good that today the residents of the metropolis can go away from the bustle of the city and plunge into the atmosphere of outdoor recreation and clean air. For this, hotel complexes are opening in the suburban area. One of them, Art Village Club, is located in the Moscow region.

About Organization

This place is intended for those who would like to escape from the bustle of the city and restore their strength. Art Village Club has everything you need to make vacationers feel comfortable.

There is a swimming pool, a bathhouse, a spa, a gym, a restaurant where you can try original dishes. The hotel rooms are decorated in a contemporary style and feature a trendy design. The organization is located at: Moscow region, Solnechnogorsk district, Golikovo village, house 1A on Ryabinovaya street. The hotel is ten kilometers from the stadium in Khimki.

Services

Guests at the Art Village Club, a rural complex, can enjoy free internet and private parking.

It offers services for celebrations: weddings, banquets and so on. The hotel complex includes the Dacha Hotel, where all participants of the celebration can stay. Designers designed the rooms in such a way that the newlyweds felt comfortable, and the honeymoon left them with wonderful and unforgettable impressions.

The characteristics of the organization Art Village Club in Moscow include the following:

  1. Fifteen guest rooms and 4 country houses.
  2. Four standard rooms.
  3. 4 studio rooms.
  4. Superior rooms.
  5. Two junior suites with windows to the garden.
  6. The bathhouse.
  7. Gym.
  8. Riding, cycling and tennis facilities.
  9. A restaurant.
  10. SPA-center.
  11. Beauty saloon.
  12. Banqueting hall.

Rooms

All rooms for visitors and country houses are original. Their design is comfortable. The creators of the Art Village Club hotel complex have tried to plan the rooms so that even the most demanding guests are satisfied with their surroundings.



There are several types of rooms in the hotel:

  1. Studio New York. These rooms are designed for two people. They are equipped with a kitchen, decorated in a modern western style.
  2. Apartment New York. These rooms consist of two tiers. They are designed for those who, during the period of outdoor recreation, would not want to forget about the atmosphere of urban premises.
  3. Junior Suite Paris. These are comfortable rooms equipped in modern style. Their decor reminds guests of the capital of France and helps create a romantic mood.
  4. Family Studio. Superior rooms. Suitable for those who come to rest with their soulmate or friends. The rooms include a spacious lounge and kitchen.
  5. Studio Stockholm and Apartment Stockholm. The rooms are designed for two persons, decorated in bright colors. Their design is minimalist.
  6. Standart 41, 43, 44. The apartments are made of natural materials (stone, wood, natural fibers). Scandinavian-style rooms set up their inhabitants for relaxation and tranquility.
  7. Standard with terrace. This is a room with a terrace, which is suitable for a secluded pastime or family vacation.

Country houses

For those who come to rest with friends or want to organize a wedding at the hotel, the following types of cottages are offered at Art Village Club:

  1. Villa Ametist. A country house, the atmosphere of which can rightly be called luxurious. This room includes five bedrooms and can accommodate from ten to twelve guests.
  2. Villa Vintage America and Villa Herbarium. These rooms have a modern, colorful and comfortable design. Such houses are suitable both for a friendly company, and for couples with children. The cottages are equipped with four bedrooms, an attic, three sanitary units and a kitchen-living room with a table, sofas and armchairs. Near the houses there is a barbecue. The rooms are decorated in a contemporary style with light shades.




Banqueting hall

This room is designed so that visitors can hold various celebrations here (weddings, birthdays, and so on). In the banquet hall of the Art Village Club tables are designed for fifty guests, there is also the opportunity to use the kitchen and technical equipment. The event participants are provided with a flat-screen TV, rooms with terraces and living rooms, additional equipment (microwave, oven). The hotel is quite a popular venue for wedding receptions.

All the best for honeymooners

One of the important things that worries the bride before the wedding is her appearance. But this problem should not bother the woman who decided to spend a solemn day in the Art Village Club suburban complex. The hotel provides all the conditions for the bride to look great, and the holiday went well. Specialists of the spa center and beauty studio will do everything to make the woman‚Äôs makeup, manicure and hairstyle look great, and she herself feel relaxed and confident. Stylists will help you choose outfits for the groom, bride and guests in accordance with their wishes.



The future wife in a suburban complex can spend a bachelorette party. For this, the Art Village Club has a saltwater pool. Also, participants in the celebration can use the services of a professional photographer. After such a bright and unforgettable bachelorette party, the bride's mood, of course, will be excellent.

Additional services

In the suburban complex there are many opportunities in order to relax and forget about the bustle of the metropolis. Lovers of active pastimes can visit the gym, group workouts, indoor and outdoor pools. Those who would like to pay attention to themselves are provided with the services of a beauty salon, a bathhouse, a spa center.

Visitors can do massage, makeup, manicure and hairstyle, hair treatments, skin cleansing. There is also a restaurant on the territory of the Art Village Club complex, where you can order European dishes and exotic dishes for every taste.

Reviews

Most hotel guests claim that they were satisfied with his work. About the suburban complex Art Village Club reviews prevail positive. Visitors talk about the good quality of staff, the convenience of the location of the institution (it is not too far from the city). The guests liked the equipment of the rooms and the decor in them. They are also pleased with the visit to the bath.

People like the quality of food prepared in the hotel restaurant. Also, the guests are impressed by the beautiful nature, the opportunity to celebrate in a large company. They are pleased with the variety of offers for recreation and sports.

However, the hotel has flaws and is reflected in some reviews. For example, there are visitors who were disappointed that there was not enough entertainment for the children. Some are not comfortable with the view from the windows. There are guests who say that for additional services at the hotel you have to pay a lot of money. When conducting a wedding, guests also need to remember the rules of the day at the hotel. For example, you can not turn on loud music in the hotel complex after 11 pm.
