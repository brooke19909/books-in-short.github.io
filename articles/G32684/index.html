Overview of the vacuum cleaner Karcher DS 5500

Karcher has been operating for more than half a century. Its foundation was in 1935. Very quickly, products won leading positions in the global market. The concern produces climate systems, household and industrial equipment. Being the largest trademark in the world, which always uses only innovative technologies, it always attracts increased attention to its products. The first such model was the Karcher DS 5500 vacuum cleaner . He opened a model range of units with an aquafilter. Currently, the device has, of course, outdated characteristics, but it copes with the main task perfectly.

Let's look at the features of the DS 5500, as well as its advantages and disadvantages.



Characteristic

Karcher DS 5500 - a vacuum cleaner in which air purification is carried out using an aquafilter. It is currently not possible to purchase a new device. The sale of such a model is carried out only in the secondary market at a price of up to $ 200.

What technical characteristics is this device equipped with? The first thing you should pay attention to is the consumption of electricity. During operation, the cost will be 1.5 kW. That is why you can‚Äôt call the Karcher DS 5500 vacuum cleaner economical. The instructions that are included in the kit contain information on technical equipment, operating rules and safety. In it, you can also see data on the dimensions. Case dimensions: 48x30x52 cm. Vacuum cleaner weight: 8.5 kg. Please note that when cleaning uneven surfaces, carrying the device in your hands is quite difficult. The engine that the model is equipped with consumes 1,400 watts. The kit includes a dust collector of 2 l and four nozzles. The vacuum cleaner was produced in two colors: black and yellow. The network cable is short - only 5.5 meters. The tube is telescopic. The device is equipped with a fine filter and aqua. During operation, reproduces noise as high as 70 dB. Karcher DS 5500 is used for wet and dry cleaning. There is a suction power regulator located on the body. The cable is rewound automatically.



Benefits

Although the DS 5500 is already significantly outdated, it still has significant advantages. First of all, this is a thorough air filtration. During cleaning, automatic humidification occurs, so the room feels fresh, like after rain. Also, Karcher DS 5500, thanks to the aquafilter, practically does not clog. After cleaning, you do not need to disassemble the device in order to clean it from dust inside. The only thing to do is rinse the filter itself. Given this parameter, now it is not necessary to worry that during operation the suction power will decrease.

Over the long time this model has existed, many have been able to verify the durability and reliability of the device. The manufacturer was able to extend the service life thanks to the aquafilter. Absorption of dust by a vacuum cleaner is one hundred percent. Also, the exhaust air is maximally purified, which cannot be said about other models that are designed for dry cleaning.



Aquafilter Features

As mentioned above, the Karcher DS 5500 model is equipped with an aquafilter. This technology was first used by this particular brand. What is its feature? Filtration is carried out by purifying polluted air with water.

The first filters were a geometric shape. In it, under high pressure, a water vortex formed. Now a multi-stage system is used. Surprisingly, using a vacuum cleaner equipped with an aquafilter, you can clean not only surfaces from dirt, wool, but also the air in the room, for example, from cigarette smoke.

The principle of operation of an aquafilter is as follows:

  • The first stage is the penetration of dirty air inside.
  • The second stage is integration with a water vortex.
  • The third stage is the sedimentation of dirt at the bottom of the filter.
  • The fourth stage - purified and humidified air moves further through the system.




Karcher DS 5500: reviews

Unfortunately, many buyers in such devices found flaws. It is worth noting that they are quite substantial. First of all, there are complaints about the bulky design and heavy weight. Also, these criteria can be a problem in finding the optimal storage location. The downside is the HEPA filter. Humid air can lead to the accumulation of microbes in the structure, therefore it is necessary to dry it after each cleaning. You will also need to constantly wash the dust collector, since liquid dirt adheres during operation, and when dried, it is washed off with great difficulty.
