Solnechnogorsk in Crimea

On the Black Sea coast of Crimea, along the route connecting Alushta and Sudak, there is a small town with the summer name Solnechnogorsk.

The city is located in the amphitheater, which is formed by the mountains of Demerdzhi and Karabi-yayla covered with dense forests.

The mountains are cut by gorges, which are formed by the mountain rivers Ksir-Pata and Ulu-Uzen running to the sea. Numerous tourists get to this picturesque place along the mountain serpentine.

Due to the location of the town, all hotels in Solnechnogorsk (Alushta) are a stone's throw from the pebbly-sandy beach, which stretches for 3 km. Modern entertainments (cinemas, discos, clubs) are located in Alushta (it is about 20 km), from Solnechnogorsk you can go on excursions in the Crimea, take a walk in the vicinity where the largest Crimean waterfall is located.
