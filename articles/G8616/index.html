Explay Dream. Smartphone Explay Dream - model review, customer reviews and experts

Among the domestic companies that produce phones, of course, there are authorities. One of these is Explay. She is known for her push-button phones, which can easily work offline for about a month. Explay is also famous for launching one of the very first smartphones on Android OS in our country. At the moment, the ranks of such devices from this brand are only replenished with new models.

At the end of 2013, a completely new flagship of the line of smartphones running on the Android OS was introduced, under the name Dream. What kind of apparatus is this? What are its characteristics? We will try to disassemble everything in order.

Equipment

The device is delivered in a small cardboard box. Having lifted a cover, you will see a smartphone. A film with brief characteristics of the device is glued on its screen. Below, under a cardboard stand, there are additional accessories in the form of a charger, clips for removing SIM cards, a USB cable, a standard wired headset, a user manual and a warranty. Amateurs and specialists note that all components are laid neatly and tightly, which guarantees their integrity.





Construction and appearance

Smartphone Explay Dream evokes the feeling of a copy of well-known manufacturers. The same typical spade form factor, rounded edges and the absence of hardware buttons on the front of the device. The frame around the screen is quite small (about 4 mm). Thanks to the rounded back, the thickness of the smartphone is 9.4 mm imperceptible. It would be possible for the manufacturer to slightly reduce the height of the device, but this is a trifle.

The back cover is non-removable, as is the battery. Above is the peephole of the main camera at 13 megapixels and a flash. Bottom built-in speaker. The inscriptions are of high quality.

The bottom end has a headphone jack and microphone. The upper edge has only a micro-USB slot, with which the device is charged and synchronized with a computer.

The left side has slots for SIM-cards, and the right one has volume and lock-off buttons.

With mechanical action, noticeably high build quality, as there are no backlashes and creaks. Everything is quite simple and durable.

Display

Explay Dream Blue has at its disposal a high-quality five-inch IPS-matrix with high resolution and capacitive sensor. The display boasts not only this, but also natural color reproduction with high contrast. Even in sunlight, you can easily distinguish a picture on it. This is very good for a budget line smartphone.





At the top of the screen is a protective glass called Corning Gorilla Glass 2, which is resistant to mechanical damage and scratches. But there is no oleophobic coating here. For this reason, the screen quickly becomes dirty.

Performance

So we got to the "heart" of Explay Dream. A performance review has always been the main one, because not only for their beauty they like smartphones running OS Android.

So, the familiar quad-core processor called MTK 6589T is already installed on Explay Dream. Additionally, it was slightly ‚Äúdispersed‚Äù from a frequency of 1.2 to 1.5 GHz. Also in this smartphone uses the graphics accelerator model PowerVR SGX 544MP. It is not enough for heavy graphics, but it pulls ordinary games without interruptions and jerking pictures. 2 GB of RAM is enough for a quick response of the device. It is worth noting that when idle, it has 1.4 GB of free space.





As a result of testing with various utilities, no problems were found, and the gadget did not reach the ‚ÄúGoogle‚Äù brainchild of Nexus 4 in performance. During operation, the smartphone heated up to 40 degrees Celsius, which is quite normal.

Offline work

As for the Explay Dream battery, the feedback from users and specialists is not very good. For such an energy-intensive device, it is not enough. When testing with the AnTuTu utility, only 437 points were issued. This means that with inactive use, your smartphone can hardly survive until the evening charge. So, when watching Explay Dream video, it will work only 3 hours, surfing is performed for 4 hours, and listening to music with the screen off will be a 20-hour pleasure.





conclusions

As a result of the review, we can make a small conclusion about the Explay Dream device. Reviews from experts and users are almost the same. The smartphone has a very high-quality screen, a sensitive sensor, good cameras and high performance for its segment. Only one thing is upset: due to the rather weak battery, which is designed for less consumption, the battery life is significantly reduced. The small weight of the device, high-quality assembly, and much more, along with the positive features already enumerated, more than cover the disadvantages of the lack of oleophobic coating and the ability to expand memory.
