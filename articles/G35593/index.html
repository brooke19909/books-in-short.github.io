Hotels, Staraya Ladoga: addresses and reviews

Staraya Ladoga is an urban settlement located on the banks of the river. Volkhov, once considered the first capital of Russia. It has a thousand-year history and constantly attracts the attention of researchers. Many tourists come here from different parts of the world.

In Staraya Ladoga there are a lot of attractions and historical monuments, the rarest works of architecture and fortification, there is an archaeological museum-reserve. Its ‚Äúcalling card‚Äù is the Old Ladoga Fortress with its unique ancient temples.





Place to sleep

People coming here often wonder where to stay. Let's see what hotels Staraya Ladoga offers tourists. There are several of them.

Guest House "Staraya Ladoga"

Located in the heart of the village on Sovetskaya street, house 6. It offers guests four rooms of various price categories and a shared kitchen. Each room is equipped with a TV, there is a separate bathroom with a bath or shower. You can use the service "room service" and order meals without leaving the room. Internet access is available throughout the hotel. "Staraya Ladoga" works around the clock.

Among the advantages of the hotel, guests distinguish its convenient location, good wireless Internet. Judging by the reviews of tourists, the hotel rooms are clean and comfortable, junior suites have their own kitchen with beautiful furniture in the old Russian style.

There are people who have developed a negative opinion about the guest house "Staraya Ladoga". Many people think that the cost of living is very high (rooms are offered from 2000 rubles), the rooms are cold (but, upon request, the hotel owners provide a heater). In the shared kitchen there is only a microwave and tables, there is no gas, it is impossible to cook on your own.





Hotel "Rook" (Staraya Ladoga)

Provides guests with five rooms worth from 2 thousand rubles. All rooms have a shower, furniture, TV. In addition, the mini-hotel has a relaxation room, a cafe, a swimming pool and a sauna for up to ten people, there is a parking lot on site. The hotel administration is ready to receive guests at any time of the day.

"Rook" is located at 3 Sovetskaya Street (center, near the Assumption Monastery).

Guests using the services of the hotel respond positively about it. Allocate a beautiful interior, wooden furniture, spacious rooms. They praise the cleaning of the rooms and the work of the staff. Judging by the reviews, the hotel staff can provide dishes, a kettle, cutlery upon request, and, if necessary, put a cot in the room.

The hotel is conveniently located for pilgrims.





"Staroladozhskaya cottage"

In addition to the above hotels, to the list, which includes local hotels, Staraya Ladoga offers to add the recreation center "Staroladozhskaya Dacha", located just two kilometers from the village (village of Seltso-Gorka, Chernavino, 1/2). Here you can rent a comfortable cottage with all amenities, where a wonderful view of the garden or river opens from the windows. Superior houses have a sauna or a spa bath. Extras include internet access, cable TV and private parking. Guests can rent bicycles and ski equipment, play tennis or go fishing.

Reviews about this recreation center are extremely positive.





Note that for those who are looking for a place to sleep or a hotel, Staraya Ladoga offers apartments and houses for rent. Usually at the entrance to the village there are people who provide their housing for rent, and you can also use special sites with message boards to search for apartments.

For everyone who wants to relax in such a picturesque and historical place as Staraya Ladoga, hotels and recreation centers will be happy to open their doors.
