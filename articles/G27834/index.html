Side is one of the most famous Turkish resorts. Ramada Resort Side 5 * is a worthy place for those who like to relax in comfort.

Hotel Summary

Ramada Resort Side 5 * hotel is located on the outskirts of the resort town of Side, about half an hour from its center. Nevertheless, all the main tourist sites and attractions are located here. The journey from the airport to the hotel will take about an hour.

Hotel Room Overview

The Ramada Resort Side 5 * hotel warmly opens the doors of 291 rooms to accommodate its guests. The apartments of this hotel can be divided into several subgroups:

  • Family room (area - 50 sq. M) - has two bedrooms, combined with an internal door. It is assumed that the room with a double bed will accommodate parents, and the second (with small beds) will accommodate children. It is also possible to arrange extra beds. The hotel has 43 rooms of this category.
  • Superior (area - 30 sq. M) - 60 spacious one-room rooms equipped with a double bed or small single rooms. At the request of guests can be placed an extra bed in the form of a folding bed or a baby bed.
  • Standard room (area - 30 sq. M) is the most numerous category of apartments: the windows of 130 rooms overlook the garden, and the remaining 58 overlook the pool. Like the previous type of rooms, guests can be accommodated in one large bed or in two single beds. The hotel has two rooms of the category "standard", which are equipped specifically for a comfortable and safe stay for people with disabilities.




In the rooms

A distinctive feature of the rooms of the Ramada Resort Side 5 * hotel (Turkey, Side) is a modern bright, but unobtrusive design that pleases the eye and creates a good mood.   • air conditioning;

  • the bathroom is equipped with a built-in shower, a powerful hairdryer, as well as a cosmetic table and a large mirror.

Claims to the hotel

The Ramada Resort Side 5 * hotel has some shortcomings in its work, namely:

  • tourists often come across extortion tips from hotel staff;
  • hotel rooms require redecoration (and in some cases - overhaul);
  • poor quality of cleaning;
  • there are no natural juices on the menu - tourists are offered powder drinks with a large number of dyes;
  • a big drawback is the lack of Ayran traditional for Turkish cuisine (with the exception of a bagged one);

  • the hotel does not fully justify five-star accreditation;
  • lack of disposable cosmetics in the bathroom. All detergents are poured directly into the dispensers.

Useful information and hotel policies

The Ramada Resort Side (5 stars) has developed certain rules that govern the stay of tourists. The following main points can be distinguished, which should be familiarized with before going on vacation:

  • Check-in at 14:00, but subject to availability, you may be registered earlier. If all rooms are occupied, guests are sent to eat in the hotel restaurant.
  • Keys and bracelets must be handed over to the reception on the day of departure no later than noon.
  • Children under 12 years stay free in the room if an extra bed is not required.
  • A baby cot is available free of charge for children up to 2 years old.
  • For the resettlement of children in additional places will be charged half the cost of living in an adult room. The price of an extra bed for an adult is 70%.
  • Each room, regardless of size or category, has a maximum of one extra bed.
  • Pets are not allowed in the hotel.
  • The hotel has the opportunity to use bank transfer.
  • When booking a tour, a certain amount of funds is blocked on your card account until arrival at the hotel.




General impression of the hotel

The hotel leaves an ambiguous impression on its guests. By and large, tourists are accustomed to large areas of decorated areas with green grass and fountains in hotels in Turkey. In this case, the space is quite small.

Turning to the living conditions, we can say that the hotel does not quite live up to expectations. The originality of the interior of the Ramada Resort Side 5 * deserves special attention. Photos of tourists captured bright colors and innovative design solutions. Nevertheless, the impression is spoiled due to old furniture, as well as the general deterioration of the decoration of the rooms. True, reconstruction is planned at the hotel, which gives hope for an improvement in the situation.

Perhaps the only thing that does not cause special complaints among the guests is food. The assortment and quality of cooking almost completely justifies the five-star status of the hotel.
