Unprecedented charge of vivacity from rest - Blue lakes Lukhovitsy

How often you hear during conversations with friends or relatives, what baggage of unforgettable impressions they received from the next trip abroad. In this case, the exotic beauties of tropical landscapes of Thailand, the unobtrusiveness of the tourist sector of Egypt, and the hospitality of Turkish hotels, which has already become a parable in languages, are necessarily listed.





Meanwhile, every Moscow family will be able to have a wonderful rest, having arrived on the Blue Lakes (Lukhovitsy). Getting to this place in the suburbs will be very simple and easy, especially for those who own a car. Take a weekend trip along the Ryazan highway in the direction of Voskresensk to discover the Blue Lakes (Lukhovitsy) for yourself and your family, which will undoubtedly help you get a boost of energy and completely forget about the stress accumulated during the week from working inside gray concrete boxes.

When autumn is in the yard, one of the brightest and most expressive seasons of the Russian nature, the Blue Lakes will fully reveal its charms to you one hundred percent. Among these are coniferous forests with islands of birch groves that are still not too affected by civilization, the clear, with an always blue tint water from these reservoirs, neat sandy beaches, and, of course, the fresh and satisfying air of this geographical area in the Moscow Region.





In one of the once popular songs there were such lines: "Flowers, berries, mushrooms are found in the Moscow Region." All these gifts of Russian nature will be given to you by the Blue Lakes (Lukhovitsy) - a complex of natural reservoirs consisting of seven lakes located in the vicinity of the ancient Russian city of Kolomna.

For fans of outdoor activities, in addition to picking mushrooms and berries, Blue Lakes will provide an excellent chance to go fishing on their picturesque shores, to train stagnant muscles during boat trips along the water surface of one of the lakes. In the summer months, visitors to the lakes in Lukhovitsy have a great opportunity to ride water bikes or ride an inflatable banana and rush along the beach line with a breeze.





Here everyone can choose something to their liking. And if you take a tent with you to the Blue Lakes in Lukhovitsy, you will get the opportunity to sit by the fire on a warm summer evening. Eat potatoes baked on a bonfire or taste fragrant fish soup from freshly caught lake fish.

And be sure that after this every updated cell of your body will transform your outlook beyond recognition and will make you take a fresh look at many of the problems that have accumulated during everyday work.

Rest on the native nature in the near Moscow region, which will give you the Blue Lakes (Lukhovitsy), is also a very inexpensive way to spend a weekend and unwind. Such a vacation will save a considerable part of its funds for the family budget. And most importantly, no problems, worries and stresses from buying a tour, getting visas to distant overseas countries, passport control at the border and tiring sitting in front of the airliner window. All around are native nature, native speech and people giving serenity and complete relaxation for the whole weekend spent in this wonderful corner of the Moscow Region.
