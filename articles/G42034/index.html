Big Utrish: hotels and leisure

If you have chosen the Black Sea coast for your vacation, pay attention to Big Utrish. This is not just a resort place, but a unique state reserve, which is located 15 kilometers from the famous Black Sea city of Anapa.





Description of the Great Utrish

Some tourists do not like Anapa for its winds and the bloom of the sea in the hot season. But in Big Utrish there are no such problems. It has an exceptionally clear sea, with no signs of algae, and a mild, calm climate.

Anapa Big Utrish is a dendrological reserve. This means that in the vicinity of the village of the same name there are many relict junipers that release a huge amount of volatile products into the air, which beneficially affect the human respiratory system.

Due to the state reserve, it is forbidden to actively develop this area. There are few hotels in Big Utrish. But many tourists stop on the beach right in the tents.

The beaches in Big Utrish are pebble, clean. On the shore there is all the infrastructure: these are sun loungers, sun umbrellas, and changing cabins.

Descent into the water is steep enough, which is somewhat inconvenient for those who have small children. But, on the other hand, in any case, children should be closely monitored on the seashore, and given the lack of a gentle entry into the water, vacationers of Big Utrish will have an additional incentive to control the younger generation.

If residents of big cities want to take a break from the hustle and bustle of unspoiled nature, you can walk two or three kilometers to Maly Utrish. There are very special wild beaches, without crowds of vacationers and merchants. And to completely merge with the sun and the sea, you can sunbathe on the nudist beach, which is located there.





Hotels of Greater Utrish

As mentioned earlier, there are few places to stay. But among the proposals from local residents, one can choose quite worthy accommodation options in different price categories. Prices per day vary from 1,000 to 10,000 rubles.

Addresses and a brief description of several hotels in Big Utrish:

  • Guest House "On Mozhzhevelova". Address: Bolshoi Utrish, Mozhzhevelovaya street, 1/3. Prices here start at 1,000 rubles and end at around 6,000 rubles per day. All amenities, of course, are.
  • Guest house "Valentina". Address: Bolshoy Utrish, Mozhzhevelovaya street, 2/3. The cost of rest at this hotel starts at one and a half thousand and ends at around five thousand rubles. The cheaper rooms are located on the common floor of the house, not in the room.
  • "Mountain air". There is a mini-hotel of Big Utrish with such a poetic name on Lesnaya Street, house 5. The price varies from 1800 rubles to 9800 rubles per day.

Infrastructure





As in any resort town, the entire infrastructure of Greater Utrish is well developed. Vacationers will find here several cafes and dining rooms, shops and a market. But there are no ATMs and a post office, as well as night discos. But for such entertainment you can always go to neighboring Anapa.

Tourists who choose the Great Utrish nature reserve as their vacation destination prefer to spend time fishing. This type of recreation is especially common here because of the large number of fish that live only in clear water.

This area has its own yacht club called Fort Utrish. Here you can rent all the equipment and go spearfishing, or learn to dive, windsurf, rent a boat or yacht.

If you are tired of noisy sea resorts and want to immerse yourself in the healing atmosphere of ancient relict forests and clear sea water - welcome to Big Utrish.
