Komsomolsky Pond, Stavropol: the formation of a reservoir within the city

Today the Komsomolsky pond in Stavropol is a favorite vacation spot for residents of the regional capital. There are conditions for a safe swimming, you can sunbathe and just relax, relaxing in the forest.

The history of the Komsomol pond in Stavropol: how did the lake appear?

According to archival documents, the reservoir was formed in the valley of the Tashly River in the XVIII-XIX centuries. In those days, the lack of water was more than palpable. So much so that because of her they fought and even killed. The existing Tashla River could no longer make up for the amount necessary for residents for various purposes, including irrigation of gardens. Therefore, it was decided to build an artificial dam. With its help, it was possible to create a large body of water, which, in turn, will help get rid of the problem of water supply for the whole area.

The uppermost reservoir was precisely the Bishop's, and now Komsomolsky pond in Stavropol. Just below him another merchant Sipyagin was dug. Later, the place became a favorite recreation area for citizens - there were baths, a boat station and diving towers.





Komsomol pond today

The city lake is surrounded on three sides by Tamanskaya Lesnaya Dacha, the fourth leads onto Kavaleriyskaya Street. An asphalt path with markings for cyclists and pedestrians goes around the pond. The length of the pond is 300 m, the width of the western part is not more than 50 m. On the other hand, a concrete dam has been created. Its width is 130 m.

Komsomolskiy pond in Stavropol is well suited for recreation at any time of the year. In addition, various amphibians live here, such as the lake frog, tree frog and toad. Caddis flies and fish live in the water. For example, here you can catch minnows, crucians and even common carp.





Recreation and entertainment on the lake

In the cold season, the lake area offers pleasant walks in the fresh air. Once surrounded by the forest, you can spend hours walking along the asphalt path, admiring the beautiful views. Lovers of outdoor activities prefer to jog and ride bicycles, and young people even arrange beach volleyball. In addition, the Veranda restaurant is open on its territory - a luxurious place for a perfect holiday.

Many are interested in the question of whether it is possible to swim at the Komsomolsky Pond in Stavropol. Some time ago, indeed, prohibition signs prevented this. But then, when the lake was lowered, cleaned and filled with water, they allowed to swim again. Now there are sun loungers installed, you can stay in the sun or in the shade of trees. In summer, ice cream, drinks and snacks are available. For children often set attractions. In general, the pond now looks much better, safer and more developed than 10-15 years ago.

They do not stop improving the pond. It is expected that the lake will be filled with spring water, due to which it will be visually (and really) much cleaner. Water will turn blue, and the principle of its entry into the pond will also change. To achieve the desired goal, biologists were involved without harming nature.

A comprehensive development of beach infrastructure and the installation of drinking fountains are also planned. Who knows, maybe the positive changes in the beloved city reservoir will not stop there?





How to get to the Komsomolsky pond in Stavropol?

You can approach the city lake by going down Kavaleriyskaya Street. It has a large parking lot, which is impossible to drive past. Public transport on the street Cavalry does not go, therefore, if you do not have your own car, you need to either take a taxi or walk part of the way. For example, through the territory of the City Clinical Hospital No. 2, located on the street. Balakireva. There is also a route through Taman Forest from ul. Semashko (Lenin) and Kulakov. But without knowing the terrain, walking alone is not recommended.
