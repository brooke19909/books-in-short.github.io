Audiophile headphones: rating, review of the best models

Audio lovers are extremely kind to everything that relates to sound, including gadgets. Therefore, they approach the choice of audiophile headphones with all care and scrupulousness. The good old principle applies in this segment: the more expensive, the better.

But the scrupulous choice and the rather considerable cost of such gadgets are justified by the exceptional sound. And the latter is especially important for audio fans who are willing to pay almost any money for it.

We will consider the best audiophile headphones that can be found on the shelves of domestic stores. We will analyze the remarkable characteristics of gadgets, as well as the available pros and cons. For a more visual picture, the devices will be presented in the form of a rating, where the opinions of thematic magazines and respected reviewers are taken as a basis.

Here we have soft and voluminous bass, an exceptional balance of medium and high frequencies with a wide stage, as well as an excellent software component. Sony uses its own codecs, allowing you to listen to tracks without loss of quality. You can buy a device for about 28 thousand rubles.

Westone w40

Westone's audiophile in -ear headphones from the W40 series performed particularly well when playing classical music as well as rock. The sound of high and medium frequencies worked out extremely sensibly. The local bass can be called calm. They do not bulge and do not put pressure on the ears, as in dynamic models.

Also pleased with the ergonomic part. With Westone's audiophile headphones, you can walk hours and hours without removing or even noticing them. Nothing will stop you from enjoying your favorite songs. Build quality, durability and other operational characteristics are fully consistent with the high cost of the gadget. And this is almost 36 thousand rubles.

Advantages of the model:

  • perfect sound for this form factor;
  • high ergonomic performance;
  • original and nice looking design;
  • durability of a design;
  • thoughtful and convenient control panel;
  • rich scope of supply (interchangeable ear pads, cables, covers, etc.).

No flaws were identified.

Bose QuietComfort 35 II

A model from a famous American brand is ideal for any musical direction. In addition, the gadget can also perform a wide range of tasks: music, games, TV, conversations, etc. Separately, it is worth noting the excellent noise reduction, which allows you to completely immerse yourself in your world. The device can not be called popular, because it costs about 27 thousand rubles.

In terms of sound quality, the model is quite a bit behind the leader of our rating, but it wins in functionality. The gadget has a pair of microphones, full support for Apple devices, voice dialing, a mechanical volume control, NFC technology and the ability to connect a cable. This last point is especially important for gamers who are picky about response times.

Advantages of the model:

  • excellent sound in all musical genres;
  • exceptional noise reduction;
  • comfortable to wear design;
  • wide functionality;
  • autonomy up to 20 hours;
  • convenient management.

Disadvantages:

  • the model comes to Russia with a very high price tag;
  • a lot of fakes (especially on "Aliexpress").

Plantronics BackBeat PRO 2

Many audiophiles consider this model to be the best in their price category. The gadget can be called universal due to the presence of a pair of microphones. So the headphones are great not only for listening to music, but also for gaming.

The device has well-set bass, excellent noise reduction, a decent set of functions and ease of use. Headphones have moderate bass with a barely noticeable dip in the middle. There are no complaints about other frequencies. The gadget is omnivorous and practically not picky about the style of compositions.
