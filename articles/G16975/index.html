The Royal Apollonia Beach 5 * (Cyprus / Limassol) - photo and reviews of tourists

Cyprus is a place where many come to spend an unforgettable vacation. And before you go on a trip, you need to choose a place in which you need to stop for a while. The Royal Apollonia Beach 5 hotel is an excellent option . Cyprus is famous for this place, and therefore every year the hotel receives a lot of guests. Moreover, it is located in the city of Limassol. And this, as you know, is the second largest city on the whole island, which is an economic, financial, cultural and, of course, a tourist center.





Paradise vacation

Cyprus is famous for its clean beaches, excellent service hotels, Greek cuisine and, of course, local attractions. This is a beautiful Mediterranean resort where you can enjoy a warm pleasant climate, beautiful scenery and sunbathe. In addition, Cyprus pleases with very affordable prices, and this cannot but play a big role in choosing a vacation spot. Also on the island there is a wide selection of hotels of various categories - from five-star apartments to budget options. But I must say that even the economical options are very well decorated and offer good service. The Royal Apollonia Beach 5 hotel in question has all the benefits of a five star hotel and should be considered in more detail.

general characteristics

Royal Apollonia Beach 5 is located in a city called Limassol, very close to the beach. Five kilometers separates it from the archaeological site in the ancient city of Amathus. If we talk about what Royal Apollonia Beach 5 (Limassol) is famous for, then the first thing to note is its excellent cuisine. It must also be said that all rooms are made in an exclusive style, decorated in the best Greek traditions. And from their windows you can enjoy a magnificent view of the Mediterranean Sea or the Troodos Mountains, depending on the chosen apartments. In addition, all rooms are air-conditioned so that guests feel comfortable in hot weather. Although, I must say, Cyprus has a very pleasant climate, which, in fact, attracts tourists, so guests of the island will not have to die from the heat. Royal Apollonia Beach Hotel 5 is next to a clean, spacious beach with free umbrellas and sun loungers, so sea lovers will definitely enjoy it.

Service

It is worth noting that the service at Limassol Royal Apollonia Beach 5 is really top notch. The hotel offers a wide variety of services designed to help the visitor feel at home. Everyone knows how sometimes it is difficult to understand what and where is located in a foreign country. Guests are offered a currency exchange, the ability to rent a car (it is not always convenient to travel by public transport), conducting various excursions, booking plane tickets, a hairdresser, laundry, and also calling a doctor. And, of course, polite and correct staff. At Royal Apollonia Beach 5, each guest is treated with care and respect, which undoubtedly adds prestige to this place.





Entertainment and leisure

In order for the rest to be a success, you need to take your time with something interesting and fascinating. Royal Apollonia Beach 5 Hotel (Limassol) has plenty of activities to help you have fun. After all, constantly going somewhere and going on excursions at some point will get tired, and you will want something else. It offers an outdoor pool, a shaded terrace, as well as one on which you can relax in the sun. The picturesque garden will appeal to lovers of landscapes and peaceful relaxation. Also, lovers of outdoor activities and sports will not have to be bored here: tennis court, table tennis, billiards - tourists can do anything. By the way, you can rent not only a car, but also a bicycle. There is also equipment designed for practicing various water sports, a fitness center, a sauna, and a wellness center. SPA-services are also offered to guests, which especially attracts girls staying at Royal Apollonia Beach 5 (Limassol). Reviews about the services of the beauty salon leave positive, especially those related to the massage offered at the hotel. Recording, of course, goes forward several days, but a highly qualified professional massage therapist serves guests.





Additional services

Hotel Royal Apollonia Beach 5 (Limassol) offers a host of services, in addition to the above. Today it is very difficult to do without the Internet, and therefore in public places wireless high-speed network access is provided. The rooms also, however, at an additional cost. Free parking is available on site, reservation is not required in advance. Also, if necessary, you can organize the delivery of drinks or food to your room - it can be either a hot lunch or a packed lunch, which, by the way, can be taken with you if a person goes, for example, on an excursion or on a day trip to the beach . The hotel has a 24-hour reception desk. By the way, check-out and arrivals are carried out quickly and efficiently, since the hotel administration understands that the person is tired of the road, he needs to relax. Other guests are offered a left-luggage office, concierge services, a living room with a TV. In the respectable Royal Apollonia Beach 5 everything is so thought out that if a person needs it, he can even use the services of shoe shine, ironing and dry cleaning. Also, the attention of tourists is invited to a banquet and conference room, a business center, a place where you can print documents or make photocopies. There is also a gift shop on site. What can I say, there is everything for a comfortable stay and more!

Vacation with children

Many tourists come to this place with their children. The hotel allows children of absolutely any age! Moreover, if the child is less than two years old, then he can live for free, especially for him a free cot is provided. If his age is up to 12 years, then half of the cost of living for one adult is charged for his placement. In the event that he is over 12, then he will have to pay 75%. Upon request, guests are provided with an extra bed, and in order for the service to be performed, it is necessary to voice your request before check-in, in advance.

But that is not all. If a person needs a safe, then he will be provided with him. Need to buy something from the products? No problem, there are various shops on site. Can't the guests smell tobacco? Food and Kitchen

In the beginning, it was said that Cyprus is famous for its amazing national cuisine, delicacies and a variety of dishes. It should be noted that food is really a very important aspect for a full comfortable rest. At The Royal Apollonia Beach 5, there are no food problems for anyone. The restaurant with a professional chef provides its guests with a varied menu and fresh, delicious food. Also, hotel guests are provided with breakfast in their room, which is far from every hotel. Guests can order any dish they like that will be specially prepared for them, or take food from the buffet. If you wish, you can visit the snack bar. For guests with a special food attitude, a diet menu is provided.

Accommodation

There are 204 rooms to choose from, including Deluxe, Honeymoon and Junior, as well as Executive. It should be clarified what these names mean. So, the first category of rooms is a spacious luxury apartment. The second - rooms specially for newlyweds. And the third category includes executive class apartments. Each room is special. But all apartments are equipped with air conditioning, telephone, minibar, TV and radio, as well as such nice extras as a hairdryer, coffee and tea making facilities, slippers and a bathrobe. Of course, guests are offered a private bathroom with all necessary facilities. Even upon arrival, guests will enjoy a pleasant surprise in the form of water and fruits, as well as sweets and canapes during their stay. And each room has a balcony with stunning views of the picturesque surroundings.
