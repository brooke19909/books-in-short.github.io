The best hotels in Voronezh in the center

Voronezh is a large Russian city located on the banks of the river of the same name. This locality is considered the cradle of the navy, as well as the birthplace of the airborne troops. In addition, this city is rich in historical and cultural attractions. If you plan to get to know them, stay at one of the hotels in the center of Voronezh.

Hotel "Ramada Plaza"

The Ramada Plaza Voronezh City Center Hotel is located in the city center at 36A Ordzhonikidze Street. In this institution, guests can expect to receive the following services:

  • conference rooms;
  • restaurant "Ramada";
  • safe at the reception;
  • non-smoking rooms;
  • elevator;
  • gym;
  • laundry;
  • visa support;
  • underground parking;
  • taxi call;
  • organization of excursions;
  • organization of business and special occasions.

The hotel has 210 rooms for guests. Namely:

  • standard (30 sq. m) - from 4300 rubles per day;
  • Superior room (35 sq. m) - from 6000 rubles per day;
  • Junior Suite (40 sq. m) - from 7500 rubles per day;
  • luxury (41 sq. m) - from 8000 rubles per day;
  • apartments (60 sq. m) - from 1500 rubles per day.

Reviews about hotel "Ramada Plaza"

Among the hotels in Voronezh in the city center, this institution is especially popular. Here are some positive reviews you can hear about him:

  • polite, friendly and very competent staff;

  • Ample underground parking
