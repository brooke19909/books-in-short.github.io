Nikon Coolpix P520 - model review, customer reviews and experts

The Nikon COOLPIX P520 digital camera is a compact super zoom camera with rich functionality. This device has an 18-megapixel matrix created using BSI CMOS technology (with backlight). The lens has a pretty decent zoom ratio - 42x. The camera under consideration is equipped with a 3.2-inch monitor with a diagonal of two planes and a resolution of 910 thousand pixels. The device is capable of recording video in Full HD quality with stereo sound, in addition, there is a high-speed photo and video shooting mode.





Specifications

Matrix - 18 megapixels (4896x3672, 1 / 2.3 inches). The lens is 41.7x optical zoom (24-1000 equiv. Mm), F / 3.0-5.9. Information is stored on SD, SDHC or SDXC memory cards; In addition, there is a built-in memory of 15 MB. Photo file format - JPEG; video files - MOV (1920x1080p) with stereo sound; as well as 640x480, 1280x720 or 1920x1080p. The Nikon COOLPIX P520 has a combined AV output and USB, as well as mini-HDMI. Overall dimensions of the device are 126x84x102 mm.

Super zoom

Cameras of this type are commonly called superzoom. Let's understand what is meant by this term. These are devices that are equipped with a small matrix and have a stationary lens, characterized by a huge zoom ratio. Another popular name for this class is bridge cameras, which implies an intermediate position of such devices between system and compact cameras. Almost all compact digital cameras with a small matrix size are characterized by an extremely high zoom ratio. For devices with a large matrix, a similar approximation is impossible to realize, since the optics would be too big, heavy and expensive.

In fact, only a super-powerful approximation is already enough to justify the existence of this class and ensure its popularity. However, the Nikon COOLPIX P520 camera (photo above) stands out from the crowd by the fact that its functionality is very rich, and in appearance and control system it is close to SLR cameras. The zoom ratio of this camera reaches 42x, but the range starts from a fairly wide angle (24 equiv. Mm), which greatly expands the possibilities of taking pictures. And BSI technology (backlighting) allows you to read the picture at high speed, and this ensures the Full HD video format and high-speed shooting mode.



Design

For some reason, in this case, the manufacturer departed from the usual traditional standards - strict black. As a result, this model has three color options. The Nikon COOLPIX P520 Red camera, as the name implies, is made in red. Although in fact it is closer to the color of rotten cherries. This model looks quite stylish. The next camera - Nikon COOLPIX P520 Black 1 - is made in matte black, which is considered traditional for photography. The next option is also black, but with a glossy sheen, making the device glamorous and more effective. This model is called Nikon COOLPIX P520 Black 2.

Control system

The camera has a very successful execution of the case, it fits comfortably in the hand. The rubber overlay on the handle with a notch is tactile. However, if you do not take care of it, it will clog up with dust very quickly, and then it will not look so elegant. Between the lens and the grip there is an autofocus backlight and an automatic timer timer. She has an orange glow. Around the shutter release button is a lever that controls the zoom. This is where an interesting ergonomic feature of this model lies: the pseudo-mirror design of the Nikon COOLPIX P520 implies that you need to hold it, like mirrored devices, with the palm of your left hand under the lens, and place your thumb on the left, on the lens barrel.

Just in this place, the designers placed the zoom control lever. This control partially imitates the zoom ring on the lens. This element allows you to not only zoom in / out, but can also be used for manual focus. On the left side of the case there is a button with the image of lightning, it is designed to raise the built-in flash. In this model, it is not possible to install an external flash. In the place where this connector is usually located in cameras, the Nikon COOLPIX P520 has a stereo microphone and an antenna for the GPS module. Next to the zoom lever there is a programming button - "Fn". It allows you to set one of the following parameters: image size, image quality, PictureControl color schemes, white balance, shutter mode, metering type, ISO sensitivity, stabilizer mode and autofocus zone mode.



Inconvenience of management

One of the miscalculations of the developers of this camera is the location of both drives on the rear panel. As a result, the thumb of the right hand always has to rush between them and all the buttons. It is much more convenient to work when one disc is in front, on the handle. The next inconvenience is the inability to switch the image output between the electronic viewfinder and the monitor by pressing a button. In addition, there is no sensor for automatic switching in case the viewfinder is close to the face. The picture is displayed on the viewfinder when the monitor is turned by the screen to the body. This implementation is somewhat inconvenient when it is necessary to view the photographs taken during the shooting process. Also, the electronic viewfinder suffers from poor image quality. Nikon COOLPIX P520 Camera: User Reviews

Consider the positive aspects of this model. Most amateur photographers choose this unit due to the huge zoom ratio and the presence of a wide angle in this mode. They note effective optical image stabilization, a unique control for different types of zooming or manual focusing, a complete set of PASM modes. Many amateurs call the advantages of this camera to be interesting parameters for handheld night shooting ("Portrait" and "Landscape"), combining several frames and improving quality at a high ISO level.





In addition, high-quality Full HD video with stereo sound and high-speed video recording modes, the ability to edit clips (trimming and saving frames as a separate file) are very popular. The built-in GPS navigation system, which records the coordinates of the photo location and route (even when the device is turned off), is also interesting. What do professional photographers say about the Nikon COOLPIX P520? The reviews say that it has a high level of exposure bracketing, a live histogram on the monitor during shooting, high-speed high-speed shooting, D-Lighting, which allows you to highlight dark areas, the ‚ÄúBacklight‚Äù function, panoramic shooting with a viewing angle of 180 and 360 degrees in the same wiring with auto-gluing, as well as adjusting the intensity of noise reduction.

disadvantages

The Nikon COOLPIX P520 camera (reviews by amateur photographers confirm this) has a low aperture ratio, a narrow aperture adjustment range, the presence of pronounced noise reduction artifacts even at a low ISO level, and low speed in many modes. Users note unstable operation and intermittent freezing of the device, fast discharge of the battery. The camera does not support RAW format, there is no connector for an external flash, an HD / HS switch (high speed or high resolution video), an orientation sensor (you have to manually rotate the pictures when viewing them). In addition, switching data input from the monitor to the electronic viewfinder is performed by turning the monitor to the device body; when you enable the viewing function, the last frame made is always displayed; slow display (up to two seconds) of detailed information about the photo.
