Egypt, Sharm El Sheikh, Otium Amphora Hotel: photos and reviews of tourists

A little over 40 years ago, a small fishing village in the Shah's Bay in Egypt was unknown to anyone until they discovered that it was in a picturesque, ecologically clean place with a wonderful climate. So there was a resort place Sharm el-Sheikh, today popular in the whole tourist world.

Not only local attractions, but also hotels located in the most picturesque areas are fighting for the championship of the best resort in Egypt . One of them, Otium Amphora Hotel 5 (Sharm El Sheikh), is located on the very shore of the Red Sea.

Resort

In fact, Sharm El Sheikh is a huge tourist area, divided into several resort areas, each of which has its own ‚Äúset‚Äù of attractions to attract customers.

  • The Bay of Elders has not only hotels located in 3 rows along the sea, but also numerous restaurants, fitness clubs, shopping malls and diving centers. Naama Bay (as the local population calls it) is the oldest district in this area. It is here that Otium is located (Amphora Hotel, Sharm El Sheikh).   • Ras Umm al-Cid is popular with divers, as there are real coral gardens in the bay of the same name.




The remaining areas of the resort are younger, but they are quickly built up with new hotels and entertainment complexes.

Sharm El Sheikh Ecosystem

Due to its location, the resort has a wonderful climate, which allows it to meet guests year-round. In summer, the air warms up to + 40 degrees, and water - up to + 30. At the same time, the heat is transferred quite easily, due to the lack of humidity. Rains in these parts are extremely rare. Everything is done here for the well-being of holidaymakers, equipping the entire Sharm el-Sheikh with the best household appliances. The Otium Amphora Hotel, for example, has air conditioning in every room.

In winter, there is no such heat, and the sea also favors swimming at + 20 degrees (water temperature). The only inconvenience to those who usually feel cold can be delivered in winter nights when the air temperature drops to + 15 degrees. Nevertheless, during the New Year tours, the hotels here are as crowded as in the summer.





The warm Red Sea delights lovers of the underwater world with its clear water and beautiful coral reefs. The Egyptian government strictly monitors its natural heritage, therefore, in some places it is forbidden not only to fish, but also to feed fish, and protected areas are strictly protected, which does not scare tourists away, but, on the contrary, attracts them.

Location of the Otium Amphora Hotel

Where is Otium located (Amphora Hotel, Sharm El Sheikh)? The photo below shows that it is located on the seashore, in the Naama Bay area. It is only 4 km from the Old Town with its bazaars, coffee houses and restaurants with local cuisine.





Located in the beautiful bay of Amphora, named after the Greek ship that died 200 years ago off its coast, the hotel is just a couple of hundred meters from the sea. Its bottom is unsuitable for swimming, as sharp stones do not allow to enter the water, but you can use a special floor for diving.

Corals are the attraction of this coast. They attract divers from all over the world, so the Egyptians are so sensitive to the safety of reefs, and the sunken ship gives the place an additional charm, curious for diving enthusiasts.

Diving enthusiasts

All divers in the world know Egypt well. Otium Amphora Hotel can be called their favorite place, as it is located in close proximity to the reefs and the national reserve.

These places are suitable for both professional divers and those who want to try their hand at this business without taking special courses. Experienced trainers help to make the first dive at 10 meters, holding the beginner by the hand.

Daily boat excursions consist of traveling to various coral reefs with diving, sunbathing on the deck, diving with masks and a delicious lunch at the end of the walk. Fans of scuba diving are awaited by caves and reefs of extraordinary beauty, a huge number of fish of the most diverse species and colors, and experienced divers await wrecks.





At the request of customers, the tour can be ordered on its own route, but the service will cost more. One of the favorite places for tourists to get acquainted with the natural resources of Egypt is the Ras Mohammed Nature Reserve.
