Perla Beach Resort (Primorsko, Bulgaria): description of rooms, service, reviews

Bulgaria is perhaps the most popular destination for Russian tourists of any age. For older travelers, the love of the picturesque views and beaches of Sunny Beach, Nessebar and Kranevo still has pre-perestroika roots. Youth reliably occupied the cheerful Golden Sands. But most of all, Bulgarian resorts are chosen by small tourists, and, of course, by their parents.

They are attracted by a pleasant subtropical climate, even fine-grained sandy beaches, picturesque views, spicy local cuisine, ripe juicy fruits and infrastructure designed for children. And the cherry on the cake of the advantages of Bulgarian resorts are quite affordable prices, the absence of language barriers and unique excursion programs.

Hotels in Bulgaria for families with children are also very welcome. Many of them adopted a brilliant manner of service in Greek and Turkish hotels, introduced all kinds of children's clubs, and gathered animation teams. We also designed the food for tourists on the All Inclusive system, which is well suited for guests with children.

In summer, there is no time difference between Moscow and Bulgaria, in winter it is minus 1 hour. Upon arrival, tourists are often ready to go to the beach or to the pool and do not lose half a day to sleep and adapt to the new time.

Advantages of Primorsko

Tourists who are looking for comfortable hotels in Bulgaria for families with children can immediately be advised to narrow down their searches and find a hotel within Primorsko. This is an absolutely completed resort with well-established service, pleasant sights and a decent hotel base.

Primorsko, with its natural wealth, picturesque cliffs, pristine forests and rare plants, is considered a protected area. But in the center of the nature reserve there is a wonderful highly developed resort, which is visited by 75 thousand tourists every season.

How to get to Primorsko

In the summertime, from Moscow to Bulgaria, in addition to regular flights from Aeroflot and Bulgaria Air, charter flights also fly. Charters from travel companies accept the eastern airports of Varna and Burgas. Primorsko is located in the south, 50 km from Burgas Airport.

This resort is suitable for tourists who cannot tolerate long transfers. From the airport to the town can be reached in 30 minutes.



Location of the hotel Perla Beach Resort 4 *

This large hotel complex, consisting of 4 hotels that have a common infrastructure. The hotel is 20 meters from Primorsko beach, 0.5 kilometers separates the hotel from a large water park.

The hotel is 60 km from the international airport of Burgas. And 6 km from the hotel there is a small airfield for local flights on small planes with a 950 m long route.



The Ropotamo cult reserve is just a 20-minute drive from the hotel. The reserve is a favorite sightseeing site. Marvelous tropical vegetation, overhanging cliffs, dunes and caves are best viewed from the river.

It will be interesting for children, and some adults, to look at the wildlife of the reserve, especially on turtles, dolphins and even seals, if you swim to Cape Maslen Nos.



A half hour drive separates the hotel from the Arkutino Botanical Garden, where rare species of cacti and the largest variety of water lilies in Europe grow.

Classification and description of rooms at Perla Beach Resort 4 *

Repair and renovation in the rooms took place in 2014.

Standard rooms Single and Double can accommodate a maximum of 3 people. Room size is 30 square meters. m

Family rooms are designed to accommodate a maximum of 4 people. Room size is 35 square meters. m. All rooms have sea views.

There is an elevator in every building of the hotel.

The rooms are not pet friendly.

Each room has a balcony or terrace, bath or shower, refrigerator, air conditioning, hairdryer, TV, free Wi-Fi.



Paid safe and luggage storage are available at the reception.

Check-in occurs after 14:00, check-out is after 13:00.

Room cleaning, linen and towels are free and daily.

Hotel Food System

Hotel "Pearl Beach Resort" 4 * operates on an all-inclusive system. All inclusive is open from 07:30 to 23:00.

As part of this catering concept, hotel guests are offered the following options:

  1. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served in the hotel's buffet restaurant. Breakfast lasts 2.5 hours after 07:30, lunch starts at 12:00, and dinner at 18:00.
  2. Complimentary coffee, tea and pastries all day at the hotel's restaurant and bars.
  3. From 10:00 local alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks.
  4. Light snacks, sandwiches, snacks from 10:00 to 18:00.
  5. Free ice cream from breakfast until 23:00.

Tourists must pay extra juice, imported alcoholic drinks and cocktails with them.



In their reviews, tourists spoke positively about the quality and variety of food in the hotel. A lot of fresh vegetables and salads, side dishes and burning vegetable dishes, soups. The amount of meat, fish, seafood is also satisfactory. And a very large selection of sweets, including five options for fresh fruit, pastries and other desserts.

Depending on the season, the hotel makes some adjustments to the All Inclusive system. When checking in, a new actual program is provided to each guest.

Hotel beach

Near the Perla Beach Resort 4 * hotel - 20 meters from its territory - there is a beautiful city sandy beach of Primorsko. Since the beach is municipal, sunbeds, umbrellas, drinks and beach sports on the beach are paid by tourists additionally.





The hotel does not provide beach towels.

The beach is wide, fine-grained, clean. Of course, rescuers work there. The waves on the beach are not high, which does not always please lovers of water sports, but it is ideal for children. The entrance to the sea is gentle and even, the sea is clean, the water is clear.

Hotel service

In Perla Beach Resort 4 * animation works. Sports animation includes water aerobics (water gymnastics). In the evening, competitions and performances are held for hotel residents. Children's animators spend entertaining games by the pool with young guests, classes in a mini-club and discos.





Club "Tukan" accepts children from 4 to 12 years old, classes in the club are held in Russian.

Of the children's options, the hotel also has high chairs in the restaurant and a cot in the room.



Also, all hotel guests can visit the water park of Primorsko, which is within five minutes of the hotel, for free and unlimited.

The hotel has a laundry service (for a fee), there is a conference room and an equipped business center, there is a safe at the reception (for a fee), free Wi-Fi throughout, currency exchange, and paid bike rental.

Hotel reviews

Reviews of tourists about Perla Beach Resort 4 * are mostly positive. Of the advantages of the hotel, tourists most often note a convenient location near the city center, beach and water park, the presence of a large territory and diverse infrastructure. Also, classes at the children's mini-club were pleasant to young guests and their parents, animators are polite and correct.

The opportunity to visit the water park for free is also a big bonus of the Perla Beach Resort 4 * hotel, tourists with children said that they used this pleasant opportunity almost daily. The water park in terms of the number of attractions is not inferior to many European counterparts.

Travelers also praised the hotel staff. Workers have a good understanding of Russian, responsive and responsive.



Of the minuses, some guests noted poor housekeeping, slow internet and lack of beach towels.

Like every resort hotel, Perla Beach Resort 4 * has its pros and cons. But definitely more pluses. The hotel is located in the center of the cozy town of Primorsko, close to the airport and the picturesque nature reserve. Near the hotel is a comfortable and clean beach. The hotel works according to the full All inclusive concept, has a good animation team and a large landscaped area.
