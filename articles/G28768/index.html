The model is a compact camera equipped with a 16-megapixel sensor and 30x zoom. In the manufacturer‚Äôs lineup, it replaced the L810 modification, which had the ability to zoom 26 times. Specifications

Basic technical parameters make it possible to evaluate the working potential of the device, which, in particular, shows the separation of the Culpix line from established simple solutions. How significant progress is, can be judged by the technical characteristics:

  • The number of pixels is 16.7 million.
  • The sensitivity range is from 125 to 3200 ISO with the possibility of auto-correction.
  • Camera resolution - maximum format 4608x3456.
  • The crop factor of the device is 5.62.
  • Focus - from 22.5 to 675 mm.
  • 30x zoom
  • The number of optical components is 12.
  • Video Recording - 1920 x 1080 HD and FullHD at 30 frames per second.
  • Batteries - 4 AA batteries.
  • The dimensions of the camera are 111x76x85 mm.
  • Weight in a completed state - 470 g.

Outwardly, the capabilities of the model look more than acceptable for their segment, but it is worth considering that there is no question of a complete comparison with representatives of classes that support similar working properties. It is enough to note the performance of the Nikon Culpix L-820 apparatus - the characteristic for the shooting speed is expressed at 2.1 frames per second, which is a modest result for modern ultrasounds. Although burst shooting may pleasantly surprise you - 38 shots for the JPEG format.

The appearance of the camera does not stand out. We used the traditional form factor for the ultrazoom, combined with a large pen and a large lens. Technological features

The base of the camera is a 30x Nikkor zoom lens. In addition to the basic performance characteristics, compared to the previous zoom, this component was added in bright colors. But its main features lie in the possibilities of a hyperzoom, which was distinguished by less pronounced chromatic aberrations, an improved optical system, and high sharpness indices at the edges of the image.

Got a design and a dual stabilization system formed by electronic and optical correctors. The first implements stabilization due to high values ‚Äã‚Äãof photosensitivity, and the second - according to the classical scheme due to the dynamic lens in the lens. But these are not all the significant improvements that the Nikon Coolpix L820 received. Features of the model are also reflected in sensory functions. These include the ability to recognize camera vibration and motion in the frame. It is also worth noting the above-mentioned serial shooting of 38 frames. This option was implemented precisely in the framework of the task of sharpening images. From the captured array, the system automatically selects the frame with the sharpest image. By the way, the manufacturer especially recommends using this shooting mode when the charge is running out, and there is no time for lengthy experiments with the settings.





Functional

To begin with, it is worth noting that the device received a full set of shooting modes that were used in other representatives of the Culpix family. Among them, one can single out macro photography, subject shooting, face focusing, etc. The exposure provides a wide exposure range, however, manual settings lovers will have to get used to the automation by this parameter. There are several exposure measurements, including spot, center-weighted and multi-zone. Among the modern functions, it is also worth highlighting 3D shooting, white balance adjustment, a built-in flash with a radius of 6 m, as well as red-eye reduction.

At the same time, there remained a wide range of popular options that Nikon Coolpix L-820 never received. For example, in the video mode, there is no viewfinder, although manufacturers who rely on stabilization and zoom often provide this opportunity. As for communication, it corresponds to the modern level. There are audio and video outputs, USB, as well as an HDMI port. By the way, those who are counting on large volumes of materials should take care in advance of SD, SDXC or SDHC cards of the right size. The fact is that the main memory of the model is only 65 MB.

Machine Menu

This part already traces the features of previous versions of Culpix, which were developed with emphasis on simple and clear menus. Although a considerable part of users are repelled by excessive simplicity, this concerns structuring with pictograms and a large monochrome font. On the other hand, fans of the brand trace in this an original style, reminiscent of the implementation of mirrorless cameras of the same manufacturer.

Through the menu, the operator gets access to all the tools for the initial setup and post-processing of materials shot with the Nikon Culpix L-820 camera. The manual also notes the benefit of automatic settings - in particular, the white balance can work regardless of the current shooting format, making corrections depending on the lighting. As for further editorial processing, in this regard the set of tools is not rich - the main program is D-Lighting and the crop function.

Video shooting

In the main mode, the camera supports shooting HD-clips, but if you wish, you can turn on FullHD, which also activates stereo sound with a zoom function. The advantages of implementing video management include a special button that starts the process. The features of this mode in the apparatus "Nikon Culpix L-820" include a good zoom, which is softer than in the process of taking photos. The frequency is 30 frames per second, but if necessary, it can be raised to 240 frames per second. To do this, you have to turn off the sound recording and set the minimum resolution. In this way, an 8-fold deceleration can be achieved.

Conclusion

The example of this camera proves once again that attempts to transfer individual successful models from initial segments to at least the middle class do not fundamentally change the product. Especially if the developers also try to preserve the price niche of the model. By increasing or adding individual functions and characteristics, the creators deprive cameras of other qualities.

In this case, overload is observed as an optional option at the expense of practicality, as evidenced by the reviews (Nikon Coolpix L820). At a minimum, in terms of its capabilities, the model can belong to the segment of budgetary household cameras. But then she will lose in convenience to lightweight solutions with impeccable ergonomics.
