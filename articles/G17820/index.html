Ambulance from mobile: is it easy?

An ambulance from a mobile phone can be called absolutely free, and most importantly, quickly. Each operator provides such a service.





Each operator has an ambulance call from a mobile phone available by its unique number. MTS and MegaFon subscribers can seek medical help by dialing 030. Remembering it is not difficult. And to this we need to add that an ambulance is called from a mobile absolutely free.

Take, for example, Moscow. In this metropolis, in just a few seconds, you will contact medical personnel and you will be provided with an ambulance. It should be remembered that in order for the ambulance team to come to you as soon as possible, you need to accurately and clearly answer all the questions of the paramedic. In addition, the caller must provide the name and surname of the patient, his age and reason for the call. You must also provide the exact coordinates of the location. Now back to the mobile phones. As already mentioned, each operator has its own unique number. And this is very important, because an unpleasant situation can happen at every moment. Depending on the operator, the ambulance from the mobile may be called:

  • MTS (GSM) - 030;
  • Tatinkom (DAMS) - 03;
  • Tatinkom (GSM) - 030;
  • MegaFon (GSM) - 030303;
  • Beeline (GSM) - 030 or 003;
  • Sky Link (GSM) - 903.

You can write these numbers in the phone memory or remember. After all, if you try to call an ambulance from your mobile using number 03, he may not give the desired result. There are mobile phones that do not support two-digit numbers.

However, as you know, there are different points. Ambulance from mobile MTS and other operators is called by number 112. These three numbers work when the balance of funds is zero or negative. You can call 112 even from a SIM card, access to which is blocked.

If the case is very serious, it is best to immediately call the rescuers. From the landline phone number - 01, MegaFon and MTS mobile operators - 010, Sky-Link - 901, Beeline - 001. The employees of this service will call an ambulance.





In a large city, an ambulance arrives at the place of call in about 20 minutes. For small towns there is no exact figure, but help should be immediate. If suddenly for some unknown reason you were denied an ambulance brigade, contact the police immediately.

And in the end it is necessary to mention that emergency medical assistance is called, including from a mobile one, according to certain rules throughout the country. Every citizen should know them. Regardless of the operator, the ambulance call is free throughout the coverage area.

So remember the emergency number and other emergency services for your mobile operator. After all, forethought will never do much harm.
