How to put money from a credit card on the Tele2 number?

Mobile operators offer subscribers a lot of ways to replenish their account. One of them is a transfer from a bank card. This is not only a popular, but also a profitable option for depositing funds into an account: there is no commission. In addition, a change in the balance of a mobile number occurs in minutes. About how you can put money from a credit card on the Tele2 number, we will tell in this article.





Transfer options

Transferring funds from a card to a mobile number account can be done in several ways: through the official portal of the Tele2 operator, from a cell phone by entering the appropriate commands and sending SMS requests to the allocated numbers, through a bank terminal, through invoices. Below will be described in detail all of the above methods.

How to deposit money on "Tele2" through a bank card at an ATM?

Deposits can be made through a bank terminal. The commission does not charge such a payment and arrives almost instantly. In order to transfer funds, you need to insert a card into the receiver, log in by entering a PIN code, select “Service payment” or “Transfer” in the menu (depending on the bank, the name of the menu sections may change). Then select “Mobile Communications” in the list and specify the operator for the transfer. In some ATMs, the determination of the payment destination occurs after entering the number. After that, it remains only to indicate the amount of payment in the appropriate field, check the input data and make the payment. After the operation, the check should be kept until the funds arrive at the operator’s account.





How to put money from a bank card on the Tele2 number via the Internet

This method of replenishing an account is very popular due to its simplicity, efficiency and convenience. Indeed, in order to increase the balance on the account of a mobile number, you only need to have Internet access. On the official portal “Tele2” you must go to the section for payments. Here, in order to complete the transfer, you do not even need to go to your personal account. It is enough to enter the following data:

• mobile phone number to which funds are to be paid;

• payment amount (the minimum threshold for transfer is set to 50 rubles; thus, fifty or more rubles can be transferred to the account);

• information about the bank card (No., surname and name of the holder, month and year until which the card is active, three-digit number on the back of CVV2 / CVC2).

After this, it remains only to verify the correctness of the entered information and complete the transfer of funds.

For customers who are planning to constantly make such transfers, it is possible to link a bank card to an account. This will allow you to not enter the above data at each next payment, i.e. significantly simplify the process of transferring money. Binding is carried out in your account via the corresponding menu item.





Without card

How to put money on Tele2 without a bank card via the Internet? Via online banking. Information about the card will not be needed to make such a transfer. It is enough to indicate the mobile operator, the amount of payment and make a payment. Here you can configure auto-completion (may not be available for all mobile operators).

Pay via phone

You can also top up your Tele2 account via your mobile phone. Payment by credit card in this case occurs only if there is a link to the number. Otherwise, it will be impossible to use the phone to transfer funds. Thus, if the card is tied in the personal account of the “Tele2” user, then if you execute the request * 109 * payment amount # (or send the payment amount in SMS message to number 109), the funds will be transferred to the account. There are similar services for the banks themselves, which allow sending money to the “Tele2” mobile account by sending SMS to a specific number. You can also deposit money from a credit card using special combinations of symbols, which can be specified directly in the issuing organization.





Automatic payment function

Auto payment allows subscribers to forget about regular replenishment of the account of Tele2 number. You can put money from a credit card automatically. It is enough to indicate the amount of the balance, upon reaching which the account will be replenished. You can configure such functionality in your personal account on the mobile operator’s portal. A similar (or similar) opportunity may exist on online banks. At the same time, in some of them you can configure a specific period after which transfers will be made.

Using bank cards to pay for mobile communications not only saves the subscriber’s time, but also allows you to make payments without commission.
