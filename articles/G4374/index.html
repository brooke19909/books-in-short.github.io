We knit a scarf yoke

The scarf collar has gained popularity not only as a practical component of the wardrobe, but also as an original accessory that adds zest to sports and classic outfits. Light and airy or warm wool, almost every fashionista has it. How to wear a scarf clamp, today there is no need to explain. Dense and elastic models that fit the neck and reliably protect from the cold are more suitable for men and children, women prefer the option of the snut, freely falling on the shoulders and, if necessary, covering the head.

If you are interested in a scarf collar, knitting it yourself will not be difficult. For those who are still just mastering the knitting skills, a suitable option would be a model connected by English rubber, which we will consider in more detail.





For work, we need:

- half-woolen yarn 250-300 m long per 100 g - 400 g,

- knitting needles, a number more than indicated on the yarn label,

- sewing needle with a big eye.

Execution of English gum :

In the first row we remove the edge, 1p. front, 1p. remove on the right crochet, repeat to the end of the row.

In the second row we remove the hem, we sew the loop with the crochet on the front, 1 point we remove on the right crochet needle, repeat until the end of the row. We repeat knitting according to the scheme the required number of rows.

Scarf-collar - knitting with an English elastic band

We collect 20 loops on the knitting needles and knit a free viscous sample. Based on the size of the fabric, we calculate the desired number of loops, which will be approximately 120-130 pcs. We knit an English elastic band 50 cm high. If this scarf is enough for you, close the loops, trying not to tighten them tightly. Gently connect the seam, after which the scarf is slightly moistened and stretched.





The second option for knitting a collar is also simple in execution, but for it we need circular knitting needles. We collect on the knitting needles the number of loops, calculated according to the sample, and knit 10 rows round the face viscous, then the same amount of the wrong side. We continue to work until the product reaches the desired length. If you want to expand the scarf so that it freely covers your shoulders, evenly add loops in the first and last of the wrong rows. We close the loops freely, pull the finished scarf. Such a collar can be knitted simultaneously from two balls, alternately changing the color of the stripes.





For a child, you can knit a scarf-clamp, which will tightly fit the neck, and protect the throat, as well as go down on your shoulders. We will knit a product sample with a 2x2 elastic band, and already on it we will calculate the required number of loops in the work. We knit the scarf with an elastic band of 15-20 cm, after which we expand it by adding 4 purl stitches in each row at regular intervals. For example, the work is designed for 80 loops, of which 20 strips are made with purl and 20 are front loops. We knit from the crochet an extra crossed wrong side in 1, 6, 11 and 16 bands, in the next row we shift and add loops in 2, 7, 12 and 17 bands, etc., We knit with the addition until the fabric reaches the desired length.

Now that we have examined in detail the very principle by which a scarf collar is knitted, you can execute this model with any volumetric or relief pattern you like. A mohair yarn scarf knitted with openwork will require more time and patience, but with some perseverance you can become the owner of a real fluffy masterpiece.
