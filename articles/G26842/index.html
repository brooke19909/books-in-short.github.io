Jasmine (Hurghada) - a fairy tale hotel. Green oasis in the desert

Hotel "Jasmine" (Hurghada) is an excellent complex for families. It is famous for its beautiful, green territory, an abundance of various colors, spacious beaches and excellent pools. And if you are going on vacation to Egypt (Hurghada), the Jasmine Hotel will be the best option.

Location

Hotel Jasmine Village is located on the seafront, just six kilometers from the airport. Hurghada is 15 kilometers away. The building was built in the late eighties, but in 2002 a qualitative and modern reconstruction was made, and the premises look one hundred percent. It has a great design and all the conditions are created for the full enjoyment of your vacation.



Description

The hotel "Jasmine" (Hurghada), made in the style of oriental tales, is surrounded from all sides by magical gardens of greenery. This complex consists of two-story buildings and one-story bungalows, they have 463 rooms with balconies and terraces, which offer a bewitching view. All apartments are comfortable and convenient, room service is available round the clock. Attentive staff, high service and maximum amenities - all this awaits you at the Jasmine Hotel. Each room is equipped with air conditioning, refrigerator, TV and telephone.



Hotel Territory and Infrastructure

Hotel "Jasmine" (Hurghada) occupies a huge area, the territory is a unique green oasis in the desert, with beautiful plantings and exotic flowers. Egypt, as you know, is one of the sunny and hot countries, so a walk along the shady alleys of the hotel complex will be a suitable option for those who can‚Äôt stand the heat of the lunch. From the buildings only a few meters to a gorgeous beach, the sea here is warm and gentle. To relax on the beach there are sun loungers, umbrellas, hotel guests can use all these accessories for free. Hotel Jasmine Village has a well-developed infrastructure: there are three restaurants with various cuisines, bars, cafes on the territory where you can have a great time with friends, family or enjoy the sounds of amazing oriental music. And if you want to combine leisure and work, the hotel has a chic conference room with 50 seats.

The pool of turquoise water will be an amazing place to relax, which will help to cool off perfectly, and works in the cooler weather. And, of course, tourists are given the opportunity to visit the Turkish bath, sauna and soak in the jacuzzi. For small tourists, a separate pool has been built, a playground is also equipped for children, and a mini club is working. And even there is a small zoo with interesting animals.

Entertainment

Hotel "Jasmine" (Hurghada) for lovers of outdoor activities offers water sports. This is diving, water skiing, windsurfing. On the territory are equipped tennis courts, a volleyball court, there is a gym, a riding club, an aqua center. Unique and magnificent rest will be given by the Jasmine Hotel (Hurghada), prices for a seven-day tour for one person range from 600 to 800 dollars.
