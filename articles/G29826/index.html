How to find out who the phone number is registered to

Sometimes it becomes necessary to find out to whom the phone number is registered. Most of all, this is due to unwanted and frequent calls and messages that contain a threat or advertisement.



You just need to know that when ascertaining the owner you cannot violate certain laws in order to avoid problems with law enforcement agencies. Consider a few points on how to find out to whom the number is registered.

Firstly, if you are offered to pay for subscriber information, know that they want to deceive you. You can get all the necessary information absolutely free. Just follow a few conditions. No need to purchase databases - this is an illegal method of action.



Secondly, there are times when you find someone else's SIM card. And to find out to whom her number is registered, you just need to go to the website of her operator. Before that, insert the SIM card into the phone, go to the site, then in your personal account, and after that ask for the password. When it is received, go back to your personal account - and there will be information about the tariff and full name subscriber.

If you use the Internet through a modem, then this SIM card can be inserted into it. But only if the card operator matches the operator of your modem. After that, you must also go to the site and repeat all of the above actions.

Another reliable way that talks about how to find out to whom the number is registered is by contacting law enforcement agencies. This may be the police, prosecutors and federal law enforcement agencies. Especially if you get bored with unpleasant calls containing threats.

Of course, you need to write a statement. In it, indicate all your requirements and claims. When your application is accepted, law enforcement agencies will make a request to the operator, who will necessarily provide the necessary information. And you, as a victim, will receive all the information about the SIM card subscriber.



The fourth method, which contains information on how to find out to whom the number is registered, is to contact the office of the telecom operator. There you will need to write a statement stating the reason for your appeal. Company representatives will consider this request and may respond to it.
