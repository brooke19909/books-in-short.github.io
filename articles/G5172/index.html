When planning a vacation in the Russian capital or coming to it on business matters, you are probably thinking about where to stay. If you are looking for a luxurious and fashionable hotel with world-class service, pay attention to the main hotel - Radisson Royal. It combines a great location and increased comfort. Hotel Radisson Royal, Moscow: location





The hotel is located in the heart of the Russian capital, on the bend of the Moskva River, at the intersection of Novy Arbat and Kutuzovsky Prospekt. These are the most prestigious highways of the city. The White House, which is located opposite, can serve as a guide for visiting guests. Walking distance to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Consulates, Expocentre Fairgrounds, Moscow City, World Trade Center. A few minutes away by shuttle bus or your car are the main attractions of not only the city, but the whole country: Red Square, the Bolshoi Theater, the Kremlin, the Tretyakov Gallery, etc.

It is easy and quick to get to the Radisson Royal Hotel 5 * from Sheremetyevo 2 (32 km) and Vnukovo (31 km).
