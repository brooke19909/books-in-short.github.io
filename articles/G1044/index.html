Headphones Koss UR20: description, specifications and reviews

The segment of classic closed headphones is quite tightly occupied by budget models, among which it is not easy to choose a truly high-quality option. Of course, the cost of such devices is also low, but you have to pay for savings with operational characteristics. Nevertheless, major manufacturers of sound equipment and seek in the initial link to maintain the proper level of quality. In this regard, we can note the version of Koss UR20, an overview of which is presented below. This is not to say that the model impresses with the reproduction of the entire frequency scene and is characterized by increased comfort in handling, but for a budget class this is a very decent solution.





Model Overview

Headphones are a traditional performance of a closed monitor-type form factor with full bowls. It should be noted that the manufacturer Koss willingly produces models for the mass consumer. That is, most of the line is still represented by devices with fairly average quality. So, the Koss UR20 model, whose price is about 2000 rubles, is knocked out of this context precisely due to the decent quality of the materials used.

Unfortunately, this did not help raise the level of sound parameters, but there is no need to talk about a serious failure. We can say that this option is designed for an audience that is used to using cheap headphones, but decided to try out a better playback tool. In other words, the development of the Koss UR20 is the optimal transitional option from frank budget employees to the middle level.





Headphone Specifications

Nominal performance indicators also testify to the company's desire to break out of the ranks of manufacturers of strong monitor headphones . The model surpasses the entry-level representatives in a number of characteristics, which, however, are worth exploring in more detail.

  • Type of device - monitor dynamic headphones.
  • Connection - via cable.
  • The lower frequency level is 30 Hz.
  • The upper frequency limit is 20,000 Hz.
  • The impedance of the model is 32 ohms.
  • The sensitivity indicator is 97 dB.
  • Mass - 284 g.
  • Type of fixation - design in the form of a headband.
  • Connection is one-way.
  • The connector is a standard mini-jack.
  • Cable length - 180 cm.

The main indicator due to which the Koss UR20 version is ahead of its competitors in class is a wide range of reproduced frequencies. In terms of sensitivity and level of resistance, this is far from a leader, but even here the balance of settings is noted.





Appearance

Even with a visual inspection, you can highlight the features that raise the device above the budget segment. Although plastic is mainly used in the construction, one cannot but note its high quality and pleasant sensations from touching. The headband is represented by a double arc, while in the lower part in contact with the ears, a durable fabric finish with leatherette firmware is used. Unfortunately, the back side has no slots, so the dense ear cushions for Koss UR20 headphones become an obstacle to heat dissipation. As a result, ventilation suffers, and ears sweat quickly. But there are pluses in the performance of cups. They are also made of plastic, which enhances reflective qualities. As for sound insulation, it is extremely well executed. This is a weak point not only in cheap devices, but in this case, the developers have provided a wonderful barrier against third-party sounds.





Features

It is not necessary to wait for new technological accents from this class, but designers nevertheless surprised with something. This primarily relates to the design. The Koss UR20 model has a folding device, allowing the user to minimize its dimensions. The cups are organically and compactly pressed against the headband, providing ease of transportation. In addition, the headphones are equipped with an induction coil, in which the core is made of ferrite. This element is supplemented with a 3-centimeter membrane. This solution cannot be called an innovation - moreover, it has become traditional for mid-range models, but, again, in low-cost devices, the presence of this part is almost never found.

Sound quality

Of course, the main parameters for assessing the quality of headphones are the ability to reproduce sound waves. The claimed frequency range as a whole is revealed confidently, but different registers have their own nuances. It is noteworthy that the developers intentionally minimized the sound in the lower range for a more pronounced processing of high notes. This was done in order to achieve symmetry throughout the sound picture. Despite this adjustment by the Koss UR20, the bass that is characteristic of such devices remained dominant. Unfortunately, the weak taming of the lower frequencies prevented the full reproduction of the remaining sounds. Especially in club music, these nuances are noticeable.





But the model loses calm compositions purely and naturally. This is explained by the fact that a special coating was used in the manufacture of the back of the cups. As a result, there is a good resonant ability in the middle range, which draws the overall sound quality. As for high notes, there are failures and cuts. But it is worth noting that in this spectrum finer settings for headphones are required - it is obvious that a device of this class will not cope with this task in principle.

Positive feedback about the model

Most of the positive feedback relates to design characteristics. Owners note a comfortable headband with optimal flexibility, quality cup and ear cup materials and ergonomic adjustment mechanisms. It is also worth noting the form factor, which provides a folding system. The obvious advantages include sound insulation Koss UR20. Reviews indicate that even on the street the case reliably protects from the intrusion of excess noise. With regard to sound, of course, opinions are not so enthusiastic. The indisputable advantages of headphones in this regard include mid-range operation and bass reproduction. If you need a device for universal listening, then the capabilities of the Koss model will be enough.





Negative reviews

Critical reviews nevertheless note some gaps in the design and the unsatisfactory quality of reproduction of individual frequencies. Regarding the design, users emphasize the lack of decompression and lack of ventilation function. There are claims to the strength of the materials from which the Koss UR20 headphones are made. Reviews, in particular, note the feeling of flimsy fasteners, but damage to plastic elements is extremely rare. The main claims are addressed to the characteristics of sound. For example, tracks with pronounced vocals are not fully processed by the headphones. Orientation to the bass makes itself felt in dynamic compositions.





Conclusion

The model is worth considering as a universal tool for playing music. Sophisticated music lovers who love detailed and deep listening with emphasis on each note, Koss UR20 headphones are unlikely to work. In extreme cases, to improve playback quality, you will have to take care of a good sound source in advance. Experienced users also recommend supplementing the device with an external EQ so that the songs do not sound too one-sided. Otherwise, this is a good option for operation outside the home. Foldable design, cable with excellent length, material reliability, excellent noise reduction and ergonomic advantages allow you to comfortably use this model on trips. At a price this option, of course, surpasses many competitors with the same characteristics, but in terms of the combination of advantages there are almost no equal analogues. Therefore, it is worth looking for the closest rivals already in the middle link of monitor headphones. True, their cost will go far beyond the budget price tag.
