Hotel "Russia" (Samara): comfort at affordable prices

Samara is a large city that is located on the banks of the Volga. It is home to more than 1,170,000 people. The city has a large port and many industries in different directions. In Samara, tourists can visit interesting and beautiful sights. For a comfortable stay after a productive day, the Rossiya Hotel (Samara) provides its guests with comfortable rooms.

Hotel Features

The building is located in the center in an ecologically clean area. Where is the hotel "Russia" (Samara)? Address: st. Maxim Gorky, 82. The windows of the rooms offer beautiful views of the promenade. Most Samara sights are within 100-200 m from the hotel:

  • Strukovsky garden.
  • Philharmonic.
  • River Station.
  • Opera and Ballet Theatre.




Not far from the hotel there is a large city beach. Here in the summer you can relax from the hustle and bustle after the heat.

Professional staff is always friendly to guests and visitors to the hotel. At any time, employees are ready to help visitors to resolve any issue.

Accommodations

Hotel "Russia" (Samara) has on its base 175 rooms of different levels of comfort.

1. "Economy" - a room with a modern renovation and one or two single beds. The room is equipped with everything necessary:

  • TV
  • telephone;
  • ventilator;
  • set of dishes;
  • a bathroom;
  • hair dryer.

Most rooms of this comfort level have a beautiful view of the Volga.

2. "Standard" - a room with a double bed or two single and a half cost from 2,500 rubles. per day. The room is equipped with the necessary equipment and things for a comfortable stay:

  • TV
  • fan;
  • a kettle and a set of dishes;
  • c \ y;
  • hair dryer;
  • bathrobe and slippers;
  • safe.

3. "Panorama" - a room with a double bed and several windows that provide incredibly beautiful views of the Volga. The cost of such a room is from 2 900 rubles. per day. The room is equipped with everything you need, like the "standard", with the addition of a floor air conditioner.





4. "Comfort" - rooms costing from 3,700 rubles. per day. They consist of two rooms. One has a double bed or two and a half, and the other has a folding sofa. The room is equipped with everything necessary. It also has a modern split system.

5. "Business Panorama" (from 4,500 rubles per day) has several large windows overlooking the Volga. The room consists of two rooms with air conditioning and all necessary equipment.

6. "Studio" (from 7,000 rubles per day) is equipped according to all the rules for arranging rooms of the highest category.

7. 2 and 3-room apartments with a kitchen (from 8 500 rubles per day) have an increased level of comfort. The rooms are renovated and have the necessary equipment. The furniture is modern and comfortable.

All rooms have (free) WI-Fi network.

Booking and meals

Hotel Samara "Russia" provides an early check-in from 00.00 to 14.00 with an additional charge of half a day. Standard payment is subject to check-in at 14.00 and check-out at 12.00. Room reservation can be done by calling (846) 339-03-97 or online with the date of arrival and departure.

Hotel "Russia" (Samara) has a cafe and a restaurant at its base. Here you can order a full meal or just one of the options (lunch or dinner). The hotel often hosts promotions that include breakfast in the room rate.

Additional services

Convenient conference rooms and facilities for meetings and trainings are offered by the Rossiya Hotel. G. Samara is the business center of the district, and the need for such events is constant.





The hotel provides its guests with additional services:

  • parking;

  • barbershop;
  • business center;
  • gym;
  • safes.

Hotel "Russia" (Samara) can organize all stages of conferences and meetings. The newlyweds are also invited to book a "Wedding Room" with arrangement in the theme of "wedding" with a late check out at 23.00 and breakfast in the room.
