Kabardinka - a recreation center or a popular resort?

Who doesn’t want to leave the gray dusty city in the summer and plunge into the gentle waters of the Black Sea? Many have already heard about the Black Sea resorts, but do everyone know about Kabardinka? This is a rather small stretch of coast, covering the area of ​​one small village, it is able to say so much about itself that most famous health resorts can simply envy the benefits of the Kabardinka resort. The recreation center "Pearl" is a real find for those who wish not only to relax, but also to improve their health.





Kabardinka - a resort or a health resort?

The village is located between two hills near the seashore, surrounded by pine and juniper groves. Sea access goes through the Tsemess Bay, famous for its natural attractions. The air in this area has healing properties, so the resort can be safely called a health resort near the Black Sea.





The best place to relax is Kabardinka. The recreation center not only received the honorary title of the entire resort village, but also the right to receive guests on its territory all year round, which is very important, given the rather mild climate of this area. Vacationers come constantly, despite seasonal restrictions.

What is attractive Kabardinka

As in ancient times, vineyards with flourishing winemaking, which is an integral part of the Kabardinka resort, grow on this land. The recreation center "Lavender" can offer vacationers a tasting of famous varieties of wine that you can not only taste, but also buy for yourself or as a gift to your friends and relatives.





Not only Kabardinka is famous for its wines, the famous juniper groves, covering an area of ​​about 1000 hectares, play a very important role in the climate and the healing air. As you know, juniper releases special essential oils into the air, the so-called phytoncides, which disinfect, disinfect and purify the air.

Only one hectare can produce up to 30 kg of such phytoncides in one night. This amount is enough to clean one polluted city. What can we say about the resort of Kabardinka surrounded not only by the warm sea and relic juniper, but also by a pine forest ! The recreation center "Salvation" for its guests provided not only a comfortable location, but also wellness treatments.





Holidays for the whole family

No wonder in the middle of the 20th century the resort was recognized as a children's health resort, and now this status has not lost its significance at all. Many tourists decided to spend their holidays in the resort of Kabardinka 2013. The recreation center "Riviera" was marked by innovations in the services provided and significantly expanded its ownership.

This wonderful resort is located very close to Gelendzhik, on the one hand, and Novorossiysk, on the other. Thanks to this, getting to the village is not difficult, and the developed transport connection maintains communication with major cities around the clock. Many pensions and hotels have their own parking, this is an important fact for owners of private vehicles.





This year, the resort of Kabardinka was pleased with many of its guests. Recreation centers have adjusted prices for vacationers of different income levels, and this has made it possible for many middle-income families to recover on the Black Sea.
