The best hotels in the center of Moscow: photos and reviews of tourists

Moscow is a huge city, which is visited annually by a lot of people on various occasions. Tourists and visitors, of course, prefer hotels in the center of Moscow. The choice of hotel complexes in the modern capital is quite extensive. In our article we want to review hotels in the center of Moscow.

"Renaissance Moscow Monarch"

The Hotel Monarch Center Moscow is located in the huge business district of the capital at the intersection of Leningradsky Prospekt and Begovaya Street. When living in a big city, it is very important to be near transport stops in order to be able to quickly reach your destination. The most popular hotels in the center of Moscow near the metro, because this particular type of transport is most convenient for the metropolis. Near the Renaissance Moscow Hotel are the Begovaya and Dynamo metro stations. The complex is a sixteen-story building, made in a modern design and offering accommodation in 366 comfortable rooms. Institutional apartments are presented in a variety of categories. Among them there are not only one-room rooms, but also chic apartments, even for longer stays.

The Renaissance Moscow Monarch Center Hotel (Leningradsky Prospekt) offers its guests to visit several restaurants and a stylish bar. Restaurant "Mosaic" offers a wide selection of Russian and European dishes. And the Vostok establishment serves gastronomic delights of different cuisines of the world. The complex also has a modern congress center with 12 halls of various capacities.

In addition, the hotel has a fitness center, which has an aerobics room, a fitness room, a hammam, a pool with waterfalls, a jacuzzi, a solarium and a massage room. Hotel "Monarch Center Moscow" is convenient not only for relaxation, but also for business trips.

The room fund of the institution is represented by spacious apartments, among which there are deluxes, suites, executive rooms, corner suites and even Presidential apartments. All rooms are equipped with LCD TVs, a working area, a safe, a telephone, a minibar, ironing facilities, and Internet.

On the upper floors of the hotel there is a relaxation terrace, a library and a bar. This is a recreation area where you can have a great time and admire the capital.

Guest reviews

Continuing the review of Moscow hotels in the city center, it is worth noting that Renaissance Moscow is an institution that has established itself on the best side. According to visitors, the hotel offers very comfortable apartments and a decent level of service. Guests also praise the buffet in the restaurant. The friendly staff of the complex are also pleased. The institution can be safely recommended as a wonderful hotel in the center of Moscow with breakfast, convenient in all respects for guests of the capital.

Radisson Royal Hotel

If we talk about the best 5 star hotels in the center of Moscow, then this includes the Radisson Royal Hotel. The luxurious establishment is located in the very center of the capital and is aimed at very wealthy people. Near the hotel there is the Government House, the Moscow-City complex, the Russian Foreign Ministry, the Kremlin, foreign embassies, Red Square, the Tretyakov Gallery, St. Basil's Cathedral. The hotel complex is quite large, its total area is 2140 square meters. On its territory there are seven restaurants, an indoor pool, a bathhouse, a sauna, a library and much more.

The hotel rooms have a luxurious design that is amazing. All guests are offered a buffet breakfast, as well as meals in any establishment throughout the day. The complex has a wellness club, which is equipped with an excellent 50-meter pool. There is a professional gym, spa, sauna, hammam, massage rooms, entertainment for children. On the ground floor of the hotel there is a gallery of boutiques where you can buy jewelry and souvenirs and clothes.

Radisson Royal Hotel can be called the most dynamically developing congress hotel. It has a serious high-level conference center equipped with the best equipment.

Reviews on accommodation at the Radisson Royal Hotel

Hotel guests leave the best reviews about the establishment, noting the high level of service, tastefully renovated rooms and good tasty food. A high class establishment is pleasant in every way. Of course, as in any hotel, there are not very friendly staff representatives, but even their incompetence is not able to spoil a good impression of such a wonderful place. Frequent guests of the complex recommend visiting the spa and the huge pool, this beautiful place will give you many wonderful minutes and real relaxation.

Marriott Moscow Grand Hotel

The Marriott Moscow Grand Hotel is located in the business center of the capital and is a five-star establishment. A few steps from the hotel are the main places of interest in Moscow, including Red Square and the Kremlin. The hotel is located on the famous Tverskaya street.

The Marriott Moscow Grand Hotel offers 387 apartments, in addition, the hotel has three executive floors. The complex has a sports center, a business center, a lobby bar and several restaurants.

Despite the status of the institution, not all guests are satisfied with it, casting doubt on the five stars of the hotel. The hotel complex is equipped with good rooms in which there are malfunctions (in an institution of this level they simply cannot be discussed). The hotel staff is friendly, the service is fast and of a high standard. Well, and, of course, pleased with the excellent location of the building. After all, not only Belorussky Train Station, but also all the significant places of the capital are located very close by.

Courtyard by Marriott Moscow

The Courtyard Marriott Moscow Center Hotel is a beautiful establishment located in the heart of the city. Literally ten minutes walk from the Kremlin and Red Square.

The room fund of the institution consists of 218 apartments, among which there are 10 business studios and 29 superior rooms. Guestrooms are equipped with plasma televisions, minibars, satellite channels and air conditioning. Ironing facilities, a hairdryer and a safe, as well as hot drinks kits are provided. The rooms are in classic design.

The main restaurant serves a buffet. The second food establishment specializes in Mexican and American cuisine. In the lobby, guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

The hotel has a network of function rooms. One of them belongs to the category of the largest in the capital. Modern equipment and convenient location of the institution makes the business center of the complex a good place for events. Such a hotel in the center of Moscow is convenient for living and for organizing business meetings.

"Nikolskaya Kempinski Moscow"

Among Moscow hotels in the city center there are many fashionable establishments. This can be called the hotel "Nikolskaya Kempinski Moscow", which is located on Lubyanka Square. Red Square is just a ten-minute walk away. The hotel complex has chic and elegant rooms, done in brown and beige colors. All of them are equipped with modern appliances and expensive furniture.

Guests can eat at the restaurant "Mosaic", which is equipped with an open kitchen. Here you can relax and eat grilled dishes prepared right before your eyes. In addition, the institution offers Russian, European and Oriental dishes. It is equally interesting to spend time in the Orlov bar or on the terrace of the Mojito bar.

Nikolsky Kempinski Moscow is one of the best hotels in the center of Moscow. The Bolshoi Theater is just five minutes from the hotel. In addition, the Lubyanka metro station is located nearby, so getting to the hotel is easy.

Azimut Olympic Moscow

If the five-star status of an institution is not a mandatory factor for you, then as an option you can consider hotels in the center of Moscow 4 stars. One such place is the Azimut Olympic Moscow Hotel, which is located on Olympic Avenue. Near the hotel there are two metro stations - Dostoevskaya and Prospekt Mira, as well as attractions such as the Catherine Garden and the Olympic Sports Complex. The complex has 491 rooms. All apartments are equipped with modern furniture, a safe, a minibar, a TV and other amenities. The hotel also has club rooms with a separate seating area.

This apartment includes dinner, breakfast and snacks at the bar. In addition, hotel guests can use the fitness club, equipped with an indoor pool, a beauty salon, restaurants and bars, a solarium, a jacuzzi, and a massage parlor. Breakfast and meals are not included.

Reviews for accommodation in Azimut Olympic Moscow

According to tourists, among the hotels near the center of Moscow, Azimut Olympic Moscow stands out. Its quiet and comfortable rooms guarantee a great stay. Pleasant staff is always glad to visitors. The hotel is in a good location, very close to a park where you can walk with children. Getting to the hotel is also convenient, thanks to a good transport interchange. Hotel guests also praise the food in the restaurant. In general, the complex may well become one of the options when looking for a hotel or hotel in the center of Moscow.

"Holiday Inn Moscow Simonovsky"

Holiday Inn Moscow Simonovsky is a four-star complex built near the Moscow River. The hotel is located on Simonovsky Val Street. Near the hotel there is the Kremlin, St. Basil's Cathedral, the Old Tretyakov Gallery, a zoo. The hotel complex has a fitness center, a bar and a business center.

Near the hotel there is a metro station "Krestyanskaya Zastava". The complex offers 217 rooms equipped with kitchenettes, televisions, safes and air conditioning. A buffet breakfast is served daily in the restaurant. Throughout the day, visitors can enjoy delicious international dishes. There is also a lobby bar and grill bar, gym and fitness center on site.

According to guests of the capital, the hotel has a good location and is good for living. The rooms are beautiful and new, they are equipped with new furniture and fixtures. The rooms have many outlets, which is very convenient, so you can work on a laptop anywhere. The restaurant covers a fairly varied and delicious breakfast. As for the service, there are some minor flaws. Hotel guests definitely recommend it for their stay.

The three-star establishment offers soundproofed rooms with a good decor, made in the best classical traditions. The apartments are equipped with private bathrooms and desks.
