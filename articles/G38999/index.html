Military sanatorium "Volga" (Samara): medical services, prices and leisure

When the question arises of a truly high-quality and wellness vacation, without hesitation you should go to the Volga sanatorium (Samara). Recreational institutions of this type offer holidays in the bosom of wildlife, as well as pastime away from the gassed and crowded metropolis. It is here that one can correct shaky physical and psycho-emotional health.

Description





The health resort is conveniently located on the coast of the deep Volga river, among mixed forests (birch, spruce, centuries-old oaks), only 15 km from the central part of the city of Samara. From the windows of the boarding house you can see a picturesque panorama of the Zhiguli Uplands and the forest. The territory has its own well with hydrogen sulfide water.

The doors of the complex are open all year. The residential building is large enough for 500 people. Small children from the age of five are accepted. Residence





For comfortable accommodation, the sanatorium Volga (Samara) offers a 5-storey building. Available there are one and two-room numbers (luxury). In the standard rooms you will find the necessary furniture, a separate bathroom with a shower, as well as a TV, a landline telephone, a radio and a set for making hot drinks (kettle, dishes).

Two-room numbers are more spacious and are divided into zones of a bedroom and a drawing room. Instead of a shower cabin there is a full bath, there are soft chairs and a mini-wall. This room has a balcony. Daily cleaning is provided in each room.

Nutrition





The price includes not only treatment, but also three meals a day in the dining room. The menu is custom. For people with digestive tract disorders and other diseases, a diet is provided, which is selected by experienced doctors. Military sanatorium "Volga" in Samara invites everyone to look into the barbecue or bar with a wide range of dishes and drinks.

Coastline





What a vacation without sunbathing and swimming! The health resort has its own stretch of coast, located about 200 meters away. The beach is sandy, equipped with sunshades and sunbeds. The safety of guests is monitored by a rescue service. The coast is guarded around the clock. For active entertainment there is a volleyball net and a badminton court. There is a rental point where you can take a boat or a catamaran and go to inspect the beauty of the local landscape.
