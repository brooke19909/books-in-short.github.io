Finland, Saimaa Holiday Club: photos, prices and reviews

Every year, a unique entertainment complex called Saimaa Holiday Club, located in the vicinity of the Finnish town of Imatra, near the Russian border, is gaining popularity among our compatriots. Here, travelers will find a whole host of funs for every taste: from a huge water park to bowling, golf, horse riding, skiing and ice skating and much more. Tourists can accommodate in a comfortable hotel. If you, like thousands of our compatriots, decide to discover this unique place, we suggest that you familiarize yourself with its main characteristics, pricing policy, as well as the reviews of Russians who have already visited the Saimaa Holiday Club.





Where is the entertainment complex

Holiday Club Saimaa 4 * is located in the center of southeastern Finland, in a place called Rauha. Two interesting natural sites are located in this area: the Vuoksa River, the waters of which do not freeze even in winter, and the largest lake in the country, Saimaa. A few years ago, a landmark created by human hands appeared here. We are talking about the subject of our story - the entertainment complex "Saimaa Holiday Club". The nearest railway stations are in Imatra (7 kilometers) and Lappeenranta (35 kilometers). The last city has an airport. By the way, many Russians come here with their own cars or buses. Indeed, the distance, for example, from St. Petersburg to the entertainment complex is only 230 kilometers.

Description of the hotel Saimaa Holiday Club

The hotel complex consists of three main buildings and several additional buildings and cottages. In total, the number of rooms is represented by 221 comfortable rooms. There are also rooms specially equipped to accommodate people with disabilities. We suggest that you familiarize yourself with the categories of rooms available at the Holiday Club Saimaa.





Holiday Club building

Here the largest number of rooms is located - 137. They are spacious and designed for comfortable accommodation of people arriving for families on vacation. Each room of this type can accommodate three adults or two adults and two children. There are: a double bed, a sofa bed, a TV, a fridge, a bathroom with a hairdryer, a bathrobe and all the necessary bath accessories, as well as a large or French balcony.

Castle Building

63 rooms of this category are decorated in a romantic style and are perfect for staying in love or a married couple. They are equipped with a private bathroom with everything you need, a large double bed, comfortable furniture (armchairs and a sofa) and a plasma TV.

Gant building

This Saimaa Holiday Club hotel has luxurious and spacious rooms that have been decorated with the support of the eponymous trading house. They have a large double bed, plasma TV, folding furniture (sofa or two armchairs), a private bathroom with a bathrobe, hairdryer, cosmetics and toiletries, all necessary for making coffee or tea. Some of these rooms also have magnificent views of Lake Saimaa.



Apartments in villas and cottages

Here are the best rooms in the entire hotel complex. So, for example, Holiday Club Saimaa Superior Apartments have, in addition to the bathroom, items of upholstered furniture, a refrigerator, a TV, separate bedrooms, a glazed balcony with great views, a fully equipped kitchen and a private sauna.

restaurants and bars

Everyone who is on vacation at the Saimaa Holiday Club can easily find here dishes for themselves to their taste. For example, in the main restaurant of the hotel-entertainment complex "Easy Kitchen" three times a day a buffet is served, represented by a variety of perfectly prepared first and second courses, as well as snacks and desserts. Having visited the Le Biff restaurant, you can taste the juicy steaks cooked on charcoal with excellent wine. In addition, there are several bars in the Holiday Club Saimaa (Imatra), including a sports bar with large screens for watching sporting events and karaoke.





Aquapark

One of the best water sports complexes in Finland is the Holiday Club Saimaa water park. Here you will find plenty of opportunities for both active and interesting recreation, and for relaxation. "World of saunas"

This interesting establishment is located directly on the territory of the Saimaa Holiday Club water park. However, you will have to pay extra for entering it (about seven euros). Here you will be invited to visit four saunas: steam, traditional Finnish, salt and master saunas. At your service there is also a swimming pool, as well as a bar with a wide selection of drinks.

Spa

If you want to relax and take care of your beauty and health, do not hesitate to visit the Harmony SPA salon, located in the basement of the Gant building. Here you will be offered a wide range of cosmetic procedures and various types of massage. In addition, a beauty salon also operates on the basis of the SPA salon, where you can use the services of a hairdresser, as well as make manicures and pedicures.

Infrastructure for children

If you decide to come to Saimaa Holiday Club with the whole family, then note that there is a kindergarten called "Children's World". It works on a paid basis. In it, you can leave under the strict supervision of educators and nannies children from 1 year. Here your kid will have a great time in games and various developmental activities.





Additional objects

In addition to all of the above, on the territory of Saimaa Holiday Club there is also a Gant brand store (located in the Gant hotel building), a library with a tea corner (located in the Castle building). There are also several conference rooms of various capacities, suitable for various events, both business and ceremonial.

Accommodation cost

As for the pricing policy of Saimaa Holiday Club, it is consistent with the level of service and comfort offered. So, a seven-day accommodation in a double room will cost you more than 45 thousand rubles, in a family room (two children + two adults) - from 51 thousand rubles, in a double room with access to the spa - from 53 thousand rubles, in a family room with access to the spa - from 65 thousand rubles.
