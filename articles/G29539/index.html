How to flash Lenovo A319: instructions

Sometimes our mobile gadgets begin to behave inappropriately. There may be several reasons for this. But most often this behavior is associated with the firmware of the device. For some reason, it was damaged and therefore began to work unstably. In this case, a flashing is required. Gone are the days when you had to go to service centers for this procedure. Now any user can flash the device. Consider the firmware process in more detail. For an example, consider how to flash Lenovo A319.





What kind of apparatus is this?

"Lenovo A319" refers to a budget line of devices with advanced functionality. It copes with all tasks and has high-quality sound. But what is more valuable in it is that it lends itself very well to flashing and customization. Only he, like all devices on the MTK platform, very often has the property of losing IMEI after flashing. But you can restore it very easily. Nevertheless, we pass to the most interesting. How to flash Lenovo A319 through a computer?





PC firmware

Yes, if you install the official firmware from Lenovo, then you can not do without a computer. First of all, you need to download the entire driver package for this gadget. Then we download the firmware program called SP FlashTool and the firmware itself. First you need to install all the drivers and only then move on to the next steps of the process. In some cases, you will have to turn off digital signature verification in Windows. And now we will learn how to flash Lenovo A319.





After installing the drivers, we upload the firmware file to the folder with the downloaded program. Turn off the device and remove the battery from it. Now run SP Flashtool, in the window that opens, look for the "Scatter Loading" button and click on it. Select the firmware file with the extension .txt and the word "scatter" in the name. Next, click on the "Download" button. Now you can connect the turned off smartphone to the PC. The firmware installation starts. The process will be completed when a green circle with the text "OK" appears on the monitor screen. Disconnect the gadget from the PC and reboot it. This is how you can flash Lenovo A319. The instructions are simple and straightforward.
