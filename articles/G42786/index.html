Model positioning

Appearance and design





The Samsung Alpha phone is presented in an aluminum case, even advertising slogans speak of this. Due to this, the model looks really elegant, especially in combination with a narrow metal frame around the display. As for the back cover, it is removable and made of plastic, which by touch resembles leather. Thus, holding the phone is convenient and pleasant.

With navigation, the Samsung Galaxy Alpha is doing great. Traditionally for the manufacturer, the model comes with a central button for minimizing programs, two side buttons "Properties" and "Back", as well as another mechanical screen lock button located on the side panel. It is made of metal, so it‚Äôs nice to use it. In addition, she decently acts, which allows you to press it without much difficulty.

Protect your phone from water and dust

Since we already mentioned the Galaxy S5, it should be said about its important quality - complete protection against water and dust. The manufacturer mentioned this ‚Äútrick‚Äù more than once in its advertising campaign, and, in fact, due to it, the Korean model was positioned as more developed than the iPhone 5S.

In principle, if you think logically, this approach is justified by the fact that the phone is positioned as stylish due to the thin body; the practicality of its use in everyday life is achieved through a metal panel and strong glass on the sensor, and in principle, it does not need a waterproof case. Again, technically isolating such a thin model as a Samsung Alpha phone would probably be impossible.





Display

Interestingly, the Alpha model does not have the huge display that Samsung loves so much. The phone is equipped with a 4.7-inch screen, built on the basis of Amoled technology. If we talk about operating it as a whole, we can say that the display transmits a high-quality picture, preserves color reproduction at different viewing angles, and in general, Samsung Alpha (the live review confirmed this) is quite acceptable for the ‚Äúnon-flagship‚Äù.

On the other hand, if you criticize the model as being sold at the cost of the most advanced smartphone lineup, you can mention PenTile pixel placement technology. Due to its use in the phone screen, the image may not seem so high-quality, especially if you work with Alpha closer than at a distance of 20 centimeters. Simply put, if you bring the display closer to your eyes, you can see pixels.





Samsung Alpha Platform

If we talk about the platform on which the device is developed, it should be said that this is Exynos 5 Octa, which consists of as many as 8 cores. Of these, 4 have a clock frequency of 1.3; and 4 - at 1.8 GHz. With such a basis, of course, the novelty responds quite quickly and dynamically: no slowdowns or freezes in the work with the device were noticed (and it‚Äôs difficult to make the Samsung Galaxy Alpha smartphone buggy). According to the test from the AnTuTu app, the device is even faster than the S5 (which is amazing).





As for the graphics engine, the device uses the Mali-T628 MP6, which is capable of transmitting FullHD graphics. The image quality of the smartphone is up to par.

Alpha Mobile Phone Memory

Revealing the characteristics of the model, it is also necessary to mention this characteristic of the device. According to reviews, Samsung Alpha is not equipped with a card slot; instead, the manufacturer endowed the model with 32 GB of internal memory. Of these, by the way, only 25.4 GB can be used for filling. Is it a lot or a little? Hard to tell. In fact, considering how high-quality image transmission is in a smartphone, how bulky photos taken with a camera can be, you can complain that 25 gigabytes is not enough for a smartphone of this level. However, on the other hand, captured photos can always be downloaded to a computer, and the phone will again get a bunch of free space. Perhaps the developers are right, and more than 32 GB on the phone is not required. Moreover, a fixed amount of memory allows for more accurate optimization of the use of space on the device, which is also important. Another plus - at Samsung Alpha the price will be the same, while a competing company sets a different cost based on the amount of device memory.

Camera

Users also leave feedback about it. Samsung Alpha has two devices for capturing images: a front camera with a resolution of 2 megapixels and a rear camera designed for 12 megapixels. However, the value is not so much the number of pixels as the optimization of the matrix used to create images. And, as a series of photos taken with the Galaxy Alpha shows, everything is fine with the smartphone. Pictures come out great; perhaps the phone can be called one of the most advanced in the market from this point of view.

Alpha, of course, is equipped with a flash. In addition, as in the case of the Galaxy S5, there is a heartbeat sensor next to it, which encourages users to a healthy lifestyle and fitness.

Battery and endurance

The battery capacity of Alpha is, according to official figures, 1860 mAh. If this is not enough, you can purchase a special case for Samsung Alpha, in which an additional battery is built-in to recharge the device. The fact that developers use this particular battery is explained by the small size of the device and, of course, the limited free space under the phone cover.

In general, due to the austerity of charge consumption, as well as due to the optimization of all processes that take place in the device during active operation, according to reviews, Samsung Alpha can last about 11 hours of active use. If we compare, for example, with the iPhone 5S, which has a 1560 mAh battery, then for Samsung such a technical solution is a kind of success. A battery with a capacity of 2800 mAh was installed in the S5, but the processes there are probably less optimized, because the charge keeps the device an order of magnitude less (than Alpha with the same battery could). And so - the phone has a small vertical battery, which, according to the classical scheme, is placed under the cover. You can get it, in fact, at any time.

Model price in the domestic market

The phone has been on sale in the domestic market since September 12 last year. And then, and now it is offered at a price of 24,990 rubles. For this amount, a person gets a stylish, well-balanced business segment phone that can compete in performance with the advanced models of the Korean giant Samsung. It is possible that in small online stores on Samsung Alpha the price will be slightly lower.

Conclusion

So, considering all the factors, the Alpha as a whole leaves a pretty good impression. It is simpler than S5, but not inferior to it in matters of build quality, performance and functionality. Moreover, we can say that the style of the device is ahead of the flagship model. The same is confirmed by the reviews. Samsung Alpha, quite possibly, will become the next round in the history of the development of the Samsung mobile department due to a completely new format - a stylish metal phone of the highest segment. And this, obviously, confirms the high demand for the model, despite the fact that the presentation of the new product has been held for a long time.

If you need a phone that is practical enough for continuous use, but has a stylish look, then this is clearly Samsung Alpha. A review of the device proves this claim!
