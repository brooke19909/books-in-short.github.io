What is the principle of operation of split systems



Usually we used to call wall split systems air conditioners. This type of appliance has become most widespread in apartments and small offices. Mounting them is quite easy, but it is very convenient to use in everyday life. In the process of choosing such a device, you need to pay attention to its power: it should be enough to heat or cool the air, but there should not be too much so that the air conditioner does not wear out too quickly. The equipment of wall-mounted air conditioners includes a number of functions included in the base volume of each manufacturer. Among them can be distinguished, for example, air ventilation mode, timer, remote control, as well as many other features. The principle of operation of split systems is usually the same, but there are nuances associated with it. There are wall-mounted inverter options equipped with an inverter compressor. This type in comparison with others consumes significantly less energy, about 30%. It continues to operate at reduced power even when the required room temperature level is reached. This type of appliance is much less susceptible to wear.

The principle of operation of split systems implies that the bulk of the devices are equipped with the I SEE function. This maintains a specific temperature by scanning a specific area with an infrared counter. After that, the signal is transmitted to the control system, which directs the air stream in the desired direction, deploying the blinds. The principle of operation of split-systems of modern production allows you to distribute air throughout the room evenly, since the flow is released at an angle of 150 degrees.



The basic design of wall-mounted air conditioners involves the use of the principle of fuzzy logic, which implies automatic temperature control. It turns out that he himself is able to determine environmental conditions in order to "adapt" to them. The consumer has the opportunity to adjust the temperature using the remote control.

Considering the principle of operation of an air conditioner split system, we can say about what types of these devices exist. This will help to understand what their differences are. The most common wall options that exist in a wide range of power. A column split system is a device designed to cool a large area.





It is attached to the floor, and is made in the form of a column with a diameter of 50 cm. Floor-and-ceiling solutions allow you to place them as you like, harmoniously fitting into the interior design. The cassette type split systems are attached to the false ceiling. Usually they are used in supermarkets or stores. As mentioned earlier, the principle of operation of split systems is similar, but differs from the operation of conventional air conditioners.

This type of device fits perfectly into any type of interior due to the increased popularity, especially in recent years. And if we take into account the principle of operation of the split system, it becomes clear that this is the optimal solution to ensure comfortable conditions in the home.
