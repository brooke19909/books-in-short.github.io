Where to look for a beach? Amenities for travelers

Goa is a place that most tourists associate with beach holidays, because the coast of the island is rightfully considered one of the most beautiful in the world. It is easy to guess that the hotel is located next to the famous Varca beach. Regenta Resort Varca Beach 4 * (reviews of tourists confirm this) has a favorable location, because the hotel territory extends almost to the coast.

To soak up in the rays of the gentle sun, to bathe in warm water is always a pleasure. Nevertheless, many tourists prefer to spend time more actively.
