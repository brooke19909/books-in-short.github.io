Master class: funny penguin from a plastic bottle

In order to make an original penguin garden figurine, you will need the following tools and materials:

  • 2 liter plastic bottle;
  • acrylic paints (black, white, yellow);
  • toy eyes;
  • black cardboard;
  • glue.


So, consider how to make a penguin from a plastic bottle. Take a transparent container, wash it and clean it of paper labels. Now pour black paint into it and close the bottle with a lid. Shake the plastic container lightly so that the paint gets on all the walls of the container. After that, open the bottle and leave it alone for a while.

Charming crafts made from plastic bottles: do-it-yourself penguins

While our workpiece dries, we will take care of the wings of the penguin. We cut out two pieces of oval shape from thick black cardboard. These will be the wings. Now take white acrylic paint and draw on the outside of the bottle the abdomen of a bird. Yellow paint decorate the beak and paws. Glue the prepared wings. That's all, the penguin is almost ready. It remains to attach toy eyes to the bird's face. At will, you can decorate the craft with sequins, beads or fabric. Now you know how to make a penguin from a plastic bottle. In fact, this is not at all difficult. Even a child can make such a craft, of course, under the supervision of an adult.

Charming penguin garden figure . With our own hands we make out a summer cottage

We present to your attention another simple technology for the manufacture of crafts "penguins from plastic bottles." To create it, you should prepare such consumables:

  • a bottle;
  • cotton balls;
  • black and orange cardboard;
  • toy eyes;
  • black feathers;
  • a red or orange piece of fabric;
  • acrylic paint;
  • glue.

How to make a penguin from a plastic bottle, we will tell further. First, paint the container with black acrylic paint on one side - this will be the back of the penguin. Then take cotton balls and place them in a bottle. Through transparent plastic fluffy pieces of cotton wool will be clearly visible. They will make the abdomen of the bird very realistic. Then we perform the wings of the penguin - cut out oval blanks from black cardboard and glue them on the sides of the bottle. Let's make a muzzle - we attach toy eyes to the lid of the container, and we make a nose out of an orange piece of fabric. From black feathers we will make a "crest". That's all - the fun and cute craft from a plastic bottle is ready. Show your imagination and decorate your penguin as you wish. You can glue sequins, beads, buttons, tie a bow or scarf, and also make a funny hat from a regular old sock.

We make a large penguin from a 10-liter plastic eggplant: the necessary tools and materials

If you want to decorate your garden, make beautiful crafts from large plastic bottles. Penguins from large containers are very interesting. Such products will decorate a stone or alpine hill and perfectly fit into any landscape. So, let's look at how to make a penguin out of plastic bottles. To make one big bird, you need two plastic containers, one with a capacity of 10 liters, and the second - 1.5 liters. It is also necessary to prepare a sharp knife, acrylic paints (black, white and red), sand to weight the bottle and a wooden peg for the beak.

Funny plastic garden figure

So, how to make a penguin from a plastic bottle with a volume of 10 liters: take the prepared container and cut off the neck, but so that the narrowing part remains. Pour some sand into the container (for weighting). To make a head, use a 1.5 liter bottle. Cut it in half and insert the part with the neck into the "body" of the bird. Now fasten the details: make an awl heated with a fire or a nail to make holes in the plastic and thread the wire through them. Make a beak from a pre-ground wooden peg. Insert it into the neck of a smaller bottle. After making the frame, you can start painting the penguin. Black paint the back and wings. Red paws and make a beak. White acrylic paint the abdomen. After completing work, allow the product to dry. That's all, the charming penguin is ready.

You can look for him a place in the garden. From smaller bottles, you can make kids - as a result, you get a family of funny and smart penguins. Good luck with your creative experiments!
