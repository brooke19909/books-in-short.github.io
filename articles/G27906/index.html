Hotels in the center of Kazan with inexpensive breakfast: an overview of the best hotels, description, photos and reviews

Are you interested in hotels in the center of Kazan inexpensively and with breakfast? In the article you can get an answer to this intriguing question. Read more about where to stay in the third capital of Russia, read on.

Grand Hotel in Kazan

Located on St. Petersburg, house 1.

This is a chic hotel, huge banquet rooms, marble stairs. The cost of a single room is 4,250 rubles. with breakfast. If this room accommodates two, its price will increase to 5,750 rubles. Guests are provided with a double bed, a desk for work, a compartment for storing valuables. The private bathroom includes a washbasin, shower, towels, hygiene products and a hairdryer.

Room for two people with two single beds costs 5,750 rubles. (with breakfast), it also has a work desk, chairs, TV, wireless Internet, air conditioning. Also for the convenience of guests there is a telephone, bath, toilet, clean towels, hairdryer. The rooms are clean, renovated recently, beautiful bedspreads, curtains on the floor, white tiles in the bathroom.

A suite costs 12,000 rubles., Breakfast is included in this price, Wi-Fi is available. The room consists of several rooms: bedroom, living room, bath and toilet. There is an armchair and TV, minibar, telephone. The bathroom has all the necessary accessories, as well as clean towels, a hairdryer, slippers and a bathrobe.

Hotel rating is 8.9 out of 10 points according to the famous tourist portal. The road to the city center and train station does not take much time, the distance is about 1.5 km. The hotel has a sauna and gym, and offers panoramic views from its windows.

The main minus, according to the guests, is a buffet with a modest assortment of dishes and the fact that the windows cannot be opened.

AMAKS Safar Hotel

Located on Odnostoronka Grivka street, 1.

Room for one person with breakfast costs 2 295 rub. Guests have at their disposal one bed, wireless internet. In addition to the bed, there is a mini-sofa, a table, a shower, a toilet, as well as a work table, a refrigerator, a telephone and an armchair. The room is very clean, fresh and spacious, the administration provides slippers, clean towels and a hairdryer.

A room for two people costs 3,060 rubles. (with breakfast). It has 2 beds, there is Wi-Fi, a mirror, a bedside table and a work desk. There is also a shower, toilet, cooler in the common corridor.

A family or company of three can stay in a room for 4,080 rubles, which already includes breakfast. In addition to three beds, it has a work table, clothes rack, telephone, chairs. Guests are provided with clean towels and bed linen. The room has a shower, toilet, slippers, hairdryer, fridge and minibar.

The hotel rating is 8.5 out of 10. Check-in at the hotel is possible from two in the afternoon, check-out before lunch. In case you order a late departure (from 14:00 to 18:00), payment will be hourly. Leaving after six in the evening, be prepared to pay the full cost of the room per day.

According to visitors' reviews, everything is fine, there is billiards, a sauna, a disco, a bowling alley. Of the minuses, guests note that the building is tall, you need to wait for the elevator, the furniture is outdated, the old plumbing, although everything is clean and tidy.

Hotel "Tatarstan"

The modern hotel building is located at: Pushkin street, house 4.

The cost of living with breakfast in a double room is 3,200 rubles. It has a large bed and a view of the historic city center. The room has a shower, toilet, desk and chairs. On the floor in a dedicated room you can find a microwave, cooler and iron.

Hotel rating is quite high - 8.9 points out of 10. Check-in from 14:00, check-out before noon. The hotel is located in the city center, the distance to the railway station is about 1.4 km. You can order lunch at a cost of 300 rubles, dinner at a price of 400 rubles. Payment for accommodation at the hotel accept both cash and plastic cards.

Hotel Chaliapin Palace

You can book an inexpensive hotel in the center of Kazan with breakfast at the following address: Universitetskaya Street, 7/80. A double room costs from 3 800 rubles per day, in the presence of a large double or two separate beds, a TV, a table and chairs. The bathroom has a toilet, bathtub, bathrobe, personal hygiene products and slippers.

Spacious junior suite with breakfast costs from 5,850 rubles. It has a balcony with views of the pedestrian street. For lovers of comfort and luxury, the hotel offers a presidential suite - rich four-room apartments at a cost of 42,000 rubles per night.

The hotel's rating based on guest reviews is 9.3 out of 10. The hotel guests liked the rich interior, Baroque restaurant, interesting multi-level stairs, delicious food, good service. Guests also have free access to the sauna, pool and gym.

Park Inn by Radisson in Kazan

In the center of the city, the Kazan hotel can be booked inexpensively and with breakfast at the following address: Lesgaft street, 7.

The cost of a double room is 7,200 rubles per day, breakfast is included. For a comfortable stay in the room there is a double large bed, a work desk, a TV, a coffee machine. There is a private bathroom with a shower, hairdryer and hygiene items. All interior items are new and modern, the hotel has recently been renovated.

The hotel rating is 8.9 out of 10 points. The distance from it to the city center is 2.7 km, to the railway station - 2.5 km.
