"Rostoshinsky ponds" - the embodiment of comfort

The cottage village "Rostoshinsky Ponds" (Orenburg) is located near the city. Only three kilometers separate the territory of the village from urban infrastructure.

To the Zagorodny highway of Orenburg, enveloping the city on the east side, there is an Autodromnaya street leading to the Rostoshi district. After driving only three kilometers along this street, you can get into the cottage village "Rostoshinsky Ponds". This location of the village does not prevent its residents from being considered urban and having a city residence permit.
