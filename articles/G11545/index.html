Balearic Islands: Magaluf, Mallorca

When the resort of Magaluf is called the "pearl of the Mediterranean", this is not a big exaggeration. The flow of tourists coming here from all over the world confirms this opinion. The Balearic Islands, located near the Spanish coast - a fabulous place. And one of the best resorts on the islands is, of course, Magaluf. Mallorca annually hosts a lot of people seeking it here. Such a reputation is expensive. And she is well deserved.



Palma de Mallorca, Magaluf

What is this popularity based on? Mainly on the positive reviews of those who have rested on the coast of the Gulf of La Palma before. This is a modern resort complex with a highly developed tourism industry and transport communications. The international airport is fifteen kilometers from here. This fact allows you to easily get to Palma Nova and Magaluf. Mallorca is famous for its coast with endless beaches and entertainment venues, but these two resorts traditionally attract the most attention, regardless of the season. In fact, these two resorts have long merged into one, their borders are very arbitrary, and they are separated only by a small rocky cape.

In order not to be disappointed in this place, you should know in advance what you want to find here and why you came to Magaluf. Mallorca has many excellent vacation spots in any season of the year, but not all of them are the same. And the resort of Magaluf is widely famous for the fact that it is usually noisy and fun here. And the fun often goes on all night long. With dancing, music, loud laughter and nightly swims along the coast. Needless to say, not everyone is happy with this circumstance.


Many noted that in the neighboring Palma Nova, the situation is much calmer and quite favorable for a comfortable family vacation. But if your soul does not crave calm, but scope, fun and a dose of healthy adrenaline, then the right direction for you is Magaluf, Mallorca, the Balearic Islands. Your expectations will not be deceived.

How to plan your vacation wisely

Any normal person looks forward to vacation, sea, sun and tropical greenery outside the hotel window. And all this is abundant here. But an orthodox beach holiday has the amazing property that it is able to quickly tire of its monotony. And even such a luxurious resort as Magaluf, Mallorca can tire slightly. A map of the island is available at any hotel, you just need to take it and plan a short trip to the famous island, where you have been destined for tourist destinations.

Mandatory to visit the capital of the island - Palma de Mallorca. This is the oldest city founded even before our era during the heyday of the Roman Empire. A visit here is not just a great shopping trip and a walk through the many small souvenir shops abundantly located on old streets. This is a kind of walk through the well-preserved medieval past past the towers of an ancient fortress and Gothic cathedrals. A short visit here from Magaluf is like a short time travel.
