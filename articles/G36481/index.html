Location and Specialization

Hotel Alinn Sarigerme Boutique is located near the center of the resort village of Sarigerme. This is a small village in the south-west of Turkey, on the shores of the Mediterranean, but still a little bit of the Aegean. We can say that it is located at the confluence of both water areas. If we talk about administrative division, then Sarigerme belongs to the Ortaka district of Mugla province. But it is also customary to attribute this resort to the zone of influence of the city of Fethiye. Some even believe that this is the Marmaris area - at least, such information can sometimes be found on tourist sites. There are family-friendly hotels in Sarigerme, but Alinn Sarigerme Boutique has a completely different specialization. Guests who are under the age of eighteen are not accommodated in this boutique. So if you do not like children's screeching and splashing around the pool, here you are. You also need to consider the fact that the animation is the most minimal. Muffled music plays in the bars, everything is aimed at a quiet and relaxing holiday. Fans of noisy parties should look for another resort, closer to Izmir.





Where to walk

Sarigerme is a fairly small village surrounded by mountains with coniferous forests and olive groves. The former fishing village has a post office, a local market and, of course, the main shopping street. Shopaholics will be somewhat disappointed, but in vain. Here you can also get some basic necessities and beach accessories - bathing suits, towels, textiles. Hotel Alinn Sarigerme Boutique is located close to the center of the village, on a high sea shore. On the way to the beach, you'll walk past chain hotels like Iberotel, Magic Life, and Robinson. As everywhere in Turkey, there is no shortage of souvenir shops, but no one will pester you on the beach - local residents do not wander along the coast, praising their goods. Almost all hotels in Sarigerme are five-star boutiques.

When to go to Sarigerme

Those who have been on the Anatolian coast of Turkey are probably familiar with the hot and humid summers of the Mediterranean. And although strictly geographically the resort lies on the shore of the second water area, the climate there is the same as in western Turkey. A cool breeze from the Aegean Sea blows all summer , softening the tropical heat. However, the waves sometimes raise serious waves.
