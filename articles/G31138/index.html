Reviews: Touch Bank. Banking services

Today we will find out which Touch Bank is receiving feedback. In addition, you need to get to know this organization better. After all, this company is becoming more and more popular among the population. And although there is not much information available about it, people are not too afraid to trust Touch Bank. Why does the organization so attract customers? What services does it offer? Is it really worth contacting if necessary? Numerous reviews received by Touch Bank will help to understand all this. Maybe this is a really useful and conscientious organization!



Description

What is Touch Bank? This is a modern banking company, which conducts its activities mainly through the Internet. That is, virtually. Touch Bank does not have a huge number of branches - only the head office in Moscow. Maybe it is because of this that some reviews express doubt about the company's integrity. After all, any virtual banks are not credible. They often turn out to be the most common deception.

But Touch Bank, as indicated on the official website, operates under the license of a large banking organization, OTP Bank. That is, a financial institution is a kind of affiliate. OTP Bank should be responsible for Touch Bank. For this reason, organizations can truly be trusted. After all, if you ask OTP Bank, it turns out that Touch actually works under their license. In fact, there is no concern that Touch Bank is a non-existent company.

Services

What activities does the organization conduct? It is already clear that this is a real bank. But only Touch Bank fully exists on the Internet and has a single office. As already mentioned, this is a virtual organization.



But she provides the services exactly the same as the most ordinary bank. What kind of reviews does Touch Bank receive for its services? Mostly positive. After all, you can:

  • Open a bank account.
  • Make a contribution.
  • Get a loan.
  • Issue a debit card.
  • Get a credit card.
  • Use insurance services.
  • Connect to the bonus program.

The only thing that cannot be done here is to make a currency exchange. Not too bad news, given that we are dealing with a virtual bank. Such a service simply physically cannot be realized. As you can see, all Touch Bank services are similar to those that exist in real banks. Only numerous reviews indicate that loans and bank cards are in great demand. Almost every client mentions them.
