Hotel Amwaj Blue Beach Resort Spa (Egypt / Soma Bay). Photos and reviews of tourists

The Amwaj-Blue-Beach-Resort SPA complex was opened in 2010 in the resort town of Soma Bay. The hotel accommodates more than 1,200 tourists. This is one of the few boarding houses in Egypt where you can relax with body and soul. A large SPA center with a variety of beauty treatments is open on site. Relaxing at the hotel, guests can combine a pleasant and useful stay: play sports, attend wellness treatments and bask on the beach. To get to the hotel Amwaj-Blue-Beach-Resort-SPA is possible from the airport of Hurghada (45 km).





This is a real oasis in the middle of a sandy beach. Here the animation program is well thought out for both children and adults. Each performance is held with interesting contests and jokes. The contingent of the complex mainly consists of couples with children, since for the kids the hotel has everything for an interesting and fun pastime.

Beach

The Amwaj-Blue-Beach-Resort-SPA tourist complex has a separate beach area, which is 260 meters from the hotel. All the amenities on the coast are free. The beach is sandy with convenient and safe access to the sea. Until the evening on the beach, there is Sun Set, a restaurant where you can try delicious and juicy grilled dishes.

An outdoor pool with sun loungers and awnings is built on the equipped hotel grounds. Near the pool there is a massage room and a bar. Sports activities: aerobics, fitness room, golf, futsal, darts, tennis court, volleyball, diving, billiards, bocca, camel riding, horse riding and jet skiing .
