PrimaSol Sineva Park 3 *, Bulgaria, Sveti Vlas: overview, features and reviews

The sea, beaches, healing sea air and exciting entertainment are just what a person needs from time to time to take a break from hard work and daily routine. And as a refuge, tourists often choose sunny Bulgaria, which can provide a really comfortable and fun pastime.

There are many hotels on the Bulgarian coast. One of the popular places is the Primasol Sineva Park 3 * hotel . Of course, before the trip, every traveler tries to find the maximum amount of data about a particular place. So where exactly is the hotel located and is it far from it to the sea? Is it suitable for families with children? What conditions can be expected? What reviews do travelers leave about the hotel? Answers to these questions will help many readers.

Where is the hotel located?





Naturally, first of all, people are interested in the question of exactly where the Primasol Sineva Park 3 * hotel is located . Bulgaria, St. Vlas (a cozy town by the sea) - it was here in 2000 that this hotel was built. It is located on the second beach line, but, nevertheless, the coast can be reached quickly. The distance to the center of the town is only a kilometer, and therefore you can have fun and enjoy the active spa life. By the way, about the same distance to the famous Sunny Beach.

The international airport is located about 42 km, but you should not worry about the distance, as tourists, as a rule, are provided with a comfortable transfer. The hotel itself is a large three-story building. Its territory is quite spacious, clean and nicely furnished. By the way, in 2014 there was a major overhaul, after which the complex became even more comfortable and attractive.

Hotel Primasol Sineva Park 3 * (Bulgaria, Sveti Vlas): room description





Living conditions are an extremely important point, because at the end of the day you want to relax and unwind in a comfortable, clean room. The hotel offers its customers a settlement in one of 92 spacious rooms of various capacities. But no matter what category you choose, you can count on a spacious, clean room, furnished with stylish furniture and equipped with modern appliances. And almost everyone has access to a private terrace or balcony.

You can count on a standard set of household appliances, without which it is difficult to imagine a truly comfortable stay. In particular, you will have a powerful air conditioning system, a TV (you can watch local and satellite channels), as well as a telephone, a safe and a minibar (filled with water, all other drinks are delivered for a fee). In the bathroom you can always freshen up in the shower. There is a toilet, a mirror, hygiene items, clean towels and a hair dryer (not in all rooms).

Catering for hotel guests





At the territory of Primasol Sineva Park 3 * hotel there is a good restaurant where you can eat and just relax in a pleasant setting. Paying for accommodation, travelers can independently choose a convenient food system for them, whether it be only breakfasts, breakfasts and dinners. Guests can even refuse to eat at the hotel, but visit the restaurant, paying each bill separately.

Do not forget that in the resort village there are many restaurants, bars and cafes (including establishments located on the seashore). Here, every tourist will be able to choose exactly the place that he really likes. And in the local market and in shops you can buy various products, fresh fruits, etc. Food in Sunny Beach will not be a problem.

The coast here is covered with sand and pebbles, as well as descent into the sea, and therefore tourists are advised to acquire suitable shoes for greater convenience and safety.

The beach here is public, which means you have to pay for the use of sun loungers, umbrellas, beach towels. By the way, the prices here are not so high. There is a volleyball court on the shore. The sea here is mostly calm, and therefore this place is perfect for recreation not only for adult travelers, but also for children.

Water activities

Can the guests of Primasol Sineva Park 3 * hotel count on water entertainments? Bulgaria is a tourist country that has become popular precisely due to the opportunity to relax and have fun on the picturesque beaches.

Naturally, there are sports centers on public beaches where people can rent a catamaran or boat, book a boat trip. Here you can ride a banana, water skiing, parasailing and sailing. Windsurfing enthusiasts have no less fun. Naturally, on the shore you can rent the necessary equipment and even take a few lessons from real professionals.

Does the hotel offer additional services?





Of course, hotel guests can count on additional service. There is a laundry and dry cleaning service (paid separately), a currency exchange office. If necessary, you can rent a safe from the administrator, where your things will be safe. Also, guests have the right to use the hotel parking. Here (or in any other place of the city) you can rent a convenient car or any other type of transport, which greatly speeds up the movement around the neighborhood and opens up new opportunities for travel.

In case of health problems, guests can count on the help of a qualified doctor.

There is constant wireless Internet access, which is very convenient for any modern traveler.

How do guests spend their free time?





In terms of entertainment, the hotel also has something to offer the tourist. In the courtyard there is a spacious outdoor pool with a terrace. Here, guests bathe, sunbathe, organize various competitions and just chat. Tennis courts are at your disposal, next to which you can rent balls and rackets. You can have a good time playing billiards or table tennis.

A golf course is also available for guests, as well as a volleyball court. The health center has a well-equipped gym, sauna and jacuzzi, as well as massage rooms. A beauty salon is constantly working, the presence of which women will enjoy. And do not forget about sightseeing holidays, because Bulgaria is a beautiful country with an interesting history and culture.

Children's leisure on site

Parents prefer to spend holidays with their children, which means that it is important for them to have certain conditions for a comfortable stay with their child. What can the Primasol Sineva Park 3 * hotel offer? St. Vlas is a great place for a family holiday, as here both adult tourists and their children will find entertainment.





As for the hotel itself, here you can count on some additional services. For a small fee, a rollaway bed will be delivered to your room. In the courtyard there is a spacious but shallow pool, which is perfect for relaxing children of all ages. There is also a well-equipped playground with swings, as well as a games room with a variety of toys and attractions. By the way, teachers and animators are involved with children here, so little tourists simply have no time to get bored.

Hotel Primasol Sineva Park 3 * (Bulgaria): traveler reviews

Surely every tourist knows that communicating with people who have already managed to visit a particular hotel can be very informative. So what do travelers say about the Primasol Sineva Park 3 * hotel complex? Reviews are mostly positive. Here, tourists can enjoy a beautiful view of the sea, the cleaned territory and, of course, the healing sea air, thanks to which thousands of tourists come to the Bulgarian coast.

The rooms, according to the guests, are comfortable and clean, and in the restaurant you can eat delicious food at any time. The hotel has a favorable location, because it is easy to get to the busy center from here, but in the evening you can spend peace and quiet. The road to the sea is convenient, and therefore daily walks for travelers are not at all a burden.

According to the reviews of the guests of the complex, the Primasol Sineva Park 3 * hotel really offers a high level of service. The hotel staff is polite, attentive and welcoming. And almost all employees speak Russian fluently, which is convenient. Moreover, the prices for holidays here are not so high, when compared with other hotels. Therefore, travelers recommend this place for a fun beach holiday.
