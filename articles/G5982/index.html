The recreation center "Friendship"

The recreation center "Friendship" in Illichivsk is a full-fledged budget vacation for picky tourists who prefer the proximity of the sea and good food to the comfort of rooms with a pool. This place is located on the very shore of the sea in the buildings of Soviet times, which have undergone updating. To accommodate guests of the recreation center "Friendship" there are apartments and rooms of 4 categories. The rooms of the 1st category and apartments have everything you need for a comfortable stay. The remaining types of rooms are dwellings with limited or minimal amenities.

Services:

  • Change of linen 1 time in 5 days.
  • Paid private parking.
  • The provision of personal care products once every 5 days.
  • Free wireless internet.
  • Hot and cold water.
  • Optional: catering 3 times a day, to choose from.

Organization of excursion program is possible. Children under 5 years old stay free of charge. On the beach there is a lot of entertainment.

Holiday Village "Falcon"

The recreation center "Falcon" in Illichivsk is located in the suburbs, 250 meters from its own beach. This is a 3-storey building with rooms for 2-4 people. The rooms of the main building have all the conditions for a comfortable stay. And in wooden houses - the minimum conditions for relaxation. These rooms can be equipped with a fridge and TV for an additional fee.



Guests of the recreation center "Falcon" are offered 3 meals a day on a fee basis. There is a rental of boats and fishing equipment. For lovers of outdoor activities there is a sports ground. Free parking is available on site.

Sanatorium "Seagull"

The recreation center "The Seagull" in Illichivsk is a sanatorium-type recreation complex. The base is located on a hill near the sea in the suburbs. It has a large adjacent green area. For the settlement of holidaymakers in comfortable conditions there is a main building with 2-bed standard rooms, there are separate summer houses with different configurations.

A mandatory requirement for vacationers in the "Seagull" is a certificate of vaccination.



This is not an ordinary recreation facility. Here you can take a course of sanatorium and preventive treatment for a number of diseases:

  • musculoskeletal system;
  • gynecology;
  • injuries of various etiologies;
  • metabolism;
  • gastrointestinal diseases;
  • respiratory system;
  • liver and kidney.

The recreation center has its own rooms:

  • acupuncture;
  • Massage
  • inhalation;
  • physiotherapy exercises;
  • mud therapy;
  • paraffin treatment;
  • gynecologist;
  • the dentist.

There are facilities for making oxygen cocktails. Nutrition is carried out in accordance with the prescribed diet. For those who wish there is always mineral water and special decoctions of herbs.



There are restaurants and cafes with a common menu on site. Nearby there are entertainment venues, night clubs. The recreation center provides rental for catamarans and water scooters. For children there are rides and water slides on the beach. There is a private car park. The recreation center "The Seagull" is a combination of a pleasant vacation at sea with useful wellness procedures.

Of course, in this city there are many other places for spending holidays. Everyone chooses a wallet to their taste. One thing is for sure: a vacation in Ilyichevsk (whose recreation facilities will allow us to spend time with benefit and pleasure) will appeal to anyone.
