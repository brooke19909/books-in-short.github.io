Sanatorium "White Nights" (Sestroretsk): photos and reviews

The Leningrad region, although rich in swamps, has plenty of various places of recreation (such as hotels, motels, camp sites). Especially near the coast. One of such establishments popular among Petersburgers is the White Nights sanatorium (Sestroretsk), where visitors with cardiac abnormalities are primarily expected. Here, in the resort area of ​​St. Petersburg, near the coast of the Gulf of Finland, you can relax and heal the shattered health.

Location

The sanatorium "White Nights" (Sestroretsk) is located in the Leningrad Region, near St. Petersburg, almost in its suburbs. The institution itself is located on a large island that divides the Sestra River into two branches, next to the Dunes sanatorium.

Favorable climatic conditions for recreation creates a large forest park with a large number of conifers and sea air in the Gulf of Finland. The picturesque landscape, in addition, give numerous canals and rivers. The White Nights sanatorium is located in Sestroretsk very conveniently, as the proximity to St. Petersburg ensures a constant influx of customers. The ecological situation here is very favorable for healing.





The facility is located on the 38th kilometer of Primorsky highway in the city of Sestroretsk near St. Petersburg. Telephone for information (812) 437–31–93.

Area of ​​activity

The sanatorium “White Nights” (Sestroretsk), founded in May 1988, is able to receive patients with the widest range of pathologies, thanks to a powerful medical and preventive base. The institution offers a whole range of programs for the rehabilitation and recovery of patients with cardiovascular diseases, disorders of the musculoskeletal system, for the treatment of digestive and nervous organs, for which 220 sanatoriums have been opened in the cardiology, therapeutic and neurological department. Particular emphasis is placed on the early diagnosis of diseases and non-drug treatment. In addition to traditional complexes, in recent years, relatively new programs have been popular:

  • “Medical therapy”, “Anti-tobacco”, “Healthy spine”, lasting for two weeks;
  • "Antistress" and "Overweight - not for me", designed for ten days;
  • “Healthy liver”, including recovery from three to six days;
  • "Landmark" - for four days;
  • “Two days of beauty and health”;
  • "Week of beauty and health."

Additional service

In addition to the high level of room service and their good equipment, the White Nights sanatorium (Sestroretsk) offers guests during their leisure hours a visit to the cinema and concert hall, where attention is presented to interesting news of Russian rental, or to the dance hall, where discos are regularly held.

Fans of outdoor activities can compete in a table or tennis tournament in an indoor warm gym or swim in their own twenty-five-meter pool, there is also a gym. A small beauty salon with a hairdresser and a manicure office offers its services. You can take a steam bath in the sauna or play billiards, go for a massage or work out with therapeutic exercises with a specialist.





The sanatorium also has a conservatory, a library, a cafe and a small bar, its own shop. You can use unlimited wireless internet. There is a rental of skis, Finnish sledges and bicycles, depending on the season, there are badminton and volleyball courts.

Room Categories

Health resort "White Nights" is a sanatorium (Sestroretsk), which is a six-story brick building, equipped quite modern and convenient.

For patients, several accommodation options are offered, depending on the number of people:

  • one room for one person, equipped with a bathroom with a bathtub and a TV;
  • one room for two with a double bed;
  • two-room "Suite" for two people consists of a living room, equipped with a set of upholstered furniture, and a bedroom with a wide berth;
  • three-room suite for two guests, includes a living room with a sofa and armchairs; in the first room - a single bed and upholstered furniture; in the bedroom there is a double bed and a TV; the bathroom is large, separate.

Five meals a day are offered, including the option to order a therapeutic diet (pre-ordered) No. 5, 8, 9, 10 and 15.





Medical services

Many Petersburgers seek to visit the White Nights health resort (sanatorium). Sestroretsk is located just 38 kilometers from the northern capital, that is, you can get here very quickly and easily, and the climatic features - being in a large pine park - contribute to the saturation of the air with useful ions.

Together with the natural factor, a whole range of medical procedures and studies is offered. Patients with cardiovascular pathologies can have an ECG done in a timely manner. Laboratory studies will allow you to control the correctness of the prescribed procedures. If necessary, exercise therapy and other physiotherapeutic procedures are prescribed: massage (both traditional and underwater), inhalations, aerophytotherapy, therapeutic swimming, electrotherapy, applying special mud, visiting a salt cave, warming up with paraffin and ozokerite.

Additionally, patients can undergo a bowel examination in the monitoring room, undergo oxygenation in the high-pressure chamber, or under conditions of discharged (artificial "mountain") air. There are thalasso and hirudotherapy rooms, a dry carbonic bath and an infrared sauna. You can visit the solarium, dentist or beautician.

The presence of contraindications to any procedure is considered individually.





Natural factor of recovery

It will be useful for a person even without special health problems to visit the White Nights sanatorium (Leningrad Region). Sestroretsk is considered a resort place due to its natural features, one of which is the healing mud of local lakes, related to silt.

Its beneficial effect on the body consists of mechanical and biochemical factors, as well as biostimulating and antibiotic effects. Mud procedures are prescribed for problems with the musculoskeletal system or diseases of the peripheral nervous system, after which there is an improvement in metabolism and the intensity of inflammation decreases. The healing power of the air determines the proximity of the sea bay and the pine massif.

As an additional therapeutic factor, we can distinguish favorable climatic conditions of a moderate continental nature, in which mild winters and non-hot summers are successfully combined.

Leisure activities

In addition to medical procedures, the emotional mood of patients is very important. Therefore, the sanatorium "White Nights" (St. Petersburg / Sestroretsk) offers residents an extensive cultural program. You can go on a sightseeing tour of the palaces and suburbs of St. Petersburg or a tour of the picturesque places of the Karelian Isthmus. On the territory of the sanatorium, evenings with dance programs are arranged, films and concerts are offered to the attention of vacationers. Sometimes they organize meetings with popular artists. There is an opportunity for holding various seminars, conferences and business meetings. It offers residents a cafeteria and bar, a beauty salon and a hairdresser, a pharmacy and a shop, as well as a parking lot. The territory of the sanatorium is promptly cleaned and ennobled.





Organization of spa treatment

Being in resorts of this type is good because it combines the elements of healing and relaxation, that is, therapeutic measures are carried out gently and unobtrusively and are perceived by patients more kindly than in hospital walls. For many patients, referral to a sanatorium is a continuation of medical therapy in a specialized institution.

The complex of measures at the White Nights institution (sanatorium) consists in organizing close medical supervision, advising specialists of various fields, round-the-clock medical services by nurses, prescribing diagnostic and treatment procedures according to the health resort card and the patient's condition.

additional information

For placement in the sanatorium, it is necessary to provide an identity document (passport - for an adult, for a child - a birth certificate), and medical papers (spa card, ticket and policy of compulsory medical insurance). Children are accepted only over the age of four, always accompanied by their parents.

Check-in usually occurs in the morning at 8.00, check-out at 20.00. If there is no personal vehicle, you can get to the institution from St. Petersburg in two ways: from the Finland railway station to Sestroretsk station, then change to bus number 310 (go to the ring), or from the Staraya Derevnya metro station in St. Petersburg by bus to the stop "38th kilometer of Primorsky highway".





Should I go to the resort "White Nights"

After all, an institution for treatment and rest over a long time of work has gained extensive experience and material base, which allows us to successfully carry out therapeutic and preventive measures and help people with a wide range of pathologies of varying degrees of development.

In general, the opinion about the rest at the White Nights institution (sanatorium) is positive, although there is some dissatisfaction with the service and equipment that does not always work, but you can find a reason for dissatisfaction in the five-star hotel. We can say that the price-quality ratio is fully justified.





Thus, the majority of visitors to the White Nights sanatorium (Sestroretsk) leave positive reviews, as the variety of treatment methods is complemented by their location in a picturesque area. The rooms are quite comfortable and equipped with everything necessary.
