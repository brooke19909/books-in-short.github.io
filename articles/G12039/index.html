Hotel Eftalia Marin resort 5 * Hotel (Turkey, Alanya): photos and reviews of tourists

If you like to relax in Turkey and are looking for a comfortable five-star hotel on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea, then as an option for your stay we suggest considering Ephtalia Marine Resort.



Location, general information, photo

This hotel is located in the resort area of Turkey Alanya, four kilometers from the village of Konakli. Antalya International Airport is 100 kilometers away. In the vicinity of the hotel there are lots of shops, souvenir shops, restaurants, bars, tour agencies and other tourist sites.

Eftalia Marin Resort 5 * (Alanya) was opened in 2014. The area is nearly forty thousand square meters.

The housing stock consists of 491 comfortable rooms of the categories Standard, Standard Large, Standard Steam-Up, Family, Duplex. The hotel also has five apartments, specially equipped for people with disabilities.

Eating at Ephtalia Marine is organized according to the ultra ol inclusive system. The hotel has a main restaurant (buffet) for 1250 people, two a la carte restaurants and several bars (including by the pools and on the beach).

The hotel has two outdoor and two indoor pools, children's pools, sun terraces, a water park, a spa, a fitness center, an amphitheater, sports grounds, a water entertainment center, a mini club and much more. In the daytime and evening, guests are invited to an animation program, in the evenings discos are held. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel.

In general, according to travelers from Russia, this hotel is perfect for both family and youth recreation.





Eftalia Marin Resort 5 *: traveler reviews

Since the hotel becomes a kind of second home for tourists during their overseas holidays (especially when it comes to the all-inclusive concept), it is very important not to make a mistake in choosing it. After all, if the hotel does not suit you, then this can spoil all the impressions of the long-awaited vacation. In this regard, travelers try to learn in advance as much as possible about the hotels they like. An invaluable source of information is the reviews of those who have already stayed in one place or another. After all, former guests try to tell in all details both about the advantages and disadvantages of a particular hotel, which allows you to make the right choice. Today we invite you to get acquainted with the opinion of Russian tourists about the hotel "Eftalya Marine Resort 5 *" in Alanya. Just note that the hotel has a very decent rating (4.4 points out of five possible), which suggests that most of the guests were very pleased with how they spent time here.



Rooms

Our compatriots were pleased with the apartments offered to tourists at the Eftalia Marin Resort 5 * hotel, a photo of which can be seen in this article. Judging by their comments, the rooms here are spacious, newly renovated, comfortable, with quality furniture and appliances. Guests also note that upon check-in, the administrator at the reception often offers for a small surcharge to stay in a room of a higher category. Of course, this can only be expected if the hotel will have available apartments at the time of your arrival.

In general, in the rooms, according to vacationers, there is everything you need: a hairdryer, a safe, comfortable large beds, well-functioning air conditioning, a TV with Russian-language channels, and spacious furnished balconies with nice views. There is also free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel. True, according to our compatriots, the Internet does not catch well in all rooms.



Cleaning service

Complaints to the work of the maids of the hotel Eftalia Marin Resort 5 * reviews of our compatriots do not contain. As guests note, cleaning is carried out efficiently, every day. Maids are not lazy not only to clean up trash and brush off dust, but also to wash floors. Towels and bed linen are regularly updated, and supplies of personal hygiene products are replenished.

As for the rest of the hotel employees, they made a positive impression on tourists. Travelers note that the staff at Ephtalia Marine Resort does its job well. All employees are friendly, friendly and executive, many understand the Russian language.



Nutrition

This item, as a rule, causes great concern for those travelers who plan to relax in the hotel on an all-inclusive basis. As for the Ephtalia Marine Resort, here, according to our compatriots, the food is organized at a very decent level. So, practically round-the-clock guests have the opportunity to eat in one of the restaurants and snack bars of the hotel.

The main restaurant is open buffet style. The selection of dishes for breakfast, lunch and dinner, judging by the reviews of tourists from Russia, is very wide. In addition, the restaurant has a children's table. Visitors always have the opportunity to try dishes of meat, poultry, fish, seafood, snacks, fruits and vegetables, pastries, sweets. During the day, vacationers can eat fast food and enjoy ice cream. There are also bars serving a wide selection of drinks for every taste.

As for restaurants, where you can order a meal on the menu (a la carte), most of our compatriots do not recommend spending time on them. According to them, the choice of dishes here is practically no different from the main restaurant, and it takes a long time to wait for the order. However, for a change, you can still go here once.

Beach vacation

Hotel Eftalia Marin Resort 5 *, according to travelers, is located very close to the sea. The beach can be reached in 5-7 minutes. The path runs through the tunnel. The beach here is large enough, sandy, equipped with a sufficient number of sun loungers and parasols for all vacationers. So, according to tourists, there is no need to pre-occupy sunbeds. There is also a bar on the beach where you can refresh yourself with a drink and a snack. The only minus some tourists call sunset in the sea. It is quite rocky, so you need to be careful, especially if you go swimming with children.
