The World Hotel 3 * (Vietnam): photo and description of the hotel

Nha Trang is extremely popular as a holiday destination for Russian tourists in Vietnam. The climatic conditions of this resort allow you to spend time here year-round. Moreover, in Nha Trang there is such an alternative to the stormy sea as the entertainment town "Vinpearl" with a huge water park. But where to stay at the resort? We suggest you consider the hotel The World Hotel 3 * as an option. This hotel is suitable for budget travelers who do not like to overpay for luxurious interiors and other "chips". But if you analyze the reviews of tourists who visited this hotel, then it seems that the services here are not in the top three, but in the full four. Slippers, branded cosmetics, tea and coffee sets ... But let's not get ahead of ourselves. Let's take a look at the points of all the pros and cons of the "World Hotel". And this will help us reviews and photos of tourists.



Location of the hotel The World Hotel 3 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang)

The hotel is located in the northern part of the resort. Tourists called this location very successful. Close to the city center (about seven minutes walk), but the street is quiet. From all sides the hotel is surrounded by the same hotels in which foreigners settle. To the nearest beach go exactly two minutes. It is less crowded than in the city center. Also nearby is the famous Dam Market. Nha Trang Railway Station is a five-minute drive away. But the road to the Kamran international airport will take three quarters of an hour. The Ward Hotel is suitable for travelers who prefer outdoor activities. Three hundred meters from the hotel are the museums of Alexander Yersin and Han Hoa. Walking distance to the Nha Trang Center Shopping Mall and the city's cathedral. And a little further away are attractions such as the Ponagar Towers, Tram Huong and Long Son Pagoda. There are several shops near the hotel.



Territory and building of the resort complex

The World Hotel 3 * is a typical city hotel. He has no territory as such. Only parking at the building, places on which are free for hotel guests' vehicles. There is no pool at the hotel either. But in the past, nothing of the sort happened. Yes, sea views are far from every room. In many rooms, windows face the courtyard or wall of a neighboring hotel. The building has two elevators. Therefore, rooms on the upper floors are preferred. There, noises from the street do not reach, and the view is better.



Number of rooms. Guest Room Categories

In the seven-story penthouse of The World Hotel 3 * there are seventy-two rooms. They are divided into categories: "Deluxe", "Senior Deluxe", "Superior". All these rooms are designed for comfortable accommodation of two guests with the possibility of sharing a third in an extra bed. There is also a "Suite". It has one Kingsize bed and a second smaller double bed (or sofa bed). The suites are located in a penthouse with a semicircular wall and panoramic sea views. Family Rooms will be a convenient place to accommodate large families. There is also a room for allergy sufferers and a Suite for honeymooners. All rooms for guests have televisions, air conditioning, safes, electric kettles with accessories for self-preparation of hot drinks, equipment for ironing. In the bathrooms there is both a bath and a shower.



What tourists say about hotel rooms

The lowest category in The World Hotel 3 * hotel is Superior. These rooms do not have a balcony and the windows face the back yard. Also, superior bathrooms have shower only. But this category of rooms impressed tourists with brand new comfortable furniture and working equipment. The bathroom includes slippers and branded toiletries. They give not only soap and shower gel, as expected from the treshka, but also shampoo, cotton buds, toothbrushes and toothpaste. Higher room categories include bathrobes. These rooms have spacious balconies. In addition, the rooms, starting with Superior, have a refrigerator, hairdryer, safe. All rooms have fast and free Wi-Fi. The reviews also gratefully note the presence of a blanket (since in almost all hotels in Southeast Asia they hide only with a sheet) and a large selection of pillows.



What kind of food does the complex provide?

At The World Hotel 3 * (Vietnam), breakfast is already included in the room rate. They are served buffet style in the restaurant on the ground floor. But the catering establishment does not stop its work after a morning meal. Hotel guests can pay at the tour operator or immediately upon arrival at the hotel for lunch and dinner. Most travelers preferred a half-board diet. That is, they had breakfast and dinner at the hotel, and dined at the many cafes in Nha Trang. In addition to the restaurant "The Ward" (on the ground floor of the penthouse), the hotel also has a bar. It offers a variety of drinks and light snacks.

What tourists say about food

Breakfast at The World Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang) consists of a large number of dishes. Groups from China often stay at this hotel, and people in the Middle Kingdom love to thoroughly eat early in the morning. Therefore, in addition to traditional omelettes and scrambled eggs with salads and rolls, full hot dishes were served for breakfast - meat with potatoes, etc. Day and evening meals were even richer. They are also served buffet style. There were always fruits - watermelons, bananas. Lots of fresh vegetables. Soup at this hotel is served for dinner. Of course, lobsters do not spoil guests here (this is a three-star hotel), but fish and shrimp often appeared on the tables. In addition, all rooms have an electric kettle. The maids give two bottles of drinking water every day and put bags of tea, coffee, sugar. Within walking distance from the hotel there is a Lotus supermarket. There you can buy everything missing.



Beach

There is no pool at The World Hotel Nha Trang 3 * hotel. But this hotel is not even on the first line, but very close to the sea. To be precise, then on the second - right behind the Michelia Hotel. Go to the nearest beach for two to three minutes. The place is not crowded, calm. But those who want to snorkel may not like it there. The fact is that the Kai River, which carries sludge, flows into the sea not far from the sea. Therefore, the water in the sea off the coast is a little muddy. This beach is municipal. Admission is free, but the rental of sunbeds and umbrellas is paid. A set of beach equipment costs three hundred rubles. If you want a relaxing holiday, without the buzz of water scooters and the screech of vacationers, then here you are. The sandy beach is quite well maintained and equipped. There are several inexpensive cafes on the waterfront.



Hotel infrastructure

Of the free services at The World Hotel 3 *, reviews mention wireless Internet access and parking. Wi-Fi is very fast, allows you to support video communication. At the 24-hour reception you can book excursions, rent a bike or scooter. For guests with extra money, the hotel also provides VIP services. These include transfers from and to the airport, room service, and concierge services. The hotel has a conference room that can host participants in business meetings, and a fully equipped business center with necessary equipment. Dirty clothes can be taken to the laundry or dry cleaning services. And the hotel has a small library with a set of beach reading. The hotel has a very effective policy to attract families. So, a child under six years old can not only stay, but also have breakfast at the hotel for free. And children under the age of eleven need only pay for their morning (and others, if desired) meals.

Hotel The World Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang): reviews

Most tourists really liked the hotel. Reviews note that the photos of the hotel, rooms, etc. are true. To this, it should be added that bed linens are covered with snow-white, and towels give fluffy and soft. Some rooms where no one has lived for a long time may smell damp. But tourists say that the staff without any problems provides you with another room of the same category. Nevertheless, the guests recommend not booking a Superior, but at least a Deluxe. The difference in price between these categories of rooms is not so significant. The hotel staff described the staff as welcoming, diligent and very friendly. Many write that they would love to come back here again.
