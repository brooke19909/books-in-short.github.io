Grand Excelsior Al Barsha 4 * (United Arab Emirates, Dubai): room description, service, reviews

United Arab Emirates - a country visited by tourists at any time of the year. This is primarily due to the favorable climate for recreation, which becomes hot in the summer and more moderate in the winter, but still the water in the sea is warm throughout the year.

One of the most popular hotels in the region is Grand Excelsior al Barsha, which offers its guests the best conditions for relaxation.

Rooms

Currently, Grand Excelsior al Barsha offers tourists 230 luxurious apartments with the best living conditions, which are divided into several categories: superior, deluxe, executive, as well as luxury. Regardless of the category, each of them has a spacious balcony with stunning views of the surrounding metropolis. Guests of the hotel may well enjoy the excellent climatic conditions created by the air conditioning system installed in each apartment.

All rooms have an individually equipped bathroom with a full set of sanitary ware, as well as a wide selection of bath accessories.

The apartments of the Superior group offer vacationers excellent living conditions, which include a decent rest and a cozy atmosphere created with the help of a carefully thought-out interior. Grand Excelsior al Barsha's room descriptions often specify that each room is 35 square meters. m. It is the only, but very comfortable room, furnished with expensive furniture, made mainly of wood.

The number of berths here is designed for a couple of adult guests, as well as for one child - a separate children's bed is provided for him. The rooms of Grand Excelsior al Barsha also have a furniture set designed for work - it consists of a table and an upholstered chair.

In their free time, guests can watch their favorite satellite broadcasts on the plasma screen that hangs on the wall. For a tea party, there is a kettle and a set of dishes for three. If desired, guests can use the electronic safe, telephone and minibar, the range of soft drinks which is updated daily.

Family facilities

The description of the Grand Excelsior Hotel al Barsha often emphasizes that the hotel positions itself as a place ideal for spending a family vacation. In particular, this is due to the presence of conditions suitable for small children. Almost every room (with the exception of the executive and luxury classes) has a cot designed for a child's age from 4 to 12 years. If it is not provided in the room, you will have to pay for the installation separately.

The hotel also has an individual children's section in the pool. This niche has a non-slip coating, and the depth is shallow. This allows parents to realize that their child is completely safe.

Entertainment

Tourists in their reviews of Grand Excelsior al Barsha very often note the fact that, while staying on its territory, you rarely have to get bored. The fact is that every night for guests a disco is organized, designed for adult guests - it runs throughout the night. At this time, hits of foreign performers, who are loved and recognizable by many, play in the hall. Entrance to the disco is paid, all drinks and snacks that are offered here are also paid by the guests.

If you wish, any tourist relaxing in Grand Excelsior al Barsha can find entertainment to his liking next door to the hotel. Not far from it there are a huge number of restaurants, bars, karaoke, as well as night clubs, which open their doors to their visitors every night. Many tourists in their reviews about this place recommend specific places to visit, talking about their subtleties and level of service.

Nutrition

At Grand Excelsior al Barsha, tourists who take a rest with an all-inclusive package can visit the main restaurant, which organizes a daily buffet. The hotel administration emphasizes the fact that before visiting this place it is necessary to make a preliminary reservation of a table for yourself and your companions. In total, Grand Excelsior al Barsha has three restaurants in which professional chefs can surprise guests with deliciously prepared dishes of oriental and European cuisine. Guests pay special attention to the meat restaurant, whose menu offers luxurious grilled dishes.



If desired, vacationers can visit the bar, where in the daytime they serve original snacks and desserts, and in the evening it turns into a playground for a disco. There is a wonderful bar menu, which contains a huge variety of soft drinks and bright cocktails.

Vacationers who stay at the hotel with their children noticed that there is a children's menu in catering establishments, which includes only the best and most delicious dishes. For the smallest, there are comfortable high chairs, the meal on which will be even more enjoyable.

SPA and sport

SPA area is incredibly popular among guests of Grand Excelsior al Barsha 4 *. It is small in size, its structure includes only a sauna and a hammam - a traditional Turkish bath. In a separate room there are a number of tables, at which professional massage therapists work - their services are paid.

Fans of a sports lifestyle can afford to visit the gym, which is located directly in the Grand Excelsior al Barsha building. Any visit to it is charged according to a separate price list; here it is possible to provide the services of a personal trainer.

Aquazone

In the open area, which is adjacent to the hotel, in the middle of blooming gardens, there is a small aquazone, which consists of a swimming pool. It has an automatic water purification system, so that the filling is crystal clear. There is no water heating in the niche - the optimum temperature is achieved solely under the influence of sunlight.

Around the pool there is a sun terrace, on which comfortable sun loungers are installed, as well as beach umbrellas, which save vacationers from direct sunlight. Towels can be rented upon request.



Business Conditions

A huge number of guests arriving at the hotel on business visits. Very often, this category of people needs conditions for negotiations, presentations and other events related to doing business. In order to create optimal conditions for such customers, the creators of the hotel provided seven well-equipped conference rooms of various sizes on its territory: the smallest of them can accommodate 20 people, and the largest - up to 500.

Each room has all the equipment necessary for holding meetings. Lighting and video devices are installed here, there is an office and copy equipment, as well as a small number of stationery. The furniture includes large lacquered tables made of precious wood, as well as comfortable leather chairs.



Beach

Grand Excelsior al Barsha (Dubai), according to tourists, is not the best option for a beach holiday. This is due to the fact that it does not border the coastline. However, if necessary, everyone can visit the city's well-maintained beach, which is located 5 kilometers from the building - you can get to it by bus, which runs daily from the hotel to the sea and back.

Tourists note that this beach is small, but very well ennobled. Staying on it, you can rent sun loungers, umbrellas and towels, as well as air mattresses. Various activities are also possible along the coastline.

Tourists reviews

Vacationers at Grand Excelsior al Barsha are usually satisfied with the service provided here. In their comments about the rest, they note the excellent arrangement of rooms, convenience and excellent organization of leisure and entertainment. Much attention is paid by the guests to the high level of service provided here.

A huge number of tourists say that the restaurants of the hotel prepare excellent food, they recommend that you definitely try the traditional oriental cuisine performed by local chefs.

As for prices, on average, according to travelers, a trip to Dubai from Moscow with a weekly stay in this hotel costs about 95-110 thousand rubles. In this price, operators include not only accommodation in a superior room, but also a flight with a transfer.

Very often, travelers who have been here recommend going to their families and friends to rest here, motivating them with their positive and vibrant reviews about this place.
