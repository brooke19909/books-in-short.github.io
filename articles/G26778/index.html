Four-star hotel Beldiano (Kemer) - one of the best in economy class

Half an hour drive from Antalya Airport - and you are in the resort village of Beldibi, located between the picturesque Taurus Mountains and the gentle Mediterranean Sea. This coastal area is decorated with banana groves and numerous coves. Beldibi is favorable for shopping: there are many compact souvenir and food shops and small markets. Having planned more thorough purchases, it is better to realize your fantasies in Kemer, which is 15 km away.

Many vacationers in this area remembered for a long time the words: "Turkey, Kemer, the hotel" Beldiana ".

When resting in this region, one must be selective, because Beldibi's niche in the resort business of Turkey is an economy class, 3-4 star hotels prevail here, and it is advisable to choose the best of them. The beaches are mostly small pebble. But among the many hotels there are those that stand out with a quality guesthouse and bulk sandy beaches.

Among the economy hotels that can be recommended is the four-star Beldiano Hotel (Kemer), located in the first line and consisting of a cozy four-story building.
