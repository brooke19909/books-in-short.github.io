Hotel The Ocean Blue ex Medblue Kremasti 4 (Greece, Rhodes): photos and reviews of tourists

There is a beautiful legend about the island of Rhodes, according to which at the time when Zeus was distributing the most beautiful places on earth to the Olympian gods, the sun god Helios did not get anything. He began to complain. And while he spoke, a beautiful island rose up from the sea, all covered with flowers, which became the patrimony of the sun god. No wonder Rhodes is officially recognized as the sunniest place in Europe.





One of the largest islands of the Dodecanese archipelago, stretching from north to south for 78 km, with a maximum land width of about 38 km, Rhodes is a major tourist center, as well as a center for sailing. On its territory there are many hotels from 2 * to 5 *. One of the most popular destinations recommended for future guests of the island by operators is 4 * Ocean Blue (ex Medblue Kremasti), Ialysos / Ixia.

The coasts of Ixia and Ialyssos are the natural ending of a fertile valley that stretches from the foot of Mount Filerimos to the sea. These resorts are considered the best on the entire island of Rhodes. For active tourists, windsurfing, kitesurfing, horse riding, mountain biking, hiking, cycling, soccer, tennis and basketball are offered here. All of the above is just a small part of the pleasures that await the guests of the island here.
