How to make a piano out of paper: ideas, video tutorial

Musical instruments made of paper are a great thing for playing little musicians. A paper piano, guitar, or any other attribute of musical art with quality workmanship can serve as an excellent present for a musician friend. The tool in miniature size will decorate the dollhouse. Perhaps the beautiful Barbie will play music in the evenings for her friends.

Paper piano

There are several ways to make a piano out of paper. For the manufacture of keyboards, you can use the ready-made scheme. Paper parts must be printed on a thick sheet, carefully cut and glued parts. Finish the piano with varnish and leave for some time to dry completely.

The second way to make a piano out of paper with your own hands involves the use of origami techniques. In this case, the keyboards are very original and voluminous.

A simple piano for little musicians

To make a piano out of paper, similar to a real musical instrument, is possible not only for a master, but also for a beginner in the world of needlework. A simple way to make keyboards allows you to create a toy piano in minutes.

For little musicians, you can make a musical instrument from the simplest tools at hand.

Necessary materials:

  • flat box of large and small size;
  • ice cream sticks;
  • stationery knife;
  • paints;
  • hot glue.

Work steps:

  1. You will need a flat box for the piano body. It can be a box of baby food or oatmeal. Glue a smaller box on the side. Allow to dry. Before us is a case that looks like a keyboard.
  2. Paint the cardboard frame in black paint.
  3. Glue the ice cream sticks on top of the small box.
  4. Paint the wooden keys in black and white. Before the keys set the sheet music.

The little wooden piano is ready for the kids to play.
