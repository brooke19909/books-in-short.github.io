Even a novice needlewoman can crochet phone cases

Almost everyone has a mobile phone today. Many do not part with him, day or night. From constant use, the case and screen of the phone are scratched. As a result, the appearance of the mobile phone is significantly deteriorating. The case helps protect the phone from mechanical damage. In addition, it is able to mitigate the impact force from accidental fall.



In stores, prices for covers are often unreasonably high, and for some models it is quite problematic to choose the right size option. In addition, they are all of the same type. Therefore, striving for individuality, needlewomen increasingly think about how to crochet a phone case.

Preparation for work

First of all, you should choose yarn. In principle, any will do. In addition, due to the small size of the product, you can use the remains of the thread that each needlewoman has. If you plan to knit a multi-color cover, you should choose the right combination of yarn.

It is important for all needlewomen to understand that if you crochet a cover according to the description, the scheme must be finalized without fail. This is due to the fact that it is calculated based on the parameters of a particular phone. In order for the case to perfectly fit the existing model, it is important to measure it correctly. It is necessary to take into account the allowances that are necessary for the free placement of the phone.

Work on the case

Crochet phone cases knit very quickly and simply. Overpowering such a product is affordable even for beginner craftswomen.



Work begins with knitting the bottom of the cover. To do this, a chain of air loops of the desired length is knitted, plus one for lifting. Starting from the second, a single crochet is knitted from each loop in a circle. At the same time, three columns should be knitted into the extreme loops to obtain a rounding. If the mobile phone is not thin, several rows should be done.

After that, knitting continues in a circle so that each loop has one column. Work should be completed when the cover reaches the desired size and will fully accommodate the phone.

To make the clasp that all phone cases usually have , you can crochet a valve. To do this, you need to find the middle of the back and knit in straight and reverse rows, including five to six columns. After reaching the required length for the formation of a buttonhole (for buttons), five air loops should be knitted.

The resulting chain is fixed by a connecting column in the last loop of the valve. After this, the product unfolds, the resulting arch is tied with columns, the thread is fixed and breaks off. A beautiful button is sewn into the middle of the front. The finished case can be decorated with knitted flowers, beads, rhinestones or beads.

The knitting method described above is the simplest. Crochet phone cases according to this description can be knitted even by a beginner needlewoman. With experience and imagination, you can achieve exclusivity and originality of products.

The schemes for crocheting phone cases are also suitable for making bags for cameras, camcorders, tablets and other small equipment.
