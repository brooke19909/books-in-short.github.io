Must Sea Hotel 3 * (Thailand, Phuket): hotel description, reviews

Looking for options for a fun holiday by the sea, many tourists are considering Thailand. This is an amazing country with a multifaceted culture and traditions and, of course, picturesque nature and miles of beautiful beaches. The most popular resort place is the island of Phuket. Of course, over the past decades, a huge number of hotels have been built here, because the mass of tourists arriving here is increasing every year. One of the good places to spend a vacation is the Must Sea Hotel 3 *.

Naturally, during the travel planning period you need to learn as much as possible about this or that hotel. The prices in Phuket are pleasantly low, but what conditions should be expected? Do guests offer food? Are beaches, shopping and entertainment centers located nearby? Are there any conditions for relaxing with children?

Where to look for a hotel? Location Description





As you know, the location is an important point to pay attention to when choosing a hotel. Must Sea Hotel 3 * hotel has a favorable location. That is what makes her so popular. The hotel is located in one of the most popular areas of the island, just 500 meters from the famous Kata Beach. Phuket, or rather the vibrant city center, is located about 15 km. In walking distance is another beach that every tourist has heard of - Karon.

The distance to the international airport is about 45 km - the road to the hotel is unlikely to take much time (guests, by the way, are provided with a convenient transfer). This is a resort area, which means that there are shops, salons, bars, restaurants and other entertainment venues nearby.

Must Sea Hotel 3 * hotel (Thailand, Phuket): what does the territory look like?

In fact, this is a very small city-style hotel, which means that its own territory here is very small. The hotel itself is a compact four-story building, built in a modern style. The first floor is reserved for public places - there is a reception desk, a lounge and a waiting room, a cozy cafe and bar.

By the way, this is a completely new hotel - it was opened in 2011. And already in 2014, a radical reconstruction was carried out here. Reviews of regular customers indicate that the hotel is becoming more and more comfortable every year.

Room categories for guests

After a day full of events and entertainment, I want to return to a cozy, quiet room where you can relax and unwind. What the Must Sea Hotel 3 * offers:

  • Superior Room - spacious rooms with a good repair and new appliances. Some rooms have their own balcony, while others can enjoy city views from the huge windows.
  • Family Room - large rooms, which consist of a bedroom and a spacious living room with soft folding furniture. Almost all have balconies with chairs and a table for relaxing in the fresh air.

By the way, the hotel is quite small - there are 64 rooms. For many tourists, this is not a disadvantage, since the territory is always quiet and calm.

What does the room look like? Description and photo





Must Sea Hotel 3 * hotel complex can please its guests with spacious rooms with fresh renovation and stylish, discreet design. Tourists will find a large bed with orthopedic mattress, as well as some other necessary furniture, including bedside tables, a spacious wardrobe and a table with chairs.

In addition, all rooms are equipped with modern furniture. A flat-screen, large-screen TV can watch local and some satellite channels. There is also a small, but functional individual air conditioning, which works almost silently. It features a telephone and an easy-to-use safe with a combination lock. There is also a small refrigerator. Every day it is filled with free drinks, including drinking water and soda (maids bring everything else for an additional fee). Almost every room has an electric kettle, as well as a set for making hot drinks.

Guests, of course, can count on a small bathroom where you can take a shower. Maids change towels daily and replenish stocks of shampoo, soap and other cosmetics. There is also a small hair dryer, which is important for many guests.

Can I count on food?





Food is an important part of any trip. After all, during the rest I want to indulge in delicious dishes, enjoy the delights of exotic Thai cuisine. For tourists who have paid for accommodation, Must Sea Hotel 3 * provides daily breakfasts. They are held in a cozy restaurant in the form of a buffet - you yourself choose the dishes. Reviews indicate that the menu here is not bad. It includes light snacks and salads, toasts, fried eggs, fresh pastries, side dishes, fruits and, of course, drinks.

The hotel has a small bar where you can always order a cup of aromatic coffee, an exotic cocktail or a stronger drink.

The rest of the day, you can eat both in the local restaurant and in the cafe, which is full in the district. Food prices are reasonable - you will have a chance to get addicted to amazing Thai cuisine.

Where is the beach located? Entertainment on the coast





Almost every tourist arrives on the island, planning to spend time on the beach, because Phuket is famous for its luxurious coastline. The world famous Kata Beach is just a short walk away . And in 10-15 minutes you can walk to Karon Beach, which is no less popular among tourists.

Of course, there are many opportunities to have fun, ranging from beach volleyball and water skiing, ending with sailing and scuba diving. The beaches here are clean (especially in season), the water is warm and the entrance to the sea is convenient. Since these are places of public recreation, the use of sun loungers and umbrellas is paid separately every day. By the way, on the shore there is a huge number of cozy cafes where you can order a chilled drink, dine or even treat yourself to seafood dishes.

Hotel Must Sea Hotel 3 * (Phuket): additional service





Hotel guests can count on some amenities. To begin with, it is worth saying that the reception and information desk are open around the clock. For guests' convenience, the hotel has a luggage storage room. Clothes can be taken to the laundry - the prices for washing and ironing are quite affordable.

A compact but convenient car park is located next to the hotel. You can rent a car or a moped in a hotel or in a city - this is a simple procedure, and the availability of your own transport opens up new possibilities for traveling around the island. Parking at the hotel, by the way, is free.

Throughout the hotel there is Internet access (you can connect to the network in the rooms). Reviews indicate that the speed is decent, which is important for almost all modern travelers.

The hotel does not have a medical office. In case of health problems it is worth contacting the staff - they will quickly call an experienced, qualified doctor in your room.

Leisure of tourists: how to have fun on site?





Must Sea Hotel 3 * is a small hotel, which is intended primarily for a comfortable overnight stay and relaxation. On the other hand, you can have a good time on site. There is a large outdoor pool where guests can refresh themselves or sunbathe, lying on comfortable sun loungers. By the way, this is a chance to meet and chat with new people.

You can relax in the large jacuzzi with a powerful hydromassage system - this place is also popular among guests.

Do not forget that Phuket is an island with a huge number of natural and historical attractions, so it is perfect for sightseeing holidays. The hotel has a bureau where you can help determine the direction and make an interesting route for travel.

Rest with the whole family: are there any conditions for the child?

Naturally, many parents think about whether to take their child with them to relax on Kata Beach? Phuket is a great place for a relaxing family vacation. Guests can look forward to some amenities. In particular, there are spacious rooms for families, and in which case they bring an extra bed to the room. The restaurant menu makes it possible to choose healthy dishes for the child. And finding an experienced nanny is not difficult - the cost of such a service is quite reasonable.

Unfortunately, there are no special entertainments for the guys. However, children often spend time with their parents by the pool. And the local beach is perfect for children's bathing. In the city there are many entertainment centers where you and your child can have a good time.

What do travelers say? Reviews and recommendations





Of course, in addition to official information, it is always interesting to find out what tourists who have already visited here think about the hotel. In fact, guests leave only positive feedback. It should be understood that this is a very small three-star hotel, which, as a rule, is used by tourists as an overnight stay and a place of rest. Indeed, the territory of Must Sea Hotel (Kata) 3 * is small. On the other hand, it feels comfortable and everywhere feels real home cosiness.

At your disposal are spacious rooms with a fresh renovation and modern appliances. By the way, the insulation here is excellent, tourists relax in peace and quiet. Breakfasts, albeit slightly monotonous, are still tasty and hearty. And the staff here are very attentive and helpful. One has only to say that not everyone understands the Russian language, but there are no problems with English speech. Conflicts between guests and staff are very rare.

As you know, the prices in Phuket are quite reasonable, which attracts tourists. The cost of living at the hotel is quite affordable - this place is suitable for an economical, but nevertheless comfortable stay.
