Ibiza is a beautiful island in the Mediterranean Sea, located near Spain. Amazing white beaches, the blue sea, green meadows and bizarre architecture await everyone who visits this hospitable land. Ibiza has a mild climate. The temperature in warm time does not drop below 25 degrees. The warm Mediterranean Sea also won't ruin your vacation. Until October, the water temperature reaches 26 degrees. The tourist season on the island begins in May and lasts until October.
