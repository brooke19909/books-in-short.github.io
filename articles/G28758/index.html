Hotel Sunny Hills Hotel Apts 3 * (Paphos, Cyprus): description and photos

Cyprus attracts tourists with very clean beaches, hotels with high quality services and an interesting excursion program. They come to the island in search of a quiet holiday. Here you can enjoy beautiful sea and mountain landscapes. Choosing a place to stay, you should pay attention to the Sunny Hills Hotel Apts 3 * hotel (Paphos). It is suitable for those travelers who do not like noisy tourist complexes, completely clogged with guests.

Holidays in Cyprus: hotel location

The apartments are located in the center of Paphos, which is considered the most elite resort in Cyprus. It is famous for its developed infrastructure. There are restaurants offering exquisite dishes of Greek and Mediterranean cuisine, and boutiques with branded clothing, and a large number of historical monuments. And Paphos is the birthplace of the Greek goddess Aphrodite. The only minus of the resort is the poor organization of recreation for children. The city does not have a large number of playgrounds and children's animation programs.





The Sunny Hills Hotel Apts 3 * hotel itself is located on the edge of the natural hill that surrounds the nearby villages. It offers a beautiful panoramic view of the sea. Tourists living here especially enjoy watching the sunsets and sunrises. A sandy public beach is 500 meters away. An unhurried step can be reached in 10-15 minutes. Very close is a bus stop. You can get to the city center by bus or taxi. The hotel is about 3 km away. The nearest airport is 18 km from Paphos. Transfer to the apartment takes no more than an hour.

General description of the complex

Sunny Hill Hotel Apartments opened its doors to tourists in 1994. It is a snow-white building, built in the Greek style. The last repair of all hotel rooms was carried out in the winter of 2014. The apartments are small studios. They have a private bathroom, a fully equipped kitchen, and a balcony. The staff here speaks good English, and some employees understand Russian well. The hotel welcomes tourists with small children, giving them a good discount on vacation. Unfortunately, pets are not allowed here.





Check-in at the apartment starts strictly from 14:00. If tourists arrived earlier than this time, then they can leave their luggage at the reception and go to the pool or beach. Hotel receptionists work until midnight. Guests can pay for additional services using bank cards MasterCard, Maestro or Visa. After the rest, tourists must leave the complex before noon. Although, subject to availability, you can extend your stay for several hours.

Types of rooms

All rooms at the hotel will delight guests with a pleasant interior that combines classic and Mediterranean styles. The apartments have a spacious balcony with a dining room. The windows offer amazing sea views. Upon arrival at the reception you can request a password for connecting free Internet. There are no facilities for people with disabilities in the apartment. A thorough daily cleaning of all rooms is carried out. Bed linen is replaced once a week. It is worth remembering that smoking in the rooms is strictly prohibited.

  • One Bedroom. Spacious apartment of 45 square meters. m. They consist of a bedroom, a bathroom and a living room. There is a small kitchenette. The floor is tiled with carpet. The room is designed for three people. An extra bed is a sofa.
  • Studio This room consists of one spacious bedroom, which has a living room and kitchen area. For guests are offered two single beds and a sofa soft. Ceramic tiles are laid on the floor. The total area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe apartment is 38 square meters. m

Apartment equipment

The spacious apartments of Sunny Hills Hotel Apts 3 * (Cyprus) are equipped not only with clean and modern furniture, but also with numerous amenities that will make your stay here truly comfortable. The guests of the complex will be able to feel the comfort of home, using the following equipment:

  • A TV that broadcasts a large number of satellite channels. Among them there are several Russian speakers.
  • Telephone with which you can contact the hotel administrator around the clock. International calls are available for a fee, and room service is also available.
  • A kitchenette equipped with everything necessary for self-cooking. This apartment has a electric stove, refrigerator, microwave and electric kettle. A set of dishes are provided, along with tea, coffee and sugar.
  • Separate split system for each room. Using the remote control, guests can independently adjust the temperature. In the cold season, the hotel may include heating.
  • A small electronic safe for storing jewelry, documents, money or mobile phones. You can use it only for a fee.
  • Toiletries and towels in the bathroom. For a small fee, you can also hire a hairdryer from reception.




Supply system

Hotel Sunny Hills Hotel Apts 3 * does not offer tourists a wide choice in terms of food. Firstly, most of them prefer to cook on their own in the kitchen in the room. Secondly, not far from the complex there are many restaurants and cafes, which at a low price offer travelers to enjoy Greek cuisine. Guests spend the day swimming in the sea or walking around Paphos, so it‚Äôs more convenient for them to dine outside the hotel. When issuing a ticket, they can choose either accommodation without meals, or pay only for breakfast.





Breakfast is served in a small restaurant located on site. It works from 8:30 to 10:00. There is no wireless internet on its territory. It serves standard European dishes, such as scrambled eggs, fried bacon, boiled sausages, sandwiches, cereal and some fruit. From drinks - concentrated juices, tea, coffee and hot milk, as well as mineral water. There is also a daily snack bar for tourists.

Local infrastructure

The administration of the Sunny Hill Hotel Apartments cares not only about the comfortable accommodation of its guests, but also about the services that they can use. Developed infrastructure improves the quality of service provided. Tourists staying in this complex can use the following facilities:

  • Private free parking, which is guarded around the clock. Here, tourists can leave their car at any time. No prior reservation required.
  • Paid car rental is available on site. The fee for using the machine is charged daily. The rental price does not include gasoline.
  • The hotel administrator can call a doctor in the room upon request. His services are not included in the insurance, so each appointment is paid separately.
  • Laundry, which for a fee can wash, dry and iron the clothes of tourists.
  • Luggage storage. For a small amount, you can leave large bags and suitcases here, which can take up a lot of space in the room.
  • 24-hour reception, where the administrator is constantly located.

Entertainment Organization

A variety of entertainment is also an indicator of quality relaxation. The hotel should be presented and active, and measured leisure. Hotel Sunny Hills Hotel Apts 3 * cares about its guests, so it offers them a large selection of entertainment facilities. We list the main ones:

  • Outdoor fresh water pool. For hotel guests there are free sun loungers, umbrellas and mattresses. It is open daily from 10:00 to 18:00. There is no water heating.
  • Organization of excursion programs. Here you can choose and book an excursion not only to the sights of Paphos, but also the whole of Cyprus.
  • Billiard tables. You can play here only for a fee.
  • A common living room where guests can relax and watch TV. There is a soft sofa, as well as small leather sofas and armchairs.
  • Equipment for playing darts.
  • Sauna and massage room. Provide their services for a fee.
  • Bike rent. You can ride them on the local promenade equipped with bike paths.




Facilities for guests with children

Sunny Hills Hotel Apts 3 * apartments offer a standard set of amenities for families with children. If tourists come with a small child, then they are provided with a cradle in the room. However, only one extra bed is allocated per apartment. The hotel's restaurant has baby chairs for babies. The child can also be left with a nanny. To do this, contact the administrator. He will call her for guests even at night. It is worth remembering that this service is paid. Hotel guests can also request a special children's menu in the restaurant.

Advantages of the hotel, which tourists noted

The current state of affairs at the hotel can always be found by reading the opinions of tourists who previously rested here. Apartments are considered a popular vacation spot, so they have many reviews describing both the advantages and disadvantages. First, we list the advantages of this hotel:

  • It offers amazing sea views. It is especially nice to sit on the balcony in the evening and watch the sunset.
  • The hotel is very quiet, so it is well suited for lovers of a relaxing holiday. You can sleep with the windows open and not be afraid that the sounds of loud music will wake you up.
  • Nearby there is a very good tavern. It can perfectly replace the restaurant operating in the hotel.
  • Very large rooms, the interior of which creates a cozy atmosphere. Spacious and clean terrace.
  • Air conditioning works well. In the heat, it cools perfectly and does not make noise at all.
  • The hotel administrator is very friendly, always answers questions and tries to solve problems.




Hotel shortcomings noted by tourists

Tourists who stayed at Sunny Hills Hotel Apts 3 * leave negative reviews. In them they describe the following disadvantages of the apartment:

  • The dishes in the rooms are very dirty. The residual grease and detergents are visible on it, so tourists who come here will first have to wash all the plates and mugs.
  • Bright floor in the room, which shows spots of dirt.
  • Ants crawl through the rooms, which can sometimes climb into bed.
  • The water in the sink leaves very poorly. You have to wait almost half an hour to turn on the water again. The administrator does not respond to a request to clean it.
  • Monotonous breakfast. Every day they serve the same set of dishes, which quickly bothers.

Instead of a conclusion

Hotel Sunny Hills Hotel Apts 3 * can be advised for a budget vacation in Cyprus. It is suitable for tourists who prefer a calm and respectable vacation. It often stops adults and elderly people, families with children. Young people are much less common here. There are not many night clubs and discos in Paphos, so they choose other resorts. Arriving here with children, tourists should think about the organization of leisure in advance, because the hotel does not offer kids any entertainment.
