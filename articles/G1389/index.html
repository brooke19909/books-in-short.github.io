How to free up space on the iPhone 5S: an overview of methods and useful tips

Forced reboot

The very first and easiest way to clear the memory of excess garbage is to reboot the iPhone 5S. In addition to performance gains, local OS tools will remove the cache accumulated during the session. The latter is growing day after day after almost every user action.

Naturally, completely erasing the cache on the iPhone 5S this way will not work. Rebooting only reduces it. But if you have not performed it for at least a week, then the memory gain can be very significant.

Despite the obviousness of this method, many users ignore it. Some people completely forget that they have installed one or another program. Therefore, to clean the internal memory, it is worthwhile to regularly audit applications on the iPhone 5S.

The latter is stored in the internal memory of the device and is waiting in the wings. The volume of the OS distribution can reach 2 GB, which is a lot, especially for devices with 8 GB of memory.

To remove downloaded updates and free up space on the iPhone 5S, you must perform the following procedure:

  1. Go to the "Settings".
  2. Click on the item "Basic".
  3. We find the line "Storage Usage and" Iklaud.
