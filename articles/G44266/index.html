Location

Sanya Marriott Hotel Dadonghai Bay is located just a few steps from the beach. The hotel is set amid mountains, picturesquely covered with lush tropical vegetation. This location led to a magnificent view of the mountains and the ocean from the windows of the hotel.

It should be noted that in the immediate vicinity is the Dadonghai coral reef, which is a national nature reserve. Also nearby is an outdoor park in which wild macaques live.

A 5-minute drive takes guests to the center of Sanya. It takes about 40 minutes to get to the airport. A little longer - to the edge of the world park, which is popular among tourists.

If you study the reviews left about Sanya Marriott Hotel Dadonghai Bay, you will notice a lot of enthusiastic comments regarding its appearance.
