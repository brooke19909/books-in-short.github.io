Hotel Resort Marinha Dourada 3 * (India, Goa): description, rooms and reviews

Hotel Resort Marinha Dourada 3 * over the years of its existence has won several prestigious awards. Despite the fact that there is no beach near the hotel, your vacation will be unforgettable.





Short hotel description

Hotel Resort Marinha Dourada 3 * is a small but very nice hotel located in the popular resort village. Despite the compact size of the complex, there are many opportunities for entertainment and relaxation. You can visit the spa, work out in the gym, and play chess with giant pieces in the open air.

Despite the pretty territory and rich tourist infrastructure, the main thing in any hotel is the rooms. Hotel Resort Marinha Dourada 3 * offers accommodation in comfortable apartments, where there is everything you need to provide an adequate level of comfort that modern tourists are accustomed to.

The main advantage of the Resort Marinha Dourada 3 * hotel is its picturesque territory. The hotel boasts its green gardens, which landscape designers worked on. You can contemplate all this beauty sitting in a cozy outdoor cafe, where you can taste not only Thai and European dishes, but also real culinary masterpieces from the local chef.





Hotel Location

The hotel complex Resort Marinha Dourada 3 * (India) is located in the north of India, in the resort village of Arpora. It is approximately 42 km from the international airport and 14 km from the train station. The nearest public beach, Baga, is 2 km away. You can also go to the sandy coast of Panaji, but to get to it you have to overcome 15 km.

Hotel Rooms

Hotel Resort Marinha Dourada 3 * (India, Goa) has 184 guest rooms. Most of them can be assigned to the standard category. There is only a small bedroom with a double bed (or a pair of single), as well as a bathroom and a balcony.

Positive reviews

Many tourists are attracted by the resort Hotel Resort Marinha Dourada 3 *. Reviews about him contain positive comments:





  • a beautiful green area, on which there are two artificial ponds (there are many fish, crabs and other animals);
  • polite and smiling staff who always willingly help in solving problems and issues;
  • the rooms are quite comfortable and neat, characterized by a simple but elegant interior;
  • the hotel and its territory is dominated by a quiet and calm atmosphere;
  • breakfast menu is quite varied and satisfying (there are 7 varieties of dishes to choose from);
  • every hour a free bus to the beach leaves from the hotel.

Negative reviews

Hotel guests have a number of comments on this institution:

  • there is no hairdryer in the bathroom;
  • the hotel is very far from the beach - you will have to walk at least 40 minutes on foot (it remains to ride a free bus or taxi);
  • due to the increased humidity created by artificial ponds, there are a lot of mosquitoes in the territory;
  • old furniture in the rooms, as well as frayed walls;
  • it is better to leave money and valuables in a safe, as unpleasant incidents often happen in a hotel;
  • breakfast menu does not contain meat products;
  • increased interest of local workers and vacationers in girls.

The hotel, in general, is quite pleasant, because only its territory can be called a work of art. Despite the fact that the hotel is close to many infrastructure facilities, it is quite quiet and calm. A little overshadow the rest may be too far from the nearest beach, but the problem can be solved if you use the free hotel transfer.
