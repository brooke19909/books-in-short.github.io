DIY designer Christmas trees: interesting ideas and recommendations

New Year is the most beloved holiday of many all over the world. They are carefully prepared for it. In each house, the rooms are decorated with garlands, flags, rain, various toys. The abundance of sparkling, colorful colors creates a fabulous mood, awaiting magic and the fulfillment of all desires.

All people - from small children to old people and old women, expect a miracle, believe that the new year will bring only the good.



In all organizations, enterprises parties, corporate parties are held. People wear the most luxurious outfits, interesting masks. In hands - sparklers. All this makes the holiday fun, wonderful and unique.

Forest beauty - a symbol of the New Year holiday

Mandatory symbol of the New Year is dressed up in a variety of toys, garlands, beads, Christmas tree.

Buying a Christmas tree, people try to choose the most fluffy and beautiful. And no wonder, because without a forest beauty, a holiday would not be interesting and beautiful.

Unfortunately, there are such dishonest people who go to the forest themselves and chop the New Year tree. This can lead to very disastrous consequences. If for some reason it was not possible to acquire the symbol of the New Year, you can make designer Christmas trees with your own hands. This can be done by anyone who has at least a little imagination.

Designer Christmas trees - an original approach to your favorite holiday

Many now have the opportunity to do different types of needlework. The creation of such unusual things as designer Christmas trees is also not disregarded.

To begin with, the material from which the future Christmas tree will be made is selected. You can combine different materials. The most important thing is that the end result should surprise and delight the owner of the resulting product.

Creating Christmas Trees from Tools

So that the spruce was unusual and its creation and installation were not affordable, you can make it from improvised means. For this, the remnants of polypropylene pipes, plastic bottles, the remnants of fabric and artificial leather, trunks, twigs and tree branches, paper, sticks, boxes and so on can be useful.



Let's consider several options.

1. From three polypropylene pipes, each two meters long, make a tripod. The upper part is fastened with a rope or glue. On the top, you can put an asterisk. The middle part of the tripod is wrapped with a rope. This is necessary so that the legs of the tripod do not spread to the sides.

Special thrust bearings are put on the legs so that the end of the pipe does not scratch the floor.

The main frame is ready. Now it's up to the imagination. What to make imitation of needles from? Can be made from special rain, from paper. You can stick needles from fabric or leather on the twigs. The main thing is that the right color is chosen: green, silver or black. Needles can also be made from ordinary curtains that are attached to the top and bases of the tripod.

2. An ordinary plastic bucket is taken. A gypsum solution is poured there. Until it hardens, a suitable-sized trunk must be fixed in a cast in a standing position. Most importantly, the trunk should have many branches where the imitated needles will be attached.



3. An unusual and creative tree will turn out from green pillows of a triangular shape of different sizes. You can simply stack them on the floor (first a large pillow in diameter down, then a smaller one, and so on until the end). And you can take a bucket, install there with a gypsum polypropylene pipe. Pillows gather on it (like in a pyramid). Such a tree, even if it falls, will not break anything.

The finished Christmas tree can be decorated with suitable toys, tinsel, garlands. And not only. Young children really like it when different goodies are hung on the Christmas tree: sweets, cookies, chocolates, tangerines, bananas and others.

Designer Christmas trees on the wall

In order to put a large Christmas tree, you need to have a large space. Unfortunately, not everyone can afford it. But in this case, you can find an original solution. You can make unusual designer Christmas trees that will be fixed on the wall. That is, the tree will not stand in the middle of the room, but will be on the wall, saving the space of the room.

Properly selected lighting, New Year's decoration of the room, a luxurious table will allow guests to feel and think as if there is a real Christmas tree in the room.

Design artificial Christmas trees on the wall can be made of various materials: from rain, tinsel, paper, frames and so on.

Let's consider several options.

1. Take a few ribbons - rain green shiny color. With their help, the contour of the future Christmas tree is made on the wall. The middle of the contour is also decorated with rains and tinsel. Next, toys are made. You can take real ones, or you can make them from improvised means. Lovers of such types of needlework as kanzashi, quilling, beadwork and so on can make bright colorful creative toys and bows that will not leave anyone indifferent.

2. Fans of minimalism can be offered to make a Christmas tree of garland and balls. A contour of a tree is made from a garland, and balls are hung on the tips of simulated branches. Economically, quickly and efficiently.



3. You can make a Christmas tree and from photos. Photos in a frame of different sizes are fixed on the wall in the shape of a Christmas tree. You can make it so that these are photographs of the guests themselves. Of course they will like it.

"Tasty" tree

The holiday is decorated with a rich table, on which there will be many goodies, salads, various snacks and other dishes. Of course, everyone will like delicious treats decorated in the form of a forest beauty. There are plenty of options for what to cook. Stormy imagination, delicious ingredients allow you to create an unforgettable masterpiece.

1. Bake the cakes. You can use shortbread, biscuit or honey dough. Then, with the help of a sharp knife, details are cut out (triangles, pentagons, snowflakes, stars, etc.). The resulting parts are fixed using sticks. Most importantly, you need to fix in the form of a pyramid (small to large), forming the shape of a Christmas tree. You can decorate with a fine powder of different colors and cream.

2. A very attractive Christmas tree made of fruits. The most important thing is to fix everything in a triangular shape.



The design decoration of the Christmas tree on the table will undoubtedly captivate all guests and delight the little children.

Afterword

The absence of a Christmas tree in the New Year can overshadow the mood of people. Therefore, you need to prepare in advance and purchase it or do it yourself.

Designer Christmas trees are a great solution for creative, diverse people.
