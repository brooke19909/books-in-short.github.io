O. Zakynthos, hotels: reviews and photos. How to choose the best hotel in Zakynthos

One of the Ionian Islands - Zakynthos (Zakynthos) - is known to many tourists due to its unique nature. The combination of pine-covered mountains and emerald valleys, pristine beaches and orchards - all this offers you Zakynthos. The hotels of this resort are designed for different groups of vacationers, each of which will be able to find a room to suit their requirements and capabilities. In addition, it is very important why you are going to Greece - just lie on the beach or take a walk in nightclubs. How to choose the best hotel and what to consider when choosing? To do this, we suggest that you familiarize yourself with the most popular resorts in Zakynthos to make a competent choice.

Argassi: youth vacations





Zakynthos has been known since ancient times, because it was its inhabitants who went on a trip to Troy under the leadership of Odysseus. It is no coincidence that this resort is simply filled with the spirit of antiquity and traditions. The island‚Äôs busiest resort is Argassi, which is perfect for those who plan to spend their holidays on excursions and hiking in the surrounding area. You will be offered a budget vacation at Family Inn 2 * or Edelweiss Hotel (Argassi, Zakynthos). Hotels of this level offer decent conditions of rest and quality service. Choosing this resort, you can enjoy the purity of the azure sea and the cleanest beach, here you will be offered a huge amount of entertainment for every taste. Such a rest - bright and intense - will surely please the youth.

Vasilikos: golden sands for families with children





Just 16 km from Zakynthos is the resort of Vasilikos, which is known for its clean sandy beaches with golden sand. There is a gentle entrance to the sea and shallow water near the shore, so this resort is ideal for families with small children. You will be provided with a quiet and relaxed vacation away from the hustle, bustle, and even more so of worries. The resort has a developed infrastructure - there are shops, cafes and the best hotels in Zakynthos. Reviews about them are always good, because here they triple you with maximum comfort.

Kalamaki: a place of quiet rest

Another calm resort is Kalamaki, which is actively developing. This is a real paradise for those who just want to lie on a clean beach, which stretches out with a wide sand ribbon. Here you will find a huge amount of entertainment on the water. Local Zakynthos 3 star hotels are surrounded by exotic vegetation. Thus, if you are traveling with your family and you will have small children, choose hotels in Vasilikos - here you will be provided with a quiet and measured rest.

Modern Resorts: Laganas, Tsilivi

Laganas is an amazing place in Greece, because this town harmoniously combines protected areas and well-organized infrastructure. Clean beaches, long and wide, a very beautiful Laganas Bay - perhaps the most important sights of the town. This spacious resort has enough space for everyone: those who just want to lie on the beach, and those who prefer to have fun in full.





In Laganas, one of the most famous hotels is located - Anastasia. The hotel (Zakynthos town on buildings with exquisite architecture) looks like a fabulous palace. The three-story hotel is surrounded by lush plants and flowers, the area is always clean and well-groomed. There is an unusual shape pool for children and adults, interesting entertaining programs await you every evening, and animators will occupy the children. Anastasia Beach has a large number of family rooms on the ground floor that overlook the garden. The room base is very different, so every tourist will be able to choose the suitable conditions for relaxation. You can monitor your condition in the gym, and you can diversify your evening leisure with billiards.

There is another cozy hotel in Laganas - Pallas Hotel. This is a beautiful place, attracting a huge number of tourists every year. The Palace Hotel (Zakynthos) is a three-story building that fits very romantically into an olive grove. Despite the fact that the building is decorated in a patriarchal style, in fact the hotel is one of the most modern and equipped. Rest here will be measured and calm, each room is equipped with all necessary equipment, and you can enjoy the beauty of nature from the balcony or terrace. Internet access is available in a special corner for an additional fee. There is a swimming pool with a children's area, its own restaurant, table tennis and billiards.





The leader in the number of beaches is the village of Tsilivi, which is 5 km from the city of Zakynthos. The hotels here are very different, while all of them are united by a high level of service, delicious food and a huge amount of entertainment for parents and their children. Laganas and Tsilivi are ideal resorts for those who value modernity, luxury and at the same time cosiness.

Alikes: luxury and comfort

This small resort is located in the northwestern part of Zakynthos. The place is distinguished by clear sea water, excellent fine sand beaches and comfortable air temperature. The Konstantin Beach Hotel is popular in this resort. Zakynthos allocated a place for its location on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea. Tourists love this hotel for its affordable prices and high level of service, as well as the beauty of the surrounding nature. Although Zakynthos itself is a very beautiful resort, and it is not by chance that many people who come to Greece prefer it.





If you like small and quiet hotels, then you will definitely like Konstantin Beach. There are only 48 rooms that are stylishly decorated and correctly furnished with everything you need. Free Wi-Fi is available. The hotel has an outdoor pool with a children's area and a jacuzzi, in the conference room you can hold business meetings, and a fitness center will always take care of your figure. If you prefer an economical vacation right on the beach in comfortable conditions, then you will definitely like this hotel!

All inclusive!

Greece is a tourist country that is very attentive to its guests. This can be said about any resort, including Zakynthos. All-inclusive hotels are found here in every region, so there will definitely not be problems with the choice. For example, in Aqua Bay & Waterpark (Tsilivi) you will be offered 68 rooms - both standard and family. It is important that the hotel operates on an all-inclusive system, so you don‚Äôt have to figure out where to go for a meal. Near the hotel there is a water park, which attracts a huge number of tourists.





On the territory there is a huge amount of entertainment for every taste: mountain bike rental and spa, swimming pools and gyms, table tennis and darts, in addition, each room has free Internet. Families with children can hire a nanny and let the children go under her supervision on water slides, and study the surroundings themselves. The lounge bar awaits guests in the evenings - it is so nice to relax and listen to live music. By the way, each room in the hotel is individually and stylishly decorated in natural colors. The hotel opened not so long ago - in 2013, but during this time managed to win the love of tourists. At 300 meters from the hotel there is a municipal beach where you can rent sunbeds and umbrellas. By the way, the hotel offers favorable accommodation conditions: for example, two children under 16 can live and eat for free!

Lesante Hotel & SPA (Tsilivi)





Another cozy and very modern place is the five-star Lesante Hotel. Here you will be offered 90 rooms of various classes - some are equipped with two bathrooms or a large terrace, and even a private outdoor pool with jacuzzi. Each room is equipped with air conditioning, satellite TV, minibar, has everything you need in terms of toiletries. Meals - half board, a diet menu is also offered. For children there is a swimming pool, a playground, babysitting services. The hotel is good in that it offers a large selection of programs to improve your health and relax.

Sea view village

Sea View Village is a small hotel with a unique location, as the rooms offer chic panoramic views of the Ionian Sea. It is very beautiful here: the hotel's decoration is done in a modern style, you will be offered rooms with sea views that are equipped with everything you need. The infrastructure is developed, and the rest of children on the site or in the pool is provided. The municipal beach is 500 meters from the hotel, while you can get to it by bus. Here sun loungers and parasols are provided free of charge.





Beautiful nature, modern hotels, quality service and a huge amount of entertainment for every taste - all this will be offered to you in the resort of Zakynthos. The hotels here are very diverse, so you can choose a place to stay, depending on your preferences and financial capabilities.
