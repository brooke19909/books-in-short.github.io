"Lotus" - a recreation center in Primorsko-Akhtarsk

If you are a fan of relaxation on the shores of the warm Sea of ​​Azov, you are invited to visit the "Lotus" - a recreation center in the Krasnodar Territory. Healing Azov water, a combination of sea and steppe air, as well as fruits and vegetables in abundance will make your stay here not only enjoyable, but also useful.

Holiday Village "Lotus": description

The hostel is great for families. A gentle sandy entry into the sea is an ideal bathing place for young guests of the health resort. It is shallow at the very shore, in order for adults to get to a deeper place, you will need to go a little. You can sunbathe on your own sand and pebble beach, equipped with shaded canopies and umbrellas. Here you can eat in the cafe. And if someone does not like to just lie in the sun, then they have plenty of water and beach activities. Three kilometers from the base is the town of Primorsko-Akhtarsk. If guests need to get to the market, then they are taken from the camp site by organized bus. And if you want to walk around the city, you can use the services of a fixed-route taxi, which also runs directly from the territory of the base. What is unique about the rest here is that you can visit the plantation with Indian lotuses, which is located nearby. The territory of the base is green and well-groomed, you can hide from the warm southern sun in the shade of trees, flowers grow everywhere. Children can spend their time on the playground, and lovers of outdoor activities on the sports grounds. Meals are organized in a private dining room. Parking space is available.

Guest Accommodation

The camp site "Lotus" in Primorsko-Akhtarsk is located in a stationary three-story building. There are several categories of rooms for your stay:

  • single one-room - the cost is from 1120 to 1350 rubles per day, depending on the season;
  • double one-room - from 1060 to 1290 rubles per adult and from 850 to 1035 per child under 10 years old;
  • double one-room lit. A - the price for a room is from 1120 to 1350 rubles per day per adult and from 900 to 1080 rubles per child under 10 years old;
  • triple one-room - respectively from 1000 to 1225 rubles and from 800 to 980 rubles in one day;
  • double two-room junior suite (meals are paid separately) - from 1200 to 1500 rubles per day, depending on the season.

The price of accommodation (except for the Junior Suite) includes four meals a day. At the recreation center "Lotus" all rooms are equipped with TV, refrigerator and air conditioning.
