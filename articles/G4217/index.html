The service at the reception is not very good. There are a lot of tourists, which is why there are not enough working personnel at all. Unfortunately, employees of the institution do not always offer their own help.

The hotel has several A La Carte restaurants . You can get there by appointment and eat some delicacy that is not served in a regular catering establishment.

Kitchen at the hotel

The cuisine at Lotus Bay Beach Resort (Hurghada) is quite good. Here you will find a variety of menus, and the waiters will help you choose something really worthwhile.

True, in the children's menu there is no such variety as in the adult, but the child will still not remain hungry. The chef will always find what every kid will surely like!

A variety of cereals, cereals, yoghurts have always been, are and will be at breakfast. The only thing visitors might miss is seafood. Yes, fried fish is regularly served here, but you must admit that sometimes you want shrimp and lobster.

In the main menu you will find a huge number of salads, all kinds of delicious buns, pasta, egg products, mashed soups and much more, which you and your friends will definitely like.

By the way, black cumin is present in many dishes - this is one of the features of dishes in Egypt. Believe me, this spice betrays the original taste of the dish, and it is very useful. The Egyptians consider it a panacea for all diseases.

There are enough cakes here, but not enough fruit. From drinks for adults - all that the soul desires. There are no drinks for little children. Only plain water and milk. Yes, and also, the Egyptians are not very friendly with meat, so do not count on fried steaks, and if you find something like this on the menu, visitors do not recommend trying, because here they simply do not know how to cook such kind of dishes.
