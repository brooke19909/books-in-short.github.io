Hotel "Empire" (Adler): description, reviews

The Empire Hotel in Adler offers all tourists a decent vacation in decent conditions. It becomes especially popular in the cool period, especially before the New Year's holiday, when vacationers from different parts of Russia and the world come to the ski resort, which is located near the "Empire".



Location

The address where the hotel "Empire" is located: Adler, Karl Marx, 12.

This vacation spot is within walking distance of the sea coast - literally 160 meters away. Nearby you can visit a couple of large stadiums and a museum. There are several large grocery stores in the area. Not far from the hotel there is a large city park "Southern Cultures", which, if desired, can be visited with young children - there will also be entertainment for them.

Two kilometers from the Empire Hotel (Adler) is the world-famous Olympic Village, where there are several ski slopes. Due to the proximity of this facility, the hotel is incredibly popular among fans of skiing who come in the cold season.

The nearest airport is 6 kilometers from the hotel. Due to such proximity from it to the "Empire" without much effort and high costs can be reached by taxi. If desired, any tourist has the opportunity to take advantage of the transfer service provided by this place for relaxation.

Rooms

All the rooms that are presented in the fund are located in one small building, their total number is 10. The apartments are modernly renovated and new furniture is installed. Guests in their comments often note interesting design decisions applied in the overall picture. All of them are presented in three categories: standard, family and luxury.



The apartments are equipped with individual air conditioning, as well as heating, which allows you to enjoy the warmth in winter. They also have bathrooms equipped with everything you need to maintain hygiene. Many guests of the Imperia Hotel (Adler) talk about how bath sets are provided in the bathroom, which are strictly designed for the number of guests, as well as high-quality soap products.

Standard

Rooms of this category offer conditions for comfortable accommodation for a couple of adult guests. They have a large double bed, which is covered with expensive linens, and also has a pair of side tables. In addition, to store a small amount of clothing that tourists bring with them, a wardrobe made of light wood is offered here. Some rooms have a small sofa, which can serve as a wonderful area for relaxing and drinking hot drinks.

Includes a large plasma TV with cable channels and a minibar. If desired, residents can rent a small safe for the entire period of stay in the "Empire".

Family

Fans of holidays with children can comfortably stay in special type apartments that are perfect for this category of travelers. They provide for the division of the common space into two rooms, of which both are designed for sleep and relaxation. They have large double beds and standard furniture necessary for storing clothes. If necessary, guests can make a request for the installation of a crib type "playpen", designed for a child under the age of 10 years. One of the rooms has a small furniture set for relaxing, consisting of a small glass table and a pair of ottomans.

Services

Each guest who is in the Imperia hotel (Adler, Karl Marx St.) receives a whole range of services aimed at improving living conditions. Among them, those most often in demand are those offered by laundry and dry cleaning. As for the cleaning of the rooms, it is carried out free of charge, twice a week. In reviews of the Empire Hotel (Adler), one can often find the opinion that local maids work quite responsibly and well, leaving behind clean rooms.

Completely free here they can offer a car parking service for those who come in their vehicle. Also, the attention of the guests is offered a 24-hour reception desk - here administrators can always help with resolving any issues, as well as suggest the location of city facilities.



Nutrition

In the building of the Empire Hotel (Adler) there is a very beautiful restaurant that absolutely anyone can visit. Every day it offers original dishes prepared in the best traditions of Russian, Caucasian and European cuisines. According to tourists, there is a team of professional chefs who can surprise any gourmet.

On some photos of the Empire Hotel (Adler) you can observe the interior of the restaurant, which is designed in a classic style. It contains a large number of details made of wood and velvet, which give the whole picture a certain sophistication.

Prices

The minimum cost of a room in the Empire Hotel (Adler) is from 4,500 rubles per day - standard class apartments are offered for it. As for the luxury category, the lowest price for a stay in them starts at 6,000 rubles.

Many guests in their comments consider such rates to be quite acceptable, since the service here is very good. Also, vacationers often note the presence of a huge number of interesting objects nearby (Plaza City, Adler Arena, shopping centers, a curling center, a museum of the history of the Adler region).
