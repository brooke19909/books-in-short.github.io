Hotel Pola Costa Apt 3 * (Protaras, Cyprus): overview, description and reviews of tourists

Cyprus is considered one of the most popular tourist destinations. Every year, tourists flock here from all over the world, wanting to have a good time on one of the picturesque coasts. Moreover, the island has a lot of historical attractions, so a beach vacation can be combined with interesting excursions.

As a stopping place in Cyprus, many travelers choose the city of Protaras, where tourist life is concentrated. And if you are looking for a comfortable hotel, you should pay attention to the hotel complex Pola Costa Apt. Of course, to begin with, it is worth learning about the features of this place, as well as about the impressions that remained with the former guests.

Where to look for a hotel? Description of location and territory





The location of the hotel is an important point when choosing a stopping place. Where is the hotel Pola Costa Apt 3 * located? Cyprus, Protaras, a picturesque place almost on the very shore of the Mediterranean Sea - this is where the hotel complex is located. This is a rather lively resort area, as there are a large number of hotels, restaurants and bars nearby.

The distance to the city center with all its attractions and amusements is only 2 kilometers - it is easy to overcome even on foot. The nearest airport is in Larnaca, about 55 km away. By the way, the hotel and travel agencies provide their customers with a shuttle service.

Hotel Pola Costa Hotel Apts (Cat B) 3 * is relatively small and consists of one four-story building, built in oriental style. Not so long ago, the hotel was renovated, so guests will find comfort and modern amenities. The territory of the complex is large and faces directly to the sea. There are terraces for relaxation, sports grounds, neat lawns and even a small garden. Spending time here is a pleasure.

Dining for guests: what does the hotel offer?





What kind of food can you count on when paying for a room at the Pola Costa Apt 3 * hotel? Cyprus is a country famous for its hospitality and cuisine. When you settle in a hotel, you will be able to independently choose the appropriate food plan. Most tourists prefer breakfast and dinner in the hotel restaurant. Guest reviews confirm that the food is good here. Breakfast is standard, continental, but the menu for dinner is more diverse - they offer different dishes of fish, meat and seafood.

In addition, the hotel has two bars where guests can enjoy a cocktail, a chilled drink, strong drinks and even some snacks.

In any case, food is not a problem, because Protaras is a popular resort city, and, therefore, it is full of cafes and various kinds of catering establishments.

Conveniences and conditions for entertainment on the beach





Of course, when traveling to the island, the traveler wants to soak up the gentle rays of the sun on the picturesque coast. What can the hotel complex Pola Costa Beach Apt 3 * offer? Protaras is a place famous for its picturesque beaches, and one of them is located just 20 meters from the hotel building.

This is a private, secluded stretch of coast, where only hotel guests rest. For their convenience, there are sun loungers and sun umbrellas on the shore. According to reviews, the beach here is very clean and beautiful, the entrance to the sea is comfortable, the bottom is covered with soft sand, and the depth increases gradually, so kids are happy to swim here.

On the beach, travelers can have a good time - they have the opportunity to ride boats, boats and waterskiing, do parasailing and sailing, try diving and windsurfing.

Hotel Pola Costa Apt 3 * (Cyprus, Protaras): comfort and leisure of guests





For the most part, travelers use the hotel for a comfortable overnight stay. Nevertheless, on its territory you can count on some additional conditions. For example, they offer ironing and laundry services (prices are not too high). And at the hotel at a favorable rate, you can exchange money. Hotel Pola Costa Apt 3 * has a large parking lot. By the way, renting vehicles in the city is not difficult.

You can swim and relax lying on the wide sunbeds by the pool: the water here is fresh, clean and always warm. Travelers have a good time playing table tennis and billiards. And also Cyprus is a place for excursions, because the history of the country is ancient and incredibly interesting. You can even get a guided tour on site.

What do tourists say? Reviews and recommendations from former guests

Sometimes in a few minutes of talking with a person who has already managed to visit your chosen hotel, you can get a lot of additional information. Reviews about the hotel Pola Costa Apt 3 * are mostly positive. This is a good three star hotel where you can relax. A huge advantage is the presence of its own beach, which is located just a few meters.

The hotel itself is comfortable, the rooms are quite spacious. Despite the fact that in some rooms the furniture and appliances are not new, everything works properly, and none of the guests complains about the functioning of the bathroom. They clean the rooms daily, so it is always a pleasure to relax here. The staff are very polite, always ready to help and provide the necessary information. By the way, some workers understand Russian well, which is convenient for tourists who do not speak English.

Hotel Pola Costa Apt 3 * is really well suited for a budget, but, nevertheless, comfortable stay on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea.
