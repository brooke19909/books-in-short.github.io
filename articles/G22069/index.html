Brooch tie - an original decoration for many lovers to wear jewelry

Many women (and men too) like to wear various types of jewelry. Be it rings, earrings, necklaces, hairpins, bracelets, brooch ties and much more.

Someone purchases them in special stores, and someone creates them with their own hands. Someone prefers expensive jewelry, while others are satisfied with cheaper analogues.

Expensive jewelry speaks of the wealth of their owner. But cheap jewelry can say a lot about its owner. The most important thing is to be able to choose the right material, color and size of the decoration.





Not many can afford to buy expensive things. Therefore, do-it-yourself necklaces, rings, brooch ties and so on are an excellent solution for jewelry lovers.

Consider, for example, what kind of needlework you can make a brooch tie. Kanzashi, southeast weaving, beadwork and others are excellent options. A tie made using the kanzashi technique emphasizes the individuality of the wearer.

DIY brooch tie

First, the necessary materials are taken: ribbons (satin, lace) of different colors, scissors, glue gun (PVA can be used), beautiful beads, beads, rhinestones, brooch clasp, lighter, candle, decorative elements and so on.

The color of the future brooch is selected (the color of a certain dress or suit, or neutral, suitable for any clothes).



In the figure, you can sketch the desired product.

The size is selected. Under some clothes fit a small but expressive tie. Under another - huge and bright.

An example of creating a favorite accessory

Ribbons are being prepared. One satin, about twenty-seven centimeters, three satin and three lace - sixteen centimeters each, six satin - fourteen centimeters each. All of them are folded in half and glued together, moreover, sixteen-centimeter glued together like this: one satin inside, one lace outside glued together. For bonding, you can use glue or a molten candle. You can just sew it.

Next is the process of gluing the resulting workpieces together. Two small satin are glued to a long section, one lace on them, then two more small and lace. And so on until the end. That is, seven levels are obtained.

The next step is to make a bow. It can also be made from pieces of satin and lace ribbons by gluing together crosswise. You can form a satin ribbon with a rose or other flower. He will stick on the resulting tail. In the center of the bow or flower, you can glue a large beautiful bead or cabochon, or decorate with rhinestones.



The resulting brooch tie is perfect for parties and celebrations. If the choice of material fell on more calm, neutral tones, and the decoration is not very catchy, then the accessory can be suitable for business meetings.

A brooch-tie made of ribbons at a meeting is always striking and can become an inconspicuous impetus when solving business problems. Moreover, it is perfect as a gift to your loved ones.
