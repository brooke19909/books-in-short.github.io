Samsung 34-inch Ultra-wide Curved Premium Monitor for Computer - Review and Reviews

For users who demand top quality pictures and a screen that fills the entire field of view, the famous South Korean company has released an ultra-wide curved premium monitor. The full name of the model is UltraWide Samsung S34E790C, and it boasts a giant 34-inch diagonal.

Like most advanced computer innovations, this one owes much to its appearance and characteristics to computer games. After all, the gamer is very important maximum overview of the virtual world, as well as a feeling of complete immersion in the process. Most recent games use the ‚ÄúHOR +‚Äù technology - this means that the larger the screen width, the more the picture fits on it, without changing its height - that is, an additional side view appears. Therefore, the aspect ratio of the monitors becomes 21: 9. The 34-inch curved monitor from Samsung not only meets this requirement, but also gives the user a resolution of 3440 x 1440 pixels, which provides unprecedented picture quality.



Specifications

In addition to the parameters already listed, this model boasts an LCD panel with a refresh rate of 60 Hz, which reduces flicker, improves screen comfort and safety for vision. The technology of "Vertical Alignment" ensures the saturation and clarity of colors from different viewing angles, the maximum value of which is equal to 178 degrees vertically and horizontally.

Response time is only 4 ms, typical brightness is 300 cd / m¬≤, display of 16.7 million colors is supported.

The curved Samsung monitor has two speakers of 7 watts each.

The list of ports is pretty standard, but it will satisfy almost all possible user requests: 1 DisplayPort for connecting the monitor itself; 4 USB sockets; 2 HDMI ports, a standard 3.5 mm headphone jack and, of course, a jack for the power cord.

Appearance

Samsung's curved monitor is impressive from an aesthetic point of view. Its size is striking - 82.15 x 36.4 x 5.15 cm, a slightly matte (as it is sometimes called ‚Äúsemi-glossy‚Äù) surface, which helps to maintain a relatively high level of definition compared to fully matte screens, while preventing the appearance of glare.

The bend of the model is very smooth and small. Another noteworthy aspect is the laconic frame in the form of glossy black plastic stripes. They are quite thin - only 11 mm above and on the sides and 13 mm at the bottom.



Solid base

The T-shaped stand in its form follows the curvature of the monitor. It imitates metal (actually made of silver plastic) and is rather large: 53.5 cm wide and 25.5 cm high. It is possible to adjust the angle and height of the screen.

The stand has a considerable load, because the curved monitor weighs 7.4 kg. However, she copes with it well: the screen is slightly staggered if you push the table, but when typing on the keyboard it remains motionless. The weight of the complete set is 9.9 kg, together with the packaging - 14.4 kg.

It is noteworthy that, unlike the stand, on the sides of the screen the manufacturer made a frame of metal stripes.

There is a small power LED on the right side of the bottom panel. By default, it lights up in blue when the monitor is in standby mode and does not light when the monitor is on, so as not to distract the user. These settings can be changed in the on-screen menu.



but on the other hand

The back of the monitor is decorated with black plastic with a metal texture.

The stand rests on a standard VESA mount 100 x 100 mm, so removing it, you can mount the monitor on the wall. All ports are assembled to the right of the stand, a little further from the bend of the screen.

There are holes in the base of the stand to gently lead and hide the wires.

Two speakers are located next to the ventilation holes, they can be seen only if you look at the monitor from below. This part of the body is finished with glossy plastic. The speakers are quite loud and clear, of course, they will not replace full-fledged speakers, but still their quality is much higher than the built-in analogs. In the on-screen menu, you can change the settings for sound profiles, there are 4 in total: "Standard", "Music", "Movies" and "Clear Voice". Considering what dimensions this curved monitor has, the built-in speakers will help save space on your desktop without much damage to the user.

And another interesting feature that can be found on the rear panel is the joystick button for calling up the on-screen menu and navigating through it. It is located on the right edge, if you look at the monitor in front, and it is quite easy to get it.



Impression of use

Technical specifications, of course, correspond to the level and price of the product, but how convenient are curved computer monitors from Samsung for the user?

The sheer volume of physical space and high resolution make it comfortable to work with the screen, and the gameplay or watching movies becomes much deeper and more realistic.

Many people think that the curvature of the screen will ruin and distort the picture, but here you will not find such a negative effect. The bend is smooth and subtle, which is its plus. When you work with this model for some time, you forget that it generally differs in something. Even the grid of the table in Microsoft Excel is not distorted, but for complex graphic tasks: processing photos, video, drawing - or for entertainment purposes, a large curved monitor gives a picture better than a standard one.

The manufacturer claims that a more uniform focal length (the edges of the screen are close to the eyes) creates a more comfortable and natural image and reduces eye strain. This is not to say that the curvature of the screen is a revolutionary technology that completely changes the perception of graphic information, but it certainly makes it more lively and exciting.

pros

Minuses

Factory settings give too bright, uncomfortable for the eyes picture with not the best color rendering. Therefore, you will have to reconfigure them in the on-screen menu.

Static contrast is slightly lower than other similar models.

Using an HDMI cable, the monitor works at a screen refresh rate of 50 Hz, which causes a decrease in response speed. However, when connected via DisplayPort, you can go to 60 Hz and this drawback is almost eliminated.

Despite the assurances of manufacturers, monitors with a curved screen may not appeal to all users. But this is more a sphere of individual preferences.
