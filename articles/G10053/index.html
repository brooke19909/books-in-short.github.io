Warm and decorative knee socks: patterns and description

In the off-season and cold season, such interesting accessories as knitting needles become very relevant. The schemes and description of most models are quite simple, therefore, it is quite accessible even to beginners in knitting.



Types of leggings

Depending on the functions that these products are called upon to perform, they can be:

  • Dense or openwork.
  • Long or very short.
  • With a flat bottom edge or with a foot loop.

As a rule, warming the lower part of the legs is the main task that is laid on knitted leggings with knitting needles. Schemes and a description of dense or openwork patterns are equally suitable, since a fabric knitted from a warm thread with a high wool content will in any case be warm.

However, there are models designed for decorative use. For example, long leggings made of cotton or linen. They are worn with closed shoes or short boots when the air temperature is already well above zero. Naturally, only openwork patterns should be used to knit such leggings with knitting needles (it is better to select patterns and description with small openwork holes).

Long leggings for dancing

The most popular model was among the craftswomen, whose children are engaged in dance groups. These accessories are knitted extremely simply: the ability to perform only front and back loops will allow you to get beautiful and functional leggings with knitting needles. Schemes and descriptions for girls are given below.



Making leggings 42 cm long

To work, you will need 150 grams of mixed yarn with a thickness of 250 m / 100 grams (30-50% wool, the rest is acrylic or cotton) and knitting needles No. 3-4. The density of knitting will be 22 loops per 10 cm.

On flat or circular knitting needles, dial 68 loops. Knitting is performed as follows:

  • Two front loops, two purl for each circular row.
  • When working with straight knitting needles, the sequence for each front row is similar. In the wrong row, all the loops are knitted according to the pattern.

So, with a 2x2 elastic band, you should knit 7 cm of fabric, then you need to go to the front surface and thus perform another 28 cm.

Seven centimeters of elastic completes knitting. At the last stage, all loops are freely closed, knitted leg warmers are washed and dried with knitting needles. Character Decoding

  • The empty cell is the front.
  • The circle is the wrong one.
  • Arc - yarn.
  • Pyramid - knit three loops together so that the middle is outside.
  • Pyramid to the right - three loops knitted together with a slope to the right.
  • The pyramid to the left is the same element, but with a slope to the left.
  • Triangle to the right - two loops with an inclination in the corresponding direction.
  • The triangle to the left is the same element, but with a slope to the left.

Leg warmers

This model is designed to prevent slipping of the leggings while putting on shoes. It should be noted that it is extremely convenient, however, its appearance may seem a little rude.

The photo below shows long colored leggings with knitting needles, the diagrams and description for their manufacture are almost the same as for the previous model.



An exception is the execution of a cut for the heel:

  • At a height of 5 cm from the start of work, divide the number of loops in half and mark with markers.
  • The part of the row that is designed to accommodate the hole should be tied with a contrasting thread. The rest of the fabric is knitted according to the pattern with the main thread.
  • At the end of the work, the colored thread introduced is carefully pulled out, while transferring the loops to two knitting needles.
  • The resulting loops are closed in any convenient way (knitting or crocheting).

Such leggings also look very good in openwork.

Knitting needles: diagrams and description for beginners

The most fascinating can be called knitting short leg cuffs, designed for use with shoes with high shafts. Most often, such accessories are worn with rubber boots. Such leggings effectively warm the legs, preventing moisture from penetrating the wide upper edge of the boots.

Short leggings can be connected in just a few hours, which makes them also a great idea for a presentation.



The yarn is exclusively woolen (or with a high content of warm natural fibers).

Knitting can be done as follows:

  • Type on the needles from 15 to 20 loops (depending on the thickness of the thread).
  • Knit according to the scheme a strip equal to the circumference of the leg in the widest place.
  • Sew the finished fabric with a knit seam.

An option for manufacturing leg warmers in circular rows is also possible. In this case, you should reduce the description of knitting long leggings to the desired number of rows (about 15 cm).
