Summer outfit for girls (crochet): tunic

Summer gives unlimited scope for creativity. Children's clothing is a prime example. How much can you knit for a girl (crochet). Tunic is one such product.





It can be connected in different manners. The tunic can be straight or slightly flared. Its top is permissible to do closed or on the straps.

Stripe under the bodice

So, create an outfit for the girl. Crocheted tunic can be knitted from the bottom up or from the starting strip up and down. Here is the second option.

Dial the chain. The number of loops should be such that is enough for the entire volume of the chest. At the same time, it should be a multiple of 18, it is from this number of loops that the pattern repeats. Plus two more (for the formation of edges).

The first row is made by connecting posts, it will require one lifting loop. Second row: four loops (three of them are for lifting, the fourth is for the pattern), the double crochet is in the third of the previous row, repeat the air and the column to the end of the row. Complete with another column.





Since this is a tunic dress for a girl (crochet), flowers are useful here. The next two rows will give just such a picture.

The third and fourth rows are convenient to see in the figure below. The fifth and sixth repeat the second and first. The strip is complete.





Sleeveless bodice

So that such an outfit for a girl (crochet) - a tunic - could be worn on a naked body, it is better not to make it openwork. Alternatively, you can use double crochet. For a change, it is recommended to alternate them with pico.

The first row above the strip is completely connected from the double crochet, making a climb of three air loops. In the second row, evenly distribute fragments with pico. They will be formed by three air loops closed on two vertices of the columns of the previous row. Above them in the next row will be two air loops.

The third row should also consist only of double crochets. In the fourth row, place the pico in a checkerboard pattern and continue knitting (crochet). The tunic for the girl assumes the presence of an armhole and a neckline. They need to be accurately calculated and the necessary removal of loops done.





Openwork hem

From this moment, crocheting an outfit for a girl (tunic) goes from a strip down. The work must begin with the row, which is indicated on the diagram by the number 1. The bottom row of this diagram coincides with the first strip.





In order for the hem of the tunic to slightly diverge downward, it is recommended that the number of air loops between the fans be gradually increased in the scheme. At first, they should not be at all. Then - several rows with one air loop. Then knit them already two. If necessary, it is permissible to increase their number to three.

Hem Lace Trim

With her, a tunic for a girl with a crochet (the schemes are presented in the article) will become more elegant and spectacular. The first row of the pattern consists of air loops and connecting posts. The first arch of 8 loops is located above the first three loops of the previous row, then there is a connecting column. Next, there are three loops and another connecting one, so that the chain of three loops is parallel to the main pattern. Three more and a connecting column connected as described for the previous ones. And so on until the end of the hem circle.

In the second row in each loop of a large arch, you need to make a crochet column. Moreover, the extreme ones, that is, the first and eighth, need to be fixed on a connecting column located between two chains of three air loops.

Third row: at each vertex, connect one column with a crochet, which are divided among themselves by one air loop. Do not make a gap from the loop only at the junction of two fans.

Fourth: an arch of three air loops, which is attached to the air of the previous row by connecting posts. If you want to make the pattern more dense, then supplement it with another row, which consists entirely of connecting posts.





Flower for decorating tunics

You can attach a modest rose to her bodice. Moreover, it is not difficult to knit. To get started, dial a chain of an even number of loops. In order for the flower to be large enough, they should not be less than 40.

The first row requires three lifting loops. Then, in every second loop, you need to tie a small fan: two double crochet columns separated by one air loop.

The second row is formed by larger fans in the arch from one previous air. They consist of two pairs of double crochet posts, between which there are three aerial.

The last row - these are large fans: 9 columns with a crochet in the arches of the fans of the previous row. In the process of knitting, the strip will already twist. This direction should be used when creating a rose. Fold a neat flower and stitch it. It remains only to fix the rose on the tunic bodice.
