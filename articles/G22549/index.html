Master class "Weaving bracelets from beads"

How many people are so many hobbies. Each person has his own favorite and very interesting occupation only for him. Some people like to collect stamps, coins and notes, spending hours searching through a magnifying glass for subtle differences. The second cannot live without green plants and flowers, turning their home into a greenhouse. And some people love to invest a piece of their soul in needlework, in particular - in weaving bracelets. Jewelry and various accessories will always be in fashion, so it is not surprising that a huge number of both women and men resort to decorating their bodies with various hand and factory jewelry.

Currently, there are a huge number of materials to create truly stunning products. Weaving bracelets, necklaces, belts and other accessories is one of the most popular hobbies among needlewomen. So, you can try your hand at creating a small but cute bead jewelry. For example, in the style of braid weaving. To do this, you will need the source material of several colors, a fishing line or thread, thin needles and a little patience.

At the first stage, you will need to create a chain of bead crosses. To do this, on a fishing line from both ends we string needles. Then we collect initially 4 beads. We pierce the last, extreme bead once more, so that a cross forms.









After that, one more bead must be inserted into each of the needles, and the third - common - will close the second element of the chain.





And so we continue to achieve the required length. This cross chain is the basis of the bracelet. Other elements will be attached to it on the sides. You should pay attention to the fact that such weaving of beaded bracelets is a little time-consuming task and requires great care.

Remember that if you want this accessory to fit, the length of the chain should be approximately 1.3-1.5 of the length of the girth of the wrist. This is explained simply - weaving bracelets in a twisted technique reduces the initial length, so the initially selected proportions may be incorrect.

At the second stage, additional beads are added to the created chain from the sides. They can be either similar or perfect in a different color. In the master class, the color of pearls is selected. To create the first part of the second chain, we string three beads on the left (upper) fishing line, and one on the right (lower) line. Then we connect them.









After this, the needle, which was in the right hand, passes into the next bead of the previous chain and one bead is strung on it, and two on the second fishing line. Then the link closes.





Thus we continue weaving to the end of the chain. In a similar way, we draw up its second side.

After the braiding of the cross chain is over, you need to connect the right side with the left. To do this, a fishing line is threaded into the upper beads of one and the second side. Then a bead is strung on one of the needles, and the second needle passes through it, connecting the jewelry into a single whole.













So it is necessary to do with each bead on two sides. Due to the fact that on the side faces of the beads is twice as much as on the main chain, the product is twisted in a spiral. Weaving bracelets of this type is also called twisted. In the end, you should get such a sample.





We attach a lock to it and the accessory is ready.





Weaving bracelets from ribbons, beads, floss threads or silk cords - there is no limit to the imagination of needlewomen, because you can create a product from anything. The most important principle is patience and love for your hobby.
