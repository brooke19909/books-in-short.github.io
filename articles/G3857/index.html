House made of natural materials. We make interesting crafts with our own hands

For the manufacture of crafts, better material than that created by Mother Nature herself cannot be found. Pebbles, branches, fruits and vegetables, moss and flowers - this is not the whole list of what you can create original products from. How to make a house of natural material? From the information presented in the article, you will learn two ways to perform a similar product: from twigs and from pumpkin.



We make a house of natural materials with our own hands. Preparation of materials

We will make the product from tree branches and foliage. In addition to them, you will need such materials for work: cardboard, a thermogun or Dragon glue, plasticine, a stapler, a piece of plywood, an office knife. We will supplement the composition with such elements as a path, mushrooms, a tree. For their manufacture will need a variety of materials of natural origin: pebbles, moss, chestnuts, cones.

Description of the manufacturing process of the house

  1. Cut all the branches into identical pieces, about 20 centimeters. There should be 28 pieces. On each stick, recess from both edges.
  2. Form the house by fastening the workpieces from the branches together using a hot gun or glue. Do not forget to make holes that will serve as a window and a door. When all the walls are decorated, leave the product to dry, and begin to make the roof yourself.
  3. You can make a house of natural materials with your own hands with a flat or triangle-shaped roof . Make cardboard the basis for this part of the craft. If you want to build it in a triangular shape, bend the thick paper in three places, form a roof and secure the product with a stapler. From another sheet of cardboard, make the sides of the part and paste them into the base.
  4. Glue this part with dry leaves, starting from the bottom.
  5. On the plywood, install the box of sticks, attach the roof on top.
  6. Lay the path out of the pebbles. Paint a pine or fir cone with green gouache and stick it near the hut. From chestnuts and acorns you can make mushrooms, from moss - a flower bed.

Craft house made of natural materials with your own hands done. Such a composition will take its rightful place at any exhibition of such products.

Pumpkin is a product that is not only eaten. We make a hut from this vegetable

For work, prepare the following materials:

  • pumpkin;
  • dry leaves;
  • tops of corn;
  • toothpick;
  • knife;
  • plasticine;
  • Scotch.

How to make a pumpkin house? It is very simple. First cut off the top of the vegetable and scrub the seeds. Use a toothpick to draw the contours of the windows and doors. Cut these parts with a knife. Put the top of the pumpkin in place. Attach dry foliage on top of it with plasticine or tape. The roof is ready. Attach curtains from dry corn tops or leaves to the door and windows. That's all. Now it remains to settle residents in the house made of natural materials, with their own hands. It can be small toys or figures of people and animals made of acorns and chestnuts.
