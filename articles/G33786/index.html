Location

Hotel "Bastion" (Sudak) is located in Sudak Bay at the very foot of the Fortress Mountain (height - about 160 m). To the sea from the gates of the hotel is 150 meters. Nearby there is a local bazaar, shops, a museum, a marina where you can rent a boat, cafes, bars and, very importantly, a bus stop. Just a few steps from the hotel is a comprehensive medical center where you can get medical advice and medical procedures. Distances to large settlements in the Crimea from the hotel are: to Simferopol - 107 km, to Yalta - 131, to Feodosia - 55, to Alushta - 93, to the Old Crimea, famous for its historical and architectural sights, - 36, to Dzhankoy - 148 km, to Kerch - 153 and to Sevastopol - 180 km. Closest to all, at 7 km (this is a distance many hotel guests walk on foot as a walk), is the New World, a resort village famous for its champagne wines (there is a factory, a tasting room).

Important! The resort hotel "Bastion" (Sudak) is not located in the very center of the city, so neither loud music of discos nor the noise that usually occurs in resorts does not interfere with people sleeping at night. All that can be heard at night by opening a window is the singing of cicadas and the whisper of sea waves.





The mailing address of the hotel is: st. Ushakova, building number 3.

How to get there

An important advantage is the convenient location relative to the traffic intersection, which has a "Bastion" (hotel). Sudak is connected with all large settlements of the peninsula by a good asphalt road. To get to this hotel is simple, the main thing is to get to Simferopol. This is the most convenient starting point, as passenger and fast trains arrive here from all over Russia, and airplanes arrive from many regional centers of the country. Then you can go to the hotel by taxi or by bus. In the summer they go every 15-20 minutes. Tickets are always available at the ticket office of the Kurortnaya bus station, which is a stone's throw from the railway station. Looking for it is simple, the McDonald's restaurant serves as a guide. Travel time is a little over 2 hours. It costs up to 400 rubles per person. A taxi will leave about 2000 rubles per car.

By buses you can reach the hotel from other cities of Crimea, Feodosia, Yevpatoriya, Yalta, Alushta.

Those who wish can book a transfer from the airport or from the railway station of Simferopol.

In less than a hectare, the designers managed to set up a beautiful garden with flower beds, cypresses and other evergreens, and add a fountain colorful between them, build a decent pool with a bridge and a stone island in the center, arrange five buildings, a sauna, a bar, a restaurant, teahouse, cozy gazebos and parking. Here it is from the outside, the Bastion Hotel (Sudak). Photos of tourists demonstrate how good the area is.





Following the theme of the name, the designers diversified the appearance of the territory by placing a gun there and decorating some corners with antique chains. Plus, the whole territory is guarded 24 hours a day, and in the evening it is beautifully illuminated. As noted in the reviews, Wi-Fi decently works almost throughout the hotel.

Sea and climatic conditions

Many in the summer for the sake of the sea go to Crimea, Sudak. The Bastion Hotel, located just two to three minutes from the coast, is the place where a beach holiday is excellent. The hotel does not have its own beach, but the one nearby can well satisfy the needs of every vacationer. The soil here is sand and small pebbles, so special rubber slippers on their feet will not be superfluous. Entrance to the beach is free, but for sunbeds and umbrellas you have to shell out. But they will not let you get bored here. On the beach, leisure is organized quite well. This ride on a "banana", a catamaran and a jet ski, a variety of ball games, water slides. Nearby there are paid beaches where you can dive with scuba diving.

Leisure

"Bastion" is a hotel (Sudak), which in its small but cozy territory has a swimming pool for adults, and next to it is a "paddling pool" for kids, both with heating.
