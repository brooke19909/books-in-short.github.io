Hotel Golden Elephant Orenburg: reviews and photos

One of the most popular hotels in Orenburg is the Golden Elephant. The love of residents and visitors to this place is primarily due to the fact that the hotel offers excellent service and excellent living conditions. Often, guests in their reviews talk about the fact that in the "Golden Elephant" you can find acceptable conditions for tourists with different purposes of staying in a hotel.

Location

Hotel "Golden Elephant" is located in Orenburg, in the very central part of the city. Not far from it are such famous objects as the Musical Comedy Theater, as well as State University. In the same area there are several large shopping centers where goods of famous world manufacturers are offered, as well as entertainment for every taste. Near the hotel there is a city business center, which is especially convenient for those guests who are staying at the hotel for the purpose of a business visit.

Address of the "Golden Elephant": Orenburg, Rybakovskaya street, 59 B.



Rooms

The hotel offers several types of rooms, among which there is a "Standard", as well as those that have superior conditions. The second category includes such as: "Apartments", "Presidential" and "Suite".

The rooms of any level made a good repair. Only modern furniture is presented in the classic interior, which, according to vacationers, is very comfortable, which, in particular, concerns beds. Tourists who stayed at the Golden Elephant Hotel (Orenburg), in their comments often say that all the apartments available here are well equipped from a technical point of view.

All rooms have private bathrooms, where guests are offered a set of bath accessories. There is a complete set of plumbing necessary to maintain a clean body: a shower cabin (some rooms have a bathroom with a jacuzzi), a bathroom, and a sink.



"Standard"

Standard class apartments in the Golden Elephant Hotel (Orenburg) at first glance attract guests with their beautiful author's interior. They are located on a small area (about 35 sq.m.) and offer a set of furniture that are perfect for accommodating a couple of guests. Residents of such apartments are delighted to note that, being here, they can relax on a large double bed (in some rooms it is replaced by two single beds), covered with expensive linen. Near the berth there is a place to store clothes.

The room has a wonderful work area with good lighting. It is represented by a large desk made of wood, as well as a leather armchair, which is installed nearby.

Guests staying in Standard apartments can use dry cleaning and laundry services, as well as Internet access for free. As for the technical equipment, there is a minibar that is filled with chilled drinks, a telephone, air conditioning, a small refrigerator and a TV.

Prices

Before making your visit to the Liszt ORG "Golden Elephant" (Orenburg), you must book your desired apartments in advance. This can always be done by phone number, which is available on the main hotel website or by filling out an electronic form, which is located there.



As for the cost of living in hotel rooms, it is quite acceptable, given the whole range of offered opportunities and living conditions. Apartments of the "Standard" class here will cost from 3000 rubles per day. This price includes breakfast, which is prepared in the restaurant "Golden Elephant" (Orenburg). Transfer service

If necessary, the hotel can arrange a transfer service for the guest from the arrival point, which can be a railway station. It is necessary to agree on its provision in advance - in this case, at the appointed time, a comfortable passenger car will be waiting for the guest, which will take the tourist to the hotel in the most comfortable conditions in a short time.

Everyone can also use the services of private carriers. In this case, the tourist will have to indicate the exact address of the Golden Elephant Hotel in Orenburg, which is marked on its main website.
