Plaza Hotel, New York: address, photo of rooms, owner

The famous Plaza Hotel (New York) has long and deservedly been a symbol of a huge metropolis. It is located at the entrance to Central Park, on the Grand Army Plaza. This hotel is not only widely known in the world, it is recognized as one of the best in the USA.

Many events and great names are associated with it. When D. Trump acquired this hotel in 1988, he said that he became the owner of the pearl of world culture.



History

The opening of the hotel in 1907 was very solemn. Immediately they began to call him the greatest in the world. Its construction cost a fabulous amount for those times - $ 12 million. The famous American architect G. Hardenberg built a skyscraper (by the standards of those years) with a height of twenty floors. It is made in the style of a French chateau and has a rich interior - gilding, marble, mosaic, mirrors. The building is lit by 1,650 chandeliers.

This is the second hotel built on this site and having the same name. The previous building was demolished in 1905. In 1943, the Plaza Hotel (New York) acquired Conrad Hilton, paying almost seven and a half million dollars for it. He immediately began the reconstruction, which cost six million dollars.



After that, the hotel was resold several times. Its owner was the current presidential candidate, billionaire Donald Trump. In 1988, he bought the Plaza Hotel (New York) for $ 407.5 million. The current owner is the Israeli company Elad Properties.

The last reconstruction began at the end of April 2005. It cost the company a record $ 400 million. Three years later, the Plaza Hotel (New York), whose photo you can see in our article, opened its doors again. After reconstruction, it became 282 luxurious rooms and 152 private apartments. The first of them was purchased by an entrepreneur from Israel Lev Livaev for ten million dollars. The last purchase of an apartment cost the owner fifty million dollars. Today, you can also purchase apartments in this complex. The cost of an apartment of fifty square meters is 1.5 million dollars.

The Plaza Hotel (New York) has witnessed many historical events of world significance. So, in September 1985, an agreement was signed between the finance ministers of Japan, France, West Germany, the UK and the United States to reduce the dollar exchange rate relative to the currencies of these countries.

Plaza Hotel (New York): Address

The hotel is located at: New York, 37 E. 64th St. Its building and interiors were designed by architect G. Hardenberg, who took part in the construction of other hotels in the city. In 1978, the New York City Conservation Commission assigned the hotel a special status, which equated it with national historical monuments along with the Waldorf-Astoria Hotel.



From that moment, tourists began to visit the hotel with excursions. In the winter of 2009, such visits were discontinued. True, the entrance to the hotel's restaurants and bars remained accessible. Today, not only the hotel is recognized as historical monuments, but also eight of its premises - the Palm Court cafe, which has a glass dome, columns in the form of palm trees and caryatids, the Oak room, the Edwardian room, which has huge windows overlooking Central Park, the Pulitzer fountain, Big ballroom.

Celebrities at the Plaza Hotel

The rooms and suites of this gigantic complex amaze with their impressive size, luxury decoration, and exclusive decor. This has always attracted here world-famous guests.

Many celebrities stayed here at one time: singer Erta Kitt, writer Ernest Hemingway, Lisa Minelli, Truman Capote and the legendary Beatles band in full force.

Hotel Description

The Plaza Hotel (New York) is famous for its world-class restaurants and bars. Among them, one should highlight the elegant and refined champagne bar, the magnificent Rose Club, the stunning The Plaza Food Hall restaurant, which never ceases to amaze its guests with original dishes, as well as the very popular The Palm Court restaurant. It is considered the best institution in the city for afternoon tea.

Hotel guests can visit Shops at The Plaza, a spectacular shopping center. It has exclusive boutiques and great food stores. There is also a wellness center with wellness facilities.
