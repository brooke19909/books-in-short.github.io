Petersburg hotels: prices, reviews and photos

The main number of hotels in St. Petersburg is located in the city center. However, most of them are in houses that are monuments of architecture. The main places of leisure and attractions are usually nearby. This allows guests to combine cognitive activities with a business program and outdoor activities when they come to St. Petersburg.



Hotel "Moscow", for example, also provides an opportunity to save money, like other cheap hotels in the city. They provide their guests with the necessary services at reasonable prices, while in full accordance with European service and in the traditions of domestic hospitality.

More expensive establishments, such as the Oktyabrskaya Hotel (Petersburg), offer a very wide range of services. In addition, the atmosphere of luxury and comfort, which will immediately appreciate the domestic and foreign tourists.

This hotel is located in the city center near Nevsky Prospekt on the Neva Embankment. Excellent transport links (trams, trolleybuses, the metro entrance is located in the building) allows guests to get to the main historical sights: the Hermitage, the Palace Embankment, Kazan Cathedral, the Russian Museum, the Admiralty in 15 minutes.

Hotel Moscow (St. Petersburg) offers stunning views of the Alexander Nevsky Lavra (the oldest architectural ensemble of the eighteenth century), the Neva and Alexander Nevsky Bridge from the windows of the rooms. The nearest station is Alexander Nevsky Square.
