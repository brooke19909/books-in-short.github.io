Camp in Tyumen - "Energetik"

This children's recreation institution is considered an educational, suburban sports camp of a closed type, working throughout the year.





"Energetik" is located outside the city in an ecologically clean area, surrounded by a pine forest. It has everything you need for a comfortable stay:

- Landscaped buildings equipped with appliances and new furniture;

- gyms equipped with modern equipment;

- a platform for dancing;

- library;

- cinema hall;

- pool.

Camping

Children who came to rest at the Energetik camp in Tyumen are accommodated in stationary comfortable two-story buildings. Catering

Meals at the Energetik camp (Tyumen) are provided in spacious halls. Children eat 5 times a day. The menu is vitamin and balanced, provides for the daily use of meat and dairy products, fruits.





Wellness

The camp is called a wellness camp, since highly qualified medical personnel work on its territory, including competent pediatricians, psychologists, and masseurs. On its territory there are special treatment rooms in which exercise therapy, physiotherapy, massage and other medical procedures are held.
