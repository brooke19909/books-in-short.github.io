Take a look at the story

Boysman Bay of Primorsky Krai was developed several thousand years ago. Throughout all time, this territory was inhabited by peoples of different archaeological cultures. An expedition was organized to explore these places, during which participants were able to discover a unique monument. In addition to it, scientists found the remains of ceramic dishes, arrowheads, stone axes, fishing tackle made from bones and shells. Archaeologists believe that an abundance of coastal resources has led people to this bay.

Relaxation

What will surprise the Boysman Bay of Primorsky Territory with tourists? First of all, here is a paradise for people who prefer windsurfing. It is very easy to explain: the bay is constantly blown by southeast and south winds. Thanks to them, very high waves rise here.

This place is also ideal for fishermen. On the north side of the coast there are many lakes rich in various species of underwater representatives. Those who come to the bay for fishing, it will be useful to know that on the south side there is a great place where a river flows into the sea. Pestle and minnow are found in large numbers here.

Naturally, the Boysman Bay of the Primorsky Territory is not only sea open spaces, but also beautiful picturesque landscapes. You can view all this surrounding charm by climbing to Cape Red Cliff. It organizes a variety of excursions, hiking and horseback riding, boat trips and other entertainment.
