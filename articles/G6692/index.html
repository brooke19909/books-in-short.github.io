How to color pasta for crafts with children?

To diversify creative activities with children, you can use non-standard materials for crafts. Many interesting and useful for creativity can be found even in any kitchen. True, in their usual form, familiar food products are little similar to the material for creativity. How to color pasta for crafts with children?

Gouache is a universal dye in the world of children's creativity



Gouache paints can be found in every child‚Äôs arsenal. With their help, you can not only create masterpieces of painting, but also paint any small elements. How to color pasta for crafts using gouache? The technology is quite simple: determine the amount of material for painting and place it in a suitable container or ordinary plastic bag. Mix paint with water and pour into pasta. Mix the contents of the bag or container well and leave for 10 minutes. Then it remains only to drain the liquid, and leave the pasta to dry on a flat surface. A little secret: the more water you add to the staining solution, the less bright the finished color will turn out.

There is another way how to color pasta for gouache crafts. Lay out all the pasta you plan to use on a piece of paper. Apply paint with a brush, not diluting with water. During the first stage, you will be able to paint 70% of each product. Wait for the paint to dry, then turn the pasta over and paint the other side. If pasta has large holes, you can string them on sticks or thread and hang. Having fixed the pasta in a similar position, you can paint them on all sides in one go.

Food colorings for ornamental pasta



You can color pasta with food dyes. Start by reviewing the instructions for use of the selected substance. How to color pasta for DIY food colorings yourself? Be careful, paints of this category are sufficiently eaten into the skin, it is better to work with gloves. As with gouache, place the material in a plastic bag or container. Food coloring can be diluted with water, a solution of vinegar or alcohol. For best results, we recommend that you follow the paint manufacturer's instructions. The coloring solution is poured into a container with pasta and mixed well, then left for 5-10 minutes. Painted material is dried outdoors.

Non-standard paints and original technologies for their application



Do you know how to color pasta for crafts using regular greenery? From a diamond pharmacy solution, a coloring liquid can be prepared. If you add a little bit of green, the finished product will turn out gently turquoise. When using a large amount of solution, a rich green color of macaronin can be achieved. Staining is carried out according to the same scheme as with the use of food colors. The pasta is soaked in the solution for 5-10 minutes and dried. As a coloring matter, you can also use spices and products in bright colors. Turmeric and cocoa solutions add beautiful colors to pasta. You can also paint ornamental pasta with nail polishes. And this is very convenient, since each bubble has its own brush. How to paint pasta for crafts with acrylic paint? The material for coloring should be laid out on a flat surface. Acrylic paints are applied with brushes; it is recommended to wait at least 12 hours before they dry completely.

Pasta craft ideas



Conventional food pasta is an excellent material for children's games and creativity. The youngest researchers will be interested to just delve into the box with colorful pasta. Children over three years old with the help of an adult will be able to make a variety of and interesting crafts. Stringing pasta with holes on a rope, you can make beads or garlands. Almost all types of pasta produce colorful applications. Try combining pasta of various sizes and shapes. Interestingly, this material looks in combination with plasticine and salt dough. Now you know how to color pasta for crafts. It remains to dream a little, and you will definitely get bright and original products.
