Tapestry seam - secrets of thread painting

Refined art that does not leave people's minds is not the first millennium, tapestry embroidery transforms homes and offices, organically blending into any interior style. Those who at least once saw the final picture embroidered in this technique will never forget the feeling of painting with threads on fabric.

Tapestry and half cross .

In the lover's opinion, tapestry embroidery is nothing complicated, different from embroidering with a cross with just one stitch in a certain direction. But this is not so. The semi-cross technique involves the following pattern: the canvas or embroidery fabric cell intersects from the front side diagonally from left to right, and from the inside from the upper right to the lower right.

Unlike half-cross, tapestry stitch provides for the use of two cells at once for one stitch. In order to embroider one stitch using the tapestry technique, it is necessary to bring the needle to the front side from the upper right corner and introduce it to the wrong side in the lower left corner. The next stitch is executed with a broach from the wrong side of the thread to the upper right corner of the next square.
