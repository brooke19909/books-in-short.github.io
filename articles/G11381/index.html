Description and address

At the entrance to the city in the direction from Moscow, the Sport Hotel is small in size. In Yaroslavl, there has long been an Ice Palace, which was built in 1997. This sports arena owes its appearance to the local hockey team Torpedo, which at that time became the country's champion. At the end of the nineties of the last century, it became clear that the local Sports Palace does not satisfy the needs of fans. Therefore, it was decided to build the complex UKKS "Arena 2000". Almost immediately, the Sport Hotel appeared in Yaroslavl (8 Mayorova St.).

It is located in the green zone of the city, in close proximity to the Arena 2000 and the Atlant sports complex. You can get to the center by public transport from a stop located a two-minute walk away.

The distance from the Sport Hotel in Yaroslavl (the phone can be found in the city directory) is only five and a half kilometers. The same need to go to the train station. The airport is 19.2 kilometers away.
