Park Hotel "Stavropol" 4 *: description, photos and reviews

Ladies and gentlemen, if you come to the beautiful city of Stavropol, welcome to the wonderful, magnificent, chic and just wonderful Park Hotel Stavropol.

This hotel complex is modern, comfortable, functional. Here, professionalism and high quality service are in the first place, which, however, is taken care of by all the hotels in Stavropol. Park Hotel always joyfully opens its doors to guests. Everything here is designed to ensure that every visitor is satisfied with a friendly atmosphere, comfort, delicious European cuisine, excellent service. Each guest is treated like a royal person, with all honors.





Population

At the time of check-in, the guest is immediately taken their luggage and taken to the apartment, and the car, if the guest arrived on it, is driven off to free parking. Here they can wash it for an extra charge so that it shines.

The visitor has the opportunity directly on the spot to choose a free room, as well as optionally book it on the hotel website or by phone.

For accommodation, payment is made in cash or by bank transfer.

Estimated time is 12 noon. The reception is open around the clock, so guests have no time limits. Park Hotel Stavropol (address: 47-b Dovatortsev St., Stavropol) opens its doors to guests around the clock.

Rooms

Hotel Park Hotel Stavropol is a hotel that combines all the amenities.

It offers 40 rooms of special comfort. Each is made in a classic style. The situation is new, all materials and equipment are high quality.

Rooms vary in size. There are very small ones, but they are incredibly cozy and concise, and there are large, luxurious ones. Everywhere is Italian furniture. You can pre-book rooms if you decide to visit the Park Hotel Stavropol. You can find out the telephone number on its official website or in the telephone directories of the city.

Also in the apartment at the disposal of the guest is:

  • Bath and shower.
  • Towels, soap, shampoo.
  • Hair dryer.
  • Electric kettle.
  • Extra blanket and blanket.
  • Slippers.
  • Air conditioning.
  • Plasma TV with remote control.
  • Furniture: sofa, table, chairs, chest of drawers, wardrobe, armchair.
  • Table lamp or floor lamp.
  • Wardrobe.

Conference hall

A few words should be given to the room where the conference room is located. He is huge. The area is 130 square meters. m. Due to this, he can simultaneously accommodate about 100 people. This is useful if seminars and other events are held.

The hall has special equipment, and it is also equipped with the following:

  • Screen, projector.

  • Mineral water.

In order to rent a conference room, you have to pay 1,500 rubles per hour. For conference participants, the hotel provides a very flexible system of discounts on accommodation.

A restaurant

This gourmet restaurant is located in the Stavropol Park Hotel. It is located under a glass dome on the top floor of the hotel.

The restaurant is called Paradise. Here you can have a leisurely breakfast, dine in a pleasant atmosphere and spend an amazing romantic dinner. The restaurant has an atmosphere of luxury. Paradise furnishings are made in the Greek style. The hall has columns and bas-reliefs.

The menu is represented by European cuisine, as well as author-made dishes. The restaurant sounds pleasant music, the level of service of the highest class.

Reviews
