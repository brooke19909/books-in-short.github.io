Holiday Village "Vokhotka": description, features, services and reviews of tourists

If you have long dreamed of a quiet and relaxing holiday on the lake, the recreation center "Vokhotka" is ideal for you. Here you can feel complete unity with nature, take a break from the bustle of the city and restore your strength.





Brief description of the hotel

The recreation center "Vokhotka" is a complex of cottages on the very shore of the lake. This is a great place for a relaxing or active holiday, friendly gatherings or a romantic trip. Despite the remoteness from urban civilization, this recreation center has the most modern infrastructure. Also, a lot of outdoor activities have been prepared for you (sports, boating, etc.).

The recreation center "Vokhotka" offers you accommodation in comfortable wooden cottages, where a cozy home atmosphere reigns. Here you will be given the opportunity to enjoy the most modern amenities that you are accustomed to in the city. From the terrace of some houses you can enjoy the lake.

Choosing for your vacation such an option as recreation center "Vokhotka", you can not worry about the safety of the car, because there is a guarded parking lot. You can completely restore your strength and improve your health by visiting a traditional Russian bath. And interesting rest and good mood will provide you with forest walks or fishing on the lake.





Location: Vokhotka, recreation center

This institution is located in the village of Olkhovka in the Leningrad Region. In the immediate vicinity is the picturesque lake Sukhodolskoe. This place is located about 70 kilometers from St. Petersburg. The nearest motorway is 10 kilometers from the recreation center, and Pulkovo International Airport is 120 km away.

The structure of the recreation center

The recreation center consists of five lines of cottages:

  • "Lazurnaya" consists of 5 houses located directly on the lake. Also nearby is a traditional Russian bathhouse. The rooms offer the most scenic views.
  • "Rowan" line is a complex of 6 cottages, each of which can accommodate up to six people. The peculiarity of the apartment is a cozy rustic design.
  • Amber is one of the most economical options. The low price somewhat compensates for the lack of a beautiful view of the lake. In all other aspects, the cottages are as comfortable as on the previous lines.
  • A distinctive feature of the "Nut" line is that the cottages are equipped with a sauna. In addition, it is the central part of the recreation center, from where it is convenient to get to any of its points.
  • The Golden Line is a complex of 6 cottages, each of which is designed to accommodate 10 people. They have a high level of comfort and have not only a sauna, but also washing machines, kitchens and other amenities.




Room Amenities

Comfortable living conditions are offered to their guests by Vokhotka, a recreation center. Guest reviews indicate the availability of the following amenities:

  • comfortable beds with orthopedic mattresses (can be combined or shared);
  • TV connected to a satellite dish;
  • bedside tables where you can place your cosmetics and other personal items;
  • spacious wardrobe for your clothes;
  • a powerful refrigerator that will keep your products fresh;
  • an electric kettle and dishes so that you can please yourself with hot drinks at any time;
  • combined bathroom with shower;
  • The bathroom has an electric hairdryer, bath accessories, and a set of cosmetics.




Hotel Infrastructure

This tourist base has the following infrastructure facilities:

  • private parking area with video surveillance and 24-hour security;
  • throughout the territory there are arbors equipped with barbecue facilities;
  • the reception is open around the clock, and therefore you can always get answers to your questions;
  • large terrace with sun loungers;
  • traditional Russian baths;
  • special entertainment rooms where board games, karaoke and more await you;
  • a large playground with many attractions;
  • a restaurant of traditional Russian cuisine;
  • a bar with alcoholic and refreshing drinks;
  • children's buffet.

Tourists comments

Rest in this institution is characterized by the following negative aspects:

  • since there is no air conditioning in the rooms, it is simply impossible to stay there in hot weather;
  • the rooms do not have double beds - guests are offered to sleep on shifted one and a half;
  • the iron is only at the reception and it is the only one for the entire hotel (besides, you have to carry the ironing board in your room yourself);
  • a huge drawback is the lack of a private beach;
  • not very tasty breakfasts;
  • there are not enough benches and arbors in the territory;
  • dirty bedspreads in the rooms;
  • there is no practice of congratulating the birthday on behalf of the hotel;
  • there are very few trees and other plantings on the territory.

Here you can hide from the bustle of the city and enjoy the beautiful nature. There is a forest on one side and a picturesque lake on the other. It is worth noting a lot of accommodation options that differ in cost and level of comfort, as well as quite reasonable prices for accommodation and meals (and this despite the remote location). Great attention is paid to the leisure of vacationers (both adults and children).
