Nikon 3200 camera: review, photo, reviews

The Nikon-3200 camera, the review of which is presented below, is positioned by the manufacturer as an entry-level SLR camera, which is aimed at fans. According to expert reviews, at the time of its appearance, the model for a number of characteristics looked noticeably more attractive in comparison with many direct competitors. First of all, this concerns the cost of the device.



general description

The appearance of the camera has not changed in any way compared to its predecessor - the Nikon-3100 model. The only exception is the panel with the shutter button, which has moved down. Although this appearance is very harmonious and neat, it is still outdated. The black Nikon-3200 case is made of matte plastic, the quality of which is hardly high, with rubberized inserts. The creators of the model also saved on tactile sensations. However, do not forget that it is designed for lovers, most of whom will not pay attention to these flaws. The developers also provided a red version. The dimensions of the camera are 125x96x76.5 millimeters, and its weight without a battery is 505 grams.

On the front side of the device there is a receiver for remote control, auxiliary illumination of automatic focus, as well as a large button that is used to change the lens. Main technical parameters

The Nikon-3200 camera is equipped with a 24 megapixel CMOS-matrix, which can be called a rather interesting idea of ‚Äã‚Äãdevelopers for a budget amateur device. It performs well at high ISO values ‚Äã‚Äãand boasts a wider dynamic range not only in comparison with the previous version of the camera, but also with many competitors in its segment. On the other hand, such a matrix makes the model very sensitive to the choice of optics, because such a large number of pixels must be provided with good light. According to expert reviews, the lens, which is included in the standard package, does not cope with this task in the best way. As for the shooting speed, it is on average 4 frames per second. The developers did not stint on a good processor, so the camera works pretty quickly in all aspects.

Interface

In the domestic market, the Nikon-3200 camera is sold with a fully Russified menu. Information about the shooting parameters can be displayed to the user in the form of numbers or graphs. For novice photographers, the second option will be more convenient. Accelerometer, which allows you to change the orientation of the image on the display, can be called quite useful. The user chooses the color scheme of the interface (black, blue or orange) on his own to his taste. The developers have provided for the presence of pictures and symbols for almost all settings items. This greatly facilitates the work with parameters.



Taking photos

Using the Nikon-3200, photos can even be created in a resolution of 6016x4000 pixels. Due to this, images can be printed on a large poster or tightly cropped when the need arises. Such permission entails a size of JPEG files up to 15 megabytes. Today, the market has a large selection of inexpensive drives that will help to cope with this problem (the device supports media up to 64 GB). There are no special claims to the device in terms of image quality. On the other hand, the same cannot be said for a standard lens. If the image in the center of the frame is clear and sharp, then there is a lot of blur at the edges. In other words, it is advisable to always place the subject in the center of the frame. In order to fully reveal the potential of the 24-megapixel sensor, it is recommended to purchase the appropriate lens, the cost of which reaches the mark of 1000 US dollars. When buying this model of camera, a person wants to save primarily, so such a large surcharge for optics will be illogical.



Video recording

Making videos is one of the main strengths of the Nikon-3200. The instruction for the device states that this camera is capable of shooting video in Full HD and HD formats at a speed of 30 and 50 frames per second, respectively. More operational management of this mode has become thanks to the presence of a direct start button. This model was one of the first ‚ÄúDSLRs‚Äù to support tracking auto focus during movie creation. The quality of the resulting picture can be called quite high, especially considering the cost and class of the device. As standard, sound is recorded in monoform, but the ability to connect an external microphone is available here. In conditions of insufficient light, the auto focus may be mistaken.

Autonomy

High autonomy - this is not the strongest side of the Nikon-3200 model. User reviews of the camera are an additional confirmation that a full battery charge is enough to create about 600 photos. This is the average for a 1030 mAh battery. On the other hand, taking into account the fact that in this case we are talking about an entry-level SLR camera, it can be called very acceptable.



conclusions

Summing up, it should be noted that the Nikon-3200 camera was a logical continuation of the development of the amateur line of SLR cameras from this manufacturing company. If we compare it with its predecessor, we can safely say that the developers did not make a major technological breakthrough due to this device. The novelty has acquired a new matrix, and also boasts a higher quality of pictures and videos. Along with this, its value increased by about 5 thousand rubles. There are a number of disadvantages in it, due to the simplicity of the camera, the lack of a stabilizer, a rotary screen and some other flaws. Be that as it may, it should be remembered that in this case we are talking about the simplest SLR camera that was created for beginner amateur enthusiasts who are unlikely to pay much attention to the mentioned disadvantages. Moreover, against the background of competitors in its segment, the device looks more than worthy.
