We are going to Baikalsk. Hotels: review, rating, description, rooms and reviews

Baykalsk is one of the smallest cities located in the Irkutsk region. Only 10 thousand people permanently live here, but this does not prevent tourists from being frequent guests here. What do you think is of interest to people who plan to come to a particular locality? Of course, accommodation! In this case, renting an apartment is a bad option, because it is best to settle in some hotel where they will offer the proper level of comfort and reasonable prices.

Are you going to go to Baikalsk? We will discuss city hotels in this short article. In addition, the topic of reviews will also be touched, we will find out contact details and many other important information. Let's start!

"Comfort"

This establishment is a small private cottage, which is represented by 27 modern rooms. The hotel complex includes a bathhouse, a cafe-bar, a paid parking zone, a billiard room and a huge number of other infrastructure facilities. It is also worth noting that the territory of the hotel is closed to outsiders in order to provide its guests with the highest level of security.





Like any other hotel, the Uyut Hotel (Baikalsk) has its own characteristics, among which it is worth highlighting a wide variety of rooms, because there are not a single pair of rooms that would at least somehow remind each other. All rooms are made in different colors, and also have completely different finishes.

In addition, the hotel is located within the city and has a breathtaking view from the windows to Lake Baikal.

Services and reviews

At this stage of development, the hotel complex offers its guests to visit the cafe-bar, where you can try delicious dishes at affordable prices. Just note that breakfast, lunch and dinner are not included in the room rental price, which some hotel guests consider it a drawback.





Among the advantages, hotel guests note the possibility of ordering food in the room, as well as the presence of a sauna, a Russian bath and a massage room on the territory of the tourist complex. In addition, each room in the private hotel has satellite TV, and for an additional fee, any guest has the opportunity to visit the billiard room.

One room can accommodate from 2 to 6 people at a time.

"Sable"

Arriving in Baikalsk, hotels of this level are worth visiting one of the first, because the complex includes a huge number of infrastructure facilities. Here anyone can have a great time in the SPA-salon, restaurant, wellness center or comfortable rooms. Excellent wireless internet is available throughout the hotel project, and satellite TV is available in each room.





It is also worth noting that the Sobolinaya Hotel (Baikalsk) offers its guests rooms decorated in bright colors, where you can use a desk, towels and other amenities. In addition, the hotel has a shared sauna, and for lovers of an unforgettable vacation, a bathroom with a hydromassage is available.

Youth

Recently, the possibility of booking has become one of the most popular among tourists. Almost every hotel offers to make a reservation for those who plan to come to Baikalsk. The hotels of the city work around the clock, so you can check into a reserved room even if you are a bit late.

"Surf"

This institution can hardly be called the best, but it is worthy of attention. Hotel "Surf" (Baikalsk) is located near Lake Baikal, so from its windows a gorgeous view of this lake opens. Here everyone has the opportunity to stay in comfortable rooms with exclusively wooden furniture. Each room has a TV and excellent wireless internet is available.

It is also worth noting the availability of a restaurant working around the clock in the hotel, where you can taste a variety of dishes at affordable prices. In addition, this complex has a sauna and Turkish bath.

Reviews and prices in "Surf"
