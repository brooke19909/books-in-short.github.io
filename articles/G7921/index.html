SMS service features

SMS center

The main setting for the text messaging service on the Megafon number, as well as on the SIM card of any telecom operator that provides such a service, is the SMS center. Each operator and region has a personal number. In modern devices, its configuration is carried out automatically and human participation, as a rule, is not required at all. However, if there is a problem with sending and receiving SMS, then first of all you need to check how correctly the number is entered in the settings in the "SMS Center" ("Megaphone") field. To configure SMS yourself have to perform a few simple steps.





SMS center setup

If the text messaging service on the number is connected and there are difficulties with its use, then you need to go to the "settings" of the cellular device. Basically, on modern devices, you can find this in the section of the same name. Here, in the line "SMS Service Number" (the name may differ on different versions of operating systems), you should check the correctness of the number entry.

Firstly, it must be specified in the international format +7.

Secondly, it must be specified for the region in which the SIM card is purchased and registered. You can clarify the number through the contact center operator or by visiting the page with frequently asked questions on the operator‚Äôs resource for your region - here is a description of the main services. After the number is entered in the appropriate field, you should save the settings, wait up to fifteen minutes and try to send a test message.

For what reasons can not send and receive messages?

In addition to the fact that there is no SMS center number, in the gadget settings, the reasons for not being able to send and receive messages may include the following points:

  • SMS service on the room is disabled. This can only be done by the operator at the initiative of the client. You can clarify the status of the service by calling the contact center.
  • The cell number of the person to whom you are trying to send some information through messages is incorrectly indicated in the line: for example, not completely or without a seven or eight at the beginning.
  • A ban on the use of the service. This prohibition can be established by both the operator and the subscriber himself. To remove it, you must enter the query # 330 * 1111 #. Please note that such a command removes all prohibitions that were set on the number. After entering it, turn off and then turn on the device again for quick and correct application of the settings.
  • If the settings of SMS are correctly registered on the Megafon number and there are no other restrictions, but you still cannot send the message, then make sure that there is enough money on the balance to send the message.
