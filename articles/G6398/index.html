LCD "Olympus" in Kazan: description, features, reviews

Among the new buildings in Kazan, a special place is occupied by the Olimp residential complex. It is located in the Volga region at the intersection of the two largest streets of the city: Victory Avenue and Sorge Street. LCD has become a joint work of two companies. The business plan was developed by SENK, construction work is carried out by BAM Engineering.

During the implementation of the project, 2 houses with a height of 19 and 24 floors were erected. The total number of apartments is 272. According to the business plan, housing in the Olympus residential complex belongs to the comfort class and involves comfortable apartments and well-thought-out infrastructure of the district.

Characteristic features of LCD Olympus in Kazan

During the construction of buildings, all requirements and norms of European standards were taken into account, which indicates a high quality of construction work. At the same time, this project has some features that distinguish it from many other new buildings in the city.



  • Monolithic frame technology using reinforced concrete was used. The enclosing walls are made of silicate brick. The lining consists of porcelain tiles, and a mineral wool slab acts as a heater. All building materials are products from well-known manufacturers.
  • When facing, the technology of the ventilated facade is used. It allows you to insulate the building, block the destruction of the facade and prevent the appearance of fungus and mold.
  • Increased sound insulation of floors and walls. This characteristic increases the comfort of housing in the residential complex "Olympus" (Kazan).
  • The ceiling height in apartments is 3 meters, in commercial and office premises on the ground floors - 3.9-4.8 meters.
  • There are 2 underground levels for a parking lot with 4 exits and entrances.
  • The underground part of the residential complex is equipped with special fire compartments.
  • The houses are equipped with passenger and freight elevators. With their help, you can get to any residential and underground floor.

The device of residential buildings

Residential apartments are not located on all floors, some of them act as commercial and technical areas. It provides only 1 entrance and 105 apartments. House number 2 with a height of 24 floors also provides 1 entrance with 147 residential apartments. Parking in both houses is underground.
