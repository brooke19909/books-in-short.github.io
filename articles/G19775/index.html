Hotel Ocean Bay Hotel 2 * (Vietnam / Nha Trang): photos and reviews of tourists

More recently, the tourism business of Vietnam has reached the world level of quality service for travelers. If you add a pleasant climate, magnificent views and hospitality of local residents, then vacation in this country promises to be unforgettable.

Nha Trang Bay is located in the southern part of Vietnam, and the hotels on its shore are known not only for quality service, but also for beautiful views. Ocean Bay Hotel 2 * in terms of the services it claims is a minimum of 3 stars, which is noted by those who rested in it.

Nha trang

This resort has quickly become popular with tourists around the world thanks to more than 7 kilometers of white sand beaches, the presence of beautiful bays, islands and coral reefs.





The resort region of South Vietnam was developed taking into account the attraction of travelers of all categories. There are secluded and quiet places suitable for a relaxing family vacation, for example, a small hotel Ocean Bay Hotel 2 *, designed for only 38 rooms.

Fans of diving come here from all over the world, as they know not only about the beauty and richness of local reefs, but also the affordable prices for tours.

Fans of outdoor activities will enjoy sporting events, and for those who choose health tourism, the doors of cosmetic and massage parlors with a wide range of services are open.

Exciting rides are organized for children, which will allow you to have fun, and party lovers are waiting for discos, night clubs and numerous bars.

Thus, Nha Trang today is the most sought after and popular resort, providing its guests with a wide selection of entertainment and good time.

Hotel location

Ocean Bay Hotel 2 * is a fairly young hotel (opening year - 2009th), but it immediately began to position itself as a vacation destination for couples without children, business people and elderly travelers. For those who want entertainment and nightlife, it is not suitable. Here, those who value silence and solitude with nature feel good.

The hotel is located 40 km from the airfield and 4 km from the center of active spa life. The privacy gives this place a special attraction, given that the beach is only 150 meters away, and to diverse entertainment and services - 10 minutes by taxi.

The hotel is located in a picturesque area in the north of Nha Trang, not in the resort area, so you go to bed early, but there are many non-tourist cafes and restaurants where you can taste real Vietnamese cuisine.





The building of 6 floors, decorated with large blue glass windows, makes it look like an Ocean Bay Hotel 2 * wave rising above the land. Reviews of vacationers note that for a 2-star hotel it is very clean and comfortable, and what else does a traveler who comes to spend a rest on the sea and see an exotic country at an affordable price need?

Beach near the hotel

For many tourists traveling to relax on the sea, the beach, its location and infrastructure are important.

The beach near the Ocean Bay Hotel 2 *, judging by the reviews, is not yet fully equipped. It could be called wild if there were no sun loungers and umbrellas. Using them is not included in the price of the permit, so you have to pay extra, although it costs a penny.

Clean sand, good entry into the sea with a gentle slope, convenient for swimming - all this is noted by reviews. For people accustomed to beaches with a more developed infrastructure, there were not enough bars with their refreshing and alcoholic drinks and light snacks, as well as showers and pools.





Those who are annoyed by such trifles visit one of the city beaches, to which a bus passing 50 meters from the hotel will take you. There is everything you need for even the most experienced travelers: from freshly squeezed juices to renting catamarans and boats.

For those who need silence on the beach to hear the sound of the surf, the coast near the Ocean Bay Hotel 2 * is the best option.

Nha Trang (Vietnam) is famous for its many kilometers of beaches, so you can choose anyone by ordering a taxi or using the bus that runs until 19 hours.

Leisure

The most popular sport in Vietnam is diving, as Nha Trang is famous for its corals and their inhabitants. Although many travelers are afraid of the range and price of flights, accommodation, meals, diving and entertainment in this country are so cheap that they more than justify the money spent, which is confirmed by the services at the Ocean Bay Hotel 2 *.

Vietnam, the reviews of which are the most enthusiastic, attracts more and more tourists every year, many of which are becoming constant adherents of this country.





Beginners in diving feel especially good, since from October to May the water warms up in the sun to the bottom. Here you can find not only the most beautiful coral reefs in the world, but also various sunken ships inhabited by exotic fish that attract both professional and novice divers.

In addition to diving in Nha Trang , sports such as surfing and windsurfing are well developed.

sights

In the immediate vicinity of the hotel is the beautiful Cape Hong Chong, and the famous Cham towers are only 20 minutes on foot. They were built in the period from 7 to the beginning of the 12th century. Chamma people in the ancient place of worship of the gods. And in our time, the Vietnamese come to the towers for prayers, observing the rites of their ancient ancestors.

In addition to the towers, you should visit the Long Son Pagoda, built in the late 19th century. This is a whole complex, the main attraction of which besides the temple is a majestic white 14-meter statue of Buddha. All this is "protected" by stone dragons, so revered in Vietnam.

The market located next to the hotel will also bring a lot of pleasure and help determine souvenirs for relatives and friends.

Hotel today

Perhaps Vietnam is one of the few countries where a hotel with 2 stars, like the Ocean Bay Hotel 2 *, is on a par with 3-star analogues in other countries, or even surpasses them. This hotel is perfect for a quiet relaxing holiday, thanks to which you will love this country, and not only the services and entertainments that it provides to its guests.
