Mammals are amazing animals that have filled our planet from the hottest countries of Africa to cold Antarctica. They can be found almost everywhere, these unique inhabitants of our globe are very similar in behavior to humans: they also care for their cubs, feed them milk and take care of them. An interesting fact about mammals: previously, an animal such as a beaver was considered a fish, so the Catholic Church used its meat on fasting days, Orthodox believers generally refused to eat such a product, because they were sure that it was a saint.
