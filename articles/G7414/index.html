Hotel Drita Resort Hotel Spa 5 * (Turkey / Alanya / Mahmutlar): photos and reviews of tourists

Many tourists, planning their trip, stop at the colorful Turkey. Its many resort towns are characterized by clean beaches, crystal waters of the Mediterranean Sea, beautiful landscapes and favorable climatic conditions.

Hotel Drita Resort Hotel Spa 5 * (the description will be presented to your attention below), located in Mahmutlar, offers its guests high-quality service, comfortable accommodation, a developed entertainment infrastructure and an unforgettable pastime.





general characteristics

The hotel complex Drita Resort Hotel Spa 5 * (photo in the article) was erected and began receiving guests in 1997. The last renovation was carried out in 2011. On a compact territory with a total area of ‚Äã‚Äã6000 square meters, there is a park area with tropical palm trees, green lawns and well-groomed alleys. The main and only building is represented by a seven-story snow-white building equipped with elevators.

Antalya International Airport is 140 km away, but this does not interfere with the annual growth of tourists in these surroundings.

Pets are not allowed. Smoking is prohibited throughout the hotel. It is possible to move people with disabilities in specially equipped rooms. The staff speaks four languages: English, Russian, Turkish and German. The rooms can accommodate an extra bed.





Number of rooms

The total number of apartments in the Drita Resort Hotel Spa Mahmutlar 5 * is a little less than 200, which differ in the opening views and dimensions. All of them belong to a single category "Standard". The interior has a modest design, but comfort and a sense of taste are present in every apartment. There are no unnecessary elements, the atmosphere is characterized by conciseness and simplicity. The floors are laminated. Exit to the balcony allows you to meet the rising rays of the sun and the flickering of the first stars from the walls of your own rooms.

Arrangement

The furniture set is made of high quality wood, consists of a soft bed, a large wardrobe, bedside tables, a table and chairs. The presence of air conditioning, TV, telephone and safe creates comfortable living conditions. Replenishment of the minibar is possible only with an additional payment.

The bathroom is decorated with ceramic tiles, equipped with a sink, bathtub, shower and hairdryer. All the necessary personal care products are present. The number of towels complies with international standards.





Supply system

According to the concept of "All inclusive", which adheres to the hotel Drita Resort Hotel Spa 5 * (Alanya), the food is served in the form of a buffet. The buffet type of organization of food consumption allows everyone to independently choose their favorite culinary masterpiece in the amount that is necessary to satisfy the hunger. Five meals a day are provided in the main restaurant, the capacity of which is 420 people. It has an inner room and an open terrace overlooking the beautiful horizons of the Mediterranean Sea and mountain hills. The interior is made in warm colors. Friendly atmosphere provides a pleasant meal. As guests in numerous reviews note, the Drita Resort Hotel Spa 5 * is prepared very tasty. Staying hungry is hard here. Even the most sophisticated tourist will choose a dish to his taste.

Three bars located by the pool, on the beach and in the lobby can quench your thirst at any time of the day. Light snacks, ice cream and pastries will allow you to have a bite after long voyages and gain new strength for the subsequent swim.

A visit to the Ottoman restaurant Marina, which operates according to a custom-made menu, is possible only in the evening on a preliminary reservation. Compliance with the dress code is required.

Extra payment is required only with freshly squeezed juices, Turkish coffee and imported alcohol.





Aquazone

Swimming in the scorching sun is what thousands of tourists come to Alanya for. The swimming area is represented by the main outdoor pool. Its area is 350 square meters, has a rounded shape. Nearby is a children's pool. Such a neighborhood provides unobstructed observation of the bathing of his child. Around there are sun loungers, umbrellas, showers and one water slide, the descent from which will delight every guest of the hotel complex.

Regular water aerobics classes contribute to a variety of monotonous swimming. Simple exercises will help maintain the body in good shape, and the mood - at the highest level.

A similar swimming area is available in the main building, which is designed for swimming in the cold season. It is heated and has a gradual increase in depth from 130 cm to 145 cm. A pleasant blue atmosphere and large flowers in pots complement the pleasant atmosphere. Opening hours are from 8am to 6pm.

Beach

Drita Resort Hotel Spa 5 * hotel (Turkey) is located on the first coastline 50 meters from the azure coast of the Mediterranean Sea. In order to get to the beach, it is necessary to cross the roadway, using the underpass. Finding yourself in the sand and pebble spaces equipped with numerous sun loungers with soft mattresses for comfortable sunbathing, you can start tanning.

The entrance to the water is shallow, dotted with pebbles. For those who prefer to hide in the shade, there are umbrellas. A towel dispenser is available for a fee. A wooden pier allows you to descend into the water on specially equipped steps, immediately at a depth. Diving from the pontoon is prohibited.

For those who, in addition to swimming and lying, prefer active entertainment, there is an equipped volleyball court. Teams are honestly fighting for the championship, gathering every day and enjoying the game and communication. Water rides are represented by riding a jet ski, ring or canoe. Surfing, diving and parachuting along the boat will give a lot of unforgettable emotions, extreme and drive to those who decide to try these water activities.





Toddler Leisure

In order for each child to feel incredibly happy and satisfied, the administration of the Drita Resort Hotel Spa 5 * hotel (Turkey, Alanya) thought out a number of entertaining entertainments both in the kids club, whose doors are open for guests aged 4 to 12 years old, and in the fresh in the air. Under the supervision of a qualified teacher, children will show their talents in drawing, creativity and quick wit.

A professional team of animators, transforming from fairy-tale characters into clowns, gives children a real celebration of happiness, joy and fun.

In the fresh air, in addition to the swimming pool, children can have fun in the game complexes made of high-quality plastic. Carousels and swings, slides and labyrinths will allow you to spend energy reserves and prepare for a healthy night's sleep.

The evening disco ends with the entertainment of the kids.

The restaurant provides high chairs, and playpens are installed in the room upon request. Babysitting services are paid extra. No wheelchairs available.





health and beauty

For those who care about their appearance and want to always look young, a multifunctional SPA center is open for all guests, regardless of gender and age. Its main purpose is to heal the body and restore strength, giving joy and peace to the soul. A range of procedures, from wrapping with seaweed to thermal baths, offers its customers this facility. The skin will gain an updated fresh look, and body volumes will begin to decline rapidly.

For those who can not stand the high temperatures of the sauna, it is proposed to visit the hammam. Well-heated marble will give leisurely relaxation and inner peace to each visitor. Thanks to this procedure, harmful toxins, toxins are removed from the body, and blood circulation is normalized, thereby affecting the general condition of a person.

An unprecedented feeling of relaxation can be experienced at the hands of professional massage therapists who are able to relax every part of the body, saturating it with oxygen and new energy.

A visit to a beauty salon will please mainly girls: after all, everyone dreams of looking beautiful, even on vacation. Hairdressers and specialists in manicure, pedicure and other areas will create a new interesting image that will delight its owner and others, relatives.

Sport

For those who cannot imagine their life without physical exertion, and those who decide to put their figure in order, the doors of the fitness center are open. Numerous exercise machines, treadmills, dumbbells, balls and other equipment will help in achieving the goal. Individual trainers will help you to schedule the necessary loads, in order to avoid excessive overwork.

A basketball court, soccer field and tennis court with concrete cover are located next to the main building. Lighting, rental of balls and rackets is paid extra. It is possible to use the services of a coach who will teach the art of tennis from the basics.





Entertainment

Darts, billiards and table tennis diversify pastime within the walls of the Drita Resort Hotel Spa 5 * (Turkey, Alanya). Read an interesting book, secluded in a quiet and comfortable room, available in the library.

In the evening, entertaining dance shows, acrobatic performances and humorous scenes are pleased to be presented by talented artists of the animation team. Entertaining contests and quizzes, as well as the direct participation of holidaymakers in various activities raise their spirits, bring everyone together and give an unforgettable experience. The evening ends with a disco, which is held indoors, admission is free.

In general, regarding the organization of leisure activities for both adults and children, tourists leave only positive reviews. There were no serious complaints. Everyone will find something to their liking.

Additional services

The list of services not included in the price includes ironing, shoe shine, laundry and dry cleaning - payment is made upon receipt of the order. Wi-Fi is free in public areas. For those who arrive by car or use the rental service to get acquainted with local attractions, it is proposed to leave the car in a guarded parking space.

In order to combine carefree pastime with work, you can use the rental of conference rooms. Equipped with all the necessary screens, projectors and microphones, they will ensure that a business meeting is held at the level.

There is a special offer for honeymooners, which you can use by presenting a marriage certificate. An additionally decorated room with candles and rose petals, a plate of fruit and a bottle of champagne will give a romantic mood and unforgettable emotions to a young couple.

The presence of a mini market and souvenir shops allows you to make the necessary purchases without leaving the walls of the hotel. A round-the-clock transfer is paid extra.

Currency exchange and an exciting tour can be booked at the 24-hour reception.
