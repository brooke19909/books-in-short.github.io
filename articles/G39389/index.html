Alfa-Bank partners: list

Alfa-Bank is one of the largest backbone financial institutions in Russia. This is a universal institution that enjoys the high trust of customers and partners. The organization confirms its reputation as a leading one, and ranks among the five largest in the market. Read more about the history, achievements, innovations, partners of Alfa-Bank.

Creature

The history of the credit institution began in 1990, when investors from Cyprus decided to establish a bank. Over the first 15 years, a large network of branches was created, the latest technologies were introduced, the range of services was gradually expanded, and the first partners of Alfa-Bank appeared. But first things first.



1990-1996: creating infrastructure, laying down the principles of financial policy, asset management system. A well-thought-out approach to laying the foundation for subsequent activities allowed the organization to survive the financial crisis of 1995 and enter the top three institutions in which the first Eurobonds were placed.

1997-1998: continued strengthening in the market, the emergence of new partners, the highest ratings from leading international rating agencies. Business diversification: merger with Alfa Capital. The emergence of the leasing company of the same name.

1999-2000: Active growth of the Alfa-Bank regional network. Partner banks, the list of which is presented on the official website of the organization, provide intermediary services to customers at preferential rates.

2001-2002: emergence of the new Alfa-Insurance TM. Acquisition of Amsterdam Trade Bank (ATB).

2003-2004: launch of the innovative Alfa-Bank Express project. In the Russian market in those years, medium-sized enterprises were a promising segment. Their need for banking services is great, and there are few offers on the market. The new medium-sized business lending program was developed taking into account international experience and the needs of domestic customers.

One of the areas of business development is the provision of loans for the purchase of a vehicle.
