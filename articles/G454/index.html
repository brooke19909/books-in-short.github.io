Cottage "Gregory" in Yekaterinburg: a cozy place for celebrations

Located in the city of Yekaterinburg (closer to the south-west), the Gregory cottage enjoys considerable popularity among consumers of services related to the rental of real estate. The owner offers to rent housing for recreation, events, birthday parties, weddings or other celebrations. The main advantage of housing is convenient placement. It can be reached by private transport, and by public, and by taxi.

Features Reservation

The terms of the rental property require the daily rent of the cottage "Gregory" (Yekaterinburg) by the owner to the client. In this case, the person responsible for housing, prescribes the terms of the transaction. For example, check into the cottage is in a non-standard format. Most apartments and other cottages have a standard check-in time, as a rule, this is lunch time, and check-out is before 10-11 a.m. You can only enter Gregory Cottage (Yekaterinburg) from 18:00 in the evening.

Appearance and living conditions

Gregory is a two-story house with a stylish modern interior, made in accordance with European standards. The advantageous location of the cottage - on the shore of the pond - attracts the attention of customers who want to organize a celebration in a beautiful place or arrange an exit ceremony, to conduct a photo shoot.





The house has a modern renovation. Inside the house there are 4 rooms with provided sleeping places in the form of double beds and Euro-book sofas. At the request of customers, you can arrange an extra bed for a fee.

In the cottage "Gregory" (Yekaterinburg) 2 bathrooms with showers - one on the floor.

It offers renting a full kitchen, it is equipped with modern appliances for quick and convenient cooking or heating of food brought. This can be done on your own or to order services, after agreeing them with the administrator of the institution.

Entertainment and leisure

From entertainment in the cottage "Gregory" in Yekaterinburg (whose photo you see in this article) you are waiting for:

  • Finnish sauna
  • massage chairs;
  • outdoor pool;
  • billiards;
  • fireplace;
  • board mini-games;
  • children's entertainment room;
  • the possibility of fishing in your own pond;
  • ennobled house territory with gazebos and barbecue facilities for cooking barbecue, barbecue;
  • banquet room for up to 60 people.




All services, except for relaxing in the sauna and by the pool, are free of charge on condition of booking for the whole weekend, in other cases they are paid extra.

How much will it cost to rent a house

Rest in a sauna with a swimming pool - from 1000 r. / Hour.

Cottage rental: weekdays (provided up to 5 people vacationers) from 7500 p. / Hour; weekends - from 45 000 p. / hour (all inclusive).

Other details of the rental and booking of the cottage can be found by calling the administrator.

How to find "Gregory"?

If you have your own car, then from the city center, head to the southwestern region. From March 8 Street, turn onto Schorsa, then continue to Amundsen, then turn right to Krasnolesie Street. Noticing the yellow sign of the Gregory cottage, head further down the street.





Using public transport, you need to get off at the Dvinskaya stop (bus number 50a).

What customers say

Reviews about the Gregory cottage in Yekaterinburg are very controversial. Some respond positively to a vacation or a banquet. Others talk about the lack of professionalism of the cottage administration. In general, the object is not bad in terms of location and living conditions.

If you need a beautiful place for a festive ceremony, you can safely book a house for an hour, day or several days. Cottage "Gregory" was created for small events of a cultural nature: weddings, birthdays, engagements.
