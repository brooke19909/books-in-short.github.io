How to sew a beach dress with your own hands?

When the season of blue sky and sun begins, sand and rivers, the beach and the sea, hot summer days are in full swing. Summer is a vacation, rest and entertainment. Families rush to the village, out of town, to the cottage, to the lake, river and sea. It is urgent to replenish your wardrobe with an original and light outfit, for example, sew a beach dress with your own hands. Such an outfit will become an indispensable companion in the country, you can take it on a trip to the sea, it is light, airy and absolutely does not hinder movements.





For summer holidays you need a practical dress, which will take up little space, and always multifunctional. Do not hesitate. You can make a beach dress with your own hands very quickly, without much hassle and the presence of special skills, because everything ingenious is simple.

The fabric can be chosen to your liking, from knitwear to beach towel. Add to this about half an hour. The result - a do-it-yourself beach dress is ready. It is suitable for dressing immediately after bathing, and for the usual walk along the coast.





Having decided on the fabric of the dress, we proceed directly to the cutting. The pattern will be in the form of a rectangle, the short side of which is equal to the desired length of the product. When cutting, the length of the straps must be taken into account. The thigh circumference, combined with the width of the odor, is the long side. All edges of a rectangular slice need to be processed, but first you need to cut the upper corners in a semicircle. Then it remains only to sew the straps and the beach dress with your own hands is completely ready!

Natural light fabrics will be an ideal material for such an outfit, because it should not hamper the movements. As for color, designers recommend bright dresses with polka dots, with floral motifs, various prints. Marine topics are also relevant. A beach dress for the full will perfectly hide the imperfections of the figure and make the silhouette more slim. Such an outfit has a wide and straight cut without tucks, folds are collected at the cut line. Fabric - knitwear or pleated silk.
