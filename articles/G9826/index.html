Hotel Alara Hotel 4 *, Alanya, Turkey: photos, reviews

"Incekum" from Turkish is translated as "fine sand". And this resort village fully justifies its name. The local sandy beaches are actually very beautiful and comfortable for relaxing. Another feature of Incekum is that it is one of the oldest resorts in Turkey. And the comfortable hotels here, of course, built a great many. For example, Alara Hotel 4 * deserved quite good reviews from tourists.

Where is

This complex was once built directly in the village of Incekum on the very shore of the Mediterranean Sea. The closest town to the hotel is the city of Alanya. The distance from the Alara Hotel 4 * to the center of the latter is 25 km. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel is another popular resort village - Avsallar (3 km).





Thus, this hotel is located quite close to the airport in Alanya Gazipasa. From the air transport hub in Antalya to the hotel, tourists will have to get at least 2 hours. The distance between this airport and Alanya is about 100 km. Vacationers who have made hotel reservations on their own are likely to have to travel by intercity bus. Such a trip would cost about $ 12.

General description of the hotel

Hotel Alara Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya) began to accept tourists who came to rest in Incekum for a long time. This hotel opened in the distant 1983. In addition, the last repair was carried out here back in 2008. That is, the complex is quite old.

Alara Hotel occupies the rooms equipped in several multi-storey buildings with stylish modern architecture. This hotel belongs to the category of beach. A very beautiful, well-landscaped courtyard is, of course, one of the main advantages of Alara Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya). And the photos, which can be seen in the article, confirm this fully. In addition, its employees clean the territory of the complex very carefully. So many tropical plants have been planted in the courtyard of this hotel that it even resembles a mini-botanical garden.





On the websites of tour operators, you can often read information that the Alara Hotel 4 * hotel (Turkey, Alanya) is suitable for both young people and couples or senior vacationers. But judging by the reviews of tourists who have already visited this hotel, this is not entirely true. According to the majority of vacationers, young people at this hotel will simply have nothing to do. In fact, this complex is still better suited for tourists from 40 or even 60. There are not so many entertainments for guests here. And the Incekum village itself is visited mainly by lovers of measured beach holidays. Those who are looking for various kinds of entertainment in Turkey should rent a hotel directly in Alanya. This city is quite rightly considered to be a youth resort. There really is where to have fun.

The apartments in this hotel are rented mainly by Germans and Russians. Of course, very rich people would consider this complex not too convenient. However, for tourists of average wealth, judging by the reviews, it fits very well.

Similar hotel

Gathering information about Alara Hotel before a trip to Turkey, vacationers who have rented rooms here should be a closer look. The fact is that in Alanya there is another hotel with a similar name - My Sea Hotels Alara. It is also a fairly comfortable four-star hotel, providing tourists with comfortable rooms. However, My Sea Hotels Alara 4 * (Alanya) is located in Turkey not in Incekum, but in the village of Okurcalar. This hotel has a larger area than Alara Hotel. Yes, and it works in a slightly different way. This concerns, for example, hotel catering.

Partnership agreements are concluded with Mysea Hotel Alara 4 * (Turkey, Alanya) almost the same tour operators as with Alara Hotel. Therefore, confusing these hotels is really very simple.





What rooms does it offer

For guests of Incekum, Alara Hotel has 132 rooms. Most of them belong to the standard most popular among holidaymakers. The hotel also has 9 family rooms. The furniture in all rooms is wooden. Facilities in the rooms of the hotel guests are provided with the following:

  • TV;

  • small fridge bar;

  • telephone.

Of course, all the rooms rented out at the hotel are equipped with split systems. Hotel guests can watch one Russian-language channel on TV. For a minibar, residents of this complex need to pay separately. In addition, each room has a balcony (or terrace). Here guests can relax in the fresh air, admiring the picturesque surroundings of the hotel or its courtyard. Also on the balcony is very convenient to dry beach items. In the hotel's shower rooms, a hairdryer is provided.

Rooms

What should you know before traveling to Alara Hotel 4 * (Alanya, Turkey)? Room reviews are mostly good.

The advantages of the rooms offered in this hotel by many tourists are primarily their cleanliness. Maids come to the hotel rooms daily. Moreover, judging by the reviews, they carry out cleaning carefully. Bed linen in the complex is also changed on time.

The furniture in this old hotel is, of course, far from new. However, it looks, judging by the reviews, still quite worthy. The beds are comfortable, and the tables, wardrobes and nightstands are not collapsed.





Not bad, judging by the reviews, household appliances also work in this hotel. The only thing is that the televisions in the hotel, as noted by its former guests, are installed quite old. Even for PU, sometimes not all buttons work from them.

Showers in this hotel, according to guests, are quite comfortable. The only thing in the bathrooms for residents is not provided shampoo. Experienced tourists recommend this accessory to vacationers who have rented rooms in this recreation center, it is imperative to take with them from home on the road. Shower gel, unlike shampoo, is given to hotel residents.

What you should know about

The rooms in the hotel Alara Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya), therefore, are rented quite comfortable. However, this hotel has one rather serious minus. The hotel is part of the Alara group of complexes. And it often happens that tourists who rent rooms at the Alara Hotel are offered to check in to other hotels on the network upon arrival. Moreover, the administration may even share vacationers who have arrived in groups.

If this happens, tourists should defend their right to check in at Alara Hotel 4 *. With a certain degree of perseverance, the hotel administration finally goes to meet its guests and populates them in those rooms that they rented.

Service on the territory of Alara Hotel guests, judging by the reviews, is offered very good. The main advantage of this recreation center, tourists-travelers consider, of course, that they can use the amenities of two hotels at once. Dining at the hotel

The complex operates on an all-inclusive system. That is, hotel guests can take any drinks and food available in the dining room and bar at almost any time completely free of charge. At the same time, residents of the hotel can go not only to the restaurant itself Alara Hotel. According to the rules of the complex, its guests have the right to freely visit the dining rooms of all hotels of the Alara group.

The dining room of the Alara Hotel 4 * complex itself is highly praised by many of its guests. The menu in the hotel restaurant is actually very diverse. Drafts of the hotel guests are offered just a huge amount. Judging by the reviews, the meat dishes in the hotel restaurant are served mainly from poultry. Most often it is a turkey or chicken. Pasta in the dining room of Alara Hotel is always available. Many tourists recommend for beginners who decide to stay in this complex, be sure to try the local eggplant. They are prepared by the cooks of the hotel restaurant in a variety of designs and very tasty.

Entrance to the sea on the hotel beach is very convenient. This is noted by many tourists who have ever stayed at the Alara Hotel.

Swimming at the hotel's beach is thus very convenient. But Alara Hotel 4 * (Alanya, Turkey) earned good reviews not only for its clean sand and convenient entry into the water. Small beaches

In addition to the main one, guests of the Alara Hotel 4 * hotel (Turkey, Alanya, Incekum) can also visit two small picturesque beaches located nearby. The sea and sand are just as clean. However, resting on these beaches, parents should take a closer look at the children. The fact is that on the rocks that are on the coast in this place, special algae grows, upon contact with which a burn is formed on the skin.

Useful advice

In addition to shampoo, some tourists recommend our compatriots who have booked rooms at Alara Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya, Incekum) to bring along some kind of allergy remedy. There are no mosquitoes in this hotel. The facts of food poisoning at the hotel have also never been recorded. An allergic reaction in the form of a rash in the first days after check-in, as some travelers note, occurs in many hotel guests. This is explained, apparently, by an excess of ultraviolet radiation and the composition of local water.
