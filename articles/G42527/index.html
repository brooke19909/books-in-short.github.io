Where in Crimea it is better to relax with children: reviews about hotels and beaches

Crimea is the Russian peninsula, which is very popular with tourists from all regions of the country, as well as neighboring states. On its territory, a wonderful climate reigns, which attracts the attention of vacationers. They also enjoy visiting local attractions and exploring the culture of the multinational population of the peninsula.

Often, tourists prefer to go here to relax with their families. Where in Crimea is it better to relax with children? Where to go and what to see? What features is this type of holiday famous for, and which beaches of Crimea are most suitable for families with children? About all this further.

Features for families with children

What should be considered when arranging holidays with children in Crimea? In the reviews, many tourists often share their comments on this subject. They say that, on the whole, this peninsula is a great place for spending summer time here, the climate and surrounding nature are quite favorable therefor. However, of course, all tourists should pay attention to some points, choosing a vacation option.

When arranging a vacation with children in the Crimea, one should especially responsibly approach the choice of an area for living. Experienced family tourists recommend the east, south and west coast of the peninsula and not the central part of it for families with a child, since it is quite problematic and long to get from the cities located far from the coastline with a child in the company.

As for the nuances associated with the choice of the type of housing for living, parents who have previously had a rest with their child recommend turning their attention to motels and boarding houses, where three meals a day are provided. In the event that you have a very small child in your arms, you should give preference to the private sector, where, as a rule, there is the possibility of self-cooking - it will certainly be fresh and made from the necessary products.

When choosing a place of rest with children in Crimea, it is necessary to take into account the fact that in some cases you should give your preference to hotels, boarding houses and sanatoriums that have a private beach, which is closed for visiting by unauthorized persons. This is especially true during the peak holiday season, which occurs in July and August. This observation is connected with the fact that during this period it is very difficult to find a normal free place on a public beach. Some tourists generally recommend that parents arrange a family vacation in the company of a child in mid-September - at this time there are not many people in the resorts, and the weather allows you to stay at sea for at least a day.

So where is the best vacation with children in Crimea? In the reviews of tourists, individual resorts are often mentioned, as well as the advantages and their disadvantages. Further we will consider the most popular regions.

Theodosius

This resort is located in the southeastern part of the peninsula and is considered one of the best places for organizing children's vacations in the Crimea. In the summer, favorable weather prevails in this region.

There are a large number of excellent beaches, the total length of which is more than 20 kilometers. All of them are clean, covered with soft sand and quite small, with a gradual descent into the sea. This factor must certainly be taken into account by tourists at the time of organizing holidays with children in Crimea. In addition to the magnificent beaches, there is beautiful nature and very fresh air, which is valuable not only for children, but also for adults.

Where can I spend time with my child, relaxing in Feodosia? First of all, you should pay attention to the possibility of taking walks along the city's promenade. It always has a large amount of entertainment, as well as urban musicians. From the promenade you can watch the beautiful port, in which large ships regularly arrive.

A family trip to the Nemo Dolphinarium, where there are dolphin therapy courses and regular performances, will be very exciting. It is located on the Golden Beach, which is located in the direction of the Kerch highway.

In addition to the dolphinarium, tourists are very fond of visiting the water park located in the direction of Koktebel. It offers attractions for vacationers, both for adults and for children. In addition to them, there is a large sauna and several jacuzzis with hydromassage.

As for the hotels that are most suitable for families with young tourists, among them are the "Scarlet Sails", the guest house "Sefa" and the sanatorium "Health". In each of these places there are playgrounds, animation and a separate meal for babies is offered.

Zander

Another city in the Crimea, where it is better to relax with children, is Sudak. People often come here with young tourists, not only because the settlement is small in size and has a beautiful, clean nature, but also because all the shores of Sudak are rather shallow, so they quickly warm up, and the water temperature is maintained at a high level for a long time . There are practically no waves near the coast of the city.

It is in Sudak that the best yarns of Crimea are for relaxing with children. All of them have a certain feature, which is a gray color, provided due to the warm and safe fine pebble coating. The beginning of the beach season here is very early, and it lasts quite a long time - for 138 days.

As for entertainment, while in Sudak, you can visit the interesting Genoese Helmet festival with your child , which is held every year from July to August. New World

This settlement is a small village, which is quite inconspicuous among tourists. However, in vain, since the best holidays in Crimea with children can be organized here.

The New World is located next to Sudak and is washed by the waters of the same shallow small bay, which is part of the Black Sea. The peculiarity of this settlement is that its coast (Tsarsky beach) is washed by the waters of three colors, provided due to the different depths of the reservoir. According to tourists, the water here is clean, the beaches are gentle and very warm, and the nature is truly magnificent. All these qualities inherent in the village make it an ideal place for a better vacation with children in Crimea.

Around the New World there are a huge number of legends associated with pirates - no doubt, it will be very interesting to listen to both adults and children who come to rest with their parents.

Koktebel

The city of Koktebel, which is also located on the Crimean coast, attracts a lot of tourists' attention. It is famous for its active life, as well as regularly held festivals and holidays, in which it will definitely be interesting to participate both for children and adults. The location of the beaches of Koktebel in a quiet bay, where there are practically no waves, as well as a gradual hundred-meter descent into the sea makes it an ideal place for families with children in Crimea. In the reviews of tourists very often it is said about the ideal beaches of the village - they are all covered with sand and quite warm.

In the city you can visit several interesting places, among which travelers often highlight Kara-Dag, as well as the famous Golden Gate, a historical monument from the time of the horde. In these areas, you can also search for real treasures - by doing this, kids can feel like real gold diggers. No less interesting places will be Dinoterium and the Karadag Natural Museum.

What are the best Crimea hotels for families with children in Koktebel? First of all, you should pay attention to the hotel "Villa Classic". Despite the fact that it is small in size, this place has excellent conditions for families with children, including a closed territory, playground, as well as beautiful nature. Also, the Tropical Wave boarding house is considered to be a great place to relax, where there are a couple of separate pools for the child, an outdoor area for entertainment and games, a games room, as well as the possibility of organizing a free transfer to the picturesque city beach, which also has a lot of children's entertainment. The hotel has its own shoreline area, which is completely covered with clean and very soft sand, and also has a gentle slope into the sea.

Yalta

Where in Crimea is it better to relax with children? According to many travelers, an excellent option is Yalta - a resort city located on the southern coast of the peninsula. It is here that whole families often come. Tourists determine their choice by the presence in this coastal city of a huge amount of entertainment, clean and beautiful beaches, as well as the best Crimea all-inclusive pensions.

Holidays with children can also be organized in the suburbs of Yalta - Livadia and Gurzuf. These settlements are small in size and quite quiet. As for Gurzuf, this village is famous for the famous Artek camp, which is the dream of millions of children from all over the former USSR.

The best hotels for families with children in Crimea are also located in Yalta. Among them, tourists often highlight a lot of positive comments such as Yalta Intourist and Palmira Palace. The first is a small hotel town, on the territory of which there are seven beautiful beaches washed by clean, warm and not very deep sea. For the youngest guests throughout the resort period there are small cafes, playgrounds, groups of animators who entertain guests. On the territory of the hotel complex "Yalta Intourist" there is a large water park, which is regularly visited by entire families who organize their holidays in Yalta.

Hotel Palmira Palace offers travelers a very beautiful holiday, organized in accordance with all European standards. It is located near the famous Swallow's Nest. As for the infrastructure for family vacations, it is well developed. In particular, tourists should pay attention to the presence of the Limpopo children's club, a large number of outdoor and indoor playgrounds, swimming pools, as well as an animation team that amuses guests throughout the resort period.

If you want to have fun with the whole family, in Yalta you can go to the Glade of fairy tales, a stay in which, of course, will appeal to kids under the age of 10, and adolescents will be happy to make an exciting climb to Mount Ai-Petri. In addition, the whole family can go to the local zoo, which contains interesting representatives of the world of flora and fauna, as well as an aquarium. In addition, in Yalta there are a large number of natural attractions, a large botanical garden, palaces, as well as the famous Swallow's Nest.

Big Alushta

Big Alushta is a whole region in Crimea, ideal for families with children. If you do not know where it is better to relax with a child on the Crimean coast, then it is best to go there.

This region includes several small and fairly quiet settlements, which are washed by the clean and warm waters of the Black Sea. Here you can find a large number of beaches, the shores of which are rather shallow, warm, making them great for relaxing with a child.

Fans of a noisier vacation can stay in the city of Alushta, and those who want to spend their time in a more relaxed atmosphere should pay attention to the small villages of Rybachye, Utes, Lazurnoe, Partenit, as well as everyone else who is next door to them.

Some of the best hotels in Crimea for families with children are located in Alushta, and in smaller settlements located next to this city, you will have to be content with living in the private sector, however, according to tourists, this region perfectly welcomes any guests.

Among the hotels in Alushta, according to tourists, the most suitable for families with a child is the Golden Ear, which offers all-inclusive accommodation. It is recommended to book trips to it long before the start of the holiday season, as this place for recreation is very popular among travelers who prefer a vacation in Crimea. In their comments, they note that the price of the package in the "Golden Ear" includes not only accommodation, but also a whole range of spa treatments, as well as treatment. As for the conditions for a children's stay, during the entire holiday season the hotel has a large team of cheerful animators, a huge games room, a playground, several heated pools, and the hotel also has a private pebble beach.

Hotel Porto Mare, which is also located in Alushta, offers excellent conditions for children and for leisure activities for adults. Among these, tourists most often in their reviews note the presence on its territory of a large children's club, daily parties, as well as several large pools in the hotel.

Staying in Alushta, parents with children can visit the big dolphinarium or the Dendrozoo park. For evening walks, the city promenade, which is equipped with a huge amount of entertainment for every taste, is perfect. It has a large entertainment center for children "Almond Grove", which is paid to visit, however, according to tourists, is quite fascinating.

In the vicinity of Alushta, you can observe the magnificent nature, which attracts tourists with the opportunity to organize hiking.

Evpatoria

Most of the best resorts and sanatoriums for holidays in Crimea with children are located in Yevpatoriya - another famous town. The beaches of this village are covered with a lot of soft and very fine sand, and the descent into the water here is quite gentle, which is in the hands of tourists vacationing with kids and teens.

The city has a huge amount of entertainment for children and their parents. Among the places suitable for joint trips, in the reviews of travelers, the dolphinarium, the Clown House, as well as Aqualand, a water park equipped with everything necessary for a very active holiday, are often mentioned. Evpatoria also has a museum of pirates of the Black Sea, which is visited with great interest by both adults and children. In the park named after Frunze there are a huge number of attractions, as well as walkways. On the territory of this place for rest there are cafes and small restaurants where you can have a tasty snack.

According to tourists, the best places to stay with children in Yevpatoriya are the Dolce Vita, Lake of Dreams, Planet boarding houses, as well as the Petrel sanatorium. Many tourists in their comments point out that in the territory of Yevpatoriya there is an excellent clinic where parents with a small child can always go if the baby feels unwell. It is located near the water park.
