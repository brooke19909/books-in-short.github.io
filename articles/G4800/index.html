Battery

When you think about how to find the best gaming tablets, think about the battery. The logic is simple: the higher this number, the longer your device will last. This, again, depends on the charge consumption. For small 7-inch devices that consume relatively little energy, 3.5-4 thousand mAh of charge will be enough; while larger gaming tablets (10 inches) need higher rates.
