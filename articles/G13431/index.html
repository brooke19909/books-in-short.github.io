How to knit a dress with your own hands

Many fashionistas often face the problem of choosing an unusual dress model. After shopping in the city, you notice that most styles are of the same type. In such

situation it is worth considering creating an exclusive model with your own hands.

Some prefer to use the services of a seamstress, and some prefer to knit a dress with their own hands. Knitting is an extremely fascinating process that improves attention and accuracy. With the help of knitting needles or a hook, you can create truly unique masterpieces that will ideally fit exactly your figure. Knitted dresses are equally good for going to work, and for a romantic date. Creating new styles, playing with the length and texture of knitting, you will get an incredible opportunity to diversify your wardrobe.

Knitting provides a huge scope for your imagination. You will be able to knit any type of dress, inventing models yourself. Even if you cannot come up with something yourself, do not be discouraged. New models of dresses from famous designers will also inspire you to unusual experiments with yarn and hem length.



Light tunic dresses are especially relevant in the spring-summer season. For several years they have not gone out of fashion. A simple, but at the same time sophisticated short lace dress will emphasize the beauty of the legs and the slimness of the figure. You can supplement such an outfit with various accessories, for example, with a thin strap or an unusual removable collar, which today have confidently entered into fashion.

Another advantage of knitted dresses is that they look advantageous and on full women. It is only necessary to think over a model that will hide the flaws of the figure and emphasize its merits.

Starting to crochet a dress, you must first select the appropriate size of the tool, as well as the material from which it is made.





The size of the hook depends on the thickness of the yarn that you will use in your work. To make sure your tool is comfortable, it‚Äôs best to tie a small sample first. If the hook is too thick for the selected thread, the product will be loose. And if you use thin, when you are going to knit a dress with thick yarn, the fabric will turn out to be very tight, dense.

When choosing a hook, you need to pay attention to the material from which it is made. Metallic ones are better for knitting fine yarn. Warm winter dresses are preferably made using a large plastic hook.

As for the choice of yarn, here everything will depend on what dress you intend to make. For summer models, it is better to choose cotton, as this material breathes well and keeps its shape perfectly. Bamboo yarn is very popular now, but you should be careful with it - after washing, the product can stretch out. If you are going to knit a dress for the winter, it is better to use half-wool (a combination of wool and acrylic), pure wool or mohair. Then the thing will turn out warm. These types of dresses are best made without large lace inserts. In the cold season, tight knitting is more suitable.
