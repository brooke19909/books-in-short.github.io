Details on how to make a dart

Today we will tell you how to make a dart at home. There are games that are suitable for children, and adults will be interested. One of them is darts. This is a very exciting activity that allows you to relax and have a good rest with family or friends. Darts are played both in the yard and in the apartment, in the house or in the workplace. You can, of course, purchase a ready-made kit, but its elements are lost over time, and you cannot always find them. What to do in this case: buy a new kit or make a dart with your own hands? The answer is obvious - to find the necessary materials at home and create it yourself, saving time that could be spent on going to the store. The process of making a dart is quite simple, and all materials are usually found within a few minutes. This is a fascinating creative activity, the implementation of which takes very little time - a maximum of 30 minutes.

What materials need to be prepared?



We turn to the practical part of solving the question of how to make a dart with your own hands. You will need: a dry branch (the tree species does not matter); cardboard or bottle made of plastic; a needle designed to inflate balls; steel wire (3-4 cm, diameter from 1.5 to 2.5 mm), a large needle for sewing or a nail (30-40 mm); duct tape or patch.

Tool



To solve the question of how to make a dart, many tools will not be needed. Availability required:

  • scissors;
  • a knife;
  • nippers;
  • an abrasive tool or material (such as a file or sandpaper).

Phased production



Having prepared all the listed tools and materials, proceed to the implementation of the process itself. Next, we will step by step describe how to make a dart.

  1. Take a branch and cut a small piece from it, about 9 cm long.
  2. Further work is necessary with steel wire. If you have a gypsy needle, separate 3-4 cm from it. From this material you will create the sharpest part of the dart. In extreme cases, you can use a nail without a hat. In order to solve the question of how to make a dart, check whether its front part (nail, wire) is sharp enough. For maximum security, this task should be completed at the final stage.
  3. Remove the tip from the ball needle through which air enters when pumping. You will need another part of it, screwed into the pump.
  4. Put the iron element in the branch. Screw it in from the front. In this case, the softness of the core will make this task easy.
  5. To adjust the branch size to the diameter of the ball needle, remove the layer of wood at the top of the dart. After making sure that the iron element can be used, tighten it firmly.
  6. Apply masking tape or other material. A patch is ideal. Wind it at the location of the piece of iron. Thus, it will be possible to securely fix the iron element on a wooden base.
  7. Then proceed carefully. At the top of the branch, make two incisions in the form of a cross, into which you will later install the wings. Use the thin blade knife to minimize the risk of damage to the workpiece. The depth of the cuts should be about 5, but not less than 4 cm. Now you know how to make a dart without damaging your fingers and without destroying the workpiece itself.
  8. The notches allow the tail of the dart to expand. Using the patch, tightly connect the parts of the wooden branch formed after the incisions. You should also wrap the patch a little lower, after which the upper winding can be removed - everything will hold.
  9. If you like to experiment, make wings not from cardboard, but from a plastic bottle. You will need a piece of plastic carved in the shape of a butterfly. Make two elements out of it, dividing in the middle. Bend each part in half so that an angle of 90 degrees is formed. An important advantage of plastic is its ability to withstand moisture. If you are interested in how to make a dart at home, which will last a long time, use plastic rather than cardboard.
  10. Set the wings in the incisions to the stop. It is important that they attach with effort. Sharpen the dart and you can start using it. As a target, use any board printed on paper or with a painted target.

Alternative



Next, we will tell you how to make a dart out of paper. We need a sheet with dimensions of 7 by 7 cm. We bend the prepared paper twice diagonally. Insert the needle. We attach four matches to the top of the dart. Firmly wrap the structure with threads.
