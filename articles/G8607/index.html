How to make a witch costume?

The witch costume, which was used only in the theater during the Soviet period, has recently been very popular at Halloween. According to the western interpretation of this image.





In order for the image of the dark heroine to be successful, black clothing is first of all necessary, which will be supplemented with accessories. You can take a dark purple, black or dark brown turtleneck and skirt, or you can dress of one or another length. A black cloak is thrown over them , which is simple enough to sew.

For a cape, we measure the girth of the body with our hands. You also need to determine the length of the cloak, plus the margin for the collar (if you plan to do it). To give the image of mystery and gloom, a witch costume must be made long. Cut off the required piece of material and process the edges with an overlock. Next, measure about 25 cm from the top, fold the fabric and make a line at a distance of 1.5-2 cm from the fold. In the resulting "pipe" we pass the tape, which will be tied around the neck. After the cap is glued, it is connected to one of the details of the fields (dark side up). The parts of the cone protruding from below are cut, smeared with glue from the outside and turned away upwards. The second part of the hat field is glued from below with the dark side down.
