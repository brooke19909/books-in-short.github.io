Pushkin Park in Saransk - a great place for a family vacation

Park named after A.S. Pushkin is a peculiar symbol of the city of Saransk. Shady alleys, a children's town, a cheerful train running around the territory, Pushkin corners - this is Pushkin Park in Saransk.

The park is located in a lowland, so slopes from the central square of the city lead to it. One of them is a fountain. In the warm season, everything here is decorated with colorful flowers, numerous flower beds are pleasing to the eye. Those who pass here enjoy beautiful views of the park.

How it all began

The history of Pushkin Park in Saransk begins in the distant 1899. Then it was organized more like a garden on Uspenskaya Square - in honor of the centenary of the birthday of the greatest poet. In 1935, the park was enlarged, received the status of a city park of culture and recreation, and since then it has been transformed more and more every year.

Rides

If you want to have fun and ride interesting attractions, come to the Pushkin Park in Saransk. The attractions here are different: children and adults, calm and with a splash of adrenaline. If you like dynamic ones, choose Emelya, Drop Zone or Waltz.

There are a lot of options for children of all ages in the park: from swans and a train to races, shooting galleries and trampolines of all kinds. Attraction prices do not bite.

Ferris wheel - a attraction beloved by the townspeople. From its upper point, beautiful views of the clean, well-kept city center. For guests of the city this is a great opportunity to decide on the further hiking route.

Park Chips

The duck island attracts little visitors, it is so great to watch duck families from an arm's length. Children are delighted when they feed the ducks. The island is well located in the shade of trees, giving coolness even to the hottest heat.

The live calendar is another feature of the park. It is located near the entrance and is a pleasant reminder of the date you visited the park. Every morning, workers change the date to delight visitors with a floral calendar.

Pushkin's panel is another element that emphasizes the name of the park. The panel is of impressive size, but always perfectly even, fresh and invariably green.

There is one zoo in the city, and it is located in the park. Here you can get acquainted with exotic animals, feed rabbits, touch turtles. Children are always surprised by unusual birds. The territory of the zoo is very clean, well-groomed. There are wooden arbors, statues of characters from your favorite cartoons.

The recreation park occupies a vast territory. There are many beautiful arbors and benches, a fountain, a stage for events, a pier where you can ride a catamaran or a boat.

If you have children, be sure to come to Pushkin's park in Saransk. Work mode:

  • Mon: 13:00 - 22:00;
  • Tue-Fri: 11:00 - 22:00;
  • days off: 10:00 - 22:00.

For children, this is a real paradise: simulators and numerous equipped playgrounds, statues and cartoon characters throughout the park, a fun train, music, ice cream, attractions. The kid will say thank you for sure!

If you were lucky enough to visit the Pushkin Park in Saransk in the summer, then you will appreciate how many all kinds of flower beds and flower arrangements are in it. A huge number of people daily work on landscaping.

The Park of Culture and Rest named after A.S. Pushkin is a wonderful green zone in the center of Saransk, an important attraction of the city and a favorite resting place for residents and guests of the capital of Mordovia.
