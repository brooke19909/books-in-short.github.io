Hotel Titan Garden Hotel 4 *, Turkey: description and reviews of tourists

When you have to decide where to relax with high quality and not very expensive, you can safely choose the resorts of Turkey. Sandy beaches, azure waves, many attractions and developed tourist infrastructure make your vacation pleasant and varied. Low prices for accommodation allow you to come here to almost any average resident of Russia. You can comfortably stay here in one of the hotels located between the cities of Alanya and Konakli, Titan Garden Hotel 4. It is far from the last in the list of the most famous hotels. Thousands of tourists come here every year, many of them return with pleasure the next year.



general information

Titan Garden Hotel 4 is a large modern hotel, which was built in 2003. A couple of years ago they redecorated here. It offers bright, clean and comfortable rooms that have everything you need for a good stay.

The total area reaches 7500 square meters. The hotel is represented by two buildings of 6-story buildings, which houses 327 rooms. Of them:

  • 232 - the number of standard rooms;
  • 47 - the number of family rooms equipped with intermediate doors;
  • 48 are Superior Rooms.

Around the hotel there is a well-equipped area, there are several pools, water activities, places for relaxation.

Hotel Location

This 4-star hotel is located in the seaside resort of Payallar, almost 100 meters from the coastline. You can get to the beach in just 5-7 minutes.

Tourists arrive here by plane. It lands at Antalya airport, from the airport to the resort area you can successfully reach both by public transport and taxi. The journey takes approximately 1.5 hours. Another option to get here is to take a plane ticket to Gazipasa (from here you can get to the hotel in 1 hour).

Located between Konakli and Alanya, Titan Garden Hotel 4 (Turkey) compares favorably with other hotels. From here, in a short time you can reach many important places:

  • the ancient city and historical center of Side (55 km away);
  • Alanya city center (nearby, only 12 km);
  • Konakli city center (approximately 800 m);
  • a mall called Megamall.

The staff at Alanya Hotel Titan Garden Hotel 4 offers a variety of services to make guests feel cozy and comfortable.

  • Reception desk. She works around the clock.

  • Free use of Wi-Fi in the lobby.
  • Concierge services are available.
  • Chemical washing and ironing.
  • Transfer services. Meeting at the airport.
  • Fresh press delivery.
  • Possibility to use the “call-alarm” service.
  • Daily maid service.
  • Change of linen 2 times a week.
  • Using a left-luggage office.
  • Safe for an extra charge.
  • Room and room service.

For business travelers there is the opportunity to take advantage of:

  • spacious conference room;
  • telephone fax.

As additional services, travelers are offered:

  • car rental for comfortable movement;
  • tour desk (there are many interesting places in the vicinity, so you should use this offer);
  • purchase of tickets;
  • currency exchanger.

Comfort in the rooms

Hotel Turkey Titan Garden Hotel 4 is fully consistent with the stated 4 stars. With the relatively low cost of rooms, everything is provided here that tourists may require.

Each room has:

  • TV with satellite television;
  • mini-bar with water (it is filled on the day of arrival of guests);
  • telephone;
  • safe (it can be used with a certain payment for this service);
  • air conditioning with separate system.

The rooms have a bathroom with shower, hairdryer, free toiletries.

Each room has a balcony with a wonderful view. The windows overlook the well-kept garden and pool - vacationers can choose what is more to their liking.



Hotel staff

The staff speaks several languages:

  • Turkish

Entertainment at the hotel

In such a large comfortable hotel guests will not be bored. It offers well-designed hotel facilities.

  • The main pool at the hotel. Its depth reaches 150 cm, and in volume it is 525 cubic meters.
  • Small relaxation pool (indoor). Its depth is 150 cm, and the volume is not less than 180 cubic meters.
  • Another outdoor pool with a water park. The depth here is less than in the previous one - only 110 cm, the volume is 120 cubic meters. There are 2 water slides.
  • Around the pool there are a sufficient number of sun loungers and beach umbrellas.
  • Tennis court.
  • Playground.
  • Well-equipped fitness center.
  • Animation. There is a day and evening program.
  • Equipment for darts.

health and beauty

In addition to swimming in the sea, entertainment on the beach and hiking in the sights, tourists can have a wonderful rest and relax at the hotel. Titan Garden Hotel 4 can offer:

  • a trip to the sauna;
  • professional spa services (full range);
  • jacuzzi;
  • hairdresser services.

The spa offers a complete list of such services. Here, massages of individual zones or the whole body, scrub treatments, spa baths and many other pleasant and useful body treatments are performed. Hotel guests can choose the Turkish bath (hammam) or sauna to their liking.

Fun for kids

The smallest travelers will not remain idle. For them, there is a lot of entertainment on site:

  • Kids club. This service is intended for children from 4 to 12 years old. In the club, children play, frolic and are constantly under the supervision of nannies and animators.
  • Playground on the street.
  • Two children's pools at the hotel. Depth up to 60 cm.
  • Cot. At the request of guests, a playpen can be installed in the room.
  • The restaurant provides highchairs. Therefore, there will be no problems with feeding and placing a young child.
  • Dishes of children's cuisine are provided.

Dining at the Titan Garden Hotel 4 *

At the hotel every day open their doors:

  • 2-floor restaurant;
  • pool bar;
  • 4 bar lounges;
  • a bar with refreshments and junk food on the beach;
  • coffee and tea in a public place.

The buffet restaurant serves breakfast, lunch, dinner and nightly soup. Those who prefer to spend all their time on the beach can have a snack right there. The beach bar always serves fresh, varied food. These are salads, fresh fruits, fries, hamburgers, spaghetti. You can quench your thirst with mineral water, drinks, juice and a rather popular delicacy - ice slash (a delicious fruit cocktail mixed with ice crumbs).



After lunch, the poolside bar serves tea with lush fresh pastries. Tea is buffet style.

The wonderful Turkish Gozleme dish (Turkish flatbread) can be tasted in the main garden. They are served daily from 11:00 to 15:30 hours.

All Inclusive

In addition to the usual accommodation, Titan Garden Hotel 4 offers an all-inclusive rate. The prices for such accommodation are slightly higher, since meals are included in restaurants and bars on the territory of the hotel. There are, however, slight limitations in this regard.

The cost of living in a room using this system includes:

  • meals in the restaurant (the buffet system operates here);
  • different snacks;
  • local drinks;
  • use of a minibar in the room (there are restrictions);
  • in one or several restaurants of the hotel there is a restriction on the types and quantity of drinks.

Imported and elite alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks are paid separately.

What can I see nearby?

Living at Alanya Titan Garden Hotel 4 * (Turkey), lovers of excursions can go for a walk to explore local attractions. Among the natural beauties it is impossible not to note:

  • Damlatas cave (located 14 km from the hotel);
  • banks of the Dimchay River (36 km);
  • scenic views of the Sapa Dere Canyon (55 km);
  • Manavgat waterfall with a viewing platform (50 km from the hotel).

You can go shopping and souvenirs in the following places:

  • Megamall shopping center (800 m from the hotel);
  • Bazaar in Alanya (open on Fridays, located at a distance of 14 km);
  • Bazaar in Konakli (900 m, open on Wednesdays).

Those who love nightlife and fiery dances can visit the Summer Garden disco.



Beach

Those who stayed at the Konakli Titan Garden Hotel 4 stars, the hotel offers all the amenities to relax on the beach and swim in the sky-blue waters of the Mediterranean Sea. The beach is covered with pebbles and coarse sand. The area is clean, well-groomed and well equipped for recreation:

  • Changing cabins are available.
  • A sufficient number of sun loungers, mattresses and umbrellas are offered.
  • You can use the shower.
  • Towels can be borrowed with a cash deposit, which is returned after rest.

There are a lot of entertainment on the beach, so there is something for everyone to enjoy:

  • Diving. The Mediterranean coast of Turkey is perfect for scuba diving. There are experienced instructors and rental of all necessary equipment.
  • Parasailing. Parasailing, mounted on a motor boat, has become one of the most popular, among the available entertainment.
  • Water skiing.
  • Riding on motor boats and sailboats.

Outdoor enthusiasts are offered rafting on the Alara River.

Additional Information

Before heading to the Titan Garden Hotel 4, you should familiarize yourself with some of the features of accommodation:

  • Check out time is 12:00.

  • Self-registration is only possible after reaching 18 years of age.
  • Children on site can stay from any age.
  • Pets are strictly prohibited here.
  • The hotel has rooms for people with disabilities.
  • Smoking is allowed on site only in specially equipped areas.




Titan Garden Hotel 4: Turkey reviews

Like any other public institution, the hotel managed to get positive and negative reviews. The customers who left negative reviews mostly noted the following disadvantages.

  • Weak (monotonous) animation. In general, this information is only relevant for couples with children.
  • Pebble beach. When planning a vacation, this must be taken into account. However, there are sandy beaches a little further.
  • Extreme heat that air conditioners cannot handle. To make the weather pleasant, it is better to go to Turkey in May, June, September or October. At this time, the air temperature reaches 27-32 degrees, and the water has already warmed up enough. July-August is the hottest time in the area. The thermometer column is held at 35-38 degrees.

At the same time, most tourists about the Titan Garden Hotel 4 leave positive reviews, and many travelers have come back here more than once. Among the advantages:

  • daily quality housekeeping;
  • timely replacement of both bed linen and towels;
  • very helpful, smiling staff;
  • varied menu - always available meat, poultry and fish, snacks, salads, a lot of fresh fruits, lush fragrant pastries.

Given all of the above, we can conclude: the Titan Garden Hotel 4 hotel fully confirms its status as a 4 star and, despite some shortcomings, is in high demand among tourists. Buying tours with accommodation in this hotel is for couples with children, newlyweds, young people and people aged. In other words, at the Titan Garden Hotel 4 everyone who loves beach vacations, excursions, diving, water activities will find something to their liking.
