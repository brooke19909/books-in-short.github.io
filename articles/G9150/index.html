Michell Hotel & Spa 5 * (Turkey / Alanya): photos, prices and reviews of tourists

The Turkish coast of the Mediterranean has long been our favorite vacation spot. Firstly, close, secondly, inexpensively, thirdly, qualitatively. In addition, the climate here is just perfect for the Russians and does not require any acclimatization. In addition, in these parts there are many historical sights - a rich heritage of the peoples (Armenians, Greeks, Byzantines, Phoenicians, etc.) that once inhabited the territory of modern Turkey. And this feature attracts on the coast not only fans of beach vacations, but also sightseeing tours.



Holidays in Turkey

The biggest advantage of this country for tourists is free, that is, visa-free entry. When crossing the Turkish border, it is enough for citizens of the Russian Federation to present a passport, a ticket to a particular hotel, for example, to Michell Hotel Spa Alanya, and a return ticket for the plane. Until 2013, you could stay in Turkey without a visa for 60 days, but today this period has decreased to 30. As noted above, there are many places worth visiting in this country, but its main feature is still magnificent beaches and chic Hotels located along the entire coast. Among them you can find both budget 2-3 * and chic five-star deluxe hotels, which is the brand new and built by modern technologies Michell Hotel Spa 5 * hotel, which is located in the village of Avsallar, Alanya resort. By the way, until recently, this resort did not have a single five-star hotel. However, today, 400 meters from the beach, its original eight-story building resembling a motor ship flaunts. Before proceeding with the description of this hotel, I want to talk a bit about the area in which the Michell Hotel Spa is located.

The coast of Alanya impresses vacationers with a ‚Äúbare‚Äù, devoid of vegetation terrain. However, this does not apply to the village of Avsallar, in which the Michell Hotel Spa Alanya 5 is located. This situation is facilitated by the location of the resort on the shore of the bay, which protects it from the winds. In other villages of Alanya (Inzhekum, Mahmutlar, Konakli, etc.), everything is different, there is practically no vegetation. Probably because of this, Alanya is not among the most popular tourist destinations in Turkey. Reviews of tourists who have already managed to visit this chic hotel are quite positive. However, according to almost all vacationers, there is still much to be done to bring it to a state of 5 *. Climate

Since Alanya is located at the foot of the Taurus Mountains, it is protected by them from the invasion of winds blowing from the north. The subtropical Mediterranean climate contributes to the establishment of wonderful weather conditions for a beach holiday. The swimming season here lasts from late April to November, and in Avsallar, the warmest of the villages of Alanya, from early April to late November, that is, a month longer than in other areas. If you have not had time to relax this year, then you are still ahead. Until the end of November, you can visit Alanya and stay at the chic Michell Hotel Spa. The only thing to consider when buying a tour to this hotel is that the children in this hotel have nothing to do. For them there is no entertainment and amenities. In a word, although this is not said openly, the hotel is absolutely not suitable for couples with children.

How to get there

Avsallar is located 140 kilometers from the airport of Antalya, where planes arrive from different parts of the world, including from many large cities of the Russian Federation. This terminal is the closest to Alanya. And if you are going to the Avsallar village to relax in the five-star Michell Hotel Spa 5 * hotel by air, you must purchase a ticket for the flight to Antalya, and from there you will need to go by road to the resort first to Alanya, and then another 24 kilometers to Avsallar. For a fee, Michel Hotel employees will meet you at the airport and deliver you directly to the hotel complex. However, you can also use regular minibuses (fare 5 euros).
