Hotel Fortuna Phuket 3 * (Phuket, Thailand): photos and reviews of tourists

Do you want to take a break from everyday work, relax under the rays of the gentle sun in the shade of a large tree, enjoy exotic landscapes, eat overseas dishes, swim in the sea? Then you need to visit an amazing country called Thailand.





general information

Thailand has an incredibly rich culture and stunning nature in its beauty, which is why every year the number of tourists who want to visit this country is only growing. Travelers from all over the world come here to relax. Someone comes for just a week, and someone is delayed for a month, not able to leave such a splendor.

In Thailand, there are a number of the most preferred areas for recreation, where the infrastructure for the country's guests is most developed. The best of such places is recognized, according to vacationers, the resort of Phuket. It is in this province that there are most hotels, boarding houses. There are at least hundreds of them, so any vacationer has the opportunity to find for himself something suitable to his taste and financial capabilities.

The most popular place is considered to be the Fortuna Phuket hotel (Thailand, Phuket). The reasons why tourists choose this particular hotel are simple. It has excellent cuisine, luxurious rooms, great service, perfect cleanliness and polite staff. In general, all the conditions for a great holiday can be found only here. Noisy youth companies, couples with children, and people of more advanced age will find a lot of interesting things for themselves.

Now let's talk in more detail about the place of residence. After all, this is incredibly important for the traveler, because everyone wants to spend their vacation in comfort.
