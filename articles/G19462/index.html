Uralsib credit cards: reviews, registration, conditions, grace period, cash withdrawal

Uralsib Bank began operations in 2005. Today it is in the Top 10 largest financial and credit organizations of the country.

The bank offers its customers more than 15 types of credit cards. Each product is distinguished by discount programs, special offers, annual maintenance costs, bonuses, etc. Uralsib cards are protected as much as possible. A microchip is built into them, the PIN code is available only to the client, each operation is accompanied by an SMS notification, purchases are impossible without one-time passwords (3D-Secure system). So how do you choose the best? Let's try to figure it out.





Lending terms

The bank defines a number of conditions for opening a credit card. Here is some of them:

  • The credit card of Uralsib Bank is subject to overdraft. That is, a loan opens in the event of an overspending of personal money.
  • Such a card is issued only to the client of the bank. That is, it is necessary to have at least one account in it or to receive wages through Uralsib, etc.
  • Interest on Uralsib credit card - up to 28% per annum. They are selected individually in each individual case. For example, if documents confirming income were not provided when opening a credit card, then the maximum rate will be set.
  • The credit limit approved by the bank is 500,000 rubles.
  • The grace period is most often set at 60 days.
  • Uralsib credit cards (this topic is emphasized in the reviews) are issued for three years, but you can use a loan as long as a deposit account is opened.
  • A fee will be charged for the operations of transferring funds and issuing cash. Most often, it is 3.9% (but not less than 300 rubles).
  • Card maintenance costs an average of 299 rubles. in year. It depends on the class and type of card.
  • Almost every card has a compliment bonus program. The points earned give you the opportunity to purchase tickets, book hotels, etc.




Borrower Requirements

Uralsib does not expose any special conditions and rules to the borrower. Standard requirements:

  • Russian citizenship.
  • Have registration at the place of residence in the region where the card is issued for at least 6 months.
  • Age limit of 25 years.
  • A borrower who has reached his 60th birthday and does not have life insurance or disability risks, the bank is entitled to suspend the Uralsib credit card (reviews warn about this).
  • The borrower must hold the last position for at least three months.

Annual interest rate

For all types of credit cards, it is 28%. In some cases, for example, for owners of salary programs, the rate may be reduced. Each package of documents is considered individually.





Validity

Any type of credit card of Uralsib Bank is valid for three years. The agreement with the bank may be prolonged simultaneously with the reissue of the card, subject to the requirements of Uralsib. In addition, any of the loan programs must have a grace period (that is, repayment of debt without interest). Unlike most banks in which the benefit is valid for no more than 55 days, Uralsib provides 60 days.

By the way, the bank makes concessions to customers and makes it possible to replace the card under an outdated lending program with a new Uralsib credit card with a grace period. Denied only to those who have "hitches" in the credit history.

Credit card activation

Any kind of it requires activation. The procedure is simple: any operation in a terminal or ATM requesting a PIN code (it is indicated in a secret envelope issued with the card).

Cash withdrawal

The procedure can be completed at any of the Uralsib ATMs and the terminals of partner banks. These include Mezhtopenergobank, Rosinterbank, Uglemetbank and others.

In any of the terminals, this service will cost from 300 rubles to 3.9% of the issuance amount.





Making payments

According to the bank‚Äôs terms, you can pay off the debt in several ways:

  • Directly in the office.
  • At the Uralsib ATM or any other that supports the cash in function.

  • Withdrawal from a salary card.

Repaying the loan, according to the terms of the bank, is possible with any amount, but it should be at least 5% of all debt.

How to order?

There are two ways to get a bank credit card:
