Do-it-yourself paper pigeons: great fun and a beautiful gift

A lot of people around the world like to craft something and make their own hands from a variety of materials. Someone earns a living by this, someone just rests from everyday work. Joint creativity is, of course, a great pastime for adults and children, helping to develop the child‚Äôs creative abilities and bring him closer to his parents. In this regard, crafts from the world of origami are well suited. This ancient Japanese art makes it possible to create real masterpieces of fantasy and creativity from a simple sheet of paper. And indeed, any paper creations are good, despite its fragility and fragility.





Here, for example, pigeons made of paper, made with your own hands - this is a great decoration at home or a toy for children. Such birds can be folded for games, fun, or in order to brighten the holiday. A handmade bird gift is also a great idea for those who want and can give something unusual and extraordinary.





Pigeons made of paper with their own hands are made as follows: you can fold them according to the traditional scheme, which is provided by the art of origami (here you only need paper and nothing more), or you can draw, cut out a figure and glue it. The second option is simpler and characterized by greater detail and clarity. The first is sketchy, but that does not lose its charm and charm.





So, doves of paper with their own hands are folded according to the following scheme. A square sheet of plain white stationery is taken in white or another color, which is more to your liking. The sheet is folded diagonally into a rhombus, then it is folded and folded along the other diagonal so that in the end it turns out to be a triangle. Now you need to bend up the upper corner of the resulting triangle, spread the paper layers and bend the figure in half (that is, bend its lower half up). Now the side of the triangle is lowered down - this is the wings of the future bird. The next step is that the craft needs to be rotated 180 degrees, then bend the wings and bend the beak. That, in fact, is all. Thus, the paper sheet is folded quite simply and quickly, you just have to follow the long-developed rules and canons of paper art, and then you can do almost anything, even a flashlight made of paper with your own hands.

The second way to create paper birds is simpler and more visual. To do this, first on a piece of paper of a color you like, you need to draw separately the outline of the body with the tail and the connected wings. Now both parts are cut and bent along the fold lines on the back. Then bends up and tail. The body of the future bird, as well as the head and beak below are fastened with glue. Now the second blank is taken - the wings. It just needs to be greased with the same glue and attached to the body. That, in fact, is all. Pigeons made of paper, made by hand, are not only simple to execute, but also very beautiful. They can be left at home or presented to someone, pleased with an elegant figure of friends and acquaintances. The paper symbol of peace will give you a good mood and make you smile.
