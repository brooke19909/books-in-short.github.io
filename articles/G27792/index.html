Hotel "Yalta-Intourist 4 *": reviews, photos, address, telephone

Hotel Yalta-Intourist is located at the foot of Ai-Petri Mountain, on the Black Sea coast. It is surrounded by Massandra Park with exotic plants and trees. The complex is one of the largest on the Black Sea coast of Crimea.





Where is it, how to get, phone

Hotel "Yalta-Intourist" is located in the center of Yalta. You can get to it by city bus number 34. There is a free shuttle service for guests from Simferopol Airport to the hotel and vice versa. For its implementation, you must contact the administration of the Yalta-Intourist Hotel (phone: 8-800-555-65-35, a call throughout Russia is free).



Description of rooms

"Yalta-Intourist 4 *" can simultaneously accept about two and a half thousand people. This is not surprising, because the building is large in width and has 16 floors. The year of its construction is 1977, the last major overhaul was made in 2014.

To the choice of vacationers, the Yalta-Intourist hotel provides the following types of rooms:

  • Standard.
  • Standard with increased comfort.
  • Junior suites for one and a half rooms.
  • Two-room junior suites.
  • Suites.
  • Apartments.

Pets allowed. True, for a dog, well, or a well-fed cat more than 5 kg, you will have to pay extra.

Each room must include the following:

  • Balcony with a panoramic view that inspires relaxation.
  • TV panel, cable TV on more than 70 channels.
  • Safe for storing securities and valuables.
  • Refrigerator (its dimensions are directly proportional to the class of the room).
  • Air conditioning.
  • Bed (two single or one double). Bedside tables.

Number standard differs from a similar, but improved, terms of repair.

Junior suites and suites have two zones:

  • guest room (with sofa);
  • relaxation area.

Two-room rooms are not separated by a door. They are equipped with a separate bathroom (shower or bath). A nice addition is the presence of two balconies.





Conference organization

"Yalta-Intourist 4 *" for guests engaged in the business sector, offers conference rooms and a large conference hall that seats up to 1,500 people. This room hosts events of international scale (children's "New Wave", trade and industrial exhibitions). Every year, the UN and UNESCO organize conferences here.





Bars and restaurants of the hotel complex

The variety of bars and restaurants that the Yalta-Intourist Hotel boasts is capable of satisfying the tastes of any vacationing guest. In total there are 25 such institutions. Consider the most popular of them.

The Khrustalny Restaurant is perfect for banquets and large celebrations. The hall can accommodate 800 people - this is the largest indicator on the entire coast of Crimea.





Restaurant "Ai-Petri" - you can say the attraction of the hotel. Located on the 16th floor of the hotel. The windows offer a gorgeous view of the resort town and the sea. European and sophisticated dishes

The main restaurant of the hotel is the Marble Restaurant. It is famous for its culinary masterpieces. It can offer dishes from Russian and European cuisines. Every evening, evenings are organized on the theme of the traditions of the peoples of the world.

Gazebo Bar is located by the hotel's pool and offers its visitors a wide variety of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks. Periodically, parties are held on various topics and holidays.

The lobby bar is a cozy place of the hotel. In the morning, you can have breakfast with a cup of coffee and homemade cakes. The bar offers a large assortment of cocktails for children and adults.

Cafe-bar "Cinema" offers the best dishes of European cuisine. Images from favorite movies are shown on the big screen. The whole atmosphere of the institution is saturated with the spirit of cinema.

Beach Hotels: reviews

The private beach of the closed type has the Yalta-Intourist Hotel. The photo below demonstrates its staffing with umbrellas and sunbeds. An interesting feature is the ability to go down to the beach with an elevator in 1 minute. Shade canopies, sun loungers, toilets, changing rooms are included in the room rate.

Reviews about the hotel

Massandra Park leaves no one indifferent. It is saturated greens and clean air. The territory is constantly monitored: exotic trees and bushes are cleaned, trimmed, and vegetation is looked after. Near the entrance to the hotel is a small pond with swans and large fish. A little further there is a fountain. At night, the building is beautifully lit.

Payment system

The entire territory of the Yalta-Intourist Hotel has its own payment system. You can pay in bars and restaurants located on the territory of the hotel complex only with special cards or a chip built into the bracelet. For many, this seems convenient. However, there are those who consider this simply a waste of time upon arrival.
