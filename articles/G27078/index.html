Historical moments

The history of the village where Aquamarine is located is very interesting. Vityazevo - a small village - in the recent past was a Cossack village, based on the site of a defensive redoubt. During the war, Major Vityaz heroically fought with the Circassians here, and even after a serious wound, he continued to command army units, inspiring his warriors to continue resistance. It was after his death that this area from Pontus Euxinus was first renamed into a village, and then into the village of Vityazevo. Most of its population was Greeks.
