Hotel "Aldemar Royal Knossos" 5 * (Crete, Greece): photos, review and reviews of tourists

Crete fell in love with tourists because of the picturesque natural landscapes, combining mountain landscapes and snow-white sandy coasts. People come to the island not only for a beach holiday, there are also ancient sights of ancient times, various shops, and cozy Greek taverns. At the same time, a lot of hotels have been built in Crete. If you wish, you can rent an inexpensive room in a budget hotel or rent spacious apartments in a luxury resort. It is for tourists who appreciate a high level of service, the Aldemar Knossos Royal 5 * hotel was built. We will tell about its territory, infrastructure and numbers in this article. And also consider the reviews of guests who rested here earlier.

Basic tourist information

Immediately it is worth noting that the hotel "Aldemar Knossos Royal" (Crete) belongs to the category of respectable complexes, so a vacation here is not cheap. At the same time, tourists generally positively assess the local level of service and consider that the price is consistent with quality. The hotel itself is part of the large tourist chain Aldemar Resorts, which is the leading Greek market. In addition, next to the complex is another hotel of this network, so the territory they have in common. However, this does not affect the quality of service.

The hotel itself was built in 1992. In total, it offers tourists 373 spacious rooms. At the same time, future guests, when buying a ticket, can choose where to settle: in the main building of the complex or in small two-story bungalows. The hotel is decorated in a traditional Greek style. It features a garden, an outdoor pool and a sunbathing area.

The hotel has standard rules for settlement and accommodation. Even tourists with the smallest children can relax here. But you cannot come here with animals, they are not accepted even for a fee. Russian-speaking staff at the hotel work daily, so people who do not speak English do not have to worry. The staff will always help to solve pressing issues and fill out the necessary documents.

Where is the Aldemar Knossos Royal Resort?

Another advantage of relaxation in this place is its advantageous location. The hotel is built right on the coast, on the first beach line, so tourists can reach the sea in a matter of minutes. The complex is surrounded by a dense forested area, so it is quite quiet here, day and night. For guests' convenience, a bus stop is located right at the hotel entrance. From here they can reach the town of Hersonissos, which is 2 km away. At the same time, a free bus leaves from here daily, delivering tourists to large shopping centers located nearby.

Crete has its own international airport, where flights from major Russian cities fly quite often in the high season. It is located next to Heraklion, which is about 40 km from the hotel. This distance tourists overcome in about 1 hour. At the same time, guests of the Aldemar Knossos Royal Hotel (island of Crete) can pay for the transfer, and they will be picked up from the airport by a comfortable bus equipped with air conditioning and comfortable seats. Also, guests can get to the hotel by taxi or rented car.

Beach Holidays: Sea and Pool

The beach at the Hotel Aldemar Royal Knossos (Crete) has its own beach. It is fully equipped for a comfortable stay. Sun loungers, sun umbrellas and towels are provided free of charge. The beach at the hotel is sand and gravel. At the same time, a few meters from the shore in the water there is a large stone slab, on which you can injure your legs. In order not to slip on it, it is recommended to wear special shoes.

A distinctive feature of this hotel is the outdoor pools. All of them are filled exclusively with sea water. There are four of them, but two of them are designed for VIP guests. Nearby, as expected, is a spacious terrace where you can lie down to relax on a comfortable sun lounger. In bad weather, guests can enjoy the indoor freshwater pool.
