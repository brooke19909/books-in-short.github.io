Hotel 4 * Peach Blossom Resort (Thailand / Phuket): description, photos and reviews of tourists

Hotel 4 * Peach Blossom Resort is a comfortable modern hotel that many will like. Here, families and noisy companies, as well as those who love peace and solitude, will have a great rest. The surroundings of the hotel are incredibly beautiful nature, as well as golden azure beaches.





Hotel Description

4 * Peach Blossom Resort is a paradise on the shores of the Andaman Sea. In the interior of the hotel, as well as in the design of its rooms, wood predominates, which only emphasizes the ecological orientation and philosophy of unity with nature. Nevertheless, the room has many modern amenities and other achievements of progress.

Since Thailand is a very original and atypical country, then staying at the 4 * Peach Blossom Resort hotel , you should definitely use the services of a travel agency that will offer you many exciting sightseeing routes. And if you like to explore the sights on your own, then rent a car and hit the road.

Not only that, near the hotel there are two excellent well-kept beaches. The 4 * hotel Peach Blossom Resort also provided for guests the opportunity to swim in the pool. And if you still want a thrill, then be sure to go surfing, sailing or diving.





Hotel location

The resort and hotel complex Peach Blossom Resort 4 * (Phuket) is located between two famous sandy beaches - Kata and Karon. It is about 20 minutes by car or bus to Patong. But the road to the airport will take twice as long.

Apartment Amenities

The rooms of the hotel Peach Blossom Resort 4 * (Phuket) expect the following amenities:

  • individual air conditioning (namely a split system) will help you create ideal climatic conditions for living in your room;
  • the bathroom has a modern shower with hydromassage (the villas have a bathtub), as well as a hairdryer, bath accessories and disposable cosmetics;
  • the apartments have a kitchenette (and some have a full kitchen for cooking);
  • thanks to the TV connected to the hotel satellite dish, you will always be up to date with world news, and you will also be able to enjoy watching your favorite films and entertaining programs (Russian-language channels are available);
  • all apartments have a set with which you can make fragrant tea or invigorating coffee;
  • thanks to the balcony or terrace, you will have your own corner for relaxing in the fresh air.

It is important that room service is available around the clock, so in case of problems or malfunctions, all problems will be immediately fixed. It is also important to have special apartments designed for non-smoking guests.

Important information

Of course, information about the services and the number of rooms is the most important when choosing a hotel, but do not forget about the internal rules that govern the stay in resort hotels. So, about this institution you need to know the following:

  • as in most other resort hotels, check-out hours that regulate the time of check-in and check-out are assigned, respectively, at 2 pm and 12:00;
  • children under 4 years old without extra bed, as well as babies under 2 years old in a special crib, live in the apartment absolutely free of charge;
  • the cost of an extra bed (in any room it is possible to provide such a service only to one person) is 1000 Thai baht ;
  • on a credit card at the time of booking, as well as for the period of residence, a certain amount is blocked as a deposit;
  • newlyweds and those who have given preference to this hotel will be given a pleasant surprise in the form of a cake and festive decor in the apartments, and regular guests are encouraged by a plate of exotic fruits.
