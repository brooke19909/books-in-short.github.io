Gazprombank: a wide range of leasing

Gazprombank Leasing is a large company founded in 2003. Today it is part of Gazprombank Group of Companies, which includes several of the largest financial, media, petrochemical and engineering companies. That is why millions of Russian residents trust her. She is one of the ten largest leasing companies in the country and has long been established in the leading market positions.

Millions of people choose Gazprombank Leasing, because this company has a lot of advantages:

  1. Impeccable reputation.
  2. Reliability.

  4. Only an individual approach.
  5. High quality standards in the work of the company.

How is the clearance going?





Depending on the chosen leasing program, the transaction will be executed according to its plan. The simplest and fastest system is the choice of the Standard package. In this case, the transaction will be concluded as follows:

  1. It is necessary to submit an application. This can be done through the official website of the company.
  2. A specialist of Gazprombank Leasing contacts the client within 2 business days.
  3. It is necessary to collect and provide all the necessary documents.
  4. The final application is considered 3 working days, after which a decision is made.
  5. If the answer is yes, then a special leasing agreement is signed within 2 days.

Individual programs are carried out on conditions developed for a particular client.

Requirements





The lessee has certain requirements:

  • If this is a Standard program, then the client company must conduct its activities for at least 2 years. Moreover, the annual revenue of the enterprise cannot be less than 200,000,000 rubles.
  • If investment leasing of equipment is expected, it is important to provide a business plan with details. A license must be available. Finally, the client company cannot have any debts.
  • Need special equipment or construction equipment? You can lease it provided that you have a license and no debts to any counterparties. Additionally, an item on the amount of payments and the selection of an object is added to the agreement.
  • If railway transport is leased, then the client should already have rolling stock on its balance sheet . Another condition is the absence of debts for both loans and taxes.
