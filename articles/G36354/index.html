The best hotels of Maldives

Maldives - this is one of those heavenly places on Earth in which you want to buy a one-way ticket. Sandy azure coast, cozy beaches, exotic fruits and beautiful palm trees - these are the things that the resort can offer. But even more captivating is the ultramodern, chic, comfortable and luxurious hotels of the Maldives. There is no need to talk about the level of service in hotels of the islands. Everything is equipped here at the highest level, and smiling staff is ready to fulfill the whims of guests around the clock.



A View Of Three Star Hotels In The Maldives

The Maldives resort ( we attach the photo of hotels to the article) can offer an economical type of vacation in the form of hotels, which are assigned only three stars. Unfortunately, guests will have to be satisfied with the services of not too high quality and they will need to live in rooms that have not seen repair for a long time. But if there is a chance to save a couple of thousand dollars, then such things seem only trifles.

What four-star hotels look like

Rest in the Maldives is always relevant . Hotels with four stars are always in demand among tourists. The numbers in them are bought up at a speed greater than that of light. The level of service and equipment in institutions of this category is at a fairly high level. The cost of a single apartment starts at $ 90 per night.

Four Star Hotels

If tourists are not satisfied with cheap hotels, then they can always consider more expensive offers. In this case, the Maldives offers 4-star hotels. In such establishments, the rooms are more elegantly furnished, and here guests are provided with a wider range of services than in institutions that have only three stars.

Adaaran Prestige Ocean Villas is located on Hudhuranfushi Island. The villa is perfect for those who want to spend a relaxing holiday with friends or family. For your comfort, you will find DVD players, satellite TV, and a minibar in your room.

Hotel Elite Inn is located in Hulhumale. The institution has only ten rooms, but all of them will give a homely atmosphere and comfort. They have LCD televisions, air conditioners, refrigerators and other household appliances so that guests can feel as comfortable as possible.



The Somerset Hotel in Male is located on the oceanfront. Each of the 30 guestrooms has a private bathroom or shower room. A hairdryer and toiletries are provided free of charge for all guests. Every day, staff clean the rooms. The hotel has many opportunities for physical education, among which I would like to mention an ultramodern fitness center.

What a five star hotel offers

Hotels (Maldives, 5 stars) offer rooms that are highly comfortable and equipped with the latest technology. For one night in a similar hotel will have to pay 200 or more dollars. In the most expensive institutions, this amount reaches 2.5 thousand dollars. Not everyone can afford to rent an apartment in such a hotel. Well, the lucky ones who can afford such a luxury get a highly professional service, breathtaking breakfasts and other delights of relaxation.



Five Star Hotels

For those who are accustomed to not denying anything to themselves, businessmen have come up with five-star hotels. The Maldives resort is not far behind in this matter. 5 star hotels are presented in a wide range. For example, the Banyan Tree Vabbinfary hotel was created for those travelers who like a beach holiday away from human civilization. Clients will enjoy a complete relaxation at the local spa with a full range of treatments. The hotel has a restaurant where guests are offered a continental breakfast every morning .





If you are relaxing in the city of Hembadu, in the Maldives, and you are not constrained by the means, then you can choose the Vivanta by Taj Coral Reef hotel for your vacation. Guests can enjoy exotic local specialties at the hotel's restaurant. All guests enjoy a complimentary breakfast in the morning. The resort has a Russian bath, an outdoor pool and a fitness center.

On the North Male Atoll, in the city of Huwafen Fushi, the five-star Per Aquum Huvafen Fushi Hotel has been popular with visitors for several years. The facility has 44 rooms, as well as private swimming pools for diving. Each room has a patio or balcony. It offers digital TV, free internet and an orthopedic mattress.

Finally, One & Only Reethi Rah offers the option of renting a catamaran or seaplane to explore the natural surroundings.



Hotels for honeymooners

Some Maldives hotels are just made for honeymooners who want to spend their honeymoon on the islands . Most local hotels can often be found in wedding magazines all over the planet. So, ideal for a honeymoon hotel in the Maldives are Constance Halaveli Resort, Kanuhura and Naladhu Maldives. All these are five-star institutions with the highest level of service.

Constance Halaveli Resort are elegant villas decorated in a traditional Maldivian style. The villas are built over the waters of the lagoon. The spacious terraces of each are equipped with private pools. The sites offer stunning ocean views.



Kanuhura are also chic villas whose spacious rooms feature sophisticated interiors. Each room is made in warm colors, and the floors, decoration and headsets are made of natural wood. The rooms are decorated with colorful pillows, bedspreads and rugs with ethnic patterns.

The luxurious Naladhu Maldives Hotel is ready to give a newly-married couple a truly paradise vacation. Lovers will fully feel the care of the hotel staff: they will have romantic dinners in the restaurant, surprises with candles in the bathroom, free spa offers for two and many other gifts.
