Grand Bahia Principe San Juan 5 * is a cottage complex for a beach holiday, located almost on the seashore. This is the north coast of the Republic of Dominican Republic. Puerto Plata and surroundings

The area where the province of Rio San Juan is located is the north of the Dominican Republic. It is famous for its beautiful long sandy beaches and pristine jungle. Between his cities and villages, they move either by taxi or by local buses. The latter are very cheap, they travel slowly, stop near each pillar, but with their help you can literally get to any place of interest. Therefore, it is worthwhile to understand their schedule and directions. Breathtaking beaches stretch for about a hundred kilometers. One of the best restaurants in the resort is located in the village of Cabarete, not far from our Grand Bahia Principe hotel. The capital of the resort is interesting for its colonial architectural style, mansions and palaces of Spanish aristocrats. She is also known as one of the first places where the famous discoverer Christopher Columbus arrived.

Grand Bahia Principe San Juan 5 * Resort is located seventy kilometers from Puerto Plata Airport. Despite such an insignificant distance, the transfer to the hotel is quite tiring.
