Hotel "Oasis", Lazarevskoye: overview, description and reviews of tourists

Big Sochi is the most popular resort in Russia, where everything you need for a good rest is concentrated: a crystal clear and gentle sea, a unique subtropical climate, picturesque mountains, healing mineral waters and mud, as well as many other favorable factors.

Village Lazarevskoye, Big Sochi

Lazarevskoye is a village that has established itself as one of the best resorts in Greater Sochi. The infrastructure of the village is very developed, and every year there are more and more attractions for tourists. There are two water parks, ethnographic museums, a dolphinarium and an aquarium in Lazarevsky.

For family holidays in the village there is a chic park of culture and relaxation with modern attractions and all kinds of entertainment. The ferris wheel in Lazarevsky is one of the largest in Russia. In the evenings, the park hosts foam parties, discos and events for all ages. No one will be bored, there are no problems with leisure in Lazarevsky.





The sea is the main attraction of these places. Actually, because of him tourists come here. In the Lazarevskoye Sea, it meets all the expectations of vacationers. Clear water, a shallow bottom and gorgeous pebble beaches, which are considered one of the widest on the entire Black Sea coast.

Special attention should be paid to the small, but very beautiful and cozy embankment of the village. In the evenings, vacationers stroll along it, admire the seascape and sunset. A huge number of restaurants and cafes located on the waterfront are waiting for their visitors.

Southern plants - palm trees, laurels, cypresses, figs and tangerines, which are enormous in the Lazarevskoye village - are pleasing to the eye and add positive.





Many believe that Sochi is a fairly expensive resort. In Lazarevsky, the choice of housing is very wide, and its prices are very diverse, so to speak, for any pocket - both luxury housing in expensive hotels and cozy comfortable rooms in private mini-hotels at affordable prices. Those tourists who love a noisy vacation can settle in the very center of the village, and for lovers of peace and a more relaxing holiday there is accommodation on the outskirts.

Oasis Hotel, Lazarevskoye

The small hotel "Oasis" is located in the center of the resort village of Lazarevskoye, on the second line from the beach, not far from the picturesque promenade. Near the hotel is the village railway station.

Hotel "Oasis" (Lazarevskoye) is a compact four-story building, which houses the entire number of rooms, sauna, cafe and bar. The hotel was built in 2005, and in 2015 the building was renovated.





Oasis Hotel (Lazarevskoye) 2 * is a two-star hotel with a comfort level. The hotel area is small, the courtyard around the building is very small, but quite comfortable and clean. A one-story dining room building under the eponymous name "Oasis" is attached to the hotel. The dining room is available both to hotel visitors and other vacationers of the Lazarevskoye village.

The administration of the Oasis mini-hotel (Lazarevskoye) is located on the ground floor of the building. At first glance, the hotel creates a good impression. A recent European-style repair is visible. Everything is clean and civil. In the corridor, on the ground floor, paintings decorate the interior, sofas stand, a large plasma is fixed on the wall.

Accommodation for vacationers

Hotel "Oasis" (Lazarevskoye) offers tourists the services of standard and luxury rooms.

The furnishings in the rooms are standard. The interior of the rooms is in pastel colors. Double rooms are all one-room. Some have a double bed, others with two single beds. A standard set of furniture, everything you need and nothing more. Each room has a TV and air conditioning. Conveniences in all rooms: in some - a shower with a curtain and a toilet, in others - a bath. The rooms located on the second, third and fourth floors have a small balcony.





The deluxe rooms at the Oasis Hotel (Lazarevskoye) are all two-room, with a maximum occupancy of four people. One room is a bedroom with a double bed, the second is a living room with upholstered furniture. The decor in the room is rich, stylish. Furniture, household appliances and plumbing of high class. Some rooms have a chic corner bathtub with a jacuzzi, while others have a shower under the glass.

Room prices

Oasis is a 2 * hotel (Lazarevskoye), which does not have the highest class, therefore the prices are quite reasonable. So, for example, a standard double room located on the ground floor in June 2016 cost 2530 rubles, in July it was 2645 rubles, in August - 2900 rubles, and in September - again 2530 rubles.

A double room in the Oasis Hotel (Lazarevskoye) on the 2nd, 3rd and 4th floors with a balcony, a little higher class, the so-called standard improved, in June 2016 it cost 2645 rubles, and in August - 3300 rubles.

Two-room suites in June and September 2016 cost 3600 rubles, in July and August - 4000 rubles.

Prices for 2017 have not yet been formed.
