Unrivaled Egypt. Hurghada, Sharm El Sheikh and Taba Resorts

Egypt is one of several countries where the mysteries of ancient civilization are combined with a wonderful beach holiday. And the climate of this country allows you to relax all year round, the swimming season here is open even in winter. Therefore, the best resorts in Egypt attract tourists from all over the world. And the coast of this country is very diverse. There are colorful coral beaches with clear water and an unusual underwater world, and sandy beaches with a gentle entry into the sea. And one who has ever visited this country returns here again, and not alone, but with friends and relatives.





And this diversity baffles some tourists who come to Egypt. Resorts here are scattered all over the coast, and choosing them is not easy. Take Hurghada at least. The city is ideal for families with children. It is sunny all year round, there are endless sandy beaches and opportunities for diving and surfing. And for the residence of tourists on the coast built over 200 modern hotels. Among them are such well-known hotel chains as Marriott, Hilton, Intercontinental, Sheraton and many others. In addition, new hotel complexes and hotels are constantly being built here and old ones are being improved.

And the staff of these hotels can be explained in many European languages. Therefore, Russian-speaking tourists who do not speak foreign languages ‚Äã‚Äãfeel quite comfortable here. Also, all resorts in Egypt, including Hurghada, take care of the normal nutrition of tourists. It is organized here in the form of a buffet, and the range of products is plentiful and varied. In addition, you can have a snack here in some restaurant. They are located in the hotels themselves, on the beaches and in the old part of Hurghada. Even tourists are waiting for not only beaches and food, but also exciting excursions. These are tours to Cairo and Luxor, trips to the desert in jeeps and sea voyages to the coral islands.





But diving enthusiasts do not have to drag scuba gear with them to Egypt. Resorts are provided with everything necessary for this entertainment. Yes, and you can see the local underwater world without diving, but through the windows of the Sinbad submarine. It lowers tourists to a 20-meter depth, where marine inhabitants of a wide variety of shapes and colors are found. Also, lovers of nightlife will not be bored here. They are waiting for discos, cafes, restaurants and casinos. It is especially advised to visit the palace, called the "Thousand and One Nights," which in the evenings hosts performances on the theme of life in this country in the era of the pharaohs.

And on the coast of the Sinai Peninsula is located the ‚ÄúBay of Sheikhs‚Äù, or Sharm El Sheikh - a city that Egypt is proud of. Resorts here are famous all over the world. Charter planes fly here all year round, bringing tourists from Europe and, more recently, from Russia. In hotels of Sharm el-Sheikh, senior officials also like to relax. Such as the Prime Minister of Israel, the Arab sheikhs and the President of Egypt. It also hosts international business meetings attended by politicians such as Yeltsin, Clinton, Chirac and Mitterrand. Diving enthusiasts also appreciate this resort. Indeed, it is here that the Ras Muhammad National Park is located, which has no equal in the Northern Hemisphere in terms of the diversity and number of corals, marine fauna and flora.





There are also chic beaches, as well as lagoons, mountains and canyons in the small town of Taba. It is located in the place where Israel and Egypt border. Resorts here are presented by luxury hotels with all the conditions for an excellent holiday. It offers a great view of the colorful granite mountains that are bordered by sandy beaches. Here you can taste the freshest sea fish and make a trip to the island of Pharaohs, where the crusader fortress called Salah El Dina, dated to the 12th century, is located. Taba also has its own coral reef, which divers love.
