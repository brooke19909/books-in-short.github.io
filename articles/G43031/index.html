Infrastructure



The territory of Venus Beach Paphos 5 * (Paphos), although small, but well-maintained and always clean. There is where to walk, sit and enjoy the sea.

The infrastructure of this five-star hotel is fully consistent with the declared category. On the ground floor of the main building there is a hairdressing salon, telecom room, a small but well-equipped conference room, ATMs, a newsstand, shops, a concierge, a left-luggage office and a currency exchange office. Personal secretary and translator services are provided, there are also two meeting rooms.

You can order a taxi from the 24-hour reception desk on the second floor of the reception desk, arrange a transfer to the airport, and also use a photocopier and fax machine. Here you can rent a vehicle, including a bicycle.

The Venus Beach Hotel 5 * (Paphos) hotel also provides the opportunity to organize banquets and celebrations on site. The range of services includes not only the banquet table and its maintenance, but also entertainment events for guests, including the invitation of musicians.



There is a tour desk on the ground floor. If you wish, you can take the opportunity of sightseeing accompanied by a Russian-speaking guide. There is a large plasma TV in the lobby of the hotel. Those awaiting check-in can have fun waiting for room keys.

At the registration desk you can rent a depository safe for especially valuable items.

The hotel has a convenient parking lot. Booking a parking spot in advance is not necessary.

Check in

The official check-in time at the hotel is two in the afternoon. However, the registration desk is open around the clock, so the check-in will take place promptly and without unnecessary delays.



Departure time from Venus Beach Paphos is noon. Anyone who is forced to wait for the transfer can leave their belongings in the storage room and continue to relax until the arrival of the shuttle, which takes them to the airport.

Upon arrival, according to the rules in force in any hotel, you need to leave a deposit: in cash or by credit card. For check-in, an identity document is required.

Housing stock

Venus Beach Paphos 5 * (Paphos) is a six-story building, which is equipped with two hundred sixteen rooms for the simultaneous residence of five hundred and fifty people. Vacationers will be able to get to their floors with modern elevators.

The rooms have the following categories: Double, which has one double bed, Twin Superior - with two separate beds, Single, Triple Suites and Quard Family, designed to accommodate four people at once.

The standard rooms are eighteen square meters. Superior apartments consist of two rooms separated by a door.

The rooms have balconies overlooking the pool, sea or patio. They have plastic chairs and small coffee tables.



The air-conditioned rooms of the hotel have a paid minibar and a safe, a TV showing three Russian channels, including NTV and Pervy. There is also a work and coffee tables, armchairs, a coffee set, a refrigerator and a VCR are also offered (the latter is in Superior and Suites).

For those who like to relax during the day, blackout curtains are provided on the windows.

The room temperature is manually controlled. On arrival, all rooms have fresh flowers, and higher-class rooms have a vase of fruit. Upon arrival, the newlyweds are also offered a bottle of wine.

The property has been recently renovated. The colors in the rooms are selected in such a way as to match the atmosphere of relaxation. On the walls are paintings painted in watercolor. There is a large mirror, laminate flooring.

The hotel on the ground floor has rooms for disabled guests. For wheelchair transportation all conditions are provided in the territory, including the restaurant.

Bathrooms

Bathrooms in Venus Beach Paphos combined. They include a shower and bath, Superior or Suites are also equipped with bidets, bathrobes and slippers.

A hair dryer and all necessary personal care products are free of charge. They are updated daily. New sanitary equipment is installed in the bathrooms, non-slip ceramic tiles on the floor.
