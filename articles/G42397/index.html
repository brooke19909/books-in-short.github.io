Kabardinka: attractions for every taste





Kabardinka is an amazing small resort located just a dozen kilometers from Gelendzhik. Many lovers of a relaxing summer vacation prefer this place to all other villages of the Krasnodar Territory. Kabardinka is the driest place on the coast. Here, from May to November, the season lasts. Covered on one side by Cape Doob, and on the other by the Markotkh Range, Kabardinka hardly knows rainy and inclement weather. Winds are also rare. The village also attracts with its healing climate: juniper forests enrich the air with substances useful for respiration and heart. All this beauty and ecological grace are combined with a well-developed infrastructure. Sights of Kabardinka in the Krasnodar Territory are known far beyond the village. And there are a lot of interesting places. Vacationers from all over the coast come to the village: a map of Kabardinka with sights is sold from Sochi to Novorossiysk. The main thing is to have time to see everything.

Kabardinka: attractions and interesting places

The most unusual on the entire coast is the house, which is located at the intersection of Peace and Revolution Streets. This is a small but very comfortable room suitable for housing. Everything is as usual in it: a small kitchen, a bedroom, a guest room. However, this house ... is upside down. Chairs and tables, sofas and kitchen utensils, even the bathroom and shower, are securely attached to the ceiling. Only the staircase leading to the house has the usual layout. Such inverted houses are only in Tennessee, Germany and Kaliningrad. Kabardinka, the sights of which are not limited to this house, now stands in a row with them. No less interesting for tourists is the Alley of Civilizations, located in the old park. Bypassing only half a hectare, you can make a trip around the world. Here is the Pyramid of Cheops, and ancient Greek temples, and palaces of the Renaissance, Renaissance. All of them are located among the exotic vegetation for Kabardinka. The most attractive architectural structure, many consider the sculpture "The Seven Deadly Sins", standing at the pond and made according to all the canons of medieval art.





Kabardinka: attractions and history

Not far from the village, on Cape Doob, a memorial dedicated to the memory of those killed on the ship "Nakhimov" is installed. The lives of the dead here are represented in the form of broken pipes, and the wave that engulfed the ship, in the form of a broken circle of concrete. At the highest point of the memorial, a clock is installed, raised from the sunken Nakhimov. Their arrows forever froze at position 23.20, the time when the tragedy occurred. There is also a monument to the Heroes of War. At one time, the battery of Captain Zubkov did not let the fascist fleet into Tsemess Bay. Flowers are constantly carried to these memorials, here lovers swear fidelity.

Its attractions are so diverse that a two-week vacation may not be enough to visit each.
