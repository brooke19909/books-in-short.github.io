Acer laptops: owner reviews, models and specifications

In the segment of mobile computers, there are certain market leaders who specialize in entry-level models and mid-range devices. One such company is Acer. Acer laptops, reviews of which we will analyze a little later, are compact, powerful, use high-quality materials and have a beautiful design. These devices are a worthy alternative to Apple products. Yes, and laptops from the "Acer" are unmatched cheaper. So, let's move on to the specifics. But first, some general information about the manufacturer.

About Acer

The company was founded in China in 1976. Initially, the manufacturer was engaged in the production of personal computers. It was this company in 1985 that created the world's first 32-bit computer. The notorious IBM was in second place. Over its history, Acer has absorbed many electronics manufacturers. By the way, BenQ products (so well known to Russian users) are also the brainchild of Acer. In addition to laptops and PCs, the company also produces monitors, communicators, computer peripherals, keyboards, drives, projectors and other necessary goods. However, Acer laptops work best. User reviews make it clear that the laptops of the company are reliable, modern and work perfectly in any conditions.

Over its long life, the company "Acer" has managed to give the world a huge number of high-quality devices. Currently, the manufacturer may well compete with giants such as Samsung and Apple. Only their industries are fundamentally different. Acer has computers and everything related to them. But the two giants mentioned above focus on mobile devices. However, we move on to user reviews of various laptop models. Acer laptops, reviews of which we will analyze later, are an example of a modern mobile computer. And now you will understand why.

Positive feedback on the Acer Aspire SP515

This laptop is positioned by the company as a device for productive work. Therefore, there is neither a powerful video adapter, nor a new-fangled solid-state drive. But there is a high-quality screen, a comfortable keyboard and no less convenient touchpad. According to user reviews, this device seems ideal for those who work a lot at the computer. The IPS-matrix of the screen will not let your eyes get tired, and your fingers will not hurt from a perfectly made keyboard. In general, this is exactly what distinguishes Acer Aspire laptops. Positive reviews are well deserved. And their overwhelming majority. Especially the owners note the powerful and productive Intel Core i5 processor. An important role here is played by 8 gigabytes of RAM, working in dual-channel mode. Professional programmers note a rather high speed of the laptop in the process of compiling something.

Negative reviews for the Acer Aspire SP515

But the negative reviews about this model come from those who could not play World of Tanks on this laptop. Indeed, the power of the integrated Intel UHD Graphics 620 video adapter is clearly not enough for these purposes. But this is a laptop for work. Do not forget about it. For games, it is better to consider the gaming line of the same manufacturer. Acer laptops, reviews of which we are now reviewing, have every chance of becoming the first devices after Apple.

Positive feedback on the Acer Extensa 2540

This laptop leaves a double impression: on the one hand, it has the latest generation Intel processor and a solid-state drive on board, but on the other, a rather budget screen based on the TN matrix. How to explain such a move on the part of the company is not clear. But let's see what users say about a device like the Acer Extensa laptop. Reviews say that the vast majority of owners of this car are quite happy with the purchase. Users claim that the laptop works quickly and seamlessly (which is not surprising, with such and such characteristics). Also, users note the high quality of the materials used in the assembly. A lot of praise is heard in the address of the company and for the modern ultra-thin case of the device. In general, users are happy with this laptop. It resembles Apple products. The only difference is that it costs two times cheaper.

Positive feedback on the Acer Aspire ES1

Another workhorse is the Acer Aspire ES1 laptop. Reviews about him are full of positive epithets. But there are a lot of those who categorically did not like the product. Nevertheless, this is a very modern laptop with a powerful processor, a decent amount of RAM (8 gigabytes) and a Full HD-screen. Users note that working with this device is a pleasure. It affects the ergonomic keyboard and Full HD-screen with IPS-matrix. The increased battery capacity is another reason for laudatory od. With such a battery, you can work from almost anywhere, without really thinking about recharging. This is exactly what the owners of a wonderful device say. Negative reviews about the Acer Aspire ES1

Where without negative reviews? The users did not like the fact that the device comes with only one (and very weak) built-in video card - Intel HD Graphics 505. It will only work for the "Merry Farm". However, this is a laptop for work. But the fact that there is only one slot for RAM (and that one is busy) is really a minus. Based on the name, it can be assumed that this laptop is for those who like to travel. What is unusual about the Acer Travelmate laptop? Reviews about him clearly make this clear.
