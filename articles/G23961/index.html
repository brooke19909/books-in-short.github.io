About Vodafone tariffs in detail

When switching to Vodafone with MTS, it is necessary to study all tariffs in detail in order to choose the most suitable for yourself. You must also know that in addition to RED S, M and L, there is also a RED XS tariff. You need to learn to understand these packages. It is important to understand how to switch from MTS to Vodafon.

Regardless of which of these 4 tariffs the subscriber chooses, calls within Ukraine to Vodafon numbers will be unlimited free. The ‚ÄúEther‚Äù package, which opens the Vodafone TV service, is available only for RED L.



Social networks allowed in Ukraine, and WhatsApp and Viber messengers are unlimited in all packages except RED XS. In RED XS, social networks and instant messengers are not separated from the other Internet at all, which is given in this case for a month of 1 GB. Other packages will be able to please subscribers with the Internet in the amount of 4, 8 and 12 GB, respectively, according to an increase in the monthly fee. Internet operator Vodafon supports both 3G and 2G. Vodafon will give subscribers a free 2 GB Internet, regardless of the connected tariff, and if the limit is reached, then for each subsequent 500 MB you will have to pay 500 hryvnias.

It is possible to call numbers of other Ukrainian operators for free from any tariff, but the number of minutes per month is limited. For RED XS, it takes 60 minutes, for RED S - 75, for RED M - 105, and for RED L - 300 minutes. If the limit is exceeded, the cost of 1 minute of conversation with a subscriber of another operator will be 0.25 hryvnia.

There are no free calls abroad on the RED XS tariff, and therefore the cost of such a call will be 3 hryvnia per minute. On other tariffs, calls abroad can be made free of charge - 25, 35 and 75 minutes in ascending order (S, M and L). If free minutes are spent, the cost of 1 minute of conversation at these three tariffs will be 0.50 hryvnia.





SMS and MMS messages can be sent for free at S, M and L. tariffs. The limit on the number of sent messages is 25, 70 and 150, respectively. And on XS, 50 messages are given per month if you pay for this service. The cost here is 1.50 hryvnia per day. If you exceed the limit of SMS and MMS, you will need to pay an extra 0.50 hryvnia for each message.

With all this, the new Vodafon connection provides its users with small gifts in the form of free Internet, as a promotion for new RED XS and RED S subscribers.
