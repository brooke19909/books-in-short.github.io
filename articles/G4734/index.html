How to sew a sheet on an elastic band? Convenience and comfort do it yourself

The main component of bedding, no doubt, is the sheet. Special demands are made on her, because, as they say, she lies closest to the body.

But not in every set there can be a sheet with an elastic band, which has become very popular not so long ago. This is mainly due to the fact that nowadays, beds with high spring mattresses are increasingly used, which is almost impossible to fill with a regular sheet, since the latter is quite narrow and constantly slides during sleep. Advantages of an elastic sheet



Unlike their usual relatives, such sheets do not stray and do not slide off the mattress, securely fixing on it. They do not need to be corrected. Agree, it is very convenient! In addition, they look beautiful and neat on the bed, giving it an aesthetic look.

An elastic sheet is perfect for those who sleep very restlessly, constantly knocking it into a lump. In addition, with its help you can hide the mattress. Such sheets will be comfortable on cots when a waterproof diaper is placed on the mattress . Therefore, many women will be interested in our tips on how to sew a sheet on an elastic band.

Terry sheet - comfortable luxury

Among the many types of sheets, people prefer soft terry rather than traditional linen or calico. A terry sheet has a number of undeniable advantages: its fabric absorbs moisture better than others, so such textiles are simply irreplaceable in the summer. And in winter, rest on it will be extremely comfortable, since this material perfectly retains heat. Such products look great and last a long time.





Terry sheet with an elastic band has special functionality. Due to its elasticity, it is often used as a mattress cover. The sheet will hold on to it very tightly, so you do not have to straighten the fabric from the folds every morning.

How to sew a sheet on an elastic band quickly and easily

Everything is pretty simple here. You can cope with the task without even having sewing skills.

1. First you need to pick up the fabric. It is better to use cotton, chintz, calico or cambric. The size of the fabric should fully correspond to the size of the mattress, taking into account all parameters: length, width and height. It is important to leave allowances for the hem for gum from 5 to 7 cm and 2 cm for shrinkage of the fabric. Therefore, to the width and length of the mattress you can safely add its height and 7-12 cm additionally.

2. It is very important to draw the pattern correctly. To do this, draw a rectangle of the mattress in size with all the allowances. The pattern should be in the form of a rectangle with squares in the corners.



3. After that, you need to sweep the depth of the mattress from all four sides. The result is a kind of cover. Be sure to do a fitting and if all goes well, then the extra squares from the four corners can be cut off. In order not to see the cut, the seam must be bent twice and gently sweep.

4. Now we attach the elastic. There are two ways: around the perimeter of the sheet or only at the corners. Consider both options. If you select the first, then you need to bend from about 2 to 4 cm and sew on a typewriter. Next, insert the rubber band into the hole and pull it to the desired size.

5. The second option will be mastered by more experienced craftswomen. Elastic bands will need 30 cm at an angle of 15 on each side. So, you need to bend the edges of the corner with an iron and sweep, then place the elastic in the hem, stretching it. The corner with the inserted rubber is simply hedged by the main seam.

So the sheet is ready. Now you will know how to sew a sheet on an elastic band and you can always do it yourself at any time.
