A bit about the city

Before you specify the phone code of Yaroslavl, let's talk a little about this unique city. If you do not go down in history, then Yaroslavl in 2010 celebrated its 1000th anniversary. This is the third largest city in the Central Federal District of the Russian Federation. The distance from Moscow to Yaroslavl is 280 km.

The city was founded by Yaroslav the Wise, in whose honor it got its name. It was first mentioned in 1071. The first monasteries of Yaroslavl already existed in the XII century. During the period of unrest, the city had a hard time, it was crushed and divided. As well as in Soviet times, when many ancient monuments of architecture were destroyed. The Great Patriotic War took almost 500 thousand inhabitants to the front. More than 200 thousand never returned to their hometown.

The Soviet era has passed. And now Yaroslavl is a very beautiful city, claiming to be the "heart of the Golden Ring of Russia". Many buildings, monuments of architecture, have been preserved and restored. There are churches and monasteries. On the territory of the city there are 2 convents: Kazan and Tolgsky. There are also two men, both of them are in the center of the city. This is the Kirillo-Afanasievsky Monastery and the Transfiguration of the Savior.
