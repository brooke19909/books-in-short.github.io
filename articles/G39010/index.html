Hotel Pacesetter 3 * (Rimini, Italy): photos, reviews

Hotel location

The six-story hotel Pacesetter 3 * (Rimini) is located in the Viserba district. This is a bit far from the center - to the main attractions of the city, as well as to the station from it about the same distance. It takes about a quarter of an hour to get there by bus. But he stands right on the beach by the Adriatic Sea, so that at night the sound of the waves calms you, and in the morning you wake up under it. Near the hotel there are two large grocery supermarkets - Konad and Coop. Right near the hotel entrance there is a bus stop number four (just the one that goes to the center and the train station), and a Tabak stall where, among other things, they sell tickets for public transport. The historic center can also be reached on foot, especially since the walk in Rimini itself is always very pleasant.



Guest rooms

As befits a typical Italian three-star hotel, Pacesetter 3 * (Rimini) is not too big, but rather homely. There are only forty rooms. They are designed for one, two or three people. There are family rooms with bunk beds for children. The rooms are almost all the same type. The room has a bathroom with shower and toilet, satellite TV, free safe. The temperature in the room allows you to set the air conditioning. In all areas of the hotel catches the Internet. But if the location of your room is such that the signal is weak, you can take the password from the cafe opposite and use their Wi-Fi. Many rooms have small balconies and sea views.

Nutrition

Hotel Pacesetter 3 * (Rimini) has its own restaurant on the second floor. The lobby has a bar with a cozy veranda in the shade. Hotel guests usually take either a breakfast room or half board, because in the afternoon they have lunch on the beach or on excursions. Breakfast at the hotel is not too chic - but it is so customary in Italy. These are mainly croissants, bread, crackers, jam or jam, cheese and sausage slices, coffee, milk and juices. But many called the dinners simply magnificent. There are always two hot dishes to choose from (one of which is pasta), delicious desserts. The second is given alternately meat, fish and chicken. The portions are huge and satisfying. Products are bought immediately before cooking, everything is exceptionally fresh. And for dinner they still put a bottle of water and a glass of dry wine. Apples, tangerines, grapes, watermelons are portioned on the table from fruits. If you go on an excursion very early in the morning, then you will be offered to take breakfast with you. Beach and sea vacation

We already mentioned that the seashore is literally ten meters from the hotel, except maybe across the road. Hotel Pacesetter 3 * (Rimini) has its own beach at number twenty-one. It is divided by breakwaters with the coast of other hotels. The sand is very fine, like flour. The beach is well equipped, there are many different kinds of water sports. But sunbeds and umbrellas are very expensive - eighteen euros for a couple of pieces per day. They are not allowed to sunbathe in the sand on their own towels, but on the stones - as much as you like! The beach is well suited for families with small children. There are also many cafes, restaurants and bars right by the water.
