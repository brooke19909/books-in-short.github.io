LCD "City of Embankments" in Khimki: review, description and reviews

The housing problem is especially acute for modern Muscovites: some get families, others come from different regions of the country and also need their own housing. To rent an apartment in the capital is quite expensive, which is why, sooner or later, we begin to think about its acquisition. If we talk about real estate within the city, that is, within the MKAD, its value is fabulous. That is why at present, new buildings near Moscow are very popular , and not separate high-rise buildings, but entire residential complexes with their own infrastructure and the most comfortable living conditions. LCD "City of Embankments" - an author's project being built in Khimki. The proximity to the capital has largely determined its popularity. But our task is to evaluate the project from all sides within the framework of this material.





about the project

The developer based the project on the concept of building a "city in a city", which is so popular at present. The quarters of the new residential area are formed by five- and six-story houses. Between them, recreation areas, children's and playgrounds, sidewalks, walking and bicycle paths are organized. Closed courtyards provide maximum comfort and safety to all residents: there is no through traffic, and the noise from parked cars is minimal. Moreover, underground parking is provided for car owners. And the territory of the complex is fenced around the perimeter and is under round-the-clock protection with a video surveillance system.

The ecology of the LCD "City of Embankments" is generally favorable. Residents of the area have long been fond of parks and squares. Moreover, the largest shopping and entertainment centers are located nearby.
