The recreation center "Dewdrop", Astrakhan. Description, reviews.

Surely there are many fans of a quiet and comfortable holiday in the shade of thick trees on the picturesque riverbank. And if you want to add comfortable and modern living conditions to the clean air, the Rosinka recreation center in Astrakhan invites you.

Description

Rosinka is located on the banks of the Volga, 15 kilometers from Astrakhan, not far from the village of Rastopulovka. Choosing this place for your vacation, you can enjoy not only the impressive expanses of the river, but also the views of the endless Astrakhan steppes. Here you are guaranteed an unforgettable vacation and exciting fishing.

For lovers of an active lifestyle on the territory of the recreation center "Rosinka" (Astrakhan) sports grounds are equipped. There is also a relaxation area for tourists, where you can plunge into the cool pool in the summer heat and soak up the sun in a comfortable sun lounger.

Near the pool is a bar. It also has its own restaurant.

Guests of the complex can leave their vehicles in a guarded private parking lot. The nearest airport is Narimanovo, 22 km from the hotel.

Check-in time at the recreation center is 14.00, check-out time is 12.00. The address of the Rosinka base: Astrakhan, Volga Region, 1.5 kilometers southwest of Rastopulovka.

Guest Accommodation

The recreation center "Rosinka" in Astrakhan) offers vacationers several accommodation options.

  • The house has two bedrooms (each has a large double bed) and a living room with two folding sofas. It can accommodate six people.
  • Three bedroom house. Each room has a large double bed. It also provides accommodation for six people.
  • Three bedroom house. Each room has two single beds. Six people can be accommodated at the same time.
  • House with four bedrooms. Each has two single beds. Designed to accommodate eight people.
  • The standard bungalow has two bedrooms, each with three single beds.
  • Economy Quadruple Room with Four Single Beds.
  • Economy double room with two single beds.

Some houses and rooms have a private terrace or balcony with a wonderful view of the river. Some have an equipped kitchen. Each room has a refrigerator, TV, air conditioning. Mosquito nets are installed on the windows.

Services

The services of the recreation center "Rosinka" in Rastopulovka (Astrakhan):

  • Volleyball Court. For fans of sports games tables for table tennis are installed, badminton equipment is provided.
  • There is a cozy billiard room. The service is provided for a fee.
  • The tourist complex has a swimming pool with sun loungers, parasols and a bar.
  • You can visit the sauna. Paid extra.
  • On the first line is the beach.
  • You can eat in the cozy restaurant.
  • There is a playground for children on the street, and young tourists can play board games or puzzles together with friends indoors.
  • At the camp site located in the Volga region, fishing is organized for you.
  • Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas.
  • Laundry and housekeeping services are charged separately.
  • A gift shop is located on site.
  • Transfer from and to the airport is possible.
  • Parking space is provided free of charge.
  • For events there is a banquet hall.
  • Room service is also available.
  • In the fresh air for guests there is a gazebo for a picnic with an outdoor fireplace and barbecue.
  • For evening walks there is a beautiful shady garden.
