Hotel "Starfish" (Kabardinka): reviews and photos

Rest is perhaps the only way to escape from the hustle and bumpiness of everyday life, to gain strength and patience for further discoveries and achievements. For each of us, relaxation is something special, we decide what it will be, but to deny the love of people for the sea is simply pointless. Numerous studies and observations of scientists have shown that the sea really calms, relaxes, helps to escape and at least temporarily forget about everything. And, of course, the southern sea resorts allow you to get a beautiful even tan, temper your body, and due to the developed infrastructure, have a great time and get unforgettable emotions.

Modern tourists increasingly prefer overseas resorts, and all because they offer excellent conditions for organizing an unforgettable vacation and first-class service. But do not forget that in Russia there are also beautiful and noteworthy vacationers places. Resorts of the Krasnodar Territory, popular in the Soviet years, are now forgotten by many. Yes, of course, they can not compete with the fashionable European resorts, but in fact rest here is much cheaper. If your family has small children, you are not ready for long and tiring flights, you decided to go to the sea by personal car - the Russian sea resorts will be an excellent option.





How do you like Kabardinka? This place is preferred by families with young children, and all thanks in large part to the chicly equipped beaches. Even if you plan to spend the whole day on the coast or on excursions, you still need to decide on a hotel or hotel. So, we propose to pay attention to the hotel "Starfish" (Kabardinka). How suitable is this place for relaxation? What do the reviews of real vacationers say about him?

Hotel Description

So, the hotel "Sea Star" (Kabardinka) is a small private hotel, represented by several buildings. If you are looking for a place in the center of the resort village, in close proximity to the coast and all infrastructure, this option is definitely suitable for you. The hotel does not have any category, there are not so many reviews about it, but still enough to evaluate it from all sides.





Territory

Most hotels in the Krasnodar Territory cannot boast of spacious and equipped adjacent territory. The small private hotel "Sea Star" (Kabardinka) was no exception . Reviews of tourists emphasize that after Turkey, Tunisia and Egypt, getting used to Russian resorts is quite difficult. Nevertheless, the owners of the hotel have beautifully designed even a small courtyard area: there is a small well-kept garden and even an artificial pond with waterfalls - a favorite place for hotel guests. In the evenings, vacationers gather at the grill, prepare barbecue and share their impressions.

Rooms

Vacationers who like Kabardinka, the guest house "Sea Star" can offer good living conditions. Each room, regardless of its category, has air conditioning and satellite TV. Tourists are offered a choice of several categories of rooms, each of which differs from the previous degree of comfort. In general, the decor and interior are more than modest: the furniture is simple, some tourists say that it looks like furniture from a hospital, the team of professionals certainly did not work on the interior.





The simplest option that the Sea Star Hotel (Kabardinka) can offer is a standard room with a bathroom, designed for two or three people. The walls are painted in pastel colors and the floor is tiled. Nutrition

Going on vacation, hardly anyone wants to spend time cooking daily, standing at the stove, so many tourists will surely like the opportunity to rent a room with three meals a day. If you want to cook something special, or you took small children with a special meal to take with you, you can always rent a room with a kitchen and use household appliances, a refrigerator and utensils. Moreover, in walking distance from the hotel there are enough bars, cafes, restaurants, hot corn, ice cream, fresh fruits and soft drinks are sold on the beach.





If we talk about food in the restaurant's cafe, it is quite simple, devoid of frills, but no one will definitely remain hungry.

Service

If you are looking for a good and affordable resort that is rapidly developing and improving, you should like the resort village of Kabardinka. "Starfish" (photos and reviews of tourists will become an information base for you) many people like. Vacationers who managed to travel around the country and the world, really note a good hotel service. The cordiality and hospitality of the staff is the first thing that newly arrived guests feel. You must admit that after a tiring road, whether it is a flight by plane or a trip by car, you really want to get the keys to your room as soon as possible and relax. So, realizing this, the hotel administration often goes towards the guests and populates them in a room ahead of schedule.

All vacationers can count on 24-hour room service. Cleaning is carried out daily, during the absence of vacationers. Tourists notice being in the room of service personnel only with made up beds, clean plumbing, updated towels in the bathroom. In general, tourists note that the rooms are really cleaned very well, there is simply nothing to complain about.





On the territory of the hotel, all vacationers will be able to use wireless high-speed Internet. The signal quality can hardly be called excellent, but in order to exchange messages with relatives and friends, reading the news is enough.

Beach and sea

If you are looking for a quiet, calm, at the same time beautiful place on the sea, you will surely like Kabardinka. Hotel "Starfish" (reviews of tourists, a detailed description - all this speaks of its proximity to the sea) will not leave anyone indifferent. Indeed, the coast is only 5-7 minutes walk. Vacationers will find a wide pebble beach with developed infrastructure. It has everything that is needed for a beach holiday: sunbeds, umbrellas, rental of sports equipment and for water sports.

To summarize

In general, the Hotel "Sea Star" (Kabardinka) is well suited for those vacationers who are accustomed to a fairly modest vacation, do not count on luxury apartments and are ready to put up with some disadvantages. The hotel staff is friendly and polite, trying to make each guest's stay as comfortable as possible. This is really a budget option, which is worth a closer look.
