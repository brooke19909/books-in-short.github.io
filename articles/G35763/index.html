Tour operator "Windrose" in Rybinsk

Rybinsk is the largest city on the reservoir of the same name, otherwise called the Moscow Sea. This place annually attracts thousands of fishermen from all over the country, as well as lovers of hiking and outdoor recreation. However, residents of Rybinsk also sometimes go on vacation outside their city, and the Windrose travel agency helps them in this .





Company Services

The Windrose of Rybinsk is the official representative of a large Russian company, a national tourist operator, whose head office is located in Moscow. For almost 30 years of operation, this holding has combined more than a hundred travel agencies and operators.

In Rybinsk, this company has been operating for several years and has gained a good reputation. It offers customers any kind of tours, including last minute and seasonal, insurance, visa, transfer and much more.

Rosa Vetrov (Rybinsk) cooperates with such large tour operators as Pegas Touristik, Coral Travel, Biblio Globus, TUI, TEZ Tour and others.

Additional agency services





The Windrose not only sells tours, but also prepares for travel at all stages. So, the company has a visa center where you can order a visa to any country in the world. The cost of preparation without consular fees and filing entries is 2000 rubles. A full package costs from 7.5 (Schengen zone) to 13.5 (United States) thousand rubles.

The Wind Rose of Rybinsk also helps in obtaining a foreign passport. The company will not do this for the client, but on the website you can find detailed information on how and where the paperwork is performed, and you can also download the questionnaire. For any questions, you can contact the company office, they will certainly help you.
