Do-it-yourself denim bags

Not people who don‚Äôt wear jeans. Each one has a pair of old jeans in the closet. If you have long wanted to buy a comfortable bag for relaxation or everyday wear, feel free to get old pants and other unnecessary things from the mezzanine.

So you breathe new life and make room in the closet. Make a trendy and easy-to-wear denim bag. It will be an ideal option for both workdays and a friendly picnic.

Denim bags have a rather interesting history. They were first made in the Latin American quarter. The fact is that the residents did not have enough money, especially for clothes. Old jeans found their application in creating stunning handmade accessories. This thing is very versatile. With her, you can go to work every day, and at the same time, it is simply indispensable during a beach holiday, since you can put absolutely everything that you may need to lay on its bottom. Jeans handbags are also very easy to clean from dirt.

There are things and accessories that are sewn simply and without fancies, and among them, of course, are jeans bags. Patterns of such products consist of two or three elements, so you can make such a thing in the shortest possible time. Consider several ways to create such bags.



"Shorts"

Take your old jeans. Cut off their legs. Sew holes in the armholes (it is better to stitch them on a sewing machine). Then attach a fastener to your jeans belt that you can buy at a sewing store. From the leg make straps and sew to the zone of the belt. By the way, an excellent pocket for any little things will come out of a fly. Decorate the bag as you like.

Torba

Denim bags of this model are very roomy and comfortable. Cut off both legs. So, you again got three details. Take one of the legs and turn inside out. From the second cut a circle with the same diameter as the hole of the armhole. Sew a circle to the inverted trouser leg from the wrong side, then turn it back to the front side. Now cut a strap or two, sew it with one end to the bottom, and the other - on a knot, and tie the top of the bag in a soldier‚Äôs bag. If you want to create a horizontal bag, then sew two circles, and sew the whole length of the zipper into the base leg and attach the straps on the sides. By the way, the remaining shorts can also be used. Cut pockets from them and sew them to the bag.

If your product is worn, holes appear in it, patch them with interesting patches, which will add unusualness and originality to your bag. Denim bags are washed in the same way as regular jeans. You can send this accessory to the washing machine or soak it in hot water.



It is desirable to equip denim bags with various decorations and additions during their manufacturing. So, you can sew all the stripes, and they will hold more securely. If you still have old parts from bags and clothes, think about how to best apply them to your bag. Do not be afraid to experiment. Acrylic paint is suitable for denim if you want to paint your masterpiece. And if you like fashion trends, then you can create copies of branded bags by simply finding the right patterns.
