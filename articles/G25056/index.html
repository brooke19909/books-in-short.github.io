Kostomuksha hotels: description and reviews

Kostomuksha is a city in Karelia, 35 km from the border with Finland. It was once built to service a mining and processing plant.

The compact settlement is located on the shores of Lake Kontokki. Given the 13 km distance from the industrial zone, a large number of green spaces and proximity to the ancient Karelian villages, the city is quite attractive for tourists. Kostomuksha hotels will provide you with a warm welcome and quality service.

Hotel Kotiranta

If you want to relax in a warm homely atmosphere, pay attention to the guest house "Kotiranta", which means "hometown" in Karelian. The institution is located at 16 Oktyabrskaya Street. This is a park area in the city.

A three-bedroom house is available for guests, which can accommodate up to 10 guests. Cost - from 8400 rubles per day. The walls of the house are made of natural Karelian pine, which makes your stay as eco-friendly as possible.

Guests can take advantage of the following benefits:
