Note to the craftswomen: how to trim jeans under shorts

Jeans - universal clothing. They can be carried as pants for many years, and then turned into shorts and used for more than one spring-summer season. Only for such a magical transformation you need to know a few technical subtleties.



Preparatory stage

How to trim jeans under shorts: in detail we will consider a little later. In the meantime, we will discuss the general points:

  1. First, pick up those pants that fit you well. Remember: if the jeans are wide in the hips, baggy on the buttocks, or if you are too tight in them, the look in the update will be far from chic.
  2. Secondly, the invoice. For the ignorant: in the question "how to cut jeans under shorts", the type of material plays an important role. 100% cotton or fabric with a small admixture of man-made fibers is ideal. And interspersing rubber and stretch material in this case will serve poorly, and it is better not to experiment with such trousers. But your usual x / b also lends itself well to processing.
  3. Now such a little trick about how to trim jeans under shorts. If the pants you have chosen are wide and baggy, wash them a couple of times in a typewriter and dry them in it. Surely they will sit down a little and then they will definitely become fit for you. Remember, already in a cropped form, a thing will definitely not decrease.

Style Selection

Guessing with style is no less important than figuring out how to trim jeans under shorts. You can make capri, bermuda, breeches. Each type of clothing has its own characteristics. Capri, if cut off at the ankles or in the middle of the calf, will look great with shoes on both low and high heels. In addition, this is a profitable option, if plainly you have not yet decided on the length. You can make them shorter at any time! And if the jeans are a little too big for you, the legs can be put on the cuffs: baggy will become part of the style and will not spoil the look, but, on the contrary, will emphasize the subtlety and grace of your waistline. But Bermuda suggest length to the knee and above, to the middle of the thigh. They are good in tight form, and in a slightly flared.

Consider this when thinking about how to trim jeans under shorts, and your new thing will be incredibly stylish. If you settled on the classic, the edges of the legs should be 3-6 cm from the knee. And a short option: their length is 5 cm from the outer edge. It is these mini ones that are good as tight-fitting ones. If they are wide on you, they will expose too much of the body.

Jeans are turning ...

If everything is thought out and taken into account by you, it is time to start practice. Technique of the process, how to cut jeans under shorts:

  • Put the trousers on a flat hard surface and mark the cut borders with chalk. Planning a fringe - lower the lines by 3-4 cm. You will hem the hem - by 1.5 cm. If you are going to turn it up for cuffs - make the length 10-15 cm longer.
  • The legs should not be cut off in a straight line, but at a certain angle to the outer edges, reminiscent of the Latin letter V. With mini shorts, be careful: a too deep neckline will make them very immodest.
  • Trim the edges of the cuts, if this is your plan, and pad or make cuffs. Even if you want a fringe, also cut a little, otherwise the fabric will crumble.
  • For fringe, pass the shorts through several wash cycles or rub the edges with a regular cooking grater.
