Indesit refrigerators, reviews and specifications



Indesit will last a long time, one advertisement says. Indeed, the equipment of this brand is in great demand among the population. Why are Indesit refrigerators so attractive? Customer reviews indicate that price, appearance, user-friendly design and reliability are primarily attractive.

Refrigerators of this brand produce different heights: 85 cm; 1.4 meters; 1.6 m; 1.8 m; two-meter. You can choose the right one for each apartment or room: an 85 cm model will stand flush with the countertop and will be able to replace the minibar, a low unit with a height of 1.4 cm and an upper freezer will fit perfectly into a small apartment, and the two-meter giant is suitable for a spacious kitchen. Such a refrigerator will include not only essential products, but also all kinds of supplies and pickles.

What else motivates buyers to buy Indesit refrigerators? Reviews of some owners suggest that the color scheme plays a significant role in the selection. Indesit refrigerators differ from each other not only in appearance, but also in design, in particular, the defrosting system. Models with classic auto-defrosting are on sale (condensate accumulates on the back wall and enters through the drain pipes into the bowl above the compressor from which it evaporates), and with the modern No Frost system - in this case, the evaporator is hidden behind the back wall, and cold air in the chamber is circulated by a fan. In addition, the unit with the No Frost system is more expensive than similar models with auto-defrosting.

What is the price of Indesit refrigerators? Customer reviews and opinions agree on one thing - they can afford most consumers. Low white models with auto-defrost are cheaper than tall, silver ones with a display and the No Frost system. By the way, refrigerators with a display appeared in household appliances stores not so long ago, but have already managed to win the sympathy of customers. They look very stylish and modern, the control panel allows you to set the temperature in each chamber separately.

What else can attract an Indesit refrigerator? Feedback from potential buyers suggests that they are attracted to convenient drawers in the freezer. They are solid, made of durable white plastic. Even if the products that are stored in the freezer leak, only the drawer gets dirty, and the entire chamber remains clean.

A three-year warranty is another feature that Indesit refrigerators have. Customer reviews say that this bonus often becomes a decisive moment when purchasing. Indeed, the fact that the service center will be able to repair the unit for free for 3 years or exchange for a new one calms many. Even a wealthy person will be sorry to throw out the refrigerator, which worked for 1 year, and then at least three in stock. In general, refrigerators of this brand represent the ideal ratio of price and quality. They can satisfy the needs of the most demanding customer.
