Hotel Restaurants

Hotel Sirene Belek Hotel 5 * gives its guests the opportunity to experience all the delights and subtleties of national Turkish cuisine. Guests with other culinary preferences will also have something to offer. So, the following restaurants are recommended for visiting:

  • Kybele is the main restaurant serving buffet meals. You will be amazed by the assortment and taste of the dishes. It is worth paying attention to the luxurious design of the dining room with oriental decorative elements. There is an outdoor terrace.
  • Limone is a restaurant specializing in Italian dishes. The restaurant owes its special atmosphere to its interior, decorated with elegant wooden elements, as well as sophisticated stained-glass windows.
  • Mermaid specializes in seafood dishes. The interior in white and blue tones with sand elements creates an atmosphere of the underwater kingdom.
  • Sini is a sumptuous restaurant serving Turkish cuisine. The spacious hall has a rounded shape. A central place is occupied by a hearth-roaster, where before the eyes of visitors cooked cakes, kebabs and other dishes.
  • Dragon is an Asian restaurant decorated in red, black and golden colors. Particular attention is paid to the preparation of many types of sushi.
  • Aspendos Food Court is a cozy open-air snack restaurant where you can eat during the day. This place is in close proximity to the stage. Also in the evenings you can watch fountain shows from here.
  • Cafe-confectionery is a real paradise for the sweet tooth. Here is a huge assortment of pastries, cakes and other products. Particular attention is paid to traditional Turkish sweets. Refined interior and pleasant music complete the impression.




Hotel Bars

  • The lobby bar will give its guests coziness and a relaxed atmosphere. Here you can hold business negotiations or chat with friends over a cup of coffee. In the evenings, there is live instrumental music for guests.
  • Hoopoe is a luxurious establishment with a trendy interior. Black and red tones in the interior give not only aesthetic pleasure, but also energize for vivid entertainment.
  • The bar on the pier gives guests a unique opportunity to enjoy their favorite drinks and light snacks while admiring the sea.
  • Aspendos is a bar located in the Aspendos Food Court Restaurant. In addition to a huge assortment of drinks, you will also enjoy bright concerts and shows that are regularly held here.
  • Aspendos Night Club is a great place for nightly entertainment and fiery dances. Every evening you can enjoy a spectacular show of fountains.
  • Oliympic Bar - a cozy restaurant located in the garden, next to the Olympic pool. Here you can have a tasty snack between the main meals.

Water activities

What could be better than cool, refreshing water in hot weather? Sirene Belek Golf Wellness Hotel provides the following water sports opportunities:

  • The Palace pool is not just a place for swimming, but also a decoration of the territory. The original form and decorative bridges, as well as greens create a special atmosphere. Incendiary entertainment is constantly held here.
  • If you were taken aback by the cold weather, you can take a swim in the indoor pool with six lanes. The ceiling is an imitation of the starry sky.
  • Those for whom swimming is not just entertainment, but sports, can visit the outdoor Olympic pool with 10 lanes. Its length is 50 meters. Located near the villas.
  • On the territory there are 2 children's pools, one of which is subordinate to the mini-club. They are equipped with all kinds of attractions and small fountains.
  • The hotel has its own water park, which is located in close proximity to the Olympic and children's pools. At the disposal of vacationers - 5 extreme slides. The depth of the pool is 120 cm.
  • The 300-meter-long beach is equipped with everything necessary for a complete sea holiday. You can also relax on a 1,200-square-foot pier. m, from which you can go down to the sea by stairs.

Additional Information

Be sure to check out the rules of accommodation at the Sirene Belek Hotel 5 * hotel (Belek). The following information can be considered the most significant:

  • registration of guests begins in the afternoon, and eviction is carried out in the period from 12:00 to 13:00;
  • There is no charge for children under 6 years when using existing beds;
  • Babies under 2 years old are provided with free cots;
  • pets are not allowed in the hotel;
  • credit cards accepted;
  • the pool and beach schedule may vary depending on weather conditions (the same rule applies to recreational activities);
