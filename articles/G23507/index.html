Visit the aquarium in Volgograd!

In the shopping and entertainment center "KomsoMALL" (Volgograd), the oceanarium is open! It has two large halls, and in them - more than two hundred animals and fish from all over the world. Volgograd is perhaps the only large city in Russia that does not have its own zoo. Therefore, there are a lot of people who want to visit the aquarium in Volgograd.



The staff includes an ichthyologist who observes animals, but there is currently no research work , the aquarium is a regular exhibition, which, like other exhibition centers and museums in Volgograd, is visited by residents and guests of the city.

The area occupied by the aquarium in Volgograd is almost a thousand square meters. There are many aquariums with various fish, and there are also fur seals, crocodiles, otters, monitor lizards and other exotic animals for which living conditions are created that are close to the present. It is planned to bring and penguins. Here live predatory piranhas, which, gathered in large flocks, can pose a threat to humans, and there are their herbivorous species.

In the aquarium there is an aquarium with African fish of a very beautiful color, they are much friendlier than piranhas. And the poisonous lionfish just bewitches with its beautiful and leisurely movements. But you just have to hesitate, and with one touch she kills her victim, because she has deadly poison at the ends of the needles. Her aquarium stands apart from others.





Great admiration for both children and adult visitors to the shark. They are still very young. One of them - black-feather - moves without stopping. They say she will die, stopping. The other, the Belopera, all the time lies at the bottom. They live in a huge water corridor, which is filled with more than a hundred tons of water, which will allow fish to grow large. Nearby are striped and predatory moray eels, followed by ramps.

Of great interest are African turtles, whose age for two is more than two hundred years. They were brought to the aquarium in Volgograd from Africa, saving them from poachers. Stupid and Armor - that's the name of the turtles - live in an aviary with sand. Everyone is given the opportunity to take a photo with them as a keepsake. Children like to watch turtles fed, and especially large numbers of visitors gather during their feeding.
