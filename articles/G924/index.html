Residential complex "Young" (Ryazan): why should you choose an apartment in this place?

The Semchino district is being actively built up and the Yuniy residential complex is the first stage of a large-scale expansion of the framework of Ryazan's residential areas. In developing the project, the Northern Company focused on the embodiment of all the needs and desires of modern men and women to create high-quality and affordable housing in a new format.

The conditions in which the LCD will be located, namely, an active transport system, developed infrastructure, moderate building density and the presence of green spaces, remoteness from city bustle and noise make the complex preferred when choosing comfortable housing near the city for families with children and those who love silence.

Wonderful fishing in the vicinity of the LCD

Surely you heard that fishing is not a hobby, but a way of life. For lovers of such an activity, the Yuniy residential complex in Ryazan (stage 4) is an ideal place located near the Bystritsa River, which flows only 1,500 meters from the house. The banks of the river are suitable for both summer and winter fishing. A little further (3,000 meters from the complex) on the river Vozhe gather fishermen going to pike, crucian carp, scavenger, perch. But the most notable bite on the Oka, where more than 30 species of fish are found. Here, by the way, not only fishing is good. Gentle shores, sandy beaches and clear waters of the river contribute to summer rest by the water.
