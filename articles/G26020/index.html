Costa Adeje - a paradise for tourists

Costa Adeje resort is located in the southern part of one of the provinces of Tenerife. It has a pleasant climate and amazing beaches that make this place the most attractive destination for tourists in the Canary Archipelago.

Description

The town of Adeje, even before the Spanish conquest, served as the residence of one of the kings of Tenerife. It was a small town with developed agriculture. The location on a beautiful island with a mild climate made Adeje popular among tourists. This contributed to the transformation of the city into one of the most visited resorts in the world.





On the coastline with Costa Adeje are the most popular resorts - Las Americas and Los Cristianos. The nearest airport is South Tenerife Airport, only 10 km away. The population in the town is not more than 50 thousand people. Travel business is for most the main means of earning.

Costa Adeje (Tenerife) is mainly visited by couples or tourists who prefer a relaxing and cultural holiday.

Hotel complexes

Five-star Costa Adeje hotels are not inferior in class to the best similar hotels in Europe. High level of service in four-star hotels.

The best hotel on the island is Iberostar Anthelia . He was awarded the highest rating in terms of service, living conditions and food.

No less popular is the Sunset Bay Club. The hotel has a good level of service and moderate cost of apartments. A distinctive feature of Sunset Bay Club is the presence in each room of an equipped kitchen with a full range of necessary accessories. On site there is a swimming pool and a children's playground for games.

Couples can stay at the Fanabe Costa Sur Hotel. The hotel has four stars and is famous for its comfortable and cozy rooms. The hotel also has a wellness center, steam bath and jacuzzi.

The guests of Lagos de Fanabe Reson deserve close attention. On its territory there is a huge fitness center, and the cuisine is diverse and exquisite dishes.

Beaches

Tenerife has more than 4 km of sandy beaches. The beaches of the resort of Adeje are considered the best. Here, calmly and comfortably, you can relax with the whole family. For this, the beaches are well equipped and have a full range of services.





The main beach in the south of Tenerife and the most popular in Adeje is Fa√±abe Beach. It is located in a small bay, which is protected by the marina, so there are no big waves here. A cozy atmosphere and snow-white fine sand have a pleasant and comfortable rest.

With children it is better to relax on the beach of Playa del Bobo. It is located in the city of Adeje and is considered one of the best beaches for children, since the sea is always calm. The beach is not only conveniently located, but also has excellent service, well-developed infrastructure and excellent restaurant.

Playa del Duc beach is fully equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable holiday for tourists. The advantage of this beach is a large amount of greenery, thanks to which even on a very hot day you can feel refreshing coolness.

Almost all Adeje beaches have Blue Flags, which indicates a very high level of cleanliness and comfort.

sights

Spain (Costa Adeje is no exception) has its own unique culture and rich historical heritage. On the south coast of Tenerife you can see historical monuments, unique towns, stroll along the beautiful streets and visit museums.

The most popular sights:

  • Municipal Museum of Sacred Art.
  • Typical Canary buildings on the farm of Caserio de Taucho.
  • Mudejar style building - Santa Ursula church.
  • Eagle Park (Jungle Park), where you can see more than 300 species of exotic birds and enjoy the unique landscapes of Adeje.

Entertainment

In Tenerife there are entertainment for every taste. The resort has a wide range of sports facilities and all the conditions for outdoor activities. There is a golf club with its own field. Fishing enthusiasts can rent a boat, boat or sailing ship.





There are many places in the vicinity of the resort where both adults and children can spend their leisure time. These are water parks, aquariums, theme parks for children, paintball fields, bowling clubs and so on.

Numerous shops and supermarkets are scattered throughout the resort, so there are all the opportunities for shopping. An exclusive shopping center is located on Del Duque Square, and there are many fashion boutiques, jewelry and souvenir shops in the Playa del Duque beach area. Unique products and accessories can be purchased on Bruselas Avenue, where the traditional market is located on Thursdays and Saturdays.

An ideal place for evening walks is the promenade connecting the two towns - Adeje and Arona. There are terraces along the coast where you can enjoy national cuisine and enjoy the unforgettable sunsets of Tenerife.

In the small town of La Caleta, there is the best restaurant in Tenerife. It serves local fish and seafood dishes. You should go here to taste the real cuisine of Adeje.

Fans of vivid impressions will be able to conquer one of the pearls of our planet - the volcano, which is the highest in Spain and the third highest in the world. To climb to its peak, you need to go along the cable car from Adeje to the peak of Taide.





Usually, tourists who once relaxed in Tenerife make these trips permanent. Costa Adeje attracts with its luxury, comfort and affordability.
