How to make a photo clearer in a few minutes?

For a long time trying to make the photo clear, but nothing works? Then this article will be your salvation. Many of the life hacks below will improve the quality of shooting even on the most ordinary camera. You will learn how to make a photo clearer without special skills and efforts.

Steady camera position

The main reason for blurry photography is movement during shooting. In order to prevent this, you need a stabilization system in the phone. If this is not on your phone, then you can purchase a tripod that will fix the position of the device. But many do not want to purchase such a holder, as it is too bulky and inconvenient. In this case, it is recommended to hold the smartphone with both hands when shooting.

Also, shaking of the device may occur when you need to touch the screen to take a photo. You can fix this using the timer, which is built into the smartphone‚Äôs camera by default. Another good way is to use the volume buttons to take a picture without taking your hands off the case. Also, if you connect the headphones to the phone, then using the button on them you can take a photo.

Since making a photo clearer is quite a difficult process, programmers have come up with special photo applications that have an anti-shake function. Camera FV-5 is just such a program for the Android operating system. And for users of iOS phones, ProCamera will be the best program for these purposes.

Lens cleaning

Very often the pictures are blurry due to the fact that the camera lens is fogged up or there are spots and dirt on it. To eliminate this problem, it is enough to wipe the lens with a soft cloth, and it is best to use special wipes for the lenses for this purpose. But if stains or dust are not wiped, then you need to take it to a service center. Specialists will either do professional cleaning or completely replace the camera.

Also on sale there are sets of lenses for the phone that improve the quality of shooting. These lens attachments allow you to take macro shots, the fisheye effect, etc.

Focus adjustment

Most people use auto focus when taking a photo, but it doesn‚Äôt always work perfectly. Therefore, this is also the reason for not clear photos. On many phones, you can adjust the focus by tapping the screen where you want the focus to be. There is also a very useful focus lock function that allows you to change the frame, but the focus will be fixed at the place where you set the point. This is a great option to make your photo clearer.

For iPhone device owners, to lock the autofocus, just click on the desired location on the screen and hold until a lock appears. In order to return autofocus, you need to click once at any point on the screen.

But users of an Android smartphone will need to download additional applications, since by default this function is not built into the camera. The Camera FV-5 program we mentioned above has a focus lock, called AF-L.

Light setting

It is known that in good lighting quality photos are obtained. Therefore, it is worth paying special attention to exposure during shooting. If there is not enough lighting, and you try to arrange the correct exposure, the camera will keep the lens open for a long time. This means that photos can be blurry and with noise.

One of the ways to make a photo clearer is not to use digital zoom, because when you approach a future picture, sharpness and clarity are lost.
