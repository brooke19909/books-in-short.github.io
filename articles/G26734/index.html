Create together: Unicorn cross-stitch. Schemes

In childhood, all children read fairy tales and, of course, met on the pages of many magical fairy-tale creatures: Pegasus, hypogryphs, dragons, mermaids, firebirds and unicorns. They will be discussed in our article. After reading it, you will see several patterns for embroidering a unicorn with a cross, and you will also learn what materials are best used in your work.

To create paintings, you need a canvas for embroidery, a woolen or cotton floss, a needle with a wide hole.

Monochrome cross stitch unicorn

If you are just learning to embroider with a cross, then take the simplest pattern. Similar patterns for cross stitching for beginners differ in a small amount of materials. In addition, monochrome embroidery has its own style and charm. Below we have given you an example of such embroidery.





To embroider a unicorn, you need to buy a canvas for embroidery and special threads. Most often, a floss is used for these purposes. The threads are cotton, woolen, viscose and even synthetic. For simple monochrome (black and white) embroidery, cheap Chinese mouline thread is suitable. As a rule, Chinese goods do not differ in richness of colors, so it is not suitable for large cross-stitch.

As you can judge by the unicorn cross-stitch pattern, you need a small 15 x 20 cm canvas section. First, determine the center of the embroidery. To do this, divide the sides in half and draw lines between the opposite. The intersection of the line will be considered the center of the embroidery. Start embroidering with it.

Please note that it is easier to make a mistake in monochrome embroidery because there are more than twenty crosses in long rows. To avoid mistakes, take breaks more often and double-count the number of crosses.

Complex multicolor unicorn cross stitch pattern

Below we presented a fairly simple embroidery, but using several colors. To create such a unicorn, you will need an 8x6 centimeter canvas and a mouline thread of several colors (gray, black or dark gray, lilac, light lilac, pink). To this cross stitch pattern for beginners, you can pick up a Chinese mouline thread, there are definitely enough shades for you. Moreover, for the mane and tail, you can buy a floss with a metallic effect. This will make embroidery more interesting and vibrant.





First, define the center of the embroidery as described above. Then embroider the outline from gray. It is noteworthy that in this beautiful cross-stitch for the contour, gray is used in two shades. You can take one, this will not spoil the embroidery. After the outline, proceed to the wings, mane and tail. At the end of the work, iron the embroidery. Done!

Embroidery for baby items

Many children's items can be decorated with cross-stitch. You can make a drawing on clothes (for example, a sweater, a headdress or a T-shirt) or personal items (for example, on a pillow or bag). The embroidery will look especially beautiful on the curtains in the nursery. As an element, we have given the unicorn cross-stitch pattern. It is small, its size depending on the size of the cell will be from 8x8 cm to 10x10 cm.





As with the previous schemes, first determine the center of embroidery, and then start working. As you can see, the unicorn itself is made in lilac and purple shades, but you can make it in blue or pink.

Large cross stitch

Large pictures embroidered with a cross can already be framed and hung on the wall. Below we have shown the cross stitch pattern of a unicorn from a popular children's cartoon called "Friendship is a miracle!"





To embroider a picture, you need a canvas measuring approximately 30x35 cm, as well as a gray floss, three shades of purple, violet, blue, blue and black.

First determine the center of the picture. You can start work either from the contour or from the mane. Note! If you embroider on colored fabric, you must also embroider the pony body itself. And if you embroider on a white background, then the pattern will seem more voluminous. Although in the case of a white background, the little body can be left blank.

Embroidery Tips

So you saw the unicorn cross stitch patterns. Now we will give you some tips to help improve your work:

  1. Only select a trusted floss manufacturer. Otherwise, your work may lose color after a few years, become faded and inexpressive.
  2. Woolen threads give more volume. If you want to create a fluffy and soft unicorn, then take the woolen thread of the floss.
  3. If the unicorn is small, and its eye consists of only one cell, then it can be embroidered with a bead - the picture will appear more lively.
  4. You can use additional decorative elements - rhinestones, satin ribbons and beads.
  5. If you want the embroidery to be more detailed, then take a canvas with small crosses. However, remember that working with such a canvas is much more difficult.

As you can see, the unicorn cross-stitch pattern can easily turn into a real picture and become an adornment of the interior or even clothing.
