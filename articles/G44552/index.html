200 ruble note: how was the image chosen for it?

Last year it was announced that they would soon issue new bills: 200 and 2000 rubles. The authorities do not attribute this event to inflation, because, in their opinion, the calculation for buyers will be simplified with such money. According to the head of the Central Bank, such banknotes are issued with inflation of 4-6%, and the Government is striving for such indicators. The authorities promise to reduce inflation to 4% by the end of 2017.





With the advent of such a message, they immediately began to decide what a 200 ruble bill would look like, as well as a 2000 ruble bill. Crimean cities will probably be depicted. To solve this, the authorities granted the right to vote to the Russians. Therefore, in the fall of 2016, an online voting was organized, thanks to which the choice was made.

How was the choice made?

Many citizens of the country are interested in what the bill of 200 rubles, 2000 rubles will be. Half of the citizens put forward a proposal that there should be new entities on banknotes - Crimea and Sevastopol.





Voting was conducted in several ways:

  • On the air "Russia 1" it was necessary to send SMS to a short number, writing in it the desired place in the Russian Federation.
  • Pass the vote on the official website.
  • Take part in the event during the open door of the Bank of the Russian Federation.

Every citizen could participate in this vote. A 200 ruble bill was chosen by more than 3 million people in the country. During the discussion of the type of banknotes, regional authorities proposed the use of images of their subjects. Everything was decided on the basis of voting.

Why are these denominations chosen?

Not many understand why these particular denominations are chosen, and not others, for example, 2500 and 3000 rubles. This is probably due to the tradition according to which in many countries they issue bills with the numbers 2. Especially since a coin of 2 rubles has been in circulation for many years.
