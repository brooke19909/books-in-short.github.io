Patterns of tree leaves - ideas and photos

Autumn gives amazing shades to the leaves, applies fancy drawings to them. This contributes to the awakening of creative thought. Someone wants to immediately lay out patterns from the leaves of trees, others will think of turning this natural material into a colorful picture.

Frame for drawings and photographs.





For her you will need small leaves. Walking in the autumn in the yard, in the park, in the forest, collect a few leaves of mountain ash, alder, linden, aspen, oak. Pay attention to fancifully painted ones. So that on the road they do not deform and do not lose shape, put them between the pages of the magazine, in the folder that you took with you specifically for this case.

Arriving home, put the leaves between two layers of thin cloth, iron. Then they will have the desired shape. If the leaves have not completely dried out the first time, repeat this procedure.

Now take a photo frame, lay out patterns of tree leaves between the glass and cardboard surfaces along its edge, place the photo in the center, and the original thing is ready.

A hammer to help





If you want to make an original pattern of leaves, the application will turn out extraordinary. For this you also need to collect the source material. Pay attention to dry leaves. They can be collected in the summer, at the same time taking flowers. It can be a leaf and the color of acacia, linden, pick a few bright wildflowers. From all this you will make patterns of tree leaves and other natural materials.





Put thick paper on a kitchen board, lay out fragments of future work on it. Having them, select by size, color scheme. Draw a simple pencil around the outline of each leaflet. Cover the work with clear cellophane. Lightly tap on each leaf with a hammer. You will see that they began to secrete juice. He will paint the outlined plants in the desired color, and as a result you will get a natural picture.

After that, carefully remove the remaining flowers and leaves with tweezers and cover the resulting creation with an acrylic spray. Let the work dry, after which it can be framed and hung as an original painting in the most prominent place in the apartment.

Wall mat

Patterns of tree leaves will look amazing on a textile product. To do this, you need to prepare the leaves, as described in the first example. Still need white and colored fabric.





Lay in front of you a rectangle cut from a light canvas. Sew brown lace on it so that you get small squares, in each of which there will be a place for a certain leaf. He will tell you how to make such patterns from tree leaves, photo.

Now glue a leaf into each cell. In this case, the fabric should be horizontal. Wait for the glue to dry. On the wrong side, lay a dense colored fabric, sew the edges of both fabrics.

Panel

You can make a panel of leaves "Autumn Pattern" in a slightly different way. The finished product will be more durable, it can be washed.

When you sew a braid on a white cloth, attach each leaf to a fabric that is similar in color, for example, an oak leaf to yellow, outline, cut out in shape. Thus, lay out the prototypes of the leaves of the trees on a brown, green cloth. After that, place each blank in its cell trimmed with braid. Sew in with an overlock or overcast. Finish the work by sewing patterns of autumn leaves on a dense colored base. A photo will help you understand how this is done. Such a rug is not only hung on the wall as a panel, but also placed under your feet.

Modern motives





The next work looks very stylish. You will need thick black cardboard, scarlet paint for fabric, glue and maple leaves. Let them be of different sizes. To find these, you can collect them not only underfoot, but also tear them from the trees.

After ironing these natural materials, lay them on waste paper, cover with red paint and let dry completely. Smearing the back of the sheet with glue, put it on cardboard. At the same time, try to keep the size. Place small leaves next to each other on one side. Large can border the top and bottom of the panel. It will also tell you how to make such patterns from autumn leaves, a photo from our review.

You can arrange the work in a slightly different way, taking instead of black white thick paper, replace maple leaves with rowan leaves, cover them with green, brown colors. It all depends on your preferences. Then you will get your favorite leaf pattern.

Hedgehog and owl applique

This type of work evokes imagination in children and adults. After you bring the leaves home, look, maybe one of them looks like a prickly hedgehog, and the other should only be slightly trimmed as it turns into an owl.

Draw on the light paper the snub-nosed face of the hedgehog, draw with a pencil the place where his body will be. Start gluing maple leaves with sharp edges up, starting from the back of the animal. Place the next sheet a little lower, then another, until you reach the abdomen of a hedgehog. You got a funny animal with original spines. Their role can be played by leaves of other trees, the main thing is to place them with their point up.

Before you make an owl, also outline the contours of its head and body on paper. Stick small leaves on them to symbolize feathers. If the leaves are large, cut them with scissors. Glue the eyes out of colored paper, and the pattern of autumn leaves in the form of an owl can be considered complete.

Other types of applications





The pattern of leaves in a strip has a strictly defined shape. To do this, take a sheet of paper, unfold horizontally. Now it is necessary to draw two parallel segments along the entire length of the canvas. The distance between these lines is such that the largest leaf, for example, maple, fits inside the strip.

Now you need to see how to beautifully arrange the natural material. You can alternate large with small leaves, or act differently, placing large first, and put small ones between them. As the latter, oak, alder, rowan leaves can act. When all fragments of the panel are laid out, turn each inside out, spread with glue and apply in its place.

You can arrange application fragments in strict geometric order. To do this, cut out squares, rectangles from them with scissors, glue them between two drawn segments. A specific pattern can be posted in a strip. Cut a circle from the yellow leaf , glue it, and place the rowan leaves around , you will get a colorful daisy. You can make several of these colors. A pattern of leaves in a strip made in the form of snowmen will look beautiful. To do this, cut out three circles of different sizes and stick them on top of each other.

Interesting look of the work, designed in the form of several bands. Then the whole canvas will be covered with intricate patterns.

Turning mountain ash, acacia into sakura





There are other tricks with which you can lay out a pattern of fallen leaves. A photo will help you figure out how to do a similar job. Gather small fallen leaves of acacia and mountain ash. Arriving home, put them between the pages of the magazine or ironed. When they dry, you can begin to work.

On black paper, draw a sakura branch, let it dry. Put some PVA glue in an unnecessary container, add red gouache to it, mix. You should get a pink paste. Now you need tweezers: carefully take the first leaf of mountain ash or acacia, dip it into the sticky mass. Then attach to the drawn sakura branch. Next to this, place 3 more impromptu flowers, as a result, each of them should consist of four petals. Let the work dry.

Pattern of leaves, applique on fabric, window, paper.





There are many other ideas on the autumn theme. If you have a plain t-shirt, vary it. Attach the maple leaf to a red, yellow, and brown fabric. Cut along the contour, place the parts on the front of the T-shirt, pin them with pins. Overlock over the contour. It will help to understand how to make such patterns from the leaves of trees, photos. In order to have more autumn accessories on the appliqu√©s, acorns are made from small beige and brown rags. Dark fabric will turn into their hat. Now you can try on a new thing.

If you do not want to part with the last echoes of autumn in winter, glue the dried leaves on the window pane. Then each time, looking out of the house onto the street, you will see not only snow, but also the autumn picture. On glass, paper, you can lay out the application in the form of a firebird. A large oval leaf will turn into her body, another, smaller, arrange perpendicular to it, this will be the neck of the peacock. The third, round, will become the head. Do the peacock tail as follows. First draw it on paper, then lay it out with green leaves. When they dry, glue on top a few sheets of yellow and red.

Such a bright, cheerful pattern of autumn leaves will delight you in the cold season and allow you to mentally travel to autumn, filled with bright colors.
