Labinsk hotels: description, addresses, services, reviews of tourists

Labinsk is a small town located in the south of the Krasnodar Territory and is the administrative center of the Labinsky district. A little more than 60 thousand people live in the city. Labinsk got its name from the Laba River, which is a tributary of the Kuban.

The former Don Cossack village, and now the city of Labinsk attracts travelers with structures of the XIX and XX centuries, churches, mineral springs, unique historical monuments. If you plan to visit this city, we will present you with a list of Labinsk hotels, visitor reviews, recommendations of experienced travelers.
