Museum of entertaining science "Experiment" in Irkutsk

For many families in our country , the issue of joint recreation in the territory of their village remains relevant. How to spend a day off so that it is interesting for both adults and children? This problem has been resolved in Irkutsk. The Experimentarium, a museum of entertaining science, is equally interesting for both adult visitors and children.

Museum History

The museum began its work in 2008. It has two branches: in the 130 quarter and in the Academgorodok. The ‚Äú experiment ‚Äù in Irkutsk has almost non-repeating devices in the branches. The opening of this unique institution took place at the Irkutsk Center of the SB RAS with the aim of promoting scientific ideas among the population. It should be said that the creation of such an institution was thought back to in the nineties of the last century.



Initially, the museum was placed in the city energy college, then it moved to the ISU astronomical observatory. After some time, the Irkutsk Science Center kindly provided a room located on Lermontov Street. The Experimentarium in Irkutsk today presents about three hundred interactive instruments and displays created by the museum staff.

Museum Description

The Experimentarium Museum in Irkutsk is an amazing place where visitors can see, touch unusual exhibits, try to understand such complex disciplines as auto mechanics and electromagnetism, optics and physics, and take part in interesting experiments.



When visiting the " Experimentation " in the Academgorodok one should not rush. To get acquainted with the exhibits, it is necessary to allocate at least three hours. In the " Experimentation " of Irkutsk, the number of interesting instruments and devices is constantly growing . It is interesting that most of them are made from improvised materials by the museum‚Äôs specialists: from old system units, monitors, bicycle wheels .

Even ordinary nails and sand are used to demonstrate "miracles." For example, the law of conservation of momentum is clearly demonstrated here with the help of billiard balls. And the model of the Earth‚Äôs atmosphere, in which lighter molecules fly up and heavy ones fall down, is made of brightly colored foam and an ordinary fan.

Among the curious exhibits of the Irkutsk Experimentarium Museum visitors include a chair with 1,024 nails driven into it. Any guest of the museum can sit on this chair, and without prejudice to his health: due to the large number of nails, the fulcrum increases and the weight is distributed evenly on these sharp objects. No less interesting is the unusual device for launching smoke rings.



Those who wish can stand on a light bulb that is inserted into the bank. The lamp does not break regardless of the weight of the tester. In the " Experimentation " of Irkutsk, a record was set, set by a man weighing one hundred and forty kilograms. The bulb has withstood.

Master classes

In the " Experimentation " you can attend master classes in which the children, as well as adult visitors, will be able to make their own hands (under the guidance of an instructor) an uncomplicated scientific device or toy and pick up their crafts for memory. During exciting activities, children are shown various laws and phenomena in a simple and accessible way. A small interactive introductory lecture, which demonstrates the experiments and explains what is happening on the topic of the master class, takes place before the practical classes, which last about an hour.



Birthdays

This most beloved children's holiday, which, as you know, happens only once a year, in " Experimentation " turns into a fascinating adventure. Fantastic experiments, interesting scientific workshops, space travel to the planetarium, entertaining master classes are waiting for the children. On this day, every child becomes a pioneer.

Birthday program includes:

  • Master class (optional).
  • Science show.
  • A quest around the museum, accompanied by a Detective, Pirate or Good Fairy.
  • An interactive tour.
  • Elegantly decorated room for refreshments.
  • Congratulations and, of course, a gift to the hero of the occasion .
