How to enable GPS on Android 2.2 and configure it?

To activate this module, just use the curtain on the phone screen. By sliding it, the user sees a quick settings menu. In it, and you need to click on the corresponding GPS icon. After a few seconds, the positioning module is activated.

After GPS for Android is connected, you can go to the settings. To do this, you must:
