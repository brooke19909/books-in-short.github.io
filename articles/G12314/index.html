Payment

The hotel accepts both cash and credit cards. It should be taken into account that payment is made in Belarusian rubles everywhere in Belarus.

So that tourists can avoid difficulties with payment, a currency exchange office was opened on the second floor. Some guests complain that the rate in hotels is not as profitable as in the city. However, this is completely untrue. As a rule, the difference in exchange rates between different banks is very small.
