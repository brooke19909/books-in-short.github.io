Hotel Diamond Cottage Resort & Spa 4 *, Thailand, Phuket - overview, features and reviews

Every travel enthusiast knows about Karon Beach, one of the longest beaches in Phuket. It stretches for as much as 5 kilometers, and tourists love it very much.

In the immediate vicinity of the beach is the Diamond Cottage Resort - a chic hotel with a decent level of service, which will be discussed now.

Location

The resort hotel complex is located among the hills, directly between the beaches of Kata and Karon. In just a couple of minutes you can walk to the shores of the Andaman Sea.

International airports are 31 and 81 kilometers from the hotel (Phuket and Krabi). Within walking distance are coffee shops, the Plaza Kata shopping center, bars, the Karon night market and many other interesting places.

You can also quickly get to the famous Chalong temple and pier, Phuket Simon cabaret, a monument to two heroines and, of course, the Phuket Fantasy show.

SPA salon

It deserves special attention. Many stay at Hotel Diamond Cottage Resort & Spa 4 * in order to arrange relaxation for their body and soul. After all, here, in the salon of the hotel, a really impressive range of services is provided. Here are just a few of them:

  • Traditional massage. It is done for the whole body, arms, neck, head, back, legs, as well as doubles.
  • Fragrant relaxing massage. It is performed with ylang-ylang, peppermint and lemongrass oils.
  • Deep tissue massage. Helps relax muscles.
  • Honey hour massage. They make it with the addition of freshly squeezed orange juice, which has a tonic effect.
  • Lymphatic massage. Performed using light pressure. Helps stimulate the body's immune system, improves blood circulation.
  • Body treatments: peeling using herbs, honey and sesame seeds, salt, cream scrub (coffee, coconut), etc. Help cleanse the skin, effectively remove dead epithelial cells.
  • Nutritious wraps using extracts of aloe vera and cucumber, natural clay, honey, milk, etc. These procedures help to quickly moisturize and soothe the skin, stimulate collagen production, and also have a detox effect.
  • Face care: natural masks using the best ingredients (fresh cucumbers, honey, aromatic essential oils, etc.). Cleansing, exfoliation, detox, radiance, cleanliness - this is what these procedures are aimed at.

In addition to the above, the spa of the Diamond Cottage Resort (Thailand, Karon Beach) offers dozens of other treatments, as well as whole packages that include several services at once.

Who should go here?

This question also needs to be answered. Phuket, in particular Karon, is a place for people longing for an active pastime. The resort is not suitable for individuals who want to relax from the hustle and bustle of life, as life here is in full swing.

When is it best to go to Karon? In January, December and February. In winter, comfortable weather reigns here (30-32 ¬∞ C), there is no unbearable heat and rain. The sea, of course, is also warm - from 26 to 29 ¬∞ C.
