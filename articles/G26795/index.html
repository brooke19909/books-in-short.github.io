Mayflower Beach Resort 3 * (India, Goa): description of rooms, reviews

Goa is one of the best resorts on the planet. Thousands of tourists come to this Indian state every year. Vacationers here are waiting not only for the gentle sea and the hot sun, but also for new acquaintances, rich leisure and exciting parties. Many vacationers choose the quiet resort village of Calangute, located directly on the coast of the Arabian Sea, to relax in Goa. There are fewer vacationers in this village than in Panaji and Mapusa, and the infrastructure is developed just fine. Among other things, here you can always rent inexpensive and at the same time quite comfortable housing. For example, the comfortable rooms are offered to guests of Calangute by the Mayflower Beach Resort 3 * hotel.

Location Features

This hotel is located in the village, on the second coastline, in a very quiet area. Hotel guests have the opportunity to visit one of the most famous beaches in Goa - Baga. The international airport is 46 km from the hotel. To get to the Mayflower Beach Resort 3 * hotel, most tourists prefer a taxi. If you wish, vacationers who have booked rooms at this hotel can order a transfer from the administration. The same method will be as convenient as possible for those who prefer to travel by train. The railway station from Calangute is also quite far away - 20 km. A taxi ride to the hotel from the station costs about 200 rupees.





General description of the hotel

Represents the Mayflower Beach Resort 3 * hotel (Goa) the modern beautiful building constructed in the Portuguese style. The walls of the apartment building itself are painted with a rich blue color, and the parapets of the balconies are white. Both of these shades are pleasantly combined both with each other and with lush tropical vegetation growing in the yard and even with water in the pool. The hotel building looks impressive and presentable. Judging by the reviews of tourists, it has a pleasant rest and its interior design. All rooms are decorated in a very beautiful and unusual Indian style for a European person.

The owners of the hotel are Kevin and Sylvester, and they, like all the hotel staff, are very friendly to the guests. If you have any problems, the hotel owners can be contacted safely. Kevin and Sylvester value the reputation of their hotel very much and do everything possible to ensure that guests are satisfied with the service. Good reviews on the web are available about many Indian hotels. About the Mayflower Beach Hotel, they are not just good, but excellent.

Mayflower Beach Resort 3 *: description of hotel rooms and reviews about them

According to most tourists, the Mayflower Beach hotel in comparison with many other similar establishments in India - the place is really very convenient and comfortable. The rooms here are spacious, clean and well equipped. In addition to beds, bedside tables and wardrobes for clothes in the rooms there are:

  • refrigerator and TV;
  • ventilation and air conditioning;
  • safe.

All rooms have balconies. It is certainly very convenient for beach lovers. In any case, problems with drying bathing accessories at the residents of the Mayflower Beach Resort 3 * hotel (India, North Goa) never arise. For a fee, guests who rent rooms at this hotel can use the minibar. Of course, each apartment has its own bathroom and shower. For the convenience of guests replenished toiletries are provided.





In total, the hotel rents three types of rooms:

  • Deluxe AC Room.
  • Deluxe Room.
  • AC Deluxe Room.

Each room is designed for 1-3 people. If necessary, the administration allows the settlement of the fourth guest, providing an extra bed and mattress. Young children can stay with the parents for free.

Accommodation prices

Of course, in order to relax in Calangute, at the Mayflower Beach Resort, tourists usually buy packages. India (Goa) - a resort, in principle, relatively inexpensive. This also concerns the cost of housing. Of course, in Sochi you can relax a little cheaper. But even some middle-income Russian citizens can go on a trip to Goa. A tour to India with accommodation at the Mayflower Beach Resort hotel will cost an average (depending on the operator) 39 thousand rubles (1 adult). Actually the rental of rooms in this hotel in terms of Russian currency costs at least 3,100 rubles per person per day.

Hotel infrastructure

Hotel amenities for guests are as follows:

  • bicycles for rent;
  • car rental and parking;
  • tour agency;
  • playground.




For a fee, guests can use a service such as calling a doctor to their room. The hotel also has a laundry room where you can wash dirty items.

Special amenities

Of course, like almost every three-star hotel, this hotel has a pool. It is small in size, but the water in it is clean and always warm. There is a swimming pool in the courtyard of the hotel and is equipped with sun loungers and umbrellas. Tourists can use sun loungers for free.

Many guests find the hotel courtyard with a swimming pool very cozy and comfortable. Too many people are never here. Therefore, guests can relax in a deck chair in almost complete silence. The bowl of the pool is decorated with beautiful blue tiles, and along its perimeter there is a balustrade and tubs with tropical plants. In general, the hotel pool looks very impressive and presentable, and relaxing here is pleasant and convenient.

Another additional service at the hotel is an exchange office. The course, judging by the reviews of vacationers, is quite profitable here.

Dining at the hotel

To rent a room in the Mayflower Beach Resort 3 * hotel is possible with or without breakfast. The hotel also has a comfortable, cozy restaurant. Here, as elsewhere in the hotel, tubs with beautiful plants are installed. The menu in the restaurant, according to many guests, is not very diverse. However, according to most tourists, the food in this institution is quite tasty and, most importantly, not too expensive.

In addition to the restaurant, the hotel also has a bar. Here you can order light snacks and all kinds of drinks. There is a bar in the lobby of the hotel.





Beaches

The coastline of the Arabian Sea is approximately 250 meters from the hotel. To get to the Baga beach, hotel guests have to walk no more than 2 minutes. The coast in this place of the ocean is covered with fine sand, suitable for relaxation.

Baga is one of the largest, cleanest and rave beaches in Goa. It originates from the mouth of the river of the same name. After a few kilometers, Baga smoothly passes to Calangute beach. Both local Indians and European tourists relax here. Very often on the beach you can find cows, which, as you know, in India are treated in a special way.

Swimming at the beach at the Mayflower Beach Resort 3 * hotel is very convenient. The entrance to the sea is smooth, and no boulders are found. In the evening, many entertainment venues work at Baga. If desired, tourists can rent a boat and visit another beach, located behind the protruding into the sea mountain range - "Ajuna". Here on Wednesdays a day market is organized.

Accommodations

The front desk at this hotel is open 24 hours a day. Pets are allowed, but only upon prior request. Hotel rooms are available both for smokers and for those who are deprived of this bad habit.





Calangute Infrastructure

The Mayflower Beach Resort 3 * hotel is located, as already mentioned, in a very quiet area. However, in the immediate vicinity of it passes one of the busiest and rave streets of Calangute. Here, if desired, you can visit bars, restaurants, cafes, discos, markets, etc.

Especially popular with domestic tourists is North Goa. Prices in shops and canteens are not too high (fresh, for example, costs 80 rupees, and beer - 54 rupees), there are many entertainments.

The town of Calangute itself, according to many vacationers, is the most noisy resort village of North Goa. More recently, Calangute was nothing more than a provincial fishing village. In the 70-80s, the village suddenly fell in love with the followers of the then widespread hippie movement. "Flower Children" came here in companies and organized communes. Later, vacationers simply intrigued by tales of his beauties began to flock to Calangute. Gradually, an excellent tourist infrastructure developed in the city.





Today in Calangute you can find everything you need for a pleasant stay. Shops in the town sell beach accessories, original Indian souvenirs, seafood, all kinds of goodies. The cafe offers tourists mainly dishes of local and impromptu European cuisine. Of course, there are massage salons in Calangute. In general, you can relax in this town very well.

Tourists recommend going to Calangute in the fall or winter. The weather in Goa in October - March is very good and has a pleasant rest. The average air temperature in these months is 33-35 degrees, and the water in the sea is 24. There is almost no rain in India at this time.

sights

A huge number of different places to visit is what distinguishes Goa. The Mayflower Beach Resort 3 * hotel, among other things, offers its guests assistance in choosing excursions. At a hotel you can buy a trip to interesting places in Goa at a low cost. For example, hotel guests have the opportunity to visit the Dudhsagar Falls, Brahma Karambolim Temple, etc.

The name of the Dudhsagar Falls translates as "an ocean full of milk." Its total height is 603 meters. This is perhaps the only noteworthy tourist attraction in North Goa. The waterfall looks very impressive. In addition, at its foot you can swim in the cool, crystal clear fresh water.

Brahma Temple is located in Sattari Village, 2 km from Karambolim Village. It is dedicated to the Indian god of creation. This beautiful religious building is located in a dense forest. It was supposedly built in the V century. Its main attraction is the best image of Buddha in the world.
