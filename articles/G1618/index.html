Tours and excursions in Mexico

Mexico is a country of amazing contrasts. It blended the traditions and lifestyles of Portuguese sailors, Native American Indian tribes, and the mysterious culture of the Caribbean natives. On its coast are the best beaches of the American continent.

On the mainland, in the silence of tropical thickets, there are a lot of ancient monuments and hillforts. The choice of tourists is an abundance of cognitive and fascinating excursions in Mexico. And the country is famous for tequila, hearty and delicious cuisine, fiery dances and the fantastic underwater world of Cozumel.

Settlements

The capital of the state is Mexico City. The city is one of the hundred largest megacities in the world. On all sides it is surrounded by mountain ranges. Its center is Constitution Square. The architectural ensemble of the historic part of Mexico City is Baroque. Most excursions in Mexico introduce tourists to this magnificent and deliberately pompous quarter.

Travelers are brought to the city park "Alameda", on Garibaldi Square. They are offered a stroll along Paseo de la Reforma Avenue. Outskirts of the metropolis - a place in which the entrance to foreigners is closed. They are dangerous even in the daytime.

Guadalajara is considered the most developed and dynamic city in the country. Many excursions in Mexico include her visit in their overview programs. The city's calling card is thousands of roses that adorn local avenues and streets, parks and squares. Traditionally, all the most interesting sights of Guadalajara are concentrated in its historical center.

The metropolis is replete with fashionable hotels and restaurants, expensive shops and luxury boutiques. It has everything for an idle and fun life. The most prestigious is the western part of Guadalajara. The middle class chooses the suburbs of Tonal and Tlakepake. By the way, in these settlements are the best souvenir shops.

Monterrey is another point that is included in many excursions in Mexico. This is an important financial center of the country. The city imitates successful US settlements in every way. The local public is well provided. The people are not in poverty. Almost nothing remains of authentic architecture. Local residents prefer modern architectural forms. The nature reserve is another advantage of the prosperous Monterrey. The megalopolis is surrounded by flood meadows and endless valleys, mountains, canyons and even sandy deserts.

Coast

Cancun is the epicenter of attraction for beach holiday fans. The city occupies the coast of a snow-white lagoon. It is a series of stately hotels in which Europeans and tourists from the United States rest. Excursions in Mexico from Cancun are mostly entertaining. They are designed for several hours. Travelers are carried on comfortable buses equipped with air conditioning.

Only the resort of the Mayan Riviera can compete with the welcoming Cancun. It is chosen by vacationers with small children. Noisy Acapulco invites you for entertainment and shopping. In reviews of excursions in Mexico praise trips to Los Cabos. This is a favorite vacation spot of the Mexican elite. Those who like a quiet and secluded vacation choose Puerto Vallarta.

Tula and Teotihuacan

An excursion to the cloister of civilization of the Indian tribes is designed for eight hours. It begins at sunrise. Travelers are introduced to the ancient warriors of Atlanta, who are located in the ancient settlement of Tulu. This is the former Toltec capital. It reached its peak in 1,200 BC. Later, tourists will see the mysterious pyramids.

From Chichen Itza to Valladolid

This program is considered the most popular. Its route runs past the most interesting tourist attractions. It is designed for one day. The first stop is the archaeological ensemble of the city of Chichen Itza. Later, tourists will have the opportunity to swim in the karst reservoir. For lunch, vacationers are brought to the El Hardin restaurant. Service is carried out according to the "Buffet" system.

After the meal, an excursion to the production of tequila follows, which ends with a tasting of this strong drink. The return journey runs through the city of Valladolid. Tourists are told about the traditions of colonial architecture. Travelers are shown the Catholic Cathedral of San Gervasio. If desired, vacationers are brought to the museum of semiprecious stones. The local souvenir shop sells crafts from local craftsmen.
