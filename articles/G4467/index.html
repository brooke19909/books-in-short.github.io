Specifications

Inside this radio is a four - channel amplifier type MOSFET with a capacity of up to 50 watts. This suggests that you can use this radio even without a separate amplifier. Power enough for the eyes. Car radio connector for connection - standard DIN. This makes it compatible with all cars (any year of manufacture). There is also a digital tuner with memory for 24 stations and support for FM-AM broadcast waves, a five-band equalizer and AUX input for connecting an external audio source. The type of connector is a 3.5mm mini-jack.





But the main "trick" of this car radio is the USB-connector. Through it, you can connect both flash drives and smartphones based on the Android operating system. The car radio supports such devices completely and can both play sound files from the internal memory of the smartphone and charge the device while it is connected to it. This is the main feature of the Pioneer MVH-180UBG. The reviews of the owners indicate that any smartphone based on Android 4.0 and higher is detected by the radio without any problems.

Features car radio
