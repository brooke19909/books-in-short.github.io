Territory, how to get that is nearby

To get to the hotel from the airport (unless, of course, a transfer is included in your ticket), you need to take the bus number one hundred and eleven to the Costa Adeje station. The buildings of the Best Jacaranda 4 * hotel are in the garden, terraces descending from the hill. The buildings form a kind of courtyard with a large pool.
