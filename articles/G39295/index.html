Hotel White Pearl Suites 4 * (India / Goa): photos and reviews of tourists

Goa has been one of the best world resorts for more than 50 years. Residents of distant foreign countries prefer holidays in the northern part of the coast, the tourism industry is more developed here. The hippies introduced the Russian-speaking population to the local beaches, as, in principle, to other resorts such as Ibiza, Bali and the city of Matala on the island of Crete.

What is North Goa famous for?





On the territory of North Goa there are a lot of hotels, hotels, entertainment centers and restaurants in which tourists can relax comfortably. Specially equipped beaches for a safe pastime. In principle, everyone can afford a vacation in Goa. Leisure prices are fairly affordable, so this is a popular place among young people. It is very economical and convenient to stay in the so-called guest houses. These are private guest houses, the owners of which live with guests or nearby. A small minus of such housing is the lack of reception, so you have to agree with the owners in advance. If you are an energetic and cheerful person, then a vacation in Goa is for you, because here you can meet interesting people and have fun from the heart. Entertainment on state beaches can last until dawn: incendiary dances, bars, night shops. And also here you can visit unforgettable carnivals.

Local entertainment



On the coasts of North Goa, their own, free laws of life. Tourists prefer to relax at popular tech parties, moving to the local music - goa trance. This is mobile music with sounds of nature and melodic rhythms. Trans parties can take place in the most extraordinary places. At various parties you can meet Indian tramps, local hippies, rastamans, ravers and a lot of interesting characters. After a nightly disco, this whole crowded crowd together with tourists can pour out in the middle of the beach, not earlier than at lunch, having plenty of rest, having a bite at local establishments and going to a new party.

Since groups of freaks may soon leave Goa, you should definitely visit excursions to their habitats. The civilized world of tourism has become more popular here, and there is little room for local hippies. However, many still come to these parts to lead a free and trouble-free life, and some even meet old age as a hippie. In North Goa, of course, there are places with a more familiar atmosphere of relaxation.

In the local flavor, you can also meet the usual nightlife with interesting parties, listen to familiar music and chat with locals or tourists.

Relaxing holiday



If you chose Goa as a trip, then you will find exotic places of nature, acquaintance with Indian traditions, tasting of traditional cuisine.
For those who are tired of city bustle and noisy parties, couples, or those who prefer a relaxing holiday, it is better to visit the south coast. Here you can admire the tall palm trees, listen to the noise of the Arabian Sea, enjoy the local sandy beaches to the fullest. Thanks to affordable prices for accommodation and meals, a tropical vacation in Goa is becoming more affordable every year. Local travel agencies can offer you interesting trips to the most attractive places in India. When planning a trip abroad, you need to take care of where you will live in advance. There are many hotels on the north coast. One of them will be discussed in this article.

White Pearl Suites 4 *: hotel description





One of the best hotels in North Goa is recognized as White Pearl Suites. It is located in a natural lagoon. Residents of the hotel can observe from their windows all the beauties of the city of Baga. This is one of the many advantages of the White Pearl Suites 4 * hotel. Room descriptions are presented below.

Accommodation



In total, the hotel has 12 comfortable deluxe rooms. The apartments are decorated in a modern style and equipped with all necessary appliances. The rooms have a large terrace overlooking the beauty of the black cliffs and the azure lake. Each comes with cable TV, a work desk and free toiletries. It is possible to book connecting rooms. Buffet breakfast is included in the price.

Entertainment
