SKB Bank: reviews of employees and customers

Today we will talk about one of the largest regional banks. To be more precise, this is the second institution in the rating of assets that conducts its activities mainly in the Sverdlovsk region. Moreover, taking into account the geographical specifics, SKB-Bank, reviews of which will be presented below, is considered a fairly prominent institution throughout the Ural region.

In the article, we will describe the bank, describe its most popular products, and also provide feedback from both customers and employees found on the network.

general information





Let's start with the characteristics of the bank, its general description. According to official information from shareholders, he belongs to 98% to billionaire Dmitry Pumpyansky (the remaining share is in the hands of minority investors). This is confirmed by the fact that the bank is engaged in servicing settlements of the Pipe Metallurgical Company, which is controlled by the same person.

However, if we look at the funding sources of this institution, we notice the lion's share related to deposits. Probably, this area is singled out as a priority.

If we turn to history, it was founded back in 1990, but at that time it was a branch of Agroprombank, only later it was renamed the Bank for Assistance to Commerce and Business. It is from here that the contraction that we see today has occurred. Until 2013, the bank changed hands, until it was bought out by a group led by Pumpyansky.

To understand the scope of activities, you should look at the following figures: SKB Bank (employee reviews can confirm this) consists of seven branches operating in Moscow, Khabarovsk, Vologda, Udmurtia, Volgograd, Ryazan and Omsk. In total, the institution is divided into 69 management offices, as well as 108 operating rooms. For the convenience of working with clients , the bank has a network of terminals (about 200 machines in different cities) and almost 600 ATMs. If you read the reviews on the highest echelons of management devoted to SKB-Bank, you can find out more than 2.3 million serviced cards. Partially these data are confirmed by the bank itself.
