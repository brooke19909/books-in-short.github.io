Sea Grace Aparthotel 3 * (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria): hotel description, services, reviews

Sea Grace Aparthotel 3 is a hotel located between the famous Bulgarian city of Nessebar and the famous resort of Sunny Beach. It is located near the sea, and at the same time, not far from an amazing place, breathing antiquity and authenticity. Tourists praise the hotel for its beautiful and comfortable rooms and apartments, excellent service, comfort, pleasant atmosphere and the opportunity to relax and forget about all everyday problems. The hotel is a type of apartment with additional services. It was built in two thousand eight, and was completely renovated in 2012.



Nessebar

This ancient Bulgarian city is located in the very south of the resort area under the eloquent name Slenchev Bryag (Sunny Beach). Nessebar is the oldest city in Europe. Its history has about three millennia. If the new part, where our apart-hotel is located, is located on the mainland, then the old part is on a small peninsula that extends directly into the sea. With land it connects a narrow four-hundred-meter strip of land.

Once in the old Nessebar, you will be fascinated by it. The ancient port and medieval churches, narrow streets and souvenir shops, fortified walls and old houses made of fired brick - you can wander around for hours. But the most important highlight of Nessebar is the temples. The oldest church is the Metropolitan. It was built on the foundation of a former ancient Greek temple dedicated to Apollo. But other churches are not inferior to this in beauty and interior decoration - Christ Pantocrator, St. Paraskeva, St. Stefan ... No wonder Nessebar is considered a museum city and is under the auspices of UNESCO. And in new areas there are water parks and entertainment centers where free buses go.



How to arrive, location

If you do not have permission to enter the Schengen countries, you need a special visa to Bulgaria. They issue it in the usual manner, because the information that you can get such a stamp in your passport upon arrival is incorrect. This would be contrary to EU rules. To get to Sea Grace Aparthotel 3, you need to fly to Burgas Airport. These are the air gates of Bulgaria closest to Nessebar and Sunny Beach. The airport is twenty-five kilometers from the hotel. From there you can take the express train going to Nessebar. Some tourists prefer a shuttle bus that stops in Sunny Beach, and then a minibus.

The hotel is located in the so-called New Nessebar, and walk to the old center for about a quarter of an hour. To the sea - eight to ten minutes. Very close to the hotel are supermarkets, shopping centers, discos, night clubs and other entertainment. Almost at the entrance to the hotel there is a bus stop, so you can easily get to the old town and Sunny Beach.



Territory and services

Sea Grace Aparthotel 3 consists of four beautiful snow-white six-story buildings, built in a functional style. Elevators work everywhere. The buildings are new, and when creating this resort in the construction, innovative materials were used. Around the buildings is a landscaped garden. On the territory there is parking for fifty cars, a number of shops. The hotel practices 24-hour customer service. Near the reception and pool there are areas where free Wi-Fi catches. Guests can rent bicycles at the hotel to drive along the coast, play table tennis and darts. Children play in a modern and safe playground. The garden has a large screen for watching movies outdoors. Dirty things are taken to the laundry, but this is a paid service. For extra money you can go to the massage parlor or spa with a jacuzzi.



Apartments and studios

The Sea Grace Aparthotel has 3 one hundred twenty rooms. Fifty of them are luxury apartments with one or two bedrooms and a living area. All rooms are air-conditioned and have large balconies. The rooms and apartments are designed so that light and space dominate, creating a special atmosphere. And from the top floors there is a beautiful view of the sea. The studios are equipped with an American kitchen, where there is a small refrigerator, electric stove and microwave. In the shower there is a hairdryer. There is a boiler in the bathroom. This means that there will be no problems with hot water. The apartments have full kitchens, sometimes combined with one of the rooms (living room). Folding sofas and armchairs can be used to accommodate more than two people. At your request a crib will be delivered. The rooms have satellite TV with four Russian channels. Bed linen and towels are changed every two days.

Where to go and go

In addition to the ancient Nessebar, hotel guests usually visit local attractions as well as natural attractions.
