Samsung Galaxy S6 Edge Plus: review, specifications and reviews

Samsung knows a lot about creating phablets. For the first time, the company threw a challenge to this segment of the market, creating the first device that entered the product line of the Note line. Today we will deal with another similar device of the corresponding segment. This is the Samsung Galaxy S6 Edge Plus, a review of which is presented in this article.

Prerequisites for the creation





After entering the large market Galaxy S6 Edge , Samsung faced one interesting phenomenon. Demand for this device is not that off scale, but it was at a very high level. You could even say that this company did not expect. So it turned out that demand exceeded the possible supply volume. The engineers and other employees of the company had to get out of it somehow, come up with a new move that could correct the current state of affairs at that time.

If we consider this situation in more detail, more specifically, it should be noted that the production of curved displays has been increased to a large extent. The consequence was an increased release of models in which such components were used. The emphasis that was previously placed on the Note product line was biased. Thus, the corresponding devices simply ceased to be the main flagships and phablets of the company. It is interesting that such a decision was made by the company without indicating any specific reasons. This is what confused a huge number of buyers around the globe.

So, the Samsung Galaxy S6 Edge Plus, an overview of which is given in this article, is positioned as a flagship. And this, therefore, means that the corresponding company is trying to implement the device with the greatest efficiency, based on the success of the previous model. Engineers and designers, designers of the company made a lot of efforts in order to provide us, ordinary buyers of the device, with what we wanted so much. On the other hand, this trend is inextricably linked with the rejection of more active PR line Note. Of course, this will require the introduction of lightweight device configurations into the smartphone market.

Positioning





Samsung Galaxy S6 Edge Plus, which we will review in this article, is a rather expensive device of the company. At the same time, he can offer us a very interesting and original design. We assure you that you will not find such a thing with any competitor of the company. The flagship is designed for use by a mass audience. But for whom was this device created?

Samsung Galaxy S6 Edge Plus, the characteristics of which we will list below, has a slightly different positioning compared to the Note line. The device was created for those people who were primarily interested in a beautiful design and a large screen. Nevertheless, to call such an audience large will be quite difficult. Any company that is engaged in or engaged in phablet sales, of course, knows that such devices, although they are sold, did not and do not have a tendency leading to a sharp increase in popularity (and, consequently, sales). Although here in terms of Asian markets you can argue. There phablets disperse, as they say, just with a bang. But we must take into account the situation as a whole, right?

So, to summarize the intermediate result. The Samsung Galaxy S6 Edge Plus smartphone, reviews of which will be given at the end of this article, is a device equipped with a large, impressive screen. The device is based on a huge number of different functions that will appeal to many users. Moreover, to successfully control the device does not even need a stylus.

Design





Probably, one of the highlights of this device can be called a screen curved on the sides of the case. This makes the appearance of the smartphone somewhat unusual, and therefore interesting. In fact, everything is so. You can easily recognize the flagship in the device, and this is also a thoughtful move. From the point of view of the image, this device attracts attention to itself, too. On the smartphone market, nothing like this currently exists. Nevertheless, we can take Edge Plus and its predecessor and compare their appearance. In this case, we notice the almost absolute identity of the devices. At first glance, it seems that this is one and the same model, without any noticeable differences.

But such an opinion is erroneous. Firstly, smartphone designers have worked (and not bad) on changing the shape of the case. The kink that goes along the metal edging has become noticeable. This is a well-thought-out decision by company employees. What are the practical reasons for this? With the help of a kink, it was possible to ensure that the case does not cut into the palm, but lies comfortably in it. The predecessor of our current device could not boast of such. Holding Edge Plus has become much more convenient. At the same time, one should not even talk about the affected design.
