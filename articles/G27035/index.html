The charming embroidery pattern "Pansies" cross will give a feeling of happiness

Embroiderers have their own motto, which reads: embroider until the thread runs out. Why is embroidery so popular with crafts today? It has a long origin. In ancient times, embroidery on clothes and household items was used not only for an aesthetic purpose, but also served as a talisman, had magical powers. Currently, it is used only for decoration to create an unusual, original style. Many people use embroidery in home decor.

Bright flower and its meaning

What will make you smile? Cross stitch patterns "Pansies", "Violets"! These flowers, looking like cute faces, will make you smile at any time of the year. The name of the pansies is viola. Many legends are stacked about this flower.

In the nineteenth century, when flowers were actively used to express feelings, he symbolized the following: "I love you," "I think of you," or "miss you." And now many believe that embroidered pansies mean happiness and a desire to be with your loved one always. Pansies also symbolize a person‚Äôs admiration for other people. That is why such an image is used in the emblems of numerous charitable foundations around the world. For those who do not think about the signs and the secret meaning of embroidery, these plants, unusual in their color variety, evoke only joyful associations with summer, sun and joy.

Big and small forms

The cross-stitch pattern for Pansies is made on Aida fabric or on any other fabric suitable for this type of embroidery, for example, cotton, linen or a mixture of cotton, linen and viscose.

The fabric may vary in color, sometimes repainted. The main thing is that it is in harmony with the selected embroidery pattern. Finished work must be consistent with a specific idea. Simplified patterns of embroidery are usually intended for beginners. For lovers with experience are subject and very complex images. Sometimes simple embroidery is no less beautiful than large embroidered paintings. To create products of small forms, a small number of colors and a stylistic image of a flower are required. However, a neat cross-stitch made with love looks beautiful anyway.

Particularly good are pansies in decorating napkins, tablecloths, small cards, needle beds and biscuits. Embroiderers with experience decorate clothes with embroidery. The process itself provides inner satisfaction, the opportunity for self-expression and the creation of something beautiful. Cross stitching is both difficult and useful. Those who engage in this type of needlework deserve respect for their dedication and skill.
