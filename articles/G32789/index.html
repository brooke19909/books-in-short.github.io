Location

The subject of the Russian Federation is the Republic of Bashkortostan. Mezhgorye is a city located on its territory and is divided into two regions. The first one is Central (Kuzelga microdistrict), located at the foot of Yamantau Mountain in the South Ural Reserve. The distance from Ufa is about 140 km.

The southwestern Mezhgorye (Tatla region) is located at the foot of the Dunan Sungan mountain of the same reserve. If you go from Beloretsk, you will have to overcome about 35 km.



Briefly about the main thing

The territory of the city of Mezhgorye (Republic of Bashkortostan) is crossed by two rivers. These are the Maliy Inzer and Bolshaya Kuzelga watercourses. The city is located at an altitude of 500 meters above sea level. The population is more than 16 thousand people, according to the 2015 census. Among them are Russians, Bashkirs, Ukrainians, Tatars and other nationalities. The city is quite young, founded in 1995 by the Decree of the President of the Russian Federation.

Misterious story

The history of the creation of the city is full of legends, in which everyone who at least once heard about this city believes. According to one version, secret weapons, nuclear warheads and much more are interesting and secret. But another version says that a bunker has been built in Mezhgorye (Bashkortostan), in which there are huge reserves of food and treasures. In it, in the event of a negative situation in the country, the first persons of the state can live for a long time calmly and completely comfortably. Rumor has it that the construction of an underground city for 300 thousand people, the so-called reserve capital of the country, is still ongoing in Mezhgorye.

City development

The city of Mizhhiria (Bashkortostan) has the status of a closed territorial entity and it is hardly possible to go on vacation here to get acquainted with local attractions, for example, the ski resort "Abzakovo". Just want to note that you can admire the infinitely beautiful and picturesque landscapes.

Mezhgorye is among the top 10 safest places found by scientists in the world in which it will be possible to survive during and after the end of the world. Despite the special secrecy, people also live here and culturally develop in 3 municipal cultural institutions. Children go to kindergarten. In the city of Mezhgorye (Bashkortostan) there are 5 preschool institutions, 3 schools, children's interest groups and studios. There are also two hospitals, pharmacies, outpatient clinics.



Every year, on the banks of the Inzer River, a city festival of art song is held. You can see many monuments to the heroes of the Second World War, builders, the Poklonnaya Cross, as well as a monument to the ruler of Spain, Francisco.

Sports occupy a special place in the life of local residents; a stadium, a tennis court, sports halls, chess clubs, and fitness clubs function in the city.

Mount Yamantau - a great place to relax

Despite the impossibility of the public to get to Mizhhiria (Bashkortostan), many tourists can climb secretly on Mount Yamantau. According to them, since 2000, in order to engage in mountaineering here, a number of rules must be observed. Such measures are necessary, since the mountain is located on the territory of the reserve and is strictly guarded by rangers and military. It is necessary to observe silence, not to make fires and, in general, not to betray his presence, unless, of course, there is a desire to get acquainted with the "culture" of the special forces and the harsh laws of huntsmen.

Those who ascended to the top say that the mountain is a small plateau, and there is also a lot of debris thrown by the builders long ago. Tourists also saw many mines that go deep into mountain formations.



There is an opinion (the legend of the ancestors) that Yamantau is the heart of the Universe, it supplies blood (energy) to everything on the planet. And if, despite all the obstacles created by the local authorities, you manage to somehow get on a mountain or swim in the Small Inzer River, then you will be filled with powerful energy that will bring health, strength, wisdom and immortality to your life.
