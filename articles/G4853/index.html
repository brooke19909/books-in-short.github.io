About the bank

SKB Bank began its work in November 1990. This financial institution has received all the economic and historical prerequisites to be called a backbone organization in the country's financial market. The Bank is constantly introducing new products and improving customer service. SKB provides its customers with the opportunity to effectively manage their money, and the bank is constantly studying all the needs of citizens, regardless of their social status and feasibility.

How to open

There are several ways to open a deposit . The easiest is to go to the official website (SKB Bank). Deposits on the site are presented in a complete list with comprehensive information about them. After filling out the application, the manager usually contacts the client to clarify some issues. Next, you need to visit the office to open a deposit. An exception is the online wish fulfillment + contribution.





The second way is to directly visit any convenient bank office. Here you can also get comprehensive information and immediately open a deposit if you have all the necessary documents.
