Scanner Setup

Most smartphones have a similar way to configure the scanner, the only differences are in the interface. Therefore, we consider a universal technology to answer the question of users: how to install a fingerprint on a phone. First you need to go into the general settings of the device, and then follow the steps described below.

Instruction manual

You should go to the menu, which is responsible for changing scanner options. Most likely, it is called ‚ÄúLock Screen‚Äù. There will be a sub-item, the functions of a fingerprint scanner are contained. Now click on "Manage fingerprints." If the phone does not have an alternative unlock method (PIN, password or graphic), the system will automatically offer to configure it. It is necessary.

After installation, a menu will open where the instructions are described. "OS" will ask you to "scan" your finger at different angles several times. As soon as the system completely remembers the fingerprint, it will immediately save it and notify you of the successful process. This completes the setup.
