Features of the charger Acer Iconia Tab A511 and its advantages

Today, Acer Iconia Tab is in high demand in many countries of the world and the Russian Federation is no exception. This tablet has many advantages. This power adapter (charger) is used for models such as Acer Iconia Tab A701, A700, A511 and A500.

The main feature of this adapter is the plug, which looks like an ordinary USB-micro connector, but at the same time it performs only the charging function. This connector is also equipped with a special additional group of several contacts, which are designed for the full supply of mains voltage, the nominal value of which is 12V, which is necessary for fast charging of tablets, models Acer Iconia Tab A701, A700, A511 and A500. In addition, the charger also has a very compact size - this allows for example to always take on a trip or to work.

It is worth noting that the Acer Iconia Tab A511, A500, A700 and A701 network charger has a rather unusual appearance. One of the main advantages of the Acer Iconia Tab A511 AC charger is that the cord is wide enough, which gives the Acer Iconia Tab A511, A500, A700 and A701 even more durability and reliability.

Especially in the charger it is worth highlighting the modern appearance and a fairly simple device. In addition, the power cable of the charger is quite long and it is possible to charge the Acer Iconia Tab at any distance convenient for you. The standard color of this model is black. But there are several basic color variations on sale. In addition, this device is perfect for full power, as well as charging any battery in devices such as Acer Iconia Tab A511, A500, A700 and A701 and so on. It is especially worth noting that the charger is quite powerful - a full charge of the device is carried out for 45 minutes - 1 hour.

In addition, you need to pay attention to the fact that this charger is for all the main varieties of Acer Iconia Tab, including A511, A500, A700, A701 and some others. In principle, it is universal, but only for Acer devices. In addition, this charger has a standard plug (input) that can be used in various Euro outlets.

On some models, the Acer Iconia Tab is provided as standard. When the charger fails, there are two months to return the money or replace it with a new one.
