The best hotels in Bishkek

Bishkek is the capital of Kyrgyzstan and is a major administrative center of the region. The city has concentrated the state authorities of the republic.

Features of choosing a place to stay

The most acceptable accommodation option will be accommodation in one of the many hotels located in the city center. From the whole variety of housing, you can choose the most suitable in terms of cost and services offered. When choosing central hotels in Bishkek, it should be borne in mind that the cost of living in them will be slightly higher than in those located at a distance from the center. But budget options here too.

Grand Hotel

This hotel is the most fashionable in the city. Its interiors are pompous and decorated in Baroque style. Classic style successfully combined with modern equipment. The number of rooms consists of sixteen rooms, designed for the simultaneous accommodation of thirty-five people. Elite hotels in Bishkek offer unsurpassed living conditions to their VIP clients.

Number of rooms





The Grand Hotel is a combination of a classic interior and modern equipment. Corridors, landings, openings and the lobby of the floors are decorated in the classical Baroque style. Guests can stay in suites, junior suites or standard rooms. Let us dwell in more detail on the description of the hotel rooms.

The most comfortable and the largest in area (sixty square meters), of course, are considered suites. They have a bathroom with a modern shower and a chic King Size bed. A little more modest in terms of occupied space (fifty square meters) are the junior suites. Instead of a shower cabin, they have a bathroom. King size bed provides a pleasant sleep.

Standard rooms are designed for two or three guests. They have separate beds according to the estimated number of guests, as well as a bathroom. All rooms are equipped with modern fixtures, satellite TV. Almost all hotels in Bishkek strive to adhere to European standards of service.

Other services

Meals are served in the Grand Restaurant, whose menu includes dishes of national and European cuisines. Breakfast is included. There is also a bar where guests can enjoy their time.

The list of services includes the ability to use the laundry, leave valuables in a guarded safe. The staff speaks Russian and English, so communication difficulties should not arise. Security of accommodation is ensured by the availability of round-the-clock security. The hotel also has secure parking with a car wash.

Bishkek hotels in the center not only allow their guests to enjoy comfortable conditions, but also provide an opportunity to get acquainted with the main attractions of the city. Due to its convenient location in the center, travelers have the opportunity to meet interesting locals. For the convenience of guests, the hotel provides a taxi call service. At the request of customers, room service is also provided.

Accommodation at the Grand Hotel is available to people with good incomes. But there are budget hotels in Bishkek. Cheap hotels are not located in the heart of the city, but nevertheless they are not much removed from it.

Hotel Bishkek Boutique





Located three kilometers from the city center, in the industrial area, the hotel belongs to the category of three stars, it has twenty-five rooms equipped with a minibar, TV, furniture. The comfort of guests' stay is ensured by the presence of air conditioning and a refrigerator in each room. All rooms are decorated in a classic style.

It has the largest clothing market in Bishkek in Central Asia - Dordoi. Hotels located near shopping areas are notable for their low prices and good conditions.

Dining at the Bishkek Boutique Hotel is carried out in a cozy lobby restaurant. Guests, due to the presence of kitchen equipment in the rooms, have the opportunity to cook their own meals. The list of services includes the ability to use a safe, free parking, dry cleaning. Guests are delivered to their rooms by high-speed elevator. Throughout the territory, Internet access is provided.

Hotel Dostuk





Dostuk Hotel (Bishkek) is located near the administrative center, not far from the city circus and major shopping complexes. From the windows of the guests a panorama of the city opens. The rooms are decorated in a modern style and are superbly equipped with all kinds of household appliances. The hotel provides standard services for guests (the ability to leave valuables for storage in a safe, access to the sauna, gym, beauty salon). The list of available entertainment also includes: massage services, billiards and a casino. Guests with children can leave them for a while with the nanny.

For all kinds of business events, presentations, the hotel has three conference rooms accommodating forty, sixty and eighty-five people, respectively.

There are more affordable hotels in Bishkek. Cheap, but quite comfortable hostels of the city are always welcome new guests.

Apple Hostel (Bishkek)





In addition to luxury hotels, Bishkek hotels are also represented by establishments belonging to an inexpensive segment of the market. An example is Apple Hostel Bishkek, which has been operating since 2005. It has seven rooms (double with a private bathroom and shared, designed to accommodate a large number of guests). The dormitory rooms have bunk beds. The toilet and bathroom for guests staying in the dormitory rooms are located separately on the floor. Hostels are quite popular today. Many people choose this option immediately, ignoring almost all the hotels in Bishkek. For rent here you can rent a comfortable place to sleep.

Apple Hostel Bishkek was opened in the summer of 2015 by a family of travelers who, from their own experience, know what is needed for a successful holiday. The hostel staff will gladly share all their travel tips not only in Bishkek, but throughout Central Asia. Cooking is done in the shared kitchen, and reception is in the living room. Free breakfast is provided. The rooms can accommodate children. Smoking is prohibited, except in a designated place.

Cottages and economy hotels in Bishkek are not necessarily poor conditions and terrible service. Quite the contrary - everything is provided for in them so that the budget tourist does not feel disadvantaged.

Hostel Interhouse





Another relatively inexpensive accommodation option in the capital of Kyrgyzstan is to stay at Interhouse Hostel, located in the Oktyabrsky District. From the hostel you can quickly walk to the city center and train station. More than thirty kilometers separate it from Manas Airport.

There are twenty-two rooms with retro-style furniture. The rooms are decorated in soothing colors. They include a TV and kitchenware. Meals are provided in the shared kitchen. Breakfast is free of charge. Also, at the request of guests, they are served tea or coffee at any time. Shared bathrooms and bathtubs are located outside the rooms.

The staff is available to provide all the necessary information about local attractions. After the excursions, you can relax comfortably in the living room and watch TV. Payment is due before arrival by bank transfer. The amount is reserved and is not refunded to the client in case of unforeseen circumstances.

Bed & Breakfast - the opportunity to have a good rest in a cozy and inexpensive mini-hotel

The list of options for accommodating guests of the city will be incomplete if you do not talk about cozy mini-hotels. An example is the Bed & Breakfast. Despite the small size, guests are provided with quite comfortable conditions. Due to the pricing policy, focusing on budget holidays, the mini-hotel is popular among tourists. In the immediate vicinity are the largest shopping and entertainment centers and shops, which is also convenient for guests.

Relaxing in the mini-hotel is ideal for tourists traveling in groups. Living in it will cost less than renting an apartment in the city. The number of rooms consists of four rooms, simultaneously accommodating twenty people. The four most comfortable rooms have bathrooms and are equipped with a bath. For guests of other rooms amenities are located on the floor.

Guests have the opportunity to cook their own meals in the well-equipped home kitchen. Attentive and qualified staff ensures constant cleanliness and order in the rooms and premises.

The list of services provided to guests includes the ability to store valuables in a special chamber, ironing and massage. There are always fresh newspapers and magazines in the lobby. Free internet access is provided throughout. For guests who do not know the city, guides and maps are provided.

Three-star hotel "Futuro"





Not everyone can afford to choose elite Bishkek hotels. Inexpensive accommodation options are, fortunately, also available. Hotel Futuro is located in the private sector close to the Historical Museum and a complex of government buildings. It consists of twenty rooms, decorated in an original manner. All rooms have excellent soundproofing, air conditioning, TV. Guests can watch satellite TV. Each room is equipped with a safety deposit box and kitchenware. A bathroom with a bathtub is located directly in the room.

Meals are provided in the cafe, and breakfast is included in the price. Lunch and dinner are buffet style. Free coffee and tea with snacks are always available at the bar. At the request of guests, a shuttle to the hotel and vice versa is provided. For guests arriving for business purposes, a business center operates.

Mini-Hotel Solux

The hotel is next to the Republican Academy of Sciences, the State Philharmonic and the central square. A complimentary tea set is provided. The rooms are equipped with modern appliances and necessary furniture, there is a bathroom and a bath. The comfort of your stay is achieved through installed air conditioning. Meals are provided in the dining room. Breakfast is included in the price.

Hotels (Bishkek) are always happy to accommodate guests who come for various purposes. Most preferred for tourists is accommodation in small hotels. There, for a relatively small cost, they will receive quite comfortable living and recreation conditions. As a rule, most small hotels are located in the central part of the city, close to historical and cultural attractions.

The hostel accommodation option is suitable only for the most demanding category of tourists. For city guests who have arrived in the republic as part of a business trip, the best option would be to check into a large hotel that offers maximum comfort and provides a wide selection of entertainment for every taste. Accommodation in a large hotel, for example, in the Grand, will emphasize the status of the guest in front of business partners and will contribute to the successful solution of the trip.
