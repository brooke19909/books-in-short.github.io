Hostel in Kaliningrad cheap: list, description, reviews

Many tourists are looking for a great hostel in Kaliningrad. You can relax here inexpensively, but where is described later. In the article, we consider the most popular places for tourists. All provide clean rooms and good service.

List of hotels worth considering:

  • In like;
  • "The very center";
  • "Patriot";
  • "Deima";
  • "Birdhouse";
  • "Bed".

In like

The cost of rooms at this hotel is at least 500 rubles per day. Shuttle services are available at an extra charge. There is free internet. The rooms are equipped with all necessary appliances and furniture. There is a shared bathroom and toilet on the floor. Equipped kitchen where you can cook yourself something to eat. In Like Hostel is located close to shops, restaurants.

There is also a terrace. Barbecue facilities can be rented. For cars there is public parking, the place on which is free, and private - there is a paid place on it. The hotel has a safe. Each room is heated. There is a playroom for children. Families can stay in special rooms. Smoking is allowed only on a designated area.

Of the advantages highlight the design of the hotel, as well as its location. Of the shortcomings - residents notice that they do not clean up in the kitchen, smoke in the wrong place, the administration does not respond to this. Families with children should also be careful, as some guests drink alcohol.

"The very center"

An excellent hotel, the cost of living in which starts at 500 rubles per day. Hostel Samy Tsentr in Kaliningrad is located next to historical buildings, as well as a botanical garden.

Guests are accommodated in comfortable rooms, where there are clothes hangers, linens, a safe, a wardrobe, and heating is also provided. There is a shared kitchen equipped with kitchen utensils, dishes, you can dine or cook something in your room.

In 2017, a major overhaul was carried out. For those arriving by car, parking is provided. The hotel has internet access. Transfer service available. There is a laundry room. You can take books from the administration. Rooms are cleaned every day.

In the reviews, many residents note excellent value for money. The room is clean, linen and towels too. The kitchen has everything you need for cooking. There is a transport interchange near the hostel.

"Patriot"

This hotel is located near the center of Kaliningrad. Hotel "Patriot" is inexpensive, you will have to pay about 700 rubles per day (minimum price). The hostel was founded in 1988. There are 9 floors. Located just 10 minutes from the center, and 30 minutes from the airport. Nearby there is also a botanical garden. There are 77 rooms. They are comfortable and designed for people with different levels of budget. There is a cafe in the hotel where you can have a delicious lunch. You can use the free internet. There is a conference room where you can hold an event or a business meeting. There is also a beauty salon on site. Rooms are cleaned every day. The reception is open 24 hours a day. Special rooms are available for families.

The reviews note that the staff is friendly, and the rooms are comfortable.

Deyma

The cost of the rooms is about 1 thousand rubles. The price includes breakfast. The place where the Deima Hotel (Kaliningrad) is located is quiet and calm. Near historical monuments.

All rooms are decorated in the same style. They have a sleeping place, additional furniture made of wood, a refrigerator, a TV with tuned channels. There are shared and private bathrooms. In the morning, breakfast is prepared for guests. The menu has a huge number of dishes and desserts, as well as drinks.

Clean shoes for free. Laundry facilities are available. A beauty salon is open next to the hotel. A hairdryer is provided. An indoor pool is open all year. There is a sauna, a billiard room. The hotel has an ATM, massage services.

The reviews are described by friendly staff. Easy access to train stations. The breakfast is full, so you can not think about food until lunch.

Birdhouse

Hostels in the center of Kaliningrad are quite expensive, so it is better to prefer options that are not far from it.

The cost of a bed in the Birdhouse per day is about 600 rubles. To get to the Cathedral, you will have to spend about 5-7 minutes. Guests enjoy free internet, a 24-hour front desk and a games room for children. If you wish to relax in nature, you can rent barbecue facilities.

All rooms are decorated in warm and bright colors. They have all the necessary equipment and furniture. In the shared kitchen you can have lunch and cook your own meals. The staff speaks not only Russian, but also English. There is an outdoor terrace. The rooms are cleaned every day, this service is free. There are separate rooms for families. No smoking on site. Guests have a common living room with a TV. A transfer service is available.

In the reviews, buyers note that the staff is responsive and always makes concessions. The hostel is located near the city center. Many are advised to buy breakfast at the hotel, as they are delicious.

KoykaGo

The hotel is located next to the city park. The administration offers the services of a tour desk, a games room, and there is also a gift shop. There is also a 24-hour front desk.

There are 8 different room types in the room stock. They have both family and budget options. The design of all rooms is attractive. The beds are made of natural wood. All the necessary equipment for relaxation offers this hostel in Kaliningrad. You can eat inexpensively. Products can be bought at the store and then cooked in the shared kitchen. In addition, drinks and food machines are installed. Nearby are restaurants and cafes.

In the common area is a computer. Fax and printer work. Office services are provided for guests. You can call intercity.

Rooms are cleaned every day, there are places for smoking. Bike rental is available. For luggage storage there is a separate luggage, but it is paid - 100 rubles.

The reviews describe that when entering the hotel you need to change shoes. In the next building there is a cafe where you can eat cheaply. Accommodation at KoykaGo is at the highest level. This option is suitable for those who are not satisfied with self-cooking.

Summary

As follows from the reviews, each tourist will be able to choose from the presented options the most suitable hostel for him. In Kaliningrad, inexpensively and comfortably, you can relax at any time of the year. The staff in all institutions is responsive, always speaks with a smile, and is ready to fulfill any request.
