Hotel Elinotel Polis Hanioti 3 * Halkidiki, Greece: reviews

Halkidiki Peninsula is the main resort of mainland Greece. Both wealthy and economical tourists come here who prefer a relaxing beach holiday. In summer, the peninsula is surrounded by greenery, so swimming in the sea can be combined with walks in the parks. Of course, do not forget about the historical sights of ancient culture, which are also many here. For a budget holiday, tourists who come here often choose the city hotel Elinotel Polis Hanioti Kassandra. How is it remarkable? Where is? What service does tourists offer? We will try to answer these questions in our article.

Where is the hotel situated?

Arriving in Halkidiki, you should know that the peninsula is conditionally divided into 3 parts: the resorts of Kassandra, Athos and Sintonia. Each of them has its own specialization and features. Our hotel is located in Kassandra, which is considered the most densely populated place on the peninsula. There are many traditional Greek taverns, restaurants and bars of local cuisine, night clubs. In short, the resort is fully focused on outdoor activities. Elinotel Polis Hanioti 3 * complex was built in the small village of Hanioti, which is famous for its long promenade and clear sea.

The hotel is located on the second line 200 meters from the public beach. The central part of the village with nightclubs, restaurants and bars is 500 meters from the hotel, so their night noise does not interfere with the guests here. Thessaloniki International Airport is approximately 90 km from Chanioti. You can get to the hotel by taxi or public transport. If desired, tourists can also arrange a paid transfer in both directions, and then the complex will meet them at the airport and take them directly to the place.

Hotel Details

The small city hotel Elinotel Polis Hanioti (Greece) was opened in 1991. The local tourist network of the same name, which contains several more hotels, is in charge of its leadership. The complex has practically no territory of its own. It consists of a four-story building built in a modern Mediterranean style, an outdoor pool and a small sun terrace. The hotel is completely fenced from the city by a high white fence. Inside the area is decorated with small trees and flowers growing in pots.





The hotel is suitable for tourists who do not like crowded accommodation options. It offers a total of 50 rooms, in which up to 100-120 guests can simultaneously stay. The staff of the hotel is small, but all the staff speaks not only Greek, but also English. Some employees understand the Russian language a bit. You can check in here with young children who are older than 8 months. But the hotel does not accept tourists with animals. Registration of guests is carried out from 15:00 to 21:00. If you arrive sooner or later, you should notify the property in advance. Departure from the hotel is from 07:30 until noon.

Hotel Rooms

Since the Elinotel Polis Hanioti complex (Greece, Kassandra) is a budget three-star hotel, you should not count on first-class rooms. The last complete renovation was carried out here in 2010. The repair was done in a classic style: the walls are painted in pastel colors, tiles are laid on the floor, there is a minimal amount of decor elements. But each room has its own bathroom, equipped with shower, sink with mirror and toilet. Some apartments also have an outdoor terrace or balcony overlooking the pool and terrace. Housekeeping is provided by maids daily, and they only change linen and towels 2 times a week.

More about room equipment

The equipment of the rooms of the Elinotel Polis Hanioti 3 * hotel is not very diverse. Reviews of tourists note that the set of amenities here is minimal, however, the rooms have everything that the picky guests may need. In addition, most of them are provided to guests free of charge. We list the amenities that all rooms of this hotel have:

  • Plasma TV with connected cable channels, among which there are several Russian-speaking;
  • individual air conditioning and control panel, it is also worth noting that its use is paid - many hotels in Greece have this feature;
  • a small fridge or minibar - you can use them for free, but for filling, as a rule, a fee is charged;
  • a small safe in which you can put your documents, money, mobile phones, jewelry and other personal items for safekeeping;
  • Wired telephone, with which you can contact the hotel administrator or call home;
  • built-in hairdryer in the bathroom;
  • replenished toiletries (shower gel, solid soap and shampoo).

How are meals served at the hotel?

As a rule, tourists staying at the Elinotel Polis Hanioti 3 * hotel (Greece) prefer to spend most of their time outside its territory. Therefore, when applying for a ticket, they pay only half board (breakfast and dinner), having lunch on their own in the taverns and restaurants of the village. However, there is the concept of "All inclusive", which is suitable for those who spend a lot of time at the hotel. It includes full breakfasts, lunches and dinners at the buffet, as well as ice cream, soft drinks (tea, coffee, mineral and sparkling water, concentrated juices), local wine and beer. For a fee, you can purchase imported alcohol.





The main restaurant is open daily on site and serves a common buffet. He specializes in European and Mediterranean cuisine. You can enjoy refreshing cocktails, including alcoholic drinks, at the poolside bar. It also has its own outdoor terrace.

Hotel infrastructure

The level of service provided at the Elinotel Polis Hanioti 3 * hotel (Halkidiki) is fully consistent with its stardom. Here you will not see many infrastructure facilities, however, the hotel has everything necessary for relaxing unassuming tourists. We list the main ones:

  • a shop where you can buy snacks, tobacco and alcohol products, newspapers, souvenirs, beachwear and shoes;
  • public parking near the hotel, which guests can use for free;
  • car rental, which can be arranged for a fee at the reception;
  • safes and luggage storage rooms located at the reception;
  • laundry - in order to hand over clothes for washing, they need to be transferred to the administrator;
  • fax, photocopying and currency exchange services at the reception, which operates around the clock;
  • shared living room on the ground floor, equipped with sofas, TV and air conditioning;
  • library with books in Greek and English;
  • The hotel building has a freight elevator.




Beach, pool and entertainment

Most tourists coming to the Elinotel Polis Hanioti 3 * hotel (Halkidiki) choose it for a beach holiday. However, the hotel does not have its own plot near the sea. Therefore, guests visit the public beach, which is 200 meters from the complex. It is fully equipped for tourist needs. The coast is completely sandy, without sharp pebbles. The entrance to the sea is comfortable and even, which makes it a good place to relax with children. The only negative - for sun loungers, sun umbrellas and beach towels, tourists must pay from their pocket. There is a water entertainment center on the beach where you can rent a motorless or motor boat, such as a catamaran or a boat. Next to it there are nets for playing beach volleyball.





You can swim in the outdoor pool, which is located on site. Unlike the beach, there is no need to pay for sun loungers and umbrellas. A bar serving refreshments is nearby. For lovers of outdoor activities in the complex is open gym. For a fee, in the evening you can play billiards with friends or go to the sauna with a steam room. There is no wireless Internet in the hotel.

Relax in a hotel with children

When arriving at the Elinotel Polis Hanioti 3 * hotel (Halkidiki) with children, it is worth considering that extra beds are not available in the rooms. The only exceptions are baby cradles, which are suitable for babies up to two years old. They are issued to guests upon request for free, but are not always available. If the child sleeps in such a crib, then you do not need to pay for his stay.

There is no entertainment program for younger guests at the hotel, so their leisure falls entirely on the shoulders of the parents. However, there is a shallow bathing area for them in the pool.

Elinotel Polis Hanioti 3 * (Halkidiki): positive reviews

Picky tourists, as a rule, are satisfied with the rest in this place, so good reviews leave it. Those who counted on more comfortable accommodation do not recommend coming to this hotel. In a word, the hotel has enough advantages and disadvantages. First, consider the advantages of relaxing in this place:

  • convenient location, as the hotel is quite close to the beach and the center of the village, but at the same time is far from noisy night clubs;
  • good and varied food, new dishes from the chef regularly appear on the menu;
  • clean and always tidy up hotel territory, also employees are constantly engaged in cleaning the pool;
  • polite and pleasant staff who always try to please the guests;
  • the rooms do not seem small and cramped, although they have a small area;
  • comfortable and large beds.

Draw conclusions

Often the Elinotel Polis Hanioti hotel receives negative reviews. However, this is not a reason not to choose it for your vacation. If you are looking for a place for an extremely budget holiday and do not expect at the same time a first-class level of service, then most likely you will like this hotel. It has become a favorite destination for tourists with young children and older couples from Europe. Much less often youth companies and adults stop here, who prefer to choose more prestigious accommodation options.
