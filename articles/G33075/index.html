Tassel earrings

Earrings are one of the most popular and favorite accessories among women. Currently, each fashionista has at least 5-6 pairs of earrings. However, not only ready-made accessories are designed to decorate, you can make them yourself, from improvised materials.

In order to make tassel earrings, we need:

  • Thin fishing line.
  • Needle.
  • Beads.
  • Schwenzy.
  • Beads.
  • Fixing pin with ring.
  • Scissors.

It is not difficult to make such earrings, but it is very important to do this in stages, namely:

  1. At the first stage, you need to insert the line into the needle and string the beads on it. There should be at least 10 such bead threads. The length and color of bead threads should be chosen depending on your preference.
  2. Prepared bead threads should be tied in a knot at the very bottom of the fishing line.
  3. The resulting bead brush must be secured to the mounting pin and decorated with beads.
  4. At the final stage, the resulting workpiece should be attached to the sleeves. By the way, shvenzy can be any: in the form of a clip fastener, with the English lock and in the form of a hook.

As you can see, making jewelry with your own hands is not difficult, but the result will surely please you and give your image originality and sophistication.

Delicate spring necklace

Recently, the necklace has again become fashionable and has firmly consolidated its position. Such an ornament will enliven the image and give it a certain share of romance. However, it is not necessary to run to the store for the next piece of jewelry, as you can make necklaces from beads and beads yourself at home.

In order to create such a delicate decoration, we need the following components:

  • Scissors.
  • Glue.
  • Fishing line.
  • Pink beads.
  • Pink beads.
  • Clasp for necklace.

Making homemade jewelry from beads and beads is very easy. To do this, you must:

  1. Using glue, glue the pink beads in the shape of a flower. There should be 20-30 such flowers.
  2. String pink beads and the resulting flowers on the fishing line in this way: beads 1 cm, flower, then again beads 1 cm and flower again (ideally, the length of the necklace should be 20-25 cm).
  3. At the final stage of making jewelry with your own hands, fasten the clasp on the necklace.

Necklace made of beads "Girl's Tears"

A fringed beaded necklace does indeed resemble flowing droplets, like girl‚Äôs tears or rain. To make a jewelry yourself, you will need:

  • Scissors.
  • Fishing line.
  • Clasp.
  • The beads are white.

Perhaps this is the easiest decoration to perform, and even an aspiring craftswoman can make it affordable. However, accuracy is very important in this seemingly simple matter. To make such an ornament, the following manipulations should be performed:

  1. Take a fishing line 25 cm long and put on beads.

Brooch in the style of boho

Until recently, the boho style was used only in clothing. However, as it turned out later, it can also be used for the manufacture of accessories. In stores you can buy bags, belts and even boho-style jewelry, but we will go the other way and craft a leather brooch on our own.

To make such a boho-style jewelry, we need:

  • Burgundy leather.
  • Lace.
  • Beads.
  • Beads.
  • Universal adhesive.
  • Clasp for brooches.
  • Accessories in the form of petals.

Since the boho style implies layering, the manufacture of such an instrument implies a phased implementation, namely:

  1. Cut a strip of 1 cm wide and 10 cm long from the skin. A volumetric rose should be formed from the prepared strip by stitching or gluing it together.
  2. A circle with a diameter of 5 cm should be cut from lace. The circle must be gathered and stitched with folds.
  3. A leather rose should be glued to the resulting lace blank.
  4. Beads and beads should be glued to a rose. And accessories in the form of petals should be sewn or glued under a rose.
  5. From the skin it is necessary to cut a circle corresponding to the diameter of the brooch, and stick it to the wrong side of the brooch.
  6. At the final stage, the clasp should be glued to the brooch.

Such a leather brooch will certainly noticeably revive a boring blouse or dress and allow you to look original in any way.

Pearl bracelet

Do you like to wear bracelets? Perfectly! This accessory perfectly emphasizes the tenderness and beauty of the hand, therefore, every girl (woman) should have such a decoration and preferably not one. Bracelets can be any: gold, silver, made of beads, beads, leather, but the most beautiful and delicate is a pearl bracelet. Let‚Äôs try to create a simple bracelet with your own hands from pearls, especially since it is not at all difficult, and for its manufacture we need a minimum of materials:

  • Pearl beads (large and small).
  • Fishing line.
  • Lock.
  • Needle.
  • Scissors.

In order to make a pearl bracelet, you need to cut the fishing line to the size of your wrist, leaving 3-5 cm in stock. At the final stage, you should fix the lock on the fishing line, and now the jewelry, made by yourself, is ready.

Braided Bead Bracelet

Which of us in childhood was not fond of weaving? It's time to remember these skills and weave a bead bracelet. In order to weave an elegant bracelet, you will need a special machine. However, you can make it yourself by scoring cloves on a plank from two sides.

So let's get started. To weave a bracelet, we need:

  • Threads.
  • Needle.
  • Clasp.
  • Beads.

The bracelet weaving technique is as follows:

  1. The machine must be pulled in 5-6 rows.
  2. 5-6 beads should be strung on a thread with a needle and weaved them into threads on the machine, prying them from above, then from below.
  3. Thus, the entire bracelet should be woven.
  4. At the very end of the bracelet, loop fasteners should be formed.
  5. On the received loops fasten a fastener.

By the way, the bracelet can be both plain and colored, patterned. To make a color bracelet, you should alternate beads of different colors, according to a pre-compiled scheme.

In such a simple way, you can make any jewelry that can later be worn by yourself or presented as a gift to relatives or friends.
