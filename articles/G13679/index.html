Cardboard: frame

The most affordable way to create horns is cardboard. Old cartons can be found in everyone‚Äôs house, and we‚Äôll try to create chic demon horns from them. This material will be needed a little, one middle box will be quite enough. To create large, wide horns, you need a piece of A4 paper and a pencil. Mark a spiral in the form of a horn of the desired size, width, shape. Using a stationery knife, cut two cardboard horns on a stencil.

The photo of the demon horns shows that they need volume, for this, cut circles of various widths into the eye. At the same distance, make cuts on the horns along, but not from the edges, but in the center so that the circles can be inserted into the products. That is, the circles should be of different sizes, from large to small. From the same cardboard, it is necessary to cut strips, also of different sizes. Stripes need to be bent in a semicircle and try on between the circles in the horns of the demon. They will be our volume.

Mugs must be glued, PVA glue is unlikely to work, so use super glue or a glue gun. After the frame is ready, you can proceed to its processing and painting.

Horns decoration

Take PVA glue and a wide bandage. Wrap the horns with a bandage, generously lubricating with glue. Leave to dry. It is advisable to make two layers. When the horns are dried a second time, they can be attached to the rim on which they will rest on your head. Self-tapping screws are ideal for this, drill several holes parallel to each other so that the horns do not seem lower or higher. And also drill horns.

Next, we strengthen the dense bandage more and more, while trying to maintain ribbing and generously cover with PVA glue. If you have a mass for modeling or plasticine - great! With their help, you can create additional mass that will complement the missing tubercles and grooves. Additional details will give more realism to your horns.

You can paint the demon horns with both gouache and spray can. Creative people can add sparkles and shadows. Choose a suitable color, natural or black. So, from simple materials at hand, you can create an incredible accessory for any outfit.
