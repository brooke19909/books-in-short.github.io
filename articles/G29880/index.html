Andaman Phuket Hotel 3 *: photo, description, reviews of tourists

If you plan to relax on the island of Phuket (Thailand) and are looking for an inexpensive, but comfortable hotel, then Andaman Phuket Hotel 3 * could be a good option.



General information, photo

This 3-star hotel is 700 meters from the famous Patong Beach. The distance to the airport is 50 kilometers.

Andaman Phuket Hotel was built in 2011. It has 60 deluxe rooms, which are located in two five-story buildings. Upon check-in, a security deposit of $ 100 is charged. Upon departure, the money is returned. All rooms have air conditioning, TV, telephone, refrigerator, safe, bathroom with hairdryer and a small balcony.

In addition to residential rooms, the hotel has a restaurant, spa, gym, outdoor rooftop pool, laundry, parking, car rental.





Andaman Phuket Hotel 3 *: traveler reviews from Russia

When planning a trip to a particular country, many modern tourists try to get acquainted not only with general information about the hotel of interest, but also study the comments of other people who have already visited it. Thanks to this approach, you can learn not only about the advantages of the hotel, but also about many of its shortcomings, which can save you from disappointment. Therefore, we bring to your attention the generalized reviews of our compatriots who have recently stayed at Andaman Phuket Hotel 3 *. Immediately it can be noted that almost all tourists do not regret their choice at all. According to them, this hotel is fully consistent with its category and justifies the cost of living. But we offer to understand everything in more detail.

Location and Housing

According to travelers, the location of the hotel under consideration is very successful. So, Andaman Phuket Hotel is located almost in the city center. In its vicinity there is a large shopping center, a grocery hypermarket (many tourists bought fruits here to take home with them), lots of cafes, restaurants and bars, as well as a huge market where you can find a variety of products.

As for the rooms, in general, the guests were satisfied with them. However, according to them, the windows of the apartments overlook either the garden or the neighboring building. Experienced travelers advise the first option to those who want to enjoy the pretty scenery from the room, and the second to those who do not want to be disturbed by extraneous noise from the street. All apartments here have a pleasant design, it is noticeable that they have recently been renovated. Our compatriots note very comfortable large beds. All the equipment, according to them, is not new, but in good working condition.





Nutrition

The price of accommodation in Andaman Phuket Hotel 3 * (Thailand, Phuket) includes breakfast only. According to our compatriots, they are standard for this country. So, you will not find sausages and cheese here. But you can taste eggs, sausages, dried cereal, rice, pasta, fresh fruits and vegetables, as well as delicious pancakes that bake right with you, with jam. During the day you can eat in the surrounding cafes and restaurants.

Entertainment

The nearest Patong beach, according to tourists, from Andaman Phuket Hotel can be reached in 10 minutes by leisurely step. On the way you need to cross the road. The beach here, according to most of our compatriots, is not very clean, but overall it's nice to relax here. However, many tourists rented a scooter and went to other beaches.

Since Andaman Phuket Hotel 3 * is a typical city hotel that does not have its own territory, special entertainment is not offered to guests here. However, tourists can have a good time on the roof of the hotel, which has a small pool and sun loungers and parasols. There is a spa on the ground floor of the hotel. According to our compatriots, they make an excellent and very inexpensive massage in it.
