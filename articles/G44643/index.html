Sunny Adler, guest house "Valentina": description, reviews

On the border of Russia and Abkhazia there is a cozy Adler - the seaside town of Greater Sochi.

Since Soviet times, the Adler resort has been actively developing. The healing mud of these places and unique mineral waters contributed to the development of the sanatorium.





Thanks to the 2014 Winter Olympics, Adler has tremendously updated the infrastructure. Modern hotels and hotels, new restaurants of the European level, amusement parks and shopping centers were built.

Adler's advantages are a subtropical climate, a flat terrain and a gentle beach, excellent transport links, a huge selection of housing for every pocket, the absence of a railroad by the sea and a huge amount of entertainment and attractions. The clean sea, pebble beaches, and well-developed infrastructure annually attract a huge flow of tourists.

Accommodation in Adler

The variety of accommodation in Adler is very large - new fashionable world-class hotels, glamorous hotels, large pensions and resort complexes - all these delights of tourism are here. However, vacationers in the private sector are most in demand among holidaymakers.

A huge number of private mini-hotels and guest houses offer tourists a good rest in comfortable conditions at affordable prices. Private pensions rent rooms in all corners of the city. The competition in the private sector is very great and contributes to the fact that the owners try to organize the maximum comfort of their stay and offer the ideal price for a vacation. Often, tourists, satisfied with a warm welcome, return annually to visit this wonderful town - Adler again.

Guest house "Valentina"

Guest House Valentina is located in the very center of Adler. The road to the beach will take about ten minutes. The mini-boarding house is a three-story building of a cottage type, painted in a pleasant pink color. The highlight of the guest house is a picturesque spacious courtyard with gazebos, alleys and a chic garden with fruit trees.





What rest without barbecue? Its preparation is a favorite pastime of many vacationers who come to Adler. Guest house "Valentina" will provide its guests with this opportunity, because the courtyard has a well-equipped barbecue area.

For those who wish to cook their own meals, the guest house has an equipped shared kitchen. Kitchen utensils are provided in full.

If you want to relax in comfortable conditions at affordable prices, then you should visit the guest house "Valentina" (Adler, Lenina, 286.)

Accommodation for vacationers

The hotel has double and triple rooms of economy class and rooms with standard amenities, designed for 2, 3, 4 people who visited Adler.

Guest house Valentina offers the following economy class rooms. They are small but comfortable. Each room has a TV, modern comfortable furniture, a fan. There are no separate amenities in such rooms, they are common on the floor for several rooms, but at the same time there are few such rooms on the floor, and no one experiences discomfort. This accommodation option is perfect for youth holidays with friends or for large families. They are located on the ground floor of the building.

Standard rooms, in addition to a TV, are all equipped with air conditioning and a refrigerator. Toilet and shower are for individual use.

The prices for holidays in Adler depend on the season and room category. Guest house "Valentina" offers the following rates:

  • Economy for 2 people - from 700 to 1000 rubles per room.
  • Economy for 3 people - from 1100 to 1500 rubles per room per day.
  • Rooms with amenities for two people - from 800 to 1600 rubles.
  • Standard for three - from 1000 to 1800 rubles per day.
  • Quadruple rooms with amenities - 1600-2200 rubles per room per night.
