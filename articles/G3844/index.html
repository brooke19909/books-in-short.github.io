Master classes: how to knit a tunic for women and little fashionistas

Tunic is a universal thing. It can be worn as beachwear or as an office outfit. With the help of such a little thing, you can easily create many stylish sets yourself.

Tunic. What is its plus?

This type of clothing has recently entered the wardrobe of young and adult fashionistas. The tunic is ideal for relaxing, at home, walking, it can be worn even in the office. And it is combined with clothes of different styles. Many people wear it with leggings, a skirt, shorts or just on the beach. The tunic can be short, long or knee-deep. This item comes in a variety of sleeve lengths and necklines. For sewing use elastane, cotton, knitwear.

Knitted tunics came into fashion . If you decide to independently connect this thing, then decide which model you want. There are many schemes. With the help of them, you can learn how to knit a tunic. We offer to start with lace. So, we knit with needles tunics for women and little girls with their own hands. Prepare everything you need for this and get started.

How to knit a tunic (female)

For knitting, you will need four hundred grams of acrylic yarn, knitting needles No. 5 and hook No. 4. Start knitting from the back. Dial 146 loops. Knit 133 rows, alternating the pattern: one edge, twelve front loops, one edge and twelve front loops, and so on to the end. To narrow the tunic, make a decrease on both sides, that is, in every tenth row, one loop five times lower. Further to the 148th row, dial the product with a garter stitch. And then, up to 186, alternate the following loops - one edge and eight purl. From 187 to 196 rows, dial loops with a garter stitch.

To make the neck, it is necessary in 197 row to divide the work into two equal parts.

Dial each side separately. Leave one for now and knit the other. Do not forget to close one loop on each side in each second row, and so on five times. Then close all the loops. Do the same with the second part.

Perform the front part as well as the back. Start assembling the product. Perform shoulder and side seams with a mattress seam. Enter the needle one at a time, first from the edge of one part, then from the edge of another.

Such a thing is suitable for slim women with a size of 44.

Knitted tunic (children)

To make this outfit with your own hands, you will need yarn (one hundred grams), knitting needles No. 3 and a hook No. 3. Dial a chain of 72 loops. Knit in front according to the following pattern with one cloth. The first two rows - the front surface. The fourth is the wrong one. Alternate knitting of this type from the fifth to ninth rows fifteen times. Then dial one loop on the front and the wrong side, alternate them to the end of the row. Knit like this ten more times. Then close eight loops on both sides for the armholes. Continue to knit the tunic with an elastic band (alternating the back and front loops). After 34 rows, close the central twelve loops to cut the neck.

Reduce the side loops in every second row: three - once, then two - once, one - two times. Continue tying the remaining loops with an elastic band, and so eighteen rows. Knit close. The back fits in the same way.

To assemble the tunic, crochet the side and shoulder seams. Unpack the product.

Such an outfit will be at the time of a one-year-old baby. Knitting a tunic is easy. And most importantly, it looks beautiful.

A tunic is a product that girls and adult women will wear with pleasure. If you master the technique of knitting, you can make unique patterns. The main thing is to show your imagination and your skill.
