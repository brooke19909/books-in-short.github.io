Blue Star Hotel 4 * (Alanya): photos and reviews of tourists

At the end of the summer season, our compatriots are in a hurry to share their impressions of the past vacation. Some tourists talk about their holidays at foreign resorts with friends and colleagues, while others post reviews on the Internet to help other travelers. Judging by the comments, Turkey can be considered the most popular destination in the past holiday season, and Alanya is the most popular resort. There is absolutely everything that is required for a good holiday - luxurious beaches, beautiful hotels, excellent service and an interesting cultural program. If you are interested in a vacation in Turkey, Blue Star Hotel 4 * (Alanya) can be a great option for you. This hotel received numerous positive comments from Russian tourists, so our article today is dedicated to him. You can find out all the features of staying at Blue Star Hotel 4 * (Alanya) and decide how much a tour to this hotel complex suits you. In many sections of the article, we will refer to the reviews of our compatriots posted on the Internet to give readers the most reliable information.





Blue Star Hotel 4 * (Alanya): hotel description

The hotel complex was erected in the mid-nineties of the twentieth century. Initially, he positioned himself as a family hotel for a calm and measured rest. Twelve years ago, repair work was carried out in residential buildings and on the territory. Reviews of Blue Star Hotel 4 * (Alanya) often indicate that the hotel is well equipped and stands out with fresh repairs. This provides him with continued popularity among Russian and German tourists.

The hotel complex consists of two residential buildings located next to each other. One consists of nine floors, and the second of five. Both residential blocks provide accommodation in two hundred and eighty rooms. Most of them are standards, the rest are designed for couples.

Blue Star Hotel 4 * (Alanya) is located on the second coastline, about six hundred meters from the beach of hotel guests.

Hotel location

Located Blue Star Hotel 4 * in Alanya. Alanya Center is the place from where it is very easy to go on an exciting journey to explore Turkey. Hotel guests say that most interesting excursions organized themselves. They especially liked wandering around the Old Town. Typically, such an excursion costs at least thirty-five dollars, and outside the group you can explore the ruins for free.

Keep in mind that you will have to travel from the international airport to the hotel for at least three hours, taking into account arrivals at other hotel complexes. On average, about one hundred and thirty kilometers separate the airport terminal from the Blue Star Hotel 4 * (Alanya). In Turkey, Alanya and other resort areas are located at a decent distance from Antalya, where the country's main air gate is located, so every tourist should be ready for a long trip to the hotel.





Brief description of the beach

Guests of Blue Star Hotel 4 * (Alanya) give a description of the beach area in their reviews quite often. Of course, some guests do not particularly like the fact that it takes about ten minutes to go to the sea in the heat. However, other guests take this prospect with enthusiasm and talk about the fact that along the way you can purchase various souvenirs or goodies sold in the shops of local merchants.

If you do not want to go to the beach on foot, then use the free shuttle service. This service is quite popular. On the beach area owned by Blue Star Hotel 4 * (Alanya), there is a bar where guests can get free drinks and snacks. The institution starts working at ten in the morning.

At the entrance to the water there are plates, so you should be very careful not to slip on them. Tourists claim that if you walk along the wide beach area, you can get to the famous fortress and climb its walls with the funicular. In this part of the coast, the beach is the most beautiful.





Infrastructure

The hotel has two swimming pools with slides, this was noted by almost all guests of the Blue Star Hotel 4 * (Alanya). Russian tourists go to Turkey mainly with children, so it is extremely important for them that the hotel has slides, so loved by small guests.

Guests eat in the main restaurant, however, it is the only one in the hotel. In addition to it, there are four more bars for guests.

Guests who cannot leave their affairs for a week highly appreciate the conference room for one hundred people equipped in the hotel. It usually hosts business meetings, celebrations, and guests also have access to office equipment. However, all services related to events are paid.

The reception is open around the clock, but the reception staff do not always understand Russian well. Keep in mind that most of the negative reviews about the Blue Star Hotel 4 * (Alanya) concern the staff who are on duty at the reception. Often guests are faced with indifference to their requests, rudeness and attempts to settle tourists in a room of a lower category than indicated in the voucher. However, with some persistence, all problems can be solved quite easily. Often all the controversial issues helps to resolve the tour operator.

The main building houses a spa and hairdresser. Nearby you can exchange currency, although many travelers do not advise doing this. Indeed, almost everywhere in Turkey you can pay with dollars and euros.

Also in the hotel there is a small shop where you can buy souvenirs and daily necessities. Prices are low, in some cases you can even bargain with sellers.

Facilities and service

Hotel service corresponds to four stars, tourists often write about this. But the main disadvantage they consider weak Wi-Fi. This causes a lot of problems for people who work remotely and daily need the Internet. Even for the extra money in the hotel it is impossible to connect to a good network.

If you want to get from the airport to the hotel complex faster than all other tourists, then arrange an individual transfer with the hotel administration. In the same way, you can leave for the airport after the rest. However, do not forget that this service is paid extra.

If you have any health problems, be sure to consult a doctor who is at his workplace twenty-four hours a day. But the first examination of the doctor will be paid.

Many guests are very pleased to use the laundry facilities. In one day, things will be washed, ironed and returned to the tourists' room.

Those guests who want to get acquainted with the sights of the resort can contact the hotel‚Äôs tour desk. Here, experts will find you a good and inexpensive tour, taking into account all your wishes.

Near the hotel complex there is free parking. You cannot reserve a place on it in advance.

Helpful information

Guests usually check in from two in the afternoon. If you arrive at the hotel early in the morning and your apartments are not ready yet, then the employees will not be able to complete all the documents before the due date. However, in the process of waiting, vacationers will be able to use all infrastructure facilities and have a meal in the restaurant.

Eviction from the room takes place before twelve o'clock in the afternoon, by this time it is necessary to take out your things to the hall and pay all bills.

It is impossible to stay at the hotel with pets, the administration prohibits such accommodation. This rule is indicated on the hotel website.





Hotel accommodation with children

In almost every room, you can install an extra bed for a child or adult. But keep in mind that this request should be voiced at the stage of acquiring a tourist permit.

If your child is not yet ten years old, and you are ready to place him on the bed in the room, then there will be no additional charge for accommodation.

Children under two years old can be accommodated in extra beds for free. For older children will need to pay an amount in the amount of seventy percent of the total cost of living.

Fun for kids

Since the hotel has a lot of families with children of different ages, the topic of infrastructure for the child becomes a natural concern for tourists.

Photos of Blue Star Hotel 4 * (Alanya), posted on different sites on the Internet, allow you to get an idea of ‚Äã‚Äãhow the kids can occupy themselves. Usually they prefer a special pool with slides. Here, small guests can spend time from morning to late evening.

Lovers of active games visit the playground or mini club. Occupation here will be found for every toddler and older child. The club can leave children from four to twelve years old.

The hotel does not provide for a children's menu, but among the variety of dishes you can always pick up something to feed the baby. At the request of vacationers in the restaurant can be provided highchairs.

Entertaining activities

The hotel has a very positive attitude towards family vacationers, so it tries to provide guests with a large amount of entertainment for every taste. Free of charge, guests can use volleyball and basketball courts, play billiards and table tennis. A gym is available for all guests and aerobics classes are held daily. Some guests are happy to rent bicycles and take evening walks through the streets of the city.

The animation team also works well at the hotel. At nine o'clock in the evening, a show program begins, in which all guests often take part.

For a surcharge, guests have access to a sauna and massage parlor. The guests leave positive feedback about the work of spa specialists.





Blue Star Hotel 4 * (Alanya): room description

The hotel has two hundred and eighty rooms. Basically, they differ in the size of the rooms, the equipment of the apartments is identical. The rooms are decorated quite simply, they have a TV that catches only one Russian channel, air conditioning, mini fridge and safe. Using the safe is paid, a day costs a little more than one dollar. Also at the reception it will be necessary to leave ten dollars as a deposit. When leaving the hotel, money is returned.

When you check in the room will be a bottle of drinking water. In the future, its supplies are not replenished, but the guests get out of the situation by pouring water from the coolers.

In the bathroom there is only a hairdryer, but it has a small capacity, so guests prefer to use their household appliances brought from home. Of the toiletries in the bathroom there is only liquid soap, so take care of the cosmetics yourself.

Room cleaning is done once every two to three days. By its quality, guests have no complaints.





Food concept

The hotel complex operates on an all-inclusive basis. Almost around the clock, guests have access to food, and its quality and variety, according to vacationers, deserves even ten points. Most of the dishes relate to European cuisine, so guests can always choose something to feed the child.

Judging by the guest reviews, the waiters in the restaurant are very fast. They instantly clean the empty plates, sweep the crumbs from the tablecloth and bring drinks. The guests of Blue Star Hotel 4 * (Alanya) write in the comments that every day there were numerous meat and vegetable dishes on the tables, for breakfast the chef fries an omelet with different toppings, and for dinner there is always one or two dishes cooked on the grill. A large number of enthusiastic reviews are addressed to confectioners, their creations are sorted out by guests almost instantly.





To summarize

Despite some shortcomings, most guests recommend this hotel to other tourists. It fully complies with the declared status and provides vacationers with a high level of comfort. Therefore, if you are already planning your next vacation, then you can safely choose Blue Star Hotel 4 *.
