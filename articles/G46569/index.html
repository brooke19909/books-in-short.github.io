Corn: Euroset credit cards

Recently, credit cards have become increasingly popular. Euroset did not lag behind its competitors and released its own plastic called Corn. This card is the product of one of the most famous payment systems MasterCard, which allows you to make purchases with great convenience in stores and other points of sale where you can pay with a credit card.



Credit card "Euroset": how to get

If you urgently need money to pay your bills or buy a new thing, but still far from a salary, you can always solve this problem using credit cards. Euroset offers to issue a loan to Corn, simply by contacting one of its 2,500 branches located in any corner of the Russian Federation. The whole procedure will take only 30-40 minutes, while the borrower is not required to provide a guarantee or pledge. To draw up a card, a client needs to have only a passport and a valid plastic card of any bank. After approval of the loan, cash can be obtained at the cash desk or at any ATM. However, you can simply continue to pay by credit card and receive small pleasant bonuses from the Euroset company.



Credit card. terms of Use

The Corn service provides a very convenient way to pay bills at any shopping center or restaurant where there is a machine for cashless payments. In addition, the owner of the plastic card receives a nice bonus in the form of a 1% receipt of all money spent back to his account. This function is called Cash Back, and it works in almost all stores in the country. Naturally, you can also use credit cards to pay for utilities, the Internet and other things. Euroset will accrue bonus points to the account for each spending made with the Corn card, however, when using it, the following conditions must be met:

  • maximum loan amount - 300 thousand rubles;
  • interest rate is individual in each particular case and may vary in the range from 24.9 to 79.9% per annum;
  • grace period - 55 days;
  • the penalty for stopping loan payments is 750 rubles;
  • payment period - 25 days;
  • minimum loan payment - 5% of the total debt amount;
  • for payments made by a third party, the company may request an additional commission.
