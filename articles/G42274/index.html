How to borrow on "Motive" for 5 days?

The telecom operator Motiv appeared on the market back in 1996 and began its work under the Beeline brand. After the federal company in 2002 decided to independently advance in the markets of the region, the company was forced to create its own brand. As a result, the company settled on the name "Motive".

Today, the operator provides services in four regions: Sverdlovsk and Kurgan Regions, Khanty-Mansiysk and Yamalo-Nenets Autonomous Districts. In the Perm Territory, a subsidiary of the group providing local wired communication services operates. The main focus of the Motive is the development of cellular radiotelephone communications. The number of subscribers, according to the company, exceeds 2 million people.

Why borrow money from an operator?
