Calls to Crimea from Ukraine and vice versa are still being made. So, first there are three basic characters: +38. This is followed by the operator code. Changes in urban communications

The Ministry of Communications, as it became known, developed and sent to the public a draft order, according to which residents of the Crimea and the hero city of Sevastopol will soon receive Russian fixed-line telephone codes. Accordingly, the question of how to call the Crimea began to be decided almost immediately after its occurrence. This document implies certain changes to the numbering plan of the Russian Federation. Thus, the long-distance code (digits - 365) is allocated in order to use it in telephone wire communication networks on the territory of the peninsula. The code with numbers 869 will be applied in Sevastopol. Cellular Changes

If we talk about how to call to Crimea on a mobile phone, then here is a slightly different situation. Experts say that there is no need to allocate any additional mobile codes, since they have sufficient numbering capacity. But absolutely all companies that previously worked in Ukraine but now find themselves in Russia will need to renew their licenses, which will take some time.





Documentation

It is worth noting, speaking about how to call the Crimea, that until the first of January next year, the so-called transitional period operates on the peninsula. And this means that operators can still work under Ukrainian licenses that currently exist. So far, only the operator Rostelecom has started its work in Crimea - directly through a subsidiary called Miranda Media. Until the moment the frequencies appeared on the peninsula, specialists carefully prepared the frequency-territorial plan. This information was reported by Vadim Ampelonsky, who is the press secretary of Roskomnadzor. The preparation was completed in early June, after which the issuance of permits immediately began. The specialist also made a reservation that the territorial administration of Roskomnadzor was being formed in Crimea. In the capital of Russia, they are now preparing the necessary documents necessary in order to legalize a legal entity or create it. In general, in the cities of Crimea, as well as in Sevastopol, it is already possible to purchase a SIM card, the only problem that may arise is long lines. By the way, for one passport, which you must have with you, you can buy several cards.
