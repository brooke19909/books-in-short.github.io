The best receiver "Denon": reviews

Movie lovers will not be able to get past a high-quality receiver. This device provides gorgeous multi-channel sound in movie mode. An important plus is the fact that most of these devices do not hit the dirt in the face when working in stereo as an amplifier. Now on the market there are many different devices of this type. But the Denon receiver looks ideal in terms of price-quality ratio. Moreover, it does not matter which model is in front of us. Even budget devices from this manufacturer are capable of producing high-quality sound that will please both audiophiles and cinema lovers. We will analyze the best models in more detail. But first, a few words about the manufacturer.

About Denon

It is worth noting immediately that the prototype of the modern Denon company appeared in Japan already in 1910. And the company was created by no means a Japanese (which is absolutely unbelievable), but an American. Initially, the company was engaged in the release of records with classical music. In 1939, the Japanese bought the same profitable production and rebranded it. Since then, the company began to be called Denon. And she began to produce phonographs, record players and devices for recording them. The largest radio stations in the world lined up for Denon products. The first receiver was released in the 90s of the twentieth century. Since then, the Denon receivers, reviews of which we will analyze a little lower, have become a real bestseller.

It was Denon that released the world's first CD player. But everyone quickly forgot about it somehow. The palm went to such giants as Sony, Philips, Pioneer and so on. Nevertheless, true connoisseurs of quality sound prefer the Denon technique. The equipment from this manufacturer is able to satisfy even audiophile requests. However, we were distracted. Consider the devices from this manufacturer and select the best Denon receiver for home use. Moreover, his abilities will be taken into account not only in terms of multi-channel sound, but also in stereo mode capabilities.

Denon AVR-X520BT

The youngest model in the line of receivers from Denon. However, this particular model boasts support for wireless interfaces and decent sound quality in both multi-channel and stereo modes. The Denon 520 receiver has an output power of 90 watts with a resistance of 6 ohms and 70 watts with a resistance of 8 ohms. For a budget device, this is good power. The lower limit of the frequency range is 10 hertz, the upper one is 100,000 hertz. Also pretty good. You can easily connect 5.1 speakers to the unit. Plus there is a special output for an additional subwoofer. Although with good speakers, a separate low-frequency is not needed at all. The receiver can work with Dolby Digital, DTS-X, DTS HD Audio and other popular audio formats. At the same time, the device copes with the reproduction of classical musical compositions in stereo mode.

Receiver Features

The receiver "Denon AVR-X520T" has a huge number of different connectors for connecting various equipment. Along with standard coaxial or optical interfaces, HDMI and USB are also available. This enhances the functionality of the device. There is also a Bluetooth 2.0 transmitter, which allows you to play music from a smartphone or laptop wirelessly. However, the quality of such playback leaves much to be desired, since there is no AptX profile. Nevertheless, this receiver is a modern and multifunctional device for high-quality sound reproduction. The device can adapt to the particular layout of the room and evens out the sound in all directions thanks to the spatial microphone used to calibrate the channels.

Denon AVR-X1300W

And this is a model from the middle price category. It is more functional than the previous one. And the specific power is higher. The Denon 1300 receiver provides 120 watts with a resistance of 6 ohms and 80 watts with 8 ohms. Excellent performance. You can connect a 7.1 speaker system and another additional subwoofer to the receiver. This allows you to achieve maximum spatial sound. The range of frequencies supported by the receiver is from 10 Hz to 100 kHz. Very good. You can also connect an additional speaker system from another room wirelessly to the receiver. The sound quality will not suffer, since the asset of this device has a high-quality AptX profile for Bluetooth. The device also has a Wi-Fi transmitter, which made it possible to support AirPlay technology.

Receiver Features

It is worth noting that this receiver "Denon" perfectly shows itself when working in stereo mode. Sound quality is virtually indistinguishable from expensive audiophile amp models. Modern processing methods and powerful processors have made this possible. To calibrate the speakers , a special spatial microphone is used, which comes with the receiver. In general, "Denon AVR-X1300W" is a great mid-range option for those who want to not only watch movies with high-quality spatial sound, but also listen to music in maximum quality.

Denon AVR-X2200W

Model from the premium segment. The Denon 2200 receiver belongs to the best representatives of its class. It can provide power from 95 to 125 watts with different resistance. Also, the range of reproducible frequencies ranges from 10 Hz to 100 kHz. But the main difference between the model and all the previous ones is the presence of an integrated DAC with a depth of 24 bits and a sampling frequency of 192 kHz. This ensures the highest sound quality in all conditions. This receiver supports all possible audio formats (DTS X, Dolby Atmos, Dolby Surround, DTS HD and so on). There is no problem playing the soundtrack of movies and music in HD. Quality at the highest level. Therefore, this device belongs to the premium segment.

Receiver Features

The receiver "Denon AVR-X2200W" has at its disposal a huge number of connectors for connecting various equipment. There are both familiar coaxial interfaces and tulips (RCA), as well as very rare HDMI and optical connectors. There is support for Bluetooth and AptX profile. There is also a pretty good Wi-Fi transmitter. And also the Denon stereo receiver supports AirPlay technology. By the way, you can connect a 7.1 speaker system and one additional subwoofer to this device. This will provide the most spatial sound when watching a movie. And there is also a calibration microphone in the kit, which will help you adjust the acoustics in accordance with the parameters of the room. In general, the AVR-X2200W is the best device for home use. It will satisfy both music lovers and cinema fans.

Positive hardware reviews from Denon

If you need to find out how a particular device behaves in real conditions, it is better to refer to the reviews of those who have already purchased this device. Dry numbers of the manufacturer will not say anything sane, but live user comments will make it clear how much the declared characteristics correspond to real ones. So what do users say about a product like the Denon CD receiver? It is worth saying right away that most of the reviews are positive. The owners are very happy to purchase. They note that they have not heard such high-quality sound yet. Even when using more expensive devices. Of course, users say that without other components of an appropriate level, all sound quality cannot be obtained. But this is understandable to all sane people.

Also, many users were struck by the wide capabilities of even the most budget models. They stated that other manufacturers definitely do not. They also note the ease of setup of the receiver. The user only needs to press the remote button a couple of times. The rest of the device will do it automatically. The owners of these receivers are also impressed with the wireless capabilities of the devices. Not all (even more expensive models) from other manufacturers are able to receive music through the air. And at Denon, please. Such multifunctionality and versatility makes these devices almost an ideal option based on the ratio of "price-quality".

Negative reviews about the equipment "Denon"

However, there are those who categorically did not like the Denon receivers. As a rule, negative comments belong to terry audiophiles, fans of analog stereo sound. They sincerely believe that digital technology only degrades sound. These comrades, riding engineering, consider tube amplifiers. Therefore, receivers for them have no value. And yet they buy them. Probably just to scold the sound quality. And this is despite the fact that even if there are distortions from the working processor of the receiver, they still can not be heard with the naked ear. Well, not snobs? In general, these citizens complain about the lack of stereo sound quality. While most users do not feel anything. Do not pay attention to the comments of such snobs. Denon receivers provide high quality sound in any mode. And this is an indisputable fact.

Conclusion

So, the most popular and interesting Denon receivers were considered above. These devices provide high quality sound in stereo mode, the maximum spatial sound when watching a movie and have a whole range of additional options. Just superb devices for home use.
