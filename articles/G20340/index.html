Maxholiday Hotels Belek ex Vera Mare (Turkey, Belek, Bogazkent): description and reviews

Maxholiday Hotels Belek ex Vera Mare 5 * is a relatively new hotel that offers modern conditions for tourists from different regions of the world. Here, as noted by travelers, there is a rather pleasant pricing policy that allows you to visit this place at least every year without much damage to the family budget. The average cost of a weekly trip for two here is about 40-45 thousand rubles.

general information

Maxholiday Hotels Belek ex Vera Mare was recently built - in 2008. From that moment on, small renovation works were carried out on its territory, during which the interior was improved and the technical equipment of the rooms was improved. One of the largest renovations was held in 2012.

On its territory of 14,000 square meters. m. the hotel has four modern buildings, in which the number of rooms is concentrated. They also have several places for recreation and entertainment. In the open area there are several sites for active pastime of tourists. Guests who visit this place in the midst of the spa period are delighted with the gardens that are available at the Maxholiday Hotels Belek ex Vera Mare.



Location

The hotel is located in one of the most famous regions of Turkey - Belek. Here you can arrange a relatively quiet and relaxing holiday, which will take place without too much noise. That is why this place is often chosen for a family trip, as well as for traveling with children. The central part of this region is 7 kilometers from the location of Maxholiday Hotels Belek ex Vera Mare Resort.

The nearest air traffic point is 45 km from this place - in the large resort town of Antalya. It is from here that every guest at the time of arrival is picked up by a comfortable car, which the hotel sends as part of the provision of a transfer service.



Rooms

The Maxholiday Hotels Belek ex Vera Mare offers 289 apartments located in its buildings. All of them are divided into two classes: standard and family.

Each room of the hotel has a modern built-in climatic equipment that allows you to adjust the favorable temperature conditions in the rooms individually, based on the preferences of the guest.

In addition, the apartments are equipped with a bathroom, which includes modern plumbing, as well as bathrobes, slippers and large bath towels, issued to guests at the time of arrival in the room.

Standard

Standard apartments are equipped with the basic furniture set, which provides accommodation for three guests. There is a large double bed and one single bed, between them there are small cabinets where there are lighting fixtures. Opposite the places for sleeping, there is a large wardrobe, on the surface of which there is a plasma TV that broadcasts a huge list of satellite channels. As a decor on the walls there is a large mirror and several lighting fixtures.

The rooms have a seating area, which is represented by a combination of a soft sofa and a wooden table.



Family

In their reviews of Maxholiday Hotels Belek ex Vera Mare, guests often say that the hotel is perfect for families with all their families, even if children are present. For this category of guests a place to relax creates the most comfortable living conditions. One of them is the presence of large two-room rooms, divided into two bedrooms.

In each room there are two berths, as well as small cabinets for small personal items. One of the rooms is better equipped from a technical point of view - it is equipped with a TV, safe and minibar. In addition, on a paid basis from the apartments you can make calls on the international line - this service is paid separately.

If desired, guests can sit in a small corner for relaxation. There is also access to a balcony from the room - from it you can admire beautiful views of the mountains, gardens and, of course, the sea.



Conditions for children

Many tourist comments state that Maxholiday Hotels Belek ex Vera Mare provides all the conditions necessary for a comfortable stay for children and their parents. First of all, they are due to the possibility of free cots in the room. Tourists are also very pleased with the availability of a stroller rental service - it is provided daily, the cost is about $ 5 per day.

In the open area there are several places for active pastime of children. One of them is an open area, which is located near the aquazone. There is also a small pool, which is built so that children can stay in it as safely as possible.

If you need to leave the child for a while, parents can use the paid babysitting services, which are paid hourly. There is also a mini club where animators will be with the kids.

Entertainment

Maxholiday Hotels Belek ex Vera Mare provides a range of facilities designed to entertain guests on their premises. One of them is fun programs that are held in the evening. Very often, in hotel reviews, guests talk about the possibilities of having fun during their period.

In the daytime, guests can take part in team games and sporting events held in the aquazone.

Throughout the rest period, guests are entertained by a team of animators consisting of 12 people. As guests from Russia note, a pleasant point is that some of them are fluent in Russian.

Nutrition

The hotel pays special attention to the provision of food for guests. To this end, several catering establishments are presented in its building, where excellent dishes prepared in different styles are served.

Three restaurants serving Italian, Turkish and also fish dishes are most popular here. A buffet is organized here every day, for which guests staying on packages that include the all-inclusive concept are invited. Before visiting any of them, it is necessary to make a preliminary reservation of the table.

There are also five bars at Maxholiday Hotels Belek ex Vera Mare, which offer unique menus, which include a wide range of snacks, salads, desserts and, of course, a variety of drinks.



SPA

SPA services, which are provided in the hotel center, are paid. The description of Maxholiday Hotels Belek ex Vera Mare states that they are provided with a thermal complex consisting of a hammam and a sauna. The center is separately presented, where cosmetologists, a hairdresser and a manicurist provide services. The spa also has a massage room, in which, according to vacationers, professional masters can easily immerse any client in a state of complete relaxation.

Sport

Fans of an active lifestyle can not interrupt their ongoing workouts while staying at the hotel. For this, a room has been created here, where a small number of simulators are presented, which are designed to work on different muscle groups. You can visit it only for a fee.

Entrance to the sports grounds in the gardens, which are located in the open area, is completely free for guests.



Aquazone

Some photos of Maxholiday Hotels Belek ex Vera Mare show the huge aquazone available at the hotel. Within its borders, guests of different age categories like to relax.

Beach

On the coastline, the hotel has its own plot, which offers a wonderful vacation for its guests, in a clean and beautiful environment. Here, as tourists note, an excellent descent into the sea and warm water in the coastal part. Such conditions are quite safe for children.



Vacationers have the opportunity to sit on free sun loungers under large umbrellas that perfectly protect from the sun. Here you can also have fun by paying for water sports. Some travelers advise renting a yacht or boat for a short time and go aboard to enjoy the open sea and beautiful coastal landscapes.
