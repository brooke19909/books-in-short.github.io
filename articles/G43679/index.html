Solimar Ruby Hotel 4 * (Greece, Crete, Malia): description and reviews

One of the popular tourist destinations is Greece. In the north of Crete off the coast of the Aegean Sea is located the resort town of Malia. Travelers are attracted by natural beauties, clear waters, incendiary clubs and historical sites. The resort positions itself as a family: here everyone will find something to their liking.

The Solimar Ruby Hotel 4 * hotel complex (Crete, Heraklion) offers city guests comfortable accommodation, rich leisure and high quality service. He was awarded four stars, which in the opinion of experienced, is fully justified due to its many advantages.





General characteristics of the hotel

The institution was opened at the end of the last millennium in 1997. Many years of experience in the development of hotel business have borne fruit: over the years, improving service and renovations, the last of which was carried out in 2013, optimal conditions have been created for a comfortable stay of guests. On the compact territory is the main two-story building with an additional one-story Annex building, which is located 60 meters from it. Free areas are landscaped: trimmed shrubs, fragrant flowers and tropical palm trees delight the guests.

To check in after a tiring flight, it is necessary to master the move of 32 kilometers, which will take less than an hour. Holidays with pets will not be possible, as their accommodation in the apartment is strictly prohibited by the rules. Smoking is allowed only in designated areas. The distance to the sea is 600 meters. Nearby are archaeological sites and a palace.

Upon arrival, all guests wear a hypoallergenic bracelet that must be worn throughout the stay.

Nutrition

Solimar Ruby Hotel 4 * receives a positive plan for catering. Like most high-end hotels, the one we are considering adheres to the All-Inclusive concept. Three meals a day are organized within the walls of the main restaurant. Guests are invited to enjoy Mediterranean and traditional cuisines served in buffet style. Beach

The urban beach area has a sand and pebble coating. The entrance to the water is gentle, in some places there are stones. The infrastructure is at a decent level: there are changing cabins, a toilet and a shower. Towels are dispensed on a deposit basis. The hot sun will allow each vacationer to find the most beautiful dark skin color. Travelers are incredibly pleased with the beautiful mountain landscapes, whose admiration is bewitching.

Pastimes of children and adults

For active entertainment for young guests, there is an outdoor playground. The children are led into unprecedented enthusiasm by riding downhill, on a swing, climbing stairs. You can spend the evening in the TV room watching an interesting movie with your hotel neighbors.
