Perm recreation centers - where you can relax in comfort?

Every modern person living in the city needs at least sometimes to be distracted from the bustle of everyday life, going out into the countryside. After all, this is a great way to replenish the energy spent during the workday. For a country holiday, you can choose fishing, hiking, rafting on the river or a picnic. However, some people cannot live without comfortable conditions, and spending the night in a tent get even more tired. It is for them that Perm recreation centers offer their services. Here you will get the opportunity to enjoy the beauty and harmony of the Ural nature, while taking advantage of all the benefits of civilization.

Offers its guests a wonderful recreation center Perm. "Expanse" is one of the most popular accommodation options for lovers of nature and comfortable conditions. This base is located near the village of Trinity, at a distance of 30 km from Perm. The Sylva River flows nearby. In winter, up to 400 people can live in the base, in summer up to 200. There are all conditions for family and corporate recreation.

Asphalt paths and residential buildings are located throughout the recreation center (Perm). "Expanse" takes care of the comfortable rest of each of its guests. There are also wooden arbors, from which a magnificent panorama opens. Fishing, volleyball and paintball facilities are available. In summer, vacationers can rent a jet ski, a boat, a boat, and in winter a snow scooter. There is also a tubing track at the base, where adults and children can have fun riding "cheesecakes." Accommodation and meals in the "Expanse"

"Razdolye" is a recreation center (Perm, Trinity), where tourists, in addition to numerous entertainments and magnificent panoramas, are also offered excellent conditions for accommodation and meals. So, for guests staying at the base there are two buildings. In one of them are located the rooms of the class "luxury" and "junior suite", and in the second - "economy". The suites and junior suites are equipped with a bathroom with a toilet, and the economy rooms offer amenities in the hallway. In addition, tourists can choose a separate house for 15 people to stay, which is very convenient for a large company that wants to live in one place.





In the new building there is a restaurant with 200 seats, where guests of the recreation center can taste delicious dishes and drinks. In the building with economy rooms there is a cafe-bar that seats up to 50 people and offers all kinds of drinks and hookah. There is also a summer dining room and barbecue facilities on the territory of Razdolye, which is especially enjoyed by lovers of barbecue and barbecue.

The recreation center "The Seagull"

In the village of Ilyinsky there is a boarding house "Chaika" (recreation center). Perm has many camp sites on its territory, but this is one of the most versatile holiday destinations. Here you can both calmly contemplate nature and arrange noisy parties, corporate events or weddings. All rooms are characterized by comfortable living conditions. There is also one that is specially equipped for honeymooners. At the same time in the residential building of the base can accommodate up to 90 people. On the territory there is a cafe open all day with two halls. There is also a terrace that can accommodate up to 100 people.

Cooking on a bonfire or grill for lovers of barbecue and a picnic in the fresh air with great views is another opportunity that the Seagull provides. The recreation center (Perm) will allow you to remember the rest in the best colors. From the platform with barbecue there is an entrance to the sauna. And in it are a swimming pool, a kitchen and a large lounge.

Fans of active pastime can play football or paintball. On the territory of the recreation center, animators organize all kinds of events, including sports games, contests, competitions, etc.





Zhebrai is an excellent recreation center at any time of the year

At a distance of 30 km from Perm is the recreation center "Gebrey". In winter, all lovers of alpine skiing come here, and in the summer, people who like swimming in the river and a gorgeous beach like to spend time here.

For living, the Zhebrei boarding house, like many other recreation centers in Perm, offers rooms in the main buildings or separate houses. In any case, tourists can use household appliances, a bathroom, hot and cold water. And there is also the opportunity to take a steam bath in a real Russian bath! At the same time, no more than 30 people can live at the base, which is especially liked by lovers of a calm and measured rest.

Base of rest "Sport"

On the shore of Sylva there is a recreation center "Sport". Perm is located just a few kilometers from here, which is very convenient if you suddenly need something in the city. The room base is represented by ten four-bed log houses, each of which is equipped with a bathroom, two separate rooms and a kitchenette. Guests of the recreation center can eat on their own, preparing food for themselves in the kitchen, or centrally in a cafe located on the territory of the "Sport". In the case of centralized meals, half or full board can be ordered .

Especially satisfied are the tourists who spent their summer holidays at this camp site. Indeed, in the warmer months you can play football on a specially equipped field, swim in a pond, play tennis, ride catamarans and boats, as well as fish. There is a bathhouse and several banquet halls suitable for organizing any event for guests of the Sport recreation center.





The recreation center "Polytechnic"

On the territory of the village of Skobelevka there is a recreation center "Polytechnic". Perm is 42 km from here. However, vacationers here rarely need to go to the city, because the Polytechnic offers all the conditions for living and entertainment. The Volga Riverbank is literally a 10-minute walk from the camp site. For accommodation, guests of the Polytechnic are offered summer houses, two-story log houses for a different number of people, as well as 2 buildings with standard rooms. All rooms are equipped with comfortable beds, bedside tables and wardrobes. Showers and toilets are on the floor. Log houses are equipped with all the amenities inside and are beautifully furnished, however, as a rule, large companies move into them.
