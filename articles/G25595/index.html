Hotel Williams Beach Retreat 2 *: overview, description and reviews

Endless sandy beaches on the shores of the Indian Ocean, clear turquoise water, sunny cloudless weather, richness of flora and fauna, ancient cultural monuments - all this and much more makes the Indian state of Goa attractive for tourists from all over the globe. That is why resorts with hotels of various comforts stretched along a long line along the sea: from the richest chalets with pools to economical rooms with limited amenities.

One of the picturesque places in Goa is the village of Colva with an amazing beach of the same name and 2-4 star hotels. A worthy representative of the hotel service in its category was the Williams Beach Retreat 2 * hotel (Colva, South Goa).





Colva, South Goa

In the era of colonization, Colva Beach in the Southern District of Goa was a resting place for the upper strata of Margao. From the luxury and wealth of its inhabitants to our time, only beautiful houses and memorable villas have remained.

With the growth of the tourist flow to Goa, Colva beach is gaining unusual popularity among locals and visitors of India. The coastal infrastructure begins to develop rapidly; hotels and shops began to appear around. brief information

Hotel Williams Beach Retreat 2 * opened its welcoming doors for the first visitors back in 1986. Since that time, thousands of travelers from different parts of our vast planet have repeatedly chosen it as the place of their overnight stay during their stay in Goa.





The hotel is 200 meters from the beach, which is surrounded by countless coconut trees.

Williams Beach Retreat Colva 2 * is focused on beach vacations primarily for adults and families with school-age children.

Location

Hotel Williams Beach Retreat 2 * has an advantageous location in relation to the transport hubs of Goa:

  • Dabolim International Airport is 24 kilometers from the hotel;
  • Kamali railway station, connecting this region with other settlements of the country, is located 10 kilometers;
  • the city is the second largest in the state in Margao, the hotel is 6 kilometers away.

Number of rooms

Hotel Williams Beach Retreat 2 * (South Goa, Colva) is ready to offer its guests 36 standard rooms located in two two-story buildings. The main building has 20 rooms, and the next one by the pool - 16. From the windows of the living quarters you can see the pool, patio or hotel grounds.

The modest size of the rooms in the hotel allows you to install only two beds, so if you need a third guest (adult or child), then he will be provided as an extra bed only a floor mattress, not a bed.





Each room of Williams Beach Retreat 2 * has:

  • one double or two double beds;
  • bedside tables and wardrobe;
  • telephone and TV;
  • private bathroom with shower, washbasin and toilet;
  • balcony.

Some of the premises are equipped with air conditioning, others have only ceiling fans.

There is no safe in the room, but it can be used for an additional fee at the reception.

The price includes housekeeping and a change of bed linen and towels upon request. If the guest needs round-the-clock service, then it can be provided, but for an additional fee.

Hotel Infrastructure

Hotel Williams Beach Retreat 2 * offers its guests not so much entertainment, but there are enough of them to spend time comfortably:

  • a restaurant serving light international dishes for breakfast;
  • pleasant bar, conducive to long friendly conversations;
  • a swimming outdoor pool, sun loungers, mattresses and sun umbrellas around it;
  • theme nights and poolside barbecue;
  • car parking;
  • airport shuttle;
  • currency exchange;
  • laundry;
  • a tennis court and volleyball court are very close to the hotel;
  • medical services;
  • massage;
  • beauty saloon;
  • tour agency.




Colva Beach

The road from the Williams Beach Retreat 2 * hotel to the Colva city beach is not at all long and does not take more than 10 minutes for vacationers. The beach itself is sandy, with a very convenient entrance to the sea. Umbrellas and sun loungers are available for a fee.

On the beach, every vacationer will find something to his liking, and no one really has time to miss.

Here you can play sports on water or land, splash around in the transparent waves of the Indian Ocean or enjoy the touches of the gentle sunshine. Or you can just wander silently along the beach, listening to the sounds of nature, or hire a boat and go to explore the depths of the sea.





Here you can have an excellent shopping experience and have a delicious lunch at local cafes on the coast, tasting truly divine seafood.

Colva Beach has long been loved by locals who come here as soon as possible to escape from the unbearable heat.

Positive hotel reviews

Returning from the resort, many rested and tanned tourists immediately seek to share their impressions and emotions on the pages of tourist portals.

Reading them, you can see that among the popular budget hotel complexes in the Southern District of Goa, the Williams Beach Retreat 2 * hotel really occupies an honorable place at the top of the list. Reviews about him annually appear on the Web. Already have stories of tourists this year. Here are the positive characteristics of the hotel that many travelers have noted:

  • good location relative to the beach and the sea, shops, cafes and fruit shops ;
  • the territory of the hotel, large enough for Goa hotels, is cleaned efficiently;
  • clean and deep pool;
  • everything is old in the room, but it works properly.
  • a good Wi-Fi router signal, allowing some vacationers to use the Internet not only at the reception, but also in their own room;
  • the cuisine of the hotel restaurant is tasty, affordable, and the portions are large;

  • attendants are polite and unobtrusive.




A few negative comments about the hotel

Very modest service and limited facilities, some guests say, are offered by the Williams Beach Retreat 2 * hotel. Their reviews have a negative connotation.

In this regard, you need to understand and be prepared for the following features of a budget holiday:

  • small and simple room;
  • old noisy air conditioning;
  • washed bedding;
  • modest breakfasts: toasts, jam of one kind, butter, juice or tea (coffee);
  • temporary lack of hot water;
  • no fridge.

In general, all vacationers agree that for a budget holiday on Goa Williams Beach Retreat 2 * is suitable for all ninety-nine to one hundred percent.

Features of a stay in Goa

In order for the rest not to be spoiled by severe burns or bites, an gastrointestinal upset, or other sudden illness, it is necessary to observe some simple recommendations:

  1. Do not consume drinks and food purchased from street vendors, do not eat or drink in catering establishments that are questionable from a hygienic point of view. In cafes and restaurants located outside the hotel, it is right to pay attention to the observance of sanitary and hygienic conditions by employees before ordering food, for example, when cooking, washing dishes, and cleaning the premises.
  2. Whenever possible, wash your hands with a disinfectant or treat with an antiseptic.
  3. Wash fruits thoroughly before use, rinse your mouth only with bottled water.
  4. To drink water you also need only bottled water from familiar manufacturers.
  5. Long exposure to the sun can also be dangerous. To prevent skin burns, even in cloudy weather, it is recommended to apply a sunscreen with a high factor.
  6. To avoid close contact with local residents, it is better not to use public transport, preferring taxis or auto rickshaws.
  7. Exercise caution when swimming in the open sea, avoid potentially dangerous encounters with marine representatives of the animal world.
  8. In some hotels of low categories, the appearance of annoying insects is possible, which can only be removed from the rooms by special means.
  9. If you are going on an excursion, be sure to bring along repellents and antihistamines.
