Radio setup: overview, step-by-step instructions, features and recommendations

Correct tuning of the walkie-talkie makes it possible to optimally maintain contact at a given distance, regardless of weather conditions, mobile coverage or terrain. A similar system operates thanks to radio waves on different bands. An essential role in the operation of the device is played by its competent tuning. To do this, it is necessary to study the attached instructions and take into account the features of the device (some of them have a narrow specialization: railway, hunting, car options).





Recommendations

Setting up a walkie-talkie of any type involves adjusting the supply and reception of signals at a specific frequency. Otherwise, amateur or professional modification will work at incomplete power or with interference. The most modern modifications are automatically configured, do not require special adjustment. However, they are more expensive. Other models are divided into portable or stationary devices. The arrangement and adjustment of both options is approximately identical, although there are some peculiarities.

Kenwood

Using the example of this manufacturer, we consider the adjustment and installation of a stationary type walkie-talkie. Initially, it is necessary to fulfill a number of requirements that contribute to increasing the efficiency of subsequent operation of the device, while minimizing the possibility of failure of the transceiver. The station itself is a unit installed in the passenger compartment of the vehicle, equipped with an external antenna. In many ways, tuning the Kenwood walkie-talkie of a stationary type depends on its proper installation.





Basic installation rules:

  • Do not mount the element on the bearing parts, it is best to do this on the body. This solution reduces losses in the transmission and reception of the signal.
  • It is advisable to mount the antenna at the highest point of the body, which increases the quality of reception.
  • The working part of the receiving device should be located at a distance of not less than 0.5 meters from other parallel metal surfaces. This will make it possible to level the absorption of the incoming pulse.
  • The magnetic antenna must be fixed in a stable position, providing the most stable reception and data transmission.

Radio antenna setup

It is advisable to use a professional analyzer to tune the antenna. In its absence, the most often used KVS-meter. All manipulations are performed on a clean and even surface, away from potential interference in the form of concrete or metal structures. In addition, there should not be similar devices nearby whose operating range is 27 MHz.

After connecting the FAC meter, measurements are taken on the grids and channels. This makes it possible to display an extensive reception picture. Next, the calibration of the FAC meter is carried out by switching the toggle switch to the FWD position. On the device itself, the range at number 20 is set in AM mode. Next, the talk button is activated and held. Synchronously rotates the CAL controller clockwise until the walkie-talkie pointer is at the far right (SET).

Further tuning of the walkie-talkie channels is done by switching the FAC meter toggle switch to REF mode. At the same time, the button on the tangent should be kept pressed. Then the information that the device gives out is taken, the minimum of the pic meter is determined, the antenna is adjusted to the required range. In case of overestimating or lowering the required limit, the antenna is made shorter or longer. Control measurements are repeated until the coefficient of picnic coefficient is less than 1.5.





It is worth noting that the lower the FAC, the better the transmitting and receiving power of the pulse. Ideally, the coefficient should be close to unity. However, to achieve such an indicator is almost impossible due to the presence of a variety of interference. If the value exceeds 3, the transmitter cascade and the entire apparatus are likely to fail.

Channels for truckers

It is difficult to imagine modern long-distance freight transportation without walkie-talkies. They help to correct the route, report on road and transport problems. In order for the device to work reliably, it is necessary to correctly configure it. As a rule, it is produced in the following sequence:

  • An antenna is mounted on the central part of the roof, the protective cap is removed, the adjustment screw is fixed to the limit.
  • A pic meter is connected between the walkie-talkie and the antenna.
  • The device turns on, then the long-range mode is set (usually this is channel 15).
  • Press and hold the tangent, at the same time bringing the FAC knob to the SET position.
  • Then the handle is moved to the REF position, the current value of the unit will be displayed on the upper part of the scale. The coefficient using the adjusting screw is adjusted to an indicator not higher than 1.5.
  • The control screw is fixed by means of a lock nut, a protective cap is put on, and information on the settings of the FAC is additionally checked.

When setting up a walkie-talkie of this type, keep in mind that this device belongs to narrow-band devices, and therefore it is better to make settings on the main wave.
