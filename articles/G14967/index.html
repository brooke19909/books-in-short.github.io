A beautiful gift for children and adults - a bouquet of balloons

A bouquet of balloons is a favorite gift for romantic and mischievous girls. An experienced master in newfangled twisting can create such a wonderful present. You can also make a similar bouquet yourself.

Recommendations

In order for the flowers to be performed according to all the canons, it is necessary to remember a few simple rules:



  • when twisting and inflating the balls, even the most experienced craftsmen break the ball, so the nails should be pre-cut and processed with a nail file;
  • round or pear-shaped balls are not intended for twisting;
  • inflate the balls (flowers and stems) is not too tight, after inflating to the desired length, it is worth blowing a little air to simplify twisting;
  • pushing the air of the ball is from the neck, the tail always has room for balancing;
  • for decoration or inscriptions it is worth using markers exclusively based on water (alcohol is too aggressive and can damage the material).

Making a bunch of balloons

Spring and unimaginably touching tulips will become a chic decoration. Therefore, we propose to create a similar bouquet of balloons with your own hands. To do this, you will need:

  • 7 emerald balls for stems;
  • 4 pink and 4 snow-white balls for flowers;
  • 3 pale pink beads for decoration;
  • manual (machine) pump for inflation.

Creature.



  1. We start with a simple blow-up: it is necessary to pump up gradually, not forgetting the free tail. Finished balls (white and pink) must be divided into five equal parts, and twist two bubbles (the inflated part of the bubble that forms between the twists). Then they must be twisted into a loop. We put the third and fourth bubble of the same length to the other two, stretching it into the middle. Tulips for the bouquet are ready.
  2. Next, you need to work out the stems, blowing the required number of emerald balls. At their end, you need to form three small loops.
  3. After this, the stems and buds should be fixed together. To make the bouquet of flowers from balloons more colorful, the stems can be intercepted with a bow of pale pink balls.

Present of roses

No less chic looks a bouquet of air roses - due to the rich and vibrant flowers. Flowers can be presentable red or more noble yellow.

To make a bouquet of balls, you will need:

  • 5 green (or moraine color) balls for stems;
  • 10 yellow flower bulbs;
  • pump.

Creature.

  1. The inflated yellow ball must be lowered a little (release 5 cm) and twist the right edge into the steering wheel.
  2. The ends should be carefully tied so that the knot gets inside the glomerulus, so the future rose will hide the knot behind the petals. Here is the first ball to form the outer rose petals.
  3. The second also needs to be inflated, leaving the tail free. One bubble must be twisted from the free end of the ball, it will be the core of the rose. Around it (the size of a palm) we form two more bubbles, the remainder of the yellow ball is twisted around a bundle of bubbles and cut off the excess.
  4. Next, we make the stem of the rose. To do this, inflate the green ball and release the tail. The first and fundamental bubble will be about 10-15 cm, it blocks the head of the rose from the leaves.
  5. Next, you need to connect the head of the rose and the stem, weaving two yellow balls among themselves. From green we form leaves of a rose, tightening three bubbles between themselves. Next, you need to fix the leaves on the stem, for this it is worth twisting the leaves on the border of the stem bubble.

To make the bouquet of balls more colorful and positive, roses can be created from colorful balls - orange-scarlet petals, red-white and crimson-red look beautiful. The darker ones should be used as external petals, then the flower will play especially sensually.

Conclusion

Now you know how to make beautiful bouquets of balls. We hope that you can create such beauty.
