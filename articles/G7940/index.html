How to make a notebook for large format dolls and Monster High?

Without exception, girls love to play with dolls. Games in a dollhouse or with doll daughters will become even more interesting if you add a variety of household trifles to the playing space. Today we will tell you how to make a notebook for dolls and other stationery.

We make notebooks for playing with large-format dolls





In the children's room of each girl there are large dolls depicting children. With such toys you can play "mother-daughter", but the game "to school" will be no less exciting. How to make a notebook for large dolls ? You will need: white paper and color for covers. It is very convenient to use separate pages from school notebooks, they already have markup. Take a few copybook sheets (4-6 pieces are enough), fold them in half all together. At this point, you can trim the notebook in height if necessary. Now make a cover of colored paper, cardboard or postcards. You can use a beautiful cover from a notebook. How to make a notebook for dolls from the resulting blanks? You just need to stitch them in the middle at the bend. In the end, do not forget to stick a rectangle of white paper on the cover - do you want to write the name and surname of the student?

How to make a notebook for dolls "Monster High" and "Barbie"?



Having learned to make notebooks in which large dolls will be written, it's time to try to make the same accessories for Barbie and Monster High dolls. If desired, notebooks can be made the most real - just use graph paper for their manufacture. The ideal size of one page is 3 x 5 cm, draw the entire sheet into such rectangles. Cut notebook sheets in pairs so that one side to the other rests with the long side. Notebook covers should be 0.5 cm larger on each side than pages. To make one notebook, fold 4 sheets in a stack. Attach a blank for the cover above. There are two ways to make a notebook for Monster High dolls: leaflets can be stapled or stapled. Fold the pages attached to the cover in half to make a booklet. If you did not find graph paper, you can use white sheets, such as printer paper. Glue a small piece of paper on the cover on which you can write the name and title of the item.

Secrets of making folders for notebooks



Now you know how to make a notebook for paper from paper, but where to store school supplies? Try to make a folder out of cardboard. Start with a template. On a paper draft, draw two equal rectangles. Between them there should be a small allowance - this is the end of the future folder. One of the rectangles will be the cover, and the second will be the back of the product. The back should have movable sides. It is not difficult to make them, just draw narrow stripes on the three sides of one of the rectangles. Try to cut out the template and fold it, as in the photo at the beginning of this paragraph. Now you can transfer the pattern to cardboard and cut out the required number of school folders. The finishing touch is attaching ties from the thinnest ribbons. Knowing how to make a notebook and a folder for dolls, you can regularly supply your toy school with the necessary number of stationery. Enjoy your game and productive training!
