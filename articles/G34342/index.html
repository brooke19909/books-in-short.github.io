Korston Hotel, Serpukhov: room selection, booking convenience, quality of service, additional services, photos and guest reviews

For many centuries Serpukhov was known as a reliable southern outpost of the Moscow principality on the Nara River. The starting point of the glorious centuries-old history of the fortress city is called 1339.

To accommodate guests in the city there is a wide selection of hotels of various classes. One of the popular options that offer visitors a high level of comfort and a wide range of quality services is the Korston Hotel (Serpukhov).

Acquaintance

This modern hotel welcomes tourists directly in the city center. The architectural ensemble in which the hotel is located consists of the tallest buildings in the region. Its windows and balconies offer impressive panoramic views of the city's beauty and the river valley. The Korston Hotel in Serpukhov (photos are presented in the article) offers a wide range of basic and additional services necessary for a comfortable stay.

To accommodate guests here are offered 79 comfortable rooms of various categories, including standard, junior suite and suite, equipped in accordance with the latest hotel standards. The rooms at the Korston Hotel (Serpukhov) are equipped with the latest technology and comfortable furniture. Each is equipped with climate control, a flat-screen TV, a seating area and a private bathroom. Interior design in guest rooms is done in light and soft colors.

In the restaurants of the Korston Hotel (Serpukhov), professional chefs prepare Japanese, European and Italian dishes for guests. The bar offers a rich assortment of strong drinks, wines, cocktails, as well as coffee and tea.

For business negotiations and business meetings, two conference rooms are equipped with all necessary technical equipment.

Near the Korston Hotel (Serpukhov) there are public transport stops, shops and cafes. Tourists are happy to visit the Museum of History and Art - the city's closest attraction to the hotel.

If you want to have fun, residents of the Korston Hotel (Serpukhov) can visit a cinema, a nightclub or karaoke. Young guests await in a fun and interesting children's amusement park. For lovers of the club style of relaxation, the doors of the club "Stylyagi" are open, where they can have fun and excitement. According to reviews, the hotel has one of the best movie theaters in the city. In addition, guests can play billiards, go bowling, etc.

About location

The Korston Serpukhov Hotel (address: 142200, Russia, Moscow Region, Serpukhov, 1 Borisovskoye Shosse) is located in the very center of the city, where Voroshilov Street intersects with Borisovskoye Shosse. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel there is a second concrete ring. On it, you can travel to the A108 highway (Kiev highway), convenient travel to Domodedovo Airport (78 km) and to the territory of new Moscow.

Motorists locate the hotel as the 78th kilometer south of Moscow along Simferopol highway. On an unloaded and high-quality highway from the capital, you can get here in 1 hour.

Distances

The hotel is located near the Serpukhov Museum of History and Art (a 15-minute walk). The railway station is 3 km from the Korston Hotel (Serpukhov), 78 km from Domodedovo Airport (Moscow). The city center is about 2 km from the hotel, and the Oka River is 3.6 km away.
