A little bit about the project

Vernadsky Avenue, or rather, the space between it and Academician Anokhin Street, was not chosen by chance for the construction of a premium residential complex. This is one of the most picturesque and favorable areas for living in Moscow. All the necessary infrastructure, parks, squares, walking and recreation areas are presented here in sufficient abundance and diversity - you definitely will not be bored.

According to the plan of the developer, the elite new building rises above the natural landscape, represented by a lake of natural origin and the stunning Troparevsky park - and all this is the Mirax Park residential complex. Object address: Vernadsky ave., 94, building 1-5. The complex promises to be one of the best in Moscow, so it definitely deserves special attention.





Architecture

The residential complex "Mirax Park" is represented by 4 buildings from 31 to 37 floors. Fifth low-rise building will be appreciated by lovers of space and a variety of planning solutions. Together, they create a single architectural concept, personifying the seasons of the year. This is the main feature of the Mirax Park residential complex. The year of construction, or rather, the final completion of the project, is of interest to many. Today it has been commissioned, most of the apartments have already been sold out, but you still have the opportunity to acquire prestigious housing in one of the best areas of the city.

Continuing the review of the architecture of the complex, I want to note bright color accents: with a cursory glance, an association with the colors of the seasons arises. The complex really stands out against the background of typical modern buildings, rises above them.

Features of the residential complex

LCD "Mirax Park" is endowed with a number of features. First of all, these are ventilated facades that ensure reliability, durability of the building and maximum comfort of living. All houses are equipped with high-speed and almost noiseless European-made elevators. And the decoration of public spaces is made using unique building materials: marble, wood, granite. Many residents note: when you get into the entrance, you get the full feeling that you are in the lobby of the Palace of Arts, a prestigious business center.





A distinctive feature of the complex are panoramic windows that provide the building with excellent heat and sound insulation properties. They are in harmony with the facade of each building, creating its unique appearance.

Social infrastructure

LCD "Mirax Park" is intended not only for young residents of the city, but also for families with small children.
