Club Boran Mare Beach 5 * Kemer: review, features and reviews

For lovers of Turkish resorts, of course, Kemer is familiar. The beautiful nature of these places in combination with the clear sea is a real paradise for holiday-makers. In our article we want to tell about the Club Boran Mare 5 * hotel, located near the village of Goynuk.

A bit about the hotel

Club Boran Mare 5 * was built in the resort region of Kemer - the village of Goynuk. Getting to the hotel is not difficult, since the distance from Antalya Airport is only 45 kilometers. To the center of Kemer can be reached by dolmushes, which run during the day, the distance between the towns is only seven kilometers.



Club Boran Mare 5 * has been operating since 1994, and the last major renovation was carried out in 2011. The picturesque territory of the complex is surrounded by forest stands. The hotel includes three-story buildings, as well as two-story buildings and bungalows.

Club Boran Mare Beach 5 * has an excellent infrastructure, which creates the prerequisites for a comfortable and pleasant stay. The complex has all the conditions for tourists. The hotel has its own spa, where you can enjoy a series of wellness treatments for body and face.

Beach vacation

Club Boran Mare 5 * hotel has a beautiful private beach with pebble and sand cover. The complex is built on the coast, so the road to the sea takes no more than two minutes. The beach area is equipped with sun loungers, umbrellas and awnings; at the reception, guests are given towels. On the coast there is a pier and a pontoon. A bit about the resort

As we already mentioned, Club Boran Mare Beach HV 5 * is located in the area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe village of Goynuk, which is now quite popular among tourists. The town is only seven kilometers from Kemer.



Goynuk gained popularity thanks to stunning landscapes. The village is considered to be one of the most picturesque places on the coast. The difference between Kemer and the village of Goynuk is not so great. Not all tour operators subdivide these two resort places. For vacationers, the only important thing is that they have a different coastline. In Goynuk, the shores are more rocky, there are gorges and cliffs formed by the mountains approaching the sea. Holidays in the town are comfortable for families with children and lovers of mountain tourism.

Once these lands were occupied by robbers. Later, in the XI century. Turkmen robbers seized the land. They gave the village its current name. The coast of the village of Goynuk covers mountains from all sides, and its streets are completely planted with orange trees.

Resort Attractions

Goynuk is a small town, so significant attractions in it are difficult to find. But in Kemer there are more than enough of them. The only thing that you can see in the area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe village of Goynuk is a mountain canyon. Fans of mountain tourism will certainly appreciate this place. In addition, vacationers can go to Kemer. The road to it on dolmushi does not take much time. In the city you can visit the local market, which operates several times a week. Not far from Kemer are the ruins of Phaselis and the ruins of ancient Olympos. These places are also interesting to visit. But lovers of natural beauty should go to Mount Yanartash, which is always shrouded in smoke. Flames burst from its bowels. Whether this is true or not is up to you to judge. But to visit this amazing place is worth it.
