"Ashatli" - a recreation center in the Perm region

The Perm Territory attracts tourists from Russia and other countries of the world not only with a mass of various historical sights, but also with its picturesque nature. Local recreation centers can be located both in the forest and in the mountains. Many tourists are interested, for example, in the Perm complex ‚ÄúAshatli‚Äù. There is more than one recreation center with that name in the region. There are only two Ashatli complexes in the Perm Territory. One of these bases is located near the town of Tchaikovsky in a pine forest. The second complex is located in the mountains in the south-west of the region, between the villages of Chernushka and Barda.



Forest base

What amenities does tourists offer located in the Ashatli pine forest (recreation center). Tchaikovsky is located at a distance of about 5 km from this complex. The forest surrounding the base from all sides, according to many tourists, is very picturesque. The base of "Ashatli" takes vacationers in four buildings. The main building of the complex has three floors. Next to it is one small guest house and three large ones. All base buildings are built of environmentally friendly wood. Also on the territory of the complex there are two log baths - large and small.



"Ashatli" is a recreation center on the territory of which for the convenience of guests there are gazebos and tables. Also, tourists, if desired, can independently fry barbecue in this complex. It is worth living in a tourist center from 3 000 p. per day (per room).

Rooms: description

If desired, tourists can rent a standard or economy class room in the main building at the Ashatli recreation center. All rooms for rent in the center have a TV and a bathroom. In standard rooms, among other things, there are refrigerators.

The rooms in guest houses are designed for more demanding guests. Here, tourists can use both kettles and microwave. The most expensive rooms also have kitchenettes.



Complex infrastructure

"Ashatli" is a recreation center on the territory of which, in addition to baths, tourists can visit cafes. There is also a banquet hall in this center. His vacationers often take off for anniversaries, holidays in the company of friends, corporate parties. The residents of the complex can hand over the overall things to the left-luggage office.



Pavilions with barbecues installed in them in this recreation center are paid. They should rent 400-500 p. / H. In addition to the barbecue, tourists can rent a cauldron. It is not necessary to take skewers and grates with you to the base. These accessories can also be rented on site.

Winter fun

Thus, quite a good service offers its guests "Ashatli" (recreation center). Perm, as you know, is located in central Russia. In winter there is a lot of snow. In the cold season, the Ashatli base, located near Tchaikovsky, is very popular, including among skiers and snowboarders. In winter, the complex also operates a rental center for sports equipment.

For lovers of outdoor activities on the basis of "Ashatli" a 320 m long track with a slope of 60 degrees is provided. Also on the territory of the complex there is a descent designed not only for professionals, but also for beginners. The length of this route is 3.5 km, and it has both complex sections and simple ones.

Recreation center "Ashatli" (Tchaikovsky): reviews of tourists

The opinion about this center among vacationers has developed mixed. Many tourists include rather spacious rooms and the availability of a place near the houses where you can fry the barbecue as a hotel plus. The disadvantage of the base are cold and not too clean showers. Vacationers and cafes in this center are not particularly praised. Dishes are offered expensive and not always fresh. It's better to take food to the base with you.

But on the territory of the Ashatli complex, there are only positive reviews on the Web. The tracks here are really very good. In any case, ski lovers can spend very good time in this center.
