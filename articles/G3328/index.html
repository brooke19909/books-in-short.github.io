Paradnaya Hotel, Yaroslavl: description, room overview and reviews

One of the most popular establishments of this locality is the Paradnaya Hotel. Yaroslavl is a large hospitable city that will certainly make a striking impression on tourists.





Brief description of the hotel

Going on a tourist or business trip, pay attention to such an accommodation option as the Paradnaya Hotel. Yaroslavl will give you a lot of vivid impressions, and this hotel will provide comfortable living conditions. Here you can take advantage of such necessary services as wireless Internet, round-the-clock service and other services.

Cozy rooms with luxurious decoration are offered to their guests by the Paradnaya Hotel. Yaroslavl will open before you in all its glory if you look at it from the windows of the apartments. Here you can relax on comfortable orthopedic furniture, as well as take advantage of the most modern amenities. The apartments create an atmosphere of absolute peace and homeliness.

The Paradnaya Hotel provides its guests with full-fledged meals. Yaroslavl is a big city with a huge number of cafes and restaurants, but one of the best establishments works in this hotel. Here you can taste the most sophisticated signature dishes from the chef, as well as enjoy a huge assortment of light desserts. Separately, it is worth noting the beautiful interior and the calm atmosphere of the establishment.





Hotel Location

Paradnaya Hotel in the city (Yaroslavl, by the way, is a rather large settlement, so without finding the address it will be difficult to find the hotel) is located on Chelyuskintsev Street, 14. This is a historical center, which is included in the list of world cultural heritage. The road from the train station to the hotel takes no more than 10 minutes, and from the airport - about half an hour. Nearby is a bus stop with which you can quickly get anywhere in the city.

Description of rooms

Comfortable living conditions are offered to its guests by the Paradnaya Hotel (Yaroslavl). Reviews of tourists indicate the possibility of accommodation in the rooms of the following categories:

  • Apartments of the comfort category are the most economical accommodation option. One room has a double bed, as well as a small sitting area with a soft sofa. The combined bathroom is equipped with a bath.
  • Duplex apartments have a fairly large area and occupy two floors at once. On the first level there is a living room and a study. Folding upholstered furniture can serve to accommodate additional guests. On the second floor there is a cozy bedroom.
  • Deluxe apartments are a spacious room with three windows, thanks to which the room is very well lit and looks much more spacious. There is a large double bed, a workplace, as well as a spacious wardrobe. You can relax after a busy day in the hot tub.
  • Attic "standard" is suitable for lovers of a quiet secluded vacation. It is worth noting the availability of a full-fledged workplace, as well as other conditions necessary for a comfortable stay. The attic window opens onto a courtyard, sheltered from city noise. Accommodation can be made on a large bed or a pair of one and a half. It is also possible to stay in single rooms.
  • Loft apartments are furnished in an ultra-modern minimalist style. Light colors and strict geometric forms prevail here.




Apartment Amenities

In all rooms of this hotel you can use the following list of amenities:

  • wireless internet, the use of which is included in the price;
  • widescreen TV connected to a cable operator;
  • an electric kettle, as well as a coffee maker so that you can always please yourself with hot drinks;
  • reliable safe with electronic combination lock, which will ensure the safety of your valuables and documents;
  • a minibar, which can also be used as a refrigerator;
  • the bathroom has a bath or shower, as well as an electric hairdryer, a set of bath and cosmetic accessories;
  • individual air conditioning, which will provide a comfortable air temperature in the room.

General impression

The Paradnaya Hotel (Yaroslavl, Russia) is quite popular among visitors. Reviews of tourists indicate the extremely favorable location of this institution. This is the central part of the city, from where you can easily reach all major cultural attractions. Also in the immediate vicinity is the train station.

The hotel pays special attention to the design and functionality of the rooms. The interior is made in bright colors, due to which the area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe room seems visually larger. It is also worth noting the rather high ceilings (with the exception of attic rooms). Windows let in a lot of sunlight. Pleased with the tea set, but refrigerators could be less noisy.

Special attention should be paid to the restaurant that operates in this hotel. Of course, the breakfast included in the room price is unlikely to impress anyone. But if you make an order on the menu, you can enjoy unique gourmet dishes from a professional chef. This place is loved not only by tourists, but also by citizens. Nevertheless, try to avoid checking into the rooms located above the restaurant, otherwise the smell of food will be constantly present in your room.

If we talk about the shortcomings, it is worth noting the lack of their own car parking. In addition, all possible parking spaces are always occupied by cars of visitors to the restaurant, and hotel guests have nowhere to put their transport. That is why, choosing this hotel, take care of placing your cars in advance.
