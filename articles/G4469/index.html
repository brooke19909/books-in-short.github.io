An example of technical excellence - the Stels Navigator 610

In today's transient world, great attention is paid to a healthy lifestyle and physical activity. One of the important components of a healthy holiday is tourism and similar pastimes. The cycling sport has deservedly gained immense popularity among the population, which helps to keep the body in good shape and receive positive emotions. The most suitable option for beginner riders will be such a two-wheeled transport model as the Stels Navigator 610.





The historical roots of the brand

Stels appeared on the world stage relatively recently - in 1998. From this moment on, the pace of its development rapidly increased, and it took on incredible proportions. Today, the brand can be proud of a number of models of its own production, the number of which exceeds 200 units. One of the most popular modifications of two-wheeled vehicles is the Stels Navigator 610 bike. It combines the most necessary and modern.





Briefly about the main thing

Today, the Stels Navigator 610 Disc bike is quite popular among buyers. This vehicle is classified as mountain bike. It is designed to ride on imperfect soils and off-road. The frame of the bike has an aluminum interpretation that provides incredible lightness to the product. Thanks to the spring-elastomeric design of the fork, the vehicle smoothly moves around any type of terrain. The reinforced rim design makes it easy to carry out repair work, which involves straightening and adjusting the deformed shape of the latter.

The bike brake system is disc. This allows you to operate the vehicle even in harsh and extreme conditions. The basic equipment of the bike implies the presence of a comfortable footrest and plastic modification of the wings, which protect the wearer from dirt and water getting on clothes in rainy and inclement weather.





Technical features

The purpose of the vehicle is to ride in highlands and cross-country, which involve extreme and slightly careless behavior on the road and other soils. Therefore, the bike has powerful technical characteristics:

  • Modifications to the frame - 15.5; 17.5; 19.5; 21.5 inches The latter is made in an aluminum interpretation to ensure ease and further maneuverability of the product.
  • The fork of this vehicle has a spring-elastomeric construction.
  • Wheel size is 26 inches.
  • The bike boasts a reinforced rim design.
  • The fork travel is 80 mm.
  • The brake system has a mechanical disc design.
  • The number of speeds of this vehicle model is 21 units.

Brake system

The Stels Navigator 610 bike model has amazing and fairly modern V-brakes. Their feature is the unique principle of contact between the pads and the rim, which slows down its torsion. This integral component of each vehicle is quite easy to maintain. The brakes of this bike are lightning fast. They do not overheat, which positively affects the life of the model.





Price policy

An amazing vehicle from the affordable price range is the Stels Navigator 610 bike, whose customer reviews are often positive. Proof of this are excellent quality, excellent performance and unrivaled durability. The cost of the vehicle at a discount from the manufacturer varies from 14,000 to 14,500 rubles.





Stels Navigator 610: customer reviews. Pros, Cons, and Subtleties of a Bicycle

According to numerous reviews of owners and industry experts, these models are a kind of tandem of incredible reliability and excellent quality. The main advantage of such a vehicle is its lightness, which is achieved thanks to the aluminum frame. Excellent cushioning allows daily movement on dirt roads and paving stones without harming the structural integrity of the product. Riding steep hills or driving with obstacles is not a problem. Obstacles can be easily overcome thanks to the amazing speed switch and a sufficient number of modes.

Model Stels Navigator 610 is equipped with a fairly high-quality rubber with amazing protection. It has excellent adhesion to any soil and serves the owner for more than one year. Among the positive points that cannot be noted during operation, there are rubberized handles on the steering wheel. Thanks to the latter, the hand does not slip on them, does not get tired and is in a comfortable position.

Despite the many positive aspects faced by owners of the Stels Navigator 610 Disc bicycle, there are not only positive reviews about it. The negative side of the vehicle must include a hard seat, which does not provide the desired comfort during long trips. Also, cyclists often have problems with pedal integrity. The latter during careless operation very quickly become unusable and require immediate replacement.
