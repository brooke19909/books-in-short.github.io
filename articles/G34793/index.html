Avliga Beach 3 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach): description of rooms, services, photos

The largest resort in Bulgaria - Sunny Beach - is located on the shores of a small bay in the Black Sea and is surrounded from the north by the Stara Planina massif. The area in which it is located is awarded the UNESCO Blue Flag. And not without reason. There are no large settlements and industrial enterprises near Sunny Beach . That is, this resort is located in an absolutely environmentally friendly place.

Sunny Beach is popular with tourists from all over the world. Of course, very often vacationers come here from Russia. Domestic tour operators work with many hotels in Sunny Beach. For example, Russian tourists, if desired, can rent rooms at any time in the rather comfortable Avliga Beach 3 * hotel .





Where is the complex

Avliga Beach Hotel is located in the very center of Sunny Beach, near Burgas. The distance from it to the sea is only 50 m. Its guests can literally get to the beach in 2-3 minutes. Burgas Airport is approximately 25 km from the hotel. It takes less than an hour to get to the hotel from here by transfer bus. The distance to the center of Burgas from the complex is 35 km.

What is a hotel

This hotel was built in 2001. In 2016, large-scale repairs were carried out here. This complex accepts guests in one four-story building of original modern architecture. The hotel courtyard is well arranged and landscaped. The center provides literally everything for a comfortable stay of guests.

The reception in this complex is open 24 hours a day. Guests are allowed to stay at the hotel with pets (weighing up to 5 kg). Also in the rooms of the center ashtrays are installed. Checkout time at this hotel is 12:00.





Description of rooms rented at the hotel

All rooms of the complex belong to the class Standard Room 2 ADL (two adults). Guests of Sunny Beach have the opportunity to rent here one of 120 comfortable rooms. The area of ​​each of them is 19 m 2. Although the hotel rooms are designed to accommodate only two people, if necessary, tourists can order another extra bed. The rooms for the convenience of residents include:

  • TV;
  • telephone;
  • mini bar;
  • wardrobe with shoulders;
  • desk;
  • chair bed;
  • air conditioning.

If desired, guests can use the rooms and the Internet. But for this service you will need to pay separately. Air conditioning at the hotel is centralized. Of course, like any other three-star hotel, Avliga Beach rooms have an individual shower. There is a balcony (terrace ) in the rooms rented at this hotel .





Hotel Housing Reviews

Tourists attribute their rather large area and cleanliness to the advantages of rooms rented in a hotel. They clean the rooms at the Avliga Beach 3 * hotel room every day. Towels in the shower are changed once every 2-3 days. Many experienced tourists advise newcomers to leave maid tips on the beds at the hotel. In this case, they will be more flexible.

The furniture in the rooms of the complex, according to many residents, is quite comfortable and new. Air conditioners, televisions and other household appliances usually work properly. Refrigerators in this hotel, according to many guests, are not too good. At least the water in the bottles in them has to be cooled for a very long time. Praised by former residents of the hotel and its showers. They have a relatively small area, but they are always clean.

Hotel Infrastructure

The rooms in the Avliga Beach 3 * Sunny Beach hotel , therefore, judging by the reviews of tourists, are quite comfortable. The same can be said about the service provided to residents in this complex. At the hotel, tourists can use:

  • tennis court;
  • outdoor pool;
  • jacuzzi;
  • safe at the reception.

There is also a beauty salon for guests. Safe at registration paid. Of the additional services for residents are:

  • massage room;
  • Fitness Centre;
  • sauna;
  • Internet cafe;
  • billiards.




For all these amenities, residents need to pay separately. There is also a mini golf course on site. For children, the complex has a plunge pool and a play area in the yard. Of course, there is also a gym in this hotel.

Infrastructure Reviews

The guests staying at Hotel Avliga Beach are mostly Czechs and Germans. Sometimes these guests, unfortunately, behave quite noisy. However, many tourists still consider this hotel to be much quieter than most nearby.

There are no negative reviews about the hotel staff. Most of the guests consider the hotel staff to be responsible, polite and welcoming. No cases of theft were noticed at this hotel.

Most guests praise the pool. Swimming in it, according to many tourists, is quite convenient. Sun loungers and parasols by the pool can be used free of charge. However, tourists usually have to take sun loungers early in the morning. Unfortunately, they are not enough for all tenants. The disadvantage of this hotel, many tourists believe that there are never animations.

Nutrition Reviews

If desired, vacationers can rent rooms at the Avliga Beach 3 * hotel (Sunny Beach) with breakfast or half board. In the latter case, among other things, tourists are offered dinner. On the food in the dining room of the hotel there are relatively good reviews. For dinner, the hotel serves reasonably tasty dishes. At the same time, the menu itself in the restaurant of the complex is quite different in the evening. For children, you can take soup or porridge. Breakfast in this complex tourists are not too praise. In the morning, only omelets, bread, milk, tea and butter are served in the restaurant . There is, of course, coffee in the dining room of this hotel. However, he is here, according to the residents, not too good. In fact, this is a regular acorn or oat drink. Therefore, experienced tourists recommend that beginners buy coffee in the city and just take it with them for breakfast.





The service in the dining room of the hotel, according to many guests, is not too good. Sometimes even dishes may be missed by all visitors. The waiters at the hotel, unfortunately, are not particularly polite. They, like the maids, constantly have to leave a tip. The disadvantages of the restaurant, many tourists include the fact that the water from the cooler is free to take here only for breakfast, lunch or dinner. The rest of the time you have to pay for it.

In addition to the dining room, the hotel also has a small bar. Here you can order all kinds of drinks. There is, as already mentioned, a small convenient Internet cafe in the hotel. In addition to accessing the network, in this institution you can order all kinds of light snacks.

Reviews of tourists about the sea

The beach is located almost on the territory of the Avliga Beach Hotel itself. In order to get ashore, residents of the complex just need to cross the road. First on the beach is a paid area. Tourists with limited means need to go through about 12 rows of sunbeds. Next begins the free zone.

Most of the guests praise the sea, the main plus - it is clean and close to the hotel . The nature around the beach of the hotel, according to most tourists, is simply luxurious. And the coastline itself is very convenient for relaxation. The entrance to the sea at this place is fairly gentle. The shore itself on the beach is covered with sand.

Rent umbrellas and sunbeds on the beach can, of course, and residents of the Avliga Beach 3 * hotel. But their rent is quite expensive. For the kit you need to pay about 16 leva. Therefore, most tourists prefer to sunbathe on the beach of the complex yet on the rugs.





On the pedestrian street along the beach, there are many shops, bars and cafes. Prices are relatively low. In stores, tourists can purchase beach accessories, products, souvenirs, etc. The catering establishments serve delicious dishes of local and European cuisine.

sights

Of course, the guests of the Avliga Beach 3 * hotel, like any other tourists who choose Sunny Beach as their vacation destination, can also see many different interesting places here. The most popular attraction located next to the hotel is the ancient city of Nessebar. If desired, it can be reached simply by public transport. In total, the trip will take no more than 10 minutes.





The city of Nessebar is located on the peninsula of the same name. Its old part is a real open-air museum. About 40 ancient churches were built here, some of them are preserved almost in their original form. The oldest is the basilica of St. Sophia. Her tourists should definitely visit. Also in Nessebar vacationers can purchase all kinds of souvenirs to commemorate a visit to Sunny Beach.
