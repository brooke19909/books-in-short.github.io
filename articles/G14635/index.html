Virtual SIM for registration, calls and SMS

A virtual sim card is issued through special service sites, they provide services for free or for money. But most of these services provide a temporary period or clearance with restrictions. There are also stationary operators who will issue such a SIM card, but all information about the profile will be downloaded "by air". The virtual SIM itself replaced the old standards of the mobile network, when everyone was tied to operators, tariffs, this is especially noticeable for people who use SIM cards in different places with different communication coverage.

SIM card concept

This is a special module that identifies the subscriber in the cellular network.

They support a variety of digital mobile cellular standards and frequencies.

They can perform different functions:

  • Be a memory for phone numbers.
  • Keep lists of calls that have been missed and received, outgoing calls.
  • Receive messages.

But now these functions are not used, since modern phones already come with a large amount of memory, and on the SIM cards themselves only a small part of the information.

SIM card design

Usually, SIM cards are issued by transferring passport data to mobile operators, so that, in turn, they allow you to use their services.

A virtual SIM card may also need this data, but you can use the services to hide your identity.

Before registration, it is necessary to carefully consider all the options that the service and / or mobile operator provides.

Differences between virtual number and SIM card

At times, not all operators have advantageous offers when designing a virtual SIM. Then a virtual number comes to the rescue.

The virtual number works like a virtual SIM card or city number, you can receive calls and messages via the Internet. No equipment needed. Forwarding possible.

It is also protection against spam, but it is especially useful when shopping at some online stores or when applying for a loan from various sellers (you do not need to ignore the bank). In the same sense, it saves you from deducting funds from the telephone balance after downloading for "free."

Again, you save on calls to different countries and cities. You can make calls at competitive rates, you do not need to carry a telephone cable.

How to make virtual sims, design a virtual number

As an example. Do you have a MTS SIM card and need a virtual SIM card?

How to attach to MTS:

  1. We go to the official website in your personal account.
  2. There we connect the service "Virtual Number".

More options. Connection of a virtual number (without category):

  1. Dial the combination * 760 * 01 # (call). For the "golden" category at the end * 02 #, for the "platinum" * 03 # instead of * 01 #.
  2. Send a message with the text "01" to the number 7600. For "gold" 02, for platinum 03.
  3. There are features of a call using a virtual number. When dialing a number, you need to enter additional characters, which can be viewed in more detail on the official website.

It is likely that your operator provides this service, just look in your account and on the website of the service provider. You can call the operator, and he will explain all the features and nuances.

So, you have decided to issue a virtual SIM card. To do this, you will need to perform several actions:

  3. Choose a suitable rate.
  4. Proceed to use the card for calls, messages and doing various things.
