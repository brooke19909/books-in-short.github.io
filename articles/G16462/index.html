Thailand, Phuket in November: features of rest, weather and reviews of tourists

Many tourists, before certain events often traveled to Turkey and Egypt, are now exploring new lands in search of their ideal vacation spot. Among the popular countries are Vietnam, Greece, Italy, Spain, Thailand and some others.

One of the Thai resorts, which is often chosen by tourists from Russia, is the province of Phuket. It is located in the southern part of the country and is an invariable choice of hundreds, maybe thousands of vacationers from all over the world. Autumn has already arrived, not far off its last month, and some have not had time to rest this year. For those who plan to spend their holidays in Thailand, today we will tell you what Phuket is in November, and whether it is worth flying there at all times.





What weather is characterized by November Phuket?

Together with November, the rainy season in Phuket ends. There is already much less rainfall, and the temperature of water and air is close to comfortable. All November is characterized by such parameters, that is, it doesn‚Äôt matter if you fly to Thailand at the beginning of the month or at the end.

However, it is important to know that in October-November, rainfall, thunderstorms and cloudy weather are still possible in Phuket province.

The time is approaching the high season, so with each November day the weather is more and more suitable for recreation. This is expressed not by an increase in temperature, but, on the contrary, by a decrease. It becomes not so hot and stuffy, so the rest is much more pleasant.

Along with this, prices for trips are increasing and the number of tourists on the beaches of Phuket province is increasing. In November, water in the sea gradually becomes more transparent, and the chance of a storm or the appearance of large waves is much lower.

Low or high prices? Phuket in November: ticket prices

Prices at this time are no longer as low as in September and October, when there are heavy rains, but since November is a transitional month (from the rainy season to high), you can still find quite a reasonable price for hotel accommodation and inexpensive cafes and restaurants But you need to prepare for the fact that hotels are being rebuilt to the influx of tourists, so less and less attractive offers for accommodation are found.

What can be done in Phuket province in November?

Anything! This month is suitable for a beach holiday, when tourists are waiting for white sand and warm sea water. Season opens on the Similan Islands of Phuket, which are famous for beautiful tropical vegetation and are located on the border of the Indian Ocean and the Andaman Sea.

This area is very beautiful, nature, blooming after the rainy season, beckons holidaymakers into its arms. You can walk, enjoying its charm, and take pictures in the most attractive places in Phuket. Here you can order and conduct the most unforgettable photo shoot.

The water in the sea in the kingdom of Thailand (Phuket) in November is mostly calm. But true surfers should not stop anything. It is recommended to go to the West of Phuket: there are ideal conditions for surfing, as well as other water sports.

Shopping - what could be nicer for lovely ladies ?! In November in Phuket, many shopping centers and markets arrange sales, where there are huge discounts. Why not take advantage of this privilege and buy gifts for yourself and your family?

It is recommended not to lose all your time on the beaches and be sure to go on one of the many excursions. During this event, you can get acquainted with the sights and other interesting and beautiful places of Phuket. Nothing prevents an independent walk in the city. For example, a trip to an amusement park, considered the largest in Thailand, would be a good choice.





Thai holiday Loi Kratong: this is not to be missed!

One of the most striking events in Phuket in November is the Loy Krathong festival. The name translates as "floating boat of leaves." This is an incredibly beautiful and romantic holiday, which is necessarily held only in November - on the day of the twelfth full moon.

The essence of the event is that the locals make boats from the leaves of a banana tree. There is no special technique for the creation of ‚Äúships‚Äù: as they say, who cares. But often you can see real works of art.

In the evening, boats are laid on the water. So they symbolize gratitude to water and light for life and food. Coins and candles are placed inside the boats, the sea shimmers with lights, which looks very impressive.

At the same time, a festival of air lanterns is held. Lighted sky and water are a very beautiful and unusual sight that will leave the most pleasant memories in the soul of tourists.





Conclusion: is it worth it to fly in November?

You should definitely visit Thailand at least once in your life. Phuket (November or April - this parameter does not play a special role) is a beautiful island where you can find pleasant activities for yourself even in the rainy season.

The above information suggests that in the last autumn month in the province you can spend time with benefit: swimming and sunbathing, surfing or other sports, go on a tour and arrange shopping, take a walk while admiring the tropical vegetation, and capture all this on a camera .

November is attractive because it marks the transition to a high season, but prices, meanwhile, have not yet managed to soar to unimaginable proportions. Therefore, the answer to the question of whether to choose Phuket for the November vacation can be unequivocally: yes.
