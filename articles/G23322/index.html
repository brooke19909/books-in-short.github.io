Hotel Renaissance in Moscow.

Each city has hotels that are worth visiting at least once in a lifetime. Moscow offers tourists a huge number of hotels of different levels of service and unequal pricing. If you want to make your stay comfortable and memorable, then the Renaissance Hotel in Moscow is what you need. It has a five-star category and always attracts visitors to the city with a powerful infrastructure, chic rooms and a wonderful attitude of the staff towards the guests. The location of the Renaissance Hotel in Moscow is perfect for those who want to be in the center of events of metropolitan life and see local attractions. Nearby is the Circus on Tsvetnoy Boulevard, the Theater of the Russian Army, Red Square, the Kremlin and Catherine Park. You can reach all these places in a short time. As well as interesting exhibition halls, shopping centers, cafes and restaurants are within easy reach.

The Renaissance Hotel in Moscow managed to survive a lot during its 20 years of existence. It was built in 1991 under a different name, and in 1993 got its name due to joining the Renaissance hotel group. Four years later, this hotel became the property of Marriott Hotels Collection. A few years ago, the reconstruction of this wonderful hotel was completed and its internal appearance has almost completely changed.

For many years now, the Renaissance Hotel in Moscow has been hosting guests from various social groups. There were also foreign stars, politicians, all kinds of celebrities. Millionaires are very fond of this hotel, they know that they will receive first-class service for their money. In addition, the hotel attracts businessmen, ordinary tourists, participants in various festivals and exhibitions, as well as VIPs from around the world. He managed to earn an excellent reputation and is at the peak of popularity.

The Renaissance Hotel in Moscow offers its guests 481 rooms, decorated to the highest standard. The hotel has 412 standard rooms, most of which are non-smoking. They contain everything you need for a good stay: three beds, satellite TV, telephone, minibar, ironing board with iron, and a bathroom with hairdryer. The hotel also has 5 rooms, which are called three-room suites. There is a bedroom, a living room and a meeting room. The hotel offers Club Floor services. There are 59 rooms. This floor has its own lobby with a bar, reception service, and also provides butler service. Among the suites there are also 2 club and 2 family.

The Renaissance Hotel in Moscow offers guests a huge number of various services and facilities. You can go to the sauna, pool and gym, use the parking, car rental or taxi service, change money. Women will certainly appreciate the beauty salon, dry cleaning and laundry services. There is also a cinema and an interesting souvenir shop. This hotel has 12 banquet rooms where you can hold any celebration. A business center is open around the clock. The Renaissance Hotel in Moscow is known worldwide for its high level of service and well-developed infrastructure.

This is the place that will leave you with the most pleasant experience. Reviews for the Renaissance Hotel in Moscow are mostly positive. People tend to leave here with positive emotions and thanks to the hotel staff. Although there were some clients who noted the impolite attitude of the service staff, not too thorough cleaning of the rooms and other disadvantages and shortcomings in the organization of the service. In this case, you still need to make a discount on the human factor. Although it is safe to say that only professionals work here.
