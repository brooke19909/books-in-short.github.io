The best hotels of Russia: review, description, rating

Russia is a truly great and big country. Its open spaces are vast. Probably every resident wants to visit many cities of his homeland. But here the question arises: where to stay for the night, especially if you want a chic vacation? The answer is simple: look for the best hotel in the city. But how to find him? That is why today we will talk about the best hotels in Russia.

"Sheraton Palace"

The Sheraton Palace Hotel (Moscow) was built in 1993, 11 years later, in 2004, a complete reconstruction was carried out that completely changed the concept of the complex. Now the hotel has one of the highest quality ratings. It is worth noting that this hotel belongs to the Sheraton company, which has more than 400 hotels in 70 countries of the world. The room fund of one of the best hotels in Russia totals 204 rooms of various categories. All of them are made in a modern style, in calm warm colors.

  1. Classic room. Area - 24-27 square meters. m, designed for 2 people. It has soundproofed windows, a bed designed specifically for this network, wireless Internet, a safe and a private bathroom. The average room rate is 13,000 rubles per day.

The hotel also offers club rooms, Junior Junior Suites, Executive Suites, Club Junior Suites and others. On the territory of one of the best hotels in Russia there is a large, modern fitness center, the Vladimir restaurant, which serves international cuisine, a bar, live music, and Japanese cuisine. The hotel is located 3 km from Red Square and is located on Tverskaya-Yamskaya Street, 300 meters from the metro station. Private secured parking is available on site.

"National"

The famous National Hotel was built at the very beginning of the last century, in 1903, according to the project of the famous architect Alexander Ivanov. This hotel is a real monument of Russian architecture, 3 styles were skillfully intertwined in it at once: classicism, modern and renaissance. The hotel has 202 rooms of various categories. All of them are made in the same style, in red and beige colors.

  1. Classic rooms. There are two options: for one or two people. The area varies from 10 to 35 square meters. The National Hotel's room has a private bathroom, Wi-Fi and a safe. Price - from 7700 to 9700 rubles per day.
  2. Presidential Suite. Room size is 87-97 square meters. m. This room has three rooms, a bathroom with its own Jacuzzi. The highest state officials of many European countries like to stay here.

On the territory of one of the best hotels in Russia operates a fitness center and spa. The complex is located in the heart of Moscow, on Mokhovaya Street, just 200 meters from Red Square.

Novotel in St. Petersburg

Novotel in St. Petersburg (center) was built in 2005. Now he has the highest quality rating in this city. The hotel has an unusual architecture, represented by two round buildings with a height of 9 floors. It is made in the Italian style, which recalls the architectural ensemble of the 17th century. The hotel has 233 rooms, all of them are equipped in a modern way, in bright and rich colors.

  1. Standart room. Spacious room in bright colors. It has wireless internet, a TV, a safe and a private bathroom.
  2. Junior Suite The most expensive hotel room. Its area is 35 square meters. m. Spacious bedroom and stylish living room for 2 adults and 2 children. The average room rate is 16,000 rubles per day.
  3. Executive room. Can accommodate 2 adults and 1 child. There are luxury cosmetics, a coffee machine. The price for the room is about 12,000 rubles.

The hotel is located in the very center of St. Petersburg, a three-minute walk from Nevsky Prospekt and Moskovskaya Station.

Hotel "Cannon Inn"

The hotel building is the real cultural heritage of Russia. Members of the secret Northern Society once gathered in this building, and the great Russian poet A.S. Pushkin lived in the neighborhood. The last restoration of the hotel took place in 2017. The hotel has 33 rooms, they are made in a classic style, in light shades with the addition of bright accents.

  1. Standart room. Rooms are 16-18 square meters. m, view from the window to the patio. All rooms are similar, but not the same. The decoration uses natural materials. It offers tall beds with silk linen, free internet, a bathroom and a TV. The price starts from 7650 rubles per day.

There is a restaurant on site serving European and Russian cuisines. The hotel is a minute's walk from Palace Square.

Prometheus Club

Stylish, modern hotel on the Black Sea, Sochi, Lazarevskoye. Built in 2003. The number of rooms totals 325 rooms of different categories. Each room has a private bathroom, a safe and a TV. The windows offer beautiful views of the sea. On the territory of several pools, a spa, 5 restaurants with different cuisine and a bar.
