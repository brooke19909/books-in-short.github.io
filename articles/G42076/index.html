Hotel Perunika 3 * (Bulgaria, Golden Sands): overview, description and reviews of tourists

Bulgaria offers travelers a quality and affordable vacation, so it always remains a popular tourist destination. And the country is famous for its healing resorts, which attract people who want to improve their health. Hotel Perunika 3 * (Golden Sands, Bulgaria) offers guests low prices and a good level of service. Who should use this complex?

General information and cost of hotel accommodation

The tourist complex "Perunika" 3 * (Golden Sands, Bulgaria) was opened in 1996. It is considered a new hotel, because most of the hotels in the resort were built in the second half of the XX century. On a small landscaped area is the only six-story building, offering tourists more than 150 rooms. This place is designed for a relaxing holiday, therefore it attracts older people and couples with children. In addition, pets are allowed here at an additional cost. The hotel must be notified in advance of their arrival.





The cost of living depends on the selected room, type of food, as well as the season. The tour will cost more if you go in July and August, because these months are considered the warmest here. It is much cheaper to go here in June or early September. The temperature at this time is several degrees lower, but the sea is still warm enough for swimming. The minimum price for a standard double room is approximately 2000 rubles per night. In the midst of the tourist season, its cost can increase to 6,000 rubles. You can pay for accommodation and additional services with Visa or MasterCard.

Location of the complex

Hotel Perunika 3 * (Bulgaria, Golden Sands) is located in a quiet location, remote from noisy bars and night clubs. It is located on the second beach line, and the distance to the sea is approximately 300 meters. To reach it, tourists need to get around the road and hotels on the first line. The complex is not so far from the center of the resort. It is within walking distance of shops, bars and restaurants located 250 meters from Perunika. Nearby is also the Aquamania Water Park, as well as the local casino.





Golden Sands Resort is 25 km from the nearest airport of Varna. Travel time to the hotel from there will be about an hour by bus. A taxi or a rented car can be reached in about half an hour. Coming here, you should definitely visit the ancient Aladzha monastery, as well as numerous mineral springs. They offer healing procedures with sea water and healing mud.

Number of rooms and its equipment

To accommodate its guests, the Perunika 3 * complex (Bulgaria, Golden Sands) has prepared 174 comfortable rooms. Their last repair was completed in 2004. All rooms are cleaned daily by the hotel. When making a ticket, tourists can choose the following types of apartments:

  • Standart room. It has two single beds, and an extra bed is a folding sofa. There is a private bathroom and a terrace overlooking the park or the sea. Designed to accommodate two people and a child.
  • Family apartment. It is assumed the settlement of a family of two adults and two children. Some rooms have a bunk bed. A prerequisite is the presence of a bathroom, as well as a balcony that overlooks the sea or mountains.
  • Apartments. Two-room suite in which a family of four can live. It has a bedroom, a living room and a bathroom. Features a spacious furnished balcony or terrace with sea or park views.




In addition to furniture, each room is equipped with air conditioning, cable TV, refrigerator and direct dial telephone. Bed linens and towels must be changed twice a week.

Dining at the hotel

High-quality and varied food can be offered for its guests by the Hotel Perunika 3 * (Bulgaria, Golden Sands). Prices for hotel accommodation also include the cost of food. Tourists can choose either an all-inclusive system or pay for breakfast only. The latter option is suitable for people who prefer to spend time outside the hotel. The all-inclusive concept includes breakfast, lunch and dinner, as well as ice cream, snacks, soft drinks and spirits made in Bulgaria. Once a week, guests can enjoy a theme evening dedicated to national cuisine or seafood.





All meals are served at the buffet in the main restaurant, which has 120 seats. For a bite to eat or a drink, guests can enjoy the pool bar. For an additional fee, tourists can dine at the restaurant, which serves Bulgarian and Mediterranean cuisine. Here visitors are served √† la carte. A night bar is open around the clock, which also hosts night discos.

Organization of children's leisure

The tourist complex Perunika 3 * (Bulgaria, Golden Sands) is well suited for families with children. The hotel offers good discounts on accommodation for children under 12 years old, and kids under 2 years old can even stay for free. On request, a baby cot can be delivered to the room for a fee of only EUR 2 per day. All restaurants are equipped with high chairs for feeding.

For safe bathing of children, the hotel has a shallow pool, the water in which is always warm. Also, kids can always play on the free playground, equipped with a sandbox, swings and slides.

Local service and facilities

Relaxation at the Perunika 3 * hotel (Golden Sands) involves not only the availability of comfortable rooms, but also a developed network of infrastructure facilities that help make your stay as comfortable as possible. All guests can appreciate the quality service, using the following services:

  • Dry cleaning and laundry, where tourists can take their clothes and shoes for cleaning.
  • Equipment for travelers with disabilities. There are special ramps on the stairs, and in the main building there are two elevators, including a freight one.
  • Safe box for storing valuables, documents and money. It is located at the reception and its use is not included in the price.
  • 24 hour reception. There is always an administrator.
  • ATM and currency exchange.
  • A shop selling souvenirs, snacks, tobacco, newspapers and various drinks.
  • Paid parking, where you can not only leave your car, but also rent a car for independent travel around the country.
  • A hairdresser that provides its services for a fee.
  • Call a GP in the room.
  • The Internet. It is provided free of charge only for owners of the all-inclusive system.
  • A conference room equipped with modern computer technology.
  • Banquet hall designed for celebrations.




Entertainment on site

The thought-out organization of leisure is offered for tourists by the Hotel Perunika 3 * (Bulgaria, Golden Sands). Resting here, travelers will find both calm and active entertainment options. Almost all amusements are free for all-inclusive owners. The rest of the tourists are provided at an additional cost. We list the main ones:

  • An outdoor pool with a sun terrace. Sun loungers and sun umbrellas are available for free.
  • Spa area where you can order a relaxing and tonic massage, visit the sauna.
  • Garden with walking paths and the ability to fry meat or fish on the grill.
  • Fitness center with its own gym. Aerobics and water gymnastics classes are also held for tourists.
  • Billiard room.
  • Equipment for playing table tennis.
  • Bicycles for rent.
  • Darts.




Reviews of tourists: positive

The opinions of previous hotel guests are often a decisive factor when choosing a place to stay. Guests of the Perunika 3 * complex (Bulgaria, Golden Sands) leave different reviews . So, positive note the following advantages of the hotel:

  • Good location, suitable for both young people and couples. Nearby are nightclubs, restaurants, and sports fields.
  • Delicious breakfasts that are suitable for both children and people suffering from stomach diseases. The cook prepares amazing pancakes.
  • The hotel is on the second line, but only 5 minutes to the sea.
  • Friendly bartenders, as well as good staff in the restaurant. They always smile, love to talk.
  • Spacious rooms with a design that does not irritate the eye. They have not been repaired for a long time, but they still look clean and tidy.
  • A small pool, which is always in the shade. It is constantly cleaned, trash and fallen leaves are caught.




Reviews of tourists: negative

Unfortunately, some tourists were unsatisfied with the rest at the Perunika Hotel 3 * hotel (Bulgaria, Golden Sands). Reviews about him are often negative. In them, guests describe the following disadvantages of accommodation:

  • The hotel staff hardly speaks Russian. Tourists who do not know the basics of the English language may encounter difficulties in solving problems.
  • Internet only works in the lobby. The room may not be cleaned for several days in a row. Even after going to the reception, the maid is very poorly cleaned.
  • The hotel is trying to write off money for allegedly spoiled furniture or bedding. Therefore, it is worth checking their safety before entering the room.
  • The complex has a lot of Germans resting, who constantly drink and make noise. It is better to take rooms with windows to the pool, because there are much quieter.
  • All food is either salted or sprinkled with spices.
  • Air conditioning is not working properly.
  • A hairdryer and iron are issued only upon request for reception.

Draw conclusions

Hotel Perunika 3 * (Bulgaria, Golden Sands) is equally well suited for young people and families with children. Nearby there are many entertainment facilities: nightclubs, casinos, restaurants, as well as gaming and sports venues. You should not hope that the tourist complex will offer you the highest class service. This is a good hotel suitable for those who want to save on vacation. Low prices here are consistent with the quality of service.
