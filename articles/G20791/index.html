Water parks

If you are looking for where to go with children in Lazarevsky, then in no case do not miss this option. After all, here even on the hottest day you can find a place where it will be cool and comfortable. And how much fun kids bring with them pools of water and slides. There are two water parks in Lazarevsky. Let's consider both options for a visit.

Nautilus: Victory Street 2/1

This place is considered one of the best for recreation and entertainment. Here at the same time can be up to two thousand people. The water park has a lot of greenery, which pleases the gaze of vacationers. What activities are provided for children here? There is a special corner for them in the park called Fantasy. What does it include? Pool with clean water, the temperature in it is slightly higher than in adults. There are also small slides and climbers. Everything is very beautiful and bright. Toddlers and older children enjoy spending time here. It will be interesting to know what are the ticket prices here? Children under three can stay here for free, up to 12 for 900 rubles for the whole day. An adult ticket costs a little more expensive - 1100. If you do not want to spend the whole day here, then you can pay for only four hours. The cost of tickets will be two hundred rubles cheaper.



"Starfish"

Not all vacationers in Lazarevsky know that there is not one, but two water parks. If you are thinking where to go with children in Lazarevsky, then we recommend that you visit both. Waterpark "Starfish" is located in the heart of the city. Nearby is another place where to go with a child in Lazarevsky 2017 (as well as in other years) will be very interesting and informative. This is a dolphinarium. We will also talk about him. The water park has a lot of entertainment for adults and children. There is also a children's town. As well as a playground for games, where there are always a lot of laughing and joyful children. In the middle of the pool there is a bright "mushroom", from the hat of which water runs. Children with great pleasure get under it to take a shower. There is a cafe in the water park. They sell a lot of dishes for children. Bright design and aromatic smells evoke appetite. Let's see what prices are here. Children under five can relax for free. Up to nine years, the ticket price will be 800 rubles. And for everyone else - 1000 p. Ticket prices are for the whole day. The address of the Starfish water park is Lazareva Street, 108 A.



Dolphinarium

Here you can see a unique show with sea animals, as well as observe the behavior of dolphins and other inhabitants. Your children will be delighted with what they show here. Sea lion and dolphins twist hoops, sing songs and even draw. After the performance, children can come closer and get to know the "artists" better. And for a fee, take a picture and swim with them in the pool. Trainers will tell you how to deal with dolphins correctly, and also share interesting information from their lives. Such a pastime will have a beneficial effect on the emotional development of your children, as well as teach them to respect animals.

Dolphinarium address - Lazarev street, 108 A.

Penguinarium "Tropical Amazon"

Another place in Lazarevsky where children can observe the behavior of some animals and birds. A penguinarium is worth a visit if only because there are the largest penguins in Russia. In addition to them you can see monkeys, fur seals, pheasants, peacocks and other birds. Of particular interest in children are penguins. Visitors can see them not only strolling along the shore, but also swimming in the pool.

The address of the Tropical Amazon Penguinarium is 108 A. Lazareva Street.

City park of culture and rest

A large number of pleasant emotions brings rest in Lazarevsky. Where to go with a child to make him interested? We have already offered you several options. There are a lot of places to relax. We offer to get acquainted with one more. It will be interesting for both children and adults. The Park of Culture and Leisure named after the 30th anniversary of the Victory is a place where everyone can find something interesting for themselves. Here you can walk along the alleys or relax on a bench in the shade of trees. But the most important thing is that the park has a large number of different attractions. One of them is the Ferris wheel. Together with the child, rising to a height of several meters, you can see the surroundings of Lazarevsky. It will also be interesting for your children to visit an inverted house. All the usual things that surround us in everyday life are set upside down here. Inflatable trampolines, trains and cars are very popular among children of any age. There are also a large number of beautiful trees and plants.

The address of the city park of culture and rest is Pavlova street, 2.

Your child will love this place. There are bright and colorful attractions on which even the smallest children can ride. Particularly popular are: "Horses", "Train", "Caterpillar" and others. Prices for attractions - 100 rubles. How does the park work? Daily, from 10 a.m. to 10 p.m.

"Berendeevo kingdom"

All children love fairy tales very much. Be sure to take them on an excursion to this amazingly good and beautiful place. Here is a large number of fairy-tale characters made of wood. Children love to be photographed next to them. There are also waterfalls that are literally mesmerizing.
