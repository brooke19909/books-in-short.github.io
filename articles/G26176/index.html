How to change the date in Galaxy S II: detailed instructions

Sooner or later, each owner of a mobile phone thinks about how to change the time or date in the device. This is especially true in Russia, where the clock no longer translates into winter and summer time. Many residents of this country have phones from Samsung. Therefore, today we will figure out how to change the date in the Galaxy S II . What should be known to each owner of this gadget? What difficulties can be encountered when changing the specified parameter? Answering all these questions is not as difficult as it might seem! Even a novice user will be able to, following the suggested instructions, change the date on his mobile phone.

Preparation for the process

How to change the date in Galaxy S II Plus? Before looking for an answer to this question, you need to take into account one small feature of this phone. The thing is that the owners of modern smartphones often have problems with changing the date and time. Why?



All due to the synchronization of these parameters with the Internet. How to change the date in Galaxy S II? First you need to turn off the synchronization of time and calendar with the Internet. The idea is realized in just a few movements.

More precisely, the time and date synchronization is disabled as follows:

  1. Go to the main menu of the included device.
  2. Go to the "Settings" item.
  3. Scroll through the list to the very end. Click on "Date and time".
  4. Click on the first line in the window. It is called "Time and Date."
  5. A short list of options will appear. It is necessary to put a check mark next to the "Turn off" function.

After saving this setting, you can talk about successfully disabling synchronization. Now you can think about how to change the date in the Galaxy S II.

About changing settings

In fact, everything is extremely simple. Changing the date or time on your Galaxy S II mobile phone takes a few minutes.

In order to bring the idea to life, you will have to follow some instructions. They look as follows:

  1. Go to the main menu of the smartphone. Click on the gear image ("Settings" menu).
  2. Select the line "Date and time".
  3. Examine the information that appears. Changing the date occurs after touching the "Set Date" line. Here (the menu item "Set time") you can set the clock.
  4. Using the arrow keys, adjust the day, month and year displayed on the device.
  5. Click on the "Install" button.

This is how the main parameters for displaying date and time on Galaxy S II mobile phones change. There is nothing special about it!

Parameter Issues

Sometimes even the proposed steps do not help. The owner of the mobile phone still can not figure out how to change the date in the Galaxy S II. Each time you restart your smartphone, all settings are lost.



In such circumstances, you can try:

  1. Disconnect the phone from the network. It is necessary to abandon the use of Wi-Fi and mobile Internet. It is advisable to take the device offline. After that, change the date and time according to the previously proposed algorithm.
  2. Take Galaxy S II to a service center. Sometimes the inability to change the date is the result of a system failure or breakdown. Specialized services will quickly help solve the problem.
