Pensions Sukko. Rest and Wellness

One of the most favorite vacation spots among domestic tourists is the small resort village of Sukko. There are boarding houses for every taste and budget. This is a wonderful place where adults and children can enjoy not only a wonderful beach holiday, but also significantly improve their health. Sukko is considered a true all-Russian health resort. On the Black Sea coast there are a huge number of multidisciplinary sanatoriums, but Sukko health resorts have undeniable advantages among their "brothers." The secret is that the surroundings of the village are real natural inhalers. Sukko is surrounded by protected areas and mountains. The village itself is located in a picturesque valley with the same river and lake. This is a favorite place for travelers who are tired of the bustle of the city, nature lovers and those who want to improve their health.

If you plan to spend a vacation in a domestic resort and can not decide on a place, definitely come to Sukko. Rest in the boarding houses of this resort will be remembered for a long time. Here guests can comfortably stay in numerous health resorts. At this resort you can not only have a great time, but also improve your health. The most popular motels and resorts will be presented in this article.

Health resort "Gold Coast"

The sanatorium is located at the lower reaches of the river. Nearby is an observation deck, sandy beach.

The health resort employs exclusively professional medical workers. The sanatorium is multidisciplinary. Moreover, a children's camp operates on the basis of the sanatorium. Accommodation, meals, wellness and leisure - all this is included in the price of the permit.

Sanatorium "Yuzhanka"

The health resort offers a full board of services. The resort is located on the main square. Nearby is an amusement park and a marina. The sea is only 100 meters away. The building is a three-story boarding house, decorated with verandas. The rooms are fully equipped with everything necessary, there is access to the loggia.

On a well-groomed landscaped area is a spa, parking, playground, private beach. Meals are served in the common dining room, breakfast buffet.

Pension "Delmont"

Located on Central Street, nearby is the favorite restaurant "Nirvana". The boarding house has rooms of different capacities, but they are all chicly furnished, cozy and comfortable.

Meals are served at the local restaurant, which is decorated in a French style. On the territory there is a swimming pool, you can swim even at night.

Pension "Ivushka"

A boarding house of the wellness type has been known to many since Soviet times. It is located on the very bank of the Sukko River. Ivushka has its own beach 400 meters from the building. In the forest through which you need to go to the beach, there are sports fields. The pension provides vacationers with 120 rooms of various comforts. For living, you can choose the main building, cottages or houses without amenities. The rooms of the main building and cottages have all the amenities, access to a balcony, comfortable furniture, TV. Sukko boarding houses provide full nutrition. It is served in the banquet hall. The price includes meals.

On the territory there is a play area for children, parking, pool, gazebos. You can rent a bicycle and some types of water transport.

Pension "Gulfstream"

Among the mountain ranges, in juniper groves there is a wonderful Gulf Stream boarding house. In this place, nature itself heals. The combination of air saturated with pine needles and a sea breeze makes this place a truly paradise. Children definitely need to be brought here, especially those who have weakened immunity.

The beach is just 300 meters away. The boarding house accepts guests until November. On the territory there is a swimming pool, dry cleaning, bike rental, sauna, cafe, playground. It is possible to book a tour. The boarding house is guarded round the clock.

Meals are provided in the spacious dining room three times a day.

Reviews

You can talk endlessly about Succo boarding houses. Many tourists choose this resort for its excellent climatic conditions, picturesque landscapes, warm clear sea and healing air. Local health resorts are open all year. Vacationers note the ideal living conditions, good service, delicious food and professionalism of employees.

Sanatoriums and pensions Sukko offer ideal conditions for those who are cent comfort and care about their health.
