Holidays in Bulgaria - a trip to the Black Sea pearl.

Every tourist traveling to a particular country wants to get an unforgettable experience and a lot of pleasure. Few travelers know that almost no country in Africa or Asia can be compared in terms of combination of price and quality, as well as the level of service with the resorts that Bulgaria offers. Demanding tourists will find here everything that the soul desires: magnificent sea, dazzling clean sand, many hotels and hotels of various categories, cheap fruits, fine Bulgarian wines and a favorable climate. Holidays in Bulgaria are diverse and diverse. That is why last minute packages to Bulgaria are always popular and in great demand.

Those who like to relax on the hot sand will be glad to have more than 80 well-equipped beaches in this country. And winter resorts are waiting for adherents of a cool pastime. In the mountains of Bulgaria, all ski slopes are perfectly technically equipped. You can improve your health in this country - healing and mineral springs are located at the foot of the mountains.

Holidays in Bulgaria are attractive with a mild, gentle climate. Here you will not suffer from the sweltering heat, because the temperature regime of this country is as comfortable as possible for a good rest. Excursions last-minute tours to Bulgaria are best purchased for the period from May to October, but experienced tourists recommend spending time on the beach in July-September.

The most popular resort is undoubtedly Golden Sands. It is located in a large park with the same name. This resort has become famous all over the world for its healing springs and the most favorable environmental conditions for you. And, of course, on the beaches there is the finest golden sand, which gave the resort the right to proudly bear its name.

Holidays in Bulgaria are also good because there are many resort towns that you must visit, because you can see a lot of interesting things. For example, lovers of underwater fishing will have a good time in Chornomorets, because near this town there is a stunning Vromos Gulf, as if intended by nature for diving.

Fans of skiing also often prefer holidays in Bulgaria to any other. For example, the resort of Bansko is world famous. Here, in addition to well-equipped tracks, there is also a school where professional instructors teach everyone who wants the ability to ski and snowboard. You need to come here not earlier than in December, and no later than in April.

Among the popular Bulgarian Black Sea resorts is Varna. Here you can not only soak up the beach, but also get on fascinating excursions to local attractions. You can also visit the theater, opera, a large number of museums and so on.

The Obzor resort has also gained great popularity. Here you can have a good rest not only for adults, but also for teenagers and children. A large number of discos, attractions, pioneer camps, cafes, playgrounds only contributes to this. The review also suggests that tourists improve their health at mineral springs, die out with healing air and enjoy the stunning beauty of the scenery.

Holidays in Bulgaria are well suited for lovers of antiquity. There are many places in this country where you can see ancient architecture, stone pavements, and feel the spirit of the Middle Ages. Such cities include Sozopol, Nessebar and others.

Bulgaria is a developing country, so several million tourists come here every year. It is an ideal place for families. If you have never been here, then you certainly should correct this annoying oversight.
