Larissa Garden 4 * Hotel - a great option for a beach holiday

Description. Larissa Garden 4 * is conveniently located on the second beach line, just 120 meters from the sea, in the small resort village of Beldibi.



The Turkish city of Kemer is located at a distance of 15 from it, and the center of Antalya is 35 kilometers. The hotel was built in 2004, and the last major reconstruction was carried out three years ago.

The fairly developed infrastructure of the hotel includes a medical office, a cozy lobby, laundry, beauty salon, dry cleaning, ATM.

At the round-the-clock registration desk you can order a taxi, arrange a transfer, rent a car or equipment for water sports, use a depository safe, and fax.

The nearest post office is 800 meters, the same to the police station. The hotel has its own free parking, which does not need to be booked in advance.

The nearest airport is 40 kilometers from the hotel.

The rooms. In the four-story building Larissa Garden 4 * there are 190 rooms of the categories "standard", "family" and "deluxe", designed for a maximum of three people. All are equipped with necessary furniture, individual air conditioning, satellite TV with 12 Russian channels, a direct dial telephone, a paid minibar and a small safe where you can put a laptop.

The combined bathrooms have showers, a hairdryer and everything necessary for personal hygiene.



The rooms have ceramic flooring and each room has a balcony.

On request, you can get extra bedding or use the "auto wake up" service.

Nutrition. Larissa Garden 4 * offers full-board meals. Buffets are served in the restaurant according to the schedule in a separate spacious room. All drinks of local production, including alcoholic drinks, are given free of charge.

Those who wish can make an order from the menu and sit on the outdoor terrace.

The hotel has four bars: by the pool, in the restaurant, as well as a vitamin and snake bar. Here you can taste signature coffee made according to the classic Turkish recipe.

The beach of the Larissa Garden 4 * hotel is private, pebble. It can be reached in seven minutes. The entrance to the sea is gentle, everything you need - umbrellas, sunbeds, trestle beds - is free of charge.

Additional Information. Larissa Garden Hotel 4 * offers its customers to use the outdoor swimming pool with slides, as well as a jacuzzi, sauna, Turkish bath and massage room. Those who prefer to relax actively can engage in beach volleyball, non-motorized and motorized water sports, and play table tennis.



On the territory there is a good animation for children and adults, every night discos are organized.

There is a playground for children and two shallow pools next to the main one so that parents do not worry about their children.

Reviews Larissa Garden 4 * hotel, reviews of which are mostly positive, fully corresponds to this category. The area and rooms are always clean, the staff is friendly and ready to solve problems very quickly, the food is hearty.

The hotel is perfect for a beach holiday with both family and friends.
