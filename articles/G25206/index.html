LG L70 D325: smartphone review

Relatively recently, the notorious company LG released a new smartphone called LG L70 D325. The phone belongs to the budget price category and costs only 6,500-7,000 rubles (about 2,700 hryvnias). What do we get for this price? You can find out the answer to this question by reading this review of the LG L70.

Smartphones from LG

LG products have always been famous for their high quality and innovation. LG is not afraid to experiment. The best engineering minds of the company regularly improve and modernize products. This also applies to smartphones. LG is constantly introducing new technologies, software, etc. Sometimes these experiments are unsuccessful. But despite this, the company is moving in the right direction. She does not stand still. With each new announcement, the quality of LG smartphones is improving, the company is evolving.

How did the company surprise its fans this time? Could the new smartphone of the LG L70 line live up to expectations? Let's get it right.

LG L70 review Appearance

The first thing you should pay attention to is design. The device has small frames around the screen. Thanks to this, LG managed to make the device more compact. If we compare the L70 D325 with other similar phones, then the LG product has rather modest dimensions. And this is definitely a huge plus.





The layout of the elements in the model D325 is classic. Above the screen near the upper edge is a speaker. Under it you can see the logo of the manufacturer. A little to the right is the front camera and proximity sensor. Under the screen are touch keys. Interestingly, instead of the usual three buttons, there are already four of them. The new key is responsible for switching between SIM cards. Now you can change the SIM card with just one click. Quite an interesting solution that will surely make life easier for millions of customers.

At the bottom there is a main microphone and a Micro-USB connector, at the top there is an additional microphone and audio jack. On the left and right are the keys to adjust the sound and turn on the smartphone. At the back you can see a 5 megapixel camera, a multimedia speaker and the LG logo. The cover itself is made of somewhat rough material, so that the smartphone will not be able to slip out of hands.





As for ergonomics, the LG L70 D325 is okay with this. The keys are where they should, the presses feel good. After a couple of days of use, the phone becomes part of your hand. Thanks to the same frames around the screen, you can use your smartphone with one hand. Also, additional gestures help to control the phone. For example, you can turn off or on the screen using a double tap (the so-called Knock-Knock function), hold a call by simply turning the phone over, etc.





Specifications

The smartphone runs on Android 4.4.2 (latest version). On top of this system, a proprietary shell called LG Optimus UI is used. One of the main advantages of the OS is the new energy-saving mode. The smartphone has a bunch of different functions that help save battery power. And this is a pretty big achievement. LG was able to solve one of the main problems of Android devices.

Perhaps now let's talk about the filling. LG L70 D325 is equipped with a Qualcomm Snapdragon 200 processor, which includes two cores operating at a frequency of 1.2 GHz. As for memory, the D325 has a whole gigabyte of RAM. Unfortunately, only 340 MB is available to the user. Also, the smartphone has a built-in drive with a capacity of four gigabytes (only 1.3 available to the user). Perhaps this is the main drawback of this model. Indeed, with such a meager amount of memory, installing some large games will be quite difficult. If we talk about performance, the phone has a rather mediocre performance. You can run simple arcades without any problems, but you are unlikely to be able to play cool projects with 3D graphics.

LG L70: reviews

Perhaps, nothing characterizes the phone so vividly as consumer reviews. What do customers think about the LG L70 D325? The reviews are pretty controversial. Some liked the phone, some did not. If you add a little specificity, then the smartphone is praised for its high build quality, the current version of the software, excellent autonomy, a good speaker and additional features in terms of energy saving. In turn, the majority of those who bought do not like the low resolution of the screen, the camera, the small amount of memory and mediocre performance.





Total

We can say that the LG L70 D325 is a good smartphone in its price category and an excellent budget model. The device, although it has some of the disadvantages we already mentioned above (like poor performance, poor camera quality, etc.), but at the same time it has a lot of positive qualities (like a unique energy-saving mode, new functions, etc.). Of course, you can find the phone much better and more productive. But for the money you are unlikely to find a device that will surpass the technical characteristics of the LG L70 D325.
