Club Calimera Sunshine (Greece, Crete): hotel description, services, reviews

Crete is deservedly considered one of the most beautiful and developed Greek islands. A resort like Club Calimera Sunshine is fully compliant with this place. A special relaxing atmosphere has been created here, reflecting all the charms of measured Greek relaxation.

A bit about the hotel

This resort is a complex of two hotels - Sunshine Calimera Club, Kreta Annex 5 *. By 2012, the reconstruction of public areas was carried out, as well as the overhaul of the number of rooms. In a cozy green area there are low snow-white buildings, made in accordance with local architectural traditions. It is also worth noting the possibility of relaxing on an all-inclusive basis. An additional testimony in favor of this hotel will also be a luxurious beach that has been awarded the Blue Flag.





Location

In the southern part of the island of Crete, popular among travelers, there is a resort complex Club Calimera Sunshine 5 *. It is located in the cozy tourist village of Kutsunari, which is about 8 km from Ierapetra. The distance from Heraklion International Airport is 93 km.

Sunshine Kreta Club Calimera is often chosen by active travelers. So, nearby are the following tourist sites:

  • Milon Waterfall (5 km);
  • Ayia Photia Beach (6 km);
  • Chrissy Island (6 km);
  • archaeological museum (10 km).

Accommodation

Hotel Club Calimera Sunshine has 326 rooms for guests. So, the following options will be available to you:

  • Double standard rooms can be located both in the main residential building, and in separate bungalows. It can accommodate from 2 to 3 people. The room has a large French bed, as well as a soft sofa for relaxing or additional accommodation. The rooms are decorated with local motives, while maintaining modern functionality. You can enjoy garden, mountains or sea views from your private furnished balcony. Two rooms of this category are designed to accommodate people with disabilities.
  • Family rooms have recently been completely refurbished. It can accommodate up to 4 people. The room consists of two rooms, which are separated by arches from each other with a sliding door. The bedroom has a large bed and sofa, and the living room has two folding sofas. There is a furnished terrace, relaxing on which you can enjoy the garden, mountains or the sea coast.




In the rooms

Club Calimera Sunshine provides its guests with a feeling of comfort and homeliness. This was achieved thanks to such amenities in the rooms:

  • a bathroom with a bath or shower;

restaurants and bars

In the territory of Club Calimera Sunshine there are a number of restaurants and bars that are responsible for the full nutrition of guests and providing their drinks. Guests can visit the following places:

  • Dionissos is the main restaurant with a varied buffet. thrice a day guests are invited to try the best local and international dishes. Themed dinners are also held several times a week, accompanied by folklore shows.
  • The Mamma Mia Restaurant will immerse you in the heady atmosphere of Italy. There are many varieties of pizza and pasta, as well as a huge selection of meat and fish dishes. A delicious addition to dinner is a delicious dessert or a glass of tart wine.
  • Zorbas is an open-air restaurant serving a wide range of traditional Cretan cuisine. Elegant surroundings and a relaxed atmosphere contribute to pleasant friendly gatherings, as well as romantic dates.
  • Kapetanios is an establishment that combines a luxurious restaurant and a cozy lounge bar. The impression of the author's dishes will be even more vivid, thanks to the magnificent view of the sea.
  • All hotel bars (lobby, pool, beach) have a wide range of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks. Here you can also enjoy fresh juices and the most unusual cocktails. Guests can satisfy their hunger with light snacks, pastries or junk food.

Crete, Club Calimera Sunshine - negative reviews

In order not to be disappointed in the chosen place for relaxation, it is worthwhile to familiarize yourself with possible negative points in advance. Many travelers choose a tourist destination such as Greece, Crete. Club Calimera Sunshine Kreta has received such negative comments:

  • high prices for the organization of excursions;
  • transfer from the hotel takes almost 3 hours on a winding serpentine, which is quite tiring;
  • the territory is not very conveniently planned for those who carry small children in wheelchairs (paths are located so that you need to go around the territory for a long time to get to the desired object);
  • in the restaurant's menu there is not enough fresh fruit (even at the height of the summer season);
  • tasteless cocktails with a specific composition;
  • due to the specific location of the hotel, the area is heavily blown by the winds;
  • all the most interesting sights are located on the other end of the island;
  • rooms in the annex are not repaired;
  • due to malfunctions of the sewer system, an unpleasant odor is often present in the bathroom;
  • animators, for the most part, are English-speaking;
  • in case of incomplete occupation, administrators willingly upgrade the room category (of their own free will and at no extra charge), but at any time you may be required to move to a room of your category;
  • the poor layout of the bathroom greatly conceals the spacious area;
  • instead of drinking water, guests are provided with desalinated taste with a brackish flavor (food is also prepared on it);
  • quite boring and monotonous evening show programs.

This hotel can rightfully be considered one of the best on the coast of the Lebanese Sea. Here, guests like well-groomed territory, and comfortable rooms, and quality food. But the main advantage is a huge beach with clear water. Before the trip, it is worth considering the remote location of the hotel, as well as the strong wind blowing.
