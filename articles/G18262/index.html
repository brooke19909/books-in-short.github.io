Human pressure as an essential indicator of health status

Human pressure is arterial pressure, the indicators of which are determined by the activity of the heart. When measuring pressure , the so-called upper - systolic level, which occurs at the time of contraction of the heart muscle, and the lower - diastolic, which occurs at the time of relaxation, are taken into account.

Normally, blood pressure should not exceed 120/80. Moreover, many doctors agree that this indicator of blood pressure is ideal. Nevertheless, the ideal pressure of a person is 110/70, the owners of which can only be healthy people. Of course, it is necessary to strive for this indicator - to strengthen your health and try to maintain such a result for many years.

It is generally accepted that with age, body weight tends to increase, and at the same time, an increase in blood pressure indicators is also an age norm. First of all, reconsider your lifestyle: maybe there is something that needs to be changed and a few steps taken towards your health. If before hypertension was considered a disease of the older generation, today it is "getting younger." Young people, barely reaching the age of 25, begin to suffer from this ailment. However, recognizing the disease in time, it is possible to cope with it, it is only necessary to eliminate the causes of its occurrence.

  • Get rid of excess weight, which increases the risk of hypertension.
  • Cut back on salt.
  • Reduce alcohol consumption to a minimum.
  • If you smoke, try to get rid of this addiction.

Blood pressure instability is a normal phenomenon in the physiology of the human body.
