Knit pullover for women

If you want to wear a warm, pleasant for the body thing that no one else in the world will have, then knit a pullover for women with knitting needles. In order to clearly see patterns or drawings on it, they take non-fluffy yarn.

Knitted pullover with knitting needles looks beautiful . The female style allows for more patterns than the male, so there is where to expand your imagination. In this photo, several types of knitting are clearly visible. All of them together allowed us to create a very spectacular thing.





Where and how will we do the waist?

Waistline emphasizes the figure "elastic band". This knitted pullover with knitting needles is female, so charming that any fashionista will like it. And the line of elastic, which should be at least 15 cm high, will make the figure even more feminine and attractive.

The elastic band is 2x2, that is, 2 facial ones are knitted first, then 2 wrong ones, and so on until the end of the row. Now the product is turned upside down, and the female pullover is knitted with knitting needles already on the inside. Where there was a front, they tear the wrong side, and where there was a wrong side, they knit the front one. The waist line was perfect, you can continue needlework.

Knitting: Women's pullover - create a pattern

After completing the waistline, knit with the front stitch: on the face, and on the wrong side, respectively. Further, a height of 5 cm on the line of the blades creates a very interesting pattern, which is not difficult to make. To do this, knit 3 loops, 1 wrong side, and so on until the end of the row. On the wrong side, we slightly shift the picture. To do this, we knit the first loop front, then 3 purl and so on to the end of the row. Now on the face at the very beginning you need to knit the first loop of the facial. The second - wrong side, then 3 facial, then 1 wrong side, 3 facial, 1 wrong side, 3 facial and so on until the end of the row. Turn the product over to the wrong side. We knit: 2 wrong, 1 front, 3 wrong, 1 front, 3 wrong and so on.

As a result, stripes of three facial loops that are shifted to the right are obtained on the face. Same drawing

We wash on the shelf and sleeves of the product.

Drawing "rhombuses"

Rhombuses will look spectacular on knitwear. The purl loops located inside and outside the rhombuses emphasize their relief. Rhombuses can be made of any length and width. The main thing is to observe proportionality. For example, their widest place will be 7 loops long, then the rhombus should have 5-7 rows in height.

  • 1st row: start from the bottom of the rhombus on the front side, knitting 3 loops wrong, then 2 facial and again 3 loops wrong.
  • 2 row: on the reverse side, the rhombus, as it were, begins to expand. Therefore, first knit 2 facial, 2 wrong, 1 facial, 2 wrong, 2 facial.
  • 3 row: 1 wrong side, 2 facial, 3 wrong side, 2 facial, 1 wrong side.
  • 4 row, as the 2nd.
  • 5 row, as the first.
