Hotel David, Adler: overview, description, rooms and reviews of tourists

Hotel "David" (Adler) is very favorably located on the banks of the Mzymta River. The path from it to the main transport hub is only 10 minutes. The largest airport, Adler-Sochi, is 3.5 kilometers away. In each room, regardless of class, there is a comfortable loggia on which any guest can enjoy sunbathing with pleasure.



In addition, guests of the hotel are provided with a table tennis court, the stay on which is not paid, and time is not limited by any limit. Also available for holidaymakers is a restaurant with an extensive menu.

Visitors check in at 2 pm, and eviction takes place at 12:00.

Number of rooms





Of particular note are the rooms at the David Hotel. Adler hotels are famous for their comfortable living conditions. This complex is no exception. "David" has 60 rooms of various classes, occupying six floors. Each of them has a split system, refrigerator, TV, balcony, bathroom with shower or bathtub, all necessary accessories. Each room can be equipped with a rollaway bed. Meals are provided in a specially equipped dining room, the capacity of which exceeds 120 people.

All Adler infrastructure facilities are within walking distance of the hotel. In the central part of the city there is a magnificent promenade, a huge number of shops, an amusement park, there is a children's cafe on the promenade. Nearby is a good grocery market with an abundance of goods. Nearby stretch 2 beaches - children and adults. A park for children called Southern Cultures is also located near the hotel.

Services

Vacationers are provided with an ironing board, a room for playing billiards, a free guarded parking area. The hotel reception offers a free kettle and everything you need for making tea or coffee.

For those who want to go to the city, there is a transfer, it costs, by the way, very inexpensively. Accommodation prices are very low, each room is equipped with a double bed, an armchair, a dressing table, a coffee table, a wardrobe or a dressing room.

Each room has a TV and fridge, a bathroom with a shower and all the necessary accessories, a bed, an armchair and a sofa. For accommodation you need to pay in advance by credit card or in cash, and in addition you must provide identification documents.

Features of accommodation





Teenagers over 15 years old are allowed to register. If the child is younger than this age, then registration is done automatically with the parents.

For foreigners who want to relax in the hotel, the administration kindly provides documents for a visa for international movement. A child whose age does not exceed 7 years is accommodated together with a parent or guardian. No children's beds. Dogs are welcome at the hotel. The weight of the animal should not be more than 5 kilograms.

Additional services

Internet connection is provided free of charge both in the hotel lobby and in each room. Near the complex is a beautiful garden with many figures of landscape design. The reception has a convenient safe for storing valuable property.
