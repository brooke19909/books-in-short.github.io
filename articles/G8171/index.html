Home economy. Personal finance management. How to keep a home budget

The home economy is particularly interested in those who have just escaped from the care of their parents and gone for free bread. And if father and mother took care of this before, now the young man has to do it. Here is the management of personal finances and will be considered in the framework of this article.

general information





The home economy implies the solution of arising economic and personal issues and problems due to available resources. The primary are the physiological needs of a person - water, food and sleep. After that, it is necessary to solve the issue of comfort - what to wear so as not to freeze, and where to live. This is the minimum basis that must be solved in order to exist peacefully. Of course, you can raise more questions about the rest, and about self-realization, self-development and many more different things, but for now we will concentrate on food, sleep, water and clothes.

So that the body was strong, you need proper and high-quality nutrition. Suitable water is also required. All this needs to be supported by financial investments. The home economy almost never has a problem with this, since making money on it is still not problematic. In extreme cases, you can farm and grow food for yourself. But still, we are interested in what directly to buy it. In order to save personal finances, one should be interested in the venues of fairs, where you can buy potatoes, cabbage, corn and other gifts of land grown on the land by the same farmers. In addition, the high concentration of sellers and buying directly from manufacturers contribute to the opportunity to spend less money. Thanks to this, a home budget is saved.

First steps





But how is the home economy optimally organized? Planning, optimization, and saving have a big impact here. We very often like to nod their heads toward American millionaires.   1. Form a family budget. You need to familiarize yourself with the basic methods of managing all means, consider the pros, cons and make sure that this process is not tedious and burdensome.

Planning, drafting and optimization

So, home accounting now needs to be organized. The family budget, alas, often does not forgive miscalculations, and this must be approached with full responsibility. Initially, it is necessary to analyze the cash flows that a person or a cell of society receives. After that, you need to do planning. Here it is necessary to highlight the short and long term prospects. The first includes the solution of problems that are now and will not go anywhere. So, it is necessary to pay utility bills, food, transportation expenses. All this must be taken into account and must be reflected in the short term of the family budget. This will allow you to build a balanced budget that will satisfy basic needs. But what about the long term? This should include who there is a desire to become. So, if there is a goal to become an entrepreneur with multimillion-dollar incomes, then you need to take care of the basis. In other words, it is necessary to provide that some personal finances should be directed to a certain deposit account in a bank or to a mattress, for subsequent withdrawal at the right time, when a new life will start. In order to evaluate your strengths, you can initially create three types of budgets: optimistic, realistic and pessimistic, and then evaluate what is better at it and act within the framework of the tools used earlier. Although a triple approach can be applied in the future, if you consider that it is necessary and effective.

In addition, you can monitor your health so that you do not have to subsequently spend significant funds on medications. If desired and possible, you can notice a car / public transport on a bicycle. Thus, it will be both savings and health benefits.

Create your budget

So, you need to evaluate how much you can count on. It is best if you rely on data from last year during such an operation. Thanks to this, it will be possible to have an approximate basis for spending. Indeed, if the budget is drawn up for the first time and there is no true data, it may turn out that a lot has not been taken into account, and the expenses have been underestimated. True, it is necessary to make adjustments to reflect inflation. Since there is no faith in official data published by the government, the percentage of the increase in utility bills can be taken as a reference point. Such elements of the home economy will make it possible to adopt a family budget close to reality. Also, it is not necessary to choose an absolutely optimal development path; care should be taken to ensure that various sudden expenses are foreseen. This may be the need for treatment, and pocket money for children, or the banal desire for sweets, when everything is bad and you need to at least somehow console yourself. True, more than five percent should not be allocated to this business. Some of this can be used a little differently. Consider a situation where parents give out pocket money to children. You can not limit it, but go the other way. Here, for example, is one small option. Once a month, children are given a certain amount, which they can spend at their discretion. Let's say three hundred rubles, although you can stop a thousand. This is not regulated in any way, but at the same time there will be no support anymore. That is, if the child spends all the money at once - well, he himself decided so, no sweets, toys and other things until the end of the month. In the future, he will already think about what to do with the funds allocated to him, and learn to handle money carefully, build his budget.
