Hotel Matiate Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Kemer): description, photo, reviews of tourists

No wonder the resorts of Turkey have long and firmly held a leading position among numerous tourist destinations. You should not be surprised, because the sun and the sea, the high-class service that almost all hotels in this country offer, the all-inclusive system, so convenient for families and families, entertainment programs - all this attracts travelers from all over the world.

Turkey Resorts

Judging by the reviews, Russian tourists feel equally good in all corners of this maritime state. Kemer, Antalya, Alanya, Belek, etc. - all these resorts give travelers not only an excellent rest under the gentle sun, swimming on magnificent beaches and the opportunity to try delicious dishes and drinks from national cuisine, but also a chance to see local attractions, many of which are not one dozen centuries.

The fact that Turkey is almost always in high demand among Russians is unanimously confirmed by all domestic tour operators. The cost of tours here depends not only on the category of the selected hotel and on the prestige of a particular resort, but also on the time of year. For example, in July Turkey is in the midst of a high season, and therefore the prices are appropriate, but in October you can relax here much cheaper.

Reasons for the popularity of Kemer

Being relatively small, this resort town is already well known to the Russians, who have long been fond of its hotels, built on the very shore of the sea with its beautiful beaches. Translated from Turkish Kemer means "belt." The resort is famous not only for its magnificent nature, but also for inexpensive hotels offering quality service and a fairly developed infrastructure.

First of all, those tourists who prefer to spend a beach vacation come here. This resort has many excellent pebble and sand bathing areas. Most hotels have their own beaches, where vacationers can not only swim and sunbathe, but also ride a banana, parachute and scooters, go on excursions by boat and so on. Therefore, there are rarely winds, and waves at sea.

Here, every tourist can find an option to stay in their wallet - from budget economy guest houses to luxurious apartments. Almost all hotels are suitable for a relaxing holiday with family. Moreover, many of our compatriots for relaxation choose Kemer and its surroundings, for example, Beldibi, where this four-star hotel was built.

Description

Matiate Hotel 4 * was built in 2004 on the second line from the sea. This hotel complex has a fairly convenient location. Matiate Hotel 4 * is located in the village of Beldibi, twelve kilometers from Kemer. The international airport is 40 km away. This is a definite plus for those who come to rest with the kids, who do not tolerate a long transfer. In less than an hour, tourists in comfortable shuttles get to the hotel. A private sandy beach is three hundred and fifty meters away. The Lycian ancient city of Phaselis is a twenty-minute drive away.

The area on which the Matiate Hotel 4 * complex is located (Kemer, Beldibi) is a little more than six thousand square meters. But, despite such a small territory, it fit all the infrastructure needed for a comfortable stay.

There is a bus stop near the hotel entrance, within walking distance of shops and restaurants. The last major renovation at Matiate Hotel 4 * was carried out in 2015. With travel agencies, this hotel is positioned as a suitable option for a family vacation: all the conditions for children are provided here.

Infrastructure

Being a four-star, Matiate Hotel 4 * (Kemer) has a fairly developed infrastructure. In its administrative building there is a hairdresser, a medical room for emergency assistance (paid service), laundry, dry cleaning. There is a small shop on site, there is an inexpensive souvenir kiosk. On the ground floor in the main building there is a juicer, a coffee machine, as well as an ATM. There is also a point where you can exchange currency.

Matiate Hotel 4 * has its own small parking. It is provided to residents free of charge and without reservation.

rules

Reviews about Matiate Hotel 4 * are mostly positive. Check-in to the rooms is carried out without undue delay, promptly, regardless of the time prescribed by the rules. With free room, customers expect a maximum of fifteen minutes. At the reception desk, a check-out is issued to every person settling in, which describes all the services provided at the hotel.

Upon departure, vacationers must vacate the rooms they occupy until twelve in the afternoon. For convenience, customers are allowed to pay the bill not only in cash, but also with plastic cards. Those who prefer the latter option, in order to avoid misunderstandings, it is recommended to discuss in advance with the administration all issues of interest.

Nutrition

Matiate Hotel 4 *, like the vast majority of Turkish hotels, operates according to the generally accepted all-inclusive concept, valid from eight in the morning until midnight. This food system includes breakfast and dinner - normal and late, lunch, the ability to get snacks throughout the day, as well as soft and alcoholic drinks of local manufacturers. You must pay separately for imported whiskey, beer, champagne, fresh and ice cream.

Breakfast is served from eight to ten in the morning, lunch is from twelve to two in the afternoon, and dinner is from half past six to nine in the evening. Meals are presented as a buffet.

The restaurant, designed for two hundred seats in a closed room and eighty - on the terrace, is open by reservation. In addition, the Matiate Hotel 4 * has three bars, including the pool.

For children

Many tourists come to this hotel with their children. For their little guests in Matiate Hotel 4 * all conditions are created.
