Holiday Village "Salute" (Verkhnyaya Sysert) - the best place for leisure

The recreation center "Salyut" (Yekaterinburg) is attractive for vacationers because it is located in the ecological zone on the peninsula of Verkhnesysert pond. Here you can completely relax surrounded by Ural nature.

Description of the area

The recreation center "Salute" is 60 km from Yekaterinburg. It is located in the Sverdlovsk region in the village of Verkhnyaya Sysert. Here, a real forest with centuries-old pine trees surrounds the camp site, and thanks to this, the local air is especially clean. The beautiful nature is adorned by a large pond and a clean beach. From the windows, vacationers can enjoy the forest landscapes. In the summer, the local territories are rich in mushrooms and berries, which can be picked plenty.



Base Accommodation

The Salute recreation center (Sysert) receives 140 guests at a time each summer, and in the winter there is an opportunity to accommodate 80 people. To do this, the camp site has rooms for guests with different incomes. Not only buildings were built on the territory, but also cottages and summer houses.

Services and entertainment at the camp site

The recreation center "Salute" (Verkhnyaya Sysert) for guests organized a bath with a pool and a sauna. There is a massage room and a solarium. In the evening, you can head to the disco bar or to the dance floor under a canopy. A children's room is open for children, where a nanny works for free.

Here you can play football, paintball, tennis or billiards. There are gazebos and barbecue facilities on site. In winter, the dance floor is poured under the ice rink. Inventory rental.

Beach

It is especially pleasant that the Salyut recreation center (Verkhnyaya Sysert) prepared a spacious beach area, on which there is a volleyball court, umbrellas and sunbeds. There is also a place for playing futsal. You can rent a catamaran or a boat.

In the evening, the beach area is beautifully lit by lanterns. Every Friday and Saturday a bonfire is made and a disco begins, which lasts from 23.00 to two at night.

Fishing

Another advantage that can please the recreation center "Salute" (Verkhnyaya Sysert) is full fishing. The local pond is home to roach, tench, bream, pike and perch. Also a few years ago they launched a silver carp, grass carp and carp.

Other features of the camp site



Some people like to celebrate significant events on the basis of Salute. The local cafe can accommodate up to 60 visitors. But if desired, the event can be organized in the fresh air - on the dance floor under a canopy. For business guests there are two conference rooms.

It is worth noting that the cafe offers vacationers three meals a day.

It is interesting that in the summer you can send your child to a children's race. For young people, excursions and dance evenings are organized at the base. And most importantly - meals five times a day.

In this place you can relax well, not only in the summer. In winter, interesting events are also organized for guests of the base.
