What water park in Crete do you visit?

We go to exotic resorts primarily over the warm sea, the gentle sun and a lot of fresh juicy fruits. But if the rest does not last 3-5 days, then lazy lying on the beach will soon become boring. But not in Greece, on the island of Crete! It provides a lot of entertainment for children and their parents, as well as large and noisy companies. A place that everyone will enjoy is the water park. There are four of them in Crete! Arrange a tour of them?





Water City - water paradise in Crete

The first and largest water entertainment complex is Water City, which spreads over 80,000 square kilometers and fits all that is needed for a perfect holiday. How to get here? It is located near the village of Hani-Kokini and a few kilometers from the fashionable resorts of Malia and Hersonissos. This is the most interesting water slide park in Crete. Water City Waterpark will not make bored either adults or children. What is there?

Party place Star Beach

In the same resort, in the very center of Hersonissos, there is another water park. In Crete, this is a favorite place for party-goers (Star Beach). In addition to water, there are also usual attractions, as well as a spa, many interesting bars and restaurants, as well as a special area for parties. Young people love this place. By the way, the peculiarity of this park is that the entrance here is free, but you will have to pay for each individual attraction.

Stone Limnoupolis

The third water park in Crete - Limnoupolis - is located in the province of Chania. Its peculiarity is in a fancy design with a predominance of artificial stone. Caves, small mountains and grottoes are made of it. Very beautiful and lovely place.
