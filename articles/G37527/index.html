Hotel Long Beach Garden 4 * (Thailand, Pattaya): photos and reviews

Wongamat Beach is the most favorite vacation spot for both visiting tourists and the people of Thailand themselves. Inhabitants of the northern part of Pattaya also love this place, since it is the most well-groomed and picturesque coastal area with azure water and clear white sand. This area is chosen for families with small children and retirees, and just those who do not like active parties, which, incidentally, can still be visited outside the area. Near the beach, guests are greeted by many hotels that combine affordable prices and high quality service. One of those is the Long Beach Garden Hotel 4 *.

Why choose a vacation on Vogamat beach?

As for the beaches, the length of their territory is more than 1.5 kilometers. The entire recreation area is strewn with golden white sand, in which you can rarely find the smallest stones. The central beach area, which is fully equipped for a comfortable rest for any tourist, is considered to be a favorite place of calm relaxation for travelers.

There are secluded, quiet corners both in the north and in the south of Thailand, however, this does not mean that there are fewer tourists here, it all depends on the season chosen for the trip. How to get there

In order to get to this place, you need to take a bus passing through the second street, drive to its end and return to the beach or get off at 18 Naklua Soi Street and walk a few more meters. The bus route ends near a sculpture depicting dolphins. By the way, the fare in any transport costs about 10 baht. For those who do not want to languish in a stuffy bus, there is the opportunity to catch a taxi, but this does not mean that walking already completely lose their relevance.

Upon reaching da Jomtien, you can also catch a bus called Songteo, but a ticket for it will cost 20 baht instead of 10. These buses drive through Thai streets such as Tappraya, Second, Beach, and Dolphin Sculpture. Here the tourist has a choice: you can transfer to the songteo and get to Naklua, or you can walk for a few more minutes, enjoying the fresh air. Walking takes 20 minutes, and a taxi can take 15, but it will cost 100 baht.

Room Service





Even if you arrive at the hotel well after midnight, you will be kindly provided with a room. If desired, it can be changed to a more suitable one. Please note that not all apartments have sea views. There are rooms from whose windows you can see the unfinished building of the neighboring hotel. The sight is not pleasant. Another minor minus is the smell of dampness. But for Thailand, this is considered the norm due to high humidity. You quickly get used to this nuance.

The atmosphere in the room is comfortable. There are beds of impressive sizes, furniture made of wood. In some places, you can observe traces of the hands of previous guests in the form of scratches or broken pens. Please note that the railing of the balconies are low. Therefore, residents of the upper floors should be careful about the surroundings. But it's worth it, because the melodious sound of the surf comes to your ears, ships float before your eyes. And even the unpleasant specific smell, which Thailand is famous for, will not be able to spoil a pleasant experience. Balconies are designed to enjoy the scenery while sitting on them. Yes, and the Thais themselves are not tall.
