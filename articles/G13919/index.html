Reviews

According to user reviews, it is recommended that you choose a JVC home theater projector for your home theater by identifying the main tasks that you plan to solve with the device, as well as what content you will play. In this case, you should pay attention to one of the main parameters - resolution. For example, a projector with a resolution of 1920x1080 is required to demonstrate film production in full HD. And for displaying a Power Point presentation or watching a training DVD video, a resolution of 1024x768 or 1280x800 is enough.

In their advice, owners of JVC projectors advise choosing a device for home theater systems that provides the most deep and accurate color reproduction and high contrast. When buying, also take into account the lighting and the size of the room.
