How to send money from "Tele2" to another number?

Service activation and deactivation

There is no need to specifically activate the Mobile Transfer service; it is available by default to all subscribers who are individuals.

If you want to ban the Mobile Transfer service from your phone number, you need to dial * 145 * 0 # and press the call button. The answer will come to you in the form of SMS. If you decide to return the mobile transfer option back, call the operator at 611.

Help information about the service can be obtained by dialing * 145 #, and then following the network prompts.

How much does mobile transfer cost?

Of course, this service is not provided free of charge, a commission is charged for it. If you make a mobile transfer to the number of the Tele2 operator in your region, then the operation will cost you 5 rubles, regardless of how much you transfer.

In all other cases, in addition to 5 rubles, 5% of the transfer amount will also be deducted from you.
