Abkhazia Waterfall "Men's Tears": description of the attraction, photos and reviews

Abkhazia is a country that amazes with the beauty of its nature and the diversity of natural landscapes. There are high mountains, and green valleys, and virgin forests, and water cascades. What sights should you see if your vacation destination is Abkhazia? Waterfall “Men's Tears” is one of the unique natural objects that everyone will like.

Beautiful waterfall with an unusual name





Abkhazians are sensitive to ancient traditions, and even today they believe in many of them. In this country, legends and tales are associated with almost every place. Do not be lazy to ask locals and enjoy interesting stories if your vacation destination is Abkhazia. Waterfall "Men's Tears" got its name for a reason. According to an ancient legend, once lived in this area a girl named Amra. She was incredibly beautiful and modest. Every day Amra went to graze goats on the banks of the Bzybi River, and in order not to be bored during work, she sang songs. The sounds of the beautiful voice of the beautiful woman were heard high in the mountains where her beloved Adgur was hunting. A mermaid lived in the waters of the Bzybi River , watching Amra daily. Once the water maiden could not restrain envy of the earthly beauty and pushed her into the water. The river god punished the envious mermaid and turned it into stone. But it was impossible to save Amra. High in the mountains, the bridegroom of the beautiful girl, Adgur, with his heart felt that trouble had occurred. And then the young man dropped a few mean tears. It was at that moment that Abkhazia found its new attraction. Waterfall "Man's Tears" is the cry of Adgur for his beloved.

The mystery of colorful ribbons





Today, a tall (about 15 meters) waterfall with a poetic name is one of the popular tourist attractions. A narrow, stormy stream runs rapidly from the mountain, which is scattered in a cascade of spray near the road. You can get close to the waterfall, there is a special bridge. A separate pipe with drinking water was separately brought nearby. Here you can buy souvenirs, wine and honey, as well as tie a ribbon. This tradition raises many questions. Near the "Men's Tears" ribbons are traditionally tied to love or fulfillment of desires. It is believed that as soon as the dream comes true, a bright piece of matter will come off and be carried away by a rapid stream.

Where are the "Men's Tears"?

It is worth noting that Abkhazia is a relatively small country, and it is difficult to get lost in it. Where are the "Men's Tears"? It makes sense to combine a trip to this attraction with a visit to Lake Ritsa. The exact address of the object: Abkhazia, the waterfall "Men's Tears". How to get to the sights? The waterfall is located on the road to Lake Ritsa, it is impossible to drive it. If you are going on an excursion from New Athos, you need to move along the E60 highway, turning near the village of Blaburkhva. Tourists are also offered ready-made excursions. Most tour operators offer to visit the “Men's Tears”, “Girl's Tears” and Blue Lake during a trip to Lake Ritsa .

Abkhazia Waterfall "Men's Tears" (photo). Tourists reviews





Abkhazian waterfalls are popular with all tourists without exception. If you believe the reviews, "Men's Tears" is not the most interesting and large water cascade. But still this attraction is fascinating, and also attracts with its accessibility. It’s not difficult to get to the waterfall, which is especially popular with tourists. Near it you can take picturesque photos. Do not forget to tie a ribbon on a wish. Many vacationers say that the hidden truth comes true, and Abkhazia realized all their dreams. Waterfall "Men's Tears" is a great place to test the magic of this country.
