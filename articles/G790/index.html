Hotel Delfin 3 * (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria): photos, reviews

If you want to unusually relax and enjoy all the charms of inspired and idle relaxation, to feel the extravaganza of unforgettable sensations, then you can go to Bulgaria, namely visit Sunny Beach. Delfin 3 * - this is a huge hotel complex that meets all modern trends, which is located here. He is always ready to meet guests. Here you will find cleanliness and comfort. However, first things first.





Hotel Features

The construction of the Delfin Nessebar 3 * hotel (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria) was carried out in 1982, and at the beginning of the 21st century the hotel underwent significant reconstruction and modernization. The hotel consists of one seven-story building, and the hotel territory is 5 thousand m 2 .

The advantage of the hotel is that all rooms face directly on the sea, pools and clean beaches. The rooms are modern, satisfying the requirements of even the most avid vacationers furniture.

Going to the window, you can see wonderful landscapes of the magical coast of the Black Sea, on which just a few meters from the hotel complex is located the magic beach of Cocoa.

A beautiful pool and a chic restaurant are located in the garden, where there are also bars for guests. The restaurant is famous for its varied and delicious cuisine.

Also, for outdoor enthusiasts, the hotel provides tennis courts, places for fitness and a sauna, and does not forget about the fair sex. There is a beauty salon and a wellness center so that they can look great on vacation.

Hotel "Dolphin 3 *" provides the most common type of relaxation - this is a beach. Mostly visited by families with children, retirees, as well as youth companies. Nationality does not matter for the hotel, since we are all people under the same sky.





Hotel rooms

At your choice, the hotel provides a variety of rooms, differing in capacity and price range. The rooms are divided into 2 types:

  1. "Standard" (the presence of two single beds, a folding bed and a large armchair). Room size is approximately 22‚Äì25 m 2 . Can accommodate two adults and a child.
  2. Triple Room (there are three single beds, a large armchair and a folding bed). The size of this room is 27-30 m 2 . Three adults and a child or four adults can occupy.

All rooms at Hotel Delfin 3 * (Sunny Beach) are located with sea views. In modern rooms, there are a telephone, air conditioning, minibars, satellite TV. Room cleaning takes place daily, bed linen is changed once every 5 days, and towels are changed every 2 days. For a fee, in the room you can connect the Internet.





Eating places

The hotel has a breakfast buffet, half board - breakfast + dinner and various cold or hot drinks for a fee.

The restaurant has local and European cuisine. For lovers of a variety of drinks, there is also a variety of imported alcohol and cocktails for every taste. For alcohol, you can order a variety of grilled specialties.

Bathing places

Children and adults can relax and swim in the on-site pool. Hotel staff carefully monitor its condition and often change the water so that guests are always satisfied. For guests near the pool there are umbrellas and sunbeds. If you are tormented by thirst, at the bar you can order any drink for an adult or for a child.

From the hotel Delfin 3 * (Sunny Beach), the photo of which you see in the article, there is a short distance to the sea, about a hundred meters. In time it will take no more than 7 minutes, in a calm step. The sand on the beach is clean and without stones. Umbrellas, mattresses and sun loungers can be purchased on site, and not taken from the hotel or brought from the house.





Hotel services "Dolphin 3 *"

Those who visited the hotel by car are provided with parking near the "Dolphin" 3 *. For those people who have expensive jewelry with them, large amounts of money or documents, a service for storing things in a safe at the administration has been created.

Vacationers in the summer can sometimes overshoot in the sun, outbid, get injured, which is why there is a doctor on duty at the hotel.

At the reception you can provide a service - exchange currency.

Those who like to ride in beautiful places can rent a car.

Hotel "Dolphin 3 *" provides guests with dry cleaning, which copes with pollution of any complexity.

Conditions for the younger generation

As for the children, they will not be bored. Especially for children there is a small and safe pool and a playground where they can swim and meet other children. Parents can safely sit in a bar or relax by the pool and watch the child.

For the younger generation there is also a special baby food.

Absolutely for young children, from 8 months to 3 years old, a high chair and a cot in the room are provided, but the administrator should be warned in advance.





Entertainment for guests

To pamper yourself, you can book a massage, go to the beauty salon or the sauna, you can also relax by visiting the fitness room, gym or playing billiards. In the evening you can go to the restaurant.

The services provided contribute to and favor your good mood and fill you with a sense of calm and relaxation from all the accumulated problems and worries. After an active day, it will be useful to go to the gym, where professional fitness trainers, taking into account your individual, physical characteristics, will select the most appropriate set of physical exercises that will help your body recover after strenuous walks in beautiful places.
