Pools



The hotel has two indoor pools and three in the fresh air (fresh water). One with sea and one with water slides. Tourists staying in villas are provided with eight separate heated pools. Public bathing places are closed from 8 p.m. to 6 a.m. for the safety of guests at the Amara Dolce Vita hotel. Turkey takes care of the health of its guests.

Entertainment

Visitors can have a great time at various cultural events in the daytime, at evening and night shows, live performances by professional groups, karaoke, dance classes, parties on the beach and in the garden, in Internet cafes and at film screenings.

For children

Children from 4 to 12 years old can have fun at the kids club, have fun in the pool and have a wonderful time at the playground. For older children, the hotel provides educational courses, games and children's discos. If necessary, you can call the nanny.

Beach

The sandy and pebble beach of the hotel has a length of 630 meters, showers, changing rooms and a marina. The entrance to the sea is gentle. Like Amara Dolce Vita, Turkey will be remembered for a long time by the hot sun and vivid impressions warming up on long winter evenings.
