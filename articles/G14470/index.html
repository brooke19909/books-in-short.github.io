How to choose a place to stay

If you have finally decided to go on vacation to the Krasnodar Territory, take the time to explore the area in which you plan to stay and select a hotel with a convenient distance from the sea. Holiday villages are divided into several types depending on the cost of living.





Wealthy families with children will have a great time in a sanatorium, not far from which there is a water park, dolphinarium or summer playground for animated performances. If you are going to heal, but do not yet know where it is better to relax in the Krasnodar Territory, give preference to a small, calm village. As a rule, food and accommodation in such a place will be cheaper than in a large sanatorium, and you will be provided with real peace and relaxation.

Regardless of which resort your attention was paid to, ring several hotels and make sure that there are available rooms. Many families prefer to drive their own cars and take pets along with them. To avoid unpleasant surprises upon arrival at the sanatorium, find out if there is a nearby parking lot and whether you can take a cat or a dog with you to your room.
