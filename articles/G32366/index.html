Hotel Middle East Hotel 2 *, Dubai: description and reviews

Back in the late 70s, there was nothing in the UAE except the barren Arabian desert and Bedouin tents. Today, this country makes just a lot of money from tourism. During the season it is visited by thousands of travelers who want to relax by the sea. And of course, one of the most popular resorts in the UAE is the largest city in the state - Dubai. There are many hotels of various price categories. For example, tourists with a limited budget can rent rooms in the two-star hotel Middle East Hotel 2 *.

Where is the hotel situated?

This complex was built in one of the most prestigious areas of Dubai - in Deira. This part of the city is rich in various historical sights, as well as in cafes, restaurants and shops. So the guests of Middle East Hotel 2 * have the opportunity to use the infrastructure of Dubai to the fullest. Very easily, residents of this hotel can get not only to the city center, but also to any other part of it. The nearest metro station is only about 400 meters away.





This hotel is quite conveniently located in relation to the sea. The hotel is considered a city, so it is, unfortunately, relatively far from the beach. But its guests can still get to the sea without any problems. To do this, they need to catch a bus and drive a few stops.

Tourists consider the location of the hotel to be very good in relation to the airport. To get to the place after a long hard flight to the UAE, Russian vacationers can literally 10 minutes.

General description of the hotel

The Middle East Hotel 2 * (Dubai) accepts guests in one multi-storey building with original spectacular architecture. This complex was built a long time ago - in the 80s of the last century. Yes, and renovation at the hotel for the last time was carried out already in 2011. That is, tourists should not hope for too new decoration and furniture in the rooms of this recreation center.

One of the features of the hotel is that there is a night club with Indian dancers on its territory. Many tourists consider it almost a brothel. The music here always sounds very loud anyway. Therefore, this hotel, according to tourists, is not suitable for families with children. In addition to a club with dances, three more such institutions operate on the territory of this complex. Some residents of the hotel believe that clubs bring the main income to the owners of the hotel. Therefore, unfortunately, they do not pay too much attention to the condition of the rooms and the infrastructure of the complex.

The design of this hotel, as well as its decor, is reminiscent of the design and interiors of inexpensive Indian hotels. And this is completely unsurprising. The main contingent of the hotel are the Indians. Accordingly, both the service and infrastructure of this hotel are aimed primarily at guests of this nationality.





Reception at the hotel is open 24 hours a day. Pets are not allowed on site. At the entrance, experienced tourists advise beginners to carefully check the number and indicate all the shortcomings to the staff. The fact is that sometimes in this hotel, upon departure, guests are presented with unreasonable claims regarding, for example, broken plumbing or household appliances.

What is the hotel room stock?

All apartments of the Middle East Hotel 2 * are class standard. For the convenience of guests in the rooms are provided:

  • coffee maker;

  • mini bar;

  • refrigerator;

  • TV and telephone.

Some rooms at this hotel are air-conditioned, while others have fans. Available in hotel rooms and balconies. Showers in the hotel, despite the fact that it is only a two-star, in the rooms are individual. Guests of this hotel can store valuables in safes.

Middle East Hotel 2 *: reviews on rented rooms

As already mentioned, the Middle East Hotel is a fairly old hotel. Worth living here is relatively inexpensive. Accordingly, the rooms here for rent, unfortunately, are not too comfortable. The rooms offered to travelers at the hotel are very small in size. The maids in the hotel almost never come to clean the rooms. The only advantage of the hotel in terms of cleanliness is that in its corridors there are always wheelbarrows with washed sheets, pillowcases and towels. Guests can take them for a replacement at any time.





Unfortunately, there are also generally not very good reviews about the work of household appliances in the hotel. Air conditioners are installed powerful, creating a fairly pleasant microclimate in the rooms. But at the same time, these household appliances work, as vacationers note, too loudly. The presence of refrigerators, tourists, of course, also refer to the advantages of the hotel. But this equipment in the hotel, judging by the reviews, is quite noisy and also not particularly reliable.

Another serious drawback of the Middle East 2 * complex (UAE, Dubai, Deira), tourists consider the presence of a large number of cockroaches in the rooms. The disadvantages of the hotel rooms are, of course, the lack of Wi-Fi. Soundproofing here, as tourists say, also leaves much to be desired. In addition, the staff of this hotel begins to make noise in the corridors in the morning very early. And this can greatly interfere with the rest of the guests.

Room Advantages

Probably the only plus of the rooms rented in this hotel, tourists consider rather comfortable furniture. Vacationers can sleep in beds in Middle East rooms in comfort. Also, tourists include the fact that here guests are offered free tea and coffee.

Experienced tourists advise beginners to rent rooms located on the ground floor. They are the ones in the hotel who are considered the best in terms of comfort and sanitary condition.

Hotel Infrastructure

In the budget hotel Middle East Hotel 2 *, guests, unfortunately, are not offered either a pool or even free parking. Residents of this hotel can leave the equipment rented in the UAE only in the private parking lot located next to it for 10 dirh per hour.

Of the elements of hotel infrastructure in the Middle East, there are only:

  • safe;

  • currency exchange point;

  • luggage storage.

The hotel also has a car rental service, laundry facilities and a business center. Guests of this hotel can climb to the upper floors, of course, by elevators.

Beach

To the sea, guests of the Middle East Hotel 2 *, as already mentioned, can be reached by public transport. But to go to the beach on a city bus, the residents of this hotel still have no special need. The fact is that in the morning at 8:45 a local shuttle bus arrives at the hotel. On it to the beach, hotel guests can get absolutely free. Experienced tourists are not advised to stay late on this bus. Otherwise, the transfer may go to the beach without them.

On the way to the sea, the bus usually calls at several more hotels. However, most tourists do not see this as a particular problem. The fact is that traffic in Dubai in this area is not too intense, and the roads here are simply excellent.





Guests of the Middle East Hotel 2 *, unfortunately, have to visit a paid beach. Entrance to it costs 5 dirhams (about 50 rubles). Sun loungers and parasols on this beach can also be borrowed for a fee. But toilets and showers on the coast, vacationers have the opportunity to visit completely freely.

A feature of the hotel's beach is that there is a park with a small shop on its territory. Here you can buy some kind of drink or ice cream.

If you want to sunbathe on this beach, tourists can simply mats. But in the sand by the sea in this place there is still quite a lot of garbage. This is for tourists who decide to book a place in this complex, you also need to know.

The sea itself, judging by the reviews, is still quite clean and transparent. From time to time, flocks of beautiful translucent fish swim in the water here.

From the beach, a shuttle beach bus picks up tourists at one in the afternoon. It is recommended that guests at the Middle East Hotel 2 * (Duba i ) come to a stop early - 10-15 minutes before departure. Otherwise, they may simply not have enough space on the bus.
