When will the metro come to Odintsovo?

The question of when the metro will be in Odintsovo is extremely relevant both for the indigenous people of this small town in the near Moscow region, and for everyone who often has to be there. The distance to Moscow from here is quite small, but it takes a lot of time and nerves to overcome it daily. A significant part of Odintsovo residents goes to work in the capital. The lack of reliable transport communications creates domestic discomfort and social stress in a city near Moscow.
