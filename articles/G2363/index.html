Overview of the sanatorium "Rus" (Hot Key) - description, features and reviews

In one of the most picturesque places of the Krasnodar Territory is the sanatorium "Rus". The Hot Key will delight you with beautiful nature and clean air. The sanatorium provides treatment in the following areas:

  • gastrointestinal tract diseases;
  • diseases of the musculoskeletal system;
  • nervous disorders;
  • respiratory tract diseases;
  • childhood diseases.

About the resort

The unique location boasts the sanatorium "Rus". Goryachiy Klyuch is located at the intersection of the main highways of the resort of southern Russia. This is the southern coastal slope of the Caucasus Mountains. Due to the unique landscape and nature, many hiking trails have been created here. The main wealth of the hot key is healing air. Its purity is due to the lack of industrial enterprises and a huge number of pine trees. Having made a short trip by car or bus, you can get to Tuapse, Gelendzhik and other coastal cities.
