Dessole Saadia Resort 3 * (Monastir) - hotel for a comfortable stay

Dessole Saadia Resort 3 * (Monastir) is a hotel in Tunisia with an advantageous location near the sea. The hotel itself has everything you need for a comfortable stay. And so most visitors decide to stay here.

Bars and restaurants on site

From ten in the morning in the bar Dessole Saadia Resort 3 * Monastir you can order alcoholic drinks. There is an opportunity to accommodate one hundred people in the room, and on the street there are an additional three hundred places. The pool bar is open until six in the evening, on the beach - the same.





There is also the Piano Bar, the mode of operation of which is from 18:00 until midnight, 60 people can easily accommodate in its room. At the beach restaurant you can order snacks from 11:00 to 16:00, after which, until five in the evening, tea is served. There is a place called The Kiosk where ice cream is served. And, of course, coffee (not free) - at Cafe Maure - from six in the evening until midnight.

And the last place where you can spend your leisure time is a disco bar in which you can stay from 22:30 to two in the morning. Inside calmly fits 150 people. All listed facilities are located on site. As you can see, the choice is wide, each visitor will be able to find what he likes.

Holidays in Tunisia

All tourists, potential guests, before booking Dessole Saadia Resort 3 * Monastir AI, make up their vacation plan. In Tunisia, tourists must visit the colonial avenue of Habib Bourguiba and the medina, in other words, the old city. This is a very interesting place from a historical point of view, it was founded in the eighth century.





The mausoleum of Torbet El Bay, as well as numerous palaces, attracts attention. Fans of painstaking art will be interested in visiting the mosaic museum. And, of course, the main attraction of the city is Carthage with a magnificent amphitheater.

Tunisia is not a very big city, when compared with any other resorts. Dessole Saadia Resort 3 * (Monastir) is located in the tourist area called Skanes - it is located north of the city of Monastir. Only a few minutes walk separates the hotel from the beach - wide, spacious, sandy, with clear sea water. This is an ideal place to relax, which will especially appeal to people who come here just to enjoy the sun baths and the warm azure sea.

The climate in Tunisia is perfect for such a beach holiday. The most important thing is not to overheat in the sun. In the hottest hours of the day you can return to the hotel and relax in a cool room. Dessole Saadia Resort 3 * (Monastir) is so favorably located that from it you can easily and quickly get to any place or attraction.
