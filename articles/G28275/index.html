How to block a Beeline number?



At first glance, the service seems unnecessary. Why block your number, and with it the ability to receive calls from loved ones, acquaintances or colleagues? However, this may come in handy if you do not intend to use the SIM card in the near future from your mobile device, or it is lost. Blocking a Beeline SIM card is not difficult.

To do this yourself, go to your personal account on the official website and follow the instructions. Or call 8 800 700 0611. At the same time, be prepared to give the passport details of the owner of the contract. And in the case of registration of a number for an organization - its TIN and legal address. You can also ask for help at any nearest branch of the Beeline network.

In order to subsequently unlock your number, you will need to send an e-mail to the address specified in the contract in the contacts for mobile communications. Or you need to call 8 800 700 0611, again providing passport data, or contact the salon.

What to do if the SIM card is lost

We have already talked about how to block the Beeline SIM card, now let's figure out what to do in case of its loss. As a rule, this is done when the phone itself is lost. Therefore, remember that when buying a new cellular device, you must record its serial number. Dial the combination * # 06 # and the call key. The screen should display a 15-digit code. This combination of numbers is unique for each phone, write it down and keep it in a safe place. In case of theft, you should call your mobile operator and block the phone, after which, even after changing the SIM card, it will be impossible to turn on the mobile device.



How to block a lost SIM card, they will explain to you by phone hotline. You need to contact the nearest mobile phone salon with documents. The consultant will offer to replace the SIM card and will block the lost copy.
