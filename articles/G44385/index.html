The luxury hotel in Turkey "Dolphin Imperial" is a real gem among sea resorts. It is located on one of the most picturesque coasts of the Mediterranean Sea, in the Lara region, just 15 km from Antalya. International flights are accepted at the airport, located just ten kilometers from it.

The convenient location, the magnificent design of the hotel building and chic interiors have made it a popular destination for vacationers, which accepts Turkey. Hotel rooms

It offers guests 650 well-furnished rooms, decorated in a modern style, with views of the sea or the surroundings and the picturesque landscapes that Turkey is famous for. Hotel "Dolphin Imperial" has 6 categories of hotel rooms:

  • Lagoon Delphin Room - 21 rooms (area is from 36 to 40 sq. M). For guests of rooms of this category there is the possibility of using a shared pool specifically for the aqua room.
  • Family room - 47 rooms (area is 56 sq. M). A bedroom with a double bed, a balcony, a shower room and a children's room, in which there are two single beds.
  • Suite - 2 rooms (area is 58 sq. M). They have a living room, bedroom, bathroom and two balconies. The room has several beds: single, double and sofa.
  • Junior Suite - 66 rooms (area is 45 sq. M.). A room with a shower, two balconies, a single bed and a sofa sofa.
  • Superior - 459 rooms (area is 30 sq. M.). The maximum possible accommodation is 4 people. One room with a balcony, a bathroom, a double, a single bed and a sofa.
