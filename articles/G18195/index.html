How to transfer money from MTS to MegaFon: options for resolving the issue

A mobile phone has become an integral attribute of our daily lives. It has to be used both at work and in your free time. Naturally, you can choose different operators and any tariffs, choosing suitable conditions for each situation. Sometimes situations arise when on one of the SIM cards the funds run out and you need to urgently replenish your account. MTS and MegaFon are among the leading mobile operators in Russia, so for many this article will be relevant. It indicates four ways of transferring money from MTS to MegaFon. We will discuss them in more detail.

Statement



If you have a lot of time and you are the owner of the Mobile TeleSystem SIM card, you can transfer money to MegaFon from MTS in the following very simple way. Find the nearest MTS office, look there and write a statement. It must be indicated in it that you want to receive cash on your mobile phone account. In order for the application to be accepted and considered, the SIM card must be registered in your name, and you must have a passport with you as an identity document. After receiving the money, you can replenish your account in MegaFon through special terminals. But it is worth considering that this method is rather energy-consuming, the MTS office may be far from you, and you will have to wait for the return of funds.

USSD request

If, nevertheless, you are somewhat limited in time, and only a mobile phone is at hand, then you can transfer money from MTS to MegaFon as follows. Enter the code * 115 #, press the call button. You will see the menu of the special service "Easy Payment". In the category "Mobile Phone" you must enter the number to which you intend to transfer money, and the amount. To confirm, you will need to reply to the message from the number 6996. It will come to you within 15 minutes. To refuse the service, you will need to enter 0, any other text is accepted as consent to the payment.



Mobile Banking Application

If there is Internet access on your mobile phone, then you can find and install an application from MTS called Mobile Banking. This service is connected completely free of charge from the operator. You will not need to pay for using the application either. The only waste is Internet traffic, it is paid in accordance with the rates of your tariff plan. In order for the application to work without problems on your mobile, in addition to it, another application must be installed - "MTS Money". Using this program, you can transfer from MTS to MegaFon, transfer money to an electronic wallet, replenish your account, work with your bank cards from MTS Bank.





Online funds transfer

If you are sitting at a computer or tablet, and you have a question about how to transfer money from MTS to MegaFon, then you can not rush to look for a phone. This operation can be performed without using it. To do this, go to the site of the operator "MTS". There, select the page regarding the payment of the MegaFon subscriber‚Äôs bill. A window will open in front of you, in which you need to enter the following data: the transfer amount and the MegaFon subscriber number to which you are going to transfer it. If the payment is made from the MTS subscriber account, this must also be indicated in this form. Click "Next". At this point, an authorization window opens. To enter your personal account, you must enter the mobile phone number attached to the MTS operator, from which the funds will be transferred, and your password. If you have not used this site before, and you do not have a password, then click on the "Get Password" link. After that, you will still need to find the phone, because it will be the message with the password that will come to it. As soon as you enter the "My Account" - confirm the password. Mission accomplished!





Important point

Before transferring money from MTS to Megafon, read the following information. Not every MTS tariff allows you to transfer money to numbers of other operators. Such a service is not available in the Super MTS and Super Zero tariff plans. In addition, it is among the paid. A commission will be charged for each payment. If you used the latter method, then for each transfer you will be charged 10 rubles. If you transfer money through a USSD request, the commission will be 10% of the transferred amount. After making the payment, at least 10 rubles should remain in your account. Its maximum amount should not exceed 1000 rubles. Only 5 payments are allowed per day.

Now you know how to transfer money from "MTS" to "Megaphone", so do not get confused when you have such a need. Right now you can choose an option convenient for you and use it at any time. If you are wondering about how to transfer money from Megafon to MTS without a commission, we note that this option is not currently available.
