The figure from balloons is an excellent decoration of the holiday

Any holidays, memorable or solemn events, especially birthdays, anniversaries and children's events, require special decoration, beautiful, colorful and interesting. One of the original jewelry can always be a pretty figure made of balloons. Such figures can even be a modern and rather original gift, which will always please both the congratulator and the hero of the occasion. You can make them with your own hands, because the diagrams of figures from balloons are simple and understandable, you just need to decide and choose what exactly you want to do.





How to make a flower from balls

Any shape of balloons is made quickly enough, simply and requires a small set of materials. This is a hand pump and, of course, balls for modeling. To make a flower, you need to take only two balls of different colors, for example, green and yellow. First, the flower itself is made.

So, a yellow ball is inflated using a pump, a tip is left (three centimeters), tied to a knot. Now we tie the beginning and the tip of the resulting sausage into two knots, getting a ring. The next step: the ring must be bent in half and twisted twice in the middle. The resulting figure should be divided into approximately three parts. To do this, you need to twist it in two places, and then put everything in an accordion. Now we take it, connect the twisting places with our fingers and twist the three petals. Everything, a flower with six petals is ready. But such a figure of balloons also requires a stem. To do this, you need to inflate the green ball, inflate as well (not too tight so as not to burst) and tie. On the resulting sausage, we measure ten centimeters and twist it, and then we back as much from the place of twisting and twist again, connecting so that the bundle of the sausage is fixed by twisting. Now we insert the resulting stem seal into the center of the flower. The stalk needs to be bent and twisted again to create a semblance of a petal.





Ballfish

The shape of the balloons can be anything, if desired, anyone will be able to make a cute fish. The materials needed are the same: a pump, one round ball, two balls for modeling, paints and two small round balls. First you need to inflate the ball for modeling, but not completely and not very tightly. In the middle we twist the sausage, back out from this place by a couple of centimeters and twist it again, then back out to the other side and twist it again: that's it, the sponge of the fish is ready. The next stage: inflate small balloons and bind them, twisting each other, and then insert between the lips of the fish. Now we have a muzzle where small balls are eyes.



Now you need to inflate a simple big ball, tie it, and tie it around with a long ball for modeling (which also twist together with a round ball knot), and then twist its ends in the form of a tail or simply tie them together with a double knot. The final touch is to draw small eyes for the fish, which is why paint was needed.

Conclusion

About the same pattern is the manufacture of figures from balloons of any size and complexity. It can be bundles of flowers, figures of clowns or animals, air hearts and houses. The main thing is not to restrain imagination and create for your own pleasure.
