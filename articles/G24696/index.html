Ski resort Kranjska Gora, Slovenia: photos, hotels, reviews of tourists

Amateurs and professional skiers like to spend time in Slovenia, where there are many resorts that combine picturesque slopes, long sports tracks, crystal clear air and excellently developed infrastructure. If you want to have a good rest in Slovenia, Kranjska Gora, a small resort town, is ideal for this.



Location and landscape

Kranjska Gora is the common name of a resort town and locality in the north-west of the country, located in the Upper Sava River Valley, surrounded by the Julian Alps, which are partially located in Italy, and the Karavanke mountain range.

In the past, the Krajina region, to which this territory belongs, was part of Austria-Hungary. Now, only proximity to the Austrian border, the distance to which does not exceed two kilometers, can recall Austrian rule. The picturesque town of Kranjska Gora in Slovenia (photos you can see in this article) is located at the junction of the borders of Slovenia, Italy and Austria. The three countries are separated by only a few kilometers: Austrian Villach is 23 km away, Italian Tarvisio - 22 km.



The ski resort Kranjska Gora in Slovenia combines three ski centers: Podkoren, Planica and Kranjska Gora. This is one of the most popular, fashionable and, it should be recognized, the most expensive resorts in the country. It is located at an altitude of eight hundred and ten meters above sea level at the foot of Vitrank Mountain. This is an all-season resort. The ski season starts here in December and lasts until March.

Climate

Kranjska Gora in Slovenia is a place characterized by an alpine climate with snowy and long winters that last four to five months, and rather cool and short summers. In January, the average air temperature is around - 8 ¬∞ C, in July +23 ¬∞ C.

Kranjska Gora in Slovenia: resort description

This resort town has a special festive atmosphere. It is always crowded, because not only experienced skiers come here, but also beginners who only learn the basics of skill. Kranjska Gora in Slovenia attracts winter sports enthusiasts with its well-equipped slopes. Everything is thought out here to the smallest detail for the convenience of athletes. The highest level of slopes in this resort is evidenced by the fact that international skiing competitions are held here annually. The rest of the time, skiers relax here, from beginners to professionals.



Tourists from all over Europe come here. The magnificent nature, sincere cordiality and hospitality of local residents, clean air only reinforce a pleasant impression of this place. Kranjska Gora in Slovenia (holiday reviews confirm this) is an attractive place where the old and the new are bizarrely intertwined: fashionable hotels designed for Dutch and British tourists, with modest taverns and inns for skiers who have more modest wealth.

Lifts

There are still lifts that can be attributed to museum exhibits: low-speed towbars and single chairlifts. In Europe, there are almost none. But it is noteworthy, and it must be noted that all these mechanisms are in perfect condition and, according to vacationers, only add to the charm of the resort. 23 lifts will deliver you to the top, and 18 of them - tow-lifts.



Slopes and tracks

If you have never skied, then Kranjska Gora in Slovenia is the perfect place for you. Professional experienced instructors put on skis even those who saw skis only in the photo. It is important that all instructors speak English. The ski school located here specializes in beginners. Tourists from Austria, Italy, Germany and many other countries come to learn this skill.



Often they come to rest with young children, who can be left with teachers in the children's room, while parents master a difficult science. On the second level there are tracks for those who have already learned to ski a little: the so-called blue tracks. Mostly they are located on Mount Vitrantz. Here they are led by low-speed chairlifts, which we talked about. These tracks will be interesting for beginner athletes.

Slalom and speedways are located a little to the side, for professionals. They do not intersect with children and for beginner athletes. They are led by modern high-speed lifts. There are also interesting cross-country skiing trails here. Experienced instructors also work here.



Please note that in the mountains there are no bars and restaurants. If you feel hungry or just want to relax and have a cup of hot aromatic coffee, you will have to go down to the foot. Relax in such a resort as Kranjska Gora in Slovenia, and lovers of hiking in the mountains. For them, laid routes in a picturesque area. In addition to skiing, vacationers can visit the sports center, swimming pool, indoor tennis court, ice rink. And those who wish can take a ride on horses through the picturesque area.
