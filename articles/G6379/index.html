Italy islands for holidays: tourist reviews

Italy is one of the most beautiful countries in the world. Everything is fine here, from the unusual shape of the country, similar to the elegant boot with a lady's foot, and ending with the appearance of the word "Italy", which appeared in Greece and translates as "country of calves", because the bull has long been the main symbol of Italians. The state, washed from all sides by the seas, surrounded by mountains and volcanoes, as well as preserving the most ancient cultural and historical monuments, is the best place for a fantastic trip.

All year round

Many tourists come to the country on the Apennine Peninsula in order to recharge with pleasant emotions, get real aesthetic pleasure and just relax for your pleasure. After all, the islands of Italy, resorts and cities are perfect for travel lovers in any season.

In winter, better resorts than the resorts of Monte Rosa, Cervinia, Valle Daosta, and you can not think of. They are located at the very foot of the famous Alps and represent a wide range of entertainment - from freestyle to downhill skiing. This type of vacation will surely appeal to true connoisseurs of winter sports. In addition, in winter in Italy, the time of big sales, as well as you will be pleased with the decline in housing prices.

If you are planning to go on vacation in the fall or spring, we advise you to visit Rome. It is one of the oldest cities with a long history. There are still preserved monuments of ancient centuries: the Colosseum, the Roman Forum and the Pantheon - the temple of all gods. But the islands of Italy are best suited for summer holidays. There are about six hundred of them, and each will give you the opportunity to sunbathe on a snow-white sand under the hot Italian sun!

Islands of Italy: tourist reviews

There are all conditions for a comfortable stay of travelers. On the Internet you can find a large number of positive reviews about holidays on the islands of Italy. Let's get acquainted with some of them:

  • First of all, all vacationers celebrate stunning landscapes, the study of which can take more than one hour. In order to get to know them better, rent a car and go with a breeze to explore the local flora.
  • Hotels also receive top rated tourists. Friendly and always helpful staff, clean and spacious rooms, and the opportunity to enjoy the masterpieces of Italian cuisine three times a day. Tiramisu, Italian pasta, panakota, risotto - far from a complete list of culinary delights that you can try.
  • The beaches of the islands of Italy are always perfectly clean, there is no hint of garbage.
  • A lot of cafes and restaurants are located near the sea coast.
  • For those who prefer outdoor activities, there is the opportunity to go diving. You can see outlandish corals, unusual marine life and much more.

In order to understand which resort is best to visit in the first place, look at the photos of the islands of Italy, which show all the splendor of these lands. The blue warm sea and the warming sun - this is the recipe for an amazing summer vacation! Further we will introduce you to some islands of Italy, where you can have a great time.

Sardinia

This is just a godsend for those who dream of a secluded corner hidden from prying eyes. The mysterious and mysterious island of Italy, Sardinia, is located in the center of the Mediterranean Sea. Here, ancient monuments - the famous nuragas, which are large buildings of large cobblestones, have been preserved in their original form.

The most popular holiday destination in Sardinia is Cagliari. Here you can see the Carthaginian fortifications, medieval churches, the Roman amphitheater. In addition, you can find pink flamingos on the beach, according to the legends of local residents, it brings good luck.

If you like all kinds of outdoor activities, then be sure to visit Santa Margarita di Pula. This wonderful place has everything you can for golf, tennis, diving. In addition, the island has water parks, nightly entertainment, interesting shows are held with incendiary animators and fun contests. There are also souvenir shops and boutiques where you can buy gifts for friends and acquaintances, as well as purchase something for yourself.

Capri Island (Italy)

This is one of the most luxurious world resorts where you can completely relax and forget about all the problems under the rays of the scorching sun. The island is located on the shores of the Gulf of Naples, next to another famous Italian resort - the city of Naples. This is a real find for lovers of shopping and noisy fun discos.

Among the main attractions, the bath-baths of the priest of Tiberius, which have come down to us from antiquity, the cliffs of Faraglioni in a bizarre shape in the middle of the sea, on which the world's only blue lizard lives, are distinguished. The symbol of the island of Capri is a blue grotto, named so because of the amazing azure color of the water. You can visit this place as part of an excursion group going there on special boats.

Sicily - heavenly pleasure

It is the largest island in Italy, located on the Mediterranean coast. Familiarity with it is usually decided to start from its capital - the city of Palermo, where tourists can see the famous architectural monuments. Among them, special attention should be paid to the royal palace of the Palazzo Normanni. Then you can explore the underground funeral galleries, where once noble citizens and kings were buried. And do not forget to visit the botanical garden!

Another attraction of Sicily is the active volcano Etna. Its dimensions exceed Vesuvius. All kinds of processions and theatrical festivals dedicated to the heavenly patrons of the cities located on the island are very popular here. These enchanting shows remain in the memory for a long time.

Healing springs and beautiful museums

Ischia Island in Italy is the most popular resort on the Gulf of Naples. This is a unique healing place with radon hot geysers and healing springs of thermal waters. Among cultural attractions, we recommend visiting the Pithecus Museum, Aragonese Castle and the Cathedral of the Assumption of Our Lady. You will get a lot of unique experiences. This is the best place for a relaxing family vacation with children.

The bright sun and the azure sea

A real holiday for vacationers is the resort of Lampedusa. This is a small island in the Mediterranean Sea, which is geographically located between Sicily and Tunisia. Not many people know that Russian Emperor Paul I liked to relax here. Lampedusa is famous for its bays, caves, and rocks. In addition, here travelers will see small, gingerbread-like houses. This island is ideal for those who are looking for a relaxing place to relax.
