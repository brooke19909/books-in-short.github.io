Unawatuna, Sri Lanka: tourist reviews, description of beaches, hotels

Sri Lanka is a popular destination among lovers of beach and sea recreation. One of the most attractive places on the island is Unawatuna. According to reviews, Sri Lanka is a great place for measured relaxation. Unawatuna beaches, according to the Discovery channel, are among the top ten in the world. Is this really so, each tourist must decide for himself.

Location

If you read reviews about Unawatuna in Sri Lanka, then you will probably have a desire to visit this wonderful paradise. It is difficult to say what attracts vacationers in this region, but the flow of tourists does not decrease year by year. Exotic jungle, narrow streets, ocean waves - all this awaits you on the beautiful coast.

Unawatuna (the reviews are given in the article) is a very small resort town located on the south coast of the island just 150 kilometers from the airport. The village is located five kilometers from the large port city of Galle. Unawatuna is located on a rocky land stretching into the ocean, and is surrounded by coral reefs of the Rumassala Nature Reserve.

  1. To the south: Ahangama, Koggala, Talpe, Mihiripenna.
  2. To the north: Bussa, Gintota, Galle, Tirangama, Dodanduva, Hikkaduwa.

According to reviews, Unawatuna in Sri Lanka is the most popular place among Russian-speaking travelers. The city has several diving centers, a post office, a police station, a bus and railway station, guesthouses, hotels, bars, cafes and restaurants. The region has a developed infrastructure.

According to tourists, Unawatuna in Sri Lanka is an incredibly calm and quiet place. The measured life of the local population attracts travelers from all over the world. The resort is family oriented. It attracts with its beautiful beach, surrounded by gardens and palm trees. Many tourists come to the city solely for the opportunity to live away from civilization.

Weather and climate

When is the best time to relax in Unawatuna (Sri Lanka)? The amount of precipitation, the level of humidity and the height of the waves influence the rest. Summer in the region lasts from March to July. From August to February, the so-called winter lasts.

According to tourists, summer is not the best time to relax in the southwest of the island. Turbid water, raging water and high humidity will spoil the overall impression of relaxation. But at this time the lowest housing prices are observed.

In the fall, everything in Sri Lanka is fragrant and blooming; fun reigns on the beaches all night long. The ocean is very calm, therefore, on the beaches of Unawatuna (Sri Lanka), the photo of one of which is given in the article, many couples with children of different ages relax. Heat on the coast is easier to tolerate due to lower air humidity. Favorable weather conditions and the influx of tourists causes a rise in housing prices.

Spring is the beginning of the low season. There are few tourists at this time on the beaches, the ocean is almost calm, but thunderstorms and storms regularly occur on the coast. Spring is suitable for secluded relaxation.

Winter is a high season, therefore it is necessary to book accommodation in advance. At this time, the ideal weather for swimming is set in Sri Lanka. Therefore, all couples tend to come to the island in the winter.

Long beach

What are the reviews about the beaches of Unawatuna (Sri Lanka)? According to tourists, the most visited place in the resort is Long Beach. The beach is called the most beautiful in the region. It is located 130 km from the city of Colombo. The beach is modest in size, but there are always a lot of tourists here. The coast is located in a natural bay, which is protected by reef from powerful ocean waves. At the very beach, the wild jungle begins. There are practically no waves on the beach, as they are delayed by the reef. On Long Beach, couples with kids prefer to relax. It is very convenient to do snorkeling. For children, the western part of the beach, which has a flatter sunset, is most convenient. In the east of Long Beach, the coast is patchy. Sometimes there are bald spots of stone.

Near the beach, hotels of different price categories are built. Here you can even find family-type hotels. But there are no large hotels on the coast, so tourists come here on their own.

In the immediate vicinity of the beach there are cafes and restaurants. In the evenings, a large number of guests gather here. Tables are set right on the sand, and the territory is lit with torches. Such a romantic atmosphere and the sound of the ocean attract tourists.

Jungle beach

A 15-minute walk from the main beach is another not less romantic place - this is Jungle Beach. There are numerous cafes on its coast. Having ordered any drink in one of them, you get a deck chair absolutely free.

Bonavista

Just a few kilometers from the town of Unawatuna, in the village of Katugoda is the beach of Bonavista. The coast and bay are protected from winds and waves by a reef.

Delawella

Delawella is another Unawatuna beach located a few kilometers from the city. Amazingly beautiful place has one drawback. The coast is located along the road.

Budget Housing

Hotels in Unawatuna (Sri Lanka) are many. But most of the housing in the region is guesthouses with a minimum set of amenities. That is why the resort is so popular. It is very popular among young people and those tourists who travel around the island on their own.

Expensive hotels in Unawatuna are also available, but there are not so many of them. The cost of living in budgetary institutions is from a thousand rupees (less than $ 10). However, it is worthwhile to understand that for such a modest amount, you will receive a room without repair with a fan, shared toilet and cold water.

The cost of living in an apartment with amenities (with hot water and air conditioning) starts at $ 25 (1700 rubles).

Which hotel to choose?

According to reviews, hotels in Unawatuna (Sri Lanka) differ in cost depending on the location. The most expensive establishments are located on the beach. Cheaper are located a little further from the coast.

Most of the guest houses and hotels are concentrated along the Welle Dewalaya Road. Those tourists who want to live in the center and near the beach can stay here.

You can also settle along Matara Road and in the Rumasalla forest. Many tourists who are going to visit the resort for the first time are wondering if it is worth to book hotel rooms in advance. It all depends on how demanding you are at the level of housing. According to experienced travelers, inexpensive apartments with good ratings are booked in search engines for several months. But you can find cheap housing on your own upon arrival in the city.
