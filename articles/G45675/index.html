Tax refund when buying real estate. Refund of excess tax

When buying an apartment, a person often gives most of his savings, so help from the state will not be superfluous. It is possible to return part of the money if you perform a certain sequence of actions.

Tax deduction

For real estate that has been acquired as a one-time payment, the excess tax refund is calculated in full if the total price of the property does not exceed 2,000,000 rubles. When purchasing real estate through a mortgage, a person will be able to receive full compensation only if the value of the property does not exceed 6,000,000 rubles.

The amount of deduction, the amount of which is determined by the state, is calculated only from the money that a person paid personally when buying an apartment. Income tax is not refunded for making additional payments, they are not compensated. If the property is acquired by several owners upon acquisition, each of them receives tax compensation in the amount equivalent to its occupied part in the premises.





Features of tax deduction

When a person buys real estate with a price of less than 2,000,000 rubles, then even after a few years you can get the missing amount to get the full amount of compensation, and not to calculate the percentage from each new acquisition.

If a person is simultaneously engaged in the sale of real estate, in which he lived until recently, and then decided to purchase a new property, the proceeds of 13% can be used to pay tax on the sale of his own apartment. This function may be required only though the property has been owned for no more than 3 years.





Persons entitled to a tax refund

  1. People who are citizens of the Russian Federation, while having official income, from the amount of which deductions to personal income tax are continuously made. This is because in the case when a citizen pays 13% of the salary in favor of the state, then he has the right to receive this money, in turn, when he can acquire real estate.
  2. Only residents of the Russian Federation have the right to apply for a tax deduction. Moreover, they are required to stay within the borders of the Russian Federation for at least 183 days. It is noteworthy that this period is forbidden to break, that is, if a person ever needed to leave the country during the countdown of these days, their number is canceled, upon return, the period will again have to be counted from the first day.
  3. For regular travelers, this compensation is very difficult to obtain. People whose type of professional activity is classified as folk crafts are not eligible to receive. It is necessary to confirm that a person receives income from this type of activity. Formally unemployed do not fall into this category either.
  4. Pensioners whose regular income is more than 5534 rubles. They must earn it themselves and at the same time be officially registered at work, paying income tax every month. This amount should officially appear every month with a pensioner, since pension is not taxed.
  5. Minor children. You can get around the limitation of their incomplete legal competence, even though some part of the property is immediately executed for the child. For him, his parents or official guardians must submit an application, provide all the documents.




People who are not eligible for tax refunds

  1. Officially issued IPs, as their activities provide them with the opportunity to receive a monthly income that will be above average.
  2. Legal entities, since taxes are never returned to the name of the organization.
  3. Working citizens who are not registered on the basis of the labor book, but conduct professional activities on the basis of an employment contract or illegally.
  4. Officially unemployed stratum of the population, registered on unemployment.
