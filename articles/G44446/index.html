Hansa Dishwasher Overview

In order to use good appliances in your kitchen, you should choose high-quality appliances from trusted manufacturers. The reviews of the owners of such devices are called particularly successful products from the manufacturer Hansa. Consider all the options that the company offers.

Machine manufacturing

The brand referred to in the article is German, but the devices are manufactured in Poland. Many are interested in the question of why this happens. In order to understand the logic of the manufacturer, you need to learn more about the history of the company.

She began to work in 1957. Her first product was the production of gas-coal tiles. Almost twenty years later, an electric model appeared. In 1992, a factory was opened, which was engaged in the development and production of embedded equipment. Her factory was located in Poland. Two years later, the range was replenished with refrigerators. More recently, in the 90s, the manufacturer added washing machines to the catalog. A factory was built separately.

The company appeared on the Russian market in 2000. Buyers quickly noticed the brand. Dishwashers are currently manufactured in Poland. All products are made according to European and German standards, do not worry. Therefore, dishwashers are in demand.

Hansa 436 eh

The design of this device was distinguished by the fact that it has excellent quality and pleases with a long service life. But what are her characteristics?

This unit is a full-sized device. It is designed for washing immediately 14 sets of dishes. Built-in drying, which is quite convenient. The car is included in class A in terms of energy consumption. This option is the most economical and perfect for those who have constant problems with washing dishes. For one cycle, the device uses no more than 10 liters of water.

Among the functions of the machine are the following: child protection, a convenient display, a well-constructed leakage protection, a rinse aid indicator, and the presence of a timer. Detergent can be used in powder or tablet form.

The device can work in several modes. The option of half loading, quick washing and the ability to rinse the dishes that are most contaminated is integrated. The mode of delicate functioning, as well as a children's option are also available.



Features

Judging by the reviews, this device is sold in silver. Installation and use of the device does not cause any difficulties. The presence of an individual tray for spoons, forks and knives pleases many housewives. Also, thanks to the volume of the machine, glasses, pans, pans and plates can be washed at the same time.

As for the noise level of work, it is at a low rate. This is confirmed by all buyers of this device. Thanks to this, you can start the equipment even at night.

Summary

The described Hansa built-in dishwasher is considered the best purchase for those who are planning to use the appliance at home. The functionality is quite wide, and very little water is consumed. I must say that among all the devices from the company, this product is considered one of the most successful and, accordingly, common from dishwashers.

Hansa ZIM 414 H

This dishwasher has a built-in design. She works with 9 standard sets of dishes. There are four operating functions, specially equipped with leakage protection.



Control Panel

On the control panel, you can see the buttons and indicators. What are they responsible for? The first ones allow you to choose the dishes washing options, and the second ones show which programs are selected, whether there is salt and rinse aid. On and off keys are also available. Separate indicators correspond to them.



Modes

Available, as already mentioned, 4 modes. Among them: intensive, IVF program, "3 in 1" mode and a short wash. The first allows you to clean even the most contaminated dishes with dried food. The cycle lasts 130 minutes. A short wash is necessary in order to quickly wash lightly soiled dishes. The main thing is that there should not be particles of starch on it. The IVF program is needed if the dishes are practically uncontaminated.

Then what is the difference between this mode and the previous one? The fact that, working in this mode, the machine can cope with starch. The cycle lasts 160 minutes. The "3 in 1" mode, as a rule, is considered everyday by many owners. Thanks to him, you can cope with both complex pollution and lungs. The cycle time is 160 minutes. It should be noted that each of the modes works with the drying function, except for a short wash.

Hansa ZIM 636 EH

This model - the built-in Hansa dishwasher (good reviews about it) works with 14 sets of dishes. It has 6 programs, electronic type control, received a child lock and leakage protection. Has a delayed start option.





Control

The device has keys and indicators. The first allows you to turn on / off the device, work with a timer, load dishes. The indicators show the operation mode of the device, the delay time, and also notify that the salt or rinse aid has run out.



Programs

Intensive mode lasts 140 minutes and is suitable for those who need to wash complex dirt.

Normal washing is used for daily cleaning. Cleans both fresh food debris and dried ones. A full cycle is completed in 160 minutes.

The special mode allows you to carefully and gently wash glass products to shine. The duration of the cycle is 2 hours.

An additional quick wash mode allows you to get rid of grease and other contaminants on dishes in half an hour. It is popular with buyers. It should also be noted that the dishwasher easily copes with this task.
