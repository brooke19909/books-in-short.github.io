Hotel Archibald City 4 * (Prague, Czech Republic): photos and reviews of tourists

The Czech Republic is a country in which tourists often come to explore its attractions. There is a huge number of architectural objects that are unique in nature. During their stay in the cities of the Czech Republic, a large number of travelers prefer to stay in hotel rooms, which offer all the conditions for a comfortable stay and excellent service. One of the most popular among them is Archibald City 4 * in Prague.

general information

This vacation spot was opened relatively recently, in 2005. It offers all the conditions that are perfect for a family, romantic getaway or a solo trip in order to solve important business issues or just for tourism.

In accordance with international standards, for the decent living and service conditions that are provided in Archibald City Prague 4 * (Prague), the hotel deservedly has a four star status, which, according to tourists vacationing in it, is fully justified.

The building, which is reserved for the hotel, has a fairly modern look, which was achieved through its complete reconstruction. Currently, reviews of Archibald City 4 * in Prague can often be found positive comments that the hotel rooms have been modernized and have a beautiful elegant interior.



Location

This vacation spot is located in the central part of Prague, near the Charles Bridge and literally 150 meters from Wenceslas Square. Within walking distance from Archibald City 4 * (Prague) are such famous attractions as the National Museum and Old Town Square, where there are huge clocks with chimes preserved from the Middle Ages.

Not far from its location are large shopping centers, shops, as well as places for entertainment. There are several places where you can go with children or with your loved one, and they are within walking distance of Archibald City 4 * (Prague).

The hotel is located in a location with excellent transport interchange. Not far from it there is a public transport stop, from where you can leave in any direction by bus, tram or taxi.



Rooms

All apartments available in Archibald City Prague 4 * in Prague are concentrated in one large building, which is located next to small but very cozy cafes: Arte and Smecky. In total, there are 80 rooms, which are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay of guests with different requests.

Many reviews of the Archibald City 4 * hotel (Czech Republic / Prague) state that the interior, which is available in the apartments, sets the tone for relaxation and a favorable rest. It is decorated in a calm combination of noble beige, white and gray colors, using a moderate amount of lighting fixtures. Guests also note that all the furniture that is in the apartment has a modern look and is very comfortable.

All rooms have an individual bathroom, in which modern sanitary ware is presented, and a bathroom is combined with it. Each guest at the entrance to the Hotel Archibald City 4 * (Prague) will be taught a personal set of bath accessories, which without fail includes a bath towel, bathrobe, slippers, and soap accessories needed for taking a bath or shower.



Room furniture

In the one-room apartments of the hotel, a pair of guests can be accommodated, for which one large bed with an orthopedic mattress is provided. If desired, travelers can request the installation of an extra bed, however, it is provided for a fee.

The room provides a place to store a small amount of clothing guests. It is represented by a large wardrobe, which is installed along one of the free walls. For small personal items, there are two bedside tables, on one of which a telephone is installed, you can make international calls on it, however this service is paid.

In Standard rooms in Archibald City 4 * in Prague, floors are covered with a quality laminate, under which there is heating. This ensures a warm air temperature in the room in the cool season.



Technical equipment

Each room has a plasma TV connected to a satellite broadcasting system. As many tourists note, it also broadcasts Russian-language channels, which is very convenient for guests who come from Russia.

If desired, vacationers can use the minibar, which is available in the room - its assortment is updated daily with fresh soft drinks. In addition, it is possible to rent a safe, which is designed to ensure the safety of small valuables. Such conditions are provided on a paid basis, subject to the daily rental payment.

The entire territory of Archibald City 4 * (Czech Republic / Prague) is equipped with a modern fire protection system. This also applies to rooms with special sensors.

If desired, guests can use the air conditioning, which is in the apartment. With their help, it seems possible for tourists to adjust the desired temperature regime, regardless of weather conditions on the street.



Room service

In reviews of tourists about Archibald City 4 * (Czech Republic / Prague), it is often said that the rooms of the hotel are constantly maintained clean. Once every two days, a maid visits each room and performs cleaning, leaving behind a completely clean room. Twice a week, bed linen and towels are replaced with fresh ones.

All vacationers who spend their time at this hotel can enjoy free Wi-Fi access in their apartments for free. Thus, they can always keep in touch with their friends and relatives, even being with them at a considerable distance.

If necessary, food from the restaurant can be delivered to the room, however, such a service is paid.

Paid services

For a fee, guests of this hotel can take advantage of some opportunities that will make their stay in this place to relax more comfortable.

If desired, tourists can take their belongings to dry cleaning or laundry. As they note in their reviews, these services employ real masters of cleaning and washing clothes, because here for a very small fee they can give things their original clean appearance.

Staying at the Hotel Archibald City 4 * in Prague, travelers can book for themselves and their companions an individual or group sightseeing tour of local attractions. As they note, such trips are carried out in the company with a Russian-speaking guide who knows the history of the city and its most striking sights.

Many travelers recommend taking the opportunity to rent a car at the hotel. For such a car, a free parking space in a guarded parking lot is offered. Vacationers are advised to go on such vehicles to explore local attractions and visit interesting places.



Nutrition

The reviews about Archibald City 4 * (Czech Republic / Prague) often talk about the delicious and versatile food that is offered at the hotel. Here, according to vacationers, a small buffet is organized, where guests are invited every day to eat. Three times a day a buffet is served here, which serves dishes prepared in the best traditions of European cuisine. In addition to snacks and salads, the all-inclusive system offers tourists drinks of local production, which do not include alcoholic beverages.

For children, the cafe has a separate menu, which contains bright and very tasty dishes that delight the eyes of the kids.



Additional living conditions

The hotel offers the opportunity for a comfortable stay for some special categories of guests. These, first of all, include families vacationing with children. For such tourists, Archibald City 4 * offers the possibility of free installation of a crib in the room type, which is suitable for young guests under the age of 12 years. In addition, all parents can use the paid services of a nanny, whose work has a considerable number of positive reviews. If necessary, it is necessary to notify the administration in advance, indicating the exact time.

The hotel also has two rooms that offer conditions for a comfortable stay for people with disabilities. Their peculiarity lies in the fact that they are equipped with custom-made furniture, taking into account all the needs of people with disabilities. The bathroom of such apartments has special plumbing.

Entertainment

A tour desk is available on site to arrange a tour of local attractions. This can be done on a paid basis and, as travelers say, the cost of such a service is quite acceptable. If you wish, you can include a trip to the desired place with an excursion during the purchase of a ticket to the hotel at a travel agency, but experienced tourists do not recommend this, as in this case its price will be much higher than the one set at the place of rest.

Hotel guests can use the hall, equipped for playing billiards, darts and chess. Fans of reading books can check out the on-site library. As vacationers note, it contains literature of various genres.

Prices

The cost of a tour to such a hotel is often indicated in the comments that guests leave after their stay. So, the average price for a weekly stay in a standard double room is about 60,000 rubles. It includes not only rental apartments, but also all kinds of transportation costs in both directions.

Most tourists consider this payment quite acceptable, setting as an example all the conditions provided for hotel accommodation. Their reviews about Archibald City 4 * (Czech Republic / Prague) and photos attached to them can be found on sites of a certain subject, as well as on personal pages of travelers on social networks. Often, sharing their impressions, they recommend going to rest in such a hotel to their relatives and friends.

Promotions and discounts

Often the hotel offers various discounts and bonuses for those who live in its rooms. An example of this is the possibility of receiving a 10% discount if full prepayment is made for accommodation. Moreover, in this case, a prerequisite is the payment 10 days before arrival.

Also, everyone who wants to relax for four or more days will receive a 5% discount on the entire bill issued by the hotel. In this case, there is the possibility of canceling the reservation, and no prepayment is required. The possibility of such savings, of course, pleases travelers.

Nearby facilities

In the vicinity of this hotel there are a considerable number of attractions that tourists from different countries like to visit. One of them is the Prague State Opera, which is within walking distance of Archibald City. On the way here you can admire two major monuments: St. Wenceslas and Jan Palach. Fans of exhibitions, being here, do not miss the opportunity to go to the Langhans Gallery, and fans of nightlife prefer to go to the Duplex Club, where every evening the golden youth of the city and nearby settlements gather.

Within walking distance from the hotel there are two metro stations that can be reached in any direction: the Museum and Pavlova.

After spending a small amount of time on the road, you can go to the Prague Zoo or to the historic castle, where you can learn a lot. With children, you can go to the water park "Aqua Palace". Such a trip will definitely become unforgettable.
