Cactus Hotel 2 * (Cyprus, Larnaca): description, service, reviews

The quiet resort of Larnaca is especially popular with tourists who prefer a quality and at the same time inexpensive vacation. This town attracts mainly those vacationers who are looking at the sea for unity with nature and tranquility, rather than noisy entertainment. Accordingly, the infrastructure of many local hotels is aimed specifically at senior citizens or tourists with children. The Cactus Hotel 2 * is no exception in this regard . It reads that this complex is best suited for families.

Where is the hotel located

The hotel was built directly in the city of Larnaca. The distance from its buildings to the center of the named resort is only about 2 km. And in order to get to the sea, guests of Cactus Hotel 2 * need to overcome about 300 m. That is, its residents can walk to the hotel beach in 7-10 minutes. This tourists consider, of course, a big plus of the hotel.





Successfully enough, the Cactus complex is located, according to vacationers, and in relation to the airport. Flights Moscow - Larnaca, airline tickets for which many domestic carriers sell, land at an airport located in the south of the city. The distance from this air hub to the hotel is only 5 km.

Especially convenient, such a location of the complex is, of course, considered by tourists who came to rest in Larnaca with children. Indeed, in transfer buses with long journeys of young travelers, unfortunately, they often get sick.

The described hotel is located, judging by the reviews of tourists, in a fairly quiet area. Noise does not interfere with guests here. At the same time, the entire infrastructure of Larnaca is located within walking distance - all kinds of cafes, shops, bars and restaurants work near the Cactus Hotel 2 * .

Also close to this complex are such interesting sights as, for example, Larnaca Castle, city fort, Europe Square, etc.

General description of Cactus Hotel 2 *

The complex was built at the end of the last century - in 1997. Large-scale repairs were last carried out here in 2005. The rooms here are equipped in one multi-storey building. Despite the fact that the Cactus Hotel 2 * hotel is of the urban type, it still has a small courtyard. The territory of the hotel looks, according to tourists, neatly and quite presentably.





Please note that tourists are not allowed to stay in the hotel with pets or smoke in its rooms. Reception at the hotel is open around the clock. Paying for services at the hotel guests can either cash or cards.

Unlike many other hotels in Cyprus, this complex does not belong to any major chain. Judging by the available information, this hotel is a small family business.

Room Reviews

Accommodation in the Cactus Hotel 2 * hotel (Larnaca) , according to vacationers, is quite convenient. The pluses of the rooms are the guests, first of all, their cleanliness. Maids do daily housekeeping. Bed linen in the hotel is also always clean. The towels of the employee of the complex are changed at every cleaning, and at the same time without reminders from the guests.

The main drawback of the rooms of this hotel, tourists consider old furniture and a bit of a shabby finish. After all, the complex is quite old by hotel standards, and the repair here has been carried out for quite some time. However, the beds in this hotel, judging by the reviews, are quite comfortable.

Also, the minuses include a large number of ants. Therefore, experienced tourists advise taking care in the hotel rooms more carefully, without leaving crumbs and drops of juice or sweet tea.





The shower room in the Cactus Hotel 2 * also earned not very good reviews. They complain that the plumbing is installed old (except for toilets). However, hot and cold water is always available at the hotel. Air conditioning at this hotel also works, as tourists say, usually in good condition .

Family Travel Services

For children, the hotel is equipped with a good, judging by the reviews, playground. In the dining room of the complex, among other things, there are highchairs for the smallest. The hotel also has a qualified babysitter. She can be assigned to supervise the child for a fee.

What vacationers think about hotel service

The services provided by tourists on the territory of the Cactus Hotel 2 * (Larnaca) are considered by tourists to be quite high-quality. Quite a lot of services are offered to residents in this hotel. And this despite the fact that the cost of living here is very inexpensive.

The pool at the hotel, as vacationers note, is comfortable and good. Even tourists who are very demanding in terms of cleanliness usually swim in it with great pleasure. There are many sun loungers by the hotel pool.

By all accounts, with the service at the Cactus Hotel 2 * guests are invited to a good one. Good reviews on the web are available about the staff of this hotel. The hotel employees behave kindly and cordially towards the guests. Vacationers also believe that some of its employees can speak Russian quite well.





Cactus Hotel 2 *: nutrition reviews

About hotel rooms and the services provided here to tourists on the Web, so there are basically only good reviews. But the food at the Cactus hotel, most vacationers consider, unfortunately, not too high quality.

It is possible to rent rooms in this hotel as simply with breakfast, as well as with a guest house or on an all-inclusive basis. Of course, each of these schemes in one way or another can be convenient for vacationers. However, experienced tourists are advised to rent maximum rooms with breakfast in this hotel. They recommend lunch and dinner in the city.

The disadvantages of the hotel dining room include, first of all, very tasteless food. Unfortunately, they prepare dishes here from cheap products. In addition, the menu itself in this complex is monotonous. In principle, the lack of an assortment of dishes is a common misfortune of all 2 or 3-star southern hotels. But the Cactus hotel in this regard, according to tourists, "exceeded all expectations."

In addition, tiny portions are offered to guests here. Additives for residents in the restaurant of this hotel is not provided. Thus, for dinner and lunch, most tourists remain hungry. The only thing is that the employees of the hotel‚Äôs dining room try to give large enough portions to the children resting here.

Where to eat in the city

The food in the Cactus Hotel 2 * earned about not too good reviews from tourists. Judging by them, it is the dining room that is the main disadvantage of this hotel. But, as already mentioned, there are many cafes next to this hotel. Most of these restaurants in Cyprus offer a good menu, and the dishes are inexpensive. There are a lot of taverns on the promenade - on the hotel beach. These establishments also deserve good reviews.

Sea near the hotel

Closest to the hotel Cactus Hotel 2 * r is the beach "Kastela". Its feature, among other things, is that it is divided into two parts. In one of them, paid sunbeds and umbrellas are installed. In another vacationers sunbathe simply on towels. This beach belongs to the group of municipalities; therefore, tourists are not charged for the opportunity to sunbathe here in the sand.

In terms of cleanliness, Kastela beach has earned good reviews. No rubbish, including small, is lying around here. The sea itself in this place is also not dirty, transparent, calm and warm. The entrance to it is gentle. So the beach "Kastela" - just the perfect place to relax with children.





Instead of a conclusion

Having settled here, you can save money and at the same time spend your vacation in comfort. The only thing is not to take rooms with a boarding house in this hotel, since the guests are fed here (at least that was until 2017) is really not too good.
