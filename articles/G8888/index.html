Ski resort "Olympic Park" (Ufa): description, prices and reviews

In Ufa there is a wonderful resort for skiers and snowboarders - Olympic Park. It is located within the city limits and occupies a picturesque forest zone. The complex is popular among those who like active winter vacations. Skiing is exactly what attracts crowds of fans of this sport. Four tracks of varying difficulty are specially equipped here, so both beginner skiers and real professionals can ride in the park.

Description

Of course, you will definitely like Olympic Park. Ufa is famous for its many resorts, but this one is considered one of the best. It is a modern complex, which is attended by athletes of different ages. They also like to come here to relax with the whole family. Due to the fact that there is a ski lift for children, the child can also learn skiing, even if before that he had never practiced this sport. In order to teach beginners to conquer the mountain peaks, experienced instructors specially work here.



Athletes can safely ride and not worry about their safety, since the surface of the slopes in the park is always in perfect condition. Snow compaction equipment is operating in the center. If necessary, staff can turn on guns that produce artificial snow. There are rental points on the territory of the park, where vacationers rent everything they need for full skiing. If suddenly guests of the resort get hungry, then you can stop by for a tasty meal in a restaurant or cafe working in the center.

Trails

As mentioned above, Olympic Park (Ufa) has four slopes of varying difficulty, with excellent equipment. So that guests of the resort can better navigate among them, it is worth telling more about each route:

  • Green descent. It is suitable for beginner skiers, as there is a minimal difference in height. There are no sharp turns along the entire length of the route, so you can drive along it without unnecessary worries.
  • Blue slopes. They are ideal for skiers with minimal skiing experience. There are two such roads. The height difference on them is approximately ninety meters, and the length of each of them is no more than half a kilometer. There are already sharp turns on these slopes, so the athlete will be guaranteed a dose of adrenaline.
  • The red line is for real professionals. It is considered the most difficult in the resort. In addition to sharp turns, it is endowed with straight sections, so you can develop more speed during the descent. Another beauty of this route is that it runs in the middle of picturesque forests. Therefore, skiing seems even more extreme and exciting.

One thousand six hundred meters is the total length of all slopes located in the territory of the resort "Olympic Park". Ufa invites everyone to visit this wonderful resort. How long does the season last?

The staff of this ski resort is trying to prepare all their slopes as early as possible so that park guests can enjoy extreme skiing and snowboarding in mid-November. For this, ideal conditions are formed here at this time of year, since the air temperature in Ufa in the last autumn month does not drop below -5 degrees Celsius. In December, mercury bars drop to -12 frost. And this temperature is maintained throughout the winter months. Therefore, at this time of the year there are no problems with snow at the resort.

In early spring, the air begins to warm up. For example, in April, the temperature is already about -2 degrees. It is then that the season ends at the Olympic Park ski center (Ufa). In the summer, the resort does not stop its main work and is temporarily re-qualified as a regular recreation center.





Cost of visiting

Having such wonderful tracks and an excellent level of service, it is quite accessible to different strata of the population of Olympic Park in Ufa. Prices at the resort are pretty reasonable. Thus, one lift will cost the athlete 65 rubles, but, of course, it is much more profitable to purchase a subscription. For example, on weekdays three hours of skiing will cost 560 p., And on weekends and holidays - 660 p. It is possible to purchase a subscription for the whole week, paying 3400 rubles or for the whole season for 16 500 rubles.

Those guests who came to the resort without their own equipment can rent ski equipment for 300 rubles per hour for adults or 200 r. for children. Novice athletes who need instruction can order an individual lesson with a trainer. It is estimated at 800 rubles.





Guest reviews

Everyone who has already been to this center says that this is a wonderful place for active leisure. People like the fact that there are slopes for skiers with any skiing experience. In addition, there is a cafe on the territory of the park where they cook very tasty, and also very friendly staff work. Therefore, after active skiing it is very pleasant to sit and relax in such a wonderful atmosphere.
