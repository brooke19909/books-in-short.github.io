How to transfer money from Beeline to Kiwi: methods, conditions and step-by-step instructions

In the modern world, the rhythm of life has accelerated significantly, there is already not enough time to make purchases in cash. Many do not want to mess with pieces of paper and trifles. To solve such problems, payment systems were created that provide users with the opportunity to issue an electronic wallet. The most popular service of this kind in Russia is Qiwi. The company has existed on the market for 11 years, and since its inception has not let its customers down. In this article, we will consider all the ways how to transfer a certain amount from Beeline to Kiwi Wallet.

Opportunities for Qiwi Wallet Owners

There are many services for customers on the payment service website. One of them is the transfer of funds in various ways:

  • transfer to other electronic wallets;
  • transfer to bank cards;
  • translation by email;
  • transfer to a bank account;
  • use of a money transfer system;
  • purchase Qiwi vouchers or request a transfer of funds.

The list of serviced bank cards is standard: Visa and Mastercard. But, besides this, you can create virtual bank cards of Qiwi service.

Terms of transfer and commission

How to transfer from Beeline to Qiwi Wallet: user manual

The transaction algorithm is as follows:

  1. Log in to your account on the Beeline website. We find the tab "Replenish wallet" in it. If you have not previously registered with the payment system, she will offer to do so. Why do you need to specify only a mobile phone number.
  2. Next, you should find another tab with the name "Other Methods". In the list presented, select "From phone balance." On the next page, a list will be displayed for choosing a mobile operator, indicating the transfer fee.
  3. User authorization takes some time, then a window is provided for entering the amount for the transfer.
  4. We confirm the operation by entering the received SMS password.
  5. The transfer is completed, in a few seconds the money will appear on the Qiwi wallet.

Algorithm for transferring money from a Qiwi-wallet to a Beeline number

One of the most popular services for Kiwi-wallet holders is to top up your phone balance. Make it simple:

  1. We go to the Qiwi website, getting to the main page.
  2. We find the tab "Pay" and go to it.
  3. In the presented categories we are looking for "Cellular communication", from the list we select Beeline.
  4. In the columns we enter the phone number and the amount for the transfer. We check the data and click "Submit".
  5. The payment procedure will need to be confirmed via SMS code.

How to transfer without paying a commission?

Qiwi wallet replenishment security

Before transferring from Beeline to Qiwi, you should take care of the security of personal data and financial resources. A number of simple rules must be followed:

  1. Activate the SMS alert of financial transactions. You can always find out when a third-party person tries to use your account.
  2. Do not make transfers from other people's computers or other gadgets, as confidential information may be stored in them.
  3. When making an operation from a smartphone, it is necessary to set an additional password for the program.

What if there is no money to transfer?

There are situations when it is urgent to deposit money into an electronic wallet, but there is no way to transfer from a Beeline account to Qiwi. In this case, an alternative method will help - a quick loan. It is necessary to apply for a loan and wait for its approval. After the application is resolved, the money will be transferred to the account. We should not forget that loans are issued under certain conditions, which include the interest rate. Read them carefully before submitting the application.
