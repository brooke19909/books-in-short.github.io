Hotel Noria Resort 4 * (Egypt / Sharm El Sheikh): photos, description and reviews of tourists

Affordable leisure and quality service are some of the main reasons why tourists choose for themselves Egypt, Sharm El Sheikh. Noria Resort 4 * is exactly the ideal combination of low cost and high quality service. In addition, an important point is the location of the hotel in the heart of Naama Bay among many dizzying entertainment.





Location

Hotel Noria Resort Hotel 4 * is located in a quiet bay of Naama Bay in the heart of the famous tourist area. From the airport to the hotel complex will have to overcome a distance of 10 km. Despite the fact that the hotel is located quite close to the sea coast (only 300 meters), nevertheless, for maximum convenience, guests regularly run a bus between the main building and the beach.





Number of rooms

Hotel Noria Resort 4 * (Sharm El Sheikh) is quite compact. It consists of five buildings, in which there are only 176 guest rooms. Most of the rooms (168) belong to the standard category, and only eight are luxury apartments. The main difference between them is the area. The standard one-room rooms can accommodate two guests, and it is also possible to arrange an extra bed. Suites include a bedroom and a living room with a sofa bed. Another advantage of rooms in this category is a bewitching view of the sea.





The rooms of the Noria Resort 4 * hotel are comfortable, cozy and equipped with all necessary amenities for a good stay and relaxation. So, in each room are provided:

  • bathroom with bathtub, shower and built-in electric hairdryer;
  • own exit to the balcony;
  • individual air temperature control system;
  • reliable fireproof safe;
  • telephone with connection to the international line;
  • TV and satellite dish;
  • minibar, which is filled for a fee.




Infrastructure

In order to make hotel guests feel cozy and comfortable, the management made sure that they were provided with all the necessary services that could satisfy continuously arising needs. To this end, the Noria Resort Hotel 4 * has developed infrastructure:

  • branded restaurants, where the cuisine of different nations of the world is presented, as well as several bars specializing in snacks and drinks;
  • modern business center with the latest equipment;
  • spacious conference room, which can also be used for celebrations and banquets;
  • two large freshwater pools;
  • a safe to ensure the safety of cash, valuables and documents;
  • if you stained your clothes, then this problem can be solved with the help of laundry or dry cleaning;
  • the hotel has its own parking with 24-hour security and video surveillance;
  • for an additional fee, you can get a doctor if the situation does not fall under insurance;
  • wireless and cable internet;
  • many grocery and gift shops;
  • exchange office and ATM, as well as terminals for non-cash payment for services;
  • a bus to the beach and back;
  • ordering sightseeing tours;
  • beauty salon and massage room;
  • guests have the opportunity to travel around the hotel on electric vehicles;
  • own beach with free sunbeds, mattresses, umbrellas and towels.




Children's Infrastructure

Ensuring a comfortable and complete children's vacation is one of the primary tasks of the Noria Resort 4 * hotel. For this purpose, the following facilities operate on the territory of the resort and hotel complex:

  • a children's pool with a depth of about 0.5 meters with slides and the possibility of heating;
  • a game club where experienced animators work, who will prepare an interesting entertainment program for kids;
  • playground with lots of entertainment;
  • It is possible to use the services of a qualified nanny;
  • the cafe has a special children's corner with comfortable furniture and all kinds of entertainment;
  • if necessary, you can request for a cot in the room.

Active leisure and sport

For those who do not like to sit still, and prefer to be in continuous motion even during their holidays, the Noria Resort 4 * hotel (Sharm El Sheikh) offers the following opportunities for active pastime:

  • two well-groomed tennis courts, one of which is illuminated by floodlights in the evening;
  • ping-pong tables;
  • billiard room;
  • opportunities for playing water polo in the pool;
  • horse rides;
  • volleyball court;
  • the possibility of visiting mountain excursions on ATVs or bicycles;
  • many marine attractions, among which tourists most like banana, skiing, fishing and boat trips;
  • scuba diving.




restaurants and bars

Food is one of the most important moments of relaxation, because it is on the quality of products and the range of dishes on offer that the mood of hotel guests, as well as their general impression of this resort, largely depends. So, the hotel complex Noria Resort 4 * offers its guests to visit the following institutions:

  • The main restaurant is a spacious dining room, which also has an extensive outdoor terrace. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served in buffet style. Guests are offered a variety of international dishes, so everyone will find a treat to their taste.
  • Pizzeria is perhaps the most popular establishment, which the Noria Resort 4 * hotel is rightly proud of. It offers traditional Italian dishes prepared according to the classic recipe. In addition, the chefs of the restaurant offer you many original variations of pizza, there is also the opportunity to experiment with toppings.
  • A traditional English pub invites guests to listen to live piano music and sample many beers.
  • The billiard club, in addition to its main purpose, offers you to enjoy watching sports matches or your favorite films with a glass of beer or wine.
  • Pool and beach bars offer a huge selection of refreshing drinks. Also here you can satisfy your hunger between main meals.

Positive reviews

One of the most famous hotels in Naama Bay is Noria Resort 4 *. Reviews about him are mostly filled with positive and pleasant memories of the rest in this resort and hotel complex. So, tourists say the following about the hotel:

  • advantageous location in the center of the popular resort area;
  • large comfortable rooms;
  • varied menu;
  • unobtrusive animation and entertainment;
  • impressive architecture of the hotel, made with a touch of antique style;
  • rather low tour price for this level of service;
  • the hotel restaurants have a dress code, which eliminates unpleasant spectacles during meals;
  • the menu contains a huge number of varied and delicious desserts;
  • correspondence of the price of hotel accommodation and quality of service.




Negative comments

Unfortunately, there are no ideal hotels, because this is a rather specific area, where much depends on the human factor. Hotel Noria Resort 4 * also raises some complaints from tourists, the most significant of which are:

  • some rooms of the hotel are in need of repair, as well as updating the furniture stock;
  • low-quality alcohol is one of the negative features that characterize Egypt, Noria Resort 4 * was no exception;
  • staff cannot be stirred without a tip;
  • lack of heating in the pools;
  • too high noise levels both on the hotel premises and in the rooms;
  • lack of sunbeds on the beach and near the pool;
  • expensive and low-quality Internet (for half an hour of work with interruptions you will have to pay $ 3);
  • a bar located on the beach is not included in the All Inclusive system, therefore drinks and food are paid for in it (it is noted that in other institutions located nearby, prices are much lower);
  • animation does not organize enough sporting events;
  • tasteless soups and unripe fruits in the restaurant;
  • a very small beach (but you can freely enter the coast of a neighboring hotel and admire the coral reefs).

Pleasant surprises

Hotel Noria Resort 4 * (Sharm El Sheikh) appreciates and respects its guests, and therefore does not forget to regularly make pleasant surprises for them. In accordance with the terms of the loyalty program, the following bonuses are available to tourists:

  • Newlyweds, arriving at the hotel for a honeymoon, are entitled to receive a compliment in the form of a small festive cake. During check-in, you must inform the administrator about the desire to receive a bonus. In order to be guaranteed the right to a compliment, it is necessary to indicate the honeymoon for the purpose of the trip at the stage of buying a tour, as well as take a marriage certificate with you.
  • A birthday cake is also provided for those who live in the hotel. To avoid unpleasant situations, it is better to remind your reception staff about your birthday in advance (about two days before the expected date).
  • A special sign of gratitude and attention for regular guests is a fruit basket in the room. Tourists who come to the hotel twice or more fall into this category.

In general, the impression of tourists about this place is positive.
