Vacation at the recreation center "Forest" in Nizhnekamsk

Want to relax in nature, but can not imagine your life without the usual comfort? Think which country to buy a ticket to? No need to travel far! At your service recreation center "Forest" in Nizhnekamsk. This country club is suitable for both couples with children and for those who are in active search. Modern infrastructure will allow you to relax comfortably even away from the city.

Infrastructure recreation "Forest"

The modern recreation center "Lesnoy" in Nizhnekamsk offers vacationers not only a restaurant with exquisite cuisine and a bar, but also a bathhouse, a sauna, spacious billiards, a playground, a parking lot, a left-luggage office, and a conference room for business negotiations .

By the way, the recreation center has non-smoking rooms. The tourist complex has 27 comfortable houses, where two to eight people can live simultaneously. There are both regular rooms for couples and luxury rooms for honeymooners.

Each house is equipped with heating, air conditioning, cable TV and free wi-fi. The recreation center "Forest" in Nizhnekamsk upon check-in offers a pleasant bonus in the form of free parking and transfer.

Recreation and entertainment facilities

Do you think that relaxing away from the city will be boring and uninteresting? Not at all! Here you can not only play billiards, but also go fishing, and even go canoeing - the recreation center provides sports equipment rental services. By the way, if you get tired of the local cuisine, you can prepare a barbecue in the fresh air.

The recreation center "Lesnoy" in Nizhnekamsk is an excellent choice for those who prefer outdoor activities rather than city entertainment. If you are looking for a place to relax, celebrate a birthday, anniversary, New Year, then pay attention to the recreation center "Forest" - the prices here are quite reasonable - about 5000 rubles per day for a room.
