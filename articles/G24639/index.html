Pension "Dagomys-Olympic": reviews, photos, contacts

Resorts of the Krasnodar Territory were popular even in the days of the youth of our grandparents. However, they began to show special interest after it was announced that Sochi would become the venue for the 2014 Olympics. As a result of the tremendous work on the territory of the city and in its environs, new ultra-modern sports and entertainment infrastructure facilities were created that today make recreation in the Krasnodar Territory even more interesting and comfortable. Among the best accommodation options, tourists already vacationing in the southern capital of Russia, invariably call the Olympic-Dagomys pension in Sochi, well-known throughout the former USSR. It is located in a picturesque place with abundant vegetation, surrounded by majestic mountains, a few hundred meters from the sea.





The Olimpiysky-Dagomys boarding house, the photo of which is available in the review, recently won the Bird of Happiness prize of tourists - the laureate of the Crystal Globe professional award, which is a double and unbiased confirmation of the quality of the services provided there.

Treatment

Pension "Dagomys-Olympic" provides a range of medical services aimed at the prevention and effective treatment of diseases of the connective tissue and nervous system, musculoskeletal and circulatory systems, as well as the upper respiratory tract.

For this purpose, various treatment methods are used there, including:

  • physiotherapy (UHF therapy, inhalation, electrophoresis, magnetotherapy, ultrasound, OKUF therapy);
  • balneotherapy (air bubble, bischofite, iodine-bromine and other baths);
  • physiotherapy exercises;
  • drinking treatment with various mineral waters;
  • medical massage.

The boarding house has an acupuncture specialist, pediatrician, neurologist, psychotherapist, dentist, orthopedic traumatologist, therapist and physiotherapist.





In addition, as prescribed by the doctor, patients can receive treatment as part of programs for the improvement of allergies and diseases of the respiratory system, heart and blood vessels, rehabilitation of diseases of the musculoskeletal system, etc.

SPA-center

When else can you take care of your beauty and recovery, if not on vacation. For these purposes, the spa of the Olimpiysky boarding house is intended. There are rooms for thalassotherapy, thermal procedures, a gym and a phyto-bar. The specialists of the center are specialists in their field and will help relieve muscle tension, restore the beauty and elasticity of the skin.

Pension Olimpiysky-Dagomys: (Sochi): reviews

Since this medical institution has been operating since the beginning of the 1980s, several hundred thousand people from all over the former Soviet Union managed to visit there.
