Hotel Marjan Island Resort SPA 5 * (United Arab Emirates, Ras Al Khaimah): photos and reviews of tourists

The UAE is famous all over the world for its picturesque beaches and luxurious hotel complexes. One of them is the hotel Marjan Island Resort SPA 5. Here travelers can provide high-quality service and modern comfort. So what kind of reviews do tourists have already managed to spend their holidays here?





What does the territory look like? Location Description

The huge hotel complex Hilton Marjan Island Resort SPA 5 * is located on the picturesque island of Al-Marjan, which is part of the famous city of Ras al-Khaimah. The center of the capital can be reached in about an hour. The nearest international airport is 48 km away.

The hotel is very big. It consists of a modern ten-story building and 150 small, cozy chalets that will appeal to travelers who prefer privacy.

Hotel Marjan Island Resort SPA 5 *: what do the rooms look like?

The hotel is considered quite large, as it consists of 484 rooms. These are both standard rooms and deluxe rooms. The balcony overlooks the sea and the beach, so it is always a pleasure to spend time here. As in almost all UAE hotels, here the oriental style combines favorably with modern amenities. Of course, you can count on comfortable furniture, as well as cutting-edge appliances.





You can watch satellite channels on the big screen, use the safe, receive drinks from the minibar, create comfortable conditions using the air conditioning system, use the phone and the alarm clock. There is a kettle and everything you need for making hot drinks. Incidentally, some rooms have a separate living room with elegant upholstered furniture.

The room has a luxuriously decorated bathroom with the necessary set of plumbing. Also, guests receive soft bathrobes, slippers and clean towels.

Restaurants and cafes on site

Paying for accommodation in the hotel Marjan Island Resort SPA 5 *, you are free to choose the most suitable food plan for you. Nevertheless, most travelers prefer the all-inclusive system, as it is not always convenient to get out to the city to eat. Moreover, in the main restaurant, tourists will find a huge selection of dishes for every taste, quality service and a pleasant atmosphere. There is also a special children's menu.

There are two √† la carte restaurants, one of which serves delicious seafood. The hotel has two bars where you can order drinks, desserts and even light snacks. By the way, alcohol is not sold at the hotel.





Beach facilities

Of course, tourists come to the UAE in the first place with the aim of a good rest on the beach. Hotel Marjan Island Resort SPA 5 * has its own spacious stretch of coast. The beach here is sandy, but with pieces of shells, so it is better to get the appropriate shoes. The entrance to the sea is gradual and cleared of stones. You can use free sunbeds, umbrellas, and get clean towels. There is a sports center where tourists have the opportunity to engage in more active pastime, rent water transport, and take training courses.

Entertainment, additional service and leisure guests

This hotel is a large entertainment complex where everyone can find something to their liking. For example, you can relax by one of the three outdoor pools. By the way, there are places for bathing children. If you travel with a child, you can count on the constant work of the children's club, entertainment on water slides and playgrounds.





For a small fee, guests can visit the spa, a large modern gym, relax in the jacuzzi and steam room. There is a billiards room, tennis tables and a fairly large golf course.

Of course, almost the entire territory has access to the Internet. The hotel is often used for various business events, and therefore there are conference rooms and a well-equipped business center where you can use faxes, computers and rent the necessary video and audio equipment.

If desired, guests can contact the tour desk for assistance. Real professionals work here who will provide you with all the necessary information, help you choose and buy an interesting sightseeing tour of the surrounding area and even the whole country.

UAE, Marjan Island Resort SPA 5 *: how do tourists respond?

What do people who have already spent at least a few days here say about the hotel? Hotel Marjan Island Resort SPA 5 * leaves only a pleasant experience. On the territory of a lot of entertainment, and the city can be easily reached. The beach is clean, well maintained and closed to outsiders. The rooms are actually comfortable, the equipment is working properly, cleaned every day. Food at a more than decent level - the choice of dishes is always large, the food is fresh and tasty. As for the employees, they are polite and helpful, always ready to communicate, help with problems. In a word, this place is perfect for almost any type of vacation, although quite often parents with children stay here.
