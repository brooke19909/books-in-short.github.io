The very first tablet made by Apple appeared in April 2010. Then they released another 10 new models that differ in appearance and features. Several methods described in this article will help determine the iPad tablet models .

IPad Models: Description

Pro is the latest tablet model released in 2016. It has an aluminum slim body in standard silver or gold colors, as well as dark gray and pink; 2 cameras, one of which with a flash; four speakers. There are 2 types: with Wi-Fi and with a nano-SIM card. The price depends on the amount of memory: 32 GB, 128 GB, 256 GB.

Air is a thin tablet with Wi-Fi and Wi-Fi + nano-SIM card, two cameras and the same number of speakers, released in late 2013 and early 2014. Dimensions: width - 169.5 mm, length - 240 mm, display - 9.7 inches, a thin frame around the display, which can be white or black, and the aluminum case is dark gray or gray. Four memory sizes: from 16 to 128 GB.





Air 2 - a thin (6.1 mm) tablet, released at the end of 2014. In addition to two primary colors (gray, dark gray), it also began to possess golden. As in the previous case, it has 4 types of memory capacity, two cameras, one of which with a flash, a white or black front panel, Wi-Fi and a SIM card (LTE). The only thing is that this iPad no longer has a silent mode switch button.

Mini - a tablet, released in November 2012, has the following dimensions: thickness - 7.2 mm, width - 134.7 mm, length - 200 mm. It looks small with a gray or blue-gray aluminum case. IPad mini models have three memory sizes: 16, 32 and 64 GB. On the left side there is a slot for a nano-SIM card.





Mini 2 is a tablet with a Retina display. Released at the end of 2013. It is practically no different from the previous tablet, it only has the clearest image on the screen and the best camera. Added a new larger amount of memory equal to 128 GB. It happens with both the Wi-Fi function and the LTE / Wi-Fi function.

Mini 3. went on sale at the end of 2014. In addition to the new color (gold), it is no different from the aforementioned iPada.

Aypad - the very first of the line of "apple" tablets, released in 2010. There is no camera on it, the color of the front panel is only black and the back is silver. Dimensions: length - 242.8 mm, width - 189.7 mm, thickness - 13.4 mm. Memory capacities: 16 GB, 32 GB and 64 GB. The slot for a SIM card is standard, it also has a Wi-Fi function.

In 2011, iPad 2 was launched for sale. It is slightly different in size and thickness in that they are slightly smaller. In addition to the black front panel, white appeared. Cameras also appeared on one side and the other. Photo quality and image clarity are poor (pixels are very noticeable). Input for sim - micro. Supports Wi-Fi.

3rd generation - was published in March 2012. A little thicker than their "brothers", but the length and width remained the same. The color of the front panel can be white or black. It has 2 cameras and three memory sizes: 16, 32, 64 GB. It supports Wi-Fi and Wi-Fi + 3G (micro SIM card on the right side).

The iPad 4 generation went on sale in November 2012. The tablet has 3 varieties. The numbers are indicated on the back of the iPad 4. Models will be considered in more detail below. In external dimensions, it differs little from previous iPads, but internal signs have a big difference. There are also several colors: silver, dark gray, golden and gray-blue.

  1. The number A 1337 means that you have a model of the 1st generation iPad, Wi-Fi + 3G SIM card.
  2. Number A 1219 also speaks of the 1st generation, which has a Wi-Fi function + a SIM card with 3G.

  4. Serial number A 1403 indicates a 3rd generation tablet with Wi-Fi + 3G (micro-sim (Verizon)).
