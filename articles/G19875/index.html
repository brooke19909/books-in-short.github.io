DIY cork board from improvised materials

Cork board can be a real find not only for convenient placement of notes, but also as an interesting decor. If you need space for planning, posting notes or visualizing desires, you need to understand how to make a cork board yourself. This convenient accessory - decorating an empty wall - is incredibly convenient for placing small notes and photographs, drawings or wishes.

It is impossible to imagine an office, school or kindergarten without a cork board on which plans, announcements, congratulations and so on are placed. Such an accessory is actively gaining popularity in home use. You should find out what it is and how to make a convenient cork board for notes with your own hands.

Wall organizer

The material for creating a cork board is cork oak bark. To create a structure, you can purchase a thin base in the construction market - a rectangular sheet made of pressed cork. It is convenient to attach photos and notes, posters and various information materials to the finished product using push pins or needles.

Corkboard can be used to create panels from photos and children's drawings. The plane is easily divided into sections according to the days of the week, converting it into a convenient scheduler.

A creative approach will help you to make an excellent decor from a cork board using various additions and decorations. The article describes how to implement the plan in practice.

Cork Board Material

To create a cork board with your own hands, you will need:

  • frame;
  • cork board sheet;
  • ruler;
  • pencil;
  • sharp clerical knife.

As you can see, extremely simple and affordable materials are needed to create a cork board. Perhaps you have an old frame lying around that you can use. Restore its appearance with paint.

The material from which the frame is made can be any: aluminum, plastic or wood, the main thing is that this is combined with the overall design of the room. And if you recently had a repair, then perhaps a cork substrate sheet remained in your bins - it is from it that the board considered in the article is made.

Master Class

  1. Most often, cork base is sold twisted into a roll. The day before creating a cork board with your own hands, untwist it, press it with heavy objects so that the material straightens out.
  2. Measure the frame from the inside, transfer the marking to the cork canvas. Strip the frame, paint or varnish. Leave to dry.
  3. According to the marked markings, cut the desired section of the cork sheet. While the frame dries, the measured segment can be decorated with drawings, markings or paint.
  4. Fasten the cork board to the frame, print some nice pictures, family photos and attach it to the board with push pins. On this basis, notes are great for practical office sticks.
  5. Make some small boards, place them on the wall - they will serve as a bright decoration of the room, created by your own hands. Cork boards look harmoniously in absolutely any room: in the nursery, living room or in the kitchen.

Wine cork board

An excellent solution for wall decor will be boards made of wine corks. They are comfortable and interesting to look, organically blending into any interior. Wine corks can be collected after a feast or purchased at a winery for a nominal price.

If you have accumulated enough wine corks, you can use them to create a cork board. First of all, inspect the ornamental material for signs of a drink. To get rid of stains, put dirty corks in a pan, fill with water and boil for about half an hour. Dry them and inspect again. The cleaned jams can be floated, the rest boil again. The material used to create wine corks is waterproof and will not swell from boiling.
