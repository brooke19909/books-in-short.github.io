Rugs of shreds. How to Knit Patch Mats

Experienced craftswomen to create their products use everything that comes to hand. Even unnecessary things that gather dust in a closet for a long time are used. From them you can make, for example, original rugs from shreds. Consider several options that differ from each other in technology.





Varieties of rugs

Before you begin to work, you need to decide not only on the texture and color of the material, but also on the manufacturing technology. Rugs from scraps of fabric are completely different, but each of them can be attributed to one of the groups.

  1. Wicker. This category includes mats made of braids, as well as made according to weaving technology.
  2. Lush. The volumetric structure is obtained due to the partial fixing of the patches on the base. The free ends fill the space and create a 3D effect.
  3. Knitted. Rugs of shreds are carried out using a working tool - a hook. And the threads are replaced by long strips.
  4. Stitched. Variegated patchwork patterns are created from original compositions in the form of folded fabric squares in triangles.

Below are descriptions of the proposed manufacturing options. Also given tips on the design of the raw edges of the products and some secrets when using technology.





We collect a rug from fabric braids

One of the manufacturing options is the layout of wicker blanks. To do this, cut unnecessary things into strips of the same size (approximately 9 x 12 cm). Then double each of the ribbons, hiding the raw edge inward. For the convenience of weaving, you need a dense roller or a stiff pillow. Fasten the first three ribbons on it and begin work. Twist the stripes in the form of a pigtail. Sew a new ribbon to the end that ends with hidden stitches. In the same way, increase everything in turn. Rugs of shreds, woven in the form of braids, can be of various shapes. The easiest way to get round. Lay the prepared ribbon in a spiral on a fabric base, twist it tightly. Then fasten the pigtail on the back. If you want to make a square or rectangular rug, arrange the blanks in a straight line. After that, carefully cut the sections with fabric ribbons and decorate with a fringe.





Lush patchwork rugs

A very original and simple design is the assembly of small pieces of fabric. For this, pieces 2 x 8 cm in size will be required. They need to be sewn onto a dense base. For convenience, pre-draw a grid with a bright pencil or ballpoint pen on it. The size of each cell is 2 x 2 cm. Then glue ‚ÄúMoment‚Äù on each of the cross-shaped intersections of lines lightly (with one or two drops) glue one tissue piece. This is done for the convenience of further manufacture on a sewing machine. After the blank of the future rug dries out a bit, fix the lines with an ordinary seam, directing the free ends of the ribbons on the sides. For greater strength, do this twice. Using this technology, it is possible to create ornaments of various colors, arranging the desired piece of a certain tone according to the scheme. As a basis, try to take the simplest patterns for cross-stitching. Or just stitch the blanks in a checkerboard pattern.





How to crochet a patchwork rug?

This is another of the simplest ways to make products. Indeed, certain skills and knowledge are not required at all regarding how to knit a shredded rug with a hook. Everything is very simple to do. Cut the fabric into long strips of the same width (3-3.5 cm) and wind them into balls. Knit with a single crochet. Place the joints of the strips with dense stitches or remove the loose ends inward. If the fabric is not too dense, you can move from one strip to another, tying them together. Using the proposed technology, in addition to traditional colorful knitting in a circle, you can make very elegant rugs with ornaments. Prepare for work several plain balls of various colors. It is also necessary to come up with a color scheme. Then make blanks in the form of small squares. Arrange them, creating an intricate pattern, and sew them together. So you can easily get a rug in the form of a flower or a fairy-tale hero.

Mesh-based patchwork

A hook may also be needed with another method of making rugs. The basis in this case is not a dense fabric, but a mesh web. The patches are fixed on it by tying or with the help of not quite ordinary technology. The photo clearly shows the whole process of work. To do this, you need a special hook with a hole on the reverse end, through which the working thread is passed. Pass the tool through five to six cells, then wind several turns of patchwork tape on it. Gently pull the hook, and an arc of spirals forms on the working thread. Repeat the described step, directing the hook-needle to the following mesh cells. Try to tightly fasten the spiral segments formed on the base, moving along the perimeter (from the edges to the center) or parallel to one of the sides of the workpiece. With this technology, you can use separately taken chopped ribbons without first connecting them together.





Products from rolled fabric squares

When performing this version of the mat, it is not necessary to take care of the same structure and thickness of the fabric. The main thing is to cut out equal-sized squares. Workpieces before fixing on the basis of the roll two times diagonally. The result is small four-layer triangles. Iron them to secure the shape. After that, lay the blanks in rows on a fabric base, alternately attaching with a machine seam. Each next will cover untreated sections. Decoration of product edges

With many of the technologies described above, crocheted mats from scraps are made with open sections along the entire canvas or part of the perimeter. How to beautifully process and decorate the sides of products? Choose one of several options.

  1. Fringing with a ribbon from fabric. To do this, use strips with a width of at least 5-6 cm. Hiding the edges inward, process the mat, gently edging in a circle.
  2. The formation of fringe from working threads. This option is suitable in the case of making a rug according to weaving technology, in which the two sides do not require additional processing, and the other two consist of ‚Äúweft‚Äù ends. Use them, fluffing for splendor, or decorate with additional segments.
  3. Hanging fringes on the finished edge. In some cases, the mats are completely finished externally and do not require additional decoration (patchwork, braiding, crocheting). However, if desired, additional fringe can be hung using tape pieces of fabric.




Useful Tips

Performing the work on the technologies described above, you need to know certain secrets. How to make a rug of shreds beautiful and high quality? We offer to put into practice several useful tips.

  1. When making braids from fabrics of various structures, make a few ‚Äúprobes‚Äù. This will determine the most optimal width of the strips for each material, so that the finished wicker blank is uniform in thickness. So, for example, ribbons from thin fabrics should be much wider than dense woolen ones.
  2. In the event that the edges tend to fray heavily, it is better to remove them inward. Therefore, when cutting into strips, make the necessary allowances for the hem.
  3. When making the base, use a double layer of fabric. Then the shred rugs will keep their shape better when tuning the pieces. The tone of the lower layer, as a rule, coincides in color with the upper one. And the use of similar textures of materials will greatly facilitate the work.
