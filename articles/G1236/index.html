How to calculate a car tax in Russia

Automobile taxes in Russia must be paid by the person for whom the vehicle is registered. Such a norm is established by the Tax Code in article number 357 and applies not only to car owners, but also to owners of motorcycles, boats, yachts, motor boats, helicopters, etc.

The ownership of the car is established based on the ownership of the legal or natural person in the car, as evidenced by the passport of the vehicle issued by the traffic police, or on the basis of an agreement or reference account, which also certifies the right to a car.

How to calculate car tax? To do this, you need to know the engine power in metric or off-system units. Metric is the value in kW, and off-system is horsepower. Convert KW to hp possible by multiplying the kW by 1.35962. These data can be taken from the data sheet or, in the absence thereof, obtained on the basis of an examination conducted on the basis of Article No. 95 of the Tax Code of Russia.
