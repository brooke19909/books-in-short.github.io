Niagara: an open-air water park awaits guests

On Turgenevskoe highway, near Krasnodar, a new water park "Niagara" has opened. The modern aquacomplex offers guests water rides and various types of slopes, a huge swimming pool, a cozy relaxation area for adults and a zone for children's fun, a restaurant and a cafe-bar.

How to find

At the very peak of last summer in the Republic of Adygea, in the Takhtamukaysky district, on the Turgenevskoye Highway, a new water entertainment center, Niagara, was opened - the latest generation water park. The complex is located behind the Mega shopping center (50 m) along Turgenevsky Highway 35. The new water park is seasonal, because it is in the open.

If in general

The water center offers four slopes, a children's water area, an adult swimming pool and a restaurant.

On both sides of the entrance are locker rooms, comfortable showers and clean comfortable toilets. The undressing rooms are spacious, roomy, even at rush hour there is no crowding. Lockers are locked.

You can grab a bite to eat at the large indoor cafe, which is located in the heart of the Niagara water park. The water park has several outlets for forgetful guests. In them you can buy little things necessary for relaxing on the water: bathing circles, sunblocks, towels, etc.

In the adult half, in addition to slopes, a pool with a hydromassage area is organized in the pool, there are geysers and a “sea wave” installation.





The water park has a separate pool where exciting games are arranged, and at certain hours experienced instructors teach swimming.

Niagara is waiting for guests

On weekdays, especially on the first shift, it is not very crowded in the Niagara water center. The water park comes to life on the weekend. A night club is added to the two day shifts, which “buzzes” until three in the morning.

The first shift on any day of the week starts at 10 in the morning and ends at 2 in the afternoon. After a technical break , the second shift begins. It lasts from 15:00 to 19:00. Tickets can be purchased for a specific shift, as well as for the whole day. From Monday to Friday, an adult ticket costs 1,400 rubles (one shift - 700 rubles), a child - 1,200. On Saturday and Sunday, admission for an adult costs 1,600 rubles, for a child - 1,300 (one shift - 700 rubles) .

According to guests, the water park "Niagara" keeps prices unchanged during the season.

A small minus of the water entertainment center, guests note a small number of umbrellas and descents for adults, as well as too short a menu in the cafe. Some still complain of poor landscaping throughout. But here you need to understand that "Niagara" is a water park that has recently opened, and green spaces need some time to grow.

The center has a spacious guarded parking.

Zone of children's happiness

The territory of children's water activities is quite large. Water rides have all the necessary safety indicators. Interactive water game system is especially popular with fidgets.

The children's zone is divided by age categories. For kids there is a zone with a depth of 40 cm and figures of fabulous animals. For guests a bit older, there are already “not children's” decorations with closed and open slopes. On weekends, children are entertained by animators.





Children in the water around the perimeter of the pools are watched by lifeguards.

Warm nights for adults

On warm summer evenings, the Niagara water park (Krasnodar) lights colorful lights and opens a night club. Here the fun with music and swimming lasts until three in the morning.





“Foam weekend” takes place on Friday and Saturday night

Promotions, coupons, discounts

Water Park "Niagara" (Krasnodar) participates in various promotions. For example, on specialized sites you can buy discount coupons. The number of such tickets used by one guest is unlimited. But everyone will be accepted only once.

The coupon entitles you to a 50% discount on entertainment in the park from ten in the morning until seven in the evening. For adults, this amount is reduced to 800 rubles, and for children - up to 650 rubles. Children under three years of age are not affected by the action. They visit the water park for free.

Rules and restrictions

It is strictly forbidden to come to all attractions with bracelets, piercings, chains, earrings and other small metal products, both on the body and on bathing suits.

Drunk persons are generally prohibited from entering the Niagara Entertainment Center. The water park does not have advance registration of guests. It is enough to present at the entrance a discount coupon or ticket.

Young guests can come to the water entertainment center only with accompanying persons over 18 years old.

In September, the water park opens only on weekends.





And finally

Guests who visited the Niagara Waterpark leave different reviews. But it should be noted that almost everyone is impressed by the feature of the water center - the local plastic card. She can pay for absolutely everything in the park. Unspent output amount is returned.

Niagara is an unforgettable adventure for the whole family, cozy romantic dates and fun parties with friends. Come and see for yourself!
