Hotels, "Prospect Mira": list, addresses, rating of the best, room reservation, services, pricing, visitor reviews and tourist tips

Near the metro station "Prospekt Mira" there are many cultural places that many tourists seek to see. For example, the House of Irida or the House-Museum of Shchepkin M.S. Also, city guests can watch the Lomonosov Botanical Garden or the Sand Theater, Griboedov Corner. Many tourists want to stop nearby the Prospekt Mira metro station to see all the sights nearby. For this purpose, both hostels with affordable rooms and hotels with luxury apartments are suitable.

Hotels near Prospect Mira metro station

About 34 hotels and hostels are located within walking distance from this metro station. Here you can stay from 3 000 to 30 000 rubles for the night. At the same time, the price is not always directly related to the quality of service and the interior of the rooms. Below we will consider hotels by rating depending on the visitor reviews.

1st place - Garden Ring Hotel

A sufficiently large and well-known hotel, which is in first place according to visitors' reviews, is located at: Prospekt Mira 14, p. 2. This hotel is located near the metro station "Prospekt Mira" and other transport lines.

A rich stylish interior in the middle class style will meet you at the doorstep of the hotel. The building looks quite respectable both outside and inside. The meeting room is often occupied by well-known companies.

The hotel has a restaurant and laundry facilities. It features a large pool, sauna and steam room. The gym will also help you stay fit even on a business trip.

The rooms have everything you need, from slippers and hygiene items to a telephone and air conditioning. Guests can enjoy a meal or drink at the apartment or use the minibar. Garden Ring - a hotel (Prospekt Mira, Moscow), the average room rate in which per day is about 8,000-10,000 rubles.

Many visitors respond excellently to the hotel. They note an excellent breakfast in the restaurant, a beautiful interior, many figurines and small gizmos that give comfort both in the common room and in the rooms.

Some people note that the restaurant is too small, and as a result it is not always possible to find a place for two in the morning. The breakfast assortment is also somewhat unusual - a lot of pickles and sausages, but not enough dairy products. There are guests who did not like the bathroom - the shower was uncomfortable.

2nd place - Avenue Hotel

The hotel is located at: Shchepkina street, 32, building 1, takes second place in the ranking of popular hotels on Prospekt Mira. The average room rate per day is from 10,000 rubles. The hotel's interior is made in the lush baroque style with notes of classic luxury. Royal beds, lots of stucco moldings and velvet upholstery - all this makes you feel like in a fairy tale.

The hotel provides a standard set of services: housekeeping and linen change, laundry, hairdryer, concierge services. Breakfast can be packed with you. There is a fridge and minibar, free Wi-Fi and a 24-hour reception.

Some guests were dissatisfied with the boorish behavior of the staff. They note that the level and quality of service falls in it every month. The guests of this hotel note that they will not stay here anymore.

Among the guests there are many who leave positive reviews about this place. However, a very high price makes you stay here not often.

3rd place - Cosmos Hotel

If you walk along Mira Avenue, then you will definitely pay attention to this high-rise building - this is a huge arched structure, which beckons you to go inside, and there is a famous hotel. In the ranking of hotels takes an honorable third place.

Cosmos Hotel (Prospekt Mira, 150) offers comfortable rooms of different levels: standard, superior, junior suites and suites. The cheapest number will cost about 3,000 rubles, and the most expensive - 11,000 rubles.

All reviews about the hotel are just wonderful. Guests love the setting and the service, but the most impressive are the views. Units note that it's time to update the interior and furnishings at the hotel.

4th place - Aparthotel Garden Embassy

On Botanical Lane, Building 5 (Moscow), there is a chic hotel (Prospekt Mira, near), which, according to visitors' reviews, received 9.2 points out of 10 and fourth place in the rating. The average room rate per day is about 10,000 rubles.

All visitors in their reviews talk about the unusually beautiful view from the panoramic windows to the Botanical Garden. The rooms are made according to the type of studios, so you can cook breakfast, lunch and dinner on your own. A big plus is the availability of all the shower accessories in the room, as well as a video call.

Of the minuses in the reviews speak only about the cost of the apartment. The price range of the hotel is above average, even by Moscow standards.

5th place - Room on Malaya Sukharevskaya

The hotel is in fifth place in the ranking of popular hotels, although in terms of comfort it may take the first one. Address: st. Malaya Sukharevskaya 1, p. 1. The nearest metro station is less than 500 meters. Guests rate the hotel 9.1 out of 10. The average room rate is about 2,000 rubles.

The rooms include a TV and free Wi-Fi, shared facilities and a bathroom, as well as a kitchen for all guests.

All guests are pleased with the proximity to the metro and the availability of shops and cafes nearby. Most of their reviews note the wonderful atmosphere and friendly hostess of the inn.

Among the shortcomings, guests indicate poor sound insulation and a glass door (light from the corridor interfered with sleep). Some guests have noticed that bedding is worth updating.

6th place - apartments near the metro station "Prospekt Mira"

38 Protopopovsky Lane - the location of this hotel. The metro station itself is less than 700 meters on foot. The average cost of a room is 8,000-10,000 rubles. Very close is the picturesque center of Moscow. Guests rate their stay at the hotel well and it is ranked sixth in the ranking.

Many visitors note the optimal ratio of price and quality of service. The rooms have everything you need: Internet, minibar, refrigerator, hairdryer, bedding. Laundry facilities are available. The restaurant and bar on the ground floor are always ready to feed their guests and treat them to delicious drinks.

Of course, there are visitors who were dissatisfied with the interior and pricing of the hotel. However, there are very few such reviews from guests.

7th place - mini-hotel "Slavyanka"

In Moscow, on Kalanchevskaya street (32), there is an inexpensive but comfortable hotel with rooms from 2,000 rubles per day. The cost of one hour is from 500 rubles, however, the minimum order for a room is 3 hours. This hotel (Prospekt Mira - metro station - a 15-minute walk) ranks seventh in the ranking of popular hotels. To the metro station on foot about 10 minutes or 800 meters.

The hotel is small, located in a four-story residential building. The rooms are clean and comfortable. The whole interior is quite simple and unpretentious.

Most guests of this mini-hotel believe that this is a convenient and inexpensive place for sleeping and relaxing. But the units were dissatisfied with not too polite communication with the administration and the lack of cutlery in the rooms.

The interior of the rooms is very simple, no frills. According to guest reviews, it is an acceptable hotel for a short rest.

"Slavyanka" - a mini-hotel (Prospekt Mira metro station) with maximum amenities for a minimum price. It is very convenient for guests who are passing through the city, or just for relaxing.

8th place - Moniki Hostel

This is a very budget place to stay on Shchepkina street, 60/2 p. 2. The Prospekt Mira metro station is 5 minutes on foot (less than 500 meters). It is chosen by many tourists for an inexpensive price and eighth in popularity. For the day spent in the hotel, you need to pay less than 1,000 rubles. In addition to the sleeping place, you have an open kitchen with the necessary utensils and appliances, a bathroom and laundry for a separate cost.

The mini-hotel has economy rooms, and there are simply beds in male and female rooms. The cost of both of these is quite affordable by Moscow standards. In the first case, you will have to pay about 2,000-2,500 rubles, and in the second less than 1,000 rubles.

Reviews of visitors to this place are mostly positive. Fairly spacious and clean rooms. There is everything you need.

Shared apartments are sometimes too noisy, however, this is the fault of the neighbors, not the hostel. Some bathroom fixtures require replacement.

9th place - Hostel "Studio 47"

In ninth place - a mini-hotel, which is open at: Shchepkina street, 47/1. The apartments in the rooms are provided as a bed equipped with a curtain. The average cost of an overnight stay in this place is from 600 rubles. The nearest metro station is 300 meters away. Studio 47 is a hotel (Prospekt Mira, a metro station within walking distance) with minimal amenities, but at a good price.

Visitors should pay attention that this is a fairly lively street and place, so you should not count on silence. The hostel is optimal not only in terms of location, but also in terms of price and quality. The rooms are spacious and comfortable enough. The curtains on the beds allow you to delimit the personal space of guests.

10th place - hotel "Mira"

If you walk along Durov street from the Prospekt Mira metro station, you will definitely stop near the red building number 36. This is an inexpensive and comfortable hotel near the metro (200 meters), which is in tenth place in the ranking. Here you can spend the night for 3,000-5,000 rubles.

Most guests in their reviews speak of excellent breakfasts. Very nice and nice staff. The rooms have everything you need: a bathrobe, slippers, soap accessories, a bottle of water, tea, coffee and cookies in each room (there is a capsule coffee machine). Everything is comfortable, clean and beautiful. Ironing facilities are available upon request.

Among the shortcomings indicate that some would like a greater variety of pastries for breakfast. There are guests who note poor quality plumbing in the room.
