Deposits: types and conditions

Simplicity and convenience are important criteria of all products that Bank Grow offers. Contributions are a vivid confirmation of this. In a credit institution, there are two main types of investments for private clients. These are the tariffs "Free GROWTH" and "Maximum GROWTH". Let us consider in more detail the conditions of each of them.

Free

The first is a kind of wallet with "dripping" interest on it. What does it mean? This deposit is opened on demand, and withdrawal does not affect the size of the interest rate. You can top up your account, as well as withdraw money, at any time. Moreover, the minimum balance is fixed (10 thousand rubles), and the interest is high enough for a deposit with such conditions. The maximum amount for this tariff is 30 million rubles or a million dollars (which practically does not limit the capabilities of customers). The term of the deposit is from three months to a year with the possibility of prolonging the contract. Percentage - the value is relative and is set depending on the period and amount of the down payment (maximum 9.2%).

Maximum

If you do not plan to withdraw money during the term of the deposit, then it will be more profitable to use the "Maximum GROWTH" deposit. At this rate, the bank offers the best interest rates with their capitalization. In addition, favorable conditions for early termination of the contract are provided. Like the previous one, this contribution can also be replenished during the validity period. As for interest, they reach 10.5% in rubles and 4.5% in foreign currency. There are also significant differences in the amounts and terms. So, the minimum contribution is lower and amounts to 3 thousand rubles, and the deposit term is extended from a month to three years.

The bank‚Äôs website has a convenient calculator on which everyone can calculate the percentage of the deposit, depending on the planned amount and term. In addition, individual and priority services are provided for bank depositors. The first is provided when investing in the amount of 4.5 million rubles and includes special conditions for banking services, personal manager, VIP offices and so on. The second - with a deposit of more than 1.5 million rubles. In both cases, the client receives many very pleasant privileges.
