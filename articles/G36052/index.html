Krabi La Playa Resort Krabi 4 * (Thailand, Krabi Province): photos with description, service, reviews of tourists

Krabi is a small, picturesque island located near Phuket in Thailand. He is famous primarily for its soft sandy beaches and karst caves. Here conditions are created for both active and relaxing holidays. For tourists, many hotels are open, including budget ones. If you want to have a good rest on the island without spending a huge amount, then pay attention to the beach complex Krabi La Playa Resort in Thailand. It is about him that we will tell in more detail in this article.

Hotel location

In their reviews, tourists indicate that they were satisfied with the location of the hotel. Krabi La Playa Resort 3 * complex is built in a green area near the coast. Only the highway separates it from the beach. The distance to it is about 150 meters. The hotel is located in a large tourist area, so it has a developed infrastructure. Other hotels, restaurants, small cafes and shops are nearby. The central part of Krabi is approximately 8 km from the hotel. You can get to it in 20 minutes.

The nearest international airport accepting flights from Russia is located in Phuket. The distance to it is about 30 km. From the airport, tourists can get to the hotel on their own by taxi, regular bus or tuk-tuk. If you wish, you can also pay in advance for the transfer. The average travel time to the hotel is about half an hour.

Entertainment

There is no private beach at Krabi La Playa Resort (Krabi). But this complex is conveniently located between two municipal sections of the coast, where all conditions for a comfortable stay are created. Tourists can also swim by the outdoor pools on site. There are 2 of them at the hotel. Their area is 855 and 170 m 2 . Next to them is a terrace where you can sunbathe on sun loungers or hide from the sun under an umbrella.

There is also a spa on site, but access to the hotel is not included. For a fee, tourists can book a massage or go to the jacuzzi or sauna. At the reception you can rent diving equipment or pay for diving lessons.

Accommodation with children

Krabi La Playa Resort (Krabi) is a fairly popular holiday destination for tourists with small children. They are attracted by the favorable location of the hotel, which is located on a quiet street, and a green area. In addition, kids can check in here at a lower cost.

A set of facilities for children at the hotel is standard. Upon request, a cradle is brought to the room for guests with babies. The restaurants have high chairs for feeding. So that children do not get bored, a playground and a shallow pool are open for them on the territory. If desired, parents can leave their kids for a while in the mini-club. Any children over 2 years old are accepted there.
