Hotel Aqua Mare Bomo Club 3 * (Greece / Halkidiki): photos, reviews, reservation

Greece is an amazing country of azure beaches, incredibly gentle sunshine, beautiful golden sand and lots of entertainment that will appeal to even the most demanding and picky clientele. Among other things, on the territory of sunny Greece there is a huge number of diverse hotels that meet the most modern standards of quality of service for vacationers.
One of the most popular places for accommodation in Greece is the Bomo Club Aqua Mare 3 * hotel (Halkidiki, Kallikratia), which is located at a distance of about 35 kilometers from the city of Thessaloniki. The main airport of Greece is at a distance of about 25 km. There is a public transport stop near the hotel complex.

Shops, supermarkets, restaurants, cafes and other infrastructure are within walking distance of the hotel, which greatly facilitates the stay of vacationers at the resort.

general information



The hotel in Halkidiki Aqua Mare Bomo Club 3 * was built in 2009, and underwent the last major renovation in 2011.

The building of the complex is located on the territory of the park zone with an amazing landscape and all the necessary infrastructure.

Accommodation



The first building of the hotel consists of 4 floors, each of which is equipped with comfortable rooms with a shower, a bathroom, a TV, a refrigerator, a dedicated line for Internet access, a hairdryer, towels, an extra bed for older children. There is a dressing table for applying makeup. If desired, you can order delivery of gastronomic delights directly to your room.

Also, in each of the rooms, in addition to what was listed earlier, there is a wardrobe and a desk to fill in any papers and for other needs. Every day, at the request of the client, the hotel staff brings fresh exotic fruits and berries to the room.

In addition to the main building, the hotel has a comfortable cottage with all the necessary equipment for cooking, Internet access, utensils and furniture.

The hotel has rooms for both smoking clients and those who lead a healthy lifestyle. In the halls there are hangers for outerwear, as well as terry robes and slippers. Each available room has a sun terrace or wide balcony. All windows have a panoramic view, there are triple double-glazed windows.

Additional services

For a fee, you can use the safety deposit box provided at the reception. A miniature bar is also available only for a fee, there are air conditioners and other equipment for regulating temperature conditions.
The rooms have modern cordless telephones with pre-connected international cellular rates. The bathroom has a pre-prepared apparatus for drying hair, all the necessary accessories for the implementation of hygiene procedures. Room service is provided by staff for a fee, dust and dirt are cleaned, as well as linen and towels are changed three times a week.

Hotel service

Regarding the issue of food, the Bomo Club Aqua Mare 3 * hotel (Greece) provides services of restaurants serving international cuisine, European and local culinary masterpieces. For guests with private vehicles, the hotel has a guarded area with many parking spaces. Unfortunately, pets are not allowed to enter this hotel complex with pets. Check-in of new guests at the hotel takes place from approximately 2 pm, and departure from 12. Transfer from the complex to the airport and back is available for vacationers for a separate monetary reward.

The hotel has an elevator chamber, a lobby and a laundry service with a dry cleaning service for the most difficult pollution on things.

Beach

The beach area is equipped with:

  • Sun beds.
  • Deck chairs.
  • Umbrellas.

On the beach, all amenities (sunbeds, deckchairs, sun umbrellas and water transport) are provided for a small tip. Payment for any services at the hotel is carried out using various types of credit cards.

The best criterion for evaluating the hotel Bomo Club Aqua Mare 3 * - reviews of its guests.

What do tourists say?

According to the reviews of most guests of the complex, the hotel is very cozy and comfortable. There is style and good taste in everything. The staff is friendly and immediately available. Guests are met by the hostess of the hotel with her daughter.

In front of the building on the street on either side of the entrance are comfortable sofas, tables and chairs. Flower pots and flower beds with exotic plants immediately set the atmosphere for comfort. It is pleasant to spend free time with a cup of tea at noon or in the evening. Such events can also be arranged on the balcony, which is available in each room.

Inside, the hotel has a homely atmosphere. More like a guest house complex Aqua Mare Bomo Club 3 *. The photos of the hotel presented in this article reflect only part of its cozy splendor. The design of the hotel is entirely the merit of the hostess.

About accommodation





Guests are greeted by the daughter of the hostess of the establishment. Upon check-in, a key is issued at the reception and a full briefing is provided on the location of all hotel infrastructure facilities. Unfortunately, luggage will have to be carried to the room on your own, but this is not a problem, since the hotel has an elevator.

If you check in after lunch, guests can count on a hearty dinner at the Pirates Tavern. It is only necessary to present a coupon, which indicates the name of the hotel and the duration of the stay.

The hotel itself provides its guests only breakfast.

The tavern menu does not differ in variety. Customers can choose one dish from each category. Drinks are soda with toppings and wine, but you can take only one drink to choose from.

Nutrition





Dining at the hotel on a buffet. Breakfast includes scrambled eggs, cereals, salads, sausages, pasta, sweet pastries, toasts and fruits. Hot coffee drinks - cars, juice in decanters. Breakfast is available from 8.00 to 10.00.

There should be no problems with food, as there are many restaurants near the hotel.

Places near the hotel

Since the hotel provides breakfast only, you will have to have lunch or dinner in other establishments. Not far from the hotel at the end of the promenade there is a wonderful tavern "Dolphin". Owned by a family of Russian immigrants. It is cozy, like home and very tasty food. Draft wine deserves special attention. Almost all Russians from the hotel go to dinner there. The tavern even prepares Russian dishes, and desserts are always given as a gift. The portions are just huge. One is enough for two hungry people. Dinner costs an average of 20 euros. The food is very tasty and fresh. The owners themselves recommend which dishes are worth ordering, and which are better to refrain from. It is possible to make a menu in advance the next day.

Beach vacation





The beach can be reached in just 5 minutes. The beach is covered with soft sand. The entrance to the sea is gentle. It is very convenient to swim with children. The beach on the left side of the hotel has a wide coastline. Almost no one uses sun loungers, but you need to bring beach towels with you.

The sea is wonderful, but there is one danger in it - sea urchins. There are a lot of them on the pier and on the rocks. Surprisingly, the locals are not afraid of them and calmly walk on them. Moreover, they even eat them. At the end of the beach there are many pitfalls. It is better to swim in diving equipment.

Basically, the bottom is sandy and clean. Sometimes small fish, beautiful shells and crabs are found.

General impressions





Most domestic tourists recommend the Aqua Mare Bomo Club 3 * hotel. Reservations in advance are welcome, but not required. Although in season the hotel is completely full.

The city of Kallikratia itself is a good resort with a developed infrastructure. You can easily get to Thessaloniki and other major cities of Greece. Shopping and excursions to these places will leave a lot of pleasant impressions.

It is definitely necessary to rent a car. But in Kallikratia no one provides such services, unfortunately. Behind the car you can go to Thessaloniki. A bus ticket costs 3.5 euros, children - for free. The road takes 30 minutes. Buses run every hour.

On Angelaki Street, there are numerous offices offering car rental. For a deposit, you must have a credit card with you.

Especially popular among Russian tourists is the Aqua Mare Bomo Club 3. The description of its main advantages, according to domestic travelers, is presented above. Especially our brother liked cozy rooms with access to the backyard. There you feel in solitude, but there is always someone to talk to and share your impressions with, as the audience is mostly Russian-speaking.

In Greece, it is not customary to leave a tip. Perfectly removed, and even laid out of shells still life to you and without money.

Mistress Maria is an incredibly creative person. She thinks all the details of the design herself. Sometimes at the hotel some elements of the decor change.

The evening at the hotel is filled with romance. Candles are lit in numerous lamps, illuminating everything around with an atmosphere of warmth and comfort. You will not see this in any five-star hotel in Turkey and Egypt.

Those who like parties, noisy events, discos and nightclubs should definitely choose another hotel. Kallikratia is famous for silence, measured and peaceful. Here, even in taverns you will not hear loud music. It is an ideal place for families with children. It is very convenient to attend excursions. You can even roam alone in the quiet streets and feel completely safe.
