The rooms.


Almost all rooms at Kahramana Naama Bay overlook the courtyard, which is unforgettable thanks to the huge pool and colorful lanterns that turn on at night! Therefore, many of the hotel guests are willing to pay a certain amount to be moved to rooms with a view of the courtyard. The room itself is equipped with good furniture. The toiletries in the bathroom have everything except bathrobes and slippers. A TV with satellite TV shows 2-3 Russian channels, by phone you can order round-the-clock room service or night cleaning (paid services). Nutrition.


In the main restaurant, meals are served on a buffet basis. Breakfast usually consists of eggs, cereal, yogurt, toast, sausages, fruits and vegetables. But lunches and dinners are much more diverse: several varieties of fish and meat in various forms, soups, vegetables and fruits, as well as insanely delicious Egyptian pastries. From drinks - a packaged hour and coffee. In the evening, at the entrance to the dining room you can enjoy a punch.

Beach.


Kahramana Naama Bay Hotel has its own sandy beach, a 5-7 minute walk from the main building. There are no coral reefs here, so there is no need to stock up on special swimming shoes. On the beach there is a bar from the hotel where you can quench your thirst or have a snack. The entrance to the sea is convenient, if you have a swimming mask, you can see a huge number of colorful fish, which the Red Sea is famous for.

Additional Information.
The hotel area is small, but well-groomed and planted with various shrubs. In the center of the courtyard there is a huge pool of bizarre shape, the water in which is constantly heated. Near the pool there are many sun loungers and wicker basket chairs. At the bar in the center of the courtyard you can enjoy alcoholic and non-alcoholic cocktails or just enjoy a cup of coffee. On the territory of the hotel there are 4 restaurants, in addition to the main one: Italian, fish, international and with traditional Egyptian cuisine. In addition, the hotel has an exchange office, in which the rate is slightly higher than in local bank branches.

Reviews Despite the fact that the reviews about the Kahramana Naama Bay Hotel are somewhat different, we can say that this hotel is intended exclusively for young people. For married couples who prefer a more relaxed pastime, this hotel will not be able to provide the desired privacy. Music, sounding from a dozen clubs located nearby, implies a vibrant nightlife. Kahramana Naama Bay is a hotel for fans of outdoor activities and parties.
