Pela Maria 3 * (Crete, Greece): hotel description, services, reviews

Travelers who are interested in Greece should pay attention to such a resort complex as Pela Maria 3 * . Crete will give you a lot of positive emotions, as well as open up new facets of relaxation.

Location

Only 40 meters from the magnificent sandy beach is the Pela Maria 3 * hotel. Greece, about. Crete, Heraklion, Venizelou, 177 - the address of this complex. The hotel is 30 km from the international airport. The seaport is 28 km away.

Guest apartments

Guests of the Pela Maria 3 * resort complex will feel homely warmth and absolute comfort. Crete will demonstrate its famous hospitality, and this hotel will provide accommodation in the following categories of apartments:

  • A single room is often chosen by independent travelers who do not want to share their room with an outsider or pay for 2 places at once. There is a comfortable orthopedic bed, a desk, a lounge chair and a clothes rack. The balcony has plastic furniture.
  • Standard double room in its equipment resembles the previous category. The main difference is the presence of a second bed, an additional armchair and, of course, a more spacious area.
  • The triple standard is great for families. The room has 3 single beds, which can be moved if necessary. There is a sitting corner by the window with a coffee table and three armchairs.
  • Superior is an Superior Room. The room has a large French bed and folding upholstered furniture. There is also a cosmetic table with a large mirror. The hallway has a spacious wardrobe. In the triple variation of the room, in addition to the double bed, one and a half is also provided.
  • The family room consists of a bedroom and a living room, which are conditionally separated by a bedside table with a TV. In addition to a large bed, the room also has 2 folding sofas. There is a small dining area.

Catering establishments

Do not underestimate nutrition as an integral part of good rest. This issue is given serious attention in Pela Maria 3 *. Crete is characterized by interesting culinary traditions, which you can find in such places:

  • Restaurant "a la carte" will delight guests of Pela Maria 3 * (Hersonissos) with an extensive Mediterranean and international menu. Particular attention is paid to fish dishes. There is also a luxurious wine list.
  • The lobby bar provides a relaxed and friendly atmosphere. Here, guests love to spend time at work or in a friendly conversation. Serving alcohol and refreshing drinks, tea, coffee and pastries.
  • The pool bar is open during the summer (May to October). In addition to alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, you can also enjoy ice cream and refreshments.

General impression

A good option for a summer sea holiday is Pela Maria Hotel 3 * (Crete). Convenient location with regard to resort infrastructure and tourist attractions makes this place ideal for active travelers. In addition, there are several well-maintained beaches nearby. It is worth noting especially the quality of food and friendly service at the hotel. As for the number of rooms, it is not in the best condition. Outdated repairs, shabby furniture and not the latest technical equipment can somewhat spoil the impression. However, if you are not used to spending a lot of time on site, these shortcomings will not be too striking.
