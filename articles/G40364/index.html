Gemini Hotel 3 (Greece, Corfu): photos and reviews of tourists

Many sophisticated travelers dream of spending their long-awaited vacation not somewhere, but in Greece, on the island of Corfu. Here travelers can choose for themselves any hotel, hotel room, booked in advance.

At the moment, we will talk about the hotel Gemini Hotel 3 *. It is located in one of the most picturesque and romantic places in Corfu, where tourists will be surrounded by the mysterious atmosphere of ancient legends and myths combined with exotic nature. Also, vacationers are waiting for excellent service, magnificent beaches and the warm sea.

Before deciding on a vacation in Gemini Hotel 3 * (Corfu), reviews of which will be presented to your attention in the article, it is worthwhile to find out more about this place in more detail. And we will help with this.





Hotel

The hotel is located 50 meters from the beach, which is located in a village called Messonghi, located on the island of Corfu.

Hotel Gemini Hotel 3 * is located in the village of Gouvia. This is an amazingly beautiful place where, in addition to the beauties of nature, there is the opportunity to stroll through the many shops and exotic bars.

We note right away that the hotel is not very large, having a three-story building in its arsenal, but the area near it is quite spacious, reminiscent of an overseas fairy garden. Here, the gaze of vacationers pleases with emerald succulent grass, numerous, fancifully trimmed shrubs, outlandish plants and incredible tall trees reaching the clouds with branches. In the shade of huge leaves you can hide from the heat with a burning-cold cocktail.





Rooms

The hotel Gemini Hotel 3 *, reviews of which are diverse, there are 125 rooms. There are standard double apartments, one-bedroom, three-bedroom and VIP rooms.

By the way, Visa cards, as well as Master Cart and Diners Club are accepted for payment.

At the hotel there is an ATM, which is located three hundred meters from the building.

But pets are not allowed in the hotel.

Each room has a spacious balcony or terrace, upon reaching which the vacationer has the opportunity to admire the pool, the landscape of Corfu and the blue transparent sea.

Apartments are cleaned daily, toiletries are changed. It is also not necessary to remind about the change of bed linen and towels.





Additional amenities in the room

  • A safe for storing jewelry, money, and other things in it.
  • Air conditioning, but its use is paid.
  • Bath or shower. The floor in the toilet is tiled.
  • The Internet. The service is paid.
  • Satellite television.
  • Small fridge. User charges apply.
  • Accessories for taking a bath, shower. Shampoos, conditioners, foams, new washcloths, clean towels.
  • Minibar with alcoholic drinks, gas and juices. Use of the bar is paid.
  • Iron.
  • Hair dryer.

Of course, all the equipment is in good condition and makes it possible to spend rest in comfort. By the way, another indisputable advantage of the rooms is the presence of a stove with two hobs, a toaster and a kettle. In the apartment, as well as on the terrace, there is a table. Guests often have breakfast or dinner after him, enjoying the view of the sunset.





Nutrition

Let us give him a few words. Vacationers have the opportunity to choose a full range of dishes for breakfast, lunch and dinner, and can also stop the choice at only one meal.

The food in Gemini Hotel 3 *, according to the reviews of many vacationers, is incredibly tasty, all dishes are fresh and have a special local flavor.

But there are no private restaurants on site. But there is a nearby, called Lliada Hotel. Here tourists will find delicious national Greek cuisine, where hospitable and friendly waiters will always tell you in detail about each of the items on the extensive menu.

Pool

It offers guests a luxurious pool, where there is a children's area with a playground for games, lots of toys, swings and carousels.

In the pool there are two bars serving alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, fresh juices, pastries, sandwiches and other snacks, slices, as well as dishes prepared on the grill.

Around the reservoir there are sun loungers, umbrellas, and a large slide is installed in the pool itself.





Entertainment on the coast

All that vacationers did not find in the Gemini Hotel 3 * hotel itself (Greece, Corfu, Messonghi) can be found on the territory next to it.

First of all, it is a magnificent beach, where for a couple of euros you can ride a boat, a catamaran, a scooter. Also, for a fee, they will bring you delicious juice, a comfortable sunbed and a fresh sandwich.

The bank is covered with small pebbles, so do not forget to get special slippers, slates.

The water in the sea is transparent and there are practically no waves. So both sunbathing and swimming is one pleasure.

Also not far from the hotel there is a water park and an amusement park where you can have fun for both adults and children.





Hotel reviews

Gemini Hotel 3 * (Corfu) gets both positive and negative reviews. Many vacationers note the following advantages and disadvantages of relaxation.

  • Rest in the hotel is quiet, comfortable, cozy, calm. Designed for people of all ages. The hotel has a lot of entertainment that meets all the requirements of a demanding clientele.
  • Music that sounds outside does not interfere, thanks to the sealed windows.
  • Pleases the area near the hotel. She is beautiful, landscaped and exceeded all expectations. You can spend hours walking past flowers, trees, breathing in aromas and enjoying the light, hot sea breeze.
  • Frustrated by the lack of a DVD player and headphones in the room. It was not possible to watch discs with your favorite films.
  • Few Russian-speaking population, lack of communication. But this is more likely not a disadvantage of the hotel, but simply the internal impressions of the rest in general.
  • Delicious food, a wide range of dishes, the kitchen is designed for both lovers of fatty foods and dieters. Every day they serve fish delicacies, meat, but there was no pork and beef. A variety of salads, snacks, as well as incredible ice cream and pastries.
  • Nearby is a supermarket and a tavern, so there is the opportunity to buy products on their own, and prepare the necessary dish in the room.
  • The sea is very close. The water in it is clean, a lot of fish. The beach with sand and pebbles, mixed. You can swim in a mask and look at the underwater world.
  • I liked the courtesy of the staff, no rudeness, aggression. Always in place. Cleaned every day, are interested in what is needed, offer help.
  • It seemed to the guests that there were few shops with affordable clothes located nearby the hotel.

Summing up, we can say that I liked the stay at Gemini Hotel 3 *, I was impressed by the island of Corfu, a specific atmosphere. Beautiful people. Rest was a success.
