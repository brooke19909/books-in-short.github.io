Hotel "Bogatyr 4 *" (Sochi, Adler): reviews, photos, contacts

The city of Sochi has always been considered one of the most popular resorts in the Russian Federation. And after the Winter Olympic Games were successfully held here in 2014, its fame has gained a worldwide scale. And this is not surprising, because there are not many places on earth where you can relax year-round and at the same time so varied. In summer, the tender Black Sea is at guests' disposal, and in winter, the snow-capped Caucasus Mountains.

In connection with the holding of the Olympic Games in Sochi itself and its urban areas, a large number of new and modern hotels have appeared, in which guests of the resort can be accommodated with all the amenities. One of them is the Bogatyr Hotel, which is rather unusual in its architecture.

Hotel in the amusement park

In the Adler district of Sochi, on the eve of the 2014 Olympics, many new objects have appeared that were created specifically for numerous guests of the resort.

One of these places was a large theme park dedicated to the historical and cultural heritage of the whole of vast Russia. It received a simple and understandable name - ‚ÄúSochi Park‚Äù - and has already managed to become a favorite place for many vacationers, although it was officially opened only in June 2014.

What the hotel looks like, its photo

To match the whole style and mood of Sochi Park, this hotel was built in a very unusual style. The hotel is a real medieval castle, which was created specifically for the comfortable stay of any tourist.





Address of the hotel "Bogatyr"

Bogatyr is a 4 * hotel built in Adler on the Imereti Lowland. Sochi Park, inside which this hotel is located, is located very close to the Olympic Park and its sports facilities.

To get to the hotel, you need to know its exact address. It is located in the Adler district of Sochi, on Olympic Avenue, d. 21. Tourists can approach the hotel in various ways, you just need to choose the most convenient.





How to get to the Bogatyr Hotel for free

Every day, except for those which the park itself does not work (Tuesday and Monday), there are free buses for 88 seats from Sochi railway station and the MoreMall shopping center to Sochi Park. They will comfortably transport guests to their destination, while there are no intermediate stops on the route.

Other routes to the hotel complex

The Bogatyr Hotel, whose contacts are easy to remember, is not very far from the railway station called Olympic Park - at a distance of about 800 m. If you wish, you can also walk to the hotel from it. But for greater convenience of tourists, a minibus runs on this route, which will take any passenger to the entrance to Sochi Park absolutely free. You will be taken to the station by the high-speed Lastochka electric train.

If you are going to the Bogatyr Hotel from the airport in Adler, you must first get to the New Centre shopping center by shuttle bus or bus 135, and then transfer to bus 124 or 124 and get off at the Olympic Park stop. On the same bus you can come here from Sochi railway station or by bus 100 from Adler station.





It is easy to get here for those who decided to get to the city of Sochi by car. Bogatyr (4 * hotel) will look forward to all lovers traveling by car. In order to come here without problems, you need to go along the M24 highway from the city center to Triumfalnaya Street or to Champions Street until the entrance to the Olympic Park appears.

Contact details

The theme park Sochi Park has its own call center. By calling on the phone, every tourist can find out any information of interest to him about the park itself. Here, all the necessary and interesting data for the guests are transmitted by the Bogatyr Hotel. The call center phone is available absolutely free of charge for the whole territory of Russia.

What rooms are in the hotel

Exclusively and unusually designed, the only residential complex of the Bogatyr Hotel offers its guests spacious rooms, the total number of which is 278. Among them there are different categories: from a standard, contented simple room, to royal suites with a unique atmosphere.

No matter which room guests choose for their vacation, they can count on a magnificent view from the window. In front of them can open magnificent mountain landscapes, relaxing boundless expanse of the Black Sea or stunning views of racing tracks and an amusement park.





All the rooms at the Bogatyr Hotel will give guests unforgettable comfort and will surprise you with the unique luxury of interior designs. Thanks to the modern equipment of the rooms, which includes air conditioning, telephone, minibar, safe, satellite TV and free wireless Internet, your stay in the hotel will be truly memorable.

You can learn more about the availability of rooms and order the one you like by calling the Sochi Park call center or the Bogatyr Hotel (Sochi) itself. Telephone of the hotel reservation department: +7 (862) 241-77-77.

Standard rooms

The most simple rooms in the hotel are of two types - these are standard and superior rooms.

In the standard room, a large double bed and other furniture necessary for a comfortable stay awaits guests. Different room sizes are possible, from 31 to 36 square meters. There can also be a sofa or a bunk bed for children, so the maximum number of guests in the apartment is 4 (two children and one adult). The room can be combined with another one and get a spacious two-room family room.





Superior type differs from standard rooms in increased comfort. Room size varies from 37 to 43 square meters. It is also possible to place here not only on a double bed, but also on two single beds.

Variety of suites

Various suites available at the hotel are designed for those tourists who prefer convenience along with luxury. There are five types of suites, and each of them is a real work of art, which the Bogatyr Hotel is ready to offer its guests. A photo of these numbers is a direct proof of this.

The Junior Suite category offers a single room in which there are two twin beds or one large. Designed for a maximum of three adult guests, the room is made in a medieval style and has possible areas from 46 to 55 square meters. m. One of these rooms is designed for people with disabilities.

The suites have two rooms: a bedroom with a large bed and a living room with elegant furniture. There is also a special dressing room. The room is designed for two guests.

A vacation worthy of royal blood awaits anyone who stays in Queen Suite. There is a large and very comfortable bed with a special ottoman at its feet. Soft chairs and a glass table will allow you to comfortably spend your free evenings. There is a dressing room and a special guest toilet. Designed for two guests.





In the King Suite, you can have a royal rest with four. To do this, there is a bedroom and a living room with wide and soft beds, comfortable armchairs, two bathrooms and a truly chic interior. The total area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe room is 130 square meters. m

All tourists who visited the Bogatyr Hotel (Sochi) leave extremely positive reviews about the local rooms. Hotel facilities

The Bogatyr Hotel (Adler) not only provides accommodation to its guests, but also takes care of many of their needs. The price of each room already includes buffet breakfast, 24-hour parking for cars, free Wi-Fi and use of a safe.

For younger guests, the hotel has a special club "Carousel", open every day for 12 hours. Here the children will be engaged in funny animators and professional educators. Among the classes there are exercises, discos, fun starts, all kinds of creativity and games and watching cartoons.





Bogatyr is a 4 * hotel, which has ski equipment rental, which is in demand in winter among active sports enthusiasts. There are also indoor and outdoor pools, a gym. In a special bureau you can book some excursion in Adler, Sochi and their environs.

During the summer season, guests can use the city's pebble beach, which is just 300 meters away.

Tourists who visited the Bogatyr Hotel (Sochi), reviews about the services of the hotel leave mostly positive. Guests especially like the fact that the price of the rooms includes the opportunity to visit Sochi Park with all its attractions. Vacationers are also delighted with the spa complex. Disappointing them only that the cost of his visit is included only in the payment of the most expensive rooms.

Food

So that guests do not have to go outside the hotel in search of any place to eat, there are two excellent restaurants and one bar.

Guests have breakfast at the Ballada restaurant, located on the ground floor of the hotel. Here it is also possible to taste delicious lunches and dinners in a spacious hall that can accommodate up to 185 people.

You can enjoy classic Russian cuisine or a varied European cuisine on the second floor of the hotel, if you go to the Sadko restaurant. He is famous for the best chefs who were invited to Adler.

Many of our compatriots, who chose the Bogatyr Hotel for their holidays, leave mixed reviews about food. Often the stay of tourists is overshadowed by the fact that there are no places near the hotel where you could eat inexpensively, which is why you have to be content only with the Bogatyr restaurant food.

For spa lovers

For real pleasure, the Bogatyr Hotel offers its guests to visit the Wellness & Spa club. For those living in expensive suites, this is available for free, while for the rest, spa services are paid.





It is here that you can swim in the indoor pool or swing in the gym. A variety of hair and nail care services will allow everyone to feel like a royal person, and you can relax well at the hands of real massage professionals. You can also use the bath complex, which includes a hammam, a Russian bath and a Finnish sauna.
