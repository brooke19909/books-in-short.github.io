What is the best wild beach in Gelendzhik?

Gelendzhik is a large resort city located on the Black Sea coast. Any tourist will find entertainment to their taste and wallet here. But where to go swimming and sunbathing is a very difficult question, because there are too many suitable places. What is the best wild beach in Gelendzhik?

On large beaches during the tourist season, water slides or entire children's water parks are often also established. But if everything is so good, why is today the wild beach in Gelendzhik left far from alone? In most recreational areas, camping is prohibited. If there is room for cars, then you will also have to pay for parking or entry.

The benefits of wild beaches



Despite its untidy and lack of offers of additional services, spontaneous beaches annually attract a large number of tourists. They have a pleasant informal atmosphere, it is easy to meet and make friends with their neighbors. If, on the contrary, you came for the sake of privacy, it is enough to put up a tent a little further from the rest, and no one will disturb you. If you wish, you can find a wild beach where you can swim and sunbathe naked. You should not think that places that are not equipped for bathing and relaxation are not crowded, in the middle of the swimming season there are not much less people here than in the central city recreation area.
