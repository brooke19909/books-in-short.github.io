Knitted gloves as an accessory

Since each person is the owner of strictly individual characteristics of each hand, when knitting there cannot be a standard set of loops, adding their standard number, the process can only obey the method of knitting.

If you are making knitted gloves for the first time, then either knit them for yourself, or someone you can approach with a fitting at each stage of execution. You should start your first gloves with a model without patterns (complex). Use different colors, but rely on a common technique.

This article will talk about how to create fingerless gloves. Knitted products with free fingers will look like a fashion accessory on your hand . For their knitting you will need up to 130 g of cotton yarn with a weight of 50 g per 125 m, a set of knitting needles No. 3. The following abbreviations will be applied in the article: p. - loop, p. - row, circle. - circular.



Knitted gloves are always made in a circular spiral with the help of five knitting needles. You need to start from the wrist. Usually they are knitted with dense patterns, in a stocking method using ornaments or colored stripes.

First, try to tie the left glove to the size of a female hand. But first of all, do not forget to take measurements:

  • The circumference of the brush will be about 19 cm.
  • The length of the future product from the wrist to the base of the thumb is up to 6 cm.
  • From the wrist to the base of the little finger, about 9 cm.
  • From the wrist to the base of the index finger, about 10 cm.

Quantitative calculation





Using knitting needles No. 2, knit one sample using the method of stocking . From it you can determine the density of your knitting style, for example, three and a half loops per centimeter long. Now you can easily calculate the total number of loops: 3.5 p. Per 19 cm of the circle of the brush is 66.5 p. We round the result to an integer, reducing it to 66 loops. If the number is not arithmetically divided by 4, i.e. not a multiple of the number of knitting needles, reduce the stitches to a multiple of four to correctly complete the gloves. Knitted with needles, these cute products, when correctly calculated, will turn out with the correct shape of the hand.

Knit the left glove

The cuff will be separated from the glove. It needs to be tied in a circle, cut the thread and fasten. And the wrist should start from the edge of the cuff, from the 5th point from the very beginning of the circular row. The first circular row begins with the first loops, then repeat the rapport of 8 loops twice and knit from the 1st row on the remaining 25 loops. So go on 12 rows. From the next round. R. knit brushes on the first 23 p. of persons. smooth surface (inner side of the palm) and continue on the back side 25 p. Knit another even row.

Thumb hole

Place the beginning of the wedge on the 4th spoke. You need to start it after the elastic. Do the first addition by tying the loop; do not touch the last addition. Then - the crochet, knit the last loop on the front, again the crochet. The next four circles are without additions. The second addition will be without the last 3 p. Before the stitches and after them, throw one crochet. Now 4 points have been added to the spoke. Subsequent additions - after 3-4 rounds, each time without tying an odd number of loops. When the base of the finger is reached, the application ceases. Tie the loops in front of the wedge loops; in turn, string them into two pins. Above them, make air loops, which will be half as much. You can knit to the height that knitted gloves were conceived, then cut the thread and fasten.

Pinky hole

The hinges are divided into 2 and 3 spokes. Tie a stitch of the second knitting needle to the little finger and throw 8 stitches to one pin, and to the other - 7 from the 3rd knitting needle. Next, throw on the 2nd spoke 4 airs, tie the stitches on the 3rd spoke. All knitting was concentrated on 3 knitting needles, the 4th remains working. Tie 3-4 circles reaching the base of the index finger.

Forefinger hole

Place 7 stitches on two knitting needles and work from the palm of your hand. Attach the thread. Knit 7 facial loops, add a loop, again 7 liters. Add another loop, throw 3 p. From the loops of the middle finger onto the 3rd spoke. Spread the loops evenly over 3 knitting needles. On the trail. a circle. R. reduce the number of loops. You can knit to the desired height, then cut the thread and fasten.



Middle finger hole

Place 8, 8, and 7 loops on the three knitting needles. Remove 9 stitches from the thread on the 1st knitting needle from the top half of your product, and transfer 7 stitches from the bottom to the 2nd knitting needle. Dial 4 new loops from the jumper and knit four loops of the upper half, with the 4th knit, knit the remaining 4 loops, add the 4th air loops to them. Follow your finger like your index finger.

Ring finger hole

Throw the loops from the working thread onto the knitting needles in the proportions of 7, 7 and 3, taking the start from the upper half. Use a three-loop knitting needle to loop from the middle finger jumper. Then knit in a circle, like the previous fingers before that. So your first knitted gloves ended.
