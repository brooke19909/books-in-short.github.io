Tenda Hotel 3 * (Bodrum): reviews, photos

Bodrum is the unspoken capital of Turkey. The number of bars located in this part of the coast is huge. The resort occupies fourteen bays. The contingent of vacationers is the Scandinavians, the British, the French and the Russians.

For adventure





The municipal center is divided into two parts. In the east stretches a narrow, but equipped with everything necessary beach. Pubs, snack bars and pubs are concentrated on it. The visiting card of the western quarters of Bodrum is the marina. This area is chosen by a wealthy audience that values ​​peace and solitude. There are large supermarkets and expensive restaurants around luxury hotels.

Near the luxurious mansions, the white sails of moored yachts rise. Their owners are regulars of closed clubs. Frequent winds that run on the coast contribute to the rapid development of the water entertainment industry. From year to year, new surf schools and equipment rental stores open in Bodrum.

Location





In their reviews of Tenda Hotel 3 * in Bodrum, tourists note the excellent transport accessibility of the hotel. It is located in the compact village of Gumbet. The center of Bodrum is three kilometers away. The beach is 150 meters from the residential building. An hour by car is the nearest international airport.

Comfortable buses run from the exit from the passenger terminals of the Mughla air gate to the resort. Ticket price 250 rubles. Taxi drivers charge 1800. When ordering a transfer to the Tenda Hotel 3 * (Bodrum) hotel, reviews confirm this, the trip will cost less. The driver meets his customers. Helps them carry baggage and load it into the car. He is well versed in the provinces and easily finds a hotel.

On the way, the driver can offer a visit to the winery or cheese factory. There’s no need to settle for this adventure. Cars that cater to foreign tourists are equipped with air conditioning. If necessary, you can order a minibus. The standard fare of Bodrum minibuses is 40 rubles. Official carriers ask for 70 plus 80 rubles for boarding the car. for each kilometer traveled.

Accommodations





The hotel was built in 1995. Photos and reviews of the Tenda Hotel 3 * in Bodrum indicate that public areas are outdated and in need of repair. Room renovation took place in 2014. The area occupied by residential buildings is 10,000 m². At the disposal of tourists eleven two-level mansions.

The hotel does not accept guests with pets. There are no special rooms for people with disabilities. Smoking is not allowed in the lobby or hotel rooms. For violation of this requirement faces a large monetary fine. But in their reviews of the Tenda Hotel 3 * (Bodrum), Russians noted that Europeans often did not comply with the requirements. The hotel staff turned a blind eye to this.

The registration procedure for new guests begins at 14:00. Perhaps early settlement. Leave the room on the day of departure before 12:00. Luggage storage is available. The information desk operates around the clock. Its employees are always ready to help in solving everyday issues. Tenda Hotel 3 * in Bodrum is focused on the reception of couples and families with children.

Number of rooms





Vacationers are accommodated in standard suites. Their area is 20 m². Features a balcony with sea or garden views. The microclimate provides air conditioning. The remote control should be obtained from reception staff. You must pay extra for using the safe. The cost is 250 rubles. The TV is connected to cable TV. You can find Russian federal channels.

In Tenda Hotel Bodrum 3 * (Gumbet, Bodrum) free internet access is only available in the lobby. Flooring - ceramic tiles. The furniture is presented by beds, a wardrobe, nightstands, a table and chairs. The bathroom replenishes the supply of soap, shampoo and shower gels. Towels are provided. Room cleaning is carried out every day. Sheets are changed three times a week.

Long-distance communication is paid. For the delivery of food and ready meals in the room you need to fork out. Family apartments, according to the description of the Tenda Hotel 3 * (Bodrum), occupy 30 m². The bathroom has a shower. The use of the refrigerator is permitted for a surcharge. There is only one room in the suite.

The Budget Room is a small living area of ​​20 m². The furniture in it is simple but functional. Windows face the courtyard. Suites of this category are in a separate building. If necessary, the hotel provides a cot. This option is included in the price.

Infrastructure





Dining at Tenda Hotel 3 * (Gumbet, Bodrum) is carried out in bars and restaurants located on the territory of the hotel. The bistro near the water zone starts working at 10:00 and ends at 02:00. The all-inclusive service system is valid until 23:00. There is no need to pay extra for cooking diet foods. The main dining room opens at 08:00. Breakfast ends at 10:00.

Lunch starts at 12:30 and lasts until 14:00. The snack bar serves guests from 15:00 to 17:00. Dinner is from 19:00 to 21:00. The assortment of the restaurant includes fish and meat dishes, as well as food for vegetarians. Turkish-made wine, beer, vodka, lemonade and fruit drinks are poured in bars for free. Ice cream, juices and imported alcohol for cash.

On the photo of Tenda Hotel 3 * in Bodrum it can be seen that the hotel administration creates all the conditions for a comfortable stay. A doctor is on duty at the hotel. There is a currency exchange office. There is a hairdresser, laundry service, shops and gift shops. The lobby accepts plastic cards issued by international systems Visa, MasterCard.

Entertainment





Hotel guests enjoy the municipal beach. It is located a hundred meters from the residential buildings. Towels are provided subject to a deposit. The standard rate for the rental of sun loungers, tents and umbrellas is 150 rubles. The entrance to the water is pebbled. There is sand. To get to the sea, you need to cross a busy highway. There is no underpass.

In the evenings, residents of the hotel are entertained by artists and musicians, acrobats and gymnasts. Themed shows for children. There are four instructors in the animation team. Some employees speak Russian. Entertainment programs are held from May to October. The hotel has outdoor pools. They function seasonally. Water in them is not heated. Filtration and cleaning system works.

The size of the large pool is 90 m², and the small is 77 m². Bathing in them is allowed from 08:00 to 19:00. The aquazone does not work at night. The backlight turns on. Tourists relax at the tables. Sun loungers and umbrellas are free of charge. There are slides. Access to attractions is open from 10:00 to 12:00 and from 15:00 to 17:00.

In the bath complex there are saunas, steam rooms, a hamam. There is a massage room. They don’t take money for renting billiard tables. Entrance to the gym is free. Rental of equipment for diving and extreme sports is paid.

Reviews

Travelers from Russia complained about the rudeness of the staff. The waiters refused to serve in the restaurant. The maids neglected their duties. Had to ask for clean towels and remind about cleaning. The road to the sea runs through a city dump.
