Raiffeisenbank: reviews of ordinary consumers

The last two decades in the Russian Federation are marked by the rapid development of the credit market. Its growth was slightly slowed by the international economic crisis, however, this only had a positive effect on the players. Raiffeisenbank, reviews of which make it possible to judge as a very good organization, entered the Russian market back in 1993 and has since managed to establish itself in this country. Today, this financial institution, a subsidiary of Raiffeisen Bank International AG Group, provides its clients with the most comprehensive range of services in the credit market. It provides the opportunity to place deposits and receive loans to both corporate and private clients, both residents and non-residents of the country in any currency convenient to them.



Raiffeisenbank, whose reviews are mostly positive, is always active both in the interbank and foreign exchange markets and is constantly looking for ways to expand. Most often this happens by absorbing other financial institutions and combining their infrastructure with the European giant. In 2007, Raiffeisenbank acquired Impexbank and began to consistently occupy a place in the top ten in the credit market of the Russian Federation.

At the moment, it is difficult to find a more stable company than Raiffeisenbank. Reviews of experts call it the most reliable bank among all representatives of this sector in the Russian Federation. All its programs have some advantages over similar services of other companies.
