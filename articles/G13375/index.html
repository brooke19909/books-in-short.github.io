Yekaterinburg hotels with a swimming pool: rating, description, photos and reviews

Yekaterinburg is the capital of the Urals, one of the largest megalopolises in Russia. The city has many interesting sights, beautiful, ancient temples and churches, wonderful parks and a botanical garden. For guests of Yekaterinburg, all conditions are created for a carefree vacation. They do not need to worry about the place of residence, because in the city there are a huge number of hotels from economists to 5-star ones. The article will present the best hotels in Yekaterinburg with an outdoor pool. So, let's get acquainted with them.

The best Yekaterinburg hotels with pool: rating

Today, the city has more than 20 hotels. Half of them offer their guests unlimited access to the spa area with a Turkish hammam and a Russian steam room, massage room, as well as a huge swimming pool. The tourist who first visited the city, his head goes round, from so many options. In order that you do not have to rack your brains for a long time on the question of which option to book, the article offers a rating of the most popular hotels in Yekaterinburg with a swimming pool. We hope this list will help you make a choice:

  1. Hotel "Ramada" takes first place. It is located near Koltsov Airport, and is ideal for you if you arrived in Yekaterinburg by plane. In addition, the hotel has comfortable rooms equipped with the latest furniture and appliances, a restaurant, a large spa complex and its own private beach.
  2. In the heart of the city is the Green Park Hotel. It is he who gets the second line of the rating. In hotels the atmosphere of real home comfort reigns. Having been here once, you will definitely want to come back again. Near the hotel there are many large shopping and entertainment centers, the Arboretum and Green Grove Park.
  3. In the huge skyscraper "Vysotsky", it seems, there is absolutely everything. There are offices of various companies, and restaurants with delicious dishes, and an observation deck, which offers stunning views of Yekaterinburg. The last floors of the building are occupied by the Vysotsky Hotel. Thanks to the location, as well as coziness and comfort that await hotel visitors, he closes the top three winners.
  4. If you want to relax in nature, we suggest you book a hotel house in the suburban complex "Sands". It is located on the shores of Lake Shartash. Here you can enjoy the harmony of silence and be alone with nature. The Sands complex closes our rating, taking fourth place.

Ramada

The capital of the Urals offers its guests an unforgettable vacation at the Ramada Hotel. This is an amazing place where you can enjoy the comfort of your entire stay. Near the hotel there is a large parking lot, which is set around the clock monitoring. At the entrance you will be met by a doorman and help with heavy suitcases. Also, when booking a room, you can order the service: meeting by car at the airport. You can choose the following option for your stay:

  1. Standart room. There are 20 of them in the hotel. Each is designed for two people, equipped with a double bed, with a special orthopedic mattress and the most modern technology. The room has a shower room and air conditioning. Cost: from 5000 rubles per day (breakfast included).
  2. Superior Room. It is also designed for two people, the fee for an extra bed is 1500 per day. A child under 7 years old can stay in a room with parents for free. It differs from the standard: the presence of a spacious dressing room and a larger area. Price: from 6400 (breakfast is included in this price).
  3. Suite All rooms in this category have a minibar and a telephone. The suite is divided into two rooms: a large living room with a cozy working area and a modern TV, and a bedroom with a soft bed and wardrobe. The price of living in such paradise conditions is 9600 per day.
  4. The Ramada Hotel also offers special rooms for people with disabilities, for newlyweds and for those who have arrived in Yekaterinburg for business.

The main advantages of the hotel: a 24-hour restaurant on site and a huge spa complex equipped with a hammam, steam room and pool. "Ramada" is located at the 10th km of Koltsovsky highway, 15.

"Green Park Hotel"

Looking for a lodging option in the city center? Check out the Green Park Hotel. It is located in the very center of Yekaterinburg (address: Narodnaya Volya, 24). There are many public transport stops, so you can easily get anywhere in the city. Green Park is a unique hotel in which the warmth of homeliness is harmoniously combined with rich Italian furniture.

The entire interior is made in deep, eye-pleasing shades of red, terracotta and dark chocolate. Each room (110 pcs.) Has recently been renovated, they are equipped with air conditioning and the latest technology. Visitors can book: standard, business or luxury. Price per day: from 3100 rubles. It is worth noting that this is one of the best hotels in Yekaterinburg with a pool and a Russian steam room. There is also a 24-hour fitness room, restaurant and lobby bar. You can order delicious dishes directly to your room.

"Vysotsky" - Yekaterinburg hotel with pool and spa

A hotel room will be a great option for those who value luxury and are used to surround themselves with rich furniture. Vysotsky is a 5-star hotel with an amazing opportunity to see Yekaterinburg from a bird's eye view, as well as city attractions such as the Temple on Blood and the Isetsky residential complex.

You can book rooms of the following category: single or double standard, junior suite and suite. Each decoration is huge panoramic windows. You will not see such beauty in any hotel in Yekaterinburg with or without a pool. Upholstered and comfortable furniture will give you the opportunity to relax.

You will enjoy the unique interior of the hotel, all rooms are made in rich, golden beige colors. Guests can swim in the pool, visit the Vladimir Vysotsky Museum, take a steam bath in the sauna and eat well in cafes and restaurants located in the skyscraper building.

Address: Malysheva, 51. Cost per day: from 5000 rubles.

The best vacation in the suburban complex "Sands"

Do you like to relax in tents and with friends? Then this country hotel with a swimming pool (Yekaterinburg) is what you need. There are many entertainments for guests: paintball, laser tag, rope park, climbing wall, large swimming pool, four saunas and the opportunity to ride bicycles and roller skates.

The beautiful restaurant is decorated in a pirate style. Here you can taste delicious dishes of European and Russian cuisine. The local chef is a true professional. Cozy wooden houses are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay.

You can choose: "Comfort" house (for two people), VIP-house (for two, there is an additional barbecue area and a music center with karaoke), "Black Pearl" house (a huge room designed for a company of 6 people). Cost: from 2000 rubles per day.

Address: Sands, 13.

Reviews

Guests of the best hotels in Yekaterinburg leave mostly rave reviews. They note the cleanliness and comfort of the rooms, comfortable furniture and good appliances. Also, visitors are satisfied with the opportunity to swim in the pool, relax in the hammam and in the massage room. In addition, they note the high professionalism of hotel employees who are always welcoming and willing to help.
