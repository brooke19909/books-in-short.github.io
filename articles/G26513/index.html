How to use Apple Watch: instruction manual

Smart gadgets have flooded the market long ago. They have become an integral part of the life of almost every person. And if at first it seemed that users were paying tribute to fashion, now it is clear that such devices have become assistants for many. Of course, smart phones are most actively used now, but the clock is not far behind. How to use Apple Watch?

What is it?

Apple Watch is a smart watch that was developed by Apple. They are designed to synchronize with the smartphones of the company, so often the owners of the iPhone are available.

Of course, the device was originally designed as a watch. But later it began to acquire a huge number of useful and useless functions. During its existence, the watch has become so versatile that many do not understand how to use the Apple Watch.

Versions

The first Apple Watch appeared in 2014. Then it was a simplified version of what we have now. Although even then they looked solid and stylish. Since 2016, the company produced annually one modification. Therefore, by the beginning of 2019, we have Apple Watch 4 - the most advanced model.

Functionality

It turned out that it was not easy to understand how to use the Apple Watch. There are no instructions in Russian as such. Of course, the kit comes with a booklet that briefly describes how to connect the watch to a smartphone. On the official website you can find more detailed gadget settings. But how exactly to use the device: where to click, what to choose and so on - there is nothing like that.

Therefore, to begin with, you need to figure out what functions the accessory generally offers, in order to later understand what opportunities open up for Apple Watch users.

The first feature is watch activity. Until you look at them, they will be in the off state. As soon as you look at the display and turn your hand, the screen will light up. As soon as you expand your hand, the device will immediately turn off the display. If you need to do this quickly, just cover the screen with your palm.

You can type messages from the device. It is not difficult to do this: since the screen is very small, you need to enter text by voice input. There is a built-in fitness tracker inside the device. It takes into account a huge number of parameters and monitors your health.

You can also use Siri's virtual assistant. There is an email client, but it shows letters in a compact form. There is no web browser in the device, as well as dialing. In general, the clock is not designed for games, although a couple of puzzles can be installed.

Start

So, you have become the proud owner of any version of the watch. You need to configure the gadget. To do this, you must turn on Bluetooth on your smartphone. Next, connect the watch to recharge. Now turn them on, holding the wheel on the side. Next, the gadget will prompt you to select the language to use.

Now you need to create a pair between devices. What do I need to do? First, open the Apple Watch software on your smartphone. It will be able to connect to the watch. The smartphone opens the camera and asks you to point the lens at the Apple Watch display. It is necessary that the device falls into the yellow square.

This will synchronize. You will need to do a simple device setup.

Customization

So, you need to decide which hand you put on your watch. After that, you need to accept the terms of iTunes, and then use your Apple ID. Next, you will need to confirm the nearest service centers, agree with Siri, and use diagnostics.

How to use Apple Watch? To do this, you need to set a password for the gadget, and then configure the unlock. You can choose to simultaneously activate the watch and smartphone.

Next, you need to configure the software installation. For example, you can automatically download versions of applications that are installed on the iPhone to the watch, but at the same time have an analogue for the Apple Watch. What is Siri?

As mentioned earlier, Siri is a virtual assistant developed by Apple. It works on almost all devices of the company. Smart watches were no exception. How to use Siri on Apple Watch?

To start, the virtual assistant must be called. There are two ways to do this: using the button and voice. In the first case, just hold the wheel. In the second, you will need to say the command: "Hello, Siri." Thus, the virtual assistant will become active, and it can be used.

In this case, working with Siri is no different, as if you called it on a laptop or smartphone. You can ask her for anything that interests you.
