What is not a pity to throw, but you can put into business

To create original jewelry and functional items, not only purchased expensive accessories and other accessories are used, but also waste material. These are objects that a person used in everyday life, and then throws it out as unnecessary. It includes used cardboard packaging, plastic and glass bottles, egg cells, old tires and parts from various failed mechanisms. Everything that is not a pity to throw out, in skillful hands can turn into an original item, which can become an interior decoration or a gift for a dear person.

Original decorations for the street

In the design of a summer house or a personal plot, you can use junk material on a grand scale. These are old tires, from which you can make multi-tiered flower beds, and bright sculptures. They are also used for the manufacture of outdoor furniture, which is not afraid of the vagaries of the weather. Old buckets and broken dishes can serve as material for street lamps and flower pots.

The ubiquitous plastic bottles are an indispensable material for the design of beds and flower beds. With their help, you can not only decorate the site and outline the boundaries of planting, but even use them as a device for irrigation, when it is not possible to often visit the suburban area. And glass bottles are not only the basis for crafts, but also cheap building material from which craftsmen build arbors and other buildings.



In skillful hands, even bottle caps turn into material for creativity. A boring fence made of netting netting or a blank wall of a house turns into art objects after they are decorated with a mosaic of colored bottle caps.

Crafts with children

Throw material is a great foundation for children's creativity. Kids are happy to come up with new possibilities for using seemingly unnecessary items. Children's imagination allows you to turn several plastic cups into characters of your favorite fairy tale.



Crafts from waste material for children's creativity should be selected taking into account the characteristics of the development of children. Usually they use plastic bottles and caps from them, disposable tableware, plugs for toilet paper and paper towels, buttons, nutshells and much more. It should be easy for kids to work with materials: cut them, puncture, glue and perform other manipulations.



From the cardboard boxes left after buying the furniture, you can make a real kitchen for a little hostess. And for the boy - to make a car in which he will drive around the apartment. Yes, you never know what loving parents can come up with for their children. The joint production of such crafts is very close, and new ideas come during work. Adults perform complex steps, and the baby can be entrusted with a decorative finish.

You can make a house or a store out of unnecessary cardboard by cutting windows and doors. A small box can be used to make a dollhouse. Inside it can be glued with the remains of wallpaper, curtains can be cut from scraps of fabric. Furniture is also made from everything at hand. Making handicrafts from waste material is a fascinating activity that helps adults return to childhood, and children will learn how to create new things with their own hands.

New Year decoration

New Year - a holiday that everyone is waiting for, young and old. Prepare for this day in advance, decorating a Christmas tree and an apartment, making gifts. And on the eve of this holiday, as never before, junk material will come in handy. Old catalogs and magazines can be used in the manufacture of garlands and flashlights. From blown bulbs, original Christmas toys are obtained, which no one else has. Plastic cups can become the basis for toys if they are painted with bright colors and add shine.



Working with junk material teaches both adults and children not only to create and develop, but also to find innovative solutions. Teaches frugality and love for the environment.

Before sending any item that has become unusable into the trash, you should carefully look at it carefully. Or maybe this is not dumping material at all, but the basis of a future masterpiece. You can always throw away what is no longer needed, but giving a second life is already art.
