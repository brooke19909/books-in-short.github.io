DIY children's album

Toothless smile, the first step, grimy face and even the first tear - all this I want to keep in mind and proudly show to friends and relatives. A children's album composed using scrapbooking technique will help you in this way.



Start small

Burning with enthusiasm when viewing many pictures, parents are often lost and do not know how to tastefully arrange the first children's photo album. If they have enough free time and skills in scrapbooking, the children's album will be truly exclusive. Otherwise, you can try to make an original out of a regular photo book.



To do this, you need a simple album with inserts for photos. It is worth choosing the option in which there are fields for notes. Next, you should stock up with 4 sheets of thick paper, which in size perfectly repeat the outline of the pages of the prepared photo book. This is the main set on which the future children's album will be built.

Now you should decide on the design theme and its color resolution. For example, the theme "Teddy" for the boy Misha. Therefore, you need a set of scrapbooking paper with the image of Teddy Bear. If it is not, then it will be enough to look at old children's books and magazines from which you can cut out the image of the world famous teddy bear, and use colored paper in pastel shades for the background. Curly hole punch, glue, scissors, decorative buttons, beads, braid are also useful.

The work begins with the fact that a new background with an application invented by the parents is glued to the cover of the photo album. Each page should be designed separately, depending on the semantic load that it carries.
