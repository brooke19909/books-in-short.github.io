The recreation center "Crystal", Anapa. Photos and reviews of tourists

The recreation center "Crystal" is located on the outskirts of the city of Anapa, in the Krasnodar Territory. Its territory belongs to the village of Blagoveshchenskaya, from which it is close at hand to the airport and railway station. In addition, if you are a travel enthusiast in a personal car, then a lightly loaded track runs nearby, so it is very easy to get to these parts.



Nature

Around the base there is clean, almost untouched fine sand, so the impression of an uninhabited island is created. One of its sections is washed by the open sea, others - by estuaries. There are two of them - Kiziltoshsky and Vityazevsky estuaries. The base can boast of iodine-bromide therapeutic fumes, which are shown to many to improve health, therefore it is believed that here is an eco-resort No. 1. Khrustalnaya is a recreation center, which is also famous for a large amount of healing mud that has a beneficial effect on the body. The open sea, the absence of bays and capes, the waters of which are often muddy, create conditions for healthy swimming in the purest water of the salt sea.

Comfortable rest

Eco-resort "Crystal" (recreation center) is located on a large, fully landscaped area, which will certainly affect the comfort of the holiday. Not far from it is equipped with an entertainment area, here you will surely have fun from the heart. Visitors will immediately be offered a choice of rooms. There are options for placement in detached houses, which are equipped with all amenities. Another alternative is rooms located in small buildings. The recreation center "Crystal" simultaneously accommodates 250 guests.



Many tourists are interested in the issue of nutrition. Is food included in the price of the tour? Meals are not included in the price of the trip, but the Khrustalnaya recreation center (Anapa) offers to use the services of a dining room or visit a cafe located nearby. For those who do not like to eat in public places, the conditions for self-cooking are created.

Separate houses

Comfort class rooms are suitable for those who want to relax together or in a family circle without strangers, they are designed for two, three or four. Such conditions become a good option for a family with a child, and comfort will also be acceptable for newlyweds. Such houses are located at a distance, so peace is ensured.

The house has one room in which there is everything necessary for comfort, for example, it can consist of either three twin beds, or one double and one single. The recreation center "Crystal" provides guests with a refrigerator, wardrobe, air conditioning and TV, which are in each room. The rooms are equipped with a bathroom, which has a bathroom, washbasin, hot water is supplied around the clock. The houses have small verandas with furniture, it is very convenient for evening conversations with tea.



Enclosures

Standard rooms are located in small buildings. Each has five apartments with one room. As in a comfortable room, it can accommodate two or three people. Inside, it is a room with single beds, bedside tables, a wardrobe for linen, a TV is offered on request. The building has a shared toilet and a shower room. A two-compartment refrigerator is located in the lobby. A large gazebo near the building will accommodate everyone. Hygiene products and towels are provided for guests.

Leisure

Active guests will not be bored, because you are offered a lot of diverse and interesting programs - the Khrustalnaya recreation center took care of this. Anapa is a resort town located on the Black Sea coast, so you can go windsurfing, diving, diving, try your hand at parachuting. If you are a lover of adventure covered in romance, then you probably want to study the history of sunken ships, because they are available for research, but keep in mind that they are at the bottom of the sea.



Relaxing holiday

For those who love a quiet pastime, eco-resort No. 1 "Crystal" (recreation center) offers a body cleansing procedure. Take a steam bath in a real Russian wood-fired bathhouse, then swim in the pool, and then relax in the relaxation room - is this not a vacation? Also for a relaxing holiday for guests in the base are gazebos that are convenient for board games or just chatting. Also for convenience, the Khrustalnaya recreation center (Blagoveshchenskaya) offers holidays to be celebrated in a small but cozy cafe, and use sun loungers and parasols on the beach.

Summer is a great time for yoga, ayurveda and other health-improving exercises that improve body and soul. Therefore, the Khrustalnaya recreation center every summer season opens its doors to seminars where communication takes place between professionals who are beginning to study and those who would like to embark on the path of this unity and join in improving methods.



Reviews

What are the reviews of tourists? Do you like it eco-resort? Khrustalnaya is a recreation center (Anapa), which, according to tourists, has both advantages and disadvantages. From the facts that aroused the positive emotions of vacationers, the following can be distinguished:

  • a free sand and shell beach, which is very close to the recreation center;
  • children's animation twice a day with drawing, modeling, crafts, outdoor games, watching cartoons;
  • morning exercises are carried out on the seashore;
  • free yoga classes twice a day;
  • fresh linen for only 200 rubles, as there is a laundress that washes well;
  • in the afternoon live music sounds and prices are pleasing;
  • good nutrition;
  • Cozy base with helpful staff.

Of the disadvantages are:

  • no sound insulation in the houses;
  • parking cars is problematic;
  • mosquitoes;
  • lack of electric kettles in the rooms;
  • water does not leave well in the shower;
  • floors cannot be washed due to lack of rags and buckets.

These are the reviews of tourists.

Unforgettable vacation

The sandy spit of fine golden sand is spread over more than 20 kilometers; its boundless space creates the feeling that you are on a fabulous uninhabited island. But this is still not a fairy tale, but a reality, and nature and the Khrustalnaya recreation center give it to people. Anapa (photo of the city depicts the seashore) has a comfortable bottom of the sea. Gradually deepening, allows the summer sun to warm water to a temperature of 27 degrees. Swimming in the sea for adults and children gives a lot of pleasure, heals and fills with a pleasant experience. Therefore, the rest here is unforgettable!



The target audience

The guests of the base are people who lead the right way of life or wish to improve their health, broaden their horizons and get acquainted with new physical and spiritual techniques for them. Tourists who come here want to get the result from the application of the ancient knowledge of the East about the integrity of the health of the body.

Holiday Village "Crystal" - a great choice for a vacation with the whole family! Each family member will choose a hobby for themselves. Parents can be at the sea or a seminar, while the children will be engaged in the work under the supervision of a sensitive and attentive teacher. Indeed, the base staff has extensive experience in conducting creative as well as sporting events for children. The resort allows children to have fun, not forgetting the benefits, because professional teachers who know and love their job work here. The resort territory is huge, owned by 4 hectares of the coastline, which allows guests to feel like natives on a desert island. There are no fences, no extra people, only you are alone with the sun and sea.

Individual program

You can use the services of a specialist nutritionist. He will specially develop an individual recovery program for you, which can include losing weight, detox, as well as saturating your body with vitamins and amino acids, all within your wishes and needs. Proper nutrition at the base is not just empty words: the chef will specially prepare for you wholesome, and most importantly, delicious vegetarian dishes from natural eco-friendly farming products. A yoga instructor will deal with your health every day in an individual mode, correctly calculating the load, and reckoning with your level of fitness of the body and spine for stretching. The Ayurveda master will select an individual program of healing and beauty-promoting treatments and massages according to your wishes.



Four hectares of its own territory is an ideal platform for beach games, children's recreation, as well as wellness practices. Food - only environmentally friendly, with the possibility of ordering a vegetarian table and raw food diet. Products are purchased from Kuban farmers, the ecological table consists of vegetables, fruits, dairy and other products. The base is a great place for solitude, meditation, as well as group practices.
