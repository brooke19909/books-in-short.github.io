LCD "Dubki" - a new housing complex in Orenburg

LCD "Dubki" is created for those who want to live in an area with a clean environment and at the same time not experience any inconvenience from the lack of familiar comfort. Located in the city, it has a developed infrastructure and accessible parking.





Complex project

Two years ago, the association of developers began the development of a new microdistrict. The project was called LCD "Dubki". This complex is located right in the city center, which gives it special value. The development project involves the construction of 28 residential buildings on the 10th, 14th and 17th floors. In addition, it is planned to build two more elite five-story houses with a high level of comfort. Everyone who wants to move to a residence in the residential complex ‚ÄúDubki‚Äù will be able to choose a home that will fully satisfy him: from a 1-room to 3-room apartment ranging in size from 38.5 to 94.4 square meters.

When buying a home, you can get a long-term mortgage.





Location

LCD "Dubki" for the convenience of citizens is being built almost in the center of Orenburg, in the Leninsky district along Uralskaya Street. Therefore, the entire infrastructure of the city will be at the services of residents. At the same time, the developer tried not to disturb the natural landscape in order to create the most favorable environmental situation in the region. Silence, a beautiful view, a forest - this is what everyone who buys an apartment in the complex will receive.

Infrastructure

In addition to educational institutions, shops and other necessary buildings, the complex involves the construction of additional infrastructure: kindergartens, schools, underground parking lots, sports and playgrounds. In addition, an additional convenient transport hub is already being built here.

The complex is being built in order to provide the population with comfortable housing, beautiful parks, in which it is good to take a walk in the evening. There will be no parking problems, and everyone can quickly get all the necessary services for a full life.





Oaks are being built by several construction companies, each of which bears joint responsibility and directs all its efforts to ensure that the microdistrict turns out to be comfortable, with a rationally distributed area.

Description of objects

The houses are built from panel blocks, which allows maximum soundproofing of the premises, to keep them warm during the off-season and gives a beautiful aesthetic appearance. On the ground floors, the location of non-residential premises is provided, in which shops, offices, beauty salons will be placed on a rental basis in the future.

Exterior facades are insulated with a multilayer system of mineral wool, trimmed with a decorative coating. This will save heat inside the apartment and increase the amount of heat saved, significantly reduce the cost of its consumption.

The layout of the apartments will satisfy the most demanding residents. The rooms are spacious, separate. The dimensions of the kitchen facilities will please any housewife. And three-meter ceilings visually make the rooms larger and provide opportunities for decoration during repair work.

In addition, in each apartment, individual devices are installed to account for heat consumption.

First stage houses have already been built, and some apartments are settled in Dubki residential complex. Reviews of new homeowners in this complex are only positive. The builder has an opinion as trustworthy and attentive. Also, good reviews about the quality of the rough finish of the apartments, after settling the residents do not have to redo anything. All work was done in good faith, which cannot but rejoice those who were lucky enough to purchase real estate in a new housing complex in Orenburg.
