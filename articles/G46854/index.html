Unpredictable Egypt. El Gouna - Eastern Venice

One of the most accessible and attractive countries for tourists is Egypt. El Gouna is a resort located a little north of the famous Hurghada. Despite the proximity of such a noisy entertainment center, it has a special charm. It is often called Eastern Venice, because a large number of islands, the presence of boats that run instead of cars, the close proximity of hotels and restaurants to the sea - all this is characteristic of the legendary Italian city. Well, travel lovers know that Egypt can also boast of such a treasure.





El Gouna is an ideal place to relax with children. Due to the large number of islands that are located near this town, the sea here is shallow and always warm. Sandbanks sometimes stretch for half a kilometer in length, so there is no need to fear that the child will be in the open sea. At the same time, extreme lovers can find entertainment here. On the beaches of Zeytouna Beach and Mangroovy Beach there are services that offer any equipment for kitesurfing, as well as provide boats and catamarans for rent. Therefore, you can at any time ride a yacht on the waters of the Red Sea and enjoy the mysterious Egypt.



El Gouna is located on the islands of the Red Sea, which are separated by numerous canals and small channels. And each of these islands, as a separate mini-state, is distinguished by its features, design and services. The most popular resort island is Zeytuna. Here are the most fashionable hotels, spa centers, gyms and golf courses. A huge number of summer open cafes and elite expensive restaurants are concentrated on the same island-beach. In such establishments, tourists are offered both local cuisine with all its features, and Russian and European dishes familiar to us all. Also in this place is a small historical museum, which presents artifacts that characterize Ancient and Medieval Egypt.



Excursion El Gouna - Luxor or El Gouna - Cairo is one of the attractions that all vacationers like. This resort is very quiet and calm, you can always find a deserted beach where no one and nothing will distract you from your own thoughts and dreams. But sometimes you want to plunge into the active world, where traffic is noisy, people are bustling, and most importantly - all this happens against the backdrop of sand and pyramids. That is why for one day many people are sent to large cities, get acquainted with new customs there, meet new people and find out everything that Egypt is famous for.

El Gouna is one of those eastern resorts that the European elite of society has chosen. That is why a large number of cottages and villas have been built here, in which wealthy Germans and Dutch come to rest. Those who go to this recreation area should keep in mind that the locals practically do not know the Russian language, but they are fluent in German and English. The service is aimed specifically at European tourists.

As you can see, the resort of El Gouna, Egypt is very peculiar and extraordinary. Reviews about this town are unanimous: the place is quiet, calm, but at the same time not boring and very colorful.
