Sights of the resort region

The village of Peresyp (Temryuk district, Krasnodar Territory) is a very small settlement, but despite this, it is full of interesting places that tourists constantly visit. The resort area is famous for the famous wineries "Fanagoria" and "Zaporizhzhya", therefore, as part of the excursions, tourists visit the tastings of real Kuban wines. Every vacationer can try these sweet drinks.



The second most beautiful place, favorite among tourists, is the Valley of lotuses. It is located in the Akhtanizovsky estuary. A plantation of amazing Indian flowers grows on the water, therefore, to admire them, travelers are taken by boat along the river.

Near the village of Peresyp (only 1 km) there are therapeutic muds to which vacationers come from all over the country. This place has a name - mud lake Sinaya Balka. It can be visited both independently and as part of the tour. Peresyp Beaches

For those who prefer a more relaxing holiday instead of sightseeing trips, a wonderful beach is offered in the village of Peresyp (Krasnodar Territory). Rest in this place is pleasant, as the beach is covered with fine quartz sand. Tourists can not be afraid that they will hurt themselves on the sharp edges of shells or hit stones - it is very comfortable to walk along the seashore.

The sea also has the potential to plunge into its open spaces - the water here is surprisingly blue and clear. It is suitable for families with children, as it is very calm and shallow. Also for little vacationers on the beach there are water rides for children. In addition, Peresyp beach also involves outdoor activities - tourists are invited to ride a catamaran, a "banana", a water bike, and parachute over the waters of Azov.



Recreation facilities Peresyp (Krasnodar Territory)

In this village you can always stay in one of several tourist camps. Each of them is characterized by comfort and high quality service. Having settled in this place, you can significantly diversify your sea vacation with additional entertainment, as well as paid and free services. Bases can include cinemas, bars, pools, sports fields, billiards, discos and much, much more. In addition, they usually have their own first-aid post, souvenir shops, and the Internet.

Golden Beach and Dolphin

The most famous stopping places include Golden Beach (Krasnodar Territory, Peresyp, 58 Kalabadka St.) and Dolphin, located at Krasnodar Territory, Peresyp, ul. Calabad, 84.

This recreation center is located within walking distance from the sea, has its own parking lot and comprehensive meals. There is a hotel for visitors with rooms of various price categories. The camp site has its own small water park, camping, honey and wine baths, a mud lake.

The Dolphin camp site is located on the coast of Azov, but at the same time, 20 km from it you can swim in the Black Sea. This intersection of waters gave the climate softness and incredible terrain. There is a large area, which is rich in fruit trees and flowers. Nature, untouched by man, surprises with its naturalness and beauty. Along the territory of the recreation center are gazebos and fountains, for young tourists there is a playground.

Forward and Galant

No less hospitable, and therefore popular are the Forward (Krasnodar Territory, Peresyp, Kalabadka St., 80) and Galant (Krasnodar Territory, Peresyp, 35 Bondareva St.).

With its warmth, the first camp site gladly welcomes guests vacationing both alone and with their families and friends. Fishing in Peresyp of the Krasnodar Territory

The village has tourists not only for its beaches, attractions and the availability of entertainment, but also fishing. The silence and comfort of this place attract both fishing enthusiasts and professionals.

In some places of the village there are specially allotted "ripe" waters for such an activity. They usually catch carp, crucian carp, and bream.
