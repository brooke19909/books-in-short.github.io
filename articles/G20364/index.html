Smartphone Asus ZenFone 2 Lazer ZE500KL 16Gb: customer reviews and specifications

Most well-known smartphone manufacturers have already released their new flagship devices this year. Nevertheless, since most devices came out in the middle price category, it is also important to consider phones that have a low price tag.





Hardware specifications and packaging

The Asus ZenFone 2 Lazer ZE500KL 16Gb smartphone comes in a typical ASUS branded package, in a glossy box that has an image of the smartphone on the top side and an indication of its main features printed on the bottom and sides. The sliding cover comes off quite easily, and inside it you will find a smartphone located on top of a cardboard tray.

Inside the package, along with the new gadget, you will find the following: charging and data cable, power charger, warranty and user manual.

ASUS offers 6 color options for the Asus ZenFone 2 Lazer ZE500KL 16Gb LTE: black, gold, gray, red, white and purple.

Specifications

ZenFone 2 (ZE500KL) has an interesting 5.0-inch IPS LCD display with a resolution of HD 1280x720 and a pixel density of 294 dpi. This smartphone runs on a 1.2 GHz quad-core processor, namely Qualcomm Snapdragon MSM8916 410. This processor is equipped with a built-in Adreno 306 video chip (450 MHz), which supports OpenGL ES 3.0 and all the features of unified shaders. In addition, the device has 2 GB of RAM.





In terms of storage devices, ZenFone 2 has 16 GB of internal memory, of which only 10 GB can be used for its intended purpose. However, this is not a problem, since you can use microSD cards with a capacity of up to 128 GB. The battery life of this smartphone is provided by a well-balanced removable lithium-polymer 2070 mAh battery.

The main camera is manufactured by Toshiba and has 13 megapixels, 5 lens elements, autofocus, dual LED flash, laser focusing technology and HDR capabilities, while the front camera has a resolution of 5 megapixels.

Speaking of connectivity, we note that the Asus ZenFone 2 Lazer ZE500KL 16Gb (which is generally positive) includes a MicroUSB 2.0 port, a Bluetooth 4.0 chip and a wireless network that uses 802.11 A / B / G / N / AC standards. In terms of wireless support, you can connect to Wi-Fi networks with a frequency of 2.44 to 5 GHz. The ASUS ZE500KL functionality also offers the ability to use two SIM cards at the same time, but only one of them supports 4G mobile networks (LTE and HSPA +). The second SIM will only offer 2G connectivity.

Dimensions

The Asus ZenFone 2 Lazer ZE500KL 16Gb smartphone has a height of 143.7 mm and a width of 71.5 mm. The thickness of the device is from about 3.5 mm to 10.5 mm due to the curved back, which has a bulky middle and very thin edges. These dimensions of the device provide him with a very small mass - only 140 grams.

Platform and interface

Battery

A battery capacity of 2070mAh will allow you to use the Asus ZenFone 2 Lazer ZE500KL 16Gb smartphone (reviews of which did not work for long, were a little disappointing) for 10-12 hours with moderate use and 6-7 hours with intensive mode. Camera

The main camera on the ASUS ZE500KL has 13 megapixels and uses a Toshiba PixelMaster sensor. It comes equipped with laser autofocus, which allows you to provide a focusing time of only 0.3 seconds (this feature was also available on the flagship ZenFone 2). The camera works fine and when shooting at close range, if you place the lens 5 cm from the captured subject, you get a great macro shot. As follows from user reviews, on this device you can take pictures with a maximum resolution of 4096x3072 pixels.

Nevertheless, in low light conditions, image clouding is quite noticeable. ASUS is trying to solve this problem with a low-light mode, which manages to eliminate interference, but it also reduces the sharpness of details. To help you in such situations, we have found that using a dual LED flash can reduce noise.

At the same time, the dual LED flash provides accurate color and especially skin tones in portrait shooting. It is worth mentioning the fact that Asus ZenFone 2 Lazer ZE500KL 16Gb (black and other color options) offers a number of camera functions that are not available in most middle-class smartphones. First of all, it is the presence of manual mode, GIF mode, a timer, simultaneous shooting of several frames (the camera captures several images from which you can choose the best one), the Selfie option (allows you to use the rear camera for self-portrait), the mini mode (blurs the surroundings of the subject ), HDR and others.

The front camera, with an extension of 5 megapixels, will help you take a lot of good selfies on which you will look good thanks to the available effects and settings.

Asus ZenFone 2 Lazer ZE500KL 16Gb - specifications and verdict

As the above review shows, ASUS ZenFone 2 ZE500KL is a mid-range smartphone with excellent technical specifications. Its functionality will not allow you to play the most demanding games available on Google Play, but it is undoubtedly able to satisfy all the needs of the average user.
