Village Severskaya of the Krasnodar Territory: description, photo

The village of Severskaya in the Krasnodar Territory is the administrative center of the Seversky district. It is located in the southwestern part of the region, on the left tributary of the Kuban - the Ubin River. It is 34 kilometers from Krasnodar, 110 from Novorossiysk. The Krasnodar-Novorossiysk highway passes through the village, and there is a railway connection to Krasnodar-Krymsk. The village is unusually beautiful and cozy. The highlight of this area can be considered an abundance of orchards.

History

The territory located between the Przeciz and Sukhabl posts was occupied by units of the Seversky Regiment. From May to June 1863, the Highlanders began to attack the regiment. This could only be prevented by the creation of a post to control the terrain. Dragoons of the regiment from the reserve squadron were engaged in construction. June 22, 1863 the construction of the post was completed, it was named after the builders Seversky. Later, on the site of the abolished post, a village was built, called the Severskoy.





In 1864, the Cossacks of the Azov army were settled and settled here, in the village. They are considered the founders of the village. Historians call the founding date of the Seversky village of Krasnodar Krai May 28, 1864.

During the Great Patriotic War in 1942, the village was occupied by the Nazis. From the very first days of the occupation, enemy troops began the extermination of civilians in Seversky. In fierce battles for the village, Soviet troops lost the training division battalion with a landing, 6 tanks. But, despite the huge losses, our soldiers liberated Severskaya in February 1943.

The population of the Seversky village of the Seversky district of the Krasnodar Territory

According to the data of 1881, in the village of Severskaya there were 278 houses, the population was: women - 687, men - 707 people. Worked 2 forges, 3 shops, there was one apiary. As of 2017, the population is 118,973 people.

The village of Seversky is surrounded by beautiful mountain landscapes, the air here is unusually clean. Migrants from different regions choose a village for permanent residence, due to this the population is growing steadily. Therefore, development is actively ongoing.





The settlement has a developed infrastructure, fully gasified. In the village of Severskaya there are three kindergartens, four secondary schools, an assembly college and a lyceum, a museum, and a market. The following industries are well developed here:

  • gas;
  • oil;
  • machine-building;
  • processing;
  • food;
  • construction.

Agro-industrial production is developed. In the structure of production, industry leads (81%), then trade and food (9%), construction (5%), and agriculture (2%). Business and farming are also developed here. The Cossacks occupy a special place - it is the backbone of the police station. Excellent conditions are created for the development of local folk art. The natural resources of the area include:

  • gas;
  • oil;
  • clay;
  • non-metallic materials;
  • limestone;
  • mineral water;
  • forest resources.

Property of the village

The real estate market in the village is represented by new and old housing stock. You can buy an apartment in a multi-storey building from 1 to 3 rooms in the very center of the village. The price ranges from 550 thousand rubles for a one-room apartment to 3.5 million rubles for a three-room apartment. In the private sector, a huge selection of houses in different price ranges. The price of an old small house can start from 1,400,000 rubles. A solid house in the village of Seversky Krasnodar Territory in good condition can be bought in the range from 2 to 6 million rubles. A luxurious mansion will cost more - its cost will be more than 10 million rubles. The price of land has a wide spread, it depends on the number of acres and location.
