Hotel Don Juan Beach Resort 3 * (Dominican Republic / Santo Domingo): reviews and photos

Resort Don Juan Beach Resort 3 * is popular among those people who want to relax in an unusual, exotic place. Many tourists coming to the Dominican Republic decide to stay here. And there are several reasons for this.





Briefly about the hotel

Don Juan Beach Resort 3 * was built in 2003. This is a resort complex located literally 20-30 minutes drive from Las Americas International Airport. The city in which the hotel is located is known as Boca Chica. It is the municipality of the province of Santa Domingo, founded in 1779.

The hotel consists of several buildings 3-4 floors high. Most rooms have garden views. Of the others, at sea. The hotel has a little more than 220 rooms. Interestingly, this hotel was originally a cozy fishing villa. And now it is a modern resort hotel. Many people come here, because the hotel is located in close proximity to snow-white beaches, surrounded by tropical vegetation.

Leisure

Don Juan Beach Resort 3 * has an outdoor pool, which is open all year round, and a picturesque garden. Here, every evening, animation staff conducts various thematic entertainment shows. There is a separate mini-club for children, so kids who their parents decided to take with them on a trip will not be bored.

Guests have the opportunity to rent equipment for various water sports (canoeing, kayaking, catamaran), try fishing, or simply get acquainted with the underwater world, diving with a mask and snorkel. By the way, this hotel is also known for its excellent diving center Treasure Divers.

For those guests who do not like such a pastime, there is a bike rental service. Many enjoy exploring the local beauty while riding this vehicle around the area.

By the way, the hotel also has a tennis court, a fitness center, as well as a massage room, a jacuzzi, a Turkish bath, a solarium, mini-golf and horse riding. In addition to the above, guests can do aqua aerobics, Spanish classes and dances (they are also held here regularly). In general, guests of Don Juan Beach Resort 3 * will not be bored.
