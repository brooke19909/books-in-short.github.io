Hotel Ozkaymak Marina Hotel 5 * (Turkey, Kemer): traveler reviews

Choosing a place for a long-awaited vacation or annual vacation, many prefer hot countries and swimming in salty healing waters. However, some tourists would like to pay attention to excursion pastime. These two different directions are successfully combined in one of the popular resorts in Turkey - Kemer. In addition to getting a dark tan, here you can expand your horizons and visit a lot of attractions. However, travelers choose this place not only due to a wide range of entertainment. Fresh air, beautiful landscapes, pure nature are what attract thousands of tourists to the colorful Turkish lands.

The five-star Ozkaymak Marina Hotel is an institution that will give a carefree vacation, a good mood and first-class service to each of its visitors. Friendly and helpful staff, varied and satisfying cuisine, as well as a good location, according to vacationers, are the main advantages of this hotel complex.

Location

The convenient location of the Ozkaymak Marina Hotel Kemer 5 * hotel notes in its reviews the majority of tourists. The hotel complex is located in the heart of the resort town of Kemer. Within walking distance are a yacht club, discos, bars and boutiques. The nearest international airport is 55 km away. Overcoming such a distance is not at all tiring after a flight.

Features of accommodation

Pets are not allowed in the apartments of all categories. There are rooms for the disabled, equipped with all necessary facilities. There are adjacent chambers, which allows you to stay on vacation with a large company. Smoking is strictly prohibited throughout. The standard check-in time is 14:00. Room walls must be left before noon. The staff speaks Russian, which makes it easy to resolve issues.

Supply system

An incredible abundance and satiety of dishes is noted in their reviews about Ozkaymak Marina Hotel 5 * by many guests. Five meals a day are organized in the form of a buffet. Each vacationer has the right to independently choose the visually liked masterpieces of local chefs and fill their plate with them in the required quantity. As many guests note, the food here is hearty and amazingly tasty. It's simply impossible to stay hungry. According to a pre-established schedule, meals are held in the main restaurant, whose interior contributes to an increase in appetite and easy communication. White ceilings with spotlights, numerous windows and sconces on the columns fill the hall with light and warm atmosphere.

In addition to this institution, there is a fish restaurant, whose doors are open only for dinner. It is necessary to pre-book a table and observe the dress code. In the daytime, you can enjoy a variety of snacks at the snack bar.

Thirst quenching is possible in one of the four bars located on the beach, by the pool, in the lobby and in the restaurant. There is a diet and children's menu. Fresh juices, foreign drinks and bottled drinks require additional payment.

Aquazone

One of the important leisure activities is swimming and swimming. The latter is incredibly good for health and favorably affects almost all vital systems in the human body. That is why a spacious swimming area attracts travelers to the walls of the Ozkaymak Marina Kemer Hotel 5 * hotel complex (Kemer). There are two outdoor pools. Their area is 600 and 900 square meters. They are not equipped with a heating system and are filled with fresh water. Their surroundings are numerous sun loungers with umbrellas. Regular water aerobics classes conducted by professional instructors support the body in good shape and improve mood.

Another important advantage of the Ozkaymak Marina Hotel 5 * hotel (Kemer) is the presence of a mini water park, which is represented by three bright slopes for children and adults. Skating brings a lot of smiles, joy and positiveness to each guest.

In winter, tourists can use the heated indoor pool.

Beach

Hotel complex Ozkaymak Marina Hotel 5 * (Turkey, Kemer) is 300 meters from its own beach. The roadway lies between them, but there is no underpass. The coastline is 100 meters wide. An artificial bulk beach is sand and gravel. The entrance to the sea is gentle, mostly pebble. The water is amazingly clear and transparent. Snorkeling and scuba diving in the deep spaces are not only exciting, but also in demand among experienced divers and beginners alike.

The coastal strip is dotted with sunbeds with mattresses. For lovers of shady spaces and those who want to hide from the sun's rays, numerous umbrellas are installed. Beach facilities include changing cabins, a beach towel dispenser and showers.

One of the most common types of pastimes is volleyball. Every day, tourists gather on the equipped field to fight for the team championship. Riding a banana and scooters is to the liking of fans of drive and extreme sports.

Children's leisure

Ozkaymak Marina Kemer Hotel 5 * receives a positive plan very often from tourists who come to rest with their children. Here, children are fully responsible for organizing leisure activities. The kids spend most of their free time in the pool. However, they can diversify their leisure time by playing in the children's mini-club, where under the guidance of experienced teachers, kids frolic, draw, play outdoor games and watch cartoons. Numerous slides, swings and merry-go-rounds were built in the fresh air, where the children at the same time spend energy and are charged with new energy for future achievements. Throughout the day, a professional team of cheerful animators amuses the kids.

Babysitting services are also available. Parents can leave in the care of professionals babies of any age. The restaurants have high chairs. A child under 5 years old, without providing him a bed, lives with his parents for free.

Entertainment

The doors of the fitness center are open all day for sports enthusiasts. A well-equipped room with mirrors allows you to maintain the body in good shape, and if necessary, to throw off a couple of extra pounds. This prospect is for vacationers. The whole training process is accompanied by experienced instructors who help in the distribution of loads.

In addition to physical exercises, tourists in their free time enthusiastically compete for the championship in billiards and table tennis. A visit to the sauna and jacuzzi restores strength, heals the body and breathes new life into it. The magic hands of professional massage therapists give true pleasure to every cell of the body. Facial and body skin care procedures are incredibly popular among the female half of vacationers.

Racket rental or a game with a coach on a concrete-coated tennis court is charged extra. The evening ends with an animation program with contests and a disco.
