Attractions and shops

From St. Petersburg one-day tours to Finland provide a unique opportunity to visit the beautiful town of Lapeeenranta. One of the most famous local attractions is the ancient Linnoitus Fortress. It is located on a hill, low walls do not interfere with a beautiful view of Lake Saimaa. There are several interesting churches and museums in the fortress at once, among them are surprisingly beautiful old buildings and the oldest Orthodox church in Finland.

Majurska cafe is also worth a visit - this is a true paradise for lovers of retro interiors. The cozy coffee shop and pastry shop is completely lined with antique armchairs, tables, sofas, vintage interior details are hung on the walls, and a good-natured hostess in an old-fashioned apron and cap welcomes guests. This cafe has very tasty pastries, and the prices are even lower than in St. Petersburg.

In addition, it is worth visiting local shops. Prices in Finland are much lower compared to the cost of similar goods in Russia. Here you can buy clothes, shoes, souvenirs, furniture, household appliances and much more.
