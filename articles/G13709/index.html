"Litvinovo" - a sanatorium in the suburbs. Overview, description, services, treatment features and reviews

When choosing where to improve your health near Moscow, pay attention to the treatment at the Litvinovo sanatorium. The main advantage here is its own source of mineral water. A convenient location of treatment rooms in a residential administrative building will be a favorable factor for people who have problems with the musculoskeletal system, and just the elderly.





Where is located

Departing from Moscow for 80 km, you find yourself in the vicinity of the city of Naro-Fominsk, located on the banks of the Nara River. "Litvinovo" - a sanatorium, which is located on the estate, formerly owned by the princes Shcherbakov. This is a favorite resting place of the intelligentsia and creative people (writers, artists).

Walking in the mixed forest of the sanatorium, enjoying the view of the flowing river from the steep bank and looking at the old linden alley, you feel the joy of life.





Litvinovo is a sanatorium where there is its own apiary, with which up to 1000 kg of honey of two types is collected: flower and linden. Honey treatment improves immunity, stimulates the body's metabolic processes and activates the breakdown of fats.

Sanatorium "Litvinovo" of the Moscow region is a balneoclimatic resort, whose main purpose is to treat with mineral waters and climate. Here patients who have disorders of the musculoskeletal system, diseases of the cardiovascular, nervous and digestive systems can improve their health.

Where do the guests live?

In the two-story building "Litvinovo" (sanatorium) there are 79 rooms with 135 beds. Next, we will consider all categories of apartments.

Single

They are divided into two categories - with and without a balcony. The furnishings of the room include a chair, table, bedside table, fridge, bed, wardrobe and shower. The room has the ability to install an extra bed (folding bed). There are 10 rooms with a balcony and 15 without a balcony.

Double

They are also divided into rooms with and without balconies. The interior is the same and includes two single beds, TV, 2 chairs, refrigerator, wardrobe, 2 nightstands, shower. There is the possibility of installing an extra bed (folding bed). With a balcony of numbers 16, without - 21.





Triple

Two-room suite with 3 beds, 3 bedside tables, 2 TVs, 3 chairs, 2 wardrobes, 2 tables, 2 refrigerators and a shower. There is the possibility of installing additional space (beds). This category has 2 rooms.

Junior Suite

Two-room 2-seater junior suite is presented in 4 variations, each of which necessarily has one 2-double bed, 2 bedside tables, wardrobe, 1 or 2 TVs, chest of drawers, shower. Rooms differ in the presence of upholstered furniture and armchairs. Here you can install 2 extra beds (cot and sofa). There are 7 such numbers.





Suite

One-room 2-seater suite includes a double bed, a chest of drawers, 2 bedside tables, a refrigerator, a TV, 2 armchairs, a table, a wardrobe and a shower cabin. It is possible to place an extra bed (folding bed). These numbers are available in the amount of 2.

The two-room 2-seater suite has 3 variations of furnishings, which consists of a double bed, 1-2 bedside tables, a corner sofa, 1-2 TVs, a display case for dishes, a standard or corner bath. You can install 2 extra beds (cot and sofa). There are 4 rooms.

Two-room double suite with an entrance hall has two configurations:

  • TV, fridge, bedroom set, 2 sofas, showcase for dishes, 2 tables, floor lamp, 2 armchairs, bathroom and balcony;
  • 2 TVs, one 2-bed, 3 tables, 2 chests of drawers, a showcase for dishes, 2 armchairs, a sofa, 2 wardrobes, 5 chairs, a corner bathroom, an entrance hall and a balcony.

How is it treated?

Such a medical base, as here, can not boast of all the sanatoriums of the Moscow region. Litvinovo accepts patients with diseases of the digestive tract, nervous, cardiovascular system, musculoskeletal system.

The health resort provides treatment for its guests with physiotherapy:

  • Electrophoresis, where drugs are delivered through intact skin using electric current.
  • Galvanization - there is a stimulation of blood flow and lymph circulation, an increase in the secret function of the glands, anesthesia when exposed to constant electric current.
  • By interference currents - the effect on peripheral blood circulation, an increase in tissue temperature and activation of metabolic processes in them.
  • Sinusoidal modulated current - the vasomotor center is activated , the arterial flow increases, and the venous, on the contrary, decreases, the tone of the intestines increases, metabolic processes in the liver are activated, edema decreases.
  • Diadynamic current, where such diseases are treated: gynecological, cardiological pathologies, pulmonary (bronchitis and bronchial asthma), diseases of the ENT organs, periodontal disease.
  • Ultrasound - the lymphatic and blood vessels expand, the microcirculation of the blood improves, and the temperature in the tissues rises. Ultrasonic waves can reduce pain and inflammation, relieve itching, and strengthen regenerative processes.
  • Electrosomal - normalizes the higher nervous system, improves blood supply to the cerebral cortex, normalizes blood coagulability, and lowers blood cholesterol.
  • Local darsonvalization - when exposed to high frequency currents on certain parts of the body, the blood vessels dilate, tissue nutrition improves, blood circulation is activated. Facial skin becomes better, wrinkles are prevented, and hair follicles are stimulated.
  • Microwave therapy - it is prescribed for pneumonia, cholecystitis, hepatitis, some diseases of the nervous system, inflammation in the nasopharynx, diseases of the pelvic organs, and eye damage.
  • Decimeter microwave therapy - relieves subacute and acute symptoms of the following diseases: peptic ulcer, cystitis, prostatitis, neuritis, pneumonia, tracheitis, inflammation of the sinuses.
  • UHF-therapy - treats inflammatory processes in the pelvic organs, musculoskeletal system, lungs and stomach.
  • Magnetotherapy - it is used for the following diagnoses: thrombophlebitis, arthritis, allergic and skin rashes, osteoarthritis, severe bone fractures.

Contraindications for treatment

  • The presence of acute infectious diseases in which the isolation period has not yet expired.
  • All blood diseases that are in the acute stage.
  • Any diseases in the acute stage: chronic in the acute stage and with a complication in the form of a purulent process.
  • Any diseases that should be treated in a hospital.
  • Diseases in which patients need specialized treatment and care.
  • Abundant and private blood loss.
  • Tuberculosis at any stage.
  • Late pregnancy.
  • Malignant tumors and neoplasms.
  • Venereological diseases in acute form.
  • Extreme depletion of any origin.
