Oasis Beach Club Alanya - cheap and cheerful or fun and noisy?

Oasis Beach Resort is positioned as a network of low-cost hotels for budget holidays for tourists and travelers. Branch offices are located not only in Turkey, but also in other countries.

But let's talk about a Turkish hotel offering relatively inexpensive tours.

Oasis Beach Club Alanya is a four-star club-style hotel located 12 km from Alanya and 2 km from Konakli. Previously, it belonged to the Majesty network, but was sold in 2011. Previously, the complex served mainly tourists from Germany, but now the main contingent of hotel guests is residents of Russia, Ukraine and Turkey itself.





As the name implies, Oasis Beach Club Alanya HV-1 positions itself as a club hotel. Recall: this means that its territory is closed and access control operates on it. The main feature of the club complex is the safety of living in it - a large guarded area, adapted primarily for families and children, two-three-story bungalows and an all-inclusive mode. Let's look at each of these points separately.

It should be noted right away that the layout of the Oasis Beach Club Alanya is not entirely thought out. A disco and a restaurant with live music, regularly and fairly loudly playing until two in the morning, are located in close surroundings of residential buildings, near the sea. Therefore, those who want to get enough sleep will have to face this problem.

The territory of the complex is very extensive and, unfortunately, not too well-groomed. In addition, tourists note that both room cleaning and linen change often cause complaints.





Everything that is supposed to be provided for children is really provided - animator services, a kids club, a children's disco, a shallow pool and even water slides. Present, of course, for a fee, and all other standard pleasures, such as riding a "banana" and parasail. The hotel's beach is pebble, the entrance to the sea is rocky, so diver's shoes will be very appropriate.

There are no a la card restaurants in the hotel. There is a paid restaurant and a hookah. There is a restaurant and two bars for catering to tourists. The menu is very peculiar - it has a lot of cereals and sweets, but surprisingly few meat and fish dishes. The quality of alcohol in bars is standard for such hotels - at an average level.

If you look at the reviews of the Oasis Beach Club Alanya hotel that guests who have visited it are lagging, you will notice an interesting feature. Oasis Beach Club Alanya causes some surprise among tourists who are counting on a family vacation, accustomed to a certain level of comfort. The one who arrived alone, with a disposition for a fun and noisy pastime, and does not attach much importance to the external attributes of comfort, on the contrary, is completely satisfied with the complex. After all, it has everything you need for relaxation - a Turkish bath with a sauna, a tennis court, a fitness center, and animation with show programs.





An elegant park is located next to the hotel. It is also possible to get an excursion at least for rafting, at least for diving, at least to the water park, at least in Pamukkale and Jerusalem.

Summarizing all of the above, we can conclude that this inexpensive hotel is more suitable for youth recreation, for those who value the presence of the sea, the sun, a disco and noisy fun, rather than a high level of comfort.
