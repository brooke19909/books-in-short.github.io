Angel made of cotton pads in different ways.

Decorate the interior will help a cute angel made of cotton pads. Even a beginner in needlework will cope with its creation.

Method one





What you need to make an angel from a cotton pad with your own hands :

  • Vata.
  • Cotton pad.
  • Threads are white.
  • Rhinestones or sequins, thin ribbon.

Working process:

  1. Roll a small ball out of cotton wool (this is the head of the future angel).
  2. Divide the cotton pad in two. In the center of one of them put a cotton ball, sew it. Bend the disk in half, sew along the perimeter of the fold and tighten the thread. The wings of an angel turned out.
  3. Fold the second half of the cotton pad in half, bend the edges to the center, sew and flip (this body).
  4. Sew the body to the wings.
  5. Decorate the craft. To do this, sew the ribbon on the head, depicting a halo, glue rhinestones on the wings and body.

It turned out a little angel.

The second method, more complicated





An angel made of cotton pads can be a more complicated method. In this, for example, you will have to sew a lot, but due to the small size of the original parts, this will not be easy. Materials:

  • Cotton pads.
  • Threads are white.
  • Vata.
  • Small beads in two colors.
  • Fishing line.
  • Decorative star.

Working process:

  1. Wrap some cotton in a cotton pad to form a ball. Secure it with threads. The head turned out.
  2. On a cotton pad, draw the body of an angel in the form of a bell, divide it into two layers.
  3. Start stitching both layers, at the end fill the body of the angel with a small amount of cotton wool and finish sewing.
  4. Put together two cotton pads, on the top draw a wing, cut it out. Sew each disk along the edge. Sew both wings to the back of the angel.
  5. String about 5-7 beads on a fishing line and fix them in a ring. Sew between the head and body of an angel.
  6. Sew beads on the wings, and a star on the body.

Done!

Third way





An angel made of cotton pads and beads turns out to be very beautiful. You only need the discs themselves, white beads and threads! What do we have to do:

  1. On one cotton pad, draw the body and head of an angel, on the other a figure in size a little smaller than the body, another two wings. Cut out.
  2. Sew along the edge of each detail. When you do this with wings, string beads with each seam. The same thing with the figure, which will be located on the body of an angel.
  3. Sew the bottom of the angel dress with beads, but each seam should be accompanied by five beads. This can be done separately. That is, first just flash the bottom, and then additionally with beads.
  4. Sew beads onto a smaller body.
  5. Put all the parts together. Sew wings on the back of the body, and a figure on the top.

Done!

Souvenir





How to make an angel from cotton pads in the form of a souvenir? Easy! Materials:

  • Cotton pads.
  • Walnut shell.
  • Three wooden beads: one large and two small.
  • Felt-tip pens.
  • Threads.
  • Clay "Moment".

Working process:

  1. On a cotton pad, cut a circle that fits in half the size of the walnut shell. Sew it along the edge and lay it inside. Can be glued for reliability.
  2. On another disk, cut out small wings and glue them on the back of an angel.
  3. Cut a hat on the head of an angel to the size of a large bead. Sew along the edge, put on the head, add a little glue.
  4. Halve the cotton pad. Twist each part into a tube, attach to the craft, if the size fits, stick, if not, then cut it again.
  5. Glue the small beads into the angel's hand.
  6. Felt pens to gently draw a face.

Angel is ready!
