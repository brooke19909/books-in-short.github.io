Reviews for the tablet BQ - 7082G Armor. Specifications

Reviews on the BQ-7082G Armor tablet include this device in a series of entry-level portable computing devices. At the same time, it has high reliability thanks to a special case and a long battery life. This mobile computer also has a number of key features that significantly increase its functionality. It is also worth noting that it has a very affordable price. All these characteristics make its purchase very justified. It is the capabilities of this mobile device that this review is dedicated to.

Equipment. Appointment

Reviews tablet BQ of this model is positioned as a well-equipped device. In the box with the tablet you will find:

  1. The tablet itself.
  2. Charger.
  3. User's manual.
  4. Company card with warranty.

To synchronize with a personal computer, you need to disconnect the USB wire from the power supply and connect it to the computer system unit. The only drawback of the above list is the lack of a starter pack. The manufacturer shifted this issue to the end user. He must choose the tariff plan that best suits his needs.

The first thing that users of this tablet computer pay attention to is the shockproof case. That is, such a device is perfect for those who lead an active lifestyle and travel a lot. In addition, this tablet is equipped with a 4000 mAh high-capacity battery. If you add to this the improved hardware energy efficiency of this mobile device, you can count on 3-4 days of battery life on a single battery charge.

Another important feature of this mobile system is the presence of a USB connector. That is, it can also be used as a device such as PowerBank. This feature of the tablet will be very relevant in field conditions.

Key Specifications

Reviews on the BQ tablet of the model in question indicate a low level of its performance. And this is not surprising. It is based on an outdated model of the central processor - Spreadtrum SC7731C. It includes four energy-efficient cores, which in the highest performance mode can accelerate to 1.3 GHz. The amount of RAM in this mobile device is 1 GB, and the size of the internal drive is 8 GB.

The diagonal of the screen is 7 ". Its resolution is 1024 x 700. The type of matrix used in its production is TN + Film. The image quality on it is good, but still inferior to more expensive screens based on IPS technology.

The increased battery life can boast a tablet BQ Armor. Reviews indicate that it can operate up to four days on a single battery charge.

Owner reviews. Cost

Reviews on the tablet BQ-7082G characterize it as a reliable and energy-efficient device. In addition, it has a very low cost. You can buy such a tablet computer for only 4490 rubles. Given the previously given technical characteristics, such a cost is quite justified.

Conclusion

Almost all reviews of the BQ-7082G tablet characterize it on the positive side. Although it is positioned by the manufacturer as a device for people who travel a lot and often, it can be used not only in such extreme conditions. The only significant limitation in this case is the low performance of the central processor. But, on the other hand, this increases its battery life. Therefore, this tablet is perfect for those users who need an inexpensive, reliable and energy-efficient mobile computer.
