Rest "savages"

If you want to completely move away from civilization and spend your holidays alone with tents, songs around the campfire and a huge starry sky, then your vacation in the Tula region is the best for you. Here, in addition to the corners with grandiose hotels already chosen by tourists, there are absolutely wild places where you can come and enjoy the silence.

We have prepared for you a list of beautiful places in the Tula region. They are ideal for relaxing with tents:

  • The village of Conduk. This settlement is located in the Uzlovsky district and is notable for lakes, which used to be coal mines. Over time, coal mining was suspended, and the landscape took on bizarre shapes. Everyone who first lands on these lakes is amazed at the turquoise water and admires the unusual mountains, reminiscent of the Martian hills cut by the winds.
  • The village of Redridge Mountains. If you have always dreamed of seeing the landscapes of Switzerland, then this place is literally created for you. All local residents have long called it the ‚ÄúTula Switzerland‚Äù and are sure that nowhere else on the planet is there a more bewitching beauty of nature. Here, on the banks of the River Beautiful Swords, you will find not only a measured rest, but also adventures associated with the mystical past of this place. The fact is that in the nineteenth century the village of Krasnogorye was known as Vedmino. And there were good reasons for that. For example, the locals were always surprised by the huge boulders scattered in the hills. In their outlines, the resemblance to the skulls of ancient animals is clearly read, and many legends and ancient legends are associated with the megaliths themselves.
  • The village of Kozje. If you are an adventurer and adore unusual places, then you will like a vacation near the village of Kozie. It is here that the most famous megalith in the Tula region is located. It is a huge boulder weighing twenty tons, located on three smaller stones. It is called "Stone Horse" and always attracts the attention of tourists. However, it is better to campground at some distance from the megalith, which is a strong source of energy. It is believed that every man who sat on it will gain incredible masculine power, and a woman can easily conceive a child. Historians claim that there was once a pagan temple in this place. Therefore, from vacation in these places you can bring just incredible finds.
  • Ishutinsky fortification. This place is located about one hundred eighty kilometers from Tula, but there are always a lot of people here. Unlike the previous options, where you can stay in solitude and completely merge with nature, on the Ishutinsky fortress you will not be able to enjoy peace. Residents of Tula and surrounding villages often come here for a weekend to spend a weekend in the lap of nature near the Beautiful Swords River. She makes several sharp bends in this place, forming real bubbling natural pools resembling a jacuzzi.

Tula Region Hotels

When you are looking for a hotel complex for the next weekend or week, do not forget about its location. Most often, the best hotels are located in a noticeable distance from the city, near a reservoir, and have their own territory. The most comfortable hotel complexes and guest houses of the Tula region can be considered the following:

  • "Captain Club".
  • "Thundering Camping".
  • Inshinka SPA.
  • "Three Bears".

We will tell you more about all the institutions listed above.

"Captain Club"

This hotel complex is located only 56 kilometers from Tula, so you can come here for the weekend and spend time in comfort. "Captain Club" is located within the cottage village of Novikovo, and therefore, getting to this hotel will be quite easy.

Guests are offered several accommodation options:

  • apartments for four with equipped kitchenware and utensils;
  • luxury cottages with a maximum capacity of six people;
  • spacious houses where you can move in a large company (up to ten people).

The hotel complex has a very wide selection of entertainment. In the Tula region, however, almost every recreation center or hotel can please its guests with a developed infrastructure. "Captain Club" offers the following entertainment:

  • playgrounds;
  • mini zoo;
  • gym;
  • pool;
  • tennis court and so on.

Tourists in the hotel complex will obviously not be bored. The cost of living in a family room ranges from 4.5 thousand rubles.





"Thundering Camping"

The club hotel is located in a picturesque location away from the main settlements. It consists of a single two-story building with two types of rooms. The average size of apartments does not exceed 26 square meters.

Guests can use the restaurant, play billiards or celebrate a family celebration in the cozy banquet room. The cost of living starts from 700 rubles.

Inshinka spa

In the village of Inshinka there is a hotel complex, which is well known to all locals. You can relax here inexpensively and comfortably. Guests are offered accommodation in six categories of rooms. The simplest are standard class apartments, followed by suites (classic and superior). The family room consists of two rooms, which can comfortably accommodate at least four people. The most expensive are honeymoon rooms and luxury apartments.

Guests can use the sauna, pool and SPA center. There is a playground for kids. The average cost of living in a room is 2.5 thousand rubles.

"Three Bears"

If you want to relax in the most fabulous place in the Tula region, then come to the cottage village "Velegozh Park". On its territory there is a magnificent guest house "Three Bears", located from Tula at a distance of seventy kilometers.

Those who dream of good fishing in the Tula region should also pay attention to this hotel complex. Here is a magnificent pond with an abundance of fish and a dedicated beach area. In winter, a ski slope is available for outdoor enthusiasts.

Guests of Three Bears are accommodated in cozy cottages, which are divided into categories according to the level of comfort. Each has a kitchen area equipped with modern appliances. Bathrooms have washing machines, and some houses even have their own sauna. It is noteworthy that in each courtyard there is a barbecue with a gazebo, so guests spend most of the evening here.

Vacationers can book a horse ride, ride the Oka or play volleyball at a special site. The cost of rest in "Three Bears" starts from 2.5 thousand rubles.





Recreation facilities in the Tula region

Most tourists prefer to relax in a large company and are looking for suitable places. The following three camp sites are considered the best in the Moscow Region:

  • Park Hotel Golden Sturgeon (Tula Oblast). It is located forty kilometers from Tula, where the Sturgeon River winds with a blue ribbon. Guests can live in the main building or rent cozy cottages equipped with the latest technology. The park hotel is open all year and can provide its guests with a large amount of entertainment. Many guests admit that they consider Golden Sturgeon the best recreation center in the Tula region.
  • "Veles forest". The recreation center is located near the village of Borschevka and has its own pond with an abundance of fish. Usually the first time fishermen come here for the first time, who are attracted by the opportunity to catch up to twenty species of fish. But after everyone necessarily comes here one more time with his family to enjoy a wonderful vacation surrounded by wild forests. It is interesting that at this recreation center you can choose from two possible accommodation options: four comfortable cottages or equipped camping on the shore of the pond. It often hosts festivals, concerts and recreational activities. For kids, even animators work. It's nice that the cost of living starts at 500 rubles.
  • "Brother". In Soviet times, this base was very well known to the Komsomol members who gathered here for various training events. Now "Bratina" is a modern complex with a wide selection of entertainment and comfortable accommodation. For 4 thousand rubles a day, vacationers get the opportunity to live in two-story houses with equipped kitchens. If you wish, you can eat in the restaurant "On the stumps", where shows are held every evening. Guests can take part in organized hunting or fishing, as well as rent various sports equipment.




Where can I relax on the Oka?

The most popular places for tourists are those that offer rest on the Oka. There are quite a lot of such people in the Tula region, but we have selected the most interesting options for you:

  • The estate "Needles". In this amazing place on the banks of the Oka, you can forget about the bustling city life.
