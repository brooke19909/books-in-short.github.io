The meaning of the letters SPQR

The statement had an archaic meaning, since, according to scientists, it came from the time of the founding of Rome. There are many definitions of the acronym SPQR. Almost all of them mean the same thing: the greatness of Rome and its senate. This underlines the fact that citizens of the republic were proud of their political system and therefore made their unspoken symbol SPQR. Ancient Rome, as it developed, conquered many states and made them provinces, thereby emphasizing the greatness of its state and state system.
