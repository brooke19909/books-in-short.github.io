Moscow Region Hotels - Rest

Every modern successful person appreciates an elite vacation, which offers places in the suburbs. The high popularity of these places is due to the high quality of service, as well as its proximity to the capital of Russia. Despite the fact that the rest near Moscow is considered to be elite, ordinary residents of Moscow also appreciated the high level of convenience and effectiveness of the rest near Moscow. In elite sanatoriums and boarding houses you can not only relax well, but also improve your health. And hotels near Moscow are one of the best places for spending weekends, May holidays or short winter vacations.

In the elite suburb of Rublevka, known to everyone, there are luxurious cottage villages that are able to give each of their guests an unforgettable pastime. Prices, by the way, pleasantly surprise. This option is most suitable for families. Recall at least the small river and pine forest, which are located in the neighborhood, and it will become clear - this place is the best solution for spending the weekend. There is everything to make your vacation useful and varied. In the villages there is a developed infrastructure, thanks to which everything you need is always at hand. In addition, you can quickly go to the capital to solve urgent issues or just for shopping.

Hotels near Moscow offer all the opportunities for corporate leisure. Here you can easily organize various events. In cozy elite rooms, top-class conference rooms, excellent banquet rooms and spacious venues for team games and celebrations, you can have a great time, as well as tackle working moments via the Internet. All conditions are created here in order to play collective sports games, as well as engage in team sports. Some hotels in the Moscow Region offer a tennis court, and some even offer a football stadium. Hotels worth discussing in more detail are described below.

In a picturesque and ecologically clean corner of Moscow Region, the Foresta Festival Park Hotel is located. The place for the hotel was chosen in the Chekhov district. The main features include a unique multi-level landscape, an apple orchard, as well as an oak grove - all in order to relax and distract from the bustle of the metropolis. On the territory of the hotel visitors can receive more than one and a half hundred different services, presented by comfortable rooms, cottages, Italian trattoria "San Remo", beer restaurant "Kaiser", sushi bar, pools, SPA, baths, children's rooms, shooting gallery, tennis and many more to others. The only drawback of this hotel is the high cost of the stay and additional services, but can a good vacation cost cheap !?

Foresta Tropicana Hotel is a place offering original room interiors, as well as a unique, one of the widest list of additional services in the Moscow Region, the total number of which exceeds three hundred. The features of this place include the ability to combine high-class relaxation and the services of a modern SPA center. The hotel territory is a wonderful place for diverse relaxation - from noisy entertainment to privacy in silence.

There are also chic hotel complexes near Moscow. One of the most famous is Abramtsevo. Hotel class ****, and its territory takes the place of the former estate of the Trubetskoy dynasty. The uniqueness of the vegetation, represented largely by fir trees, makes it possible to feel the unique aroma and feel complete relaxation. Abramtsevo is an almost perfect place for family and corporate vacations. Here you can either spend a quiet weekend surrounded by family members, or hold seminars, symposia and presentations.



Reviews of hotels near Moscow are almost always full of admiration. No less popular among Muscovites and all residents of the country are the sanatoriums of the Moscow Region. And this is not surprising, because the first and second are always distinguished by excellent service. In sanatoriums for a very long time the most outstanding personalities of our state have been recovering, including political figures of the highest echelon of power, as well as members of their families. In the health resorts of the Moscow Region, you can as soon as possible undergo a full examination of the body, as well as receive an individually designed special recovery program. In the sanatoriums, additional services are offered that allow you to make the Moscow Region vacation diverse, pleasant and exciting. For example, you can use the services of the spa, which achieves complete relaxation. In addition, the sanatoriums have swimming pools, bath complexes, beauty salons, as well as gyms. All this is worthy of attracting the attention of the most attentive to the quality of guest services .

The Moscow Region in a short time received a highly developed infrastructure and turned into an elite place of life and relaxation for many famous politicians and businessmen. At the same time, you should not think that in the suburbs there is no place for a simple person. Hotels near Moscow are characterized by the cost of services, which varies quite a lot, which makes recreation affordable for people with different income levels.
