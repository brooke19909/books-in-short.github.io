A fluffy skirt for the girl. With our own hands we update the wardrobe of the little princess

Do you want your little daughter-daughter to look beautiful and elegant? Then among the things in her wardrobe a fluffy skirt must be present. For a girl with her own hands, every mother can make such a piece of clothing. This article describes two ways to complete this outfit. Following the following instructions, even a beginner in sewing will easily perform elegant fluffy skirts for girls. Photos of such products you see before your own eyes. If you were inspired by such beauty, welcome to the master class.



Skirt-sun: preparing materials and making patterns

For many decades, the cut hem "flare" does not go out of fashion. We offer you to sew a sun skirt for the baby. For work, prepare the following materials:

  • atlas;
  • tulle;
  • elastic;
  • non-woven;
  • slanting inlay;
  • sewing threads.

The product we will perform the so-called "full sun." In other words, the hem will be made of fabric carved in a perfect circle. A pattern of a fluffy skirt for a girl of this kind is presented in the photo. According to size, make a piece of paper folded four times according to this drawing. Next we work with the atlas. Fold it four times, pin the pattern on top with pins and cut it out. This is a blank for the bottom of the skirt. The upper tier will be made of tulle . It fits exactly the same as the previous one.



From the atlas you still need to make a blank for sewing a belt. Cut a strip 12 cm wide and a little longer than the waist circumference of the crumbs. Glue the part with non-woven fabric.

How to sew a fluffy skirt for a girl with her own hands?

Grind the lower edges of both parts with an oblique trim. Fold the belt in half horizontally and sew on the bottom. Next, connect the part into a ring. First, sew on the satin part of the product to the belt, forming folds as necessary. Then sew a tier of tulle. Pull the elastic into the belt. Thus, a fluffy skirt for the girl with her own hands is made.
