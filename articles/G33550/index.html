Hotel "Palma" in Abkhazia: description and reviews

The most popular Caucasian resort of Gagra, which used to be in the USSR, attracts tourists with its clean beaches, beautiful pine groves and warm gentle sea . Constructed in this city, of course, are comfortable hotels. For example, some tourists who decide to relax on the Black Sea in Gagra stay here at the comfortable Hotel Palma. Abkhazia is currently one of the most accessible resorts for Russian tourists. And the main contingent of this hotel is precisely our compatriots.

Location Features

This popular tourist hotel was once built on the very shore of the Black Sea. The distance from her buildings to the beach is only 30 meters. Gagra city center is 5 minutes' drive away. In the immediate vicinity of the complex are located:

  • grocery store;
  • pharmacy;
  • Entertainment Center;
  • bus stop.




Tourists can get to Gagra from Russia both by plane and by train. You can come here by your own car. The nearest airport is located about 45 km from the hotel - in the city of Adler. The border between Abkhazia and Russia runs 10 km from the complex. If desired, tourists who decide to rent rooms at the Palma Hotel can book here, including a transfer from Gagra Train Station or from Adler Airport. But vacationers, of course, will have to pay separately for this service.

What is a hotel like?

The Palma Hotel in Abkhazia in Gagra receives tourists in one rather large three-story building of modern construction. His courtyard, of course, has its own.

From all sides, this comfortable hotel is surrounded by a beautiful forest. The hotel area is also landscaped. According to tourists, the very appearance of the Hotel "Palma" in Abkhazia (and the photo " on the page confirms this) gives visitors a holiday mood. The courtyard of the complex is fenced with a reliable fence. Therefore, strangers cannot get into its territory. Feel free to go for a walk around the yard hotels can be visited by tourists and their children, but nevertheless, many vacationers believe that this hotel is best suited not for families, but for romantically-minded couples.





This hotel operates not only in summer but also in winter. Children are accepted to the hotel from any age. Smoking in hotel rooms is, of course, prohibited. You cannot live in this complex with pets.
