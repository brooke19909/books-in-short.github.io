Bulgaria, Complex Joya Park 4 *: photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Holidays in Bulgaria, Russian tourists choose because of the warm, mild climate, accessibility and proximity. You get used to the local temperature quickly, the cuisine is similar to Russian, and European-style vacations are inexpensive. Joya Park Hotel 4 * located in the heart of the resort area. Here, elderly and young couples who have chosen to rest with their kids will feel great.

A bit about the resort

Golden Sands is located in the national park. Amazingly beautiful resort is chosen by tourists from all over the world for relaxation.

For its part, the Bulgarian government is providing additional funds to preserve the magnificent flora and fauna of the park. It is comfortable and pleasant to relax here, and the Joya Park 4 * hotel is a real gem among other places of residence. The Golden Sands resort even received the Blue Flag at the time - this is an international award worthy of the place where excellent environmental conditions are created. It is not difficult to guess that the main advantage of this part of the coast is the golden fine-grained sand that shelters the beaches.

Territory

Around the hotel Joya Park Complex 4 * there is an amazingly beautiful park where you can stroll in the evenings or in the afternoon hide from the midday heat.

Exotic plants and beautiful flowers grow everywhere. In the center of the park there is an adult pool with a special section where children can swim and frolic. Sun loungers and umbrellas are available upon request for free.


Paid services

In the hotel Joya Park Complex 4 * room service is provided, there is a laundry, dry cleaning and a supermarket. For an additional fee, you can also use the safe at the reception (for EUR 15 per week) - it is much more reliable to store documents and valuables. The hotel offers car rental services: this is a very convenient service for those tourists who are accustomed to visiting historical sights on their own or just love to travel, exploring the surroundings. A shuttle service is available upon request.

The hotel has a conference room for 45-50 people, where modern equipment for presentations, business meetings and conferences is installed. For tourists with cars or hotel guests, car parking is provided (for EUR 5.10 per day), where a video surveillance system works. There is also an ATM. The hotel can pay by credit card.

Number of rooms

The hotel is equipped with 50 standard rooms and 18 apartments (15 single and 3 double). Depending on the type of room, there is a balcony or access to the terrace, from where you can explore the picturesque surroundings and enjoy the sea.

A shower or bath is provided, some have a shower cabin with all necessary accessories. The rooms of the Joya Park 4 * hotel are air-conditioned, have a TV with satellite channels, a seating area. A direct telephone works for communication.
Internet is available (paid service). There is a desk. The rooms are designed for non-smokers. For smoking there are specially equipped places on site.

The difference between the apartments is that they are more spacious, on the floor - parquet or terracotta (in the rooms - tiles). There is a lounge with modern upholstered furniture and equipped kitchenette (electric stove, individual dishes), a minibar and a refrigerator, as well as one or two bedrooms. Housekeeping is provided daily. Bed linen is changed 2 times a week. If you need a bathrobe or need to change a towel, then you should warn the maid about this. Rent bathrobes for a fee. The minibar is filled at the request of visitors. If you are traveling with children from the age of 8 months to 3 years, then a cot will be installed in the room for them (the service is free).

Children are charged. For a baby whose age has not reached 2 years, you need to pay EUR 1.5 per day. Installation of an extra bed will cost 50% of the room price for a child under 12 years of age and up to 80% of the cost if the age exceeds 12 years.

The minimum cost of one-bedroom apartments will cost 1470 rubles per day. For apartments with two bedrooms, vacationers will have to pay 3150 rubles. In case of cancellation of the reservation, the cost is not refunded if the cancellation occurred less than a week before the expected arrival. The price paid for the room is subject to a fine.

Nutrition

Depending on the cost of accommodation, tourists can order several food options: only breakfast, half board (breakfast and dinner) or All Inclusive. The Joya Park 4 * hotel (Bulgaria) also has a restaurant with access to the garden (a heating system has been thought out in case the evening turns out to be cool).

The dishes here are very tasty, European and Bulgarian cuisine predominates . They offer a wide selection of seafood dishes, and even the most demanding visitors will appreciate the assortment of Black Sea delicacies. If you believe the reviews, then breakfasts are monotonous, and this is a little disappointing, because eating fried eggs, sausages and fruits is constantly boring. But for lunch and dinner, the menu is expanded, everything is prepared very tasty. A variety of fruits are always available, as well as watermelons and melons, after lunch you can order ice cream. Local drinks can be consumed immediately after breakfast until 22.00 in unlimited quantities.

There is a bar next to the pool where you can prepare a delicious cocktail upon request. A lobby bar is open around the clock. If you want to try something tasty, you can visit the cafes and bars located nearby. The food there is inexpensive, but nutritious and varied.

Beach vacation

Given that the Joya Park 4 * hotel is located in the center of the amazing Golden Sands resort, visiting the beach is a must for a great vacation. Golden sand covers the coast (hence the name of the area). Sea water is carefully inspected for the content of dangerous animals and fish, so that tourists can swim completely calmly.

Sun beds, umbrellas and mattresses for vacationers are available for a fee. The hotel has its own beach, but it is at some distance, and you need to get to it by transport. This is not very convenient, so most tourists relax on the nearest city beach.

In addition, you can rent motor boats, water parachutes and hang gliders, scooters and water skiing, banana boats - this allows you to diversify your vacation.

Things to do at leisure

If you want to maintain an active lifestyle - look at the gym, visit the bathhouse or sauna, spa. On the territory of the hotel Joya Park 4 * (Bulgaria) there is a special sports ground with artificial lighting, where you can play volleyball and basketball, a tennis court is equipped.

There are separate facilities for playing table tennis. There is a billiard room, you can play darts and futsal. A massage therapist and a cosmetologist work, there is a hairdresser, as well as manicure and pedicure rooms. All listed entertainment and services are ordered in advance at an additional cost.

In the evenings, animated events are organized on site for adults.

What to do with children

Hotel Joya Park 4 * (Bulgaria) is designed for tourists of different categories. If you come with the kids, you will be pleased that the hotel has a children's pool. A corner with a slide and a swing is equipped, where children can have fun and actively spend time. There is no children's animation, but for vacationers of any age, there is a huge water park nearby with many slides, attractions and entertainment that vacationers of the Joya Park 4 * hotel will definitely like. Reviews admiring tourists will leave no doubt that everything is thought out here for a good and eventful leisure. Inside the water park there is an artificial river, waterfalls, the ruins of the ancient city (artificial). Children fall into the wonderful world of adventure and have fun from the heart.

How to diversify your vacation

Of course, being constantly in the hotel Joya Park 4 * (Golden Sands) is not interesting. I would like to explore the surroundings and make the rest complete. Especially for lovers of active pastime, there is a horse base nearby, where you can take a horse ride and even book a couple of interesting individual lessons. Adults can go on a boat trip, because the prestigious yacht club with a marina is very nearby.

The resort is also known for its thermal springs. Modern balneocenters function here, where they offer anti-stress programs, mud therapy, aromatherapy and apitherapy sessions (treatment with honey and other beekeeping products).
