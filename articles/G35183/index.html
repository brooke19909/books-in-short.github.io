The capital of Bashkortostan - the millionaire city of Ufa - is located on the banks of the Belaya River and stretches fifty kilometers. This city is diverse and diverse. And in the composition of its population there are probably all nationalities. And they all coexist peacefully under the Bashkir sun.





A little about Ufa

Many thousands of years ago this area was already inhabited. Even before the first streets of the city appeared. In different places, ancient settlements were discovered, which are evidence of the life of people here in antiquity. During the reign of Ivan the Terrible, the first wooden buildings began to appear. After some time, a city spread out on this area, in which the fate of the Bashkir and Russian peoples was intricately intertwined. The center and foundation of this city was the Kremlin. At present, the thirty-three-meter Friendship Monument recalls it.

Population

The city is constantly growing and developing. Several thousand people are added per year. Accordingly, there is a need for the construction of more and more micro-districts, high-rise buildings and infrastructure. Moreover, growth occurs naturally, and not at the expense of visitors. The boundaries of the city are constantly expanding, and already the outskirts are densely populated. To accommodate all residents in comfortable conditions, it is necessary to build many residential complexes. One of these is LCD Iskra (Ufa).
