The easiest option is to make tulips, where a chocolate egg acts as the base of a flower. To make such a composition as in the following illustration, you need to perform the following actions:





  1. Buy the right amount of Kinder.
  2. Prepare the appropriate number of colored napkins.
  3. Take the first egg and wrap it in a napkin so that the apex is approximately in the center.
  4. Trim the excess edges.
  5. Fix the egg with a floral flying napkin on a kebab skewer.
  6. Wrap the stalk to the bottom.
  7. Cut leaves from green cardboard or paper and fasten them on the stem.
  8. Also make all the other tulips.
  9. Combine all the flowers in a bouquet and put in a beautiful vase or wrap with corrugated paper. Decorate the item with a bow.
