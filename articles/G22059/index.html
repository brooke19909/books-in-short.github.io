Pension "Odyssey" (Anapa)

Wondering where to stay while relaxing in Anapa? Odyssey is a wonderful guesthouse with a long history, which is located just 50 meters from its own sandy beach.

brief information





Pension Odyssey (Anapa) first opened its doors in 1961. The last reconstruction was carried out in 2009. On a green area of ​​6 hectares there are several residential and administrative buildings. Various types of entertainment are available to vacationers: film screenings, dances, sports games and much more.

Number of rooms





Guests of the boarding house "Odyssey" (Anapa) are offered to stay in one and two-room rooms of the "standard" category. The apartments are located in two-, three- and four-story residential buildings. Room Types:

  • one-room single, double, triple and quadruple;
  • two-room double.

All rooms have single or double beds, wardrobes, bedside tables, tables and chairs, as well as a refrigerator, TV and fan. Two, three and four-bed one-room apartments have one extra bed, and two-room apartments have two. A bathroom with a shower is in the rooms.

In total, the room fund of the Odyssey boarding house has 426 apartments in six buildings.

Facilities and service





The infrastructure of the recreation center "Odyssey" is:

  • car parking;
  • administrator's left-luggage office;
  • round-the-clock medical center;
  • computer room;
  • club;
  • dance floor;
  • indoor cinema;
  • tour agency;
  • a shop and a fruit stall;
  • clothes row;
  • bike rental, sports and beach equipment;
  • children's playground and room;
  • sports grounds.

Guests of the boarding house can enjoy such types of entertainment as playing volleyball, basketball, football, badminton, tennis or table tennis, discos, participating in recreational cultural events, watching movies in the cinema, sightseeing tours.

Meals are provided in the spacious dining room. The price includes breakfast, lunch and dinner. The menu always has dishes of meat, fish, fresh vegetables and fruits, dairy products. Those wishing to taste barbecue pork or sturgeon, soft drinks, beer and Anapa wines should visit the summer cafe, which is also located in the boarding house.

A ten minute walk from the buildings is its own sandy beach. It has a rental of beach equipment, sun loungers, awnings, locker rooms, and also has a lot of entertainment: slides, water skiing, banana, boat rides, boat trips, kitesurfing, windsurfing and so on.

Treatment takes place on the basis of neighboring boarding houses. For an additional fee, vacationers can attend hydromassage sessions, therapeutic baths, galvanic mud, inhalation, classical or manual massage, as well as undergo physiotherapy, ECG and ENT room.

Reviews for the boarding house "Odyssey" (Anapa)





For more than half a century of its existence, a huge number of people have visited the boarding house. Some came here with their children, others preferred to relax alone. Someone just wanted to sunbathe on the beach or have fun, someone wanted to get better. For all the time I received the boarding house “Odyssey” (Anapa) reviews not only positive but also negative. Here are the main points that you should know for those who are going to visit the recreation center in the near future:

  • In addition to the beautiful and well-groomed territory, the pension has its own landscaped beach. There is also a rescue post.
  • The dining room serves delicious dishes, large portions. On the tables are always vases with fresh fruit.
  • Rooms are cleaned, as well as garbage and towels are changed only upon request.
  • For self-cleaning, appropriate equipment is provided on the floor.
  • A large playground is equipped for children, where there are slides, swings, a Swedish wall and a real ship.
  • Movies for adults and cartoons for children are shown every day at the cinema.
  • Around the boarding house there are a large number of shops, cafes and various entertainments.
  • In some cases, old furniture has not yet been replaced with new furniture.
  • Cold water is supplied around the clock, and hot water is only a few times a day.
  • Free beach chairs and tents are available on the beach.
  • There are no air conditioners in the rooms, instead of them there are fans. But still stuffy enough.
  • Wireless internet is available only at the reception.
  • An electric kettle, as well as an ironing board with an iron, are designed for one floor.
  • Pets are not allowed to be settled.
  • The territory of the boarding house is fenced, security is at the entrance.

The location of the recreation center

Pension Odyssey is located at Anapa, Pionersky Prospect, 267. It is 8 km from the local airport, and only 5 km from the railway station. The guesthouse can be reached by taxi or public transport. Bus No. 113 leaves from the airport, and No. 100 and No. 129 departs from the railway station. Shuttle buses No. 114, 134, 128 and 124 go from the bus station. You need to get off at the Odyssey Pension stop.
