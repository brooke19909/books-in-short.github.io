Hotel Flisvos Beach 2 * (Greece, Crete): photo and reviews of tourists

Hotel Flisvos Beach Resort is an ideal place for those who dream of a holiday in Greece, but do not want to spend huge sums of money on accommodation. Here for the minimum cost you will find all the amenities needed by a modern tourist. This budget hotel is located on the seashore, at the foot of the mountains. All this makes the atmosphere around the hotel incredibly picturesque and simply unforgettable.

Hotel Flisvos Beach Resort

Holidays in Greece is a dream of many tourists, but sometimes the cost of living becomes too high on the way to its implementation. The exit may be Hotel Flisvos Beach, which is quite economical, but at the same time comfortable hotel. Here you will have the opportunity to use wireless Internet throughout the entire territory for free. In addition, you will be provided with services such as currency exchange and vehicle rental.

The quality of rest depends on how comfortable the rooms are. Speaking about the Hotel Flisvos Beach hotel, we can say the following: they correspond to all the basic needs of tourists. Since the summer in Greece is very hot, you will certainly be pleased with the availability of air conditioning. You can also store the products you bought in a large, modern refrigerator. The best part is the incredibly beautiful sea view that opens from the windows of the apartments.

Flisvos Beach Hotel can be proud of its restaurant. For a relatively low price, you can try the most delicious dishes of traditional Greek cuisine. In addition, you can enjoy all the delights of an international menu. Every morning at this restaurant you can enjoy a delicious and hearty breakfast. One of the main advantages of the restaurant can be considered that it is located right on the sea coast.





Resort Location

Flisvos Beach Hotel is located in the north of the Greek island of Crete, in close proximity to a huge and clean public beach. This place can be called the center of tourist life, because there are many shops, restaurants, as well as nightclubs and other entertainment facilities. It is also worth noting that the hotel is located relatively close to Heraklion International Airport - only 25 km. If you want to get to the hotel by bus, the journey will take about an hour and a half.





Flisvos Beach Hotel 2 *: guest rooms

The hotel is a bright two-story building which houses 79 guest apartments. All rooms of the Flisvos Beach Hotel 2 * hotel can be divided into several main categories:

  • Studio apartments are quite popular due to their large area, as well as a good layout. This is a spacious room, which is divided into several zones. At the entrance to the room you find yourself in the hallway with a large mirror and a wardrobe, but looking at the decorative partition, you will see a small bedroom. It has two one and a half or one large bed. There is also a pair of soft chairs and a coffee table, which serve as an impromptu living room.
  • Family apartments are a large two-room suite. One of the bedrooms is equipped with a large double bed, a wardrobe, as well as a small soft sofa with a floor lamp for reading. The second room is a little smaller and is equipped with two single beds. It is worth noting that both bedrooms have their own bathrooms, which is especially convenient if there are many people in the room.
  • Luxury apartments are the best in this hotel. There are two spacious rooms, one of which is considered a living room, and the second - a bedroom. The bathroom has a large bathtub, as well as a huge illuminated mirror to make it easier for guests to shave or do makeup.

It is worth noting that each of the rooms of the Flisvos Beach Hotel Hersonissos 2 * resort has a balcony, from which you can admire the beautiful sea and mountain landscapes. They also have a device for drying bath towels and bathing suits.





The amenities provided in the apartment

Each of the rooms at Flisvos Beach Hotel Hersonissos features the following amenities:

  • the refrigerator, which is provided for storing the products you bought, can also act as a minibar if you pay for this service upon check-in;
  • the bathroom has all the necessary set of bath accessories and cosmetics, as well as an electric hairdryer to create a beautiful styling;
  • a small balcony with a clothes dryer, as well as a couple of plastic chairs, so you have the opportunity to enjoy the picturesque scenery;
  • a large modern TV connected to a satellite dish gives you the opportunity to watch dozens of entertainment channels, among which are also Russian-speaking;
  • a small kitchenette with a microwave, electric kettle, and a set of dishes and cutlery;
  • landline telephone serves as a means of communication with the reception, and by prior arrangement can also be connected to an international line;
  • all rooms have free Wi-Fi;
  • thanks to an individual air conditioner, you can adjust the air temperature in the apartment to suit your needs.




Resort Infrastructure

Flisvos Beach Hotel offers its guests to use the following infrastructure facilities located within the territory of:

  • a large area for guests to relax, which includes a blooming tropical garden, adult and children's pools, as well as a spacious terrace with a large number of sun loungers and umbrellas to protect from the sun;
  • a large private parking area with 24-hour security, where guests can leave their vehicles absolutely free;
  • a luxurious restaurant on the coast where you can try traditional Greek dishes made with the freshest products;
  • the bar offers a huge selection of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, as well as the opportunity to watch sports matches;
  • the hotel has all the necessary amenities that will make recreation for people with disabilities as comfortable as possible;
  • there is a reception desk in the lobby, the staff of which will come to your aid at any time of the day to solve problems related to hotel accommodation;
  • a safe is provided for guests, where you can leave your valuables for an additional fee;
  • equipment and inventory for playing table tennis.




Service and facilities provided to guests

At Flisvos Beach Hotel Rethymno, guests can enjoy the following amenities:

  • a continental breakfast is served every morning in the hotel restaurant;
  • There is an own vehicle rental service where you can rent a car or a bike;
  • at the reception you can exchange currency at a fairly favorable rate;
  • at the tour desk you can book a study tour to explore the island's natural and cultural attractions;
  • for an additional fee, reception staff will purchase tickets for you at a convenient date for you in any direction.




Useful information about the hotel

It is worth noting the presence of some features and rules that govern accommodation at the Flisvos Beach Hotel 2 * resort (Crete):

  • Check-in at the hotel is possible not earlier than after two in the afternoon (if there are available rooms, the registration procedure can be accelerated for an additional fee);
  • It is necessary to solve all organizational issues related to the departure before noon (for each hour that you spent in the room over the allotted time, additional payment will be charged);
  • It is strictly forbidden to bring pets to the hotel;
  • extra beds are not provided to guests, due to the fact that the layout does not allow this;
  • for children under 12 years of age discounts are given both on accommodation in rooms and on meals in the hotel restaurant;
  • when booking a room, you must make an advance payment by transferring funds to the hotel account (otherwise the order will be canceled);
  • upon registration, you will have to leave a certain amount of money as collateral;
  • An indispensable condition that must be observed at the time of check-in is the provision of a valid photo ID.

  • compliance of the cost of living with the quality of the services provided, as well as the living conditions;
  • new bedding that smells good thanks to rinse aid;
  • just an ideal location - it is not only close to the sea, but also to all the main facilities of the resort infrastructure;
  • thanks to the work of the owners of the hotel, a friendly, family atmosphere reigns in it, which is carefully supported by professional staff;
  • the balcony offers incredibly picturesque views, and since it is available in all rooms, each of the guests has the opportunity to enjoy this beauty;
  • convenient layout of rooms, which involves dividing a very large space into separate functional areas;

Negative hotel reviews

Despite the huge number of positive comments, there were some flaws and negative aspects at the Flisvos Beach Hotel. Reviews about this place indicate the presence of the following negative aspects:

  • the beds themselves are quite new, but the mattresses are old and squeezed, and therefore it is simply impossible to fully rest on them - in the morning the back and neck are very sore;
  • very old washed towels, which, in addition to gray color, are also characterized by scuffs and even holes (nevertheless, if you leave a tip to the maid, they will replace the towels with new ones);
  • the doors in the rooms creak and slam very loudly, because of which it is simply impossible to relax peacefully while in the apartment;
  • the air conditioner is very old, because of which it practically does not cool the air, but it is incredibly noisy;
  • the rooms on the ground floor are very damp, and therefore mold appears on the walls, which also emits a not very pleasant smell;
  • the hotel does not accept plastic credit cards, and therefore you will have to carry a large enough amount of cash to pay for accommodation and other services;
  • eating in a restaurant is quite expensive, and therefore it is better to order dishes in neighboring establishments;
  • the apartments have only two outlets, and therefore you have to choose which equipment you are going to turn on (in addition, one of the outlets is non-standard, which is why it will not be possible to connect a charger to it);
  • the food in the restaurant, although tasty, but rather monotonous, and therefore very quickly bored;
  • for the use of sun loungers and umbrellas a rather high fee is charged - as much as 5 euros (but on other beaches this service is even more expensive, and therefore vacationers simply have no choice);
  • the cleaning is mediocre enough, but if you want to improve the situation, then by all means leave a tip to the maid;
  • some rooms do not have a hairdryer;
  • due to the fact that the hotel is very old, plumbing works poorly here, as well as very shabby furniture.

General impression of the resort

A huge number of comments from travelers allows us to add some impression about the Flisvos Beach Hotel 3 *. Reviews especially note that this hotel is a kind of economy option. Nevertheless, the low cost of living does not adversely affect the quality of the services provided. Perhaps the only drawback is outdated furniture, as well as plumbing. And in the rooms it would not hurt to make redecorating.

One of the main advantages of this institution can be considered its location. Quite rarely you can find a budget hotel located on the first coastline. However, the hotel does not have a private beach, and guests are invited to use the public beach. If you need a sun lounger or umbrella to protect from the sun, then they will be provided to you at an additional cost. The area in which the hotel is located is the center of tourist life, where travelers can find many shops, restaurants and entertainment venues. There is also a good traffic intersection.

It is worth noting that this hotel is an ideal option for active tourists who spend a minimum of time at the hotel, and therefore do not want to overpay for a room. For relatively little money, you can get a cozy overnight stay, as well as hearty and tasty food. The rest of the time is recommended to be spent swimming in the sea and visiting local attractions. Otherwise, you will certainly be struck by the outdated atmosphere of the hotel, as well as insufficient quality cleaning.
