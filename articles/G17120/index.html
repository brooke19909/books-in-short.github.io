Number of rooms

Blue Sea Beach Resort consists of a four-story main building and two additional buildings. In total, the hotel can offer its guests 338 rooms of various categories:

  1. Single room - single apartments with a single or double bed, from the balcony of which overlook the sea or mountains. The room is equipped with a bathroom with all bath amenities, Internet, hairdryer, air conditioning, telephone, TV, safe, refrigerator and desk.
  2. Double Room - a double apartment with two single or one double bed. The room is equipped with a bathroom with all bath amenities, Internet, hairdryer, air conditioning, telephone, TV, safe, refrigerator and desk.
  3. Triple room - triple apartments with standard equipment.

The hotel does not accept guests with animals.

A little bit about the resort ...

As we already mentioned, Blue Sea Beach Resort (Rhodes, Faliraki) is located on the island of Rhodes, which is washed by two seas - the Mediterranean and Aegean and stores the ruins and a monument of bygone times. The island is quite small, but for tourists it opens with a whole country with ancient ruins, quiet streets of old cities and loud rhythms of night clubs.



The most lively resort of Rhodes is Faliraki - a place on the Mediterranean coast with beautiful golden beaches, cozy hotels, coniferous forests and a very vibrant nightlife.

How to get to the hotel?

Blue Sea Beach Resort Hotel arranges a shuttle service for its guests. But, in principle, tourists can independently reach their destination. This is not at all difficult, thanks to the good location of the complex. Faliraki is ten kilometers from the airport. There are several ways to get to the town. For example, you can hire a taxi, which on average will cost you up to 75 euros. And you can use public transport. A ticket to Rhodes will cost only two euros. Then, at East Side Bus Station, you need to buy a ticket for the shuttle bus to Filiraki for two euros. Transport runs every twenty minutes, the entire road will take no more than half an hour. The airport and the capital of Rhodes have many car rental locations. The average cost of one day rental is 25-30 euros. You can order a car while at home, and upon arrival she will be waiting for you at the airport. By car you can visit all corners of the island, but you should remember that local expensive ones are very narrow and winding, only an experienced driver can master them.

Landmarks of Faliraki

Faliraki has become popular for its beautiful beaches, which are considered the best in all of Rhodes. Almost all of them are awarded with blue flags, which are awarded only to environmentally friendly places.

Blue Sea Beach Resort is located near Faliraki Beach, which is about four kilometers long. There is a very clean and transparent sea. There are a lot of water activities on the beach: parasailing, catamarans, bananas, windsurfing, jet skis.

Faliraki is the center of all kinds of entertainment throughout the island. Therefore, vacationers from all over Rhodes come here. The city has the famous water park, which is considered one of the largest not only in Greece but also in Europe. Every year it begins to work at the very beginning of May. On the territory of 100,000 sq. M. There are many attractions for adults and children, as well as cafes, restaurants, shops.

In addition, there are several Luna parks in the city, and Fantasy, located in the coastal area, is considered the best. Faliraki has a developed nightlife, in the evening clubs begin to work, entertaining guests until the morning.



Rhodes is rich in sights, relaxing on the island, you should pay attention to visiting the most interesting places such as the Lindos Acropolis, Rhodes Fortress, Mandraki Harbor, the Palace of the Grand Masters, Butterfly Valley, Suleiman Mosque, Rodini Park and much more.
