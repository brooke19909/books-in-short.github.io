Arriving in Hainan airport opens the gate to earthly paradise

Located on a relatively small island in the South China Sea, Hainan is the southernmost and smallest province of the People 's Republic of China. The area of ​​the island is about 35 thousand km 2 , the area of ​​its water area is about 2 million km 2 .



The name of the island is translated as "south of the sea." Hainan received this name due to the fact that it is located south of the southernmost mainland point of China. This island is called the "island of coconuts." Another unofficial name for Hainan is “the island of longevity,” since there are about twenty thousand people who are over 90 years old.

Famous for its superb beaches and clear waters, Hainan is the most popular resort on a global scale. Hainan can only be reached by sea or by air, which is why the airport on Hainan, literally, is a window to the world. There are two airports here: Haikou Meilan in the north and Sanya Phoenix in the south.

Hainan Province: Haikou Meilan Airport

Opening in 1999, Haikou Meilan has become the largest international airport and the largest on the island. It is one of the busiest airports in China. Busy airways connect Haikou Meilan with Hong Kong, Malaysia, and Japan. It is designed to service up to 9.3 million passengers a year. Arriving in Hainan, Haikou Meilan Airport is simply amazing in its view. Stylized as the dwelling of local residents, the terminal building is surrounded by a beautiful tropical garden. At the airport there are many restaurants offering their visitors traditional Chinese dishes. The air traffic control tower is considered the highest in Asia and reaches a height of 70 m.

The nearby town of Sanya can be reached by high-speed train from Meilan Train Station , which can be reached directly from the airport. The center of Haikou is easily accessible by road (bus or taxi).



Hainan Province: Sanya Felix Airport

Only slightly inferior to Haikou Meilan in size, Sanya Felix International Airport is located amid picturesque local nature 15 km north-west of the resort town of Sanya. It was opened in 1994, it serves 168 scheduled flights, 130 of which are domestic, 35 - international and 3 more - regional. The second largest airport on Hainan Island, however, takes 17th place in terms of throughput among the airports of China. Properly constructed and comfortable building accommodates more than thirty stores, as well as about a dozen food outlets. You can get from the airport to the city by bus or taxi.



Arriving in Hainan, Russians and residents of several other countries, a visa can be issued immediately upon arrival. What is the name of the airport in Hainan, where can this be done? A visa can be issued at any of the airports, provided that the passenger flew to the island without change in mainland China for a period of no more than 15 days. For Russian citizens who have made their tour through a travel agency collaborating with a Chinese tour operator, a visa-free regime is 21 days (for a group of more than two people). At the same time, passport control is carried out at Sanya Airport according to visa-free lists previously submitted by the host.
