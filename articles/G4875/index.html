Park-hotel "Sova", Ryazan: description, address, photo, reviews of tourists

One of the best places to stay in Ryazan is the Sova Park Hotel, reviews of which are mostly positive. This is due to the high level of service at the hotel, as well as the increased level of attention to each guest - vacationers, as you know, love it very much.

So, why is Sova Park Hotel in Ryazan so widespread?



Description

This place is a huge hotel, which was built and decorated using original design ideas. This provided a beautiful exterior and interior view of every corner of the room in which the hotel is located. In each of its zones, a distinctive style is clearly traced, which helps to ensure a distinctive atmosphere.

The interior of the hotel successfully combines luxury and simplicity, interwoven with homeliness, as well as a stylish interior and comfortable conditions for each guest. Business meetings are often organized here, families come here to relax, and hotel rooms are very popular among young couples who want to retire in a more romantic setting.

Park Hotel Sova in Ryazan is a complex that harmoniously combines ten comfortable rooms on its territory, a spa center that includes Russian and Finnish-Turkish baths, as well as a restaurant where guests can eat delicious dishes.

Very convenient is the location of the park-hotel "Owl" (Ryazan) - it is located right in the city.

Suite

The hotel provides only one deluxe room, but it occupies two levels of the park hotel. This room consists of two rooms - a living room and a bedroom with a huge double bed. This room is a little over 43 square meters. m. As for the cost of settlement in such a room, it is equal to 6,000 rubles if the lodger is settled alone, and 7,000 if there are two lodgers. This price includes additional services - breakfast in the room and buffet.

The interior of the suite perfectly combines shades of white and light coffee colors. There are a lot of details made of light wood, and the windows have lightweight textiles that give visual lightness to the overall picture of the interior. A spiral wooden staircase leads to the second floor of the room.

From the furniture in the bedroom there is a huge king-size bed, next to which are two bedside tables. there is also a large plasma TV, telephone, two half chairs, a table and a safe for storing valuables. Near the bedroom there is a shower room - it has a bath kit and a hairdryer. To control the air temperature in the bedroom, air conditioning is installed.

In the living room of the suite room of the Sova Park-Hotel (Ryazan) there is a nightstand with a sofa, a TV, a table, a minibar. There is also a toilet room, all the necessary toiletries are offered.



Junior Suite Room

Also in the park-hotel "Sova" in Ryazan there is one room of the highest category (but not luxury), which is called the junior suite. It is located on the same tier, but is a double apartment. It also consists of a living room and bedroom.

The bedroom of this room is also equipped with a huge double bed with side tables. There is also a separate wardrobe for guests' personal belongings. In the living room of this room you can sit on a sofa, semi-armchairs or chairs, in front of the TV. For greater comfort, guests in the junior suite, as well as in the deluxe room, have a minibar, a safe and a telephone.

Separately located shower room, equipped with necessary bath accessories and a hairdryer. The room is also equipped with air conditioning.

A restaurant

Owls guests have the opportunity to have breakfast, lunch and dinner in the good restaurant, which is located in the complex. Here is a very extensive menu, which offers dishes of author's cuisine, an assortment of exclusive desserts and a chic winery of expensive wines.



In this restaurant you can also organize any event, whether it is a birthday party, corporate party, family dinner or a business meeting with business partners.

The restaurant has light brick walls and parquet floors in light wood. Large restaurant windows are covered with heavy chocolate-colored curtains. The guests of the restaurant can sit in comfortable soft chairs with high gray backs at tables covered with white tablecloths.



Russian sauna

The park-hotel "Sova" (Ryazan) has a real spa center, where guests relax. One part of it is a real Russian bathhouse.

On the territory of the Russian bath there is a large indoor pool in which guests of the spa area can cool after visiting the steam room.

The bathhouse itself is made in all the best traditions of the Russian people, plus everything, it is still supplemented with some modern elements that provide the comfort of guests staying in it.

In addition to the steam room, the bathhouse has a separate relaxation area, which is equipped with a large wooden table and wooden chairs, which can accommodate a company of 14 people. Here, on one of the walls, there is a large plasma TV for watching television.

The bathhouse has an outdoor relaxation area, which is located near the entrance, in the courtyard, among the greenery of grass and shrubs. Here guests can sit in soft armchairs, near a wicker table.

In this bath, the services of a professional bathhouse attendant can be provided, which will select the optimum temperature for soaring, prepare brooms and conduct bath procedures. The Russian bath also offers massage, peeling and body wraps.



Finnish-Turkish bath

The Finnish-Turkish bath is another part of the spa complex located in the Sova Park Hotel. This area is divided into a Finnish bath and a Turkish steam hammam. All guests who visit the Finnish-Turkish bath are given the opportunity to have a good rest, improve their health and relax. This part of the spa area also has a pool.

Guests who relax in the Finnish-Turkish bath can enjoy a cup of green tea from the herbs in a comfortable relaxation area.

About 6 people each - so much can accommodate a hammam and a Finnish bath located on the territory of the Sova Park Hotel in Ryazan.
