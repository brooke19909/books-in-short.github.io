Cinema opening

In March 2006, the Karo Film network invited Petersburgers to the building of the Continent shopping and entertainment complex, located in the Kirovsky district of the Northern capital. Here, on the fourth floor, the opening of the Karo Film Continent movie theater on 99 Stachek was held. This is a seven-day modern multicomplex.

Location

Cinema "Karo Film Continent" on Stachek has a favorable location. It is located at the intersection of several highways of the metropolis, which are classified as major. In addition, the Kirovsky district, where the Continent shopping center is located, is one of the most dynamically developing in the city. All this, as well as the modern equipment of the cinema, allowed him to enter the list of the best multiplexes in St. Petersburg and gain great popularity among the audience. Nearby is the Komendantsky Prospekt metro station.

Design

The style inherent to the Karo Film multicomplexes is easily recognizable by the audience. First of all, the design of all cinemas has a tendency to futurism. The same characteristic can be given to the Continent complex on Stachek. In the lobby of the cinema, the viewer finds himself in a bright world of shiny surfaces and rich colors.



The interior of the Continent multicomplex on Stachek is simply mesmerizing. It is made of glass with mirror reflection and resembles a ship. In addition, each cinema hall has its own color scheme. It is in harmony with the installed seats for the audience.

The Continent Cinema on Stachek offers viewers a choice of more than just a session. The visitor has the right to get into a certain room where the film will be shown.

The price of watching movies at the Continent Cinema varies. Ticket prices range from one hundred to seven hundred and fifty rubles. Certain factors influence the price.
