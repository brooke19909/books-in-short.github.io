Where to go on a beach holiday in February

Long gone are the days when vacation in February was associated with us only with ski resorts. Thanks to the rapidly developing tourist infrastructure of different countries, you can not send bathing suits, a mask and fins far to the mezzanine, but go to the sea at any time of the year. Moreover, a beach holiday in February 2014 is forecasted in the same price ranges as it was this year. That is, no jumps in the cost of tours are foreseen, and you can plan your vacation and spend on it without much loss.



For starters, consider the most budgetary beach vacation in February. Inexpensive you can relax in Egypt. All political disturbances occur only in the capital and major cities of the country. The resorts of the Red Sea are quiet and calm. However, the weather may disappoint you. There is almost no rain at this time of year, but seawater, to put it mildly, is not warm - only 20 degrees, and in places where the bottom is shallow - + 15. But the sun warms already in summer, do not forget the protective cream. Of the two renowned resorts of Egypt, we recommend choosing Sharm El Sheikh: the mountains of the Sinai Peninsula create protection against cold winds.



Similar comfortable weather conditions await you in the United Arab Emirates. There, a beach holiday in February means warmer air than in Egypt (+ 25), but also a colder sea. However, the service in the UAE is much higher than in the North African country. Each hotel has heated pools, water parks operate at the resorts. If you have already chosen the UAE, then the warmest resort in this country is Fujairah. Nights still make it possible to forget that winter is in the yard. Take a warm sweater or windbreaker with you.

A beach holiday in February will be truly cloudless in exotic countries. In regions close to the equator, there are only two seasons - dry and wet. Throughout this climate zone, February is a very good month. The sea is calm, the sky is clear, the temperature of both air and water is approaching + 28. But this also means that the last month of winter here is the peak of the season. And this fact affects prices and the occupancy of beaches. Southeast Asia (Thailand, Cambodia, South Vietnam, the islands of Taiwan and Hainan) is interesting because in February the Chinese New Year is rapidly and colorfully celebrated here .



A beach holiday in February in Europe is also possible. These are the Canary Islands. Although they belong administratively to Europe, they lie quite close to the sultry African coast. Lush vegetation, mild climate, service at the highest level - your vacation on the islands will be ideal. This Christian land in February celebrates the beginning of Lent with great fanfare. Thousands of tourists come to admire the carnival annually.

The most luxurious beach vacation in February awaits VIP clients in the Maldives and Seychelles, in the southern part of the Indian state of Goa, in the Bahamas and in French Polynesia. Secluded bungalows on stilts at sea, the turquoise lagoon of the atolls, spa and boutique hotels will make your honeymoon unforgettable. It is good for those who are looking for romance, tranquility and unity with nature. You will also find excellent facilities in Kenya, Sri Lanka, Cuba, the Dominican Republic, Mexico and Brazil. There, beach vacations can be combined with a rich excursion program.
