LCD "Ministerial", Kiev: description, reviews

Kiev is a metropolis, historical and cultural capital of Ukraine. A city with narrow streets, lots of attractions and hospitable residents. Kiev residents have long been in need of new residential neighborhoods, modern complexes with comfortable living conditions. Every resident of the metropolis wants at least at home to feel as comfortable, relaxed as possible, enjoy the clean air and have a great time in the company of relatives and friends. LCD "Ministerial" - a vivid representative of modern projects worthy of attention. Do you want to know how comfortable and thoughtful the complex turned out? Then we offer to evaluate the project with us from all sides.

Construction Features

Modern technologies and innovations used in construction - this is the difference between the project of the LCD "Ministerial". Responses of residents confirm that the developer applied a conceptually new approach to construction and thought through everything to the smallest detail. The European level of decoration of apartments will satisfy even the most demanding and demanding customers, and will also save a significant part of the budget for subsequent decorative decoration.





All buildings are built using monolithic-brick technology - the best engineering solution for modern apartment buildings. It allows you to build strong, reliable and durable buildings, ideal for life. For the construction of walls used ceramic brick of a traditional red hue. With its help, it was possible to give an external aesthetics to each case. The system of ventilated facades provides high heat and sound insulation properties. All entrances are equipped with high-speed silent elevators, intercoms, fire alarms.

Location

For the construction of the residential complex "Ministerial", the Obolonsky district of Kiev was chosen - a full-fledged residential quarter with developed infrastructure that meets all the requirements of modern residents. The microdistrict is literally surrounded on all sides by a forest belt, within walking distance from new buildings are parks, squares and ponds, where you can have a great time in the company of relatives and friends. The area is one of the most prestigious in Kiev, so it definitely deserves special attention. A developed transport network, new road junctions allow all residents of the complex to move around the city without the slightest problem, choosing public transport or a personal car.





Infrastructure

Recall that the LCD "Ministerial" is located in one of the most comfortable areas of Kiev. Within walking distance are all the necessary shops and supermarkets, modern fitness centers, sports clubs, restaurants and cafes, bank branches, beauty salons and service organizations are functioning.

In the microdistrict, social infrastructure is also well developed: educational and medical institutions, post offices, libraries, and much more. Residents note that they easily placed children in kindergarten and school, they are completely satisfied with the level of medical care.

The construction of the residential complex "Ministerial" has a positive effect on the development of the entire microdistrict: the developer does not spare funds for the improvement of the adjacent territory, landscaping, the construction of modern playgrounds and sports grounds that meet all safety requirements.

Apartments, layouts

The developer made a lot of efforts in order to use every square meter of area. Here you definitely will not see once typical apartments with a disgusting distribution of space. Comfortable kitchens, spacious isolated rooms of optimal size, niches for built-in furniture, dressing rooms - a modern look at residential real estate from an experienced and reliable developer located on the construction market for more than a dozen years. Residents note that not all Kiev new buildings have well-thought-out layouts and competent distribution of living space.
