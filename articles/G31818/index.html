Baku is a city of contrasts

On the Absheron peninsula in the coastal part of the Caspian Sea is the legendary ancient Baku - a city built by many peoples. The capital of Azerbaijan is a large cultural, industrial and economic center in the Caucasus. The history of the city holds many interesting events, which are largely reflected in historical monuments. As a result of the collapse of the Soviet Union, Azerbaijan was going through a difficult time, which led to a difficult economic situation. Recently, the population of Baku has increased significantly due to the improvement of the condition of the city as a whole. Today it is considered a powerful industrial and business center.

Currently, Baku is a city that Azerbaijanis can truly be proud of. The most interesting architectural monuments are concentrated in Icherisheher (Inner City). Tourists will be interested in traveling around the area, visiting the Minaret of Sinik-Kala, the Shirvanshahs Palace, the octagonal mausoleum. In the outer city (Bayyrsheher) the best conditions of modern civilization are created. There are many skyscrapers, museums, shops, boutiques, entertainment centers, shopping centers. There are also theaters and other cultural institutions.

Modern Baku is a city known for its wonderful resorts with mud baths, which are popular with both tourists and locals. The Absheron Peninsula is famous for a large number of ancient fortresses, all kinds of towers, the historical past of which carries away in the distant days of the ninth - thirteenth century AD. For travelers, mini-tours of ancient places are provided by tour desks. The majestic Icheri Sheher, called the fortress, is located in the heart of the city, on the seashore. This architectural complex is similar in structure to a city built in medieval times: it is surrounded by powerful walls of the fortress. Of particular interest to tourists are the Archaeological and Ethnographic Museums.

The ancient city of Baku (photo) is a city of romance, which has a good rest. Its promenade is of particular interest to vacationers. The best conditions for tourists are created here. Excellent service, picturesque nature attract travelers to Baku - the city is interesting and original. On the coast there are many arbors, fountains, which against the background of the dense crowns of trees and shrubs produce an indelible vivid impression.
In terms of tourism, Baku is a beautiful city, the climatic conditions of which make it possible to receive wellness treatments. Excellent ecology, a large number of vegetation, well-maintained seaside beaches, sunbathing - these factors make the city one of the best resorts. The waters of the Caspian Sea are useful both from the point of view of recovery, recreation, and because of the ability to provide the city and country with fish. In summer, the air temperature is from +30 degrees, in winter the temperature column does not fall below +5 degrees. The favorable temperature here makes you feel especially comfortable.
