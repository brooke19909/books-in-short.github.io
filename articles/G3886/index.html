Where to go to have a rest in the winter? Where is warm, colorful and comfortable?

Quickly pack your bags and ski straight into the mountains! This is just what you need to do in the cold season. We will focus on what you want to do in the cold season: bask in the sun, splash around in the sea and enjoy the colors of the unknown, inhale the smell of an unfamiliar and eat exotic dishes under palm trees. Where to go to have a rest in the winter? Where is warm, sunny and joyful? Consider the options.

Sri Lanka, Merisa

Already imagine two kilometers of pristine nature and the whitish-golden sands of a colorful bay? This is exactly the place where it is warm to relax in the winter. Stunning sunsets and sunrises, a pleasant climate and the safety of beaches - all this is about the island of Sri Lanka. Swimming in the waters of one of the best beaches in the world, do not forget to visit the lion rock Sigiriya.

Goa, Palolem



Goa is a tiny state of India. If you want to not only sunbathe and swim, but also have fun, go to the village of Vanator or Arambol. And if your desire is complete relaxation, then go to Palolem - the best beach of Goa. This place is very cozy, small, the entire coast is strewn with palm trees. With one contemplation of a photo of these places, the question of where to go in winter to relax can be resolved by itself.

Thailand, Pranang



Stunning cliffs, white sand and crystal clear water - all this is Pranang, a true paradise for lovers of marvelous natural landscapes and climbers. This beach is also known as Lost Land. In addition to a stunning beach holiday, you will visit a real ancient volcano and local women who, on their way to Pranang beach right in the boat, will prepare for you a delicious local pad Thai dish.

Mexico, Las Almandas



Where to go to have a rest in the winter? Where is warm and truly romantic? We present you Las Almandas. This is where you can feel the real charm of solitude. A deserted and insanely beautiful beach, the whole ocean is only at your disposal. Just 10 years ago there was a jungle, and now it is one of the best beaches in the world, where thousands of tourists with cameras do not run. The hotel complex can accommodate only 30 guests, its territory is 1,500 acres.

Cuba, Varadero



And who canceled luxury? This place has repeatedly won first places in the ratings of the best beaches in the world. It is here that stars spend their holidays, who always know where to go to relax in winter, where it is warm and sunny. The Cote d'Azur, the perfectly blue sea, the sun, white sand and a developed tourist complex with entertainment and hotels - that's all for you! Arriving in these places and enjoying the beach, the sun and local beauties, do not forget to visit the Ambrosio Caves to see the cave paintings of the ancient Indians with your own eyes. Also, head over to the Al Capone Museum and, of course, have fun at the Cuban disco. Local life is in full swing every day! Now you know where to go to relax in the winter, where it is warm and colorful, and we wish you the most exciting journeys!
