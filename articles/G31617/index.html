Grinder, or angle grinder

An angle grinder is often called a grinder in honor of Bulgaria - the country where it was developed by SPARKY and put into production. Currently, this power tool is widely used in everyday life and in production, because users have found an incredible amount of diverse work for it.

An angle grinder can not only cut metal with abrasive disks and stones of various origins with diamond wheels, but also grind / polish different surfaces with the use of card brushes, abrasives, polishing coats. Now almost no one uses other tools for these purposes, for example, a hacksaw or gas welding, and why, when the angle grinder copes with all kinds of work perfectly.

All branded grinders are produced according to the most stringent quality standards, they pass test tests in production before replenishing the manufacturer's line.

All companies, and there are many of them today, produce their own line of grinding machines of various sizes, layouts and capacities. The class and technical capabilities, as well as the cost of angle grinders, depend on the quality of components and materials, as well as the technical know-how applied in the construction of a particular model, especially in the configuration of the most critical components.
