Unfading beauty: weaving flowers from beads



These lovely bouquets will become a pleasant unfading gift for your loved ones.

Field bouquet

Let's get acquainted with the simplest circular technique. Weaving flowers with beads for beginners usually occurs after acquaintance with this simple pattern. Making such buds is quick and easy, and the result will surely please you. So, let's make a simple bunch of wildflowers. We will need beads of blue and white shades, as well as a few large yellow beads for the middle. Here is the sequence of work steps:

- Type a large amount of beads onto the weaving wire.



- Roll the first ten pieces into a ring and twist the wire to make the first loop.

- Wrap the resulting workpiece with another round of beads and lock it again.

- Repeat the same action a third time. You have one petal ready.

- Make all five petals in a similar way, collecting them on one wire.

- In the middle, insert another bead of a contrasting color and a larger one. Weaving flowers from beads in this way will become your favorite hobby, and you can get not only such buds, but also, for example, roses or other similar ones.

Elegant necklace



Often this is a rather long and tedious task - weaving with beads. Flowers for beginners can be collected in an elegant necklace. It is much faster and easier to do. Just look at this beauty of wire and crystal beads! A schoolboy will cope with this, and as a result, both a necklace and a bracelet can be made using a similar technique. The main thing is to choose high-quality material in pleasant, matching tones.

Gerbera weave





If you have never done this hobby, then weaving flowers from beads should start with the above methods. To gerberas should proceed a little later. To do this, you will need several types of beads in different colors, a round mesh with a diameter of 3 cm, a rod, fishing line, wire and a thin needle. If you've never worked with a grid, then this is much simpler than it sounds. This will be the base of the flower. On it, we first embroider the middle. Let it be black beads, arranged in a circle of 5 pieces in diameter. To make the flower voluminous, you need to lay it in several rows - 4 or 5. And then tie the resulting cylinder with beads of a different color. Try to do it randomly, leaving columns or semicircles. So you give the flower a natural velvety. When you have tied the net, you should get a fluffy sun with a black center. Now proceed to the petals. They should be oblong with sharp corners. You can collect them in the technique indicated above. Such weaving of flowers from beads is used very often. It remains only to attach the petals with wire to the grid. Everything, the flower is ready. Collected buds can and should be on a rod, which can also be tied with beads for beauty and even greater effect.



Conclusion

If you think this hobby will not bring you any pleasure, then you probably have never tried it. This meditative lesson is suitable for a person of any age. And the finished work can be an elegant hat decoration, decorative kitchen panel, table decor and so on. Weaving of flowers from beads can also be applied in nature. You can decorate with these products a bag, necklace, card, finally. Dance your imagination, and she will have a place to roam!
