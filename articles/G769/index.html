Hotel "Tukan" (metro station "Ilyich Square"): description, reviews

Moscow is a huge metropolis, where many tourists and nonresident citizens come daily. Very often, people look for a good place in the capital for leisure and overnight, and some just need to find a hotel that can host them for a few hours. An excellent solution for this would be the Tukan Hotel. Here you can sleep well and relax from a long trip or spend a romantic evening. We suggest that you familiarize yourself with the services and rooms of this institution in more detail in the article below.

Description of the complex

Hotel "Tukan" ("Ilyich Square") is a cozy and well-groomed mini-hotel located within walking distance from the center of Moscow. The institution invites all guests of the Russian capital to settle in unique rooms and take advantage of quality services. First of all, hotel customers are attracted by its location, as well as reasonable prices and the possibility of hourly booking.

Location coordinates

The institution is located in a quiet courtyard, located in the central part of the city. The exact address of the Tukan Hotel (Ilyich Square): Moscow Central District, Vekovaya Street, building 20A, building number 3.

Nearest subway stations: Ilyich Square, Rimskaya, Taganskaya, Kurskaya, Baumanskaya. Not far from the hotel is the railway station "Moscow-Commodity-Kurskaya", Paveletsky and Kursky stations.

Briefly about the rooms

The mini-hotel near the metro station "Ilyich Square" - "Tukan" has 25 comfortable rooms of various price categories. Each room is a luxurious and elegant room, which can be rented both for a day and for one hour. All hotel rooms have everything you need for a comfortable stay:

  • mini bar;
  • air conditioning;
  • balcony;
  • TV;
  • telephone;
  • safe;
  • kettle;
  • modern and beautiful furniture;
  • shower;
  • hair dryer;
  • toiletries

Services

Hotel "Tukan" ("Ilyich Square") provides its guests with high-class service and a variety of services.

Among the main advantages:

  • sauna;
  • Internet in the lobby;
  • Internet in the rooms;
  • free parking;
  • 24-hour reception;
  • food delivery;
  • dry cleaning;
  • transfer (for a fee).

Opinion of visitors

Judging by the reviews, the Tukan Hotel on Ilyich Square is a great place to relax. Many hotel guests note that the staff is friendly and friendly, who never asks unnecessary questions and, if necessary, ensures complete confidentiality. The hotel's rooms are chic, each room is clean and comfortable, has an individual, original design.

Visitors also leave good reviews about the on-site bar. There is a good selection of alcoholic drinks, delicious snacks, breakfast can be ordered. Room service is also available. All dishes are delicious, but the prices are a bit overpriced. Another important advantage of the hotel is its excellent soundproofing in the rooms.

Among the shortcomings of the hotel, some customers note:

  • High prices, although they are fully consistent with the services provided.
  • Bad smell in some rooms.
  • Furniture in individual rooms requires replacement.
