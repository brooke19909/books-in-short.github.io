We make a house of natural materials with our own hands. Crafts for home, kindergarten and school

Crafts made from natural materials are made by children almost all year round. As a rule, exhibitions of such products are periodically held in all educational institutions. Moms and dads think about how to make something new and original. We devote this article to such a product as a house made of natural materials. Do-it-yourself crafts of such a plan is very interesting. Of course, the kids themselves will not be able to do this, but with your help they will completely cope with this task. To help all parents and children, we show the newest and most interesting versions of the DIY houses do-it-yourself crafts, photos of these products. Watch and take notes.



You can make a house of natural materials with your own hands (crafts from vegetables) from a round pumpkin. To do this, cut three holes in it with a knife: two will serve as windows, and the second as a door. Clean the seeds from the vegetable. Cover the top of the product with a roof made of sorghum (material for making brooms) or pine branches. From dry sticks, grill into window openings. You can decorate them with a curtain of leaves. On the third opening, make a door from the bark of a tree. In such a house you can settle men from acorns and chestnuts.

Dry branches

A house made of natural material (confirmation of this photo) can be made of a wide variety of materials. From the following description, you can learn how to perform a similar craft from dry tree branches.

Prepare the following materials for work:

  • plasticine;
  • even dry branches;
  • cardboard;
  • thin wire;
  • dried leaves.

From plasticine, make the foundation in the form of a square platform. Insert close to each other twigs of 8-10 centimeters long, forming walls in the house. On one of them leave a doorway. When the walls of the building are ready, at their corners fasten four sticks with wire and connect them together from above, making a blank for the roof. Cover it with foliage on top. Doors made of cardboard. In such a house, you can safely settle three piglets from a fairy tale. In this dwelling no Wolf is not afraid of them.
