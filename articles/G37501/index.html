Odintsovo, LCD "Central": description, reviews

Odintsovo is a small town near Moscow with developed infrastructure. All those who look after an apartment for themselves in this city are primarily attracted by the availability of real estate. Indeed, at present, the city is being actively built up; new quarters and microdistricts with excellent living conditions are appearing here. The developed transport network allows you to reach the capital in any way that is most comfortable and convenient without unnecessary difficulties. If you are considering exactly Odintsovo for your stay, the Tsentralny residential complex will be a good option. Our task is, within the framework of this material, to evaluate the conditions that the developer offers to potential residents, as well as to review the feedback of those who have already managed to purchase apartments in the new complex.





about the project

Comfort class apartments in a new residential neighborhood west of Moscow, in the very center of Odintsovo - Tsentralny residential complex. The feedback from the first customers only confirms that the project deserves special attention. Two residential buildings, based on monolithic brick technology, all the necessary infrastructure facilities are what the developer will offer all potential buyers.

The project is being handed over in two phases, the first house has already been commissioned and occupied, the second building is planned to be commissioned in the 4th quarter of 2017. Work is carried out clearly on time in compliance with technology and sequence.

Location

Microdistrict "Central" (Odintsovo), reviews of which are left by all buyers, is located only 8 km from the Moscow Ring Road on a site that, in addition to two residential buildings, will accommodate all the missing infrastructure.





The developer is positioning his project as a new residential neighborhood in the city center. In fact, the first residents emphasize that the microdistrict has only an indirect relation to the city center. This is still the northern part of the city, almost the outskirts of residential areas - this must be taken into account in the process of choosing an apartment. And this district of the city is hardly prestigious. Many residents call the remoteness from the city center in this case an advantage: there are no harmful industries, nearby there are enough green areas, parks and squares for walking and relaxing.

Transport accessibility

A well-developed transport network, excellent road junctions are one of the reasons why many people choose Odintsovo for their lives. LCD "Central" from the builder is located within walking distance of public transport. By public transport you can get to the metro station "Victory Park", "Youth". About two kilometers from the new buildings is the Odintsovo railway station, which can be reached with the help of numerous buses and minibuses. But, perhaps, the main advantage of the complex is its proximity to the exit to the understudy of the Minsk highway. In this case, we are talking about a high-speed toll highway, opened in 2013. By personal transport, the travel time to Moscow is 15-20 minutes.





Infrastructure

In addition to two residential buildings, the development plan provides for the construction of a school for 810 places, a modern kindergarten. In the near future, a modern clinic, pharmacy, several modern sports grounds, even a stadium will be built in the area.

The first floors of the buildings are reserved for commercial facilities. Ground parking is for 171 cars. Landscaping of the local area is represented by walking areas, bike paths, as well as modern playgrounds and sports grounds. All residents can relax and enjoy the pleasant silence and coolness in the forest park zone, located within walking distance from the project.

Flats

The variety of planning decisions is what all residents of Odintsovo, LCD "Central" will receive. According to the residents of the first commissioned house, the rooms are rather big, the kitchens are spacious and bright, the hallway has a place for a full wardrobe.





Finished housing for rent with pre-finishing, which provides for the installation of high-quality glazing, including balconies, a modern heating system with high-quality radiators, plumbing. You can buy an apartment not only for cash, but also with the help of a profitable mortgage program. Apartments in ZhK Tsentralny (Odintsovo) are designed for different segments of the population, here are various options for the number of rooms and area, among which everyone will find the best.

To summarize

ZhK Tsentralny (Odintsovo) is a project of a modern residential complex, an entire microdistrict, where excellent living conditions for various segments of the population are created. New buildings are distant from the city center, but are being built by a reliable and experienced developer - InvestStroyRegion. At the moment, an apartment in the second stage building can be purchased on favorable terms. Do not miss the unique chance to get your own housing in the new district of Odintsovo.
