Hotel Golden Tulip Khatt Springs Resort SPA 4 * (United Arab Emirates, Ras Al Khaimah): traveler reviews

Cheap holidays in a 4-star Emirate hotel - is it possible? Quite. If you carefully choose a hotel. For example, Golden Tulip Khatt Springs Resort SPA offers guests fairly modest prices for accommodation. But there are still often discounts that make the cost half the price. However, in this case it is important to choose the moment that is most suitable for the reservation. In the meantime, you can talk about the advantages that made this hotel very popular.





general information

The hotel complex, built in the traditional Arabic style, was opened ten years ago, in 2006. The last restoration was carried out in 2010, so everything inside is beautiful and modern.

And there is Golden Tulip Khatt Springs Resort SPA in Ras Al Khaimah. This emirate is located in the northern part of the UAE. This region is of particular interest to tourists. It is here that tours are organized that are focused not only on beach vacations, but also on acquaintance with cultural attractions. After all, it is here that the ancient city of Julfar with watchtowers and forts is located.

The hotel is urban, it is located at the foot of the majestic Mount Hajar. It takes about 30 minutes to go to the sea, but a free bus belonging to the hotel takes everyone there. By the way, even from the hotel there are routes to the most popular shopping centers in Ras al-Khaimah. Dubai Airport is about 50 minutes away.

What is in the hotel?

On the territory of the Golden Tulip Khatt Springs Resort SPA Wi-Fi is provided, but for 60 dirhams per day (about 1000 rubles at the current rate). There is free parking near the hotel. In the lobby, guests can see the 24-hour reception, currency exchange, a tour desk (ticket sales are also available) and luggage storage. Concierge services are also available.

The hotel also has a laundry with dry cleaning, where they still clean shoes, iron clothes and can even compress trousers. There are conditions for business travelers - one conference room (with a capacity for 60-70 people), a meeting room and a business center. There are also several shops on the territory (among them souvenir and food) and car rental.

In addition to free trips to the beach and the shopping center, the hotel provides a shuttle service to / from the airport, VIP services, non-smoking rooms, apartments for tourists with disabilities, as well as food, drinks, packed lunches and fresh newspapers.

I am glad that the staff speaks nine different languages. To be more precise, he speaks Arabic, Azerbaijani, German, English, French, Filipino, Hindi, Urdu and even Russian.





Leisure

There are several ways to have fun at Golden Tulip Khatt Springs Resort SPA. It features a year-round outdoor pool and a sun terrace. There are also hot springs, which are known as onsen. An indoor pool is also available, as is a modern gym. By the way, while adults have fun, their children can play on the playground. Baby sitting will be organized - professional educators work here, who can be hired for an additional fee.

The Golden Tulip Khatt Springs Resort SPA 4 * hotel, the photo of which is provided above, has its own SPA center with a thermal pool, a sauna, an ice grotto, and another 14 treatment and massage rooms. You can also do yoga or meditation here. Ayurvedic treatments are also very popular here. However, a full list of services offered by the SPA center is available upon arrival.

The hot springs

This is the main advantage of the Golden Tulip Khatt Springs Resort SPA. The reviews left by people who rested here confirm this fact.

A separate complex was erected above the hot springs, to which you need to go down the stairs with a canopy from the sun. Or along the ring road. Polite staff greets in the building, offering to sign up in a customer book. There you need to indicate the number of the room in which the guests live, the number of people and time. Then they give out towels and offer treatments.

The complex has been operating since 7 am, and at this time there are no visitors. So if you want silence and absolute solitude, you can come directly to the opening.

The room is divided into female and male zones. The pool is located under a canopy, outside, and steps lead to it. And around - a picturesque area with flowering vegetation and palm trees, where you can walk. The water in the bath is about 40-45 degrees. On the contrary, you can see the clock, so you can monitor the time. After all, everyone knows - in order for this procedure to be beneficial, you need to start from 5 minutes.
