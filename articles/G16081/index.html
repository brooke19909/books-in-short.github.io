Useful iPhone Applications: An Overview of the Most Popular Programs

There are more and more applications that Apple offers for the smartphone, and choosing the most interesting and best from them is quite difficult. Therefore, it is worthwhile to review some of them - those that are most popular.





Speaking of which useful iPhone apps exist, Pocket should be noted. This program allows you to save interesting information in the form of articles or videos, and subsequently not only get acquainted with it, but also edit as you wish. The application is simple and convenient. You can add your favorite article to Favorites or share it with friends in the social. networks. The only drawback is that the utility does not have a Russian-language version, however, without this, understanding the program is quite simple.

Naming useful applications for the iPhone, it should be noted Speedometer. This program shows how fast you are moving, receiving this information using a GPS sensor. This application is free.





The program AirDisplay is useful to those who have a shortage of working space on the monitor. The application costs a little more than 300 rubles, but before you purchase it, you should consider whether you really need it. The utility allows you to use the device as an additional monitor. It is convenient to display playlists from a music player, some tools from programs such as Photoshop, Lightroom, etc.

It is impossible to name the most useful iPhone applications. After all, they are all individual and their choice, first of all, depends on the preferences of the gadget owner. For example, travelers may be interested in LINGOPAL 44. This program is not just a translator, but a universal phrasebook that supports audio in more than forty languages. The phrases in it are divided into categories, so it‚Äôs easy to find the right one. The application has a Russian-language interface and fairly simple settings.





In addition, Yandex.Navigator can come in handy on the road. Google has a similar program. Such useful applications for iPhone 4 will help not only on long journeys, but also in their native area, as traffic jams are shown here, routes are laid, and the approximate travel time is also calculated. A feature of this program is the presence of voice search, which is not available for all such utilities.

When naming useful iPhone apps designed for schoolchildren and students, mention should be made of MyScript Calculator. This is not an ordinary calculator, as it may seem. The program interface resembles a notebook sheet. The only things here are the up, down, and clear buttons. On the sheet, the expression is written in handwritten text, and then the application itself will put an equal sign and give the result. This program greatly facilitates the life of students who are not "friends" with mathematics.

Useful iPhone applications are used in various fields and were originally designed so that owners can use their gadget to the maximum. Having chosen the necessary program, you can be sure that it will have a convenient interface. Useful iPhone applications are divided into free and those that need to be bought. Therefore, you just need to decide what exactly you expect from your smartphone, and then download and install utilities.
