How to find out TIN debts

A modern and very convenient service allows you to find out TIN debts without a personal trip to the tax service. It provides an opportunity to get all the necessary information on existing debts to individuals and legal entities. The Internet service provides access to tax debt data in all spheres of human life. Debts to transport, land, property funds and all other organizations are taken into account.



You can quickly find out tax tax arrears according to the following scheme. First you need to go to the state website, which is called nalog.ru. The nuance of this operation will be the need to agree that the visitor will have to provide their personal data. Among the indicated information will be the name, region of residence and TIN. The program itself will process the data and provide complete and up-to-date information.

The personal account gives access to information about all the vehicles of the payer who wants to know the TIN debts, about his real estate and all tax amounts. You can see not only current debts, but also the amounts paid, accrued fines and the presence of overpayments for such payments.



If an individual is registered on the site, he will need a registration card. To get this document, it is enough to personally contact the nearest inspection of the Federal Tax Service of the Russian Federation. You will need to take with you the original or a clear copy of the TIN certificate, as well as any identification card, for example, a passport or a pension certificate.

If we are talking about tax arrears of various individual entrepreneurs or LLCs, then when registering on the site you must specify "Individual Entrepreneur". From your personal account it will be possible to freely control all current settlements with the state budget, conduct reconciliation acts, and the latest payment deadlines will be accurately known. You can also find out TIN arrears at the regular tax office, however, patience for standing in line and documents to confirm the identity of the payer will be required.



Interested only in transport fines?

To get acquainted with the current amount of tax on a car, you can also use the official state website of the Federal Tax Service of the Russian Federation or visit the government portal "State Services". Finding out TIN arrears is a great idea, as currently every vehicle owner must pay a tax, which is calculated depending on the maximum engine power of the car. It is better to take care of paying all fines and penalties in advance, since any delay is paid separately and at a higher tariff.
