A hut on chicken legs

If you ask any child where Baba Yaga lives, the answer will be the same for everyone - in the hut on chicken legs. Even a simple house made of branches is difficult to make, but here it still has to stand on two legs. How to do this? Let's try to explain in detail now.

How to make Baba Yaga's house?

In order for the structure to hold tightly on two pillars, you need to pick up either thick saw cuts of branches, or take two wooden spools of thread. The second option is preferable, since they have a base that extends downward, which will securely hold the house.

If you still use branch cuts, then you need to strengthen them with plasticine on the basis of the craft, making claws spread out in different directions. They will give stability to the structure.

The house itself is made from a flat foundation. It can be plywood or a piece of very durable cardboard. The ceiling above is the same. Between these two planes even branches of any tree are glued. You can fasten them with wire or tie a rope together.

Also, for strength, the walls of the house sit on a layer of plasticine, which is attached around the entire perimeter of the house on the inside and on the floor of the house and on the ceiling.

The roof can be made of cardboard by gluing a pyramid. On top of it, put a layer of autumn yellow leaves on the glue. Such a composition can be put up for the contest as an autumn crafts by Baba Yaga.
