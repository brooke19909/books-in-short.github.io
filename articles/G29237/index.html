Openwork crochet - the best hat in the summer

It's no secret that women of all ages love to fashion, and hats occupy a significant place in their wardrobe. These are hats, hats, and berets, and many other little things. But not everyone can afford such a variety due to limited financial opportunities. Then crochet comes to the rescue. It is these beret models that are very popular in the summer. They are not so expensive, and they can be connected in a short period of time. Also, they are easily suited to dresses of different styles for women of any age categories.



The most common in the summer is openwork crochet. It is it that allows the head not to get warm in the bright and scorching sun, and also protects well from ultraviolet rays, which, as you know, are the main cause of hair burnout and their further loss.

If you use crochet, the summer beret will turn out to be very light and well ventilated, which is undoubtedly appreciated at high temperatures. The executed openwork will emphasize your individuality and the whole image in an original way. A bright color scheme will refresh you and give a summer mood.

To create a romantic image, craftswomen decorate hats with beads, rhinestones, beads and feathers. Also, the openwork crochet can be made from sectionally dyed yarn, which will make it suitable for most of the things from your wardrobe.

What you need to know before knitting



Openwork crochet is made only of one hundred percent cotton. The thickness of the thread will depend on the model you have chosen for knitting. You will also need a centimeter tape, scissors, a hook corresponding to the thickness of the thread, a pattern and finishing material (beads, feathers, etc.)

To correctly and quickly tie a summer crochet, the scheme should be clear and fairly simple. If you do not have enough experience, then do not take up knitting complex patterns. To get started, choose something simpler, and then it will be possible to proceed to more complex models. However, all berets are quite easy to perform, differ only in the complexity of the finish.



You also need to consider that the resulting product may be slightly smaller or slightly larger than the estimated size. This is primarily affected by the thickness of the thread and the scheme of work. If you have chosen a pattern for knitting for babies, then, naturally, the resulting crochet openwork beret will be much smaller than your head. The same thing awaits you if you select a thin cotton thread. A big beret will turn out with an incorrectly selected hook. To avoid such problems, before knitting, read the relevant literature.

How to complete the last rows of knitting

It happens that the already connected thing does not suit you a little because of the mistakes made during the work, and it is a pity to dismiss. There are several tricks used in such cases. If your takes a little too big, then when doing the final rows, you can knit several loops together. But it is worth doing it evenly, not tightening the product much. If your beret turned out to be narrow, then wet it well and pull it on a template corresponding to the size of your head. And in this form, dry. Beret will acquire the required size.
