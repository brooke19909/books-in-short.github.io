Hotel Asdem Park Hotel 4 * (Kemer): photos and reviews of tourists, location, description

Kemer, being a relatively small resort town, despite its young age, is already known among Russians for its beautiful beaches and the gentle sea. Translated from Turkish, its name sounds like a "belt." In addition, the resort is famous for its inexpensive hotels with a well-developed infrastructure.

Reasons for the popularity of Kemer

Excellent weather and a warm sea, an unforgettable smell of real Turkish coffee, the surrounding mountains and coniferous forests - all this attracts tourists here. In April, a sailing regatta takes place in Kemer, so avid sailors come to the city. The high season starts here from the end of May and lasts until the beginning of November. The temperature in Kemer is set to moderately hot, without rain.

People who prefer a beach holiday come here. At the resort you can see excellent pebble and sand bathing areas. Some hotels have their own beaches, on which vacationers are offered a lot of entertainment: parachuting, banana, scooters, excursions on boats and yachts in the open sea and so on.





Those who prefer to combine relaxation with sightseeing can visit the ruins of the ancient city. Another amazing place in the vicinity of Kemer is Yanartash - Tierra del Fuego, where small clots of gas escaping from the ground, when in contact with air, ignite. The sight, judging by the reviews of tourists, is bewitching, especially in the dark. At the foot of Yanartash is the temple of Hephaestus destroyed by time .

In the west of the resort there is an open-air ethnographic museum. Here, tourists learn everything about the life of nomadic peoples, get acquainted with ancient crafts and national dishes of a distant era.



Kemer is surrounded by the highest mountains of southern Turkey. Their height reaches 2365 meters above sea level. Tourists can climb to the top with the newly built Olympus cable car - the longest in Europe and the second largest in the world.

Kemer Hotels

Many residents of the city understand Russian well. On the streets of Kemer, you can even find signs in our native language. Turks are unobtrusive people, they behave quite correctly, always trying to come to the rescue. Kemer is a very clean resort, it has many monuments and fountains, flower beds with beautiful flowers are planted everywhere.

Today, many agencies offer trips to this Turkish region. The average cost of tours per person in a three-star hotel can be about fifteen thousand rubles. In high season, prices may vary depending on charter flights. Russians can get to Kemer by plane to Antalya, and then transfer to the selected hotel.





The resort offers accommodation for more than one hundred and seventy hotels of various categories of comfort. Here everyone will be able to find a hotel to their taste, from budget economists to luxurious apartments. Kemer hotels can be divided into two types: the first - for outdoor activities, more suitable for young people, the second - for a relaxing time with children. Almost everyone necessarily has their own entertainment program designed specifically for children. One of them is Asdem Park Hotel. Hotels of different stardom, which are located on the coast itself, attract Russians primarily because of their low prices and rich infrastructure.

general information

The building was built in 1998 on the second line from the sea. The hotel has a rather convenient location - in the center of the resort area, only five hundred meters from the coast and almost a kilometer from the harbor. Bright buildings of the Asdem Park Hotel 4 * welcome guests all year round.

The airport is only fifty kilometers away, which is very convenient for children who do not tolerate a long transfer. There is a bus stop near the entrance. Near the hotel there are many shops and restaurants.

The last major renovation in it was carried out two years ago. The total area on which this city hotel stands is four thousand square meters.

Rules of residence

Excursions

Of course, Turkey, and Kemer in particular, among Russians is associated primarily with relaxing on the beach by the pristine Mediterranean Sea, but still it is worth getting acquainted with the history of the region, visiting its theaters and ancient cities, centuries-old ruins of temples. For example, fifteen kilometers from Kemer, the ancient city of Olympos once flourished. According to legend, it was here that the gods lived. The city is located very high from sea level. Here, tourists can see the classical theater and the remains of baths, laid out in very beautiful mosaics, as well as an old temple.

In addition, not far from Kemer is another ancient city - Kekova, located on the island of the same name. Once there was a major earthquake, as a result, the city almost sank. Therefore, tourists can visit it only by sea. The excursion is carried out on boats, from which you can see the most unusual artifacts. For lovers of underwater exploration, the northern side of this island, on which Roman pirates often stayed, will be interesting. Here, tourists often discover the most unexpected and unusual gizmos.

The cost of the tour depends on which tour operator or agency in which it was purchased. For example, a boat trip with a local guide costs an average of forty-five dollars. At the same time, many vacationers prefer to buy sightseeing tours in an office located in the main building of Asdem Park Hotel 4 * (Kemer).
