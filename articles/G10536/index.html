Avena Resort & Spa Hotel 4 * (Alanya, Turkey): description, service, reviews

The Turkish resort of Alanya enjoys constant popularity among domestic tourists. This southern city offers vacationers not only a quality vacation, but also a lot of entertainment. Of course, here at any time you can rent a comfortable and at the same time not too expensive housing. For example, many tour operators from Russia work in Alanya with the Avena Resort & Spa Hotel 4 *.

Where is the complex located?

Only positive reviews can be read about the location of the hotel. The complex Avena Resort & Spa Hotel 4 * is located 200 meters from the beach in the Obagel area . Its guests can get to the sea in about 5-10 minutes. Alanya city center is approximately 2.5 km from the hotel.





Domestic holidaymakers arrive in Turkey, of course, usually by plane. The final point of flight for tourists who have booked rooms in this hotel, while this may be the airport or Alanya itself, or Antalya. In the first case, you will have to get to the hotel no more than 20-30 minutes. Tourists arriving in Antalya will have to travel to the hotel by bus for a long time. The distance from the airport of this city to Alanya is about 130 km. That is, the road will take at least 2.5 hours.

The city's seaport is 5 km from the hotel. The D400 motorway, which is quite significant in Turkey, also runs nearby.

Avena Resort & Spa Hotel 4 *: description of the complex

This hotel received its first guests quite a long time ago. This hotel opened in 2003. It was originally called the Gold Safran Hotel. Renamed it relatively recently. But in any case, by hotel standards, the complex is considered quite old. However, the situation is being rescued by a large-scale reconstruction, which was carried out here recently. As a rule, in reviews of 2017, hotel guests do not note any of its shortcomings related to the age of the complex.

This hotel accepts vacationers in one large 5-storey building. Regarding the design of the hotel building, reviews from tourists are extremely positive. The hotel looks modern, original and quite stylish. The courtyard at the Avena Resort & Spa Hotel, although small, is its own. It looks, according to tourists, quite neatly and effectively. The layout of the hotel, judging by the reviews, is quite convenient.
