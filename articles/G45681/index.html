Dolphinarium in Chelyabinsk - a fun weekend for the whole family

On weekends and holidays, most families face the question of how to have fun. Joint leisure is very important for a full relationship, especially if adults work all week and are not able to spend a lot of time with their families.

An active and interesting vacation with a lot of positive emotions is very important and brings great benefit to both children and adults. There are a huge number of ideas that will help turn the weekend into a real holiday. One of them is a visit to the dolphinarium. Watching the shows of these unusually positive and friendly mammals will bring joy and fun to adults and children.



Dolphinarium in Chelyabinsk invites visitors to see a stunning show, which is attended not only by dolphins, but also white whales, as well as seals. Visitors have the opportunity to enjoy the magnificent spectacle and even become participants. During the program, various options for the interaction of spectators with animals are provided, including for the most desperate experiment lovers there is the opportunity to ride dolphins or take memorable photos.

Schedule

The Dolphinarium in Chelyabinsk (2013 has not brought any changes in this regard) is located in the Gorki shopping center. This is a unique opportunity to take part in an amazing show of marine animals for all residents of the city. The Dolphinarium in Chelyabinsk, whose address is Artilleriyskaya Street, is easiest to find from the Meridian highway. The dolphinarium's working hours are Wednesday, Thursday and Friday, 18:00. For the convenience of viewers on weekends and holidays, the number of sessions increases to three per day - at 12:00, 15:00 and 18:00. Dolphinarium will work at the specified address until December 15.



Ticket price

The Dolphinarium in Chelyabinsk is an opportunity to give yourself and your loved ones a holiday and an unforgettable experience, especially since it is quite affordable and will not hit hard on the wallet of the head of the family. Prices for entry tickets start at 400 rubles. For this money you can buy a ticket for 8 or 9 row. In the future, their cost changes as follows: 6-7 row - 500 rubles each, 4-5 row - 600 rubles each, 2-3 row - 700 rubles each. The highest prices are for front row tickets. The pleasure of seeing the performance in full detail from the closest viewpoint will cost 800 rubles.

When planning a trip to the dolphinarium in Chelyabinsk, visitors should pay attention to the fact that tickets are sold only in cash. Payment by credit card is not made. Dolphinarium administration does not provide discounts and benefits for visitors. All tickets can be purchased in advance at the box office of the dolphinarium.

Dolphinarium in Chelyabinsk can be visited free of charge by children under 3 years old. At the entrance, parents need to show a document (birth certificate) that can confirm the age of the child. Kids from 3 years old need to purchase a full ticket.



In the dolphinarium visitors are given a unique opportunity to swim with dolphins. Unforgettable feelings and exciting emotions are guaranteed! The duration of one session is 5 minutes. Cost - 3000 rubles. Visitors should pay attention to the need to pre-register for such sessions.
