We crochet stylish booties for a boy

With booties - cute fishnet shoes - it's great to start your acquaintance with a hook. Many young mothers want to make such warm and beautiful products for their babies with their own hands. On the Internet you can find many workshops on the manufacture of all kinds of shoes, boots, slippers and booties, imitating sneakers.

Unfortunately, most of them are for girls. And in this article we suggest you make crocheted charming booties for a boy. The step-by-step technique for their manufacture is not particularly labor-intensive, so any needlewoman can perform such a product.

Booties for a boy (crochet): getting started

In order to tie beautiful shoes for your baby, you will need to purchase yarn, as well as prepare hook number 2.5. You will need Turkish Jeans threads in two colors - white and blue. The shoe sole may be approximately as follows.

So, crocheting booties for boys begins with the manufacture of soles. Its length will be 11 cm. First, we collect 15 air loops (abbreviated VP). Then add three more loops for lifting. We introduce the hook into the fourth VP and knit a crochet column. We perform another 13 loops with a crochet (CH). Only the last VP, which we use to create the soles of the soles, remains untied. We will knit six double crochet into it.
Now we need to go to the other side: we again perform 14 loops, and in the last we knit 5 CH. We close the second row with a half-crochet without a crochet.

We continue to knit the soles of "shoes" for the boy

At the beginning of the third row, we collect three air loops. Next, in the next six we knit two double crochets. After that, we make 14 double crochet. In the next five loops we knit two CH, in the sixth - 1 CH. We close the row again with a single crochet. We crochet the booties for the boy: the fourth row begins with three lifting loops. We knit 14 single crochet posts. We make 1 CH, then in the next loop of the base we perform two loops with a crochet. Repeat the item six times. Now do 14 CH. We repeat the scheme "1CH + 2 CH in the base loop", excluding the last yarn loop. The fifth row we knit with a single crochet. This is the sole you should get.



Beautiful crocheted booties: schemes for a boy

After manufacturing the soles, we proceed to the manufacture of "sides". The sixth and seventh rows are crocheted in single stitches in the base loops. Important: the sixth - be sure to carry out the front loop to translate knitting into a perpendicular (relative to the sole) position. Booties for a boy: crochet the connecting posts, making the eighth row. Now visually divide the shoe in half, marking the place where the sock will be. We get to the markup, we knit a series of connecting columns and perform "puffs". Make a space between them in two loops. You should get 10 magnificent columns. For greater strength, we tie the product with connecting posts. That's it, your booties are almost ready. It remains only to make it more elegant. To do this, decorate the shoe with crochet columns with contrasting blue yarn. According to the same scheme, make the second booties. Such beautiful and stylish shoes will surely please your baby and help keep his legs warm.
