Overview of hotel-palaces in Turkey

On the coasts of the Black, Aegean, Marmara and Mediterranean Seas, resort areas of Turkey are located. The most expensive hotel in Turkey, which is prestigious, must have 5 stars. We describe the most popular of the most expensive.

Mardan Palace is a palace-hotel in Antalya, built for one and a half million dollars in 2009. Its beaches are considered the best, sand for them was specially imported from Egypt. At the disposal of the guests of this hotel is the largest swimming pool in Turkey. The cuisines of different nations are represented in 10 restaurants, a nightclub on 3 floors is waiting for its visitors. The hotel has 560 rooms, the cost of accommodation comes to 20 thousand dollars per night.





The exclusive and most expensive hotel in Turkey - Kempinski Hotel Barbaros - is distinguished by tropical landscapes, tranquility and silence. It is the calm atmosphere that attracts Hollywood stars here, tired of the flashes of cameras and longing for privacy.

Istanbul also houses the chic Ciragan Palace Hotel, which belonged to the Ottoman Sultans in the 16th century. This is a palace plus a hotel complex combining modern style with baroque. The most expensive room is the Sultan Suite ($ 7,500 per night).

One of the best hotels - Attaleia Shine - is located in Belek. This is a complex of two buildings that are interconnected. The hotel offers exclusive service, rooms with chic furniture, equipment using the latest technologies.





Rixos Premium Bobrum is a premium hotel located in a pine forest. High-quality service, elegance and, of course, the high cost of a stay are its main features.

These are star-based hotels, but besides them there is an HV system and Holiday Village. Instead of rooms there are only bungalows, houses on two, three or four floors. Each room has a separate entrance. True, such hotels do not exist above 4 stars, although this does not mean that the level of service in them is worse. This should be judged not by the stars, but rather by the reviews of those who have already been there.

This is not a complete list of hotels that have the status of "The most expensive hotels in Turkey" (photos are given in the article). Every year they are replenished with hotel complexes, palace apartments with the highest service. They can occupy a small territory, or can a huge area.

If you need the most expensive hotel in Turkey, then of the whole variety of palace complexes, you need to rely on your taste when choosing, and any of the above can satisfy it.
