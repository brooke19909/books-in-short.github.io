Organ Hall of Omsk: how to get there? A photo

Find out where the Omsk organ hall is currently located. Omsk is a city in which special attention is paid to chamber music. That is why a unique architectural complex has appeared in its historical part, which can rightfully be called a "temple of music." Before talking about his whereabouts, we highlight the interesting facts of its occurrence.





Pages of history

How did the organ and chamber music hall appear in the city? Omsk is proud of the fact that in 1906 the construction of a large architectural complex began in its center, which included a hotel, a public meeting, as well as a Crystal Palace cinema. Since 1922 they began to call it "Artistic". This complex was listed as the center of social and cultural life of the city. For several decades, the Khudozhestvenny cinema was considered the best in Omsk, it featured performances by musical groups, domestic and foreign films were shown, and film festivals were organized.

Since 1940, a "live orchestra" was playing before the sessions, then a concert bureau appeared on the basis of the cinema. It was on its basis that an organ hall was subsequently created. Omsk is still proud of its philharmonic.





Main characteristics

The giant instrument, currently located in Omsk, has 3 manuel, 64 registers, 3,700 pipes. In the fall of 1998, the first serious concerts were organized, involving the performance of organ music.

Currently, the walls of this temple of music are the soloists of the Philharmonic, the stars of the world stage. Philharmonic musicians are proud that every year participants of the Siberian International Organ Music Festival gather in their walls.

Today, work is underway on the design of concert "life support" systems, premises are being created for staff, artists. Reconstruction of the upper tiers is also underway. There are make-up and rehearsal rooms, a hotel, a recording studio, and workshops. The large solemn hall is simultaneously able to accommodate about 200 spectators. Such a unique combination of a monument of architecture and history with a concert and exhibition venue has a positive effect on the cultural development of the region.

Organ revival

Since 1997, an organ previously located in St. Nicholas Cossack Cathedral was moved to the Khudozhestvenny cinema. This musical instrument was created in 1983 by the specialists of Rieger-Kloss.

An organ hall did not immediately appear here. Omsk miraculously preserved not only the organ, but also the Orthodox church itself, in which it was first located. Given that the organ is a heavy structure, during the reconstruction of the cinema, a special foundation was created for it, a bridge was made. About five tons of broken glass were used to give the foundation increased strength. The hall with columns, a balcony, stucco moldings turned out to be ideal in acoustics for placing an organ in it.

Distinctive features

Where is the organ hall in Omsk? This question is often asked by music lovers who find themselves in the city. The address and travel options are listed above, now we will find out in more detail why residents are proud of their organ hall?

Since 1950, the Omsk Folk Russian Choir was formed, then a unique symphony orchestra appeared, and since the end of the last century, a chamber orchestra has been operating in the city.

The concert hall was erected as a separate building on Lenin Street in 1967. The author of the project was A.M. Karimov. A few years later, the hall was updated, modern acoustic equipment appeared in it, it became the main stage of the Children's Omsk Philharmonic. Here, the artists of numerous city creative associations act as artists.

Organ Hall in Omsk

It is called the second regular concert philharmonic venue, the grand opening of which took place in 2002. Placed such a scene in the historical part of the cinema "Art" after the completion of a major reconstruction, designed area for 246 seats. In addition, a chamber music hall was created in the building, designed for eighty seats.

Since 2003, a specialized international festival has been held at this concert venue, which gathers guests from all over the world.





Philharmonic Offers

The Philharmonic always indicates the main events that are held in the halls for the next month, and also makes an announcement of the events for the next three months, so that residents and guests of Omsk can pre-purchase tickets for their chosen concert.

In 2011, a large-scale reconstruction of the Omsk Philharmonic Concert Hall took place. At present, this stage venue fully meets all international requirements and standards. The concert hall has amazing acoustic parameters, as well as all the technical characteristics for conducting full-fledged concerts of organ music.

Many musicians who started working in Omsk have gained worldwide and all-Russian fame and popularity.

For example, Lyubov Polishchuk and Valery Makarov, popular actors of theater and cinema, began their professional career on the stage of the Omsk Philharmonic.

Conclusion

Since 2009, many young performers from various countries take part in the International Festival of Violinists named after Yu. I. Yankelevich, organized at the concert venues of the Omsk Philharmonic.

It was the Omsk Philharmonic that initiated and organized many large cultural forums. Thanks to the support of the regional government, it hosts the participants of the Siberian International Festival of Organ Music, which is attended by the best organists in the world. Also, the New Music Festival is organized on the site of this institution, where you can hear symphonic novelties.

Since 2009, young performers have been striving into the walls of this temple of music to take part in the International Violin Competition, considered one of the most prestigious events in the world of classical music.

At different times, the following performed at the Omsk Philharmonic: K. Eliasberg, M. Rostropovich, D. Matsuev, V. Repin, L. Isakadze, K. Eliasberg, A. Fiseisky, H. Gerzmava, Yu. Temirkanov.

Citizens are rightly proud of their unique philharmonic society, as well as an organ hall, amazing in acoustics and design. In order to get here to the competition events, young performers, masters of the world classical scene undergo a rigorous selection.
