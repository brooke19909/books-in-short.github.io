Budget holidays at sea: the best options

In our article we want to talk about where you can relax cheaply. Budget vacation at sea is the most relevant topic in the summer. You can organize a vacation not only in Russia, but also abroad.

About vouchers ...

Independent trips to resorts usually cost much less than trips. But this rule does not always work. For a number of foreign destinations, it is sometimes cheaper to buy a last-minute tour, the cost of which can be even lower than the flight. You can pick up such trips on your own resources.





Rules for finding a budget holiday

If you plan to independently organize for yourself a budget vacation at sea, then you should be guided by the following rules:

  1. The first thing that will help to save money is the refusal to rest during the high season. When planning a vacation, you should focus on the beginning or end of the season. For domestic resorts, this is May, early June and September. Firstly, at this time there is no such tangible heat, and secondly, the sea is warm. And the prices are significantly different. If swimming in the sea is not a matter of principle for you, and you are ready to relax by the pool and go on excursions, then in countries such as Bulgaria and Turkey, you can economically relax from October to April. During this period, even the most luxurious hotels give significant discounts, so the rest becomes much more affordable. Many complexes of foreign resorts have heated pools, which save the situation when the sea is cold.
  2. If you want to spend your vacation in Bulgaria and Turkey, then you should stop your choice not on half board, but on the beloved All Inclusive concept . Initially, it may seem to you that you are overpaying the extra money, but in fact, the purchase of drinks and food will cost you much more.
  3. In order to organize the most budget vacation at sea abroad, you must purchase last-minute tours or use the early booking service.
  4. Do not forget that leisure alone is much more expensive than in a company.

Crimea

Speaking of budget holidays at sea with children, it is worth paying attention to the Crimea. In 2017, the cost of a ticket from Moscow to Simferopol by plane will cost about 7,000 rubles, and in the fall and spring - even cheaper. From Simferopol you can reach any resort on the peninsula.

The most budgetary vacation at sea in Russia can only be subject to living in the private sector. In Crimea, for example, you can choose to relax small villages - Olenevka, Nikolaevka, Storm. In such places in guest houses you can find rooms even at three hundred rubles a day. In larger and more famous resorts, prices are higher. The minimum cost of living increases to 600 rubles per room. The average cost of living in 2017 in the private sector starts from a thousand rubles for double apartments. In general, the villages can be safely considered as a budget holiday on the Black Sea with children.

As for food, as a budget option, you can consider different eateries and dining rooms, national cafes, dining rooms at guest houses with home cooking, fast food establishments. Remember that the more you move away from the coast, the lower the price of food. In dining rooms and inexpensive cafes you can eat for two hundred rubles, and in an inexpensive restaurant for no less than 400 rubles. More affordable food prices in small resort towns. Bulgaria

In Bulgaria, you can organize the most budget holidays on the Black Sea abroad, which is why all the local resorts are incredibly popular among tourists. The most democratic place to stay is Sunny Beach. The fine sand on the beaches and the gentle entrance to the sea have long been loved by supporters of beach-sea recreation. The coast has a width of about forty meters, and in length it stretches for eight kilometers. In modest hotels, you can rent a small room for $ 19 per day.



Even more budget holidays on the Black Sea can only be in small Bulgarian coastal villages and towns. In these areas there are many two- and three-star hotels, as well as guest houses in the private sector. Locals often rent tourists the upper floors of their houses for rent. It can be economical to rest in Kranevo, Balchik, Nessebar, St. Vlas, Pomerania, Ravda and Tsarevo.

Sometimes tours to the resorts of Bulgaria can be purchased at very affordable prices (from 20 thousand rubles). And promotional offers can be even more attractive. And this despite the fact that the price of the tour includes accommodation, flight, insurance and transfer.

Bulgaria is interesting to many travelers because it offers a budget vacation at sea in September. At this time, the air temperature drops to +25 degrees, there is no summer heat and heat, and sea water remains warm. In September, it is good to relax in Nessebar, Sofia, Sozople, Balchik, Plovdiv and Varna. The cost of living in the resorts is reduced, because there is not a large influx of tourists.

Thailand

Another budget option for a seaside vacation is Thailand. This country is no less popular as a beach-seaside resort than Egypt and Turkey. The high season in Thailand starts around mid-autumn and ends early in winter. Therefore, the most budget tours are in May and April.
