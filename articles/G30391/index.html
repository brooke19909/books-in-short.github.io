Hotel Kleopatra Aytur Suit Hotel Apts 3 * Alanya, Turkey: overview, description and reviews

The Turkish resort of Alanya, located on the Antalya coast of Turkey, has become relatively popular with tourists relatively recently. Vacationers began to come here on vacation about 17 years ago. However, for almost two decades, local entrepreneurs have tried to establish the city’s infrastructure in such a way that travelers could rest here as comfortably as possible.

Built in Alanya, among other things, and many hotels of different price categories. For example, quite good rooms and quality service are offered to vacationers, including Russian, in this city, the Kleopatra Aytur Suit Hotel Apts 3 * hotel.

Where is the hotel located

This complex belongs to the type of urban. That is, it is located not in the suburbs, but directly in Alanya itself, about a 20-minute walk from its center. His exact address is as follows: Saray Mah. 912 Sok. No 6 . There are many cafes, bars and shops nearby . For example, next to this hotel is a large shopping center "Migros".





Also nearby this hotel there are a lot of interesting city attractions. The most popular among tourists is the medieval fortress of Alanya. It is located at a distance of only 2 km from Kleopatra Aytur Suit. It even offers views of some of the rooms in the hotel. Alanya Port is located just 1 km from the hotel. In walking distance from the complex there is also a water park.

The hotel is approximately 30-35 minutes drive from the nearest Gazipash Airport. The distance to Antalya from Alanya is approximately 130 km. From the airport of this city, tourists can reach the hotel in about 1.5-2 hours.

In relation to the sea, according to many travelers, the hotel is located quite well. The coastline is about 200 meters from the hotel. That is, hotel guests can walk to the beach in a few minutes.

General description of the complex

Kleopatra Aytur Suit Hotel Apts 3 * belongs to the three-star category. Outwardly, according to tourists, it looks pretty pretty. They take guests in this complex in one multi-storey building, built in 2015. The architecture of the hotel building cannot be called too original. But against the background of nearby city buildings, the complex building still looks quite impressive .





The reception, as in most other three-star hotels in Turkey, Kleopatra Aytur Suit Hotel Apts 3 * is open around the clock. There are no special rules for guests staying at this hotel. Naturally, you cannot smoke in the rooms or in the corridors. Also, the hotel does not accept tourists who come to Alanya with pets. Apart from Russian holidaymakers, the majority of people living in this hotel are mostly elderly Swedes, Dutch and Germans.

What apartments are offered

Rooms for a three-star hotel in Kleopatra Aytur Suit Hotel Apts 3 * were equipped, according to tourists, not bad. All units include a kitchenette . The bathrooms are equipped with Turkish jacuzzi . Also in the hotel rooms tourists can use:

  • air conditioners;

  • TVs

  • refrigerators;

  • safes.

The beds in the hotel apartments are folding. Bathrooms offer hair dryers. This hotel has rooms with balconies. One of the features of this hotel is that tourists in the rooms rented here can use the Wi-Fi network for free.





Kleopatra Aytur Suit Hotel Apts Room Vacation Reviews

The opinion of tourists about the rooms rented at the hotel has developed quite well. The rooms for vacationers are offered quite spacious. Furniture and appliances in the hotel rooms are new . There are good reviews on the network about the cleanliness of the rooms handed over at the hotel. Another advantage of the hotel rooms among tourists is good soundproofing. Noise does not interfere with tourists resting in the hotel from the street or from the corridors. Fast in hotel rooms, judging by the reviews, and wifi.

Some of the drawbacks of the rooms rented at this hotel are that the floor here is tiled. Walking on it, unfortunately, is not too pleasant. Indeed, this material itself is quite cold and, moreover, slippery when wet. In addition, the hotel does not offer slippers.

Hotel service

The rooms in the Kleopatra Aytur Suit Hotel Apts 3 * hotel (Alanya) , despite the fact that it is only a three-star hotel , are comfortable and well equipped. The infrastructure in this complex is also very well developed. At the hotel there are:

  • open pool;

  • sun terrace;

  • tour agency.





Parking for vacationers who rented cars in Alanya, the hotel provides covered.

Infrastructure and staff reviews

The service on the territory and Kleopatra Aytur Suit Hotel Apts 3 *, the court, according to the reviews of tourists, is offered not bad. The presence of a covered car park hotel guests relate, of course, to its advantages. The only thing is the pool in this hotel, which, unfortunately, is always in the shade, so the water in it is usually cool. In very hot weather, swimming in the hotel pool is probably pleasant, but many tourists would still like the water in it to be at least a little warmer.

The guests consider the hotel’s lack of laundry and even an iron on its territory as some drawback. Hotel guests are forced to wash and iron dirty items in the dry cleaners of the city. Unfortunately, this procedure is quite expensive here.

Polite and helpful staff - this is what Kelopatra Suite Hotel in Alanya has earned good reviews from tourists. Kleopatra Suit Hotel (Alanya) already has its loyal customers. Some vacationers come here annually . And the merit of this is the availability of efficient and responsible hotel staff .





Nutrition

Rooms at the Kleopatra Aytur Ap ar ts 3 * hotel (Alanya) are offered to tourists with breakfast. The dining room of the complex has earned good reviews from guests. The menu for the residents of the hotel is designed diverse. The dishes themselves in the restaurant of the complex, judging by the reviews of the guests, are hearty and tasty. Quite good in the hotel's dining room, according to tourists, drinks are also served. The only thing that the hotel restaurant is forbidden to bring their own food and alcohol. But guests and guests of the hotel can order lunches and dinners directly in their rooms. Tourists, of course, attribute the availability of such a service to the advantages of this complex.

Regarding the cleanliness of the restaurant hall, as well as the work of the waiters, vacationers have no complaints about this hotel. Only the hotel kitchen deserved some complaints from the guests. Here, according to some vacationers, it could be a little cleaner.

Lunches and dinners of the hotel guests can cook for themselves in the room. However, the tiles in the kitchens of the hotel apartments, unfortunately, may be inoperative. But they are replaced by serviceable hotel workers, usually at the very first request of guests. Residents of the complex can also have good lunch and dinner in any cafe located next to it. Dishes in local catering establishments are served, judging by the reviews, delicious. Moreover, the food in the cafe next to the hotel is inexpensive.





Holidays by the sea

The advantages of the Kleopatra Aytur Suit Hotel Apts 3 * complex include guests and the opportunity to visit the nearby beautiful and clean Cleopatra beach. Hotel guests need about 5 minutes to go to the sea. Tourists do not need to pay for entry to Cleopatra beach. Sun loungers and umbrellas here are 5 lire.

The coastline in this place is covered with soft sand, but when entering the sea itself there are fragments of concrete slabs. Therefore, tourists should be careful next to the hotel. Many vacationers advise vacationers who have booked rooms at the Kleopatra Aytur hotel , be sure to take snorkeling masks with them on vacation. In the sea near the hotel you can admire the flocks of beautiful tropical fish.
