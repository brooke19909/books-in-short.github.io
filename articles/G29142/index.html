Hotel Kirman Hotels Sidemarin Beach & Spa 5 * (Side, Turkey): photos, reviews

Holidays in Turkey rightfully occupies the first position among other foreign countries. Such demand is associated with excellent service and affordable cost. Kirman Hotels Sidemarin Beach & Spa * is an all-inclusive hotel. The complex began its work in 2016 and is considered a new hotel in Side.

Where is it and how to get there?

Kirman Hotels Sidemarin Beach & Spa 5 * is located 5 km from the resort town of Side and is located in the village of Kumkey. You can get here from Antalya Airport, which is 60 km from the complex.

The hotel provides a shuttle service. Also from the airport you can take a taxi or get there by public transport. An independent trip will come out in time 2 times longer, but cheaper than an ordered car.

The hotel operates year-round. Guests can contact the employee at the reception at any time of the day, and settlement also takes place.

Description of Kirman Hotels Sidemarin Beach & Spa 5 * in Turkey

The hotel is located directly next to the sea, the road to which takes no more than 10-15 minutes with an average step. The building has 5 floors and was built in the form of a traditional Turkish castle with rounded shapes and many golden details.





The territory of the complex occupies about 18,000 m 2 . Near the hotel there is a modern and spacious parking lot with 24-hour security. Guests can rent a car from the hotel reception.

The decoration of Kirman Hotels Sidemarin Beach & Spa 5 * is striking in its richness and beauty. Here in the hall the floor is laid with marble slabs, and amazing patterns are decorated on the walls.

Upholstered furniture at the reception is made of natural materials, and guests can relax here with a cup of coffee and read the latest press.

Terms of Accommodation

Hotel Kirman Hotels Sidemarin Beach & Spa 5 * offers its customers to use rooms of different class of comfort and with a settlement of a certain number of people:

  • standard for 2, 3, 4 people with a view of the sea or the surroundings;
  • family rooms for 3, 4, 5, 6 people with a view of the immediate surroundings;
  • economy class rooms for 2-3 people.

All rooms have a private washroom and shower. Here, guests are left with disposable miniature hygiene kits, bathrobes and slippers. All rooms have a modern plasma TV with satellite channels in Russian.





All rooms are equipped with air conditioning. In hot weather, they cool the air remarkably, and in the rainy season, you can use it to warm the temperature in the room.

Many rooms have balconies. From them you can admire the surrounding areas and the sea. The rooms have minibars. They replenish free stocks of soft drinks and mineral water.

For a separate price, you can use the small safe to save your personal funds during your vacation. Free internet is available throughout. But, it must be borne in mind that the speed is low. For higher rates, tourists will have to pay extra.

Beach

Kirman Hotels Sidemarin Beach & Spa 5 * is located on the first coastline. This means that the road to the sea takes no more than 10 minutes with a walking step. The beach here is sandy and belongs to the hotel.

Guests of the complex can use free sun loungers, parasols, towels. Also, those who wish are given mattresses for swimming. On the beach there is a bar that belongs to the hotel. In it, guests can take the necessary drinks for free.





The beach is cleaned daily. Tourists can walk on the sand without shoes and not be afraid to get hurt. The bottom is also cleaned at the entrance to the sea, so little vacationers can safely play in this area.

Pools

The complex has several pools. All of them are filled with clean water, which is passed through special filters. Also, several times a day they are cleaned with special equipment.





Outdoor swimming pools in complex 2. In one of them the descent from the water slides. There are three of them. One is made in the form of a spiral, and the other two have a steep descent. In one of the pools a children's zone with a shallow depth is organized. It is fenced with special sides.

The complex has an indoor pool. It works in the cool season. There is also a children's area and hydromassage facilities. Sun loungers are installed in this area and a small bar is open seasonally.
