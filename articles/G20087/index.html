What is half board in a hotel?

If you plan to choose a hotel with this type of food, then look for the designation HB (Half Board). This option implies that the price includes accommodation and two meals a day. Consider specifically what is included in half board:

  • breakfast - various foods (pancakes, salads, fruits, eggs, porridge, granola, croissants, etc.) and soft drinks (coffee, juice, milk, tea);
  • dinner is only food (salad, bread, fish or meat and sweets). Sometimes they still serve water in a jug.

Usually food is on the buffet. The time for food consumption is predetermined and limited, say, from eight to ten in the morning and from six to eight in the evening. In some hotels you can order dinner instead of dinner. For everything else (alcoholic drinks, snacks around the pool, drinks) you will need to pay extra, though not immediately, but at the end of the vacation. When you leave, you will be provided with an invoice.

In addition to the usual half board, there is an extended one. It means so - HB +. Such meals include breakfast, dinner plus alcoholic drinks (local producers) and soft drinks during lunch. The exact list of drinks depends on the hotel.

What is the difference between HB + and HB?
