Limassol, water park Fasouri Watermania: description, reviews

The famous and interesting city for tourists is Limassol. Excursions here are informative and full of interesting data. However, this is by no means all that a tourist can reward himself with.

Description

A great place for lovers of water recreation can be a water park in Cyprus, which won the widespread love of visitors. Throughout the city, this is one of the most important places for family entertainment. Positive emotions here are received by children and parents.





The opening took place in 1999. During the construction, materials and equipment of a safe and modern nature were used, raw materials and technical developments were better used. Because of this, and now the administration is awarded a large number of awards for the safety and quality of the services provided. In 2007, the institution was called the best in the European space; in 2012, a prestigious certificate was received for excellent service.

The institution operates, as a rule, from May to October, since it depends on climatic conditions. In late spring and autumn, opening hours are from 10:00 to 17:00, in the summer from 10:00 to 18:00.

A wonderful experience brings water park in Limassol. Entrance prices are as follows: 29 euros for an adult, 16 - for a child older than 3 years. Toddlers under 3 years old are admitted free of charge.

There is a drive, adrenaline spills out when you move down one of the 22 local slides. For each age, there is a solution.

How to get there

Location - An area called Fassouri. To reach it, you need to drive 15 minutes from the city center or 10 minutes from the highway called Limassol - Paphos.

Shuttle buses operate in the right direction throughout the day. You can catch transport at one of the bus stations, the first of which is located near La Meridien - a hotel in which many tourists live.

In the morning there are 4 flights. In the evening there are as many of them, as you need to take the guests of the water park home. If traveling from Paphos, public transport can be taken on Tuesday, Friday and Sunday, arriving at the bus stop at 8:30. Tickets are booked in advance by pre-purchasing them on the website or from a tour operator. The schedule will also not be superfluous to check with the guide.





Rating of visitors

Customers note the presence of a huge number of interesting amenities that distinguish the complex from other institutions of a similar orientation. The massage parlor is the newest acquisition in the water park by the Fasouri Watermania administration in 2012. Many people relax here and feel completely refreshed as a result.

Such a salon makes it possible to feel relaxed and restore strength - both emotional and physical. Spa specialists offer short sessions, they will not take more than half an hour, but the results are amazing.

After the procedure, the body is relaxed, it is stimulated to further activity. Premature orders are not needed, as the "come first - first served" policy applies. Customers note the high convenience of such a service. Direct treatment (lying on a deck chair) is also available. This helps a person to completely relax and forget about all the worries. Visitors say that all the details in the complex are worked out quite well and professionally. I want to come here again.





Interesting services

Definitely worth getting into this complex when visiting Limassol. The water park is the place where you can get a souvenir not only with a fridge magnet, but also with a tattoo for a while. A drawing is selected from an extensive list that can be printed on the skin with anti-allergic, environmentally friendly and time-washable material.

There is another service for which the water park in Cyprus has created its own salon with live fish. This is the largest such establishment on the whole island, which offers its guests entertainment, as well as a relaxing way to enjoy foot massage. You can experience some tickling. Fishes are used to safely and gently exfoliate the skin of the legs. The above service is available at an additional cost.

Shopping and rental equipment

In the souvenir shop you can buy summer supplies, clothes for going to the beach, bags, towels and more. Truly unique can be called a complex created in a city called Limassol.

The water park is equipped with a Kodak establishment. Local photographers, true masters of their craft, are working to capture the wonderful moments of your holiday. The store will provide you with everything you might need for your own cameras, for example, tapes or batteries.

High level of amenities. There are about 2 thousand sun loungers located throughout the park. One umbrella is also issued for every two places, for the formation of shade and protection from the sun. It is provided at no charge. Inventory can be immediately taken by the visitor who first came and took the line.





Guests can now make their purchases using only one card. You no longer need to carry cash or credit cards in the park during your stay. A special settlement system has been developed that is available for use when shopping at a water park gift shop. Hungry, visitors go to a local restaurant or pizzeria, a wonderful grill bar, coffee shop, cocktail bar. The choice is really wide.

Security

Fasouri Watermania has a high level of protection for human life. All rescuers are professionally trained and have a solid first aid practice. Pipes, rugs, life jackets are issued free of charge. There is an ambulance station, fully equipped for emergency situations. It is run by licensed medical professionals who are on duty during business hours.

In order to appreciate the quality and efficiency of the administration, it is worth coming to Limassol. The water park is equipped with means to protect your values. These are small lockers located next to sun loungers. Toilets you can find in various places throughout the complex.





All these advantages make Limassol quite attractive. The water park on its territory is several steps higher than most other similar complexes of water entertainment, so it is a kind of example of quality. It is useful to visit it to give the impression of a really high level of service, as well as to receive an amazing charge of vivid emotions.
