The best camp sites of Ulyanovsk: photos and reviews of tourists

With the onset of the first spring heat, everyone begins to wonder where to spend a vacation. Not everyone can afford a vacation in Turkey or Egypt. Yes and no, this is not necessary. After all, there are wonderful places to relax in your native country. What are comfortable camp sites of Ulyanovsk. Both adults and children will find entertainment for themselves, and young people in a noisy company will be able to spend unforgettable time in general!

Chalet "Oriole"

Not far from the children's camp. Deeva is located the "Oriole" camp site. Ulyanovsk is rich in its natural resources. It is no accident that many rest houses have been built within the city, as well as outside its borders. But the Oriole camp site is a special place. People say that you can have a great time here not only on summer days, but also in winter. The reviews suggest that the holiday home is especially popular during the Christmas holidays. Many come here as a family and spend time away from everyday hustle and bustle.

What benefits do guests indicate in their comments? Firstly, the Oriole camp site has a good location. Ulyanovsk is located near the Volga. It was on the shore of this waterway that the rest house was built. In the summer, people prefer to spend time on the beach. And in the cold season, the camp site is very popular among fishermen. During the day, you can pay attention to your favorite hobby, and in the evening spend time with friends, chat about problems, fry the barbecue. Barbecue, skewers, dishes - all this can be rented in a holiday home.



Secondly, visitors are offered comfortable wooden houses for living, which keep cool in the summer and do not let the cold in winter. Additionally, you can use the heater. But in the cold season, people usually come for several days, and spend most of the time around the campfire. Therefore, heaters are rarely used.

Thirdly, the Oriole camp site offers everything you need in order to organize high-quality meals. The staff claims that most vacationers prefer to cook their own meals. Guests are provided with electric stoves and all the necessary equipment. And for those who do not want to bother with cooking during their holidays, a cozy restaurant offers their services.

Holiday House "Albatross"

The hostel is very popular not only among local residents, but also among tourists from other regions. Those who visit Albatros one day will remember Ulyanovsk camp for a long time. The holiday home already has its regular customers, who spend all weekends and family holidays in the base. This place is perfect for both noisy companies and for a quiet family vacation. People say that there are all conditions for living with preschool kids.

The tourist base "Albatross" is located within the city. Vacationers can not only spend time on the banks of the river or around a fire, but also visit interesting sights outside the holiday home. There is a volleyball court on site and there is also a table tennis court. In addition, you can rent a catamaran or book a fascinating boat trip along the Volga.





If you believe the reviews, the Albatros holiday home is perfect for people with financial difficulties. The hostel (Ulyanovsk) offers houses for 3-4 people costing no more than 3000 rubles per day. For noisy companies, there are separate apartments that can accommodate up to 12 people at a time. The cost of renting such a premises is 3500 rubles per day. To celebrate a birthday, you can rent a cozy wooden gazebo. For one hour you will have to pay 700 rubles. The daily rent is 10,000 rubles. And those who want to take a steam bath will have to pay 650 rubles per hour. The fee is taken regardless of how many people will be present in the bath.

Chalet "Admiral"

For those who prefer to relax at a high level in the territory of their native land, the Admiral tourist base is suitable. The reviews of those who were fortunate enough to spend a weekend or vacation here speak for themselves. The Admiral complex is not much different from the famous hotels of Turkey and Egypt. There is everything in order to spend an unforgettable vacation with family or friends. The tourist base is popular not only among tourists. Here the best conditions are created for weddings, corporate parties, graduation parties and birthday celebrations. It is possible to use not only a cozy banquet hall, but also hire a host for your holiday.





How to get here? In the railway district is located the holiday home "Admiral" (Ulyanovsk). The hostel is familiar to every local. Therefore, visitors easily find their way to their destination. The staff recommends that those who are visiting the city for the first time take a taxi. The cost of a trip from the station to the camp site will not exceed 200 rubles. In the summer, the holiday home is very popular among tourists. Therefore, it is advisable to book a house for living in advance. You just need to call the number: 8-917-620-41-07.

The holiday home is open all year. The cost of renting a house with heating is 7500 rubles. According to guest reviews, this is a comfortable apartment with all amenities. At the same time, up to 4 adults can live here. The cost of renting a summer house is 3,500 rubles. Vacationers are pleased with the additional opportunity to use the gazebo, rent a barbecue and skewers. It is worth noting that the entrance to the territory is also paid and is 100 rubles for each visitor older than 5 years.

Of course, many call the Admiral (Ulyanovsk) a rather expensive holiday home. The hostel is fully consistent with its name. In addition, do not forget that a quality and comfortable stay can be obtained only for the appropriate fee.

Hotel complex "Forest Story"

It is not just a place to relax. It is this institution, according to reviews, many choose to stay during a business trip. The Lesnaya Byl camp site (Ulyanovsk) has an excellent conference room. Many leading companies regularly negotiate here and solve important organizational issues. But without quality rest, productive work is impossible. That is why the hotel complex has everything you need for relaxation. In the summer, you can organize an excellent vacation on the lake or in a cozy gazebo. As in other places, it is possible to rent a barbecue and everything necessary for cooking barbecue. In addition, you can use the services of a professional chef who will cook any dish according to the customer.



Ulyanovsk camp sites are great for outdoor enthusiasts. On the territory of the hotel complex there is a volleyball court, a place for football, a gym. Inveterate fishermen claim that here you can rent all the necessary equipment for your favorite hobby. People who prefer to relax on the water are offered boats, catamarans, air mattresses. For an additional fee, you can ride a boat on the Volga.

According to reviews, the camp site "Forest Story" refers to the average price category. For a standard double room a day you will have to pay 3500 rubles. The price of accommodation does not depend on the time of the year. Another thing is the category of premises. The most expensive are deluxe rooms. For a day of living in them you have to pay 6,000 rubles. For this money not only comfortable apartments will be provided, but also personal hygiene products, a hairdryer, an iron, and also the Internet.

Chalet "Volga"

The rest house got its name thanks to the great river of the same name, which is located within Ulyanovsk. The base is located in a picturesque place - Nagorny. It is especially popular in the summer months. It is here that you can recharge your batteries with clean air and beautiful nature. The hostel belongs to the low price category. For only 3,000 rubles a day, you can rent a two-room apartment for 7-8 people at a time. The house has a bathroom and kitchen. Additionally, bedding, dishes, as well as barbecue and skewers can be provided.



A wonderful beach is equipped on the territory of the holiday home. Both noisy companies and families with children can have a great time. On the beach there are comfortable dressing rooms and gazebos, allowing you to hide from the scorching sun. Visitors claim that it is very clean, litter bins are installed every 50 meters.

For those who do not want to spend time in the kitchen during the rest, the Volga camp site (Ulyanovsk) offers the use of a canteen. It is possible to plan the menu. To do this, you must submit an application to the responsible canteen (per day). This is especially convenient for families with small children. Not every baby will eat unusual food.

Low cost of living has done its job. If you believe the reviews, in the summer there is no end to those who want to relax at the Volga camp site. To rent a comfortable house, you must book it in April. To do this, just call the number: 8-906-46-33-70. You will have to make an advance payment for accommodation in advance, which will be 20% of the daily cost.

Holiday Village "Beach"

On the shore of the Staromaynsky Bay in a cozy pine forest there is a small tourist base "Coast". The holiday home is located 50 km from Ulyanovsk and is very popular among residents of this region. Vacationers claim that the crystal clear air with coniferous aroma fills with energy and gives a positive mood.



Chalet "Coast" (Ulyanovsk) offers two accommodation options. The main building is equipped with more than ten rooms with all amenities. The rooms have a toilet, bathroom, kitchen. There is a TV. One room can accommodate up to 4 people at a time. It is possible to additionally install a crib. Also on the territory of the base there are separate wooden houses with accommodation for up to 6 people each. They have a shower and toilet. The staff claims that this option is more popular in the summer, which is understandable.

"Arkhangelsk settlement"

In the Zavolzhsky district of Ulyanovsk, there is another luxury recreation center. Arkhangelsk Sloboda attracts tourists not only from Russian regions, but also from neighboring countries. This place was especially fond of wealthy Poles, Belarusians and Ukrainians. The holiday home is located 10 km from the city, on the banks of the Volga River. The camp site has its own comfortable beach with white sand. In the summer, every vacationer can rent a mattress, sunbed, parasol. There are many outdoor cafes near the beach. If you believe the reviews, the prices here are very affordable.



In the winter, the Sloboda camp site (Ulyanovsk) is also very popular. Many families come here during the Christmas holidays. On the territory of the holiday home there is a cozy restaurant with a banquet room. Therefore, the "Arkhangelsk settlement" is perfect for weddings, graduations and other celebrations. The cuisine here, as evidenced by guest reviews, is particularly sophisticated. To book a banquet hall, you need to call the number: 8-422-75-70-20 for several weeks. The recreation center "Cozy"

When discussing Ulyanovsk, one cannot help but recall such a recreation center as "Cozy". The name already speaks for itself. Here, every vacationer expects a truly homely atmosphere. In addition, there is clean pine air, complete silence, as well as delicious Uzbek and European cuisine. On the territory you can really take a break from everyday work and everyday problems. Here you can forget about work and household chores. The Uyutnaya hostel (Ulyanovsk) invites guests at any time of the year.

What can the holiday home staff offer? First of all, accommodation in comfortable rooms for 2, 3, 4 and 6 people. Each room has a bathroom and shower. An extra bed is available. Recreation Center "Expanse"

In the Cherdaklinsky district, not far from the center of Ulyanovsk, there is a tourist base, which is especially popular with families with small children. There is all the entertainment for little tourists. These are wonderful carousels, swings, trampolines and inflatable slides. While the kids are busy doing what they love, parents can gather their thoughts and gain strength before the new labor week. In summer, people often come here on weekends. But for a good rest base "Expanse" is perfect!

On the territory of the holiday home there are summer buildings with a capacity of up to 8 people, as well as winter houses with double rooms. In the cold season, hunting and fishing lovers come here most often. In addition, you can rent a cozy sauna, a gazebo, as well as everything you need for cooking barbecue. Visitors note that in winter, the camp site offers much less entertainment. The houses are mostly empty.

Like other tourist centers in Ulyanovsk, the Razdolie Holiday House offers a comfortable banquet hall with a capacity of up to 300 people. This is a great place for a wedding or prom.
