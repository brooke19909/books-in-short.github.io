A few words about the screen

Now it's time to consider the detailed specifications of the Xiaomi Redmi 4 Prime. First, let's deal with the features of the display. The screen in this model is definitely an advantage. With a 5-inch size, the manufacturer offers the user Full HD quality, and this will undoubtedly please many. It is worth noting that the front panel is covered with 2.5D glass. The image is characterized by high contrast and good color reproduction. There are no comments on density, since the resolution of the display is as much as 441 ppi. Such an indicator in this price category is worthy of respect. With brightness problems will not arise at all. Its range is more than enough to work outdoors in sunny weather. The viewing angles did not disappoint. Even with a strong tilt, the image remains almost unchanged. The sensor is sensitive, it works clearly. The only thing is, some users have noticed that in winter, in the cold, you sometimes have to press twice.



Memory shots

The capabilities of the cameras did not disappoint the owners of Xiaomi Redmi 4 Prime. The characteristics of the main are quite consistent with modern requirements. It is based on a 13-megapixel sensor with f / 2.2 aperture. The highest level of detail is achieved in the center of the frame, and a faint blur appears at the edges. But by and large, photos are obtained of proper quality. The dynamic range is optimally maintained. With poor lighting, the clarity of the picture decreases, but all objects are clearly distinguishable, no noise. Most all users praise the "Panorama" and HDR. Video recording is carried out with a resolution of 1080p. There are no special comments on the quality.

The selfie camera is equipped with a 5-megapixel module. The menu has different modes. However, it is not possible to improve the color of photos even when choosing the highest settings, they remain somewhat muffled.

Xiaomi Redmi 4 Prime: hardware specifications

It's time to talk about the performance of this model. The ‚Äúbrain‚Äù of the gadget is the chipset for eight cores of the Qualcomm brand. The manufacturer for the smartphone chose the Snapdragon 625 model. The processor is manufactured according to the 14 nm manufacturing process. As the coprocessor, the developers chose the Adreno 506 accelerator. It allows you to quickly launch modern games at high graphics settings. And this suggests that in its segment the gadget is the undisputed leader.



Memory

It is impossible to evaluate the real performance of the gadget without indicators of the amount of RAM. Even the best hardware will not work correctly with a small RAM. The developers took this criterion seriously by installing three gigabytes of RAM in the Xiaomi Redmi 4 Prime 32Gb. The type of memory is LPDDR3. The device is able to easily cope with several running applications. Owners can play games and simultaneously watch videos, while opening, for example, five tabs in the browser. Based on this, we can safely say that the smartphone will cope with multitasking.

How much ROM is in Xiaomi Redmi 4 Prime? 32Gb! Well, that's pretty good. Only, about 10 GB of storage is already occupied by system files. The undemanding user of the remaining 20 will be quite enough for work. And those who want to increase memory are invited to use the drive, however, then they will have to abandon the second SIM card.



System platform

The Xiaomi Redmi 4 Prime smartphone is under the control of the sixth Android. It was not surprising to anyone that the manufacturer used the proprietary MIUI 8 interface on top of the system. Now the user has access to such functions as duplication of applications, screenshot of a web page, and built-in antivirus. As for the latter, the application not only checks the system for viruses, but also monitors the condition of the battery and RAM, and also controls applications that run in the background.

Battery Life

Do not disappoint the owners of Xiaomi Redmi 4 Prime battery specifications? Definitely not! In the device, the developers installed a 4100 mAh battery. In the talk mode, the device can work up to 19 hours. If the user connects to the Internet for surfing, the device will need to be recharged after 15 hours. And you can watch high quality video for almost 8 hours. It is recommended to use a power supply with 5V / 2A output to charge the smartphone.
