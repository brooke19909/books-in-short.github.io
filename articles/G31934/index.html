Materials and Tools

To make such a gift for mom on March 8 with her own hands, loving children will first need to collect the necessary accessories for work. Namely, you will need:

  • Ready pillow. If your financial resources are limited, you can buy the smallest, but ideally it is necessary to focus on the tastes of your lady. The most popular format is the so-called Euro-size, which is 50 x 70 centimeters. You can find it in any bedding store or order it online.

  • Whatman and pencil to sketch a sketch of a future print.

How to do it?

This creative gift for March 8 with your own hands is created according to the following scheme of work.

  1. Sketch a sketch of an invented drawing on whatman paper. Ideally, it should be the same size as the pillowcase itself. So it will be easier for you to understand what size its parts should be and adjust to the necessary parameters.
  2. Now apply the image to the fabric using paints or markers. You need to be extremely careful, because fixing the flaws in this work is extremely difficult. If you are not confident in your abilities, it is better to practice several approaches to hone each movement.
  3. Let the paints dry. Put a pillowcase on the pillow, wrap it with gift paper and ribbon - and the creative gift for March 8, made by yourself, is ready.




Homemade soap

This craft is an analogue of an expensive cosmetic product. It takes less money to purchase its ingredients, but the creation process takes several days. This product is very individual, you can use in it exactly those components that your lady loves the most.

In addition, each girl knows that it takes a lot of time and effort to make it. Therefore, having made such a present, you can show the full extent of your love for a woman, and that nothing can stop you from striving to make her pleasant.

What does it consist of?

So how to surprise mom? Do-it-yourself creative gift in the form of an exclusive soap can be made using the following ingredients and tools:

  • A transparent or white base, which can be used as the simplest baby soap, as well as a special mixture bought in the needlework shop.
  • Base oil. As a rule, almond is used, but if you want, you can choose another option.
  • Essential oil. It should be chosen not only for its pleasant aroma, but also for the properties that it possesses. For example, lavender calms, and orange tones. In addition, you should definitely know that your lady is not allergic to this component.




  • Dyes. You can use either powders specially formulated for this purpose or natural ingredients such as fruit juices and cocoa.
  • Additional additives. Choose them at your discretion. It can be dried flowers, honey, glycerin and any other components that you consider necessary. Such ingredients will become a source of bonus qualities of soap. It, for example, can become moisturizing or healing, etc.
  • Dishes with which you can organize a water bath. For this purpose, any metal pan and ceramic bowl, the diameter of which allows it to stay on weight between the walls of the first, is suitable.
  • Cookie cutters. You can buy both special for soap and take advantage of improvised means - children's for sand and for baking dough.
  • Milk or cream.

Work process

Do-it-yourself creative gift for March 8, ideas for which are not inferior to ready-made presentations, can be made as follows:

  1. Finely chop or rub the soap base . This is necessary so that it melts better.
  2. Melt it in a water bath. In this case, the composition must be constantly stirred so that it does not burn.
  3. Add the base oil. For 100 grams of the base, it accounts for 3 teaspoons.
  4. Add some milk or cream, and then carefully mix all the ingredients.
  5. When the mass becomes liquid and homogeneous, remove it from the heat and add a couple of drops of essential oil, not more than a teaspoon of glycerin, dyes and other selected additional components.
  6. Pour the mixture into the molds and leave it in a cool place for a couple of days, until it completely hardens.
