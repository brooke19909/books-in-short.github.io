Magic Garden Resort 4. Comfortable hotel complex

Description: In 2002, in the center of the popular Kemer resort, located 50 km from Antalya and the airport, the comfortable Magic Garden Resort complex was built - a snow-white four-story building with a well-groomed green area and a figured pool. Shops, bars and discos are located near the hotel, and a private beach, 1.7 km away, can be walked or take a free bus.



Magic Garden Resort 4, which was renovated in 2012, is ideal for a relaxing family or active holiday.

Rooms: Of the 67 hotel rooms, 63 are classic rooms with air conditioning, a balcony, a telephone, and 4 are comfortable suites. All rooms have en suite facilities, a hairdryer and hygiene products. There is Wi-Fi internet access and a paid minibar with chilled drinks.

Three times a week, a change of linen awaits guests. Wet cleaning come to do daily. Pets are not allowed.

Meals: Magic Garden It offers all-inclusive service and provides breakfast, lunch, lunch, afternoon tea and dinner in a self-service format - buffet. Tables are set both in the restaurant and on the street. To choose a drink, guests can head to the lobby or pool, where a wide range of bars are open.
