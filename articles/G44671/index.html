Location

For the construction of the residential complex "Matreshkin Dvor" in Novosibirsk, the Kirovsky district of the city was chosen, namely the intersection of Petukhov and Shmonin streets. Technology

Matreshkin Dvor in Novosibirsk, a photo of which you can see in our material, is a complex built using modern technologies and high-quality materials. The construction was based on panel technology.
