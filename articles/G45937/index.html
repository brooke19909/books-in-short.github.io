Cafe "Peacock", Bryansk - description, menu, reviews

Recently, many new cafes have opened in Bryansk, one of which is the Peacock cafe. For Bryansk, this institution is unique due to its identity. Here you can not only eat, but also enjoy the views of luxurious and noble birds.

Cafe "Peacock" (Bryansk)

This institution has appeared in the city recently, but has already been able to establish itself on the positive side. The love of visitors to the Pavlin cafe in Bryansk was won by the strict observance of a number of principles. Firstly, the dishes here are prepared exclusively by professionals. Secondly, the institution seeks to maintain excellent service. The third principle of the cafe is affordability.

Also for maximum comfort of guests on the territory of "Peacock" there is a parking with video surveillance and a luxurious landscaped area with green spaces and artificial ponds. For a more multifaceted vacation, you can sing in karaoke, visit the sauna or bowling and billiards rooms.

Local menu

The menu of the cafe "Peacock" (Bryansk) is distinguished by a variety of different dishes. The menu of this institution includes:

  • Cold appetizers - as a snack in the restaurant you can order a cheese plate of four types of cheese with grapes and honey. They also offer to try beef carpaccio, tongue slicing, caprese. Assorted cafe "Peacock" in Bryansk serves meat, fish and vegetables.
  • Hot snacks - several types of potato pancakes and julienne are waiting in the wings at the Peacock Cafe. For hot, it is also worth ordering delicious Milanese eggplants.
  • Beer snacks - for lovers of a hoppy drink, the cafe provides savory sausages, delicious shrimps, croutons and nuggets. Also, envelopes from pita bread and suluguni will not leave anyone indifferent.
  • Soups - for the first course there are three types of soup. Classic noodle soup, unusual cheese and delicious cream of champignon soup will satisfy even the most demanding client. A rich and satisfying borsch is also available to order.
  • Salads - the list of these dishes can be listed endlessly. The Peacock serves such classics as Olivier, Greek, Caesar, and delicious new products in the form of salads Old Munich and the Burgermeister.
  • Hot dishes based on meat - beef stroganoff, shank, roast, roast and other delights of Russian cuisine are prepared by the excellent cooks of the Pavlin cafe in Bryansk.
  • Pasta - spaghetti, fettuccine and farfalle.
  • Hot fish dishes - here the guest will have to make a difficult choice between such fish as mackerel, salmon, sea bass, red-eyed, dorada and cod.

Also in the cafe serves dishes prepared on the grill. Barbecue is prepared from pork, salmon, chicken and lamb. As a side dish, guests choose between different types of potatoes and vegetables. Cherry Temptation is the undisputed leader among the desserts of the Peacock cafe in Bryansk.
