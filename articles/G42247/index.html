Curious facts

  • At the site of the park was once a city beach.
  • A large number of trees and shrubs, including pine forest, grow here.
  • Despite the fact that the park is still very young, it was opened in 1975, restoration work has already taken place in it. The update was carried out under the guidance of the famous French architect Olivier Dome.
  • In 2015, "Scarlet Sails" in Voronezh entered the top five of the best parks in Russia.
  • The largest city events are held here.
  • The territory of the park is more than six hundred thousand square meters.

What to do in the Scarlet Sails Park in Voronezh?

It is best to come here in the early morning, because you will not notice at all how time will fly by. You may ask what you can do here? There will be enough entertainment for a wide variety of tastes. Judge for yourself:

  • Here you can get to an interesting place called "Bird City". Birdhouses with such names as: "Theater", "Phone", "Police" and others are hung on trees. Everything is like people have. Visitors to the park love to walk and take pictures here.
  • Ride a bike or roller skate? Easily. The park has special tracks for this.
  • You can sit on a bench in the summer theater, which very often hosts various performances by local artists. Celebrities from other countries come here. For example, several years ago, a Haitian Marlene Dorsen performed here.
  • It is tempting to go to the cafe, which is located in the park. Enjoy delicious desserts and a cup of aromatic coffee or tea.
  • In the chess house you can play your favorite board games or read the books that are here.
  • There is Wi-Fi in the park, so Internet lovers also have something to do.
  • There is an opportunity to get acquainted with the historical past of the city. On the central avenue there are a large number of information stands.
  • Play tennis or basketball, as well as play sports on the installed simulators.
  • It‚Äôs easy to rent boats or catamarans that you can rent.
  • You can sunbathe on the beach, but remember that swimming is strictly forbidden.
  • Children enjoy spending time in a special area. Here for them a real wooden castle was built, in which there are slides, swings and much more.
  • You can ride on various carousels or jump on trampolines.
  • For older children there is a rope town and a climbing wall. These attractions are very popular not only among children, but also among adults.
  • There is a huge number of picturesque corners and interesting figures, next to which you can take pictures.




"Scarlet Sails" (Voronezh): how to get there

The park's address is 93 Arzamasskaya Street. You can get here in many ways. Choose which one you like best:

  • Bus number 120B, 122, 34, 91 and others.
  • Trolleybus No. 11, 4.

Stop - Arzamasskaya street. From here to the park is a stone's throw away, and any passerby will tell you the direction of movement. In any case, you definitely will not pass by the Scarlet Sails park in Voronezh.

Opening hours and visiting rules

It works seven days a week, from 07:00 to 23:00. Before you enter the park, pay attention to the rules that are written on the sign hanging in front of the entrance. Observe them necessarily. The rules are far from complicated:

  • Do not drink alcohol;
  • Do not walk dogs;
  • throw garbage in strictly designated places;
  • smokers and intoxicated persons are strictly prohibited;
  • flowers and plants are forbidden to tear;
  • You can not launch various salutes, fireworks, sky lanterns.
