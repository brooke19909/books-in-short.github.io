  • good location in the forest zone, but not very far from the city;

Classic Hotel

On Kuznetskostroevsky Prospect, 21 is located the Classic Hotel in Novokuznetsk. This is a comfortable hotel, the interior of which is designed in an elaborate palace style. It is interesting that each room has a unique individual interior. The hotel offers its guests the following:

  • guest rooms of various categories from standard to VIP (from 2500 rubles per day);
  • hearty breakfast included in the price;
  • restaurant of European and Russian cuisine;
  • business lunch;
  • a bar with alcoholic drinks and snacks;
  • a pool table;
  • Finnish sauna
  • beauty salon (across the street from the hotel).

Guest reviews

Guests who visited the Classic Hotel leave such positive reviews about this establishment:
