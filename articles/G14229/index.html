Cheap electronic watches: an overview of models, features and reviews

The modern world is filled with an incredible variety of events and phenomena that are rapidly replacing each other. Often a person, especially a busy one, literally loses his head, gets confused in his plans, forgets many things because of stressful conditions. Therefore, various devices that can provide great support come to the rescue. Among them are watches. They allow you to keep track of time and rationally distribute your actions. In this article, we consider options for cheap electronic watches.





Wrist Watch. Concept and types

Before moving on to the main topic of our conversation, we need to deal with its main theoretical components. A watch is a small device with a strap showing the current time, as well as some other data related to this category (date, day of the week, stopwatch, etc.).

A watch is divided into three types:

  1. Mechanical.
  2. Quartz.
  3. Electronic.

Our task is to consider the last view. A wristwatch is a device in which a clock is used by a generator that converts its periodic oscillations into special signals that repeat after one second, minute or one hour, respectively. To purchase such a device, you need to know its functional components.





Functional characteristics of electronic watches

Under the functional characteristics understand the combination of mechanisms and parts that determine the operation of the device and ensure its protection. Electronic watches have the following characteristics:

  1. The dial . The main component in general of any watch. It shows time and other additional information. In our case, the dial will always be electronic, may vary in size, color of the displayed numbers.
  2. Power source Simple models use a regular battery, while more advanced models use a battery.
  3. Glass . This component is located on top of the watch and protects its internal parts from mechanical stress. Glass can be plastic, mineral, sapphire.
  4. Shockproof . This characteristic shows the survivability of the watch with strong or weak shaking of the device.
  5. Water resistant . The parameter shows the degree and quality of the tightness of the device, protects it from penetration into the water and moisture.
  6. Additional options . These include the backlight, alarm clock, stopwatch, heart rate monitor, etc. The more expensive the watch, the more such options.

Price categories

The price of the device determines the quality and availability of additional options. If you are ready to give an impressive amount, you will get not just a watch, but also a small wrist computer that can show the weather, make calls to friends, and monitor your health. A vivid example is the well-known Apple Watch or Android Wear.

For the average amount of money you can buy a regular accessory that will be made at a decent level and will not leave any defects upset for its buyer. But cheap electronic watches, on the contrary, are inexpensive, but require great care in handling, since budget materials can make themselves felt in cases of neglect. Despite these peculiarities, such devices are very popular in the consumer goods market and are in great demand. It's time to talk about them.

Cheap electronic watches. Pros and cons

When it comes to inexpensive goods, there are always doubts and concerns even when thinking about buying. But do not rush, because these devices have a lot of advantages. Here is some of them:

  1. Saving . This is the main and undeniable plus - in any case, the buyer gives a little money and thereby does not particularly reduce his budget.
  2. Rationality . Often, electronics manufacturers complement their devices with the latest technological innovations, which not everyone needs. For example, a person is unlikely to need the option of measuring his heart rate if he only needs to find out the time and set the alarm.
  3. Efficiency This concept should be attributed not so much to the watch as to the purchase itself. For a small price you can buy a good accessory that can satisfy the basic needs of the buyer.
