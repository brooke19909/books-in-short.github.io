The recreation center "Flora Park" in Solnechnogorsk

The capital of Russia is a huge city, which is becoming more and more every year. There are enough parks in the city, but they are all surrounded by industrial or residential buildings - this makes it difficult to plunge completely into the atmosphere of relaxation. This problem can be solved by visiting holiday homes in the Moscow region, for example, you can visit Flora Park.





Location

The base is located in the south-west of the Moscow region, in the Solnechnogorsk district. Not far from the park is the famous Istra Reservoir, which was built in the 17th century. Full base address: Solnechnogorsk district, Trusovo village.

Ways to get there

You can get to the park on your own using your own car. For this, it is necessary to leave from the Moscow Ring Road to Pyatnitskoye Shosse, and then head towards Solnechnogorsk. After about one hour drive, there will be a Rosneft gas station on the left, after which you will see a sign towards the village of Sokolovo. Before leaving the village, turn left onto the country road that leads to the village of Trusovo. And after 3 km you will reach the gates of Flora Park.





To travel by public transport you need to get to the Kryukovo station from Moscow. This station is located in the city of Zelenograd. From the Kryukovo station to the village of Trusovo, you can take the number 403 bus directly. Bus number 497 takes you to the village of Sokolovo, from where you can reach the park in 20 minutes. Buses to Solnechnogorsk leave daily from the Voykovskaya metro station , from where a 497 bus runs to the village of Sokolovo.

Istra Reservoir

This is one of the largest and at the same time oldest reservoirs in the Moscow Region. It was created at the beginning of the last century for water supply of the capital. The basis of the reservoir is a dam on the Istra River, built in the small village of Rakovo. Vacationers in the Flora Park can enjoy the magnificent scenery of the area, crystal clear water and an attractive temperate climate. The only drawback of the river is that Istra does not have access to the open sea.

Recreation center "Flora Park"

It is located in the thick of coniferous forest, on the banks of the Istra River. On the base you can live in comfortable guest houses, cottages and in the main building of the hotel. Only two guest houses: for 12 and 14 people. Each of them is a two-story wooden building, equipped with bedrooms, bathrooms and toilets. Each bedroom has single or double beds. On the ground floor of the houses there is a TV room, a swimming pool, a billiard room, and a kitchen. The cost of living in a guest house is 28 thousand rubles per night.

Family cottages are located on the river bank and can accommodate 8 people. The house has 4 bedrooms with beds and a TV. The price of one night will be 10 thousand rubles. In the main building there are about 50 economy class rooms. The cost of living varies from 2 to 5 thousand rubles.





On the territory of the recreation center "Flora Park" in Trusovo there are many opportunities for entertainment: a boat station, a sauna, billiard rooms, summer gazebos, a sports ground, a stage for performances and a banquet hall. The price includes meals in the dining room "Flora Park". For a fee, guests can use gazebos with barbecue facilities for self-catering.

The recreation center administration provides weddings, birthdays, corporate parties, concerts, anniversaries, weekends in the suburbs.
