The main advantages of the card "Visa Classic" ("Sberbank")

Plastic cards entered our everyday life not so long ago, but have already taken strong positions, allowing us to save time every day and providing round-the-clock access to financial management. This service is the most popular among the population, as it allows you to independently and at any time do the necessary operations: transfers, payment for services, non-cash payments, etc. Plastic cards help to save a lot of time and free it up for matters more important than queues or waiting.





One of the most popular cards among the population is "Visa Classic". Sberbank, one of the oldest and most reliable banks in Russia, offers to arrange it in any of the branches. At the same time, customers get the opportunity to take advantage of bonuses and special promotions.

Conditions for obtaining a card "Visa Classic" ("Sberbank")

To become the owner of the card, you do not need so much. A client can become a citizen of the Russian Federation over the age of 18 years, having a permanent residence permit in the country. Moreover, when issuing an additional card, the minimum age is 10 years. In order to start registration, you must fill out an application in person or on the Internet, present your passport and sign all the necessary documents. The application is considered approximately 2 business days, after which the client is informed of the decision.





Features and advantages of the card "Visa Classic" ("Sberbank")

When preparing documents, customers can choose the currency of the account: Russian rubles, Euros or US dollars. Validity - 3 years. At the moment, all cards issued by Sberbank are equipped with a chip to protect information. This will help protect the client and his savings from unauthorized spending. For each card, you can draw up additional financial products. Many other benefits are also available to customers when contacting Sberbank. The Visa Classic card allows everyone to become a member of loyalty programs. In addition, you can receive discounts and bonuses from partners of the bank.
