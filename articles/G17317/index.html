Independence Palace, Minsk: address, description, excursions

The capital of the Republic of Belarus, Minsk, is striking in its grooming. This is not a huge metropolis, but a very comfortable administrative center, compact and small, in comparison with the major capitals of the world.

Where is this building located?

The architectural gem is the Palace of Independence (Minsk). It is located near the State Flag Square. His address: Pobediteley Avenue, 12. Nearby is the BelExpo exhibition center.





In a short period of time, the Palace of Independence has become the hallmark of the capital. This is a great example of Art Nouveau architecture.

All entrances to the palace are equipped with modern metal detectors built into arched passages. As well as conveyors with x-rays, which are checked including trucks entering the territory.

Description of the Independence Palace, which is located in Minsk

The construction of the palace was finally completed about three years ago, its grand opening took place in October 2013. Around the palace there is a low elegant trellised fence. The new residence of the president is also equipped with protective devices. They are 100% likely to scare away birds and rodents from the building. The palace is equipped with a spare autonomous sufficiently powerful power supply unit.

Independence Palace (Minsk) is an imposing tall building with a glazed facade. Glass walls on the sides are interspersed with national ornaments.





In the middle of the facade is a massive wide staircase. On the central pilaster of the building there is an image of the coat of arms of the Republic of Belarus. The Palace of Independence (Minsk) is located, as it were, on a hill, which is a neat lawn. In front of the facade are three large pedestals with rather large stone bowls with vegetation on them. Directly between the fence and the building are very beautiful flowerbeds.

How is the building decorated inside?

Inside the palace, many rooms are decorated in the style of classicism. For example, the Hall of Ceremonies. From its windows a wonderful panorama of the State Flag Square opens. There is a unique picture gallery of the palace, most of the exhibits in which are gifts of honored guests, including heads of state, to the president of Belarus. The main hall of the Palace of Independence (Minsk) is decorated with a copy of the Slutsk belt, which is one of the symbols of the state. The hall of the building is striking in its beauty, brilliance, scale. For interior decoration, for example, stair railings, varieties of valuable wood were used. Lighting equipment for the palace, in particular chandeliers and lamps, was made by the best European companies.

Independence Palace in Minsk. Is the tour conducted?

In 2015, the inauguration ceremony of the president was held at the Palace. Negotiations of leaders of the largest countries on various issues, conferences of state and international level, and awards ceremonies for the outstanding services of citizens of the country are held here. In order to see the interior of a luxurious palace, you must send a request for a tour. But the mechanism of such events has not yet been adjusted, and only honored people of the republic or participants in international youth conferences, employees of the diplomatic corps of other states received visits.





So far, such tours are not conducted for a wide range of people. It is known that similar events were organized for students of the international forum held in Minsk. And also for fifty honored citizens of the city and region. This excursion group included doctors, teachers, athletes, scientists, production leaders.





The palace has a banquet hall, a winter garden and a helipad.
