Hotel Krabi Resort 4 * in Thailand: photos, reviews, leisure

Thailand is a unique country that amazes tourists with its delights and leisure opportunities. It is important to understand that the right choice of hotel is the key to an unforgettable vacation.

One of the most popular places for recreation is the hotel Krabi Resort 4 * (Thailand / Ao-Nang). Further details about the features of living in it, as well as the basic principles of servicing guests.

General information

Krabi Resort 4 * is the only hotel on Ao Nang Beach, which, located on the first coastline, has a private beach. It is worth noting that tourists are attracted to it not only by its good location, but also by its territory covered by green spaces.

The hotel in question was built in 1982, and the last renovation of the main building and buildings located on the territory adjacent to it was made in 2017.

Tourists vacationing here highly appreciate the level of service and living conditions at the hotel. According to their reviews, Krabi Resort 4 * has a rating of 8.5 out of 10 (according to the Booking portal), which is an excellent indicator.

Location

Many comments left by tourists about the hotel in question indicate that it has an amazing location and, it is worth noting, its advantage is not only in proximity to the beach.

Hotel Rabi Resort 4 * (Thailand / Krabi) is located almost 150 km from the international airport (Phuket) and a 35-minute drive from the local air traffic hub. It is worth noting that all tourists with trips to this place for recreation can use the paid transfer service, which should be ordered in advance. All those who did not provide for a transfer on their tour have the opportunity to get to the hotel by taxi.

Most travelers who visit the hotel can appreciate its favorable location in relation to the tourist area, which has a large number of places for entertainment (restaurants, karaoke, nightclubs, bars, dance floors and more).

As for the Krabi Resort 4 * (Krabi / Krabi), then this place for rest offers 215 comfortable apartments to the attention of vacationers. All of them are located in the main building, as well as in several villas, presented in the form of bungalows - the rooms in them are perfect for a romantic and family vacation.

Conditions for children

As mentioned above, the Krabi Resort 4 * hotel (Ao-Nang) is ideal for families. This belief is due to the fact that on its territory all the conditions necessary for a joint rest of the kids and their parents are offered. What special conditions are prepared at Krabi Resort for the youngest guests?

It is worth noting that the hotel in question has a large and safe aquazone designed specifically for bathing children. Slides are installed in it, and a system for maintaining the water temperature at a comfortable level is also provided. Near the aquazone there is a large playground for outdoor activities, which is also very popular with kids.

The kids restaurant has a large menu that includes vibrant and healthy dishes. Moreover, in the catering establishment you can watch highchairs and a special corner for games.

As for the comfort of living, upon prior request, a cot can be installed in the hotel room for guests aged 3 to 12 years. This service is free.

If necessary, on a paid basis, parents can order a nanny for the child. Her work is carried out by reservation and is paid hourly.

Hotel Service

For all guests the hotel offers high-quality service, which is carried out by well-trained staff, among which there are Russian-speaking staff.

Attention tourists Krabi Resort offers the opportunity to book tours at the tour desk. Experienced guests recommend applying for his services only as a last resort, since the cost of the excursions presented here is much higher than when ordering them in a travel package during the purchase of a ticket.

The Krabi Resort Hotel has a 24-hour reception desk, behind which there is an administrator at any time of the day or night, ready to answer any questions about leisure, accommodation and service. It is worthwhile to understand that anyone who wants to can come here and order a taxi or consult about the location of city objects.

Among the additional services, the hotel in question offers:

  • ironing and washing;
  • use of guarded car parking;
  • free wifi
  • ATM;
  • currency exchange point;
  • transfer.

On the first floor of the main building of Krabi Resort there is a medical office, in which a doctor is on duty every day. Its services are paid and are available only upon request.

Entertainment

Krabi Resort Hotel offers a wide range of entertainment designed for tourists with different wishes. In particular, here you can visit the rooms designed for playing billiards, chess and table football. For fans of a sports lifestyle in the open area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe hotel there are several sites for team games.

Hotel guests can rent bicycles. As experienced tourists note, they can travel around the neighborhood, exploring local attractions. The hotel also has a diving club, access to which is paid.

In addition to all of the above, the hotel has a celebration service, the staff of which can always help cheerfully celebrate any holiday at the Krabi Resort.

Every day the hotel hosts entertainment programs in which everyone can take part. At this time, the guests are entertained by a team of English-speaking animators, consisting of five people.

Spa

Krabi Resort has a small spa area in which specialists are ready to carry out a whole range of procedures for body and hair. In reviews of local masters, guests of the hotel in question very often notice that talented masseurs work here, ready to please everyone with a real Thai massage.

In addition to massages, the spa offers a wide range of cosmetic body treatments. All of them are provided only for a fee.

Nutrition

As for food, guests of the Krabi Resort can visit the The Boat restaurant located on its territory at any time. The menu of this establishment offers delicious Thai and international dishes. It is worth noting that every day in the main hall of the specified institution a large buffet is served, which everyone can visit. You can visit the institution from 6:30 to 10:00.

In addition to The Boat restaurant, the hotel in question has three bars: Junk Bar, Pool Bar and Skipper Pool Bar & Restaurant, the last two of which are located in the open water zone, and their menu offers guests a huge selection of drinks, fruits, desserts and snacks.

Beach

Most of the reviews left by tourists about the Krabi Resort Hotel indicate that a significant advantage of the place to stay is its location right on the beach. This allows guests not to waste time traveling from the hotel to the sea, as well as being completely safe during their holidays.

On the closed beach, everything is arranged for a comfortable pastime of visitors. In particular, there are large umbrellas, deck chairs, air mattresses, free towels and even a large cocktail bar, visiting which vacationers have the opportunity to quickly cool off with a tasty drink, as well as refreshment.

Diving and water skiing classes are provided along the coastline. Here everyone has the opportunity to rent a boat and go on a short trip across the ocean, which will certainly be remembered for a long time.

Cost of rest

Speaking about the cost of rest in the considered hotel in Thailand, it is important to note that it is at an average level. Subject to a 12-day stay, the price of a full travel package is about 110,000 rubles per guest. It should be understood that the indicated amount includes not only accommodation, but also a round-trip flight, food and even some entertainment. If you want to book a tour of any attractions, it is important to discuss this possibility with a travel agent in advance, since their price is not included in the indicated amount.

Other chain hotels

It is worth noting that the Krabi Resort 4 * hotel is part of the structure of the tourist network of the same name. Which includes other, no less popular places, designed for comfortable recreation and entertainment of tourists:

Let us further consider briefly the features of these places, indicating the reviews left by tourists in their address.

Krabi Maritime Park Resort & SPA 4 *

Built in 1995, the hotel today is actively attracting the attention of tourists from different regions of the world, even though it is located on the third coastline from the sea.

Attention tourists Maritime Park Resort & SPA hotel offers 221 comfortable rooms, as well as a lot of pleasant entertainment on its territory. The beach is located at a distance of 19 km from the hotel - several times a day a bus runs there, transporting vacationers free of charge.

The hotel offers excellent conditions for children to relax, which is why it is often a great option for a family pastime.

Chada Resort 4 *

Tourists praise this place very much. Of all the hotels in the Krabi chain, the Chada Resort 4 * is the closest to the Krabi airport (3 km). Also within walking distance from it there are places for entertainment and relaxation that tourists love to visit (Roots Rock Reggae Bar, Ao-Nang Mosque, Tipsy Bar).

In the reviews left by tourists about this hotel, one can often find comments that decent service is provided here, as well as comfortable rooms with views of the picturesque bay and flowering gardens.

On the territory of the Krabi Chada Resort 4 * there is an aquazone, a restaurant, a lobby bar, and free parking. Room rates include daily breakfast and Wi-Fi access.

Thai Village Resort 4 *

Another worthy option. According to many experienced tourists, an excellent choice for a family holiday is the Krabi Thai Village Resort 4 * hotel. The advantage of this place is the proximity of the hotel to the coastline (10 minutes walk).

The hotel has a traditional Thai-style d√©cor with stunning views of Nopparat Thara Bay.

The hotel has a large and beautiful garden, which has three large pools. Thai Village Resort also has restaurants and a large spa.

In the reviews left to the address of the hotel in question, it is often noted that its great advantage is delicious food in a local restaurant.

Tipa Resort 4 *

Within walking distance from the popular Ao Nang beach is a decent and very beautiful hotel. Krabi Tipa Resort 4 *, in their comments left to him, many tourists note that the great advantage of the hotel is its location in a very beautiful place, surrounded by green gardens, not far from the shores of the bay.

The apartments are furnished with natural wood furniture. Each room has a large furnished balcony, where you can have a great time sunbathing or reading a book, as well as drinking morning coffee.

On the territory of Tipa Resort 4 * there is a large pool, an international restaurant, as well as a spa center that offers real Thai massage.

Beyond Resort 4 *

The large and luxurious Beyond Resort 4 * hotel is the newest place for tourists to relax in the Krabi chain. This hotel was built in 2012 and during its short existence managed to gain a good reputation and great popularity.

Krabi Beyond Resort 4 * hotel is located in one of the secluded corners of Klong Muang Beach. On its large and green territory there is not only the main building, but also several two-story villas, as well as bungalows. According to the guests, this place is perfect for a romantic and family vacation.

As for the entertainment offered at the Beyond Resort 4 * hotel, among those it is worth highlighting the presence of a large open water zone, gym, spa. In addition, the hotel has a large restaurant.

In the comments of Russian tourists about the hotel Beyond Resort 4 * it is often said that the vacation spot offers excellent conditions, but it has one significant minus - its staff does not have Russian-speaking people.
