LCD "Gogol" (Gelendzhik): comfortable housing at a reasonable price from the developer

LCD "Gogol" (Gelendzhik) consists of two-section 6-story residential buildings of comfort class. This quarter is located just 500 meters from the sea in an area with an actively developed infrastructure and economic improvement. The complex is located in Gelendzhik on Gogol Street, 7. The developer is the Gelenstroy company. The final delivery of the residential quarter is scheduled for December 2017. Turnkey acquisition of real estate is now available.

Detailed project description

The project has a complex of 280 residential apartments with improved conditions. The layout includes studio apartments, one-room and two-room apartments. Available for sale housing with pre-finishing and installed heating system. 85% of apartments have access to a private balcony or loggia.
