How to fill out a mail notification in accordance with all the rules?

Almost all the inhabitants of the country at least once in their life received a notification from the postal service that the parcel had arrived. Such notifications are sent by mail, that is, you can see them in your mailbox for correspondence. What does this notice indicate? What must be done to receive the shipment stated in the notice? Can I refuse to receive a parcel? We will consider all these issues in this article, as well as talk about how to fill out a mail notification - many people are faced with the problem of correctly entering information.





What does the mail notification testify to?

Notification is sent to the recipient by the postal service in order to inform him that the parcel is waiting for him in the corresponding department. The formation of such a notice is made the maximum the next day by the employees of the company after the corresponding shipment has appeared on the shelves of the post office. On the front side of the form are recorded the recipient's data and information about the parcel itself (value declared by the sender and other parameters of the dispatch, for example, delivery cost). What to do with the information form?

Recipient's actions after receiving a notification

After a person receives a notice of parcel, he needs to perform the following steps:

  • Make sure that the notice is really intended for him - on the front side of the form there is information about the recipient (name and address).
  • Fill in the form - the fields for the recipient are located on the back of the sheet; You can perform this simple task at home, or, directly, in the post office where you should receive a shipment (how to fill out a notice of the Russian post will be described later).
  • Go to the post office - the address and the period of work are indicated on the form itself, taking with you a passport (of the person whose name the parcel arrived in) and the form.

Is it obligatory to receive a parcel?

To receive a shipment or not is decided by the person to whom it is intended. After receiving the mail notification, you can simply ignore it. In addition, if the person whose name is written in the notice of departure is not going to receive it and the question of how to fill out a mail notification does not concern him, then you can also contact the office where this box is located and officially refuse to receive it.
