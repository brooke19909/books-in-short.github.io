The best top-loading washing machines: rating, description, reviews

Indispensable modern technology today are washing machines, which save a lot of free time, which can be spent on third-party affairs and hobbies. Despite the fact that horizontal loading machines are incredibly popular, vertical loading washing machines are the best for many consumers. Such models are ideal for older people or those who have back problems - there is no need to bend over. In addition, according to experts, top-loading washing machines are the best technique for large families in which a lot of laundry accumulates: it is much easier to load it into the drum from above. Such models are practical and convenient, which is highly appreciated by the hostesses.

Rating of the best models of household appliances

Below is a rating of the best top-loading washing machines, according to numerous experts and specialists. They select the winners in terms of functionality and quality.

Zanussi

The first place in the ranking of the best top -loading washing machines goes to Zanussi. The device has a capacious drum for 6 kilograms of things. The machine is controlled from a digital display. A feature of Zanussi, for which it is in no small measure ranked first in the ranking of the best washing machines with vertical loading, is a large number of drum revolutions - 1200 when spinning things. The spin speed can be adjusted either automatically or manually for each of the washing programs. The forced spin stop function allows you to save things that should not be washed in the selected mode. The device is listed in many Top-10 washing machines with a vertical load due to the energy category A ++. The machine has eight automatic washing programs, but the advanced functionality and the ability to manually adjust some parameters significantly increase its capabilities. The maximum sound level during the operation of the machine is 58 dB, while it can increase by only 20 dB in the spin cycle. Almost silent operation is an indisputable advantage of Zanussi technology. The drum is made of high-strength and high-quality plastic. The presence of a timer allows you to delay the start of the program and the machine for a certain time.





Electrolux

The Swedish company Electrolux produces high-quality household appliances, including the best top-loading washing machines. Customer reviews on the model are positive: everyone notes the quality of the equipment, a capacious drum for 6 kilograms of linen, made of high-strength plastic. The housing of the washing machine has partial leakage protection, which avoids unforeseen situations. When spinning things, the drum rotates at a speed that can be adjusted by the user and at a maximum of 1300 rpm. the laundry is wrung out almost silently thanks to the automatic positioning of the drum.

For a full washing cycle, the Electrolux typewriter requires 0.15 kWh / kg, which is a very good indicator, due to which the equipment is ranked as A ++. The washing process is greatly facilitated thanks to 11 different programs, coupled with the ability to set a specific temperature. During the spin cycle, the noise level does not exceed 77 dB, during the entire washing process - 53 dB. The ability to use liquid powder allows you to wash things in cold water. You can start the washing machine at any convenient time thanks to the timer and delayed start options.





Indesit

The next top rated top-loading washing machine is Indesit's home appliance model. It does not differ in outstanding characteristics, which is fully offset by excellent quality and efficient operation. This model is ideal for those who choose a washing machine in terms of price - quality. The maximum capacity of the drum is 5 kilograms, in the spin mode - 500 rpm. The washing machine tank is made of high quality durable plastic.

A full washing cycle is very economical, since the machine spends only 52 liters of water. A digital display is not provided, but despite this, the control of the equipment is intuitive and simple. The lid and its fasteners are made practical and convenient, loading linen does not cause problems. There are only three specialized programs, which is few, but they work in good faith. The user can separately adjust the water temperature and the spin speed of the laundry for each program to avoid damage to things during washing.





Candy

The Italian company Candy is one of the leaders in the household appliance market, so it is not surprising that their products have been included in the rating of the best top-loading washing machines. Over the years, this model has received a large number of positive reviews from both experts and ordinary buyers. The manufacturer proudly calls EVOGT the best top-loading washing machine and has every right to do so: the drum holds 7 kilograms of laundry, while its maximum spin speed in the spin mode is 1300 rpm. There are 18 special programs, with each of them changing the temperature and the number of revolutions. Electronic control of the machine, switching between modes and temperature control are carried out by commands given from the digital display.

The washing machine has a good economy: it spends about 0.17 kWh / kg of energy and 48 liters of cold water in one cycle. The tank is completely plastic, of high quality and durability. The flaps of the drum cover open neatly and smoothly. The washing machine has a mixed mode in which you can wash different types of laundry without harming them. The timer allows you to delay washing for a maximum of 24 hours.

A distinctive feature of the model is its efficiency - for one washing cycle it requires 48 liters and 0.15 kWh / kg of energy, due to which it is assigned to the A ++ energy saving class. Bosch

The narrow washing machine from the most popular manufacturer Bosch actually has standard width dimensions, but due to its height it seems more slender and neat. The technique is practical, convenient and efficient. The operation of the machine is fully controlled electronically with LED indicators, while there is no display.

The model is protected against leaks thanks to a sealed housing and tank - the latter, by the way, is made of high-strength plastic. The drum holds 6 kg of clothes and linen, while in the spinning mode, the speed is 1000 rpm. There are 8 special washing programs, each of which consumes no more than 0.15 kWh / kg. The noise level during the spin cycle is almost minimal - only 74 dB, the rest of the time - 59 dB. The quality of linen with mixed washing does not deteriorate. The built-in timer allows you to delay the launch until 12 hours.





Indesit

Closes the rating of the best washing machines with a vertical loading device from the company Indesit, characterized by good quality and affordable cost. The huge advantage of this machine is 18 programs, including a special mode for curtains, which is very unexpected and useful. The number of revolutions in the spin cycle and the water temperature are set separately for each cycle: the maximum speed of the drum is 1000 rpm. Energy consumption of 0.15 kWh / kg per cycle, which corresponds to a high class of energy consumption.

The capacity of the drum is 5 kilograms, in addition, there is a built-in function to control the imbalance, which eliminates unnecessary noise and vibration. Adjustment of operating modes is carried out through a digital display.
