Wireless iron "Tefal" - a convenient technique for housewives

Every housewife is familiar with the situation: the laundry has dried after washing, and a long, exhausting work is waiting on him to iron it. This is not one problem. As a rule, you need to arrange the ironing board in such a way that it is convenient for you and there is an electrical outlet nearby. During operation, the cord may twist, interfere. The Tefal wireless iron will help out, which is very convenient. It makes ironing a simple and easy task. A popular manufacturer of household appliances offers this wonderful new product and guarantees comfortable work.

How iron works



The new model is unique due to the fact that it functions without a wire. Specialists developed it in such a way that it is not charged from an electrical outlet using a cord, but from a special charge. The base is attached to the ironing board. For twenty five seconds, the device operates autonomously. This time is quite enough for the Tefal iron to go through the workpiece several times before adjusting or turning it over. Then the device returns to recharge to the base.

Instrument Specifications




For hostesses, this device will be simply indispensable. The iron has a powerful steam boost, there is a special button on the casing to adjust and feed it. This function can be used for clothes that are hanging on a hanger. Using the Tafal wireless iron when ironing, you can not be afraid that it will burn or stain the laundry. The sole of the device glides easily over any fabric; this is facilitated by the presence of longitudinal lines and microholes. The pointed shape facilitates work when processing hard-to-reach spots. The anti-drip double system protects the laundry from getting water on it when ironing. How convenient is the Tefal wireless iron, any customer can appreciate it. The reservoir of the device is designed for three hundred milliliters and has a large hole to fill it with water. In addition, the device has a very convenient ergonomic handle.
