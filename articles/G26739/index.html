How to sew a summer dress with your own hands? Beginner's Memo

Someone's summer is over, while someone else is just ahead. It would seem that it's time to put summer dresses in a closet, but not for everyone. Due to the fact that many people spend their vacations in autumn or winter, people try to get out to the sea, beaches, and heat. What is needed there? Naturally, summer outfits. Unfortunately, a huge selection in stores does not always satisfy the needs of customers. A legitimate question arises: "How to sew a summer dress with your own hands so that it is perfect?" It is not so difficult!



First steps

Many modern ladies have already forgotten which way to approach the sewing machine. But in vain. After all, the perfect dress can be sewn by yourself. Yes, such that everyone envy. How? There are a few basic steps:

  • choose a model;
  • find a pattern;
  • pick up consumables.

The latter is especially important. Before you sew a summer dress with your own hands, decide on the fabric. It should be light, pleasant colors. It is desirable that she does not crumple too much, is opaque and strong enough. Not to the detriment of its airiness and lightness, of course. The fabric should be selected and threads of a suitable color. They should not be noticeable. Exception - the selected model may contain elements stitched so that they stand out. Immediately think about how the dress will be fastened. Buttons, rivets, zippers? Choose their color to match the fabric or, conversely, radically opposite - at your discretion.



Important stage

When all the previous steps are completed, proceed to the most important thing. Take your measurements. Those that need to be shot on the chosen style. How to sew a sheath dress with your own hands? First, measure your waist, chest, and hips. If it is closed to the shoulders, then the girth of the shoulders too. But for the summer, such a dress is not entirely suitable. When the measurements are taken, transfer them to the pattern. Where to find her? For example, ask the older generation. This option is not bad, because the mod has the property of returning. Surely my mother, grandmother, aunt somewhere were covered with patterns of beautiful models. Do not forget that it is worth leaving allowances, which then will play a good service. All details of the dress (skirt, top, sleeves, if any) are sewn on a typewriter. Previously, they can be swept away with hands, for fitting. Or stab with tailor's pins.

After the dress model is ready, you can start sewing on buttons, zippers and other things. The last moment is the attachment of decorative elements. If you think about it, you need to choose them before you sew a summer dress with your own hands. Evaluate whether they are needed at all or if accessories can replace them.





Advantages of independent sewing

Before you make a dress with your own hands, evaluate the advantages of this method:

  • You can choose any model and style;
  • the dress will fit perfectly;
  • low cost of work;
  • pride in oneself.

If there are very few sewing skills, you can ask for advice from more experienced craftswomen. In the end, there are many girls who are happy to share their master classes, give advice and help completely free of charge. And nobody canceled the sewing and sewing courses. Now you know how to sew a summer dress with your own hands. The machine and the advice of experienced seamstresses will help you with this! Good luck in your endeavors.
