Rest in Astrakhan, on the Caspian Sea: fishing recreation center "Dubravushka"

The cities of Russia in terms of the wealth of flora and fauna will make healthy competition to almost any overseas resort. Mother Nature generously endowed her native land with great blessings, which we enjoy and are proud of. More and more often, our compatriots choose the Caspian capital, Astrakhan, for their holidays. The outskirts of the city have long been considered a haven for fishermen and hunters.

Holidays in Astrakhan, on the Caspian Sea and the Volga River, are popular all year round. The coast of a huge lake is strewn with fine sand, surrounded by lush vegetation and relict plantings. In addition, many mud and mineral springs, which are known for their high healing power, are concentrated on the territory. Unlike the Black Sea resorts, Astrakhan beaches are not crowded with people, which makes it possible for privacy and relaxation.

Where to stay?

Numerous residential facilities have been built off the Caspian coast, from economy class to luxury apartments. It is easy to find an acceptable accommodation option when coming to Astrakhan. Recreation (the private sector is most popular with budget tourists) in the resort is affordable and convenient. Owners of apartments and houses offer to rent a room or a small house near the sea for any period.





Most often, cleaning is included in the price. Friendly tenants often offer guide services. If such housing does not suit you and your goal is fishing, then choose a recreation center somewhere outside the city. Almost all tourist complexes are equipped with parking, gazebos with barbecue facilities and a Russian bath.

You can rent the necessary gear for fishing. The Dubravushka base offers its guests to combine outdoor activities with a cultural and entertainment program. This is a great option for a family weekend with children on the banks of the Volga River.

Fishing recreation center "Dubravushka" (Astrakhan)

Not far from the city center, a cozy complex, designed for an audience of different ages, has settled down. With joy and hospitality, he throws open his doors year-round and offers an extensive range of services. Rest in Astrakhan (on the Caspian Sea, on the basis of Dubravushka) makes it possible to isolate oneself from the endless rhythm of the metropolis and feel like part of the surrounding nature.





In any season, guests can organize sea cruises, unforgettable fishing, hunting, walks along the protected places of the Volga River Delta. You will see with your own eyes how the lotus grows, feed the waterfowl, get acquainted with the biosphere reserve.

Accommodation options

Holidays in Astrakhan, on the Caspian Sea, will appeal to any traveler, especially if you stay at the Dubravushka base. Peak tourists fall in the summer months, so it is recommended to make a reservation in advance for a suitable room.





The following room categories will be offered for guests:

  • Room of the 1st category, designed for 4 people. This is a spacious apartment with a separate lounge (living room) and bedroom. There is also a small corridor with furniture, a separate toilet and a bathroom with shower. The room is equipped with air conditioning, which works on heat / cold. In the living room you will find a flat-screen TV with a DVD player. The apartments are ideal for couples with children.
  • Room of the 2nd category, consisting of a bedroom and a corridor. Furnished with upholstered furniture, appliances. Individual use of the bathroom. It has an electric kettle, a set of dishes, a compact refrigerator.
  • Room of the 3rd category (double), an exact copy of the previous room.
  • Summer cottages for two guests overlooking a picturesque green park. The house has the necessary furniture, air conditioning. True, the toilet with shower is located in a separate building.

Catering

Several establishments have been built on the territory where they will provide tasty and satisfying food. It should be noted that the recreation center "Dubravushka" (Astrakhan) does not provide food, you will have to pay separately for it. The complex's cafeterias are quite roomy, with live music, a karaoke room, a banquet room and a summer terrace. The menu is full of a wide selection of dishes. Here you will be treated to freshly baked fish according to the chef's recipe, and you will also taste local wine and beer.





At the base, fruits, vegetables and all kinds of greens are grown specially for guests. With a great desire, tourists can do their own cooking on the grill or barbecue. For these purposes, gazebos with benches and a table are allocated. Necessary household utensils and dishes are rented out.

Service and entertainment

Recently, rest in Astrakhan has received positive reviews. Locals and non-resident tourists say that the best places on earth are hard to find, especially for avid fishermen. The tributaries and eriks of the Volga are teeming with large trophies. It is for this that people from other regions come here.





In winter and summer, it is possible to catch perch, catfish, carp, pike, pike perch, roach or roach. Even a child cannot leave without a fish. Only in these places is it possible to catch a two-meter sturgeon and a beluga weighing up to 200 kg. Fishing rods, spinning rods, motor boats and other equipment are provided to help fishermen.





Holidays in Astrakhan, on the Caspian Sea, are another expanse for young children. On the base of the built outdoor play area with carousels, colorful characters. Many sports fields and tracks for active activities. For adults - table tennis, billiard room, outdoor pool, massage room, beauty salon. Departures outside the complex are organized for outstanding objects of the city.

What do tourists say about holidays in Astrakhan?

Reviews in general about the city are filled with positive. The mass of indelible impressions of what he saw remains in his memory for a lifetime. The atmosphere of goodwill contributes to a complete immersion in the world of carelessness and comfort. Successful fishing trips give the rest fullness.

A busy weekend program awaits you in the complex "Dubravushka" (Astrakhan). Holidays at sea (enthusiastic reviews of the base), according to most people, are unlikely to be compared to a fishing corner on the Volga. Picturesque landscapes, the rich history of the region, interesting leisure - this is only a small part of what awaits you in Astrakhan.
