Where is the main bus station of the capital of Ukraine

It is located at a distance of 318 meters from the Demievskaya metro station. So, if you suddenly need to come to the Central Bus Station (Kiev), his address is: Moscow Square, 3. Thanks to the help of the metro of the capital, do not be afraid to get lost. Five minutes on foot - and you in the subway, or vice versa, left the subway, five minutes passed - and already in place. In the building of the bus station, everything is done as expected: ticket offices for the sale of (preliminary) tickets, cameras work where you can store luggage, a first-aid post, mother and child rooms, a toilet, a waiting room, an electronic board with available seats and a bus departure schedule. If you want to eat, you can contact the fast food restaurant, which is located nearby.

It was built in 1957-1961. It is designed on the basis that it will send 600 flights and seven thousand passengers daily. The Central Bus Station (Kiev) is located next to a large transport interchange. Here converge: Druzhby Narodov Boulevard (from here you can get to the left bank of the Dnieper), Krasnozvezdniy Prospekt (from the Solomensky district), 40th Anniversary Boulevard (it leads to the Teremki district) and the latter - Science Avenue. From here, to the very center of Kiev - Maidan Nezalezhnosti - can be reached by metro in seven minutes, except for the time for descent to the metro and climb.

Central Bus Station, Kiev. How to get there

To get to this transport center of the capital of Ukraine, using city transport, you can use buses, minibuses, and trolleybuses. But for us, the most interesting way is to get to the Kiev Metro, especially from the railway station, because it is there that people come most often. From the Vokzalnaya station, you reach Khreschatyk, then go to the Maidan Nezalezhnosti station and arrive where you need to go to the Demievskaya station. The whole journey will take 20-25 minutes, this is taking into account the rise and descent into the subway. For comparison: opposite the main entrance to the railway station, you can take the shuttle bus No. 539. The journey will take a maximum of 20 minutes, but this is only if there are no traffic jams.





That is, if your time is not limited, then take the minibus, otherwise we advise you not to risk it - go by subway. From here, from the railway station, you can also take bus number 5. It leaves from the same place as the minibus, but rarely. Rides, if there is no traffic jam, 20 minutes. And another five minutes you need to spend from Izyumskaya street to the bus station. The option is beneficial if you have large bags.

Flights from Kiev bus station and the question of its abolition

This station sends a lot of flights domestic (in Ukrainian cities) and international, including to European countries. The following destinations are most popular: Belarus, Spain, Czech Republic, Bulgaria, Austria, Hungary, Estonia, Lithuania, Moldova, France, Russia, Greece, Italy, Germany and Poland. Ticket offices where you can purchase tickets are located in the main hall, and there are also ticket offices of carriers. In 2013, the issue of the abolition of this transport facility was raised.

It was planned to close the Central bus station (Kiev) and transfer part of the passenger flow to other Kiev bus stations. For example, the bus station on the Teremki, which was then being built, was to become part of its receiver, at the same time, it was planned to build several more similar ones on the outskirts of the capital. But these plans were suspended for well-known reasons, and now their perspective is unknown. More on this in the next section.
