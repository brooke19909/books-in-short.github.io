Holiday complex "Shepalovo"

The recreation center "Shepalovo" (Primorsky Territory) is located in the Noginsk urban district in the vicinity of Cape Povorotny. Guests settle in the capital two-story buildings. The apartments are offered in two, three and four places.

Number of rooms

Category "Standard". The rooms are furnished with a wardrobe, bedside tables, a mirror, a large double bed, a dining table with chairs. A shared shower, toilet and washbasin are located on the floor. Towels and towels are provided upon check-in. Cleaned in the apartment daily. The rules for living in the complex forbid cooking in rooms.

The recreation center "Shepalovo" (Primorsky Territory) invites guests to superior rooms. The double is furnished as "standard", but it has cable TV and a TV.





Superior rooms are triple and quadruple equipped with a TV, fridge, beds, a table with chairs, bedside tables, a mirror, a wardrobe. Toilet, washbasin with shower is located on the floor. Sets of bed linen with towels are issued to vacationers upon check-in.

For guests with plenty, the Shepalovo recreation center (Primorsky Territory) offers cozy and comfortable rooms - two-room Suites. They are designed for four guests. Equipped with double and single beds, TV, wardrobe, sofa with comfortable armchairs, a table and chairs. In one of the rooms there is a kitchen area with dishes for eating, a microwave, a spacious refrigerator, and an electric kettle. Upon arrival, guests are provided with towels with linen and an iron. Shower, washbasin, washing machine and toilet are on the floor.

Leisure facilities

Included in the price of the ticket: cleaning once a day, accommodation, once every five days, changing towels, and bed sets - ten and three meals a day.

Children under three years of age are not provided with a berth; payment is 400 rubles per day (utilities only). For guests from three to twelve years a discount is given - 20%.



The administration of the recreation complex allows you to bring small pets with you (you need to agree on this in advance).

In the summer, a sauna is open almost around the clock in the base.

Parking is located inside the base. It is guarded and paid.

All year round, guests are offered various entertainment programs and sightseeing trips at the Shepalovo recreation center (Primorsky Territory). Reviews, both about winter holidays, and about summer, joyfully happy. Here you can take several riding lessons from a professional trainer. There are also weekly sporting events, discos and competitions.

Leisure

The recreation center "Shepalovo" (Primorsky Territory) has a well-equipped volleyball court, as well as a football and basketball, a zone for playing in the towns. For younger guests there is an equipped playground.

For adults, there are several billiard and tennis tables at the base. Organized rental equipment, including sports. As well as boats, catamarans, and in winter - skates, sleds and skis.

"Sandy shore"

From year to year, more and more vacationers prefer the Primorsky Territory, Shepalovo recreation center. "Sandy coast" is located in a fabulous forest on the coast of a crystal clear lake.



Guests are accommodated in two-story log cabins. They thought out all the necessary conditions for a comfortable stay. There is cold and hot water, a kitchen area with a central sewer, a toilet, a shower with a washbasin. As well as a TV and cable TV. Near each house an arbor with a barbecue area is organized.
