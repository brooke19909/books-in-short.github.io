Picturesque vacation spots in Kurgan

The most picturesque place in the Urals is the city of Kurgan. Thousands of Russians and foreigners have chosen this place for a good rest with the whole family. Kurgan is also famous for the fact that in the very center of the city for viewing to locals and vacationers are displayed aircraft - airplanes and helicopters that can be viewed from the outside and from the inside.

Historical reference

The city was founded in 1679 by Timofei Nevezhin - a peasant who fell in love with these beautiful places. And after him people were drawn there. First, this area was called Tsarevo Gorodishche, then - Tsarekurgan settlement, and only in 1782 the settlement was named Kurgan.

Now the city is famous for its machine-building enterprises, of which there are more than ten, well-developed metallurgical industry, pharmaceuticals, and food industry enterprises.

Picturesque Vacation Places in Kurgan

The beauties of this land fascinate tourists. The fresh lake with the colorful name Dubrovskoye is a great place for a family holiday. The male half can enjoy fishing, the female - sunbathing.

Also a very popular place for mass recreation in Kurgan - Blue Lakes. Everything here is equipped for a comfortable stay on the beaches - and play areas for young vacationers, and cafes for older generations. On the territory are equipped toilets, ballot boxes, rescue teams work.

Another place where vacationers can cleanse their souls and pray is called Chimeevo. Here, everyone who wants to bathe in the holy spring or in the font, which is heated in the cold season. There is also the opportunity to stock up with holy water, which contains such a trace element as silver. At the monastery there is a hotel where people wishing to stay longer in this sacred place can stay, and the first three days (overnight and meals) are provided free of charge.

Entertainment

Naturally, rest in Kurgan will not be especially bright if you do not visit places where you can have fun. Families with children will be interested in a walk in the zoos, where visitors can see various exotic animals. Nature lovers can take a tour of the botanical garden and admire rare plants. Tourists who come to rest in the Kurgan are invited to actively and have fun in the amusement park, plunge into the world of extreme sports and get a vivid, unforgettable experience. And for lovers of gentlemanly games in the city there are places where you can play billiards.

Youth are invited to brighten up their holidays in Kurgan by visiting numerous clubs, cafes, discos.

And who does not like water parks? Perhaps there are very few such people. All kinds of water rides, slides, pools are pleased to bring to the attention of guests of the city the local administration.

Tourists are offered a huge selection of accommodation - from economy class rooms to suites in comfortable hotels, cottages, hotels. Vacationers will be able to book for their family the selected room for rest in the Barrow. The telephone number on which housing can be ordered (a recreation center outside the city or on the central streets) is indicated on the websites of the city.
