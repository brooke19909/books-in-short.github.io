Hyatt Place Dubai Al Rigga 4 * (UAE, Dubai city): description

On the territory of the Deira district in Dubai, the Hyatt Place Dubai Al Rigga 4 * hotel is located, where vacationers often stop to get a vivid experience. Deira is an amazing region of contrasts. Here you can see many attractions and wander through the old streets.

Address and Location

Hyatt Place Dubai Resort Al Rigga 4 * is located at: Al Rigga Road, 33178 Dubai, United Arab Emirates. Nearest airports:

  1. Dubai International Airport. It is 3.2 km from the hotel.
  2. Sharjah Airport - 20.3 km.
  3. Al Maktoum International Airport - 44.3 km.

Near the hotel there are many cafes, restaurants, attractions. After all, the Deira district is the center of Dubai, from where you can walk or get anywhere.

Just 0.5 km away is the famous landmark - the Deira Clock Tower. This is an original monument, which was erected as a symbol of the city.

Almost 3 km from the hotel there is a large mosque Grand Mosque, located between a large bazaar and a museum.

In addition, there are several large shopping centers, an aquarium, an underwater zoo, a skyscraper and many other attractions of the city nearby.

Terms of Accommodation

It is advisable to book rooms in the hotel in advance. As a rule, there are no empty seats on the day of arrival.

Check-in is allowed from 14:00, and check-out is strictly until 12:00. You can also come with children of any age. Moreover, if the child is under 6 years old, he is provided with a berth for free. Unfortunately, pets are not allowed, even for a fee.

If multiple rooms are booked at the same time, a certain discount system applies. This is discussed personally with the administration. Cancellation and prepayment policies vary by room.

After booking the apartment, funds are debited within three business days. Suitable cards for payment:

  1. AMERICAN EXPRESS.
  2. VISA.
  3. MasterCard.
  4. JCB.
  5. UnionPay.

Funds are blocked until the arrival of vacationers.

At the time of check-in at the reception, the guest is required to provide a card that was used for booking and an identity card with photo. Special customer requests can be fulfilled at an additional charge.

Hyatt Place Dubai Al Rigga: room description

The hotel staff offers comfortable and modern rooms that are decorated in warm and soothing colors. The simplest and most inexpensive with two single beds and one sofa bed. Another relatively cheap room with one large bed and one sofa bed. Such apartments are about 27 square meters. m

The hotel also has 48 sqm suites. m, where there is one living room and bedroom with one large double bed and a sofa. This room also has a dining area for 4 people. In addition, the hotel has many ordinary apartments and suites with pool views.

Each room with all necessary amenities. This is a bathroom with hygiene products and a hairdryer, a TV, an electric kettle, drinking water, an alarm clock radio, a mini fridge, an iron with an ironing board, a separate work space, slippers, a bathrobe.

Prices

Room rates vary by location, convenience and size. The minimum price per day is about 2 thousand rubles. If vacationers prefer breakfast in the room, then the cost is about 3 thousand rubles. The average price of a room per day is 5-6 thousand rubles. Of course, much depends on the time of year.

At the height of the season, prices rise significantly. For example, in Dubai in May, the weather is ideal for relaxation. In June it is too hot, and in April the air is still cool. Therefore, in May, the cost of rooms is much higher.

Nutrition

Of course, you can eat outside the Hyatt Place Dubai Al Rigga. Each tourist will find a cafe or restaurant to his liking. However, there are places to eat at Hyatt Place Dubai Al Rigga:

  1. Restaurant Coffee to Beverages Bar. Here the service is exclusively on the menu. You can come for breakfast, lunch and dinner.
  2. Restaurant Gallery Cafe. It prepares dishes from different cuisines of the world: Indian, Indonesian, local, international. There is not only an √† la carte menu, but also a buffet.

The hotel also has a children's buffet, coffee shop and bar. Each guest will be able to relax and unwind in the evening.

Territory

A comfortable stay at the hotel for tourists is guaranteed. The hotel has two terraces. One of them is for tanning, the second is just for relaxation. You can ask for barbecue equipment from the staff, but they are paid separately.

The hotel has many amenities: free Wi-Fi, indoor and outdoor pools, paid parking, fitness center, bar, tour desk, concierge service, shoe shine, shuttle service, smoking area, room service, ticket service , exchange office, ATM and night club with a DJ, but this entertainment is paid separately. The fitness center has modern exercise equipment with an LCD touch screen.

The hotel also provides dry cleaning, ironing, laundry and trouser presses. However, vouchers are not included in the price and are paid separately. To get to any attraction, you can rent a car.

Service

The hotel service is excellent. You can contact the staff at any time, and it will help to solve almost any issue.

The maids clean the rooms every day. In addition, linen and towels are changed. And if any bathing accessories are running out, the maids immediately provide them.

The staff speaks several languages: Arabic, German, English, Spanish, French, Hindi, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, Filipino. Therefore, guests and hotel workers have no communication problems.

Vacation with children

Of course, many tourists come with children of different ages. This is a hotel welcome. True, there is no babysitting service, but there are many children's entertainments. For example, a pool. There is not much water in it, just what a child needs.

In addition, right next to the hotel there are playgrounds where it is not very crowded and there is enough room for everyone. And the children are pretty loyal. The staff is calm about children's noise, which is important for parents.

Kids who are under 6 years old can live in the room for free. Especially for them, almost every room has a bed or sofa bed.

As for baby food, the menu has light dishes that are designed for both small and adult guests.
