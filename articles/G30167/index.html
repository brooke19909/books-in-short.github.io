Island Description

Crete is one of the largest islands in the Mediterranean Sea and the largest - in island Greece. Its area is more than 8000 square meters. km, the length of the coast is about 1000 km. Its geographical location is unique - the area where 3 parts of the world meet: Africa, Europe and Asia. Its shores are washed by three seas: Libyan, Cretan and Ionian. The capital of Crete is the ancient city of Heraklion. Here are the main international airport on the island. Another is located in the city of Chania. In the beach season, regular and charter flights to both of these air harbors are made from major Russian cities. Crete today is considered one of the most developed tourist centers not only in Greece, but throughout Europe. And this is facilitated by both geographical and climatic features, as well as the presence of valuable historical and natural attractions, of which the ruins of Knossos Palace, the medieval Fortezza castle built by the Venetians, the Samaria Gorge, the famous cave of the Supreme God Zeus, etc. are the most popular. the islands are Agilos Nikolos, Malia, Hersonissos, Panorama, Chania, Agia Pelagia, which houses the Blue Bay Resort Spa 4 *, and others.



Aghia Pelagia Village

This small, cozy tourist village is located on the northern coast of Crete, 20 kilometers from Heraklion. The sea in these parts forms a wonderful bay with clear water, and this is the biggest advantage of the resort. Compared to other areas of the island, the infrastructure is not very developed here, but for a quiet family holiday it is an ideal place. Despite ancient history, this area began to develop as a tourist center only in the mid-60s of the 20th century, and soon a beautiful resort was formed on the site of a fishing village. The low buildings of numerous hotels built in the traditional Greek style are arranged in the form of an amphitheater. And below the turquoise bay flaunts with white and golden beaches, which are the paths from the hotels. One of the best hotels forming this peculiar cascade of beige buildings is the Blue Bay Resort Spa.

Infrastructure

Therefore, if you can not imagine a vacation without an incendiary party, you better go to a more suitable place. But the Blue Bay Resort Spa and other hotels in this village are designed for a more quiet and measured relaxation. True, people with disabilities will also be uncomfortable here, because winding steep paths lead to the sea from hotels. However, representatives of this category of citizens can relax in the equally pleasant pool water, while admiring the beautiful sea views. But for museum lovers here is a real paradise. Guests of the Blue Bay Resort Spa 4 * hotel can visit the museum of the famous Spanish Renaissance painter, a native of Crete El Greco, as well as the Iraklinsky Historical and Archaeological Museum and the Cretan National Historical Museum. Well, fans of myths and legends can immediately go to the ruins of the famous Knossos palace and see firsthand what remains of the mysterious labyrinth of the Minotaur.
