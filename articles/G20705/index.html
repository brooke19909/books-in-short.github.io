Sousouras Hotel 3 * (Greece / Halkidiki): overview, description, beach, rooms and reviews of tourists

If we are talking about beach holidays in Europe, many tourists prefer Greece. This country has something to offer the tourist - it is historical sights, and an interesting culture, and, of course, incredibly diverse and picturesque nature.

One of the most popular tourist destinations in Greece is the Halkidiki Peninsula. Of course, on its territory there are a huge number of hotels and even entire resort complexes for every taste and budget. And one of the good places for a temporary stop is the hotel Sousouras Hotel 3 *.

Features of the location and description of the territory





Of course, when choosing a hotel, tourists primarily pay attention to its location. Where is the hotel complex Sousouras Hotel 3 * located? Halkidiki, Kassandra, the picturesque resort village of Chanyoti - it is here, just 50 meters from the sea, that this cozy hotel is located. By the way, the distance to the center of the town is no more than 100 meters.

Within walking distance are cafes, shops and even a shopping center. The nearest international airport is in Thessaloniki, 105 km away.

Sousouras Hotel 3 * consists of several five-story buildings, as well as small bungalows. By the way, the hotel was opened in 1984, the last restoration was completed in 2015. It offers not only spacious buildings and modern furnishings, but also a beautiful area where you can have a great time.

What rooms offer guests?

The hotel offers its guests a choice of one of 80 rooms in the following categories:

  • Standard rooms - spacious rooms with a sleeping area and a balcony, located in multi-storey buildings.
  • Family rooms - rooms consisting of a separate bedroom with a large bed and a living room with upholstered furniture and folding sofas. Their area is 27 square meters, and the rooms are designed for 3-4 people.
  • Family Bungalows - Large rooms located in separate bungalows. There is a bedroom with a sliding interior door (there is a double bed in this area), as well as a living room with a large sofa bed and two separate single beds. The rooms are designed for five guests.

Hotel Sousouras Hotel 3 * (Halkidiki, Kassandra, Chanoti): photo and room information





Regardless of which building or room you are placed in, you can be sure that you will spend time in comfort. The rooms have comfortable furniture and a spacious balcony with wonderful views of the coast.

All rooms are equipped with the necessary equipment, without which the comfort of a modern traveler is simply impossible. Guests can use individual air conditioning, as well as a telephone and radio. You can skip the evening watching satellite channels on TV. There is also a minibar with a fridge (drinks are delivered to the room only upon prior request).

Surely you will be pleased with the spacious bathroom, where not so long ago all the plumbing was completely changed. You can relax in a hot bath, take a shower, and at any time of the day, since there are no problems with water supply. There is also a toilet and a washbasin. Guests are provided with clean towels and toiletries, which are replenished every day.

Dining at the hotel: what can a tourist count on?





It is difficult to imagine a vacation without delicious food, because when traveling to another country, I want to enjoy not only the sun and the sea, but also delicious dishes. What does the Sousouras Hotel 3 * hotel complex offer its guests in this regard?

Paying for accommodation on the territory of the hotel, you yourself can choose a convenient meal plan for you, whether it is only breakfast, two or three meals a day. You can refuse the restaurant services at all (this point needs to be checked with the administrator at the time of booking).

Meals are held in the spacious hotel restaurant. Guest reviews suggest that the menu here is quite diverse - guests are treated to dishes of international and Greek cuisine. Breakfast includes eggs, sausages, salads, snacks, fruits and pastries. Lunches and dinners are more diverse - guests can choose from different meat and fish dishes. Seafood appears from time to time on the menu. It is worth noting that in the evening drinks are paid separately.

On the territory of the Sousouras Hotel 3 * (Halkidiki) there are several bars, as well as a colorful Greek tavern, which is definitely worth visiting at least once. Of course, you can eat on the territory of the town, where during the season a huge number of various catering establishments are open.

Where is the beach located? Entertainment and amenities by the sea





Many people prefer to spend their holidays basking in the gentle rays of the sun and swimming in the clear sea waters. What does the Sousouras Hotel 3 * offer its guests? Greece, Kassandra, Halkidiki - these are places that are famous for their picturesque beaches. And the hotel owns its own private beach area, located just 50 meters from the main building.

The coast here is covered with small pebbles. The entrance to the sea is convenient, and the depth increases gradually. Tourists are advised to acquire special shoes. By the way, guests enjoy free sun loungers, umbrellas and large, clean towels.

Of course, if desired, tourists can spend time more actively. Nearby is a sports center where you can rent non-motorized water transport (boats, catamarans). Also, guests love to water ski, banana or boat. Travelers have the opportunity to do parasailing, windsurfing, scuba diving and some other sports.

Hotel Sousouras Hotel 3 * (Greece, Halkidiki, Kassandra, Chanyoti): list of additional services





Of course, hotel guests can count on modern comfort and some additional amenities. For example, in a hotel you can exchange currency without any problems. At the reception you can get a free personal safe, which is always under guard.

You can also use local parking. By the way, renting a car, bicycle or any other form of transport on the territory of the town is not difficult at all - this is how most tourists travel around the peninsula. There are also several shops within the hotel, including a grocery and souvenir shop.

Leisure of guests: entertainment on site





Despite the fact that most guests prefer to spend days on the beach or in the city center, the hotel can also offer guests some entertainment. For example, a nice bonus is the presence in the courtyard of the pool with warm, fresh water. There are plenty of sun loungers around - guests can sunbathe, relax and spend time in pleasant communication.

There is also a tennis court, as well as a professional instructor who, for a small fee, will give some useful recommendations. For fans of active pastime, a basketball court is equipped on site. Also, guests have fun playing billiards and ping pong. In the evenings, everyone is invited to a disco club where you can dance to fiery rhythms.

Conditions for families with a child





Many people want to relax with their children, because kids also need healing sea air and vivid impressions. Of course, for parents issues of comfort come to the fore. Immediately it is worth saying that the Sousouras Hotel 3 * hotel is quite suitable for families with children. Several professional nannies work on the territory (for an additional fee), extra beds are brought to the rooms upon request, and the menu in the restaurant is varied - there is always something tasty and healthy for the child.

As for entertainment, here the hotel has something to offer. For example, kids love to splash around in a special pool with warm water. And for them a spacious playground and a playroom are equipped - the children really have something to do.

How do former guests respond about the hotel?

A brief conversation with people who have already spent several days at the hotel can be very informative. What do tourists really think about Sousouras Hotel 3 *? The reviews are mostly positive - this place is really good for a leisurely beach vacation.

The hotel area is beautiful and well-groomed, and it is always pleasant to spend time in the rooms - it is light and fresh here, there is the necessary equipment and furniture. The staff are friendly and hospitable, always trying to help and have a good command of English (some understand Russian as well). The menu in the local restaurant is sometimes a little monotonous, but there is always a choice, and the dishes are incredibly tasty.

The highlight in this case is the presence of its own quiet and clean beach. And very close by there are plenty of attractions and attractions.
