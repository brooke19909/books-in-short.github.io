Skater Alexei Urmanov: biography and photos

Skater Alexei Urmanov is a famous person in the world of sports. This man, who devoted his life to a career, was able to become a good husband, create a strong friendly family. His performances are watched with interest today, and the skater himself has long been engaged in coaching.

Biography

The future skater Alexei Urmanov was born on November 17, 1973 in St. Petersburg (at that time the city was called Leningrad). He gained his fame thanks to his mother, who brought four-year-old Alyosha to the rink in a small church on Vasilyevsky Island. The boy himself was not yet sure what kind of sport he prefers to practice, but obediently got on his skates. This influenced his biography in the future.



The boy was immediately lucky with the coach - Nina Nikolaevna Monakhova became his second mother. Immediately there was an interest in classes. The first coach, Alyosha, organized local competitions for her students and took them to international tournaments. A jump in career development occurred after entering the junior roster. The young man began to win medals and soon became a silver medalist at the World Championships (1991). After this performance took place in the adult team, where the guy took an honorable sixth place. In 1996, he received the specialty "trainer-teacher." Then Alexei Urmanov, a skater whose biography is inextricably linked with sports, began to occupy higher podium fame.

Sports achivments

Briefly imagine the career of Alexei Urmanov as follows:

  • 1990 - took 3rd place in the championship held in Minsk, accurately performing the quadruple jump (for the first time in the history of figure skating).
  • 1991 - became the champion of the USSR in this sport.
  • 1999 - took third place in the European Championship.
  • Since 2001, has been engaged in coaching.

Victories and Failures

After Alexei Urmanov became a famous person, he was expected to have a medal at each competition. Trying to live up to the expectations of his fans, relatives and friends, he gave all his best.



As in any sport, injury is possible in figure skating . They did not pass by and Alexei. Subsequent failures forced the skater to leave, he was escorted with applause, bombarded with flowers, asked to stay. And he returned, only already in professional sports. Skater Alexei Urmanov was greeted with no less stormy applause.

Personal life

Skater Alexei Urmanov has a strong friendly family. He is bound by marriage with his wife Victoria, whom he met while visiting common acquaintances. Interestingly, for a long time she had no idea about Alexei's stellar career. Victoria's friend recognized the famous personality. Three years after meeting the woman gave her husband twins - two boys. The couple called them Vanya and Andrey.

Authoritarian and democratic

Skater Alexei Urmanov, whose personal life has developed successfully, says about himself that he is different in raising children. It exhibits rigidity, discipline and democracy equally. Alexey made an attempt to introduce the boys to figure skating, but they did not feel the interest that their father felt in his time. Urmanov did not insist and gave his sons the right to choose. Twins attend some sports sections, but do not get carried away with anything serious.



pupils

The first student of the skater was Valery Vorobyov. Then another girl appeared who they already wanted to expel. A year after the start of coaching, Alexei was asked to work out with Angela Pytkina. Then the number of students continued to increase. The most successful was Sergey Voronov, who is a two-time champion of Russia. There is another outstanding personality in the history of sports - the Russian Jean Bush, who was also raised by Alexei Urmanov.

The skater whom he trains never forgets and speaks warmly of him. He uses his methods of education as a trainer. Alexey believes that not only a whip, but also a carrot is needed in the work. According to him, one should love his students, like all people in general, and treat everyone as a person. In addition, Alexei has no doubt that his students will pay him the same.



Not so long ago, Julia Lipnitskaya, Olympic champion in figure skating, asked to transfer her to Alexei Urmanov. According to her, she is grateful to the former coach, but believes that it is time to grow up the career ladder and achieve new heights in sports. The girl decided that Alexey Urmanov would help her in this. Skater, Lipnitskaya sure, able to teach her a lot. That is why they turn to him for help.

Alexei Urmanov is a skater with a capital letter. Assertiveness, love and interest in his activities allowed him to become a famous person in the world of sports, to win medals, to take first places. His performances are a small performance in such a complex world of figure skating. Now Alexei Urmanov is trying to put all his knowledge and experience into students. And he succeeds with it.
