Review of Lenovo A606. Characteristics and reviews

The Lenovo company pays much attention to the production of cheap smartphones. As a result, a series of budget devices "A" has become quite extensive. The representative of this line is model 606. What is capable of surprising the device?

Design





Inexpensive smartphones, as a rule, have a rather mediocre appearance, and the Lenovo A606 is no exception. The characteristics of the case come down to using ordinary plastic with a rough surface for ease of use.

The design of the device is similar to a number of models. It will be incredibly difficult to distinguish a smartphone among the brothers from the series ‚ÄúA‚Äù.

The color scheme does not improve the impression. The user is offered only a black and white version. Actually, the design does not stand out against the general background.

The location of the external parts is already familiar to Lenovo. The front panel has a display, a front camera, a speaker, sensors, a company logo and touch buttons on it.

On the right side is the volume control and the power button. The top end is reserved for the USB jack and 3.5 connector, and the bottom is a microphone.

The panel at the back of the device placed a flash, the company logo, camera and main speaker.

Unfortunately, one does not have to wait for an interesting appearance from inexpensive phones. The only thing that pleases is the build quality and the presence of an oleophobic coating.

Display





The screen installed in the Lenovo A606 smartphone will not be able to surprise the user. The characteristics are rather disappointing, or rather, the 5-inch display has a resolution of only 854 by 480. Even without looking closely, you can notice the pixels, in fact, this is not surprising at 196 ppi.

IPS matrix allows a little smooth overall impression . The device received decent viewing angles and shows itself well in the sun.

Another unpleasant moment of the display is the recognition of the sensor with just two touches.

Hardware





The company did not stint to install a powerful filling in the Lenovo A606 phone. Features emphasize the high-performance MTK processor with four cores. For a budget employee these are excellent options. And given the frequency of 1.3 GHz for each core, the smartphone will cope with many tasks. The Mail-400 MP video accelerator looks rather weak in the overall picture, but nevertheless it performs its duties properly.

A nice detail for a budget device will be the availability of a gigabyte of RAM. Among competitors, it looks like unprecedented luxury.

In the device of the company, as always, the built-in memory is lame. Of the installed 8, only 4.7 GB is available. However, it is possible to expand the flash drive up to 32 GB.

Camera





Most of the "A" series is equipped with 8 megapixels, they did not make an exception for the "Lenovo" A606 either. The characteristics of the photo are quite average, but there is no reason to expect more. The resolution of the camera is 3264 by 2448, and this is enough for everyday use.

The front camera has acquired a good 2 megapixels, allowing not only to make video calls, but also to make self-portraits.

System

The device works under the guidance of Android 4.4. It is he who occupies 3.3 GB of smartphone memory. The system is somewhat outdated and will not allow to fully reveal the potential of the hardware. Fortunately, updating the device with FOTA will be quite simple.

The proprietary Vibe UI shell is also present in the Lenovo A606. Interface features resemble Apple products. Games and applications do not have a separate folder and are placed on the desktop. To many owners, this seems uncomfortable.

Communication

In addition to working with the usual 2G and 3G networks, the device also supports LTE. This feature greatly improves the impression of the smartphone. The phone also has Wi-Fi and bluetooth functions.

Price

Considering what characteristics the Lenovo A606 has, the cost of the device is quite low - about 6 thousand. We can safely say that the powerful filling and low price do not leave competitors a great chance. This situation was due to the decision of the manufacturer to reduce the cost of their devices by 23 percent.

Autonomy

Under the cover of the smartphone you can find a 2000 maH battery already familiar from the ‚ÄúA‚Äù series. Such a battery will provide a day of life for the A606 in passive mode. When working actively with the device, the charge will last about 4-6 hours, depending on the functions used.

Positive reviews

Stuffing - this is the most noticeable advantage of the Lenovo A606. Characteristics allow you to use the device both for work and for entertainment purposes. The device will ensure the stable operation of almost any application.

A nice bonus in the form of 4G should also not be deprived of attention. Working with an advanced network takes the smartphone to a new level.

Low cost is another plus of such an advanced phone. Even some of the A606's predecessors have a much higher price tag.

Negative reviews

The main disadvantage of the phone is its low screen resolution. Even by the standards of state employees, the display was not the most successful.

A more common problem is the boring appearance of the phone. Naturally, you can‚Äôt expect delights from cheap devices, but all models in the series are almost the same.

Total

The disadvantages of A606 are fully compensated by the positive sides of the device. The phone is a great choice for the buyer. Once again, Lenovo products showed their best side.
