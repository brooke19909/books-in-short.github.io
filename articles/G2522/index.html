By the way, the guests of this hotel, as well as all the guests of the Jumeirah Group hotels (Bourge al-Arab, Madinat, Emirates Towers), admission to the water park is free. Wild Wadi (water park): prices

As in all advanced cultural institutions, Wild Wadi has a flexible pricing policy. A typical entrance costs 130 dirhams for adults, and 110 AED for children (4-12 years old). Translated into European currency, this equals approximately 27.6 and 23.4 –Ñ, respectively. But if you come to the water park "at sunset", then pay 100 dirhams for an adult and 85 for a child. This corresponds to 21 and 18 –Ñ. The concept of "sunset" varies depending on the season. In winter, it is three hours before closing time, that is, from 15 to 18.00. If you suddenly forgot at home bathing suits, beach shoes or sunblock, these things can be purchased at the "medieval bazaar" in the park. Towel rental will cost you ten dirhams (2.1 euros). The use of a locker for storing especially valuable things costs the same amount. When buying a ticket, they put a bracelet on your wrist that contains a waterproof electronic card. You can put any amount on it and then pay with it for food, souvenirs and individual attractions. Unused money is returned to you upon exit. When you visit the water park again, you are entitled to a discount.



Rides

Through the entire territory of the water park, the Juha's Journey artificial river flows 360 meters in length, which makes unexpected turns, forms lagoons and even creates an eighteen-meter-high waterfall. Twenty eight slides of different lengths work here - from 12 to 130 meters, and another 23 swimming pools, including those with an ocean wave. The total length of the entire water artery, tangled like a maze, is almost two kilometers. The water temperature in both winter and summer is maintained at +28 ¬∞ C. The main thing that attracts visitors to the Wild Wadi water park in Dubai is the Jumeirah Sceirah attraction. It represents the highest water slide in the Old World - 33 meters! Moreover, it is replete with all sorts of turns, and the raft with a passenger at the end of the path reaches a speed of 80 km / h. Those who think it is too much can ride the Master Blaster - eight slides connected to each other by one pipe, from which they move in single and double circles.



Vacation with children

It should be noted right away that some visitors are not allowed on some attractions. They are weeded out by height, checking the child with a scale on the wall: if it is higher than 110 centimeters, it passes, if it is lower - no. But Wild Wadi (water park) takes care of the leisure of its youngest guests. Especially for them, a shallow lagoon Juha's Dhow with a stranded ship is equipped. Many fountains, slides and waterfalls will not frighten the peanut, but will delight him. Nearby, there is a children's playground with amusing swing-carousels. If your young child is a brave dozen, welcome to Family Ride - an alloy on a large inflatable raft. There is also a slow river Lazy River, lazily flowing throughout the park.

Wild Wadi (water park): reviews

Most of all, visitors liked the fact that they did not need to climb, panting along the long stairs, so that then they would slide down a hill for a minute. You will be taken to the top of the attraction by a special device - "a waterfall, flowing the other way around." Everything looks like this: you get on the raft and rescuers push it to the pipe. Special nozzles push water upward, moving you too.

Guests also remember the pools with artificial surf. Waves sometimes reach 2.5 meters. This is where you can safely surf, because about fifty rescuers are constantly watching the vacationers. Visitors also joyfully noted the fact that, in addition to the Shahbandar's Quay bazaar with snacks, Sinbad's Galley eatery and Julshan's Kitchen restaurant, there are many picnic places in the water park. So you can stock up on provisions and go there all day.
