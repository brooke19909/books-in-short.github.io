Hotel Atlantica Gardens 3 * (Cyprus, Limassol): description, photos, reviews of tourists

Cyprus has long been loved by tourists from all over the world for its beautiful sea coast and incredibly beautiful sights. Many tourists flock here every year. An incredible number of hotels and complexes have been built on the island, designed for a completely different level of guests. And therefore, among a large number of hotels, everyone will be able to choose the most affordable option for themselves. In our article we want to talk about one of the budget hotels of this beautiful resort. Atlantica Gardens 3 * (Cyprus, Limassol) is located in the tourist region of Limassol.

A bit about the hotel ...

Atlantica Gardens 3 * (Cyprus, Limassol) is a three-star complex in Cyprus, located 100 meters from the center of Yermasmoia. The hotel belongs to the resort area of ‚Äã‚ÄãLimassol, and the Old Town is only five kilometers away. The beach is located 300 meters from the hotel.

Hotel address: Yermasoya, Cyprus, PO Box 52001. The nearest airports are located 70 kilometers from the hotel in the cities of Larnaca and Paphos. The bus stop is a five-minute walk from the complex. The region has excellent transport interchange and regular bus service. In the summer, transport runs from 7.00 to 23.00, and in the winter until 19.00.

Atlantica Gardens 3 * (Cyprus, Limassol) is a part of the Atlantic hotel chain. It is located in a quiet area surrounded by gardens, in the heart of one of the most prestigious areas of Yermasoyia. The hotel provides guests with a high level of service and a variety of recreational activities.



The complex has been operating since 1990, and since then, thousands of tourists visit it annually. Its complete reconstruction was made in 2010.

The hotel is focused on comfortable, economical family vacations, but the youth here will be very interesting.

Number of rooms

Atlantica Gardens 3 * (Cyprus, Limassol) accommodation in rooms of the following categories:

  1. Twin Room measuring 26 square meters. The apartments are designed for comfortable accommodation of two adult tourists and one child.
  2. Studio Room is 26 square meters. The rooms can accommodate three guests.
  3. One Bedroom Apartment is 52 square meters. The apartment has one double and two single beds. The room is designed for a family with two children.

All apartments are regularly cleaned and equipped with individual air conditioning. In total, the hotel has 96 rooms. The apartments have tiled floors. The rooms are equipped with TVs, satellite channels, a balcony or a terrace, a safe, a telephone, a bathroom with a hairdryer, a refrigerator, a coffee maker or an electric kettle.

Dining at the hotel

Atlantic Gardens (Limassol) offers its guests two types of food: half board and all inclusive. All Inclusive includes: breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks (from 10.30 to 12.00 and from 15.00 to 18.00), coffee, tea and sweets (from 16.30 to 17.30), locally-made ice cream, as well as alcohol and soft drinks of local production (from 10.30 and up to 0.00 hours).



There is a bar near the pool, there is also a Mocha Cafe, a restaurant called Agora Restaurant and a banquet hall for celebrations of 700 square meters.

"Atlantic Gardens" (Limassol) has a good infrastructure, so the rest for tourists is comfortable. Hotel guests can use not only all the benefits of the complex, but also visit the territory and all the facilities of the neighboring Oasis Hotel, as they are part of a single Atlaptik chain.

In Atlantica Gardens Apts 3 * (Cyprus, Limassol) there is a parking and car rental. A gift shop, jewelry and mini market are located on site. The hotel has an elevator, security, currency exchange, laundry, reception is open around the clock, so there is no problem with the settlement if you arrive at a later time.

Sports and Entertainment

Hotel Atlantica Gardens 3 * (Cyprus, Limassol) has a swimming pool with a well-equipped relaxation area with sunbeds and umbrellas. Daily animators conduct classes in water aerobics. The hotel has its own spa and wellness center. During leisure, tourists can enjoy massages, a jacuzzi, a bath, a sauna, a beauty salon and a fitness center.



The hotel has animation, live music. Vacationers can visit the Internet cafe, cinema, karaoke, take part in entertainment programs. Animators offer guests to play darts, futsal, billiards, volleyball, table tennis, archery, visit the tennis court, gym.

How to get to the hotel?

As we already mentioned, Limassol does not have its own airport. The two closest are in Larnaca and Paphos (both at approximately seventy kilometers). To get to the hotel, you can use a taxi, bus. Public transport takes no more than 45 minutes. In addition, minibuses run between cities. It is quite convenient to get to Limassol by water. For example, ferries from cities such as Beirut, Ashdod, Haifa, Crete, Beirut, Rhodes go here.



For those who plan to travel to the cities of the island, the most convenient means of transport are buses that regularly fly. You can also move around the city by taxi and public transport.

Around Limassol there are many beautiful wild beaches, but they do not go to transport at all. Therefore, your car here will be very helpful. The cost of renting a car is very affordable, so even people who prefer an economical vacation can afford it. Resort beaches

The beaches of Limassol are incredibly good. They have gentle descents to the sea and well-developed infrastructure. Most of the coast is sandy, but there are also sand and pebble areas. However, sand in this area is far from ordinary. It has a volcanic origin, and therefore contains flint, which has a beneficial effect on metabolic processes in the skin. The central beach of Ladies Mile is considered one of the best. It is equipped with everything you need for a good holiday. But the coastline here is quite narrow. On the beaches of Limassol, tourists are offered all kinds of sea sports and entertainment. The resort is incredibly popular, and therefore always crowded with vacationers.
