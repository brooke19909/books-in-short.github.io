Orka Nergis Beach Hotel 4 * (Marmaris, Turkey): hotel description, service, reviews

Like many Marmaris hotels, Orka Nergis Beach Hotel is simultaneously located close to the city center and the beach. Tourists who are regulars of this resort, position their pastime on it (including hotel accommodation) as a good vacation for the right price. The last repair here was made in 2014. Private beach, swimming pools, good rooms, excellent location - these are not all the bonuses of staying at the hotel. Getting into it, you are plunged into a special atmosphere, which, probably, is difficult to meet elsewhere in this country. And the real home comfort that will surround you during your vacation will give it a special charm.



Marmaris

This is one of the most unusual resorts in Turkey. So, having arrived in Marmaris, at the Orka Nergis Beach Hotel, you will immediately realize that this city is very different from Antalya and Alanya. Marmaris is not good palaces for tourists and not animation - it is a city of parties and walks. People come here to swim in the purest water, where the Mediterranean Sea merges with the Aegean, and in the afternoon seek for themselves entertainment. Starting from Icmeler, along all the hotels of the first line there is a promenade - a beautiful promenade. The closer to the center, the more it becomes lively. Music always plays there, various bars and restaurants work, and in the port there are snow-white yachts, one more beautiful than the other. Want exotic? Amazingly beautiful streets lined with white houses with terraces and tiled roofs twined with bougainvilleas of different colors lead directly up to the port, on a cliff. Upstairs is the fortress built by Suleiman the Magnificent, turned into an interesting museum. And at the highest point of the cliff there is an amazing coffee house with great views. Going down you will find yourself at Bar Street, where young people walk around all night until the morning. And on the main square of Ataturk in the evening, singing fountains turn on.

How to get there

To get to the Orka Nergis Beach Hotel, you must first fly to Dalaman Airport. From there, by bus or dolmush you will reach the city center. Directly from the airport - from the domestic flights hall - the Hawash company carries it. But such buses run quite rarely. You can take a taxi from the airport to the Dalaman bus station. From there you can catch a shuttle bus to Marmaris. And already from the resort station you get to the hotel with one of the city dolmushi. Using the same transfer, if such a service is included in the price of your ticket, you will reach the very gate of the hotel directly from the hub.



Location

Orka Nergis Beach Hotel is ninety kilometers from Dalaman Airport, where almost everyone who is vacationing in Marmaris arrives. It is located not too far from the historic center and the famous Bar Street. All these attractions and attractions are only one and a half kilometers away. It is easy to get there by one of the city minibuses, which constantly run along the highway leading to the Aegean Sea, or walk along the beautiful promenade along the sea and beaches. Near the hotel there are several supermarkets, both large and small - Kipa, Migros. Before each of them go 3-7 minutes.

These are five white buildings that stand on the very shore of the sea. One of them is six-story, the rest are slightly smaller. The promenade separates the hotel from the beach. Orka Nergis Beach Hotel: room description

How are tourists lodged in this hotel? The hotel offers guests almost two hundred and fifty rooms. 195 of them are standard, and 51 are family-run, which can also accommodate large companies. Any standard room can accommodate no more than two adults and one child. Its filling is usual for resort hotels - a room with a double bed, a shower room, a balcony, a minibar, a TV. Safe is paid.

The family room is almost the same as the standard one, it only consists of two rooms and has two more folding sofas. Some units also have a kitchen, equipped with a stove and microwave. Special beds are available for young children. Two bottles of drinking water are put into each room daily. The furniture is simple, but solid, not old, not broken. Air conditioning is not the newest model, but with manual control. Two Russian channels are shown on TV. The rooms are cleaned well, the bed is clean. There are tables and chairs on the balconies. Many rooms have direct or side sea views, while others have garden and pool views.



Services and entertainment

The hotel has free Wi-Fi. But he does not work in rooms, but at the reception desk. Tourists say that it catches best in the morning and before dinner, when there are fewer people on the web. Here you can rent a car, take things to the laundry and dry cleaning. Guests use the sauna, hammam, massage services. A fitness center and gym are available. You can play billiards and table tennis. What other activities does Orka Nergis Beach Hotel have? First of all, this animation, both daytime and evening. Although, of course, this is not Alanya, but in the morning you are not allowed to be bored. Games, contests, water aerobics - all this in the afternoon. And in the evening - a variety of shows and concerts. For kids from four to twelve years old a mini-club is open, but English-speaking. According to tourists, the hotel service is at a very high level. If you want to sit in a calm environment and listen to music, visit the institution "Pukka" a stone's throw from the hotel. It has a very pleasant atmosphere and real relaxation. The audience at the hotel is very different - both Russians, Ukrainians, Europeans, Turks, and citizens of the Middle East. But no one annoys anyone, young people relax in their own way, family people - on their own. Everything is very decent.
