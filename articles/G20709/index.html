Historical reference

Earlier, at the place where the Millennium Square is located in Saransk, there was a stadium called "Lighting". It was designed for the reception of 14 thousand guests, had a huge visor over the central stands and an impressive territory of heated lawns. It was an arena for the games of the local football club Mordovia. In 2010, the dismantling of the sports and entertainment complex began. Now all home competitions are held at the Start stadium.

The opening of the reconstructed area took place in 2012. Today, the description of Saransk and the Millennium Square are synonyms. This is a unique landmark of the highest level.
