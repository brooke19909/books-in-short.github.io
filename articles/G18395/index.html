Hotel Utopia (Turkey, Alanya)

Choosing a hotel in Turkey is a tedious task for a meticulous tourist. Turkey honestly fulfills its presence in the ten most visited countries of the world and provides almost three thousand hotels of various levels and services for leisure, satisfying the needs of up to 40 million tourists a year.

Even among such a number of offers, the Utopia World Hotel , Turkey (Utopia World) stands out .

Location

The placement of the hotel is its highlight. Utopia Hotel (Turkey) rises on a hill, at the base of the ancient city of Siedra, and occupies more than one hundred square meters of territory in which eucalyptus trees and centuries-old pines are adjacent to banana plantations and flower beds pleasing to the eye. The city of Alanya is twenty kilometers away, Antalya Airport is 155 km away.

What do tourists visiting the hotel say?

Reviews are inconsistent. In the minuses of the Utopia Hotel (Turkey), they note the distance from Antalya Airport (4-5 hours by bus), problems with Wi-Fi, a fairly large distance to the sea with a decent number of steps that you need to overcome on the road. But most vacationers agree that the beautifully maintained hotel grounds, amazing sea views, spacious rooms, attentive staff and a varied menu are at an excellent level and attract thousands of tourists to the hotel. This is evidenced by the overall rating of the hotel on the well-known booking.com resource - 8.2 out of ten. Thus, Turkey and the Utopia Hotel (5 stars) will satisfy the bulk of discerning tourists.

Interesting near

If you are bored with the territory of the Utopia Hotel in Turkey, then there is the opportunity to visit nearby interesting places.

Literally two kilometers away is the architectural and historical complex - the ruins of the ancient city of Siedra, which arose in the third century BC. e. Here you can wander among the columns, look into the cave that stores color drawings, and consider mosaics.

Five kilometers away is the promenade of Mahmutlar, where it is nice to walk at sunset.

If you drive six kilometers, you will meet the ancient city of Leartis-Learty, according to legend, founded by the Roman emperor Claudius. These ruins are particularly attractive due to their picturesque location and rich history.

At a distance of eight kilometers from the hotel is a park on the Kastel embankment. A fountain and a waterfall, bright flowers are in perfect harmony with the mountains and the sea coast. Walking in the park will bring harmony and peace.
