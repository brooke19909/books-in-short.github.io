Rest in Cuba: a fairy tale in reality

Choosing a country for a perfect holiday, it makes sense to stop your attention in Cuba. This island, washed by the warm waters of the Caribbean Sea, will give tourists exciting excursions, chic living conditions, wonderful beach relaxation and will leave only pleasant impressions in your memory. Holidays in Cuba are a great opportunity to get acquainted with the unique culture and traditions of Liberty Island.

The best time for such a journey begins in late autumn and ends in early spring. Indeed, in May the rainy season comes into its own. He makes the inhabitants of the Republic of Cuba soak for six months. And although the average air temperature on this tropical island never drops below 26 degrees with a plus sign, the main tourist season falls in October-April.

Holidays in Cuba are a great occasion to get closer to pure untouched nature. To do this, you can go to the fourth largest island of the state of Cayo Coco - a paradise, whose true owners are important pelicans, snow-white coco ibis and delicate flamingos. What could be more beautiful than a flight of graceful pink birds against the background of the crystal clear blue surface of the sea? Probably only calmness and bliss, which can be felt in full only as a guest of the protected Cayo Coco. In addition, the island will surprise fans of diving with an unusually beautiful and rich underwater world, lovers of the mysterious with wild, unexplored jungle, and lovers of relaxation will be happy to visit one of the best beaches in the world.
