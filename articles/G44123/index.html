Belt of Venus: description, meaning

There are two interpretations of the concept of "belt of Venus." From the point of view of palmistry, this is a curved line in the palm of a person. In geography, the phrase has a different meaning related to the atmosphere. This is what pink or orange lines are called, which are the transition from the dark sky below to pale blue above. You can see the spectacle early in the morning or at sunset. This is a very beautiful natural phenomenon - the belt of Venus.

Relations

Often a belt can be seen next to the marriage line. For people who believe in the power of lines in the palm of their hand, this is not a good sign. First of all, the statement is connected with the fact that the belt of Venus in this case speaks of increased eroticism. It may indicate the infidelity of one of the spouses. The double borders of the belt of Venus often indicate a possible marriage breakdown with subsequent voluptuousness.

Owners

The ring of Venus is often marked by artists and musically talented people. This is explained by the fact that when creating the next masterpiece, they are guided by a sense of love. The presence of the belt of Venus is able to endow a person with a life-giving way of thought, high sensuality, a desire to go beyond the real.

Heart line

If the heart line is less pronounced than the ring of Venus, then a person is closed in himself. He lives in his own world of illusions. Own fantasies and emotions haunt the soul. It is often difficult for others to understand such an individual. His strange behavior is not substantiated by anything. The sharpness of mood swings blocks the ability to be open.
