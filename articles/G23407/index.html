Forgot password on iphone? Do not know what to do?

Perhaps, almost all users of newfangled iPhones today seek to protect their personal information stored on devices. But very often this leads to its absolute loss. After all, if you forgot the password on the iphone, then after several unsuccessful attempts to remember it, it will simply be blocked. What to do then?



Keep calm

Of course, on the Internet, various craftsmen posted a lot of information on how to independently deal with this problem and again begin to use your favorite "apple". But often following such tips leads to damage to the device and the need for thorough repair. Therefore, if you forgot the password on the iphone, it is better to follow one very effective way. They say that it always works and never fails. In case of loss of a password, it must be synchronized with a personal computer. To do this, enter the iPhone in DFU mode. To do this, we reboot the device (to do this simply, you must simultaneously hold Lock and Home for about 10 seconds).



Restore content

The appearance of the company logo on the screen will mean that all your actions have been completed successfully. Next, release the power button and continue to press the home button. If everything is done correctly, the USB cable will light up on the display and it will require you to connect to iTunes. If you forgot the iphone 4s password, and it already has a connection to iTunes, then a message about the discovery of a new device will appear on the display of the personal computer. Here you need to click the "restore" button. Similarly, synchronization will perform the process of recovering absolutely all data. The user, even if he previously forgot the password on the iphone, will receive the device in the form in which it was previously in DFU mode (but now without a screen password).
