"Onega" (recreation center): description, services, reviews

The recreation center "Onega" is comfortably located on the shore of Lake Lembolovsky, among a pine forest. In summer, you can take a walk in the surroundings, swim and sunbathe in the lake, enjoy games in the fresh air. In winter, summer fun gives way to winter ones. Skiing, snowboarding and much more are available.

general information





On the territory of the Onega recreation center in Vaskelovo, vacationers are offered various types of active entertainment (for example, volleyball, soccer, cycling and so on), as well as calm and measured leisure time on the lake. Private sandy beach is located just 500 meters from the residential building. Number of rooms





The residential building is a three-story building without an elevator. Guests are offered to stay in the following rooms:

  • two, three and four seater economy;
  • economy with kitchen;
  • double comfort;
  • double comfort is small;
  • double comfort with a kitchen;
  • family;
  • two-room suite.

All rooms have single and double beds, bedside tables, wardrobes, a table with chairs, and a TV. The economy category apartments are not equipped with amenities. Toilet and shower are on the floor. The remaining apartments are equipped with a bathroom with shower. The self-catering units include a kitchenette with a small fridge and an electric kettle. The luxury apartments also have an electric stove and a sofa.

Facilities and service





Guests of the recreation center on Onega are available:

  • transfer;
  • Wireless Internet (available only in the lobby of the residential building);
  • car parking;
  • small and large conference rooms for 15 and 40 seats;
  • banquet room for up to 60 guests;
  • corporate sites from the fresh air, which can accommodate from 50 to 200 people;
  • sports grounds for volleyball, soccer and basketball;
  • a rental point for sports and beach equipment (bicycles, balls, deck chairs, skis, and so on);
  • shooting range;
  • darts;
  • table tennis;
  • barbecue facilities and gazebos;
  • sand beach;
  • bath and sauna;
  • massage room;
  • excursion programs to interesting places;
  • game town for children with various slides, swings and other entertainments;
  • children's room.

The price includes breakfast or full board. Meals are served in the dining room on the ground floor of the residential building. There is also a cafe and there is a banquet room for up to 60 guests.

Not far from the Onega recreation center, only twenty minutes by car, is the year-round Igre ski resort, where you can go skiing and snowboarding.

Pets are not allowed on the camp site.

Reviews about the recreation center "Onega"





They come to the camp site both for family holidays with children, and to spend time with friends. Some come only on weekends, others are delayed for a week or more. After analyzing the reviews about the Onega recreation center, the following points can be highlighted that will become useful to future guests:

  • The recreation center has an excellent location. It seems to be close to the railway station, but there is no characteristic noise. Around is quiet and calm.
  • The rooms are clean, tidy. There is no European-quality repair, but everything is neat, the furniture is new.
  • A real children's town with a rope park is equipped at the base.
  • A good place to spend various holidays, such as birthdays and corporate parties.

  • Onega recreation center organizes show programs with hosts on holidays. So you do not have to come up with entertainment yourself.

Location

Baha Rest "Onega" is located at: Vsevolozhsk district, the village of Vaskelovo, Career Street, 13. You can get here both by personal car and by public transport. You need to drive a car along Priozerny highway. After about 35 km there will be a turn to the village of Vaskelovo. And after 4 km you need to turn on the sign "Recreation center" Onega ".

To get by train, you should take the train from the Finland station to the station Vaskelovo. From the platform you need to walk along the asphalt road to the sign "Recreation center" Onega ". You can also get here by regular bus from the city of Vsevolozhsk.
