Beverly Plaza Hotel 3 *, Pattaya, Thailand: Travel Reviews

A visit to Thailand for many can be a journey into a real fairy tale. The magnificent nature, beautiful Buddhist temples and monuments, friendly people and national Thai dishes will amaze the imagination of many of those who have never been to this wonderful country. Now it is very easy to find the packages you are interested in in Thailand, the prices of which, unfortunately, are increasing every year. Many tour operators offer tours to this country, but a potential tourist often has a very justified question: what is the best place to go?

Why go to Pattaya

If you want to relax on the sea and enjoy the unique flavor of this country, then the best choice for you will be the city of Pattaya. It is convenient to go on various excursions from here, and the prices in Pattaya are very affordable. Near the city there is an island that can be reached by boat from the local port. There are beautiful beaches, and the water is much cleaner than in the city itself.





In Pattaya itself, there are many interesting places that are definitely worth visiting for each guest. This Crocodile Farm, and the famous Walking Street with its unique entertainment for adults, and the amazing Mini Siam Park.

Where to stay in Pattaya

Of course, in this city there are a large number of hotels, among which anyone will choose the suitable one. There are expensive luxury hotels, and very cozy "treshki", which with their service can easily give odds to any more stellar European hotel.

A good option for those who prefer a rather economical, but at the same time comfortable stay, the Beverly Plaza 3 * hotel is suitable. Both young people and older couples can stay in it. Here everyone will be very cozy and comfortable.

Description of the hotel Beverly Plaza 3 *

The Beverly Plaza Hotel is a bright building consisting of three buildings in four and five floors. It was built a long time ago, back in 1983. However, in 2007 the hotel was completely renovated, and now in its updated buildings every day joyfully welcome new guests. The hotel area is not very large, but it has its own, quite spacious pool with sunbeds and umbrellas.





Guests can eat in one of the four restaurants available at the hotel. When checking in, two food options are offered: only breakfast or breakfast plus dinner. Breakfast is organized here as a buffet and served in a restaurant by the pool. So guests have the opportunity to eat and enjoy the blue water surface.

The restaurants offer Thai, Chinese, European and Indian dishes at any time of the day. So no one will be left hungry here for sure. In Beverly Plaza Hotel 3 * there are two 24-hour bars. Guests can enjoy drinks and light snacks there.

Where is the hotel Beverly Plaza 3 *

This hotel has a very good location. Beverly Plaza Hotel 3 * is located in the southern part of the center of Pattaya, on Phratamnak Road. From the international airport located in Bangkok, it is separated by a distance of 126 kilometers. Just 300 meters away is the famous and bustling Walking Street, where nightlife boils year-round. Near the hotel there are a lot of various shops, massage parlors and local cafes. Prices in Pattaya allow you to enjoy a massage at least every day, so you should definitely visit local specialists at least once. So, a foot massage will cost 100 baht, and a classic one - 200.





To get to the city beach, you need to walk only 600 meters. However, it is not famous for its cleanliness, so many people prefer to go to the local port and from there go by boat to the island of Koh Lan. This small island has excellent sandy beaches, which is why most of the guests of the city tend to get here. The port itself is also within walking distance of the hotel, which is very convenient.

Hotel services Beverly Plaza 3 *

In one of the hotel buildings there is a fully equipped conference room that can accommodate up to 100 guests. Thanks to this, the hotel can not only relax, but arrange a working meeting or conference. For a fee, you can also use the laundry service, call a nanny for the child, order a Thai massage.

The rooms are cleaned daily. Bed linen and towels are also changed every day. In the restaurants for the youngest guests there are highchairs, and in one of them there is even a special children's menu. However, there are no special entertainments for children here, there is only a small playground.





In one of the restaurants of the Beverly Plaza Hotel 3 * there is karaoke, here you can have fun and pleasantly spend one of the evenings. The hotel staff does not miss a single national holiday. On this occasion, there are always some kind of performances that all tourists will certainly enjoy. Periodically, some shows and entertainment programs, discos and parties are held. But of course, if guests want a real holiday in the evening hours, then it is better to go for a walk around the city and look at the neighboring Walking Street.

Hotel Rooms

On five floors of the Hotel Beverly Plaza 3 * hotel (Pattaya), guests are offered 192 comfortable rooms of various categories. They have everything you need to make your stay comfortable and set up only for relaxation.

The following room types are offered to vacationers:

  • Superior with one large double bed or two twin beds.
  • Junior Suite with a double bed.
  • Deluxe - rooms in which you can choose two single beds or one double bed.
  • Executive Suite with a double bed.




Each room has a TV with satellite channels, a refrigerator and a minibar, a telephone and free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, a shower and bath, a work desk and a coffee table. Tea and coffee making facilities are also provided for guests, so you can always relax in the room with a cup of hot drink. The suites also have soft sofas. If necessary, a hairdryer or an extra bed, including a nursery, can be requested in the room.

All apartments are quite spacious, but do not differ in any special setting. There are no designer interiors here, they are simply furnished with standard good furniture that will be familiar and comfortable to any guest at the Beverly Plaza Hotel. The variety of views from the windows is also not here. Guests from their rooms can enjoy either the pool or the pretty busy street.

How much can I rent a room at Beverly Plaza 3 *

If you are looking for trips to Thailand, the prices for holidays in Pattaya will interest you first of all. For those who plan to stay at the Beverly Plaza 3 * hotel, the cost of each type of room, as well as additional breakfasts, lunches and dinners, is posted on the hotel’s website.





So, a superior room will cost 1800 baht per day (about 3500 rubles with a rate of slightly less than 2 rubles for 1 baht), a deluxe room will cost 2000 baht (almost 4000 rubles), a junior suite - 3000 baht (almost 5800 rubles. ), and for the most expensive exclusive one you will have to pay 3,500 baht (almost 7,000 rubles). For 500 baht (or about 1000 rubles) an extra bed can be ordered in the room.

Reviews about hotel Beverly Plaza 3 *

Of course, before you finally choose a hotel, you need to find out the impressions of those people who have already rested in it. This is very simple in our century, because there are a large number of resources on which tourists post their travel reviews.

Of course, reviews about the Beverly Plaza hotel can also be easily found on any such portal. Judging by them, this hotel is more suitable for single people or young couples who come to Pattaya and are going to actively attend excursions and various entertainment places. Indeed, animation is practically absent in the hotel itself, and for the children there is almost no service at all.

But guests speak very well about the varied food in local restaurants, as well as about very affordable prices. So, for example, for an excellent lunch you will have to pay only 300 baht. They leave normal reviews about the hotel rooms, however, many say that it would be nice to have them repair and renovate old furniture. Complain about the noise from the street, which often interferes with a comfortable stay.





In general, many choose Beverly Plaza Hotel 3 * for its good location and very attractive prices. However, if the hotel management made repairs in the entire building, then there would be clearly more enthusiastic reviews and visitors here.
