Hotel Lebed 4 * (Bulgaria): description of rooms, services, reviews

Bulgaria is the most popular and favorite country among tourists. There are a lot of hotels, budget hotels, children's camps and health centers. The main and most famous resorts in Bulgaria are Golden Sands, St. Constantine and Elena, Sunny Beach, Kranevo, Pamporovo and Borovets. The oldest and most beautiful resort is St. Konstantin and Elena. It is considered one of the most popular on the entire coast of Bulgaria. Its geographical location is about a few kilometers from the city of Varna and near the very noisy youth resort Golden Sands. In ancient times, on the territory where the hotel complexes are now located, there was an ancient holy monastery, after which the name of the resort did not have to be invented. The village was created a long time ago and was considered the most popular and famous balneological center. The combination of sea breeze, mineral healing properties of water and heady mountain air makes this place unique. The resort of St. Constantine and Elena is significantly different from the others in the presence of steep descents and ascents, narrow streets and rocky terrain. There is a very steep coast, descent to the sea - only by stairs. But this does not frighten tourists, the resort is, has been and will be the most popular and picturesque in Bulgaria.

Climate

The climatic conditions here are quite comfortable, and the sea air only adds a feeling of freshness and purity. The temperature of the water from the healing springs is approximately 42 degrees. Incredible stories tell that water here has healing properties. It is saturated not only with beneficial substances such as calcium and iodine, but also with various sulfates and magnesium ions.

The sea and sand here are very clean, despite the narrow coastline. The main difference between the beach of the resort of St. Constantine and Elena is that its sand is gray. The time of year significantly affects how much a ticket to Bulgaria will cost. The price will be slightly higher in the summer. In spring and autumn you can relax at a lower cost.

The special beauty and mystery of this resort center is emphasized by: a weighty rocky coast, the presence of many coves, a steep and curved descent to the sea, narrow streets and walkways along the streets, the presence of numerous buildings. The water in the sea is crystal clear. For those who love extreme sports, there is the opportunity to satisfy their desires and go scuba diving or diving with a snorkel. Underwater beauty will not leave anyone indifferent.

To rest brought only positive emotions, it is best to take care of staying at the resort in advance. A worthy place of accommodation is the Lebed Hotel (Bulgaria).

Description





This is a very beautiful and modern hotel. It is located a few kilometers from the international European airport. Delivery of guests and tourists to the hotel complex is carried out by transfer. The beautiful building of five floors, renovated in 2000 according to the most fashionable and European standards of hotel design, is very cozy and attracts tourists with its beauty and convenience of equipment. The hotel complex Lebed 4 * (Bulgaria) is located on the first coastline, which greatly increases the significance and popularity among tourists and visitors of the country. Within walking distance is the picturesque promenade.

Accommodation





The number of rooms consists of 128 apartments of various categories, comfort and equipment. On four floors there are 2 elevators and 104 rooms (maximum for 3 people), 20 family apartments with two rooms and 4 suites, designed for 4 people. The apartments are equipped with all amenities in order to make hotel guests as comfortable as possible. There is even a telephone in the room. There are also extra beds (on request), split systems, cable TV, wireless Internet, a personal hygiene room, a small bar, a balcony with beautiful views of the sea and the enchanting greenery of the hotel. Cleaning and changing of bath towels is carried out every day, the bed is changed several times a week or at the request of vacationers.

Service

The hotel complex Lebed 4 * (Bulgaria) belongs to the category All Inclusive Premium, which is important for respectable tourists. The price includes the ability to use indoor and outdoor pools, sun loungers, umbrellas and bathrobes after bathing.

Entertainment





For lovers of water adventures, yachting, scuba diving, water aerobics, surfing and diving are offered, and for adherents of a relaxing holiday - fitness, tennis, billiards. Children will be presented with a fun children's program, a water park with attractions, a playground, a children's menu and, of course, many fun adventures.

Free use also includes wireless Internet access WiFi, there is a 24-hour parking for car owners. All other services offered at the hotel are at a charge.

On the territory of the hotel there are swimming pools, a safe, a theater, 24-hour parking, Internet, cafes and restaurants with various cuisines. Pets are strictly forbidden. For a good rest among the picturesque nature, the Lebed 4 * hotel (Bulgaria) is ideal.

Tourists reviews





In appearance, the hotel building does not look respectable, but inside everything is decorated in a modern style.

The hotel rooms are not large in size, but they are very cozy and comfortable. On the floor there is a tile, bath, toilet, shower, TV, air conditioning - there is everything you need to relax. Bed sheets are changed twice a week. A children's bed can be provided for free, but this must be agreed in advance. By the arrival of guests in the room put the playpen for the child. Valuables can be stored in the safe at the reception. The service is paid.

The hotel Lebed 4 * (Bulgaria) is very well fed. Meals are included in the price. Guests are offered a choice of soups, hot dishes, a variety of fruits and vegetables, all kinds of side dishes, desserts and sweets. It is bad that for small children there is no separate table. But then there are dietary dishes that can well feed a child. The restaurant operates on an all-inclusive basis. In the middle of the day you can visit the so-called ice cream house, where you can get plenty of cold treats and at the same time completely free.

In Hotel Lebed 4 *, in addition to the main, there are 5 more a la carte restaurants. Dishes in these establishments are not very diverse, but they are cooked with high quality.

Beach vacation

The hotel has its own beach. The sea and sand here are beyond praise. A trip to the beach takes only 3 minutes. After a hearty breakfast, you can head to the sea by stairs. Lazy people can take a detour through the Rubin Hotel and go down by elevator there.

The cleanliness of the beach area is carefully monitored by staff, cleaning it daily. But there is still enough debris. After all, guests of all 5 hotels spend their leisure time here. It is best to swim in the morning, the water in the sea at this time is clean and warm. After lunch, you can meet algae, especially when the sea waves. Since the resort of St. Constantine and Elena is considered balneological, it is noticeable in every hotel in this place. For example, a special pipe connected to a hydrogen sulfide source comes to the beach here.

On the beach you can use sun loungers and umbrellas for free. Mattresses are available for a fee. Towels are issued free of charge on cards. At the reception they change to fresh ones. Refreshments can be made at one of the beach bars.

Leisure





This hotel is definitely suitable for families with children and for senior citizens. Young people should choose a more lively place. From entertainment there is only evening animation and various games (volleyball, table tennis, billiards). Excellent conditions are created for children. There is a game club, playground, inflatable attractions. Some of the children's entertainment are paid.

There is practically no entertainment outside the hotel. Nearby is a street with various shops, restaurants and supermarkets.

Those who come here with young children should be very attentive. Children can easily get various infections. Varna Hospital is almost always crowded with sick children. This can significantly overshadow your stay in Bulgaria. Unfortunately, local hotels do not provide qualified assistance of doctors.
