A trip to Turkey with a small child



Summer. Time to think about rest. But if you just recently could, without thinking about anything, lie on the beach for hours, now there is no such possibility: a child has appeared. The question immediately arises as to whether it is worth planning a vacation somewhere far from home. In this article you will learn how to go on holiday to Turkey with a small child.

First of all, when planning a vacation with a child, several factors must be taken into account. The first is acclimatization. An adult quickly gets used to the new climate and is easier to tolerate the resulting discomfort. A child's reaction can be unpredictable. Therefore, before going on a long trip with a child, be sure to consult with a pediatrician.

But why to Turkey with a small child? The mild climate and nature of the Mediterranean combine perfectly with the high quality of service. This will appeal not only to you, but also to your child.



The best time to go to Turkey with a small child is between the change of seasons. Best at the very end of spring or at the very beginning of autumn. At this time, the water in the sea is warmed up quite well, and you will not need to swim in heated pools instead of the sea. It should also be borne in mind that from 11 to 16 hours it is undesirable to be in direct sunlight. Well, if the child is accustomed to daytime sleep, then you will have less trouble at this time.

Going to Turkey with a small child, do not know where to stay? Before choosing a hotel, read reviews and recommendations. But the best conditions are provided, of course, by a five-star hotel. It is very pleasant upon arrival to find a crib in the room ... Also a good hotel provides babysitting services, which will undoubtedly create additional comfort for you if you want to go shopping. All five-star hotels in Turkey provide entertainment for children. Parents can spend this time in any other place without worrying about the baby. That is why Turkey is the most popular holiday destination for tourists with children, with a small child it is very comfortable and safe here.

How to feed a baby?



Also an important topic. Children who are breastfeeding will not cause much trouble. But do not worry if this period in your life has already passed. Hotels in Turkey perfectly combine not only comfort in service, but also a high level of service. In addition to local cuisine, the menu also includes cereals, boiled rice, different types of meat, broccoli, yoghurts, which are added to various dishes that children like very much. In Turkish supermarkets you can buy baby food.

Rest with a small child will be the most unforgettable for you, the main thing is to think over all the little things in time. Be sure to bring a first aid kit with you. Going to Turkey with a small child, you will get a lot of pleasure and a lot of positive emotions.
