Akbulut Hotel & Spa 4 * (Turkey, Kusadasi): room description, service, reviews

Lovers of lingering feasts, foamy discos and long conversations over alcohol do not go here. This hotel is chosen by connoisseurs of a relaxing holiday.

A 7-night tour in the Akbulut Hotel Spa 4 hotel complex (Kusadasi, Turkey), taking into account the flight, costs 66.2 thousand rubles, while the cost of a hotel room is 23.4 thousand rubles. This budget hotel is in demand among couples, as well as among people who prefer a calm, measured, comfortable stay.





It was built in 2009, its full modernization took place in 2014. The hotel is located on the European coast of Turkey, on the Aegean Sea. Nature here is gracious. The air is saturated with sea ions and resins of pine forests. People come here to relax and improve their health.

Among the vacationers there are most British and Irish, Russian-speaking tourists are relatively few. Therefore, communication with the hotel staff is best done in English.

Location

The four-star Akbulut Hotel Spa is 95 km from the airport. However, since the current version of the transfer involves passing other tourists to their hotels, its duration for future Akbulut guests in time is 2.5-3 hours.

The resort town of Kusadasi, where this guesthouse is located, is famous for its beaches and emerald water. Directly opposite the hotel complex is a legendary place - the Cave of Zeus, where, according to legend, the most famous pagan god of thunder was born.

Nutrition

In the Akbulut Hotel Spa hotel complex meals are organized according to the principle of a buffet. The cuisine is European and Mediterranean. The menu of lunch and dinner always contains several main dishes of meat and fish. Beach vacation

The journey from the Akbulut Hotel Spa to the hotel beach takes only 2 minutes. He is well-maintained. Vacationers are provided with sun loungers and umbrellas. There is a shower, there are changing cabins. A buffet is available on the beach in a separate kiosk.

The entry into the water is rocky, so it is advisable to take beach slippers with you. Near the coast, among sea weeds, sea urchins are found.

The beach itself is bulk sand. The entrance to the sea is rocky. The beach has a small marina suitable for mooring boats and boats.

However, leisure enthusiasts on the coast of the Aegean Sea are not limited to visiting the coastal section of the hotel. They strive to get to the best beaches in the city of Kusadasi: Central, Lady, Guzemchamly. Since on weekends they are filled with locals, tourists on Saturday-Sunday usually plan excursions.

Excursions
