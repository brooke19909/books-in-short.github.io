Sunshine Patong Hotel 3 *, Phuket, Thailand: photos and reviews of tourists

Today, more and more tourists who want to relax on the beach, turn their eyes to Thailand. Indeed, this country boasts breathtaking nature, a warm sea and miles of clean sandy beaches. Along with this, accommodation prices are very affordable.

As you know, one of the popular resort areas is the city of Phuket. Here is located a small modern hotel Sunshine Patong Beach 3 *. Of course, before the trip, people try to find the maximum amount of useful information about a particular hotel. So what does this place look like and is it possible to have a decent rest here?

Where to look for a hotel? Location





The modern, cozy Sunshine Patong Hotel is located in the famous resort city of Phuket, namely in the Patong Beach area. Despite being a city-style hotel, it is surrounded by lush greenery. Also nearby is a beach where you can relax. Near the hotel there are many restaurants and shops, as well as a large shopping center where you can buy almost anything.

Thailand, hotel Sunshine Patong Hotel 3 *: description of the territory

This is a small hotel, which, however, owns its own territory. The hotel consists of a large, modern building. By the way, the interior design of the complex is a perfect blend of modern and Thai style. In the courtyard there is a rather large pool. The entire territory is decorated with greenery, shrubs, exotic flowers, beautiful fountains and interesting Thai decorations.

Photo and description of rooms in the hotel





The hotel complex can be considered relatively small. Nevertheless, you can check into one of the 83 rooms. What categories of rooms does the hotel offer Sunshine Patong Hotel 3 *?

  • Superior rooms.
  • Deluxe rooms.
  • Junior suites.
  • Family rooms.

Regardless of the category, you can count on a spacious room, furnished with modern comfortable furniture. The design of the rooms is pleasant, there is access to the balcony, although not in all rooms. It overlooks the hotel courtyard and the surrounding area.

Of course, in the room you will find the necessary household equipment. You can spend a free evening watching TV. By the way, it even shows one Russian channel. Guests can use the telephone. There are fans that are indispensable in the hot season. There is also an air conditioning system with a convenient remote control. Residents are also provided with a safe, which is suitable for storing documents and valuables.

The room has a bathroom. Here guests can take a shower or relax in the bathroom (depending on the category). There is a mirror, sink, toilet, a set of towels and various personal hygiene products. Water is supplied constantly, towels are changed every day, and stocks of soap and shampoos are replenished as they are used. Reviews say that the rooms are perfect for a comfortable stay.

Can I eat at the hotel?





Sunshine Patong Hotel 3 * provides free breakfast for travelers who have paid for accommodation. Buffets are served every morning at the hotel restaurant. It serves traditional international breakfasts including scrambled eggs, toast, fresh pastries, juices, flavored tea and coffee.

For lunch and dinner, you can in the same restaurant. But in this case, food is prepared to order and the bill is paid separately. By the way, the prices here are very low, so you can enjoy traditional Thai and international dishes.

Food in any case will not be a problem, since there are small colorful cafes and cozy restaurants near the hotel where you can eat and have a good time in the home.

What is the distance to the beach?





Can Sunshine Patong Hotel 3 * offer a beach vacation? Phuket is a city that is famous primarily for its beaches. Unfortunately, the hotel does not own its own part of the coast, but the rather famous public beach of Patong is located not so far - the distance to it is about 800 m. It is very convenient to reach the sea in some 15 minutes.

The coast here is covered with soft sand, as well as the seabed. This is a quiet area where you can swim and relax. By the way, for a small fee, tourists rent comfortable sunbeds. Of course, there is the opportunity to engage in water sports windsurfing, boating, boat, water skiing.

In addition, if you walk along the coast, you can find more secluded places to relax, picturesque "wild" beaches. And travelers often buy excursions to the islands, where you can also have a great time.

Does the hotel offer an additional service?





Naturally, travelers are provided with some additional services that will help to make their stay at the Sunshine Patong Hotel 3 * more comfortable. For example, concierge and laundry services are available. And here the reception is constantly working. 24-hour room service is available, but this service must be paid separately.

In addition, there is a small parking area with a modern security system. You can rent a motorcycle or scooter right at the hotel, which is often done by travelers. Hire here is inexpensive, plus it allows you to freely and quickly move around the neighborhood, visit the sights, stroll along the remote beaches.

The territory also has constant access to the Internet. You can also use a copier, scanner, and fax.
