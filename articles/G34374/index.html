DIY handbag for a girl: several workshops

A handbag for a girl, made with her own hands, is done quite simply. The easiest option is to make several cups on one pattern, only to decorate them in different ways. We invite you to find out how you can sew handbags for girls with your own hands, you do not need to do patterns at all.

We sew the basis





Here is such a handbag for girls, created with their own hands, can become the basis for various design options. It is done quickly enough. You can use natural linen, cotton and even fleece to create it. Then let the child decide what thing he wants.

Instructions on how to sew a handbag for a girl with your own hands:

  1. Take the fabric for the front of the bag and cut two identical parts out of it. Their size depends on what size handbag you want to make.
  2. Cut two more pieces of fabric for the lining, which should be the same size as the previous ones.
  3. Cut two identical long strips. These will be handles for the bag.
  4. Fold the two face parts together inward. Sew them together on three sides.
  5. Sew the two parts of the lining in the same way.
  6. Take one strip and stitch it along the long side. Similarly, make another part.
  7. Turn the handles face up.
  8. Iron the handles so that the seam runs in the center of one of the sides.
  9. Now put the lining inside the main part of the bag. Insert the handles between the layers. You can fix them with the help of pin needles.
  10. Stitch all the parts together, leaving a small hole so that the bag can be turned out.
  11. Turn out a bag and sew a hole.

The foundation is ready. You can leave the bag like this. But you can decorate it and make it fantastic.

Fabric dyes are absolutely safe, you can draw them with a brush or with your fingers. And if you want to make colored circles, then an ordinary wine cork is useful.

To do this, dip the wooden lid in the paint, and then make an imprint on the fabric. You can also cut out some shapes on traffic jams (for example, stars or hearts). Thus, the child will be able to put a lot of different stamps on the cup.

Other decor options





A hand made bag for a girl will turn out to be more interesting if voluminous and shiny elements are involved in the decor. It can be artificial flowers, beads, rhinestones, sequins, bows and ribbons.

Large parts can be hung on handles. For example, one large flower can significantly change the appearance of a handbag. A small shiny hardware can be glued to the front side of the cup. They can be either separate elements or assembled into one picture. For example, make an apple from a strass. To do this, use red and green bugles. The first is the apple itself, and the second is the leaf on top.

Choose contrasting threads, then a handbag for a girl, made with her own hands, will be bright and beautiful. Insert a button or sew a button.
