Aegean Breeze Hotel 5 * hotel in its category is considered the most budget. It is the only hotel complex located in Fanes located close to the international airport. Housing stock

In Aegean Breeze Hotel 5 * (Rhodes) three hundred and five rooms. They are equipped in stylishly decorated double, triple and family bungalows. All rooms overlook the garden, pool or sea.

The rooms have all the necessary furniture for a comfortable stay: built-in wardrobe, luggage rack, work desk, bedside tables. All apartments are equipped with satellite TV, mini fridge, telephone. There is everything you need for making coffee or tea.

Each hotel has its own established canons. Aegean Breeze Hotel is no exception. Rhodes is an island where travelers from many different countries come. They all speak different languages. There are also clients who do not know Greek or English. Therefore, for the convenience of servicing guests, hotel staff can freely communicate with tourists in four languages, including Russian.

Upon arrival, guests are presented with a memo indicating what time they need to visit the restaurant, what drinks can be obtained free of charge according to the all-inclusive concept, and for what you must pay, the time of arrival and departure.

Upon receipt of the number, you must present an identification card with a photograph. Check-in time is two in the afternoon, check-out is scheduled for noon. The minimum age for self-registration customers is eighteen. Aegean Breeze Hotel also includes a deposit in cash or by credit card. You can pay only in euros.

The hotel does not allow pets, even the smallest. Extra rollaway beds are not available. Upon request, postal services, room service, iron and ironing table can be obtained for a fee.
