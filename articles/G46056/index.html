Floor Acoustics "Tycoon": specifications and reviews

To create an audiophile sound at home, three main components are required: a high-quality player (CD or vinyl), excellent quality amplifier (receivers will not work) and the corresponding speaker system (stereo only). At the moment, there are many manufacturers on the market who offer pretty good speakers. But we need the best. In this regard, floor magnets Magnat are very interesting. She is rightfully considered one of the best in her class. And its segment is exceptionally premium. We will consider the most interesting and advanced models from this company. But first, a few words about the manufacturer.

Magnat Company

The German company began its journey in 1979 with the release of premium speakers. Her first speakers were called All Ribon 10. And they made a splash in the world of sound. Prior to this, not a single floor acoustics sounded so high quality. From this moment begins the ascent of "Tycoon" to Olympus. In the eighties of the last century, the company was closely engaged in the production of car speakers, having achieved certain successes in this field. A bit later, magnate amplifiers began to appear. Both automotive and conventional. The company produced both audiophile versions on lamps and traditional models with transistors. However, it was precisely the floor-mounted acoustics Magnat that were most in demand.

Magnat Shadow 207

Premium acoustics "Tycoon Shadow 207" has a chic appearance and the same sound quality. And this speaker system is simply incredible power (for its size) - as much as 340 watts. In this case, the range of reproduced frequencies ranges from 22 hertz to 48 kilohertz. This is the main feature of high-end acoustics. It is worth noting that even at maximum volume, the speakers do not distort the sound in any way. But this is provided that all the rest of the equipment is of the corresponding class. Floor acoustics "Tycoon Shadow 207" has an excellent design. The case is quite thin (which is strange for speakers with such power). All speakers are covered with a protective net in the color of the case. The case itself is made of high-quality MDF and coated with excellent veneer with excellent polishing. Acoustics fits perfectly into any interior. But it looks most spectacular with bare speakers (without a protective grille).

Magnat Quantum 677

And these are already columns from the budget segment (yes, there are some).
