Here you can find something for absolutely everyone - the entertainment is so multifaceted that not only daytime, but also nightlife will bring a lot of bright colors to the long-awaited annual vacation.

Bang Tao Beach is located on the northwest coast and is washed by a large open bay. This luxurious beach attracts wealthy vacationers from all over the world.
