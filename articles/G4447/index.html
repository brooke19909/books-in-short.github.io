Work speed





In this article, we talked about the amount of data transmitted by the operator; however, we did not address the issue of speed, although this is an extremely important point. The fact is that some companies are resorting to various ways to optimize their mobile Internet management policies, including speed limits. For many providers, for example, we see unlimited traffic allocated to work at a certain speed. This means that the user will not be able to download large files simply because of the presence of such a filter. And working with it in full mode is very difficult - such packages are not suitable for fans to download movies, music and games online.

As noted on the company's website, the tariff from Beeline (Highway) has a connection speed of 10-20 Mbit / s when working via LTE (4G) connection. In the format of a 3G network, this figure drops to 3-5 Mbit / s, and when working in 2G (GPRS), it is not higher than 60-100 Kbit / s. Naturally, the figures given may vary, depending on the load on the network and the strength of the signal that your device catches. This speed, according to subscribers, is quite enough for work, online games and even for watching movies online (on an LTE device).
