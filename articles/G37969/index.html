We are going on vacation to Kazakhstan. Schuchinsk - a great place to relax

Often, choosing a place to stay, many people think: is it worth going abroad or relaxing near the house? Perhaps a great place to do this is Kazakhstan. Schuchinsk is a resort city located on the lake. Pike. The tourist who set off this wonderful area will not regret his choice for even a second. Here you can see all the beauty of nature, enjoy a wonderful vacation and gain new strength.



Briefly about Kazakhstan

Kazakhstan is a state located on the continent of Eurasia. Most of the territory belongs to Asia, the smaller - to Europe. The relief here is different: in the north - the West Siberian plain, in the south - the Tien Shan ridges, in the west - the Podural plateau and the Caspian lowland, in the east - the mountain systems of Altai and Tarbagatai. The climate type is sharp continental. Seasonality is clearly expressed. In winter, there can be both frost and thaws. January temperatures range from + 1 ¬∞ ... + 19 ¬∞ C. Hot summer. There is practically no rainfall. The temperature in July can exceed + 30 ¬∞ C. That is so special is Kazakhstan. Schuchinsk is located in the northern part, so there can be severe frosts in winter, lasting about two weeks. Sustainable heat begins in June. The heat peak is in July. The average temperature in this month is + 20 ¬∞ C.

Description of Schuchinsk

So, it was decided to go on vacation to Kazakhstan. Schuchinsk is a resort city. Administratively refers to the Burabay district of Akmola region. In the city you can enjoy nature and clean air. However, this does not mean that there is no industry at all. A meat processing plant, a dairy plant, a sewing factory, etc. work in the city. That is why the main income of the city is not only tourism. The population is about 46 thousand people.

Offers for tourists

Finding temporary housing is not difficult. There are a large number of advertisements for apartments. The first few minutes of the search will bring about the following result: about 7 objects, among which there will be pensions, hotels, private houses, etc.



The beauty of the city will not leave anyone indifferent. Unfortunately, there are no cultural attractions except those that nature itself has created. However, the level of service will replace cultural trips, will allow you to spend time on recovery.

Resort Benefits

Rest in Schuchinsk (Kazakhstan) has its advantages. It offers mud baths, hiking in the mountains, there are mineral springs, clean air, sandy lake beaches. Here you can not only relax well, but also improve your health.

Beaches are a separate issue. Their quality is comparable to resorts in Europe: everything is clean and tidy. The town has many cozy cafes where everyone can eat inexpensively and tasty. For lovers of water rides there is a catamaran rental. Guided tours of the local few attractions are also provided.

Popular resorts

Having studied a large number of reviews, we can conclude that this resort is very popular and has a good reputation. On its territory there are many health centers where residents of the CIS countries come together .

Among the popular health-improving institutions, the following can be noted: Svetly and Schuchinsky sanatoriums, Zeleniy Bor sanatorium and sanatorium, Zhumbaktas boarding house.

Famous people

Many famous personalities were born in a state like Kazakhstan. Schuchinsk is the birthplace of popular actors, outstanding scientists, promising athletes and even politicians. Among the most famous: Timur Batrutdinov, Maxim Yaritsa, Sergey Dorogov, Sergey Odintsov, Boris Nikolaev, Peter Morozov (awarded the title of Hero of the Soviet Union), Boris Klimychev, Alexander Veretelny, Andrey Kondrashov, Ilya Motovilov, Svetlana Kapanina, Evgeny Koshevoi, Evgeny Velichko.



How to get to the city?

Shchuchinsky bus station daily receives a huge number of flights from the CIS countries. For example, a bus from Yekaterinburg leaves at 9 a.m., from Omsk at about 1 a.m. Unfortunately, you cannot get here by plane or train. However, there are excellent roads that tourists can travel in personal cars. More than 10 bus routes run through a city such as Schuchinsk (Kazakhstan).
