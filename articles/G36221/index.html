Hotels of Karelia: description, selection, reviews

Karelia for a long time did not attract special attention of tourists. But recently, interest in this region has grown significantly. The Russians fell in love with this land for its stunning untouched landscapes, silence and tranquility reigning in them, water open spaces, alluring eyes and crystal clear air. People come here to relax privately, as well as to recreation centers, to boarding houses, hostels, hotels. In this article we will consider the most popular hotels in Karelia, the prices for accommodation in which are quite reasonable.

Hotel by the famous lake

Hotel Onega Castle is located on the embankment of the famous Lake Onega in the center of Petrozavodsk. The local flavor, amazing views from the windows of the institution on the embankment of the city and Lake Onega, a high level of service - this is how this hotel can be described. It contains 32 rooms.



Cost: from 3200 rubles per day.

Hotel "White Sea"

Hotel "Belomorye" is located in the city center, near the railway station. Near the hotel you can see a local attraction - interesting rocks with petroglyphs.



Hotel guests can use the shared kitchen. Various cafes and restaurants are nearby. The reception is open 24 hours a day. The staff can arrange interesting excursions for you. Shuttle service is available at an additional cost. If you decide to travel by car, free parking is available on site. There is free internet throughout the hotel.

In total there are 5 rooms, furnished with high-quality wooden furniture. The corridors of the hotel have en-suite dormitories.

Cost: from 1500 rubles per day.

Hotel Onego

Onego Hotel is the only institution of this type in the city, located on the embankment near the water station and city center. Ten years ago, the building was reconstructed. The number of rooms consists of 26 rooms. It is designed to accommodate 45 guests with the possibility of adding additional beds.



Cost: from 2100 rubles per day.

Hotel "Ladoga Manor"

Ladoga Manor is located 250 kilometers from the Northern capital, 16 kilometers from the picturesque Sortavala. Free internet access is available. You can also visit the modern bath complex. In case you arrived by your own car, parking is at your disposal. You can order an airport shuttle if you wish. The staff of the 24-hour reception of this Karelia hotel will answer all your questions at any time. Smoking is allowed on the terraces of the cottages.

Cost: from 1800 rubles per day.

Hotel "Frigate"

Hotel "Fregat" is located in Petrozavodsk on the embankment of the lake. There are no roads nearby, and this, in turn, has a positive effect on the noise level - it is very quiet in this place. Nearby is a branch of the Kizhi Museum, and tourists can get acquainted with interesting exhibits if they wish. You can also walk from this Karelia hotel to the water sports center and theater. The hotel offers its guests comfortable rooms with a view of Onego and all amenities.

Cost: from 3500 rubles per day.
