How to check tax arrears without leaving home?

Today, paying taxes on time is not only a matter of conscience, decency and law-abidingness of each individual citizen of the Russian Federation. Recently, having unpaid tax debts, you will not be able, for example, to travel abroad, at the pre-flight control, according to your passport data, an employee will see this information. Someone deliberately evades taxes, someone - out of ignorance or forgetfulness, does not pay off the debt to the state. In all cases, it is important to know how to check tax arrears so as not to get into an unpleasant situation and control the situation. We will tell you how to do this today.





It does not matter who you are: an individual entrepreneur, a legal entity or an individual - today each category of citizens has their own tax obligations to the state. Insure yourself against an unpleasant surprise and read about how to find out the tax debt.

Internet is our helper

Now Russia has become a civilized country: today, many public services can be obtained without leaving the walls of your home or office. That's the question: "How to check tax arrears?" - today we give the answer: "Of course, via the Internet!" Now it is not at all necessary to go to the inspection, stand in line, write a statement and wait for the necessary information.

A few simple steps

Here is a step-by-step instruction on how to check tax arrears:

4. In the window that appears, find the "Bank of enforcement proceedings" - here you will look for your person.

5. Scroll down the page below until you see three points: individuals, legal entities and individual entrepreneurs. Choose who you are.

6. Next, you must specify the region, the one where you are registered with your passport.

7. Enter your last name, first name and patronymic if you are an ordinary person (individual) or company name if you are a legal entity.

8. Indicate the date of birth in order to avoid the search for your candidacy among namesakes.

9. Enter the code that you see in the picture - this is the protection of the site from robots and virus programs.

Further, there are two scenarios:

1. You will see a table in which all the amounts of taxes by their types will be indicated.





2. An inscription will appear in front of you, indicating that nothing could be found at your request.

Here's how to check your tax debt.

Many people think that it is necessary to check the arrears of transport tax on the website of the STSI. Not at all, if you have a debt on this type of monetary burden, it will also be reflected on the official website of the bailiffs.

Debts on non-payment of utility bills, alimony, outstanding loans and borrowings - all this you will also find on this site if one of the following conditions is met: if legal proceedings have been passed on these debts and a decision has already been made.
