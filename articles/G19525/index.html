Radisson Blu Resort Sharjah 5 * (Sharjah, United Arab Emirates): description, service, reviews

The history of the Radisson Blu Resort Sharjah 5 * in the UAE, Sharjah, began in the distant 80s of the 20th century. Then the very popular institution was called differently - Radisson SAS Sharjah. In the new century, in 2009, rebranding and reconstruction was carried out, which opened a new page in the history of the institution. Travelers can appreciate the high standards of service and comfortable living conditions.

Location

On the shore of the picturesque Persian Gulf is a resort Radisson Blu Resort Sharjah 5 *. United Arab Emirates, Sharjah, Corniche Street, PO Box 3527 - hotel address. It is 15 km from Sharjah Airport and 18 km from Dubai Airport.

Relaxing at this hotel, you can visit a number of tourist sites. Namely:

  • Museum of Art (0.2 km).
  • History Museum (0.3 km).
  • Museum of Islamic Civilization (1 km).
  • Sharjah Heritage Museum (2 km).
  • Khalid seaport (2 km).
  • Mega Mall (3 km).
  • Al Jazeera Park (3 km).
  • Aquarium (5 km).
  • The Great Mosque (15 km).

Number of rooms

Accommodation of guests of Radisson Blu Resort Sharjah 5 * in the UAE, Sharjah, is made in a 14-storey building and several detached cottages. Here are the categories of rooms available to guests:

  • Standard - a compact room with a view of the Persian Gulf. There are options with large or twin beds. There is also a sitting area with a pair of armchairs and a coffee table.

  • Business class - a spacious room that is designed for those who come to the UAE for business purposes. In addition to the bed and sitting area, there is a workplace, an ironing board and a trouser press.

  • Junior Suite - a spacious two-room suite. The bedroom has a king size bed, and the living room has upholstered furniture.

  • Suite - a luxurious room decorated in a modern Arabic style. There are one or two bedroom options. This room type also has a living room and a private balcony.

  • Cottages are detached houses located next to the pool (with direct access). Each cottage has a large bed and a seating area.

Additional Information

In addition to information about the services and photos of the Radisson Blu Resort Sharjah 5 *, also take a look at some additional information about the hotel. Here are some points to consider:

  • check-in of newly arrived guests is from 15:00 to midnight;
  • on the day of departure you need to leave the room before noon;
  • Two children under 12 years old can stay in the room free of charge, subject to the use of existing beds;
  • children under two years old are provided with free cots;
  • the cost of an extra bed is 100 dirhams (1791 rubles) per night;
  • no alcohol is sold at the hotel;
  • It is forbidden to bring food purchased outside the hotel.

Negative reviews

Radisson Blu Resort Sharjah 5 * is characterized by some shortcomings and shortcomings. Reviews of tourists about this institution indicate such negative points:

  • the rooms have a smell of dampness;
  • the windows in the rooms do not open, so there are no ventilation options (and the air conditioning does not give a feeling of fresh air);
  • inconvenient location of the watering can in the shower;
  • maids do not clean rooms every day;
  • unreasonably high prices in restaurants;
  • monotonous nutrition;
  • lack of animation;
  • the hotel has virtually no opportunities for relaxing with young children;
  • inconvenient location regarding tourist sites and attractions;
  • very expensive minibar;
  • the furniture in the rooms is outdated both morally and physically (a lot of breakdowns and scuffs);
  • polluted coastal zone (packages, plastic cups and other garbage float in the water);
  • guests cannot be sure that they will be accommodated in the exact room format that was specified at the time of booking (referring to large or twin beds);
  • slow waiters;
  • during religious holidays, in the evenings and at night, the hotel is terribly noisy;
  • almost the entire hotel smells of tobacco smoke (and in some places you can find cigarette butts thrown out by unscrupulous vacationers);
  • after three minutes, the hair dryer overheats and turns off.
