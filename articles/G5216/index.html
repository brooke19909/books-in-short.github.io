So, how can we use our Angel origami craft?

Origami Christmas angel has always been a classic home decoration for Christmas and New Year. Let us recall the old traditions, but we will make the New Year‚Äôs angel in a new way - according to the modular scheme of paper. What does modular mean? This folding of different individual parts without the aid of glue. For beginners, a simple scheme of an origami angel is more suitable.Therefore, we will first consider this option from a single sheet of paper.

Fold half the sheet, then again. After this we unfold the paper - two cross-shaped bends should turn out. Then we bend all the corners to the center, turn the craft over. We extend the pocket and begin to give the central part the shape of the head. Then again we turn two corners to the right and to the left - these will be two wings. Origami is ready. It can be hung on a string, the figure will flutter in the wind.
