Xenon lamp Philips D4S: description, technical parameters

Today, many people have cars. Due to the large number of vehicles and poor lighting, accidents have become more frequent. For this reason, it is worth very carefully approaching the choice of lamps for your car. The Philips D4S Xenon Lamp is a great choice for several reasons.

Xenon lamp description

Here it is worth starting with the fact that xenon lamps are very different from ordinary ones. The main difference lies in their work. The Philips D4S xenon lamp , like any other, does not work by heating the filament, but by passing an electric discharge between the electrodes. Because of this, an electric arc arises. It is even more appropriate to note here that in this case it is not an arc, but a plasma. Explain the appearance of the glow is quite simple.

It has a conical shape formed near the cathode. The strength of the luminescence depends on the proximity to the cathode and decreases with distance from it. The glow of the Philips D4S xenon lamp is very similar to daylight, as the light emits evenly and travels across the entire spectrum.
