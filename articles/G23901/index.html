A little about Volgograd

Former Stalingrad (the site of the Battle of Stalingrad), a hero city, a huge metropolis - all this is now Volgograd. It cannot be said that at present it is the largest industrial center of the country, since many industries are in a difficult economic situation. Although it was due to a decrease in production in the metallurgical and chemical industries, the ecological situation of the city came to some equilibrium. Road transport continues to be the main polluter of the atmosphere, throwing about 70% of harmful substances into the air.





It sounds paradoxical, but among cities with such a population Volgograd takes first place in terms of low wages, worn-out infrastructure, and a small number of organizations representing small businesses. With such indicators, only Volgograd officials cannot complain - they have the highest salary ratings in the Southern Federal District.

Location of the residential complex

The residential complex "Albatros" (Volgograd) is located almost in the center of the city at the intersection of Poddubnoy, Bashkirskaya and Metallurgov Avenue. For owners of their own cars, it is not difficult to get to the complex along the indicated streets. Those who do not move on personal vehicles will be able to get to the house by various types of public transport: tram, minibus.
