How to make a flower of tulle with your own hands?

Fatin is a wonderful material for creativity and needlework. This is a mesh and fairly lightweight fabric made from polyester thread. It can be shiny, matte and translucent. It has a smooth and uniform surface. This interesting material was invented in the United States almost two hundred years ago. In the article we will tell you how to make a beautiful flower of tulle with our own hands . You will need a few materials: the mesh fabric itself, scissors, felt, thread, glue, beads and a base for a hairpin or rim.

The simplest flower of tulle. Master Class

It is worth noting that flowers can be made not only from tulle, but also from other fabrics. First you need to build the core of the bud. To do this, take a small piece of thick and hard felt and sew beads on it. Pearls and stones imitating it, made of plastic or stone, go well with tulle.

So, take a small piece of mesh fabric about a meter long and about ten to twenty centimeters wide, as well as a thread and a needle. Begin sewing tulle to the core so that the strip has uniform beautiful folds. Next, look at the flower and decide if you will still add details to your product.

By the way, you can take tulle of different colors. For example, strips sewn near the middle will be light, and those that are farther away will be dark. So a tulle flower will look prettier and more interesting. In addition, you can add lace to your buds. This element is especially suitable for wedding decoration. Now you know how to make a tulle flower simply and quickly. It will take you no more than one hour to create such an ornament.





The core for the flower. How to make?

Suppose you don‚Äôt have felt. What then to do? As a basis for a flower, you can use a variety of materials. For example, a wooden bead tied with cotton threads is very good for creating a convex core. It will be convenient for you to sew on strips and to attach tulle. In addition, you can take a piece of any thick tissue that will not crumble.

Be sure to go shopping that sell jewelry accessories. Often there you can see metal filigree. They can also be used for the core of the flower. It is necessary to sew beautiful beads to them, and then tulle itself. A filigree will be convenient for creating a hairpin or brooch, since it is convenient to attach a base to it.

As you can see, making a flower from tulle with your own hands is quite simple. You do not need special skills, tools and expensive materials. From tulle you can make a beautiful and inexpensive decoration for your child to school or a friend for a wedding. Moreover, not only flowers are made of this beautiful material, but also beautiful dresses for photo shoots.





A more sophisticated model of a flower of tulle and lace

Before you make a flower of tulle and lace, carefully consider the color scheme, the size of the bud itself and, of course, how you will sew tulle and other materials. It is good if the strip sizes are different. In the middle of the flower should be short petals with wavy edges. The farther they are from the core, the longer and wider they should be. You can alternate layers of tulle with lace or satin fabric. As soon as you finish the tulle flower, you can sew on it a mesh fabric in the form of leaves. In addition, such a bud is decorated with feathers matched to the tone, or even with wool.





How to make a rose? Flowers from tulle (do-it-yourself)

The master class on creating roses from tulle is quite simple. You will not need special patterns and tools. Take a strip of tulle length from a meter to two. Its width should be from ten to thirty centimeters. The longer and wider the strip, the more fluffy and larger your tulle flower will be. Take this tape and fold it in half. Then simply thread the needle thread through the long side and gently tighten. Note the edges so that the product does not bloom. If desired, you can decorate a tulle flower with beads, beads or sequins. Your decoration is ready!





Small flowers in jewelry

A tulle flower in a bracelet or bandage will look very beautiful and unusual . To create such an ornament you will need a very small piece of tulle and a bead for the core. First, cut a small strip of material about twenty-five centimeters long. Its width should be from five to ten centimeters. Next, make a flower like you did a rose. A small bead with a diameter of six to ten millimeters will act as a core.





What else can you do yourself from tulle?

Fatin is a true salvation for the photographer. If you know how to work with this inexpensive material, then you can easily make a luxurious dress for a photo shoot or flowers for decor and decoration. Dresses from tulle can be made both long and short. True, you will have to spend quite a lot of material so that the outfit does not appear through. But the result is worth it. The resulting product will be fluffy, delicate and very beautiful! Moreover, from tulle you can make a beautiful veil or just a cape on your head.

As decorations for the festive interior, you will be able to create beautiful flowers that resemble fairy clouds in appearance. With such buds it is convenient to dance at performances and holidays. In addition, these colors can decorate the ceiling.
