Business or leisure?

Korston Hotel & Malls is a chain of hotel and shopping complexes in Moscow, Serpukhov and Kazan. If you are planning an excursion or a business trip to the capital of Tatarstan, then it is better not to find a place.

The Korston Hotel (Kazan) opened in 2006 and became the first hotel shopping and entertainment complex in the region. Seven years later, the Korston Tower high-rise building appeared on its territory. The total investment in this facility amounted to 160 million dollars.



It is worth noting that the organization of business events is a priority for the hotel:

  1. The complex is located in the center of business activity of the metropolis, at the intersection of major highways.
  2. Nearby is a modern business center (Class A offices).
  3. The hotel has conference rooms and meeting rooms, as well as everything necessary for organizing events of any complexity.

The Korston Tower rating is slightly higher than the Korston Royal. First of all, guests like the panoramic view that opens from the windows of the room. In addition, the room interior and long breakfasts at Le Buffet leave a pleasant impression.
