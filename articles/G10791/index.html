Refresh your wardrobe using a crochet or knitting belt

The belt has always been one of the fashion accessories. With it, you can favorably emphasize the ideal proportions of the figure or, conversely, hide the flaws. In the wardrobe of every woman there are always several beautiful things in color, shape and material. Needlewomen, of course, will make themselves a crocheted belt or knitting needles without difficulty. Do-it-yourself wardrobe details are always particularly appreciated. Moreover, you can create exactly what you need: a narrow openwork crochet belt for airy dresses and blouses or a wide, knitted with a dense pattern knitting, belt for jeans and sports skirts. It all depends on imagination and skill. We suggest you to make for yourself an original and fashionable belt, crocheted or knitted - of your choice.





Lilac-pink crochet belt

This model is universal and very simple in execution. The belt looks great with both summer skirts and sportswear. Coloring can be any, but multi-colored melange yarn is best suited.

Materials required for work:



- yarn - 100 grams;

- hook number 3.

Knitting designations:

1 - double crochet, VP - air loop, ** - rapport (repeating pattern).

Pattern Description:

1 row - a chain of VP;

2nd row - 3VP for lifting, skip three loops on the base, * three 1, three VP, skip three loops * on the base *, then repeat ** to the end of the row, 1 in the last loop of the chain;

3rd row - 3VP for lifting, * three 1 into the arch from 3 of the previous row, three VP *, repeat **, 1 in the last loop of the base;

4 row and subsequent repeat 3 row.

Working process:

  1. Dial a chain of 24 VIs.
  2. Crochet a crocheted belt according to the description of the pattern. The length of the belt shown in the photo is 150-160 centimeters. If desired, you can make the product shorter or longer.
  3. Decorate the finished belt with fringe tassels.

Knitted belt with needles

The proposed belt can be made even by a beginner, inexperienced knitter. The finished product is double and looks like a belt, since it is quite short. It is more suitable for trousers and skirts of a sporty style, at the same time it is threaded in loops sewn to the clothes.

Materials required for work:





- yarn - 70-80 grams;

- knitting needles number 2.5;

- hook number 2.

Knitting designations:

KP - edge loop, LCP - front loop, IZP - purl loop, SP - untied removed loop, ** - rapport (repeating pattern).

Pattern Description:

1st row - KP, * LCP, IZP *, LCP;

2 row - KP, * LCP, SP *, LCP;

3 row and subsequent repeat 2 row.

Working process:

  1. Dial 80-90 loops (the number depends on the required length of the belt, which will correspond to the waist circumference).
  2. Knit from 8 to 15 rows in a pattern to obtain the required belt width. The most suitable size is 4-5 centimeters.
  3. In the last row, all the loops are crocheted.
  4. The belt will be tied with a few chains of air loops, crocheted and fastened on both sides of the belt.

As you can see, it is not difficult to make a knitted belt with a crochet and knitting needles. You can also decorate the product with beads, knitting them in the process and evenly arranging the ornament, or use several matching shades of yarn. Imagine and create unique fashion accessories for yourself and your loved ones!
