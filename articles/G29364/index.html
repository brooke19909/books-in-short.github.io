What is famous for and where is Chelyabinsk located on the map of Russia?

Where is Chelyabinsk located? On the map of Russia there are many famous large cities and glorious small towns. The administrative center of the Chelyabinsk region occupies a worthy place among the leading industrial regions.

What secrets does the history of the city keep? What does this regional center look like now? There are a lot of questions for tourists going to this region and for those who need to make a business trip to a large industrial city. To begin, consider where Chelyabinsk is located on a map of Russia.





Geography

Chelyabinsk is conveniently located east of the Ural Mountains. He is the southern neighbor of Yekaterinburg, the distance to which is about 200 km. The area is recognized by geologists and geographers as the conditional border of Western Siberia and the Urals. The Leningradsky Bridge is the connecting link between the "Ural" and "Siberian" banks of the Miass River, which is the main waterway of Chelyabinsk.

The city of Chelyabinsk on the map of Russia, intended for motorists, is highlighted by the Meridian highway passing through a residential area, part of which is the recognized border of Siberia and the Urals. The south-western and northern neighbor of the city is Sosnovsky district, in the east it borders on the satellite city of Kopeisk. In the north-east of Chelyabinsk - Krasnoarmeysky district.

In the urban area is a major donor of drinking water - the Shershnevskoe reservoir. In the vicinity stretch picturesque clear lakes: First, Smolino, Sineglazovo. On the territory of Chelyabinsk there are several small streams carrying their waters to Miass. Among them are Chernushka, Igumenka, Chikinka, Kolupaevka.

Chelyabinsk stands on a terrain with hilly terrain in the west, which is gradually changing and to the east, closer to the city of Miass, turns into hollows with lakes and swamps. Both banks of the river are covered with frequent shrubs.

The location of Chelyabinsk on a map of Russia indicates the presence of the city in the zone of the Yekaterinburg time zone. Regarding Moscow time, there is a constant bias denoted by MSK + 2.

History

When did Chelyabinsk arise and where is it located? This city appeared on the map of Russia in 1736 thanks to the decision of Colonel Tevkelev to lay a fortress on the site of the Bashkir settlement of Chelyaba. The consent of the owner of the land - Bashkir Tarkhan Shaimov was obtained, which ultimately contributed to the release of the Bashkirs from taxes. The status of the city was assigned to Chelyabinsk after half a century - in 1787.

Until the end of the 18th century, Chelyabinsk was a calm county town, in which events of a global scale rarely occurred. At this time, placer gold mining started on the Miass River. And with the official opening of the gold mine, the "gold rush" swept the edge. The first half of the 19th century was characterized by the intensive development and establishment of trade and crafts. In the Soviet period, Chelyabinsk became a large industrial and industrial center. In the 90s of the last century, the city experienced a difficult financial crisis. Many enterprises went bankrupt, workers were not paid wages, unemployment increased, and social programs were not sufficiently funded.

Modernity

The reforms carried out have yielded results: enterprises in the industrial sector began to work actively, budget revenues increased, and incomes of citizens began to increase. Many factories and combines presented their products on the world market. Reconstruction of roads and construction of modern transport highways has started.





Today this city is an administrative center of regional significance with a population of over 1,100 thousand inhabitants.

The location (see where Chelyabinsk is located on the map of Russia) and the perfect transport interchange helped him to become one of the largest industrial cities in Russia. In his arsenal are enterprises of metallurgy, mechanical engineering, instrument making, light and food industries.

Large trade structures of both international and federal companies work in Chelyabinsk. There are banking and credit institutions. Chelyabinsk is a significant cultural center. The city has an unusually developed infrastructure of the hotel sector, which provides visitors with comfortable and affordable housing.
