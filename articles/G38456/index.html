Central Belarusian Botanical Garden. Minsk receives guests

Botanical gardens and greenhouses were invented by lovers of the beauties of nature and exotic a long time ago. They are currently open in many cities. One such amazing place will be discussed in our article.

Description



We go to the capital of Belarus to visit the local botanical garden. Minsk is a wonderful city in many respects - clean, well-groomed, comfortable. Parks, squares, fresh green lawns, overgrown trees around residential buildings, alleys along the streets ... However, one of the main attractions of the city is precisely the botanical garden. Minsk got the opportunity to open it even before World War II - in 1932. Naturally, in severe cruelty and years of fascist occupation, the garden was badly damaged. Therefore, during the restoration of the city, special attention was paid to the planting and cultivation of new plantings. Thanks to the reconstruction, the territory of the arboretum expanded, and its collection was enriched. Minsk is known not only in the CIS countries, but also in Europe for its unique arboretum.

Going on an excursion



So, you are in the capital of Belarus and are going to visit the famous reserve. Do not hurry! First you need to find out if the botanical garden (Minsk) is open, its working hours and other organizational issues. What can we tell you about this? Excursions - collective and individual - in the arboretum begin on April 30 and end on October 31. That is, it is open precisely in those months when the plants are most active: they are covered with greenery, bloom, fragrant, and bear fruit. In late autumn, winter, and early spring, nature begins to rest, sleepy numbness. And the "inhabitants" of the reserve are gaining strength in order to once again open up the hospitable embrace of their green kingdom with the onset of real heat. The exception is the greenhouse - it is available at any time of the year. The second important point about how the botanical garden functions (Minsk). Opening hours: from 10 a.m. to 8 p.m., from Tuesday to Sunday. On Monday, the arboretum does not accept guests.

There, on unknown paths



Initially, the arboretum was founded in the Belarusian capital in the very place where the agricultural exhibition was located before. The territory was carefully selected, because it was supposed to be an object of republican significance. An area of ‚Äã‚Äã90 hectares in a pine forest was most suitable. As already written, later its territory grew to 153 hectares. In the 60s a lake was built here. And in 1967 the park was equated with the research institute of the Academy of Sciences of Belarus and received the corresponding status. Since then, the garden has been the most important state base for scientific research in the field of crop production. On its territory there are several biological schools conducting experiments on plant breeding, survival of species in the Belarusian climate. A special object, thanks to which the capital's botanical garden (Minsk) stands out, is a greenhouse. Its opening took place in 2007. Exotic plant species such as the Madagascar bottle tree, bamboos and eucalyptus trees, cacti and agaves, jasmine, magnolia, orange and coffee trees, cypresses and palm trees settled here. The inhabitants of the tropics and subtropics feel very comfortable in the greenhouse and grow to the delight of scientists and tourists.
