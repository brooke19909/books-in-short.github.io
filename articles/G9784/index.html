Skullcandy JIB

Conventional in- ear headphones that promise to give acceptable sound quality at a low cost. They are connected using a cable with a connector at the end ("Jack" 3.5 mm). Skullcandy Hesh 2

The full-size Skullcandy Hesh 2 earphones are among the best in the line. At the same time they have an affordable cost. The connection is made using a cable 120 cm long, the connector is 3.5 mm Jack. The design is based on an electrodynamic emitter. Headphones reproduce frequencies in the range of 18-20000 Hz. Skullcandy aviator

These are stylish and expensive Skullcandy headphones. Made in an elegant form with ear pads in the form of triangles. An interesting model with bright coloring and good characteristics. They are made in a form quite typical for on-ear headphones . The material of manufacture is high-quality and lightweight plastic.
