How to choose an airplane on the remote control?

If you or your child have been dreaming of flights since childhood, are a fan of computer games and flight simulators, go to the radio-controlled toy store and fulfill your dream. Aircraft on the control panel - which of the boys in childhood did not dream of such a thing? Now the shelves of children's and specialized stores are bursting with an abundance of models from a variety of manufacturers. Standing for hours at the windows will not make the decision easier, because buying, albeit a toy, an airplane on the control panel is a serious step.





What should I look for when making a choice?

All models are classified by the following parameters:

  • engine's type;
  • type of control panel;
  • the size;
  • case manufacturing materials.

There are aircraft on the control panel and the type of engines:

  • with internal combustion engine;
  • electrolytes;
  • gliders;
  • jets.

Manufacturers produce models of various sizes - from several meters to ten centimeters in wingspan.





How to choose an airplane on the remote control? Internal combustion engine

Such models, perhaps, are suitable only for experienced professionals. Airplanes with an internal combustion engine have good speed and increased maneuverability. They require specific care skills and management experience. The internal combustion engine of aircraft is quite difficult to maintain for beginners in aviation. Classes with this model require a large open space.





How to choose an airplane on the remote control? Electric motor

The model with an electric motor is easy to operate, but due to the low speed it is subject to a stronger effect of the wind in comparison with models on internal combustion engines. The battery lasts for 10-15 minutes of flight. The electric motor of such a toy as an airplane on the control panel is simple and convenient to maintain. Small models with such a motor can be run in buildings.





How to choose an airplane on the remote control? Multichannel

Remote controls differ in the number of control channels. The more of them, the higher the ability to control the aircraft. The simplest of them are two-channel, they allow you to adjust the number of engine revolutions and the direction of flight. In more complex models, a four-channel and more control panel is installed, which makes it possible to really feel the flight.

Ideal for beginners aircraft on the control panel

Beginners should purchase a device that is not too complicated. If you are a beginner amateur aircraft modeller, pay attention to the training aircraft on the control panel. As a rule, these are two-channel models. Easy control and perfect aerodynamic characteristics allow you to control a training aircraft even in conditions of strong crosswind. Since the most difficult piloting element is landing, the speed of this maneuver is reduced. Such models are most consistent with the requirements of most novice aircraft modellers. They are easy to manage, maintain and repair, because they were created and designed initially for training flights. Also pay attention to the convenience of the device controls. And most importantly, you just have to like the plane on the control panel externally, because only truly beautiful models can challenge the air element.
