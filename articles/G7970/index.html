Description of the Supra SCR-575W DVR

Supra SCR-575W is an optional device to feel as safe as possible on the road. The device constantly records video and audio using highly efficient compression of the material. Video recording takes place with a wide viewing angle. If you set the appropriate setting in the device, then when the place on the memory card starts to run out, information will be written over the oldest. Also, the device is capable of recording sound that is made in the cabin.

All information that was recorded on the device can be easily reproduced on a computer. If you connect the monitor to the device, you can watch the video that is being recorded in real time.

Safety measures and working conditions

In order to ensure the correct operation of the DVR, you must follow all instructions. In a review of the Supra SCR-575W, we will talk about them.

Never need to open the case of the device during its operation, as the user may be shocked. Maintenance of the device should only be carried out with the help of specialists. You do not need to leave the device for a long time on the control panel if sunlight is exposed to it. Due to overheating, the device may malfunction. It is not necessary to put objects on it. Also, children should not be allowed to the DVR.

If you use parts or accessories that are not intended for a particular Supra SCR-575W DVR and are not official, then the device may work poorly or completely break down.

During movement, do not touch the device and carry out any manipulations with it.

As for operating conditions, the device must be installed in such a way that nothing obscures the view. The tint of the windshield should be removed, otherwise the quality of the recorded video will be low. Regularly check the direction and viewing angle of all lenses, especially if the driver was driving on rough roads. The quality of the recorded video is greatly affected by the lighting conditions. The device should not be exposed to direct sunlight, should not be affected by moisture and heat. Permissible temperature range is from 0 to 40 degrees. Environmental humidity can be up to 80%.

Malfunctions

If the Supra SCR-575W device, the review of which we are bringing to an end, does not turn on, then it is necessary to charge the battery and leave the device for a while. It happens that moisture gets into it. This is what causes the system to shut down.

If the power button does not work, then you need to contact the service center.

If the battery does not charge, then, as a rule, this occurs due to the fact that it has failed or the DVR was not mounted correctly.

If there is no sound, then, according to the manufacturer, the cause of such a malfunction may be the establishment of a low volume level in the settings.

If video recording is not performed, then, as a rule, either the memory card is not inserted, or it is not installed correctly, or its size is not enough.
