What is needed

The ideal option for material for creating clothes for Barbie dolls will be the remains of any yarn, skeins of old threads. Of the tools you need only a hook and sewing needles.

In addition, you will need several crochet dress designs for Barbie so that the girl chooses an option to taste.

The simplest dress for a doll

For beginners, a model of a summer dress is suitable, which has a straight silhouette and rests on two thin straps.

In any case, it is worth measuring the parameters of the doll, which will determine the width of the future product. When creating a model, you should use a simple crochet dress scheme for Barbie:

  1. Dial a chain of air loops, which is about a centimeter larger than the width measure.
  2. Knit the fabric using single crochet stitches. The length of the canvas is determined by the height of the doll and the preferences of the needlewoman.
  3. Having finished the first fabric, you need to start knitting the second.
  4. Then the canvases are sewn together and where the top of the dress is, 2 straps are formed from chains.

Such a description of the dress for Barbie crocheted is not too difficult to implement, so even a beginner needlewoman can make a beautiful outfit. On the canvas, you can make embroidery or applique.

You can use a pattern that does not differ in complexity from the above, but at the same time tie the product to get much faster. If you use columns with 1 or 2 crochets in your work, you will get the original model as in the photo above.

Ball Gown for Barbie Crochet

You can create a more festive costume for the doll by learning how to knit openwork napkins. The scheme of the dress for Barbie crochet in a ballroom style is not too complicated if you can knit a crochet and a crochet.

The principle of creating a ball gown for Barbie:

  1. Dial a chain of 15 air loops. Lock the chain in the ring.
  2. The first row to tie with ordinary columns without a crochet.
  3. In each subsequent row, you need to evenly add 8 columns.
  4. After forming the base of the skirt, you should choose any openwork pattern for a napkin.
  5. When the skirt is ready, you need to tie a bodice. Tie a cloth that in width will correspond to the height of the doll's torso. You can use the simplest pattern, which consists of single crochet columns.
  6. Then the workpiece for the bodice needs to be sewn so that a ring is obtained.

The next step will be the assembly of the ball gown. The process is very simple: you need to sew the workpiece of the bodice to the skirt. To do this, you can use a thread with a needle or a hook. A seam made with a needle will be thinner and more accurate.

To increase the volume of the skirt, it is worth making the lower skirt of tulle. On a regular elastic band, which is inserted in underwear, sew several strips of tulle. The mesh material should be trimmed to the length of the hem. You can sew pieces of material on the dress. It is enough to fix them with a needle and thread under the seam where the bodice connects with the skirt.

After assembly, it is worth decorating the product. On the back of the bodice you can sew a satin ribbon in the form of lacing. This decor option will make the inconspicuous upper part of the dress more elegant. In addition, lacing will be a good imitation of a full corset. Beads, beads, sequins and other shiny jewelry can be sewn along the edge of the skirt.
