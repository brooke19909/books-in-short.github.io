The battery runs down quickly on the iPhone 6: possible causes and solutions

Most smartphone users rarely notice increased battery consumption, having the habit of connecting the device to the network several times a day, even without much need. Nevertheless, if the phone loses more than 20% of the charge overnight, this causes bewilderment and the question of why the battery on the iPhone 6 quickly runs out. The solutions and causes of such problems will be discussed in the article below.

Cause for concern

For each device, the reason can be individual, depending on the device itself and the functions that its owner most often uses. Nevertheless, there are five of the most common reasons, because of which the battery on the iPhone 6 quickly dies.

Possible reasons:

  1. Functioning geolocation. Usually this option is activated by default, and most background programs are constantly connected to the GPS receiver. For this reason, he has to restart very often, which leads to a charge consumption.
  2. The backlight can also drain the battery quickly . This happens as a result of setting the brightness to maximum. Firstly, it is energy-consuming, and secondly, it does not have the best effect on vision.
  3. The Internet. It is important to monitor the network to which the smartphone connects and, if possible, choose Wi-Fi instead of 3G.
  4. Synchronization. The iPhone has an automatic mail update feature, which is installed by default. Regular network connections quickly drain the device, and notifications sometimes distract.
  5. Automatic updates. The activated mode of automatic updates each time loads the programs installed in the device, and does this even when the battery in the device is already exhausted. However, this can be done manually and as needed.

Let's consider such inconveniences in more detail.

In recent generations of the iOS operating system, most applications run in the background. For example, in some social networks, the news feed is constantly updated, mail notifies you of incoming letters, and so on. As a result, the iPhone battery runs out quickly.

Animation and screen brightness

If the battery runs out quickly on the iPhone 6, the cause may be an animated splash screen. A special program or a separate team is also responsible for it. Very often, the animation not only consumes, but also slows down the process of charging the phone. If it is installed on an ongoing basis, then over time it will consume a lot of battery power. Since this is not a fundamental function, but only a beautiful screensaver, it can be turned off in the same way as it was turned on.

Screen brightness is another feature that, if improperly installed, will quickly drain the smartphone. Often the automatic brightness setting makes it possible to adjust it to the lighting. Airplane Mode, Mobile Internet

In some cases, smartphone users are faced with increased battery consumption when traveling outside the city. This can happen due to being in places with poor network coverage, in other words, in the ‚Äúdead zone‚Äù. The iPhone system begins to use all kinds of resources to search for a signal, which inevitably affects its autonomy. You can avoid excessive charge consumption by activating flight mode. To do this, go to the Settings tab, find the Flight Mode at the top and put the slider in the on state. Wireless systems will be deactivated, while Wi-Fi and Bluetooth can be turned on separately.

Often, with access to the Internet, many programs work without stopping. If the signal level is low, the device makes increased efforts to find the best connection. As a result, consuming battery power. You can disable it in the settings section of the "Cellular" tab.

Turn off spotlight, push notifications, photo stream

Spotlight can also cause abnormally high levels of charge. If it is always on, it may malfunction. To maintain fullness of the battery, it is necessary to stop the operation of this mode.

Push notifications not only distract from using the smartphone, but also connect to the Internet, which leads to a fast discharge of the battery. Disabling them in the device settings significantly saves the energy of the device.

A photo stream is a transfer of photos from a device to IKlaud in automatic mode when connected to Wi-Fi. Such actions adversely affect the battery charge. To maintain the desired interest, you should turn off automatic transmission and resume it when you need it.

Charging failure and notification calibration

Often the battery on the iPhone 6 runs out very quickly due to a charger malfunction. For example, in the process of powering the smartphone, the display shows the full charge of the battery, in fact, the device is partially charged. For this reason, it seems to the user that the phone is quickly discharging. In this case, you must try to charge the device with another accessory, and if the problem is not solved, you need to take the phone to the workshop.

Another reason the battery runs out quickly is due to calibration. Speaking in accessible language, smartphone firmware. Special applications will help you to check and configure the battery. They display battery statistics on a particular gadget. It is quite fast and convenient, moreover, this method has helped to fix problems for many users.

Wi-Fi, bluetooth and startup

The autoload function of programs and applications has several advantages and carries a lot of useful information downloaded from other devices. However, in addition to its advantages, it leads to a quick discharge of the smartphone. You can control and disable this feature in the iPhone settings.

Most people using a mobile device, Wi-Fi and Bluetooth are constantly in the activated state, regardless of whether they are currently in use or not. If you need to save charge, these functions must be enabled only at the time of use. It should be noted that the battery consumption for Wi-Fi is an order of magnitude lower than when you turn on 3G. And some users almost never use bluetooth, while it remains on by default.
