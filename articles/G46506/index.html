"Seahorse", Koktebel: location, rooms, additional services, photos and reviews of visitors

The small and very cozy town of Koktebel, located in the southeast of the Crimean peninsula (near Feodosia), annually attracts travelers. A wonderful vacation on the Black Sea, picturesque nature near the extinct volcano Karadag and the eponymous nature reserve, lush greenery and gentle sun - what else is needed for a pleasant vacation? An equally important role is played by the choice of successful accommodation at the resort. After all, this particular place will become your temporary home for the period of rest, it is here that you will return with a feeling of pleasant tiredness after a busy day, it is here that you need to gain strength for new adventures. The ideal solution is choosing a hotel in a park area. Hotel "Seahorse" in Koktebel (Crimea) has always been popular with tourists.

Features

The building of the boarding house " Seahorse" (Koktebel, Crimea) was built in accordance with the original architectural solution - with nice ladders and passages between three floors and an attic. The interior is very reminiscent of the home environment: everything is cute, comfortable, in a warm color scheme, clean and tidy.

The main feature of the Sea Horse in Koktebel is the location of the mini-hotel on the territory of a beautiful green park. It is very quiet and calm, the paths are tiled, a beautiful pool with sun loungers, elegant benches surrounded by well-groomed flower beds, subtropical trees and shrubs are adjacent to blue spruce and pine trees.

Service

Arriving in Koktebel in the Crimea (in the "Seahorse", in particular), vacationers hope to improve their health by breathing healing sea air, gain strength and recharge their batteries. The administration of the boarding house takes into account these needs of tourists, so here they are offered a huge range of wellness services for every taste in the spa. Noteworthy is the democratic cost of the course of procedures that (subject to the promotion) can be purchased for 2700 rubles. This includes a visit to the hammam and Finnish sauna, salt cave, sessions of unique hardware massage of the legs and head based on oriental acupuncture techniques. People who are difficult to tolerate heat can visit an infrared sauna, where the body warms up without stress on the cardiovascular system due to special waves. In order to be in shape and not get out of the usual rhythm of training, vacationers can visit the well-equipped small fitness room.

In the afternoon heat it is pleasant to soak up in arbors or on chaise lounges in a shadow of sprawling trees near the pool. You can sit on the terrace with a glass of wine, enjoying a gorgeous view of the picturesque sea and mountain beauties of Koktebel.

Young guests will find a colorful playground in the fresh air with slides and swings, a bright trampoline.

Booking and reviews

Many tourists independently booked accommodation in a boarding house, which they were satisfied with (the cost was no commission). They note that the procedure on the official website is very convenient and simplified as much as possible. You must specify only the most important information (arrival dates, room category, information about guests), you need to pay only one day of stay or 20% of the total cost. The administration will send the contract and vouchers by e-mail. The final settlement is made by the client upon check-in.

Popular excursions

You can not visit Koktebel without visiting the reserve Kara-Dag (Black Mountain). This is a very beautiful and interesting mountain volcanic complex. Legend has it that in this place of St. George the Victorious saved the inhabitants from the terrible serpent. Nearby are the Golden Gate (a symbol of the resort) - whimsically protruding stone blocks from the sea.

Travelers were also delighted with Cape Chameleon, which is located near Quiet Bay. Water here changes its color during the day, striking the imagination of tourists. Popular trips to Mount Koklyuk and Uzun-Syrt (Klimentyeva Gora).

You can still go to the Museum of Nature of Karadag, the house-museum of M. Voloshin, which was so fond of M. Tsvetaeva, M. Bulgakov, M. Gorky. Be sure to bring the children to the dolphinarium, where bottlenose dolphins, fur seals and even the polar whale perform. It is interesting and fun in the water park, dinotel - a place where different birds live in amazing pavilions.

Pension "Seahorse" in Koktebel is the place that travelers recommend. It is rare to find a hotel about which there are no negative reviews at all.
