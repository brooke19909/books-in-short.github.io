Stari Hrast (Montenegro): photo, hotel description, prices and guest reviews

Small but very picturesque Montenegro offers the most varied vacation: on wild beaches, and among the rocks, and near olive groves, and in comfortable hotels, and even in small hotels, which are very numerous on the coast. But there is one thing that unites all these types of holidays - it's the gentle, warm sun and the purest water in the sea.

Holidays in Montenegro



The period when vacationing in this country was not very expensive was already a thing of the past. Today, tours to Montenegro are quite comparable in price to holidays in leading European resorts. For Russians, this wonderful corner is attractive in several ways at once. In their reviews, our compatriots especially note the amazing combination of natural beauties with unique cultural monuments, beautiful bays and beaches and, of course, Mediterranean cuisine and the friendliness of the local population.

Montenegro hotels, regardless of their stardom, offer a European-level service, combined in some cases with a family atmosphere. This is especially true of mini-hotels, in which those who prefer a quiet, measured rest away from the hustle and bustle prefer to stay.

Tourists who prefer parties in a classic resort performance, go to Budva, Becici or St. Stephen. And for a vacation with a touch of sophisticated comfort in the midst of stunning landscapes and surrounded by artistic bohemia, you need to go to Herceg Novi.





Stari Hrast 3 *

This 3-star hotel was built in 1990. The last major overhaul was carried out three years ago. It is located in the heart of the lively and amazingly picturesque Montenegrin resort town of Becici.

Hotel Stari Hrast 3 * (Montenegro) is positioned as a pretty good option for couples with young children, as well as for young people who prefer to actively relax. It is surrounded by a small garden, there are many cafes and restaurants nearby.

Infrastructure

The hotel has its own parking. It does not need to be reserved in advance. In the building, which is a three-story villa built in a Mediterranean style, you can order a taxi or use the shuttle at the receptionist. The nearest airport is twenty-five kilometers.

Rules and prices

In Stari Hrast 3 * (Montenegro), although there is a predetermined time for arrival and departure, however, the owners do not create bureaucratic delays. Room keys can be obtained at any time of the day or night from the receptionist.

The cost of a tour to this hotel for two for a week with the concept of a continental breakfast meal when departing from Moscow starts from 460 euros. Prices vary depending on the season and the availability of the charter.

Housing stock





Stari Hrast (Montenegro) is a small hotel. Its rooms are located in one three-story building. There are twenty of them. Tourists are offered accommodation in standard rooms with categories A and B, which are equipped with a bathroom.

The Russians who visited Stari Hrast leave mostly positive reviews, noting a good combination of price and quality of the services offered. In addition to beds with comfortable mattresses, the room has a kitchenette, work desk, chairs, wardrobe and bedside tables.

Ceramic tiles on the floor. The rooms are equipped with split air conditioning, a TV and a refrigerator. A stove in the form of a hob is equipped in the kitchen corner, there is all the necessary utensils.

The rooms have a balcony or terrace in front of the rooms, depending on the floor. The bathrooms have decently working plumbing and a hair dryer. All bath accessories are updated daily.

Nutrition

For those who have purchased a ticket to Stari Hrast 3 *, the following food concepts are offered: full board, half board and continental breakfast. If you wish, you can only pay for accommodation, and eat in the surrounding restaurants or cook yourself.

Those who prefer not to cook during their holidays can enjoy the buffet in the restaurant. The menu includes Mediterranean dishes. In hot weather, you can reserve a table on the terrace, where, while admiring the sea and the surrounding scenery, you can taste seafood, which the restaurant chef cooks wonderfully.



Beach

The sea and the sun - this is exactly why many tourists come here. And the Russians are no exception. Since the Stari Hrast 3 * hotel does not have a swimming pool, guests can sunbathe and swim only on the beach. The seashore is just a five minute walk. You can visit the municipal free beach, located three hundred meters away, or you can negotiate and use the swimming area belonging to the neighboring hotel.

In any case, the sea, which is the same everywhere, will not disappoint tourists. The coast in this part is sandy and pebble and clean. It is equipped with awnings and sunbeds, for which you need to pay separately.

Additional Information

Stari Hrast has internet access throughout. The hotel has a TV room and a games room. Those who come here for thrills and do not accept lying for many hours under the sun are invited to rent a catamaran, ride a banana, slide down a water slide. Many tourists rent a boat and go fishing.

Snorkelling in Montenegro come from all over the world. The underwater landscapes of Becici are truly amazing. Fans can go diving or windsurfing under the supervision of licensed instructors.





Excursions

The range of attractions available from Becici is wide. You can go to the canyon of the Tara and Moraca rivers, which is simply huge. As a protected place, an oasis of pristine nature is listed as a UNESCO heritage site.

The tour will be extremely interesting, since it is here that the highest mountain bridge in Europe is located - Djurdjevic Tara. Just looking at this structure is enough to fully appreciate the courage of its creators.

Reviews

Stari Hrast is popular with Russians. And this is evidenced by the impressions of all those who have already managed to rest here. Why do compatriots choose Stari Hrast? Reviews are called as the main reason for the excellent ratio of quality of services and the cost of tours. Guests are particularly happy with the calm, homely atmosphere that prevails at the hotel. The staff knows everyone by name, treats them as personal guests. No complaints about the proposed food. The only thing many are dissatisfied with is the lack of a pool, but the nearby sea completely compensates for this shortcoming.
