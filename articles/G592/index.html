Cottage Village Sofrinsky Ponds

Muscovites are so tired of the hustle, bustle and crowds of people that they increasingly pay attention to suburban villages, where you can, without losing access to the capital's infrastructure, enjoy peace, tranquility and breathe fresh air. The cottage village Sofrinsky Ponds is an ideal place to live. If you have not heard about the project, we strongly recommend paying special attention to it.





about the project

Just 27 km from the Moscow Ring Road in the direction of the Yaroslavl Highway on the border with the village of Sofrino, the eponymous cottage village is located. Stunningly beautiful place with equipped park area by the pond. If you are looking for a modern residential complex built in a historical place near Moscow, where all the necessary conditions for a comfortable life are created, the village will definitely live up to your expectations. The complex is surrounded by magnificent centuries-old oaks, keys, ancient ponds, which makes it truly a unique place.

Transport accessibility

Sofrinsky Ponds is a cottage village located just 27 km from the Moscow Ring Road. By Moscow standards, this is pretty close. Residents receive peace, tranquility, clean air, and at any time they can get to the capital. The Yaroslavl highway is quite busy, so due to the numerous traffic congestion, many prefer to use public transport. In the Yaroslavl direction, there are enough railway routes running with enviable regularity. Just 1.5 km from the village of Sofrinskie Prudy is the Sofrino railway station, the journey time to the Yaroslavl Station will take no more than 40 minutes.

Price policy

The cost of a plot in the cottage village Sofrinskie Prudy starts at 3 million rubles, which is very affordable for such a picturesque and environmentally friendly place. Reviews

Reviews about the village are very controversial. Of course, most of those who managed to visit the site are delighted with the chosen place. Just imagine: you will live in close proximity to old estates, wander along shady alleys, enjoy peace and tranquility. At the same time, the project has a number of shortcomings.
