Restaurant "Crown" in Yaroslavl: menu, features and location

If you are planning a family celebration or a warm meeting with friends, then you should stop by the Korona restaurant in Yaroslavl. An institution with a luxurious banquet hall cordially opens its doors to guests from all over the world. The restaurant is open daily and around the clock. Payment is made by cash or bank transfer.





What is served in the institution

The Korona Restaurant in Yaroslavl serves European, Caucasian, Russian and Oriental dishes. In addition, you can taste the masterpieces of the author's menu, which will surprise you with an exquisite and unforgettable taste. Frequently ordered dishes are:

  • crab salad, with tongue, seafood, Olivier salad;
  • garlic soup, borsch, salmon ear, kharcho, hodgepodge;
  • hot fish dishes (sea bass, salmon, dorado);
  • hot dishes of poultry and meat (veal, pork, chicken);
  • snacks (pancakes, squid rings, shrimp, fried cheese, julienne);
  • pasta (black spaghetti, spaghetti with cheese, pasta with salmon);
  • grilled dishes (kebab, mutton fillet, ribs, loin);
  • side dishes (spinach with egg, boiled and rustic potatoes, vegetables);
  • cold appetizers (basturma, tongue, fish platter, meat and cheese plates), etc.




In addition to the main dishes, you can taste delicious homemade desserts and ice cream. In the wine list, each guest will find the most suitable drink for himself. In the restaurant "Crown" in Yaroslavl, whose photo is surprisingly beautiful interior, you can try delicious coffee drinks, liquors and tinctures.

Services

The restaurant "Crown" in Yaroslavl has the opportunity to use free Wi-Fi. The guests of the establishment can order dishes from the menu with delivery to the specified address and taste delicious business lunches. The restaurant and hotel complex "Royal" offers to visit the hammam, rooms of European level. If desired, guests can try aromatic hookah.

The Korona Restaurant in Yaroslavl is an excellent place for holding corporate parties, receptions (there is a buffet menu), beautiful weddings, birthday parties and other celebrations.





The interior and features of the banquet hall

Restaurant "Crown" in Yaroslavl is located at: st. Prospect of October, 88a. The average amount per person is:

  1. In the banquet hall "Royal" - 2 700 rubles.
  2. In the VIP banquet hall "Boyarsky" - 1 700 rubles.
  3. In the VIP banquet hall - 1,500 rubles.

On the territory of the establishment there is a fountain and a well-kept lawn, which gives the institution its own character. If desired, guests of the restaurant can use the modernly equipped rooms of the Korona Hotel, as well as swimming pools and a bathhouse.





The interior of the Korona restaurant in Yaroslavl is distinguished by a truly royal design, high ceilings and large windows. Round tables, covered with expensive tablecloths in delicate pastel shades, and chairs with brocade covers give the restaurant a special atmosphere of wealth and luxury. Above is an original and attractive illumination on a multi-level ceiling with intricate lighting. Well, the marble finish perfectly complements the interior design. The capacity of the hall is 500 people. There is a VIP room, the capacity of which is 100 people, and a banquet VIP room for 50 guests.
