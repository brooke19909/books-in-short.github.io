What can be made of wax: interesting ideas, technique and examples with photos

If you want to craft a little, then it is important to inquire about what can be made from wax. Working with such material is very simple, the result is almost always perfect. You can work with a new base or secondary raw materials. For decoration, it is enough to use any materials and devices.

Standard Candle Wax

From the many candle cinders, several new household lighting fixtures can be made. Using paraffin candles, it is easy to create a decorative version of the device that will decorate the interior of the room. In addition, it can be a good gift. Here's what wax candles can do. If you stick to the standard method. To make a new candle you need:

  1. A certain amount of cinder or a few new candles.
  2. Cotton thread. A floss is also suitable.
  3. Form for filling from any material.
  4. A stick made of wood or a knitting needle.
  5. A container for melting wax over a water bath.

Candle Making Algorithm:

  1. You need to tie a cotton thread to a stick or needle. Lay the holder on the form so that the thread touches the bottom and fits exactly in the middle of the container. Thus, the preparation of placing the wick for the future candle is being done.
  2. Melt the wax in a container installed in a water bath. It is advisable to grind the material with a knife. To dissolve quickly, grate the wax. In the process of melting constantly interfere with the consistency.
  3. Pour wax into mold in 3 steps. Each filling is carried out after the previous hardens. During the cooling period of the material, the remaining portion should be in a water bath.

After completion of work, it is necessary to let the wax solidify completely. To do this, leave the workpiece in the form for at least 1 day. Then lower the mold for several seconds in hot water and quickly pull out the finished candle. Cut the wick, leaving a small piece.

What application can be in wax at home

It turns out that wax can be used for other purposes, except for the manufacture of candles. The options for working with this material are very diverse and differ in their technology. What can be made of wax other than candles, and for what purposes the material can be used:

  1. Batik - painting fabrics for dyeing.
  2. Painting eggs for Easter.
  3. Rubbing garden and garden tools that protects the metal from rust.
  4. Rubbing fabric and textile shoes to create a waterproof shell.
  5. Liquid wax is ideal for polishing furniture.
  6. When working with wood, it prevents the wood from breaking if waxed with nails and screws.
  7. Due to the application of a small layer of wax on the pulp, waxed paper is obtained, which is actively used in the household.
  8. Wax is suitable for shoe care.
  9. Wax in combination with petroleum jelly is an ideal skin protection in winter.

The main thing is to choose the material suitable for each option and know the principles of use.

How to use in the game with children

When the question arises as to what can be made of wax, mothers immediately remember the babies. It turns out that having a little study material, you can come up with a lot of interesting activities.

What can be made of beeswax for a child and with a child? A whole bunch of options can. Here are the most interesting:

  1. If you warm up the wax a little, then the material will be an ideal option for sculpting.
  2. Figures can be cut out from hardened wax using plastic tools or a heated spoon.
  3. If you melt the wax and add a little dye to it, you will get universal crayons for drawing on paper, asphalt.
  4. Heated wax can be used to paint pictures that can be further decorated with beads or beads.
  5. You can create puzzles from wax. Such a toy will be unusual to the touch, malleable in the game, parts are easy to replace or repair.

Features of the use of wax in the game with children are determined by the age limits and skills of the crumbs.

Secrets to working with wax

To work with wax for pleasure, and the material to be malleable and well set, it is worth knowing a few secrets:

  1. It is impossible that water gets into the consistency at any stage. Fluid will degrade traction.
  2. At the time of heating, you need to constantly mix the composition so that the base warms up evenly.
  3. When pouring molds, you need to take into account the size and volume of the base so that the wax solidifies evenly.
  4. Do not heat the material too much - this can make the wax too soft, the product will not keep its shape.
  5. It is not advisable to cool the wax with special devices. For example, leaving a form with a workpiece in the refrigerator.

Thanks to this, it is easy to figure out what can be made of wax efficiently and quickly. If you observe all the nuances in the process of working and preparing the material, then there will be no problems.

How can I decorate

Having decided on what can be made of wax, it is worth considering the options for decorating an already finished product. Nothing comes to mind? Here are some suitable ways to decorate:

  1. If you slightly heat the surface of the wax product with a hot spoon, then on the surface you can make a pattern of beads or sequins.
  2. The finished candle can be wrapped with twine, ribbon or yarn. Under the winding, you can slip a twig from the tree, berries, herbarium.
  3. An original decoupage can be made on the wax coating. Can be painted with markers or paints.
  4. With the help of threads it is easy to form a pattern, which can then be painted with paints.
  5. In the process of filling the wax product, inside you can put colored pebbles, shells, dried flowers.

Due to the softness and suppleness of the material, the decoration options are diverse.

How to give wax a scent

If a wax product will be used for decoration, making "toys" for children, interior candles, then it is worth using an unusual method of decoration - aromatization. In addition to the pleasant smell, visual effects may also be present.

What can be done with grated wax so that it acquires a peculiar aroma:

  1. Place the workpiece in a smelting tank.
  2. Before filling directly, add a little flavor.
  3. Allow wax to solidify. With thermal exposure, a pleasant smell will spread throughout the room.

A suitable option for flavoring will be: vanilla, coffee, cinnamon, flowers, tea, perfume. As well as your favorite aromatic oil.
