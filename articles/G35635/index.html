A planetary mixer to help confectioners

When creating his masterpiece, any confectioner strives for individuality. Achieving this goal is possible through the use of various kitchen utensils, including a planetary mixer. It is worth considering this device in more detail.

Instrument Features





The planetary mixer is intended for mixing and whipping a variety of mixtures, souffl√©, creams, meringues, dough, fruit and berry purees. When it is used to mix products, the process of saturation with air occurs, due to which it is possible to obtain a very airy and light consistency at the output. This condition of the product is achieved due to the movement of the corolla around its axis. Currently, most planetary mixers have no more than ten speeds.





Application

The planetary mixer can be used both in small pastry shops and in large enterprises. In the second case, the recommended volume is 120 liters. These devices are equipped with a lifting automatic bowl, as well as programmers that allow you to knead the test according to the program set in advance. This allows you to make the kneading process constant and uniform.

The standard planetary mixer contains:

  • bowl made of stainless steel;
  • whisk;
  • shoulder blades;
  • hook;
  • special gutter designed for draining liquid.

Each model of the device can be equipped with additional accessories: a whisk, a bowl, a stand for nozzles, a trolley for a bowl. If you need to purchase a mixer with a planetary bowl, you should pay attention to whether its equipment is suitable for you, as well as the number of rotation speeds. The greater the number of speeds it has, the more you get the opportunity to cook different types of products, different consistencies, from kneading creams to kneading dough. The cost of the device is influenced by the method of raising and lowering the bowl - manual or automatic.

Criteria for choosing a good model





Before deciding which device suits you, you need to determine the purpose of its use. The power of the mixer depends on a set of speeds and functions. The maximum indicator can reach up to 2200 watts. The bowl should be made of stainless steel, then it will last as long as possible. A whisk, a beater and a hook are usually included in the standard package of nozzles. The first is intended for whipping souffl√©s, mousse, eggs, dough for dough and other things. Thick yeast dough is mixed with a hook. Fillings, glazes and more can be made using a spatula. Due to additional nozzles, the kit can be expanded. Thanks to a special rotating shaft, the device can become a vegetable slicer, a juicer or a meat grinder. The device can be equipped with a touch or mechanical control panel. The first is quite stylish, but the second will last longer. A mandatory element of equipment is a timer, sometimes it is able not only to signal, but also to stop the operation of the device.
