Larco Hotel 2 * (Larnaca, Cyprus): hotel description, services, reviews

Holidays on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea is the dream of many! A considerable number of travelers want to spend their holidays in one of the resorts of sunny Cyprus. And, it is desirable that it be as budgetary as possible. But is it possible? Quite! In Cyprus there are countless different hotels, and the rooms in many of them are accessible to people with average earnings. For example, Larco Hotel is a 2-star hotel, which is one of those options. And it can be considered in more detail.





general information

So, Larco Hotel is located in Larnaca - the third largest city in Cyprus, located in the southeastern part of the island.

In the immediate vicinity of the hotel are such interesting places as the Pattiqueio Theater, St. Lazarus Church and the Byzantine Museum, Fort Larnaca, Finikoudes Embankment, Salt Lake, Harbor, Hala Sultan Tekke and much more. Most of the above is located at a maximum of 1.5 km from the hotel. And this can not but rejoice the visitors - you can reach on foot.

There are many attractions in this place, but most tourists, of course, come here for a beach holiday. And here is the first feature of the hotel - it is 200 meters from the sea. Near the hotel building you can find a bus stop, from which there are minibuses going to different ends of the city. The airport is also close - just 10 minutes drive.

Services

The Larco Hotel has everything a tourist might need for a vacation. There is free high-speed Wi-Fi available throughout the hotel, parking near the hotel building, and in the lobby there is a 24-hour reception, a luggage room, a currency exchange office with a favorable rate and a tour desk that also sells tickets.

In addition, the hotel can find laundry and dry cleaning services, where guests are also ironed. There is a small business center with photocopy and fax machines, a beauty salon and a hairdresser.

It is also worth noting that Larco Hotel provides transfer services. But if a person needs his own vehicle, he can rent a car right at the hotel. Such an opportunity is also provided.

In addition to the above, guests are offered services for the delivery of drinks and food and preparing lunch on the way.





Leisure activities

Cheap hotels (and the Larco hotel is one of them), as a rule, does not have many additional amenities, such as its own SPA-salon, casino and night club, like many resort complexes. But, however, here you can find what to do with yourself.

The hotel has a nice well-tended sun terrace with sun loungers and umbrellas, as well as a swimming pool. The hotel itself has a bike rental desk, table tennis, a games room and a fitness center with modern exercise equipment. Lovers of relaxation, both soul and body, will like the sauna. But the most important entertainment is, of course, the beach. Many guests are advised to go to the one that is located behind the San Remo Hotel - there are good comfortable sunsets, and there are places where you can snorkel.

Nutrition

Most tourists, going on vacation in Larnaca, pay special attention to the issue of nutrition. Well, the hotel restaurant offers several options - OB (accommodation only), BB (breakfast), HB (half board) and all inclusive.

Guests say they feed well here. Breakfast is standard, European - fresh bread from which you can make toasts, eggs in various variations, coffee, teas, milk and juices, jams, several types of cereals, sausages, vegetables, fruits, etc.

Lunches and dinners are more diverse, in addition, dishes are not repeated every day, which cannot but rejoice. Pork, fish, beef, poultry, potatoes, couscous, spaghetti, baked and fresh vegetables, beans, rice, salads ... this is just a small list of what is served here - you can list for a long time. But everyone will find a dish to their taste. People who came here on vacation in Larnaca recommend paying only for breakfast if you want to please yourself with variety while eating at local taverns and restaurants. Of which, by the way, there are many, and in most prices are more than reasonable.

General impressions

About the rest in Larnaca, during which I happened to stay at the Larco Hotel, the guests respond positively, for the most part. The main advantages are the beach in the immediate vicinity, and, in general, a convenient location. The center is a 15-minute walk away. There are, of course, nuances - some consider the number of rooms obsolete, the apartments are not too spacious, and the shower device is not very convenient. But in contrast to all the minor flaws, an extremely favorable price is set. You can book rooms at really low prices. Which are even less - if you reserve apartments in advance of your trip, or in the so-called ‚Äúlow‚Äù season, when there are very few tourists in Cyprus.

Tourists are also advised to book apartments with windows facing the front of the hotel - they are quieter.

In general, this is an ideal hotel for people who dream of a budget, but a pleasant vacation. Numerous positive reviews, as well as the promise of tourists to return here again, are the best evidence of this. If you want to make sure of this yourself - you should go on vacation to Cyprus and stay at this hotel.
