Sabina Hotel 3 * (Greece / Rhodes island) - photos, prices and reviews

The island of Rhodes, part of Greece, has long been loved by tourists from all over the world. Moreover, travelers come here in search of noisy entertainment and vivid impressions, as well as a secluded comfortable vacation on the shores of the warm sea. In addition, Rhodes is famous for having a lot of interesting sights, picturesque nature and great climate. A wide hotel base and developed tourist infrastructure allow travelers to find a place of accommodation and entertainment for every taste and budget. If you have chosen the island of Rhodes (Greece) for your summer vacation , Sabina Hotel 3 * will become the best hotel for those who are looking for a comfortable stay at an affordable price. We offer you to get to know this hotel more closely, and also to find out if our compatriots liked it here.





Where is the hotel situated

The three-star Sabina Hotel is conveniently located in a resort village called Theologos of the Greek island of Rhodes, on the shores of the Aegean Sea. This settlement has all the necessary tourist infrastructure (taverns, bars, shops, souvenir shops, tour agencies and more). The distance to the island's capital, the city ‚Äã‚Äãof Rhodes, is only 20 kilometers. You can get to it by bus (the stop is near the Sabina Hotel 3 *). Also in the vicinity of Theologos is one of the most famous attractions of Rhodes - Butterfly Valley. As for the airport, it is only five kilometers from the hotel. So after landing, the road to the hotel will take no more than a quarter of an hour. By the way, having learned about the proximity of the airport, some tourists begin to worry about the fact that noise from take-off and landing aircraft can interfere with their rest. However, according to the Russians who stopped at the Sabina Hotel, the airways do not pass over the hotel, so the buzz of air liners will not disturb your peace at all. The hotel in question boasts its own beach, only 150 meters away from it.





Description Sabina Hotel 3 *, photo

Rhodes, as already mentioned, offers tourists both large noisy hotels and hotels for a quiet measured rest. ‚ÄúSabina Hotel‚Äù refers to the second group. Hotel Policies

As in many other hotels in Greece and other countries, Sabina Hotel 3 * (Theologos) check-in for guests staying after 14:00. However, if you arrive earlier, then if you have free rooms you will be placed immediately. On the day of departure, the occupied room should be vacated before noon. You can pay for accommodation and use of additional services at the hotel both in cash and with a plastic card of Mastercard, Visa or Maestro systems.





Accommodation for guests with children

Sabina Hotel 3 * is always warmly welcomed by guests staying on vacation with families with children of all ages. In addition, babies under two years old will be provided with a playpen for free. There is also the possibility of an extra bed (one for each room). This service is paid. The fact that you need an extra or a crib, you should inform the hotel in advance and get confirmation from it.

Guests traveling with their pets

This point should be paid close attention to those travelers who plan to visit Rhodes in the company of their four-legged pets. So, in accordance with the internal rules of the Sabina Hotel, guests with pets are not allowed here. In this regard, you will either have to part with your pet during the holidays, or choose another hotel for your stay.





Number of rooms

As already mentioned, the Sabina Hotel 3 * (Rhodes) has at its disposal 69 rooms. The housing stock of the hotel consists of standard (single and double) and family rooms (accommodating up to four people). They are decorated quite simply, but they are very comfortable. In addition to the necessary furniture, they have their own bathroom with shower, air conditioning, TV, telephone and balcony. On the floor - ceramic tiles. Some of the rooms have a magnificent view of the Aegean Sea. Rooms are cleaned daily. Bed linen and towels are changed by the maids twice a week.

restaurants and bars

When booking a room at the hotel, you can choose one of two types of food: breakfast only or half board (breakfast and dinner). At the same time, meals in Sabina Hotel 3 *, reviews of tourists confirm this, it is very high quality and quite diverse for a three-star hotel. In addition, the hotel has bars where you can order any drink to your taste.





Beach vacation

As already mentioned, the Sabina Hotel 3 * has its own beach, located just 150 meters away. The beach is coarse. There are sun loungers and umbrellas that protect from the sun. In addition, on the beach for a fee you can use equipment for water activities and sports.

Hotel infrastructure

Sabina Hotel 3 * has everything you need for an excellent vacation and pastime infrastructure. So, there is a 24-hour reception, swimming pools for adults and children, a playground, a restaurant, bars, Wi-Fi in public areas, car rental, free parking and a mini market. You can also store valuables in a safe at the reception, as well as dry cleaning and laundry services.





Cost of hotel accommodation

As for the price of accommodation in the Sabina Hotel, it is quite consistent with the category of this hotel. So, a seven-day accommodation here at the height of the holiday season in July and August will cost you in the amount of 30 thousand rubles (double room) and 32 thousand rubles (double room).
