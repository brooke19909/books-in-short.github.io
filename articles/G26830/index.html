Hotel Sunmerry Hotel 4: reviews and descriptions

Description: Hotel Sunmerry Hotel 4 (Kemer) is located near the airport of Antalya, in the small village of Belbidi. For guests, the institution first opened its doors in 2005. Now it is a hotel with a modern developed infrastructure, ready to receive tourists at the highest level. The main focus of the institution is a family vacation. Youth companies will also have a good time at the hotel, for which a huge amount of entertainment is provided here. The spacious grounds include a beautiful garden. The proximity to major cities, such as Antalya or Kemer, allows tourists to get out of the hotel on excursions without spending a lot of time on the road, which in some cases is very tiresome.





Rooms: Hotel Sunmerry Hotel 4, reviews about it are numerous, has rooms of various categories. Each is equipped with air conditioning and carpeted floors. The service includes daily cleaning. There are several rooms with facilities for people with reduced mobility. Most apartments have a furnished balcony or terrace.

Meals: At the Sunmerry Hotel 4, reviews note this, quality food is provided, organized as a buffet. The restaurant, located on the outdoor terrace, which seats up to 200 people, serves a chic table with a variety of dishes three times a day. There is also a children's menu. On the territory of the institution there are three bars.





Beach: The hotel has its own beach, which is located across from the establishment. Here guests are offered umbrellas, mattresses and sun loungers. Pebble beach. There is a point on the beach where you can rent diving equipment.
