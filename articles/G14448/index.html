Alpina Phuket Nalina Resort & Spa 4 *: hotel reviews

Phuket - the largest island of Thailand, which is located in the Indian Ocean. Back in the 19th century, the main industries here were the extraction of tin and rubber. Now it is a world famous resort that attracts with its sea beauties, unforgettable diving, graceful Buddhist temple architecture and rich nature.

Cosiness and comfort

Alpina Phuket Nalina Resort & Spa 4 *, located on Karon Beach, is great for family holidays. It is convenient to relax with kids and teens. There is a playground for little travelers, a nanny provides their services, for the convenience of the kids there is children's furniture.



The hotel also feels comfortable for those who prefer to travel alone, because you can find a company to your liking already in Phuket.

This hotel is also the best choice for those who do not leave business even on vacation. There is every opportunity to hold business symposia. Several conference rooms equipped with all necessary equipment meet modern business requirements.

Thai spa

Rest is relaxation and bliss. The hotel spa offers Thai massage, which has absorbed the best of traditional Chinese and Indian medicines.

Despite the pain in the muscles that can occur immediately after the massage, an unprecedented energy boost is felt after half an hour.



After cosmetic procedures and facial massage, wrinkles disappear, and the skin becomes younger before our eyes. They will enhance the effect and bring real relaxation reflexology, wraps with seaweed and mud.

Convenience for every taste

The rooms of the Alpina Phuket Nalina Resort & Spa 4 * hotel are distinguished by cosiness, thought out to the smallest detail design, stunningly beautiful view from the windows. There is everything to feel comfortable.

Air conditioning allows you to relax in the cool of a cozy room during the midday heat and at night after the dance floor or evening shows. Comfortable high-quality furniture and a clean bed in combination with the fragrant air of Phuket make sleep sound and calm. You can drink tea or coffee in the room, for the preparation of these drinks there is all the necessary utensils. And free Wi-Fi and television will allow you to be in touch all the time and to know the latest news.

Depending on your preference, you can choose a room with access to the pool or luxurious apartments with a huge bathroom with Jacuzzi, kitchen, living room, dining room, access to the pool and lagoon.

If you want to relax in a big company, then the best option is to stay in a tropical villa. It even has its own well-kept courtyard, a picturesque garden of tropical plants and its own pool.

The morning before breakfast, as well as the midday heat, is best spent by the pool. Here you can enjoy fresh water, relax in comfortable sun loungers, sip refreshing drinks and cocktails, as well as refresh yourself with barbecue dishes.

Hotel Alpina Phuket Nalina Resort & Spa 4 * has a refined and varied cuisine. Small picks and adult gourmets will be pleased with seafood, meat, vegetables, desserts and a variety of fruits.

Culinary establishments

At the Clef Lounge bar, you can enjoy any of your favorite alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, which are plentiful. And Piece of Cake will not leave indifferent lovers of pastries and desserts.



In the evening, it is so pleasant to have dinner in a good company at Pizzeria Mama Mia - a restaurant of traditional Italian cuisine, where you can try delicious pizzas and pastas.

If you want exotic food, it is better to go to Tropicana. Here you can try both traditional Thai cuisine and your favorite international gastronomic hits.

Kata - The Pearl of Phuket

Hotel Alpina Phuket Nalina Resort & Spa 4 * is located 700 meters from the magnificent Kata Beach. Of course, the path from the hotel to the beach can be overcome on foot, admiring the green streets. But the hotel takes care of its guests, so it is better to use a comfortable free hotel bus, which takes you to the beach in 3 minutes. It runs every hour during the day.

Kata Beach is one of the best in Phuket. It is clean and beautiful. Thickets of casuarine trees protect from sun and heat. The sand is small and silky, and the descent into the water is gentle and pleasant. Stones rarely come across in the water, mostly the bottom is strewn with the same gentle sand as the beach itself.

Here everyone will find entertainment to their liking: there are points for renting equipment for yachting and windsurfing, surfing, you can rent water skis and ride a banana. And in the northern part of the beach there is a coral reef with bright inhabitants, which will not leave indifferent fans of diving and snorkeling.

It is so convenient to sit on a deck chair, contemplate the azure expanse of the sea and relax from swimming or surfing. The waiters will bring drinks and snacks directly to the deck chair.



It is so pleasant to return from the beach to Alpina Phuket Nalina Resort - a cozy, hospitable and cool hotel that will forever remain in your memory. Here you can relax in the hall, furnished with modern comfortable armchairs, climb into a cool room, contemplate the fish in the pond or relax by the pool.

The best choice for a comfortable and bright holiday

Hotel Alpina Phuket Nalina Resort & Spa 4 * is the best choice for spending a honeymoon or just getting away from winter frosts or autumn slush.

Hotel guests always note the hospitality and friendliness of the staff, comfortable rooms, special beds deserve special praise, slippers and bathrobes that other 4 * hotels do not provide.

Hotel breakfasts, included in the price, are distinguished by their exquisite taste and a wide selection of European and Thai salads, meat dishes, sweets and juicy tropical fruits. You can also order tidbits for your lunch and dinner.

Thais decorate their dishes with exotic plants, and the food every time looks elegant and extravagant. Despite the fact that the beach is located seven hundred meters from the hotel, guests note that it is very close. Apparently, a wonderful mood contributes to this, which creates a riot of nature in combination with the tropical sun and walks to the beach through cozy streets.

It is also recommended by tourists who choose Phuket for their unforgettable vacation, be sure to take more excursions and not be limited to beach vacations and promenades.
