What will help you learn how to disable the service on Megaphone

Everything related to changing services by phone numbers of the Megafon mobile operator can be found in the following ways: by coming to the nearest office of the company, calling the hot number, and going to the subscriber’s personal account. The managers in the salon and on the phone will listen to you, ask clarifying questions and tell you how to solve the problem. There is only one “but”: the company’s office still needs to be reached. In addition, they often have problems with the Internet, so in order to learn how to disable the service on the Megaphone, it will take a long time.

Less time will be required to call the company hotline. The phone numbers of Megaphone are 8 800 550-05-00 and 0505. However, before managers start talking with you, you will have to talk with an answering machine. Even if you quickly connect with the operator, it’s not a fact that he will immediately answer the question: “How to disable the service on the Megaphone?” A favorite pastime for telephone managers on all cellular networks is to transfer a call from one department to another.





The fastest and most nerve-saving way is to log into the subscriber’s personal account. You can do this on the official site of Megaphone. At the top of the page, on the right, is a SIM card icon and the inscription “My Account” (in brackets “Service Guide”). Click on the link and go to a new window. Enter the mobile phone number in the window and click on the “Login” button. If you have already used this service and remember the password, enter the numbers and go to your personal account. If not, you can get it in several ways:

- dial the command * 105 * 00 # and click on the "Connect" button;

- send an SMS with the text "00" to the number 000105;

- click on the link “Get Password” and answer the security question.

You can find out how to disable the service on the Megaphone from your personal account in the "Rates and Services" section. Here are listed all the opportunities provided by the company to the subscriber. All basic services are divided into groups. There are five of them: "Basic Services", "The rest", "Finance", "Internet", "Popular". You just need to select the service group and the opportunity that you would like to disable, and then uncheck the box next to its name.





For example, find out how to disable the "Live Balance" service on the Megaphone. We go to the subsection "Changing the set of services", select the group "Finance" and uncheck this type of service. It can also be disabled by telephone by typing * 135 * 0 #. In response, you will receive a message that the option will be disabled for 10 minutes.





In order to learn how to disable the Weather service on Megaphone, you need to go to the Additional Services section. From there, go to the Mobile Subscriptions page, enter the phone number, check digit, sent SMS password again and select “Weather”. A list of your subscriptions will be displayed at the top of the page, so that an unnecessary service can be quickly found. You can disable this type of service by sending an SMS with the text “stop pp” to 5151.

From the foregoing, it can be seen that the fastest and most effective way to find out how to disable the service on the Megaphone is to use the subscriber’s personal account.
