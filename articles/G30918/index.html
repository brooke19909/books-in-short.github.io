The recreation center "Blue Lake" (Berdsk, Novosibirsk Region): recreation, prices and reviews of tourists

For any person living in the bustle of a noisy and gassed metropolis, it is important to properly organize your vacation or weekend. How nice to break out for a couple of days at a comfortable base, which would be located in a forested area. Our article is dedicated to one of the best tourist complexes in the city of Berdsk, Novosibirsk Region, a complex under the beautiful name "Blue Lake".





According to vacationers and the approval of local authorities, this is one of the few places of relaxation where pleasure and benefit are harmoniously intertwined. The recreation center "Blue Lake" is simply surrounded by greenery. Near it are concentrated recreational and natural resources, unique objects of historical and cultural heritage. Large-scale sporting and social events are held here regularly.

General information about the pension





A 10-minute walk from the picturesque Ob reservoir is a magnificent recreation center "Blue Lake" (Berdsk). She is known far beyond the region. Very popular among couples with children, wildlife connoisseurs and fishing enthusiasts.

The guesthouse's doors are open year-round. In autumn and winter, good discounts are provided, and for regular customers - benefits. People come here who want to enjoy the silence, get positive emotions and recharge their batteries on the coast of a clear lake filled with carps. For each age category there are classes on interests.

Number of rooms





In a pine forest, two-story buildings, wooden summer houses and comfortable cottages made of timber are comfortably located. The recreation center "Blue Lake" offers standard, "junior suite" and "luxury" rooms. Summer houses are double, equipped with a bathroom and water supply with cold water, their cost is within 1300 p.

If you want more comfortable conditions, we recommend that you settle in a two-room "junior suite". It is divided into a living room, bedroom and kitchen area. It has a loggia, necessary appliances (especially convenient for parents with children) and the Internet. This number will cost you 3000 p. (per day for 1 person).

Apart from the rest of the houses are guest cottages, designed for those who are accustomed to luxury. The first floor consists of a large fireplace room with modern furniture, a dining and kitchen area, as well as a pool, a bathroom and a sauna. On the second floor - 4 rooms with a refined renovation. On holidays, you will be charged at least 15,000 p. (per day), on weekdays - about 10,000 p.

Nutrition





Separately, the hotel pays for meals. If desired, you can order only breakfast, as well as a two-time or three-time diet. The recreation center "Blue Lake" (Novosibirsk / Berdsk) has on its territory a spacious dining room, which serves delicious homemade dishes. Caring and skilled chefs prepare airy pastries and delicious desserts, which, undoubtedly, will appeal to the sweet tooth.

Service offered

The highlight and pride of the boarding house is the presence of a natural lake. In the summer, the swimming season opens. The beach is clean and well maintained. The recreation center "Blue Lake" is equipped with cozy gazebos with awnings, tables and barbecues, where guests can usefully spend their leisure time: fry kebabs, cook any dishes on the barbecue or sing with a guitar.





For large companies, a huge terrace with 200 seats is offered. After a busy day's rest, be sure to visit the sauna or the original Russian bath with brooms. This is a great option for complete relaxation and purification.

Free parking for 25 cars is available. For active people, the Blue Lake Base will offer sports in the gym. At any time of the year, the complex organizes mass fishing for carp and grass carp. During major holidays, such as Epiphany, Christmas, Shrovetide, Easter, the administration arranges fun festivities and swimming in a pond.

Tourists who come to combine leisure with work can always use three conference rooms for 20, 60 or 150 people. All rooms are technically equipped for business or special occasions. A large flat-screen TV is provided for video presentations. Professionally organized any event will help attentive staff.

Children's entertainment





The recreation center "Blue Lake" accepts parents with young children. For children under 10 years of age (without beds), you can not pay extra. There will be no time for kids and teenagers to be bored and naughty, because a spacious game town with swings, slides and horizontal bars has been built for them. There is a center for the rental of children's ATVs.

Active leisure

In addition to fishing and hiking tours, the recreation center "Blue Lake" (Berdsk) recommends horseback riding or water activities. There is a tennis court and a football field. There is a billiard room, and in winter a skating rink. Sports equipment is issued at the rental point - on bail.

Summing up, I want to add that the tourist complex "Blue Lake" (Novosibirsk / Berdsk) is suitable for people of different social status and financial wealth. Management wisely approached the organization of services and services, so it is always crowded (especially in summer) and comfortable. Numerous reviews confirm this fact.

In this green corner of the Novosibirsk region developed health, sports, beach and sightseeing tourism. The boarding house was created to give new forces and a cheerfulness to residents who are tired of urban everyday life. This is facilitated by wildlife, clean air, the absence of a noisy public and a friendly atmosphere.
