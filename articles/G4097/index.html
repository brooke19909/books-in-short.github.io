Greece

If we talk about the best resorts in the world, then Greece should definitely be noted. It can be considered a budget option. If you compare, of course, with the Bahamas, for example, or Miami. Greece leads the list of the most popular and best resorts in Europe.

Greek islands are very popular among holidaymakers. For example, the one called Santorini. A great option for romantics. It is in this place that you can admire amazing landscapes, soak up the modest secluded beaches of black sand and swim in the warm sea.

The island of Halkidiki impresses with the number of interesting excursions offered. Mykonos, Rhodes, Crete and Corfu are considered ideal for young people, because it is where the noisiest and largest dance floors and night clubs are located.

Speaking about the best resorts in the world, it is worth saying that Greece is a state of contrasts. Modern expensive hotels with an all-inclusive system, night clubs and restaurants, as well as excursions, ancient sights, picturesque views - there is everything for everyone. A universal resort where everyone will find something to do. That is why Greece is one of the most popular European holiday destinations.

In general, Jamaica is definitely among the best resorts in the world. Photos of these places prove this undoubted fact. So if you have money and want an unforgettable experience, warmth and exotic, then you should fly to Jamaica!

Winter resorts

Of course, most often people leave to relax in warm places where the sun shines brightly and the ocean or sea surf caresses their ears. However, sometimes you want a real Christmas tale - lush snow, hot mulled wine and skiing! Well, for this there are the best ski resorts in the world.

The first thing to note is the Alps. This is the place that takes first place in the list of "Best Resorts in the World." The rating first draws the attention of people studying it to Switzerland. This is one of the most expensive, developed and picturesque countries in Europe. And there the mountain ranges occupy a vast territory. Switzerland is an ideal place for people who love winter romance. It is so nice to go skiing or snowboarding downhill, return to a cozy wooden guest house with a fireplace and enjoy a cup of hot cocoa.

The Italian Alps, by the way, are also popular. This is an incredibly picturesque place. In addition, there are different slopes for people of varying degrees of preparedness. Surprisingly, if we talk about ski resorts, then Italy comes out more expensive than Switzerland. But more beautiful, it is worth recognizing.





The pearl of Russia

It is nice that the Russian resort is included in the list of the best ski resorts. And this is Sochi, Adler, Krasnaya Polyana. The place where the Winter Olympics was held! High, massive mountains, strewn with snow, snowboards, skis, cozy souvenir shops, five-star hotels and excellent restaurants, various kinds of entertainment - all this can be obtained by arriving in the resort capital of Russia, in Sochi. In addition, it is a city of contrasts. It is worth going down from Krasnaya Polyana, when a tourist gets into the warm and sunny Adler with Olympic facilities, shopping and entertainment centers and a noisy sea promenade.
