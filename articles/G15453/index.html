What to look for: user recommendations

When deciding which bank has the most profitable consumer loan, reviews should be studied first. Those who have already taken loans repeatedly have come across a number of pitfalls that you can easily ignore. Consider the most common of them:

  • Sometimes, in commercials, banks use only part of the real information about credit rates and loan conditions. The fact is that this or that rate is calculated on the basis of the documents provided. In some situations, a minimum commission is possible only after completing comprehensive insurance. If it is not done, then the interest commission automatically increases, which the client of the bank does not even guess about until he receives the first notice of debt.
  • Some, deciding which loan is the most profitable, consider that with an early repayment of a loan, you can significantly save on overpayments. However, this is not the case. Some banks provide penalties for early deposit of funds. However, such conditions are usually typical for not the most popular organizations, but only for those that just want to get a lot more commissions, without looking far into the future and not particularly puzzled by their reputation.
  • Many believe that by making monthly payments through the cashier at the bank, you can save a lot. This is actually not the case. Redemption through special terminals or when using a computer is much more profitable. In this case, no operator service fee is charged.
  • The biggest problem banks customers face is late payment fines. If you comply with the terms of the contract in good faith, then there will be no consequences. All confusion begins when a bank customer makes a payment later than the promised date. In this case, the penalty system may differ depending on the bank. To avoid troubles, it is necessary to clarify the operator‚Äôs possible sanctions. It is also worth clarifying which paid services will be included in the package. Sometimes a loan involves an additional monthly payment of notifications that come to the phone. If the payment amount does not include this option, then additional interest for late payments may begin to accrue.

Before signing the contract, you need to re-read it several times and check with the operator the exact size of the interest rate, as well as the exact amount from which it is calculated.
