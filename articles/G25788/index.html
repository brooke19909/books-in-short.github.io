Guest House "Comfort", Adler: description, infrastructure and reviews of tourists

Adler is one of the most sought after and popular Black Sea resorts. A unique sea and developed infrastructure of a modern resort city are inviting guests here.





What accommodation to choose in Adler?

Most often, future guests of the resort town begin to think about organizing a summer sea holiday long before it begins. Today there are a huge number of offers from travel agencies, organizations and individual entrepreneurs, individuals who offer services in this area. The proposed options are often similar in terms of the set of services and price; the choice always remains with the customer. The best option for your vacation is to rent an inexpensive guest house in Adler.

This is the name of the cottage, the owners of which are private individuals. For tourists, separate rooms are rented in it, usually there are not more than thirty of them. The cost of living in such cottages is an order of magnitude lower than in hotels, and the level of service is often not inferior to them. Great competition obliges the owners of guest houses to provide quality services, to carry out work on the improvement of the house and the surrounding area, to introduce new, sometimes unique, options for vacationers.

Guest House Uyut (Adler)

One of the factors that vacationers make a choice when booking accommodation by the sea is its convenient location. The Uyut Guest House in Adler has this advantage. St. Lenin, on which it is located, is the central thoroughfare of the city. This allows tourists to actively use city transport and easily get from the station or airport, visit interesting places of the city and its surrounding corners, where there are many attractions and objects offering a wide variety of entertainment.



Guest House Uyut (Adler) is located next to the sea and the promenade, a distance of three hundred meters separates them. The famous resort town is within walking distance from it. This location is convenient for everyone - families with small children, youth and people of considerable age. Each of them will find his own in this entertainment area. This is a visit to the water park or seawater pool, dolphinarium or oceanarium, nightclubs and bowling. In the resort town there are many restaurants and cafes that adequately meet and deliciously feed their guests. Night clubs, discos and bars attract lovers of nightlife here.

Accommodation in the guest house "Comfort"

Cozy and beautiful rooms for location and relaxation are provided by the Uyut Guest House (Adler). This establishment is small, guests are offered a choice of 18 rooms, in which one to four people can be accommodated. If necessary, the owners will offer to supplement the room with another berth - a folding bed, such a service is possible with an additional fee.



All rooms of the guest house are equipped with furniture - these are beds with bedside tables, spacious wardrobes for things. The comfort of the rooms increases the availability of air conditioning, a refrigerator and a TV. Guests, based on their financial capabilities, can choose two options for living: rooms with a bathroom and shower or rooms where the amenities will be shared, located on the floor.

Ironing facilities are offered to guests, they are in the equipped common ironing room. For guests' needs there is a washing machine.

Territory and beach

Guest House Uyut (Adler) is located in a fairly quiet corner of the resort town. The owners do a lot to ensure that the territory of their possession is landscaped, cozy and beautiful. In the courtyard of the guest house there is a relaxation area - under the umbrellas are comfortable tables with chairs. An equipped platform with barbecue facilities will allow vacationers to taste fresh barbecue or baked fish. The guests here are pleased to get to know each other, communicate pleasantly. One of the "advantages" of the guest house is the parking lot, which residents can use.



The beach is nearby, within walking distance along a quiet and shady street. This is a city pebble beach. It is clean, the necessary equipment can be rented here for a fee. Beach holidays will be remembered by the warm sea and visiting beach attractions. There are showers and a toilet.
