Frequency converters for pumps: prices, specifications and reviews

Frequency inverters for pumps are needed to adjust the power of the motors. As a result, the pressure in the system is maintained at the proper level. High-quality converters can save a lot of electricity. And this should be taken into account. At the same time, a wide variety of pumps can be used. The most common are systems for supplying water to the house. Converters are also required for circulation pumps. Additionally, they can be installed in fountains and aquariums.





Converters Features

A distinctive feature of all converters for pumps is their simplicity. However, they do not need any maintenance and work absolutely automatically. Additionally, it is possible to control them through a personal computer. You can also set individual schedules of the device. Moreover, their efficiency is about 90%. You should also be aware that an expansion tank is not needed for pumps with converters. Thus, the pressure is always maintained at an optimal level.





What characteristics do the converters have?

Important characteristics of the converters are considered input voltage, as well as power. Additionally, the manufacturer always indicates the type of control. Today, there are scalar and vector control devices. The rated current parameter depends on the power of the model. You can also highlight the indicator of the output frequency. Usually it is indicated in the range from 0.1 to 600 Hz. Overload capacity is calculated as a percentage. The degree of protection of the converter housing is indicated by a special marking. The operating temperature of the device by the manufacturer is also mandatory. Among other things, it should highlight the parameter of acceleration time, as well as braking.
