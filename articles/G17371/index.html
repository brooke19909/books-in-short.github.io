Smartphone "Lenovo S660" (Lenovo S660): specifications, settings, firmware and customer reviews

For Lenovo, 2014 was a very productive year. The model range was supplemented by both budget devices and powerful smartphones. One of the presented novelties was the S660 model.

Design





Phone "Lenovo S660" is a fairly compact device. The size of the device is slightly smaller than usual due to the screen of 4.7 inches. A slight decrease in the diagonal made it possible to make a gadget comfortable to use. The user can work with the device even with one hand.

The material of the case has undergone drastic changes. In Lenovo S660, aluminum replaced plastic. This solution significantly increased the smartphone's resistance to scratches and dents. Of course, if you want to damage the device, it will not be difficult, but the metal saves from accidental injuries.

In such an interesting case material there are also disadvantages. The surface of the smartphone incredibly quickly collects fingerprints. Naturally, wiping the phone is not difficult, but it is unpleasant.

Due to the metal, the device looks solid and pretty stylish. Although there are no special changes in appearance, the highlight in the device is definitely present.

The front side placed a display, sensors, camera, speaker, touch buttons and logo on it. On the right side is the volume control and the power button. The back side got a logo, a main camera, a speaker, a flash and a microphone. The top end has become a haven for the headphone jack, and the bottom is reserved for USB.

In general, the device has an interesting appearance. The build quality is not satisfactory, and the durability of the device at altitude. It is a suitable design for an entertaining smartphone.

Display





Equipped with "C660" screen only 4.7 inches. Quite a strange solution for a company that equips even budget devices with a large size. An unpleasant fact will be the low resolution. Smartphone "Lenovo S660" has a total of 960 by 540 pixels. This is an incredibly low feature and is more suitable for phones with four inches. Accordingly, pixels are visible even with the naked eye.

The IPS matrix of the smartphone brightens the situation somewhat. The screen on the Lenovo S660 is quite bright, which ensures high-quality work in the sun. There is also a manual and automatic adjustment of the display. In addition to brightness, the device boasts a high viewing angle.

Camera





I got "Lenovo S660" familiar to low-cost models of 8 megapixels. An acceptable camera for a budget employee looks rather strange in a more expensive series. The resolution 3264 by 2448 also worsens the impression. All the characteristics speak of the average quality of the images, but things are much worse.

In fact, the device takes photos that are expected more from a five-megapixel camera. The device lacks sharpness, but the noise is in excess. The "Lenovo S660" setting will not solve the problem either. The user will have to forget about night photos, because the pictures are incredibly bad.

A similar situation happened with video recording. The shooting takes place in HD-quality, but the result is disappointing. The camcorder has the same problems as the photo.

Poor implementation of the main camera does not give hope for a front camera. The phone has a peephole of only 0.3 megapixels and is suitable only for video calls. You should not expect more from the front-end.

The strongest side of Lenovo S660 is the hardware. The company equipped the phone with a MTK processor commonplace for the Chinese. Naturally, the Snapdragon would be the best choice for an advanced model, but the Chinese have taken the path of saving. The processor has four cores, each of which operates at a frequency of 1.3 GHz.

Although there are no doubts about the performance of the filling, similar parameters are present in many state employees of the company. There is a feeling that the "C660" is not in its series.

The smartphone has a gigabyte of RAM. This amount of memory is enough to maximize the potential of the device. The combined efforts of the RAM and the processor allow the "C660" to easily solve a lot of problems.

Another parameter will not especially please the owners of Lenovo "S660". We are talking about 8 GB of native memory. Unfortunately, the user will be able to use only 6 GB. The remaining memory is allocated for Android. You can increase the memory with a 32 GB card.
