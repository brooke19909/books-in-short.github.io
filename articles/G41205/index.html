Phone Xiaomi Redmi 4 Pro: review, specifications, reviews

Xiaomi Redmi 4 Pro smartphone is a worthy continuation of the legendary Redmi 3S. The latest model made a splash and became a bestseller. After the sale, users and experts recognized it as the best in the segment of budget devices. Undoubtedly, the gadget perfectly combines the level of quality and price.

The Chinese manufacturer regularly updates the lineup. The fourth generation of Redmi is represented by three models. The most popular is precisely Xiaomi Redmi 4 Pro. The price for it varies within 130 dollars. What exactly is the price offered to users? What are the advantages and disadvantages of this gadget? As part of the article, answers to these questions will be given, as well as technical specifications and feedback from the owners.



Equipment

Designers worked very hard, developing the design of the packaging box. Outwardly, it looks simple, but quite attractive. You may find distant similarities with Apple products. The basis was taken in white. Against his background is a photograph of the smartphone Xiaomi Redmi 4 Pro. It depicts a gadget from both the front and the back. In the upper right corner is the company logo, highlighted in orange. Directly below the image is the name of the phone. Everything is quite simple, but tasteful.

Looking inside the packaging box, you can see that the device is packed very tightly. Directly beneath it are a USB cable, a power adapter and a paper clip that opens the SIM card tray. There is also a package of documents. In it, in addition to the manual, the manufacturer provides a certificate and a warranty card. Headphones and other accessories are not provided. They will need to be purchased separately.

Design

Review Xiaomi Redmi 4 Pro must begin with a description of the design. The appearance is pretty ordinary. For him, the manufacturer used a standard, generally accepted style. A rectangular case with slightly rounded corners looks quite manly. The first thing that catches the eye of buyers is the unusual material of the back panel. With a cursory glance, it is almost impossible to determine that it is metal. It feels a little rough, but quite pleasant. The only thing that testifies to the metal surface is a pleasant cold during touch. A plastic insert is located only at the top of the gadget. The Xiaomi Redmi 4 Pro phone is assembled perfectly, there are no gaps.

By purchasing this model, users checked the build quality. Comments to her did not arise. All parts are fitted as accurately as possible. Squeaks and backlash are absent. If you do not look closely at the manufacturer, then at first glance the phone will be difficult to call a state employee. In the hand, according to users, the device is quite comfortable. The Xiaomi Redmi 4 Pro has a fingerprint scanner. The need to unlock the device using the key disappears by itself. The scanner is located on the rear panel at the top. The location of all communication connectors is identical to the Redmi 3S model. The changes affected only the camera lens, which is now closer to the center, and the location of the speaker hole (brought to the bottom).

According to users, the only drawback is the lack of backlight control panel. All keys on it are touch-sensitive. They have special designations. The lack of backlighting affects only the aesthetic perception, but during operation it does not cause the user any particular inconvenience.



Talk about the screen

In Xiaomi Redmi 4 Pro, the display specifications impressed everyone without exception. Few people expected such indicators from the state employee. Its size is 5 ∫. It is manufactured in compliance with IPS technology. The manufacturer used 2.5D glass. The resolution with which the picture is displayed can be considered amazing. It equals 1920 √ó 1080 px.

High-quality color reproduction, excellent contrast and maximum viewing angles are significant advantages of this phone. The brightness range allows you to comfortably work on your smartphone both in a dark room and in direct sunlight. The developers have provided in the settings special options that provide the user with a choice of any mode - calm or saturated. There is also an automatic function for adjusting the lighting. However, according to users, there are several flaws. If you accidentally cover the sensor, the device instantly reacts and changes the brightness level, which is sometimes quite annoying. In most cases, this option is very rarely used. The owners claim that the most comfortable is the brightness set at 40%.

The glass that protects the screen has excellent properties. It is most resistant to scratches and scuffs. When falling from a small height, it prevents the appearance of cracks. The developers did not forget about the oleophobic coating. Unfortunately, it does not fully protect against the formation of fingerprints on the surface, but still reduces the level of soiling.



Hardware "stuffing"

It's time to consider the performance characteristics in Xiaomi Redmi 4 Pro. Naturally, this indicator is the most important for most buyers. The model is based on the Snapdragon 625 processor. It is based on 8 computing modules that accelerate to a frequency of 2000 megahertz. The processor is assembled according to the technical process of 14 Nm. As you know, the lower this indicator, the more efficient the hardware platform.

The main chipset is paired with the Adreno 506 graphics accelerator. It can quickly process any type of image. Each user can not worry about the fact that when you install an animated image on the main screen, the device will begin to slow down or freeze.

This phone model can compete with some flagships in terms of RAM. The developers used in it as much as 3 GB. Naturally, such equipment will appeal to even the most demanding user.

In this line, there are two versions: 32 and 64 GB. Xiaomi Redmi 4 Pro supports working with external drives. They are installed in the same tray as the SIM card. Expand storage up to 128 GB.

What about the level of performance? According to this criterion, users did not have any comments. All tasks are carried out with a bang. Even powerful games run smoothly on the device and work without brakes.



Platform

Xiaomi Redmi 4 Pro 32Gb is based on Android 6 version. By the way, the same equipment is used in the version on 64Gb. User reviews often raise the topic of custom firmware. In this case, the information is only partially presented in Russian. Using this version is extremely inconvenient, so the owners have found a way to rectify the situation. Through Mi Flash, you can change the firmware to the official MIUI 8. After installing it, the device works smoothly, stuttering, freezes, crashes and other troubles do not arise.

Some owners are faced with a couple of unpleasant moments. They appeared during the unlocking of the device with a swipe and after installing some themes from the store.

Camera Features

The optics used in the Xiaomi Redmi 4 Pro, the reviews received mixed. In some users, they say that the photos are of a fairly high quality. Others have information about digital noise. Most often, it occurs if you take pictures in low light. Also, the camera does not work well if there are a lot of light sources. In this case, it is recommended to use manual focus.

It is the latter that allows you to significantly increase the quality of photos. There is a flash. Optics can not be called the strongest side of this gadget. But as for its price range, it does pretty well. Users say that it is necessary to adapt to it.

In order to take pictures with the Xiaomi Redmi 4 Pro phone while on the street, it is recommended to activate the HDR mode. With its help, the color saturation is significantly increased.

As for the front camera, its capabilities are limited by a 5 megapixel sensor. Selfies are pretty decent. However, during shooting, you must constantly monitor the lighting. If it is not enough, then blurring and poor detail are clearly visible in the photographs.





Battery Life

The duration of a single charge on the Xiaomi Redmi 4 Pro (32Gb // 64Gb) will depend on the firmware version. Unfortunately, the custom one very quickly consumes battery life. With an average load, the user will have a 100% charge for 1-1.5 days. However, the situation is corrected by installing the official MIUI 8 Global firmware. With it, the device is able to work for two or three days. Such indicators are provided by a lithium-ion battery. The Xiaomi Redmi 4 Pro is equipped with a battery with a capacity of 4100 mAh.

Those owners who use the smartphone quite intensively can expect about 35 hours. And only after that you will need to connect to the network.

Communication opportunities

The Xiaomi Redmi 4 Pro review will not be complete if you do not consider its communication capabilities. This version of the gadget has an infrared port, "Bluetooth" version 4.2, which is highly energy efficient, Wi-Fi module.

According to most users, the device works well with networks of both the third and fourth generation. Navigation capabilities are provided by GPS and GLONASS.
