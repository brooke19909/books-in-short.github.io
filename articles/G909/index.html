How to get to Abkhazia from Moscow

Abkhazia is an amazing country that attracts thousands of tourists every year. Most vacationers come to rest from neighboring Russia. If you still do not know how to get to Abkhazia, then it's time to find out. The departure point will be Moscow, which separates 1820 kilometers from the capital of Abkhazia - Sukhum.



Stage One: Moscow-Adler





Travel to Abkhazia can be divided into two parts. At the first stage, the main goal is to get to Adler. This is one of the districts of the city of Sochi, not far from which the Russian-Abkhaz border is located. You can get to Adler by plane from airlines that fly directly to Sochi. These include Ural Airlines, Yakutia, Aeroflot, Sky Express, Avianova. The flight takes 2 hours and 20 minutes, the cost varies from 3 000 rubles. Another option is by rail. But how to get to Abkhazia by train? There is no direct flight. From the capital of Russia you can only get to the Veseloe station. Then you can get to Abkhazia by the same train, you just have to pay extra. Travel time will be more than a day, and the minimum price is from 2 100 rubles per person. From Moscow, you can also get to Sukhum by bus in 29 hours (it will cost 2,500 rubles).

Stage Two: Adler Abkhazia

Now it is necessary to answer the question of how to get to Abkhazia from Adler. Firstly, to the border from Adler, which is about 8-10 kilometers, you can take a taxi at a price of 300 to 500 rubles. If you want to save, then choose public transport. True, you will have to go with transfers, and then walk another two kilometers. All together will cost you 30 rubles. When you cross the border, you can take a bus or take a taxi. There is another way how to get to Abkhazia from Adler - by train or by train. From Adler you can get to Sukhum or Gagra for 300 rubles or more. You will have to stand at the border for about an hour.





Crossing the border

For Russian citizens at the border, a visa is not required, as well as a foreign passport. It is enough to have with you a general document proving your identity. Immediately at the customs point you need to take out medical insurance. You can import no more than $ 10,000 from Russia, and if you export more than $ 3,000 from the country, you must declare this amount. If you enter the territory of Abkhazia by your car, then prepare a technical passport and driver's license for verification. If you are traveling by proxy, you must also have with you the consent of the owner to take the car abroad. These rules that are simple for Russian citizens must be observed in order to visit such a unique country as Abkhazia.

How to get there by car

If you still do not know how to get to this country with your car, you need to carefully study the map. You can also rely on the navigator or your own language, which, as you know, will bring not only to Kiev, but also to Abkhazia. In any case, head for the city of Sochi, then you will have to drive another 9 kilometers to the village of Vesyoloye. From here to the border - a stone's throw. If you are traveling in the hot tourist season, then get ready to stand at the border for several hours.
