"Alize Baby Wool": reviews of needlewomen, composition, colors

When the desire comes to create this or that thing, as a rule, its appearance is already quite clearly formed in the imagination. What shape will she have, what tools may be needed, what kind of decor. However, the problem often arises of choosing the material from which the product will be created. This applies directly to such types of applied art as crocheting or knitting, because the choice of yarn is simply huge. Even a concrete understanding of the characteristics of the yarn, such as composition, thickness and color, tactile impressions, does not save.

Materials whose characteristics are the same can behave very differently both during knitting and during operation. Much depends on the raw materials and the manufacturer, which becomes more every year. They are especially careful in choosing yarn for knitting for children. Since their skin is very sensitive, the material should be not only beautiful, but also soft, wear-resistant and hypoallergenic. One of these materials is considered to be Alize Baby Wool yarn. Reviews about it for the most part are very laudatory, however, in order to finally make a decision whether this material is suitable for knitting the intended products, it is necessary to carefully study its characteristics.

Manufacturer

They make yarn in Turkey. The Alize factory has been operating since 1984, in its assortment of more than 90 types of yarn, and every year it expands more and more. Here you can find both completely natural and mixed or synthetic threads, they differ not only in composition, but also in the way of torsion. The presence of both classic and fantasy materials allows you to create amazing products using completely simple schemes. The price range is quite wide, Alize factories produce both budget types of yarn and elite ones. Also pleases with the color palette of each line, thanks to this, even the most picky buyer will be able to choose the right yarn for a particular job.

From the line of children's yarn, Alize Baby Wool is especially popular.

Composition

This yarn consists of 40% wool, the same contains acrylic, as well as 20% bamboo. Wool is used not as merino, but as sheep, which significantly reduces the cost of the product. In addition, used restored bamboo fiber obtained by chemical treatment of the plant - viscose. If the raw materials were not washed well enough, the reagents can cause an allergic reaction, therefore, before using the finished product, it must be washed.

Specifications

In a small skein weighing 50 g is 175 meters of uniform thread, 3 mm thick. The yarn is quite voluminous, it consists of three threads twisted together. It has a light noble shine, very soft, a little cool at the first touch, it easily lets air through, but it retains heat. Such characteristics were achieved thanks to the competent selection of the proportions of the materials included in the composition. The manufacturer recommends using knitting needles from 2.5 to 4 mm and a hook number 1-3, however, according to reviews, Alize Baby Wool is too easy to stratify when crocheting, so it‚Äôs better to use a larger tool number.
