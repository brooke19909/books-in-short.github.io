How to get to Nizhny Novgorod from Moscow by car, train or bus

Nizhny Novgorod, one of the most famous millionaire cities, has attracted more and more tourists to its territories in recent years. This is partly facilitated by the government‚Äôs emphasis on the development of domestic tourism, but to a greater extent our compatriots and foreigners are attracted by the successful development of leisure services and facilities provided by both local authorities and entrepreneurs. In this regard, many are interested in how to get to Nizhny Novgorod from Moscow quickly, cheaply and with minimal loss of time.





Things to do

Nizhny Novgorod (in Soviet times - the city of Gorky) is rich in historical places and objects of cultural heritage. There is something to see here. Founded in 1221 by Vladimir Prince Yuri Vsevolodovich at the confluence of two large rivers, the Oka and Volga (famous arrow), it attracts tourists of various ages and areas of interest to its walls. Here you can not only enjoy the historical heritage of your native history, but also visit the Krasnoye Sormovo shipyard, visit the museum of the Gorky Automobile Plant, one of the domestic engineering giants, visit IT centers, ride two lines of your own metro, and just wander around central streets, enjoying gorgeous panoramic views of the Volga and Oka.

By plane

But now you packed your bags, stocked up with a map of the area and a list of attractions that you must visit. You only have a few days to rest and the logical question arose about how to get to Nizhny Novgorod from Moscow quickly. You can use the services of air carriers. Almost all major Russian operators: Aeroflot, S7 Airlines and UTair - direct flights from all three capital airports to Nizhny Novgorod Strigino daily. The average cost of such a flight will be 4,500 rubles. Over time, there will be no problems either. Cruise cars start from the capital from early morning until late at night. The plane "Nizhny Novgorod - Moscow" is on the way a little over an hour. Even taking into account the road to the airport, this is the fastest way to get to the capital of the Volga region. Tickets can be purchased at www.aviasales.ru or www.skyscanner.ru.





By train

How many of us have ever dreamed of falling asleep to a muffled knock of wheels lying on a reserved seat shelf and looking out the window at the scenery of our native country flying by? Many people now choose to travel by train, just on the basis of these considerations. The Nizhny Novgorod-Moscow train spends about six hours on the journey, depending on the number of stops. Moreover, trains come to Nizhny not only from the capital, but from many other cities of our country. These are Adler, Usinsk, Vorkuta, St. Petersburg, Kazan, Novorossiysk, Yekaterinburg and many others. A lot of branded trains arrive at the central station of the city: "Swallow", "Strizh", "Malachite", "Nizhegorodets", "Volga", "Vyatka" and "Northern Urals". Named train "Nizhny Novgorod - Moscow" spends less time on the way than a regular fast train. This is because the stopping points of such trains are located only in the largest cities in the direction of their route. "Swallow" flies a distance of 442 km in 4 hours and 13 minutes. On the way, this branded train has only 6 stops (including the final). However, there is an even faster option. On the route of the branded train "Strizh" - "Moscow - Nizhny Novgorod" - there is not a single stop at all. Travel time of this express train is 3 hours 35 minutes. The average fare is 1760 rubles. The ticket can be purchased at www.tutu.ru.





By bus

Bus "Nizhny Novgorod - Moscow" daily runs between the two cities. From the capital, you can hit the road from the bus stations at the metro stations "Schelkovskaya", "Novogireevo" and "Krasnogvardeiskaya". The cost of the trip will be from 700 to 1200 rubles, and the travel time is about 7-8 hours. The ticket is purchased for a specific seat at the bus ticket offices of the city or at www.busfor.ru. The seat is assigned to the passenger for the entire trip to the final destination. At stops, you can leave the cabin for the time agreed by the driver, without fear that someone will take your place. Landing in the cabin is carried out only on tickets. The bus "Nizhny Novgorod - Moscow" makes about 5-6 stops on the way. Express trains departing from Art. m. "Schelkovskaya" drive all the way non-stop and arrive at the square in front of the Moscow station (Revolution Square, 5a). The travel time of such an express train will be 6 hours (in the absence of traffic jams and traffic jams).





Own car

You can get to the capital of the Volga region by your own transport. Experienced motorists are always ready to tell you how to get to Nizhny Novgorod from Moscow in your car. To do this, just turn off the Moscow Ring Road (MKAD) to the Gorky Highway, the M7 highway (European route E22), and then follow the road signs in this direction. On the way, the autotraveller will pass cities such as Noginsk, Elektrostal, Orekhovo-Zuevo, Vladimir, Vyazniki, Dzerzhinsk and many others. Bypassing Vladimir and his suburban settlements, a magnificent bypass road was built. However, be careful not to violate the speed limit. DPS outfits are often on duty there. In the absence of traffic jams and not exceeding the speed by car, you can reach Nizhny Novgorod in 4.5-5 hours.
