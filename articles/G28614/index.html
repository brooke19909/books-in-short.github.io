How to make a valentine from corrugated paper or napkins

The proposed material is not quite suitable for folding, since it is not elastic enough and has a thin and delicate structure. Therefore, it can and should be used in a completely different way. Want to get a chic 3D-style heart? Corrugated paper or simple thin napkins are ideal for modeling such a hanging Valentine's card.

  1. Make a round paper base from cardboard with a diameter of about 3-4 cm.
  2. Cut from corrugated paper using a template, approximately 70-100 blanks.
  3. Form the foundation of the future heart from a well bending, but at the same time strong wire.
  4. Wrap the workpiece with synthetic winterizer or cotton, securing the top with elastic tape (preferably white).
  5. Fix each circle on the future Valentine's card with the help of glue ‚ÄúMoment‚Äù. To do this, drip a little in the middle, then lean it against the frame with this place and press down with the back of the pencil for 1-2 minutes. Adjust the density as you like.

How to make an Origami pendant Valentine?

    1. Take the square, put the top on the bottom.
    2. Repeat the previous step, doing the same with the sides.
    3. Flatten the paper. You have a square with two cruciform lines. They will need to be guided in future work.
    4. Tuck the bottom of the workpiece to the mid-horizontal line.
    5. Draw two diagonal lines. Below they should meet in the middle corner at the edge. On the sides they are directed to a point located in the middle of the upper side walls. Tuck the paper underneath along the marked diagonal lines. As a result, the bottom will get a heart shape.
    6. Place two sides on the main part of the valentine from paper, bringing them together.
    7. Tuck the two uppermost corners diagonally underneath. The ribs should be parallel to the lower triangle.
    8. Having outlined a fold on the workpiece just above the average horizontal line, fold the top edge down. When folding, the upper corner will fall into the pocket.
    9. Bend the small upper corners, giving the product the appearance of a heart.

Making a template and folding a Valentine's handbag

Consider how small souvenirs can be made for surprises and sweets. Try to collapse such a basket using the proposed template. To do this, print it out by selecting the desired scale. On it, make a blank from a fairly thick cardboard. If desired, before starting work, the base can be decorated with other finishing materials. Mark the dashed lines for folding, bending the workpiece along them and drawing a ruler along the seams for clarity, and holes for threading the ribbon. Do not make holes larger than 0.5 cm in diameter to avoid tearing the material. Place the workpiece in front of you in an upright position, as in the picture. Having taken the right and left upper parts, direct them towards each other and cross them. As a result, you should have matching holes. Do the same with the lower parts of the layout, lifting the middle link towards you and giving the product the desired shape. After threading the ribbon, the original paper bag is ready!

How to make small crafts with a surprise inside?





Even a simple heart cut out of paper can be decorated in a new way. To make such a valentine from colored paper, you need to make a template. Fold the material in half and place the fold vertically on the right side. Draw a curved line simulating a half heart. Cut a two-layer workpiece along the contour and expand it. To get more elegant crafts, use colorful fabrics or decorate monophonic items with multi-colored appliqu√©s and ornaments. The edge of the hearts can be cut with curly scissors in the form of cloves. Three design options for paper surprise Valentine

Consider the methods of combining two layers into one whole product. In order to do the job accurately, apply a thin, barely noticeable line of the future seam on the front side with a simple pencil.

First option. Machine line. Choose bright threads of contrasting shades for decoration. Set the maximum stitch length. In the places of the beginning and end of the seam, machine fix the seam.





The second option. A line of iron staples. To make ordinary iron clips for a stapler look more attractive, paint them with nail polish. To do this, cover the entire workpiece, without dividing into parts, with a thin layer on top and wait until it dries completely. Then insert the staples into the stapler and secure the product, moving along the intended line.

The third option. Lap seam. To make the product look elegant, you must first make a two-color lace before starting work by manually weaving yarn of different colors. Then, with the help of a gypsy needle, make neat holes at the same distance, through which pass the makeshift thread. Fill valentines with content just before giving.
