Kolibri Hotel 4 * (Turkey): reviews

In Turkey, a huge number of hotels for every taste of tourists. Very often, travelers prefer small hotels with no animation. A calm and comfortable stay offers Kolibri Hotel 4 * .

Where is?

The complex is located in the village of Avsallar. Antalya International Airport is approximately 100 km away. Getting to the hotel is easier and faster using the shuttle service. This service is paid separately and ordered in advance at the time of purchase of the tour.





Not far from Kolibri Hotel 4 * there are a variety of small shops and souvenir shops. Nearby are other complexes with almost the same arrangement.

Description

The territory of the hotel is small in comparison with large hotels. The complex is located on 2 thousand m 2 . The building has 6 floors. It was originally built in 1998, and in 2016 it was completely restored.





The hotel area is very well maintained and beautiful. Sidewalks are laid along it all the way to the beach. In the evening, colorful lights turn on everywhere. Large soft pillows are laid out on the lawns, on which guests can sit and relax. Such a cozy atmosphere is conducive to communication with neighbors.

Almost on the shore there are original gazebos with a large number of textile elements. It offers a beautiful view of the sea. The territory of Kolibri Hotel 4 * is divided into several relaxation areas. Guests can relax with a glass of wine on the spacious terrace, by the pool, in the small garden or in the gazebos.

Accommodations

Kolibri Hotel 4 * offers customers to settle in one of the rooms according to their wishes. They are all equipped with modern furniture and have a balcony with sea, city or garden views.

  • The standard double room is decorated in a discreet classic design. It has two single or one large bed, bedside tables, wardrobe, TV with satellite channels, air conditioning. The bathroom has a hairdryer and a set of hygiene items.
  • The basic room for 2 people is equipped according to the standard principle. Additionally, it has a minibar and single beds only.
  • The family room consists of a spacious room divided into zones. One has a double bed and the other has a fold-out sofa. Equipped with air conditioning, TV with satellite channels, minibar, hairdryer. The furniture is equipped with a wardrobe, bedside tables, work and coffee tables, chairs. The bathroom has hygiene items.




A safe is available in all rooms at an extra charge. Parents with babies are also provided with a cot with sides at their request. Guests can use the wake-up service.

Nutrition

Kolibri Hotel 4 * in Turkey operates on an all-inclusive basis. The guests of the complex fully eat in the restaurant on the basis of the "buffet". Here, for each meal, a varied menu is offered with the obligatory serving of several types of meat and fish.

Tourists, at their request, choose their favorite dishes and can take them in any quantity. There is also a snack bar near the pool. All kinds of alcoholic and milkshakes are prepared in it. At certain times, guests can enjoy fruit or light meals.





The complex has a small restaurant. In it, in addition to the main food, vacationers can make an order on their menu and relax with a fragrant hookah. Here you can celebrate any holiday with a birthday cake or other original dishes.

Beach and Pool

Kolibri Hotel 4 * in Alanya is located 50 meters from the sea. The hotel has its own private beach; only hotel guests can relax on it. Sun loungers and parasols are provided here.

There is a small bar on site where vacationers can take soft drinks or fruits. The entrance to the sea is gentle and sandy, so you can safely swim with young children.

The complex has an outdoor pool. It is divided into several zones:

  • children's (fenced);
  • adult
  • for descent from the hill.

The pool is always clean water. The staff daily cleans the bottom using special equipment. Water passes through filters.





A water slide in the form of a spiral is installed here. It has a not very high height, so both adults and children can descend from it. Around the pool are comfortable sun loungers and protective umbrellas. A special anti- slip coating has been laid .

Entertainment and additional services

Kolibri Hotel 4 Alanya has a small spa. In it, clients can take a steam bath in a Turkish bath according to all the traditional rules with the help of a specialist, go to the sauna or receive comprehensive skin care. There is also a massage room in the salon.





A playground has been built for young visitors on the territory of the complex. It is located far from the pool and is fenced. Here are installed modern plastic labyrinths and slides. The swing is located on a special coating that protects children from injuries in the fall.

The hotel does not use the services of animators, so in the evenings it is quiet and calm. Guests often gather on the terrace and chat with each other. Fans of outdoor activities have the opportunity to take an excursion program. You should familiarize yourself with its cost and route at the reception.

Kolibri Hotel 4 * in Turkey (Avsallar) has a spacious conference room. It has all the necessary equipment for meetings and negotiations. Businessmen also have a banquet room, which is served by a local restaurant.

Laundry facilities are located on site. There you can iron clothes. At the reception you can arrange car rental and use luggage storage.

Kolibri Hotel 4 * in Turkey: positive reviews

On various sites there are a lot of comments about the work of the complex. Tourists indicate that the rooms are comfortable and clean. The decor is not sophisticated and expensive, but the repair is fresh and the furniture in the rooms is modern. All devices are working properly.

Guests are satisfied with the friendly attitude of the staff. And they also indicate that many hotel workers speak Russian. This moment greatly simplifies communication and solving problems.





Many positive reviews are available regarding nutrition. Tourists are satisfied with the taste of dishes and a variety of menus. They note that in nearby 5-star hotels, meals were much more modest and worse in taste.

According to vacationers, the water in the pool is always clean and there was enough space on the sunbeds around it for everyone. Guests are pleased with the bartender's work in the snack cafe. He always listens to the wishes of tourists and makes cocktails in all proportions.

Guests are happy with the beach facilities. They are pleased with the gentle entrance to the sea and the sandy shore. Vacationers with children are happy with the opportunity to bathe their kids in clear water. Here, guys can play with sand while adults are sunbathing.

The guests of the complex praised the availability of cozy gazebos almost on the shore. They often in the evenings after an active day there enjoyed the beauty of the sea and silence. Cocktails and other drinks are served here from the bars.

Negative reviews

There are negative comments on the work of Kolibri Hotel 4 * in Turkey (Incekum, Alanya). Young tourists say that after a few days of staying here it becomes boring. Therefore, people who love noisy entertainment, it is better to look for hotels with animation and night clubs.

Some guests note that the maids are cleaned every few days and the quality is clearly lame if you do not tip them. In some reviews, there is dissatisfaction with the fact that Turkish families vacationing here are almost all the time in the pool, often there is very noisy and crowded, children splash and scream.

Tourists also indicate that when checking into a hotel, you can get a good room only after an offer to tip the manager at the reception. Without them, guests can enter the room no earlier than a few hours.

You may find dissatisfaction in the comments about overpriced spa services.
