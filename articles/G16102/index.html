Holiday Village "Mountain Air", Pavlovka. Description. Reviews

The recreation center "Mountain Air" in Pavlovka is an ideal place both for lovers of measured relaxation away from city noise, and for fans of an active lifestyle. And the Pavlovsk reservoir, on the banks of which it is located, is an excellent opportunity for inveterate fishermen to sit with a fishing rod on a morning dawn.

Description

The majestic Bashkir nature, clean mountain air and forest silence - all this you will find at the mountain air camp site in Pavlovka. It is surrounded by coniferous forests, steep mountain slopes, and a beach is equipped for guests on the banks of the Pavlovsky reservoir. The territory of the health resort is green and shady, especially numerous luxurious birch trees adorn it. Outdoor picnic enthusiasts can make use of the barbecue area. It offers vacationers boats, boats, bicycles, you will be helped with the organization of fishing. On a large equipped sports ground, those who wish to move are fighting in sports battles. Young tourists play in the playground.

Meals are organized in your own cafe. There is also a buffet with drinks, barbecue and freshly baked pastries, while refreshing drinks can be enjoyed at the beach bar. On a large territory there are many different houses, each of which has its own terrace. It offers tourists an equipped sanitary-shower complex, there is a shared kitchen. Finnish houses have their own bathroom. A Russian bath is working for you, you can swim in the summer pool. Car owners are offered a place in a guarded parking lot. The address of the recreation center "Mountain Air": Pavlovka, a village in the Nurimanovsky district of the Republic of Bashkortostan.

Accommodation for vacationers

For guests in the picturesque territory of the health resort, accommodation is offered in various houses, both ordinary summer and comfortable:

  • double summer house - the cost of living is 600 rubles per day for one person;
  • triple summer house - the price is 600 rubles per day per person;
  • four-seater summer house - the cost of living is also 600 rubles per day per person;
  • Finnish house (with two bedrooms, a kitchen, a refrigerator and a bathroom) - designed for four people with the possibility of one extra bed, the price for it is 4500 rubles per day.

The cost of all accommodation options at the camp "Mountain Air" in Pavlovka is indicated without meals. In the summer houses there is the necessary furniture. The terrace offers wonderful views of the lake or the forest.

Additional services

In order to make your vacation in the forest on the shore of the lake rich and varied, on the recreation center "Mountain Air" in Pavlovka guests are offered various services and entertainment.

  • There is a bar for you at the convenient beach, and for children there are children's sandboxes.
  • Tourists are given the opportunity to rent boats, catamarans, mountain bikes, sports equipment.
  • The camp site organizes boat trips for you.
  • You will also be helped with the organization of fishing.
  • Fans of reading on vacation are offered library services.
  • In a real Russian bath there is a spacious relaxation room.
  • Despite the fact that there is a beach on the lake, there is also a summer pool.
  • In addition to sports fields, there are tables for playing table tennis and billiards.
  • You can cook in the shared kitchen.
