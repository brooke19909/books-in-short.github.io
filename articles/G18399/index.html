A device that improves the indoor microclimate: types and features

Technology does not stand still. Modern industry creates unique devices that can significantly improve living conditions. Some of the devices not only improve the quality of life, but also improve the indoor climate. Which, in turn, affects the well-being of a person, increases his attention, perseverance and concentration. What microclimate devices currently exist?



The most popular equipment is air conditioning.

According to sanitary requirements, the air should be renewed in the living room once per hour. Air conditioning is not just a device that improves the microclimate. This device was made for heating air in the winter and for cooling it on hot summer days. However, even the most advanced models of devices provide a small supply of clean air. This figure is not more than 36 cubic meters per hour. But in the average room air contains about two times more.

Air purifiers

Such a device that improves the microclimate can be purchased without difficulty in almost any store. However, before making a purchase, you should carefully study the capabilities of the device. If we are talking about large rooms, then a device that improves the microclimate should be more effective. In such cases, photocatalytic systems are used, as well as ecorecreators. They are able to clean large volumes of air.



Oxygen generators

Such a device that improves the microclimate can quickly saturate the room air with oxygen. In other words, ventilate the room. In large cities, this is almost impossible. After all, here the air is poisoned. Refresh the indoor climate will allow the oxygen generator. Usually the device is turned on at night and installed in the bedroom. It is worth noting that there are no medical contraindications for using such a device.

All kinds of ionizers

Quite widespread ionizers. Of this category, the most popular microclimate improvement device is the Chizhevsky chandelier. Thanks to such devices, dust particles are charged and then adhere to various surfaces. As a result, the air is cleaned. To obtain the best effect, surfaces must be cleaned of dust on time. Otherwise, after the device is turned off, it will again fall into the air.
