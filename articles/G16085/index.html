We plan a trip: how much to fly to Turkey

Turkey is a wonderful country, which is famous not only for its magical resorts and excellent sweets, but also for its magnificent landscapes, interesting excursions and a friendly population. Today, Antalya, Side, Kemer, Marmaris and Bodrum are considered the most popular holiday destinations. To enjoy the history of the country and the enchanting landscapes, sights, tourists visit Troy, Istanbul, Ephesus, Pamukkale and Hierapolis. But all vacationers are interested in one main question - how much time to fly to Turkey.



Features of Turkey

Wonderful Turkey is often called the "land of contrasts." This is an extraordinary country with a great history and hardworking people. Going on vacation to a warm state, of course, many are wondering how much to fly to Turkey. Unfortunately, the exact time cannot be called, since it directly depends on the departure point. Depending on the distance of your city to a mysterious country, the flight also changes, namely its duration. Of course, other factors influence this, but on the whole, time is calculated in minutes.

A place to forget

Many have been to Turkey, while others only dream of visiting it. One way or another, people return to an extraordinary country for new emotions. They are fascinated by the ruins of Troy or the captivating splendor of Pergamum. Tourists expect to see the ruins of Ephesus and mentally return to the past, they are interested in the artistic and cultural heritage of the country. Also, some just come to rest. After all, everyone knows that in the country you can find economical, but no less comfortable resorts, where you can relax and forget about everything. And in this case, tourists are interested in how much to fly to Turkey, to this divine place.

Flight time

Today, a magnificent country presents to the world some of the best resorts, charming landscapes, architectural monuments and attractions. The purpose of a trip to a warm country is different for each person, but all of them are united by the desire to get to the place where the fairy tale begins as soon as possible. That is, tourists are interested in how much to fly to Turkey.



Let's start with the great city of Moscow. To get from the capital of Russia to Turkey, tourists will need no more than four hours. Often time frames range from 2 hours 50 minutes. up to 3 hours 40 minutes You can also fly to the country in a few hours from cities such as St. Petersburg and Nizhny Novgorod. In this case, the trip will take approximately 3.5-4 hours. As for the distant cities of Russia, here time is slightly increasing. For example, it takes about seven hours to fly from Krasnoyarsk to Turkey, 6 hours 20 minutes from Novosibirsk, and so on.

Modern turkey

The country is truly rich in spiritual and material terms. Today, on the streets of cities you can meet those who support Islam, and people who have borrowed the traditions of nomads. I would like to note the important religious holidays of the Turkish people. They include Sheker-bayram, Kurban-bayram. During these events all around calms down. They are usually celebrated in the family circle, but if a tourist supports Turkish residents, they will be happy to share their emotions with a dear guest. Also, the Turks celebrate the festival of circumcision, which attach great sacred importance. All this can be seen when you come to a stunning country. Tourists should be familiarized with the information on how much to fly to Turkey in order to properly plan their day and further vacation.



Turkey Attractions

Usually, when planning to visit an eastern country, everyone is interested in how many hours to fly to Turkey. On average, the time that tourists need is 4-4.5 hours. Accurately calculating everything until the last minute is very difficult. Indeed, in addition to the fact that people live in different places, other factors should be taken into account. For example, did you land on time, what are the weather conditions and will the country's airport be able to receive its guests at the scheduled time.

For clarity, we present the flight time for some cities. For example, a tourist from Sochi will reach Turkey in 5.5 hours. If you fly from Ufa, this time will be reduced by 1.5 hours. You can get from Yekaterinburg in 4-4.5 hours, and from Surgut in 6.5 hours. Thus, you can approximately determine how long the plane flies to Turkey ( depending on the departure point).

It is also important to pay attention to the airport at which you will land. There are several international agencies in Turkey ready to accept any kind of aircraft. Thus, everyone can arrive in Istanbul faster or, conversely, later than, for example, in Antalya. When choosing a ticket, one should take into account how far the hotel is from the airport and by what transport it can be reached (if not provided by the travel company). Having learned in advance all the details of the trip, you will be able to fully enjoy a visit to Turkey.
