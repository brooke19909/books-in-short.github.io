A bouquet of coffee and tea - how to make?

Tea and coffee have long been considered a good gift. It is both tasty and practical. And if the drink is well packaged, then no one will even guess how much such a present costs. The original design option is a bouquet of sweets, tea, coffee. You can create it yourself. To do this, you do not need to be a craftsman and buy expensive materials.



What can be in the bouquet

Such a presentation may include not only tea and coffee. The bouquet can be decorated with a soft toy, sweets, cookies, jars of honey and jam, pretzels and other unusual decorations. In other words, you can do what the fantasy tells you.

It is worth noting that bouquets of tea and coffee are just a unique gift that can be given not only to a loved one, but also to a colleague, and even to the boss. In addition, such a present can be made independently without any special costs.



What will be needed for a bouquet

To make the present original and beautiful, you should prepare all the necessary components in advance. So, to make a bouquet of coffee and tea, you will need:

  1. Basket or frame. This will serve as the basis for the future bouquet.
  2. Tea and coffee.
  3. Decorative elements.
  4. Double-sided tape, stapler, glue, preferably odorless.
  5. The film is transparent and dense.
  6. Beautiful paper for wrapping.

What tea and coffee to choose

Almost everyone can make bouquets of tea and coffee. In order for the gift to bring pleasure to the recipient, you should carefully consider the choice of the type of drink. After all, for example, tea can be white, black, green, with different spices, with fruits, berries, herbs and chocolate. What about coffee? There are arabica, robusta, ground, beans, African, Italian, French and so on.

If you don‚Äôt know the exact preferences of your friend, then picking up a drink will be much more difficult. In such a situation, it is best to make a bouquet of coffee and tea by choosing several varieties of an elite drink. In addition, such products are often packaged in separate bags or original jars, which can become the center of the composition.



How to make a bouquet of coffee and tea

First you need to prepare tea and coffee bags. If the colors they do not fit the overall composition, then you can make a special package. To do this, you need wrapping paper. From it you can make bags suitable in size and color.

When the tea and coffee packaging is ready, you need to decorate everything with satin ribbon bows or other decorative elements. The hole in the bag is best done with a hole punch.

A bouquet of coffee and tea made by yourself should be beautiful. Therefore, the bags with the drink need to be laid out in prepared paper envelopes, and then carefully fill them with a frame or basket.

How to fill the void

If you simply put tea and coffee bags in a basket or on a frame, voids form between them. As a result, the bouquet does not look very beautiful and unfinished. To prevent this from happening, you must fill the void. To do this, you can use stationery corrugated paper, "raffia", "sisal", as well as a special filler, wood or paper. As a result, a bouquet of coffee and tea will turn out to be more refined. Corrugated paper cut into thin strips is ideal.



Finishing finish

The finished bouquet can be decorated with large beads, flowers or bows from satin ribbons. When the decoration is completed, the present should be wrapped with film. It is advisable to use a transparent and dense material. The film must be fixed on the frame leg. You can do this with a satin ribbon.

A bouquet of coffee and tea should be decorated with corrugated thick paper, while the edges need to be folded and slightly stretched. You can fix everything with glue. It is advisable to use transparent and odorless.

Finally

A bouquet of coffee and tea is the perfect gift for anyone. You can collect the present yourself, including imagination and spending a little time. For decoration, you can use not only satin ribbons. For these purposes, lace, balls and cones of sisal or rattan, soft mini-toys, birds, butterflies and more are suitable. When choosing decor elements, you should focus on the general style of the composition, as well as on who the bouquet is for: a girl, a man, a mother, a grandmother, a boss, a colleague, or a friend.
