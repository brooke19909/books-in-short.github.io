Hotel Sunrise Holidays Resort 5 *, Egypt, Hurghada: overview, description, characteristics and reviews

The Sunrise Holidays Resort 5 * hotel, working on the concept of "adults only", was built twenty years ago. But that did not make him old-fashioned. In two thousand and five, he underwent a complete renovation and reconstruction and now is a modern beach resort complex. Even the most lazy tourists are satisfied with this hotel. Beach, restaurant - almost a stone's throw. And most of the rooms do not even need to go out to watch animated shows - all this can be seen from the balcony. But active vacationers are delighted with the hotel. She has a very high rating. Why? We will talk about this below.



Hurghada, Sheraton District

This is the resort place where the Sunrise Holidays Resort 5 * hotel is located. The reviews claim that it is located five to ten minutes on foot from the "backbone" of Hurghada - the famous Sheraton street. It connects the promenade and the old city. There are many fast-food chains here - McDonald's, Kentucky Fri Chicken, or Pizza Hut. But there are no good restaurants with famous chefs and gourmet cuisine on this street either. She is known for the mass of shops with trinkets, where you can roam, ask the price and bargain. Well, if you want to buy branded things, then you - at the very end of the street. Life on it boils in the evening. That's when tourists from nearby hotels massively go to the promenade. Unlike the rest of Hurghada, it is extremely clean. Transport on the street does not go - it is completely at the mercy of pedestrians. Tourists write that the Sheraton Road was created in imitation of the Barcelona embankment. No wonder even the lanterns here are made in the same artsy style.

Five Star Hotels in Hurghada

Hotels of such a high class are located in this Egyptian resort by the sea. They all have their own beaches, or at least direct access to water. Animation, entertainment, first-class and varied food - all this distinguishes five-star hotels from others. They lure tourists with exclusive restaurants, bars where they make all kinds of delicious cocktails, and various services like spas and beauty salons. But even against this background, our Sunrise Holidays Resort 5 * hotel wins noticeably. The price for a room starts from three thousand nine hundred rubles, and for this money you can get a room with a sea view and first-class service. Neither children will bother you (the hotel only accepts guests over sixteen), nor drunken vacationers. There are not so many Russian tourists here, and the hotel is aimed at travelers from European countries and the United States.



Hurghada and hotel tours

This Egyptian resort area can be called the "elder" in the field of recreation. People have been rushing here for more than a decade, and even have their favorite hotels using their loyalty programs. At the same time, "Sunrise", judging by the reviews, breaks records for popularity. Many companies operating in Russia offer trips to the Sunrise Holidays Resort 5 * hotel. Pegas Touristik, TezTour, Aneks - most of the operators who organize trips to Hurghada offer their customers the services of this hotel.

Location

Hotel Sunrise Holidays Resort 5 * is located in one of the most picturesque places of Hurghada. To the international airport, as well as to the old center, about five kilometers. The hotel stands on the coast of the Red Sea. On the left side of the hotel there are two wild beaches, from which it is good to swim on reefs and watch fish. If, leaving the hotel, turn right, then ten minutes later you can find an inexpensive fruit shop and a store with fixed prices. From the hotel close to the center of Hurghada. There, on Sheraton's main street, there are many shops where they buy souvenirs cheaper than at a hotel. Tourists noted that this location of the hotel is doubly good. On the one hand, to get to the center and its entertainment and shopping, you do not need to get on a transport. On the other hand, no noise is heard from the streets.



Territory

Hotel Sunrise Holidays Resort 5 * is a large seven-story building. Its territory is very cozy, clean and beautiful. She is small, and this is not surprising. After all, the hotel is very close to the city center. But tourists claim that this has its advantages. Many palm trees, flowering shrubs, greenery. The park fits perfectly into the overall style of the hotel. It is built by terraces, and everything that happens at various sites always remains in your sight. There are many shops with different little things and souvenirs. The hotel has a very comfortable atmosphere, tourists feel at home.

Residence

Hotel Sunrise Holidays Resort 5 is a major hotel. It has three hundred and eighty rooms. The vast majority of them have sea views and a nice balcony. Some rooms have a garden view. The rooms are air-conditioned (central system), with cable TV. The room has a safe and a refrigerator, a kettle, a set for making hot drinks, dry cream, sugar (even cane and for diabetics). The furniture is of high quality, in excellent condition. All equipment and electronics work without interruption. The cleaning is great. You will be escorted upon arrival, your luggage will be brought to your room. There is a hairdryer, bathrobe, slippers. In the bathroom there is a double-sided mirror for applying makeup. On the balcony there is a table, a couch and two armchairs. Those vacationers who had sea views from the windows claim that the best time in the hotel is to drink coffee in the morning and enjoy the scenery. They give drinking water to the room, but if it is not enough, you can take it at any restaurant. Guest summary: there is everything for a comfortable stay and relaxation.



Nutrition

Hotel Sunrise Holidays Resort 5 * (Egypt, Hurghada) feeds guests according to the "all inclusive" system. The main restaurant of the El Sol Hotel not only serves delicious dishes. A delightful view opens directly from the tables with food. It has a very pleasant atmosphere and friendly atmosphere. In addition, in the "buffet" system, vacationers are served by two more establishments - Aida and Arabian Nights. They specialize in Egyptian and Oriental cuisine. All these establishments are included in the all-inclusive, and the dishes in them are changed daily. There is a √† la carte restaurant in the hotel called Mediterranean. People go here to try fish and seafood. Three bars - by the pool, on the beach and in the lobby - offer drinks, cocktails and snacks, as well as grilled dishes. Good hookah at the Grand Cafe. Singers perform in the lobby bar in the evenings, it is pleasant to listen to them after dinner. Yes, and you can dance to music here.





The cuisine in the hotel is excellent, the choice of dishes is diverse, especially salads, cheeses, sausages, stuffed vegetables. Mashed soups are good, especially French onions. A lot of fruit, and whole, not chopped. Great desserts and pastries. The dishes are washed very carefully, no poisoning has been noticed. Every evening - a themed dinner, to which the music in the restaurant corresponds. Managers constantly monitor the work of waiters and cooks. Everyone is good and the bars.

Service and entertainment

The Sunrise Holidays Resort 5 * hotel has a parking for car tourists, a seminar room, a hairdresser, and an Internet cafe. Laundry and exchange services are available. The gym is a free service, but sports shoes are required to visit it. On the second floor of the hotel you can play billiards. Table tennis - in a cafe by the pool. Paid spa services are provided - a sauna, a hammam, massage sessions (right on the beach), foot peeling with fish. Good daytime animation - vacationers are involved in aerobics, belly dancing and aqua gymnastics.





The staff is wonderful, not only professional, responsive and welcoming, but also with a sense of humor. Tourists claim that the hotel staff cheer them up in the morning. The waiters work efficiently, always smiling, you will not notice sour faces. Almost all staff understands Russian and speaks many other languages. The staff are very friendly, and if you contact them with a request, you will see that they are really happy to fulfill it. Tourists appreciated the evening animation perfectly. Many different competitions, games, show programs. If you want to have fun after ten in the evening, then the animators collect those who wish and take them to the nightclubs of Hurghada.

Sea and pools

The Sunrise Holidays Resort 5 * hotel (Hurghada) has a good equipped and very clean beach - a stone's throw from the building. With him there is a small artificial lagoon, where it is always quiet, in spite of any winds and waves. Yes, and the ebbs and flows do not interfere with swimming. But if you are an avid diver, the entrance to my open is also nearby. On the beach there is everything - sun loungers, umbrellas, sunbeds, mattresses, towels for sunbathing. There are always places, nothing to be occupied in advance. Entrance to the water is very convenient. No coral slippers are needed. One of the most comfortable sunbathing areas is the pier. The beach bar is one of the best. It has good pizza and good snacks. If sellers are pestering, you can take the red flag and hang it on an umbrella - then they will not fit.





You can play volleyball at the beach. An international diving center is also working here - one of those that Egypt is famous for. The Sunrise Holidays Resort 5 * hotel has a pool. The water in it is warm in summer and winter, because in cold weather it is heated.

Tours and Sightseeing

At the Sunrise Holidays Resort 5 * hotel (Egypt, Hurghada) they go not only to sunbathe, soak up the waters of the Red Sea, but also to combine relaxation with something informative. Therefore, tourists are advised to at least take a city tour, visit the Grand Aquarium with its tunnels and labyrinths of sea water and amazing fish and the Jungle water amusement park. They recommend a great excursion to Egyptian Venice - El Gouna. You can still take a yacht to the islands, especially to the Giftun archipelago, treat yourself to a desert safari, drive ATVs to the Bedouins. And if you want to realize a childhood dream and learn the mysteries and secrets of Ancient Egypt, then you should visit the ancient Luxor. Gods and priests, ancient temples and tombs, legends and myths will come to life before you, as if several millennia have not passed. From Hurghada, they organize two-day trips to Cairo, Alexandria and even Israel.



Shopping prices

The location of the hotel near Sheraton Street is a huge advantage. You leave the hotel, walk for a few minutes, and now you are merging with the festive crowd of tourists. Everywhere festive showcases are burning, sellers are inviting. Of course, you can make purchases without leaving the hotel. But in his shops everything is much more expensive. Therefore, it is better to go for souvenirs to the old city. Figures of the gods, papyrus, cotton products, hookahs, aromatic oils ... Of course, you should be afraid of fakes, but for this it is worth choosing stores more solid. The main thing is to be able to bargain. With perseverance and luck, you can bring down the price several times. In a word, there is a huge selection of everything Hurghada is known for. The Sunrise Holidays Resort 5 * hotel offers many excursions, but tourists are advised to buy them on the street. The cost of travel is dramatically different, but the service is the same. For example, the price of a trip to Luxor is ninety dollars per person if you purchase it at a hotel. And with street guides, the same trip will cost you forty USD. Going with the animators to a nightclub costs ten dollars.
