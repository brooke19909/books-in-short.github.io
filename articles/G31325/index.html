Abramovich, Lisin, Deripaska and other richest people of Russia

In order to make capital in a short time, extraordinary abilities are required. Great inventors and musicians, economists and artists, creators of financial empires, gangsters, and other respected people have become all over the world what they have become, for several reasons, including through a combination of personal qualities such as caution, courage and intelligence.

The richest people in Russia became billionaires in just two decades. At the same time, none of them managed to create ingenious inventions or works of art. They did not use the most advanced technologies, like Henry Ford, did not write computer programs like Bill Gates. There was no need for this, but it is possible that the abilities would not be enough. The richest people of Russia were at the right time in a suitable place and took advantage of this. Of course, in order to occupy such an advantageous position in relation to the sources of material wealth, they had to try.
