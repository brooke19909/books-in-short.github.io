Hotel The Golden Crown Colva 4 * (India, Goa): photos and reviews of tourists

Goa is the main resort of India. It is completely unlike the rest of the country. The gorgeous coast has a length of 110 kilometers and is divided into 40 magnificent beaches. Fort Aguda divides the island into two parts: North and South. The first is characterized by affordable prices, and in the second - everything is much more civilian, hotels are characterized by increased comfort, there are many five- and four-star hotels (for example, The Golden Crown Colva E 4 *), which are designed for a respectable vacation. Recently, this tourist destination has become increasingly popular among tourists from Russia and the CIS countries.





Features of rest

In Goa you can get a real relaxation, because here life is truly serene, no one is in a hurry and enjoys life. Of course, here you can enjoy the gentle sea, lie on the luxurious white sand beaches and get a wonderful golden tan. Europeans here do not feel completely alien to them, because the local culture is a mix of European and Indian. In addition, you can relax here throughout the year, although the worst months to visit the resort are still June, July and August. During this period there are often heavy rains, the sea is not clear, and there are many waves. Of course, it’s never cold, and very hot, too. The maximum air temperature is 33 degrees, and the minimum is 20-24. By the way, many hotels, such as The Golden Crown Colva Goa, are great for spending a honeymoon. In winter and summer, the temperature keeps from 23 to 33 ° C.





North South

As already noted, the southern part of Goa is intended for a respectable vacation. And this means that tourists of medium and higher income come here. These are mainly couples with children, businessmen who are on a well-deserved vacation, etc. They are usually accommodated in hotels of category 4 and 5 stars. Russians most often choose the Quartet, for example, all the same Golden Crown 4 * Colva. But in the northern part, youth companies prefer to stop. Therefore, it is considered "party". Popular hotels here are “kopecks” and “treshki”.

A bit of history

Legend has it that the first European to set foot on the island of Goa was the Portuguese navigator Afonso Albuquerque. He was amazed at the landscape that opened before him and almost choked. Following him, the colonialists who arrived on the island decided that Goa should become the capital of Portuguese India. According to eyewitnesses, buildings of incredible beauty were discovered on the island - the best of those that have been seen during the development of the Indian Ocean. After that, for about 400 years, Goa was under the oppression of Europeans, which affected its culture, including architecture.





What can be done in Goa?

If you are a fan of a quiet and measured rest, adore natural beauties, like to bask in the southern sun and swim in the purest water, then Goa is an ideal place for this. And if you also dream of comfort, but at the same time want to save a little, it is better to buy a tour to one of the best four-star hotels on the island, for example, at The Golden Crown SPA Colva. Relaxing in Goa, you can take an underwater walk and watch under the water for stingrays, sea turtles, an incredible amount of exotic fish and plants.

Here, in addition to a beach holiday, you can also heal the body, do rejuvenation and weight loss. Many hotels on the south coast have spa centers. In The Golden Crown Colva, tourists can get a wide range of spa services, among which I would especially like to note various wraps with algae, exotic herb juice, special types of massage, baths and masks, etc. If you are a fan of excursions, you can visit the state capital is Panaji. This is if you are interested in historical attractions, and lovers of natural beauty can go to the mysterious Dudhsagar Falls, to a crystal clear lake, to spice plantations, you can also ride elephants or hunt crocodiles.





How to get to The Golden Crown Colva (Goa, India)

If you bought a tour to the above hotel, then you have to get here for quite a long time. First you will need to fly from Russia to Delhi or Mumbai. The cost of tickets to the capital is about 25 and 28 thousand rubles, respectively. After arrival, you will need to transfer the following airlines to Goa for the flight: Air India, Etihad Airlines, Indigo Airlines, or go to the island by train, which is much cheaper. An even more affordable option is a bus ride. Just for this you need to arm yourself with patience. After all, the road takes up to 15 hours.





Hotel Description

The Golden Crown Colva E was founded in 2004. The buildings are built in the traditional style of the island. The last time the hotel was reincarnated in the spring of 2010. By the way, until 2015 it was called The Golden Palms Hotel and SPA Colva. The hotel complex consists of five buildings: the main large building, where public areas are located, as well as 4 two-story Anex buildings. The hotel has a total of 50 comfortable rooms of various categories. The hotel is intended for those tourists who came to Goa in search of peace and silence, as well as for vivid unforgettable experiences.

Location

Goa Airport, called Dabolim, is only 20 kilometers from The Golden Crown Colva, and 6 km from the train station. It is located near the capital of the island, the city of Paranja, in the resort village of Colva. The most beautiful beaches - Miramar Beach and Caranzal - are just 10 minutes away by car. The nearest beach is at a distance of 150 meters, if you go through a fishing village, and if to “Boomerang”, then about 500 meters. If you want to relax in a hotel located on the first beach line, then you better not take a tour in Golden Crown 4 * (India). Colva Beach is a wonderful resort, but here on the first line there are mainly five-star hotels.





Number of rooms

As already noted, the hotel has only 50 rooms of the following types: Deluxe Pool View, Deluxe and Deluxe A / C. All of them are designed for two guests, but they can accommodate up to 3 people. There are rooms between which there is a door, and here you can move a large family or a group of friends of 6 people. Hotel rooms are strictly non-smoking.

Description of rooms

Almost all rooms are the same size, furniture and device. The difference can be in the view from the window and what kind of beds are in the room, they can be either double or single. All rooms have balconies with outdoor furniture, equipped with beautiful bathrooms, which have both a separate shower and bath. Here you will find a full set of toiletries, which are updated daily, as well as a bathrobe, hairdryer, slippers, etc. The rooms also have an electronic safe, TV, telephone, Internet, minibar (for a fee), a refrigerator, a set for tea -coffee.

Hotel infrastructure

The enclosures of The Golden Crown Colva Hotel are scattered across a lush tropical garden between lush flowering plants. There is also a swimming pool, around which deck chairs with umbrellas are placed. The hotel has two restaurants, as well as a bar-cafe, a parking lot, a currency exchange, a souvenir shop, etc. The hotel has a first-aid post, a laundry room and a hairdresser. Here you can rent a bike or car.





By the way, the hotel is suitable for various business events for up to 120 people. It is for such a number of people that the conference room is designed.

Nutrition

The hotel offers guests two types of food - BB (breakfast only) and All (all inclusive). If you choose the second type, you can use the services of a bar for free. Breakfast is served from half past seven to half past ten. During this time, the Bamboo top restaurant serves a buffet. Lunch lasts three hours - from half past two to four, and dinner - from 19:30 to 22:30. By the way, according to an old English tradition that has been preserved in Goa since the time of colonization, an hour is intended for tea, as is customary in the United Kingdom, from half past four to half past five. All tourists can enjoy snacks and drinks throughout the day. Hanging Garden Restaurant is located next to the pool. It serves a delicious barbecue, as well as some Indian dishes. But at Harbor Cafe you can order more familiar dishes.





Free and paid service

Tourists at The Golden Crown Colva are served around the clock, and completely free of charge. Umbrellas and trestle beds near the pool are also free services. Using the pool is also completely free. The same goes for the gym. Unlike most hotels around the world, a certain amount must be paid for parking a rental car. Room service food is also a paid service.

Service for children

The hotel has a children's playground, as well as a plunge pool. You can order a bed for the baby, as well as call a nanny in your room and keep it until late in the evening.

Beach

As elsewhere in Goa, in Colva the beaches are covered with golden sand. Here you will be offered various water activities, starting with scooters and ending with diving. According to vacationers, locals are very curious, especially when it comes to white people. Therefore, employees of a cafe with a stick drive them away from their territory. Natives are also prohibited from entering the water and approaching tourists. By the way, almost all the beaches here are municipal (public).





Guest reviews

In general, the ratings of tourists who visited this hotel are good. However, there are a few drawbacks. For example, the view from some rooms is not very pleasant. The Goa residents, who are calm and non-punctual in many respects, do not want to even put tourists in their reserved rooms even half an hour earlier than they should.

Excursions

For guests of the hotel, excursion tours are organized from time to time, and then you and your guide will go to explore the expanses of this amazing country. Nevertheless, you can also explore the surrounding areas on your own, go shopping and buy gifts for relatives and friends. The most popular goods that tourists take with them are various spices, the famous Indian tea, and port. Some shops in the country abound in souvenirs.
