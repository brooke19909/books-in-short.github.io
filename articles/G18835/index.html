How much does a pilot earn? Civil Aviation Pilot Salary

It seems to many that the work of a pilot does not require special knowledge and efforts. Modern aircraft have an excellent complete set, an electronic and safety system. The pilot can only gain the desired height, select the desired course, and you can safely transfer control of the aircraft to the autopilot. In fact, the prestigious position of a pilot must be earned through long-term training, internships and medical examinations. Constant checks and medical examinations accompany pilots throughout their career. And they have a huge responsibility for the lives of numerous passengers. The article tells how much the pilot earns, as well as what his salary depends on.

Military pilots

Their remuneration directly depends on the available military rank and position. Their salary is fixed, but bonuses for the complexity of the flight are added to it. Sometimes they make up 100% of the main cash payments. The salary of a pilot with the appropriate rank and piloting on military aircraft is about 100,000 rubles per month. This figure includes the salary of the pilot and surcharges for flights of varying complexity.

Civil Aircraft

The salary of a civil aviation pilot depends on several factors:

  1. The place of work is the prestige of the airline.
  2. Type of aircraft.
  3. Experience.
  4. Flight schedule.

In reputable large airlines, aircraft have high-tech equipment that meets all modern requirements. Employment is high, and the competition for the vacancy reaches several dozen people. In such a company, pilots can earn up to 150,000 rubles a month. If the aircraft commander flies within a specific region, this figure is reduced to 100,000 rubles.

How much does a pilot with solid work experience earn? To become an aircraft commander, you need to have at least 4000 flying hours. The pilot‚Äôs professionalism, his excellent health, and the ability to quickly and correctly navigate in difficult situations are added to this requirement. Therefore, the aircraft commander will have the highest salary. The second pilot will have it two times less.

Different models of aircraft require a variety of knowledge from the pilot. The higher the class of the aircraft the pilot controls, the more salary allowances he receives.

Pilots are set a minimum number of flight hours per month. For processing there is an increased payment.

Russian pilots

How much do civilian pilots earn in Russia? The average salary of Russian pilots is 140,000 rubles per month. It reaches a maximum value of about 200,000 rubles in large cities, including Moscow, St. Petersburg. In Barnaul, Novosibirsk, this figure drops to 75,000 rubles per month. The more flights a pilot makes per year, the higher his salary will be.

The large Russian airline Aeroflot offers aircraft commanders a salary of 400,000 rubles. In addition, pilots enjoy a number of benefits provided by the company. Among them: a social package in the amount of about 30,000 rubles, specialized medical care for the pilot and all members of his family, payment of hotels and hotels in the cities of arrival.

An interesting fact is that pilots are not charged extra for hazardous working conditions.

Chinese pilots

Aviation of the Pacific and Asian region is developing rapidly. A Chinese pilot can earn about $ 16,000 a month. The remuneration of the commander of the EMB-190 starts at $ 15,000. Chinese airlines promise to pay $ 14,000 per month to the first Airbus A320 pilot. The aircraft commander Boeing B777 can earn about 182,000 dollars a year.

Chinese airlines offer future employees not only prestigious salaries, but also attractive working conditions: the minimum number of flight hours per month is 50 (instead of the usual 80-100).

A Chinese pilot who has worked at the airline for more than three years deserves bonuses in the form of a cash reward, which is about $ 36,000.

American pilots

How much does a civil aviation pilot earn in America? The average salary here varies between $ 250 per 1 hour of flight. American airlines offer pilots a minimum salary of 12 thousand per month, and the maximum amount of wages can reach 35 thousand dollars per month.

American pilots also receive a variety of bonuses that take into account their professionalism, work experience, flight schedule, type of vessel on which flights are made.

How much do European airline pilots earn?

The salary of the aircraft commander Lufthansa is about 9,500 euros per month. After 15 years of experience, the salary of a civilian pilot of a European airline can reach its maximum mark of 19,000 euros.

In the UK, a senior airline assistant earns about $ 32,000 annually. The salary of more experienced pilots is 34,000-37,000 dollars a year. In large airlines, the salary of the aircraft commander can reach $ 200,000 for 12 months of flight.

Employees of the French airline Air France often organized strikes, because of which management had to cancel more than 50% of flights. Air France employees insisted on reforming the airline. Numerous conflicts have nevertheless been resolved. According to data collected in recent years, Air France pilots earn about EUR 19,000 per month.

Profession pilot in demand in Germany. Training for future pilots takes place in professional schools related to a particular company. To get training and obtain a pilot's license for a small motor plane, you need to invest about 20,000 euros. But this amount quickly pays off. The salary of German pilots is about 3,500 euros per month.

Eastern Region Countries

How much does a Turkish airline pilot earn? The average salary of pilots there is 13,000 Turkish liras. They are also paid extra 2.5 thousand in compensation due to a possible depreciation. The lowest salaries are set by low-cost airlines, charters. For an hour of flight, they pay about $ 120. The minimum flying time per month is about 70 hours. Therefore, the average monthly salary is around 8400 dollars.

In Honduras, pilot salaries are low. The average annual income of the aircraft captain is about $ 50,000. Standard requirements are set for aviators: excellent knowledge of the theoretical part of exact disciplines, the availability of specialized education and good health.

The pilot's profession remains one of the most prestigious and unique. If you make an effort (both material and moral), you can successfully become the captain of the aircraft. But it must be remembered that the pilot will always bear a huge responsibility for the life of the passengers carried. He must be prepared for risk and possible dangers.
