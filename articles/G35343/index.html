Tours to Bora Bora with flight

Bora Bora is one of the islands in French Polynesia in the Pacific Ocean. It is an atoll or coral island in the form of a ring surrounding the lagoon. The strait connects the lagoon to the ocean.

Barracudas, sharks and stingrays live in the lagoon, which can be observed under the supervision of an experienced instructor. On the land side of the island, hiking and all-terrain vehicle walks are conducted. Bora bora is a very green island: orchards, thickets of ferns and flowering orchids, tropical forests completely cover the entire territory.

Mount Otemanu is considered the highest point of the island. It offers a beautiful view of the atoll. Below are caves where birds such as frigates can be seen.

Bora Bora - tropical paradise

Luxurious beaches and luxury hotels are located in bays in the southwestern part of the island.

A trip to Bora Bora is considered a luxury vacation. Famous persons and stars of Hollywood come here. The most popular are five-star hotels, of which the majority of the island.

Prices of weekly tours to Bora Bora start from 1,500 euros / 106.7 thousand rubles. per person with a flight in the low season and from 3000 euros (from 213 thousand rubles) in the high season. In Polynesia, severe storms and typhoons are possible in late autumn and winter. And the dry season starts from June to August. July is the best month to relax.

There are several ways to get to the island:

  • Flight through Tokyo to Tahiti.
  • Flight through New York, Los Angeles + Paris (need a transit visa).

From Tahiti to Bora Bora, local airline flights fly and speed boats go. At the airport of the island, where all tourists arrive, there are hotel reception desks. Next, guests sit on catamarans, which deliver them to the chosen place of rest.
