The best tariff for MTS mobile Internet

The company Mobile TeleSystems has developed a line of TP Smart. Each of its tariff plans has a predefined amount of Internet traffic (starting from 1GB, ending with 10GB.). How to choose the best tariff for mobile Internet? You can determine the suitable option by reading their conditions. For example, the Smart tariff implies 3 GB of Internet per month (plus 600 minutes and 600 messages, which can be used even while on intranet roaming). All this pleasure will cost 550 rubles / month. More traffic is available at the Non-Stop TP - 10 GB (with 400 minutes and messages) for a monthly fee of 500 rubles. In this case, even if the installed Internet volume is exceeded, you can connect additional packages (500 or 1000 MB). The tariff line is in great demand, other TPs that are part of it can be successfully used in a tablet PC. What tariff is better than MTS mobile Internet? To deal with this issue, a description of the proposals from other well-known operators should be given.

Tariff plans for the Internet from "Megaphone"

Like the two previous mobile operators, Megafon offers users not only comprehensive tariff plans (implying packages with a certain amount of services), but also a number of options that allow you to use the Internet on any TP. Among the tariffs, one can single out: M - 5 GB (for 500 rubles, plus 400 messages and 550 minutes) and L - 7 GB (for 800 rubles; which also includes 1000 minutes and messages). You can determine the best tariff for mobile Internet by understanding how much service is needed. If you need more gigabytes, then pay attention to the Internet packages M, L, XL, which include 16 GB / 36 GB / 30 GB (when used from seven in the morning to one in the morning) and unlimited use at night. The cost of such offers will be slightly higher: the M package will cost 590 rubles, L - 890 rubles, the last tariff with the maximum unlimited Internet will have a monthly subscription in the amount of 1290 rubles.





Conclusion

How to choose the best rate for mobile Internet? Pay attention to the tariff plans with the included service packages. Among the options offered by mobile operators, there are truly advantageous offers. If you do not need packages of minutes and text messages, then select any TP without a monthly fee, with a minimum cost of calls and adjust it by connecting one of the options for the Internet (the volume can be selected according to need). Do not forget to specify additional terms of use for each such package for your region. Connect options that extend the speed if the set volume is not enough for comfortable use.
