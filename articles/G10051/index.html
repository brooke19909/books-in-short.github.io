How to get a loan under maternity capital

Not everyone knows that today many families have a unique opportunity - to get a loan under maternity capital. First of all, this applies mainly to those loan programs, thanks to which many families have a chance to solve their housing or "educational" problems.





How to use maternity capital in order to purchase housing on credit? First you need to clarify that this benefit is provided to the family as state support after the birth of the child, as well as in cases where the family adopted a second child. According to the current legislation, a family can spend the received maternal capital only in three cases:

  • - increase mother's pension at the expense of capital;
  • - pay for the education of the child;
  • - spend these funds to improve the living conditions of the family.

Due to the sufficient time remoteness of the first two points, most Russians prefer to spend this capital on the latter.

It is important to note that the family can manage this money even before the child is 3 years old, that is, as soon as the parents receive a certificate of capital, then immediately after that they can issue a mortgage or pay off a loan taken earlier.

However, it should be noted that not all organizations issuing loans for the purchase of real estate accept this allowance for the payment of the down payment.

There are only a few ways to use a loan under maternity capital and its funds as a mortgage loan:

- pay the first installment when buying a home;

- to pay for an already taken real estate loan.
