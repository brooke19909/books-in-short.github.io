White Rock Castle 4 * (Balchik, Bulgaria): hotel description, services, reviews

Today, Bulgaria is considered one of the most popular resort countries - thousands of tourists come here every year, wishing to have a good time on the golden coast. This country can please travelers not only with the gentle sea and beautiful nature, but also with good service and very affordable prices.

One of the popular Bulgarian resorts is the town of Balchik. Of course, a huge number of hotels were built on its territory. And one of the rather prestigious, and at the same time inexpensive, is considered White Rock Castle 4 *.

According to reviews, this is a really good place. Nevertheless, an experienced traveler, while planning a vacation, tries to get as much useful information as possible about the chosen place. What does the hotel look like and what amenities in the rooms should you expect? Do you have bathrobes, towels and other amenities? Are there any catering facilities in the hotel? Is it suitable for family holidays?

Features of the location of the hotel complex





Of course, the location is a powerful argument when choosing a hotel. The modern hotel complex White Rock Castle 4 * is located almost in the center of the town of Balchik. From here it is a stone's throw to cafes, shops and entertainment centers. By the way, just a five-minute walk is the Botanical Garden with a huge collection of flora representatives - both children and parents have a good time here.

It is worth saying right away that the hotel is located some distance from the beach, or rather, 1000 meters. Tourists love to take walks to the sea, but if this is not for you, you can always use the shuttle service or public transport, which runs here regularly. By the way, the nearest international airport is located in the city of Varna - the distance to it is about 47 km.

White Rock Castle Hotel 4 * (Bulgaria, Balchik): what does the territory look like?

To begin with, it is worth saying that the hotel was built recently - for the first time tourists began to receive here in 2013. The hotel is a large, six-story building, built in a modern style. Own territory is available, although not too large. Nevertheless, there is a swimming pool, relaxation terraces and even a small flowering garden.

The interior decoration of the building is also modern, even luxurious - marble floors, large halls for relaxing and waiting, upholstered furniture, in a word, everything you need for comfort. The hotel has a system of elevators, so do not worry about moving between floors. Everything here is really very well thought out.

Dining for guests: what to count on?





Of course, during the holidays you want to treat yourself to delicious food. What can a traveler expect? It is immediately worth noting that the majority of Balchik hotels offer tourists breakfast - this is a very common practice. The rest of the time, travelers can eat at any of the cafes in the city, as the prices here are not too high.

Paying for accommodation at the White Rock Castle 4 * hotel, by default you get the right to a daily, morning meal, which takes place in the form of a buffet in a local restaurant. Reviews indicate that the breakfast menu is straightforward - there are salads, fresh fruits, cereals, granola, fried eggs, fresh pastries. By prior arrangement, travelers can pay for lunches and dinners in the same restaurant in advance - the choice in the afternoon and evening is much greater, since the menu already contains dishes of fish, meat, seafood. The food at the hotel is at a decent level - the food is always fresh and tasty, so no one leaves the hungry restaurant.

Convenience and entertainment by the sea





Going to spend time in one of the resort towns of Bulgaria, tourists, of course, expect to have a nice rest on the seashore. What awaits guests of the White Rock Castle 4 * hotel complex? Balchik is a town famous for its beautiful coast. About a kilometer from the hotel is one of the most popular public beaches in the area. A convenient pedestrian road leads to it, but tourists always have the opportunity to use public transport or transfer services.

According to reviews, the sea here is incredibly warm and clean, and the beach is cleaned daily. The entrance to the water is convenient, and the bottom is covered with soft sand. For a small fee, you can rent an umbrella and a deck chair, and there are also several bars on the shore that offer not only chilled drinks, but also snacks. Of course, you can engage in water sports, ride a banana, water skiing, boat, boat, try parasailing and windsurfing. And on the shore there are volleyball courts. Do not forget about the fun beach parties that are held here daily.

Can I count on additional services?





Of course, when arriving at the hotel, you can count on some additional service. In particular, the reception is open 24 hours a day. At the hotel you can get important information and exchange currency at a favorable rate. For a small fee, guests can use the laundry or dry cleaning services.

Almost everywhere tourists can connect to the Internet, and the speed here is pretty good. The hotel has a business center where you can use office equipment, and a spacious conference room, which is equally often used for business seminars and for organizing holidays. Guests can enjoy free parking on site.

Leisure for hotel guests





According to reviews, you can always have a good time at the hotel. In the courtyard for tourists is equipped with a spacious pool, around which comfortable sun loungers and wide-brimmed umbrellas are placed. Here you can sunbathe, refresh yourself or just relax.

There are spacious golf courses near the hotel building - fans of this sport will surely be able to have a good time here. There is a spacious gym with modern equipment and professional instructors. Guests can use the sauna and jacuzzi, and they also have the opportunity to improve their health, because there are mineral springs near the hotel.

Conditions for a family holiday: is it possible to take a child with you?

As you know, tourists often go on vacation with their child. What in terms of family vacations can the White Rock Castle 4 * hotel complex offer? Bulgaria is a great place to relax and improve health, and this, as you know, is necessary not only for parents, but also for their offspring. The hotel also tried to do everything so that the pastime of children was pleasant and comfortable.

Of course, the hotel has spacious family rooms. But even if you settled in a standard room, you can count on an extra very comfortable bed for a child. In the restaurant, waiters politely serve high chairs for the smallest guests. Naturally, the menu has a sufficient number of healthy and nutritious dishes that are suitable for children.

Despite the fact that most of the time, travelers usually spend on the beach or in the city, kids can have fun at the White Rock Castle 4 * hotel. A spacious playground with swings, slides, sandboxes and other attractions is equipped for them here. And in the pool there is a special section with a shallow depth - kids love to splash around here, because the water is always warm and clean.
