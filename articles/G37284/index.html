The recreation center in Omsk "Sunflowers": description, services, reviews

Residents of megacities who would like to spend time in nature do not have to travel long distances. In the regional districts of many cities there are recreation facilities where you can enjoy peace and quiet, have a nice and active rest with benefits for health and mood. One of these institutions is described in the article.

The recreation center in Omsk "Sunflowers": general information

This organization is located in a suburban area, on the coast of Omi. The recreation center is a picturesque and beautiful place, this is a great option for a fun pastime in the company of relatives and friends.

Here you can have a picnic in the lap of nature, fry kebabs, go fishing, organize a student event. For lovers of sports and a healthy lifestyle, the base offers many opportunities for outdoor activities.

To check the guests at the recreation center in Omsk "Sunflowers" offers rooms with all necessary amenities, as well as cottages. Those who wish can stay at the campground.

For the leisure of visitors, this institution has everything you need: football and paintball courts, a shooting range, barbecue facilities, fishing equipment, a swimming pool, skiing tracks, slides, a sauna complex, equipment for playing table tennis and so on.

Where are the "Sunflowers", a recreation center in Omsk, and how to get there? This organization is located on the banks of the Om River, thirty-six kilometers from the city along the Syropyat highway.

Accommodation and meals

The following types of rooms are offered for settling guests at the base:

  1. Economy class rooms for two with 2 beds, a table, a wardrobe, a TV and a refrigerator.
  2. Rooms for 2 people category Junior Suite. This type of room has a bed, sofa, nightstand, chest of drawers, TV and DVD player, toilet and bath, shower, fridge.
  3. The room is designed for two persons, luxury. The room consists of two rooms.

Those who wish can live in cottages. At the base there are houses designed for two or three persons. They have everything you need for a comfortable stay.



Prices for accommodation in this organization vary from a thousand to two thousand rubles.

On the territory of the recreation center in Omsk "Sunflowers" there is a cafe in which food is offered to guests: breakfast, lunch and dinner. For those who are going to organize friendly gatherings in the company in the open air, tables and barbecue facilities are provided.

Leisure activities

What opportunities for active pastime can vacationers use? The following leisure activities are offered to guests:

  1. Billiards and table tennis.
  2. Football.
  3. Shooting gallery
  4. Riding on catamarans.
  5. The game of golf.
  6. Bicycle rides.
  7. Parties on a designated area.
  8. Karaoke.
  9. Chess, badminton and checkers.
  10. Sports equipment rental.
  11. Fishing.
  12. Relaxation on the summer terrace.
  13. Trampolines.




The recreation center in Omsk "Sunflowers" is perfect for hanging out with friends or family, and for youth companies, and for holding various celebrations.

Opinions of the guests about the institution

If you read customer reviews about the work of this organization, we can conclude that not everyone liked the rest on the base. However, there are clients who do not regret that they have chosen this institution. They claim that this place is beautiful in nature, you can have a great time in the company of friends and relatives.

Also, some guests liked the recreation center in that it has many opportunities for active leisure and a variety of entertainment. There are clients who appreciated the good work of the staff of the institution. However, there are negative reviews about the organization Sunflowers (recreation center in Omsk). Among the shortcomings of the institution are the poor quality of food in the cafe, as well as the non-observance by its employees of basic sanitary and hygienic standards, the lack of proper cleanliness in the room where they eat food. In addition, interruptions in water were periodically observed in the rooms, plumbing was out of order. The bath complex was not warm enough.

The recreation center "Sunflowers" is located in Omsk at the address: Russia, Omsk Region, pos. Kormilovka, Pervomaiskaya street. Some guests claim that it is difficult to get to the place, especially in winter, when there is a lot of snow on the roads.
