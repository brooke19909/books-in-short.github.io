Origin of goods

A pillow in the shape of a man owes his appearance to resourceful Japanese. She began to sell in 2004, and she immediately gained popularity among single women. A male version soon followed. It gradually developed to a pillow in the form of a woman with silicone implants in the chest and buttocks, which, according to the manufacturers, are very realistic to the touch. In Japan, this product is still very popular.

How to make a pillow-person yourself?

It will cost much cheaper than the purchased one, it won‚Äôt take a lot of time to manufacture, and it won‚Äôt require cutting and sewing skills.

You will need:

  • Men's shirt or long-sleeved T-shirt. If you want the printed cavalier to be larger, then do not forget to take the size XL or even XXL.
  • Glue gun or fabric glue, double-sided tape.
  • A few clothespins.
  • Scissors.
  • Chalk or marker on the fabric.
  • Material for stuffing: synthetic winterizer, silicone balls or granules of foamed polystyrene (be careful with them, if there is a small hole in the product, then you will remove the granules throughout the house).

How to make a pillow in the shape of a man with his own hands?

  1. Fold the shirt on a flat surface, draw a line at a distance of 4-5 cm from the buttons, cut.
  2. Cut sections: bend and secure with glue from a gun, glue for cloth or tape.
  3. Take a few clothespins and align the edges. Using glue, connect them, and also seal the collar and the holes between the buttons. Keep the bottom of the shirt and sleeve open.
  4. Stuff the pillow.
  5. Cover the bottom and the edge of the sleeve.

You can make a pillowcase for a pillow on the same principle. If you are going to wash your craft, then you need to use waterproof glue or give preference to a thread with a needle.
