How much does an electrician get: salary level by region, prospects

In our world, which is constantly and rapidly changing, there are not so many specialties that are likely to continue to exist in the future. The profession of an electrician, which, moreover, is more and more in contact with the profession of an electronic engineer, is one of the few whose good prospects can and should definitely be believed. However, not only the prospects of this profession are interesting, but also how things are going with the wages of electricians today, that is, how much an electrician receives here and now.

What salary depends on

In a general sense, an electrician is a too general concept, which includes many specializations, levels and categories. Very conditionally, they can be divided into two large groups, depending on the level of education. These are electrical engineers with a higher technical education and electricians of various specializations with a secondary technical education.

The former are engaged in the design of new and supervision of the operation of existing systems, while the latter directly carry out the necessary installation, repair and maintenance work on all electrical circuit systems: from generating electricity to delivering it to the final consumer, including the creation and maintenance of instruments and equipment.

According to the unified tariff-qualification reference book of working professions, electricians are assigned digits from 2 to 6 inclusive. The higher the discharge, the, accordingly, the more the electrician receives. How much such a specialist receives a salary also depends on the level of admission. There are 5 admission groups for electrical safety that determine the qualifications of the employee, and a list of the work that he has the right to perform.

The salary of an electrical engineer is mainly determined by the position that he occupies in a particular enterprise. Well, the enterprise itself, in which the specialist works, is of considerable importance.

Supply and demand

Relatively recently, the Ministry of Labor published a list of the most popular professions in Russia, which also includes the profession of an electrician. According to another document of the same Ministry, last year, for example, a vacancy of an electrical engineer with a salary of 195,000 rubles was included in the list of the highest paid, along with the commander of the aircraft and the captain of the ocean ship. It turns out that the profession is scarce and highly paid? So how much do electricians actually get in Russia?

The salary level of electrical engineers

They are white-collar workers in this profession. Here, the salary depends almost exclusively on the position held and on the particular employing organization. The dependence is very simple, the higher the position, the higher the salary of an electrical engineer, the richer the company - the richer the electrical engineer.

If a graduate of a higher educational institution in Moscow can claim a maximum of 30,000 - 35,000 rubles, then experienced leaders reach heights of 150,000-200,000 or more rubles a month. What other income can an electrician have?

A very common phenomenon in the circle of electricians is self-employment. However, some often combine employment and part-time jobs in a private manner. In Russia, the market for such services is extremely large. A significant part of the population across the country has cottages that need almost constant maintenance and periodic repairs, and the main housing in the private sector is not far behind in generating orders for electricians.

Prices for services sometimes allow them to earn significant amounts, up to 80-100 thousand rubles a month. Often in addition to basic earnings. Of course, such earnings cannot be called reliable and regular, it all depends on the constant availability of customers, available free time and willingness to work without days off and holidays, and often without holidays.
