Contrast colors and high resolution.





Modern TVs with a curved screen, and so far there are not so many, are equipped with innovative systems and technologies. So, Peak Illuminator is the technology responsible for intelligent backlighting. Its task is to reduce the power consumption of the TV in dark areas, increasing the brightness of bright areas. Accordingly, this provides excellent contrast against a deep black background.

Another technology is UHD Dimming. It is responsible for the high quality of the image, however, for the perfect display of details on the screen, the image must be further processed. The essence of technology is that the screen is divided into sections. For each of them, contrast, color and level of detail are selected, which together allows you to improve the image. Such cropping significantly improves the quality of the picture.

4K UHD is a completely new format that only curved screen televisions have. Due to the crystal clear image and amazing detail on any screen, this format looks simply gorgeous. No less striking is the incredible color palette, because Samsung PureColour technology focuses on a harmonious combination of shades that create a gorgeous image. Another technology responsible for the saturation and naturalness of colors is Nano Crystal. So if a harmonious combination of contrast, brightness and clarity is important for you, then you will definitely like the curved screens.

Pros and cons





For many of us, the old technique is familiar, and we perceive various modern trends, if not negatively, then with a fair amount of doubt. The same view is caused by many curved TV screen. Of course, he has pros and cons. Let's try to group them all in one section, in order to finally conclude whether the screen is good or bad. The main advantages include the following:

  1. The increased viewing angle. A curved screen, especially if its resolution is 4K, allows you to see many small details.
  2. High sharpness and its depth create a sense of volume, which is visible in several planes at once.
  3. Thanks to the curved shape, the screen area visually becomes larger.
  4. Stylish design that can become a bright accent of any room.
