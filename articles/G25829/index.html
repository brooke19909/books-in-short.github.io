Park Side Hotel 3 * (Side, Turkey): description, service, reviews

Side - an ancient Greek city on the south-west coast of Antalya - is a popular tourist resort, loved by the crystal clear waters, magnificent beaches, rich vegetation, and a stable mild climate. The beach season here lasts from late April to the last days of October. Summer heat, at which the temperature rises above +35 ¬∞ C, is easily tolerated by vacationers, because the evening coolness is replaced daily by the evening coolness, accompanied by a light sea breeze. In winter there is little snow; precipitation occurs mainly in the form of rain.

Side is attractive for tourists not only with sandy beaches and the warm sea, but also with a wealth of historical sights and leisure programs. The city is secretly called the "open-air museum." In the perimeter of the spa town are the ruins of ancient cities, causing the interest of many tourists. To accommodate guests in Side built many hotels of different levels. Vacationers have left many positive reviews about Park Side Hotel 3 *.

Acquaintance

The cozy Park Side Hotel 3 * invites guests to spend an excellent and inexpensive vacation on the picturesque Mediterranean coast. Vacationers here have the opportunity to have a meaningful and interesting leisure: swim in the outdoor pool, play board games, visit the Turkish hammam or take part in fascinating animated performances. Due to the excellent transport interchange, guests can make an independent trip to one of the most striking attractions of the resort - Manavgat Waterfall (it takes about 1 hour to get to the road). Beach lovers can use umbrellas (free). Windsurfing, as well as canoeing, jet skiing and a banana will give a lot of joyful emotions and impressions to vacationers.

Location

Park Side Hotel 3 * is located on the Mediterranean coast, 1 km from the historical center of the resort of Side. The hotel is 1.5 km from its own sandy beach. From the hotel to the airport - 65 km. The center of Manavgat is 2 km, and Antalya is 75 km. Address: Cennetler Mevkii Side, Kemer Mah, Antalya / Manavgat / Turkey. Guests evaluate the location of the hotel as not bad, but they believe that the sea and entertainment are still far from here.

Park Side Hotel 3 *: description

The hotel is surrounded by unique natural and historical attractions.
