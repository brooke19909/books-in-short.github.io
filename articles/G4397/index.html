Fleece socks pattern

This article will be useful for those who want to learn how to sew socks. Someone likes to knit, and someone sews, everyone has different hobbies. What makes a hobby good? It should be fun, give positive emotions and be accessible to all. You can find a lot of information about knitting socks, but there is practically nothing about sewing, and it is difficult to find a pattern of socks.

What material to choose?

To get such soft and warm socks, you should choose Russian-made microfleece, since there is a possibility that this pattern of socks may not be suitable for other material.

Microflis was originally conceived as a substitute for wool. The advantage of this material is that it is completely hypoallergenic. Fleece is very popular among tourists, skiers, climbers. Their hats, scarves, mittens, gloves are certainly made of fleece. In the wardrobe of a modern man, of course, there is a pair of warm, cozy fleece sweatshirts. You can easily sew socks from this material. After a couple of hours, you can already bask in your favorite chair by the fireplace, and your legs will be warmed by new exclusive handmade socks.

Socks pattern

And the first thing you need to work is patterns. A pattern is a product outline drawn on paper or cardboard. To make a pattern, it is best to take graph paper. Patterns you can find further.

Technology

To stitch the neck of the sock, fold the sections labeled 1, 2, and 3, 4, align them, and sew. Fold the resulting circle in a circle in half and twist. Pieces 5, 6 fold with 10, 11. You will get the lower part and the heel of the toe. As a result, the sole of the socks comes out.

Then sew the upper part of the sock with the sole (sections 12, 14 with sections 12, 14), pushing the lower part of the toe onto the upper (in sections 14, 8). Left a little. Sew the elastic with the toe (sections 1, 2, 2, 3 and sections 12, 13). Turn out the finished product. Repeat the same steps with the second toe. And, of course, wear it to your health!

First of all, such socks can be sewn for children, the material is very soft and does not contain allergens. They can also be sewn for the husband. The fleece is warm and helps legs not to sweat, which is especially important if a man loves hunting, fishing and other active activities.
