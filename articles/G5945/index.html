Tsalos Beach Hotel 3 * (Greece / Crete) - photo and reviews of tourists

Every corner of Greece is beautiful. But Crete cannot be compared to anything. Having decided to relax on this island, feel free to choose the Tsalos Beach Hotel 3 stars. Here the atmosphere reigns, the staff is attentive and responsive, the service is impeccable, and the location will delight even the most demanding tourist.



How to get there

Tsalos Beach Hotel Analipsis 3 is located in the small village of Analipsis, on the coast of the sea. Although it originated in the 18th century, the infrastructure here is modern. Beautiful asphalt roads that can be used to get to any corner of Crete, supermarkets, taverns for every taste, excursion bureaus, car, motorcycle and ATV rental stations are waiting for vacationers here. All this within walking distance of the hotel. The famous city of Hersonissos is only 3 km from here, and the center of the island's capital, Heraklion, is 20 km away. Stormy life is boiling there, there are a lot of night clubs, bars, restaurants, there is an international airport that receives planes from Moscow and other Russian cities, as well as many cities of the world. From Athens you can sail to Crete and by ferry. Further to Tsalos Beach Hotel 3 by taxi or bus. A bus stop is a few steps away.



Hotel Description

Very convenient for a perfect vacation and beautiful Tsalos Beach Hotel 3. Crete is replete with palm trees, citrus, delicious flowers. An incredible number of them in the hotel. Green spaces surround every building here, invariably pleasing to the eye. Vacationers are accommodated in several two-story buildings, modern, practical, built in the Greek style. The territory has a relatively large pool (depth up to 2.5 meters). Next to it is small and small for babies. There is also a playground with a swing for them. Tourists are provided with sunbeds, umbrellas, inflatable mattresses, balls, children's swimming equipment. Everything is free. Three steps away is a bar with a large selection of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks. Also on site there is parking, a minimarket, a souvenir shop and a newspaper desk. The hotel staff is unusually helpful and friendly. Workers do not speak Russian, but they know English perfectly.



Rooms

Types of rooms Tsalos Beach Hotel 3 is represented by 52 rooms, of which 30 are two-room apartments (area up to 45 sq. M) and 22 studios (up to 35 sq. M). All of them are extremely spacious, furnished with modern comfortable furniture. Luxury and piling up of excess items can not be found here, only the most necessary to create coziness and convenience to guests. They are decorated in light, unobtrusive colors. Windows overlook either the coast or the pool and garden. For a carefree stay, the rooms include:

- Large kitchen with stove and set of dishes;

-electric kettle;

-coffee maker;

-TV (not in all rooms);

-air conditioning (paid);

-safe (paid);

-refrigerator;

-radio point;

-telephone;

-bathroom with shower or bathtub, washbasin and toilet;

Balcony or terrace (there is a summer table and armchairs).

Room service is quality, daily. Change of linen every 7 days, change of towels in a day. All cleaners (maids) are conscientious and even, not a single guest noticed cases of theft of property.



Nutrition

Since each room at Tsalos Beach Hotel 3 has its own kitchen, everyone can cook their own meals. This is especially convenient for morning coffee. There are many supermarkets with high-quality and inexpensive products in the village, so there are no problems with cooking. Those who do not want to trouble themselves can order breakfast at the hotel. As a rule, they are standard continental, including coffee, tea, juice, milk, an egg (boiled or scrambled eggs), cereal or muesli, cheese, butter, jam, buns in their menu. The hotel has its own cozy restaurant. Visitors can enjoy not only delicious dishes, but also a beautiful view of the sea. In addition, in their reviews, vacationers recommend lunch and dinner in the restaurants of neighboring hotels and in the taverns of the village. The most popular are located at 100 meters, an inexpensive restaurant "Marina" with unusually flavored fish dishes and dishes of squid, octopus, shrimp. A little higher up the road visitors will find the Paul Marie tavern, where Greek folklore evenings, fiery national dances, and wonderful music are often arranged. Sea and entertainment

Just a couple of tens of meters from the sea is Tsalos Beach Hotel 3. O. Crete, Heraklion and other areas of the island are famous for their excellent beaches. Most of them are rocky, but there is a sandy beach near the hotel. Reviews indicate that the entry into the sea is convenient, the depth is small. Sunbed and umbrella cost 3 euros. You can agree for this amount and for 2 sunbeds with an umbrella. Sometimes staff treats their customers with fruit. The only negative is the frequent winds. It depends on the location of the village. If it happens that during the holiday there will be high waves from this side of the island, you can choose a beach at the other end, located in one of the quiet picturesque bays.

But to come to Crete and spend all the rest on a sunbed is not particularly interesting. Active tourists can rent a car (average price of 30-50 euros per day) and travel around the whole island, having got acquainted with the mass of attractions. Sightseeing tours can be bought at the hotel itself, or you can use the services of numerous tour bureaus of the village. Prices and quality of service there will pleasantly surprise you. Saves around 50 euros per unit. Throughout the season, trips are arranged all over the island with a visit to the Knossos Palace, and in the high season you can visit the amazing uninhabited islet of Kufunisi.

Additional Information

A comfortable, carefree holiday is provided by Tsalos Beach Hotel 3. Reviews of those who have been there are the best confirmation of this. This hotel is ideal for parents with children, youth, anyone who leads an active lifestyle. It is not particularly comfortable here only for those who are not going to leave the hotel often, expecting daily entertaining animations from its employees. All that such people can count on is music in the bar and infrequent evenings of national dance.

Also, for some guests, too small showers are criticized, but in general, according to the vast majority of tourists, the Tsalos Beach Hotel, declared as a three-star, fully corresponds to its category, being one of its best representatives.
