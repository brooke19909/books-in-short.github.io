Do-it-yourself slippers pattern made of fur: cut master-class

Fur slippers not only look beautiful, but also perfectly warm your feet in the cold season. Children will especially like them, which sometimes simply cannot be forced to walk around the house in indoor shoes. You can buy soft beautiful slippers or make them yourself. Making slippers with your own hands from fur is not a very complicated matter and even beginner needlewomen can do it.





What will be the slippers?

Before you start making fur slippers, you need to decide how they will look. The easiest way to design ordinary slippers. Material for this will require a little, and the pattern of slippers with your own hands from the fur in this case is easy.

You can also make slippers with backs or with an extended boot. In such products, even in the winter, the legs will be comfortable and warm. True, in this case, a pattern of slippers with your own hands from fur will require effort and patience, and a little more material will be needed.

Materials at hand

To make a pattern, you need to have at hand suitable tools and the material from which the products will be sewn. You need cardboard for making patterns. You also need to prepare a pencil, scissors, chalk, needles and threads in advance. Often, a pattern of slippers with your own hands made of fur is based on the measurements obtained, so a tailor's meter will be needed for work.





Fur slippers

After preliminary preparations, you can begin to build a pattern. Slippers consist of only two parts, so you can design them in a couple of hours. If a pattern of slippers made of fur is built with your own hands, it is difficult to guess the size, so it is better to follow a long-established scheme. A master class will help to cope with this work:

  • you need to put your foot on the cardboard and circle it with a pencil;
  • 2 cm along the entire circumference should be added to the resulting figure;
  • a leaf is applied to the upper part of the foot so that it passes from the fingers to the rise;
  • at the place of contact with the floor, the sheet is encircled with a pencil;
  • to the resulting picture in places where the part will be attached to the sole, you need to add 2 cm;
  • the details are cut out and copied so that for the left and right slipper there are patterns.

When a pattern of slippers from fur is done with your own hands, you can proceed to further work. The resulting drawings must be laid out on the material, outlined and cut out the details. Since each part is divided into internal and external, for sewing one slipper you will need 4 blanks: 2 for the sole and the same for the top.





To make the slippers warmer, you can make a batting insert in the sole. It is better to cut a couple of millimeters less around the circumference, then after connecting the parts it will be hidden, and the products will turn out beautiful and neat. The outer part of the sole is better to cut out of synthetic or genuine leather, which will increase the wear resistance of shoes.

The final step will be the connection of the parts. First you need to fasten the sole and top of the product with pins, stitch the joints and make the edge trim.

Slippers pattern with backdrop

Products with backs are designed in the same way as slippers, but you still need to add the back part. The pattern of this part can be done in a similar way by applying a leaf to the leg, and outline, given the height of the backdrop.

It is easy to make a piece of detail by making measurements. To do this, measure the foot:

  • attach the beginning of the meter to the inner side of the foot at the very bottom to the place where the upper part of the slipper will pass;
  • grasp the back of the foot with a meter;
  • fix the length on the outside of the foot, where the top of the product passes.




The height of the backdrop should be no more than 7 cm, then slippers will be comfortable. Using the obtained dimensions, you need to draw a detail on the cardboard, not forgetting to increase the seams. The resulting pattern is applied to the material, details are outlined and cut out. First, the upper parts are sewn together and then attached to the sole. For one slipper with a back, 6 parts are cut out (with a heater for the sole they will turn out 7).

Pattern of slippers made of fur with an extended boot

It’s a little harder to make a pattern of slippers from fur. The sole pattern is created as described above. After that, the center of the heel should be marked on the received part and the foot should be measured along the entire circumference. A rectangle of previously obtained length and arbitrary width (up to 7 cm) is drawn on paper. This will be the side of the slipper. The length of the foot to the rise is measured, and the resulting measurement is indicated on the sole, starting from the center of the toe. A line is drawn along the width of the sole. On this line, the part is bent and transferred to the material: this will be the upper part of the slipper.





Using the patterns you need to cut out the details of the future product. First, the top is connected to the side, and its length is measured along the upper circumference. This measurement is needed to calculate the width of the shaft, the length of which is taken arbitrarily. All sizes are transferred to paper, and a rectangle is drawn. This will be the elongated top of the slipper. The bootleg is attached to the previously made upper, which is sewn to the sole. In conclusion, the back seam is sewn. When constructing patterns, one should not forget about allowances for seams. For products made of fur, it is better to make them at least 2 cm.

Finally

Do-it-yourself slippers pattern made of fur is easy. If nevertheless there are difficulties, or there is no desire to tinker with measurements, you can find patterns in various magazines. But there is an important point that should be remembered: the pattern of slippers from fur in full size will allow you to sew the most appropriate size products. Having made indoor shoes with your own hands, you do not have to spend money on its purchase. After all, you can give a second life to a fur coat or sheepskin coat, which has long been gathering dust in the closet.
