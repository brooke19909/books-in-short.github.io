Charming islands of Greece: Corfu

If paradise exists on Earth, then these are the islands of Greece. Corfu, Rhodes, Santorini and many other objects impress with their picturesque landscapes and unique architecture. In this article we will dwell on the most beautiful island of the Ionian Sea - Corfu (in Greek, Kerkyra).



Islands of Greece: Corfu - the land of beautiful views

The territory of the island of Kerkyra is covered with lush fragrant vegetation: cypresses, citrus and olive trees. The main settlement of the island is the city of Kerkyra. The population of the capital of the island of Corfu consists of Greeks, Italians, Jews and representatives of other peoples. For its centuries-old existence, Kerkyra has constantly been the subject of controversy between the Romans, Turks, British, and Byzantines. On the whole, the imprint of the Byzantine culture is most felt in the appearance of this settlement .

Islands of Greece: Corfu - Attractions of Kerkyra

Saint Spyridon is considered the patron saint of the city, his relics in a silver sarcophagus are still stored in an old church, named after the savior of Kerkyra.

In addition to the church of St. Spyridon, the capital of Corfu has in its arsenal many attractions:

  1. Archaeological Museum. The pride of his collection is the frieze with the image of the Gorgon.
  2. Museum of Asian Art.
  3. Museum of Byzantine Culture.
  4. A municipality called San Giacomo (17th century).
  5. Ionian University. This is an educational institution whose purpose is to revive the former glory of the Ionian Academy.
  6. Cathedral. Here are valuable icons of the Byzantine era. It is considered the main temple of the capital of the island of Corfu.
  7. An ancient palace with two arches, built at the beginning of the XIX century.

Islands of Greece: Corfu - famous places

The sights of Corfu can be seen not only in Kerkyra, but also in many other places. In the town of Gasturi there is an amazing Achillion palace with a beautiful park. 8 km from Kerkyra is the city of Gouvia - the former Venetian port, and now a very developed tourist center. It has cozy beaches, excellent taverns and restaurants. In a charming calm bay there is a place called Kondokali with a harbor for 600 yachts, incredibly beautiful nature and many shops and cafes. Lovers of peace and quiet can stay in the quiet village of Nisaki. The beaches here are pebble with an admixture of small stones. In the picturesque city of Kassiopi, the ruins of an ancient Byzantine fortress have been preserved. Photos of Corfu island (Greece) are really amazing.



The west coast of the island is washed by the Adriatic Sea. Of particular interest in this region is the city of Paleokastritsa with the monastery of the Virgin, which was built on the rocks. This unique corner is famous for its hills, grottoes and turquoise sea water. Nearby is the Angelokastro Fortress, or the Castle of Angels, built in the 13th century.

Cozy bays, magnificent hills, beautiful cities and villages - all this can be offered to you by the island of Corfu (Greece). Traveler reviews say that it is impossible to forget this land. Everyone who has been here has hopelessly and forever fallen in love with this luxurious and colorful island.
