LCD "Park Alleys": photos and reviews

The capital of our state has long been densely populated. The population of the metropolis is increasing not only every year, but also every month. Visitors need to live somewhere, so construction in Moscow and the Moscow Region is proceeding at a fast pace and does not stop even in spite of the crisis. One of the projects is the Park Alleys residential complex in the Krasnogorsk district of the Moscow region.





A little about the city of Krasnogorsk

In the north-west of Moscow, at a distance of 2 km from the Moscow Ring Road and 22 km from the center of the capital along the Volokolamsk and Ilyinsky Highways, the city of Krasnogorsk is located. Its population is just over 150 thousand people. Since 2018, the city will become the administrative center of the Moscow region. A blue metro line was drawn here: Myakinino - the first Moscow metro station outside the border of Moscow.

The city has built a lot of residential facilities. New buildings Krasnogorsk offers locals and visitors. Among them there are complexes of both business class and comfort class - for every taste and size of the wallet.

Description of the complex

LCD "Park Alleys" the developer designed according to the original plan. The complex consists of five buildings, built on a monolithic-frame technology and on an individual project. All houses have a height of 17 floors. The construction of the residential complex is carried out as part of the reconstruction project of the neighborhood ‚ÄúCobblestone village‚Äù. The complex offers apartments of various layouts: one-room, two-room and three-room. The footage of the first starts at 33 square meters, and the latter are quite spacious, their footage is more than 92 square meters. All apartments are rented by the builder with a turnkey finish.





Ecological situation

Many believe that the city of Krasnogorsk is an ideal place for permanent residence. Around the city is a large green massif. Small rivers flow nearby. Industrial plants located within or near the city do not produce waste that is harmful to health. The wind from Moscow does not bring exhaust fumes here. All these factors create the most favorable environmental situation in the complex.

Speaking directly about the Park Alleys residential complex, the developer chose the most successful land in the city - next to the city park. Some of the apartments in the complex even have a view of it. Nearby are Ivanovo Ponds, where you can walk and relax.

Infrastructure: external and internal

The infrastructure of the complex, like many other new buildings (Krasnogorsk), offers its residents built commercial premises on the ground floors of residential buildings. Owners of cars will appreciate the spacious underground parking. The developer designed the construction of a comprehensive school, a sports complex with a swimming pool, two shopping and office centers.

Considering that the Park Alleys residential complex is being built within the city of Krasnogorsk, then all its infrastructure is at the disposal of their future residents. Until the social system objects declared in the project have been built, for example, a school or kindergarten, you can use those located within walking distance from the complex. There are several such establishments and residents can choose exactly where to take their child. The city has clinics, supermarkets, museums, educational institutions of secondary vocational education, various banks, the Palace of Culture and the stadium. In a word, all the objects that are necessary for a comfortable existence.

As for the intra-complex infrastructure, here the developer will not leave his customers to wade through the mountains of construction debris. The court territory will be ennobled by lawns and flower beds. It is planned to equip several children's playgrounds and sports grounds. Access roads to the complex will be asphalted, guest parking is equipped for guests of the complex.

Reviews about the complex

LCD "Park Alleys" (Krasnogorsk) reviews contain both positive and negative. A sufficient number of negative statements. Most of them are associated with a long construction and the postponement of the delivery of keys to the apartment for almost two years from the fourth quarter of 2015 to the second quarter of 2017. Many buyers of residential real estate sue and exact a penalty related to non-fulfillment of the terms of the share agreement.





But there are also many positive reviews from those who have already settled. Especially, a lot of them are written by residents of dilapidated housing, on the site of which the Park Alleys residential complex was built. Krasnogorsk fulfilled its planned activities for the renovation of old dilapidated housing in the neighborhood "Cobblestone Village", relocating people from the demolished high-rise buildings to new apartments. People are satisfied with the developed infrastructure. Those who have children take them to the nearest school and kindergarten. A children's clinic is also within walking distance of the complex. Many are happy that the prices of apartments in the complex are quite low.
