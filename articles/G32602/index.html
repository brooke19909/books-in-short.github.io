LG 49UJ634V TV: owner reviews and specifications

The LG 49UJ634V TV belongs to a series of multimedia centers with flagship characteristics . Feedback from owners and specialists about this premium solution, its main parameters, as well as the setup procedure for this device will be discussed in this material.





Positioning. Equipment

As noted earlier, the LG TV model under consideration belongs to the premium solutions segment. 49 inches - the screen length, an advanced and significantly improved software shell combined with an expanded set of possible connection methods indicate that this multimedia center was the flagship. Typical equipment for this device. It included the following components:

  1. TV.

  2. Support and fastening to it.

  3. The control panel and everything necessary for the implementation of its power supply, a set of batteries.

  4. Warranty card.

  5. Power cord

  6. User's manual.

The delivery list of this multimedia center is balanced, and it has no drawbacks.

Picture

The presence of excellent image quality boasts modern LG TVs. The 49-inch screen diagonal, IPS matrix type and 4K resolution are far from a complete list of those parameters that indicate the maximum quality of the displayed image. The image refresh rate is 1600 MHz, and viewing angles can reach 178 o .

The screen coating of the television device is matte. Separately, it should be noted that there is support for a mode such as HDR Effect. It improves picture quality in the most dynamic scenes. The color rendering and contrast of this device are really excellent and there are no drawbacks.





Sound

20 watts of sound power in nominal mode can be provided by the LG 49UJ634V TV . Owner reviews at the same time indicate a sufficient level of the issued signal by the acoustic subsystem. As expected, the latter includes two speakers. The level of rated power of each of them is limited to 10 watts.

If necessary, you can connect external speakers to the multimedia center. For this, the manufacturer equipped this device with an audio jack of 3.5 mm format and a digital port. These two switching methods are wired. If you need to output sound wirelessly, you can use the Bluetooth transmitter.





Communications

Ideal by today's standards, the communication set boasts the hero of this review. With its help, the maximum flexibility of this device is provided when interfacing external tuners, receivers, DVD players and computers. This list consists of the following connection methods:

  1. The main type of port in this case is digital HDMI. With their help, you can display a picture from modern satellite tuners, DVB-T2 receivers, personal computers and even DVD players. There are 3 such connectors in this device.

  2. The second most important connection method is the universal USB connector. They are used to receive pictures from a mini-PC or to play various multimedia files from external storage devices.

  3. For switching with outdated satellite receivers and DVB-T2 tuners, this multimedia center is equipped with two types of connectors: composite and component.

  4. There is also one antenna input. Moreover, not only cable or local broadcasting can be connected to it, but also a set of satellite equipment. That is, the built-in tuner in this case is very, very universal.

The list of network connection methods consists of Wi-Fi and, of course, RJ-45. Most often, the first of them is used to connect to the global web. It is wireless and simple enough to use. In the second case, it is necessary to fail the twisted pair cable. These two methods allow you to use the software function Smart TV and without the possibility of obtaining information from the Internet, it will not work.

Software shell

Under control of the operating system Web OS version 3.5, the LG 49UJ634V TV considered in the framework of this material functions. Reviews indicate a high level of its functionality, which is not inferior to competing platforms AndroidOS and TizenOS. The system software of this device is relevant and constantly updated. In addition to this, more and more mini-utilities for this software platform are constantly coming out.





Reviews Current price. Conclusion

You can buy such a TV with a large diagonal at a price of 45,000-50,000 rubles. Given its nominal positioning, such a price tag is quite justified. Its advantages include the following:

  1. Excellent image quality.

  2. Software environment.

  3. Flexible communication kit.

  4. Excellent sound quality.

  5. Energy efficiency.

  6. Universal tuner.

But there are no drawbacks and minuses in such a device as the LG 49UJ634V TV. Reviews only, as noted earlier, indicate a high cost. But it is due to the premium device. In the end, it can be noted that this is a great solution for home use.
