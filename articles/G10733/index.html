Hotel Flamingo, Adler: reviews, photos

If you dream of a carefree sea holiday, then the Flamingo Hotel is perfect for you. Adler is famous not only for its beautiful beaches, but also for its unique nature. In addition, tourists will find a huge amount of entertainment that will make your vacation unforgettable.

Hotel Flamingo (Adler) is a modern private hotel in which each guest will feel at ease and comfortable.
