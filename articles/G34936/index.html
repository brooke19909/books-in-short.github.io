Why update apps?

Companies developing mobile applications are constantly working to improve their product in order to make it more functional, interesting, fast and easy to use. That is why updates in the Play Market periodically appear for applications already installed on the mobile device.

The main reason you need to update utilities is software imperfection. The creators are in a hurry to share their developments, while others are not ahead of them. However, often the software has many bugs, errors that slow it down. Over time, releasing updates, these shortcomings are fixed, offering an improved version of the utility. In addition, many applications can be attacked by viruses that are launched to steal passwords, personal data, etc. In turn, the developer, in addition to improvements, seeks to increase the level of security.

But sometimes situations arise when you need to return to the old version. Then information may be useful, how to undo the last update of the application on the "Android". More about this later in the article.

The need for periodic updates

No specific recommendations can be made here. The fact is that many users of the Android operating system have hundreds of applications installed and at least 10 of them will require updates daily. This slows down the device to a certain extent, and if updates occur without connecting to a Wi-Fi network, it also significantly consumes mobile Internet traffic.

In favor of a periodic update, it is worth saying that there are utilities that a user cannot do without in everyday life. After updating the device, they work better and faster, they allow you to solve tasks, without resorting to unnecessary actions and the use of third-party programs.

Some applications can be updated less frequently or not at all. These are the programs:

  • not having access to the Internet;
  • not reading the contact list;
  • not leaving information in memory;
  • without access to SMS messages.

There are a lot of such applications, for example, Compass or Flashlight. If they already work well, then updating them is useless. As for the rest of the programs, before installing the new version it is necessary to familiarize yourself with the description for it. If it offers a solution to problems such as a utility vulnerability, a solution to the problem of excessive memory consumption, then, of course, it is recommended to update.

If the benefit seemed obvious, and after installation the user did not like the new functionality, then he can cancel the update of the application on Android. This is not difficult to do if you use the information in this article.

Cancel updates

If, for some reason, the download of the patch begins without a corresponding notification, the update can be interrupted. The easiest and fastest way to remove the application update on Android is as follows:

  • open the notification tab and find a specific application;

When you independently download the latest updates for your favorite programs and games, the user has the opportunity to reduce the consumption of Internet traffic and speed up the phone, since the software will not be constantly downloading in the background. In particular, manual control is recommended for those device owners who have downloaded a lot of applications, while the capabilities of the mobile device are limited. In addition, with manual installation, the user knows what he was doing, and if necessary, can solve the problem of how to cancel the update of applications on the Samsung Android or other smartphones.

Among other things, constant downloads help to quickly reduce battery power. After they are turned off, from time to time updates must be carried out independently.
