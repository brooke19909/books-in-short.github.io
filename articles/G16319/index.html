Hotel La Mer Hotel 5 *, Turkey, Kemer - overview, description and reviews of tourists

The choice of many tourists when planning an annual vacation falls on radiant Turkey and its cozy resort town of Kemer. The combination of comfortable accommodation, high level service and favorable climatic conditions annually attract new travelers to this region. The five-star hotel "La Mer" (Kemer, Turkey), the description of which will be presented to your attention in the article, has successfully incorporated the above characteristics. It is open to receptions all year round.

general characteristics

Hotel "La Mer" ( La Mer Hotel, 5 stars ) was commissioned and opened to welcome guests in 1997. The main and only snow-white building is represented by a five-story building equipped with three elevators. It is located among the many green deciduous trees, as if in a forest. The total area is more than 30,000 square meters. It is well maintained, well-maintained and maintained in constant cleanliness. The hotel territory is full of fragrant flower beds, the smell of greenery, numerous relaxation areas and hammocks. The last renovation was carried out in 2011.

The distance from La Mer Hotel 5 * hotel (Turkey, Kemer) to the nearest international airport is 46 km, which is comfortable for arrivals, since there is no need for long bus transfers.

Check-in with pets is strictly prohibited, as is smoking in rooms. For this purpose specially designated places are intended. There are rooms for people with disabilities. The La Mer Hotel 5 * hotel (Turkey, Kemer) is intended for both youth and family vacations. The staff speaks Russian, Turkish, English, German.

For those arriving by car, a free parking space is provided. Baby cots are available upon request.

Beach

Hotel La Mer is located on the first coastline at a distance of 150 meters from the Mediterranean Sea. The spacious beach, represented by a sand and pebble coating, has a gentle entrance to the water, which ensures the safety of swimming for the kids. The cleanliness of the local waters favors snorkeling. You should continue your acquaintance with the underwater world by immersion under water at a greater depth, where the interesting underwater inhabitants and unique flora seen will forever remain in the diver's memory.

Every traveler seeks to get a beautiful tan thanks to the rays of the Turkish sun. This can be done comfortably thanks to the beach, equipped with numerous soft loungers in the form of mattresses. For those who prefer shady accommodation, you can sit under umbrellas or awnings. Towels are provided free of charge.

The built pier is strewn with sunbeds. It allows you to feel on an island surrounded on all sides by a water abyss. Diving from it is strictly prohibited, and everyone can use the special descents in the form of a ladder. Motor boats and pleasure boats, which offer access to the open sea, regularly approach it. The path to the pier in the form of a wooden path goes from the hotel.
