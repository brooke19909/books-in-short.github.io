Hotel Villa Ocean View 3 * (Sri Lanka / Wadduva): description, photos and reviews of tourists

The small resort town of Wadduwa, located in the west of Sri Lanka, is approximately 33 kilometers from the capital. This settlement with a fairly small area is known primarily for the production of vinegar and palm wine. But for some time now, Wadduva has become Sri Lanka's most visited resort. The city has earned such popularity due to its beautiful beaches stretching for eight kilometers, the purest water of the Indian Ocean, tropical gardens, huge plantations of coconut palms and cinnamon, whose aroma literally hovers in the air.





Hotels in Wadduwa

Tours to this resort have, for some time now, become very popular with our compatriots - beach vacation lovers. Wadduwa hotels are located on the beach quite far from each other. Thus, guests are provided with silence and privacy.

The resort got its name from local doctors: the Vedas lived in this area. Today, they have been replaced by experienced specialists working in SPA salons and health centers, offering tourists yoga classes, Ayurvedic treatments and meditation.

In general, there are few hotels in Wadduwa. Among them there are very large hotel complexes with their own SPA salons, and cheaper mini-guest houses. Almost all accommodation options, regardless of category, are built on the coast, although with rare exceptions. Some hotels are located far from the bathing area, for example, Kumbukgahawatta Hotel. They have their own, already existing contingent of holidaymakers who need isolation and peace on vacation. For those who plan not only to swim and sunbathe, but also to travel to local attractions, relatively inexpensive three-star hotels, for example, Villa Ocean View 3 *, are most suitable.

Description

This hotel complex, located on the west coast of Sri Lanka, in the Wadduwa region, has two categories. The three-story building, in which rooms with increased comfort are equipped, is positioned as a four-star.



A two-story bungalow Villa Ocean View - 3-star. The hotel has direct access to Wadduwa Beach and is built 120 meters from the Indian Ocean.

He has a large, very well-groomed area. Apparently, therefore, Villa Ocean View Hotel 3 * has recently been very popular among Russians. Here you can spend a real paradise vacation. Many hold their wedding celebrations among coconut trees, exotic tropical plants, against the backdrop of the blue sea and snow-white sands. The hotel has provided many services for such ceremonies.

Herons are gracefully walking around the hotel, completely fenced off from the outside world, there are chipmunks and monkeys.

The main advantage of the Villa Ocean View Hotel 3 * hotel, according to Russians, is its proximity to the airport. And although Russians come here to have a rest with very young children, nevertheless even adults can hardly transfer a long transfer after a long flight. To the airport - 65 kilometers, to the capital of the country - Colombo - half an hour drive.

Beach

Villa Ocean View Hotel 3 * (Sri Lanka) was built with direct access to the sea. The famous Wadduwa Beach is only one hundred and twenty meters distant from the main building of the hotel. This is a fairly large swimming area: its width ranges from ten to sixty meters. The length of the beach is ten kilometers.

The descent from the hotel to the shore is comfortable, gentle. The entrance to the water is sandy; no special rubber shoes are required. Swimming in the Indian Ocean with large waves is prohibited. This is followed by specially trained rescuers. Since the beach is public, you need to pay extra for umbrellas and mattresses.
