The sensor does not work on the iPhone: what to do?

Check accessories





There are times when they interfere with the correct operation of the touch screen. For example, if the protective film was not glued correctly, it can contribute to this. In addition, the market now has a huge number of undeniably cool bumpers for smartphones, but not all of them are made soundly. If the bumper partially covers the display, it may interfere with its normal operation. To check if this is the reason, just remove it from the device and again use the touchscreen a bit. If normal operation is restored, it is really a bumper, and you will have to do something about it. If nothing has changed, then you need to go further and look for the cause of incorrect functioning.

If the sensor does not work for you on an iPhone 5S or another device of this American company, specialists with the appropriate work experience will always be able to first recognize and identify the cause of the malfunction, and then localize and eradicate it.
