What can I advise in conclusion

The article lists the most rated tablets from the budget line, whose prices as of the second half of 2015 are no more than 10,000 rubles in many online stores. Which is the best budget tablet among the above, it's up to the owners only. Each person has their own opinions and preferences. Some may praise technology, while others scold. No one is safe from defective products.

When searching for the right tablet, it is recommended to focus on what size the screen should be and what the purpose is. Inexpensive tablets, as a rule, are equipped with processors with a number of cores of 2 or 4. It must be said right away that a dual-core processor is not suitable for heavy games, therefore it should not be taken for such purposes. And 4 cores are enough for virtual entertainment.

As for the screen size, it also has important factors: what is a tablet for? How will it be exploited? Indeed, for example, a large screen is not suitable for reading, your eyes will get tired. On the contrary, in order to fully spend time on the Internet, it is desirable to have a large screen. And if both? Then the golden mean will do. Indeed, many cheap tablets have a screen diagonal of 7 to 10 inches approximately. So, you should choose a device with a screen of 8-9 inches.
