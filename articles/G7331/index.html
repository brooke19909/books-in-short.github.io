Repair by specialists

Free Repair

You can try to find the official service center recommended by the company in your city. Warranty repair Apple free is only possible in it. A private company, which has not signed an agreement with an "apple" company, will not undertake it. Defective items that were not subject to external damage (that is, they were malfunctioning from the very beginning) are subject to free repair.

All Apple products have a one-year limited warranty. During this time period, defective items can be replaced or repaired for free.



What is not included in the warranty

During operation, owners are often to blame for device breakdowns. So, for example, many users seek to violate the terms of the guarantee by installing content that is not certified by the company. And, by the way, jailbreak, which about 40% of owners sin, is one of them. If the device fails because of this, then you can forget about the warranty right away. In addition, there are a number of standard breakdowns that are not subject to free repair. For the money produced:

  • replacement and correction of items that are not owned by Apple, even if supplied with the gadget (for example, non-branded headphones that are so fond of "giving" in stores when buying);
  • repair of parts that tend to wear out (batteries, protective films, covers, etc.);
  • repair of hacked or previously repaired elements (that is, if previously someone already tried to fix broken parts, the warranty disappears).

In the latter case, there is an exception. For example, Apple (iPhone) warranty repairs were performed by an authorized service center. In this case, the one-year right to a free replacement of the fault is retained. The repair mark should be in the documents on the gadget and, of course, will be displayed in the personal data stored in the company.
