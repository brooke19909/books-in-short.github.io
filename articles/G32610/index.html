Hotel Gelina Village Hotel Resort Spa 4 * (Corfu, Greece): photos and reviews of tourists

Corfu gained its popularity due to its mild and pleasant climate. Tourists are happy to spend their holidays here. In summer, the island has all the conditions for recreation, there is practically no rain, which means you can safely enjoy the wonderful weather. The sea breeze smooths out the summer heat well, so being on the island is a pleasure. Even those for whom the heat is simply contraindicated can spend their holidays here. In particular, this applies to pensioners and children. There are no health problems that can be significantly affected by fever. Compared to other Greek islands, the beach season in Corfu comes later, but this does not affect the popularity of the island. The hottest month is July, the air temperature reaches +28 . But at this temperature the sea remains cool and fresh, its temperature does not exceed 22 degrees. Due to such weather conditions, the island has become a popular tourist destination.

If in July you can relax by the sea, then May and June can be devoted to spiritual enrichment. Of course, you can also swim, but the air temperature is not too high at this time, so the weather conditions are more conducive to walks in parks and other attractions. As for the latter, there are quite a lot of them. Archaeological excavations are especially active in Corfu. This action can be observed and even take an active part.

The best time to travel to Corfu

If a tourist intends to go only to the sea and relax to the sound of the surf, then it is best to implement his plan in July. This is the warmest month of the year. The water is warm enough, sometimes it reaches a peak temperature - 25 degrees. This is a very comfortable indicator, even small children can swim in such water.

August is not the best month for a calm and measured rest. There is a full house on the island, it was during this period that the maximum number of tourists came here. Prices during this period rise quite noticeably, even plane tickets are getting more expensive. If a tourist does not have financial restrictions, then he can choose August for a vacation, and in order to save, it is better to come in July.

The best time to relax is September. The fact is that the flow of tourists is significantly reduced, workdays begin, children go to school and so on. At the beginning of September, prices drop significantly, rest becomes affordable, but at the same time it is not inferior in August to its comfort. The sea remains as warm, and hotels will gladly accept guests and even make discounts. But in the second half of September, it is not recommended to go to Corfu: the weather changes begin, and it is very difficult to predict here whether it will be warm or the rainy season will begin. Therefore, for the purpose of a comfortable and budget holiday, it is better to go to the island in the first half of September. A trip with young children is also better to postpone for this period. Pleasant climate, a small number of tourists - this is exactly what the baby needs.
Well, having arrived on the island, you need to settle down somewhere, and, of course, I want to choose a worthy place. Such a place could be a hotel Gelina Village Hotel Resort Spa 4 *.



Description of the complex

The hotel is located near the center, but you cannot reach it on foot. The path from the center to the hotel is 35 kilometers. The complex is quite large, so there are almost always free rooms. In total there are 350 rooms of various categories. Each of them has everything necessary for the comfort of tourists. The administration does everything so that guests return to them again and again.

Also, the complex can offer a conference room for business guests. For young people in the summer there is an open-air cinema. You can not only watch an interesting film, but also admire the stars. The hotel has a huge number of shops, here you can buy not only souvenir products, but also clothes, products and even jewelry. There is a beauty salon, dry cleaning and laundry, where you can completely tidy yourself and solve any problem regarding appearance. There are entertainment for outdoor enthusiasts, and any taste preferences are taken into account. From entertainment available archery, aerobics, water polo and much more. It is amazing that there are so many sports that even for a week at the hotel it will be difficult to try everything.

For lovers of delicious food there are several restaurants with good cuisine. You can taste excellent dishes and especially delicious wine. The journey from the airport to the hotel takes about an hour by bus in the highlands. On arrival, you can go for breakfast, or you can immediately check in. Sea and beach





The coast, according to tourists, is just wonderful. The entrance to the sea is gentle, which, again, pleases parents with children. Soft sand at the bottom. There are several entrances to the beach: through a huge slab, on stones or on sand near the Pirate bar. There are no jellyfish in the sea, no dead fish, no nasty algae. During a storm, this, of course, cannot be avoided.

Nutrition





The hotel operates on an all-inclusive basis. There is an incredible amount of food here, but the lines are also huge. For dinner, it is better to arrive on time. Then you can find a free table, and all dishes will be available. If you are late, you can wait in line for a long time at the table, and then for food. For dinner, experienced tourists recommend going to a fish restaurant. True, they serve fairly uniform seafood dishes. But then you can avoid the hustle and bustle and hungry neighbors. The assortment always has squid, fish soups, mussels with sauce, small fish in the form of chips. Fruits are also served here. To whom it is not enough, you can go to a supermarket located near the hotel and buy there anything you like.

Employees of the animation club are true professionals in organizing children's leisure. Kids are entertained by the pool, in the water park, on the beach, and in any free area. In the evening, adult programs are held. Although small guests can have fun at the evening disco. After this, you should definitely visit the water park. Here you can see fakirs, magicians, and even yogis. At a children's concert, your children can show all their talents. Adults can laugh at the Comedy Club show, sing in karaoke or enjoy national dances.
