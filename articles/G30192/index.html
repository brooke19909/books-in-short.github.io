Hotel Edem Holiday Club 2 * (Greece, Pieria): overview, description, rooms and reviews

Pieria is one of the most picturesque corners of Greece, offering tourists around the world excellent conditions for a comfortable stay. This resort is located near the famous Soloniki, at the foot of Mount Olympus. In Pieria, vacationers are waiting for not only the most beautiful sandy beaches and clear sea, but also a pleasant unobtrusive service. There are, of course, just a huge number of convenient, inexpensive hotels built here. For example, comfortable rooms for tourists are offered by a small budget hotel Edem Holiday Club 2 *.

Hotel Location Features

This hotel is located right by the sea, 2 km from Paralia Katerini. The nearest airport to the complex is located quite far - 115 kilometers. Tourists can get to the Edem Holiday Club 2 * hotel (Pieria) either by bus provided by a tour operator, or by taxi or by rented car. The last way, many vacationers consider the most convenient. You can rent a car in Greece, if you wish, immediately upon arrival directly at the airport. In the future, it can be very useful, including for exploring local attractions. Guests of the Edem Holiday Club can park their rental cars right on its territory with free parking.





General description of the complex

Represents the hotel Edem Holiday Club (2 stars) a small cozy complex of bungalow type. The territory is well landscaped and landscaped. The courtyard of the hotel is covered with beautiful tiles, which is very convenient to move around, and turned into a real luxurious garden of tropical vegetation. From all sides the hotel territory is fenced with a reliable fence. Outsiders cannot enter the courtyard of the complex, as it is guarded.

The reception in this complex is open 24 hours a day. Smoking and pets are not allowed in the hotel rooms. In addition, the hotel provides special conditions for the disabled (specially equipped rooms). The reception at this hotel is right on the street. In order to arrange check-in or check-out, tourists need to contact the administrator in the window.

Number of rooms

In total, the hotel Edem Holiday Club 2 * (Pieria) offers guests of Greece 50 comfortable rooms. If necessary, vacationers can rent here:

  • Doubles BunkBed (20-22 m 2 );

  • Single Rooms & Double Rooms (18-20 m 2 ).

Rooms for rent at the hotel are equipped with all necessary furniture and equipped with modern household appliances. In addition to beds, wardrobes and bedside tables, vacationers have the opportunity to use:

  • split system;

  • TV

  • mini fridge;

  • by phone.





The television at this hotel is satellite. Residents, among other things, can watch Russian channels. Rooms also include free Wi-Fi. Vacationers can relax in the evenings on the wide furnished terrace. If necessary, an extra rollaway bed can be ordered to the room for a fee.

Of course, there are individual showers in every room of the hotel. In addition to the standard set of toiletries, tourists can use a hairdryer here. Unfortunately, there are no bathtubs in the hotel showers. To take water procedures, residents must use a regular watering can.

Therefore, to be in the hotel rooms, according to many tourists, is quite pleasant. As for bedding, unfortunately, it is not changed very often. Hotel service

Of course, the residents of the hotel can use all kinds of amenities and directly on its territory. For vacationers at the Edem Holiday Club Hotel "2 stars" ("Edem Holiday Club") are provided for:

  • free parking;

  • pool with sun loungers and umbrellas;

  • garden;

  • paid safe at the reception.

Parking at this hotel is quite large and, most importantly, guarded. Guests can use the sun loungers and umbrellas by the pool for free. Most travelers who rented rooms at the Edem Holiday Club 2 * hotel (Greece, Paralia, Katerini) prefer to leave valuables and papers at the reception in the safe. Infrastructure Reviews

Of course, the Edem Holiday Club 2 * hotel is just a two-star hotel (although 3 appears on the sign at the entrance to it). Therefore, there are not so many amenities for residents here. But in general, rest in this hotel, most of its guests consider it quite convenient. In particular, tourists praise the hotel pool. It is small in the hotel, but the water temperature in it is always comfortable. And hotel employees do not pour too much bleach into it. In addition, by the pool, the safety of vacationers, and especially children, is monitored by a specially trained employee.

Tourists consider only a large number of ants on its territory to be some drawback of the hotel. In the evenings, these insects can prevent guests from resting.

Service Reviews

Most tourists consider the hotel staff to be very polite and helpful. Administrators of newly arrived tourists are greeted warmly and with a smile. Especially praise Russian vacationers working at the reception staff of the hotel Anastasia and Janis. In some cases, rooms in this hotel can be accommodated even before checkout time. Sometimes tourists arriving too early will be offered breakfast. But, unfortunately, you can take it only for an additional fee (5 euros per person).

Hotel Beach Reviews

The hotel is located Edem Holiday Club (Olympiaki Akti, Greece), as already mentioned, right on the beach. Its guests need to go to the beach no more than 2 minutes. In order to get to the sea, residents just need to cross a country road. This, of course, is very convenient especially for the elderly and tourists with children.

Actually, the hotel's beach itself has earned relatively good reviews from vacationers. The sea here is always very clean and, most importantly, warm. The entrance to it is very gentle. Vacationers need to go up to the waist in the water for about 20-30 meters to reach the depth. Of course, it is very convenient, especially for bathing children.

Sun loungers and umbrellas on this beach are paid. But many vacationers simply use those that are installed by the pool. After all, it is located just a couple of steps from the coast.

Actually, the coastline itself opposite the Edem Holiday Club 2 * hotel, unfortunately, is not too clean. No cigarette butts and pieces of paper here. But, unfortunately, in the sea itself, a lot of algae always floats in this place. They are thrown ashore, and they are mixed with sand. Of course, no one removes algae on the hotel beach. Therefore, the coastline does not look very neat here.

Available Attractions

Reviews of the Edem Holiday Club 2 * hotel on the network are good also because here, in addition to everything else, there is also a small tour desk. Of course, for many tourists, and especially for beginners, its presence can be very convenient. However, experienced tourists are advised to purchase excursions not in the hotel itself, but in Paralia, which takes less than 20 minutes to reach from the hotel. Here, various kinds of miniatures can be bought almost 2 times cheaper than in a hotel. In Katerini and Soloniki, experienced tourists advise beginners to take a taxi or just take a bus on their own.





Also, some vacationers recommend that guests simply rent a car for a sightseeing trip from a hotel guide on a security deposit of 150 euros. In this case, it will be possible to visit such interesting places in Greece as Meteora, Platamonas, Olympus, etc. And, of course, the residents of the hotel should definitely go to Athens. This city is located quite far from Pieria. But if you wish, you can always cheaply book a room at night in any of the Athens hotels.
