A few words about the manufacturer

Before you start the review of Vertex Impress Lion 4G, let's talk a little about the company itself. She appeared in 2010. At the time of formation, it was a venture capital fund, the capital of which was limited to 4 million dollars. The company did not have a lot of employees, however, this did not prevent it from developing successfully. Three years later, assets significantly increased and amounted to $ 90 million.

In 2015, the products of this brand began to appear on the markets of Europe and the countries of the former CIS. A year later, representative offices were opened in Russia, France, Ukraine, Germany, and Kazakhstan.

This company is considered a major supplier of accessories for gadgets. More recently, phones have also been sold under this brand. The first line of smartphones is called Impress. It is not chosen by chance. Translated from English, this word means "impress." The manufacturer thus wanted to show that phones, even for little money, can surprise a modern user.
