Olympic Kosma Hotel 3 * (Greece / Halkidiki) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

The first of them is the most densely populated. Here, numerous tourists are offered developed infrastructure, entertainment and hotels for every taste and budget. Kassandra is famous for both its excellent beaches stretching for 50 kilometers, and the rich nightlife. If you, like tens of thousands of tourists from all over the world, decide to spend your vacation in this paradise and at the same time want to save on accommodation, then consider the three-star Hotel Olympic Kosma as an option for accommodation. Today we will get to know this hotel more closely, and also find out whether our compatriots liked it here.





Where is

Olympic Cosma is located half a kilometer from the center of the resort village of Khanoti (Kassandra, Halkidiki). Such a location is a plus, since the place here is quite calm and quiet, and at the same time, all the entertainment is within walking distance for hotel guests. In addition, the hotel is located just 200 meters from the sea. Therefore, leaving the hotel, in just a couple of minutes you will find yourself on a magnificent sandy beach.

Transport

Most of the tourists traveling to the Olympic Cosma Hotel fly to Thessaloniki Airport. This airborne is called "Macedonia", and the distance from it to the hotel is about 90 kilometers. Thus, upon arrival, you can reach the hotel in about an hour and a half. As for the movements directly on the peninsula, you can do this either by taxi or public transport, or by renting a car.





Halkidiki, Kassandra. Olympic Kosma Hotel 3 *: photo and description

Olympic Cosma is a small three-star hotel built in the traditional Mediterranean style. Here, tourists are invited to stay in one of 140 comfortable rooms of standard or family type. Hotel Policies

As in most other hotels in different parts of the world, Olympic Kosma (Greece) has a time set by the rules for check-in and check-out. So, check-in of arriving guests is carried out after two in the afternoon. However, at an earlier visit, they will try to accommodate you as soon as possible (if there are available rooms). On the last day of your stay at the hotel, the occupied room should be vacated before noon.

Special conditions

Both Olympic cash and plastic cards of payment systems such as Mastercard, Maestro and Visa are accepted for payment at Olympic Cosma. All additional services should be paid immediately, as they are not included in the total bill for accommodation.

Since this hotel also positions itself as a family, guests with children of any age are always welcome here. Extra beds can be provided for them. As for tourists with pets, the possibility of their accommodation should be discussed in advance with the administration of Olympic Kosma Hotel 3 *.





Number of rooms

The housing stock of the hotel consists of 140 comfortable rooms. So, there are the following types of apartments: standard double (22 sq. Meters, two adults + 1 child), family with one bedroom (35 sq. Meters, three adults + 1 child) and two (45 sq. Meters, four adults guests). All rooms are air-conditioned, have their own bathroom with shower and hairdryer, TV, refrigerator, telephone and a terrace or balcony. For an extra fee, guests can use the safe and room service. Cleaning in rooms Olympic Kosma 3 * (Greece) is carried out daily. Sheets and towels are updated several times a week.

Nutrition

When booking accommodation in Olympic Cosma, you can choose the right meal plan for you: only breakfast, half board or all inclusive. All meals are organized in the hotel restaurant and are held in buffet style. In addition, Olympic Kosma (Kassandra, Chalkidiki) has two bars with a wide selection of drinks: by the pool and in the lobby.

Beach vacation

Like the vast majority of local hotels, Olympic Cosma does not have its own beach. The nearest municipal recreation area is 200 meters away. The beaches are sandy. The entrance to the water is shallow, at the bottom - small pebbles. For the rental of sunbeds and a parasol, you will have to pay extra.

Entertainment and infrastructure

The three-star Olympic Kosma Hotel has all the necessary infrastructure for a comfortable stay. So, there is a 24-hour reception desk, where you can ask a question at any time. In addition, part of the hotel staff speaks Russian, so you can communicate without problems. At the hotel you can use Wi-Fi, leave things in the luggage room, hand over clothes to the laundry or dry cleaning service, spend time in the TV room. The hotel has an outdoor pool with a children's section and a sun terrace.





Cost of living

As for the cost of accommodation in Olympic Kosma Hotel 3 *, the prices here are in full accordance with the hotel category and the conditions and services offered in it. So, a seven-day stay in a hotel in the midst of the tourist season will cost you from 40 thousand rubles for a double room and from 45 thousand rubles for a triple.

Olympic Kosma Hotel 3 * (Halkidiki): reviews of Russian travelers

In order for you to have a more reliable and complete picture of this hotel, we suggest that you familiarize yourself with the impressions of our compatriots who have been here this year. Looking ahead a bit, we note that most tourists were satisfied with the hotel. According to them, Olympic Cosma is a very decent three-star hotel, which is an excellent combination of price and quality. Many guests do not exclude the possibility of returning here again, and are also ready to recommend the institution to their friends and relatives planning to spend their holidays on the Greek peninsula of Halkidiki. Now we offer to understand everything in more detail.





Comments of tourists about the number of rooms, location and territory of the Olympic Cosma Hotel

In general, most of the guests were quite satisfied with the rooms provided to them. True, experienced travelers advise if possible to settle in rooms whose windows do not open onto the pool, since during the day it can be quite noisy. As for the rooms themselves, they are all very clean and comfortable. Furniture and appliances are not new, but everything is in good condition. If any breakdowns occur, then the reception desk allows you to quickly resolve all problems. Pleased guests Olympic Kosma Hotel 3 * and the quality of cleaning in the rooms. So, the maids were visited every day, their work was done neatly and properly, towels and bed linen were changed several times a week. By the way, bedding, as well as bath, is of very good quality. According to our compatriots, here you will not find torn sheets and pillowcases.

A small minus was considered by some tourists the fact that the elevators in the buildings run only between the first and fourth floors. Thus, the guests, who were placed on the second and third floors, are forced to overcome one passage on foot. This is not very convenient, especially during check-in and check-out, as you have to carry heavy suitcases. However, experienced travelers advise when checking in to ask the receptionist to help with the luggage transfer. In this case, the hotel employee will deliver your things to the room itself. It is also recommended to do when leaving the hotel.

Travelers also respond positively to the location of Hotel Olympic Kosma 3 *. So, due to the fact that it is located almost on the outskirts of the village, it is quite quiet here, and no noise from the central streets will prevent a quiet rest. At the same time, all tourist infrastructure facilities are located literally within walking distance. Also, guests of the hotel were pleased with the proximity of the beach. So, the distance to it is only a couple of hundred meters. The beach closest to the hotel is sandy. As already noted, the entrance to the water is gentle, at the bottom there are small pebbles. However, some tourists found this beach too dirty and chose to relax on the neighboring one. You can also reach it in just a few minutes.





Reviews of tourists about nutrition in Olympic Cosma

A very unusual situation for a three-star hotel is the fact that the vast majority of travelers staying here were satisfied with the food. So, according to our compatriots, the variety of the Olympic Kosma Hotel 3 * menu, of course, cannot be compared with the set of dishes in five-star hotels, but there is always something to choose from. Tourists note that always on the table were meat and fish dishes, salads, fruits, delicious pastries and desserts. In short, you will not stay hungry here. Experienced travelers recommend those who plan to often go on excursions to take not "all inclusive", but only breakfast or half board. In this case, you will not be attached to the hotel in terms of nutrition and you can taste different delicious dishes in the many restaurants and taverns, which are abundant in Greece. As for the Olympic Cosma bar, there were no complaints about it either.

Impressions of travelers about entertainment and staff

One of the main advantages of the three-star hotel "Olympic Cosma", many of our compatriots consider its staff. So, in their opinion, the hotel employees are always friendly, friendly, attentive and do their job well. Tourists were also pleased with one of the hotel administrators, who, in addition to his main duties, periodically pleased the guests with a professional piano playing.

As for entertainment, since Olympic Kosma (Halkidiki) does not have a large territory of its own, it cannot offer any special activities to its guests. Many tourists are also pleased to note the fact that the hotel has no animation, so they were able to relax perfectly, without getting annoyed by the constant noise. In general, according to travelers, the main attractions of Olympic Cosma guests are trips to the beach, walks in the surroundings with visits to restaurants, taverns, bars, shops, as well as trips to attractions.
