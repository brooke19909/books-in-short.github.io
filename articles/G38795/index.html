Kahya Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya): photos and reviews of tourists from Russia

Comfortable accommodation is offered by the four-star hotel complex Kahya Hotel, which has successfully established itself in the hotel industry. The hotel receives mostly positive reviews. What did the complex deserve such close attention? This we will discuss in the article.

Advantages of Alanya

Choosing a resort for the implementation of the long-awaited vacation takes a lot of time. A planned trip with friends or a family trip implies the need to take into account the interests of each participant. Turkey is one of the most popular countries, which annually receives a large number of guests. Alanya is one of the most popular and cozy towns on its expanses by the Mediterranean Sea. There are many reasons for this demand: golden sands, a shallow sea, rocky capes, secluded bays, and most importantly - affordable prices.

The flow of tourists is constant throughout the year. Thanks to its long history, visiting local attractions diversifies the leisure of guests. The infrastructure is developed and modern.

general characteristics

The Kahya Hotel 4 * (Alanya) has an amazing history. Its discovery took place in 1960. Over the years of its existence, it has been incredibly transformed and has undergone numerous renovations. It is represented by a single building of five floors. Today it is a modern building equipped with elevators. Its decoration is in line with modern trends. The compact area has a swimming area and a mini water park.

The distance to the airport is 130 km, which requires additional transfers after the flight. The staff speaks four international languages. Disabled persons are welcome, pets are not allowed. Smoking is strictly prohibited not only in the chambers, but also in public places. The staff, as noted earlier vacationing here, is friendly and helpful: any request is fulfilled in a matter of seconds.





Accommodation

The number of rooms includes 247 apartments, which differ in views from the windows, area and capacity.

As noted in numerous reviews of tourists, the interior is modest, but modern, without gloss and pretentiousness. The color scheme of the decoration is light tones of beige. The walls are hung with paintings by local painters. The floors are covered with ceramic tiles. There is a balcony with wicker furniture in every room.

The furniture is presented as a standard set: beds, table, wardrobe, chair. There is an individual split system, a plasma TV, telephone and safe. The minibar is replenished upon payment of the corresponding fee. Wi-Fi - in the public domain.

The bathroom is equipped with a hairdryer, shower or bathtub, sink and hairdryer. Complemented with towels and personal care products.





Nutrition

Meals are organized according to the buffet three times a day according to a predetermined schedule. The buffet way of serving dishes allows the vacationer to independently fill his plate with as much food as he is ready to consume. Various healthy products, including a wide selection of meat, vegetables, cereals, fruits, fish and others, will allow everyone to get enough. Little guests are provided with high chairs. Kahya Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya) often receives positive reviews for catering. As tourists note, the food here is made from fresh products and amazingly tasty.

The atmosphere of the main institution is a little gloomy, as experienced, but everyone enjoys the panoramic windows and exquisite serving. There is a summer terrace. Total capacity - more than 500 people.

All local drinks are included in the All Inclusive concept, freshly squeezed fresh juices and imported alcohol are subject to additional payment.

Meals in the restaurant, which operates on a custom-made menu, is not included in the price of the permit and is payable upon visiting. Here you can taste exclusive masterpieces of talented culinary specialists who sincerely love their profession. Pre-registration and compliance with the dress code is required.





Beach

Hotel Kahya Hotel 4 * (Alanya) is located on the first coastline at a distance of 100 meters from the sandy beach. In order to achieve it, you must go through the equipped park zone with a pond.

The Mediterranean of these regions is unusually transparent, which makes it possible to view the bottom with all its inhabitants. A gentle entry into the water ensures the safety of the play of the kids.

The infrastructure includes a shower and changing rooms. You can enjoy air baths and find a unique tan by relaxing on a cozy sunbed with soft bedding. For lovers of shade there are umbrellas and awnings. There is a rescue service on the beach, the staff of which is ready to help swimmers at any time.

For those who prefer outdoor activities, there is a volleyball field where holidaymakers compete for a team victory, communicate and relax. A lot of water activities are in demand: riding a banana, scooters and a ring, windsurfing and snorkeling. The diving center allows you to go on a journey through the amazing underwater world full of unique flora and fauna.
