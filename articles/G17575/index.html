Knitted autumn hat. Crochet and knitting patterns

In the fall, every female person wants to have the most unusual and attractive look. You can look elegant and romantic only thanks to wearing the most beautiful things. Recently, women have been focusing on hats. The most fancy knitted hats, bright and original, are a great addition to any fall outfit.

It turns out that most needlewomen knit hats on their own, and then show off in them. There is nothing complicated in knitting hats. And you can verify this by trying to make several models of autumn options, which today are the most preferred for wearing in the cool season.

What yarn to choose for knitting women's hats

Since autumn is a time of gloom, a multi-colored autumn hat will help to bring some bright shade into the image, to raise the mood with its warm tones, resembling summer. What yarn to choose to do the job, so that the product eventually worn for a long time and warmed in cool weather?

The autumn hat is designed to be worn not in severe frosts, it protects against moisture and cool winds, so you should not choose too thick yarn, tight knitting, and clean wool for its execution.

The best option is acrylic yarn with cotton. It is well worn, erased, the product does not lose its original shape.

Autumn hat is a mandatory attribute of outerwear, which may be needed in September, not to mention later autumn. Therefore, its varieties are many.

Pinocchio headdress for October

This classic hat, knitted with a simple pattern, in the form of a long cap, like the hero of the children's fairy tale ‚ÄúPinocchio‚Äù, with a large pompom is perfect for strict stylish looks and sports.





To knit such a model on a head circumference of 56-58 cm, 120 grams of acrylic yarn will be needed. You can safely experiment with color. A great option for autumn is a brown, yellow mottled thread.

Model Flowchart

The work begins with a set of four needles No. 4 110 loops, knitting will be performed in a circle, as in the first version. The pattern is very simple - one-on-one elastic band (one wrong loop, one front loop).

After knitting eighteen rows of the pattern, it is displaced and knitting continues. This is done in order to obtain a beautiful lapel cap. After forty centimeters from the beginning of knitting, the reduction of loops begins according to the scheme:

- the first row: knit together every two stitches together in two;

- the second row is repeated as the first;

- the third and fourth: every two loops are knitted face.

The remaining loops are pulled together with a thread, a pompom is sewn onto the end of the resulting cap, the diameter is chosen as desired, the pompom with a diameter of 10-15 cm looks great.

Unforgettable takes "Openwork"

It‚Äôs easy to crochet autumn hats for women . The beret shown in the photo below is decorated with an openwork rim.





For its implementation, you will need a hook number 5 and 120 grams of acrylic-cotton yarn.

Work flow chart

Crochet autumn hat in the form of a beret is tied from the top. To do this, gain a chain of two air loops and knit seven single crochets in the first loop. In each subsequent row, two single crochet stitches are knitted into each air loop. Knitting is done round-robin, in each row, seven additions of loops are made. As a result, a circle with a diameter of 26 cm is obtained. After its knitting, work on the openwork pattern begins, you need to perform it according to the diagram below in the photo.





The height of the pattern is four cm. After knitting it, you need to proceed to reducing the loops in the following order:

- first row: every 15 and 16 loops are knitted together;

- second row: every 14 and 15 loops are knitted together;

- third row: every 13 and 14 loops are knitted together.

Having performed the reduction of loops, then the base of the beret is knitted - four rows with single crochet. Openwork pattern can be decorated with small beads by sewing them on its elements.

Autumn hats for girls

No less simple and interesting are the hats for girls. Here such a zabavny model fits very easily.





The work is done in a circle with knitting needles, like the first two models. Any pattern can be chosen. It can be colored weaves. Having tied the cap in height, reduce the loops from the center. Leave only on two sides of the loop for further knitting of the ears.

This model is interesting in that it can be beaten in different color shades. Ears can be knitted different - long, short, even made from a hat-cat hat-hare or hat-fox.
