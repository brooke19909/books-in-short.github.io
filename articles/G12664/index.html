Pensions and resorts in Kabardinka: photos and reviews of tourists

15 km from the famous resort town of Gelendzhik there is a wonderful village that is able to captivate tourists with a relict pine forest and picturesque nature. In our article, we will talk about Kabardinka. The unique natural conditions of this area are skillfully used in spa treatment. Judging by the reviews, thousands of patients from all over the former USSR were able to evaluate this.

Sanatoriums of Kabardinka will not only help you improve your health, but also allow you to feel the mental lift.

Medical direction

The main medical profile of health resorts is the treatment of diseases of the nervous system, respiratory system, circulatory system, connective tissue and musculoskeletal system. Kabardinka sanatoriums offer their patients not only treatment, but also rehabilitation after illnesses and injuries.



The healing and therapeutic effect of the procedures harmoniously complement peace and silence, clean sea air, which is saturated with volatile products. In addition, the regimen, high-quality three meals a day (including dietary) nutrition, and walks in the fresh air contribute to the improvement of the condition of patients. We bring to your attention the most popular health resorts of Kabardinka.
