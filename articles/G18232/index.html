Hotel Kaftans 3 * (Turkey / Kemer) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists





One of the most popular resort regions of the Republic of Turkey is Kemer. It is famous for its diverse hotel base, represented by both inexpensive hotels and luxury five-star hotels that can satisfy the needs of the most spoiled travelers. Today we offer to get acquainted with a mid-priced hotel called Kaftans 3 *. We will find out what tourists can count on here, how much it will cost to stay, and also we will understand what impressions our compatriots had on their holidays at this hotel.

Where is located Kaftans hotel 3 * (Kemer)?

This hotel is located near the center of the resort town of Kemer. The town itself is located in the homonymous tourist region of Turkey on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea. This place is very picturesque and famous for its wonderful climate. It is about fifty kilometers away from Antalya International Airport . Thus, after arriving in Turkey, the path to the hotel will take you about one hour. The distance from Kaftans Hotel 3 * to the beach is about several hundred meters. To get to the beach, you must cross the road.

What is a three-star hotel "Kaftans" in Kemer?

The Kaftans Hotel 3 * (Turkey) received its first guests back in 1993. The last reconstruction in the hotel was carried out 10 years ago - in 2004. The hotel consists of one three-story building, equipped with an elevator and having a completely modern look. It has 68 standard rooms, which offer a pleasant view of the city or pool. Hotel Policies

According to the rules adopted at the three-star Kaftans Hotel, check-in at the rooms of newly arrived guests takes place after two in the afternoon. If you have arrived at the hotel earlier, and the room you have booked is not ready yet, you can leave your luggage in the luggage room and go for lunch, to the pool or for a walk in the city, and possibly immediately to the beach. As soon as the room is vacated by previous guests and cleaned by the hotel staff, you will be accommodated in it. As for the day of departure, you need to leave the hotel before noon. In this case, payment should be made for accommodation, as well as the use of additional services. If you have already paid for your stay at the hotel with your tour operator, then you will only have to pay a fee for additional services if you used them. At the same time, you can pay in Caftans 3 * in cash. The issue of the possibility of using plastic cards at the hotel should be agreed with the administration in advance. As for the placement of pets at the hotel, unfortunately for people who prefer to travel with their four-legged pets, their accommodation in Kaftans 3 * is not allowed.

Number of rooms

Hotel Kaftans Hotel 3 * offers everyone to stay in one of 68 comfortable standard rooms. The total capacity of the hotel is 160 people. Each hotel room is cozy, convenient and equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay of guests. So, here there is a bathroom with shower and hairdryer, individual air conditioning, telephone, TV, as well as a minibar, the use of which is paid. On the floor - carpet. Each room also has a private balcony with pool or city views. Each room is 16 square meters. Designed apartments for two guests with the ability to accommodate another additional guest.





From 10 p.m. to 7 a.m. you can order room service for an additional fee. Rooms are cleaned every day. Linen is changed three times a week, and towels are changed twice a week.

Nutrition

Meals at the Kaftans 3 * hotel (Turkey) are organized according to the All Inclusive system. The hotel's restaurant serves a buffet three times a day, offering guests to taste delicious dishes of Turkish and international cuisine, as well as various snacks and delicious desserts. There are also diet dishes. You can enjoy your favorite drinks in two bars of the hotel. Here you can order Turkish coffee, freshly squeezed juice and foreign drinks for an additional fee.

Pool

On the territory of the Kaftans Kemer 3 * hotel there is an outdoor pool with a children's section. Near it is a sun terrace with comfortable sunbeds, sun umbrellas, the use of which is free for hotel guests. Here in the bar you can also enjoy your favorite non-alcoholic and alcoholic drinks.

Sea beach

Hotel Kaftans Hotel 3 * (Turkey) is located on the second coastline and is removed from the beach at a distance of about five hundred meters. At the same time, on the way to the beach, you will need to cross the busy road. The beach itself is pebble, municipal. Rent of sunbeds, umbrellas and mattresses is paid here. Also on the beach there is the opportunity to engage in water sports. This pleasure is also paid. Twice a day from Kaftans 3 * a free shuttle service to hotel guests is organized to the beach.





Entertainment

Since the territory of the Kaftans 3 * hotel is small, there are no special opportunities for outdoor activities. However, you can have fun playing darts, backgammon, chess, billiards or table tennis. Also in the evenings there are cheerful discos.

Infrastructure

Hotel Kaftans 3 * has everything necessary for convenient and comfortable pastime of its guests infrastructure. So, the hotel reception is open 24 hours a day, so if you need late check-in or problems arise that need urgent solutions, you can always contact the reception on duty who will do everything possible to solve your problem.

The hotel also has its own parking, the use of which is free for guests. Therefore, if you decide to go on vacation in your car or plan to rent a car already in Kemer, you will not have any difficulties with parking.

Also at the hotel you can exchange currency, use dry cleaning and laundry services, leave valuables in a safe at the reception, book an excursion or transfer to the airport that interests you, and rent a car. The hotel also has a shop where you can buy souvenirs for memory and things necessary for a beach holiday. For a fee, you can use a computer and Wi-Fi access. The hotel also provides babysitting services for a fee. In addition, if you or your family members are unwell, you can call a doctor.





The cost of living in the hotel Kaftans 3 *

Despite the fact that this hotel is located almost in the center of Kemer, the prices for rooms in it will pleasantly surprise tourists. So, a seven-day tour with meals on the system "All inclusive" can be purchased at a price of just 12 thousand rubles.

Kaftans hotel 3 *: traveler reviews

As for the comments of tourists who visited the Kaftans Hotel, unfortunately, most of them are not positive. We offer to understand this in more detail.

Surprisingly, the Kaftans hotel 3 * managed to earn negative reviews on almost all counts, with the exception of its location. So, since it is located almost in the center of Kemer, literally a few steps away there are lots of shops, restaurants, bars, night clubs and other places for entertainment. The nearest beach is just 10 minutes walk at a slow pace. This fact was appreciated by tourists.

As for the rooms and the device of the hotel itself, here a rare traveler was satisfied. So, according to the general opinion of our compatriots who spent their holidays at the Kaftan Hotel 3 *, furniture, plumbing and other furnishings in the rooms are very old and in a state far from ideal. In many rooms, air conditioning did not work, taps, handles and cabinet doors constantly broke. Also, the vast majority of travelers were unsatisfied with the quality of cleaning, as well as the state of bedding.

Food in Kaftans 3 * (reviews of tourists only confirm this) also do not deserve special praise. So, despite the fact that the price of a ticket to this hotel includes meals according to the All Inclusive system, not all vacationers took the opportunity to have lunch and dinner at their hotel. According to them, the choice of dishes is extremely scarce, vegetables and fruits are stale and weathered, the taste is also very controversial.

Many of our compatriots were dissatisfied with the service staff. So, according to numerous comments, the staff showed rudeness at the slightest expression of dissatisfaction on the part of the hotel guests and was not very keen to correct the situation.

In general, most tourists agreed that the Kaftan 3 * hotel does not quite correspond to the declared three stars. Nevertheless, it is not bad for a budget youth vacation. After all, young people, as a rule, spend little time at the hotel, preferring to spend days on the beach, go on excursions or walk around the city, and go to bars and discos in the evenings. For families with children, experienced travelers strongly do not recommend this hotel.

Kemer: resort description

Today, Kemer is a world-famous Turkish resort with a developed tourist infrastructure and a host of hotels and entertainment for every taste and wallet size. It is located 42 kilometers southwest of Antalya. On the one hand, Kemer is washed by the waters of the Mediterranean Sea, and on the other, it is protected by the high Toros mountains. There are many pine forests in the region, and the sea air, combined with cool mountain winds, creates a unique climate that is very good for health. In addition, despite the fact that the summer here is quite hot, and in August the air temperature often rises to +35 degrees Celsius, the heat is not felt so much and is tolerated quite easily. All these features contribute to the magnificent comfortable rest of many tourists from all over the world.

Holiday villages Kemer

The Kemer tourist region includes several resort villages: Camyuva, Beldibi, Tekirova, Kirish and Goynuk. In each of them you will find a lot of hotels that can satisfy all the needs of tourists. There are both inexpensive two- and three-star hotels, and luxury four- and five-star hotels. We offer you to learn more about each of the resort villages of Kemer.





Beldibi village is located 15 kilometers from Kemer. Here, the majestic mountains come very close to the waters of the Mediterranean Sea, creating a picturesque landscape. Another feature of this place is the presence of many pines of Mediterranean species. Among them there are trees, the needles of which reach fifteen centimeters.

The village of Goynuk is seven kilometers away from Kemer. On its territory you will see many orange and pomegranate groves. In addition, there is a natural monument - the canyon of the Goynuk River. This is a great place for walking.

The village of Kirish is located just five kilometers from Kemer. This is one of the most picturesque corners of the Turkish coast. Arriving here, you will not get tired of admiring the emerald waters of the Mediterranean Sea, coniferous forests, steep cliffs and secluded small bays, sheltered from the winds.

The village of Tekirova is comfortably surrounded by high mountains, fragrant pine forests and orange groves. There is a large pebble beach. Also in Tekirova you can go diving and ride a yacht.

Camyuva village is located at a distance of 10 kilometers from Kemer. The main feature of the landscape of this place are the majestic peaks of the Taurus Mountains. Wide sand and pebble beaches and emerald clear waters in the village of Camyuva have a peaceful rest. Also in the resort you will find tennis courts. There are all conditions for surfing, sailing or water skiing. In addition, to the pleasure of tourists there is the possibility of practicing other water sports.
