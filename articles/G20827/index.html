Hotel Golden Beach Hotel 3 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang): overview, description and reviews

Golden Beach Hotel 3 * (Vietnam) is one of the best inexpensive hotels in Nha Trang. Many Russian tourists stay at this hotel. The rooms here are quite comfortable, and the infrastructure is well developed. Tourists, among other things, are offered a huge number of additional services.

Hotel Location Features

The hotel is located Golden Beach Hotel Nha Trang 3 * (Vietnam), reviews about which are good, not too far from the city center and at the same time quite close to the sea. The distance from the beach to the hotel is about 500 meters. Hotel guests have only about seven minutes to go to the coast. The nearest airport to the hotel is 26 km away. Arriving guests can get to the hotel either by transfer or by taxi. In total, the path to the hotel takes about 20-30 minutes.





In the immediate vicinity of the hotel is all the infrastructure necessary for a comfortable holiday for tourists Nha Trang. Hotel guests have the opportunity, without spending a lot of time, to visit shops of various specializations, pharmacies, massage parlors, markets. Transfer from the hotel to the airport is very inexpensive.

General description of the hotel

This hotel accepts guests in a multi-storey relatively new building of modern architecture (concrete, glass). Check-in at Golden Beach Hotel 3 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang) is open around the clock. Guests are populated at 12 noon. Residents must leave at 14 hours of the last paid day. Some respected employees at the hotel are able to speak Russian, which is certainly very convenient. Rooms at the hotel are non-smoking. Pets are not allowed in the hotel. At the entrance to the hotel, instead of a deposit, guests take away passports. Store them in a safe.
