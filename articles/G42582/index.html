Knitting a skirt with knitting needles: patterns and patterns

A garment such as a skirt is always present in the wardrobe of any woman. The skirt makes the image more feminine, light and dreamy. However, it is not always possible to choose the perfect model in stores that is suitable in all respects.

In this case, the solution can be knitting a skirt with knitting needles with your own hands. After all, with very little effort, you can create a unique thing, beautiful and fashionable.

Pencil skirt

For those craftswomen who are just learning to knit, the easiest way is to complete a pencil skirt. This feminine model is suitable for almost everyone, regardless of body type and height, and even the most inexperienced needlewoman will like the ease of knitting a skirt with knitting needles.

Most often, such a skirt is knitted using intricate braids and aranes, these patterns allow you to achieve maximum fit. It is better to knit this skirt model in a circle using knitting needles on a fishing line of a suitable diameter.

Typically, knitting a pencil skirt starts from the bottom up. Having collected the required number of loops, they should be closed in a ring. Then separate the loops with special markers, indicating the back and front of the future skirt. Then knit according to the selected pattern until the fabric reaches the desired length.

For the elastic, which will be located at the top of the skirt, it is better to take a little smaller diameter knitting needles, then it will turn out denser. Experienced knitters recommend threading a gum thread into the belt, then it will sit denser on the figure.

Such a classic skirt will certainly become a lifesaver in the wardrobe of any woman.

Peacock eye pattern

This perky pattern is often used when knitting skirts for girls and young girls. Due to the peculiarities of the pattern, the skirt will have a flying, flared shape.

To make the peacock eye pattern more textured, it is better to use smooth, thin yarn. The length of the skirt can be varied at your discretion, and a magnificent mini will look good, and a floor skirt in an up-to-date boho style.

Openwork zigzag pattern

A very beautiful, bright skirt can be connected with a very simple zigzag pattern. The description of knitting a skirt with knitting needles with this pattern will not be long, the rapport of the pattern itself consists of several rows.

The skirt is knitted in a circle, on annular knitting needles. Before starting work, you need to tie a small control sample and calculate the required number of loops from it. It is better to start knitting from the bottom up, gradually reducing the width of the skirt cloth according to the scheme. Continue to work with a zigzag pattern, remembering to alternate yarn. At the end of the work, knit 10 rows with the front stitch - this will be the skirt belt. Fold the belt in half along the inflection line and carefully hem. If desired, you can insert an elastic band.

Using this simple pattern, you can combine yarn in the product not only in different shades, but also in different textures. Then knitting a skirt with knitting needles for women will turn into a pleasure, and the thing will be truly unique.

Openwork skirt

Perhaps the most romantic can be called a skirt, knitted with an openwork pattern. Such models are always liked by both young girls and fans of the current boho style.

Most often, lace skirts are worn in warm time, so for knitting it is better to choose thin cotton or linen threads. Although you can choose a gentle mohair, and then the skirt will look good with autumn ankle boots.

Depending on the type of figure for knitting an openwork skirt, you can choose an A-silhouette or a more sophisticated half-sun. For craftswomen who are proficient in knitting techniques, you can offer the silhouette of the "Elven Tower". It takes a long time to count the loops, but the result will definitely please.

Knitting a skirt with knitting need to start from top to bottom. On the circular knitting needles, dial the desired number of loops, knit several rows with an elastic band. Then continue the work with the selected pattern, gradually increasing the number of loops.

Almost any openwork pattern, from simple to filigree complex lace, is suitable for knitting. If you don‚Äôt have much experience, but you really want a smart lace skirt, you can perform even stripes of lace, and additional loops to be knitted with a facial smoothness.

Knitting a fishnet skirt for women with knitting needles will certainly cheer you up, and the thing itself will become one of the basic in the wardrobe.

Wrap skirt

With the onset of cold weather, I want to please myself with warm soft things. One of these things can be a classic wrap skirt, knitted from warm soft yarn.

The advantage of this model is that it is made according to an absolutely uncomplicated pattern. Most often, thick yarn and knitting needles of the appropriate diameter are used, so the work will go pretty quickly.

When knitting a skirt with knitting needles, you can choose a relief pattern such as simple arana or textured pattern "locks". And you can emphasize the beauty and elegance of the silhouette, using a simple facial surface and woolen threads of your favorite classic shade.

This model is suitable not only for slender girls, but also for owners of curvaceous forms, because thanks to the vertical line of smell, the figure will look much more slim.

New Year Returning

A few years ago, the elegant and sexy silhouette of the year was undeservedly forgotten, but now it is again at the height of fashion. Which is not surprising, because this is one of the most feminine types of skirts: the model emphasizes the waist and scatters down a beautiful frill.

One year old skirt is suitable for any season - you can knit it from thin cotton with the addition of viscose or choose a warm yarn and a dense pattern.

To knit a skirt "Year" you first need to make a pattern in accordance with the parameters of the figure. Its length without a shuttlecock can be up to the knee or be slightly lower. On the pattern, it is necessary to mark the line of the hips, to this length it will be necessary to expand the knitted fabric.

To get started, you need to dial the required number of loops on circular knitting needles and tie 8-10 rows with an elastic band. Then you need to gradually increase the number of loops. Continue adding loops until the fabric of the product reaches the line of extension of the hips, then begin to knit straight.

Having tied the length of the skirt almost completely to the shuttlecock, it is necessary to reduce a small number of loops. The fit of the skirt in the knee area will visually increase the slimness of the figure.

Then double the number of loops (dial additional loops of yarn over one) and tie a shuttlecock of the desired length on them. Hinges to close. And you can try on your new feminine skirt!
