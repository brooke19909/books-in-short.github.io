A fully functional method to reduce consumption is the use of reactive power compensators. They have been used in industry for a long time, but household models have appeared relatively recently. As you know, electricity does the job. It is for her that we pay. But only one part of the work is useful, and the other is undesirable. For example, the rotation of the motor shaft is a pure benefit, but its heating is a side effect, although also committed by electricity, for which you have to pay. A household compensator is plugged into a home outlet and reduces the reactive power spent by powerful appliances . Please note: the more motors (coils, capacitors, transformers) are used, the higher the efficiency.

More serious options are solar panels and wind generators. By installing any of the solutions, you can think about refusing centralized energy supply. The service life of polycrystalline solar cells in solar panels is at least 20 years. Newer panels based on amorphous silicon serve less - up to 20 years, at best. With a sufficient amount of funds, the elements are collected in batteries. In a private house, you can make a roof of them. This increases the power output. A wind generator is an equally attractive alternative. Modern models work without vibration, so they can be placed next to the house.

  • the need to use rechargeable batteries to accumulate charge reduces usability. The lead battery lasts up to 5 years, then it needs to be replaced. The inverter circuit also has a shorter service life than the windmill or panel itself.
