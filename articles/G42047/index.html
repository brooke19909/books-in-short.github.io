How to pack flowers so that the bouquet looks spectacular?

Those people are mistaken who believe that the beauty and originality of a bouquet depends only on the colors included in the composition. Packaging is no less important in this matter. If it harmoniously combines with flowers, it emphasizes their tenderness and beauty. Today, florists and designers have a huge arsenal of options for the original design of compositions.

How to pack flowers: choose material



First of all, try to move away from stereotypes. Show your imagination for packing flowers using various materials. It can be a special net, sisal, corrugated paper, rice cloth, fabric (felt), transparent mica, etc. How to pack flowers, it is up to each of you. However, it should be remembered that the beautiful and skillful design of the bouquet is a manifestation of respect for the addressee of the congratulation.

Use corrugated paper

According to many florists, such material, made in Italy, is suitable for passionate natures, and paper from Taiwan is suitable for more sophisticated persons. But in any case, the choice is yours.

How to pack flowers with corrugated paper?

The basic rule of florists: a bouquet should be made regardless of

available packaging, and corrugated paper should complement the flowers. This material is refined and elegant. It does not dazzle and allows you to create small stylish bouquets. Such natural material is popular among florists. It is perfectly draped and also has a very affordable price. Corrugated paper is designed to unobtrusively emphasize the luxury of a bouquet. Therefore, it should not be too much. Choose its shade to match the color of the buds. Packaging that is brighter than the bouquet itself looks tasteless. Use corrugated paper in the same shade.

How to choose a package

In order to decide in what form you will give flowers, you need to learn about the preferences of the person to whom you are going to present them. When deciding how to pack flowers, remember that a variety of decorative elements are in fashion today: laces and ribbons, bows and beads, crystals and rhinestones. We use felt



Today, floristry is developing rapidly. More and more packaging materials are appearing. And the question of how to pack flowers in felt is not too complicated. This is a surprisingly soft and obedient material, which is a universal package. Usually it does not require any additional decorative elements.

Packing in felt is not a very complicated process. The most important thing is to properly arrange the plants and form a bouquet of the shape that you like best. Then the base of the bouquet, along with decorative greenery, is pulled by tape. Next, measure the felt, cut it off the roll and wrap the bouquet. The edges of the packaging can be fastened with a stapler or gently sealed with transparent tape. Tie the leg of the bouquet with a ribbon of contrasting color.

Today you learned a little about how to pack flowers. Show your imagination, and then your bouquets will always be the most beautiful and original.
