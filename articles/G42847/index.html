East Sea Resort 3 * (Thailand / Pattaya): photos and reviews of tourists

East Sea Resort 3 * hotel attracts travelers from all over the world with its territory literally drowning in verdure, small but cozy bungalows, as well as a surprisingly calm atmosphere. Pleasantly surprises vacationers and a great combination of good location - almost in the center of Pattaya, quality service and affordable prices.

Pattaya - a resort for an unforgettable vacation

Pattaya is one of Thailand's most renowned travelers. Active development of this resort was in 1961. Since then, about 120 different hotels, many cafes, restaurants, clubs, shops and other facilities for a comfortable stay have appeared on the territory of the city.

Conventionally, Pattaya resort is divided into three areas - North (Naklua) - it is here that the East Sea Hotel, Central (Phattaya) and South (Jomtien) are located. On the beach of any area, vacationers are invited to take a walk on the sea by boat or yacht, ride a scooter or a jet ski, fly a parachute behind a boat, go windsurfing or snorkeling.

There is something to do in the resort and in the daytime. So, the Mini Siam complex deserves special attention, in which a huge number of reduced copies of architectural monuments of Thailand are collected, as well as many exotic plants.

Hotel location

Pattaya Resort (Thailand), which is located on the territory of East Sea, is located at a distance of 120 km from the international airport in Bangkok. However, the journey from the hotel to the "air station" takes no more than two hours by public transport, and if you order a transfer from the hotel, then even less. Hotel East Sea Resort 3 * is a true paradise for lovers of a relaxing and enjoyable holiday.





The hotel territory is a real tropical garden with lots of palm trees, flowers and other greenery. The hotel was opened in 2008, and the last restoration in it was carried out in 2011. Pleasantly pleased vacationers and the favorable location of the hotel - only 3 km from the center of Pattaya. It will take you about 20 minutes to overcome this path on foot, but you can also use one of the regular buses that regularly ‚Äúwalk‚Äù to the center past the hotel.

Infrastructure Sea Resort

East Sea Resort 3 * is a bungalow type hotel. Its total area is more than 20,000 m¬≤. Bungalows are interconnected by cement paths, on both sides of which various greens and trees grow. The hotel has a restaurant, bar, free internet cafe and 4 pools with a jacuzzi. From the threshold of some bungalows, you can immediately jump into the water and enjoy a swim.

Number of rooms

40 one-story bungalows - these are the rooms of the East Sea Resort 3 * hotel . Pattaya has many hotels on its territory, but not every one of them has an equally calm atmosphere. Each bungalow can have 2 to 4 rooms. In total, the hotel has 175 standard non-smoking rooms. Each room can accommodate no more than 3 people, but initially they are designed only for two. In some rooms, at the request of guests, an extra or a cot can be installed.





All rooms are equipped with private bathroom. Hot and cold water is available at any time of the day. Each room has a TV with Russian channels, a refrigerator, a hairdryer, a double bed, air conditioning, a wardrobe, bedside tables with nightlights and a telephone. A safe and minibar are available at an extra charge. It is worth noting that the prices for these services are quite acceptable. Rooms are cleaned daily, towels change at the same rate. Pets are allowed, provided that they weigh no more than 5 kg.

Nutrition

The East Sea Resort 3 * hotel offers breakfast only. There is no buffet system here; waiters deliver all food separately for each table. The breakfast menu consists of approximately the following list of dishes: scrambled eggs or fried eggs, toast, ham, sausages, jam, butter, fruits, juices, hot drinks.

As for the rest of the meals at the hotel, there is a restaurant here, where you can order your favorite from the menu at any time of the day. At the restaurant you can enjoy both European and Thai cuisine. In addition, the hotel has a bar open around the clock, which offers not only the widest selection of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, but also snacks, desserts and many other dishes. It is worth noting that the food in the restaurant and in the bar is always fresh and tasty.

If the variety of food that is offered at the hotel is not enough for the tourist, then he can always go to the resort center and visit one of the many catering establishments that Pattaya (Thailand) has on its territory.

Tour Prices

For two adults, a 7-day vacation in Thailand with accommodation at the East Sea Hotel will cost about $ 2,000. The price of this tour will include air tickets and insurance. You will need to pay an additional transfer from the airport to the hotel or use public transport, which Pattaya has enough. Hotel prices include breakfast only, while lunches and dinners must be paid extra. However, the prices in the restaurant and bar of the East Sea Hotel are quite reasonable.





Entertainment and additional services

There are not so many options for an interesting pastime at the hotel, since it is more intended for a relaxing family vacation. Of the main entertainment - swimming in the pool and enjoying all kinds of drinks in the lobby bar, in the territory of which, by the way, free Internet access is provided. It is worth noting that sunbeds and umbrellas by the pool are paid, only towels are provided for free. Among the additional services it is also worth noting the availability of dry cleaning and laundry.





The East Sea Resort 3 * hotel does not have its own beach, but there is a public beach at a distance of 500 meters from it. The shore and entrance to the sea are sand and pebble. There is a road between the hotel and the beach.

Hotel reviews and photos of tourists

A true paradise for a pleasant and measured rest - that is how the majority of tourists characterize the hotel East Sea Resort 3. Reviews about this hotel are mostly positive. Everyone notes a good combination of cost and quality of service. Vacationers were pleasantly pleased by the convenient location of the hotel - almost in the city center, near the beach.

As for the rooms, their utmost simplicity is noted, but there is not a single complaint about the cleanliness of the room or bathroom. Some tourists complained about the lack of a hairdryer, which, according to the tour operator, should have been provided. Traveler reviews say good things about East Sea staff. Hotel employees are ready at any time to come to the rescue or answer questions of interest. Most hotel staff speak good English.





According to tourists, after a vacation in Pattaya, the East Sea Hotel, they managed to bring a lot of photos with them. Even without leaving the territory of the hotel, you can make many interesting pictures. Indeed, everywhere here is very green and bright, and bungalows surrounded by pools deserve attention. And if you want to make your photo album even more diverse, then you should go for a walk in the center of Pattaya.
