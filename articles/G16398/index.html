Hotel Astoria Hotel Spa 4 * Kemer, Turkey: reviews

One of the most visited resorts in Turkey is Kemer. It is located between the boundless expanse of the Mediterranean and the steep Taurus mountain range. Picturesque landscapes, amazing nature, crystal clear sea and the historical heritage of the village are what attract tourists to these parts. The four-star Astoria Hotel Spa is a great place for experienced guests to spend their long-awaited summer vacation.





general characteristics

The hotel complex, due to its compactness and unprecedented cosiness, giving a relaxing stay and good mood, attracts a large number of tourists from year to year. The convenient location, due to the distance of 55 km from the international airport, contributes to the absence of long transfers after the flight. Undoubtedly, this is to the liking of holidaymakers, especially those who went on a trip with the kids. Kemer city center is 150 meters away. The area is full of shops, cafes, bars, restaurants and other leisure facilities.

Hotel Astoria Hotel Spa 4 * was opened in 2006, and immediately took its rightful place in the tourism industry in Turkey. Last update made in 2013. The main and only building, having four floors, is located on a space of 2100 square meters. There is a pool and park area. The territory is always clean and well-groomed, flowers are fragrant, palm trees save from the hot heat, greens have a beneficial effect on the health of the body.

Settlement with animals is not possible. There is a room for people with disabilities. Smoking is not allowed throughout. Distance to the sea is 300 meters. Payment is made by cards of international banks.





Accommodation

All apartments of the Astoria Hotel Spa 4 * (Turkey, Kemer) are represented by a single Standard category, which differ only in the opening view from the windows. Their total number is 78 units. The area varies from 20 to 24 squares, the maximum possible placement is 3 people.

Glazing - French, allows the light of bright rays to freely penetrate the guests, so that the room is always bright and comfortable. In addition, sconces and spotlights play a role, filling the room with artificial lighting.

The interior of the room has a modern, simple finish without extravagant decor elements. On the walls are paintings, there are vases with flowers. The floors are laminated. The color scheme of pastel colors prevails, however it is shaded by bright bedspreads, curtains and dark wood furniture. There is access to a balcony equipped with furniture necessary for meeting the sunset.

Catering at the Astoria Hotel Spa 4 * (Kemer) takes place in the form of a buffet way of serving food, where each vacationer independently chooses their favorite dishes. The buffet spread thanks to its convenience and a number of advantages. Five meals a day are held in the main restaurant, which is designed for 240 people. It has a summer terrace.

According to tourists, the interior of this institution has the appearance and growth of appetite, as well as a pleasant meal. Despite some simplicity, the design is tasteful.
