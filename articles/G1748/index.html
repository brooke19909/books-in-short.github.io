Machine made of sweets. Learning to do it yourself

A gift in the form of sweets will please anyone - both a child and an adult. But today it has become fashionable to give a regular box of sweets. Present from sweets can be issued in original compositions: bouquets, topiary, ships, cars. Making such gifts is a simple matter, and each of you will be able to cope with it yourself. In this article, a master class is presented to the attention of readers, which describes how to make a machine from sweets with your own hands. Having carefully studied the information, you will see that such a composition is easy to manufacture.

A sweet gift is a candy machine. Materials Preparation Stage



A car is what we will make of sweets. For work, we need the following materials:

  • a piece of foam 5-6 cm thick;
  • thick cardboard;
  • White paper;
  • glue;
  • thermal gun;
  • compass;
  • sandpaper;
  • candy.

Sweets will need several types: small caramel "Eclair", rectangular in type "Cow" or "Chamomile", round - "Golden Lily", long - "Ice stick" or "Konafetto". Of course, your candy machine can also be made from other sweets. The main thing is that their shape and size correspond to those indicated in the master class.

Performance composition



- Case. Cut two parts out of the foam. The size of one is 10x20 cm, the other is 10x10 cm. Cut the last one a little on one side so that there is a smooth descent. Sand the sections with sandpaper. Glue the blanks together so that the small part is large on top, while shifting to one edge. If you do everything right, you will immediately notice how the product takes the form of a car. Next, glue the blank with paper.

- A car made of sweets, of course, cannot be without wheels. We will make them out of cardboard. Draw circles on it that are suitable in proportion to the car (about 5-6 cm), and cut them out. It is desirable to take cardboard corrugated or ordinary in two additions. One way or another, round parts should be tight.

- Making the product candy. Glue a round sweetness in the middle of each wheel - these will be disks. Around them, fasten the caramels in a circle. Next, attach the wheels to the foam blank in the right places. Now decorate the car body with rectangular candies.

Skip the places where the windows are supposed to be. Glue the sides as well. If sweets of similar shape do not fit on the edges of the composition, decorate them with small caramels. The end sides of the machine are made of candy tubes.

Decoration of the composition

The candy machine is almost ready. It remains to revive her. Draw or make eyes on the front window using the application method (as in the characters of the Cartoon Cars). If you are preparing a sweet gift for an adult, then you can glue his portrait on the side of the side glass or draw a cartoon.

Now you know how to make a car out of sweets. This workshop is an example for beginners in this field. You can come up with the shape of the car yourself, pick up any other sweets for its manufacture. Imagine and create. Enjoy your creative moments!
