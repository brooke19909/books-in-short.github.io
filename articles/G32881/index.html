The comparative historical method includes several research methods.

An external reconstruction technique is often used. It represents the identification of genetically identical words and morphemes in related languages. At the same time, they reveal the results of regular changes in the sounds of the source language. In addition, a method is used to construct a hypothetical proto-language model and rules for deriving certain morphemes in descendants.
