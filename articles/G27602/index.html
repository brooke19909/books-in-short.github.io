The Melody Phuket Hotel 3 *, Thailand, about. Phuket: overview, description and reviews

Phuket Island is one of the southern provinces of Thailand, a resort very popular with Russian tourists. It is located in the Adaman Sea and is connected to the mainland by a bridge. The tourist infrastructure on this island is developed just fine. There is also a huge number of comfortable hotels. A very good opinion among holidaymakers has developed, for example, about the hotel The Melody Phuket 3 *.

Hotel Location Reviews

This three-star hotel is located in the village of Karon. This resort village deals with a small area near the ocean. The village is located about an hour from Phuket Airport. Tourists arriving to the island by plane can get to the hotel either by transfer bus or taxi. The island's capital, Phuket, is only 15 km from the hotel.

In the village itself, the hotel, according to many tourists, is located quite conveniently. Everything necessary for a comfortable stay Karon's infrastructure is in close proximity to it.





Its guests consider a rather large distance from the sea to be a certain disadvantage of the hotel. The nearest beach to the hotel, Kata, is about 800 meters away. It takes about 15 minutes to get here from the hotel. Karon Beach is located a little further than Kata - a kilometer from the hotel. Also, some hotel guests prefer to travel by public transport to Surin Beach. This place is located 20 km from the hotel. The exact address of the hotel is as follows: 179 Patak Road, Muang, Karon, Phuket, Thailand 83100 Kata Beach.

Road to the beach

To the seashore from the hotel The Melody Phuket 3 * (Phuket, Kata) to go, as already mentioned, is quite far. For young tourists, the road may not seem too tiring. However, for older guests, as well as families with children, a path of 15 minutes in the heat, on busy streets without sidewalks, is most likely not to be convenient.





To get to the sea, when leaving the hotel you need to turn left, go to the intersection and turn again in the same direction. Then you should again go to the intersection. Turning right here, you can get to Karon Beach, to the left - to Kata. And there, and there you can relax very well.

Karon Beach Features

At this point in the ocean, the coastline is covered with very high silica sand. Therefore, when walking, he crunches under his feet like snow. The total length of Karon Beach is 6 km. Moreover, the coastline is very wide. Therefore, even with a large crowd of people, a place for new vacationers is always located. The sea water on this beach is crystal clear. The entrance to the ocean in this place is gentle, and the excitement is not too strong. The Karon beach is very well equipped. There are plenty of sun loungers, and you can rent them at a low price. There are many different restaurants and cafes on the coastline.

Kata Beach

It is believed that this place is best suited for families with children. Kata is a very calm beach. At the same time, the infrastructure here is developed no worse than on Karon. The length of Kata beach is 1.5 km, width - 50-70 m.





The coast in this place of the ocean is absolutely flat. Therefore, shallow water is formed, convenient for bathing children. There are absolutely no stones in the water at the entrance to the sea. There are fewer sunbeds on this beach than on Karon. However, there are almost always free. Palm trees grow along the coastline, and tourists have the opportunity to sit in the shade if necessary.

All over Kata beach, as well as on Karon, all kinds of cafes and restaurants are scattered. There is no transit road along the sea here. Do not settle on the beach and all sorts of discos. Therefore, silence, apart from the sound of waves, practically does not violate the shore.
