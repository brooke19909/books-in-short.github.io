Thanthip Beach Resort 3 * (Thailand / Phuket): description and reviews

Planning an unforgettable vacation in Phuket in Thailand? Looking for a comfortable but inexpensive hotel near the beach, but some distance from the bustling city center? Then be sure to pay attention to the three-star Thanthip Beach Resort. We will talk further about what this hotel offers its guests.



Location

Thanthip Beach Resort is located on Phuket Island, just two hundred meters from the popular Patong Beach. In its vicinity there are many cafes, restaurants, shops, massage parlors, tour desks and other tourist infrastructure facilities. Patong city center and Bangla Road, a popular pedestrian street, are around 500 meters from the hotel. You can walk here in a quarter of an hour. The nearest international airport is fifty kilometers away.



Thanthip Beach Resort 3 * (Thailand, Patong, Phuket): description and photo

This hotel was opened in 2006. Since then, renovation work has been carried out here several times. Until July 2011, the hotel was called Thanthip Villa.

This hotel is perfect for a relaxing comfortable stay. On its territory there is a small tropical garden, a swimming pool with a children's section, a sun terrace with comfortable sun loungers, a restaurant and a bar. The housing stock of the hotel consists of 77 rooms of standard, superior and deluxe categories (with a balcony or direct access to the pool). All apartments are freshly renovated and equipped with everything necessary for living: furniture, a TV, a fridge with a minibar, air conditioning, tea set, hairdryer, and a bathroom. Rooms are cleaned on a daily basis.

The hotel reception is open 24 hours a day. Throughout the hotel, including apartments, guests can use free Wi-Fi.



Cost of living

An important factor when choosing a hotel for most tourists, of course, is the price of accommodation in it. As for the three-star Thanthip Beach Resort (Patong, Phuket) we are discussing today, the cost of living here, of course, can be called very budget. So, for example, accommodation for two in the month of August will cost you in the amount of 18 thousand rubles for a standard room and 23 thousand rubles for an improved room. Accommodation in superior apartments during the week will cost from 27 thousand rubles. These prices include breakfast in the hotel restaurant.
