Vologda, hotel "Spasskaya": description, features and reviews

Thousands of tourists come to Vologda every year who want to get acquainted with the history of the region and visit holy places. There are more than two hundred cultural and historical monuments.

Finding accommodation in Vologda is not difficult, it is much more difficult to choose the best option from the presented accommodations in guest houses, hostels, mini-hotels and hotels of the city.

Choosing a hotel for accommodation

Choosing a hotel in Vologda, you can be based on the following criteria:

  1. Walking distance to the main attractions of the city or proximity to a developed transport network.
  2. Design rooms.
  3. Additional amenities.
  4. Price per night.

Among the many offers in the city of Vologda, the Spasskaya 3 * hotel fully meets all the selection criteria listed: it is located near the Vologda Kremlin, the rooms are decorated in a classic style without excessive luxury, excellent service is offered, and the room category directly affects the cost of living. For travelers with a modest budget, a standard with all amenities is offered. For guests with large facilities, a luxury suite or apartment.

About hotel

Spasskaya Hotel opened in 1990 and is focused on the reception of guests of various levels.





Arriving at the hotel, guests immediately find themselves in a spacious hall that can accommodate more than one tourist group. There is also a reception desk where guests are registered and receive the necessary tourist information about the city and its attractions (Vologda map, information booklets).

The hotel staff is polite and helpful, tries to fulfill all the requests of guests, guarantees order and silence in the building. Most visitors associate the reigning atmosphere with home comfort and coziness.

Location

The building of the Vologda Spasskaya Hotel (reservation department phone: (8172) 72-01-45) is located in the historical center at the address: Vologda city, Oktyabrskaya Street, 25. It is only ten minutes walk from the Vologda Kremlin. Also within walking distance are other attractions of the city: Spaso-Prilutsky Monastery, Petrovsky Dom, the Architectural and Ethnographic Museum of the Vologda Region, the Museum of the World of Forgotten Things and others. The design of the Spasskaya Hotel (Vologda) fits perfectly into the architectural style of this part of the city.





A little more time (15 minutes) will take a walk to the train and bus stations. Convenient transport links allow tourists to easily reach any attraction of the Vologda Territory.

Additional services

For corporate meetings, business meetings, seminars, presentations, lectures and trainings in a city such as Vologda, the Spasskaya Hotel offers a conference room for up to one hundred people. It is equipped with comfortable furniture and modern equipment:

Additionally, package offers are offered for event participants. When renting a conference room, a discount is provided for organizing a business lunch, buffet or coffee break.

Positive feedback from vacationers

Proximity to the Kremlin and other sights of the city is the main advantage enjoyed by the Spasskaya Hotel (Vologda), reviews of which on the Internet are approximately half negative. Nevertheless, in addition to an excellent location, vacationers note:

  • cleanliness of rooms;
  • delicious and varied breakfast;
  • the friendliness of the staff;
  • quick check-in, even before checkout time;
  • good internet throughout the hotel complex.




Negative guest reviews

Many tourists are attracted by Vologda. Spasskaya Hotel is associated with the Soviet era among some guests. Here, in the Soviet way, it is an unimportant organization of a meeting of guests and a coupon food system, the lack of a hairdryer in the room and saving on toiletries, old furniture, narrow beds and hard mattresses.

According to many guests of a city such as Vologda, the Spasskaya hotel (prices for accommodation, by the way, are not at all low here) needs modernization. A person with a complaint will most likely not like the hotel. But those for whom the indicated minuses are not so fundamental can safely stop here, as there are enough positive moments too.

Spasskaya Hotel can be recommended for independent nights to independent travelers without children or business travelers.
