Vardane - attractions of the Sochi resort

A place where you can relax from the bustling city, enjoy the fresh air, retire with nature - this is Vardane. Sights of this resort annually attract a lot of tourists. Fresh, clean air, the sea is nearby, the mountains are an ideal vacation spot for people tired of noisy cities.





Location

Vardane is located near Sochi, 25 kilometers from the center. Many people come to rest in the Lazarevsky region, and this resort is no exception. If we talk about the Vardan, the attractions and entertainments of which attract tourists, then the first thing is, of course, the sea. And the climate. These places do not know such a season as winter, the weather is always pleasant there. This is all due to the comfortable climate of humid subtropics. Accordingly, the summer is long, so that tourists come to the village of Vardan all year round. Attractions that should be noted first of all are stunning boxwood forests and spacious meadows. In general, the nature in these places is magnificent. Take, for example, the Berendeevo kingdom and the Mammadov Gorge - these names are recreational facilities that are part of the Sochi National Park. It is located very close to Lazarevsky. There is something to see - rubble, grottoes, waterfalls, landscapes. And for lovers of drive in Vardan there are trips on ATVs. This is great entertainment that will definitely appeal to thrill-seekers. And many decide to book an equestrian ride - this will be an ideal pastime for lovers of horses and nature. The reserve area, which conduct a tour, amazes with its beauty, tea plantations and a picturesque mountain river.
