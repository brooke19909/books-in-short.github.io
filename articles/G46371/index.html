Portable electronic steelyard - a useful acquisition for the home

Electronic steelyard is a compact device that allows you to carry out fairly accurate weight measurements in various conditions, whether it is a visit to the market, a trip to nature or fishing. There is a flight on economy class aircraft, and it is important to know the exact weight of the luggage, such as a bicycle. To find out how much an item weighs up to 10 grams, help hand-held electronic scales - steelyard. What kind of device is this? Let's consider in more detail.

Description



The electronic steelyard consists of a liquid crystal display located in a plastic case. On top of the case there is a metal ring, for which the device is held during weighing. With this device you can get the exact weight of the item, even if it is large enough. To do this, the electronic steelyard should be fixed on a strong support. 40 kg is the maximum weight limit for this appliance. The minimum border is 10 g.
In order to carry out a weight measurement, hang the item on a special hook. Sometimes it is necessary to know the weight of the goods without packaging. What to do in this case? Hang empty containers on the hook and press the TARE key. After zeroing, you can load the goods and perform a ‚Äúclean‚Äù weighing.

Weight fixation

To have the exact weight, you should wait until the load on the hook stops flapping. At the same time, after about two seconds, the numbers will appear and fix on the display. A lock icon or the inscription ‚ÄúLOCK‚Äù appears on the scoreboard. After this, sometimes you need to continue the measurement. Off

If it is necessary to turn off the electronic steelyard, release the hook from the hinged load and then press the ON / OF key. If you need to start work again, press the same button again. At rest, for example, more than 120 seconds of inactivity, the electronic steelyard will automatically turn off.

Additional Information

1. When turning on the steelyard and when weighing, the device should be positioned vertically.
2. Remember that you should hold the electronic steelyard only by the ring, otherwise the accuracy of measurements can significantly decrease . In addition, the device itself may be damaged.
3. Vibrations and strong shocks should be avoided during weighing.
4. If you need to wash the steelyard, you should do this with a wet, damp cloth. You can add a little detergent. It is not recommended to use gasoline, soda, solvents or other corrosive substances for cleaning.
5. Do not forget to change the batteries in time, otherwise they may leak and spoil the "filling" of your steelyard. If the scales have a long rest, then at this time it is better to remove the batteries from it.
6. In the event of a breakdown, you must take the device to a service center or consult a specialist. It is not recommended to make repairs yourself.
