Mirror Lake. Another natural wonder

Mirror Lake is a place of unprecedented beauty. Clean air, clear water, a convenient beach - all this attracts vacationers, fishermen and tourists. These places are great for relaxing with family, friends or for a corporate trip.





Origin of the lake

Zerkalnoe Lake is of glacial origin and is located in the water system of the Wieliczka River, not far from the lakes Krasnogvardeiskoe and Podgornoe. The lake is 4 km long and 1 km wide. The lake has a deep water, the maximum depth is 16 meters. The lake has the shape of an elongated drop. In the southeast of the lake are two islands. Locals are used to calling them Razluki Island and the Island of Love, and the geographical name of the islands is Big and Small.

How to get to the lake?

There are two ways to get to the Mirror Lake. The first way is to go by car along the Primorsky highway to the village of Zelenaya Roscha, from there turn right and go towards the railway station of Yappilya. There are convenient porches on the south side of the lake. The second method is to take a train from St. Petersburg to Zelenogorsk, then transfer to a train and get off at Yappilya station. Already from this station to the lake will remain 2 km on foot.





Where did the lake get its name from?

Crystal clear water and gave the name to this lake. Even at a depth of about 10 m, visibility is good and scuba divers do not even need a flashlight. Pine forest surrounds Lake Zerkalnoe from all sides. The Leningrad Region has many natural attractions, but this lake is one of the cleanest and deepest in all of St. Petersburg. There are convenient parking lots for cars along the entire coast of the reservoir. Mirror Lake has a clean sandy bottom, as it has low overgrowth. Only in the northern part of the lake can one meet small thickets of algae.

There are excellent beaches around the perimeter of the reservoir, which have a convenient gentle slope to the water. They are equipped with changing cabins and even rent catamarans, boats and other equipment. And every summer the beaches here are filled with vacationers.

The natural world of the lake

In the center of the lake there is a funnel, the diameter of which is about 20 meters. Algae, in which schools of fry are found, have grown on the edges of the funnel. In the funnel there are keys from which pure water comes. The lake is rich in a variety of fish, most often fishermen come across roach, perch, ruff and bream. A little less often on the lake you can catch zander, tench, pike and burbot. Among fans of sitting with a fishing rod, the Mirror Lake is very popular. Fishing in these places will provide a good catch, which will not be embarrassing to show friends.





Tourism and the lake. The recreation center "Lake Mirror"

In recent years, tourist attendance of the lake has increased markedly. This is due to the open recreation center, which was named in honor of the lake "Lake Mirror". Here, far from the noisy cities in a pine forest, tourists can retire to nature. And for a moment forget about everyday worries. At this recreation center you can have a great family vacation, have fun and relax. The recreation center "Lake Mirror" places its visitors in the main and hotel buildings.
