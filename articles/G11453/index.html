Airport in Krasnodar: general information

The "southern capital" of Russia is often called the city of Krasnodar of amazing beauty. The airport of this major administrative center receives a large number of flights daily. The city is distinguished by its hospitality; it welcomes its guests surrounded by green streets and picturesque coastal areas. Krasnodar can rightfully be called a comfortable resort area that meets all modern requirements. Every year a large number of tourists visit it, seeking to relax in the south of Russia.

Krasnodar Airport

At 12 kilometers from the center of this city operates an international airport, which is the main flight harbor of the Krasnodar Territory. Currently, he is working with more than 40 carriers. Airplanes departing from this airport travel in 56 directions (30 of them are international). Flights from St. Petersburg, Sochi, Khabarovsk, Volgograd and many other cities of Russia land on the airfield. The destination of a large number of flying ships is Krasnodar Airport. The route of some aircraft runs through Moscow and Europe.

The main airport of the Krasnodar Territory (also called "Pashkovsky") is endowed with high throughput. It has two runways that can function simultaneously.

Development Facts

The starting point for the formation of the airport of Krasnodar is 1932. During this time period, 7 aircraft designed to combat harmful insects landed on the lands of the Pashkovsky state farm. After that, an air base was formed in these places.

In 1934, it underwent reorganization, as a result of which the bulk of the transport ships began to carry passengers. During the war years, Krasnodar airport aircraft delivered fuel and ammunition to the front. They transported wounded soldiers. After the end of World War II, the airport began to accept aircraft of the Li-2 and Il-12 type.

In 1960, an air terminal was built in Pashkovsky, and a concrete runway began to be built. In 1984, another section of the airfield was adapted for takeoff and landing of aircraft.

Infrastructure

Airport Krasnodar-1 has a developed infrastructure. It has terminals designed to serve international and Russian flights, a cargo terminal. In the immediate vicinity there is a hotel and several hotels where passengers can spend the night.

The terminal has superior areas, shops, cafes, and a pharmacy. Krasnodar Airport offers passengers on-board catering services and medical facilities. This air harbor has a certain infrastructure for passengers with disabilities. There is a special board in the terminal building, information from which people with low vision can read. It provides an opportunity for visually impaired passengers to find out the necessary information.

Krasnodar Airport is equipped with specially equipped toilets. They are intended for citizens who have difficulty traveling. There are ramps in the terminal building, there is an induction system that can improve the performance of hearing aids. There is a tactile tile on the street , which serves as a guide to the pedestrian crossing for visually impaired passengers.

How to get to the airport

Krasnodar Airport is located very close to the central part of the city. Reach it really by public transport or taxi. Trolleybus number 7 leaves in this direction every 20 minutes. Travel time on this vehicle is only an hour. Minibuses leave from different points of Krasnodar, following to the international airport.

Passengers who value comfort often get to their destination by taxi. This vehicle is also chosen by those who are afraid of not having time for their flight. For security reasons, although many private carriers operate, you should use the services of an official taxi.

Currently, Krasnodar Airport is of strategic importance for our country. This largest flying harbor, according to many, is the main air gate of the southern region of Russia.
