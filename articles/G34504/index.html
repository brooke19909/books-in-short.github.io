Hotel Bravo Djerba 4 * (Tunisia, Djerba): description, services, reviews

Tunisia attracts Russians primarily with its African exoticism. It is unusual for us culture, architecture of buildings, customs, and all this I want to see. Tunisia also attracts with its warm climate, blue-turquoise sea and white-gold beaches under palm trees. Hotel Bravo Djerba 4 * is a win-win choice for anyone who dreams of a carefree relaxing holiday away from the noise and smog of European cities, who decided to give themselves summer in the cold winter, who love to discover something new.

general information

The island of Djerba lies just 2 km from the continental part of Tunisia, and it is small in area. The distance between its extreme western and eastern points is slightly more than 28 km, and from north to south it is even less. You can go round it in just half a day. But it is here that the best chic and respectable hotels in Tunisia are located. We will talk about the most beautiful of them, which has recently been called Hotel Bravo Djerba 4 *. Previously, this hotel was known as Vincci Resort Alkantara, and this is still preserved on some maps. It was built in 2003, but in 2015 the owner changed here, and with it the name. Now the hotel is undergoing large-scale reconstruction to receive tourists at a very high level.



Location

Hotel Bravo Djerba 4 * is located in the Midoun region on the outskirts of the small Arab village of Agir, to the center of which is about two and a half kilometers. Some take the name of the region for the city, but the village of Djerba Midoun is located not on the coast, but in the depths of the island, from the hotel in question it is more than 6 km. The island of Djerba itself lies in the gulf of the Mediterranean Sea of ‚Äã‚ÄãGabes, and the village of Agir and its environs are located on the banks of a quiet shallow bay, which has a beneficial effect on the local microclimate.

There are no infrastructure facilities of any importance in the hotel area, mainly tourists will observe classic African landscapes. But on the way to the village, which runs literally nearby, there are several shops with local goods and sweets.

How to get there

Holidays in Djerba are so popular that they built a large international airport. It is called Zarzis. You can fly here from Russia by direct flight from May to mid-October. In Bravo Djerba 4 * from the airport, modern transfers deliver. The distance is 25.4 km, travel time is about an hour. It is best to take a taxi to your hotel on your own. Prices are strictly on the counter, so they won‚Äôt take too much. Tourists who bought a tour to Bravo Djerba 4 * out of season will have to fly to Tunisia to the mainland, and to Djerba to get by domestic airlines. From here, if desired, you can get to the island by bus. It will take 8 hours, but tourists will take a small tour of the country and sail on a ferry. From other Tunisian airports in the winter, getting to Djerba is long and troublesome.



Description

Bravo Djerba 4 * impresses and falls in love with tourists at first sight. The hotel is one long four-story building in the Arabic style. It is effectively reflected in the blue waters of a huge pool through which a pretty bridge is thrown. Despite the fact that Djerba is Africa, and most of the island is deserted, the entire hotel is surrounded by greenery. Mostly different palms grow here, but there are many flowers, evergreen bushes. The kids are interested in something like a small zoo located in the backyard. Here you can feed the goats, lamb, look at the hens, ducks, turkeys. There are no sports grounds on the territory, only free parking is provided, which is important for guests who decide to rent a car. Inside Bravo Djerba 4 * is decorated in a marine theme. The floors are made according to the type of ship decks, the design uses marine ropes, portholes, and other ship attributes. On the "deck" you can climb the stairs in several steps.

Infrastructure

The reception is located in the beautiful, stylish and very spacious lobby of the hotel. The front desk staff speaks Arabic, French and English. Free luggage storage is provided here, where you can leave your suitcases for a while while preparing your room. Also at the reception, tourists can buy excursions, exchange currency, order a baby nanny, rent a car. The infrastructure of the hotel is rich and shaped so as to satisfy the tastes of each guest. In the lobby there is a store with diverse products, souvenirs, profitable SIM cards, and cosmetics. For a pleasant pastime there are restaurants, bars, a snack bar, a spa, a fitness center, indoor and outdoor pools, and facilities for entertaining children. For business events, Bravo Djerba has 10 conference rooms of various sizes and capacities.

Rooms

A total of 310 beautifully decorated rooms have the Bravo Djerba infrastructure. Reviews about them are mixed.

Tourists like their spaciousness and rather interesting design in a marine theme, which gives their appearance a special charm and creates a sense of celebration, pleasant non-standard. Each room has a large balcony, on which plastic chairs with a table are freely placed. But the views from the windows are not all pleasant. They can be on the sea (surcharge is required), on the territory (garden and pool), on the road, on the desert. A small nuance - long corridors with numerous turns lead to some rooms.

The category of all Bravo Djerba 4 * rooms is one - ‚ÄúStandard‚Äù, but they can be designed for two, three or four guests. If necessary, staff install an extra bed. Equipment - furniture in the Arabic style, air conditioning (central, only works in high season), safe (paid, plus a deposit is needed), TV, pay phone. The hygiene room has a washbasin and bathtub with shower, the toilet is separated by a wall panel.

Nutrition

Tunisia hotels buy packages with Al. Bravo Djerba has two restaurants. In one there are breakfasts (beginning at 6 am), lunches and dinners. The menu is very rich, there are dairy, meat, fish, vegetable dishes, pastries, pancakes, pancakes, coffee (from the machine), tea. For lunch, there is always a choice of soups, a variety of side dishes and salads, meat, chicken, sometimes seafood, fruits, sweets, ice cream.

The second restaurant is a la carte. Free here you can eat once a week.

For snacks, tourists can visit the snack bar all day, which offers drinks, pizza, various snacks.

The hotel has three bars - in the lobby, on the beach and near the pool. It offers beer, wine, some European alcoholic drinks, cola, sprite, juice.



Service for children

Bravo Djerba 4 * kids are always welcome. For them, the rooms provide free cots, the restaurant has a children's menu. On the territory of the hotel, a playground with rides was built for children, a separate pool up to 65 cm deep. The building has a mini-club where educators work with young guests. There is also a children's animation in Bravo Djerba, which, according to tourists, is excellent. A cheerful team of animators conducts aerobics, exercises with children, teaches them dances, and arranges a mini-disco.

Leisure for adults

People of any age are interested in relaxing at the Bravo Djerba. There is a huge outdoor swimming pool, always clean and not smelling of bleach. Around him in a sufficient number of beds and umbrellas. On one side of this marvelous hydraulic structure, a bar is located right in the water. In bad weather, tourists can visit the indoor pool, in which, according to brochures, sea water and heated. For sunbathing, there is a comfortable sunbathing terrace surrounded by flower beds.



For relaxation and wellness, the hotel has a spa, where there is a massage room, hammam, sauna, beauty salon, hairdresser.

For sports enthusiasts, game rooms with billiard and tennis tables are included in the infrastructure (sports equipment is paid).

Animators entertain tourists from morning to night, organize sports games, shows, dances. Everything is fun and creative.

In the evening, the hotel hosts discos for which free admission, but drinks - for the money.

Sea

Almost every tourist goes for a beach holiday in Bravo Djerba 4 *. Djerba has a beautiful coast with white sand, blue and turquoise sea. The climate on the island can be said to be hot. In the summer here, often in the shade, the air warms up above +35 ¬∞ C, and in the winter months its temperature keeps around +20 ¬∞ C during the day. You can swim in the sea from June to November.

Bravo Djerba 4 * is located almost on the shore, you only need to go to the beach on the bridge in the pool. The coast here is not very clean, because in the bay where the hotel is located, sea currents constantly carry heaps of algae. Every morning they have to be cleaned with a tractor. Depth in the sea begins very gradually, you need to go to it about 70 meters, especially during low tides. There is a pontoon on the beach, but the depth is shallow on its edge. From entertainment here they offer a ride on camels and horses, which are always clean, well-groomed and quiet.



200 meters from this beach there is another, where the coast is much cleaner, but there are no sun loungers and umbrellas.

Additional Information

The guests of Bravo Djerba 4 *, in addition to a beach holiday, can have fun on excursions. Tourists speak well about a crocodile farm, a trip on a pirate ship, trips to Humm-Souk (the capital of Djerba), to Sousse, to the water park. Shopping on the island is very good in the village of Agir and in the city of Midoun. Its nice feature is that Tunisians do not impose their goods and remain friendly, even without making purchases from them. Desert jeep safari is always in demand among young people (a two-day tour). Everywhere, except for a jeep safari, tourists can go sightseeing or on their own by taxi. The second option is much cheaper. Taxi drivers on Djerba are very helpful and honest, they can not only deliver to the desired facility, but also wait with the meter off.
