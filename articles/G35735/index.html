How to make animals from plasticine: spend time with a child

Many parents mistakenly believe that only teachers should deal with their children - first in kindergarten, then in school. But if you want the child to really develop and not have any problems with learning in the future, you need to start at home. In many families, the only leisure time is watching TV shows or cartoons, playing games on a computer. But what about creativity? For example, modeling clay from animals or any other figures and characters is a good way to develop fine motor skills of fingers, thinking, and imagination. It is not so difficult to allocate half an hour of time to work out with the baby. Do you know how to make clay from animals?

What do you need?



First of all, prepare the workplace. Work better on the table. It is necessary to spread a large sheet of whatman paper on it (then cleaning at the very end will not be necessary). Stock up on paper towels to wipe your hands. Of the tools you need wooden toothpicks, stacks and so on. Plasticine must be soft so that the child can fashion something from it. Teach him to knead the material with his hands. How to make animals from clay, if you do not take the time to prepare? The kid needs to be taught and mixed colors, so more opportunities will open before him. From two pieces of different colors, it turns out to be easy to get a new shade.
