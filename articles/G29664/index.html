Network failure

What does it mean that the number you dialed is temporarily blocked? This phenomenon is sometimes, extremely rare, a common systemic failure. That is, the caller is mistakenly given a similar message.

That is why the first piece of advice you can give is to call back. Or, for example, first restart your phone, and then resume the attempt to communicate with the person. It is likely that the failure will be fixed. Accordingly, it will turn out without any problems to contact one or another subscriber. And the message will no longer bother. However, usually a similar phenomenon occurs quite reasonably. But for what reasons? There are several options for the development of events.

Not used for a long time

Why do they say "The number you dialed is temporarily blocked"? The next, more relevant scenario is when the person who is being called does not use the SIM card for a long time. In such situations, automatic number blocking is used. Neither call nor write SMS / MMS will work.



In this situation, we can only wait. Until the subscriber independently unblocks the number, everyone who calls him will hear no beeps in response, but a message like "the number you dialed is temporarily blocked." Therefore, you can simply somehow report a similar phenomenon to someone whose SIM card has been blocked. Maybe the person does not suspect that he has communication problems.

Balance

What's next? Perhaps the last scenario is the most common case. Instead of beeps, the caller heard "the number you dialed is temporarily blocked"? Beeline, MTS or any other operator - this is not so important. The main thing is that the reason may be the negative balance of the SIM card.

The thing is that modern operators automatically block numbers with a certain minus in the account. At such moments, a citizen can neither make or receive calls. Moreover, even exchanging messages (including receiving them) will fail.

Unlock Tips and Tricks

Someone said that he heard the words "the number you dialed is temporarily blocked" when you call? What to do in this case? It is worth paying attention to a few tips that quite often help.



The algorithm of actions may consist of the following:

  1. A person whose SIM card is locked should contact a mobile operator and find out why the interlocutors hear the message mentioned above instead of beeps.
  2. If you have not used the number for a long time, then you can write a statement of the established form for unlocking. The same thing is done if the subscriber once used the "Voluntary blocking" service. All operators have a similar opportunity.
  3. Top-up often solves all problems. In particular, if the reason lies in the negative balance of the SIM card.
  4. You can unlock the number directly by phone, during a conversation with the operator. Such a service is provided by many companies.
  5. Russian companies offer to unlock the number through the use of the "Personal Account" on the official websites of operators.
