Cardboard roll rocket

The following do-it-yourself craft for Cosmonautics Day requires painstaking work. This is a rocket. To make it, you will need a cardboard roll, a sheet of cardboard, scissors, red paint, a brush. So, take a cardboard roll from paper towels. In the upper part we make incisions, twist in the form of a cone, glue it. Now make a rocket stand. We take a square of cardboard, cut in half, cut each half, insert into each other. You will get peculiar legs, in the lower part we cut out small triangles. We cut the bottom of the rocket and insert the legs there. We will make four rolls from an additional sheet of cardboard. Next, insert them into the bottom of the rocket between the legs. It remains only to paint the product in red and affix identification marks.

Application "Rocket"

What else can be made crafts for Cosmonautics Day? With your own hands you can make the application "Rocket". You will need a sheet of black cardboard, scissors, colored paper, glue. First, we will make blanks from colored paper: a blue rectangle; a triangle of blue equal to the smaller side of the rectangle; small green square; two green triangles; red square; two red stripes; three yellow triangles. Glue a blue rectangle on the black cardboard in the middle, add a triangle on top. This will be the basis of the rocket. Now glue the green square, it will be a window, from the bottom and top glue the red stripes. We place legs on the sides. It will be green triangles. At the bottom we fasten a red square, a turbine from which sparks fly - yellow triangles. Such an application will decorate the wall or can be used as a postcard.
