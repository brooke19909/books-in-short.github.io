Thanks to this device, the driver will be able to find out the mistakes of his car, read information from sensors, check their operability. The scanner is used not only to perform diagnostics, but also to correct errors.

Performance of tasks

How to connect an adapter via OBD2 connector

This scanner is connected for automatic diagnosis of any car brand using a special OBD2 connector. It was created by a special society of transport engineers. It is used in order to receive all data from the vehicle control unit. The location of the port depends entirely on the make of the machine, the year of manufacture and its model. Most often, manufacturers place the device under the steering wheel or under the dashboard. Sometimes there is a connector in the glove compartment.
