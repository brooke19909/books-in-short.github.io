The recreation center "Golden Sands" (Novoselitskoe) - overseas holiday at home

Two years ago, in the presence of the head of the Stavropol Territory, the Golden Sands recreation and recreation complex was opened. It is located on the banks of the unique Volchye Vorota reservoir, 15 km from the village of Novoselitskoye.

Leisure facilities

The recreation center "Golden Sands" (Novoselitskoe) has a huge advantage - a place. In addition to clean, calm water, there is a high, clear sky, free, boundless steppe, the healing smell of herbs and lulling silence.





The water has a sandy beach. The complex has two swimming pools: an adult and a children's. Sports and gym facilities are open around the clock. In summer, guests will find outdoor fitness equipment, volleyball and soccer fields. As well as ATVs, catamarans, jet skis, mountain bikes and rowing boats.

The recreation center "Golden Sands" (Novoselitskoe) offers the services of a riding instructor. There is an opportunity to organize riding on a chaise, horseback riding and motorboats, fishing and hiking with Scandinavian sticks. The territory of the complex is equipped at the level of foreign health resorts. The rooms provided to guests are divided into luxury, standard and separate VIP-cottages.

A bathhouse and a billiard room are open all year. The food system is a buffet. Local products, environmentally friendly. The cuisine is European and Russian. Bread and sweet pastries are made at the bakery base from flour produced in the Stavropol Territory.





Wi-Fi is free throughout the complex.

Apartments

The recreation center "Golden Sands" (Novoselitskoe) offers vacationers several types of rooms.

  • Standard is one room with an improved layout. It is equipped with a large French bed, wardrobe, TV, fridge, split system and safe. The bathroom has all the necessary accessories.
  • Suite - two rooms. It is furnished with a large bed, a sofa and a wardrobe. A refrigerator, TV, split system and safe are also required. The bathroom has a set of necessary accessories, including slippers and a bathrobe.
  • Family cottages are separate buildings. They are located in the VIP-zone, have an individual entrance and a beach. The room has two comfortable bedrooms and a kitchen combined with a dining room. And like in other apartments: wardrobe, TV, fridge, air conditioning and safe. The bathroom has a standard set, including a hairdryer, slippers and a bathrobe.




The price of all rooms includes three meals a day and daily cleaning. Children under six eat for free. A child from six to 12 years old eats for 500 rubles, over 12 - for 1000.

In the coming year, the recreation center "Golden Sands" (Novoselitskoe) apartment prices will remain unchanged. A day, for example, in a double standard costs 3,500 rubles. This is the cost of meals for one guest and accommodation. If another person enters the room, he will only need to pay for food.

To order a reservation, you must call the telephone number indicated on the official website of the base from 8:00 to 12:00 and from 15:00 to 22:00.

Caring for the little guests

Children enjoy spending time in a contact zoo and aquacenter. It has a well-developed infrastructure that allows you to conduct classes on the water and play. There are many attractions in the park: descents, waterings, fountains, a swimming pool with a tower, a lazy river and others. The aquacenter is open year-round.

Also, the recreation center "Golden Sands" (Novoselitskoe) has a large summer play area for active children's fun. In the warm season, the complex is organized by developing thematic shifts. Particular attention is paid to children with cerebral palsy. They are invited to a free rehabilitation course, part of which is dedicated to working with horses.

Organization of holidays

Modern infrastructure, a year-round reception and a restaurant with a huge hall make it possible to conduct various events in the complex, whether it be seminars, forums, corporate parties, weddings, anniversaries, children's parties and so on.





Holiday Rules

In the Stavropol Territory (Novoselitsky District), the Golden Sands recreation center receives guests based on the rules applicable in the hotel industry, approved by the Government of the Russian Federation.

The privilege at check-in is for vacationers who have booked a room. It persists until midnight. In case of delay, guests are placed in the general queue. To confirm your reservation, you must present a unique digital code.

Registration of guests is carried out according to the civil passport of the Russian Federation, children under 14 years old - according to the birth certificate with a registration sheet.

Check-in time is 14:00, checkout time is noon.

The guest is obliged to observe the rules of the schedule established in the complex, cleanliness and preserve the property transferred to him during the rest.

We invite you to relax in the Stavropol Territory, the village of Novoselitskoye. The recreation center "Golden Sands" is waiting for you!
