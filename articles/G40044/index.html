Nha Trang in September: tourist reviews, weather, holidays

For every tourist planning his vacation in Vietnam, the weather issue is especially important. In the article we will try to answer in detail the question about what weather awaits those who are going to visit the most popular resort of this country - Nha Trang - in September.



Features of a Vietnamese resort

For those who have heard about the frequent earthquakes and tsunamis in Asia, it will be useful to find out that such natural disasters do not occur in Nha Trang. And all this because of the favorable location of the resort. Nha Trang is located in the bay, therefore it is protected from the open sea. In addition, a large number of islands near the resort contribute to a calm environment, stop strong waves and wind. The main volcanic activity occurs in the north of Vietnam, fortunately, the Nha Trang resort is far from these places. Therefore, in this tropical paradise you can feel calm and safe for at least the whole year.



The weather in Nha Trang in September is ideal for a comfortable stay

The Vietnamese beach resort Nha Trang, located on the shores of the South China Sea, in September there is excellent weather. At this time, it is hot (but not as hot as in summer) and sunny. How will the sea be seen by those who came to rest in Nha Trang in September

As already mentioned, Nha Trang is located on the white sandy beaches of the South China Sea. However, the sea can change its color depending on the time of year. And this feature is caused, of course, by weather conditions. Nha Trang in September pleases with excellent and calm weather, which is why the sea at this time of year is calm, clean, transparent and azure. So, the waves can be seen only 2-3 times a month, the water is warm. For example, those who visited the Vietnamese resort of Nha Trang in recent years in September even leave comments that they had to look for cooler currents in the sea, it was so warm. By the end of September and until February, waves begin to appear in the sea, they can pick up and bring some garbage to the shores, the water mixes with the sand, so it becomes gray. But those who like to swim in the waves will surely enjoy such a southern sea.

Russian season in a Vietnamese resort. Why locals do not rest at this time?

From October to December in Nha Trang the highest rainfall occurs, this season is called rainy. Oddly enough, it was at this time that a huge number of Russians, Slavs, appeared in the resort. But even stranger is the fact that during this period the prices for tickets and accommodation in hotels in the city increase significantly, so the Vietnamese themselves do not rest in Nha Trang these months.



To go or not to go to Vietnam in September?

It would seem that the difference is only one month, and the resort of Nha Trang can be transformed like this: waves, gray sea, and even higher prices. Therefore, for those who doubt whether it is worth going to Nha Trang in September, we will answer with confidence: worth it. According to the reviews of experienced tourists choosing this resort from year to year, September is perfect for a beach, sightseeing and nightly rest in this city.

Nha Trang: holidays in September, traveler reviews

The main thing that tourists pay attention to when they get to rest in Nha Trang in September is a beautiful beach, white sand and blue sea water, as well as delicious fresh seafood. Most hotels in Nha Trang are located in close proximity to the beach. While you are a guest of one of them, there is the opportunity to use the sun loungers of your hotel on the beach, towels that are sure to come in handy after a good swim in the South China Sea. Those who visited Nha Trang in September note that the water was perfect and the air temperature hot enough to want to plunge into the cooling water, but not too sultry to feel uncomfortable in the sea. Fresh air, vivid landscapes can dissolve all worries and fill the soul with positive emotions.

Eat Seafood, or Restaurant Reviews Nha Trang

Where can seafood be fresher than where they were caught directly ?! In Nha Trang there are good restaurants that tourists can safely choose. They have such a system: going into a restaurant, you see a lot of aquariums located in the back of the room. In each of them there is a certain type of seafood that can be extracted during one lunch or dinner. All seafood is alive and swimming in its aquariums. The client either independently chooses the type of seafood and the method of their preparation, or asks to prepare a dish at the discretion of the cook. Such fresh dishes are unlikely to leave anyone disappointed. Be sure to try seafood here!

Travel to small islands around Nha Trang, scuba diving

Each tourist can book an excursion to small islands near Nha Trang. Visits include sightseeing. Travel companies make such trips in private boats either individually or for a group of tourists. A sea trip includes diving, when you can swim next to flocks of colorful fish, getting unforgettable pleasure from it, forgetting about everything for a moment.



Conclusion
