G Smart Phones: Benefits, Model Description

Not every mobile device user is willing to fork out for an expensive iPhone. Often, consumers give preference to cheaper counterparts, which are practically not inferior to products of promoted brands according to capabilities, design, characteristics and ease of use. One of the best options, according to the price-quality indicator, is the smartphone of the Asian brand G Smart. We will talk about him in our small material.

Briefly about the manufacturer

The Taiwanese company Gigabyte was founded in 1986 by five students. Initially, the organization was exclusively engaged in the development of motherboards, ordering parts and materials from other large manufacturers, but over time it increased its momentum. A few years later, in 2005, the first communicator under the G Smart brand was released. In 2006, the company received the prestigious JIDPO (Japan Industrial Design Promotion Organization) award for the Good Design Award 2006. Young developers did not stop there and next year released the world's first communicator with a built-in analog TV tuner, which was a curiosity at that time. Since then, G Smart phones have captured the world market and to this day remain one of the leaders in sales.





The main production facilities of the company are concentrated in the central part of Taipei. Today there are several factories in China and Hong Kong. In total, the development team employs about 700 people. About 60% of them are working on creating new models of mobile gadgets.

Benefits

In our article, I would like to talk about the advantages of Gigabyte G Smart phones, consider popular models. So, the presented series of phones has the following advantages:

  • They are a budget option for a wide consumer audience.
  • They have advanced functionality along with popular brands such as HTC, Apple, Samsung, Motorola.
  • Differ in excellent quality.
  • Support 2 SIM cards.
  • They have a rich palette of color schemes.
  • They stand out for their high-quality finish and assembly of materials.
  • They have an official guarantee and support service.

Features G Smart Classic

These phones have a wide selection of models. Let's look more specifically at the G Smart Classic. This device operates on the basis of the Spreadtrum SC7731 processor with 4 cores on board and a clock frequency of 1.2 GHz, which provide the smartphone with good performance and confident stable operation for a long time on a single battery charge.





The user can simultaneously work with several applications, without delay, view individual web pages, listen to music, take photos and videos and be always in touch. Such capabilities are provided by the functioning of the productive processor.

The smartphone runs on the stable Android 5.1 Lollipop operating system. New IPS technologies enable the user to enjoy the magnificent image at any time of the day and provide a clear picture from different viewing angles.

If you become the owner of a G Smart phone, there is no longer a need to purchase other mobile devices for different tariffs, since this device has a slot for two SIM cards. Now you can always stay in touch with friends, relatives and work colleagues, and the presence of two cameras - the main and the front will allow you to make video calls and take pictures of the most important moments of your life.





Features of G Smart Roma

The G Smart Roma mobile phone is very compact and convenient, while it has the ability to work with two SIM cards at once to access GSM and 3G networks. The phone is equipped with a good 4-inch IPS screen, which makes it possible to experience a fairly high quality and image depth. Decent performance of this smartphone is provided by the Mediatek MT6572 processor with a frequency of 1.3 GHz and the current operating system Android 4.2 Jelly Bean.





On board there is 1GB of RAM, which allows you to work with all applications and programs without delay. The abundance of multimedia capabilities does not end there. The phone is equipped with two cameras: the main and front, which allows you to take not only photos in good Cossacks, but also conduct video conferences.

Finally

In general, G Smart phones are suitable for every experienced user. After all, they have all the features for accessing the Internet, working with applications, shooting photos and videos. Therefore, if you need an inexpensive and reliable assistant, then the G Smart phone will be an excellent choice or gift for family members.
