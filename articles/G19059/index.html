Step 1

Open iTunes.

Step 2

Select "Add File to Library" or "Add Folder to Library" in the "File" menu. If you focus on the first option, you can select individual films to import into your account. The second allows you to add formatted files (MOV or MPEG-4) to the playlist.

Step 3

On the computer, locate the folder in which your MOV or MP4 files are stored by clicking on the "Add File" or "Add Folder" dialog box. Select the desired movie or file, and then click the Open button. Films will be uploaded to the service library.

Step 4
