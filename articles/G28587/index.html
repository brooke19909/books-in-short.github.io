Master class: do-it-yourself paper water lily

Today we will tell you how to make a water lily out of paper with your own hands. Schemes for this will be presented below. These colors can decorate a festive feast or the interior of the home. If we make a water lily made of paper with our own hands, it will fill the house with a cheerful atmosphere. In addition, such exercises can train patience, attention, as well as develop motor skills.

Instruction manual



Next, a detailed master class will be given, upon repetition of which you will get a water lily from paper with your own hands. First, take a piece of paper. Draw a circle on it. This can be done by hand or using a compass. The diameter of the figure we need is 16 cm. In total, we make a pair of such circles on two sheets. We move on to the next step in resolving the issue of how to make a water lily out of paper with our own hands. We make a circle of smaller diameter. The diameter of this figure is 8 cm. The circle is divided by two perpendicular lines. Connect their ends with dashes. The result should be a rhombus. We divide it into a dozen parts. In the center we draw another circle of even smaller diameter. Now draw the leaves. We create lines slightly arched.

Petals



Our final goal is a paper water lily. With our own hands we repeat the above manipulations for all circles, supplying them with petals. Next, we need scissors. Cut circles and petals. Go to the next step. Bend the petals up. Take the yellow paper. From it we cut a strip with a width of 1 and a length of 20 cm. Using a ruler, mark the dashes on the strip every five millimeters. According to the resulting strokes, we cut a fringe, which in the center will decorate our water lily. Using the handle, we turn the strip in the shape of a spiral. Cut out a leaf for our water lily. The spiral is placed in the center. We complement the fringe. The scheme described in this material is not the only one, there are many of them, but a similar approach can be called convenient.

Final manipulations



A do-it-yourself water lily made of paper can be made even simpler. Take a sheet of paper in the shape of a square. We bend it diagonally. After deploying. Bend the corners of the square towards the center. The parties must match. Then we rotate the square so that the corners are perpendicular. As a result, the square turns into a rhombus. The corners are bent again to the center. Turn the craft to the wrong side. Again we bend the ends towards the center from this position. Next, we move the triangle a little to the side. We find the following similar geometric figure from the back. We bring up the given triangle. Gently bend it towards the front of our workpiece. We press on the base. We wrap it in a circular motion at the corner of the square. It is important not to damage the paper. You can bring the petal to the front side.
