Fashionable top photographer Terry Richardson, photo

Terry Richardson is a popular American photographer, the son of the famous fashion photographer Bob Richardson. As a child, he lived in Paris, New York, London and Los Angeles. Over the years, Terry has earned himself an impeccable reputation, and is currently a sought-after top photographer. In his pictures, the most famous top models, pop stars, musicians and movie stars.





Photographer Terry Richardson: his work

Richardson with his catchy appearance: a mustache, whiskers, plaid shirts and vintage glasses is an important figure in the world of fashion and show business. Among the many stars who worked with him, one can name Lady Gaga, Georgia May Jagger, Gisele Bundchen, Kate Moss, Lydia Hurst, Kate Upton, Miranda Kerr, Rihanna, Will Ferrell, Woody Allen, Jared Leto and many others, the list goes on infinity.





From the beginning of his career, he adhered to simple aesthetic principles, often using an ordinary white background and minimal props for photography. With incredible charisma, Terry Richardson has become a true legend and celebrity in the field of show business.





Facts from the biography

Terry Richardson was born on August 14, 1965 in New York in the family of photographer Bob Richardson and stylist Annie Lomax. As a child, he moved to Paris with his parents, who soon divorced. In 1971, he and his mother moved to America.





Terry spent his youth in Hollywood, Los Angeles, where he attended high school. In the early 1990s, Terry Richardson played in several punk bands, and at the age of eighteen he began to take up photography. This harmless hobby gradually grew into a real passion. Despite his shyness, a talented teenager felt that he could show his creativity through his photographs. In 2012, Terry Richardson presented to the public his first exhibition in Los Angeles, California.





Carier start

After some time, his career in this field went uphill when he moved to New York and convinced Vibe to take a series of photos with his father. Although this collaboration was unsuccessful, photographs taken by Terry Richardson marked the beginning of a grand career.





Gradually, he began to collaborate with many manufacturing companies for the largest fashion brands, as well as for the most famous designers, such as Yves Saint Laurent, Jimmy Chu and Tom Ford; with the most sought-after models and celebrities, including Lindsay Wickson, Crystal Rennes, James Franco, Karl Lagerfeld, Chloe Sevigny, Lara Stone. And even US President Barack Obama posed in front of his lens.





From a simple assistant to a professional

Terry Richardson, whose photographs appear on the covers of popular fashion magazines, began his career as a simple assistant who gradually learned to photograph his surroundings, and later people. As a result, he began working with the biggest celebrities in Hollywood. These included Leonardo DiCaprio, Lindsay Lohan, Mickey Rourke and others. Richardson also did a lot of work for such well-known brands as Hugo Boss, Gucci and Dolce and Gabbana.
