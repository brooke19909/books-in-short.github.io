Sofitel Hotel, Taba (Egypt): description, reviews, photos

Egypt is a wonderful country to relax. Here everything favors a wonderful pastime: magnificent beaches, clear sea water, excellent climatic conditions. But in order to truly succeed, you need to choose a decent place to live, one of which is the Sofitel Hotel (Taba). They know how to take care of the wonderful and comfortable rest of the guests.

Hotel location

Sofitel Taba Heights Hotel is located in the resort area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe Sinai Peninsula. This is a very picturesque place: on the one hand there is an endless shimmering expanse of the Gulf of Aqaba, on the other - the lilac haze of the mountains captivates the view. The hotel is 20 km from the tourist town of Taba and 40 km from the international airport. Sharm El Sheikh International Airport is also not very far - 230 km. You can get from it to the hotel in 4-5 hours, which largely depends on the driver and the number of passengers who are transported to hotels.



It is also worth noting that the resort area borders with Israel and Jordan. Even from the hotel you can see the Jordan River and explore the nearby territory of neighboring states.

Hotel exterior and interior

From the first glance at the Sofitel Hotel (Taba, Egypt), its guests will be fascinated by the beauty and magnificent classical Arabian architecture of the complex. It consists of three rooms that amaze with delicate pleasant colors. Numerous columns and arches give the impression of antiquity and grandeur, allow you to immerse yourself in the rich history of this country.



The interior of the Sofitel Hotel (Taba) will not disappoint tourists. Behind the open doors, guests will see the rich decoration of a rather spacious lobby. Monumental columns and a rounded high ceiling with bright stars make it a truly fabulous place. The original picture, in the form of torches, complements the overall picture, a luxurious fountain framed by greenery and a mosaic floor casting glaze.

Rooms and Accommodation

Sofitel Hotel (Taba) offers 442 rooms, each of which is maximally adapted to the comfortable stay of guests. Among them, you can choose the following types:

- Sea View - a single room with an area of ‚Äã‚Äã34 square meters. m, which overlooks the sea. You can choose a room with a double bed or 2 single beds. - Beach Front - 44 rooms with beautiful views of the sea. They are quite spacious: their area is 38 square meters. m. Each of the rooms of this type has a terrace. - Garden View - same as Sea View rooms, but they offer a view of the beautiful garden.

Do not forget about people with disabilities at the hotel. A specially equipped room is provided for them, in which everything is thought out for a comfortable stay of such people. Getting to the room is not difficult, as the hotel has wheelchair access.



All rooms have various equipment, which can be used for free (TV, air conditioning, safe, hairdryer) or for a certain price (phone). The bathroom has all the hygiene products provided for in five-star hotels. Towels and bedding are clean and not satisfactory. Linen and housekeeping are provided daily.

All the furniture in the rooms is new and very beautiful. It gives a special charm to the interior, in which the Arabic style and modern trends are harmoniously intertwined.

The room has tea facilities, and tea and coffee are always present. For a surcharge, guests can enjoy a minibar, as well as room service. Wireless internet can be used in any room.

Hotel Sofitel (Taba, Egypt) does not disappoint guests during the settlement. The reception managers are very friendly and provide guests with the keys to their rooms as quickly as possible. Baggage is delivered very quickly.

Territory and infrastructure

Undoubtedly, the Sofitel Hotel (Taba) is an excellent choice for tourists who want to see how beautiful Egypt is. Guest reviews confirm that this place is a true paradise. Beautiful stepped buildings are buried in the greenery of gardens, in which it is very pleasant for people who want to retire. On the vast territory there are many flower beds that are replete with various bright colors. The tracks near them, strewn with falling petals, look especially impressive. High palms give the hotel a special touch.

The Sofitel Hotel (Taba) is replete with many pools. One of them is filled with sea water. There is also a heated pool. Some ponds are more like decorative elements, and rarely swim in them. An especially beautiful place on the territory of the hotel is an islet located in the main pool, which can be reached by special passages. It has an original jacuzzi. Near the pools there are enough deck chairs with umbrellas. There are also shops where you can just relax and enjoy the picturesque area.

The whole area is very clean and well maintained. The staff is very thoroughly cleaned daily.



The golf course is the real pride of Sofitel Hotel (Taba). Reviews about him are only positive. Here, many guests not only tried themselves in this incredibly exciting game, but also systematically honed their skills. Here you can use the services of an instructor who will clearly explain and show how to play golf.

It‚Äôs quite exciting to spend time on the tennis court, where you can also use the services of an instructor. There is also a volleyball court on site. Guests who prefer to spend their free time actively will not be bored here.

You can improve your appearance in the beauty salon, which operates at the Sofitel Hotel (Taba). Reviews of tourists talk about the variety of procedures that the salon offers. But many did not like their high cost. The services of a massage therapist are not cheap, but they are worth it.

For business people, the hotel has the opportunity to take advantage of modern, well-equipped conference rooms. Here you can conduct negotiations, various conferences, sign contracts. The capacity of the halls is from 30 to 200 people.

Dining at the hotel

Alladin Restaurant provides food at the Sofitel Hotel (Taba). The description of this place in reviews of tourists is filled with enthusiasm. The restaurant is quite spacious, very cozy. Like all the rooms in this hotel, it is decorated in Arabic style. The original columns and the magnificent ceiling with stunning finishes look very beautiful. There are always plenty of tables here, and all guests can quickly find their place. Many people prefer to take a seat on the terrace adjacent to the restaurant, which offers beautiful views of the sea.

Meals are provided through the all-inclusive system, which is valid from 7 a.m. until 10.30 p.m. The restaurant serves quite a lot of dishes, which are very diverse. Guests at this place will never be hungry. Served here are a lot of meat and fish dishes, various salads. Also, guests do not complain about the shortage of fruits that are always present here.



Local drinks are served in the restaurant, as in the bars, for free. Guests can enjoy a variety of juices or indulge in drinks for adults. If you want to quench your thirst with imported drink, then this can only be done at an additional cost.

Good snacks and drinks are offered at the Taba Heights Bar, located by the main pool. Here you can enjoy burgers or other snacks, taste branded flat cakes with filling. The bar makes delicious fruit shakes.

Guests can enjoy dishes of various cuisines at the a la carte restaurants located on the territory of Sofitel Hotel (Taba). Reviews about them are often positive. The guests are particularly popular with dishes of Japanese and Chinese cuisine. But you can only visit such restaurants by appointment. Moreover, booking a table gives you the right to a small discount.

Hotel beach

Tourists who prefer swimming in clear sea water can safely book tours to the Sofitel Hotel (Taba, Egypt). The beach belonging to this hotel is very large and well equipped. It has many sun loungers under umbrellas, which can be covered from the wind with portable fences. Towels are always enough for everyone.



The beach is mostly sandy, like the bottom of the sea, so here you can wander barefoot. But there are places strewn with pebbles. Walking on them is better in special shoes. The sea near the beach is not very deep. For the convenience of guests who want to swim and dive, a pontoon has been built along which you can go to the coral reef and swim at normal depths. It has a special place where you can wear a mask and fins.

The pontoon offers a beautiful view of the seabed. Very often you can see various fish from it. Many guests managed to admire the stingrays and octopuses, and some even saw sea turtles.

Animation and performance

Animation has proven itself quite well, which entertains both adults and young guests of Sofitel Hotel (Taba). Guest reviews about the performances are overwhelmingly positive. The animators' program is quite diverse, the performances continue from morning to evening.

After sunset, the hotel hosts various entertainment programs. Here you can watch enchanting shows, enjoy the belly dance or participate in karaoke. Fans of noisy parties can visit a disco, the entrance to which will be free.

Entertainment

Guests at the Sofitel Hotel (Taba) will not be bored. It offers a lot of entertainment that will make the pastime of each guest as saturated and entertaining as possible. The hotel offers a variety of sports for fans of outdoor activities. Here you can play volleyball, golf, table tennis. For an additional fee, you can play billiards and play tennis. The hotel also has experienced masters of gymnastics and dance, who conduct classes with guests.



Water lovers can go diving. In the absence of skills in this sport, you can enroll in a diving school. But you have to pay for it separately. Also for a certain price you can go surfing, windsurfing, canoeing.

Those who want to get in perfect physical shape can visit the gym, which has a large selection of different simulators.

Children's leisure

Young guests pay a lot of attention to the Sofitel Hotel (Taba). Feedback from grateful parents confirms the excellent attitude of the staff towards the children. For children, the hotel provides cots if necessary. The restaurant has special chairs for them.

The hotel has a playground where children can have fun, having fun with various games. There are also special pools for them. Young guests can visit the mini club, which is open for children from 4 to 12 years. If parents want to leave, they can use the services of a nanny for a certain cost.

Nearby activities and excursions

Many tourists, because of the opportunity to visit Israel and Jordan, choose the Sofitel Hotel (Taba). Reviews of these beautiful places will not leave anyone indifferent. Guests are invited to visit the Jordan River as part of the excursion, where Jesus Christ was baptized in the distant past, to visit the Mount of Temptation, to visit the Gethsemane Church. Also, guests can enjoy the beauties of the wonderful Jordanian city of Petra or plunge into the enchanting world of Cairo.

The hotel provides an opportunity to visit the monastery of St. Catherine, located on the beautiful mountain of Moses.

As for water excursions, there also will not have to look for a long time for a suitable option. Guests can book reef visits. During swimming, it is possible to observe many species of marine life, go fishing or scuba diving. Fans of luxurious relaxation should pay attention to VIP cruises, which are carried out on a first-class comfortable yacht. During this excursion, guests can swim, enjoy a variety of delicious snacks and drinks. You can enjoy fish and corals from a submarine, which also hosts excursions.

Closer to get acquainted with the beauty of the desert will allow motorcycle safaris. During this excursion, guests can ride an ATV on sand dunes themselves. As part of such an excursion, hotel guests visit the Bedouin village, where they are given a wonderful tea prepared according to a special recipe. Guests of Sofitel Hotel can visit the Abu Galum Nature Reserve and get to know the local flora and fauna more closely.
