Mobile guide: how to connect and how to disable roaming on a megaphone

Man is inclined to change places. And even if his work is not connected with frequent trips, then during the holidays many go on vacation to the sea coast or take sightseeing tours. In any case, you need to take care not to be left without communication away from home. To do this, you need to clarify in advance how to connect roaming on a megaphone.



To do this, you can contact your operator for support or a service office for help. Experienced consultants will certainly tell you in detail about the features of the service in Russia and abroad. But they go on vacation not only abroad. Many people prefer to relax in Russia. For such clients there is intranet and national roaming on Megaphone. Since the company provides services throughout Russia, subscribers usually use the internal network. If the Megafon network is not available for some reason, then the device automatically finds the network of another operator, and then it will already be national roaming. Unfortunately, the prices for the services of the latter are much higher.

Therefore, some subscribers prefer to stay for a short time without communication, but not to overpay. In this case, they are interested in how to disable roaming on a megaphone.
