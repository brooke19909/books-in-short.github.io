Checkin Pineda 3 * (ex. Koppers), Spain, Costa del Maresme: photos and reviews of tourists

Spain is one of the most popular European seaside resort destinations. In our article we want to talk about one of the budget hotels of the Costa del Maresme called Checkin Pineda 3 *. For tourists of considerable interest is the resort itself, which is famous for its pebbly beaches and a quiet calm atmosphere.

A bit about the hotel

Checkin Pineda 3 * is located in the heart of the resort of Pineda de Mar. The nearest Girona Airport is only 32 kilometers away, while Barcelona Airport is 72 kilometers away. The capital can be reached in less than an hour.





Checkin Pineda 3 * was built in 1977, renovated in 2014. The hotel is on the second coastline, an eight-minute walk from the beach.

Number of rooms

Checkin Pineda 3 * has a total of 358 rooms, which are divided into the following categories:

  1. Quadruple Room.
  2. Superior triple apartment.
  3. Superior double room (equipped with a double bed).
  4. Single Room.
  5. Triple room.
  6. Standard apartments.
  7. Superior Quadruple.




All rooms are decorated in a classic style and equipped with air conditioning, a balcony, central heating system, telephone, mini fridge, safe, bathroom, desk.

Dining at the hotel

Checkin Pineda 3 * (Spain) offers its guests a buffet. It is possible to design half board or full board, as well as All Inclusive. As a rule, hotel guests choose half board, as many tend to see local attractions. In general, the food is very good, but not varied. Very few fruit tables, usually two kinds.





Hotel infrastructure

Checkin Pineda 3 * (Spain) has a good infrastructure, thanks to which the hotel has everything you need for a comfortable stay. The hotel has a 24-hour front desk, a left-luggage office, a rental point for bicycles and cars, as well as a laundry and a shop.

Sports and Entertainment

Hotel Checkin Pineda 3 * has a swimming pool with a furnished relaxation area. There are two lounge bars with sofas on site.

For kids there is a playground. Guests of the hotel can play billiards and use the free Wi-Fi in the lobby. During the high season, tourists are offered free bicycles and kayaks (on bail), and an inflatable fortress is installed for children.





Beach

Guests of the Checkin Pineda (ex. Koppers) 3 * hotel can access the municipal beach, located near the complex. The road to the sea is no more than eight minutes. The beach is clean, with excellent entry into the sea.

A bit about the resort

As we already mentioned, Hotel Checkin Pineda 3 * (Pineda de Mar) is located in the resort town on the famous Costa del Maresme, between Girona and Barcelona. The region is known for miles of beaches with fine sand and the beautiful Mediterranean Sea.

Pineda de Mar was once a very small fishing village. In the mid-twentieth century, the wealthy residents of Barcelona began building up the local coast with private mansions, the very first hotels for wealthy vacationers began to appear. By the end of the century, tourism completely covered the entire coast. Gradually, a huge number of hotels and hotels of various levels were built. Currently, the resort can be visited by people of various incomes.

How to get to the resort?

Getting to Checkin Pineda 3 * (Costa del Maresme) is easy. Direct flights from Moscow to Barcelona are carried out regularly throughout the year. Pineda de Mar is just 57 kilometers from Barcelona. You can get there by train (they go every 20 minutes) or by car. By train, the journey takes about an hour. You can also rent a car or take a taxi (excellent quality track, but paid).





Local beaches

The resort's climate is quite typical for the Catalan coast of the Mediterranean Sea: summers are quite hot here, and the average temperature is more than +30. In winter, the temperature does not drop below +5 degrees. Snow and hail in this region is a fantastic phenomenon. But it rains in the fall quite regularly.

The resort is famous for its coast. Pineda de Mar beach stretches for almost three kilometers, and in width it reaches 40 meters. Here you can see a small silvery sand and a larger yellow-golden. Although the beach is quite long, it is very well equipped. It operates rental boats, deck chairs, umbrellas, catamarans.

Pineda de Mar beach annually receives a blue flag from UNESCO. Rescue workers are on duty around the clock, and special descents are equipped for the disabled. It is thanks to the landscaping that the coast is so highly rated among European beaches.





Local Attractions

Pineda de Mar is divided into two parts: historical (old) and tourist (new). The ancient district is located around the church of St. Mary, which was built in 1079. The facade of the building is made in the Baroque style and looks very unusual. A dumb witness to past years is the Can Can Jally Palace, which was built in the 15th century. The city also has archaeological artifacts. One of them is the watchtower of Sant Jaume, the Devil's Bridge, the ruins of the ancient Roman aqueduct. Montpalau Castle, which is partially preserved in the city, is no less interesting to tourists.

Local funds hold one of the best graphic collections in the world. The local library holds many events and exhibitions on the schedule throughout the year.

Checkin Pineda 3 *: reviews

Speaking about the hotel, I certainly want to pay attention to the reviews of vacationers who have recently visited it. Checkin Pineda (Pineda de Mar) 3 * is a budget hotel on the coast of the famous resort.

The complex has a good location, being a five to seven minute walk from the beach and the railway station. According to vacationers, the hotel has completely different rooms. If you are not lucky and you were placed in apartments that you do not like, then at the reception you can always solve this issue. Most of the rooms are spacious and decorated in a classic style. Furniture and plumbing are good and in good condition. Quality cleaning is done daily. Linens and towels are not washed and regularly change according to the schedule. The apartments have a TV, but, unfortunately, there are no Russian channels in the list of programs.

As for the service staff, it is worthy of all praise. The maids are always friendly and smiling, doing their job perfectly. Reception workers are also very helpful in the settlement and are ready to help in the future if you need anything.





Unfortunately, the hotel does not have a left-luggage office. And she is often needed: if you, for example, are going to leave later than the appointed time, then the bags can be left near the reception. During check-in, duty officers settle very quickly, even if you arrive late at night.

Checkin Pineda 3 * (Pineda de Mar) offers its guests excellent food. Local chefs cook tasty and satisfying, it is noticeable that they are trying hard. Of course, there is no restaurant variety of dishes on the menu, but it is difficult to set such requirements for a three-star hotel. Guests of the complex have the right to choose an acceptable food option: half board or full board.

There is also the opportunity to take advantage of All Inclusive. However, experienced tourists are not advised to purchase such tours, because there is no that familiar understanding of "all inclusive", which we are used to in Turkey and Egypt. Anyway, certain restrictions will be imposed. For example, bartenders pour drinks very reluctantly. Therefore, in this hotel it makes no sense to pay "all inclusive". For those vacationers who love to travel, half board is an ideal option.

The beach is not far from the hotel, to reach it, it will take no more than seven minutes. The local coast is very clean and well equipped. On the way to the beach there are many cafes, restaurants and shops. Near the hotel there is an excellent supermarket where you can buy groceries. In general, the city is full of interesting places to wander around.





The hotel has a children's and adult pool with a well-equipped relaxation area. There is a mini club and a playground for children. There is very little animation in the daytime, but in the evenings there are daily entertaining shows and shows. The territory of the complex is clean and well maintained. The hotel itself is not new, but it is clear that they are trying to maintain it in a normal, well-groomed condition.

According to tourists, the hotel is sometimes very noisy in the evenings due to music coming from the street. Yes, and in the rooms themselves the insulation is very poor, the walls between the rooms are very thin, sounds are heard in the corridor and the neighboring apartments. The most noisy rooms are those that overlook the pool. This is not surprising, since adults and children squeak during the day, and entertainment programs take place in the evenings. But in such rooms a beautiful view and large terraces, all this compensates for certain disadvantages.

Most of the guests are Europeans (French, Germans, English). There are very few of our compatriots. Sometimes large groups of children or people with disabilities come to the hotel.

Experienced tourists are certainly advised to diversify a beach holiday and devote part of their time to trips to neighboring cities. A must-see place should be Barcelona. This stunning city with incredible fountains is worthy of attention.





Instead of an afterword

Checkin Pineda 3 * (Spain, Costa del Maresme) is an excellent budget hotel for those tourists who want to relax on the sea and see the sights, and at the same time are limited in means. The complex has several advantages and can be a great place for a good vacation. Even if the hotel has some shortcomings, then they can be safely attributed to the institution level. In general, positive guest reviews give reason to recommend this hotel as a place of rest. According to tourists, the complex is fully consistent with the price - quality ratio. If you do not make excessive demands and understand the shortcomings with understanding, then relaxing in an economy-class hotel can be quite acceptable.
