Haier LE32K5000T LCD TV: reviews, specifications and features

An excellent combination of cost and technical characteristics compares favorably with the background of similar entry-level devices Haier LE32K5000T TV . Owner reviews highlight its excellent functionality. On the basis of such a device, you can easily organize a home multimedia entertainment center.





TV niche

The entry-level niche is occupied by HD-enabled TV devices. They have modest specifications and minimal cost. The main disadvantage of such solutions is the low quality of the output image. It is to this group of TV solutions that the Haier LE32K5000T TV belongs. Owner reviews prove this once again.

Mid-range devices include FullHD-resolution televisions with similar technical parameters. The key difference in this case is the higher quality of the displayed image. Another argument is the higher cost of products that belong to this market segment. In the case of premium solutions, the format of the output image corresponds to 2K or even 4K. Significantly improves the characteristics of the TV, but its cost increases.

Equipment

On the end-user side, the Haier LE32K5000T is well-equipped. Customer reviews in this case only positively characterize the solution in question. The manufacturer included the following in the delivery list:

  • TV with two coasters.
  • Power cord for powering the TV device.
  • The control panel and, of course, a set of batteries for it.
  • Quick Start Guide.
  • Warranty card.

To use the TV, this is enough. Well, in all other cases (for example, outputting an image from a satellite tuner or a picture from a PC), you will have to additionally purchase communication wires.





Specifications

From a technical point of view, there is no problem with equipping a Haier LE32K5000T TV. Reviews highlight the excellent set of tuners, which includes one analog, two digital and one cable. In this case, viewing of television channels of both meter and decimeter ranges is possible. You can watch broadcasts in enhanced digital quality using the built-in tuners of DVB-T and DVB-T2 standards. It is also necessary to note the presence of a CAM slot in which you can install a decoding board for encrypted channels. You can watch programs from the cable operator. Another important feature of this is the availability of support for the popular Smart-TV option.

Connection Methods

There are no flaws in the LE32K5000T communication list. It supports all possible ways of receiving and sending information, both wired and wireless.

Development engineers included the following ports and transmitters on this list:

  • 1 antenna input for connecting an external antenna.
  • 3 wired HDMI ports for switching with various modern receivers.
  • 2 USB ports for installing flash drives.
  • 1 RJ-45 socket for wired connection to a computer network.
  • The component connector is designed to connect to TV outdated models of satellite tuners. For the same purpose, there is a SCART connector.
  • The list of wireless interfaces includes only a Wi-Fi transmitter that can be used to connect to a wireless segment of a computer network.




Screen

A reasonably good matrix of a budget level is equipped with a Haier LE32K5000T LCD TV. Reviews and opinions really emphasize that the image quality in this case is excellent. The matrix of this device is made using TN + Film technology, has a diagonal of 81 cm (32 inches). Its main disadvantage is a reduced viewing angle, which is equal to 160 degrees. The resolution of the output image is 1366 X 768. That is, the picture corresponds to HD-quality (720p). The brightness of the matrix in this case is 220 cd / m2, and this value is quite sufficient for watching various television programs and films. The contrast was 3,000,000: 1, and the response time was 6.5 seconds. The last value indicates that even when viewing the most dynamic scenes, there should not be any problems with image quality.

Acoustic subsystem

The sound in this TV is output in stereo. As a result, the number of speakers in this TV solution is 2. Each of them has a power of 16 watts, and this is quite enough for comfortable viewing of TV shows and not only. If there is a need to connect an external sound system, then there is everything necessary for this: a set of 3.5 mm audio ports will solve this problem without any problems.





Cost

Today, for 10,000-11,000 rubles, you can buy a LED Haier LE32K5000T TV. The reviews, in turn, indicate that this is a reasonable price for such a TV solution. Another plus is the equipment, which allows immediately after the acquisition to begin to use the intended purpose of the television device in question.

Connection and setup

The procedure for setting up this TV for its intended purpose consists of the following steps:

  1. We extract all the contents from the box.
  2. We install the TV device on 2 supports and fix it.
  3. We connect the corresponding cable to the antenna input.
  4. Install the batteries in the control panel.
  5. Turn on the TV.
  6. We go into the menu and find the option to automatically search for available channels. We activate it.
  7. At the end we save the updated list of found channels.

After that, you can start watching television programs.

Reviews

Customer reviews of the Haier LE32K5000T suggest that this TV has excellent value for money. It has all the attributes of mid-range TV devices. The exception in this case is only the resolution of the screen matrix. And this is the only drawback of this solution.

But the pluses in this case include:

  • Availability of support for Smart-TV technology.
  • Optimum communication set.
  • Impeccable list of integrated tuners and a slot for installing a decoder.
  • Democratic value.




Summary

Just great for watching various TV shows and movies in a small room Haier LE32K5000T TV. Reviews indicate that in such conditions you can get the most out of using the device for its intended purpose. Well, a lot of additional options allow not only for its intended purpose to use it, but also turn it into a full-fledged multimedia center.
