Rest in the hotel complex "Milky Way", Novosibirsk

The Milky Way Hotel (Novosibirsk) is located in a pine massif by the Ob River. The combination of coniferous and river air creates ecological recreation with a healing effect.

Cozy rooms, a large equipped area, good service infrastructure with Internet all year round await guests by companies, families with children, young couples, personally. On guard of the peace of holidaymakers is a security post and constant external video monitoring.



Housing

The Milky Way Hotel (Novosibirsk) can simultaneously accommodate up to 70 people in 28 rooms. Most of the rooms are concentrated in the main 4-story building, the rest are scattered around the green zone in the form of wooden houses. The organization of additional seats for a fee.

According to the comfort of housing is divided into:

  • economy;
  • standard;
  • Suite
  • apartments.




Economy class

Small rooms for 2 guests can be supplemented by another 1 bed. Furnishings include comfortable furniture, TV. The bathroom is combined with a shower. The set of personal hygiene includes soap, shampoo, toothbrush, paste.

Standard class

2-bed rooms (+1 additional) are located in heated houses. The interior is made of wood. Filling - comfortable furniture, TV. Bathrooms are equipped with showers, underfloor heating. Hot water is supplied uninterrupted. Towels and a hygiene kit are prepared for each guest.

1-room Suite

2-bed rooms can be rented in a brick building or in the architectural object "Ship".

Comfortable beds, a wardrobe, a table with chairs, a refrigerator, tea-ware make up the decoration. TV in the main building is powered by a satellite dish (over 40 channels). Bathroom - with underfloor heating, shower, sanitary ware, hygiene items.



2-room Suite

Superior rooms in the main building are designed for 2 guests with the possibility of accommodation for the night 2 more. It has a living room with dining area, bedroom, balcony. The TV in each room catches over 40 channels through a satellite dish.

There are 2 bathrooms: Jacuzzi + toilet, shower + toilet, heated floor, towels, hygiene items.

The balcony offers a panorama of river expanses.

Apartment with fireplace and sauna

4 guests are waiting for a fireplace room with TV, 2 bedrooms in a natural wood interior with comfortable modern furnishings. Bath, shower, toilet in the room. Each guest is prepared a hygiene set, towels. The price includes an hour session in the sauna.

Rent for a day or more includes breakfast and lunch.

Location

It is convenient to come to the recreation center "Milky Way" (Novosibirsk) in your own car. Free parking is organized in front of the entrance, free parking under a canopy is available on site.

Location of the Milky Way hotel complex: Novosibirsk, the village of Mochishche, ul. Agafonova, 1a.

Landmarks: from the metro Zaeltsovskaya to the Rest House Mochishche, buses No. 147 and minibuses No. 347 run. Next - in a passing car.
