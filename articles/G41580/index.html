Rizzi ex The Orange Fun World Hotel - hotel in the vicinity of Kemer (Turkey)

Rizzi (ex The Orange Fun World Hotel) is a four star hotel. It is located in a resort village called Camyuva. The village is located near Kemer (at a distance of no more than ten kilometers), in a very picturesque place surrounded by beautiful mountains. To get to the nearest airport located in Antalya, you need to drive 80 km. The hotel was renamed in 2011. And under the previous name, it opened in 2002. This is one stylish five-story building with three elevators. The territory surrounding it - more than two and a half square kilometers - has a beautiful well-groomed garden. From the windows, of course, the sea is not visible. But it is very close.





Rooms

Rizzi (ex The Orange Fun World Hotel) provides vacationers with 114 rooms of a standard type. These are rooms with an area of ​​19-20 square meters, very comfortable, compact and made in a modern style. Beds can be separate or reserved for couples, depending on the type of room. There is a carpet on the floor. Each apartment has a balcony or loggia. All these rooms have amenities that are appropriate for a hotel - bathroom or shower, and also provides various cosmetic and toilet trifles, a hairdryer, a telephone (they can be used by direct dialing, but not through the reception) and satellite TV. Of course, the room has a safe, which is important for people who have arrived with cash or expensive devices, and a refrigerator with a minibar. But these are paid pleasures. In hot weather, vacationers may well appreciate the benefits of these amenities. Air conditioners in the rooms work fine, but only when the door to the balcony is closed. If necessary, a cot for a small child can be put in the room.

Nutrition

Guests at the Rizzi Hotel (ex The Orange Fun World Hotel) can enjoy the all-inclusive system. The restaurant has a summer terrace, although there is also an indoor lounge. Here, 250 people can eat at the same time - there is enough space for everyone, because the room is very spacious. Therefore, queues for food, as often happens, is practically absent here. In addition, there is a special restaurant with fifty seats, where you can order from the menu. And vacationers by the pool can enjoy snacks and drinks without interrupting the sunbathing. Free entry to the bar until ten in the evening.



Beach

Like many of the Quartet, the former The Orange Fun World Hotel has its own access to the sea and a sunbathing and swimming area. It is 150 meters from the building itself. To get there, you need to go through a shady street, where you will find charming shops with fruits, souvenirs and beach accessories. Of course, there is pebble, because in the Kemer region there are only such shores. But the pebbles are small, almost like sand. The beach and the sea are incredibly clean. And umbrellas and sunbeds give without any payment. There are a lot of different sports activities on the water, you can’t list everything. In addition, on the territory of the hotel itself there is a beautiful outdoor pool, where you do not even need to reserve places on the sunbeds. And the excellent water slides cheer up so that some do not even want to go to the beach.

Additional Information

The reception at Rizzi (ex The Orange Fun World Hotel) is open twenty-four hours a day. Hotel staff can speak English and German, also speak Russian. If you want to use the parking lot, rent a car or get access to the Internet, you will have to pay extra for these services. Also, additional money will cost the opportunity to wash and clean clothes, use a hairdresser or call a doctor. And, of course, like any self-respecting Turkish hotel, Rizzi (ex The Orange Fun World Hotel) has its own hammam, sauna and massage room. There is also a fitness room. Sports enthusiasts will be pleased with the volleyball court. And those who like board games can have fun with billiards or table tennis. There is a special club for children, where kids from four years old and teenagers to twelve will find company. You can dance in a disco club (but drinks are paid), and if you come for serious business meetings, then you have a conference room at your disposal, where up to one hundred and fifty people can gather.



Reviews

Many tourists visiting the hotel praise the food and rooms. Daily cleaning, polite service staff, beautiful figures on the bed - all this deserves a kind word. Those who visited here claim that finding a hotel was not difficult, as there are a lot of good reviews about this hotel. People liked the traditional Turkish cuisine - eggplant, olives, grilled meat, greens, rice ... The beach was given special praises - it was called one of the best in the district. Well, the animation with the evening show, as guests say, is always on top - they especially remember the fire show. Some write in reviews that everything turned out even better than they expected. It is calm and comfortable for couples with children to relax, it is convenient to make outings in the vicinity. Kemer, Phaselis, waterfalls, cable cars - everything is so close that you can simply use the shuttle bus to visit these places.
