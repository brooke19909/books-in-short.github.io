New Year's embroidery: patterns, beliefs and recommendations for needlewomen

New Year has always been one of the most fabulous periods: making wishes, fulfilling dreams, hoping for the best. But for the mood to be truly festive, you need to escape from everyday worries. This will help New Year's embroidery. The schemes in this article are simple, they take a little time. Great for beginners. By the way, embroidery can be used as a gift for loved ones or for decoration in your home.

Brief information about cross stitch

To learn how to embroider a cross, you should start with the simplest patterns. One must be prepared for perseverance, unhurriedness and have patience to bring things to the end.

It is recommended to embroider in two threads, and it is better to cut as much as required to create several adjacent crosses of the same color. In order not to get confused, you can cross out the steps taken on the diagram.





New Year's embroidery patterns usually have a medium or high level of complexity. The exception is the inscription.

What gives embroidery to the needlewoman

Every needlewoman knows how embroidery calms the nervous system. All problems recede into the background, even unfulfilled dreams do not give cause for worry, on the contrary, there is a persistent belief that everything will be fine. Therefore, usually some pictures are considered symbols of a dream come true. New Year's embroidery can also be a reason for joy. You can choose a scheme based on your preferences: Christmas tree, Santa Claus, snowflakes, funny animals or fairy tales. At the end of the work, a feeling of joy and peace may appear.

Some beliefs

There are some beliefs that before embroidering you need to make a wish. But the work itself must have a specific "magic" symbolism. In fact, such signs are a psychological attitude. The fact is that a person's nervous system returns to normal, the brain is relaxed, the body is also not in tension. As you know, all good things come when we stop caring for something intensely and bother.





Choose the patterns of New Year's embroideries you like and do the work for health and good. After all, such work will also help to tune in to this unusual holiday, which is full of miracles and hope.

What are the schemes

This article provides some patterns for New Year's embroidery, which you can take as a sample. You should only buy canvas and threads of suitable colors. It is advisable to buy a floss. By the way, the remaining threads can be applied additionally to the same scheme or already to new works. Thus, you can embroider a few simple crafts and present to loved ones.

Ways to use finished work

When the work is finished, it can be applied in different ways. It all depends on what size and shape it has. For example, if just a Christmas tree is embroidered, then it can be cut along the contour, sheathed so that the threads do not bloom, and make a pendant.





In November-December, large lots of holiday sets and Christmas cross-stitch patterns are usually delivered to hobby shops . Therefore, you should go and choose the option you like. Then, with a calm soul and in a good mood, proceed to needlework.
