The Serendipity Hotel Beach (Unawatuna, Sri Lanka): description, services, reviews

Some tour operators recommend visiting Sri Lanka in November. The last month of autumn can be a great start to the holiday season. Especially when it comes to Sri Lanka. Here at this time is the height of the tourist season. But not all of Sri Lanka's coast is suitable for relaxing in late autumn. In some areas it can rain, in others rainfall is much less common. Nevertheless, the resorts are always a huge number of tourists. Rains in the tropics tend to end quickly. They are immediately replaced by the gentle sun.

Features of rest in November





Sri Lanka in November is an ideal place for a budget holiday. At this time, the season here is just beginning, so prices have not yet managed to rise.

In some countries, November is already very cold and dank, but not in Sri Lanka. It is hot and sunny. But this is a completely different heat, not scorching and not burning. Nevertheless, the temperature can reach 30 degrees.

The hottest on the west coast. This is where many beautiful resorts are located. But in these places the heat will not cause inconvenience. Tourists are saved from it by sea breezes.

The Indian Ocean is famous for its warm waters at any time of the year and on any coast. Water temperature fluctuates within 28 degrees.

What to do in autumn in Sri Lanka





The end of autumn in Sri Lanka among experienced tourists has always been associated with beach vacations. Warm water and the gentle sun make it truly unique. On the west coast, you can not only sunbathe, but also for a long time is in the water. In November, there is practically no storm.

But if you like to jump on the waves, then it is better to go to the north-east or southern part of the coast.

Local entertainment

November is a great time for sightseeing trips. You can get acquainted with all the sights and unusual traditions of Sri Lanka. It is an island of festivals and holidays. Let them not so much in November, but nevertheless it will be possible to walk on some of them.

But on the island, the number of working souvenir shops and shops closed during fasting is significantly increasing.

In mid-autumn, all Indians celebrate the enchanting festival of Deepavali, or the Fiery Festival. This celebration is dedicated to the goddess of fortune, prosperity and fertility Lakshmi. But no matter how fun it is to walk on the streets of Sri Lanka, you will have to return to the place for the night. Naturally, it should be comfortable and cozy. A great option would be the hotel The Serendipity Hotel Beach.

Description





The hotel is located in Unawatuna - a cozy town in Sri Lanka. From all the windows of the hotel you can enjoy views of the sea. Attractions such as the Japanese Pagoda and Yatagala Temple are nearby. The surrounding area is a great place for diving.

Services





Guests at The Serendipity Hotel Beach 3 can eat at the local restaurant. You can also cook your own barbecue. For this, there are all the necessary accessories. A car can be parked for free near the hotel. Throughout the territory there is the possibility of access to the Internet. Some rooms have balconies and relaxing terraces. Each has a bathroom with a built-in shower, cable TV. Guests can enjoy their time in the lounge area. It is possible to rent a bike or car.

Number of rooms





The Serendipity Hotel Beach has several types of rooms. Both budget tourists and people with decent incomes can live here. For everyone there is an affordable option.

Budget apartments

The economy room is equipped with one double bed. There is a balcony with a view of the picturesque garden. The room has a fan, mosquito net, wardrobe, bathroom with shower, bathroom. The floor is tiled with marble. Entrance to the room is separate, internet is free.

Deluxe room

The superior double room has one large bed or two single beds. There is also a balcony, bathroom. The bed is covered by a canopy, which serves as an obstacle to mosquitoes. This type of room has a private terrace and a dining area. Additional amenities: electric kettle, linen, towels, toiletries, work area, air conditioning, TV. The apartment is on the 3rd floor.
