Seashore Pattaya Resort 3 * (Thailand / Pattaya): hotel reviews

Hotel Seashore Pattaya Resort 3 * is a rather inexpensive by today's standards resort hotel with 134 rooms in the heart of Pattaya, consisting of four 4-storey buildings, which will offer each guest cozy and comfortable rooms, impeccable service and a good mood throughout your vacation.

Upon arrival, you will need to pay a deposit by credit card or cash and an official photo ID.

Address. Location What is worth a visit?

Seashore Pattaya Resort 3 * is located on Central Pattaya Road. The beach is located just 350 meters from the hotel, but be careful, because from April to September, jellyfish appear off the coast.



Hard Rock Cafe is 800 meters away. Alcazar Cabaret is 100 meters away and there are many other bars, cubes, massage parlors and restaurants. Be sure to visit Cherry'sRestaurant, where you can sample Italian and French cuisine, and Moom Aroi Restaurant, which specializes in Thai national cuisine.

It is also worth noting the presence of a menu in Russian at almost all food outlets. Nearby (somewhere in one kilometer) is the Central Festival Pattaya Beach shopping center, where you can buy branded clothes and souvenirs.

In 15 minutes you can reach the famous Walking Street with its nightclubs and other entertainment venues. We also recommend visiting the Pattaya Water Park and the Artin Paradise Museum, located 10 minutes from the hotel. By visiting it, you will plunge into the world of contemporary art and get a lot of vivid photographs.

It is recommended that you rent a vehicle for the rest, so that you can easily get to the places described above and, if you wish, just arrange a discreet trip to Pattaya. But more accurately, Thais on the street - this is a great demonstration of Brownian movement in real life.

Please note that the hotel building has a free parking zone.

About the hotel. Benefits

Wi-Fi is free throughout. Each air-conditioned room has a seating area with great views of the large outdoor pool with built-in fountain or the picturesque garden for walks.

You will enjoy the hotel's terrace, as well as many entertainment programs and a children's pool. Seashore Pattaya Resort 3 * also provides such amenities in rooms as air conditioning, hairdryer, TV with 4 Russian channels, a safe where you can put money and documents, an electric kettle, a coffee maker and a refrigerator, so that you do not have to go down to the reception to have tea or coffee (free bottled water is also included). Believe me, you will not encounter the problem that you have nowhere to clean, for example, recently bought yogurt.



Do not forget about dry cleaning and laundry services. Rooms are cleaned daily. Bathrooms, of course, include towels and all the necessary toiletries and cosmetics. There is also a balcony where you can go out and enjoy the relaxing atmosphere of Seashore Pattaya Resort 3 *. You can always book a massage or play table tennis or billiards.

The restaurant and cafe at the hotel are open daily from 06:00 to 01:00. Here, in an informal setting, guests will be offered an abundance of Thai and international dishes, breakfast, lunch and dinner are provided, if desired with room service. As mentioned earlier, to other food outlets and shops from the resort hotel Seashore Pattaya Resort 3 * is only five minutes walk.
