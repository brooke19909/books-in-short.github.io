Best hotels in Side, Turkey: reviews and photos of tourists

Side is a popular Turkish resort. It is located on the Mediterranean coast. Its closest neighbors are bustling Antalya and provincial Alanya. Traditionally, the first line belongs to the premium Side hotels.

Inexpensive options are concentrated closer to the center of the village. They are a fifteen minute walk from the coast. The lion's share of hotel complexes is focused on beach vacations. They offer a variety of animation, theme and sightseeing programs.

Sanis Kumkoy Beach Resort Hotel & Spa

This luxury hotel is located four minutes from the well-maintained sandy beach. It has its own recreational area on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea. The complex has outdoor swimming pools equipped with heated water.

Aerariums operate, umbrellas and deck chairs are arranged. According to the Russians, Sanis Kumkoy Beach Resort is the best hotel in Side that offers all-inclusive accommodation.

Optimal choice

A night for two travelers in this hotel will cost about 6500 rubles. The price includes a variety of meals in the restaurant and bars, car parking, room service, daily cleaning and towel change, the use of sports and entertainment infrastructure of the complex.

The business card of Sanis Kumkoy Beach Resort is impeccable service and genuine care for young travelers. Most Russians recommend this hotel in Side for families with children. For kids, day clubs work. Possibility of calling a nanny is provided. The animation team speaks English.

“Villa Side Residence” (Hotel Villa Side Residence)

The hotel's residential buildings rise directly on the Mediterranean coast. To the beach at hand. Own recreation area is equipped with changing cabins, deck chairs, awnings. There is a bar on the beach serving lemonades and juices, soft drinks and cocktails.

Excellent service and affordable cost are the indisputable advantages of the complex. Over the years, he has been at the top of various ratings for hotels in Side (Turkey). The Russians put him a solid five.

One night at Villa Side Residence Hotel costs 5,700 rubles. This amount includes buffet meals and 24-hour room service. At the disposal of guests are swimming pools, sports fields, a spa center. Tourists are accommodated in standard rooms with air conditioning and private bathrooms, in family apartments, superior suites.

Independent opinion

In their reviews of hotels in Side, travelers were delighted with the spacious and bright rooms. They like the food served in the restaurant as part of the buffet. Tourists noted a convenient schedule of water slides. Of the shortcomings in hotel accommodation, Russians highlight the lack of staff attention. Since the hotel is focused on the placement of Germans, Europeans are served first of all.

Royal Dragon Hotel

This hotel welcomes travelers year-round. Service is implemented in the all-inclusive system. The price includes a flight, group transfer, accommodation in a room of the selected category, medical insurance, meals and an entertainment program. According to the majority, Royal Dragon is one of the best 5 * Side hotels in Turkey.

The infrastructure of the complex is represented by tennis courts, equipped with night lighting, outdoor pools with heated water, a tropical garden and bowling. All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, flat-screen TVs, which are connected to Russian-language channels.

Bathrooms include amenities, hair dryers, bath towels and bathrobes. Bed linen should be replaced once every three days. Rooms are cleaned regularly. There are no complaints about its quality.

Alternative look

Reviews of Side hotels in Turkey claim that you must pay extra for choosing a room with scenic views. The hotel has no secure car parking. Cars have to be left in the municipal parking lot. In some standard rooms, there were no announced LCD TVs. There was an outdated video equipment produced in the early 2000s.

Residential buildings require repair. The hotel was commissioned in 2008. Air conditioners need maintenance and total cleaning. Not all staff speak English.

Lotus (Lotus Hotel)

This is one of hundreds of small hotels in Side. "All inclusive" is not implemented in it. The complex occupies a niche of inexpensive hotels and offers a minimum range of services. The cost of living is only 1700 rubles per night. Meeting with subsequent escort from the airport is paid. It must be agreed upon in advance. Estimated fare - 3000 rubles.

The nearest beach is nine minutes walk from the Lotus. In high season, vacationers are delivered to the coast for free. The bus runs on a schedule several times a day. All rooms are air-conditioned. The remote control must be received independently from the reception staff.

Key points

The low cost of living more than compensates for the remoteness of the hotel from the first line in Side. Among the advantages of the hotel, Russians distinguish the following points:

  • availability of free guarded car parking;
  • good view from the windows;
  • outdoor pool with clean water;
  • spacious family rooms with kitchenettes;
  • wide selection of rooms;
  • proximity to the attractions of the resort.

Near the hotel there are restaurants and inexpensive eateries. Within a radius of two kilometers there is an abundance of architectural monuments and objects demanded by tourists. This is a museum of local lore, the remains of Roman buildings, the ancient city with an amphitheater, a marina, a promenade, and ancient Greek temples.

Kamer Motel

The hotel offers affordable but comfortable rooms in the very center of the resort. The path to the beach takes no more than five minutes. It offers guests a private recreational area on the Mediterranean coast. Despite the modest size and lack of a wide range of additional services, the Russians recognize this hotel as the best in Side. He is chosen by couples, youth companies and families with small children.

Each room of the motel has comfortable and soft beds, a refrigerator, air conditioning. From the windows you can see the sea or the interior of the hotel. Outlandish tropical flowers and palm trees grow in the garden. The price of accommodation, which is 2400 rubles per night, includes a rich and tasty breakfast. Guests are offered fruit and vegetable slices, sausages, sausages, milk porridges, cheese sandwiches and pastries.

Remarks

According to the Russians, not all air conditioners work properly in all rooms. Climate systems can not cope with the load. Therefore, it is very hot during the day. Parents complain about the abundance of stones on the beach. The coastal zone is not completely covered with sand. Lack of own parking is a minus. Clients have to travel to the hotel on their own from the municipal parking lot, which is a ten minute walk.

Food lovers give low ratings to breakfast dishes. For ten days of rest, tourists want variety. Fried eggs and sausages quickly get bored. In winter, the administration saves on heating rooms. It’s cool at night. Travelers complain about the low data rate when connected to the network.

Miramare Queen Hotel

For accommodation at this hotel they ask for 8,000 rubles for a room designed for two people. Hotel service is provided by the all-inclusive program. Tourists are offered family and standard rooms.

The minimum room area is thirty square meters. One apartment can accommodate three adults plus a small child. Our compatriots recommend this hotel. People come here on the recommendation of relatives and friends.

Welcome

Guests celebrate unobtrusive and friendly service. The hotel staff speaks not only English, but also Russian. The interior of the complex pleases with well-groomed and clean. Everywhere flower beds and leafy palm trees. Arbors for rest in the shade of tropical plants are installed.

The hotel put a solid four. List of services of the Miramar Queen Hotel hotel:
