Oryol - Bryansk: train and bus timetable between cities

Trips between neighboring regions of Russia were and remain relevant. Passengers always want to know the optimal path between the basic settlements, the timetable of transport and travel time.

Distance: Oryol - Bryansk

Between the cities of Oryol and Bryansk, the distance is relatively small. If you take the length of the path in a straight line, then we are talking about the length of the path of 118 km, and you will have to drive 129 km along the highway. By car, at the usual speed of travel, the path can be overcome in two and a half hours. The bus ride, of course, will be longer.





The trip Oryol - Bryansk by bus

Between these cities both direct and transit flights run. In the direction "Oryol - Bryansk" the first bus leaves at 06:45. It will reach the final stop in 2 hours 45 minutes. The next flight leaves the Orel city bus station at 7 hours 50 minutes in the morning. The third morning bus to Bryansk leaves daily at 9:10. The first daily flight on Thursdays and Saturdays leaves Orel at 10:30, and on the remaining days 10 minutes later. Also during the day flights in the direction of "Oryol - Bryansk" are operated at 11:20 and 12:20.





Two evening flights according to the standard schedule depart without changes at 17:15 and 17:50. At night and in the afternoon there are several more passing buses that follow through Oryol and Bryansk. We are talking, for example, about the route "Voronezh - Smolensk", which leaves from the Oryol station daily at 03:46 and arrives at the Bryansk station at 6 a.m. Also, one transit bus in the above direction arrives in Bryansk at about 16:30.

What settlements are between Bryansk and Orel?

The first stop after leaving the city of Orel will be at the 17th minute of the trip. The bus "Eagle - Bryansk" at this time will pass the village of Solntsevo. 10 minutes after leaving Solntsevo, people living in the village of Ledno will be able to join the trip or, conversely, get out of transport. In the village of Naryshkino, the flight "Oryol - Bryansk" (the bus always leaves on schedule) will stop at the 35th minute of the trip. Then, after 12 minutes, the transport will pass through Selikhovo, and then stop in the village of Bunino (9 minutes after the previous stop). On the way to the final destination, the bus will make several more stops with an interval of 4-45 minutes: Muravlevo, Gorki, Khotynets, Dronova, Marinichi, Dolgiy, Karachev, Krasnye Dvoriki. Note that buses do not stop at bus stations in these villages. Passengers can get on or off the bus at public transport stops.





Electric trains in both directions

In addition to bus services, passenger trains and electric trains run between these neighboring regional centers. Every day, about 10 electric transport flights between Bryansk and Orel are carried out. The Bryansk-Oryol electric train is a convenient and fairly cheap way to get from one point to another. Passengers usually spend about 3 hours on the way. The first electric train from Bryansk leaves according to the schedule at 03:51 and arrives at the terminal station at 06:59. At 9 a.m. 9 a.m., the second train departs for the day in the direction of Orel. At 12:14 she should arrive at the terminal station. If the passenger is late for this time, he will have to wait at the station for another 3 and a half hours, until the next train to Oryol leaves at 15:55. The last train "Bryansk - Orel" leaves on the route in the evening, at 21:15.





From Oryol, the electric train departs at 04:25, 08:26, 12:55, 16:16 and 20:25.

In which towns does the train stop? The first stop on the way to Orel is the 131st kilometer platform. Also, the train passes:

- Bryansk East (10 minutes after departure);

- 126th km (at 04:06);

- Snezhetskaya (at 04:12);

- Beloberezhskaya (after 5 minutes);

- White Coast (6 minutes after the previous train stop);

- stop 109th kilometer (again, the interval between stations will be 6 minutes);

- Mylinka (9 minutes after the 109th km);

- 94th kilometer;

- Karachev (interval between stations from 7 to 10 minutes).

The Bryansk - Orel electric train passes through the following stopping points: 84th and 81st kilometers, Odrinskaya, 73rd km, Khotynets (the only parking lot more than 1 minute), 53th km, Shakhovo, Selikhovo, Naryshkino , 24th and 21st kilometers, Sakhansk, Tson.

Schedule of trains towards Orel

Talking about the Bryansk-Oryol train as a direct route is not worth it. On this route only passing trains run. In each of the trains, passengers are offered a choice of seats both in reserved seats and in compartment cars. Estimated cost of a one-way trip:

- from 822 to 970 rubles in the reserved seat;

- from 1455 to 1560 rubles in a compartment.

In the direction of Orel trains run according to the following schedule:

- the composition of "Smolensk - Anapa" leaves at 00:05 and arrives in Oryol at 02:35;

- the train "Minsk - Adler (Anapa, Mineralnye Vody)" leaves Bryansk at 01:00 and arrives at the neighboring regional center at 03:22;

- at 09:19 (arrival in Oryol 12:50) the flight "Smolensk - Adler" leaves;

- the train "Brest - Saratov" leaves on different days in different ways, the schedule and time of departure must be clarified at the railway station.





How to get from Orel to Bryansk by train?

The flight from Adler (Anapa, Mineralnye Vody) to Minsk via Oryol and Bryansk leaves at 04:27 and arrives in Bryansk at 06:32. At 06:20, an Anapa-Smolensk flight leaves Orel in the direction of Bryansk. The Adler train on the same route leaves Orel at 11 hours 35 minutes, and the Saratov-Brest flight (the train does not run every day) leaves from the station in Orel at 14 hours 22 minutes.

Different trains travel differently from one city to another (the time period varies from 2 hours 42 minutes to 3 hours 15 minutes).

Conclusion

The trip "Eagle - Bryansk" (bus and train) will bring great pleasure to tourists who have arrived in Russia. Even if you ride at night, you can still appreciate the beauty of nature. A day trip will give passengers even more pleasure.

Let everyone choose the type of transport specifically for themselves, based on financial capabilities and time of arrival at the final destination. The distance "Oryol - Bryansk" in any case is not so big, so getting from one city to another quickly is quite real.
