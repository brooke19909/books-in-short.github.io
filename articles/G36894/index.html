Is it possible to eat at the hotel at other times of the day?

The restaurant ("Park Inn", Petrozavodsk) is famous throughout the city. The sign above the Paulaner establishment is fully justified. And you can feel it right away: the restaurant hall is decorated in a classic Bavarian style. But that is not all. The restaurant has a mini-brewery. So to the original Bavarian dishes you will bring unfiltered varieties "Weissen", "Lager" or "Dunkel" of their own manufacture. The chef of the restaurant knows a lot not only in German cuisine, but also in Karelian. And in the evenings (daily from 9 p.m. until midnight), live music is played at the Pauliner and you can dance. This two-story restaurant-brewery serves a la carte guests, but at certain times it offers set breakfasts (250 rubles per person), lunches (450 rubles) and dinners (650 rubles). If you want to just have a little refreshment, then the Coffee Shop works in the hotel lobby. In addition to bar drinks and cocktails, this coffee house specializes in sushi. In the cozy Coffee Shop hall it is so pleasant to relax on soft sofas, warm yourself with tea and treat yourself to delicious desserts. In all categories of rooms above the standard, bags for self-preparation of drinks are provided, and coolers with hot and cold drinking water are on the floors.
