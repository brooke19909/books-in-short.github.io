Instructions for making skirts for the mouse Minnie Mouse

It may seem incredible to someone, but sometimes a modern girl does not have this item in her wardrobe. In this case, the skirt is easy to make with your own hands.





For sewing, a bright red or pink fabric, best with pea colors, is suitable.

  • For a skirt, a rectangle is cut with a width equal to the length of the product. The long side of the rectangle can be arbitrary. But the bigger it is, the more magnificent the skirt will be.
  • Having folded the rectangle in half across the front part inward, they cut it together, turning it into a ring.
  • One edge of this ring is cut so that the bottom of the product is not frizzy. You can sew lace or a satin ribbon frill over it with a sewing seam.
  • The other edge is also cut, but so that a drawstring for an elastic band or lace is obtained. It is worth considering that you need to make a small hole in the line so that subsequently you can stretch an elastic band or lace inside the drawstring.

Instructions for the manufacture of protruding ears

Since making a mouse costume at home can be quite simple even without special skills, just picking out things that are suitable in color from the wardrobe, you should stop at one of the important accessories without which the outfit will not be finished. These, of course, are ears! Make them easy.

  • It is enough to cut out two circles from cardboard, which will correspond in size to the size of future ears.
  • 4 parts are cut from the fabric, which are equal to cardboard patterns with allowances for seams.
  • Carefully tuck the edge of the fabric inward and fasten with a bast.
  • On cardboard circles lay the details of the fabric with the sides facing out, take it.
  • With a machine seam , ears are sewn along the edge. You can even line over cardboard templates.
  • The broom is removed.

The ears are ready. They are attached to the rim or cap made of cloth with glue or thread.

Fabric hat for mouse

The baby will look charming if she does not wear a headband with ears on her head , but a sewn hat. Such an attribute is more practical: the cap does not fly off, does not put pressure on the head. Therefore, this option is best for little girls.

The hat can be sewn without any patterns. Simply measure the volume of the head and the height of the cap. The head circumference should be divided into six and a wedge pattern should be constructed on the eye - each one from the drawing course probably remembers what the ball looks like.

When cutting, make standard increases on the sides - 0.7 cm - and from the bottom 1.5 cm. Six identical parts are cut out - wedges.

Ears can also be drawn by eye. The ears are stitched first. A cardboard is inserted into them. When stitching wedges, the ears should be sewn in. Of course, it is necessary to measure in such a way that they are arranged symmetrically.

At the bottom, so that the cap does not fall off, an ordinary linen elastic can be sewn. The edge itself must be hemmed. But you can also attach ribbons that the child will tie under the chin.
