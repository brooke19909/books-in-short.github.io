Service

It is no secret that the life of any equipment depends not only on compliance or non-compliance with the rules of the instructions, but also on the care of it. A slow cooker is no exception. Care Rules:

  1. To get rid of the unpleasant odor, before using the internal surface of the device should be treated with table vinegar and activate the "Soup / stewing" mode, placing in a slow cooker ¬Ω lemon.
  2. Professional multicookers can be washed in the dishwasher (if similar equipment is used for washing).
  3. Bowl detergents - non-abrasive substances, soft sponge.
  4. Use a damp cloth to clean the case.
  5. The removable diffuser and the condensate tank, which is located under the lid of the internal type, must be washed after each cooking.
