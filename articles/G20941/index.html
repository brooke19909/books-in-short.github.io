Making beautiful flowers from satin ribbons

If you want to bring something new and beautiful to your life, then you have come to the right place. In this article we will talk about how and why to make flowers from satin ribbons. It would seem that nothing unusual, but how easily and simply you can decorate your world with the help of satin ribbons.





Where to use?

Although it is a simple flower made of satin ribbon, but it can perfectly emphasize your evening outfit. Neat, not very lush and made in delicate shades of pink, purple, it is perfect for everyday clothes. A jacket or blouse will instantly turn into exquisite wardrobe elements. Also, this is a good option for creating hairstyles. Large flowers made of satin ribbons will look wonderful both with loose flowing curls and hair gathered in a bun. You can easily decorate your clutch or sandals, it all depends on your imagination. A good option is a set of such flowers. For example, for hair, create a large flower, a middle one for the belt, and a small one for the bracelet on the arm, which can also be made from stretch fabric and elastic, inserted into the drawstring. Several flowers will be the best gift for any fashionistas. It is with their help that you can create a unique image.





Create flowers from satin ribbons

For one of the types of large flowers, we need a wide satin ribbon of any color (5-7 cm wide). Cut the lengths of 7 centimeters of the required quantity. On the one hand we glue two corners together to make a pointed edge, and on the other hand we bend two corners outward and also glue. Such petals will need to be attached to a circle of the required diameter cut out. It is better to do in several tiers, so that the flower is more magnificent. To prevent the fabric from spreading, you can process it in places of cuts using fire from a candle or match. After the flowers from satin ribbons are ready, you can attach a beautiful button or a large bead to the middle.




Miscellaneous techniques

You can make flowers from satin ribbons in different colors, as well as using other techniques. For example, a rose or poppy from rounded petals will look very beautiful. Cut the squares from the ribbon and cut the edges, rounding them. Be sure to burn the fabric carefully only along the edges, which will make the petals more natural and alive. You can also work in the technique of bows. Cut pieces of tape several times longer than the width and glue the two free ends in the middle on the back side. We fasten several of these bows in the middle pieces, and on top we fix everything with a button. Feel free to experiment in this matter, perhaps you will come up with your own successful technique, and your flowers from satin ribbons will become the most popular among masters. In the technique with bows different colors are well combined. Each new tier, moving away from the middle, should be slightly longer than the previous one. After the glue has completely dried, the flower must be attached to the hoop or elastic band. It is best to fasten to clothes with a pin so as not to spoil the fabric.
