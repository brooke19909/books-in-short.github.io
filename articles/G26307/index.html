Virtual reality helmet for a computer: review, ranking of the best

Technology does not stand still, and virtual reality is already replacing the 3D image on the monitor screen. Here we already have completely different sensations and impressions. For the implementation of panoramic video meets a special VR-headset. Most often it is called a 3D helmet or 3D glasses.

And if five years ago, the purchase of such an innovative device flew into a pretty penny, today a pretty decent virtual reality helmet for a computer can be bought for four thousand. Naturally, more advanced devices have advanced functionality and will cost much more.

We will try to weed out the grains from the chaff and designate the best virtual reality helmets for the computer for 2019. The list included the most sensible, according to experts and users, devices. For a clearer picture, the models will be presented in the form of a rating.

Rating of the best 3D virtual reality helmets for computers:

  1. HTC Vive.
  2. Oculus Rift CV1.
  3. "HP Windows Mixed Reality Headset."
  4. Samsung Gear VR SM-R325.
  5. Zeiss VR ONE Plus.

Consider the remarkable characteristics of each participant.

HTC Vive

The virtual reality helmet for a computer from the HTC Vive series is perhaps the best that the consumer VR device segment has to offer. The high cost of the gadget, which is almost 50 thousand rubles, is determined by the presence of advanced tracking sensors in space and additional controllers.

The graphic part of the virtual reality helmet for a computer in games is also at a high level: 1200 by 1080 pixels for each eye at 90 frames per second. The device also boasts the presence of an advanced gyroscope, accelerometer and laser position sensor. This whole set with high accuracy captures the position of the head. An independent sensor is responsible for the movement of the player himself.

Additional experience with virtual reality provides a powerful front-facing camera. There is enough content for the gadget. Games and some specific applications for this gadget are compiled by Valve, plus Steam offers a wide selection of VR programs.

Model Features

To date, the 3D virtual reality helmet for a computer from NTS has the most advanced controllers and an optimal tracking system. The only negative that domestic consumers complain about is the very high price.

Advantages of the model:

  • complete freedom of movement in virtual reality with unique controllers;
  • perfectly set tracking system;
  • an abundance of software for a virtual reality helmet;
  • the computer and the platform do not matter for connecting the gadget;
  • high ergonomic performance.

No flaws were identified.

Oculus Rift CV1

Gadgets from the "Oculus" are enviable among gamers around the world. The virtual reality helmet for the computer of the Rift CV1 series is considered the best model of the brand. The device turned out really high-quality, with it you can immerse yourself in VR.

The model is equipped with an OLED screen, where each eye has 1200 by 1080 pixels with a refresh rate of 90 Hz. Pleased with the viewing angles of a virtual reality helmet for a computer - 100 degrees. The device also received advanced sensors and infrared sensors for positioning the head, which makes the VR world even more responsive.

Model Features

To connect a virtual reality helmet to a computer, it is enough to have a card with an HDMI interface. Users did not note any problems with the platforms and the "stuffing" of the PC. Naturally, such a sophisticated gadget will cost a tidy sum. On the shelves of domestic stores, a helmet can be bought in the region of 35 thousand rubles.

Advantages of the model:

  • maximum immersion in virtual reality due to advanced sensors and sensors;
  • matrix with a response of 2-3 ms;
  • multi-platform;
  • high-quality assembly;
  • comfortable to wear design;
  • attractive design;
  • impressive delivery set.

No flaws were identified.

HP Windows Mixed Reality Headset

In third place in our ranking is a model from the well-known brand HP. The design itself is made of high quality plastic, and the lenses are made of organic glass. The helmet received sensible infrared sensors, an accelerometer and a horoscope. All this allows you to accurately track the user's position in the virtual world.

The visual portion rested on the shoulders of two 2.89-inch LCD displays each. A resolution of 1440 by 1440 pixels is enough to display a picture in high detail. Helmet viewing angles in the region of 95 degrees with a screen refresh rate of 90 Hz.

Also, users were pleased with the availability of convenient branded controllers from Microsoft. They are only slightly inferior in functionality to the two previous counterparts, but users do not note a critical difference.

Model Features

As for ergonomics, then from a habit the helmet may seem heavy. 800 grams for such equipment is really a lot, but the company almost completely leveled this minus due to the soft and comfortable headband, as well as competent weight distribution. So users leave mostly positive reviews regarding the comfort of the device. Helmet is a frequent visitor in specialized stores where you can buy it in the region of 30 thousand rubles.

Advantages of the model:

  • almost complete immersion in the world of VR;
  • convenient design;
  • advanced integration in Windows 10;
  • an abundance of interfaces for peripherals;
  • high-quality assembly;
  • Warranty from the manufacturer for 18 months.

Disadvantages:

  • controllers work only on the bluetooth protocol;
  • some have problems with IPD (Interpupillary Distance).

Samsung Gear VR (SM-R325)

In the mid-price segment, this helmet has no equal. The model surpasses its opponents both in equipment and in configuration. The device received a gyroscope, proximity sensor and accelerometer. As a result, the user receives a aligned and optimized image regardless of the plane of location.

The ergonomics of the helmet did not disappoint. The wireless touch joystick is equipped with a trigger and it is a pleasure to control it. To connect mobile gadgets, micro-USB and Type-C interfaces are provided. Synchronization takes place without problems, especially with devices from Samsung.

Zeiss VR ONE Plus

The VR headset from a well-known German brand received a lot of praise from users around the world, including from Russian consumers. For a complete immersion in the virtual world, you additionally need a smartphone with a normal gyroscope and accelerometer on board. The helmet perfectly synchronizes with both the Android platform and iOS.

The device lacks functionality for optical settings, because there is no need for this. The fact is that the manufacturer equipped his helmet with proprietary aspherical lenses, so that you can do without adjusting the interpupillary distance. A range of 53-77 mm is enough for all categories of users.

There are no complaints about the ergonomic part. The device sits comfortably on your head and does not bother even after several hours of operation. The universal tray is suitable for mounting smartphones in almost any form factor.

Model Features

There are no problems with maintenance either. Removable linings made of high-quality foam are easy to clean, and if necessary are replaced with new ones. In addition, the headset allows users who wear glasses with diopters to work comfortably. The cost of a helmet in the domestic market fluctuates around 5 thousand rubles, which is quite acceptable for the average gamer.

Advantages of the model:

  • excellent viewing angles in the region of 100 degrees;
  • an abundance of interfaces for connecting third-party peripherals;
  • comfortable to wear for long periods;
  • universal tray for smartphones;
  • extremely high-quality assembly;
  • the ability to upgrade to VR ONE Connect with two-handed controllers;
  • attractive cost.

Disadvantages:

  • no joystick included;
  • no support for 6 inch screens.
