Perm, hotel "Ural": reviews, photos, address

As of 2015, in Russia there are about 10 thousand hotels. By 2018, this amount should increase by at least a third. In the first place in the number of hotels is the Central District. About 2 thousand of such objects are concentrated on its territory. In second place is the Southern Federal District. The farther from the capital, the fewer objects.

Hotel "Ural", the city of Perm - the best place to relax

The city of Perm has a sufficient number of hotels, but only a few stand out among them, which are worth paying attention to. For the comfort of guests there are several different options: Atriu, Benefit, ASobol, Ural.

One of the notable hotels in the Southern District is the Ural Hotel (Perm). The 3-star Russian hotel has established itself not only among residents of the country. Many foreign nationals often stay here. Ural Hotel (Perm) has an address accessible to everyone - Lenin Street, 58. Guests of the city can use the map for quick navigation, however, Lenin Street is one of the central in the city, it is simply impossible to find it.





Hotel Feature

The number of rooms is 405 rooms on 8 floors. Among this huge amount, you can find an option for every taste and budget. From "Standard" to "Lux" you can book a room and be sure of a comfortable stay. This is a hotel that is equipped with modern furniture and allows you to enjoy a cozy atmosphere.

Promotional hotel offers

The hotel is constantly pleased with promotional offers that allow you to save money and at the same time relax in excellent conditions. One of such promotions is a 7% discount on rooms from the reception for guests who have visited the establishment at least 6 times before.

Great promotion for the weekend. Room rates are reduced by 50% between Friday and Sunday.

If you have been to the hotel before, you can provide a guest card and still receive a 5% discount on accommodation.

If you book a room and use an Alfa Bank card at the same time, you can count on a 5% discount.

Promotions of the restaurant and sauna

Promotions apply not only to hotel accommodation. There is a good offer for those who wish to visit the sauna. You can celebrate a birthday in the sauna. You can present a subscription to her visit. At the same time, the birthday person receives a discount of 10% for 10 days before and after the date of birth.

The hotel restaurant has its own discounts. For example, visiting the institution from 12:00 to 16:00 will save 30% on the main menu. Lunch can be ordered for 350 rubles.

A gift certificate can be purchased not only in the sauna, but also in the fitness center. It will be a good gift for a person of any status and age.

There is a great offer for honeymooners. When ordering a banquet in the restaurant hall, a room is provided at a special price, as well as a large number of gifts.
