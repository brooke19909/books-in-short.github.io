The most inexpensive option would be a recreation center, but if a person came to the village on a working visit, this would not suit him. It remains to consider the cheap hotels of Krasnodar. They certainly will not leave indifferent any visiting person. This article discusses the most optimal hotels and hotels that have received many positive reviews, so that everyone can choose a suitable option for their stay.
