Globales Playa Santa Ponsa 3 * (Spain / Mallorca) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Many lovers of beach vacations, as well as kiting and windsurfing, have long preferred the island of Mallorca to the mainland of Spain . In addition to developed tourist infrastructure and luxurious beaches, you will find picturesque nature and a warm mild climate. If you want to spend an unforgettable vacation in one of the most comfortable resort towns of Mallorca, then we recommend you the Playa Santa Ponsa 3 * hotel, located in the town of Santa Ponsa. We offer today to learn more about this hotel, the conditions of accommodation and services available here, as well as the impressions of our compatriots who have had a chance to stay here.





Location

This hotel, as the name suggests, is located in Santa Ponsa. This is a small resort town located on the coast in the southwestern part of Mallorca. Not far from here and to Palma de Mallorca (capital of the island). You can get to it by bus in just 20 minutes. The hotel is located almost on the beach. So, the nearest beach can be reached in just five minutes. At the same time, on the way to the sea, you will pass along a picturesque green alley inhabited by noisy colorful parrots. Tourists planning to stay at Playa Santa Ponsa and the proximity of the airport will delight. So, the distance to the air harbor is only 30 kilometers. Therefore, after landing on the way to the hotel, it will take you about half an hour, which will be especially convenient for those tourists who travel with young children.





Playa Santa Ponsa 3 *: photo and description

This hotel belongs to the Global Hotels and Resorts, which also owns two hotel complexes in Mallorca - Pionero and Santa Ponsa Park. As for the Playa Santa Ponsa, it was built in 1968. In 2009, a major overhaul was made, so today guests can stay in comfortable, updated and equipped with all necessary hotel rooms. Housing fund Globales Playa Santa Ponsa 3 * is represented by 209 standard and family rooms, located in one four-story building. The hotel has an outdoor freshwater pool, sports fields, a sun terrace, a restaurant and a bar. Playa Santa Ponsa is great for a relaxing holiday by the sea.

Hotel Policies

In accordance with the internal rules of Playa Santa Ponsa 3 * (Mallorca, Spain), check-in for arriving travelers takes place here in the afternoon. On the day of departure, you must also leave your room before 12 noon. If you arrive at the hotel earlier than the check-out time, then if you have free apartments, they will try to populate you immediately, without condemning them to a long wait. You can pay for additional services and accommodation in cash or by credit card.





Special conditions

At the Playa Santa Ponsa 3 * hotel, they are always happy to welcome guests traveling as a family with children of any age. Moreover, for babies up to two years arenas. Please note that there is no capacity for extra beds in the hotel rooms, therefore, when booking your stay, you should immediately select the apartments with the number of beds you need. As for guests who are accustomed to go to the resort in the company of their four-legged pets, their accommodation in the hotel is not allowed.

Housing stock

The number of rooms Playa Santa Ponsa 3 * (Mallorca, Santa Ponsa) is represented by 209 rooms. All of them are located in one four-story hotel building. Guests are invited to stay in standard single, double or triple rooms, as well as family rooms. All apartments have their own bathroom with shower, hairdryer and all the necessary bath-toiletries, air conditioning, TV, telephone, balcony and safe (for a fee). On the floor - carpet. Rooms are cleaned and towels are changed every day. The maids renew their bed linen twice a week.





Dining at the hotel

When booking hotel accommodation, you have the opportunity to choose the right meal plan for you: from breakfast only to all inclusive. All meals are held in the hotel restaurant. During the day, you can also have a snack and a drink in the snack bar by the pool.

Beach vacation

All beaches in Santa Ponsa are municipal property. Therefore, the use of sun loungers, umbrellas and other equipment here will require an additional charge. The beach closest to Playa Santa Ponsa is just 200 meters from the hotel.

Cost of living

Prices for accommodation in Playa Santa Ponsa 3 * are fully consistent with the hotel category and the accommodation conditions offered here, as well as the level of service. So, a seven-day accommodation in August will cost you from 900 euros for a single room, from 1200 euros for a double room and from 1700 euros for a triple room. The indicated price includes breakfast.

Playa Santa Ponsa 3 * (Mallorca): traveler reviews from Russia

A lot of people who plan to spend their holidays abroad always try to study the comments of other tourists who have already visited this or that country and have stayed in a particular hotel. If you are one of them, we suggest that you do not waste time looking for reviews, but immediately familiarize yourself with the generalized impressions of our compatriots who stayed at the Playa Santa Ponsa hotel during their trip to Spain.





Comments of Russians about the number of rooms and the location of the hotel

Judging by the reviews of travelers, the rooms in this hotel are pleasantly decorated, quite spacious and equipped with everything necessary. If, after checking in, you do not like the apartments offered, you can always ask the administrator to offer you a different room (for example, if you did not like the view from the window or is too noisy). As a rule, such requests are quickly granted. Many tourists note the fact that bathrooms have fairly small showers. However, according to them, this is not uncommon for European hotels, and this option of a shower cabin is much better than a simple shower tray, which is quite common in hotels in Spain and neighboring countries.

The guests liked the location of Playa Santa Ponsa 3 *. Guest reviews indicate that the hotel is in a very pleasant quiet location surrounded by beautiful nature. You can reach the sea in three minutes, and along the way you pass along an alley with parrots, which causes a storm of delight not only in children but also in adults. Also in the vicinity of the hotel there are many restaurants and shops. A few dozen meters from Playa Santa Ponsa there is a bus stop with links to neighboring towns.





Reviews of our compatriots about food and hotel staff

Most tourists were quite satisfied with the quality of food at this hotel. According to them, there has always been a sufficient selection of dishes for every taste. Experienced travelers recommend taking half board. This will allow you, if you wish, to try the cuisine in various restaurants, of which there are a great many in the vicinity of Mallorca. There were tourists dissatisfied with the variety of menus in the hotel restaurant. However, their indignation was perceived by our compatriots with sarcasm, because it is unreasonable to count on a smoldering buffet with caviar and squid in a three-star economy class hotel.

As for the staff at Globales Playa Santa Ponsa 3 *, many travelers had an exceptionally positive impression of him. So, all the staff here are very friendly, pleasant, attentive, always happy to help. In addition, everyone copes with their duties perfectly. For example, there were no complaints about the regularity and quality of cleaning in the rooms. Moreover, many tourists note the fact that the maids did not even need to be reminded of the need to replenish shower gel or toilet paper supplies or replace bedding and towels. Also, our compatriots were pleased with the fact that almost all the time the hotel was a Russian-speaking employee. You could always turn to him with any questions.

Impressions of tourists about beach vacations and other entertainment

Since the vast majority of tourists come to the Spanish coast, including for beach holidays, the presence of beaches near the hotel and their quality plays a very important role. As for the Playa Santa Ponsa 3 * hotel (Palma de Mallorca), it does not have its own beach (like the vast majority of hotels in Spain). All beaches here are in municipal ownership, so you need to pay for the rental of sun loungers and parasols. However, you can always stay on a towel or rug you brought with you on a stretch of beach not occupied by sun loungers. For this, no one will demand money from you. So, the beach closest to Playa Santa Ponsa is just a three-minute walk away. It is perfect for families with children, as the entry into the water is gentle, without stones. Experienced travelers also recommend that all tourists do not dwell on one beach, but take walks along the coast and try a variety of beaches.

In addition, Playa Santa Ponsa guests can also enjoy the outdoor pool. It has a sun terrace, and there is also a bar with a large selection of drinks for every taste, ice cream and snacks. In addition, many tourists are advised to rent a car for at least a couple of days and make independent trips to places of interest on the island that interest you.

Conclusion

Well, we can say that our compatriots found the Playa Santa Ponsa 3 * hotel not only corresponding to their category and cost, but also superior to them. Therefore, if you want to have a great time on the coast of Spain and at the same time save on accommodation without compromising the level of comfort and service, then feel free to come to Playa Santa Ponsa.
