Card with an individual design Sberbank: development and production time

In recent years, there are more and more people who want to stand out from the general mass of people. This was the impetus for the development of a new sphere - the development of unique design solutions. Design development is also popular in the manufacture of plastic bank cards. Banks, focusing on the preferences of their customers, offer them the application on the cards of all kinds of images. What does Sberbank offer?

The essence of the project

A card with an individual design Sberbank allows the client to feel special. This service, called "Individual Design", allows customers to take a direct part in creating the appearance of a personalized monetary document. Sberbank also offers to consider plastic card samples with various image options (from animated to business) and choose what you like. The customer’s imagination is not limited to this - you can make a card with the image of your own authorship.





Customized Card Features

A card with an individual design by Sberbank can be valid only for a three-year period. Settlement currency may be rubles, US dollars or euros. In this case, the client can choose one of two payment systems - “MasterCard” or “Visa”. It is also possible to issue cards for children of a client over the age of 10 years with the consent of the parents. Cards have an electronic chip and are issued by the bank outside the framework of the salary project. The issuance of credit funds overdraft cannot be made. The card can be blocked in case of theft or loss. In addition, holders participate in the “Thank you” program, the essence of which is to accrue bonuses for purchases made in stores through bank transfer. Bonuses can be used to pay for subsequent purchases.





Additional features

In addition to the basic privileges, customers receive additional features:

  • Calls to the call center can be made at any time of the day, including holidays and weekends.
  • Payment of goods and services by bank transfer.
  • Additional discounts from card payment systems.
  • If you lose your plastic card abroad, you still have the opportunity to receive cash.
  • You can manage your bank account and pay for goods and services through online services.
  • There are several ways to replenish bank plastic cards.
  • An account can be linked to an electronic wallet.




Replenishment

A card with an individual design of Sberbank can be replenished in the following ways:

  • By transferring from another bank card.
  • Using payment terminals.
  • By bank wire transfer from other accounts.
  • When using VisaDirect and MasterCardMoneySend servers.
  • By personal appeal to a branch of Sberbank.




Cost

The production of plastic cards with an individual design will cost customers 500 rubles. Maintenance during the year costs 750 rubles. Sberbank charges a fee of 500 rubles for the secondary issue of the card. Replenishment and payment of service charges is carried out without commission. The limit of spending funds during the day is up to 1.5 million rubles. Cashless payments are also made without commission. However, a statement of the latest transactions on the card will cost 15 rubles. The same amount is charged upon request through another credit institution or bank.





What cannot be shown on the map

Design development is a creative process. However, there are prohibitions on the placement of images on plastic cards. It is forbidden to use:

  • Pictures and photographs are protected by copyright, including those posted on the Internet.
  • Images containing textual and digital information.
  • Materials that represent the image of coins, banknotes, medals, stamps, banknotes.
  • Images showing slogans, symbols, emblems, flags, symbols of power.
  • Photos of public people and fictional characters.
  • Images with anti-social, anti-religious or anti-cultural themes.
  • Images of pornographic content.
  • Images from films and animated films, illustrations in print media.
  • Images of advertising content.
  • Logos of famous brands.
  • Photos of people in uniforms with decals and emblems of organizations.
  • Images showing alcohol-containing drinks, tobacco and alcohol products.
  • Materials associated with gambling.
  • Materials depicting military equipment and weapons.




If the client wants to post material that is recognized as prohibited by the bank, the issuance of a plastic card is refused. This rule does not apply to those materials that are in the Sberbank gallery on its official website.

How to make a Sberbank card with an individual design

To order a designer plastic card, you must fill out the appropriate application in the approved form on the website of Sberbank. This procedure is simple and intuitive.

In the section with bank cards, you need to select "Cards with an individual design." After that, a new page opens, where the customer places an order: you must select the account currency and agree with the terms of the user agreement.

Then the site offers card design options with variations of the color scheme. A user image may also be uploaded, provided that the client has not infringed copyright on him. The picture angle can also be changed, its individual fragments selected and enlarged. The illustration is applied only on the front side of the plastic card.

The final step is to confirm the phone number and fill out the application on the proposed form with the personal data of the client. Here you also need to specify the branch of Sberbank in which the client will receive a card.

When the card with an individual design of Sberbank is ready, the client will receive a notification on his mobile phone. As you can see, everything is simple and clear.

The production of plastic cards with a unique design is a fairly popular service. It is provided by one of the largest banks in our country - Sberbank. You can order such a card without leaving your home. The registration process is quite simple and will be understandable even to a person who has little experience working on the Internet. In addition to the appearance of such cards provide customers with many additional features. Sberbank gives a unique chance to its customers to show their individuality even in the most ordinary things.
