Adler hotels by the sea: photos, prices and reviews

In 2014, the name of the Russian city of Sochi thundered around the world. After all, it was here that the Winter Olympic Games were held. However, unlike the planet‚Äôs population, the inhabitants of the former USSR are well acquainted with this city. The reason for this is its excellent geographical position, which allows you to relax here all year round. On one side, Sochi is surrounded by mountain elevations. On the other hand, the gentle waters of the Black Sea wash it. Therefore, it is not surprising that tourists who conquered the Abkhaz mountains in winter hurry to bask on the soft beaches of the resort in the summer.





Under the hot Sochi sun

Among travelers, vacation in Adler is very popular . Hotels and hotels in this district of Sochi are always happy to welcome guests who are hungry for warmth in their halls. Depending on preferences, tourists can be accommodated in upscale establishments characterized by first-class service, as well as in small pensions in the private sector. The hotels located by the sea enjoy a special privilege for travelers . Adler, whose photo is striking in its beauty, is the most popular district of Sochi, where even the stars of show business, the elite of sports and culture love to relax. That is why even the smallest hotel-type establishment here has a wide range of services. This mainly concerns hotels located near warm beaches.





Entertainment near the hotel

Adler hotels on the beach include many establishments that differ among themselves in terms of occupied territory, the categories of rooms provided, the range of services, cost of living and other factors. Many upscale hotels have their own pools and private beach. Others rent trestle beds and umbrellas. Thirdly, we are always happy to offer childcare services and games rooms. For example, the three-star hotel Aphrodite. This establishment is located near the resort town. The latter, in turn, daily entices guests with a variety of entertainments, such as a water park, dolphinarium, oceanarium, all kinds of attractions for children and their parents, a healing spring with mineral water and much more.





Private hotel "Aphrodite": room categories

Restaurants and bars, clubs and shops, cafeterias and night discos - all this also has Adler. The private hotel "Aphrodite" is a rather "young" establishment located on the picturesque Black Sea coast. The hotel offers rooms of the following categories:

  1. Standard. The room in question is designed for two people. However, for each room in the hotel there is the possibility of an extra bed. Standard apartments are equipped with a split system, telephone, refrigerator and TV. Customers have the opportunity to sit comfortably on the balcony, which offers stunning views of the Black Sea. The cost of such a room, and the rest also, varies depending on the season and varies from 2200 rubles to 5700 rubles per night.
  2. Suite Such a room has all the characteristics described above, but, in addition, the room has a reception area. The price of accommodation is 3200 rubles in the low season and 9000 rubles in the high season.
  3. The next category is studio. In this room, the bathroom is equipped with a shower. The price varies from 2600 to 9000 rubles per day.
  4. Standard room does not have a balcony, unlike all of the above. A traveler who opts for this option will have to pay from 2000 to 5500 rubles per day.

In addition, the private hotel Aphrodite offers its guests animation, car and bicycle rental services, excursions, meals, transfers, wellness treatments and much more. A cosmetologist, massage, spa and sauna are also available for guests of the establishment.





And the spark will light up

Many Adler hotels by the sea have double, triple and quadruple rooms at their disposal. For example, an institution called "Spark." This cozy hotel will delight parents with children under the age of six: these babies are accommodated for free. The maximum cost in the institution in question is 3,500 rubles in the high season for a room designed for four people. An apartment for two on average will cost 2000-3000 rubles. Wireless Internet, meals, transfers, climate control in the rooms, TV, refrigerator, parking, water activities - all this is offered by the Ogonyok Hotel.





Cozy rest in the hotel "Calm"

Many other Adler hotels have similar services. Feedback from visitors about the establishment called "Calm" allows us to hope that here guests will find excellent service at the highest level. Comfortable and cozy rooms, cleaning every day, amazing kitchen, clean pool, secure parking, free shuttle service - these are not all the services offered by the impeccable employees of the establishment. Room rates vary. So, in low season the price of accommodation varies from 1600 (standard room) to 5000 rubles (apartments). At the peak of the influx of holidaymakers, the price of a room of the first category rises by 1000 monetary units. Between the sixteenth of July and the twenty-sixth of August, the apartment will cost the visitor 9,000 rubles per day. Comprehensive nutrition for one person will cost 520 rubles per day.





Morning goodies and discounts for kids

Many Adler hotels by the sea offer their customers breakfast as a bonus. Such institutions, for example, include the Flamingo, Berth, Adelphia and others. All these hotels are happy to accommodate their guests in rooms of various categories and different prices. Each hotel has a discount system for children. For example, in an institution called Adelphia, children under seven years old can live for free. However, they are not allocated a place and breakfast. Also, for children under twelve years of age, the cost of food is halved.





Hotel "Almira": rest with comfort

Some Adler hotels by the sea have their own, albeit small, beach. This allows guests to relax without any fuss and be sure that there is always a place near the warm sea. Towels, umbrellas, a toilet and other services are provided to residents free of charge. To institutions of a similar category the Almira hotel belongs. In addition to nine categories of rooms costing from 1700 to 15 000 rubles, customers will be very pleased with their own beach complex. In addition, children under three years old can stay at the hotel for free. For older kids will have to pay 1000 Russian rubles per night.

"All inclusive"

The Finnish sauna, Turkish bath, billiards, laundry, shuttle service, excursions and many other services provide their customers not only the Almira establishment in question, but also other Adler hotels on the seashore. All Inclusive is a fairly popular service that resorts in Turkey and Egypt offer. On the territory of Russian relaxation oases, such a service is not yet very developed. However, in Adler there is a group of hotels that are pleased to offer their customers the All Inclusive service. Such establishments include Velvet Seasons, Bridge Resort, etc. These hotels are always ready to please their customers with pleasant surprises and dinners on the buffet system.
