Blue Dream Hotel 3 * in Turkey: photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

Turkey is considered one of the most visited countries by Russians. Every second resident of Russia definitely visited Turkish resorts. What is the secret of the popularity of this country? The answer is simple. The Russians are attracted by the low cost of tours, a variety of hotels and a favorable Turkish climate. But which hotel to choose in order not to miscalculate? In order to spend your vacation with dignity, you need to prepare for the trip in advance and start by examining the hotel information and reading reviews. In this article we will talk about the Blue Dream Hotel 3 * hotel chain - hotels where you can spend your vacation well and calmly, without overpaying for additional services.





Description of the Blue Dream Hotel hotel chain in Turkey

Some people think that all Turkish hotels are the same. But this opinion is erroneous, since local hotels differ not only in stardom, but also in the quality of the services provided. In addition, it all depends on the location of such institutions. The larger the resort, the more services the hotel provides. The same goes for the Blue Dream network. The number of hotel chains is small, but there are still differences in service.

In Turkey there are two such hotels - in Alanya and Bodrum. Surely, anyone has heard of the Blue Dream Hotel 3 * (Greece) - this hotel is focused on a different circle of tourists than Turkish hotels. The Greek hotel complex is more likely to meet Europeans, and the requirements for this hotel are completely different.





Blue Dream Hotel in Bodrum

The three-star hotel is designed for less demanding travelers. The hotel has an attractive view, but an unusual location becomes the decisive criterion in favor of a stop at this place - the hotel is located right on the beach.

Blue Dreams Hotel (Bodrum) consists of several small cottages, but the complex belongs to the category of medium. It has 196 rooms, which occupy two- and three-story hotel buildings. More than 2/3 of the rooms have a balcony with views of the sea landscapes and the coastline. The hotel has two types of rooms - standard and family. The set of services in the rooms is standard, in each room there is a shower / bathroom, telephone, air conditioning, minibar, refrigerator. Linen is changed several times a week, cleaning is daily.





Hotel Infrastructure

Club Blue Dream Hotel 3 * (most often the hotel is called that), despite its small stardom, has a wide infrastructure. The hotel complex has 5 bars, a restaurant and a shop. Tourists are offered dry cleaning, laundry, currency exchange, parking, car rental and a medical office. Vacationers can spend time by the pool, especially since there are 4 open and 1 indoor pond on the territory. For adult tourists there are 2 water slides where you can have fun and frolic.

Entertainment at the Bodrum Hotel

Blue Dream Hotel 3 * has a wide entertainment program for its guests. Tourists are waiting for animation, evening shows, discos and live music. Guests can book a massage directly in their room, visit the gym or sauna. In addition, you can relax and get maximum pleasure in the Turkish bath. Active hotel guests can play volleyball or tennis - there are two tennis courts and a special room for board games on site. Water sports are also held in high esteem - windsurfing, canoeing, boating, etc.

Blue Dream hotel 3 * (Alanya): description

In the center of the famous resort of Alanya, a cozy attractive Blue Dream Hotel 3 * hotel is located. Konakli (in Russian "Konakli") is located a few kilometers from the city center. Many of our compatriots value calm and silence, because this is what Blue Dream Hotel 3 * (Alanya) boasts of.





How to get there

The hotel is located in the center of Alanya, 120 km from Antalya Airport. For tourists who have arrived from far away, the road to the hotel will be somewhat tiring, as it will take about an hour and a half.

Description of rooms

The hotel has only 30 rooms of a standard type. They are equipped with everything necessary for living - a private bathroom, toilet, air conditioning, balcony, hairdryer, telephone and minibar. Due to the small number of rooms, cleaning is carried out daily, there are no "strains" with the delivery of linen or towels.

Beach description

Private beach Blue Dream Hotel 3 * (Alanya) is sandy. It is located across from the hotel building, just a few minutes walk. A free service includes the use of sun loungers and umbrellas.





Infrastructure / Entertainment

Despite the fact that the hotel is designed for a small number of guests, it has everything you need for a fun holiday - a swimming pool, gym, disco. Tourists can visit the sauna or Turkish bath, book a massage or play billiards, chess or darts. A minimum set of entertainment is enough for a comfortable pastime. You can play volleyball at the beach or enjoy water sports.

Children's holiday

In Blue Dream Hotel 3 * (Turkey) provides for families with children. Like the Bodrum Hotel, there is a children's menu, a sports field and a pool for young tourists. In addition, parents can always use the services of a nanny, who works around the clock. The small territory of the hotel allows you to monitor the location of the child, wherever he is.

A hotel in Alanya is most often chosen by those who want to live in the city center, but without overpaying. Also, this hotel will suit students who do not have the means to stay in a youth hotel, or couples who are looking for peace for their children. However, not everyone is ready to live in a local "treshka". Some complain that the hairdryer did not work during the stay, the toilet paper holder was broken , or the light was off. Someone was not satisfied with the food in the hotel, in their opinion, the dishes were from the same products, that is, they did not differ in variety. But these minuses did not upset others at all, they rejoiced at the sea, the sun, a wonderful climate and affordable prices.

There are always a lot of Russian tourists at this hotel, so there is always at least one Russian-speaking employee at the reception. "Allclusive" is usually appreciated by tourists. Comparison of the cost of tours

As you know, the cost of tours even in the same hotel chain is not the same. You can book a double room for an amount equal to one voucher. It all depends on the period and season of the trip, the number of days and the type of room (standard, family or apartment). The cost is also affected by the carrier company of your choice. Some flights are more expensive than others, so most often tourists fly on charter airplanes. Sometimes travel companies sell tours at a reduced cost even to the most luxurious hotels, offering travelers various bonuses and discounts. Some people prefer to fly only on burning packages, saving their budget. Therefore, a comparison of the prices of hotels in Bodrum and Alanya will be approximate.

To relax in the three-star Blue Dream Hotel 3 * (see photo in the article) Bodrum 7 days / 8 nights, for two adults you will have to pay 60-65 thousand rubles. Accommodation in a standard room for 11 nights will cost about 90-100 thousand rubles. As for the hotel in Alanya, the cost of the tour is much lower. For example, for 7 nights in a room with one bedroom you will pay only 16,000 rubles. Together there you can relax in the range of 35-40 thousand rubles per week.
