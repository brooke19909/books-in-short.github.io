Babaevo hotels: overview, services provided, guest reviews

There are railway cities in Russia. And there are those born by the railway that feeds them. In the west of the Vologda Oblast there is a small, but very important transport city. This is Babaevo. It is located on the banks of the Kolp River.

What to do in Babaevo?

Babaevo is a small town, but nevertheless there are quite a lot of attractions and interesting places in it. Particularly noteworthy is the pre-revolutionary railway station, next to which there is a monument dedicated to the 100th anniversary of the formation of the railway junction of the Babaevo station - an Er 771-11 series locomotive.

The station overlooks Railway Station Square with the Alley of Heroes of the Soviet Union, which houses a war memorial with eternal flame. You can get acquainted with the history of the city in the Babaevsky Museum of Local Lore named after Maria Vasilyevna Gorbunova. The city has a church of the Holy Apostles Peter and Paul. One of the attractions for many visitors is the pine forest, located in the very center of the city. And of course, after a busy program, guests are invited to stay in one of the hotels in Babaevo, Vologda Oblast. Here everyone can find a accommodation option in accordance with their preferences. The most popular Babaevo hotels will be described below.
