Regions of the Krasnodar Territory (list): characteristic features

The southernmost region of our country, having the historical name Kuban, occupies a rather large territory. The river of the same name divides its lands into two parts. In the north of the region are plains, in the west is a mountainous area. A complete list of areas of the Krasnodar Territory (some of which will be discussed in this article) includes 38 names. The centers of most areas are villages.





Seaside areas

Krasnodar Territory is a center of tourism. Vacationers begin to come here in the spring, and end in late autumn. On the Kuban land there are a large number of resorts. The quality and cost of the services provided in them can be chosen according to your desires and financial capabilities.

The list of regions of the Krasnodar Territory includes the Tuapse, Temryuk and Slavic regions of the Kuban, located on the seashore. Tuapse district is particularly popular among tourists who can not imagine their vacation without the gentle rays of the sun and warm sea. This area is located in a small area.





Temryuk district is located in the west of the Taman Peninsula. It is located on the shores of the Azov and Black Seas and especially attracts vacationers who go on vacation with kids.

Absheron district of Krasnodar Territory

Absheron district is located in the south of the Kuban. It has significant differences from other lowland territories of the region. Roads in these places lie among mountains and thickets of dense forests. The region is famous for its sights and the beauty of amazing natural corners.

The village of Mezmay, which is surrounded by high cliffs, attracts tourists with a mysterious energy. Representatives of creative intelligentsia from Russia and foreign countries like to come to it. In this village there is something to admire lovers of antiquity. In Mezmay, you can see dolmens, medieval monuments of architecture.

On the territory of the Absheron region of the Krasnodar Territory there are Azish caves, which are true "natural wonders". People suffering from allergy symptoms are well aware of the Lagonaki area. If you visit it during the flowering of ragweed, you can forget about allergies for a long time.

In the city of Khadyzhensk there are sources of mineral waters that help in the treatment of a large number of diseases. They are used not only for drinking, but also for bathing.

Mountainous areas of the Kuban

At any time of the year, tourists who do not imagine their vacation without mountains like to come to Krasnodar Krai. In winter, they ski here.

Gersenova Mountain attracts a large number of tourists to the Seversky district of the Krasnodar Territory (located on the 28th place in the list). Here, from a height of almost 60 meters, you can enjoy the amazingly beautiful panorama of the surrounding landscapes.

Otradnensky district is considered a favorite place for climbers. On its territory, there are bizarre cliffs resembling huge mushrooms, in particular, a rock called "Drum".

Agricultural areas of the region

One of the largest agricultural areas of the Kuban is considered the Red Army district. In the list of districts of the Krasnodar Territory, he ranks 15th. On this territory there are more than 100 thousand people involved in crop production and animal husbandry. Residents of the Uspensky, Krylovsky and Pavlovsky districts also work mainly in the agricultural sector.





In the areas of the Krasnodar Territory, a favorable environment for living. Due to climatic conditions and favorable geographical position, the agricultural industry is developing in them, a large number of industries operate, and minerals are extracted. The Black and Azov Seas provide opportunities to get a lot of fish. Each region of the Kuban is unique in geographical location, climatic conditions. People living on the Kuban land, glorify it far beyond the borders of Russia. Moreover, not only in words, but also in deeds.
