A mild climate, relatively constant air temperatures without sharp fluctuations, picturesque vegetation in the form of oleanders, magnolias and cypresses growing in the park - all this makes these places attractive to many visitors. The village has the necessary infrastructure for accommodation and recreation of tourists. Numerous attractions work for them, and excursions to local attractions are arranged.
