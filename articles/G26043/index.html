Dedalus Village 3 * (Crete, Greece): description, photos and reviews

If you plan to spend an unforgettable vacation on the island of Crete (Greece), Dedalus Village 3 * is a hotel that is ideal for comfortable and affordable accommodation. We invite you to get to know this hotel better by learning what is offered to guests. We will also find out the opinion of our compatriots who have already visited the hotel in question.



Location

Hotel Dedalus Village 3 * is located in one of the oldest villages on the island of Crete - Kutulufare. Hersonissos is just one and a half kilometers away. So you can even walk here on foot. The nearest airport is in Heraklion, just 23 kilometers from Koutouloufar. So upon arrival you can reach the hotel in just half an hour. There is a bus stop within walking distance of the hotel , from where you can reach other towns of Crete by public transport.

Since the Daedalus Village is located on a hill, it offers stunning views of the coast and Hersonissos itself. The beach is 800 meters from the hotel. In total, fifty meters from the hotel there are taverns, cafes and restaurants.

Housing stock

In total, the hotel has 53 rooms. They are located in the main building, as well as several bungalows and maisonettes. Dining at the hotel is not expected, but all rooms have their own kitchenette with everything you need. So if you want, you can cook your own food. If you don‚Äôt feel like spending time in the kitchen, you can always eat in one of the local taverns, which are abundant in the vicinity of Dedalus Village 3 *.

The hotel rooms have all the necessary furniture, TV, refrigerator, iron, ironing board, hair dryer. Air conditioning and a safe are available at an additional cost. The rooms have a balcony with a table, chairs and clothes dryer. Rooms are cleaned every three days, and bed linen is updated once every 5 days.



Infrastructure and entertainment

Since most tourists come to Crete in search of a beach holiday, the issue of proximity to the sea plays an important role. As for the Dedalus Village 3 * hotel, it is 800 meters from the nearest beach.

The hotel has two outdoor pools - large for adults and small for the youngest tourists. You can have a great time here if for some reason you do not want to go to the beach. You can also play billiards and ping pong at the hotel.

The reception at the hotel is open almost round the clock, so at any time you can contact the administrator with a question or request. The hotel has a tour desk where you can book an excursion that interests you. Also, by contacting the reception, you can order a transfer to the airport, taxi, rent a car and exchange currency.



Dedalus Village 3 *: tourist reviews

Since it is important for many people planning a trip to find out the opinions of travelers who have already stayed in one place or another, we will make your task a little easier and give generalized comments from our compatriots about their vacation at the Daedalus Village Hotel in Crete.

First of all, many tourists liked the almost homely atmosphere in this hotel. According to them, being here is very nice. Cozy rooms, well-groomed green spacious territory and friendly and always helpful staff reinforce this impression.

As for the rooms Dedalus Village 3 *, the reviews of travelers in their regard are more than positive. So, many tourists enthusiastically recall that the apartments have everything that may be required - from a hairdryer and iron to a kettle, openers and even graters in the kitchen. However, vacationers who have been here warn that bath accessories (soap, shampoo, shower gel , etc.) should be taken with you or bought on site, as the hotel does not provide them. Also note that you will probably have to buy drinking water at the supermarket, as the water comes from the tap with a brackish flavor.

Positive guests speak about their own hotel grounds. According to them, it is quite spacious. Here you can always find a pleasant place to relax. The guests liked the Dedalus Village 3 * and pools (both adult and children's). Tourists also note a beautiful citrus garden and the presence of free parking.

As for the beach, it is indisputable that the distance from the hotel did not please all the guests. According to them, the road to the sea does not cause inconvenience, but, returning to the hotel, you have to overcome a rather steep climb, which can be a problem for older people and parents with small children. The beach itself, according to tourists, is clean and equipped with everything necessary.
