Olenegorsk Hotels (Murmansk Oblast): addresses, description, reviews

The Murmansk region is gaining more and more popularity every year among residents of Russia, the near abroad and Asia. This is due to the increasing interest of visitors to the region to tourism. Do not forget that the Kola Peninsula has a large supply of unique resources, so business travelers can also come here. In any case, in the cities of the Murmansk region, you must first find accommodation.

Camping "Aurora Lapland"

Campers "Aurora Lapland", which is located near Olenegorsk, Murmansk and Kirovsk, in a picturesque corner of the northern tundra, are perfect for vacationers who have chosen tourism as their main goal. This authentic country-style hotel is full of comfort and warmth. And the proximity to the lake called Zayachye allows you to diversify your vacation with fishing and boating.

A continental breakfast is served every morning at the hotel bar, at an additional cost. Free private parking is available on site. Guests are welcome to rent a sauna at an additional cost. All services are paid for in cash only. In "Aurora" you can celebrate any celebrations or just spend a vacation with your family.

Hotel "Miner" in Olenegorsk

The hotel is well located in the city center. It is located near the Olenegorsk railway station. From Murmansk to the hotel "Miner" 90 kilometers, you can get on the Leningrad highway or by train.

In this place it will be convenient to accommodate people who have arrived in the city on work issues, tourists, sports teams. In the immediate vicinity are the Olenegorsk Ice Palace and the management building of Olkon OJSC.

The number of rooms of the hotel allows vacationers to choose accommodation affordable. Here you can reserve deluxe, standard or economy rooms. All rooms are equipped with a TV, fridge, kettle and wireless internet. Each room also has a private bathroom with hygiene products. On the territory of the hotel there is an opportunity to leave the car in free parking.

Hotel address: Olenegorsk, 38 Stroitelnaya Street.

Sanatorium "Olkon"

Among the hotels in Olenegorsk there is a sanatorium. It was built for the employees of the mining and processing plant in order to improve the general state of health. And since mining and production are quite harmful, the resort is very popular among residents of Olenegorsk and other nearby cities of the region. A few years ago, investment funds were allocated to update the sanatorium's room fund, the entire building and medical equipment. However, it is very difficult to rent a room for a visiting visitor, since the workers of the plant are primarily located here for the prevention of various diseases common in this working environment.

They treat diseases of the cardiovascular system, restore the system of the gastrointestinal tract, and increase the body's immunity in the harsh conditions of the north. Apartments on Pionerskaya

Cozy private apartments with one room and a balcony offer affordable accommodation. There is a separate bathroom, kitchen, dining room. Nomare are designed for three people. Aurora Lapland's

Among the hotels in Olenegorsk this mini-hotel offers the lowest price. The hotel has several rooms accommodating one or two people. There are rooms with one large bed or two twin. A continental breakfast is served daily at an extra charge. The rooms are equipped with a kettle and wardrobe. This hotel is located in Olenegorsk at Parkovaya street, house 14.

Apartments on Stroitelnaya street

One of the best apartments in Olenegorsk is located on 49 Stroitelnaya Street. Here is one of the best services among all the apartments in this city that can accommodate up to 5 guests. There is a bedroom with five beds in the form of folding sofa beds. The room also has a small flat-screen TV. The equipped kitchen is equipped with an induction stove, microwave, electric kettle, refrigerator and a set of necessary utensils, including for cooking. The bathroom, separate with a bathroom, is equipped with modern sanitary equipment of Russian and European production, as well as a comfortable and deep bath. Guests praised the condition of the apartments and the quality of service.

Apartments on Energetikov street

This apartment can accommodate up to 6 people in two separate rooms. The simplicity of the interior is offset by cleanliness and comfort, as well as the availability of everything necessary for temporary residence. For your comfort, there is free Wi-Fi, a flat-screen TV with cable channels. The apartments are ready to host including foreigners, as the staff is fluent in English. Pets are also welcome here for which there is no extra charge. Beforehand, it is necessary to inform the owners of a possible arrival with animals. You can find apartments at 8 Energetikov Street.
