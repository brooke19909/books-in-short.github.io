Krini Beach 3 * (Greece / Crete) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Travelers come here for the sake of magnificent sandy beaches, wonderful climate, an abundance of historical monuments, as well as developed tourist infrastructure. Here both family people with children and youth will find something to do. The hotel base of Crete offers accommodation for every taste and budget. Here you can enjoy both comfortable five-star and affordable economy class hotels. The latter category includes Krini Beach hotel 3 *, which we would like to get to know more closely today. Krini Beach 3 * (Crete): traveler reviews

Since many of our compatriots, preparing for a trip to a particular country, carefully study the comments of other tourists both about sights and hotels, today we decided to find out what Russians think about their stay in Creeny Beach, who happened to be here in this year. First of all, it should be said that the vast majority of travelers were quite satisfied with their choice of hotel. But we offer to understand everything in more detail.

So, the first thing that tourists pay attention to when checking into a hotel is, of course, the condition of the rooms. Krini Beach 3 * (Greece) is no exception. Most of the guests were satisfied with the condition of their rooms and the work of the equipment. So, despite the fact that the hotel has only three stars, it is regularly renovated, the furniture is not new, but it is in good condition. Refrigerator, TV and air conditioning, plumbing work without interruption. Our compatriot also liked how regular cleaning is carried out here. As a result, the rooms are always clean and tidy. The only thing some tourists were a little unhappy with was the noise in the rooms due to their location next to the restaurant. Also, the guests complained about the noise, whose windows overlooked the road. However, all these problems could easily be fixed by closing the balcony door tightly.

Another very important issue for many tourists is the food in the hotel. It should be noted that the food in Krini Beach 3 * (Crete) seemed quite satisfactory to the vast majority of guests. Of course, the menu here is not as diverse as in four- or five-star hotels, but a sufficient selection of high-quality and delicious dishes has been presented. Also, our compatriots were pleased with the presence of fish and seafood dishes, fresh fruits and delicious desserts on the menu. Tourists who came to rest with children also did not complain about food, since the children here ate with pleasure to the joy of their mothers and fathers.

Many travelers call its location a great advantage of the Creeny Beach Hotel. So, it is literally 50 meters from the beach. However, the beach here is far from the best, since when you enter the water there are large sharp stones. But if you are not too lazy to go just a hundred meters to the side, then you will reach a more convenient beach with a sandy entrance to the sea. There is also a bus stop 200 meters from the hotel with links to Rethymnon in just 10 minutes.

Our compatriots were also satisfied with the staff and the quality of service at the three-star Hotel Creeney Beach. So, all the hotel staff were always friendly, smiling and ready to help. A pleasant fact was that many hotel workers speak Russian.

As for the pools, here they are regularly cleaned. So, both adults and children can swim without fear. Also, many travelers noted in their reviews the lack of animation on site. For some, this was a small minus, while someone, on the contrary, was only too happy.

In general, the vast majority of tourists were quite satisfied with their stay in the three-star Cretey Beach Crete hotel. They admit the possibility of their return to this hotel in the future, and are also willing to recommend this place to their friends and relatives.
