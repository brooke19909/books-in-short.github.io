Apple Time: Apple Watch Accessories

Now Apple Watch can be safely called the next victory of the very well-known company that for many years was built under the strict guidance of Steve Jobs. What can be a wristwatch made by Apple? Not just smart, but genius. One movement - and instead of the dial you have before your eyes a wide range of functions: from sending SMS to listening to music. Of course, the secret of the genius of such watches lies in the iPhone with which they are paired.
But we will not talk about them at all, but about accessories. And now there are a great many of them.





Original Apple accessories for smart watches have long been inferior in number to devices from third-party manufacturers, which are usually cheaper, but often lower in quality. Such devices are not limited to straps: various devices for protecting the watch case, original solutions for recharging, and much more are presented on the market.

It's time, as they say, to separate the chaff from the grains and talk about the best accessories for the Apple Watch.

Trident Valet Stand

From the side, this accessory looks like a stylish case for storing watches: external leather trim, a velvet backing under the glass cover and a watch holder. But this functionality is not limited. Using the case, you can charge your Apple Watch and iPhone at the same time.





A watch stand is, in fact, a charger. It connects to the battery using a lighting cable. The glass cover serves as a stand for the smartphone, which is charged using a standard USB cable. The remaining battery power is indicated by diode indicators located on the stand. Thanks to its powerful battery, Trident Valet will be your ideal travel companion. The accessory is available in different color options.

Nomad Pod Portable Charging

Portable charging that easily fits in your pocket. This device looks like a hockey puck, only slightly smaller in size - 75 millimeters in diameter and 25 in height. The power supply weighs 80 grams.





As for the chargers for Apple watches, accessories of this type usually require additional cables, but not the Nomad Pod. The device has a special slot in which the watch is inserted for recharging. The creators of the accessory did not pass by the ability to charge smartphones. The device can charge any device with a connector for Micro or regular USB. And information about the remaining battery charge is displayed on the LED display.

Price of the device: from 60 US dollars.

Proper WatchKeeper Dock and Case

If you are looking for a reliable and stylish case for your watch, then pay attention to the model from the Australian manufacturer Proper. In addition to being a case, it can also be used to charge the watch. The cable is conveniently hidden in an elongated case, which, by the way, is trimmed with genuine leather. The top cover can be left open and used as a dock.

The accessory is very light, and its dimensions are 9x2.2x1 inches.

Pad & Quill Timber Catchall

The Pad & Quill company produces perhaps the highest quality accessories for the Apple Watch. Timber Catchcall is a luxurious retro mahogany watch and smartphone stand made by hand. The accessory can be folded, making it compact and easy to carry in your pocket. There is a hole for the cable. To ensure that the stand does not slip, four rubber buttons are located on the bottom.





The price, however, bites - about a hundred dollars, but there is also a smaller option with a stand only for one watch. Its cost is slightly lower.

Powerbeats 2 Wireless Headphones

Perhaps the most desired accessories for Apple watches (iPhone users also purchase them for their smartphones) that expand their functionality the most are wireless headphones.

Powerbeats 2 Wireless from Beats Audio, an experienced audio technology manufacturer - plug-in bluetooth headphones. They are interconnected by a short wire, which is needed to transmit a signal. This posting will not hurt you at all, in addition, there was a place on it for a miniature control panel. Through it, you can control the playback of music and even receive calls. Powerbeats are conveniently attached to the ears with elastic arms. Headphones are waterproof. The batteries should last for six hours of continuous playback.

Apple Watch Protection: Accessories

It is recommended that you protect the display of your smartwatch. Judge for yourself: this is not a smartphone that lies quietly in its case on a table, in your pocket or in a bag, while you are, say, busy with work. Watches are constantly at risk while they are in the hands of the owner. Even if you work at a computer, not to mention travel in public transport. This means that it is worth considering separately other types of devices for watches Apple - accessories to protect the display.





Apple Watch screens are very durable, but the risk of scratching them is always very high. This can be avoided with a special protective film. In ArmorSuite packaging you will find two such membranes and detailed instructions. The film is designed in such a way as to completely cover the display and bend around the edges to protect the glass. With such a membrane, you will be much calmer for your favorite watch. The cost of the film is about ten dollars.

Another good manufacturer is Zagg. They have long specialized in protective films for smartphones, and now they have taken care of smartwatch displays.
