Aquamare Beach Hotel & Spa 4 * (Cyprus, Paphos): hotel description, reviews

Guests of the Aquamare Beach Hotel & Spa can enjoy all the delights of a Cyprus holiday. Here, quality service and local traditions of hospitality create a special warm atmosphere.





Short description

Unforgettable moments of paradise relaxation give you the hotel Aquamare Beach Hotel & Spa. The friendly attitude of the staff creates an atmosphere of home comfort, and the quality service is brought into line with international standards. It is here that travelers will find all the components of an unforgettable vacation: blue pools, the sea coast, good food, comfortable rooms, vibrant entertainment and much more.

Location

The Aquamare Beach Hotel & Spa is located just 3 kilometers from the city center. At the same distance is the fishing harbor. The international airport is 15 km away.

The tourism and infrastructure facilities closest to Aquamare Beach Hotel & Spa are as follows:

  • Paphos water park (1 km);
  • St. Paul's Church (3 km);
  • early Christian basilica (3 km);
  • golf course (5 km).

Hotel Rooms

Resort Aquamare Beach Hotel & Spa 4 * has 134 comfortable rooms for travelers. They can be divided into the following categories:

  • Standard rooms are designed for 2 adult guests with the possibility of additional accommodation for 1 child. The room can be fitted with a double or a pair of twin beds.
  • Studio - spacious apartments, which are a combination of a bedroom and a sitting area with a soft folding sofa. On the balcony there is a comfortable deck chair, as well as a folding clothes dryer.
  • Family apartments are two spacious bedrooms, one of which is for children, and the second is for adults (there is access to a balcony). Bathrooms are adjacent to both rooms.
  • Royal Suite is striking in its size and pleases the eye with an elegant interior. The bedroom has a four-poster bed. The living room is combined with the dining room and a small kitchenette. The bathroom is equipped with a hydromassage bathtub.




Room Amenities

One of the main indicators of comfort is the availability of all necessary amenities in the rooms. The equipment of the Aquamare Beach Hotel & Spa 4 * apartments is as follows:

  • a balcony with comfortable plastic furniture and a portable towel dryer (in some rooms - clothesline);
  • a service, an electric kettle, as well as coffee, tea and sugar bags;
  • a safe with a combination lock will serve as a reliable repository for your valuables;
  • a minibar, which is also used for products;
  • satellite television (the channel package also includes Russian speakers);
  • a radio that will provide pleasant music in your room;
  • individually controlled air conditioning allows you to cool or warm the air in the room to create a comfortable microclimate;
  • combined bathroom with shower or bath, as well as a hairdryer and bath accessories;
  • connection with the reception and access to the international line will be provided by a landline telephone;
  • Sensitive smoke detectors provide fire safety.




Infrastructure

Resort Aquamare Beach Hotel & Spa (Cyprus) boasts a developed infrastructure. On the territory you can use the following objects:

  • the main hall, where the 24-hour reception is located, as well as a recreation area;
  • an elevator, thanks to which you can comfortably get to the upper floors of the residential building;
  • large garden with flowering shrubs and palm trees;
  • access to wireless Internet throughout;
  • Luggage storage, which can be used by guests awaiting check-in, or after eviction;
  • shops with fresh products, hygiene items, as well as clothing;
  • souvenir shop;
  • conference rooms of various capacities for business negotiations and other events;
  • decorated rooms for banquets and celebrations;
  • free parking area with private covered area.




Catering establishments

The Aquamare Beach Hotel & Spa takes care not only of a quality vacation, but also of the full nutrition of the guests. You can visit the following catering establishments:

  • Kirke is the main restaurant, where meals for guests are organized in buffet style. The emphasis is on traditional local dishes, but there is also a European menu.
  • At Ambrosia Restaurant, guests can have lunch and dinner. Several times a week, theme nights are held dedicated to different cuisines of the world.
  • Nereus is a restaurant serving international cuisine. Here you can taste dishes from all over the world while enjoying live music.
  • Kima is the favorite bar of guests who like to spend all their free time in the pool. Here you can not only refresh yourself with a cocktail, but also refresh yourself with junk food.
  • In the Nectar Lounge Bar, guests can enjoy a cup of coffee or a glass of local wine in a relaxed atmosphere, as well as enjoy cakes and other pastries.




Entertainment

A bright and unforgettable vacation will give you the Aquamare Beach Hotel & Spa. Tours to this hotel are in great demand, because the following entertainments await guests here:

  • a fitness center where the most modern equipment is collected and professional trainers work;
  • diving lessons and diving arrangements;
  • Illuminated tennis court for playing in the dark;
  • sports animators regularly conduct classes in Pilates, yoga, aerobics, as well as oriental dances;
  • a sauna where you can relieve emotional and physical stress after a busy tourist day;
  • table tennis game;
  • outdoor playground.




Wellness center services

Hotel Aquamare Beach Hotel & Spa 4 * has a large spa and wellness center. The following services are provided in 20 therapeutic rooms:

  • deep massage aimed at full muscle recovery;
  • procedures using hot stones;
  • a special complex for athletes aimed at eliminating muscle pain and the effects of injuries;
  • aromatherapy;
  • hydromassage;
  • mud wraps;
  • anti-aging procedures for facial skin.

Service for guests

The Aquamare Beach Hotel & Spa (Paphos) is based on the highest standards of service. The following service is available to guests:

  • lease of vehicles for independent movement around the island;
  • copying documents, sending faxes and other office services;
  • organization and holding of weddings;
  • assistance in acquiring tickets for aviation and maritime modes of transport;
  • ironing and washing personal items of guests; free local press;
  • delivery of orders from the restaurant to the room.

Additional Information

In any hotel there are some rules in accordance with which the accommodation and rest of guests are organized. It is worth paying attention to the following points:

  • check-in is after 14:00, and all issues related to eviction should be resolved before noon;
  • throughout the territory, with the exception of specially designated places, smoking is prohibited;
  • hotel rules do not allow pets;
  • cots for babies under the age of two are provided free of charge;
  • from November to March, the water in the outdoor and indoor pools is heated;
  • when booking a room on your credit card, a certain amount of funds will be blocked as collateral;
  • guests are not allowed to visit guest rooms;
  • Access to the spa is available only to guests who have reached the age of 18;
  • it is allowed to use only a personal credit card, on which the passport data of one of the guests staying in the room are applied.

Negative reviews

Unfortunately, there are no ideal hotels, and therefore you should be prepared for the negative moments that you may encounter at Aquamare Beach Hotel & Spa. Paphos is visited by many tourists, and based on their reviews we can talk about the following:

  • in the hotel all drinks are paid (even for drinking water you need to pay extra);
  • rocky entrance to the sea (legs can be injured on sharp blocks);
  • there is practically no animation in the hotel, and therefore it is not suitable for lovers of noisy and active recreation;
  • in stores in the territory, prices are several times higher than in neighboring retail outlets;
  • construction is underway nearby, which creates a lot of noise and spoils the view from the window;
  • Wireless Internet only works at the reception desk;
  • chefs put too much pepper and spices in the food (even in the dietary dishes that are usually given to children);
  • despite the fact that breakfast is set at 7:00 a.m., staff completes serving and opens a restaurant no earlier than 7:30 a.m.
  • the hotel is located near the Paphos airport, and therefore planes often fly over it, creating a lot of noise;
  • an adapter for sockets needs to be taken with you (for many tourists it becomes an unpleasant surprise that the device cannot be requested at the reception).

General impression

Greek hotels immerse travelers in an atmosphere of serenity and relaxation. The coast of the Mediterranean Sea, in turn, gives beautiful nature and warm water. If we talk about this particular institution, then it is for those who love a quiet and leisurely pastime. This is a great option for a family or romantic getaway. Despite the numerous shortcomings in the organization of work, they cannot cover all the inherent advantages of this institution.

Perhaps the only thing that can seriously disappoint travelers is a rocky beach. But this is rather a characteristic feature of Paphos. In addition, you can always take a walk along the coastline, choosing a more convenient place for swimming. The hotel staff will kindly show you good places for a beach holiday, as well as enlighten about the entertainment facilities in which you can properly unwind.
