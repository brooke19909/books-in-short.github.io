Guest house "Irina", Lazarevskoye: overview, description and reviews

The resorts of the Black Sea coast of the Krasnodar Territory have always been very popular among vacationers. Indeed, in these places there is everything that a person needs for a good rest: the cleanest sea, magnificent nature, subtropical vegetation, a unique climate, healing air, developed infrastructure and a wide selection of housing for every taste and pocket.

The village of Lazarevskoye, Sochi

Among the resorts of the Krasnodar Territory, a special place is occupied by the picturesque village of Lazarevskoye, which belongs to the territory of Greater Sochi.

The developed resort infrastructure attracts a huge flow of tourists to this hospitable seaside town.

A special attraction of Lazarevsky is the modern park of culture and recreation, which is located right on the waterfront. The highlight of the park is a huge Ferris wheel, which is considered the largest in Russia. It‚Äôs breathtaking when you are at the top of this wheel. Views of the sea and mountains are simply incomparable.





In addition to the big ferris wheel , a lot of rides, swings and carousels work on Lazarevsky embankment. In the evening, the park organizes all kinds of contests, discos and other entertainment events that will not leave indifferent even the most picky tourists.

The beaches in the village are some of the best on the entire Black Sea coast. A variety of water activities, boat trips on a yacht, boat and catamarans are available to tourists in full. The sea is generally a fairy tale. The bottom is flat, without cliffs, the water is so clean that every pebble is visible.

Private sector of the village

A huge amount of housing offers its guests the village of Lazarevskoye. Elite hotels, motels, large pensions and hotels and numerous offers from the private sector. Private guest houses for rent in any corner of the village. Each owner seeks to create the most comfortable living conditions, as the competition is quite high.

Mostly private hotels are cottage-type buildings with two to three floors and a small but ennobled territory. The advantages of guest houses: cozy courtyards with flowerbeds, alpine slides, lawns, benches and gazebos. In the courtyard there is usually a place for barbecue, there is a common kitchen for self-cooking. Most guest houses have their own orchard.

Typical for the private sector are affordable prices and standard living conditions, but in almost every holiday home there are rooms with increased comfort, luxury apartments and suites.
