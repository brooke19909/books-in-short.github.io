Do-it-yourself baby clothes. Ideas

If the family has children, then the presence of toys in the children's room is mandatory. Of course, the boy always plays with various cars and robots. But the girl, most likely, is inseparable from beautiful dolls and fat babies, baby dolls.





And of course, she wants to not just play with her toys, but periodically change her wardrobe, wash and iron clothes. That is, to be a real mother to her little girlfriends. And how to make clothes for a baby doll with your own hands? To diversify your wardrobe, you need to pick up a needle, threads and several pieces of different fabrics.

Panties

How to sew clothes for a baby doll with your own hands? Bobblehead is a toy likeness of a small child, more precisely, a baby. Therefore, the clothes should be sewn accordingly. Overalls, romper suits, blouses and vests. Each mother made all these products at least once in her life. After all, even at labor lessons, the basics of sewing precisely these items of children's clothing were taught. Do-it-yourself clothes for a baby doll are made quite quickly. Literally in the evening you can sew a large number of things. For this, it is only necessary to build patterns of basic objects.





Even those women who have never done them in their lives will cope with the building. To do this, simply attach small things to the paper. You need to draw a marker around the edge of the pants. Then transfer the pattern to the fabric in double size (with allowances for the seams). Then sew along the side seams and step lines. Insert an elastic band into the belt. That's all, the panties are ready.

Advice

In the same way, you can sew a bobbin t-shirt or T-shirt. For more beautiful and complex things, you need to make a pattern on the basics, measuring the chest circumference, waist circumference, the length of the back to the waist and the length of the navel. Having drawn a model of clothes, calculate it with the help of taken measurements and build a pattern on paper, which then transfer it to the fabric.





Clothing for dolls dolls, made with their own hands, it turns out beautiful, subject to the basic rules. When sewing products, do not forget about seam allowances. Otherwise, problems with size mismatch may occur. Regardless of whether the product is sewn by hand or on a typewriter, do not forget about even stitches. It is always necessary to trim the remnants of protruding threads, do not leave unbroken portions. In sewing, do not rush. Everything is best done neatly and clearly.

How to sew clothes for a baby doll with your own hands, if there are patterns? It is necessary to re-draw the drawing on tracing paper. Then transfer all parts to the fabric, leaving allowances for the seams. After this, the details need to be sewn. Following all the instructions, you can easily make wonderful costumes.

From threads

Do-it-yourself clothes for a baby doll (patterns of which there is no way to find or they are too complex) can be made by preserving the product‚Äôs basics, but without the use of small seams, tucks and folds. And then all the shortcomings can be covered with bows, beads, lace. Clothing for a baby doll with their own hands can be not only made of fabric, but also knitted or crocheted. Thread products are pretty elegant and easy to make. For this, patterns and patterns are not needed.

Dress





We figured out how to sew clothes for a baby doll with our own hands. And how to make a thing with yarn and knitting needles? We show an example of a dress knitted with knitting needles and of fine yarn. First you need to tie a rectangle equal to the volume of the waist. Then, a trapeze is knitted along the lower edge, the length of which corresponds to the desired length of the dress. Finished parts are sewn together and then along a common seam along the back. The finished dress is put on the baby doll. After that, they are fixed on the shoulders with the help of ribbons or elastic, sewn to the dress.

Decorate things

Clothing for a baby doll, made with your own hands, is quite cute and interesting if you decorate it with small buttons, sparkles, bows and other decor items. If the stitched thing is slightly large, then you can pick it up using a thread in the waist and neck.

Task for the girl

Not only mothers can sew clothes for a baby doll with their own hands, such a thing can be entrusted to the owner of the dolls. Each girl aged 5-7 years can do some simple things for a baby doll. For example, a poncho or a flared sun skirt. Indeed, in these products there are not a large number of parts. For manufacturing, you only need to cut the workpiece from the fabric and, putting it on the small doll, fix it on the waist and neck.





Sewing clothes for a baby doll with your own hands contributes to the development of fine motor skills, attention, measurement accuracy and accuracy. All these skills can be obtained in just a couple of sewing lessons. And the made thing will be not only a new thing for the baby doll, but also a subject of pride for the child.

Little conclusion

The process of creating things for the baby doll is a great option for spending free time on a winter evening. After all, the work of mother and child not only brings good results, but also promotes communication and understanding.





You should not start sewing complex products for toys at once. You must first learn how to do simple things. And then gradually move on to the intricate ones, containing various small details and decor items. However, one should not be afraid to start sewing for a baby doll. Indeed, for the manufacture of clothing does not require a lot of materials, nor will it hit the financial well-being of the family. You can sew from the remnants of fabric, from old things that have gone out of fashion, from pieces of fur or leather. Original products are obtained from completely ordinary materials such as cotton or chintz.
