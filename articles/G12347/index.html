City Fryazino, Moscow region: description, photo

The city of Fryazino, Moscow Region, is located only 20 km from the capital of the Russian Federation in a northeastern direction. The riverbed of the Lyuboseevka River passes through its territory. According to the 2015 census, its population reaches almost 59 thousand people. And it is worth noting that since 2007 this figure has increased markedly, by almost seven thousand. In total, there are more than 1,100 cities in Russia, and the city of Fryazino (Moscow region) occupies the 283rd position in terms of population.



A bit of history

Presumably the name "Fryazino" came from the Italians - Fryazin, who built the Moscow Kremlin, the cannon yard, many fortresses and factories.
On the site of the present city were the villages of Fryazinovka and Chizhovo and the village of Grebnevo. In the latter, under Prince Trubetskoy, entrepreneurship was born: silk-weaving factories were founded. This is what influenced the rapid development of the city.

In 1901, the construction of the silk-weaving factory A.M. Kaptsova, who provided work to all residents of Fryazino (Moscow region). This enterprise was nationalized in 1918, and liquidated in 1929. In its place in 1933 the Radiolamp plant appeared, thanks to which Fryazino became the center of the electronic industry. Five years later, the village becomes a working village. In 1943, a research institute with an experimental plant was opened. Later, a branch of the Institute of Radio Electronics of the USSR Academy of Sciences was built. The status of the city was assigned only in 1951. The Fryazino index (Moscow region) - 141190. It became the city of regional subordination in 1968.

During perestroika, a conversion of defense enterprises was carried out, on the basis of which the production of medical equipment, hearing aids and LED lighting devices began. There is a production of high- power fiber lasers , and the Maisky Tea factory operates.



Neighborhood

The climate in Fryazino is temperate continental. Forests alternate with swamps. Most fields are cultivated. A large lake appeared on the site of Bulygin peat bogs , in the vicinity there are Barsky ponds. These are one of the most picturesque places of the Meshchera lowland. In summer, you can have a wonderful vacation in nature. On the field there are beveled areas, a volleyball net is stretched , you can play football. The presence of a reservoir allows you to fish and ride in inflatable boats. There is a small marina with which you can dive. A makeshift bridge leads to an island in the middle of the pond.

sights

The key attraction of the city of Fryazino (Moscow region) is the Grebnevo estate. The complex occupies a large territory. Previously, it consisted of a main palace, 2 wings, 2 churches, a stone church - the only one with an archangel holding a cross on its dome, chic front gates, outbuildings, decorative elements, a park and apple orchards. The windows of the palace overlook a picturesque pond. The history of the estate begins in the 16th century. At different times, it belonged to Belsky, Bibikov, Golitsyn, Panteleev. Each made additions to the architectural ensemble. In Soviet times, the estate was a sanatorium, in the 1980s, the recognition of the value of Grebnevo turned the complex into a cultural center, which was closed due to a fire. Currently, the facility is not guarded and is used mainly for extreme tourism.

Also in Fryazino (Moscow region) you can relax at the recreation centers. The houses are located in a protected area, have barbecue areas and catering facilities.



Transport sphere

The city is being built and developed. Fryanovskoe highway passes through it. The city itself is located on the seventh kilometer. A bus station has been built on Polevaya Street. Working transport in the city is represented by buses and minibuses. And of course, there is a train station.

It is convenient to get to Fryazino from the capital of Russia by car or public transport. Those who are suitable for the second option can use the train. From Yaroslavsky Station in Moscow to Fryazino it takes about an hour. And also by bus - routes No. 361, No. 335.
