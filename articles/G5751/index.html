BIN - what is it? What is a BIN card?

To transfer funds from one account to another, several details are used: card numbers, full name. the recipient and the sender, the name of the bank, etc. The good news is that not all of this data needs to be known for most transactions. For example, to transfer funds from card to card within one bank, it is enough to indicate only the plastic number. Another thing is if the transaction is carried out between credit institutions. Then you must specify the BIN. What is and where to look for it, read further in this article.





Definition

Bank Identification Number (BIN) is the first 9 digits of the card. It serves to determine the credit institution in the payment system, is formed on the basis of the standards of the Central Bank. Using BIN, you can find out:

  • type of payment system (MPS);
  • card issuing bank;
  • type: credit or debit;
  • View: Maestro, Electron, Classic, Gold, Standard, Platinum.

This is what BIN is. Card readers in the process of identification process data from the magnetic strip and chip. They are used to transmit information on payment details, one of which is BIN. What is so important can trade establishments learn? They receive information from cards to track transactions by types of payment instruments and their types, holders. This method of reading significantly speeds up the processing of information, reduces the risk of fraudulent transactions. This is what the Oschadbank BIN card is.





Data compliance

Information about the code can be found in publicly available sources. But there it is rarely updated, and incorrect data is often displayed. In addition, banks do not consider BIN an important requisite. Therefore, do not track the correctness of its use. In Russia, there is no big difference between debit and credit cards. But in the United States or EU countries, the percentage of the commission paid by the recipient is very different. Therefore, some stores set strict requirements for the type of payment instrument.

Decoding BIN

What is the first digit in the code? It means the type of payment system:

  • 3 - American Express;
  • 4 - Visa;
  • 5 - MasterCard;
  • 6 - Maestro;
  • 7 - UEC.

But this is only in theory. For identification of MasterCard codes in the range of 50-55 are used. Since 2006, the Bankcard system has been operating in Australia and New Zealand under the numbers 5610, 56021-56022. Another example: American Express allocated codes 34, 35, and 37 for the Japan Credit Bureau. Although the Maestro social card issued by Sberbank of Russia starts with 6390, and the gold Visa with 4276.

The third and fourth figures indicate the territory within the state. For Russia, this classification takes place according to OKATO. The card issued by the Moscow branch of Sberbank will contain code 45, and the St. Petersburg institution - 40. The fifth and sixth digits inform about the conditional number of a particular unit for the calculations of the Central Bank. It can range from 00 to 99. The last three numbers in the BIC coincide with the corresponding data in the correspondent account (it consists of twenty digits). They range from 050 to 099. Option one: on the front side of the plastic

Since the BIN is part of the card number, you can find it out by looking at the payment instrument. On the front side there are 15-19 digits that are applied with paint or by extrusion. This is the number. Usually it contains 16 digits (4 blocks of 4 digits), 19 are used in additional payment instruments that are issued as part of a single client account, 15 in American Express. What is a BIN card? This is the first 9 digits of the number. The rest contains information about the type, currency, region of issue, etc. The last, sixteenth, is a check number, which determines the "correctness" of the card number.

Option two: contact the bank branch

Sometimes on the map not 16 digits are indicated, but only the first or last four. In this case, to find out the BIN, you need to call the hotline, dictate the full name, card number, code word. The same information will be requested when visiting a bank branch. But then you have to stand in line and provide a passport to the employee. If you still have a piece of paper with a PIN code or an agreement to the card, then you can see the code there.
