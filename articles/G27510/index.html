Baby coat

Outerwear for little fashionistas is the most diverse. Girls need to choose those things in which children will not only look beautiful, but also feel comfortable. You should pay attention to bright fabrics. Girls love colored things with interesting stripes. It is worth starting from this, making a coat. You can successfully combine the classic style with bright inserts and applications. The pattern of a coat with a raglan sleeve for a girl is given above. If desired, it can be somewhat modernized. But you should do this carefully so as not to violate the proportions of the product. We print out the Burda pattern. We will sew a coat with a raglan sleeve in the following order: first we need to make sleeves, then side seams, and only then connect the parts. Some may doubt this sequence. Indeed, experienced seamstresses know that it is easier to insert a sleeve into an open armhole than into a closed one. But this method is simply not applicable with raglan. There are no shoulder seams in the coat; it is the sleeve that forms them. Therefore, so that the product does not skew during the creation process, you should clearly follow the instructions.
