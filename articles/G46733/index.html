Hotel "Olympus", Sochi: address, description, reviews

The city of Sochi is considered one of the best seaside climatic and balneological resorts. Here, a subtropical climate due to the proximity of the Black Sea and healing hydrogen sulfide springs are surprisingly combined. Holidays in Sochi is not only a wave of positive emotions, but a tour of numerous local attractions. That is why many today prefer to come on vacation here.



Hotel Olympus

Sochi today has become quite a popular tourist destination. The city has built a lot of comfortable hotels. The first line from the sea is literally built up with five- and four-star hotels. The apartments in them are quite expensive, and therefore not always accessible to ordinary tourists. But there are also hotels where prices will suit even the average Russian. One of them is the Olympus Hotel. Sochi is a place where people come primarily from across the sea. Tourists want to settle as close to it as possible. Therefore, if the location of the hotel is the first line, then, most likely, the rooms in it are almost never empty.

Address and general information

Nearby are many well-known resorts and pensions. The hotel building literally rises above the city. Amazing views of the sea, the majestic peaks of the Caucasus Range, original architecture, luxurious furniture, affordable price - these are not all the reasons why many people choose the Hotel Olympus.



Sochi, whose resort and sanatorium zone is quite large, after the Olympics became a city that today is comparable to many famous tourist centers of the world. Near the hotel there is a convenient funicular, which takes you to the well-equipped beach in a few minutes.

Within three kilometers are the city's Singing Fountains, a seaport, a lake with swans, the Cathedral of St. Michael the Archangel. The distance to the railway station is two and a half kilometers, and to the international airport by car from the Olimp Hotel - 30 minutes.

Housing stock

In a modern six-story building located in the resort area, next to such prestigious resorts as Rus, Rainbow and Neva, there are a total of fourteen rooms. All are equipped according to international standards. In general, the Olimp Hotel (Sochi) is equated to a three-star category. It has four standard rooms, the same number of one- and two-room suites. There are also premium apartments.

Superior rooms are equipped with a double bed, wardrobe, dressing table, chest of drawers, sofa bed, refrigerator, TV. Since the hotel operates year-round, double-circuit split systems are installed in all areas, including public ones. In the hallway there is a luggage rack, a safe.





In the bathrooms there is a bath, toilet, washbasin, hairdryer and a set of hygiene products for residents.

All rooms have small, furnished balconies. On the floor in the rooms - ceramic tiles. The living rooms are luxury, studio and premium - carpet. All space is connected to the wireless Internet.

Suites

In addition to superior rooms, the Olympus Hotel offers two-room apartments for living. The suite has a separate bedroom and living room. The rooms are designed in a classic style. The bedroom has a double bed, there are also bedside tables, dressing table, chest of drawers. In the living room there is a large corner sofa, a wardrobe, a refrigerator, a coffee table. Both rooms have plasma TVs. There is also a safe, sewing and a set of dishes. The bathroom has expensive plumbing.

Prices

From February to November, tourists will have to pay two thousand five hundred rubles for two for a standard double room. In the high season, prices rise to three thousand nine hundred rubles.



In turn, the studio category rooms will cost, respectively, two thousand seven hundred and four thousand three hundred, and luxury - 3800 and 5200 rubles. On holidays, payment is equal to the cost in high season.

What is included in the price

Mini-Hotel Olympus is located in the center of Sochi. It was opened in 2011. The hotel has a small but well-equipped and always clean area. The price of tours to Olympus includes not only accommodation, but also a free breakfast. Also free are wireless Internet, telephone use, personal safes in rooms, secure parking, ordering tickets for an airplane and train, calling a taxi or ambulance, mail delivery, using an iron and ironing board. At the reception you can also book a table in the restaurant.

Additional services

The price does not include room service and excursion services, including a guide, as well as laundry, concierge services: you must pay for the delivery of various goods, including food from city restaurants, at the request of residents.

For a fee, a shuttle service from the railway or airport terminal, descent to the resort beach "Zapolyarye" by cable car, the use of sunbeds, umbrellas are also available.





Nutrition

The cost of living in the Sochi Olympus Hotel includes a continental breakfast. Buffet dishes are served in the dining room each morning. There is also a cafe-bar on site, where, upon reservation, you can get more than sixty varieties of dishes on the menu.

Hotel Olymp is located in the central district of Sochi, where there are many restaurants and pizzerias. Therefore, hotel guests can have lunch and dinner at one of these establishments.

Entertainment

Hotel "Olympus" is located in an area with a new and convenient infrastructure, which was specially equipped for the Winter Olympics. Living in the shortest time can get to the center of Sochi and Adler, taking the train, bus or taxi. Within reach are Rosa Khutor, from where walks to the mountains surrounding the city are organized. Olympic and Sochi Park can be reached on foot in just fifteen minutes. Interesting events are often held here, there are dizzying rides. In general, the convenient infrastructure surrounding the hotel is one of the main reasons why many people choose Olympus (Sochi).





Reviews

The hotel fully met the expectations of most tourists. In almost all reviews, people say that their vacation was a success. I liked everything: clean tidy rooms, staff work, and location. There are, of course, several opinions that the menu in the bar is scarce, but this problem is solvable, since there are many similar establishments within walking distance. Although the prices are not low, the food is fresh and of high quality.

Girls working at the reception, smiling beauties who are always ready to help. Most of the guests rated the hotel rooms at a solid "five", and even with a plus. There were no problems with wireless Internet either.

Sochi residents can easily and quickly get to the center of Sochi. Within walking distance is the free Albatros Beach, which, although municipal, is quite well-maintained. In fifteen minutes you can reach it, however, some did not like the expensive way back uphill.

By funicular you can get to the beach of the neighboring sanatorium Zapolyarye. In addition, guests of Olympus can use its infrastructure - playgrounds, a gym.

In general, tourists from the hotel had only good impressions. They recommend it, and they themselves, as they say, will be happy to come here again.
