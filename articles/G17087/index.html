"On the Volga"

10 minutes from Balakovo is a beautiful base "On the Volga". The name was given to her for a reason. After all, it is located right on the banks of the river. The base is surrounded by picturesque nature, there is a clean sandy beach. On the territory there is a bathhouse, there are banquet rooms where family celebrations and corporate parties are held. For tourists who want to play active games, tennis and volleyball courts are equipped. Also, the administration of the recreation center Balakovo "On the Volga" organizes fishing. You can catch both from the shore and from the boat. For living in the territory built wooden houses in two floors. They contain the necessary furniture, thanks to which the conditions of rest become as comfortable as possible.

"Dubrava"

On Privolzhskaya street in house number 6 there is a hotel house, which is focused not only on recreation, but also on recovery. A forest grows on the territory, thanks to which the air in this area is filled with freshness and aromas of various vegetation. From the windows of all rooms you can enjoy views of the Volga or the Deserted Island. The administration of the complex offers services for celebrations, seminars and conferences. For this, special banquet rooms are provided.

A bathroom, a TV, natural wood furniture, a refrigerator, air conditioning are all equipped with rooms at this recreation center (Balakovo). Accommodation prices vary depending on the category: minimum for economy - 1900 rubles, maximum for double apartments - 4700 rubles. Upon request, the price includes a set breakfast (300 rubles).



"Forest Nymph"

A small recreation center "Forest Nymph" invites guests to relax inexpensively on the banks of the Volga. On its territory there are 4 houses with a capacity of 20 beds. The minimum cost of living on weekdays is 125 rubles, on holidays and weekends the prices are slightly higher - 150 rubles. You can rent a bed in a multi-bed room. Also at the base is a detached house accommodating 6 people. The entertainments of this recreation center (Balakovo) consist in the presence of a basketball ring, a field for beach volleyball and badminton, a tennis court. There are also gazebos with equipment for making barbecue, and an indoor venue for large celebrations. Pets are allowed at the base.
