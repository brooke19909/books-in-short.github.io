Anapa, LCD "Parkovy": description, location

In the south of Russia there is a city - a pearl among the resorts of the Krasnodar Territory - Anapa. The population of the city is a little more than 73 thousand people, but developers do not bypass it with their attention, since many people are moving here to bask under the hospitable southern sun. A promising area is the construction of new residential complexes in Anapa. LCD "Parkovy" is a striking example.





Location of the residential complex

Near the Eastern Market on Parkovaya Street, 79, the indicated residential complex is located. Just a little over two kilometers separates the house from the edge of the Black Sea. You can get here from the city center on foot, as, indeed, in almost any corner of the city. You can also get by public transport - minibus - in a few minutes. In terms of transport accessibility, the residential complex is an ideal option for settlement. Parkovaya Street overlooks the city‚Äôs major transport artery, Krestyanskaya Street, which passes into Anapskoye Shosse.





Description of the complex

LCD "Parkovy" in Anapa consists of two residential buildings with two entrances each. The houses have the same number of floors - 18 floors each. Traditionally, the first floors are reserved for non-residential premises and the placement of social infrastructure objects in them. And the top floor is designed by technical. Therefore, residential floors in buildings turn out to be only 16.





In the houses of the Parkovy residential complex , all types of popular apartments, from one-room to three-room apartments, are offered for sale. Moreover, the developer paid special attention to kitchens and dressing rooms. They turned out to be spacious. There are high-speed elevators in the entrances. Security of residents is provided by intercoms.

Internal infrastructure

Many social infrastructure facilities will be located on the first floors of residential buildings. These will be shops, pharmacies, beauty salons. Residents will not have to leave the Parkovy residential complex for small goods and travel to the city. Common areas are refined and finished with chutes.

The developer allocated about 35 acres of land to place a walking zone on it for residents of the complex. This zone, like the entire local area, will be landscaped, which is provided for by the construction project. In addition, the developer in the Parkovy residential complex (Anapa) plans paving footpaths, sports and playgrounds for children, planting trees and shrubs, guest parking and underground parking.

Complex developer

The developer of the Parkovy residential complex (Anapa) is a new construction company - Anapsky Project LLC. At the moment, this developer does not have completed projects, but its sole owner is an investment company, which has more than a billion rubles in assets. This allows us to talk about the reliability of the developer itself and inspires confidence that the project will be completed on time.



External infrastructure

Almost all Anapa residential complexes are located in areas with good infrastructure. In this city there are practically no industrial enterprises. Everything is aimed only at ensuring the comfort of its residents and guests. And speaking of the Parkovy residential complex in Anapa, it is directly located a few meters from the Eastern Market, where farmers bring their goods on weekends.

There is no shortage of schools or kindergartens. The only inconvenience for kids and their parents is the need to cross the busy peasant street and Anapskoe highway in order to get to the place of study. There are many different supermarkets and small shops in the area.

Ecological situation

Anapa is a city to which they move here not in order to inhale the smog of the metropolis. The sea and clean air - this is its main advantage. Unfortunately, since the Parkovy residential complex (Anapa) is located almost in the center of the city itself, it will not be possible to name the perfect ecology of this place. But given that the distance between the residential complex and the sea promenade is only slightly more than two kilometers, it is difficult to imagine a better location. Those who want to live in urban conditions, but at the same time in close proximity to the sea, cannot find a better option than the described residential complex. It has everything you need for a leisurely life and relaxation at sea. It is also a great investment option.
