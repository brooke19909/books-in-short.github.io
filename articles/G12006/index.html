How is cellular communication arranged?

The entire territory of the country is divided into specific cells. With the help of repeaters, uninterrupted communication is constantly ensured in them. The distance between the transmitters depends only on the actual location of the repeater. They have a certain capacity, i.e. the number of mobile phones that can work simultaneously. In urban areas, it is much higher than in the village. Naturally, the subscriber moves around the territory, so the network monitors it in a certain way and switches from one transmitter to another. If the phone is turned on, from time to time it sends a signal to the repeater, even if it is not used for conversation.





Services of mobile operators

In fact, it is almost impossible to determine the city by phone number. The operators themselves provide such a service. They have very sensitive equipment, which makes it possible to know the location of the phone very accurately. For this purpose, the direction finding method is used. A similar service is offered by the mobile companies themselves. They have very finely tuned equipment, which allows you to determine the location of a cell phone up to several tens of meters. These devices have a great opportunity to calculate the location by receiving a signal from three different points. The operator catches it on his computer. The repeater emits a standard - a long digital signal. A mobile phone receives it and a small part of it re-radiates. After the signal that was received from the repeater is compared with the standard, it determines the time delay, and after that you can find out the city by phone number.

Information accuracy



During operation, each phone periodically sends a request to determine its location. The operator has information about where the subscriber was a few minutes ago. The more often calls are received, the more accurate the information. You can find out the city by phone number accurately, but you need to pay attention to a number of factors. First of all, it is interference that reflects the radio signal. The topography of the area also plays a significant role. Attention must be paid to the size of the cell and the number of phones that work in it, as well as to where the base stations are located. After all the data that is received on the computer is analyzed, it will be possible not only to find out the city by the cell number, but also the movement of the subscriber at a certain time. This information is in the database, which contains everything you need about mobile stations.





Search for information over the Internet

You can recognize the city by phone number using the virtual network. To do this, the phone code must be uploaded to the search engine. Not only the city has its own code, but also the region and country. On the corresponding resources there is a special background information in full. If the call was supposedly made from Russia, then just enter the code in a special window, and you do not even need to fill in the remaining fields. The system will give all the information about the range of numbers that has been entered. Useful Tips

There is an alternative for information. A special program has been developed for cell phones that is able to automatically determine the region and city.
