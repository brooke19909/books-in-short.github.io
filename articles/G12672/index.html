"Moreon" - a water park for the whole family

For many families, visiting the water park has long become a good tradition. It is here that at any time of the year you can feel the charm of relaxing on water, calm down and be distracted from everyday fuss, have fun from the heart. With a lot of competition in this industry, visitors began to choose the best conditions for their money, and now water parks should be distinguished by originality of rides and quality of service in order to stand out from the crowd of monotonous offers.



Moreon is a water park in Moscow, which is considered the largest in the city. It is here, in a modern and beautiful building, designed according to the latest requirements and in compliance with all safety standards, visitors can spend time in water activities.

Moreon is a water park that prides itself on a variety of slides of varying degrees of extremeness. There are options for both desperate adrenaline lovers and the whole family. In addition, during the entertainment you will not leave the feeling that you are on the seashore. When decorating the space, a huge amount of greenery was used, which creates an indescribable atmosphere.



Additional services

Moreon is a water park with a wide range of additional services. Here, in addition to outdoor activities, you can choose a relaxing option of water treatments. Several types of bath complexes include steam rooms and saunas, where visitors can cleanse not only the body, but also the spirit. Qualified staff selects a variety of options for programs for the body and skin. This is a great reason to improve your health and become younger and more beautiful in one session. In the water park, visitors can order a fish peeling service - small fish will cleanse the skin of dead cells and help it to tone up. After this procedure, the appearance of the legs and arms improves, and the light touch of the fish on the surface of the skin gives an indescribable feeling.

Conditions for children

Moreon is a water park in which children will really like. Here they can immerse themselves in the real atmosphere of pirate everyday life and have fun in the company of friends. Professional animators will ensure that all children have fun. Leaving a child in the children's pool, you can be sure that he is in safe hands. Only experienced teachers, trainers and instructors work in the water park with young children. The Moreon is proud of this. The water park, photos of which confirm this, provides the highest level of security.



In addition to standard entertainment, the water park also offers a stunning wave pool. Being here, you can feel like splashing in the gentle waves on the sea coast. This is how you will feel surrounded by palm trees and joyful, smiling faces! And at that moment when an active vacation will make itself felt, and you will feel a sense of hunger, at your service - a variety of cafes for every taste.

So, there was already more than one reason to visit the Moreon water park. How to get to this island of fun and relaxation? For residents of Moscow it will not be difficult.
