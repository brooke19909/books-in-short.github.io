Rublevsky Beach: timetables, events, prices, White Park

Warm weather, clear water, beautiful green landscapes, yellow sand and fresh air - this is ideal for a family vacation on a summer weekend.





Most lovers of spending time near the water on a sultry summer day go to the beach to enjoy plenty of outdoor activities and get a bronze tan. One of such places near Moscow is the beach "Rublevo", which is located on the banks of the picturesque bay. The beach is interesting in that it is divided into two zones and is suitable for families with children, friends and barbecue, as well as for individual private events and parties.

Zone 1

On the territory of Rublevsky beach, there are open-air locker rooms, bathrooms, there are left-luggage offices and showers. For those who want a snack, open summer cafes and barbecue. For children there are two water slides (paid), there is a special children's swimming area with a convenient descent and a trampoline. There is an opportunity to rent a deck chair and barbecue, and for water activities - a scooter, boat, catamarans, water catapult, sphere. For ground entertainments - paintball, table tennis, shooting gallery, equipment for volleyball and other beach games. Various parties with DJ performances and foam parties are held.





Weekend Schedule:

  • entertaining music programs from 14-00 to 21-00.

Zone 2

This is a separate area from Rublevsky Beach called White Park, which is equipped for various corporate events, anniversaries and parties on the banks of the Picturesque Bay. White Park is a three snow-white tents and a large same snow-white summer terrace near the water. The design of this place is restrained and modern, the lawns and bushes are neatly trimmed, green lawns and interesting designer bouquets and flower beds look great against a snow-white background, which makes the atmosphere of this place easy and comfortable. White Park can accommodate up to 3 thousand people; outdoor recreation is possible here: banquets, weddings, parties, corporate parties, receptions, presentations, performances and other private events.





The cuisine of White Park is mostly Italian and American. The chef is a universal specialist of the highest level who undergoes internships in various countries of the world and constantly improves the technology, delighting his guests with incredibly delicious new products and passing on invaluable experience to his colleagues. The menu is perfectly balanced and will appeal to any gourmet, and when organizing events, it is possible to create an individual menu based on personal preferences.

On the territory of the park, it is possible to implement any ideas and outstanding solutions. White Park has a beautiful outdoor area and equipped beach, there is parking and transport accessibility. The organization of fireworks, fire shows, equipment rental is feasible.

White Park on Rublevsky Beach has the opportunity to hold luxury events, prestigious presentations and shows. There is a helicopter landing pad and a yacht is acceptable. The staff has a high level of training and has extensive experience in serving and meeting guests, table setting.
