Master class for parents with children: crafts on the theme of "Summer"

Work with crafts is a great way to keep a child busy, instill in him a love of beauty, an attentive attitude to the world around him, develop hand motor skills, precise movements, and finger dexterity. Therefore, dear parents, older brothers and sisters, when there was a free minute, call your kids and make crafts with them!

Application "summer landscape"



The simplest kind of crafts on the theme of "Summer" - application. She needs sets of colored paper, ordinary stationery glue, a clean linen rag (soaking off excess drops of glue), scissors, a simple pencil and an eraser. And, of course, a little imagination. To begin with, ask the child: ‚ÄúWhat is summer?‚Äù Listen to what subjects he will begin to talk about, what associations appear in his mind. So choose the plot of your picture. One of the simplest and most popular options for crafts on the theme of "Summer" is a landscape picture. Take the album sheet and, together with the child, sketch out a landscape scheme: what will be where. Suppose, in the upper left corner or close to it - the sun with rays, further - a few bizarre clouds. It turns out this is the sky. The lower part of the sheet of your crafts on the theme "Summer", respectively, should represent a lawn with green grass, colorful flowers. Or just a few flowers, a flowering bush, etc. A tall tree whose crown reaches the blue skies. Cut small red and yellow circles - these are apples, stick them on the picture. An additional interesting element of the application-crafts on the theme of "Summer" will be a house under the apple tree. Here is such an interesting and cute landscape in a rustic style you have succeeded. Fill it with a frame and hang it on the wall. Sun, sea and boat



The seascape is another bright and fairly simple example of a color application. Such a plot is also ideal for crafts on the theme ‚ÄúHello, summer!‚Äù, Because it is at this time of the year that parents most often take their children to the sun to sunbathe, swim. Or go to the nearest body of water. Such voyages are always joyful, long-awaited and full of the most vivid impressions for children. So, again we take up a pencil with an elastic band, again mark out the spheres of the sky, and this time the sea. The sun is yellow or orange, the rays are an alternation of both colors. The sky is blue or blue. Do not forget about a few clouds and seagulls. Cut them out of white paper in the form of the Latin letter V. Stick the sea from light blue paper, individual waves on top from green. Lamb - from white. And to complete your marine fantasy in the form of crafts on the summer theme, of course, a sailboat. And a golden strip of shore at the very bottom of the sheet. Great, right? Put in a frame on a bookshelf and admire the joint creativity!

Hello forest!



Application on a forest theme is another summer story. Cut the dark green silhouettes of triangles of Christmas trees of different sizes. Between them, paste the trees with curly crowns, with greens of a lighter shade. Do not forget about the grass, flowers and berries. You can depict low bushes with sparkles of berries, a fun family of mushrooms. Or just create an applique, on which there will be a bouquet of flowers, a branch with cherries or cherries, red tomatoes and perky green cucumbers, elegant butterflies.

The main thing is that summer will come to life in your work, and you will be happy to meet him.
