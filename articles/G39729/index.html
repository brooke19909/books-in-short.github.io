How to sew a corset with your own hands beautifully?

Corsets have always been considered only underwear for women, which serves to raise and hold the chest in the desired shape, as well as to tighten the waist. It should also be noted that in order to ensure harmony in the Middle Ages it was necessary to tighten five of them. Young ladies were dragging them so much that they could hardly breathe (no matter what you go for your own beauty!). But times changed, fashion also, so corsets gradually weakened, and subsequently the girl could calmly tighten it alone, without the support of her parents or friends.

The destination itself has also changed. If earlier it was exclusively underwear, then modern corsets are urged to decorate the female body and make it as sexy as possible. Precisely because such a robe has become very popular, we offer you ways to sew a corset with your own hands. Be sure that the husband / young man will certainly appreciate this desire, because in this outfit you will look not only slim, but also damn sexy.



Before describing the entire manufacturing process, we note one important detail: the corset can be sewn in different ways depending on the era that you want to recreate. Do not be surprised, because the modern choice of these products is amazing. Moreover, they have long been radically different from each other, not only in style, but also in patterns, certain details, as well as drawings and the shape of the bust. This fact must be considered before starting work.



Having decided on the style, we move on to how to sew a corset with your own hands and what materials will be needed for this. It should be noted that the outfit is usually sewn one or two sizes smaller than your own. This is what allows you to achieve the effect of the most slender waist. The pattern needs to be done taking into account your parameters in full size, after which you can begin the manufacturing process. Choose the fabric you like. Remember that a corset sewn with your own hands will be really good only if you pick up dense material that will not stretch or diverge at the seams. In addition to good and durable material, you will need scissors, the pattern itself, pins and, of course, a sewing machine.

Now we will describe how to sew a corset with your own hands. The process, in principle, is quite commonplace. Next, try on a corset. If he sits well and you are satisfied with the intermediate result, then you can proceed to the processing of the wings (places for inserting stones and lacing). Be careful, because they should be located evenly and not too tighten the matter. From below, they should be well-wired, and from above they should be slightly ajar so that their location can be corrected.

When the places for the backstage are stitched, we insert the bones, lacing and again we go to measure the corset. Only this time it is necessary to tighten it and see if it pulls well enough.



If you are satisfied with the result, then you can already stitch the product to the end. To do this, we fasten lacing, drawstrings and bones using machine lines. Do not forget that completely suturing the bones is not recommended (very often they must be pulled out during machine wash).
