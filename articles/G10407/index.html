Is it worth it to relax in Goa in February?

The Indian state of Goa attracts travelers from all over the world with breathtaking landscapes, a warm ocean, vibrant tropical vegetation and unique local flavor. As in many other places of rest, there are months more or less favorable for rest. Does it make sense to go to Goa in February, the winter month when the body already needs rest, and is still a long way from next summer? Let's take a closer look.





When is Goa holiday season?

The climatic conditions here are such that the whole year is conditionally divided into two large periods - the rainy season and the dry season. Rains and high humidity begin in October and last inclusively through March. This is not the best time to relax in Goa. In the daytime, the temperature often exceeds +40 degrees. Such heat, combined with high humidity, is difficult to tolerate; being here during this period is completely uncomfortable. Bathing will not bring pleasure either, because the ocean is very restless during the rainy season, dangerous by huge waves and undercurrents.

By November, the rains subside, and the holiday season starts, which lasts until the end of April. It was at this time that many tourists rushed to Goa. Especially a lot of holidaymakers here in the New Year and Christmas holidays - holiday prices at this time reach their peak, and resorts are crowded with holidaymakers. But tours to Goa in February cost tourists 15-20% cheaper than similar offers in the period December-January, because after the New Year influx of vacationers it becomes less. Not only the tour itself will cost cheaper, but also rest on the spot: prices in February for souvenirs, clothes, transportation, rental housing in Goa are lower than in other periods.





February is a time favorable for diving, visiting excursions to nature reserves and historical sights and, of course, beach vacations. This month there is an optimal combination of day and night air temperature, as well as water temperature.

Weather in Goa in February

In the last winter month, noticeable differences are observed between daytime and nighttime temperatures. And this is good, because after the heat of the day comes the long-awaited evening and night coolness. The average temperature here during the day is around + 31 ¬∞ C and rarely exceeds + 35 ¬∞ C. At night, the temperature is about 10 degrees lower, so evening and night walks, visiting night markets will be comfortable. Summer heat is perceived quite easily, thanks to a refreshing light breeze from the ocean. The sky in Goa in February is clear, there is no cloud cover, and there is no rain. There are no cloudy days.

For relaxation you will need light summer things. And perhaps the only jacket and jeans should be taken in case of a cool evening, although, most likely, they will not be needed. The February weather in Goa is surprisingly stable and almost never brings vacationers unpleasant surprises.





February water temperature

Beach lovers can safely choose Goa for their holidays in the last winter month. There are practically no big waves at this time, you can swim for a long time and with comfort. The same applies to children for whom swimming will be completely safe. The water is very pleasant and warm. Its average temperature is + 27-28 ¬∞ C. Those who like not to get out of the water for hours, swimming in Goa in February will bring maximum pleasure.

Rainfall and humidity

Statistics show that February is the driest of all months. That is, the probability that the rain will catch you on vacation is negligible. Relative humidity this month is about 69-70%, which is not much by local standards. For the entire season, this is the lowest humidity. Meteorologists believe that in all weather conditions, a vacation in Goa in February is ideal, that this time is better suited for relaxation than any other.





It is interesting to know that at the end of February Goa traditionally hosts the annual carnival - a vivid and memorable sight. It includes theatrical performances, colorful and noisy street processions, incendiary dances. Those who will be there at this time should definitely attend this event. In all respects, a vacation to Goa in February promises to be extremely successful. If you diversify pastime on the beach with walks to local attractions, excursions and shopping, you can have a great vacation here.
