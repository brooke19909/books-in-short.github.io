Hotel Pafian Park Hotel Apts (Cyprus / Paphos), Travel Photos and Reviews

The family-run Pafian Park Hotel Apts is built on the Mediterranean coast, on Chloraka Beach, in the popular Cyprus resort of Paphos, half an hour from the airport. It appears under the name Pafian Park Hotel-Apartments 4. Formerly it was called Sentido Pafian Park Hotel. In 2011, the guesthouse was completely renovated, and now it is a renovated, modern and welcoming tourist center with a rich internal infrastructure.





Decorated in a rustic style, the Pafian Park Hotel Apts is one main building and 19 two-story buildings. The hotel has professional service and Russian-speaking staff. Within walking distance are a bus stop, night clubs, restaurants, shopping and souvenir shops. Also nearby are the Lighthouse, Port and the Archaeological Site.

The cozy guesthouse offers guests quality service, a relaxed holiday and a relaxing atmosphere at affordable prices. The price of the ticket includes insurance, transfer, meals. Pafian Park Hotel Apts 4 is focused primarily on family vacations with children, business and youth.

Number of rooms

Guests are offered comfortable rooms (144) of various categories and levels. The windows of the apartments offer a picturesque panorama of the blue sea or green territory. The rooms have extra beds - folding beds.





The rooms have a well-equipped kitchen area with a refrigerator, coffee makers, dishes, a kettle, and a toaster. For private use there will be a bathroom with a mirror and a hairdryer. All necessary hygiene products are provided free of charge.

For a surcharge, you can use the safe and the Internet. It also has a color satellite TV and CD player. Daily maid service is provided.

Additional Information

Exciting performances and vibrant animation received rave reviews. Pafian Park Hotel Apts does everything for its customers to make their stay even more beautiful and interesting. Sporting events, contests and shows for children in Russian are held throughout the day.

Children can play at the kids club, splash around in the safe pool or have fun in the playground. The restaurant is specially designed for kids with high chairs and a separate menu.

Digest

Warm sea, golden beaches, pleasant climate and first-class amenities await you at Pafian Park Hotel Apts. The hotel is visited by tourists of different age groups: young people, couples in love and adults who want to spend an unforgettable romantic weekend in a secluded paradise.

Here you can spend all day indulging in a leisurely and pacifying vacation on a sandy beach, traveling around the city, studying culture, or doing active sports: surfing, underwater hunting, diving, fishing. In a country with a developed tourism industry, you will spend your vacation in a special atmosphere of hospitality, enjoying the traditions and customs of exotic Cyprus.
