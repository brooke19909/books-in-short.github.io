Detailed description

Vacationers of the boarding house can afford to go to Anapa every evening and enjoy all sorts of resort entertainment. But there are no such beaches and such a sea as in Dzhemete anywhere else. "Dzhemete" - a recreation center near the sea - offers its guests comfortable conditions for an unforgettable vacation.

The territory of the boarding house looks especially picturesque. It is simply buried in the greenery of deciduous and coniferous trees and fragrant flowers. Near each building there are separate arbors in which you can spend time in the fresh air and enjoy the surrounding beauty.

Dzhemete - a recreation center near the sea - has two outdoor pools with fresh water. One for adults, the other for children. Near each pool there are plastic sun loungers.

Children of any age will feel great here. For kids, you can rent a stroller, playpen or crib. Older children - rollers or a scooter. Qualified educators are ready to provide their services and engage with children throughout the day.

For adults, their own entertainment is provided. You can visit the Finnish sauna, swim in the pool, book a massage course, play table tennis or volleyball. Near the pool there is a bar. All guests of the pension can use the Internet for free.

Infrastructure

Pension "Dzhemete" has a fairly developed infrastructure, which is essential for a comfortable stay and good mood. In the pension there are saunas and baths. The services of the bath complex are paid, but the prices are quite reasonable. But the pools can be used at any time absolutely free.
