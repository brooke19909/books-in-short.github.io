The recreation center "Golden Bay" (Pitsunda): description, reviews of vacationers

The resort town of Pitsunda is a cozy, picturesque corner on the Black Sea in Abkhazia, in a country that was created for recreation. The uniqueness of Pitsunda is that this paradise combines the cleanest sea, warm sun, beautiful mountains, gorgeous beaches and magnificent relic pines grow right at the very water, filling the air with coniferous aroma.

The combination of such natural factors favorably affects human health, resting Pitsunda get rid of nervous stress, restore the body's strength and build up immunity. If you want to relax in a quiet picturesque place and restore your health, you will find amazing Pitsunda.

Recreation: Golden Bay

Abkhazia is a country where you can relax even with a small budget. Five kilometers from the center of Pitsunda in a mountain gorge, among the protected forests near the mountain river Ryapsh, there is a recreation center "Golden Bay". If you prefer a budget holiday in a quiet, picturesque place, away from civilization, alone with magnificent nature, then you should pay attention to this wonderful place.





The Golden Bay (Pitsunda) has a landscaped area that is buried in the greenery of the park. Everywhere flower beds, malls and benches are shattered.

The recreation center is a complex consisting of eighteen summer houses and a two-story new building.

On the territory of the recreation center "Golden Bay" (Pitsunda) there is a cozy dining room where you can order full three meals a day. The spacious dining room of the dining room is equipped with a large TV and air conditioning.

Accommodation for vacationers

At the same time, the recreation center "Golden Bay" (Pitsunda) can take about two hundred people vacationing. In one-story summer houses, economy rooms are for rent. Shower and toilet in such rooms are common for several rooms (from two to four). It is very convenient to rent a house for a large friendly company or for a family.





Each room is equipped with necessary furniture and a fan.

One of the eighteen one-story houses for rent standard rooms, with amenities. There are seven such numbers.

In two-story cottages - superior rooms standard +. In addition to shower and toilet, these rooms have TVs and air conditioning.

Holiday Prices

Prices for accommodation at the recreation center are very affordable. So, for example, placing one person in an economy room will cost from 900 to 1400 rubles per day, depending on the season. A standard room with amenities costs from 1000 to 1680 rubles per day, and a standard + room will cost vacationers from 1200 to 1950 rubles. The price of rest includes full three meals a day in the dining room of the boarding house.

Children at the recreation center "Golden Bay" (Pitsunda) are given discounts on accommodation, depending on age, they range up to 40%.

The infrastructure of the recreation center

On the territory of the recreation center there are many places for leisure activities. Near each house there are places for relaxation, benches, places for cooking barbecue.

There is a volleyball court on site, an equipped football field, and a basketball court. Ping-pong tables are available for table tennis lovers.

There is a small billiard room, equipped playground with a sandbox. On the meringue, bicycles are available for rent on which you can ride and enjoy the local beauties.

The beach is fifty meters from the recreation center. The beach is spacious, pebbled and very picturesque.





The recreation center has its own store, also within walking distance of a small market, several cafes and a restaurant of Abkhaz cuisine, which is located in a neighboring boarding house.

The surroundings of Pitsunda are full of attractions. The fabulous nature of Abkhazia will not leave indifferent any vacationer. Crystal clear lakes, noisy waterfalls and mountain streams, gorges and mysterious caves attract tourists. Local tour desks organize excursions to local beauties.

Guest reviews

The Golden Bay (Pitsunda), reviews of which are rather controversial, is, first of all, a budget vacation spot. Tourists, accustomed to foreign resorts and comfortable accommodation, leave negative reviews about this place. Yes, there is no developed infrastructure, expensive restaurants and nightclubs, so nightlife lovers will not like the rest in the Golden Bay.

However, as they say, to each his own. Those vacationers who knew where they were going leave rave reviews from staying in this wild place.





Tourists talk about the cordiality of the staff and adequate hosts. About food in the dining room of the boarding house reviews are mostly all positive. The cuisine is Russian, European and local. The dishes are very diverse, the portions are large, they serve meat, fish and fruit.

Regarding economy rooms, guests respond differently. There are complaints about the inconvenience of beds and poor quality plumbing. Other vacationers talk about cleanliness and comfort.

Based on the reviews of vacationers, we can make an unambiguous conclusion - this is an inexpensive recreation center, the charm of which is in its silence, location and simplicity. This fabulous place is perfect for large friendly companies, as well as for families with children. If you want to relax from the noise of the city, to be alone with the beautiful nature and enjoy the mountain and sea beauties, then here - Pitsunda, Golden Bay (recreation center) will welcome you cordially!
