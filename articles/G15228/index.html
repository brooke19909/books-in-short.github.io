Relax at Atlantis Resort, Cyprus

Description. Cyprus is one of the most beautiful and sought-after holiday destinations in the eastern part of the Mediterranean basin. According to legend, the island of Aphrodite is considered a place of love. It has unique natural conditions, which allows tourists to relax here all year round. As with any popular resort, Cyprus has many different hotels. Let's talk about one of them - Atlantis Resort. Cyprus is a place where you can always have a good rest not only with your body, but also with your soul. And in this hotel you feel it especially strongly. The unusual and alluring atmosphere of the island adds mystery to it. If we talk about the Atlantis Resort, Cyprus has allocated for him, one might say, the most wonderful place.





The rooms. This four-star hotel was built in 2001, and since then a huge number of tourists have been there. For guests, the hotel has 280 rooms designed for any material and social status. Have you decided to have a good rest at a reasonable price? You can find everything you need in Atlantis Resort 4 * - Cyprus will open here in all its splendor! The rooms are equipped with all necessary equipment. Balcony or terrace, air conditioning, kitchen, refrigerator, minibar, satellite TV, telephone and jacuzzi - all at your service.

Food . The cuisine of the Cypriots is truly amazing. This is a kind of mix of cuisines of different nations. The local population does not imagine cooking without a grill. The hotel has the following power systems: half board, breakfast only, full board. Local alcoholic drinks and soft drinks are free, imported drinks and freshly squeezed juices can be borrowed for a fee.

Beach. Is Atlantis Resort far from the sea? Cyprus is a small island, and everything here is in relative accessibility. The hotel you are looking for is located 45 kilometers from the airport of Larnaca and just 100 meters from the beautiful beach. If you decide to spend a day by the sea, you can use umbrellas and sun loungers.





Information for vacationers. For lovers of outdoor activities the hotel has a gym, tennis courts, table tennis tables. You can also play basketball and beach volleyball. There is a water sports center, so adventure lovers have the opportunity to go diving. Professionals work at the center, and if you have never tried scuba diving before, you will be trained in this.

Atlantis Resort rooms are cleaned on all days except Sundays. Cyprus is, of course, the cleanest sea, however, the hotel has two pools. If traveling with children, a babysitter is available for a moderate fee. There is a children's pool, playground, cot in the room.

You can use the Internet (for a fee) at the Atlantis Resort Class. Cyprus is famous for its Internet cafes, which are here at every step.



Digest reviews. The comments that tourists leave on the hotel website speak for themselves. They are happy with their stay and recommend travelers to go here. As can be seen in the example of one Atlantis Resort hotel, Cyprus offers many services for a comfortable and complete stay. Visit the Island of Love, and you will be delighted with its beauty and grandeur!
