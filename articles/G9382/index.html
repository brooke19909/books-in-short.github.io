How to get to Tunisia?

The Tunisian government is very interested in attracting large flows of Russian tourists, therefore, an agreement on visa-free entry (for a period of 90 days) of tourists from Russia to the territory of Tunisia was signed between the two states. For several years now direct regular flights have been made from Moscow to the capital of this African country. In the summer, there are charter flights to other cities of Tunisia. If you bought a tour to the Chiraz Thalasso 3 * hotel, then it is most convenient for you to go either to Monastir or Enfid. The flight duration from Moscow to Tunisia is just over 4 hours. Both airports are approximately the same distance from the Skanes resort. It is here that on the first beach line is Chiraz Thalasso Resort 3 *. Usually, the price of the tour includes a transfer, and tourists are met by the hotel staff upon arrival and comfortably delivered to the hotel complex. Hotel Chiraz Thalasso 3 *: general description

The three-story building of the hotel was built in 1995. By the way, for Tunisia this is a pretty young hotel. After all, most of the hotels on the local coast were opened in the seventies of the last century and have never undergone reconstruction or modernization. Until June 2013, the hotel belonged to the Prime Life hotel chain and was called Prima Life Skanes 4 *, Chiraz Club. However, today it is simply called Chiraz Thalasso 3 *, and not 4, but 3 stars flaunt over the facade. It is likely that recently the service at the hotel has ceased to correspond to the level of this brand, and they have stopped working with it. However, this establishment at first glance leaves the impression of a quite respectable hotel complex: a vast green area, several beautiful swimming pools (adults and children) with sea water and a mini water park, tennis courts, restaurants, cafes and bars, as well as its own comfortable beach with a full range beach and water activities.



Number of rooms

The Shiraz Talasso Hotel has 334 rooms of two categories: family and standard (double and triple). Bathrooms are fitted with a shower, toiletries and a hairdryer. The rooms have air conditioning and a refrigerator, safe (for a fee), satellite TV. Many rooms overlook the sea and the hotel grounds.

Hotel Facilities

Since the Shiraz Talasso hotel has a well-equipped conference room, various conferences, seminars, and forms often take place here. In addition, the hotel has a SPA center, where, for a fee, various thalassotherapy procedures are performed, as well as a sauna and a hammam, gym, fitness club. The hotel has a mini market, laundry and dry cleaning, a beauty salon , doctor services, currency exchange, car parking, etc.



Entertainment

As already noted above, the hotel has pools and water slides, so this hotel is also called Chiraz Aquapark Thalasso 3 *. For sports enthusiasts, there are 4 tennis courts, a football, basketball, volleyball field, billiards and table tennis, chess, darts, etc. In the evenings, the hotel hosts fun animations for both adults and children in French and Italian languages. Unfortunately, there are practically no Russian-speaking employees in hotels in Tunisia .
