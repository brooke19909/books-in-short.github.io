Hotel "Murom Estate": address, description of rooms, reviews

The ancient city of Murom has always attracted many tourists who want to touch Russian history. You can also get acquainted with national traditions by staying at the colorful Hotel Murom Estate, located on the outskirts of the city. Here you will feel in a real Russian fairy tale. At the same time, the guests are provided with the most comfortable living conditions.

Description

Under the picturesque hill on which the Murom Manor hotel complex is built, the flowing beauty Oka flows. Sitting on the terrace of wooden towers, you can admire the picturesque distances that are part of the untouched historical landscape.

Holy Trinity Church is located within walking distance, and to reach the Transfiguration Monastery, you will need to overcome about three kilometers.

The hotel consists of two chopped houses. It has its own restaurant, which serves Russian cuisine. The common areas have free Wi-Fi access. Tourists can leave their vehicles in a free guarded parking lot. There are picnic areas and barbecue facilities. There is a playground for children, there is a library. Guests can swim in the indoor pool, relax in the sauna. This is a great place for fishing enthusiasts.

The hotel "Murom Estate" is located at: st. Priokskaya, 77 in the Karacharovo microdistrict of the city of Murom.

Guest Accommodation

A large chopped three-story house is designed to accommodate 24 people and consists of twelve rooms. In a small two-story chopped house for guests, three rooms are designed for seven people. Guests at the Murom Manor are offered rooms in the following categories:

  • two-room family triple with one large double and one single bed - 6000 rubles per day on weekdays and 6500 rubles on holidays and weekends;
  • two-room family quadruple with one large double and two single beds - 6000 rubles per day on weekdays and 6500 rubles on holidays and weekends;
  • junior suites with two single beds - respectively 4,000 rubles and 4,500 rubles per day;
  • junior suites with a large double bed and sofa - respectively 4,500 rubles and 5,000 rubles per day;
  • Junior suites with one double bed - 4,500 rubles per day on weekdays and 5,000 rubles on holidays and weekends;
  • luxury rooms with a large double bed and a sofa - 5500 rubles on weekdays and 6000 rubles on weekends and holidays.

Room rates include breakfast. If tourists want to book the whole complex at once, then on weekdays its cost will cost 67,000 rubles, and on weekends and holidays - 74,000 rubles.

Terms of Accommodation

In order to avoid any misunderstandings during the check-in at the Murom Usadba Hotel (Murom), you must familiarize yourself with the accommodation conditions in advance:

  • Children are accepted at any age.
  • Up to the age of 12, two children can stay for free, provided they are accommodated in existing beds.
  • If an extra bed is required for a child under 12 years old, then an additional charge of 1000 rubles per night will be charged.
  • Additional accommodation for adults and older children will also cost 1000 rubles per night.
  • To provide extra beds, a preliminary request is required, as well as confirmation by the hotel that this service will be available.
  • Paid additional services are calculated separately from the room price.
  • By prior arrangement, pets are allowed in the hotel (paid service).
