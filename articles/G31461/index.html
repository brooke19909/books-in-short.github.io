Paradise Beach 4 * (Sveti Vlas, Bulgaria): hotel description, services, reviews

Paradise Beach Hotel (Bulgaria) is located in a beautiful and picturesque place that cannot but please the eye. Here the guest is offered all the necessary living conditions, as well as entertainment for every taste and age. The complex is located in the bay, where you can retire and enjoy the silence. The conducive atmosphere will help you to fully relax and remember this vacation for a long time.

Infrastructure





The hotel complex includes four buildings in which rooms for guests are located, several restaurants and bars, a large pool. Guests loved the panoramic elevators overlooking the sandy beach. Paradise Beach Hotel (Bulgaria) offers a huge range of all kinds of procedures and entertainment. Thus, you can do tennis, aerobics, fitness. More extreme activities are offered, such as skydiving, windsurfing. And lovers of more passive relaxation can visit the rejuvenation, relaxing massage treatments, sauna and Turkish bath.

For children

Hotel Paradise Beach 4 * (Bulgaria) did not forget about its little guests. In this place, the child will definitely not be bored; leisure is well organized. A team of professional animators works with the children, who are ready to give the kids joy at any moment. The hotel also hosts various theme events, discos and beach parties. Some customers can play their wedding here and order an away ceremony.





Hotel location

The location of this establishment is very advantageous. There is a hotel in a picturesque corner of a sandy bay. The only problem is that it is far from the airport. Therefore, after landing the aircraft will have to overcome a path of 30 km.

Room Options

Vacationers are offered double and triple rooms. If guests come as a large family, then they can move into a special spacious family room, where it will be convenient for each member of a large company. The rooms are stylishly decorated, and a nice addition is the presence in each of them of a balcony with a wonderful sea view. The room has a whole set of necessary furniture, as well as a bright and spacious bathroom. A satellite TV is also included. Children will be able to watch cartoons at any time.

Places to Eat

On the territory of the hotel Paradise Beach 4 * (Bulgaria, St. Vlas) is a restaurant Panorama. This is a wonderful place with a magnificent view of the beach and the city.





Visitors can not only enjoy delicious food, but also please the eye. The food here is delicious and the beauty of the panoramic view is impressive. A visit to such an institution will be remembered for a long time.

There is also a tavern on site. Here you can have a tasty snack, and just visit such an atmospheric place is a must. The lobby bar is designed for those who are in no hurry and loves to enjoy really delicious coffee.

Also on the beach there is a small bar where you can try a variety of cocktails.
The hotel has an indoor pool. The nice thing is that it has a special section for children. The water in this zone is warmer than in the adult pool, so the child will definitely not get sick.

Another great service is that the hotel takes care of business people who have come not only to relax. Here you can arrange business negotiations, various kinds of conferences. For these purposes, a special room has been equipped where you can comfortably discuss all the necessary issues and carry out the planned activities.

Hotel Paradise Beach St. Vlas 4 * (Bulgaria) is a wonderful choice, because there is everything you need for a good rest and even work. This is confirmed by numerous reviews of vacationers.





Guest Comments

Rest in this hotel does not bring disappointment to anyone. As for the rooms, it is necessary to choose between spacious apartments with 2 rooms or small triple rooms, but with a picturesque view of the sea. If necessary, reserved seats can be replaced. Everything for the convenience of customers has been done at the Paradise Beach 4 * hotel.

Location Reviews

The most successful buildings for settlement are 3 and 4. In the first building, the view is not very picturesque, the 5 building overlooks the sea and the pool, so in the evenings it can be noisy. Day and night near the pool entertainers are entertainers, music plays loudly.

The hotel is not new. But both the territory and the rooms are clean and comfortable. The beds in the apartments are not very high, the mattresses are new and comfortable. Sleeping is very comfortable. There are extra pillows in the closet. It is convenient that there are bedside tables, a small wardrobe. Each room has a safe, but its use is paid. The TV is on the table with two spacious niches. It is old and catches only a few channels. But on vacation this is not the main thing.

It is disappointing that the rooms do not have refrigerators. On the balconies there is plastic furniture and a clothes dryer. Air conditioning works fine. Insects in the rooms are not observed.

The bathroom is not large. It contains a sink, a bathtub and a bathroom. Toiletries are available. You can also use shower caps, cotton pads and chopsticks. For each guest, 4 small and large towels are provided. There is a hairdryer, but of an old model. Better bring your own.

If there are problems with water or sewage, you must contact the reception. Specialists will quickly fix it.

Internet is available for free only at the reception and in the lobby bar. The hotel itself has an exchange office and a minimarket on the second floor.

Nutrition

The food at the Paradise Beach 4 * hotel (Bulgaria, Sveti Vlas) is very tasty. The food is varied and nutritious. But almost all tourists recommend not ordering food on an all-inclusive basis. After a while, the food may get bored, but since everything has already been paid for, you will have to go to the dining room. It is best to order breakfast only, and during the day have a snack in restaurants and cafes. The hotel's restaurant serves meat and fish dishes every day. Sometimes you can enjoy seafood. Huge selection of salads, vegetable dishes, side dishes, appetizers and sauces. Very tasty breakfasts (scrambled eggs, pancakes with sweet sauces, cheeses and sausages, and even pies). Dairy products and coffee are poor. Of the fruits, watermelons, oranges, cherries and grapefruits are especially tasty.





The restaurant itself is in a very interesting place. Seagulls are constantly circling around, trying to steal something from the table. The staff drives them, as there are special places for birds where they are fed.

Beach vacation

A panoramic elevator delivers tourists to the beach. This is a real miracle. Not only does the hotel offer spectacular views of the mountains and the sea, it also looks very advantageous from the bottom due to the elevator. Four huge elevators deliver guests to the beach and back without any problems. There are practically no queues. There are many sun loungers on the beach, you can not borrow in advance. Beach towels are changed several times a day. Sunset at sea gentle.

On the beach there are all kinds of water activities.

Where to go on excursions?

For walks, you can choose the neighboring resort of Elenite. There are many streets with shops, cozy cafes, exchangers, attractions and tour desks. You can also visit the water park located within the Royal Castle.

On the way to Elenite there are several eateries with seafood delicacies. You can also go on an excursion to the town of Nessebar on a ship that leaves from the pier three times a day.

Near the hotel there is a bus stop with buses to Elenite and Nessebar. The schedule hangs at a stop. You can go to the resort Sunny Beach for sightseeing. St. Vlas can also surprise you with interesting places to visit. Not too curious tourists may not even leave its borders in search of new adventures.

From a stop near the hotel, a bus leaves every 15 minutes to the water park in Sunny Beach. You can also visit the cheaper water park in Nessebar.





The hotel offers several types of excursions. Tourists especially recommend going on a day trip to Istanbul. The tour includes visits to the Bosphorus, a leather factory and many other interesting places in the city. Meals are included. But it is better to take lunch at the reception at the hotel.

Also, vacationers are advised to go on an outdoor fishing trip. You don‚Äôt have to worry, you are guaranteed a good catch. In addition, the whole group is taken to a wonderful restaurant for lunch. Here you will be fed incredibly delicious seafood and drink aromatic wine. Moreover, such trips provide a great opportunity to meet new people. You can always make new friends in an interest group.

Tourists especially praise the trip to the Rila Monastery, the Bulgarian village and the Valley of Roses.

General impression





St. Vlas delights its guests with its warm beaches. There are many cozy hotels, one of which is Paradise Beach 4 *. This is a great place to accommodate tourists with an average income. The hotel deserves its stars. The rooms are in good condition, the service is excellent, the food is tasty and varied. The large pool is equipped with a children's area, so the kids will not be bored here. Until late in the evening, entertainers are entertained by funny animators. The hotel contingent is different. Here you can meet young people, family people with children, and tourists of advanced age.

The beach area is very comfortable. You should not even bother going to the beach. An elevator with an unrivaled panoramic view brings guests to it. Sunset at sea is very convenient for families with children. Entertainment on the beach is also enough.

The hotel is located in a picturesque place, so almost all rooms offer gorgeous views. The rooms themselves are equipped with good furniture. Cleaning, linen and towels are carried out regularly.

Meals are included in the price. The products are always fresh and well cooked.

Of the minor minuses can be noted the presence of an old TV and hairdryer. Some rooms are noisy due to on-site programs. If you take food on an "all inclusive" system, then in a few days such food can get bored. Internet in the rooms does not catch. There are not enough hangers in the cabinets. The rooms do not have a refrigerator and a minibar. Some rooms do not have a very scenic view.

In general, the hotel can be recommended to different categories of tourists with an average level of income.
