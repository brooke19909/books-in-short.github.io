Terms of Accommodation

Rooms at the Royalton Punta Cana 5 * hotel need to be booked in advance, as the place is quite popular and there will simply not be any rooms available at the right time.

Check-in is allowed from 15:00 to 23:30. And departure strictly 12: 00-12: 30. You can live with children of any age. Moreover, if a child under 12 years old does not sleep in a separate bed, then only 50% of the room price will be charged. Extra beds are not available. But pets are strictly prohibited, even a separate fee will not help.

When booking 6 rooms or more, certain conditions apply and additional funds may be charged that are debited three business days after the reservation. Therefore, it is important that the required amount is on the payment card.

Service at Royalton Punta Cana

The hotel service is excellent. The staff always makes concessions to guests and tries to help in resolving any issue. As a rule, at the reception guests are offered a wide variety of services. For example, guests can contact the tour desk to help you choose the right destination for your vacation. Moreover, the hotel is responsible for the organization. However, for a fee.

The rooms are wet-cleaned daily. At the same time, towels, bed linen. However, if the client does not want to clean the room, this must be warned in advance.

Territory

The hotel tourists relaxed comfortably. Indeed, in addition to restaurants on the territory there are many amenities. For example, free Wi-Fi, a swimming pool, parking and a bar where you can spend the evening leisure. There is even a room for spa treatments.

Vacation with children

Many tourists come to rest with children of different ages. And not in vain. Indeed, next to the Royalton Punta Cana 5 * hotel there are playgrounds. And the hotel has all the conditions for relaxing with children. Kids are allowed to swim in the pool with adults. Yes, and the hotel has a mini water park for older children.

In addition, the Bavaro Adventure Park has fun with the whole family. There is excellent staff service, a clean beach, a beautiful small water park, trains for kids, bungees for adult children, ATVs for teens.

So, children of any age will enjoy this cozy park. True, the weekend is very crowded and all the rides are too long queues.

On the territory of the hotel there is an additional service - babysitting for children. The ticket price is not included, but paid separately.

For adults there is a business room and a banquet room. They are paid separately.
