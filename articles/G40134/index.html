Hotel Klas Dom Suite Annexe 4 * (Turkey, Alanya): description and reviews of tourists

Cozy and calm is a small resort in Turkey - Alanya, which is located at the foot of the Toros Mountains, among many citrus trees and palm trees. The old town with narrow streets and colorful shops, many cafes and restaurants will appeal to every traveler who finds himself here.

This is indeed a place for a family vacation, because the shallow sea is quite warm and safe for young children. Rapid heating of water is carried out due to the remote location from the cold currents of the Mediterranean and Aegean Seas. Close proximity to the mountain ranges protect the town from the cold north wind. Here is a network of hotels and entertainment Klas Hotel.





Description of the hotel complex

One of the relatively small but comfortable hotels in the Mahmutlar district, which is rapidly growing and has a well-developed infrastructure, is the Klas Dom Suite Annexe 4 *. Its description will be presented to your attention in the article.

This is a six-story building, the first floor of which is occupied by street cafes and shops, and the other five are housing for tourists. It was built in 2000, the last restoration was carried out in 2009. The total area is 3,000 square meters.

Nearby are the vegetable and clothing market, currency exchange points, pharmacies and banks. For the comfort of holidaymakers, the hotel is equipped with an elevator. On the territory are:

  • Lobby bar;
  • a restaurant;
  • open pool.

Hotel Rooms

The hotel Klas Dom Suite Annexe 4 * (Turkey, Alanya) has 45 rooms. A little more than half - overlooking the waters of the Mediterranean Sea, and from the remaining unbelievable mountain landscapes. The apartments are all of a standard class and consist of two rooms connected by a door - a bedroom and a living room. Exit to the balcony allows you to enjoy the amazing sunset and meet the early dawn in the fresh air, without leaving the hotel complex.

The interior of the rooms is modest, there is no clear style, but this does not interfere with the rest. All chambers are equipped with air conditioning, satellite TV and a small kitchen. The bathroom is equipped with a telephone, hairdryer, shower or bath. The furniture of the rooms is simple, but there is everything you need: beds (two single or one double), a folding sofa and armchairs, bedside tables, a wardrobe, a mirror, a table and chairs. Flooring - tiles.

Guests note that the rooms, although not luxurious, are clean and comfortable. Cleaning is carried out regularly and thoroughly. The hotel has all the conditions for a comfortable pastime of tourists.





Features of settlement and provision of services

When checking in, all guests living in the Ultra All Inclusive system are worn a bracelet at the reception. It will indicate that this client has some freedom of action. So, vacationers are allowed to use the entertainment of the nearby hotel of this chain Klas Hotel Dom.

The ability to use the services of the main building, located 250 meters from a permanent place of residence, which allows holidaymakers to diversify their leisure time. Wi-Fi throughout is included in the price.

Please note that check-in with pets is strictly prohibited.

Ultra All Inclusive Nutrition Concept

Meals, as in the vast majority of hotels in Turkey, are provided here according to the buffet system. A number of locally produced alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks are provided free of charge around the clock. Some imported alcoholic beverages, freshly squeezed fresh juices, ice cream and Turkish coffee are paid extra. A minibar in the room is also a paid service.

Eating takes place in an open restaurant, designed for 150 seats, and it is located in the main building. Location - near the pool. According to guest reviews, the menu does not have enough meat dishes, but the rest of the food is tasty and served in an acceptable amount. There is a bar on the beach, also included in the Ultra All Inclusive system.
