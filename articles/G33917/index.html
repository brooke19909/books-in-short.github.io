"Sochi-Breeze Hotel": description, reviews

The Sochi Breeze Hotel is located in the very center of the famous resort of Sochi. This is an ideal place for those who like a holiday by the sea and at the same time want to enjoy all the benefits of urban infrastructure. The hotel welcomes guests all year round.

Description

From the three-star Sochi-Breeze Hotel to the sea coast is only 300 meters, or 5 minutes walk. Guests can sunbathe on the equipped pebble beach. The hotel has its own medical base. In the daytime, in extreme heat, it features a swimming outdoor pool with sun loungers on site. You can work out in the gym. Children can spend their free time in the playroom, where the teacher will watch them. Also for young tourists the hotel has animators. Secure parking is available at an additional cost. For vacationers, several types of permits are offered, from standard to the all-inclusive category. Meals are organized in the hotel restaurant. For guests' convenience, an on-site ATM machine is installed. By prior arrangement, pets are allowed in the hotel. The nearest attractions include the unique Arboretum Park and the Sochi Circus. In the evenings near the Sochi Breeze (see the hotel photo below), beautiful lighting, guests can relax on benches surrounded by well-groomed trees, take a walk along the city streets. The hotel is located at the address: Kurortny prospect, 72.

Accommodation for vacationers

To accommodate guests, the hotel offers several categories of rooms, the price of which includes accommodation, wellness and meals, depending on the chosen package.

  • double standard - from 3000 to 5000 rubles per day;
  • double standard superior - from 4000 to 5200 rubles per day;
  • Voyage Suite - from 4300 to 6300 rubles per day;
  • prima suite number - from 4,500 to 6,500 rubles per day;
  • lux-breeze category number - from 5100 to 7100 rubles per day;
  • Business Suite - 8,200 rubles per day;
  • junior suite - from 4700 to 6700 rubles per day;
  • panoramic apartments - from 4900 to 6900 rubles per day.

Each room has the opportunity to additionally accommodate one adult, for a child from 3 to 12 years old a fee is not charged.

Hotel Sochi Breeze. Room Amenities

Each of the 63 standard rooms is equipped with the following amenities:

  • TV with satellite channels;
  • telephone
  • air conditioning;
  • mini-bar;
  • bathroom with shower and hairdryer.

In other categories there are additional amenities:

  • The standard superior rooms (51 in total) have a radio;
  • eight two-room voyage suites and four prima suites have a living room with upholstered furniture, as well as a bathtub;
  • four business-class two-room suites with built-in Italian furniture have a living-dining room, the room is equipped with a music center, an air purifier, a bathroom with a hydromassage, and a separate bathroom for guests;
  • in the two-room breeze suite there is a living room-bedroom and study, DVD-player, jacuzzi;
  • in the three-room apartment panorama, in addition to the living room and two bedrooms, there is a kitchenette, a jacuzzi, a radio and a refrigerator, a bathroom with a bathtub;
  • in three two-room junior suites there is also an additional DVD player, a bathroom with a bathtub, a jacuzzi.

Additional services

To ensure that your stay in Sochi at the Breeze hotel is rich and memorable, a sufficient number of additional services are provided here.

  • Outdoors, there is an outdoor heated pool with a sun terrace and a bar.
  • Sports enthusiasts can also use the gym.
  • The opportunity to visit the private beach "Sunny". If the subscription is not included in the price, then payment for it is made separately.
  • The restaurant organizes meals on the menu or on the buffet. For children under seven years of age, a discount of 50% applies.
  • In the children's playroom, young tourists are under the supervision of a teacher. Babysitting services are available.
  • You can relax in the Turkish bath, massage room, visit the solarium.
  • There is a spa and wellness center.
  • The tour desk can assist with organizing interesting excursions.
  • Laundry facilities are available at an extra charge.
  • Guarded parking, paid separately.
  • Throughout the hotel there is access to free wireless internet.

SPA-center "Lotus"

Sochi-Breeze Spa Hotel in Sochi offers wellness treatments at the Lotus SPA Center. Here, several types of procedures are provided for you.

  • Restorative therapy is a bath with the addition of delicate essential flower oils and algae. The approach to the appointment is individual.
  • Thanks to the hot tubs you get a boost of energy.
  • Here, experienced massage therapists will help relaxers.
  • Cosmetology services are offered.
  • Guests can also regain their strength with a wellness shower and pearl baths.
  • There are also several steam rooms, the services of a bathhouse attendant are offered.
  • You can get a boost of vigor in contrast fonts.
  • Vitamin cocktails are prepared at the fitness bar.

sights

The main attraction, directly on the territory of which the Sochi Breeze Hotel is located, is, of course, the arboretum. It is practically an open-air museum where you can admire more than two thousand species of plants from around the world. Also, many places in the resort town that you can visit are located within walking distance from the hotel.

  • To attend a performance in the circus, it is enough to walk only 100 meters. In the large hall, designed for 2000 people, convenient viewing galleries are arranged for the audience.
  • At a distance of almost two kilometers you can visit the first Orthodox church, which was built in the Black Sea district in the 19th century - this is the Cathedral of the Archangel Michael.
  • In the evening, guests can visit the Summer Theater, which is located 500 meters from the hotel.
  • A little further is the Winter Theater.
  • The Art Museum is not only an opportunity to join the beautiful, but also to look at the main architectural monument of the resort city.
  • Also, guests can visit the literary and memorial museum of N. A. Ostrovsky, in which the staff preserved the authentic atmosphere and atmosphere of the time of residence of an outstanding writer.

  • I really liked the pool, and you can swim until 22-00, there is always a lifeguard nearby. Especially the pool helps out when there is a storm at sea.
  • Near the pool there is an excellent bar serving soft drinks, as well as delicious pizza, chicken and skewers on skewers. All this can also be ordered in the room.
  • Loved the spa treatments. It was enough and those that were included in the price, but also at the request of many paid services.

  • Guests at the Sochi Breeze Spa Hotel (photo below) especially like the opportunity to take massage treatments in the jacuzzi right in the room.
  • All unanimously note the very high-quality housekeeping.
  • The price is consistent with the quality.

Negative reviews of tourists

There are also negative reviews about some moments of relaxation at the Sochi-Breeze spa hotel.

  • In one of the rooms, the air conditioning was an old model, which was quite noisy.
  • Guests also write about shortcomings in the organization of catering .. The restaurant cannot accommodate everyone at the same time. Therefore, if you are not lucky to take a table right away, you have to either wait until the seat is vacant, or come in about 40 minutes before closing.
  • As for the quality of the prepared dishes, there were a lot of semi-finished products and a very small assortment of pastries, few fruits.
  • Also, some guests believe that the rooms have very small bathrooms, they can hardly be turned around.
  • Due to the lack of a balcony there were problems with drying towels.
  • And there were also comments that in the evenings wifi works poorly.
