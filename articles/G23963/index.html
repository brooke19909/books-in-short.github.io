Where is the best place to relax in Turkey? Hard question

If you already got bored with summer vacations in the expanses of your native country, then a trip abroad will be the best way to restore your strength. Sunny Bulgaria, diverse Ukraine, unknown Montenegro, alluring Greece or elegant Italy - each country is good in its own way. But exotic Turkey is a special corner, breathing a unique flavor.

Where is the best place to relax in Turkey? This question is often asked by travelers who decide to spend their holidays in Asia Minor, between the four seas. The Black, Aegean, Marmara and Mediterranean Seas have their own characteristics and advantages. This country will amaze you with fashionable resorts, excellent service, interesting excursions. The many-faced East is so peculiar that no one will be bored here.

The traditional Muslim state is today a civilized industrial state. It does not matter where you decide to relax in Turkey, your vacation will fly by like an instant, leaving only vivid memories.

Here, for several millennia, the West and the East, Europe and Asia, Christianity and Islam have met. The rich history left behind incredible cultural monuments. Urartians, Greeks, Hittites, Lydians, Phrygians and Romans left behind traces in the form of temples, tombs, theaters, churches, bridges, caravanserais, madrassas, mosques and palaces. Here, the past, side by side, harmoniously coexists with modernity. The lively bazaars are successfully complemented by business districts.

Before choosing where to relax in Turkey, decide what kind of vacation you need. If you want to enjoy the crystal clear sea and the beach with golden sand - Antalya, Mersin, Belek are open for you. If you are more attracted to the architectural masterpieces of the past - go to Istanbul, Bodrum, Ephesus, Pergamum, Miletus. Cappadocia will meet you with breathtaking nature and cave cities. But, of course, you can combine all types of recreation into one by ordering an extensive tour to this fabulous country.

So where is the best place to relax in Turkey? Almost throughout the state the bright sun shines all year round. The emerald sea and endless beaches, majestic pine trees and picturesque waterfalls are complemented by the legends of Troy and other ancient shrines. In addition, first-class service and absolute comfort, to which a modern traveler is used, will appeal to even very demanding tourists. The proximity of the flight and the relative cheapness speak for themselves. Therefore, it is difficult to unequivocally answer the question: where is it better to relax in Turkey?

Arriving in the hospitable east, you will be fascinated by the atmosphere of peace and serenity. The land, which remembers the rise and fall of several civilizations, holds many secrets, only slightly opens its curtain. Where is the best place to relax in Turkey? Probably in Istanbul, in a city that was the capital of three empires, which is located simultaneously on two continents. This majestic Constantinople or Constantinople has preserved more attractions than any other city in the world. Topkapa, Hagia Sophia, the harem, the Roksolana Mosque are necessarily included in the route of each city tour. And here you will find shopping and entertainment for every taste: discos, rafting, fishing, yachting, safaris, diving, paintball.
