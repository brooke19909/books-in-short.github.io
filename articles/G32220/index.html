Alva Donna World Palace 5 * (Kemer, Turkey): description of rooms, service, reviews

Kemer is one of the most famous and frequently visited resorts in Turkey. It is here that a huge number of hotels are located that are regularly recommended to their customers by Russian tour operators. One of them is Alva Donna World Palace 5 * - a hotel that offers a wide range of services and conditions for a proper stay and a memorable vacation.



Location

This resort corner is located in Turkey, on the very coast of the Mediterranean Sea, in the village of Kirish, on the border with Kemer - just six kilometers from its center. Guests living here can admire the rocky slopes of the picturesque Toros Mountains, at the foot of which Alva Donna World Palace 5 * (Turkey) was built.

Kemer has a tropical but not hot climate. In summer, there is practically no heat here - the air is humid and very pleasant.

general information

Alva Donna World Palace 5 * in Kemer (Turkey) is a hotel occupying a vast territory (about 110 thousand sq. M) near the seashore. It was built back in 1994, but since then various repairs have been carried out regularly in buildings and on the territory in order to maintain all existing buildings in proper form and improve conditions. The last repair was carried out in 2015.

The hotel has six large buildings for tourists, as well as the corresponding infrastructure, providing a wide range of entertainment.



Rooms

The entire room fund of the hotel is concentrated in huge modern buildings, each of which is a four-story white building with beautiful finishes. In total, Alva Donna World Palace 5 * (Kirish) offers travelers 816 luxury apartments, of which 658 are standard, 132 family, 18 suites, as well as 8 superior rooms for the disabled.

Each room is furnished with modern expensive furniture, they have electronics, as well as individual bathrooms. In addition to all this, throughout the entire territory (including apartments), a network of air conditioning systems has been installed, the devices of which allow guests to adjust the temperature regime based on individual preferences - with this opportunity, relaxation becomes even more pleasant.

Family

Family-type apartments are perfect for those who are going to relax with relatives, a small group of friends or with children. These are rooms consisting of two rooms, which occupy a total of 56 square meters. m. As a rule, 4 or 5 people settle here.

Guests who come to the Alva Donna World Palace 5 * hotel (Turkey / Kemer) and check into family rooms receive four full beds and one extra bed on a folding sofa. Also, if necessary, a cot can be delivered to the room. The rest of these apartments are completely reminiscent of the standard - the technical equipment is the same.

Suites

Luxury apartments are made in a classic style, there are also used a lot of wood details. Each room has a large outdoor terrace on which guests can not only relax and sunbathe, but also enjoy beautiful views of the sea coast.

The room consists of two parts: living room and sleeping area. The suites are designed to accommodate 4-5 people, so these apartments are ideal for living with the whole family. The living area has a large soft sofa and an additional TV.

The huge bathroom has all the necessary bath accessories, towels, which are replaced every day, as well as bathrobes and slippers.



Superior rooms

Designed for people with disabilities. The hotel has only eight apartments of this type and all of them are on the ground floors of the buildings. Each of them has custom-made furniture, taking into account all the possibilities of disabled people living. They also have a smart home system installed, which eliminates the load on guests, which provides additional comfort.

These rooms fully comply with all European standards.

Conditions for children

Alva Donna World Palace 5 * in Kemer (Turkey) is a hotel ideal for families with children of any age. On its territory there are many entertainments for kids, all conditions for their comfortable living and safety are provided.

First of all, for younger guests there is a separate pool and a large playground. For a fun pastime, a large club has been created where kids can play, chat with peers and develop - they are watched by animators and professional nannies.

On the local sandy and pebble beach, everything is also arranged with the maximum comfort for such an age category of guests - a gentle slope into the water, and there is also a swimming pool filled with sea water.





Caring parents can hire a nanny for the child (the service costs about $ 30 per day) and go to a disco in the evening, where show programs are held. In the daytime, children will certainly be happy to visit the local restaurants, which have separate menus for the most fastidious gourmets.

Nutrition

In the Alva Donna World Palace 5 * hotel, meals are provided in local restaurants, cafes and bars. There are as many as three gourmet establishments serving a buffet for guests. In addition, guests are served classic Italian and Turkish dishes, there is always a fish menu. The guests themselves note that cooks who work in the kitchen have extraordinary professional skills that can surprise the most sophisticated gourmets.

The hotel has seven excellent bars, which are located near the pools, on the beach, as well as in the buildings. They serve excellent snacks, a small amount of light salads, alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, tea, coffee, as well as desserts and home-made ice cream.



Pool

One of the most popular entertainment in Alva Donna World Palace 5 * is a stay in the pools, which are five in the hotel.

Four luxurious pools are located in the open air, the most important one occupies a large area - 1650 square meters. m - and is equipped with a system that creates waves. There are also Olympic, semi-Olympic and relaxation. Each of them is filled with crystal clear water: it is regularly cleaned.



The fifth pool is indoor. It is located in the main building of the hotel, its area is relatively small - only 200 sq. M. Guests prefer to have fun in it during the cool time, as the water here is automatically heated.

Spa area

Tourists staying in this recreation area can enjoy the results of visiting the spa center, which is available at the hotel. Its structure includes a large sauna and a Turkish bath - a hammam, which you do not need to pay to attend. There is also a large massage room where only professionals work. In addition, guests have the opportunity to visit separate treatment rooms, of which 12 are in the buildings, and three in the local garden, which is spread throughout the hotel. In them, for a fee, you can order peeling procedures, body wraps, as well as receive other spa services.



Sport

Sports guests will also not be bored on vacation, because the infrastructure of the Alva Donna World Palace 5 * hotel allows them to actively engage in their health and improve their shape, not looking up from the rest. There is a huge sports hall, which contains simulators acting on different muscle groups. It can be visited not only by adult guests, but also by children (under the supervision of parents).

The hotel has nine well-equipped tennis courts, as well as various venues for team games (football, volleyball, basketball). Here you can rent equipment necessary for games and sports, but for a fee.

Beach

The hotel has its own sand and pebble beach, which stretches along the coast for almost half a kilometer, and its width is 50 meters. The territory of this recreation area is very well equipped - there are locker rooms, toilets, as well as showers. Free of charge, guests are provided with sun loungers, umbrellas, towels and even air mattresses.



Tourists staying on the beach can enjoy water sports or just have fun. Here you can rent equipment, for example, a surfboard, scuba gear or other accessories. Tourists can also enjoy water skiing, air cushioning, parachuting into the air or scuba diving.

In the beach area for guests are equipped with VIP arbors, ideal for family vacations or friendly companies, but this requires a preliminary reservation.



Services

On the territory of Alva Donna World Palace 5 * the service is very well developed, a large number of services are provided here, both free and for an additional fee.

Guests can use the local car parking for free, but you will have to pay for a car rental. Throughout the hotel there is free access to the Internet.

For a fee, guests have the opportunity to use dry cleaning and laundry services, visit a beauty salon and call a doctor who is on duty from 11 a.m. to 4 p.m. When sending to this hotel, you should know that there are no ATMs on its territory, but plastic cards of international formats are accepted.
