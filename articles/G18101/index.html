Needlework lessons. How to make topiary from cotton pads?

One of the most popular decorative elements in the interior today is topiary. This small handmade artificial tree captivates with its beauty and originality. They make such souvenirs from a wide variety of materials: paper, flowers, coffee beans, sweets and fruits. But few people know that you can make topiary from cotton pads. From such a simple and affordable material, a very tender and pretty tree is obtained. In this article, we will tell you how to make such a topiary with your own hands. Photos of such souvenirs and the necessary material for their implementation, you can also see here.

Learning to make a tree. Preparation stage

To make a souvenir you will need:

  • cotton pads;
  • a foam ball or ball of yarn;
  • sewing thread in the color of the discs;
  • dry wooden stick;
  • a flower pot or any other similar container;
  • gypsum;
  • green yarn;
  • glue "Moment", "Dragon" or thermal gun;
  • stapler;
  • artificial greens;
  • narrow satin ribbon;
  • decorative elements: beads, stklarus, figurines of birds, butterflies.

Making topiary of cotton pads

Flowers. Roll the cotton pad "roll" and wrap the thread from below or fasten with a stapler. Turn the top of the workpiece out and straighten. It turns out the product in the form of a rose flower. Complete these 10-12 items. If necessary, then you can add.

The basis of the tree. Make a hole in the foam ball and glue a wooden stick into it, which will serve as the barrel. Following the instructions on the package with gypsum, dilute this material and pour into a container. Insert your tree in the middle. Hold it for a few minutes, allowing the base to freeze a little.

Product design. Glue flowers on the entire surface of the ball. Attach the leaves between them. Wrap the tree trunk with colored tape or wrapping paper. Glue artificial greens, beads or glass beads onto the surface of the gypsum. Tie a ribbon bow to the pot . The cotton topiary is ready.

Such a souvenir can be additionally decorated with beads, bows, decorative butterflies, ladybugs or birds.

Little tricks



If you do not have a foam ball, then as a basis you can take a ball of yarn. Wrap it with white paper and scotch tape. Then attach the flowers on top. Such a cotton topiary will also look beautiful.

As a stand, you can use plastic packaging for products (yogurt, sour cream, mayonnaise). To make them look aesthetically pleasing, wrap them with a piece of cloth or brown paper, and tie a ribbon on top.

Perform the "tree of happiness" by the strength of each of you. As you can see, the materials for the topiary are the most simple and inexpensive. As a result, you get a fabulously beautiful souvenir, which can become not only an adornment of the interior of your home, but also a wonderful gift for friends.
