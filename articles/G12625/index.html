Mobile phones ZTE: reviews. Phone ZTE V815, ZTE S207: reviews

The popularity of mobile devices has led to the fact that in recent years a lot of new, previously unknown players have appeared on the smartphone market. The bulk of the companies are immigrants from China, high-tech companies with cheap labor and potential that can bring the brand to leadership positions around the world.

One such company is ZTE. Earlier, probably, no one had heard of her in our country due to her activities in the Chinese market. Meanwhile, the brand has existed since 1985, and now it is already operating in 160 countries around the world. Moreover, budget phones, which will be discussed in this article, brought fame to the brand. How worthwhile can be called the company's products, what are the advantages and disadvantages of smartphones manufactured with the ZTE logo - we will tell in this article.

The lineup

So, the company's models that have ever been produced and put on sale are in a special list posted on the official website. Here you can find the names, photos of devices, as well as their full technical specifications.

The general impression about ZTE phones (which we will publish further reviews in the text) is that we have before us - an exhibition of copies of Samsung, HTC, LG, Apple, and other devices. It is immediately obvious that the company was not engaged in the development of individual designs for its equipment - everything was copied from the most successful models of competing companies.

However, this is not news for Chinese manufacturers. You can verify this by looking at Lenovo, Zopo, Huawei and the same ZTE phones. Customer reviews demonstrate that people are especially unable to distinguish between these phones. In addition, a clear focus on the more well-known companies (concerns Samsung), which are the guides in the world of device design (unless you talk about copying Apple's style, of course), is obvious.

Market positioning





Although the Chinese themselves obviously do not disclose this. The company positions itself as an individual manufacturer of affordable devices, which can be counted on the market a lot. It feels like the factories that make ZTE devices are trying to release as many different models as possible. By the way, they are not original with the names here either - ZTE phones (reviews of them you will see later) are called the same as the models of those companies that originally developed them. See for yourself: ZTE S6 (Galaxy S6), Grand S3 (like Samsung Galaxy Grand), Star (like Galaxy Star) and many others. It can be seen that the Chinese developers are hoping, therefore, to "chop off" a little the authority of the Korean company, to nail the glory of its products.

The above leads us to the conclusion that the design and names (that is, in essence, form) of ZTE are like a carbon copy. Therefore, to understand how its models are generally interesting to the buyer, we will consider some of them.

Review Blade S6





Externally, the model is very similar to the iPhone 6. According to customer reviews, from afar it is difficult to distinguish it from the "apple" representative. Only, unlike Apple, ZTE is much cheaper, due to which the finishing materials of the case, as well as all the quality of its fit, is one level lower. So, at first it may seem that the device is made of metal, but with a more detailed palpation, it becomes clear that this is plastic.

The phone pleases with interesting design solutions in some details, for example, a round button to return to the home screen. It glows with a pleasant blue color, and in order not to blind from it at night, you can reduce its brightness in the settings.

The technical equipment of the device is of particular interest to gamers. So, Blade operates on the basis of an eight-core processor, 4 cores of which give a clock frequency of 1 GHz, and another 4 - 1.7 GHz. Due to this, in the Asphalt game, for example, the device is capable of reproducing graphics in the ‚Äúaverage‚Äù mode.

The phone can be called quite nimble in work. As for the screen, it shows the picture quite clearly, although at a certain viewing angle pixels are visible. The camera on the Blade S6 leaves much to be desired, despite 12 megapixels.

Grand S3 review





This device can be called advanced when comparing all ZTE cell phones. Feedback from fans of the company indicates that it has not yet been widely sold, but it was announced at the exhibition of affordable Android devices. As for the technical characteristics of the device, the Grand S3 runs on a 2.5-GHz processor, is equipped with a 3100 mAh battery, as well as a 16-megapixel camera.
