Do-it-yourself phone case

Today, almost everyone has a mobile phone. This device has become an integral part of our lives. To extend its service life allows the cover, which performs in this case a protective function. On sale you can find a large number of similar products designed for a particular brand of apparatus. However, they all have a standard appearance and are not particularly original. If you want to have a unique thing, then you should make a phone case with your own hands.

To do this, it is enough to master the basics of some kind of needlework and acquire a material suitable in color and quality. Then you should measure the size of your phone and develop a pattern that exactly repeats the shape of the device. Everything, it's time to get to work!



If you decide to sew a phone case with your own hands, then you need to cut two rectangles. The first should fully comply with the prepared pattern. However, seam allowances should also be provided. The size of the second will depend on the type of fastener planned. If there is a desire to fasten the cover with a zipper, then both squares should have the same shape and parameters. If button sewing is planned, it is necessary to provide a certain distance to form a buttonhole.

For those who decide to crochet the phone case, it will be much easier to complete the planned work. Indeed, the size and shape of the finished product can always be adjusted directly in the process. If the result is not pleasant, then the cover can be dissolved and made again.

In this case, it is best to knit a product in a circle, starting at the bottom. This will avoid unnecessary seams. The pattern can be chosen as you like. Can be crocheted with single crochet. Such a product will be more dense and will hold its shape well. As a decoration, you can use beads or various knitted elements, for example, flowers and leaves.

If you give preference to some openwork pattern, you will get a more beautiful product, however, its protective functions will be small. In this case, it is best to make a two-layer phone case with your own hands, consisting of a dense inner part and an openwork top connected by a contrasting thread. This will allow, while maintaining the protective function of the product, to give it a beautiful appearance without additional elements.

Also, a do-it-yourself phone case can be made using knitting needles. You can knit such a product in a circle or by manufacturing two separate paintings. It will be softer than in the case of performing work with a hook, but at the same time quite dense. The main thing is to choose the right number of knitting needles.



The shape of the product can also be very diverse. You can tie a phone case on your arm or neck. In the first case, the length of the sewn tape should be much less. Using this product will be much more convenient. The second option, most often, is preferred by children.

Thus, making a case yourself is not so difficult. The main thing is to have a desire and possess at least minimal needlework skills.
