Guest house "Acropolis", Vityazevo: description, service, reviews

Vityazevo is a resort village where a lot of hotels have been built to accommodate tourists. The Black Sea and developed infrastructure every year makes this place more popular. The Acropolis in Vityazevo creates all conditions for a cozy and interesting holiday for tourists.

Where is it located and how does it work?

The hotel is located on the first coastline. "Acropolis" (Vityazevo) is located on Alexandriysky proezd, 9. This area is well located in relation to almost all entertainment centers.





From Anapa here, about 30 minutes drive. Therefore, lovers of excursions will be able to easily travel to the city.

Guest house "Acropolis": description

The hotel occupies a fairly large area. The building has 5 floors. The complex is equipped with a spacious guarded parking for personal vehicles of vacationers.





The yard is as green as possible. Workers take care of the plants, and even on the hottest days they remain fresh. Paving slabs are everywhere laid across the territory, in the evening it is well lit by decorative lanterns.

The reception is located on the ground floor. Managers work here around the clock. They are always ready to solve the everyday problems of tourists.

Accommodations

The hotel offers rooms with different levels of comfort for a certain number of people:

  • family two-room suite with a balcony - there is a double bed and one single in the bedroom, a folding sofa, bedside tables, a wardrobe, a toilet and shower, air conditioning, a refrigerator, TV with satellite channels, a balcony with furniture are installed in the living room;
  • double - has a double bed and a folding chair for one person, bedside tables, coffee table, wardrobe, balcony with furniture, refrigerator, air conditioning, TV with satellite channels, toilet and shower;
  • triple room - one double and one single bed, a folding armchair, bedside tables, wardrobe, TV with satellite channels, air conditioning, refrigerator, shower and toilet, a balcony with furniture;
  • four-bedded room - two single and one double bed, bedside tables, wardrobe, fridge, air conditioning, TV with satellite channels, shower and toilet, balcony with furniture.




All rooms have free Wi-Fi access.

Guest house "Acropolis": service

The hotel has experienced maids. They carry out housekeeping at the request of customers. Bed sheets and towels are changed every 3-4 days. At the request of customers, an additional cleaning service can be carried out in case of unforeseen circumstances.

The reception is constantly staffed by the hotel. They quickly check in the guest rooms, and also spend at the same pace check out. At any time, you can contact them with problems in everyday life, as well as with the organization of sightseeing tours.

The property provides a shuttle service to guests from the airport or train station at no extra charge. It must be agreed upon in advance when booking a room.

There is a vending machine at the hotel where you can buy soft drinks. There is also a small laundry room in the Acropolis (Vityazevo). Tourists can use its services for a fee.

Food in the complex

It has a spacious dining room, decorated in a classic style. In it, if desired, all guests can eat at the same time. Often vacationers from other bases come to dine at the Acropolis Guest House.

Nutrition and demand for it is due to the professionalism of the cooks. They always use only proven fresh products. The menu is made taking into account the nutrition on it of all age groups. Therefore, guests boldly come here to eat with their children, starting from the age of one.





Tourists can pay in full 3 meals a day throughout the stay or order individual meals. The dining room has special racks that host a variety of dishes. Guests can choose what they like, and it will be served by the employee at the counter. Thus, the food is based on the principle of "buffet".

The complex has a bar. It offers customers non-alcoholic cocktails and spirits. They serve light snacks and desserts. In the evening, you can order freshly prepared barbecue. At the weekend, live music is played at the bar.

Pool and beach

On the territory of the Acropolis (Vityazevo) there is an outdoor pool. It has a separate area for children. It is fenced off from the main with special sides. Access to the pool is via small stairs.

There are no sun loungers around. For them, a separate territory is provided on the roof of the dining room. An area for sunbathing and relaxation is organized here. Comfortable sun loungers that do not absorb moisture are enough for everyone. There are also tables and plastic chairs under parasols. In this area, tourists can have a snack and a variety of drinks.





The water in the pool is fresh and heated. She regularly passes through modern cleaning filters. Every morning, workers use a special vacuum cleaner to clean the bottom of small debris and sediment.

The beach is a 10-minute walk away. It has a sandy surface. You can rent sun loungers and umbrellas. All kinds of water activities are organized on the beach:

  • riding a banana and buns;
  • slides;
  • trampolines;
  • extreme trips on boats and ATVs.

A variety of cafes and bars are on the shore. Small trading stalls with souvenirs and goods for a comfortable stay are installed.

Children's area

The administration took care of an interesting vacation in Vityazevo ("Acropolis") of its young guests. Here, in a specially designated area, a modern children's playground is installed and equipped. It is removed from the pool for the safety of the kids.

Bright carousels, houses, slides, swings are installed on a special coating. On the soft floor, the kids love to crawl and explore the world. This coating protects children from injury in the event of an accidental fall.

On the fences around the site, scenes from favorite Disney cartoons by children are drawn. Thus, children fall into a fabulous and vibrant adventure. In the evening, animators work with the guys. They hold interesting competitive programs for children of different ages.





Most often, animators dress up in all kinds of costumes. During these hours, parents can safely relax on the terrace or in the bar, as the children are completely passionate about the event.

Entertainment

Not all Vityazevo hotels can boast of such a good location relative to the centers for recreation in the village. The Acropolis is 5-10 minutes from the amusement park. A large Ferris wheel is clearly visible from its territory.

Also, vacationers can visit the dolphinarium, oceanarium, water park. A variety of small cafes and restaurants allows you to choose your own menu and atmosphere to your liking. In the evening, there are many night bars and discos.

Despite the fun and animation, the noise from the entertainment complexes does not reach the Acropolis (Vityazevo), so guests of the complex can relax after a busy day.

The hotel has a small team of animators. They have an entertainment program after dinner. Both children and adults can take part in it.

Additional services

Acropolis Guest House in Vityazevo has designated smoking areas. They are removed from places where other guests rest, so the smoke does not reach and does no harm to anyone.

All rooms have balconies with tables and armchairs. Here tourists can enjoy the beautiful view and relax in the evening with a glass of wine or a cup of hot coffee.

The complex has a special area for a picnic. A barbecue is installed here and you can take coals at the reception. This vacation is paid separately by tourists.

100 meters from the hotel there are grocery stores and small restaurants. Also on the way to the beach you can buy fresh fruit at the local market.

Reviews on the operation of the complex

On the Internet you can find a sufficient number of various comments left by vacationers of the Acropolis in Vityazevo. Reviews are both positive with some comments, and completely negative.

Almost all reviews draw attention to the friendliness and friendliness of the staff. Also, hotel guests are satisfied with the food in the complex. Only in some reviews can you see that there was a lack of variety in the menu.





With soundproofing in the rooms, according to the comments, everything is fine regarding the noise coming from the street. The walls between the rooms, according to reviews, are quite thin and vacationers can listen to what neighbors in another room are watching on TV.

Tourists in their comments indicate unscrupulous cleaning of the rooms. They claim that maids have to call on their own. Some rooms smell of sewage and hoods do not work well.

Guests are pleased to have furnished balconies in each room. In the evening, a beautiful view of the amusement park opens. Its illumination pleases the eye and there is something to watch. Also, vacationers are pleased with the opportunity to use an ironing board and an iron, which are installed on each floor.

Parents highly appreciate the arrangement of a playground in the complex. They indicate that children enjoy spending time there. They find friends here and actively move, releasing their energy.
