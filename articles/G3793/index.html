Steigenberger Aqua Magic 5 * (Egypt / Hurghada) - photos, prices and reviews

Today, the Steigenberger Aqua Magic 5 hotel can be considered one of the most popular holiday destinations on the coast of Hurghada. After all, here you will find not only comfortable rooms and first-class food, but also a lot of entertainment and, of course, professional service.

Hotel Location





The picturesque hotel complex Steigenberger Aqua Magic 5, built in a restrained oriental style, is located in the famous resort city of Hurghada, which today is rightfully considered one of the most popular holiday destinations in Egypt. The coast is just 400 meters away. The city center is about five kilometers from the hotel complex - it can easily be reached by taxi or public transport. The airport is six kilometers away. Cairo is 450 km away and the famous Luxor Temple Complex is 290 km away.

In the middle of the picturesque gardens, in the territory of one of the most famous resorts in the world, the Steigenberger Aqua Magic 5 hotel complex is located. Hurghada, El Masaya in particular, is a great place for a rich and fun holiday.

Brief description of the hotel complex

Hotel Steigenberger Aqua Magic 5 has a relatively large territory with well-designed infrastructure. By the way, it is worth mentioning that the hotel was opened in January 2014 and today is one of the most beautiful and modern complexes on the coast.





Throughout the territory there are small buildings built in an interesting oriental style. Here there are large curly shaped pools, and patios where you can have a great time in silence, as well as narrow walking paths decorated with quaint arches, and spacious sun terraces with a wonderful view of the surroundings. Of course, all this is decorated with large palm trees, blooming gardens and bright flower beds. This is what the Steigenberger Aqua Magic 5 hotel complex looks like. Photos of tourists confirm that the area is really well-groomed. The courtyards are cleaned several times a day, and the pools are cleaned almost daily.

To begin with, it is worth noting that there are smoke detectors, as well as a climate control system with convenient control. Here you will find tiles, a large bathtub combined with a shower, as well as a washbasin, toilet and a wall mirror with lighting. For each tenant, they leave a set of clean towels and a set of branded personal care products.

It is these comfortable rooms that await travelers at the Steigenberger Aqua Magic 5. Tourist reviews confirm that the rooms are cleaned every day, and bed linen and towels are changed regularly. By the way, if you wish, you can order the morning awakening service. Room service is available around the clock, but this service is charged separately.

Beach and water recreation

Golden beaches and clear sea waters are what the resort town of Hurghada is famous for. Steigenberger Aqua Magic 5 owns its own small stretch of coastline, located about 300 meters from the hotel complex. Immediately it is worth noting that the beach is kept in perfect cleanliness. Here, hotel guests can use sun loungers with comfortable mattresses, as well as umbrellas. Descent into the sea is convenient and completely safe. What could be better than soft sand under your feet, the gentle sun and the pleasant sound of sea waves?





Naturally, lovers of more active recreation will also find something to do. For example, surf lovers often come here. In addition, you will have the opportunity to engage in motorized water sports, go water skiing or catamarans. Entertainment and leisure
