Crochet pattern

A useful hobby such as hand knitting continues to be popular. For centuries, it has been used for the manufacture of vital interior items and clothing. Today, his role is reduced to creative self-expression and a kind of therapy.





What a hook is good

Crochet has many advantages over knitting. Among the most obvious are:

  • The presence of only one working loop.
  • The ability to change the direction of knitting at your discretion.
  • The widest selection of patterns, characterized by ease of execution.
  • The ability to make additions and reductions of loops, due to which it is possible to obtain paintings of various shapes and configurations.

That is why knitted toys, summer fishnet things, clothes for animals and other similar products are much easier to carry out using a hook.

Why you need to tie the edge

Possession of the basic principles of crocheting will be useful even to the most ardent adherents of knitting needles. Crocheting products (schemes for such work is very easy to find) will be useful for decorating a variety of objects:

  • Hat.
  • Blouse, sweater and cardigans.
  • Plaids and knitted blankets.
  • Napkins and towels (both knitted and textile).

Strapping requires all open edges of the product. Some models include the implementation of strips, edges, or other types of design edges in the knitting process.

This method has its advantages: the strapping forms a single whole with the canvas, there are no sharp transitions, you do not have to worry about the accuracy of its execution and the coincidence of the density parameters. However, if with the help of tying it is planned to adjust the dimensions of the product, apply a different color or texture of yarn, as well as a specific pattern, then the only way out is to crochet the edge of the product. Openwork corner design is the most popular type of harnesses. The scheme of crocheting the corners of the products has its own pattern: the mandatory expansion of the canvas at an angle of 45 degrees. Comparison of two such angles gives a right angle (90 degrees). This principle must be taken into account if you have to independently develop a scheme for tying them. For example, if you liked the pattern, but the diagram of the formation of the angle is not shown.

Knitting needles

Separately, it is worth mentioning the process of crocheting knitting products. This is done for several purposes:

  • achieving a neat edge;
  • if you wish, decorate the edge with an openwork pattern;
  • when knitting a button placket;
  • if necessary, change the dimensions of the product (increase the length or width).

The process is started after the main work is completed, all the details are connected and sewn. The best preparation of the product for strapping will be its steaming with an iron or washing in warm water. So you can see the real dimensions of the product.

Then perform the first row of strapping, which always consists of single crochet (RLS)! Here you have to strain your spatial imagination, since the principle of ‚Äúloop into loop‚Äù is irrelevant, especially when working with the side edge of the product. It is necessary to select the number of eyelet loops.

Insufficient amount will lead to contraction of the canvas, and excess - to the effect of quilling. Therefore, experienced knitters recommend performing a test strapping on a piece of the product, at least 15 cm long (for an objective assessment of the result).
