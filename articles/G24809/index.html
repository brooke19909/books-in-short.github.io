How can I find out the registration number of the FSS for a TIN?

To officially register as a legal entity, business entities send a request to the tax service. Tax, after it puts the new company on record, transfers the data to the Social Insurance Fund. It happens automatically.

This fund belongs to extrabudgetary. Engaged in the following:

  • pays sickness benefits;
  • provides payment of birth certificates;
  • pays child benefits;
  • provides citizens with benefits;
  • pays childbirth benefits;
  • makes disability payments.

Who can get the FSS policyholder number?





The policyholder number is available only to those entities that are going to employ workers. You can find out the FSS number only in this category.

It is important to remember that if an individual entrepreneur wants to temporarily hire a seasonal employee, then he is obliged to register with the Social Insurance Fund no later than the tenth day after the employee begins to work.

If this is not done or done at the wrong time, then the situation could threaten fines. The fines are now quite noticeable, amounting to several minimum wages.

Deregistration in the FSS

The business entity is obliged to notify the extrabudgetary fund of the termination of its activities. To do this, it is necessary to submit orders for the dismissal of employees, a document confirming the termination of activity from the Federal Tax Service, and a certificate of absence of debt.

If the subject needs to find out the FSS registration number by TIN, then you need to decide for what purposes it is needed.

What is the number required for?

The registration code is necessary in order to control the subject at the time of fulfillment of duties to workers and temporarily non-working employees of the organization.

Registration in the FSS is necessary for the payment of mandatory contributions that are directly provided for by the legislation of the country, and reporting.

Also, a registration code is assigned to be submitted to state authorities for control.

What does the number mean?





This number consists of a set of numbers, where the initial four means the code of the Fund's branch, and the next six are the personal number of the insured, which is entered in the FSS register. This code does not change, even if the company moves to another location.

For example, an employee of an organization was injured or ill. In this case, a document on temporary disability is drawn up, where one of the main points will be the registration number of the policyholder. A woman who is going on maternity leave is also provided with a certificate of incapacity for work indicating the number of the insured.

If there is any controversial situation between the insurer and the insured, the task is to find out the FSS number. He is needed to fill out reports.
