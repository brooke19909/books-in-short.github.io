Hotel check-in rules

So, populate arriving tourists in rooms as a rule should start after 14:00. However, the hotel staff always tries to do everything possible to reduce the waiting time in case of early arrival of guests. Therefore, if you arrived earlier than two in the afternoon and there are vacant rooms of the category reserved by you in Rain Beach, they will populate you immediately. But in the midst of the holiday season, which occurs in July and August, the hotel may not be available. In this case, you still have to wait until the previous guests have vacated the rooms and the maids have prepared them properly for check-in. In order not to lose time, you can leave bulky things in the luggage room and start relaxing by visiting a restaurant or bar, swimming in the pool or the sea, soaking up under the gentle rays of the sun or taking a pleasant walk through the hotel complex. On the day of departure, you must vacate the occupied room before noon. At the same time, it is necessary to hand over the room keys to the reception desk and pay for the entire stay, as well as the use of additional services, if any. If you have already pre-paid your stay at Raina Beach Hotel (Turkey) with your tour operator, then at check out you will only have to pay for services that are not included in the price of the ticket.

Hotel rooms

As mentioned above, Raina Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey) is a modern hotel complex with 132 comfortable rooms located in a large six-story main building equipped with elevators. The number of rooms is represented by the following categories: seven economical single rooms (12 square meters, no balcony); 94 standard rooms (28 sq. Meters); 30 standard rooms with magnificent sea views (28 sq. Meters). There is also one room, specially equipped for the comfortable accommodation of people with disabilities. All rooms at Raina Beach Hotel (Alanya) have: private bathroom, equipped with everything you need, air conditioning, television (satellite TV), telephone and wireless Internet access.
