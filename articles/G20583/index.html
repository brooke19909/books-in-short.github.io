A trip to Genichesk - rest on the coast of the Sea of ​​Azov

Warm sea, sandy beaches for every taste, medical and cosmetological mud, developed urban infrastructure - we will not talk about expensive resorts in Israel or Turkey, but about the coast of the Sea of ​​Azov (Genichesk). Rest in these parts will amaze with variety.



A few words about Genichesk

A small and cozy resort town is located in the Kherson region and is famous for its southern, temperate continental climate, "children's" beaches within walking distance, mud baths.

Genichesk has unique natural sites:

  • Spit Arabatskaya Strelka, extending far into the Sea of ​​Azov and popular among lovers of wild beaches and car camping;
  • hot springs of mineral water;
  • mud of Lake Sivash and other small estuaries;
  • reservoirs located throughout the Arabat Spit.

We select housing in the private sector

Residents of the private sector offer vacationers who come to the city of Genichesk to stay in their homes. Rest on the sea in these parts can afford almost everyone. Friendly owners are ready to rent apartments for a reasonable fee in any part of the city. So, for a family vacation with young children, housing near the "children's beach", located almost in the center of the city, is suitable. Young parents will be pleased with the depth of the sea near the coastline, which does not exceed 50 cm (an adult knee-deep), extending hundreds of meters to the horizon. A well-maintained city beach with full infrastructure, attractions and cafes will welcome tourists who come to Genichesk. Rest in the private sector, (vacationers' reviews are generally positive) will save a decent amount of accommodation. Housing prices in the central area, which is located within walking distance to the sea, will cost from 60 hryvnia per person per day. When choosing an apartment, you should pay attention to the location in relation to the sea. The coastal streets are Azovskaya, Primorskaya, Kirov and Makharadze.



Active recreation on the shore of the Sea of ​​Azov

For lovers of relaxation without children, noisy companies and wild beaches, you should pay attention to the private sector in the Arabat Strelka area. The depth of the sea off the coast of the spit is quite deep. Here you can find equipped beaches of boarding houses and camping sites, as well as "wild" beaches, secluded bays. If you select a room for living in this area, you get a quite inexpensive vacation in Genichesk. Guest reviews indicate some limitations in comfort, which affects the formation of prices. Room rental on Arabatskaya Strelka varies within 40 hryvnias per person. The most popular holiday destinations on the spit are the villages of Gengorka and Schastlivtsevo.

Organized vacation

Holidays on the Sea of ​​Azov are suitable not only for lovers of the private sector, but also for vacationers who prefer comfortable living in a complex with wellness treatments. For these purposes, it is recommended that tourists visiting the city of Genichesk stop at sanatoriums. Recreation facilities and pensions also offer a wide range of services to tourists. However, one must understand that the prices for staying in such institutions are much higher than those of private owners. The cost of a standard double room starts from 200 hryvnia per day. The presence of a refrigerator, air conditioning and a bathroom in the room increases the rental price to 300-400 hryvnia.

Hotel complex Nariman

One of the popular hotels is Nariman, which is located 100 meters from the coast of the Sea of ​​Azov on the street. Makharadze, the city of Genichesk. Rest here is the most comfortable and expensive. The hotel's room fund consists of apartments of the Standard and Junior Suite class starting at 300 hryvnias per day. Additionally, you can order three meals a day - 200 hryvnia per day per person. In addition, Nariman offers rental cottages for 3 and 4 people, which will cost from 400 hryvnia per day. The advantages of the hotel are its proximity to the sea, the availability of Internet and satellite television, its own territory, parking.

Hotel Orchid

On the street Mira near the children's beach and the city market is the hotel "Orchid." Prices for hotel rooms start from 450 hryvnia per day with meals included. The hotel complex has a swimming pool, playground, barbecue and sauna. All rooms are located in a large 3-storey building. The hotel territory is closed and guarded, there is a parking lot.

Pension "Kalyvan"

More budget vacation offers boarding house "Kalyvan" on the street. Azov, the cost of living per person starts from 50 hryvnia per day. In the immediate vicinity of the recreation center is a supermarket and pharmacy. Self-catering in two equipped kitchens is provided.



The recreation center "House with a smile"

On Primorskaya Street there is a “House with a Smile” - a private recreation center (Genichesk). Prices can not be called low, but it is justified. First of all, I want to highlight a convenient location. Nearby is a supermarket, market and beach. Each room has a private exit to the terrace. The cost of apartments starts from 300 hryvnia per day.

Pension "Brigantine"

On Arabatskaya Strelka you can stay at the Brigantine boarding house in the village of Schastlivtsevo (17 km from Genichesk). On an area of ​​7 hectares there are comfortable five-story buildings and small cottages. The boarding house has its own sandy beach with all the infrastructure. Room rates start at 180 hryvnia per day.

The recreation center "Svitanok-2"

Also in the village of Schastlivtsevo, but already 25 km from Genichesk, there is a recreation center "Svitanok-2". A room with three meals a day will cost from 200 hryvnia per day. The pension has its own beach, cinema. 3 km from the base is a hot spring.

Pension "Kadosh"

In the village of Gengorka near Genichesky, at the very beginning of the Arabat Spit, there is a private boarding house “Kadosh”, next to which there is a hot clinic “Hot Dzherelo”. Here you can combine rest by the sea with the restoration of the body in hot springs. Accommodation with meals in a boarding house will cost from 200 hryvnia per day.



Thus, the city of Genichesk, the vacation in which is famous not only for the warm and calm Sea of ​​Azov, opens up great opportunities for tourists. Here you can improve your health, freshen up on healing springs and mud. Those who have been to these parts once come back again and again, preserving and transmitting the contacts of the welcoming owners, pre-booking rooms of the hotel's hotel fund.
