Vistasol Punta Cana Beach Resort & Casino 4 *: reviews, photos

If you plan to relax in the Dominican Republic and are looking for a comfortable hotel on the ocean with excellent service, then Vistasol Punta Cana Beach Resort & Casino 4 * may be a very suitable option for you.



Hotel location

The hotel is located in the most beautiful, according to tourists, location of the Dominican Republic - on the beach of Bavaro. Higuey is 45 kilometers from the hotel. The nearest airport is located in Punta Cana, 20 kilometers away.

Vistasol Punta Cana Beach Resort & Casino 4 *: photo, general information

The hotel was built in 1992, and in 2004 there was a large-scale reconstruction. The hotel is part of the Vista Sol Hotels chain.

The housing stock of the hotel consists of 470 standard, superior and junior suites. They are located in ten two-, three- and four-story buildings, half of which are equipped with elevators. The apartment has everything you need for a comfortable stay: comfortable furniture, bathroom, hairdryer, TV with Russian channels, air conditioning, tea set, safe (for an additional fee), balcony or terrace. Cleaning is done daily, and towels and linen are updated several times a week.

The hotel infrastructure includes shops, souvenir shops, a supermarket, a medical office, massage parlor, laundry, car rental, tour desk, currency exchange and much more.

The hotel complex has three large pools, a jacuzzi, sun terraces, a casino, a disco club, and sports grounds. From morning until late at night, the hotel has a team of animators. During the day, they offer to play water polo, volleyball, darts, do gymnastics, water aerobics, yoga, and in the evenings conduct entertaining shows and performances. According to tourists, every Saturday there is a fun party on the beach. The hotel also has a mini-club, where the youngest tourists go with pleasure.

Hotel Residential Fund

Judging by the comments of our compatriots, they were pleased with the rooms offered by them at the Vistasol Punta Cana Beach Resort & Casino 4 * hotel (Dominican Republic, Punta Cana) . So, according to them, the apartment was recently renovated. They are spacious, clean and comfortable. Furniture and appliances are in good condition. If you find any malfunction, then report it to the reception, and the problem will be fixed as soon as possible.

As for the location of the rooms in the hotel, according to tourists, they differ only in the presence or absence of ocean views. After all, the road to the beach from all residential buildings will take about the same time. Many of our compatriots are advised to use the safe in the room. This service costs only ten dollars for the entire stay, but your documents and valuables will be safe. Room cleaning

Most of the guests had no complaints about the work of the maids of the Vistasol Punta Cana Beach Resort hotel. So, according to them, the cleaning was carried out efficiently and every day. The maids change the towels in the rooms if they throw the dirty ones on the floor. Bed sheets were updated several times a week. Check in, departure

Judging by the stories of our compatriots, regardless of the time of your arrival at Vistasol Punta Cana Beach Resort, you should not count on checking in before three in the afternoon. Therefore, if you plan to arrive at the hotel in the morning, then be prepared for the fact that you will be given room keys only after lunch. However, this does not mean that you have to spend all this time in the lobby next to the reception. So, you can leave your things in the luggage compartment and go to a restaurant, bar, swim in the pool, take a walk around the hotel complex or go directly to the beach. After lunch, you will need to go to the reception again, take the key to the apartments you are due and transfer your suitcases and bags there.

On the day of departure, the room should be vacated before noon. If you plan to leave the hotel in the evening, then your stay can be extended at an additional cost. However, few of the guests do this. After all, you can again leave things in the luggage room, and continue to enjoy your vacation yourself, using the entire infrastructure of the hotel.
