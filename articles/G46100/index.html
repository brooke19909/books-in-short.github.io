Aventura, a park in Spain: the whole range of entertainment for your family

The park was built on the Mediterranean coast of Spain, in the city of Salou, the famous resort of Costa Dorada. In addition to the most attractions, there is a magnificent PortAventura Aquatic water park, a golf club, four hotels, excellent sandy beaches and a shallow sea. Aventura, a park in Spain, is one of the largest in Europe, in it in one day you can visit almost the whole world and get a lot of impressions. You, like your children, will not forget this journey for a long time.



Aventura Park in Spain will offer you to visit China, Mexico, Polynesia, the Mediterranean, the Wild West. Children will be able to visit a children's country called Sesame. Concerts, theatrical performances will be organized for you. Attractions - more than 30 pieces, for every taste. You can eat in various themed cafes and restaurants, tasting the cuisines of different countries and peoples. In boutiques, souvenir shops and shops located in the park, you can buy crafts and souvenirs.

Aventura (a park in Spain) is located a hundred kilometers from the country's most famous city - Barcelona. You can get here in different ways: by bus or train, by taxi or by ordering a transfer. If you settle in one of the four hotels located on its territory, then you can absolutely free to visit the park at any time. You can also stay in the city of Salou, which is very close.



The schedule of the park is constantly changing, so before you go there, you need to clarify it. It usually operates from March to October, plus during the Christmas holidays. It’s more profitable to buy tickets for rides at once for several days. The entrance ticket gives the right to visit any of them for free for the entire duration. There are various categories of tickets. For example, one day costs for adults / children / seniors respectively - 45/39/39 euros. For seven days - 115/92/92 euros. Children under three years old can go to the park for free.

When visiting attractions, you should know and remember that Aventura, a park in Spain, is a rather dangerous place, it has various access restrictions. So,

for example, only those above 1m 40cm will be allowed to enter Shambhala, Dragon Khan and Flight of Condor. There are attractions where children are not allowed unaccompanied by adults. It is advisable to familiarize yourself with all this beforehand so that there is no surprise at the last moment. You can do this, as well as get a lot of other useful information on the sites of the park: in Russian and Spanish.

As well as Spain itself, Port Aventura amusement park is a very famous place for families, so try to pre-purchase tickets. It will most likely be cheaper and will not have to stand in line for a long time under the hot enough sun. By the way, do not forget to put on hats and comfortable clothes so that you can reliably distribute small accessories to prevent them from falling out when using attractions.

Aventura, a park in Spain, will keep you in suspense until the very last minute of its work. And his work ends every day with a magnificent beautiful show. It runs on a lake in the "Mediterranean" zone.

Do not forget to bring a camera or camcorder with you to capture for a long memory the most interesting moments of relaxation.
