For lovers of needlework: a doll from tights, a master class for beginners

There are no homemade dolls in the world. And elegant tildes sewn from pieces of fabric using Norwegian technology Tony Finger. And snub-nosed Waldorf, having nice little faces and chic hairstyles. And simple in appearance, but rich in content, traditional Russian columns. And dolls are made of wood, stones, shells. An equal member of this wonderful toy family has become a doll from tights. A master class for beginners is quite simple. Everyone can make a wonderful doll from their own tights.

Manufacturing methods



Tights are the most wonderful material for making dolls, because most models of this item of women's wardrobe are flesh-colored. And blacks make wonderful blacks and mulattos. In addition, stockings and tights are very soft and plastic. They can be stretched as you like to make the doll a funny nose or a funny face. In appearance, home-made dolls from tights are very different: from a chubby pink-cheeked little toddler to a big-breasted gnome or a wrinkled old woman.

You can make them in three ways - volumetric on the frame, volumetric without the frame and flat. If the doll is made on a wire frame, it can not only stand, sit or lie, but also raise its hands, bend over, turn its head and remain in that position. Toys without a frame are a little simpler; the position of their torso and limbs cannot be changed. But they are also very cute and pretty. Let's consider in detail how to make dolls from pantyhose without a frame.

Materials & Supplies



I would like to immediately notice that dolls and other toys from tights and stockings are made only by hand. That is, a sewing machine, if necessary, is only for creating clothes. To complete the doll you will need:

1. Tights. You can take the old, in which the ‚Äúarrow‚Äù was accidentally formed, or you can buy new ones for your creation. Not very good if the tights are too thin. To make the first doll from pantyhose easier, a master class for beginners recommends taking a denser material, for example 40 DEN and higher.



2. Filler. If for other dolls even trimming fabric will fit as a filler, then for creating from pantyhose you need a synthetic winterizer. In extreme cases, you can take very finely chopped pieces of tights. However, with such a filler to form a face is more difficult.

3. Cloth for doll clothes.

4. Threads matching the tights. If you try, they can be pulled out of the tights themselves.

5. Threads for doll hairstyles. Woolen yarn or boucle are suitable.

6. Buttons for the eyes. If there are no suitable ones, you can sew eyes from pieces of skin or embroider them directly on the face of the doll.

Work Technique



The simplest doll from tights, a master class for beginners on the manufacture of which is given, is performed like this. Cut the desired size piece from the tights to make a tube. Gently cut one end of it by an amount equal to the doll's future legs. All this is carefully sewn up.

Next, tightly fill the resulting structure with a synthetic winterizer and sew up the second end. Select the neck and tightly tie it with a thread. Sew doll handles separately, fill them with padding polyester and sew to the body. You can also highlight the seams on the already finished torso handles. On the face in the place of the alleged nose, take the tights with two fingers with a synthetic winterizer and pull it a little. Fix this place with a needle and thread. Ears and eyelids are also made, if needed. Make a face with button eyes, embroider a mouth. You can lightly tint the doll's cheeks.

To make hair, the threads are laid out in several layers and stitched. The resulting wig is attached to the head. Such a hairstyle will perfectly hide unnecessary seams and knots. You can also sew the hair directly on the head, each thread separately. If you make the doll more complicated, you can form a lot of folds, creating her facial expressions, fingers on her arms and legs, lips, buttocks. All this is done with a thread and a needle.

Frame doll from tights. Workshop for beginners



As a frame, you can take a strong, but easily bendable wire. From it, twist the body of the future doll, 2 hands with fingers and 2 legs. Tighten each wire part with a synthetic winterizer, fixing it well. Then sheathe it all with pantyhose. They need to be stretched to each finger of the doll, to each rounding of the wire and carefully stitched with thread. Then stitch it all together.

The second material for the frame is an ordinary plastic bottle. This container must be cut, stepping back from the neck by a distance equal to the size of the future doll. Next, the resulting frame is wrapped with synthetic winterizer. This is the body of the doll.

The head for frame dolls is prepared separately. It can be performed on a wire frame, or can be made simply of pantyhose tightly stuffed with synthetic winterizer. It will be round or oval - the choice of the master. To make the doll‚Äôs nose more prominent, you can insert a piece of sintepon that you need in size into the pantyhose and sew it with threads. Bottle-based dolls have no legs. To avoid this being seen, they are dressed in dresses with long skirts.

Flat dolls



In addition to voluminous, flat dolls from tights look very interesting. For beginners, making them will be easy. For this, in addition to the above materials, you will need a piece of cardboard. On it you need to draw the contours of the desired doll. As a rule, its dimensions are small. This figure must be pasted with a thin padding polyester.

To make the head rounded, put more filler in this place. Next, tighten the resulting structure with pantyhose and carefully sheathe it. If you insert a magnet on the back of the doll under the tights, it can be clung to metal surfaces. The head of a flat doll can be done separately, and then sew it to the body. Each craftswoman sews clothes for her creations to her taste. Flat dolls are often made in the form of angels. Their wings look especially beautiful if made from thin white feathers.

Making Sock Puppets

If in the house there were unnecessary colored tights, for example woolen, or socks, they can also come in handy. MK "Dolls from tights and socks" is very simple and ideal for beginners. The woolen thing is laid out on the table and cut into three parts. The heel is thrown. The lower part is outlined in three lines that highlight the legs of the doll, composed together, and the handles pressed to the body. These lines are stitched. The resulting design is filled with padding polyester and sewn. The doll‚Äôs head is made separately from the same sock or from another material. The upper part of the sock can be used for a hat.
