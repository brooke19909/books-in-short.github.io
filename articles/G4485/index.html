Crochet applications: diagrams, descriptions and use cases

There were a lot of yarn of different colors, but one texture. Where to adapt it? Why not make all kinds of applications (crochet)?

Where can I use drawings from a related applique?

Everything will depend on the chosen topic. Cars and animals come in handy for decorating faceless children's clothes. But the flowers or figures of girls in chic dresses will suit those who are older. Knitted applications (crocheted) will look good on a purse or blouse.

There is another option - to connect several identical elements, and then the application will become a rectangular napkin. Those who have sufficient experience will be able to connect them so that they make a circle.





"Machine"

It consists of four parts: housing, two wheels and headlights. Knitting of the applique (crochet) begins with the largest part - the body. The size of the finished machine will depend on the initial chain. Let there be 27 air loops in it.

First row: two lifting loops, one double crochet in each loop of the set chain.

In the second lifting loop there will be three and double crochet in all the peaks except the second.

Third row: three loops, two double crochets from the second and fourth vertices. At the end of the row, do not knit a column from the penultimate loop, and make the last two with one vertex.

Fourth row: three loops, double crochet in all the vertices of the previous one.

Fifth: air loop, 4 single crochet, 15 air loops, connecting to the last loop of the row.

The last row of the hull (in the direction is a continuation of the previous one): an air loop, connecting posts on the trunk, bottom and hood of the future machine; at the beginning of a long chain of loops, perform a single crochet, on the arch - 12 single crochet, then another single crochet.

The second part of the applique (crochet) is the wheel. That is, there should be two of them. On a ring of three loops, connect 6 connecting posts. The second circle consists of 12 such columns, that is, in each loop there should be two of them. In the last round, alternation is performed: one column from one loop, two columns from one loop.

The third part is the headlight. It fits like a wheel, only you need to stop after the first row.

"Cat"

They can be made in different sizes and colors, and you get a real cat family. Such knitted appliqu√©s (crochet) will come in handy for decorating girls' clothes.

It is supposed to start with 26 loops - this will be the basis for the cat's legs and tummy. The first 9 loops are the back foot: connecting column to the second loop from the hook, single crochet, connecting 6 without crochet.

On the next loop, a half crochet, then a double crochet. On its top, dial a chain of 14 loops. Tie them with connecting posts, there should be 12, and the last one must be connected in the first loop of the chain. This is the tail that will need to be sewn, curving with a question mark.

Then the back of the cat will be knitted. The double crochet is on the same base as the one that became the basis for the tail. In the next loop, two columns with two double crochets, then in one - two columns with three double crochets. In the next two: one with two crochets, the second with one. Continue with double crochet and connecting in one loop.

Continuation of the application (crochet) in knitting a cat's head. On one loop, connect: 5 air, on it connecting to the second, single crochet in the third chain; two double crochet; 2 air, on them a connecting column; double crochet; 2 aerial and loop-connecting warp head.

It remains to tie the front foot. On the last 9 loops of the initial chain, tie: 6 single crochet, single crochet, single crochet.

"Caterpillar"

Such applications (crochet) for boys and girls come in handy. In the first case, a cap can be made on her head, and she will turn into a military or police officer. For the girl, it‚Äôs permissible to decorate the application with a bouquet of small flowers.

The entire caterpillar consists of seven circles. The largest is the body, the smaller will go to the head. Four even smaller sizes are required for the neck and tail. Another smallest comes in handy for the tip of the tail.

All circles are knitted according to one principle: on a ring of 4 loops, perform 10 connecting posts. In the second round, add 4 loops evenly. At this point, you should stop when knitting circles for the tail and neck.

To make the torso, continue knitting. In the third circle, evenly increase the number of columns by 6. The fourth circle is supposed to be further expanded by 6-8 loops. The tip of the ponytail knits in the same way as these circles, only you need to dial 3 loops, the columns should be 7, and in the second row add 5 loops.

When knitting the head, you need to make small changes in the second row. In places where the cheeks will be located, it is necessary to make columns without a crochet.

Sew all the details together. Then tie the antennae. They are the same circles as the tip of the tail, and are attached to the head with air chains of 5-6 loops.





The completion of the application (crochet) for children's clothing is to tie the body of the caterpillar and make her paws. Fasten the yarn to the junction of the head and neck. Run three connecting posts, 5 double crochet in two loops, another 5 double crochet in the first two stitches of the body. Four connecting posts to the first leg. Leg: 7 air, close the last 3 ring, tie 6 columns with a crochet on them, tie the foot with connecting posts. Two connecting posts - and another foot. Six connecting - and the foot, so on all circles of the tail. The tail of the tail is simply tied with connecting posts.
