Hotel Club Almoggar 4 * (Morocco / Agadir): photos and reviews of tourists

Agadir Resort is located on the Atlantic coast. Beach lovers come here from all over the world to enjoy the sultry Moroccan sun. A vast strip of golden sandy beaches, filled with the aroma of eucalyptus and pine trees, the hospitality of local residents - all this ensures Agadir the glory of one of the most popular world resorts.





Where can a tourist stay? Club Almoggar 4 * hotel (Morocco, Agadir) opens its doors to those wishing to spend their holidays here.

Why do you need to go on vacation to Agadir?

For lovers of beach holidays, the hot Agadir will give truly unforgettable days. Tourists can do sports here, from windsurfing to horse riding. The most popular are fishing, water skiing or biking, yachting. Picturesque nature is ideal for camel rides. Many people like to play golf, tennis in the fresh air. Tourists who visit the resort in May can take part in water skiing and surfing tournaments.

The city offers comfortable hotels for tourists. One of the most popular is the Club Almoggar 4 * hotel.

For those who wish there are centers of thalassotherapy. Often concerts, festivals, and colorful shows are held here. Fans of noisy parties can go to local clubs, bars and restaurants that are open throughout the night. They offer great service. Club Almoggar 4 * among them is no exception. On the menu of local establishments you can see dishes such as lobster and prawns grilled, traditional local specialties - kebab and couscous. Club Almoggar 4 * guests can enjoy local cuisine in the hotel restaurant.

Tourists can buy Moroccan souvenirs in the city's trading shops - carpets and ceramics from Safi, as well as leather goods.

In Agadir there are not as many attractions as lovers of excursions would like. But they always have the opportunity to visit the old Marrakech and other Moroccan cities.

The climate of Agadir is subtropical, mild. The recommended period for coming here on vacation is from May to September.

Club Almoggar 4 * (Agadir): acquaintance

The hotel is located in the center of the tourist area at a distance of 600 m from the center of Agadir. From it to the airport - 27 km (30 minutes drive).

Hotel Club Almoggar 4 * (Morocco) has been open to guests since January 2010. The huge tropical park in which the hotel is located is adjacent to a 9-kilometer sandy beach. Check in, room description

In the hotel of the city of Agadir Club Almoggar 4 *, tourists from Moscow arriving in the evening flight arrive at around 19:00. The room is provided immediately, without delay.

Tips for guests at Club Almoggar

Based on the reviews left, we can draw the following conclusions:

  • It is better to choose a room on the second floor for settlement, here the screams of gulls are not so worried.
  • The first floor is not advised to choose: this is the entrance courtyard.
  • According to former residents, the rooms located on the second floor are good. They have ocean views. But for them an extra charge is provided (5 euros per day). About the beach

Across the road from the hotel is a private beach, provided for the services of residents. This is a small, sand-covered, square, land area separated by a fence from the rest of the municipal beach. Sunbeds here at 9 in the morning are arranged by a hotel employee. Prior to this, they are all tied with an iron chain, so coming here earlier does not make sense.

Sun beds on the beach are sorely lacking! You can only take a seat if you come here at nine in the morning. Anyone who arrives at 10-11 hours, will be forced to either go back to the hotel, or lay out their towels directly on the sand. The ocean at first glance seems cold, in fact, the water is very warm, you can swim for a long time. In the depths you can see small fish. After lunch, the water becomes unclear, sometimes trash floats in it: plastic packaging or bird feathers. Strong waves do not exist here. For beach towels that are issued at the exit, you must leave a deposit: 100 dirhams per piece. At the end of the rest, the deposit is returned. Users are advised to take their own towels to the beach.
