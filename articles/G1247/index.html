How to make a swan from a towel with your own hands

Osibori technique came to us from Japan, it is the art of creating beautiful figures from towels. Naturally, you will have a question about how to make a swan from a towel? In fact, making it simple and fast enough! To do this, you need a few towels (bath and a hand towel), a little patience and, of course, desire. And in just a couple of minutes you will get a masterpiece.

If you decide to decorate your room, make a pleasant surprise for friends or please your child, then you will certainly be interested in a master class where you will learn how to make a swan from a towel. Not only will the creation process bring you a lot of pleasure, it will also amuse your family and friends.

Towels should be clean, possibly slightly starched, to keep their shape well. To prevent the fabric from being excessively soft, it is undesirable to use an air conditioner when washing. For convenience, you can use a hard surface such as a table. It is advisable to make a swan in the place where it should be, otherwise the ‚Äúbird‚Äù may simply fall apart when carried.

Subsequently, when you already learn, you can make a swan even on a soft bed.

Now we will consider in detail how to make a swan from a towel. By the way, it is not necessary to twist a piece of white classic towels. From bright and colored fabrics, these birds look more original.

When creating a swan or two, make sure your towels are of equal thickness and size. In addition, it is desirable that the towel is plain. For decoration, you can use all kinds of accessories - fresh flowers, hearts, sweets.

Towel swan

Let's start creating our masterpiece. To do this, consider in detail how to make a swan from a towel step by step. There is one caveat: you need to roll the towel tight enough so that the figure keeps its shape well and looks neat.

We place the towel of your choice in horizontal position. Then we fold the upper corners inward so that they touch their sides exactly in the center. Then you should roll the fabric in an even roll - from the bend line to the center - holding it with your fingers. It is necessary to roll so that both convolutions lie parallel to each other. You should have a long shape that looks like an arrow.

To form the figure of a swan, you need to fold the resulting arrow in half, observing the proportions of the length. Then you should lift the upper part of the towel, make a neck out of it, smoothly turning into the head.

It is interesting that the created design can be left in the received version or improved, adding one more - for the volume. Why do you need to take another small towel (you can hand) and roll it along the long edge into a roll. Then fold in half and lay on top of the swan's back. Thanks to this, the neck will stay more stable.

Varieties of swans

There are other options for making a swan out of a towel. For example, with a tail reminiscent of the beautiful and magnificent tail of a peacock. To do this, use the above description. Then you should give the lean tail the most "magnificent plumage". Take three more bright towels of different colors, fold them in the form of a fan and place them at the base of the neck, thereby covering the body of the created bird.

In addition, you can make a swan with spread wings. To do this, put a towel on the back of the bird, collecting it in an accordion. Clamp the middle of the towel with your hands, and straighten the fabric on both sides of the wings, giving the shape of a beautiful fan.
