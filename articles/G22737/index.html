The most common sizes of rhinestones

Any clothes, headdress, bag or phone case can easily be decorated with beautiful shiny beads or pebbles. Their name is mysterious and short - rhinestones. Such jewelry has become an integral part of the decor of almost any type of clothing, shoes, electronics and much more.

How rhinestones appeared

Size division

Sizes of rhinestones are necessary in order to determine the correct number of beads for work, to find out how many they will be needed for a particular picture, and also to understand how best to lay it out. First of all, remember that all sizes are given in millimeters. The stones are divided into sewn, glue and rhinestones in the studs.





Sewn on rhinestones come in the form of an oval, boat, drop, sheet, as well as in the form of different geometric shapes. This also includes Rivoli. These are round sewn rhinestones from 10 to 20 mm in size, which are considered the most popular.

Glutinous rhinestones come in the form of a hat, hemisphere, octagon. But the main form is a crystal with many faces. Stones differ in size and color.

The main sizes of rhinestones

  • Sew on rhinestone. The smallest is 7 mm, the largest is 50 mm. It all depends on the size of the stone. If it has a geometric shape, then the size is represented by one value (for example: 14 or 16 mm).
  • Rhinestones in the form of leaflets, boats, a drop are measured in two numbers - the length and width of the stone (for example: 10/18, 15/25 mm, etc.).
  • Glutinous rhinestone. This also includes a strass ribbon. The smallest is 1.3 mm and the largest is 14.5 mm. The most common sizes of rhinestones: 2.7-2.9 mm, 3.8-4.0 mm, 6.32-6.5 mm.
  • Rhinestones in the pins also have a size. It is defined in the same way as with glue stones (for example: 3.4-3.6 mm).




Any craftswoman knows the whole table of strasses and can easily determine the exact number of beads that is necessary for work. Also, with the help of stones of different sizes, the conceived pattern takes on the correct shape and beautiful volume.
