DIY nesting doll made of puff pastry, plasticine, fabric or paper

Matryoshka is a beautiful painted doll of cylindrical shape. Traditionally, it is made of wood and has a cavity inside, in which there are similar toys, but a little smaller. At the same time, they are necessarily inserted into each other.

Any tourist would never leave Russia without this chubby beauty in a bright sundress and shawl. It has long won worldwide fame and is now considered not just a traditional souvenir, but one of the cultural symbols of our country, along with the Dymkovo toy, samovar and balalaika. In this article, we will tell you how to make a nesting doll with your own hands from various materials: salt dough, fabric and even paper. We recommend crafting this craft together with your child, he will definitely like the creative process and the result.

Master class: textile nesting doll. Do it yourself do-it-yourself fabric

A beautiful nesting doll can simply be made from textiles. If you know how to sew at least a little, pay attention to the next simple and interesting workshop.



How is a nested doll done with your own hands? First you need to prepare the following materials and tools:

  • cotton fabric of different colors (plain white, beige, burgundy);
  • motley fabric with a floral pattern;
  • synthetic winterizer;
  • lace;
  • threads
  • a brush;
  • pencil;
  • scissors;
  • acrylic paints.

After acquiring everything you need and preparing the workplace, you can begin to work. Do-it-yourself nesting dolls made of fabric is very simple. You can use this pattern.



Technology for making crafts from textiles

Now we will tell you directly about how to sew a nested doll with your own hands. To get started, take a white cloth, selected for the top of the toy, and fold it in half. We transfer the pattern to it, but not the whole, but only its upper part. We cut along the contours and get two blanks that will become the face and back of the head of the toy. In one of the details we make a round hole. Next, take a beige fabric and cut a small square out of it. From a dark red cloth we cut out a strip, it will become a doll‚Äôs handkerchief. We also prepare a strip of lace equal to the length of the workpiece from burgundy material. Now we sew a lace braid to one dark red strip from one edge. Turn the part over and iron it on the front side. On the prepared beige square from one edge we impose the blank of the scarf. From above we close these two parts with a white cloth with a hole cut out in it. We fix all the elements with pins and sew them with a hem seam (or loop). Turning the product over, cut off the excess beige and dark red materials. The result is a head with a face and a handkerchief on the forehead.





We continue to create colorful textile souvenirs with our own hands.

We proceed to the manufacture of the body of the doll. To do this, take a colorful material with a floral print and fold it in half. We cut out the bottom of our nesting dolls (the lower part in the pattern). And now we add all the details (head, back of the head, two body elements) with the front side inward, slightly departing from the edge, mark the outline with a pencil and sew the product along it. Attention: the bottom of the nesting dolls is not yet sewn Through it we will fill the doll with a synthetic winterizer. We cut off the excess material, we turn out the toy and stuff it with a padding polyester. It is necessary to evenly distribute the filler and straighten it. After that, we sew the bottom of the toy with a blind seam. Well, now we decorate the matryoshka at our discretion: we sew the lace to the body (at the border of white and multi-colored materials), beads, sequins, beautiful buttons. Pencil out eyes, eyelashes, eyebrows, nose and lips. We color them with acrylic paints. We draw cheeks with blush. That's all, our textile nesting doll is ready!



We make a piece of salt dough

A magnificent painted nesting doll can be made not only from textiles, but also from other improvised materials. For example, very interesting crafts are obtained from salt dough. Try to make such a doll with your child, modeling not only brings satisfaction, but also develops fine motor skills. So, let's discuss how do-it-yourself nesting dolls are made from salt dough. Actually also very simple. First you need to cook the dough. We offer you such a tried and tested recipe. Take 1 cup of salt (small, extra), 2 cups flour, 250 ml of cold water and mix the ingredients. To them, add 1-1.5 tbsp. l vegetable oil. The dough according to this recipe is elastic and soft. Now we make a blank from cardboard - we cut out the silhouette of a nesting doll. We roll out a piece of dough with a rolling pin, giving it an oval shape. Moreover, its thickness should be at least 6 mm. We attach a cardboard silhouette and cut the matryoshka body on it. Next we sculpt the face (a small circle). A little later - pens, and then attach them to the sides of the workpiece. Using the stacks, we draw the eyes, mouth, cheeks. Now leave the workpiece to dry for a while.



Bright nesting doll. With our own hands we paint the product with watercolors

First, paint with beige paint the face of our nesting doll and palm. Then we draw a scarf in watercolor. Blue paint the sleeves, and white paint the shirt. We design a skirt with any bright watercolor. If desired, on a sarafan, you can draw some kind of pattern, for example, flowers, geometric shapes, etc. Now we proceed to the most difficult part of the work - draw eyes, nose and sponges. Do not forget about the hair of the doll. If desired, we also decorate the shirt, performing various patterns on it. After the paint dries, we cover the craft with varnish. That's all, the doll is done with your own hands! It can be glued to cardboard, decorate the background and framed. So you get a beautiful panel.



Let's make a doll from paper

Together with your children you can make a funny doll made of cardboard. For work, you need some materials and tools:

  • thick white A4 paper;
  • paints;
  • scissors;
  • ruler;
  • glue stick;
  • white cardboard;
  • cutting substrate.

A computer and printer are also required. After preparing the necessary "consumables" get to work. First print this template here.

Then cut out the details from paper. We apply the resulting blanks to cardboard, circle them and cut them along the contour.

We paint our craft

Now we proceed to decorate the nesting dolls. With gouache or watercolor, we begin to draw all the details. We decorate a scarf with pink paint, beige - a face and pens. We paint the skirt with red paint. The sleeves of the shirt can be made light green or blue. The hair of the pupa is made in brown. Next, draw small details - eyes, sponges, nose and cheeks. We decorate the craft as you like, for example, draw flowers or any fantasy patterns on the hem of a sundress. We also paint the second part (the back of the doll) with gouache and leave the craft to dry. After that, glue both parts on top and bottom on the protrusions using glue stick. Now the craft has gained stability.

Do-it-yourself nesting doll made of paper. By analogy, we make another smaller doll. We hope you and your children enjoyed our workshop.

Instead of a conclusion

So, in this article we talked about how you can easily and simply make a bright and elegant doll - a nesting doll. You can make magnificent interior things from salt dough, paper and fabric with your own hands and, importantly, spend your free time with benefit, introducing your child to creativity.

If desired, the nesting doll can be made from any other materials, such as plasticine, ready-made mass for modeling, "cold" porcelain and even knitting threads. Create and go for it!
