The formation of a pension is of interest to almost every working citizen. Today it remains to be seen how good the organization is the National NPF. What kind of company is this? What are the pros and cons of cooperation in this non-governmental fund clients? Should I apply here to form the funded part of the pension?

Description

National NPF is a non-state pension fund . It offers insurance and pension savings services. No other activity is carried out, which pleases customers.



NPF National is a typical representative of NPFs in Russia. In it, the open contribution is not only preserved, but also slightly increased. On this, all the activities of the company cease. When the time comes, the organization pays cash to customers. At any time, you can transfer the savings to another non-state pension fund.

Profitability

An important factor is such an indicator as profitability. Many potential investors pay attention to it. After all, some are not satisfied that pension savings will be safe and sound. I would like to multiply them a little more. And the more, the better.

National NPFs have good returns, according to statistics. It is about 13.21% per year. But customers say the opposite. Some contributors note that NPF National is not suitable for increasing pensions. The real yield here is about 5-8%. Therefore, counting on a significant increase in savings is clearly not worth it.



Due to such discrepancies, the National NPF receives mixed reviews from customers. Some say they are satisfied with the increase in their future retirement, while others call the organization deceivers and scammers. In fact, the discrepancy between the real situation and the statistics is easily explained by inflation. With confidence we can only say that NPF National does not offer the highest profitability. But the organization does not deceive its customers. Pension savings are really increasing. Though not as fast as we would like.
