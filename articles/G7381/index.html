"Gull"

The beach is located in the heart of Adler. It is considered the largest and most crowded. Entertainment in this part of the resort for every taste and budget. On the beach "Seagull" in Adler, you can meet dolphins. Vacationers say that bottlenose dolphins swim almost to the shore.

After sunset, the beach turns into a huge open-air concert venue. On improvised stage perform street musicians and dancers. Arrange fire shows, discos and foam parties.

The Seagull is one of the few sandy beaches of Adler. Therefore, it is popular among children. They swarm with pleasure on wide shallows, creating fancy castles and palaces.

"Southern seaside"
