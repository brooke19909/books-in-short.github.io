The best resorts of the Kaliningrad region: list, description, prices

At the beginning of the 19th century, doctors came to the conclusion that fresh sea air, sun, mineral water and healing mud can not only support health, but also help in the treatment of the most serious diseases. During this period, numerous European resorts were developed.

Today, the resorts of the Kaliningrad region are one of the leading resorts in the country, which have the highest quality of services for treatment and restoration of the body. There are a lot of sanatoriums in the region, both private, recently built, and working for more than a decade and have accumulated tremendous experience in various fields of medicine.

The main features of the resorts of the Kaliningrad region

The cold but gentle Baltic Sea, washing the shores of the westernmost region of Russia, welcomes guests with a unique climate and mild weather - mild summers and warm winters.

At the resorts of the region, a variety of methods are used to treat a wide range of diseases. Here you can find both the traditional spa treatment with peat mud, hydrocarbonate mineral water and through climatic features, as well as unique techniques that are characteristic only for this region. This is, for example, amber therapy.





Resorts of the Kaliningrad region are places where they successfully treat diseases of all body systems - from the nervous and cardiovascular to the digestive and respiratory. They also recover after operations, treat anti-stress programs and help to lose weight.

Pearl of the region - Svetlogorsk

Before becoming a part of the Soviet Union, Svetlogorsk bore the German name Raushen. Here, in the 19th century, the first baths were built, from which the resorts of the Kaliningrad region trace their history. Among the features of the city, it can be noted that it is located on a hill, so special descent and even a special elevator are equipped here to approach the sea. Due to the fact that the city is located in a small bay, the water here is warm and clear. The mild climate and special air are extremely healing.

The Curonian Spit is a nature reserve and national park, protected by UNESCO as a unique natural site. A special microclimate reigns here, allowing you to relax and gain strength, therefore, there are a large number of boarding houses in the district without rest. The Curonian Spit offers a unique opportunity to swim right away in a salt and fresh water body - the Baltic Sea and the freshwater Curonian Lagoon are adjacent here. How to get to the resorts of the Kaliningrad region

You can arrive by train, for example, from Moscow. Trains arrive at the South Station, from where you can go to the suburbs and to any resorts in the Kaliningrad region - again, by train, train or minibus, as well as take a regular taxi around the city and suburbs.

Arriving by plane will be met by Khrabrovo Airport, 20 km from the city. There is a bus to the city from the airport and, of course, there is a taxi.

You can also come to the region by car - through Lithuania or Poland, but be sure to check the necessary documents for transit.

What you need to visit the resorts of the Kaliningrad region

The special status and location of this region will require the preparation of certain documents. For arriving Russians, a passport is required and a Schengen visa may be required. A passport is needed if you are traveling by train or car, and only a Russian passport is sufficient for those arriving by plane. Schengen is also needed for those who travel by car through Lithuania (or you can apply for a double transit visa), and for those who want to travel to neighboring countries, being already in Kaliningrad.

Also, treatment documents in the sanatorium will require medical documents and a spa card.
