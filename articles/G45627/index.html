Hotel "Mriya" (Crimea, Yalta). Hotel "Mriya" in Simeiz

In 2014, a luxurious innovative complex was built in Yalta - the Mriya Hotel (Crimea). The doors of the hotel complex are hospitably open for tourists all year round. Its service is raised to the height of international standards of five-star hotels. The hotel was designed by British architect N. Foster.





The new Yalta health resort is unique and recognizable throughout the world. The infrastructure of the resort-hotel is thought out to the smallest detail. The luxurious resort hotel "Mriya" is a magnificent pearl that appeared on the Crimean coast. Location

The luxurious Mriya Hotel is located in the magnificent park of the Simeiz village. Simeiz, and hence the health resort located in it, is 25 kilometers from Yalta. The sanatorium is adjacent to the water park "Simeiz."

Hotel facilities

Guests are served in 4 restaurants and a bar. The resort complex has a well-maintained beach, strewn with small pebbles. Guests enjoy a swim in the outdoor or indoor pool.
