DIY snow globe: how to make an amazing gift

A snow globe is an amazing souvenir that for a long time reminds of the Christmas holidays. This is one of the most popular gifts. In this case, you can create a snow globe with your own hands. How to make this a miracle? Everything is quite simple. The main thing is to have everything at hand.



What is needed for this

What do you need to make a snow globe with your own hands? How to make a neat gift? To work, you will need:

  • Beautiful container made of transparent glass. It must be tightly closed. Otherwise, its contents will simply pour out. A jar of baby food is suitable for work.
  • Figurine. It will be inside the glass ball. Therefore, the figure must correspond to the size of the container.
  • Waterproof glue.
  • Liquid glycerin. This component should fill the tank 1/3. In addition, the amount of liquid glycerin depends on how you want the snow to fall. The main thing is not to add too much. Otherwise, the "snow" will not settle.
  • Water. It must be distilled, boiled or filtered. If you take water from the tap, then after a while the ball will lose its attractiveness and become cloudy.
  • Glue gun.
  • Various sequins, fine polystyrene and so on.




What will be needed for decoration

How to make a snow globe with your own hands for a gift? The finished product should be beautifully decorated, and this will require:

  • Scotch.
  • Satin ribbons.
  • Cardboard, preferably not very hard.
  • Scissors.
  • Self-adhesive film of golden color.
  • The brush is thin.
  • PVA glue.

Training

So, we will create a snow globe with our own hands. How to make the basis for the product? To begin with, it is worth thoroughly washing the glass container inside and out. You must remove the label. You also need to wash the additional jewelry and figures that will be inside the container. If this is not done, then the water inside the ball will quickly deteriorate. You can treat everything with boiling water.

DIY snow globe: how to do it?

When all the details are ready, you can get to work. To begin with, stick to the lid all the figures and additional parts with a glue gun. As an imitation, you can use beads, sparkles and tinsel. The latter option has certain subtleties.

It is necessary to take a container. If its volume is 500 milliliters, then 150 to 250 ml of glycerol should be poured into the jar. The remaining free space must be filled with water. It should be borne in mind that some of the volume will be taken by figures. Therefore, it is not recommended to pour water to the very edges.





Pour tinsel into the resulting liquid, and then gently stir everything with a teaspoon. If part of the sparkles floats on the surface, then they should be carefully removed. Leaving such tinsel in a container is not recommended, as it will not settle. As a result, the snow globe will not look very beautiful.

Final stage

Now we make a snow globe with our own hands, carefully immersing the figures fixed on the lid in a container of water. Details are recommended to tighten up a little. Due to this, all air bubbles should come out. The container must be tightly closed so that liquid does not leak out of it. For reliability, you can go with universal glue at the joints. This is required to secure the cover.

Decorate a snow globe

Now you know how to make a snow globe with your own hands. It remains to hide the lid and decorate the product a little. From dense, but not hard cardboard, you can cut strips and make a stand for the snow globe. After this, the blank must be pasted over with a golden film. The diameter of the stand should correspond to the diameter of the cover.





After that, the workpiece can be decorated with all kinds of twigs and ribbons. It all depends on your imagination. On the surface of the snow globe, you can draw curls of sparkles. This is usually done to hide the thread. To begin with, it is worth diluting PVA glue and water in a ratio of 1 to 1. You need to add sparkles to the resulting liquid and mix everything. For drawing it is better to use a thin brush.

In conclusion

So you can make a snow globe with your own hands. The master class is quite clear and simple, so almost everyone can create such a gift. A snow globe is not just a souvenir. This is a piece of magic that can be presented to both a child and an adult.
