DIY dog mask

After reading this article, you can make a beautiful dog mask on your own, which can be worn at a carnival or a children's party. You will learn several manufacturing methods - from paper, felt and polymer clay. So let's get started.

Paper mask of a dog on his head

To make such a mask is quite simple. You will need thick colored paper or cardboard, glue, scissors. If there is no colored paper, then you can take a simple one and decorate it later with paints, felt-tip pens or pencils. You can use the template that you see below.





First, cut the silhouette of the face out of brown cardboard. Then from pink make a triangular nose and mouth. Make cuts for the eyes. Make eyebrows out of black cardboard. Glue all the parts together. Cardboard dog mask is ready! Now all you have to do is sew or glue the elastic band for fastening on the head.





There may be many options for making such a mask. In addition, you can make a volumetric muzzle if you make it from triangles, which you then glue.

DIY felt dog mask

For the manufacture of masks, it is better to buy a thick and dense felt, as such material will wrinkle less and sit on your head better. You can use the template shown above, or draw your own, for example, as in the picture below.

It is worth noting that the mask itself is best made from thick and dense felt, eyes and nose can be made from thin. Sew an elastic band or two cords on the wrong side to secure the mask to the head. The dog mask made of felt is ready! If desired, you can make bangs on the head of faux fur.
