Pshad waterfalls. Excursion to the most beautiful places

In the wonderful Krasnodar Territory, near Anapa, there are amazing and incredibly beautiful Pshad waterfalls. They consist of a hundred sources, among which there are giant mountain rivers and calm streams flowing down the stones. Everyone can look at this miracle of nature, the main thing is to choose a suitable route and travel time.



Departing from Anapa and passing Novorossiysk, and after it Gelendzhik you get to Pshadsky waterfalls. The tour usually covers only eight sources, and also includes a walk along the waters of the Red River. On the way to the waters of this region, travelers also pass the ancient valley of dolmens, which were built on the territory of our country by the peoples who lived here long before the advent of the state system. It is believed that any desire made here comes true.



Departing from Anapa to Pshad Falls, tourists get the opportunity to see a monument to the sunken ships along the way, as well as a monument erected in honor of the sailors of the revolution. And, of course, just unforgettable moments spent in an SUV that moves along mountain cliffs and slopes. Therefore, if your vestibular apparatus is disturbed, it is better to refuse the trip.

When approaching directly to their destination, everyone usually stops in Pshad. In this village, tourists can have a snack, relax, but the most important thing is to swim in one of the most healing springs in the Krasnodar Territory called Natasha. After that, everyone rises on foot along the mouth of the river, which spills from nine meters high in powerful streams. It is in this place that Pshad waterfalls in all its glory become accessible to the eye. And also only here you can feel incredibly clean air with your whole body, from which, it seems, you can suffocate.



Based on the foregoing, we can conclude that such a trip will not be boring, moreover, you will forever be remembered as one of the most striking and extreme. That is why before the trip you need to properly equip yourself, choose the right clothes and shoes. It is worth noting that the Pshad waterfalls and the valleys that precede them are very steep and dangerous, so on your walking path always look under your feet and calculate strength. Local guides also recommend wearing the most comfortable and comfortable shoes so that nothing distracts you on the way.

Freshness, stunningly beautiful nature, mountain slopes and high-speed streams of clear water - all these are Pshad waterfalls. How to get to this area, you will tell your guide, who will accompany all tourists during this trip. Climbing on their own to the tops of these mountains is very problematic, since some sections need to be overcome on an SUV, and some should be walked. If you book this wonderful sightseeing tour, you can see the local nature in all its glory and power. Have a nice trip!
