Hostels in Tashkent: overview, addresses, reviews

The capital of Uzbekistan, Tashkent, is a city with a rich historical past. To see beautiful sights, cultural monuments, visit museums, many tourists come here every year. Despite the large number of international-class hotels, many travelers are interested in budget accommodation. Hostels in Tashkent will offer city guests comfortable accommodation at affordable prices.

Hostel "Topchan"

The hotel is located in the Mirabad district of Tashkent. The central railway station is 2 kilometers from here, and the international airport is 3 kilometers away. Within walking distance from the hostel you will find a supermarket, market, cafe. Guests can park their car at the free secure parking. Access to wireless Internet throughout the hotel is free. Upon request, an airport shuttle can be arranged for you at an additional cost. The hotel has an ATM, a mini market, luggage storage, and ticket service. The shared kitchen is equipped with modern kitchen facilities and a washing machine. To accommodate guests in the hostel "Topchan" offers several categories of rooms:

  • double economy with two single or one double bed - 1934 rubles per day for two and 1612 rubles for one person;
  • triple - 2579 rubles per day;
  • family quadruple (there is a bathroom) - 3224 rubles per day;
  • common room for women - 580 rubles per day for one place;
  • common room for women and men - 580 rubles per day for one place.

Room rates include breakfast. All rooms are equipped with a desk and lockers. Use of the bathroom and toilet is shared. Hostel "Topchan" is located in Tashkent at the address: Marta 8, 104.

Guests have access to a shared bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer. The hotel has seven rooms. To accommodate guests, the following options are offered:

"Art Hostel"

If you are interested in a hostel in Tashkent inexpensively with a pool, the Art Hostel is at your service. All rooms are decorated in a traditional Uzbek style. You can prepare anything in the shared kitchen, and within walking distance of the hotel there is a supermarket, bars and restaurants. The hotel has a library, bicycle rental and badminton equipment are provided. There is a currency exchange office, a bureau for organizing excursions. An airport shuttle can be arranged for an extra fee. Ticket service, laundry and ironing services are provided. Wireless internet access is free. Free private parking has an electric vehicle charging station. There are sun loungers and parasols around the pool. You can relax outdoors on the shaded terrace. For accommodation, guests are offered several categories of rooms:

  • six-seater for men and women - 709 rubles per day for one place;
  • six-seater for women - 709 rubles per day for one place;
  • general four-seater - 709 rubles per day for one place;
  • single economy - 1161 rubles per day;
  • double economy - 1594 rubles per day;
  • Triple standard - 2311 rubles per day;
  • four-bed economy - 2406 rubles per day.

Room rates include breakfast. Located "Art Hostel" in Tashkent at the address: Zanzhiborg street, 3.
