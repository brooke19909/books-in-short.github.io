What is a temporary disability certificate?

A sick leave is an official document issued by a medical institution as a confirmation of a citizen‚Äôs disability. You can issue it only by the number that you received a request for medical help (turn up at the clinic, hospital or call a doctor at home). The sheet reflects information about the patient, his place of work and position, date of treatment and diagnosis code. As treatment progresses, notes are made about the extension or closure of the sick-list. The document is fixed with the official stamp.





After recovery, the employee must go to work on the next business day after the closing date of the sick leave. The medical form is handed over to the accountant. The employee receives the allowance due some time after the calculations and accruals.

Who has the right to "go on sick leave"?

Of course, no one forbids a citizen to seek medical help and to be on outpatient or inpatient treatment. The only question is whether everyone can count on compensation for the earnings that would have been received if not for the disease? The main and quite obvious condition is to be officially employed. Only an employment contract drawn up in accordance with the Labor Code of the Russian Federation and providing a citizen with a ‚Äúsocial package‚Äù can 100% guarantee payments on a temporary disability sheet.

If desired, an individual has the right to independently transfer the amounts to the Social Insurance Fund, which forms the same experience affecting the payment of hospital benefits. This is most relevant for entrepreneurs and small businesses that work exclusively "for themselves."





The second requirement, of course, is the provision of the original medical document confirming the fact of illness and the inability to perform official duties. After fulfilling these two conditions, the employee gives it to the accountant, who asks the question: ‚ÄúWhat is the sick leave?‚Äù

For what cases a disability document is drawn up?

You can count on such a benefit only in cases established by law. Not every disease or treatment (especially preventive) will be the basis for the payment of benefits and the official pass of working days. A sick leave sheet is opened if:

  • an employee (including a non-resident of the Russian Federation) fell ill or was injured, and therefore cannot fulfill his duties (this includes cases such as termination of pregnancy or installation of a prosthesis);
  • a child under the age of 7-15 years old or another member of the family who needs care is sick;
  • preschool is closed for quarantine;
  • sanatorium-preventive procedures are appointed as the final stage of treatment;
  • the employee who was dismissed from the enterprise no more than 30 days before his illness and in the absence of a new position fell ill;
  • the employee goes on maternity leave (relying on maternity benefits).

In all these cases, a formally arranged citizen has every right to receive compensation for his earnings for ‚Äúsick days‚Äù. The exception is only those injuries and injuries that have been received as a result of alcohol and drug intoxication or caused specially.

Who pays the allowance?

The question of who and when has the right to open a disability sheet and receive the money due is clarified and closed. But what happens after the paper is submitted to the accounting department? How is sick leave considered, and most importantly, who pays the bills? 3 years ago, the obligation to pay disability benefits fell on the shoulders of the employer. Of course, the company then also received compensation from the Social Insurance Fund, which was completely equal to the amount paid on the sick-list.

After 2013 and to the present day, the payment procedure has changed a bit. Now, the FSS is directly and completely responsible for this process. All that is required of the company is to prepare and send the necessary documents.

The allowance is assigned from the first day indicated on the sick leave. In this case, the payment of the first three days is the responsibility of the employer. The amount for the rest of the period is transferred by the FSS.

How sick days are considered: rules and features

The amount due to the employee on the sick leave is directly affected by the length of service and the total wage, which is calculated for a certain billing period. In this regard, a number of rules and computational features arise. At the same enterprise, two employees and even for the same term can go on sick leave at the same time. However, the final payments received by them will be different.





How is sick leave considered correct? Follow the following sequence of calculations:

  1. Determine the billing period.
  2. Calculate earnings for a specified period.
  3. Calculate the average daily amount of earnings.
  4. Calculate the daily rate of payment, taking into account the experience.
  5. Set the total amount payable.

The definition of hospital payments still takes into account the length of service. It is divided into three time categories: less than 5.5 years, 5.5‚Äì8.5 years, more than 8.5 years. An incapable employee is entitled to 60, 80 and 100%, respectively, of the above specified length of service from the withdrawn result of the hospital allowance. Do not confuse insurance and seniority. In fact, an individual could work for 5 years or more, but the employer in bad faith fulfilled his obligations to transfer funds to the Social Insurance Fund, in connection with which the insurance experience is much shorter than the labor period.

In cases where the employee went on sick leave without having served six months, the total amount of payments should not exceed 1 minimum wage (from January 1, 2016 it is 6204 rubles).

Calculation of average earnings and total payments

The next step in calculating sickness benefits is to determine your average earnings. Knowing the revenue base for two years, it is easy to calculate the average daily payments. It is worth noting that the amount of annual wages has a limit. In 2015, it amounted to 670 thousand rubles. If the revenue base exceeds the established amount, the calculation should use the limit value according to the Labor Code of the Russian Federation. Calculation of average daily earnings is extremely simple to perform: income for the billing period is divided by 731.





Multiplying the number obtained by the insurance period, we find the amount of the due temporary disability benefit for one day. Increasing the amount of payment by the number of days of illness, we determine the total sick leave payment. How compensation is considered for the period of the illness in the standard case has been clarified. Consider other situations.
