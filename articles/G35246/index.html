The recreation center "Rus" in Tchaikovsky: description, entertainment, reviews

Bor, a large water area, strawberry and mushroom places, a sandy beach - these are not all the advantages of the Rus recreation center in Tchaikovsky. It will be interesting and fun to spend a vacation or weekend at any time of the year.

The recreation center "Rus" in Tchaikovsky: coordinates

The complex is located in the Perm region of the Republic of Udmurtia. It is located 5 km from the city of Tchaikovsky on the banks of the Kama River.





How to get to the recreation center "Rus" in Tchaikovsky? If you go from Perm, then after the traffic police post at a distance of 1.5 km a large poster with a picture of the complex will be placed. Here you need to turn left and go directly to the base.

In the case of travel from Udmurtia, you need to go along the main road through the whole of Tchaikovsky to the highway leading to Perm. Here after 5 km the sign "Recreation Center" is set. Near it you need to turn onto a forest road and move to your destination.

Description of the complex

The recreation center "Rus" (Tchaikovsky) has become a leader in the tourism competition of the Volga region. Such a high award was obtained as a result of evaluating many criteria:

  • location;
  • cost of living;
  • quality of service;
  • the availability of recreational activities;
  • food quality;
  • reviews of tourists.

The recreation center "Rus" in Tchaikovsky is surrounded by forests and on the banks of the river. Thus, you can combine relaxing on the water and walking along the forest. According to experts, there is an ecologically clean area.





Rooms from economy class to suites are available for tourists. Therefore, people with different incomes and everyday needs can relax here. The complex has thought out entertainments that will make the pastime active and eventful at any time of the year.

Accommodations

The recreation center "Rus" in Tchaikovsky provides its guests with cozy rooms of different comfort classes:

  • A country detached cottage for 20 people is designed to stay throughout the year. It has modern heating equipment. In total, the house has 5 rooms accommodating 4 people. They have double and single beds, bedside tables, wardrobes, tables and chairs. The cottage has a kitchen with modern appliances. There is also a spacious banquet hall with karaoke. The house has several bathrooms and showers. On the street there is a picnic area with barbecue, a cauldron and firewood.
  • The suite consists of 2 rooms. The room has a spacious double bed, TV with satellite channels, wardrobe, small refrigerator, microwave, electric kettle. The living room is equipped with a folding sofa and other furniture. The room has its own shower and toilet.
  • Junior suite is one room with a double bed, TV, wardrobe. microwave, electric kettle and small fridge. It has a bathroom and shower.
  • Standard rooms can accommodate up to 5 people. They are equipped with single beds, TV, electric kettles and small wardrobes. Bathroom and shower in the common corridor.
  • Economy rooms have a minimum of necessary furniture and are designed for up to 5 people. The toilet and shared shower are on the street.
  • In summer houses from 2 to 6 people can live. They have beds and bedside tables. A bathroom and shower are shared by everyone in the complex.

All rooms have free internet. Bed linen is changed every 2-3 days. The maids in the rooms are cleaned daily.

Catering and organization of banquets

On the territory of the recreation center "Rus" in the city of Tchaikovsky there is a spacious dining room. Here guests can pay three meals a day or choose one of the meals per day, at their discretion.





For tourists who live in suites and junior suites, breakfast is included in the price. Guests who stay in standard, economy class rooms and summer houses are provided with 3 meals a day in the dining room. Its cost is included in the price of accommodation.

For food guests use Russian, dietary and vegetarian cuisine. Tourists can additionally order traditional oriental dishes. Especially popular are kebabs and pilaf.

The complex has 3 banquet rooms with different capacity for guests:

  • winter (up to 80 people);
  • summer (up to 100);
  • small (up to 20).

When ordering dishes only from the local menu, the administration provides rental rooms for free.

Entertainment

At the recreation center "Rus" in Tchaikovsky, various events are thought out for an interesting pastime at any time of the year. In the summer season, tourists can enjoy water activities. At their service:

  • equipped beach with a children's area;
  • riding a banana and a tablet;
  • boat and boat rental;
  • walks on the ship "Rus".

A playground and volleyball and soccer areas are available on site. And also for guests various show programs are arranged:

  • fist fights;
  • sumo wrestling;
  • discos of the 80s;
  • foam parties, etc.

The complex has table tennis tables. There is also a billiard room.

Fans of active and extreme recreation can fight paintball. There is the necessary equipment, and an interesting zone for the game is equipped in the forest strip.





In winter, tourists can go skiing and ice skating. The necessary equipment can be taken from the administrator. And also throughout the year, there is a sauna in the territory where you can steam using birch brooms.

The complex has a cafe with karaoke. Here several times a week various shows and discos are held.
