4-star Rhodes hotels: description and reviews of tourists

Many thousands of years ago, Rhodes was a major trading and economic center. Today it is a favorite place for a huge number of tourists. They are attracted by both natural attractions and cultural sites, as well as Rhodes hotels (4 stars), which can offer excellent conditions for a comfortable and pleasant stay.

The hotel was built in 2002 and consists of a main three-story building and a number of two-story bungalows. In total, Lindos Princess has 582 rooms, each of which is equipped with new furniture, which not all Rhodes hotels (4 stars) can boast of. Hotel Riviera

Looking at Rhodes hotels (4 stars), one cannot ignore the Riviera. This hotel is located on a busy promenade. Nearby are numerous nightlife, taverns, a popular beach, as well as travel agencies offering various excursions, thanks to which tourists can better see authentic villages, excellent landscapes, as well as taste delicious local dishes.

The Riviera Hotel offers 64 individually furnished rooms with individual air conditioning and TV. Guests at Riviera can enjoy sea or city views while relaxing on the separate loggia each room is equipped with.

The hotel has a swimming pool, and nearby is its own luxurious beach.





Sunshine Rhodes Hotel: leisure facilities

In the resort of Trianta (6 km from the airport), Sunshine Rhodes is located. Its rooms are distinguished by luxurious decoration and the presence of climate control, which distinguishes Rhodes hotels (4 stars) from many others. Some rooms have wonderful sea views. All rooms have a private bathroom with a hairdryer. They also have a mini fridge and satellite TV.

The hotel has several bars, a Zorbas tavern and two restaurants - Dionissos and Mamma Mia. At any time of the day, vacationers can quench their thirst with juices or other drinks. The tavern serves original Greek cuisine, while Mamma Mia serves a variety of Italian dishes. Buffet breakfasts and lunches are served at Dionissos Restaurant.

Sunshine Rhodes, like many other hotels in Rhodes (4 stars), offer their vacationers all the conditions for an active pastime. Vacationers can play mini-golf or beach volleyball. In addition, those who wish can rent a mountain bike or attend an aerobics class. Children can swim in the pool or play on a special playground.

Hotel reviews of Rhodes hotels

Rhodes hotels (4 stars) reviews are mostly positive. All vacationers praise comfortable and clean rooms, friendly staff, an excellent animation team, as well as numerous opportunities for active pastime. Families with children also mention that an extra bed is installed in the room upon request and does not require additional money.

A lot of good has been said about the well-groomed and spacious areas of 4 * Rhodes hotels. Only some tourists make small complaints about breakfast, claiming that they are too monotonous. Pebble beaches, which are characteristic of many resorts of Rhodes, also cause some discontent, however, this problem can be solved by purchasing special shoes sold in many local stores.
