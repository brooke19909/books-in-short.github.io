The best shopping centers of Istanbul

The modern city, located on two continents, continues to attract tourists with completely different, sometimes very exotic tastes. Constantinople was a very important point of the Great Silk Road, and now Istanbul is rightfully considered one of the main cities for good shopping.

The opportunity to choose unique goods starts from the Grand Bazaar itself, crosses the Golden Horn Bay, rushing on opposite sides from the Galata Tower, from fashion boutiques of Istanbul designers to huge shopping complexes.

Since many travelers wish to devote part of their time in the former capital of the Byzantine Empire to global shopping, with the help of the recommendations of the famous Turkish newspaper Hurriyet ("Hurriyet"), the online magazine Mymerhaba ("Maymerhaba") and tips collected on the shopping forums, You can make a list of the five largest and most popular shopping centers in Istanbul.
