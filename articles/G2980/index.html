How to close a credit card of Trust Bank?

It is important to clarify the presence of debt on it when closing a credit card. If there is a debt, it will need to be paid, if the card balance is positive, the balance must be collected. After that, when the credit card balance is 0 rubles, it will be possible to proceed to its closure.

To do this, you will need to contact the branch of Trust Bank and write a statement about closing the credit account on which the funds were provided. In addition, it is important to clarify and disable all services connected to the card.

It should be noted that a certain period of time elapses between submitting an application and closing a card, in case some transactions made with a credit card could be delayed and arrive later. At Trust Bank, such a period is 35 days.
