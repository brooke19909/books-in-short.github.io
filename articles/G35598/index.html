Tourist Gelendzhik: entertainment and leisure options for tourists

Resorts of the Krasnodar Territory today are not only a close and affordable vacation for Russians, but also an option for a comfortable and interesting vacation. The infrastructure of the region is developing rapidly. This is especially noticeable in large cities. For example, Gelendzhik entertainment offers its guests the most diverse today.





Olympus Park - all kinds of entertainment in one place

Where to go on vacation all day with family or alone? In the city of Gelendzhik and its environs there are several such places at once. One of the most popular is the Olympus Amusement Park. Gelendzhik offers its guests a variety of leisure options. But the maximum amount of entertainment in one territory you will find here. At Olympus you can visit the oceanarium, zoo and terrarium. Young visitors to the complex can play in the children's town, and their parents will be interested in playing in the bowling club or visiting a concert hall. Tourists are also offered options for active pastime: fishing, jeeping, horseback riding, ATV rental. Want to see all of Gelendzhik in front of you? Fun that will not leave indifferent in the Olympus Park is the cable car and the observation deck at an altitude of 600 meters above sea level.

What else to do in Gelendzhik?

If you decide to settle in a resort town during your vacation, the rest will not be boring. In and around Gelendzhik there are many comfortable beaches. Many of them have small water parks - inflatable slides and other attractions, hydromassage installations and other entertainments. Tourists are also offered boat trips, catamaran rides, paragliding, parachuting. Be sure to try scuba diving if your place of relaxation is Gelendzhik. For fun, you can also try windsurfing or waterskiing. There are enough nightclubs, bars in this city, where you can dance in the evenings, and water parks offer foamy parties. Local travel agencies offer vacationers a variety of excursions to nearby natural attractions and to neighboring cities. Gelendzhik is a resort that is ideal for family or individual holidays, here everyone will find something to their taste.
