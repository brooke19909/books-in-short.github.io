Aeropark (Bryansk): description, photos, reviews

In November 2013, one of the largest Russian shopping and entertainment centers opened its doors. At Aeropark Bryansk, guests are invited to visit a variety of shops, cozy cafes and restaurants, beauty salons. According to reviews, the mall has a beautiful and stylish interior, here you can not only make pleasant purchases every day, but also have a good rest and have fun with the whole family.





For the convenience of guests there are car and bicycle parking. According to the authors of the reviews, at the SEC “Aeropark” (Bryansk), guests of all ages find something interesting for themselves. Many become his regular visitors.

Aeropark (Bryansk): acquaintance

A popular shopping complex in Bryansk (Russia) is located at: st. Bypass, 30. Works from 10 to 22 hours. Aeropark (Bryansk), the photo of which is presented in the article, is one of the largest shopping and entertainment centers in the region. The complex has more than 200 shops, 15 restaurants and cafes, several beauty salons, has an eight-room multiplex “Luxor” and an entertainment center “Boomerang”. In addition, there is the largest extreme park in the city, Atmosphere, as well as Jungle Call, the largest contact zoo in the Russian Federation.

Brands

Aeropark (Bryansk) is one of the first shopping and entertainment centers in the city to bring together such popular brands as The Snow Queen, Decathlon, Bershka, Zara, House, Cropp, Pull & Bear, Stradivarius, Reserved Sinsay. In addition, guests are given the opportunity to visit the shops "Daughters and Sons", Terranova, hypermarkets OBI, Media Markt, "Lenta", as well as furniture stores "Furniture Hall".

Entertainment

In “Aeropark” Bryansk, festive events for the whole family are regularly held. Guests have the opportunity to attend sports, concerts with the stars, exhibitions, fashion shows and much more. Particular attention is paid to young visitors, for whom fascinating free programs are held at the Aviator Kids Club.





In addition to shops, the center has a wardrobe, a fitness club, a food court, a food court, photo printing and a children's room with entertainment. The presence of a playroom is an excellent opportunity for parents to leave their children under the supervision of experienced teachers while visiting sales.

Facilities, infrastructure

Many people call the airport a center, without which the city can no longer do. Today, the sales area of ​​63,000 square meters. m accommodated more than 200 Russian and international operators. On the territory of Aeropark in Bryansk there is a convenient parking for 3500 cars, as well as a large bicycle parking. For those who are unable to come by car, you can use a fixed-route taxi (free), which runs according to the schedule every half hour to different points of the city, which, according to visitors, is very convenient. Minibus awaits at the entrance to the shopping center, on its reverse side.

Cinema "Aeroparka" in Bryansk

Luxor cinema in the shopping and entertainment center is in high demand among visitors. The poster of the cinema is located both in the premises of the center and on the site. Online you can not only familiarize yourself with the schedule of sessions, but also purchase tickets. Guests who visited the Bryansk Cinema at Aeropark, leave the most positive reviews.

Luxor Cinema, opened in November 2013, is called one of the best movie venues in the network. The presence of an ultramodern multiplex in the Aeropark shopping center, according to visitors, is a big plus: guests can make purchases and then relax while watching a fascinating movie. The cinema has a spacious lobby and eight comfortable cinema halls, accommodating more than 1900 guests and equipped in accordance with international standards. There is also a VIP lounge seating 48 spectators.





The cinema is equipped with sound and projection equipment from the best manufacturers, screens with a special coating that allows for demonstration with clarity of the frame - HFR. There are popular movie display formats: 2D and 3D. In the big hall, you can use the innovative Dolby Atmos multi-channel sound system. In the cinema "Luxor" ("Aeropark") you can visit the beer restaurant "Posadoffest".

Cinema reviews

The authors of the reviews note that the cinema has a large area, a huge bar and many places where guests can wait for the start of the sessions. There is a dressing room and spacious toilets in which there are never any queues. Guests do not respond so positively to the work of cash desks: they note that large queues are always created here, and often some cash desks do not work. But users are given the opportunity to purchase a ticket in one of the four available self-service terminals by card or in cash. The interface, as noted by visitors, is quite simple and intuitive, so tickets can be bought in a few minutes.

In the main bar, visitors share, there is a wide selection of various sweets that you can enjoy while watching the movie: here you can buy standard popcorn, soda (draft and bottled), hot dog, nachos, cocktail, beer, ice cream and much more. Guests who buy a lot of drinks are provided with special trays. Visitors like the fact that the bartenders of the cinema are well-mannered, dexterous and constantly smile at the guests. Many families visit the Aeropark shopping center (Bryansk) every weekend. Cinema reviews receive in large quantities. People gratefully note the friendliness and helpfulness of all the staff.





Those guests who were “lucky" to be late and had to enter the hall when the trailers were shown, when the lights were already off, do not risk stumbling in the dark at all: the hall is equipped with lights for rooms and steps. The aisles are wide enough, sitting people do not have to cuddle up to the seats, letting in the latecomers. Armchairs are also very popular with viewers, they are called soft, comfortable, of decent width and quite high. Not a single head will be higher than a chair in your field of vision, and there is absolutely no need to look for the screen gap between the tops of the spectators sitting in front.

Many visitors praise the sound and image quality. Ticket prices refer to the average price range in the city. The authors of the reviews acknowledge that Luxor in the SEC “Aeropark” (Bryansk) has become their favorite movie theater. A visit to the institution is recommended to all moviegoers.
