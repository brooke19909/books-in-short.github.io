Samsung ML-2160 - a great entry-level laser printer

Low cost combined with good technical characteristics makes Samsung ML-2160 one of the best solutions in the segment of economy printers. The advantages of this model of peripheral printing solutions include impressive print volume and high speed. The printer is great for implementing small local printing systems.







Niche print node. Equipment

Reviews of Samsung ML-2160 confirm that this is an entry-level printer, because it has a limit of 10,000 pages per month by default. The minimum set of possible connection methods, the mediocre quality of the output documentation and the affordable cost of the laser printing unit are far from a complete list of factors that indicate the budget affiliation of the laser printer. As a result, the product will fit perfectly into the composition of small local printing systems. It can be a small office, a house, or a small computer workgroup. The scope of supply for this peripheral product includes:

  • a printer;

  • cartridge;

  • warranty card;

  • communication wires and power adapter;

  • user's manual;

  • CD with software and full version documentation.





Paper and performance specifications

The largest page size this printer can work with is A4. In addition to it, support for other formats is also implemented at the software level - A5, A6, folio, letter, B5, B6 and even C5. The nominal density of the substrate should be in the range from the least permissible 60 g / m 2 to the maximum possible 163 g / m 2 . The device can display the first page with dimensions A4 in really fantastic 8.5 seconds. In the future, a fixed print speed of 20 sheets per minute. The format of the output documents is 1200X1200.

Interfaces power usage

Samsung ML-2160 is classified as a low-end laser printer . Therefore, you do not have to expect an extended set of connection methods. The only interface for switching is represented by standard USB. Its main modification for connection is 2.0, but it is possible to use the rest of its versions. In extreme power saving mode, the printer consumes only 0.9 watts. When you enter standby mode, this value increases to 30 watts. During operation, the level of energy consumption increases to 310 watts.

Cartridge. Drivers

The cartridge model for this printer is the Samsung MLT-D101S. Its nominal volume is only 700 pages. There is also an improved version of the cartridge for such a laser printer - this is the MLT-D101X. In this case, the print limit is 1,500 sheets. You can refill and reuse the cartridges, but then you need the updated Samsung ML-2160 printer firmware, in which such restrictions will be removed. Only two types of operating systems have drivers. This is Windows and MacOS. But Linux and UNIX do not have drivers for this peripheral device.





Brief setup algorithm

The setup procedure for using the Samsung ML-2160 consists of the following steps:

  1. Unpack the shipping cardboard box. We extract the contents from it.

  2. We install the printer at the place of use, which should be next to the system unit of the computer.

  3. We remove transport tape and other fastenings from the device.

  4. We connect external wires to the computer and the power supply network.

  5. We apply voltage. At the end of initialization, install the cartridge.

  6. We install drivers and other necessary software.

  7. We reboot the computer system in order for the changes to finally take effect.

  8. We print a test page.

Cost. Owners opinion

The printer Samsung ML-2160 is currently priced at 4,500-5,000 rubles . Owner reviews indicate such shortcomings:

  • the minimum set of wired interfaces, which boils down to just USB;

  • average print quality due to printing technology;

  • lack of drivers for operating systems of the Linux and UNIX families.

The advantages of the printer include:

  • high speed;

  • the lowest costs for basic and auxiliary consumables;

  • impressive print volume per calendar month;

  • simplicity and ease of setup, which even a poorly trained user can handle.





Summary


Samsung ML-2160 is a balanced, cost-effective solution for organizing a small local or network printing subsystem . Given the positioning and cost of the printer, there are no significant drawbacks. But the advantages of this peripheral device are much greater, and they allow it to be successfully used both at home and in a small office. True, in other cases, the use of such a printer is unjustified. Instead, it is preferable to use devices with more advanced specifications and higher cost. This will optimally organize the documentation printing subsystem.
