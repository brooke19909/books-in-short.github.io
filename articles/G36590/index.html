Video baby Philips Avent SCD 603: description and reviews

The digital baby video monitoring system (or Philips Avent SCD 603 baby monitor) is designed for remote monitoring of the baby. This gadget aims to facilitate the life of a young family in caring for a newborn. The baby monitor has a huge number of advantages that encourage young parents to buy a digital system.

What is a baby monitor?

This gadget consists of two parts: one part (receiver) is installed in the room with the child, the second part (transmitter) is taken by parents to the room in which they will be. The Philips Avent SCD 603 baby monitor comes with a charger, holder and a special cord. It allows you to mount the transmitter on the parent's belt.

The Philips Avent baby monitor offers convenience to the customer in that he can independently adjust the necessary parameters, such as screen brightness and contrast. This feature allows you to achieve better image quality and clearer video shooting baby. The device has not only the ability to control the condition of the newborn, but also transmits sound of excellent quality, which further facilitates the life of a young family.

The Philips Avent SCD 603 baby monitor, according to customer reviews, has a huge number of advantages that allow you not to worry about the safety and comfort of the child. Most young families prefer to use this device from the first days of the life of a newborn.

The benefits of a baby monitor

Like any device, a digital video monitoring system for a child has several advantages that encourage customers to make a choice when purchasing a particular model.

The Philips Avent SCD 603 00 baby monitor, according to reviews, has a number of such advantages:

  1. A rather large radius of action is 150 m, which does not decrease considering the walls and other high furniture. The device has high-frequency waves that pass through obstacles and transmits an image of excellent quality.
  2. Powerful battery that lasts for 12 hours and reduces the need for additional charge. The Philips Avent baby monitor, according to reviews, works all night long after the battery is fully charged.
  3. Ability to control volume and control low charge.
  4. The baby monitor is equipped with a unique system that allows you to get a clear image at night without additional illumination.
  5. The Philips Avent SCD 603 baby monitor allows parents to remotely play lullabies for their baby. Thus, if necessary, mom or dad, without looking up from their affairs, will be able to reassure the child with the help of this gadget.

Buyers consider the advantages of a baby monitor to be quite low, which allows most families to purchase a device, as well as the ability to connect multiple cameras to monitor a child moving around the house.

Customer reviews

The Philips Avent SCD 603 baby monitor has a fairly large number of positive customer reviews. They note that the instruction that comes with this system is written in an accessible language and allows you to quickly understand the functions of the device.

The baby monitor is easily and quickly installed in the room of the newborn and can be attached to the wall. The device is securely attached to all surfaces, it is almost impossible to remove if the child is already quite old and can try to get a camera.

The Philips Avent baby monitor, according to reviews, does not make loud sounds that can scare or wake a baby. In the settings, you can set the volume of sounds, put the device to sleep mode, which allows you to not turn on the indicator of a low percentage of battery charge. Buyers also note the excellent image quality on the screen, which makes it possible to see even the smallest details in the picture.

The system recognizes movements and reacts to them with the movement of the camera. This allows parents to have a wide range of visibility of the child, and many of them prefer cameras that are with a motion sensor. The Philips Avent baby monitor has several models in its assortment, but the SCD 603 is one of the best among a large selection.

Conclusion

Each family independently makes decisions on the purchase of this system, after weighing all the pros and cons. The benefits of using the Philips Avent baby monitor are huge, while there are no flaws in customer reviews. She is able to make life easier for a young family several times in the process of growing up the baby. The baby monitor is an affordable gadget that can be purchased at many hardware stores.
