Capsule coffee machines: review, specifications and reviews

Capsule coffee machines, an overview of which will be given in this article, burst into our lives rapidly. Their popularity among lovers of a fragrant drink is growing at the same rapid pace. Sales of these devices are growing quite fast, and therefore among their users serious disagreement erupts: ‚ÄúDo I need capsule coffee machines for the home, and which manufacturer is best to choose?‚Äù

Appearance story

The inventor of the capsule coffee machine is Eric Favre. The new device was patented by him in 1978. Soon, unusual coffee brewing machines began to be produced by some companies. However, the first models of such devices, according to their characteristics, were far from ideal.





Capsule coffee machines gained their popularity only in the late eighties of the last century. Then some models of these devices appeared in mass production.

Distribution in the Russian market

In our country, unusual devices for brewing coffee have appeared relatively recently. However, customers quickly appreciated their convenience and ease of use. And today, capsule-type coffee machines that are relatively affordable for a wide range of consumers help many Russians perfectly prepare a fragrant drink. Such an appliance independently brews unsurpassed coffee, and does it in a matter of seconds.

Today in the consumer market you can buy such devices from dozens of well-known manufacturers. These include the largest concerns that produce not only devices for brewing a fragrant drink, but also coffee capsules for them.

It is worth noting that the manufacturers of such equipment do not stop there. That is why the technology of the process produced by such a coffee machine is constantly being improved. As a result, customers are happy to use the equipment they offer.

Making a Flavored Drink

How does a capsule coffee machine work? Instructions for its use indicate that the process of preparing a fragrant drink is impossible without the use of special capsules. These are small packages in which ground natural coffee is located. Other ingredients may be included. Capsules are packaged in such a way that the volume of each of them is enough to prepare a portion for one person.



The package with ground grains is placed in the machine and punctured using a special device. Hot water passes through the openings thus obtained, the pressure of which ranges from fifteen to nineteen bar. At this time, coffee is brewing. Moreover, the duration of this process is only a few seconds. In addition to the convenience of brewing, user reviews also note that the coffee machine does not need to be washed after the work it has done. To clean the device, simply remove the packaging that has already been used.

Advantages of coffee makers

All capsule coffee machines, the review of which is on the websites of their companies, are convenient devices that allow users to prepare a fragrant drink very quickly and without any extra effort. In addition, the main advantages of a number of models available on the consumer market include the fact that:
- for the preparation of a fragrant, tasty and high-quality drink does not require any special skills;
- it is possible to easily obtain such complex drinks as latte and cappuccino, mokachino and many others;
- the device works much quieter than classic coffee machines;
- such devices have an affordable price, affordable for a wide range of customers.

Disadvantages of capsule machines

Customer reviews point to some negative aspects of the device for brewing a fragrant drink from special packaging. The most important of these is the limited choice of coffee. So, a person who has in his arsenal a classic device has the ability to brew various drinks. They will differ in strength, cooking methods, as well as varieties of coffee. All this cannot be done with a capsule machine. Of course, manufacturers produce various types of single-serving packages of coffee for such devices. But, despite this, their range is still limited. This leads to the fact that the user can prepare only what the manufacturer intended.

Another negative side of the capsule coffee machine is the high cost of consumables. This significantly increases the price of one cup of aromatic drink.

Despite the weaknesses of capsular coffee machines, buyers choose these units. Users prefer the guaranteed taste of their favorite drink. But before buying such an easy-to-use device, it is worthwhile to consider in more detail capsule coffee machines offered on our market. An overview of the main such systems is necessary for study in connection with a large assortment of goods and various operating principles.

This system of capsule preparation of an aromatic beverage was invented in the USA. Its developer was the company Kraft Foods, which produces units in conjunction with the company Bosch.

The Tassimo system has significant differences from those described above. So, any Bosch Tassimo capsule coffee machine is equipped with a low-power pump, giving out only 3.3 bars. This does not interfere with the process of preparing the drink due to the fact that the capsules for Tassimo produce much larger sizes than their existing counterparts. Moreover, they have the form of a disk, in which there are nine grams of uncompressed coffee. In this regard, Tassimo capsule coffee machines do not need powerful water pressure to knock out the necessary aroma. An unpressed obsessed drive easily gets into your cup.
