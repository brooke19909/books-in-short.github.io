Navajo Bay. Paradise for lovers

Greece is an amazing country that can be proud of almost everything: the ancient culture, on the basis of which the whole world is developing, and the divine nature, and the fertile climate, and the transparent sea. And the sights of this country are its hospitable people and delicious cuisine, ancient architectural masterpieces and heroic legends about the past.



Each island of Hellas is not like any other. Each piece of land, immersed in the emerald green palm trees and turquoise waves of the Mediterranean Sea, is special. The fashionable Rhodes, the majestic Athens, the mythical Ithaca, setting on the pacification of Crete have long been appreciated by tourists. And for those who want to protect themselves from the busy bustle of megacities, there is Zakynthos island. Navajo Bay here is a beautiful place in the country, very quiet. In this paradise, you can retire, swim in warm water, take sun baths, lying on a magnificent clean beach surrounded by majestic cliffs. The observation deck, which is located high above sea level, will give you a breathtaking landscape: a bird's eye view of nature. Photos from here will decorate your collection. And you will remember your vacation with pleasure for a very long time.

Shipwreck Bay, aka Navagio Bay, Zakynthos Island, Greece - this is an exceptional place where you can only get by water. Therefore, you should take care of renting a boat in advance, sign up for a yacht cruise or a sea excursion. Also, travelers like to take a boat trip around the entire island.



But, of course, a fascinating adventure begins with a hotel. Hotels on the island of Zakynthos are the most diverse in style and price. All with impeccable service, they will delight you with a nutritious breakfast and transfer to the port by minibus. Amazing impressions will give you the opportunity to meet the dawn on a snow-white yacht, to watch the city wake up under a salty breeze. The voyage can be accompanied by interesting stories from the guide.

Navajo Bay has another name - Smugglers' Bay, because in the middle of the beach lies the rusted skeleton of the Panagiotis ship. Previously, it belonged to smugglers who abandoned it after another breakdown. The appearance of the ship will fill the tourists with the spirit of adventurism characteristic of pirates of the last century.



Azure water is surprisingly clear. Snow-white sands are surrounded on three sides by impregnable cliffs. They attract fans of extreme sports. Navajo Bay is a popular destination for base jumpers, amateurs and professionals. Adrenaline rush is guaranteed to you even from watching extremals, if you are not ready to jump down yourself.

An exciting and exciting journey awaits you. Navajo Bay knows how to surprise you. But also do not deny yourself the pleasure of walking on foot inland, breathing in the fragrant air and listening to the birds singing. Complete peace and tranquility, absolute relaxation and peace. The incredible island of Zakynthos is waiting for you!
