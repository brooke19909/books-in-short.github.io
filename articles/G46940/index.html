Hotel Grand Hotel Kempinski: description, rating, reviews of tourists

Naturally, from time to time, every person wants to relax from the daily bustle. Picturesque landscapes, warm sea and clean beaches - this is what you need in order to relax physically and emotionally. The Black Sea coast is very popular among tourists. And not everyone has the opportunity to relax abroad.

A great place to spend a vacation or even a weekend is the coast of Gelendzhik. Over the past few years, many hotels of different sizes and categories have been opened here. Hotel Grand Hotel Kempinski is considered one of the brightest and most comfortable.

Of course, during vacation planning, each person tries to find the maximum amount of information about the place in which he is going to stay. So what does Kempinski Grand Hotel 5 * look like? What conditions can a traveler expect? Can I eat and have fun at the hotel? How do other tourists respond about it? This information will be useful to many readers.

Hotel Grand Hotel Kempinski: where is it located?





An important point for any hotel complex is the location. The location of the Hotel Grand Hotel Kempinski is one of the advantages of the hotel, as it is easy to get to almost anywhere in the resort area from here. The hotel was built on Cape Tolstoy, and therefore from any point it offers a wonderful view of the bay. From here begins the famous Gelendzhik embankment throughout the country, which stretches for almost ten kilometers.

Gelendzhik city center is within 4 km. Some tourists prefer to cover this distance on foot, but those guests who do not like such long walks can rent a car. You can get from the airport in the city for 5-10 minutes, which is also convenient. The famous resort town of Anapa is about 2 hours away.

Short description of the hotel area

Hotel Grand Hotel Kempinski is a large multi-storey building located very close to the seashore. The hotel owns a large territory, which is generously decorated with greenery and flower beds. On the territory there are fountains that bring relief in the summer heat, benches where you can relax, and even a small park area with paths where you can spend time alone with your own thoughts.

By the way, in 2010 the hotel was completely renovated and today meets all tourist standards.

How many rooms are on site?

This hotel is quite large and consists of 379 rooms of different categories:

  • standard rooms with panoramic windows and furnished balconies, their area is 29 square meters;
  • Superior standard rooms measuring 31-33 square meters with a spacious balcony and a king size bed;
  • 64 Superior Twin Rooms;
  • 45 deluxe rooms with stylish designs and panoramic windows that overlook the bay;
  • 59 spacious deluxe rooms with twin beds and sea view;
  • 12 large suites, which consist of a bedroom and a spacious living room with dining and working area (there is appropriate furniture);
  • 18 suites with two rooms and breathtaking sea views;
  • 26 combined deluxe rooms, which, if desired, turn into spacious, three-room rooms, which are great for relaxing with a large family or company;
  • 14 suites located in the bungalow, they consist of two spacious rooms and two separate bathrooms;
  • 14 three- room suites, which are also great for a whole family;
  • 3 luxurious presidential rooms located on the eighth floor of the hotel; their area is 200 square meters; There are several rooms with stylish and comfortable furniture, as well as large terraces with panoramic windows and cutting-edge appliances.

Hotel Grand Hotel Kempinski: photo and description of rooms





A comfortable, cozy room is just what a tourist needs in order to relax from an eventful day. Regardless of which room you are staying in, you can be sure that you will have a good time here. All rooms are furnished with modern furniture, and the necessary set of equipment is also present.

You can use a modern air conditioning system. There is also a telephone, a TV (you can watch satellite channels), as well as a small coffee maker and an electric kettle. There is a safe for storing small items, as well as a minibar. Some rooms have a desk and a well-equipped workplace. Guests can enjoy free internet access.

The bathroom has a modern shower and, of course, hot and cold water. Also, each guest receives a set of towels, a soft bathrobe and slippers. There is a powerful hair dryer for drying hair. The rooms are cleaned every day, and bed linen is changed 2-3 times a week.

Meals for guests





For many tourists, good food is part of a quality vacation. What does the Kempinski Grand Hotel Gelendzhik offer? The price includes daily breakfast. Tables for guests are served in the main hotel restaurant called Riviera. Here you will find hot appetizers, vegetable salads and toasts, homemade cakes, for dessert - real masterpieces from the chef. Of course, you can count on aromatic coffee and your favorite tea varieties.

Are there any other catering establishments?

Hotel Grand Hotel Kempinski offers its guests to visit the restaurant "Fusion", which is located on the eighth floor. Here you can enjoy luxurious dishes, grilled meats, as well as breathtaking panoramic views from large windows. And guests are offered a choice of a large number of quality wines.





And by the pool there is the Azur cafe, where guests are offered not only drinks and cocktails, but also snacks and Mediterranean dishes prepared by a chef from Italy. The fitness club has a phyto-bar that will delight fans of a healthy lifestyle with firming cocktails, vitamin mixes and individually made herbal gatherings.

Beach holidays for tourists





Of course, people who come to the city are looking for not only a comfortable room, but also beach fun. The hotel is located within a picturesque private beach, where only guests of several neighboring hotels have a rest. The entrance to the sea is convenient, and the coast itself is clean and well-groomed. For a small fee, you can rent a comfortable sun lounger and relax in the shade of a large umbrella. Fans of more active recreation will not be disappointed, as it is easy to rent a boat or a catamaran here. For a reasonable fee, you can go water skiing, boats and even yachts. There are also interesting attractions for children - here everyone can have fun.

Can I count on additional services?

Is it really comfortable to stay at Hotel Grand Hotel Kempinski? The hotel rating is high on almost all Internet resources, and the reviews about this place are positive. The hotel has all the conditions for a truly comfortable stay.





To begin with, it is worth saying that the laundry constantly works here. In public areas there is free access to high speed Internet. If necessary, you can order the service "alarm clock" - at the appointed time one of the hotel staff will call you in your room. There is ample parking, which residents can use for free. It also helps you arrange transfers (for example, to the airport or to the city center). And employees are happy to organize various celebrations and business events.

Entertainment, leisure and leisure guests

What to do in your free time at Hotel Grand Hotel Kempinski? 5 stars mean something, and therefore you can be sure that you can find a suitable interesting occupation. In the courtyard there is a large pool where you can refresh yourself. Around are sun loungers and sun loungers. This is a kind of center of public life for tourists - here they relax, have fun, take sun baths and just chat with new friends. Residents can also use the indoor pool with a heating system, which is very convenient in cool weather.





Sports enthusiasts will not be disappointed either. There are two tennis courts at the hotel, where you can practice the game. There is also a large fitness center with a gym. Here you will not only be able to practice the simulators, but also get the advice of an experienced trainer, and even if you wish, go to group sports (for example, aerobics or Pilates). There is also a large spa, solarium and ten massage rooms.

Are there any conditions for children?

Of course, many people prefer to relax with the whole family, along with children. And for parents, questions about whether there are conditions for a fun pastime on the territory of the Kempinski Grand Hotel Gelendzhik are interesting.

Of course, if necessary, a rollaway bed will be delivered to the room, and in the restaurant menu you can choose the appropriate dishes for the child. Children often swim in the pool, but there is no special closed section here, and therefore water activities are possible only under the supervision of parents. On the territory there is a spacious playground with swings, where the guys can have fun and get to know each other. There is also a playroom for older children. Also every day the Kempinski kids club works, where you can leave your child under the supervision of experienced teachers and animators. Here, your children will be able to play, master needlework, dance and participate in various team games. Reviews indicate that the hotel is perfectly adapted to a rich and interesting family vacation.

How do guests respond to the hotel?

The opinion of people who have already managed to spend at least a few days at the hotel is always a source of valuable information. What do travelers say about the Hotel Grand Hotel Kempinski? The description on the official website of the hotel is true. The hotel area is really big and beautiful, it is always nice to walk here. You can relax by the pool, which is cleaned regularly. The advantage is the wonderful panorama that opens from the hill, and the proximity to the sea.

The rooms on the territory of the hotel are spacious, the furniture is modern and comfortable, and household appliances work. By the way, the rooms are really cleaned daily, and the maids do their job perfectly. Breakfast is hearty and delicious, and you can have a good dinner at the local restaurant.

Here mainly couples have a rest, but quite often various business events are organized, as well as celebrations, including even wedding ceremonies. The time spent at the Kempinski Grand Hotel leaves an exceptionally positive impression.
