The specifics of the resort Monastir (Monastir)

Nerolia Hotel Spa 4 * is a hotel located in an area such as Skanes. Tourists who came to Tunisia for the first time have a logical question: what is it? The coast near Monastir abounds in shallow lagoons. And the inhabitants of the city earned their living by the production of salt. They just got it. They blocked the lagoon and waited for the sea water to evaporate under the rays of the African sun, after which shining white crystals were collected in shovels. One of such sand spits, which are a natural barrier to the sea, is Skanes. Now it is completely at the mercy of hotels. Because of this, the city of Monastir managed to maintain its ancient appearance, and it still looks as if the winds of globalization have bypassed it.

Skanes is a five-kilometer spit, the width of which ranges from three hundred meters to half a kilometer. Therefore, the concept of "far from the sea" there simply does not exist. There are only two lines. Front-line hotels are located on the sea coast. And second-line hotels, such as Nerolia Hotel Spa 4 * (hotel photos are presented in the article), rise by the salt lake. But far from Skanes, it‚Äôs to Monastir. After all, you need to go around the lagoon to get into the city, and this is at least eight kilometers. By the way, to Sousse - the youth resort in Tunisia - to go a little more - 13 km. But trains and buses connecting the two cities go along the spit. The railway station, by the way, is located five hundred meters from the hotel. You can also ride a traditional Tunisian carriage drawn by a pair of horses. Monastir Airport is also located on Skanes . But the sound of boarding and taking off liners is not heard from the hotel we are describing.
