Work and compatibility

Drivers often leave feedback about the ELM327 autoscanner and its work. To start operation of the device, it is necessary to find out the compatibility of devices. The work of the autoscanner is in its interaction with the adapter, thanks to a special protocol. It is called "One Bot Diagnostic." That is why you need to connect to the engine correctly.

This standard is supported by all American cars that have been produced since 1996. If the car is European, then its models have been supporting since 2001 (gasoline type engine) and from 2014 (diesel engine). If the driver wants to use this autoscanner on a domestic car, then it is necessary to clarify the existence of such a protocol.
