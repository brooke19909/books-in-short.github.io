The best hotels of Turkey for adults, 5 stars

Those who do not want to hear during the holidays how the children frolic on the hotel territory, as well as the constant shouts of their parents, are invited to relax in the best 5-star hotels in Turkey for adults. They have a special romantic atmosphere, this is a great option for a honeymoon, for those who want to relax from the hustle and bustle, recover during a vacation, forget about stress.

Where are the hotels located for a relaxing holiday

Such hotels await guests in all resort areas of the country. In them, people often come to rest in pairs.





They are waiting for comfortable rooms, well-maintained beaches, entertainment and excursions.

Hotels:

  • Sentido Lykia Resort Spa in Fethiye;
  • Sign by Ersan in Bodrum;
  • Sentido Flora Garden in Side.

Let's consider them more similarly.

Sentido lykia

Hotel Sentido Lykia Resort Spa is located on the seafront, among the mountains and coniferous forests. Mountain air in combination with sea freshness, a unique microclimate have a beneficial effect on human health.

All rooms at this hotel have modern amenities, balconies, satellite TV, sea, garden or pool views. According to tourists, it is better to stay in rooms with garden views.

The Sentido Lykia Resort Spa has amazing sports facilities. There is a three-hole golf course, 19 pools of different sizes, you can do paragliding with landing on the territory, visit the fitness center, and in order to relax and unwind, the hotel offers a spa.





In 9 restaurants of the hotel, adult tourists are invited to taste international dishes, as well as seafood delicacies, steaks. The food in the hotel is generous, varied, deserving the most rave reviews.

The adult hotel in Turkey is located 71 kilometers from Dalaman Airport, some distance from the city. It is convenient for visiting the ancient sights and delightful corners of Fethiye. You can spend a day on the world famous for its beauty Blue Lagoon beach, go on an excursion to the Butterfly Valley.

From the adult hotel in Turkey Sentido Lykia there is easy access to the territory of Liberty Hotels Lykia, the infrastructure of which they can freely use. On the beach there is a fenced off area for nudists.

Sign by Ersan

The five-star adult hotel in Turkey Sign by Ersan in Bodrum is for adults only. One of its big advantages is its excellent location on the waterfront, just 5 km from the center of Bodrum, Halikarnas night club, St. Peter's Temple, and 40 km from the airport.

The hotel has everything for a wonderful vacation. The rooms are tastefully decorated in a contemporary style, some even have mini-pools or a hot tub, private terraces and excellent views of the Aegean Sea.





The rooms have plasma TVs, air conditioning, Wi-Fi. The bars and restaurants have a large selection of drinks; guests celebrate excellent cuisine and a wide selection of dishes.

The spa center offers rehabilitation and wellness treatments, a sauna, hammam and a refreshing dip in the indoor pool.

This adult hotel in Turkey has its own private comfortable beach about a kilometer long.

They spend unforgettable days in luxury together. Guests celebrate the excellent service of the hotel.

Sentido flora garden

This hotel is an oasis of comfort and tranquility on the Mediterranean coast. The nearest cities are at least 20 km from the hotel.

If something needs to be bought at the shopping center, tourists can visit the Kyzylot village, 5 km away, with a free bus.

Sentido Flora Garden Hotel is located on the shores of the Aegean Sea, with a beach length of 850 meters.

Some of the 224 rooms are located in comfortable bungalows, everything is provided for a comfortable stay in pairs, so at the tables, under umbrellas on the beach, the seats are equipped for two.





The beautiful rooms with a stylish interior have all the necessary amenities for a quality stay: a plasma TV, satellite TV, a minibar is regularly filled, and the beautiful bathroom has a hairdryer, toiletries, and some rooms have a jacuzzi.

The hotel has villas with their own pool, which is great for newlyweds.

Great food, fresh air, spa treatments and sports contribute to wellness and relaxation. Guests have access to a sauna and hammam.

For a fee you can live here with your four-legged pets.

Hotel Turkey for adults is suitable for those who are going to bask in its hospitable walls all the time of rest, since traveling to neighboring cities is far and inconvenient.

Many concerts with live music are organized here.

Weather

The weather allows you to relax on the coast of Turkey from approximately April to October. If the air temperature in April approaches 22 degrees on average, then the water barely reaches 18 degrees. Water warms up to 25 degrees by mid-June, but at this time the summer heat comes to 25 degrees in the shade. However, those who have vacation on schedule do not have to choose.

This weather lasts until September, it becomes noticeably cooler, the velvet season begins, when it is not so hot, the water has not yet had time to cool.
