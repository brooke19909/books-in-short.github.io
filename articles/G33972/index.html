Rostov-on-Don hotels in the city center: an overview of the best options, photos with descriptions, reviews

Tourists who decide to visit the beautiful city of Rostov-on-Don, proudly standing on the banks of the mighty Don, should take care of the overnight stay in advance. Despite the fact that there are a large number of placements, hotels in the center of Rostov-on-Don are especially popular. This is explained by its convenient location, access to all types of urban public transport and close proximity to the main attractions of the city.

Hotel prices in the center of Rostov-on-Don depend on the hotel, room category and additional services that are provided to guests. When booking a room, you can call directly the appropriate hotel service, the manager will answer all your questions about accommodation.

Hotel Rostov-on-Don in the city center is surrounded by historical buildings, business centers, shops, restaurants - everything you need for business and a great pastime. The architecture of the hotel building attracts particular attention. Its owners managed to keep the building of the nineteenth century to the present day in its original form.
