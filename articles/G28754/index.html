Duikin duct air conditioners. Description and feedback

Duikin duct air conditioners, the main purpose of which is the creation and further maintenance of indoor air parameters, are able to provide a certain temperature, relative humidity and cleanliness over an area of â€‹â€‹up to three hundred square meters.

The device consists of outdoor and indoor units. The first of them is installed outside the air-conditioned room. Installation of the indoor unit is carried out behind the panels of the suspended ceiling, which is the main advantage of channel systems. Such air conditioners can find their application in those rooms in which there is a special design requirement. The indoor unit of the system is compact in size. The size of his profile in height does not exceed three hundred and ten millimeters. Air movement is made by special bends, which are also mounted behind the panels of the false ceiling.





Duikin ducted air conditioners are characterized by two main parameters. These include air pressure and cooling capacity. System operation is performed sequentially. The air flow into the room occurs after it passes through the intake grille, indoor unit, as well as the exhaust system. There may be situations when this path has a considerable length. In this case, a fan is installed in the bends. This device must have a high static head to overcome high resistance. Depending on the air pressure, the number and type of bends installed in the system are determined.

Duikin ducted air conditioners form condensation during their operation. To remove it, certain models are designed with an integrated drain pump.

Duikin ducted air conditioners have a standard external unit design, which is typical for split-type systems. The distance between it and the internal device can be significant. The outdoor unit is mounted at ground or roof level. The internal part of the system is located on the necessary floor of the building.





The main principle of operation of ducted air conditioners is air recirculation. In the case when the outside air has a low temperature, there is a need for a heating system, which is represented by heat pumps.

Air conditioners "Daikin", reviews of which positively characterize their ability to create a healthy indoor microclimate, are intended for installation in offices, as well as hotel, restaurant, retail and administrative facilities. They can be mounted in elite cottages and apartments with large halls.

The advantages of duct type air conditioners are:

- hidden installation;

- low noise level;

- the possibility of conditioning large areas;

- compatibility with ventilation system.





Daikin air conditioners are serviced by specialized service departments. Its purpose is to maintain the entire system that creates the necessary air parameters in good technical condition.
