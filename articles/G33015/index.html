Dana Beach Resort Hotel, Hurghada: description and reviews of tourists

Hurghada is considered one of the most popular destinations in Egypt. According to statistics, the main part of the proposals of tour operators is formed for this resort on the Red Sea. But few years ago, few people heard about the existence of this small fishing village on the coast of Egypt.



Hurghada

Today, this popular tourist destination is known worldwide. The city has excellent infrastructure. Spanning tens of kilometers, the resort became famous not only for its chic hotels, but also for bays, such as the famous Soma Bay or Makadi Bay.

Hurghada is a perfect sandy beaches, equipped with everything you need: sun loungers, umbrellas; Numerous cafes right on the seashore serving delicious ice cream and refreshing drinks night clubs where incendiary oriental rhythms sound; light and music fountains; excellent cuisine in restaurants and much more.

There are no coral reefs, which is probably why diving enthusiasts choose other Egyptian resorts, but constant blowing winds are a great opportunity for surfing and kiting.

The undoubted advantage of Hurghada is that it is much faster to get to historical sights from here than from many other Egyptian cities located along the shores of the Red Sea. This is facilitated by the excellent quality of the roads that lead from the resort to Luxor, Cairo and other places.

Hurghada Hotels

Hotels, of which there are about two hundred, are located mostly on the coastline. Among them are five-star apartments, and 4 * or 3 * hotels, and even guest pensions designed for the most unassuming traveler. Hurghada tourism infrastructure is growing every year. Moreover, most hotels today are being built in new areas, away from the noisy center, where it is quiet and calm.



The cost of tours to this resort varies depending on the season. The largest flow of tourists, according to operators, is observed in late autumn, early spring and on New Year's holidays. Prices in hotels become cheaper in the summer, that is, at a time when the weather is hotter on this coast of the Red Sea.

Hurghada is considered the hotel capital of Egypt. The fact is that the number of hotels per each local resident of this resort is the largest in the world. Moreover, the prices in hotels have a very wide range: in general, they are affordable for the average tourist.

For example, for staying in a standard room with a flight from a Moscow airport, as well as with transfer and meals according to the all-inclusive concept at the five-star Dana Beach Resort hotel, you will need to pay between sixty five thousand rubles (per week). In general, as evidenced by the reviews, this hotel is not only optimal for its category prices, but also with excellent infrastructure.

Description

Located twenty minutes from the center of Hurghada, Dana Beach Resort (Egypt) is a great place for a family vacation. One of its undoubted positive aspects is its proximity to the airport. Having overcome a ten-kilometer distance in a few minutes, tourists find themselves in a hotel, which is especially convenient for those who come to Hurghada with small children. After all, kids, as you know, are naughty and poorly tolerate the transfer, especially in hot weather.



Dana Beach Resort (Hurghada), located on the first line, is part of the famous Pickalbatros hotel chain. This means that guests are guaranteed to receive the highest quality service. Its territory, somewhat reminiscent of a garden of paradise from an oriental tale, is completely thought out. The landscape is arranged in such a way that the greenery of lawns and palm trees is in perfect harmony with the snow-white buildings and blue water of artificial reservoirs.

general information

Judging by the reviews of tourists, the feature of the Dana Beach Resort 5 * hotel (Hurghada) is not only its large and well-equipped territory with developed infrastructure, including for children, but also a magnificent artificial canal. The reservoir, the so-called "lazy river", rightfully considered the hallmark of this hotel complex, has two functions. Besides the fact that it is very refreshing, flowing through the whole territory, the canal also has a transport purpose. Several times a day, a boat takes him living at the Dana Beach Resort to his own sandy beach.

Other advantages of living in this five-star complex travel agencies call a varied menu offered in five themed restaurants, the presence in the territory of a specialty coffee shop and free Wi-Fi.

Despite the fact that the hotel was built more than ten years ago, today it looks new and meets the requirements of even experienced travelers. Last renovated at Dana Beach Resort in 2015.

Infrastructure

Dana Beach Resort 5 * (Hurghada) has, as befits a hotel of its category, developed infrastructure. Such a wide list of services that are offered here is sometimes not found even in fashionable European hotels. In its two-story administrative building, which is separate from the main buildings, a hairdresser is operating. Here it is also possible to seek emergency medical attention from a qualified doctor (paid service), to give clothes to the laundry. For businessmen, a conference room with all the necessary equipment is provided. There is also a meeting room in the business center where fax and photocopying are installed. In addition, guests can use the services of a porter, concierge.



On the territory of Dana Beach Resort there are several small shops, including a kiosk, where you can buy souvenirs and gifts for relatives and friends fairly inexpensively. A coffee machine is installed on the ground floor of the main building, here you can also use an ATM and a currency exchange office. In the office located at the reception, you can rent a car.

The hotel has its own parking lot. It is offered to residents free of charge and without prior booking. The registration desk is open all day. Here you can rent a left-luggage office or a safe-deposit box, and if necessary, order a return transfer.

Current regulations

As in all other hotels, Dana Beach Resort (Hurghada) also has its own estimated hours. For example, on the day of departure, tourists, according to the rules, must vacate the room strictly until noon. After handing over the keys and final payment for all those additional services that were not included in the tour package, travelers, waiting for the transfer, can sit in the cozy lobby and drink coffee or a soft drink.

For the convenience of their guests, the administration gives them the option to close their accounts in cash or with credit cards. However, those who prefer cashless payments, in order to avoid any misunderstandings, should discuss in advance all the nuances of such payment.

Housing stock

Dana Beach Resort 5 *, reviews which are often left positive, is considered great. It has seven hundred thirty-four rooms located in two-, three- and four-story buildings, which are hidden in the greenery of palm trees. All rooms, judging by the reviews, are comfortable. They are dominated by warm sandy colors, the design style resembles a Mediterranean.



Tourists are offered a choice of rooms with balconies and terraces overlooking the sea or the internal canal. The categories are: Superior, Junior Suites and Suites. Most rooms are classified as standard. They have a TV and telephone, minibar, refrigerator. Bathrooms combined. Plumbing is new. On the floor - non-slip ceramic tiles.

Nutrition

Dana Beach Resort 5 * accepts guests according to the All Inclusive system. Tourists here do not need to worry about food, as three times a day in the main restaurant they are served buffet breakfast, lunch and dinner. The concept also provides snacks and local drinks in any of the eight bars operating on the territory, including the pool and the beach, tea and pastries in the lobby. Vacationers need to pay for ice cream and freshly squeezed juice.

The hotel has four restaurants, three of which are √† la carte. Having pre-booked a table, vacationers can taste dishes of Italian, Mediterranean and, of course, Egyptian cuisine.

Beach

The hotel is built on the first line, a hundred meters from the sea. Those who do not want to go there on foot can take a boat and take a walk along the inner canal. There is a bar on the beach where you can have a snack and sip drinks from local producers.

Umbrellas with sunbeds and mattresses, beach towels - all are provided free of charge. The beach is sandy.

For children

Hotel Dana Beach Resort 5 * by tour operators is positioned as a place where you can comfortably and carefree relax with children. Therefore, the infrastructure is largely provided for small customers. At the request of parents, a cot is provided in the room, and you can make an application for a special menu at the reception upon arrival. The restaurant provides free high chairs. There is also a mini club and a shallow pool.



Entertainment

On the beach, vacationers can engage in water motor sports, rent a catamaran or fishing boat, ride a glass bottom boat, go surfing or snorkeling, and also dive to the depth under the supervision of an experienced instructor.

The hotel has three outdoor pools, there is the opportunity to do water aerobics, water polo, you can soak in the jacuzzi, visit the fitness center and hammam, play mini-golf, billiards, tennis (including large) or chess. And this is not a complete list of all the entertainment that Dana Beach Resort offers its customers.
