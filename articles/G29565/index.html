What stuff pillows. Materials, tips

The quality of your sleep, and therefore your health, depends on the right choice of pillows. Modern pillows are able to provide support for the head, cervical vertebrae, and position the back in an anatomically correct position. Therapists strongly recommend choosing high-quality pillows, because only they will provide a good rest, help you relax after workdays. A low quality pillow can provoke chronic headaches, sprains in the neck and back.





How can I fill a pillow? Pillows can be hard, soft and combined. Stuffed with both artificial fillers and natural ones. There are many differences in these two types of filling. Therefore, the question of what stuff pillows should not be left open. Well, now about everything in order.

What stuff pillows?

Natural fillers - natural and environmentally friendly. However, for people with allergies, some types of them will not benefit. In addition, pillows with natural fillers are quite expensive, this is not affordable for everyone. Almost all of them periodically need special care.

Habitual fillers

The most common options, but already quite outdated, are bird fluff and a natural feather. The pillow becomes resilient and supple, supple and soft. Goose filler costs a bit more than duck down. Chicken feather pillows are unsuitable due to high humidity. Eiderdown is the best natural filler. Pillows with eider down quickly dry, practical, comfortable, last up to 20 years.





Unusual natural fillers

You can fill the pillow with the hair of a camel, sheep. They are liberal in price, but they tend to heal during sleep. Fillers such as rice and buckwheat husks also have a beneficial effect. However, such pillows are not durable (no more than 6 months). They also make specific sounds when turning. Fill pillows even with hops, rice, natural silk.

Artificial Fillers

Those with allergies should opt for modern hypoallergenic pillows with synthetic fillers. Synthetic, as a rule, are slightly lower in cost than pillows with natural natural fillers. Caring for them is much easier, most of them can be washed and dried in the normal mode of the washing machine.

Comforel

It is one of the most popular and inexpensive synthetic fillers. It is a small balls of soft and fluffy synthetic fiber. It is approved by doctors, does not cause allergic reactions, is easily and quickly erased, perfectly retains its shape.





Silicone

"Silicone boom" could not get around even the production of pillows. An amazing feature of silicone pillows is that the more you whip them, the larger they become. They have increased elasticity, easily restore their usual form.

Synthetic winterizer

The synthetic winterizer is the most loyal at the price artificial filler. In such pillows, ticks and microorganisms do not start, they absolutely do not smell and do not cause allergies. Wash and dry in the washing machine. Such pillows serve up to 10 years. How to fill a pillow with a padding polyester? Everything is simple. It is necessary to correlate the dimensions of the cover and the filler. The synthetic winterizer to lay down in layers until the density is sufficient. It is better to make more layers than necessary, in which case the pillow will be as elastic and durable as possible. It is better not to stuff the cushion from the padding polyester directly, but to fill it already after stuffing. Thus, the synthetic winterizer will lie down without tubercles, evenly distributed over the entire surface.

Holofiber

The best artificial filler. It goes well with filling in a compartment with natural materials. What is the best way to contribute to deep sleep if a pillow is not stuffed with a mixture of sheep‚Äôs wool and holofiber? You need to wash them at a low temperature.





Orthopedic pillows

Innovative materials with a memory effect are able to accurately take the shape of your body. They respond to heat and pressure. The memory-effect pillow is easy to calculate: it restores its shape slowly, adjusts rather than pushes. Orthopedic pillows can be soft, semi-soft, semi-rigid, hard. Made of latex and polyurethane. They help fight chronic diseases of the spine and head. Washing in the washing machine is strictly prohibited! Orthopedic pillows for people with different height and weight are presented on the modern market. Age and gender are also considered by manufacturers. That what orthopedic pillows stuff with - scientific technology.

All materials to one degree or another are useful, convenient, practical. How you can fill a pillow is up to you. But if you intend to use the same pillow for more than one year, preference should still be given to more expensive options.





DIY pillows

Interior decorative pillows decorate any room. What will be needed in order to create a masterpiece? First, decide on the fabric. Linen, cotton, fleece, felt, jeans will work fine, but with knitwear and stretch you need to be careful. Knitted and fur decorative pillows are the trend of the season. Decoration of decorative pillows is a real homework. Mistresses use ribbons, buttons, beads, sequins, ribbons, applique, bows, flowers, paint pillows with paints, embroider them.
