Video recorder "Karkam Combo 2": owner reviews, description and technical specifications

DVRs today are in almost every second car. And this is not surprising, because this extremely useful device is indispensable in the event of an accident and in resolving conflict situations. It is the record from the DVR that can be a key argument in a dispute with the traffic police. Therefore, car owners are interested in acquiring a reliable multifunctional device. That is why many of them turn their attention to the Karkam Combo 2 model.

Firm

Many car owners are interested in questions about what kind of company "Karkam", where it produces goods, whether it can be trusted. This company is registered in Russia and specializes in the production of various automobile devices. Karkam Electronics has a developed network in our country, which includes branded stores and service centers.

Like many other manufacturers, the company assembles its machinery in factories in Southeast Asia. For all products, including the Karkam Combo 2 DVR, the European guarantee form applies - even if there is no repair point in the city, the equipment can be sent by mail order to a single central service center.

Description

"Karkam Combo 2" combines the functions of three devices at once: a DVR, a radar detector and a GPS navigator. Despite the fact that its cost is slightly above average, it saves its owners a lot of money due to its versatility. The case of the device is made of matte black plastic. On the one hand, there is a liquid crystal display with control keys on it, on the other hand, the recorder‚Äôs eye and a laser detector lens.



On the sides there are: a slot for a memory card, an on / off and reset button, connectors for connecting to a PC and an input for a power cord.

Record

In recording mode, you can select the following options:

  1. Resolution.
  2. Cycle of the video (1, 3 or 10 minutes).
  3. Photosensitivity.
  4. Brightness level.
  5. Shooting mode (movement, portrait, automatic, fireworks, snow, beach, landscape and twilight).
  6. Adjusts the microphone sensitivity.
  7. Adjustment of recording intervals before and after triggering the detectors (5, 10, 30 or 60 minutes).
  8. Automatic shutdown after (0.5 sec., 60 sec., 3 min., 5 min., 30 min., 1 hour).
  9. Adjust color balance according to lighting.
  10. Sound recording (in standard mode or with low sensitivity).
  11. Select recording quality.


The ability to adjust a large number of recording parameters allows you to optimize the work with the registrar "Karkam Combo 2". Owner reviews indicate that drivers most often prefer to adjust the device "for themselves" rather than use the factory settings.

Control Panel

The control panel of the DVR is mostly Russified, which greatly simplifies the process of working with the device. However, there are a number of buttons whose value you need to know by heart:

  1. P OWER - responsible for turning on and off the DVR, as well as for changing the recording mode.
  2. PAGE DOWN - allows you to turn off the microphone, increase the volume and focal length (in image mode).
  3. PAGE UP - increases the focal length in video mode, decreases the volume, enables manual lock.
  4. MENU - opens access to all settings.
  5. OK - start and end of recording, confirmation of the selected mode, turning the screen on and off.

This model supports a memory card from 4 to 32 GB. The operating temperature range of the DVR is from ‚Äì80 to +25 degrees.

Photography

Very often in an accident there is a need to fix the scene of an accident in the first seconds. If the camera is not at hand, and the pictures from the camera are not particularly clear, the Karkam Combo 2 DVR can come to the rescue. Reviews say that car owners quite often use a similar method of fixing the scene of the accident and find it convenient enough. In the shooting mode, you can adjust the following parameters:

  1. Select image resolution.
  2. Brightness level.
  3. Choice of photo quality (super high and high).
  4. Adjust the color saturation.
  5. The choice of scene modes (the same as in the movie mode).
  6. Color balance level (cloudy, auto, daytime shooting, tungsten 1, 2, 3).
  7. Adjustment of color saturation in three versions (black and white pattern, bright colors, nature).

Equipment

Options at "Karkam Combo 2" - standard. First of all, the recorder itself is included in it. The second is a power cord that connects to the cigarette lighter, and a USB cable through which data is transferred from the DVR to the computer.





The presence of a suction cup mount is mandatory. With it, the device is installed on the windshield at a point that provides maximum visibility. And the last thing that should always be included in the package is the manual and warranty card.

Operation in radar detector mode

The description for the Karkam Combo 2 dashcam will be incomplete unless you consider in detail how it works in the radar detector mode. The vehicle owner can adjust the following parameters:

  1. Program alarm when speeding. Possible options range from 10 to 160 km / h.
  2. Set the radar sensitivity in two variations (medium or high).
  3. Set the mode of operation of the radar: "city" or "track". In this case, the device selects the necessary frequency range corresponding to the specified road section.
  4. Set the current speed when the GPS correlates with the speed on the dashboard.


In addition, there are several secondary settings. These include the inclusion of a continuous signal when a specified speed is exceeded, a change in time zone, automatic shutdown of alerts, and other parameters.

However, if you buy these devices separately, and choose devices with similar characteristics, the savings will be about 30%. No need to clutter the windshield and dashboard with a bunch of devices - just install "Karkam Combo 2". Reviews describe the price as consistent with the real quality of the goods.
