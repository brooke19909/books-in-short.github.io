How to restore contacts on a SIM card? Call Detailing with a Mobile Network Operator

Owners of smartphones prefer to store contacts on plastic media, that is, a SIM card, because phones are less durable and often change. However, this option is not the most reliable way: the card may be damaged or lost along with the gadget. Nevertheless, all the contacts that were on the SIM card can be restored, and in different ways. This is the official and surest way to recover lost information. If for some reason you lost contacts on the SIM card (losing your phone, breakdown, etc.), then your mobile service provider is obliged to help you recover information.

To do this, you need to contact any branch of your mobile provider and write a statement. After which, the operator will provide you with a printout of all incoming and outgoing calls for a certain period of time.
