What to see in Bulgaria? Options Overview

But is it time for us to go on vacation? This question, especially in the summer, is asked by the majority of the working population. Where to go, how to relax comfortably and have a great time? Our answer for you: "It's time to Bulgaria."

This is an amazing place where you can admire the beauties of nature, actively relax, enjoy the summer sun and warm sea, and just have a great time.

What to see in Bulgaria?

There are an incredible number of beautiful and unforgettable places in this country, and in order not to waste time in the first place, we recommend that you first visit the capital, Sofia, where the main attractions are concentrated there. This is the Banja Bashi mosque, and the rotunda of Sveti Georgi, and the Church of Light Week and Light Sofia. In the center of the city is a very beautiful Alexander Nevsky Cathedral.

The city of Varna is also rich in sights. This is the largest port city where you will see the Archaeological Museum, Roman Baths, the Assumption Cathedral, which is located in the city center. This is one of the oldest cities in Europe, which will introduce you to the ancient history of the country. There is much more to see in Bulgaria. We will list this below.



Do not forget to visit the city of Veliko Tarnovo. It will surprise you with its unique architecture, many historical monuments, and it is in this city that you can see examples of architecture - the Church of the Holy Virgin and the Forty Holy Martyrs and the Cathedral of Peter and Paul. This city will not leave you indifferent.

Where to stay?

Resorts in Bulgaria - this is exactly the place where you can have a wonderful time, enjoy a relaxing family vacation, improve your health in local medical institutions. The best places for rest that Bulgaria can offer us are hotels, Golden Sands. The most popular hotels are Admiral Park 5 *, Acacia 3 *, Aqua View 4 *, Arabella 4 * and many others.

The system of operation of most hotels that Bulgaria offers us is all-inclusive.

And, of course, do not forget to visit one of the most beautiful and modern water parks in Eastern Europe - Aquapolis, which is located in the northwestern part of Golden Sands. It combines elements of a natural park and artificially created fountains, pools, water slides, waterfalls, etc.
