Rest in the Czech Republic

One of the most beautiful and interesting countries in Europe is considered to be the Czech Republic. There are a lot of places without visiting which rest in the Czech Republic will be boring and bland. The country is so diverse that here everyone can find something fascinating and memorable.

A separate attraction can be called Prague, which is a favorite vacation spot for tourists from all over the world. Here, literally every corner breathes history. When planning a vacation in the Czech Republic , you must definitely include a visit to the Golden Street located in Prague. This is a small quarter with miniature two-story houses where the guards used to live. Later there was a prison, after which the district became criminal in nature. Today it is a network of museums and shops located in extremely unusual buildings. The most romantic place can be called Charles Bridge, on which there are 30 copies of statues. The originals, due to their great age and the negative impact of the external environment, as a result of which they are destroyed, are safely removed.

For tourists, the city of Kutna Gora will be very interesting , which was included in the UNESCO list of attractions of world significance. It is here that the Church of All Saints was built. This is perhaps the most original church in the world, since it is decorated with the remains of about 40 thousand people. For its decoration, skulls and bones were mainly used. After visiting such a place, you understand that rest in the Czech Republic will definitely not be boring. To plunge into the history of the country of the Middle Ages, you need to visit Detenice. This is the castle where the weapon exposition is located. According to legend, the treasures of the knights of the Order of Malta are hidden in the dungeons of the castle. The highlight of visiting this place is its restaurant. Here, tourists will be somewhat shocked by such a deep immersion in history. They are attracted by the relatively low price of accommodation and excursions. Families with children come here to relax, big noisy companies, couples in love. Everyone can find for themselves entertainment in this country.
