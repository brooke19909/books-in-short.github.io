Kaliningrad hotels: prices, reviews and photos

In Kaliningrad, you can find a huge number of offers on placing city guests in hotels, guest houses , etc. All of them differ from each other in the level of service, the availability of additional services, prices, etc. In this article we will consider the most popular hotels in Kaliningrad.



Triumph Palace

This hotel is a modern luxury establishment that embodies the best dreams of a good rest. A stunning hotel complex in which reign comfort, hospitality and luxury. Close attention to detail, as well as top-quality service, merge here.

Cost: from 2300 rubles per day.

Hotel blues

Considering the hotels of Kaliningrad, it is worth highlighting the Blues Hotel. It provides a full range of quality services, attentive, professional service, comfortable hotel rooms. Employees of the institution will do everything to make guests feel as comfortable as possible, and the period of stay here was comfortable for them. The hotel is a project of the hotel corporation from the USA BEST WESTERN.

It is located on the street. Borodino, which opens up many paths to various attractions of the city - theaters, museums, temples, the first promenade, palaces.

Cost: from 3800 rubles per day.

"Golden Bay"

The Golden Bay Hotel (Kaliningrad) is included in the Slavyanka complex, which unites 10 hotels in various cities of Russia. The four-story building was restored in 2006. Cozy, affordable rooms can satisfy even the most demanding of tastes. They have everything that is necessary for work and leisure. The unique location of the institution, modern communication services, excellent service make the hotel one of the most attractive recreation centers.



It is located near the business and historical center of Kaliningrad, near the bus and main railway stations. This allows you to take walks to famous architectural monuments - the organ hall of the Philharmonic and the Cathedral.

Cost: from 1650 rubles per day.

Hotel Albertina

Some hotels in Kaliningrad are located in picturesque quiet corners of the historical part of the city, including Albertina. This guesthouse owes its name to the Albertina University, which was founded in 1544. Its centuries-old history is reflected in the interior of this hotel.

This is a great place for lovers of beauty and peace. The atmosphere of hospitality will allow you to feel the unhurried passage of time, as well as to experience the true taste of life. Hotel "Ibis", Kaliningrad

This hotel has 167 rooms, 2 meeting rooms with a total capacity of up to twelve people and a conference room that hosts events for up to 50 people. Rendez-Vous Bar and Sud & Cie Restaurant are always welcome. The hotel features free Wi-Fi.



The Ibis Hotel (Kaliningrad) is located in the historical center of the city, near the Rybnaya Derevnya quarter and the Cathedral. Near the hotel is the exhibition center of Kaliningrad Baltic Expo. The distance to Khrabrovo Airport is 15.35 km, 3 km to the railway station.

Cost: from 2500 rubles per day.

Oberteich

The hotel is located in Kaliningrad, on the shores of Lake Superior, in the historical center. Around there is a stunning garden with amazing lawns and flowering plants, the windows offer excellent views of the Lower and Upper Lakes. The hotel offers guests suites equipped with bathrooms.

Each room has a telephone, satellite TV, free internet, room service. On the second floor there is a loggia. You can live with kids: there is a high chair, cot, toys, and walkers.

Hotel Chaika

Chaika is a modern business hotel for business people. He is one of the most fashionable in the whole city. This is a kind of synthesis of a boutique and business hotel.

It is located in the green part of the historical part of the city, in Amalienau - the elite district of Koenigsberg. It was built in 1908, completely renovated - in 2008.

The design of the hotel strikes a balance between the elegance of the style of the thirties of the 20th century and office modern practicality. The house is an architectural monument.

Cost: from 3500 rubles per day.
