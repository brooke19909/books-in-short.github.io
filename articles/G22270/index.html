Rest in the Chelyabinsk region at recreation centers, in houses and savages

The stuffy city, the streets heated by the bright sun, constant stress at work and many other reasons awaken our frantic desire for a good rest. Some identify relaxation with a long sleep, a cozy sofa and gatherings with a book in their hands. However, in the absence of an air conditioner in the house, the desire for lazy spending time will be equated to a struggle for survival: at high temperature on the street, the apartments heat up quite noticeably, which cannot but affect the well-being and mood of their inhabitants. Therefore, the only solution in this situation is to rest in the lap of nature. Anyone who can carve out a few days for communication with the beauties of the world, hurries to pamper your exhausted body with tropical and equatorial waters. Others, to whom only weekends are allocated for relaxation, choose nearby ponds and recreation centers where you can stay as a savage in tents or in comfortable conditions. It all depends on the desire and capabilities of the person. Of course, preference is given to places that are closest to the house. For example, rest in the Chelyabinsk region. Such outings will appeal to lovers of natural beauty, virgin nature and vast expanses. Where can I spend a relaxing weekend here? What bases and sanatoriums are in this region? Let's get a look.





Relax completely: choose a category

Holidays in the Chelyabinsk region, as well as in other areas, can be divided into three areas:

  1. Relaxation in an organized manner. This includes the opportunity to forget about the bustle of city life in the vast cozy resorts and boarding houses.
  2. Sanatorium treatment. Not many of us can boast excellent health. That is why, whenever possible, we try to breathe new strength into our tired organism and get rid of the problems that torment us. This option is perfect for those who care about their own health above all the benefits of sea resorts.
  3. Rest savages. Tents, a guitar, a bonfire, an ear in a bowler hat - for many, this is how a complete unity with nature seems.

Let us consider in more detail each of the categories presented.

Location at the lakes: boarding house "Forest Estate"



I would like to note that the sanatoriums and rest houses in the Chelyabinsk region number in the hundreds. Still, this region belongs to the Urals, and where, if not in the Urals, you can improve your health and protect yourself from the stress of the outside world? On the territory of this region there are a huge number of lakes - more than three thousand. Therefore, it is not surprising that near each reservoir you can find a place for a good rest. So, on the shores of Lake Akakul is located "Forest Estate" - a recreation center in the Chelyabinsk region. It is located on the border with the Sverdlovsk region.





Outdoor activities and lazy leisure

This recreation center offers its guests not only a complete quiet rest in the lap of nature, but also a lot of entertainment for every taste. For those who want to swim in the base there is a specially equipped beach. In summer, a water park is open. In damp weather, saunas will help to warm up, in which pools are an additional element. For active recreation sports equipment rental is provided here. You can ride a bicycle, zip along a quiet lake surface on a fast jet ski, play badminton and other outdoor games on the courts, and shoot at the shooting range. In winter you can go skiing and ice skating.





Rooms, cottages and meals

Such places of rest in the Chelyabinsk region, as the base "Forest Estate", are a great opportunity to relax with the whole family. Both adults and children will find entertainment to their taste here. For the younger generation, there are specially equipped rooms with game equipment. Billiards, bowling, tennis - all this is available on the territory of the holiday home. You can improve your health and relax in peace in the massage room or during long walks along forest paths. By the way, proper nutrition plays an important role and is developed at a very high level. Many holiday homes in the Chelyabinsk region boast a buffet service. "Forest Estate" is no exception. A summer cafe, a sports bar, a restaurant, a cafe bar and a dining room are many options for a delicious lunch. You can spend your vacation in a hotel room or a detached cottage. Moreover, for the first option, there are various categories, including economy class, standard, suite, deluxe, deluxe rooms and apartments.

Organized vacations in the Chelyabinsk region are offered by the Rodniki summer club, the Fongrad spa hotel, the Uralsky Zori hotel complex, the Berezka, Golden Beach, Lakes Country and many other establishments. A huge plus of any of these pensions is their location: each of the institutions in the region is located on the shore of a lake or river.





Getting rid of problems

Rest in the sanatoriums of the Chelyabinsk region is suitable for those who wish to improve their health in the bosom of the Ural nature. The proximity of the Ural Range has created a unique climate in this area, allowing you to deal with various ailments. That is why many resorts are located in the region. The services offered are aimed at combating various diseases, as well as preventing their occurrence. Diversities are such sanatoriums as Kisegach, Pearl of the Urals, Spruce, Uvildy Sungul and some others.

Some types of services

Specialists of such institutions as Sosnovaya Gorka, Karagaysky Bor, Dalnaya Dacha, Utes and others will help to cope with the problems of the musculoskeletal system. The Ural Sanatorium offers its visitors comprehensive programs for resolving issues in the field of gynecology, nervous and endocrine systems, metabolism, respiratory organs, etc. The Yangan-Tau medical institution has become famous throughout Russia for its healing sources. Many stories are associated with these resorts. Living examples of miraculous healing give people hope for deliverance even from serious illnesses. Medical rest in the Chelyabinsk region in summer and winter, for children and adults, for those suffering from various diseases and trying to simply strengthen their immunity - the sanatoriums of the South Urals are always happy to help the client choose the option he needs.





One on one with nature

There is another category of recreation that attracts the attention of tourists - it is the rest of the savages. In the Chelyabinsk region, you can not only live in comfortable rooms and listen to the instructions of doctors, but also plunge into the world of uncharted beauties and untouched taiga, living in tents near the lake. Thirty kilometers northeast of the regional center is a large reservoir called Sugoyak. This lake is very popular among fishermen and lovers of camping. Sugoyak's water is enriched with minerals and salts, which beneficially affect human health. That is why part of the shoreline of the lake is strewn with various types of boarding houses and recreation centers. However, there are territories that are ideal for savages. There are specially equipped sites where you can easily set up a tent.

Healing waters

In the Etkul region, there is a delightfully beautiful salt lake called Big Shantropay. Many tourists who visited this pond note the amazing energy coming from this place. Crystal clear water, healing mud, excellent sites for setting up tents, remoteness from civilization and complete unity with nature - this should be a real rest for the savages.

In the Chelyabinsk region there are many lakes. Some of them are familiar to the public by boarding houses and sanatoriums located near ponds. For example, lakes Turgoyak, Uvildy, Arakul. Fisherman's Note

For secluded relaxation, the Argazinskoye reservoir is suitable . This artificial lake is located twenty kilometers from a settlement called Karabash. The considered reservoir is the main source of fresh water for the regional center. The absence of salts and minerals in the liquid makes this source unattractive for placing recreation centers and medical dispensaries here, which is why Lake Argazinskoye is an excellent option for savages and fantastic fishing.

Rest in the Chelyabinsk region is not only an opportunity to relax and forget about your work and home troubles, but also an amazing chance to stay in the bosom of the untouched and unique in its beauty Ural nature.
