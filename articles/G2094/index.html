Pension "Blue Torch", Koblevo: overview, description and reviews of tourists

The Blue Torch (Koblevo) is located on one of the shores of the Black Sea. Its exact location: the Ukrainian part of the resort village. The boarding house is distinguished by the fact that it has landscaping of a high level. This attracts nature lovers and just those who like to lie on the grass under the shadows of the trees.



Briefly about the activities of the boarding house

The Blue Torch (Koblevo) has a dining room on its territory, where they offer three meals a day. There can simultaneously be 200 people. Vacationers are offered delicious vegetables and fruits. The dishes that are prepared here are distinguished by the highest technology. Celebrations and banquets are also held in the dining room. The cost of food per person per day is 170 UAH. (430 rub.).

On the territory there is a paid parking where you can leave your car, bus or trailer. For entertainment, a summer playground is offered. At the same time 20-30 people will fit on it. There is also a barbecue on which you can fry kebabs on your own.

For those who love active sports, the administration has established a tennis court, playgrounds for games (football, volleyball, basketball). Thanks to this, championships, marathons and other competitions are held on the territory.

The cinema is designed for 200 seats. Here they can not only show films, but also hold festivals, seminars, meetings, etc. This is possible due to the presence of a conference hall.

On the beach, it is proposed to rent a jet ski, catamaran, banana. Water slides installed.



Tourist info

The Blue Torch (Koblevo) is located near the beach, only 75 meters from it. The pension offers double rooms "junior suite", as well as double, triple, quadruple "standard".

All rooms are equipped with household appliances, in particular, TVs and refrigerators. There is a balcony and a bathroom with hot and cold water. Minimum room rate - 280 rubles per day.

Relaxation

Few people know Koblevo. Recreation facilities (the "Blue Torch" the most visited) constantly receive guests. This resort is located between two areas: Nikolaev and Odessa. Among other places of the Black Sea region, Koblevo is considered the most famous. What distinguishes him from others? First of all, a well-developed infrastructure aimed at improving the conditions for visitors. There are many night clubs, cafes, water parks, there is a dolphinarium. All of them belong to different price categories, which allows people with any budget to visit them.



Suggestions for children

Children constantly come with their parents to the Blue Torch (Koblevo). Prices for a wellness stay are quite reasonable. There are stationary and campgrounds. In them, children learn to be friends, behave correctly in the company. Counselors will never offend a child, they will always cheer him up and will do everything so that he gets an unforgettable experience. A shift, which lasts two weeks, costs about 4,500 UAH. (12 thousand rubles).

As for recovery, the boarding house is able to offer many options for sick children. Active activities are being carried out with them, which provides for both winter sports and summer sports.

There are only two campsites . There are constantly organized quizzes, contests, competitions. Few of the children will remain unhappy.

Excursions

Pension "Blue Torch" (Koblevo) constantly arranges various kinds of excursions. Many tourists will be happy to get acquainted with the sights of the region in which they came to relax. There are many monuments, natural treasures, historical monuments. The advantage of this type of vacation is that at a low cost of time and money, you can learn a lot and have a great day.



The boarding house directs on three types of excursions. Let's consider them in detail:

  • Weekend tours. They are organized in order to view some interesting areas of the Odessa region, Nikolaev, Kherson and some others. There is no fixed cost, this information can be found directly from the management of the boarding house or on the official website. For example, to visit Sofievsky Park you need to pay 355 UAH. (890 rub.). The more people gather in a group, the lower the cost.
  • Multi-day excursions. These tours are organized by the company "Hen Tengri." Departure is carried out from Nikolaev. Departure from other southern cities of Ukraine is also possible.
  • Foreign tours. The boarding house organizes trips to many European countries. The approximate cost and time of dispatch must be clarified with the administration.

Reviews for the boarding house "Blue Torch" (Koblevo) are quite contradictory. If we consider what satisfied guests write, we can say for sure that they liked the beach. By the way, those who come here to swim do not sit in the rooms, so all conditions are suitable for them. The food in the dining room is not impressive, but you can go to the store and cook your own meals. Everyone likes to relax in the resort of Koblevo. The Blue Torch base, unfortunately, for many guests did not become the one where you want to return. Why is there such an impression? The rooms were liked by a small percentage of guests. Many note that the furniture is old, popping, some already almost falling apart. Cleanliness is not at the best level. Worms and cockroaches were seen in the dining room. This indicates poor sanitation. In some areas of the base where the coastline begins, people notice a small accumulation of garbage. Many waiters and other workers are unfriendly and may even be rude. The administration does not respond to this, complaints are simply ignored.
