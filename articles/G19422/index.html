AyDaDom: developer reviews

Buying your own home right now is extremely difficult. Especially when it comes to a city like St. Petersburg. Therefore, more and more people prefer to turn their attention to the suburbs. This solution has many advantages. Among them are relative unity with nature, and a much more pleasant ecological situation (compared with the city), and the ability to build your dream home or make it ready so that it looks perfect from your point of view. Also, the population density here is significantly lower than in the metropolis, which adds psychological comfort.

Life in the suburbs attracts many. However, with the acquisition of this kind of housing sometimes there are many difficulties. As a rule, buyers are forced to invest in real estate, which is still under construction. This is associated with great financial risks. If for some reason the developer is not able to complete the project, equity holders may lose their money and not get the expected housing. How to avoid such troubles? It is important to pay attention to what kind of reputation the developer has earned. Many citizens have invested their savings in the projects of AyDaDom. What is the situation with the developer today? Do interest holders regret their decision? We will talk more about this later in this article.

About company

A popular group of construction companies in the Russian Federation was AyDaD (St. Petersburg). Acted from 2009 to 2016. The main specialization of this company is suburban construction. The developer paid attention to the construction of cottage villages in the region.

What are the basic principles of the functioning of AyDaDom? Reviews of the company are distinguished by the following: safety, quality service, customer comfort. The developer also offered the following services: after-sales service, legal support, security services, technical supervision.





"AyDaDom": company reviews

What feedback did customers give about this developer? Buyers incredibly liked the development plan for all AyDaDom projects (Pavlovsk, Zhemchuzhina, Honey Meadow and others). Indeed, the company came up professionally: each village had to be gasified, electrified and, in general, equipped with all necessary amenities. All this was much appreciated by the buyers. Customers also noted the availability of after-sales service as a ‚Äúplus‚Äù. Others liked the planned developed infrastructure of the cottage villages, as well as their excellent placement in relation to other objects, cities, transport hubs. However, in a certain period, everything changed. The company "AyDaDom" for some reason fell into decay and was close to bankruptcy. During this period, work was slowed down or suspended. Dissatisfied reviews of interest holders began to appear, which did not understand what, in fact, were the reasons for the delays. However, the changes were not long in coming. The situation was saved by the Finnish company Luonto, which bought out a block of shares in AyDaD and took upon itself all the obligations of the developer for the construction of cottage villages and after-sales service for those already built.

At a certain point, it became difficult or almost impossible to timely pay workers wages. Nevertheless, this did not end the projects of the AyDaDom Group of Companies. Employee reviews reflected all the changes that took place in the company. Today, the situation has fundamentally changed. How did this happen? Let's consider further.

Luonto

This company in April 2016 bought "AyDaDom" and assumed all its obligations, which it faithfully fulfills. Today it is one of the largest developers of suburban areas of St. Petersburg.

This Finnish company applies all the previously gained experience in order to build high-quality cottages in the Russian Federation. Specialists are professional, customer-oriented and ready for development and self-improvement. To date, the company owns more than one thousand hectares of territory, and offers its customers to purchase a plot in one of the fifteen cottage villages.
