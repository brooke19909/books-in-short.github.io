Cheap hotels in Tver: description, reviews, addresses

Tver, a city founded in the XII century, attracts a large number of tourists. It is located near Moscow, on the banks of the Volga River. The city has a very rich historical past and friendly residents who with great pleasure answer questions from visitors. There are a large number of cultural and architectural monuments, as well as other attractions. The city will be interesting to both adults and children.

In order for your stay in Tver to bring only joy, you need to think about the issue of housing in advance. Let's get acquainted with inexpensive hotels. In Tver there are enough of them. In the article you will find all the necessary information about them.

Features of inexpensive hotels in Tver

Arriving in another city, we try to solve the housing problem as soon as possible. In Tver, you can choose a variety of options. It has luxury apartments and modest but clean hostels and hotels. If you arrived in the city for a few days, it is better to find hotels in Tver inexpensively. Prices will depend on the number of additional services, as well as the comfort of the rooms. If you want to rent a separate room, it will cost more than a bed in a shared room with other guests. In this article we will talk only about the cheapest accommodation options.





Hostel "Kalinin"

The convenient location of the hotel attracts a large number of visitors. Within walking distance are the main attractions of the city, as well as shops, cafes and restaurants where you can eat. The hotel rooms are all clean and bright, as well as well refurbished. They have beds, upholstered furniture, tables, chairs, mirrors, wardrobes and much more. You can rent a room here for four or eight people. As well as a separate room. How much will it cost you?

  • A bed in a dormitory room is about 500 rubles.
  • Twin room can be rented for 1498 rubles.

The hostel has a shared kitchen where you can cook for yourself. Rooms should be booked in advance, especially in the summer, as the number of visitors increases.

The address of the Kalinin hostel is 19 Volny Novgorod Street.





Isaevsky guest house

We offer you a great place to relax and stay. The hotel is located in a quiet area. Thanks to this fact, you can relax and regain your strength after long walks in the city. To get to the center of Tver, you only need ten to fifteen minutes. Near the hotel there are a botanical garden and the river Tvertsa.

Here you can rent a budget double room for 1100 p. If you wish, you can order breakfast for a fee. The cost is 300 rubles. Buffet breakfast. The rooms have everything you need to relax. The toilet and bathroom are shared.

Hotel address - Lieutenant Ilyin Street, 3.





Hostel "Comfort and Coziness"

This place can also be attributed to the category of inexpensive hotels in Tver. The building has three floors. There is a spacious and bright kitchen where you can cook. There is a washing machine, if necessary, you can tidy your things. All rooms have TVs, upholstered furniture, carpets. Bath amenities are provided. A bed in a dormitory room can be rented for 360 rubles. Here you will always be provided with clean and well-ironed bedding.

The address of the Hotel Comfort and Coziness is Volokolamsky Lane, 27.
