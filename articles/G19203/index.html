How to find a lost iPhone?

In order to quickly locate the phone in case of loss, you must first install a special application on it called Find My iPhone. This program allows using another device to find your lost iPhone, send a signal to it, and also block it from unauthorized access. So, do the following:



  1. Download and install the Find My iPhone app on your gadget.
  2. We launch it and go through authorization, where you will need to specify your Apple ID.
  3. In the privacy settings, enable the geolocation function ("Settings" - "Basic" - "Privacy" - "Geolocation").
  4. We activate iCloud, and then turn on the Find iPhone feature. This is all done in the "Settings" menu.
  5. We put the password to unlock the phone. This is done so that the person who falls into your hands of your iPhone cannot turn off this setting.
