How to style medium length hair yourself?

A holiday or party is one of the significant events in the life of every woman. And to look one hundred percent, you need to create the appropriate style. Holiday hairstyles are distinguished by a variety of shapes, so the question arises of how to style medium-length hair so that the hairstyle looks unusual and emphasizes individuality. Each woman is looking for her own version, corresponding to her attitude.



  1. If you have a bob haircut, create a hairstyle that mimics some negligence. To do this, you need a hairdryer with a regime of cold air. Before you style your hair with a hairdryer, it is necessary to wash it not only with shampoo, but also with balm. Dry clean hair with cold air to give the strands a shine and naturalness. The effect of negligence can be created by whipping or separating and gel fixing individual strands.
  2. You can change the image using retro hairstyles. To learn how to style your middle-length hair in retro style correctly, just follow our tips.
    Straighten your hair with an iron, after processing it with a small amount of mousse, and comb back. Smooth all clumping strands. On the sides of the hair can be fixed with invisibility, so that individual hairs do not spoil the picture. Also comb back the bangs and stab with a rhinestone decorated with a hairpin. Sprinkle hair with strong fixation varnish.
  3. If you have a caret, then the question of how to style your hair with medium length should not bother you at all! With this haircut, you can change styling without much work every day! Experiment with parting and bangs. Twist the ends in or out, combing the hair over the ears. Put on a nice bezel or tie a ribbon on top of your bangs. Get a hairstyle in the style of the 80s, previously loved by many American housewives.
  4. Before you quickly and beautifully style your hair, look in the wardrobe. Do you have a little traditional black dress? So, festive styling for medium hair in the French style will do just fine. To do this, you will need decorative studs and a strong hold varnish. Dry your head, pulling the strands in a smooth wave. Comb them back and stab them with a bunch just below the crown. In this case, the ends of the strands must be left loose. Carefully try to hide the tips inside the bundle. Decorate the middle of the bunch with a couple of decorative hairpins. Then very carefully you need to smooth the hair with a comb so that not a single lock is knocked out. Finally, you need to spray the styling with varnish, and your French bundle is ready. A certain departure from the classic French styling will be the previously released locks that frame the face. They must be tightened with a curling iron and fixed with varnish. You can also leave one strand from the middle of the beam. This option will emphasize the line of the neck and shoulders, especially if your evening dress is open.

Follow these simple tips, and you will no longer be bothered by the question of how to style your hair with medium length, because now you can change your hairstyles yourself every day!
