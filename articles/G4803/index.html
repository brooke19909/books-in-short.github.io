Massif Kara-Dag

"Kara-Dag" is translated as "Black Mountain". It was here, according to the legends of the ancient Greeks, that the entrance to the kingdom of Hades was located. Here the heroes came to perform their exploits. All rocks, in a bizarre form resembling the figures of living creatures, frozen in stone, have their own names. Once you check into the As-El Hotel (Koktebel), you can immediately book a tour of Kara-Dag to see the beauty of the massif, the history of which dates back to the Jurassic period, to meet the stone inhabitants of its peaks: Leo and Warrior, Sphinx and Elephant, Falcon and Frog. After listening to the legends, stand on the cliff of "Ivan the Robber", walk along the gorges and dream up about the pirate boats hidden in the Robber Bay.
