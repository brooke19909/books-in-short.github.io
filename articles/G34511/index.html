Hotel Pera Inn Hotel 3 *, Alanya: description and reviews of tourists

Alanya is a dynamically developing Turkish resort. Located on the picturesque coast of the Mediterranean Sea. It is famous for its vast beaches, rich nightlife, an abundance of restaurants and shops. In the town there is a farmer's market.

Towards the sun





Hotel Pera Inn Hotel 3 * is located almost in the center of the village. The main attractions of the municipality are about five minutes leisurely walk. Go to the beach a little longer. If you do not rush, then the road will take a quarter of an hour. From the hotel you can easily reach the popular Cleopatra recreational zone, the ancient castle, the complex of caves Dalmash and Dim.

Pera Inn Hotel 3 * is focused on the reception of families with small children, couples and the elderly. Therefore, in the evening, peace and quiet reign in the courtyard of the hotel. It is noisy only near the pool. The international airport located in neighboring Antalya is 130 kilometers away. The hotel offers a paid shuttle service. If desired, you can use public transport or taxi.

Recently, aircraft from Russia are landing not only in Antalya, but also in the neighboring town of Gazipasa. Between him and Alanya only forty kilometers. Official carriers charge 3,000 rubles for this journey. For a minibus for eight passengers, they will take 3,400. Transporting a company of 13 people will cost 4,200.

Information for consideration





The coastline stretches 600 meters from the residential building Pera Inn Hotel 3 *. The hotel is on the second line. Throughout the complex there is a stable connection to a Wi-Fi network. Opposite the main entrance there is a car parking. Places on it are free. No reservation is required.

Near the Pera Inn Hotel 3 * there are cafes and eateries. The prices are affordable, the food is delicious, the portions are large. There is also a grocery store. In its assortment preservation, sugar, tea, coffee, sweets, fresh fruits, berries and vegetables.

Welcome!

Check-in starts at 14:00. But tourists who arrived at the hotel a little earlier are accommodated without delay. The exception is the high season, when a huge number of guests accumulate in the hotel. You need to vacate the room no later than 12:00. The staff is polite and helpful, but speaks only English. The reception staff, which operates around the clock, are always ready to help. They solve household issues, call a taxi and a doctor, contact representatives of insurance and travel agencies.

  • luggage storage;
  • food in a restaurant;
  • use of the pool;
  • Internet connection;
  • walks in the garden;
  • provision of a crib (preliminary application required);
  • playground;
  • daily cleaning;
  • bed linen change;
  • issue of bath towels;
  • replenishment of stock of hygiene products.

Comfort

For an additional fee, tourists can use the laundry, dry cleaning. After purchasing a subscription, a Turkish hammam, sauna and steam rooms are open for them. Three kilometers from the building is a golf course. Massages must be booked in advance.

For business people, Pera Inn Hotel 3 * (Alanya) offers a conference room equipped with office and computer equipment. They have negotiators at their disposal. For a small fee, guests use fax and telephone services, photocopying. A doctor‚Äôs services are more expensive than the established tariff for doctors in a city hospital.

Water activities





The local beach is equipped with sun loungers, parasols and wooden flooring. Showers, toilets, changing cabins work. But experienced tourists went to rest in the Cleopatra area. To get there, you need to take a minibus. Buses run every five minutes past the hotel. The driver himself will stop where required. The entire journey takes no more than seven minutes.

The water on this beach has a distinctive turquoise hue. The recreation area is municipal, so you will have to pay for sun loungers and tents. Those who are not ready to splurge can spread a towel right on the sand. Bars and inexpensive bistros work. They serve cocktails and simple snacks. Many guests of Pera Inn Hotel 3 * (Alanya) at the end of their vacation chose Cleopatra.

The pool at the hotel is only one. It is small. Next to it is a children's aquazone. The water in it is always warm. Inflatable toys need to be bought independently. There are no slides and attractions. Dry towels are not dispensed. There are not enough sun loungers during the midday heat. To comfortably stay by the pool for lunch, you need to skip a meal in the dining room.

Positive impressions





Most of the tourists remained rather smiling and friendly waiters, maids and administrators. It was necessary to communicate with them in English, but this fact did not spoil the impression of rest. In their reviews of Pera Inn Hotel 3 * (Alanya), guests praised the cook who came from Tajikistan. He made delicious soups.

All the furniture in the room was in excellent condition. Plumbing worked. Bags with shower gel and shampoo were not given. There was only a dispenser with liquid soap, the supply of which was replenished from time to time. TV showed Russian channels. A powerful air conditioner easily coped with the Turkish heat.

Local pastries are very fond of vacationers. Delicious buns, pies, puffs and cakes were served for breakfast, lunch and dinner. Everything was hot and fresh. The bar was treated to local alcohol drinks. Beer and wine were enough for everyone. If you order a cocktail or food in the bar on the coast, which belongs to the ownership of Pera Inn Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Alanya), a sunbed and an umbrella were provided free of charge.

Recommendations
