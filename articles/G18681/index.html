LCD "Malevich" in Yekaterinburg: infrastructure, reviews

Today, living in the city center in a new, spacious and comfortable apartment with an improved layout is the ultimate dream of almost every person who keeps up to date. The developed infrastructure of modern microdistricts allows you to use all the benefits of civilization, which distinguishes new buildings from other housing options.

The developer of Uralstroyinvest CJSC, which is specialized in the construction and sale of housing in the Yekaterinburg region, offers to turn the above dream into reality. This, in particular, is about the LCD Malevich. It is considered the first conceptual project in the capital of the Urals. All residential buildings represented in it will have a unique, colorful design (in terms of architecture), but together they will make up a single concept of style called Suprematism. From the windows of the new building, the city is visible at a glance, since the Malevich residential complex is located in the very center of the metropolis (Kirovsky district, intersection of Tramvaynoy lane and Mayakovsky street).





Within walking distance from the construction site are shopping centers, recreation and entertainment areas, educational and cultural institutions, social infrastructure.

Infrastructure

The degree of improvement of the territory is pleasantly striking in the complex "Malevich" (LCD). Apartments here should be purchased primarily because of the infrastructure.
