Bbk receivers and their description

To watch digital broadcasting in the DVBT2 standard, a modern TV must be equipped with a special built-in tuner that supports the necessary standards, or with additional special equipment that can process such signals with a digital receiver.

The BBK Electronics brand is known for its modern line of good quality consumer electronics. Bbk digital television receivers are comfortably used in everyday life and not only allow you to receive a high-quality signal, but also record your favorite TV shows in your memory.

The company has specially developed an intuitive InErgo menu, and a convenient remote control makes it easy to understand all the functions of the device.

How to set up a BBK receiver

When you first set up the device you will not have any difficulties - it is extremely simple. You only need to determine the type of TV that you plan to connect.



For not very new models, for example, with a CRT-tube and the like, you should connect using the cord through the component video output. If you have a newer model equipped with an HDMI port, then we will use only the port itself. You may have to buy a cord, but considering that the receiver will output Full-HD images, it's worth it. Even with the naked eye, a difference in image quality is noticeable.

All ports and connectors are located on the rear panel of the receiver: antenna output and input, component and composite outputs, coaxial digital audio output and HDMI output. The USB connector is located on the front panel. In addition, on the front side there are receiver control buttons and an information panel. They are completely duplicated on the remote control.
