Rest in Beregovoy, Feodosia, Golden Beach: photos and reviews of tourists

Beregovoe is a tiny village located on the Crimean coast. It is located just six kilometers from the bustling Feodosia. The resort attracts tens of thousands of tourists every year. His calling card is Golden Beach. In Feodosia, the bathing season begins in late May, and ends in mid-September.

First impression

Tourists who happened to visit the peninsula note that a special microclimate reigns in Beregovoi. The Black Sea in these parts is shallow. The depth in it increases gradually. Water heats up quickly. Winds at the resort are rare guests. At the Golden Beach in Feodosia there are water attractions. Tourists ride on "bananas" and "tablets", catamarans and boats.

The recreation area is equipped with toilet and shower stalls. For athletes pull a volleyball net. The instructors are responsible for safety. During the unrest, the entrance to the sea on the Golden Beach in Feodosia is restricted. In high season, music from open verandas of street cafes and eateries sounds on the shore. Photographers and artists offer their services.

Hotels

In Beregovoi a large number of hotels. There are no large hotel complexes. Dozens of private guest houses offer their services. At the disposal of vacationers are options of economy class and premium level. If you believe the reviews of those who visited Beregovoy last year, the minimum cost of living for one person was 450 rubles per night.

The cheapest deals can be found a ten minute walk from Golden Beach. In Feodosia and on the first line in Beregovoy, the price for a single room exceeds 1,000 rubles. Having paid 2,000, travelers get a comfortable room with a private bathroom and a bathroom, a swimming pool on site. This amount includes using the lobby, connecting to the Internet, a place for a car in a car park. Air conditioning in the room is required.

Entertainment

The epicenter of the resort life of Beregovoy is the embankment. Here it is called the promenade. Along it is concentrated a huge number of cafes and restaurants for every taste and budget. In summer, there are street discos, bars and souvenir shops. There is an amusement park with attractions, carousels, slot machines. Barbecue is prepared on the promenade, treated with cotton candy and ice cream. When night falls, the promenade transforms. It blooms with neon lights for signage and advertising posters. Sounds clockwork music.

Not only youth companies, but also families with children go for an evening walk along the embankment line. The atmosphere of celebration and fun reigns by the sea. Sometimes on the Golden Beach in the village of Beregovoe (Feodosia) fireworks are launched. If local entertainment is boring, tourists recommend going to a neighboring city. Feodosia is only fifteen minutes by shuttle bus. Buses run almost around the clock. Taxi drivers charge 500 rubles for their services.

In addition to a leisurely vacation in Beregovoy on the Golden Beach (Feodosia), tourists can enjoy sightseeing trips. The routes are very different. Beginners are taken to the ruins of an ancient fortress. Then it is proposed to visit the colorful Armenian church. And in Feodosia there is a museum, the exposition of which is dedicated to the life and work of Alexander Green. Fans of long trips in their reviews recommend going to Yevpatoriya, Mezhvodnoe, Kerch or Yalta.

Negative moments

Beregovoe is a small village, so the choice of discos and clubs in it is small. Fans of busy nightlife can be bored. The neighboring Theodosia comes to the rescue. Families with young children and older people complain about difficult entry into the sea. In some places there are large boulders and sharp cobblestones. During calm, they do not interfere. But during a period of unrest, they can injure.

In calm weather, the water in Beregovoy is clear and transparent. But the rest of the time the sea is muddy, it contains a suspension of sand and silt. On rainy days, life in the resort freezes, and the water turns yellow. The clay washed off the slopes colors the sea. Two days after precipitation, the water area again becomes clean.

Golden Beach Benefits

Tourists have found a lot of advantages that this small village has, located six kilometers from Feodosia.

  • Firstly, they respond well to the location of the resort. It is in close proximity to the main attractions of Crimea. From Beregovoi, sightseeing buses run to Bakhchisaray, Simferopol and Sevastopol.
  • Secondly, travelers appreciated the huge selection of modern and comfortable accommodation. General options and premium rooms are available. But air conditioning is not in all rooms. This is a minus. When choosing a hotel, be careful. The description of the hotels indicates the remoteness of the residential property not from the well-maintained beach, but from the sea. This means that the house may be on a cliff or not have a safe descent to the water.
  • Thirdly, Russians believe that rest in Beregovoy will be cheaper than in other settlements of the southern coast of Crimea. Prices in local restaurants and cafes are lower than in neighboring Feodosia, and the quality of the dishes is not inferior. The farmer's market is open on weekends, and in high season, trading is carried out almost every day. It offers vacationers fresh local fruits and vegetables, berries, seeds and nuts, dairy products. At the market they buy meat, eggs and fish.

Black Sea

Even in August, the beach is not crowded. The water in the sea is clean, and jellyfish are rare guests. For an additional fee, beach chairs and umbrellas are offered on shore. The entrance to the water is almost flat everywhere. Exceptions are wild beaches. The choice of excursion programs is not inferior to the offers of travel agencies of Feodosia. Passing by the coastal highway allows you to travel along the South Coast by car.

Vacationers recommend the Golden Beach for the installation of tent camps. If desired, everyone will find a quiet and secluded place. Here couples with young children and youth companies can easily get along. Nobody bothers anyone.
