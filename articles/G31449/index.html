Hotel Eden Resort & Spa 5 * (Sri Lanka): description and photos

Decided to spend an unforgettable vacation in Sri Lanka? Do you want to stay in a five-star hotel with comfortable rooms and excellent service on the shores of the Indian Ocean? In this case, be sure to consider the Eden Resort & Spa (Beruwella). We suggest further together to find out what is offered to tourists here.



Location

Eden Resort & Spa is located on the southwest coast of Sri Lanka on the outskirts of the resort town of Beruwella. An excellent two-kilometer beach is just a hundred meters from the hotel. In the vicinity of the hotel there are several restaurants, cafes and shops. However, for shopping experienced travelers are advised to go to the city center. The nearest airport is Colombo, 96 kilometers away. Tourists usually take about 2.5 hours to get to the hotel if you are traveling by bus provided by a tour operator. Indeed, in this case, travelers are transported along the road to other hotels. If you book an individual transfer, then the path to Eden Resort & Spa 5 * (Beruella) will take much less time.



Description

The five-star Eden Resort & Spa was opened in 1995. Large-scale renovation works were carried out here in 2013. The hotel's housing stock is represented by 158 comfortable rooms located in a four-story building. The area of ‚Äã‚Äãits own territory is 2.2 hectares.

The hotel has several restaurants offering local and European cuisine, bars, a large pool with a children's section and a jacuzzi, a sun terrace with comfortable sun loungers, a SPA center with a wide selection of health and beauty programs, a beauty salon, business center, fitness center, laundry and dry cleaning, sports fields, billiard room, tour desk and much more. Entertainment is available in the evenings. Eden Resort also offers wedding ceremony services.

The cost of accommodation at the hotel includes only food. However, when booking, you can also immediately pay for both lunch and dinner. The food here, according to tourists, is very good, and the menu is varied.

Hotel guests were very pleased with the beach holiday. The sea here, they said, is often turbulent, but for most travelers this was not a problem.



Housing stock

As already mentioned, the hotel Eden Resort & Spa 5 * has 158 rooms. The apartments here are of the following types:

  • Superior (38 sq. M., Maximum occupancy - 3 guests, king, queen or single beds).
  • Deluxe (49 sq. M, the maximum can accommodate three guests, are located on the ground floor, there is a terrace).
  • Superior deluxe (38 sq., Located on the second floor, there is a balcony with wooden floor).
  • Paradise room (43 sq. M, maximum occupancy - 2 guests, are on the third floor, king-size beds, it is impossible to install playpens for children).
  • Paradise Suite (53 sq. M., Designed for two guests, located on the fourth floor, two rooms, a bathroom with a bathtub and a Jacuzzi).
  • Penthouse (49 sq. M, maximum 2 people, two rooms with a door, a king size bed, a bathroom with shower and bathtub, a spacious balcony with panoramic views, outdoor shower).

All apartments have the necessary comfortable furniture, minibar, air conditioning, satellite TV, telephone, bathroom, hairdryer, balcony or terrace, tea set. Towels are cleaned and changed every day.

Opinion of tourists

Judging by the reviews of our compatriots who visited the Eden Resort & Spa, they were generally very pleased with the hotel. A vivid confirmation of this is the hotel rating based on guest ratings. It makes 4.5 points out of the maximum possible five. According to tourists, this hotel is perfect for different categories of vacationers - couples in love, newlyweds, families with children and older people.
