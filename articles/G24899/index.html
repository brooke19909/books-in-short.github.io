HTC Phones: Customer Reviews

The days when only two or three manufacturers showed off mobile phones on store shelves are long gone. Now the assortment of gadgets is able to impress even the most demanding customer. Products are divided into categories according to cost. The budget segment is crowded with Chinese gadgets. On average, you can find phones from almost all manufacturers, of course, except for Apple. The latter company specializes in producing only flagship devices. Unfortunately, not all buyers are available. But at present, worthy specimens are already found in the middle segment. To them, undoubtedly, HTC phones are ranked, the reviews of which will be considered in this article. This is not the first year that a Taiwanese company has been launching smartphones for the domestic market. Her products have fans. Even with the fact that she is not a sales leader, in the assortment range, of course, there are such models that are worthy of attention. Let's get acquainted with the features of HTC smartphones.

If you read the user reviews, you can notice such a pattern. Most of them say that the manufacturer has too large a lineup in which it is easy to get confused. They also believe that the company has an uncompromising policy. It was she who led to a decline in consumer demand. In 2017, HTC decided to rehabilitate itself in the eyes of customers, releasing several flagships, for example, HTC U11. But the most significant drawback of these mobile gadgets is the high price. According to buyers, it is unreasonably high. Naturally, this affects the level of sales. If you compare HTC with another Taiwanese company, Asus, then the last one in 2016 sold about 21.5 million phones against 13 million.

Let's first look at the reviews on HTC Sensation series phones. The first device of this line appeared on sale in 2011. In total, it includes three models. All of them belong to the middle segment. They have a monoblock type of housing. They work on Android.

Lineup HTC Desire

Now let's move on to the reviews of HTC Desire phones. The series was launched in 2010. The manufacturer has not in vain chose this name. It translates from English as "welcome." It is noteworthy that in this line there are devices of the budget price category and the middle one. They are all based on the most popular OS. And users consider this an indisputable plus. It is Android that is currently an optimally balanced system with great capabilities. The second version was installed on the first phones of this line. In 2014, the fourth one has already been sold (for example, the HTC 210 phone). We will consider reviews of other characteristics of this model a bit later. In 2016, smartphones are already on sale with Android 6.0. In the reviews, users drew attention to the branded shell of HTC Sense. This is a definite plus, which speaks of the desire of the manufacturer to highlight their products against the background of other gadgets.

What about performance? Of course, its level is growing. The "younger" smartphone was equipped with a Qualcomm-branded MSM7225A processor. The maximum of its capabilities was limited by a frequency of 600 MHz. RAM was also not surprising in large volumes. The Desire C model had a total of 512 MB. But in 2016, the manufacturer released the HTC Desire 10 Pro. It has excellent features. Powered by Helio X10 chip. Computing modules (8 cores) are accelerated to 1800 MHz. The system row is 64 bits. It is important that the developers added four gigabytes of RAM to the capabilities.

There are, of course, other phones in this line. They implemented more modest characteristics than Desire 10 Pro. For example, in the category of up to 10,000 rubles, the Desire 628 model attracted the attention of buyers.

HTC One X10 specifications and reviews

Reviews on the phone HTC (Dual SIM format "nano") One X10 in most cases are positive. The gadget's strengths include good performance (Helio P10), beautiful design, excellent cameras (8/16 megapixels), Android 6.0 with a proprietary shell, decent RAM (3 GB), 5.5-inch screen with a pixel density of 400 ppi and 4000 mAh battery. HTCWindowsPhone Series

At the Taiwanese manufacturer, not all devices are based on Android. There are also those that are running Windows Phone. Unfortunately, these HTC phones cannot be called popular, reviews are confirmation of this. This operating system cannot compete with Android. Potential buyers are those people who are already fed up with the interface of the latest OS. The most striking example was the device with the prefix Mozart. He has occupied a certain niche in the middle segment. Let's look at what customers think about it.

HTC 7 Mozart

This model appeared on sale in 2010. It is ideal for those who do not like large devices. The smartphone differs from the above in that it runs on Windows Phone 7. Conclusion

What is the general opinion of customers about HTC products? In general, the manufacturer tries to follow the modern requirements as much as possible. However, if the device receives excellent characteristics, then the final price is pretty impressive. And this is the point that almost all buyers consider a significant drawback. But in defense of this manufacturer, I must say that all smartphones are high-quality and work without failures for a long time.
