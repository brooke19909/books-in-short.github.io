Hotel Dessole Dolphin Bay Resort 4 * (Greece, Crete): description, reviews of tourists

Crete is an island with a developed tourist infrastructure, clean beaches and picturesque nature, so it is not surprising that it attracts a huge number of travelers from around the world. And although the cost of the rest here cannot be called budgetary, many guests from the CIS come here every year. There are many small and inexpensive hotels on the island, but many prefer to stay in comfortable resort hotels. One of such complexes will be discussed in this article. We will tell you more about the Dessole Dolphin Bay Resort 4 * hotel in Crete, its rooms, infrastructure, as well as the reviews of tourists who have already spent their holidays here.

More about the location of this complex

Judging by the reviews of tourists, the Dessole Dolphin Bay Resort (Crete) has a very favorable location. It is built directly on the first line, and its territory is almost adjacent to the coast. Therefore, you can reach it in a matter of minutes. Also, guests positively assess the proximity of the hotel to infrastructure facilities. For example, a bus stop is just 50 meters from the main building of the complex. The hotel itself is located on the outskirts of the small resort village of Amoudara. Its central part is only 300 meters away from the hotel. Therefore, tourists can easily walk not only to restaurants and cafes, but also to large shopping centers. At the same time, the noise of roads and nightclubs does not reach the hotel, so the rooms are always quiet.

A significant advantage of this hotel is its proximity to the capital of Crete - the large city of Heraklion. It is 8 km from here, and can be reached by bus or taxi. In addition, not far from the capital is the international airport. It is 12 km away from the hotel. Therefore, tourists can reach their vacation spot in less than 1 hour.

Basic hotel information

The resort complex Dessole Dolphin Bay Resort (Greece) was built in 1981, however, in the reviews, guests note that the hotel does not look dilapidated or old. And all because cosmetic repairs are carried out almost annually here. The last major renovation ended at the hotel in 2014. The complex itself occupies a rather large area of ‚Äã‚Äãapproximately 54,000 square meters. m. For its most part there is a well-kept Mediterranean garden with small walking paths for walks in the shade of trees. The main building is a small three-story building. Part of the rooms, reception and other administrative facilities are located here. But there are also small two-story bungalows on the territory for lovers of secluded relaxation. In total, the hotel offers guests 259 rooms.

This hotel does not have any special requirements for guests, as it tries to adhere to European standards of service. For example, the arrival and departure of tourists is made only at certain times. So, registration of tourists is carried out from 14:00 until midnight, but they must leave their rooms before noon. You cannot come here with animals of any size, such guests will simply be denied service. In addition, it is worth noting that the hotel has Russian-speaking staff who are always ready to help guests from Russia who do not know English.

The rooms of this hotel do not differ in large area, have a simple design and high-quality equipment. It is immediately worth noting that there are no adjacent apartments in the hotel, so large families will have to rent several living rooms at once. Most of the hotel are standard rooms. They are equipped with a double bed or two single beds. In addition to the living room, there is an open balcony or terrace, as well as a combined bathroom. What equipment can be found in the room?

Tourists living in the rooms of the Dessole Dolphin Bay Resort 4 * hotel (Amoudara) are provided with a standard complex of amenities, which includes all the necessary equipment for a relaxing break. In addition to furniture, guests can find a TV in the living room, to which a set of cable channels is connected. At the same time, guests from the CIS countries are provided with several Russian speakers. All rooms have air conditioning to cool the room. On the balcony you can find a clothes dryer. Of the additional equipment, guests are offered a safe where you can leave your valuables and documents safe, but its rental is not included in the price and is paid separately. All rooms also include free Wi-Fi.

The bathroom offers a standard set of bath accessories, which includes not only a set of towels, but also shower gel, shampoo and soap, as well as toilet paper and napkins. All of them are replenished daily by the hotel staff, who carry out housekeeping. Bed linen at the hotel decided to change at least several times a week.

What kind of food does the hotel provide?

Dessole Dolphin Bay Resort also includes all-inclusive meals. As expected, guests can eat daily at the buffet in the main restaurant of the complex, as well as order local drinks in bars. The concept itself works exclusively until midnight, then you can eat or drink only for a fee. Alcohol is not served to guests under the age of 18. The restaurant also offers free vegetarian, diet and baby food. Ice cream is served daily from 15:00 until 17:00. In addition, tourists can sign up for a special themed dinner, which takes place at Pasiphae Beach Tavern every day from 19:30. It also hosts daily afternoon tea parties.

There are several catering facilities on site. Guests can visit:

  • the lobby bar, which opens in the evenings, it offers alcoholic and soft drinks, as well as premium alcohol, coffee and tea;
  • beach bar - specializes in soft drinks, works in the daytime;
  • pool bar;
  • Androgeo Brasserie - Open four days a week, serving Greek draft wine and beer.

Beach holidays at the hotel

Dessole Dolphin Bay Resort has its own stretch of coastline, which is approximately 130 meters long. The entrance to the water here is gentle, but the beach itself is not sandy - there is pebble in it, so you need to use shoes so as not to injure your legs. But for free guests can use sun loungers and umbrellas, play beach volleyball. For a fee, water activities are available in the special center.

The hotel has two outdoor pools. The main one is located at the main building, and the second - at the bungalow. Both of them are filled with fresh water and do not heat up. Also, the second pool has three water slides for adults and children over ten years old. Both ponds have a terrace for relaxation with free sunbeds.

What other entertainment is there in the hotel?

Dessole Dolphin Bay Resort offers a standard entertainment package for holiday resorts. However, some of them are provided only for a fee. For example, all the services of a local spa are not included in the price. Guests can visit the hammam, jacuzzi, sauna and massage room.

There are also open areas for playing basketball, tennis, volleyball and futsal. In the morning and afternoon, the hotel‚Äôs animators conduct wellness and sports classes in water aerobics, dance classes. In the evenings, they also organize themed shows and discos for adults.

Conditions for placing with small children

Since the Dessole Dolphin Bay Resort hotel is significantly removed from the bustling center of the resort, tourists with young children often choose it for relaxation. They are also attracted by their proximity to the sea, because with a child it is very difficult to travel long distances every day. At the same time, the hotel is considered a family hotel, so guests can find a standard list of amenities for young children. Upon request, children under 2 years old are provided with a cot and a baby potty, and they are given out completely free of charge. In restaurants and bars, you can use high chairs for feeding without restrictions. But for a fee, guests can even rent a stroller, as well as call in the room a nanny who takes an hourly fee.

There is not much entertainment here, but the children will certainly not be bored. They can swim in a shallow pool, which is additionally equipped with water slides. For outdoor games, two playgrounds are equipped at once. There are swings, slides, and a sandbox. Children can also be left in the mini club, where children from 4 to 12 years old are accepted. It works every day, and an experienced team of animators is engaged in children, among which there are employees who speak Russian.
