How to weave Santa Claus from gum? Tips, instructions

Amazing rubber bands "Fanny Lum" conquered the whole world with its versatility and stylish appearance. Although the history of weaving began with all kinds of bracelets, today craftsmen already offer detailed instructions and recommendations for making a variety of crafts - vegetables, fruits, humans, birds and animals. Your dream is a ready-made man-made gift for the New Year - Santa Claus from gum? On the Rainbow Lum machine, specially designed for complex products, you can make your own classic figure of a magical grandfather with a lush white beard.





Materials and Tools

First, imagine Santa Claus - how do you want to see him? Will he wear his traditional red costume? For example, to add originality to the future figure, the masters propose replacing the slightly bored red palette with a new one - purple. Find out how to weave Santa Claus out of rubber bands so that it captivates not only with a stylish appearance, but also with the fashionable colors of the New Year's outfit.

For work you will need:

  • gum "Fanny Lum" black, purple, pale orange and white;
  • two black beads for the eyes;
  • two beads for the nose and belt (it is better to take contrasting and well combined with the suit, for example pink and white or purple and green);
  • two hooks;
  • padding pad for stuffing;
  • machine Rainbow Lum.

Getting to work





First, remove the middle row of posts from the machine, and rotate the side rows so that the open sides look in the opposite direction. If you already have experience in creating interesting products from Fanny Lum, you are likely to guess how to weave a figure of Santa Claus from gum .

Making a souvenir starts from the bottom - with boots:

  1. To do this, throw on the columns double rubber bands of black color, twisting them with an eight.
  2. Throw one more black elastic band on top without twisting. Weave both boots at the same time, so that later the legs of the figure can be easily connected.
  3. Dump the lower rubber bands to the center of the machine.
  4. Spray the black ‚ÄúFanny Lum‚Äù, without twisting, around the perimeter of the resulting rectangles and dump the elastic bands of the lower row to the center. Continue weaving until you have a total of six rows. Each time lower down the finished intertwined levels.

Continue weaving

After you finish the boots, proceed to decorate them:

  • To weave fashionable white lapels for grandfather's shoes, throw a four-rubber white rubber band on the hook, hook the second white rubber band with the same hook and drop all the loops on it.
  • After making twelve such blanks, sketch them around the perimeter of the resulting rectangles.
  • Fold the bottom row of elastic bands in the center and sprinkle the purple ‚ÄúFanny Loom‚Äù without twisting them.

Repeat the previous step three more times.

The third stage - do the torso





Boots are ready! If before that you did not suspect how to make Santa Claus out of elastic bands, now you will surely be able to not only follow all the recommendations to the end, but also repeat the weaving of the figure, or even teach someone of your friends a new complex skill.

When the grandfather‚Äôs shoes are completely ready, you can proceed to the manufacture of the body, combining both legs:

  1. To do this, carefully move the right leg one column to the left.
  2. Above, along the perimeter of a large rectangle, sketch, without twisting, purple elastic bands.
  3. Between the two rectangles, cross the two purple gum crosswise, twisting them with a figure of eight.
  4. Drop the elastic bands of the lower row in the center.
  5. Next, you need to expand the torso. To do this, transfer the upper rubber band from the far right column of the lower row to an additional hook, transfer the lower rubber band to the neighboring right column, and the upper one back. The same actions must be repeated in the remaining corners of the rectangle.
  6. Throw in the purple elastic bands without twisting them around the perimeter of the rectangle.

Fourth stage

The most difficult part of the work has been completed, and now those who have long been interested in how to weave Santa Claus out of rubber bands will be completely clear what to do next. For beginners, the most detailed instructions are kept in weaving. So:

  • Dip the bottom rubber bands to the center. Please note that on the extreme columns, in the bottom row, there will be one elastic band, and on the central columns - four.
  • Weave the edge of the grandfather's caftan according to the same principle by which white lapels for boots were made. It will be necessary to make sixteen blanks from white rubber bands.
  • Discard the rubber bands of the lower row in the center, throw purple gum around the perimeter. Repeat.
  • Spread black rubber bands around the perimeter.
  • Drop the elastic bands of the lower row in the center. Pass the purple elastic into the bead (respectively, white or green) and pull it over the two central columns of the first row. Throw purple gum on the remaining columns.

Make hands

There is very little left, so now you know how to weave Santa Claus out of gum. The next step is hand manufacturing:

  • Throw on a hook a white elastic band in four turns, hook a hook on two white elastic bands and drop all the loops on them.
  • Weave another five double purple elastic bands. Then hook one purple ‚ÄúFanny Loom‚Äù and hook all the loops off the hook onto it. Repeat for the other hand.
  • Throw your hands on the side posts, and throw the purple rubber bands over the rest.
  • Remove the elastic bands of the lower row to the center, tuck your hands inside the weave.

Now you can weave your head. To do this, scribble white gum on the first row of columns, and violet on the rest.

Weaving a beard

How to make Santa Claus from rubber bands quickly and easily? Follow the instructions and try to weave the character's beard in the most interesting way:

  • Throw on the hook first one rubber band in two turns, then the next. Hook the elastic band, throw all the loops from the hook onto it. Throw such rubber bands on the columns of the front row, and on the rest throw a white ‚ÄúFanny Lum‚Äù in the usual way.
  • Remove the elastic bands of the lower row to the center. In the next three rows, sprinkle such rubber bands around the entire perimeter of the weave. In the third row, do not forget the pink bead for the nose.

The face is flesh-colored from Fanny Lum:

  • Spread them on the four central columns of the first row, then throw on the remaining columns white rubber bands for the beard.
  • The gum of the lower row is dumped into the center. Flesh-colored rubber bands are thrown on the first row of columns, adding black beads for the eyes.

The next step is to narrow the weave. To do this, repeat the narrowing of the trunk described above. The figure should remain on eight columns.

Final stage





So that you can complete the weaving of rubber bands, Santa Claus must be fully prepared. It remains only to complete the manufacture of the head:

  • Throw in a rubber band on the central pair of columns of the first row, on the rest - white, in the usual way. Discard the center of the bottom row of elastic bands. Repeat.
  • Transfer the loops from the machine to a couple of hooks.
  • Turn out the resulting figure and fill it with a padding polyester. Transfer the hinges to one hook. Crochet a white elastic band, fold all the loops. Make the last loop and thread it inside the figure.

You have got a real folk character from children's fairy tales. And all that was needed to create it was a small machine, several beads, two hooks and a set of elastic bands for weaving.
