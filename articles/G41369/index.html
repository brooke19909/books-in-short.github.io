Cavo Maris Beach Hotel 3 *: hotel description

A correctly selected hotel is the key to a successful vacation. One of the main features of the Cavo Maris Beach Hotel 3 * hotel is its location. The work of the Cavo Maris Beach Hotel 3 * hotel is planned so that you can satisfy all sorts of wishes of guests and make your vacation unforgettable. Cavo Maris Beach Hotel 3 *: room description

Comfortable accommodation is the key to a good rest. The resort complex Cavo Maris Beach Hotel 3 * (Cyprus, Protaras) has 246 spacious, well-decorated rooms. You can stay in guest apartments of the following categories:

  • Standard rooms are great for two guests. A baby cot or a cot can be provided for the child. There is also the possibility of single occupancy with the option of sharing. The room has a large or a pair of twin beds, a private balcony with a sliding glass door. You can stay in an adjacent apartment.
  • Family apartments accommodate 4 guests (plus extra beds). There is one large bed, as well as 2 twin beds. The room is characterized by a large area and convenient layout, so you will not feel crowded. The whole family will comfortably accommodate the whole family on a large balcony with a picturesque view.
  • To accommodate large companies, a mezzanine is offered . This is a two-story room equipped with a staircase made of natural wood. On the second level there is an individual bedroom with a dressing room and a bathroom. On the ground floor, in addition to the guest bedroom, there is also a living area.
  • Premier apartments allow you to immerse yourself in a sophisticated atmosphere. The bedroom has two single beds, and the lounge area is equipped with soft sofas. The balcony offers a bewitching sea view.
  • The hotel has 3 rooms specially equipped for disabled guests. Everything is thought out here for their comfort: convenient location of sockets and switches, as well as hangers and door handles, increased width of doorways, safety handrails. The room is designed so that the guest can freely move around on it in a wheelchair.

Answers to frequently asked questions

When choosing a hotel for travelers, a lot of questions come up that can become decisive in the organization of leisure. If we talk about the hotel Cavo Maris Beach Hotel 3 * (Protaras), then it gives answers in advance to most of them:

Question Answer
1. What are the conditions for using Wi-Fi? Guests enjoy unlimited free Wi-Fi access. To assign an individual password, you need to contact the reception.
2. How is the waiting for registration? If the hotel has a sufficient number of free apartments, then you can use both early check-in and late check-out. Otherwise, you can spend time in comfortable waiting rooms.
3. Is a dress code set at the hotel? Swimwear is only allowed on the beach and pool. Throughout the rest of the territory it is recommended to be in casual clothes. To visit restaurants, men need to have a shirt and long trousers.
4. What is the room electric power system? The rooms are equipped with sensors, according to which the electricity is turned off when there is no movement in the room. If the guest needs round-the-clock power supply (for example, for medical devices), such an opportunity will be provided.
5. Do I need to bring towels for the beach? Free beach towels are provided at the hotel. If necessary, they are replaced with clean ones.
6. Do I have to pay for using beach attributes? Sun loungers and umbrellas are available on site for free. Facilities located on the beach are subject to a surcharge.
7. Is it possible to change the power system? Yes, but only if you intend to stay at the hotel for more than 4 nights.
8. Is it possible to change the room category? Yes, but only if there is enough space available.
9. Is it possible to cook food in my room on my own? Not. For security reasons, the rooms do not have any kitchen appliances other than an electric kettle.
10. Is it possible to create a diet or vegetarian menu? Upon arrival, inform the restaurant manager or chef about your contraindications or special requests. Individual dishes will be prepared for you or the appropriate products from the available assortment will be selected.
