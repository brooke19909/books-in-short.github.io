Game process

In the twelfth fantasy, the player manages the actions of a team of three heroes, simultaneously interacting with the NPC and enemy units. This game is fundamentally different from the previous parts of the series in that the player can now rotate the camera 360 degrees with the right joystick of the gamepad. In the city, the passage of the secrets of Final Fantasy 12 is available only by one character. In enemy zones, two more heroes join him, controlled by a computer.

This is the first game in the Final Fantasy 12 PC RUS series, which uses a unique turn-based combat system in real time. It is called Activ Dimenshion Battle, shortly ADB. To conduct battles, the player uses a specialized menu in which the battle process stops until each character is assigned an action for the next move. All of them need to be given a command manually, if this is not a guest character who has artificial intelligence. All actions will be divided into five types: attacking, magical, haze abilities, gambits and the use of objects.

The new generation battle system makes random encounters - all opponents are visible on the minimap, and in all cases not provided for by the plot, the player is able to avoid a battle with the enemy. Actions begin when a detachment approaches a sufficient distance to an aggressive opponent or attacks a passive monster. When a player performs an action, directional arrows appear in the game, uniting the attacker and the attacked, and the color of this connection indicates the nature of the attack. A game squad can consist of no more than three characters, and during the battle you can replace the killed or wounded heroes, but only in cases that are not provided for by the storyline. The consequence of a special type of battles was the refusal of developers from the victory scenes traditional for previous games of the Final Fantasy 12 Zodiac series. They survived only in boss battles.

Another update of the gameplay for passing Final Fantasy 12 zodiac job system are gambits. The player was given the opportunity, subject to certain conditions during the battle, to carry out pre-selected actions. The gambit system consists of three parts: priority, target orientation and the action itself. The command to be executed is called an action. Priority is an attribute that selects which gambit will work if there are two or more involved. The goal is the property of the gambit, for example, the restoration of mana, if the health level drops below 50%.

In the twelfth part of the series, a new ability appears - the haze. This is an analogue of mana, with which the player can summon creatures into his squad and inflict special attacking combinations. Weapons Final Fantasy 12 has special ultimate abilities. These attacks are tied to using the quickening system. Using the "License Board" of any character, you can teach these skills. Using the controller, through the use of button combinations, you can make huge attacking combinations, after which the final blow occurs.

Hero Evolution System

The game, as a typical representative of an RPG, has a system of gaining experience and leveling characters. To reach a new stage in development, it is necessary to kill a certain number of enemy units. With each subsequent level, the total number of health points, strength, agility, magic and others increases. Also, as you progress, the secrets of Final Fantasy 12 are unlocked by the same ‚ÄúLicense Board‚Äù. It is divided into two parts. In the first, all licenses are associated with magic abilities, magic rings, as well as constant passive attribute bonuses. The second part of the ‚ÄúLicense Board‚Äù gives you the opportunity to get unique armor and mythical weapons of the ancients. To obtain these gaming capabilities, the player spends a certain amount of resources earned during the passage of the storyline.
