Mystical Green Island (Rostov-on-Don): recreation, recreation

Rostov is a unique city in the south of the Russian Federation. It is famous not only for its historical buildings and rich cultural and architectural heritage, but also for the unusual and even mystical island, which is located on the river. Don, in the city limits. It is called Green Island (Rostov-on-Don), and its length is only 4 km in length. Until now, it has caused great interest among specialists in anomalous zones, and people have a double impression - admiration and inexplicable fear.





A little information

For a long time in its place was a village with a private sector and well-groomed alleys. Today it is a resort area with two streets (District and Island). The main part of the preserved and existing hotels is located along the District Ring. The coastline extends here.

The land received its name due to the fact that its entire territory is buried in vegetation, fruit trees and berry bushes. In some clearings you can see a blackberry. Willow, sedge and tall poplars grow everywhere. The green island (Rostov-on-Don) needs reconstruction, now its streets are broken, and paths with alleys are littered with trees.

Therefore, the best transport for moving around the territory is a bicycle. Despite some abandonment and low population, Rostovites are still drawn here. Speaking flattering words about this picturesque corner of Rostov, it is impossible not to mention the mystical side, which haunts the townspeople.
