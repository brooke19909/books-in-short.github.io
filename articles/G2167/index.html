Browse Popular Creative Speakers

High-quality music is a guarantee of a good time. But not every device can please a sophisticated listener with a refined sound. For this reason, many do not get the proper pleasure from listening to music or watching a movie. But we assure you, all these problems will disappear when you discover the columns from Creative.

Computer speakers Creative

It often happens that the desktop space is strictly limited. In this case, it makes sense to choose small speakers that will not take up much space, but will be able to generate high-quality sound. Fortunately for you, Creative produces a number of models of computer speakers, among which there will definitely be something that you like. For example, Creative A50 speakers.

They are compact, occupy a minimum of working space and, thanks to the strict design, look organically in any environment. At the same time, their external simplicity is perfectly combined with the simplicity of the internal, because the whole process of connecting the speakers to the computer will not take you a couple of minutes. All that is required of you is to connect the USB cable and MiniJack 3.5 to the system unit of your PC.

Creative Portable Speakers

Music should be everywhere. High-quality musical accompaniment can turn a boring meeting of old friends into a perky friendly party. In order for everyone to be able to please others with good music, there are Creative NUNO speakers.

Simplicity is the word that can describe this device. It is unusually simple in its execution and use. Small size, light weight, wireless connection to any phone, laptop or computer and ease of operation - these are the qualities that make this portable speaker so popular.

For special occasions, the company has a similar but much more portable Creative NUNO Micro speaker. Made in a miniature case, it retained all the basic qualities of a full-sized original.
