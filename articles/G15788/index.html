Hotel Linda Resort Hotel Linda Resort Hotel 5 *: overview, description, characteristics and reviews of tourists

Hotel Linda Resort Hotel Linda Resort Hotel 5 * is a chic resort where there is everything you need to spend an unforgettable vacation in warm and sunny Turkey. Side is just a wonderful place for those who want to plunge into the oriental flavor, while maintaining the ability to live in comfortable conditions.





Brief information about the hotel

Hotel Linda Resort Hotel Linda Resort Hotel 5 * provides tourists with all the necessary conditions and services so that they feel no less comfortable than at home. In this regard, the hotel apartments deserve special attention, which, in addition to the chic interior, are also distinguished by the presence of all the necessary list of amenities and technical equipment.

Special attention at the Linda Resort Hotel Linda Resort Hotel 5 * is given to nutrition. It is carried out on an all-inclusive basis, which implies a sumptuous buffet in the main restaurant of the hotel. In addition, guests will not stay hungry during the day, because numerous bars offer a variety of light snacks, as well as delicious pastries.

The Linda Resort Hotel Linda Resort Hotel 5 * took care of its guests to lead a healthy and active lifestyle. For this purpose, a modern tennis court is equipped , as well as a well-equipped gym. On the beach you can play volleyball, and after a day full of active games, you can relax in the sauna and enjoy a professional massage.





Location

Hotel Hotel Linda Resort Hotel 5 * (Side) is located just 3 km from the city, in an incredibly scenic area. Very close to the hotel (500 meters) is the famous Lake Titreyen, which attracts tourists from all over the world. But after passing only 600 meters from the main building, you will find yourself on a wonderful private beach of the hotel. The distance to the center of Manavgat is 5 km, but to get to the airport, you will need to overcome as much as 70 km.





Hotel rooms

Hotel Linda Resort Hotel 5 * (Side) is characterized by a relatively small size and has in its room stock only 185 apartments. Guest rooms practically do not differ from each other, in addition to the occupied space, as well as capacity. Based on this, all hotel rooms can be divided into standard double and triple rooms. Guests can be accommodated in king-size beds or single beds. It is also allowed additional placement on folding beds or on soft sofas.





Room Amenities

Each room of the Linda Resort Hotel 5 * (Titreyengol) provides a maximum of amenities that provide a comfortable stay for tourists:

  • air conditioning to regulate the air temperature in the room;
  • bathroom with toilet, hairdryer, cosmetic set and spa bath;
  • modern flat widescreen TV with the ability to watch movies from external media;
  • telephone with direct access to the international line;
  • for an additional fee, they will fill the minibar daily with your favorite drinks;
  • the room has an electronic safe, for the use of which a separate tariffing is also established;
  • small balcony with plastic furniture.

Hotel Linda Resort Hotel Linda Resort Hotel 5 * provides its guests with the following list of services and travel services:

Recreation, sports and entertainment

You can actively relax and fill your leisure with vibrant events, taking advantage of the wide range of services that Linda Resort Hotel 5 * (Turkey) provides its guests with:

  • in the summer, guests enjoy the use of a clean outdoor freshwater pool, which has a special children's compartment with water slides;
  • several modern tennis courts, which are equipped with lights for use at night;
  • in the spa you can use the services of a sauna and Turkish hammam, as well as undergo many procedures for both beauty and to restore health;
  • the wellness center offers massage services, as well as aromatherapy and more;
  • in the cold season, you can go swimming in the indoor pool;
  • hotel guests have access to a long private beach with several toilets, a shower, a dressing room and a bar (sunbeds, towels, mattresses and umbrellas are included in the price of the hotel);
  • a special playground is equipped for children;

Negative reviews

Many tourists have already managed to visit the Linda Resort Hotel 5 * hotel. Reviews about it, in addition to positive comments, also contain a number of significant comments about the work and the level of service:

  • the hotel is far enough from the beach (you need to walk about 10 minutes);
  • when entering the water, a person immediately goes to a great depth, which is not very good for tourists with children, as well as for those who cannot swim;
  • in spite of the fact that in most hotels of such a high category the filling of the minibar is included in the price of the ticket, this service is prohibitively expensive here;
  • there are some problems with ventilation, due to which there is an unpleasant odor in the bathroom of some rooms;
  • a coffee and tea set is provided in the room, but their supplies are not replenished, even if the drinks have already run out.
