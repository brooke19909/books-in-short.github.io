Yalta hotels: overview, reviews of tourists

Crimea is a unique place both for a relaxing holiday on the Black Sea, and for travel lovers. And every tourist wants to visit Yalta at least once. A magnificent embankment, sparkling blue water under the bright southern sun, a colorful market, cozy cafes - this city has everything in order to warn the slightest wish of visitors. Well, the comfortable living conditions that Yalta hotels offer their guests will complement your experience.

Hotel Intourist

Interestingly, once the rest here was available only to members of the Soviet government and foreigners. Now the Yalta-Intourist Hotel is the largest resort center of the southern coast of Crimea, where anyone can relax. Each room of the hotel complex has a balcony. The hotel infrastructure includes:

  • water park with a play area for children and slides for adults;
  • sandy two-level beach, for the convenience of descent to which an elevator is provided;
  • heated seawater pool;
  • infinity pool, which has a hot tub and bar;
  • a swimming pool equipped with towers for diving enthusiasts;
  • two children's pools;
  • several restaurants;
  • children's playgrounds;
  • several sports fields;
  • Wellness complexes.

In the mornings, the breakfast buffet is elegantly served on the outdoor terrace with sea views. Having overcome only 5 kilometers, you can visit the Massandra Palace and the Nikitsky Botanical Garden. Each room includes breakfast. If you wish, you can choose a placement option where the price will also include dinner, or payment with full board. Accommodation in rooms of different categories will range from 6,000 rubles to 19,500 rubles in one day.

Park Hotel

Park Hotel in Yalta is located in a cozy building with classic balconies. From some rooms, tourists can enjoy wonderful views of the Black Sea. You can dine in the colorful wooden restaurant. The hotel area is cozy, a lot of greenery and flowers, elegant terraces are arranged around. To visit the Alexander Nevsky Cathedral, you need to walk only 450 meters. Shops, bars and restaurants are within walking distance. The beach is a 6-minute walk from the hotel, and ten minutes is enough to get to Massandra Beach. Compared to other Yalta hotels, the prices here are perhaps one of the most affordable. For a double superior room you will pay 3800 rubles, and for apartments 4900 rubles in one day.

There is a large pool with sea views and a bar. Buffet breakfast is included in the price. You can also eat at a table on the balcony of your room, which offers wonderful sea views. Living in a comfort double room will cost you 7,000 rubles per day. The highest cost for deluxe rooms is up to 14,000 rubles.

Hotel "Crimean"

The Krymsky Hotel is located on Polikurovsky Hill, which is the historical center of old Yalta. A large resort town when it was a small fishing village, which gradually expanded. From the former settlements on the hill, only the bell tower was preserved, near which the hotel is located. From the windows of the hotel you can see the Yalta Bay, the majestic landscapes of the Crimean Mountains, as well as Cape Ai-Todor. At a distance of 4.5 kilometers from the hotel is the fairy tale zoo. The road to a pebbly furnished beach runs through Massandra Park. For car owners on the territory of the hotel there is a parking lot, there is a children's playground. There is a private cafe. The cost of a single room is 2500 rubles, a double - 3500 rubles per day. In total, the hotel offers rooms of four categories, each room has a balcony.

Hotel Mriya Resort & Spa

If you are interested in relaxing in a spa hotel in Yalta, pay attention to the five-star Mriya Resort & Spa. Well-maintained, equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay, your own beach is a 4-minute walk from the hotel. Guests can also swim in the pool on site. The spa and wellness center offers tourists a sauna, hammam, hot tub and fitness room. In the medical center you will have the opportunity to conduct diagnostics on modern equipment. There are several restaurants and bars. Balconies offer magnificent views of the sea. The grounds of the Mriya Resort & Spa are stunningly beautiful. Compared to other hotels in Yalta, the prices for accommodation here are very high. Double deluxe room with garden view costs 15 799 rubles per day, the cost of a family suite with sea view is 27 699 rubles per day. For a three-bedroom villa, you will have to pay 107,899 rubles per night.

Hotel Bristol

The three-star Bristol Hotel is located in the city center, just 200 meters from the promenade. As well as near other Yalta hotels, next to it, in the immediate vicinity, there are several attractions. Tourists can take a walk in the Seaside Park, visit the Chekhov House-Museum, ride the Yalta-Gorka cable car. A walk to the beach will take you no more than three minutes. In addition, if the weather is not conducive to walking, guests can swim in the indoor pool or relax in the sauna. Many have chic rooms with sea views. Guests can enjoy pizza at the hotel's restaurant. In the morning, a rich breakfast buffet is served here. The cost of a budget double budget room at the Bristol Hotel is 4465 rubles per night, accommodation in a deluxe room will cost 7885 rubles a day. Room rate includes breakfast.
