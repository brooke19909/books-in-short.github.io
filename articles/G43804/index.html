Where to stay: popular recreation centers of Odessa

Odessa is one of the most popular resort cities. Here, on the Black Sea, all conditions for a real summer vacation are created. But in order not to overshadow your vacation with unnecessary worries, it is important to think in advance about where you will stay. Numerous recreation centers of Odessa allow you to choose housing that meets the needs and capabilities of each guest. Below are some places where vacationers stop every year.

"Sunny"

One of the most popular is the recreation center "Solar" (Odessa). Many people like its location. It is located in the Luzanovka district, the most promising place for tourism in the city. It has developed infrastructure, a shallow and shallow bottom of the sea, reasonable prices, and it is quite close to the city center.

This recreation center offers accommodation in rooms ranging from inexpensive to apartments. The sandy beach is only five meters away. There is a wellness complex on site. Three meals a day are provided here and a bar-buffet is functioning.



"Good Farm"

Recreation facilities of Odessa are located along the entire coast. All of them offer comfortable accommodation and developed infrastructure. But those who want to be outside the bustle of the city and at the same time not go too far can think about staying at the Good Farm base. This place is only 15 minutes from the center. On the basis of the conditions for a pleasant outdoor recreation.

Gulf Stream

This cozy place is located in the resort village of Zatoka. It is worth noting that the people who stayed there were convinced that it was no worse than recreation centers in Odessa. This well-maintained place is under constant guard and there is a high level of service. Especially impressive is the huge variety of flowers and lush green alleys.

"Natalie"

Natalie is a quiet, comfortable recreation center (Odessa, Zatoka). There are also rooms designed for every budget. On the territory there is a restaurant with free Wi-Fi, a fountain, gazebos and barbecue facilities. The base has its own pool and sun loungers, and the beach is 150 meters away. It is nice to relax here, as pines, spruce, shrubs, flowers and other plantings grow on the territory.



"Pension Liman"

This recreation center is also located in Zatoka. For your stay, you can choose a cottage, a standard room or a suite. The dining room provides meals. There is an Internet, a club and sports grounds. If necessary, a grill with firewood is provided for free here. Guarded parking lot. For children, entertainment programs are organized.

"Lagoon"

The recreation center is also located in the resort village of Gribovka. It has a perfectly clean beach and air. Guests are pleased with beautiful cozy houses with comfortable rooms. There are activities on the beach that help you relax and feel like a real spa.

"Micron"

Mikron is among the inexpensive but well-maintained recreation centers. Here you can have a good rest with children or stay if you plan a long vacation. The hostel stands on a spit between the Dniester estuary and the sea.

Primorye

This camp site is located in a secluded place in Zatoka on a sand spit. "Primorye" differs from any recreation center in Odessa in its quiet environment, so those who like a quiet pastime by the sea, come here. Also here you can relax couples with children.



"Richard"

Recreation center of the European level. It offers deluxe rooms and European standard. The base has its own beach. The hotel provides free sun loungers, mattresses, towels and a playground for children.
