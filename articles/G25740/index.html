Hotel Adora Garden Hotel 3 * (Side) - overview, description and reviews of tourists

The Turkish Adora Garden Hotel 3 * hotel offers guests from around the world good conditions for living, which are combined with a fairly high level of service. In accordance with international standards, this place was given the status of "three stars", it belongs to the category of small places for recreation, which is associated with a small number of rooms.

general information

Adora Garden Hotel 3 * has been hosting tourists on its territory since 2003 - it is he who is considered the date of construction of this place. At the moment, thanks to periodic renovations, the hotel building has a modern look and offers guests wonderful, freshly renovated rooms. Location

In their reviews of Adora Garden Hotel 3 *, tourists often say that the hotel is perfect for a quiet family holiday, due to its location in a quiet place.

It is located in the largest resort city of Turkey - Side. Its center is located literally 1.5 kilometers from the hotel, which allows guests who want to have fun, go in search of interesting places in this area. This can be done by car or on foot. The city beach is located quite far from the hotel - 1 km.

As for transport nodes, the Adora Garden Hotel 3 * (Side) is located near the bus stop - 200 meters. The nearest major airport is located in Antalya, which is 60 km from this place.

From the very moment the hotel was founded, a modern air conditioning system was installed in all of its rooms, which allows guests to enjoy the optimum indoor temperature in any weather. Air conditioners are regulated from the central control panel.

Also, all rooms have a bathroom, where there is a standard set of sanitary ware, as well as a set of bathrobes, bath towels, soap products are also offered in an amount equal to the number of persons settled.

"Standard"

A large proportion of the apartments are represented in the Standard category. According to the tourists who rested in them, tranquility and comfort reign in the territory of such rooms. Each of them has an outdoor terrace where guests can sunbathe or simply enjoy the beautiful view of the gardens and mountains.

There is a large double bed with orthopedic mattress, as well as a pair of pedestals attached to it, where it seems possible to store small things. There is a small wardrobe for clothes, as well as a pair of armchairs and a tea table in the apartment.

Along one of the walls of the room there is a small table where there is an electric kettle with all the accessories necessary for making hot drinks.

In their free time, tourists can watch a plasma TV, which is available here, and, upon request, a safe and a minibar can be installed in the apartments, the assortment of which, if paid daily, will be updated with cold drinks of local production.



"Family"

In such apartments, many guests recommend relaxing with their entire families. There are also all conditions in order to cook for yourself food or hot drinks.

Family rooms of the Adora Garden Hotel 3 * hotel (Turkey, Side) can accommodate three guests at a time, for which there is one double and one single bed. Near them are also installed small cabinets and wardrobes. If necessary, upon an additional request, the administration completely free of charge installs a crib of the "playpen" type in the apartment for a child aged 4 to 12 years.

A separate place in the room is occupied by a kitchen corner, where you can prepare food for yourself and your family. Especially its presence is appreciated by guests who are on vacation with young children - according to them, you can feed your child healthy freshly prepared food. Here, tourists can use an electric kettle, stove, refrigerator, and the whole process can be carried out on the table.

For meals, the apartment has a dining area, represented by a small table and four chairs near it.

Conditions for children

Parents who live in Adora Garden Hotel 3 * with their children, as a rule, share their impressions about the conditions created by the hotel administration for a comfortable stay for kids. First of all, they are associated with the availability of free bedding for children.

In the open area, kids can have fun on a large playground, which is equipped with everything necessary for an active children's pastime. In addition to her, the aquazone has a small pool.



Aquazone

Guests staying at the Adora Garden Hotel 3 * (Side) are very fond of spending their free time in the water zone, which is located next to the main building of the hotel. It is presented in the form of a large and clean sunny terrace, where there are several sun loungers designed for a comfortable stay for guests. All of them are placed under large folding umbrellas, which perfectly protect vacationers from the harmful effects of sunlight.

In the very center of this terrace is a small pool in which there is always clear water. The process of water heating is not automated in the niche - it is carried out only under the influence of the sun's rays, however, the cleaning is carried out due to the installed system. At the height of the spa season, there is a bar in the aquazone area where tourists can afford to cool off with cocktails.



Nutrition

When tourists talk about Adora Garden (Turkey / Side), many turn their attention to the food that is provided here. It is carried out in a small restaurant, which is located in the building of the building, where all available rooms are located. A daily buffet is served here, where guests are invited. It offers dishes prepared according to traditional Turkish recipes. Many travelers recommend staying here to try food prepared from meat on an open fire - according to them, it has a special taste.

In addition to the restaurant, the Adora Garden Hotel 3 * has a small bar, in which the menu offers a huge assortment of drinks, many of which are locally produced. Talented bartenders work here who can surprise tourists with their skill in preparing cocktails. The assortment contains a selection of snacks, as well as fruits. Sweet desserts are preferred by local desserts, most of which are classic oriental.



Services

The hotel offers a wide range of services for all its guests, among which the possibility of car rental is especially popular. A huge number of tourists recommend going to the most interesting places in Side and its environs by rented vehicles: some prefer to go to an entertainment club at night, and some prefer sightseeing. There are some tourists who successfully combine both possibilities. By the way, parking for cars is provided completely free of charge, in an open area located on the territory of the hotel.

If desired, guests can use the ATM located on the first floor of the building, however, the presence of "live" money is not necessary here - the hotel accepts plastic cards of international categories.



Thermal zone

Hotel guests often visit the local thermal zone, a positive opinion about which is also reflected in many comments about the rest. It consists of a large steam room and a hammam. Here, according to tourists, a team consists of several talented parmeisters who can make hot procedures more enjoyable. After visiting the steam room, many guests go to make water procedures in the pool.

Beach

The coastline of the Mediterranean Sea is a kilometer from the hotel, which does not please many tourists. That is why the hotel provides a free shuttle service to guests on the territory of the sandy city beach and back.

According to vacationers, the beach line here is quite clean, the water near the shore is warm, and the slope is gentle. Here you can rent chaise lounges and umbrellas, mattresses and towels need to be brought with you, as their rental service is not provided here.

Along the coast you can have fun. There is an opportunity to go water skiing, a jet ski, and tourists often rent yachts and boats in order to send on a short trip.



Prices

In reviews of Adora Garden Hotel 3 * (Turkey / Side), it is often said that relaxing at a hotel is relatively inexpensive for travelers. That is why often their choice stops at this place for relaxation. As practice shows, the average cost of a ticket to the hotel is about 40,000 rubles, it is designed for a weekly stay for two in a Standard room.

If desired, tourists can include in its price a transfer service, which is paid and is provided only upon prior request, which must be done at least one day before their arrival in Turkey. Otherwise, the guest will be forced to use a taxi, which is not only expensive, but also not always comfortable.



In Adora Garden Hotel 3 * (Side) prices are relatively low and for the provision of additional services. As guests note, renting a safe will cost about $ 1.5 per day for its use, the same goes for the minibar.
