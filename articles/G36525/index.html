How to mold a house of plasticine?

A fabulous house for small residents and miniature dolls is easily and simply molded from plasticine. To create, you need to prepare the following tools:

  • plasticine of different colors;
  • cardboard;
  • a small rolling pin;
  • scissors, a simple pencil, a ruler;
  • a thick needle.

The procedure is simple, it can be mastered by both a child and an adult.

  1. On a cardboard using a ruler and a pencil, draw a house template: four walls, four parts of the roof.
  2. Cut cardboard parts.
  3. Tear a large piece from gray plasticine, knead it in your hand and evenly distribute it on the cardboard walls of the future hut. Using a rolling pin, carefully level the walls so that the bulges do not protrude and the surface is flat.
  4. Using a thick needle and a ruler, draw bricks on the walls.
  5. Connect the parts of the walls, carefully connecting at the fold.
  6. Cut the pattern of the roof. To tear off several identical pieces from blue plasticine, roll them a little to the shape of a flat oval, so that they look like shards. Stick on the roof template. Leave room for a pipe molded from gray plasticine. Make a cube, draw bricks with a needle and build the product into the roof.
  7. To mold small squares from yellow plasticine, 2 pieces. It will be windows. Blue color is useful for the manufacture of window frames. Make four small flagella and stick around the yellow square. Paste in the middle two crossed strips in the form of "+". With a knife, cut out a place for windows and attach them to the walls.
  8. Roll out a rectangle from red plasticine. Round off the top. Roll the door handle out of a small ball. Embed the resulting door in the main wall of the house.
  9. Set the house on an unnecessary drive or any other surface. From green plasticine make a lawn.
