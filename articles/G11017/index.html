Can I pay with a Sberbank card abroad?

Yes, of course. It will not be a surprise to anyone that any plastic card of a Russian bank can be successfully used abroad. However, it is worth considering the payment system to which the miniature gadget is attached.





For example, the Mir map has appeared in Russia today. It is very convenient for citizens in many ways. Can I pay with a Sberbank card abroad if it relates to this payment system? Not. You can use it only within the country. Abroad, it will be inactive.
