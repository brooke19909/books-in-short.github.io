Centara Blue Marine Resort Spa Phuket (Thailand): photos and reviews of tourists

If you want to spend your vacation in a luxury resort and spa hotel, then you should go to Thailand. There are many such complexes. And one of the most popular is the Centara Blue Marine Resort & Spa Phuket.





general information

The hotel complex was opened a little more than ten years ago. But the last reconstruction dates from 2010, so the hotel looks new both inside and out.

Centara Blue Marine Resort & Spa Phuket is located in a very convenient place for tourists. That is, in close proximity to the most popular beach in Phuket, which is Patong. It can be reached in a couple of minutes. And even closer is Kalim Beach. True, it is regularly exposed to frequent tides. Nearby are the boxing arena, Bangla Road (the epicenter of the resort‚Äôs nightlife), as well as a cabaret, go-kart track and Prince Songkla University. These are the sights closest to the hotel. And the airport is relatively close. Drive about 50 minutes. So the place where the hotel is located is convenient in every sense of the word.

Service

At Centara Blue Marine Resort & Spa Phuket, there is absolutely everything that guests may need. Naturally, this hotel offers free Wi-Fi and parking (near the building), a 24-hour reception and luggage storage, a tour desk and ticket service. If necessary, guests can take advantage of the receptionist and concierge. And here you can call the nanny, which is very convenient for people who come with children. A professional educator will take care of the child while his parents relax and enjoy each other's company.

It is worth noting that for business travelers in the hotel all conditions are organized. There are six meeting rooms and a large conference room with a capacity of 200 people. There is also a computer center.

In addition to the above, Centara Blue Marine Resort & Spa Phuket offers dry cleaning and laundry services, an airport shuttle, and newspaper service and room service. However, this is not all. VIP service and car rental are also available here. The staff speaks both Thai and English, so you can contact the administrators for any questions.





Things to do?

Mostly people come to the Centara Blue Marine Resort & Spa Phuket 4 * in order to spend most of their time sunbathing on the beach and swimming in the sea. However, for this resort hotel is intended. Beach towels are provided for guests, and a free, open mini-bus to and from the hotel takes them to the sea. It takes a few minutes to go, but anyone who does not want to do this can find out the schedule at the reception and use this convenient service.

An outdoor swimming pool is on site along with a terrace where guests sunbathe. The hotel also has a library, a billiard room, table tennis, a games room and a full-fledged fitness center with modern fitness equipment. If you want, you can rent a bike or take yoga classes in the hall.

When you are in Phuket, you should definitely go on an eco-tour, go parasailing and diving, try sailing, windsurfing, go water scooters or even parachute. All this is possible here.

For body and soul

As you can understand from the name of the complex, the Centara Blue Marine Resort & Spa Phuket hotel, the photo of which is presented above, has its own spa center. And staying at this hotel, you just need to go there at least once.

There are several treatment rooms, some of which are designed to serve couples. Various treatments are offered: from Ayurveda to massage and aromatherapy.

Vanilla-chocolate masks and body wraps, flower foot baths, softening and moisturizing skin mousses are popular. Here, special cosmetics are used, saturated with vitamins E and A. Thanks to their effects, the skin is restored and regenerated. Mousses also include extracts of orange, cocoa, lime and coconut flakes. But applying this mass is only the beginning of a pleasant procedure. Then the client is given a relaxing massage that relieves stress. Shea butter used in the process perfectly moisturizes the skin. And so that the client can prolong the effect, at the end of the procedure he will be presented with a bottle with this healing agent and shower gel.

And this is just one of the services available here. A full list will be available upon arrival, guests are given brochures.

Good to know

Many people would like to spend their holidays at Centara Blue Marine Resort & Spa Phuket. The reviews that other tourists leave about their vacation here, for many cause a corresponding desire. However, more on that later. In the meantime, it is worth noting the attention of several important nuances that are worth knowing.

Check-in here starts at 15:00, and departure - until 12:00. The hotel is allowed to stay with children. If people came with a child who is under 12, then you will not have to pay extra for his stay. But he will sleep on the beds in the apartments.

The kid is not even two years old? Then he will be provided with a crib, also free of charge. If there are two children, then for the second one you will have to pay 588.5 TNV per day (about 1,050 rubles at the current rate). But he will be provided with an extra bed. If you need it, then you should contact the administration before arrival. A confirmation of the provision of this service will be sent to the guest. And by the way, the same thing needs to be done to organize the transfer. At least 72 hours in advance, the guest must contact the hotel by telephone, order a service and indicate their flight details.

About staff

Many travelers who visited this complex, note the attention of its employees. Cleaning is done daily; towels and linen are also changed. As recent reviews show, the hotel has a Russian-speaking administrator who can be contacted for any questions.

More guests talk about the fact that the territory of the hotel is huge, but it is constantly monitored. As well as the condition of the pools. Everywhere is clean and tidy. The area, by the way, is green, there are a lot of flowers around, and the lobby has a small decorative pond with carps.

In general, if we conclude about the staff - they serve quickly, well, paying attention to all the little things. And it pleases.





What else do guests say?

Hotel Centara Blue Marine Resort & Spa Phuket receives mostly positive reviews. People who have been here say that the hotel is quite quiet, since it is located at a fairly large distance from the central noisy streets. Higher rooms offer chic panoramic views of the surroundings.

And the attitude towards the guests is a separate issue. On an important event for the guest (birthday, for example), he is congratulated - they bring the cake to the room.

If you need something, you just need to ask. Additional shampoos or gels, fresh towels - the request will be fulfilled immediately.

And tourists are advised to take rooms with sea views. Because otherwise, there is a risk of getting apartments whose windows do not open a picturesque panorama, but the corridor of the building located in the neighborhood. However, all the nuances can be clarified even when drawing up a reservation request.

Tourists assure: being here on vacation, you need to enjoy the atmosphere, comfort and delights of the resort. And the hotel with reasonable prices and decent service will make the pastime even more enjoyable.
