When is it issued?

You may notice that the system in special cases is very incomprehensible and confusing. Therefore, it is worth paying attention only to the standard situation that is understandable to all citizens. An important point is when you can get a deduction for a child. And in what cases it stops.



Accrual is possible immediately after the birth of the baby. This rule applies to parents. In some cases, you can get this benefit in double size. Not always, but there is such a prospect. It is worth noting - a deduction for children is given to each parent. And if one of them refuses this benefit, the second gets the opportunity to double the benefit.

You should not rejoice. After all, there are a number of restrictions that do not allow you to issue a refusal in favor of the other spouse. For example, when someone does not work (is a housewife), or is on maternity / maternity leave. If people are officially married, as a rule, there are no problems with this issue. So, a double deduction can be received by either of the spouses when both work, if the second half refuses this benefit. There is nothing difficult to understand in this.

More problems happen when there is no official marriage.
