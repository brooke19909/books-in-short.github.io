Turkey, Kemer, Hotel "Stella": overview, description and reviews of tourists

For a summer vacation or a long-awaited vacation in Turkey, many tourists are guided by certain criteria. Sea vacations on the Cote d'Azur, the opportunity to visit historical sights or museums, admiring the beauties of local nature or vibrant nightlife - everyone prefers to spend their leisure time in different ways.

Choosing the settlement of Kemer and its environs, travelers receive a virtually Swiss corner on the land of the Turkish state. Here the air is saturated with pine aroma, steep Toros mountains are fascinating and inspiring.

The opportunity to choose the necessary level of comfort at various prices, from fashionable hotels to budget hotels, gives everyone a chance to relax here, regardless of income level. Among other similar four-star hotels can be distinguished hotel complex Stella.





Location

The village in which it is located, Camyuva, is 10 km from Kemer and 65 km from Antalya Airport. The coastal area extends in a wide strip along the coast of the Mediterranean Sea. Clean air, the incense of gardens and the smells of coniferous trees heal the body and give a charge of vivacity, energy and positive for a long time. From here you can see the gentle peaks of the mighty Taurus Mountains. Multiple entertainments within the beach area, restaurants, bars and cafes will certainly please holidaymakers and fill the rest with new unique emotions.

Number of rooms

There are two types of apartments:

  • standard;
  • family apartment.

All rooms of the standard category are one-room with a bathroom. The maximum number of beds is three. The furniture is standard, it includes: beds, a chair, a dressing table, a luggage rack and a spacious wardrobe. These are the economy rooms at the Stella Hotel (Kemer). They can afford tourists with an average income.

Family - two-room, identical in structure. The bathroom is equipped with a sink, toilet, modern shower and hairdryer. The provided set of hygiene products will certainly please guests. The apartments are comfortably decorated with paintings, wall sconces and original curtains. The floors are covered with high quality laminate.

Each room has a balcony, where you can spend a cozy romantic evening or meet the dawn without leaving your room. Air conditioning, cable and satellite TV, a mini fridge allow you to fully enjoy your long-awaited vacation.

Chambers are cleaned daily by professional maids who delight guests with amazing masterpieces of towels, decorating them with sweets. Every three days there is a change of linen.





All Inclusive Concept

The concept of the Turkish All-Inclusive Hotel includes the following advantages: free meals in the restaurant and drinks in the bar. All juices and alcoholic cocktails are included in this system. However, they are free until 22 hours 30 minutes. After already paid extra. The opportunity to play table tennis and darts will appeal to fans of sports games. A Turkish bath, sauna and gym are components of this program and are included in the price.

Features of settlement and accommodation

It is worth noting the amazing friendliness of the staff. The employees are very attentive to their guests, greet them with a smile and are ready to help upon request. A free bottle of drinking water is delivered to your room. Further replenishment of the minibar is not included in the price and is paid extra.

Upon request, Stella staff can deliver food and drinks to the apartments.

Additional service

Imported alcoholic drinks and freshly squeezed orange juice in all hotel establishments are paid extra.

Using the Internet is a paid service. There is luggage storage space. Reception is open 24 hours a day. At the reception you can purchase phone cards. This allows you to make calls in any direction directly from the hotel room or during the tour.

Laundry and dry cleaning services are available at an additional cost. Payment for all paid services can be made by bank transfer using bank cards.

Water activities

There is a large pool, where there is enough space for all guests. Numerous sunbeds with mattresses, umbrellas and tables will turn your vacation into a beautiful fairy tale. A distinctive feature of the pool of this hotel is a high and long slide - an open, winding yellow gutter, where skiing is available for all age categories.

Near the adult water zone is a small children's pool. The youngest guests can spend time here on their own, because they will always be in front of their parents thanks to a well thought out location. Tourists can only enjoy the summer hot rays and surprisingly clean and healthy air, lying on a sun lounger with a free cocktail from the local bar. Such a holiday gives unprecedented emotions and a lot of positive impressions.





Dining

Eating vacationers carry out the buffet system 6 times a day. Varieties of meals: breakfast, brunch, lunch, snack bar, afternoon snack, dinner. For breakfast and dinner, 2 hours are allotted, the remaining meals are carried out within an hour to an hour and a half. The hotel "Stella" (Kemer, Turkey) has:

  • main restaurant;
  • bar;
  • Internet cafe;
  • beach bar.

The main restaurant is located on the ground floor with panoramic windows overlooking the pool. Tables with umbrellas are set in close proximity to the aquazone. Thus, you can enjoy the excellent culinary dishes of local chefs, try the masterpieces of Turkish cuisine without losing sight of the swimming and frolicing kids.

The bar is located near the pool on the opposite side of the housing. Every day, according to the All Inclusive concept, from 10:00 to 22:30, it delights guests with a wide range of alcoholic, non-alcoholic drinks and fresh drinks.

In an Internet cafe, you can combine a light snack with the ability to access the Internet. However, the network connection service is paid extra. Also in the complex there are numerous vending machines with drinks.





Beach area

After a good rest after long hours of travel, there is a desire to plunge into the clear waters of the Mediterranean Sea. Hotel "Stella" (Kemer, Turkey) is located on the first coastline, sand and pebble beach is located within walking distance from it. The shallow and well-warmed sea, combined with gentle descents into the water, made this resort village a place for families.

The private beach is equipped with sun loungers with parasols. Towels are provided free of charge. A bar nearby provides vacationers with all the necessary soft drinks in a wide assortment.

Children's entertainment

Hotel "Stella" (Kemer) receives positive reviews from grateful parents. For children, the administration equipped a playground under the open sky. A slide, a swing and a carousel - all for a fun holiday for small active guests. The restaurant provides free highchairs and a cot in the room.

Daily children's animation pleases travelers with its contests and fun.





Fun for all age groups

It should be noted that an active vacation with many different entertainments is available in the Stella hotel complex. Billiards, darts, table tennis, volleyball for team games will entice adults with their children into the world of sports, strength and health. A equipped gym is available at any time of the day.
Live music in the courtyard makes it possible to enjoy the singing of talented performers.

Using the bicycle rental service, you can go on a tour of the amazing natural beauties located near the hotel.
