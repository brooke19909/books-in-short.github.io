Waterpark in Serpukhov - the best place for a family vacation in the city!

Serpukhov is a regional center in the suburbs, today is a fairly large and diversified city. In recent years, the infrastructure of the village has improved significantly. Parks and recreation areas are being improved, new public and recreational institutions are appearing. Not so long ago, a large water entertainment complex was opened. What is a water park in Serpukhov and is it worth the time to visit it? Especially for you - a detailed description of the complex and useful information about it, as well as feedback from visitors only in our article.

City of Serpukhov: water park - opening of the year



Water pools and slides are a gift to residents and guests of the city from the SU-155 company. On December 14, 2014, the B-Class shopping and entertainment complex hosted the opening of an aqua park and an ice arena. Singer Tatyana Bulanova and the Summer Garden music group performed at the event. The deputy head of the city, Ekaterina Ogneva, also delivered a welcoming speech to the guests of the holiday, and after that the deputy of the Moscow Duma Mikhail Leontyev handed over the trowels to the chairman of the board of directors of SU-155, which laid the first brick of the second building of the shopping center. After the official part of the water park in Serpukhov, guests were pleased with a colorful presentation. However, the entertainment program for the event guests and the New Year's gift fair also stretched for several hours.

Prices and useful information for visitors

The water park is open daily without lunch and weekends from 10.00 to 22.00. An entrance ticket is purchased for two hours and gives the right to use all attractions and pools without restrictions. There are two time rates: daytime and evening. For the first, tickets for adults cost 350 rubles, and for children - 200 rubles, for the second: adults - 450 rubles, children - 300 rubles. On Friday, weekends and holidays are more than 100 rubles for all age categories. The water park in Serpukhov is located at 55 Moskovskoye Shosse, B-Class shopping and entertainment complex (4th floor). In pools, comfortable warm water temperature is maintained year-round. To ensure the safety of visitors, lifeguards always work at the water park.
