These hotels are in good standing with tourists. They have common features and differences.

This four-star hotel is small and quiet. It is located in the second line on the coast of Ras Om El Sid Bay. Its number of rooms is small, it consists of 70 furnished comfortable rooms for guests, the bulk of which are of the Standart type, designed for a maximum of three people. In Tropicana Tivoli Hotel Hotel 4 * there are several "double" rooms such as Duplex, Suite and Apartment, which can accommodate up to 7 tourists.

The hotel rooms are comfortable, they are equipped with a modern bathroom with a shower and have a balcony, equipped with a table and a couple of chairs for relaxation. The hotel restaurant provides guests with half-board meals, consisting of breakfast and dinner. International cuisine is served. At the hotel there is another restaurant, working by order, in which you can try oriental and Egyptian cuisine.
