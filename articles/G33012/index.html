The problem of lack of money is familiar to almost everyone, and the question of how to save money arises in many families, regardless of what the family income is. How familiar this is - it seems that the earnings are considerable, but the money goes like water in the sand! For an ordinary average family, methods of saving also affect such an expense item as food, because in grocery stores and supermarkets more than half of the family budget remains. It would seem, how can you save on food? But to learn healthy nutrition and at the same time save up to 30% of family money per month is quite realistic.

2. Buying semi-finished products is not only unprofitable, but also harmful to health. Often they are prepared in their own workshops from far from fresh products. You can cook semi-finished products for a whole week at home, having allocated a little time for this on a day off. Having stuck dumplings, cutlets, dumplings and freezing them for future use, you can ensure yourself quick cooking on weekdays and not splurge on sausages and instant convenience foods.

3. Saving the family budget can increase significantly if you plan the menu for the week and buy products strictly according to the list. It is easy to find a lot of recipes on the Internet or in magazines, such as, for example, cooking a new dish from the remnants of dinner in the morning, using stale bread, or making a wonderful casserole from boring pasta.

4. Caring about how to save money, you can not help but think about preparations for the winter. When there are a lot of cheap fruits and vegetables on the market, you can make jam, preserve vegetables, make soup dressings for the future - it is good for your health, and beneficial for your wallet. Do not forget about this method of preserving vegetables, berries and fruits, like freezing.

5. Good savings come from bulk purchases of potatoes, cereals, sugar, flour and other products. From wholesale suppliers all this can be bought much cheaper. In addition, the availability of stocks will reduce the number of trips to the store, and hence the likelihood of succumbing to the temptation to buy something extra.

Nevertheless, competitors with higher prices have sales and promotions, using which you can please your budget with good savings.

7. In any store among the goods you can almost always find an alternative with a lower price. Typically, these products are located on the lower shelves, and at eye level, cunning sellers have the most expensive products to attract attention.
