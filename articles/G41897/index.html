Koh Lan Island (Thailand). Beaches of Koh Larn

Thailand is the best place to relax. In this country, there is absolutely everything that a tourist needs for an ideal vacation - these are wonderful white sand beaches and attractions that you must see with your own eyes, because only in this way you can be sure of the charm of this place.

The largest island located near Pattaya

Of course, the most famous resort in the country is Pattaya. The islands (Ko Lan, Sichang) of Thailand, however, are also very popular among vacationers. A huge number of tourists visit them annually.

The island of Koh Lan, or as it is also called Coral Island, is the largest of them. Due to the huge number of reefs located on the coast, it primarily attracts its visitors with its perfectly clear sea, as well as beaches. Literally every 40 minutes, a ferry arrives at this place, which is almost always full of people.

The approximate length of the island is only 4 kilometers, and the highest point in this place is located at an altitude of about 205 meters above sea level. The whole economy here is 100% dependent on earnings from tourists, which, thanks to the close location of Koh Lan to a very popular resort, is quite large.





Things to do on Koh Lan

By the way, there is no public transport familiar to Europeans on the island, this is due to the fact that the distance between the settlements here is scanty. But, despite this, the so-called motorcycle taxis are quite popular in this place. For a tiny fee, they will take you anywhere in the island, including the beaches. They are famous for this place. Here you can spend your time swimming in the clear water and basking under the gentle rays of the sun. Sea activities - this is why tourists primarily visit the island of Koh Lan. The beaches here are always clean, as is the water itself.

What can you do here? Diving, snorkeling, riding on bananas, as well as tablets - this is just a small list of entertainment, so you certainly will not be bored!

Great place to stay

At any time of the year, the island of Koh Lan is beautiful. Thailand is always suitable for recreation, which is why so many people decide to go on vacation just to this place. Here you can have a fun and unforgettable break from a boring life.

Hotel complex Koh Lan Resort

In the south of the island there is a large hotel complex called Koh Lan Resort. This place has all the conditions so that you can spend your vacation exactly as planned. There are always a lot of couples staying with and without children, as well as the newlyweds who have chosen this great place to spend their unforgettable honeymoon. Koh Lan Resort is always cozy and calm; it is an ideal place for those who seek privacy and silence.

In order for you to fully explore the island of Koh Lan, you need to rent a boat or a small boat. So you can enjoy the pictures enchanting the eyes, which nature itself draws in plenty.





Temple with Buddha

On this little trip, you will most likely need a map. The island of Ko Lan, despite its small size, also has its own temple, which is the main attraction of this place. There is quite a large statue of Buddha, which is installed on the highest point of this island. There is also an observation deck with a beautiful panoramic view of the surroundings, so when going on vacation, do not forget to bring a camera with you, it will certainly come in handy.

To summarize

Every tourist needs to visit the island of Koh Lan. Thailand is a paradise vacation, so if you ever will be, for example, in Pattaya, then do not forget to visit this beautiful place at least during the tour, because it is worth your attention!

You will remember this vacation for life, because beautiful, bewitching pictures of nature will not leave anyone indifferent. Transparent sea, blue sky, white sand shimmering in the sun - all this is about Ko Lan, an amazing island of Thailand!
