Messonghi Beach Holiday Resort 3 (Greece / Corfu): description and reviews

If you want to relax on the beach surrounded by landscaped gardens, you should pay attention to Messonghi Beach Holiday Resort 3 *. The modern resort complex will surely satisfy the needs of modern travelers.





Location

This resort complex is located in the eastern part of Corfu. Messonghi Beach Holiday Resort 3 * is surrounded by greenery and is located in close proximity to the Ionian Sea. Given that the resort complex is located between the villages of Messongi and Moraitika, the hotel guests will have plenty of opportunities for tourism and entertainment. Corfu can be reached in just 45 minutes. The road by bus will cost you a little more than 2 euros.

Number of rooms

Comfortable living conditions are the key to a successful holiday. In Messonghi Beach Holiday Resort 3 * you will be offered the following options for accommodation:

  • Beachfront Bungalow - Comfortable rooms completely renovated in 2015. This is a cozy spacious room with a sliding panoramic window that opens onto its own spacious terrace. You can enjoy views of the sea or the mainland.
  • Superior Plus is a category of spacious double rooms, completely renovated in 2016. The design is made in azure-sand tones. There is a large or a pair of twin beds, as well as a place for an extra folding bed. There is a private balcony with a magnificent view of the garden.
  • Superior family room includes accommodation for four guests with the possibility of installing an additional clamshell. There are two layout options. You can stay in a room with four single beds. There is also an option with two twin beds and one bunk.
  • Family Bungalow has two rooms. The first has two twin beds. In the second, in addition to two berths, there is also a bunk bed for children. An additional advantage is the presence of a panoramic terrace overlooking the sea or the hotel gardens.
