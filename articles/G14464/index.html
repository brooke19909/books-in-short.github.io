Globales Palmanova Palace 4 * (Spain / Mallorca): photos and reviews of tourists

Guests of the Globales Palmanova Palace 4 * Resort can enjoy a full rest in sunny Spain . This is a unique combination of quality service, home comfort and incredibly beautiful nature.





a brief description of

An unforgettable vacation promises travelers a resort hotel Globales Palmanova Palace 4 *. Palma Nova is a popular resort, which is famous not only for the gentle sea, but also for its beautiful nature and a lot of attractions. The hotel was built in 1972, and the last reconstruction is dated 1995, and therefore the decor is quite old. The hotel has a small territory of 1600 square meters. m, in which the central place is occupied by an eight-story building, designed for 185 rooms.

A 4-minute walk from the well-maintained municipal beach is the resort hotel Globales Palmanova Palace 4 *. Spain, Palma Nova, 15 Miguel Santos Street - the address where travelers can find this hotel. The main street with many bars and entertainment centers is a 5-minute walk from this hotel. The facility is located 14 km from the city of Palms and 25 km from the international airport.

Resort Rooms

Comfortable living conditions are provided by the guests of the Globales Palmanova Palace 4 * Resort Hotel. Guests can be accommodated in the following categories of apartments:

  • A single standard is provided for those travelers who like to relax on their own. In addition to a single bed, the room also has a desk. For relaxation, a comfortable armchair is installed at the window.
  • The double room is a cozy bedroom with a pair of beds. If necessary, they can be shifted. There is also a sofa in the room, which can be used to relax or accommodate an additional guest. The hallway has a coat rack and a luggage stand. It also has a private balcony.
  • For family accommodation there is a triple room. It has two shifted beds and one separate. To accommodate an extra guest can serve as a folding sofa. It also has a desk and access to a balcony.
