Swans from a napkin for serving a festive table

The festive table requires special serving, but do not litter the space with ridiculous decorations. The best option may be the original composition of napkins. Using the origami technique, you can make any shape out of a napkin. The most common option is the swan.

What napkins can you make a swan

In order for swans from napkins to look relevant and aesthetically pleasing, one must take into account the fact that it is not possible to make a figure out of any format and material. There are many conditions for the implementation of quality work, a lot depends on the type of napkin.

  1. It is worth choosing a large size napkin. In the process of creating a figure, the size of the product will decrease.
  2. Color also matters. It is better to use shades that are as close as possible to the overall table setting.
  3. Cellulose density should have an average value so that the figure does not tear in the hands, but also remains flexible and keeps its shape.

If you take into account all the nuances regarding the choice of basic material, you will get a beautiful and high-quality decoration for serving a festive table.

Swan creation scheme

Having mastered the origami technique, it will become clear how to make a swan from a napkin so that it is as similar to the original as possible. The algorithm for creating the simplest ornaments in the origami technique:

  1. Place the tissue in the standard assembly in front of you. The open side should be in front of the eyes of the performer.
  2. Bend the napkin so that the near corner is superimposed on the opposite.
  3. Return the product to its original position to get a bend along the side corners.
  4. Bend the right and left corners to the central bend.
  5. Turn over the napkin.
  6. Bend the right and left corners on the other side again.
  7. Bend the napkin in half so that the upper corner is pressed down.
  8. Bend a small portion of the corner to form the bird's head.
  9. Spread all parts of the product, sort out open corners.

All bends should be done carefully so that the figure has clear lines and does not fall apart after installation on a plate.

Secrets to making and decorating

To create a quality product, you need to know some secrets:

  1. To make the bird look more presentable, you can decorate the napkin with ribbon, lace. Elements are additionally finished with beads.
  2. To make the figure more voluminous and overall, several napkins can be used simultaneously.
  3. An swan made of origami textile napkin will look expensive.

If you choose all the materials, jewelry correctly, then swans from napkins will turn out elegant and expressive.
