Where to go with children Gelendzhike in the summer and winter?

When you go to the sea to a new place, the question arises as to which places to visit in the first place. It is especially important to consider spending leisure time if you are going on vacation with children. Since children are not interested in everything that adults can offer them. It is better if you discuss your routes with your child in advance so as not to be in an awkward situation when the baby does not share your interests before the start of the tour. Gelendzhik

Gelendzhik meets millions of tourists every year. These are couples in love, families with children, single travelers. They all come here in order to relax, have a good time, escape from the city bustle and bring home a huge amount of impressions and souvenirs.

Every vacationer wants his vacation to be unforgettable. But if you and your family have come to Gelendzhik, where to go with the children is the main question for the rest.

Gelendzhik is a modern tourist city where every vacationer can find something to his liking. But many couples have a question when they come to Gelendzhik, where to go, what to see with the children, so that the whole family is happy and happy with their rest. In addition to the beach, you can go to the water park, dolphinarium, museums and amusement parks. Every day of your stay in this city will be remembered for something special.





Sights of the city and its environs

This city is considered quite large, there are many places where you can go in Gelendzhik with a child. There is a huge concert hall "Olympus", as well as many shady squares and parks with carousels for kids and adults.

The resort is also famous for ancient dolmens, which are located in the valley of the Janet River. Every tourist is simply obliged to look at the beauty of these waterfalls and bring some photos from these places. They can be reached by car or by bus. Also, special excursions go here, which can be ordered on the territory of the hotel in which you live and in other special places. Often such excursions are not limited to one attraction and allow you to visit several places at once in one day, learn a lot of interesting things about the city and its past.

For those who love nature, it is proposed to visit the Dzhanhotsky Bor, where Pizunda pine trees grow, which were even listed in the Red Book of Russia. The incredible landscapes of this area are simply mesmerizing. If the question of where to go with young children in Gelendzhik has not yet disappeared, then the list of attractions can be safely continued.

You definitely need to show children the Parus rock, which is located right on the coast and is unique. Photos on the background of such landscapes will occupy the main place in the family album. And it will be interesting for children to look at such beauty.





Fun for kids

Water Park "Golden Bay"

This is the very place in Gelendzhik where you need to go with the children first. This is a relatively new water park, which was opened in 2004 and to this day remains the largest water park in all of Russia. It works only in the warm season, since it is in the open. Here, both adults and children can find entertainment to their liking. Many pools and sun loungers, various slides and other attractions.

At the entrance you can buy a subscription for the whole day. The price will vary depending on how tall the visitor is. Children with height up to 106 centimeters admission is free.

Getting here is quite simple, as the water park is only five kilometers from the city center. Shuttle taxis and a special free express go here.

All attractions here are distributed by height and age. The developers provided for everything and took care of children of all age categories. Among other things, you can play beach volleyball at the water park, visit the arboretum or cafe.





Gelendzhik Dolmens

Dolmens are such stone structures of various shapes. Their main feature is the hole. Historians claim that they appeared about five thousand years ago. But until today, no one has been able to give an answer, why did the ancient people need them. Someone suggested that these are buildings of aliens, and someone claims that ancient priests were buried here. Not a single version is officially confirmed, so we can only guess about their origin and purpose. This is the place in Gelendzhik where you can go with the children, but if they are already older and are interested in all sorts of myths and legends.

You can get to them by your own transport - it is 16 kilometers from Gelendzhik in the village of Renaissance. Also some buses go this way. But the best option for tourists is considered a tour. You will be brought to a place, they will tell you a little about this place, you can also visit several more places and learn a lot of interesting things from the guide.

Old park in Kabardinka

If you have not yet figured out where to go with children on holiday in Gelendzhik, then try to offer them a visit to the old park in Kabardinka. It became the first architectural park in this territory. This is a huge territory that accommodates many architectural buildings from different eras and times.

Even now, new architectural structures are still being built in the park, so it will be interesting to visit this place even for those who have been here before.

The prices here are quite reasonable: entrance for an adult costs 500 rubles, for a child over 6 years old - 200 rubles.

It is located not far, but not quite close. Sixteen kilometers from the city. If you drive your car, then spend about 30 minutes on the road. But you can save your own gas and get to the park by public transport.





Safari Park

If you have just arrived in Gelendzhik, where you still have not decided what to see, what to see with your children, then feel free to go to Safari Park. One of the most popular places in the resort. Here, as already mentioned, live animals who came here for rehabilitation. In the park you can see a bear, tiger, leopard, lion, puma and many other animals and birds. Here, animals do not sit in cages, but are in their natural habitat both in winter and in summer.

But this park is not only attractive to animals. It is divided into several sites, each of them is a must visit. Here is the Maritime Museum, a visit to which will be a very informative pastime not only for children but also for adults. You can also go to the terrarium and visit the arboretum. Every year, more and more places are opened here for the entertainment of tourists. Almost all of this is included in the ticket price.

For lovers of extreme sports there is a cable car that in 20 minutes will lift you to an altitude of 640 meters above sea level. It is from this height that you can admire the panorama of the Gelendzhik Bay.

Tunnel Aquarium

Another interesting place in Gelendzhik where you need to go with your children is the tunnel aquarium. Children really enjoy visiting such places. Here they will see the most diverse inhabitants of the underwater world: a small shark, small rays, clown fish and many, many others. A unique feature of this place is that the aquarium is located on the walls and on the ceiling. At the time of the tour it seems that you are walking along the seabed.

On the second floor of the aquarium is a small zoo with various small animals. Some of them can even be picked up and photographed. Children really like this kind of entertainment and contact with little fluffies.

Dolphinarium

Another favorite place for children. Some tourists come to rest outside the swimming season, and the question arises of where to go with children in the winter in Gelendzhik. That dolphinarium will be a great place for winter entertainment.

Dolphinarium began its work in 1992. During this time, the composition of the artists included not only dolphins, but also fur seals, lions and walruses. They all get along well with the coach and year round delight their visitors with wonderful performances.

After the performance, it is allowed to take a few photos with the artists on your own camera and even swim with dolphins in the pool. It costs a lot, but a child and an adult take tremendous pleasure from it. As you know, dolphins can even treat some of the worst diseases.

The dolphinarium is located in the very center of the city, you can get to it from anywhere in Gelendzhik.

House Museum of Vladimir Korolenko

In the small village of Dzhanhot there is a house-museum of the famous Russian writer Vladimir Korolenko. The village is located about 20 kilometers from the city itself. The writer himself created the project of this estate. The family loved to spend time here. Almost all buildings that were built by Vladimir Korolenko and his brother have survived to this day. All walls, objects, furniture are saturated with the atmosphere that reigned in this family.

Hippo Water Park"

Another water park in Gelendzhik. In area, it is not as large as the Golden Bay, but it is not inferior in types of entertainment. There are more than fifteen slides of different heights and three large pools. There is also a relaxation area for adults, where they can sunbathe on comfortable sun loungers, and children can spend leisure time on a dry playground. Also in the water park there is a cafe and a pizzeria. Getting to the Hippo water park is very easy, because it is located on one of the central streets of the city.





Waterpark "Dolphin"

The oldest water park in Gelendzhik. Here, as in the previous two, there are many slides and entertainment for adults and children. Finding a water park "Dolphin" is very simple: it is located right on the central beach. Here the most reasonable prices, this is the place most often visited by those tourists who have come on a budget holiday, but want to get a charge of positive emotions. The territory of the water park has everything that a modern tourist may need.





Maritime Museum in Safari Park

A visit to this place will be an interesting pastime for both adults and children. Here are more than two thousand exhibits that were raised from the bottom of the sea. These exhibits include more than 600 coins that were collected from the seabed, as well as the remains of various underwater inhabitants. Some species have long ceased to exist.
