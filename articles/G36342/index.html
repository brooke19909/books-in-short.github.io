Hotel Royal Thalassa Monastir 5 * (Tunisia, Monastir): overview, description and reviews

Tunisia is one of the most sought after holiday destinations. It offers travelers a multitude of hotels for both first-class and budget holidays. Popularity was brought to him by snow-white sandy beaches, as well as the rich history of the country. Fans of luxurious relaxation will appreciate the hotel Royal Thalassa Monastir 5 * (Tunisia, Monastir ).

Basic information about the complex and its location

The hotel was built in 1999. It is located on the wide beach of Monastir . The complex consists of one building, built in a modern classic style. The three-story building has 280 comfortable rooms designed for several people. The last redecoration of all hotel rooms was carried out in 2008. The thalassotherapy center brought popularity to the hotel. People come here to heal, improve the condition of the skin of the face and body. You can come here with children of any age. And pets are strictly prohibited. People with disabilities are better off choosing a different hotel. This complex is not equipped with ramps and other amenities necessary for a comfortable stay.



Hotel Royal Thalassa Monastir 5 * (Tunisia) has an advantageous location. It is just 2 km from the local airport. The resort can be reached in just ten minutes. Monastir city center can be reached by taxi. It is approximately 6 km from the hotel. Distance to the sea is approximately 100 meters. To reach it, tourists must cross a small garden.

Hotel Rooms

The hotel has 280 rooms. Among them there are standard rooms designed for budget accommodation, and spacious apartments for a chic vacation. Some rooms have balconies. All rooms are cleaned daily by maids, who also regularly change linens . The windows overlook either the sea or the pool. Non-smoking apartments are available.





Royal Thalassa Monastir 5 * complex (" Royal Thalassa Monastir ") offers the following types of rooms:

  • Standard (28 sq. M.). Suitable for two adults and one baby. Most of the rooms are located on the ground floor. There are apartments with sea views. The floor is tiled.
  • Junior Suite Thalassa (90 sq. M.). It consists of a bedroom with a living area. Guests have access to a private massage room, as well as a separate Turkish bath located in the bathroom. Laminate flooring. The room is designed for two adults.
  • Junior Suite Thalassa with Jacuzzi (90 sq. M.). Own hot tub on the balcony is the main difference between this room and the previous one.
  • Junior Suite Royal Thalassa (150 sq. M.). Huge apartments designed for six people. The room consists of three bedrooms, a living area, a bathroom. They have a private pool.

Room features

Each room of the Royal Thalassa Monastir 5 * hotel (Tunisia, Skanes ) is equipped not only with furniture, but also with equipment that is necessary for a first-class stay. Tourists who check into any apartment can evaluate the quality of the services offered using the available equipment. It:

  • A central air conditioner that makes it easier to tolerate hot and humid weather. Regulated by hotel staff. It usually works from June to September.
  • A safe for storing valuables. Free for guests.
  • Flat-screen TV with the ability to view channels in Russian.
  • Internet is wireless. Hotel guests can use it for free.
  • A telephone with which you can call friends at home for a fee or contact the hotel staff. Room service is also available.
  • Toiletries in the bathroom. The set includes soap, shampoo and shower gel. They are replenished as needed. Bathrobes, slippers, a hairdryer and towels are provided free of charge.
  • A minibar is delivered to your room upon request. Its contents are not included in the price. Some rooms also have a small fridge.
  • A set of dishes for making tea or coffee.

What services does the hotel offer?

For travelers who came to rest in Tunisia, the Royal Thalassa Monastir 5 * hotel has prepared a lot of services that they may need during their holidays. The following infrastructure facilities are offered to guests:

  • Three conference rooms. The largest of them accommodates more than 400 people. All of them are equipped with modern computer technology.
  • Therapist's office. Each reception is paid separately.
  • 24 hour ATM. With it, tourists can cash out their money at any time . A currency exchange office also operates.
  • Free car parking. Car hire is available for a fee.
  • Mini market , souvenir shop and pharmacy. Here, hotel guests can buy inexpensive drugs, food, tobacco, alcohol and souvenirs.
  • The beauty salon offers guests to undergo a complex of wellness treatments for the face, make-up, manicure or a fashionable haircut.
  • The common lounge is equipped with soft sofas and TVs.
  • Internet corner for tourists wishing to use the high-speed connection.




Catering

Hotel Royal Thalassa Monastir 5 * (Tunisia) offers its guests to choose their own food system when issuing vouchers. By paying only for breakfast, tourists can fully eat in the morning. The price will also include soft drinks. With half board, they can also dine in the restaurant for free. The all-inclusive system is a universal option for tourists who spend most of their time on site. The price includes 3 meals, as well as local soft drinks and spirits (draft beer, liquor, wine, vodka, whiskey and cocktails). The system operates daily from 10:00 to midnight.





All hotel guests can visit the following catering facilities:

  • Restaurant Chef's Market. Here are the main meals. A buffet is served for visitors. There is an outdoor terrace.
  • Restaurant Mona. It accommodates about 100 people. Guests are served √† la carte. For owners of the all-inclusive system, one free visit per week is provided.
  • Restaurant La voile. Located on the beach. It offers grilled dishes.
  • Pool Bar.
  • Moorish cafe.
  • Beach bar. It only works in the summer.

Entertainment program for tourists

A well-thought-out leisure program can be offered to guests by the Royal Thalassa Monastir 5 * hotel. Tunisia is also famous for its water activities. Most of the entertainment facilities are free for guests, but some of them are nevertheless provided only at an additional cost. We list the main types of leisure in the hotel:

  • Fitness center . On three floors of the building there are rooms for yoga and aerobics. There is a spacious gym. His visit is paid.
  • Water entertainment center located on the beach. Tourists can rent a catamaran, canoe, as well as go water skiing, windsurfing and diving.
  • Equipment for playing classic and table tennis, beach volleyball, basketball and handball. There are several golf courses nearby.
  • Tour agency. A Russian-speaking guide will help you choose a sightseeing tour, for example, to the Sahara or to the monuments of ancient Carthage.
  • Entertaining animation. It works day and night. Sports events, theme nights with live music, contests and discos are organized.
  • The largest thalassotherapy center in the country. It offers treatment with sea water and healing mud. You can also visit the sauna, Turkish bath, massage room, jacuzzi. For guests of Royal Thalassa Monastir 5 * (Tunisia) special wellness programs lasting 4, 5 and 9 days have been developed.
  • Outdoor pool with whirlpool. Its depth is 2 meters. Free umbrellas, sunbeds and mattresses are provided.
  • Indoor saltwater pool. It works in the winter season. The water in it is constantly heated.




What can a hotel offer children?

The hotel is in a quiet location, which is great for families with children. For the youngest guests, the complex has prepared the following amenities:

  • cot, which is available on request in the room for free;
  • high chairs for feeding in all restaurants of the hotel;
  • shallow pool (60 cm) with fresh water;
  • playroom;
  • mini-club for children over four years old;
  • round-the-clock services of a qualified nanny.

Opinions about the advantages of the hotel

Hotel Royal Thalassa Monastir 5 * (Tunisia), reviews of which are most often positive, is a popular place to stay. According to the guests, the complex has the following advantages:

  • Very spacious rooms. The bathroom is huge, and plumbing is working properly. There is always hot water.
  • The room is cleaned daily, and for small tips, beautiful figures made of bed linen leave on the bed.
  • 4 towels are provided per person.
  • The food is tasty and varied. They serve several types of meat and fish every day, and everything is prepared according to local recipes.
  • Warm sea with a shallow entrance. Perfect for children and those who swim poorly.
  • The spa provides a magnificent massage, for which I want to return here again.




Hotel Opinions

Not only the advantages describes any average review of Tunisia ( Monastir ). Royal Thalassa Monastir receives comments from tourists. They describe the following disadvantages:

  • There are a lot of mosquitoes and flies, so insect repellent is indispensable here.
  • Maids sometimes forget to bring water to their rooms in the morning.
  • Cosmetic products give low-quality. If they run out, then you will not be given new ones even after a request.
  • In autumn, a lot of jellyfish are found in the sea, which greatly burn the skin.
  • The hotel staff is not friendly, although not rude. Tourists from Russia are dismissive.




Should I relax in this complex?

This hotel is well suited for a relaxing holiday. Reviews of Royal Thalassa Monastir 5 * ( Monastir , Tunisia) suggest that this place will be a great option for families with children, the elderly and businessmen. In addition, the price of rest here is consistent with the level of service.
