Holiday Village "Mustang" (Dnepropetrovsk): description, prices, reviews

The recreation center "Mustang" in Dnepropetrovsk is a place where you can have a great time with your family or friends. The boarding house is open at any time of the year. But the greatest influx of vacationers is observed in the summer.

Basic information

The Mustang Recreation Complex began to function just a few years ago. Original wooden cottages harmoniously look in a pine forest. Everyone can spend a weekend or vacation in an ecologically clean area. It can be easily reached with your own transport. The recreation center is located in the village of Kirovskoye, 15 kilometers from Dnepropetrovsk. The exact address is Orelskaya street, house 3. In the summer, a bus also runs here.

The territory of the complex exceeds 1.3 hectares. For the most part it is a pine forest. The territory is beautifully landscaped, the complex is fenced. It is possible to leave your vehicle in a round-the-clock guarded parking lot.

The recreation center "Mustang" in Dnepropetrovsk is a wonderful place for various events. On the territory there is a wonderful restaurant where guests can be offered almost any European cuisine. There is also a summer wooden platform with 70 seats. Weddings, birthdays are often celebrated here, corporate events are held.

Rooms and prices

Offers comfortable rooms of various categories recreation center "Mustang" in Dnepropetrovsk. Prices are appropriate. The most popular are the double standard rooms in the eco-hotel. The room has a double bed, internet, TV, fridge. For a daily stay here you have to pay 1000 rubles. A little more expensive the same number will cost in a wooden cottage. Price per day - 1100 rubles. Those who are used to relaxing in comfort can rent a double deluxe room. If desired, you can install an extra bed. This option is perfect for families with children. The cost of daily living is 1800 rubles.

It is possible to rent a separate cottage. It will be a wooden two-room house. It can accommodate four people at a time. It is possible to additionally establish a few more beds. The house has a bathroom, shower, internet, TV in each room, refrigerator. The cost of daily rent is 4000 rubles. A two-level four-bed cottage will cost a little cheaper - 3500 rubles.

Additional services

The recreation center "Mustang" in Dnepropetrovsk is a place where everything is thought out to the smallest detail. Everyone can find an interesting lesson for themselves. It will be useful to spend time on the tennis court or in the gym. There is also a real Russian bath. A lot of good reviews can be heard about the equestrian club. An exciting horse ride in the spring forest will not leave anyone indifferent. If you wish, you can use the help of an instructor.

The administration of the Mustang recreation center in Dnipropetrovsk takes care of the younger generation. On the territory there is an excellent playground, characterized by increased security. Children can have fun with peers on a swing or roller coaster.

Those who plan to spend time in the Mustang boarding house will not have to worry about food. Real kitchen professionals work in the base kitchen. Vacationers can be offered dishes of European, Russian and Ukrainian cuisine. Breakfast is included.

Reviews about the Mustang complex

You can learn a lot of information about the recreation center from the numerous thematic forums in Dnepropetrovsk. Almost all locals at least once in their life rested here. Attracts a boarding house and tourists from other cities. Clean pine air, excellent service, friendly staff - all this can not but rejoice.

A lot of good reviews can be heard about the equestrian club of the complex. The greatest enthusiasm for horseback riding is among the smallest vacationers. Reviews show that for such fun it is better to choose cool spring or autumn weather. Then the horses behave more calmly.

The complex pleases visitors with a large number of additional services. Secure parking, private bath, billiards - all this has a wonderful rest. For a few days you can forget about workdays and completely immerse yourself in positive emotions.
