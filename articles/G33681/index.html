Dessole Hermes Hotel 4 * (ex. Hermes Hotel), Greece, about. Crete - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Crete is perhaps the most wonderful resort in Greece. On this wonderful island, there really is everything: parks of incredible beauty, and deep lakes, and beaches with white sand under green palm trees, and as many as three seas (to choose from), and many attractions, and the country's best entertainment complexes. And there are many excellent hotels that provide ideal conditions for relaxation. Dessole Hermes Hotel, whose photo is given below, is considered one of the best. Here guests are always welcome and always strive to do everything to make them satisfied.



Location, how to get there

Dessole Hermes Hotel 4 stars is located almost in the center of the most popular resort of Agios Nikolaos on the coast of the fantastic beauty of Mirabello Bay. It is approximately 63 km from the airport and seaport in Heraklion, 36 from the city of Ierapetra, 73 from Sitia. In Agios Nikolaos there is also a large seaport, as well as a relatively large intercity bus station. You can get to Crete by plane from almost any European country, and by boat - from many cities and islands of Greece and Europe. The road network on the island is remarkably developed, and the roads themselves are in excellent condition. You can easily get from the airport to Agios Nikolaos by bus, for which you need to get to the bus station (from 6-00 in the morning until 1-00 at night there is a regular bus number 1). If you wish, and 50-60 euros, you can take a taxi. There is another way to get to the Dessole Hermes Hotel. To do this, you need to rent a car right at the airport, and hand it in when you depart from the island to your homeland.

Description

Dessole Hermes Hotel 4 stars were built in 1972, but in 2009 they were completely renovated. The hotel area is small but very beautiful. Near the entrance there is a free car park. The hotel itself consists of two buildings: one 4-storey, the other - 5. The pool is located on the roof of one of the buildings. Around the reservoir there are sun loungers, umbrellas, and a snack bar. Guests can use the hotel's high-speed wireless internet throughout the hotel. The reception is open around the clock, there is a Russian-speaking staff. Here you can order tickets for the ship, plane and excursions, use postal services, and hand over laundry. Nearby there is a left-luggage office, a currency exchange office, a jewelry store, a cozy TV lounge, and a lobby bar. In addition, the Dessole Hermes Hotel has a spa, a sauna, a gym, a massage room, a place for playing table tennis, and for business meetings there are three conference rooms equipped with modern technology.

Leisure

It is very difficult to get bored while relaxing at the Dessole Hermes Hotel. Crete is such an interesting island that, being here even for a long time, it is impossible to visit all its interesting, noteworthy places. In this case, tourists can only "sympathize" - where they can find time on beaches with a magnificent sea, and on sightseeing, and evening entertainment, and shopping.

The hotel is located a couple of minutes walk from the city center with its finest nightclubs, discos, bars and taverns in Greece, where Greek artists perform every night. Within walking distance from the hotel is a free beach with emerald perfectly clear water. The shore here is sand and small pebbles, it is easy to enter the sea. A little further there is a sandy beach. Umbrellas and sunbeds everywhere are paid, the price depends on the season. On average, it is 2.5 euros for renting a sunbed and 2 euros for an umbrella. In general, the coastline of Crete stretches for 1046 meters, and most of it is occupied by well-equipped beaches. There are even nudist ones among them. In order for people not to be bored, numerous beaches provide numerous recreational facilities in the form of jet skis, "bananas", catamarans, there are diving and surf clubs, a water park. In their free time from bathing and sunbathing, tourists can visit a lot of excursions, since these tours are sold both in the hotel and in many offices of the city. The most popular cave is where Zeus grew up, the labyrinth of the Minotaur, archaeological sites in Malia, Gortina and other villages, the Samaria Gorge, Rethymnon with its magnificent Venetian castle, Spinalonga island and dozens of others. When is the best time to travel to Crete?

Very good 4 star Crete hotels. According to numerous reviews of tourists, it provides a chic vacation with royal food, impeccable service and talented animation. However, if there is no weather during the holidays, even the best hotel will not save the mood. When is the best time to go to Crete? Winters here are warm but damp; summers are dry and hot. It starts to rain from the end of September, but the maximum rainfall occurs in the winter months and March. In April and approximately until May 15, short showers are also possible. The spring in Crete is fabulously good. There are few tourists, prices are low. And there is such beauty around! Wildflowers bloom, the slopes of the mountains cover with a bright carpet. The air temperature is + 16- + 19, and in shallow water the sea warms up to +17. There are no powerful northerly winds yet, so it is not cold to get out of the water. In June, the thermometer rises already to + 20- + 23, the sea is calm, it is clear in the sky. In July it gets hot, the water warms up to +22, the north winds begin to blow, bringing coolness and waves. August is similar to July with the difference that the air is even hotter and the water is warmer. September is a wonderful month for a good rest. Unpleasant northerly winds become milder and gradually change direction to southerly. During this period, rains are not excluded, but the air temperature is kept at + 28- + 29. October is also still warm and sunny. And although there are almost no deciduous trees in Crete, the aromas of autumn still float in the air. But you can still swim, as the water perfectly holds the heat accumulated over the summer, and the winds are very small. Even in November in Crete you can relax. True, it starts raining every week, but there are still many sunny days, and the mercury column stays firmly at + 17- + 18. In winter, it is good to celebrate Christmas (December 25) and New Year, as well as get acquainted with the sights. The weather at this time is very damp and windy, many clubs and restaurants are closed, but there are also those that work all year round.

Greece, Crete, hotels, prices

In Crete, at any time of the year there are hotels ready to provide guests with a decent vacation. For example, Palazzino Di Corina 4 stars, located in the center of the wonderful town of Rethymnon. Prices in Crete during the off-season in the "fours" are approximately the same and amount to 50-60 euros per day, and in the "three rubles" - 35-40 euros. During the season, rates rise to 60-70 euros per day without meals and 80 euros with a continental breakfast, as in the Dessole Hermes Hotel.

Greece is a country where all kinds of miracles are possible. An example of this is the Arhontiko Eugenia Hotel, located in Chania. Its design is something fabulously beautiful, created according to the individual design of the hostess Eugenia. Each room has a chic decor, four-poster beds, a bathroom and a veranda, a small kitchen with utensils. The sea, town shops, bars, taverns and restaurants are within walking distance, which is very convenient. But the most unusual thing about this hotel is that there are no fixed prices, you can bargain with the hostess! As you can see, in Greece, not only everything is there, but everything is possible.
