Rest in Ukraine: Blue Lakes (Dneprodzerzhinsk)

In the Dnipropetrovsk region, not far from the city of Dneprodzerzhinsk, the village of Yelizetovka is located. Here, right next to the village, there is an amazing lake with the beautiful name Blue. By age, it is very young, educated about seventy years ago. Initially, sand was taken here for construction work that was underway in the city at that time, landscaping microdistricts along the left bank of Dneprodzerzhinsk.

As a result, keys were dug in dug pits, gradually filling them. So formed a fairly vast pit with clear water. Its depth reaches 20 meters with a conical bottom. The total area is about 200 thousand square meters. m. Excellent drainage of the sandy bottom, lack of wastewater from the Dnieper, make the water in such a reservoir as the Blue Lakes (Dneprodzerzhinsk) surprisingly transparent, clean. The great value of the object is that it is fresh and can be used for drinking, which is important.



Briefly about the lake

There are many legends about the Blue Lake, it is so unique. Healing clay with healing properties, pure fresh water, is so delicious that those who have been on vacation, breathed in the heady air and drank water, are impressed for a long time.

Blue Lakes (Dneprodzerzhinsk) predominantly have a very interesting bottom structure. Clay, which in its composition is healing, gives a pleasant color to water. On the perimeter, a pond is surrounded on one side by a centuries-old, indescribably beautiful, pine forest, on the other - deciduous. Reeds grow along the shores, due to the dense thickets of which it is not always possible to get to the water.

Relaxation

Healing water, heady pine air attract tourists at any time of the year. A favorite vacation spot for residents of all settlements, they even go from Dnepropetrovsk. They get to the Blue Lakes (Dneprodzerzhinsk) by car, along a dirt road. A large area around the lakes was leased. Rest became comfortable, convenient, but paid. Entrance by car costs 120 rubles, or 30 hryvnia. If you wish, you can book a table with barbecue, a day this service will cost 50 hryvnia (200 rubles). A catamaran ride costs at least 160 rubles. per hour (40 UAH). There are cafes on the coast where delicious food, but everything is very expensive, like in resorts. There is a beach with water slides, there is a rental of umbrellas from the scorching sun, there are comfortable sunbeds.

On the territory of such a reservoir as the Blue Lakes (a vacation here is notable), it is very clean, well-groomed. Beautiful bases at the service of vacationers are Zaimka and Villa Materik. The rooms are cozy, clean, comfortable. The cost of living varies from 600 to 800 rubles per day, or (150-200 UAH).

Part of the coast without any civilization was mastered by lovers of recreation in tents, savages. Eating at the stake, swimming in the purest water, heady, pine air - what could be better?

Vegetable world

The open shores are densely covered with vegetation. The most amazing plant - reed, represents a family of cereals. The main role of these thickets in the regulation of ecological balance. Reed, growing in marshes, dries them over time. Young tender shoots are edible. They taste like asparagus.
Near the coast, on the surface of the lake, duckweed grows. Such a reservoir as the Blue Lakes (Dneprodzerzhinsk), the rest on which will amaze anyone, only benefits from this kind of vegetation. Thanks to her, he looks more elegant. Here you can also find a beautiful plant called water pine (small three-lobed). Nevertheless, the main phytomass is mosses along the entire coastline of the lake.



How to get there

Getting to the Blue Lake is quite simple: by car from Dneprodzerzhinsk, turn left after the unfinished building. If you go from the direction of Dnepropetrovsk, you need to turn right. Next - on the map. Blue Lakes (Dneprodzerzhinsk) knows Google, so you can also find the way with it.
