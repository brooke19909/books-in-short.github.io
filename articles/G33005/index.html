How to change the country in the App Store: tips, tricks, instructions

The App Store is a convenient application for downloading programs and games to apple gadgets. This utility has a variety of settings. Sometimes games and applications become unavailable for download in a particular country. A similar scenario makes you think about how to change your country in the App Store. What does that require? And is it even possible to change the available data in the application under study?

Reality or fairy tale

Can I somehow change the country in the App Store? On an iPhone or any other device from Apple - this is not so important. The main thing is that all these devices work under the control of the same operating system, in which certain rules and principles are observed.



You can change contact information on the App Store. But for this you have to try pretty hard. It is not recommended to just change the country, because the reverse process may require considerable effort. The company Apple is trying to provide complete safety data. Therefore, some operations associated with changing the location require special attention.

Ways to change information

How to change the country of the profile on the App Store? There are several scenarios. How to act? It all depends on the user's preferences.

A person can edit data from the App Store in the following ways:

  • via a mobile device;
  • using iTunes.

There is no significant difference regarding how to change the country. We will look at both ways to bring ideas to life.

What is required

How to change the country in the App Store? To do this, a person needs to prepare in advance. Otherwise, the implementation of the idea will cause a lot of trouble. In some cases, changing the country will fail.



So, to edit the data in "Epp Store", the following components are needed:

  • Apple phone / tablet
  • a computer;
  • Internet connection
  • data from a bank card.

Nothing more is needed. If you have all of the above items, you can easily edit the contact details in your Apple ID profile.

From a mobile device

Now we can begin decisive action. Let's start with the simplest option - changing information using a mobile device. How to change the country in the App Store?



In order to bring the idea to life, it is necessary:

  1. Turn on the device. Go to the main menu of the gadget, open the "Settings".
  2. In the list that appears, click on the line "iTunes and the App Store."
  3. Click on the item responsible for the Apple ID.
  4. Select the option "View AppleID."
  5. Find and click on the "Country" setting.
  6. Click on "Change region / country".
  7. Select the required data and then confirm the changes. To do this, click on the "Terms and Conditions".
  8. Agree to the terms. You need to click on "Accept".
  9. Indicate data from a credit card. Skipping this step will fail.

That's all! Now it‚Äôs clear how to change the country in the App Store. After the proposed actions, the data in the Apple ID will change.

Via iTunes

This is only one of the scenarios. The fact is that changing the data from the Apple profile is possible through iTunes. A similar arrangement takes place, but it is not in special demand among users.

What do you need to work with iTunes? How to change the country of the account in the App Store in this way? To do this, you must follow the following algorithm of actions:

  1. Install and run on iTunes computer. The software is preferably the latest version.
  2. Log in to your Apple ID profile.
  3. Click on the icon with the username. Select the Account Information option.
  4. Click on "Change Country / Region".
  5. Set the appropriate parameter and click on the "Change" button in the right corner of the window.
  6. Agree to the terms and conditions.

Done! Now it‚Äôs clear how to change the country in one case or another in the App Store. In fact, everything is not as difficult as it seems. Even a novice user will be able to bring the idea to life.





Features of changing parameters

What else should everyone know about the process under study? The fact is that AppleID data is far from always being edited. In certain circumstances, adjustments to the profile are not possible.

To avoid this, you will need to follow a few simple conditions. Namely:

  1. All subscriptions in the App Store must be disabled. This is a required item, without which editing is not possible.
  2. Money in the application account must be spent.
  3. Is the content rented? It is necessary to wait until the end of this process. While you can rent software, you cannot make changes to the Apple ID.

Perhaps this is all. When the above conditions are met, a person will be able to change the region or country in the App Store.

Without card

But what if the user initially created a free account? That is, without billing information. How then to make changes to the profile?

This cannot be done today. Change the country in the App Store without a map will fail. If you wish, after saving new data, it is allowed to get rid of payment information. But all AppleID profile adjustments require data from a credit card.

Secrets to Changing Data

But there is one small trick that can help in the implementation of the task. How to change the country in the App Store? To do this without a credit card or if the methods listed above did not help.



To quickly change the country in the App Store, you can use the creation of a new Apple ID profile. This is the only way to realize the idea if a person initially creates a free account and does not have a bank card.
