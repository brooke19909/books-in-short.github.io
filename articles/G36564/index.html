How to make a Shambhala bracelet with your own hands and get an original jewelry?

The Shambhala bracelet is a jewelry popular among people of all ages, but young people especially love it. You can buy a little thing in a store. To always stay on the crest of the wave and adhere to fashion trends, it is not necessary to purchase a finished product. You can get acquainted with the technique, examples of how to make a Shambhala bracelet with your own hands, and create your own, unique amulet.





There is nothing complicated in weaving, as the macrame technique is used here. A bracelet will require 3 meters of a waxed cord, scissors, nine large round beads (they can be metal, similar to crystals made from natural stones or ceramics), nail polish. To make the decoration smooth and beautiful, you need a weaving board. Having become acquainted with how to make a Shambhala bracelet with your own hands, you will see that this is a very fascinating and uncomplicated task. And you will surely be pleased with the result.





First, you need to fix the nail on the board, tie a cord to it, tighten the knot. Then, on the resulting base of the jewelry, beads should be strung in a planned manner and the second end of the thread should be fixed on another nail. Now you need another cord two and a half meters long. It must be tied to the base, stepping back two centimeters from the end, tighten the knot and begin weaving. If you are familiar with macram√©, then the following steps of the instruction on how to make a Shambhala bracelet with your own hands will not be difficult. The basis of the technique is the creation of nodes. They are made as follows: one end of the cord tied across the cord must be brought into the base and left on top of the right. That, in turn, hold over a vertically stretched thread and start for the left segment. Tighten the ends, you get a knot. Then continue to weave them, but each time you should start with the thread that is higher. When twenty knots are obtained, a series of beads comes, they must be braided in the same way, three nodes should be created between each decorative element.





Over time, you will see that there are many ways to make a Shambhala bracelet with your own hands, and all of them are quite affordable. At the end of creating any model, we need to weave fifteen knots and remove the work from the studs. Then on both sides it is necessary to tie the threads in the usual way and grease their ends with nail polish. To create a free clasp, the bracelet must be closed in a circle and braided at its ends with a cord, which performed the main work. The remaining free threads can be decorated with beads of a smaller diameter, and their ends treated with varnish or glue.

For beginners, a double Shambhala bracelet may seem complicated. But it is made almost the same as single. The main difference is that for weaving, a longer vertical thread is required and on the basis of 2 knots are created side by side. They form a double row, which includes beads. Decorative elements should be placed vertically. Before creating jewelry, you should consider how the scheme of the Shambhala bracelet suggests their location. Each bead is tied with two knots.
