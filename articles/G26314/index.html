Hotel Laxmi Morjim Resort (Morjim, India, Goa): overview, description, characteristics and reviews of tourists

Hotel Laxmi Morjim Resort (Morjim, India) offers you a budget vacation option in a real tropical paradise called Goa.





Hotel Description

Hotel Laxmi Morjim Resort (Morjim, India) is a relatively small institution that offers guests comfortable accommodation at a relatively low price. It is here that you can plunge into the features of local culture. In addition, you are given a unique opportunity to experience the life of Indians from within.

Although the apartments at Laxmi Morjim Resort (Morjim, India) are modest, they nevertheless provide a sufficient level of comfort so that tourists can relax between intense excursions and study tours. It has all the necessary furniture, as well as other amenities that no modern traveler can do without.

Despite the fact that the hotel Laxmi Morjim Resort (Morjim, India) has only 2 stars, there is a rather rich resort infrastructure. You have the opportunity to go on a tour or rent a car to independently see all the local attractions. You can also use the parking lot, hand over your clothes to the laundry, etc. The highlight is the restaurant, where you can fully appreciate all the bright shades of the taste of the national Indian cuisine, as well as try popular dishes from other nations of the world.





Hotel location

Hotel Morjim Laxmi Resort 2 * is located on Goa in the popular tourist village of Morjim. To swim in the gentle waves of the ocean, you have to overcome the whole 4 km that separate Ashvem beach from the hotel. A kilometer from the hotel is the famous Morjim Temple, which also needs to be visited by all tourists vacationing here.

Speaking about the location of the hotel Morjim Laxmi Resort 2 *, it is worth noting the good transport interchange in the area. So, just 17 km from the hotel is the Tivima bus station, 25 km - Mapusa railway station, but the distance to Goa International Airport is already 55 km.

Hotel Rooms

All apartments at Laxmi Palace Resort Morjim are classified as standard. They are quite spacious and can accommodate up to three people, 2 of which will be placed on a large double bed (or a pair of single beds), and an additional guest on a folding bed. A pleasant moment can be considered that each room has access to its own balcony. It is not only possible to dry bathing suits and towels on it, but also admire the views of the garden or the street, sitting on wicker chairs with soft upholstery.





Hotel Room Features

Since the resort Laxmi Palace Resort Morjim 2 * has a low status, the equipment of its rooms is also quite modest. Nevertheless, everything necessary to ensure the minimum necessary level of comfort for guests is available in the apartments. So, in each issue there is:

  • a fan that will help at least minimally cope with the hot Indian climate;
  • there is a small TV, which, despite its disrepair, broadcasts cable channels in fairly good quality, among which there are Russian-speaking;
  • the furniture provides for a large bed or a pair of single, bedside tables, as well as a wardrobe with hangers for your belongings;
  • each room has a small kitchenette with a small fridge, an electric kettle, a dining table and chairs, as well as a set of dishes and coffee;
  • the bathroom is a combined shower and toilet, where, among other things, there are bath accessories;
  • All apartments have a balcony with a rope for drying clothes and towels, as well as a pair of comfortable wicker chairs so you can sit outdoors enjoying the surrounding views.




Hotel infrastructure

Hotel Morjim Laxmi Resort 2 * (Goa) offers its guests to take advantage of the following opportunities provided by the resort infrastructure:

  • 24-hour reception desk, where at any time of the day or night you can get answers to your questions, as well as solve problems arising in the process of living;
  • a restaurant with national Indian cuisine, as well as with many international dishes that are more familiar to European tourists (only breakfast is included in the price);
  • the hotel operates a network of free wireless Internet;
  • not far from the hotel there is a public beach where sunbeds and umbrellas are available at an additional cost (only towels are provided at the hotel)
  • in the courtyard of the hotel is a beautiful green garden with palm trees and other exotic plants characteristic of the area;
  • if you want to sunbathe without leaving the hotel, then at your service there is a large terrace in the open air;
  • the hotel has a laundry room where they quickly and inexpensively wash and iron your clothes;
  • You can rent a car or other type of transport (bike or motorcycle) to independently explore all the nearby attractions and visit other settlements;
  • parking is provided for your rented vehicle, the use of which is absolutely free.




Useful hotel information

The following information should be known about this hotel:

  • Registration and check-in at the hotel is strictly after 13:00, regardless of the time of your arrival;
  • on the day of departure, all guests are obliged to leave their rooms and return the keys for reception until 11:00;
  • due to the compact size of the room and its layout, you can install no more than one berth;
  • for children in this hotel there is no special infrastructure, and young guests are not given discounts and benefits for accommodation;
  • the hotel does not have terminals of international payment systems, so only cash is accepted.

Positive reviews

For a budget traveler, Morjim Laxmi Resort 2 * is simply an ideal option. Reviews about him are characterized by the following positive comments:

  • the main advantage of this hotel is its budget rates (other hotels with the same level of service, but with a large number of stars are much more expensive);
  • an indisputable advantage is the presence in the rooms of the balcony, where you can not only dry clothes, but also sit in the fresh air in comfortable chairs (there is practically nowhere to do this in the territory)
  • rather inexpensive restaurant with delicious Indian dishes at fairly low prices compared to other institutions;
  • the rooms are quite spacious;
  • the staff at the reception is very polite and knows their job well.




Negative reviews

Among the negative comments of the guests of this hotel, it is worth noting the following:

  • there are no air conditioners in the rooms, and the fan practically does not cope with air cooling in the room;
  • there is no pool at the hotel, and the nearest beach can be reached for a sufficiently long time;
  • neither the hotel nor its immediate surroundings have any entertainment opportunities;
  • the furniture in the rooms is very old; many things require repair or a complete renovation;
  • breakfasts are held too early (around 6 a.m.), and their menu is quite modest;
  • plumbing is leaking in the bathroom, and there are also some interruptions in the supply of hot water;
  • room cleaning is very mediocre, and bed linen and towels do not change at all for the entire period of stay at the hotel;
  • noise from the street enters the apartment;
  • the front door and the entrance to the balcony are closed quite unreliably.
