How to tie a half-column with a crochet, a column with a crochet and without it

On the shelves of shops, on TV or just outside, on a girl and woman you can often see a beautiful knitted thing. It can be anything from an autumn coat to a summer swimsuit. Knits always stay in fashion and never get boring. The abundance of threads of different companies, quality, appearance and color pleases fashionistas and needlewomen. But what to do if you do not know how to knit and do not even know where to start.

Then you have come to the address. After reading this article, you can learn the basics of crocheting and learn how to knit booties for a child or doll. In the future, you can take on the implementation of more complex patterns and time-consuming things.

If you are new to this business, then you need to learn something about crochet hooks and threads. Hooks differ in size and materials from which they can be made. Their numbering starts from one and increases with the addition of half, for example: 1; 1.5; 2; 2.5; 3 etc. The tool can be made of metal, wood or plastic. The hook is selected directly under the thickness of the yarn. There are all kinds of threads, colors and thicknesses. You can even find yarn with beads and sparkles. It can be fluffy, and as a result, the product will also be fluffy. Such threads are best used when knitting sweaters, winter scarves, hats. But there is also a smooth yarn that can be suitable for creating summer tops, shorts and swimwear.



Having picked up the thread and the hook, you can begin training. Hold the hook like a table knife or like a pencil, whichever suits you best. Any product starts with air loops, on which a half -crochet with a crochet, a crochet with or without a crochet, and much more will be tied in the future.

The simplest knitting after air loops is a single crochet. Constantly training, you will learn not only to knit complex and beautiful patterns, but also to understand knitting patterns, and thanks to them to create things you like. Or to independently invent all the details of the product. It may seem difficult for a beginner to find a place to thread a hook.

If the pigtail of the air loops is connected correctly, not very tight and not too loose, then finding a loop will not be difficult. You should not have difficulties with grabbing the thread if the hook is correctly selected. After making an additional air loop, insert the hook into the pigtail and grab the thread, thread it. There are two loops left on the hook. Now grab the working thread and pull it through all the loops. On the hook you should have one loop left. When you can already master well the skill of crocheting columns without a crochet, try moving on to a more complex look - a half-crochet with a crochet. Crochet, start tying the air loops. Crochet the crochet, i.e. wrap the work thread around the tool. After that, insert the hook into the loop of the previous row or the pigtail of the air loops and extend the working thread. It should now have three loops. The one that is closer to the hook itself is now grabbed and pulled further through the two remaining ones. This is the double crochet.

Now try alternating a double crochet with a single crochet. When it starts to turn out well and quickly, then you can proceed to the next task in order to better master crochet. The half-column and the double crochet are very similar. Start knitting as if you were going to do what you already learned - a half crochet. When you have three stitches left on the hook, double crochet and knit two stitches together. Then - again the yarn and connect the remaining two loops into one. You just crocheted a double crochet. A little training - and you can use the hook to create the things you like.
