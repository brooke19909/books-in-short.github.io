Shipka 3 * (Sunny Beach / Bulgaria) - photos and reviews of tourists

Sunny Beach resort in Bulgaria is a great option for summer holidays on the Black Sea coast. It is perfect for families with children, as it has a long beachfront with delicate golden sand and a gentle entrance to the sea.



Holidays in Bulgaria are absolutely safe. The abundance of entertainment, the lack of a language barrier and good cuisine make the trip around this country a pleasant memory that you will definitely want to repeat again.

How to turn your vacation into an economical and entertaining trip? We will get acquainted with some of the intricacies of traveling to this resort.

Hotel selection

Sunny Beach resort offers tourists a huge selection of hotel options for accommodation. Depending on your preferences, you can stay in a fashionable five-star hotel, the territory and services of which to explore the whole vacation. You can book apartments with your own kitchen and live like in an apartment.

Modern Hotel Shipka 3 * is located fifty meters from the coast. This is a high-rise building, from the upper floors of which there is an amazing panoramic or side view of the sea, the resort of St. Vlas and the entire resort of Sunny Beach, where the hotel is located.





Location

Hotel Shipka 3 * (Sunny Beach) is located in the northern part of the resort. In walking distance is the coastline of the sea. The length of the beach strip is impressive, you can go along the seashore to the end of the beach area to the south for an hour and bask in the sun in the golden dunes. If you head north, you will get to the picturesque resort of St. Vlas, a fashionable place on the mountainous forested slopes, cascading down to the sea.

In the case of evening walks, you should visit the lively promenade, which leads to the resort center and offers a wide selection of different entertainment - from restaurants to attractions. All this pleasure is within walking distance for residents of Shipka 3 * hotel. sunny Beach different liveliness and the presence of a large number of entertainment venues. This is a zone of casinos, slot machines, night clubs and restaurants with live music and karaoke.

As noted by tourists, it is difficult to count on a room change upon arrival, so it is better to take care of this at the time of booking when booking a trip and carefully inspect the room upon check-in to exclude room malfunctions.





Buffet breakfast is included in the price. The hotel restaurant offers dinner and lunch service. But there is no need to limit yourself to the services of your hotel. Believe me, in Bulgaria it is worth and you need to eat in different restaurants and taverns in order to taste all the delights of Bulgarian cuisine. Each of the restaurants has its own hook - a signature dish whose taste will be remembered for a long time.

Hotel Shipka 3 * (Sunny Beach) can be booked all possible types of outdoor activities at sea: a yacht trip, skiing, parasailing (parachuting) and much more.

Entertainment

Often, tourists, buying a trip, expect to receive a range of entertainment services from the hotel. This is typical for hotels in Turkey, Egypt. In these countries, rest is bought in a hotel with all its infrastructure.

Bulgaria is a completely different matter, and rest in one country cannot be compared with rest in another. Hotel Shipka 3 * (Sunny Beach) appreciated primarily for its location. If you need to swim in a large pool, you can use this service on the territory of a neighboring five-star hotel. When you leave the hotel on the promenade, you have plenty of opportunities for entertainment.

A rich program of excursion services, which can be used at the reception of the Shipka 3 * hotel:

  • Golden Sands with a visit to Varna.
  • A trip to Gabrovo with an excursion to Shipka and Etar (the current museum).
  • Excursion to the Valley of Roses.
  • Boat trips and ethnic programs.

However, five minutes from the hotel there is a bus stop and for 2-4 leva you can reach the cities, a walk through which will be a meeting with the culture of the country. Half an hour away is Nessebar - a museum city. Or you can visit Sozopol if you drive south of the city of Burgas, the oldest city with Greek sources of culture.

Vacation Reviews

Holidays in Bulgaria is an opportunity to travel around the country. A visit to its taverns and bananits, tasting seafood and meat dishes cooked over an open fire will not leave anyone indifferent. If you properly organize your vacation, not like lying by the pool in a hotel, but a combination of thalassotherapy and acquaintance with the rich folk culture of the country, then your vacation will immediately turn into an exciting trip at a very reasonable price. Lovers of holidays in Bulgaria, according to their reviews, are attracted by the following advantages:

  • Lack of language barrier and free movement around the country.
  • Slavic cuisine.
  • A wide variety of excursion programs.
  • The proximity of cultures.
  • Short flight time.
  • The quality of the beaches.
