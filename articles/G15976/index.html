Communications

There are no questions about the list of ports, sockets and connectors Panasonic TX-32DR300ZZ. Owner reviews indicate that the communication list of such a device does not really have any drawbacks. The Panasonic company included the following connection methods into it:

  1. Three digital HDMI connectors. With their help, you can output the signal from a personal computer, DVD-player or satellite receiver. Also in such a connector, you can also connect a mini-PC and thereby improve the capabilities of the solution in question due to the appearance of such an option in software such as Smart TV.

  2. One specialized antenna input to which either a local antenna or cable operator equipment can be connected. But directly switching to it a satellite signal reception system is unacceptable. The tuner built into the TV is not capable of processing such information.

  3. Two universal USB connectors. They allow you to directly output multimedia content from flash drives or external hard drives. Also, such a connector is also involved in organizing the power supply of a mini-PC installed in an HDMI format port.

  4. One composite and component input is integrated in this device. Both the first of them and the second are mainly used when connecting to today's outdated satellite tuners and receivers. Also, with their help, switching can be carried out with outdated models of DVD-players. In addition to this, it is often possible with their help to receive television broadcasts from digital tuners DVB-T2 in an external version.

  5. The CI expansion slot is used when it is necessary to decode the received television signal using hardware.

  6. As previously noted, this solution is also equipped with one 3.5-mm speaker port.





Setting procedure

Reviews on the Panasonic TX-32DR300ZZ TV indicate the simplest setup algorithm . A review of the characteristics of its software component, as noted earlier, indicates the absence of the Smart TV function. Therefore, the setup procedure in this case comes down to just assembling the TV, making all the connections necessary for its normal operation and searching for all available channels.

That is, the pre-operational configuration algorithm consists of the following steps:

  1. Mounting the TV support, which is included in the package.

  2. Connect the power wire and signal cable. According to the latter, the signal can come both from the cable provider, and from the local antenna.

  3. Install the batteries in the control panel.

  4. Turn on the TV.

  5. After that, you need to enter the settings menu of the latter and select "Channels".

  6. At the next stage, activate the item "Auto Search".

  7. Next, in the query window, select the type of television signal source (cable TV, analog TV, digital TV).

  8. After the search is completed, we save the list of television channels and the device in question is now ready for use.
