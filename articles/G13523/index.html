Review of the conditioner LG S09SWC

The new LG S09SWC air conditioner, an overview of which is presented in the article, uses a special technology called JetCool. Thanks to it, the air in the room will cool in just five minutes, and the "dead zones", which do not get a fresh stream, are eliminated.

The device has a mode of operation Power Heating. It is necessary in order to heat the room at a time when the heating is not yet turned on, or is already turned off, and it is cold outside.

The air conditioner works with a multi-level air purification system. Due to it, it is possible to destroy all existing bacteria and allergens several times.

An option is built in that allows you to automatically drain the air conditioner case after working in cooling mode. This allows you to kill all bacteria and prevents the appearance of an unpleasant odor.

Installation of the device system is very quick, does not create any inconvenience and is as simple as possible.



Unpacking

Seal the device at all manufacturing plants equally reliably. But what awaits the buyer after unpacking?

The design of the LG S09SWC air conditioner is classic, no different from many other LG devices. The shape line has smooth and slightly curved outlines, so the device as a whole looks pretty harmonious. The front end received a wave-like style of performance. The plastic is installed in white, the smell does not come from it. The design is quite simple and miniature. This could end the description of the appearance of the device, but it is necessary to say a few words about the ionizer. It is made in a bright light green color. Given that it comes as standard, this nuance is a huge plus. The part is located under the blinds. It can be seen from a distance well, as it is made in bright color. Installed a little crooked. Access to the item is easy. Cleaning the ionizer will be as simple as possible, as well as replacing it.

Advantages

The device works with new technologies. It has a completely low noise level and also purifies the air efficiently.





The remote control has received several new elements, so many buyers say that this is an advantage. It is now wide and rectangular, respectively, easily lies in the hand. Using it is easy. The keys are large and round.

Three new features of LG S09SWC (positive reviews):

  • Air sterilization. The ionizer control key is as convenient as possible. After its use, the room is cleaned of bacteria and other pollutants.
  • The second new key allows you to turn on the power saving mode. How does this function work? With it, you can reduce the speed of the compressor blades for the same power as it was.
  • The cleaning unit is also displayed on the control panel. Thanks to the key, you can quickly remove unnecessary moisture from the air conditioner.

Another advantage of this appliance is that a fan is installed in the indoor unit. The noise level is reduced due to the decrease in air resistance. The developers achieved this by placing the blades of the device at a completely different angle.

disadvantages

In the market, the price of the LG S09SWC device is slightly overpriced. There are also the following disadvantages: the ionizer is poorly fixed in the block. But, in general, it is practically not noticeable, if you do not look closely. The plastic is not very high quality, but at a height of 2.5 m from the floor it will not be evident.





Summary

LG, as expected, has provided a high-quality device. Air conditioning, which was made in China, is not inferior in quality to its competitors from Turkey. Of course, there are drawbacks, but no one is safe from them. All functional blocks work quietly.

Features include the Plasmaster Ionizer. The indoor unit is cleared automatically. Electricity is consumed in equal amounts regardless of power. There is a special mode of conservation of electricity.
