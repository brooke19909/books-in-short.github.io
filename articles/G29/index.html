Mophie juice pack air

Many do not dare to invest in an external battery, because they are confused by the insufficient compactness of some models or the need to connect to them through a cable, which makes it uncomfortable to hold a smartphone in your hand and simultaneously charge it. Especially for this, Mophie Juice Pack Air, a portable charger for iPhone 5 in the form of a case, has long been released.

That's right: this device is simply worn on your smartphone and serves as a cover (there are even models made of more durable material that will protect the phone from falling), and when it becomes necessary to recharge, with a simple touch of a button it turns into a portable battery.

The main disadvantage is the battery capacity: only 1700 mAh. About the same in the battery of the iPhone 5s itself. The capacity of the Mofi battery is enough for about one full charge of the iPhone. There are much more powerful models, but they are not so convenient.

This portable charger for iPhone 5s in its characteristics is the exact opposite of the previous model. Firstly, it is distinguished by its size: the device is quite large, you can‚Äôt call it compact at all - 4.4 x 6.6 x 0.6 inches. And it weighs a kilogram. Xiaomi is a manufacturer of well-known smartphones in the Asian market. And after he released a series of stand-alone chargers, products began to be in demand among iPhone users.

As the name implies, the battery capacity is 10400 mAh. These are about four full charges of the iPhone 5s, which is pretty good. The device is very compact, its weight is small - 250 g. Powerbank is equipped with a USB port and four LED charge indicators.

What distinguishes Xiaomi portable batteries from others is its stylish minimalist design. This model will look great next to your iPhone. At the same time, the price of the device can not but rejoice - from 1500 rubles.

Finding this model is not so easy right now, but it's worth talking about it. Perhaps this is the most versatile portable charger for iPhone 5. Reviews of it very flattering, so pay attention to the "pauerbank".

In principle, if you often use it to transfer Wi-Fi, it is unlikely to charge your smartphone with it, very often the external battery will drain.

The wireless capabilities of the device are not limited to wireless Internet transmission. It can also read SD memory cards, for this there is a special connector in its case.

It should be noted the dimensions of this model. Powerbank is as small as it could have been, providing it with all the extra features.

Finally, we move on to a truly compact and miniature model. Jackery Mini fits not only in the bag, but also in the pocket of your trousers.
