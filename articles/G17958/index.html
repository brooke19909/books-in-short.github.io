Lublin hotels: addresses, location, description of hotels and rooms, services provided, photos, reviews

The ancient Polish city of Lublin is located at the intersection of trade and economic routes from Western to Eastern Europe. A fairly large city is unique in an amazing combination of Polish, Lithuanian, Jewish and Belarusian cultures, which organically complement each other. Since the 13th century, legislative and educational activities have been carried out in this city; the largest Rabbis Academy in Europe is located here.

Today, Lublin is a modern, developing university city, in which more than 80 thousand students come to study and relax.

If you are planning a business trip to Lublin or want to go there for excursions, you are of course interested in where to stay in the city. In this regard, do not worry. There are plenty of hotels in Lublin in Poland - from luxury hotels to modest hostels. Some of them are presented in this article.

Lublin Hotels: Alter

The magnificent Hotel Alter is located next to the Sobieski Palace. It is located in a 16th century apartment building in the historical part of Lublin. All rooms are decorated in a simple classical style, beautifully furnished, equipped with the necessary modern appliances and minibars. Each has a private bathroom with a bath or shower, bathrobes, a hairdryer and necessary toiletries.

It features an indoor pool, a spa and wellness center with a hot tub and sauna, and free Wi-Fi. The hotel has a bar and restaurant.

At the front desk, visitors are served around the clock. Judging by the reviews, the friendly and friendly staff will help you book tickets for return travel and sign up for an orientation tour of the city.
