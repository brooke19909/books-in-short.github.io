Device Overview

The Prestige DVR 390 video recorder, reviews of which are distributed on the thematic portals of technology lovers, belongs to budget devices, and at the same time is characterized by a very competitive level of functionality. The device provides good quality shooting at any time of the day.

The Prestige DVR 390 video recorder (user reviews regarding its characteristics is extremely positive) has a fairly large 2.7-inch touch screen capable of functioning at a resolution of 1920 by 1080 pixels. The shooting speed that the recorder provides is 30 frames per second. The Novatek 96650 processor provides a sufficiently high performance device.





The device is equipped with a convenient swivel bracket. The DVR has infrared illumination, which allows you to shoot in the dark. Among other notable functions of the device is the WDR function, which allows you to improve the information content of the picture by optimizing the illumination when shooting.

The device is equipped with a built-in microphone that provides sound recording in synchronization with video recording. The good quality of shooting, which the Prestige DVR 390 device can provide (customer reviews can confirm this), is combined with the ease of management and simplicity of the DVR.

The compact size of the device allows you to place it on the windshield so that it will not interfere with the review. The DVR has the function of viewing video recordings on the display.

If we consider the reviews that characterize the Prestige DVR 390 device positively, the advantages of the device are, according to DVR users, the presence of a G-sensor. It helps to ensure, first of all, the stability of the DVR in motion on uneven surfaces, during braking.

Specifications

Let us now examine in more detail the main characteristics of the Prestige DVR 390. Reviews about them are also among the most popular among those found on thematic portals. The device is equipped with:

- brown or black case - depending on the specific delivery;

- motion sensor;

- G-sensor;

- backlight;

- one camera with a resolution of 4 MP, which allows you to shoot with a viewing angle of 170 degrees, able to rotate, equipped with a 1/4 inch matrix and 6 lenses;

- processor type NTK96650;

- built-in battery with a capacity of 450 mAh, which can be charged from the on-board network at a voltage of 12-24 V.

Among the main functions of the device:

- video recording in the resolution of 1920 by 1080 pixels at a speed of 30 frames per second, with recording in a file of the MOV type;

- photography at a resolution of 4032 by 3042 pixels;

- providing continuous cyclic video recording with reflection of time and date;

- night shooting;

- data recording on a microSD memory card up to 32 GB.

The device supports popular communication interfaces - AV, miniHDMI, miniUSB. The DVR operates at temperatures up to -40 degrees.

As we noted above, a Prestige DVR 390 device is equipped with an NTK 96650 processor type. Owner reviews may imply a general assessment of the device's performance based on the relevant characteristics. It will be useful to study the features of the processor in more detail.
