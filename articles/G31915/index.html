Doreta Beach Resort & SPA 4 * (Greece / Rhodes): photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

The Greek island of Rhodes has long been chosen by our compatriots as a great place for a summer vacation. And there are good reasons for this. Indeed, Rhodes is known for its fashionable hotels, magnificent beaches, entertainment for every taste, ancient monuments and secluded and quiet places. If you are just now looking for a good hotel on the Greek island of Rhodes, then pay attention to Doreta Beach Resort & SPA 4 *. It is about this hotel complex that will be discussed further. We will find out what it represents, what services it offers to guests and what kind of entertainment you can count on here.





Doreta Beach Resort & SPA 4 *: photo, hotel location

This hotel complex is located on the west coast of Rhodes, near a village called Theologos. Rhodes is 17 kilometers away and Lindos is 50 kilometers away. From the nearest airport "Diagoras" to the hotel to go only seven kilometers. Thus, after arrival, you can arrive at the hotel in just a few minutes. As for the beach, since the Delta Beach Resort & SPA 4 * is located on the beach, the beach is literally a few tens of meters from the hotel.





Hotel Description

Doreta Beach Resort & SPA 4 * (Rhodes) received its first guests back in 1976. Since then, he has undergone modernization several times. The last restoration was carried out in 2009. The hotel itself is positioning itself as a great place for family and youth recreation.

Hotel Policies

In accordance with the internal rules adopted at Doreta Beach Resort & SPA 4 *, check-in of newly arrived guests at the hotel is carried out after one in the afternoon. If you have arrived earlier, and the room you have booked is not ready yet, then you can leave your luggage in the storage room and go for lunch or straight to the beach. On the day of departure, you must leave the room before noon. Payment for accommodation must also be made upon check-out. You can pay in cash or through a plastic card systems "Visa", "Mastercard" or "American Express".

Since the hotel, among other things, positions itself as a family institution, children of any age are allowed here. So, if you came to rest with a baby younger than two years old, who will sleep in your room in a crib, then you will not have to pay separately for his stay. If you place a child from two to twelve years of age in existing bedding, this will also be free. For the placement of older children or adults in an extra bed, it will be necessary to pay 70% of the total price per person per night. Please note that only one extra or baby cot is allowed in each room. You must also inform the hotel in advance that you will need this service.





Doreta Beach Resort & SPA 4 * (Greece) is classified as Pets Friendly. This means that guests traveling with their pets are allowed here. However, you should notify the hotel in advance if you plan to arrive with your four-legged friend. No extra charge for this.

Number of rooms

Doreta Beach Resort & SPA 4 * is a fairly large hotel. So, its number of rooms includes 390 rooms of various types: standard, studios, suites and apartments. Each room has a balcony with a wonderful view of the sea, mountains or garden. Also in all rooms are air-conditioned, satellite TV, refrigerator, bathroom, hairdryer. Some rooms also have a jacuzzi.

Room cleaning and linen and towels are changed regularly. You can also order room service for a fee.

Every day you can taste delicious dishes of both Greek and international cuisine. Cooks can also prepare vegetarian dishes upon prior reservation. The hotel does not forget about the youngest guests. For them there is a special children's menu, as well as high chairs.

Infrastructure

The four-star Doreta Beach Resort & SPA hotel has a well-developed infrastructure designed to make guests stay as comfortable and enjoyable as possible. So, for the convenience of guests, the reception is open 24 hours a day. Thus, at any time of the day or night, you can contact the administrator for help. Here you can use the safe. This service is paid. Almost all the administrators at Dorete Beach Resort & SPA speak Russian. Therefore, you can easily communicate with them.

On site there are shops selling souvenirs, jewelry and various items that may be required on vacation. Also in the hotel you can book this or that tour, rent a car or bicycle and exchange currency.

Doreta Beach Hotel has ample parking, which guests can use for free. Also, the hotel has access to wireless Internet.
