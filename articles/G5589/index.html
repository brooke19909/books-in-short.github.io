Flower made of felt. We create beautiful products with our own hands

Felt, as a material for creativity, is increasingly gaining good fame. Its advantages are many: it cuts well, the edges do not fray, it does not crease, and it is easy to care for. It is a pleasure to work with this fabric. This article is about a product like felt flower. Such an article can be an interior decoration, a toy, a hair accessory, an element of a bead, bracelet or brooch. Right now we will learn to make a variety of flowers from felt fabric with our own hands. To work?



Lily

How to make a flower of this kind of felt? It is very simple. Cut a triangle out of paper . Now draw three petals on it (the corners of the figure are the tips of the petals). Cut the resulting template and transfer it to a white felt, folded in half. Next, cut out two of these parts. From a green fabric, perform a leaflet. Attach one blank with petals from one edge to it with a thermogun or a needle with a thread, and then the other, placing it so that the petals do not lie on top of each other. From yellow felt, make the center for the lily. To do this, cut a strip with a length of about 10 and a width of 1.5-2 centimeters, cut it along one edge and twist into a roller. Fasten the edge and sew or glue the part in the center of the workpiece to the petals. That's all. A felt lily flower made in literally a quarter of an hour.



Chamomile

You can make a voluminous flower from felt by studying the following information. For work you will need:

  • felt;
  • thread floss ;
  • filler (synthetic winterizer, cotton wool, holofiber);
  • bright button;
  • scissors;
  • paper;
  • pencil.

What should be done before sewing felt flowers? Patterns, of course. Fold a square sheet of paper four times, and then another triangle. Cut the wide edge of the workpiece in a semicircle. When you unfold the paper, you will see a flower with six petals, similar to a camomile. This is the pattern of the future product. Transfer it to felt and cut out two of these parts. Decorate the part that will serve as the front of the flower as follows: in the middle, sew on the button (middle of the chamomile), on the petals you can embroider floss, beads or sequins. Next, fold the two blanks together and seam them ‚Äúover the edge‚Äù. When it remains to sew 1-2 cm, set aside the needle and fill the product with filler. Then complete the connection of the parts. On such a flower made of felt, you can attach a leg from a wooden skewer by gluing it from the wrong side with a hot gun, or introduce it inside the product before stitching it. Wrap such a stalk with green paper or ribbon, and a beautiful daisy will be able to stand in a vase.

rose flower

You only need ten minutes to make this type of flower. Cut a circle from felt, and then cut it in a spiral. Prepare a thread with a needle and a bead so that they are at your fingertips. Starting from the center, twist the spiral, picking up the fabric with your fingers, forming a flower. Sew the bottom edge so that the product does not open. Sew a bead in the center of the flower. That's all, a flower from felt - a rose - is ready. If you want to put this craft in a vase, then make a leg for it in the manner described above.



Flowers made of felt are performed quickly, and look amazing. You can make an accessory of this kind for each outfit (brooch, hairpin, elastic band for hair, belt). In this way, you will not go unnoticed. Get inspired and needlework!
