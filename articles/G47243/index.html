DIY felt crown. Interesting ideas, tips and patterns

Felt is a soft, pleasant to the touch, easy-to-use material, therefore it is often used for the manufacture of soft toys, crafts, panels and room decoration. In this article we will talk about another unusual use of material beloved by the craftswomen. It will be about making a crown of felt.





They do it for holidays and matinees in kindergarten, for example, for a costume of a princess or Cinderella. Often they put it on the baby to celebrate a birthday. In the middle of the crown, in this case, they make an application of a figure indicating how old the child is.

Any girl wants to feel like a princess, this is known even to people who are far from pedagogy and psychology. Therefore, the crown of felt may be needed by the child for the usual game. Some girls are happy to wear it just at home. How to make it from felt? Let's look at the manufacturing options and patterns, according to which you can draw the contours of the future subject.

The simplest crown

In order to create such an ornament, you will need to buy a sheet of orange felt, half a meter of elastic, a thread with a needle, scissors and a template. To know the diameter of such a felt crown, you need to calculate it approximately with a flexible meter. For example, let's take a diameter of 21 cm. Then, following the pattern, it will be necessary to mark the corners for the crown equal to 3 cm. We will get 7 sharp protrusions of a triangular shape. The depth of cuts can be selected by eye.





Approximately 3 cm is left from the corners to the base so that the crown of felt holds firmly. If you simply sew the edges of the crown together, it will turn out soft and will not keep its shape. To prevent this from happening, you need to strengthen the base with another layer of felt.





To do this, a strip is cut out, equal in size to the part that is without triangular corners, and from the inside you can sew the second layer, it will perfectly hold the glued rim on felt.

Useful Tips

To the corners of the crown looked to the side, you need to give the pattern a trapezoid shape, sewing the edges not in a straight line, but at an angle. You can also advise, decorate a decorative product. For example, use half beads or pebbles of rhinestones glued to the tops of triangles. Another option - you can draw a circle on the top of each triangle when making a template, and pebbles on it.





Such double-layer crowns also look beautiful. You can leave an orange color on top and make the bottom layer completely white. Then the product will keep its shape well and there will be no need to glue the base. It remains at the end to sew the elastic - and the crown is ready.

Flower crown option

To make such a crown of felt with your own hands, you need to purchase beige, yellow, blue, blue, orange and pink sheets of felt, threads with a needle to match the main color and beads. You can make such an ornament around the entire head, but basically it is done only on the frontal part of the head. On the back of the head is an elastic band. It can simply be sewn to the edges of the product, or you can sew it with a cloth.





First you need to draw a template, according to which the pattern will be performed. To do this, measure the circumference of the child‚Äôs head and divide its value in half. For example, if it is 50 cm, then draw a rectangle with one side equal to 25 cm. We take the height of the crown by eye, if desired. Next, you need to find the middle and put a point on opposite sides. It will be equal to 25: 2 = 12.5 cm. Connect these points and continue the line up 3 cm. We put there a point that will be the top of the crown. It remains to connect it with smooth lines with the long side of the rectangle.

Next is the pattern of the crown of felt. To do this, fold in half a sheet of beige felt sheet and, having outlined a template, cut along the contours. Two layers are sewn together along the perimeter of the fabric, you can insert a template from cardboard into the inner layer. Then the crown will become stiff and will not deform.

Remains the work on the flowers. For each flower you need to draw two patterns, one larger, the other smaller. Sew them in by overlaying them with a bead. A gum is sewn on the back.

Interesting pattern

A child can make a crown not only standard, but also curly. To do this, you can use the templates, which are in large quantities on the Internet, or you can draw yourself. To make such a carved pattern symmetrical, before drawing a template, the paper is folded in half and a pattern is cut out, like a Christmas snowflake.





It turns out very original. Before starting work on the manufacture of the template, you can come up with additional decorations and prepare a pattern by their shape.

For example, if there are flowers, then the tops can also be cut in the shape of the petals. And if there are buds of roses, then you can cut out the trapezoid, on each of which the bud will cling. There are many options. It can be butterflies, dragonflies, leaves, in the middle of the figure, if the crown is intended for the birthday man.

Crown for girls

For the manufacture of jewelry for a daughter, pink, red, yellow or beige colors are usually chosen. Additional elements are hearts, flowers, rhinestones, beads or pebbles.





You can sheathe the product with lace or make voluminous flowers from satin ribbons or the same felt.

Crown for boys

How to make a felt crown for a boy? Here you have to show ingenuity and skills of the artist. Colors for boys use green, blue, blue, red. You can make the application of a typewriter or an airplane, clouds and grass, make embroidery of a bicycle, as in the photo.





The little prince will be delighted with any character in a cartoon or fairy tale.

Autumn felt crown

If the birthday person celebrates his birthday in the autumn, then you can use natural elements for the crown.





It can be leaves or acorns, a hedgehog with an apple or a pear on needles, a squirrel with nuts, any berries, for example, bright red clusters of viburnum.

You can fantasize in different ways. Felt is such a convenient material for crafts that any item can be realized in felt products.
