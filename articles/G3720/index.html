Greece. Hotels of Crete 5 stars

Crete is a living illustration of ancient Greek myths and legends. This is the birthplace of the very first European civilization - Minoan. Tours to Crete are often chosen because of the highest service offered by local hotels. They are especially popular among the most demanding tourists, lovers of "refined" relaxation.





Crete Resorts

The magnificent 5 star hotels of Crete are famous all over the world. But the beaches will compete with them: snow-white sand and generous sun - this can be enjoyed endlessly, especially if you like a relaxing holiday. The proximity of resorts to Heraklion allows you to fulfill dreams of outdoor activities, here everyone finds exactly what they want.

Have you always snoozed a passion for antiquities? Then there is the opportunity to visit a variety of museums, and even ongoing archaeological excavations.

Gouves

This resort area is located in the heart of the northern coast of Crete. Convenient geographical location (just east of the capital) allows you to see the sights of the entire island. The village itself surprises tourists with its architecture.

The cozy hotels of Crete 5 stars will present a fabulous vacation. One of the most famous is the Grecotel Amirandes. The cozy rooms of the hotel are equipped with a large LCD TV, comfortable furniture, a luxurious bathroom. Deluxe rooms have an additional exit to the garden with a private pool. A few steps from the hotel, in the garden, is a beautiful beach. A huge pool allows you to swim without going to the coast. There is a SPA salon for guests. The cost is from 8000 rubles per day per person.





Malia

This is a very interesting and unforgettable town, half of it is the old Malia, with narrow streets, with flower-beds with curly flowers, with small taverns from which music sounds. The second half is the embankment, the world of tourists and unrestrained fun until the morning. Here, most of all, youth companies like to relax.

5 star hotels in Crete are distinguished by exquisite taste and impeccable service. This also applies to Royal Heights. Guests are accommodated in elegant rooms or in small villas with private pools. There is a cozy kitchen with a microwave and a refrigerator, a living room with upholstered furniture and air conditioning. There is a huge fitness center, experienced trainers who will help put in shape, and a beauty salon. You have every chance to return from vacation irresistible.

Breakfast is served every morning in the cafe, and an abundance of snacks, pastries and fruits makes your appetite worse and eliminates the need for lunch. The rest of the time you can choose one of the restaurants, cafes or eateries. If you come with children, then they will not be bored. There is a games room with animators and nannies on site. The clean and equipped beaches of Malia are a two-minute walk away. Cost from 3000 per person per day.

5- star hotels of Crete work here: Alexander Beach Hotel, Ikaros Beach and others.





Rethymno

The capital of the same area. Without exaggeration, this is the most beautiful city in all of Greece, more reminiscent of an open-air museum, reflecting the history and culture of Crete. Rethymno 5 star hotels presents environmentally friendly beaches. Steep cliffs and sparkling bays, fountains and waterfalls are also characteristic features of the city. Not enough day to list local beauties.

Gregotel Creta Palace is located in the immediate vicinity of the city center. Elegant air-conditioned rooms with parquet floors , a minibar and large TV. Daily linen, bathrobes and slippers are changed. On the territory there are 10 restaurants and bars, swimming pools with slides for children. There is a gym, guests will appreciate the sunny courtyard with a large hot tub. Cost from 6000 per day per person.

At any time of the year you will find the island of Crete. 5 star hotels will take into account all your wishes and make your vacation unforgettable. Sentido Aegean Pearl and Aguila Porto Rethumno receive regular guest reviews. The brilliant rooms, the beach, outdoor and indoor pools create all conditions for a good holiday. Breakfast and dinner are included in the program and are carried out as a buffet. The cost of rest is from 3500 per day per person.





Hersonissos

This is a real paradise for youth recreation. Nowhere else in the country can one meet so many entertainment venues, filled to the brim with thirsts for outdoor activities. Incendiary music here does not subside until the morning. The town is located in a very picturesque place where the rocks of Lasithi are washed by the waters of the Mediterranean and Aegean Seas.

All hunters for colorful impressions await the island of Crete. 5 star hotels will take care of your convenience. The most appreciated by tourists is the Bella Beach Hotel. The elegant rooms are equipped with modern appliances. There are furnished balconies on which to spend so good evenings. The restaurant serves breakfast, lunch and dinner. A large selection of dishes will delight even gourmets. It offers guests several restaurants and a large wellness center. There is a sauna, hammam, there is a basketball court. Cost from 4000 per day per person.

You can recommend other hotels of Crete 5 stars located nearby. This is Greta Maris Beach Resort, Albatros SPA, Stella Palace, Hersonissos Palace.

Elounda

This is a real pride and decoration of the island. Picturesque and diverse landscape, mild climate and endless sea - what else is needed during the holiday? On the promenade built several expensive, fashionable hotels. The level of service here is very high, trained staff takes into account every nuance to make your stay perfect.

Let us bring to your attention more hotels of Crete 5 stars. Elounda Bau Palace offers spacious rooms with an author's design, some have beautiful terraces leading to the garden, and if medieval romance attracts you, choose apartments with a fireplace. Lunches and dinners are practiced outdoors. The restaurants offer local and European cuisine, an incredibly large selection of snacks, side dishes and desserts. The cost of 10,000 rubles per day.

This is not the only resort that Crete (Elounda) can boast of . Hotels 5 stars are located here very often, the city is always happy for guests. Elounda Peninsula All Suite offers visitors luxurious apartments and villas with private pools. Many of the rooms have magnificent bathrooms and stunning living rooms with sea views. The cost is from 10,000 per day per person.





Chania

This area is famous for its miles of beaches and amazing, pink sand, which is not found anywhere else in Crete. It is easier to find a modest and inexpensive hotel here, but there are options for lovers of exclusive holidays. Magnificent 4-5 star hotels in Crete will create a holiday atmosphere for you throughout your vacation. This is Ambassadors Residence, its elegant, individually decorated rooms with impeccable taste and free Wi-Fi. Most apartments offer views of the endless azure blue.

Breakfast includes dozens of varied dishes. The rest of the food is based on the menu. Medical, wellness and beauty treatments are available upon request. The cost is from 5000 rubles per day.

Casa Delfino Hotel is located in an old Venetian mansion of the XVII century. The charm of antiquity and modern amenities create a unique flavor. The comfortable rooms are decorated with luxurious fabrics, the bathroom is lined with marble, comfortable furniture and household appliances complete the picture. Every morning a deluxe breakfast is served in the fresh air.

Steel

Excellent infrastructure makes it convenient for a measured, relaxing holiday. The favorable location allows you to see all the sights of eastern Crete. Here are built the best hotels of Crete 5 stars, the most famous - Blue Sea Village resort. The rooms are decorated with a delicate taste that will not go unnoticed by connoisseurs. The food of the guests is monitored by a team of professional chefs, true masters of their craft. Here you can taste dishes of almost any cuisine of the world. There is a gym, massage room, steam bath and sauna. The entertainment program will not let bored adults or children, every evening you will find a bright disco, as well as interest clubs. Without wasting time, you can pack up on a fascinating tour and replenish your photo album with wonderful views.

Kissamos

This town is for those who want to spend time in peace and quiet, not rushing to explore the natural beauty. Local hotels are distinguished not only by the highest level of service, but also by relatively low prices. Molos Bay Hotel offers bright, spacious rooms with orthopedic beds, air conditioning and a minibar. Excellent service will allow you to relax as much as possible and enjoy the rest. Restaurants daily offer a huge amount of delicious delicacies, as well as local wines.

Heraklion

This is the administrative center of Crete. The city is aimed at those who are interested in ancient Greek history, Minoan culture or architecture. It is an ideal place to relax on the island of Crete - Heraklion. 5 star hotels are not uncommon here, on the coast there are at least six establishments of such a high class. This is the incomparable Aguila Atlantis Crete, which is located next to the archaeological museum. The rooms are equipped with modern appliances and furniture. The hotel has a luxurious garden, 24-hour room service. There is a swimming pool, sauna. You can hire a babysitter for your child.





Hotels for honeymooners

Celebrating such a grand event in Crete is more than just a dream. Gregotel Kalliston is a fashionable hotel that is located on the seafront. Its name translates as "beautiful", this applies to a wonderful location among wonderful nature, and to the sophisticated luxury of the interiors, and to the highest service. The hotel offers rooms of various categories, which are decorated with sophisticated taste. The staff will do everything necessary so that on such an important day you feel like a real king and queen, and will definitely return to celebrate the anniversary.

Local restaurants organize not only delicious breakfasts, lunches and dinners, but also themed evenings, with original decoration and exquisite goodies. The best collection of wines has about 10,000 varieties from all over the world. For the entertainment of guests there are several pools, a spa center, sports grounds for tennis, volleyball, aerobics. Nearby is a water sports center. Show programs and discos are regularly organized.

This, of course, is far from all 5 star hotels in Crete. Reviews of tourists are always full of warm memories of the holidays. This applies not only to elite, but also simpler hotels (3, 2 stars). They are distinguished by a more modest interior, but no less attentive attitude of the staff. The days spent here will be unforgettable.
