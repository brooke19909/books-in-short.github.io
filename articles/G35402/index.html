The main attractions of Beidaihe. How to get to the city?

Beidaihe is a small coastal town in China, which has become a cozy tourist corner. It has landscape and entertainment parks, gardens, motels and beaches. He is loved not only by tourists, but also by the Chinese themselves. What sights of Beidaihe are worth seeing? Let's find out.

Beidaihe is now a famous resort. The city has two sanatoriums and a medical diagnostic center. On a coastal strip of 11 kilometers there are three beaches. Dragon head

Beidaihe's most interesting and grandiose attraction is Laolongtou Park. From here begins the Great Wall of China, and its end, going into the sea, is called the Dragon's Head. In this part, the wall looks brand new. After being destroyed by enemies, it was rebuilt in 1984. In addition to the Great Wall tower in the park, you can see Chinese buildings, the Nereus temple with sculptures of the gods, and the stone city built during the Ming Dynasty.





Longevity Gorge

Just an hour's drive from Beidaihe is the Longevity Gorge. It is located at the base of Mount Yang and covers almost 50 square kilometers. The main attractions are the through cave, the Nine Heaven arbors and the mountain of five Buddhas, the road to which leads through amazing mountain landscapes buried in green trees.

Lantern Park

It is good to come here with children, but lovers of a dream park will also like it. It has many quiet paths, bridges, ponds and stone compositions. Everything here is inspired by Chinese folklore. Main Object - Amazing House. This is a fabulous palace with labyrinths, secret passages, decorated with mirrors and sculptures of legendary characters.





How to get to Beidaihe?

Next, you will need to transfer to ground transport. Buses run directly from Beijing Airport to the city. They go from morning to evening, so there is no need to specially adjust the schedule for them.
