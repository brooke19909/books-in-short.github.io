Technical specifications Asus ZenFone 6

The Android 4.3 operating system was originally installed with a future update to 4.4. Asus releases regular updates. The Intel Z2580 Atom processor is dual-core. Asus ZenFone 6 is available, depending on the country of destination, with 8 and 16 GB of memory. Only Asus ZenFone 6 16gb is officially delivered to Russia, Ukraine, the CIS. We continue the review by checking the battery. Its capacity is 3300 mAh. The design is not removable.

Gaming Performance

In productive racing simulators, graphics demonstrate aerobatics. Not a hint of slowdowns, the picture is smooth. The accelerometer and gyroscope work out "excellent", fixing the slightest displacement of the smartphone body. It is in these games that users want to play.

Dynamic shooters, fighters also show live graphics. It is unlikely that there are games that can overload the Asus ZenFone 6. Asus has demonstrated that a high-performance system can be built on the basis of only a dual-core processor and not the most powerful graphics chip. The specific Intel Atom architecture, 2 gigabytes of RAM, high-quality components, regularly updated software make a positive contribution.

There are also flaws in Asus ZenFone 6. Reviews indicate that if you play for a long time, the device heats up significantly. This fact does not affect work. Also, users complain about the difficulties with installing the popular game "GTA SA".

Camera

Without exaggeration, the main built-in 12.6 megapixel camera with autofocus produces some of the best quality shots among smartphones of the same price category. Captures excellent FullHD video. Autofocus is fast. The photo program will delight you with many presets for a variety of shooting modes. Even a child, choosing the appropriate mode, will catch a good frame on the Asus ZenFone 6. A review of the photos taken leads to the idea that top-end smartphones have not just equaled, but have surpassed compact digital cameras in video quality.

Provides panoramic shooting, timer, mode in low light. You can make thumbnails, highlight in the frame, a simple photo montage. The Depth of Field feature allows you to highlight the foreground and blur the background. "Improvement" will make the photo more contrast, sharp. From several frames you can make GIF animation.

The front camera 1.2 MP other tasks. She is able to capture her beloved, make a video call, chat on Skype and other programs that broadcast streaming video.

Working hours

The battery capacity of 3300 mAh is far from record. Already produced models of more than 5000 mAh. But such a capacity allows you to maintain parity between size / weight and working time. With moderate use (calls, SMS, Skype, the Internet, reading, a few games and movies), the Asus ZenFone 6 mobile phone lives for two days without recharging. In saving mode and minimum calls - 2.5-3 days. If you continuously play resource-intensive games, the charge will last for 3-4 hours. Watching movies (including online in HD format) - up to 10 hours. Talk time - 32-34 hours.
