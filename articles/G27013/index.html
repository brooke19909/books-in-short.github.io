Hotel Cretan Village Hotel 4 * (Greece / Crete): photo and reviews of tourists

If you want to feel the Cretan flavor, as well as immerse yourself in its culture, go to the Cretan Village Hotel 4 *. Interior, architecture, nature, hospitality - all this and much more will be the key to an unforgettable Greek holiday.





Resort Description

Characterizing the resort hotel Cretan Village Hotel 4 *, it is worth noting that in translation its name means "Cretan village". Indeed, his style is fully consistent with the interior and architectural traditions of local settlements. Hotel staff are doing everything possible so that guests can immerse themselves in the life and traditions of the Cretans.

One of the main features of the Cretan Village Hotel 4 * hotel can be considered that it is located on a mountainside. Thanks to this, guests can contemplate the incredibly beautiful seascapes that open from windows and from viewing platforms. It is also worth noting the huge number of greenery and walking paths that create a special atmosphere.

Despite the fact that the Cretan Village Hotel 4 * is a family business, it operates in accordance with the highest quality standards (ISO 9001). This institution began to work in 1997, and 10 years later it was renovated. For accommodation, guests are offered 37 comfortable villas. It is worth noting that the hotel welcomes guests from May to October.





Dislocation

Only 300 meters from the sand and pebble beach is the Cretan Village Hotel Apartment 4 *. Crete, Agios Nikolaos, Amoudara, Main Street, 72100 - the exact address of this resort institution. Just 50 meters from the hotel there is a bus station, from where you quickly get to Elounda (12 km), Spinalonga island (14 km), as well as Heraklion (70 km).

Description of guest apartments

If not only comfort is important for you, but also homeliness and local flavor, then you will certainly like the guest rooms that Cretan Village Hotel Apartment 4 * offers. It is worth noting that the design of the rooms is thought out to the smallest detail in order to create a warm atmosphere of the Cretan home, as well as to satisfy all the needs of modern travelers. You will be offered the following options:

  • Cozy one-bedroom villas are perfect for a romantic getaway. Here, two guests will comfortably accommodate, and there is also the possibility of installing two additional places. The living room has two soft sofas, a fireplace, a dining table and a kitchen corner. The bright bedroom has a huge four-poster bed.
  • Three bedroom villa can accommodate up to 6 people. Unlike the previous category, there are two large bedrooms with king size beds. The bathroom is equipped with a bath.
  • If you love traveling with your family, then pay attention to family rooms. There is an adult and a children's bedroom, as well as a full kitchen with all the necessary appliances for cooking. There is also a small fireplace room with cozy upholstered furniture.

General impression

At the first acquaintance with the resort complex Cretan Village Hotel 4 * (Ammoudara) the word "hotel" does not come to mind. This house is warm, cozy, welcoming. Naturally, such an atmosphere can be attributed to the traditional Cretan interior and architecture, but do not forget about the people who work here. The family owning this hotel literally puts their soul into their brainchild. The hostess personally supervises the work of subordinates, and also ensures that the guests do not need anything. Perhaps the only thing that staff can be blamed for is slowness. But even this primordially Greek trait can be considered part of the local flavor.

Cretan Village Hotel Apartment 4 * (Lassithi) is an ideal place for those who are looking for peace and tranquility. This is due to several factors. Firstly, there are no playgrounds and other infrastructure on the territory that would attract families with small children who would disturb the peace of other guests. Secondly, there is no animation, nor any opportunities for fun and entertainment. In addition, after 10 pm, the hotel does not have a single service (including a restaurant and bar). But this does not mean that you have to be bored. After enjoying the silence, you can go to the territory of the neighboring five-star hotel, where concerts, discos and other entertainment events are held every evening.

Perhaps one of the most serious shortcomings of the hotel is its remoteness from urban infrastructure. Therefore, this option is most often chosen by tourists with a car or those who like to walk. In addition, older people, as well as those who have health problems, should be careful about this accommodation option. Be prepared for the fact that every day you have to go down and climb the steep stairs many times (unless, of course, you plan to spend days in your room).
