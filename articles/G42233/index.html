United Arab Emirates Hotels with Private Beach: Top Four

Hotels with a beach in the UAE - the embodiment of a true fairy tale of the East, a demonstration of wealth and abundance. Local history is closely intertwined with modernity: objects of ancient architecture and modern buildings of water parks and zoos, eternal deserts with their indigenous Bedouin inhabitants and the luxury of modern cities.

Terrain and climate



The United Arab Emirates is a small but many-sided state. The local nature and landscape are very diverse: a large territory of the UAE is occupied by deserts (stone, sand), here you can see oasis islands and quiet wadi - river beds that have dried up long ago, sand dunes smoothly turn into rocky peaks of Al Hajar, where lakes hide from crystal clear water. The local climate is dry tropical. Sandstorms are common and rains are rare. The period from October to May is the best for a trip to the UAE. Hotels with their beach will present high-class service, and nature will favor moderately hot midday air and pleasant evening coolness.

Four of the best hotels

Tourists traveling on vacation to the Emirates should know that UAE hotels with their own beach are a frequent occurrence. They come in different gradations, ranging from five-star to three-star. Below are just some of the UAE hotels with their own beach, but rest assured, the best.

Burj al arab



The Arabian Tower is a place that has concentrated all the chic and luxury of the UAE. The sail hotel is located on the sea on an artificial island, connected to the land by a bridge. All hotel rooms have a high level of comfort, and the view from the window leaves the impression of a floating ship. Vacationers can have a wonderful time in the beauty salon, organize a conference or a seminar in the business area with free Wi-Fi, enjoy the wonderful pool, jacuzzi, Russian, Finnish and Turkish baths.

Jumeirah beach hotel

Distinctive features of this hotel complex are elegance and originality of style. The hotel has 538 rooms and 19 villas with terraces designed in Bedouin style. Countless restaurants and bars, entertainment for every taste for both adults and children - the Jumeirah Beach Hotel has it all.

Royal Beach Resort & Spa

Surprisingly, in the UAE there is another hotel, one of the five best in the world. The comfortable rooms with a terrace include a DVD player, plasma TV, coffee maker and safety deposit box. The hotel has a rooftop pool where anyone can enjoy stunning views of the blue abyss of the ocean. A golf course, tennis courts, a Russian bath - all this is at the guests' disposal. The territory of the Royal Beach Resort & Spa is guarded; no outsiders are allowed to enter the beach area.

Jumeirah Zabeel Saray Dubai



The hotel is decorated in Ottoman style, so every vacationer will be able to plunge into the atmosphere of the Ancient East, feel all the splendor and experienced power of the Sultans. The hotel complex has seven restaurants, a spa, tennis courts and several basketball courts.

Hotels in the UAE with a private beach are an excellent place to relax. The cleanliness, order and excellent attitude of the staff, as well as an unusual range of services - all that a visitor can expect from a vacation. A variety of UAE hotels with their own beach - a guarantee of upscale relaxation! We wish you a pleasant and memorable trip!
