Pinsk hotels: overview, description, reviews

Pinsk is a very unusual and beautiful city located at the confluence of the Pripyat and Pina rivers. The real cultural capital of Polesie is not too big and very welcoming. But even in this small village there are very comfortable hotels for both budget tourists and wealthy people.

List of the best hotels in Pinsk

There are 4 best and most popular hotels in Pinsk:

  • "Pripyat."
  • "Sport".
  • "Wave".
  • Aelita.

All Pinsk hotels offer many free services, as well as comfortable and spacious rooms. Here guests are always welcome and ready to provide them with comfort and coziness.

Hotel "Pripyat"

Hotel "Pripyat" in Pinsk is considered the most expensive and elite. The minimum cost of a room for 1 person is about 1,900 rubles ($ 27). The hotel has more than 200 different rooms for every taste.

The price of any of them includes:

  • parking (with round-the-clock security);
  • free internet in the entire hotel;
  • safe (right in the room);
  • buffet (early breakfast only).

The room has everything you need - a double bed, a balcony, a TV, a wardrobe, bedside tables, everything for a shower and so on.

Room rates also depend on citizenship. For Russians and Belarusians, prices are lower than for foreigners from Europe.

For wealthy guests there is a luxury suite. This room has two large rooms and one double bed, as well as two bathrooms and a jacuzzi in the bathroom. The cost of such apartments is the same for foreigners and Russians - 5,300 rubles ($ 75) per day.

The hotel also has a restaurant and cafe. For special occasions and business meetings, you can pre-book a banquet hall with a capacity of up to 50 people. The hall is decorated in the style of the 18th century, which makes the environment more comfortable and solemn.

Hotel Sport

Another good hotel in Pinsk, located in the city center. It was built in 2004. The hotel can accommodate more than 160 people in 64 rooms. The average cost of a double room is about 1,000 rubles ($ 15), and a single room is not more than 800 ($ 12).

In addition to free internet and parking, any guest can order:

  • Banquet in the restaurant at the hotel;
  • conference hall;
  • Breakfast, lunch and dinner in the restaurant.

In addition, by contacting the phone in the room with the administration, you can order a ticket for the plane, to the theater or call a taxi. There is no need to pay for this service at the hotel. Only the ticket itself is paid.

Attention! Breakfast is not included in the full room rate, they are paid separately.

The hotel has single and double rooms, as well as suites with more spacious rooms and improved interior items.

In single rooms, shower, toilet and bath are common areas. They are located on the floor. The room has only a bed, TV and desk.

Hotel Aelita

Hotel Aelita is located a 5-minute walk from the main train station. In addition to the free Internet (which is sometimes not), all services are available at an additional cost. Breakfast is also not included.

Aelita has three floors and only 15 rooms - standard single, double and several double suites. Triple rooms in the Pinsk Hotel "Aelita" are not available.

The cheapest and easiest room is sure to have:

  • bed;
  • TV;
  • a wardrobe and a small bedside table;
  • shower, bathroom, sink;
  • radio;
  • table and chairs;
  • telephone.

The cost of a single room varies from 1,600 to 1,750 rubles ($ 23 - $ 25).

In the suite, in addition to all the above items, there is also a safe and a refrigerator.

Twin rooms have twin beds. Separate beds are also available in suites and ordinary rooms.

Important! If a person has already booked a room in the Aelita hotel and for some life circumstances could not arrive, he needs to notify the administration no less than 24 hours in advance. Otherwise, the advance payment will not be refunded, and the visitor will have to pay the number and all booked services.

Reviews of hotels in Pinsk

The most laudatory reviews of visitors can be found about the Sport Hotel. Guests praise the staff and are quite happy with the comfort of the rooms. The complaints are caused only by periodic disconnection of the Internet.

Visitors also leave excellent reviews for the Volna Hotel. They like clean and comfortable rooms, the level of service, friendly and helpful staff. Guests expect to arrive at this place again upon arrival in Pinsk.

But about the hotel "Pripyat" guests respond unflattering. Residents complain about old repairs, dilapidated flooring, hard and uncomfortable beds. Food according to guests also leaves much to be desired.

The same opinion remained and guests of the hotel "Aelita". According to them, all rooms need to be repaired for a long time, they have terribly creaky beds and uncomfortable mattresses. All bedding in dirty stains. The railway is very close, unpleasant sounds of a moving train are constantly heard. Guests complain about the poor isolation of the rooms, because of which the voices from the next room are perfectly audible.

Despite all the shortcomings in Pinsk, you can find a decent hotel in which guests can relax in comfort.
