Phu View Talay Resort 3 *, Pattaya, Thailand: hotel description, tours, reviews

Pattaya is one of the most popular resort towns in Thailand, located in the southeast of the country. It is located on the sandy sunny coast of the Gulf of Thailand. For tourists, this is truly a paradise, everything at this resort is designed for a comfortable stay. Here you will find a large number of hotels in different price categories. Particularly attractive for travelers is Phu View Talay Resort 3 * (Pattaya). And now we‚Äôll talk about this hotel in more detail!

Description

The eight-story building Phu View Talay Resort 3 * (Pattaya, Thailand) is located in a quiet secluded area in the south of Pattaya, against the backdrop of Thai nature. Nearby (300 meters) is Cozy Beach, walking can be reached in 5-10 minutes.



The city center is a ten minute drive, there is a developed infrastructure. The city is famous for the abundance of entertainment centers, discos and other entertainment venues. Mostly Pattaya lives a nightlife.

And the hotel itself was built quite recently (2010) and offers its visitors modern comfortable rooms.

Interior

Since the Phu View Talay Resort 3 * hotel, reviews of which will be presented in this article, is a new building, the design in it is quite modern. The hotel itself is very clean. The floors in the lobby and in the rooms are tiled.

The hotel has sixty-four rooms, which are in different price categories. The rooms are bright, well-kept, with a balcony. The rooms have either one double bed or two single beds. Each room has a large bathroom with a modern shower and a toilet.

The air conditioner is located in each room and has a control panel; if you wish, you can choose a temperature regime that is comfortable for yourself.

They also offer satellite TV, which has 4 Russian-language channels, a refrigerator and a telephone. If you wish, you can rent a safe that will bring you into the room. Guests are provided with clean towels.



The rooms are cleaned regularly, they are kept clean.

Nutrition

Phu View Talay Resort 3 * every morning invites living guests for breakfast, which are held in the dining room. A large buffet is set for up to seventy-five people.

There is also an open-air restaurant nearby. In this restaurant you can not only taste delicious traditional dishes of the country, but also watch their cooking.

The kitchen is open to guests, and local chefs are happy to show their skills. Nearby is a bar with various drinks from all over the world.

The bartender-Thai will prepare any cocktail: both non-alcoholic and alcoholic. In order to visit a night club, it is not necessary to go to the city. On the territory of the hotel Phu View Talay Resort 3 *, which is not so difficult to find on the map, there are not only night clubs, but also bars with different themes.

Service and maintenance

The hotel building has Wi-Fi. Room service is available. Also on site there is a laundry, underground parking. If necessary, you can use the services of an exchange office.

For those who are used to driving cars, there is a special service for their rental. If you wish, you can go to the hairdresser, which works on site.

You can swim not only in the sea, because there are well-equipped pools with sunbeds. For younger visitors there is a separate pool in which there is a little water with a temperature of 28-30 degrees.



Service is at the highest level. If you came to rest with a small child, then they will put a cot for the baby in your room.

And one cannot fail to mention the famous Thai massages. Massage services are provided upon request to all visitors to the hotel.

Why visit Phu View Talay Resort?

The main criterion for the attractiveness of this place is the low prices for service and hotel accommodation. The vast majority of staff speaks Russian.

The velvet season for visiting is considered to be the winter period, but the air temperature at this time does not fall below thirty degrees of heat. At this point, the thermometer keeps throughout the year.

Pattaya is considered a night city. It is usually noisy there at night. If you settle in a secluded, standing on the background of nature Phu View Talay, then the noise at night will not bother you. Built not so long ago, the Phu View Talay Resort 3 * hotel, however, is indicated on the map, as it is the most visited hotel in Pattaya.

The beach, located not far from your location, is quite clean and comfortable, although not quite large. Here you can rent a sunbed and parasol. This, of course, for some money, but is relatively inexpensive. Comfortable temperature in the resort period, short rains that refresh the hot climate of this resort, fell in love with tourists.

Phu View Talay Resort 3 *: reviews

The comments of tourists who have been here are quite diverse.




Many write that this is the hotel with the most affordable prices among all such hotels in Pattaya.

Workers are greeted very friendly and courteous. The path to the sea takes five minutes. Uncomfortable when going to sea is the road, the descent and rise are very steep. But these inconveniences, like the narrowness of the beach, are compensated by a very clean and warm sea. Breakfasts are delicious and hearty, although not varied. On the way to the sea you can visit the cafe.

Some tourists did not like the rather poor equipment of the rooms, the small and shallow pool. It was annoying that there were no kettles in the rooms, and the hairdryer had to be rented at the reception for a fee.

Many write comments that describe both the good and bad aspects of staying at this three-star hotel.

For example, the hotel area is relatively quiet and calm, in contrast to the bustling center of Pattaya. In this fact, of course, there are advantages and disadvantages. Advantages - quite calmly at night, disadvantages - far from the center.

Walking to the center for about thirty minutes, but it would not be so inconvenient if it were not for the steep winding road.

Usually people come to a hotel only to spend the night, because in the evening there is absolutely nothing to do there, it can be boring for young people.

Undoubted advantages: rooms with a good area, clean, comfortable. The furniture is new. Laundry, which is located not far from the hotel, perfectly launches and ironed linen literally for a penny. The hotel staff are very friendly. As soon as you enter the hotel building, bags are immediately taken from you and taken to your room.

They clean the hotel every day, towels, shampoos, soap, toilet paper are brought daily. The only bad thing is that they bring one towel, but this is not very convenient.

Good budget hotel

According to the reviews of tourists who visited this hotel, it may be suitable for different categories of travelers. The staff tries to provide the most comfortable conditions for all categories of citizens who have come to rest.

The proximity of the beach impresses not only people who come for a family vacation, but also young people who like to frolic in the water.

The hotel accepts tourists throughout the year!
