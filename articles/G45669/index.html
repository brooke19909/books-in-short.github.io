The adapter to the motoblock will give work more use

The motor-block for agricultural work is an irreplaceable thing, especially in a large farm. There is work all year round on the farm, in the country, in the communal institution for such a tireless assistant. Not surprisingly, the demand for this technique is increasing year by year.





A zealous owner, acquiring such a device for his needs, will undoubtedly make sure that the unit is multifunctional and does not stand idle. This will require a lot of additional attachments, and its installation will significantly simplify the adapter on the walk-behind tractor. It is this unit that is the link connecting the walk-behind tractor and additional equipment: potato diggers, hills, plows, plane cutters, harrows, etc.

It turns out that this valuable device turns a simple walk-behind tractor into a mini-tractor and at the same time significantly expands its capabilities. You should understand this: first, the adapter is installed on the base, and only then various attachment mechanisms are attached to it. Replacing one type of equipment with another does not take much time.

The trolley adapter for a walk-behind tractor is a mechanical device that consists of a frame and a seat, a pair of wheels and a clutch unit.





Having such a thing, you do not have to manually direct the walk-behind tractor, sometimes making considerable efforts. It will be possible to control the unit with the help of a lever, sitting on the operator's seat. All equipment involved is in front of the eyes of the employee. The ability to work while sitting allows you to save a lot of strength and health.

The adapter to the walk-behind tractor makes it easy to carry out many land cultivation and land cleaning operations: digging and cultivating, hilling, spraying, weeding plantings, digging potatoes, and cleaning garbage and snow. To it you can pick up a trolley with a body for transporting goods. To increase the number of simultaneously used implements (a pair of plows, ridgers, etc.), a double universal coupling is used. The gauge width is adjusted for ease of use. The adapter to the walk-behind tractor is equipped with a special pedal, thanks to which the attachments can always be raised above the ground if you need to make a turn or move from place to place. Some models have a telescopic drawbar. The wheels are usually mounted powerful, equipped with ball bearings.





The adapter to the walk-behind tractor can greatly simplify the work of the digger, especially when large sections are to be processed. The variety of these units together can satisfy the existing demand, you can choose a mechanism for almost any walk-behind tractor. However, this simple device is quite expensive, so some of the farmers - for all trades of craftsmen - are wondering whether to make an adapter to the walk-behind tractor with their own hands. By and large, this task is quite feasible, although it will require certain skills, a sufficient amount of materials, special drawings that can be found without problems.
