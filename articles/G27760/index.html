Moskvarium: visitor reviews. Oceanarium at VDNH

On August 4, 2015, a new aquarium was opened in Moscow. The first visitors to this grandiose object were Vladimir Putin and Sergey Sobyanin.





High-ranking guests visited each of the halls of the aquarium and praised all the work carried out in it.

Grand construction

The official opening of the new unique facility took place on 08/05/2015. All previous work on its creation and bringing it to the level necessary for normal operation required the expenditure of a large amount of efforts of the city authorities. The working name that the aquarium received is Moskvarium.

The grandeur of the object, first of all, lies in its scale. Throughout Europe, there is no such type of structure that could be comparable in size to the Moscow Aquarium. Grandiose and the filling of the halls. Located a few thousand kilometers from the waters of the oceans, Moskvarium holds 25 million liters of water, in which 700 tons of sea salt are dissolved. The imagination and the number of attractions of the aquarium are amazing. In addition to numerous sea and river inhabitants, here you can see belugas, killer whales and dolphins, which not all such objects can boast.



In order to feed all the inhabitants of the oceanarium, a lot of food is required. It is purchased with the proceeds from the sale of tickets, as well as government funding.

Location

Moskvarium, whose address is Moscow, Prospect Mira, d. 119, is not difficult to find. The building of this aquarium is located at VDNH behind the Oil pavilion, between the Theater and Garden passages.

Even knowing what the Moskvarium address is, it is better to get to it using the simple route description below. From the metro station "VDNH" you should go to the main entrance to the exhibition. Further, the path will pass along the alley up to the square on which the aircraft are located. A rocket installed in front of the Cosmos pavilion can also serve as a guideline. Next, turn left. The oceanarium is located after a hundred meters.

Those who decided to visit the Moskvarium, how to get there faster from the main gates of the exhibition? You can take a branded train. He will take you to the aquarium, making two stops along the way - at the South and Khovansky entrances.

Internal organization

Moskvarium in Moscow is a center of marine biology and oceanography. In this building there are 80 aquariums, in which 8,000 different marine animals live, gathered here from different parts of the world. Among them, almost five hundred species of various fish.



The building of the Moskvarium is a whole complex that can be divided into three zones. The first of these is the Aquarium, the second is a Dolphin Swimming Center. In the third zone is the Main Hall, designed for performances by circus artists and marine animals.

Get to know better ...

There is a touch-pool zone in the Moskvarium. Here, some underwater inhabitants such as small needle tails and starfish can be touched by anyone, without raising them to the surface. According to visitors' reviews, not only children, but also adults love this area. Everything is provided for the convenience of the kids. They can stand on stools to easily put their hands in the water. So happens their maybe first acquaintance with wildlife.

Work of the Center specialists

Each of the visitors is amazed at the volume of the water world collected in the Moskvarium. And, of course, maintaining all this huge economy at the right level is worth a lot of effort. Temperature and technical conditions of aquariums are strictly controlled by automation. Forty-seven engineering systems are continuously operating in the complex. They purify air and water. But, despite this, the technical specialists of the oceanarium exercise additional control over all systems in manual mode. This is necessary in order to prevent any, even the slightest malfunction.

The state of the microflora of water is analyzed using a computer. At the same time, specialists take such samples manually. To maintain the salt state of the water each month, the aquarium uses 80 tons of salt.

Medical examinations of its inhabitants are regularly conducted at the Moskvarium. For this, the laboratory of the complex has all the necessary equipment. Using modern devices, specialists conduct gastroscopic, radiological and dental examinations of fish and animals. Bacteriological and cytological studies are conducted here.

Cost

Moskvarium sets ticket prices depending on the time and day of the visit. So, from Monday to Thursday for an adult you will need to pay 600 rubles., And for a child - 400 rubles. But this is only from the beginning of the opening of the aquarium to 16.00. After this time, Moskvarium raises ticket prices for adults by 200, and for children by 100 rubles. The same cost of visiting is saved from Friday to Sunday. Moreover, these prices are valid throughout the day.

Due to the large influx of people wishing to visit the Moskvarium, a discount coupon is not yet available. However, on weekends, the administration offers Muscovites and guests of the capital family tickets. Their action extends for three days (from Friday to Sunday). For 2500 rub. Aquariums can be admired by two adults and one child. There is another option for such tickets. For two adult visitors and two children you can give 3200 rubles. At the same time, children up to a year can look at the underwater world for free. But a child from a year to 12 years old can pass on a children's ticket.

For visitors of privileged categories provides Moskvarium discounts. So, for members of large families and category II invalids tickets are sold on weekdays until 4 p.m. for 300 rubles. After this time - for 450 rubles. There are benefits for WWII veterans and disabled people of group I.

All other citizens can only eat up that in the near future in order to visit Moskvarium at a lower cost, a discount coupon will be offered on the Internet. In the meantime, tickets at regular cost are sold at the box office of the aquarium or on its official website.
