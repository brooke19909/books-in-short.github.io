Packing check

The easiest way to understand how to distinguish the original "iPhone 7" from a fake is to look at the box and equipment.

Apple is known for its reverent attitude to detail. Developers are trying to keep branded elements in everything. Therefore, attentive users will probably notice inaccuracies in the package.

This box is made of thick cardboard. It has sharp corners. The logo is embossed. There is a sticker on the back of the box that contains all the information about the device.

In order not to go to court, many Chinese manufacturers provide different data. For example, they may write iPhone with errors. Here you can find a variety of variations: from iPhon to lPhone. In the latter case, the lowercase letter L is used instead of i.

Also, the main logo can be modified. For example, an apple is bitten on the other hand. Some display a pear. In general, each manufacturer, as it may, is twisted. The most accurate copies are usually obtained by identical labels on the sticker, and dense quality packaging. In this case, it remains to look inside.

Package Check

Apple's packaging is almost never changed. Some serious changes are always mentioned at the presentation. Therefore, it is easy to trace and check the box for the presence of required components.

So, there is always a charger inside, and the Lightning power cable is supplied with it. Also, the user can use the original headset. Wireless headphones are now especially popular. In a special envelope is the accompanying documentation. There is a paper clip to remove the SIM card tray.

Separately, you can consider accessories. They are made of durable plastic. They should not be scratched, burrs, cuts or other defects. The power cable should be soft. Each component is neatly packaged. Now the company is practicing the supply of elements in separate boxes.

Visual inspection

How to distinguish an original iPhone from a fake? In this case, it all depends on the specific model. It is important to understand the difference in smartphone modifications. Details and differences need to be found. But there are common elements by which you can recognize the replica.

All parts of the original device fit tightly. There can be no backlashes and creaks. Gaps are also excluded. The body of the device is monolithic. It is impossible to disassemble, remove the cover, remove the battery, etc.

You can understand a lot from the buttons. They should clearly respond to pressing, not play and do not creak. There are some points that fake always showed:

  • removable battery;
  • the presence of two SIM cards (with the exception of new items in 2018);
  • the presence of a slot for a memory card;
  • micro USB connector instead of Lightning;
  • telescopic antenna.

Next is a closer look at the logo. It is made of stainless steel. It can not be fingered or torn off. It is reliably recessed into the housing and is part of it.

ITunes compatibility

As mentioned earlier, iTunes can help with the question of how to distinguish the original iPhone 5s from a fake. The program is proprietary and helps to synchronize smartphone data on a PC.

Questions about Headphones

As mentioned earlier, a careful inspection of the configuration can help determine the replica. Now all new iPhones come with the original EarPods headset. To identify a fake smartphone, it is enough to distinguish the original iPhone headphones from a fake. How to do it?

Some people think that just listening to the sound is enough to identify the original headset. Apple branded headphones are very high quality, they give a deep, clear sound and low bass.

Next is a closer look at the details:

  1. The original headset has a dense dark blue metal mesh inside the ear cushions. A fabric mesh is often used in a fake.
  2. There are no gaps between the parts of the headphones in the original. Fakes are usually made sloppy, so they have large gaps.
  3. The original headset has a marking of the right and left earphones. The fake does not have it or it was made incorrectly.
  4. The material is smooth and free from defects in the original.

Sometimes the manufacturer does not bother and does not even repeat the original form of ear pads. He does not attach too much importance to a quality wire, if it is a wired version. It is very thin and rough, due to which it can quickly crack or tear.
