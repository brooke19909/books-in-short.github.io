Tossa de Mar: reviews of the amazing resort of Spain

The cozy resort town of Tossa de Mar is located on the Costa Brava. Reviews of this seaside village indicate that this is an amazing corner of Spain, where you want to return after your first visit. The strength of his attraction lies in historical monuments, in contemporary color, in beautiful narrow streets. In addition, the city is located in a rather beautiful place among the grottoes, cliffs and bays. It is simply surrounded by greenery and is a great place for a full family vacation.



What else is attractive to Tossa de Mar? Reviews of many tourists say a lot of positive things about the beaches. The coastline of the resort stretches for 14 kilometers. It is divided into numerous small beaches, many of which are located in secluded bays. Among all, four main beaches can be distinguished. Central is considered urban - Gran, the border of which from the northern part is a rugged rocky stretch of coast. The beach is fully equipped for relaxation. There are many restaurants and cafes. In summer, there are many local holidays. There is another beach nearby - Reig. It is not equipped, therefore, those who seek solitude prefer to relax here.

Those who have already been vacationing in Tossa de Mar leave reviews about Codolar Beach, which is only 80 meters long. It is located almost in the center of the city. Despite the large number of holidaymakers, it has a Blue Flag. It provides excellent conditions for diving. A significant advantage of the beach is its protection from the winds. This attracts not only vacationers, but also lovers of fishing. Nearby is the pebble beach of Mar Menuda. All are equipped for a great holiday. There is a playground, cafes, attractions.



In addition to a beach holiday in Tossa de Mar, reviews mention many interesting places that are recommended to be seen during the holiday. The main attraction of the city is located in its southern part. This is the fortified city of Vila Vella, whose construction dates back to the 12th century. It currently houses the City Museum, which presents a wonderful collection of ancient Roman mosaics and unique works by Marc Chagall. It is recommended to visit the ruins of the ancient Els Ametllers villa, which were discovered during archaeological excavations conducted in 1914.

Many more sights can be seen walking around the city of Tossa de Mar (Spain). Photos of these places cannot fully convey their beauty and uniqueness. You need to see them with your own eyes. So, in the Old Town stands the church of San Vincent. It belongs to the most photographed place in the city. The structure of this structure dates back to 1755-1776. Initially, the interior was decorated in the Baroque style. After its destruction, the church was restored, but with significant changes. Only some elements of the apse with sculptures have survived, some of the fragments of pedestals, part of the spire of the church.



But this is not all that can be seen in Tossa de Mar. Attractions can be complemented by such a structure as the chapel of La Capilla de la Virgen del Socorro, which was built in the 16th century. The temple rises at the crossroads of two roads, one of which leads to Girona, and the second to Lloret. Bays of Pola, Lievado, Bon, Givarola are popular with tourists. It should be noted the governor's house. It houses the city museum, which is considered the first modern museum in Catalonia. The monastery of El Santario de San Grau, which is located in the mountains of Sadiretes, is also noteworthy. The construction of the building is made in the Gothic style. Around him are numerous legends.
