Dayi Diamond Hotel 3 * (Turkey / Alanya) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

One of the most attractive places for economical holidays in the Republic of Turkey is the village of Mahmutlar, located in the province of Antalya. There are many hotels here, prices at which even in the midst of the tourist season will pleasantly surprise travelers. One of these hotels is Dayi Diamond Hotel 3 *, which we want to tell you about. You will learn about what services are provided to its guests, what impression he left with the vacationers and what kind of entertainment you can count on here.





Where is the hotel located

Dayi Diamond Hotel 3 * is located in the resort village of Mahmutlar. The center of one of the largest tourist cities in Turkey - Alanya - 10 kilometers. The nearest Gazipasa airport is only 36 kilometers from Mahmutlar. Therefore, upon arrival here you can reach your hotel in less than an hour. Another major airport in Turkey, located in Antalya, is located 120 kilometers from Mahmutlar. Accordingly, it will take more than two hours to travel from it. Therefore, when planning your stay at Dayi Diamond Hotel 3 * (Alanya), try to find a flight to Gazipasa Airport.

You can get to the city from the hotel by local minibus (dolmushe). His stop is just a hundred meters from the hotel. The beach is also very close - literally a few tens of meters. However, in order to reach him, you need to cross the road.

Hotel Policies

According to the internal rules adopted by Dayi Diamond Hotel Mahmutlar 3 *, check-in at the hotel is carried out after two in the afternoon. If you arrived earlier than this time, you can leave your luggage in the left-luggage office at the reception and go to lunch or go directly to the beach. In the event that previous guests have already managed to move out of the room and the hotel staff has prepared it, you will be settled immediately. You must leave the hotel on the day of departure until 12 noon. Then you should pay for your accommodation and additional services, if you resorted to those. We accept both cash and plastic cards of the Visa and Mastercard systems . If you have already settled with a travel agency, then at check out from the hotel you need to pay only additional services.





Being a family hotel, Dayi Diamond Hotel 3 * allows children of all ages to be accommodated in its rooms. If you have a child under 12 years old in your room using existing beds, then his stay will be free. Toddlers under 2 years old will put a cot. For placing an older child or an adult in a room on an extra bed you will have to pay 70% of the cost. Maximum in each room can be set only one such bed. If you plan to use such a service, you must first coordinate this with the hotel.

As for pets in Dayi Diamond Hotel 3 * (Alanya), their stay on the hotel is not allowed.

Number of rooms

Dayi Diamond Hotel 3 * has 68 standard 2 and 3-bed rooms. Family rooms are also available. All are simply decorated, with a living room and a bathroom, a balcony. Each room has air conditioning, hairdryer, cable TV (including in Russian), telephone and mini fridge. Hotel guests have the opportunity to order room service, as well as use the minibar (for a fee).

Infrastructure

The 3-star Dayi Diamond Hotel in Mahmutlar has all the necessary amenities for a comfortable stay. So, here you can take advantage of dry cleaning and laundry services, leave valuables in a safe at the reception, order a babysitting or concierge service, rent a car or book an excursion that interests you. Also on site there is a currency exchange, a shop with souvenirs and everything you need to relax. Free wireless internet is available in all public areas of the hotel.

The hotel reception is open 24 hours a day, so if you check in late or in case of an unforeseen situation, you can always contact the reception on duty who will handle all issues. The hotel also takes care of the health of its guests. It has its own medical center where you can go in case of illness.

If you plan to come to Turkey by private car or rent a car here, then do not worry about parking. After all, the hotel has its own parking lot, the use of which is free for guests. You can also book airport transfers at the hotel.





Entertainment

The 3-star Dayi Diamond Hotel has an outdoor pool with a children's section. Near it, you can comfortably sit on comfortable sun loungers. In addition to relaxing by the pool and on the beach, you can play billiards, darts or table tennis. The hotel also has a TV room. You can also go diving or fishing for a fee. The hotel also has children's and adult animations.

Sea beach

The Dayi Diamond Hotel in Mahmutlar has its own sandy beach. Here you can use sunbeds, umbrellas and mattresses for free. There is a water entertainment center on the beach where you can rent a catamaran, scooter, ride a banana, etc.





Cost of living at the Dayi Diamond Hotel

As already mentioned, this hotel is positioning itself as an economical option for a great holiday in Turkey. Indeed, the price of accommodation in it will pleasantly surprise you. So, for a seven-day stay in a double room you will need to pay about 20 thousand rubles. This price fully includes food and some drinks.

Dayi Diamond Hotel 3 *: tourist reviews

For many people, when planning a vacation, it is important to clearly understand what awaits them at the chosen hotel. Therefore, we bring to your attention several generalized reviews of tourists who stayed at Dayi Diamond.

In general, most travelers think the hotel is well worth the money. So, very many appreciated the location of the beach - across the road, literally 150 meters from the hotel. Everyone liked the sand on the beach and the gentle entrance to the sea. This was especially welcomed by tourists who came to rest with young children.

Another indisputable advantage of the hotel is its friendly and helpful staff who are trying to quickly solve problems. Some employees speak excellent Russian.

As for food, here the opinions of tourists are divided. So, some vacationers believe that the food was quite acceptable for a three-star hotel: a fairly varied menu, all fresh, delicious pastries. The other part of the tourists was not satisfied. In their opinion, the choice of dishes could be diversified a little more. Also, many did not like that only watermelon was offered from fruits. Vacationers bought everything else at the nearest supermarket.



Some were a little disappointed with their hotel rooms. According to them, furniture and sanitary ware are far from new, and sometimes something works poorly. Others are sure that for the level of a three-star hotel everything is quite tolerable.

The largest number of negative reviews concerned housekeeping. Many guests were surprised that upon booking the hotel they were provided with information on daily room cleaning and regular linen changes. In fact, it turned out that if you want your room to be tidied up, you must inform the reception in advance.

The local animation made a good impression on holidaymakers. So, the smallest guests of the hotel danced with pleasure at the children's disco. Adult guests enjoyed the show program designed for them. There were various funny contests, and belly dancing, and a fire show, etc.

All tourists, without exception, appreciated the location of the Dayi Diamond Hotel. After all, it is located in the center of the village, close to a variety of shops. Also, near the hotel, a market unfolds twice a week, where you can buy fruits, vegetables and various sweets, as well as clothes and various souvenirs.
