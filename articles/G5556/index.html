Hotel Delfin Mar Complex 4 * (Santa Ponsa, Spain): photos and reviews of tourists

Santa Ponsa is a charming town in Mallorca. Its location is more than convenient. To the island's capital (Palma de Mallorca), take a bus for about fifteen to twenty minutes. And the town itself is very quiet, although not without the joys of civilization. There is a market, and shops, and restaurants, and cafes. But Santa Ponsa is not neighboring Magaluf. In the evenings there is quiet, chamber. The central part of the town is flat. The hills start a little off. The coast is also beyond praise. The surrounding hills overlook the sea and form a convenient bay called Cala Santa Ponsa. And in this wonderful place there are two hotels that are part of one complex - Delfin Mar Complex 4 *. What are these hotels, we will tell in this article. The main material for its writing was the reviews of tourists and their photos.



Location, territory, buildings

The hotels are located very close, literally thirty meters from each other. But their addresses are different. Hotel "Dolphin Mar" is located on King Sancho Street 11. And Hotel Delfin Siesta Mar is located on Lisboa Street 4. From all of them go to the sea for about three minutes or one hundred and fifty meters. And unlike other hotels in Santa Ponsa not on the stairs or the steep slope.

Delfin Mar Complex 4 * (Mallorca) photos are presented as typical resort hotels. Floor buildings are surrounded by a beautiful garden. Hotel Dolphin Mar is larger. It has two outdoor pools and one indoor heated. He eats a children's "paddling pool". The Dolphin Siesta Mar Hotel has only one seasonal pool - in the fresh air and without heating. Tourists say a lot of laudatory words about the territory of the complex. Although hotels are lonely buildings, there is no atmosphere of crowding. Balconies offer lateral sea views.

As a result, the trip drags on for an hour and fifteen minutes. But if you want to visit the party of Magaluf, you are on the 104th. Not far from the Dolphin Siesta Mar hotel is the Eroshki store, there is also a food department where you can buy ready-made dishes. Santa Ponsa is located on the southwestern tip of the island. Rooms Delfin Mar Complex 4 * (Mallorca, Santa Ponsa)

Let's start the description with the Dolphin Mar Hotel. In it there are only ninety rooms of one category. They differ from each other except for roominess. Several rooms are designed for three guests. Six rooms are connected by a door. They are suitable for those who come as a big family or company. Two more rooms are fully adapted to the needs of people with disabilities. All guest rooms have balconies overlooking either the garden or the pool. But the building is located so that from all rooms the sea is visible. Spacious, bright, comfortable bedrooms are equipped with air conditioning, LCD TVs (there is one Russian channel), a safe. The door of the rooms Delfin Mar Complex 4 * (Santa Ponsa) opens the electronic lock. There is a safe in the closet. Each room has a working space: a spacious desk, a reading lamp. Bathrooms include a hairdryer.

The four-story building "Dolphin Siesta Mar" contains one hundred and thirteen comfortable rooms. Most of them are designed for two guests, but there are rooms for single tourists and those traveling with the whole family. Each room has a furnished balcony, mini fridge, safe, satellite TV. In the bathrooms there is either a bath or shower.

Meals at the Dolphin Mar Hotel

By this criterion, the hotel is the best in the Delfin Mar Complex 4 * complex. Tourists are advised to take "all inclusive" in order to fully enjoy those culinary masterpieces that create a cook in the restaurant "Dolphin Mar". Weather permitting, breakfast and dinner are served on the outdoor terrace. Meals are buffet style. But there is a "trick" - an open kitchen. At the request of the client, the steak is fried from the selected piece of meat right in front of the tourist. If you eat according to the all inclusive system, then table wines, beer, mineral water and juices are free at all meals. With half board (breakfast and dinner) drinks are served in the evening, and their price is indicated on the invoice. Dishes are served a wide variety. Every week there is a gala dinner where you can simply swallow your tongue - such a feast for the stomach. Reviews say that even breakfasts at this hotel, although they always consist of the same set of dishes, are very diverse. Several types of fried eggs, sausages, baking. There are always a lot of fruits and vegetables.

Sea and beach

To the coast with two hotels Delfin Mar Complex 4 * (Mallorca) walk about two minutes. The coast of Santa Ponsa Bay is covered with soft golden orange sand. The coast is quite spacious (stretches for two kilometers) and wide. The beach is fully equipped by European standards. Rescuers are on duty, there is a first-aid post. The beach has a free toilet and shower. There are changing cabins. Although the hotels are almost on the first line, the beach does not belong to them, but because there are umbrellas and sunbeds paid. Sunset at sea is ideal for bathing children. The bay penetrates deep into the mainland, and therefore there are no big waves (at least during the swimming season). The winds are also moderate, only a light breeze accelerates the Mediterranean heat. The Dolphin Mar Hotel has two outdoor pools (one of them is for children) and an indoor heated pool. Around them are free sunbeds and umbrellas. Hotel "Dolphin Siesta Mar" was limited to one pool with a children's section. But next to it there is also an equipped sun terrace.

Services at Dolphin Mar Complex

Everywhere you will find a service corresponding to the class of four stars, regardless of which hotel you are in. Delfin Mar Complex 4 * provides its guests with many services. Business meetings and meetings often take place at the Dolphin Mar Hotel, because there are two conference rooms for forty and one hundred and twenty seats. And for tourists who come to rest, this hotel provides a gym, sauna, board games (including billiards), bike rental. At Dolphin Siesta Mar, guests are also not deprived of care. Jacuzzi, sauna, massage, gym, ping-pong, bocce - these are just a few of the long list of amenities that are provided at the hotel. For children, the Dolphin Mar Hotel has a playroom.



Delfin Mar Complex 4 * (Mallorca): reviews
