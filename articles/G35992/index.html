What threatens climate weapons

Climatic weapons are a complex of various measures that affect the air, earth and water of the planet. The influence exerted can cause devastating consequences at a selected point on the Earth. Climatic weapons are weapons of mass destruction that have tremendous power, which can wipe out not only a certain part of the planet, but also a whole continent. Climatic weapons can change the climate in a particular area, causing flooding or drought, it can destroy the soil, or cause technological disasters, affecting various industrial facilities.

But the creation and development of climate weapons is associated with a large number of restrictions. In order to have any effect on the climate of a certain territory, a tremendous amount of energy is required. Moreover, a possible effect can easily get out of control and turn against its creator.



Despite the ban by the international convention on conducting such experiments, research is still ongoing. Modern scientists say the impossibility of creating a controlled type of climate weapons.
