Hotel Sonesta Pharaoh 5 (formerly Melia Pharaoh)

Description: Sonesta Pharaoh Beach Resort 5 Hurghada - one of the old and well-established resort hotels in Hurghada. It is located eight kilometers from the airport and eighteen from the city center. On the vast (75,000 m2) garden area there are: the main building with rooms on the upper two floors, the Sport building next to sports fields and tennis courts, and a number of bungalows surrounding the pool. In this oasis of entertainment and relaxation, you can move around on electric vehicles.





Hotel Sonesta Pharaoh 5 consists of: 360 rooms in buildings and 98 apartments in a bungalow (76 of them with a living room and one bedroom, and 22 with two). Bathrooms feature hair dryers and bidets, and toiletries are replenished daily. There is also a minibar, the contents of which (except for drinking water) are paid.
