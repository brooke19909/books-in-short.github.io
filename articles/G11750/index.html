Openwork summer blouse (crochet): scheme and description of knitting

The approach of the warm season inevitably causes revival in the ranks of knitters. Their desire to replenish their wardrobe with beautiful things, to work with interesting high-quality yarn and to master new patterns is quite understandable. To create one of the most popular lace products you will need a diagram and description of a summer blouse crocheted.



What is a sweater, and what can it be

In fact, a jacket is called clothing for the upper body with a fastener passing through the entire height of the front part. In practice, both pullovers and tops are so called.

A classic summer crochet blouse (the diagram and description will follow) is made of cotton, linen, viscose or artificial yarn. Speaking about the use of non-natural materials, it is worth mentioning that acrylic is extremely undesirable. Products from such a thread do not heat in the cold season and become hot in the warm. If there is a desire to knit from synthetics, it is better to stop on microfiber or polyamide. At least they look interesting, since they imitate the appearance of silk thread.

The formation of a single piece of fabric from some simple pattern, the compilation of a product from individual motifs, or a combination of different techniques - any of these methods will work if a summer blouse is crocheted. The scheme and description for beginners are available in many magazines for needlework.

It is very convenient that the figure shows a wide range of sizes. Such a bonus is infrequent, and its special value is that it helps to properly increase or decrease the product. Often, the disadvantage of independent change is the violated proportions and the ugly silhouette of the blouse.

If desired, the length can be varied as you wish.

Making coquette

This model provides for the presence of a yoke, connected in a circular fashion with simple double crochets. Depending on the abilities of the craftswoman and her wishes, any other pattern can be used.

The first row is a chain of air loops, its length is equal to the volume of the roll. Next, you need to connect the row with columns with a crochet, making additions at twelve points at equal intervals. The expansion of the canvas should be performed in each row, shifting the points in a checkerboard pattern. If you add the addition of columns in the same place, you will get a circle of irregular shape and corners will be clearly visible.

Thus, you should continue to work, as a result, you will get a yoke perfectly round in shape.
