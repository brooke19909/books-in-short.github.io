How to sew a petticoat quickly?

How to sew a magnificent petticoat is most often recognized by those who, by occupation or hobby, are associated with rock and roll. Indeed, models with just such an element received another development in the 50s of the last century. And before that, in the 16th century, petticoats were used to shape ordinary products. With their help, the female figure received a thin waist and quite lush hips. In addition, the lush element of underwear in the best way brightened up the flaws of the silhouette.



Before stitching the petticoat, you need to choose the right material for it. Usually, cotton articles are taken for this, as they are cheap enough, silk, lace, netting or a combination of them will also work. In the latter cases, especially when using lace materials, the cost of the resulting product can be sky-high. The general rule is that the lighter the fabric of the main skirt, the stiffer the fabric of the petticoat.



After selecting the material you need to determine the length of the product. It can be shorter or longer than the skirt, if it is made of beautiful fabric or has spectacular ruffles. How to sew a petticoat so that the silhouette looks harmonious? To do this, it should not be very short (usually do not take a length of less than 0.5 meters for adult options for medium height). The width of the material in this case will be the minimum length of the future product.

If you want to know how to sew a petticoat correctly, then you should start with pre-treatment of the fabric. Cotton samples must be washed beforehand. Steam all fabrics to determine the possible shrinkage of the material. Next, the desired length is cut, and the resulting rectangle is folded with the front sides inward. Side sections are trimmed, after which they are joined together at a distance of two to three centimeters from the edge. The seam is heat treated with steam.



How to sew a petticoat quickly? To do this, you do not need to think about complex belt processing. It is processed into a hem, so that there is a space of one and a half centimeters for threading the gum (using a safety pin , followed by stitching the ends). In some models, you can make several seams for elastic bands located one above the other. The lower edge of the product is also processed with a wide hem for folding, in which for rigidity you can insert a fishing line or crossbar - a corset plastic strip.

How to sew a petticoat from a net? In principle, exactly the same as from ordinary fabric. However, if the net is stiff, then many craftswomen prefer to perform a lower skirt on a yoke, which does not create additional volume in the waist and hips. Such a petticoat should be shorter than the main product, because peeking mesh does not look aesthetically pleasing enough. If the model requires that the lower skirt of such a material be visible, then the hem should be decorated with a narrow strip of lace along the entire length.
