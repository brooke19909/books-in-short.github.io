Ilf Hotel, Prague - overview, features and reviews

Our compatriots can visit the Czech capital of Prague with different goals. Russians come here to study, on business trips, to be treated, or just to look at the sights of this beautiful old city. In any case, travelers arriving in Prague, of course, need, among other things, comfortable accommodation. You can rent quite comfortable rooms in this city, for example, at the Ilf Hotel. In Prague, judging by the reviews of tourists, there are hotels with more comfortable accommodation. But this complex for the unpretentious traveler, most likely, will suit very well.

Hotel Overview

This modern hotel belongs to the three-star category. Accordingly, the service is offered to guests here, albeit not as impressive as in the expensive complexes of Prague, but still quite worthy.

Settling tourists arriving in the Czech Republic at the Ilf Hotel begins at 2 p.m. Guests should leave the hotel at the end of their stay at 10-12 hours. Paying for various services, hotel guests have the opportunity, including using cards, including Visa.

If you want to rent rooms in this hotel, even those tourists who came to the Czech Republic with pets can. However, the administration of this complex must be notified in advance when booking a pet. The payment for the number in this case is likely to be increased.

Another feature of the Ilf Hotel (Prague) is that smoking is prohibited throughout. Cigarette lovers who check into this hotel need to go outside if necessary.

Hotel Location

To get to the Ilf Hotel, travelers should first go down the subway. Further, guided by the map, you need to get to the station "Budejovicka". The area in which this recreation center is located is called Prague-4.

Worth staying at this hotel is not too expensive. Moreover, many travelers consider its location very successful. The infrastructure of Prague near the hotel is developed simply superbly. In the vicinity of this hotel there are many shops of various specializations, cafes, restaurants, pharmacies, etc.

For example, directly opposite the hotel there is a large shopping supermarket. There is also a cheap Bill grocery store near the hotel. To the left of the hotel there is a street where many pubs work. If necessary, Ilf guests can reach the city center by metro in just 10 minutes.

Hotel rooms

For rent in the Ilf hotel only standard and superior rooms are available. The rooms of this complex are designed for one, two or three people. In addition to ordinary rooms, travelers are also offered specially equipped for the disabled. All apartments rented at the hotel include televisions, telephones and safes.

Outer clothing and suitcases hotel residents have the opportunity to place in a spacious closet. Of course, the hotel rooms have showers and toilets. Unfortunately, the hairdryer in the Ilf complex is not offered to guests. Not provided in local showers and a set of toiletries. The household chemicals intended for adoption of water procedures should be taken by guests of this hotel with them on the road from home.

Unfortunately, there are no refrigerators in standard rooms at this hotel either. Reviews of tourists about Ilf (Czech Republic, Prague) exist mostly good. But the lack of a refrigerator, many travelers, of course, are among the big disadvantages of this hotel. Moreover, it still belongs to the three-star class.

What is the opinion of the residents about the rooms

In most cases, tourists who have ever stayed at the Ilf Hotel rent rooms here are praised. The pluses of the hotel's rooms are travelers, first of all, their cleanliness. Maids carry out cleaning at this hotel quite often and carefully. In any case, judging by the reviews of tourists, there are no unpleasant odors in the rooms at the hotel.

The advantages of being handed over in this complex of rooms, many tourists, among other things, include the ability to independently adjust the batteries. In the winter season, this can be very useful.

On the minus of the rooms of the Ilf Hotel in Prague, some guests include the fact that there are no bedspreads on the beds. However, bed linen in this complex is usually offered to guests quite clean and fresh.

In addition to household chemicals, travelers who decide to stay in Prague at this hotel should take a boiler for a trip. Unfortunately, teapots in the rooms of this hotel are also not provided.

Hotel service

On the territory of the Ilf Hotel for the convenience of guests are equipped with:

  • laundry;

  • exchange office;

  • luggage storage.

The reception at this hotel is open 24 hours a day. Unfortunately, the hotel administrators speak Russian very poorly. Communicate with them better through an interpreter in the tablet. If necessary, in this hotel its guests can, among other things, use the WiFi network. The guests of the complex do not need to pay for this service.

In addition to Wi-Fi, if necessary, tourists at this hotel can also sit at computers. They are installed next to the reception. This hotel service is also free. True, there are usually a lot of people wishing to use it at a hotel.

Since no entertainment is provided for residents in this hotel, most tourists believe that it is most suitable for family travelers as well as senior citizens. On the sites of tour operators in the ratings of this hotel usually give 3-4 points out of 5 possible.

Hotel Feature: Meeting Rooms and Library

Businessmen, among other things, can rent rooms in this hotel for business events. Very often, at the Ilf Hotel (Prague), for example, the Czech Institute for Postgraduate Education organizes various kinds of medical courses and seminars. Students checking into the hotel at the same time, among other things, are made available computers with Internet access and a library of specialized literature.

In total, this hotel has 9 conference rooms with a capacity for 20-50 people. There is also a congress hall for 120 people. All such rooms in the hotel are equipped with video and audio equipment, as well as projectors and laptops.
