Hilton Sharm Waterfalls Resort 5 - guaranteed high level of relaxation

Description of the hotel. The modern frantic rhythm of life does not allow a person not only to relax and rest - he does not give a chance to even look around. The burden of worries pressing on the shoulders, gray everyday life and hurrying crowds of people only increase a person's desire to go on vacation and relax. Currently, lovers of sunbathing seek to go to hot and exotic Egypt. It is in the vastness of this country that a huge number of resort oases hides, where exhausted and tired travelers seek.





  Sharm el-Sheikh lives a vibrant and vibrant life, welcoming thousands of tourists every year. A huge number of hotels for every taste and every wealth are concentrated here. A luxurious and sophisticated hotel is the Hilton Sharm Waterfalls Resort 5, located just 20 km from the airport. Situated a few kilometers from Naama Bay, this hotel is famous not only for its proximity to this attraction: travelers from all over the world are attracted by an unprecedented tranquility, high-quality service and amazing artificial waterfalls. Here the whole atmosphere is saturated with relaxation and positive emotions.

The Hilton Sharm Waterfalls Resort 5 enclosures spread out in a slightly chaotic manner on hilly terrain with fantastic sea views. Each hull is like a white seagull circling near its personal sea - a fantastically curved pool.





Description of rooms. The comfortable apartments include 401 rooms. Standard rooms, deluxe rooms, junior suites, suite-room, and presidential room are available for booking. High-tech technologies go hand in hand with the development of room infrastructure: a constant attribute - a TV connected to satellite broadcasting, a small refrigerator, air conditioning, wireless Internet access, a minibar and coffee and tea making facilities - the Hilton Sharm Waterfalls Resort 5 administration has provided all to make the visitor feel at home. A tea / coffee in the room service is provided complementary. Depending on the category of apartments, the room can have either a shower or a bath.

Nutrition. What rest will be complete without an excellent healthy diet? Hilton Sharm Waterfalls Resort 5 offers travelers a buffet-style restaurant. Fans of exotic foods and those who are eager and not afraid to try new things can turn their eyes towards a Thai or Italian restaurant. Seafood fans will be delighted to have a delightful seafood restaurant on site.

Hilton Sharm Waterfalls Resort 5 also offers its customers various bars and cafeterias where you can not only cool off with refreshments and have a snack, but also relax.





Beach. Like numerous resort hotels of this level, this institution offers visitors a closed sandy beach, which is located at the foot of a hill. You can walk to it or go down on the funicular, enjoying the amazing view that opens up. Coral reefs dotting the coast are a favorite subject to study not only by divers, but also by ordinary lovers of marine life. On the beach there are not only umbrellas, trestle beds and towels, but also two bars that will allow travelers starving in water to eat and gain strength.

Additional Information. It offers visitors a huge amount of sports entertainment, separate pools for children and adults, a cinema, bicycles, massage, SPA center, sauna, hairdresser, babysitting services, free parking, a conference room, a gift shop, a hookah bar, laundry, services doctor, ATM, fitness center and much more.

Reviews An excellent hotel for a relaxing, comfortable and cozy family vacation, filled with harmony with nature - this is what visitors who have visited the Hilton Sharm Waterfalls Resort 5 say. Reviews of tourists who wish to spend a great time in a calm and relaxed atmosphere are imbued with sincere admiration for the beauty of the hotel and the quality of service . The part of travelers who hoped to find some unprecedented entertainment on the hotel territory is disappointed. The average rating for visitors was 4 out of 5.
