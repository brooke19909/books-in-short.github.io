How to put money in a bank at a percentage: conditions, interest rate, tips for a profitable investment

Every person who cares about their future at least once thought about passive income. Should banks be trusted?

After the frequent revocation of banking licenses and the periodic bankruptcy of financial institutions, the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãputting money in a bank at interest causes distrust among the population. Therefore, first, we will understand some of the nuances of financial security.

Today, there is such a thing as a guarantee of deposits. What does it mean? In the event that the license was revoked from the bank, its bankruptcy and other troubles, the state guarantees the depositor a refund. The insured amount of the deposit in 2018 is 1.4 million rubles. That is, payments on deposits not exceeding this amount will be made immediately. If the amount that is on the account is greater, first the depositor will be returned an amount of 1.4 million rubles. And the rest of the deposit can be received only after the banking property is sold. Please note that deposits opened in dollars or euros are paid in Russian rubles at the exchange rate of the Central Bank of the Russian Federation on the date the license was revoked or the bank was declared bankrupt.

What types of accounts are subject to state insurance?

These guarantees apply to the following types of deposits:

  1. Deposits
  2. Accounts opened on demand.
  3. Accounts linked to bank payment cards (including pension and salary projects)

Not subject to state insurance

  1. Accounts drawn up in foreign branches of the bank.
  2. Metal deposits.
  3. Savings books and certificates.
  4. Accounts that were opened on trust
  5. Bearer.

Do all banks participate in the insurance project?

Most banks of the Russian Federation work with a state insurance agent to protect the financial interests of their depositors. But, unfortunately, not all. The fact is that such cooperation is not a prerequisite for banking. The bank board itself decides whether or not to insure the deposits of its customers. As a rule, this is neglected by small financial institutions and banks that have recently appeared on the market.

How to find out if a bank participates in a deposit insurance system?

Getting this information is pretty simple. There are several ways to do this:

  1. By phone hotline.
  2. Clarify the information directly in the branch of the bank itself.
  3. View on the official website of the Central Bank of the Russian Federation in the section "Directory of credit organizations" (this is the most reliable source of information to date).

As you can see, if a bank participates in the insurance system, then there is no need to fear for the safety of funds.

What to look for when choosing a bank deposit

A deposit is a type of deposit that is opened for saving money and receiving passive income in the form of accrued interest. What should I look for before concluding a contract?

  1. Contract time. The longer the money is stored in the bank, the higher the income.
  2. Minimum deposit amount (the amount needed to open an account).
  3. Interest rate. It is she who is an indicator of the profitability of the deposit. The higher it is, the better.
  4. Interest calculation conditions. They can be attached directly to the main deposit amount, transferred to a separate account or credited on the day the deposit agreement expires.
  5. Type of interest income. The deposit rate can be fixed or floating. The fixed interest rate does not change throughout the term of the contract. Unlike floating. Such a rate can both increase and decrease. Depending on the situation in the financial market.
  6. Terms of early termination of the deposit. Unfortunately, situations in life are different. Therefore, it is very important to provide for the possibility of early termination of the deposit agreement: when and how to do this, what are the conditions, are there any penalties, etc.
