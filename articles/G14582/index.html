Azure Phuket Hotel 3 * (Thailand / Phuket, Patong Beach): description, photos and reviews of tourists

Thailand is the only country in Southeast Asia that has maintained independence, a country of a warm sea, bright sun and smiles. From the west, the kingdom is washed by the Andaman Sea, and from the east by the Gulf of Thailand. The tourist season here lasts almost all year round, and even though the rainy season lasts from May to November, there are always many tourists in Thailand.

The island of Phuket is especially in demand among travelers. Here tropical showers are much less frequent, their season lasts from June to October. Despite the rainfall, the weather on the island is always warm. The most favorable time for relaxation is considered to be the period from November to March. Water at this time is warmed up to +28 , air is up to +30 . However, prices for rest during this period are the highest. Those who want to save money need to choose a budget hotel with a minimum of services.





This article will focus on one of these hotels in Thailand - Azure Phuket Hotel 3 *. This place is considered one of the most economical hotel complexes on the island of Phuket, which is pleased to offer guests of the kingdom its services at very affordable prices.

Many Russian tourists opt for this hotel, since Thailand is a country where people do not come to spend a lot of time at the hotel. As a rule, a hotel here is only needed for an overnight stay, because the rest of the time tourists enjoy time spent outside it.

Brief information about the complex

Azure Phuket Hotel (ex. Sunshine) was built in 2011, but it was so called at the end of 2016 after the renovation.

The hotel is a six-story building with a rooftop pool and comfortable rooms equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. The hotel provides non-smoking rooms. A few minutes walk from the hotel is the most famous beach of the island - Patong, a market, many inexpensive cafes, restaurants and retail outlets. When checking into the hotel, a deposit of about 1800 rubles will be charged. (1000 baht).





Hotel Location

The hotel is located on one of the most popular tourist islands in Thailand - Phuket - surrounded by green nature, on the second coastline on the outskirts of Patong. The distance from Azure Phuket Hotel 3 * to the beach is about 800 meters (10-15 minutes walk). Phuket Airport is a 40-minute drive away. From Azure Phuket, many of the island's attractions can be reached on their own.

Number of rooms

In Azure Phuket Hotel 3 * (Phuket, Patong) 83 comfortable rooms of different categories. There are ordinary standard rooms (Superior), stylish rooms (Deluxe) and chic apartments (Family Rooms and Juinior Suites).





All rooms have TV, bathroom with shower, air conditioning, telephone, safe, refrigerator, hairdryer, kettle, iron (on request). Not all rooms have a balcony.

Service

The Azure Phuket Hotel 3 * offers an outdoor pool, a jacuzzi, a sauna, a 24-hour front desk, a laundry service, a shuttle service, motorbike and car rental, luggage storage, free Internet access, parking, and room service.





Nutrition

The existing food systems at the Azure Phuket Hotel 3 * hotel (Phuket): BB, HB, FB. The complex's restaurant serves international and Thai dishes daily. Breakfast in the restaurant is organized by coupons. If desired, lunch and dinner can be paid on site. Food is brought to the room for an additional fee.

Judging by the experience of many travelers, food at a hotel in the resorts of Thailand is the last thing to worry about. There is a huge variety of places where you can have a tasty and inexpensive meal. Even in supermarkets you can buy ready-made full-fledged dishes on substrates, fruit slices, pastries, and often there are good discounts on such products. You can also eat very varied and very tasty in the mackerel and food courts, on average dishes from 60 rubles. (35 baht). And another place where delicious and inexpensive food is sold is the night market. Here you can try many unusual things: from crocodile meat skewers to worms and grasshoppers.





For vacationers with children

Azure Phuket Hotel 3 * accepts children of different ages. For babies, a cot is available upon request. In the adult pool there is a section for young travelers. There is no children's playground and animation at the hotel.

What to do on vacation in Phuket

Many tourists planning their holidays in Azure Phuket Hotel 3 * are wondering what to do on vacation and what attractions and excursions to visit. Below we will consider the most popular places recommended for visiting.

Of course, the best thing to do on vacation in Phuket is to relax on a snow-white sandy beach, but a little relaxation on vacation also does not interfere. Patong gladly presents this opportunity. This area is famous not only for its three-kilometer beach, but also for its vibrant nightlife. Phuket, like all of Tai, is known for the sex industry, often there are noisy carnivals and gay parades, and nightlife is very vibrant and fun. Fans of such fun should definitely visit Bangla Road - here, with the sunset, the most unforgettable nightlife begins.

For fans of extreme sports, Patong offers to ride a bike, arrange a descent on the ropes or go on a trip on elephants. The tour ‚ÄúFlight of Hanuman‚Äù is very popular, river rafting, ATV riding, safaris are in demand.

The most popular excursions are tours to the Bounty Islands with clear water and white sand. These great beach destinations are great for diving.

The excursion to the islands of Phi Phi is very popular. It includes a visit to 6 islands, a tour of the cliffs, a Viking cave and a beach with wild monkeys.

Fans of shopping in Patong also have something to entertain themselves. At night, endless markets unfold here, where you can buy whatever your heart desires.

The cost of trips

Thailand is considered that country, the cost of trips to which can be much lower than a separate hotel reservation and airfare. That is why the bulk of tourists come here on holiday packages, although if you plan a long vacation, then, of course, it is worth comparing prices: it is likely that an independent trip to this Asian country lasting about a month will cost less.

Also, the price of tours to Tai much depends not only on the date of vacation, but also on the resort where you plan to go. The cheapest destinations are Pattaya and Bangkok, here you can often find last-minute tours at low prices. The sea, of course, in these cities is not as clean as on the islands, but the sights and nature are not inferior.

Prices for trips to Phuket and Samui are an order of magnitude higher than for tours to Pattaya, but despite the high cost, the islands are gaining more and more popularity with tourists every year. If you carefully examine the tourist market, then here you can find a cheap hotel and truly enjoy the beauty of the Gulf of Thailand.





For example, the price of a tour to the economical Azure Phuket Hotel (ex. Sunshine) 3 *, Patong, is approximately 50,000 rubles for 10 days for a double room, including breakfast.

It is important to answer that the main ticket price is the price of the ticket, and with an increase or decrease in the number of days of stay at the hotel, the price does not change significantly.

Reviews on Azure Phuket Hotel 3 *

Despite the fact that the hotel presented above has only three stars by European standards, reviews about it are pretty good. Many tourists vacationing here note that the hotel rooms are spacious, the furniture is not bad, the bedding is clean, and cleaning is done daily. The location is good: there is a market nearby where you can eat tasty and inexpensive, many shops and cafes. The staff is smiling and friendly. The hotel organizes sending on various exciting and interesting excursions.

Among the shortcomings of the complex, tourists usually highlight the lack of knowledge of the Russian language, poor and monotonous food, lack of fruit, good coffee, and local dishes. Breakfast is ordinary: scrambled eggs, salad, sausages, rice, toasts, eggs. And another big minus, which is often written in reviews, is the noise under the windows. Even at night, traffic on the road does not stop, the sound of cars and motorcycles prevents sleep.

Summarizing

Azure Phuket Hotel Patong 3 * (Thailand, Phuket) is a very modest and economical hotel, from which you should not expect too much.





It is ideal for unassuming people who plan to visit many attractions and excursions during their holidays and are not going to sit in their rooms. For people who like to look for cons or are used to relaxing in hotels of a higher category, the complex may not be suitable.
