Sharm Grand Plaza Resort 5 *, Egypt: hotel description, traveler reviews

If you are looking for a comfortable hotel on the Red Sea coast, then as one of the suitable options we suggest you consider the five-star Sharm Grand Plaza Resort.



Location, general information, photo

Sharm Grand Plaza Resort 5 * is located in the Nabq Bay area, 29 kilometers from the Old City and 26 kilometers from Naami Bay. Sharm El Sheikh Airport is just eight kilometers away.

The hotel was opened in 2006. It is built in traditional Arabic style and is located in a large beautiful green area. The housing stock of the hotel complex has 547 comfortable rooms located in several two- and three-story buildings. There are standard, superior, deluxe, family, junior suites and executive suites.

Dining at Sharm Grand Plaza is organized in an all-inclusive format. The hotel has several pools, sun terraces, restaurants, bars, sports fields, a spa and much more. The hotel complex offers its guests both a daily and evening animation program.

Sea

As our compatriots note in their reviews, the Sharm Grand Plaza Resort 5 * hotel (Sharm El Sheikh, Egypt) will not disappoint beach lovers. So, from residential buildings to the sea, you can walk in about 5-7 minutes at a leisurely pace. The hotel has its own fairly large sandy and very clean beach. There is a sufficient number of sun loungers. So there is no need to get up early in the morning and go ashore to take a sunbed, as is the case in some other hotels, here. There is also a bar on the beach where you can either order your favorite drink or have a snack if you wish. As for the sea, you can enter it both from the shore and from the pier. Not far from the coast is a coral reef inhabited by a mass of amazing creatures. Therefore, be sure to bring a snorkeling mask with you to enjoy the colorful marine life. It is also advisable to purchase special shoes so that when entering the water you do not injure your feet on sharp corals and stones.
