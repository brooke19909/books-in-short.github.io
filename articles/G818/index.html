Hotel Delphin Botanik World of Paradise 5 (Alanya, Turkey)

Description: Delphin Botanik World of Paradise 5 complex is very well located between the Turkish resort cities of Alanya and Side, near the village of Avsallar. Antalya Airport is about 90 km away. Six 5-storey hotel buildings stand in the middle of a large (90,000 m2) well-kept garden. The buildings were erected in 1988. The last time they were renovated recently, in 2009, this hotel boasts that it is located on the first line of the beach. Those who have children will especially enjoy the rest in these places. There is a lot of entertainment for young guests, there is an amusement park, water attractions and more.



Delphin Botanik World of Paradise 5 consists of: 617 rooms with private spacious balconies. Most of them overlook the garden, and a little less than half have a sea view. All guest rooms are air-conditioned, they have the latest model flat-screen TV, minibar (drinks are set daily), electric kettle and work desk. The interior successfully combines the touches of modern design and traditional Turkish design. A roomy electronic safe is responsible for the safety of things. The vast majority of rooms (563) are standard doubles. Six of them are able to accept people with special needs. The rooms are small - 27 m2, but very comfortable. There are 28 rooms with an adjacent door, for families of four adults. And finally, the services of the most demanding guests include 26 two-room rooms (54 m2), designed for five people. Maids clean every day, and linen is changed three times a week.





Meals: At Delphin Botanik World of Paradise 5, 6 restaurants and 9 bars open their doors to guests. In addition to the main dining restaurant, where visitors can order vegetarian or diet food, you can also visit a la carte restaurants for free (you need to take a ticket in the lobby). The widest choice of cuisine is provided: Italian “Dolce Vita”, Chinese “China house”, Japanese “Daitokai Teppenyaki”, Turkish “Ottoman”, fish restaurant “Blue Fish”, sushi bar. In bars (on the beach, by the pools, in the buildings) there is always decent alcohol, ice cream, soft drinks.

Beach: Delphin Botanik World of Paradise 5 - very close to the pebble beach. This is a two-level bathing place. Loungers with umbrellas are set closer to the sea, canopy tents are on the upper terrace. All beach equipment, as well as towels are provided for free use. The territory by the sea is very well-maintained: there is a shower, changing cabins, wooden flooring is laid to the sea through pebbles. Entering the water is convenient for children.





Additional Information: Delphin Botanik World of Paradise has private parking, which is free of charge for hotel guests. Several pools (there are children's, with slides) will replace the sea for you when it storms. In the SPA center you can relax in the hammam, pump up muscles in the fitness room and enjoy a marathon in the beauty salon. There is a cinema on site. The administration takes care of the evening leisure of the guests: regularly hosts shows, music concerts, discos. There are children's, teenage and adult animations. For guests with a car, the hotel provides free parking. You can book excursions, including to Luxor and the pyramids, without leaving the hotel. Experienced instructors will teach you to scuba dive.

Reviews: Impeccable cleanliness of rooms, funny but unobtrusive animators, a spacious, shady park, helpfulness of workers - this is how you can summarize the comments made by users who visited Delphin Botanik World of Paradise 5. Reviews also do not ignore the excellent food. Breakfast includes fresh juices and yoghurts. Especially touched guests with a variety of drinks from the minibar. Yes, and many regarded the free Wi-Fi as a significant plus of the hotel. Many tourists advise renting bicycles: the terrain is flat, the tracks are specialized, and Turkey is a very safe country for cyclists (and cyclists). Young guests were pleased with the visit to the mini-club.
