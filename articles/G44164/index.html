DIT Evrika Beach Club Hotel 4 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach): hotel description, services, reviews

What could be better than relaxing by the sea? Only if he is facilitated by a stay in a good hotel serving his guests on an all-inclusive basis. If thoughts of such a vacation creep in more than once, then it is worth thinking about a trip to Bulgaria. And to be more precise, then at the DIT Evrika Beach Club Hotel. What is interesting about it? And you can talk about this in more detail.





general information

DIT Evrika Beach Club Hotel is located in Sunny Beach. This is one of the most popular Bulgarian resorts. People go here for impressions. Life here is in full swing - you will not be bored. Although in the morning the resort is more or less quiet. But closer to noon, you can already see that a long 10-kilometer strip of sandy beach is actively filled with tourists. And in the evening, life is full of music, lights and carelessness.

The hotel is located in the northern part of the resort, in close proximity to the sea (3 minutes to go). And the beach area at the hotel is private, by the way.

The airport is relatively close - in Burgas (approximately 35 kilometers distance). From there, Sunny Beach can be reached in various ways. For example, getting on bus number 10. This is the most economical option.

General services

"Eureka Beach Club" is a hotel in which everything is done for guests. Everything has been created for their comfort. There is free Wi-Fi, private car parking, and the reception is open 24/7. Guests can take their things to the laundry and dry cleaning, where they are not only washed, but also ironed. And in the lobby there is a left-luggage office and a currency exchange. Guests who were at the hotel are advised to change money there. The course is profitable.

The hotel also has a small business center with fax and photocopying, car rental, shops (among which there is still a souvenir), a hairdresser with a beauty salon. Press services are also provided.

Especially pleasing is the fact that the hotel has a Russian-speaking staff. The hotel also speaks Bulgarian, German, English and French. Therefore, none of the visitors should have a language barrier.

Other entertainment

In DIT Evrika Beach Club Hotel, in addition to the water park, you can still do sports. There are all the necessary conditions and opportunities for this. There are several modern outdoor venues - several of which are designed for playing volleyball, soccer and boccia. This is on site. But there is also a beach volleyball court - it can be found on the beach.

In addition, the hotel has a gym with modern equipment, and a wellness center. There you can enjoy a sauna, quality deep massage, a jacuzzi and a bath.

The hotel also has animation staff, constantly entertaining guests, there are darts, billiards, table tennis, a games room and karaoke. For children - a separate playground.





Restaurants

In DIT Evrika Beach Club Hotel 4 * there are several excellent establishments in which guests can satisfy their hunger and thirst. The first step is to talk about Main Restaurant. It serves its visitors an all-inclusive system and serves European, Mediterranean and Bulgarian cuisine here. By the way, many dishes are prepared right in front of the visitors. The restaurant is large, designed for 330 people, so there is enough space for everyone.

There is also a thematic institution in which guests are served Asian dishes. The restaurant is rather big - it can accommodate 160 visitors inside, and 80 on the terrace. By the way, it is located next to a private beach and a pedestrian alley. And it is here that you can order a romantic dinner in the open air (exclusive offer).

The next restaurant is called Bulgare. As you might think, looking at the name, it prepares dishes of Bulgarian cuisine. And here you can try really unusual, traditional delights for this country. For example, stuffed peppers, puff pie, kapamu, kavarma, fish and meat dishes made on the grill, and national sweets.

The last restaurant in the DIT Evrika Beach Club Hotel 4 * is Italian, called Bella Vita. It has a terrace with panoramic views of the pools. The menu is varied, here you can order everything - salads, antipasti appetizers, spaghetti, pizza, seafood, meat, tiramisu, panna cotta and much more.





Bars

Naturally, in the DIT Evrika Beach Club Hotel (Sunny Beach) there are also establishments of this nature. The first thing worth noting is the snack bar. It is designed for 250 visitors, and is located next to the pool. There from 12:00 to 15:00 you can have a delicious lunch, or order an afternoon snack from 15:00 to 17:00. During the day, salads, sandwiches, hamburgers, pizza, barbecue, juicy fruits, desserts and drinks are served here.

There is also a lobby opposite the reception desk. It offers hot and cold drinks, exclusive alcohol.

There is also an institution called Rainy Bar, with an internal space for 120 people. The terrace can accommodate an additional 150 visitors. This is a very cozy place - with a pleasant interior, a relaxing atmosphere and a whole library inside. There are often evenings of live music.

There is also a poolside bar and beach bar.





Apartment Options

The hotel has a fund of 633 rooms. All of them are divided into categories. There is a Family room, 2-seater "standards" and apartments for one person. The average price starts at 4,000 rubles per day. Cost may vary depending on seasonality. The cheapest thing is in late spring and early fall. But even this is a low price for accommodation in a 4-star hotel, which also has an all-inclusive service. Most of all, by the way, the hotel has 2-bed apartments. There are 461 of them in the hotel.

All rooms are attractively decorated. No frills, but comfortable. You can verify this by looking at the Family room photo provided above. The rooms have a large double bed, cable TV, a sofa bed, telephone, hairdryer, safe, climate system, a small refrigerator and a bathroom. In general, everything you need to relax. The only difference between the apartments is their area and the number of beds. Family rooms have the most.
