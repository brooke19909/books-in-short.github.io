Hotel "Mermaid 2 *" (Nimfa-Rusalka): service and reviews

Increasingly, Russian tourists choose Bulgaria for the summer holidays, namely the resort town of Sunny Beach. It is rightly called the Bulgarian Riviera for its unique natural environment, favorable climatic conditions, wide sandy beaches and countless entertainment facilities. The southern and northern beaches were awarded the Blue Flag certificate for cleanliness and grooming.





In Sunny Beach, a peaceful atmosphere harmoniously intertwines with an idle nightlife. This is a fairly energetic, rich tourist attraction resort. The main advantages of the city-museum are a developed tourism industry, convenient location with respect to outstanding objects and a huge selection of hotel complexes of different price categories.

Even those who do not have large fees will be able to stay in the cozy budget hotel “Mermaid 2 *” (Nimfa-Rusalka). A boarding house was built in the park area of ​​Sunny Beach resort, near the cities of St. Vlas, Burgas and Nesserb. It is proud of its unique nature, comfortable conditions and quality service. Within walking distance from the complex are discos, restaurants and shopping centers.

Accommodation





Hotel Nimfa-Rusalka 2 * (Bulgaria) is two residential buildings connected to each other. On the ground floor you will find a spacious and comfortable lounge with air conditioning, a 24-hour reception, a TV and upholstered furniture. There are 200 comfortable rooms for guests. Maintenance and cleaning are available at an additional cost.

Linen is changed every seven days. All rooms have balconies, shower rooms and air conditioning. A compact fridge and TV are also included. If desired, you can pay for the Internet and a safe.

Supply system

Tasty and varied food awaits tourists at the Rusalka 2 * Hotel. Nimfa-Rusalka serves local and European dishes in the hotel's restaurant. Tables are served in buffet style. On the territory there are several bars (one near the pool) and a cozy restaurant.





Coastline

The Rusalka 2 * hotel does not have its own coastal zone. Nimfa-Rusalka is located about five minutes walk from the municipal beach. The shore is clean, well-groomed and not crowded. The only negative is that all amenities are paid. The entrance to the sea is safe and even, the depth does not start immediately, which is very convenient for children.

In addition to passive relaxation, you can pay attention to outdoor activities such as scuba diving, catamaran or banana riding. There is a diving and windsurfing school on the coast. A sports equipment and boat rental center has been opened.

Infrastructure

According to guests, the hotel service is fully consistent with the 2 * category. Nimfa-Rusalka is more focused on an impartial tourist with low demands and a great desire for travel. A pool was built on the territory adjacent to the building, near which free awnings with sun loungers are exposed.





You can play tennis and engage in other active sports. You can always rent a car in a hotel and go on your own to get acquainted with the local surroundings. There is a cash desk for currency exchange, there is an ATM and a pay phone. Despite its low star rating, the hotel has shops, souvenir shops, a beauty salon and even a conference room.

There are play areas for children in the complex Nimfa-Rusalka 2 *. Reviews of people who have visited this sunny place are completely different. On some, the hotel made a positive impression overall: it was pleased with the food, cleanliness of rooms and a friendly atmosphere. But there were those who prejudiced the services of the complex, however, there were few of them.

We’ll warn you right away if you are expecting this category of luxury and chic from a tourist boarding house, you are unlikely to get it - we recommend that you find another place, more impressive. Well, those who want to pick up unforgettable and vivid emotions, visit interesting and informative tours for "ridiculous" money, made the right choice.
