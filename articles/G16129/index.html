Bellevue Apartments And Studios 3 (Crete), reviews

The seaside resort of Agia Pelagia spreads along the coast of Crete. In this abode of peace and tranquility, the cozy Bellevue Apartments And Studios 3 hotel complex, consisting of small villas, has settled down. It is ideal for relaxing romantic couples and lovers of peace and tranquility.

Hotel location

There is a car from the villa complex to the airport in Heraklion (the journey takes 30 minutes). The center of Heraklion is removed from them at a distance that can be overcome in 20 minutes. Guests can walk to the center of Aghia Pelagia from Bellevue Apartments And Studios, and the beach surrounding Ligaria Lagoon. A pleasant walk takes only a few minutes.





The hotel is surrounded by impressive sights: Cathedral of St. Minas and two museums (historical - The Natural History Museum of Crete, ethnological - Museum of Cretan Ethnology). One kilometer from the villas lies the wonderful beach of Mononaftis. Near the hotel there is a diving club Stay Wet. The complex is adjacent to the Knossos Palace and the amphitheater.

Hotel Description

Bellevue Apartments And Studios 3 (Crete) was built in 1987. The complex is formed by 18 comfortable villas. The villas were renovated in 2007. The hotel has a magnificent garden, a large outdoor pool, furnished with sun loungers with umbrellas, free parking, a mini market and a souvenir shop. Common areas are connected to free Wi-Fi.

Description of apartments

The rooms are decorated in a classic bungalow style. Guests are accommodated in two types of apartments - Studio Standard and Bedroom Apartment with spacious loggias or terraces. The rooms offer scenic views of Ligaria Bay, the garden and the mountain range.





The apartments have a kitchenette with a mini fridge, stove, kettle and kitchenware. The rooms are equipped with individual air conditioning, telephone and central heating. Rooms are equipped with cable TVs and safes. The bathroom has a shower, towels and a hairdryer.

Type of food in the hotel

The tavern restaurant at Bellevue Apartments And Studios 3 specializes in half board. In the morning, a rich continental breakfast is served , while in the evening, international and Greek dishes are served. Refined regional dishes are prepared with olive oil, which is produced in Crete. A large assortment of teas are prepared in the lounge bar.

Beach vacation

Bellevue Apartments And Studios 3 is located on the second beach line. The sea is 600 meters away from it. The beach stretches along the protected bay of Ligaria with emerald green waters. It is calm here, so the water in the sea is almost always calm, there are no huge waves on its surface. The sand and pebble beach is perfectly organized, with it there are many convenient calls to the sea.





Entertainment and sports

The range of entertainment at the hotel is rather meager. These are mainly excursions, beach and water sports. Tourists are engaged in windsurfing, scuba diving and parasailing. In the evening, tourists are attracted by thousands of lights that broke out above the taverns and bars of the town of Agia Pelagia, located next to the Bellevue Apartments And Studios 3. The music is pleasant in these establishments, and the dishes exude breathtaking smells.

The attention of travelers is attracted by the Konnos Palace, which has undergone a partial restoration. This is the most legendary of the palace complexes on the island of Crete, shrouded in the secret of ancient civilization and entangled in the intrigues of the great royal people who lived in it.

Tourists who can not imagine rest without a vibrant club and nightlife, go to Heraklion or Hersonissos in the evening. The dynamic life in the entertainment venues of these resort towns begins to fade when the dawn breaks.

In the center of the island lies the famous Lake Zaros with clear mineralized water. The brand of Zaros mineral water has become famous in all European cities. On the coast of the lake nesting ducks and geese. On the banks, in cozy corners, taverns have been added, where guests are pampered with excellent fish dishes.

A panoramic view of the marine life in two remarkable aquariums of Agia Pelagia is an incredible delight for visitors. In the fun water parks of Heraklion and Hersonissos with magnificent landscape design, an abundance of attractive water attractions and sparkling animated show programs.

Impressions of tourists about the hotel

From the analysis of reviews left by tourists, it becomes clear that the Bellevue Apartments And Studios 3 hotel complex is mainly used for overnight stays.





Some of the vacationers note that breakfast is monotonous, without any frills - sandwiches with ham, cheese, tea, coffee, milk and some fruit. Dinners are not served at all. Guests have two options - to cook on their own in a tiny kitchenette or eat in the nearest restaurants and taverns. Vacationers really like the food in the nearby tavern Eleven. It serves delicious Greek and international dishes.

According to vacationers, the main advantages of the hotel are its good location and inexpensive accommodation. Since tourists come to the hotel mostly just to spend the night, the lack of comfort in the villas few of them annoying. Having regained strength over night, they leave the hotel complex the next morning, going to the beach or excursions. Many vacationers rent a car and drive all day long to interesting places of the legendary island of Crete.
