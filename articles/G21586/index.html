Coral Beach Hurghada Resort 4 *: beach features, tourist photos and reviews

If you love the sea and always dreamed of admiring gorgeous coral reefs and bright exotic fish, then the Coral Beach Hurghada Resort 4 * hotel is exactly what you need.





What the hotel offers

Despite the fact that the main purpose of a trip to Egypt is to enjoy a sea holiday, the hotel's infrastructure is of no small importance. Coral Beach Hurghada Resort 4 * in this regard can offer its guests the following:

  • 4 large restaurants, one of which serves as the main dining room, and the remaining 3 are classified as √† la carte;
  • 5 modern bars where you can try a wide variety of drinks and have a light meal;
  • 2 clean spacious pools (one of them is equipped with equipment for heating water);
  • The conference room can accommodate up to 100 people. It has everything you need for business events. Also, at your request, hotel staff can convert the room into a banquet hall;
  • it is possible to secure a safe deposit box at the reception for your number;
  • SPA salon with a wide range of relaxing, cosmetic and wellness treatments;
  • a beauty salon where you can make a haircut, manicure or styling;
  • courier and postal services;
  • medical office;
  • 24-hour reception;
  • water sports and entertainment equipment rental office;
  • bicycles for rent;
  • animation show program.




Rooms

In the hotel Coral Beach Hurghada Resort 4 * guests can stay in the following rooms:

  • Premium Class Room - one room with a double bed and a sofa bed. There is a bathroom and a terrace.
  • Classic Standard is a small, bright apartment with a bathroom and a compact balcony.
  • Deluxe Room - the size of this category of rooms is comparable to standard rooms. A distinctive feature is the view of the sea coast, as well as the presence of both a bath and shower.
  • Deluxe Section Room - the room consists of two rooms - a bedroom and a living room. In all other respects, it is absolutely identical to the previous category.
  • Classic Suite - a bedroom, a living room and a small kitchen are compactly located in one. spacious room. There is also access to a private terrace.
  • Royal Suite is a spacious royal apartment with two bedrooms (each adjoining to a private bathroom), as well as a living room-studio, which includes a small kitchen.

It is important that several rooms of the Coral Beach Resort Hurghada 4 * hotel are equipped in accordance with special standards designed to ensure a safe and comfortable stay for people with disabilities.

Services included in the price

By purchasing a ticket to the hotel Coral Beach Hurghada Resort 4 *, you automatically get the right to receive the following list of services:

  • daily visit to a modern fitness center;
  • half an hour a week you have the right to use a tennis court, ping-pong tables, as well as all the equipment and equipment necessary for these games;
  • beach volleyball field;
  • a half-hour visit to the billiard club once a week;
  • participation in recreational activities and shows;
  • attending aerobics classes;
  • once per meal you can dine in Arabic and Italian restaurants.




Relax in a hotel with children

In order for the youngest tourists to have the opportunity to have a full and fun rest, like their parents, the following main features are provided in the Coral Beach Resort Hurghada 4 * hotel:

  • the kids club hosts daily game and entertainment events, as well as dancing and visual arts classes;
  • daily after dinner, in one of the hotel's clubs or in the open air, animators hold a children's entertainment show and an incendiary disco;
  • in the heated pool there is a special children's compartment of small depth, equipped with two water slides;
  • in the hotel, in the shade of a palm garden there is a large playground with swings, ladders, a sandbox and other entertainments;
  • children, as well as their parents can visit the small zoo, which contains exotic birds and some species of animals;
  • in the main restaurant of the hotel there is a specially equipped children's corner with comfortable furniture and a TV where cartoons are constantly broadcasted;
  • This room can accommodate a baby bed.

Restaurants on site

The skill of the chefs is one of the main features that Coral Beach Resort Hurghada boasts. There are several restaurants here, each of which warmly welcomes guests and is always ready to please them with unusual gourmet dishes. So, tourists can visit the following places:

  • MERMAID is the main restaurant of the hotel, which is always waiting for its guests for breakfast, lunch and dinner. By the way, this is the only institution in the hotel where you can try local wine for free during lunch and dinner (it is paid in other restaurants and bars).
  • ANDIAMO - A restaurant specializing in the preparation of Italian dishes. The most popular, quite expected, is pizza. The restaurant hall is combined with the lobby bar.
  • El FALAHA is an Arabian restaurant where everything from the interior to the aromas of spices is aimed at immersing you in the atmosphere of oriental culture and traditions.
  • ATLANTIS - is a fish restaurant, which is most popular among guests of the Coral Beach Resort Hurghada (Egypt). Here they cook only from the fish that was just caught from the depths of the Red Sea.




Hotel Bars

Bars are responsible for drinks and light snacks at this resort. Hotel Coral Beach Resort Hurghada 4 * (Hurghada) invites its guests to visit the following institutions:

  • BLUE MOON is a stylish and relaxed lobby bar that is open 24 hours a day for tourists, offering them a wide selection of drinks and snacks.
  • Aquarius Pool & Snack bar - gives guests the opportunity to have a snack and a cocktail without leaving the pool.
  • El FALAHA CAFE is a traditional Arabic hookah with national music and a characteristic oriental interior. It is important to note that a visit to this institution is not covered by the cost of the permit and requires additional payment for the services provided.
  • ROTANA CAFE - incendiary discos are held here every evening. It is worth noting that only the entrance to the bar and participation in recreational activities is free. All drinks and food are available at an additional cost.




Coral Beach Resort Hurghada 4 * - beach features

Perhaps the most important advantage that this hotel can boast is its chic, spacious, well-groomed sandy beach. Getting on the fabulous sea coast, tourists completely forget about all the shortcomings and oversights made by the hotel staff.

The hotel has a fairly long sandy beach, as well as two amazingly beautiful lagoons that are protected from the penetration of noise and wind. The main feature of the coast, for which crowds of tourists flock here every month, are live coral reefs with many colorful exotic fish. You can look at them through special underwater goggles (you can rent them directly on the beach) or admire them without any additional equipment.

On the beach there is a large comfortable pier for walking and diving into the water. There is also a diving center, as well as instructors in water sports. For the comfort of vacationers, they are absolutely free of charge provided with a huge number of sunbeds and umbrellas, as well as mattresses and beach towels.





Positive reviews

One of the main points that affects the reputation of the hotel Coral Beach Resort Hurghada 4 * - reviews. Hurghada is a popular resort that is visited by millions of tourists every year, so there are a lot of comments about hotels. So, there are the following positive reviews about this hotel:

  • Quick check-in and check-in.
  • A concierge leads the guest room to help bring luggage. If tourists settle in the bungalow, they are taken in a miniature electric car.
  • Good orthopedic mattresses on the beds.
  • Spacious bathroom with quality fixtures.
  • Wet cleaning is provided every day and towels are changed.
  • A varied menu and fresh produce.
  • A clean beach with gorgeous coral reefs.
  • There is a free sightseeing bus to the center of Hurghada.
  • Upon arrival at the hotel, each guest is issued a coupon for free services.
  • Of the cosmetics in the bathroom, only shampoo and soap in dispensers are offered. Disposable individual sets are not provided.
  • Good animation.




Negative reviews

One of the most sought after tourist destinations is Egypt. Hotel Coral Beach Resort Hurghada 4 *, despite its good reputation, makes some oversights, because of which tourists have a number of comments and complaints to it:

  • towels and bedding are quite old, with characteristic scuffs and stains;
  • old broken sunbeds on the beach;
  • low quality local alcoholic beverages;
  • too many spicy spices in the dishes;
  • despite the fact that in one of the pools there is the possibility of heating, it is not included even in frankly cold weather;
  • few fruits on the menu;
  • animals at the zoo do not receive proper care;
  • in spite of the fact that the wireless Internet in the hotel is paid (besides it is quite expensive), the connection quality is very poor;
  • most of the fountains provided for by the landscape design project do not work (according to hotel employees - for the purpose of saving).

General impression

This hotel can be considered an ideal place for those who love the sea and natural beauty, and at the same time are not very picky about the little things and do not demanding maintenance. Despite the former luxury of the hotel, the impression is spoiled by its apparent deterioration. Also, the quality of service and the appearance of the hotel is affected by the desire for widespread savings.
