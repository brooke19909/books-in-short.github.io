Trading system "Sniper": full description

Among the many different trading strategies for trading, one can distinguish a rather sensational Sniper tactic. This technique was developed by a practicing trader Dmitriev. The trading system "Sniper" is an indicator-free strategy based on an understanding of the financial market and levels of market quotes. A major role in it is played by the psychology of trading and money management. Dmitriev in his methodology uses very simple and convenient tools, which, with the appropriate approach and proper application, show remarkable results.

Strategy Terminology

Dmitriev in his strategy uses new terms that must be studied in order to understand the meaning of the Sniper trading system and a full description of the theoretical material provided.

Levels of trading strategy "Sniper":

  1. Maximum and minimum of the past day.
  2. Banking levels.
  3. Impulse levels.
  4. Total impulse levels.
  5. Levels of a sharp change in trend.

It is these concepts that underlie the strategy. With their help, the trader builds blocks on the chart and determines the future market direction. Levels are important elements of the entire trading system.

The previous day's high and low levels

Most traders use minimum and maximum values ‚Äã‚Äãor extremes in their strategies. These can be indicators of candles, fractals and levels that line them up. Dmitriev offers the following scheme of work:

  1. Over the past trading day, you must note on the chart the maximum and minimum values ‚Äã‚Äãof the daily price and set the levels. The easiest way is to use the rectangle tool in the terminal.
  2. As soon as the market price approaches the marked levels, you need to open a deal during the rollback or reversal of quotes.
  3. Stop-loss is set within 15-20 points.
  4. Take-profit is set according to the general rules of the trading strategy.

Typically, this entry point does not guarantee a large profit and is used in the system as additional information.

Banking levels (BU)

This is one of the main and important rules of the strategy. Banking levels are used in it throughout its existence and are designated - BU. Even after the latest additions to the Sniper 4 trading system, they remained unchanged.

Dmitriev defines them as strong levels of support and resistance. He explains this by the fact that all major market participants do not work through the usual "Forex" terminals, such as MetaTrader, but through banks. Their main task is not a speculative transaction familiar to the trader, but the maintenance of the balance of supply and demand. Large banks periodically make exchange transactions, for example, sell dollars and buy euros, and therefore it is very important for them to know the closing price of yesterday‚Äôs session. Knowing this pattern, one can expect strong currency movements from this banking level.

Building Rules:

  1. Include a period separator on the chart.
  2. According to London time, set the level at 00:00: if the bullish candle closes on the timeframe H1, the line is drawn above, and if it is bearish, below.
  3. An important condition: the bank level only works the next day.

According to the terms of the trading strategy, this type of level is marked in green.

Impulse Levels (DUT)

No less important characteristics are possessed by pulse levels, denoted as DUT. They are characterized by three signs: the price repels them several times; this level must be broken through by impulse movement (its size is at least 6 points); price fixing on this indicator.

According to the terms of the trading strategy, as soon as the market price fixes at the impulse level, a point appears for opening a position. You can work with these levels on any timeframes starting from M1 and ending with H4. The most profitable are: M5 and M15.

Total Pulse Level (TIU)

Dmitriev chose the abbreviation TIU for their designation. They are divided into local and total levels. Local ones are used on lower timeframes - M1 and M5, and on older ones, starting from M30 to H4, total levels are used. They have no differences in construction and are determined by size.

A sale transaction is carried out when the market price breaks the total impulse level at H1. Then it is necessary to wait on the M5 timeframe for the false breaking of the total level up and enter the market at the first local impulse level.

Important! Quite often, newcomers make mistakes when, after breaking through the total impulse level on the H1 timeframe, they expect another false breakout on M5. As a result, market quotes do not repeat the false breakout and unfold. Many perceive such a movement as a buy signal, this is their mistake. It is important to remember that the total impulse level must be broken only falsely, and only once. Only under this condition can we get a reversal signal to open a position.

Levels of abrupt trend change (URST)

These levels have the abbreviation URST and are used in all variations of the Sniper trading system up to version 4. They are also considered the basis of the strategy. Levels of a sharp change in trend are the configurations of Japanese candles; quite often there are options for a ‚ÄúPin-bar‚Äù or ‚ÄúSpire‚Äù. All of them consist of shadows, on which the market direction usually unfolds. That is, these are reversal figures and, accordingly, levels. Usually they are used to open positions in the opposite direction or to take profits on previously opened orders.

The scheme of work with URST:

  1. It is necessary to mark the place on the graph with a red rectangle where there is a sharp change in the trend of market movement.
  2. When the price approaches the level of a sharp change in trend, it is necessary to open a deal in the opposite direction of the current impulse in anticipation of a reversal of the market movement or its rollback.
  3. The Stop-Loss protective order is placed at a distance of 25 points from the opening of the order.
  4. The entry point needs to be analyzed on the M5 or M15 timeframes.

Money Management: Safe Method

In Russia, the Sniper x trading system is a fairly well-known strategy for trading on the Forex. Its highlight can be considered a unique profit-taking system or the "Safe Rule". Dmitriev offered traders an original, simple and reliable solution to partial fixation of already initially received income.

The "safe rules" are pretty simple. By and large, they are very similar to the system of transferring a transaction to breakeven, but have their own distinctive features. The rule is based on the laws of the financial market, which determine that all points of opening transactions on the "Forex" in 80% of cases allow you to earn 15-20 points. That is, in whatever direction the price goes, but in any case, having reached a certain level, it will make a rebound of 15-20 points.

According to the "Safe Rules" in Dmitriev‚Äôs system, you need to close part of the position, taking into account profit from a closed share. Moreover, the closed part should cover the Stop-loss loss if the price goes in the opposite direction from the transaction. As a result, using this technique, that is, by closing a certain percentage of the order share in a reliable "safe", in the future the position will occur in safe mode.

To get more profit, Dmitriev offers to transfer the remainder of the order to breakeven as long as the market moves in the direction necessary for the trader. Thus, you can earn a lot more profit and increase profit margins.

The "Safe Rule" solves two problems at once:

  1. With a favorable outcome, it allows you to initially take profits and earn even more in the future when transferring an order to breakeven.
  2. In cases where the market price is against the trader, exit the transaction without loss, since the ‚Äúsafe‚Äù will securely record profit partially, and if the remainder of the order is closed by ‚ÄúStop-loss‚Äù, then the profit will be equal to losses and, as a result, the trader will not lose money.
