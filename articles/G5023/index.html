Children's coat is milky. Preparatory stage

We will figure out what is needed in order to make a children's knitted coat, designed for a baby of three to four years.

Will be needed: yarn in a mixed composition, where at least half is wool, 400 g (per hundred meters 240 g), knitting needles No. 5 and five buttons.

Patterns that the knitter will use in the work on the product:

  • shawl smoothness - means that in all rows all the loops will be facial;
  • braids - 1x1 and 2x2;
  • a pearl pattern, or in a different way - a mess: in the first row of knitting, two front loops and two wrong ones should be alternated; in the second row, as in the rest, knit two wrong ones over two front loops, and two facial ones over two wrong loops.

We knit the front shelves for a milky coat

To make one front shelf, you need to dial 70 stitches on the knitting needles and knit a full width fabric using a pearl pattern. The exception is only six extreme from the center of the loops. They need to be knitted for the strap with a garter stitch, and the entire height of the detail.





After 25 cm is ready from the beginning of work, it is necessary to reduce the loops - every two, until there are 32 working loops. When knitting the next row, four facial loops and two wrong loops must be alternated. And then - from four faces to form a 2x2 pigtail, making a cross in every fourth row. To form the armholes, after 30 cm from the beginning of knitting, reduce three loops once on the outer edge, once two loops, once one loop.

At a height of 15 cm (according to the diagram), in order to accurately make the neckline on the inner edge, close once six loops, once three, once two and once one loop.

We knit the back of a children's coat of milk color

When 25 centimeters are ready, after every two loops we reduce the loops for as many pieces as will be necessary to leave 64 pieces on the needles (twice as much as on each of the shelves).

In the next row you need to alternate four facial with two wrong. In the next rows, a 2x2 braid is made of four faces, and in every fourth row you need to make a cross.





To form the armholes in this coat, after 30 centimeters have been tied, three loops, once two, and once one loop should be reduced along the outer edge. For the neck at the 20 cm center, close 13 stitches. Tie each part separately until the yoke reaches 22 centimeters.

Knit sleeves, belt. Putting the pieces together

To knit the sleeves, you need to dial 35 loops on the knitting needles and begin work, alternating four front and two wrong. Of the four faces, a 2x2 pigtail is tied, a cross should be made in every fourth row.

After 14 cm in height is ready, you need to add loops, knitting two loops from one. Continue so that the number of loops is 72. After that, move smoothly to the pearl pattern. Knitting nine centimeters, symmetrically on both sides - in order to make the sleeve round - reduce once three loops, once two and once - once. Having knitted another ten centimeters, close all the loops.





We knit a belt on the back. Collect ten loops and tie a scarf with a viscous strip, the length of which will be 12 centimeters.

Now you can start assembling. Side and shoulder seams are sutured. Mittens should be slightly tucked around the gusset and carefully sewed into the armholes. The belt is sewn with two buttons to the back. Now, along the neckline of the coat, raise 55 loops for the collar and knit only ten centimeters with a garter stitch.
