Chelyabinsk region: rest. Chelyabinsk, recreation: reviews and prices

Chelyabinsk is a South Ural million-plus city located in a picturesque area with a fairly temperate climate. In the vicinity of the big city you can find many wonderful places to relax in nature. There are so many options that everyone will choose for themselves what suits their preferences and budget. You can come to relax at any time of the year.

The Ural city, being a large industrial and commercial center, is nevertheless surrounded by very beautiful nature: mixed forests, green hills and mountains, as well as a whole placer of large and small lakes. Numerous recreation centers are located on the banks of reservoirs. In Chelyabinsk, their official representative offices are located, where you can find out all the details of interest and book tickets.

"Silver Sands" and "Crystal" on Lake Turgoyak

Recognized as a protected natural monument, this amazingly beautiful transparent lake with a rocky bottom and great depth attracts tourists. The water on Turgoyak is clean, but cold: you need several consecutive hot days for it to warm up for optimal temperature. But even if bathing is not the first number in the list of your priorities, you will still have a pleasant rest.

Chelyabinsk, the recreation center "Silver Sands" is:

  • comfortable and beautiful houses;
  • relaxing natural atmosphere.

What pleases Silver Sands?

This place is for those who love the Ural nature. Here you can live in comfortable cottages with all amenities, as well as in nice summer houses. Separate houses are available year-round. There is no own food and store at the base, but food can be prepared in the kitchens.

From all sides you are surrounded by a friendly forest, filled with the sun and the aroma of herbs. Tall slender pine trees stand like columns in a palace, and forest paths lead to a lake beach covered with sand and surrounded by warm stones.



In summer and winter you can go fishing here. And during a pleasant summer or autumn walk through the forest, look for mushrooms and berries or bring a treat for birds and squirrels with you. In the forest shade it is so nice to sit on a comfortable bench, breathe in the aroma of the forest and listen to the birds singing or calmly read a book.

You can always play table tennis, beach soccer and volleyball, ride a boat or catamaran. Kids will love the kids' town with a swing. In general, the very long-awaited, pleasant and relaxing holiday awaits you here.

Chelyabinsk, recreation center "Crystal":

  • unusual infrastructure;
  • variety of leisure activities.

Multifaceted Crystal

Direct communication with amazingly beautiful, untouched nature, as well as all the conditions for living with comfort - this is what awaits you here. In cozy and warm houses there is everything that a modern city dweller may need. But you just have to step into the open door - and you will find yourself in a pine forest.





Moving along paths and romantic wooden ladders (they were built in impassable places), you will go down to the unique Turgoyak lake. Its clear waters are surrounded by a rocky and sandy beach, where there was a place for wooden pontoons with all kinds of rental equipment, and for cozy gazebos, and for corners of untouched picturesque nature.

Also at the base you can find a tent city with mangal zones, as well as rental of ATVs and a bathhouse located right on the shore.

In winter, the base's surroundings are covered with snow and resemble an illustration for a fairy tale. In warm houses you can spend weekends and celebrate holidays. Take a walk among the fluffy snow creaking under your feet, inhaling with pleasure the clean, frosty air saturated with the scent of coniferous forest. Ride the whole family on a sled from the snowy slope. Swim in the hole or take a steam bath (you can combine one with the other!). And ride around the frozen lake on fast ATVs, admire the pictures of the Ural winter.

"Diamond" and "Forest Tale" on Lake Uvildy

Another unique lake in the Chelyabinsk region. Its waters are not only pure, but also healing: the substances contained in them strengthen the immune system and have a beneficial effect on the skin. There is a sandy beach on the shore, and the lake itself is surrounded by deciduous forest, pleasing the eye with its lush greenery. Here you will find a good rest.

Chelyabinsk, recreation center "Almaz":

  • healing lake;
  • cozy atmosphere.

Healing "Diamond"

Here you can stay in cozy forest houses with a kitchen area and equipped with everything necessary. The base operates throughout the summer months. Each vacationer here will find entertainment to their taste: take a dip in the healing lake and sunbathe on a comfortable beach, take a steam bath, play a variety of sports games in the fresh air, and just take a walk through the forest or along the lake, enjoying wonderful views and knowing that You deserve this pleasant stay.





Chelyabinsk, recreation center "Forest Tale":

  • originally equipped territory;
  • entertainment program for each season of the year.

The come true "Forest Tale"

Once you are in the base, you will immediately fall in love with everything that surrounds you. After all, here is not only stunning natural landscapes, but also the original infrastructure with numerous arbors, huts, houses, playgrounds and decorative elements made of wood.

This is an ideal place to relax with children, for them there are many entertaining and developmental programs: from a modern playroom and cinema to creative studios and a contact zoo.

Adults will not get bored either: here you can find sports courts, bike and roller rental, paintball, a beach on the banks of the healing lake of Uvildy, a dance floor, a cafe-bar, a swimming pool, several varieties of baths and much more.



There are a huge number of thematic contests in which the whole family can participate and get prizes. There is also a dining room where you can taste delicious home-made cooking at reasonable prices.



The busy life at the base does not freeze even in winter: at this time of the year you will have fun rushing through the snow on tubing and skiing, fighting paintball with friends, bathing in the bathhouse, swimming in the ice hole or just spending a quiet evening in the house, listening to how firewood crackles in the fireplace.

Recreation centers (Chelyabinsk): prices

The price range for accommodation is quite wide and depends, of course, on the comfort of the proposed conditions and the diversity of the camp site infrastructure. Prices range from 250 to 2000 rubles. for the place.
