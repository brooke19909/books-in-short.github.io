Briefly about the main thing

So let's take a closer look at the beautiful city of Saki. Where is it located? This settlement is located on the Crimean peninsula, on the shore of the Kalamitsky Gulf. The city of Saki is one of the oldest resorts on the Black Sea coast. It got its name from three settlements - Tatar, Russian and German. Today, the city is world famous for the treatment of various diseases with mud and water treatments.

Weather features

Saki is a resort town where tourists can experience the pleasure of a wonderful climate, consisting of sea and steppe. The weather here is mostly sunny, there are very few rainy days. It is hot in summer, but thanks to the winds from the sea, there is no stuffiness. And the winter in this city is mild with rare frosts. In addition, the air in Saki is saturated with fumes from the sea, mineral springs and Saki Lake. Locals are proud of the above thermal springs, salt pond and arboretum park.



Relaxation

Saki is a resort where vacationers are offered not only treatment with mineral waters and healing mud. Here you can enjoy a beach holiday. Since the town is located five kilometers from the sea, it is difficult to get to the beach on foot. Therefore, it is recommended to use minibuses that will easily and quickly deliver tourists to the sunny Black Sea coast. Near the coast, the sea is not very deep, and therefore it is convenient and safe to relax with children here. And the beaches themselves are sandy. There are also areas with therapeutic mud, therefore, in addition to swimming and sunbathing, you can enjoy mud therapy, and itâ€™s completely free.

Rest in sanatoriums

Tourists who visit this city are offered sanatorium rehabilitation. The most important and popular is the Saki complex. This institution provides the following services: procedures for patients with gynecological, urological diseases, problems of the nervous system, spine and even infertility (both male and female). For wellness treatments, baths with mineral waters, silt mud wraps, massage, acupuncture, herbal medicine and others are used.

In addition to the above complex, in the city of Saki there are:

  • Pension named after Burdenko, which provides services for wheelchair users.
  • Military pension named after Pirogov, specializing in the rehabilitation of people with disabilities. The treatment uses mineral and thermal waters.

Also in this resort town provide medical and cosmetic services spa centers "Jurmino" and "Blue Wave".



Infrastructure

Saki is a resort that accepts a large number of tourists. In addition to complexes and resorts, those wishing to heal and just relax in a great place can book rooms in hotels and rest homes. There are a lot of such establishments in the city, so everyone can choose the most suitable option for themselves. All rooms are decorated in a modern style, equipped with necessary furniture and appliances (air conditioning, refrigerators, etc.). You can eat not only in the hotels themselves, but also in numerous Saki cafes and restaurants. Also for an unforgettable vacation in the territory of this resort town there is a water park and other entertainment complexes.

Cultural lovers can visit the oldest spa park, local history museum, as well as take walks in the sights.



Economy

Saki is a city rich in mineral springs. It operates a plant that produces mineral water. The most popular is the "Crimean", which has received worldwide fame. Also in the city there are dairy and bread factories. There is a research institute specializing in the development of technologies for the iodine-bromine and manganese industries.

Come on holiday to the resort city of Saki! This journey will be remembered for a long time and will leave a lot of pleasant memories.
