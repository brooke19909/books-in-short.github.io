How to choose the right background?





In addition, the background is important. If the subject is on the background of poles or wires, then a good frame will not work. The background must be chosen so that all attention is focused on the subject. This means that the person being photographed must be in contrast with the background. In a closed room, you can try to photograph a person against the background of indoor plants or busy with some usual thing. The light surface of the walls is also suitable. If there is no suitable background, then you can practice in portrait shooting, as in this case it will turn out to make the background blurry. You can also use the special focus mode, if available in the camera.

How to learn to photograph professionally? Pose selection

In the event that a person has very rounded facial features, you can choose such an angle so that the lighting falls on only half the face. The part with less light should be rotated towards the camera lens. In addition, very often novice amateur enthusiasts make one mistake related to the incorrect setting of hands. If the hands are placed along the body, then this does not look very nice. It is better to keep the hands in the usual position for a person.
