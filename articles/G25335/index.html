Holidays in Spain in May: photos and reviews

Spain for many years has been worthily ranked first among the leaders of world tourism. Holidays in Spain in May especially attract tourists from all over the world. This country is famous for its velvet climate, warm Mediterranean breezes, delicious cuisine of the long-livers of the world and, of course, a rich cultural heritage.

Country history

The ancient Romans called the "Spain" (Hispania) the Iberian Peninsula. And in ancient times it was called Iberia, by the name of the ancient tribes living on these lands. Moors dominated Spain for a long time, and only at the end of the fifteenth century did it free itself from their power. After Christopher Columbus discovered America, the country became powerful, was able to create the best army and navy. Since those days, Spain has become a kingdom where "the sun never sets."





Benefits of a Beach Holiday

The warm climate and long coastline attract tourists here. Holidays in Spain in May are suitable for people who want to lie on gorgeous huge landscaped beaches under the gentle warm sun, breathing in the fresh sea aroma, taking a walk on the golden hot fine sand, and then plunging into the clear azure warmed sea and, of course, admiring the unusual landscapes.

What especially attracts tourists on the beaches of Spain is the small coastal areas of the resorts, which are great for families with children. And all this natural beauty is surrounded by majestic mountain peaks and nature reserves, which are especially striking in the huge rocks on the coast, strewn with olive and pine groves, quiet bays.





Spanish culture

it a country of a unique culture in which peoples of different traditions and faith have left their mark: the Romans, Celts, Iberians, Basques, Arabs, and French. Tourists often prefer tours to Spain in May, combining holidays on unforgettable beaches with an introduction to the ancient culture of the country. She captivates with rhythmic flamenco with the sound of heels and the game of castanets and, of course, the legendary bullfighters.

The best Mediterranean resort surprises guests from all over the world with its unique culture, a variety of monuments of the country's history, developed infrastructure and attention to tourists. Travelers will be able to book a choice of room in the chain of the best hotels or in a private hotel, book a luxury villa for a large company or rent new apartments for families.

The best excursions

Resting in Spain in May, it is impossible to travel without amazing and informative tours for both children and their parents. You can go on a tour of such sights:

  • to the mesmerizing ancient monastery on the slope of the mountains of Montserrat;
  • find the home of Salvador Dali;
  • to visit the Pablo Picasso Museum, admire the works of Rubens;
  • remember forever the miracle of the architect Gaudi;
  • Appreciate the many local churches
  • visit the best amusement parks - "Port Aventura", "Terra Mitica";
  • take a closer look and feed different animals in safari parks or zoos;
  • stroll through the nature reserves;
  • admire the mountain waterfalls and lakes;
  • take part in fun holidays in different cities of Spain;
  • Become a member of this medieval knightly tournament;
  • touch the unforgettable extravaganza of the festival of the best fountains in the world.

Every year, vacationing in Spain in May, thousands of travelers strive to go on a breathtaking bullfight - a battle between brave matadors and ferocious bulls. And football fans will be welcomed by the homeland of one of the world winners of this game - Madrid. He will surprise his guests with world-famous football teams and a huge stadium.





Spain in May: weather

Reviews say that it is very rarely cloudy and rainy here. The most popular are tours from May to October. The weather in Spain in May is already warm and not yet exhausting, during the day the temperature in resort towns reaches +27 degrees, and at night it does not drop below +15. Here in the Mediterranean resort you can bask all year. Beach holidays are activated from June to October.

Mediterranean diet and natural winemaking

Spanish cuisine is one of the most correct Mediterranean centenarial diets, which necessarily includes olive oil, fresh natural wine and seafood. And of course, an abundance of fresh fruits and vegetables.

Buying tours to Spain in May, tourists will not be able to resist such a dish as gazpacho soup - this is Spanish cold tomato puree soup. It is also worth trying jamon and paella with seafood. Be sure to try the sweet masterpieces invented here, one of which is marzipan. And do not forget to enjoy refreshing sangria with Spanish fruits.

Spanish wines from local grapes grown on the sunny slopes of the mountains of Spain and carefully collected by the hands of the locals of this extraordinary sunny and friendly country, will surprise you with an exquisite aroma and amazing bouquet. And the prices for local wines are surprisingly very affordable.





Entertainment Reviews

Spain is an amazing and unique friendly country where you can relax in completely different ways. Judging by the reviews, a vacation in Spain in May is well suited for lovers of vibrant nightlife. At the local youth resorts you will not fall asleep until the morning. Tourists get acquainted with the temperament of local residents. A family with young children, newlyweds on a honeymoon, a cheerful company of friends, and even those who want to be alone with untouched nature can relax in comfort in Spain. You can rent a car on favorable terms and visit the sights of the world resort.

A lot of positive reviews about shopping. After all, not so long ago, Spain was considered the first country dictating global fashion trends. To this day, the products of major global brands can be found on the shelves of shopping centers.

In Spain there is no bad time of year for traveling. Each city in this hospitable country surprises in its own way, and then the trip becomes unforgettable, and I want to return to these places again and again.
