Ischia. Review of life "on the volcano"

Somewhere in the Tyrrhenian Sea, almost at the Gulf of Naples, sung by poets and artists, there is a small island created by volcanic eruptions. Ever since the time of Julius Caesar, it is famous as a resort that can restore youth and give beauty. To get there, you must first fly to or arrive in Naples, and from there sail by ferry. There are several cities. Even its capital is Ischia. The review of tourists who have visited here makes us understand that it is in this city that a beginner should settle in to begin acquaintance with the sights. After all, the capital (of the same name with the island) is divided, as it were, into two halves. One of them is called Ponte, and the second - Porte. History has left its mark on literally every corner of this island. The former luxury of Rome and the traces of Saracen power, the threat of eruptions and Mediterranean color - all here await guests and tourists.



Ischia. Review of active vacationers about attractions

History buffs are advised to visit the local archaeological museum of the capital, where you can see many interesting exhibits dedicated to the antiquities of the island. However, the most worthy and popular place for tourists, of course, is the castle of the Kings of Aragon. This huge complex is barely crowded on a tiny cliff near the main island. Now you can get there on the bridge. By the way, the name of the second half of the capital, Ischia Ponte, is connected with this place. And in another district of her, near the port, there are many beautiful and cozy restaurants and free (which is rare for Italy) beaches. The castle is very picturesque, and its fortresses and walls seem to climb sharp stones. In the summer you can visit the beautiful botanical garden "La Mortella". Of course, it is very small, this island of Ischia. The reviews of the people who examined it, however, tell us that there is another curious garden, which also must be visited, Ravino, with exotic plants and living sculptures created from cacti. It is even more attractive for tourists, especially those who come with children. You can make real Italian shopping on the main shopping street of the capital - Via Corso Vittoria Colonna.

Where to live: Italy, Ischia

Hotels, reviews of which, as a rule, are not bad, are located both in the capital and in small towns. If you want to travel not only around the island, but also to the nearby regions of Italy, then it is better to settle in Ischia Port. In this case, a bonus will be not only the immediate proximity to the sea and restaurants (local odes with fish and rabbit should be written in a separate ode), but also with ferries. They regularly depart from here to Naples, Sorrento and Capri. And if you want a budget holiday, it is better to take a hotel in the center of the main city. You can get there inexpensively and even with half board. Moreover, hotel food is in no way inferior to restaurant food, and even cheaper.
