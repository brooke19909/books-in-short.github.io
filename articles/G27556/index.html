Hotel "Seliger Peak": a description of the rooms and guest reviews

The tourist base "Seliger Peak" invites you to relax in the lap of nature. Here you can take refuge from the bustle of the city, restore inner harmony and enjoy incredibly beautiful landscapes.

Brief description of the hotel

Hotel "Seliger Peak" is a great option for both those who love outdoor activities, and for those who love peace and quiet. There is also a gym, and table tennis, and equipment for playing checkers and chess, and much more. Especially popular is a service such as a traditional Russian bathhouse, which not only brings pleasure, but also is incredibly healthy. Guests can also enjoy fishing on the lake.

The main feature of the Seliger Peak Hotel rooms is wooden finishes. Thanks to her, a pleasant coolness and simply breathtaking aroma remain in the room. The furniture is also made of wood, there are wicker elements. It is worth noting the availability of all necessary amenities that provide a comfortable life for a modern tourist. Almost all apartments have a private balcony with lake views.

The Seliger Peak restaurant serves dishes that relate to traditional Russian cuisine. A feature of the institution can be considered that the food here is prepared exclusively from local organic products. You can enjoy the craftsmanship of cooks in a beautifully decorated room or on the outdoor terrace.





Hotel Location

Hotel "Seliger Peak" is located in central Russia in the Tver region on the Valdai Upland. It is about 400 kilometers from the capital. The distance to the nearest town of Ostashkov is approximately 55 km.





"Seliger Peak" - description of rooms

In this hotel you can choose for yourself the following accommodation options:

  • Classic single room in a small area contains all the necessary amenities for a comfortable stay. There is one and a half bed, dressing table, private bathroom, as well as a balcony with a view of the forest.
  • Classic double apartments can be equipped with a large or a pair of one and a half beds. In a relatively small area there are all the amenities that a modern tourist needs - a desk, chairs, as well as a balcony and a bathroom.
  • The suite consists of a bedroom and a living room. Guests are accommodated in a double bed. Folding upholstered furniture, which is located in the living room, can serve as an extra bed.
  • Family rooms can accommodate 3 and 4 guests. In the first case, we are talking about a spacious room with three single beds. The second option involves two bedrooms, each of which is combined with its own bathroom.




Room Amenities

Tourist Hotel "Seliger Peak" offers its guests to use the following list of amenities in the apartments:

  • orthopedic furniture made of natural wood;
  • a TV connected to a satellite dish, so you can fill your leisure time while in the room;
  • mini-bar, which is filled with alcoholic and carbonated drinks for an additional fee (can be used as a small refrigerator);
  • modern shower with backlight and hydromassage;
  • an electric hair dryer, thanks to which you quickly dry your hair and do a styling;
  • bath and cosmetic accessories;
  • balconies equipped with sun loungers will allow you to comfortably enjoy the picturesque lake;
  • Beach towels are provided during the summer season.




What is included in the price

If you have chosen a vacation option such as the Seliger Peak camp site, then you can be sure that the following list of services is included in the price:

  • regular cleaning of the room, as well as the replacement of bed linen and towels every three days;
  • use of private secure parking;
  • visit to the gym;
  • use of hammocks, deck chairs, parasols, as well as beach towels;
  • visiting the library, which specializes in the best foreign detectives;
  • billiard club;
  • karaoke in a restaurant;
  • hot drinks in the lobby area.

Hotel facilities

The Seliger Peak Country Hotel provides its guests with the opportunity to take advantage of the following infrastructure facilities:

  • in the courtyard of the hotel there is a green garden with many flowers;
  • a spacious terrace with sun loungers is equipped;
  • your favorite drinks and light snacks are always available in the hotel bar;
  • in the restaurant you can order dishes prepared according to traditional Russian recipes;
  • in winter, ski equipment is rented;
  • Using wireless internet is absolutely free throughout the hotel;
  • you any time of the day you can seek help or advice from reception staff;
  • a special conference room is equipped for organizing business meetings;
  • celebrations and banquets are possible.




Entertainment program for guests

Hotel "peak Seliger" has prepared the following entertainment program for vacationers:

  • walks on the lake on a comfortable boat;
  • excursions to the monastery of Nilova deserts, which is considered one of the most important Russian shrines;
  • riding on catamarans, as well as rowing;
  • organized picnics in nature, as well as trips for mushrooms and berries;
  • horse riding training;
  • Sightseeing aerial view (by helicopter);
  • outdoor painting lessons;
  • winter skiing in the forest;
  • participation in themed village festivities;
  • Russian bathhouse in compliance with all ancient traditions;
  • bathing in holy springs;
  • excursions to places where the most famous paintings of Savrasov and Shishkin were painted;
  • organization of corporate programs.




Useful information for guests

It will be useful for tourists to familiarize themselves with the following information about this hotel:

  • check-in of newly arrived guests begins after six in the evening (if there are available rooms, it is possible to check in earlier);
  • check-out is possible until 16:00 (late check-out is possible);
  • There are no room charges for children under three years old if they can stay in existing beds;
  • special cribs will cost you 300 rubles per night;
  • folding beds for children under 12 years old cost 950 rubles per day, and for adults - 1900 rubles;
  • pets are allowed for an extra fee;
  • there are terminals for payment by credit card;
  • Within 5 days after making the reservation, a partial prepayment via bank transfer is required.




"Seliger peak": reviews, advantages

Guests of the Seliger Peak Hotel note the following positive moments of relaxation in this institution:

  • the hotel is located in a very beautiful place with wonderful views; excellent quality of food in the restaurant;
  • the territory is very large - there is where to walk;
  • the staff is very polite and attentive; wonderful bath;
  • quiet and calm atmosphere;
  • beautiful views open from the windows of the rooms;
  • glad that the walls of the rooms are lined with wood;
  • happy with the opportunity to relax with pets.

Negative reviews

In addition to the positive, negative feedback is also left about this institution. Hotel "Seliger Peak" is characterized by the following disadvantages:

  • there are no telephones in the rooms, and therefore, if you have any questions, you have to go to the reception yourself;
  • the prices in the restaurant are too high (apparently, the administration takes advantage of the fact that vacationers have no alternative);
  • there are no air conditioners in the rooms, which is why it is incredibly stuffy there;
  • too many mosquitoes;
  • poor sound insulation, because of which you will hear everything that happens in the neighboring rooms;
  • there are no conditions for children - no playground, no playroom;
  • the territory is poorly looked after (even the grass is not mowed);
  • maids do not replenish hygiene supplies;
  • access to the hotel is difficult due to the terrible quality of roads;
  • the refrigerator does not work well - practically does not freeze; in houses practically does not catch wireless Internet;
  • the rooms have very old TVs with poor image quality.

General impression

It is worth noting that the Hotel "Seliger Peak" is an ideal place for fans of a quiet and relaxing holiday in the lap of nature. The hotel is located in a remote location. On the one hand, this provides a calm environment, but on the other, access is difficult. The best option is to take a taxi or own car, but you should be prepared for the fact that the roads here are just disgusting. In addition, there is no infrastructure nearby (shops, cafes, etc.). In view of this, the hotel has greatly overestimated prices for both accommodation and food.

As for the rooms, their indisputable advantage is the wooden wall cladding, which gives coolness in the summer and warmth in the winter, as well as a pleasant aroma. But in hot weather the air conditioner is sorely lacking. In addition, it is worth hanging mosquito nets on the windows, because in the forest zone there are a lot of mosquitoes and other insects. Also be prepared for the fact that if you do not live in an administrative building, then you will not be able to catch wireless Internet.

The hotel area is simply huge, which is an indisputable advantage for lovers of barbecue and outdoor entertainment. Nevertheless, she is very groomed. The grass is not mowed, in some places litter and other unpleasant things. As for entertainment for children, they simply do not exist (either indoors or outdoors). Although this option is stated in brochures and booking sites. Nevertheless, quite interesting field events are organized here, as well as walks along Lake Seliger.

Particular attention should be paid to food at the hotel. Here you are given the opportunity to try traditional Russian cuisine, which is prepared with local organic products. If we talk about prices, then they are too high, but, due to the absence of other similar establishments nearby, guests have to put up with the situation. On the whole, the Seliger Peak Hotel is a good place for those who love silence and want to get to know the traditions of the Russian hinterland. Beautiful nature and clean air compensate for all of these shortcomings.
