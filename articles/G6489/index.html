Sanatorium Anapa "Youth" (MIA) - reviews and description

On the Black Sea shore is a large sanatorium "Youth" of the Ministry of Internal Affairs. Anapa has long been famous as a great vacation spot. Thousands of tourists come here every year to enjoy the splendor of nature and soak up the sunshine while enjoying the beautiful sea.





History

Having become an excellent center for rehabilitation medicine and rehabilitation, the Yunost sanatorium in Anapa was founded on August 15, 1952. Initially, it was a pioneer camp for the children of police officers in the Krasnodar Territory. Today it is one of the oldest health resorts. In 1989, the pioneer camp was transformed into a holiday home. Now, like every sanatorium in Anapa, Yunost hospitably opens its doors to all comers.

Territory

The boarding house is spread over an area of ​​nineteen hectares. The distance from the city is only four kilometers. There are buses to the center of Anapa.

Sanatorium Anapa "Youth" is located on Pioneer Avenue, on the second line from the coast. Its huge protected area is completely planted with emerald lawns. And the cozy romantic alleys along which vacationers stroll in the evenings are great for a secluded holiday. Around the flower beds are fragrant roses, creating an atmosphere of celebration and tranquility.

From the holiday home you can walk to the fruit market, nearby you can buy souvenirs in small shops, taste seafood with live music in the restaurant. There is a disco, there is a nice cafe nearby. A fifteen minute drive away is the city promenade and chic entertainment venues. The resort has its own unpaid parking, a place on which you do not need to reserve in advance.

Infrastructure

The Yunost sanatorium, Anapa, reviews of which are mostly positive, is not only an excellent holiday home with a beauty salon and library, but also the oldest hospital in the region, equipped with the latest medical equipment. Experienced specialists work here who carry out accurate diagnostics of all organs, restore the musculoskeletal system, remove nervous system disorders, and also perfectly fight airway diseases.



Excellent results in the healing process are achieved comprehensively: with the help of physiotherapeutic procedures, psychotherapy, acupuncture, light therapy, laser therapy and inhalation. If necessary, balneotherapy is prescribed, as well as herbal teas, massage. Daily walks along the seashore also help the body recover.

Rooms

Sanatorium Anapa "Youth" after a complete reconstruction meets its visitors perfectly equipped rooms. Guests are accommodated in two buildings - five- and four-story. Comfortable rooms are designed to accommodate from one to three people. They have all the amenities for a relaxing holiday in comfort. They are equipped with modern appliances: TV, refrigerator, air conditioning. The bathrooms have a dryer, a large makeup mirror. All plumbing is new.





Beach

Like every Anapa sanatorium, Yunost also has its own excellent beach, only four hundred meters distant from the buildings. The length of the sea line is five hundred meters. Clean sand, beach equipment - all this contributes to an excellent pastime. Those who wish can ride a boat or scooter.

Nutrition

Sanatorium Anapa "Youth" offers vacationers three meals a day. Tables are served buffet style. The menu for residents is compiled specifically by nutritionists. It has salads, meat, fruits, vegetables and baked. Fresh dairy products are sourced from local farmers. Reviews of vacationers about food are mostly positive. If desired, you can order a service such as "breakfast in the room." There is a bar-cafe on the territory of the sanatorium, where light snacks and drinks of both local and foreign production are offered.

For children

Many, according to reviews, prefer to come with their whole family to the Yunost sanatorium of the Ministry of Internal Affairs. Anapa has always been famous as a great place for children. It has a very mild climate, clean mountain air and a convenient entrance to the sea for little ones. The sanatorium of the Ministry of Internal Affairs "Youth" was no exception. Anapa is a city in which many people prefer to relax with the whole family.



For children, Yunost offers a playground and a room equipped for games. If desired, you can order a diet menu in the dining room. The entrance to the water on the beach is gentle, comfortable. Entertaining events are organized for children in the boarding house, young animators take work very responsibly.

Entertainment

The resort has comfortable sports grounds. There are billiards, table tennis. Sports equipment rental must be paid separately. Those who prefer outdoor activities are offered a well-equipped gym and sauna. The resort has a small indoor pool, open all year.

In the evenings, discotheques are held at the holiday home, there is an excellent dance floor and a club where you can watch movies. A tour desk is located in the central building. Especially vacationers like visiting the Golden Beach water park and the dolphinarium, where marine animals perform.

Additional Information

Check-in time is for breakfast, and departure is after dinner. Children are taken to the sanatorium from the age of four. Pets are strictly forbidden.

From the railway station to the Yunost sanatorium you can take a minibus No. 10, and from the airport - No. 3, 114, 128, 134. During the season, visitors have the opportunity to watch water sports such as surfing or yachting regattas. Over the past few years, the resort has become a favorite destination for large-scale bicycle races.

Reviews

The mild Mediterranean climate (warm winters and sunny weather in summer) attracts many to this region. The sanatorium of the Ministry of Internal Affairs "Youth" is never empty. Anapa, reviews of vacationers about which indicate that the swimming season lasts until mid-October, is crowded even in December and January. Sanatorium "Youth", according to many who lived in it, is a great place to spend a vacation with the whole family.
