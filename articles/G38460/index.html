Credit card "Conscience": how to apply?

Credit cards in Russia are in great demand. They allow you to buy things as soon as required, and then close the debt with interest. Credit cards work on different programs. They play an important role for customers. More recently, the Conscience card appeared in Russia. How to arrange it? What kind of plastic is this? We will have to answer such questions further. In fact, such a proposal may seem attractive to some residents of the country.



Description

What is a Conscience card ? This is the first thing everyone will have to figure out.

This name is a plastic card from the Kiwi. "Conscience" is the most common credit card. She can pay for purchases, and then pay off debts. It would seem nothing difficult. But in fact, the Conscience card (how to arrange it, we will consider later) allows you to take goods not on credit, but in installments. You can call this plastic an installment card or an interest-free credit card. A pretty tempting offer is to buy and not overpay.

About limits

How to draw a "Conscience" card? First you will have to familiarize yourself with the basic terms of use. Plastic is issued for a maximum of 5 years. After that, he will have to reissue.

Primary requirements

How to draw a "Conscience" card? To do this, the client will have to meet certain conditions of Qiwi Bank. Usually there is no problem with this item. But everyone still needs to know about the requirements.

So, in order to draw up a "Conscience" installment card, you will have to:

  • reach a minimum of 18 years;
  • have a permanent residence in the Russian Federation;
  • be a citizen of Russia;
  • do not have open loans (debts);
  • have a good credit history;
  • have an official source of regular income (it is sometimes required to confirm it when making plastic).

Perhaps this is all. Installment cards can be issued without confirmation of official income. But it is better if by the time the request is made the citizen will have the appropriate papers. They will greatly simplify the procedure.

In addition to the requirements listed above, a citizen must have a personal mobile phone. Otherwise, the card cannot be issued and issued. After all, "Conscience" is tied to a mobile phone.
