Alushta - where is it?





Alushta is a small city located on the southern coast of the Crimean peninsula. Its name comes from the ancient Greek word "draft". Due to the presence of two passes, this resort always has hot weather in the summer, while winter is mild enough. A distinctive feature of the resort is that the water here is always warm, its temperature does not fall below +8 degrees Celsius even in the coldest months of the year.

The main pearl, which the city of Alushta is famous for, is the sanatorium "Slavutich", reviews about this institution are mostly positive. High-quality treatment combined with fresh air, warm sea and the ability to watch amazing landscapes make this city a wonderful vacation spot. Another reason to come here is low prices.
