Family complex "Kabardinka": description and reviews

Not all modern tourists can afford expensive overseas resorts. Moreover, in our country there are quite a lot of military personnel who, by virtue of their profession, cannot leave the country. Do not forget that in Russia there are good resorts, which are ideal for recreation, including with children.

The Black Sea coast of Russia has beautiful beaches, chic nature, developed infrastructure and excellent opportunities for organizing leisure activities. Currently, this direction is very popular among our compatriots. The largest tour operators offer comprehensive tours, including flight by plane or train travel and accommodation in a hotel of the selected category.

Of course, even before the trip, you need to get acquainted with the potential options for hotels, read reviews about them, and make up your own opinion based on the information received. We want to bring to the attention of all those tourists who decide to visit the Black Sea coast of Russia, the Kabardinka family complex. Based on the reviews of real vacationers, we will try to conduct an objective review of it and determine how suitable this place is for relaxation. Moreover, most likely many will want to compare this option with expensive and fashionable foreign hotels.





About the complex

The place where the Kabardinka family complex is located - Russia, Gelendzhik. It is located in the picturesque region of the Caucasus, on the coast of the legendary Tsemess Bay, among luxurious coniferous forests and fruit trees. This is a great place for those tourists who want to be alone with nature, enjoy the sunsets and meet the sunrises on the coast.

If you want to spend a vacation with benefits for your health, "Kabardinka" will definitely suit you. The family complex is a kind of boarding house with modern medical equipment from leading manufacturers and qualified medical staff. The reviews of tourists indicate that they help to fight against diseases of the gastrointestinal tract, metabolic disorders, and problems of the cardiovascular system.





Location

The family complex "Kabardinka" is located just 2 km from the village of the same name. The nearest resort town, Gelendzhik, is also nearby - only 15 km. Many vacationers emphasize that due to some features of the location, the family complex is more suitable for a calm and measured relaxation away from the hustle and bustle of the resorts.

If you want to visit a cafe or restaurant, enjoy the infrastructure of the resort area, you can always walk to the nearest resort or go to Gelendzhik. And all this is Kabardinka (SK). The family complex is really located in a picturesque place of the Caucasus Mountains, from where amazing views of the resort surroundings and the entire Black Sea coast open.

Rooms

It is foolish to hope that in the boarding houses located in the cities on the Black Sea coast, you will find chic rooms. Gelendzhik, Kabardinka (Gelendzik, Kabardinka) do not become exceptions.

Kabardinka (family complex) offers rooms of various price categories. In the reviews of vacationers it is indicated that here you will find very simple rooms with amenities on the floor, as well as comfortable two and triple apartments, and superior accommodation. That is why every tourist can easily choose the best option for themselves, starting from their taste preferences and financial capabilities.





All rooms are simple and sophisticated. Vacationers and tourists note how comfortable and functional rooms Kabardinka SK (family complex) can offer. Reviews of tourists are mostly positive, which, of course, affects the rating of this option.

The rooms show signs of fresh renovation. The furniture here is new, quite comfortable. Tourists like that all rooms are facing the sea, so the window offers stunning views of the local beauty. The rooms are equipped with the necessary equipment: there is a TV with satellite channels, air conditioning or a fan (depending on the category), a minibar, a refrigerator. The equipment is in good condition, there are no interruptions in the supply of hot water.





Vacationers complain about the small size of the rooms. So, a family of three in a standard home will be really crowded. Of course, many relate to the choice of a hotel as a place where they will only spend the night. But I would like more light and space - guests write in reviews.

Another drawback is the lack of wireless Internet, even for an additional fee. Modern tourists simply can not imagine their life without smartphones, tablets, round-the-clock communication with relatives and friends through the use of the World Wide Web. Therefore, they will certainly be upset because of her absence.

Nutrition

The cost of living in the family complex includes full three meals a day, organized in the dining room of the boarding house. Vacationers say that its interior can hardly be called ultramodern, but it is quite suitable for a meal.

Judging by the reviews of the guests, several types of cereal, eggs, pasta, sausages, cold cuts, cheese cakes, pancakes with cottage cheese and sour cream, coffee, tea, juices and fruit drinks are served for breakfast. Agree that for a boarding house this menu is quite diverse. For lunch, surely serve soup, main course, salad. Dinner is easier. In principle, the menu is quite balanced: eat, and meat, and fish, and fruits, and vegetables.

Infrastructure

If you are looking for a place that is calm and remote from major coastal resorts, Kabardinka (a family complex) is perfect for you. Photos of tourists demonstrate a good boarding house infrastructure. There is a disco club where all lovers of such leisure can dance and enjoy their favorite musical compositions.

In the evening, you can stroll along the promenade, which stretches for a length of 300 meters. Often in a boarding house conduct business conferences, seminars, training. This is possible due to the presence of a conference room, which capacity is 30 people.





Entertainment and sports

The family complex "Kabardinka" offers excellent opportunities for organizing leisure activities. Tourists do not have to leave its territory in order to have a great rest. On the territory there is a small pool equipped with sunbeds and umbrellas. Proximity to the sea does not affect its popularity among customers. There is not enough space here in the season, and for children, the pool is a favorite pastime; they are ready to dabble in warm water for a day and night.

Do not want to miss workouts even on vacation? There is an excellent gym with a sauna, a jacuzzi and a massage room. The complex has a basketball court, soccer field, tennis court. Here you can rent sports equipment and equipment, master the water sports that you have long dreamed about.

Leisure for children

The family complex "Kabardinka" is quite often chosen by vacationers with small children. All parents will surely find useful information that there are separate cots for kids, an amazing playground, and a pool with inflatable slides. Moreover, parents can always use the services of animators and a nanny: leave their baby under their close supervision.





Service

For guests' services - round-the-clock guarded parking for cars. Many tourists come here in a private car, so now there is no reason to worry about his condition and protection. The rooms are cleaned daily, bed linen and towels - as they become dirty.

For the safety of valuables, money and documents, a safe is located located at the reception's administrators. Of course, all vacationers can attend the wellness and preventive procedures provided for by their tour.





Beach and sea

The family complex boasts its own pebble beach, located just 300 meters from the main building. The sea is crystal clear, a cool breeze blows. What else do you need for a great summer vacation on the coast? On the beach there are locker rooms, showers. The guards control the number of vacationers, do not let unauthorized persons into the territory. That is why even in the season there is always a place.

To summarize

If you are looking for a good option for a holiday on the Black Sea coast of Russia, pay attention to the Kabardinka family complex, located in a picturesque region. Here you will not only have a great time, relax from the hustle and bustle of resort towns, but also improve your health through medical procedures. The guesthouse is ideal for families with children. Young people will also like it, because all the necessary entertainment is literally within walking distance. We wish you a pleasant stay!
