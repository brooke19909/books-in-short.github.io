Tube nose

One of the easiest ways to make such a craft is to glue pieces of plastic that fit together in shape. How to make Pinocchio's nose in this way? We select plastic tubes of four sizes. Cut them into pieces. The thin tube should be the longest. Now we put the blanks on top of each other according to the principle of the pyramid. Fasten the tubes with each other should be superglue or a hot gun. The base on the nose can be made of plastic.
