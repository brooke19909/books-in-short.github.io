What problems may arise?

Even if the user correctly fulfills all the set conditions and starts the programs in the necessary sequence, the result will still not be guaranteed. It is possible (but not necessary!) That the program may require root privileges, since in fact the PC system, whether Windows or Mac OS, must penetrate Android OS and start capturing sound from the microphone of the smartphone. It is unlikely that in most standard firmware from official developers this is enabled by default. After all, such actions clearly violate the Android security system and jeopardize the confidentiality of data stored on the device.

Alternatives

How to make a microphone from a phone? You can connect to it another high-quality microphone. Why not?





You can purchase an adapter for Jack - audio / video connectors - and connect some kind of lapel microphone to it. The problem is only one thing: the microphone may not work at all in this way. And it does not even depend on the presence or absence of root rights. They are not needed here.

The fact is that some smartphones, when connecting an external headset built into the headphones, continue to record sound from the main microphone, and some begin to record sound from the microphone of the headset.

To check whether it is possible to connect an external microphone to the smartphone, just connect any headphones with a headset to the phone and check whether the sound will be recorded.

If the answer is yes, then you can safely get an adapter and an external headset, if not, you should not spend money on anything. You need to look for another way.

By the way, on iOS devices, an external microphone cannot be definitely connected. Apple developers are generally famous for making their OS as closed to external influences as possible. This applies to any accessories that can somehow harm the security of the system.
