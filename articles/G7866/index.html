Radisson Resort (hotel, Zavidovo): description, rooms and reviews

Zavidovo is a small, but very beautiful and popular village located in Konakovsky district of the Tver region, which is not far from the capital of the Russian Federation. One of the main attractions of this village is the Doibica River, on which it is located. Only one and a half thousand people live here, but this does not stop new tourists from constantly coming here, because in the village of Zavidovo there is one of the most popular hotels, which is in great demand. Today we will discuss this project.

Hotel (Zavidovo) Radisson Resort is an excellent hotel complex, which includes a huge number of various establishments, including a restaurant. In this short article, we will discuss this project in detail, find out reviews about it, the exact address and many other useful information. Let's get started now!

Location

This unique hotel complex is located in the village of Zavidovo, where you will be pleased with magnificent forests, huge lakes and beautiful rivers. In addition, visiting this hotel, you can play golf on a special field, as well as find yourself on the territory of the always cleaned beach.





Radisson Resort Hotel (Zavidovo) is located very close to Moscow, so in just a few tens of minutes you can reach it via the M11 expressway. If you plan to travel here by train, expect a minimum of 2 hours.

You can also get here by train. To do this, you will need to go to the station, which is called the same as the village itself. Then use the transfer services, and after 10-15 minutes you will be at the door to your room.

In 50 minutes you will reach the hotel in Zavidovo from the Sapsana stop in Tver. In this case, you should also order a transfer.

By the way, if you drive your car, you can count on completely free parking. In addition, the services of the project guests are always proven taxi at the lowest prices in the area.

Well, for special guests there is a unique and quite expensive service, which involves delivering a client to the Zavidovo Hotel Resort from Moscow by helicopter. Something we chatted, so write down the address, and we will continue: the village of Zavidovo, Tverskoy Boulevard, 2nd house.

Number of rooms

Any guest of the Radisson Resort Hotel has the opportunity to stay in one or more of 239 rooms. So, you can rent rooms of the following categories: Standard, Junior Suite, Suite, Superior, Presidential Suite. In addition, at your request, the Radisson Resort Hotel team will provide special rooms that are connected by additional doors. It is also worth noting that for people with disabilities, the complex also has special rooms, which is a huge advantage.





The hotel rooms are made in different styles: some are decorated in natural white tones, others are presented in bright colors, and others generally have a special interior of luxury and wealth. By the way, the interior design of the Radisson Resort hotel complex was handled by an experienced designer from Sweden.

Children at the hotel

At the Radisson Hotel (Zavidovo), your little children are always taken care of. Children under 12 years of age have the opportunity to stay in the same room with no extra beds for free, and a bed for babies and toddlers is also free, but it is provided only upon prior request and if it is not occupied by anyone.





If you want your child to live in an additional place at Radisson Resort, you will have to make a small surcharge, the amount of which depends on the number you choose.

Pets in the hotel

Recently, more and more often, many hotels simply forbid their guests to stay with their favorite animals. Fortunately, the Zavidovo Park Hotel Radisson Resort has not imposed such sanctions, so you have the opportunity to stay here with your dog or cat. Please note that it is worth notifying the administration of the project about your little friend in advance.

It is also worth noting that for the day of your stay at the Radisson Resort Hotel of your pet you will have to pay 2 thousand rubles. At the same time, at the time of registration, the hotel team will charge you 7 thousand rubles, which will be returned upon departure if the property in your room remains intact.
