Which celebrity died of AIDS? AIDS celebrities

HIV is one of the worst infections of the 21st century, known as the immunodeficiency virus and killing more than 2 million lives each year, including celebrities who died from AIDS. According to 2015 data, the number of HIV-infected people in Russia exceeded 900 thousand people, which, of course, indicates the serious scale of the problem. AIDS destroys the immune system and leads to the fact that seemingly common diseases, which in other cases are treatable, lead to the death of a person, because his body is absolutely defenseless in front of them.

AIDS Day Remembrance Day

Every year on the third Sunday of May, International AIDS Day is commemorated. The main goal of this event is to draw attention to the acquired immunodeficiency syndrome as a real threat to society. This day began to be celebrated since 1988, when the AIDS epidemic was at the peak of its spread. Since then, a lot of effort has been made to search for highly effective drug methods for fighting HIV, however, unfortunately, such vaccines have not yet been found. Therefore, even with active treatment and the patient having sufficient financial resources for this, the disease still remains an invincible enemy. This sad fact is confirmed by deceased celebrities from AIDS.

AIDS is an idol-killing killer

AIDS arose in the era of rock and roll, sex and drugs, but those who do not lead a hectic life can also become infected, because cases of infection through the blood during operations are not so rare. HIV does not spare anyone: neither the poor, nor the rich, nor the townsfolk, nor the stars. Which celebrity died of AIDS, and what caused their infection?

Freddie Mercury



Unfortunately, AIDS celebrities are far from rare. One of the most famous people who died from this disease is the lead singer of the rock band Queen Freddie Mercury. On November 23, 1991, the famous singer officially announced his HIV disease, which was an unexpected blow for his fans and even relatives. For several years, the singer hid his fortune and continued to actively perform concerts. And just the day after his statement, Mercury died of bronchopneumonia, which developed as a result of AIDS.

Freddie Mercury was neither a drug addict nor a homosexual, so the reason he became infected with AIDS remains a mystery. According to one of the most popular hypotheses, the rock singer was deliberately infected through the blood of his ill-wishers. Mercury funded the creation of the Mercury Phoenix Trust, a charitable foundation for HIV patients.
