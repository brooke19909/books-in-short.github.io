The area is divided into a living room, dining, sleeping area, as well as a kitchen and a separate bathroom. The room is furnished with everything you need for comfort. There is a telephone, radio, satellite TV, powerful air conditioning, ironing facilities. The kitchen is equipped with an oven, stove, toaster, microwave, refrigerator and all other amenities. Guests can also use the wake-up service and turn on the heating if it gets cold.

One of the reasons why Sofianna Hotel Apartments 4 * became so popular is the price. A week of living in a studio for two people will be only 26,000 rubles. Helpful information

Many people do not mind spending their holidays at Sofianna Hotel Apartments 4 *. Cyprus, Paphos - these are popular places. So it is better to book an apartment in advance in order to manage to choose a room for your liking. The hotel has only 100 rooms, so by the beginning of the summer there are practically no free rooms.

Check-in starts at two in the afternoon, and check-out lasts until noon. If the hotel has available rooms of the category reserved by the guests, they can be settled early.

Guests arriving at the hotel with very young children (up to two years old) are provided with special cots. But only one in the room is the maximum. If it is needed, it is worth reporting this to the accommodation facility in advance. And when making a reservation, you must specify the details of your credit card - either "Visas" or "Master Card", which must be provided upon check-in.

Advice

Guests who have already been here are advised to go to Coral Bay Beach. There is a stop near the hotel, and from there a bus number 615 runs right to the beach. Drive about 25 minutes. This beach is marked with the EU Blue Flag for cleanliness, because it is so loved by visitors.

If you take the 611th bus, you can get to the water park for about 15 minutes. An entrance ticket costs 30 euros. The water park itself is open from 10 in the morning until 18:00. Inside, in addition to a variety of slides and water rides, drinks and ice creams are sold - all for five euros. It is better to go to the water park in the morning, as there will be less people there.

And souvenirs can be purchased at a huge market, to which there is a 611th bus. There are reasonable prices and just an incredibly large selection. And not only souvenirs, but also products, clothes, Greek cosmetics and other things.

As many assure, in order to walk full day, it is enough to spend 4 euros a day in this store, and acquiring everything you want to try. Naturally, everyone recommends vegetables, olives, wine and famous oil.

The beach can be reached quickly. The closest is equipped with toilets, showers and changing rooms, which is quite convenient. There are sun loungers with umbrellas, cost 5 euros together. But, if you go a little to the left, then you can find such an option. It is usually chosen by people who come with children. Although in the hotel itself, by the way, there are very few people who took their kids with them on vacation. The beach is far away - this is considered by many to be an inconvenience.

Guests are advised not to book excursions, as you can just go out in the evening and walk around the surroundings - the sights are here at every step.

If you make a general conclusion, we can say with confidence: Sofianna Hotel Apartments 4 * (Cyprus) is an excellent choice for those people who want to get a lot of impressions from their holidays in Greece, as well as spend a minimal amount of money.
