What to wear with a cropped sweater? Fashion trends

Knitted sweaters owe their appearance to the ancient Greek fishermen. The clothes they wove from yarn were warm and practical. She well protected from cold and damp. But hundreds of years passed, and the sweater entered the wardrobe of women and firmly settled there. Modern fashion designers and stylists highlight individual fashion trends for knitwear products that are dedicated to a variety of styles. Today, a sweater is the kind of clothing that nothing can replace. Every self-respecting girl has in her wardrobe at least one model of this garment.

Women's cropped sweater is one of the most relevant trends of the season. The original style will be able to deliver true pleasure to its owner, as it will allow her to look relaxed and attractive.

Style and length

The length of this fashionable product suggests that the stomach will be barely covered. A sweater with a shortened front makes it completely naked. Such clothes look very impressive and non-trivial. However, a shortened sweater is suitable for a girl only if the weather is warm outside and she has the correct body proportions.

But the main task of any sweater is to give heat and warm the body. How to wear this garment in rainy and cold weather?

If a girl prefers a shortened sweater as an independent clothing, then its length can be compensated by trousers or a skirt with a high waist. In this case, the outfit will completely cover the body, and you will be warm and comfortable.

It is worth avoiding to put on a loose base under a shortened sweater, as it can give the ensemble a sloppy and shapeless look. It is better to turn your attention to tight-fitting things.





What to wear with a cropped sweater?

The first thing to learn is that a short sweater is very rarely worn as an independent item of clothing. If you want to see it in an authentic form, then only if the bottom of the ensemble will be with a high waist. In all other cases, they put something under it.

The desire to somehow diversify your wardrobe is a loyal assistant in creating an ensemble that could be worn for any reason. The first idea of ‚Äã‚Äãa successful outfit is the layering of the kit. In such a case, a cropped sweater is worn over long clothes.

Multilayer outfits are distinguished by their versatility. They can be successfully worn by a girl or woman of any age category. In addition, a combination of clothes of different lengths will be a good way to correct figure flaws.





The second idea of ‚Äã‚Äãa successful outfit with a short sweater is the principle of youth style. That is, a shortened sweater should be worn on some basic tight foundation: it can be a dress, a topic or a T-shirt. In such an ensemble, the leading role is assigned to color combinations. The outfit can be matched in matching or contrasting shades.

With a full skirt

A fluffy short skirt will be a great addition to a cropped sweater that is knitted on thick knitting needles. A tight base is a prerequisite for this kit. If the girl has imperfect forms, then the ensemble can be supplemented with a wide belt that will perfectly highlight the waist. A leather belt with a large buckle will look especially advantageous.

If the hem is high waist

If you like high-waisted clothing, a cropped sweater is exactly what your wardrobe lacks. In this case, it can be worn on its own, the main thing is to ensure that the length of the bottom covering the stomach is sufficient. Especially advantageous in such outfits look voluminous on top of the sweater, but with an elastic band on the bottom that wraps around the torso.

You can complement a cropped sweater with skirts and high-waisted jeans, but loose models should be avoided. It is better to choose trousers and skirts of tight-fitting styles. A-shaped silhouette is also suitable for a skirt, if it is not too wide.





Youth style

In this style, a cropped sweater can be worn over any tight-fitting base: turtlenecks, tops, T-shirts and more. These outfits are based on interesting color combinations: contrasting or, conversely, suitable in color.

For example, you can wear classic blue jeans and a light blue short sweater over a turtleneck. And you can combine an outfit of pastel shades, for example, light blue, soft pink and beige.

Also look for a cropped short sleeve sweater. The combination of such a bright model will look spectacular with the base bottom of a calm shade. So, for example, you can put on a purple cropped sweater over a gray turtleneck, complement this ensemble with a skirt with a print in which there is purple, and an interesting look is ready!





Business style

For a business wardrobe, a shortened sweater will also not be an extra purchase. In a business style outfit, it can be worn over a blouse, turtleneck or T-shirt, and sometimes even over a classic white shirt. This ensemble can be complemented with a pencil skirt or black flared trousers. The only rule: in this version of the outfit, a turtleneck or blouse is tucked into trousers, but not worn out.
