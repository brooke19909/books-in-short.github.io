Hotel Tsambika Sun in Archangelos 2 * (Greece, Rhodes): overview, description and reviews of tourists

Rhodes is the destination of many Russian tourists. This Greek island is washed immediately by two seas - the Aegean and the Mediterranean and is, according to most vacationers, a real paradise on earth.

Rhodes is popular with both lovers of a relaxing beach holiday, and active. There are all sorts of attractions worth visiting, there is just a huge amount. The infrastructure in Rhodes is also well developed. Removing affordable housing if necessary on this island will be completely uncomplicated. For example, the Tsambika Sun in Archangelos hotel offers very comfortable rooms. According to many holidaymakers, this hotel is most suitable for families. Hiking trips to interesting places in Rhodes are also often found at this hotel.

Location Features

Hotel Tsambika Sun is located in the village of Archangelos, 300 meters from its center. Stegna Beach is 3.5 km from this hotel, and Tsambika is 4.5 km. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel there is such an interesting attraction as the Castle of St. John.

Russian tourists who book rooms at this hotel usually fly to Rhodes at Diagoras Airport. The distance from it to the hotel is about 33 km. Tourists from the airport can get to the hotel in about half an hour. It is best to go to the hotel, according to many vacationers, by taxi. You can also book a transfer in advance at the hotel. This service is available at Tsambika Sun in Archangelos for a fee.





Features of rest

Of course, in relation to the sea, the Tsambika Sun hotel is not very convenient. However, at the same time he is really in a very interesting place. Actually the village of Archangelos itself is located between the two most popular resorts of Rhodes - Faliraki and Lindos. It was founded a very long time ago - almost at the same time as the castle of St. John of the 15th century towering above it.

Archangelos is one of the few villages in Greece where the traditional local way of life is still preserved. Thanks to this, the village leaves tourists with an unforgettable experience. People in Archangelos still live as their ancestors centuries ago. From all sides the village is surrounded by orange, olive, lemon plantations and vineyards. In addition to agricultural work, the villagers manufacture goat leather shoes, ceramics and carpets.

All buildings in Archangelos are painted in different colors. Each of the local houses, in fact, is a mini-hotel, where you can stay if you wish.

General description of the hotel

Hotel Tsambika Sun in Archangelos (Rhodes) welcomes guests in small two-story buildings built in the Greek style. The courtyard of the hotel is tiled and well landscaped. Flower beds are planted next to the buildings and all kinds of exotic plants are planted. The hotel and its courtyard look, according to most tourists, thus very impressive.

This hotel was built quite a long time ago - in 1987. However, the furniture and equipment here are not at all old. In 2010, a large-scale reconstruction was carried out in the hotel. Check-in at this hotel is at 15:00, check-out is at 12:00. Extra beds in hotel rooms are not provided. Smoking and living in rooms with pets are not allowed.





Number of rooms

In total, the Tsambika Sun in Archangelos hotel offers tourists 50 comfortable standard rooms. If desired, the hotel can rent a room for 1-3 people. This hotel also has family apartments. In addition to beds, all rooms are equipped with:

Unfortunately, there are no TVs and refrigerators in the rooms rented in this two-star hotel. If you wish, guests can use the free Wi-Fi right in the room. In addition, the rooms of the hotel include an individual shower and a rather large balcony with outdoor furniture.

Beaches

In addition to a car, you can rent a bike at the hotel. Many tourists do just that in order to be able to quickly get to the beach. Some go to the seashore from the hotel and just hitchhiking.

Stegna Beach in Rhodes is very popular among tourists. The coastline here is very wide and covered with white sand. The main advantage of this beach, many vacationers consider the less crowded. Mostly only those tourists who have chosen one of the hotels of Archangelos for sunbathing and sunbathing are swimming here.
