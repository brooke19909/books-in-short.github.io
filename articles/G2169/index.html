For those who went to Sevastopol

If you really want to visit this resort, but are not sure that you have enough money, you need to carefully calculate everything. For Russians, the best option is to use the airlines. From Moscow to your destination, that is, to Sevastopol, you can fly non-stop, which will cost about 12,000 rubles (with transfers 11,000). You can reduce the cost of the trip a little more if you use the airlines of Ukraine. In this case, you will fly to Simferopol for about 10,500 rubles, and if with transfers, then for 9,500. Next, you will need to travel by train, it will cost 300 rubles. As for the cost of living, then the prices are more reasonable than in Yalta. A quite comfortable room with a TV and air conditioning can be rented for 600 rubles per person, without an air conditioning 400. As for the hotel, you will have to pay about 6 thousand (a double room) for a week of budget accommodation. So, on average, you will need from 20,000 to 30,000 rubles for a flight and accommodation in a hotel or a room for two people for a week. Do not forget that you need to eat, in the worst case it will take about 6-7 thousand a week, and at least have a little fun, and this is 3-5 thousand more. Therefore, take with you 35-40 thousand rubles, this will definitely be enough for you.
