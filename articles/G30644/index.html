Hotel Samui First House 3 *, Thailand, Chaweng: photos, reviews

Fans of exotic countries often prefer a vacation on Koh Samui. This is a relatively small oasis of life, where people of all ages will find something to their liking. Beautiful seaside landscapes with islands rising from the waters, ubiquitous palm trees, tropical plants and azure shores fascinate the eye of a newly arrived tourist on vacation.

The long history of the island of coconut palms, of which there are over 3.5 million, allows you to plunge into the world of past years and expand your knowledge. An interesting fact is that, according to local laws, it is forbidden to build buildings above the legendary beauties. The three-star Samui First House Hotel offers its guests to enjoy unforgettable days spent on the island in comfortable rooms with all amenities.





Location

Getting to your destination is not easy. Not everyone will experience a long flight with a change in Bangkok. However, a reward in the form of an unforgettable vacation will captivate the eyes of a tourist. From the capital of Thailand on the flight takes about 1 hour 15 minutes.

The peak tourist season falls on January-February and July-August. A warm mild climate, the absence of hot heat and warmed water are favorable for healing the body.

Equipment

To make travelers feel at home, the rooms have everything you need. A maximum of four people can stay in one room. The furniture set is made of high quality real wood. It includes: double and single beds, a desk, wardrobe, luggage stand, chairs and bedside tables. Near the latter are numerous sockets.

The presence of an individual air conditioning system enables each guest to create the necessary microclimate for him. Tea and coffee set is updated daily. To start the morning with a cup of fruit tea or aromatic coffee to the liking of each guest. Bathroom

The bathroom has a modest decoration, and is made in different colors. Outside mirror, washbasin, hairdryer and bathroom with shower are present in each restroom. Flowers in a vase add coziness and warmth to the space. A set of personal care products, including soap, shampoo, gel and balm, have a pleasant aroma and is updated daily. White towels are soft and present in the required amount.

Nutrition

The choice of food system is quite democratic: every visitor chooses what he wants. It can be only breakfasts, breakfasts + dinners, or the All Inclusive system. The interior of the main Leerawadee restaurant is very modest. This is an open terrace equipped with mounted fans and surrounded by perimeter columns. Green plants in flowerpots complement the interior, the design of which is associated with unity with nature. The furniture set is simple but comfortable. The serving is decent. Thai and European cuisines are served here. Samui First House 3 * reviews of a positive plan are often received precisely for catering. The food is served buffet style. Each vacationer independently chooses their favorite dishes in a volume that will satisfy the feeling of hunger. Children's menu - free, approximate contents - rice, chicken soup, sandwiches, etc.

Local chefs definitely love what they are doing, because their culinary masterpieces do not leave indifferent any vacationer. The abundance of dishes, according to tourists, is incredibly varied, satisfying and tasty.





Aquazone

One of the main activities on vacation is swimming. In the Samui First House hotel complex, this can be realized outdoors near your place of residence. The positive side of this process is explained by the usefulness for the whole organism: the functioning of the circulatory system is normalized, the lungs and respiratory system as a whole develop, muscles develop, and excess weight is lost. Accelerated metabolic processes, strengthening immunity, achieving flexibility undoubtedly heals the body.

The swimming pool, whose dimensions are 12 by 28 meters, has rounded shapes and contains additional areas: a children's part and a jacuzzi. Kids can frolic under the supervision of their parents and splash around enough under the gentle rays of the sun. The pool is surrounded by tropical plants, palm trees and deck chairs with soft mattresses. Umbrellas will be needed for those who prefer to hide in the shade. There is fresh water in the aquazone and a gradual increase in depth is designed. In the evening, this area is subject to regular cleaning.





Hotel Samui First House 3 * (Thailand, Chaweng): beach

Due to its convenient location and being on the first coastline, Samui First House Hotel is in unprecedented demand. The natural landscapes opened from the coast, which can be reached in less than five minutes, fascinate bathing tourists. The beach infrastructure is quite developed: there are locker rooms, showers and water activities. The beach is exceptionally sandy, the entrance to the sea is gentle. The water temperature does not drop below 27 degrees.

Numerous loungers with soft bedding and shadow canopies contribute to amazingly tanned skin color. The security service and rescuers tirelessly monitor the safety and order of vacationers.

The list of water activities will allow you to experience a sense of drive and adrenaline not only for kids, but also for their parents.

Entertainment

In addition to a pleasant beach holiday, you can enjoy the mass of entertainment on offer. To maintain your health, you can head to the well-equipped fitness room. There are cardiovascular equipment, dumbbells, barbells, exercise bikes, treadmills and a host of other equipment. The acquisition of beautiful forms is guaranteed to everyone: extra pounds will disappear before our eyes, and professional instructors will help in this.

A visit to the sauna requires an additional charge. This procedure has a positive effect on all body systems, removes toxins, moisturizes the skin and guarantees a good mood. An accompanying Thai massage allows you to experience a feeling of true relaxation.

The staff of the beauty salon will help each visitor to create a new image. Thus, not only a dark brown tan, but also a beautiful hairstyle will complement the charming appearance of customers.

You can play table tennis at the Samui First House 3 * hotel (Chaweng Noi, Thailand). However, most holidaymakers say that it is better to go on excursions to local beauties.
