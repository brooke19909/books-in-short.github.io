Coconut Beach Resort 3 * (Thailand / Chang): location, description, infrastructure, photo of rooms, reviews

Thailand's Koh Chang Island is not as popular with tourists as Phuket is. However, people who once visited it, dream of coming back here again.

Despite the infrastructure that has recently begun to develop, there are already many hotels where the resort offers high-quality service. One of those is Coconut Beach Resort 3 *. We will consider it in the article.

Location

Koh Chang Island is a paradise in Thailand. About 80% of its area is covered by jungle wilds, and some beaches are still considered wild and protected, being territories of nature reserves. Only 20% of the island is built up with entertainment and health centers, as well as hotels.

Coconut Beach Resort 3 * is located in the western part of the island, on Klong Prao Beach, which is the longest in these parts - at least half an hour is required to get from one end to the other.

It is also worth knowing that the sea here is rather shallow the first 100 meters, and therefore this place is loved by travelers who are used to taking their children on vacation.

It should also be noted that Sapparod Bay Ferry Terminal is a 20-minute drive from this hotel. The nearest airport, located in the Thai city of Trat, is 25 kilometers from here.

Leisure

The vast majority of travelers who decide to spend their holidays at this hotel are on the beach all the time. Because it's really gorgeous here. But the leisure infrastructure is well developed. If the beach or excursions are tired, you can do something yourself without leaving the hotel. Here is what is available here:

  • Sun terrace
  • BBQ area.
  • Beautiful secluded garden.
  • A swimming pool and a bar next to it.
  • Water Sports Center: diving, snorkeling, fishing, motor boat and canoe rafting, etc. Tourists are provided with all the necessary equipment. And even boat tours are organized.
  • Library.

It should be noted that in the evening they organize an entertainment show program. Therefore, you will definitely not be bored while staying at the hotel.

Nutrition

On the territory of Coconut Beach Resort 3 * there is a separate restaurant. If you believe the reviews of people who were vacationing here, then the food there is pretty decent. The food is always fresh, moderately diverse, there are plenty of fresh fruits and vegetables.

For lunches and dinners, guests usually go to establishments located within walking distance of the hotel. They say that almost in every cafe they cook excellent seafood. And right in front of the hotel (across the road) there is an excellent coffee shop where divinely make invigorating drinks and pastries.

And not far from the hotel there are shopping malls where you can buy anything you want, and for little money.
