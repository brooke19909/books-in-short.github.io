MTS 970 smartphone: specifications, price, reviews.

Mobile TeleSystems, like many other companies, launched the production of smartphones that are blocked only on the use of SIM cards of this operator. At the same time, such smartphones have good characteristics and a low price.

MTS 970: characteristics of the main parameters



The MTS 970 smartphone is based on Alcatel. This is not the first product of cooperation between a mobile operator and a mobile phone manufacturer.

On the basis of Android, inexpensive models of smartphones are increasingly appearing. Among them is the MTS 970, the characteristics, the price and purpose of which suit many mobile users. First of all, the Android platform is convenient, which is understandable to most users. MTS 970 also has the following system characteristics: 4 GB - total memory and 512 - operational. This data will allow you to easily work with various Internet applications. However, it is worth noting that you should purchase a memory card for this phone (it supports microSD format up to 32 GB), since free memory for installing applications the first time you turn on you will only have 1.4 GB available. The fact is that the operator by default adds you several applications that provide additional account management capabilities and various services from the operator. The speed of applications is ensured by a single-core 1 GHz processor, which is enough for surfing the Internet with the help of special applications, as well as using all the necessary functions.

Camera options



The MTS 970 camera features are more than usual for carrier phones - 3.2 megapixels. However, she does not have a flash, which does not distinguish her from such devices. At the same time, the photos from MTS 970 are not bad, they can be shared via the Internet, published on social networks. They will not differ from those made with an ordinary camera. In the camera menu, you have the option of choosing a shooting mode, setting exposure parameters and white balance. In addition, delayed descent and various focuses are possible. The camera interface is not too overloaded, but at the same time there are the most necessary functions with which you can make high-quality photos. There is also a function for creating videos. Writing speed 25 frames per second. The video quality is very good, such a video can be posted on the network. There is also a front camera for communicating on Skype and making video calls. It is rather weak, only 0.3 megapixels, but this power is enough for the interlocutor to see you.

Display Settings and Features





The MTS 970 smartphone has the following display characteristics:

  • Two-point multi-touch, which is enough to enlarge photos and most games.
  • Diagonal of 3.5 inches.
  • Resolution 320 x 480 pixels.
  • Capacitive TFT - type of display.

The screen is covered with transparent plastic from damage. It is difficult to use the smartphone in the sun, since the image is difficult to see on the screen under such conditions. It is also characterized by an average degree of contrast and a good margin of brightness. Users argue that for comfortable use in the premises it is necessary to remove the brightness almost to a minimum. During a voice call, the display turns off because the smartphone has a proximity sensor. This is a very convenient feature, so you do not drop or hold the call by accident.

Battery for battery life



The passport of the device indicates the battery capacity of 1400 mAh, which allows 3.5 hours to watch video on it, as well as 2.5 hours to spend time playing the game. The MTS 970 battery capacity characteristics are among the highest among other budget smartphones. Charges the battery from the USB port of any PC, as well as from an ordinary electrical outlet. In order to fully restore the battery level, you must leave the smartphone connected to a power source for 1.5 hours.

Internet access

In order to be able to communicate using social networking applications, as well as go to the necessary pages on the Internet, you need to configure data transfer. It is quite simple, since it uses a full-sized SIM card, and all network settings have already been entered. Therefore, you can work in 3G immediately after the first inclusion of the smartphone. Technical characteristics of MTS 970 allow it to easily open various Internet pages, download videos and play music from various Internet resources. You can also access the network via Wi-Fi, if there is an available network near you. The device will detect it and when it is open, it will connect to it.

Additional communication options



Also, the MTS 970 smartphone, the technical characteristics of which are quite high in this regard, can transfer data through a Bluetooth connection. This is very convenient for owners of laptops, since you can do without a wireless connection to transfer photos or other files. In addition, using a Bluetooth channel, you can use a wireless headset with your phone. It is possible to connect the device to a personal desktop computer using USB. Convenience lies in the fact that the folders of the memory card and the device itself will be displayed on the computer. This is a very convenient option for working with all files.

In addition to standard voice calls, it is possible to make a video call, for which you need to have an active Internet connection. It can be done using a standard resource or using special applications (Skype is one of the most popular in this segment).

System operation and preinstalled programs



The above has already mentioned a wide range of software from the operator. It is worthwhile to consider in more detail the system refinement from the operator. At the first acquaintance with the system, you will notice an abundance of branded MTS applications:

  • Second memory.
  • Application for viewing TV programs on the device.
  • Quick access to MTS services.
  • Application for installing beeps to your number.

In addition, in the phone book you can find all the necessary numbers for communication with the operator's services.

In the list of preinstalled applications you can also find Google services, as well as a voice recorder, a player for watching videos and listening to music tracks. The phone works with all applications, showing high performance. There are no complaints from users about freezes and other system glitches.

Input method

The smartphone has a SwiftKey keyboard by default, which is highly learnable. At the same time, he studies your style and frequency of using words, so that later it is possible to quickly type sentences. It is used not only in social networks and SMS, but also in the mail service. Also, this keyboard is very convenient to use and has many options that are convenient for the user. The only drawback is the letters that are too small for typing, but this is not a drawback of the keyboard itself, but the need for developers to make it compact due to the small screen size.

Energy saving system optimization

The MTS 970 smartphone, the technical characteristics of which are very diverse, differs from others in energy control. To do this, it has a special application in which several battery usage modes are preinstalled. Flexible application customization is also available for personalization.

For example, three modes are available for selection:

  • Normal.
  • Energy saving.
  • Optimal.

Using the application, you can find out the estimated remaining time of using the device. Statistics are also available that reflects the time elapsed since the last connection to the power source. By the way, even without using this application, the battery holds for a long time. With the active use of the phone (listening to music, surfing the Internet, chatting on social networks, watching videos), the battery may last for the whole working day. Reduce the number of hours of operation on the battery only the game.

MTS 970: specifications, reviews and comparison with similar smartphones



A large number of opinions have been gathered about this device on the network, most of which boil down to the fact that the smartphone is of acceptable quality for such a low price. Compared with more expensive models, a number of shortcomings can be identified. For example, many complain about the quality of the speaker. The interlocutor is audible, and there are no annoying rustles and interference on the line, but the quality is worse in comparison with more expensive phone models.

There are also claims to the display, in comparison with similar models they are acceptable. But if you compare with the devices of the class above, you can see that the font is displayed slightly differently than with models with a higher screen resolution.

The display also has a number of complaints. It has already been mentioned that he has a fairly small viewing angle, as well as displaying objects in sunny weather. In general, it can be noted that in comparison with IPS display systems, the quality is somewhat worse. At the same time, typing and touchscreen by users were rated quite high.

Of the advantages, users have identified a convenient virtual work plane, which can be completely changed for themselves. It is worth noting that in total there are 3 desktops in the phone, by default two of them are absolutely empty. You can fully equip these planes for yourself and your needs.

If we consider the MTS 970 phone, the characteristics of which are quite acceptable for its price segment, from the point of view of the consumer, then it is very attractive for purchase. For a small price, it presents a large number of options, and all the shortcomings are not significant. It is well suited for customers of the MTS operator. Indeed, for not the highest price, you can have access to the Web and a sufficient number of convenient services on hand. There is also a modification of the MTS 970 h, the characteristics of which are not inferior to the considered model. As for the cost, this phone can cost you up to 2,000 rubles, depending on the conditions of purchase.
