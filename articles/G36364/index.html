Sea Side Resort & Spa 5 * (Crete, Greece): description of the hotel, services, reviews

Sea Side Resort & Spa is a 5-star hotel that welcomes thousands of tourists every year who want to have a good rest in Greece. There is everything that will help to make your vacation more vibrant and productive. However, first things first.





Briefly about the hotel

Sea Side Resort & Spa was built in 2004. In 2009, it was decided to redecorate this complex. And a year later, in 2010, a new block of rooms was built, which was placed 400 meters from the main building. He, by the way, has his own, separate pool. After the construction of the new block, the room stock of the hotel complex increased to 248.

The hotel is located on the first coastline, so you can walk to the beach in a few minutes. Also nearby is the airport. It is located in Heraklion, and this is a little over 20 kilometers from the hotel.

By the way, the complex itself is located in a resort called Agia Pelagia. The new national highway E75 leads to it. The resort itself is a picturesque place located in a cozy bay with a spacious sandy beach. This quiet and calm fishing village is very popular among visitors. Largely also because the Sea Side Resort & Spa is located here - one of the best hotels in all of Crete.

Recreation and entertainment

This hotel has everything that will help guests brighten up their leisure time and have fun. The name of the hotel has the prefix "spa", which clearly indicates that the hotel has a SPA center. There you can enjoy various wellness and beauty treatments. Manicure, pedicure, massage, Turkish bath, sauna, jacuzzi, indoor pool, hammam, yoga - guests have the opportunity to do anything.

The Sea Side Resort & Spa also has a fitness center, basketball and volleyball courts, a soccer field, a billiard room and a tennis court. Of course, there are two more outdoor pools with a sun terrace. Although many people prefer the beach. Not surprising, because people come here to enjoy a sea holiday. On the beach you can still do various water sports. For example, diving.

And in the evening, a team of animators arranges shows and entertainment programs at the hotel. Guests who come with children may not worry about the fact that they will be bored. For the smallest, the hotel has a separate pool, a mini club and a playground.





restaurants and bars

On the territory of Sea Side Resort & Spa 5 * there is an excellent restaurant where guests can try Greek and international dishes. Inside, a romantic atmosphere reigns, and the room itself is furnished with beautiful and expensive furniture. Here guests have the opportunity to order fish or grilled meat - such dishes are cooked in front of their eyes. If you do not want to go to a restaurant, you can request delivery of food and drinks to your room - the service is no worse than in the restaurant itself. By the way, people with children can choose something for their child from a special menu for kids.

In general, a variety of dishes is present. There is everything - starting with fish and meat, ending with desserts and fruits. By the way, guests pay special attention to the high quality of drinks. Especially local wine. Staying here on vacation, you should definitely try it at least once.

In addition to the restaurant, the Sea Side Resort & Spa 5 * hotel has two bars. One is in the lobby. It serves drinks and a variety of snacks. In the evening, pleasant live music sounds here. The second bar is located by the pool. Guests can enjoy tea, coffee, cakes and juices there.

Guests say that in the evening there is a turn for dinner - but this is at the height of the season. There are two ways to solve this small problem - either come to the restaurant later, or come to rest in late spring or early fall.





About service

Sea Side Resort & Spa (Crete) offers 24-hour room service. But there is much more. In addition to all of the above, the complex has a private car parking, car rental, shops (food and souvenir), as well as a laundry with dry cleaning. If guests have a need, they can call a nanny to sit with the children, or a doctor in case of health problems.

The hotel also has a conference room, as well as an office where you can receive or send a fax, as well as make a photocopy of any papers and documents.

The hotel employs highly qualified staff engaged in room service and guests. Workers speak German, English and Greek. And yet, importantly, 12 Russian-speaking people are currently working in the complex. They are always ready to assist guests, if necessary.

And guests are often greeted upon arrival with fruit and wine. Compliments for newlyweds, birthday parties and regular guests are also provided. This attitude towards tourists cannot but rejoice.
