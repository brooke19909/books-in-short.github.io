Sea of ​​Azov in August: holiday reviews

Choosing a place to stay, most often Russians recall the Black Sea, despite the fact that there is a great alternative. The Sea of ​​Azov is an excellent budget vacation, magnificent beaches, sun and fun. If your vacation falls at the end of summer, then not every resort can give you an excellent experience, which cannot be said about a vacation on the Sea of ​​Azov in August. Reviews emphasize that until mid-September, tourists will find very pleasant weather here, guaranteeing a gentle tan and beach activities for at least a full day.



general description

To begin with, this is an amazing and not at all deep sea. This is very important if you have a vacation with children. Its waters warm up very well already at the beginning of summer and maintain a comfortable temperature until the fall. Very warm Sea of ​​Azov in August. Reviews emphasize that it is during this period that it is especially good. Sand and shell beaches are simply loved by children, because here you can find so many treasures!

Budget vacation

It is for this reason that most Russians choose the Sea of ​​Azov. In August (reviews say that discounts really work) prices are reduced, which can save a lot. Locals willingly rent out their housing to vacationers, so renting a suitable house, room or room is not difficult. If in July they are usually crowded, then by the end of summer a large number of excellent options will be released.

Today we’ll try to consider the most famous and popular resorts and decide where it is best to go on vacation next season. Which cities and villages are ideal for young people and the elderly, for a young family with children, lovers of a secluded, active or sightseeing vacation?



Climatic conditions

By the end of summer, many resorts become unsuitable for swimming for one simple reason, it gets too cold. The temperature of the Sea of ​​Azov in August allows you to continue the beach season until September. In any case, at the end of August the air temperature is about +28 degrees. It’s cooler at night, but you can walk without a windbreaker.

Someone says that the Sea of ​​Azov is dirty, but it's hard to agree with that. The muddy bottom helps to raise the suspension, but it is good for health. It rains infrequently in August, and for the most part they are warm and short-lived. But the waves can be dangerous, like on any other sea. Despite the fact that here is shallow, and the entrance is gentle.

This is the main selection criterion, especially if you have to go a large family. Each resort town or village is different in its prices, so you need to analyze the whole picture in advance. Based on the principle of economy, it is best to come to the resort at the end of the season, in August. Reviews The Sea of ​​Azov is called the “warm lagoon”, while emphasizing the accessibility and sophistication of recreation on its shores.

The cheapest thing is to relax in small villages with undeveloped infrastructure. In fact, you will have sun, air and water. These are the villages of Kuchugury, Peresyp, Ilyich. For the resort town, this is quite a bit. A room in the private sector goes from 300 to 800 rubles per day.

Most expensive vacation in Yeysk. This is a large city that provides many opportunities and, accordingly, requires more money. Holidays on the Taman Peninsula

Many people know this place as the village of Golubitskaya. This is a small village that is surrounded by greenery and vineyards. It is 8 km from the city of Temryuk and an hour's drive from Anapa. If you want to visit Krasnodar, then it is 149 km away. An ideal place for an amazing vacation, even if it's August. Rest on the Sea of ​​Azov is a pleasure for body and soul. You are waiting for amazing beauty estuaries, overgrown with lotuses and representing an unusual sight during flowering. What else is Taman Peninsula ready to surprise you with?

Reviews of experienced tourists say that here is just a paradise for families with children. Sand, mixed with small shell rock, enriched with useful minerals, adults will also like them. The sea here is warm and shallow, and the bottom is sandy and flat. On the territory of the village there are also natural attractions. This Golubitsky lake is large, but shallow, with healing mud.

From entertainment, it is worth noting the largest water park in the Sea of ​​Azov. A guest house is located on its territory. If you stay in it, then you can come for free even to the water park itself, even to the dolphinarium. There is a wonderful ethnographic complex Ataman in Taman. This is an open air museum. Or you can go to an ostrich farm or visit a mud volcano. There is still the opportunity to attend a sailing school, visit a windsurfing club or paragliding center.



Debt or rest on the spit

The end of summer for many is the last opportunity to relax in the outgoing season. And quite good, which is confirmed by numerous reviews. What is the Sea of ​​Azov in August? It is warm and tender, affectionate and alluring. And if you crave a calm and quiet holiday, the Dolzhanskaya village, which is located on the Long Spit , will be an excellent option . In fact, this is a nature reserve with unique nature, which is good for families.

On the Long Spit there is a sand-shell beach, which adults and children will love. A unique microclimate reigns here. Pine forest and steppe grasses, a whole carpet of flowers, all this enhances the healing effect of mud baths and mineral-rich beaches. All this as a whole creates a healing and very useful environment for the human body. There is not much entertainment here, only horseback riding in the surroundings and riding lessons.



Big city Yeysk

What do tourists who travel to the Sea of ​​Azov at the end of August usually choose? Reviews say that at the end of summer, many no longer hope for warm weather without rain, and therefore refuse to go to small villages without developed infrastructure. After all, the beach is the only entertainment there. Instead, head to a major city. The Sea of ​​Azov in Yeysk in August (reviews say that at this time it is good here as never before) perfectly warmed up. Water is like fresh milk, and a good ten meters from the shore preserves knee-deep. Ideal for families with children. In addition, Yeysk is one of the best mud resorts on the Azov-Black Sea coast. The resort is located at the base of the spit, on the peninsula. And next to it lies the southern port city with many parks and green alleys, shady squares.

Things to do in Yeysk

On the shore of the Taganrog Bay there is a water park "Nemo". Here you can spend the whole day rolling down the hills and enjoying the spray of water. Be sure to visit the dolphinarium and the crocodile canyon with amazing snakes and crocodiles, as well as turtles. Continue an informative tour will allow the shark reef oceanarium. In the evening, you can visit the oldest park in the Kuban on the bay. There are more than 30 different attractions.

Even in the peak season, you can rent a house here for 2000 rubles per day. At the end of August, you can easily find cheaper options. To make it easier to navigate, think about what you would like to do. If swimming is the priority, then, accordingly, focus on the beaches. It may well turn out that the warm weather will stay until the very end of summer, and you will enjoy the sea to the fullest. The main beaches are located on the Yeisk Spit, it is the Central city and children's beach. Huge areas are covered with delicate sand, equipped with umbrellas and sun loungers.



Kuchugury Village

Small but very cozy, it is the perfect solution to visit the Sea of ​​Azov in early August. Reviews say that at this time the weather is fine, so you will spend all your free time on the beach. The village is located 80 km from Anapa, so if you wish, you can take a tour. Here you will find a sandy beach with fine golden sand. And the sea in this place is always clean and shallow. Ideal for families with children. From anywhere in the village you can reach the beach in just 5-15 minutes.

There is not much entertainment here, and if the weather is rainy, it can be very boring. On the beach you can ride a "banana" and fly a "parachute". The place is quiet, the rest is calm. By the way, it is in August that an abundance of fresh fruits awaits you. This famous southern resorts, including those located on the Sea of ​​Azov. Reviews Kuchugury in August is called a real paradise in order to improve health. The sun, air, sea, fantastic nature and natural fruits - what else is needed in order to recharge your health for the whole year ahead?



Healing Lake "Blue Beam"

Just 15 km from the village of Kuchugury is another small village called Peresyp. Small and very cozy, it is great for families. In August there are not too many tourists, but the weather is fine, which allows you to spend time on the beach with almost no limits. Nearby are the Lotus Valley and the healing lake "Blue Beam". 150 km from Krasnodar is another beautiful resort called Primorsko-Akhtarsk. It is located 150 km from Krasnodar. Small, with cozy beaches and pretty guest houses, he waits for his guests throughout the summer. But on the eve of autumn, it is especially quiet and cozy. If you like noisy parties, you can be a little disappointed, this time is best for a secluded holiday in the lap of nature.

Instead of a conclusion

With the onset of August, rest on the shores of the Sea of ​​Azov does not lose its attractiveness. There is still a whole month of heat ahead. The first half of August is a velvet season for those who do not like intense heat. It's time to get a great tan. Additional advantages are low prices for trips and an abundance of vegetables and fruits. But the second half of August is already a lottery. She can be pampered with warm days, and sometimes it will charge the rain. At this time, it is better to visit the large and developed resort cities. If the weather turns bad, you will find something to do.
