How to find out the owner of a phone by phone number: ways

Often there is a need for personal purposes or at work to find the person to whom the number is registered. It so happens that the number recorded on a napkin is forgotten, or someone called from an unknown number. How to find out the owner of the phone by phone number?

What are the ways?





This is not an easy task. But still there are ways to determine the owner of the phone by its number. In short, there are two ways to solve this problem. Official request





You can find out the owner of a phone number (MTS, Beeline, Megafon) if you officially contact the company employees by writing a statement stating the reasons for the appeal. It is necessary to fully state all the motives. For example, if there were threats or there is concern for the health and life of family members, as well as the safety of valuables. Employees of a cellular company may well provide the necessary data after they consider the application.

How to find out the owner of the phone by phone number? Information about him can be obtained if you contact the law enforcement agencies, which may be the FSB, the police, the prosecutor's office. Here it is also necessary to write a statement in which all the reasons, requirements and claims will be detailed. After considering the appeal, a criminal case may be instituted, and then law enforcement agencies will make a request to the telecom operator, which by law must post all the information about the owner of the phone number. Thus, during the investigation all the necessary data will be obtained.

Informal ways





At the time of payment for cellular communication services, the employee making the payment sees all the information about the owner of the phone number to which funds are credited. You can find out the owner of a cell phone number if you try to ask the manager the name of the owner of this account. It is likely that the employee will provide the necessary information. If this does not happen, then you can try to contact other communication stores.

If this is possible, then you do not need to use the database of telephone numbers, access to which can be both free and paid. These databases may be incorrect, outdated. They may also contain malicious software. It is worth noting that such methods can entail a criminal case, since working with such programs is not legal.

If the database offers to send a message to some number so that an access code to information is sent, you should not believe this. This is a fraudulent way to withdraw money from your mobile phone account. No code will be received.

How to find out the owner of the phone by phone number, if it is illegal? You can easily find out the region in which the number was registered, as well as the operator. There are similar databases on the network in the public domain. You just need to enter the necessary phone number in a special form, and information about the city of registration and the operator who services this number will be displayed.
