The plastic of the device is rough, which allows the tube not to slip in the hand and protects it from falling. At the rear bottom of the handset is a compartment with two batteries. The Panasonic KX TG1611RUH cordless telephone (see the overview of this unit below) does not interfere with a conversation. The sound is clear, without hissing, rustling and echo. The phone represents the best combination of price and quality. It is a great option for home and office.

Getting started with the phone





The Panasonic KX Cordless Telephone TG1611RUH should be prepared before use. You need to connect the telephone cable and plug from the AC adapter. The telephone cord, on the other hand, should be inserted into the telephone wall socket until it clicks; the adapter should be connected to the mains. DSL / ADSL filter is not included. To use this technology, it will need to be purchased separately.

After connecting to the outlet, the electric cord is secured with clamps. Do not connect the power supply to an outlet through an adapter or tee.

After connecting the wires to the base, the batteries must be inserted into the handset, in accordance with the polarity. Alkaline and manganese batteries must not be used for the device. The first charge of the batteries should last at least seven hours.

The electrical adapter must always be plugged in. From time to time, it may heat up, and this is considered the norm. The power supply is connected to an outlet located vertically or on the floor.

When a power outage, the device does not work. In this case, a regular telephone should be provided, operating only from a telephone line.

Making calls





The DECT Panasonic KX TG1611RUH cordless telephone allows you to both receive and make calls.

To make a call, you need to pick up the phone and dial the desired number. If the number is entered incorrectly, then it is deleted by pressing the C button. After the number is dialed, press the button with the image of the handset. At the end of the conversation, the cordless handset is placed on the base unit or the conversation end button is pressed. You can adjust the volume during a call. To do this, use the up and down buttons.

The ten most recently dialed numbers are saved and redialable. The length of each number dialed must not exceed 24 digits. To redial, select the desired number from the list and press the call button. Numbers from the redial list can be edited, saved and deleted. If during a conversation there is a need for a pause, then press the P button.

Answering a call should be done in the same way. When making a call to the telephone, press the call accept button twice. The first press determines the phone number, the second connects to the subscriber. To reject the call, press the crossed-out button.

About the phone book

The Panasonic KX TG1611RUH cordless telephone is equipped with a phone book. This function allows you to not dial the number every time in manual mode.

To add an entry to the phone book, click on the button with the image of the expanded book. Select the new entry option and press ok. After that, the fields for entering the name of the contact and his phone number will appear. After entering the data, double-click on approx.

To enter the following information, you need to click on OK, enter the name and phone number of the subscriber, and again on OK. This should be done until all numbers are entered in the book. After recording data, press the crossed-out button. To confirm the performance of these actions, you should once again go to the phone book, select the "Memory" option and click on OK.

To find the desired number in the phone book, you need to use the "Entire list" function. A list of phones will be displayed, after which you need to select the desired entry using the up and down buttons. To call the selected subscriber, press the button with the handset. In order to see the number, select the option "Show No.".

To edit the entry, go to the phone book, find the desired contact and select "Fix." Fields with already entered data will appear, they will be available for editing. After changing the data, click OK twice and exit the menu.

How to identify a subscriber





Supports the caller ID feature of a Panasonic telephone. The radiotelephone supports services such as Caller ID and Caller ID. The specified service is connected to the telephone company operator. By default, the device is set to the caller ID option.

Three modes are available for subscriber identification, these are:

  • AND HE;
  • disabling the caller ID function;
  • Caller ID (connected to the device in manual mode).

To find out the phone number of the person who is calling, you need to press the call button if the device is configured for Caller ID. If the Caller ID option is connected, then you do not need to click anywhere. Data will be displayed automatically.

When connected to the PBX information may be distorted. When using the Caller ID service, the display may show a file outside the region, which means that a person is calling from the area where the service is not provided. A hidden number may appear, indicating that the subscriber forbids to disclose information about himself.

Safety precautions





To make the device last longer, you should handle it correctly. Power sources must be used as indicated on the device. The power adapter must be fully inserted into the power outlet.

The power supply and handset need care. The device must be regularly wiped with a dry cloth, previously unplugged. Accumulation of dust can cause fire and damage to the unit. If the phone is smoking, they unplug it from the wall outlet, wait until it cools down and take it to a service center.

To avoid electric shock, do not take the plug with wet hands. The device should be protected from rain or other liquid. The phone is kept away from devices that are controlled automatically. The phone‚Äôs radio waves can interfere with their operation.

Errors and malfunctions

Despite the fact that this unit is of high quality and rarely breaks, from time to time it also malfunctions:

  • The memory is full. This means that the phone book is full and you need to delete unnecessary entries.
  • The handset does not turn on and the batteries are charged. The handset should be installed on the base and turned on.
  • The radiotelephone is not working. It is necessary to check the polarity of the batteries, the charge of the batteries, the connection of the base to the mains. You should temporarily disconnect the adapter from power to reset the settings, and turn it on after a certain time.
  • There is no information on the display. The handset is not turned on and needs to be turned on.
  • There is no tone signaling that the device is ready. Check all telephone network connections and adapter connections to electricity. Disconnect the unit from the telephone line. Connect the telephone line to another device, check if it works properly.

To avoid these and other malfunctions, instructions will help. The Panasonic KX Cordless Telephone TG1611RUH should only be used for its intended purpose, according to safety precautions.
