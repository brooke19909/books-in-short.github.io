iPhone: error 14. Reasons for appearance

As for the root causes of such a malfunction, they are first associated with physical breakdowns when the device cannot connect to a stationary computer via a USB cable.





The likely cause of this situation is often called physical cable damage, when the "apple" mobile device cannot be synchronized with Windows even if the corresponding program is installed in the environment of this operating system.

Error code 14: physical damage to the cable

First of all, check the connection density of the mobile device to the USB port. It may well be that the device is designed to connect USB 3.0, and you included it in USB 2.0. Recognizing the ports on a computer or laptop can be quite simple, since the port of the third version inside is painted blue.

It is possible that error 14 is also issued because the cable used for the connection has physical damage. Take another cord and check how the device will behave.

Exposure to viruses

Finally, it is believed that one of the causes of error 14 may be a viral effect. If iTunes works in its own environment (meaning iOS and Mac OS x), it is not affected by malicious codes (viruses do not exist for such systems in nature).

In addition, there is such a thing as an additional Bonjour applet, which is installed in the system regardless of the user's wishes when installing the main iTunes package. Most of these viruses simply replace the original applet or disguise themselves in the Task Manager when viewing active system processes.
