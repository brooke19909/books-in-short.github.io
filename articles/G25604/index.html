Impressions of guests about the housing stock of the hotel

The rooms in Serenity Makadi Beach 5 * seemed to our compatriots very spacious and well decorated. However, according to some tourists, some of the apartments need a little cosmetic repair. Pleasantly surprised travelers with more than spacious bathrooms. People having a rest here recommend taking a room with a sea view if possible. In addition, the apartments have a large balcony with furniture, where you can comfortably accommodate and watch the beautiful sunset. The rooms also include electric kettles and coffee and tea sets. All equipment here works without interruption. If something fails, then notify the administrator at the reception, and the problem will be fixed very quickly. The guests were also pleased with the fact that there were coolers in the corridors. There is no iron in the rooms, but it can always be borrowed at the reception if necessary.

As for cleaning, it was carried out daily and very carefully. Regularly replenished stocks of soap and shampoo in the bathroom. Also, the maids were not lazy to build intricate figures from towels and sheets. In particular, children were delighted with such swans, crocodiles and other creatures. According to the guests, towels in Serenity Makadi Beach 5 * cleaners change most often only if they notice a dirty towel thrown on the floor. Sheets are updated a couple of times a week.

Travelers' opinions on food and staff at Serenity Makadi Beach

As for food in Serenity Makadi Beach 5 * (Hurghada, Egypt), then most of the guests were very pleased with the buffet menu in the main restaurant of the hotel. Guest reviews of beach vacations

As for a beach holiday in Serenity Makadi Beach 5 *, then almost all tourists were satisfied with it. So, in their opinion, the hotel‚Äôs own beach is very large and comfortable. Near the coast, the sea is shallow, so you can wander knee-deep in some water and play with the kids. Those who want to swim, it is better to go into the sea on a wooden pier.
