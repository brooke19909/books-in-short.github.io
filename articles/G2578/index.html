Section 2. Arkaim (Chelyabinsk region). Opening stages

It turns out that this historical monument was opened not once, but three at once. For the first time, in 1957, it was discovered and mapped by the military. After some time, civilian cartographers noticed Arkaim from the air, but they decided that this strange object was classified, so it never got on the cards.

Another 18 years have passed. In the summer of 1987, schoolchildren from the local archaeological circle went to this territory. Their task was not to search for this monument at all, the guys were instructed to examine the territory with the aim of further building a special irrigation system necessary for establishing water supply in the agricultural areas of the region. They did not attach particular importance to this mission. However, during the work in the steppe zone , the remains of a settlement under the name Arkaim (Chelyabinsk region) were discovered.
