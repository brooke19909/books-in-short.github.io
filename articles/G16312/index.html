Where and when is it better to go to the UAE? Features of nature and tourism in the Emirates

Luxurious life and huge tourist flows are the main signs of the United Arab Emirates (UAE). The young Federation of seven independent states occupies 600 km of the southern coast of the Persian Gulf. Many travelers wonder: “When is the best time to go to the UAE?” We will figure out which seasons and destinations are preferable for a vacation in the Emirates.





Legacy of the past and modern luxury

The oldest settlements in the current territory of the Federation appeared in 5500 BC. e. For several centuries AD, it was under the control of the Portuguese, Ottoman and British monarchs. Emirates gained independence only in the 60s of the last century. It has long been mined pearls, considered one of the best in the world. Later, the richest oil and gas reserves were found, which ensured the country's prosperity.





Natural conditions, despite the small size of the territory under consideration, are diverse, although about 80% of the area is sandy and rocky landscapes. The oasis revived by the landscape, covered with “bread” of the Arab peoples - date palms, acacia trees and eucalyptus trees. Everywhere you see the beds of drying rivers - the wadi. In the east, the rocky peaks of the Hajar mountains rise, painted with paints of all shades.

When is the best time to go to the UAE?

Half a century ago, only dunes, rare caravans of camels laden with bales, fishing boats in the bay could be seen here. Now, against the backdrop of the desert, magnificent city buildings flaunt, lively resorts are spread out. Thinking about when to go to the UAE, it should be noted that the entire territory of the Federation is significantly affected by dry tropical air. The best time to travel is from October to May, when it is not too hot (25-30 ° C). The water temperature in the bay in winter is 15–20 ° C, and by May it reaches 30 ° C. In the summer, air in cities heats up to 38–42 ° C, water - up to 35 ° C. On a well-maintained sea coast, the climate is not as dry and hot as in the desert outback, where the thermometer does not always stop at 50 ° C (June-August).





In the northern part of the UAE, there are more fertile lands; there are fresh water reserves. In terms of weather, the most comfortable months for visiting the emirate of Ras al-Khaimah are November-March, when the temperature is 25–35 ° C. From the obsessive heat and stuffiness of the desert you can hide under the canopy of the garden city of Al Ain. There is no sea in this part of the Federation. The air is sultry all year long. But mountain peaks and cool springs protect from the heat of the tropical Sun. An oasis generously supplied with underground water, fragrant with exotic greenery.

What to do on vacation in the UAE?

So, you have a long-awaited vacation ahead! It's time to decide where and when to go. In the UAE, you can fully enjoy a well-deserved rest and remember these days for life. You can experience the exotic and spicy spirit of the Arab East in any of the seven emirates. Here is a short list of activities that the local resorts can offer you:

  • serene elite rest on the shore of luxurious pools, spa treatments in hotels;
  • water tours of the Persian Gulf on the original floating Dhow craft;
  • visiting cultural and historical sights;
  • visiting exotic oriental bazaars;
  • active water recreation and diving in the Persian Gulf;
  • jeep and camel safaris;
  • shopping in shopping centers, boutiques, souvenir shops in Dubai, Abu Dhabi and other cities;
  • climbing the mountain peaks of Hajar;
  • Visit to a wild reserve in the emirate of Umm al-Kaiwan.




UAE attractions: where is the best place to go?

The Persian Gulf is the main source of prosperity for the state. On its shore is the capital Abu Dhabi and the second largest emirate - Dubai - the tourist center of the UAE. When is it better to go to rest so that the heat and heat do not spoil the impression of new buildings and elite resorts that are striking in their color? Autumn or winter. It's damp and cold here, and there is heaven on earth. Infrastructure, business activity, trade, environmental care are at the highest level. Recreation, recreation and entertainment in Dubai year-round delight with its sophistication. Abu Dhabi is a kind of Middle Eastern Manhattan, but with a lot of Muslim shrines. The bazaars of the city fascinate with a huge selection of spices, exotic fruits, craftsmen's products. There are a lot of greenery, parks, gardens, fountains that give the desired coolness. In the capital cities of seven states of the Federation, you can find budget accommodation options and luxury 5-star hotels. In Dubai, Abu Dhabi and Sharjah there are the most prestigious, including unique, hotels with 6 and 7 star status. Amazing in architecture, they offer a sky-high level of luxury, all types of service and leisure.





Variety of conditions in the Emirates

Sometimes fans of different types of recreation have doubts: is it worth going to the UAE? They are associated with the increased popularity of the Emirates, a very large influx of tourists. But this does not mean at all that because of travelers and vacationers there is nowhere to step forward. In addition, if you do not want to be, so to speak, at the epicenter of events, there are directions and attractions that are less “untwisted”, but also very interesting. The rich palette of the Persian Gulf, mountain and desert regions leaves no room for doubt. In the Emirates, conditions have been created for all types of tourism. These include:

  1. Water. Numerous islands, coral reefs and sandbanks rise above the blue surface of the bay, where you can get acquainted with marine life. Organized access to the open sea on fishing boats for everyone.
  2. Extreme. In desert areas, outside cities, sand dunes explode with yellow fountains under the wheels of mighty jeeps. Caravans accompany tourists sitting on the backs of living “desert ships”. Camel races are held on a special track.
  3. Informative. Architectural pearls - mosques, palaces, forts, modern skyscrapers. Archaeological excavations are underway in the territory of the Emirates, and artifacts found in the earth occupy a worthy place in the expositions of numerous museums. Live “exhibits” can be seen in zoos, aquariums.
  4. Wellness. Bathing in the mineral water of mountain springs will be remembered for a long time.
  5. Ecological, gastronomic and other types of tourism are developing. In some cities, prohibition is in force ; in others, mores are more free.




The most attractive destinations for elite relaxation are, of course, Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah and Ajaman. If this pleasure is beyond your means, do not rush to get upset: in each of the emirates the charm of the East is combined with luxury and modern entertainment of the highest level.
