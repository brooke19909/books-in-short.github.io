The most fashionable jackets for women spring-summer 2013

The jacket is one of the basic elements of a women's wardrobe. It is practical, very comfortable and suitable for both business style and evening out. Jackets, originally designed exclusively for men, at the dawn of their existence were sewed mainly in the military style, which is extremely popular now among women. It is impossible to imagine a modern urban woman without a jacket or jacket.

Women's fashion jackets for spring-summer 2013 are presented mainly in dark colors. Black, brown, brown, dark green, gray - these are their primary colors. Military style involves the presence of lapels, wide belts, large buttons and even shoulder straps. Despite such decor elements, women's jackets in the army style do not hide femininity, and even vice versa, emphasize the dignity of the figure.





For romantic young ladies, women's fashion jackets for the spring-summer season of 2013 are presented in the form of shortened jackets of light, pastel shades. You can wear such jackets with leggings, jeans, high-waisted trousers and even with puffy skirts and light lace dresses. Light-colored women's jackets are perfect for summer evening walks - they will not only be a great addition to a chiffon dress or skirt, but also protect against evening coolness or light wind. The combination of a jacket with jeans is a great option for every day, and with pants - for the office and business meetings.

2013 fashionable women's jackets are also presented in oriental-style, tweed, Coco Chanel-style models , and elongated - cardigans. In spring and autumn, fur jackets remain relevant . The best decoration for a fur jacket is a wide and effective belt. Elongated jackets also look great in combination with a belt - this visually makes the figure more slim, visually increases the length of the legs. However, bold and extravagant ladies this season will be able to experiment, giving preference to deliberately rude, emphasized-male models of jackets in the style of boyfriend. In this case, famous designers prefer the brightest and most flashy colors - from coral red to leopard print.





Young girls who actively attend parties and nightclubs also did not go unnoticed by fashion designers - they are offered a wide range of jackets made of shiny fabrics. Women's jackets for young and active are also represented by original models with short sleeves or even completely sleeveless. Long, reaching almost to the middle of the thigh, such a jacket looks absolutely unsportsmanlike in contrast to a classic vest.

The most fashionable women's cardigans (photo)





Despite the diversity of the range of jackets, the most popular models of the spring-summer season will remain classic, elongated or shortened, but certainly fitted models of calm shades in combination with a belt, on one button, on a ribbon, or without buttons and any other mounts. However, there are no strict recommendations when choosing a jacket in the spring-summer 2013 season, so every woman can choose a model to her taste, suitable for a particular situation.
