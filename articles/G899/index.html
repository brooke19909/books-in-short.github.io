The objective need for construction



The history of the White Tower (Yekaterinburg) is interesting and sad. It arose simultaneously with the Ural Heavy Engineering Plant (in March 1928, immediately after the liberation of the city from Kolchak, the first stone of the plant was laid) and was called the Water Tower of UZTM. It was located in the Ordzhonikidze district of the city - then Sverdlovsk. She was needed by shock workers. Water was brought to the social town for the needs of residents by horses. In 1928, with the support of the chief engineer of Uralmash V. Fidler, a competition was held. Constructivism - a style characterized by rigor, geometry, laconic forms and monolithic appearance, was in great fashion. The sketch of V.M. Reisher won. The structure was erected on a hill, its own height is 29 meters. All this made the tower dominant over the surrounding territory.

The start went wrong



A beautiful modern facility, the White Tower (Yekaterinburg), personified a bright future. The building was bleached with lime and immediately received the appropriate name among the people, different from the official one. Despite the fact that amendments taking into account safety measures were made by V. Fidler himself (the condition that the tank should be only metal, and the need for additional legs under it), there were some troubles. In the manufacture of the huge, largest at that time in the world (in Soviet Russia there was such an understandable weakness, because the country itself is the largest in the world), a completely new technology was applied. Welding seams replaced the rivet, but the tank did not pass the first test, and 750 cubic meters of water poured down from a 29-meter height down through a torn bottom. Not only did this cause the untimely death of the wonderful man Vladimir Fedorovich Fidler, but death saved him from the fate of the ‚Äúenemy of the people." The building, however, after correcting the errors taken into account, was restored and successfully operated until 1960.

White tower today

An abandoned object is what the White Tower (Yekaterinburg) has been since then. Her address: st. Donbasskaya, d. 1. In 1993, the car market ‚ÄúAt the White Tower‚Äù appeared at the foot of an abandoned facility. And after he received his first visitors, this trading company regularly increased year by year, new points of sale of spare parts, service facilities appeared. Now at the same address is a huge auto show "White Tower". Yekaterinburg officially opened this complex in 2013.

Occupying 13,000 square meters meters, designed for 700 cars, the White Tower autocall is the central automobile platform of Yekaterinburg and the Sverdlovsk Region. It is located next to the famous White Tower Market. The three-story car dealership contains 9 service units, 1 tire fitting and 4 washing. Thanks to this largest enterprise, the name of the abandoned object is well known to everyone. Maybe this will help draw the attention of the authorities to him and move the problem of an interesting historical monument off the ground.
