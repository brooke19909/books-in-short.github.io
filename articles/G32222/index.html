Hotel Amaan Bungalows Nungwi 3 *, Tanzania, Zanzibar: photos, reviews of tourists

Some of our compatriots dream to spend the winter holidays so that then they will remember about this vacation for a long time. Asian countries, previously popular, are gradually fading into the background and the Russians are looking for new exotic places in which vacations can be turned into a real adventure.





Increasing interest in travel agencies has been noted for Tanzania. This African country attracts tourists with its originality, magnificent beaches, amazing turquoise ocean and unusual excursion programs. A significant disadvantage of such permits is the fact that the classification of local hotels often does not coincide with the European accepted throughout the world. Based on this, a hotel complex, which is awarded three stars, can be much better and richer than a five-star hotel. However, this does not always happen, and sometimes three stars mean a budget complex with a minimum of services. In addition, there are not so many real reviews about Tanzania hotels on the Internet, which also causes certain difficulties.

If you are also thinking about spending your holidays in a similar place, we suggest you stay at Amaan Bungalows Nungwi. Traveler reviews of this hotel characterize it as a pleasant place to experience local traditions and cuisine. As you probably already guessed, our article is devoted to rest in this hotel complex. Welcome to Amaan Bungalows Nungwi!

General description of the hotel

Amaan Bungalows Nungwi 3 * refers to mini-hotels, the number of rooms in which does not exceed eighty rooms. Therefore, when you come here, you can not worry about the fact that there will be a huge number of other vacationers disturbing your peace.

The hotel is built on the coast and many balconies literally hang over the water, revealing magnificent views of the coral reefs. The hotel territory is surrounded by greenery, tourists enthusiastically describe the lush bushes, exotic flowers and trees that make the complex literally in the Garden of Eden.

Amid this beauty, it is somehow difficult to focus on the small territory of Amaan Bungalows Nungwi 3 * hotel. In addition, its area satisfies all the requirements for a three-star hotel complex. Therefore, it is quite comfortable for all categories of tourists.

Amaan Bungalows Nungwi 3 * building is a low-rise building that fits perfectly with the surrounding beauty.

Hotel Location

The Amaan Bungalows Hotel is located in Tanzania's Zanzibar Nungwi. This small and calm village is built on the north coast of the island. She can not boast of anything remarkable, but tourists say that it is very pleasant to relax here, thanks to the cordiality and kindness of local residents.

The hotel complex has a very convenient location in relation to cafes, restaurants and markets. Often, tourists write in their reviews that the prices in shops and on spontaneous trading floors are quite reasonable. At the same time, for the asking price, you can become the owner of truly unique and high-quality things.

A clear minus of Amaan Bungalows Nungwi can be considered the fact that it is located at a sufficient distance from Kisauni Airport. From it to the hotel will have to overcome more than fifty kilometers. Therefore, do not recklessly go on vacation with young children. It will be quite difficult for them to endure a difficult and long journey. In addition, tourism in Tanzania is under development, and medical care even under insurance leaves much to be desired.





Beach area

If you look at the photo of Amaan Bungalows Nungwi 3 *, then you will definitely come across an image of the beach area. Most vacationers write that just for the sake of this heavenly pleasure it is worth going to the hotel. The beach adjacent to the hotel consists of white sand and has a very convenient entrance to the water. Only in some places stones are found, but they are clearly visible under the water column.

Swimming on the beach can be easily diversified by diving or snorkeling. Near Amaan Bungalows Nungwi 3 * there is a beautiful coral reef. There are many species of fish and other marine inhabitants. Most tourists spend all their time ashore, conquered by local beauties.

Tourists notice that the beach is quite well equipped by local standards. On its territory there is a pier with sunbeds, a small number of sun loungers are set in the sand. You can enjoy refreshing drinks at the coastal bar, but keep in mind that freshly squeezed juices and local alcohol can be considered the most delicious. It is of good quality, but you should not order cocktails from it at the bar. Since most of the Tanzanians profess Islam, they do not drink alcohol-containing drinks and rather unsuccessfully mixes them together.

Infrastructure

Amaan Bungalows Nungwi 3 * (Zanzibar), judging by the reviews, has not too luxurious infrastructure, but tourists will be provided with everything necessary for a good stay.

The hotel complex is made in the Tanzanian style, which shines literally in every piece of furniture. In the same design, all the buildings, sculptures and infrastructure in the hotel are made. Many of his guests celebrate the relaxation area by the pool. He is in Amaan Bungalows Nungwi 3 * alone, so there are always going to quite a lot of tourists. The guests write that the pool is regularly cleaned, the water in it always looks clear and fresh. However, if you plan to spend the day in this recreation area, then the sun loungers should be occupied even before breakfast. Otherwise, all the sun loungers will be occupied by other vacationers.

The hotel has one restaurant and bar, where guests are always happy to offer snacks and soft drinks.

At the reception, vacationers can use luggage storage, there is also a safe. Our compatriots are advised to definitely use this service, since in Tanzania there is a high probability of theft. Therefore, in order not to spoil your vacation, you should protect yourself and not lead into the temptation of potential thieves.

Free parking and a currency exchange service are available. However, guests are still advised to change money in the village. There you will be offered a more favorable rate, although the difference in this case will not be significant. If desired, local institutions can pay in US dollars. They are freely accepted in Zanzibar.





Facilities and service

Parsing this topic, do not forget that Amaan Nungwi Beach Resort Amaan Bungalows 3 * is a three-star hotel. Therefore, coming here, be prepared for some of the features of the local flavor. They consist in the fact that all the attendants, although friendly, are lazy and slow. All your requests, of course, will be fulfilled, but not always exactly as necessary. It is worth accepting and not spoiling your vacation with unnecessary claims to the staff.

The hotel has free Wi-Fi, the signal is sometimes very stable and allows you to freely surf the Internet, and sometimes disappears.

From the reviews of Amaan Bungalows Nungwi, it can be concluded that the hotel provides a transfer from the hotel at the request of guests. This service requires additional payment, however, its cost is not high, and time savings will be significant. In addition, it is much more pleasant to get to the hotel in a comfortable individual minibus than with the rest of the tourists who arrived in Tanzania.

The hotel also provides dry cleaning along with laundry. If you need to clean clothes, feel free to contact here.

Entertainment

Almost all of the entertainment at the hotel is paid. However, many vacationers use them to diversify their leisure, and are very satisfied.

For guests who wish to relax, the hotel staff can offer spa access and massages. Vacationers respond very well about it and advise potential tourists to definitely use this service.

Water activities are organized at a high level in the hotel. Guests have the opportunity to book diving, surfing, fishing and various excursion programs for themselves. By the way, our compatriots in their comments note that it is quite safe to take excursions in the hotel and on the street. You will enjoy the sea adventures that you can offer.

Hotel accommodation with children

Of course, our compatriots do not often go to Tanzania with their children. However, if you plan such an adventure, then know that you will be happy to be accommodated in a hotel room with a child of any age.

Children under four years old can stay with their parents for free. They are provided with cots. For children up to twelve years old, hotel staff will put an extra bed in the room. Twenty-five dollars a night must be paid for it.

Only one bed can be installed in the apartment, otherwise the room will be very crowded and uncomfortable.





Catering

Tours to the hotel complex are sold only with breakfast included in the price. This is enough for most tourists, because they mostly dine with fruits, and go to local restaurants for dinner on the coast.

If you want to dine on site, you will be offered local and European dishes, as well as a wide selection of seafood.

Breakfast is served in buffet style. They begin at half past seven in the morning and end at ten in the morning. Many guests note that most of the guests come to the restaurant to the opening itself, so there are very few empty seats at this time. However, after half an hour or an hour the influx of visitors subsides, and the dishes are updated.

Some of our compatriots come to the beginning of breakfast for coffee, and then go to the pool. And after an hour they return and begin to eat.

The choice of dishes is quite standard for any treshka. Guests will be offered fried eggs, scrambled eggs, hot pancakes, stewed vegetables and potatoes, sausages and various gravy with meat. Also available is always a wide selection of fruits, biscuits, several types of pastries, cereals, milk, coffee and tea.

In general, such an assortment is enough to not remain hungry. In addition, all dishes are very tasty and satisfying, so tourists generally praise the local cuisine.

Music is played in the bar in the evenings, and guests can enjoy fresh drinks and cocktails. The terrace offers gorgeous views of the Indian Ocean.





Brief description of the number of rooms

Almost all apartments in the hotel are ascetic. They are furnished with a minimum of furniture and many guests lack nightstands, cabinets and chairs. There are fifty-two rooms in the hotel, which are divided into three categories:

  • Sea View Rooms. Sixteen rooms of this type are located above the water itself, they literally hang over the ocean, as they are on the beach itself. These apartments are quite spacious and designed for one or two people.
  • Deluxe rooms. Seventeen rooms can accommodate three people, they are located in a certain distance from the beach area, surrounded by shady trees.
  • Garden view rooms. You can stay here with your family, but note that beds in nineteen rooms of this category are only single.

All rooms are made in the Zanzibar style and look very unusual. Please note that there is a lack of a kettle in the rooms. Therefore, you will not be able to enjoy evening tea or hot coffee on the balcony.

Voucher cost

An African trip, by definition, cannot be cheap. A tour for ten days from Moscow will cost two tourists from one hundred twenty-eight to two hundred thousand rubles, depending on the day of departure.

However, if you wish, you can purchase a two-week tour for about the same cost. To do this, it is worth contacting different agencies and subscribing to the newsletter in order to monitor all changes in value in real time.





Amaan Bungalows Nungwi 3 *: reviews

Before you go on a long and not quite ordinary trip, it is worth carefully studying the reviews of our compatriots who managed to visit this hotel. So what do they write about their vacation?

Of course, all tourists note a certain poverty of local residents, a low level of service and a rustic atmosphere in the rooms. But if you are ready for discoveries and not too picky, then you will definitely like Amaan Bungalows Nungwi.

Here you are guaranteed a warm welcome, delicious food, an abundance of interesting excursions and non-trivial shopping, which will please fans of exclusive and rare things. The hotel offers a magnificent beach with a clear ocean and the opportunity to enjoy nature almost untouched by man.

Our compatriots recommend the hotel complex to tourists without children, set up for outdoor activities, and large companies.
