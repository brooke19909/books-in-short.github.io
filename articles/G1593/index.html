Bali Island, Seminyak - a resort for the rich. Description and reviews of tourists

For many, an indicator of well-being in recent years has become the opportunity to visit the island of Bali for recreation . Seminyak, one of its most prestigious regions, is dearly loved by wealthy tourists from Europe and the United States. There is everything that travelers demand for exotic, and at the same time, you will not be deprived of any of the achievements of civilization. If the resort, located in the neighborhood - Kutu, is called respectable, then Seminyak has earned a reputation for glamorous. And not without reason.





Why go here

We can ask a question about how and why travelers with money choose when arriving in Bali, Seminyak. Of course, first of all, for the sake of its beach - clean, wide, with fine sand, as if arising from brochures. Accommodation here is also prestigious and of high quality. The region is convenient and compact. It has all the necessary infrastructure, and in addition, everything you need is right around the corner. Most restaurants in Seminyak are awarded different awards for cuisine and class service. For art lovers, the resort has art galleries. And after sunset, a rich and vibrant nightlife begins.

How to get there

To get to this resort village, you need to fly to the airport Ngurah Rai (Bali). Seminyak is located from this hub (its second name is more popular, it is Denpasar) no more than half an hour away. Rarely does anyone get here on their own. The vast majority of hotels send customers a shuttle service, which is included in the price. But if no one meets you, then you can take a taxi.

Some companies provide machines at the front desk, but say it is too expensive. And if you go outside and call a Blue Bird taxi, then pay the average price for Bali. If you use public transport, it is not too convenient. The bus does not stop in Seminyak, but only in neighboring Kut. And from there, about a quarter of an hour you need to go to the place.

Where to sunbathe and swim

It is believed that the local beaches are the best in Bali. Seminyak is famous for its coast. They are not crowded, snow-white, very wide, there is always a lot of sun. It is believed that there are two beaches in this area, but it is unlikely that anyone will be able to say exactly where one begins and the other ends. The first of them - Double Six - is called in the English style, and the second - Petinenget - in Indonesian. They are all well equipped, but there are differences.

Double Six will appeal more to families with children. There are many water activities, a lot of cafes and a beautiful promenade. Therefore, in the evenings, festivities are arranged. And Petingenet got its name from the temple of the same name, located very close by. There are always big waves, so surfers like him very much. In addition, there are practically no people here. But in no case do not go into the water if you see a red flag on the beach! This means that strong currents have come that can carry you away.
