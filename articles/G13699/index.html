Berdyansk Spit

The city of Berdyansk is considered the capital of the Azov resorts of Ukraine. The infrastructure of the city is very developed, and in the summer the resort is crowded with vacationers.

The pride of Berdyansk is a spit deep in the sea, which is one of the largest in the Sea of ‚Äã‚ÄãAzov. Its length is about twenty-five kilometers.





In this unique corner of nature, many boarding houses, recreation centers, hotels, resorts and children's camps are located, which are located directly by the sea throughout the spit. There is everything for a good rest: markets, shops, cafes and restaurants, night clubs, a water park, amusement parks and water activities.

The coast of the Berdyansk spit is a long strip of the cleanest sandy beach. Water in the sea here is much cleaner than in Berdyansk itself.

Shuttle buses constantly run to the city, so vacationers can easily get to the center of Berdyansk in a matter of minutes. And there is a lot of entertainment: the promenade, restaurants, the zoo, endless souvenir shops and many other attractions.

Guest reviews

Hotel "Gold Coast" (Berdyansk) - quite a popular holiday destination. There are plenty of comments about it, and based on the reviews of vacationers, we can conclude about the pros and cons of the hotel.

Almost every review speaks of the magnificent well-groomed territory of the hotel. The rooms, according to tourists, are all worthy, with a fresh renovation, clean and very comfortable. The hotel infrastructure is also praised, no one was bored.

Food, like everything else, was not satisfactory. Vacationers talk about the variety of dishes, large portions and delicious food.

Based on all of the above, we can make an unambiguous conclusion about the recreation center "Golden Coast" (Berdyansk).
