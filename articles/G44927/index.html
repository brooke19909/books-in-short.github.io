Not sure what White Lake is? Altai Territory will tell you about him

Tourism is the most popular type of outdoor activity. Today, many refuse to travel to various resorts in the world and prefer the vastness of their native land to them . Altai Territory is especially popular among holidaymakers. After all, it is full of amazing natural places that amaze with their beauty. To see the White Lake, the Altai Territory is visited annually by many visitors. Especially for these purposes, a comfortable sanatorium is being built today on the lake, and soon it will begin to receive vacationers.

Location of White Lake





White Lake has a convenient location in a very wide basin of the Kolyvan Ridge and is almost round in shape. Numerous tourists seek to visit Lake Beloe (Altai Territory) on vacation. How to get there - you can see on any navigation map. It is located almost 300 kilometers from Barnaul. If you go in the direction from Kolyvan, then after 8 kilometers from the village on March 8 there will be a lake shore. Moreover, the road here is asphalt and convenient for driving on any car. For those who have never been to these parts, it will be useful to find out about the Lake Beloe (Altai Territory) reviews of tourists who were lucky to visit it. It is connected by an artificial channel to the river, bearing the same name - Belaya. To prevent the lake from becoming shallow, the mouth is closed with a special dam. It functions perfectly and regulates the water level in the lake.

Beauty of White Lake





The beautiful natural landscapes of the lakeside territories make here come back again and again. A deep lake with clear water is striking in its crystal transparency. Despite the depth, the bottom is clearly visible. Mount Sinyuha, located in the same place as the White Lake, Altai Krai glorifies more than other attractions. On the lake itself there is an island of granite rocks, which attracts a variety of legends and traditions. Someone says that the island is artificial, while others claim its natural formation. Over time, the island is completely overgrown with trees and shrubs, which makes the view simply fantastic. There is a feeling that the trees grow directly from the water. For tourists, the northern part of the lake is particularly attractive, since the shore here is more gentle and allows you to go down and swim in warm waters. Moreover, it is here that there is a small beach of fine sand. The southern and eastern shores are steeper, although there are coastal strips consisting of pebbles. On all sides is surrounded by numerous shrubs of White Lake. Altai Territory is rich in honeysuckle, viburnum, wild rose.

Fishing for visitors





The lake is very rich in fish, many tourists come here precisely for this purpose. Especially for lovers of fishing on the lake, moorings with boats are equipped. Since the shores of the lake are overgrown with shrubs, and the coastal strip is very shallow, fishing is possible only from boats. The Altai Territory , rich in fish, makes the region of the country the richest in natural resources. Here you can catch carps, perches, pikes, as well as many other types of fish. In a short period of time you can get a decent catch. The main thing is not to abuse the gifts of nature and take as much as you need, and not because of excitement.

Competitions for fishermen

Today, there are special tourist programs for gambling fishermen, during which there are competitions for fishing. However, here, after the fish was weighed, it must be released back into the lake. This satisfies the requirements of fishermen and preserves the ecological situation without reducing the number of fish stock.
