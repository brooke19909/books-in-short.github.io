The cheapest microwave. How to choose a microwave and not overpay

Nowadays, manufacturers of microwave ovens literally climb out of their hands, trying to make their products as functional as possible, capable of dozens of operations. Their technique does not just perform its immediate task - it warms up and thawes. She also fries, steamed, baked foods and cooks a variety of dishes. But such functionality is not necessary for everyone. Therefore, before buying, you should carefully study how cheap models differ from expensive ones, and also for which options it is worth overpaying and for which not.

How much a microwave is worth depends on a number of factors. First of all, on the type of control. In the second, from the manufacturer. Also, the cost is affected by the presence of additional functions, a grill, power level, color and volume.

Control

It can be mechanical or electronic. The latter option also includes microwave ovens with a touch panel. Budget models are devices with mechanical control. It is on them that it is worth paying attention to buyers who are thinking about how to choose a microwave oven and not overpay.



The mechanical control is very simple. There are 2 handles on the panel: one controls power, the other a timer. If you want to warm up a bowl of soup, a couple of minutes are enough, and at least 10-15 may be needed to defrost a large volume of products. Some customers are wondering why power adjustment is needed. Let the microwave work at maximum! After all, so she warms up and cooks faster. You can‚Äôt argue with this statement, however, in the mode of defrosting products, maximum power is not needed and it must be reduced. Otherwise, you can get dried on the surface and ice inside the product.

Electronics provides much more opportunities. For models with this control, a display appears on which you can see the time remaining until the end of cooking / heating. Electronics allows you to accurately set the duration of work depending on the type and weight of the product. Such models are intended not only for defrosting and warming up, but also for preparing various dishes.



Customers who are interested in the cheapest microwave without additional options should pay attention to models with mechanical control.

Volume

Standard models come in a volume of 17-23 liters. Some buyers mistakenly think that the cheapest microwave will be small. The smaller the volume, the lower the price. There is indeed a correlation between these two characteristics, however, some small microwaves are not cheap at all, for example, Hotpoint-Ariston MWHA 13321 CAC. This is due to the non-standard sizes of such equipment, which is rarely found in stores and can be considered an exclusive product. The main advantage of such models is not the price, but the small dimensions, which allow you to save space in the kitchen.

Capacious microwave ovens are usually taken to prepare or heat a large volume of products. A spacious 26-30 liter chamber will easily include a wide dish or a whole turkey.

Dimensions

You should also pay attention to this characteristic if you need a microwave. Sizes of models are not always indicated by sellers of goods. To obtain reliable information about the dimensions of the equipment, you should refer to the instruction manual. A small microwave does not take up much space on the countertop, fits easily on a shelf, or enters a small niche.



The dimensions of microwave ovens indirectly depend on their volume. Obviously, models with a 30-35 liter camera simply cannot be small. However, the increase in size can occur both due to changes in height and width, and due to depth. Therefore, before buying, you need to evaluate each parameter separately, and not just the dimensions as a whole.

Grill function

If you need a microwave oven not only for heating, but also for cooking, you should pay attention to the model with grill. This device is an additional heating element inside the chamber that allows you to use the microwave as a small oven: prepare hot sandwiches, bake pies, cook porridge. The products are not cooked-stewed, but delicious, fried, with a crispy crust. How much does a microwave with grill cost? Definitely more expensive than without it, after all, don‚Äôt worry, but you need to pay extra for an additional function. An example of a budget model with grill is the Horizont 20MW700-1378B.

Power

If the buyer does not want to wait 3-4 minutes for a serving of soup to warm up, you should pay attention to such an indicator as power. Depending on the model, it can fluctuate in the range of 500-1500 watts. The higher the number, the faster the appliance will cook, the more expensive it will cost.

Color

The cheapest microwave is classic white. It is universal and suitable for any interior. If the microwave is needed in gray or steel, you will have to pay extra for the design.

Camera coating

It is no secret that during operation the inner surface of the camera is very dirty. Therefore, to facilitate the work of housewives, manufacturers have developed a special coating - easy-clean enamel. Compared to a normal surface, it is much easier to wipe off grease and soot spatter from it. Easy-clean enamel is gray or blue and slightly increases the cost of the microwave oven.





Manufacturer

The price of the microwave, of course, affects the place of assembly. For many years now, Asian manufacturers ‚Äî Korea and China ‚Äî have been leading the market, and the quality of the equipment they produce is steadily growing. The cheapest microwave oven with a probability of 99.9% will be collected in these countries. European manufacturers, not inferior in quality, are far ahead in price. The Gorenie microwave oven assembled in Slovenia will cost two times more than the similar functionally assembled model in China.

Additional functions

These include: convection, a double boiler, a double-triple grill, automatic cooking and a number of similar options. All of them, one way or another, increase the cost of microwave ovens. A model with a lot of additional functions should be taken only if the owner plans to cook in it regularly. For example, everyone in the family adheres to a healthy diet and regularly prepares diet meals. In this case, a microwave with an integrated double boiler will become an indispensable assistant for them. Otherwise, the money will be thrown to the wind.
