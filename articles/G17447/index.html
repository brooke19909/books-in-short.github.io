Ski resort Ore, Sweden: slopes, accommodation, reviews

Many contemporaries are familiar with the glorious kingdom of Sweden. Its territory was concentrated in northern Europe, on the Scandinavian peninsula. In the north-west of Sweden, the Scandinavian mountains stretch. The temperate climate of the country, moving from marine to continental, allows tourists to enjoy winter fun. From December to March, guests are welcomed by the ski slopes in Ore (Sweden). Many skiers, even those who have visited the Alps, have fallen in love with this Scandinavian winter resort. In Sweden, "Ore" is considered a highly organized place with a well-thought-out system of lifts, modern equipment rental points. Here is an excellent European service, comfortable hotels. We invite you to get acquainted with the main routes, accommodation and reviews of the resort Are.

Winter activities at the ski resort "Ore"

If you look at the map, you can see that the western part of the country is located near Norway. "Ore" in Sweden is the largest resort in the country, Stockholm is located 620 km from it. You can fly there from Russia to the nearest airport, Ostresund. The location of the ski slopes was the foot of the wooded mountains. The highest of them - Oreskutan, has a height of 1429 km.

In 2008, the international magazine Conde Nast Traveler conducted an annual rating of ski resorts, where the Swedish "Ore" became the winner. After arriving at this place, tourists will enjoy a special atmosphere. First impressions cannot be described in words. "Ore" in Sweden gives a feeling of home comfort. Here, they not only ride the most modern ski equipment, but also enjoy the sun and tea in the mountain huts.

Any friendly youth company will find here ways for entertainment, because this place is considered a resort of world significance. It is distinguished by nightlife, bars, restaurants, clubs. Those who wish can dance here all night, try gourmet food in a national setting.

4 main areas for skiing and living

"Ore" in Sweden occupies a rather large territory, divided into four zones. The first of these is called Are By. The second zone is Ore Duved, the third is Ore Tegefal, and the fourth is Ore Bernen. All these areas are connected by bus routes, which adhere to a strict schedule.

Reviews about the "Ore" in Sweden indicate that it is well equipped and has a European level of service. The slopes are thoroughly prepared by snow groomers, all tracks at night are well lit. Almost the entire season, natural snow lies on the tracks. If necessary, snow dusting from special cannons is connected. There are developed tracks for downhill and flat skis. Snowboard lovers have opened a snow park. In "Au" everything has been done so that the skiers enjoy the beautiful long and wide slopes. All lifts are united by a common system for the resort. The location of hotels and apartments is also carefully thought out.

Ski slopes "Ore"

In total, there are 89 slopes of varying difficulty in Ore. The safest are green tracks, there are 18. There are blue (34), then red (30), black (4) and black and red (3). The length of the longest slope is 6.5 km. The highest descent is 1274 m above sea level. The total mileage of all processed routes is 97 km. To lift the skiers on the slopes, 42 lifts operate. In one hour they let in up to 50,000 people.

Other resort features

What other activities besides skiing from the slopes can be found in Ore? Those who wish can fish here under the ice of Lake Oreshen. Those who love heights are trained in hang gliding. Extreme sportsmen can take a parachute jump. Dog sledding, snowmobiling, modern sledding - all at the service of lovers of winter fun in the "Au". To experience the thrill, guests are invited to take a ride on the Zipline cable car. Its feature is that people are placed in a special capsule that moves at a speed of 70 km per hour.

Additional Information

Tourists like to bring souvenirs in the form of glassware or chocolates from Ore. Of all the wonderful restaurants in the resort, I would like to highlight the institution Buustamon Fjallgard. The resort has many hotels and residential complexes of the highest class. There are many bed and breakfast establishments. Holiday Club Are Apartaments is 350 meters from Auray Train Station, where many tourists stay. Very close to it, a hotel in the style of the Hotel Granen lodge was built, near which the ski slopes are located.

The Swiss ski resort is not the most expensive in Europe. Prices here depend on the season of stay and the class of accommodation chosen. A room in a three-star hotel will cost around 1,200 SEK. You can save on accommodation if you stay in a guesthouse. There the room costs about 600 SEK. A room in a five-star hotel with all amenities will cost 2,400 SEK per person. Many tour operators provide information on the cost of housing in the "Au". It is worth noting that a hotel booked in advance will be much cheaper.

Resort Reviews Are
