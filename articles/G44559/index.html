Hotel Perdikia Hill Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Fethiye): traveler reviews

Excellent exotic living conditions are offered to guests by Perdikia Hill Hotel 4 *. A lot of positive comments were left about him, which contain the opinions of tourists about the services provided within the walls of the hotel, as well as about the living conditions for guests.

All travelers staying at the hotel on a package tour, freely use the all-inclusive concept provided for in its framework, which implies the availability of three meals a day in the restaurant, participation in entertainment programs, as well as visits to the most interesting places.

general information

In 2005, the first doors of Perdikia Hill Hotels & Villas 4 * were opened for all foreign tourists. From then until now, guests from Russia who like the prevailing pricing policy in it are very fond of coming to this place. In 2012, a major reconstruction was made in the building of the main building, during which the interior was completely updated, and the technical equipment of the apartments was improved - in each of them individual air conditioners appeared, thanks to which the guests can now maintain the temperature regime in their room at the desired level.

Location

Perdikia Hill Hotel 4 * is located in Fethiye, which belongs to the large resort region of Ovacik. Its center is located 3.5 kilometers from this place, and all the entertainment in Fethiye is concentrated 5 kilometers from here - tourists can visit clubs, restaurants and several other types of establishments.

The coastline is quite far from the hotel, however this does not make it less popular among travelers. As for the airport, it is located 50 kilometers from Perdikia Hill Hotel 4 * (Fethiye) - in the large city of Dalaman.



Rooms

The hotel fund offers tourists a small number of apartments - 98. All of them are very comfortable and provide for the presence of modern furniture on their area. Fresh repairs were made in them, which tourists who visited the hotel in 2017 already managed to notice. The rooms are divided into two groups: Standard and Family, depending on the layout and on how many guests can be accommodated in them.

Each type of apartment has a private balcony with beautiful views of the urban surroundings and the sea in the distance. Each of them also has a well-equipped bathroom - it has a complete set of plumbing and bath accessories, designed for each tourist individually.

Standard

In Perdikia Hill Hotel 4 * (Fethiye), these types of rooms offer double and triple accommodation in their rather spacious area. Depending on this, a different number of beds is provided for guests: two small or one double. If a room has a pair of beds, then one of them will be single, and the second - designed for two.

For clothes of guests in the Standard apartments in Perdikia Hill Hotel 4 * there is a large wardrobe, which is divided into several shelves for sorting. To work with documents in the room there is a desk and a chair, where you can also place a laptop brought with you to rest. As a decor here, a large mirror in a beautiful silver frame hangs on the wall.

Opposite the sleeping places there is a suspended plasma TV, on which international channels are broadcast, some of which are in Russian.



Family

Guests in their reviews about the rest of the hotel often mention that some of its rooms are great for tourists with children. All of them belong to the same category - Family. This group has two-room apartments: one of them has a bedroom, and the other has a comfortable living room. As for places to stay, they are designed for three adults. If necessary, a baby cot can be installed in the apartment, however, you need to make a preliminary request for this, as the number of beds is limited.

Family rooms include wardrobes for clothes, a desk, and a safe, which is available for a fee.

The apartments are equipped with a large plasma TV, as well as a telephone, calls on which are not included in the price - they are paid separately.

Conditions for children

Guests staying on vacation with children can be completely sure that the kids will definitely enjoy being here - a number of certain conditions have been created in Perdikia Hill Hotel 4 *.

First of all, they consist in the availability of a kids club on the territory of the hotel. Parents can send their kids here for entertainment - here animators look after them, play with them, and also watch their favorite cartoons. Children staying here get acquainted with their peers, thereby developing the skill of sociability. Tourists recommend leaving the smallest guests with a nanny, whose services can also be used at Perdikia Hill Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Fethiye), but they are paid hourly.



In the open area, kids have the opportunity to visit the playground and pool. And in one place and in another place, they can quite actively spend their time enjoying games. In the pool, everything is designed taking into account safety requirements, the same applies to the site.

Many parents in their comments left on the results of a holiday with a child, note that a positive decision by the hotel is to provide extra beds for babies, as well as special chairs in catering establishments. By the way, the restaurant for children has a separate menu.

Nutrition

In reviews of Perdikia Hill Hotel 4 *, hotel guests often share their opinions on what kind of food is provided to travelers in this place. They note that the hotel has two restaurants in which guests can enjoy the amazing taste of dishes prepared in the traditions of Turkish and European cuisines. You can visit them on a free basis, and every day a buffet is organized here.

In addition to restaurants, there are three bars in the recreation area. They can delight visitors with delicious snacks and desserts. There is also a large list of cocktails and drinks available to order in a special bar card.



Additional features

Staying at the Perdikia Hill Hotel & Villa 4 * (Turkey / Fethiye), guests can attend various excursions organized by a special bureau located on the ground floor of the building. All of them are paid and are not included in the price of the permit.

Not far from the tour desk is the doctor‚Äôs office, where you can always turn for advice or help. Also at this place, travelers can visit several small shops that offer Turkish goods, ideal as souvenirs for relatives and friends.

Guests can spend time outdoors on the hotel's sun terrace and swimming in the pool. Visiting this place is completely free. The terrace is small in size, sun loungers and umbrellas are installed here, next to it there is a bar with cocktails and soft drinks. In its very center is a swimming pool with clean water.



Indoor facilities include a spa area that includes a sauna, pool, jacuzzi and hammam. There is also a massage room, where masters can please guests with pleasant procedures.

Beach

The hotel does not have its own beach site, but this does not stop its guests from enjoying the waves of the Mediterranean Sea. In their comments, they talk about visiting the coast, which is 300 meters from the hotel - it can be easily reached in five minutes walk.

As travelers say, the city beach here is pretty clean and well maintained. On its territory there are several changing cabins, public toilets and showers. If desired, everyone can rent a deck chair or umbrella - they are issued at a special point for a small fee.

There are many entertainments along the coastline, among which there are many extreme ones. Here, tourists can learn to surf and rent a yacht for a while for a walk in the open sea - often this option is used by couples who visited Turkey for a romantic trip.



Prices

The cost of a full package for a trip to the Perdikia Hill Hotel is an average of about 50-55 thousand rubles. Such a price is given in many well-known travel agencies that offer trips to this region. They note that the package necessarily includes not only rent of a double room for a week, but also all transportation costs.

As a rule, travelers get to Turkey by plane, and they are picked up from the airport by car or bus, which takes them to the place of rest as soon as possible. Tourists note that in this case the road passes in very comfortable conditions. In addition, the cost of providing the transfer is much lower than the local taxi services, so no one recommends abandoning it when compiling a tourist vacation package.
