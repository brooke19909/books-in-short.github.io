Villa Milica 3 * (Montenegro, Petrovac): description, service, reviews

Montenegro is a country where thousands of tourists from Russia and neighboring countries travel annually. Here you can see a lot of picturesque places that will win the heart at first sight. Here you can also swim in the sea, near which there are a lot of hotels for guests from abroad.

Villa Milica 3 * in Montenegro is one of the most comfortable hotels where you can stay for a reasonable price. Not far from it there is a sunny beach, and also from here you can easily get to the main attractions of the country.



Hotel Location

Villa Milica is located in a very beautiful place - the city of Petrovac, which is located on the Hercegnovskaya Riviera, almost on the very coast of the warm and clean Mediterranean Sea. There are beautiful picturesque gardens all around, bypassing which you can admire the endless expanses of the sea.

Tourists during their stay here, thanks to the mild and very warm Mediterranean climate, will not become weary of the heat. There are a large number of sunny days a year at a temperature comfortable for the body.

While on site, you can enjoy views of the Bay of Kotor, which is located nearby, as well as the tops of the cliffs that tourists love to conquer in their free time.



Rooms

The hotel has a small number of rooms - it consists of studio and luxury apartments, of which there are a total of 14 units.

Each of them has been completed with designer renovation with a unique interior in a modern European style. For the convenience of guests there is the necessary modern furniture, as well as individual bathrooms, which are equipped with showers and bathrooms. There is also a hair dryer in all apartments.

Guests can independently adjust the temperature conditions to the most comfortable with the help of modern air conditioners, which are in every room.

Studio Rooms

Studio-class rooms consist of one room of a small area, in which there is everything necessary for a couple of guests to stay. Some have wonderful views of the sea coast and water. Also, tourists living here have the opportunity to use free access to the Internet, constantly being in touch with their relatives and friends.

Tourists staying in studios can stay in comfortable large beds with hard mattresses. They can put their personal things in a small wardrobe closet, which is located against the wall, and valuables - in an electronic safe, for the use of which you will have to pay a separate fee. If desired, guests can use the minibar, but also not for free.

On the wall of the room there is a large plasma TV broadcasting satellite channels, including in Russian. Guests can use it for free.



Suites

Up to 4 people can be accommodated in luxury rooms - this is the number of beds on two large beds that is provided for in them. Here are two rooms for guests, one of which has a sleeping area, and the other has a living room. There is also a private terrace of impressive size, on which a small set of furniture is installed - a table and a couple of armchairs. On them, vacationers can take sunbaths or just admire the surrounding view.

Room is equipped with satellite TV, Wi-Fi access. There is a small personal kitchen. There is also a small dining area. In winter, guests can use the heater, which is also installed in the room.



Conditions for children

Hotel Villa Milica does not claim to be a family-friendly holiday destination, but there are still a small number of conditions on its territory in case vacationers decide to come with their kids.

First of all, it is possible to install a crib in any category of a room, and this is done completely free of charge. In addition, a playground for active pastime is equipped on site. Children under seven years old are free of charge.

Beach

Most tourists come to Petrovac in Montenegro in order to relax on the Mediterranean coast and gain strength before the start of a new working period. This hotel is no exception. Villa Milica has its own private sandy beach, which is located a little less than five minutes walk from the territory of the holiday destination. In order to get here, you need to overcome the track and the promenade.

The beach area is landscaped, it has amenities for a comfortable stay: shower toilets, as well as changing rooms, which guests can always use without a fee. They are also provided with comfortable sun loungers, umbrellas and air mattresses, but they will have to pay rent. Descent into the sea is very convenient, gentle, it is safe for children to be here.

On the sea coast, vacationers can not only sunbathe, but also have fun. Here you can rent a yacht or a small boat and go to conquer the sea. Also, tourists, being here, can ride a jet ski or hovercraft. For a fee you can go water sports, equipment for which you can rent.

Nutrition

Residents of Villa Milica 3 * (Montenegro) can eat their own cooked food (if they have rented a deluxe room) or in a small cafe located on its territory. Here tourists can be surprised by deliciously prepared Mediterranean food, as well as European dishes. The prices, as vacationers note in their comments, are quite reasonable, and the food at Villa Milica is healthy and tasty.

Sports and other entertainment

Sports enthusiasts will not find a large gym in the hotel, however there are other conditions for active leisure time. In particular, everyone can engage in free of charge on sites equipped on the street. In addition, for a fee, guests can hire an instructor and learn canoeing and surfing skills. For a certain fee on the coast you can go fishing. A bicycle rental service is available on site - many tourists rent this type of transport in order to explore the surroundings.

You can also visit the massage room, which is located in the Villa Militsa building - real professionals work in it to help you relax.

Pool

The guests of the villa have the opportunity to relax not only on the sea coast, but also near the pool, which is located directly on its territory. It is not very large in size and works only in the warm season, as it is open. In the tank, water is constantly purified.

Around the pool there is a small sun terrace with sun loungers - guests of the hotel can relax and sunbathe on them.

Services

Villa Milica provides a range of services for all guests, which make your stay even more comfortable. In particular, everyone can hand over their personal belongings to the laundry or dry cleaning - this is inexpensive, but the result, as vacationers note, is simply magnificent.

There is a 24-hour reception desk, where Russian-speaking administrators are always ready to help with the solution of various issues, to advise on the provision of services or living conditions. Here you can rent a left-luggage office for your luggage, if any.

All tourists who get to the vacation destination by plane arrive at the international airport of Podgorica or Tivat. From any of them you still need to get within 30-40 minutes. In order to significantly shorten the path of tourists and make their stay on the road more comfortable, the hotel provides everyone with a shuttle service, which is paid separately (many operators include them in the price of the ticket).

Travelers who wish to relax in this villa in Petrovac (Montenegro) must always remember that there are no smoking rooms, pets are strictly prohibited and the hotel does not accept plastic cards - cash only.



Prices

The cost of a ticket to the Villa Milica 3 * hotel, the description of which is presented here, directly depends on which airport will depart. For residents of Moscow, its average cost is about 62,500 rubles, subject to a one-week stay together in a studio room. This price includes the cost of a round-trip flight, room rental, meals, as well as a transfer.



Tourists reviews

A large number of reviews about Villa Milica can be found on social networks, as well as on thematic sites on the Internet. Tourists very often note with positive comments the picturesqueness of the place in which it is located, as well as the internal relaxed atmosphere. Often, vacationers say that there is a polite and professional staff who can always find an approach to the guest, treats them politely and with understanding. Here, great attention is paid to guests who relax with young children, creating a maximum of comfortable conditions.



Prices are generally pleasantly pleasing to all tourists. On their part, the quality of dishes prepared in a local cafe is also noted as positive. Otherwise, the service at Villa Milica is also at its best, due to which tourists get a good rest on its territory, receiving a lot of positive emotions and making a large number of vivid photographs.

Hotel guests recommend coming here to their relatives and friends, as well as to subscribers on social networks, leaving sometimes enthusiastic reviews about the rest. Often they attach a large number of colorful photographs to their comments.
