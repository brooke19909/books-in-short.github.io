Spain, Tenerife Island - a picturesque corner in the Atlantic Ocean

Spain is famous for its amazing climate and picturesque natural landscapes. The island of Tenerife is of volcanic origin, it is located in the Atlantic Ocean and belongs to the Canary Islands archipelago. Above its entire territory rises the majestic volcano Teide, which is the highest point and serves as a good guide for passing ships.

Island climate

Tenerife is characterized by a unique and mild climate, it is warm all year round. The temperature throughout the year is from 20 to 28 degrees, a significant impact on the weather conditions of the island have such phenomena as the proximity to the African continent and various currents. A feature of this area is the eternal "reign of spring", there is no sweltering heat.





Picturesque nature of Tenerife

Many natural attractions are famous for Spain. The island of Tenerife also has unique natural landscapes that create indescribable beauty. There are many green valleys and mountain gorges, bizarre rocky shores and sandy beaches. Very popular among holidaymakers on the islands of Spain, Tenerife leads the list of places most visited by tourists. People come here to shelter from the winter cold and enjoy the riot of greenery and warm sunshine.

Island beaches

Along the entire coastline stretch amazingly beautiful beaches. Over rocky cliffs that frame the coast, gorgeous views of the ocean open. The most popular beaches in Los Cristianos and Playa de las Americas, there are all conditions for a great vacation. There are many different bars and restaurants on the territory; zones for practicing various water sports are equipped.



All about attractions

Many different natural attractions, architectural structures and historical monuments keep the islands in Spain. Tenerife is one of the most amazing natural areas, here nature has created magnificent mountains and forests.

The capital of the island of Santa Cruz de Tenerife is a real corner of beautiful parks and gardens, interesting architecture and monuments. This beautiful and magnificent country of Spain, the island of Tenerife is worthy of real admiration: photos brought from a trip will always remind you of the magnificent peaks and coastal zones. Not far from the city is the Teide volcano, which houses the national park. The tourist center of Puerto de la Cruz is an amazing and unique city, here in the Orotava Valley there is a parrot park, in which more than two hundred species of these birds are collected. Another famous and striking attraction is the beach of Playa de Martianes with a huge sea lake and a modern holiday complex. Of the natural attractions, it is worth noting the dragon tree, which is a symbol of the island. Picturesque resorts attract tourists from all over the world Spain. The island of Tenerife is an ideal place for a good rest in harmony with nature.
