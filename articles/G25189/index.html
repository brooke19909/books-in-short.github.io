Nearby Attractions

Due to its convenient location, the main sights of Karlovy Vary are relatively close to the Tchaikovsky Hotel.

Closest to the complex is the Peter and Paul Church. This temple has the status of the Russian Church and is the only one in the city where the Russian aristocracy and representatives of princely bloods have long come.

In walking distance, on Castle Hill, is the Anglican Church of St. Luke, whose decoration is made in a pseudo-Gothic style. The church was built at the expense of the British, vacationing at the resort in the XIX century.

400 meters from the hotel there is Oleniy Skok Mountain, which has an observation deck overlooking Karlovy Vary. You can climb it by breaking 126 steps, or by funicular.

If you go to the Market Square, which is located 600 meters from the resort complex, you can see such a landmark as the Castle Tower. It is the last surviving fragment of a Gothic fortress, the construction of which dates from the middle of the XIV century.

The church of St. Mary Magdalene is 700 meters away.

From the hotel there is the opportunity to walk to one of the most famous attractions of the resort - the Diana Tower. The observation tower is located on the hill of Friendship, rise to it by funicular. It offers a picturesque panorama of the city.

Within 800 meters there is the Jan Becher Museum, in its place the first Becherovka healing liquor factory was once built. Here you can get acquainted with its history and go for a tasting.
