Hotel Grand Memories Varadero 5 * (Cuba, Varadero): overview, description, rooms and reviews

Cuba, Liberty Island is one of the most popular tourist destinations. Thousands of tourists come here every year in search of a relaxing holiday on the Caribbean. One of the most populated resort cities is considered to be Varadero, because here travelers are waiting for miles of white beaches and, of course, hundreds of comfortable hotels. One of the good places for a summer vacation is the hotel complex Grand Memories Varadero 5 *.

Of course, when choosing a hotel you need to consider many factors, including location, living conditions and services, food and entertainment on site. So what does the hotel look like and what do the former guests say about it?





Location Description

Of course, to begin with, it is worth learning about exactly where the hotel complex is located. Reviews of the hotel Grand Memories Varadero 5 * (Varadero) suggest that the location is quite convenient. The hotel is located just a few meters from the white sand beach - you can walk to it in a few minutes, even if you walk very slowly. This is a tourist area, nearby there are other hotels, cafes, entertainment complexes and shops.

The distance to the center of Varadero can be overcome in about 14 minutes, and already there will be no shortage of entertainment and attractions. In the hotel you can easily arrange a transfer - you can get there in 10-15 minutes. The airport is 45 km away.

Hotel infrastructure features

Grand Memories Varadero (ex Riu Varadero 5 *) is a large hotel complex, which consists of many separate two and three-story bungalows. The hotel area is simply huge, and goes directly to the seashore.

Reviews say that the hotel is very beautiful. It was opened just a few years ago, in 2010. Since then, repairs have been carried out here, and today the hotel meets all international requirements. The area is decorated with palm trees and bright flower beds. There are walk alleys, terraces for sports and recreation, a garden lush with greenery, secluded grottoes where you can relax in peace and harmony with nature - travelers are offered everything they need for the most pleasant and eventful vacation.





Hotel Grand Memories Varadero 5 * (Cuba, Varadero): description and photos of rooms

This is a large hotel with 1,100 rooms. For the most part, these are standard rooms or one-room suites. In addition, Royal Suites (in addition to the bedroom has a spacious living room with upholstered furniture), as well as large rooms of the Royal Master Suite, which consist of a spacious living room, two bedrooms and two separate bathrooms with hydromassage systems.

According to reviews, the rooms are spacious with large windows and access to a private balcony. Guests can count on comfortable furniture and modern appliances. The air conditioning system will help fight summer heat. The room also has a ceiling fan, safe, TV (some satellite channels can be watched), telephone, iron and ironing facilities.

The room has a spacious bathroom where you can take a shower or relax in the hot water (in some rooms the bathtub is equipped with a hydromassage system). You will be provided with a hair dryer, clean towels (they are changed daily) and various hygiene items.





Where to eat at the hotel?

The hotel complex Grand Memories Varadero 5 * operates on an all-inclusive basis. You can count on good nutrition, as well as free drinks from a local manufacturer. By the way, even very late dinners are held here. There are several places at the hotel where guests are offered a bite to eat. There are two main restaurants where guests are invited for breakfast, lunch and dinner. Everything takes place in the form of a buffet, and guest reviews suggest that the menu here is varied, the dishes are always fresh, and all are served in sufficient quantities.

In addition, the Grand Memories Varadero 5 * hotel invites travelers to an Italian, Creole and Asian restaurant, where you can please yourself with delicious dishes. The pool has several bars (two of which are located directly in the pool), where guests are offered drinks and snacks. There is a cafe on the beach, and another bar in the lobby. There are no problems with food at the hotel guests.





Beach: beachfront amenities and leisure facilities

Arriving on the island, travelers, of course, seek to have fun, relax and improve their health on the beach. What does the hotel complex Grand Memories Varadero 5 * offer? Varadero is a resort that is famous for its incredibly beautiful coast. The hotel is located almost on the seashore and owns its own beach area.

The coastline is quite wide and covered with soft, white sand. The entrance to the sea is gradual, there are also small areas where kids splash around. Of course, the hotel provides its guests with comfortable sunbeds, umbrellas, large beach towels. Directly on the beach, you can get a chilled drink or even a light snack.

As for active leisure activities, there are more than enough opportunities here. You can ride boats, water skiing, bananas, rent boats and catamarans, play beach volleyball, engage in sailing and windsurfing, take a short diving training course. Travelers really have fun.





Additional service on site

Since this is a large resort complex, they tried to create all conditions for a relaxing holiday on its territory without minor household problems. For a small fee, tourists can use the laundry and dry cleaning services, as well as hand over ironing clothes. Here you can exchange currency. As you know, Cuba is a country where connecting to the Internet is not so simple. However, in public areas, a connection is available but is available for a fee.

In case of health problems, be sure to contact the staff - they will immediately call you a qualified doctor. You can rent a car at the hotel, and this, in turn, opens up new possibilities for traveling around the country. There are also several shops where you can buy some food, interesting souvenirs and various household trifles.





How are tourists having fun at the hotel?

At the hotel, travelers can not only relax in the evenings and eat, but also have fun. In the courtyard there are two huge pools with terraces, solariums around - you can swim, lie on a comfortable sun lounger, take sun baths and, of course, chat with other tourists.

There is also a spa with a jacuzzi, sauna, beauty and massage rooms. There is also a gym with modern equipment and experienced instructors. By the way, from time to time there are group sports classes. It is nice to have fun playing tennis and ping pong. A large golf course is located next to the hotel. And guests here are entertained by experienced animators. In the daytime, they organize various entertainment and sports events and competitions, and in the evenings you will find rich show programs and live music. Later, travelers relax in a nightclub.

Of course, you can have a good time outside the walls of the Grand Memories Varadero 5 * hotel. Cuba is a beautiful country with a rich culture and a lot of attractions. In the hotel, and in other places, you can purchase an interesting sightseeing tour.





Are there any conditions for relaxing with a child?

Is the Grand Memories Varadero 5 * hotel suitable for a family vacation? Of course, yes, since most of the guests are parents with children. Travelers can choose more spacious rooms, and an extra bed in the room is delivered free of charge. The on-site restaurant offers a large selection of dishes for children. Polite waiters provide a special chair for feeding small guests.

Kids are also not bored at the hotel. For them there is a shallow clean pool, as well as a spacious outdoor playground. There is also a playroom (mini-club) where children play and develop under the supervision of experienced educators. Throughout the day, children are entertained by funny animators - they organize various performances, competitions, contests, team games, and evenings in the evenings.

Grand Memories Varadero Hotel 5 *: guest reviews

As you know, reviews of people who have already managed to visit a particular hotel contain a lot of useful information. This hotel complex has a good reputation - travelers respond warmly to this place. It has everything you need for a comfortable stay. The rooms are spacious and clean, the appliances are modern and, of course, in good condition. The staff here are very nice and helpful, and the service is of a high standard.

The undoubted advantage is its own beach and a large territory that can accommodate more than a thousand tourists at once. Here you can relax and have a good time, and there are plenty of entertainment for guests of different age categories. Tourists recommend the hotel complex Grand Memories Varadero 5 * (Cuba, Varadero). Prices for tours are quite reasonable - about 170-180 thousand rubles per week in a double room with flights. Cuba is perfect for almost any type of vacation, and attentive service and modern hotel comfort will make your vacation unforgettable.
