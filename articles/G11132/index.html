The most famous sights of the Moscow region: Abramtsevo estate

In order to get to an amazing and interesting place, it is not necessary to go far and spend a lot of time on the road. Unusual sights can be found in the near Moscow region. One of such amazing places is the Abramtsevo estate. What is interesting about this complex, and how to get to it?





A bit of history

The first mention in the historical documents about the estate dates back to the middle of the sixteenth century. At that time, the lands belonged to Mr. Volynsky, and the estate was called Abramov. However, there is almost no information about the fate of the estate in subsequent centuries, and it begins its modern history only in the eighteenth century. The famous landowner Fyodor Golovin seriously engaged in land improvement, and it was he who created the complex, known today as the Abramtsevo estate. It is noteworthy that the estate brought a little profit, but the stunning nature and favorable location attracted owners who replaced each other in this place. The famous writer Aksakov had a chance to live in it, and then the Mamontov family became the owners of the estate. The new owners carried out a large-scale reconstruction and erected several new buildings.

From antiquity to our days



What is the Abramtsevo estate today? What kind of sights can you see here? The building of the estate itself is not of great interest - it is the standard house of a small landowner of that time. It was not possible to save the ceramic factory built by Mamontov. But any visitor can see with his own eyes the unique collection of the museum, which includes more than 28 thousand exhibits. Here you can see authentic pieces of furniture and household utensils of different periods, works of art and samples of folk crafts. The second-hand collection is also amazing, which includes not only rare books, but also manuscripts. The estate Abramtsevo has a chic park and a pond. On the territory of the estate there are also some outbuildings, the Spasskaya Church, the Polenov cottage.

Abramtsevo (estate): how to get and get on a tour



The museum complex has a complex address, located near the urban settlement of Khotkovo, Sergiev Posad district, in the village of Abramtsevo. The distance from Moscow is about 60 km. From Yaroslavl station there are electric trains to Abramtsevo station, then you will have to walk a little. You can drive a personal car along the Yaroslavl highway to the turn on Leshkovo, then following the signs. You will pass Khotkovo and will be able to stop near the gate of the museum-reserve. It has secure parking, cafes and souvenir shops for tourists. Group tours should be arranged with the museum in advance . The museum is open from 10 to 19 every day, it is possible to change the work schedule during the holidays. For single tourists and small groups, the cost of access to the park will be less than 100 rubles, you will have to pay separately for visiting each exhibition. You will need at least 2 hours to explore the entire complex. Amateur shooting in the territory is allowed, it is necessary to arrange an individual photo session with the administration. It is definitely worth a visit to this place for a closer acquaintance with the history of the native state and its culture. Without a doubt, you will appreciate the local beauty of nature. According to the generally accepted opinion, the Abramtsevo estate is considered more beautiful in the summer months, but even in winter it looks charming.
