Appearance

The smartphone received a 4.7-inch display, but at the same time it looks quite large. The main processor is the MediaTek MT6582 SoC platform , known for most budget models from 2014-2015. The crystal was made according to the 28 nanometer process technology, which provides fairly low power consumption and low heating of the device when working with resource-intensive processes.

The processor works in conjunction with 1 GB of RAM. This is a bit by modern standards, but quite enough for comfortable work with two or three lightweight applications at the same time. In order to unload the RAM to the maximum, the system is optimized by the manufacturer, as a result of which the user is always available about 500 MB, the rest is occupied by system applications. Smartphone Communication Features

Nowadays, a smartphone without 4G support is sometimes considered too outdated, but do not forget that the higher the communication standard, the greater the battery consumption. In this model, there is no 4G support, but the mode of all frequencies and 3G standards, including HSUPA and HSDPA, is fully implemented.

SIM cards are used in a standard size, you do not need to cut or change them to start using your smartphone. When calling traditionally, one of them becomes inactive, since the device is equipped with only one radio module.

In addition to the radio module of the cellular network, the Lenovo S650 telephone is equipped with Bluetooth 4.0 generation modules, as well as Wi-Fi with support for b / g / n protocols. If necessary, the smartphone can be turned into a portable Wi-Fi router in several stages. Check with your operator in advance whether additional funds will be charged for this, since recently in some companies a similar practice has appeared.

The smartphone does not do without a GPS module. The signal level of the satellites is pretty confident even in cloudy weather. When testing in most cases, a cold start took about 1 minute, while a hot one took less than half a minute, or even several seconds.





Memory

The internal non-volatile memory of the smartphone is not particularly happy with its size. It is a built-in flash drive with a capacity of 8 GB. About 3 GB of it are occupied by system files. In total, a little less than 5 GB of disk space is available to the user from the box.

To expand it, you can use microSD memory cards with a capacity of up to 32 GB, preferably class 10, in order to ensure maximum performance. According to the feedback of users who tried to use larger memory cards, the smartphone simply refuses to see them, so when buying it is worth counting on the limits declared by the manufacturer. However, comparing prices for Lenovo smartphones, this model wins in terms of price / quality ratio, and it‚Äôs worth considering - perhaps this amount of memory will be quite enough.

A charger is supplied with the smartphone, capable of delivering power up to 1 A. At these rates, it takes about 2 hours to fully charge the battery.
