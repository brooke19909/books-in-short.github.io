Aquamarine 4 * (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria): description, rest and reviews of tourists

One of the most popular summer destinations for Russians and residents of other CIS countries for many years is Bulgaria. This is not surprising, because the cleanest sea and picturesque nature are perfectly complemented by excellent hotels for every taste and budget and the hospitality of the Bulgarians themselves. In turn, one of the most popular resorts of this country is Sunny Beach. It is located just 30 kilometers from the large city of Burgas. There are many different hotels. Today we want to tell you about the Aquamarine 4 * hotel. We learn about what this hotel is, what services it offers to guests, as well as about the possibilities of local relaxation.





Bulgaria, Hotel Aquamarine 4 *: location

This hotel is located just one kilometer from the center of the famous Bulgarian resort Sunny Beach. The distance to Burgas, one of the largest cities in Bulgaria, is only 30 kilometers. By the way, in Burgas there is also the nearest airport. Therefore, if you are going to Sunny Beach, it is best to choose Burgas as the arrival airport. Hotel Aquamarine 4 * is only 250 meters away from the Black Sea coast. There is also a bus stop just a hundred meters from the hotel , from where you can go to the nearest settlements. Near the hotel there are many shops, restaurants, bars, cafes and nightclubs. Thus, you will not be bored here.

The rules of the four-star hotel "Aquamarine"

In accordance with the rules of the Aquamarine Hotel 4 *, check-in of newly arrived guests is carried out from two in the afternoon. If you arrive at the hotel earlier, then provided that the booked room has already been vacated by previous hotel guests and prepared by the staff, you will be immediately accommodated in it. If the room is not ready yet, you can leave your luggage in the storage room and go for lunch, to the pool or straight to the beach. On the day of departure, the room must be vacated by noon.

Since Aquamarine 4 * (Sunny Beach) positions itself as a family hotel, children of all ages are allowed here. However, the issue of additional payment for a child‚Äôs accommodation should be clarified separately with the hotel. As for extra beds, please note that in addition to the two existing beds, it is possible to place another adult or cot in each room.





We hasten to disappoint fans traveling in the company of their four-legged pets. So, in accordance with the internal rules of the hotel, the placement of guests with pets is strictly prohibited.

As for the accommodation fee, it is made on the day you leave the hotel. You can pay in cash or with a Visa or MasterCard plastic card. If you have already paid in advance for accommodation to the travel agency in which you booked the tour, then you will only have to pay for additional services (minibar, room service, etc.).

Sea beach

Most of the beaches in Bulgaria are municipal. The nearest beach from Aquamarine Hotel is 250 meters away. Therefore, it can be reached on foot in just a few minutes. It is sandy. For the use of sunbeds and umbrellas will have to pay separately.

Meals at the hotel "Aquamarine"

The price of accommodation in the four-star Bulgarian hotel "Aquamarine" includes only breakfast. However, you can immediately pay for all-inclusive meals. A buffet is served each morning in the hotel's restaurant . You can have lunch and dinner here, having ordered from the menu a variety of dishes from both Bulgarian and international cuisine. There is also a lobby bar on site where you can enjoy a cup of coffee or a skillfully prepared cocktail in a pleasant and relaxing environment.





Infrastructure

Hotel Aquamarine 4 * (guest reviews only confirm this) has a developed infrastructure that allows you to make the rest of tourists as comfortable and varied as possible. So, there is a large parking lot, the use of which is free for hotel guests. Therefore, if you decide to go on vacation to Sunny Beach by your own car or plan to rent a car directly in Bulgaria, then you will not have any problems with parking.

As for the reception, the on-duty administrator is here around the clock. Thus, in case of any problems you can always contact here. There is also a safe and luggage storage at the reception. Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas of the hotel.

Hotel "Aquamarine" offers its guests a number of paid services. These include dry cleaning and laundry services, room service, use of the conference room, banquets. In addition, you can rent a bicycle or a car at the hotel, book an excursion you are interested in, or buy a souvenir or anything you need to relax in the shop. In addition, an airport shuttle will be arranged for an additional fee.





Entertainment at the four-star Bulgarian hotel "Aquamarine"

The hotel has a swimming pool with an area designed for the youngest guests. Near the pool there are sun loungers and umbrellas, the use of which is free for hotel guests. In addition, guests of "Aquamarine" can visit the gym, massage, sauna, play table tennis, billiards or darts.

Aquamarine Hotel 4 * (Bulgaria): tourist reviews

Almost everyone, planning a vacation, tries to consider the maximum possible number of reviews both about the country he wants to go to and about local hotels. Therefore, we bring to your attention the generalized opinion of tourists who stayed at the four-star hotel "Aquamarine", located in the Bulgarian resort of Gold Coast.

So, it should be noted that most travelers speak extremely positively about this hotel. Almost everyone notes the exceptional cleanliness of the rooms and regular thorough cleaning. The friendly staff also leaves a pleasant impression. A big plus is that many of the hotel staff speak or understand Russian well. In this regard, here you will probably not have difficulties in communicating with the hotel staff.





Tourists leave good reviews about food at the Aquamarine Hotel. So, the menu here is quite diverse: guests are offered various types of meat, fish, vegetables, fruits, delicious desserts and ice cream. The hotel's restaurant also serves national cuisine.

I liked the vacationers and the beach. It is sandy and very clean. There are many bars near the beach where you can order both non-alcoholic and alcoholic drinks, as well as a varied snack.

As for the shortcomings, the main of them, tourists consider the lack of sunbeds near the pool in high season. But this problem is characteristic not only for Aquamarine, but also for the vast majority of hotels. Therefore, if you plan to spend several hours near the pool, it is recommended to get up early and take a deck chair, putting a towel on it. Another disadvantage, some tourists consider the lack of television channels in Russian in the rooms, although most vacationers do not attach any importance to this.

Almost all vacationers agree that the four-star hotel "Aquamarine" is an example of an excellent combination of price and quality. Most of the tourists are ready to recommend it as a good place to spend a vacation with their family and friends.
