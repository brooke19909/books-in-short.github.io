For the safety of people and things, fire and security alarms were created. But simply purchasing them is clearly not enough. What is a security and fire alarm?



The installation of these systems is complicated, and before you begin to work, you must understand what you have to deal with. So, they are a set of technical means that are used to detect a fire in a timely manner, as well as unauthorized entry into a protected area. In the case of fixing problems, a signal is sent to the control room to send a quick response detachment and mechanical methods of influence are activated (if we talk about fire alarms, the automatic fire extinguishing system starts).

Categories



We know what security and fire alarms are. The installation of this security complex can be carried out in three approaches, which depend on the categories of equipment:

  1. Designed for centralized management. By this we mean the presence of a computer with special software that manages all the technical details.
  2. Equipment designed to collect and process information that comes from sensors. These include control panels for fire and security.
  3. Directly the sensors themselves, which record violations of activity, as well as warning devices (for example, loudspeakers).

Installation of fire alarm systems differs from security ones usually by the location of sensors. So, for the safety of the premises, it is necessary to keep windows under control, monitor whether someone is moving at the wrong time, and carry out many other operations.
