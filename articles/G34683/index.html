How to disable the weather forecast for "Beeline" yourself?

An option from Beeline, which provides subscribers with weather information online, has previously been quite relevant. Indeed, you must agree that it is very convenient to check what temperature conditions await us and whether to take an umbrella before leaving the house. However, due to the current availability of unlimited and wireless Internet, the weather forecast can be obtained for free. In this case, you can even choose the appearance of a form that displays information. In this regard, many customers have a question about how to turn off the weather forecast for Beeline. This article will provide several ways to get rid of an unnecessary service.





Disconnect via mobile device

Remove from the list of services available on the room, the weather forecast can be even from the most mobile gadget. To do this, use the following USSD request: * 111 * 4751 #. In response, a notification will be sent that the option is disabled. View the weather forecast in a previously familiar way will no longer be possible.

Internet deactivation

Before describing how to disable the weather forecast for Beeline via the Internet, I would like to draw attention to the fact that you can use a personal Internet assistant to carry out any operations on the room. And to get access to the page of your number, just register on the portal, log in, and then select the "Disable" command in the list of services, opposite the desired service.

In the list of available options for activation, you can find useful and interesting services that can be useful to the client in everyday life or when being, for example, in roaming.





Using Room Management Services

For those subscribers who are interested in how to deactivate the Weather service from Beeline in other ways, we inform you that it is also possible to use general services for managing the number. There are two ways to access the service menu:

  • Enter USSD request
  • call the short number.

Conclusion

Earlier in this article, we presented several methods that will answer the question of how to turn off the weather forecast for Beeline.
