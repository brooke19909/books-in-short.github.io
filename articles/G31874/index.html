Krasnodar: Theater Square (history, significance and location)

Young people like to gather at the Theater Square in Krasnodar. And more recently, this place was called the October Revolution Square, but they decided to change it to something more modern. However, his beauty and significance did not change at all from this.

Description

In such a famous city as Krasnodar, Teatralnaya Square is surrounded by historically significant buildings. On one side is the Ekaterinodar fortress, where the city hospital is currently located. Also located here are the city hall building, the Drama Theater, which is now lined with europanels. Back in 1897, a monument dedicated to the bicentennial of the Kuban Cossacks was erected on the square. In Soviet times, it was destroyed, but in 1999 the monument was restored.

The fountain on Theater Square in Krasnodar is the largest fountain in southern Russia. To look at it, not only residents from different parts of the city come here, but also non-resident tourists, as well as foreigners.

Theater Square (Krasnodar) - the administrative center

It is on the square that almost all the cultural and business life of the entire city is concentrated. Near it there are many offices and various institutions where thousands of citizens work. Around the fountain in the evenings, magnificent light music shows and performances are held. In such a beautiful city as Krasnodar, Teatralnaya Square is famous mainly due to this fountain, which is considered to be the hallmark of the whole Kuban.





In the fountain, 375 nozzles and more than 600 lamps interact with each other. It is they that allow you to create water figures for various melodies that can charm Krasnodar residents and visitors. Of course, the cost of creating and maintaining such a chic structure was impressive, but it paid off. And how happy the children are when they are brought to a real water show!

Location

In the historically significant city, which is Krasnodar, Teatralnaya Square is located on Krasnaya Street. It also crosses Budenny Street. From these names alone it already blows the history of our glorious people. Nearby is the building of the Gorky Academic Drama Theater and the building of the local administration. Getting there is not difficult: any local will easily show you the direction and number of the public transport route.
