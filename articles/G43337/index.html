Hotel "Crystal" (Novy Urengoy): address, contact information, description of rooms, photos and reviews

If you are looking for accommodation in Novy Urengoy, Hotel Crystal will certainly interest you. This is a comfortable establishment in the northern part of the city next to a picturesque lake. Guests are provided with comfortable living conditions and a host of related services.

Location

Hotel "Crystal" in Novy Urengoy is located at microdistrict. Anniversary, 2 / 4A. It is only a 20 minute drive from the airport and a quarter hour walk from the train station.

Contacts of the Crystal Hotel in Novy Urengoy can be found on the official website. The institution can be reached as follows:

  • From the railway station you need to get to the stop "KSK Molodezhny" by bus number 7. Transfer to bus number 8 and get to the stop "Student campus". Walk 100 meters to get to the hotel.
  • From the airport to the stop "KSK Molodezhny" can be reached by bus number 1. Transfer to bus number 8 and get off at the same stop "Student campus".

Additional Information

If you plan to stay at the hotel in question, check out some additional information. Here are the main ones:

  • The hotel has a single checkout time - noon.
  • For early accommodations, an additional charge of half the cost of living will be charged. For early check-in from 06:00, an additional hourly fee will be charged.
  • If the guest delays in the room up to 6 hours, an additional hourly fee is charged.
  • If you check in at midnight and check out at noon, payment for the room will be charged in half.
  • If you check in after the checkout time and stay before 12:00 the next day, the fee will be charged for a full day, regardless of the number of hours of stay.
