Jasmine Palace Resort 5 * (Egypt / Hurghada): photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Egypt is an interesting country with many resort areas. In Hurghada, the tourism business began to take shape several decades ago, which means that the infrastructure of the Egyptian holiday is well developed. There are many hotels that are suitable for tourists with different requirements and preferences. Hotels of the highest category ("deluxe" or 5 stars) are in great demand among tourists. An example is the hotel complex Jasmine Palace Resort 5 *. It will be discussed in this article.





Briefly about the resort

Before we talk about the hotel, let's see what the city of Hurghada is like. A map of Egypt will tell you where this famous resort area is located. It is divided into two parts: the "old" city with attractions and architectural monuments and the "new" city with a large number of modern hotels.

It so happened that in many ways the quality of service and service in hotels depends on the location of the resort. Everyone knows that European hotels are fundamentally different, for example, from Turkish, despite the same category and stardom. Naturally, in "older" buildings, accommodation will cost less than in recently rebuilt hotels. Before an exciting trip, be sure to take a look at the year the hotel was founded.

Hurghada from year to year gathers millions of foreign tourists on its beaches. Many are attracted to the magnificent climate of the resort, because here you can relax all year round. A varied excursion program, clean beaches, coral reefs - all this attracts travelers from different countries.

Hurghada hotels are located throughout the city, the best hotels of Egypt (5 stars) are built along the coastline, some are near artificial lagoons.

Jasmine Palace Resort is located near Hurghada, 22 km from the center and 18 km from the international airport, so tourists quickly get to the hotel.

Entertainment and leisure activities



At Jasmine Palace Resort (Hurghada) There is plenty of entertainment for every taste: 7 pools, one of which is heated, a diving center, an animation program by the pool and on the beach. There is an aqua center where you can ride a water slide, ski and swim under the supervision of a coach.

For charming ladies, a beauty salon and a hairdresser are open from morning until evening. Tourists can get temporary tattoos, take part in sports and entertainment events, competitions, show programs, play giant chess, etc. The hotel has a so-called Health Center with a sauna, jacuzzi and steam room.

Lovers of outdoor activities can play beach volleyball, darts, billiards or visit the gym. No less popular way of spending leisure time was snorkeling, canoeing and surfing with a parachute (kiting).

From 22:00 to 24:00 you can dance at a disco or listen to live music. For those who wish to continue the banquet, dancing is provided until 2:30, but for a fee.

Opportunities for children

A vacation at the Jasmine Palace Resort with children will become less troublesome for parents, as there is everything necessary for entertaining young travelers on the territory: an outdoor heated pool in the winter, animation programs and playgrounds.

Children from 4 to 12 years old can attend a special children's play club, which is open daily from 10:30 to 16:00 local time. Babysitting services are paid separately.

The restaurant does not have a separate children's menu, but there are special chairs.

Cost of tours

Over the past ten years, tours to Egypt have become the most popular for Russians. Hotels that tourists choose most often have a high star rating (from 4 to 5). Most likely, this is due to the adequate cost of rest. The average price of a trip to the Jasmine Palace Resort for two with accommodation in a standard one-room suite will be 36,000 - 40,000 rubles for 7 nights.

Before buying a tour, it is worth noting the fact that rooms with garden views are much cheaper than others. For example, the same ticket to a room with a view of the pool will cost from 40,000 - 45,000 rubles.

The bonuses of the hotel, the so-called compliments from the hotel, affect the price of the tour: a meeting of the newlyweds with fruits and champagne, a birthday present (cake) and presents for regular customers (fruit basket). To receive bonuses you must provide the relevant data:

- A copy of the marriage certificate, if you are newlyweds;

- a copy of your passport if you had a birthday;

- data on previous visits, if you are a regular customer.
