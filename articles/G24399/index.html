How to register a personal account?

To register a personal account, you need to visit the Federal Tax Service once to obtain a password. Before that, you will have to write a statement. You can pre-fill all the data on the service. What data must be entered during registration:

  • Personal data.
  • Phone numbers, email.
  • Passport data.
  • INN

After receiving the password, you must change it within one month. Otherwise, the personal account of the taxpayer will be closed. This is for the safety of citizens.

The first three days after registration and receipt of the password, data about the taxpayer is generated. No need to be surprised if at first you do not find the necessary information on the site.

If the car tax does not come, what should I do?

The first is to calculate it yourself in order to know how much you need. But the peculiarities of the transport tax are that there is no definite formula. Many people mistakenly believe that driving experience affects the amount. But no. The insurance depends on how experienced the driver is and how old the car is. To calculate the tax yourself, you need to know two things:

Sales Tax: Accruals and Benefits

Tax on the sale of cars is not included in the category of transport. It is taxed according to completely different formulas. Therefore, if a car sales tax has come, you must carefully check everything. As a rule, a resident independently submits a declaration. The following should be considered here:

  • The life of the car.
  • Selling price.




If you have been an owner of a car for more than three years, then no declarations are required. This is not taxed. If the owner is less than three years (according to MREO), then the amount depends on it. Any sale of less than 250 thousand is also not subject to personal income tax. As for the large amounts, here the resident can choose the tax base:

  • Revenue minus expense. For example, I bought for 500 thousand rubles, sold for 400 thousand rubles. Therefore, there are no taxes.
  • Revenue minus deduction. For example, a car was bought for 160 thousand and sold for 260 thousand. It will be beneficial for a resident to apply the formula: (260-250) x13.

To apply the first formula, it is necessary to confirm all the data with sales contracts, checks, receipts, etc. In the second case, 250 thousand is a tax deduction.

Transport tax: accrual problems

Vehicle owners often face problems related to the transport tax:

  • A tax on a stolen car comes.
  • Obligatory to pay for someone else's car.

The last item is most common.



The tax on the sold car came: what to do?

Such a situation is far from uncommon. There are times when the owner sold the car a few years ago, and the tax still comes. The reason for this is that the former owner did not remove the car from the traffic police register, limiting itself only to the contract of sale. This causes a number of problems.

On the one hand, a person is not the owner of the car, and on the other, all the data in the traffic police is for him. But the new owner of the vehicle has long sold it to another, and that third and so on. There is no end and edge in this situation. The way out is to file a wanted number (not to be confused with theft), and then deregister the car.

You can provide a sales contract in tax, but they rely on the database of the traffic police. This document may simply not be taken into account. Thus, you must first pay all taxes, and then prove through the court that the car is not yours.

If the car is stolen, then you need to find an investigator conducting this case. Take the necessary documents from him and come to the tax office with them.
