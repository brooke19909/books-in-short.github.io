Description of the recreation center "Ivanhoe" in Volgodonsk

The camp site is located on the banks of the Don surrounded by a unique park. For picnic lovers, the designers made improvised tables from polished boulders, which you can sit around on stump-chairs. For living there are houses, trailers, comfortable rooms. If you want to visit the knightly Middle Ages, at your service is an exclusive house made of rubble stone. Tourists can leave their cars in a large and convenient parking lot located near the recreation center. For the catering of tourists there is a cafe with a capacity of 50 seats. There is a banquet hall. There are several gazebos for guests. There is for several people, as well as one large gazebo, which can accommodate a company of up to 100 people. All arbors are equipped with sockets, there is lighting. Tourists can make the necessary purchases in a small grocery store. For lovers of outdoor activities equipped with sports fields, you can go fishing or just sunbathe on the beach. Cultural events are held daily for holidaymakers. The recreation center "Aivengo" in Volgodonsk welcomes guests all year round. It is located at: Rostov Region, the city of Volgodonsk, Rest Street, 5.
