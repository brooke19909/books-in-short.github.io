Nightdress: pattern, model selection, sizes. Women's nightgown

Every woman can sew a nightgown. The details of the decor will depend on the skill of the dressmaker, but the basis will not change. In a few hours, a piece of material will turn into a new thing. There are so many different types of knitted lace, ribbons, nets and appliqu√©s on the shelves of sewing shops! Of course, you can buy a ready-made nightdress. You will not have to build a pattern for it, but an independently sewn thing has the warmth of human hands. This is a good gift for a loved one and the opportunity to take care of him.

On the same pattern, you can sew a summer dress, sundress, topic or tunic. So this is in every way a useful activity. Usually, even a tailoring and sewing course begins with sewing a nightdress. On the processing of the neck, hem, side seams, mastery is practiced. On the construction of flirts, pockets, collars - modeling.

Size: fabric count

The principle of constructing a pattern of a nightdress is that the drawing is repelled from the most voluminous place of the body. Someone has breasts, someone has hips. Be that as it may, you will need two pieces of fabric with a width equal to half the control volume plus an increase of ten centimeters to freedom. If the selected material has a width, for example, 80 centimeters, then for women's clothing will have to buy four lengths.

This product parameter is measured from the seventh cervical vertebra through the highest point of the chest to the length that is intended. At the same time, it is necessary to stand directly so that distortions in measurements are not obtained. It is better to ask someone to help take measurements. Five centimeters per hem are added to each part. If the fabric is calculated in a store, five centimeters are added to this number and multiplied by two or four, depending on the width of the fabric.

To process the seams and the neckline, you need a slanting inlay to match the tone of the fabric. Some, on the contrary, choose a contrast, and then it acts as an element of decor. Threads are cotton, size 50 for chintz and 40 for flannel. They, too, are matched to the tone of the fabric. It is better to choose lace, seam, braid, ribbons, having material with you. It will be a shame if the purchased decor does not fit the color.

One-piece sleeve product

The nightdress should be comfortable, not to impede movements. According to the principle of a pattern of a nightgown, the lower Russian shirt was sewn in antiquity. Instead of tucks, assemblies were made on ribbons. The sewing place of two pieces of canvas was closed with embroidery. The nightdress can also be made in the Russian style and trimmed with ethnic motifs. She will hide the uneven seams that the beginner will make.

For the first experiment, choose a model with a minimum number of seams, cuts, without coquette and set-in sleeves. If the material allows, you can build a pattern directly on it on the wrong side. To do this, you need a washable felt-tip pen. Those who are afraid to work as a felt-tip pen can purchase tailor-made colored crayons or take a remnant. Get a clear, thin line.

The construction of the sleeve is carried out according to the principle of undershirts. To do this, 24 centimeters (for 48 sizes) are laid off from the control point in both directions, drawing a square. Add eight centimeters and draw a smooth rounding, as shown in the figure.

The simplest pattern: style selection

This model is a bit more complicated. Fold the fabric in half to form a rectangle. The fold passes along the shared thread. The figure shows a simple pattern of a nightdress, built on the back of a piece of old wallpaper. If you decide to use them, then pin the parts as close to the bend as possible. How to build such a pattern will be shown below.

After marking the length of the product (point A - point 102), one fourth of the chest circumference is measured. Draw a horizontal line. Its level is determined by taking measurements. A line of the side seam is drawn from it using the entire width of the fabric. Adjust the length of the product. The lower part is ready. The upper one is built according to the drawing, using individual measurements. The back of the nightgown is cut out in the same way - several lines are built at the control points and connected.

The figure shows how to model a women's nightdress on the straps. Sundresses and open dresses on the floor are sewn according to the same pattern. When modeling, the chest lateral tuck closes, and at the same time it expands at the waist. When a skirt is cut, these elements are not taken into account. Insert lace strips from sewing. The length of the straps is determined individually. A braid is inserted in the side seam.
