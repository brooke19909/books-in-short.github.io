Camp "Friendly" for the summer holidays of your children

Long summer holidays make parents think about how to diversify the leisure of their son or daughter. Children's camp "Friendly" in Genichesk is what you should pay attention to. It is in this beautiful institution that the children will have a great time, improve their health and receive unforgettable emotions.



Where is the camp "Friendly"

The Druzhny DOL is located in a picturesque and beautiful corner of Ukraine. The camp "Druzhny" in Genichesk, that is, in the immediate vicinity of the town, is located on the Arabatsky arrow. You can get to the camp by car or by shuttle bus from the city of Kherson.

Children accommodation

Children aged 6-17 are admitted to the Druzhny camp. They are divided into units according to age. Children live in comfortable and cozy rooms. The buildings are two-story and can accommodate up to two hundred and fifty children at a time. Each room has:

  • sleeping places in an amount of 4 to 6, depending on the number of children living;
  • bedside tables near each bed for storage of personal belongings;
  • the room also has a wardrobe in which children can put their clothes and shoes;
  • also all rooms have small balconies.




On each floor there are:

  • shower with round-the-clock hot and cold water;
  • Bathrooms are also located on the floors of the buildings.

The buildings are equipped with modern equipment and redecorated.

Camp infrastructure

On the territory of the children's camp "Friendly" there is everything that is necessary for a good rest and a varied pastime. The VOL has the following infrastructure:

  1. Residential building for vacationing children.
  2. There is also a concert hall that seats up to 500 people at a time.
  3. Films are shown in the same room.
  4. There is a sports ground on the territory where a football field, a volleyball court, and basketball hoops are located.
  5. Along the entire perimeter of the Druzhny camp, arbors for detachment and social events are installed.
  6. And the most important nuance is that there is a private beach near the territory.




All buildings are capital, built of high-quality and durable materials. Therefore, on the territory of the children will be in complete safety and comfort.

Children's leisure in the Druzhny DOL

Camp "Friendly" is a place where children will experience real childhood and a surge of emotions. A full rest with proper and rational nutrition, as well as in the company of professional teachers, animators and organizers, boys and girls will be busy, passionate. The main types of entertainment in the Druzhny DOL are:

  1. Creative events during which children can show their talents and feel the spirit of competition. These events will bring together boys and girls in teamwork in the preparation process.
  2. Evening entertainment is usually offered to boys and girls to choose from. For those who wish to have fun and have fun, you can go to a disco, where modern music sounds, and various dance competitions are held. Those who want to relax are invited to visit the cinema, where they watch their favorite children's films and cartoons.
  3. Also, boys and girls can have a pleasant and exciting time attending training classes. In the Druzhny camp, there are circles where children are taught to sew, draw, and make various crafts from natural materials.

By sending your child to Druzhny DOL, moms and dads can be calm. The constant support of professional teachers, experienced medical staff, trusted chefs and round-the-clock protection will ensure a safe, fun and enjoyable stay for children.
