"Kudryashovskaya Zaimka": description and prices

Modern man increasingly feels the need to be in the lap of nature. After all, it was there that he could take a break from the noise of the city, throwing aside everyday issues. The recreation center "Kudryashovskaya Zaimka" guarantees its guests a quality vacation, which they can independently plan according to their desire. Here you can simply enjoy the beauty of nature or spend time learning equestrian skills.

general information

Holidays at the base can be both a wonderful vacation and a wonderful reason to have a great weekend. Guests come to "Kudryashovskaya Zaimka" families, companies or couples. Here you can rent a house for the required number of people. Meals are made in a cafe with an excellent menu. The base itself is located in the village of Priobsky, which is famous for the beauty of nature and clean air. Nearby is a lake, the road next to which is highly appreciated by guests for their gorgeous views. Finding a vacation spot close to the city makes it very popular. Therefore, there are always visitors here. People come to take a break from the bustle of the city, breathe clean air and ride horses. Often there are photo shoots or romantic dates. Any celebration held in such a beautiful place will undoubtedly be remembered by every vacationer.

On the basis of "Kudryashovskaya Zaimka" a small zoo is open in which children and adults can chat with animals. There are not only bears, but also a raccoon and even a camel with a monkey. Guests love to come here in the cold season, as you can go skiing or special sleigh rental. Children here will always be interested. While they play, adults can enjoy cooking barbecue. A lot of animals and birds live in the forest, a meeting with which will allow you to see quite rare specimens. Among them there are storks, capercaillie, chipmunks.

How to get there

You can reach your destination on your own. Base address "Kudryashovskaya Zaimka": Novosibirsk region, Priobsky village, Lesnaya Polyana street, building 3a. By public transport, tourists should get off at the Marx Square subway station. Next, you need to find a stop called "Crystal Shop". A bus number 1999 and a minibus (1120) run from it. Exit should be at the stop "Kudryashovskaya Zaimka".

Additional services

Any rest will become more memorable if it is combined with pleasant leisure. "Kudryashovskaya Zaimka" offers its guests a lot of opportunities to spend their free time perfectly. Here, tourists can play soccer or volleyball to maintain a healthy lifestyle. Paintball enthusiasts will also be pleased, as it is also available here. Many guests like to spend the evening preparing dishes on the grill. This makes food much tastier and healthier.

The equestrian base, which is located in a place of rest, deserves special attention. Many guests come here to ride or chat with horses. These beautiful and intelligent animals give you unforgettable moments of joy. For the safety and comfort of clients, instructors work who can accompany during trips. A school is open for beginners, where they can learn to ride horses. Among other services there are even horseback riding trips, which can last from 6 hours, and can take several days. Many visitors book a costume horse carriage. Thanks to him, any holiday will become even more spectacular.

Holiday Prices

Guests can stay in rooms with private facilities. Each room has a certain number of beds. You can familiarize yourself with the approximate cost in advance. The room usually has a toilet and shower, kettle and other necessary things.

The recreation center "Kudryashovskaya Zaimka" offers the following prices:

  • Double room - from 2600 rubles per day.
  • A room for 6 people costs from 4800 rubles per day.
  • Room for 8 people - from 6400 rubles per day.

At the base, you can order additional services for a fee. So, the price in "Kudryashovskaya Zaimka" for the rental of horses is from 250 rubles per 20 minutes. The rest of the offers on horseback riding can be found at the club.

Approximate cost for other services:

  • Football field - 500 rubles per 1 hour.
  • Volleyball field - 300 rubles per hour.
  • Equipment and equipment for paintball - from 100 rubles per person.
  • One coloring ball costs 1.8 rubles.
  • Rest in the bath - from 1250 rubles per hour.
  • Barbecue area - 100 rubles for 1 hour.
