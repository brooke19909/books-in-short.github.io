Hotel Naslada 3 * (Balchik, Bulgaria): description and reviews of tourists

One of the most popular tourist cities and major ports of Bulgaria is the city of Balchik. It is surrounded by beautiful nature on the shores of the warm Black Sea. This town has a unique and interesting architecture: cute colorful houses are located here amphitheater. Balchik boasts a fairly wide hotel base. There are hotels of different levels of stardom, and boarding houses, and motels. If you consider the city of Balchik as a place of relaxation, "Naslada" will be an excellent option for a budget and comfortable accommodation. Today we offer a closer look at this hotel. We will find out what and at what price the guests are offered, and also find out whether our compatriots liked the rest in this hotel.

Hotel Location



Hotel Naslada 3 * is located in a very good location - near the former summer residence of the royal family of Romania, as well as the Botanical Garden, which is the second largest in Europe after Monaco. The city center is only 1 km away, so the entire infrastructure of this large tourist center is within walking distance of the hotel. The distance to the nearest resorts to Balchik is relatively small: to Golden Sands - 20 km, to Albena - 9 km, to Varna - 36 km. The nearest airport is in the vicinity of Varna, 46 km from the hotel complex under consideration. Thus, after arriving at the air harbor, your journey to Naslad will take less than an hour. On the way, you can admire the natural beauties of this region of Bulgaria. Since the vast majority of tourists go to the Black Sea coast mainly for beach holidays, the issue of remoteness of the coastline plays a big role in choosing a hotel. As for the hotel complex under consideration, it does not have its own beach. The nearest beach is municipal and is 600 meters away. At the same time, the road to the sea runs through the picturesque Botanical Garden.

Bulgaria, Balchik - "Naslada" 3 *: photo and description of the hotel



This hotel complex has an average size and consists of three four-story buildings with beautiful names "Naslada", "Svetlana" and "Yovanna". The hotel has 160 comfortable standard rooms and apartments. The guests of the resort complex are provided with a wide range of various services designed to make the vacation interesting and unforgettable. The hotel staff is responsive and attentive, always ready to help tourists in resolving any issue. The hotel complex Naslada 3 * (Bulgaria) is perfect for families with children and older people, as well as for fun youth.

Hotel Policies

In the hotel complex under consideration, the rules clearly establish the time of check-in and check-out of guests. So, the resettlement of arriving tourists is carried out after two in the afternoon. However, this rule cannot be called too strict. The hotel administration is always trying to make the rest of its guests as convenient as possible from the first minutes after their arrival at "Naslad". If you arrived at the hotel earlier than 14:00, then the staff will do everything possible to check you in the room of the selected category as early as possible. However, this is not always possible at the peak of the holiday season, when the hotel is 100% full. In this case, you can leave things in the luggage room and go for lunch, to the pool or for a walk. As for the day of departure from Naslada 3 * (Bulgaria), it is necessary to vacate the occupied room before noon. At the same time, you should pay at the reception during your stay (if you have not fully paid for the tour to your tour operator). Please note that the hotel only accepts cash payments. If you plan to pay with a plastic card, then you should first ask the hotel administration if this is possible.

Accommodation for guests with children



Hotel Naslada 3 * (Balchik) welcomes tourists who come on holiday with children of all ages. In accordance with the rules of the hotel, if you place kids up to three years old in your room on the existing sleeping places in it, then you will not have to pay extra. For children from 3 to 12 years old on extra beds will have to pay 50% of the cost of living per person per day. Accommodation of older children will cost 80% of the cost per night per person. Please note that only one extra bed can be accommodated in each room. The need to provide such a service should be informed in advance to the administration and wait for confirmation from it.

Accommodation with pets

The good news for tourists who prefer to travel in the company of their pets will be that the Naslada 3 * hotel allows their accommodation. However, you must first notify the administration of the hotel complex and wait for a positive response from her. Also note that this service may be provided for a fee.





Number of rooms

As mentioned above, the hotel complex under consideration includes three four-story buildings with 160 comfortable rooms located in them. The rooms here are represented by standard rooms and apartments. Each of them is comfortably furnished, equipped with air conditioning, refrigerator, TV, telephone. In the bathroom you can find all the necessary bath amenities. There is a balcony. Cleaning, linen and towels in the rooms of the three-star hotel "Naslada" (Balchik) - reviews of tourists only confirm this - are carried out on a regular basis.

Dining at the hotel

The room rate in the Bulgarian hotel "Naslada" includes only breakfast, but for an additional fee during the day you can have lunch and dinner with delicious dishes of both traditional Bulgarian and international cuisine offered in the restaurant of the hotel complex. In addition, the lobby bar offers a wide selection of local wines and other alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, as well as snacks. The hotel also has a barbecue area and a poolside bar.





Sea and beach

As already mentioned, the hotel "Naslada" (Balchik) does not have its own beach. By the way, most hotels on the coast of Bulgaria do not have their own beaches. The distance from the hotel in question to the Black Sea is about 600 meters, and the road runs through the picturesque Botanical Garden. The beach is sandy, is in municipal ownership. Sun loungers and parasols can be used on a fee basis. The beach has everything you need for entertainment and sports on the water.

Entertainment

On the territory of the Naslada 3 * hotel there are two excellent pools. There is a swimming pool for the youngest guests. In it, they can safely frolic in clean fresh water. Near the pools there is a terrace with sun loungers, tables and umbrellas, and a bar with a wide selection of various drinks is nearby.

Outdoor enthusiasts can go to the gym, play ping pong or billiards, rent a bicycle or enjoy water sports on the beach.

Guests wishing to relax can enjoy the on-site sauna and massage services.

Infrastructure



Hotel "Naslada" (Bulgaria), traveler reviews are another confirmation of this, has everything you need for a comfortable stay and a peaceful and relaxing holiday for its guests. So, for example, the reception here operates without interruptions around the clock, and the staff is multilingual: most employees speak Russian well. Turning to the reception, you can exchange currency, rent a bicycle, book a tour, and if necessary call a doctor.

The hotel has free parking for guests, so if you decide to rent a car or arrive in Bulgaria by your own car, then you will not have problems with parking. The hotel complex Naslada 3 * also has a shop where you can buy everything you need for your vacation.

The cost of living in a hotel

Due to the fact that the hotel "Naslada" is a three-star hotel, accommodation in it can be classified as budget. So, accommodation for one tourist in a standard room will cost from 16 to 20 euros per day, depending on the season. The price for accommodation in triple apartments is from 39 to 55 euros per day.
