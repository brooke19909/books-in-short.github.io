Keychains made of beads are not only an original jewelry on your phone or keys, but also a memorable gift.

Keychains from beads to the phone are always very beautiful and varied. That is why in our time this activity, and especially woven beaded key chains, has become especially popular among needlewomen. After all, in the process you can show all your artist talent and creative abilities, creating a real masterpiece to the envy of everyone around. But the most important thing is that such a work will be done with your own hands, and it is not difficult to do it at all, you just need a little imagination and a little patience.

This lesson is suitable for beginner needlewomen. Large and complex products require a lot of patience, craftsmanship and skills. And anyone can weave key chains from beads, and the patterns of their weaving are so diverse and at the same time simple that the whole process will bring a lot of positive emotions. There are colorful elephants and dogs, cute cats and cubs, and many other funny objects and animals. There is no desire to work according to ready-made schemes from the Internet, you can safely connect your imagination and get to work!

So how to weave beaded key chains? Firstly, to master this beautiful art, of course, you will need beads, it is best to choose Czech one. It is the most even and smooth, with a rich palette of colors and shades, and coloring, resistant to various natural factors. Secondly, you need a fishing line on which beads will be strung, but it is better to opt for copper wire. She is thin and flexible. Such a keychain will keep its shape perfectly. In addition, it is much easier to work with copper wire, unlike fishing line, which will require a special thin needle. But this is not a problem. Nowadays, you can easily find even the thinnest bead needle, which allows you to work with the smallest beads.

It is best to immediately prepare everything you need for work. To make beautiful keychains from beads, you will definitely need various chains and carbines, as well as beads, pins and scissors. The workplace should be freed from unnecessary items and covered with a white cloth, so it will be much easier to work, and even if the beads crumble, they will be easy to assemble. Before starting work, you should definitely decide on the shape and color of the keychain. A variety of needlework sites can help with this, where professional craftswomen tell and show how to weave key chains with beads with their own hands.

Keychains made of beads on the phone, made in the form of a zodiac sign, are very beautiful. Their manufacture is quite simple, but at the same time, such a gift for a close friend will become one of the most memorable, original and touching. There is no nicer gift than a surprise prepared with your own hands, in which a piece of soul and love is embedded. Such bead trinkets can be made according to ready-made schemes, or you can come up with an original pattern yourself. It can be anything, for example, a heart or some kind of geometric figure. In the manufacture of this design, you need to be guided by only one single rule - the scheme must be slightly stretched along a vertical line. Only then will the finished product get the correct square shape.

All of these gorgeous bead charms are braided using hand weaving technology. Mastering this technique is very easy. Such work should always start from the bottom corner on the right side and move up. If such a decoration is made on copper wire, it can be done in one layer, as the wire frame will help him maintain his shape, but if the fishing line is chosen as the basis, it is better to make the keyring double. In this case, all the extreme beads and the last row should be connected. In the end, it remains only to attach a carbine to the keychain. Such a decoration will always look fresh and original on the phone, keys or in the car.
