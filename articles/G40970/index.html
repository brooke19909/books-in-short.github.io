Guide for travelers. Sights of Berdsk

For many people, including residents of Russia, the word "Novosibirsk" says little. This is somewhere in Siberia - that's all that can answer. But the mysterious name Berdsk generally stands beyond understanding. However, today the southern part of Siberia is being built up and populated at such a speed that not only Novosibirsk itself, but also its satellite towns have become very popular for moving. Today we want to talk about the tiny Berdsk. This is already a practically sleeping area of Novosibirsk, which is rapidly growing and developing. We want to tell readers about the sights of Berdsk. Especially useful information will be for those who plan to move to these parts in the future.





general description

You can find many reviews about this town. For some, it is tiny and boring, but for others it is very cozy and more suitable for life than a metropolis. In fact, it grows and blooms. Well-groomed streets and squares, lots of greenery and luxurious flower gardens - all this makes the city very nice. Sights of Berdsk are not outstanding, in each city there are historical monuments and special places. But let's talk about everything in order.

To a modern look

The city was founded back in 1716, but today you cannot find a single old building here. However, natural beauty more than compensate for this shortcoming. The fact is that the waters of the Ob reservoir absorbed the old city. But today, their vast expanse is the best decoration. In the summer, many citizens and guests of the city rest on the banks of the reservoir. Speaking about the sights of Berdsk, you can not ignore this amazing reservoir. You are waiting for the most beautiful places and protected pine forest, resorts and holiday homes.



Bird Spit

While in the city, you must definitely choose the time to go to the reservoir. It stretches along the entire settlement, but it is the spit that is the most favorite place for tourists. At any time of the year you are guaranteed stunning landscapes and excellent outdoor recreation. The spit coast stretches for 80 km, here everyone will find a place to taste. And what photographs are obtained here is just a sight for sore eyes.



History of the city

Sights of Berdsk can tell a lot of interesting things about how the city was created and grew. If cultural education is included in your plans, then be sure to visit the Museum of History and Art. It contains various exhibits and photographs telling about the complex history of the city. Exhibitions of various subjects allow us to consider each individual episode of the story in detail. The creation of the Berdsk prison, the conquest of Siberia, the life of the Russian peasant and much more.

Temples and churches

Sights of Berdsk include a stunning temple, which began to be built today, in 1992. The Transfiguration Cathedral in white stone looks very impressive and noble. Here lie the relics of St. Akaki Athos. The belfry has nine bells. The cathedral has icons brought from monasteries. Some of them contain the relics of the great martyrs.

The Church of the Presentation of the Lord is a small wooden temple restored on the site of an old church. He is very comfortable. There are many ancient icons on the iconostasis.



Cultural rest

In the central region there is a House of Culture, and in the microdistrict - another one, called "Homeland". There are various circles and sections, performances of ensembles and simply themed evenings. City cinema "Orion", equipped with modern equipment, invites guests to view the most interesting paintings. Sights of the city of Berdsk are not only churches and museums. In 2007, the Ice Sports Palace was opened here. It is located on Borova Street. There are training facilities, choreography rooms and a huge fitness center. Here you can get lessons in figure skating, as well as hone your skills. The relaxation area allows you to relax in the skillful hands of a massage therapist.

Parks and squares

The sights of Berdsk do not end there. This city is very green. A huge number of small squares and walking areas allows you to spend the evening exactly as you wish. The city has a Victory Park, where a majestic monument is erected in memory of the feat of local residents in the struggle for freedom. The mass grave of the partisans who fell during the Civil War was preserved.

The main attractions of Berdsk also include a monument to Afghan soldiers, a monument to sailors and Zoe Kosmodemyanskoy. The park of urban culture and recreation is a great place to spend time with your family. There are attractions, pavilions and cafes, equipped playgrounds. Festivals for the holidays are most often held in this park. Come here with the kids, they will love it.
