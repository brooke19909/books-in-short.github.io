Smartphone BQ Strike 5020: specifications and description

BQ Strike 5020, the characteristics of which will be discussed in this article, is a Russian-made smartphone. Assembly is carried out in China. At retail, the price does not exceed 5,750 rubles ($ 100). It is such a low cost that makes this gadget attractive to most buyers. And what characteristics can it boast of? Let's get it right.

Appearance

The BQ Strike 5020 smartphone looks quite normal, which does not cause any negative emotions. The rear panel has a matte surface, so that even the black version is not easily soiled. A metal plate runs in the center of the housing. It performs several functions at once: it removes heat and gives rigidity. The side frame is plastic and has an arched shape. Although it is convenient to hold the device in your hand, the dimensions (145 √ó 73 √ó 10 mm) and weight (174 g) are a little disappointing. It is not very clear why the manufacturer made the case so thick. As a rule, this is dictated by a capacious battery, but not in the case of this model.





On the front side there is a panel with navigation buttons. They are not highlighted. Users noted the assembly with positive reviews. Even if you press hard on the cover, a deflection will not form. Slots, backlash and creaks are absent. The only thing that spoiled the impression of interaction a little was the easily soiled screen. The prints on it are erased quite problematically. This disadvantage is dictated by the lack of oleophobic coating.

BQ Strike 5020: specifications

In order for the smartphone to only please the user, it is necessary to select it, focusing on the technical characteristics. When purchasing BQ Strike, do not make an exception. Just note that the equipment of the phone fully corresponds to its value, so you do not have to wait for miracles from the "filling". So, let's look at the main characteristics of the BQ Strike 5020.





  • Screen. A comfortable diagonal size of 5 inches is offered on this gadget. High detail, wide viewing angles and realistic color reproduction are provided by the IPS matrix. HD resolution is laudable. Includes film.
  • Camera. The main module, which is based on a 13-megapixel sensor, was pleased with the excellent characteristics. There is autofocus, there are no comments on its work. In low light, you can turn on the flash to take a more detailed picture. A 5 megapixel camera is offered for selfies.
  • Hardware platform. The system row is 32 bits. The main chipset is MT6580 manufactured by MediaTek MT6580. For work with graphics used Mali-400 MP. The amount of memory is quite modest. And if users are still ready to put up with one gigabyte of RAM, not everyone will agree with the available 4 GB of internal (only 8 GB). The only thing that calms down is the presence of support for flash drives up to 64 GB.
  • Battery. This is the weakest side of the gadget. Battery capacity - 2000 mAh. With a small load, one charge is enough for 24 hours. In order to restore the battery life, it will take 2.5 hours.
  • OS Branded shell is missing. The phone is running Android 6.0.

BQ Strike 5020: reviews

Most buyers find this smartphone attractive. Of course, price plays an important role. For relatively little money, the manufacturer offers a good screen, a high-resolution camera and the current version of the operating system.





Customers also highlighted the presence of an independent slot for a memory card, so there is no need to abandon the second SIM card. The performance characteristics of the BQ Strike 5020 are a little short, but in terms of price / quality, you can put up with this. The system functions quite tolerably; no serious malfunctions were noticed. But if all of the above can be safely attributed to the merits, then the battery is clearly the weak side of the gadget. Owners will have to get used to the fact that you need to connect a smartphone to charging every night.
