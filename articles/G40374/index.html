Huawei Honor 6: model review, customer reviews and experts

Everything necessary for work is in the boxed version of Huawei Honor 6. A review of the specifications for its delivery indicates the presence of such components and accessories:

  • Brand stereo headset.

The physical buttons for powering up and volume control are displayed on the left edge of the smartphone, and the other three touch buttons, as expected, are harmoniously located under the screen.

Graphics

The Huawei Honor 6 phone is equipped with a high-performance graphics adapter called the Mali-T628 MP4. The latter will cope with any task today. Photos and videos

As expected, there are two cameras at once in this smart phone. The one that is displayed on the back of the device is based on a 13 megapixel sensor element. The developers did not forget to expand the capabilities of this camera using autofocus, digital zoom and dual LED backlight. In general, it is impossible to find fault with the quality of images obtained with its help: they are impeccable. Also, with its help, you can record in HD format. The quality of the videos in this case is also excellent. There is also a front camera for Huawei Honor 6. Photos taken with it will be much worse: it has a sensor of only 5 megapixels. Battery features

One of the main disadvantages of this device is the built-in battery, which in case of breakage can only be replaced in a specialized service center. On the other hand, such a constructive solution provides impeccable build quality of the housing. The capacity of the complete battery is 3100 mA / h. One charge is enough for 2-3 days of battery life with an average level of use of Huawei Honor 6. Reviews of some owners suggest that with maximum battery life savings, this value can be increased to 4-5 days. But at maximum load of this device, one charge lasts a maximum of 8-12 hours. That is, every evening this smartphone will have to be put on a charge at a similar intensity.

As expected for the Huawei Honor series of devices, the firmware of this device is supplemented by the proprietary shell Emotion UI version 2.3, which is literally crammed with a number of useful utilities. There is also a set of basic applications and standard programs from Google on the device. Communications

Impressive opportunities for data exchange in Huawei Honor 6. Reviews of satisfied owners on this issue once again confirm this. In this case, we are talking about the following communication modules:

  • The main way to connect to the global web is through Wi-Fi. In this case, the peak data transfer rate can reach a fantastic 150 Mbps. That allows you to download and upload files of any size. The only drawback of this wireless standard is its small range, which in the best case can be 10 meters.
  • This smartphone model can be equipped with one or two slots for installing SIM cards. The first slot allows the device to work in all mobile networks: GSM, 3G and LTE. As a result, if there is a 4th generation connection, the speed can reach the same 150 Mbps. But the second slot (if any) allows the gadget to function only in networks of the 2nd generation.
  • The developers have not forgotten about bluetooth - this is an ideal solution for the exchange of a small amount of information between mobile devices.
  • For navigation, a universal ZhPS module is installed in this device, which allows this smart phone to exchange information not only with GPS, but also with the domestic GLONASS.
  • To connect to a computer and to charge the battery, the most popular port today is used - microUSB.
  • For external acoustics, it is best to use a 3.5 mm port. It is brought to the top edge of the smartphone.
