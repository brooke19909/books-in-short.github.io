Xiamen, China: description, attractions, leisure

The most curious point on the world map: a port city, an island city, a city-reserve and the most beautiful place on the entire coast of China - Xiamen. It is interesting to absolutely everyone, including both modern and colonialist architecture.

For several centuries, this is one of the important ports of the country, and in the 80s of the last century, Xiamen became the first economic zone. Since then, the city has been developing rapidly. And today the best vacation in China at sea can not be found.





Location

This amazing seaside city is located in Fujian province. It is territorially divided into several parts: the mainland Haitsan and Jimei and the island Gulanyuy, connected to the mainland by a dam. A hiking trail, a motorway and a railway run along it.

History

The first mention of the city of Xiamen in China is found in manuscripts dated 1387. The city began with a sea fortress. And in the XVIII century, Xiamen already briskly trades with the cities of Southeast Asia. In 1727, the district capital of Quanzhou was transferred here to control all growing companies and operations.

In 1842 (according to the Nanjing agreement), the city was opened for trade with European powers. International companies knew him as Amoy (due to the peculiarities of the local dialect).

By the end of the 19th century, Xiamen became the main port for coolie workers who emigrated from the country to America. From the beginning of the last century, the development of the city practically stopped, until it, like the whole country, was swept by the economic revolution of the Deng Xiaoping era .

By the end of the 20th century, Xiamen received the status of a special economic zone and quickly became one of the leaders in the modernization and transformation of China.





Description

This city in China is one of the richest and fastest growing with a population of almost 4 million people. The south coast of Xiamen is beaches of stunning beauty and the main campus of a local university, the west - modern port facilities.

Mostly foreigners live on the islet of Gulanyuy. Sometimes the area is called paradise. Perhaps because mopeds, cars, rickshaws and motorbikes are prohibited here.

Jimei District is famous for Jimei University and the Turtle Park. This is a kind of memorial complex dedicated to the famous national hero Chen Jiang. By the way, he himself was buried in a park and a traditional tortoise grave.

Attractions Xiamen

The main one, of course, is the sea. But the centuries-old history of the city offers hundreds more options for interesting places. Consider the most interesting places.

Tourist mecca

One of the most favorite places for tourists is the national treasure of China - the Nanputuo Temple of the Tang Dynasty. Followers of Buddhism and just pilgrims most often get religious tours to this temple.





Its semicircular golden pavilions with green roofs, the Halls of the Great Mercy, the Great Hero, the Heavenly Kings and the Precious Hall amaze with their luxury. Inspection of the temple begins with two ponds, on both sides of which are the temple gates.

In the main Hall of the King of Heaven, Maitreya, the Buddha, is sitting in a pose with crossed legs. Behind him stands the guard and the main protector of Buddhist teachings. Behind the doors of this Hall, a small courtyard opens with a Bell Tower and a Tower of Dragons. After passing through the courtyard, the temple guests enter the Heroic Treasure Hall. This building has two floors and three Buddhas. The foot of the statues is made in the form of a sacred lotus. They are embossed with biographies of the Buddha and the famous Chinese traveler - the monk Xuan Zang.

The Hall of Great Mercy is a recognized spiritual center. It is made in the form of an octagonal pavilion and hides a statue of Guanyin inside.

The Hall of Great Compassion introduces tourists to the figures of badhisattvas. At the back of the courtyard of the temple is a pavilion built in 1936. It houses collections of calligraphy, Buddhist manuscripts, ivory sculptures and other Chinese art.

Tourists are invited to visit the library of the temple, followed by tombstones and small halls.

Museums, parks, lakes and other attractions

A visit to the Piano Museum will be quite interesting. It is located in two beautiful buildings right in the center of Shuzhuhanhuayuan Garden. The museum has collected more than seventy instruments from around the world. From it, moving along the main street, you can get to the building of the former British consulate, built in 1870. Today, the Museum of Coins is open in it.





Xiamen in China is famous for its unique Yuandang Lake. It is famous not only for picturesque views in the evening (the illumination of numerous sculptures is turned on), but also for large flocks of herons.

No less beautiful is the promenade of Lujiang Dao. It stretched along the western tip of Xiamen. And although not as long as in other major cities, it is very shady and comfortable. At its intersection with the Zhongshan Lu shopping district, there is a ferry pier.

Locals prefer to spend family vacations in Bai Lu Zhou Park, and tourists in Qingyuan Piano Garden. In addition to the usual shady alleys and quiet paths, the park is interesting for its art gallery and audio-video hall.

From this park in the cabin of the cable car you can reach the Rock of Sunlight. This is the highest point of Xiamen Island - 93 m above sea level. The panorama opening from this place is amazing. Already in our time, on the eastern side of the peak, the Temple of the Rock of Sunlight was erected with an open-air statue of Guanyin, but surprisingly fits into a single ensemble and style of this part of the island city.

The Hulishan fortress, erected, according to historians, at the end of the 19th century impresses with its foundation. It is made from a mixture of brown sugar, clay, sand, camphor, wood syrup and glutinous rice. The fortress itself is granite. It contains the cannons of the Ming Dynasty.





A famous attraction is the Xiamen University. Rather, the campus of students of Jimei. Each building is erected in the architectural style of Fujian. The most striking holiday of the town, according to tourists, is the Dragon Boat Festival.

The Botanical Garden in Xiamen (China) is represented by a huge number of interesting subtropical plants.
