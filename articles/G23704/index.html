For what children and adults love the water park "Batumi"

An adult and a child love to have fun and experience a lot of new positive emotions. All this can be obtained by visiting the water park "Batumi"





When and where was it built?

The exact date of the opening of the entertainment complex is unknown. This approximately happened in mid-2008.

Waterpark "Batumi" has the same name with the city in which it is located. It can be found on Batumskaya Embankment (Sheriff Khimshiashvili Street, 12). This is a very bright and clean place, near which there are many natural sources that favorably affect the state of the human body.

The main attractions

Waterpark "Batumi" includes more than 10 types of attractions and entertainment attributes for any age category, namely:

  1. Five types of pools with various purposes. The easiest is for beginners who first entered the water. There are also separate water spaces for children and sports. The most extreme is the wave pool, in it even the bravest man will receive a lot of adrenaline.
  2. Six water slides with various sizes are equipped on the territory of the water park.
  3. There are also various water rides, such as swings and huge balloons.

The park periodically hosts recreational activities on various subjects. Every weekend, open-air discos are held.





Additional services

Many people visit the Batumi water park not to go for a swim. There are many more reasons to come to this place:

  • A huge number of sun loungers, equipped in the park. Everyone can comfortably relax, sunbathe and have a cold drink in a beautiful setting.
  • Conveniently located lockers for storing personal belongings and a locker room.
  • There is also a cozy cafe on the territory of the entertainment complex, where the most juicy and delicious dishes are prepared for visitors.
  • We should also talk about all the conditions intended for the youngest guests. The smallest pool with fountains is equipped for them. A child of three years of age can play here. It is also possible to visit the nursery. Several times a month for kids, discos are held with the participation of animators - heroes from popular cartoons.
  • Another advantage is the availability of a hotel complex in the park, which is very convenient for nonresident citizens.




We should also say about the bath complex. The entertainment complex also has a thermal zone. Here you can visit the Russian, Turkish and Finnish baths.

Reviews

For nine years, a huge number of people have been visiting the Batumi water park. Reviews about him can be heard very different. Basically, you can see comments from them as follows:

  • I like the fact that children under 6 are free. In other similar entertainment complexes, this age limit is only up to three years.
  • Many people express a negative opinion that all pools and slides never work at the same time. They are always included separately. People who come from other cities would like to enjoy their vacation as much as possible.
  • Another drawback is the cold water in the shower stalls.
  • Separately, visitors praise the good-natured and polite staff.
  • Most of all, positive feedback is received by employees of children's entertainment centers. These are miracle educators who easily find an approach to each kid. They seem to create a holiday for every little visitor.

Everyone can visit the water park in Batumi at any time. The prices here are the most optimal. Approximately the cost per person from 6 years is 30 GEL. Given the current exchange rate, this is 669.46 rubles.
